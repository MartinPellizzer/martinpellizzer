Geranium haussknechtii

Geranium haussknechtii, commonly known as the Schomburgk geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pinkish-red flowers. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring full sun to partial shade.

Geranium haussknechtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in rock gardens and dry landscapes.
