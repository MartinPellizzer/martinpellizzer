Garnotia emodi

Garnotia emodi, commonly known as the Himalayan snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its elegant, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cold, mountainous environments. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, often covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Garnotia emodi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in alpine gardens.
