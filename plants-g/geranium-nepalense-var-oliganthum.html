Geranium nepalense var. oliganthum

Geranium nepalense var. oliganthum, commonly known as the Nepal geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It features upright stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners interested in native flora.
