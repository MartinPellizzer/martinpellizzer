Geranium purpureum var. intricatum

Geranium purpureum var. intricatum, commonly known as the purple geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, pentagonal flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with contrasting veins. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming season add ornamental value to gardens.
