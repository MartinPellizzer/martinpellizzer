Goodenia peacockiana

Goodenia peacockiana, commonly known as the peacock goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
