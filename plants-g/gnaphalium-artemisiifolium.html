Gnaphalium artemisiifolium

Gnaphalium artemisiifolium, commonly known as rough hairgrass, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.
