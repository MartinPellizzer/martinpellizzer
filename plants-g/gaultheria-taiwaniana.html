Gaultheria taiwaniana

Gaultheria taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan wintergreen, is a shrub native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible and have a sharp, peppery flavor. Gaultheria taiwaniana thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its aromatic leaves contain compounds similar to those found in wintergreen oil, giving it a distinctive scent.
