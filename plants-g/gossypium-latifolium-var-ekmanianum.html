Gossypium latifolium var. ekmanianum

Gossypium latifolium var. ekmanianum, commonly known as the broad-leaf cotton variant, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and produces fluffy, white cotton-like fibers. This variety is native to certain regions of South America, particularly in Brazil. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is notable for its potential in textile production and ecological significance in its native habitat.
