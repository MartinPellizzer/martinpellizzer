Grevillea australis var. subulata

Grevillea australis var. subulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Grevillea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters, which appear in spring and attract nectar-feeding birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
