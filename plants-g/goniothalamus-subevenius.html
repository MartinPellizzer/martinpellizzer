Goniothalamus subevenius

Goniothalamus subevenius, commonly known as the Sarawak tamanu, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Borneo.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds.

It thrives in humid, lowland rainforest environments and is valued for its medicinal properties.
