Gymnostachyum magnum

Gymnostachyum magnum, commonly known as the giant gymnostachyum, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can reach several feet in height, composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil. Its leaves are typically broad and elliptical, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

Gymnostachyum magnum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
