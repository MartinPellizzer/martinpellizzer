Gonostemon glanduliflorus

Gonostemon glanduliflorus, commonly known as the glandular-flowered gonostemon, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are often covered in small, glandular structures. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
