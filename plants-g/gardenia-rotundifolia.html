Gardenia rotundifolia

Gardenia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, round-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, white to yellowish flowers. The plant is typically evergreen and thrives in humid, tropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in horticulture for its showy blooms.

The fruits of Gardenia rotundifolia are small, round, and contain a single seed.
