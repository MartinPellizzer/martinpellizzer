Genista macrotropis

Genista macrotropis, commonly known as the broad-leaved broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or scrubland habitats.

Genista macrotropis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
