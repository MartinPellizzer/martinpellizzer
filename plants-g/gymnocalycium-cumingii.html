Gymnocalycium cumingii

Gymnocalycium cumingii, commonly known as the Cuming's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its globular, ribbed stems that are typically green or blue-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the growing season, which are often followed by red, spherical fruits. Gymnocalycium cumingii is a succulent, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems to survive in arid environments.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
