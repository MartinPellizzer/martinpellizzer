Gyptis pinnatifida

Gyptis pinnatifida, commonly known as the slenderleaf gyptis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Gyptis pinnatifida is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, showing some adaptability to different environmental conditions.
