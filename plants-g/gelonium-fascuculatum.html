Gelonium fascuculatum

Gelonium fascuculatum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gelonium fascuculatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
