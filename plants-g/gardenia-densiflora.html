Gardenia densiflora

Gardenia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain hard, black seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia densiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
