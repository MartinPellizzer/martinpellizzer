Genista argentea

Genista argentea, commonly known as silver broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring slender, often thorny stems. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in dense, cylindrical clusters during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and covered in a silvery-white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Genista argentea is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in Mediterranean climates.
