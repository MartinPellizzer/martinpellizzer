Genista Ramosissima

Genista ramosissima is a hardy shrub native to the Mediterranean region known for its dense, sprawling growth and vibrant yellow flowers. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal and rock garden settings. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it a valuable addition to xeriscaping and erosion control efforts. The plant's feathery leaves and clusters of bright blooms add both texture and color to the landscape. Despite its tough nature, Genista ramosissima is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal water and care once established.
