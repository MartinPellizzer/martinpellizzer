Genista nyssana

Genista nyssana, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features slender, often branching stems that are covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in dense, terminal clusters during the spring. It is adapted to dry, sunny environments and has a deep root system for water absorption.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional herbal medicine.
