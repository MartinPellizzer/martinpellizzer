Gerbera lyrata

Gerbera lyrata, commonly known as the singing gerbera, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant features upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Gerbera lyrata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
