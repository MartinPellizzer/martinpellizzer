Geonoma macrostachys var. poiteauana

Geonoma macrostachys var. poiteauana, commonly known as the Poiteau's bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnate leaves that are often dark green and glossy.
