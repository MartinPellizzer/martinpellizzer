Geranium triste

Geranium triste, commonly known as the sad geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that are hairy and often branched, supporting clusters of small, five-petaled flowers. The flowers are typically pink or red, with a distinctive dark red or purple central spot. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

Geranium triste is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
