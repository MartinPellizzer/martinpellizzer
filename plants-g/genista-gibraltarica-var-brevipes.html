Genista gibraltarica var. brevipes

Genista gibraltarica var. brevipes, commonly known as the Gibraltar dwarf broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the rocky landscapes of Gibraltar.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves that are adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat. It is a hardy species that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
