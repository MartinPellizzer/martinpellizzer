Grevillea greyi

Grevillea greyi, commonly known as the Grey's Grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea greyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
