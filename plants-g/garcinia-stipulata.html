Garcinia stipulata

Garcinia stipulata, commonly known as the red mangostin or mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The plant produces a distinctive fruit with a bright red outer rind and a segmented, white, juicy pulp. Garcinia stipulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and the medicinal properties of its fruit.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
