Glottiphyllum uniondalense

Glottiphyllum uniondalense, commonly known as the Colombian grass.

It is a perennial grass native to South America, particularly Colombia. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
