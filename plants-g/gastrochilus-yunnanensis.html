Gastrochilus yunnanensis

Gastrochilus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan gastrochilus, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
