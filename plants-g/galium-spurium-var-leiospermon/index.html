Galium Spurium Var. Leiospermon

Galium spurium var. leiospermon is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. It is known for its small, clustered white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are slender and slightly hairy, giving it a unique appearance. Due to habitat loss, this variety is becoming increasingly endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
