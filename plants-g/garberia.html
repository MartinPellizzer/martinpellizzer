Garberia

Garberia, commonly known as the bluebell, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are characterized by their showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Garberia species are native to southern Africa, where they thrive in dry, sunny environments. They typically grow as annual or perennial herbs with narrow, grass-like leaves.
