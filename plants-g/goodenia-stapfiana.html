Goodenia stapfiana

Goodenia stapfiana, commonly known as the Tasmanian goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania, Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and heathlands. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
