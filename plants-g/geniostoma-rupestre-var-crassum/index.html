Geniostoma Rupestre Var. Crassum

Geniostoma rupestre var. crassum is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family known for its vibrant foliage and unique growth habits. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with ample sunlight. Its thick, leathery leaves display a rich array of colors, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. While it requires careful attention to watering and soil conditions, it rewards caretakers with its beauty and resilience. This variety is particularly valued for its ability to adapt to various indoor settings when properly cared for.
