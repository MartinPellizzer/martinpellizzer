Gardenia burha

Gardenia burha, commonly known as the Burmese gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.

The fruit of Gardenia burha is a hard, woody capsule that contains seeds.
