Gentiana baoxingensis

Gentiana baoxingensis, commonly known as the Baoxing gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with a fine layer of hair.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
