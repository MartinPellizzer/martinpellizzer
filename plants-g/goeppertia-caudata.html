Goeppertia caudata

Goeppertia caudata, commonly known as the Cat's Tail Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green veins and lighter green or silver streaks. The leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
