Genista densa

Genista densa, commonly known as the dense broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring slender, often thorny stems. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista densa is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in Mediterranean climates.
