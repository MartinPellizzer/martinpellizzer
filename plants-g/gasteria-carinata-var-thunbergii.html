Gasteria carinata var. thunbergii

Gasteria carinata var. thunbergii, commonly known as the Zulu Lily or Thunberg's gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent central spines. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped inflorescences that bloom in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for dry environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and resilience in both indoor and outdoor settings.
