Gesneria mornincola

Gesneria mornincola, commonly known as the morning glory gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub with thick, fleshy leaves that are glossy and dark green. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Gesneria mornincola is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
