Gonolobus rostratus

Gonolobus rostratus, commonly known as the rostrated morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a herbaceous vine characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Gonolobus rostratus is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
