Gossia clusioides subsp. maoyana

Gossia clusioides subsp.

maoyana, commonly known as the Yunnan gossia, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly in Yunnan Province. It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form with glossy, dark green leaves.

The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments within forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
