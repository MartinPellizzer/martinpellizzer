Gunnera insignis

Gunnera insignis, commonly known as the giant rhubarb, is a striking plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped leaves that can reach up to three meters in diameter. The plant belongs to the family Polygonaceae and is known for its robust, rhizomatous growth habit. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy surface, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers in summer.
