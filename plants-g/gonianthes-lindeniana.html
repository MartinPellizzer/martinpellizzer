Gonianthes lindeniana

Gonianthes lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindena's ghost orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, fragrant flowers that resemble ghostly apparitions, hence its common name. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a single, showy inflorescence. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires specific mycorrhizal associations for nutrient uptake.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists.
