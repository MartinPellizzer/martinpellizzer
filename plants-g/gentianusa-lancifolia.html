Gentianusa lancifolia

Gentianusa lancifolia, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentianusa lancifolia is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
