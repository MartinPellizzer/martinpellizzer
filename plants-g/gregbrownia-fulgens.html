Gregbrownia fulgens

Gregbrownia fulgens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the warmer months. The plant has pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Gregbrownia fulgens is also known for its rapid growth and ability to provide dense shade, making it both visually appealing and functional in landscaping.
