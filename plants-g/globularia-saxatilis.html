Globularia saxatilis

Globularia saxatilis, commonly known as the rock globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and is often found growing on rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature.

Globularia saxatilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
