Guioa discolor

Guioa discolor, commonly known as the white guioa, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually white or pale green on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
