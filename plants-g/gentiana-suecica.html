Gentiana suecica

Gentiana suecica, commonly known as the Swedish gentian, is a perennial herb native to northern Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of fine hairs.

Gentiana suecica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
