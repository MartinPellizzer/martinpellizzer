Galium lucidum var. atlanticum

Galium lucidum var. atlanticum, commonly known as Atlantic bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial with slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a glossy appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
