Guzmania tricolor var. variegata

Guzmania tricolor var. variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Flame Plant, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

This evergreen plant features a rosette of broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with white and red variegation. The inflorescence consists of a colorful, branched flower spike that can produce small, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

The plant is popular as a houseplant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
