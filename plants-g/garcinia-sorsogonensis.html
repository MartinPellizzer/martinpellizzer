Garcinia sorsogonensis

Garcinia sorsogonensis, commonly known as the Sorsogon Valley Garcinia, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. Its fruit is notable for its high content of bioactive compounds, including hydroxycitric acid.

Garcinia sorsogonensis is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
