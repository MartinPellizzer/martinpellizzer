Geijera parviflora var. crassifolia

Geijera parviflora var. crassifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved geijera, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are broader than those of the typical variety. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for use in landscaping and restoration projects.
