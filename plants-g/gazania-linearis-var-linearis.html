Gazania linearis var. linearis

Gazania linearis var. linearis, commonly known as the linear gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red center, which bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to xeriscapes.
