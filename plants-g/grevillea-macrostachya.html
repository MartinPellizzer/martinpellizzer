Grevillea macrostachya

Grevillea macrostachya, commonly known as the broad-leaved grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea macrostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
