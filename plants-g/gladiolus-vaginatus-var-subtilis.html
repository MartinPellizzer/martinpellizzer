Gladiolus vaginatus var. subtilis

Gladiolus vaginatus var. subtilis, commonly known as the slender gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves and has a fibrous root system. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
