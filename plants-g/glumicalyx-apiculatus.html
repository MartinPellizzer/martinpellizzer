Glumicalyx apiculatus

Glumicalyx apiculatus, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, cylindrical inflorescence and small, spike-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
