Gesneria acrochordonanthe

Gesneria acrochordonanthe, commonly known as the horned gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a prominent throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria acrochordonanthe is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
