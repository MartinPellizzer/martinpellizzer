Graphorkis concolor var. alphabetica

Graphorkis concolor var. alphabetica, commonly known as the Alphabetica Orchid, is a rare and unique variant of the Graphorkis genus.

It is characterized by its striking, alphabet-shaped petals that give it its distinctive name. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful blooms that appear during the wet season.
