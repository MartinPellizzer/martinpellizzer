Glossarion rhodanthum var. bracteatum

Glossarion rhodanthum var. bracteatum, commonly known as the bracteate glossarion, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of South America.

It features fleshy, spiny stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, vibrant red flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to small, scale-like structures, which help minimize water loss.

This variant is prized for its unique appearance and adaptability to harsh environments.
