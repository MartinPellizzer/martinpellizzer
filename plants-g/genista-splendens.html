Genista splendens

Genista splendens, commonly known as the golden broom, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
