Gentiana scabra subvar. angustifolia

Gentiana scabra subvar. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy surface.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
