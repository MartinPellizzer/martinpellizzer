Genista germanica f. humilis

Genista germanica f. humilis, commonly known as the low-growing Spanish broom, is a compact shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has wiry, thorny stems and needle-like leaves, typical of the Genista genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
