Gymnocline chiliophylla

Gymnocline chiliophylla, commonly known as the fiery leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a vibrant red to orange hue that intensifies in sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, slender fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The botanical features of Gymnocline chiliophylla make it a striking addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
