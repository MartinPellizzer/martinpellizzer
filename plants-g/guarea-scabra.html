Guarea scabra

Guarea scabra, commonly known as the rough guarea, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and satinwood species. The plant is characterized by its rough, bark-like texture and dark green, opposite leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody, spiny fruits.
