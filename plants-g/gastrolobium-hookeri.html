Gastrolobium hookeri

Gastrolobium hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's wattles, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

The plant produces yellow or orange pea-like flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are phyllodes, which are flattened leaf stalks that perform the function of typical leaves.
