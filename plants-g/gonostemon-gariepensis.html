Gonostemon gariepensis

Gonostemon gariepensis, commonly known as the Karoo starwort, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers and succulent leaves, which help it survive in arid conditions. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in the Karoo region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Gonostemon gariepensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
