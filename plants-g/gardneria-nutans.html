Gardneria nutans

Gardneria nutans, commonly known as the hanging garden hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces fruit that resembles a capsule. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Gardneria nutans prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
