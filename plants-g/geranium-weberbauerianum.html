Geranium weberbauerianum

Geranium weberbauerianum, commonly known as the Weberbauer geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in countries such as Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium weberbauerianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
