Gloxinia antirrhina

Gloxinia antirrhina, commonly known as the velvet leaf gloxinia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Gloxinia antirrhina is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
