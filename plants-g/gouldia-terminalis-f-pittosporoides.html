Gouldia terminalis f. pittosporoides

Gouldia terminalis f. pittosporoides, commonly known as the Pittosporum-type Gouldia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of both Gouldia terminalis and Pittosporum species.

It is a shrub native to Australia, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.

The foliage is dark green, and the flowers can range in color from white to pink, adding visual interest throughout the year.
