Gnaphalium pilosellum

Gnaphalium pilosellum, commonly known as silky cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant is recognized for its soft, silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Gnaphalium pilosellum produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.
