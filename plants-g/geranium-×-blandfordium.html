Geranium × blandfordium

Geranium × blandfordium, commonly known as the Blandford geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
