Gastrochilus garwalicus

Gastrochilus garwalicus, commonly known as the Garwal orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically blooms during the spring and early summer, and its flowers are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
