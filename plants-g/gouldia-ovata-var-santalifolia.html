Gouldia ovata var. santalifolia

Gouldia ovata var. santalifolia, commonly known as the Santalum-like Gouldia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and aromatic leaves. The plant has a woody stem and is often found in coastal regions with sandy soils.
