Gmelina siamica

Gmelina siamica, commonly known as Siam rubberwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Oleaceae and is characterized by its rapid growth and high-quality timber. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in panicles.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
