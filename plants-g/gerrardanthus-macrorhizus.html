Gerrardanthus macrorhizus

Gerrardanthus macrorhizus, commonly known as the large-rooted gerardia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its robust, fleshy roots and upright, branched stems. The plant produces showy, daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Gerrardanthus macrorhizus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
