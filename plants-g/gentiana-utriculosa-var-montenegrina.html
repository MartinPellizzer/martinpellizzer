Gentiana utriculosa var. montenegrina

Gentiana utriculosa var. montenegrina, commonly known as the Montenegro gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
