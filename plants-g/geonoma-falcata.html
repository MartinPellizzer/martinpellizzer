Geonoma falcata

Geonoma falcata, commonly known as the sickle fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, sickle-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, clustered spore sacs on the underside of its fronds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
