Gymnosphaera vexans

Gymnosphaera vexans, commonly known as the devil's pine, is a unique gymnosperm with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its cone-like structures that resemble pine cones but are structurally different. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves that are tightly packed around its central stem. Its reproductive structures are specialized, with seeds enclosed in protective cones rather than fruits.

This species is adapted to harsh environments, showcasing resilience and unique evolutionary traits.
