Gentiana graebneriana

Gentiana graebneriana, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high altitudes. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, spurred flowers that bloom in late summer.

Gentiana graebneriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
