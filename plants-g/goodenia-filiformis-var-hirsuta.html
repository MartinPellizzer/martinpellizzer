Goodenia filiformis var. hirsuta

Goodenia filiformis var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or arid regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
