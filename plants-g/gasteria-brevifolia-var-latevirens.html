Gasteria brevifolia var. latevirens

Gasteria brevifolia var. latevirens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white to yellow marginal markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
