Guatteria pannosa

Guatteria pannosa, commonly known as the panose guatteria, is a tropical evergreen shrub belonging to the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America, where it thrives in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features simple, ovate leaves with a smooth, waxy surface and a distinctive fragrant aroma. Guatteria pannosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
