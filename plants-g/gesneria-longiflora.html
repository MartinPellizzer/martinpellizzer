Gesneria longiflora

Gesneria longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. It typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments.

Gesneria longiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
