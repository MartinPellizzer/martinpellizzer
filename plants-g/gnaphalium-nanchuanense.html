Gnaphalium nanchuanense

Gnaphalium nanchuanense, commonly known as the Nanchuan feather grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fluffy flowers that produce copious amounts of fine, white to pale yellow pollen. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and covered with fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its native environment.

Gnaphalium nanchuanense is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate, feathery foliage.
