Grevillea tripartita subsp. tripartita

Grevillea tripartita subsp.

tripartita, commonly known as the Three-lobed Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features striking, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange, often with a distinctive yellow throat.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its unique floral structure and hardy nature contribute to its appeal in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
