Gesneria purpurascens

Gesneria purpurascens, commonly known as the Purple-leaf Gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and green variegated leaves, which give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid conditions. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, adding to its visual appeal.

Gesneria purpurascens thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
