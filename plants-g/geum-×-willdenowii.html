Geum × willdenowii

Geum × willdenowii, commonly known as the willdenow's avens, is a hybrid plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal leaves that are palmately lobed and its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America.

Geum × willdenowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
