Gnetum nodiflorum

Gnetum nodiflorum, commonly known as the nodiflorous gnetum, is a tropical evergreen plant belonging to the family Gnetaceae.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring compound leaves with a distinctive nodular arrangement of flowers. The plant exhibits a mix of gymnosperm-like and angiosperm-like traits, making it a fascinating subject in evolutionary botany. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically clustered in axillary or terminal inflorescences.

Gnetum nodiflorum is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
