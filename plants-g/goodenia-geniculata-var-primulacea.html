Goodenia geniculata var. primulacea

Goodenia geniculata var. primulacea, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale blue, with five petals that are reflexed at the tips.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
