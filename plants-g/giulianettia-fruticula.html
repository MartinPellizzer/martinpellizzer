Giulianettia fruticula

Giulianettia fruticula, commonly known as the goldenweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

Giulianettia fruticula is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
