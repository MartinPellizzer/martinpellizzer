Gymnosporia acuminata var. lepidota

Gymnosporia acuminata var. lepidota, commonly known as the silverleaf gymnosporia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by woody, spiny fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
