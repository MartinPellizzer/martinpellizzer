Gesneria rutila var. atrosanguinea

Gesneria rutila var. atrosanguinea, commonly known as the black-veined gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves with prominent, dark red to black veins. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange and yellow, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its unique foliage and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
