Geranium × incisum

Geranium × incisum, commonly known as the cut-leaf geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive lobed or cut leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

Geranium × incisum thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.
