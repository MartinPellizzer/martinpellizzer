Gasteria barbae

Gasteria barbae, commonly known as the Natal carpet plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white or grayish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Gasteria barbae is prized for its compact size and ornamental appeal in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
