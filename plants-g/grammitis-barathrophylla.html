Grammitis barathrophylla

Grammitis barathrophylla, commonly known as the rusty-veined fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Grammitis barathrophylla prefers humid environments and is often found in shaded, moist forest understories.
