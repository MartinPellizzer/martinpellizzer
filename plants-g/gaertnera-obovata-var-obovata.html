Gaertnera obovata var. obovata

Gaertnera obovata var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed gaertnera, is a species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage with oval, leathery leaves that are broad and ovoid in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the summer months, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits.
