Grammitis universa

Grammitis universa, commonly known as the common fernlet, is a small, evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understoreys and rock crevices. Grammitis universa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological systems.
