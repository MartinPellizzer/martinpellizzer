Gentiana berezovcaeana

Gentiana berezovcaeana, commonly known as the Berezovca gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its showy, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowers bloom in late summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitats.
