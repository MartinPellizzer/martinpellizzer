Guazuma utilis

Guazuma utilis, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of South America.

Guazuma utilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
