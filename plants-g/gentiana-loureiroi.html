Gentiana loureiroi

Gentiana loureiroi, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

The genus Gentiana is known for its showy, often blue or purple flowers, and G. loureiroi is no exception.
