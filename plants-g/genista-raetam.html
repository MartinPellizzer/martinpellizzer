Genista raetam

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, often in alpine or subalpine environments. Its growth habit is shrub-like, with multiple branches and dense foliage.
