Geum sikkimense

Geum sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Himalayan regions of India, Nepal, and Bhutan.

It features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
