Genista armata

Genista armata, commonly known as the armed genista, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and northern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The species is characterized by its spiny stems and is often used in landscaping for its hardy nature and ornamental value.
