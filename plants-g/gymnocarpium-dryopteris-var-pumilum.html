Gymnocarpium dryopteris var. pumilum

Gymnocarpium dryopteris var. pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf male fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

This variety is distinguished by its smaller size compared to other members of the Gymnocarpium genus.
