Gardenia ewartii

Gardenia ewartii, commonly known as the Ewart's gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia ewartii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
