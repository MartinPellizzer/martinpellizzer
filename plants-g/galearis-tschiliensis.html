Galearis tschiliensis

Galearis tschiliensis, commonly known as the Chilean bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Galearis tschiliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
