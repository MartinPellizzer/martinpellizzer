Geranium mooreanum

Geranium mooreanum, commonly known as the Moore's geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or purple blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Geranium mooreanum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
