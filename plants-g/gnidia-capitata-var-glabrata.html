Gnidia capitata var. glabrata

Gnidia capitata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-headed gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical heads. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
