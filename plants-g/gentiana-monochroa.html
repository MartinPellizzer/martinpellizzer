Gentiana monochroa

Gentiana monochroa, commonly known as the single-colored gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which are typically tubular and arranged in a spike. The plant prefers cool, moist habitats such as meadows and forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating.

Gentiana monochroa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
