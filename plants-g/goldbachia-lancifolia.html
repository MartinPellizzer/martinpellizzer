Goldbachia lancifolia

Goldbachia lancifolia, commonly known as the lancifoliate goldbachia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and a woody stem structure.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid climates.
