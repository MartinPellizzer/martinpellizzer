Geranium pusillum

Geranium pusillum, commonly known as the dwarf geranium, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, palmate leaves with a hairy texture and produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink or white. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few centimeters, making it well-suited for rock gardens or containers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Geranium pusillum is valued for its ornamental appeal and easy care requirements.
