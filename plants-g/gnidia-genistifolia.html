Gnidia genistifolia

Gnidia genistifolia, commonly known as the yellow starburry, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are pinnate, with small, oblong leaflets, and the stems are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant is characterized by its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
