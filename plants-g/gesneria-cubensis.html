Gesneria cubensis

Gesneria cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Cuba.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually elliptical with a smooth texture, and the flowers come in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Gesneria cubensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
