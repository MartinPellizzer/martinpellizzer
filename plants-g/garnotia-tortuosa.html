Garnotia tortuosa

Garnotia tortuosa, commonly known as the twisted garnet, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Garnotia tortuosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
