Gladiolus nyasicus

Gladiolus nyasicus, commonly known as the African gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to eastern and southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.
