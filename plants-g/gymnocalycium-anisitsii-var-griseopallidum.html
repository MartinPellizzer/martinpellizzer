Gymnocalycium anisitsii var. griseopallidum

Gymnocalycium anisitsii var. griseopallidum, commonly known as the pale gray moon cactus, is a succulent cactus native to Argentina.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive pale gray to bluish-green coloration. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a compact growth habit.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
