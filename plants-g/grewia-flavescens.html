Grewia flavescens

Grewia flavescens, commonly known as the yellow globemallow, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Grewia flavescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
