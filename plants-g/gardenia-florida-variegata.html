Gardenia florida variegata

Gardenia florida variegata, commonly known as the variegated gardenia, is a cultivar of the gardenia plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or yellow variegation patterns. The plant produces fragrant, waxy white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

Due to its unique foliage and attractive blooms, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
