Gardneria ovata

Gardneria ovata, commonly known as the white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardneria ovata is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes other hibiscus species.
