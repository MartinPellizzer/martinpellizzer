Garcinia buchananii

Garcinia buchananii, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and yellowish bark. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. Garcinia buchananii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental gardening and reforestation projects.
