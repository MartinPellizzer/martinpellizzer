Gomidesia anacardiifolia var. opaca

Gomidesia anacardiifolia var. opaca, commonly known as the opaca varietal of the genus Gomidesia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often leathery in texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, followed by small, round fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
