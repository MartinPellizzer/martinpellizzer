Gonancylis thyrsoidea

Gonancylis thyrsoidea, commonly known as the thyrsoid dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. The plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in dry, sandy soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
