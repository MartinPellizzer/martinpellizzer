Guioa dictyophylla

Guioa dictyophylla, commonly known as the Philippine guioa, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are arranged in clusters, while the fruit is a small, woody capsule.

Guioa dictyophylla is valued for its ornamental value and is often planted in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
