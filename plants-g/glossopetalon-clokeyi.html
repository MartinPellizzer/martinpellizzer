Glossopetalon clokeyi

Glossopetalon clokeyi, commonly known as the Christmas Island palm, is a unique flowering plant native to Christmas Island in the Indian Ocean.

It is a small, tree-like plant with a single trunk and a crown of feathery leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. Its roots are adapted to the nutrient-poor soils of its habitat, and it plays an important ecological role in the island's ecosystem.
