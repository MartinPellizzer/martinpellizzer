Gesneria onychocalyx

Gesneria onychocalyx, commonly known as the black-tipped gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with red veins. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria onychocalyx is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
