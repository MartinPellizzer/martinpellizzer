Grevillea beadleana

Grevillea beadleana, commonly known as the Beadleana grevillea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features striking, recurved red or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Grevillea beadleana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
