Glechon

Glechon, commonly known as the "golden groundsel," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy yellow flowers and is native to various regions in Europe, Asia, and Africa. Glechon is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
