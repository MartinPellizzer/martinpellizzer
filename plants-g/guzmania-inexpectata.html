Guzmania inexpectata

Guzmania inexpectata, commonly known as the unexpected guzmania, is a striking member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can range in color from red to orange. The leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, forming a central cup that collects rainwater.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
