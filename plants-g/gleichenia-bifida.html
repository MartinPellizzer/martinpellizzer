Gleichenia bifida

Gleichenia bifida, commonly known as the split spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, divided fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a soft, grass-like texture.

Gleichenia bifida is a perennial plant that spreads by rhizomes, forming dense mats in its natural habitat.
