Gleichenia peltophora

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are often covered in a layer of tiny, scale-like structures. Its sporangia are clustered in small, rounded structures called sori, which are typically located on the underside of the fronds.

Gleichenia peltophora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
