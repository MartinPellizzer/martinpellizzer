Gardenia anisophylla var. macroptera

Gardenia anisophylla var. macroptera, commonly known as the large-leaf gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are broad and elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, white to yellowish flowers with prominent yellow stamens. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
