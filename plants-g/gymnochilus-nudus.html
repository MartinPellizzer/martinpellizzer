Gymnochilus nudus

Gymnochilus nudus, commonly known as the naked lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elongated leaves and a single, showy flower with a distinctive, naked lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a contrasting lip that is smooth and not hairy.

Gymnochilus nudus is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
