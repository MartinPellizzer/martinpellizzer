Grevillea ericifolia var. muelleri

Grevillea ericifolia var. muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are densely arranged along the stems. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in cylindrical clusters, which bloom in spring. It is adapted to sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
