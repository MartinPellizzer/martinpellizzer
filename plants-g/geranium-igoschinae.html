Geranium igoschinae

Geranium igoschinae, commonly known as the Igoschina geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or purple blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Geranium igoschinae thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.
