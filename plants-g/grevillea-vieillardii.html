Grevillea vieillardii

Grevillea vieillardii, commonly known as the Sturt's Desert Rose, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, crimson or orange-red flower spikes that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to arid environments, making it drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Grevillea vieillardii is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and hardy nature.
