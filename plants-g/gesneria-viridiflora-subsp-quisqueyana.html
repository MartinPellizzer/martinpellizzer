Gesneria viridiflora subsp. quisqueyana

Gesneria viridiflora subsp.

quisqueyana, commonly known as the Ecuadorian gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a waxy coating, and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white and yellow.

The plant thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it suitable for humid tropical or subtropical gardens. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor settings.

Its flowering period typically occurs during the warmer months, adding visual interest throughout the year.
