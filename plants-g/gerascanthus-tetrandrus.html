Gerascanthus tetrandrus

Gerascanthus tetrandrus, commonly known as the four-thread geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers typically bloom in shades of pink or red.

Gerascanthus tetrandrus thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
