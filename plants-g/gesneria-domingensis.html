Gesneria domingensis

Gesneria domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Caribbean, particularly Dominica.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, strap-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the warmer months.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and vibrant blooms.
