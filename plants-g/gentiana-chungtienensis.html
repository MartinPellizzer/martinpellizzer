Gentiana chungtienensis

Gentiana chungtienensis, commonly known as the Chungtien gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem, while its flowers are tubular with five lobes.

Gentiana chungtienensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological value.
