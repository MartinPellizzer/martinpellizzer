Galium Meliodorum

Galium meliodorum is a unique flowering plant known for its delicate appearance and subtle fragrance. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is commonly found in moist, shaded environments. This species is often referred to as the sweet woodruff due to its sweet scent, which becomes more pronounced as it matures. Its small white flowers bloom in clusters, adding a touch of elegance to its foliage. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant in the botanical world.
