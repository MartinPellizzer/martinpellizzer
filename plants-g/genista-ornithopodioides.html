Genista ornithopodioides

Genista ornithopodioides, commonly known as the silverleaf broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine layer of white, waxy hair, giving the plant its distinctive silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
