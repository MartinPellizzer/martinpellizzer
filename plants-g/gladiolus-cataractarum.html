Gladiolus cataractarum

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, supporting large, showy flowers that come in a range of vibrant colors. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or streamside habitats. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.
