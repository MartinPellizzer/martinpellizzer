Galeottiella nutantiflora

Galeottiella nutantiflora, commonly known as the hanging-flower plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

This plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in containers.
