Guenthera Repanda Subsp. Almeriensis

Almeria bellflower, scientifically known as Guenthera repanda subsp. almeriensis, is a rare and striking plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant is characterized by its vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in the spring. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
