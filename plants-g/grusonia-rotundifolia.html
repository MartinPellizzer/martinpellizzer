Grusonia rotundifolia

Grusonia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf grusonia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats due to its drought-resistant properties.

Grusonia rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
