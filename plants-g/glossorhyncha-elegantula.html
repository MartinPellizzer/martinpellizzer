Glossorhyncha elegantula

Glossorhyncha elegantula, commonly known as the elegant leaf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
