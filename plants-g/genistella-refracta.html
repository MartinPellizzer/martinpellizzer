Genistella refracta

Genistella refracta, commonly known as the refracta broom.

It is a flowering plant in the Fabaceae family, characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to parts of southern Africa. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.
