Gloxinia floribunda

Gloxinia floribunda, commonly known as the silverleaf gloxinia, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its tuberous roots allow it to store nutrients and water, making it resilient in varying conditions.

Gloxinia floribunda is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and vibrant blooms.
