Gentiana liangshanensis

Gentiana liangshanensis, commonly known as the Liangshan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes, often in forested areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of down, while its stems are erect and often branched.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also of interest in botanical research due to its unique adaptations to its high-altitude habitat.
