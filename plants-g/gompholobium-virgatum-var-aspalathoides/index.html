Gompholobium Virgatum Var. Aspalathoides

Gompholobium virgatum var. aspalathoides is a striking member of the Fabaceae family known for its vibrant yellow flowers. This plant is native to Australia and thrives in coastal and sandy environments. Its unique leaf structure and bright blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike. The variety aspalathoides is particularly noted for its resemblance to the well-known aspalathus plant. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and stabilizing coastal soils.
