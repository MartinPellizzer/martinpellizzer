Galipea longiflora

Galipea longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered galipea, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
