Garcinia pallida

Garcinia pallida, commonly known as the white Garcinia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pale green to white flowers and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits that contain a single seed, making it a potential source of natural compounds. Its botanical features make it of interest in both horticultural and pharmacological research.

Garcinia pallida thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
