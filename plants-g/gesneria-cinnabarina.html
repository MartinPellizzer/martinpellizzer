Gesneria cinnabarina

Gesneria cinnabarina, commonly known as the red-flowered gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria cinnabarina is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
