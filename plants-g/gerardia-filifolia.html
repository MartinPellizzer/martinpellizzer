Gerardia filifolia

Gerardia filifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, and it blooms in the spring.

Gerardia filifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
