Galega caribaea

Galega caribaea, commonly known as the Caribbean goat's bean, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Galega caribaea is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil makes it an important plant in ecological restoration efforts.
