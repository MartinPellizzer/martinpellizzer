Gelonium oxyphyllum

Gelonium oxyphyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its silvery-green leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value and ease of care have contributed to its widespread cultivation in temperate regions.
