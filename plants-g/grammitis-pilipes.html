Grammitis pilipes

Grammitis pilipes, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green and covered with a thin layer of trichomes. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Grammitis pilipes has a unique reproductive structure, with spores produced on the underside of its fronds in specialized sporangia.

Its botanical features make it a popular specimen for both horticultural and scientific study.
