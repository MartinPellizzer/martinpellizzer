Gardenia kabaenensis

Gardenia kabaenensis, commonly known as the Kabaen Island gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of Kabaena in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively resistant to pests and diseases.

Gardenia kabaenensis is a rare species, making it a sought-after plant in botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
