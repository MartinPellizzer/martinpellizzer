Gladiolus tristis var. ruber

Gladiolus tristis var. ruber, commonly known as the red-flowered gladiolus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with a fibrous root system. Its flowers are arranged in spikes and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and elegance.
