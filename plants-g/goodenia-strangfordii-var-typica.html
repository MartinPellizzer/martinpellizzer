Goodenia strangfordii var. typica

Goodenia strangfordii var. typica, commonly known as the Strangford goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spurred flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in full sun and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
