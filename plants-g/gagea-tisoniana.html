Gagea tisoniana

Gagea tisoniana, commonly known as the Tison's bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots and narrow, grass-like leaves.
