Gymnocalycium × momo

Gymnocalycium × momo, commonly known as the hybrid moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant features a colorful, fleshy crown that can range from yellow to red, while its base is typically green. Gymnocalycium × momo thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to maintain its vibrant appearance.

It is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
