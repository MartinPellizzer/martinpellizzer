Gnidia harveyana

Gnidia harveyana, commonly known as the Natal star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a central disc. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers bloom in late summer.

Gnidia harveyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to coastal and sandy soils.
