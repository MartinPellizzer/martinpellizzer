Gentiana yokusai

Gentiana yokusai, commonly known as the Japanese gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the gentian family, Gentianaceae, and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest floors. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentiana yokusai is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional use in herbal medicine.
