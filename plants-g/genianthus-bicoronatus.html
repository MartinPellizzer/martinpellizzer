Genianthus bicoronatus

Genianthus bicoronatus, commonly known as the double-crown genianthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive double corona, giving it its common name. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
