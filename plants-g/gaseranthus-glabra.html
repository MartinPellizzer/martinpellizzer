Gaseranthus glabra

Gaseranthus glabra, commonly known as the smooth gaseranthus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, glabrous leaves and stems, which give it its scientific name. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Gaseranthus glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
