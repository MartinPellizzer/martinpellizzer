Glossorhyncha hunsteiniana

Glossorhyncha hunsteiniana, commonly known as the narrow-leafed starling orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating that helps retain moisture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
