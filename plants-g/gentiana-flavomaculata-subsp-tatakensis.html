Gentiana flavomaculata subsp. tatakensis

Gentiana flavomaculata subsp.

tatakensis, commonly known as the Tatakensis gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its yellow spots on the petals, which are more prominent than in other members of the species. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

The plant is notable for its medicinal properties and is valued in traditional herbal remedies.
