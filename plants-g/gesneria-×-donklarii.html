Gesneria × donklarii

Gesneria × donklarii, commonly known as the hybrid gesneria, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Gesneria arboricola and Gesneria rigida.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria × donklarii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
