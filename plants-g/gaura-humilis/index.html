Gaura Humilis

Gaura humilis is a delicate flowering plant known for its soft pink to white blooms that sway gracefully in the breeze. Native to the southern United States, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. This low-maintenance plant is a favorite among gardeners for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its unique, fern-like foliage adds texture and interest to garden landscapes throughout the growing season. With its charming appearance and ecological benefits, Gaura humilis is a valuable addition to any native plant garden.
