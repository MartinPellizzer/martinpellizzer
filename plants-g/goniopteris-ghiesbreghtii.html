Goniopteris ghiesbreghtii

Goniopteris ghiesbreghtii, commonly known as the Ghiesbreght's water fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The fronds are pinnately divided, with small, linear pinnae that give the plant a lacy appearance. It thrives in freshwater habitats such as ponds and slow-moving streams, where it contributes to water oxygenation.

This plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, showcasing its adaptability to humid environments.
