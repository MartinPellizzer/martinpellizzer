Grewia florida

Grewia florida, commonly known as the flame flower, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red, resembling flames. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture, adding to its distinctive appearance.
