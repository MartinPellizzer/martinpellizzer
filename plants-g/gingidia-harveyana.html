Gingidia harveyana

Gingidia harveyana, commonly known as the Harvey's ginger, is a flowering plant native to the humid regions of eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers, and is characterized by its ornamental foliage and fragrant flowers. Gingidia harveyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual and olfactory characteristics.
