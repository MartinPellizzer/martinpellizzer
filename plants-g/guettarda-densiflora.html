Guettarda densiflora

Guettarda densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered guettarda, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Guettarda densiflora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
