Goodenia concinna

Goodenia concinna, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the spring and summer months.

Goodenia concinna is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
