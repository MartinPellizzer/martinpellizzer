Gymnopetalum cochinchinense var. incisa

Gymnopetalum cochinchinense var. incisa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a loose cluster. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and ecological preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
