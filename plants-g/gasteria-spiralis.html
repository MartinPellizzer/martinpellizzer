Gasteria spiralis

Gasteria spiralis, commonly known as the spiral gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that spiral upward, creating a unique and compact appearance. The leaves are thick, fleshy, and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
