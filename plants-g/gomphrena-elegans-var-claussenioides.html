Gomphrena elegans var. claussenioides

Gomphrena elegans var. claussenioides, commonly known as the Claussenioides gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spiky bracts that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by vibrant, papery leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is primarily due to its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
