Gesneria pedicellaris

Gesneria pedicellaris, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of orange and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria pedicellaris is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
