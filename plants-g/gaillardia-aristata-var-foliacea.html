Gaillardia aristata var. foliacea

Gaillardia aristata var. foliacea, commonly known as the foliage gaillardia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the central and eastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange daisy-like flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
