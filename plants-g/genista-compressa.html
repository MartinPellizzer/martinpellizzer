Genista compressa

Genista compressa, commonly known as the creeping broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, spreading stems that often lie close to the ground, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and have a distinctive compressed appearance.

Genista compressa is adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy plant in arid environments.
