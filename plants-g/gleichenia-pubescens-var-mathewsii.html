Gleichenia pubescens var. mathewsii

Gleichenia pubescens var. mathewsii, commonly known as the Mathews' Gleichenia, is a fern species native to specific regions of Australia.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The fronds are typically narrow and elongated, with a distinctive pubescent (hairy) texture on the underside. This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rock crevices and forest floors.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studies on fern morphology and adaptation to specific ecological niches.
