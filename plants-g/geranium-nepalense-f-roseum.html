Geranium nepalense f. roseum

Geranium nepalense f. roseum, commonly known as the Nepal geranium or roseum geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces pink to rose-colored blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a suitable ornamental plant for gardens.

Its compact size and vibrant flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
