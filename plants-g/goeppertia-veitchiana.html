Goeppertia veitchiana

Goeppertia veitchiana, commonly known as the Veitch's calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, patterned foliage that changes color with light exposure. Goeppertia veitchiana is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
