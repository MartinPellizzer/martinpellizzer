Gasteria parvifolia

Gasteria parvifolia, commonly known as the dwarf gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Gasteria parvifolia is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and striking appearance.
