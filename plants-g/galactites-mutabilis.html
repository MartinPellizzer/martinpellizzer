Galactites mutabilis

Galactites mutabilis, commonly known as the variable milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on environmental conditions. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweeds known for their milky sap.
