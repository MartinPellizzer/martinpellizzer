Geranium acetosum

Geranium acetosum, commonly known as the sour geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is also known for its aromatic leaves, which emit a sharp, citrus-like scent when crushed.
