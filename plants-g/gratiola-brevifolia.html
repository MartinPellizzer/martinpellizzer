Gratiola brevifolia

Gratiola brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved yellowroot, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

Gratiola brevifolia is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
