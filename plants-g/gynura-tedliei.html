Gynura tedliei

Gynura tedliei, commonly known as the velvetleaf or gynura, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaves. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often dark green with a distinctive waxy texture. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by fluffy, white seed heads.

Gynura tedliei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
