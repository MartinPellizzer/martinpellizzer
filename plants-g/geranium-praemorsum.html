Geranium praemorsum

Geranium praemorsum, commonly known as the blue geranium, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
