Grevillea lanigera

Grevillea lanigera, commonly known as the woolly grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant red flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea lanigera is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract native wildlife.
