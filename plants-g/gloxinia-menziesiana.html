Gloxinia menziesiana

Gloxinia menziesiana, commonly known as the Menzies gloxinia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a velvety texture. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Gloxinia menziesiana requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight to flourish.
