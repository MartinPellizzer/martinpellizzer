Genista kochii

Genista kochii, commonly known as Koch's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it typically grows as a shrub or subshrub.

Genista kochii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
