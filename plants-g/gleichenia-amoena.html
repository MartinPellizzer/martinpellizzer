Gleichenia amoena

Gleichenia amoena, commonly known as the pretty fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant is native to various regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and India, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Gleichenia amoena is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded gardens.
