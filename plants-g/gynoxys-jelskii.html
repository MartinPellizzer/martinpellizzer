Gynoxys jelskii

Gynoxys jelskii, commonly known as the Mexican flameflower, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub native to Mexico and Central America, characterized by its bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. Gynoxys jelskii is also valued for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
