Geranium ocellatum var. himalaicum

Geranium ocellatum var. himalaicum, commonly known as the Himalayan geranium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and it produces small, clustered blooms during the summer months.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
