Guzmania × barbiei

Guzmania × barbiei, commonly known as the Barbie Peace Lily, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Guzmania, belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a striking epiphytic plant native to South America, characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that resembles a flower. The plant features a rosette of narrow, waxy leaves that collect rainwater, forming a central pool used for nutrient absorption. Its foliage is often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, adding ornamental value to indoor spaces.

Guzmania × barbiei thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining potting media.
