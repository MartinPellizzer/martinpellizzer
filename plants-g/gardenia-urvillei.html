Gardenia urvillei

Gardenia urvillei, commonly known as the Urville gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their beauty and scent.

Gardenia urvillei prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
