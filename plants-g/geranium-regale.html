Geranium regale

Geranium regale, commonly known as the royal geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
