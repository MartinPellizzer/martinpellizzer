Gibasis speciosa

Gibasis speciosa, commonly known as the showy globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and it produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
