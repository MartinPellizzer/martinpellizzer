Gesneria dolichostyla

Gesneria dolichostyla, commonly known as the long-styled gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, elongated staminodes that give it its common name. It features broad, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture.

Gesneria dolichostyla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy, tubular flowers.
