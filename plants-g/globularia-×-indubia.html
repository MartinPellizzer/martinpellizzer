Globularia × indubia

Globularia × indubia, commonly known as the hybrid globularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, globular growth habit, forming a compact, rounded shape. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in summer. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Globularia × indubia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and resilience in coastal environments.
