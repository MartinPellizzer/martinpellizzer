Galphimia angustifolia var. ovalifolia

Galphimia angustifolia var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf galphimia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its slender, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
