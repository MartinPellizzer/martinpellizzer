Gerbera cordata

Gerbera cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in a range of colors including red, yellow, and orange. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Gerbera cordata is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, adding continuous color to landscapes.
