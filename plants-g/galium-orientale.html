Galium orientale

Galium orientale, commonly known as silverweed, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features slender, ascending stems that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Galium orientale is typically found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks.
