Genista tricuspidata var. vilosissima

Genista tricuspidata var. vilosissima, commonly known as the silky broom.

It is a shrub native to southern Europe, characterized by its dense, thorny growth and bright yellow flowers. The plant features small, trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and inland habitats.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a popular choice for landscaping in Mediterranean climates.
