Gomphostemma chinense

Gomphostemma chinense, commonly known as Chinese ginseng vine, is a climbing plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.
