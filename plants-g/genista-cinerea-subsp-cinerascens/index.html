Genista Cinerea Subsp. Cinerascens

Genista cinerea subsp. cinerascens is a rare and delicate shrub native to the rugged coastal regions of southern Europe. This subspecies is characterized by its pale green foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found along cliffs and dunes where it withstands harsh maritime conditions. Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss, it is considered an endangered plant species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical specimen for future generations.
