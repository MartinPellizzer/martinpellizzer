Gorteria diffusa subsp. diffusa

Gorteria diffusa subsp.

diffusa, commonly known as the common gorteria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features basal rosettes of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in dense clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
