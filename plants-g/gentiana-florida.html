Gentiana florida

Gentiana florida, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture.

Gentiana florida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
