Gongora ilensis

Gongora ilensis, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple and green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elliptical and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Gongora ilensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to maintain its ornamental qualities.
