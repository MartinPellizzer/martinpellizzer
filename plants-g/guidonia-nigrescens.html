Guidonia nigrescens

Guidonia nigrescens, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are borne on erect, branched stems. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae), which includes other familiar plants like tomatoes and peppers.

Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, and they bloom in clusters.
