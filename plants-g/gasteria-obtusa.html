Gasteria obtusa

Gasteria obtusa, commonly known as the Cape foxtail, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellow markings. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular white flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria obtusa is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
