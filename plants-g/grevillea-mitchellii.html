Grevillea mitchellii

Grevillea mitchellii, commonly known as Mitchell's grevillea, is a shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It features striking, feather-like leaves that are often reddish or orange in color. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea mitchellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
