Gerascanthus bicolor

Gerascanthus bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its striking bi-colored flowers, typically featuring a red or purple upper petal and a white or yellow lower petal. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
