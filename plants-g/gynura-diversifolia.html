Gynura diversifolia

Gynura diversifolia, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the variegated nettle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves that often feature white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Gynura diversifolia thrives in humid environments and prefers partial shade to full sun.
