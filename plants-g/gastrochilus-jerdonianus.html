Gastrochilus jerdonianus

Gastrochilus jerdonianus, commonly known as the Jerdon's stomach orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
