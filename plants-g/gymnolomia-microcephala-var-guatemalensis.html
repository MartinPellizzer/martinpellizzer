Gymnolomia microcephala var. guatemalensis

Gymnolomia microcephala var. guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan dwarf orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. The plant features slender, hairy stems and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing close to the ground.

This variety is valued for its unique morphology and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
