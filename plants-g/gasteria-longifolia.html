Gasteria longifolia

Gasteria longifolia, commonly known as the Long-leaf Gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped white flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves.

Gasteria longifolia is popular as a low-maintenance indoor plant due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
