Galeobdolon chinense var. subglabrum

Galeobdolon chinense var. subglabrum, commonly known as the smooth-flowered Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, typically blooming in summer. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are smooth and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
