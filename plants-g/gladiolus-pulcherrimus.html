Gladiolus pulcherrimus

Gladiolus pulcherrimus, commonly known as the beautiful gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant typically blooms in summer and prefers well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.
