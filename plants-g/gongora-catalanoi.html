Gongora catalanoi

Gongora catalanoi, commonly known as the Catalonian gongora, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as cloud forests. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture.

Gongora catalanoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
