Gnidia pubescens

Gnidia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The bark is smooth and grayish, with a slight aromatic scent when crushed.
