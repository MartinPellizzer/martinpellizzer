Glaucium flavum var. plenum

Glaucium flavum var. plenum, commonly known as the full-flowered yellow hound's tongue, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking, yellow, spurred flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique flower structure and vibrant color.
