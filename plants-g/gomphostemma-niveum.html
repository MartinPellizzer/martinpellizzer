Gomphostemma niveum

Gomphostemma niveum, commonly known as the white-stemmed gomphostemma, is a member of the Menispermaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its smooth, pale green stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.

Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, it is often found in moist, shaded environments.
