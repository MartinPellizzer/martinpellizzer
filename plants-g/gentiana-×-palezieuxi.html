Gentiana × palezieuxi

Gentiana × palezieuxi, commonly known as the pale purple gentian, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which are typically tubular and bilaterally symmetrical. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, where it thrives in moist, shaded habitats. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and often covered with a fine pubescence.

Gentiana × palezieuxi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
