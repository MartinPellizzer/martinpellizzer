Genista tridens var. intermedia

Genista tridens var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are lance-shaped and slightly hairy.

The species is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in coastal and rocky environments.
