Gardenia foliolosa

Gardenia foliolosa, commonly known as the leafy gardenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are elongated and have a distinct venation pattern. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
