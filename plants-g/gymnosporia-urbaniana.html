Gymnosporia urbaniana

Gymnosporia urbaniana, commonly known as the African mountain thorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fibrous covering. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Gymnosporia urbaniana plays a role in local ecosystems by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
