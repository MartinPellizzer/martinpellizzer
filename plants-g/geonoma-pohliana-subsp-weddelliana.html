Geonoma pohliana subsp. weddelliana

Geonoma pohliana subsp.

weddelliana, commonly known as the Weddell's geonoma, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit.

The plant features lanceolate, dark green leaves with a smooth texture and a distinctive, short petiole. It typically thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.
