Govenia

Govenia, commonly known as the pink-flowered orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Indonesia. Govenia species are characterized by their showy, often pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. They typically grow as epiphytes or terrestrials, attaching themselves to trees or growing in moist, shaded environments.

The genus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
