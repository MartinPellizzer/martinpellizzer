Gutierrezia spathulata

Gutierrezia spathulata, commonly known as narrowleaf bisbee goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

Gutierrezia spathulata is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
