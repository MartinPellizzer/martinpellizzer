Genista pauciflorum

Genista pauciflorum, commonly known as the sparse-flowered broom, is a flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, thorned stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in dry, open habitats.

Genista pauciflorum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
