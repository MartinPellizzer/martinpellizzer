Gireoudia cardiocarpa

Gireoudia cardiocarpa, commonly known as the heart-shaped fruit plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped fruits, which are typically red or orange when mature. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale yellow. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy surface.

The botanical features of Gireoudia cardiocarpa make it a unique and ornamental addition to tropical gardens.
