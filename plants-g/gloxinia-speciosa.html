Gloxinia speciosa

Gloxinia speciosa, commonly known as the showy gloxinia, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often a deep green. Gloxinia speciosa is a tuberous plant, meaning it stores nutrients in underground stems.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
