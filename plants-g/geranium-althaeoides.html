Geranium althaeoides

Geranium althaeoides, commonly known as the althaeoides geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens.

Geranium althaeoides is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
