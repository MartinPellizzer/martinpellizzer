Galactites elegans

Galactites elegans, commonly known as the elegant milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in umbels. Its latex sap is milky and can be toxic, containing cardiac glycosides.
