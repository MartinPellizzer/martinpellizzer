Grevillea baileyana

Grevillea baileyana, commonly known as the pink and white grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features striking, feather-like foliage that ranges from pink to white in color. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea baileyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
