Gibbaeum haaglenii

Gibbaeum haaglenii, commonly known as the Cape mountain snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
