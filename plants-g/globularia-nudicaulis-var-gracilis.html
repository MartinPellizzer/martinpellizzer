Globularia nudicaulis var. gracilis

Globularia nudicaulis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender globe flower, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, globular, pale green to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, mat-forming growth habit with hairy stems and small, alternate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique, rounded flower clusters and drought tolerance.
