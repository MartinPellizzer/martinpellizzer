Grevillea shuttleworthiana subsp. shuttleworthiana

Grevillea shuttleworthiana subsp.

shuttleworthiana, commonly known as the Western River Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bottlebrush-like red or orange flowers.

The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal and riverine habitats, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
