Grammatophyllum fenzlianum var. measuresianum

Grammatophyllum fenzlianum var. measuresianum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can reach several meters in height. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, colorful flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a symbol of prosperity in some cultures.
