Gesneria haitiensis

Gesneria haitiensis, commonly known as the Haitian gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Haiti and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, often found on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical with a glossy texture, and the flowers come in shades of yellow and orange.

Gesneria haitiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
