Galium ussuriense

Galium ussuriense, commonly known as Ussuri bedstraw, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

Galium ussuriense has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often hairy and may have a slightly aromatic scent.
