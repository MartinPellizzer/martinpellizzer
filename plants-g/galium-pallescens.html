Galium pallescens

Galium pallescens, commonly known as pale stellaria, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically features slender, branching stems covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Galium pallescens thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
