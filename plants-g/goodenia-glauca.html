Goodenia glauca

Goodenia glauca, commonly known as the blue goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in heathland and woodland habitats. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia glauca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
