Geranium jaekelae

Geranium jaekelae, commonly known as the Jaekel's geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Geranium jaekelae is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
