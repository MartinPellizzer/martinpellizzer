Gymnanthemum malabaricum

Gymnanthemum malabaricum, commonly known as the Malabar starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.
