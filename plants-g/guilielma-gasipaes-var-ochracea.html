Guilielma gasipaes var. ochracea

Guilielma gasipaes var. ochracea, commonly known as the ochraceous guilielma, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
