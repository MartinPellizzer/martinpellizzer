Gladiolus triphyllus

Gladiolus triphyllus, commonly known as the three-leaf gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white, and are often used in ornamental gardening. This species is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated for both garden decoration and cut flower arrangements.
