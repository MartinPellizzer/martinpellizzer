Gardenia faucicola

Gardenia faucicola, commonly known as the Hawaiian gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and strong, sweet scent.

Gardenia faucicola is also valued for its cultural significance in Hawaiian tradition.
