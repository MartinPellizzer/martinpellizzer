Gentiana reussii

Gentiana reussii, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Gentiana reussii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and partial shade.
