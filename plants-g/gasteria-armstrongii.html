Gasteria armstrongii

Gasteria armstrongii, commonly known as the Armstrong's gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on tall stems, typically in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Gasteria armstrongii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
