Genista longipes

Genista longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed broom, is a flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and parts of southern France. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. It produces yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring.

Genista longipes is often found in dry, open habitats such as scrublands and rocky slopes.
