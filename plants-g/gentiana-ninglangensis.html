Gentiana ninglangensis

Gentiana ninglangensis, commonly known as the Ninglang gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family, known for its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in cool and moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowers are tubular with prominent blue petals.

Gentiana ninglangensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
