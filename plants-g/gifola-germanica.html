Gifola germanica

Gifola germanica, commonly known as the German bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Gifola germanica is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and gardens due to its ornamental value.
