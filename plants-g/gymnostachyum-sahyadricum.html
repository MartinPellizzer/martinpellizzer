Gymnostachyum sahyadricum

Gymnostachyum sahyadricum, commonly known as the Western Ghats gymnostachyum, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical evergreen forests. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a leathery texture and dark green coloration. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are usually white or pale pink in color.

Gymnostachyum sahyadricum plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat, contributing to the biodiversity of the region.
