Gloxinia perennis

Gloxinia perennis, commonly known as the everlasting gloxinia, is a perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect sunlight.

Gloxinia perennis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
