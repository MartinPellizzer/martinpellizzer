Gasteria vlokii

Gasteria vlokii, commonly known as the Vlok's gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or grayish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular, white flowers. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water.

Gasteria vlokii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
