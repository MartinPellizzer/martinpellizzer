Gentiana scabrida var. horaimontana

Gentiana scabrida var. horaimontana, commonly known as the Horai Mountain gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its leaves are often covered in fine, scaly hairs, giving it a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vivid floral display.
