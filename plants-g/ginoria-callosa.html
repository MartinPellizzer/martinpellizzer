Ginoria callosa

Ginoria callosa, commonly known as the hairy ginoria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
