Gentiana junussovii

Gentiana junussovii, commonly known as the Junussov's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Gentiana junussovii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and resilience.
