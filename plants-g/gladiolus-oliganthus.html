Gladiolus oliganthus

Gladiolus oliganthus, commonly known as the slender gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its botanical characteristics include a rhizomatous root system and showy inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in temperate climates.
