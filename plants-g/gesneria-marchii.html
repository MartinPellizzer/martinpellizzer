Gesneria marchii

Gesneria marchii, commonly known as the Marchii gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria marchii is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
