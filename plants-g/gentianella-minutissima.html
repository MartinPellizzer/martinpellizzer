Gentianella minutissima

Gentianella minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf gentian, is a small flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its tiny, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes. It has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in meadows and mountainous habitats.

Due to its small size and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
