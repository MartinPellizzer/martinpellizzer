Geonoma tenelliana, commonly known as the small-leaf fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are often clustered at the base. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
