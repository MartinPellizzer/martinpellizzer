Gesneria spicata var. schiedeana

Gesneria spicata var. schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiede's gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features dark green, glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery bloom. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden environments.
