Geranium thunbergii f. pallidum

Geranium thunbergii f. pallidum, commonly known as the paleleaf geranium, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Japan.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically pale green, giving it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces clusters of delicate, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and compact growth habit, it is often used in rock gardens and as a container plant.
