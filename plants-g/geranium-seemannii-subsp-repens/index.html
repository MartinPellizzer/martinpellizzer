Geranium Seemannii Subsp. Repens

Geranium seemannii subsp. repens is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is known for its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of delicate pink or purple flowers. This subspecies thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful flora.
