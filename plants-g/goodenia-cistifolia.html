Goodenia cistifolia

Goodenia cistifolia, commonly known as the woolly goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia cistifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
