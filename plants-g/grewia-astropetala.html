Grewia astropetala

Grewia astropetala, commonly known as the starfruit tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It produces small, yellow, star-shaped fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor. The plant has simple, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The flowers are typically white or pale yellow and are clustered in small, terminal inflorescences.
