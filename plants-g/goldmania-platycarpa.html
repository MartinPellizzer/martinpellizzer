Goldmania platycarpa

Goldmania platycarpa, commonly known as the broad-fruited goldmania, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in secondary forests or disturbed habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
