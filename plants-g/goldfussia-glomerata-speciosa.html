Goldfussia glomerata speciosa

Goldfussia glomerata speciosa, commonly known as the showy goldenfussia, is a striking perennial plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range from golden yellow to orange-red, depending on the variety. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem, giving it a neat and structured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring through early autumn.
