Grevillea dolichopoda

Grevillea dolichopoda, commonly known as the long-stemmed grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea dolichopoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
