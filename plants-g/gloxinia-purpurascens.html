Gloxinia purpurascens

Gloxinia purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-leaved gloxinia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple foliage. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Gloxinia purpurascens is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental leaves and vibrant blooms.
