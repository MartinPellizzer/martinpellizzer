Genista duriaei var. sanctorum

Genista duriaei var. sanctorum, commonly known as the Sanctorum Broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, needle-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in Mediterranean climates.
