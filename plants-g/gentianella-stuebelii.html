Gentianella stuebelii

Gentianella stuebelii, commonly known as Stuebel's gentian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late summer.

Gentianella stuebelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
