Garcinia alepensis

Garcinia alepensis, commonly known as the Aleppo garcinia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces bright orange, fleshy berries that are rich in bioactive compounds. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.

Garcinia alepensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties in various cultures.
