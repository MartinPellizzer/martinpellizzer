Gaillardia aestivalis var. aestivalis

Gaillardia aestivalis var. aestivalis, commonly known as the annual blanketflower, is a vibrant flowering plant native to the United States.

It features showy, daisy-like blooms with bright orange and red petals, often with a dark central disk. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is a popular choice for gardens due to its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
