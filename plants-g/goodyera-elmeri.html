Goodyera elmeri

Goodyera elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's bog orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and delicate, white to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest floors, often in association with decaying organic matter. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

Goodyera elmeri is a low-growing herb that thrives in humid, cool environments.
