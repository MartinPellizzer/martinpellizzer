Grevillea velutinella

Grevillea velutinella, commonly known as the silky grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Grevillea velutinella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native landscaping.
