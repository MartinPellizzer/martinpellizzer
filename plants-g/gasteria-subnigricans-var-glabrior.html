Gasteria subnigricans var. glabrior

Gasteria subnigricans var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-leaved gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a bluish tinge, and it produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is popular as a low-maintenance houseplant.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a favored choice for succulent enthusiasts.
