Genoplesium acuminatum

Genoplesium acuminatum, commonly known as the slender sun-orchid, is a species of orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, erect stem and a single, showy, pale yellow to greenish-white flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it produces small, dark brown seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
