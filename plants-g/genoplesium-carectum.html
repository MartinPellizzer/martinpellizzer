Genoplesium carectum

Genoplesium carectum, commonly known as the slender leek orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces a single flowering stem with several flowers.

The species is listed as vulnerable due to habitat loss and is often found in specific regions of New South Wales and Queensland.
