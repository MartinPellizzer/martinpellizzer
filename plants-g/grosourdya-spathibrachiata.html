Grosourdya spathibrachiata

Grosourdya spathibrachiata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow patterns. The plant produces striking, orchid-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Grosourdya spathibrachiata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
