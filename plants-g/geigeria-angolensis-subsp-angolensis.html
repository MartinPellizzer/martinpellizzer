Geigeria angolensis subsp.

angolensis, commonly known as the Angola geigeria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with slender, branching stems and small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.
