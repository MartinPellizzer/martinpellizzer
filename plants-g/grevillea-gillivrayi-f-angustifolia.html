Grevillea gillivrayi f. angustifolia

Grevillea gillivrayi f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking appearance.

This variety is a popular choice for coastal and arid garden settings.
