Gardenia parvifolia

Gardenia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved gardenia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are small and elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia parvifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
