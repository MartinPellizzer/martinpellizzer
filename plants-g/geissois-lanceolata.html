It is a shrub native to the southeastern United States, particularly found in Georgia and Florida. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves that are usually opposite in arrangement. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters and are followed by small, winged fruits.

Geissois lanceolata is often found in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in open woodlands.
