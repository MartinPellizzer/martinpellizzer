Gardenia sinensis

Gardenia sinensis, commonly known as the gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, waxy white flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine. Gardenia sinensis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its fruit, a bright yellow berry, contains seeds that are dispersed by animals.
