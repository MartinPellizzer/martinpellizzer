Grevillea meisneri var. rhododesmia

Grevillea meisneri var. rhododesmia, commonly known as the red-stemmed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in dense, cylindrical clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowering display and drought tolerance.
