Guettarda burchelliana var. nitens

Guettarda burchelliana var. nitens, commonly known as the shining guettarda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy, dark green surface. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
