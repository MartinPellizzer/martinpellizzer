Genista tridentata subsp. lasiantha

Genista tridentata subsp.

lasiantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, wiry stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The leaves are narrow and trifoliate, giving the plant its common name. It is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, often found in coastal areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh, sunny conditions.
