Geunsia farinosa f. serratula

Geunsia farinosa f. serratula, commonly known as the serrated farinosa, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers with dark centers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats. Its leaves are serrated, giving it a distinctive appearance, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

This variety is noted for its adaptability to harsh environments and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
