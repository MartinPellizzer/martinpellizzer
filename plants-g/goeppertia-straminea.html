Goeppertia straminea

Goeppertia straminea, commonly known as the Yellow-topped Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a distinct yellow margin. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia straminea is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
