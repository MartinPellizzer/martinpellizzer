Gesneria lopezii

Gesneria lopezii, commonly known as Lopez's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, while its flowers come in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Gesneria lopezii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
