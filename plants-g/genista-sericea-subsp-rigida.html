Genista sericea subsp. rigida

Genista sericea subsp.

rigida, commonly known as the rigid broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, rigid stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The leaves are narrow and covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
