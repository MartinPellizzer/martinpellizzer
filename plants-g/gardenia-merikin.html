Gardenia merikin

Gardenia merikin, commonly known as the Merikin gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant, waxy white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain hard, brown seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial shade to full sun.

Gardenia merikin is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in warm, tropical climates.
