Gentiana carpathica

Gentiana carpathica, commonly known as Carpathian gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Gentiana carpathica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
