Goniophlebium amoenum var. pilosum

Goniophlebium amoenum var. pilosum, commonly known as the hairy fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with numerous adventitious roots, allowing it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety appearance.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in humid, indoor settings.
