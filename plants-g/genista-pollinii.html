Genista pollinii

Genista pollinii, commonly known as the Pollini's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in pairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Genista pollinii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
