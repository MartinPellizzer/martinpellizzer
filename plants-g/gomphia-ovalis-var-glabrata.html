Gomphia ovalis var. glabrata

Gomphia ovalis var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth gomphia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically glabrous, or smooth to the touch. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
