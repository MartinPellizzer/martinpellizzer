Goodyera ramosii

Goodyera ramosii, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces delicate, spiral-like inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have three petals and three sepals.

Goodyera ramosii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
