Gentiana yakushimensis

Gentiana yakushimensis, commonly known as the Yakushimense gentian, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Gentiana yakushimensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
