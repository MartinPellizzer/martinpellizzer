Gladiolus serapiiflorus

Gladiolus serapiiflorus, commonly known as the African gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant is characterized by its robust growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for cut flowers and garden displays.
