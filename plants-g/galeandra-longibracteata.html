Galeandra longibracteata

Galeandra longibracteata, commonly known as the long-bract galeandra, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Galeandra longibracteata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
