Guioa lasiothyrsa f. elmeri

Guioa lasiothyrsa f. elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's guioa, is a variant of the guioa species native to the Philippines.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its smooth, pale bark and elliptical, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that split open to release seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and secondary growth areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
