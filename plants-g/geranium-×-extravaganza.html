Geranium × extravaganza

Geranium × extravaganza, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a popular ornamental plant prized for its vibrant flowers and foliage.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is a hybrid between Geranium phaeum and Geranium sanguineum. The plant typically features showy, five-petaled blooms in shades of red, pink, and white, which appear in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is often dark green with a slightly hairy texture, adding visual interest to gardens.

Geranium × extravaganza thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for borders and containers.
