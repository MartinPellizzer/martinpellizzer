Gentiana filipes

Gentiana filipes, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentiana filipes is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
