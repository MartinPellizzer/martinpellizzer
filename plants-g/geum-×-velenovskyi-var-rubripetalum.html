Geum × velenovskyi var. rubripetalum

Geum × velenovskyi var. rubripetalum, commonly known as the red-leaf bergenia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking red-tinged foliage, which adds vibrant color to gardens throughout the growing season. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It prefers partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
