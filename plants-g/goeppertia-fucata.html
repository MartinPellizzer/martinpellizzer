Goeppertia fucata

Goeppertia fucata, commonly known as the silver stripe calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled leaves that feature a distinctive silver stripe running along the midrib. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia fucata is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
