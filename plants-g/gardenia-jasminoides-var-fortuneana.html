Gardenia jasminoides var. fortuneana

Gardenia jasminoides var. fortuneana, commonly known as the fortune gardenia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a subtle, sweet fragrance. The plant produces large, waxy, white flowers with a prominent yellow center, which bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping and as a houseplant.
