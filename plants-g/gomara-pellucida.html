Gomara pellucida

Gomara pellucida, commonly known as the glassy gomara, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
