Geigeria acicularis

Geigeria acicularis, commonly known as the needleleaf geigeria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Geigeria acicularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
