Glosocomia lanceolata var. obtusa

Glosocomia lanceolata var. obtusa, commonly known as the broadleaf glosocomia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped, slightly broad leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
