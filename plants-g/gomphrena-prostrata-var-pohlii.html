Gomphrena prostrata var. pohlii

Gomphrena prostrata var. pohlii, commonly known as the prostrate gomphrena, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, often resembling the shape of a crown. The plant has wiry, spreading stems and hairy leaves that are typically covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.
