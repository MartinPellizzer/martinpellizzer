Geraniospermum delphinifolium

Geraniospermum delphinifolium, commonly known as the blue geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to autumn.
