Grammatotheca erinoides

Grammatotheca erinoides, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and yellow-centered white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads. Grammatotheca erinoides thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
