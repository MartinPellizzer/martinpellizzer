Gymneia

Gymneia, commonly known as the naked lady, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its lack of leaves during the flowering period. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Gymneia thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage and flowers.
