Gesneria clandestina

Gesneria clandestina, commonly known as the hidden gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually ovate and glossy, with a smooth texture.

Gesneria clandestina prefers indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
