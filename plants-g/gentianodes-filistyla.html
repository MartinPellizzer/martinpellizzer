Gentianodes filistyla

Gentianodes filistyla, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in summer.

Gentianodes filistyla is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
