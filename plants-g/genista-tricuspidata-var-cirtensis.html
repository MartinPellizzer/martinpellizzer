Genista tricuspidata var. cirtensis

Genista tricuspidata var. cirtensis, commonly known as the Cirtensis Broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features slender, often thorny stems and small, trifoliate leaves with three pointed lobes. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters and attracting pollinators such as bees.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
