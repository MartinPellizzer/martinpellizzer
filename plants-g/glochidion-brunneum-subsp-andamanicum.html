Glochidion brunneum subsp. andamanicum

Glochidion brunneum subsp.

andamanicum, commonly known as the Andaman glabrous tamarind, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Andaman Islands. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually glabrous or nearly so.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
