Gladiolus tristis var. odorus

Gladiolus tristis var. odorus, commonly known as the sweet gladiolus, is a rare and fragrant variant of the gladiolus species.

It is characterized by its striking, often pale pink or white flowers with a subtle, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are long and narrow, similar to other gladiolus varieties, and it produces tall, slender stems.

Due to its rarity and unique scent, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and among horticultural enthusiasts.
