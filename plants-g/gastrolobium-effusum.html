Gastrolobium effusum

Gastrolobium effusum, commonly known as the silky bush vetch, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets. It is adapted to dry environments and is often found in heathlands and sandy soils.

Gastrolobium effusum is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, which benefits soil fertility.
