Galium × retzii

Galium × retzii, commonly known as silverweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its prostrate or ascending stems covered in fine, silvery hairs. Galium × retzii is typically found in moist, disturbed soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance.
