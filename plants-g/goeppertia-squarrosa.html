Goeppertia squarrosa

Goeppertia squarrosa, commonly known as the Velvet Plant, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Goeppertia squarrosa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
