Glycine koordersii

Glycine koordersii, commonly known as the Koord's soybean, is a climbing legume native to Australia.

It features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, bean-like pods. The plant is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Glycine koordersii is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
