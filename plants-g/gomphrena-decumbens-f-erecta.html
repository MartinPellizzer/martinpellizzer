Gomphrena decumbens f. erecta

Gomphrena decumbens f. erecta, commonly known as the upright garden gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flower heads.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
