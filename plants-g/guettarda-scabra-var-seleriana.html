Guettarda scabra var. seleriana

Guettarda scabra var. seleriana, commonly known as the Selerian guettarda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage with elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often white or pale yellow, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal contribute to its use in both landscaping and horticultural collections.
