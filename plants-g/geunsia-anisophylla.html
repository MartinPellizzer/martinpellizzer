Geunsia anisophylla

Geunsia anisophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a distinctive blue-green coloration.

It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.
