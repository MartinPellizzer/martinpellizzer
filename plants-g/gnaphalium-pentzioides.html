Gnaphalium pentzioides

Gnaphalium pentzioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds, which contribute to its ability to colonize disturbed areas. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its evergreen foliage and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
