Gelasia Cinerea

Gelasia cinerea is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its pale blue to white petals and its ability to thrive in harsh, arid environments. This plant is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperature fluctuations. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting Gelasia cinerea is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile ecosystem.
