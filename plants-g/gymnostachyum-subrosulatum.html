Gymnostachyum subrosulatum

Gymnostachyum subrosulatum, commonly known as the pink star cactus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white or pale green spines. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or reddish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in arid gardens and collections.
