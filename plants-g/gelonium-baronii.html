Gelonium baronii

Gelonium baronii, commonly known as the baron's gelonium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Gelonium baronii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
