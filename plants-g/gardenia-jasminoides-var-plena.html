Gardenia jasminoides var. plena

Gardenia jasminoides var. plena, commonly known as the double-flowered gardenia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and large, fragrant, white flowers with multiple layers of petals. The plant produces showy, waxy blooms that are highly prized in ornamental horticulture for their beauty and scent. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
