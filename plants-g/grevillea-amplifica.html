Grevillea amplifica

Grevillea amplifica, commonly known as the broad-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features striking, bright red flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea amplifica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds.
