Gentiana polyantha

Gentiana polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Gentiana polyantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
