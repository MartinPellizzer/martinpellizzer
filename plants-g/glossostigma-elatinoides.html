Glossostigma elatinoides

Glossostigma elatinoides, commonly known as the glasswort or sea bean, is a halophytic plant that thrives in saline environments.

It is characterized by its small, succulent leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown in color. Glossostigma elatinoides is often found in coastal areas, salt marshes, and tidal zones.

Its ability to tolerate high salt concentrations makes it an important component of these unique ecosystems.
