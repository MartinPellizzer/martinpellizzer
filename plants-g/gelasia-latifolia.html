Gelasia latifolia

Gelasia latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf gelasia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant in some regions.
