Gloxinia rubra

Gloxinia rubra, commonly known as the scarlet gloxinia, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish. Its tuberous roots store nutrients, allowing it to survive periods of dormancy.

Gloxinia rubra is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
