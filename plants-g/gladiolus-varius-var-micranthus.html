Gladiolus varius var. micranthus

Gladiolus varius var. micranthus, commonly known as the dwarf gladiolus, is a compact, ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of brightly colored, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The flowers are typically smaller than those of other gladiolus species, with a more delicate appearance. This variety is valued for its resilience and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
