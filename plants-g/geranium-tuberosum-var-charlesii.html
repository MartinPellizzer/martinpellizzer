Geranium tuberosum var. charlesii

Geranium tuberosum var. charlesii, commonly known as the Charlesii geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems covered in opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a desirable addition to many gardens.
