Gilliesia graminea

Gilliesia graminea, commonly known as the grassy gilliesia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish or yellowish and appear in clusters.

Gilliesia graminea is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in dry, well-drained soils.
