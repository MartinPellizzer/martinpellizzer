Gaultheria arfakana

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants such as heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. Gaultheria arfakana is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
