Geranium glaberrimum

Geranium glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features upright, branching stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to purple, blooming from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium glaberrimum is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
