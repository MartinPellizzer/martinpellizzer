Galatella songorica

Galatella songorica, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, while its roots are often aerial, clinging to trees.

Galatella songorica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
