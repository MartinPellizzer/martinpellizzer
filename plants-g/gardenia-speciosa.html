Gardenia speciosa

Gardenia speciosa, commonly known as the white gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, white flowers that are highly fragrant. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia speciosa is also valued in perfumery due to the strong scent of its blooms.
