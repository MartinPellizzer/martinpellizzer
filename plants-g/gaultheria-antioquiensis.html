Gaultheria antioquiensis

Gaultheria antioquiensis, commonly known as the Antioquian wintergreen, is a species of shrub native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall.
