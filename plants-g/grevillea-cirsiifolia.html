Grevillea cirsiifolia

Grevillea cirsiifolia, commonly known as the silky grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, silky foliage and bright orange to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden planting.
