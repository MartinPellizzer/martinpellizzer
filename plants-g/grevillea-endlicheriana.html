Grevillea endlicheriana

Grevillea endlicheriana, commonly known as the pink and white grevillea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, fern-like foliage and clusters of pendulous, pink and white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, with narrow, leathery leaves.

Grevillea endlicheriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
