Gasteria nigricans var. fasciata

Gasteria nigricans var. fasciata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with distinct white or yellowish stripes, giving it its zebra-like appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal irrigation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
