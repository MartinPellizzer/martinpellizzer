Gentiana makinoi f. stenophylla

Gentiana makinoi f. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gentian, is a variant of the gentian species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and streamside areas. Its foliage is often more compact compared to the typical form, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

The species belongs to the Gentianaceae family, known for its showy, blue or purple flowers.
