Gleichenia splendida

Gleichenia splendida, commonly known as the splendid maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often adorned with a striking metallic sheen. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or rocky crevices, and thrives in cool, humid climates. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are released during the dry season.

Gleichenia splendida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
