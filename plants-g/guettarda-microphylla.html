Guettarda microphylla

Guettarda microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and white or pale yellow, often appearing in clusters.

Guettarda microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
