Goodenia subsolana

Goodenia subsolana, commonly known as the coastal goodenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal dunes and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to withstand salty winds and is often found in coastal ecosystems.

Goodenia subsolana plays a role in stabilizing sand dunes and providing habitat for native wildlife.
