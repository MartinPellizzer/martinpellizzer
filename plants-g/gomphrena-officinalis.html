Gomphrena officinalis

Gomphrena officinalis, commonly known as the everlasting flower or torch flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its bright red, pink, or purple bracts that resemble small torches. The plant is known for its long-lasting blooms, which remain vibrant even when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often growing in dry, rocky environments.

Gomphrena officinalis has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
