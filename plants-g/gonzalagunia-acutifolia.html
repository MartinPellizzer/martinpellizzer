Gonzalagunia acutifolia

Gonzalagunia acutifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf shrub, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or sandy soils. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to cool, temperate climates.
