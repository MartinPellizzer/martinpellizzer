Galactites flavospinosus

Galactites flavospinosus, commonly known as the yellow spiny acanthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spiny leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is covered in fine, soft hairs, and the flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
