Geranium luganense

Geranium luganense, commonly known as the Luga geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright pink or red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium luganense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover in dry, sunny environments.
