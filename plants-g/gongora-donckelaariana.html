Gongora donckelaariana

Gongora donckelaariana, commonly known as the Donckelaaria Gongora, is a species of orchid native to the highlands of Ecuador.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often at elevations between 1,500 and 2,500 meters.

Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in its natural habitat.
