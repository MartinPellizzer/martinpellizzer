Glandularia andalgalensis

Glandularia andalgalensis, commonly known as the Andalusian glandular verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

Glandularia andalgalensis is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
