Goodyera luzonensis

Goodyera luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon maidenhair fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, intricate fronds that resemble those of the maidenhair fern. Goodyera luzonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
