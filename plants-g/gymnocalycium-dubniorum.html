Gymnocalycium dubniorum

Gymnocalycium dubniorum, commonly known as the Dubniorum moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its short, thick stems that are often covered in spines, and it produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is a perennial succulent, capable of surviving in harsh, dry environments with minimal water. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
