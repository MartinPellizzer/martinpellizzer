Galium mite var. roseum

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in cooler weather. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators like bees. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and along stream banks.
