Genista hirsuta subsp. lanuginosa

Genista hirsuta subsp.

lanuginosa, commonly known as the hairy bursch, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has small, spiny pods that contain seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden use in suitable climates.
