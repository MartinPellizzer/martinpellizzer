Genista ulicina

Genista ulicina, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and flowering structures. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. Its small, trifoliate leaves are opposite and often covered in fine hairs.

Genista ulicina is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
