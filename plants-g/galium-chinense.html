Galium chinense

Galium chinense, commonly known as Chinese bedstraw, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Galium chinense is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
