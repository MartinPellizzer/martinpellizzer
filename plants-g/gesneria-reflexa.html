Gesneria reflexa

Gesneria reflexa, commonly known as the silverleaf gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria reflexa is popular among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
