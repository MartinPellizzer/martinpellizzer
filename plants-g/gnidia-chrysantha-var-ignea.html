Gnidia chrysantha var. ignea

Gnidia chrysantha var. ignea, commonly known as the flame bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to its ecological value.
