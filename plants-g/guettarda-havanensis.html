Guettarda havanensis

Guettarda havanensis, commonly known as the Cuban guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Guettarda havanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
