Guianodendron praeclarum

Guianodendron praeclarum, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a large evergreen tree native to South America, particularly found in Guyana and Suriname.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its vibrant floral display.
