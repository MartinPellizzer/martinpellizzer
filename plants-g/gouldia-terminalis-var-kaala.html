Gouldia terminalis var. kaala

Gouldia terminalis var. kaala, commonly known as the Black Gouldia, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically dark purple to black in color, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped flowers that range from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique coloration and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
