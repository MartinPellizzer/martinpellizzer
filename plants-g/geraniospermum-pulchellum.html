Geraniospermum pulchellum

Geraniospermum pulchellum, commonly known as the pink geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and compact size.
