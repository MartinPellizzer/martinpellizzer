Gymnopteris oligarchica

Gymnopteris oligarchica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed holly fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.
