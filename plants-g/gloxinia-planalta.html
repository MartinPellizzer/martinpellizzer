Gloxinia planalta

Gloxinia planalta, commonly known as the Brazilian gloxinia, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family. The plant features striking, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinia planalta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
