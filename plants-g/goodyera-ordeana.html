Goodyera ordeana

Goodyera ordeana, commonly known as the Mexican lace fern, is a species of orchid native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, glossy, evergreen leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in a spike. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often growing on trees or in rocky crevices.

Goodyera ordeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
