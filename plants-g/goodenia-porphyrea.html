Goodenia porphyrea

Goodenia porphyrea, commonly known as the purple goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to reddish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Goodenia porphyrea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
