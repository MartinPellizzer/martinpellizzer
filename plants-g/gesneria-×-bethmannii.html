Gesneria × bethmannii

Gesneria × bethmannii, commonly known as the Bethmann hybrid gesneria, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Gesneria ferruginea and Gesneria rigida.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family, characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically features broad, glossy leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of vibrant orange or red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria × bethmannii is popular among tropical plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
