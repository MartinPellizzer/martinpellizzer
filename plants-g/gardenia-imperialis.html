Gardenia imperialis

Gardenia imperialis, commonly known as the imperial gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, waxy white flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant is evergreen and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and pleasant scent.

The flowers are typically borne in clusters and bloom during the summer months.
