Grevillea sturtii var. pinnatisecta

Grevillea sturtii var. pinnatisecta, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features finely divided, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, typically during spring and summer. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
