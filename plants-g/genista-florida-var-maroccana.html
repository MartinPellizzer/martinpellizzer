Genista florida var. maroccana

Genista florida var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan flowering broom, is a shrub native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and is adapted to arid and rocky environments. It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
