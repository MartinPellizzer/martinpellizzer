Gardenia dryadum

Gardenia dryadum, commonly known as the dryad's gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is evergreen and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and pleasant aroma.
