Gesneria spicata

Gesneria spicata, commonly known as the spike gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture.

Gesneria spicata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to flourish.
