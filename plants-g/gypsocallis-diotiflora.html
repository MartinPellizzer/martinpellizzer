Gypsocallis diotiflora

Gypsocallis diotiflora, commonly known as the golden trumpet lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Gypsocallis diotiflora thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
