Guerkea congolana

Guerkea congolana, commonly known as the Congo guerkea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to central Africa.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, greyish bark. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Guerkea congolana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
