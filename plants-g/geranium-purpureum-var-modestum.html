Geranium purpureum var. modestum

Geranium purpureum var. modestum, commonly known as the Purple Geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pentagonal flowers. The plant typically produces pink to purple blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium purpureum var. modestum is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and ornamental value.
