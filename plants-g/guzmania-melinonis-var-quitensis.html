Guzmania melinonis var. quitensis

Guzmania melinonis var. quitensis, commonly known as the Quitense Flame Plant, is a striking bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It features a vibrant, colorful inflorescence that resembles a flaming flower, with bright red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant has a rosette-shaped foliage that collects rainwater, creating a micro-ecosystem for small organisms. Its leaves are broad and waxy, with a deep green color that contrasts beautifully with the vivid bracts.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
