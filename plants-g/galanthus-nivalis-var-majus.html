Galanthus nivalis var. majus

Galanthus nivalis var. majus, commonly known as the Giant Snowdrop, is a variety of the snowdrop plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its large, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous root system. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, often found in woodland areas.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and is a popular choice for gardeners seeking early spring blooms.
