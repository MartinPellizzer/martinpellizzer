Globularia dumulosa

Globularia dumulosa, commonly known as the woolly globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Globularia dumulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
