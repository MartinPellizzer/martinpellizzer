Gazania rigens var. uniflora

Gazania rigens var. uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and orange-pink petals. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form with hairy, gray-green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The blooms appear in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens throughout the season.
