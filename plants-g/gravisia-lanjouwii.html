Gravisia lanjouwii

Gravisia lanjouwii, commonly known as the Lanjouwii thistle, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are usually hairy and lance-shaped, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in dense clusters.

Gravisia lanjouwii is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
