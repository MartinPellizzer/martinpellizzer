Goeppertia aemula

Goeppertia aemula, commonly known as the silver stripe calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature silver and green stripes. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

Its foliage is not only aesthetically pleasing but also contributes to air purification.
