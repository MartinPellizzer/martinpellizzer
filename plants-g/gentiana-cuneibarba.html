Gentiana cuneibarba

Gentiana cuneibarba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which typically bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Gentiana cuneibarba is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, where it is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.
