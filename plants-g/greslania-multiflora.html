Greslania multiflora

Greslania multiflora, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a star-like appearance. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy texture.

Greslania multiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
