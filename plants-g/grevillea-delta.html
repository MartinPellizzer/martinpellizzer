Grevillea delta

Grevillea delta, commonly known as the Delta Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features bright red flowers that are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and arid gardens.

Grevillea delta is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
