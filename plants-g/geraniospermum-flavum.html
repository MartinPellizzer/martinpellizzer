Geraniospermum flavum

Geraniospermum flavum, commonly known as the yellow geraniospermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae, which includes various genera such as Geranium and Erodium. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and hairy, succulent leaves adapted to arid environments. It typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in shrublands and grasslands.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists studying xerophytic adaptations.
