Gazania varians

Gazania varians, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, hairy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Gazania varians is known for its long blooming period, often lasting from spring to autumn.
