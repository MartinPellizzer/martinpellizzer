Geesinkorchis alaticallosa

Geesinkorchis alaticallosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant has a slender, leafless stem with two narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and pale green, often arranged in a spike, and lack a distinct scent.
