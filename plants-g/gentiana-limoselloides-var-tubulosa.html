Gentiana limoselloides var. tubulosa

Gentiana limoselloides var. tubulosa, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountain meadows and rocky slopes.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for alpine ecosystems.
