Geranium × hastatum

Geranium × hastatum, commonly known as the ivy-leaved geranium, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from different geranium species.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and dark green, heart-shaped leaves that resemble ivy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
