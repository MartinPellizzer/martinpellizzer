Gleichenia tahitensis

Gleichenia tahitensis, commonly known as the Tahitian fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bright green and slightly curled. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Gleichenia tahitensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
