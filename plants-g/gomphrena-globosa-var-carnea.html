Gomphrena globosa var. carnea

Gomphrena globosa var. carnea, commonly known as the globular gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its spherical, brightly colored flower heads that range from pink to red. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often grown as an ornamental due to its striking appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The flowers are also used in floral arrangements and have cultural significance in some regions.
