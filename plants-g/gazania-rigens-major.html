Gazania rigens major

Gazania rigens major, commonly known as the showy gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features a short, woody stem with broad, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The flowers bloom from late spring to early autumn, adding a striking visual element to gardens.
