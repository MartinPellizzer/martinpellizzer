Gladiolus modestus

Gladiolus modestus, commonly known as the modest gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color and bloom during the summer months. The plant prefers well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
