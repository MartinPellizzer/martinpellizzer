Gentiana cachemirica

Gentiana cachemirica, commonly known as the Kashmir gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Gentiana cachemirica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
