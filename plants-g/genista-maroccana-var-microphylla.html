Genista maroccana var. microphylla

Genista maroccana var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved broom, is a shrub native to North Africa.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter in length. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring and attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden use in Mediterranean climates.
