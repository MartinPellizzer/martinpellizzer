Genista webbii var. boissieri

Genista webbii var. boissieri, commonly known as the Boissier's broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with several leaflets, and it produces small, leathery fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
