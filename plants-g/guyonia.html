Guyonia

Guyonia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Guyonia species are typically compact, making them popular houseplants due to their ability to thrive in indoor conditions. They prefer bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

The flowers often bloom in spring and summer, adding vibrant color to gardens and indoor spaces.
