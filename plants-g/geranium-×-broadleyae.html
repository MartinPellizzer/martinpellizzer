Geranium × broadleyae

Geranium × broadleyae, commonly known as the broadleyae geranium, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
