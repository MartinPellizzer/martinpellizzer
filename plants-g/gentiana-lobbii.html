Gentiana lobbii

Gentiana lobbii, commonly known as the Lobb's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentiana lobbii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
