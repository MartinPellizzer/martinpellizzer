Gardenia actinocarpa

Gardenia actinocarpa, commonly known as the star gardenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Gardenia actinocarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens and as a potted plant.
