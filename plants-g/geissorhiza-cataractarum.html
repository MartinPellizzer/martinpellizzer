Geissorhiza cataractarum

Geissorhiza cataractarum, commonly known as the waterfall lily, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
