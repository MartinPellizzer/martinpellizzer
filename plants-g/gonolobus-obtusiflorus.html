Gonolobus obtusiflorus

Gonolobus obtusiflorus, commonly known as the broad-flowered go-on-lub, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Gonolobus obtusiflorus is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
