Gardenia lamingtonii

Gardenia lamingtonii, commonly known as the Lamington gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are highly prized for their intense fragrance and are often used in perfumery.

Gardenia lamingtonii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
