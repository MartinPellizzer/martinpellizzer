Gardenia scabrella

Gardenia scabrella, commonly known as the rough gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their strong, sweet scent.

Gardenia scabrella prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
