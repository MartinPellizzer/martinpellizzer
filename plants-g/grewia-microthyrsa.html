Grewia microthyrsa

Grewia microthyrsa, commonly known as the small-leafed globemallow, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a dry capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Grewia microthyrsa is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid conditions.
