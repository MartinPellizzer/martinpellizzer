Gloriosa modesta

Gloriosa modesta, commonly known as the dwarf lily or miniature flame lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tuberous rhizomes and clusters of vibrant orange or red flowers. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color, and its flowers are trumpet-shaped with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Gloriosa modesta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper conditions.
