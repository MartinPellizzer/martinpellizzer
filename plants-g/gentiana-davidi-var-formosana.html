Gentiana davidi var. formosana

Gentiana davidi var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan gentian, is a striking flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and streamside habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
