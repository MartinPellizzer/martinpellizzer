Genista heteroacantha

Genista heteroacantha, commonly known as the heteroacanth genista, is a shrub native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features thorny stems and small, bright green leaves that are trifoliate in structure. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters and blooming in spring.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
