Gaertnera calycina var. variegata

Gaertnera calycina var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated calycine, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical, glossy leaves with distinctive variegation patterns of white and green, enhancing its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its visual interest. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
