Gazania × aurantiaca

Gazania × aurantiaca, commonly known as the hybrid gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid between Gazania rigida and Gazania leucolaenia, resulting in a plant with striking, daisy-like flowers. The flowers are typically bright orange or red, with yellow centers, and they bloom profusely during the warmer months. This species is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its foliage is usually dark green and woolly, adding texture to the garden setting.
