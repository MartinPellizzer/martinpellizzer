Gesneria hondensis

Gesneria hondensis, commonly known as the Honduran gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to Honduras.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually elliptical with a glossy texture, and the flowers come in shades of red, orange, or yellow.

Gesneria hondensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
