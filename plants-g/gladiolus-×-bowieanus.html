Gladiolus × bowieanus

Gladiolus × bowieanus, commonly known as the Bowiean gladiolus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, brightly colored flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces large, sword-shaped leaves and blooms during the summer months. It is native to South Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowers are arranged in spikes and are popular in floral arrangements due to their striking appearance.
