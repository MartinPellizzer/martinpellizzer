Guyonia jacquesii

Guyonia jacquesii, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. Its bark contains a milky sap, which is toxic and used in traditional medicine.

Guyonia jacquesii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
