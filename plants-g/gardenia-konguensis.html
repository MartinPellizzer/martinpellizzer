Gardenia konguensis

Gardenia konguensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in evergreen forests. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
