Genista tinctoria f. angustata

Genista tinctoria f. angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf yellowflower broom, is a compact shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, narrow, evergreen leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in racemes. It is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
