Goeppertia nigricans

Goeppertia nigricans, commonly known as the Black Velvet Canna, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and striking orange-red flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, making it a prominent feature in garden landscapes. Its foliage is often used in horticultural displays for its aesthetic appeal.

The plant prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.
