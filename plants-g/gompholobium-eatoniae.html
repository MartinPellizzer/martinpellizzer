Gompholobium eatoniae

Gompholobium eatoniae, commonly known as the Red-stemmed Gompholobium, is a member of the Fabaceae family and is native to Australia.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and heathlands.
