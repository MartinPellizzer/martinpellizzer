Gasteria acinacifolia var. ensifolia

Gasteria acinacifolia var. ensifolia, commonly known as the sword leaf gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or grayish markings. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
