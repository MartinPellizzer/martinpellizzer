Guzmania rhonhofiana f. variegata

Guzmania rhonhofiana f. variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Guzmania, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

This plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, featuring variegated patterns of green, white, and pink on its broad, strap-like leaves. It produces an upright inflorescence that bears small, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
