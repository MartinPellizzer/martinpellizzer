Gerbera pterodonta

Gerbera pterodonta, commonly known as the African gerbera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white, pink, or red petals. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil and full sun. Gerbera pterodonta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
