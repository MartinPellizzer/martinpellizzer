Geranium thunbergii var. roseum

Geranium thunbergii var. roseum, commonly known as the roseum geranium, is a compact, trailing perennial native to Japan.

It features small, rounded leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of pink to rose-colored flowers throughout the growing season. The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil. It is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its trailing habit.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and foliage, making it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
