Goeppertia joffilyana

Goeppertia joffilyana, commonly known as the Joffilyana Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, mottled foliage with vibrant color patterns. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia joffilyana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
