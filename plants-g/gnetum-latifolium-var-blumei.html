Gnetum latifolium var. blumei

Gnetum latifolium var. blumei, commonly known as the Blume's broadleaf gnetum, is a species of gymnosperm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems, scale-like leaves, and unique reproductive structures. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are unisexual, with male and female structures on separate plants, and it produces small, cone-like structures for seed dispersal.
