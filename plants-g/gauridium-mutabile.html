Gauridium mutabile

Gauridium mutabile, commonly known as the variable gauridium, is a unique plant species characterized by its morphological adaptability.

It belongs to the family Gauridiaceae and is native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. The plant exhibits a range of leaf shapes and sizes depending on environmental conditions, which contributes to its common name. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Gauridium mutabile is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and rocky crevices.
