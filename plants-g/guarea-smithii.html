Guarea smithii

Guarea smithii, commonly known as the "gumbo lima" or "sabre tree," is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, elongated fruits that resemble a sabre or scythe. The plant belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes other tropical hardwoods. Its bark is thick and rugged, often featuring fissures and ridges.
