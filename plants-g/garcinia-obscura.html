Garcinia obscura

Garcinia obscura, commonly known as the dark Garcinia, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers. Its botanical features make it a distinctive and interesting subject for study in both horticulture and pharmacology.
