Genista salzmannii subsp. lobelioides

Genista salzmannii subsp.

lobelioides, commonly known as the Salzmann's broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands. It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant has slender, branched stems covered in fine, needle-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal flowering.
