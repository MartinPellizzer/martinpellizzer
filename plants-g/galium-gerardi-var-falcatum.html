Galium gerardi var. falcatum

Galium gerardi var. falcatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf bedstraw, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features slender, ascending stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly curled or rolled appearance. Small, white flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

This plant is typically found in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
