Gastrorchis steinhardtiana

Gastrorchis steinhardtiana, commonly known as the red spider orchid, is a striking orchid species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which resemble spiders, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with good drainage. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

Gastrorchis steinhardtiana is known for its unique floral structure and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
