Grevillea halmaturina

Grevillea halmaturina, commonly known as the Halmaturina Grevillea, is a shrub native to South Australia.

It features striking red or orange flowers that appear in dense, cylindrical clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Grevillea halmaturina is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, providing nectar for native pollinators.
