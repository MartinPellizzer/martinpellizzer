Geranium pulchrum

Geranium pulchrum, commonly known as the pretty geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, five-petaled blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Geranium pulchrum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
