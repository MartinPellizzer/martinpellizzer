Gentiana spectabilis

Gentiana spectabilis, commonly known as the showy gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Gentiana spectabilis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
