Goodenia strophiolata

Goodenia strophiolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its flowers are usually pink or red, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Goodenia strophiolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
