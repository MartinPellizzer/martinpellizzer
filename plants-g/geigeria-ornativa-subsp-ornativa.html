Geigeria ornativa subsp. ornativa

Geigeria ornativa subsp.

ornativa, commonly known as the ornamental geigeria, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa. It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
