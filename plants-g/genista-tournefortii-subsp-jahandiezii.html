Genista tournefortii subsp. jahandiezii

Genista tournefortii subsp.

jahandiezii, commonly known as the Jahandiez's broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant has slender, spiny stems and small, leathery leaves that are adapted to arid conditions. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its botanical characteristics reflect its evolutionary adaptation to the harsh environments of its native habitat.
