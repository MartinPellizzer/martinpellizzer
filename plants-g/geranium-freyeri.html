Geranium freyeri

Geranium freyeri, commonly known as the Freyer's geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it grows in rocky and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, narrow leaves, and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. It blooms during the cooler months, typically from late winter to early spring.

Geranium freyeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
