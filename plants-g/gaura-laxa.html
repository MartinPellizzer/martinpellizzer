Gaura laxa

Gaura laxa, commonly known as whorled gaura, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, branching stems and clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped to ovate leaves that are usually hairy and slightly serrated. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and cottage gardens.

Its airy appearance and long bloom period add a graceful touch to any landscape.
