Gilia Latiflora Subsp. Speciosa

Gilia latiflora subsp. speciosa is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the western united states. it belongs to the plantain family and is known for its vibrant purple to blue flowers that bloom in summer. this subspecies is particularly notable for its showy inflorescences that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. it thrives in rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and meadows. due to its aesthetic appeal and ecological importance, it is a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike.
