Gentiana kirishima-montana

Gentiana kirishima-montana, commonly known as the Kirishima mountain gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to Japan, specifically the island of Kyushu, where it grows in high-altitude regions. The plant is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
