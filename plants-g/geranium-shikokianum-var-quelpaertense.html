Geranium shikokianum var. quelpaertense

Geranium shikokianum var. quelpaertense, commonly known as the Seoul geranium, is a compact, upright perennial plant native to South Korea.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces clusters of vibrant pink to purple flowers. The plant is well-suited for container gardening and rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its blooming period typically occurs in the summer months, adding a splash of color to gardens.
