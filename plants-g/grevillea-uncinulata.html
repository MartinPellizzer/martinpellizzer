Grevillea uncinulata

Grevillea uncinulata, commonly known as the Hooked Grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hooked seed pods and vibrant red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Grevillea uncinulata is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland heathlands.
