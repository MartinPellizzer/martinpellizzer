Goeppertia chrysoleuca

Goeppertia chrysoleuca, commonly known as the Golden Leaf Calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage that displays shades of green, white, and yellow, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for those seeking to add a touch of exotic beauty to their living spaces.
