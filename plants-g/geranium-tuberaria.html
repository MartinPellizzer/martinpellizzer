Geranium tuberaria

Geranium tuberaria, commonly known as the tuberous geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its root system includes tubers that store nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Geranium tuberaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
