Guatteria spectabilis

Guatteria spectabilis, commonly known as the showy guatteria, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by edible, fleshy berries. Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and showy inflorescences, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

Guatteria spectabilis is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
