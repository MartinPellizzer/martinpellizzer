Gnaphalium cephaloideum

Gnaphalium cephaloideum, commonly known as the woolly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
