Geranium onaei var. glabrescens

Geranium onaei var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly China and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and clusters of vibrant pink to purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
