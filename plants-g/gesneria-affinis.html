Gesneria affinis

Gesneria affinis, commonly known as the silver stripe gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves with silver-green markings. The plant produces vibrant orange-red flowers during the warm season, typically in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria affinis is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
