Gongora glicensteiniana

Gongora glicensteiniana, commonly known as the Glicensteinian orchid, is a rare and striking species native to the high-altitude regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, intricate floral structures. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces showy, colorful flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific conditions to bloom.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
