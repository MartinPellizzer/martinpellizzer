Genista nitens

Genista nitens, commonly known as the evergreen yellow broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

Genista nitens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
