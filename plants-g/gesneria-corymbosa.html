Gesneria corymbosa

Gesneria corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming in the spring or early summer.

Gesneria corymbosa is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and modest flowering display.
