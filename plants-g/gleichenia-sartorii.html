Gleichenia sartorii

Gleichenia sartorii, commonly known as the Sartor's fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, linear leaves that form a dense, mat-like structure on the ground. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions. Its rhizomes are wiry and help in spreading across the forest floor.

Gleichenia sartorii is known for its adaptability and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
