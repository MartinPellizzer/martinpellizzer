Gentiana nitida

Gentiana nitida, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Gentiana nitida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
