Gentiana praecox var. carpatica

Gentiana praecox var. carpatica, commonly known as the Carpathian gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
