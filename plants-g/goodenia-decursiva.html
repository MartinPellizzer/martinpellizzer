Goodenia decursiva

Goodenia decursiva, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Goodenia decursiva is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
