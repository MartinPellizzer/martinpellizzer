Glochidion psidioides

Glochidion psidioides, commonly known as the shining leaf glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often clustered at the ends of branches. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries that are edible but have a slightly bitter taste. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded areas within forests.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
