Geranium sanguineiforme

Geranium sanguineiforme, commonly known as the blood geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Geranium sanguineiforme thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and delicate floral display.
