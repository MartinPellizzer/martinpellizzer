Garcinia subelliptica

Garcinia subelliptica, commonly known as the elliptic garcinia, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or white in color. Its fruit is a small, round drupe with a thick, yellowish outer layer.

Garcinia subelliptica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
