Gesneria salicifolia var. spathulata

Gesneria salicifolia var. spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf gesneria, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a standout addition to any plant collection.
