Guarea tonduzii

Guarea tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii Guarea, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood and medicinal properties. The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
