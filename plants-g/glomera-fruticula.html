Glomera fruticula

Glomera fruticula, commonly known as the clustered glomera, is a shrub-like plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has woody stems and elliptical leaves with a smooth surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Glomera fruticula is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
