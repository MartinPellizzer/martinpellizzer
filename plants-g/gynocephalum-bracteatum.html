Gynocephalum bracteatum

Gynocephalum bracteatum, commonly known as the bracted leafcup, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a distinct bract-like structure at the base.
