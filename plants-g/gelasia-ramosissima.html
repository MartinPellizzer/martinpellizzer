Gelasia ramosissima

Gelasia ramosissima, commonly known as the silverleaf gelasia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that reflects light and helps reduce water loss. The plant has small, elliptical leaves arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits.

Gelasia ramosissima is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.
