Garcinia calleryi

Garcinia calleryi, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange, lantern-shaped fruits that are often used in ornamental landscaping. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Garcinia calleryi is valued for its vibrant foliage and showy fruit, making it a popular choice in urban gardens.
