Goodenia glauca var. glandulosa

Goodenia glauca var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive blue or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its leaves are narrow and often have a glandular surface, which contributes to its common name.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
