Gymnanthes pallens var. tenax

Gymnanthes pallens var. tenax, commonly known as the tough gymnanthes, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.
