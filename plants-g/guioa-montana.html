Guioa montana

Guioa montana, commonly known as the mountain guioa, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits.

Guioa montana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
