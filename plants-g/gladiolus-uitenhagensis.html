Gladiolus uitenhagensis

Gladiolus uitenhagensis, commonly known as the Uitenhagens gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers in a variety of colors. Its foliage consists of long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
