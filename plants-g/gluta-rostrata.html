Gluta rostrata

Gluta rostrata, commonly known as the long-beaked gluta, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and simple, alternate leaves. The tree is valued for its hard wood and has traditional uses in local medicine.
