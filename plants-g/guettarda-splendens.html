Guettarda splendens

Guettarda splendens, commonly known as the splendour guettarda, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Guettarda splendens is also valued for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
