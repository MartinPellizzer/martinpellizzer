Gardenia boninensis

Gardenia boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin gardenia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Bonin Islands.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with a yellow center. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to humid, tropical climates. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

The fruit of Gardenia boninensis is a bright red berry that contains several seeds.
