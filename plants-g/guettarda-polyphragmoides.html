Guettarda polyphragmoides

Guettarda polyphragmoides, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, elliptical fruits that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Guettarda polyphragmoides is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
