Guarea glomerulata var. obanensis

Guarea glomerulata var. obanensis, commonly known as the Obanensis Guarea, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and distinctive, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its leaves are usually alternate, simple, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
