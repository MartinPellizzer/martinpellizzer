Gymnocalycium megalothelon

Gymnocalycium megalothelon, commonly known as the giant moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to the deserts of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gymnocalycium megalothelon is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
