Gymnocline argyrophylla

Gymnocline argyrophylla, commonly known as silverleaf gymnocline, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Gymnocline argyrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
