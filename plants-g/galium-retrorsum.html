Galium retrorsum

Galium retrorsum, commonly known as wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features slender, branched stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, clustered in umbels, and bloom in early spring.

Galium retrorsum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
