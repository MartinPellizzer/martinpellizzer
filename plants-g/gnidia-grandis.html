Gnidia grandis

Gnidia grandis, commonly known as the Natal spiderflower, is a South African plant belonging to the family Rhamnaceae.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral structure.
