Galega pinnata

Galega pinnata, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive pinnate structure, and its flowers are typically violet or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Galega pinnata is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
