Gutierrezia tenuis

Gutierrezia tenuis, commonly known as narrowleaf goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the aster family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments.

Gutierrezia tenuis plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for pollinators.
