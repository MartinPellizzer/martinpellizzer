Genista cinerea subsp. obtusiramea

Genista cinerea subsp.

obtusiramea, commonly known as the blunt-stemmed broom, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It features slender, often branched stems that are covered in fine, grayish hairs.

The leaves are small, linear, and arranged in pairs along the stems. It produces yellow flowers in terminal clusters, which bloom in spring.

This subspecies is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland fynbos vegetation.
