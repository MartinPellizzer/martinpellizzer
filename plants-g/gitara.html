Gitara

Gitara, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

Gitara thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
