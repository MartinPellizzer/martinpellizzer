Gaura coccinea var. epilobioides

Gaura coccinea var. epilobioides, commonly known as the scarlet gaura, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems covered in clusters of showy, pink to red tubular flowers. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a tendency to spread via rhizomes.
