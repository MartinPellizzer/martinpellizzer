Gentiana scabrida subsp. itzershanensis

Gentiana scabrida subsp.

itzershanensis, commonly known as the Itzershan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are often rough and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branching.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
