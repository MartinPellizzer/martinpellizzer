Gymnadenia splendida

Gymnadenia splendida, commonly known as the splendid goat's beard, is a species of orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its leaves are grass-like and arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

Gymnadenia splendida is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
