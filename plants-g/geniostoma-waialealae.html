Geniostoma waialealae

Geniostoma waialealae, commonly known as the Waialeale dandelion, is a rare flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is characterized by its small, yellow composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in lowland forests and along stream banks. It is listed as endangered due to habitat loss and invasive species competition.

Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique botanical species.
