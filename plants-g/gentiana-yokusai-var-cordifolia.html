Gentiana yokusai var. cordifolia

Gentiana yokusai var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features lanceolate to heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in traditional Japanese herbal medicine.
