Geranium kiusianum

Geranium kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusianum geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
