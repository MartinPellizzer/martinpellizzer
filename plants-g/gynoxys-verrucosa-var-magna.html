Gynoxys verrucosa var. magna

Gynoxys verrucosa var. magna, commonly known as the giant gynoxys, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

This variant is notable for its size and vibrant floral display, making it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
