Graptophyllum excelsum

Graptophyllum excelsum, commonly known as the giant rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
