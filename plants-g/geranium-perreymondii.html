Geranium perreymondii

Geranium perreymondii, commonly known as the Perreymond's geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking low-maintenance, drought-tolerant plants.
