Goeppertia velutina

Goeppertia velutina, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, deep green leaves with intricate patterns of silver and white. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia velutina is a low-maintenance plant that adds a touch of elegance to any indoor space.
