Geranium pilosum var. grandiflorum

Geranium pilosum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which bloom from summer to autumn. Its leaves are heart-shaped with serrated edges and a fuzzy texture.

Geranium pilosum var. grandiflorum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
