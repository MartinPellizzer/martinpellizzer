Goeppertia hirsuta var. latifolia

Goeppertia hirsuta var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a striking silver-green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

This varietal is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
