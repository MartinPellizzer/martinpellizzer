Goeppertia dressleri

Goeppertia dressleri, commonly known as the velvetleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that display striking patterns of green, silver, and pink. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with a short stem and broad, heart-shaped foliage. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and greenhouse cultivation.
