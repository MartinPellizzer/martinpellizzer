Gambeya lungi

Gambeya lungi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gambeya lungi is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
