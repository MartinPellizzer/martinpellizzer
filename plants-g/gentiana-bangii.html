Gentiana bangii

Gentiana bangii, commonly known as the Bangii gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, and it thrives in cool, moist environments. Gentiana bangii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Gentianaceae family.
