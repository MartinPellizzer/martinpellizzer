Gentianella soratensis

Gentianella soratensis, commonly known as the Sorato gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer.

Gentianella soratensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
