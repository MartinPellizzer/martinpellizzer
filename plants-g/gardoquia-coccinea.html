Gardoquia coccinea

Gardoquia coccinea, commonly known as the scarlet gardoquia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found growing in rocky or coastal areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

The species is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
