Gynura auriculata var. puberula

Gynura auriculata var. puberula, commonly known as the velvet leaf or hairy gynura, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant has a robust root system and thrives in moist, shady environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The flowers are small and yellow, typically appearing in clusters at the base of the plant.
