Gesneria calcarata

Gesneria calcarata, commonly known as the rock gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and striking, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soil, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its natural habitat.

Gesneria calcarata is popular among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
