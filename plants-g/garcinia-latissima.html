Garcinia latissima

Garcinia latissima, commonly known as the broad-leafed garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries that contain a single seed. Garcinia latissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.

It prefers warm, humid climates and well-drained soil.
