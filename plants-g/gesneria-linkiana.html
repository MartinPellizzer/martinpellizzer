Gesneria linkiana

Gesneria linkiana, commonly known as the Madagascar flame plant, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub with glossy, dark green leaves. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Gesneria linkiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
