Gesneria cardinalis

Gesneria cardinalis, commonly known as the Cardinal Gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange bracts that surround its small, white flowers, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, adapted to retain moisture in its native environment. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria cardinalis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
