Gynura elberti

Gynura elberti, commonly known as the velvetleaf or red leaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to reddish hues. The plant typically has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by fluffy, white seed heads.

Native to tropical regions, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
