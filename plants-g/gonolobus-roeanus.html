Gonolobus roeanus

Gonolobus roeanus, commonly known as the velvetleaf or African velvetleaf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

The plant is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and agricultural fields, due to its adaptability to various soil types. The plant's rapid growth and ability to fix nitrogen make it both ecologically significant and a challenge in certain ecosystems.
