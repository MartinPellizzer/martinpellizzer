Gaya × allanii

Gaya × allanii, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Gaya and Allan’s hibiscus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with a prominent central stigma. The plant is a shrub or small tree, native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in warm climates.
