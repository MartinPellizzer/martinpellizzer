Grevillea rigida

Grevillea rigida, commonly known as the rigid grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, needle-like leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant is typically found in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.

Grevillea rigida is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for birds and insects.
