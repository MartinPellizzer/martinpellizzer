Gastrochilus perpusillus

Gastrochilus perpusillus, commonly known as the dwarf stomach orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, hairy stems and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

It is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
