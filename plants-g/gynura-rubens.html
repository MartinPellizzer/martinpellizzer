Gynura rubens

Gynura rubens, commonly known as the red-veined sorrel, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive dark green leaves with bright red veins, which give it its ornamental appeal. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic value.

Gynura rubens is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
