Gongora ileneana

Gongora ileneana, commonly known as the Ilenean orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that often display vibrant red and purple hues. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are long and narrow, adapted to capture moisture from the humid air.

Gongora ileneana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in its native habitat.
