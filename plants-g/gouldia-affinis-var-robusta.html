Gouldia affinis var. robusta

Gouldia affinis var. robusta, commonly known as the robust gouldia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and striking, showy inflorescences that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.

Gouldia affinis var. robusta is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
