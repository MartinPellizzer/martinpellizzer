Guettarda rotundifolia

Guettarda rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, featuring dense foliage and showy yellow flowers. The leaves are rounded and glossy, often arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and its use in traditional medicine.
