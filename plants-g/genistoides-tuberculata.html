Genistoides tuberculata

Genistoides tuberculata, commonly known as the rough bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species in local ecosystems and a subject of botanical study.
