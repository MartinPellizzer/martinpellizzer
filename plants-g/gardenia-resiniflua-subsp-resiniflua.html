Gardenia resiniflua subsp. resiniflua

Gardenia resiniflua subsp.

resiniflua, commonly known as the resinous gardenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant produces a resinous substance in its stems, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
