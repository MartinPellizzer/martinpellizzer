Genista cosoniaca

Genista cosoniaca, commonly known as the Cosoniaca Broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its foliage consists of small, narrow leaves arranged in pairs, and it produces seed pods that contain several seeds.

Genista cosoniaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
