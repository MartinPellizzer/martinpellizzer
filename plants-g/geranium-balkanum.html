Geranium balkanum

Geranium balkanum, commonly known as the Balkan geranium, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant features upright stems with opposite, palmately lobed leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Geranium balkanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
