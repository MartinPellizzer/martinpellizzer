Gesneria bulbosa var. cooperi

Gesneria bulbosa var. cooperi, commonly known as the Cooper's gesneria, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy rhizomes and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
