Gymnocalycium reductum subsp. sibalii

Gymnocalycium reductum subsp.

sibalii, commonly known as the Sibalii Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Argentina. It features a spiny, ribbed stem that is typically green to bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange in hue, typically during the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
