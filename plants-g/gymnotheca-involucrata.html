Gymnotheca involucrata

Gymnotheca involucrata, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Gymnotheca involucrata is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields.
