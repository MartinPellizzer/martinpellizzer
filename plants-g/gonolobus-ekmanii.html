Gonolobus ekmanii

Gonolobus ekmanii, commonly known as the African horned vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets. It has a fibrous root system and is often used in traditional medicine.

Gonolobus ekmanii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
