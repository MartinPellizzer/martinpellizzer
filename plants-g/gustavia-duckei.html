Gustavia duckei

Gustavia duckei, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.
