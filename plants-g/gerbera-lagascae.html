Gerbera lagascae

Gerbera lagascae, commonly known as the Lagasca gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white to pink petals. It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Gerbera lagascae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
