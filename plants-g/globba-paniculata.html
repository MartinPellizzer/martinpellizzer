Globba paniculata

Globba paniculata, commonly known as the yellow globba, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces dense, showy clusters of yellow flowers resembling a globe, hence its common name. The plant features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Globba paniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in tropical and subtropical landscapes.
