Garcinia rubrisepala

Garcinia rubrisepala, commonly known as the red-sepaled garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking red sepals that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits. Garcinia rubrisepala is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
