Goniopteris nicaraguensis

Goniopteris nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan moonwort, is a fern species native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, pinnately compound fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are often adorned with small, rounded lobes that give it a unique appearance.
