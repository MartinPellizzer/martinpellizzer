Gardenia somaliensis var. tubicalyx

Gardenia somaliensis var. tubicalyx, commonly known as the Somali gardenia, is a rare and unique variant of the gardenia genus native to the Horn of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with a yellowish corona. The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance and long-lasting blooms.

However, it is relatively uncommon in horticultural collections due to its specific growing requirements.
