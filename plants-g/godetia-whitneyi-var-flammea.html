Godetia whitneyi var. flammea

Godetia whitneyi var. flammea, commonly known as the scarlet godetia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, showy bracts that attract pollinators.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its ability to withstand arid conditions.
