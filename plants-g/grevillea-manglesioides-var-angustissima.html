Grevillea manglesioides var. angustissima

Grevillea manglesioides var. angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
