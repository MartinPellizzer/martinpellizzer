Gouldia terminalis f. santalifolia

Gouldia terminalis f. santalifolia, commonly known as the Santalifolia Gouldia, is a unique variant of the Gouldia genus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, red-tinged foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. The plant features small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
