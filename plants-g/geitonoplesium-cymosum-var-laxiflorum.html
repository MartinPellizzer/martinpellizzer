Geitonoplesium cymosum var. laxiflorum

Geitonoplesium cymosum var. laxiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered starfruit, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Australia.

Its fruits are typically small and sour, though they are not commonly consumed by humans.
