Geraniospermum chelidonium

Geraniospermum chelidonium, commonly known as the yellow dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that aid in its propagation.
