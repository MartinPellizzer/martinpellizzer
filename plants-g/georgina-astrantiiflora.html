Georgina astrantiiflora

Georgina astrantiiflora, commonly known as the star-flowered georgina, is a striking plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, attracting various pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
