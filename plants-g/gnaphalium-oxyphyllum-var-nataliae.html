Gnaphalium oxyphyllum var. nataliae

Gnaphalium oxyphyllum var. nataliae, commonly known as Natal's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, fluffy white to pale yellow flowers that are arranged in dense clusters. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for use in floral arrangements.
