Grevillea rogersoniana

Grevillea rogersoniana, commonly known as the pink waratah, is a striking flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, red or pink inflorescences that resemble a cluster of small flowers.
