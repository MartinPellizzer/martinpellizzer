Globba chinensis

Globba chinensis, commonly known as Chinese globba, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and covered with a waxy coating.

Globba chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
