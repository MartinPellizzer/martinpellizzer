Gardenia megasperma var. arborea

Gardenia megasperma var. arborea, commonly known as the giant gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces yellowish-orange berries that contain hard, angular seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
