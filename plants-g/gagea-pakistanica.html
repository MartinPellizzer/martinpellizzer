Gagea pakistanica

Gagea pakistanica, commonly known as the Pakistan gagea, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Gagea pakistanica is an important component of the local flora, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
