Glossochilopsis carnosula

Glossochilopsis carnosula, commonly known as the fat lip orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, thickened rhizome and produces fleshy, rounded pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves and bears small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
