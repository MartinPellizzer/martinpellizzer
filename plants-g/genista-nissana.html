Genista nissana

Genista nissana, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It has slender, branched stems and small, leathery leaves.

Genista nissana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soils.
