Graphorkis flava

Graphorkis flava, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are ovate and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

The orchid's flowers are fragrant and bloom during the spring and early summer months.
