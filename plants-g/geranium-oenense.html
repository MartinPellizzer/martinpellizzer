Geranium oenense

Geranium oenense, commonly known as the Malawi geranium, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright red or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.

Geranium oenense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
