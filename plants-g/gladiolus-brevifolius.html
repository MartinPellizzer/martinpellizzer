Gladiolus brevifolius

Gladiolus brevifolius, commonly known as the short-leaved gladiolus, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, colorful flower spikes. The plant typically blooms in summer, with flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Its foliage is characterized by its compact, narrow leaves, which are shorter than those of other gladiolus species.

This plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
