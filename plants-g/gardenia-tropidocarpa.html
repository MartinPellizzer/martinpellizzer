Gardenia tropidocarpa

Gardenia tropidocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are narrow and elongated. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in summer. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and strong, sweet scent.
