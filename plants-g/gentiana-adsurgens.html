Gentiana adsurgens

Gentiana adsurgens, commonly known as the sky gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often in rocky or gravelly soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branching.

Gentiana adsurgens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant colors.
