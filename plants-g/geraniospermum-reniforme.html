Geraniospermum reniforme

Geraniospermum reniforme, commonly known as the kidney-shaped geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Geraniospermum reniforme is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
