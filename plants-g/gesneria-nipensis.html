Gesneria nipensis

Gesneria nipensis, commonly known as the pink-stemmed gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features thick, succulent-like leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat. Gesneria nipensis thrives in humid, well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
