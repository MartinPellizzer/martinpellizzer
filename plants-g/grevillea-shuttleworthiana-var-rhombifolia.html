Grevillea shuttleworthiana var. rhombifolia

Grevillea shuttleworthiana var. rhombifolia, commonly known as the rhombifoliate grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, which appear in summer and attract nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
