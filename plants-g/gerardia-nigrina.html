Gerardia nigrina

Gerardia nigrina, commonly known as black gerardia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which are typically tubular in shape. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits known as cypselae, which contain the seeds.

Gerardia nigrina is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking floral coloration.
