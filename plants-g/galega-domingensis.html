Galega domingensis

Galega domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican goatherd's weed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly the island of Hispaniola.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
