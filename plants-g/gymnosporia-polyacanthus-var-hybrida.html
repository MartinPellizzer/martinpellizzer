Gymnosporia polyacanthus var. hybrida

Gymnosporia polyacanthus var. hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid Natal orchid, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces a unique structure called a "gymnosporia," which refers to its naked seeds enclosed in a fleshy, spiky capsule. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
