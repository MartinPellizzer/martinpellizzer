Gaura brachycarpa

Gaura brachycarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited gaura, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polemoniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Gaura brachycarpa is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
