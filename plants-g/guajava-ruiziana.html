Guajava ruiziana

Guajava ruiziana, commonly known as the white guanabana, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
