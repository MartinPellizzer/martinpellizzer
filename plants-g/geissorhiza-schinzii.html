Geissorhiza schinzii

Geissorhiza schinzii, commonly known as the African violet orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant features a pseudobulb-like stem that stores water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually dark green and fleshy, adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

Geissorhiza schinzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for indoor growing environments.
