Gasteria nigricans

Gasteria nigricans, commonly known as the Black-leaf Gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dark green, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with purple or black at the base, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for dry environments.

Gasteria nigricans is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in both indoor and outdoor settings.
