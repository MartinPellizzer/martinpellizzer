Gloeospermum sphaerocarpum

Gloeospermum sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the globe-leaf gloeospermum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are often rounded or orbicular in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
