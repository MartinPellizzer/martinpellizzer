Grevillea lawrenciana

Grevillea lawrenciana, commonly known as the pink and white grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features striking, bright pink and white flowers that appear in dense, cylindrical spikes. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically grey-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea lawrenciana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
