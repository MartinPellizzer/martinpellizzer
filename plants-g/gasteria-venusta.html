Gasteria venusta

Gasteria venusta, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or grayish markings, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking, upward-facing inflorescences with bright yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal irrigation.

Gasteria venusta is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its hardiness and ornamental appearance.
