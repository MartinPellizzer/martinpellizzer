Goodenia prostrata

Goodenia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate goodenia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Australia.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters. The plant has a mat-forming habit with slender, hairy stems that lie close to the ground. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sandy environments.
