Geranium dregei

Geranium dregei, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink to red blooms, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are usually rounded and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Geranium dregei is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
