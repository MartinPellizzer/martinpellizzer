Geonoma dasystachys

Geonoma dasystachys, commonly known as the broad-leafed fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit and elongated, often arching fronds. Geonoma dasystachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
