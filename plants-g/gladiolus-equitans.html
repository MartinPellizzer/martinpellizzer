Gladiolus equitans

Gladiolus equitans, commonly known as the mountain gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear striking, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing large, terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
