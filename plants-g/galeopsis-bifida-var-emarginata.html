Galeopsis bifida var. emarginata

Galeopsis bifida var. emarginata, commonly known as narrowleaf hedge nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers in whorled clusters, which are typical of the Scrophulariaceae family. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, often found along roadsides and in meadows.
