Galactia anisopoda

Galactia anisopoda, commonly known as the hairy cowpea, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Galactia anisopoda is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
