Galium sylvestre subsp. virens

Galium sylvestre subsp.

virens, commonly known as green wood-rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and opposite, ovate leaves.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
