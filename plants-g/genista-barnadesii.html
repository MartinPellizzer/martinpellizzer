Genista barnadesii

Genista barnadesii, commonly known as the Canary Island broom, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. Its small, clustered flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Genista barnadesii is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
