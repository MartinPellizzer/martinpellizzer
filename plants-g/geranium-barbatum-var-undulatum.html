Geranium barbatum var. undulatum

Geranium barbatum var. undulatum, commonly known as the wavy-leaf geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, undulating leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
