Gladiolus gawleri

Gladiolus gawleri, commonly known as the Gawler gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, colorful blooms. The flowers typically come in shades of red, pink, and white, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for floral arrangements.
