Galactia argentifolia

Galactia argentifolia, commonly known as silverleaf galactia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features silvery-white undersides on its leaves, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Galactia argentifolia is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
