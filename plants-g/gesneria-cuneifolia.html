Gesneria cuneifolia

Gesneria cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria cuneifolia is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and vibrant blooms.
