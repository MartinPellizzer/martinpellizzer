Goniothalamus macrophyllus var. siamensis

Goniothalamus macrophyllus var. siamensis, commonly known as the Siam giant yew, is a member of the family Myristicaceae and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a large evergreen tree characterized by its thick, leathery leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
