Greenea macrophylla

Greenea macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf greenea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Greenea macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
