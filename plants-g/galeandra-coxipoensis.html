Galeandra coxipoensis

Galeandra coxipoensis, commonly known as the Coxipoensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant orchid flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
