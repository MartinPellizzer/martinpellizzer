Gonolobus melinii

Gonolobus melinii, commonly known as the yellow false indigo, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Gonolobus melinii is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and other riparian habitats.
