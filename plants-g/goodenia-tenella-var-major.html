Goodenia tenella var. major

Goodenia tenella var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is typically found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
