Grona stolzii

Grona stolzii, commonly known as the Stolzii orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as cloud forests. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture.

Grona stolzii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
