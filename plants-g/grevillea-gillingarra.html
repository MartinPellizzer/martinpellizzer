Grevillea gillingarra

Grevillea gillingarra, commonly known as the Gillingarra's grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or greyish tinge. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in late winter to spring, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea gillingarra is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant landscapes.
