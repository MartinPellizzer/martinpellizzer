Grindelia volkensii

Grindelia volkensii, commonly known as the East African gumweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its grayish-green, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces a milky sap that is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Grindelia volkensii is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
