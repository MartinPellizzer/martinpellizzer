Geranium longipedicellatum

Geranium longipedicellatum, commonly known as the long-stemmed geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, slender stems that are often covered with fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

Geranium longipedicellatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
