Godetia viminea

Godetia viminea, commonly known as the creeping godetia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, prostrate stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread across the ground. Its small, bright yellow flowers are clustered in dense racemes, adding a vibrant touch to gardens.

The leaves are linear and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
