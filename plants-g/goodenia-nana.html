Goodenia nana

Goodenia nana, commonly known as the dwarf goodenia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, featuring a compact rosette of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Goodenia nana is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
