Gouldia axillaris f. glabriflora

Gouldia axillaris f. glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered gouldia, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and showy, pinkish-red flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance.
