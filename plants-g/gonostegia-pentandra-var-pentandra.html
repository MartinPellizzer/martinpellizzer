Gonostegia pentandra var. pentandra

Gonostegia pentandra var. pentandra, commonly known as the red-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with a yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Gonostegia pentandra var. pentandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
