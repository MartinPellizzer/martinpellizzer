Geranium yoshiianum

Geranium yoshiianum, commonly known as the Japanese geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value and ease of cultivation make it a popular choice among gardeners.
