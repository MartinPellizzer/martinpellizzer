Gouldia terminalis var. parvifolia

Gouldia terminalis var. parvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gouldia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with narrow, linear leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces clusters of small, bright orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
