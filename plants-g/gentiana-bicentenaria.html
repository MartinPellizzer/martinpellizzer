Gentiana bicentenaria

Gentiana bicentenaria, commonly known as the bicentennial gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its flowering period occurs in late summer, usually from July to September.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
