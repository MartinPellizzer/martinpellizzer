Graphorkis reticulata

Graphorkis reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a distinct net-like venation pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
