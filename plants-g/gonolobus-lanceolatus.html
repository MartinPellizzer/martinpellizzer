Gonolobus lanceolatus

Gonolobus lanceolatus, commonly known as the lanceleaf goonloob, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
