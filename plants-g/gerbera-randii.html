Gerbera randii

Gerbera randii, commonly known as the Natal gerbera, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, showy daisy-like flowers with vibrant colors ranging from yellow to red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring broad, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Gerbera randii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its long-lasting blooms.
