Gerbera Araneosa

Gerbera araneosa is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its unique appearance and adaptability. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience. Its foliage is covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy texture and aiding in moisture retention. The flowers, which come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, bloom in a spiral pattern, resembling a web or spider's web. This plant is both ornamental and ecologically significant, attracting pollinators and adding visual interest to gardens.
