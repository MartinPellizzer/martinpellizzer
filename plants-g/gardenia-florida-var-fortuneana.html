Gardenia florida var. fortuneana

Gardenia florida var. fortuneana, commonly known as the fortuneana gardenia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to ivory-colored flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

Due to its slow growth and compact form, it is suitable for small spaces and container gardening.
