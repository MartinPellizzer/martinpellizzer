Gladiolus communis var. distichus

Gladiolus communis var. distichus, commonly known as the sword lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, spike-like inflorescences that bear colorful, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with its stems being sturdy and often used in floral arrangements. Its flowers come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow, and are prized for their elegance and longevity.

The botanical features of this species make it a popular choice for gardens and cut flower production.
