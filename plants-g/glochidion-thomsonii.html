Glochidion thomsonii

Glochidion thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
