Gomesa florida f. concolor

Gomesa florida f. concolor, commonly known as the Florida concolor gesneriad, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture.

It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
