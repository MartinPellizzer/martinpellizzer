Gardenia rutenbergiana

Gardenia rutenbergiana, commonly known as the Rutenberg gardenia, is a rare and striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain hard, black seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by specialty growers.
