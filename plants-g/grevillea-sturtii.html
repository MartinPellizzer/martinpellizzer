Grevillea sturtii

Grevillea sturtii, commonly known as the Sturt's grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features striking, feather-like leaves that are often red or orange in color, providing a vibrant contrast to its green stems. The plant produces bright red or yellow flowers in clusters, which attract nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Grevillea sturtii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
