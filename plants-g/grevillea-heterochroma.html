Grevillea heterochroma

Grevillea heterochroma, commonly known as the red-stemmed Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea heterochroma is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native wildlife.
