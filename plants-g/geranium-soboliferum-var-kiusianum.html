Geranium soboliferum var. kiusianum

Geranium soboliferum var. kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusianum geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
