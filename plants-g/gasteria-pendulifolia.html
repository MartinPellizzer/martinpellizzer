Gasteria pendulifolia

Gasteria pendulifolia, commonly known as the pendant gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, with a bluish-green coloration. The plant produces long, pendant-like flower stalks that bear small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for dry environments.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
