Gladiolus × colvillii

Gladiolus × colvillii, commonly known as the Colvill's gladiolus, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are highly ornamental. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its hybrid origin, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various climates.
