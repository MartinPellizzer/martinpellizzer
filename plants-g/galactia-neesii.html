Galactia neesii

Galactia neesii, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in dry, open habitats. Galactia neesii is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, which makes it beneficial in ecological restoration projects.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for gardeners in suitable climates.
