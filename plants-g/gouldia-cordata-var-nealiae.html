Gouldia cordata var. nealiae

Gouldia cordata var. nealiae, commonly known as the Nealiae Gouldia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Australia.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, delicate white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
