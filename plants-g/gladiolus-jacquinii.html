Gladiolus jacquinii

Gladiolus jacquinii, commonly known as the Jacquin's gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences.
