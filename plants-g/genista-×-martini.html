Genista × martini

Genista × martini, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is native to southern Europe and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Genista × martini is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.
