Geraniospermum laevigatum

Geraniospermum laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth geraniosperm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, often found in rocky or arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to survive in dry conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
