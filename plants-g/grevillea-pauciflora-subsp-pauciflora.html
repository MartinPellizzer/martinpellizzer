Grevillea pauciflora subsp. pauciflora

Grevillea pauciflora subsp.

pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in native gardens.
