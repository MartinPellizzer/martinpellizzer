Grammitis semicrypta

Grammitis semicrypta, commonly known as the hidden fern, is a small, delicate fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that grow in a rosette-like pattern. Grammitis semicrypta is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
