Glossoloma harlequinoides

Glossoloma harlequinoides, commonly known as the harlequin ghost orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, translucent petals that display a vibrant mix of colors, resembling a harlequin's costume. Native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are ephemeral, blooming for only a short period before withering, making it a rare sight in the wild.

Due to habitat loss and its elusive nature, Glossoloma harlequinoides is considered a highly endangered species.
