Geranium pusillum var. rigidum

Geranium pusillum var. rigidum, commonly known as the rigid geranium, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features upright, hairy stems and small, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine, stiff hairs. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers with five petals, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a desirable addition to garden landscapes.
