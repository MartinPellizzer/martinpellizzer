Gyrostachys australis var. amoena

Gyrostachys australis var. amoena, commonly known as the pink flax lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal regions and sandy soils. The plant produces striking pink to purple flowers in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
