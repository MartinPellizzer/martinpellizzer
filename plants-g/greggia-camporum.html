Greggia camporum

Greggia camporum, commonly known as the Camporegio, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with dense, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Greggia camporum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms.
