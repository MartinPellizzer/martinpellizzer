Goeppertia gardneri

Goeppertia gardneri, commonly known as the Gardner's calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, mottled leaves that display a range of colors including green, silver, and red. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
