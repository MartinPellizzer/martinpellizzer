Gloxinia arborea

Gloxinia arborea, commonly known as the tree gloxinia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has a woody stem and can grow up to several meters in height, making it a striking addition to gardens. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Gloxinia arborea thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
