Glomera nana

Glomera nana, commonly known as the dwarf glomera, is a small herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, Glomera nana is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
