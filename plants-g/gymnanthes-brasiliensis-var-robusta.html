Gymnanthes brasiliensis var. robusta

Gymnanthes brasiliensis var. robusta, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or Natal apple, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its fruit.
