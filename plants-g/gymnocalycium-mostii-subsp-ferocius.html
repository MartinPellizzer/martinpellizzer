Gymnocalycium mostii subsp. ferocius

Gymnocalycium mostii subsp.

ferocius, commonly known as the Ferocious Crown of Thorns, is a species of cactus native to Argentina. It is characterized by its spiny, globular stems and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months.

The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.

The subspecies ferocius is distinguished by its more robust growth and longer spines compared to other members of the Gymnocalycium genus.
