Genista richteri

Genista richteri, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not properly managed.
