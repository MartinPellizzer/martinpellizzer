Gastrochilus subbilobus

Gastrochilus subbilobus, commonly known as the two-lobed orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Thailand, Vietnam, and China. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
