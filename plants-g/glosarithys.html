Glosarithys

Glosarithys, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white powder, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its roots are deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater in dry conditions.

Glosarithys is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
