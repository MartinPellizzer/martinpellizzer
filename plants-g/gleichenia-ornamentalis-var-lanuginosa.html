Gleichenia ornamentalis var. lanuginosa

Gleichenia ornamentalis var. lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky gleichenia, is a fern species characterized by its soft, hairy fronds.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Australia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with upright, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its fronds are typically dimorphic, with fertile and sterile leaves differing in size and structure.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal in shaded garden environments.
