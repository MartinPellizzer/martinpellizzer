Geonoma obovata

Geonoma obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate fern, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery, ovate-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to moist soil or rock surfaces.
