Grevillea stenophylla

Grevillea stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and vibrant red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The plant is typically found in heathlands and dry forests, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, featuring a woody stem and dense foliage.

Grevillea stenophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
