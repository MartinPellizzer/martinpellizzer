Genista csikii

Genista csikii, commonly known as Hungarian broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Hungary and parts of southeastern Europe, where it thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender, spiny stems and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers in early spring. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of small, oval leaflets.

Genista csikii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
