Gladiolus muenzneri

Gladiolus muenzneri, commonly known as the Muenzner's gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with a distinctive, narrow, and pointed shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
