Gardenia godefroyana

Gardenia godefroyana, commonly known as the African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is evergreen and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and pleasant scent.

Gardenia godefroyana is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
