Galactia mohlenbrockii

Galactia mohlenbrockii, commonly known as Mohlenbrock's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, round pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
