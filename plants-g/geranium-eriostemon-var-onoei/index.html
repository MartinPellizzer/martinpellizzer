Geranium Eriostemon Var. Onoei

Japanese geranium, scientifically known as Geranium eriostemon var. onoei, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to Japan. It is prized for its vibrant red or pink blooms and its ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings. This variety is a popular choice for both ornamental and landscape use due to its compact growth habit and long blooming period. Its foliage is often noted for its deep green color and textured leaves, adding visual interest to any garden. With proper care, Japanese geranium can become a standout feature in both traditional and modern garden designs.
