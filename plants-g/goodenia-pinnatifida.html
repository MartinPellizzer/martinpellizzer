Goodenia pinnatifida

Goodenia pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in regions with a Mediterranean climate. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a feathery appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
