Ginalloa falcata

Ginalloa falcata, commonly known as the sickleleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and sickle-shaped, giving it its scientific name.

Ginalloa falcata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
