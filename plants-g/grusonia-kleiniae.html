Grusonia kleiniae

Grusonia kleiniae, commonly known as the Kleinia's grass.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value.

Grusonia kleiniae is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid gardens due to its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
