Galearia celebica

Galearia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes starling or starling orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its flowers are often brightly colored, with a mix of white, yellow, and red hues, attracting various pollinators.

Galearia celebica is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in its native habitat.
