Geranium Carolinianum Var. Sphaerospermum

Geranium carolinianum var. sphaerospermum is a rare and charming plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the geraniaceae family and is known for its delicate pink flowers and heart-shaped leaves. this variant is distinguished by its rounded seed pods, which set it apart from other members of the species. it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for conservation efforts.
