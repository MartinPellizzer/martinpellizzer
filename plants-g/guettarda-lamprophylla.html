Guettarda lamprophylla

Guettarda lamprophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf guettarda, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its adaptability to coastal and sandy soils makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Guettarda lamprophylla is also valued for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates with partial shade.
