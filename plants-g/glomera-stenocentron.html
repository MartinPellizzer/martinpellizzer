Glomera stenocentron

Glomera stenocentron, commonly known as the narrow-centered glomerula, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, clustered umbels of white or pale yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
