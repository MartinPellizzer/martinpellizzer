Geryonia crassifolia

Geryonia crassifolia, commonly known as the thick-leaved geryonia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its arid environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
