Gesneria × purpurea

Gesneria × purpurea, commonly known as the purple gesneria, is a hybrid plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple foliage. The plant typically features upright stems with broad, ovate leaves that have a waxy texture. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of orange or yellow, which bloom in the spring.

Gesneria × purpurea thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
