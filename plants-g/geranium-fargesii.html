Geranium fargesii

Geranium fargesii, commonly known as the Sichuan geranium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features serrated, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, producing vibrant pink or red blooms.

Geranium fargesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
