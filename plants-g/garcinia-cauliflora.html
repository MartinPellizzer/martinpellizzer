Garcinia cauliflora

Garcinia cauliflora, commonly known as the pineapple guava, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often fragrant, yellow or orange fruits that resemble pineapples in appearance. The plant produces its flowers and fruits on the same branches, a phenomenon known as cauliflory. Its leaves are typically oval and leathery, with a glossy surface.

Garcinia cauliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also cultivated for its edible fruits.
