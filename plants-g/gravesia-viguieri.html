Gravesia viguieri

Gravesia viguieri, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Gravesia viguieri is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
