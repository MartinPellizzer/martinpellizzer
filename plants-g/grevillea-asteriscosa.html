Grevillea asteriscosa

Grevillea asteriscosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces striking, feather-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance and efficient water use. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Grevillea asteriscosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, providing nectar for native pollinators.
