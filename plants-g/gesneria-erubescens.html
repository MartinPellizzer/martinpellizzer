Gesneria erubescens

Gesneria erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered gesneria, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a rhizomatous evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features broad, ovate leaves with a waxy texture and a striking red or pinkish hue on the undersides. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually pink or red and bloom in the summer months.

Gesneria erubescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is relatively easy to care for in a humid, well-drained environment.
