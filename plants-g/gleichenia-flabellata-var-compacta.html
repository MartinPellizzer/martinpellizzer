Gleichenia flabellata var. compacta

Gleichenia flabellata var. compacta, commonly known as the compact maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.

It is a member of the family Gleicheniaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Australia. The plant features delicate, fern-like fronds with a distinctive, fan-shaped appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
