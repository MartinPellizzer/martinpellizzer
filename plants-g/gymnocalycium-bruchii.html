Gymnocalycium bruchii

Gymnocalycium bruchii, commonly known as the Bruch's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its globular, often ribbed stem that can be green or tinged with red. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Gymnocalycium bruchii is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its compact size and ornamental appearance.
