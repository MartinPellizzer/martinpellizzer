Gymnanthemum solanifolium

Gymnanthemum solanifolium, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet-blue flowers and broad, velvety leaves that turn reddish in autumn. Native to the eastern United States, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has a short growing season and is often found in areas with rich, loamy soil.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for both ornamental and ecological studies.
