Gardenia discolor

Gardenia discolor, commonly known as the white gardenia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, white flowers that are highly fragrant. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces yellowish-white berries that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia discolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
