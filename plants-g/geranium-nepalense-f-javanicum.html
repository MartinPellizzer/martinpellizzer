Geranium nepalense f. javanicum

Geranium nepalense f. javanicum, commonly known as the Javanese geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
