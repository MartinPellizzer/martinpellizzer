Genista erioclada

Genista erioclada, commonly known as the coastal broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils.

Its growth habit makes it a hardy, low-maintenance plant suitable for coastal landscaping.
