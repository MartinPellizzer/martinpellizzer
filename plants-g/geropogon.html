Geropogon

Geropogon, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a central disk, often surrounded by white or pale pink ray florets. Geropogon species are typically shrubs or small trees, characterized by their narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

They are adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making them resilient to drought conditions.
