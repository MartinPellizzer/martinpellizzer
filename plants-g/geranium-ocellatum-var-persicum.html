Geranium ocellatum var. persicum

Geranium ocellatum var. persicum, commonly known as Persian geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting markings. The plant has heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring through early autumn.
