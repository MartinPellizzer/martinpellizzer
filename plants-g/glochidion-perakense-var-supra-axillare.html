Glochidion perakense var. supra-axillare

Glochidion perakense var. supra-axillare, commonly known as the Perak glabrous glochidion, is a shrub native to Malaysia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, ovate leaves with a smooth surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.
