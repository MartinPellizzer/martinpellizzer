Gerbera nervosa

Gerbera nervosa, commonly known as the slender gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with vibrant colors such as yellow, orange, and red. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces showy, single-layered blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical beauty and adaptability contribute to its appeal in both horticulture and botanical studies.
