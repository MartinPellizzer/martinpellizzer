Gagea pamirica

Gagea pamirica, commonly known as the Pamir spring beauty, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the high mountain regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
