Galianthe grandifolia

Galianthe grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered galianthe, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southern United States.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are typically borne on tall, slender stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Galianthe grandifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
