Gasteria spiralis var. tortulata

Gasteria spiralis var. tortulata, commonly known as the twisted gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features short, thick, fleshy leaves that are often twisted or curled, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically a deep green color with a waxy texture, and they form a rosette shape. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, slender stems during the growing season.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental foliage.
