Goodenia laevis subsp. laevis

Goodenia laevis subsp.

laevis, commonly known as the smooth goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers.

The leaves are typically smooth and lance-shaped, with a prominent midrib. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
