Gastrochilus racemifer

Gastrochilus racemifer, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
