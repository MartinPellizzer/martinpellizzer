Grevillea sericea

Grevillea sericea, commonly known as the silky grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its long, silky, silver-green foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, which bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Grevillea sericea is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract native birds and insects.
