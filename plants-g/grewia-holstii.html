Grewia holstii

Grewia holstii, commonly known as the yellow globemallow, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Grewia holstii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
