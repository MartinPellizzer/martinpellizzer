Gerbera delavayi

Gerbera delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's gerbera, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features bright yellow or orange central disks surrounded by white or pink ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Gerbera delavayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
