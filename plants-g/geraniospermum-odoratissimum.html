Geraniospermum odoratissimum

Geraniospermum odoratissimum, commonly known as the fragrant geranium, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy, pinkish-red flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, which contribute to its distinctive scent.

Geraniospermum odoratissimum is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its use in traditional herbal medicine.
