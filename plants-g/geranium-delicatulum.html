Geranium delicatulum

Geranium delicatulum, commonly known as the delicate geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Geranium delicatulum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
