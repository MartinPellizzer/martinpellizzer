Grusonia densispina

Grusonia densispina, commonly known as the dense-spined grusonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, spiny stems and clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Grusonia densispina is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
