Gynostemma pedatum

Gynostemma pedatum, commonly known as the Chinese mountain ginseng, is a climbing vine native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, containing compounds similar to ginseng. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forested areas.
