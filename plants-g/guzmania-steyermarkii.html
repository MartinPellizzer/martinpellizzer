Guzmania steyermarkii

Guzmania steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can range in color from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms and small aquatic life. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in humid environments make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
