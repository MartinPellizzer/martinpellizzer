Gentiana germanica var. praecox

Gentiana germanica var. praecox, commonly known as early gentian, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom early in the summer, typically from June to July. The plant grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
