Gymnocalycium schickendantzii subsp. delaetii

Gymnocalycium schickendantzii subsp.

delaetii, commonly known as the Delaet's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America. It is characterized by its globular shape and a dense covering of white to pale blue spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived but attractive. It is a popular choice for succulent collections due to its compact size and unique appearance.

This subspecies is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.
