Galeopsis × carinthiaca

Galeopsis × carinthiaca, commonly known as the Carpathian hedge-nettle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Galeopsis × carinthiaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting various pollinators.
