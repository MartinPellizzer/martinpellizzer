Globularia trichocalyx

Globularia trichocalyx, commonly known as the woolly globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense, woolly foliage that gives it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Globularia trichocalyx is also valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal in horticulture.
