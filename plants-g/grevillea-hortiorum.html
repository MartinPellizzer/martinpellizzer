Grevillea hortiorum

Grevillea hortiorum, commonly known as the red flowering gum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in dense, cylindrical spikes. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea hortiorum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds.
