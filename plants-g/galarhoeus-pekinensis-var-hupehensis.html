Galarhoeus pekinensis var. hupehensis

Galarhoeus pekinensis var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese tulip tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The tree has smooth, gray bark and heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in urban landscapes.
