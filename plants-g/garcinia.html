Garcinia

Garcinia, commonly known as mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its thick, purple outer skin and segmented, white or pink flesh. The plant is valued for its fruit, which contains high levels of xanthones and other bioactive compounds. Garcinia species are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The tree's botanical features include glossy leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom before the fruit develops.
