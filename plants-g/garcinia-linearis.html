Garcinia linearis

Garcinia linearis, commonly known as the slender garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces elliptical, orange-red fruits that contain a single seed. Garcinia linearis is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for ecological and horticultural study.
