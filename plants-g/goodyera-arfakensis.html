Goodyera arfakensis

Goodyera arfakensis, commonly known as the Arfak gesellia, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and delicate, white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, drawing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat.

Due to its restricted range and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
