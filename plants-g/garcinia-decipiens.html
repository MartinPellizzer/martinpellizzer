Garcinia decipiens

Garcinia decipiens, commonly known as the false mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical, leathery leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, while the fruit is a round, segmented berry that turns from green to yellow when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
