Geranium alternans

Geranium alternans, commonly known as the zigzag geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often wrinkled and have a distinctive zigzag pattern along the midrib. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological gardens.
