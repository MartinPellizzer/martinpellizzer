Galactia jussiaeana var. volubilis

Galactia jussiaeana var. volubilis, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in warm climates and is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

Its vigorous growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental and agricultural purposes.
