Garcinia moaensis

Garcinia moaensis, commonly known as the Moa Mountain Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically glossy and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or white in color. Its fruit is a small, orange-red berry that contains a single seed.

Garcinia moaensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
