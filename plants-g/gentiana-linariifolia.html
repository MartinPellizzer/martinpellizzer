Gentiana linariifolia

Gentiana linariifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, known for its showy blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

The flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
