Gymnocalycium mihanovichii var. piraretaense

Gymnocalycium mihanovichii var. piraretaense, commonly known as the Piaroara cactus, is a species native to northern Argentina.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and a green to blue-green color. The plant features short, spiny stems and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
