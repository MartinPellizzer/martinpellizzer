Galega astragaloides

Galega astragaloides, commonly known as the hairy milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds, and its leaves are trifoliate with hairy surfaces. Galega astragaloides is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.

It has been used traditionally in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
