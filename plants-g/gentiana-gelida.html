Gentiana gelida

Gentiana gelida, commonly known as the alpine gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Northern Hemisphere.

It thrives in cool, moist environments such as alpine meadows and rocky slopes. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Gentiana gelida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
