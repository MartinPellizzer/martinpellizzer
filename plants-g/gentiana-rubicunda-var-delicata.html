Gentiana rubicunda var. delicata

Gentiana rubicunda var. delicata, commonly known as the delicate gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are often hairy.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
