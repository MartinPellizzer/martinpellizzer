Gonolobus altatensis

Gonolobus altatensis, commonly known as the mountain morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
