Grevillea wickhamii subsp. cratista

Grevillea wickhamii subsp.

cratista, commonly known as the Wickham's Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance. It is often found in sandy soils and is a characteristic component of the flora in the Geraldton area.

The subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in local ecosystems.
