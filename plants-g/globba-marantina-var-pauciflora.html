Globba marantina var. pauciflora

Globba marantina var. pauciflora, commonly known as the slender flame plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features upright, tuberous rhizomes and produces striking, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive red or purple midrib. The plant typically blooms with small, clustered inflorescences that are often hidden by its foliage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for indoor and shaded garden settings.
