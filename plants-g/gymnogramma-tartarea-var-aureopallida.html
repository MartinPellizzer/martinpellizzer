Gymnogramma tartarea var. aureopallida

Gymnogramma tartarea var. aureopallida, commonly known as the golden-pale gymnogramma, is a varietal form of the fern Gymnogramma tartarea.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically light green to golden in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a slender, wiry appearance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture due to its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
