Gazania armerioides

Gazania armerioides, commonly known as the coastal gazania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features bright yellow or orange flowers with a distinctive central disc, blooming from spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to withstand salt spray and arid conditions.

Gazania armerioides is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
