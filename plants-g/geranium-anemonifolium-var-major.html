Geranium anemonifolium var. major

Geranium anemonifolium var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered geranium, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
