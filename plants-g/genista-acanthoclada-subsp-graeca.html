Genista acanthoclada subsp. graeca

Genista acanthoclada subsp.

graeca, commonly known as the Greek bursch. It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its small, spiny leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant thrives in rocky and dry soils, often found in scrublands and coastal areas. Its subspecies status highlights its distinct morphological traits compared to other members of the Genista genus.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in arid climates.
