Graphorkis burmanica

Graphorkis burmanica, commonly known as the Burmese orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
