Gnaphalium argentifolium

Gnaphalium argentifolium, commonly known as silverleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, prairies, and disturbed soils.

Silverleaf fleabane is often used in herbal medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
