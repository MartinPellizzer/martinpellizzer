Glochidion puberulum

Glochidion puberulum, commonly known as the velvet leaf glabrous, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, pubescent foliage. The leaves are typically alternate, elliptical, and have a leathery texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by orange-red berries that are edible for some bird species.
