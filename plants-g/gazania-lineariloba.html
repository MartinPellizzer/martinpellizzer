Gazania lineariloba

Gazania lineariloba, commonly known as the narrowleaf gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange daisy-like flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Gazania lineariloba is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
