Gomesa bohnkiana

Gomesa bohnkiana, commonly known as the golden cup plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flower heads, which are composed of numerous small, tubular florets. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

Gomesa bohnkiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.
