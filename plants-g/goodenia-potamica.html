Goodenia potamica

Goodenia potamica, commonly known as the river goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often tubular or urn-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along riverbanks and in open habitats. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while the flowers range in color from white to pink or purple.

Goodenia potamica is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
