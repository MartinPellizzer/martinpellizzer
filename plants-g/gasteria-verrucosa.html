Gasteria verrucosa

Gasteria verrucosa, commonly known as the疣叶猪牙花, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white or yellow tubercles. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
