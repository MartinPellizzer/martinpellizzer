Gastrochilus scaphochlamys

Gastrochilus scaphochlamys, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Thailand, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive shape. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories.

The orchid's botanical features make it a popular subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
