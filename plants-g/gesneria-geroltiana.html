Gesneria geroltiana

Gesneria geroltiana, commonly known as the Gerolt's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Gesneria geroltiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
