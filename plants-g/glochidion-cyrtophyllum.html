Glochidion cyrtophyllum

Glochidion cyrtophyllum, commonly known as the glossy leaf glochidion, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually glossy and elliptical in shape. Its small white or yellowish flowers are clustered in inflorescences, and it produces small, round fruits that contain seeds.

Glochidion cyrtophyllum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
