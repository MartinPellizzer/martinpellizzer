Geranium sessiliflorum var. compactum

Geranium sessiliflorum var. compactum, commonly known as the compact geranium, is a compact, low-growing perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to gardens with limited space.
