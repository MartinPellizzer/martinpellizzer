Gardenia colnettiana

Gardenia colnettiana, commonly known as the Colnett's gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are known for their strong, sweet scent, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The fruit produced by the plant is a fleshy, yellow berry that contains several seeds.
