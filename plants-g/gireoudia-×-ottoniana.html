Gireoudia × ottoniana

Gireoudia × ottoniana, commonly known as the hybrid gireoudia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent when crushed.

Gireoudia × ottoniana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
