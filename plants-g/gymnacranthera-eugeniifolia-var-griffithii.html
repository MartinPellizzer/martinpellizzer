Gymnacranthera eugeniifolia var. griffithii

Gymnacranthera eugeniifolia var. griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's variant of the Eugenia-flowered gymnacranthera, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface. This variant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, particularly in parts of India and Myanmar.

It is often found in moist, shaded forest environments, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitats.
