Genista-spartium

Genista-spartium, commonly known as Spanish broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, spiny stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves and is adapted to dry, rocky soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.

However, it can become invasive in some areas if not managed properly.
