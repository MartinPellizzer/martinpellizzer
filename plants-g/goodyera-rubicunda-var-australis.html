Goodyera rubicunda var. australis

Goodyera rubicunda var. australis, commonly known as the Australian lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and its striking, lance-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky outcrops. Its flowers are arranged in a loose raceme and are often pink or reddish in color.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial shade.
