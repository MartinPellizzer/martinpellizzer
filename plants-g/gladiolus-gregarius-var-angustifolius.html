Gladiolus gregarius var. angustifolius

Gladiolus gregarius var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gladiolus, is a botanical variety native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and striking, brightly colored flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for floral arrangements.
