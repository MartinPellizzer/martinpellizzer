Guettarda angelica

Guettarda angelica, commonly known as angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.
