Glechon discolor

Glechon discolor, commonly known as the silverleaf gloxinia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Glechon discolor is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
