Gerbera elliptica

Gerbera elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with bright colors such as red, yellow, and orange. The plant features narrow, elliptical leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Gerbera elliptica is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
