Goodenia cyanea

Goodenia cyanea, commonly known as the blue goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Goodenia cyanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
