Gouinia latifolia var. guatemalensis

Gouinia latifolia var. guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan broadleaf gouinia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as rainforests. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
