Gazania kraussii

Gazania kraussii, commonly known as the yellow gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with fleshy, succulent leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Gazania kraussii is drought-tolerant and often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive blooms.
