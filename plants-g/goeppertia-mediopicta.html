Goeppertia mediopicta, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a striking member of the Zingiberaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, mottled leaves that display a mix of green, white, and purple hues, creating a zebra-like pattern. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months.

Goeppertia mediopicta thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush, decorative appearance.
