Gilia Cana Var. Speciosa

Gilia cana var. speciosa is a striking flowering plant native to the western united states. it belongs to the plantaginaceae family and is known for its vibrant purple and pink blooms. this variety is often found in rocky habitats and open grasslands. its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and ecological value.
