Genista salzmannii subsp. salzmannii

Genista salzmannii subsp.

salzmannii, commonly known as Salzmann's broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant produces small, leathery pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its ornamental value is appreciated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
