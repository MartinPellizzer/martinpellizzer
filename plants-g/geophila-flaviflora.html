Geophila flaviflora

Geophila flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered geophila, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Geophila flaviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural gardens.
