Gerbera galpinii

Gerbera galpinii, commonly known as the Galpin's gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features bright yellow to orange petals surrounding a central disk of dark brown to black florets. Gerbera galpinii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in various horticultural settings.
