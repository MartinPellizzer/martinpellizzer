Gonolobus billbergianus

Gonolobus billbergianus, commonly known as the Brazilian morning glory, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Due to its rapid growth and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
