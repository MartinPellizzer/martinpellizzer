Geranium gracilentum

Geranium gracilentum, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Geranium gracilentum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
