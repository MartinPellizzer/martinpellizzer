Gentiana borneensis subsp. borneensis

Gentiana borneensis subsp.

borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean gentian, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
