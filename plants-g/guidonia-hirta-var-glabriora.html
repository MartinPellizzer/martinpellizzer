Guidonia hirta var. glabriora

Guidonia hirta var. glabriora, commonly known as the smooth hirta, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy stems, though the varietal form glabriora is distinguished by its smoother, less hairy foliage. This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
