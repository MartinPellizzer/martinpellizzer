Goodenia robusta

Goodenia robusta, commonly known as the robust goodenia, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and heathlands. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in spring and attract various pollinators.

Goodenia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
