Gorteria heterophylla

Gorteria heterophylla, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant has basal leaves that are typically linear to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Gorteria heterophylla is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
