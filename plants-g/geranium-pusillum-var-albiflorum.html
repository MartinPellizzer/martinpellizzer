Geranium pusillum var. albiflorum

Geranium pusillum var. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, rounded leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice among gardeners.
