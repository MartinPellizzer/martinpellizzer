Grevillea baxteri

Grevillea baxteri, commonly known as the Baxter's grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea baxteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
