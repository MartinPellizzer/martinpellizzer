Gastoniella ascensionis

Gastoniella ascensionis, commonly known as the Ascension Island starflower, is a flowering plant native to the remote Ascension Island in the South Atlantic Ocean.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom during the cooler months. The plant thrives in the nutrient-poor soils of its volcanic habitat and is adapted to the island's unique climate. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping it retain moisture in the arid environment.

Gastoniella ascensionis is of particular interest to botanists due to its endemic status and limited distribution.
