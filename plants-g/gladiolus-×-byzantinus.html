Gladiolus × byzantinus

Gladiolus × byzantinus, commonly known as the Byzantine gladiolus, is a hybrid plant in the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often large flowers that come in a variety of hues. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with sword-shaped leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern. Its bulbous roots store nutrients, supporting the plant's growth and flowering cycle.

The flowers are borne on tall, sturdy stems and are often used in ornamental gardening for their beauty and longevity.
