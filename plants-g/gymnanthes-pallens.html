Gymnanthes pallens

Gymnanthes pallens, commonly known as the yellow star apple, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with smooth, pale bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright yellow, star-shaped fruits. The fruits are edible, with a sweet, juicy pulp that contains numerous seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and culinary uses in its native regions.
