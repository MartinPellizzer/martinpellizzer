Gisekia pharnaceoides var. alata

Gisekia pharnaceoides var. alata, commonly known as the winged gisekia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive wing-like structures on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
