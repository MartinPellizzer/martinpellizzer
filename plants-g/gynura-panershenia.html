Gynura panershenia

Gynura panershenia, commonly known as the velvetleaf gynura, is a tropical plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in composite inflorescences, which are typical of the daisy family. It is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
