Gesneria gardneri

Gesneria gardneri, commonly known as the Gardner's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which is known for its ornamental and often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, featuring glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate. Its flowers are usually tubular or bell-shaped, with vibrant colors such as red, orange, or yellow, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.

Gesneria gardneri is appreciated in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
