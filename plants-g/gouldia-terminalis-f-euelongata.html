Gouldia terminalis f. euelongata

Gouldia terminalis f. euelongata, commonly known as the long-stemmed gouldia, is a rare and striking cultivar of the gouldia genus.

It is characterized by its elongated, gracefully arching stems that can reach impressive heights, making it a standout in ornamental gardens. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, adding a soft, ethereal beauty to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal maintenance once established.

This cultivar is prized for its unique form and is often used in modern garden designs for its visual impact.
