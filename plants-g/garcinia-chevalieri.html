Garcinia chevalieri

Garcinia chevalieri, commonly known as the African yellow wood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a yellow, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

Garcinia chevalieri is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
