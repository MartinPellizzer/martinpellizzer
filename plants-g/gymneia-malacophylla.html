Gymneia malacophylla

Gymneia malacophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to Southeast Asia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clustered, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs. Gymneia malacophylla thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
