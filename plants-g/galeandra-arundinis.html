Galeandra arundinis

Galeandra arundinis, commonly known as the bamboo orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Galeandra arundinis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
