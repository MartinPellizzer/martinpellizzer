Geranium yesoense

Geranium yesoense, commonly known as the Yesso geranium, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces clusters of five-petaled flowers in shades of pink or red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
