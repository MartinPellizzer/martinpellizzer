Gardenia transvenulosa

Gardenia transvenulosa, commonly known as the Transvenulose Gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
