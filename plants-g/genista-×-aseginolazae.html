Genista × aseginolazae

Genista × aseginolazae, commonly known as the hybrid buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by small, dark purple pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens despite its potential to become invasive.
