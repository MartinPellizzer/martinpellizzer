Gnidia claessensii

Gnidia claessensii, commonly known as the Natal gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
