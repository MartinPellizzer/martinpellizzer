Gentiana × macaulayi

Gentiana × macaulayi, commonly known as the Macaulay gentian, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, known for its showy, often blue or purple flowers.

Gentiana × macaulayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
