Genista moleroi

Genista moleroi, commonly known as the Molero's broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to Mediterranean climates. Its yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
