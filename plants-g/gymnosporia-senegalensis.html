Gymnosporia senegalensis

Gymnosporia senegalensis, commonly known as the Senegal acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in dry, sandy soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
