Gasteria nitida var. grandipunctata

Gasteria nitida var. grandipunctata, commonly known as the Cape Gasteria, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white to yellowish marginal teeth. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white, tubular flowers. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
