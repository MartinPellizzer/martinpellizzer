Glochidion bifarium

Glochidion bifarium, commonly known as the two-lobed glabrous tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, often glabrous bark and elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.
