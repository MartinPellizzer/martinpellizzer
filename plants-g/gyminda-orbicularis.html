Gyminda orbicularis

Gyminda orbicularis, commonly known as the globe-shaped orchid, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rounded, orbicular leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Gyminda orbicularis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialty plant collections.
