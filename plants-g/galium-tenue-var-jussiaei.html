Galium tenue var. jussiaei

Galium tenue var. jussiaei, commonly known as slender bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, and it produces tiny white to pale yellow flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its stems are slender and often prostrate, trailing along the ground.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
