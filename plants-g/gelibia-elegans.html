Gelibia elegans

Gelibia elegans, commonly known as the elegant gelibia, is a unique plant species characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves and striking, elongated flowers.

It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant's botanical features include a fibrous root system and a short, upright stem. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and exhibit a slight waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Gelibia elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic and ecological significance.
