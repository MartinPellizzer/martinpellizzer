Genista acanthoclada var. balearica

Genista acanthoclada var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic broom, is a shrub native to the Balearic Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of several narrow, pointed leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
