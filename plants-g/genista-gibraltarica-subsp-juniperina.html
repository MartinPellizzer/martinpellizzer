Genista gibraltarica subsp. juniperina

Genista gibraltarica subsp.

juniperina, commonly known as Gibraltar juniperbush, is a shrub native to the rocky coastal areas of southern Spain. It is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is adapted to harsh, sunny environments and is often found growing on limestone soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in landscaping for its hardy nature and ornamental value.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
