Gomphrena perennis var. saxatilis

Gomphrena perennis var. saxatilis, commonly known as the rock gomphrena, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, feathery bracts that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is drought-tolerant and often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.

The flowers bloom in summer, adding a striking visual appeal to gardens and natural landscapes.
