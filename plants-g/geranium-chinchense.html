Geranium chinchense

Geranium chinchense, commonly known as the Christmas geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has serrated, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and foliage.

Geranium chinchense is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
