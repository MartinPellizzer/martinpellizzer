Grewia Biloba Var. Parviflora

Grewia biloba var. parviflora is a lesser-known but strikingly beautiful shrub native to southern Africa. This variant of the Grewia biloba species is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and vibrant, red-orange fruits that attract a variety of wildlife. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often found in coastal and savanna regions. Despite its ornamental value, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and restoration projects.
