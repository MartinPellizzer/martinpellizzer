Geranium aethiopicum

Geranium aethiopicum, commonly known as the African geranium, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a perennial herb with upright stems and opposite leaves that are usually lance-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Geranium aethiopicum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can also be grown as a container plant.
