Gymnocalycium lagunillasense

Gymnocalycium lagunillasense, commonly known as the Lagunilla cactus, is a species of cactus native to northern Chile.

It is a small, globular plant with a ribbed structure and typically produces pale yellow flowers. The plant has spines that are often white or pale in color, and it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Gymnocalycium lagunillasense is well adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and low rainfall, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
