Gentiana vernayi

Gentiana vernayi, commonly known as the alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine downy layer.

Gentiana vernayi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
