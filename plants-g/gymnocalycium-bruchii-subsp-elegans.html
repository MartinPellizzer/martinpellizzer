Gymnocalycium bruchii subsp. elegans

Gymnocalycium bruchii subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Moon Cactus, is a succulent cactus native to Argentina. It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange.

The plant has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stem. It produces small, spiny areoles and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance. Gymnocalycium bruchii subsp.

elegans requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
