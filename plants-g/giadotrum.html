Giadotrum

Giadotrum, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Giadotrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
