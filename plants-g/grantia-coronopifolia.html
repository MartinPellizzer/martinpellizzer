Grantia coronopifolia

Grantia coronopifolia, commonly known as the mountain holly.

This evergreen shrub is native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a waxy texture and a dark green color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
