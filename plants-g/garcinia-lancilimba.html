Garcinia lancilimba

Garcinia lancilimba, commonly known as the slender Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange-red berries that contain a single seed. Garcinia lancilimba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and unique fruit.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
