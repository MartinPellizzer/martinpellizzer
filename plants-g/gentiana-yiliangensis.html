Gentiana yiliangensis

Gentiana yiliangensis, commonly known as the Yiliang gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and meadows. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are erect and branched.

Gentiana yiliangensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
