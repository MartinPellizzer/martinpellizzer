Gnetum leptostachyum var. abbreviatum

Gnetum leptostachyum var. abbreviatum, commonly known as the dwarf gnetum, is a rare and unique gymnosperm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and distinctive, often clustered, cone-like structures. The plant exhibits a unique combination of traits, including a vascular system similar to that of angiosperms. It is typically found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
