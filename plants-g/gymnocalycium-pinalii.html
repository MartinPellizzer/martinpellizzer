Gymnocalycium pinalii

Gymnocalycium pinalii, commonly known as the Pinalii moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular, ribbed stem and is typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Gymnocalycium pinalii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and compact size.
