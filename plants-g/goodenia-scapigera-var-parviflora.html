Goodenia scapigera var. parviflora

Goodenia scapigera var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, often with a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grassy areas.

This variety is distinguished by its smaller floral display compared to other varieties of Goodenia scapigera.
