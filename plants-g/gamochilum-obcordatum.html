Gamochilum obcordatum

Gamochilum obcordatum, commonly known as the obcordate leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
