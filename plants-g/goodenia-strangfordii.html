Goodenia strangfordii

Goodenia strangfordii, commonly known as the Strangford goodenia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in coastal and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
