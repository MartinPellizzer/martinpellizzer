Gaertneria ambrosiodes

Gaertneria ambrosiodes, commonly known as the golden star ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Gaertneria ambrosiodes is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and vibrant floral display.
