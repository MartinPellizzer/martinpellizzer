Goodenia nicolsonii

Goodenia nicolsonii, commonly known as the Nicholson's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, clustered flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
