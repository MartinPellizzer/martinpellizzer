Gardenia sokotensis

Gardenia sokotensis, commonly known as the Sokotan gardenia, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, dark green leaves and its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces bright yellow berries that contain a single seed, which is dispersed by birds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils and coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable component of Socotra's biodiversity.
