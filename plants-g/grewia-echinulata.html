Grewia echinulata

Grewia echinulata, commonly known as the spiny globemallow, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, red fruits that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Grewia echinulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
