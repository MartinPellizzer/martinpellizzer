Grevillea insignis

Grevillea insignis, commonly known as the pink waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, red or pink flower spikes that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Grevillea insignis is also noted for its ability to attract native birds and insects due to its nectar-rich flowers.
