Gymnopodium floribundum

Gymnopodium floribundum, commonly known as the showy gymnopodium, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with alternating leaflets and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its root system is well-developed, allowing it to thrive in various soil types.
