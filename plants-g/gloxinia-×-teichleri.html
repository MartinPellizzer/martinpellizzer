Gloxinia × teichleri

Gloxinia × teichleri, commonly known as the hybrid gloxinia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Gloxinia bolanderi and Gloxinia moschata.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant is native to South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Gloxinia × teichleri is typically grown as a houseplant and requires regular watering and moderate humidity to maintain its health.
