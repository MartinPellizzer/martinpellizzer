Gomphrena meyeniana var. meyeniana

Gomphrena meyeniana var. meyeniana, commonly known as the common gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery bracts that range in color from red to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its small, clustered flowers are surrounded by colorful, papery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
