Gomphrena perennis var. silenoides

Gomphrena perennis var. silenoides, commonly known as the silverleaf gomphrena, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, spiky flower heads in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom from late summer to early autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a versatile addition to xeriscapic landscapes.
