Galathea

Galathea, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its showy, hibiscus-like flowers. Galathea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to salt spray and strong winds.
