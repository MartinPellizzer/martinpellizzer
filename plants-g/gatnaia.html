Gatnaia

Gatnaia, commonly known as the blue bottle plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling small bottles. The plant has a succulent stem and leaves that are typically fleshy and thick, adapted to store water in arid environments. Gatnaia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
