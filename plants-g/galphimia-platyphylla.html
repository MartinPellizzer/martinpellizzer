Galphimia platyphylla

Galphimia platyphylla, commonly known as the flatleaf galphimia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America and northern South America.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
