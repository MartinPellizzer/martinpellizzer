Gomesa ottonis

Gomesa ottonis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its bark is smooth and grayish in color. Gomesa ottonis is adapted to warm, humid climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
