Geranium brutium

Geranium brutium, commonly known as the Balkan geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces clusters of five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Geranium brutium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
