Gymnocalycium sutterianum subsp. dolezalii

Gymnocalycium sutterianum subsp.

dolezalii, commonly known as the Sutter's moon cactus, is a compact cactus native to South America, particularly Argentina. It features a short, thick stem covered in white to pale blue spines, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
