Grevillea alpestris var. helianthemifolia

Grevillea alpestris var. helianthemifolia, commonly known as the Alpine Grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically grey-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in winter, which are clustered in cylindrical spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
