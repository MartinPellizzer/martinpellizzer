Geranium nodosum subsp. eugeniae

Geranium nodosum subsp.

eugeniae, commonly known as the Eugenia geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from pink to red, and it blooms during the warmer months.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
