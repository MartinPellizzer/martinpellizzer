Geranium ruprechtii

Geranium ruprechtii, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright, hairy stems and produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink or red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered with fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
