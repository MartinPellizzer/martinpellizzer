Geerinckia couloniana

Geerinckia couloniana, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale pink with contrasting purple or brown markings. The plant produces a compact, upright growth with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in botanical collections and conservation efforts.
