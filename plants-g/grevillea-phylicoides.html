Grevillea phylicoides

Grevillea phylicoides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically grey-green in color, and it produces striking red or orange cylindrical flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Grevillea phylicoides is also an important plant for local wildlife, providing nectar for birds and insects.
