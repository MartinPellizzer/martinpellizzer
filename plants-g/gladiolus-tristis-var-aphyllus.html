Gladiolus tristis var. aphyllus

Gladiolus tristis var. aphyllus, commonly known as the leafless gladiolus, is a rare and unique variant of the gladiolus species.

It is characterized by its absence of leaves, which sets it apart from typical gladiolus plants. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are usually a deep red or purple in color. It is native to certain regions of Africa and is often found in specific ecological niches.

Due to its rarity and distinctive features, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
