Gymnostachyum sinense

Gymnostachyum sinense, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the arum family. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Gymnostachyum sinense is valued for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
