Gladiolus × sulphureus

Gladiolus × sulphureus, commonly known as the sulphur yellow gladiolus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that typically range from yellow to orange, with some varieties exhibiting a deep red or purple hue. The plant features tall, sturdy stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, supporting its showy inflorescence. Its leaves are long and narrow, arranged in a fan-like pattern, and the flowers are composed of three petals and three sepals that are often similar in size and color.

This ornamental plant is widely cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is popular in gardens and floral arrangements.
