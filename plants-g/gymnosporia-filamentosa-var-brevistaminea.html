Gymnosporia filamentosa var. brevistaminea

Gymnosporia filamentosa var. brevistaminea, commonly known as the narrow-staminal gymnosporia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid environments.
