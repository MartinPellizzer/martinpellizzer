Goodenia rotundifolia var. hirsuta

Goodenia rotundifolia var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its rounded, lobed leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
