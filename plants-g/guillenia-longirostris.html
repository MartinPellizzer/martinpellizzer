Guillenia longirostris

Guillenia longirostris, commonly known as the long-beaked guillenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. Guillenia longirostris is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
