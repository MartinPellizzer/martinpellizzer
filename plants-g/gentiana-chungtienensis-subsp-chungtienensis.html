Gentiana chungtienensis subsp. chungtienensis

Gentiana chungtienensis subsp.

chungtienensis, commonly known as the Chungtien gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
