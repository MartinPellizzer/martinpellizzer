Gastrochilus malipoensis

Gastrochilus malipoensis, commonly known as the Malipo orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, where it thrives in humid, mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. It is often found growing on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
