Gentiana urnula

Gentiana urnula, commonly known as the bell gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentiana urnula is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
