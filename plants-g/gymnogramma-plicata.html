Gymnogramma plicata

Gymnogramma plicata, commonly known as the pleated maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that have a distinctive pleated or folded appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Gymnogramma plicata requires well-drained soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
