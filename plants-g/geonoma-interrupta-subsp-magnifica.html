Geonoma interrupta subsp. magnifica

Geonoma interrupta subsp.

magnifica, commonly known as the magnifica fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive fronds with a complex, lace-like pattern.
