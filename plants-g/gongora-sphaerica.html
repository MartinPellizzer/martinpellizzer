Gongora sphaerica

Gongora sphaerica, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, globular shape and striking, colorful flowers that bloom in a variety of hues. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, while its roots are specialized for nutrient absorption in poor soil.

Gongora sphaerica is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and rarity.
