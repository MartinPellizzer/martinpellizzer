Guarea mexicana

Guarea mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican guarea, is a tropical tree native to Central America and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other hardwood trees. The tree is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in axillary inflorescences.
