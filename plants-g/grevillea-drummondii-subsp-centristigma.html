Grevillea drummondii subsp. centristigma

Grevillea drummondii subsp.

centristigma, commonly known as the Drummond's Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces striking, red or orange flowers in clusters.

The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in heathlands and open woodlands, and it plays a role in supporting local wildlife.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
