Geranium geissei

Geranium geissei, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. Geranium geissei is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
