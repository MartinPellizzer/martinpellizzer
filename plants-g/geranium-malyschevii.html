Geranium malyschevii

Geranium malyschevii, commonly known as the Malyschev's geranium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Geranium malyschevii is also known for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
