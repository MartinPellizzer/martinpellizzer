Gladiolus amplifolius

Gladiolus amplifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features tall, upright stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, supporting its vibrant blooms. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its dramatic appearance.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
