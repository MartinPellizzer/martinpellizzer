Gastrochilus grandifolius

Gastrochilus grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf stomach orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Gastrochilus grandifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
