Grevea Eggelingii Var. Keniensis

Grevea eggelingii var. keniensis is a rare and unique plant species native to the highlands of Kenya. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often strikingly ornamental members. This variant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and vibrant, yellowish-green foliage. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied and is seldom found in cultivation. Its conservation status highlights the importance of protecting such specialized flora in its natural habitat.
