Geranium columbinum var. diffusum

Geranium columbinum var. diffusum, commonly known as the spreading geranium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems that branch near the base, supporting clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant has heart-shaped to ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it produces distinctive two-lobed seed capsules. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and delicate blossoms.
