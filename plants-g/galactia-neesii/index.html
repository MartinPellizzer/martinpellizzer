Galactia Neesii

Nees' galactia, scientifically known as Galactia neesii, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and along riverbanks. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important ecological role in nitrogen fixation and supports various pollinators. Its unique botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
