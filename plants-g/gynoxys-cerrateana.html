Gynoxys cerrateana

Gynoxys cerrateana, commonly known as the Brazilian buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are alternate and have a heart-shaped base, often covered in fine hairs.

Gynoxys cerrateana thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
