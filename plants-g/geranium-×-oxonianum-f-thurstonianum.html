Geranium × oxonianum f. thurstonianum

Geranium × oxonianum f. thurstonianum, commonly known as the Thurstonian geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of showy, pink to red flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a serrated edge, while the stems are often hairy. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is often used in borders and containers due to its compact size and long blooming period.
