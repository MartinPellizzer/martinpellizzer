Gardenia vilhelmii

Gardenia vilhelmii, commonly known as the dwarf gardenia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a waxy texture. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

Gardenia vilhelmii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
