Gesneria chelonioides

Gesneria chelonioides, commonly known as the horned gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like tubers that store nutrients and water. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, with a deep green color.

It produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
