Gibbaeum album var. roseum

Gibbaeum album var. roseum, commonly known as the pink starfish plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that bear small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to white. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
