Gymnolomia patens

Gymnolomia patens, commonly known as the broadleaf tree fern, is a striking member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in a thick layer of persistent, leathery leaves. Its fronds are large, arching, and often have a distinctive, broad shape.

Gymnolomia patens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in botanical gardens.
