Gardenia merrillii

Gardenia merrillii, commonly known as the Philippine gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia merrillii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
