Gordonia robusta

Gordonia robusta, commonly known as the broadleaf gumbo or broadleaf gordonia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.

Gordonia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
