Gazania araneosa

Gazania araneosa, commonly known as the spider gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with hairy, gray-green leaves. Its flowers are typically borne on long, slender stems and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Gazania araneosa is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance.
