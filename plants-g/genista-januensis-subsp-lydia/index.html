Genista Januensis Subsp. Lydia

Genista januensis subsp. lydia is a unique shrub native to the rocky hills of southern Europe. It is known for its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. This subspecies thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive addition to gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant in botanical circles.
