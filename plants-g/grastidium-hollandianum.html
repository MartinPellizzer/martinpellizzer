Grastidium hollandianum

Grastidium hollandianum, commonly known as the Dutch grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Grastidium hollandianum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
