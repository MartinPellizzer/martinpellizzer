Genista tinctoria var. perreymondii

Genista tinctoria var. perreymondii, commonly known as the Perreymond's yellow broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soils.

This variety is a subspecies of the common yellow broom, distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and floral characteristics.
