Gesneria bonaoana

Gesneria bonaoana, commonly known as the Bonaoana gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the island of Hispaniola.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, thriving in the humid, shaded environments of tropical forests. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Gesneria bonaoana is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
