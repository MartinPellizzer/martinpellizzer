Gomesa florida

Gomesa florida, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gomesa florida is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.
