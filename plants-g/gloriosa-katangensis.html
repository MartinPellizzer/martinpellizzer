Gloriosa katangensis

Gloriosa katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga lily, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Colocasiaceae and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features long, slender stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its flowering period typically occurs during the warm season, and it is cultivated for its ornamental value.

However, it is important to note that all parts of the plant contain toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
