Galeorchis spectabilis

Galeorchis spectabilis, commonly known as the showy galeorchis, is a perennial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions, making it a popular choice for gardeners interested in native flora.
