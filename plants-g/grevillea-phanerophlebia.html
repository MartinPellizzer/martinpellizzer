Grevillea phanerophlebia

Grevillea phanerophlebia, commonly known as the red flowering grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that appear in dense, cylindrical clusters. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically grey-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea phanerophlebia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
