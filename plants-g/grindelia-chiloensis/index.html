Grindelia Chiloensis

Grindelia chiloensis is a hardy shrub native to south-central Chile and northern Argentina. It is known for its distinctive yellow flowers and ability to thrive in arid environments. This plant has been used traditionally by indigenous peoples for its medicinal properties. Its resilience makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and erosion control. Grindelia chiloensis is gaining attention for its ecological and horticultural value.
