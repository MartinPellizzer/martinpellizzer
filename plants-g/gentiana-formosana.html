Gentiana formosana

Gentiana formosana, commonly known as the Formosan gentian, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Gentiana formosana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also of interest in botanical research due to its unique adaptations to its native habitat.
