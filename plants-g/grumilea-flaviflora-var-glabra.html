Grumilea flaviflora var. glabra

Grumilea flaviflora var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-flowered grumilea, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glabrous (smooth) leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and a woody stem structure.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
