Glycosmis arborea

Glycosmis arborea, commonly known as the fragrant evergreen or wild lime, is a small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves contain essential oils, which contribute to its distinctive fragrance and medicinal properties.

Glycosmis arborea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
