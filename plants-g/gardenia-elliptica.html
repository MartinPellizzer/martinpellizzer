Gardenia elliptica

Gardenia elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, white or yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and pleasant scent.

Gardenia elliptica is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
