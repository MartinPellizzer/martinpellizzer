Galium verum f. trachycarpum

Galium verum f. trachycarpum, commonly known as the rough-seeded bedstraw, is a variant of the common bedstraw.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
