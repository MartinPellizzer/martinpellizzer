Goodenia nigrescens

Goodenia nigrescens, commonly known as the blackening goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in spring and attract various pollinators.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
