Grevillea prominens

Grevillea prominens, commonly known as the serrated grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It features vibrant, feather-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are often serrated, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Grevillea prominens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
