Goniophlebium amoenum

Goniophlebium amoenum, commonly known as the Amoena fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically arching or drooping, giving it an elegant appearance. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
