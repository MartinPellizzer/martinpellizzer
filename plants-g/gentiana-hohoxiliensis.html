Gentiana hohoxiliensis

Gentiana hohoxiliensis, commonly known as the Hohoxil gentian, is a striking alpine plant native to the Tibetan Plateau.

It is characterized by its deep blue to violet flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Gentiana hohoxiliensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to high-altitude environments.
