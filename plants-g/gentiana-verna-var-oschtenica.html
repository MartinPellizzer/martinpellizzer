Gentiana verna var. oschtenica

Gentiana verna var. oschtenica, commonly known as the spring gentian, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, while its stems are usually unbranched.

It blooms in early spring, often before the leaves fully develop, making it an early indicator of seasonal change.
