Gastridium lendigerum var. scabrum

Gastridium lendigerum var. scabrum, commonly known as the rough gastridium, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are often covered in a rough, scabrous texture, which gives it its specific epithet "scabrum.".

The species is native to parts of southern Africa, particularly South Africa.
