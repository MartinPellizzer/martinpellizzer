Goodenia fascicularis

Goodenia fascicularis, commonly known as the clustered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky habitats.

Goodenia fascicularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
