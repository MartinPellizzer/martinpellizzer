Galeopsis × ochrerythrea

Galeopsis × ochrerythrea, commonly known as the hybrid False Hemp-nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, two-lipped flowers in whorled clusters. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along streambanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common sight in temperate regions.
