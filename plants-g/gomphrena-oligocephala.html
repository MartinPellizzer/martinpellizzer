Gomphrena oligocephala

Gomphrena oligocephala, commonly known as the small-headed gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, round flower heads that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bract-like leaves surrounding the flower clusters.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive, long-lasting blooms and drought-resistant nature.
