Grushvitzkya stellata

Grushvitzkya stellata, commonly known as the star-flowered plant, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Grushvitzkya stellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
