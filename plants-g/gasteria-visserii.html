Gasteria visserii

Gasteria visserii, commonly known as the Visser's gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white to light green markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria visserii is popular as a houseplant due to its hardiness and attractive appearance.
