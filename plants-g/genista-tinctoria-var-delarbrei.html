Genista tinctoria var. delarbrei

Genista tinctoria var. delarbrei, commonly known as the Delarbre broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, often thorned stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has needle-like leaves and is known for its hardy nature, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.

Additionally, it has historical significance in dye production due to its yellow pigments.
