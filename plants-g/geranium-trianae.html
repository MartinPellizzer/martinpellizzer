Geranium trianae

Geranium trianae, commonly known as the Madagascar geranium, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Geranium trianae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
