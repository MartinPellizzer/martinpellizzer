Gregoria vitaliana f. sesleri

Gregoria vitaliana f. sesleri, commonly known as the Sesler's Gregoria, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a slightly hairy texture, and its flowers are small, tubular, and typically pale pink to white in color. The plant is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of fragrant flowers that attract pollinators.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
