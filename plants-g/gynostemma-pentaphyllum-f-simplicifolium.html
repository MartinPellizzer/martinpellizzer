Gynostemma pentaphyllum f. simplicifolium

Gynostemma pentaphyllum f. simplicifolium, commonly known as the Chinese ginseng or Korean ginseng, is a variant of the gynostemma plant characterized by its simpler, broader leaves compared to the typical form.

It is a climbing herbaceous perennial that belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, native to East Asia. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into elliptical, yellow fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits, including antioxidant properties.

This form is valued for its adaptability and ease of cultivation, making it a popular choice for herbal gardens and medicinal use.
