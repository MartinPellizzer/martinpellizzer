Gynura auriculata var. glabrata

Gynura auriculata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed gynura, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is commonly cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Gynura auriculata var. glabrata thrives in humid, tropical climates and prefers partial shade to full sun.
