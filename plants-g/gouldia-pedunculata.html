Gouldia pedunculata

Gouldia pedunculata, commonly known as the red lily, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-shaped, glossy leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. Gouldia pedunculata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
