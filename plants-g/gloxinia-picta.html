Gloxinia picta

Gloxinia picta, commonly known as the painted gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Gloxinia picta prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and long blooming period.
