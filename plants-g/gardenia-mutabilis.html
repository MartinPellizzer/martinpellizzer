Gardenia mutabilis

Gardenia mutabilis, commonly known as the variable gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white or yellowish flowers that change color as they mature. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade. Its flowers, which can range from white to yellow, are highly valued for their ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.

Gardenia mutabilis is also noted for its slow growth and relatively low maintenance requirements.
