Genista frutescens

Genista frutescens, commonly known as dwarf broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry landscapes.

Genista frutescens is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
