Gardenia stellaris

Gardenia stellaris, commonly known as the star gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

The flowers bloom in summer and are highly valued for their ornamental beauty and aromatic scent.
