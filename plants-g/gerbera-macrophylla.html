Gerbera macrophylla

Gerbera macrophylla, commonly known as the African daisy, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with vibrant colors such as red, yellow, and orange. The plant has large, broad leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Gerbera macrophylla is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
