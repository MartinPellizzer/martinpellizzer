Geranium robertianum var. macropetalum

Geranium robertianum var. macropetalum, commonly known as the large-petaled geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features upright, branched stems with lance-shaped, dark green leaves that have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers with large, rounded petals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in woodland gardens.
