Ganitrus sphaerica

Ganitrus sphaerica, commonly known as the globular acanthus, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spherical inflorescence that resembles a globe, surrounded by spiny bracts. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a deep green color and a rough texture. It blooms with small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale pink, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Ganitrus sphaerica is often cultivated for its unique appearance and is well-suited to rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
