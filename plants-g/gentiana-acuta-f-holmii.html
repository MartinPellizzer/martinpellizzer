Gentiana acuta f. holmii

Gentiana acuta f. holmii, commonly known as the Holmii gentian, is a botanical variant of the gentian genus native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine environments, preferring well-drained, rocky soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
