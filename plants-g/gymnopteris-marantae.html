Gymnopteris marantae

Gymnopteris marantae, commonly known as the Marantae holly fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

Gymnopteris marantae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
