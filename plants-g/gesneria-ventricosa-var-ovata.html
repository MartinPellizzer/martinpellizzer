Gesneria ventricosa var. ovata

Gesneria ventricosa var. ovata, commonly known as the ovate gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria ventricosa var. ovata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
