Gladiolus grandiflorus

Gladiolus grandiflorus, commonly known as the sword lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces large, showy flowers in a range of colors, including white, pink, red, and yellow, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. Its leaves are long, narrow, and sword-shaped, giving the plant its common name.

Gladiolus grandiflorus is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as cut flowers.
