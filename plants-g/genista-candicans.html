Genista candicans

Genista candicans, commonly known as the silverbush or whitebark broom, is a flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, woody pods. Its foliage is characterized by fine, silvery-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance.

Genista candicans is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
