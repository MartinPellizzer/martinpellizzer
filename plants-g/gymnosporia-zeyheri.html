Gymnosporia zeyheri

Gymnosporia zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's burr bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.
