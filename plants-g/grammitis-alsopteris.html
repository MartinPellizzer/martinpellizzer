Grammitis alsopteris

Grammitis alsopteris, commonly known as the broad-leafed huperzia, is a fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, linear, and often broad leaves that are typically arranged in a single plane. Grammitis alsopteris is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices.
