Gesneria sulcata

Gesneria sulcata, commonly known as the zigzag gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, ribbed, and often zigzag-shaped leaves that are a deep green with a waxy texture. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

Gesneria sulcata is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and relatively low maintenance requirements.
