Gardenia pulcherrima

Gardenia pulcherrima, commonly known as the Christmas gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant produces yellow berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and indirect sunlight.

Gardenia pulcherrima is popular as a ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and long-lasting blooms.
