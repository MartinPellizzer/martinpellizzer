Gomphrena eriocalyx

Gomphrena eriocalyx, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky flower heads that resemble small globes, typically ranging in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, fluffy bracts that surround the central flower, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Gomphrena eriocalyx is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
