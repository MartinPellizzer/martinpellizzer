Gunnera pilosa

Gunnera pilosa, commonly known as the giant dock, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped leaves that can reach up to three meters in diameter. The plant belongs to the family Polygonaceae and is known for its robust growth habit. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
