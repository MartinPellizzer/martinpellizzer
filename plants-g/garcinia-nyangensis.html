Garcinia nyangensis

Garcinia nyangensis, commonly known as the Nyangensis Garcinia, is a tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or orange when ripe, containing a single seed. Garcinia nyangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
