Godetia tenuifolia

Godetia tenuifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf godetia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Malvaceae family.
