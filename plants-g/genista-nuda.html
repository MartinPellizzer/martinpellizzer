Genista nuda

Genista nuda, commonly known as the naked bursch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds.

Genista nuda is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
