Gmelina philippensis f. viridibracteata

Gmelina philippensis f. viridibracteata, commonly known as the Philippine ash or green bract gmelina, is a deciduous tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Oleaceae and is characterized by its smooth, ash-gray bark and lanceolate leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by elongated, woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its timber and ornamental qualities.
