Gnaphalium corymbosum

Gnaphalium corymbosum, commonly known as the clustered everlasting, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Gnaphalium corymbosum is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
