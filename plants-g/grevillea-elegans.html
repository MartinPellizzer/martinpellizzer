Grevillea elegans

Grevillea elegans, commonly known as the elegant grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southwestern Western Australia.

It features long, narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant, showy red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant is well adapted to arid and coastal environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils. Its unique floral structure attracts nectar-feeding birds and insects, making it an important species in local ecosystems.

Grevillea elegans is also popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
