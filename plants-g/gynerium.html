Gynerium

Gynerium, commonly known as sweet flag, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetlands and marshy areas.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its aromatic, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Gynerium thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in temperate regions around the world.
