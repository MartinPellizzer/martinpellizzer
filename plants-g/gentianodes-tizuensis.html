Gentianodes tizuensis

Gentianodes tizuensis, commonly known as the Tizu gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their vibrant blue or purple flowers.
