Gentianella glossocarpa

Gentianella glossocarpa, commonly known as the glossocarp gentian, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentianella glossocarpa is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
