Garcinia schraderi

Garcinia schraderi, commonly known as the Schrader's garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces bright orange, fleshy fruits that contain a single seed. Garcinia schraderi is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Garciniaceae family.
