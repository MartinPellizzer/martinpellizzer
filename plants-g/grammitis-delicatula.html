Grammitis delicatula

Grammitis delicatula, commonly known as the delicate fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are often densely packed. The plant thrives in humid environments, typically growing on trees or rocks in shaded areas. Its leaves are usually thin and have a finely divided structure, which helps in maximizing light absorption.

Grammitis delicatula is often found in rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance in horticulture.
