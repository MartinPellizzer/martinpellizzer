Gloxinia burchellii

Gloxinia burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's gloxinia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically velvety in texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Gloxinia burchellii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
