Glyptopetalum gracilipes

Glyptopetalum gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops and in shaded forest areas, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Glyptopetalum gracilipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
