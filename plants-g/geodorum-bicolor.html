Geodorum bicolor

Geodorum bicolor, commonly known as the golden bamboo orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. Its leaves are long and grass-like, contributing to its bamboo-like appearance.

Geodorum bicolor is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
