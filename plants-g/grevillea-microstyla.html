Grevillea microstyla

Grevillea microstyla, commonly known as the small-styled grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are highly attractive to nectar-feeding birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea microstyla is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
