Gloxinia sylvatica

Gloxinia sylvatica, commonly known as the forest gloxinia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green. Gloxinia sylvatica thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its blooming period occurs during the warmer months, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
