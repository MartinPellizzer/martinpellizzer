Gunnera tayrona

Gunnera tayrona, commonly known as the Tayrona giant shield fern, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gunneraceae family and is characterized by its large, shield-shaped leaves that can reach several feet in diameter. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests and montane regions. Its leaves are thick and leathery, providing a unique texture and appearance.

Gunnera tayrona is not only ornamental but also plays a role in supporting local biodiversity.
