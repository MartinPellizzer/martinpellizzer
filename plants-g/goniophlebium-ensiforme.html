Goniophlebium ensiforme

Goniophlebium ensiforme, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped fronds that are typically 30 to 60 centimeters long. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that can form dense mats. Its leaves are usually bipinnate, with finely divided segments that give it a delicate appearance.

Goniophlebium ensiforme thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
