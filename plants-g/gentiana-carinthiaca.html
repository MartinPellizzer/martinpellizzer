Gentiana carinthiaca

Gentiana carinthiaca, commonly known as the Carpathian gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Alps and Carpathian regions.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and its stem is usually unbranched. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
