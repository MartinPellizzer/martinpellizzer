Geonoma tenuissima

Geonoma tenuissima, commonly known as the slender-leafed geonoma, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves that often form dense, low-growing clumps. The fronds are typically elongated and have a feathery appearance, with a soft, leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
