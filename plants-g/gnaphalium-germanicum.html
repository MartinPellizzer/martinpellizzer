Gnaphalium germanicum

Gnaphalium germanicum, commonly known as the German feather grass, is a perennial herb native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, fluffy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Gnaphalium germanicum is known for its ability to thrive in temperate climates and is often found in naturalized areas.
