Gymnocalycium lukasikii

Gymnocalycium lukasikii, commonly known as the Lukasik's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is a small, globular plant with a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, typical of desert plants. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late summer. The plant has a short, cylindrical root system and is adapted to arid environments.

It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and resilience.
