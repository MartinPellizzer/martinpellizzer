Genista mauritiana

Genista mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius broom, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds with a hard coat.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
