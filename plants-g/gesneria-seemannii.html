Gesneria seemannii

Gesneria seemannii, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves that often exhibit a vibrant green color with intricate veining. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of orange and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria seemannii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
