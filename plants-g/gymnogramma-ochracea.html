Gymnogramma ochracea

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its robust, leathery fronds that often form dense, bushy clusters. Gymnogramma ochracea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in humid, shaded conditions.
