Geraniospermum gibbosum

Geraniospermum gibbosum, commonly known as the globular geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dry seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Geraniospermum gibbosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
