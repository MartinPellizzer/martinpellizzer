Gasteria nigricans var. marmorata

Gasteria nigricans var. marmorata, commonly known as the marble gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are dark green with distinctive white or pale marbled patterns. The leaves are typically narrow and have a slightly pointed tip, giving the plant a compact and elegant appearance. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
