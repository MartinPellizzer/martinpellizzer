Guzmania skotakii

Guzmania skotakii, commonly known as the Skotakii Bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can range in color from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms and small aquatic life. Its growth is slow, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Guzmania skotakii is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
