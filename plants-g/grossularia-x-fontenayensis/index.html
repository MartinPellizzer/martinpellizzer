Grossularia × Fontenayensis

Grossularia × fontenayensis is a unique hybrid plant that combines the traits of several species within the genus Grossularia. It is known for its striking appearance, featuring vibrant berries that range in color from red to yellow. This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature. It thrives in a variety of climates, making it a versatile choice for gardeners. Its hybrid origin adds to its intrigue, as it showcases the potential of plant breeding in creating new and beautiful varieties.
