Genista tridentata var. stenoptera

Genista tridentata var. stenoptera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, spiny stems and narrow, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature allows it to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
