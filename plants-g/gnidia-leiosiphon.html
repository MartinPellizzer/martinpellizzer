Gnidia leiosiphon

Gnidia leiosiphon, commonly known as the smooth siphon.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender, woody stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated fruits that split open to release its seeds. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Gnidia leiosiphon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
