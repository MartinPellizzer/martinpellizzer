Grevillea hakeoides subsp. commutata

Grevillea hakeoides subsp.

commutata, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces bright red or orange flowers in clusters.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought resistance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native gardens and landscaping.
