Gouldia rotundifolia

Gouldia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed gouldia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant has oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
