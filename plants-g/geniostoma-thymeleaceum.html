Geniostoma thymeleaceum

Geniostoma thymeleaceum, commonly known as the thymeleaf spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Thymelaeaceae.

It is a small shrub characterized by its aromatic, often hairy leaves that resemble those of thyme. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It is native to regions of southern Europe and parts of North Africa, typically growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
