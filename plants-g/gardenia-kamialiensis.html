Gardenia kamialiensis

Gardenia kamialiensis, commonly known as the Kamialien gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is typically shrub-like, growing to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia kamialiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
