Grias multinervia

Grias multinervia, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange with intricate patterns. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems arising from a rhizome system. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Grias multinervia thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth.
