Gymnogramma mathewsii var. glabriuscula

Gymnogramma mathewsii var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth-leafed gymnogramma, is a variant of the gymnogramma species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, evergreen fern characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, lanceolate leaves. The leaves are typically glabrous, with a smooth texture and a pale green coloration. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
