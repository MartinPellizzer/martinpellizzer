Gastrochilus obliquus var. obliquus

Gastrochilus obliquus var. obliquus, commonly known as the oblique gastrochilus, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elliptical and covered in a dense, velvety pubescence.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are pollinated primarily by bees and other small insects.
