Gerardia flava var. macrantha

Gerardia flava var. macrantha, commonly known as the yellow gerardia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in composite heads. The plant is native to parts of South Africa and thrives in dry, sandy soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.
