Gentiana scabra var. bungeana

Gentiana scabra var. bungeana, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.
