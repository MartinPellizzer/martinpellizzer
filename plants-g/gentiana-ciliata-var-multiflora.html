Gentiana ciliata var. multiflora

Gentiana ciliata var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with ciliate margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
