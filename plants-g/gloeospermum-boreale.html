Gloeospermum boreale

Gloeospermum boreale, commonly known as the Arctic gloeospermum, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in cold, harsh environments. The plant features dense, mat-forming foliage with small, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture. Its flowers are typically pink or white, appearing in clusters during the short Arctic growing season.

Gloeospermum boreale plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for Arctic wildlife.
