Gymnogramma bidentata

Gymnogramma bidentata, commonly known as the two-toothed holly fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the undersides of the leaves, a common reproductive feature in ferns.

Gymnogramma bidentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
