Grevillea murex

Grevillea murex, commonly known as the red-stemmed Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry gardens.

Grevillea murex is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
