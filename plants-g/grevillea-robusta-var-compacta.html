Grevillea robusta var. compacta

Grevillea robusta var. compacta, commonly known as the compact grevillea, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It features striking, feather-like leaves that are typically bright green and have a serrated edge. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, which are particularly showy during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and as a decorative plant in public spaces.
