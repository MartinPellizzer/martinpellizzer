Gaerdtia undulata

Gaerdtia undulata, commonly known as the zigzag vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique leaf structure and colorful fruits make it an attractive addition to botanical gardens.
