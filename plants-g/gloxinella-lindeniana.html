Gloxinella lindeniana

Gloxinella lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenberg's gloxinia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a compact growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinella lindeniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
