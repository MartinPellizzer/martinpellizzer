Gladiolus aristatus

Gladiolus aristatus, commonly known as the sword lily, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that bear dense clusters of colorful, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are long, narrow, and sword-shaped, giving the plant its common name. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spike and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant displays.
