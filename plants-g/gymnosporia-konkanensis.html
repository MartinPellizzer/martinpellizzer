Gymnosporia konkanensis

Gymnosporia konkanensis, commonly known as the Konkan thornapple, is a shrub native to the western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces woody capsules that contain elliptical, smooth-seeded fruits. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in scrubland and coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is considered an important component of the local flora in its native habitat.
