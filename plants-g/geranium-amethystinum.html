Geranium amethystinum

Geranium amethystinum, commonly known as the amethyst geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The flowers are typically pink to purple, with five petals and a distinctive calyx, blooming in summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
