Gnaphalium sandwicensium var. molokaiense

Gnaphalium sandwicensium var. molokaiense, commonly known as Molokaʻi everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Molokaʻi.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in coastal and upland habitats, often growing in well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that produces a profusion of tiny flowers, which are arranged in compact clusters.
