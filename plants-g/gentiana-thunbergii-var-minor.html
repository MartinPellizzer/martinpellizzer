Gentiana thunbergii var. minor

Gentiana thunbergii var. minor, commonly known as the lesser gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The flowers are rich in nectar and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
