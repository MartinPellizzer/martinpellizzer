Gymnocalycium reductum var. schatzlianum

Gymnocalycium reductum var. schatzlianum, commonly known as the Schatzliana Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
