Gymnadenia muricata

Gymnadenia muricata, commonly known as the spiked gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Gymnadenia muricata is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
