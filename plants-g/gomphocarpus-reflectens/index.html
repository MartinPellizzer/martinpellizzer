Gomphocarpus Reflectens

Gomphocarpus reflectens is a unique flowering plant known for its striking silver-blue leaves and showy yellow flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its common name, "silverleaf milkweed," refers to the reflective appearance of its foliage and the milky sap it produces. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. It plays an important role in local ecosystems and has gained popularity among gardeners worldwide.
