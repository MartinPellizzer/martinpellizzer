Glochidion runikerae

Glochidion runikerae, commonly known as the Runikerae holly.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features dense, leathery leaves with a distinctive waxy texture. Its small, white flowers are typically borne in clusters and are followed by yellowish berries.

Glochidion runikerae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
