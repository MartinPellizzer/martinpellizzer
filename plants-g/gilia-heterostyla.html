Gilia heterostyla

Gilia heterostyla, commonly known as the two-styled gilia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a white throat. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces two distinct styles in its flowers, which is a notable botanical feature.
