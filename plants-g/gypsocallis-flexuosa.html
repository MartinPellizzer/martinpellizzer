Gypsocallis flexuosa

Gypsocallis flexuosa, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Gypsocallis flexuosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in botanical gardens.
