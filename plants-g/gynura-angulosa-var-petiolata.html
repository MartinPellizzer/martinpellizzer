Gynura angulosa var. petiolata

Gynura angulosa var. petiolata, commonly known as the angular gynura or petioled gynura, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its serrated, angular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique leaf morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
