Glochidion gigantifolium

Glochidion gigantifolium, commonly known as the giant-leaf glochidion, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, spherical fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Glochidion gigantifolium thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
