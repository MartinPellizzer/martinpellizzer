Gleditsia × hebecarpa

Gleditsia × hebecarpa, commonly known as the Chinese honey locust, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and thorny branches. The tree produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by flat, pod-like fruits. It is valued for its hardy nature and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various soil types and climates.
