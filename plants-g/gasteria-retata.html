Gasteria retata

Gasteria retata, commonly known as the Cape foxtail, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, tubular flowers, typically in shades of yellow or orange. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Gasteria retata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
