Garnotia caespitosa f. amboinensis

Garnotia caespitosa f. amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina tufted grass, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale yellow to greenish flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
