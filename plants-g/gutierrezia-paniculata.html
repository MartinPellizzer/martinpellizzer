Gutierrezia paniculata

Gutierrezia paniculata, commonly known as yellow threadleaf goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Gutierrezia paniculata is also known for its role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife.
