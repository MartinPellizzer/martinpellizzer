Guilielma gasipaes var. chontaduro

Guilielma gasipaes var. chontaduro, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The fruit of this plant is edible and has a sweet, fibrous pulp that is enjoyed in various cuisines.
