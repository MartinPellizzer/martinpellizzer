Gypsophila gasparrinii

Gypsophila gasparrinii, commonly known as the Gasparrini's baby's breath, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

Gypsophila gasparrinii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in garden settings with minimal care.
