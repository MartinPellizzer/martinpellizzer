Gomphrena macrocephala

Gomphrena macrocephala, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky flower heads that range in color from pink to red and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their unique shape and long-lasting color.
