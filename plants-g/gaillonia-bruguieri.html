Gaillonia bruguieri

Gaillonia bruguieri, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while the flowers range in color from white to yellow.

Gaillonia bruguieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
