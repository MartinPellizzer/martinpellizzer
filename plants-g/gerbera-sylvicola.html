Gerbera sylvicola

Gerbera sylvicola, commonly known as the forest gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with bright, showy petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Gerbera sylvicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
