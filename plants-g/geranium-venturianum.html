Geranium venturianum

Geranium venturianum, commonly known as the Venturi geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces pink to purple blooms during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are usually serrated and have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium venturianum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
