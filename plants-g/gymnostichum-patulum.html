Gymnostichum patulum

Gymnostichum patulum, commonly known as the slender spinifex, is a species of grass found primarily in arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing structure with narrow, blade-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including drought and high salinity.

Gymnostichum patulum plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for small desert fauna.
