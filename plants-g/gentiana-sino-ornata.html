Gentiana sino-ornata

Gentiana sino-ornata, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in alpine meadows and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are erect and bear the showy flowers.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
