Geranium pusillum var. condensatum

Geranium pusillum var. condensatum, commonly known as the compact geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or purple, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
