Geraniospermum ovale

Geraniospermum ovale, commonly known as the oval geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces small, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Geraniospermum ovale is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
