Goeppertia umbrosa

Goeppertia umbrosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon basin.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of deep green and silver. The plant typically grows as an upright herb, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia umbrosa is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
