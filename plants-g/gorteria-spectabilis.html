Gorteria spectabilis

Gorteria spectabilis, commonly known as the showy gorteria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small florets, often in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early autumn.
