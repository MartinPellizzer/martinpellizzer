Gasteria carinata var. verrucosa

Gasteria carinata var. verrucosa, commonly known as the疣状齿轴兰, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. The leaves are often adorned with small, white tubercles or bumps, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped white flowers.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
