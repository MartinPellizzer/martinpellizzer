Gladiolus strictus

Gladiolus strictus, commonly known as the narrowleaf gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces tall, upright stems with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant typically blooms with showy, spike-like inflorescences that bear vibrant flowers in shades of red, yellow, and white. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ornamental cultivation.
