Godetia parviflora

Godetia parviflora, commonly known as the dwarf cosmos, is an annual flowering plant native to South America.

It features delicate, daisy-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for garden borders or containers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Its botanical name reflects its small flowers ("parviflora") and its association with the genus Godetia.
