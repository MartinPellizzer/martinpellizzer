Geraniospermum elongatum

Geraniospermum elongatum, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae, which includes many species with showy flowers and distinctive leaves. The plant is characterized by its elongated stems and clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in spring.
