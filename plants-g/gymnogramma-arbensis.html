Gymnogramma arbensis

Gymnogramma arbensis, commonly known as the creeping shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 20 centimeters in length. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a distinctive shield-like appearance on the underside.

Gymnogramma arbensis is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, low-light conditions.
