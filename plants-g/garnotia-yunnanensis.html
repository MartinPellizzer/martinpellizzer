Garnotia yunnanensis

Garnotia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan carnation, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has hairy, upright stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Garnotia yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
