Geijera salicifolia var. salicifolia

Geijera salicifolia var. salicifolia, commonly known as the silky gum, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale grey bark and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by elongated, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
