Goodenia angustifolia

Goodenia angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. It produces small, papery bracts that surround its flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Goodenia angustifolia is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
