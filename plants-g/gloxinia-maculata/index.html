Gloxinia Maculata

Spotted gloxinia, scientifically known as Gloxinia maculata, is a striking tropical plant native to South America. Its vibrant foliage features unique spots and patterns that make it a standout in any indoor garden. This plant is celebrated for its ease of care and ability to thrive in moderate light conditions. With its lush green leaves and showy flowers, it adds both beauty and interest to plant collections. Whether grown as a houseplant or in a greenhouse, the spotted gloxinia remains a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
