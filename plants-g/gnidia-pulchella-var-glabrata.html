Gnidia pulchella var. glabrata

Gnidia pulchella var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The smooth gnidia is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
