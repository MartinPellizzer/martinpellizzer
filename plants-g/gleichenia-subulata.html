Gleichenia subulata

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, needle-like leaves. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually small and closely arranged, forming a dense, carpet-like structure.
