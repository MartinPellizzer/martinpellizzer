Geranium traversii

Geranium traversii, commonly known as the African geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance contribute to its appeal among gardeners.
