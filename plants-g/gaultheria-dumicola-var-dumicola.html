Gaultheria dumicola var. dumicola

Gaultheria dumicola var. dumicola, commonly known as the dwarf strawberry shrub, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces tiny, red, berry-like fruits that are edible and have a tart, slightly sweet flavor. These berries are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories or on rocky slopes.
