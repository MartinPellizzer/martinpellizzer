Gymnopogon burchellii

Gymnopogon burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
