Gymnaglossum jacksonii

Gymnaglossum jacksonii, commonly known as Jackson's goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to the central United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small clusters of individual flowers.
