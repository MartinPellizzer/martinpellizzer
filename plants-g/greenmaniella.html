Greenmaniella

Greenmaniella, commonly known as the green manilla plant, is a member of the Arecaceae family, which includes palms.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant foliage.

However, it is not commonly used for commercial purposes compared to other palm species.
