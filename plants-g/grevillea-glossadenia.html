Grevillea glossadenia

Grevillea glossadenia, commonly known as the red-stemmed grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is well adapted to arid environments. It is often found in sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Grevillea glossadenia is also known for its ability to attract native birds and insects.
