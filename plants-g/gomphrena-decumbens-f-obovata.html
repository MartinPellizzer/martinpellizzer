Gomphrena decumbens f. obovata

Gomphrena decumbens f. obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which are often used in ornamental gardening. Its leaves are typically ovate to obovate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
