Gaertneria dumosa

Gaertneria dumosa, commonly known as the hairy broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil, Colombia, and Peru. The plant is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Gaertneria dumosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
