Geranium knuthianum

Geranium knuthianum, commonly known as Knuth's geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of rounded, hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in spring and summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners.
