Genista nitida

Genista nitida, commonly known as silverbush or silky broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Genista nitida is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
