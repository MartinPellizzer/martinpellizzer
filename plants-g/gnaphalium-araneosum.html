Gnaphalium araneosum

Gnaphalium araneosum, commonly known as the spider plant or woolyhead, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia, and it thrives in moist, temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It is often found in meadows, stream banks, and other damp habitats.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
