Gynura amplexicaulis

Gynura amplexicaulis, commonly known as the heartleaf or heartleaf sorrel, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often wrap around the stems, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Gynura amplexicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
