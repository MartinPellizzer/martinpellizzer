Guapeba torta

Guapeba torta, commonly known as the "butterfly torta," is a unique plant native to the Brazilian Atlantic Forest.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flattened, fleshy leaves that resemble a torta or cake, hence its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
