Gynura drymophila

Gynura drymophila, commonly known as the silver-leafed plant or Chinese evergreen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white variegated leaves, which are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Gynura drymophila is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
