Galanthus nivalis var. hololeuca

Galanthus nivalis var. hololeuca, commonly known as the white narcissus or snowdrop, is a variety of the snowdrop plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its distinctive nodding white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom in early spring. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves and is typically found in woodland areas and along hedgerows. It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is noted for its cold hardiness and ability to naturalize over time.
