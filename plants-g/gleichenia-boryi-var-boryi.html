Gleichenia boryi var. boryi

Gleichenia boryi var. boryi, commonly known as the Bory's hair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy fronds that are typically bright green and have a soft, velvety texture. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions with high humidity. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce both sterile and fertile fronds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
