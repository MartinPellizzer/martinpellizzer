Goodenia ochracea

Goodenia ochracea, commonly known as the yellow goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Goodenia ochracea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
