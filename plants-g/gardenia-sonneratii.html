Gardenia sonneratii

Gardenia sonneratii, commonly known as the white gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain hard, smooth seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia sonneratii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic blooms.
