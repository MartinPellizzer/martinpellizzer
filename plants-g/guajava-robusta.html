Guajava robusta

Guajava robusta, commonly known as the robust coffee tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and produces small, red, fleshy fruits that contain a single seed. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and requires a lot of sunlight. It is primarily cultivated for its seeds, which are processed into coffee beans.

The plant's botanical features make it well-suited for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
