Galium rubrum var. centroniae

Galium rubrum var. centroniae, commonly known as the Centronia bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
