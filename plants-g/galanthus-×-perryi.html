Galanthus × perryi

Galanthus × perryi, commonly known as the Snowdrops, is a hybrid species of the genus Galanthus, native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its delicate, nodding white flowers that bloom early in the winter or early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette, and it typically grows to a height of about 10–20 cm. Galanthus × perryi is well adapted to moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is a popular ornamental plant in gardens due to its early blooming habit and elegant appearance.
