Gossia acmenoides

Gossia acmenoides, commonly known as the smooth gossia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Gossia acmenoides is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
