Gladiolus humilis

Gladiolus humilis, commonly known as the dwarf gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, colorful flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The flowers are typically arranged in spikes and bloom during the summer months. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant display.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
