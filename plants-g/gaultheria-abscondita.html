Gaultheria abscondita

Gaultheria abscondita, commonly known as the hidden wintergreen, is a species of shrub native to the temperate rainforests of South America.

The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible and have a sharp, peppery flavor. Gaultheria abscondita is adapted to acidic, nutrient-poor soils and thrives in shaded, moist environments.
