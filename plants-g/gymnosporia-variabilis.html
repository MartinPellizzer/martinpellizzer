Gymnosporia variabilis

Gymnosporia variabilis, commonly known as the variable gymnosporia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain its seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Gymnosporia variabilis is used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
