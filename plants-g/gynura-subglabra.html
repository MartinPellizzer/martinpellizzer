Gynura subglabra

Gynura subglabra, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, pale green fruits. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Gynura subglabra is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
