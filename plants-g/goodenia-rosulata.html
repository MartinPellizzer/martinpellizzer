Goodenia rosulata

Goodenia rosulata, commonly known as the clustered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia rosulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
