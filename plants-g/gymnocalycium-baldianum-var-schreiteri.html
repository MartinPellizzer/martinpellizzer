Gymnocalycium baldianum var. schreiteri

Gymnocalycium baldianum var. schreiteri, commonly known as the Schreiter's moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its globular, spiny stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and protecting it from herbivores.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping and cactus collections.
