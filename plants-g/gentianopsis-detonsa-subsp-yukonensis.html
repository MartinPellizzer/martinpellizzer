Gentianopsis detonsa subsp. yukonensis

Gentianopsis detonsa subsp.

yukonensis, commonly known as Yukon gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Yukon and Northwest Territories of Canada. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as tundra and wet meadows. It has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
