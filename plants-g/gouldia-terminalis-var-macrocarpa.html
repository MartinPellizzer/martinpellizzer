Gouldia terminalis var. macrocarpa

Gouldia terminalis var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited gouldia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of vibrant red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces large, fleshy berries that are a distinctive feature of this variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its visual appeal.
