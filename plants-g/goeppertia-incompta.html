Goeppertia incompta

Goeppertia incompta, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail.

This plant is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive red and green variegation. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its foliage is often adorned with red veins that resemble the stripes of a tiger, giving it its common name.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
