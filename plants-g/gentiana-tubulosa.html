Gentiana tubulosa

Gentiana tubulosa, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its stems are usually erect and somewhat woody. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and woodland edges.

Gentiana tubulosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
