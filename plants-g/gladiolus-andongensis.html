Gladiolus andongensis

Gladiolus andongensis, commonly known as the Andong gladiolus, is a species native to the mountainous regions of South Korea.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. Its leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

This ornamental plant is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
