Gynopachis deflectens

Gynopachis deflectens, commonly known as the bentleaf goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
