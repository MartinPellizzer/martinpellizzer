Galarhoeus robustus

Galarhoeus robustus, commonly known as the robust galarhoeus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the southern hemisphere.

It features a basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface that aids in water retention. The plant produces striking, umbel-like inflorescences of bright yellow flowers in the spring. Its fibrous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.

Galarhoeus robustus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
