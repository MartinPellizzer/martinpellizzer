Gymnocalycium delaetii

Gymnocalycium delaetii, commonly known as the Delaet's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its globular, spiny stems and is part of the Cactaceae family. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers during the growing season. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Gymnocalycium delaetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
