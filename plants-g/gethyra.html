Gethyra

Gethyra, commonly known as the golden star ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellow and green variegation pattern. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a spicy aroma.

Gethyra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
