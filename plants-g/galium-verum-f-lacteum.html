Galium verum f. lacteum

Galium verum f. lacteum, commonly known as yellow bedstraw, is a variant of the common bedstraw plant.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

It is noted for its medicinal properties and historical use in herbal remedies.
