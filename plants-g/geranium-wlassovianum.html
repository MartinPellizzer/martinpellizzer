Geranium wlassovianum

Geranium wlassovianum, commonly known as the Russian geranium, is a species native to southern Russia and parts of Ukraine.

It is a perennial herb with upright stems and palmately lobed leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
