Galactia dumetorum

Galactia dumetorum, commonly known as the field milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Europe.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Galactia dumetorum has a shallow root system and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
