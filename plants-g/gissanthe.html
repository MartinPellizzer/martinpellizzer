Gissanthe

Gissanthe, commonly known as the sacred orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant blooms. The plant typically features long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Gissanthe thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and cultural significance make it a prized ornamental and ceremonial plant.
