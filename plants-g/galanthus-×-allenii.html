Galanthus × allenii

Galanthus × allenii, commonly known as the Allen's snowdrop, is a hybrid species of the snowdrop genus Galanthus.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces white petals with a yellow-green spot at the base. Native to the Caucasus region, it thrives in woodland and shady garden environments.

Galanthus × allenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its early blooming habit.
