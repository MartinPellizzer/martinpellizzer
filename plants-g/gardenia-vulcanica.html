Gardenia vulcanica

Gardenia vulcanica, commonly known as the Volcanic Gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight. Its blooms are often used in perfumery due to their strong, sweet scent.

Gardenia vulcanica is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
