Gastrochilus acinacifolius

Gastrochilus acinacifolius, commonly known as the sword-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
