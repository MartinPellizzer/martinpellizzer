Genista provincialis

Genista provincialis, commonly known as the provincial broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Genista provincialis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in marginal environments.
