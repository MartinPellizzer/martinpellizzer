Grevillea micronesiaca

Grevillea micronesiaca, commonly known as the Micronesian grevillea, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive, recurved leaves and bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and tropical environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes in its native range.
