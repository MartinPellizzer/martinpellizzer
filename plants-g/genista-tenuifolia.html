Genista tenuifolia

Genista tenuifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring slender, often branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, cylindrical clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Genista tenuifolia is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
