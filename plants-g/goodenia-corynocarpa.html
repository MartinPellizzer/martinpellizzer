Goodenia corynocarpa

Goodenia corynocarpa, commonly known as the woolly goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia corynocarpa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
