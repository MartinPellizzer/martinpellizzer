Gerascanthus valenzuelanus

Gerascanthus valenzuelanus, commonly known as the Valenzuelan geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical climates.
