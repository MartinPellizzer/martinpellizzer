Gentiana germanica proles campestriformis

Gentiana germanica proles campestriformis, commonly known as the meadow gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
