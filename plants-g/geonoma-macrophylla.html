Geonoma macrophylla

Geonoma macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf geonoma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The spores are produced in clusters on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
