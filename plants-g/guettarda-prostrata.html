Guettarda prostrata

Guettarda prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of less than 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptic in shape, and have a smooth surface.

Guettarda prostrata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
