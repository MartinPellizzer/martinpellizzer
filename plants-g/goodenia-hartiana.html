Goodenia hartiana

Goodenia hartiana, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Goodenia hartiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
