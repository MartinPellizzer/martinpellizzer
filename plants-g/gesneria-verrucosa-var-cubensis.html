Gesneria verrucosa var. cubensis

Gesneria verrucosa var. cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban velvetleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the warm season, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
