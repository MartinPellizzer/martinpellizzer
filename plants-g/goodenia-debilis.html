Goodenia debilis

Goodenia debilis, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Goodenia debilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive floral display.
