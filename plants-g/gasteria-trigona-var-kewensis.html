Gasteria trigona var. kewensis

Gasteria trigona var. kewensis, commonly known as the Kew's gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white to yellow markings. The plant produces pale green to white flowers in summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It is well adapted to dry conditions and requires minimal watering.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor and outdoor settings.
