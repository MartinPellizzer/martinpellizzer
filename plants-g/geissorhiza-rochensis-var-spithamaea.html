Geissorhiza rochensis var. spithamaea

Geissorhiza rochensis var. spithamaea, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. It is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to tolerate high salinity and strong winds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it remains relatively rare in the wild.
