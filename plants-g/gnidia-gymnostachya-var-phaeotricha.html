Gnidia gymnostachya var. phaeotricha

Gnidia gymnostachya var. phaeotricha, commonly known as the yellow star lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces small, greenish-yellow berries that contain the seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
