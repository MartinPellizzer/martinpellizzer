Gentiana delicata

Gentiana delicata, commonly known as the delicate gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentiana delicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
