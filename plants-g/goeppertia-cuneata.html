Goeppertia cuneata

Goeppertia cuneata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, with upright stems and broad, lanceolate foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid conditions.

Goeppertia cuneata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
