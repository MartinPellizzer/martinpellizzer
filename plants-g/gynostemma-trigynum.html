Gynostemma trigynum

Gynostemma trigynum, commonly known as Chinese ginseng or heartleaf gynostemma, is a climbing herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, green fruits that contain seeds. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Gynostemma trigynum is also known for its adaptogenic qualities and is cultivated for its health benefits.
