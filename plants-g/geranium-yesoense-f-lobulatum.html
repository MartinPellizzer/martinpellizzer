Geranium yesoense f. lobulatum

Geranium yesoense f. lobulatum, commonly known as the Yesso geranium, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its rounded, lobed leaves and clusters of pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. It has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and small, heart-shaped leaves.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
