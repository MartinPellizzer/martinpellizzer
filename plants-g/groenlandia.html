Groenlandia

Groenlandia, commonly known as the Greenlandic willow, is a shrub native to the Arctic regions of Greenland.

It is a member of the Salicaceae family, which includes willows and poplars. The plant has slender, woody stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh, cold climates. Groenlandia plays an important ecological role in Arctic ecosystems, providing habitat and food for local wildlife.
