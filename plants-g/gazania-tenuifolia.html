Gazania tenuifolia

Gazania tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red or brown center. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.

Gazania tenuifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
