Gazania rogersii

Gazania rogersii, commonly known as the desert gazania, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.

Gazania rogersii is also known for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, adding continuous color to landscapes.
