Galium elbursense

Galium elbursense, commonly known as Elburz bedstraw, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems that often form dense mats. Galium elbursense is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience in challenging ecological conditions.
