Grevillea patentiloba

Grevillea patentiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically reddish-brown in color. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental planting.
