Grevillea willisii

Grevillea willisii, commonly known as the yellow grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is typically found in sandy or rocky soils. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Grevillea willisii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
