Gomara

Gomara, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant features thick, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped and covered in a waxy cuticle. Gomara thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
