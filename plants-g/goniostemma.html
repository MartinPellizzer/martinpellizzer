Goniostemma

Goniostemma, commonly known as the climbing gourd or arrowleaf gourd, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are typically elongated and can vary in color from green to orange.

Goniostemma is often cultivated for its edible fruits, which are used in traditional cuisines.
