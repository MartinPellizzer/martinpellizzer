Gnaphalium × subkeriense

Gnaphalium × subkeriense, commonly known as the hybrid everlasting flower, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to white and bloom in late summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental use and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
