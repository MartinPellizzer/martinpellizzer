Geranium sergievskajae

Geranium sergievskajae, commonly known as the Sergeyevskaya geranium, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant features upright, hairy stems and heart-shaped, palmate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a unique addition to rock gardens or alpine settings.
