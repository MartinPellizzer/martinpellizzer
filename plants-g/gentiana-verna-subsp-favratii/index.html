Gentiana Verna Subsp. Favratii

Gentiana verna subsp. favratii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the alpine regions of southern Europe. It is known for its striking blue petals and its ability to thrive in harsh, high-altitude environments. This subspecies is often found in rocky soils and open meadows, where it blooms in early spring. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
