Goniothalamus tamirensis

Goniothalamus tamirensis, commonly known as the Malayan tamarind tree, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the family Annonaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves with opposite arrangement. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically greenish-white in color, while the fruit is a fleshy, yellowish drupe.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
