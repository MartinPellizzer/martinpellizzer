Gentiana carneorubra

Gentiana carneorubra, commonly known as the red-flowered gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange-red flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Gentiana carneorubra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
