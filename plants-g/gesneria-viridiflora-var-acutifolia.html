Gesneria viridiflora var. acutifolia

Gesneria viridiflora var. acutifolia, commonly known as the sharp-leaved gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are sharply pointed, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria viridiflora var. acutifolia is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
