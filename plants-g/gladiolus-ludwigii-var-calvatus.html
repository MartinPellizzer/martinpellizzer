Gladiolus ludwigii var. calvatus

Gladiolus ludwigii var. calvatus, commonly known as the Ludwig's gladiolus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with striking, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

This variety is valued for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various climates.
