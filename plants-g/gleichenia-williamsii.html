Gleichenia williamsii

Gleichenia williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' Gleichenia, is a fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that form dense, mat-like growths on the forest floor. The fronds are typically green, with a smooth texture and a slightly wavy margin. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate rainforests.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms and small invertebrates.
