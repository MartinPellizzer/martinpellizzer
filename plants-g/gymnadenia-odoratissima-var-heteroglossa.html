Gymnadenia odoratissima var. heteroglossa

Gymnadenia odoratissima var. heteroglossa, commonly known as the fragrant grass pink, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy areas. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain the seeds.

This variety is noted for its distinct fragrance and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
