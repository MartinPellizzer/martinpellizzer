Gymnocalycium quehlianum var. flavispinum

Gymnocalycium quehlianum var. flavispinum, commonly known as the yellow spiny cactus, is a species native to southern South America.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
