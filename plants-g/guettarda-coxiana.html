Guettarda coxiana

Guettarda coxiana, commonly known as the yellow shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the appearance of a shower of light yellow blossoms. The plant has opposite, simple, and leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Guettarda coxiana is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its use in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
