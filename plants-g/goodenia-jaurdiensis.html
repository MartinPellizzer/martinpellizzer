Goodenia jaurdiensis

Goodenia jaurdiensis, commonly known as the Jaurdi goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, attracting various pollinators such as birds and insects.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and potentially vulnerable species.
