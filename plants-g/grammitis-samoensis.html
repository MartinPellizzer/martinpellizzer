Grammitis samoensis

Grammitis samoensis, commonly known as Samoan maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically green with a slightly glossy texture. Native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly Samoa and nearby islands, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce new fronds from the nodes.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening in suitable climates.
