Goodenia albida

Goodenia albida, commonly known as the white goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in heathlands and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Goodenia albida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
