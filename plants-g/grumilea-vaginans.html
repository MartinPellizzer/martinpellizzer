Grumilea vaginans

Grumilea vaginans, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Grumilea vaginans is an annual herb that thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in agricultural fields and along roadsides.
