Gesneria hahnii

Gesneria hahnii, commonly known as the Hahn's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of vibrant, showy flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Gesneria hahnii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
