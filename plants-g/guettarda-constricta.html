Guettarda constricta

Guettarda constricta, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
