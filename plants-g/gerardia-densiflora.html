Gerardia densiflora

Gerardia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered gerardia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, clustered white or pink flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Gerardia densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
