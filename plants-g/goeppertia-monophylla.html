Goeppertia monophylla

Goeppertia monophylla, commonly known as the Zebra Canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are adorned with distinctive white and green vertical stripes. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its unique foliage and striking blooms, it is a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
