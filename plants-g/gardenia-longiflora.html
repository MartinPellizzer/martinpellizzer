Gardenia longiflora

Gardenia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to cream-colored flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain black, shiny seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia longiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic blooms.
