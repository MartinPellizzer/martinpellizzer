Galearia ridleyi

Galearia ridleyi, commonly known as the Malayan fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, intricate fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a layer of fine, hairy structures.

Galearia ridleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
