Godetia viminea var. margaritae

Godetia viminea var. margaritae, commonly known as the silverleaf godetia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and cheerful blooms.
