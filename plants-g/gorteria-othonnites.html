Gorteria othonnites

Gorteria othonnites, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces yellow-orange disk flowers surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal gardens.

Gorteria othonnites is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
