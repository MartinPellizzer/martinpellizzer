Geranium schimpffii

Geranium schimpffii, commonly known as the Schimpff's geranium, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped or rounded leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Geranium schimpffii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
