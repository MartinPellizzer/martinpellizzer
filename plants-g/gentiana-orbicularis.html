Gentiana orbicularis

Gentiana orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which are typically tubular in shape and bloom in late summer. The plant grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often in rocky or moist soil, and is adapted to cold climates. Its leaves are usually broad and rounded, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Gentiana orbicularis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vivid floral display.
