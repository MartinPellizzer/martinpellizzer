Glochidion barronense

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
