Gaertnera australiana

Gaertnera australiana, commonly known as the Australian ironwood, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features small, leathery leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It is known for its hard, dense wood, which is resistant to decay.

Gaertnera australiana plays an important role in its native ecosystem, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
