Genista oliveri

Genista oliveri, commonly known as the Oliver's broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are arranged in cylindrical spikes. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
