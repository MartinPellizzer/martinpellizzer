Gilia achilleifolia

Gilia achilleifolia, commonly known as the showy gilia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

Gilia achilleifolia is known for its ability to thrive in arid environments and is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value.
