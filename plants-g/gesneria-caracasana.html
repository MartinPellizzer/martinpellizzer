Gesneria caracasana

Gesneria caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Venezuela, and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display, which appears in shades of red, orange, and yellow.
