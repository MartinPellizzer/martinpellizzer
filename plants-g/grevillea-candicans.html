Grevillea candicans

Grevillea candicans, commonly known as the showy grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like leaves and vibrant, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in coastal and temperate regions.

Grevillea candicans is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for native birds and insects.
