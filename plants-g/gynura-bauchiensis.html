Gynura bauchiensis

Gynura bauchiensis, commonly known as the Bauchi golden taro, is a herbaceous plant native to Nigeria.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cluster, which are followed by fluffy, white seed heads. Gynura bauchiensis is typically found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
