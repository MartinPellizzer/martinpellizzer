Gentiana igarashii

Gentiana igarashii, commonly known as the Igarashii gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and rocky areas. It produces showy, five-petaled flowers that bloom in late summer.

Gentiana igarashii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
