Genista tinctoria f. plena

Genista tinctoria f. plena, commonly known as the full-flowered yellow broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a compact shrub characterized by its dense, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features small, trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
