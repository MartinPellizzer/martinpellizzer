Gonzalea spicata var. glabrata

Gonzalea spicata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth gonzalea, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The leaves are typically reduced to small scales, giving the plant a distinctive, almost shrub-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sunlight to flourish.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
