Geranium ocellatum

Geranium ocellatum, commonly known as the spotted geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spotted leaves and clusters of pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil. It is a perennial herb that prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its ornamental value.
