Gerardia grandiflora var. integriuscula

Gerardia grandiflora var. integriuscula, commonly known as the Hawaiian gerardia, is a rare flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss and invasive species, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native range.
