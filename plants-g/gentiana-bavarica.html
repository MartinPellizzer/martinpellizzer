Gentiana bavarica

Gentiana bavarica, commonly known as the Bavarian gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of down.

Gentiana bavarica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
