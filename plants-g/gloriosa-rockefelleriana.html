Gloriosa rockefelleriana

Gloriosa rockefelleriana, commonly known as the Rockefeller lily, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread easily. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
