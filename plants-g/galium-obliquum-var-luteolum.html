Galium obliquum var. luteolum

Galium obliquum var. luteolum, commonly known as yellow wood-sorrel, is a variant of the wood-sorrel species native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, ascending stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.
