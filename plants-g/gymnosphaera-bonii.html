Gymnosphaera bonii

Gymnosphaera bonii, commonly known as the African cycad, is a member of the Cycadaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a dioecious plant, meaning it has separate male and female individuals, and produces cones for reproduction. The plant has a short trunk with a crown of pinnate leaves that are leathery and tough. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it adaptable to arid environments.

Gymnosphaera bonii is also valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens.
