Garcinia goudotiana

Garcinia goudotiana, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red, fleshy berries that contain a single seed. Garcinia goudotiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
