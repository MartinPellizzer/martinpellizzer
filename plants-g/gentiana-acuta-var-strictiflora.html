Gentiana acuta var. strictiflora

Gentiana acuta var. strictiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and its stems are often slender and erect. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
