Globularia pallida

Globularia pallida, commonly known as the pale globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has rounded, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Globularia pallida is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
