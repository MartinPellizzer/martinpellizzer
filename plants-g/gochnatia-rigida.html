Gochnatia rigida

Gochnatia rigida, commonly known as the rigid gochnatia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves and produces yellow flowers that attract pollinators. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Gochnatia rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
