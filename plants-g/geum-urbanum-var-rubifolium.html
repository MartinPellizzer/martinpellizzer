Geum urbanum var. rubifolium

Geum urbanum var. rubifolium, commonly known as the redleaf avens, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and often have a reddish hue, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
