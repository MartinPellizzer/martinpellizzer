Gnaphalium persoonii

Gnaphalium persoonii, commonly known as the slender everlasting, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers and aromatic, feathery foliage. The plant produces numerous tiny flower heads that are clustered in a dense, spherical shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Gnaphalium persoonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in floral arrangements.
