Garcinia thouvenotii

Garcinia thouvenotii, commonly known as the African yellow mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and West Africa.

It is a small evergreen tree that typically grows to a height of about 10 meters, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by fruits resembling mangosteens, with a thick, yellow rind and a sweet, aromatic pulp.

Garcinia thouvenotii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
