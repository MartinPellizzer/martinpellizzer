Gesneria vauthieri

Gesneria vauthieri, commonly known as the Vauthier's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub features glossy, dark green leaves that are elongated and ovate in shape. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in the summer months.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
