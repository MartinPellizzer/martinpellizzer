Grammitis Hainanensis

Grammitis hainanensis is a rare fern species native to the tropical regions of Hainan Island in China. This delicate plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is known for its unique morphological features. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this plant from threats such as deforestation and climate change.
