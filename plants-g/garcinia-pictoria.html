Garcinia pictoria

Garcinia pictoria, commonly known as the painted garcinia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from green to red and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to orange or red berries. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Garcinia pictoria is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
