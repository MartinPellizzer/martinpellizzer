Goodenia pedicellata

Goodenia pedicellata, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Goodenia pedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
