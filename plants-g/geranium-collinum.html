Geranium collinum

Geranium collinum, commonly known as the mountain geranium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy flowers, which range from pink to violet, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Geranium collinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and alpine displays.
