Goniothalamus macrophyllus

Goniothalamus macrophyllus, commonly known as the giant leaf sterculia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, elongated fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
