Geranium atropurpureum var. cowenii

Geranium atropurpureum var. cowenii, commonly known as the purple-leaf geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage and clusters of pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has serrated, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
