Gouania pannigera f. macrophyllum

Gouania pannigera f. macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaved gouania, is a variant of the genus Gouania, which belongs to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its notably large, ovate leaves that are typically pubescent on the underside. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique leaf structure.
