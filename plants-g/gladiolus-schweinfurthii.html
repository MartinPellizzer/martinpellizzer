Gladiolus schweinfurthii

Gladiolus schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African gladiolus, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, showy blooms that are often used in cut flower arrangements due to their longevity and visual impact. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Gladiolus schweinfurthii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral displays.
