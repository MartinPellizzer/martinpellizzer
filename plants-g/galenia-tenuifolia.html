Galenia tenuifolia

Galenia tenuifolia, commonly known as slender galenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and is often found in disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy texture.

Galenia tenuifolia is known for its ability to thrive in various environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
