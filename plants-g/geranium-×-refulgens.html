Geranium × refulgens

Geranium × refulgens, commonly known as the Regal Geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, single flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white or pale pink center. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and features heart-shaped, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Geranium × refulgens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscapes.
