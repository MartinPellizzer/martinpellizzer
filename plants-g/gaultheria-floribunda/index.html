Gaultheria Floribunda

Gaultheria floribunda is a unique evergreen shrub native to the cool, moist forests of South America. It is known for its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its dense foliage. The plant thrives in shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are thick and waxy, providing a striking visual contrast to its fragrant blossoms. This plant is not only ornamental but also holds cultural and medicinal significance in its native regions.
