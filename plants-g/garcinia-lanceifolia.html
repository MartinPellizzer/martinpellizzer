Garcinia lanceifolia

Garcinia lanceifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved garcinia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and yellowish-orange flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically harvested when ripe. Garcinia lanceifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
