Geranium purpureum f. subeglandulosum

Geranium purpureum f. subeglandulosum, commonly known as the purple geranium, is a variant of the common geranium species.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly glandular leaves, which set it apart from other forms of Geranium purpureum.
