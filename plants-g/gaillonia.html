Gaillonia

Gaillonia, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season, hence its common name. Gaillonia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular indoor plant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
