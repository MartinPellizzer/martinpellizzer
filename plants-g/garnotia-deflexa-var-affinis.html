Garnotia deflexa var. affinis

Garnotia deflexa var. affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved garnotia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
