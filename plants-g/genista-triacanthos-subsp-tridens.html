Genista triacanthos subsp. tridens

Genista triacanthos subsp.

tridens, commonly known as the thorned broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth and small, yellowish-green leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are arranged in cylindrical clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to rocky or dry environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.
