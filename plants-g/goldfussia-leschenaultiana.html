Goldfussia leschenaultiana

Goldfussia leschenaultiana, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, often growing up to 10 meters tall. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
