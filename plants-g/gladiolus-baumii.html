Gladiolus baumii

Gladiolus baumii, commonly known as the dwarf gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, colorful flower spikes that can range from white to deep red. The flowers are typically arranged in a tight, compact cluster at the top of the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Due to its compact size, it is ideal for smaller garden spaces and rock gardens.
