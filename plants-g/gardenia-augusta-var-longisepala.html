Gardenia augusta var. longisepala

Gardenia augusta var. longisepala, commonly known as the long-sepal gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers with prominent, elongated sepals. The plant produces waxy, yellow-orange berries that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
