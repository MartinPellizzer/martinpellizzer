Goodenia tripartita

Goodenia tripartita, commonly known as the three-part goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a hairy appearance. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Goodenia tripartita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
