Grevillea rosea

Grevillea rosea, commonly known as the common grevillea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features striking, feather-like leaves that are often reddish or pinkish in color. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Grevillea rosea is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract native birds.
