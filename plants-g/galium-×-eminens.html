Galium × eminens

Galium × eminens, commonly known as the tall bedstraw, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Galium × eminens is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
