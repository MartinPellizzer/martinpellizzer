Geum urbanum subvar. simplicifolium

Geum urbanum subvar. simplicifolium, commonly known as the simple-leaved avens, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features basal leaves that are simple, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in upright, branched inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
