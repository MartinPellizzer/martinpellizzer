Gladiolus refractus

Gladiolus refractus, commonly known as the reflexed gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters at the top of the stem, and they have a distinctive reflexed (bent backward) shape.

The botanical features of Gladiolus refractus make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
