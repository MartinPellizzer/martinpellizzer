Gibbaeum

Gibbaeum, commonly known as the African daisy or sand daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

Many species of Gibbaeum are adapted to arid environments, featuring succulent leaves and a deep root system for water retention.
