Gochnatia palosanto

Gochnatia palosanto, commonly known as the sacred tree of the Yoruba people, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and aromatic leaves. The plant is often found in moist, tropical forests and can grow up to several meters in height. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
