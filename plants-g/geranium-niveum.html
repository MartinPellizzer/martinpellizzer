Geranium niveum

Geranium niveum, commonly known as the snow geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its white to pale pink flowers and hairy, gray-green leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is a perennial herb that produces small, clustered flowers during the summer months.

Geranium niveum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
