Guyonia antennina

Guyonia antennina, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive inflorescence of small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in a loose spike. The plant has a fibrous root system and typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as pine flatwoods and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

Guyonia antennina is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in swampy or seasonally flooded areas.
