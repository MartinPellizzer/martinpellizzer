Grindelia nelsonii

Grindelia nelsonii, commonly known as Nelson's gumweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas. Its unique chemical composition includes sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its medicinal and ecological significance.
