Gomphrena holosericea

Gomphrena holosericea, commonly known as the velvetleaf gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery inflorescences that range in color from pink to red, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its textured appearance.

Gomphrena holosericea is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its long-lasting blooms and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
