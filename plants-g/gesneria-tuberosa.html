Gesneria tuberosa

Gesneria tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking tuberous roots and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of tubular, brightly colored flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its foliage consists of thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors.

Gesneria tuberosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
