Guioa Glauca

Guioa glauca is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines known for its ornamental value and hardy nature. It features glossy dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The tree is commonly used in landscaping due to its compact size and ability to thrive in various soil conditions. Its fruit, a small capsule, contains seeds that are dispersed by wind. Despite its beauty, Guioa glauca remains relatively underappreciated in global horticulture.
