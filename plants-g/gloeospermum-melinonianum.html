Gloeospermum melinonianum

Gloeospermum melinonianum, commonly known as the red-flowered gloeospermum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant red flowers during the growing season, adding ornamental value. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
