Gazania longiscapa

Gazania longiscapa, commonly known as the narrowleaf gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow or orange daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Gazania longiscapa is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and salty conditions.
