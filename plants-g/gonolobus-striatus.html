Gonolobus striatus

Gonolobus striatus, commonly known as the zigzag vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and distinctive, zigzag-shaped stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Gonolobus striatus is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
