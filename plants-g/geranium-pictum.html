Geranium pictum

Geranium pictum, commonly known as the painted geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form with heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their long-lasting display, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Geranium pictum prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in warm, dry climates.
