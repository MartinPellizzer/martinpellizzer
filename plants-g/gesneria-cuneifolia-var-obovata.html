Gesneria cuneifolia var. obovata

Gesneria cuneifolia var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features elliptical, glossy leaves that are broader at the base, giving it a unique, ornamental appearance. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among collectors for its vibrant foliage and compact growth habit.
