Gardenia annae

Gardenia annae, commonly known as the Annae gardenia, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, waxy, white flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm climates. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, requiring consistent moisture to thrive.

Gardenia annae is also valued for its ability to produce fragrant essential oils.
