Garcinia minutiflora

Garcinia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf garcinia or small-flowered garcinia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces small, round fruits that are yellow when ripe and contain a single seed. Garcinia minutiflora is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appearance.

Its botanical features make it a unique species within the Garciniaceae family.
