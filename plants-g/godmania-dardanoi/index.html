Godmania Dardanoi

Godmania dardanoi is a rare and enigmatic plant species discovered in the remote regions of the Himalayas. Its unique characteristics and limited distribution have sparked significant interest among botanists and conservationists. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments, where it adapts to extreme temperatures and thin air. Due to habitat loss and climate change, its survival is increasingly threatened. Researchers are working to better understand its biology and protect its fragile ecosystem.
