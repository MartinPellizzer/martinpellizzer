Gifolaria mareotica

Gifolaria mareotica, commonly known as the Mareotica thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Nile Delta region of Egypt.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant thrives in saline and alkaline soils, making it well adapted to the harsh environments of the Egyptian coast.
