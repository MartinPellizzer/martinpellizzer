Geum urbanum var. umbrosum

Geum urbanum var. umbrosum, commonly known as the shade-loving avens, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to woodland and shaded areas of Europe.

It features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, and its stems are hairy and often reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish to reddish flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
