Guajava aeruginea

Guajava aeruginea, commonly known as the silverleaf guajava, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery sheen on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry that changes color from green to red as it ripens.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
