Gluta obovata

Gluta obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate gluta, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are broadly ovate in shape. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
