Gaura australis

Gaura australis, commonly known as the Australian gaura, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Polemoniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of showy, pink or white flowers. Gaura australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
