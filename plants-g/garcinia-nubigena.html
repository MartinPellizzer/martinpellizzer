Garcinia nubigena

Garcinia nubigena, commonly known as the cloud apple or sky apple, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, red or orange fruits that resemble small apples. The plant belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and unique fruit. Garcinia nubigena thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
