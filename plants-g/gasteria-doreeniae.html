Gasteria doreeniae

Gasteria doreeniae, commonly known as the Doreen's gasteria, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage composed of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves. The leaves are often adorned with white or yellow marginal teeth, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

This plant is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
