Gramen medium

Gramen medium, commonly known as the medium grass.

It is a perennial grass species characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and fine, bluish-green leaves. This plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for lawns and ornamental landscapes. Gramen medium is known for its ability to form dense turf, providing a neat and uniform appearance.

Its botanical features include small, spikelet-based inflorescences and a fibrous root system that aids in soil stabilization.
