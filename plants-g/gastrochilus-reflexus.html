Gastrochilus reflexus

Gastrochilus reflexus, commonly known as the reflexed stomach orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive reflexed lip. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth.

Gastrochilus reflexus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
