Gasteria carinata var. strigata

Gasteria carinata var. strigata, commonly known as the Strigata Gasteria, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive central ridge and a white or pale blue powdery coating. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
