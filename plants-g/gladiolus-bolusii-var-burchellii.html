Gladiolus bolusii var. burchellii

Gladiolus bolusii var. burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, erect stems. The plant produces showy, often brightly colored flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
