Goodenia asteriscus

Goodenia asteriscus, commonly known as the starry goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Goodenia asteriscus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
