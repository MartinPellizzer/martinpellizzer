Gerascanthus goossensii

Gerascanthus goossensii, commonly known as the Goossens' geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant features upright stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is appreciated by both botanists and gardeners alike.
