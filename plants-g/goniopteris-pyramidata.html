Goniopteris pyramidata

Goniopteris pyramidata, commonly known as the pyramid fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, triangular fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a distinctive, elongated shape with a crown of pinnate leaves that are often bright green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The fronds are used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants in horticulture.
