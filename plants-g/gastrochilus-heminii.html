Gastrochilus heminii

Gastrochilus heminii, commonly known as the Heminii stone orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a distinctive, compact growth habit and dark green, fleshy leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
