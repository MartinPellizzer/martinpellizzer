Gerbera linnaei

Gerbera linnaei, commonly known as the Linnaean gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including yellow, orange, and red, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Gerbera linnaei thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.
