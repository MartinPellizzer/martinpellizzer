Gasteria croucheri subsp. croucheri

Gasteria croucheri subsp.

croucheri, commonly known as the Croucher's gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale markings.

The plant produces red or orange flowers on tall, leafless stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
