Genista burdurensis

Genista burdurensis, commonly known as the Burdurensis Broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista burdurensis is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
