Gasteria disticha var. angulata

Gasteria disticha var. angulata, commonly known as the angular gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, with a distinctive angular appearance. The leaves are usually a vibrant green, sometimes with white or yellow markings. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
