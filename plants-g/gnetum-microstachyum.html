Gnetum microstachyum

Gnetum microstachyum, commonly known as the dwarf gnetum, is a unique gymnosperm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that are typically unisexual. The plant exhibits a distinctive morphology with a short trunk and dense, leathery leaves. It produces cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs.

Due to its ecological significance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical botanical gardens.
