Galeola rigida

Galeola rigida, commonly known as the rigid galea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are often borne in clusters.

Galeola rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
