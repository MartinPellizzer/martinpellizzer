Gnaphalium evacoides

Gnaphalium evacoides, commonly known as the slender everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flower heads, which are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant's leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.
