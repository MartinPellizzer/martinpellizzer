Gymnocalycium proliferum

Gymnocalycium proliferum, commonly known as the desert rose cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick stems.

Gymnocalycium proliferum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
