Gonypetalum acreanum

Gonypetalum acreanum, commonly known as the red-flowered bellvista, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals that resemble small bells, giving it a unique and eye-catching appearance. The plant belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. Its flowers are typically short-lived but attract various pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
