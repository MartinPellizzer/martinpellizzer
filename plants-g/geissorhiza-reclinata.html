Geissorhiza reclinata

Geissorhiza reclinata, commonly known as the reclinata lily, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, pale blue to lilac-colored flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid conditions and is drought-tolerant, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Geissorhiza reclinata is also noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in local herbal remedies.
