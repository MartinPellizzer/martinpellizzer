Gentiana muliensis

Gentiana muliensis, commonly known as the Muli gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Gentiana muliensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
