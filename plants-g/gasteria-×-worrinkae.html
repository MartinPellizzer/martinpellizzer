Gasteria × worrinkae

Gasteria × worrinkae, commonly known as the hybrid gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers on a tall stem, usually in spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria × worrinkae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
