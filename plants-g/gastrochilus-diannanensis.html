Gastrochilus diannanensis

Gastrochilus diannanensis, commonly known as the Diannan orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas with high humidity and partial shade. Its roots are often found in the soil, and it has a sympodial growth habit.

The flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.
