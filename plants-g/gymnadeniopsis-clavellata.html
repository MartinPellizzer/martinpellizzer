Gymnadeniopsis clavellata

Gymnadeniopsis clavellata, commonly known as the clustered false dragonhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Gymnadeniopsis clavellata is often found in moist, sandy soils in open woodlands and along stream banks.
