Galega haynii

Galega haynii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goat's rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and can remain viable in the soil for several years.

Galega haynii is often used in traditional medicine and has been studied for its potential pharmacological properties.
