Geniostoma rupestre var. solomonense

Geniostoma rupestre var. solomonense, commonly known as Solomon's rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Solomon Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in rocky or coastal habitats. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually narrow and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its arid environment.

The species is characterized by its succulent foliage and is often found in well-drained soils.
