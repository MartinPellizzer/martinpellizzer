Gardenia jasminoides f. maruba

Gardenia jasminoides f. maruba, commonly known as the maruba gardenia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and produces large, fragrant, white flowers with a yellow center. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping and as a potted plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

The maruba gardenia is a popular cultivar due to its compact size and showy blooms.
