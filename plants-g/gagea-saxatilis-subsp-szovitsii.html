Gagea saxatilis subsp. szovitsii

Gagea saxatilis subsp.

szovitsii, commonly known as the Szovits's gagea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, white to pale blue flowers.
