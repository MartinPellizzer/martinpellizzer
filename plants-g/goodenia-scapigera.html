Goodenia scapigera

Goodenia scapigera, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in a dense spike at the top of the stem. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

Goodenia scapigera is adapted to withstand drought and is a common sight in native gardens and natural habitats.
