Glossorhyncha salmonea

Glossorhyncha salmonea, commonly known as the salmon-hued orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within rainforests, often as an epiphyte on tree trunks or branches.
