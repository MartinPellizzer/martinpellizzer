Gentiana arctophila var. densiflora

Gentiana arctophila var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with high moisture retention.
