Greenea longiflora

Greenea longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered greenea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, clustered flowers with prominent, elongated petals. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils in open, sunny habitats. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, and it blooms during the summer months.

Greenea longiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
