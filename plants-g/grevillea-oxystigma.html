Grevillea oxystigma

Grevillea oxystigma, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces bright red or orange cylindrical flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea oxystigma is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds.
