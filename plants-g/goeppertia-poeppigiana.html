Goeppertia poeppigiana

Goeppertia poeppigiana, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, striped foliage that resembles a zebra's pattern. The plant typically features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a range of colors from deep green to red and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia poeppigiana is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
