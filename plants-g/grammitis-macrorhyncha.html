Grammitis macrorhyncha

Grammitis macrorhyncha, commonly known as the broad-rhynch grammitis, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. Grammitis macrorhyncha is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic.
