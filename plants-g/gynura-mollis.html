Gynura mollis

Gynura mollis, commonly known as the silver velvetleaf, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and it produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Gynura mollis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
