Goodenia connata

Goodenia connata, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Goodenia connata is known for its ecological importance, providing nectar for various pollinators.
