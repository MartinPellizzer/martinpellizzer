Gomphrena mizqueensis

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are upright and often branched.

Gomphrena mizqueensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
