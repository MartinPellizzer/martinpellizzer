Goodenia rotundifolia var. glaberrima

Goodenia rotundifolia var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth goodenia, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It is characterized by its rounded, fleshy leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense, globular inflorescence, which is a distinctive feature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
