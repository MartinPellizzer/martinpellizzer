Gentianella Hieronymi

Gentianella hieronymi is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its striking blue or violet petals that bloom in late summer. This species is often found in rocky soils and high altitude environments. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical gem.
