Goodenia spicata

Goodenia spicata, commonly known as the spicate goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in shrubland and grassland habitats. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with five petals and prominent stamens.

Goodenia spicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
