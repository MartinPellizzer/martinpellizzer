Graphorkis lissochilodes

Graphorkis lissochilodes, commonly known as the smooth-lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a loose spike, which are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. Native to the tropical and subtropical regions of South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique floral structure and ecological niche make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
