Guaiacum abilo

Guaiacum abilo, commonly known as the West Indian satinwood, is a small evergreen tree native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the family Zygophyllaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and small, elliptical leaves.
