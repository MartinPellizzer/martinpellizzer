Gentiana langbianensis

Gentiana langbianensis, commonly known as the Langbian gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

Gentiana langbianensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
