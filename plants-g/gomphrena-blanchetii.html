Gomphrena blanchetii

Gomphrena blanchetii, commonly known as the Mexican plume or blue plume, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that range in color from blue to pink and white. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy choice for dry climates.
