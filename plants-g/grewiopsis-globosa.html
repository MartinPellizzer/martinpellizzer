Grewiopsis globosa

Grewiopsis globosa, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky inflorescence that resembles a globe, composed of numerous small, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Grewiopsis globosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
