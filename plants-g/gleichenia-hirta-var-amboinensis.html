Gleichenia hirta var. amboinensis

Gleichenia hirta var. amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboine Gleichenia, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, linear, and often hairy fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, and the sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
