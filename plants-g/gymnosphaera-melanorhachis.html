Gymnosphaera melanorhachis

Gymnosphaera melanorhachis, commonly known as the black-stemmed cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched stem that is dark in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces large, feather-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. Gymnosphaera melanorhachis is a gymnosperm, meaning it reproduces via seeds rather than spores, and it lacks true flowers.

This species is native to parts of Africa and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
