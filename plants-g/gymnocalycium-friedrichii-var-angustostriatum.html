Gymnocalycium friedrichii var. angustostriatum

Gymnocalycium friedrichii var. angustostriatum, commonly known as the narrow-striated moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to northern Argentina.

It is characterized by its globular, ribbed stems that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its striking appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
