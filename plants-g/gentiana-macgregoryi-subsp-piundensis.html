Gentiana macgregoryi subsp. piundensis

Gentiana macgregoryi subsp.

The subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, spurred petals that attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soils, often found in alpine meadows.
