Geranium × ignescens

Geranium × ignescens, commonly known as the fire geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while the stems are often hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for moist, well-drained soils.

This plant is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
