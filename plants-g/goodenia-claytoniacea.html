Goodenia claytoniacea

Goodenia claytoniacea, commonly known as the coastal goodenia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky coastal habitats. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It is well adapted to coastal conditions, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.

Goodenia claytoniacea plays an important role in stabilizing dunes and supporting local biodiversity.
