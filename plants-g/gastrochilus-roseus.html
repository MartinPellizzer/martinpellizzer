Gastrochilus roseus

Gastrochilus roseus, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring pale green stems and showy pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the rainy season.

Gastrochilus roseus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
