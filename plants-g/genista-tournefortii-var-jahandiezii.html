Genista tournefortii var. jahandiezii

Genista tournefortii var. jahandiezii, commonly known as the Jahandiez's Genista, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and flowering structures. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers in racemes, which bloom in spring. It is typically found in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
