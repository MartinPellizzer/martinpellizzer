Gomphrena nitida

Gomphrena nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf gomphrena, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, feathery flower heads that range in color from pink to red. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Gomphrena nitida thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its long blooming period, from late summer to autumn, adds vibrant color to gardens throughout the growing season.
