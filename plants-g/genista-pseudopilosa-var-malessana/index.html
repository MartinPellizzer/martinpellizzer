Genista Pseudopilosa Var. Malessana

Genista pseudopilosa var. malessana is a lesser-known yet striking member of the Fabaceae family. Native to the rocky and arid regions of southern Europe, this plant thrives in harsh, sun-scorched environments. Its distinctive yellow flowers bloom in spring, adding a splash of color to otherwise barren landscapes. The shrub is often found growing on slopes and cliffs, where it clings to the soil with tenacity. Despite its beauty, it remains a rare and underappreciated species in the botanical world.
