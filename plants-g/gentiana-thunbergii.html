Gentiana thunbergii

Gentiana thunbergii, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with a fine layer of down.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
