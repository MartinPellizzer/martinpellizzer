Glechon squarrosa

Glechon squarrosa, commonly known as the woolly glechon, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers the stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Glechon squarrosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and color.
