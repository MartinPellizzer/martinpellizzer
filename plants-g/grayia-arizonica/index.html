Grayia Arizonica

Grayia arizonica is a rare and unique plant native to the southwestern United States. It is known for its distinctive gray-green foliage and small, urn-shaped flowers. This species thrives in rocky, arid environments and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this fascinating plant for future generations.
