Garcinia nujiangensis

Garcinia nujiangensis, commonly known as the Nujiang garcinia, is a tropical plant native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces bright orange-red berries that are rich in bioactive compounds, including hydroxycitric acid. Garcinia nujiangensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.

Its unique morphology and chemical composition make it an interesting subject for botanical and pharmacological research.
