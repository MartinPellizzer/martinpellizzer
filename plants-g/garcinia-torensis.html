Garcinia torensis

Garcinia torensis, commonly known as the Torensian garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a yellow-orange berry that contains a single seed and is often used in traditional medicine.

Garcinia torensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
