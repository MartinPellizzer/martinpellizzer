Genista juniperina

Genista juniperina, commonly known as the juniper broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in rocky or sandy soils.

Genista juniperina is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
