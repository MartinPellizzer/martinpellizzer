Goeppertia undulata

Goeppertia undulata, commonly known as the zigzag calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, undulating, and variegated foliage that adds visual interest to any indoor space. The leaves are typically dark green with silver or white markings, creating a dramatic contrast. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.

This plant is popular for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
