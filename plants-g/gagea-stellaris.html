Gagea stellaris

Gagea stellaris, commonly known as the star gagea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the western United States.

It features a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a delicate, star-shaped flower with six petals. The plant typically blooms in early spring, often before the leaves are fully developed. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky habitats.

Gagea stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
