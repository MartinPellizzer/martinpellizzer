Geranium lenticulum

Geranium lenticulum, commonly known as the water geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water sources.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for wetland gardens and ecological restoration projects.
