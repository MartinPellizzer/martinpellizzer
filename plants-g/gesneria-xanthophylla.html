Gesneria xanthophylla

Gesneria xanthophylla, commonly known as the yellow-leaved gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria xanthophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
