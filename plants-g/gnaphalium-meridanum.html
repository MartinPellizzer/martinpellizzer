Gnaphalium meridanum

Gnaphalium meridanum, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

Gnaphalium meridanum is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it contributes to the biodiversity of these high-altitude ecosystems.
