Gymnocalycium amerhauseri subsp. altagraciense

Gymnocalycium amerhauseri subsp.

altagraciense, commonly known as the Altagracia moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina. It is characterized by its globular to cylindrical stem, which is typically green to blue-green in color.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is a succulent plant, adapted to arid environments with thick, fleshy stems that store water. Gymnocalycium amerhauseri subsp.

altagraciense is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
