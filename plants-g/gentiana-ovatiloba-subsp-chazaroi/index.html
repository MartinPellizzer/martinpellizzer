Gentiana Ovatiloba Subsp. Chazaroi

Gentiana ovatiloba subsp. chazaroi is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of the eastern Himalayas. It is known for its vivid blue flowers that bloom during the short summer months. This subspecies thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting its habitat is crucial for preserving this botanical gem.
