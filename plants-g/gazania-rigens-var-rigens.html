Gazania rigens var. rigens

Gazania rigens var. rigens, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange daisy-like flowers with a central disk and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is typically grown as an ornamental due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Gazania rigens var. rigens is also known for its ability to reseed itself, ensuring its presence in gardens year after year.
