Gesneria cubensis var. truncata

Gesneria cubensis var. truncata, commonly known as the Cuban gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Cuba.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
