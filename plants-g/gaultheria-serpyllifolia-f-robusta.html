Gaultheria serpyllifolia f. robusta

Gaultheria serpyllifolia f. robusta, commonly known as the robust wintergreen, is a variant of the wintergreen plant native to certain regions of North America.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, appearing in clusters during the early spring.

The plant is valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental qualities.
