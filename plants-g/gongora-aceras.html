Gongora aceras

Gongora aceras, commonly known as the hairy Gongora, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that typically feature a mix of red, yellow, and green hues. The plant has a compact growth habit with fleshy, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. Gongora aceras thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests.

Its unique morphology and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
