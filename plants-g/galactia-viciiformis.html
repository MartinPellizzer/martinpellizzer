Galactia viciiformis

Galactia viciiformis, commonly known as the sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, curved pods. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Galactia viciiformis is often found in disturbed areas and can be invasive in some regions.
