Gentianella yanachagensis

Gentianella yanachagensis, commonly known as the Yanachangen gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which includes many species of gentians known for their striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often appearing in shades of blue or purple, and bloom during the summer months.
