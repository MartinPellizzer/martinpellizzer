Geum ovaliforme

Geum ovaliforme, commonly known as the ovalleaf avens, is a perennial herb native to the northern regions of North America.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and slightly oval, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Geum ovaliforme thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along streambanks.
