Galeopsis tetrahit var. beckii

Galeopsis tetrahit var. beckii, commonly known as Beck's hedge nettle, is a variant of the common hedge nettle.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spikes. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found along roadsides and in open fields.

Its botanical characteristics make it identifiable by its distinctive foliage and reproductive structures.
