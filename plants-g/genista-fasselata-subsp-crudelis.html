Genista fasselata subsp. crudelis

Genista fasselata subsp.

crudelis, commonly known as the rough broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has small, leathery leaves and is adapted to dry, rocky soils. It is often found in coastal and inland areas with poor soil conditions.

Its hardy nature makes it a resilient plant in arid environments.
