Gymnandropogon Annulatus

Gymnandropogon annulatus is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of the United States. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring a tall stem with ring-like structures along its length. This plant is often found in prairie and grassland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
