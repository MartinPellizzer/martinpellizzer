Gesneria schiedeana

Gesneria schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiede's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub, often found in humid, shaded environments.

Gesneria schiedeana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
