Gouldia terminalis f. wailauensis

Gouldia terminalis f. wailauensis, commonly known as the Wailauensis Gouldia, is a rare and striking bromeliad native to the Hawaiian Islands.

This cultivar is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and botanic gardens.
