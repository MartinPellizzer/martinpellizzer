Gentiana linearifolia

Gentiana linearifolia, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and alpine meadows. Its leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its common name.

Gentiana linearifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
