Gonostegia hirta var. tenggerensis

Gonostegia hirta var. tenggerensis, commonly known as the Tengger mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Tengger Desert in Indonesia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster, which bloom during the dry season. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to withstand drought.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies in desert ecosystems.
