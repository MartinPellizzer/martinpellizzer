Grammitis pseudospiralis

Grammitis pseudospiralis, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a small, epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

Grammitis pseudospiralis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded, moist conditions.
