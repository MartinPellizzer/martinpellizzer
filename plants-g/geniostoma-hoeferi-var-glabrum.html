Geniostoma hoeferi var. glabrum

Geniostoma hoeferi var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth geniostoma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, delicate white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
