Geissorhiza sulphurea var. arenicola

Geissorhiza sulphurea var. arenicola, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to store moisture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil.
