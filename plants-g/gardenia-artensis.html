Gardenia artensis

Gardenia artensis, commonly known as the Chinese gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are highly valued for their intense fragrance and are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants.

Gardenia artensis thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
