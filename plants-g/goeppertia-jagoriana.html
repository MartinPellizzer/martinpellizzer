Goeppertia jagoriana

Goeppertia jagoriana, commonly known as the jagoriana calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

Goeppertia jagoriana is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
