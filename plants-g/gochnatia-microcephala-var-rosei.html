Gochnatia microcephala var. rosei

Gochnatia microcephala var. rosei, commonly known as the rose-leaved gochnatia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands. It has a compact growth habit and produces small, pale yellow blooms that appear in spring.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage.
