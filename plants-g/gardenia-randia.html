Gardenia randia

Gardenia randia, commonly known as the red gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white or yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their beauty and scent.

Gardenia randia thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
