Gastroglottis ophrydis

Gastroglottis ophrydis, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky substance. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
