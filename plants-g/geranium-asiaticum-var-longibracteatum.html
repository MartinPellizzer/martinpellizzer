Geranium asiaticum var. longibracteatum

Geranium asiaticum var. longibracteatum, commonly known as the Asian geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to Asia.

It features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers with five petals, typical of the Geraniaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
