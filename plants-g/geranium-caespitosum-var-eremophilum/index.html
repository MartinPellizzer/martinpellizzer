Geranium Caespitosum Var. Eremophilum

Geranium caespitosum var. eremophilum is a hardy perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It is known for its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it ideal for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners in dry climates. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and enhancing the aesthetic appeal of desert landscapes.
