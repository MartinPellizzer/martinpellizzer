Genipa americana f. grandifolia

Genipa americana f. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered genipapo, is a variant of the genipapo plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, making it visually striking. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or red when ripe. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth texture and a dark green color.

This cultivar is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive floral display.
