Gomphrena perennis subf. simplex

Gomphrena perennis subf.

simplex, commonly known as the common gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces vibrant, spiky flower heads in shades of red, pink, and purple.
