Galactia aschersoniana

Galactia aschersoniana, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

Additionally, it plays a role in nitrogen fixation, benefiting the surrounding soil ecosystem.
