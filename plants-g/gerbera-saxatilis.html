Gerbera saxatilis

Gerbera saxatilis, commonly known as the rock gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a range of colors including yellow, red, and orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to arid environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help reduce water loss.

Gerbera saxatilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
