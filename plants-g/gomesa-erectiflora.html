Gomesa erectiflora

Gomesa erectiflora, commonly known as the upright-flowered gomesa, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Gomesa erectiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
