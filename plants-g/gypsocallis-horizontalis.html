Gypsocallis horizontalis

Gypsocallis horizontalis, commonly known as the horizontal lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological studies.
