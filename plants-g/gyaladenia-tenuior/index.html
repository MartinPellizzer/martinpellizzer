Gyaladenia Tenuior

Gyaladenia tenuior is a lesser-known member of the orchid family found primarily in the alpine regions of Europe. This delicate plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, pale flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in rocky and moist environments, often growing in secluded mountainous areas. Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and beautiful plant from further decline.
