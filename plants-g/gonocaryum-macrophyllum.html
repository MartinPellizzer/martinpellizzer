Gonocaryum macrophyllum

Gonocaryum macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaved gonocaryum, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. It produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruits are berries that contain several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
