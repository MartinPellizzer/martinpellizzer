Grevillea cinerea

Grevillea cinerea, commonly known as the silver grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea cinerea is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract native pollinators.
