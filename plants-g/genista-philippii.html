Genista philippii

Genista philippii, commonly known as the Philippi's broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in arid environments.

Genista philippii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
