Geonoma spinescens

Geonoma spinescens, commonly known as the spiny fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Davalliaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds that are often covered in small, spiny projections. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky outcrops.

Geonoma spinescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
