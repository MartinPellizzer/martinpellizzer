Garcinia adinantha

Garcinia adinantha, commonly known as the African mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The tree produces edible, orange-red berries that are rich in xanthones and other bioactive compounds. Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
