Gardenia tessellaris

Gardenia tessellaris, commonly known as the tessellated gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive tessellated pattern on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, waxy white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Gardenia tessellaris is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for with proper pruning and fertilization.
