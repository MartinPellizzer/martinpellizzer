Guatteria multinervis

Guatteria multinervis, commonly known as the Guatteria with multivenation, is a tropical evergreen shrub belonging to the family Marcaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy, elliptical leaves that have prominent veins, giving it its scientific name. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
