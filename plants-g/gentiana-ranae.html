Gentiana ranae

Gentiana ranae, commonly known as the frog orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet petals and is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. It produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Gentiana ranae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological significance.
