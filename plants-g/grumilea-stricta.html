Grumilea stricta

Grumilea stricta, commonly known as the rigid grumilea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth, dark green upper surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in panicles, and are typically white or pale yellow.

Grumilea stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
