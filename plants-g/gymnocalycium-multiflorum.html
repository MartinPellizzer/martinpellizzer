Gymnocalycium multiflorum

Gymnocalycium multiflorum, commonly known as the desert rose or night bloomer cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers at night, which are highly fragrant and attract nocturnal pollinators. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in arid environments with well-draining soil.

Gymnocalycium multiflorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and beautiful nocturnal blooms.
