Gladiolus × nanceianus

Gladiolus × nanceianus, commonly known as the hybrid gladiolus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Gladiolus italicus and Gladiolus nancei. The plant is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors. Its foliage consists of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
