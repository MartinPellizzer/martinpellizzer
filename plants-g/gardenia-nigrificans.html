Gardenia nigrificans

Gardenia nigrificans, commonly known as the black gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, elliptical leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and pleasant scent.

The fruit of the plant is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
