Gentiana clusii

Gentiana clusii, commonly known as the Clusi's gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a raceme.

Gentiana clusii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
