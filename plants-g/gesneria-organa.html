Gesneria organa

Gesneria organa, commonly known as the Orange Gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture.

Gesneria organa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
