Gymnocalycium bruchii subsp. susannae

Gymnocalycium bruchii subsp.

susannae, commonly known as the Susanna's moon cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Argentina. It is a small, globular cactus with a thick, ribbed stem that can reach up to 15 cm in height.

The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

It is prized for its compact size and ornamental value in cactus collections.
