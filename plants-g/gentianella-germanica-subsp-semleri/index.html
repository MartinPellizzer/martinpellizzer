Gentianella Germanica Subsp. Semleri

Gentianella germanica subsp. semleri is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. It is known for its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. This subspecies is often found in moist, calcareous soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical species.
