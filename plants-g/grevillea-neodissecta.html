Grevillea neodissecta

Grevillea neodissecta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often red or purple at the base, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, which are highly attractive to nectar-feeding birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea neodissecta is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
