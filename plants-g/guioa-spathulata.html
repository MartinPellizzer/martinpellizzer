Guioa spathulata

Guioa spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf guioa, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically 5 to 10 centimeters long. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Guioa spathulata is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its use in traditional medicine.
