Gaultheria longipes

Gaultheria longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed wintergreen, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the colorful flowers.

Gaultheria longipes is also valued for its aromatic leaves, which contain compounds used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
