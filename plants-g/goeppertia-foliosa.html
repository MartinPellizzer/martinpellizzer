Goeppertia foliosa

Goeppertia foliosa, commonly known as the "Copperleaf Plant," is a tropical species native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful leaf patterns. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, red, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Goeppertia foliosa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
