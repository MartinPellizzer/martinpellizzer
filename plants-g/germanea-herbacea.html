Germanea herbacea

Germanea herbacea, commonly known as the slender Germanea, is a herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually white or pale green, appearing in clusters.

Germanea herbacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
