Goodenia holtzeana

Goodenia holtzeana, commonly known as the Holtzean goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or violet flowers. Goodenia holtzeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
