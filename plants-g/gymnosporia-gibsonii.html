Gymnosporia gibsonii

Gymnosporia gibsonii, commonly known as Gibson's burr.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its spiny stems and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a fibrous covering. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Gymnosporia gibsonii plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
