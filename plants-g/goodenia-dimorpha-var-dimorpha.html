Goodenia dimorpha var. dimorpha

Goodenia dimorpha var. dimorpha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering display.
