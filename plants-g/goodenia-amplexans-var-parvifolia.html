Goodenia amplexans var. parvifolia

Goodenia amplexans var. parvifolia, commonly known as the dwarf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, lance-shaped leaves that often wrap around the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is distinguished by its smaller leaves and flowers compared to the typical form of Goodenia amplexans.
