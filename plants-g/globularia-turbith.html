Globularia turbith

Globularia turbith, commonly known as the Turbith Globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense, rounded clusters of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, arranged in a compact, globular shape.

Globularia turbith is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
