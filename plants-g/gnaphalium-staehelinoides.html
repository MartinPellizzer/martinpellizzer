Gnaphalium staehelinoides

Gnaphalium staehelinoides, commonly known as the silverleaf cudweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas.
