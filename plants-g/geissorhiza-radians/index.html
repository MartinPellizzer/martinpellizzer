Geissorhiza Radians

Geissorhiza radians is a striking member of the orchid family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun. Its unique appearance features bright yellow petals with intricate patterns that resemble radiating lines. The plant is prized by botanists and gardeners alike for its beauty and rarity. Despite its delicate look, Geissorhiza radians is surprisingly resilient and adaptable to various environmental conditions.
