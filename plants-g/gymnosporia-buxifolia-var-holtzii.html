Gymnosporia buxifolia var. holtzii

Gymnosporia buxifolia var. holtzii, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, followed by small, red, fleshy fruits. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
