Galium lucidum var. aetnicum

Galium lucidum var. aetnicum, commonly known as the glossy bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has slender, hairy stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
