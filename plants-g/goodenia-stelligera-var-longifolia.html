Goodenia stelligera var. longifolia

Goodenia stelligera var. longifolia, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal or inland regions. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spikes. It is characterized by its long, slender stems and is often found in open habitats.

This variant is distinguished by its elongated foliage compared to other varieties of Goodenia stelligera.
