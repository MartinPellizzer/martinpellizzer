Genista juncea

Genista juncea, commonly known as slender broom, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod-bearing fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are small, trifoliate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Genista juncea is typically found in dry, sunny habitats like grasslands and rocky soils.
