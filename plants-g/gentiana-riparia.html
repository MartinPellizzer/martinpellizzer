Gentiana riparia

Gentiana riparia, commonly known as the river gentian, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland edges. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Gentiana riparia is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
