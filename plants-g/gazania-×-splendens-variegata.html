Gazania × splendens variegata

Gazania × splendens variegata, commonly known as the variegated gazania, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with contrasting central markings. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically variegated with white or cream patterns, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Gazania × splendens variegata is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
