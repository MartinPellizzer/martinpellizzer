Gardenia mannii var. honoluluensis

Gardenia mannii var. honoluluensis, commonly known as the Honolulu gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 6 feet. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and limited natural range, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
