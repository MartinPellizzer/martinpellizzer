Gossypianthus tomentosus

Gossypianthus tomentosus, commonly known as the woolly cottonbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
