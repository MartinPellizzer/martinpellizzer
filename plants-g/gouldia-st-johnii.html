Gouldia st-johnii

Gouldia st-johnii, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of bright red, waxy flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, sandy soils.

Gouldia st-johnii is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and year-round foliage.
