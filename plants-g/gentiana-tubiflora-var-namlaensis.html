Gentiana tubiflora var. namlaensis

Gentiana tubiflora var. namlaensis, commonly known as the Namlaensis gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, which helps retain moisture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
