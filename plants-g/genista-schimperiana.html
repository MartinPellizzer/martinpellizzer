Genista schimperiana

Genista schimperiana, commonly known as the Schimper's broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to parts of southern Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in rocky and dry habitats. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several narrow, linear leaflets.

Genista schimperiana is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
