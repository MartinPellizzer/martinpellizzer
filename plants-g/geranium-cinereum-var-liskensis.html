Geranium cinereum var. liskensis

Geranium cinereum var. liskensis, commonly known as the Liskens geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
