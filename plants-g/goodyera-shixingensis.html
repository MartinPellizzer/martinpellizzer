Goodyera shixingensis

Goodyera shixingensis, commonly known as the Shixing orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and intricate floral structures. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually greenish-white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.
