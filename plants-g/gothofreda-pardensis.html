Gothofreda pardensis

Gothofreda pardensis, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds that are covered in a dense layer of golden-yellow spores. The plant's leaves are leathery and glossy, providing a unique visual contrast in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
