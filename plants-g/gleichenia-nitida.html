Gleichenia nitida

Gleichenia nitida, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green to silvery in color. The plant is native to various regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Gleichenia nitida is often found growing on rocks or in forest understories, where it forms dense, mat-like colonies.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
