Girardinia diversifolia subsp. suborbiculata

Girardinia diversifolia subsp.

suborbiculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved girardinia, is a species native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lanceolate leaves.

The flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences and are usually pale green to yellowish in color. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate appearance.
