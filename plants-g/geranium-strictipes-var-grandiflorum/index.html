Geranium Strictipes Var. Grandiflorum

Swan plant, scientifically known as Geranium strictipes var. grandiflorum, is a striking member of the Geraniaceae family native to the eastern United States. This perennial herb is renowned for its showy, rose-like flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. The plant's common name is derived from the elegant, swan-like shape of its foliage, which adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks. With its vibrant blooms and unique appearance, the swan plant is a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
