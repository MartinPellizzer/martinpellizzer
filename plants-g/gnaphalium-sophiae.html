Gnaphalium sophiae

Gnaphalium sophiae, commonly known as the noble everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white or pale pink petals. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in fine, silky hairs.

Gnaphalium sophiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
