Garcinia nervosa var. nervosa

Garcinia nervosa var. nervosa, commonly known as the red veined ginger or red ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red veins on dark green leaves, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The rhizomes of this plant are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
