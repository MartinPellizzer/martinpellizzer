Genista purgans proles balansae

Genista purgans proles balansae, commonly known as the Balansae broom.

This plant is a member of the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. It features small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, typical of many legumes. The leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

It is native to specific regions in the Mediterranean and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
