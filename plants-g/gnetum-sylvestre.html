Gnetum sylvestre

Gnetum sylvestre, commonly known as the African tropical gnetum, is a member of the Gnetophyta division, which includes gymnosperms.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is known for its unique reproductive structures.

Gnetum sylvestre is also notable for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
