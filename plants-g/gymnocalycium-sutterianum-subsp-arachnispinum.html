Gymnocalycium sutterianum subsp. arachnispinum

Gymnocalycium sutterianum subsp.

arachnispinum, commonly known as the spider-spined cactus, is a species native to Argentina. It is a low-growing, globular cactus with a ribbed stem that can reach up to 30 centimeters in height.

The plant produces spiny, cylindrical flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its areoles are covered with dense, white, woolly hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
