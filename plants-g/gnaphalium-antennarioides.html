Gnaphalium antennarioides

Gnaphalium antennarioides, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and silvery foliage. The plant produces numerous tiny, white to pale yellow florets that are arranged in compact, spherical clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving the plant its distinctive silver appearance.
