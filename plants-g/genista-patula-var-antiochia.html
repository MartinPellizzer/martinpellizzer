Genista patula var. antiochia

Genista patula var. antiochia, commonly known as the Antioch broom, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, spiny stems and produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with pairs of narrow, lance-shaped leaflets. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and seasonal floral display.
