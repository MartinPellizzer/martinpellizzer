Gesneria viridiflora var. colorata

Gesneria viridiflora var. colorata, commonly known as the Variegated Green-flowered Gesneria, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves with striking yellow and white variegation. The plant produces small, fragrant, greenish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and subtle floral display make it a visually appealing addition to any collection.
