Gentiana angustifolia

Gentiana angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often in rocky or moist soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive blue-green hue.

Gentiana angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
