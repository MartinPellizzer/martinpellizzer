Garcinia tauensis

Garcinia tauensis, commonly known as the Borneo Garcinia, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark-barked trunk and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange-red berries that are edible but have a bitter taste.

Garcinia tauensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
