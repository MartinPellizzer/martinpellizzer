Gynura rosea

Gynura rosea, commonly known as the velvetleaf or red velvet plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Gynura rosea thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
