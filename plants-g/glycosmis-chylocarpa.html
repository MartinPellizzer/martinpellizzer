Glycosmis chylocarpa

Glycosmis chylocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese date tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-green flowers and small, fleshy fruits. The fruit, which resembles a date in appearance, is edible and has a sweet, honey-like flavor. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.
