Gesneria ferruginea

Gesneria ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with a reddish-brown hue on the underside of the leaves. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, often blooming during the summer months.

Gesneria ferruginea is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
