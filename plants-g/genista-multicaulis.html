Genista multicaulis

Genista multicaulis, commonly known as the evergreen yellow bush, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has wiry, spiny stems and small, leathery leaves that are evergreen throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
