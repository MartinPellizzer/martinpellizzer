Gentlea minor

Gentlea minor, commonly known as the small gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers bloom in late summer and are known for their vibrant color and subtle fragrance.

Gentlea minor is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
