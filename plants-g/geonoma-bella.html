Geonoma bella

Geonoma bella, commonly known as the beautiful geonoma, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its intricate, delicate fronds that often have a lace-like appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understories of dense vegetation. Geonoma bella is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
