Geranium yunnanense

Geranium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan geranium, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and palmate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in summer. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Geranium yunnanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticulture for its vibrant floral display.
