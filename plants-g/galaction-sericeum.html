Galaction sericeum

Galaction sericeum, commonly known as the silky galaction, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, silver-white leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
