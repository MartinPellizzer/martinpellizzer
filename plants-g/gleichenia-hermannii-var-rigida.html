Gleichenia hermannii var. rigida

Gleichenia hermannii var. rigida, commonly known as rigid Gleichenia, is a fern species native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that form a thick, mat-like growth. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its long, narrow leaves that are often curled and have a leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
