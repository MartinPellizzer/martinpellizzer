Genista × uribe-echebarriae

Genista × uribe-echebarriae, commonly known as the hybrid broom.

This plant is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Genista tinctoria and Genista ulicina. It is a shrub characterized by its dense, spiny growth and small, yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky garden environments.
