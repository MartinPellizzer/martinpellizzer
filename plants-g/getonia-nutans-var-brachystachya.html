Getonia nutans var. brachystachya

It lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on its host plant for nutrients, typically growing on the roots of grasses or other herbaceous species. Due to its parasitic nature, it plays a unique role in ecological systems by influencing host plant growth and nutrient cycling.
