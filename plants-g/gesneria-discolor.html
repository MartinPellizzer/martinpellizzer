Gesneria discolor

Gesneria discolor, commonly known as the silver stripe gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking silver-green variegated leaves with prominent white to light green stripes. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, typically blooming in the summer months.

Gesneria discolor is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
