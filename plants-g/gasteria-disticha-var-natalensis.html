Gasteria disticha var. natalensis

Gasteria disticha var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal carpet plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white veins. The plant produces pale green to yellow flowers in summer, which are borne on short, upright stalks. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water.

Its compact size and unique foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
