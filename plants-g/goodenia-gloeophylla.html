Goodenia gloeophylla

Goodenia gloeophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as plains and sandplains.

Goodenia gloeophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
