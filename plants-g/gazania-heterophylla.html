Gazania heterophylla

Gazania heterophylla, commonly known as the variable gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has a low-growing, mat-forming habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Gazania heterophylla is known for its ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
