Geranium eriostemon f. megalanthum

Geranium eriostemon f. megalanthum, commonly known as the giant-leaved geranium, is a horticultural form of the species Geranium eriostemon.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple, with five petals and a distinctive calyx. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
