Geranium undulatum

Geranium undulatum, commonly known as the wave geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with wavy edges, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Geranium undulatum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
