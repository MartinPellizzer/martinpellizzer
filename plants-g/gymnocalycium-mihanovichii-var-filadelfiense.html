Gymnocalycium mihanovichii var. filadelfiense

Gymnocalycium mihanovichii var. filadelfiense, commonly known as the Filadelfia cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Argentina.

It features a smooth, pale green to blue-gray stem covered in white, radial spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, typically at night. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.

This variety is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun conditions.
