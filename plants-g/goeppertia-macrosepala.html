Goeppertia macrosepala

Goeppertia macrosepala, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length, making it a dramatic addition to any indoor or outdoor space. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps over time.

Goeppertia macrosepala is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.
