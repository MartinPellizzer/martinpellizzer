Genista alpina

Genista alpina, commonly known as the alpine broom, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces yellow flowers in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are small, trifoliate, and covered in fine hairs.

Genista alpina thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
