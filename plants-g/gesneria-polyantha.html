Gesneria polyantha

Gesneria polyantha, commonly known as the variegated gesneria, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, which add visual interest to any garden setting. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in the spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria polyantha is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.
