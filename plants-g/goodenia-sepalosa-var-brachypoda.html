Goodenia sepalosa var. brachypoda

Goodenia sepalosa var. brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stemmed goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, and the flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

This variety is notable for its compact growth habit and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
