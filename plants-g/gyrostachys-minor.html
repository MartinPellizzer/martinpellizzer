Gyrostachys minor

Gyrostachys minor, commonly known as the lesser gyrostachys, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy environments. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are often pink or white in color. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.
