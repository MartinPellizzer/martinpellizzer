Geranium henryi var. wilsonii

Geranium henryi var. wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's geranium, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant features upright stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
