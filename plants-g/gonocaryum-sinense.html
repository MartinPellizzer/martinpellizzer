Gonocaryum sinense

Gonocaryum sinense, commonly known as the Chinese sand orchid, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Gonocaryum sinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
