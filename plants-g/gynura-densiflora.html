Gynura densiflora

Gynura densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered gynura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dense clusters of small yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Gynura densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
