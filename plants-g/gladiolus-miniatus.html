Gladiolus miniatus

Gladiolus miniatus, commonly known as the miniature gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact size and vibrant, showy blooms. The plant typically features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of colorful flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its beauty and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
