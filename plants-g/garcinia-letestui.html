Garcinia letestui

Garcinia letestui, commonly known as the Malagasy Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are rich in bioactive compounds, making it of interest in phytochemical research. Its habitat is typically moist, shaded forest understories, where it thrives in humid conditions.

Due to its unique chemical composition, it has been studied for potential medicinal and nutritional applications.
