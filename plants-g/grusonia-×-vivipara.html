Grusonia × vivipara

Grusonia × vivipara, commonly known as the viviparous geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is native to South Africa and is known for its ability to produce live young through vivipary, a unique reproductive trait. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Grusonia × vivipara is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
