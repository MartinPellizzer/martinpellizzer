Genista arbusensis

Genista arbusensis, commonly known as the Arbus Valley broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Genista arbusensis is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
