Gesneria lindeniana

Gesneria lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindena's gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, featuring glossy, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria lindeniana is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
