Gunnera petaloidea var. mauiensis

Gunnera petaloidea var. mauiensis, commonly known as the Maui giant shield fern, is a striking plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically near streams or in rocky areas. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, hairy tissue that helps retain moisture.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens for its unique foliage.
