Gardenia volkensii

Gardenia volkensii, commonly known as the East African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, waxy white flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia volkensii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
