Gesneria rupicola var. pulcherrima

Gesneria rupicola var. pulcherrima, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a hybrid plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces bright red, orange, or yellow blooms during the holiday season, which is why it is often associated with Christmas. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a sought-after ornamental plant.
