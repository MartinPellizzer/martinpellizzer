Gynochthodes reticulata

Gynochthodes reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate veining patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Gynochthodes reticulata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
