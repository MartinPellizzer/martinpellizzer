Goodenia sepalosa var. sepalosa

Goodenia sepalosa var. sepalosa, commonly known as the pink goodenia, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive pink to purple flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, elongated fruit capsules. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
