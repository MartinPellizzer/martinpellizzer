Grevillea parviflora

Grevillea parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom from spring to autumn. It is well adapted to dry conditions and requires well-drained soil.

Grevillea parviflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract native birds.
