Gynoxys dilloniana

Gynoxys dilloniana, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white or pale green spines. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and collections.
