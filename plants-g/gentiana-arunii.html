Gentiana arunii

Gentiana arunii, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at elevations above 1,500 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine downy coating.

Gentiana arunii is a perennial species that thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade.
