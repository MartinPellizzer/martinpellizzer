Gethyllis pectinata

Gethyllis pectinata, commonly known as the woolly bluemound, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features dense, woolly foliage and produces small, delicate blue to purple flowers. It is often found in grassland habitats and thrives in full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
