Geranium × splendens

Geranium × splendens, commonly known as the ivy-leaved geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and purple. The foliage is heart-shaped and often has a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for container gardening and hanging baskets.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
