Genista mugronensis subsp. rigidissima

Genista mugronensis subsp.

rigidissima, commonly known as the rigid thorned broom, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged in pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in Mediterranean climates.
