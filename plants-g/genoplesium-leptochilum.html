Genoplesium leptochilum

Genoplesium leptochilum, commonly known as the slender-lipped sun-orchid, is a striking orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often pale yellow or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single, showy flower per stem.

This species is noted for its unique floral structure and is often found in coastal or heathland habitats.
