Gorteria spinosa

Gorteria spinosa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant features wiry, spiny stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Gorteria spinosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
