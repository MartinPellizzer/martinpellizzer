Germanea myriantha

Germanea myriantha, commonly known as the starleaf lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It features striking, star-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in cooler climates.

Germanea myriantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in partial shade.
