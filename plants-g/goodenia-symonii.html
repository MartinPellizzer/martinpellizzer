Goodenia symonii

Goodenia symonii, commonly known as the Symon's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
