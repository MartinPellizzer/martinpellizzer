Genista brugnieri

Genista brugnieri, commonly known as the brugnieri broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to arid conditions. Its branching structure and dense foliage make it a hardy, ornamental species.

It is often used in landscaping for its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
