Garcinia nuntasaenii

Garcinia nuntasaenii, commonly known as the Bornean garcinia, is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to orange when mature.

Garcinia nuntasaenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has potential applications in traditional medicine.
