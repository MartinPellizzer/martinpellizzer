Gesneria warmingii

Gesneria warmingii, commonly known as the Warming's gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central America.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves that are often slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces striking, tubular-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture without being waterlogged.

Gesneria warmingii is well-suited for indoor cultivation and is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care.
