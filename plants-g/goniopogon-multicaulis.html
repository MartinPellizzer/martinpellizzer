Goniopogon multicaulis

Goniopogon multicaulis, commonly known as the many-stemmed grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It features a robust root system and produces multiple stems that are often branched at the base. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in grasslands and open habitats.

Goniopogon multicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
