Goodenia chambersii

Goodenia chambersii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in late spring to early summer.
