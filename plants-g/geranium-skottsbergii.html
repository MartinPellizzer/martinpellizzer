Geranium skottsbergii

Geranium skottsbergii, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers with five petals, typical of the Geraniaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
