Grevillea cinerea var. myrtacea

Grevillea cinerea var. myrtacea, commonly known as the silver-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers in cylindrical clusters, which are highly attractive to birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
