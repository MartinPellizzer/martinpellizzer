Globulea mollis

Globulea mollis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple, blue, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Globulea mollis is known for its ability to produce numerous blooms throughout the year under optimal conditions.
