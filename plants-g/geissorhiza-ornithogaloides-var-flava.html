Geissorhiza ornithogaloides var. flava

Geissorhiza ornithogaloides var. flava, commonly known as the yellow orchid-bulb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, which includes orchids, and is characterized by its tuberous roots and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers with a distinctive orchid-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
