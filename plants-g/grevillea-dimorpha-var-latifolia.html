Grevillea dimorpha var. latifolia

Grevillea dimorpha var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation in horticulture.
