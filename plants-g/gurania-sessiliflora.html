Gurania sessiliflora

Gurania sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flowered gurania, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with four to five petals.

Gurania sessiliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
