Gnidia anomala var. villosissima

Gnidia anomala var. villosissima, commonly known as the hairy gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are often surrounded by pale green bracts. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in forested areas.

The hairy gnidia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
