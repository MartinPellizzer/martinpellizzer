Galium sylvestre var. laeve

Galium sylvestre var. laeve, commonly known as smooth wood-rose, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, ascending stems that are often covered with short, stiff hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly hairy surface.

Galium sylvestre var. laeve is often found in moist, shaded woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
