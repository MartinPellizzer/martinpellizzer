Glossopetalon spinescens var. typicum

Glossopetalon spinescens var. typicum, commonly known as the spiny glossopetalon, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiny leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it blooms during the wet season.
