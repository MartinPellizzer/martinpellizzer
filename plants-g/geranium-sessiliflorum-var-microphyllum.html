Geranium sessiliflorum var. microphyllum

Geranium sessiliflorum var. microphyllum, commonly known as the miniature geranium, is a compact, low-growing perennial native to southern Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves that are densely packed, giving it a lush, mat-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of delicate, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink and red, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to both garden and indoor settings.
