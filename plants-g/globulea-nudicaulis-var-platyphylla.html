Globulea nudicaulis var. platyphylla

Globulea nudicaulis var. platyphylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed globulea, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of white or pale pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with fleshy, succulent leaves that are broad and rounded. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
