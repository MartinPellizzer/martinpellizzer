Gomesa planifolia var. densa

Gomesa planifolia var. densa, commonly known as the dense flatleaf gomesa, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves that are densely packed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
