Geranium lamberti var. backhousianum

Geranium lamberti var. backhousianum, commonly known as the Backhouse geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant produces vibrant pink to red blooms during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a bedding plant.
