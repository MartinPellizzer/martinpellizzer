Gastrorchis lutea var. geffrayi

Gastrorchis lutea var. geffrayi, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories where it receives indirect sunlight. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and a specialized root system adapted for nutrient absorption in nutrient-poor soils.
