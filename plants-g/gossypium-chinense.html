Gossypium chinense

Gossypium chinense, commonly known as Chinese cotton, is a species of shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It produces fluffy fibers within its seed pods, which are used for textile production. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a hairy surface.

The flowers are usually pink or white, and the plant is valued for both its fibers and ornamental qualities.
