Gyrocarpus asiaticus var. philippinensis

Gyrocarpus asiaticus var. philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The tree is valued for its aromatic seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
