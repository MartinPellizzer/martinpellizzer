Gomphostemma strobilinum var. heyneanum

Gomphostemma strobilinum var. heyneanum, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a member of the Menispermaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Native to tropical Africa, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
