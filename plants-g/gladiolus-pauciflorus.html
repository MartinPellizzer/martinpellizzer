Gladiolus pauciflorus

Gladiolus pauciflorus, commonly known as the few-flowered gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, sword-shaped leaves and showy, terminal inflorescences. The plant produces clusters of brightly colored, spurred flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental gardens and botanical collections.
