Grevillea leiophylla

Grevillea leiophylla, commonly known as the coastal grevillea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in coastal heathlands and sand dunes, thriving in well-drained, sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters. It is well adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.

Grevillea leiophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
