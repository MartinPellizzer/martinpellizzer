Gymnadenia minor

Gymnadenia minor, commonly known as the lesser grass-of-Parnassus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, tuberous roots.
