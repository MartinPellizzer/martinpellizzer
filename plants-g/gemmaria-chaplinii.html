Gemmaria chaplinii

Gemmaria chaplinii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that often display a striking combination of colors. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in semi-arid environments. Its flowering period is usually during the warmer months, and it requires specific conditions to bloom successfully.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
