Guarea subsessiliflora var. polyphyllaria

Guarea subsessiliflora var. polyphyllaria, commonly known as the polyphylla guarea, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with dense, dark green foliage. The leaves are typically compound, consisting of multiple leaflets arranged in a pinnate pattern. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a distinctive yellowish or greenish color.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds, contributing to its role in forest ecosystems.
