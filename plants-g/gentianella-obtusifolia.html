Gentianella obtusifolia

Gentianella obtusifolia, commonly known as the bluntleaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its deep blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are broad and rounded, with a distinctive bluish-green hue.

Gentianella obtusifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
