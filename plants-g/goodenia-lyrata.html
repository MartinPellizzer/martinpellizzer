Goodenia lyrata

Goodenia lyrata, commonly known as the lyreleaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive lyre-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Goodenia lyrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
