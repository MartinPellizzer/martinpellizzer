Gutierrezia leucantha

Gutierrezia leucantha, commonly known as white galleta, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The yellow flowers bloom in late summer and early fall, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
