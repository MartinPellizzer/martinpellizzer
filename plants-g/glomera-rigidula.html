Glomera rigidula

Glomera rigidula, commonly known as the rigid glomera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
