Geranium angulatum

Geranium angulatum, commonly known as the angular geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive angular leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
