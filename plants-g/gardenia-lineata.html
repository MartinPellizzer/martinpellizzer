Gardenia lineata

Gardenia lineata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are narrow and elongated. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom in summer. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia lineata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and pleasant scent.
