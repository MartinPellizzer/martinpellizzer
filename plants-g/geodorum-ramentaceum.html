Geodorum ramentaceum

Geodorum ramentaceum, commonly known as the bamboo orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, grass-like leaves and striking, lance-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps.

Geodorum ramentaceum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
