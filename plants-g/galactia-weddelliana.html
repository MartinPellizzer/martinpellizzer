Galactia weddelliana

Galactia weddelliana, commonly known as Weddell's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Galactia weddelliana is noted for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
