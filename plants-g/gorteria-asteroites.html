Gorteria asteroites

Gorteria asteroites, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Gorteria asteroites is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, daisy-like appearance.
