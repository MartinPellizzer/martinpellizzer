Gravesia biauriculata

Gravesia biauriculata, commonly known as the two-lobed gravesia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins and a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Gravesia biauriculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fragrant blooms.
