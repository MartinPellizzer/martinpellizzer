Gesneria adenantha

Gesneria adenantha, commonly known as the silverleaf gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria adenantha is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
