Gramen rubrum

Gramen rubrum, commonly known as the red grass.

It is a species of grass native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red coloration, which is most noticeable in its inflorescence. Gramen rubrum typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
