Goodyera brachyceras

Goodyera brachyceras, commonly known as the broad-leaved lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It features dense, dark green foliage with broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, with a distinctive lip that resembles a slipper. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically in forest understories.

Goodyera brachyceras is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found growing in association with other epiphytic and terrestrial orchids.
