Gouldia terminalis f. teres

Gouldia terminalis f. teres, commonly known as the compact gouldia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Australia.

It features dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. The foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. This ornamental grass thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

It is often used in landscaping for its attractive appearance and ability to form neat, compact clumps.
