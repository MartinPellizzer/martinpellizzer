Gardenia acuminata

Gardenia acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom during the summer months. Gardenia acuminata prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for cultivation in humid climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
