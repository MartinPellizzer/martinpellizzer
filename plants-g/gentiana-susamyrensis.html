Gentiana susamyrensis

Gentiana susamyrensis, commonly known as the Korean gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late summer.

Gentiana susamyrensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
