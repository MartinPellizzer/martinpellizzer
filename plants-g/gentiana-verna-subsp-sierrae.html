Gentiana verna subsp. sierrae

Gentiana verna subsp.

sierrae, commonly known as Sierra spring gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
