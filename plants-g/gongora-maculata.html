Gongora maculata

Gongora maculata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers with distinctive markings. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and the flowers bloom in spring or early summer.

Gongora maculata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
