Genista liparioides

Genista liparioides, commonly known as the Lipari shrub or Sicilian broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with slender, often branched stems and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Genista liparioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
