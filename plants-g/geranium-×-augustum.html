Geranium × augustum

Geranium × augustum, commonly known as the Regal Geranium, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from various geranium species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The foliage consists of heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of pink, red, and purple, are borne on long, slender stalks and are highly attractive to pollinators.

This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
