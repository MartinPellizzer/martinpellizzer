Gentiana calycosa var. stricta

Gentiana calycosa var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a deep green color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
