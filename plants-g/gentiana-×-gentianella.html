Gentiana × gentianella

Gentiana × gentianella, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Gentiana × gentianella is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
