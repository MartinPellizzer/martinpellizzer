Gnaphalium elegantissimum

Gnaphalium elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces a fine, feathery foliage and is often found in grasslands and open areas. It is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its common name reflects its long-lasting, dried flower heads that retain their color and form.
