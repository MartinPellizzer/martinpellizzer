Guioa truncata

Guioa truncata, commonly known as the Philippine guioa, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate, elliptic to ovate leaves with a truncate or slightly notched base. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are borne in clusters, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed.
