Gnaphalium solidaginoides

Gnaphalium solidaginoides, commonly known as golden ragwort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It has slender, upright stems and small, linear leaves.

The plant produces a large number of small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
