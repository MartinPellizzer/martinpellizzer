Gouldia terminalis f. kehena

Gouldia terminalis f. kehena, commonly known as the Kehena Gouldia, is a rare and striking variant of the Gouldia genus.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like inflorescences that display a range of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Australia and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

This ornamental plant is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in specialized gardens and botanical collections.
