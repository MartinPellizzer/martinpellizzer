Gentianella simplex

Gentianella simplex, commonly known as the simple gentian, is a small flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Gentianella simplex is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
