Gomphocentrum tenuipes

Gomphocentrum tenuipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, elongated leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Gomphocentrum tenuipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral structure.
