Gossia clusioides subsp. clusioides

Gossia clusioides subsp.

clusioides, commonly known as the glossy buckthorn, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often glossy in appearance.

The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are red, fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
