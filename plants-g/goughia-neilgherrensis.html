Goughia neilgherrensis

Goughia neilgherrensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri goughia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate aesthetic to its foliage. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Goughia neilgherrensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
