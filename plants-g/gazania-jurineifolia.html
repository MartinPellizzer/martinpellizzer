Gazania jurineifolia

Gazania jurineifolia, commonly known as the Cape gold or yellow gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, featuring fleshy, ribbed stems and thick, waxy leaves. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in summer. It is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Gazania jurineifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
