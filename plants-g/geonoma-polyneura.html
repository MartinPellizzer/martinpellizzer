Geonoma polyneura

Geonoma polyneura, commonly known as the feather-leaf fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are bioluminescent, a rare trait among ferns, which helps in attracting nocturnal pollinators.

The plant's unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
