Garcinia nitidula

Garcinia nitidula, commonly known as the white garcinia, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The fruit is a bright red, fleshy drupe with a single seed inside, and it is often used in traditional medicine.
