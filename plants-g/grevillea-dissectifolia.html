Grevillea dissectifolia

Grevillea dissectifolia, commonly known as the dissected grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely divided leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea dissectifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
