Gentiana chingii

Gentiana chingii, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered with fine hairs.

The flowers bloom in late summer and are known for their medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
