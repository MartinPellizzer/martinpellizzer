Geranium triangulare

Geranium triangulare, commonly known as the triangular geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value and adaptability have made it a favored plant in horticulture.
