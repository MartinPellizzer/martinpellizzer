Globba leucantha var. flavidula

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.
