Genista subcapitata var. holosericea

Genista subcapitata var. holosericea, commonly known as the silky broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, spiny stems that are often covered in a fine, silky white down. The plant produces small, yellow flower clusters that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and trifoliate, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
