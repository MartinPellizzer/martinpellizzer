Gasteria glauca

Gasteria glauca, commonly known as the blue gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria glauca is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
