Gongora heisteri

Gongora heisteri, commonly known as the Heister's gongora, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that feature intricate patterns and a unique, elongated lip. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests or mountainous areas. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Gongora heisteri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its exotic floral display.
