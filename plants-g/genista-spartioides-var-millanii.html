Genista spartioides var. millanii

Genista spartioides var. millanii, commonly known as the Millan's broom.

This variant is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula, characterized by its dense, thorny growth and small, yellowish-green leaves. It produces yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. The plant is adapted to rocky and dry soils, thriving in Mediterranean climates.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and hardy species within the Genista genus.
