Gravesia macrophylla f. uliginosa

Gravesia macrophylla f. uliginosa, commonly known as the swamp gravesia, is a variant of the Gravesia macrophylla species native to Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in swampy or marshy areas. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange, attracting various pollinators.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ornamental species suitable for specific garden conditions.
