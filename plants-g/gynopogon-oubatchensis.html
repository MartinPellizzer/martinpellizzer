Gynopogon oubatchensis

Gynopogon oubatchensis, commonly known as the Oubatchi grass, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Gynopogon oubatchensis is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is an important component of the local vegetation in its native habitat.
