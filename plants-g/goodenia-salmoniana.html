Goodenia salmoniana

Goodenia salmoniana, commonly known as the salmon-flowered goodenia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking salmon-pink to orange-red flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal areas. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves, and it produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
