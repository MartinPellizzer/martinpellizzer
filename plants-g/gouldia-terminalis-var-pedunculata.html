Gouldia terminalis var. pedunculata

Gouldia terminalis var. pedunculata, commonly known as the red kangaroo paw, is a striking plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, spurred flowers that resemble the shape of a kangaroo's paw. The plant belongs to the family Proteaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
