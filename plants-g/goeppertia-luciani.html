Goeppertia luciani

Goeppertia luciani, commonly known as the Luciani's calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns and variegation. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, often featuring a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
