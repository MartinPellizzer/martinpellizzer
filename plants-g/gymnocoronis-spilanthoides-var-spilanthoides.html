Gymnocoronis spilanthoides var. spilanthoides

Gymnocoronis spilanthoides var. spilanthoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, cylindrical stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense inflorescences at the tips of the branches.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
