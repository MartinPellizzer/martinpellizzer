Geniostoma longipes

Geniostoma longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
