Genoplesium morinum

Genoplesium morinum, commonly known as the Morinum orchid, is a rare and striking terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern region of Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, hooded labellum and elongated, strap-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in a spike, making it visually distinct. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically in rainforest or understorey habitats.
