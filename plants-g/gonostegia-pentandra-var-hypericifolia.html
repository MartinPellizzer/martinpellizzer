Gonostegia pentandra var. hypericifolia

Gonostegia pentandra var. hypericifolia, commonly known as the coastal heath, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to salt spray and strong winds. Its leaves are leathery and aromatic, often with a slight resinous scent.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for native wildlife.
