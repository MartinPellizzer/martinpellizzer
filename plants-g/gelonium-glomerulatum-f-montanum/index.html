Gelonium Glomerulatum F. Montanum

Gelonium glomerulatum f. montanum is a rare and striking plant known for its unique rosette structure and vibrant blue-green foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, this cultivar thrives in cooler, high-altitude environments. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens and alpine displays. The plant's ability to form dense clusters adds visual interest to any landscape. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species among horticultural enthusiasts.
