Geranium cinereum

Geranium cinereum, commonly known as the blue geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Geranium cinereum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
