Gentianella fabrisii

Gentianella fabrisii, commonly known as the Sicilian gentian, is a flowering plant native to the island of Sicily.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Gentianella fabrisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
