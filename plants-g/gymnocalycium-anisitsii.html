Gymnocalycium anisitsii

Gymnocalycium anisitsii, commonly known as the Anisitsi prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its barrel-shaped stem and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has spiny areoles and produces edible fruits similar to other opuntias. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Gymnocalycium anisitsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
