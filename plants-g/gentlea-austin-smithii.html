Gentlea austin-smithii

Gentlea austin-smithii, commonly known as the Austin-Smith's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its showy, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its flowers are tubular and often have a distinctive pattern of darker markings.

It is a perennial herb that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing its seeds.
