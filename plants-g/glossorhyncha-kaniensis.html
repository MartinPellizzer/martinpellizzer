Glossorhyncha kaniensis

Glossorhyncha kaniensis, commonly known as the Kaniensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayan regions of India.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, intricate flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forested areas. Its leaves are linear and often covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

The orchid is notable for its specialized pollination mechanism, which involves specific insect species.
