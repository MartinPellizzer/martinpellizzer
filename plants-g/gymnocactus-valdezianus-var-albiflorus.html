Gymnocactus valdezianus var. albiflorus

Gymnocactus valdezianus var. albiflorus, commonly known as the Whiteflower Gymnocactus, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green and ribbed, with a smooth, spineless surface. The plant produces striking white flowers that bloom during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
