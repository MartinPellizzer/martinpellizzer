Geranium thunbergii f. roseum

Geranium thunbergii f. roseum, commonly known as the roseum geranium, is a hybrid cultivar of the species Geranium thunbergii.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, double flowers that range from pink to deep red. The plant produces velvety, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver or white underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
