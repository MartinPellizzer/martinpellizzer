Geigeria schinzii subsp. schinzii

Geigeria schinzii subsp.

schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's geigeria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers.
