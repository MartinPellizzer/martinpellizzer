Geranium microphyllum

Geranium microphyllum, commonly known as the miniature geranium, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in diameter, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to purple flowers throughout the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its delicate appearance and easy care make it a favored ornamental plant in many gardens.
