Goodenia leptoclada

Goodenia leptoclada, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, often hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
