Grevillea newbeyi

Grevillea newbeyi, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and vibrant red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

Grevillea newbeyi is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
