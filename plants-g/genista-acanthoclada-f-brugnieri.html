Genista acanthoclada f. brugnieri

Genista acanthoclada f. brugnieri, commonly known as the Brugniera genista, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky or arid soils and is often found in coastal areas.

This variety is noted for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
