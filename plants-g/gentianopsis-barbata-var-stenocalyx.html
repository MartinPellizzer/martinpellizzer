Gentianopsis barbata var. stenocalyx

Gentianopsis barbata var. stenocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-calyx gentian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are clustered in inflorescences.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
