Gerbera aspleniifolia

Gerbera aspleniifolia, commonly known as the fern gerbera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its ornamental, daisy-like flowers. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and indoor cultivation.

Its blooms, which come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, add vibrant color to its environment.
