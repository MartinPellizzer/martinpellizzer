Gorteria barbata

Gorteria barbata, commonly known as the woolly gorteria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Gorteria barbata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid environments.
