Gasteria glabra

Gasteria glabra, commonly known as the bare-leaf gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. Gasteria glabra is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its hardiness and attractive appearance.
