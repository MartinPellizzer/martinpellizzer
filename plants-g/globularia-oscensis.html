Globularia oscensis

Globularia oscensis, commonly known as the Canary Islands globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing herb with dense, globular clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the dry, coastal climates of its native habitat.

Globularia oscensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
