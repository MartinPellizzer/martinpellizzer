Geranium molle f. stipulare

Geranium molle f. stipulare, commonly known as the soft geranium or velvet geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant is native to southern Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile choice for various garden settings.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
