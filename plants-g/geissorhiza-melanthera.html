Geissorhiza melanthera

Geissorhiza melanthera, commonly known as the black-leafed orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking dark purple to black flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a dark green to almost black coloration.

Geissorhiza melanthera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
