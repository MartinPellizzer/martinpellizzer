Goodenia intermedia

Goodenia intermedia, commonly known as the common goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
