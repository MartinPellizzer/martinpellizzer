Geissorhiza mathewsii var. eurystigma

Geissorhiza mathewsii var. eurystigma, commonly known as the giant orchid or Mathews' orchid, is a striking South African plant belonging to the orchid family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 cm in diameter, typically in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant is a terrestrial orchid with a robust rhizome system, which helps it survive in its native habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or grassland areas.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and conservationists alike.
