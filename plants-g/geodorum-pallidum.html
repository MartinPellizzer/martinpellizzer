Geodorum pallidum

Geodorum pallidum, commonly known as the pale ginger orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It produces striking, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spike and are pollinated by insects.

Geodorum pallidum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
