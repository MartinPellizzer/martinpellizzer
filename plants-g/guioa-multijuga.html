Guioa multijuga

Guioa multijuga, commonly known as the Philippine ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, aromatic bark. Due to its hard, durable wood, it has been historically valued for timber and traditional crafts.
