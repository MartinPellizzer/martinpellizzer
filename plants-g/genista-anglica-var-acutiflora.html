Genista anglica var. acutiflora

Genista anglica var. acutiflora, commonly known as the English broom or sharpflower genista, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
