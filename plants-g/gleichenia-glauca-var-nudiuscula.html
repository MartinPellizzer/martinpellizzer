Gleichenia glauca var. nudiuscula

Gleichenia glauca var. nudiuscula, commonly known as the slender Gleichenia, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a bluish-green color, with a slightly waxy texture.

The varietal form, nudiuscula, distinguishes itself by its reduced, more compact growth habit compared to the typical variety.
