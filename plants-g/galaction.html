Galaction

Galaction, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray, fuzzy leaves. Native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean, it thrives in salty, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
