Geraniospermum tricolor

Geraniospermum tricolor, commonly known as the three-colored geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its strikingly colorful flowers that display three distinct hues. The plant typically grows as a shrub with palmate leaves and produces small, showy blooms in shades of red, yellow, and white.
