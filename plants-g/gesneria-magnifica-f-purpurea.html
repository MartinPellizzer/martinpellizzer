Gesneria magnifica f. purpurea

Gesneria magnifica f. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Flame Gesneria, is a striking hybrid plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety purple foliage. The leaves are broad, elliptical, and often have a waxy texture, with a deep purple to almost black coloration. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in spring or early summer.

This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
