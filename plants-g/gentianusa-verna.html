Gentianusa verna

Gentianusa verna, commonly known as spring gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, white down.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
