Gesneria ventricosa var. obovata

Gesneria ventricosa var. obovata, commonly known as the Obovate Gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its broad, ovate-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
