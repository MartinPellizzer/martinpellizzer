Geranium frigidurbis

Geranium frigidurbis, commonly known as the cold city geranium, is a hardy perennial plant native to temperate regions.

It features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and red, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its resilience to cooler climates makes it a popular choice for gardeners in colder urban environments.
