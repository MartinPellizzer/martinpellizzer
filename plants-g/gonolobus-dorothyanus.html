Gonolobus dorothyanus

Gonolobus dorothyanus, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, orange to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and can fix nitrogen in the soil.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees.
