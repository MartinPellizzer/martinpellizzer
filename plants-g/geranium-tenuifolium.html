Geranium tenuifolium

Geranium tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that are typically narrow and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Geranium tenuifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
