Gentianella yabei

Gentianella yabei, commonly known as the Yabei gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in early summer.

Gentianella yabei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
