Geranium rosthornii

Geranium rosthornii, commonly known as the Rosthorn geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms during the summer months. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Geranium rosthornii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
