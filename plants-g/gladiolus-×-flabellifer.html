Gladiolus × flabellifer

Gladiolus × flabellifer, commonly known as the fan-flowered gladiolus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with sword-shaped leaves and a tall, sturdy stem. Its flowers are arranged in a raceme and feature three outer petals called tepals and three inner stamens.

This species is cultivated for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
