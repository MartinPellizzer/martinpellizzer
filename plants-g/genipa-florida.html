Genipa florida

Genipa florida, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or genipapo, is a small evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit.
