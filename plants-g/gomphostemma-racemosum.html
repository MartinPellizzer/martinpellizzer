Gomphostemma racemosum

Gomphostemma racemosum, commonly known as the racemose gomphostemma, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences, followed by berries that are typically orange or red in color.
