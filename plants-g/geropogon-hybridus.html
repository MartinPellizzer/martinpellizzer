Geropogon hybridus

Geropogon hybridus, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features upright stems and trifoliate leaves, which are often hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Geropogon hybridus is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
