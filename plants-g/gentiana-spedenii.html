Gentiana spedenii

Gentiana spedenii, commonly known as the Spedding gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained, rocky soils. Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early autumn, and it is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and botanical collections.
