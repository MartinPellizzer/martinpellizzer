Gavilea odoratissima

Gavilea odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet sarsaparilla, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its fragrant properties and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Polygonaceae family.
