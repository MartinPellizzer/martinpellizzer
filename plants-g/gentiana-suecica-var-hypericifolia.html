Gentiana suecica var. hypericifolia

Gentiana suecica var. hypericifolia, commonly known as the Swedish gentian, is a flowering plant native to northern Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in mountainous regions. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is known for its showy, spurred petals.

The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.
