Gasteria bijliae

Gasteria bijliae, commonly known as the Orange River Gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white markings. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Gasteria bijliae is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in low-maintenance gardening.
