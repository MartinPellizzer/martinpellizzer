Garcinia balica

Garcinia balica, commonly known as the Bornean garcinia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into orange-red berries. The plant is often found in lowland rainforests and secondary growth areas. Its fruits contain seeds surrounded by a thick, aromatic pulp.

Garcinia balica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
