Grevillea scapigera

Grevillea scapigera, commonly known as the scarlet grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces long, slender inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea scapigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds.
