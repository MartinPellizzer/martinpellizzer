Gesneria macrorhiza

Gesneria macrorhiza, commonly known as the large-rooted gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy roots and broad, glossy leaves. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Gesneria macrorhiza is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
