Gesneria betonicifolia

Gesneria betonicifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria betonicifolia is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and ease of care when provided with the right conditions.
