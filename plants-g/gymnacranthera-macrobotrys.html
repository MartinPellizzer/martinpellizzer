Gymnacranthera macrobotrys

Gymnacranthera macrobotrys, commonly known as the large-flowered sand orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, creeping rhizome and produces numerous small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal regions. Its flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and moths.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
