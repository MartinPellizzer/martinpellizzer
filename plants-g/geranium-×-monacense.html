Geranium × monacense

Geranium × monacense, commonly known as the regal geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features heart-shaped, serrated leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Geranium × monacense is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
