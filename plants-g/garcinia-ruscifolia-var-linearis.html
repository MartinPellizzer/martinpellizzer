Garcinia ruscifolia var. linearis

Garcinia ruscifolia var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries that contain a single seed. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and seasonal fruiting.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
