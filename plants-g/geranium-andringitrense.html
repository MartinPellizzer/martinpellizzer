Geranium andringitrense

Geranium andringitrense, commonly known as the Madagascar geranium, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb with opposite, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
