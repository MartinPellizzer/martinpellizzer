Galanthus elwesii subsp. baytopii

Galanthus elwesii subsp.

baytopii, commonly known as the Baytop's snowdrop, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Turkey. It produces delicate, white, nodding flowers with a greenish tinge, typically blooming in early spring.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and prefers well-drained, moist soils in partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring display.

This subspecies is closely related to other snowdrop species but is distinguished by its unique floral characteristics and geographic distribution.
