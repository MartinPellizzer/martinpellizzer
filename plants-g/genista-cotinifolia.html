Genista cotinifolia

Genista cotinifolia, commonly known as the silverbush broom, is a deciduous shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features slender, arching stems covered in small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of several pairs of oval, leathery leaflets. Its silver-gray foliage provides a striking contrast to its vibrant yellow blooms.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
