Geranium Aristisepalum Var. Typicum

Geranium aristisepalum var. typicum is a striking member of the Geraniaceae family known for its vibrant flowers and unique foliage. This perennial plant is native to regions of Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its showy blooms, often in shades of pink and red, make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The plant's distinctive leaf structure and upright growth habit add visual interest to any setting. With proper care, it can become a rewarding addition to both wild and cultivated environments.
