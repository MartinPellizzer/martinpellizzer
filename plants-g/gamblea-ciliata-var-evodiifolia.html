Gamblea ciliata var. evodiifolia

Gamblea ciliata var. evodiifolia, commonly known as the slender gambelia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and blooms.
