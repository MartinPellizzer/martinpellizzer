Globba mollis

Globba mollis, commonly known as velvetleaf ginger, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces striking, showy inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Globba mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
