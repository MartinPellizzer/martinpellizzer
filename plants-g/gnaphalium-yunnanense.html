Gnaphalium yunnanense

Gnaphalium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan featherflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its small, fluffy flowers that resemble miniature clouds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in alpine meadows and forested areas. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its high-altitude habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
