Gynostemma pentagynum

Gynostemma pentagynum, commonly known as Chinese ginseng or jiao ginseng, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is renowned for its medicinal properties, particularly its rich content of gynostemonin and other bioactive compounds. It thrives in humid, temperate climates and is often cultivated for its leaves, which are used in traditional medicine.

The plant's unique morphology and pharmacological potential make it a subject of interest in both botanical and medical research.
