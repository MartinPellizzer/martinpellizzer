Gethyllis lata subsp. lata

Gethyllis lata subsp.

lata, commonly known as the broadleaf gethyllis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late summer.

The plant has a compact growth habit with fleshy, succulent leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry garden settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
