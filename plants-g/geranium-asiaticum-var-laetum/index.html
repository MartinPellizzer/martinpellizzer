Geranium Asiaticum Var. Laetum

Asian geranium, scientifically known as Geranium asiaticum var. laetum, is a captivating perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia. It is prized for its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions. This variety is particularly noted for its clusters of pink to purple blooms that appear in late spring and early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its ornamental value and resilience have made it a favored addition to both cultivated gardens and natural landscapes.
