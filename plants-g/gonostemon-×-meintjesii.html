Gonostemon × meintjesii

Gonostemon × meintjesii, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Gonostemon acutifolius and Gonostemon hirtellus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink, with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
