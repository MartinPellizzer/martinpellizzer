Grammitis tristis

Grammitis tristis, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Grammitis tristis has a rhizomatous growth habit, with small, clustered fronds emerging from the underground stems.

Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and produce spores for propagation.
