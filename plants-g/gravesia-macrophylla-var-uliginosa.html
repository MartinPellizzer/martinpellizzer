Gravesia macrophylla var. uliginosa

Gravesia macrophylla var. uliginosa, commonly known as the swamp gravesia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in swampy or wetland habitats. Its leaves are typically large, broad, and leathery, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The species is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to tolerate waterlogged soils.
