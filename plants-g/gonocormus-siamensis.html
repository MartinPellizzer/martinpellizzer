Gonocormus siamensis

Gonocormus siamensis, commonly known as the Siam mountain orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant orchid flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
