Genitia

Genitia, commonly known as the genus *Genista*, is a group of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

These shrubs are characterized by their trifoliate leaves and yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. *Genista* species are often found in temperate regions and are sometimes used in landscaping for their ornamental value.

Some species are also used in traditional medicine or as forage for livestock.
