Garcinia xanthochymus

Garcinia xanthochymus, commonly known as the yellow Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow fruits, which are covered in a thick, waxy coating. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy green surface.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its potential health benefits.
