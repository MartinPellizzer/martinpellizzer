Gleichenia peltophora var. peltophora

Gleichenia peltophora var. peltophora, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a vascular fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a compact rosette pattern. The fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, narrow shape. This variety is native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, shaded environments.

It exhibits a unique reproductive structure with sporangia that are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds.
