Gesneria sintenisii

Gesneria sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically orange or red in color, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria sintenisii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
