Gastrochilus minutiflorus

Gastrochilus minutiflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered stomach lip, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tiny, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. The plant grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Gastrochilus minutiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
