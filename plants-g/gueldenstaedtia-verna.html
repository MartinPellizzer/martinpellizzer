Gueldenstaedtia verna

Gueldenstaedtia verna, commonly known as the spring vetchling, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

Gueldenstaedtia verna is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.
