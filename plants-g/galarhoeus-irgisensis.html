Galarhoeus irgisensis

Galarhoeus irgisensis, commonly known as the Irqisian flamebush, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant has a woody stem with thick, fleshy leaves adapted to conserve water. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Galarhoeus irgisensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and resilience in harsh environments.
