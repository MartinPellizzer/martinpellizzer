Geuncus

Geuncus, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
