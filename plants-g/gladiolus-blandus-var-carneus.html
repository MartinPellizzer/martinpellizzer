Gladiolus blandus var. carneus

Gladiolus blandus var. carneus, commonly known as the pink gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep magenta, often with a contrasting throat. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with sword-like, grassy leaves that are arranged in a fan shape. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of blooms, which are borne on tall, sturdy stems.

The botanical characteristics of this variety make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
