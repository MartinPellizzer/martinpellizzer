Goniochilus leochilinus

Goniochilus leochilinus, commonly known as the orchid with the distinctive white and green petals, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are known for their unique shape and fragrance, which attract specific pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by horticulturists and enthusiasts.
