Goodenia unilobata

Goodenia unilobata, commonly known as the single-lobed goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions.

Goodenia unilobata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
