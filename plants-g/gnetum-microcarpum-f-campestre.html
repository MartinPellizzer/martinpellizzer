Gnetum microcarpum f. campestre

Gnetum microcarpum f. campestre, commonly known as the dwarf gnetum, is a unique gymnosperm with distinctive botanical features.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its scale-like leaves and small, cone-like structures. The plant exhibits a peculiar combination of traits, including the presence of vessel elements in its xylem, which is rare among gymnosperms. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its morphology and reproductive structures make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
