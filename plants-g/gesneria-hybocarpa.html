Gesneria hybocarpa

Gesneria hybocarpa, commonly known as the giant gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria hybocarpa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
