Gomesa mandonii

Gomesa mandonii, commonly known as the Mandon's gomesa, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are narrow and sometimes hairy, while the flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters.
