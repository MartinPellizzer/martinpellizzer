Goodyera beccarii

Goodyera beccarii, commonly known as the beccari's mossy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, moss-like growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a spike, often resembling a cluster of moss. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically in forest understories.

Goodyera beccarii is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
