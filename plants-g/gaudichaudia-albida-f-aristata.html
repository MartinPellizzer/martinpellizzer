Gaudichaudia albida f. aristata

Gaudichaudia albida f. aristata, commonly known as the white-flowered gaudichaudia, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy white petals that contrast with its velvety green foliage. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique floral structure and elegant appearance make it a notable species in botanical collections.
