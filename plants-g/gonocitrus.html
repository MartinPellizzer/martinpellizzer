Gonocitrus

Gonocitrus, commonly known as the finger lime, is a unique citrus species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, finger-like fruits that contain a gelatinous, citrus-flavored pulp. The plant produces fragrant white flowers and has glossy, dark green leaves. It thrives in warm, subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gonocitrus is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary use in gourmet cuisine.
