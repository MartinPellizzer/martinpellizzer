Genista michelii

Genista michelii, commonly known as Michel's broom, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
