Gentiana pulchella var. ramosissima

Gentiana pulchella var. ramosissima, commonly known as the showy gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is noted for its bushy growth habit, with multiple branches arising from the base.
