Gentiana aomorensis

Gentiana aomorensis, commonly known as the Aomori gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
