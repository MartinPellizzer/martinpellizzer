Gastrochilus xiphostachyus

Gastrochilus xiphostachyus, commonly known as the Chinese orchid or the mountain orchid, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences.

Gastrochilus xiphostachyus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
