Gymnocalycium × contractum

Gymnocalycium × contractum, commonly known as the hybrid moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant typically features a colorful, fleshy calyx that surrounds the base of the stem, giving it its genus name. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Gymnocalycium × contractum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to bloom with showy, yellow or orange flowers.
