Gentiana pneumonanthe var. elliptica

Gentiana pneumonanthe var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
