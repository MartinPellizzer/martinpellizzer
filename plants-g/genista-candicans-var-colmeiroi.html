Genista candicans var. colmeiroi

Genista candicans var. colmeiroi, commonly known as the Alentejo broom, is a shrub native to southern Portugal.

It features slender, wiry stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being narrow and pointed. It thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is often found in coastal and sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
