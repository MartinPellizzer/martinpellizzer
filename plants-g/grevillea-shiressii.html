Grevillea shiressii

Grevillea shiressii, commonly known as the Shiress's grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like foliage and vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments. Its flowers are composed of numerous stamens that create a fluffy appearance, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Grevillea shiressii is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for native pollinators.
