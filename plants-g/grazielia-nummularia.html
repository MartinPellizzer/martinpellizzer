Grazielia nummularia

Grazielia nummularia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, round leaves that are typically gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
