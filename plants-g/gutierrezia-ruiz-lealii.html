Gutierrezia ruiz-lealii

Gutierrezia ruiz-lealii, commonly known as the desert gumweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has adapted to harsh desert conditions with deep root systems and a low, sprawling growth habit. Gutierrezia ruiz-lealii plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for native insects and wildlife.
