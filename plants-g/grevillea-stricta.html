Grevillea stricta

Grevillea stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and is well-suited to well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.

Grevillea stricta is also valued for its ability to attract native pollinators such as birds and insects.
