Geijera salicifolia var. angustifolia

Geijera salicifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed geijera, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to northern Australia.

It features lanceolate to narrowly elliptic leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, woody fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in native habitats.
