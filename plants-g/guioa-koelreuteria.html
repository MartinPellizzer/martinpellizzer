Guioa koelreuteria

Guioa koelreuteria, commonly known as the Chinese fanflower, is a deciduous tree native to East Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn, contributing to its ornamental value. The tree produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by distinctive winged fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique foliage and seasonal changes make it a popular choice for ornamental planting.
