Goodenia laevis subsp. humifusa

Goodenia laevis subsp.

humifusa, commonly known as the smooth goodenia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia. It typically features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color, blooming during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
