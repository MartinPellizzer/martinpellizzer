Gynerium argenteum var. albolineatum

Gynerium argenteum var. albolineatum, commonly known as the silverleaf arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features striking white veins on its broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense mats on the water's surface.

It is often used in aquatic gardening for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
