Geranium odoriferum

Geranium odoriferum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Geranium odoriferum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
