Gypsophila oldhamiana

Gypsophila oldhamiana, commonly known as the Chinese baby's breath, is a flowering plant native to China.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils. Its small, fluffy blooms are often used in floral arrangements due to their ethereal appearance.
