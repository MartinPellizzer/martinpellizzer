Gentiana pleurogynoides var. rigida

Gentiana pleurogynoides var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, and it often grows in moist, rocky habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
