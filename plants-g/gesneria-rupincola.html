Gesneria rupincola

Gesneria rupincola, commonly known as the rock gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or shaded environments, showcasing its adaptability to humid, well-drained conditions. Its leaves are often thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Gesneria rupincola is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
