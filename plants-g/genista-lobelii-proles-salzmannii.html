Genista lobelii proles salzmannii

Genista lobelii proles salzmannii, commonly known as Salzmann's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees.
