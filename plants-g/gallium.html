Gallium

Gallium, commonly known as a metallic element, is not a plant but a chemical element with the symbol Ga.

It is a silvery-white metal that is rarely found in its elemental form in nature. Gallium has a low melting point and is used in various technological applications, such as semiconductors and LEDs. It does not have botanical significance as it is not a plant species.

Therefore, there are no botanical aspects to discuss regarding gallium as a plant.
