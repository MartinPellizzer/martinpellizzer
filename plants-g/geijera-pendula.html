Geijera pendula

Geijera pendula, commonly known as the weeping geijera, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its drooping branches that give it a distinctive, cascading appearance. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are usually dark green in color. It produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, woody fruits.

Geijera pendula is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
