Gleichenia flexuosa f. monstrosa

Gleichenia flexuosa f. monstrosa, commonly known as the monstrous Gleichenia, is a fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated fronds that often exhibit a twisted or contorted growth pattern, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices, where it can thrive in high humidity. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a prominent midrib running through the center.

This fern is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a decorative element in greenhouses.
