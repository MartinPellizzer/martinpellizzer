Grevillea aurea

Grevillea aurea, commonly known as the golden grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically bright green and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters, which are particularly showy during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in coastal regions.

Grevillea aurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators like birds and insects.
