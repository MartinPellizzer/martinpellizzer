Gymnosporia buchananii var. longistyla

Gymnosporia buchananii var. longistyla, commonly known as the longstyle gymnosporia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spine-tipped fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The species is notable for its adaptability to harsh climatic conditions.
