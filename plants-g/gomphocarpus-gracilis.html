Gomphocarpus gracilis

Gomphocarpus gracilis, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods.
