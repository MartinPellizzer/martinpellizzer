Gymnocalycium ochoterenae

Gymnocalycium ochoterenae, commonly known as the Ochoterenae moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and its ability to produce striking, colorful flowers. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late summer or early autumn.

Gymnocalycium ochoterenae is a popular choice for collectors due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
