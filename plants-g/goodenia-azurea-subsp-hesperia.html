Goodenia azurea subsp. hesperia

Goodenia azurea subsp.

hesperia, commonly known as the Esperance Goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and heathlands. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, cylindrical seed pods.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
