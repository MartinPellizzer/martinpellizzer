Gasteranthus Timidus

Gasteranthus timidus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. Known for its striking violet-blue petals and unique, urn-shaped structure, it captivates botanists and plant lovers alike. This species is exceptionally sensitive to environmental changes, making it a valuable indicator of ecological health. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and difficult to cultivate outside its natural habitat. Its rarity and aesthetic appeal have made it a subject of both scientific study and conservation efforts.
