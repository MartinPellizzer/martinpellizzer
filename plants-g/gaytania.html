Gaytania

Gaytania, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, pyramidal growth with small, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and silver-white underneath. The plant produces clusters of bright red, star-shaped flowers during the winter months, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Gaytania is relatively low maintenance, requiring well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
