Greenwayodendron oliveri

Greenwayodendron oliveri, commonly known as Oliver's greenwayodendron, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. These flowers are followed by small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
