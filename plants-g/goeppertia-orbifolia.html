Goeppertia orbifolia

Goeppertia orbifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of deep green, silver, and red. The leaves are broad, orbicular, and often have a waxy texture that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Goeppertia orbifolia is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and air-purifying qualities.
