Galium rubioides var. pseudorubioides

Galium rubioides var. pseudorubioides, commonly known as the redstem bedstraw, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes over 12,000 species of flowering plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its stems are reddish in color, and it produces small white or pale yellow flowers.
