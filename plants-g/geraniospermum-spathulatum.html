Geraniospermum spathulatum

Geraniospermum spathulatum, commonly known as the broadleaf geranium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Geraniaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, facilitating its dispersal.

Geraniospermum spathulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
