Grewia ancolana

Grewia ancolana, commonly known as the red gum tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Grewia ancolana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
