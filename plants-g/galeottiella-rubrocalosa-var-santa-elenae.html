Galeottiella rubrocalosa var. santa-elenae

Galeottiella rubrocalosa var. santa-elenae, commonly known as the Santa Elenae Firebush, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems, creating a visually arresting display. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with a reddish hue, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
