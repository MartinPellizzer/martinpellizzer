Gladiolus magnificus

Gladiolus magnificus, commonly known as the magnificent gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces large, showy flowers in a variety of vibrant colors. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. Its flowers are typically borne in dense spikes and bloom during the summer months.

The botanical name reflects its grand appearance, making it a popular choice for both horticultural and commercial purposes.
