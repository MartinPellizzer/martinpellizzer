Gymnocalycium reductum subsp. leeanum

Gymnocalycium reductum subsp.

leeanum, commonly known as the "Lesser Hedgehog Cactus," is a low-growing, spineless cactus native to the arid regions of South America. It features a cylindrical stem that is typically green to bluish-green in color, with areoles bearing clusters of white flowers.

The plant produces small, spherical fruits that are yellow to orange when mature. It is well adapted to dry environments, with reduced leaves and a thick, fleshy stem for water storage.

This subspecies is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
