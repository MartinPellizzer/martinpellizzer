Garcinia ceramica

Garcinia ceramica, commonly known as the Ceramica Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Garcinia ceramica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its potential medicinal properties.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
