Gentiana futtereri

Gentiana futtereri, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils. It produces showy, five-petaled flowers that bloom in late summer.

Gentiana futtereri is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in high-altitude ecosystems.
