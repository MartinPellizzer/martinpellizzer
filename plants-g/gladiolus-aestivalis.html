Gladiolus aestivalis

Gladiolus aestivalis, commonly known as the summer gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear striking, sword-shaped leaves and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn.

This plant is often cultivated for its beauty and is used in gardens and floral arrangements.
