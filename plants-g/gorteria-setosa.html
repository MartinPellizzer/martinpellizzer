Gorteria setosa

Gorteria setosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gorteria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a daisy-like pattern. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats.
