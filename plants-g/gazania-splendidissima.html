Gazania splendidissima

Gazania splendidissima, commonly known as the splendid gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features a low-growing, succulent habit with thick, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Gazania splendidissima is also known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
