Gardenia augusta var. ovalifolia

Gardenia augusta var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical to oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a slight waxy coating. The plant produces fragrant, white, waxy flowers that are typically clustered in small inflorescences. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
