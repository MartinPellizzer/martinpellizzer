Geranium exallum

Geranium exallum, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are usually palmately lobed, with a rough texture and a distinctive scent.

Geranium exallum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
