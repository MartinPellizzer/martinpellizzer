Gentiana oreocharis

Gentiana oreocharis, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a striking alpine plant native to the high mountain regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its deep blue, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at elevations above 1,500 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Gentiana oreocharis is a perennial herb that thrives in cool, moist environments with ample sunlight.
