Genista flaccida

Genista flaccida, commonly known as the silky broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, leathery pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.
