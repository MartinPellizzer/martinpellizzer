Gardenia megasperma

Gardenia megasperma, commonly known as the Madagascar gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces elliptical, yellowish fruits that contain multiple seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia megasperma is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
