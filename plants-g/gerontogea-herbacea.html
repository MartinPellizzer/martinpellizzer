Gerontogea herbacea

Gerontogea herbacea, commonly known as the brittle herb, is a perennial flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its slender, often decumbent stems that lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or white in color. Its leaves are usually narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

Gerontogea herbacea is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
