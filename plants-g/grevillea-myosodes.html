Grevillea myosodes

Grevillea myosodes, commonly known as the pink and white grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves and produces striking clusters of pink and white flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance. It is often found in heathlands and rocky outcrops, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
