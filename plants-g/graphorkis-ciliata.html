Graphorkis ciliata

Graphorkis ciliata, commonly known as the slender leaved orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing in forest understoreys.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
