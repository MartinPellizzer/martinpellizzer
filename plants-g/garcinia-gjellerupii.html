Garcinia gjellerupii

Garcinia gjellerupii, commonly known as the African mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of West Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces edible, round fruits with a thick, pinkish rind and a segmented, juicy pulp. Garcinia gjellerupii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in local medicine.

Its cultivation is limited due to its specific climatic requirements and susceptibility to pests.
