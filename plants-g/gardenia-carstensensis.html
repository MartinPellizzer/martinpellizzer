Gardenia carstensensis

Gardenia carstensensis, commonly known as the Carstens gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain hard, black seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Gardenia carstensensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
