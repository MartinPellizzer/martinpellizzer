Goodenia watsonii

Goodenia watsonii, commonly known as the Watson's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia watsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
