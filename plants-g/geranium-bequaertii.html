Geranium bequaertii

Geranium bequaertii, commonly known as the Bequaert's geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for gardeners in Mediterranean climates.
