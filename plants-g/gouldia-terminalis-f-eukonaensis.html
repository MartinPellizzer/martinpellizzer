Gouldia terminalis f. eukonaensis

Gouldia terminalis f. eukonaensis, commonly known as the Eukonaensis Gouldia, is a unique variant of the Gouldia species native to specific regions of Australia.

This plant is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. It typically grows in coastal or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. The foliage is narrow and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant plant.
