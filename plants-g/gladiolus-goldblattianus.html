Gladiolus goldblattianus

Gladiolus goldblattianus, commonly known as the Goldblatt gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, showy inflorescences. The flowers are typically bright yellow or orange, with a distinctive, spurred shape that sets it apart from other gladiolus species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
