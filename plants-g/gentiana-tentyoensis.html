Gentiana tentyoensis

Gentiana tentyoensis, commonly known as the Korean gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Gentiana tentyoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
