Gardenia inermis

Gardenia inermis, commonly known as the white gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant is evergreen and typically grows as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia inermis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
