Gardenia florida f. thunbergii

Gardenia florida f. thunbergii, commonly known as the Thai gardenia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a subtle fragrance. The plant produces large, waxy, white flowers with a strong, sweet scent, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
