Gentiana plebeia var. holmii

Gentiana plebeia var. holmii, commonly known as the Holm's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and alpine meadows. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
