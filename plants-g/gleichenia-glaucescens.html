Gleichenia glaucescens

Gleichenia glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping rhizomes and its lanceolate, glossy, bluish-green fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dimorphic, with sterile and fertile fronds differing in structure and appearance.

Gleichenia glaucescens is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
