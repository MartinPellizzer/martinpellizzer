Gloxinia lindeniana

Gloxinia lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenberg gloxinia, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Spathiphyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and slightly velvety in texture. Gloxinia lindeniana thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its beauty and ease of care in suitable conditions.
