Gardenia macrantha

Gardenia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces yellow-orange berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun.

Gardenia macrantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and aromatic blooms.
