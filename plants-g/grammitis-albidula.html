Grammitis albidula

Grammitis albidula, commonly known as the white-stemmed hound's-tongue, is a fern native to the temperate regions of the southern hemisphere.

Grammitis albidula is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded gardens.
