Gasteria antandroi

Gasteria antandroi, commonly known as the African violet plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often marked with white or silver veins. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Gasteria antandroi is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
