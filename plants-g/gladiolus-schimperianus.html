Gladiolus schimperianus

Gladiolus schimperianus, commonly known as the Schimper's gladiolus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, sword-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically blooms in summer and is often found in grassland habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, round berries that contain the seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
