Gentiana iseana

Gentiana iseana, commonly known as the Italian gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Gentiana iseana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
