Genista incerta

Genista incerta, commonly known as the uncertain broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Genista incerta is often used in horticulture for its hardiness and ornamental value.
