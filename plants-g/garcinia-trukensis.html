Garcinia trukensis

Garcinia trukensis, commonly known as the Truk Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to the Caroline Islands.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are typically harvested when fully mature. Garcinia trukensis is often found in lowland rainforests and is adapted to humid, warm climates.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and potential ecological role in its native habitat.
