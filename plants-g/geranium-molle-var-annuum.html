Geranium molle var. annuum

Geranium molle var. annuum, commonly known as the annual geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

It is often used as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
