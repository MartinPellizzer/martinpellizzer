Gutierrezia wrightii

Gutierrezia wrightii, commonly known as Wright's goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and linear, gray-green leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats. Gutierrezia wrightii is adapted to hot, dry climates and is drought-tolerant, making it a resilient component of its ecosystem.
