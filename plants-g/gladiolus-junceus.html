Gladiolus junceus

Gladiolus junceus, commonly known as the narrowleaf gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in a range of colors including white, pink, and red. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and tapering. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Iridaceae family.
