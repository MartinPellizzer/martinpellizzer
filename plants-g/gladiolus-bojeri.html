Gladiolus bojeri

Gladiolus bojeri, commonly known as the Bojer's gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves and striking, showy flowers. The flowers typically come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
