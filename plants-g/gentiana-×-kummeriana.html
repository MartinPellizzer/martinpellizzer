Gentiana × kummeriana

Gentiana × kummeriana, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a hybrid plant species within the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which typically bloom in late summer. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are upright and branched.

Gentiana × kummeriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
