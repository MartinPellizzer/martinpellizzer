Gazania Rigida

Gazania rigida is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant, daisy-like blooms that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. This hardy species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries. Its ability to withstand drought and heat has made it a favorite among gardeners in arid climates. With its bold colors and easy care requirements, Gazania rigida adds a splash of cheer to any outdoor space.
