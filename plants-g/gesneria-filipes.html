Gesneria filipes

Gesneria filipes, commonly known as the silver stripe gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, silver-veined leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with tubular corollas that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Gesneria filipes thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
