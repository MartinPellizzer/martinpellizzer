Geniostoma crassifolium var. glaberrimum

Geniostoma crassifolium var. glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved geniostoma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are usually smooth and waxy in texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated as an ornamental plant.
