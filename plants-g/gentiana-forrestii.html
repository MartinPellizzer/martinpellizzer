Gentiana forrestii

Gentiana forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched.

Gentiana forrestii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
