Gentiana jaeschkei

Gentiana jaeschkei, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high elevations of the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as meadows and forested areas. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its stem is erect with few branches.

Gentiana jaeschkei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural environment.
