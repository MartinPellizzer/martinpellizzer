Geraniospermum × lateripes

Geraniospermum × lateripes, commonly known as the hybrid geranium, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Geraniospermum macrophyllum and Geraniospermum humile. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
