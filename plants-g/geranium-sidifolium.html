Geranium sidifolium

Geranium sidifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
