Genista mayeri

Genista mayeri, commonly known as Mayer's broom, is a flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, often branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, blooming in early spring.

Genista mayeri is native to southern Europe and is adapted to rocky and dry environments.
