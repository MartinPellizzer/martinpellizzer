Gardenia psidioides

Gardenia psidioides, commonly known as the Hawaiian gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 10 meters. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, with a leathery texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Hawaiian culture.
