Geonoma elegans var. amazonica

Geonoma elegans var. amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian arrow leaf, is a strikingly beautiful fern native to the rainforests of South America.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often adorned with intricate, silky hairs, giving it a soft, almost ethereal appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for tropical greenhouse cultivation. Its unique morphology and elegant foliage make it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts.

It is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic of indoor spaces.
