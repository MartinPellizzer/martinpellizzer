Giulianettia fransseniana

Giulianettia fransseniana, commonly known as the Franssen's giant gentian, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white pubescence.

Giulianettia fransseniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
