Gnidia fastigiata var. hirsuta

Gnidia fastigiata var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy gnidia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its varietal name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
