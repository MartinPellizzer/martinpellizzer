Geranium pusillum var. gracillimum

Geranium pusillum var. gracillimum, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features small, delicate flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has wiry, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its extended flowering period and low maintenance requirements.
