Gypsophila Acutifolia Lusus Latifolia

Baby's breath, scientifically known as Gypsophila acutifolia lusus latifolia, is a delicate and popular flowering plant often used in floral arrangements. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its tiny, fluffy white blooms create a soft, ethereal effect that adds a touch of romance to any setting. Despite its fragile appearance, this plant is surprisingly hardy and adaptable to various growing conditions. With its unique beauty and versatility, Baby's breath remains a favorite among gardeners and florists alike.
