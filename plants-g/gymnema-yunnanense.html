Gymnema yunnanense

Gymnema yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan gymnema, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, opposite leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions.

Due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
