Guettarda seleriana

Guettarda seleriana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has simple, opposite, and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Guettarda seleriana is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its use in traditional medicine.
