Genista paivae

Genista paivae, commonly known as the Paiva's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.
