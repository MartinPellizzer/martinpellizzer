Glochidion macrochorion

Glochidion macrochorion, commonly known as the large-fruited glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
