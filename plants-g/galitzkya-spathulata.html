Galitzkya spathulata

Galitzkya spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed galitzkya, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or pink, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Galitzkya spathulata is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
