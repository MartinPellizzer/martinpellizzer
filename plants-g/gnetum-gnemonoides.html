Gnetum gnemonoides

Gnetum gnemonoides, commonly known as the Asian gnetum, is a member of the Gnetophyta division, which includes gymnosperms.

It is a tropical plant characterized by its evergreen foliage and distinctive, often palmate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is typically found in moist, shaded environments in Southeast Asia. Its seeds are enclosed in a fleshy, red aril, which is an unusual feature among gymnosperms.

Gnetum gnemonoides plays a role in local ecosystems and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
