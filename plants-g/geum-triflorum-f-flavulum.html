Geum triflorum f. flavulum

Geum triflorum f. flavulum, commonly known as the yellow woodsorrel, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish or reddish hue, and its flowers are typically trifoliate, with three petals that are yellow or orange in color. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
