Gymnolomia squarrosa

Gymnolomia squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrosa daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typically blooming in spring. The plant grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal areas.

Gymnolomia squarrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
