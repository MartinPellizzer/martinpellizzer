Guarianthe guatemalensis

Guarianthe guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan orchid or the orchid of the Maya, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.
