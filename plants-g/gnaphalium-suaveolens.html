Gnaphalium suaveolens

Gnaphalium suaveolens, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, fluffy clusters of small yellow flowers. The plant produces a fragrant, essential oil that contributes to its pleasant aroma. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its common name reflects both its enduring blooms and its sweet, lingering fragrance.
