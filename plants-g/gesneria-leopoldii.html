Gesneria leopoldii

Gesneria leopoldii, commonly known as the Leopold's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a deep green color with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria leopoldii is popular among indoor gardeners due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
