Gardenia volkensii var. somalensis

Gardenia volkensii var. somalensis, commonly known as the Somali gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, white flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are glossy and evergreen. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
