Gardenia kalbreyeri

Gardenia kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and large, waxy, white flowers with a strong, fragrant scent. The plant produces elliptical, yellowish fruits that contain black, shiny seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to full sun.

Gardenia kalbreyeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and unique floral characteristics.
