Glandulicereus chacalapensis

Glandulicereus chacalapensis, commonly known as the Chacalapense cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh desert conditions.

Its structure and growth habits make it well-suited for xerophytic environments.
