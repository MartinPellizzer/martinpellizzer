Geranium arachnoideum

Geranium arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive, almost fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
