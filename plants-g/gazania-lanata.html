Gazania lanata

Gazania lanata, commonly known as the woolly gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has hairy, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Gazania lanata is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
