Gleichenia truncata var. plumaeformis

Gleichenia truncata var. plumaeformis, commonly known as the plumelike Gleichenia, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a leathery texture, with a complex, branching structure that enhances its ornamental value.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in shaded garden settings.
