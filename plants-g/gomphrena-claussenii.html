Gomphrena claussenii

Gomphrena claussenii, commonly known as the red flame gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky, pink to red flower heads that resemble tiny flames. The plant produces small, fluffy bracts that surround the true flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Gomphrena claussenii is known for its long-lasting blooms, which can persist throughout the growing season.
