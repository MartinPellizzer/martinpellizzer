Gnetum gnemon var. lucidum

Gnetum gnemon var. lucidum, commonly known as the shining gnetum, is a member of the Gnetophyta division, characterized by its unique combination of gymnosperm and angiosperm traits.

It is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Indonesia and Malaysia, where it thrives in humid, lowland forests. The plant has smooth, glossy leaves that are typically alternate and ovate in shape, with a deep green coloration. Its cones are small and inconspicuous, with both male and female cones occurring on the same plant.

The species is notable for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity and its potential use in traditional medicine.
