Geboscon striatum

Geboscon striatum, commonly known as the striped fern, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, and deeply divided fronds that are often adorned with a striking green and silver coloration. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Geboscon striatum is also valued for its ability to purify the air and add a lush, tropical aesthetic to indoor spaces.
