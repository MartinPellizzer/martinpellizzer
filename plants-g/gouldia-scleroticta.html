Gouldia scleroticta

Gouldia scleroticta, commonly known as the red kangaroo paw, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It is a perennial herb with distinctive, fleshy, red or orange-red flowers that resemble the paw of a kangaroo. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall flowering stem that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Gouldia scleroticta is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient plant in harsh environments.
