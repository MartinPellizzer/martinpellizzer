Gasteria radulosa

Gasteria radulosa, commonly known as the African Houseleek, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale markings. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences that bear small, white or yellowish flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria radulosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
