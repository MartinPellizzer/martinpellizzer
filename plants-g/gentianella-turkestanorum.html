Gentianella turkestanorum

Gentianella turkestanorum, commonly known as the Turkestan gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in moist, grassy habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly serrated edge.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
