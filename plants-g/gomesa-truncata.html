Gomesa truncata

Gomesa truncata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a distinctive purple underside. Its showy, tubular flowers come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white, and bloom throughout the year under proper care.

Gomesa truncata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
