Goodyera japonica

Goodyera japonica, commonly known as the Japanese basket plant, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a spike, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
