Gnaphalium stachidifolium var. sphacelatum

Gnaphalium stachidifolium var. sphacelatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands or rocky areas.

Its common name reflects its long-lasting flowers, which remain intact for extended periods when dried.
