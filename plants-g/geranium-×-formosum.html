Geranium × formosum

Geranium × formosum, commonly known as the Chinese geranium, is a hybrid species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically produces opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Geranium × formosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
