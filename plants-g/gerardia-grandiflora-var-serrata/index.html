Gerardia Grandiflora Var. Serrata

Serrated gerardia, scientifically known as Gerardia grandiflora var. serrata, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. With its vibrant green leaves and clusters of showy white or pink flowers, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil with partial to full sun exposure. Its unique serrated leaf edges make it easily identifiable and add visual interest to any landscape. Due to its beauty and adaptability, serrated gerardia is both a sought-after garden specimen and a subject of botanical study.
