Gesneria cooperiana

Gesneria cooperiana, commonly known as the Cooper's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the humid lowland forests of western Ecuador, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant features attractive, broad, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. Its flowers are typically tubular with vibrant colors, ranging from orange to red, and are produced in clusters.

Gesneria cooperiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
