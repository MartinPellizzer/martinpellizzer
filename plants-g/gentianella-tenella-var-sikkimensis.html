Gentianella tenella var. sikkimensis

Gentianella tenella var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which are typically tubular and arranged in dense clusters. The plant is native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas, particularly in Sikkim and neighboring areas. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
