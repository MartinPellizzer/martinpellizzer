Globularia punctata

Globularia punctata, commonly known as the spotted globe flower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a compact, rounded shape with silver-gray foliage that is covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to Mediterranean-style landscapes.
