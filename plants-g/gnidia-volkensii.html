Gnidia volkensii

Gnidia volkensii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a strong, aromatic scent and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It is valued for its hard, heavy wood, which is used in construction and tool-making.

Gnidia volkensii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
