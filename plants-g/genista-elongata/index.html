Genista Elongata

Genista elongata is a hardy shrub known for its delicate yellow flowers and dense, thorny growth. It thrives in rocky and coastal areas, often found along the Mediterranean coastlines. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments. Its small, leathery leaves and long, slender stems make it easily identifiable. Despite its tough appearance, Genista elongata plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and ecosystems.
