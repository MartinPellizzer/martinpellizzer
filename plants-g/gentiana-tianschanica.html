Gentiana tianschanica

Gentiana tianschanica, commonly known as the Tian Shan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually basal, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine pubescence.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
