Geonoma pycnostachys

Geonoma pycnostachys, commonly known as the dense-stemmed fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped, glossy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical climates.
