Gentianella austriaca f. albiflora

Gentianella austriaca f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered gentian, is a rare and striking plant native to the alpine regions of Central Europe.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and rocky slopes. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
