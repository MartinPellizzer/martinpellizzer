Gnaphalium einseleanum

Gnaphalium einseleanum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its fluffy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are silvery-gray, covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
