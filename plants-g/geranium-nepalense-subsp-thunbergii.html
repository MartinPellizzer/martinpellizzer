Geranium nepalense subsp. thunbergii

Geranium nepalense subsp.

thunbergii, commonly known as the Himalayan geranium, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas. It typically grows in rocky and well-drained soils at high altitudes.

The plant features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
