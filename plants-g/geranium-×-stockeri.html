Geranium × stockeri

Geranium × stockeri, commonly known as the ivy-leaved geranium, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves that resemble ivy leaves, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It is native to South Africa and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and containers.

Geranium × stockeri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for ornamental gardening.
