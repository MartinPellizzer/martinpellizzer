Gymnocalycium megatae

Gymnocalycium megatae, commonly known as the giant candleplant, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its fleshy stems.

Gymnocalycium megatae is a popular choice for collectors due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
