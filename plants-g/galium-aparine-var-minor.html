Galium aparine var. minor

Galium aparine var. minor, commonly known as cleavers, is a low-growing herbaceous plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its prostrate or ascending stems that are covered in fine, hooked hairs, which aid in its ability to cling to surfaces. Galium aparine var. minor is often found in moist, disturbed soils and is commonly used in traditional herbal medicine.
