Goodenia discolor

Goodenia discolor, commonly known as the pale goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, with five petals that are often hairy.

Goodenia discolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
