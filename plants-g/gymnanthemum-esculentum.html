Gymnanthemum esculentum

Gymnanthemum esculentum, commonly known as the edible starflower, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant has erect, branching stems and pinnately compound leaves. Its fruits are small, dry pods that contain several seeds.

Gymnanthemum esculentum is valued for its edible leaves and seeds, which are used in traditional cuisines.
