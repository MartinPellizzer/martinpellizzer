Ganymedes nutans

Ganymedes nutans, commonly known as the nodding milkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that droop downward. The plant produces a milky sap, which is typical of the milkweed genus. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Ganymedes nutans thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
