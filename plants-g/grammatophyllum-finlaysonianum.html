Grammatophyllum finlaysonianum

Grammatophyllum finlaysonianum, commonly known as the giant spider orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that can reach up to 3 meters in height, adorned with numerous fragrant, spider-like flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of light for optimal growth. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding forest floor, and it often grows as an epiphyte on trees.

This species is notable for its unique floral structure and is a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.
