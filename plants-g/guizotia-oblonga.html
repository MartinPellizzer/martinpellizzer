Guizotia oblonga

Guizotia oblonga, commonly known as the narrowleaf cowpea, is an annual legume native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, oblong leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing small, dark brown seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often grown for its edible seeds, which are rich in protein.

Guizotia oblonga is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a beneficial cover crop.
