Gaya calyptrata var. hermannioides

Gaya calyptrata var. hermannioides, commonly known as the Hermann's gaya, is a rare variant of the gaya calyptrata species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique leaf morphology, featuring elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a distinct midrib and serrated margins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, clustered in axillary inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in lowland rainforests or secondary growth areas.
