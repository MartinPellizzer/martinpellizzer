Gynopleura angustifolia

Gynopleura angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gynopleura, is a herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features slender, elongated leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its fruit is a dry capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Gynopleura angustifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
