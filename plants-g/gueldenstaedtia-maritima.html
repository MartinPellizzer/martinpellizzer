Gueldenstaedtia maritima

Gueldenstaedtia maritima, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Native to coastal regions of Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils.

It is often found in maritime environments, where it tolerates salty air and strong winds.
