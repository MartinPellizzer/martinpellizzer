Gleichenia intermedia var. dissitifolia

Gleichenia intermedia var. dissitifolia, commonly known as the hairy maidenhair fern, is a variant of the Gleichenia intermedia species.

It is a fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae, characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a distinctive, finely divided structure.

This variety is noted for its hairy texture on the underside of its fronds, which distinguishes it from other Gleichenia species.
