Goodenia ovata var. cordata

Goodenia ovata var. cordata, commonly known as the broad-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
