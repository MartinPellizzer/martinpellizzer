Gynostemma pedatum var. trifoliatum

Gynostemma pedatum var. trifoliatum, commonly known as the trileaf gynostemma, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
