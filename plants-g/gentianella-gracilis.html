Gentianella gracilis

Gentianella gracilis, commonly known as slender gentian, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems and small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slight hairy texture.

Gentianella gracilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
