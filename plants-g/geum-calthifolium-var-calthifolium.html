Geum calthifolium var. calthifolium

Geum calthifolium var. calthifolium, commonly known as white avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features basal leaves that are palmately lobed and covered in fine white hairs, giving the plant a soft, silvery appearance. The flowers are small, white to pale pink, and are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
