Gentiana sino-ornata f. alba

Gentiana sino-ornata f. alba, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to sky-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight hairy texture.

The flowers bloom in late summer and are known for their medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
