Gesneria clarensis

Gesneria clarensis, commonly known as the Clarens gesneria, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and attractive, glossy leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Gesneria clarensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
