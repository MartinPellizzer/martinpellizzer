Graptopetalum glassii

Graptopetalum glassii, commonly known as the Glass Stonecrop, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to many succulent collections.
