Graderia scabra

Graderia scabra, commonly known as the rough graderia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Graderia scabra is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens and shaded outdoor spaces.
