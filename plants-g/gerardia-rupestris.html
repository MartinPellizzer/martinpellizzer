Gerardia rupestris

Gerardia rupestris, commonly known as the rock gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky and coastal environments. The plant features small, dark green leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry and sunny conditions, making it a hardy species.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
