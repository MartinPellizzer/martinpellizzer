Geranium strigosius

Geranium strigosius, commonly known as the woolly geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Geranium strigosius thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
