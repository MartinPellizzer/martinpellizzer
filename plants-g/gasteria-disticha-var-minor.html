Gasteria disticha var. minor

Gasteria disticha var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf gasteria, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a slight blue tint, often marked by white or pale veins. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to dry environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
