Gladiolus alatus var. meliusculus

Gladiolus alatus var. meliusculus, commonly known as the silver leaf gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear showy, sword-shaped flowers in a variety of vibrant colors. The leaves are narrow and slightly curved, giving the plant its distinctive "gladiolus" appearance. This variety is particularly noted for its silvery-white variegation on the foliage, which adds visual interest.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
