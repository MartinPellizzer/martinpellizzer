Graptopetalum weinbergii

Graptopetalum weinbergii, commonly known as the Weinberg's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a compact and drought-tolerant structure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring or early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and delicate blooms.
