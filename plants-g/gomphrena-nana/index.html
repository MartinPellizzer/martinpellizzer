Gomphrena Nana

Gomphrena nana is a compact, flowering plant known for its vibrant, spiky blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The plant's small stature and colorful flowers make it an excellent addition to containers and borders. Native to southern Africa, it has gained popularity in horticulture worldwide. Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favorite among both novice and experienced gardeners.
