Geodorum densiflorum

Geodorum densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces edible tubers underground, similar to those of other arrowroot species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Geodorum densiflorum is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its edible roots.
