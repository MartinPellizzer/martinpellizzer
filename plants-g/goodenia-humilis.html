Goodenia humilis

Goodenia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf goodenia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are usually hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact form and seasonal blooms.
