Gouldia hillebrandii f. lancifolia

Gouldia hillebrandii f. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gouldia, is a striking ornamental grass native to Australia.

It features slender, grass-like foliage with distinctive, often brightly colored inflorescences that resemble plumes. The plant typically grows in dense clumps, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in garden landscapes and its ability to add texture and color to the garden.
