Graphorkis flabellata

Graphorkis flabellata, commonly known as the flabellate orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a pale yellow or greenish hue with a distinctive flabellate (fan-like) structure.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
