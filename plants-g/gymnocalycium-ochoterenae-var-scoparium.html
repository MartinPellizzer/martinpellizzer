Gymnocalycium ochoterenae var. scoparium

Gymnocalycium ochoterenae var. scoparium, commonly known as the Ochoterenae Cactus or the Desert Candelabra, is a member of the Cactaceae family.

It is a low-growing, compact cactus that typically forms a spherical or slightly elongated shape. The plant is characterized by its pale green to bluish-gray stems, which are covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring to early summer.

This variety is native to arid regions of Central Asia and is well adapted to harsh, dry environments.
