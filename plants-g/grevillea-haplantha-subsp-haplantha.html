Grevillea haplantha subsp. haplantha

Grevillea haplantha subsp.

haplantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers in summer.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, with a deep root system for water retention. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils along coastal and inland areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
