Gastrochilus sinensis

Gastrochilus sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese stomach orchid, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, grass-like foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Gastrochilus sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
