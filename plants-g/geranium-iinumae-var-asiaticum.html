Geranium iinumae var. asiaticum

Geranium iinumae var. asiaticum, commonly known as the Asiatic geranium, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, preferring temperate climates.
