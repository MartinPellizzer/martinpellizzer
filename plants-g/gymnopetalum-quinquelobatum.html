Gymnopetalum quinquelobatum

Gymnopetalum quinquelobatum, commonly known as the five-lobed bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with appropriate growing conditions.
