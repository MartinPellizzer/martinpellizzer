Grevillea diffusa subsp. diffusa

Grevillea diffusa subsp.

diffusa, commonly known as the coastal grevillea, is a shrub native to coastal regions of southern Australia. It features bright red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for coastal gardens.

Its flowering period typically occurs during the warmer months, attracting various pollinators such as birds and insects.
