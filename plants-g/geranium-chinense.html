Geranium chinense

Geranium chinense, commonly known as the Chinese geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium chinense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
