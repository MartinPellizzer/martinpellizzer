Gongora maculata var. lactea

Gongora maculata var. lactea, commonly known as the White Spotted Gongora, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features striking, brightly colored flowers with a white or pale pink coloration and distinctive spots. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically dark green and broad, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
