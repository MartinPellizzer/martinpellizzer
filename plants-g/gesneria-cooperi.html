Gesneria cooperi

Gesneria cooperi, commonly known as the Cooper's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which is known for its ornamental and often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features broad, glossy leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Gesneria cooperi thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate humidity.
