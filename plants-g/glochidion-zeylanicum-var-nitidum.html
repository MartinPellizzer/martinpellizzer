Glochidion zeylanicum var. nitidum

Glochidion zeylanicum var. nitidum, commonly known as the glossy glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy, dark green leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests and secondary growth areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
