Genista broteri

Genista broteri, commonly known as the Cape Verde broom, is a shrub native to the islands of Cape Verde.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features slender, thorned stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista broteri is adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
