Genista lydia subsp. antiochia

Genista lydia subsp.

antiochia, commonly known as the Antioch broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, wiry stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several leaflets, and it is adapted to rocky and dry environments. It is often found in open woodlands and along coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
