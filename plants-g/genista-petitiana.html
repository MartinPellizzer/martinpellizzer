Genista petitiana

Genista petitiana, commonly known as the Petitian Broom, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are arranged in racemes. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or scrubland habitats.
