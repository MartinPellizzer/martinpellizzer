Gleichenia pubescens var. glabra

Gleichenia pubescens var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth Gleichenia, is a fern species native to certain regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, wiry fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a smooth, glabrous surface, which distinguishes it from its varietal counterpart.

It reproduces via spores and plays a role in maintaining soil stability in its natural habitat.
