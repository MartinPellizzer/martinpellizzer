Gladiolus phoenix

Gladiolus phoenix, commonly known as the Phoenix gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular watering during the growing season.

The Phoenix gladiolus is also noted for its resilience and ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the summer.
