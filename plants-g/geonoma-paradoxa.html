Geonoma paradoxa

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, intricate fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive, almost lace-like appearance.
