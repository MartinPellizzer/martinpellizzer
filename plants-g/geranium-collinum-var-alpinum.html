Geranium collinum var. alpinum

Geranium collinum var. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in full sun. The plant features upright stems with opposite, palmately lobed leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are showy, ranging from pink to purple, and are borne in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and alpine plant collections.
