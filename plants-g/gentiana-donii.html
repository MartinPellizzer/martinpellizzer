Gentiana donii

Gentiana donii, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Gentianaceae family, known for its showy, often blue or purple flowers. Gentiana donii typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silky hair, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
