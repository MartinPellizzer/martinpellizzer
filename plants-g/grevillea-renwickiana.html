Grevillea renwickiana

Grevillea renwickiana, commonly known as the Renwick's waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, crimson-red flower spikes that bloom in spring and summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea renwickiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and resilience in dry, coastal conditions.
