Guioa sarcopterifructa

Guioa sarcopterifructa, commonly known as the scarlet guioa, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its fruit is a distinctive, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
