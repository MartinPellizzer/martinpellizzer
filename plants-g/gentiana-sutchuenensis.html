Gentiana sutchuenensis

Gentiana sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture, while its stems are often branched and covered in fine hairs.

The Sichuan gentian is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
