Gnetum diminutum

Gnetum diminutum, commonly known as the dwarf gnetum, is a member of the Gnetophyta division, which includes gymnosperms.

It is a small, shrub-like plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is known for its unique reproductive structures.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studies on evolutionary adaptations in gymnosperms.
