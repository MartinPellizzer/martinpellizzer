Genista tricuspidata var. virescens

Genista tricuspidata var. virescens, commonly known as the greenleaf broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, wiry stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow, trifoliate, and have a distinctive greenish hue, giving it its common name.

It is well adapted to rocky and coastal environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils.
