Gazania armerioides var. sutherlandii

Gazania armerioides var. sutherlandii, commonly known as the Sutherland gazania, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom throughout the warmer months. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, helping it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
