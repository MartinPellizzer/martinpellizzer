Geniostoma febrifugum

Geniostoma febrifugum, commonly known as the feverfew plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its use in treating fever and headaches. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both traditional and modern herbal medicine.
