Gardoquia foliolosa

Gardoquia foliolosa, commonly known as the leafy gardoquia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense mats. The leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

This plant is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
