Genista elias-sennenii

Genista elias-sennenii, commonly known as the Elias-Sennen's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features slender, thorny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
