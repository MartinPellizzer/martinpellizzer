Goodmania luteola

Goodmania luteola, commonly known as the yellow goodmania, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
