Gurania klotzschiana

Gurania klotzschiana, commonly known as the Klotzschian geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Gurania klotzschiana is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
