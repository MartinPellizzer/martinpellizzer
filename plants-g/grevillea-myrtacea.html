Grevillea myrtacea

Grevillea myrtacea, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in heathland and coastal areas, preferring well-drained soils. It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Grevillea myrtacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
