Godetia Bifrons

Godetia bifrons is a flowering plant known for its delicate, bright yellow blooms that add a splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. This annual plant is popular among gardeners for its ease of cultivation and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its simple yet charming appearance makes it a favorite in both wildflower gardens and cottage-style landscapes. With its resilient nature and vibrant flowers, Godetia bifrons remains a beloved choice for those seeking beauty and ecological value in their outdoor spaces.
