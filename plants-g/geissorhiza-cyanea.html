Geissorhiza cyanea

Geissorhiza cyanea, commonly known as the blue star lily, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a short, fibrous rootstock and produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is renowned for its vibrant blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
