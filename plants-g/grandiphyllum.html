Grandiphyllum

Grandiphyllum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical features of Grandiphyllum make it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces.
