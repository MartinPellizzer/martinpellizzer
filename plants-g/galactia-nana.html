Galactia nana

Galactia nana, commonly known as dwarf milkweed, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its stems and leaves exude a milky sap, which is a distinguishing botanical feature.

Galactia nana typically grows in moist, wooded areas and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.
