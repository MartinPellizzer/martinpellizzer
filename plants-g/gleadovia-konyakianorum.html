Gleadovia konyakianorum

Native to the high-altitude regions of the Eastern Himalayas, this plant thrives in cool, moist, and shaded environments. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are prominently displayed on the underside of its fronds, facilitating spore dispersal.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Gleadovia konyakianorum is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
