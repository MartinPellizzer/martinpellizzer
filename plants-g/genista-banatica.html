Genista banatica

Genista banatica, commonly known as the Balkan broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula, belonging to the Fabaceae family. The plant is characterized by its slender, shrub-like growth and produces yellowish-orange flowers in late spring. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista banatica is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
