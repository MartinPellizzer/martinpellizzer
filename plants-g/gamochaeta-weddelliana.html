Gamochaeta weddelliana

Gamochaeta weddelliana, commonly known as the Weddell's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

The flowers are small and clustered, producing a distinctive blue or purple coloration.
