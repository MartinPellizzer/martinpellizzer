Grewia boivinii

Grewia boivinii, commonly known as the African star fruit or yellow globemallow, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer, followed by bright red, spherical fruits. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant flowers and unique fruit.

Grewia boivinii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its drought tolerance.
