Garcinia lucens

Garcinia lucens, commonly known as the golden apple or yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellow-orange fruits with a sweet and tangy flavor. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its fruit is valued for its culinary use and is also used in traditional medicine.

Garcinia lucens is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in its native habitats.
