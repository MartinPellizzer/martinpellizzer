Gentianella crispata var. amblyphylla

Gentianella crispata var. amblyphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in woodland or meadow habitats. The plant features bright blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly curled edge, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ecological benefits to pollinators.
