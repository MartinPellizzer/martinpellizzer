Gibbaeum nuciforme

Gibbaeum nuciforme, commonly known as the nut-shaped rock rose, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with fleshy, ribbed stems and thick, waxy leaves that help store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with minimal water.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
