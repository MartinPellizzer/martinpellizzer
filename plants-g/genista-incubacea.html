Genista incubacea

Genista incubacea, commonly known as the incubating genista, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, sunny habitats, such as rocky soils and open woodlands. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by small, bean-like pods containing seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista incubacea is adapted to arid environments and is often found in Mediterranean climates.
