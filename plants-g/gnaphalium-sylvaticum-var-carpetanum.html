Gnaphalium sylvaticum var. carpetanum

Gnaphalium sylvaticum var. carpetanum, commonly known as the carpet everlasting, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flower heads, which are surrounded by silky, silver-green leaves. The plant produces a fine, aromatic foliage that is often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its flowers bloom from late spring to early autumn, adding a delicate beauty to its natural habitat.
