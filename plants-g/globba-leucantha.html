Globba leucantha

Globba leucantha, commonly known as the white globba, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.
