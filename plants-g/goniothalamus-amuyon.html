Goniothalamus amuyon

Goniothalamus amuyon, commonly known as the Philippine white wood, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by berries that turn from green to yellow when mature. Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened in its native range.
