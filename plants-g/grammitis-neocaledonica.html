Grammitis neocaledonica

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are often clustered in dense tufts. Grammitis neocaledonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
