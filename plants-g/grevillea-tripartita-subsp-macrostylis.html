Grevillea tripartita subsp. macrostylis

Grevillea tripartita subsp.

macrostylis, commonly known as the broad-styled grevillea, is a shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia. It features showy, bright red or orange flowers that are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in threes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

The subspecies is notable for its robust growth and ornamental value in gardens.
