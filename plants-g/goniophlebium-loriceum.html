Goniophlebium loriceum

Goniophlebium loriceum, commonly known as the broadleaf fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped fronds that can reach lengths of up to 1 meter. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that allow it to spread and colonize new areas. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a smooth, leathery texture.

Goniophlebium loriceum thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
