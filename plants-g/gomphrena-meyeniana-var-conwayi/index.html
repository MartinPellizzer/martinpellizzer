Gomphrena Meyeniana Var. Conwayi

Gomphrena meyeniana var. conwayi is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its vivid pink and red bracts that resemble tiny flowers. Native to the coastal regions of South Africa, this variety thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. The plant is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators. With its bold colors and easy care requirements, it's a standout addition to any landscape.
