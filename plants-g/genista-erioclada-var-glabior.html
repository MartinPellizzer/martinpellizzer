Genista erioclada var. glabior

Genista erioclada var. glabior, commonly known as the smooth broom.

This variety is a shrub characterized by its dense, spiny growth and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure. The plant is native to southern Europe and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

It is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in poor soils.
