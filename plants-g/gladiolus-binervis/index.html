Gladiolus Binervis

Gladiolus binervis is a striking member of the Iridaceae family known for its vibrant, sword-like flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in warm, sunny climates with well-drained soil. Its unique foliage features finely divided, grass-like leaves that add a dramatic visual element to gardens. The blooms come in a range of colors, from deep reds to soft pinks, making it a popular choice for floral arrangements. Despite its beauty, Gladiolus binervis requires careful cultivation to ensure its health and longevity.
