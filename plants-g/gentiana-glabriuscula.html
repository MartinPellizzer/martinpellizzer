Gentiana glabriuscula

Gentiana glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hair, giving it a slightly rough texture.

Gentiana glabriuscula is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
