Gleditsia sinensis

Gleditsia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese honey locust, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a trunk that often has a fissured, bark-like appearance. Its flowers are small and yellow-green, typically appearing in clusters, and it produces pods that contain edible seeds.

The plant is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
