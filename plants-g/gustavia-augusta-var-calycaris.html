Gustavia augusta var. calycaris

Gustavia augusta var. calycaris, commonly known as the Calycarist Gustavia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has smooth, glossy leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and emit a mild, pleasant aroma. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance in suitable climates.
