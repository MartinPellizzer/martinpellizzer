Gonostapelia maculosoides

Gonostapelia maculosoides, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, pale greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are often spotted or streaked with darker pigments, adding to its distinctive appearance.

The species is noted for its resilience and ability to survive in harsh conditions with minimal water.
