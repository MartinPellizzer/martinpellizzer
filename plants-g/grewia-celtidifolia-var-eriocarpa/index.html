Grewia Celtidifolia Var. Eriocarpa

Grewia celtidifolia var. eriocarpa is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant species native to parts of Africa and Asia. This variant of Grewia celtidifolia is distinguished by its dense, hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions. Despite its beauty and potential, it remains under-researched and underappreciated in modern horticulture.
