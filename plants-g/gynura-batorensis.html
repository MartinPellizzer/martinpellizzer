Gynura batorensis

Gynura batorensis, commonly known as the velvetleaf or batorensis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense, composite inflorescences.

Gynura batorensis is often found growing in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and along stream banks.
