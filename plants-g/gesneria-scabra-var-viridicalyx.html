Gesneria scabra var. viridicalyx

Gesneria scabra var. viridicalyx, commonly known as the velvet leaf gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy, and velvety leaves that are typically a deep green color, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and manageable size make it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners.
