Gorteria uniflora

Gorteria uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are borne singly on long, slender stems, and the leaves are narrow and grass-like.

Gorteria uniflora is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
