Geranium × bergianum

Geranium × bergianum, commonly known as the Siberian geranium, is a hybrid plant native to the cold climates of Siberia and northern regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, adapted to withstand harsh winter conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its ornamental value and hardiness make it a popular choice for gardeners in cooler climates.
