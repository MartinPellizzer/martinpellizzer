Gesneria lindmanii

Gesneria lindmanii, commonly known as the Lindman's gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with thick, leathery leaves that are often variegated in color. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, are pollinated by birds and insects.

Gesneria lindmanii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
