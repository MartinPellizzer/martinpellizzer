Gymnanthes gaudichaudii

Gymnanthes gaudichaudii, commonly known as the golden apple or yellow starfruit, is a member of the Meliaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen tree native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
