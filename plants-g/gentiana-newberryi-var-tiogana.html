Gentiana newberryi var. tiogana

Gentiana newberryi var. tiogana, commonly known as the Tiogana gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats such as stream banks and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
