Goeppertia brunnescens

Goeppertia brunnescens, commonly known as the brown-stemmed calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a distinctive brownish stem. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often featuring intricate patterns of green, silver, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
