Gaertneria cordifolia

Gaertneria cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a spathe, typical of many arum species. Its rhizomes are often harvested for their starchy roots, which are used in various culinary applications.

Gaertneria cordifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
