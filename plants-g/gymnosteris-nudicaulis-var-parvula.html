Gymnosteris nudicaulis var. parvula

Gymnosteris nudicaulis var. parvula, commonly known as the small-leafed gymnosteris, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its diminutive, narrow leaves and small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open, sun-exposed areas. Its growth habit is low and creeping, with stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread gradually.
