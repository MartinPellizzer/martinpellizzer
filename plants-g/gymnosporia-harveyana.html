Gymnosporia harveyana

Gymnosporia harveyana, commonly known as the Harvey's burr.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces woody, cone-like structures that contain the seeds, typical of gymnosperms. Its leaves are usually small and leathery, adapted to arid environments.

Gymnosporia harveyana is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
