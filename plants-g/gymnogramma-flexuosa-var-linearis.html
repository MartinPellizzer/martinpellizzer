Gymnogramma flexuosa var. linearis

Gymnogramma flexuosa var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf staghorn fern, is a variant of the staghorn fern genus Gymnogramma.

It is a epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia, typically found growing on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant features a distinctive, branching frond structure with a thick, leathery rachis and elongated, linear leaves. Its leaves are often curled or twisted, giving it a unique, ornamental appearance.

It thrives in shaded, moist conditions and is popular as a decorative plant in tropical gardens and indoor settings.
