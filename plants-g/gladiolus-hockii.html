Gladiolus hockii

Gladiolus hockii, commonly known as the Hockii gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing vibrant pink to red flowers with a distinctive, narrow spathe. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

Gladiolus hockii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
