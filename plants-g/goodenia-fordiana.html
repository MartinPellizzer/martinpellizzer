Goodenia fordiana

Goodenia fordiana, commonly known as the Ford's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia fordiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
