Geranium ussuriense

Geranium ussuriense, commonly known as the Ussuri geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky slopes. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces clusters of pink to purple flowers in the summer months. Its botanical characteristics include a slender stem and fibrous root system.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
