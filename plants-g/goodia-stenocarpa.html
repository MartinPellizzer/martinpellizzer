Goodia stenocarpa

Goodia stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited goodia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodiaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and woody stems. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in shrublands and open woodlands.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
