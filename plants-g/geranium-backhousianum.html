Geranium backhousianum

Geranium backhousianum, commonly known as the Cape Geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms during the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Geranium backhousianum is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
