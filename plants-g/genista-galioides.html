Genista galioides

Genista galioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it adaptable to rocky or sandy habitats.

Genista galioides is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
