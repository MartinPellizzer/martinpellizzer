Goodenia vilmoriniae

Goodenia vilmoriniae, commonly known as the Vilmorini's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the spring or summer months.

It is often found in open woodlands and along coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
