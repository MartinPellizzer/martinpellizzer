Genista leptoclada var. latifolia

Genista leptoclada var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in dense clusters during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
