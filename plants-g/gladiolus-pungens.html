Gladiolus pungens

Gladiolus pungens, commonly known as the sword lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Its flowers are borne on tall spikes and are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant appearance.

The plant is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
