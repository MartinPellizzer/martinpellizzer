Gentiana crispata subsp. visianii

Gentiana crispata subsp.

visianii, commonly known as the Visian's gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family. It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which are typically arranged in dense clusters.

The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and the Tian Shan. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions and well-drained soils.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
