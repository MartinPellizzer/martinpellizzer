Gesneria binghamii

Gesneria binghamii, commonly known as the Bingham gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It prefers high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a sought-after ornamental plant.
