Gomphocarpus hastatus var. angustifolius

Gomphocarpus hastatus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, angular stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap.
