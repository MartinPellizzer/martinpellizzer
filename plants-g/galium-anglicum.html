Galium anglicum

Galium anglicum, commonly known as cleavers, is a low-growing herbaceous plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems that often root at the nodes, allowing it to spread along the ground. Galium anglicum is commonly found in moist habitats such as meadows, ditches, and woodland edges.
