Goodyera Discoidea

Goodyera discoidea is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring dark green leaves and delicate white flowers with a subtle purple tint. This plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in dense forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important species.
