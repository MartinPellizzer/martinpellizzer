Globularia liouvillei subsp. greuteri

Globularia liouvillei subsp.

greuteri, commonly known as the Cape Verde globularia, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the Cape Verde Islands. It features dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically rounded and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
