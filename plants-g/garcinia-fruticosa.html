Garcinia fruticosa

Garcinia fruticosa, commonly known as the African mangosteen, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are typically red or yellow when mature. These berries contain a high concentration of xanthones, which are bioactive compounds with potential health benefits.

Garcinia fruticosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
