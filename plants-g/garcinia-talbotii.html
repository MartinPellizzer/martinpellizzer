Garcinia talbotii

Garcinia talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's garcinia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are highly ornamental. Garcinia talbotii is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a decorative plant.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to the genus Garcinia.
