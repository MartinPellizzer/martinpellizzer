Geranium penicillatum

Geranium penicillatum, commonly known as the pink geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant pink to purple flowers. The plant features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Geranium penicillatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
