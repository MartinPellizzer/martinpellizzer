Gladiolus segetum var. bornetii

Gladiolus segetum var. bornetii, commonly known as the Armenian gladiolus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, tall spikes of vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in summer, with colors ranging from white to deep red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and floral arrangements.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in horticulture.
