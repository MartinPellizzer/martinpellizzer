Goeppertia ovata

Goeppertia ovata, commonly known as the silver arrow plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It features broad, dark green leaves with a waxy, silvery sheen that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are often adorned with prominent veins, adding to its visual appeal.

Goeppertia ovata prefers bright, indirect light and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for tropical interiors.
