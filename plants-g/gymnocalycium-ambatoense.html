Gymnocalycium ambatoense

Gymnocalycium ambatoense, commonly known as the Ambato moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its swollen, fleshy stems that store water, typical of desert plants. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It has a spiny exterior with areoles that bear both spines and flowers.

Gymnocalycium ambatoense is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
