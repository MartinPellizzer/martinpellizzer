Gardenia goetzei

Gardenia goetzei, commonly known as the Goetze's gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is evergreen and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Gardenia goetzei requires well-drained soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
