Gnetum leptostachyum var. tenue

Gnetum leptostachyum var. tenue, commonly known as the slender-stemmed gnetum, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a gymnosperm, belonging to the family Gnetaceae, and exhibits unique botanical features such as opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Its reproductive structures are adapted for wind pollination, reflecting its evolutionary divergence from other gymnosperms.
