Gonolobus nummularius

Gonolobus nummularius, commonly known as the silverleaf senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically divided into five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
