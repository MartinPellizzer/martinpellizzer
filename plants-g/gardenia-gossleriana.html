Gardenia gossleriana

Gardenia gossleriana, commonly known as the white gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia gossleriana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and pleasant fragrance.
