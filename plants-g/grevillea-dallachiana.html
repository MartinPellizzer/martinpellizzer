Grevillea dallachiana

Grevillea dallachiana, commonly known as the red-stemmed Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and narrow, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are typical of the Grevillea genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions.
