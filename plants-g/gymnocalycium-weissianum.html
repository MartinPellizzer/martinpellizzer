Gymnocalycium weissianum

Gymnocalycium weissianum, commonly known as the Weissianum cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. The plant has spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and conservation efforts.
