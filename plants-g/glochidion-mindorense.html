Glochidion mindorense

Glochidion mindorense, commonly known as the Mindoro glabrous leaf.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines, specifically found on the island of Mindoro. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, dry seeds.
