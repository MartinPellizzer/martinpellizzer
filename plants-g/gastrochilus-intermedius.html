Gastrochilus intermedius

Gastrochilus intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate gastrochilus, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a spike. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
