Goodenia pterygosperma

Goodenia pterygosperma, commonly known as the broad-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

Goodenia pterygosperma is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
