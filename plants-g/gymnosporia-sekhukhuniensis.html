Gymnosporia sekhukhuniensis

Gymnosporia sekhukhuniensis, commonly known as the Sekhukhune thornveld acacia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers and small, woody pods that contain seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing resilience to drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and ecosystem.
