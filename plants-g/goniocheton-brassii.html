Goniocheton brassii

Goniocheton brassii, commonly known as the African butter tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale-green bark and large, palmate leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its latex sap is milky and can be used in traditional medicine.

Goniocheton brassii is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and carving.
