Guidonia icosandra

Guidonia icosandra, commonly known as the rock fig, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a small, shrub-like species with thick, fleshy leaves that are adapted to arid conditions. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Guidonia icosandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.
