Grevillea irrasa subsp. irrasa

Grevillea irrasa subsp.

irrasa, commonly known as the yellow grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking yellow flowers in clusters.

The plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The subspecies is notable for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
