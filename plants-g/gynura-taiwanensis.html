Gynura taiwanensis

Gynura taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan gynura, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Taiwan and is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
