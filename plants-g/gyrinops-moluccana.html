Gyrinops moluccana

Gyrinops moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan starfruit, is a tropical plant native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is a climbing vine that produces small, star-shaped fruits with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is edible, though its flavor is often described as bland or slightly acidic.
