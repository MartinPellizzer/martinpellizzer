Gagea rubicunda

Gagea rubicunda, commonly known as the redstem gagea, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, grass-like foliage and delicate, bell-shaped flowers. Gagea rubicunda is often found in rocky or open woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soils.
