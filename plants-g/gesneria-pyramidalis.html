Gesneria pyramidalis

Gesneria pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its dense, pyramidal growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the summer months.

Gesneria pyramidalis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
