Gentiana sikkimensis

Gentiana sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim gentian, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

Gentiana sikkimensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
