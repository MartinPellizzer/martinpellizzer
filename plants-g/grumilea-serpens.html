Grumilea serpens

Grumilea serpens, commonly known as the serpentine gruminel, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spiraling growth pattern, which gives it its common name. The plant exhibits a distinctive morphology with elongated, twisted stems and small, clustered leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical forests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Grumilea serpens is of particular interest to botanists and conservationists.
