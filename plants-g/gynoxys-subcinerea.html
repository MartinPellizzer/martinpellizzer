Gynoxys subcinerea

Gynoxys subcinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf gynoxys, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features small, pale green leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, often blooming in the spring.

Gynoxys subcinerea is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
