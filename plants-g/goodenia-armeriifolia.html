Goodenia armeriifolia

Goodenia armeriifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which bloom during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Goodenia armeriifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
