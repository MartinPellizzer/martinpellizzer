Greeneina poeppigiana

Greeneina poeppigiana, commonly known as the silverleaf greeneina, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
