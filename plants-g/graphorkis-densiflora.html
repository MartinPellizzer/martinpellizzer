Graphorkis densiflora

Graphorkis densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
