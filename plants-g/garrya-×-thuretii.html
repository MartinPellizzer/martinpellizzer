Garrya × thuretii

Garrya × thuretii, commonly known as the silverleaf holly, is a hybrid shrub native to California.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Garrya × thuretii is also valued for its evergreen foliage and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
