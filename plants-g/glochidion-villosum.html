Glochidion villosum

Glochidion villosum, commonly known as the hairy glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces capsules as its fruit, which contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
