Gynerium sagittatum var. sagittatum

Gynerium sagittatum var. sagittatum, commonly known as arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically alternate and have a distinctive notched base. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in shallow water or moist soil, making it an important species in aquatic ecosystems.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and stabilize soil along water bodies.
