Gardenia rothmannia

Gardenia rothmannia, commonly known as the Cape Gardenia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has broad, dark green leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Gardenia rothmannia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrant blooms.
