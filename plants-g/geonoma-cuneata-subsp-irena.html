Geonoma cuneata subsp. irena

Geonoma cuneata subsp.

irena, commonly known as the Irena's sword fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family. It is a small, evergreen fern characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a serrated or toothed margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded gardens.
