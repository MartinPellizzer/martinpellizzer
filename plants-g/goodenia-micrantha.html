Goodenia micrantha

Goodenia micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has linear, often hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and heathlands.

Goodenia micrantha is an important component of the flora in its native habitat and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.
