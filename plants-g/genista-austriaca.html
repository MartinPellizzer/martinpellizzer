Genista austriaca

Genista austriaca, commonly known as Austrian broom, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and flowering structures. The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. Its slender, branching stems are often covered with fine, soft hairs.

Genista austriaca is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in temperate climates.
