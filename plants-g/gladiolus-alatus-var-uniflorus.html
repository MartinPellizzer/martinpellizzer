Gladiolus alatus var. uniflorus

Gladiolus alatus var. uniflorus, commonly known as the single-flowered sword lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves and a single, large, showy flower that typically blooms in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their elegance and longevity.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
