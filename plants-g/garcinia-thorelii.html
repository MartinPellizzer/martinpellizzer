Garcinia thorelii

Garcinia thorelii, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by orange-red berries that are rich in bioactive compounds. Garcinia thorelii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various health benefits.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for wildlife in its native range.
