Goeppertia ovandensis

Goeppertia ovandensis, commonly known as the Ovandensis Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America, specifically Ecuador.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, often displaying a mix of green, silver, and red hues. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
