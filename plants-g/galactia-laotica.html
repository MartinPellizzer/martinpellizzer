Galactia laotica

Galactia laotica, commonly known as the Laos milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several seeds enclosed in a fibrous mesh.

Galactia laotica is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
