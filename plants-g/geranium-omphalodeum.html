Geranium omphalodeum

Geranium omphalodeum, commonly known as the star geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, star-shaped pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has upright stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Geranium omphalodeum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
