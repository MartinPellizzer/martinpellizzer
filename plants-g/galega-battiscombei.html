Galega battiscombei

Galega battiscombei, commonly known as the Natal vetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a robust root system and is adapted to grow in arid and semi-arid environments. It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
