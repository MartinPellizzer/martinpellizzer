Globularia alypum var. fallax

Globularia alypum var. fallax, commonly known as the rock globularia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a compact, globular shape, with succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
