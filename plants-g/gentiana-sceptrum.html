Gentiana sceptrum

Gentiana sceptrum, commonly known as the royal gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained, rocky soils. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
