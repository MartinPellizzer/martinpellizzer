Gesneria brevifolia

Gesneria brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and broad, waxy leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria brevifolia is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
