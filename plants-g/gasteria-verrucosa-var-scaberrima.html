Gasteria verrucosa var. scaberrima

Gasteria verrucosa var. scaberrima, commonly known as the rough-leaved gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a rough, waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements in landscaping and indoor gardening.
