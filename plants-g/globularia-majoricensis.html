Globularia majoricensis

Globularia majoricensis, commonly known as the Majorca globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, globular shape and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat.

Globularia majoricensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with similar environmental conditions.
