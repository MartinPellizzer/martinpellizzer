Geranium ibericum

Geranium ibericum, commonly known as the Iberian geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers range in color from pink to red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
