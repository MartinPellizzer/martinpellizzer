Garcinia sessilis

Garcinia sessilis, commonly known as the sessile Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its botanical features include smooth, woody stems and clusters of small, fleshy berries.

Garcinia sessilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
