Goodenia brendannarum

Goodenia brendannarum, commonly known as the Sturt's Goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, and it is often found in shrublands or along riverbanks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
