Graphorkis violacea

Graphorkis violacea, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a flowering plant native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
