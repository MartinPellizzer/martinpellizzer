Gouldia kaala

Gouldia kaala, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It is a member of the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Gouldia kaala thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
