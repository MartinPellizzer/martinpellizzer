Geranium castroviejoi

Geranium castroviejoi, commonly known as the Castroviejoi geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces bright red or pink blooms during the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
