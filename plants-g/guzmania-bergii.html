Guzmania bergii

Guzmania bergii, commonly known as the Berg's bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaf structure that collects water. The plant features vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that can range in color from red to orange, adding visual interest. Its foliage is often variegated with silver and green tones, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Guzmania bergii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
