Grevillea sericea var. diffusa

Grevillea sericea var. diffusa, commonly known as the silky grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features slender, wiry stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often covered with a fine layer of white tomentum.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
