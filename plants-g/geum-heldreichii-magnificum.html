Geum heldreichii magnificum

Geum heldreichii magnificum, commonly known as the Balkan avens, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of showy, rose-purple to reddish-pink flowers. The plant has palmately divided, heart-shaped leaves that are hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Geum heldreichii magnificum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
