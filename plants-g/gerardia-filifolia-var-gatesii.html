Gerardia filifolia var. gatesii

Gerardia filifolia var. gatesii, commonly known as the Gates' filifoliate gerardia, is a rare and unique variant of the gerardia genus.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that are closely arranged along its slender stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions with high humidity. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in clusters near the base of the plant.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species in botanical studies.
