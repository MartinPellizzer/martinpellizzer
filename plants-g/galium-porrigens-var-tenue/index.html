Galium Porrigens Var. Tenue

Galium porrigens var. tenue is a unique variant of the bedstraw genus known for its delicate, trailing growth habit. This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its small, white or pale pink flowers bloom in clusters, adding a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, contributing to its characteristic appearance. While not widely cultivated, it holds ecological significance for supporting local insect populations and maintaining biodiversity.
