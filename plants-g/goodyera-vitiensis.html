Goodyera vitiensis

Goodyera vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green, heart-shaped leaves and its clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, thriving in high humidity and well-drained soil. Its flowers are often short-lived but attract various pollinators such as bees and flies.

Goodyera vitiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
