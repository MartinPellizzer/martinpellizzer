Geranium nepalense var. thunbergii

Geranium nepalense var. thunbergii, commonly known as the Himalayan geranium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and northern India.

It features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value and ability to tolerate cooler climates make it a popular choice among gardeners in temperate regions.
