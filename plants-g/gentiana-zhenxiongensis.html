Gentiana zhenxiongensis

Gentiana zhenxiongensis, commonly known as the Zhenxiong gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with high altitudes and cool climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Gentiana zhenxiongensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
