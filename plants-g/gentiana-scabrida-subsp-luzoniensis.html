Gentiana scabrida subsp. luzoniensis

Gentiana scabrida subsp.

luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines. It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous forests. Its leaves are often covered in fine, scaly hairs, contributing to its rough texture.

It plays an important role in its native ecosystem and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
