Geonoma aspidiifolia subsp. fusca

Geonoma aspidiifolia subsp.

fusca, commonly known as the black-veined fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds with prominent, contrasting veins.
