Grevillea erectiloba

Grevillea erectiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces striking, red or orange cylindrical flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Grevillea erectiloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural landscapes.
