Grammica sandwichiana var. kailuana

Grammica sandwichiana var. kailuana, commonly known as the Kailua fern, is a tropical plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that grow in a rosette pattern. The plant typically thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in coastal areas or on rocky slopes. This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in humid, indoor settings.
