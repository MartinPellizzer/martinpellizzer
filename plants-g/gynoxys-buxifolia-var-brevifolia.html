Gynoxys buxifolia var. brevifolia

Gynoxys buxifolia var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved waxleaf, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and waxy in texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers during the warmer months. Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for hedges and containers.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.
