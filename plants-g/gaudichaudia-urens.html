Gaudichaudia urens

Gaudichaudia urens, commonly known as the slender sandmat, is a low-growing annual plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and Australia.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access water from deeper soil layers.

Gaudichaudia urens is well adapted to survive in nutrient-poor soils and extreme temperature fluctuations.
