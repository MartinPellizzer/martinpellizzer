Genista × prepirenaica

Genista × prepirenaica, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, wiry stems and compound leaves composed of several narrow leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a popular choice in gardens and landscaping.
