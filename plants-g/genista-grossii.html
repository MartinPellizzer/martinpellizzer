Genista grossii

Genista grossii, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, branching stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and Mediterranean regions.

Genista grossii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
