Glochidion montanum f. subglabrum

Glochidion montanum f. subglabrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved glabrous glochidion, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, yellowish fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the family Euphorbiaceae.
