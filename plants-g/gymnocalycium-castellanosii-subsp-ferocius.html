Gymnocalycium castellanosii subsp. ferocius

Gymnocalycium castellanosii subsp.

ferocius, commonly known as the Ferocious Crown of Thorns, is a species of cactus native to northern Chile. It is characterized by its spiny, globular stems and is adapted to arid environments.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It has a slow growth rate and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species.
