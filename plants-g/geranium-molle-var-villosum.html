Geranium molle var. villosum

Geranium molle var. villosum, commonly known as the soft geranium or velvet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of bright red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil. It is a perennial herb that prefers full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage and flowers are both attractive, making it a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
