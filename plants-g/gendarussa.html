Gendarussa

Gendarussa, commonly known as *Rauvolfia serpentina*, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces small, greenish-yellow flowers and red or orange berries. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, particularly due to the presence of alkaloids like reserpine. Its roots are often harvested for use in traditional and modern medicine.

Gendarussa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is commonly found in rainforests.
