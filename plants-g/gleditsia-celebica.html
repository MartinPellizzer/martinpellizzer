Gleditsia celebica

Gleditsia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes honeylocust, is a deciduous tree native to the island of Sulawesi in Indonesia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and thorned stems. The tree produces small, pod-like fruits that contain sweet, edible seeds. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in local ecosystems and traditional uses.
