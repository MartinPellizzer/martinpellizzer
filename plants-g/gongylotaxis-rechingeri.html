Gongylotaxis rechingeri

Gongylotaxis rechingeri, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets arranged in a feathery pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy environments.

Gongylotaxis rechingeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
