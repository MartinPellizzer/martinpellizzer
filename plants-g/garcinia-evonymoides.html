Garcinia evonymoides

Garcinia evonymoides, commonly known as the red-berried garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that contain a single seed. Garcinia evonymoides is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Its berries are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
