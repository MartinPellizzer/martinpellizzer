Gloriosa Lindenii

The gloriosa lindenii, also known as the flame lily, is a striking member of the lily family native to southern africa. it is characterized by its vibrant orange and red flowers with a distinctive curling shape. this plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions. it requires well-draining soil and partial to full sun exposure. its unique appearance makes it a favorite in both gardens and floral arrangements.
