Geranium wilfordii

Geranium wilfordii, commonly known as the Chinese geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically blooming in summer. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Geranium wilfordii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
