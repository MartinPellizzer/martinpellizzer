Gesneria odontophylla

Gesneria odontophylla, commonly known as the toothed-leaf gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with a distinctive toothed margin. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria odontophylla is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
