Goodenia viridula

Goodenia viridula, commonly known as the green goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish or yellowish in color. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. It is an erect annual or biennial herb that produces seed pods containing several seeds.

Goodenia viridula is adapted to arid environments and is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.
