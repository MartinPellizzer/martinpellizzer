Gladiolus odorus

Gladiolus odorus, commonly known as the sweet-scented gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy flowers. The plant produces vibrant, colorful blooms in shades of red, pink, yellow, and white, often with intricate patterns. Its flowers emit a subtle, sweet fragrance, which distinguishes it from other gladiolus species.

The plant is typically grown as an ornamental in gardens and is prized for its beauty and aromatic qualities.
