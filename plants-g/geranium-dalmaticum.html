Geranium dalmaticum

Geranium dalmaticum, commonly known as the Dalmatian geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
