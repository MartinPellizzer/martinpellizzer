Gomidesia nitida

Gomidesia nitida, commonly known as the velvetleaf or slender leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Gomidesia nitida is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.
