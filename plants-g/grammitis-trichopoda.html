Grammitis trichopoda

Grammitis trichopoda, commonly known as the hairy maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its leaves are usually finely divided and have a soft, velvety texture.
