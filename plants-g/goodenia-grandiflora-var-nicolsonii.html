Goodenia grandiflora var. nicolsonii

Goodenia grandiflora var. nicolsonii, commonly known as the Nicholson's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, and the flowers are arranged in spikes.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
