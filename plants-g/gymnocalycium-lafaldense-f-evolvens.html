Gymnocalycium lafaldense f. evolvens

Gymnocalycium lafaldense f. evolvens, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a unique cactus species native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which bloom at night. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its distinctive appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
