Goodenia geniculata var. robusta

Goodenia geniculata var. robusta, commonly known as the robust goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the spring and summer months.

It is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
