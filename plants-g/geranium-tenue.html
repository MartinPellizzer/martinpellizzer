Geranium tenue

Geranium tenue, commonly known as the narrowleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Geranium tenue is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
