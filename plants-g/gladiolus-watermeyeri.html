Gladiolus watermeyeri

Gladiolus watermeyeri, commonly known as the Watermeyeri gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that bear large, showy flowers in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. The plant has narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden beds or containers.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for floral arrangements and garden displays.
