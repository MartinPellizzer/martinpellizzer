Genetyllis thymoides

Genetyllis thymoides, commonly known as dwarf thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tiny, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and fragrant foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
