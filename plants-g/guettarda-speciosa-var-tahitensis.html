Guettarda speciosa var. tahitensis

Guettarda speciosa var. tahitensis, commonly known as Tahitian guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal forests and sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in tropical gardens for its beauty and resilience.
