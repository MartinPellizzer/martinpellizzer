Goodenia heterophylla

Goodenia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its variable leaf shapes, which can range from linear to ovate. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Goodenia heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
