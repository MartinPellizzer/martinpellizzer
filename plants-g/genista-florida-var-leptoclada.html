Genista florida var. leptoclada

Genista florida var. leptoclada, commonly known as the slender flowering broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to Mediterranean gardens.
