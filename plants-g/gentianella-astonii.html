Gentianella astonii

Gentianella astonii, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. The plant features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact growth habit.

Gentianella astonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
