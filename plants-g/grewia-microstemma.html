Grewia microstemma

Grewia microstemma, commonly known as the small-fruited globemallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences, giving it its common name. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered with fine hairs.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.
