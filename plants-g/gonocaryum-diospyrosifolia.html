Gonocaryum diospyrosifolia

Gonocaryum diospyrosifolia, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
