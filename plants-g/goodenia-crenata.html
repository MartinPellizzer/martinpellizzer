Goodenia crenata

Goodenia crenata, commonly known as the wavy goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, wavy-edged leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Goodenia crenata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
