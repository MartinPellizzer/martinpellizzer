Grastidium polyschistum

Grastidium polyschistum, commonly known as the stonecress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features small, pale green leaves with a slightly succulent texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is native to rocky and arid regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Grastidium polyschistum is often used in rock gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
