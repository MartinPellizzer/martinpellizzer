Genista odoratissima

Genista odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet broom or sweet yellow broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, sunny environments. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that are highly fragrant, especially in the evening. Its slender, branched stems are covered in fine, needle-like leaves.

Genista odoratissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
