Garciana cochinchinensis

Garciana cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright orange berries. The fruit contains a single seed and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
