Geranium rubescens

Geranium rubescens, commonly known as the scarlet geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Geranium rubescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
