Gerardia dispersa

Gerardia dispersa, commonly known as the scattered gerardia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and range in color from white to pale pink.

Gerardia dispersa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant.
