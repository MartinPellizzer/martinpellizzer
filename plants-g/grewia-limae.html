Grewia limae

Grewia limae, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white, fluffy flowers that resemble snowballs, which bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Grewia limae is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
