Globularia sintenisii

Globularia sintenisii, commonly known as the Canary Islands globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in its arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Globularia sintenisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh conditions.
