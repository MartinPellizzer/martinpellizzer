Govenia sulphurea

Govenia sulphurea, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble flames. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant blossoms that bloom in the summer months. Govenia sulphurea thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
