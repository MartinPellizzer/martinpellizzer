Garcinia rumphii

Garcinia rumphii, commonly known as the Kaffir lime, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves and small, round, greenish-yellow fruits. The plant belongs to the Rutaceae family and is valued for its essential oils extracted from its leaves and zest. Its botanical features include pinnately compound leaves and flowers with four petals.

The fruit is often used in cooking and traditional medicine due to its unique flavor and medicinal properties.
