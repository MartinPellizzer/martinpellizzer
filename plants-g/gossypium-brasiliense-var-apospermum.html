Gossypium brasiliense var. apospermum

Gossypium brasiliense var. apospermum, commonly known as the Brazilian cotton plant, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces fibrous seeds that are used in textile manufacturing. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
