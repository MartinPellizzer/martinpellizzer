Geraniospermum reflexum

Geraniospermum reflexum, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its succulent, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
