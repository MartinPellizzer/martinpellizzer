Galium cassium var. calvescens

Galium cassium var. calvescens, commonly known as slender bedstraw, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, ascending stems that are often hairy, and it produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters. The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This plant is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in moist, temperate regions.
