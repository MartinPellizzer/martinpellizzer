Gilia Inyoensis Var. Breviuscula

Gilia inyoensis var. breviuscula is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of California. It is known for its small, vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring. This variety is a lesser-known cousin of the more common Gilia species, often overlooked due to its subtle appearance. Found primarily in rocky soils and desert environments, it thrives in harsh conditions. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
