Gynura brassii

Gynura brassii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a rosette of broadly ovate, dark green leaves with wavy margins. Its flowers are typically yellow with a central dark brown disc, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Gynura brassii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience in various soil conditions.
