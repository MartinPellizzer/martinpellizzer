Gerbera cavaleriei

Gerbera cavaleriei, commonly known as the Cavalerie's gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with bright colors such as red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows in a compact form, with upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring to early autumn.
