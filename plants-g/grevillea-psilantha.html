Grevillea psilantha

Grevillea psilantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically grey-green in color, providing a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers in winter, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and full sun exposure.

Grevillea psilantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
