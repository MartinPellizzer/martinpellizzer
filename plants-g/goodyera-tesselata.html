Goodyera tesselata

Goodyera tesselata, commonly known as the basket lily or tesselated maidenhair, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white with a subtle pinkish hue. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Goodyera tesselata is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in its native habitat.
