Geraniospermum bifolium

Geraniospermum bifolium, commonly known as the two-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Geraniospermum bifolium is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
