Galactia dubia var. typica

Galactia dubia var. typica, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of its ecosystem.
