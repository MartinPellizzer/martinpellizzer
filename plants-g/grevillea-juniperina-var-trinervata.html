Grevillea juniperina var. trinervata

Grevillea juniperina var. trinervata, commonly known as the Three-veined Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are usually blue-green in color, with a distinctive three-veined structure. The plant produces bright red or orange cylindrical flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and full sun exposure.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
