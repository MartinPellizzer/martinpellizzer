Gentiana pulvinarum subsp. subtilis

Gentiana pulvinarum subsp.

subtilis, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowering stems rise above the foliage.

The flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators with their vibrant colors and nectar.
