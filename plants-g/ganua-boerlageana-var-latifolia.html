Ganua boerlageana var. latifolia

Ganua boerlageana var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved ganua, is a rare and distinctive plant native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
