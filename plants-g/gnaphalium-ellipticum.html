Gnaphalium ellipticum

Gnaphalium ellipticum, commonly known as the oval cudweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, elongated leaves and clusters of tiny white to pale yellow flowers. It produces a fine, powdery substance called a bloom, which is often mistaken for pollen.
