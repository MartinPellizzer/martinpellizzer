Genista ovata

Genista ovata, commonly known as the oval buckthorn, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, often spiny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, oval-shaped pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
