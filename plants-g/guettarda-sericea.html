Guettarda sericea

Guettarda sericea, commonly known as silverleaf guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small white or pale yellow flowers are fragrant and bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators.

Guettarda sericea is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
