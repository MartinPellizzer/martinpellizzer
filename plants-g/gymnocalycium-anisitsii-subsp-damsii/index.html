Gymnocalycium Anisitsii Subsp. Damsii

Gymnocalycium anisitsii subsp. damsii is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of northern Argentina. This subspecies is known for its spiny stems and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in harsh desert environments where water is scarce and temperatures fluctuate dramatically. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is highly sought after by collectors and botanists alike. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from habitat loss and over-collection.
