Grevillea neglecta

Grevillea neglecta, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish hue. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters, which bloom from spring to autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea neglecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid conditions.
