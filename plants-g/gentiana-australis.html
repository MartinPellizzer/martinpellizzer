Gentiana australis

Gentiana australis, commonly known as the Australian gentian, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, silky hair.

Gentiana australis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
