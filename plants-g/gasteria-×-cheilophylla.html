Gasteria × cheilophylla

Gasteria × cheilophylla, commonly known as the hybrid gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Gasteria × cheilophylla is popular as a decorative plant due to its compact size and striking foliage.
