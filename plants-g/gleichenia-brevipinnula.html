Gleichenia brevipinnula

Gleichenia brevipinnula, commonly known as the short-pinnate maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or rocky areas. Its fronds are usually short and pinnately divided, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Gleichenia brevipinnula is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
