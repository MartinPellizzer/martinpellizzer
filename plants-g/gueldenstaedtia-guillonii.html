Gueldenstaedtia guillonii

Gueldenstaedtia guillonii, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. Native to temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in meadows and woodland edges. Its seed pods are typically slender and twisted, containing small, rounded seeds.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often considered a conservation concern in some areas.
