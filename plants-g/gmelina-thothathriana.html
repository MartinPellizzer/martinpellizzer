Gmelina thothathriana

Gmelina thothathriana, commonly known as the slender gmelina, is a flowering plant belonging to the Oleaceae family.

It is a deciduous tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits containing one or two seeds.

It is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is valued for its timber and ornamental qualities.
