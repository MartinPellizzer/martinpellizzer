Gladiolus dieterlenii

Gladiolus dieterlenii, commonly known as the Dieterlen's gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves and is typically found in grassland habitats. It is a member of the Iridaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
