Genista subcapitata var. mariovoensis

Genista subcapitata var. mariovoensis, commonly known as the Mariovo thornbush, is a shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climate conditions. Its leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and resilience in arid environments.
