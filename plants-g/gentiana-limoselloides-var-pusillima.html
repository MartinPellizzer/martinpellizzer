Gentiana limoselloides var. pusillima

Gentiana limoselloides var. pusillima, commonly known as the small gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in mountain meadows.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and sensitive species.
