Gloxinia speciosa var. candida

Gloxinia speciosa var. candida, commonly known as the white gloxinia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Spathiphyllium genus.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, white flowers that contrast with its dark green, velvety foliage. The plant is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

The flowers bloom in spring and summer, adding a striking visual appeal to indoor spaces.
