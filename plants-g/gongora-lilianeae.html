Gongora lilianeae

Gongora lilianeae, commonly known as the Liliane's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the highlands of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking violet and white petals, with a distinctive greenish-yellow lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments at elevations around 2,000 to 3,000 meters. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom during the cooler months, attracting various pollinators.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
