Gerbera araneosa

Gerbera araneosa, commonly known as the spider gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers with bright, showy petals that come in a range of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces its flowers on long, slender stems. Its leaves are typically hairy and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Gerbera araneosa is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
