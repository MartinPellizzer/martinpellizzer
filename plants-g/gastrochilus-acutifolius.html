Gastrochilus acutifolius

Gastrochilus acutifolius, commonly known as the sharp-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Gastrochilus acutifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
