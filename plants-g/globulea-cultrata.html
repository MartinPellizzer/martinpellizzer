Globulea cultrata

Globulea cultrata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers are borne on upright stalks and are known for their vibrant color and long-lasting bloom.

Globulea cultrata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
