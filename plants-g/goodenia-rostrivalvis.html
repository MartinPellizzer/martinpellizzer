Goodenia rostrivalvis

Goodenia rostrivalvis, commonly known as the narrow-beaked goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, beak-like flower petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid and semi-arid regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
