Gynura daviesiae

Gynura daviesiae, commonly known as the velvetleaf or the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typical of the daisy family. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
