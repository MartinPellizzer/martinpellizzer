Gardenia pyriformis subsp. orientalis

Gardenia pyriformis subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers bloom in summer and are highly valued for their ornamental beauty and strong, sweet scent.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
