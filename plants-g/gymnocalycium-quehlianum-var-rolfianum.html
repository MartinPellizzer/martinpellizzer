Gymnocalycium quehlianum var. rolfianum

Gymnocalycium quehlianum var. rolfianum, commonly known as the Rolfian moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features a thick, spiny stem covered in areoles, which bear both spines and flowers. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
