Gonostemon Clavicorona

Gonostemon redweed, scientifically known as Gonostemon clavicorona, is a hardy perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia. It is characterized by its dense, mat-like growth habit and bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. This plant is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including high temperatures and low rainfall. Despite its resilience, it is considered a weed in some areas due to its invasive tendencies. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of both botanical interest and land management concern.
