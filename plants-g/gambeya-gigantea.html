Gambeya gigantea

Gambeya gigantea, commonly known as the giant gambeya, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
