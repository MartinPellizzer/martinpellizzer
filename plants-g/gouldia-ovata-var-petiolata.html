Gouldia ovata var. petiolata

Gouldia ovata var. petiolata, commonly known as the broad-leafed gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features elongated, petioled leaves that give it a unique appearance, distinguishing it from other gouldia varieties. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
