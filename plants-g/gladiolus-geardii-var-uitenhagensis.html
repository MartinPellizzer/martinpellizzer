Gladiolus geardii var. uitenhagensis

Gladiolus geardii var. uitenhagensis, commonly known as the Uitenhagen gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, producing spike-like inflorescences with numerous petals. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the vibrant blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
