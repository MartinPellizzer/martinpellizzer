Grevillea ilicifolia var. angustiloba

Grevillea ilicifolia var. angustiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces bright red or orange cylindrical flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and drought tolerance.
