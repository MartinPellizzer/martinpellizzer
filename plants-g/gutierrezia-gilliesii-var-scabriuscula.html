Gutierrezia gilliesii var. scabriuscula

Gutierrezia gilliesii var. scabriuscula, commonly known as silverleaf thread-leaf goldenweed, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small yellow flowers in composite heads, which are typical of the daisy family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil make it an important plant in ecological restoration projects.
