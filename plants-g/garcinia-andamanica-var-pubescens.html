Garcinia andamanica var. pubescens

Garcinia andamanica var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, red berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and medicinal research.
