Geranium whartonianum

Geranium whartonianum, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Geranium whartonianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
