Gardenia curvata

Gardenia curvata, commonly known as the zigzag gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are arranged in a zigzag pattern along the stem. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia curvata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
