Gymnacanthus humilis

Gymnacanthus humilis, commonly known as the lowland orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest floors and thrives in humid environments. The plant has a slender stem and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest soil efficiently.

Gymnacanthus humilis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
