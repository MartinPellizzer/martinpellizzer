Geranium mlanjense

Geranium mlanjense, commonly known as the Malanje geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems and produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
