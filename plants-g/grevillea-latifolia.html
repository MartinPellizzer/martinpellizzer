Grevillea latifolia

Grevillea latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Grevillea latifolia is also valued for its ability to attract native birds and insects.
