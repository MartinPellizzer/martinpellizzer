Glossoloma purpureum

Glossoloma purpureum, commonly known as the purple glossoloma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Glossoloma purpureum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
