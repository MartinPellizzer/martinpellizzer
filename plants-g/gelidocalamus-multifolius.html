Gelidocalamus multifolius

Gelidocalamus multifolius, commonly known as the multileaf cold bamboo, is a unique plant species native to the high-altitude regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its slender, cold-tolerant stems and its ability to thrive in harsh, mountainous environments. Gelidocalamus multifolius is of interest to botanists due to its ecological significance and potential for medicinal use.
