Guioa pubescens

Guioa pubescens, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a deciduous tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and alternate, ovate leaves with a pubescent underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed.
