Gentiana patagonica

Gentiana patagonica, commonly known as the Patagonian gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months, typically from June to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and forest understories.

Gentiana patagonica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
