Galanthus cilicicus

Galanthus cilicicus, commonly known as the Cilician snowdrop, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Turkey and northern Syria. The plant produces delicate white flowers with green tips, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, emerging from a bulbous underground stem.

Galanthus cilicicus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring display.
