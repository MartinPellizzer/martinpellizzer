Grevillea gibbosa

Grevillea gibbosa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features bright red, fluffy flowers that bloom in spring, attracting birds and insects. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Grevillea gibbosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native landscaping.
