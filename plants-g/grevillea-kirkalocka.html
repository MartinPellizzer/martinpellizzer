Grevillea kirkalocka

Grevillea kirkalocka, commonly known as the Mount Lofty grevillea, is a shrub native to South Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically grey-green in color. The plant produces striking, red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Grevillea kirkalocka is often found in rocky outcrops and is a valuable addition to native gardens.
