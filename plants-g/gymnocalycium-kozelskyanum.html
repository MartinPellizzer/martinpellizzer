Gymnocalycium kozelskyanum

Gymnocalycium kozelskyanum, commonly known as the Kozelsky's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its globular, ribbed stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by red, spherical fruits. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for succulent collections.
