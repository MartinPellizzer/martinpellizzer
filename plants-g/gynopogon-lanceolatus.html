Gynopogon lanceolatus

Gynopogon lanceolatus, commonly known as the narrowleaf gynopogon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and is known for its slender, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in composite heads that attract various pollinators.
