Garcinia maingayi var. stylosa

Garcinia maingayi var. stylosa, commonly known as the slender Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces orange-red berries that are rich in bioactive compounds, making it of interest in botanical and pharmaceutical research. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties contribute to its significance in both horticulture and natural medicine.
