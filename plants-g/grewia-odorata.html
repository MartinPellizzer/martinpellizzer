Grewia odorata

Grewia odorata, commonly known as the sweet flag or sweet myrtle, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers that are often fragrant. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Grewia odorata is often found in moist, sandy soils and is adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
