Gynura vidaliana

Gynura vidaliana, commonly known as the silver-leaf cyclocarp, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, greenish fruits. Gynura vidaliana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique leaf color and compact growth habit.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden settings.
