Garcia

Garcia, commonly known as the garcinia, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Clusiaceae.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. They are characterized by their showy, often brightly colored flowers and their ability to produce valuable medicinal compounds. Many species of garcinia are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fruit of some species, such as Garcinia cambodiana, is also used in weight loss supplements.
