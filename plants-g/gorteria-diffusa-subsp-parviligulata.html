Gorteria diffusa subsp. parviligulata

Gorteria diffusa subsp.

parviligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gorteria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring, typically in shades of yellow and white.

The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate flowers and drought tolerance.
