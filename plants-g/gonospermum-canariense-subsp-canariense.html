Gonospermum canariense subsp. canariense

Gonospermum canariense subsp.

canariense, commonly known as the Canary Island lily, is a herbaceous plant native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

The plant features strap-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in various horticultural settings.
