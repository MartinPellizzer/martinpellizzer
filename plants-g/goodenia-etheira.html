Goodenia etheira

Goodenia etheira, commonly known as the Ethiopian goodenia, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to low rainfall conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss.

Goodenia etheira is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
