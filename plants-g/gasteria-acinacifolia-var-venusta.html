Gasteria acinacifolia var. venusta

Gasteria acinacifolia var. venusta, commonly known as the Zulu sword or Zulu grass, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are narrow and sword-shaped, with a distinctive white or yellowish midrib. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular, white or pale green flowers. It is well adapted to arid conditions, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
