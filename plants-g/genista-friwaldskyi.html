Genista friwaldskyi

Genista friwaldskyi, commonly known as Friwaldsky's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of small, compound leaves, and it produces seed pods that contain several seeds.
