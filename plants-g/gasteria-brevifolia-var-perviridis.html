Gasteria brevifolia var. perviridis

Gasteria brevifolia var. perviridis, commonly known as the African violet plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
