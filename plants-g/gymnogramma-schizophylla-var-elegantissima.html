Gymnogramma schizophylla var. elegantissima

Gymnogramma schizophylla var. elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant kidney fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often adorned with fine, serrated edges. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sori, which are typically located on the underside of the fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
