Grusonia × congesta

Grusonia × congesta, commonly known as the hybrid Chinese evergreen, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Grusonia sanguinea and Grusonia lingua.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. The leaves are typically dark green with a glossy texture and can exhibit variegation in some cultivars. It prefers warm temperatures and high humidity, often requiring regular misting to maintain optimal conditions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
