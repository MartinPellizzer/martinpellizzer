Gentiana × rubra

Gentiana × rubra, commonly known as the red gentian, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which are typically tubular with a bright red or orange throat. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, this species thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces showy flowers from late summer to early autumn, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
