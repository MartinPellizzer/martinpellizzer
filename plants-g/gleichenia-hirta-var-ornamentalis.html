Gleichenia hirta var. ornamentalis

Gleichenia hirta var. ornamentalis, commonly known as the ornamental feather fern, is a variant of the feather fern species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a soft, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually dark green with a fine, hairy texture, contributing to its unique visual appeal.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
