Gloriosa revoilii

Gloriosa revoilii, commonly known as the flame lily, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, orange-red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, spiral-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
