Grevillea canescens

Grevillea canescens, commonly known as the silver grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright red or orange cylindrical flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and full sun exposure.

Grevillea canescens is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
