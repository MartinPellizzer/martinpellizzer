Gongora unicolor

Gongora unicolor, commonly known as the white orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and a unique, elongated lip that distinguishes it from other orchid species. Gongora unicolor has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the nutrient-poor soils where it thrives.
