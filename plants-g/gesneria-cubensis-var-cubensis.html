Gesneria cubensis var. cubensis

Gesneria cubensis var. cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban gesneria, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Cuba and is characterized by its evergreen, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. It produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the summer months.

This species is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
