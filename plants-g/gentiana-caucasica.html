Gentiana caucasica

Gentiana caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and forested areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Gentiana caucasica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
