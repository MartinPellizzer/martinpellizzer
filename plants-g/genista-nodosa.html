Genista nodosa

Genista nodosa, commonly known as the nodding broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, and its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets. Genista nodosa is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

It is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
