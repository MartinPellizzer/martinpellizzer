Guenthera setulosa

Guenthera setulosa, commonly known as the hairy gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers, which are typically tubular in shape and attract pollinators such as butterflies and hummingbirds.
