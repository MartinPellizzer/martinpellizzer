Gardenia affinis

Gardenia affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, narrow leaves and produces fragrant white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is evergreen and thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.

Gardenia affinis is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
