Gagea foliosa var. media

Gagea foliosa var. media, commonly known as the intermediate leaf gagea, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale pink flowers.
