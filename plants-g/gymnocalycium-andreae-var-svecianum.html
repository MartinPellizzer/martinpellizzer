Gymnocalycium andreae var. svecianum

Gymnocalycium andreae var. svecianum, commonly known as the Svecian moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its rounded, fleshy stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, which are followed by bright red fruits. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and indoor gardening.
