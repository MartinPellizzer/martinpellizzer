Gomphia vasivae

Gomphia vasivae, commonly known as the Namib Desert grass, is a unique plant adapted to extreme arid conditions.

It is a perennial grass that grows in the Namib Desert of Namibia, where it thrives in sandy soils with minimal rainfall. The plant has a low-growing, cushion-like structure that helps reduce water loss through evaporation. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a waxy cuticle, which further aids in water conservation.

Gomphia vasivae plays a vital role in stabilizing the desert ecosystem and providing habitat for small desert organisms.
