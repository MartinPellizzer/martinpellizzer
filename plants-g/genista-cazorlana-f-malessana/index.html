Genista Cazorlana F. Malessana

Genista cazorlana f. malessana is a rare and distinctive plant native to southern Spain. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its delicate yellow flowers that bloom in spring. This particular form of Genista cazorlana is characterized by its unique foliage and compact growth habit. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile plant from habitat loss and environmental changes.
