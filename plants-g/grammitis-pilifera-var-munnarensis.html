Grammitis pilifera var. munnarensis

Grammitis pilifera var. munnarensis, commonly known as the Munnarensis Grammitis, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variant is distinguished by its fine, hair-like structures along the leaf edges, giving it a unique appearance.
