Genipa aculeata

Genipa aculeata, commonly known as the sour sop or custard apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in tropical ecosystems.
