Guettarda fontanesii

Guettarda fontanesii, commonly known as the yellow shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and clusters of small, fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
