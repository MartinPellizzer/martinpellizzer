Garcinia cereoflava

Garcinia cereoflava, commonly known as the golden apple or yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are rich in xanthones and other bioactive compounds. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties. Its fruit has a tart flavor and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Garcinia cereoflava thrives in humid, warm climates and is typically found in lowland rainforests.
