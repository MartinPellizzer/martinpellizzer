Gymnocalycium castellanosii subsp. bozsingianum

Gymnocalycium castellanosii subsp.

bozsingianum, commonly known as the Bozsing's moon cactus, is a succulent native to the arid regions of South America. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems.

The plant features a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in the spring or early summer.

This subspecies is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
