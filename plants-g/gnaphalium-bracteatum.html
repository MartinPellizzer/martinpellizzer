Gnaphalium bracteatum

Gnaphalium bracteatum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in dry, sunny environments. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The plant produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its propagation across arid regions.
