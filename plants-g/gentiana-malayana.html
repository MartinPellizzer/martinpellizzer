Gentiana malayana

Gentiana malayana, commonly known as the Malay gentian, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Gentiana malayana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
