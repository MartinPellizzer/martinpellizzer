Gentiana davidi

Gentiana davidi, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height, supporting clusters of vibrant blue to violet flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Gentiana davidi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
