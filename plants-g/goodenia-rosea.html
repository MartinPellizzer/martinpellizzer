Goodenia rosea

Goodenia rosea, commonly known as the pink goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to mauve-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in well-drained environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, and it often forms dense clumps.

Goodenia rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
