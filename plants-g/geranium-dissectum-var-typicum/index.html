Geranium Dissectum Var. Typicum

Geranium dissectum var. typicum is a striking member of the Geraniaceae family known for its delicate, fern-like foliage and vibrant flowers. This perennial plant is native to parts of Europe and thrives in moist, shady environments. Its intricate leaf patterns and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental value. It is often used in woodland gardens or as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and moisture conditions to flourish.
