Garcinia menisperma

Garcinia menisperma, commonly known as kaffir lime, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, narrow leaves and yellow-orange fruits that contain a high concentration of aromatic essential oils. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal uses. Its fruit is widely used in cooking, particularly in Southeast Asian cuisine, for its distinctive flavor.

The tree also has traditional applications in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
