Gentiana bolavenensis

Gentiana bolavenensis, commonly known as the Bolaven gentian, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, which is known for its showy blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests and grasslands. Its striking blue petals are often adorned with intricate patterns, making it a visually appealing species.

Gentiana bolavenensis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
