Goniopteris rubida

Goniopteris rubida, commonly known as the red fern, is a fern species characterized by its striking reddish-brown fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a creeping rhizome with dark brown, leathery fronds that are often curled when young. Its leaves are pinnate, with lance-shaped pinnae that become more elongated as they mature.

Goniopteris rubida thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
