Garcinia kajewskii

Garcinia kajewskii, commonly known as the Kajewskii garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces yellow-orange berries that contain a high concentration of hydroxycitric acid, which has been studied for its potential health benefits. Garcinia kajewskii thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical research and horticulture.
