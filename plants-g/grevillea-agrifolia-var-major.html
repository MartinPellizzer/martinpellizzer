Grevillea agrifolia var. major

Grevillea agrifolia var. major, commonly known as the Sydney flame or coastal grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically blue-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Grevillea agrifolia var. major is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for native birds and insects.
