Garcinia kola

Garcinia kola, commonly known as African ackee, is a tropical tree native to West Africa.

It produces a fruit that contains a single large seed surrounded by a white, fleshy aril. The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with the seeds containing compounds like caffeine and theobromine. The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and cultural contexts.
