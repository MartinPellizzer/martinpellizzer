Galactodenia vareschii

Galactodenia vareschii, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow petals and a star-shaped flower structure. Galactodenia vareschii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
