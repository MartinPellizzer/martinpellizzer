Gomidesia anacardiifolia var. ovalis

Gomidesia anacardiifolia var. ovalis, commonly known as the oval-leaf gomidesia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that contains a single seed.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
