Goodenia minutiflora

Goodenia minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered goodenia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It features small, delicate flowers that are typically pale blue or violet, arranged in dense clusters along the stems. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Goodenia minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in native gardens.
