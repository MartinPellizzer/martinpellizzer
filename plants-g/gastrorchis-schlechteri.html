Gastrorchis schlechteri

Gastrorchis schlechteri, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the summer months. It is a terrestrial orchid that prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
