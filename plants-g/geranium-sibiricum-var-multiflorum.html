Geranium sibiricum var. multiflorum

Geranium sibiricum var. multiflorum, commonly known as Siberian geranium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems that are hairy and often branched, supporting clusters of small, showy flowers. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with five petals and a prominent central stamen. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are serrated and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
