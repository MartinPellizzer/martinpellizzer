Gethyllis longituba

Gethyllis longituba, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the dry environment.

Gethyllis longituba is noted for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
