Geranium grossularioides

Geranium grossularioides, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically features rounded, lobed leaves and produces pink to purple blooms during the summer months. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Geranium grossularioides prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
