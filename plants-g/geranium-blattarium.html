Geranium blattarium

Geranium blattarium, commonly known as the leaf geranium, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

Geranium blattarium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
