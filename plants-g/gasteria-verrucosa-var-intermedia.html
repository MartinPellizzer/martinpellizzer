Gasteria verrucosa var. intermedia

Gasteria verrucosa var. intermedia, commonly known as the African silver plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white or silver markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular, white to yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
