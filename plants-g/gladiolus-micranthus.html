Gladiolus micranthus

Gladiolus micranthus, commonly known as the dwarf gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of colorful, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, making it ideal for smaller garden spaces. Its flowers are borne on spikes and are often used in floral arrangements.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
