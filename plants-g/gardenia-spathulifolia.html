Gardenia spathulifolia

Gardenia spathulifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical to ovate leaves and produces fragrant, white to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its flowers are usually clustered in panicles and bloom during the summer months.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release the seeds.
