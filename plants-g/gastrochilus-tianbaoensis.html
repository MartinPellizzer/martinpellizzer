Gastrochilus tianbaoensis

Gastrochilus tianbaoensis, commonly known as the Tianbao orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions with rich soil. Its flowers are small to medium-sized, with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
