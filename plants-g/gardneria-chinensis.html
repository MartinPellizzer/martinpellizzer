Gardneria chinensis

Gardneria chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink and yellow. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Gardneria chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
