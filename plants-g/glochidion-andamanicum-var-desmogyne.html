Glochidion andamanicum var. desmogyne

Glochidion andamanicum var. desmogyne, commonly known as the Andaman glochidion, is a species native to the Andaman Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually opposite in arrangement. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in inflorescences, while the fruits are fleshy, berry-like drupes.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within lowland and hill forests.
