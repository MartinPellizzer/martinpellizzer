Goeppertia roseopicta

Goeppertia roseopicta, commonly known as the Pink Spotted Canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and distinctive pink to red spots that contrast beautifully with its deep green base. The plant produces showy, orchid-like flowers in shades of pink and orange, adding vibrant color to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Goeppertia roseopicta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to add a tropical flair to landscapes.
