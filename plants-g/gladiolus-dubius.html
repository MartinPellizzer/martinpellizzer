Gladiolus dubius

Gladiolus dubius, commonly known as the sword lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

The plant is also valued for its resilience and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
