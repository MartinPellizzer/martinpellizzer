Gladiolus hirsutus var. roseus

Gladiolus hirsutus var. roseus, commonly known as the rose-colored gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear large, showy flowers in shades of pink to deep red. The foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant green. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for summer blooms.
