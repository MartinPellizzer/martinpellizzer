Gentiana splendens

Gentiana splendens, commonly known as the Splendour gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of silky hairs.

Gentiana splendens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in garden settings.
