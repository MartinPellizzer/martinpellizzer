Gentiana aestiva

Gentiana aestiva, commonly known as the autumn gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowering stems are often unbranched.

Gentiana aestiva is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
