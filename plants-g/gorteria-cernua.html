Gorteria cernua

Gorteria cernua, commonly known as the hanging daisy, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that hang in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny conditions. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Gorteria cernua is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
