Globularia suecica

Globularia suecica, commonly known as Swedish globularia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to northern Europe.

It features small, rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped white or pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Globularia suecica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
