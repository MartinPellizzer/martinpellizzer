Geranium rubellum

Geranium rubellum, commonly known as the scarlet geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and upright, branching stems. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and produces showy, five-petaled blooms. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a hairy texture, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Geranium rubellum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
