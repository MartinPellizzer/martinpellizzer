Gravesia bullosa

Gravesia bullosa, commonly known as the balloon vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, trailing stems and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant produces distinctive balloon-like seed pods that contain numerous small, hairy seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
