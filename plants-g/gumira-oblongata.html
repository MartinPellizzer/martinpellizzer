Gumira oblongata

Gumira oblongata, commonly known as the longleaf gumira, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, oblong leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. Gumira oblongata thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
