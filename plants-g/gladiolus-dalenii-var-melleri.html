Gladiolus dalenii var. melleri

Gladiolus dalenii var. melleri, commonly known as the Meller's gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-like leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding a dramatic flair to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature contribute to its popularity in horticultural collections.
