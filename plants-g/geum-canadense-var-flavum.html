Geum canadense var. flavum

Geum canadense var. flavum, commonly known as the yellow avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system that aids in its spread and survival.
