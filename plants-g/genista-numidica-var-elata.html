Genista numidica var. elata

Genista numidica var. elata, commonly known as the tall genista, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has pinnate leaves with small, rounded leaflets, and it produces indehiscent pods that contain seeds. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
