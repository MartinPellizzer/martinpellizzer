Gasteria obliqua

Gasteria obliqua, commonly known as the zigzag gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the edges. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Gasteria obliqua is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
