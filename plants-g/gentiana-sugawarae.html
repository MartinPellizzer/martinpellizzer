Gentiana sugawarae

Gentiana sugawarae, commonly known as the Sugawara gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and streamside areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and often branched.

Gentiana sugawarae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
