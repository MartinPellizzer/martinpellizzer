Gavilea Kingii

Gavilea kingii is a rare and striking plant native to the coastal regions of South Australia. It is known for its vibrant pink and white flowers that bloom in late winter and early spring. This species thrives in sandy soils and requires well-drained conditions to flourish. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered vulnerable and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this unique and beautiful plant.
