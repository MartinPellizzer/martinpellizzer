Goodyera makuensis

Goodyera makuensis, commonly known as the Maku's lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its intricate, spirally arranged flowers. The plant typically features dark green, ovate leaves with a leathery texture. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks.

Goodyera makuensis thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
