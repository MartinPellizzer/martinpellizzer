Gladiolus aequinoctialis var. divina

Gladiolus aequinoctialis var. divina, commonly known as the Divina Gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear large, showy flowers in a range of vibrant colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces large, sword-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its blooming season typically spans from late spring to early autumn, adding a dramatic touch to landscapes.
