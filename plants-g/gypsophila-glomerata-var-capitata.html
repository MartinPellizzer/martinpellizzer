Gypsophila glomerata var. capitata

Gypsophila glomerata var. capitata, commonly known as baby's breath, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular inflorescence composed of numerous small, white or pink flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. It is often used in floral arrangements due to its delicate appearance and long-lasting blooms.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
