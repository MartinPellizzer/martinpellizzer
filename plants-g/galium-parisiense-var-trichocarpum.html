Galium parisiense var. trichocarpum

Galium parisiense var. trichocarpum, commonly known as the hairy woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. This variant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
