Gardenia vogelii var. seretii

Gardenia vogelii var. seretii, commonly known as the Seretii gardenia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces yellow berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and pleasant scent make it a desirable addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
