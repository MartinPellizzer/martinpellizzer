Gonialoe sladeniana

Gonialoe sladeniana, commonly known as the Sladen's Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage composed of thick, fleshy leaves. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
