Genista tyrrhena subsp. pontiana

Genista tyrrhena subsp.

pontiana, commonly known as the Pontic genista, is a flowering plant native to the Balkans and parts of Anatolia. It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, often found in mountainous regions.
