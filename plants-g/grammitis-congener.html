Grammitis congener

Grammitis congener, commonly known as the rock fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate fronds.

Grammitis congener is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
