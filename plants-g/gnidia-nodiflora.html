Gnidia nodiflora

Gnidia nodiflora, commonly known as the clustered gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a rough, hairy texture.

Gnidia nodiflora is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
