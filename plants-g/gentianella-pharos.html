Gentianella pharos

Gentianella pharos, commonly known as the Pharos gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their showy blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers are usually small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and bloom in late summer to early autumn.
