Gesneria refulgens

Gesneria refulgens, commonly known as the glittering gesneria, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant features dark green, velvety leaves that are often adorned with silver or white markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria refulgens is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
