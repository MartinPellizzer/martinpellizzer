Gerbera ambigua

Gerbera ambigua, commonly known as the ambiguous gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with bright, colorful petals that range from yellow to red. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Gerbera ambigua is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
