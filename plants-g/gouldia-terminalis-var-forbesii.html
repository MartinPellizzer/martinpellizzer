Gouldia terminalis var. forbesii

Gouldia terminalis var. forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grasses and sedge-like plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful inflorescences that range from red to orange. Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, with a striking contrast to the colorful flower heads.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
