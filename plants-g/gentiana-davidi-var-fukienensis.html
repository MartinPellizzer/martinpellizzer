Gentiana davidi var. fukienensis

Gentiana davidi var. fukienensis, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
