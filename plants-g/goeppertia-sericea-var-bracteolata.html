Goeppertia sericea var. bracteolata

Goeppertia sericea var. bracteolata, commonly known as the silver stripe calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-white midrib and green margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are often adorned with subtle variegation and have a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
