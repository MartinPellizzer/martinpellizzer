Goodenia cylindrocarpa

Goodenia cylindrocarpa, commonly known as the cylindrical goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid and semi-arid regions. Its flowers are usually pale blue to purple in color and are arranged in dense spikes.

Goodenia cylindrocarpa is often found in open woodlands and shrublands, where it thrives in full sun.
