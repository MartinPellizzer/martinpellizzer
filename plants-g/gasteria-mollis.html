Gasteria mollis

Gasteria mollis, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which appear on tall, slender stems. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Gasteria mollis is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and unique appearance.
