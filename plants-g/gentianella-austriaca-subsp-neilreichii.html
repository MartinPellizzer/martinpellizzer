Gentianella austriaca subsp. neilreichii

Gentianella austriaca subsp.

neilreichii, commonly known as the Austrian gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Alps. It typically grows in moist, calcareous grasslands and mountain meadows.

The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are basal, lanceolate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
