Gentiana gracilenta

Gentiana gracilenta, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are slender and often hairy.

Gentiana gracilenta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
