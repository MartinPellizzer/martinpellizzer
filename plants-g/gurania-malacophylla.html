Gurania malacophylla

Gurania malacophylla, commonly known as the velvetleaf gurania, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
