Gentianella kusnezowii

Gentianella kusnezowii, commonly known as the Kusnezow's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
