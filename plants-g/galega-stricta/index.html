Galega Stricta

Galega stricta is a hardy perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its distinctive spiny stems and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. This plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions has made it a subject of interest in ecological studies. Despite its tough appearance, Galega stricta plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and soil stabilization.
