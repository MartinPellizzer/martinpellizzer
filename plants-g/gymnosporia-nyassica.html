Gymnosporia nyassica

Gymnosporia nyassica, commonly known as the African spurge, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Gymnosporia nyassica is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
