Geniostoma rarotongense

Geniostoma rarotongense, commonly known as the Cook Islands orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the Cook Islands.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and is adapted to grow in humid, shaded environments.

Its conservation status is threatened due to habitat loss and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanical research and conservation efforts.
