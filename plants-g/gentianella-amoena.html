Gentianella amoena

Gentianella amoena, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in racemes.

Gentianella amoena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
