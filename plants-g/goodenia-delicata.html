Goodenia delicata

Goodenia delicata, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers, which are usually pale blue or purple, bloom in spring and attract various pollinators.

Goodenia delicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
