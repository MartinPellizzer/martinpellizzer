Grevillea cuneata

Grevillea cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters, which bloom from spring to summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea cuneata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
