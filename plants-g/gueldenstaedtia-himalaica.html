Gueldenstaedtia himalaica

Gueldenstaedtia himalaica, commonly known as the Himalayan gueldenstaedtia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South Asia, including the Himalayas. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that twine around supports. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval to lance-shaped leaflets.

The flowers are small, pea-like, and typically pink or purple in color, appearing in clusters along the stems.
