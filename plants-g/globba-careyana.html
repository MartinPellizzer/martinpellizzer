Globba careyana

Globba careyana, commonly known as the red ginger, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features erect, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in terminal inflorescences. Globba careyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a strong, spicy aroma.
