Goodenia krauseana

Goodenia krauseana, commonly known as the Krause's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, leaf structure. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in shrublands and open forests.

Goodenia krauseana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
