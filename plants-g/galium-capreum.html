Galium capreum

Galium capreum, commonly known as lesser bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Galium capreum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
