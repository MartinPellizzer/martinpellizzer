Gonystylus spectabilis

Gonystylus spectabilis, commonly known as the splendid ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has large, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its slow growth and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
