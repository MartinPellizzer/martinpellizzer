Gardenia crameri

Gardenia crameri, commonly known as the Cramer's gardenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, waxy flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant produces fragrant blooms that are highly valued in horticulture for their beauty and scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia crameri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
