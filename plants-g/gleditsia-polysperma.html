Gleditsia polysperma

Gleditsia polysperma, commonly known as the Chinese honeylocust, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that are often feathery and turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, green pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is adapted to a variety of soil types and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Its growth habit includes a straight trunk and a rounded crown, making it a popular ornamental plant.
