Garcinia xishuanbannaensis

Garcinia xishuanbannaensis, commonly known as the Xishuanbanna Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy fruits that are rich in bioactive compounds, including hydroxycitric acid. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique chemical composition, it has attracted interest for potential medicinal and nutritional applications.
