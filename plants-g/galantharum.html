Galantharum

Galantharum, commonly known as the snowdrop, is a genus of flowering plants in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant features strap-like leaves and is known for its ability to thrive in woodland and shady environments. Galantharum species are native to Europe, western Asia, and parts of North Africa.
