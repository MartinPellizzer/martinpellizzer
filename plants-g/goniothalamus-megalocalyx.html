Goniothalamus megalocalyx

Goniothalamus megalocalyx, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces woody, spherical fruits that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and for landscaping purposes.
