Gynura lycopersicifolia

Gynura lycopersicifolia, commonly known as the tomato-leaf gynura, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that resemble those of the tomato plant, hence its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in humid environments with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value is often appreciated in gardens due to its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
