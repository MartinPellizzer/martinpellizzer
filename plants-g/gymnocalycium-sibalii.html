Gymnocalycium sibalii

Gymnocalycium sibalii, commonly known as the Sibalii moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically covered in white or pale green spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are often borne on short pedicels. Gymnocalycium sibalii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
