Gardenia subacaulis

Gardenia subacaulis, commonly known as the dwarf gardenia, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a subtle fragrance. The plant produces small, white, waxy flowers with a strong, sweet scent, typically blooming in summer. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for container gardening.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
