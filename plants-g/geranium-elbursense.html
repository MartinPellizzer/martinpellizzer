Geranium elbursense

Geranium elbursense, commonly known as the Elburz geranium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and attractive, palmate leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
