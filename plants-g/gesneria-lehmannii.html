Gesneria lehmannii

Gesneria lehmannii, commonly known as the Lehmann's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy texture.

Gesneria lehmannii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among collectors for its ornamental value.
