Gymnandra reniformis

Gymnandra reniformis, commonly known as the kidneyleaf plant, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features distinctive kidney-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Gymnandra reniformis is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often used in rock gardens due to its drought tolerance and ornamental foliage.
