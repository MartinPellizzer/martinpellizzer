Garcinia ngouniensis

Garcinia ngouniensis, commonly known as the Ngouni orange.

This plant is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa. It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and bright orange, fleshy fruits. The fruit contains a single seed surrounded by a thick, aromatic pulp.

Garcinia ngouniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
