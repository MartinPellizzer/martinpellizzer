Garcinia loniceroides

Garcinia loniceroides, commonly known as the red-berried garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy berries that are rich in bioactive compounds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Garcinia loniceroides is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
