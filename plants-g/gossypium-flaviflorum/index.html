Gossypium Flaviflorum

Gossypium flaviflorum is a rare and intriguing species of cotton native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. Known for its unique yellow flowers and fibrous capsules, this plant has captured the interest of botanists and horticulturists alike. Despite its potential for agricultural and medicinal use, it remains under-studied and largely overlooked in modern cultivation. Its delicate appearance contrasts sharply with the robust nature of more common cotton varieties. Understanding Gossypium flaviflorum may offer new insights into the evolution and diversity of the cotton genus.
