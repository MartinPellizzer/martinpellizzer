Goeppertia reflectens

Goeppertia reflectens, commonly known as the silver arrow plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a waxy, silver-white coating, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Goeppertia reflectens is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its air-purifying qualities and aesthetic appeal.
