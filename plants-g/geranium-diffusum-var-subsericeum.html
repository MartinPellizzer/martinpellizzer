Geranium diffusum var. subsericeum

Geranium diffusum var. subsericeum, commonly known as the velvetleaf geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features spreading stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats or low shrubs. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture and a bluish-green color. Its flowers are pink to red, with five petals and a prominent white throat, blooming in summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and blooms.
