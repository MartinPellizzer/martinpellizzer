Gothofreda lanata

Gothofreda lanata, commonly known as the hairy gothofreda, is a unique species characterized by its dense, woolly foliage.

This plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in its native habitat. The flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of pale pink or white.

Gothofreda lanata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
