Grubbia rosmarinifolia var. pinifolia

Grubbia rosmarinifolia var. pinifolia, commonly known as the pineleaf grubbia, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a compact, shrub-like growth form with dense, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern and are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
