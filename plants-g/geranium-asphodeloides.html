Geranium asphodeloides

Geranium asphodeloides, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Geranium asphodeloides is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
