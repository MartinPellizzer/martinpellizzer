Gothofreda maritima

Gothofreda maritima, commonly known as the coastal ghostflower, is a rare and unique plant species found in specific coastal regions.

It thrives in saline and sandy environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh maritime conditions. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its pale, almost translucent leaves and small, delicate flowers. It plays a crucial role in stabilizing dune ecosystems and preventing erosion.

Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered an endangered species.
