Gasteria schweickerdtiana

Gasteria schweickerdtiana, commonly known as the dwarf gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Gasteria schweickerdtiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
