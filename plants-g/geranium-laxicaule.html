Geranium laxicaule

Geranium laxicaule, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and have a hairy texture. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, borne in clusters at the ends of the stems.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
