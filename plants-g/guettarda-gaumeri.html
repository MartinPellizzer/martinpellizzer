Guettarda gaumeri

Guettarda gaumeri, commonly known as the Mexican flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central America and northern South America.

Guettarda gaumeri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
