Gladiolus massonii

Gladiolus massonii, commonly known as the Masson's gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, colorful flower spikes that can reach up to 1 meter in height. The flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. This species is well-suited for gardens and floral arrangements due to its vibrant blooms and upright growth habit.

It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for both amateur and professional gardeners.
