Gentiana amoena f. pallida

Gentiana amoena f. pallida, commonly known as the pale gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to sky-blue flowers, which are typically tubular with a prominent white or pale blue throat. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and thrives in cool, moist environments. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette.

The pale gentian is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance.
