Gardenia fortunei

Gardenia fortunei, commonly known as the Chinese gardenia, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is evergreen and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

The fruit it produces is a bright orange berry that contains several seeds.
