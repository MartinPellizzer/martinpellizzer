Galium rubia

Galium rubia, commonly known as red bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are followed by small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Galium rubia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
