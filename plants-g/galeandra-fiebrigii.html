Galeandra fiebrigii

Galeandra fiebrigii, commonly known as the Fiebrig's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its flowers are borne in clusters and are pollinated by insects, particularly bees and wasps.

Galeandra fiebrigii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
