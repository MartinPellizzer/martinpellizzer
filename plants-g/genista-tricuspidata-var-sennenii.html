Genista tricuspidata var. sennenii

Genista tricuspidata var. sennenii, commonly known as the Japanese broom or Sennenii broom, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. It produces clusters of yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
