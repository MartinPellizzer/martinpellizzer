Galactia striata var. tobagensis

Galactia striata var. tobagensis, commonly known as the Toba bean, is a tropical legume native to South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, slender pods that contain several small, round seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
