Gleichenia laevigata

Gleichenia laevigata, commonly known as the smooth Gleichenia, is a fern that belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and often segmented fronds that are typically arching or creeping in growth habit. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its dark green, glossy leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or on rocky surfaces.

Gleichenia laevigata is native to various regions in the Americas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
