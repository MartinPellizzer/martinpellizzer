Gardenia macrocarpa

Gardenia macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces large, orange-yellow berries that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia macrocarpa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic blooms.
