Grevillea annulifera

Grevillea annulifera, commonly known as the ringed grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking red and yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Grevillea annulifera is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
