Galarhoeus micranthus

Galarhoeus micranthus, commonly known as the small-flowered galarhoeus, is a herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Galarhoeus micranthus is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
