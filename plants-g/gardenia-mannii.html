Gardenia mannii

Gardenia mannii, commonly known as the African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant, white to yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, black, spherical berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil.

Gardenia mannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
