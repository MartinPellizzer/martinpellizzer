Gasteria herreana

Gasteria herreana, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow markings. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Gasteria herreana is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
