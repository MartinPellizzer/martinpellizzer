Gardenia jardinei

Gardenia jardinei, commonly known as the African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are highly prized for their intense, sweet scent and are often used in perfumery.

Gardenia jardinei thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
