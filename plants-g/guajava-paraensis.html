It is native to the Amazon rainforest, particularly in regions of Brazil, Colombia, and Venezuela. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to yellow when ripe.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
