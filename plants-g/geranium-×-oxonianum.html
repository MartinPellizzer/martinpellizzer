Geranium × oxonianum

Geranium × oxonianum, commonly known as the oxonianum geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically rounded, serrated, and arranged oppositely along the stems. This plant is native to Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile choice for gardeners.
