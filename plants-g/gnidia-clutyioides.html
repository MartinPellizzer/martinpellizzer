Gnidia clutyioides

Gnidia clutyioides, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The wood of Gnidia clutyioides is dense and hard, making it valuable for construction and fuel.
