Geranium scullyi

Geranium scullyi, commonly known as Scully's geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid environments. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces clusters of showy, pink to violet flowers. It is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Geranium scullyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
