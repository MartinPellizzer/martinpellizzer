Grewia velutinissima

Grewia velutinissima, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Grewia velutinissima is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
