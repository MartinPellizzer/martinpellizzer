Genista spartioides var. pomariensis

Genista spartioides var. pomariensis, commonly known as the Pomarion broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal and Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in arid environments.
