Gentiana rhaetica

Gentiana rhaetica, commonly known as the Rhaetian gentian, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Alps.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

It blooms from June to August, making it a notable species in alpine flora.
