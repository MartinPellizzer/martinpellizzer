Glyptostrobus columnaris

Glyptostrobus columnaris, commonly known as the Chinese cliff bamboo, is a unique plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, columnar stems that grow in dense clusters, forming a bamboo-like appearance. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a compact rosette. Glyptostrobus columnaris is native to China and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant, upright growth habit.
