Gnaphalium densum

Gnaphalium densum, commonly known as the dense bluemink, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, blue to purple florets arranged in dense clusters, giving it a soft, fluffy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats.

Gnaphalium densum is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
