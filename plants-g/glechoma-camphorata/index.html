Glechoma Camphorata

Glechoma camphorata is a low-growing perennial plant known for its fragrant leaves and vibrant flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in woodland areas across Europe. This plant belongs to the mint family and is often used in traditional herbal remedies. Its camphor-like scent makes it a popular choice for gardens and medicinal purposes. Despite its modest appearance, Glechoma camphorata plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and ecosystems.
