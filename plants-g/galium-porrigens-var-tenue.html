Galium porrigens var. tenue

Galium porrigens var. tenue, commonly known as slender bedstraw, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its slender stems and delicate appearance make it a characteristic species of its habitat.
