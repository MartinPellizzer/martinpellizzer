Grammitis ctenoidea

Grammitis ctenoidea, commonly known as the feather moss, is a type of liverwort belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, carpet-like growth habit and small, closely packed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Grammitis ctenoidea plays an important ecological role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its habitat.
