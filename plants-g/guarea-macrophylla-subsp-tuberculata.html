Guarea macrophylla subsp. tuberculata

Guarea macrophylla subsp.

tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate guarea, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America. It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood and medicinal properties.

The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and distinctive, tuberculate (knobbed) bark. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.
