Gynura meyeri-johannis, commonly known as the Meyer's gynura, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including parts of India and Indonesia. The plant features heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months.
