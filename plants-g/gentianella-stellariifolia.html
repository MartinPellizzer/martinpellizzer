Gentianella stellariifolia

Gentianella stellariifolia, commonly known as starleaf gentian, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its small, clustered blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has basal leaves that are lobed and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
