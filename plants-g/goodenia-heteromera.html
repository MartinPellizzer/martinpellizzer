Goodenia heteromera

Goodenia heteromera, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. It has a compact growth form and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia heteromera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
