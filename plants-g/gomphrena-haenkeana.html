Gomphrena haenkeana

Gomphrena haenkeana, commonly known as the Mexican feather grass, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its feathery, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from pink to red. The plant is herbaceous, with slender, branching stems and small, scale-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Gomphrena haenkeana is often used in dried floral arrangements due to its long-lasting, decorative blooms.
