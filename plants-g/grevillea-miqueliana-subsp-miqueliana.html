Grevillea miqueliana subsp. miqueliana

Grevillea miqueliana subsp.

miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia, specifically the southwestern region. It is characterized by its distinctive, feather-like foliage and vibrant, red-orange flowers that appear in cylindrical clusters.

The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments. Its ornamental value is high, and it is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.

The subspecies is notable for its adaptability and resilience to drought conditions.
