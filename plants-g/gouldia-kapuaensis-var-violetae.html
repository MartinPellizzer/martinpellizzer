Gouldia kapuaensis var. violetae

Gouldia kapuaensis var. violetae, commonly known as the violet Gouldia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of the Pacific.

It is characterized by its vibrant violet and purple foliage, which gives it a unique and eye-catching appearance. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small garden spaces or container planting. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light.
