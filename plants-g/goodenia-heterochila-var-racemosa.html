Goodenia heterochila var. racemosa

Goodenia heterochila var. racemosa, commonly known as the racemose goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, racemose inflorescence composed of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, while the flowers range in color from pink to purple, attracting various pollinators.

This variant is notable for its adaptability to arid conditions and its role in local ecosystems.
