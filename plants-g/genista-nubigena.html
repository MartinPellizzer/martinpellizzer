Genista nubigena

Genista nubigena, commonly known as the cloud genista, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by narrow, linear pods. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
