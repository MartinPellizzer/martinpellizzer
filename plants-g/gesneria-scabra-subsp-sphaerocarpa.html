Gesneria scabra subsp. sphaerocarpa

Gesneria scabra subsp.

sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the rough gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America. It features glossy, dark green leaves that are covered in fine, short hairs, giving them a rough texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of tropical plants.
