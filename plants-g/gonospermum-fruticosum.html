Gonospermum fruticosum

Gonospermum fruticosum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its prostrate growth habit and hairy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically visited by various pollinators. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that contain several seeds.

Gonospermum fruticosum is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
