Grosourdya ciliata

Grosourdya ciliata, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat. The plant produces bright red, papery fruits that resemble small lanterns, which are a key feature in its common name. Grosourdya ciliata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its unique appearance and seasonal display make it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
