Gentiana anisodonta var. glabrescens

Gentiana anisodonta var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs, giving it a slightly rough texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and alpine settings.
