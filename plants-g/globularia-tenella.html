Globularia tenella

Globularia tenella, commonly known as the silver globularia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, spherical clusters of small, silvery-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Globularia tenella is valued for its ornamental appearance and drought tolerance.
