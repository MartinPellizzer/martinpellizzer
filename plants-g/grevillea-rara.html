Grevillea rara

Grevillea rara, commonly known as the rare grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, crimson-red floral spikes that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a distinctive blue-green hue.

Grevillea rara is well-suited to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
