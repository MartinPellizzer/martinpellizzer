Gymnocalycium prochazkanum subsp. ivoi

Gymnocalycium prochazkanum subsp.

ivoi, commonly known as the Ivo's moon cactus, is a succulent cactus native to the arid regions of northern Argentina. It is characterized by its globular, spiny stems that are typically green or blue-green in color.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and indoor gardening.
