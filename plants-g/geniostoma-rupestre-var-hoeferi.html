Geniostoma rupestre var. hoeferi

Geniostoma rupestre var. hoeferi, commonly known as the rock spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or rocky environments.

Its adaptability and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
