Geissorhiza sufflava

Geissorhiza sufflava, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped appearance, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, producing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers.

Geissorhiza sufflava is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
