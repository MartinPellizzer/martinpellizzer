Grammitis huancabambensis

Grammitis huancabambensis, commonly known as the Huancabamba fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green and glossy. This plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in Peru. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests or along stream banks.

The fronds are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
