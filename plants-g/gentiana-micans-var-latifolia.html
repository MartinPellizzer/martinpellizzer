Gentiana micans var. latifolia

Gentiana micans var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
