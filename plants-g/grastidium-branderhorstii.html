Grastidium branderhorstii

Grastidium branderhorstii, commonly known as the Brandenburg grass.

It is a species of grass native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, slender blades. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna ecosystems.

Grastidium branderhorstii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
