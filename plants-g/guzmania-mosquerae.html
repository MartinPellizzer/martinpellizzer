Guzmania mosquerae

Guzmania mosquerae, commonly known as the Mosquera guzmania, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia. The plant features a rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater. Its inflorescence consists of a vibrant red bract with yellow flowers, adding striking visual contrast.

Guzmania mosquerae is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
