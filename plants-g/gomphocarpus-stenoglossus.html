Gomphocarpus stenoglossus

Gomphocarpus stenoglossus, commonly known as the narrow-lipped milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves that are usually oval in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species.

The plant contains latex-like sap, which can be toxic to some animals and humans.
