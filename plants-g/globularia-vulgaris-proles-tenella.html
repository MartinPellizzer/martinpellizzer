Globularia vulgaris proles tenella

Globularia vulgaris proles tenella, commonly known as the small globularia, is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, white or pink flowers.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. Its compact growth habit and attractive flowers make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
