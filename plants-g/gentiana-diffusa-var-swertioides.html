Gentiana diffusa var. swertioides

Gentiana diffusa var. swertioides, commonly known as the Tibetan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and upright.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
