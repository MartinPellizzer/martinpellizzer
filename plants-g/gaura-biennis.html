Gaura biennis

Gaura biennis, commonly known as whorled gaura, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, branching stems that produce clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Gaura biennis is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
