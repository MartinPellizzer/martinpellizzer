Geissois intermedia

Geissois intermedia, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that turn yellow in the fall, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Geissois intermedia is also known for its rapid growth and adaptability to urban environments.
