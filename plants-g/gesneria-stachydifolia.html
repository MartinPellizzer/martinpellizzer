Gesneria stachydifolia

Gesneria stachydifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf gesneria, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria stachydifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
