Grevillea bundera

Grevillea bundera, commonly known as the Bundera grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features striking, feather-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or silver tinge. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea bundera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens and landscaping.
