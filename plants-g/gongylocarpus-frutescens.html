Gongylocarpus frutescens

Gongylocarpus frutescens, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa, characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, violet or purple flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges, and it has some medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
