Geranium pusillum var. malvifolium

Geranium pusillum var. malvifolium, commonly known as the Malvifolium geranium, is a compact, flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically velvety and have a distinctive, slightly heart-shaped form. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and unique foliage structure.
