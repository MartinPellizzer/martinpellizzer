Galarhoeus pekinensis var. sinensis

Galarhoeus pekinensis var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces fragrant blooms in spring. It is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
