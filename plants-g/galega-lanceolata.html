Galega lanceolata

Galega lanceolata, commonly known as the narrowleaf goat's rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are alternate and often hairy, with a distinctive blue or violet coloration. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various arid environments.

Galega lanceolata is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
