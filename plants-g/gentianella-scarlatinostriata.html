Gentianella scarlatinostriata

Gentianella scarlatinostriata, commonly known as the scarlet-striped gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with distinctive red stripes on the petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentianella scarlatinostriata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
