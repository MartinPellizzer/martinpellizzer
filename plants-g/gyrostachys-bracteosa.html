Gyrostachys bracteosa

Gyrostachys bracteosa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces showy, daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disc of small, yellow florets. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight waxy coating.

Gyrostachys bracteosa is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun.
