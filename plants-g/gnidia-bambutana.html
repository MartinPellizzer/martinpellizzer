Gnidia bambutana

Gnidia bambutana, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its hard, heavy wood that is resistant to decay. Gnidia bambutana is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its durable wood in construction and tool-making.
