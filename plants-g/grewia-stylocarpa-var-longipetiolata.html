Grewia stylocarpa var. longipetiolata

Grewia stylocarpa var. longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled sour fig, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated petioles that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature and is edible.
