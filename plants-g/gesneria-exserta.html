Gesneria exserta

Gesneria exserta, commonly known as the Crimson Beauty, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround small, tubular flowers, which are typically pale yellow or white. The plant has broad, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a silvery underside, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Gesneria exserta is prized for its unique appearance and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
