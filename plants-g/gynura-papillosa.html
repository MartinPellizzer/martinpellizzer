Gynura papillosa

Gynura papillosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silky lettuce, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in moist, shady environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
