Gerbera ovalis

Gerbera ovalis, commonly known as the oval gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like blooms. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Gerbera ovalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
