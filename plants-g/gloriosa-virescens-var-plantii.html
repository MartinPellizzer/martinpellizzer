Gloriosa virescens var. plantii

Gloriosa virescens var. plantii, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like orange and red flowers. The plant features strap-shaped, dark green leaves and produces long, pendulous stems that bear the showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its dramatic floral display and is relatively easy to care for with proper conditions.
