Grevillea didymobotrya subsp. didymobotrya

Grevillea didymobotrya subsp.

didymobotrya, commonly known as the red-stemmed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically grey-green in color and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in dense, cylindrical spikes, which bloom throughout the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
