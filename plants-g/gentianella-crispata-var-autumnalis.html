Gentianella crispata var. autumnalis

Gentianella crispata var. autumnalis, commonly known as autumn gentian, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly crinkled appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
