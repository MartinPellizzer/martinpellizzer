Gardenia augusta var. grandiflora

Gardenia augusta var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elliptical, yellowish-orange fruits that contain multiple seeds. It prefers warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
