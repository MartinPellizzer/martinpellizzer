Gleichenia alstonii

Gleichenia alstonii, commonly known as the alston's fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, creeping rhizome system that produces a thick, mat-like growth. The fronds are usually dark green, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, fan-shaped structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and its ability to colonize rocky or disturbed soils.
