Geranium Caespitosum Var. Fremontii

Geranium caespitosum var. fremontii is a unique and resilient plant native to the western united states. it thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. this variant of geranium is known for its compact growth habit and vibrant flower colors. it is often found in desert regions, where it plays an important role in local ecosystems. its beauty and hardiness make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
