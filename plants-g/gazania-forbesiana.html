Gazania forbesiana

Gazania forbesiana, commonly known as the Forbes' gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and produces showy blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.

Gazania forbesiana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
