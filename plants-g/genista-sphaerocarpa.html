Genista sphaerocarpa

Genista sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe buckthorn, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that are typically red or black when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Genista sphaerocarpa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
