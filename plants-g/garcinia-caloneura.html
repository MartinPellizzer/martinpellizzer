Garcinia caloneura

Garcinia caloneura, commonly known as the slender Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a yellow or orange berry that contains a single seed.

Garcinia caloneura is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses.
