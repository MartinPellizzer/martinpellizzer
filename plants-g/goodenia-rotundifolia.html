Goodenia rotundifolia

Goodenia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, rounded leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in spring and attract various pollinators.

Goodenia rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
