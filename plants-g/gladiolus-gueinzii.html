Gladiolus gueinzii

Gladiolus gueinzii, commonly known as the Gueinzii gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its erect growth habit and summer blooming cycle. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spike and are popular in gardens for their elegance and longevity.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a favored choice for both floral arrangements and landscape design.
