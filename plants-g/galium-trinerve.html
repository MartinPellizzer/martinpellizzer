Galium trinerve

Galium trinerve, commonly known as slender bedstraw, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, ascending stems that are often hairy and can branch near the base. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Galium trinerve thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
