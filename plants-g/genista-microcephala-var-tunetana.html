Genista microcephala var. tunetana

Genista microcephala var. tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian dwarf broom, is a low-growing shrub native to North Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves that are adapted to arid conditions. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
