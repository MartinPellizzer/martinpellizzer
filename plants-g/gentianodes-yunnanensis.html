Gentianodes yunnanensis

Gentianodes yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their showy blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes, often in forest understories. Its flowers are characterized by their deep blue color and five petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic and ecological significance.
