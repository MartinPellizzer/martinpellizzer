Gibasis Linearis

Gibasis linearis is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family known for its slender, linear leaves and vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-draining soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both gardeners and botanists alike. Easy to care for, it requires minimal water and can flourish in containers or in the ground. With its elegant form and colorful blooms, Gibasis linearis adds a touch of exotic beauty to any landscape.
