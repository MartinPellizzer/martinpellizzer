Grevillea latrobei

Grevillea latrobei, commonly known as the red-flowered grevillea, is a shrub native to the southeastern coast of Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Grevillea latrobei is also valued for its ability to attract native birds and insects.
