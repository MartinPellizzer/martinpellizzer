Gentianella stellarioides var. androtricha

Gentianella stellarioides var. androtricha, commonly known as the star-like gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
