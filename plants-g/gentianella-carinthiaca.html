Gentianella carinthiaca

Gentianella carinthiaca, commonly known as Carinthian gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

Gentianella carinthiaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in mountain ecosystems.
