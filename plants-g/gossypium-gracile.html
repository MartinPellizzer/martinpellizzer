Gossypium gracile

Gossypium gracile, commonly known as the slender cotton plant, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant produces small, fluffy fibers that are harvested for textile use. It typically grows as an annual herb with hairy, branched stems.

Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, and it thrives in well-drained soils with ample sunlight.
