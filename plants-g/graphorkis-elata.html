Graphorkis elata

Graphorkis elata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant produces a single, slender stem that bears a few showy, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the poor soil conditions.

It is a perennial species that blooms during the cooler months, showcasing its unique botanical features.
