Gardoquia breviflora

Gardoquia breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered gardoquia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pale blue or violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact growth habit.

Gardoquia breviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in alpine gardens.
