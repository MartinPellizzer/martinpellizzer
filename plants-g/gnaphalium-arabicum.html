Gnaphalium arabicum

Gnaphalium arabicum, commonly known as the Arabian cudweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom during the summer months. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs.

Gnaphalium arabicum is often found growing in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand dry conditions.
