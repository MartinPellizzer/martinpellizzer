Gardenia densa

Gardenia densa, commonly known as the dense gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia densa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic blossoms.
