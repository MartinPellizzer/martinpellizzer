Geranium monstrum

Geranium monstrum, commonly known as the giant geranium, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple, often with a distinctive white or yellow eye. The plant has robust, upright stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and dark green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and long blooming period make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
