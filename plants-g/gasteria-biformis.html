Gasteria biformis

Gasteria biformis, commonly known as the two-lobed gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellowish markings. The leaves are typically narrow and pointed, with a distinctive two-lobed shape at the tips. It produces small, pale green flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
