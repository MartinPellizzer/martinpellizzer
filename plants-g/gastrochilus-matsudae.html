Gastrochilus matsudae

Gastrochilus matsudae, commonly known as the Matsudae's gastrochilus, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and possesses a single, short rhizome. The plant features lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a loose inflorescence.

Gastrochilus matsudae is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.
