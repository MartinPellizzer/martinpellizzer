Gesneria oblongata

Gesneria oblongata, commonly known as the broadleaf gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy texture and a dark green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, depending on the variety.
