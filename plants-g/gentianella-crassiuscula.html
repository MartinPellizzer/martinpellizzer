Gentianella crassiuscula

Gentianella crassiuscula, commonly known as the thick-leaved gentian, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which includes many flowering plants with showy blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to retain water in its native habitat.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a striking blue hue to the landscape.
