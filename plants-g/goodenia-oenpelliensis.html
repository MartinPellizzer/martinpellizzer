Goodenia oenpelliensis

Goodenia oenpelliensis, commonly known as the Murrumbidgee Goodenia, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
