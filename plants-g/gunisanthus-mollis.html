Gunisanthus mollis

Gunisanthus mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a soft, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and are clustered in terminal inflorescences.
