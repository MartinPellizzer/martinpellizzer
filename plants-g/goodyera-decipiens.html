Goodyera decipiens

It is a small, herbaceous plant with a rosette of lance-shaped leaves that form a dense, compact structure. The plant produces a tall, slender flowering stem bearing a dense spike of small, greenish-white flowers that are often mistaken for moss due to their appearance.
