Goodenia elderi

Goodenia elderi, commonly known as the Elder's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has small, narrow leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia elderi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
