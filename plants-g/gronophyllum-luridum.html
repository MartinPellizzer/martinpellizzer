Gronophyllum luridum

Gronophyllum luridum, commonly known as the yellow grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal and dry inland regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
