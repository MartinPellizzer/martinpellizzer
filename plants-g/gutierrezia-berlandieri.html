Gutierrezia berlandieri

Gutierrezia berlandieri, commonly known as desert gumweed, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and silvery-gray foliage, which help it conserve water in arid environments. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is adapted to thrive in dry, sandy soils. Its growth habit includes multiple stems and it often forms dense stands in disturbed areas.

Gutierrezia berlandieri plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
