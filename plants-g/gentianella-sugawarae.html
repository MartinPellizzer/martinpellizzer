Gentianella sugawarae

Gentianella sugawarae, commonly known as the Sugawara's gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
