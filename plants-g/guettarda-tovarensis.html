Guettarda tovarensis

Guettarda tovarensis, commonly known as the Tovarensis guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Caribbean.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in warm climates.

Guettarda tovarensis is also valued for its ability to thrive in sandy soils and its tolerance to salt spray.
