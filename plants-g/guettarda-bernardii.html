Guettarda bernardii

Guettarda bernardii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a vibrant and showy appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Guettarda bernardii is also valued for its fast growth rate and ability to tolerate various soil types.
