Griffonia pallescens var. arborescens

Griffonia pallescens var. arborescens, commonly known as the African velvet bean tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and compound leaves with elongated leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds. It is often cultivated for its seeds, which are rich in protein and used in local cuisines.

Additionally, it has been studied for its potential medicinal properties due to the presence of certain bioactive compounds.
