Gomidesia schaueriana var. spathulata

Gomidesia schaueriana var. spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved gomidesia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves with a prominent midrib and a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
