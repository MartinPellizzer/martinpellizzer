Guzmania lingulata var. splendens

Guzmania lingulata var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendens Guzmania, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect rainwater, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms. Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.
