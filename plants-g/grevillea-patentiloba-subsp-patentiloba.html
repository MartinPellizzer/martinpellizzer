Grevillea patentiloba subsp. patentiloba

Grevillea patentiloba subsp.

patentiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and vibrant red or orange flowers that appear in clusters.
