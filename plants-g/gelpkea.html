Gelpkea

Gelpkea, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species native to tropical regions.

It features broad, glossy leaves that exhibit a striking silvery or white underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a compact and bushy structure.

Gelpkea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
