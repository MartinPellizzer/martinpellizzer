Gymnosporia maliensis

Gymnosporia maliensis, commonly known as the Natal thornveld acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain seeds, and it is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
