Geranium ornithopodum var. lilacinum

Geranium ornithopodum var. lilacinum, commonly known as the blue geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
