Gardenia schwarzii

Gardenia schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwarz gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white to yellowish flowers. The plant is evergreen and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and pleasant scent.

Gardenia schwarzii requires partial shade and consistent moisture to flourish.
