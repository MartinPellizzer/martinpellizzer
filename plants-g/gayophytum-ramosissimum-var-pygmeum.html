Gayophytum ramosissimum var. pygmeum

Gayophytum ramosissimum var. pygmeum, commonly known as the dwarf flame plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and compact size, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts.
