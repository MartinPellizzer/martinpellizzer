Geraniospermum Bicolor

Geraniospermum bicolor is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking bi-color petals that range from deep red to pale yellow. This plant thrives in harsh environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to drought and extreme temperatures. Its distinctive appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, Geraniospermum bicolor remains poorly understood and is considered a conservation concern.
