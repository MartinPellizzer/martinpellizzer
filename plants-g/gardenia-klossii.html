Gardenia klossii

Gardenia klossii, commonly known as the Kloss gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Flores in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with a yellow center. The plant is evergreen and typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia klossii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
