Greenovia sedifolia

Greenovia sedifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance. Greenovia sedifolia is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
