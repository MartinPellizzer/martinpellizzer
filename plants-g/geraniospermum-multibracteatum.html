Geraniospermum multibracteatum

Geraniospermum multibracteatum, commonly known as the many-bract geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant produces numerous bracts, which are modified leaves that often resemble petals, giving it its common name. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
