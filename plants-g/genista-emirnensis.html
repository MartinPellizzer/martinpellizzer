Genista emirnensis

Genista emirnensis, commonly known as the Emirnensis Broom, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are usually compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista emirnensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
