Galatella × subvillosa

Galatella × subvillosa, commonly known as the silky galatella, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and showy, pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a velvety texture.

Galatella × subvillosa is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
