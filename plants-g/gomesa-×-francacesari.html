Gomesa × francacesari

Gomesa × francacesari, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Gomesa frutescens and Gomesa corymbosa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a rough texture and a waxy coating.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
