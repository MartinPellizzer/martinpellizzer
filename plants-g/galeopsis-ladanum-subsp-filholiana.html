Galeopsis ladanum subsp. filholiana

Galeopsis ladanum subsp.

filholiana, commonly known as narrowleaf hedge-nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants.

The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. Its flowers are clustered in whorls and are usually pale blue to purple in color.

It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.
