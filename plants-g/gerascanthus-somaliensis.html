Gerascanthus somaliensis

Gerascanthus somaliensis, commonly known as the Somali geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features upright stems with opposite, palmate leaves that are often hairy and serrated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Gerascanthus somaliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
