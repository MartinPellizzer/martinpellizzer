Graptopetalum grande

Graptopetalum grande, commonly known as the silver rose or Mexican silver plant, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a silvery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
