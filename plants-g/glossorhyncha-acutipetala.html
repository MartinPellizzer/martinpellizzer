Glossorhyncha acutipetala

Glossorhyncha acutipetala, commonly known as the sharp-petaled orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are elongated and arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its overall graceful appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
