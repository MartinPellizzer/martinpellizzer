Genista scoparia var. andreana

Genista scoparia var. andreana, commonly known as the Andreean broom, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume-like flowers and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
