Gorteria affinis

Gorteria affinis, commonly known as the common starburry, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette growth habit and produces showy, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Gorteria affinis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
