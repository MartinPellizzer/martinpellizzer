Gesneria proctorii

Gesneria proctorii, commonly known as the Proctor's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, specifically Brazil. This plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Gesneria proctorii is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
