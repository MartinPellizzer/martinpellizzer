Gorteria arachnoidea

Gorteria arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate inflorescence that resembles a spider's web, giving it its common name. Gorteria arachnoidea is a perennial herb that blooms during the summer months.
