Glossoma acuminatum

Glossoma acuminatum, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers.
