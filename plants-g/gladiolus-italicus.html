Gladiolus italicus

Gladiolus italicus, commonly known as the Italian gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of blooms, known as a spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
