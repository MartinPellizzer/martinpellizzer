Globba argentiana

Globba argentiana, commonly known as silver globba, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters atop tall, erect stalks. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.

Globba argentiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
