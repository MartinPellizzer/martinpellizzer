Garcinia fabrilis

Garcinia fabrilis, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers.
