Gasteria fasciata

Gasteria fasciata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or light green stripes. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with small, bell-shaped white flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria fasciata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
