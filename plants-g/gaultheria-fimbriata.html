Gaultheria fimbriata

Gaultheria fimbriata, commonly known as the red-flowered wintergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the temperate regions of South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina. Gaultheria fimbriata is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
