Gesneria longipes

Gesneria longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. It produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color.

Gesneria longipes is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
