Geranium acknerianum

Geranium acknerianum, commonly known as the African geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming from spring to autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
