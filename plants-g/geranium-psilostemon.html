Geranium psilostemon

Geranium psilostemon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Geranium psilostemon is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
