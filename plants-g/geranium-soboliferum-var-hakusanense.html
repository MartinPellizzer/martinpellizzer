Geranium soboliferum var. hakusanense

Geranium soboliferum var. hakusanense, commonly known as the Hakusan geranium, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features upright, hairy stems and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in forest understories and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
