Goodenia macroplectra

Goodenia macroplectra, commonly known as the large-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or heathland environments. Its flowers, which can be pink, red, or purple, bloom in spring and attract various pollinators.

Goodenia macroplectra is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
