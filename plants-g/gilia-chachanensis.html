Gilia chachanensis

Gilia chachanensis, commonly known as the Chachanensis gilia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

Gilia chachanensis plays a role in supporting local pollinators, particularly bees and butterflies, due to its nectar-rich flowers.
