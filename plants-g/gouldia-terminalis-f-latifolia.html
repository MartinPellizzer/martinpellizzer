Gouldia terminalis f. latifolia

Gouldia terminalis f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences that display a range of vibrant colors, from red to orange and yellow. The foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. This cultivar is prized for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for garden borders and container planting.
