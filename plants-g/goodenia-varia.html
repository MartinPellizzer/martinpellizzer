Goodenia varia

Goodenia varia, commonly known as the variable goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are clustered in spikes.

Goodenia varia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
