Gomesa venusta

Gomesa venusta, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks and branches in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Gomesa venusta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its unique appearance.
