Galium petrae

Galium petrae, commonly known as the rock bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to rocky and mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant features slender, ascending stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers are clustered in umbels, and it produces small, three-lobed berries.
