Genista cinerea

Genista cinerea, commonly known as the silver broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often silver- or grayish-hairy stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, triangular pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Genista cinerea is sometimes used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
