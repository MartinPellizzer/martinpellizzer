Goodenia corynocarpa var. macrocarpa

Goodenia corynocarpa var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces large, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by animals and wind.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.
