Gasteria acinacifolia

Gasteria acinacifolia, commonly known as the sword leaf gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of stiff, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria acinacifolia is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
