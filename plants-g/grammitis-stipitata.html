Grammitis stipitata

Grammitis stipitata, commonly known as the clustered spore moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rock crevices. Grammitis stipitata plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms in its native ecosystems.
