Garcinia fusca

Garcinia fusca, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, pale bark. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. Garcinia fusca is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

It has a slow growth rate and is valued for its ornamental appearance and potential medicinal properties.
