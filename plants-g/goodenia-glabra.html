Goodenia glabra

Goodenia glabra, commonly known as the smooth goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple and bloom in spring.

Goodenia glabra is adapted to dry environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
