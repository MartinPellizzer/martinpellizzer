Guillenia rigida

Guillenia rigida, commonly known as the rigid gullion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Guillenia rigida is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
