Globba obscura

Globba obscura, commonly known as the dark globba, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces dense clusters of small, dark purple to black flowers. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.
