Geranium odoratissimum

Geranium odoratissimum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are often clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Geranium odoratissimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
