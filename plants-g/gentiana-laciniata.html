Gentiana laciniata

Gentiana laciniata, commonly known as the slitleaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Gentiana laciniata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
