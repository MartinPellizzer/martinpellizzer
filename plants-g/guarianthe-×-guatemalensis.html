Guarianthe × guatemalensis

Guarianthe × guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan orchid, is a hybrid species native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features tall, erect stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and can range in color from white to pink or purple.

Guarianthe × guatemalensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
