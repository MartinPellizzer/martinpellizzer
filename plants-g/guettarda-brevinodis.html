Guettarda brevinodis

Guettarda brevinodis, commonly known as the dwarf guettarda, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are often glossy in appearance. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or limestone soils.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
