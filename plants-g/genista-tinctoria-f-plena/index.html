Genista Tinctoria F. Plena

Genista tinctoria f. plena is a striking variation of the common yellow broom plant known for its dense clusters of bright yellow flowers. This cultivar thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its compact growth habit and abundant blooms make it both ornamental and hardy. The plant is also valued for its historical use in dye production, adding to its cultural significance. With its vibrant color and resilience, Genista tinctoria f. plena is a standout addition to any garden.
