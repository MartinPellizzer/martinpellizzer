Gossia punctata

Gossia punctata, commonly known as the spotted gossia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

The species is characterized by its distinctive spotted or dotted appearance on its leaves and stems.
