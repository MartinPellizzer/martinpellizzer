Geranium × fragrans

Geranium × fragrans, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a fragrant scent. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 30 inches and features heart-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
