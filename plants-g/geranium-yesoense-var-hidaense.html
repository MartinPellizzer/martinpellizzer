Geranium yesoense var. hidaense

Geranium yesoense var. hidaense, commonly known as the Hida geranium, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces bright pink to red blooms during the summer months. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
