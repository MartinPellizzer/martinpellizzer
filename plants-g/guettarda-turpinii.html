Guettarda turpinii

Guettarda turpinii, commonly known as the turpentine shrub, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a waxy texture.

Guettarda turpinii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
