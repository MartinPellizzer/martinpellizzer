Goniothalamus gigantifolius

Goniothalamus gigantifolius, commonly known as the giant-leaf goniothalamus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that can reach lengths of up to 1 meter, making it a striking feature in its natural habitat. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with a unique structure, and its fruit is a woody capsule containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
