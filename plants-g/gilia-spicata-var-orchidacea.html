Gilia spicata var. orchidacea

Gilia spicata var. orchidacea, commonly known as the orchid-flowered gilia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
