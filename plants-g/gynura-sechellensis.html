Gynura sechellensis

Gynura sechellensis, commonly known as the Sechellean shield fern, is a unique plant native to the Seychelles.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its compact growth habit. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a slightly succulent texture.

Gynura sechellensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
