Gentianella Septentrionalis

Gentianella septentrionalis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to northern regions of North America. It is known for its striking blue or violet petals that bloom in early summer. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and rocky slopes. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its small size and limited distribution. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and endangered flora.
