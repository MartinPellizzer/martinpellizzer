Geonoma pauciflora

Geonoma pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered geonoma, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a terrestrial bromeliad native to the tropical rainforests of South America. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and has a rosette-shaped foliage structure. Its leaves are often broad and covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in water retention.

The inflorescence produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.
