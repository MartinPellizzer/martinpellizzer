Garcinia ovalifolius var. macrantha

Garcinia ovalifolius var. macrantha, commonly known as the African mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red, fleshy berries that contain a single seed. Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and has gained attention for its potential health benefits.
