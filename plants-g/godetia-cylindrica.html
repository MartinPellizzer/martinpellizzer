Godetia cylindrica

Godetia cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical godetia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are typically erect and branched. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered with fine hairs.

Godetia cylindrica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and ease of growth.
