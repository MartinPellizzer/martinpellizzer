Galeola kuhlii

Galeola kuhlii, commonly known as the Kuhlii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale pink, arranged in a loose spike.

Galeola kuhlii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
