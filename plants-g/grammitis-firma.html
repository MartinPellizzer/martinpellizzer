Grammitis firma

Grammitis firma, commonly known as the firm grammitis, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks without deriving nutrients from them. Its leaves are usually narrow and elongated, with a glossy surface that reflects light.

Grammitis firma thrives in humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
