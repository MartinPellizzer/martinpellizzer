Gossypium lobatum

Gossypium lobatum, commonly known as the woolly cotton or desert cotton, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by capsules containing fluffy, white seeds.
