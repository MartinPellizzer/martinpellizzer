Gasteria × holtzei

Gasteria × holtzei, commonly known as the hybrid gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically gray-green with white or yellow markings, providing a striking visual appearance. This plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.

It produces small, white or pink flowers on tall stalks during the warmer months.
