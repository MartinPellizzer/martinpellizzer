Gladiolus uhehensis

Gladiolus uhehensis, commonly known as the Uhehense gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is less commonly seen than other gladiolus species.
