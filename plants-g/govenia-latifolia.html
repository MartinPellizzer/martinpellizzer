Govenia latifolia

Govenia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf govenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Govenia latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
