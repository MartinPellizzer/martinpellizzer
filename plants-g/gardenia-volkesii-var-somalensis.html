Gardenia volkesii var. somalensis

Gardenia volkesii var. somalensis, commonly known as the Somali gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Horn of Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant, waxy white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
