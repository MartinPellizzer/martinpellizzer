Gomphrena vaga var. pyramidata

Gomphrena vaga var. pyramidata, commonly known as the pyramid gomphrena, is a striking annual plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, conical inflorescences that range in color from pink to red, giving it a distinctive, almost geometric appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Gomphrena vaga var. pyramidata is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
