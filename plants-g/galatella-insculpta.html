Galatella insculpta

Galatella insculpta, commonly known as the sculptured galatella, is a rare and striking plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a distinctive, raised pattern. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of its family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas.
