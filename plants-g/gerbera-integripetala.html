Gerbera integripetala

Gerbera integripetala, commonly known as the African gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white, pink, or red petals. The plant has a rosette growth habit with thick, hairy leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Gerbera integripetala is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its vibrant blooms.
