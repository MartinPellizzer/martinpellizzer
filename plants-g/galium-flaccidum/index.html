Galium Flaccidum

Galium flaccidum is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of North America. It is commonly found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, opposite leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. Its delicate appearance and preference for humid conditions make it a unique addition to native plant gardens. Despite its modest size, Galium flaccidum plays an important role in supporting local insect life and maintaining ecological balance.
