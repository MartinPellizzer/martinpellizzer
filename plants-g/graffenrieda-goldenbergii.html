Graffenrieda goldenbergii

Graffenrieda goldenbergii, commonly known as the goldenbergii spider flower, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescences that feature vibrant yellow and orange flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are attractive to birds and other wildlife. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Graffenrieda goldenbergii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
