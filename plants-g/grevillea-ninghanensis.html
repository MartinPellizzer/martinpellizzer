Grevillea ninghanensis

Grevillea ninghanensis, commonly known as the Ninghan's waratah, is a shrub native to western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces woody, spirally arranged stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea ninghanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
