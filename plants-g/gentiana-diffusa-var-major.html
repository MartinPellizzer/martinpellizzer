Gentiana diffusa var. major

Gentiana diffusa var. major, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
