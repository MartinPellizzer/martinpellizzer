Globularia nainii

Globularia nainii, commonly known as the Maltese globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and produces small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal areas.

Globularia nainii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
