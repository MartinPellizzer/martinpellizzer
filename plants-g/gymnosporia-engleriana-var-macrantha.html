Gymnosporia engleriana var. macrantha

Gymnosporia engleriana var. macrantha, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a leathery texture.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.
