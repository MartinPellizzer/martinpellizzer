Galium sylvaticum var. pseudoaristatum

Galium sylvaticum var. pseudoaristatum, commonly known as the false aristotome, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. This variant is found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
