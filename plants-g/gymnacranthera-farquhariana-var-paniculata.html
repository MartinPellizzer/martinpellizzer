Gymnacranthera farquhariana var. paniculata

Gymnacranthera farquhariana var. paniculata, commonly known as the Farquhar's paniculate gymnacranthera, is a rare and lesser-known species within the Myrtaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in panicles, giving it its varietal name. It is native to specific regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded forest understories.
