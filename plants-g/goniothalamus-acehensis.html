Goniothalamus acehensis

Goniothalamus acehensis, commonly known as the Aceh nut tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the western part of Sumatra, Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and large, elliptical leaves. The plant produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, round fruits that turn from green to yellow when mature.

Its seeds are enclosed in a hard, woody aril, and the tree is valued for its ornamental and potential economic uses.
