Gonostemon flavopurpureus var. fleckii

Gonostemon flavopurpureus var. fleckii, commonly known as the Fleck's purple-flowered gonostemon, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that display a mix of yellow and purple hues. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
