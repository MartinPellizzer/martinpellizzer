Gymnosporia botsabelensis

Gymnosporia botsabelensis, commonly known as the Botswana thornveld acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain seeds, typical of many legumes. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species in dry regions.

Its thorny structure provides protection against herbivores, while its deep root system allows it to access groundwater.
