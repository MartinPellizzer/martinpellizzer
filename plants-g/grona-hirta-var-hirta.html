Grona hirta var. hirta

Grona hirta var. hirta, commonly known as the hairy grona, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive, rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its compact growth and long-lasting blooms.
