Guzmania spectabilis

Guzmania spectabilis, commonly known as the spectabilis bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to South America.

It features a rosette of long, narrow, dark green leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces a vibrant inflorescence with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange, often lasting for several months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Its unique structure and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
