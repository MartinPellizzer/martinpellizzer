Gladiolus platyphyllus

Gladiolus platyphyllus, commonly known as the broad-leaf gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its striking appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for floral arrangements.
