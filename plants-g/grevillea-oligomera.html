Grevillea oligomera

Grevillea oligomera, commonly known as the narrow-leafed waratah, is a shrub native to the south-western region of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers, which are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates with dry summers.

Grevillea oligomera is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and hardy nature.
