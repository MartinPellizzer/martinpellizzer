Garcinia jensenii

Garcinia jensenii, commonly known as the Jensen's garcinia, is a rare tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces bright orange, berry-like fruits that are rich in bioactive compounds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research.
