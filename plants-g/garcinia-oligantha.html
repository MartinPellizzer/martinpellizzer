Garcinia oligantha

Garcinia oligantha, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, white flowers and edible, yellow-orange fruits that have a tangy flavor. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth surface with a slightly waxy texture.

Garcinia oligantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit, which is used in traditional cuisines and for its potential health benefits.
