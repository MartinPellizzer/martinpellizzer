Gonolobus erianthus

Gonolobus erianthus, commonly known as the velvetleaf or hairy sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in many tropical ecosystems.
