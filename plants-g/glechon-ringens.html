Glechon ringens

Glechon ringens, commonly known as the common yellow daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flower heads with white ray florets, which bloom in spring and summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and coastal dunes. It has a basal rosette of hairy, silver-green leaves that help it retain moisture.

Glechon ringens is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate.
