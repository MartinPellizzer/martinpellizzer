Gardenia augusta var. kosyunensis

Gardenia augusta var. kosyunensis, commonly known as the Kosyunensis gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and light conditions.

The flowers emit a sweet, heady scent, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
