Gymnocalycium hyptiacanthum subsp. leeanum

Gymnocalycium hyptiacanthum subsp.

leeanum, commonly known as the Leeanum moon cactus, is a succulent cactus native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its globular or cylindrical stem, which is often covered in white to pale green spines.

The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
