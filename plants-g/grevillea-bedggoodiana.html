Grevillea bedggoodiana

Grevillea bedggoodiana, commonly known as the Bedgwood's grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It features vibrant, feather-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange, attracting a variety of pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea bedggoodiana is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to flourish in harsh environmental conditions.
