Gasteria subverrucosa var. grandipunctata

Gasteria subverrucosa var. grandipunctata, commonly known as the Giant Spiky Gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
