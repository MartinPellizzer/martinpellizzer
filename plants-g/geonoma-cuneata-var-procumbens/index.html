Geonoma Cuneata Var. Procumbens

Geonoma cuneata var. procumbens is a unique fern species native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its low-growing habit and distinctive, lance-shaped fronds. This variety thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its intricate leaf patterns and adaptability make it a valuable addition to botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
