Gesneria suttonii

Gesneria suttonii, commonly known as the Sutton's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub with glossy, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Gesneria suttonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
