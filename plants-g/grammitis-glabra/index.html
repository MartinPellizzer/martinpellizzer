Grammitis Glabra

Grammitis glabra is a small, terrestrial fern commonly found in moist, shaded environments across tropical regions. It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is known for its delicate, lace-like fronds that often appear in dense clusters. This plant thrives in humid climates and is often seen growing on rocks or tree trunks in rainforests. Its simple yet elegant structure makes it a popular subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts. Despite its small size, Grammitis glabra plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat.
