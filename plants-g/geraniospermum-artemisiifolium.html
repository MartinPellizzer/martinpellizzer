Geraniospermum artemisiifolium

Geraniospermum artemisiifolium, commonly known as the Artemisia-leaved Geraniospermum, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae, which includes plants known for their showy flowers and often aromatic foliage.
