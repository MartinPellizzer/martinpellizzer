Genianthus hastatus

Genianthus hastatus, commonly known as the yellow sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native environment.
