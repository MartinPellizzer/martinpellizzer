Goniopteris abbreviata

Goniopteris abbreviata, commonly known as the short-stemmed water fern, is a member of the water fern family, Ophioglossaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. Goniopteris abbreviata is a rare and ancient species, with a fossil record dating back to the Carboniferous period.
