Glycine bujacia

Glycine bujacia, commonly known as the Bujacian bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically pale yellow or white. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Glycine bujacia is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which enriches soil fertility.

It is also used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop in some areas.
