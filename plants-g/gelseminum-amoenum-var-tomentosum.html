Gelseminum amoenum var. tomentosum

Gelseminum amoenum var. tomentosum, commonly known as the tomentose yellow oleander, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
