Gastrochilus praemorsus

Gastrochilus praemorsus, commonly known as the black orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black petals and sepals, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on the trunks of trees or in rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a unique coloration that ranges from deep purple to black.

Gastrochilus praemorsus is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological significance in its native habitats.
