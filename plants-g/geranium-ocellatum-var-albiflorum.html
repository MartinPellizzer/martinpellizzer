Geranium ocellatum var. albiflorum

Geranium ocellatum var. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy white flowers with a distinctive red center, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with silver markings. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
