Goodenia arthrotricha

Goodenia arthrotricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, are often visited by insects such as bees and butterflies.

Goodenia arthrotricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
