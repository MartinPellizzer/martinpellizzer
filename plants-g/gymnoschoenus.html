Gymnoschoenus

Gymnoschoenus, commonly known as the swamp millet, is a genus of flowering plants in the grass family.

It is native to wetland and marshy areas in the Northern Hemisphere. The plants typically have slender stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. Gymnoschoenus species are often found in shallow water or moist soil conditions.

They play a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
