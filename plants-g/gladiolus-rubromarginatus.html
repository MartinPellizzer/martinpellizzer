Gladiolus rubromarginatus

Gladiolus rubromarginatus, commonly known as the red-marginated gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, showy flower spikes that bloom in shades of red and pink. The flowers typically have a distinctive red margin around the edges of the petals, which gives the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This ornamental plant is also valued for its resilience and ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
