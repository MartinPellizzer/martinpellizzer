Globularia willkommii

Globularia willkommii, commonly known as the African globularia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape, with dense clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant produces small, oblong leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Globularia willkommii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
