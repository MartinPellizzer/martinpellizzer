Gagea cretica

Gagea cretica, commonly known as the Cretan spring beauty, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Gagea cretica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its early blooming habit.
