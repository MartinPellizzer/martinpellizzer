Gnaphalium hypoleucum

Gnaphalium hypoleucum, commonly known as silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with silvery-white to pale green leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
