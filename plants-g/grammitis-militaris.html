Grammitis militaris

Grammitis militaris, commonly known as the military fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The leaves are typically bright green, with a leathery texture and a distinct, often serrated margin. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks.

The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori, which are typically located on the underside of the fronds.
