Gentiana clusii subsp. pyrenaica

Gentiana clusii subsp.

pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range. It is a perennial herb with striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hair, while its stems are erect and often branched.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
