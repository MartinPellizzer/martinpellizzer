Geranium clemensiae

Geranium clemensiae, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Geranium clemensiae is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
