Grevillea australis

Grevillea australis, commonly known as the coastal grevillea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy soils and salty sea breezes. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking floral display and hardy nature.
