Geissois superba

Geissois superba, commonly known as the golden star ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in clusters. The plant has long, slender leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Geissois superba is also valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens and floral arrangements.
