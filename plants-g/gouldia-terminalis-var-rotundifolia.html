Gouldia terminalis var. rotundifolia

Gouldia terminalis var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. The leaves are broad and rounded, contributing to its unique appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often used in rock gardens and containers due to its drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms.
