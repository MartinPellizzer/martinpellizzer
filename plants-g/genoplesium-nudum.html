Genoplesium nudum

Genoplesium nudum, commonly known as the naked pleated orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its elongated, pleated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it a distinctive and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
