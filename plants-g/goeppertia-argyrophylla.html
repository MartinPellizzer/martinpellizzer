Goeppertia argyrophylla

Goeppertia argyrophylla, commonly known as the silver-leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green patterns. The leaves are typically broad, lance-shaped, and have a waxy texture, which helps in reflecting sunlight. This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
