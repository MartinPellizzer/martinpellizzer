Glochidion subscandens

Glochidion subscandens, commonly known as the climbing glochidion, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

Glochidion subscandens is also noted for its ability to climb using aerial roots, making it a common component of forest undergrowth.
