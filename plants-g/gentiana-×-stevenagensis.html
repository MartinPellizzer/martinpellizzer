Gentiana × stevenagensis

Gentiana × stevenagensis, commonly known as the hybrid gentian, is a perennial flowering plant that belongs to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and its stems are often hairy.

Gentiana × stevenagensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
