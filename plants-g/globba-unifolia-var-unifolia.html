Globba unifolia var. unifolia

Globba unifolia var. unifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved globba, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear, with a dense rosette growth habit. The plant produces small, pale yellow to greenish flowers arranged in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
