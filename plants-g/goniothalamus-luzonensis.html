Goniothalamus luzonensis

Goniothalamus luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon tree or Luzon satinash, is a member of the Annonaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Luzon. The plant has simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
