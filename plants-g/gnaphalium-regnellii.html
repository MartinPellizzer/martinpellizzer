Gnaphalium regnellii

Gnaphalium regnellii, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is known for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is well adapted to arid conditions.

Its compact growth habit and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
