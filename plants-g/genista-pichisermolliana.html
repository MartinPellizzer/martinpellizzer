Genista pichisermolliana

Genista pichisermolliana, commonly known as the Pichisermolliana broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, wiry stems that often branch out in a bushy manner. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the rocky, sunny conditions of its native habitat.
