Geeria

Geeria, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact growth habit and showy, often violet-colored flowers. Geeria species typically have thick, waxy leaves that help retain moisture in arid environments. They are popular as houseplants due to their attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

The flowers are usually borne on upright stems and bloom in a variety of colors beyond just violet.
