Gynocephalum palmatum

Gynocephalum palmatum, commonly known as the palmate leaf yarrow, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its palmately divided leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It is often found in the eastern and central United States.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
