Gerascanthus skutchii

Gerascanthus skutchii, commonly known as the Skutch's geranium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the spring. Gerascanthus skutchii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
