Guettarda acutiflora

Guettarda acutiflora, commonly known as the sharp-flowered guettarda, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are glossy and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.

Guettarda acutiflora thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
