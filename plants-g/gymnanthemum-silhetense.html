Gymnanthemum silhetense

Gymnanthemum silhetense, commonly known as the Silhet mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern region of Bangladesh.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and mountainous regions. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

Gymnanthemum silhetense is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
