Gerbera selloi

Gerbera selloi, commonly known as the Sello's gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features bright yellow to orange centers surrounded by ray florets in shades of red, pink, or white. Gerbera selloi thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements contribute to its appeal among horticulturists and gardeners.
