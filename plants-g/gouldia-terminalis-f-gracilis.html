Gouldia terminalis f. gracilis

Gouldia terminalis f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender gouldia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a unique, compact growth habit that makes it ideal for rock gardens or container planting. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, requiring minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
