Grisseea apiculata

Grisseea apiculata, commonly known as the slender rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in racemes, often with a faint fragrance.

Grisseea apiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
