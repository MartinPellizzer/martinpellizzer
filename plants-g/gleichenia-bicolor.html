Gleichenia bicolor

Gleichenia bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that display a striking two-color pattern, typically green and brown, along their length. The plant is native to various regions in the Americas, particularly in tropical and subtropical climates. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological studies.
