Glechon marifolia f. canescens

Glechon marifolia f. canescens, commonly known as the hairy glechon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, often grayish foliage. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
