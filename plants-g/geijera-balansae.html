Geijera balansae

Geijera balansae, commonly known as the Balansae flame tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of northern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red to orange-pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas with a warm, humid climate.

Geijera balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and parks.
