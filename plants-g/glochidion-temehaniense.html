Glochidion temehaniense

Glochidion temehaniense, commonly known as the Temehani lanceleaf, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Glochidion temehaniense is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as montane forests.
