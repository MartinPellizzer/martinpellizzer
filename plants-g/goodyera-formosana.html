Goodyera formosana

Goodyera formosana, commonly known as the Formosan lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Taiwan.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and intricate, spiraling inflorescences. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that are arranged in a loose, twisting pattern. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
