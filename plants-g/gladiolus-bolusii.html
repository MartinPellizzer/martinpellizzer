Gladiolus bolusii

Gladiolus bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, colorful flowers with three petals and three sepals. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, often in shades of red, pink, and white.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
