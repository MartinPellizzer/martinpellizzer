Gentiana taiwanialbiflora

Gentiana taiwanialbiflora, commonly known as the Taiwan white gentian, is a perennial herb native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

The flowers bloom in late summer and are pollinated primarily by insects.
