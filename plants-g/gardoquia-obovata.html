Gardoquia obovata

Gardoquia obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate gardoquia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lanceolate to obovate leaves that are typically hairy and have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
