Galium multicaule

Galium multicaule, commonly known as common bedstraw, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

Galium multicaule is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in woodland edges, where it thrives in partial shade to full sun.
