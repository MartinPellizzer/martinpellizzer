Goeppertia buchtienii

Goeppertia buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtienii Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with intricate patterns of green, silver, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
