Graphorkis Milnei

Graphorkis milnei is a rare and striking member of the orchid family found primarily in the mountainous regions of New Zealand. This unique plant is known for its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in late winter and early spring. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its hardy nature, allowing it to survive in harsh alpine conditions. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Graphorkis milnei is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this beautiful and important species.
