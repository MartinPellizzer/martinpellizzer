Gardenia ovularis

Gardenia ovularis, commonly known as the oval gardenia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are leathery and glossy, with a deep green color, while its flowers are often used in traditional floral arrangements.

Gardenia ovularis thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
