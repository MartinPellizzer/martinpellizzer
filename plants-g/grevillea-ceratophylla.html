Grevillea ceratophylla

Grevillea ceratophylla, commonly known as the red-stemmed Grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces bright orange or yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea ceratophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
