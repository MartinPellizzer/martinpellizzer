Gesneria fruticosa

Gesneria fruticosa, commonly known as the flame plant, is a shrub-like species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria fruticosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
