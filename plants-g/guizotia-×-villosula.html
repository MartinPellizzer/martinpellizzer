Guizotia × villosula

Guizotia × villosula, commonly known as the hairy guizotia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to regions of South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercialized.
