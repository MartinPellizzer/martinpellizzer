Goeppertia nitens

Goeppertia nitens, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Goeppertia nitens is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its unique foliage.
