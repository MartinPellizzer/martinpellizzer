Gesneria purpurascens var. yamuriensis

Gesneria purpurascens var. yamuriensis, commonly known as the Yamuriensis Gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features broad, elliptical leaves that are dark green with a glossy surface and often have a subtle reddish or purplish tinge on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
