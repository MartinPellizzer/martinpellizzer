Geodorum cochinchinense

Geodorum cochinchinense, commonly known as the fragrant orchid or Chinese orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like China, India, and Thailand, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture, and it prefers well-drained soil and partial shade.

Geodorum cochinchinense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
