Gymnostemon zaizou

Gymnostemon zaizou, commonly known as the Chinese rose or Zaizou rose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves and produces small, aggregate fruits. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal.

However, it is not widely distributed beyond its native habitat.
