Galega hispidula

Galega hispidula, commonly known as the hairy goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are compound, with elongated leaflets that have a hairy texture, giving the plant its common name.

Galega hispidula is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
