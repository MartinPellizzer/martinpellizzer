Gothofreda arnottiana

The plant is epiphytic, growing on the trunks and branches of trees without deriving nutrients from its host. Its foliage consists of narrow, strap-like leaves that are often hidden among the dense foliage of its habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, Gothofreda arnottiana is considered critically endangered and is protected under Australian conservation laws.
