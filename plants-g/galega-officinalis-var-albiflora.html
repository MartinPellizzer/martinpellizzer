Galega officinalis var. albiflora

Galega officinalis var. albiflora, commonly known as white-flowered goat's rue, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
