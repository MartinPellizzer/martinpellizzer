Gasteria × bayfieldii

Gasteria × bayfieldii, commonly known as the Bayfield's gasteria, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white or pale veins. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear small, tubular flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria × bayfieldii is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
