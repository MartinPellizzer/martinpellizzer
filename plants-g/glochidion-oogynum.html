Glochidion oogynum

Glochidion oogynum, commonly known as the velvetleaf holly, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows up to 2 meters in height. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green on top, with a lighter underside. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange-red berries.

Glochidion oogynum is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.
