Gravesia rotundifolia

Gravesia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf gravesia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Gravesia rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral fragrance.
