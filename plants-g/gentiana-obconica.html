Gentiana obconica

Gentiana obconica, commonly known as the broadleaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a slightly rough texture.

Gentiana obconica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
