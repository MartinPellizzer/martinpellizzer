Gazania linearis var. ovalis

Gazania linearis var. ovalis, commonly known as the oval gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in a compact, low-growing shrub form, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to xeriscaped gardens.
