Gothofreda mucronata

Gothofreda mucronata, commonly known as the sharp-tipped orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters.
