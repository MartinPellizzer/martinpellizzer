Gastrolobium ebracteolatum

Gastrolobium ebracteolatum, commonly known as the slender wattle, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

The species is part of the Proteaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with specialized adaptations for nutrient-poor soils.
