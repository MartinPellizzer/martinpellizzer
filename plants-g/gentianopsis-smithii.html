Gentianopsis smithii

Gentianopsis smithii, commonly known as the Smith's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their vibrant blue or purple flowers. Gentianopsis smithii is often found in California and parts of Oregon, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions.
