Galax cordifolia

Galax cordifolia, commonly known as heartleaf galax, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white down. Its showy clusters of white to pale blue flowers appear in late spring to early summer.

Galax cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
