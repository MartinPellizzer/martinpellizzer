Gerardia delphiniifolia

Gerardia delphiniifolia, commonly known as the water gerardia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, typically found in moist, calcareous soils near water bodies. The plant features upright, branched stems with alternate, palmately lobed leaves that are often hairy. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and numerous stamens.

Gerardia delphiniifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
