Gesneria hookeri

Gesneria hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dark green, elliptical leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria hookeri is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
