Gnetum leptostachyum var. robustum

Gnetum leptostachyum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust gnetum, is a member of the Gnetophyta class, which includes gymnosperms.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its smooth, often reddish bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female structures.

Its seeds are enclosed in a fleshy, three-lobed capsule, and the plant is notable for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
