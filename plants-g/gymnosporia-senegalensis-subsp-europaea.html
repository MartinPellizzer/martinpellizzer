Gymnosporia senegalensis subsp. europaea

Gymnosporia senegalensis subsp.

europaea, commonly known as the European African thornbush, is a shrub native to parts of Africa and the Mediterranean. It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters.

The plant produces woody capsules that contain seeds, typical of the gymnosporangiate structure in its genus. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along coastal areas.
