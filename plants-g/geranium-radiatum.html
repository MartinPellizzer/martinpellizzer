Geranium radiatum

Geranium radiatum, commonly known as the radiate geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
