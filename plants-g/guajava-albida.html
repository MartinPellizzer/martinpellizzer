Guajava albida

Guajava albida, commonly known as white guajava, is a tropical fruit tree native to Central America.

It produces small, white, fleshy fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate in shape with a smooth margin. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in green or red clusters.

The fruit is valued for its unique flavor and is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
