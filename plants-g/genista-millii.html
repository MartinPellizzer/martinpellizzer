Genista millii

Genista millii, commonly known as the silverbark broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves that are usually gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, rocky environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.
