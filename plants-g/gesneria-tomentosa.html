Gesneria tomentosa

Gesneria tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety white pubescence that covers its leaves, giving it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting dark throat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria tomentosa is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
