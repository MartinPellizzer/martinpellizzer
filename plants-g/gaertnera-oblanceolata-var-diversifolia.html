Gaertnera oblanceolata var. diversifolia

Gaertnera oblanceolata var. diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated oblongleaf gaertnera, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features elongated, ovate leaves with a distinctive variegation pattern of white and green. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments with high humidity.
