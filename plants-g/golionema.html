Golionema

Golionema, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Golionema is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
