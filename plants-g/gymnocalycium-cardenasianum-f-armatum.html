Gymnocalycium cardenasianum f. armatum

Gymnocalycium cardenasianum f. armatum, commonly known as the armed cardenasian cactus, is a rare and distinctive cactus native to northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that are often covered with dense, white woolly hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. Its unique features make it a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts and botanists.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
