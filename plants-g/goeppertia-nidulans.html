Goeppertia nidulans

Goeppertia nidulans, commonly known as the Orange-veined Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with deep green veins and a rich, velvety texture. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, often displaying a variegated pattern of orange, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.

This ornamental plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
