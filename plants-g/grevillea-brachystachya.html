Grevillea brachystachya

Grevillea brachystachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically grey-green in color. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea brachystachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
