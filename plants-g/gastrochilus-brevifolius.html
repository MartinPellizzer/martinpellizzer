Gastrochilus brevifolius

Gastrochilus brevifolius, commonly known as the short-leaf gastrochilus, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers with a distinctive lip that often has a contrasting color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
