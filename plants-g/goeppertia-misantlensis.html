Goeppertia misantlensis

Goeppertia misantlensis, commonly known as the silver stripe calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-white stripe running along the midrib, contrasting beautifully with its deep green foliage. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It prefers moist, well-draining soil and consistent humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Goeppertia misantlensis is also appreciated for its air-purifying qualities and ornamental value in both residential and commercial settings.
