Goniophlebium pubescens

Goniophlebium pubescens, commonly known as the hairy fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy texture on its fronds, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments.

Goniophlebium pubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
