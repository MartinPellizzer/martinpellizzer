Gandasulium peregrinum

Gandasulium peregrinum, commonly known as the wandering leaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and bright green coloration. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pale yellow. Its rapid growth habit makes it popular for ornamental use in gardens and greenhouses.

However, it requires well-drained soil and partial shade to thrive optimally.
