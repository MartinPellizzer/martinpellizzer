Gleichenia bijouxii

Gleichenia bijouxii, commonly known as the jewel fern, is a small, delicate fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, bright green fronds that give it a lacy appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are creeping and help it spread slowly across the forest floor.

The plant is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
