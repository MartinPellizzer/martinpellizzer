Gentiana oranensis

Gentiana oranensis, commonly known as the Orange Gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North Africa.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hair.

Gentiana oranensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
