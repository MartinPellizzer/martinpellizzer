Goeppertia exscapa

Goeppertia exscapa, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features broad, dark green, velvety leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of silver or white. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a favored ornamental plant among plant enthusiasts.
