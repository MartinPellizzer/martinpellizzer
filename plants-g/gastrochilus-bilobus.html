Gastrochilus bilobus

Gastrochilus bilobus, commonly known as the two-lobed orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Thailand, Vietnam, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically have a pale yellow or greenish hue with intricate patterns. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptical in shape and arranged in a basal rosette.

Gastrochilus bilobus thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
