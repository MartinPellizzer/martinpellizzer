Grammitis hartii

Grammitis hartii, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a small epiphytic fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety fronds that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a unique texture. Native to tropical regions of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a delicate appearance that makes it popular in ornamental horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
