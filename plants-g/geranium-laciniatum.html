Geranium laciniatum

Geranium laciniatum, commonly known as the cutleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright red or pink flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Geranium laciniatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
