Galactia australis

Galactia australis, commonly known as the Australian milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to southern Africa.

It features compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and is typically found in open, sunny habitats. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Galactia australis is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
