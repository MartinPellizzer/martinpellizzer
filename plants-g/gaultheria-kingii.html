Gaultheria kingii

Gaultheria kingii, commonly known as the King's wintergreen, is a species of evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are rich in antioxidants and have a sharp, peppery flavor. Gaultheria kingii thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in New Zealand's flora.
