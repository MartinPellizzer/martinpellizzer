Gynostemma guangxiense

Gynostemma guangxiense, commonly known as Chinese ginseng or "immortality vine," is a climbing herb native to southern China.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red fruits containing kidney-shaped seeds. It is valued for its potential medicinal properties, including antioxidant and adaptogenic effects.

Gynostemma guangxiense is often cultivated for use in traditional Chinese medicine and as a health supplement.
