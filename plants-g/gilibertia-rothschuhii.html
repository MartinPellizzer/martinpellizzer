Gilibertia rothschuhii

Gilibertia rothschuhii, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.
