Gomidesia klotzschiana

Gomidesia klotzschiana, commonly known as the Klotzschian lily, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and cool climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Gomidesia klotzschiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
