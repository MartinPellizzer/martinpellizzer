Genista lasiantha

Genista lasiantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf broom, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and pinnate, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Genista lasiantha is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
