Gardenia lucida

Gardenia lucida, commonly known as the shining gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, yellowish-orange fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Gardenia lucida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
