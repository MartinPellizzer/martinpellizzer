Garnotia caespitosa f. permucronata

Garnotia caespitosa f. permucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved garnotia, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense, tufted growth with narrow, dark green leaves that are often slightly hairy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
