Galega stricta

Galega stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf goat's rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Galega stricta is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and woodland edges.
