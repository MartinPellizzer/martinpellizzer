Gnaphalium monticola

Gnaphalium monticola, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

Gnaphalium monticola is often found in high-altitude meadows and is adapted to withstand cold temperatures and strong winds.
