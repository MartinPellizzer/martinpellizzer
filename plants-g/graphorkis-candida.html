Graphorkis candida

Graphorkis candida, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the subtropical regions of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in a dense spike. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and along stream banks.
