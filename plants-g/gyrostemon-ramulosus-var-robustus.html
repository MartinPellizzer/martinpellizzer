Gyrostemon ramulosus var. robustus

Gyrostemon ramulosus var. robustus, commonly known as the robust gyrostemon, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which is a distinguishing feature.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
