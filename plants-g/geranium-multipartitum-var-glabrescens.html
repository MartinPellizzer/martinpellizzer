Geranium multipartitum var. glabrescens

Geranium multipartitum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically rounded and have a smooth texture, which gives the variety its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
