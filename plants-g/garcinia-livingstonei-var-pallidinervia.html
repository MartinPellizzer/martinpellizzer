Garcinia livingstonei var. pallidinervia

Garcinia livingstonei var. pallidinervia, commonly known as the pale-veined garcinia, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its elliptical to ovate leaves with pale veins that contrast with the dark green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries that are edible. Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Rubiaceae family.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical rainforests.
