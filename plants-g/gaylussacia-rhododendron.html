Gaylussacia rhododendron

Gaylussacia rhododendron, commonly known as the common waxmyrtle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and fragrant flowers.
