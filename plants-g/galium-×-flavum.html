Galium × flavum

Galium × flavum, commonly known as the yellow bedstraw, is a flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is a hybrid species that results from the cross between Galium verum and Galium boreale. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
