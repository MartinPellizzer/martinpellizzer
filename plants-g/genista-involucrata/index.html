Genista Involucrata

Genista involucrata is a hardy shrub native to southern Europe and northern Africa. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions. The plant is known for its clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure. This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.
