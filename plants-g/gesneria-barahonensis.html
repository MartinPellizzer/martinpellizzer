Gesneria barahonensis

Gesneria barahonensis, commonly known as the Barahonensis Gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a slightly textured surface.

The flowers, which bloom in shades of pink and white, are an important feature for attracting pollinators.
