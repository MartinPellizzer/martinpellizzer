Galeopsis dubia proles nepetifolia

Galeopsis dubia proles nepetifolia, commonly known as the lesser hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

Galeopsis dubia proles nepetifolia is a perennial herb that is native to Europe and parts of Asia.
