Goniothalamus epiphyticus

Goniothalamus epiphyticus, commonly known as the epiphytic moonseed, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has small, heart-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

It is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
