Geranium sublaevispermum

Geranium sublaevispermum, commonly known as the smooth-seeded geranium, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with erect, branched stems that often have a reddish hue. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside.

Geranium sublaevispermum is often found in grassy areas and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
