Geonoma hollinensis

Geonoma hollinensis, commonly known as the Brazilian fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically light green and have a soft texture. It is often found growing on rocks or in the understory of dense forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
