Goniocheton arborescens

Goniocheton arborescens, commonly known as the tree hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive red or purple throat.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
