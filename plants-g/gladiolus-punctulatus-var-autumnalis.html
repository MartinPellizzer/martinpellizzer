Gladiolus punctulatus var. autumnalis

Gladiolus punctulatus var. autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn gladiolus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, making it a popular choice for fall gardens. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

The autumn gladiolus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
