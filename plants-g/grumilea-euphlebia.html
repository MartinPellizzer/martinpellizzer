Grumilea euphlebia

Grumilea euphlebia, commonly known as the red-flowered grumilea, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are arranged in clusters and are known for their long-lasting color.

Grumilea euphlebia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
