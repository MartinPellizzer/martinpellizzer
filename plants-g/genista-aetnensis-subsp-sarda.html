Genista aetnensis subsp. sarda

Genista aetnensis subsp.

sarda, commonly known as the Sardinian broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, particularly found in Sardinia and parts of Sicily. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its yellow flowers bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

This subspecies is notable for its adaptability to arid conditions and its role in stabilizing coastal and coastal cliff environments.
