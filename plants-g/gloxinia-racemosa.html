Gloxinia racemosa

Gloxinia racemosa, commonly known as the clustered gloxinia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Gloxinia racemosa is known for its ability to produce multiple flower clusters along its stems.
