Gymnocalycium megatae subsp. matoense

Gymnocalycium megatae subsp.

matoense, commonly known as the Mato Mundo cactus, is a species native to Argentina. It is characterized by its spiny, globular stems that are typically greenish-gray in color.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing cactus that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often sought after by cactus enthusiasts.
