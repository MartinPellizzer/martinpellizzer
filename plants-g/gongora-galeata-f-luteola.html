Gongora galeata f. luteola

Gongora galeata f. luteola, commonly known as the Yellow Gongora, is a unique orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green coloration, which is particularly vivid in its flowers. The plant features showy, lip-shaped flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a distinct fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
