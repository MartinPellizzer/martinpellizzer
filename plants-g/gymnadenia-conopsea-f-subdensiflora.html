Gymnadenia conopsea f. subdensiflora

Gymnadenia conopsea f. subdensiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered grass pinks, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright, hairy stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The subspecies is distinguished by its denser inflorescence compared to the typical form, with more closely packed flowers. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands, often in temperate regions of Europe.

This plant is noted for its ornamental value and is of interest to botanists and gardeners alike.
