Gardenia macroptera

Gardenia macroptera, commonly known as the large-leaf gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 20 centimeters in length. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically borne in clusters. These flowers are followed by orange-red berries containing one to three seeds.

Gardenia macroptera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
