Goodenia erecta

Goodenia erecta, commonly known as the erect goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators. Goodenia erecta is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
