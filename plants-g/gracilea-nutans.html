Gracilea nutans

Gracilea nutans, commonly known as the leaning graceful vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features slender, wiry stems that often trail or climb along supports. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow.

Gracilea nutans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
