Geniostoma tinifolium var. tinifolium

Geniostoma tinifolium var. tinifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf geniostoma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and slightly curled at the edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
