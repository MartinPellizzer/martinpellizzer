Gaertnera schatzii

Gaertnera schatzii, commonly known as the Schatz's gaertnera, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are elliptical and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture in its native habitat.

Gaertnera schatzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
