Genista polyphylla

Genista polyphylla, commonly known as the evergreen broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Genista polyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
