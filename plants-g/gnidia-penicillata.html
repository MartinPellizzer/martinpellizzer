Gnidia penicillata

Gnidia penicillata, commonly known as the Cape rose or yellow star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with five petals, arranged in a star-like pattern, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gnidia penicillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
