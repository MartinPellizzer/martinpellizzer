Genista florida subsp. maroccana

Genista florida subsp.

maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan broom, is a shrub native to North Africa. It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has compound leaves composed of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant floral display and hardy nature.
