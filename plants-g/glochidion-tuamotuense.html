Glochidion tuamotuense

Glochidion tuamotuense, commonly known as the Tuamotu glabrous leaf.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the Society Islands in French Polynesia. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by glochids, which are modified leaves that resemble spines.

The species is adapted to coastal and limestone environments, showing resilience to salt spray and poor soil conditions.
