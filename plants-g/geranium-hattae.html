Geranium hattae

Geranium hattae, commonly known as the Hattae geranium, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soils. Geranium hattae is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, where it forms dense clusters.

Its botanical features make it a desirable ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.
