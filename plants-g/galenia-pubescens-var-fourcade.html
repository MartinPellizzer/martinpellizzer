Galenia pubescens var. fourcade

Galenia pubescens var. fourcade, commonly known as the Fourcade's galenia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a pubescent appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

This species is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
