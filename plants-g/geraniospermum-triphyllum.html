Geraniospermum triphyllum

Geraniospermum triphyllum, commonly known as the three-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets. The plant produces small, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
