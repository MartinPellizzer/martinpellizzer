Gesneria sceptrum

Gesneria sceptrum, commonly known as the crowned gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features broad, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and a distinctive crown-like structure at the base. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria sceptrum is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
