Gynura sumatrana

Gynura sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran gynura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Gynura sumatrana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
