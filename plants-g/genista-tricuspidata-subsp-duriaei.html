Genista tricuspidata subsp. duriaei

Genista tricuspidata subsp.

duriaei, commonly known as the Duriaei broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and northern Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

The plant features thorned stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.
