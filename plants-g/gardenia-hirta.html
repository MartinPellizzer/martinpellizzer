Gardenia hirta

Gardenia hirta, commonly known as the rough gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their beauty.

The fruit produced by Gardenia hirta is a small, fleshy drupe that contains seeds.
