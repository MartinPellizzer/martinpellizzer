Galeopsis nadalii

Galeopsis nadalii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved horse mint, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, blue to purple flowers are arranged in whorled clusters along the stems.
