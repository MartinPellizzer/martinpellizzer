Geraniospermum oxaloides

Geraniospermum oxaloides, commonly known as the oxalate-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Geraniospermum oxaloides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
