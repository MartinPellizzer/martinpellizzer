Gentiana tenuicaulis

Gentiana tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentiana tenuicaulis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
