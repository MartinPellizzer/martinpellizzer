Geranium philonotum

Geranium philonotum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
