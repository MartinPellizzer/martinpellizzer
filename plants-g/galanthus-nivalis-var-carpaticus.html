Galanthus nivalis var. carpaticus

Galanthus nivalis var. carpaticus, commonly known as the Carpathian snowdrop, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It produces delicate white flowers with a yellow-green tip, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in woodland and rocky habitats. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to naturalize over time.
