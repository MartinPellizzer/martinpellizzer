Gymnosporia senegalensis var. mildbraedii

Gymnosporia senegalensis var. mildbraedii, commonly known as the Senegalese gymnosporia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization.
