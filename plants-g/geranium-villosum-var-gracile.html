Geranium villosum var. gracile

Geranium villosum var. gracile, commonly known as the silky geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and also bear a fine pubescence, contributing to its overall textured look.

Geranium villosum var. gracile thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
