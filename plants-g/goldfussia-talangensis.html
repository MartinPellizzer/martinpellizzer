Goldfussia talangensis

Goldfussia talangensis, commonly known as the Talangensis goldenfussia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking golden-yellow inflorescences. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
