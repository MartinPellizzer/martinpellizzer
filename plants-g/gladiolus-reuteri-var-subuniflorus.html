Gladiolus reuteri var. subuniflorus

Gladiolus reuteri var. subuniflorus, commonly known as the single-flowered gladiolus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features erect, sword-shaped leaves and produces a single, striking spike of flowers, typically in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant, showy blooms that bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is relatively low maintenance and resistant to many common garden pests.
