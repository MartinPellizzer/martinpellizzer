Gladiolus marschallii

Gladiolus marschallii, commonly known as the marshallii gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for floral arrangements and landscaping.
