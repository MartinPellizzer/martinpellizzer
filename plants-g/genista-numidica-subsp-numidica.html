Genista numidica subsp. numidica

Genista numidica subsp.

numidica, commonly known as the Algerian broom, is a flowering plant native to North Africa. It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky, or sandy soils, often found in Mediterranean climates.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, and its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
