Galeorchis cyclochila var. palczewskii

Galeorchis cyclochila var. palczewskii, commonly known as the Palczewski's galeorchis, is a rare orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often in mountainous or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
