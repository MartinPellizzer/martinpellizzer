Gentiana laevigata var. laevigata

Gentiana laevigata var. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth gentian, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental value and is often found in woodland gardens and natural wetlands.
