Gnidia rehmannii

Gnidia rehmannii, commonly known as the red-flowered gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its striking red flowers, which are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
