Gongora jenischii

Gongora jenischii, commonly known as the Jenisch's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rock faces or in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rocky substrate, and it has a relatively short flowering period.

Gongora jenischii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
