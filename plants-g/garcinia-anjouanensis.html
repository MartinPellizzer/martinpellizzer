Garcinia anjouanensis

Garcinia anjouanensis, commonly known as Anjouan Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to the Comoros Islands.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. Its fruits are valued for their high content of hydroxycitric acid, which is used in dietary supplements.

Garcinia anjouanensis thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.
