Gaga complanata

Gaga complanata, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized horticulture.

Due to its limited habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species.
