Goeppertia kappleriana

Goeppertia kappleriana, commonly known as the Kappler's calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

Goeppertia kappleriana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
