Gordonia Spathulata

Gordonia spathulata is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its dense foliage and vibrant, showy flowers, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening. This plant thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
