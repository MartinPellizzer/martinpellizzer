Genista pilosa subsp. jordanii

Genista pilosa subsp.

jordanii, commonly known as Jordan's broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has compound leaves composed of several pairs of leaflets, which are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
