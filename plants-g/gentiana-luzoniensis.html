Gentiana luzoniensis

Gentiana luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
