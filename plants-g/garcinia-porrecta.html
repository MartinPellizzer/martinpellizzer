Garcinia porrecta

Garcinia porrecta, commonly known as the African yellow mangosteen, is a tropical shrub native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, edible fruits. The plant has elliptical leaves with a smooth surface and produces small, white flowers. Its fruit contains a single seed surrounded by a thick, segmented aril.

Garcinia porrecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
