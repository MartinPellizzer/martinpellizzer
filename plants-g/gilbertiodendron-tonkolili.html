Gilbertiodendron tonkolili

Gilbertiodendron tonkolili, commonly known as the African teak, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The tree produces hard, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability and resistance to termites. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
