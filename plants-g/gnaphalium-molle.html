Gnaphalium molle

Gnaphalium molle, commonly known as silky fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellow to white composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky areas.

Gnaphalium molle is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
