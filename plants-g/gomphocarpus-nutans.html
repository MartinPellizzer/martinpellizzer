Gomphocarpus nutans

Gomphocarpus nutans, commonly known as the nodding cow-plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, pendulous flowers that droop downward. The plant produces capsule fruits that split open to release its seeds. Its leaves are opposite and simple, with a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
