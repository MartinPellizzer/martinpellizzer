Geranium siamense

Geranium siamense, commonly known as the Siam geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces bright red or pink blooms, which are often accompanied by distinctive bracts. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium siamense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
