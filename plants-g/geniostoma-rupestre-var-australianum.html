Geniostoma rupestre var. australianum

Geniostoma rupestre var. australianum, commonly known as the Australian rock heath, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with small, fleshy leaves adapted to conserve water. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in dry climates.
