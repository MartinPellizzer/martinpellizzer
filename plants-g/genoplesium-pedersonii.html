Genoplesium pedersonii

Genoplesium pedersonii, commonly known as the Pederson's greenhood, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, creeping rhizome and a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces a single, hooded flower that is typically green with a yellowish throat, resembling a small helmet. It blooms in spring and is adapted to grow in sandy or loamy soils.
