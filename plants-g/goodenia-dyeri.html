Goodenia dyeri

Goodenia dyeri, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and the leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Goodenia dyeri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
