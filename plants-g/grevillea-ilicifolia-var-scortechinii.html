Grevillea ilicifolia var. scortechinii

Grevillea ilicifolia var. scortechinii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed honeysuckle, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces bright orange or red flowers that attract nectar-feeding birds and insects. The plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowering period typically occurs from late winter to early spring.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
