Globularia nudicaulis subsp. gracilis

Globularia nudicaulis subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender globularia, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, globe-shaped blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or dry habitats.

This subspecies is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
