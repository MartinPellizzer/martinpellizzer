Garuleum crinitum

Garuleum crinitum, commonly known as the white kalahari lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces long, slender seed pods that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Garuleum crinitum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
