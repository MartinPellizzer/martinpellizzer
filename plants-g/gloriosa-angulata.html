Gloriosa angulata

Gloriosa angulata, commonly known as the flame lily or zig-zag orchid, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, orange-red flowers with a distinctive zig-zag pattern on the petals. The plant produces long, spiral-shaped stems that climb using hooked tendrils. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
