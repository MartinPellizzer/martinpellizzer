Grevillea stenocarpa

Grevillea stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.

Grevillea stenocarpa is also an important plant for local wildlife, providing nectar for birds and insects.
