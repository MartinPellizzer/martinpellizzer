Geraniospermum meyeri

Geraniospermum meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer's geraniospermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae, which includes several species of shrubs and herbs. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, pink or white flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
