Glossocardia leschenaultii

Glossocardia leschenaultii, commonly known as the Leschenault's globose cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of spines, which provide protection against herbivores and intense sunlight. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its spiny exterior. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.
