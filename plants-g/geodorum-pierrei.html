Geodorum pierrei

Geodorum pierrei, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble a slipper or a lady's shoe, with a distinctive pinkish hue. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, and it produces a single, fragrant flower per stem.

Geodorum pierrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
