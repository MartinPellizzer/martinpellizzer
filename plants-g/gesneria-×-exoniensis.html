Gesneria × exoniensis

Gesneria × exoniensis, commonly known as the Exoniensis Gesneria, is a hybrid plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of vibrant orange-red tubular flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria × exoniensis is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
