Gnaphalium ivesii

Gnaphalium ivesii, commonly known as the Ives' everlasting, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, showy yellow flowers. The plant produces tiny, wind-dispersed seeds, which contribute to its ability to colonize disturbed areas. Native to the western United States, it thrives in dry, sunny environments.

Its foliage is covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
