Grewia chuniana

Grewia chuniana, commonly known as the Chinese globe-thistle, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.
