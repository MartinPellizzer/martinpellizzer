Gasteria inexpectata

Gasteria inexpectata, commonly known as the unexpected gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white to yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems, typically in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria inexpectata is popular as a low-maintenance houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
