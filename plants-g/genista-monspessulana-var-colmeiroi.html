Genista monspessulana var. colmeiroi

Genista monspessulana var. colmeiroi, commonly known as the Colmeiro's broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It features yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for coastal and dry environments.
