Gladiolus natalensis var. melleri

Gladiolus natalensis var. melleri, commonly known as the Natal gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant typically blooms in summer, with flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and adaptability to various growing conditions.
