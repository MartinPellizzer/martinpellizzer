Gerbera knorringiana

Gerbera knorringiana, commonly known as the Knorring's gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features vibrant, colorful petals in shades of red, yellow, and orange, with a central disk of yellow florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Gerbera knorringiana is also valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
