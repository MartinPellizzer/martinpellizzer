Gymnogramma argentea var. aurea

Gymnogramma argentea var. aurea, commonly known as the silver maidenhair fern, is a variant of the silver maidenhair fern native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae, characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant features bipinnate leaves with a striking silver or pale green coloration on the underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
