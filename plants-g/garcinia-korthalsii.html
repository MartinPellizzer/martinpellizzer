Garcinia korthalsii

Garcinia korthalsii, commonly known as the African mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, red, fleshy berries that contain a single seed, which is often used in traditional medicine.
