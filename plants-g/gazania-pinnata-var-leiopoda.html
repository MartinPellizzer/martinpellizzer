Gazania pinnata var. leiopoda

Gazania pinnata var. leiopoda, commonly known as the smooth gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The variety leiopoda is distinguished by its smooth, less hairy leaves compared to other gazania species.
