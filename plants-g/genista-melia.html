Genista melia

Genista melia, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring slender, often spiny stems. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the spring, which are borne on long, slender peduncles. Its leaves are small, linear, and opposite, while its fruit is a slender pod containing several seeds.

Genista melia is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as coastal areas and rocky soils.
