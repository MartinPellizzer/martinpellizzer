Gentiana burseri

Gentiana burseri, commonly known as the Burseri gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its vivid blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Gentiana burseri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
