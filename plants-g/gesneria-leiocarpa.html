Gesneria leiocarpa

Gesneria leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-leaved gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, glossy, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months.

Gesneria leiocarpa is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
