Gleichenia excelsa

Gleichenia excelsa, commonly known as the tall fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender fronds that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that form dense mats. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a distinctive, glossy appearance.

Gleichenia excelsa is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
