Gilbertiodendron straussianum

Gilbertiodendron straussianum, commonly known as the African teak, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The tree produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.

The plant also plays an important role in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.
