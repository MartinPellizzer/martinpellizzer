Gladiolus cuspidatus var. ensifolius

Gladiolus cuspidatus var. ensifolius, commonly known as the sword-leaved gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of vibrant colors. The leaves are long and narrow, giving the plant its distinctive "sword-like" appearance. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for warm climates.
