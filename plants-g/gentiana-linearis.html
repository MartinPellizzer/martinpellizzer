Gentiana linearis

Gentiana linearis, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and alpine meadows. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often reaching lengths of up to 15 centimeters.

Gentiana linearis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
