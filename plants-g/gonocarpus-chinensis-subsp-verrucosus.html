Gonocarpus chinensis subsp. verrucosus

Gonocarpus chinensis subsp.

verrucosus, commonly known as the rough-seeded goniostemon, is a member of the Malvaceae family. It is a perennial shrub characterized by its woody stems and palmate leaves with serrated margins.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by capsule fruits that are covered in warty projections. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, tropical environments.

Its unique floral and fruit morphology make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
