Godetia Macrantha

Godetia macrantha is a striking member of the Malvaceae family known for its vibrant yellow flowers and hairy stems. Native to the western United States, this plant thrives in dry, sunny environments. Its showy blooms attract a variety of pollinators, making it a valuable addition to native gardens. The plant's unique appearance and ecological benefits have made it a subject of interest among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
