Gladiolus blandus var. excelsus

Gladiolus blandus var. excelsus, commonly known as the noble gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically blooms in summer, producing spikes of vibrant, trumpet-shaped petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in many regions beyond its native habitat.
