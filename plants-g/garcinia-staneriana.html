Garcinia staneriana

Garcinia staneriana, commonly known as the red mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. The fruit's pericarp contains a high concentration of xanthones, which have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.

Garcinia staneriana thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.
