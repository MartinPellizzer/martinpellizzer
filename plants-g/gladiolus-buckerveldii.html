Gladiolus buckerveldii

Gladiolus buckerveldii, commonly known as the Buckerveld gladiolus, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that bear large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a terminal inflorescence composed of numerous florets.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
