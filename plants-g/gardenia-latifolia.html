Gardenia latifolia

Gardenia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are broad and elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, white to cream-colored flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia latifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
