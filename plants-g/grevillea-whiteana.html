Grevillea whiteana

Grevillea whiteana, commonly known as the White's Grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a reddish or yellowish hue.

Grevillea whiteana is well adapted to arid conditions, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
