Gladiolus alatus var. pulcherrimus

Gladiolus alatus var. pulcherrimus, commonly known as the handsome sword lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green stripes, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with large, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for borders and floral arrangements.

Its botanical name reflects its elegant appearance and the variability in its flower colors.
