Genista tricuspidata var. mogadorensis

Genista tricuspidata var. mogadorensis, commonly known as the Mogador broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and flowering structures. The plant features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets, and it produces pods that contain seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.
