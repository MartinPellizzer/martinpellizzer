Gentiana flaccida

Gentiana flaccida, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentiana flaccida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
