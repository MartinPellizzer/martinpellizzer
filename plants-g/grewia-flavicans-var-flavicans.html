Grewia flavicans var. flavicans

Grewia flavicans var. flavicans, commonly known as the yellow globemallow, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant yellow blooms and compact growth habit.
