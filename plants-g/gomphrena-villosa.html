Gomphrena villosa

Gomphrena villosa, commonly known as the hairy plume flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance.

Gomphrena villosa is also valued for its long-lasting blooms, which make it a favorite in floral arrangements.
