Gymnosporia linearis

Gymnosporia linearis, commonly known as the linear leaf burr bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant features. Its flowers are typically yellow and are followed by the development of woody, spiny fruits.

Gymnosporia linearis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
