Gouldia terminalis var. ovata

Gouldia terminalis var. ovata, commonly known as the oval gouldia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is suitable for rock gardens or containers.
