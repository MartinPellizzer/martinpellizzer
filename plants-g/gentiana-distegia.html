Gentiana distegia

Gentiana distegia, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, white down.

Gentiana distegia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
