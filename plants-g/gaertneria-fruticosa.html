Gaertneria fruticosa

Gaertneria fruticosa, commonly known as the tropical hibiscus, is a shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a prominent yellow or orange throat. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Gaertneria fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
