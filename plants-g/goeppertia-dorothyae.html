Goeppertia dorothyae

Goeppertia dorothyae, commonly known as Dorothy's Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, often featuring a mix of green, silver, and pink hues. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
