Gastrochilus minimus

Gastrochilus minimus, commonly known as the small gastrochilus, is a small orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
