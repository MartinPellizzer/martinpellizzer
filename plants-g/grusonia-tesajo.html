Grusonia tesajo

Grusonia tesajo, commonly known as the Tesajo flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves composed of multiple leaflets, which are typically opposite in arrangement. Its woody structure supports its role as a small to medium-sized tree in its native habitat.

Grusonia tesajo is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
