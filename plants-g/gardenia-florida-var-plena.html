Gardenia florida var. plena

Gardenia florida var. plena, commonly known as the double-flowered gardenia, is a cultivar of the gardenia plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, showy, double blooms that are typically white or pale yellow, with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves and produces yellowish-orange berries that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
