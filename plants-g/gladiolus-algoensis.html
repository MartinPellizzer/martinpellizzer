Gladiolus algoensis

Gladiolus algoensis, commonly known as the Algoenian sword lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, supporting vibrant, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with brightly colored flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Its botanical features include fibrous roots and a corm-like structure for storage.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
