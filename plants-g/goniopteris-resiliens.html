Goniopteris resiliens

Goniopteris resiliens, commonly known as the resilient shield fern, is a species of fern characterized by its large, robust fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, creeping rhizomes that help it spread across forest floors. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, shield-like appearance that gives it its common name.

Goniopteris resiliens thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
