Gentianopsis detonsa subsp. raupii

Gentianopsis detonsa subsp.

The subspecies raupii is distinguished by its compact growth habit and smaller, more intensely colored flowers compared to the typical form of G. detonsa. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
