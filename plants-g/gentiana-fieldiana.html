Gentiana fieldiana

Gentiana fieldiana, commonly known as the field gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in open woodlands or meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are upright and branched.

Gentiana fieldiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
