Gentianella tenuifolia

Gentianella tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
