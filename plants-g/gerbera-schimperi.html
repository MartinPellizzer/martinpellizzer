Gerbera schimperi

Gerbera schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian gerbera, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features bright yellow or orange petals with a central disk of darker coloration. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Gerbera schimperi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
