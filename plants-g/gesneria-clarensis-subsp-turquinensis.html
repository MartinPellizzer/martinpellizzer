Gesneria clarensis subsp. turquinensis

Gesneria clarensis subsp.

turquinensis, commonly known as the Turquin's gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the humid lowland forests of western Ecuador. It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and a deep green coloration.

The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and vibrant floral display.
