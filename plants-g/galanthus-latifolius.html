Galanthus latifolius

Galanthus latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf snowdrop, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It produces delicate, white flowers with green markings, typically blooming in early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in woodland or shady garden settings. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring display.

Galanthus latifolius is also noted for its ability to naturalize and spread over time.
