Grevillea subtiliflora

Grevillea subtiliflora, commonly known as the slender leaf grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid and coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Grevillea subtiliflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
