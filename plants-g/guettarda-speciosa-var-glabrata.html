Guettarda speciosa var. glabrata

Guettarda speciosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth guettarda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub characterized by its evergreen leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas and rainforests. Its flowers bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The smooth, glabrous leaves of this variety distinguish it from other guettarda species.
