Gardenia tubifera var. tubifera

Gardenia tubifera var. tubifera, commonly known as the fragrant gardenia, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, waxy flowers that emit a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.

The flowers are also popular in perfumery due to their intense aroma.
