Geum reptans var. macrophyllum

Geum reptans var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf bergamot, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features showy, pink to reddish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and often have a hairy texture. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and wetlands.
