Garcinia rigida

Garcinia rigida, commonly known as the hardy garcinia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Garcinia rigida thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it a unique species within the Garciniaceae family.
