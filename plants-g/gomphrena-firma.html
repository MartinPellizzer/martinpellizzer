Gomphrena firma

Gomphrena firma, commonly known as the everlasting flower or torch flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescences that resemble small torches. The plant is known for its long-lasting blooms, which retain their color and form even when dried. Gomphrena firma typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant colors make it suitable for both garden beds and container planting.
