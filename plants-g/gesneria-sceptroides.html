Gesneria sceptroides

Gesneria sceptroides, commonly known as the rock gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soil conditions, making it well-suited for rock gardens or container cultivation. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in summer and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Gesneria sceptroides is relatively low-maintenance, requiring bright indirect light and moderate watering to thrive.
