Gouldia terminalis var. lanai

Gouldia terminalis var. lanai, commonly known as the Lanai flame grass, is a striking ornamental plant native to Hawaii.

It features vibrant red and orange bracts that resemble flames, giving it its common name. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is prized for its eye-catching display and ability to add color to coastal landscapes.
