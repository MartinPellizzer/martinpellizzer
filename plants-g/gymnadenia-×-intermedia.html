Gymnadenia × intermedia

Gymnadenia × intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate grass of fire, is a hybrid orchid species native to Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a distinctive spurred lip.

Gymnadenia × intermedia is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
