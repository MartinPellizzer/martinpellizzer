Galianthe reitzii

Galianthe reitzii, commonly known as the Reitz's galianthe, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited natural range and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging plant to cultivate outside its native environment.
