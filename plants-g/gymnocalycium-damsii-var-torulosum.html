Gymnocalycium damsii var. torulosum

Gymnocalycium damsii var. torulosum, commonly known as the Torulose moon cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to South America.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its compact size and unique appearance.
