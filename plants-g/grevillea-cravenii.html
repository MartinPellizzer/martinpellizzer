Grevillea cravenii

Grevillea cravenii, commonly known as the Craven's Grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are bright green and covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces vibrant red and yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea cravenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds and insects.
