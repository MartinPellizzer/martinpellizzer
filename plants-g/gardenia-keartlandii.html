Gardenia keartlandii

Gardenia keartlandii, commonly known as the Kertland gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
