Grevillea forsteri

Grevillea forsteri, commonly known as the golden grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Grevillea forsteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
