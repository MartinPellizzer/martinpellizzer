Gentiana wutaiensis

Gentiana wutaiensis, commonly known as the Wutai gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, showy flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
