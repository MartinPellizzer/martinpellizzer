Gastrochilus fuscopunctatus

Gastrochilus fuscopunctatus, commonly known as the spotted pigmy orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact, branching structure with narrow, leathery leaves. Its flowers are small, usually pale green or yellowish, with intricate patterns of dark spots.

It is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
