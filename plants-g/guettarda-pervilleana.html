Guettarda pervilleana

Guettarda pervilleana, commonly known as the white guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal and wetland areas. The plant features simple, opposite leaves and produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in saline conditions.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils.
