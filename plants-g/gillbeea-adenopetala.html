Gillbeea adenopetala

Gillbeea adenopetala, commonly known as the silky gillbeea, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, silvery appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.

Gillbeea adenopetala is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
