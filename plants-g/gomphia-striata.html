Gomphia striata

Gomphia striata, commonly known as the striped gomphia, is a member of the Restionaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tufted, grass-like foliage with narrow, linear leaves that have distinct longitudinal stripes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
