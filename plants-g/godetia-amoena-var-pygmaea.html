Godetia amoena var. pygmaea

Godetia amoena var. pygmaea, commonly known as the dwarf godetia, is a compact annual plant native to South America.

It features small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces slender, branching stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
