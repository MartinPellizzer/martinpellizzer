Gymnocalycium kieslingii f. castaneum

Gymnocalycium kieslingii f. castaneum, commonly known as the red-fleshed moon cactus, is a unique hybrid cactus with a distinctive appearance.

It features a swollen, colorful stem that resembles a moon, often with a red or orange hue. The plant has a short, spiny stem and produces small, white flowers during the spring or summer. It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly Argentina.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often grown in gardens and as a houseplant due to its compact size and vibrant color.
