Grevillea purdieana

Grevillea purdieana, commonly known as the Red Grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It features vibrant red flowers that bloom in dense, cylindrical clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are gray-green in color, providing a contrast to its bold floral display. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Grevillea purdieana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and gardens.
