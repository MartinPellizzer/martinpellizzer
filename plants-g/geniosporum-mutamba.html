Geniosporum mutamba

Geniosporum mutamba, commonly known as the Mutamba orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate and often showy floral structures. The plant typically features tall, slender stems with clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its botanical significance lies in its specific habitat requirements and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to its rarity, it is often found in protected areas where its ecological role and genetic diversity can be studied and preserved.
