Glochidion heyneanum

Glochidion heyneanum, commonly known as the glossy leaf glochidion, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. Its small white flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are often followed by small, round fruits.

Glochidion heyneanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
