Geranium christensenianum

Geranium christensenianum, commonly known as the Christensen's geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and often have a hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
