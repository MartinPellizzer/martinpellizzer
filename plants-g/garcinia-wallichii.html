Garcinia wallichii

Garcinia wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan garcinia, is a small evergreen tree native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, red, fleshy berries that are edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes.
