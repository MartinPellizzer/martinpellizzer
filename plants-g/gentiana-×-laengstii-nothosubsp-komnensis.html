Gentiana × laengstii nothosubsp. komnensis

Gentiana × laengstii nothosubsp.

komnensis, commonly known as the Komnenus gentian, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and mountain meadows. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, five-petaled flowers.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
