Gesneria × egregia

Gesneria × egregia, commonly known as the hybrid gesneria, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Gesneria obtusa and Gesneria rigida.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its broad, glossy, dark green leaves and produces striking orange-red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria × egregia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
