Gastromeria physalodes

Gastromeria physalodes, commonly known as the balloon vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive inflated, balloon-like fruits that contain seeds. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a hairy texture.
