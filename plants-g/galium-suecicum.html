Galium suecicum

Galium suecicum, commonly known as Swedish bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb native to northern Europe.

It features slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Galium suecicum thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
