Guettarda abbottii

Guettarda abbottii, commonly known as the yellow guettarda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers. Its flowers are usually borne on short peduncles and are arranged in dense, terminal inflorescences.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
