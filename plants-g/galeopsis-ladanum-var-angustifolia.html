Galeopsis ladanum var. angustifolia

Galeopsis ladanum var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf hedge nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, erect stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in whorled clusters, which are typical of the Scrophulariaceae family. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in woodland edges.
