Gymnocalycium ochoterenae var. tenuispinum

Gymnocalycium ochoterenae var. tenuispinum, commonly known as the slender-spined moon cactus, is a succulent cactus native to Argentina.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem that is typically greenish-gray in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its spines are short and sparse, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
