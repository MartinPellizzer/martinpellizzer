Grevillea edelfeltii

Grevillea edelfeltii, commonly known as the pink mist grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, fluffy pink flower spikes that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea edelfeltii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
