Gymnema piperi

Gymnema piperi, commonly known as the Indian bitter melon, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, bitter-tasting fruits. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, green flowers that eventually develop into elongated, ribbed fruits. Gymnema piperi is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.

Its leaves and fruits are used to treat diabetes and other health conditions due to their hypoglycemic effects.
