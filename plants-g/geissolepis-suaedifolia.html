Geissolepis suaedifolia

Geissolepis suaedifolia, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming in summer. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.
