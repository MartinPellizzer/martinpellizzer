Gesneria velutina

Gesneria velutina, commonly known as the velvet gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that give it its common name. It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Gesneria velutina is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
