Grevillea diversifolia var. rigida

Grevillea diversifolia var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like foliage and vibrant, red or orange cylindrical flowers that appear in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments. Its unique appearance and ability to attract birds make it a popular choice for native landscaping.

The variety 'rigida' is distinguished by its more rigid, upright growth habit compared to other forms of Grevillea diversifolia.
