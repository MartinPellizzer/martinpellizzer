Goodyera schlechtendaliana var. velutina

Goodyera schlechtendaliana var. velutina, commonly known as the velvet millepore, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are thick and succulent, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
