Geniostoma batanense

Geniostoma batanense, commonly known as the Batan Island orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found exclusively on Batan Island in the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structures. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the rainy season. Its roots are adapted to thrive in the humid, shaded understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from deforestation and habitat loss.
