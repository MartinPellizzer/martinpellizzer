Gasteria dicta

Gasteria dicta, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of small, tubular, white or yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Gasteria dicta is valued for its ornamental appearance and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
