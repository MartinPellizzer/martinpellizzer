Gillenia stipulata var. variegata

Gillenia stipulata var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated gillenia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has palmately lobed leaves with a distinctive variegated pattern of green and white. It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
