Grevillea dimorpha var. lanceolata

Grevillea dimorpha var. lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically grey-green in color. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and full sun exposure.

Grevillea dimorpha var. lanceolata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
