Genista salzmannii subsp. parnassica

Genista salzmannii subsp.

parnassica, commonly known as the Parnassian bursaria, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by distinctive bur-like seed pods. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
