Geranium atropurpureum

Geranium atropurpureum, commonly known as purpleleaf geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a distinctive purplish hue, especially under stress or in full sun. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full to partial sunlight.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
