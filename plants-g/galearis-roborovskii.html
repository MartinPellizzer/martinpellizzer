Galearis roborovskii

Galearis roborovskii, commonly known as the Roborovskii lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its low-growing habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Galearis roborovskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
