Gerardia filiformis

Gerardia filiformis, commonly known as the slender gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand saline conditions.

Gerardia filiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
