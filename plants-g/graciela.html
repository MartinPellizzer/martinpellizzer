Graciela

Graciela, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its vibrant purple stems and waxy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers, which are followed by orange-red berries. Graciela thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
