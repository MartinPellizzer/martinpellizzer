Grandiphyllum divaricatum

Grandiphyllum divaricatum, commonly known as the variable legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities.
