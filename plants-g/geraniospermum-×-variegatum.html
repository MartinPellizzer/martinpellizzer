Geraniospermum × variegatum

Geraniospermum × variegatum, commonly known as the variegated geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves that display a mix of green, white, and sometimes pink or red hues. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with sturdy stems and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink or red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
