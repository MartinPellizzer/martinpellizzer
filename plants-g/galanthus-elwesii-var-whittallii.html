Galanthus elwesii var. whittallii

Galanthus elwesii var. whittallii, commonly known as the Whittall's snowdrop, is a rare and striking variety of the snowdrop genus.

It is characterized by its pale green to white flowers with a distinctive yellow-green shield at the base of the petals. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade. Its bulbs are perennial, and it typically blooms in early spring.

The variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and botanical uniqueness.
