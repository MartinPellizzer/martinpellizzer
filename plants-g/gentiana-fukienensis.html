Gentiana fukienensis

Gentiana fukienensis, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are usually unbranched.

Gentiana fukienensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
