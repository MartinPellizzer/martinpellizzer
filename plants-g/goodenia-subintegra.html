Goodenia subintegra

Goodenia subintegra, commonly known as the smooth goodenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive basal rosette formation.

Goodenia subintegra is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats.
