Gesneria cynocephala

Gesneria cynocephala, commonly known as the monkey head gesneria, is a tropical flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage is often bright green with a waxy texture, and it produces vibrant orange or red flowers during the warmer months.

Gesneria cynocephala is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
