Gagea subtilis, commonly known as the slender gagea, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
