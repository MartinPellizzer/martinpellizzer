Glandularia Mendocina

Glandularia mendocina is a small flowering plant native to the western United States. It is known for its delicate pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in rocky and dry habitats, often found in coastal areas. Its scientific name honors the Mendocino region where it is commonly found. Glandularia mendocina is an important part of local ecosystems and is often overlooked despite its beauty.
