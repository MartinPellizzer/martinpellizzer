Goodenia arachnoidea

Goodenia arachnoidea, commonly known as the spidergoodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, hairy, spider-like appearance. The plant features wiry stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Goodenia arachnoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
