Gesneria viridiflora subsp. viridiflora

Gesneria viridiflora subsp.

viridiflora, commonly known as the Emerald-flowered Gesneria, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America. It is a compact, evergreen shrub with broad, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins.

The plant produces clusters of small, bright green flowers that are unique for their coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. Gesneria viridiflora subsp.

viridiflora is popular among indoor gardeners due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
