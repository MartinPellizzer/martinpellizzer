Gnaphalium subundulatum

Gnaphalium subundulatum, commonly known as the slender leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are often mistaken for individual flowers, and it is known for its delicate, feathery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky areas.

The plant is also valued for its use in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms.
