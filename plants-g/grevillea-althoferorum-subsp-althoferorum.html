Grevillea althoferorum subsp. althoferorum

Grevillea althoferorum subsp.

althoferorum, commonly known as the Althofer's grevillea, is a shrub native to western Australia. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers in summer. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in full sun.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
