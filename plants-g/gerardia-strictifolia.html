Gerardia strictifolia

Gerardia strictifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
