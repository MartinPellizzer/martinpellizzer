Grandiphyllum schunkeanum

Grandiphyllum schunkeanum, commonly known as the Schunke's grandiphyllum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a woody stem and is often found in tropical and subtropical environments. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a rough texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm climates.
