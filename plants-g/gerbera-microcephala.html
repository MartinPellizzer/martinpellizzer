Gerbera microcephala

Gerbera microcephala, commonly known as the miniature gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with vibrant colors such as red, yellow, and orange. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Gerbera microcephala thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal maintenance once established.
