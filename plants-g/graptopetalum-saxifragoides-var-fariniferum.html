Graptopetalum saxifragoides var. fariniferum

Graptopetalum saxifragoides var. fariniferum, commonly known as the Silver Dust Jade, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in a fine, powdery coating that gives it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
