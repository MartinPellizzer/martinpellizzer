Goniothalamus borneensis

Goniothalamus borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean moonseed, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain multiple seeds, typical of the Annonaceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
