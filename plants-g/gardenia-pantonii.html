Gardenia pantonii

Gardenia pantonii, commonly known as the Panton's gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain black seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia pantonii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
