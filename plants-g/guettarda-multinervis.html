Guettarda multinervis

Guettarda multinervis, commonly known as the silverleaf guettarda, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Guettarda multinervis is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
