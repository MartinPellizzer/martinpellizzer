Galactia simaoensis

Galactia simaoensis, commonly known as the Simao milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its seeds are encased in pods, a typical feature of the legume family.

Galactia simaoensis is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to challenging environments.
