Goeppertia plicata

Goeppertia plicata, commonly known as the Zebra Canna, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pleated leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers with yellow centers, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Goeppertia plicata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses due to its vibrant foliage and floral display.
