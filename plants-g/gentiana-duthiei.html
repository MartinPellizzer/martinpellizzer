Gentiana duthiei

Gentiana duthiei, commonly known as the Duthie's gentian, is a species of flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Alps.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of down.

Gentiana duthiei is a perennial herb that plays a role in the ecological diversity of its mountainous habitat.
