Goodenia pilosa subsp. pilosa

Goodenia pilosa subsp.

pilosa, commonly known as the hairy goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
