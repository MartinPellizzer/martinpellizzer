Geniosporum prostratum

Geniosporum prostratum, commonly known as the prostrate geniosporum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features spreading, fleshy stems that often lie close to the ground, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow, pointed, and covered in a fine, waxy coating.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
