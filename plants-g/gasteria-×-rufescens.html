Gasteria × rufescens

Gasteria × rufescens, commonly known as the red-flowered gasteria, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with red or purple markings along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, white to pale purple flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Gasteria × rufescens is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
