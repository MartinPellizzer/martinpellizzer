Gardenia trochainii

Gardenia trochainii, commonly known as the Madagascar gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces yellow-orange berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia trochainii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and strong, sweet fragrance.
