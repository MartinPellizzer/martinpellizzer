Gardenia gummifera var. gummiferoides

Gardenia gummifera var. gummiferoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gardenia, is a variant of the gardenia genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces a yellowish resinous substance, which is a distinguishing feature of the species. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique botanical traits.
