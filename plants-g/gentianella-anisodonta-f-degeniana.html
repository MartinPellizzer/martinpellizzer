Gentianella anisodonta f. degeniana

Gentianella anisodonta f. degeniana, commonly known as the Degen's gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky outcrops. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
