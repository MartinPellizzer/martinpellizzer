Genista spicata

Genista spicata, commonly known as spiny broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Genista spicata is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
