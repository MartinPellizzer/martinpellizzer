Geranium yeoi

Geranium yeoi, commonly known as the Korean geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly South Korea.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. Geranium yeoi thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its appeal in horticultural settings.
