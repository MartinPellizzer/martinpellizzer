Geracium aureum

Geracium aureum, commonly known as the golden geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and hairy, succulent leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Geracium aureum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking color and resilience.
