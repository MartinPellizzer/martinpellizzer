Gymnocalycium mostii var. immemoratum

Gymnocalycium mostii var. immemoratum, commonly known as the Immemoratum Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Argentina.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that form a compact, globular shape. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is a succulent, adapted to store water in its fleshy stems.

This variety is relatively rare and requires specific care to thrive in cultivation.
