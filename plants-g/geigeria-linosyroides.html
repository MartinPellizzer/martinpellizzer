Geigeria linosyroides

Geigeria linosyroides, commonly known as the slenderleaf geigeria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
