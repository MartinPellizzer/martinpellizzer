Gomesa itapetingensis

Gomesa itapetingensis, commonly known as the Itapetinga gomesa, is a rare flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact shrub form and produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and early summer. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in humid, tropical climates.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists.
