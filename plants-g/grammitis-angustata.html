Grammitis angustata

Grammitis angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf grammitis, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
