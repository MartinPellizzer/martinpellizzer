Gladiolus loteniensis

Gladiolus loteniensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian gladiolus, is a species native to Ethiopia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and striking, showy flowers. The flowers typically bloom in shades of red, yellow, and white, and they are arranged in dense spikes. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its potential in horticultural research.
