Galium ellipticum var. gredensis

Galium ellipticum var. gredensis, commonly known as Gredens' bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb native to specific regions of Europe.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, elliptic-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and slightly succulent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
