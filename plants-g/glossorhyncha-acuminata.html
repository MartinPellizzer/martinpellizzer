Glossorhyncha acuminata

Glossorhyncha acuminata, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or undergrowth. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, and the flowers are often pale green or yellowish in color.
