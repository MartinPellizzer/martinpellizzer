Gilesia

Gilesia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a unique species belonging to the family Arecaceae.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant typically grows as a small shrub or tree, with a compact and rounded shape. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, often arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

Gilesia is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
