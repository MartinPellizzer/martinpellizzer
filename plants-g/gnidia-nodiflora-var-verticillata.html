Gnidia nodiflora var. verticillata

Gnidia nodiflora var. verticillata, commonly known as the clustered gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a woody stem with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
