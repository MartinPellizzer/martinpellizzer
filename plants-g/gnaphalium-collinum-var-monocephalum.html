Gnaphalium collinum var. monocephalum

Gnaphalium collinum var. monocephalum, commonly known as the single-headed cudweed, is a flowering plant native to the central and southern regions of the United States.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky soils, and it is often found in disturbed areas.
