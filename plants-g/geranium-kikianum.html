Geranium kikianum

Geranium kikianum, commonly known as the Kikian's geranium, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink to red flowers with five petals, blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
