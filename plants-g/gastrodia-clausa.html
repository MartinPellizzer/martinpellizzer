Gastrodia clausa

Gastrodia clausa, commonly known as the Chinese club moss, is a rare and endangered plant species native to the mountainous regions of China.

The plant is often found in shaded, moist environments, typically in forest undergrowth where it thrives in cool and humid conditions. Gastrodia clausa is notable for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in Chinese herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
