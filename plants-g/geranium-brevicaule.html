Geranium brevicaule

Geranium brevicaule, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically narrow and hairy, providing a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
