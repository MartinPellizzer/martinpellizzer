Goeppertia zebrina

Goeppertia zebrina, commonly known as the Zebra Canna, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a zebra-like pattern of green and white. The plant produces large, showy orange and red flowers that bloom in summer. It prefers warm, humid conditions and well-draining soil.

Goeppertia zebrina is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and floral display.
