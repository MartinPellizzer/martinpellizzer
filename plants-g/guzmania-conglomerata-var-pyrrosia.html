Guzmania conglomerata var. pyrrosia

Guzmania conglomerata var. pyrrosia, commonly known as the pyrrosia guzmania, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can range from red to orange, often resembling a torch. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms and small invertebrates. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and can display a variety of colors, including green, silver, and variegated tones.

This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.
