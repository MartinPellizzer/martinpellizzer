Grammitis subulatipinna

Grammitis subulatipinna, commonly known as the slender needle fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, needle-like fronds that are typically arranged in a dense, compact rosette. Grammitis subulatipinna thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in humid habitats.
