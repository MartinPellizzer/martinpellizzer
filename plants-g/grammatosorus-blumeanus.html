Grammatosorus blumeanus

Grammatosorus blumeanus, commonly known as the Blume's fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the tropical regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, giving it a finely divided appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocky substrates or in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
