Goodenia verreauxii

Goodenia verreauxii, commonly known as the mountain goodenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia verreauxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
