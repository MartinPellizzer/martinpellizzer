Gnetum ula

Gnetum ula, commonly known as the climbing gnetum, is a tropical plant belonging to the Gnetophyta division.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring scale-like leaves and a complex vascular system. The plant typically grows as a vine, climbing using its twining stems. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is pollinated by insects.

Gnetum ula is found in the rainforests of Southeast Asia and is notable for its role in ecological systems.
