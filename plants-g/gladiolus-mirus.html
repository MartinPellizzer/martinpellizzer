Gladiolus mirus

Gladiolus mirus, commonly known as the magnificent gladiolus, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow, often with a distinctive, forward-curving spur.

Gladiolus mirus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
