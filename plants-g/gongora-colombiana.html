Gongora colombiana

Gongora colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian gongora, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, dark purple to black flowers with intricate patterns of white and green. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its native environment.

Gongora colombiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
