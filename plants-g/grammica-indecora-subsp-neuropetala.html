Grammica indecora subsp. neuropetala

Grammica indecora subsp.

neuropetala, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grammica, is a herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It features slender, ascending stems with lanceolate leaves that are typically hairy on the underside.

The flowers are small and arranged in clusters, with petals that are often pink or pale purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky outcrops.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrower leaves and slightly different floral morphology compared to other subspecies of Grammica.
