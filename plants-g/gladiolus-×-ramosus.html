Gladiolus × ramosus

Gladiolus × ramosus, commonly known as the garden gladiolus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, yellow, and white. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a fan shape.

Gladiolus × ramosus is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.
