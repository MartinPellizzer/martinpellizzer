Grewia heteroclita

Grewia heteroclita, commonly known as the African starwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape and often have a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round, orange-red fruits. The fruits are edible and have a tart flavor, often used in local cuisines.

Grewia heteroclita is well adapted to arid conditions and thrives in full sun.
