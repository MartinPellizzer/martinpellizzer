Galactia striata

Galactia striata, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to the Americas.

It produces vibrant red or orange flowers that are followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds. The plant has trifoliate leaves and is characterized by its vigorous growth habit. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Galactia striata is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which enriches the soil.
