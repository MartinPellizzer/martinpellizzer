Globularia castellana

Globularia castellana, commonly known as the Castilian globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky inflorescence and is typically found in rocky or limestone soils. The plant produces blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental appearance.

Globularia castellana is also valued for its ability to thrive in dry, sunny conditions.
