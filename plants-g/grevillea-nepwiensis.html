Grevillea nepwiensis

Grevillea nepwiensis, commonly known as the Darling Range Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and bright red or orange flowers that appear in dense, cylindrical clusters. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun conditions. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats within the Darling Range.

Grevillea nepwiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
