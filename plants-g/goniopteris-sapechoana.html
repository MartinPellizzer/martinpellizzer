Goniopteris sapechoana

It is characterized by its large, delicate fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, almost lace-like appearance. Native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its unique morphology and ecological preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
