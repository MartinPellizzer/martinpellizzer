Goodenia sessiliflora

Goodenia sessiliflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions with a Mediterranean climate.

Goodenia sessiliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
