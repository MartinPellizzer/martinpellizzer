Geranium fiebrigianum

Geranium fiebrigianum, commonly known as the Cape Geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and prolonged flowering period.
