Greggia purpurea

Greggia purpurea, commonly known as the purple greggia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its vibrant purple petals are surrounded by pale yellow ray florets, creating a striking visual display.

Greggia purpurea is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness.
