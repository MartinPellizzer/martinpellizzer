Globularia linifolia subsp. hispanica

Globularia linifolia subsp.

hispanica, commonly known as Spanish globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is a low-growing perennial herb with dense, globular clusters of small, white flowers.

The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for Mediterranean-style gardens.
