Grevillea triternata

Grevillea triternata, commonly known as the Three-leafed Grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in threes, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Grevillea triternata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
