Gladiolus kirkii

Gladiolus kirkii, commonly known as Kirk's gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink to red blooms, which are often used in horticultural displays. The leaves are long and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
