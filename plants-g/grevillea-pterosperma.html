Grevillea pterosperma

Grevillea pterosperma, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal regions and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid climates.

Grevillea pterosperma is a hardy shrub that is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
