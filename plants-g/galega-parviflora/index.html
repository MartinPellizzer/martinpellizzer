Galega Parviflora

Galega parviflora is a hardy perennial plant known for its resilience in poor soil conditions. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or disturbed areas. This species is characterized by its small, pale purple flowers and slender, creeping stems. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators. Its historical use in traditional medicine adds to its ecological and cultural significance.
