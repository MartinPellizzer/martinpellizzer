Gonolobus jamaicensis

Gonolobus jamaicensis, commonly known as Jamaican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces violet to blue flowers that open in the morning and close in the afternoon. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.
