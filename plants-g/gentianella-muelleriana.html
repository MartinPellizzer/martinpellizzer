Gentianella muelleriana

Gentianella muelleriana, commonly known as Mueller's gentian, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Gentianella muelleriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
