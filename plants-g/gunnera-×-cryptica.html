Gunnera × cryptica

Gunnera × cryptica, commonly known as the giant rhubarb, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gunneraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant is native to South America and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its striking foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and wetlands.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red in color.
