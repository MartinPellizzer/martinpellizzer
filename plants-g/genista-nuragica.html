Genista nuragica

Genista nuragica, commonly known as the Sardinian brier, is a shrub native to the island of Sardinia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant has thorny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky and coastal environments, adapting well to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.

Genista nuragica plays a role in local ecosystems by providing shelter and food for various insects and birds.
