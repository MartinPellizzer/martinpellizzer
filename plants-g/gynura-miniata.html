Gynura miniata

Gynura miniata, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silver leaf plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, velvety, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads. Gynura miniata typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
