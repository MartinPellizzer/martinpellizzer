Grabowskia glauca

Grabowskia glauca, commonly known as the bluebush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing shrub form with dense foliage. The leaves are typically blue-green in color, giving the plant its common name. It produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
