Gardenia pallens

Gardenia pallens, commonly known as the white gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is evergreen and thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its beauty and pleasant scent.

Gardenia pallens requires partial shade and regular watering to maintain its health and bloom.
