Glyceria borreri var. erecta

Glyceria borreri var. erecta, commonly known as the upright glyceria, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils along stream banks and wetlands. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
