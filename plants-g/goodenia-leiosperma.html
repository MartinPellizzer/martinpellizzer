Goodenia leiosperma

Goodenia leiosperma, commonly known as the smooth-fruited goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and clustered leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

Goodenia leiosperma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
