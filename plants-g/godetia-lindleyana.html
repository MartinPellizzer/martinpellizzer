Godetia lindleyana

Godetia lindleyana, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Godetia lindleyana is often grown as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and ease of cultivation.
