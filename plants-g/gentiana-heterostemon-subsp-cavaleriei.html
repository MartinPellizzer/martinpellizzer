Gentiana heterostemon subsp. cavaleriei

Gentiana heterostemon subsp.

cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its stems are erect and branching.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
