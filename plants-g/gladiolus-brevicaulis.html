Gladiolus brevicaulis

Gladiolus brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-stemmed gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, spike-like inflorescences with vividly colored flowers. Its flowers come in a range of hues, including red, pink, and white, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated for both decorative and horticultural purposes.
