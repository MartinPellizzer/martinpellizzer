Genista florida f. angustifolia

Genista florida f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf flowering broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are narrow and linear, giving the plant a delicate, grass-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

This variety is a compact form of the species, often prized for its ornamental value and hardiness.
