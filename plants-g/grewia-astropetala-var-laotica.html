Grewia astropetala var. laotica

Grewia astropetala var. laotica, commonly known as the Laotian starfruit tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by fruits that are typically orange or red when ripe. The fruits are edible, with a sweet and tangy flavor, and are often consumed fresh or used in culinary preparations.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas or along riverbanks.
