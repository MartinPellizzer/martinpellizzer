Genista numidica subsp. ischnoclada

Genista numidica subsp.

ischnoclada, commonly known as the Numidian broom, is a shrub native to North Africa and the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, woody pods that contain seeds. It is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in arid landscapes.
