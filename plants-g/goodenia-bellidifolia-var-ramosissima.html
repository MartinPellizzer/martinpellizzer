Goodenia bellidifolia var. ramosissima

Goodenia bellidifolia var. ramosissima, commonly known as the silky goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs. This species thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal areas.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for native pollinators.
