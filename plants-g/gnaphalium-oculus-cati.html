Gnaphalium oculus-cati

Gnaphalium oculus-cati, commonly known as cat's eye plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually linear and grass-like, while the flowers bloom in clusters.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
