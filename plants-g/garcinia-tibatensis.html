Garcinia tibatensis

Garcinia tibatensis, commonly known as the Tibatense Garcinia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces orange-red berries that contain a high concentration of bioactive compounds, including hydroxycitric acid. Garcinia tibatensis is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits and is also studied for its role in natural weight management.

Its unique chemical composition makes it an interesting subject in both botanical and pharmacological research.
