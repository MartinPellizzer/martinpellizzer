Graffenrieda grandifolia

Graffenrieda grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered graffenrieda, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches. These flowers are rich in nectar and attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
