Gazania serrulata

Gazania serrulata, commonly known as the serrated gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange daisy-like flowers with white centers and serrated leaf margins. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Gazania serrulata is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
