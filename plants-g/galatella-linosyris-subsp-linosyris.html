Galatella linosyris subsp. linosyris

Galatella linosyris subsp.

linosyris, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and green.

The plant typically grows on rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
