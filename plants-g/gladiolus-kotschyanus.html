Gladiolus kotschyanus

Gladiolus kotschyanus, commonly known as the Kotschyan gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms in the summer months and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are long and narrow, arranged in a fan-like pattern around the stem.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
