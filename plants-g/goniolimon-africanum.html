Goniolimon africanum

Goniolimon africanum, commonly known as the African limon.

It is a succulent shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its harsh environment. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage.

Goniolimon africanum is valued for its resilience and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
