Geranium schlechteri

Geranium schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually palmately lobed, with a rough, hairy texture.

Geranium schlechteri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
