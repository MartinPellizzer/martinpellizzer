Gilbertiodendron mayombense

Gilbertiodendron mayombense, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by flat, pod-like fruits containing seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
