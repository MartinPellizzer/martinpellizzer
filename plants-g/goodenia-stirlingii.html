Goodenia stirlingii

Goodenia stirlingii, commonly known as Stirling's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Stirling's goodenia is often found in open habitats such as shrublands and woodland areas.
