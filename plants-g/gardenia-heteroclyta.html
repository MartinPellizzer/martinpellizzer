Gardenia heteroclyta

Gardenia heteroclyta, commonly known as the African gardenia, is a tropical shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces yellowish-orange berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia heteroclyta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
