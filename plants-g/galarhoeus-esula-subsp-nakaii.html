Galarhoeus esula subsp. nakaii

Galarhoeus esula subsp.

nakaii, commonly known as the Nakai's bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer.
