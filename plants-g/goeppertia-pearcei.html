Goeppertia pearcei

Goeppertia pearcei, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a striking aroid plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features broad, dark green, waxy leaves that are often variegated with creamy or white markings, giving it a luxurious appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by spathes containing berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia pearcei is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
