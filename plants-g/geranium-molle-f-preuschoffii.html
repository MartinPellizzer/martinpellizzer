Geranium molle f. preuschoffii

Geranium molle f. preuschoffii, commonly known as the soft geranium or velvet geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Native to southern Europe and the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.

The species is known for its compact growth habit and ability to withstand mild frost.
