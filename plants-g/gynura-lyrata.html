Gynura lyrata

Gynura lyrata, commonly known as the singing gynura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warm season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Gynura lyrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
