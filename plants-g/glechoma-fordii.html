Glechoma fordii

Glechoma fordii, commonly known as the Chinese ground ivy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, pale blue or violet flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Glechoma fordii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
