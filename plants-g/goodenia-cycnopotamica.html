Goodenia cycnopotamica

Goodenia cycnopotamica, commonly known as the river goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or seasonally wet environments, such as riverbanks and damp soils. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue or violet in color, arranged in spikes. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight hairy texture.

Goodenia cycnopotamica plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms in its native habitat.
