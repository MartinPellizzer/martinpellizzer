Germanea grallata

Germanea grallata, commonly known as the feathered fern, is a delicate and ornamental plant native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, arching fronds that give it a soft, feathery appearance, making it a popular choice for shaded garden settings. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly, forming dense mats of foliage.

Germanea grallata is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscape design to add texture and visual interest to woodland gardens.
