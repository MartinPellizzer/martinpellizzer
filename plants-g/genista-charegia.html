Genista charegia

Genista charegia, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Genista charegia is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility in degraded areas.
