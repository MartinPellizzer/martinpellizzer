Galeola humblotii

Galeola humblotii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces delicate, pale yellow to greenish flowers. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with red or purple spots.

Galeola humblotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
