Gymnadenia keiskei var. kinoshitae

Gymnadenia keiskei var. kinoshitae, commonly known as the Japanese marsh orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
