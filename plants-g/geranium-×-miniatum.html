Geranium × miniatum

Geranium × miniatum, commonly known as the mini geranium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces small, five-petaled blooms. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.

Geranium × miniatum prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.
