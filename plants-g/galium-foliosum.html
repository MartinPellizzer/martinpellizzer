Galium foliosum

Galium foliosum, commonly known as slender bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has slender, ascending stems covered in fine hairs and opposite, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Galium foliosum is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is a common component of early successional plant communities.
