Gymnotrix nervosa

Gymnotrix nervosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Gymnotrix nervosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

It has been used traditionally in some regions for its medicinal properties.
