Grubbia hirsuta

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Grubbia hirsuta is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in rocky and dry habitats.
