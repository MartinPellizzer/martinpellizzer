Gardenia hexagona

Gardenia hexagona, commonly known as the hexagonal gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, hexagonally shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces waxy, yellowish fruits that contain black, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to full sun.

Gardenia hexagona is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
