Geranium alnifolium

Geranium alnifolium, commonly known as the alderleaf geranium, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, woodland environments and is often found in shaded areas with well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
