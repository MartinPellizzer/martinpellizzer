Gloxinia fruticosa

Gloxinia fruticosa, commonly known as the evergreen gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Gloxinia fruticosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
