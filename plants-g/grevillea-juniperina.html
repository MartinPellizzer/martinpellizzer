Grevillea juniperina

Grevillea juniperina, commonly known as the Juniperina Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically grey-green in color. The plant produces striking, bottlebrush-like flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a hardy choice for dry gardens.

Grevillea juniperina is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
