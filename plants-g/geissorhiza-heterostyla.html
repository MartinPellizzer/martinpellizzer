Geissorhiza heterostyla

Geissorhiza heterostyla, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces showy, violet-colored flowers in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both garden cultivation and indoor planting.
