Goldfussia austini

Goldfussia austini, commonly known as the Austin goldfussia, is a striking perennial plant native to the central United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from golden yellow to orange-red. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet, with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Goldfussia austini is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
