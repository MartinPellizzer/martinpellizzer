Gardenia philastrei

Gardenia philastrei, commonly known as the Philippine gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a pleasant fragrance. The plant produces small, white flowers with a strong, sweet scent, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia philastrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
