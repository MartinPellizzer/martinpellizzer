Gasteria intermedia var. longior

Gasteria intermedia var. longior, commonly known as the Longleaf Gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow marginal teeth. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements in landscaping and indoor settings.
