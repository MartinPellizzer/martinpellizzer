Gouldia lanceolata

Gouldia lanceolata, commonly known as the slender gouldia, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, adding a unique texture to the landscape.

Gouldia lanceolata is known for its long blooming period, often extending from late spring through early autumn.
