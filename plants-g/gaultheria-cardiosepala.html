Gaultheria cardiosepala

Gaultheria cardiosepala, commonly known as the broadleaf wintergreen, is a species of shrub native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

The plant produces bright red berries that are edible and have a sharp, peppery flavor, similar to the related Gaultheria species. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude forests and mountainous regions.
