Goniophlebium benguetense

Goniophlebium benguetense, commonly known as the Benguet fern, is a species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that aids in its identification.
