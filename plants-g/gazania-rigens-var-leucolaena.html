Gazania rigens var. leucolaena

Gazania rigens var. leucolaena, commonly known as the white gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers with white centers. The plant features a low-growing, spreading habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual display to gardens.
