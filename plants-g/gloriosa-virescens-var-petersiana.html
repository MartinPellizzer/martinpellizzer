Gloriosa virescens var. petersiana

Gloriosa virescens var. petersiana, commonly known as the Petersiana Flame Snake Plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, orange-red spathe that resembles a flame. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
