Gilibertia saururoides

Gilibertia saururoides, commonly known as the red sand mat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It features a sprawling growth habit with fleshy, succulent leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its fibrous roots help it survive in nutrient-poor soils and withstand periods of drought.

Gilibertia saururoides plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for small desert organisms.
