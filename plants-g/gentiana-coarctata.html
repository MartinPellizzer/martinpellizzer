Gentiana coarctata

Gentiana coarctata, commonly known as the narrowleaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Gentiana coarctata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vivid floral display.
