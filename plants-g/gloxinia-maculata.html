Gloxinia maculata

Gloxinia maculata, commonly known as the spotted gloxinia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with distinctive red or purple markings. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Gloxinia maculata is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value.
