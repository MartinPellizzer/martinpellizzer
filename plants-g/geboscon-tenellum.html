Geboscon tenellum

Geboscon tenellum, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, smooth seed capsules. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.
