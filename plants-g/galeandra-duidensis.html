Galeandra duidensis

Galeandra duidensis, commonly known as the Duid's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, organic soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
