Gentiana holosteoides

Gentiana holosteoides, commonly known as the water gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Gentiana holosteoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
