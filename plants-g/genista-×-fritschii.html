Genista × fritschii

Genista × fritschii, commonly known as the hybrid Spanish broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as coastal areas and disturbed soils. It produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
