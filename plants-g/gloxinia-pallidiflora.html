Gloxinia pallidiflora

Gloxinia pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered gloxinia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect light. Its blooming period typically occurs in the spring and early summer.

Gloxinia pallidiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
