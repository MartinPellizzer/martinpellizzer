Goodenia cycloptera

Goodenia cycloptera, commonly known as the warty goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, warty, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions.

Goodenia cycloptera is well adapted to arid conditions and is an important component of native plant communities.
