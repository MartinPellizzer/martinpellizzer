Genista anglica var. heterophylla

Genista anglica var. heterophylla, commonly known as the English broom variant, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with three leaflets that are usually narrow and pointed. This plant is native to Europe and thrives in sandy or calcareous soils.

It is often found in coastal areas and is known for its hardy nature and ability to colonize disturbed sites.
