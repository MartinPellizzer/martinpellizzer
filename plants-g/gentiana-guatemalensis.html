Gentiana guatemalensis

Gentiana guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan gentian, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in the summer and are often visited by pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Gentiana guatemalensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
