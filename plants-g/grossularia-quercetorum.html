Grossularia quercetorum

Grossularia quercetorum, commonly known as the European mountain ash, is a species of shrub belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red berries in late summer. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
