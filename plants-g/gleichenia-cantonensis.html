Gleichenia cantonensis

Gleichenia cantonensis, commonly known as the Guangdong fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its robust, creeping rhizomes and dense, evergreen fronds. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, lanceolate shape and a glossy green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its lush foliage.
