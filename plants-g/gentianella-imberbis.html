Gentianella imberbis

Gentianella imberbis, commonly known as the smooth gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Gentianaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in moist meadows and grasslands. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentianella imberbis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
