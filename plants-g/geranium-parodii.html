Geranium parodii

Geranium parodii, commonly known as the Parodii geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Geranium parodii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
