Goodyera dongchenii var. gongligongensis

Goodyera dongchenii var. gongligongensis, commonly known as the Gongligong orchid, is a rare and delicate epiphytic orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive violet hue. The plant thrives in cool, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks in humid forests. Its flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a touch of elegance to its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in its native range.
