Goodenia discophora

Goodenia discophora, commonly known as the small-flowered goodenia, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, while its stems are erect and branching.

Goodenia discophora is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
