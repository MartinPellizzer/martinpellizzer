Geranium franchetii

Geranium franchetii, commonly known as the Chinese geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and palmate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Geranium franchetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
