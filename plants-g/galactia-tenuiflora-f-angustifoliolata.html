Galactia tenuiflora f. angustifoliolata

Galactia tenuiflora f. angustifoliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkweed, is a variant of the species Galactia tenuiflora.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of North America, particularly the southwestern United States. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, clustered white flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a distinctive, elongated shape.

This plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
