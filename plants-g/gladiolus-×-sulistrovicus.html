Gladiolus × sulistrovicus

Gladiolus × sulistrovicus, commonly known as the Sulistrovic hybrid gladiolus, is a botanical hybrid resulting from crosses between different gladiolus species.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with lanceolate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant colors and long-lasting blooms.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
