Gesneria pauciflora

Gesneria pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub features thick, glossy, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months.

Gesneria pauciflora is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
