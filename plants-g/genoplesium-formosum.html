Genoplesium formosum

Genoplesium formosum, commonly known as the Orange Spider-Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking orange and yellow flowers, which resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within rainforests or under dense shrubs. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Genoplesium formosum is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate and ephemeral blooms.
