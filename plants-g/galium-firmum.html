Galium firmum

Galium firmum, commonly known as stiffbedstraw, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

Galium firmum has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, three-lobed fruits.
