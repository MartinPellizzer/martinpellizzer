Gymnocalycium erolesii

Gymnocalycium erolesii, commonly known as the Erolesii moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that are typically green or blue-green in color. The plant produces showy, white to yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
