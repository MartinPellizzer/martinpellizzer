Grisia vieillardii

Grisia vieillardii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant features fleshy, dark green leaves that are often velvety to the touch. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Grisia vieillardii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
