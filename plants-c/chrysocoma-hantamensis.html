Chrysocoma hantamensis

Chrysocoma hantamensis, commonly known as the Hantam rock rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that help reduce water loss.

Chrysocoma hantamensis plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
