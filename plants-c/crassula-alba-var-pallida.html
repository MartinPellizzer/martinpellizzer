Crassula alba var. pallida

Crassula alba var. pallida, commonly known as the silver crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are pale blue or silver in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
