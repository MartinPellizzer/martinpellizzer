Crassula erecta

Crassula erecta, commonly known as the upright crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
