Clitoria falcata var. glabrescens

Clitoria falcata var. glabrescens, commonly known as the silky blue pea, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, butterfly-like blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain lens-shaped seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and as a traditional medicinal plant.
