Celmisia glandulosa var. longiscapa

Celmisia glandulosa var. longiscapa, commonly known as the long-stemmed celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces clusters of small, daisy-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, glandular hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.
