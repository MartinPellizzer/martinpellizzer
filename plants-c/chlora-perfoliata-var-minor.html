Chlora perfoliata var. minor

It is characterized by its basal rosette of ovate, lobed leaves that often have a waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, arranged in umbels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
