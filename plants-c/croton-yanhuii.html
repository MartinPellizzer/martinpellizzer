Croton yanhuii

Croton yanhuii, commonly known as the Yanhui croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton yanhuii typically grows in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine, though its ornamental value is its primary attraction.
