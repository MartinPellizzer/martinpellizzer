Columnea rileyi

Columnea rileyi, commonly known as the golden flame or flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.

Columnea rileyi is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its eye-catching appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
