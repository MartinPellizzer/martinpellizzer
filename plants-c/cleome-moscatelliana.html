Cleome moscatelliana

Cleome moscatelliana, commonly known as the yellow cleome, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and branching stems. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in clusters at the ends of the branches.

Cleome moscatelliana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and ease of care.
