Cycnium dewevrei var. minor

Cycnium dewevrei var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf cycnium, is a rare and lesser-known variant of the cycnium genus.

It is a herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its limited distribution.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a botanical curiosity and is often studied for its ecological significance.
