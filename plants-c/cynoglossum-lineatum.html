Cynoglossum lineatum

Cynoglossum lineatum, commonly known as heartleaf bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple. It thrives in moist, rich soils and is often found in woodland habitats.

Its showy flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
