Croton daphnargyreus

Croton daphnargyreus, commonly known as the silver croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, waxy hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton daphnargyreus is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
