Crassula Alsinoides

Lamb's ear, scientifically known as Crassula alsinoides, is a unique and eye-catching succulent native to South Africa. Its soft, velvety foliage resembles the ears of a lamb, giving it its charming common name. This plant is prized for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions. With its rosette-shaped leaves and striking color variations, it adds a touch of elegance to any indoor or outdoor space. Whether grown as a standalone specimen or in a decorative arrangement, Lamb's ear is a versatile and visually appealing plant.
