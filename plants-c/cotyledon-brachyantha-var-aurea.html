Cotyledon brachyantha var. aurea

Cotyledon brachyantha var. aurea, commonly known as the golden cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a distinctive yellow or golden edge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
