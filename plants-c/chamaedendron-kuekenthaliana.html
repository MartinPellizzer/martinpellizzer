Chamaedendron kuekenthaliana

Chamaedendron kuekenthaliana, commonly known as the Kuekenthalian flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, flame-colored flowers. The flowers are typically red or orange, appearing in clusters during the warmer months, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and ecological benefits.
