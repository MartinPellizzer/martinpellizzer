Coelogyne flaccida subsp. pempahisheyana

Coelogyne flaccida subsp.

pempahisheyana, commonly known as the Pempahisheyana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, upright pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves.

The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the dry season. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
