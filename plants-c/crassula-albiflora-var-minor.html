Crassula albiflora var. minor

Crassula albiflora var. minor, commonly known as the White Flower Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green. The plant produces small, white flowers during the cooler months, usually in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
