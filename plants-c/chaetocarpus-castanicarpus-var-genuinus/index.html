Chaetocarpus Castanicarpus Var. Genuinus

Chaetocarpus castanicarpus var. genuinus is a unique variant of a flowering plant known for its striking red flowers and hardy nature. It thrives in coastal environments, often found in sandy soils along the Atlantic coast. This plant is part of the Amaranthaceae family, which includes many species with medicinal and ornamental uses. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-resistant plants. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known species in the broader botanical community.
