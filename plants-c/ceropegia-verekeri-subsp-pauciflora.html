Ceropegia verekeri subsp. pauciflora

Ceropegia verekeri subsp.

pauciflora, commonly known as the Cape Flats ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, trailing stems and small, tubular flowers.

The plant produces pale yellow or greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in small inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
