Caralluma gracilipes var. breviloba

Caralluma gracilipes var. breviloba, commonly known as the short-stemmed nightshade, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of India.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and adaptive features make it a striking addition to succulent collections.
