Clematis fawcettii

Clematis fawcettii, commonly known as the Fawcettii Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, single or double flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces fragrant, bell-shaped blooms in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Clematis fawcettii thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
