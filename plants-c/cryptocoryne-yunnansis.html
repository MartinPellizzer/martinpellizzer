Cryptocoryne yunnansis

Cryptocoryne yunnansis, commonly known as the Yunnan cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to China.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight metallic sheen. The plant is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense underwater colonies.

Cryptocoryne yunnansis is valued in the aquarium trade for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.
