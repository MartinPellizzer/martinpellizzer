Combretum wallichii var. yunnanense

Combretum wallichii var. yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan flamewood, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southwestern China.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a leathery texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions with a temperate climate.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
