Cnicus affghanicus

Cnicus affghanicus, commonly known as Afghan sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Cnicus affghanicus is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
