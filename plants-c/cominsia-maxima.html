Cominsia maxima

Cominsia maxima, commonly known as the giant cominsia, is a striking plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual element to its appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
