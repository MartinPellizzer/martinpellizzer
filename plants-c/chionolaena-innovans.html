Chionolaena innovans

Chionolaena innovans, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with silver-gray foliage due to dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species in its native habitat.
