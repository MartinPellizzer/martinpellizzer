Chamaedorea monostachys

Chamaedorea monostachys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bamboo palm, is a compact, evergreen plant native to tropical regions of Central America.

It features slender, upright stems that produce a single, narrow leaf at each node, giving it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers consistently moist, well-draining soil. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and air-purifying qualities.

Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for small spaces and container gardening.
