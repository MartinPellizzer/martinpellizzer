Cattleya × cypheri

Cattleya × cypheri, commonly known as the Cypher Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from various Cattleya species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in tropical climates with high humidity.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
