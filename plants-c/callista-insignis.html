Callista insignis

Callista insignis, commonly known as the golden star bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged flowering period and drought tolerance.
