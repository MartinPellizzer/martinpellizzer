Camissonia Claviformis Var. Aurantiaca

Camissonia claviformis var. aurantiaca is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant orange flowers. It thrives in specific alpine environments where sunlight and cool temperatures create ideal growing conditions. This variant of Camissonia claviformis is distinguished by its unique coloration and compact growth habit. Its beauty makes it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts and botanists alike. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss and climate change.
