Cyclamen mirabile f. mirabile

Cyclamen mirabile f. mirabile, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and Iran.

It features heart-shaped, velvety foliage that is typically a deep green with a silvery underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers with prominent, showy petals and a distinctive, spurred lower petal. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Cyclamen mirabile f. mirabile is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to bloom throughout the winter months.
