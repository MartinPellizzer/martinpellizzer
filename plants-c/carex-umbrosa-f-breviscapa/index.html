Carex Umbrosa F. Breviscapa

Carex umbrosa f. breviscapa is a unique variant of the common sedge plant known for its compact growth and dark green foliage. This form thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its short, narrow leaves give it a distinct appearance that sets it apart from other carex species. While not as widely cultivated as some of its relatives, it is gaining popularity among gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants. Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a valuable addition to both natural and landscaped settings.
