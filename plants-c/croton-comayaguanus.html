Croton comayaguanus

Croton comayaguanus, commonly known as the Comayagua croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Central America, particularly found in countries like Honduras and Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that vary in hue from green to red and orange. It typically grows in tropical lowland forests and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
