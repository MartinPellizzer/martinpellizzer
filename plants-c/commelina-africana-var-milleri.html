Commelina africana var. milleri

Commelina africana var. milleri, commonly known as African bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often accompanied by white or pale blue bracts. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
