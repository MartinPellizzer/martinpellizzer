Cineraria stenophylla

Cineraria stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
