Cycas brachycantha

Cycas brachycantha, commonly known as the short-stemmed cycad, is a distinctive member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its robust, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, pinnately compound leaves. Native to Madagascar, this plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are typically dark green and can reach several meters in length.

Cycas brachycantha is known for its slow growth and long lifespan, making it a unique and ancient-looking plant.
