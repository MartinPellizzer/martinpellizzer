Calanthe daenikeri

Calanthe daenikeri, commonly known as the Daeniker's calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, rhizomatous growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers. The plant typically features broad, lance-shaped leaves and blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Calanthe daenikeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
