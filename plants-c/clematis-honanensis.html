Clematis honanensis

Clematis honanensis, commonly known as the Honan clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, often with a serrated margin. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis honanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
