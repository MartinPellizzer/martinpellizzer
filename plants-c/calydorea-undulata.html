Calydorea undulata

It is characterized by its delicate, waxy leaves that exhibit a subtle undulating pattern along their margins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the spring and early summer months. Calydorea undulata thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing in shaded forest understories.
