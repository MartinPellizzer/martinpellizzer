Crotalaria helenae

Crotalaria helenae, commonly known as the Helen's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with palmate leaves and showy yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.
