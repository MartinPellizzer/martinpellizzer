Carpolyza stellaris

Carpolyza stellaris, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

The starflower is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
