Capsella bursa-pastoris rhenana

Capsella bursa-pastoris rhenana, commonly known as the common shepherd's purse, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a distinctive wavy margin. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a short, triangular capsule that splits into three parts, releasing seeds.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
