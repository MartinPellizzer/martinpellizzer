Cotoneaster tenuipes

Cotoneaster tenuipes, commonly known as the slenderleaf cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of white to pink flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Cotoneaster tenuipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also valued in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
