Capsella bursa-pastoris f. treleaseana

Capsella bursa-pastoris f. treleaseana, commonly known as the treleasean shepherd's purse, is a variant of the common shepherd's purse.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its distinctive triangular seed pods. The leaves are typically lobed and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides.

The plant is notable for its rapid growth and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
