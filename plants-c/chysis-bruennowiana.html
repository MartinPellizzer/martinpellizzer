Chysis bruennowiana

Chysis bruennowiana, commonly known as the pink-flowered chysis, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family, native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

However, it is important to note that it is not commonly found in the wild due to habitat destruction and limited distribution.
