Crinum macrantherum

Crinum macrantherum, commonly known as the large-flowered crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with prominent yellow stamens. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often evergreen, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Crinum macrantherum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its dramatic floral displays.
