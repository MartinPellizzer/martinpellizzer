Caprifolium ciliosum

Caprifolium ciliosum, commonly known as the silky honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have silky, hairy margins.

Caprifolium ciliosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
