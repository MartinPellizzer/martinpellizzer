Calanthe hekouensis

Calanthe hekouensis, commonly known as the Hekou orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe hekouensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
