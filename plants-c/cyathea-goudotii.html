Cyathea goudotii

Cyathea goudotii, commonly known as the Goudot's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The plant has large, compound fronds that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the trunk. Its reproductive structures include both spore-bearing and leafy structures, typical of ferns.

Cyathea goudotii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
