Cornutia crenata

Cornutia crenata, commonly known as the crested cornutia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, branched inflorescence. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters.

Cornutia crenata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance.
