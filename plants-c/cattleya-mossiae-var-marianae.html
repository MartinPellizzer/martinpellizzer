Cattleya mossiae var. marianae

Cattleya mossiae var. marianae, commonly known as the Marianae Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are broad, dark green, and somewhat fleshy, adapted to store moisture.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
