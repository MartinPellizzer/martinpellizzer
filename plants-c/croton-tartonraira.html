Croton tartonraira

Croton tartonraira, commonly known as the Brazilian croton, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to shades of red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a houseplant in many regions.
