Capsella bursa-pastoris acicularis

Capsella bursa-pastoris acicularis, commonly known as the needleleaf shepherd's purse, is a variant of the common shepherd's purse.

It is a low-growing annual plant with a rosette of narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, triangular seed pods that are characteristic of the species. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
