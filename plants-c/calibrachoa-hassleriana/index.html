Calibrachoa Hassleriana

Calibrachoa hassleriana is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America. Known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped blooms, it adds a splash of color to gardens and containers. This species is particularly prized for its ability to thrive in full sun and well-drained soil. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for hanging baskets and borders. Despite its ornamental appeal, it remains a relatively lesser-known gem in the world of flowering plants.
