Cymbidium rubrigemmum

Cymbidium rubrigemmum, commonly known as the red-gemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy floral display. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cymbidium rubrigemmum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
