Cyathea orientalis

Cyathea orientalis, commonly known as the oriental tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bioluminescent, emitting a faint glow during the night.

Cyathea orientalis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in tropical forests.
