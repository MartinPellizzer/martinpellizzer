Cryptocarya burckeana

Cryptocarya burckeana, commonly known as the Burckean tallowwood, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a dry, woody capsule containing several seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood, which has been used in traditional construction and crafting.
