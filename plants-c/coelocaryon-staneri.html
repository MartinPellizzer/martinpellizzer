Coelocaryon staneri

Coelocaryon staneri, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa. The plant features rosettes of velvety, waxy leaves that are typically green or variegated. Its flowers are showy, with showy violet petals that bloom in clusters.

Coelocaryon staneri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
