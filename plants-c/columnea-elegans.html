Columnea elegans

Columnea elegans, commonly known as the elegant flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, rich soil. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Columnea elegans is typically grown as a houseplant or in greenhouse conditions due to its specific climate requirements.
