Chuquiraga sprengeliana f. subinermis

Chuquiraga sprengeliana f. subinermis, commonly known as the smoothleaf chuquiraga, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and shrublands, thriving in cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are smooth and often covered in a fine layer of white hair, which helps reduce water loss.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
