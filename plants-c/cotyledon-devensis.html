Cotyledon devensis

Cotyledon devensis, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Aizoaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
