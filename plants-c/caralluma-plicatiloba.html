Caralluma plicatiloba

Caralluma plicatiloba, commonly known as the pleated devil's backbone, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of India and Pakistan.

Caralluma plicatiloba is also known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
