Clerodendrum consors

Clerodendrum consors, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often brightly colored in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, surrounded by the showy bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum consors is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
