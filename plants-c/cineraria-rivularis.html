Cineraria rivularis

Cineraria rivularis, commonly known as the river cineraria, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and clusters of blue or violet flowers. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found along streambanks and in woodland areas. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
