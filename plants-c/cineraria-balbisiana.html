Cineraria balbisiana

Cineraria balbisiana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, ornamental plant.
