Cryptocoryne elata

Cryptocoryne elata, commonly known as the slender cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters with soft, nutrient-rich substrates. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize large areas in its natural habitat.

Cryptocoryne elata is popular in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
