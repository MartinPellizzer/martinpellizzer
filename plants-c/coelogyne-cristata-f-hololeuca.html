Coelogyne cristata f. hololeuca

Coelogyne cristata f. hololeuca, commonly known as the crested coelogyne, is a striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, waxy, and often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical climates. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The variety 'hololeuca' is distinguished by its fully white flowers, making it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
