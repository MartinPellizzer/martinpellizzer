Calathea legrelleana

Calathea legrelleana, commonly known as the velvetleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns and shades of green, pink, and red. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a velvety texture that enhances its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
