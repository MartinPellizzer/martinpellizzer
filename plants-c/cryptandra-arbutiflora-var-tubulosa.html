Cryptandra arbutiflora var. tubulosa

Cryptandra arbutiflora var. tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular cryptandra, is a shrub native to the south-western region of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or reddish in color, which bloom in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is a species of interest for both horticulture and conservation efforts.
