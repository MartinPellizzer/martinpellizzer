Cotyledon nielsii

Cotyledon nielsii, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance and ability to store water make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
