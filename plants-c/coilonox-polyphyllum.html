Coilonox polyphyllum

Coilonox polyphyllum, commonly known as the featherleaf fern, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically light green with a subtle variegation pattern. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscaping.

Coilonox polyphyllum is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
