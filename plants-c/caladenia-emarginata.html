Caladenia emarginata

Caladenia emarginata, commonly known as the Narrow-leafed Spider-orchid, is a striking orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a single, slender stem with a single or two flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its flowers have a distinctive spider-like appearance, with long, narrow petals and sepals.

Caladenia emarginata is a protected species due to habitat loss and is often found in small, isolated populations.
