Chenopodium spegazzinii

Chenopodium spegazzinii, commonly known as the Spegazzini goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and it thrives in arid or semi-arid environments.

Chenopodium spegazzinii is often found in rocky soils and can be used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
