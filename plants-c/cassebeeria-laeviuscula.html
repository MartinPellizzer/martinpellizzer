Cassebeeria laeviuscula

Cassebeeria laeviuscula, commonly known as the smoothleaf cassia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous plants and characteristic pod fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture, while the flowers are yellow and clustered in racemes.

The seeds are enclosed in elongated pods and are often used in traditional medicine due to their potential medicinal properties.
