Cystidospermum cheirolepis

Cystidospermum cheirolepis, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spurred orchid flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant blooms.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in mountainous or coastal regions.
