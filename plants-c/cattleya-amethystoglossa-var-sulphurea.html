Cattleya amethystoglossa var. sulphurea

Cattleya amethystoglossa var. sulphurea, commonly known as the Sulphur-lipped Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, golden-yellow petals and distinctive orange lip.

Native to the tropical regions of South America, this variety thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It produces large, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer, often displaying a striking contrast between its yellow and orange hues. The plant is known for its ease of care and adaptability, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Its unique coloration and elegant appearance make it a standout in both gardens and floral arrangements.
