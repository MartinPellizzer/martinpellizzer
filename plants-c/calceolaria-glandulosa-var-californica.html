Calceolaria glandulosa var. californica

Calceolaria glandulosa var. californica, commonly known as the California slipper orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features delicate, slipper-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine, glandular hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
