Chlorophytum elatum

Chlorophytum elatum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically green with white markings, and it produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its rhizomes allow it to spread easily, often producing offsets that can be separated and grown independently.
