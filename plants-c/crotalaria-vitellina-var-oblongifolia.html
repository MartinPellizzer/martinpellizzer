Crotalaria vitellina var. oblongifolia

Crotalaria vitellina var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the yellow crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with a crown-like structure. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
