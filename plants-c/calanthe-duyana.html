Calanthe duyana

Calanthe duyana, commonly known as the Duyana calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colored flowers, often featuring a combination of purple, white, and yellow hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories where it thrives on the forest floor. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Calanthe duyana is valued in horticulture for its beauty and adaptability to cultivated conditions with proper care.
