Calligonum Leucocladum

Calligonum leucocladum is a hardy shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia. It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments with minimal water. The plant features dense, silver-gray foliage that helps reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. It is often used for soil stabilization and erosion control in degraded landscapes. Its resilience makes it an important species for ecological restoration projects.
