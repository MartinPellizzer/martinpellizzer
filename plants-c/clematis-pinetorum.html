Clematis pinetorum

Clematis pinetorum, commonly known as the mountain clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, often with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its climbing habit and ornamental value make it a valuable addition to ornamental landscapes.
