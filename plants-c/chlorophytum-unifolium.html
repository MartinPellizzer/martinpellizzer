Chlorophytum unifolium

Chlorophytum unifolium, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental grass native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in a variety of light conditions.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it an easy plant to grow and share.
