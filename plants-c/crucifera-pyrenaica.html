Crucifera pyrenaica

Crucifera pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean cress, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its small white or yellow flowers are arranged in clusters, and it produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Crucifera pyrenaica is often found in meadows and along stream banks at high altitudes.
