Catasetum × splendens var. worthingtonianum

Catasetum × splendens var. worthingtonianum, commonly known as the Worthingtonian orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, each featuring a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil with high light exposure.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
