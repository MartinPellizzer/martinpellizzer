Campanula austroadriatica

Campanula austroadriatica, commonly known as the Adriatic bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in well-drained environments. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Campanula austroadriatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
