Cnicus nuttallii

Cnicus nuttallii, commonly known as the prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and can be found in prairies and along roadsides. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its common name refers to the prickly texture of its leaves and its resemblance to lettuce.
