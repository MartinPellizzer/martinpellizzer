Commicarpus grandiflorus

Commicarpus grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered commicarpus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, elliptical leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
