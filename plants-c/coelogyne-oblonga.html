Coelogyne oblonga

Coelogyne oblonga, commonly known as the long-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, elongated leaf and produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers with yellow petals and a distinctive orange lip. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Coelogyne oblonga is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beautiful, long-lasting blooms.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
