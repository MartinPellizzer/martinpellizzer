Conostegia monteleagreana

Conostegia monteleagreana, commonly known as the Monteleagre cypress, is a rare and endemic species native to the island of Monteleagre in the Canary Islands.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, cone-like structures that contain the seeds. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid and coastal conditions of its native habitat.
