Ceropegia herrei

Ceropegia herrei, commonly known as the Cape Sunset or African Violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant has waxy, succulent leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Ceropegia herrei is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
