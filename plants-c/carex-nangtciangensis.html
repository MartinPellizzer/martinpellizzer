Carex nangtciangensis

Carex nangtciangensis, commonly known as the Nangtciang sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the sedge family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Carex nangtciangensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and small fauna.
