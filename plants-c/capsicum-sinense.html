Capsicum sinense

Capsicum sinense, commonly known as the Chinese chili or Sichuan pepper plant, is a species native to southern China.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow or orange fruits that are often used in traditional Chinese cuisine. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow with five petals.

Capsicum sinense is valued for its aromatic fruits and is also used in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
