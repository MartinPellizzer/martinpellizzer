Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. foeniculaceum

Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. foeniculaceum, commonly known as the cinnamon leaf or cinnamon tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that emit a strong cinnamon scent when crushed. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval, dark brown fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are widely used in traditional medicine and as a spice in various cuisines.

The botanical features of this variety make it distinct from other cinnamon species, particularly in its foliage and aromatic compounds.
