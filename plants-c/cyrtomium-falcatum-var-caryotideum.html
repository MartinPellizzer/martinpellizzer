Cyrtomium falcatum var. caryotideum

Cyrtomium falcatum var. caryotideum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a compact evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, arching fronds that are typically gray-green with a silvery sheen, giving it its common name. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome and produces sporangia on the undersides of its fronds. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its foliage provides a unique texture and color contrast in garden settings.
