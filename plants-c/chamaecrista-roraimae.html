Chamaecrista roraimae

Chamaecrista roraimae, commonly known as Roraima burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, often appearing in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in open areas within tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
