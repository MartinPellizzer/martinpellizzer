Chenopodium × borbasioides

Chenopodium × borbasioides, commonly known as the hybrid goosefoot, is a perennial plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is native to regions of Central and South America and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Due to its adaptability, it can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
