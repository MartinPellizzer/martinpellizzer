Conioselinum anthriscoides 'chuanxiong'

Conioselinum anthriscoides 'chuanxiong', commonly known as Chinese cow parsley, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and emit a mild, spicy scent. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in umbel-like clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its delicate appearance and fragrance make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
