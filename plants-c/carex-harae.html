Carex harae

Carex harae, commonly known as the Harae sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and wetlands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets.

Carex harae is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
