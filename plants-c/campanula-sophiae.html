Campanula sophiae

Campanula sophiae, commonly known as the Sophie bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to central and southern Europe, where it thrives in woodland and meadow habitats. The plant features slender stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Campanula sophiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
