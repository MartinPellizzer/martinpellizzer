Cercestis ivorensis

Cercestis ivorensis, commonly known as the Ivory Coast orchid, is a species of orchid native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
