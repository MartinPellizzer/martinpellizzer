Craspedaria cardiophylla

Craspedaria cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaf fern, is a unique species of fern characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features intricate, serrated leaflets that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are typically located on the underside of the fronds.
