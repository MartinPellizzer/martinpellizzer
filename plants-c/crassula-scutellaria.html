Crassula scutellaria

Crassula scutellaria, commonly known as the Little Carpet Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its compact size and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
