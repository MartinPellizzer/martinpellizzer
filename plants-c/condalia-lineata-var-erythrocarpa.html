Condalia lineata var. erythrocarpa

Condalia lineata var. erythrocarpa, commonly known as the red-fruited condalia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers followed by distinctive red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture and may have a slightly hairy surface.
