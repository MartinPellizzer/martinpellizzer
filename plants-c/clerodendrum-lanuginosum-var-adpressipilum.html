Clerodendrum lanuginosum var. adpressipilum

Clerodendrum lanuginosum var. adpressipilum, commonly known as the silky clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas or near water sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
