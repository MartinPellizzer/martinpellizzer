Crotalaria brevidens var. intermedia

Crotalaria brevidens var. intermedia, commonly known as the narrowleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant has a short, upright growth habit and produces small, pod-like fruits containing seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
