Cunonia atrorubens

Cunonia atrorubens, commonly known as the red-leaved cunonia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its attractive, glossy, dark red to purple leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are borne in clusters, followed by small, round berries that turn red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking foliage.

Cunonia atrorubens is also valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
