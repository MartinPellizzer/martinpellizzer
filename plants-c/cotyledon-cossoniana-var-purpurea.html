Cotyledon cossoniana var. purpurea

Cotyledon cossoniana var. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Leaf Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with purple or red markings, especially under stress or during the winter months. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
