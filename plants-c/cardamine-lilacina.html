Cardamine lilacina

Cardamine lilacina, commonly known as the lilac buttercup, is a small perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with showy blooms. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers with five petals. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
