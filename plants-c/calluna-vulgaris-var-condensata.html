Calluna vulgaris var. condensata

Calluna vulgaris var. condensata, commonly known as the compact heather, is a dense, low-growing shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its closely packed, needle-like leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. This variety thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form thick, mat-like growths.

The plant is also known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions, making it a hardy choice for garden landscapes.
