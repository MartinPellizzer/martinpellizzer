Capillipedium kwashotense

Capillipedium kwashotense, commonly known as the Kwashotense orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has slender, strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
