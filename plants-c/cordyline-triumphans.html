Cordyline triumphans

Cordyline triumphans, commonly known as the Ti plant, is a striking evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of the Pacific.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish or purple stripe along the midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Cordyline triumphans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
