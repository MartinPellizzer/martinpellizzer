Cyperus steadii

Cyperus steadii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. Its slender, grass-like stems are often clustered and have small, scale-like leaves.

Cyperus steadii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
