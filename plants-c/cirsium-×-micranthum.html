Cirsium × micranthum

Cirsium × micranthum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

Cirsium × micranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
