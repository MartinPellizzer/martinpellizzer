Citrus nobilis

Citrus nobilis, commonly known as the lemon.

It is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its glossy green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is a rounded citrus with a thick, yellow rind and a high concentration of essential oils. The peel contains a significant amount of flavonoids and vitamin C, making it valuable in both culinary and medicinal applications.

It is also used in the production of essential oils and is a key ingredient in many traditional remedies.
