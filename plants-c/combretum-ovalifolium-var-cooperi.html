Combretum ovalifolium var. cooperi

Combretum ovalifolium var. cooperi, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are often glandular and emit a mild scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
