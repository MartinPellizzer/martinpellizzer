Cyrtochilum ixioides

Cyrtochilum ixioides, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Cyrtochilum ixioides is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
