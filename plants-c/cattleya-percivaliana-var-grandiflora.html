Cattleya percivaliana var. grandiflora

Cattleya percivaliana var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Percival's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink, often with intricate markings. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for floral arrangements and horticultural displays.
