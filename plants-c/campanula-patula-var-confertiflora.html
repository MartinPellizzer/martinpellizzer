Campanula patula var. confertiflora

Campanula patula var. confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable environments.
