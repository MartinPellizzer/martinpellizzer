Crepinia visianiana

Crepinia visianiana, commonly known as the Visianian crepe myrtle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fluffy white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and grows as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
