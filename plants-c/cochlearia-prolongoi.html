Cochlearia prolongoi

Cochlearia prolongoi, commonly known as the long-stemmed sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. This low-growing perennial has linear leaves and produces small, white or pink flowers arranged in racemes. It thrives in coastal and sandy habitats, often found on cliffs and dunes.

The plant's morphology is adapted to withstand harsh maritime conditions, making it a characteristic species of maritime flora.
