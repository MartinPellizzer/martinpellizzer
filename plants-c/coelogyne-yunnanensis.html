Coelogyne yunnanensis

Coelogyne yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are usually orange or red in color, with a distinctive yellow lip. It is characterized by its erect, hairy pseudobulbs and strap-shaped leaves.

Coelogyne yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
