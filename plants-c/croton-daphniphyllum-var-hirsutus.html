Croton daphniphyllum var. hirsutus

Croton daphniphyllum var. hirsutus, commonly known as the hairy leaf croton, is a unique species within the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated foliage, which can range from deep green to shades of yellow and red. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in complex inflorescences. Its leaves are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it the "hirsutus" designation.

This plant is native to Southeast Asia and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
