Cardamine intonsa

Cardamine intonsa, commonly known as the narrowleaf watercress, is a delicate herbaceous plant native to moist, temperate regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating in water. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round pods containing several seeds.

Cardamine intonsa is typically found in stream banks, wet meadows, and other aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats.
