Carenidium dasytyle

Carenidium dasytyle, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Carenidium dasytyle is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
