Conyza splendens

Conyza splendens, commonly known as the showy ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Conyza splendens is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
