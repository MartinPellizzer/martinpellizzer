Conophytum roodiae subsp. cylindratum

Conophytum roodiae subsp.

cylindratum, commonly known as the cylindrical conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact, fleshy, and often cylindrical or ovoid-shaped leaves that store water, typical of arid environments.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
