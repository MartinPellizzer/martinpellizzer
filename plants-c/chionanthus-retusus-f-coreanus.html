Chionanthus retusus f. coreanus

Chionanthus retusus f. coreanus, commonly known as the Korean fringed tree, is a rare flowering plant native to Korea.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its fragrant, white, fringed flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
