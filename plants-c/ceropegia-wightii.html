Ceropegia wightii

Ceropegia wightii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a trailing plant native to South Africa.

It features elongated, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is a succulent, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves.

Ceropegia wightii thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
