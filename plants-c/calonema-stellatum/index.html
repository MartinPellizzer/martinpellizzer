Calonema Stellatum

Stellate calonema, scientifically known as Calonema stellatum, is a unique and visually striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. With its distinctive star-shaped leaves, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This evergreen species belongs to the family Gesneriaceae, which includes many popular ornamental plants. Its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to thrive in non-native environments.
