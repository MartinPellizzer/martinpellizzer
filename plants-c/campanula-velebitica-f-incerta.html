Campanula velebitica f. incerta

Campanula velebitica f. incerta, commonly known as the uncertain bellflower, is a rare and lesser-known cultivar of the Campanula genus.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale blue to violet, often with a white throat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland floors or stream banks. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of down.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
