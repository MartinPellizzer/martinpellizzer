Commelina communis f. hebespatha

Commelina communis f. hebespatha, commonly known as the broadleaf commelina, is a flowering plant belonging to the Commelinaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers, which are typically borne on slender stems. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Asia and Australia.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
