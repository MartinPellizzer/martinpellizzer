Croton beckii

Croton beckii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that vary in shape and texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Croton beckii is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
