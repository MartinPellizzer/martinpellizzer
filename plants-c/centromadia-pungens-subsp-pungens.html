Centromadia pungens subsp. pungens

Centromadia pungens subsp.

pungens, commonly known as the pungent centromadia, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
