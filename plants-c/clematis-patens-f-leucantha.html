Clematis patens f. leucantha

Clematis patens f. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered clematis, is a rare and striking variant of the common clematis.

It is characterized by its showy, white blooms that contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. This plant is native to parts of Asia and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It typically grows as a climbing vine, reaching lengths of several meters.

Its botanical features make it a prized addition to gardens and botanical collections.
