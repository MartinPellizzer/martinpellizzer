Combretum bracteosum

Combretum bracteosum, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement and are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its ability to produce large quantities of seeds contributes to its spread in its native habitat.
