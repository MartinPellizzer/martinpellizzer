Cotyledon libanotica var. steudelii

Cotyledon libanotica var. steudelii, commonly known as the Steudel's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, especially under stress or in full sun. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in late spring or early summer, adding ornamental value. It is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
