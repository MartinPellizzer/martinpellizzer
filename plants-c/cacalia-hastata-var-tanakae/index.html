Cacalia Hastata Var. Tanakae

Cacalia hastata var. tanakae is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan. Known for its striking blue-violet blooms, it thrives in cool, shaded environments with well-drained soil. This variant is particularly valued for its unique coloration and elegant foliage. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of both scientific interest and botanical admiration.
