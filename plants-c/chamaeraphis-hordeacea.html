Chamaeraphis hordeacea

Chamaeraphis hordeacea, commonly known as the barley grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. It is native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.

Chamaeraphis hordeacea is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in erosion control and as a forage plant.
