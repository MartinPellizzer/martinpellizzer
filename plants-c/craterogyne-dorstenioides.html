Craterogyne dorstenioides

Craterogyne dorstenioides, commonly known as the Dorsten's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
