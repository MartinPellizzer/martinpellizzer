Calamagrostis stricta f. pilosior

Calamagrostis stricta f. pilosior, commonly known as the hairy panicgrass, is a compact, ornamental grass that exhibits dense, upright growth.

It features narrow, silver-green leaves covered with fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive, soft appearance. This variety is notable for its ability to thrive in moist to wet soils, making it suitable for wetland gardens or water features. Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, with a subtle, grassy fragrance.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits, supporting local wildlife.
