Cattleya amethystoglossa var. rosea

Cattleya amethystoglossa var. rosea, commonly known as the Rose Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking floral display.

It features large, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, typically displaying a blend of pink and white hues. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less fertile soils.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
