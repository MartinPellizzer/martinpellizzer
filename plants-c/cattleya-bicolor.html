Cattleya bicolor

Cattleya bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking bicolor flowers, which typically feature a combination of white and pink or purple hues. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces large, fragrant blooms that are highly prized in horticulture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya bicolor is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
