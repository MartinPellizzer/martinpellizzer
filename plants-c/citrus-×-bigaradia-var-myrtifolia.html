Citrus × bigaradia var. myrtifolia

Citrus × bigaradia var. myrtifolia, commonly known as the myrtle-leaved mandarin.

This variety is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its aromatic leaves and fruit. The leaves are typically narrow and have a distinctive myrtle-like fragrance. The fruit is small, round, and has a thin, easy-to-peel rind.

It is valued for its flavor and is often used in culinary and aromatic applications.
