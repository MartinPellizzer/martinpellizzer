Cosmianthemum angustifolium

Cosmianthemum angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cosmos, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and showy, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun. It prefers well-drained soil and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
