Chenopodium × schulzeanum

Chenopodium × schulzeanum, commonly known as silverleaf goosefoot, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are typically dispersed by wind. Native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, it is often found in disturbed soils and dry habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
