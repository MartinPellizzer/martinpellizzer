Curculigo glabrescens

Curculigo glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth arrowhead, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Piperaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and compact, herbaceous growth habit. Curculigo glabrescens is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
