Callianthemum angustifolium var. sajanense

Callianthemum angustifolium var. sajanense, commonly known as the Sajanense Anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a striking visual element to its natural setting. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
