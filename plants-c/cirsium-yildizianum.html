Cirsium yildizianum

Cirsium yildizianum, commonly known as Turkish thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a robust root system and can thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
