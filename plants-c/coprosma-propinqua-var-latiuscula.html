Coprosma propinqua var. latiuscula

Coprosma propinqua var. latiuscula, commonly known as the narrow-leaf coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It typically grows in coastal and lowland forest habitats, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by bright red berries that attract birds.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
