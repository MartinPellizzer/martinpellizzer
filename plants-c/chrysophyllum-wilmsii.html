Chrysophyllum wilmsii

Chrysophyllum wilmsii, commonly known as the African cherry or wild mango, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces small, yellowish-orange fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor.
