Cardamomum giganteum

Cardamomum giganteum, commonly known as the giant cardamom, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, herbaceous stems and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by dark, aromatic seeds. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
