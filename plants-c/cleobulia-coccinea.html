Cleobulia coccinea

Cleobulia coccinea, commonly known as the red cleobulia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its vibrant color and unique appearance, it is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine.
