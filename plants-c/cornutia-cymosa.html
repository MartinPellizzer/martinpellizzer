Cornutia cymosa

Cornutia cymosa, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it blooms during the summer months.

Cornutia cymosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
