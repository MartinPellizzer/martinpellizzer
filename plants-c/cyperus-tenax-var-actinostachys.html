Cyperus tenax var. actinostachys

Cyperus tenax var. actinostachys, commonly known as the spiked flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of small, spike-like flowers. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and along waterways.

Its botanical structure is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and distinctive inflorescence.
