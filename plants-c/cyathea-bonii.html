Cyathea bonii

Cyathea bonii, commonly known as the Bonii tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth rate and long lifespan. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, and the plant prefers moist, shaded environments.

Cyathea bonii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
