Calamagrostis ningxiaensis

Calamagrostis ningxiaensis, commonly known as Ningxia reedgrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments, often found in wetlands or along riverbanks. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, spikelet-bearing flowers.

Calamagrostis ningxiaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland habitats.
