Coelogyne raizadae

Coelogyne raizadae, commonly known as the Raizada's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-shaped, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Coelogyne raizadae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
