Callicarpa lingii

Callicarpa lingii, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant purple to reddish berries that appear in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple on the underside. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

The berries are not only ornamental but also attract birds and other wildlife.
