Cyclamen lilacinum

Cyclamen lilacinum, commonly known as the purple cyclamen, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East and Turkey.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple and pink during late winter to early spring. Cyclamen lilacinum thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its elegant foliage and delicate blooms.
