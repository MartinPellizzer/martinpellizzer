Cypripedium × uihleinianum

Cypripedium × uihleinianum, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive slipper-shaped pouch.

It is a hybrid between Cypripedium calceolus and Cypripedium macranthos, resulting in a plant with vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodland habitats and has a long flowering period, often from late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticultural settings.
