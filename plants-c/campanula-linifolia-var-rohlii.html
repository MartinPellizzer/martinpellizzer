Campanula linifolia var. rohlii

Campanula linifolia var. rohlii, commonly known as the Rohlii bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
