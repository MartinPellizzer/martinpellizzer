Cassia leandrii var. maesta

Cassia leandrii var. maesta, commonly known as the Maestas cassia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
