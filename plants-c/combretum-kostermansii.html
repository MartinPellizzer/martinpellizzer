Combretum kostermansii

Combretum kostermansii, commonly known as the Koster's flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange floral bracts that resemble flames. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often pubescent. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
