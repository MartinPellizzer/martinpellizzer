Clematis obvallata var. shikokiana

Clematis obvallata var. shikokiana, commonly known as the Shikokiana Clematis, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, with stems that can reach several meters in length. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and seasonal blooming habit.
