Cautleya gracilis var. gracilior

Cautleya gracilis var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, upright stems and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pale yellow to orange flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high elevations.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
