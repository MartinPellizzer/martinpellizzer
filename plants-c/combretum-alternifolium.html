Combretum alternifolium

Combretum alternifolium, commonly known as the alternate-leaf combretum, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its alternate arrangement of leaves along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, followed by woody capsules containing seeds. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptical with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
