Carex oreades

Carex oreades, commonly known as mountain sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the genus Carex in the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear both male and female flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or mountainous areas.
