Cirsium akimotoi

Cirsium akimotoi, commonly known as the Akimoto thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. It has a robust root system and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
