Chaetopappa pulchella

Chaetopappa pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful leaf lettuce, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are yellow and are arranged in a dense, disk-like structure typical of the Asteraceae family.

The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and open habitats.
