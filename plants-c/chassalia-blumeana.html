Chassalia blumeana

Chassalia blumeana, commonly known as the Blume's chassalia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender, branched stems and narrow leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a dense inflorescence, and it produces small, oval seeds.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
