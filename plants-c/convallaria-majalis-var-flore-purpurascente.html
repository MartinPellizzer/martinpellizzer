Convallaria majalis var. flore-purpurascente

Convallaria majalis var. flore-purpurascente, commonly known as the purple-flowered lily of the valley, is a variant of the traditional lily of the valley plant.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers, which contrast with the usual white blooms of the standard variety. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces a fragrant, bell-shaped flower cluster. It is a perennial herbaceous plant that thrives in woodland environments with partial shade.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
