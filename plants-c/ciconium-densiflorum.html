Ciconium densiflorum

Ciconium densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered ciconium, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or near water bodies.

Ciconium densiflorum is valued for its ornamental flowers and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
