Capsella bursa-pastoris ovifera

Capsella bursa-pastoris ovifera, commonly known as the common mouse-ear or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is native to Europe and Asia but has been naturalized in many parts of the world. It grows in a variety of habitats, including roadsides, fields, and disturbed soils.
