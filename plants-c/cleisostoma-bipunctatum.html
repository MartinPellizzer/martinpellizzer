Cleisostoma bipunctatum

Cleisostoma bipunctatum, commonly known as the two-spotted hooded orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique hood-like floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive two-spotted pattern on the lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Cleisostoma bipunctatum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
