Campomanesia salviifolia var. angustifolia

Campomanesia salviifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf satinash, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, orange-red berries. The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their astringent properties.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal uses.
