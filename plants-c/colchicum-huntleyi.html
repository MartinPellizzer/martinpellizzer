Colchicum huntleyi

Colchicum huntleyi, commonly known as the Texas autumn crocus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features showy, violet-purple flowers that bloom in the fall, typically from October to November. The plant has strappy, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous underground stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Colchicum huntleyi is part of the Colchicaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in native gardens.
