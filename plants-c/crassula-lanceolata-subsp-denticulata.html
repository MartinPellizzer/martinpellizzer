Crassula lanceolata subsp. denticulata

Crassula lanceolata subsp.

denticulata, commonly known as the Cape rose or toothed crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
