Canarium pulchrebracteatum

Canarium pulchrebracteatum, commonly known as the elegant bract canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
