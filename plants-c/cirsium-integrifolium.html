Cirsium integrifolium

Cirsium integrifolium, commonly known as the smooth thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, lobed, gray-green leaves with smooth margins. The plant produces purple to pinkish flowers in composite flower heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Cirsium integrifolium is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a hardy, adaptable species.
