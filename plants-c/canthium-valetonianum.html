Canthium valetonianum

Canthium valetonianum, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
