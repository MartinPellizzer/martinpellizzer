Cleistanthus travancorensis var. sankunnianus

Cleistanthus travancorensis var. sankunnianus, commonly known as the Kerala cleistanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features simple, opposite, and lanceolate leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in axillary inflorescences, while the fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental potential and ecological role in its native habitat.
