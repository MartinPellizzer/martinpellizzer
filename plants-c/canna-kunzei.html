Canna kunzei

Canna kunzei, commonly known as the yellow canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, often with a reddish or purple tinge along the veins. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Canna kunzei thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms in gardens and landscapes.
