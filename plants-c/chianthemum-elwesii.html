Chianthemum elwesii

Chianthemum elwesii, commonly known as the Elwes' chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.

Chianthemum elwesii is also valued for its ability to thrive in moderate climates.
