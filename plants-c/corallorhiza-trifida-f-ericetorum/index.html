Corallorhiza Trifida F. Ericetorum

Corallorhiza trifida f. ericetorum is a rare and fascinating orchid that thrives in specific ecological niches. This terrestrial orchid is known for its striking appearance and unique adaptation to low-light environments. It lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrition, making it a fascinating subject for ecological study. Found primarily in heathlands and acidic soils, it is a symbol of delicate ecological balance. Its conservation status highlights the importance of preserving its fragile habitat.
