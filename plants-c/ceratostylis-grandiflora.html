Ceratostylis grandiflora

Ceratostylis grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered hooded orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, hooded flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive greenish throat. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces numerous small, fragrant flowers throughout the year.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
