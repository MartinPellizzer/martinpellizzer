Cattleya × brasiliensis

Cattleya × brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brasiliensis orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often arranged in two rows along the stem.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
