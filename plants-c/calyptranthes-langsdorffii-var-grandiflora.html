Calyptranthes langsdorffii var. grandiflora

Calyptranthes langsdorffii var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered calyptranthes, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features elliptical to ovate leaves with a glossy, dark green surface and serrated margins. The plant produces showy, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in well-drained, fertile soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in subtropical gardens.
