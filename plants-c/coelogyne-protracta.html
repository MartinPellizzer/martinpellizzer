Coelogyne protracta

Coelogyne protracta, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. It blooms once a year, typically during the late winter or early spring.

Coelogyne protracta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
