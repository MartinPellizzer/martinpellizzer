Callistemon roseus

Callistemon roseus, commonly known as the Christmas bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and a woody, upright stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a favorite in gardens and landscapes.
