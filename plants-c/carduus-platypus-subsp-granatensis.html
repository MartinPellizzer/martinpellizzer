Carduus platypus subsp. granatensis

Carduus platypus subsp.

granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers.

The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its robust, upright growth habit. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a dense, spherical head.

This subspecies is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
