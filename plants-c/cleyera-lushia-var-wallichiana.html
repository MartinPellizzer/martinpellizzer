Cleyera lushia var. wallichiana

Cleyera lushia var. wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan cleyera, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
