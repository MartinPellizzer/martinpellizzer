Cordia schatziana

Cordia schatziana, commonly known as Schatz's cordia, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds.
