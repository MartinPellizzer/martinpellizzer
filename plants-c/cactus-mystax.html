Cactus mystax

Cactus mystax, commonly known as the devil's tongue, is a unique member of the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, spiny stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the base of the stem. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and adapting it to arid environments.

The cactus is native to parts of South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
