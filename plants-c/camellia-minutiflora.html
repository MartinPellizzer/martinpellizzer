Camellia minutiflora

Camellia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Camellia minutiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in shaded areas.
