Clerodendrum triphyllum f. angustissimum

Clerodendrum triphyllum f. angustissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf coral tree, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with three narrow, lance-shaped leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Its ornamental value comes from its showy inflorescences and attractive foliage.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
