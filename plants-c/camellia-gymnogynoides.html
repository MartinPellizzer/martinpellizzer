Camellia gymnogynoides

Camellia gymnogynoides, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a yellow or pink base. The plant has leathery, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Camellia gymnogynoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
