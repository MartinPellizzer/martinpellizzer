Cerasus mahaleb f. bernatskyi

Cerasus mahaleb f. bernatskyi, commonly known as the cherry plum, is a compact, ornamental shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by edible berries that are typically red or yellow in color. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves and a generally rounded, dense growth habit. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its visual interest and fragrance.

This variety is also appreciated for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
