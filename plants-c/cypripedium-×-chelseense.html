Cypripedium × chelseense

Cypripedium × chelseense, commonly known as the Chelsea orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the British Isles.

It is a tall, herbaceous plant with a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower that has a distinctive pouch-like structure. The plant typically grows to a height of 60–100 cm and produces a single, upright stem with a single bloom. Its flowers are highly prized for their beauty and are often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade.
