Cynanchum mulegense

Cynanchum mulegense, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruit is a follicle containing several seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
