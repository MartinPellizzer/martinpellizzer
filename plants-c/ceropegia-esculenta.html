Ceropegia esculenta

Ceropegia esculenta, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
