Ceropegia schlechteriana

Ceropegia schlechteriana, commonly known as the African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tuberous roots and produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
