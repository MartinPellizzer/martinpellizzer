Calycogonium obliquum

Calycogonium obliquum, commonly known as the zigzag lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a distinctive zigzagging stem, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Calycogonium obliquum is typically found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
