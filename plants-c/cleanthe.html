Cleanthe

Cleanthe, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a water-loving plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. The plant produces strikingly beautiful flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its roots are submerged in water, absorbing nutrients from the aquatic environment.

Cleanthe has been revered in various cultures for its symbolic and medicinal properties.
