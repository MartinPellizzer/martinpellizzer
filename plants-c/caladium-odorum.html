Caladium odorum

Caladium odorum, commonly known as the sweet-scented arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial to full shade.

Caladium odorum is often cultivated for its striking foliage and pleasant scent, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
