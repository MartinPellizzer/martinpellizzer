Croton polycarpus

Croton polycarpus, commonly known as the red torch tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that ranges from bright red to orange and yellow. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, greenish flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
