Calanthe pleiochroma

Calanthe pleiochroma, commonly known as the rainbow calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that display a variety of hues, including purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Calanthe pleiochroma is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
