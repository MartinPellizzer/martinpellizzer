Cucumis quintanilhae

Cucumis quintanilhae, commonly known as the Quintanilha cucumber, is a rare and little-studied species of cucumber native to a specific region in Brazil.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated fruits that are typically green and covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has a vine-like growth habit, similar to other members of the Cucurbitaceae family.
