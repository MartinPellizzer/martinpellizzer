Cotoneaster yulingkongensis

Cotoneaster yulingkongensis, commonly known as the Yulingkong cotoneaster, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in the spring, followed by orange-red berries in the fall. Its leaves are typically ovate with a smooth margin and a glossy surface.

Cotoneaster yulingkongensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
