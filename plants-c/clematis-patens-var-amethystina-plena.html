Clematis patens var. amethystina-plena

Clematis patens var. amethystina-plena, commonly known as the Amethystina Plena Clematis, is a compact, flowering vine native to China.

It produces dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has sturdy, twining stems that climb using its tendrils, making it suitable for trellises or support structures. Its foliage is heart-shaped and provides a lush green backdrop to the vibrant blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, thriving in temperate climates.
