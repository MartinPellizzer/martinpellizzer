Cotyledon tropaeolifolia

Cotyledon tropaeolifolia, commonly known as the "Rainbow Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, crinkled leaves that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and purple, which change with light exposure. The plant produces small, colorful flowers in shades of orange and yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
