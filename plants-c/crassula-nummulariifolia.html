Crassula nummulariifolia

Crassula nummulariifolia, commonly known as the "Lucky Coin Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are round and coin-like, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring.

Its compact size and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
