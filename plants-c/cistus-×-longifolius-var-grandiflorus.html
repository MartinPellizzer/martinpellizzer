Cistus × longifolius var. grandiflorus

Cistus × longifolius var. grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to reddish-purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal gardens.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal landscapes.
