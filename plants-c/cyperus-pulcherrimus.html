Cyperus pulcherrimus

Cyperus pulcherrimus, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are often pink or purple in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and pond edges.

Cyperus pulcherrimus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and adaptability.
