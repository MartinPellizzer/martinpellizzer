Celastrus microphyllus

Celastrus microphyllus, commonly known as the small-leaved staffvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in forested areas and along stream banks. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are usually less than 1 inch in length. Its yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the spring.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
