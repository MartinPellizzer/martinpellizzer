Corethrostylis membranacea

Corethrostylis membranacea, commonly known as the slender fringed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, and the flowers are arranged in a spike, giving it a graceful appearance.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
