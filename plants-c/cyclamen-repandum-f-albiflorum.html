Cyclamen repandum f. albiflorum

Cyclamen repandum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered cyclamen, is a hybrid form of the Persian cyclamen.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

Cyclamen repandum f. albiflorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its elegant blooms and foliage.
