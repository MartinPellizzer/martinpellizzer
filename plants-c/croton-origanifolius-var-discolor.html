Croton origanifolius var. discolor

Croton origanifolius var. discolor, commonly known as the Variegated Croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, yellow, and white. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and have a smooth texture. This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental landscaping.
