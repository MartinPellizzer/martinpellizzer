Crassula media

Crassula media, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Christmas Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Crassula media is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
