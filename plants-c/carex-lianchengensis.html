Carex lianchengensis

Carex lianchengensis, commonly known as the Liangcheng sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Liangcheng region, and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of streams.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
