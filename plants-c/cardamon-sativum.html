Cardamon sativum

Cardamon sativum, commonly known as the true cinnamon tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It produces aromatic, red-brown bark that is harvested and dried to make cinnamon. The plant's leaves are elliptical and glossy, with a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The bark contains essential oils that contribute to its distinctive flavor and medicinal properties.
