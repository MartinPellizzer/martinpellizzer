Croton astroites

Croton astroites, commonly known as the silverleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that often display a mix of green, silver, and reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Croton astroites is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage.
