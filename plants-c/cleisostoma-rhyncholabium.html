Cleisostoma rhyncholabium

Cleisostoma rhyncholabium, commonly known as the narrow-lipped cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually ovate and arranged in a compact rosette.

Cleisostoma rhyncholabium prefers shady, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.
