Cacalia didymantha

Cacalia didymantha, commonly known as the pink yarrow, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple composite flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Cacalia didymantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
