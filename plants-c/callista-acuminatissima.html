Callista acuminatissima

Callista acuminatissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved callista, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable plant for coastal and sandy environments.
