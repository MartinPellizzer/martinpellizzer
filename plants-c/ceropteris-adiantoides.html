Ceropteris adiantoides

Ceropteris adiantoides, commonly known as the water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often submerged or floating in water. The plant thrives in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine.

The fronds are typically light green and have a feathery appearance, contributing to its ornamental value.
