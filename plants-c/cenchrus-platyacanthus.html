Cenchrus platyacanthus

Cenchrus platyacanthus, commonly known as the broad-awned grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its robust growth and distinctive, flattened awns on its inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas and is often found in grasslands and agricultural fields. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and spikelet-based inflorescences.

It is adapted to thrive in warm climates with well-drained soils.
