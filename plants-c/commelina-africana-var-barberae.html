Commelina africana var. barberae

Commelina africana var. barberae, commonly known as the African spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers with prominent white markings. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy surface.
