Crinum schimperi

Crinum schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian crinum, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Crinum schimperi is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in its native region.
