Crotalaria amoena

Crotalaria amoena, commonly known as the pretty crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous plants. The plant features showy, butterfly-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive central spot. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three leaflets, and it has a taproot system.

Crotalaria amoena is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
