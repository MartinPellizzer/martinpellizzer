Citrosma mollicoma

Citrosma mollicoma, commonly known as the soft-leaved citrosma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-colored fruits resembling small oranges. Its leaves are soft and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Citrosma mollicoma is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
