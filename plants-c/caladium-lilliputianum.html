Caladium lilliputianum

Caladium lilliputianum, commonly known as the miniature caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves that come in a variety of vibrant colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few inches, making it an ideal choice for container gardening. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Caladium lilliputianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
