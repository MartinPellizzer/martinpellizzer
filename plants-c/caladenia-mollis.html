Caladenia mollis

Caladenia mollis, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Australia.

It typically grows in heathland and woodland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a single, slender stem with a single flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white with intricate patterns, and it blooms during the late winter to early spring.

Caladenia mollis is known for its delicate beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
