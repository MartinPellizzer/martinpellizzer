Cyathea klotzschiana

Cyathea klotzschiana, commonly known as the Klotzsch's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical environments. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, while its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping.

The fronds are typically bioluminescent at night, a unique feature that aids in its ecological interactions.
