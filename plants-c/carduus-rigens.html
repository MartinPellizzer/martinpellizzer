Carduus rigens

Carduus rigens, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas. The plant has upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lobed and hairy. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe but has been introduced to other regions, including North America.
