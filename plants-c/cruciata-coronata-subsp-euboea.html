Cruciata coronata subsp. euboea

Cruciata coronata subsp.

euboea, commonly known as the Euboean crown vetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters.

The plant is characterized by its robust root system and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and disturbed areas.

Due to its nitrogen-fixing ability, it plays a role in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
