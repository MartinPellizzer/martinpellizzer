Conophytum longibracteatum

Conophytum longibracteatum, commonly known as the Longibracteatum Lithops, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, stone-like appearance with thick, fleshy leaves that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water to prevent root rot.

Conophytum longibracteatum is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
