Cryptocoryne retrospiralis var. crispatula

Cryptocoryne retrospiralis var. crispatula, commonly known as the curly cryptocoryne, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, ribbon-like leaves that curl and twist, creating a unique, flowing appearance. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is well-suited for community tanks. It prefers soft to moderately hard water with a pH range of 6.0 to 7.5.

Its slow growth rate makes it an excellent choice for low-maintenance aquariums.
