Cicer spiroceras

Cicer spiroceras, commonly known as the spiral pea, is a rare and lesser-known legume species.

It is characterized by its unique spiral-shaped pods, which are a distinguishing feature of the plant. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to its rugged habitat.

Despite its botanical interest, Cicer spiroceras remains under-studied and is not widely cultivated.
