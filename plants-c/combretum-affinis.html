Combretum affinis

Combretum affinis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that turn red in autumn. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
