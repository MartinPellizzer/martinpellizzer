Clerodendrum ovale

Clerodendrum ovale, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer, creating a striking visual display. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark and leaves contain compounds that have been used in traditional medicine.
