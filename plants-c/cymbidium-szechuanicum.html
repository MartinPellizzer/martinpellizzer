Cymbidium szechuanicum

Cymbidium szechuanicum, commonly known as the Sichuan orchid, is a species native to China, particularly in the Sichuan province.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, upright stems and clusters of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring, producing white to pale yellow blooms with a distinctive greenish tinge. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for both garden cultivation and indoor growing.

Cymbidium szechuanicum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in traditional Chinese horticulture.
