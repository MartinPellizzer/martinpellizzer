Camaridium tonduzii

Camaridium tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
