Callista pycnostachya

Callista pycnostachya, commonly known as the dense-stemmed callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
