Crassula streyi

Crassula streyi, commonly known as the Streyi crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight red or purple tinge under sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula streyi is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
