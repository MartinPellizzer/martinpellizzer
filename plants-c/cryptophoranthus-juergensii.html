Cryptophoranthus juergensii

Cryptophoranthus juergensii, commonly known as the dwarf cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like arrangement of stiff, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in warm, dry climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in botanical gardens and private collections.
