Codiaeum rubrolineatum

Codiaeum rubrolineatum, commonly known as the flame tree or red-veined begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, palmate leaves that display a striking combination of deep green and bright red veins. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorching. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.

Codiaeum rubrolineatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored plant among foliage enthusiasts.
