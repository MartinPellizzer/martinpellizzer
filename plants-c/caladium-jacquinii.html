Caladium jacquinii

Caladium jacquinii, commonly known as the painted leaf or Jacquin's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is typically grown as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Caladium jacquinii is often used in gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
