Chamaecrista desvauxii var. linearis

Chamaecrista desvauxii var. linearis, commonly known as the linear senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical studies.
