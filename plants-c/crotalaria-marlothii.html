Crotalaria marlothii

Crotalaria marlothii, commonly known as the marloth's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant produces distinctive yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are palmately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Crotalaria marlothii is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is often used in traditional medicine.
