Cyathea costulisora

Cyathea costulisora, commonly known as the Costulisora tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and aiding in nutrient absorption.

Cyathea costulisora is often found in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests.
