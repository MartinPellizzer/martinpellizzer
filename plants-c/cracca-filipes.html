Cracca filipes

Cracca filipes, commonly known as the slender cracca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its slender stems and feathery leaves distinguish it from other members of the genus.

It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
