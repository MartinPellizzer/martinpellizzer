Centaurea × substereophylla

Centaurea × substereophylla, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its blue to purple composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, tubular florets arranged in a dense cluster.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations compared to its parent species.
