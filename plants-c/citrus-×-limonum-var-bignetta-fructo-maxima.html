Citrus × limonum var. bignetta-fructo-maxima

Citrus × limonum var. bignetta-fructo-maxima, commonly known as the Bignetta-Fructo-Maxima lemon, is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its unique botanical features.

It possesses a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are slightly pubescent. The fruit is typically small to medium in size, with a thick, yellow-orange rind and a high juice content. It is known for its aromatic, tangy flavor, making it popular in culinary and aromatic applications.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
