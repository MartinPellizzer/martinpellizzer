Carex stevenii

Carex stevenii, commonly known as Steven's sedge, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and along stream banks.

Carex stevenii is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support diverse ecosystems.
