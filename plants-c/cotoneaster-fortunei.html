Cotoneaster fortunei

Cotoneaster fortunei, commonly known as Fortune's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping.

Cotoneaster fortunei is relatively low maintenance and adaptable to various soil types.
