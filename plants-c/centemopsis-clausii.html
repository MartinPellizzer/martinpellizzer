Centemopsis clausii

Centemopsis clausii, commonly known as the Claus's centemopsis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
