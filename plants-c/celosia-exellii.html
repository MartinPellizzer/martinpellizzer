Celosia exellii

Celosia exellii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spire-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant is an annual or biennial herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. Its foliage is broad and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Celosia exellii is prized for its long-lasting blooms and is commonly used in garden landscapes and floral arrangements.
