Calosacme grandiflora

Calosacme grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered calosacme, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive, elongated corolla. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shady areas.

Calosacme grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
