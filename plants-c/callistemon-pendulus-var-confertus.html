Callistemon pendulus var. confertus

Callistemon pendulus var. confertus, commonly known as the dense bottlebrush, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, bright red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that appear in pendulous clusters, giving it a dramatic and showy appearance. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically a glossy green, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is known for its compact growth habit and is suitable for use in borders, containers, or as a specimen plant.
