Cyphokentia carolinensis

Cyphokentia carolinensis, commonly known as the Carolina palm, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The Carolina palm is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid environments.
