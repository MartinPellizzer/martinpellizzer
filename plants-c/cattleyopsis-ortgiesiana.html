Cattleyopsis ortgiesiana

Cattleyopsis ortgiesiana, commonly known as the Orchid of the Kalahari, is a rare and unique orchid species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It has a short-lived rhizome and produces flowers directly from the base of the plant.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
