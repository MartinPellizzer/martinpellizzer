Cryptandra glabriflora

Cryptandra glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered cryptandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, smooth, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Cryptandra glabriflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
