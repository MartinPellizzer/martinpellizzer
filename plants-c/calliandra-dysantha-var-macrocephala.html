Calliandra dysantha var. macrocephala

Calliandra dysantha var. macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from pink to red, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has bipinnate leaves, which are compound and divided into small, feathery leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant blooms.

The flowers are also attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
