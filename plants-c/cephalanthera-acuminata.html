Cephalanthera acuminata

Cephalanthera acuminata, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Europe.

It features a delicate, flowering stem that typically grows to a height of about 20-40 centimeters. The plant produces a dense spike of small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

Cephalanthera acuminata is known for its fragrant blooms and is often found in woodland areas with moist, well-drained soil.
