Catasetum ariquemense var. viride

Catasetum ariquemense var. viride, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant green flowers, which contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of showy, spurred flowers that attract specific pollinators.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings due to its specific care requirements.
