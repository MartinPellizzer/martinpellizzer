Cassia villosissima

Cassia villosissima, commonly known as velvet cassia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces long, slender pods containing flat, elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cassia villosissima is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
