Chaenorhinum rubrifolium var. maroccanum

Chaenorhinum rubrifolium var. maroccanum, commonly known as the Moroccan red-leaf chaenorhinum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Morocco.

It is characterized by its striking red or purple foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant leaf color and delicate floral display.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
