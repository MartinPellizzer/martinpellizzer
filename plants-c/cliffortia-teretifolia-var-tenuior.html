Cliffortia teretifolia var. tenuior

Cliffortia teretifolia var. tenuior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils along coastal areas. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
