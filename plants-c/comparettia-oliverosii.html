Comparettia oliverosii

Comparettia oliverosii, commonly known as the Oliveros's ctenanthe, is a rare and striking member of the Araceae family.

Native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica, it features large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green hue and prominent veins. The plant produces striking white spathes with bright yellow spadices, creating an eye-catching floral display. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of atmospheric humidity and indirect light.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by collectors and botanists alike.
