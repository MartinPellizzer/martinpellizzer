Citrus × bergamia

Citrus × bergamia, commonly known as the bergamot orange.

It is a hybrid citrus fruit that originated in the Mediterranean region. The plant is a small evergreen tree with dark green, leathery leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is typically harvested when it is still green and has a high oil content.

The essential oils extracted from its peel are widely used in perfumery and aromatherapy.
