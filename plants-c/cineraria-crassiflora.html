Cineraria crassiflora

Cineraria crassiflora, commonly known as the broad-flowered cineraria, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer, often accompanied by silvery-gray foliage. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its native arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
