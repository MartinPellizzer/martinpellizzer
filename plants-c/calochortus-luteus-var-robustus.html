Calochortus luteus var. robustus

Calochortus luteus var. robustus, commonly known as the yellow mariposa lily, is a striking member of the Liliaceae family native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive red throat, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are basal, linear, and often hairy, contributing to its rugged appearance.

This variety is noted for its robust growth and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
