Catapodium Rigidum Subsp. Hemipoa

Catapodium rigidum subsp. hemipoa is a unique grass species native to parts of southern Africa. It is characterized by its rigid, upright stems and distinctive leaf structure. This plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal dunes. Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for local wildlife. Despite its hardy nature, it remains relatively obscure in botanical studies.
