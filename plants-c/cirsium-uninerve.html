Cirsium uninerve

Cirsium uninerve, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads. Cirsium uninerve is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
