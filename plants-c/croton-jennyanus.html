Croton jennyanus

Croton jennyanus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from green to red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
