Costus phaeotrichus

Costus phaeotrichus, commonly known as the golden costus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant features tall, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green. Costus phaeotrichus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
