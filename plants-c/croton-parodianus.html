Croton parodianus

Croton parodianus, commonly known as the Parodiana croton, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are often lobed and have a velvety texture, making the plant highly ornamental. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
