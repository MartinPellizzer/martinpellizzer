Cirsium × fabium

Cirsium × fabium, commonly known as the common thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to Europe and has been naturalized in various parts of the world. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Cirsium × fabium is often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed areas.
