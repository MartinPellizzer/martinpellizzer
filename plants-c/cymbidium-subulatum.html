Cymbidium subulatum

Cymbidium subulatum, commonly known as the needle-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves and produces striking, long-lasting orchid blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white with a prominent lip, making it a popular choice for cultivation.

Cymbidium subulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
