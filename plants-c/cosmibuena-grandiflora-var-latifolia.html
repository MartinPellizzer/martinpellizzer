Cosmibuena grandiflora var. latifolia

Cosmibuena grandiflora var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf cosmibuena, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a compact form that makes it suitable for both container and in-ground planting.
