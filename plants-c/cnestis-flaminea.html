Cnestis flaminea

Cnestis flaminea, commonly known as the flame vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing shrub that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, which attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and as a living fence.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
