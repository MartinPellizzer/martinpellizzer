Cestrum haberii

Cestrum haberii, commonly known as the yellow bell vine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant is a vigorous climber with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, black seeds.
