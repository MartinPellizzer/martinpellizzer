Caulokaempferia chayaniana

Caulokaempferia chayaniana, commonly known as the Chayanana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in racemose inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
