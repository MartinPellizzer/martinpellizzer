Crassula × graeseri

Crassula × graeseri, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape, often with a bluish or grayish hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor plant collections.
