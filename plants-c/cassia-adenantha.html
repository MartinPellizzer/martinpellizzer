Cassia adenantha

Cassia adenantha, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and savannah regions.

Cassia adenantha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local medicine.
