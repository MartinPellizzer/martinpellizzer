Chlorophytum chariense

Chlorophytum chariense, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive striped, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum chariense is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
