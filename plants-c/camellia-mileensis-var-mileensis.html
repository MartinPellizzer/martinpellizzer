Camellia mileensis var. mileensis

Camellia mileensis var. mileensis, commonly known as the Chinese mountain camellia, is a species native to eastern China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and showy, camellia-like flowers that range in color from white to deep red. It produces elliptical, dark brown seeds enclosed in a hairy, woody capsule.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
