Cylindropuntia leptocaulis var. longispina

Cylindropuntia leptocaulis var. longispina, commonly known as the long-spined hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial cactus characterized by its cylindrical stems and dense spines. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring to early summer.

This variety is known for its robust spines and ability to withstand harsh desert conditions.
