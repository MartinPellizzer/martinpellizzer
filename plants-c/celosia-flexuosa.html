Celosia flexuosa

Celosia flexuosa, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, plume-like inflorescences that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, with upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Celosia flexuosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.
