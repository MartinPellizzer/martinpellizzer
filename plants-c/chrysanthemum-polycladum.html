Chrysanthemum polycladum

Chrysanthemum polycladum, commonly known as the silver mohair plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
