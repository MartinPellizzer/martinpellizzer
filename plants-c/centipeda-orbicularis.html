Centipeda orbicularis

Centipeda orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaved centipeda, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features orbicular (round) leaves that are often hairy, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Centipeda orbicularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
