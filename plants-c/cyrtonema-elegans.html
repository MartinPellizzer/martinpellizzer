Cyrtonema elegans

Cyrtonema elegans, commonly known as the silverleaf beauty, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, velvety leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Cyrtonema elegans is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
