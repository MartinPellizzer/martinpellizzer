Cypripedium × vexillarium

Cypripedium × vexillarium, commonly known as the imperial orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive white and pink coloration, often resembling a flag or banner. The plant typically grows in cool, shaded environments, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its flowers are highly prized for their beauty and are often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.

The species is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in horticultural displays.
