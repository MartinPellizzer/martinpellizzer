Ceratostylis ovatilabris

Ceratostylis ovatilabris, commonly known as the broad-leafed torch orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, leafy stems and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated, club-shaped flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts worldwide.
