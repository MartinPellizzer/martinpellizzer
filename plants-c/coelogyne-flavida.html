Coelogyne flavida

Coelogyne flavida, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces fragrant blooms that can last several weeks, making it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts.

Coelogyne flavida requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
