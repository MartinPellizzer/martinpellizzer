Cryptandra tomentosa

Cryptandra tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly cryptandra, is a shrub native to the southwestern regions of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and coastal environments.

Cryptandra tomentosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in supporting local wildlife.
