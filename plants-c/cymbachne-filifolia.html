Cymbachne filifolia

Cymbachne filifolia, commonly known as the threadleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in areas with high humidity. The plant has slender, thread-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Cymbachne filifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate foliage and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
