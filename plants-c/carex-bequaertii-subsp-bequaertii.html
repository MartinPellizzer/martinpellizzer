Carex bequaertii subsp. bequaertii

Carex bequaertii subsp.

bequaertii, commonly known as the Bequaert's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants.
