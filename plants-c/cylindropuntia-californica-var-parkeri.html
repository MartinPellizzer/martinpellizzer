Cylindropuntia californica var. parkeri

Cylindropuntia californica var. parkeri, commonly known as the Parker's barrel cactus, is a species native to California and Baja California.

It is a tall, columnar cactus with a ribbed stem that can grow up to 6 feet in height. The plant features dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its spines are typically short and white, and it produces edible, fleshy fruits that are red when mature.

This variety is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and coastal areas.
