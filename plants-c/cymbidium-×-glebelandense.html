Cymbidium × glebelandense

Cymbidium × glebelandense, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, long-lasting flowers. The plant typically produces showy, lance-shaped petals in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cymbidium × glebelandense is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
