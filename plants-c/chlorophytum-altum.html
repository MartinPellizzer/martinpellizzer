Chlorophytum altum

Chlorophytum altum, commonly known as the Tall Spider Plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, often with a white stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.

Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for both ornamental and functional gardening.
