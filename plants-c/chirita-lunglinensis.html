Chirita lunglinensis

Chirita lunglinensis, commonly known as the Lunglin violet, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from purple to blue. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and somewhat obscure species within the genus Chirita.
