Carex aristata var. kirschsteiniana

Carex aristata var. kirschsteiniana, commonly known as the Kirschsteinian sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems. The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and wetland landscapes.
