Calliandra microcalyx

Calliandra microcalyx, commonly known as the small-flowered calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that consist of numerous small, pink to red flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Calliandra microcalyx is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive flowers and is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
