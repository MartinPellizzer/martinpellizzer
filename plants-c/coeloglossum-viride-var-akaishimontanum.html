Coeloglossum viride var. akaishimontanum

Coeloglossum viride var. akaishimontanum, commonly known as the green hawksbeard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in a dense, terminal cluster, which bloom in late summer. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a preference for well-drained, rocky soils.

It is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments.
