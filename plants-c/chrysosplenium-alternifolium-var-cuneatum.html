Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. cuneatum

Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. cuneatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goldcup, is a member of the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The species is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in cool, temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
