Colocasia mannii

Colocasia mannii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, shady environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a vegetable in some cultures.

The plant's ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and water features.
