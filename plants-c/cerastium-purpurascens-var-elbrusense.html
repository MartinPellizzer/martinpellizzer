Cerastium purpurascens var. elbrusense

Cerastium purpurascens var. elbrusense, commonly known as the Elbrus mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its low-growing habit and dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers.
