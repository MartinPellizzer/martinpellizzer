Coleosanthus californicus var. normalis

Coleosanthus californicus var. normalis, commonly known as the California lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Amaryllidaceae family.
