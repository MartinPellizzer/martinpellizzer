Conocephalus grandis

Conocephalus grandis, commonly known as the large-headed conocephalus, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent, elongated spur.

Conocephalus grandis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
