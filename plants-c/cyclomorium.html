Cyclomorium

Cyclomorium, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a rare and unique plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a variety of colors, though they are not particularly showy.

Cyclomorium is noted for its slow growth and requires specific conditions to thrive, making it a challenging plant for cultivation.
