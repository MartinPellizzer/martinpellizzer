Crocus biflorus var. janii

Crocus biflorus var. janii, commonly known as the Janii crocus, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking violet-blue flowers with a yellow center, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces two to three flowers per stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate in temperate climates.
