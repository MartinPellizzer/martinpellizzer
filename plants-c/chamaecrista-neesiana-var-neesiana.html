Chamaecrista neesiana var. neesiana

Chamaecrista neesiana var. neesiana, commonly known as the yellow cassia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various ecological conditions.
