Coelorachis aurita

Coelorachis aurita, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow floral spikes. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are borne on slender stems and feature intricate, showy petals.

Coelorachis aurita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
