Cissus sicyoides f. umbrosa

Cissus sicyoides f. umbrosa, commonly known as the shade cissus, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by greenish-white berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
