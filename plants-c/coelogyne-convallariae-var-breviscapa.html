Coelogyne convallariae var. breviscapa

Coelogyne convallariae var. breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
