Calliandra imperialis

Calliandra imperialis, commonly known as the imperial calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that produce showy, pink to red flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-drained soil.

The flowers are not only visually appealing but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
