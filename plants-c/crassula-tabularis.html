Crassula tabularis

Crassula tabularis, commonly known as the stone rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge under sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Crassula tabularis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
