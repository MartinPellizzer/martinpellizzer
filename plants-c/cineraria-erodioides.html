Cineraria erodioides

Cineraria erodioides, commonly known as the rock cineraria, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
