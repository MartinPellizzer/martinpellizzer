Clitoria glycinoides var. megapotamica

Clitoria glycinoides var. megapotamica, commonly known as the yellow butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with trifoliate leaves and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
