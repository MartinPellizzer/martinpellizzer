Cardamine flaccida proles pusilla

Cardamine flaccida proles pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf water cress, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland floors. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often submerged or partially submerged in water. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and arranged in a raceme, typical of the family.

The species is characterized by its short stature and its ability to thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats.
