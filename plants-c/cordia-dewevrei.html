Cordia dewevrei

Cordia dewevrei, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Cordia, which includes over 200 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a snowball-like appearance. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is adapted to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
