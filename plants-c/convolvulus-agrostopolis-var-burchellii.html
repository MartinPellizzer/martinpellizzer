Convolvulus agrostopolis var. burchellii

Convolvulus agrostopolis var. burchellii, commonly known as Burchell's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces small, blue or purple flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Convolvulaceae family.
