Clematopsis scabiosifolia subsp. oliveri

Clematopsis scabiosifolia subsp.

oliveri, commonly known as the Oliver's Clematopsis, is a member of the Rosaceae family. It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring.

The plant features compound leaves with serrated margins, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
