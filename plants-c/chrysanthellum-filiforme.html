Chrysanthellum filiforme

Chrysanthellum filiforme, commonly known as the slender chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant colors and compact growth habit.
