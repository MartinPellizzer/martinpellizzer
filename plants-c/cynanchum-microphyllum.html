Cynanchum microphyllum

Cynanchum microphyllum, commonly known as small-leaved dogbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish flowers clustered in umbels.

The fruit is a follicle that splits open to release tiny seeds with silky appendages.
