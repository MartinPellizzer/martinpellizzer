Cnidoscolus shrevei

Cnidoscolus shrevei, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically large and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant's unique morphology and adaptability make it an interesting subject in botanical studies.
