Clematis welwitschii

Clematis welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch clematis, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet, often with a distinctive violet throat. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis welwitschii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
