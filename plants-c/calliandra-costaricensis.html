Calliandra costaricensis

Calliandra costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican calliandra, is a shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its feathery, pink or red flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

It is often found in dry to semi-arid regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential in soil stabilization.
