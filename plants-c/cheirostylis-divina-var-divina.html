Cheirostylis divina var. divina

Cheirostylis divina var. divina, commonly known as the divine orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically feature a deep purple or violet hue with intricate patterns. The plant has long, slender stems and produces clusters of small, showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
