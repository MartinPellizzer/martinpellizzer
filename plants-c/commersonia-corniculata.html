Commersonia corniculata

Commersonia corniculata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rock heath, is a flowering plant native to southern South America.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal environments. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of pink or white flowers. It is adapted to harsh conditions, including high salinity and strong winds.

Commersonia corniculata plays an important role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
