Coffea arabica var. humblotiana

Coffea arabica var. humblotiana, commonly known as the Humblotian coffee, is a rare and ancient variety of arabica coffee.

It is native to the highlands of Ethiopia and is characterized by its unique genetic makeup and distinct flavor profile. This variety is highly sought after by specialty coffee enthusiasts due to its complexity and rarity. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with specific soil and climate conditions.

Due to its limited cultivation, it remains a rare and valuable addition to the world of coffee.
