Capparis muelleri

Capparis muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's caper, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often followed by fleshy, red berries. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Capparis muelleri is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
