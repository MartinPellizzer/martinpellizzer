Centaurea aspera subsp. gentilii

Centaurea aspera subsp.

gentilii, commonly known as the rough centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow to orange-red flowers.

The plant features bristly, spiny leaves and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
