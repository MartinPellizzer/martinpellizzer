Chamaeiris carthaliniae

Chamaeiris carthaliniae, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble small stars, which bloom during the warmer months. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the undergrowth of tropical rainforests. Its leaves are elongated and dark green, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms.

Chamaeiris carthaliniae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
