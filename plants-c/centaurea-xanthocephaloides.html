Centaurea xanthocephaloides

Centaurea xanthocephaloides, commonly known as the yellow-headed knapweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields. It produces small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.
