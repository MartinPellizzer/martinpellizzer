Cynanchum komarovii

Cynanchum komarovii, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed or Komarov's milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is known for its milky sap, which is a distinguishing feature of the genus Cynanchum.
