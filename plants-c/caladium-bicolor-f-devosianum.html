Caladium bicolor f. devosianum

Caladium bicolor f. devosianum, commonly known as the "Devosianum" caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Caladium bicolor f. devosianum is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.
