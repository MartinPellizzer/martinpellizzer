Centaurea ornata var. tenuispina

Centaurea ornata var. tenuispina, commonly known as the slender spined centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in sunny, arid environments. Its slender stems and spiny leaves are adaptations to its harsh habitat, while its vibrant blue or purple flowers attract pollinators.
