Cornutia australis var. australis

Cornutia australis var. australis, commonly known as the Australian horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive horn-like seed pods and vibrant red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is found in regions with a Mediterranean climate. It has lance-shaped leaves and is often seen in coastal areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
