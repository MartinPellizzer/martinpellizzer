Chlorophytum minus

Chlorophytum minus, commonly known as spider plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with white stripes. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers on slender stems, which can be followed by bright red berries. It is a hardy plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Chlorophytum minus is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to propagate through offsets.
