Cotoneaster washanensis

Cotoneaster washanensis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its small, white to pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red berries in the fall, which are attractive to birds. Cotoneaster washanensis typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
