Cochlioda Sanguinea

Cochlioda sanguinea is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant red blooms and unique leaf structure. Native to specific regions with high humidity and nutrient-rich soil, it thrives in secluded, tropical environments. Its name, meaning "blood-red" in Greek, reflects the intense coloration of its flowers. Despite its beauty, it remains understudied due to its limited distribution and elusive habitat. This plant offers valuable insights into botanical adaptation and ecological resilience.
