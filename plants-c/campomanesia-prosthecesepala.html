Campomanesia prosthecesepala

Campomanesia prosthecesepala, commonly known as the Brazilian wax apple, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly waxy texture.
