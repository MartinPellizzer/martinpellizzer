Calathiana nevadensis

Calathiana nevadensis, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly found in the Sierra Nevada mountain range.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations, often in open meadows or along stream banks. Calathiana nevadensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also of ecological significance to local wildlife.
