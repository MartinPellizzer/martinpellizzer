Cyrtopodium paranaense var. pickelii

Cyrtopodium paranaense var. pickelii, commonly known as the Pickel’s lady’s slipper, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in seasonal habitats. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
