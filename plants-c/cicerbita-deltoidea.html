Cicerbita deltoidea

Cicerbita deltoidea, commonly known as the delta leaf bittercress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous species. The plant features small, white flowers and has oval, deltoid-shaped leaves that give it its common name. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates.

The plant is known for its peppery taste and has been used in some traditional herbal remedies.
