Ceropegia brevitubulata

Ceropegia brevitubulata, commonly known as the short-tubed ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species with fleshy, tuberous roots and slender, trailing stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a distinctive scent. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique morphology and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
