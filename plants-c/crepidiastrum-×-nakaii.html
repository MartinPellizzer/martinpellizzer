Crepidiastrum × nakaii

Crepidiastrum × nakaii, commonly known as the hybrid Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a hybrid between Crepidiastrum chinense and Crepidiastrum wilsonii, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
