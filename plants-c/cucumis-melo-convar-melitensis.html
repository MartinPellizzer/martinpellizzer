Cucumis melo convar. melitensis, commonly known as the cantaloupe melon, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its netted rind and sweet, aromatic flesh.

Its fruit is typically harvested when fully mature, and it is widely cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
