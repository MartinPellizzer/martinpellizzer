Coelorachis

Coelorachis, commonly known as the African orchid or African violet, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plants typically have sympodial growth, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Their flowers are usually showy, with intricate patterns and colors ranging from white to purple.

Coelorachis species are popular as ornamental plants due to their beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
