Coleus kilimandschari

Coleus kilimandschari, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro coleus, is a striking ornamental plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep reds and purples to bright yellows and oranges. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting veins. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor spaces.

While it is not typically a flowering plant, its foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes.
