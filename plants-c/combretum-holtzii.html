Combretum holtzii

Combretum holtzii, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that turn red or orange during the dry season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
