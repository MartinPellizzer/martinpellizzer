Chlorophytum kombense

Chlorophytum kombense, commonly known as the African Spear or Kombense snake plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems with long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often marked with white or light green stripes. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are succulent, allowing it to store water efficiently, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers on slender stalks.

Chlorophytum kombense is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
