Croton aristophlebius

Croton aristophlebius, commonly known as the white croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are usually green with prominent veins. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in panicles, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to gardens and indoor plant collections.
