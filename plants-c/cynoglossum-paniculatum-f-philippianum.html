Cynoglossum paniculatum f. philippianum

Cynoglossum paniculatum f. philippianum, commonly known as the Philippine bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in paniculate inflorescences. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
