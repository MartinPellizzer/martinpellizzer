Carelia conyzodes

Carelia conyzodes, commonly known as the silky leaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by small, parachute-like seeds.

Carelia conyzodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
