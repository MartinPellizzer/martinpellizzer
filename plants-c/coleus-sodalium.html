Coleus sodalium

Coleus sodalium, commonly known as the velvetleaf coleus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Coleus sodalium is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses due to its striking appearance.
