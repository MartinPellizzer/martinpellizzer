Cylindropsis

Cylindropsis, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their upright, cylindrical stems and dense clusters of small, showy flowers. The leaves are typically velvety and dark green, often with a waxy coating. Cylindropsis species are popular as houseplants due to their attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

They thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
