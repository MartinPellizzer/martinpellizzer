Carduus × personatoformis subsp. subinteger

Carduus × personatoformis subsp.

subinteger, commonly known as the broadleaf thistle. It is a hybrid species within the thistle family, characterized by its robust, spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is found in regions with temperate climates. Its leaves are broad and often have a waxy coating, while the flowers are arranged in a compact head.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
