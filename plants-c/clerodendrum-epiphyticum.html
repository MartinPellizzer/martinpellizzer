Clerodendrum epiphyticum

Clerodendrum epiphyticum, commonly known as the hanging clerodendrum, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, trailing stems and clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and can grow up to 10 centimeters in length. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Clerodendrum epiphyticum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
