Conopodium cyminum

Conopodium cyminum, commonly known as bishop's cap, is a flowering plant in the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of small white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
