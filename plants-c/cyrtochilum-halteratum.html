Cyrtochilum halteratum

Cyrtochilum halteratum, commonly known as the winged orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with yellow and green hues, often with a distinct wing-like structure on the petals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cyrtochilum halteratum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
