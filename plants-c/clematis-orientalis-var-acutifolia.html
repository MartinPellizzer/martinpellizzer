Clematis orientalis var. acutifolia

Clematis orientalis var. acutifolia, commonly known as the sharp-leaved oriental clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with acuminate tips, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
