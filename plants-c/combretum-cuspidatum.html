Combretum cuspidatum

Combretum cuspidatum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
