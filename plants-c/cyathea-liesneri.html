Cyathea liesneri

Cyathea liesneri, commonly known as the Liesner's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant exhibits a distinctive trunk that is covered in remnants of old leaf bases, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Cyathea liesneri plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
