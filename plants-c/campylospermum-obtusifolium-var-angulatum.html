Campylospermum obtusifolium var. angulatum

Campylospermum obtusifolium var. angulatum, commonly known as the angular campylospermum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its angular, often spiny stems and leaves that are broad and rounded at the tip. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
