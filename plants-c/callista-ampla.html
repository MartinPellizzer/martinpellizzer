Callista ampla

Callista ampla, commonly known as the large-flowered callista, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its showy, daisy-like flowers bloom in shades of yellow and orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Callista ampla is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
