Cryptantha humilis var. shantzii

Cryptantha humilis var. shantzii, commonly known as the Shantz's cryptantha, is a low-growing annual herb native to the western United States.

It typically features slender stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
