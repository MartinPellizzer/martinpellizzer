Caladium stangeanum

Caladium stangeanum, commonly known as the Stange's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium stangeanum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
