Cinnamomum dictyoneuron

Cinnamomum dictyoneuron, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or Cinnamomum, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern China and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark, which contain essential oils responsible for its distinctive scent. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a glossy surface and a serrated margin.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark purple berries, which are an important food source for various wildlife species.
