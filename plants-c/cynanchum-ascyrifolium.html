Cynanchum ascyrifolium

Cynanchum ascyrifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which is characterized by its milky sap and often hairy foliage. The plant typically features slender stems with opposite, narrow, and often hairy leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, usually yellow or greenish in color.
