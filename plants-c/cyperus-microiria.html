Cyperus microiria

Cyperus microiria, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Cyperus microiria plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
