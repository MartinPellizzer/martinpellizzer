Cotoneaster argenteus

Cotoneaster argenteus, commonly known as silver cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster argenteus is also valued for its role in supporting local wildlife, particularly birds.
