Carduus × moritzii

Carduus × moritzii, commonly known as the garden thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and is native to Europe. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in gardens and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant.
