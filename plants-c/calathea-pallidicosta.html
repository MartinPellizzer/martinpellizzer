Calathea pallidicosta

Calathea pallidicosta, commonly known as the pale-costed calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with pale green veins contrasting against deep green leaves. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
