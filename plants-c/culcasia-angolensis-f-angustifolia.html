Culcasia angolensis f. angustifolia

Culcasia angolensis f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture.

The leaves are usually broad and taper to a point, with a prominent midrib and a waxy surface. It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

It prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
