Cotyledon tenuicaulis

Cotyledon tenuicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with striking red or purple markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, or purple during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
