Carex longqishanensis

Carex longqishanensis, commonly known as the Longqi Mountain sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Guangxi and Guizhou provinces. This plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grass-like leaves and spike-like flower clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
