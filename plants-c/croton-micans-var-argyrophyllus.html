Croton micans var. argyrophyllus

Croton micans var. argyrophyllus, commonly known as the silver-leaf croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its glossy, variegated foliage.

Its leaves are typically a mix of deep green and silvery-white, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
