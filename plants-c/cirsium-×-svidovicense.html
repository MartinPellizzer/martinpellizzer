Cirsium × svidovicense

Cirsium × svidovicense, commonly known as the Svidovice thistle, is a hybrid plant species within the genus Cirsium, which includes various species of thistles.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers, typical of many thistle species. This plant is native to Central Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its growth habit is perennial, and it can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
