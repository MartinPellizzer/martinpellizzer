Corydalis Fedtschenkoana Subsp. Metallica

Corydalis fedtschenkoana subsp. metallica is a rare and striking member of the Fumariaceae family known for its vibrant, metallic-blue flowers. Native to the high altitudes of the Russian Far East, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments under the shade of dense forests. Its unique coloration and delicate structure make it a prized specimen among horticulturists and botanists. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and difficult to cultivate outside its native habitat. This plant exemplifies the extraordinary diversity found in the plant kingdom.
