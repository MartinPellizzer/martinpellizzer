Cosmianthemum longiflorum

Cosmianthemum longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered cosmos, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its showy, single or double flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with slender, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
