Coccothrinax acuminata

Coccothrinax acuminata, commonly known as the slender palm or the narrow-leaf palm, is a species of palm native to the Caribbean.

It is a small to medium-sized tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has a slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are narrow and elongated. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces edible fruits that are consumed by various wildlife.

The plant is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, making it a common sight in tropical coastal areas.
