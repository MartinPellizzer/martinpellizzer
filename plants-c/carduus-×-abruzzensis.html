Carduus × abruzzensis

Carduus × abruzzensis, commonly known as the Abruzzi thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads that resemble those of other thistle species. Native to the mountainous regions of central Italy, particularly the Abruzzo region, it thrives in rocky and dry environments. The plant is known for its robust growth and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
