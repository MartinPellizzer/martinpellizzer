Cynorkis constellata

Cynorkis constellata, commonly known as the starry orchid, is a species native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Cynorkis constellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
