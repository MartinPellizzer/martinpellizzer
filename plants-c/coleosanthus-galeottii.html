Coleosanthus galeottii

Coleosanthus galeottii, commonly known as the golden spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that resemble spider-like blooms, which are both showy and unique. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a waxy texture, complementing the dramatic floral display.

Coleosanthus galeottii thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
