Carissa edulis var. microphylla

Carissa edulis var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf Natal plum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. The fruit is edible, with a sweet and tart flavor, and is often used in local cuisines.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
