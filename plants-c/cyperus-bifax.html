Cyperus bifax

Cyperus bifax, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant has slender, grass-like stems that grow close to the ground, with small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Its leaves are narrow and slightly curved, often with a waxy coating.

Cyperus bifax is typically found in wetland habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies.
