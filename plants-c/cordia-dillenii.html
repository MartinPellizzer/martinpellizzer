Cordia dillenii

Cordia dillenii, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The tree produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
