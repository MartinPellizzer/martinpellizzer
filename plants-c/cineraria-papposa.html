Cineraria papposa

Cineraria papposa, commonly known as the woolly cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a distinctive addition to Mediterranean-style landscapes.
