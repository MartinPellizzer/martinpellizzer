Centaurea pullata var. acaulis

Centaurea pullata var. acaulis, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, bright yellow flowers with reddish-purple bracts. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
