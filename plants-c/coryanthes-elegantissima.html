Coryanthes elegantissima

Coryanthes elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant coryanthe, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, elongated inflorescence bearing numerous brightly colored, lip-like petals. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange with intricate patterns, attracting specific pollinators like wasps.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
