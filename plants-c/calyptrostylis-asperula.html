Calyptrostylis asperula

Calyptrostylis asperula, commonly known as the rough-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests or understorey areas.

Calyptrostylis asperula is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
