Calceolaria villosissima

Calceolaria villosissima, commonly known as the hairy slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Peru and Bolivia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are often violet or purple in color. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Calceolaria villosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
