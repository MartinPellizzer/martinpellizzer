Cytisus pauciflorus

Cytisus pauciflorus, commonly known as the sparse-flowered broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure. The plant features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Cytisus pauciflorus is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
