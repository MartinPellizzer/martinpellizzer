Cattleya wallisii var. splendens

Cattleya wallisii var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendens Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy blooms.

It features large, pendulous flowers with a ruffled lip and a wide range of color variations, including pink, red, and white. The plant is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It has thick, fleshy roots that store moisture, making it relatively easy to care for in a home setting.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and reliability in cultivation.
