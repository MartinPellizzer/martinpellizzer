Cynometra leptantha

Cynometra leptantha, commonly known as the slender ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and compound leaves with elliptical leaflets. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

Cynometra leptantha is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its timber and ecological importance.
