Centaurea saxicola subsp. saxicola

Centaurea saxicola subsp.

saxicola, commonly known as the saxicola centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to purple flowers in summer. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in crevices or on slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
