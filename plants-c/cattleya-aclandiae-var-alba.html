Cattleya aclandiae var. alba

Cattleya aclandiae var. alba, commonly known as the White Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking white petals and vibrant yellow lip.

Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It produces large, fragrant flowers that bloom once a year, typically in late spring or early summer. The plant has thick, fleshy roots that store water, allowing it to survive in well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and collectors.
