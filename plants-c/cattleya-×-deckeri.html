Cattleya × deckeri

Cattleya × deckeri, commonly known as the Yellow Lady's Slipper, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant blooms.

Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces large, showy flowers with a yellow pouch-like lip. It is a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and garden displays due to its ornamental value.

Cattleya × deckeri requires well-draining soil and consistent care to ensure optimal growth and blooming.
