Cheiranthus × mutallio

Cheiranthus × mutallio, commonly known as the hybrid wallflower, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Cheiranthus cheiri and Cheiranthus allionii, resulting in a plant with distinctive floral characteristics. The plant typically produces showy, bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its foliage consists of slender, lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair.

This hybrid is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
