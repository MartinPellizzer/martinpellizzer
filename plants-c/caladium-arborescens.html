Caladium arborescens

Caladium arborescens, commonly known as the tree caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant has a woody stem that allows it to grow as a shrub or small tree. Its foliage is often variegated with shades of white, pink, and green.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
