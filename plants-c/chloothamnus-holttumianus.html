Chloothamnus holttumianus

Chloothamnus holttumianus, commonly known as the Holttum's goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces numerous tiny florets arranged in composite flower heads.

It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing adaptations to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.
