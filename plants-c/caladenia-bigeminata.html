Caladenia bigeminata

Caladenia bigeminata, commonly known as the twin-lobed spider orchid, is an endemic species of Western Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, wiry stem that bears a single, strikingly colored flower. The orchid's flower has a distinctive, elongated labellum that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in sandy soils and is typically found in open habitats such as heathlands and woodland areas.
