Combretum stigmarium

Combretum stigmarium, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces small, winged fruits that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.
