Calycothrix brunioides

Calycothrix brunioides, commonly known as the broadleaf ironwood, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.

Calycothrix brunioides is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
