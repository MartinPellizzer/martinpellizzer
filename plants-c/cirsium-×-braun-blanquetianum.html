Cirsium × braun-blanquetianum

Cirsium × braun-blanquetianum, commonly known as the Braun-Blanquet's thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads that bloom in late summer. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky and dry habitats. The plant is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural reserves.
