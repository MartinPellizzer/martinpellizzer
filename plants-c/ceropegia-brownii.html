Ceropegia brownii

Ceropegia brownii, commonly known as the Brown's African violet, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, tuberous roots and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
