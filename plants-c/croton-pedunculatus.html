Croton pedunculatus

Croton pedunculatus, commonly known as the flame tree or the red flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton pedunculatus is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
