Capsella bursa-pastoris f. pinnatifida

Capsella bursa-pastoris f. pinnatifida, commonly known as the heartleaf velvetleaf, is a variant of the common chickweed.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, white flowers. The leaves of this form are pinnatifid, meaning they are deeply lobed with a distinctive heart-shaped appearance. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens and along roadsides.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds.
