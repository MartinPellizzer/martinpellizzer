Crocanthemum scoparium var. scoparium

Crocanthemum scoparium var. scoparium, commonly known as silverleaf everlasting.

It is a perennial herb native to southern Africa, characterized by its silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its fibrous roots and drought tolerance make it a resilient addition to xeriscaped environments.
