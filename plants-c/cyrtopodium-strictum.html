Cyrtopodium strictum

Cyrtopodium strictum, commonly known as the rigid orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it has a rhizomatous root system.

Cyrtopodium strictum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
