Cryptocoryne alba

Cryptocoryne alba, commonly known as the white cryptocoryne, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves that are typically light green to white in color. This plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is often used in freshwater aquariums for its ornamental value. Cryptocoryne alba is known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters, making it a hardy choice for aquarists.

Its growth is relatively slow, and it can form dense underwater foliage when provided with adequate nutrients and care.
