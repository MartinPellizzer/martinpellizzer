Colocasia esculenta f. rotundifolia

Colocasia esculenta f. rotundifolia, commonly known as the African arrowroot or round-leaf elephant ear, is a cultivar of the taro plant.

It is characterized by its large, rounded, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces edible corms that are starchy and often used in cooking. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and culinary uses.
