Cyphostemma overlaetii

Cyphostemma overlaetii, commonly known as the Zulu star or African star.

It is a climbing plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its woody stems and distinctive star-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers that are often followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
