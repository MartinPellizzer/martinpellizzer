Chrysodiscus niveus

Chrysodiscus niveus, commonly known as the white disc lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its large, white, disc-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad at the base and taper towards the tips. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among horticulturists.
