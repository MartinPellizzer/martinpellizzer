Coryphantha wohlschlageri

Coryphantha wohlschlageri, commonly known as the Wohlschlager's prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the summer months, which are followed by edible, red fruits. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Coryphantha wohlschlageri is well adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
