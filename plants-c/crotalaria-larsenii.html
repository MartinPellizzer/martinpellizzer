Crotalaria larsenii

Crotalaria larsenii, commonly known as the Larsen's spurred cowpea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with erect stems and compound leaves. Its small, yellow flowers are followed by pods containing several seeds.

Crotalaria larsenii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
