Cyperus meyenianus var. breviradiatus

Cyperus meyenianus var. breviradiatus, commonly known as the narrow-rayed flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the sedge family. It thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies.

This species is adapted to moist soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for aquatic life.
