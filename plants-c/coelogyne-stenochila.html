Coelogyne stenochila

Coelogyne stenochila, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It requires well-draining potting media and consistent moisture to flourish.

Coelogyne stenochila is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
