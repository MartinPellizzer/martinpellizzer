Cyclosorus mollissimus

Cyclosorus mollissimus, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique aquatic plant characterized by its soft, velvety leaves.

It is native to the wetlands of South America, particularly in Brazil and Argentina. The plant thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in marshy environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a dense, hairy texture that gives it its common name.

Cyclosorus mollissimus is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in maintaining water quality in its natural habitat.
