Cydonia cydonia var. oblonga

Cydonia cydonia var. oblonga, commonly known as the European pear, is a variant of the common pear species.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped fruit, which is typically smaller than the standard variety. The plant produces a sweet, juicy fruit with a thin, edible skin and a soft, buttery flesh. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its culinary uses and ornamental value in gardens.
