Cypripedium purpuratum

Cypripedium purpuratum, commonly known as the purple lady's slipper, is a perennial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a distinctive purple pouch-like lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually broad and elliptical, and it produces a single flower per stem.

Cypripedium purpuratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its ecological and medicinal potential.
