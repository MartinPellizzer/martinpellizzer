Cynanchum nummulariifolium

Cynanchum nummulariifolium, commonly known as the round-leaf milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features slender, ascending stems that bear opposite, round or kidney-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its roots are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is characterized by its unique leaf morphology and adaptability to various soil conditions.
