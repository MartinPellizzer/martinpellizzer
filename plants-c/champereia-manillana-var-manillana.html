Champereia manillana var. manillana

Champereia manillana var. manillana, commonly known as the Manila champereia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, with clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers appearing in spring. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
