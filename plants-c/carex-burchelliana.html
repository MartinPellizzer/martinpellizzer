Carex burchelliana

Carex burchelliana, commonly known as Burchell's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, grasses, and rushes. The plant features slender, triangular stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often growing in dense clumps.

Carex burchelliana is adapted to moist environments and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks.
