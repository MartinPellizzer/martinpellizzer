Cheiranthus × allionii

Cheiranthus × allionii, commonly known as the hybrid wallflower, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its showy flowers, which range in color from yellow to red, bloom in spring and early summer.

Cheiranthus × allionii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
