Calpidia gigantocarpa

Calpidia gigantocarpa, commonly known as the giant fruit calpidia, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces showy, yellowish flowers with a distinct, fragrant scent that attracts pollinators. Its leaves are broad and oval-shaped, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy coating.
