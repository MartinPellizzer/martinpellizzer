Cyathea hotteana

Cyathea hotteana, commonly known as the Hotte's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Cyathea hotteana plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to biodiversity and soil stability.
