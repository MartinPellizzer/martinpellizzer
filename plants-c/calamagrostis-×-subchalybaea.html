Calamagrostis × subchalybaea

Calamagrostis × subchalybaea, commonly known as the silver feather reedgrass, is a hybrid grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms and feathery, silvery-blue plumes that create a striking visual display. The plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a soft, bluish-green color that adds ornamental value to landscapes.

It is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
