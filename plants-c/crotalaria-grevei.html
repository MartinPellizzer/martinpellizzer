Crotalaria grevei

Crotalaria grevei, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and erect, branching stems. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Crotalaria grevei is known for its role in ecological systems, particularly in supporting pollinators and improving soil structure.
