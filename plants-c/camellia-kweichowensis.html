Camellia kweichowensis

Camellia kweichowensis, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Camellia kweichowensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
