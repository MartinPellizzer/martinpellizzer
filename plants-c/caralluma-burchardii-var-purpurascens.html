Caralluma burchardii var. purpurascens

Caralluma burchardii var. purpurascens, commonly known as the red-flowered caralluma, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of India.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, spineless stems and vibrant red flowers. The plant thrives in hot, dry climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are typically small and scale-like, reducing water loss in its harsh environment.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
