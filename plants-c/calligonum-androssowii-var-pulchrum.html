Calligonum androssowii var. pulchrum

Calligonum androssowii var. pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in sandy and saline soils, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise desolate habitat. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in a fine layer of hair, which helps reduce water loss.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing dunes and preventing soil erosion.
