Crinum venosum

Crinum venosum, commonly known as the veiny crinum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features strap-like, broad leaves that are often veined, giving it its common name. Crinum venosum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
