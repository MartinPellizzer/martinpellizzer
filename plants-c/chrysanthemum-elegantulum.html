Chrysanthemum elegantulum

Chrysanthemum elegantulum, commonly known as the elegant chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its blooming period extends from late summer through early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens during the cooler months.
