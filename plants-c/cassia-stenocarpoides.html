Cassia stenocarpoides

Cassia stenocarpoides, commonly known as the African cassia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

Cassia stenocarpoides is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
