Cryptocarya kwangtungensis

Cryptocarya kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong cryptocarya, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which are usually elliptical and have a smooth texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by oval-shaped berries.

Cryptocarya kwangtungensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
