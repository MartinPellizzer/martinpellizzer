Callistemon suberosus var. microphyllus

Callistemon suberosus var. microphyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
