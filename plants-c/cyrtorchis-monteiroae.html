Cyrtorchis monteiroae

Cyrtorchis monteiroae, commonly known as the Monteiro's orchid, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the mountainous regions of Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral morphology, featuring intricate, spurred petals and a distinctive coloration. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests at high altitudes. Its small size and specialized habitat make it highly susceptible to environmental changes and habitat loss.

Due to its limited distribution and conservation status, it is considered a species of concern in botanical conservation efforts.
