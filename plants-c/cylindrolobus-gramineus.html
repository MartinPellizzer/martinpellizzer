Cylindrolobus gramineus

Cylindrolobus gramineus, commonly known as the slender leaved grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments, often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its flowers are small and clustered, forming dense inflorescences that are often wind-pollinated.

Cylindrolobus gramineus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
