Cleome leptorachis

Cleome leptorachis, commonly known as the slender cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of small, showy yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Cleome leptorachis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
