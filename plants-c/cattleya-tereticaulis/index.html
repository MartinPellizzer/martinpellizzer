Cattleya Tereticaulis

Cattleya tereticaulis is a unique orchid native to the humid lowlands of Central and South America. This plant is known for its striking, elongated petals and vibrant floral displays. It thrives in warm, moist environments with high light exposure. Its distinctive shape and color make it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish.
