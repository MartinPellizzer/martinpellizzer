Crambe feuilleei

Crambe feuilleei, commonly known as the leafy crambe, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its basal rosette of large, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
