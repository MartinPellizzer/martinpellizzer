Cistus × skanbergii

Cistus × skanbergii, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil. Its leaves are aromatic and often covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Cistus × skanbergii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional use in herbal remedies.
