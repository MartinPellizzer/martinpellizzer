Crassula atropurpurea var. anomala

Crassula atropurpurea var. anomala, commonly known as the Forest Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a distinctive red or purple hue on the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
