Cleisostoma tenuicaule

Cleisostoma tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed cleisostoma, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendant-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has thin, wiry stems and small, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and well-draining soil.

Cleisostoma tenuicaule is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
