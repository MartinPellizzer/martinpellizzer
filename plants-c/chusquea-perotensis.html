Chusquea perotensis

Chusquea perotensis, commonly known as the Perot's bamboo, is a species of grass in the Chusquea genus.

It is native to the cloud forests of Central America, particularly in countries like Costa Rica and Panama. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are narrow and often have a distinctive silver or grayish underside.

Chusquea perotensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
