Chytroglossa aurata f. luteoviridis

Chytroglossa aurata f. luteoviridis, commonly known as the yellow-green orchid, is a variant of the orchid species Chytroglossa aurata.

This plant is characterized by its striking yellow-green foliage, which sets it apart from the typical green leaves of the species. It is a terrestrial orchid, typically found in moist, shaded environments in its native habitat. The leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

While it does not produce showy flowers, its unique foliage makes it a desirable ornamental plant for collectors and enthusiasts.
