Chlorophytum acrothyrsum

Chlorophytum acrothyrsum, commonly known as the bowstring hemp, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that resemble bowstrings. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by dark blue berries. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
