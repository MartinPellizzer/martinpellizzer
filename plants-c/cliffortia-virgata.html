Cliffortia virgata

Cliffortia virgata, commonly known as the silver leaf bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its silvery-white undersides on its narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and mountainous environments.

Cliffortia virgata is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and resilience to coastal conditions.
