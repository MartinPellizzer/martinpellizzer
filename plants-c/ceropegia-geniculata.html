Ceropegia geniculata

Ceropegia geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag fig, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky surfaces or in sandy soils.
