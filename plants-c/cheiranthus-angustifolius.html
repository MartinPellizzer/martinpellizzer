Cheiranthus angustifolius

Cheiranthus angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaf candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
