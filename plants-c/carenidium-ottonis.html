Carenidium ottonis

Carenidium ottonis, commonly known as the Otto's cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its columnar stem and spineless appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
