Cattleya schilleriana var. amaliana

Cattleya schilleriana var. amaliana, commonly known as the Amaliana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant blooms.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features tall, sturdy stems with large, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, and red. It is a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and garden displays due to its ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics include lance-shaped leaves and a complex, branched root system typical of orchids.
