Conospermum flexuosum f. ramosissimum

Conospermum flexuosum f. ramosissimum, commonly known as the silver-leaved conospermum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pink, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact form and year-round visual interest.
