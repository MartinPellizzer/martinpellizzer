Cadellia monostylis

Cadellia monostylis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cadellia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the spring and summer seasons. It is typically found in heathland and woodland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Cadellia monostylis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
