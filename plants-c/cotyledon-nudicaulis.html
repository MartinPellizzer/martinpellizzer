Cotyledon nudicaulis

Cotyledon nudicaulis, commonly known as the nakedseed cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that bloom in spring. It is often grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and ease of care.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in arid conditions make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
