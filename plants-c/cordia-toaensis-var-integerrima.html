Cordia toaensis var. integerrima

Cordia toaensis var. integerrima, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white, ball-shaped inflorescences that resemble snowballs, typically blooming during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
