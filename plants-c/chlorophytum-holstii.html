Chlorophytum holstii

Chlorophytum holstii, commonly known as the snake plant, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with striking white or silver markings. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal houseplant. Its leaves are fleshy and store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments.

Chlorophytum holstii is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, as it can help remove toxins from indoor air.
