Cycas gracilis var. viridis

Cycas gracilis var. viridis, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a cycad species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, feather-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces cycad-like cones, with male and female cones on separate plants. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
