Calyptrostegia gracilis

Calyptrostegia gracilis, commonly known as the slender calyptra orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Calyptrostegia gracilis thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in montane forests.

Its botanical features make it a unique and relatively rare species within the orchid family.
