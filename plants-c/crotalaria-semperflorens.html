Crotalaria semperflorens

Crotalaria semperflorens, commonly known as the everblooming crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom continuously throughout the growing season. Its leaves are trifoliate, meaning they consist of three leaflets, and it has a bushy growth habit.

Crotalaria semperflorens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
