Cryptandra micrantha

Cryptandra micrantha, commonly known as the slender cryptandra, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with narrow, woody stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Cryptandra micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
