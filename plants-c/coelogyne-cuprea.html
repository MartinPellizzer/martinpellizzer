Coelogyne cuprea

Coelogyne cuprea, commonly known as the red lady's tresses, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne cuprea is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
