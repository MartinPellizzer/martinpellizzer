Chloris aristida

Chloris aristida, commonly known as slender three-awn, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and distinctive awns that give it a fluffy appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

Chloris aristida is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in restoration projects due to its hardy nature.
