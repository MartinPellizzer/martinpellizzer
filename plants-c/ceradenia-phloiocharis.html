Ceradenia phloiocharis

Ceradenia phloiocharis, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Colombia.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
