Cryptangium claussenii

Cryptangium claussenii, commonly known as the golden bottle tree, is a unique plant native to parts of Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bottle-shaped trunk and thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its root system is deep and robust, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

Cryptangium claussenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
