Chodanthus splendens var. genuina

Chodanthus splendens var. genuina, commonly known as the Splendid Chodanthus, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from deep red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, silvery fuzz.
