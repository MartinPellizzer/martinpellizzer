Calamus reinwardtii

Calamus reinwardtii, commonly known as the Reinwardt's broadleaf arrowroot, is a member of the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of many aroid species.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.
