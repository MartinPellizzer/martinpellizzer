Centunculus arvensis

Centunculus arvensis, commonly known as the field centaury, is a small flowering plant belonging to the family Gentianaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are slender and branched.

Centunculus arvensis is often found in open habitats and is considered a characteristic species of certain ecological communities.
