Cattleya Reginae

Cattleya reginae is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America. Known for its large, showy flowers and vibrant colors, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. This species is particularly noted for its strong fragrance, which adds to its allure. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. Cattleya reginae is both a beautiful and rewarding plant for those who enjoy cultivating orchids.
