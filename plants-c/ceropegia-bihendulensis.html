Ceropegia bihendulensis

Ceropegia bihendulensis, commonly known as the Bihendulensis String of Hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers that are often fragrant, attracting pollinators such as moths and butterflies.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
