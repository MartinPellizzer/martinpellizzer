Coprosma nephelephila

Coprosma nephelephila, commonly known as the silver-leaved coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically silver or grey-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red or orange berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Coprosma nephelephila is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and seasonal color changes.
