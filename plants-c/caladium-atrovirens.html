Caladium atrovirens

Caladium atrovirens, commonly known as the black velvet caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically not showy, but the foliage is the main ornamental feature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Caladium atrovirens is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and as a houseplant.
