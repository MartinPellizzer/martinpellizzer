Calamus austroguangxiensis

Calamus austroguangxiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arales.

It is a climbing plant characterized by its long, flexible stems and pinnate leaves. Native to southern China, particularly the Guangxi region, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited use in traditional medicine.
