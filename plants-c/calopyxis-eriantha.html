Calopyxis eriantha

Calopyxis eriantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
