Cyrtochilum sphinx

Cyrtochilum sphinx, commonly known as the Sphinx orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, hooded flower structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are often fragrant and come in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Cyrtochilum sphinx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
