Calamagrostis canescens f. pallida

Calamagrostis canescens f. pallida, commonly known as the silver bluestem grass, is a variant of the silver bluestem plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-blue foliage, which gives it a striking appearance in gardens and natural landscapes. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, making it suitable for wetland restoration and rain garden projects. Its upright growth habit and fine texture make it an attractive ornamental grass.

It blooms with small, fluffy panicles in the summer, adding visual interest to the landscape.
