Centaurea kotschyi subsp. kotschyi

Centaurea kotschyi subsp.

kotschyi, commonly known as the Kotschyi's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves.
