Chlorophytum viridescens var. majus

Chlorophytum viridescens var. majus, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail or Tiger's Head plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically light green with striking yellow or white stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that emerge from the base of the leaves. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

It is a popular indoor plant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
