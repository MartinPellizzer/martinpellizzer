Codiaeum heroicum

Codiaeum heroicum, commonly known as the flame leaf or flame plant, is a striking tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from red, orange, and yellow to deep maroon, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, warm climates. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in gardens.
