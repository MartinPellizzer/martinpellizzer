Cypripedium × lemoineanum

Cypripedium × lemoineanum, commonly known as the Lemoine's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped pink or purple flowers with a distinctive, pouch-like lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich soils. Its leaves are broad and ovate, and it produces a single flower per stem, which blooms in late spring to early summer.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
