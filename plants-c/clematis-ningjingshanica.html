Clematis ningjingshanica

Clematis ningjingshanica, commonly known as the Ningjingshan Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, typically white or pale blue flowers. The plant features compound leaves with trifoliate or pinnate arrangements, and its stems are often covered with fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
