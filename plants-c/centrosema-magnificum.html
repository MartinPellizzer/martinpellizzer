Centrosema magnificum

Centrosema magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent crownvetch, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil fertility through nitrogen fixation. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in restoration projects due to its hardiness.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
