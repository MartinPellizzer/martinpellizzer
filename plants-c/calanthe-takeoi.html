Calanthe takeoi

Calanthe takeoi, commonly known as the Japanese calanthe, is a rare and elegant orchid native to Japan.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and showy blooms.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
