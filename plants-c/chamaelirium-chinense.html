Chamaelirium chinense

Chamaelirium chinense, commonly known as Chinese narrow-leaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces small, clustered flowers during the summer months.

Chamaelirium chinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
