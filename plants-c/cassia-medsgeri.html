Cassia medsgeri

Cassia medsgeri, commonly known as the Ethiopian cassia, is a shrub native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Cassia medsgeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local communities.
