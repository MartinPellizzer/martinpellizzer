Chrysanthemopsis

Chrysanthemopsis, commonly known as the silverleaf chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Chrysanthemopsis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
