Chamaecrista leschenaultiana

Chamaecrista leschenaultiana, commonly known as the Leschenault's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
