Crowea saligna var. macrantha

Crowea saligna var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered crowea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its long-lasting blooms and attractive foliage.
