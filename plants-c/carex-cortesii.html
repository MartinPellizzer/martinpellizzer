Carex cortesii

Carex cortesii, commonly known as the Cortes sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats. It has grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers in the summer.

Carex cortesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
