Calamagrostis × strigosa

Calamagrostis × strigosa, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It prefers full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Silver bluestem is also valued for its role in erosion control and wildlife habitat.
