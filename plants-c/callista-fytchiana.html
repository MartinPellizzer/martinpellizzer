Callista fytchiana

Callista fytchiana, commonly known as the Fytch's glorybower, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features lance-shaped, glossy leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique floral display, it is often cultivated in horticultural collections.
