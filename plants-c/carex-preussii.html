Carex preussii

Carex preussii, commonly known as the Preuss sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and cylindrical inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Carex preussii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
