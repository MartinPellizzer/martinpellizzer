Columnea tenella

Columnea tenella, commonly known as the silverleaf columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. Columnea tenella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and light conditions.
