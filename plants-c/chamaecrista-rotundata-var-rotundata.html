Chamaecrista rotundata var. rotundata

Chamaecrista rotundata var. rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, yellow, pea-like flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces distinctive, spiny seed pods that contain small, hard seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its growth habit makes it both a weed and a potential forage plant in certain environments.
