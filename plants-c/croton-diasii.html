Croton diasii

Croton diasii, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in cyathia, a unique structure typical of the genus. Croton diasii is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
