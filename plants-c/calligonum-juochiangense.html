Calligonum juochiangense

Calligonum juochiangense, commonly known as the Chinese sandbar buckthorn, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of northern China.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. The plant is well adapted to harsh environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and salt resistance. It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Its botanical features make it an important species in xerophytic ecosystems.
