Crotalaria striata f. latifoliolata

Crotalaria striata f. latifoliolata, commonly known as the broad-leaved cowpea, is a variant of the species Crotalaria striata.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and broad, ovate leaves. The plant belongs to the family Fabaceae, also known as the legume or pea family. It is native to tropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and agricultural fields.

Its leaves and flowers are used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein in some regions.
