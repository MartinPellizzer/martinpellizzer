Ceropegia prostrata

Ceropegia prostrata, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a trailing perennial that produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
