Cochleanthes Wailesiana

Cochleanthes wailesiana is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America. Known for its vibrant colors and unique floral structure, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in montane forests. Its delicate petals and intricate patterns make it a subject of both scientific study and artistic admiration. Due to habitat loss, Cochleanthes wailesiana is now considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
