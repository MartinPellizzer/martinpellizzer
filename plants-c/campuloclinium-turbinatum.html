Campuloclinium turbinatum

Campuloclinium turbinatum, commonly known as the turbanweed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

Campuloclinium turbinatum is often considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
