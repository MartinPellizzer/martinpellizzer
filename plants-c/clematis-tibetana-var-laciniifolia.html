Clematis tibetana var. laciniifolia

Clematis tibetana var. laciniifolia, commonly known as the Tibetan fringed clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the high altitudes of Tibet.

It features distinctive, finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
