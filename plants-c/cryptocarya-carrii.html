Cryptocarya carrii

Cryptocarya carrii, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is a tall, evergreen tree that can reach heights of up to 30 meters. The plant features a trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark and large, pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, and it produces dark, fleshy berries.

Cryptocarya carrii is native to New Zealand and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.
