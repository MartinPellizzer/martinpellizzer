Cnidoscolus cervii

Cnidoscolus cervii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead or arrowleaf balsam, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical regions.

Cnidoscolus cervii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
