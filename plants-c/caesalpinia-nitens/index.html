Caesalpinia Nitens

Caesalpinia tree, scientifically known as Caesalpinia nitens, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is renowned for its vibrant, flame-like yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, adding a burst of color to its surroundings. The tree's compound leaves and distinctive pod-like fruits make it easily identifiable in its natural habitat. Often cultivated for ornamental purposes, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits have made it a popular choice for gardens and urban landscapes.
