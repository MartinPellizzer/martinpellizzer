Calamagrostis × adylssuensis

Calamagrostis × adylssuensis, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a hybrid grass species native to the wetlands of North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks that are covered in silvery-gray hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces feathery plumes in late summer, which add ornamental value to gardens and natural landscapes. It thrives in moist to wet soil conditions and is often found in marshes, swales, and along the edges of ponds.

Calamagrostis × adylssuensis is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
