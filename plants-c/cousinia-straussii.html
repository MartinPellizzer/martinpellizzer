Cousinia straussii

Cousinia straussii, commonly known as Strauss's cottonweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, woolly stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellow to reddish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky habitats.

Cousinia straussii is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
