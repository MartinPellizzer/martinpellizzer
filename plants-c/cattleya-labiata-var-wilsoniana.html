Cattleya labiata var. wilsoniana

Cattleya labiata var. wilsoniana, commonly known as Wilson's cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It features showy, pendulous blooms with ruffled petals and a distinctive, often contrasting lip. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
