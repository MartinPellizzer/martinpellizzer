Centaurea × gusuleacii

Centaurea × gusuleacii, commonly known as the hybrid garden centaury, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Centaurea cyanus (blueweed) and Centaurea solstitialis (yellow starthistle). The plant typically features vibrant blue to purple flower heads surrounded by pale yellow ray flowers, which bloom in summer. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often reddish in color.

Centaurea × gusuleacii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
