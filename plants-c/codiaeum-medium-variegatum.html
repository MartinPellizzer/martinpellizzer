Codiaeum medium-variegatum

Codiaeum medium-variegatum, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored foliage that features shades of green, yellow, and red. The leaves are typically large, palmate, and have a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
