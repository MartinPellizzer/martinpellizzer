Ceropegia poluniniana

Ceropegia poluniniana, commonly known as the Madagascar starfish plant, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that resemble a starfish. The plant has fleshy, succulent stems and leaves, adapted to survive in arid environments. It produces small, tubular flowers that are often pale green or white in color.

Ceropegia poluniniana is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
