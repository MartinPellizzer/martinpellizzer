Crataegus denaria

Crataegus denaria, commonly known as the common hawthorn.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features thorny branches and produces white or pinkish flowers in the spring. Its fruits, known as haws, are red or orange berries that ripen in the fall.

Crataegus denaria is often found in woodland edges, hedgerows, and other disturbed soils.
