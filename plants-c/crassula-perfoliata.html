Crassula perfoliata

Crassula perfoliata, commonly known as the String of Hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish-green or reddish hue depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
