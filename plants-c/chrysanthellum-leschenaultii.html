Chrysanthellum leschenaultii

Chrysanthellum leschenaultii, commonly known as the Leschenault's chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Its growth habit is usually compact, making it suitable for container gardening.
