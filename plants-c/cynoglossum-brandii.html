Cynoglossum brandii

Cynoglossum brandii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Cynoglossum brandii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
