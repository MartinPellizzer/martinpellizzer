Croton trilobus var. disraelii

Croton trilobus var. disraelii, commonly known as the Disraeli croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that display shades of red, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
