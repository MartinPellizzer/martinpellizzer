Calathea louisae

Calathea louisae, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often feature intricate patterns in shades of green, white, and pink. The leaves are typically oval or heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
