Cynanchum altissimum

Cynanchum altissimum, commonly known as the tall milkweed or Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its erect stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and stream banks.

Cynanchum altissimum is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
