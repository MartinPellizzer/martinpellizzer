Carex oederi var. recterostrata

Carex oederi var. recterostrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sedges, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and North America.

The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and slightly curved, with a distinctive pale green coloration.
