Cynometra marginata

Cynometra marginata, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical tree native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The wood of Cynometra marginata is valued for its durability and is used in furniture and construction.
