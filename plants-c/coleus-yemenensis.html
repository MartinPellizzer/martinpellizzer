Coleus yemenensis

Coleus yemenensis, commonly known as Yemen coleus, is a species of flowering plant native to Yemen.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, featuring opposite leaves that are often marked with intricate patterns. Its flowers are small and clustered in whorls, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Coleus yemenensis is valued in horticulture for its striking leaf colors and ease of cultivation.
