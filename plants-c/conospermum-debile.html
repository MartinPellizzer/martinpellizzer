Conospermum debile

Conospermum debile, commonly known as the slender conospermum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to eastern Australia, particularly found in the coastal regions of New South Wales and Queensland. This shrub typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom in spring and summer.

It is well adapted to coastal environments and is often found in sandy soils.
