Cynanchum tarmense

Cynanchum tarmense, commonly known as Tarmian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions.

Its latex contains toxic compounds, making it unpalatable to herbivores.
