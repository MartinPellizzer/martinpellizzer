Comastoma pulmonarium f. albiflorum

Comastoma pulmonarium f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered lungwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of velvety, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, slender stems bearing clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are predominantly white or pale pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
