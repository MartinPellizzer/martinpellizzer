Calceolaria glabrata var. meyeniana

Calceolaria glabrata var. meyeniana, commonly known as the velvet flower or Mexican hat plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, pouch-like flowers that resemble a small hat. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually hairy and grayish-green, while the flowers come in shades of pink, purple, and white.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
