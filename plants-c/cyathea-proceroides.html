Cyathea proceroides

Cyathea proceroides, commonly known as the tall tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting the plant's structure and enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Cyathea proceroides plays an important ecological role in tropical forests, providing habitat and contributing to biodiversity.
