Chiropetalum lineatum

Chiropetalum lineatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag pattern along the stem, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Chiropetalum lineatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and delicate fragrance.
