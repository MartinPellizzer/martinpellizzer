Clytostoma ramantaceum

Clytostoma ramantaceum, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers with a contrasting color pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are often pale yellow with darker markings, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
