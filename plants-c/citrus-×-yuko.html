Citrus × yuko

Citrus × yuko, commonly known as the Yuzu.

This hybrid citrus plant is a cross between Citrus junocensis and Citrus ichangensis. It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green fruits that have a distinctive aromatic zest. The leaves are glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is often cultivated for its fragrant zest and is used in both culinary and aromatic applications.
