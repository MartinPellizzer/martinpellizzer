Cryptocarya liebertiana

Cryptocarya liebertiana, commonly known as the New Zealand mountain pepper, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible berries. Cryptocarya liebertiana thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in native forests and on rocky slopes.

Its unique botanical features make it an important species in New Zealand's ecological and horticultural landscapes.
