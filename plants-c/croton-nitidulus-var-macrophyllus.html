Croton nitidulus var. macrophyllus

Croton nitidulus var. macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf croton, is a tropical plant characterized by its striking, colorful foliage.

It features broad, glossy leaves that can range in color from deep green to shades of red and yellow, depending on the varietal form. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This varietal is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf patterns and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
