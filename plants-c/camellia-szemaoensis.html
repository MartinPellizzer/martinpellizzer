Camellia szemaoensis

Camellia szemaoensis, commonly known as the Sichuan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can be pink, red, or white. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Camellia szemaoensis is also valued for its potential in horticulture and as a source of tea.
