Caladium bicolor f. argyrospilum

Caladium bicolor f. argyrospilum, commonly known as the silver-spotted caladium, is a hybrid variety of the caladium plant.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display a combination of white and green patterns. The foliage often features a pale green or silver mottling on a darker green background. This cultivar thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full shade.

It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant and unique leaf patterns.
