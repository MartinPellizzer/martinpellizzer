Cotyledon pygmaea var. tenuis

Cotyledon pygmaea var. tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chandelier plant, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight red or purple hue on the undersides. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
