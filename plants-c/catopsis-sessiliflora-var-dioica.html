Catopsis sessiliflora var. dioica

Catopsis sessiliflora var. dioica, commonly known as the dioecious catopsis, is a species of liverwort found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Porellaceae and is characterized by its small, leafy structure with a distinct thallus. The plant exhibits a dioecious reproductive system, with separate male and female plants. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette and are covered in a layer of rhizoids.
