Coelogyne wenzelii

Coelogyne wenzelii, commonly known as the Wenzel's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the winter months. It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks without being parasitic.

Coelogyne wenzelii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
