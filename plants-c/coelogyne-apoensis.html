Coelogyne apoensis

Coelogyne apoensis, commonly known as the Apo orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a layer of velvety brown bark.

Coelogyne apoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
