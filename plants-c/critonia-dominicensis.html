Critonia dominicensis

Critonia dominicensis, commonly known as the Dominican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Hispaniola.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses in tropical regions.
