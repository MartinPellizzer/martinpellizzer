Cordia martinicensis

Cordia martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique cotton tree, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and parks in warm regions.
