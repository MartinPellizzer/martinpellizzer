Cattleya flava

Cattleya flava, commonly known as the Yellow Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive orange or red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season.

Cattleya flava is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking color and elegant appearance.
