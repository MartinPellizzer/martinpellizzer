Coprosma pusilla

Coprosma pusilla, commonly known as the small coprosma or dwarf coprosma, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It typically reaches a height of only a few centimeters, forming dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green color, with a slightly waxy texture. It produces tiny, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Coprosma pusilla is often found in coastal and inland forest margins, thriving in well-drained soils.
