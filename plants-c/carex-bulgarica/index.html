Carex Bulgarica

Carex bulgarica is a unique sedge species native to parts of Europe and Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. This plant is known for its dense, grass-like foliage and distinctive flowering spikes. Its ability to adapt to various soil conditions makes it a valuable addition to ecological restoration projects. Despite its modest appearance, Carex bulgarica plays an important role in supporting local biodiversity.
