Caladenia exstans

Caladenia exstans, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open woodland and heathland habitats. The plant features a single, slender stem that bears a single, elongated flower with a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Caladenia exstans is a rare and endangered species, requiring conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
