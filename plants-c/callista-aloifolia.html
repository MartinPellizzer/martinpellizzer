Callista aloifolia

Callista aloifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-white, velvety foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Callista aloifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
