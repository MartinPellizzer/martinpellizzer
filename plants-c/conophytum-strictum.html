Conophytum strictum

Conophytum strictum, commonly known as the strict conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Conophytum strictum is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
