Cotoneaster newryensis

Cotoneaster newryensis, commonly known as the Newry cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to western Europe.

It typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters and is characterized by its dense, rounded form. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during late spring, followed by bright red berries in autumn. Its leaves are oval-shaped with serrated edges and turn red in the fall.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
