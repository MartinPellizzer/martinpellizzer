Calathea dodsonii

Calathea dodsonii, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of deep green, silver, and purple. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often displaying a unique marbled appearance. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and a subject of interest in horticultural studies.
