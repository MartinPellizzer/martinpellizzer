Calceolaria flacca

Calceolaria flacca, commonly known as the slender slipper orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant features delicate, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Calceolaria flacca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique floral structure.
