Combretum thonneri var. laurentii

Combretum thonneri var. laurentii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in late summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or lance-shaped. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and vibrant floral display make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
