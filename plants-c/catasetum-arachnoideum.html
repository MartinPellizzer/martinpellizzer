Catasetum arachnoideum

Catasetum arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy or woolly appearance, which gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, often fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments.

Catasetum arachnoideum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
