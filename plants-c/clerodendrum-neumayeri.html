Clerodendrum neumayeri

Clerodendrum neumayeri, commonly known as the Neumayer's flame glory, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and serrated edges. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
