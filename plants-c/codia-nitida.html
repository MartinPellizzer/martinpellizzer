Codia nitida

Codia nitida, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-white, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by small, round fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
