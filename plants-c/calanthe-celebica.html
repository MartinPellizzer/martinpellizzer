Calanthe celebica

Calanthe celebica, commonly known as the Celebes Calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the Indonesian island of Celebes.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is typically found in moist, shaded forest environments. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Calanthe celebica is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
