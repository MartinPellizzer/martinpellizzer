Calanthe musa-amanii

Calanthe musa-amanii, commonly known as the Amanii calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with erect, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

The flowers are borne on tall, slender spikes and are pollinated primarily by insects.
