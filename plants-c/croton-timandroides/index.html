Croton Timandroides

Timandros, scientifically known as Croton timandroides, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. It is renowned for its vibrant, colorful bracts that resemble flowers, adding a burst of visual interest to any garden. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and light conditions to ensure optimal growth.
