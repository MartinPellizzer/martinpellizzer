Clausena wallichii var. wallichii

Clausena wallichii var. wallichii, commonly known as the white-flowered bael, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters.
