Cardiospermum vesicarum

Cardiospermum vesicarum, commonly known as the bladder gourd or bubble gourd, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the family Cucurbitaceae, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and squash. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive inflated, balloon-like fruits that develop from its flowers. The fruits are typically green when immature and turn yellow or orange as they mature.
