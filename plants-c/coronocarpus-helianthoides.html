Coronocarpus helianthoides

Coronocarpus helianthoides, commonly known as the sunflower tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, deciduous tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers that resemble those of the sunflower, and it has simple, alternate leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and along riverbanks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
