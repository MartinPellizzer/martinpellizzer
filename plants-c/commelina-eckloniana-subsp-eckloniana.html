Commelina eckloniana subsp. eckloniana

Commelina eckloniana subsp.

eckloniana, commonly known as the Ecklon's spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in wet, marshy habitats and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.

The subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
