Catalpa domingensis

Catalpa domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to the Caribbean islands, particularly the Dominican Republic.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. The tree produces long, cylindrical seed pods that split open to release its winged seeds.

Catalpa domingensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens for its distinctive appearance and seasonal blooms.
