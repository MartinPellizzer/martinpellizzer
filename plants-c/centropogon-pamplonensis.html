Centropogon pamplonensis

Centropogon pamplonensis, commonly known as the Pamplona lily, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Centropogon pamplonensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
