Cuphea scabrida

Cuphea scabrida, commonly known as rough cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a rough, textured surface. Its showy, tubular flowers come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.

Cuphea scabrida thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
