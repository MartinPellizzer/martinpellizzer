Cardamine akitensis

Cardamine akitensis, commonly known as Akitan watercress, is a small herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and medicinal plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. Its leaves are finely divided and emit a peppery aroma when crushed.

It is valued for its nutritional content and traditional use in local cuisines.
