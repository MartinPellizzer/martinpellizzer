Calesiam obcordatum

Calesiam obcordatum, commonly known as the heart-shaped fern, is a unique member of the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped fronds that are often deeply lobed and covered in fine, silky hairs. Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense mats.

The plant's ornamental value lies in its elegant, delicate foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
