Cineraria geniculata

Cineraria geniculata, commonly known as the mountain cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its blue or violet flowers and hairy, silver-green leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Cineraria geniculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
