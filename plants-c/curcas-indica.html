Curcas indica

Curcas indica, commonly known as the Chinese lantern or mock orange, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces bright red, papery fruits that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
