Canavalia mollis

Canavalia mollis, commonly known as velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods containing edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines. Canavalia mollis has a fibrous root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

It also has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
