Chenopodium macrospermum f. latifolium

Chenopodium macrospermum f. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf goosefoot, is a variant of the common goosefoot species.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its broad, deltoid-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters, and its seeds are small and often wind-dispersed. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields or along roadsides.

This plant is part of the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
