Coprosma oceanica

Coprosma oceanica, commonly known as the coastal coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, glossy green leaves and clusters of bright red or orange berries that persist through winter. The plant thrives in coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils. It plays a role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for native wildlife.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
