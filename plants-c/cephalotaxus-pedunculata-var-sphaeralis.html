Cephalotaxus pedunculata var. sphaeralis

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which consists of scale-like leaves arranged in dense, flat sprays. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
