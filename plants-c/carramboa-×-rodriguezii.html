Carramboa × rodriguezii

Carramboa × rodriguezii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring cross between Carramboa and Rodriguezia species.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful floral display. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers with intricate petal structures. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
