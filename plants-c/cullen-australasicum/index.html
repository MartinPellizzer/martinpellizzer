Cullen Australasicum

Cullen australasicum is a rare and striking member of the Leguminosae family. Native to the temperate regions of southeastern Australia, it thrives in coastal and sandy soils. Known for its vibrant pink and white flower clusters, it attracts pollinators and adds visual interest to gardens. This plant is also valued for its ecological benefits and unique botanical features. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect its dwindling populations in the wild.
