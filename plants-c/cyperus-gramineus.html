Cyperus gramineus

Cyperus gramineus, commonly known as umbrella sedge, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands.

Cyperus gramineus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive, umbrella-like appearance.
