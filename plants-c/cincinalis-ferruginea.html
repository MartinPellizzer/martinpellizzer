Cincinalis ferruginea

Cincinalis ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty cincinalis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, reddish-brown stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Cincinalis ferruginea is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can be found in botanical gardens and private collections.
