Canavalia saueri

Canavalia saueri, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Canavalia saueri is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
