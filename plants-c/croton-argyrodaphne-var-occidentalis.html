Croton argyrodaphne var. occidentalis

Croton argyrodaphne var. occidentalis, commonly known as the silver-leaf croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green tones. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This plant is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance.
