Campanula olympica

Campanula olympica, commonly known as the Olympic bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It has lance-shaped leaves and is often found in alpine environments.

Campanula olympica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
