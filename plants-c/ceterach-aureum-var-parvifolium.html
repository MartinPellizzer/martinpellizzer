Ceterach aureum var. parvifolium

Ceterach aureum var. parvifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved golden moss, is a coastal plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming species that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils along the shoreline.
