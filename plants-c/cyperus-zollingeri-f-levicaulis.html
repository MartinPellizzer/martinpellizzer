Cyperus zollingeri f. levicaulis

Cyperus zollingeri f. levicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, three-lobed flowers. It typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and wetlands.

This ornamental plant is valued for its ability to thrive in moist environments and its delicate appearance.
