Cineraria cordifolia var. samnitum

Cineraria cordifolia var. samnitum, commonly known as the Southern Italian cineraria, is a flowering plant native to southern Italy.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
