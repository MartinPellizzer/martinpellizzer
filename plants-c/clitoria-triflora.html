Clitoria triflora

Clitoria triflora, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It features striking, butterfly-like purple flowers that bloom in clusters of three. The plant has trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated pods and are used as a food source in some cultures.

Clitoria triflora is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
