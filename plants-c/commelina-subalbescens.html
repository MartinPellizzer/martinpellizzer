Commelina subalbescens

Commelina subalbescens, commonly known as the white-flowered spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Commelina genus within the family Commelinaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, bright blue or violet flowers with white markings.
