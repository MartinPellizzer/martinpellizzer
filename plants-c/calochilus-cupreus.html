Calochilus cupreus

Calochilus cupreus, commonly known as the copper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking copper-colored petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is found in regions with a temperate climate. Its flowers bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Calochilus cupreus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
