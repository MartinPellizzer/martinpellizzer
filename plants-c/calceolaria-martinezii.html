Calceolaria martinezii

Calceolaria martinezii, commonly known as the Martinez's slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine habitats.
