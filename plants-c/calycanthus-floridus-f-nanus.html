Calycanthus floridus f. nanus

Calycanthus floridus f. nanus, commonly known as the dwarf sweetshrub, is a compact shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features fragrant, reddish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its showy blooms. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique aroma and compact growth habit.
