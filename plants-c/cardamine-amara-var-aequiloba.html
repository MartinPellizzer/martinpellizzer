Cardamine amara var. aequiloba

Cardamine amara var. aequiloba, commonly known as the smooth watercress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and wet meadows.

Its seeds are small and oval, often dispersed by water or wind.
