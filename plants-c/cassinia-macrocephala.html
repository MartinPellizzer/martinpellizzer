Cassinia macrocephala

Cassinia macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed cassinia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow flower heads that are typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs.

Cassinia macrocephala is often found in open habitats such as woodlands and grasslands.
