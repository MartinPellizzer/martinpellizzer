Cineraria platanifolia

Cineraria platanifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden cultivation.
