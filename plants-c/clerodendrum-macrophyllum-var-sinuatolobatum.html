Clerodendrum macrophyllum var. sinuatolobatum

Clerodendrum macrophyllum var. sinuatolobatum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often lobed and have a slightly wavy edge. The plant produces clusters of bright red, lantern-shaped flowers that are highly ornamental. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.

The leaves and flowers are sometimes used in traditional medicine, though its primary value lies in its aesthetic appeal.
