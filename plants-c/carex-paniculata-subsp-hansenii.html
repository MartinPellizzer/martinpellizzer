Carex paniculata subsp. hansenii

Carex paniculata subsp.

hansenii, commonly known as the Hansen's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of North America. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants commonly found in wet or moist environments.

This subspecies is characterized by its upright, cylindrical flowering spikes and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in wet meadows, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.
