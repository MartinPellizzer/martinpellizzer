Centaurea × mirabilis

Centaurea × mirabilis, commonly known as the marvelful centaury, is a hybrid species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky flower heads that range in color from red to purple, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It is native to southern Europe and has been introduced to other regions, where it sometimes becomes invasive.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
