Cineraria abrotanifolius

Cineraria abrotanifolius, commonly known as the blueweed or blue cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its blue or violet flowers and hairy, silver-green leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Cineraria abrotanifolius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and resilience.
