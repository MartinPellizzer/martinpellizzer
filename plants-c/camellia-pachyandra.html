Camellia pachyandra

Camellia pachyandra, commonly known as the thick-shelled camellia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces thick, leathery fruits that contain multiple seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade.

Camellia pachyandra is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
