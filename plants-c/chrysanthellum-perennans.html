Chrysanthellum perennans

Chrysanthellum perennans, commonly known as the everlasting daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and its ability to produce a profusion of small, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow centers with white petals. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, adding a delicate, long-lasting ornamental value to the garden.
