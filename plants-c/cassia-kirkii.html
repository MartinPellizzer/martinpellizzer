Cassia kirkii

Cassia kirkii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland and savanna habitats.

Cassia kirkii is valued for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in medicine and as a source of timber.
