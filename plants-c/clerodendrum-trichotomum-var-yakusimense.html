Clerodendrum trichotomum var. yakusimense

Clerodendrum trichotomum var. yakusimense, commonly known as the Yakusimense Clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
