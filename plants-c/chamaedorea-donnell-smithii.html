Chamaedorea donnell-smithii

Chamaedorea donnell-smithii, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a tropical plant native to southern Mexico.

It is a compact, upright palm with slender, dark green, feather-like leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and air-purifying qualities.

Its slow growth rate makes it ideal for small spaces and indoor environments.
