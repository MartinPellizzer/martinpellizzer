Coelogyne pilosissima

Coelogyne pilosissima, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and clusters of hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and red, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne pilosissima is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and unique appearance.
