Cotyledon coccinea

Cotyledon coccinea, commonly known as the Ruby Necklace, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically red or pink in color, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Cotyledon coccinea is popular in rock gardens and as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
