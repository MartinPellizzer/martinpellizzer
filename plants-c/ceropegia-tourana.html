Ceropegia tourana

Ceropegia tourana, commonly known as the starfish fig or rainbow plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, succulent stems. The plant produces unique, tubular flowers that are often brightly colored, with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia tourana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
