Cinnamomum lindleyi

Cinnamomum lindleyi, commonly known as the Lindley's cinnamon tree, is a species of flowering plant in the laurel family.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica, and grows in tropical rainforests. The tree is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and small, yellowish flowers. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their aromatic properties.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
