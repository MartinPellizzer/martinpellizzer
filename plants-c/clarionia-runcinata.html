Clarionia runcinata

Clarionia runcinata, commonly known as the yellow bell.

It is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Clarionia runcinata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
