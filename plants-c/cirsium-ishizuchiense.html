Cirsium ishizuchiense

Cirsium ishizuchiense, commonly known as Ishizuchi thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Japan, specifically found in the prefecture of Ishikawa. This perennial plant is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
