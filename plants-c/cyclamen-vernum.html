Cyclamen vernum

Cyclamen vernum, commonly known as the spring cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant typically blooms in early spring, making it a symbol of renewal and the arrival of warmer weather. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Cyclamen vernum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
