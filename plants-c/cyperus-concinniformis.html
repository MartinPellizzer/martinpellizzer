Cyperus concinniformis

Cyperus concinniformis, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and shallow waters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often submerged or floating. Its rhizomes help it spread and establish in moist soils, making it a common species in aquatic habitats.

Cyperus concinniformis plays a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
