Cotula eckloniana

Cotula eckloniana, commonly known as the silver cotula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like white flowers and hairy, silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats. Cotula eckloniana is an annual or biennial herb that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
