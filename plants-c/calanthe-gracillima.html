Calanthe gracillima

Calanthe gracillima, commonly known as the slender calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense colonies.

Calanthe gracillima is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
