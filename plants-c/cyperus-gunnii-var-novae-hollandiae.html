Cyperus gunnii var. novae-hollandiae

Cyperus gunnii var. novae-hollandiae, commonly known as the New Holland flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower spikes that are typically brown or reddish in color. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, often found in coastal areas and saline soils.

This variant is notable for its adaptability to brackish conditions, making it a common species in tidal flats and estuaries.
