Chrysanthemum shihchuanum

Chrysanthemum shihchuanum, commonly known as the Shihchuan chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and a variety of flower colors, including yellow, white, and red. The plant typically blooms in the autumn season and is valued for its ornamental beauty. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for both decorative purposes and traditional medicinal uses.
