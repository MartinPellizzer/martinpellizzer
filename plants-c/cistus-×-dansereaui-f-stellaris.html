Cistus × dansereaui f. stellaris

Cistus × dansereaui f. stellaris, commonly known as the star-form cistus, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage of gray-green leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of pink to purple flowers during the late spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms.
