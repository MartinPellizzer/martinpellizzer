Conostegia inusitata

Conostegia inusitata, commonly known as the peculiar conostegia, is a rare shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a distinctive, woody stem with a rough, bark-like texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
