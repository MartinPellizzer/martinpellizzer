Chenopodium parodii

Chenopodium parodii, commonly known as the Parodii goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species adapted to harsh conditions.

Chenopodium parodii is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
