Cnestis palala

Cnestis palala, commonly known as the African spider flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow disk flowers surrounded by white to pink ray florets. It typically grows as an annual herb with hairy, branched stems and opposite leaves.

Cnestis palala is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
