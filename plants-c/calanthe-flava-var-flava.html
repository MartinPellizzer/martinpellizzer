Calanthe flava var. flava

Calanthe flava var. flava, commonly known as the yellow calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive, showy flower structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, preferring well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
