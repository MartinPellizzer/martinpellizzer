Cypripedium × oenanthum var. superbum

Cypripedium × oenanthum var. superbum, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid species native to the northeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy purple or pink flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and has a pseudobulb-like structure that stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single flower per stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
