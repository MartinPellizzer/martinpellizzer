Coelogyne tenuis

Coelogyne tenuis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its thin, wiry stems and clustered, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne tenuis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
