Centaurea conocephala

Centaurea conocephala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, bristly seeds in its composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Centaurea conocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
