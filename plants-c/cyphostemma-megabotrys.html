Cyphostemma megabotrys

Cyphostemma megabotrys, commonly known as the giant African starfish, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stems and distinctive star-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellowish berry containing several seeds.

Cyphostemma megabotrys is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
