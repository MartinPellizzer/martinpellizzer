Cinnamomum asa-grayi

Cinnamomum asa-grayi, commonly known as the Sierra Madre cinnamon tree, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in humid, mountainous regions. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a strong aromatic scent. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces berries that contain aromatic seeds.

This species is valued for its essential oils and has been used in traditional medicine and perfumery.
