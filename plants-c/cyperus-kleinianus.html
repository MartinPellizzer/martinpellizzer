Cyperus kleinianus

Cyperus kleinianus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along water edges, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its slender, grass-like leaves are often clustered at the base, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Cyperus kleinianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic habitats.
