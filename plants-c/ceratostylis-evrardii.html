Ceratostylis evrardii

Ceratostylis evrardii, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Ceratostylis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a slender stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf and produces a single, elongated flower with a distinctive, spurred lip. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a faintly colored lip that attracts pollinators.

Ceratostylis evrardii is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in forest undergrowth.
