Crassula umbraticola

Crassula umbraticola, commonly known as the shade crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, dense rosette of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green or bluish-green. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
