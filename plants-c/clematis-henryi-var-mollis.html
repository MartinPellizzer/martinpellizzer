Clematis henryi var. mollis

Clematis henryi var. mollis, commonly known as the soft Clematis, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, often with a distinctive purple hue at the base. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its elegant flowering display.
