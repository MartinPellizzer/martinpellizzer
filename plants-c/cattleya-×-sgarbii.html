Cattleya × sgarbii

Cattleya × sgarbii, commonly known as the Sgarbii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from Cattleya and Laelia species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and violet, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in varying environmental conditions. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
