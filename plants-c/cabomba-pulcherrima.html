Cabomba pulcherrima

Cabomba pulcherrima, commonly known as the sacred lotus of the Americas, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features delicate, floating leaves that are often used in floral arrangements due to their beauty. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots are adapted to thrive in shallow, still water, and it is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens.

Cabomba pulcherrima is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
