Crassula leachii

Crassula leachii, commonly known as the Cape Town carpet plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, fleshy leaves that form a low-growing, mat-like structure. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid conditions, storing water in its thick leaves.

Crassula leachii is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its compact size and ease of care.
