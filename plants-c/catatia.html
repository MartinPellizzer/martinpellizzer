Catatia

Catatia, commonly known as the catatia flower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like appearance, featuring a central yellow disk surrounded by white to pale pink petals. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.

Its botanical name, Catatia, reflects its unique and striking visual appeal.
