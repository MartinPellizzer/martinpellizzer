Cyathea tsaratananensis

Cyathea tsaratananensis, commonly known as the Tsaratanana tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in montane rainforests.

Cyathea tsaratananensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to forest structure and biodiversity.
