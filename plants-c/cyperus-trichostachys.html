Cyperus trichostachys

Cyperus trichostachys, commonly known as the papyrus sedge, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces cylindrical inflorescences with small, clustered flowers. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in suitable habitats.
