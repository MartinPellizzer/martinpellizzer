Clusia hexacarpa var. ptaritepuiana

Clusia hexacarpa var. ptaritepuiana, commonly known as the Ptaritepuian Clusia, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in humid, tropical environments.
