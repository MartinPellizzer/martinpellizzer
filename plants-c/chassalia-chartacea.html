Chassalia chartacea

Chassalia chartacea, commonly known as the paperleaf chassalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is distinguished by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are thin and papery in texture, giving it its common name. It produces yellow flowers with prominent central disc flowers surrounded by ray florets.

Chassalia chartacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking foliage and delicate blooms.
