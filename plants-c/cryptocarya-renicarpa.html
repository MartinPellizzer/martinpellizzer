Cryptocarya renicarpa

Cryptocarya renicarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cryptocarya, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy on top and lighter in color beneath. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically white or pale yellow, and it produces spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.

Cryptocarya renicarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens.
