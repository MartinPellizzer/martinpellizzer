Crassula brevistyla

Crassula brevistyla, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
