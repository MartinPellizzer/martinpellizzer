Campanula cochleariifolia f. delpontei

Campanula cochleariifolia f. delpontei, commonly known as the Delponte bellflower, is a compact perennial herb native to the Alps.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
