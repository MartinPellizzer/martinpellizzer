Coelogyne devogelii

Coelogyne devogelii, commonly known as the Devogel's orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne devogelii is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
