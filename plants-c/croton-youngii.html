Croton youngii

Croton youngii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsules that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
