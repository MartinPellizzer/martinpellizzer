Calophyllum confertum

Calophyllum confertum, commonly known as the dense flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking red or orange flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain toxic seeds, making it important to handle with care. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree's rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
