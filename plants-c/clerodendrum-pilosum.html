Clerodendrum pilosum

Clerodendrum pilosum, commonly known as the hairy clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.

Clerodendrum pilosum thrives in warm, humid climates and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
