Calicotome grosii

Calicotome grosii, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has a woody stem with dense, evergreen foliage that provides structural support. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

Calicotome grosii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
