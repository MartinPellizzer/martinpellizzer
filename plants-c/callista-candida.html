Callista candida

Callista candida, commonly known as the white callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and long blooming period.
