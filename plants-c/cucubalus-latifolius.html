Cucubalus latifolius

Cucubalus latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf cucumber, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and trailing growth habit. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant. Its fruit is a elongated, cylindrical gourd that can vary in color from green to orange.

This species is often cultivated for its edible fruits and is adapted to grow in warm, sunny climates.
