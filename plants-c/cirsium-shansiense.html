Cirsium shansiense

Cirsium shansiense, commonly known as the Chinese thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are covered in stiff, bristly hairs, which provide protection against herbivores.

Cirsium shansiense is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
