Cleisostoma capricorne

Cleisostoma capricorne, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive horn-like spur. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Cleisostoma capricorne is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
