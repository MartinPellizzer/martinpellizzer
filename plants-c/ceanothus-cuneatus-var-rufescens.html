Ceanothus cuneatus var. rufescens

Ceanothus cuneatus var. rufescens, commonly known as the redstem ceanothus, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in spring. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a silvery or grayish underside. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to sunny, arid environments.

It plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
