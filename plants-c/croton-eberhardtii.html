Croton eberhardtii

Croton eberhardtii, commonly known as the Eberhardt croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a visually striking plant. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and have a rough, textured surface. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
