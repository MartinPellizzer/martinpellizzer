Chlorophytum intermedium var. pubescens

Chlorophytum intermedium var. pubescens, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail Plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching stems with tubular leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers, though it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Its unique leaf structure makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
