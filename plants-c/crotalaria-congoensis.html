Crotalaria congoensis

Crotalaria congoensis, commonly known as Congo flame bean, is a tropical flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to Central and Southern Africa, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers and compound leaves with serrated edges. It produces elongated pods that contain several elliptical seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Crotalaria congoensis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural purposes.
