Cheilanthes chinensis

Cheilanthes chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese rock fern, is a perennial fern native to China and other parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its small, leathery fronds that are often found growing on rocky substrates. Cheilanthes chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
