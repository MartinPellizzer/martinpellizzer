Celsia pinnatisecta

Celsia pinnatisecta, commonly known as the ferny celsia, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided, feather-like leaves that give it a lacy appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are attractive to birds. Celsia pinnatisecta thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded outdoor gardens.
