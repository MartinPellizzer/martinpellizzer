Cattleya schofieldiana var. banneri

Cattleya schofieldiana var. banneri, commonly known as the Banner's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and sometimes feature a subtle purple or red hue. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, each having a distinct, fragrant scent. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

This variety is highly prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
