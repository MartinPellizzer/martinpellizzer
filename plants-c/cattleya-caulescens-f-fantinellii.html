Cattleya caulescens f. fantinellii

Cattleya caulescens f. fantinellii, commonly known as the Fantinelli's cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically has a single, robust stem that bears several blooms, each with a velvety texture and intricate patterns. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This cultivar is prized for its vibrant hues and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in tropical gardens.
