Coccothrinax alexandri subsp. nitida

Coccothrinax alexandri subsp.

nitida, commonly known as the slender palm, is a compact, clumping palm native to the Caribbean. It features narrow, feather-like leaves that are typically bright green and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into round, dark blue fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
