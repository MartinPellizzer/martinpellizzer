Cnicus praemorsus

Cnicus praemorsus, commonly known as the death's-head hawkmoth plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers with dark markings. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny environments. It produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Cnicus praemorsus is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to tolerate salt spray and harsh conditions.
