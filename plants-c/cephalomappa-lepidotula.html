Cephalomappa lepidotula

Cephalomappa lepidotula, commonly known as the velvetleaf mallow, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
