Corysanthes mirabilis

Corysanthes mirabilis, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Corysanthes mirabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
