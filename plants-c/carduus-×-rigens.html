Carduus × rigens

Carduus × rigens, commonly known as the common thistle.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Carduus, characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its leaves are typically lobed and covered in fine, prickly hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a spherical cluster.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
