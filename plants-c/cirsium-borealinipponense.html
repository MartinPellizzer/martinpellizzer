Cirsium borealinipponense

Cirsium borealinipponense, commonly known as the Japanese thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has a robust root system and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Cirsium borealinipponense is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance.
