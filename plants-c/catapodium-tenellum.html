Catapodium tenellum

Catapodium tenellum, commonly known as the slender catapodium, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are typically less than 30 cm in height. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.
