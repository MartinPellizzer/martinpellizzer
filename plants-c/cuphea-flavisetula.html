Cuphea flavisetula

Cuphea flavisetula, commonly known as the yellow flame cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Cuphea flavisetula is valued in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
