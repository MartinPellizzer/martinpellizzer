Cassia multinervia

Cassia multinervia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping panicles of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and prominent veins, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia multinervia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
