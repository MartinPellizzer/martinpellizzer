Condalia obovata var. angustifolia

Condalia obovata var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf condalia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, narrow, obovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
