Cleisostoma crassissimum

Cleisostoma crassissimum, commonly known as the broad-leafed cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves and fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their beauty and scent.

Cleisostoma crassissimum is a member of the Orchidaceae family, which includes many other tropical and subtropical species.
