Chrysanthemum hosmariense

Chrysanthemum hosmariense, commonly known as the Hosmariense chrysanthemum, is a species native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically range in color from white to yellow. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
