Curio rowleyanus f. marmoratus

Curio rowleyanus f. marmoratus, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a unique hybrid of the African Zebra Plant and the Curio rowleyanus.

It is characterized by its striking, zigzagging stems that are covered in small, rounded leaves. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light. It is a low-maintenance plant that is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental appearance.

Its distinctive foliage makes it a popular choice for decorative purposes in both homes and offices.
