Coleus suborbicularis

Coleus suborbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular coleus, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, purple, and green. The leaves are typically rounded or heart-shaped, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is popular in ornamental gardening for its striking foliage and ease of care.
