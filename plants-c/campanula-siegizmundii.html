Campanula siegizmundii

Campanula siegizmundii, commonly known as the Siegizmund bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its stems are upright and branched.

Campanula siegizmundii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
