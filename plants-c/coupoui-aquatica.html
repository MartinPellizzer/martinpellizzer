Coupoui aquatica

Coupoui aquatica, commonly known as water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that anchors it in shallow water or muddy substrates. Its leaves are typically rounded with a waxy surface that repels water, aiding in buoyancy.

Coupoui aquatica plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
