Cryptocarya oligoneura

Cryptocarya oligoneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cryptocarya, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by dark purple berries, which are an important food source for native wildlife.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its natural habitat.
