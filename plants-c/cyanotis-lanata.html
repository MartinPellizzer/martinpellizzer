Cyanotis lanata

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
