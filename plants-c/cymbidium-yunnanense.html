Cymbidium yunnanense

Cymbidium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pendulous flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
