Crinum gracile

Crinum gracile, commonly known as the slender rain lily, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking white or pale pink flowers with a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Crinum gracile is also valued for its ability to naturalize and spread through its rhizomatous growth.
