Carex Jackiana F. Ochrolepis

Carex jackiana f. ochrolepis is a striking variant of the common sedge plant known for its distinctive pale yellowish-green foliage. This ornamental grass thrives in moist to wet environments, making it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes. Its delicate, grass-like appearance adds a soft texture to garden designs while maintaining a low maintenance profile. The plant's unique coloration sets it apart from other sedges, offering a subtle yet eye-catching presence. Whether used in naturalistic settings or as a decorative element, Carex jackiana f. ochrolepis brings both beauty and ecological value to the landscape.
