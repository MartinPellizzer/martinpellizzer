Capraria Arabica

Capraria arabica is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils where water is scarce. This species is known for its delicate, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in the spring. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and fragile species.
