Clerodendrum polyanthum

Clerodendrum polyanthum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often mistaken for flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered within the colorful bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum polyanthum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
