Chlorophytum javanicum

Chlorophytum javanicum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation. The plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters at the base of the plant.

Chlorophytum javanicum is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties and low maintenance requirements.
