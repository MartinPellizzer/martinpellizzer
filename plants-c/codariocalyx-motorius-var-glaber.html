Codariocalyx motorius var. glaber

Codariocalyx motorius var. glaber, commonly known as the sensitive plant or Mimosa pudica, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its rapid leaf movements in response to touch.

It is native to South America and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has pinnately compound leaves that fold inward and droop when disturbed, a defense mechanism against herbivores. Its flowers are small, pink to purple, and clustered in spherical inflorescences.

The species is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its unique behavior and adaptability to various soil types.
