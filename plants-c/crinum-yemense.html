Crinum yemense

Crinum yemense, commonly known as the Yemen lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It features tall, erect stems that bear clusters of showy, fragrant white or pale pink flowers. The plant has large, strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Crinum yemense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
