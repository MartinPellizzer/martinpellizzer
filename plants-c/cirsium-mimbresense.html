Cirsium mimbresense

Cirsium mimbresense, commonly known as the Mimbres thistle, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, such as desert plains and grasslands. It has a short, upright stem and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Cirsium mimbresense is a hardy species adapted to arid environments and is often found in the Mimbres Valley region.
