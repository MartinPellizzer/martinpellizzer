Caladium bicolor f. macrophyllum

Caladium bicolor f. macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant leaf caladium, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, displaying vibrant colors such as red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, fertile soil. It is typically grown as an annual in temperate regions, often used in gardens for its dramatic foliage.

Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for tropical and shaded garden settings.
