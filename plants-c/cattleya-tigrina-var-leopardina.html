Cattleya tigrina var. leopardina

Cattleya tigrina var. leopardina, commonly known as the Leopard Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, leopard-like spotting on its velvety, dark green leaves.

It produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of orange and yellow, often with intricate patterns resembling a tiger's stripes. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

The plant's unique foliage and floral display make it a standout in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
