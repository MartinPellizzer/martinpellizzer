Cytisus ardoinoi subsp. sauzeanus

Cytisus ardoinoi subsp.

sauzeanus, commonly known as the Madagascar broom, is a shrub native to Madagascar. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its adaptability to arid conditions makes it a resilient species in its native habitat.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology compared to other cytisus species.
