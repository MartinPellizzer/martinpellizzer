Chamaecrista lineata var. keyensis

Chamaecrista lineata var. keyensis, commonly known as the Key West coral bean, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of bright yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal or dry woodland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
