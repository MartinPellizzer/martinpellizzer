Cardamine multijuga var. gracilis

Cardamine multijuga var. gracilis, commonly known as slender watercress, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small white or yellowish flowers. This variety is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland floors. The plant has a short stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine and is valued for its peppery flavor in culinary applications.
