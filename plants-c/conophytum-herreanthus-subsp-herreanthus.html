Conophytum herreanthus subsp. herreanthus

Conophytum herreanthus subsp.

herreanthus, commonly known as the Herre's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
