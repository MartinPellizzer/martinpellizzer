Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. subuniflorus

Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. subuniflorus, commonly known as the slender cotoneaster, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white to pale pink flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is less commonly found in cultivation compared to other cotoneaster species.
