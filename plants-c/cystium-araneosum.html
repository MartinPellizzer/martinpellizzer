Cystium araneosum

Cystium araneosum, commonly known as the spider-thorn or red thorn, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often in coastal areas. The plant produces small, pink or red flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are thorny.

Cystium araneosum is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
