Chrysanthemum grande

Chrysanthemum grande, commonly known as the garden chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to East Asia and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features feathery, aromatic leaves. Its flowers bloom in the fall and are often used in ornamental gardening.

Chrysanthemum grande is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
