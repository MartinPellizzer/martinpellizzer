Cosmos linearifolius var. maysillesii

Cosmos linearifolius var. maysillesii, commonly known as the Maysillesii cosmos, is a striking annual flowering plant native to Mexico.

It features slender, branching stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, adorned with vibrant pink to purple flowers. The leaves are long, narrow, and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is noted for its showy blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
