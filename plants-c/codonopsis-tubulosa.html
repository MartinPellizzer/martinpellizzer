Codonopsis tubulosa

Codonopsis tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Codonopsis tubulosa is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
