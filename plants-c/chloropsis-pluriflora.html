Chloropsis pluriflora

Chloropsis pluriflora, commonly known as the many-flowered greenbark, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, light green bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
