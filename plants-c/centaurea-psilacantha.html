Centaurea psilacantha

Centaurea psilacantha, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in scrublands and coastal areas. Its slender stems and small, blue to purple flowers are adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

Centaurea psilacantha is also noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
