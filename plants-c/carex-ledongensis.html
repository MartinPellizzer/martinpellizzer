Carex ledongensis

Carex ledongensis, commonly known as the Ledong sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Guangxi provinces. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its morphology includes grass-like leaves and spike-like inflorescences, characteristic of many sedges.

Carex ledongensis plays a role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
