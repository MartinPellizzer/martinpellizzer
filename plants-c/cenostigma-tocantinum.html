Cenostigma tocantinum

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.
