Centrosema scabriusculum

Centrosema scabriusculum, commonly known as rough leaved centrosema, is a leguminous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with rough, hairy surfaces and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a climbing habit and produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as woodland edges and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important plant for ecological studies and potential agricultural use.
