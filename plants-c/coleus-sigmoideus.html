Coleus sigmoideus

Coleus sigmoideus, commonly known as the zigzag coleus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green, often with striking variegation. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.

Coleus sigmoideus prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
