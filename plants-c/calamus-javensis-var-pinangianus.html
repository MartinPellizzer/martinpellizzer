Calamus javensis var. pinangianus

Calamus javensis var. pinangianus, commonly known as the Pinang cane or Pinang reed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems that are often used for making rattan and other handicrafts. Its leaves are long and narrow, contributing to its ornamental value.
