Cymbalaria microcalyx subsp. dodekanesii

Cymbalaria microcalyx subsp.

dodekanesii, commonly known as the Aegean figleaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

The plant produces small, pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique foliage and delicate flowers make it an attractive addition to botanical gardens and natural habitats.
