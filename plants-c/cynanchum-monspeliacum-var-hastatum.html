Cynanchum monspeliacum var. hastatum

Cynanchum monspeliacum var. hastatum, commonly known as the hairy milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and also bear a similar hairy texture. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in loose inflorescences.

This plant is known for its milky sap, which can be toxic and is a defense mechanism against herbivores.
