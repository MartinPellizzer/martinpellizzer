Christisonia aurantiaca

Christisonia aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange-flowered tree, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has a rounded, evergreen canopy with dark green, leathery leaves. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Christisonia aurantiaca is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
