Cinnamosma madagascariensis var. madagascariensis

Cinnamosma madagascariensis var. madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar cinnamon tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-scented leaves and bark, which emit a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences, followed by small, round, reddish-brown fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a leathery texture with a prominent midrib.

This species is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
