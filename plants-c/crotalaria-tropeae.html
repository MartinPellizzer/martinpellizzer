Crotalaria tropeae

Crotalaria tropeae, commonly known as the tropical crown flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its palmate leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces bean-like pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Crotalaria tropeae is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its tolerance to drought conditions.
