Cardiospermum acuminatum

Cardiospermum acuminatum, commonly known as the sweetleaf or heartleaf.

It is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a distinctive, inflated pod that splits open to reveal seeds enclosed in a fleshy, red aril.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
