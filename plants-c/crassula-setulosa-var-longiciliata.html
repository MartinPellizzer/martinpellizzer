Crassula setulosa var. longiciliata

Crassula setulosa var. longiciliata, commonly known as the Woolly Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The leaves are typically green with a bluish tinge and are arranged in a compact rosette pattern. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or early summer.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
