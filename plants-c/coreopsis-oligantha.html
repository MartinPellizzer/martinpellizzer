Coreopsis oligantha

Coreopsis oligantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky soils. Its flowers are composed of ray florets and tubular disc florets, typical of the Asteraceae family.
