Cactus densispinus

Cactus densispinus, commonly known as the dense spine cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in dense clusters of spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

Its spines provide protection from herbivores and help reduce water loss through transpiration.
