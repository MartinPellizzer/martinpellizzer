Cotoneaster bullatus var. floribundus

Cotoneaster bullatus var. floribundus, commonly known as the flowering cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of white to pinkish flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that attract wildlife. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its compact growth and seasonal interest.
