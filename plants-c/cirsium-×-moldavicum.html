Cirsium × moldavicum

Cirsium × moldavicum, commonly known as the Moldavian thistle, is a hybrid plant species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to the Carpathian Mountains, this plant thrives in rocky and disturbed soils. It is often found in open woodlands and along forest edges.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
