Coffea arabica var. sundana

Coffea arabica var. sundana, commonly known as the Sundanese coffee.

It is a variant of the Arabica coffee species native to the Indonesian island of Java. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and glossy dark green leaves. It produces small, red berries that contain two seeds used for coffee production.

The variety is valued for its unique flavor profile and is often cultivated in small-scale agricultural settings.
