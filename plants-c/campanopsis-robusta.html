Campanopsis robusta

Campanopsis robusta, commonly known as the robust bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy green surface.

Campanopsis robusta thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
