Cyperus soyauxii subsp. pallescens

Cyperus soyauxii subsp.

pallescens, commonly known as the pale rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Africa. It belongs to the sedge family, Cyperaceae, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically brown or pale in color. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its adaptability, it can be both a useful plant in wetland restoration and a potential weed in agricultural settings.
