Centaurea globurensis

Centaurea globurensis, commonly known as the globular centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, bract-like structures around its flower heads.

Centaurea globurensis is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.
