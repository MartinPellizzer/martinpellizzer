Calotis microphylla

Calotis microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaf burr bush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is typically found in sandy or rocky soils in arid and semi-arid regions. It has a bushy growth habit and is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.

Calotis microphylla is also valued for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
