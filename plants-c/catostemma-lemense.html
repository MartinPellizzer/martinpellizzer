Catostemma lemense

Catostemma lemense, commonly known as the African moonvine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, woody fruits. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
