Chlorophytum marlothii

Chlorophytum marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically light green with white stripes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It is a hardy species that thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Its ability to propagate through runners makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
