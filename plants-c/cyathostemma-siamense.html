Cyathostemma siamense

Cyathostemma siamense, commonly known as the Siam star vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. Cyathostemma siamense is valued in horticulture for its attractive foliage and floral display.
