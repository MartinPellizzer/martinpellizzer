Cameraria lilioasphodelus

Cameraria lilioasphodelus, commonly known as the yellow lilioasphodel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, elongated fruits.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
