Cotoneaster mathonetii

Cotoneaster mathonetii, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Cotoneaster mathonetii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
