Coleus excelsus

Coleus excelsus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for both outdoor and indoor cultivation.
