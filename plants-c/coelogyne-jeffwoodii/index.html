Coelogyne Jeffwoodii

Coelogyne jeffwoodii is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers, it is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil. Its unique appearance and ease of cultivation make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers. Coelogyne jeffwoodii is a testament to the beauty and diversity of orchids in nature.
