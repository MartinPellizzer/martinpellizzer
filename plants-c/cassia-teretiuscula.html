Cassia teretiuscula

Cassia teretiuscula, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cassia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, woody pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories.

Cassia teretiuscula is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
