Cryptandra speciosa subsp. strigosa

Cryptandra speciosa subsp.

strigosa, commonly known as the woolly cryptandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage covered in fine, woolly hairs.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
