Coleus gamblei

Coleus gamblei, commonly known as the flame wheel coleus, is a vibrant ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with patterns of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a textured appearance. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers partial to full shade, making it ideal for indoor or shaded garden settings.

Its showy leaves make it a popular choice for container gardening and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
