Crassula vaginata

Crassula vaginata, commonly known as the stone plant or silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
