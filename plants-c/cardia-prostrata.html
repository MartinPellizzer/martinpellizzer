Cardia prostrata

Cardia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate cardia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant has thin, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cardia prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
