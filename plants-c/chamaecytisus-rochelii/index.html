Chamaecytisus Rochelii

Chamaecytisus rochelii is a hardy evergreen shrub known for its vibrant orange-red flowers and dense foliage. Native to the rugged coastlines of western France, it thrives in coastal and rocky environments. This plant is prized for its ability to withstand harsh conditions such as salt spray and strong winds. Its compact growth habit makes it an excellent choice for landscaping in coastal gardens. With its striking appearance and resilience, Chamaecytisus rochelii is a unique addition to any garden.
