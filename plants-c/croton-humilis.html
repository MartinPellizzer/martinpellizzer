Croton humilis

Croton humilis, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton humilis typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its small, inconspicuous flowers are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
