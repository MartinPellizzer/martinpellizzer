Chrysanthemum sichotense

Chrysanthemum sichotense, commonly known as the Sichotense chrysanthemum, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky or semi-arid environments. The plant features daisy-like flowers with yellow to orange petals and a yellow center. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
