Czekelia Transsylvanica

Czekelia transsylvanica is a rare and elusive species of orchid native to the Carpathian Mountains. It thrives in cool, shaded forest floors where moisture and nutrients are abundant. This delicate plant is known for its small, pale purple flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical marvel.
