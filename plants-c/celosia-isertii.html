Celosia isertii

Celosia isertii, commonly known as the flameflower or crested grass.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant is characterized by its upright, branching stems and dense, feather-like foliage. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, forming dense, brush-like clusters.

Celosia isertii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
