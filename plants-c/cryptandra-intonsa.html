Cryptandra intonsa

Cryptandra intonsa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cryptandra, is a small shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalypts and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers that appear in clusters. It is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and threatened species.
