Chrysojasminum humile f. farreri

Chrysojasminum humile f. farreri, commonly known as the Farrer's golden jasmine, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, woody stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
