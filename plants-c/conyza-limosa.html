Conyza limosa

Conyza limosa, commonly known as the slenderleaf horseweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas. The plant has narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.

Conyza limosa is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides, where it can become a weed.
