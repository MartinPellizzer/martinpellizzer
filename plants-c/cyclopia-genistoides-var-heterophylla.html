Cyclopia genistoides var. heterophylla

Cyclopia genistoides var. heterophylla, commonly known as the yellow karoo bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and distinctive leaf morphology. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its leaves are often variable in shape, giving rise to its varietal name.

This species plays an important role in local ecosystems and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
