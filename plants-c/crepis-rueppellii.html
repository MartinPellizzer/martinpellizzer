Crepis rueppellii

Crepis rueppellii, commonly known as Rueppell's mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Crepis rueppellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
