Crassula connata var. erectoides

Crassula connata var. erectoides, commonly known as the upright crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
