Cryptocoryne alata

Cryptocoryne alata, commonly known as the red-veined cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with bright red veins that contrast sharply against the foliage. This plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often cultivated in aquariums due to its ornamental value. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can tolerate a range of water temperatures.

Cryptocoryne alata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced aquarists.
