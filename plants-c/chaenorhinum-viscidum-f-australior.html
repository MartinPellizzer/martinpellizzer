Chaenorhinum viscidum f. australior

Chaenorhinum viscidum f. australior, commonly known as the sticky-flowered horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a sticky texture. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive horn-shaped structure at the base.
