Curio talinoides var. talinoides

Curio talinoides var. talinoides, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, colorful leaves with variegation of yellow, white, and green, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically palmately lobed and can grow up to 15 cm in length. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries.

This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
