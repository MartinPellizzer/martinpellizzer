Cyperus supinus

Cyperus supinus, commonly known as the spiny flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. Cyperus supinus is characterized by its distinctive spiny bracts that surround the inflorescence.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in wetlands and along water edges.
