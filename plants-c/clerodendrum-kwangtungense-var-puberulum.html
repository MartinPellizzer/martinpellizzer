Clerodendrum kwangtungense var. puberulum

Clerodendrum kwangtungense var. puberulum, commonly known as the silky clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
