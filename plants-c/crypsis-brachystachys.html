Crypsis brachystachys

Crypsis brachystachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed cryptic grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often densely packed, giving it a compact appearance. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish or brownish, which helps it blend into its surroundings.

Crypsis brachystachys plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates in its native ecosystems.
