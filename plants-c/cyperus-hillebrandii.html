Cyperus hillebrandii

Cyperus hillebrandii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
