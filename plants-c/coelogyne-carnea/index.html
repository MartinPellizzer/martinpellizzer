Coelogyne Carnea

Coelogyne carnea is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant pink and white flowers, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. With proper care, Coelogyne carnea can bloom beautifully throughout the year.
