Croton acronychioides

Croton acronychioides, commonly known as the nightshade croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a glossy green color. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the evening, giving it its common name. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
