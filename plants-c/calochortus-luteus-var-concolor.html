Calochortus luteus var. concolor

Calochortus luteus var. concolor, commonly known as the yellow mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes various species of flowering plants commonly referred to as mariposa lilies. The plant is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
