Cynorkis buchwaldiana

Cynorkis buchwaldiana, commonly known as the Buchwald's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces slender, erect stems with a few lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinctive, spurred lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with specific humidity and light conditions.
