Crassula herrei

Crassula herrei, commonly known as the "Hottentot's tears," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Crassula herrei is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact growth and ease of care.
