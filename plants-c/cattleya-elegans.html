Cattleya elegans

Cattleya elegans, commonly known as the elegant cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, contributing to its ability to survive in low-light conditions.

Cattleya elegans is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
