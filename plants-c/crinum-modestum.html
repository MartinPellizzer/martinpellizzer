Crinum modestum

Crinum modestum, commonly known as the modest lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces a tall, leafless stem that bears the flowers, which bloom in summer.

Crinum modestum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a strong, sometimes unpleasant, fragrance.
