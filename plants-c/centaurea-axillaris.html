Centaurea axillaris

Centaurea axillaris, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branched stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.

Centaurea axillaris is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
