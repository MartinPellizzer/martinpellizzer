Cheiranthus capitatus

Cheiranthus capitatus, commonly known as the winter flowering cabbage, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in winter and early spring. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of white wax.
