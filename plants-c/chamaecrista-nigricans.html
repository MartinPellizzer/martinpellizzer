Chamaecrista nigricans

Chamaecrista nigricans, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several seeds enclosed in a fibrous covering.

Chamaecrista nigricans is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
