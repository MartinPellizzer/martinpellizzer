Cyperus sphaeroideus

Cyperus sphaeroideus, commonly known as globe sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments. Its leaves are grass-like and often form a rosette at the base.

Cyperus sphaeroideus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
