Clitoria leptostachya

Clitoria leptostachya, commonly known as the winged bean or evergreen pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive wing-like appearance, and its flowers are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown for both its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.

Clitoria leptostachya is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a beneficial plant in agricultural systems.
