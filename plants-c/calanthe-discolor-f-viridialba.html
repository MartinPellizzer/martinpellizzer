Calanthe discolor f. viridialba

Calanthe discolor f. viridialba, commonly known as the green-veined calanthe, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lush, dark green leaves with prominent white veins, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers, typically in shades of white and pink, with a unique, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and conservationists alike.
