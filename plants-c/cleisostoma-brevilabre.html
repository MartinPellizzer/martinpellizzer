Cleisostoma brevilabre

Cleisostoma brevilabre, commonly known as the narrow-lipped cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cleisostoma brevilabre is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
