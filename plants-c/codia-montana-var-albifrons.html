Codia montana var. albifrons

Codia montana var. albifrons, commonly known as the white-eyed codia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and dark green, glossy foliage. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are borne in clusters and bloom during the spring and summer seasons.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
