Cratoxylum racemosum

Cratoxylum racemosum, commonly known as the mountain ebony, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its dark, glossy bark and lanceolate leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
