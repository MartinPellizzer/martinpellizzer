Clematis × rubromarginata

Clematis × rubromarginata, commonly known as the red-edged Clematis, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a range of botanical features.

It is a deciduous vine characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its natural habitat includes temperate regions, where it thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade.

Clematis × rubromarginata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
