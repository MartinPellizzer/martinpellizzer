Cotoneaster sylvestrii

Cotoneaster sylvestrii, commonly known as the European cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters, featuring dense, rounded foliage. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits in the autumn. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in woodlands and rocky areas.

Cotoneaster sylvestrii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
