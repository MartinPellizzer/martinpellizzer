Centaurea ultreiae var. ultreiae

Centaurea ultreiae var. ultreiae, commonly known as the Ultrie's knapweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands. It features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its flowers are arranged in dense, composite inflorescences, which attract various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
