Columella corniculata

Columella corniculata, commonly known as the horned column, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, horn-like structures that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
