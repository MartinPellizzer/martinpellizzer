Chlorophytum kilimandscharicum

Chlorophytum kilimandscharicum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and produces small, showy flowers in a variety of colors. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is popular as a houseplant. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment.

The plant is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
