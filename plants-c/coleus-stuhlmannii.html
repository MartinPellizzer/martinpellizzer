Coleus stuhlmannii

Coleus stuhlmannii, commonly known as the Stuhlmann's coleus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in spikes.

Coleus stuhlmannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
