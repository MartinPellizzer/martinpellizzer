Clematis fragrans

Clematis fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant clematis, is a climbing vine native to Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with a sweet, honey-like scent. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, consisting of three heart-shaped leaflets.

Clematis fragrans thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental and aromatic plants.
