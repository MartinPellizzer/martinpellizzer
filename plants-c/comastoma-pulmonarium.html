Comastoma pulmonarium

Comastoma pulmonarium, commonly known as the lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple or pinkish underside. Comastoma pulmonarium is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
