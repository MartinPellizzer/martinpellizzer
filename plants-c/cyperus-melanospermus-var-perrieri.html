Cyperus melanospermus var. perrieri

Cyperus melanospermus var. perrieri, commonly known as the blackseed flatsedge, is a species of sedge native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often in disturbed soils. Its seeds are dark and hard, contributing to its common name.

It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes many other sedges and related plants.
