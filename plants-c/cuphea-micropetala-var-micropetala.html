Cuphea micropetala var. micropetala

Cuphea micropetala var. micropetala, commonly known as the flame bean or red bean plant, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that resemble small beans, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Cuphea micropetala var. micropetala is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
