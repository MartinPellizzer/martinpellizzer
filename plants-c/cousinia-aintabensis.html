Cousinia aintabensis

Cousinia aintabensis, commonly known as the Aintab cushion plant, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, low-growing rosette structure. The plant produces small, yellowish to pinkish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in rocky, arid environments and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Cousinia aintabensis is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
