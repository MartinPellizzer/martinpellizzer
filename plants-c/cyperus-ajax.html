Cyperus ajax

Cyperus ajax, commonly known as the swordleaf flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments, often forming dense stands. Its leaves are sword-shaped, narrow, and slightly hairy, while its flowering spikes are usually pale green or brown.

Cyperus ajax is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to wet conditions.
