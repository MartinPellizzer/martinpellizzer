Citrus × aurantium var. limetta

Citrus × aurantium var. limetta, commonly known as the lime.

It is a hybrid citrus plant originating from the cross between the citron and the mandarin. The fruit is small, round, and has a thin, green peel that is rich in essential oils. The flesh is juicy and acidic, making it a popular ingredient in culinary and medicinal uses.

It is also valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in perfumery and traditional medicine.
