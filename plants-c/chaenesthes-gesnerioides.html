Chaenesthes gesnerioides

Chaenesthes gesnerioides, commonly known as the Gesner's chaenesthes, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
