Cassia kuntzei

Cassia kuntzei, commonly known as yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has feathery, compound leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and its bark is smooth and pale in color. Cassia kuntzei is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.
