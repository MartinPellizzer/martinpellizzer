Canna annaei

Canna annaei, commonly known as the pink dwarf canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces showy, orchid-like flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Canna annaei is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in containers or in the ground in suitable climates.
