Callisia hexandra var. caracasana

Callisia hexandra var. caracasana, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a tropical perennial belonging to the Commelinaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces long, slender stems that can reach several feet in length. The leaves are typically narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence that gives the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is often used as a decorative hanging plant due to its cascading foliage.

This variety is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
