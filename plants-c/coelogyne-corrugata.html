Coelogyne corrugata

Coelogyne corrugata, commonly known as the wrinkled orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange with deep red markings. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne corrugata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
