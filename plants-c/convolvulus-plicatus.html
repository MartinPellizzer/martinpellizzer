Convolvulus plicatus

Convolvulus plicatus, commonly known as the zigzag morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and rocky areas.

Convolvulus plicatus is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail along surfaces.
