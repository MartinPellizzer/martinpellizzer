Clematis nannophylla var. pinnatisecta

Clematis nannophylla var. pinnatisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the eastern United States.

It features finely divided, pinnatisected leaves that give it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and native plant landscaping.
