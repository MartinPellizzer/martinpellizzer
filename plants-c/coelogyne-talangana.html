Coelogyne talangana

Coelogyne talangana, commonly known as the Talangana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, hairy leaf and produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Coelogyne talangana is also noted for its ability to bloom multiple times throughout the year under proper care.
