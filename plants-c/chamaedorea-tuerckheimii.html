Chamaedorea tuerckheimii

Chamaedorea tuerckheimii, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a compact, upright palm native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk and is characterized by its pinnately compound, dark green leaves that give it a lush, tropical appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Chamaedorea tuerckheimii is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in humid environments.
