Cordylanthus pilosus var. tenuis

Cordylanthus pilosus var. tenuis, commonly known as the slenderleaf woolypod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its small, pea-like flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces slender, hairy pods that contain its seeds.
