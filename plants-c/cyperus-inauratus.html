Cyperus inauratus

Cyperus inauratus, commonly known as the spike rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and ponds. The plant features slender, grass-like stems with spike-like inflorescences composed of small, scale-like flowers. Its roots help stabilize soil and improve water filtration in aquatic ecosystems.

Cyperus inauratus is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and environmental benefits.
