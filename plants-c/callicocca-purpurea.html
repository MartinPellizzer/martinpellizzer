Callicocca purpurea

Callicocca purpurea, commonly known as the purple flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple-pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets that provide a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms. Callicocca purpurea thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its rapid growth and dramatic floral display make it a standout in landscape design.
