Citrus elliptica

Citrus elliptica, commonly known as the sour orange.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellow-orange fruits that are typically used for their high acidity and essential oils. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their aromatic properties.

Citrus elliptica is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various climates.
