Croton lucidus var. polytrichus

Croton lucidus var. polytrichus, commonly known as the glossy croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
