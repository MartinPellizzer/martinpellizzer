Cyathea tsangii

Cyathea tsangii, commonly known as the Tsang's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its massive fronds, which can reach several meters in length.

Cyathea tsangii plays a significant role in forest ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
