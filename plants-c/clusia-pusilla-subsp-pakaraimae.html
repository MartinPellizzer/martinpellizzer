Clusia pusilla subsp. pakaraimae

Clusia pusilla subsp.

pakaraimae, commonly known as the Pakaraima clusia, is a shrub native to the Guyana Shield region. It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
