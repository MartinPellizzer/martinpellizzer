Cyperus pilosus var. polyanthus

Cyperus pilosus var. polyanthus, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often purple or pink in color. It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats.
