Crinum capense var. flore-albo

Crinum capense var. flore-albo, commonly known as the white-flowered African lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces large, showy white flowers with a distinctive, fragrant aroma. The plant features strap-like, broad leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
