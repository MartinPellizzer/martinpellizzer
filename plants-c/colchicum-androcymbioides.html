Colchicum androcymbioides

Colchicum androcymbioides, commonly known as the violet autumn crocus, is a member of the Colchicaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that produces striking violet-purple flowers in the autumn. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and a short stem that bears a single flower.

It is known for its medicinal properties, though it contains toxic compounds that require careful handling.
