Conyza monorchis

Conyza monorchis, commonly known as the one-seed mercuryal, is an annual flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and wind-dispersed seeds. The plant typically grows in open areas and is often found in disturbed soils. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a flat-topped cluster, and it produces a single seed per flower.

Conyza monorchis is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
