Conyza natalensis

Conyza natalensis, commonly known as Natal ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and herbaceous growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

Natal ironweed is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
