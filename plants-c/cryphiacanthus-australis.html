Cryphiacanthus australis

Cryphiacanthus australis, commonly known as the Australian candleplant, is a unique flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, candle-like stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white, candle-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in arid climates.
