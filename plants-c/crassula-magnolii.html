Crassula magnolii

Crassula magnolii, commonly known as the African snowplant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
