Calamagrostis erythrostachya

Calamagrostis erythrostachya, commonly known as red stem reed, is a tall, ornamental grass native to North America.

It features striking red-bracted inflorescences that contrast beautifully with its silver-green foliage. The plant has slender, upright culms that can grow up to 6 feet tall, making it a striking addition to gardens and wetlands. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshes, stream banks, and pond edges.

Its botanical features make it both visually appealing and ecologically valuable.
