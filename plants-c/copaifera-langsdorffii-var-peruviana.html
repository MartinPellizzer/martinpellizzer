Copaifera langsdorffii var. peruviana

Copaifera langsdorffii var. peruviana, commonly known as Peruvian copaiba, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

Its bark and leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
