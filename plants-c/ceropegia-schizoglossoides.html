Ceropegia schizoglossoides

Ceropegia schizoglossoides, commonly known as the starfish ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces distinctive, tubular flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia schizoglossoides is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
