Calamagrostis epigejos f. abbreviata

Calamagrostis epigejos f. abbreviata, commonly known as the dwarf reed grass, is a compact variant of the common reed grass.

It is a perennial grass native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically shorter and more upright than those of the species. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and wetland restoration projects.
