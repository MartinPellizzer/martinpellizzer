Cotoneaster alaunicus

Cotoneaster alaunicus, commonly known as the Armenian cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster alaunicus is also valued for its ecological role in supporting wildlife.
