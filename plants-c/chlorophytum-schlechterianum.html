Chlorophytum schlechterianum

Chlorophytum schlechterianum, commonly known as the Schlechter's spider plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. Chlorophytum schlechterianum is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
