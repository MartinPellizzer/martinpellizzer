Calendula algarbiensis

Calendula algarbiensis, commonly known as the Algarbi calendula, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers bloom in the summer months, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Calendula algarbiensis is also valued for its medicinal properties, similar to other calendula species.
