Cyathea oinops

Cyathea oinops, commonly known as the mountain tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical forests. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, providing structural support and aiding in nutrient absorption.

The fronds are typically evergreen, contributing to the plant's ornamental value and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
