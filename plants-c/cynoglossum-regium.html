Cynoglossum regium

Cynoglossum regium, commonly known as the royal forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Cynoglossum regium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
