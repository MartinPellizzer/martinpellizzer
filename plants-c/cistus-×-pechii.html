Cistus × pechii

Cistus × pechii, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Cistus × pechii is also valued for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
