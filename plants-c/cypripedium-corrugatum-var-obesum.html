Cypripedium corrugatum var. obesum

Cypripedium corrugatum var. obesum, commonly known as the fat-lip cattail, is a distinctive orchid species native to parts of Asia.

The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist, acidic soils. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often forming dense clumps that help anchor the plant in its aquatic environment.
