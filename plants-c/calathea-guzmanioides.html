Calathea guzmanioides

Calathea guzmanioides, commonly known as the Guzmana Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green and silver patterns. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
