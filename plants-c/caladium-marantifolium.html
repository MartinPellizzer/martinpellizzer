Caladium marantifolium

Caladium marantifolium, commonly known as the flameleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
