Carduus defloratus var. argemonoides

Carduus defloratus var. argemonoides, commonly known as the Argemone thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features robust, spiny stems that are typically upright and can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, surrounded by a ring of sharp, spiny leaves. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is often found in disturbed soils and is adaptable to various environmental conditions.
