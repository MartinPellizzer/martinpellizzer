Crotalaria arrecta

Crotalaria arrecta, commonly known as the upright crownflower, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers that resemble those of the legume family. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Crotalaria arrecta is also known for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal practices.
