Croton jardinii

Croton jardinii, commonly known as the garden croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. Its leaves are typically large, lance-shaped, and have a velvety texture.

Croton jardinii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
