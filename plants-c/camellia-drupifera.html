Camellia drupifera

Camellia drupifera, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant produces woody, spherical fruits called drupes, which contain a single seed. Camellia drupifera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.
