Callistemon cunninghamii

Callistemon cunninghamii, commonly known as the coastal bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and a woody, shrub-like structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

Callistemon cunninghamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract birds and pollinators.
