Cotyledon parviflora var. squamulosa

Cotyledon parviflora var. squamulosa, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves covered in a fine, powdery coating that gives it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
