Calothamnus roseus

Calothamnus roseus, commonly known as the pink bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bright pink floral spikes that resemble bottle brushes. Calothamnus roseus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
