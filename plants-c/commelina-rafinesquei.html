Commelina rafinesquei

Commelina rafinesquei, commonly known as the Rafinesque's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Commelinales order and the Commelinaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
