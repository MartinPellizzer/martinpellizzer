Coleus wulfenioides

Coleus wulfenioides, commonly known as the Wulfen's coleus, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from deep red to purple and green. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or white.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
