Cyrtochilum davisii

Cyrtochilum davisii, commonly known as the Davis's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring, producing fragrant, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive spurred structure. Cyrtochilum davisii prefers cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded, moist habitats.
