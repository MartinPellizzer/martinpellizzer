Clidemia cordigera

Clidemia cordigera, commonly known as heartleaf or heartleaf false hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
