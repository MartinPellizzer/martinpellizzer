Cyclanthera brachybotrys var. genuina

Cyclanthera brachybotrys var. genuina, commonly known as the dwarf bottle gourd, is a variant of the bottle gourd native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing vine with thick, ribbed, bottle-shaped fruits that are typically green when mature. The plant has palmate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that are either male or female. Its fruit is often used in traditional cooking and has a mild, sweet flavor.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
