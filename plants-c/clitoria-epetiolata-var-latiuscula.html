Clitoria epetiolata var. latiuscula

Clitoria epetiolata var. latiuscula, commonly known as the broad-leaved winged bean, is a tropical leguminous plant characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features compound leaves with broad, ovate leaflets and produces pods that contain edible seeds. Its flowers are typically bright purple or pink, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variety is cultivated for both its ornamental value and as a source of nutrition.
