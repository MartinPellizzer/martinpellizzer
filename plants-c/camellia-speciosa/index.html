Camellia Speciosa

Camellia speciosa is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern China and Southeast Asia. Known for its large, showy flowers that bloom in winter and early spring, it is a popular ornamental plant. The plant features glossy dark green leaves and can grow up to 10 feet tall in ideal conditions. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens. Camellia speciosa is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its long-lasting blooms.
