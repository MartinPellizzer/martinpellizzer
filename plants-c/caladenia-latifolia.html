Caladenia latifolia

Caladenia latifolia, commonly known as the Broad-leaved Spider Orchid, is a striking orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a single, slender stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance. The orchid's broad, lance-shaped leaves are typically green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It blooms during the cooler months, usually between August and November, and prefers well-drained soils in open habitats.

This species is valued for its unique floral morphology and is often found in grassy areas and heathlands.
