Colchicum × byzantinum var. cilicicum

Colchicum × byzantinum var. cilicicum, commonly known as the Cilician colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained, loamy soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
