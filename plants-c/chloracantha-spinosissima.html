Chloracantha spinosissima

Chloracantha spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny leaf acanthus, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with prominent spines along the margins. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage is often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its unique appearance.
