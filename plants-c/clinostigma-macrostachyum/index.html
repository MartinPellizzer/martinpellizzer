Clinostigma Macrostachyum

Clinostigma macrostachyum is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique leaf structure and long, pendulous inflorescences make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the horticultural world. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and cultivation of Clinostigma macrostachyum.
