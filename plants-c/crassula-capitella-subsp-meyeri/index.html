Crassula Capitella Subsp. Meyeri

Crassula capitella subsp. meyeri is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa. It is known for its dense rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a striking, rounded shape. This subspecies is highly popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its ease of care and ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor or outdoor gardens. Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a favorite choice for both novice and experienced plant lovers.
