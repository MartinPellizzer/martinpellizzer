Coelogyne latifolia var. macrantha

Coelogyne latifolia var. macrantha, commonly known as the broad-leafed coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows epiphytically on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers, which can be white, yellow, or pink, bloom in late winter to early spring.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
