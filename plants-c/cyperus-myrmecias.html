Cyperus myrmecias

Cyperus myrmecias, commonly known as the grassy sedgeland sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, including marshes and swampy areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and colonize new areas effectively.
