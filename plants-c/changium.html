Changium

Changium, commonly known as the goldenroot, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellow flowers, which bloom in umbels. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist meadows and woodland areas. Changium species are valued for their medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.

Its rhizomes and roots are commonly used to treat digestive issues and as a tonic for overall health.
