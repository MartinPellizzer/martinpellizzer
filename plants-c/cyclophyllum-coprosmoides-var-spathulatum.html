Cyclophyllum coprosmoides var. spathulatum

Cyclophyllum coprosmoides var. spathulatum, commonly known as the broad-leaved cyclophyllum, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features elliptical to ovate leaves with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

It is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
