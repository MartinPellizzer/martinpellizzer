Clerodendrum scandens var. speciosum

Clerodendrum scandens var. speciosum, commonly known as the showy climbing clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a vigorous climber with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and showy inflorescences.

It is often used in gardens as a climbing plant due to its rapid growth and aesthetic appeal.
