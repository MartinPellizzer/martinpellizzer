Cyperus lorentzianus

Cyperus lorentzianus, commonly known as the Lorentzian flatsedge, is a species of sedge native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. The plant prefers wet or marshy habitats and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense stands in suitable environments.

Cyperus lorentzianus is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
