Croton chiamala

Croton chiamala, commonly known as the red croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that range from green to red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its striking foliage, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
