Caprifolium pilosum

Caprifolium pilosum, commonly known as the silky honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Caprifolium pilosum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
