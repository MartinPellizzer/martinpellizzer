Combretum griffithii var. griffithii

Combretum griffithii var. griffithii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often pubescent and turn reddish in color when mature. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
