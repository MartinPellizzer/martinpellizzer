Combretum fruticosum

Combretum fruticosum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough or hairy texture. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
