Coleus camporum

Coleus camporum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Coleus camporum is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to bloom year-round under proper care.
