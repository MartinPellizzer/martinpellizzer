Carduus pungens

Carduus pungens, commonly known as the prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows up to 1.5 meters tall and thrives in disturbed soils and open areas. Its leaves are covered in fine, stiff hairs that give them a rough texture.

The plant is often found in fields, roadsides, and other sunny, dry habitats.
