Cymbidium elongatum

Cymbidium elongatum, commonly known as the long-leaf orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and elongated leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium elongatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and collections.
