Cirsium japonicum var. nipponense

Cirsium japonicum var. nipponense, commonly known as Japanese thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in woodland and grassy areas. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pinkish-purple flower heads. Its flowers are composite, consisting of both ray and disk florets.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
