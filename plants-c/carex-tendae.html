Carex tendae

Carex tendae, commonly known as the Tendae sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to parts of Asia, particularly China, and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Carex tendae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
