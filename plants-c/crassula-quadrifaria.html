Crassula quadrifaria

Crassula quadrifaria, commonly known as the four-leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight.

Crassula quadrifaria is popular as a low-maintenance houseplant due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
