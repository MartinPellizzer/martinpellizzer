Callitris monticola

Callitris monticola, commonly known as the mountain cypress, is a species of coniferous tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, tall trunk and dense, feathery foliage. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves arranged in flat sprays, typical of the Cupressaceae family. Its cones are woody and contain two to three seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
