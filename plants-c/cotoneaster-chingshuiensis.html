Cotoneaster chingshuiensis

Cotoneaster chingshuiensis, commonly known as the Chingshui cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province, where it thrives in mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, red berries that appear in the fall. It has small, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces white to pink flowers in the spring.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
