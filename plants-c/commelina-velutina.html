Commelina velutina

Commelina velutina, commonly known as velvet commelina, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color and texture to shaded landscapes.
