Carex glossostigma

Carex glossostigma, commonly known as glossy sedge.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and glossy in appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Carex glossostigma is often used in water gardens and wetland restoration due to its ability to thrive in moist to wet soil conditions.
