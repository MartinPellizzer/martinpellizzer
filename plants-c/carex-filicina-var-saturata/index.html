Carex Filicina Var. Saturata

Carex filicina var. saturata is a striking sedge species known for its lush, dark green foliage and vibrant, coppery tones. Native to moist woodland areas, it thrives in shaded environments with consistently damp soil. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add texture to garden landscapes. Its unique coloration makes it a standout choice for water gardeners and landscape designers. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to moisture levels to flourish.
