Cyrtopodium poecilum var. crispum

Cyrtopodium poecilum var. crispum, commonly known as the crinkled orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, crinkled, dark green leaves that are often adorned with a waxy, white bloom. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red or orange markings, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts.
