Chlorophytum parkeri

Chlorophytum parkeri, commonly known as the bowstring hemp, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features long, thin, grass-like leaves that are often used in floral arrangements due to their elegant appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its foliage. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation and requires bright, indirect light to thrive.

Its ability to thrive in a variety of conditions makes it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
