Cryptangium kuntzeanum

Cryptangium kuntzeanum, commonly known as the Kuntze's cryptangium, is a small shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes other aromatic plants such as eucalyptus and clove. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth.
