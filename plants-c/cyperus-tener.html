Cyperus tener

Cyperus tener, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and colonize new areas effectively.

Cyperus tener is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
