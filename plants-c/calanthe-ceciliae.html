Calanthe ceciliae

Calanthe ceciliae, commonly known as the Cecilia's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant typically features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. Calanthe ceciliae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
