Centrosema schiedeanum

Centrosema schiedeanum, commonly known as the yellow rattleweed, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a climbing habit and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial in soil enrichment and ecological restoration efforts.
