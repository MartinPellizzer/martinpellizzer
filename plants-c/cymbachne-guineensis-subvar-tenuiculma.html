Cymbachne guineensis subvar. tenuiculma

Cymbachne guineensis subvar. tenuiculma, commonly known as slender African grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, often forming dense stands. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native vegetation in its habitat.
