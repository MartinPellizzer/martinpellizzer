Chionanthus vargasii

Chionanthus vargasii, commonly known as the Mexican fringe tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Chionanthus vargasii is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ecological value.
