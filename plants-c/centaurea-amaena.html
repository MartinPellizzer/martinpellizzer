Centaurea amaena

Centaurea amaena, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pinkish-blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea amaena is often found in open woodlands and grasslands at higher elevations.
