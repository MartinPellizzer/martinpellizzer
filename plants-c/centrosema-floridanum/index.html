Centrosema Floridanum

Centrosema floridanum is a flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its delicate pink blooms. this species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and full sun. it is often found in wetlands and along stream banks. its unique beauty makes it a valuable addition to native gardens and ecological restoration projects.
