Cyclosorus jaculosus

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped thallus that is covered in tiny, leaf-like structures called phyllids. Its reproductive structures, known as gemmae, are produced in clusters and aid in vegetative propagation.

Cyclosorus jaculosus is an important indicator species for assessing the health of wetland and forest ecosystems.
