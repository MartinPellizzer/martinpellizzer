Cucurbita argyrosperma var. stenosperma

Cucurbita argyrosperma var. stenosperma, commonly known as the narrow-seeded silver-seed gourd, is a variant of the silver-seed gourd native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, cylindrical fruits with a smooth, pale green to white rind. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves. The seeds are typically narrow and white, with a high oil content.

It is cultivated for its edible fruits, which are used in various traditional dishes.
