Cuphea mesostemon f. ovalifolia

Cuphea mesostemon f. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and for its ability to attract hummingbirds.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for container gardening and landscape design.
