Cranichis crumenifera

Cranichis crumenifera, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Cranichis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small and often clustered, with a distinctive coloration that varies by species.

Cranichis crumenifera is notable for its specialized pollination mechanisms and is often found in temperate regions.
