Colysis liouii

Colysis liouii, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant yellow and orange inflorescences with intricate, fragrant flowers that bloom during the warmer months. The plant has a compact, pseudobulbous structure with strap-like leaves that help it store water and nutrients. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements have made it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts.
