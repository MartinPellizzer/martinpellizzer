Croton hastatus

Croton hastatus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.

Croton hastatus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
