Cayaponia triangularis

Cayaponia triangularis, commonly known as the triangular cayaponia, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
