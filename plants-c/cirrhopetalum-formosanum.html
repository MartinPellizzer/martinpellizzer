Cirrhopetalum formosanum

Cirrhopetalum formosanum, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in forest understories, where it thrives in shaded, humid conditions. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor, and it produces small, green leaves.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
