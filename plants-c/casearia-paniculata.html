Casearia paniculata

Casearia paniculata, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that appear in clusters, giving it a vibrant and showy appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Casearia paniculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
