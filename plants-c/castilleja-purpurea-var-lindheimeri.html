Castilleja purpurea var. lindheimeri

Castilleja purpurea var. lindheimeri, commonly known as Lindheimer's red globemallow, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and relies on other plants for some of its nutrients through specialized root structures called haustoria. This species thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and rocky slopes.
