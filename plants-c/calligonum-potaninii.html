Calligonum potaninii

Calligonum potaninii, commonly known as the sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically small and hairy, which helps reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Calligonum potaninii is well adapted to harsh climates and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
