Crassula schimperi var. illecebroides

Crassula schimperi var. illecebroides, commonly known as the Crimson Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a reddish or pinkish hue, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
