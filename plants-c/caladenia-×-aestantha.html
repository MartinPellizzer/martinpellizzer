Caladenia × aestantha

Caladenia × aestantha, commonly known as the pink-streaked spider orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and striking, spider-like flowers with a distinctive pink and white coloration. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal dunes. Its flowers are pollinated by thynnid wasps, which are attracted by the plant's unique scent and nectar.

Caladenia × aestantha is a rare and protected species, highlighting the importance of conserving its natural habitat.
