Calycophysum gracile

Calycophysum gracile, commonly known as the slender calycophycean, is a marine alga belonging to the order Calycophorales.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching thallus composed of filamentous structures. The plant exhibits a unique reproductive system, with both sexual and asexual stages occurring in its life cycle. It thrives in shallow, coastal waters where light penetration is sufficient for photosynthesis.

Its ecological role includes contributing to benthic communities and serving as a food source for various marine organisms.
