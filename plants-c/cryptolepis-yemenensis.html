Cryptolepis yemenensis

Cryptolepis yemenensis, commonly known as Yemen cryptolepis, is a shrub native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally in folk medicine for various ailments.
