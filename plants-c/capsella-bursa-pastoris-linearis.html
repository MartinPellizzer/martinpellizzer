Capsella bursa-pastoris linearis

Capsella bursa-pastoris linearis, commonly known as the slender shepherd's purse, is a variant of the common shepherd's purse.

It is an annual plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, green pods that resemble a purse, which is a distinctive botanical feature. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides.

Its flowers are typically yellow and are arranged in clusters, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
