Cynanchum tenellum

Cynanchum tenellum, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is known for its milkweed species. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often hairy and can grow up to several feet tall. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are clustered in umbels and have a distinctive milky sap.

This species is often found in open fields and along roadsides, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
