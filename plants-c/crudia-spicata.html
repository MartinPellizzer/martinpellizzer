Crudia spicata

Crudia spicata, commonly known as the spike rudbeckia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Crudia spicata is typically found in moist woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in native plant gardens.
