Campanula excisa

Campanula excisa, commonly known as the cutleaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has distinctive lobed leaves that are often deeply cut, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula excisa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
