Cyphostemma wilmsii

Cyphostemma wilmsii, commonly known as the African moonvine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by distinctive, inflated fruit capsules. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
