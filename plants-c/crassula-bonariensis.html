Crassula bonariensis

Crassula bonariensis, commonly known as the "Snowplant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
