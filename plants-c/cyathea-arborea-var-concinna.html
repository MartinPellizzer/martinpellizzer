Cyathea arborea var. concinna

Cyathea arborea var. concinna, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is a member of the order Cyatheales. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
