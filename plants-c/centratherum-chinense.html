Centratherum chinense

Centratherum chinense, commonly known as the Chinese centplant or red centplant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flower heads. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous annual or perennial, with slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a central disk surrounded by bright-colored ray florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant colors and compact growth habit.
