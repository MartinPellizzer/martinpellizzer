Commianthus discolor

Commianthus discolor, commonly known as the variegated evergreen, is a unique plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves, which display a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it ideal for small spaces or container gardening. Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, providing consistent visual interest.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Araliaceae and is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various light conditions.
