Cistanthe glauca

Cistanthe glauca, commonly known as blue cistanthe, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has hairy, succulent leaves that help it conserve water in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cistanthe glauca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
