Cyrtanthus thorncroftii

Cyrtanthus thorncroftii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cyrtanthus thorncroftii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
