Capparis yco

Capparis yco, commonly known as the Yco Caper, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible, acidic berries that are used in local cuisines. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Capparis yco thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions.
