Clitoria Javitensis Var. Portobellensis

Clitoria javitensis var. portobellensis is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant purple and violet blooms. Native to parts of Southeast Asia, this species thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and showy flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture compared to other members of the legume family.
