Clerodendrum imperialis

Clerodendrum imperialis, commonly known as the imperial clerodendrum, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers emit a strong, sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
