Crinum toxicarium var. asiaticum

Crinum toxicarium var. asiaticum, commonly known as the Asian Crinum, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllis family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that grow from a bulbous base, typical of many amaryllis relatives. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a strong, sometimes unpleasant odor.

The plant contains toxic alkaloids, making it potentially harmful if ingested.
