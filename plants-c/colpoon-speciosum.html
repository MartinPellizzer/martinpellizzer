Colpoon speciosum

Colpoon speciosum, commonly known as the showy colpoon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It features striking, palmate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its unique foliage and delicate blossoms, it is a popular choice for shade gardens and botanical collections.
