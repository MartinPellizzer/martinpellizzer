Citrus × nana

Citrus × nana, commonly known as the dwarf lemon or nana lemon, is a hybrid citrus plant originating from a cross between the lemon (Citrus × limon) and the citron (Citrus medica).

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, making it ideal for small spaces and container gardening. The plant produces small, round, yellow-orange fruits that are typically harvested when they reach a mature size. The leaves are glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
