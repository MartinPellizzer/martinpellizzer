Cistus gardi

Cistus gardi, commonly known as the Garden Cistus, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Cistus gardi is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to support local wildlife.
