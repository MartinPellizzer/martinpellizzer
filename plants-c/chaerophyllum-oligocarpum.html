Chaerophyllum oligocarpum

Chaerophyllum oligocarpum, commonly known as the lesser cow-wheat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in late spring.
