Celosia bonnivairii var. lanceolata

Celosia bonnivairii var. lanceolata, commonly known as the firecracker plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spiky bracts that resemble fireworks, which come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
