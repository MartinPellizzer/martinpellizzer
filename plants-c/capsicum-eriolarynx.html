Capsicum eriolarynx

Capsicum eriolarynx, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chili.

It is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil. This plant belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves. The fruits are typically small, round, and can range in color from yellow to orange when mature.

It is valued for its heat and flavor in regional cuisines.
