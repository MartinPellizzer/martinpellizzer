Combretum trinitense

Combretum trinitense, commonly known as the Trinidad flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to the Caribbean, particularly Trinidad and Tobago.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly rough texture. The tree produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.
