Citrus × limonum var. pulpa-dulci

Citrus × limonum var. pulpa-dulci, commonly known as the sweet pulp lemon.

This variety is characterized by its thick, juicy pulp that is less acidic and sweeter than typical lemons. It has a distinctively rounded fruit shape with a thin, yellowish peel. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
