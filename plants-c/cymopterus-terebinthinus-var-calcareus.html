Cymopterus terebinthinus var. calcareus

Cymopterus terebinthinus var. calcareus, commonly known as the California buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers in late summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
