Capsella bursa-pastoris f. aestivalis

Capsella bursa-pastoris f. aestivalis, commonly known as the summer common chickweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, alternate leaves that are usually hairy. The plant produces small white or pale pink flowers with four petals, typical of the mustard family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, fields, and along roadsides.
