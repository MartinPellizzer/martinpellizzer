Catodiacrum rubidum

Catodiacrum rubidum, commonly known as the crimson fern, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red fronds, which contrast sharply with its dark green stems. The plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is known for its delicate, lace-like appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
