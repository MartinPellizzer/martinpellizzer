Centranthus ruber subsp. angustifolius

Centranthus ruber subsp.

angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved red campion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features upright, branching stems that are typically red or purple in color, adding visual interest to gardens.

The plant produces small, pink to red flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its narrow, lance-shaped leaves are arranged alternately along the stems, contributing to its elegant appearance.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
