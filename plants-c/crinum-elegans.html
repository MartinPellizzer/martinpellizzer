Crinum elegans

Crinum elegans, commonly known as the elegant crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink, with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Crinum elegans is known for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in warm climates.
