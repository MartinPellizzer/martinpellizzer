Calathea subtilis

Calathea subtilis, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, and intricately patterned leaves that display a range of colors, including deep greens, purples, and whites. The plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is often adorned with subtle, contrasting patterns that enhance its ornamental appeal.

Calathea subtilis requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to maintain its health and vibrant appearance.
