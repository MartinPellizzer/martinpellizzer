Comparettia splendens

Comparettia splendens, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in regions with warm, humid climates. The plant features showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive greenish tinge. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, typically arranged in a basal rosette.

Comparettia splendens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
