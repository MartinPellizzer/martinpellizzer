Chlorophytum subhispidum

Chlorophytum subhispidum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, upright leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation, which helps in photosynthesis. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an excellent choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are sword-shaped and can grow up to 1 meter in height, adding a striking visual element to any space.

Chlorophytum subhispidum is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
