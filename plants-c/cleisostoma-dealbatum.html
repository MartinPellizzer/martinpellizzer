Cleisostoma dealbatum

Cleisostoma dealbatum, commonly known as the golden cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cleisostoma dealbatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
