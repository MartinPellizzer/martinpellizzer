Croton mauritianus

Croton mauritianus, commonly known as the Mauritius croton, is a shrub or small tree native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that can range from green to red, orange, or yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsules that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas or forested regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is considered rare in the wild.
