Crotalaria sessiliflora f. angustifolia

Crotalaria sessiliflora f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes, with distinctive bean-like pods that contain seeds. Its leaves are typically narrow and sessile, contributing to its specific botanical classification.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
