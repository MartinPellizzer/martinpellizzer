Centaurea nervosa subsp. josifovicii

Centaurea nervosa subsp.

josifovicii, commonly known as the Josifovici's slender centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers with reddish-brown bracts, typically blooming from June to August. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly zigzagged margin.

This subspecies is well adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
