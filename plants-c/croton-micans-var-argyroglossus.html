Croton micans var. argyroglossus

Croton micans var. argyroglossus, commonly known as the silver-tongue croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a shimmering appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
