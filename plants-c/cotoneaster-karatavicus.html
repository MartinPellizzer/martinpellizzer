Cotoneaster karatavicus

Cotoneaster karatavicus, commonly known as the Karatavsky cotoneaster, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Cotoneaster karatavicus is valued for its ornamental qualities and hardiness in challenging climates.
