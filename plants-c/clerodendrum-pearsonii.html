Clerodendrum pearsonii

Clerodendrum pearsonii, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. It produces small, yellowish fruits that are capsule-like in structure.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
