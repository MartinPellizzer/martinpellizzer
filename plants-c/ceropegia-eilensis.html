Ceropegia eilensis

Ceropegia eilensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, tuberous stems and heart-shaped leaves. Ceropegia eilensis thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
