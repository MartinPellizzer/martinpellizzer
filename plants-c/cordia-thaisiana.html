Cordia thaisiana

Cordia thaisiana, commonly known as the Thai cordia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a distinct aroma.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, spherical seeds.
