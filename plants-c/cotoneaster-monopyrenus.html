Cotoneaster monopyrenus

Cotoneaster monopyrenus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, dark green leaves that turn reddish-brown in autumn. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster monopyrenus is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun conditions.
