Comptonella lactea var. poissonii

Comptonella lactea var. poissonii, commonly known as the Poisson's milkbush, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its pale green, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, waxy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
