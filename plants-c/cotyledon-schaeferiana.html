Cotyledon schaeferiana

Cotyledon schaeferiana, commonly known as the Schaefer's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, and they often have a distinctive, almost triangular shape. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value to its appearance.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
