Cotoneaster kweitschoviensis

Cotoneaster kweitschoviensis, commonly known as the Kweitschovian cotoneaster, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or forested habitats. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated margin. Its flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in clusters, and it produces bright red berries in the fall.

Cotoneaster kweitschoviensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
