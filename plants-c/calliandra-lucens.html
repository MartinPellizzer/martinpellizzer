Calliandra lucens

Calliandra lucens, commonly known as the shining calliandra, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Calliandra lucens is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
