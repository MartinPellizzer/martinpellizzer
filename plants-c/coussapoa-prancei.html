Coussapoa prancei

Coussapoa prancei, commonly known as the Prance's cup.

It is a rare and endangered species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. This herbaceous plant is characterized by its striking violet flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocky outcrops.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is critically endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
