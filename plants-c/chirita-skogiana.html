Chirita skogiana

Chirita skogiana, commonly known as the mountain chirita, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. Due to its ornamental value, it is also appreciated in horticultural trade.
