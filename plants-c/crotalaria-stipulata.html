Crotalaria stipulata

Crotalaria stipulata, commonly known as the stipule legume, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Crotalaria stipulata is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in various environmental conditions.
