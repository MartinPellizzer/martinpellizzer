Cypripedium macranthos

Cypripedium macranthos, commonly known as the large-flowered slipper orchid, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy orchid flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, with a unique pouch-like structure at the base. The plant features a single, upright stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height, bearing several broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by bees and are often visited by various insects.

Cypripedium macranthos is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also of interest in botanical research due to its ecological and conservation significance.
