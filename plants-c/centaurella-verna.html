Centaurella verna

Centaurella verna, commonly known as spring centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The plant produces small, spiny seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or sandy habitats.

Centaurella verna is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
