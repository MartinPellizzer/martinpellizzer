Calanthe furcata f. matsudae

Calanthe furcata f. matsudae, commonly known as the Matsudae calanthe, is a hybrid orchid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features lanceolate, glossy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs. Its flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
