Cyclophyllum coprosmoides var. coprosmoides

Cyclophyllum coprosmoides var. coprosmoides, commonly known as the coprosmoides cyclophyllum, is a species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and ecological studies.
