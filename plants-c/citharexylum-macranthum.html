Citharexylum macranthum

Citharexylum macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its showy, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
