Croton polymorphus var. velleriflorus

Croton polymorphus var. velleriflorus, commonly known as the velvetflower croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, and often colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates.
