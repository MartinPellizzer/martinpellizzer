Citharexylum rosei

Citharexylum rosei, commonly known as the rosewood tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
