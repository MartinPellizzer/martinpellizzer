Cotoneaster Horizontalis Var. Typicus

Horizontal cotoneaster, scientifically known as Cotoneaster horizontalis var. typicus, is a hardy shrub native to the mountainous regions of China. It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and clusters of small, red berries that attract wildlife. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, drought-resistant plants. Despite its beauty, it requires careful pruning to maintain its shape and prevent overgrowth.
