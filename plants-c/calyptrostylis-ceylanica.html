Calyptrostylis ceylanica

Calyptrostylis ceylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon calyptrostylis, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Sri Lanka and is characterized by its unique, pendulous inflorescence and striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps retain moisture in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
