Commelina microspatha

Commelina microspatha, commonly known as the small-spurred commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes plants known for their striking floral structures. Commelina microspatha is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
