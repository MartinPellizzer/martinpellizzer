Callilepis laureola

Callilepis laureola, commonly known as the silverleaf tree or African silverleaf, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has small, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Callilepis laureola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
