Clitoria pilosula

Clitoria pilosula, commonly known as the hairy urd or slender butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and distinctive pod-like fruits. The plant features delicate, butterfly-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being hairy, giving the plant its common name.

Clitoria pilosula is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.
