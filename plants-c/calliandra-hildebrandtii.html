Calliandra hildebrandtii

Calliandra hildebrandtii, commonly known as the silky sericea.

It is a leguminous shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences and trifoliate leaves, which are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The plant is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
