Cattleya guttata var. prinzii

Cattleya guttata var. prinzii, commonly known as the Prinzii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking appearance.

It features large, velvety flowers with a unique combination of colors, often including a deep red lip and a contrasting white or cream background. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks in its native tropical habitats. Its foliage consists of broad, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
