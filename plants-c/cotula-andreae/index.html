Cotula Andreae

Cotula andreae is a small, delicate flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its clusters of tiny, daisy-like white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to any garden. Cotula andreae is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
