Cyclamen purpurascens

Cyclamen purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered cyclamen, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus.

It features heart-shaped, velvety foliage with wavy margins and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens with their delicate, spurred petals.

Cyclamen purpurascens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
