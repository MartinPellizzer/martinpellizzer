Coreopsis brasiliensis

Coreopsis brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. Coreopsis brasiliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ease of care.
