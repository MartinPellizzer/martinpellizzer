Carduus gnaphaloides

Carduus gnaphaloides, commonly known as the narrowleaf thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer.
