Cassia obliquifolia

Cassia obliquifolia, commonly known as the zigzag cassia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with a zigzag or twisted appearance, which gives it its common name. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the legume family. Its seed pods are elongated and contain numerous flat, winged seeds.

Cassia obliquifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
