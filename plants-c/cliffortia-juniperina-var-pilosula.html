Cliffortia juniperina var. pilosula

Cliffortia juniperina var. pilosula, commonly known as the silky cliffortia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique texture and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
