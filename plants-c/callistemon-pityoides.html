Callistemon pityoides

Callistemon pityoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that appear in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
