Croton danguyanus

Croton danguyanus, commonly known as the Danguyan croton, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant features palmate leaves with a waxy texture and vibrant green to reddish hues. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in tropical gardens and botanical collections.
