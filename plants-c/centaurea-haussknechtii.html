Centaurea haussknechtii

Centaurea haussknechtii, commonly known as the Haussknecht's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are upright and branched.

Centaurea haussknechtii is often found in open habitats such as steppes and coastal areas.
