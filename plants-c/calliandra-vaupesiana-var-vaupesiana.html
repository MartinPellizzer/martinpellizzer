Calliandra vaupesiana var. vaupesiana

Calliandra vaupesiana var. vaupesiana, commonly known as the pink calliandra, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery, pink to red inflorescences that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
