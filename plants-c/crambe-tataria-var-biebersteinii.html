Crambe tataria var. biebersteinii

Crambe tataria var. biebersteinii, commonly known as Siberian mustard, is a flowering plant native to the colder regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has serrated leaves and produces small, elongated pods containing seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to harsh climatic conditions.

Botanically, it is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants.
