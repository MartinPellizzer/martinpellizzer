Coleus atropurpureus var. densiflorus

Coleus atropurpureus var. densiflorus, commonly known as the denseflower coleus, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored foliage, which often displays shades of red, purple, and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its showy appearance, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
