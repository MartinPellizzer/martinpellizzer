Cissus neei

Cissus neei, commonly known as the African snow vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Vitaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, waxy, translucent leaves that resemble snowflakes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by yellowish fruits that contain tiny seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Due to its unique appearance, it is popular as a decorative plant in indoor gardening.
