Carduus

Carduus, commonly known as thistle, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are characterized by their spiny leaves and purple or yellow flowers. They are native to Europe, North Africa, and western Asia, though some species have been introduced to other regions. Many species of Carduus are perennial, with some growing as shrubs or herbaceous plants.

The genus includes both ornamental and weeds, with some species being used in traditional medicine.
