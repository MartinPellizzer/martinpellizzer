Cyathea flexuosa

Cyathea flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately divided fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to anchor firmly in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically evergreen and can grow up to several meters in length.

Cyathea flexuosa is often found in rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance.
