Cytisus supinus var. stefanoffii

Cytisus supinus var. stefanoffii, commonly known as the dwarf broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its compact form. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
