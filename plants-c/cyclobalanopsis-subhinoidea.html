Cyclobalanopsis subhinoidea

Cyclobalanopsis subhinoidea, commonly known as the Philippine ironwood, is a large evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and beeches, and is characterized by its dense, hard wood. Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
