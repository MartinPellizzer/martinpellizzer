Chlorophytum haygarthii

Chlorophytum haygarthii, commonly known as the Haygarth's spider plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, arching stems that bear small, clustered white flowers. The plant produces offsets that can be easily propagated, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light, making it adaptable to various growing conditions.
