Cephalostachyum fuchsianum

Cephalostachyum fuchsianum, commonly known as the Fuchsian stiltgrass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in wet, swampy habitats and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks.

Due to its adaptability, it can sometimes become invasive in non-native environments.
