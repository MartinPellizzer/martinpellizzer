Camaridium stenophyllum

Camaridium stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed camaridium, is a rare and specialized plant species found in specific alpine environments.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in harsh, high-altitude conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. Its root system is fibrous and shallow, allowing it to anchor in rocky or poorly nourished soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native habitat.
