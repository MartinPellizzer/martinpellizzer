Coleus verschaffeltii var. splendens

Coleus verschaffeltii var. splendens, commonly known as the splendens coleus, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep reds and oranges to purples and yellows. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is known for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a velvety texture.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight.
