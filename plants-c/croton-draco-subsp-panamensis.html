Croton draco subsp. panamensis

Croton draco subsp.

panamensis, commonly known as the Panama croton, is a shrub or small tree native to Central America. It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red or purple.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its bark is thick and fibrous, and it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.
