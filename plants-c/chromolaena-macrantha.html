Chromolaena macrantha

Chromolaena macrantha, commonly known as the tropical blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, blue-violet flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Chromolaena macrantha is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
