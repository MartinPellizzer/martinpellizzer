Callista erosa

Callista erosa, commonly known as the slender callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive shape that attracts pollinators like bees and birds.

Callista erosa is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
