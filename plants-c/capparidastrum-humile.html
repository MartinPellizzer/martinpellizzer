Capparidastrum humile

Capparidastrum humile, commonly known as the small caper, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Capparaceae and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shady areas and has a compact, shrub-like appearance. Its botanical features include small, rounded leaves and a short, woody stem.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
