Chrysophyllum delevoyi

Chrysophyllum delevoyi, commonly known as the African star apple, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers.
