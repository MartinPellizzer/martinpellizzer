Cleyera obovata

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, Japan, and Korea, where it typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. Cleyera obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
