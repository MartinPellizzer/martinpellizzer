Cestrum laurifolium f. acutifolium

Cestrum laurifolium f. acutifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leaved cestrum, is a variant of the evergreen cestrum species native to Central America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are more pointed than those of the typical variety. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a tropical aesthetic.
