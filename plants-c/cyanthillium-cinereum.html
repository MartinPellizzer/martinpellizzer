Cyanthillium cinereum

Cyanthillium cinereum, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Its leaves are covered in a fine layer of white, waxy hairs that give it a distinctive appearance.

Cyanthillium cinereum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
