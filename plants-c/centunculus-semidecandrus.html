Centunculus semidecandrus

Centunculus semidecandrus, commonly known as the lesser centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Boraginaceae.

It is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, calcareous soils.
