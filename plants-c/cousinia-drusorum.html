Cousinia drusorum

Cousinia drusorum, commonly known as the Drusian hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are often gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Cousinia drusorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
