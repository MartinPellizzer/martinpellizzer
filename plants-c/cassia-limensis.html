Cassia limensis

Cassia limensis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain flat, winged seeds. Cassia limensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
