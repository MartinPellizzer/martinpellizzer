Clematis montana var. obtusisepala

Clematis montana var. obtusisepala, commonly known as the Himalayan clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the Himalayas.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, with a distinctive, rounded petal base. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a support structure to climb.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
