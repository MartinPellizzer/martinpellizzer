Conophytum compressum

Conophytum compressum, commonly known as the "Compressum Stonecrop," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Conophytum compressum is known for its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal.
