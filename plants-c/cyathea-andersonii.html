Cyathea andersonii

Cyathea andersonii, commonly known as the Anderson's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in the soil and support its tall structure.

Cyathea andersonii plays a significant role in the ecological systems of its habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
