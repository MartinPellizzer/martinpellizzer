Cyclamen maritimum

Cyclamen maritimum, commonly known as the sea cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Cyclamen maritimum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable conditions.
