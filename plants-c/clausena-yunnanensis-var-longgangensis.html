Clausena yunnanensis var. longgangensis

Clausena yunnanensis var. longgangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Clausena, is a variant of the Clausena yunnanensis species native to China.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree characterized by its lanceolate, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruits are drupes, usually red or orange when mature, containing a single seed.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
