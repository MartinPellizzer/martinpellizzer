Cynanchum reflexum

Cynanchum reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed milkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows in wetlands and along stream banks, thriving in moist, sandy soils. Its showy, fragrant flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in the summer months.

Cynanchum reflexum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
