Croton astroites var. lanceolatus

Croton astroites var. lanceolatus, commonly known as the lanceleaf croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves, which are often bright green with a slightly waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by distinctive spiny capsules.

This species is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
