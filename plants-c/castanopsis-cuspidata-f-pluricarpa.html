Castanopsis cuspidata f. pluricarpa

Castanopsis cuspidata f. pluricarpa, commonly known as the Chinese stone oak, is a variant of the evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, beeches, and chestnuts. The plant is characterized by its dense, glossy evergreen leaves and produces small, edible acorns. It thrives in temperate to subtropical climates and is often found in forested areas.
