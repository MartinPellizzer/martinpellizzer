Cryptocentrum latifolium

Cryptocentrum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf cryptocentrum, is a unique plant species native to specific tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered together. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
