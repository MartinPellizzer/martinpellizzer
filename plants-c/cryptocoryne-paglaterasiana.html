Cryptocoryne paglaterasiana

Cryptocoryne paglaterasiana, commonly known as the Java cryptostegia, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that often develop a reddish tint under low light conditions. The plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water and prefers moderate to high water flow. It is often cultivated in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne paglaterasiana is also known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters, making it a popular choice among aquarists.
