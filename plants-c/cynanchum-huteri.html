Cynanchum huteri

Cynanchum huteri, commonly known as the Huter's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is known for its milkweed species. The plant features showy, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a hairy texture, while its stems are slender and often branched.

Cynanchum huteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has also been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
