Chrysophyllum lissophyllum f. obscurinervum

Chrysophyllum lissophyllum f. obscurinervum, commonly known as the dark-veined cherimoya, is a unique variant of the cherimoya plant.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves with prominent, dark-colored veins. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a tropical delicacy, known for its creamy, sweet flesh.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruit in suitable climates.
