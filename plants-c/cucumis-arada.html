Cucumis arada

Cucumis arada, commonly known as the wild cucumber, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a climbing vine that produces small, spiny fruits resembling miniature cucumbers. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and yellow flowers typical of the Cucurbitaceae family. Its fruits are typically harvested when green and are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The plant thrives in warm, sunny environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
