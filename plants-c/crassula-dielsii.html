Crassula dielsii

Crassula dielsii, commonly known as the Diels' Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, rounded leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula dielsii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and aesthetic appeal.
