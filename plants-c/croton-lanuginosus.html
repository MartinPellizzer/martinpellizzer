Croton lanuginosus

Croton lanuginosus, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by spiny capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.
