Crossosperma

Crossosperma, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Africa and features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white or pink petals. The plants typically have upright stems and are often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. Crossosperma species are known for their ornamental value and are sometimes cultivated in gardens.

They are also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
