Cordia Speciosa

Cordia speciosa is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant flowers and unique foliage. Native to regions in South America and the Caribbean, it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and clusters of bright yellow blossoms that attract pollinators. Often grown as an ornamental, it adds a dramatic touch to gardens and landscapes. Its resilience and beauty make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
