Cryptocarya calelanensis

Cryptocarya calelanensis, commonly known as the Calelanensis tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic and ecological significance.
