Citrus suavissima

Citrus suavissima, commonly known as the sweet lemon or sweet orange, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It produces small, round, yellow fruits with a thin, aromatic peel. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and oval-shaped, while its flowers are fragrant and white.

The fruit is prized for its sweet, juicy pulp and is often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
