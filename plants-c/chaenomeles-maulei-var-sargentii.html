Chaenomeles maulei var. sargentii

Chaenomeles maulei var. sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent cherry or Maule cherry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, single or double pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has elliptical leaves with serrated edges and produces small, red or yellow berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

The variety is noted for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
