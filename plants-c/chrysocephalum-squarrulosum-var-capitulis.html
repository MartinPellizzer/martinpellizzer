Chrysocephalum squarrulosum var. capitulis

Chrysocephalum squarrulosum var. capitulis, commonly known as the golden everlasting or yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

The leaves are typically grayish-green and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged, resilient appearance.
