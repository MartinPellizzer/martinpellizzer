Combretum volkensii

Combretum volkensii, commonly known as the red sandalwood or Volkens' flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptic to ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The wood of Combretum volkensii is valued for its durability and is used in traditional crafts and construction.
