Croton mirabilis

Croton mirabilis, commonly known as the croton or the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright red to orange and yellow, depending on the season and environmental conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance.

Croton mirabilis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
