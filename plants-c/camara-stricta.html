Camara stricta

Camara stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often narrow and have a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to purple.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
