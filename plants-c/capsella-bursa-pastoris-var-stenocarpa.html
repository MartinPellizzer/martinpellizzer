Capsella bursa-pastoris var. stenocarpa

Capsella bursa-pastoris var. stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited shepherd's purse, is a variant of the common shepherd's purse.

It is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive triangular seed pods. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in ecological and agricultural studies.
