Canna glauca var. rubrolutea

Canna glauca var. rubrolutea, commonly known as the red and yellow canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, contrasting beautifully with its vibrant flowers. The flowers are showy, with a combination of red and yellow hues, creating a dramatic visual impact in gardens. This variety thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 4 feet tall, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
