Croton jansii

Croton jansii, commonly known as the Jansii croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red, yellow, or orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and sometimes toxic species. Croton jansii typically thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in suitable climates.
