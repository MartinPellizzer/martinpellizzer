Centaurium alpina

Centaurium alpina, commonly known as alpine centaury, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, blue to violet flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. Centaurium alpina thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.

It is often found in meadows and slopes at high elevations.
