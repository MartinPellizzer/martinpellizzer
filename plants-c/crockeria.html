Crockeria

Crockeria, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, delicate appearance, featuring a single, elongated leaf and a single, showy flower that often resembles a ghostly silhouette. Native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in shaded, humid environments with high moisture levels. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without deriving nutrients from them.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Crockeria is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists alike.
