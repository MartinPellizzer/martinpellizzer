Cochlidium rostratum var. areolatum

Cochlidium rostratum var. areolatum, commonly known as the spotted horn snail, is a species of air-breathing land snail.

It belongs to the family Helicidae and is characterized by its distinctive, spiral-shaped shell with a prominent, pointed spire. The shell is typically yellowish or brownish with irregularly distributed spots or patches, giving it its common name. The snail thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and gardens.
