Cyclamen zonale

Cyclamen zonale, commonly known as the zonale cyclamen, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink or red flowers with distinctive spurred petals. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Cyclamen zonale is well-suited for indoor growing and can be propagated through seeds or tuberous roots.
