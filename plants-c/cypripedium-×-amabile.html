Cypripedium × amabile

Cypripedium × amabile, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to eastern Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, slipper-shaped flowers with a distinctive pouch-like structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are usually pink, white, or pale yellow, and they bloom in early spring.

Cypripedium × amabile is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
