Capparis luzonensis var. ampla

Capparis luzonensis var. ampla, commonly known as the broad-leaved caper, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several elliptical seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded areas of forest understories.
