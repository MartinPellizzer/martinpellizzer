Cyrtandra × ferrocolorata

Cyrtandra × ferrocolorata, commonly known as the hybrid beauty flower, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra hirsuta and Cyrtandra fragrans.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, including Hawaii and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a striking contrast between the petals and the green foliage. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and coastal areas.

Cyrtandra × ferrocolorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
