Conyza odorata

Conyza odorata, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces numerous tiny seeds that are easily dispersed by the wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open fields, roadsides, and gardens.

Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
