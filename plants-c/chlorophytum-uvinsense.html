Chlorophytum uvinsense

Chlorophytum uvinsense, commonly known as the spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are often striped with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright orange berries. It is a hardy plant that thrives in well-drained soil and moderate light conditions.

Its ability to propagate through runners makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
