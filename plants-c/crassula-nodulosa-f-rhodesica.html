Crassula nodulosa f. rhodesica

Crassula nodulosa f. rhodesica, commonly known as the Rhodes Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
