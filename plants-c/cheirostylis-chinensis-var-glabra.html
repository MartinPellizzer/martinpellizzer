Cheirostylis chinensis var. glabra

Cheirostylis chinensis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-styled Chinese orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright rhizomes and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in a spike. It prefers shady, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
