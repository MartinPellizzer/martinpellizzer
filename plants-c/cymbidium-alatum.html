Cymbidium alatum

Cymbidium alatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive, elongated shape.

Cymbidium alatum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
