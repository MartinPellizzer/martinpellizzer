Citrus × aurantium var. genuensis

Citrus × aurantium var. genuensis, commonly known as the common orange.

It is a citrus hybrid native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its oval, thick-skinned fruits that are typically orange in color. The plant produces fragrant white flowers and has a shrub-like growth habit with glossy, dark green leaves. Its fruit is valued for its juice, which is rich in vitamin C, and for its use in culinary and medicinal applications.

The plant is also cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and flowering.
