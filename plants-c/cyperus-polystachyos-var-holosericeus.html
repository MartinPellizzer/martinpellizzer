Cyperus polystachyos var. holosericeus

Cyperus polystachyos var. holosericeus, commonly known as the silky flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to brown flowers. The plant is characterized by its silky, hairy appearance, particularly on the stems and leaves, which give it a soft, almost velvety texture. It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in marshes, swales, and along waterways.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland habitats.
