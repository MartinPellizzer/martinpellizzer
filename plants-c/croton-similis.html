Croton similis

Croton similis, commonly known as the flame tree or red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Croton similis is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
