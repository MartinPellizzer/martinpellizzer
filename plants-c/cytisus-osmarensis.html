Cytisus osmarensis

Cytisus osmarensis, commonly known as the Osmer's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are small, trifoliate, and have a feathery appearance.

Cytisus osmarensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
