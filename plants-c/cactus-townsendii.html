Cactus townsendii

Cactus townsendii, commonly known as the Townsend's cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its succulent stems store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments.

It is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
