Cassinia hewsoniae

Cassinia hewsoniae, commonly known as the Hewson's cassinia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow or cream-colored flower heads. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, narrow leaves and is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
