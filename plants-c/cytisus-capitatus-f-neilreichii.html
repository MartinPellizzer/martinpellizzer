Cytisus capitatus f. neilreichii

Cytisus capitatus f. neilreichii, commonly known as the Neilreich's broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. It is typically found in coastal and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

This subspecies is notable for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens.
