Centaurea uniflora

Centaurea uniflora, commonly known as the singleflower centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to purple flowers with a yellow center. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or open habitats.

Centaurea uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
