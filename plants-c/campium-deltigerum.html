Campium deltigerum

Campium deltigerum, commonly known as the delta spurge.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
