Cymbidium sinokanran

Cymbidium sinokanran, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in winter or early spring. The plant produces elliptical or ovate leaves that are usually dark green and leathery in texture. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns.

Cymbidium sinokanran is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
