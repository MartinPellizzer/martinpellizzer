Cycas nitida

Cycas nitida, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a species of cycad native to southern China and northern Vietnam.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, cone-like structures known as strobili, which contain the reproductive organs. Cycas nitida is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water.

It is also noted for its ability to tolerate a variety of environmental conditions, making it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
