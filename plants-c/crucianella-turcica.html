Crucianella turcica

Crucianella turcica, commonly known as the Turkish crucianella, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Primulaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
