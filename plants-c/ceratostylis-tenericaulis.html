Ceratostylis tenericaulis

Ceratostylis tenericaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces a single, elongated inflorescence bearing small, greenish-white flowers with prominent, horn-like sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
