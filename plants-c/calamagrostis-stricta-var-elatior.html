Calamagrostis stricta var. elatior

Calamagrostis stricta var. elatior, commonly known as tall blue grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It features narrow, blue-green blades that are stiff and upright, growing in dense clumps. The plant produces feathery, silver-blue plumes in late summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is prized for its ornamental appeal and ability to form attractive, low-maintenance landscapes.
