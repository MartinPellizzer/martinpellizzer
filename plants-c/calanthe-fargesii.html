Calanthe fargesii

Calanthe fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' calanthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces striking, lance-shaped leaves and bears showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe fargesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a subject of interest in botanical research due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
