Cyphia corylifolia f. singuliflora

Cyphia corylifolia f. singuliflora, commonly known as the single-flowered Chinese holly.

It is a rare variant of the Chinese holly, characterized by its singular leaflet at the tip of each branch. The plant is native to southern China and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are deeply lobed and resemble those of a holly, with a glossy green surface.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
