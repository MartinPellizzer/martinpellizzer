Clusia mituana

Clusia mituana, commonly known as the Mexican beauty leaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Clusia mituana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
