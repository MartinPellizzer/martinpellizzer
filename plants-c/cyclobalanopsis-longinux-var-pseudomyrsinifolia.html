Cyclobalanopsis longinux var. pseudomyrsinifolia

Cyclobalanopsis longinux var. pseudomyrsinifolia, commonly known as the Chinese ironwood, is a member of the Fagaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a large evergreen tree that can reach heights of up to 30 meters, characterized by its dense, hard wood and broad, elliptical leaves. This species is valued for its ornamental and timber qualities, making it a significant plant in both ecological and economic contexts.
