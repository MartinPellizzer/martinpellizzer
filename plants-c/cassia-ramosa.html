Cassia ramosa

Cassia ramosa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, branched inflorescences that produce numerous yellow, fragrant flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Cassia ramosa is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
