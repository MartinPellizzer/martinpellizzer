Calamus dachangensis

Calamus dachangensis, commonly known as the Dachang reed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
