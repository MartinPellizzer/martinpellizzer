Cartrema minor

Cartrema minor, commonly known as the lesser cartrema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color, and appear in clusters.

Cartrema minor is known for its subtle appearance and is often overlooked in the wild despite its ecological significance.
