Cyathocalyx obtusifolius var. angustifolius

Cyathocalyx obtusifolius var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed thornapple, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically narrow and have a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are follicles that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
