Crassocephalum Montuosum

Crassocephalum montuosum is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, daisy-like flowers and unique leaf structure. Native to regions of Africa and parts of Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its scientific name reflects its distinctive head-like appearance, with a dense cluster of small, showy blooms. This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its eye-catching visuals and relatively easy care requirements. It also holds cultural significance in some regions where it is used in traditional medicine and floral arrangements.
