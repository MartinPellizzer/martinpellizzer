Cephalaria stellipilis

Cephalaria stellipilis, commonly known as the star-hair cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
