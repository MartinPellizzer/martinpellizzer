Cattleya × laelioides

Cattleya × laelioides, commonly known as the Laelioid Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from various Cattleya species.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows as an epiphyte on trees or as a terrestrial plant in shaded environments. The plant produces large, showy flowers in a range of colors, including white, pink, red, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in seasonal climates.

Cattleya × laelioides is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
