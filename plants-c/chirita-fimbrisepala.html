Chirita fimbrisepala

Chirita fimbrisepala, commonly known as the fringed-leaved chirita, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves with fringed edges. Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and unique foliage structure.
