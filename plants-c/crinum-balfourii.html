Crinum balfourii

Crinum balfourii, commonly known as the Balfour's crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that form a dense rosette around the bulb. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, spicy fragrance.

Crinum balfourii is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
