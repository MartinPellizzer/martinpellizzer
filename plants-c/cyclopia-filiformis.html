Cyclopia filiformis

Cyclopia filiformis, commonly known as the slender cyclopia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Cyclopia filiformis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
