Cirsium × milliati

Cirsium × milliati, commonly known as the everlasting plume thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, showy purple or pink flower heads that resemble plumes. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America. Its flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets, typical of the daisy family.

Cirsium × milliati is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in gardens and floral arrangements.
