Cynanchica deficiens

Cynanchica deficiens, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds, typical of the legume family. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health benefits.
