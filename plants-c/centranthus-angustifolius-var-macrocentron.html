Centranthus angustifolius var. macrocentron

Centranthus angustifolius var. macrocentron, commonly known as the narrow-leaved redcentrant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of tubular, red to pink flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, typically arranged oppositely along the stem. This species is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Its ornamental value is due to its vibrant floral display and compact growth habit.
