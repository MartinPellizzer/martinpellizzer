Chirita liguliformis

Chirita liguliformis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed chirita, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
