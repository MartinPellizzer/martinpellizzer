Combretum stocksii

Combretum stocksii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often giving the plant a fiery appearance. The leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
