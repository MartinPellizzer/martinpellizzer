Chamaedorea pittieri

Chamaedorea pittieri, commonly known as the Pittier's bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, arching leaves that give it a graceful, palm-like appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 feet and thrives in bright, indirect light. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and moderate humidity.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor decorating and landscaping.
