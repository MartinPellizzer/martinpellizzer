Christophoriana rubra

Christophoriana rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, red or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
