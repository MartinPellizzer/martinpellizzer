Corrigiola telephiifolia var. annua

Corrigiola telephiifolia var. annua, commonly known as the annual sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as a ornamental plant.
