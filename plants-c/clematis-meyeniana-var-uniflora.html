Clematis meyeniana var. uniflora

Clematis meyeniana var. uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to South America.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant blooms in late spring to early summer, with each inflorescence containing a single, large flower. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in garden landscapes.
