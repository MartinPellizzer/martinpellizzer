Coleus eungellaensis

Coleus eungellaensis, commonly known as the Eungella coleus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, purple, and green. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in whorled clusters.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
