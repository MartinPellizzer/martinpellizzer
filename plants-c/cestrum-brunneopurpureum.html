Cestrum brunneopurpureum

Cestrum brunneopurpureum, commonly known as the purple-brown cestrum, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
