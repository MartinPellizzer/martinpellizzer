Chrysanthemum jugorum

Chrysanthemum jugorum, commonly known as the Japanese chrysanthemum, is a species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bushy growth habit and an array of flower colors, ranging from white to deep red. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow central disks and white or colored ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Chrysanthemum jugorum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a cut flower.
