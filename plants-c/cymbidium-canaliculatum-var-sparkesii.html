Cymbidium canaliculatum var. sparkesii

Cymbidium canaliculatum var. sparkesii, commonly known as the Sparkes' crested orchid, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. The plant features prominent crested or wavy patterns on its petals, which give it its distinctive appearance. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This orchid is known for its relatively long blooming period, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
