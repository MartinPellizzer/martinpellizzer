Calathea widgrenii

Calathea widgrenii, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of deep green, silver, and red. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture that enhances its ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its unique leaf patterns and elegant appearance make it a sought-after addition to any plant collection.
