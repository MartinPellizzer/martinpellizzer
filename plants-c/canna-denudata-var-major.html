Canna denudata var. major

Canna denudata var. major, commonly known as the giant canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful, sword-shaped leaves that can reach lengths of up to 1 meter. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by vibrant, often red or orange, bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in ornamental gardening for its dramatic foliage and long-lasting blooms.
