Cynanchum laeve

Cynanchum laeve, commonly known as the smooth milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes other milkweed species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are typically pale green to yellowish in color.

Cynanchum laeve produces a milky sap that can be toxic to livestock and humans.
