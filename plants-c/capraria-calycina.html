Capraria calycina

Capraria calycina, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with contrasting yellow markings. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Capraria calycina thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
