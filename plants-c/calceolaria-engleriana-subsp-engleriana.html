Calceolaria engleriana subsp. engleriana

Calceolaria engleriana subsp.

engleriana, commonly known as the Engler's slipper orchid, is a member of the Campanulaceae family. It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands and rocky slopes.
