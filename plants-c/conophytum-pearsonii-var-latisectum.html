Conophytum pearsonii var. latisectum

Conophytum pearsonii var. latisectum, commonly known as the Broad-leafed Stone Plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit and fleshy, ribbed leaves that are broader than those of other conophytum species. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
