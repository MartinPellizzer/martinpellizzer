Cleomella plocasperma

Cleomella plocasperma, commonly known as the slender cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Cleomella plocasperma is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
