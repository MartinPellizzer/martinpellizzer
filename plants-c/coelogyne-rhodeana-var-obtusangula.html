Coelogyne rhodeana var. obtusangula

Coelogyne rhodeana var. obtusangula, commonly known as the obtuse-angled coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with elongated, white petals. The flowers typically bloom in clusters and are known for their elegant, orchid-like appearance. This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

It prefers warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.
