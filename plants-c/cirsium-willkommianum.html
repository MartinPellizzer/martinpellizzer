Cirsium willkommianum

Cirsium willkommianum, commonly known as the Willkomm's thistle, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial species characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is part of the Asteraceae family. Its robust stem and dense foliage help it survive in arid environments.

Cirsium willkommianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
