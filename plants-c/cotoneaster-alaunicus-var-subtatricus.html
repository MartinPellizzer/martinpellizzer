Cotoneaster alaunicus var. subtatricus

Cotoneaster alaunicus var. subtatricus, commonly known as the Transylvanian cotoneaster, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a decorative shrub.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
