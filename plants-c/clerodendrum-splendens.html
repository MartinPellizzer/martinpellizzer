Clerodendrum splendens

Clerodendrum splendens, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow or green throat. The plant produces small, green, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.
