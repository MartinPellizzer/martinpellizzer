Cattleya eldorado var. splendens

Cattleya eldorado var. splendens, commonly known as the Eldorado Splendens orchid, is a hybrid orchid celebrated for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with large, colorful blooms that often display a combination of red, orange, and yellow hues. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
