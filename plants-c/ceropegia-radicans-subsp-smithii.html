Ceropegia radicans subsp. smithii

Ceropegia radicans subsp.

smithii, commonly known as the Smith's string of hearts, is a climbing plant native to South Africa. It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique foliage and ease of care.
