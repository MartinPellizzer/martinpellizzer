Campanula stenosiphon

Campanula stenosiphon, commonly known as narrow-lipped bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically blue to violet in color. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
