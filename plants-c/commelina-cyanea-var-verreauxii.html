Commelina cyanea var. verreauxii

Commelina cyanea var. verreauxii, commonly known as the blue spider-orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue-violet flowers, which are clustered in a distinctive spiral pattern around a central spadix. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understoreys.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists.
