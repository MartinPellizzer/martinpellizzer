Carex sichouensis

Carex sichouensis, commonly known as the Sichuan sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan Province. The plant features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and cylindrical, with a dense rosette growth habit. Its inflorescence consists of a spike of small, greenish-yellow flowers, which are wind-pollinated.

Carex sichouensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wet meadows.
