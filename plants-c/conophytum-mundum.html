Conophytum mundum

Conophytum mundum, commonly known as the "Pebble Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance. The plant features fleshy, rounded leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months.

Conophytum mundum is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
