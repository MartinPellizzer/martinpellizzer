Cordylanthus rigidus var. brevibracteatus

Cordylanthus rigidus var. brevibracteatus, commonly known as the short-bracted woollypod, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It produces small, pale yellow to green flowers, and its seeds are enclosed in rigid, bracteate pods.
