Cryptocarya perrieri

Cryptocarya perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's cryptocarya, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to New Caledonia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, fleshy berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature.

Cryptocarya perrieri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
