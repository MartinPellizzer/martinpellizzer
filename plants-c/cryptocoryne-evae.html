Cryptocoryne evae

Cryptocoryne evae, commonly known as Eave's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that are often speckled with small, white spots. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. Its rhizome system allows it to spread and form dense underwater carpets.

Cryptocoryne evae is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
