Chamaeleon comosus

Chamaeleon comosus, commonly known as the cosmos plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and has slender, branching stems. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers are composed of a central disk surrounded by ray florets, which contribute to its ornamental appeal.
