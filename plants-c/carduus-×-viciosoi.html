Carduus × viciosoi

Carduus × viciosoi, commonly known as the bastard thistle, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Carduus nutans and Carduus acanthoides.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to reddish purple flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides. It has a robust root system and can form dense stands, which may compete with native vegetation.

Its botanical features make it both a weed and a potential ornamental plant in certain regions.
