Cattleya guttata

Cattleya guttata, commonly known as the spotted cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with distinctive dark spots on the petals. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cattleya guttata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
