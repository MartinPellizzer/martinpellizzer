Camomilla inodora

Camomilla inodora, commonly known as sweet violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture and a distinct, sweet scent. Camomilla inodora thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its calming and anti-inflammatory properties.
