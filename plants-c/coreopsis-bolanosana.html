Coreopsis bolanosana

Coreopsis bolanosana, commonly known as the Bolanosana coreopsis, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disc of tubular florets, typical of the genus Coreopsis. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its blooming period typically occurs in the spring and early summer, adding vibrant color to the environment.
