Calamagrostis deschampsioides var. hayachinensis

Calamagrostis deschampsioides var. hayachinensis, commonly known as the Japanese silver grass, is a grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and elegant, feathery plumes that appear in late summer. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The plant is relatively low maintenance and can be used as a border plant or in mass plantings.
