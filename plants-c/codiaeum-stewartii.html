Codiaeum stewartii

Codiaeum stewartii, commonly known as the flame leaf or stewart's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Croton family (Euphorbiaceae) and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Codiaeum stewartii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
