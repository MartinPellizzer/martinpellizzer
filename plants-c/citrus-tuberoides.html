Citrus tuberoides

Citrus tuberoides, commonly known as the sour orange.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its fragrant, glossy leaves and small, yellow-orange fruits. The plant is closely related to other citrus species and is known for its high acidity and aromatic properties. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow and are produced in clusters.

Citrus tuberoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of essential oils and culinary use.
