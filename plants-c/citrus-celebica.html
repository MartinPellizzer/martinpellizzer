Citrus celebica

Citrus celebica, commonly known as the Java lemon or Malabar lemon, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It produces small, round, yellow-orange fruits that are highly aromatic and have a tangy, acidic flavor. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit. Its flowers are fragrant and white, typically appearing in clusters.

The fruit is used in both culinary and traditional medicinal applications in its native regions.
