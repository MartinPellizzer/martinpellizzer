Cirrhopetalum stragularium

Cirrhopetalum stragularium, commonly known as the silky petaled orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. Its flowers are often pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate petal structures.

Cirrhopetalum stragularium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
