Chamaedorea rosibeliae

Chamaedorea rosibeliae, commonly known as the Rosibelia bamboo, is a member of the Aroid family and is native to Mexico.

It is a compact, evergreen plant with slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces striking, white spadices that emerge from the leaf axils, adding visual interest to its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture without becoming waterlogged.

Due to its elegant appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
