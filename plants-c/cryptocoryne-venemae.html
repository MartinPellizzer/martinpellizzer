Cryptocoryne venemae

Cryptocoryne venemae, commonly known as the venomous cryptocoryne, is a unique aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. Its rhizome system allows it to anchor itself firmly in the substrate while spreading across the pond floor.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging plant for aquarium enthusiasts.
