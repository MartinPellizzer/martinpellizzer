Carduus × aragonensis

Carduus × aragonensis, commonly known as the Aragon thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, this species thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.

Its structure and growth habits make it an important component of local ecosystems.
