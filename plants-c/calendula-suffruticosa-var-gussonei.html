Calendula suffruticosa var. gussonei

Calendula suffruticosa var. gussonei, commonly known as the Gussonei dwarf marigold, is a compact, shrub-like perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has hairy, aromatic leaves and stems, which contribute to its distinct appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in herbal medicine.
