Cacalia × koidzumiana

Cacalia × koidzumiana, commonly known as the hybrid bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Cacalia × koidzumiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
