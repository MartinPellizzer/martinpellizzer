Capsicum grossum var. ovatum

Capsicum grossum var. ovatum, commonly known as the large-fruited pepper or ovate bell pepper, is a variety of the Capsicum genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, ovate-shaped fruits that can grow up to 15 centimeters in length. The plant typically has broad, dark green leaves and produces flowers that are yellow or white in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible peppers.
