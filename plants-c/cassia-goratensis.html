Cassia goratensis

Cassia goratensis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain numerous seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. Cassia goratensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
