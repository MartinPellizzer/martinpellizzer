Clethra peruviana

Clethra peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian clethra, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Clethraceae and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Clethra peruviana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
