Chamaegastrodia nanlingensis

Chamaegastrodia nanlingensis, commonly known as the Nanling mountain orchid, is a rare and endemic species found primarily in the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure. The plant typically features small, dark green leaves and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
