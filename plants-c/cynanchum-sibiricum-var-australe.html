Cynanchum sibiricum var. australe

Cynanchum sibiricum var. australe, commonly known as the Siberian milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes.

Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
