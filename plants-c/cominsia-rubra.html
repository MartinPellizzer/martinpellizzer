Cominsia rubra

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
