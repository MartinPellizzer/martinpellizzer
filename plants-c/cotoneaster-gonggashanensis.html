Cotoneaster gonggashanensis

Cotoneaster gonggashanensis, commonly known as Gonggashan cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Gonggashan region of Hubei Province. The plant is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, red berries that are typically consumed by birds. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a glossy surface and a serrated margin, while its flowers are usually white or pink and appear in clusters.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and potential ecological importance in its native habitat.
