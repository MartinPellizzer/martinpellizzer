Carex pallescens f. brevibracteata

Carex pallescens f. brevibracteata, commonly known as the short-bract sedge, is a variant of the pale sedge species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its grass-like appearance and cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

This variety is often found in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and wet meadows.
