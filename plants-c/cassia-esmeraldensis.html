Cassia esmeraldensis

Cassia esmeraldensis, commonly known as Emerald Cassia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright green, feathery foliage and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces long, slender pods containing small, dark brown seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cassia esmeraldensis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and adaptability.
