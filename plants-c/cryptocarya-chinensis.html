Cryptocarya chinensis

Cryptocarya chinensis, commonly known as Chinese cryptocarya, is a flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to southern China, where it grows in mountainous regions and forested areas. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are elliptical and have a leathery texture. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a small, dark brown drupe that contains a single seed.
