Culcasia tenuifolia

Culcasia tenuifolia, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant thrives in shallow water and is often used in ornamental ponds and water gardens. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize wetlands and marshy areas.

Culcasia tenuifolia is also valued for its ability to purify water and provide habitat for aquatic life.
