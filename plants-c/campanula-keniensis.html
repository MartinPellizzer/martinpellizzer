Campanula keniensis

Campanula keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Kenya.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Campanula keniensis is often found in alpine or montane regions, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
