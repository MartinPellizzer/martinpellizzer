Chionogentias patula

Chionogentias patula, commonly known as the snow gentian, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with a fine, white down.

Chionogentias patula is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is often used in wildflower displays.
