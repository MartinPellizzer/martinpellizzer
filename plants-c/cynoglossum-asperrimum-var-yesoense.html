Cynoglossum asperrimum var. yesoense

Cynoglossum asperrimum var. yesoense, commonly known as the Yeso bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces violet to blue flowers in racemes, which bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to horticultural collections.
