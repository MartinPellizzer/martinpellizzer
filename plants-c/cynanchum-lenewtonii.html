Cynanchum lenewtonii

Cynanchum lenewtonii, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed or the milkweed vine, is a climbing plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweed species. The plant is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellowish-green with a distinctive horn-like structure at the base.

Cynanchum lenewtonii contains milky sap, which can be toxic and is used in traditional medicine.
