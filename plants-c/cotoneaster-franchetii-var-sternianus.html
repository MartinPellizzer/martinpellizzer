Cotoneaster franchetii var. sternianus

Cotoneaster franchetii var. sternianus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically dark green and slightly pubescent. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant fall foliage and persistent berries.
