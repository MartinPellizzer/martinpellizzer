Carphalea sambiranensis

Carphalea sambiranensis, commonly known as the Madagascar ironwood, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy, tubular flowers. Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
