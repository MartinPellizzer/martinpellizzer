Centaurea calalepis

Centaurea calalepis, commonly known as the Calabrian centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.
