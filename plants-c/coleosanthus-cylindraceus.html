Coleosanthus cylindraceus

Coleosanthus cylindraceus, commonly known as the cylinder sea holly, is a marine flowering plant found in shallow coastal waters.

It has distinctive cylindrical, fleshy leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh tidal conditions. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its root system helps stabilize sandy or muddy seabeds.

It plays an important role in coastal ecosystems by providing habitat for various marine organisms.
