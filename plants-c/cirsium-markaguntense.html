Cirsium markaguntense

Cirsium markaguntense, commonly known as Markagunt thistle, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as cliffs and talus slopes. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
