Cereus gladiator

Cereus gladiator, commonly known as the gladiator cactus, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a columnar cactus with a straight, unbranched stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, white, night-blooming flowers that are often fragrant. It has a spiny exterior with areoles that bear both spines and flowers.

Cereus gladiator is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
