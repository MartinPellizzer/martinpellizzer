Crocodilodes eriobasis

Crocodilodes eriobasis, commonly known as the common milkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is a member of the milkweed family (Asclepiadaceae) and is known for its milky sap, which is toxic to many animals.

This plant plays a vital role in supporting the life cycle of the monarch butterfly.
