Costus uniflorus

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and tall, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its most distinctive characteristic is its single, showy flower that blooms at the tip of a leaf-like bract.
