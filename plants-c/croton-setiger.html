Croton setiger

Croton setiger, commonly known as the hairy croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy textures. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

Croton setiger thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
