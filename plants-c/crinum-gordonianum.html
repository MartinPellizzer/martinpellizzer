Crinum gordonianum

Crinum gordonianum, commonly known as the Gordon's Crinum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, large, white to pale pink flowers. Crinum gordonianum is typically found in woodland areas and can thrive in well-drained soil with partial shade.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
