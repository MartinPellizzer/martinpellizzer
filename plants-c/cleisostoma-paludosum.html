Cleisostoma paludosum

Cleisostoma paludosum, commonly known as the swamp cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats. Its leaves are usually broad and ovate, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

Cleisostoma paludosum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
