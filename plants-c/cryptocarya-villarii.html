Cryptocarya villarii

Cryptocarya villarii, commonly known as the New Zealand myrtle, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a small, dark berry that contains several seeds.

Cryptocarya villarii is often found in coastal and lowland forests, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
