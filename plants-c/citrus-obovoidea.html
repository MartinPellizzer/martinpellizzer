Citrus obovoidea

Citrus obovoidea, commonly known as the narrow-ovate citrus.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its fragrant, narrow-ovate leaves and small, yellow-orange fruits. The plant produces citrus fruits that are typically used for their aromatic zest and juice, though they are not commonly consumed as table fruit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.

The botanical features of Citrus obovoidea make it a valuable species in horticulture and perfumery.
