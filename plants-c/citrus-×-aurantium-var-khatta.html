Citrus × aurantium var. khatta

Citrus × aurantium var. khatta, commonly known as the sweet orange.

It is a hybrid citrus plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its thick, segmented, and juicy orange fruits. The plant typically grows as a small evergreen tree or shrub with glossy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are fragrant and white, often leading to the production of high-quality fruit.

The fruit is valued for its sweet flavor and is widely used in culinary and medicinal applications.
