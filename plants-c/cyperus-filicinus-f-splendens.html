Cyperus filicinus f. splendens

Cyperus filicinus f. splendens, commonly known as the splendour flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale yellow or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in shallow water or damp soil. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in parts of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

Its botanical features make it a useful indicator species for wetland ecosystems.
