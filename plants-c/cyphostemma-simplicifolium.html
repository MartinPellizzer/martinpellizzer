Cyphostemma simplicifolium

Cyphostemma simplicifolium, commonly known as the African moon vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features slender, twining stems and palmate leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers that are followed by yellow, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and green spaces.
