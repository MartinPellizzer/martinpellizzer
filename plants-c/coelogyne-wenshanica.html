Coelogyne wenshanica

Coelogyne wenshanica, commonly known as the Wenshan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China, particularly in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne wenshanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
