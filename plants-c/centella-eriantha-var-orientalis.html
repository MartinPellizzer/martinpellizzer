Centella eriantha var. orientalis

Centella eriantha var. orientalis, commonly known as Asiatic pennywort, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of creeping stems. The leaves are heart-shaped and often covered with fine, white hairs, giving them a velvety texture. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
