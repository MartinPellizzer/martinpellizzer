Cynoglossum elongatum

Cynoglossum elongatum, commonly known as longleaf forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces clusters of small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Cynoglossum elongatum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can be found in woodland gardens or along stream banks.
