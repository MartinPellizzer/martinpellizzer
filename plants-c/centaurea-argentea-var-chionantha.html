Centaurea argentea var. chionantha

Centaurea argentea var. chionantha, commonly known as silver centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-white foliage and striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has a compact growth habit with upright stems and is often used in ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
