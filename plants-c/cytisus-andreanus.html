Cytisus andreanus

Cytisus andreanus, commonly known as the Andree's broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in mild climates.
