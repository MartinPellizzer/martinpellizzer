Croton aemulus

Croton aemulus, commonly known as the red croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep red to orange and yellow hues. The leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
