Coelorachis capensis

Coelorachis capensis, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Coelorachis capensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
