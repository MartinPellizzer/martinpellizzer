Caladium bicolor var. pellucidum

Caladium bicolor var. pellucidum, commonly known as the "White Knight," is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are predominantly white with deep green veins. The leaves are usually variegated, featuring a translucent or opalescent appearance that gives it a unique aesthetic. This variety thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.
