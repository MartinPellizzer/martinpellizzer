Crepis × garnieri

Crepis × garnieri, commonly known as the garden mouse-ear, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and dense clusters of small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features hairy stems and leaves that are heart-shaped or rounded. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in gardens and disturbed habitats.

Crepis × garnieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
