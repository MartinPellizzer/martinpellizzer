Cynanchum pycnoneuroides

Cynanchum pycnoneuroides, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruits are follicles that release seeds with silky hairs.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
