Cassia eitenorum

Cassia eitenorum, commonly known as the Eiten's cassia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Cassia eitenorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
