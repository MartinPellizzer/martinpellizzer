Critonia stigmatica

Critonia stigmatica, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spotted leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and botanical collections.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in many regions.
