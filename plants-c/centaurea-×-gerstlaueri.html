Centaurea × gerstlaueri

Centaurea × gerstlaueri, commonly known as the German thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and vibrant purple to red flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions as an ornamental or weed.

Due to its hardy nature, it can spread aggressively in disturbed soils.
