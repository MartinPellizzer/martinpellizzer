Cleisostoma brevilabium

Cleisostoma brevilabium, commonly known as the narrow-lipped cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-shaped leaves that are often dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cleisostoma brevilabium is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
