Coptosperma graveolens var. impolitum

Coptosperma graveolens var. impolitum, commonly known as the rough-leaved sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal or inland areas. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are usually rough and hairy, giving it the common name.
