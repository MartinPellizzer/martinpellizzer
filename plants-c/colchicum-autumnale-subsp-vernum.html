Colchicum autumnale subsp. vernum

Colchicum autumnale subsp.

vernum, commonly known as the spring colchicum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Colchicaceae. It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant features a bulbous corm, which serves as a storage organ for nutrients. Its leaves are lance-shaped and appear after the flowers, typically in late spring.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in woodland habitats.
