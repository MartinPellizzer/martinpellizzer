Cynara cardunculus subsp. scolymus

Cynara cardunculus subsp.

scolymus, commonly known as globe thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its spherical, spiky blue or purple flower heads, which are surrounded by a layer of bracts.

The plant has a robust root system and can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, with gray-green, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
