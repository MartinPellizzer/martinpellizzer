Cyclosorus amboinensis

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and are clustered in groups.

Cyclosorus amboinensis is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia.
