Crassula pharnaceoides subsp. eupharnaceoides

Crassula pharnaceoides subsp.

eupharnaceoides, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in winter, giving it its common name.

The plant has fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
