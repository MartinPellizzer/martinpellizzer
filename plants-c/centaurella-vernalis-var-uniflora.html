Centaurella vernalis var. uniflora

Centaurella vernalis var. uniflora, commonly known as the spring centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers with a distinctive blue or purple central disk. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.

Its botanical features make it a characteristic species of Mediterranean flora.
