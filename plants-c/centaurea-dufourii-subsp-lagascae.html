Centaurea dufourii subsp. lagascae

Centaurea dufourii subsp.

lagascae, commonly known as the Lagasca Centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright, branching stems that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces striking blue to purple flower heads, which are surrounded by pale yellow to white bracts. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture.

This subspecies is well adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and scrublands.
