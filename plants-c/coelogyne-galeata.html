Coelogyne galeata

Coelogyne galeata, commonly known as the yellow lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking yellow and green striped petals. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their fragrant scent.

Coelogyne galeata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
