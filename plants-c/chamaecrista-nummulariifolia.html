Chamaecrista nummulariifolia

Chamaecrista nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver dollar bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, round, silver-gray leaves that resemble coins, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and drought tolerance.

Chamaecrista nummulariifolia is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its purported health benefits.
