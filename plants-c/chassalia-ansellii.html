Chassalia ansellii

Chassalia ansellii, commonly known as the Ansell's chassalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It produces small, linear leaves and has a compact, bushy growth habit.

Chassalia ansellii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
