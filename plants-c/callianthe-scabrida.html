Callianthe scabrida

Callianthe scabrida, commonly known as the roughleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a head. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
