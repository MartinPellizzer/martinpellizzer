Caladenia fuscolutescens

Caladenia fuscolutescens, commonly known as the dark-veined spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a single, slender stem with a single, narrow, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, with a distinctive spider-like appearance, and are usually pale yellow with dark brown markings.

This species is known for its delicate beauty and is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
