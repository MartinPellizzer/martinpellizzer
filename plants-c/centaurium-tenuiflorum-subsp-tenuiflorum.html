Centaurium tenuiflorum subsp. tenuiflorum

Centaurium tenuiflorum subsp.

tenuiflorum, commonly known as slenderflower centaury, is a small herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in grassy areas, meadows, and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, clustered flowers with five petals, usually in shades of pink or purple. It blooms from late spring to early summer, and its seeds are dispersed by wind.

This subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
