Convolvulus liberalis

Convolvulus liberalis, commonly known as the slender morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pale blue or purple, blooming in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Due to its rapid growth and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
