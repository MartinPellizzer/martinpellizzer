Calochortus elegans var. nanus

Calochortus elegans var. nanus, commonly known as the dwarf fairy lantern, is a compact, perennial bulbous plant native to California.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically orange to red in color, with a distinctive lantern-like shape. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and blooms in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
