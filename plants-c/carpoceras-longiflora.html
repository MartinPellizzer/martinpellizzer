Carpoceras longiflora

Carpoceras longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, grass-like leaves and produces showy, orchid-like flowers with a distinctive elongated structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by insects.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators.
