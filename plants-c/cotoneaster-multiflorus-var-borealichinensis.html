Cotoneaster multiflorus var. borealichinensis

Cotoneaster multiflorus var. borealichinensis, commonly known as the Boreal Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is relatively cold-hardy and can thrive in USDA hardiness zones 5 through 8.
