Calathea arrecta

Calathea arrecta, commonly known as the arrow leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves with intricate patterns of green, white, and silver. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect light. Its foliage is often adorned with a waxy coating that enhances its visual appeal.

Calathea arrecta is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities.
