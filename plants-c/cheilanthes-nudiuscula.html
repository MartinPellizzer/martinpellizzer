Cheilanthes nudiuscula, commonly known as the slender rock fern, is a species of fern native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant thrives in rocky habitats, often growing on limestone or sandstone, and is well adapted to drought conditions.
