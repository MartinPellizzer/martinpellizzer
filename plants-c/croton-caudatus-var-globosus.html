Croton caudatus var. globosus

Croton caudatus var. globosus, commonly known as the globe croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features distinctive, palmate leaves with a velvety texture and bright green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
