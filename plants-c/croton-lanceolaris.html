Croton lanceolaris

Croton lanceolaris, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by distinctive, spiky capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
