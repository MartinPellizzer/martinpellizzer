Cyperus laetus subsp. oostachyus

Cyperus laetus subsp.

oostachyus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae. It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively and colonize new areas.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat for aquatic invertebrates and serving as a food source for certain bird species.
