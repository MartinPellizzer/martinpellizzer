Caladium bicolor var. perrieri

Caladium bicolor var. perrieri, commonly known as the Perrieri Caladium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Caladium bicolor var. perrieri is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas where it can flourish.
