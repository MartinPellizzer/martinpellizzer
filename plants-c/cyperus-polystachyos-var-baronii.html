Cyperus polystachyos var. baronii

Cyperus polystachyos var. baronii, commonly known as the Baron's flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of small, spike-like flower heads. It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in wetland ecosystems and a subject of study in botany and ecology.
