Cannabis indica var. kafiristanica

Cannabis indica var. kafiristanica, commonly known as Kafiristanica, is a rare and ancient cannabis variety native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and compact, bushy growth habit. The plant typically produces small, elongated buds with a high concentration of cannabinoids, particularly THC. Due to its genetic purity and historical significance, it is highly valued by cannabis enthusiasts and researchers.

However, its cultivation is restricted in many regions due to legal and conservation concerns.
