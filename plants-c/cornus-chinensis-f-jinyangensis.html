Cornus chinensis f. jinyangensis

Cornus chinensis f. jinyangensis, commonly known as the Jinyang dogwood, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cornaceae family and is characterized by its striking white bracts that resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its aesthetic appeal in horticulture. It has a compact growth habit with dense foliage, making it suitable for small garden spaces.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and conservation efforts.
