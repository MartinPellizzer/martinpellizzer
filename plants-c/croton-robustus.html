Croton robustus

Croton robustus, commonly known as the African croton, is a tropical shrub native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, thick leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.

Croton robustus is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.
