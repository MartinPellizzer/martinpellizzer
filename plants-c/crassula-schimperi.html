Crassula schimperi

Crassula schimperi, commonly known as the Schimperi's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
