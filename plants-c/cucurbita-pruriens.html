Cucurbita pruriens

Cucurbita pruriens, commonly known as the "velvet tare" or "burr gourd," is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a trailing vine that produces large, rough, spiny fruits with a thick, fibrous rind. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. Its fruit is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The leaves and stems are also utilized in various culinary and medicinal applications.
