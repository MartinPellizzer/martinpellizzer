Callista verecunda

Callista verecunda, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots anchor in the muddy substrate. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and lakes.

Callista verecunda is not only admired for its beauty but also holds cultural and spiritual significance in many Asian traditions.
