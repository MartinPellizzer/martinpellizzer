Cynodon dactylon f. major

Cynodon dactylon f. major, commonly known as elephant grass, is a robust perennial grass native to Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, stiff stalks and dense, coarse foliage, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters. The plant has a fibrous root system that allows it to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates. It is widely used for forage, erosion control, and as a source of thatching material.

Additionally, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance.
