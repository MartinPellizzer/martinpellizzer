Caryota elegans

Caryota elegans, commonly known as the elegant palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach up to three meters in length. The leaves are typically dark green with a glossy surface and a distinctive midrib. It produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered, fibrous fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, humid climates.
