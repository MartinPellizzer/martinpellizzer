Cynorkis laxiflora

Cynorkis laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered orchid, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant with a short, upright stem that bears several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a loose inflorescence, which are typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, woodland areas.

Cynorkis laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
