Commelina oligotricha

Commelina oligotricha, commonly known as the slender commelina, is a species of flowering plant in the family Commelinaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically blue or violet, with three petals and three sepals, and are often clustered in umbels.
