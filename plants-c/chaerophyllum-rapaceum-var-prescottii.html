Chaerophyllum rapaceum var. prescottii

Chaerophyllum rapaceum var. prescottii, commonly known as Prescott's hogweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along streambanks.
