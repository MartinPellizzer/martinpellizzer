Chersodoma deltoidea

Chersodoma deltoidea, commonly known as the delta leaf mallow, is a perennial herb native to the United States.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils. It is often found in prairies and open woodlands, where it contributes to the ecological diversity.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to native plant gardens.
