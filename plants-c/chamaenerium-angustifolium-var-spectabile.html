Chamaenerium angustifolium var. spectabile

Chamaenerium angustifolium var. spectabile, commonly known as the showy blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture, and are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in dry, open habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
