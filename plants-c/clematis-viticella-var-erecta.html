Clematis viticella var. erecta

Clematis viticella var. erecta, commonly known as the erect clematis, is a compact, vigorous climbing plant native to Europe.

It features small, delicate white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has strong, twining stems that can reach up to 6 feet in height with proper support. Its foliage is heart-shaped and provides a lush green backdrop to the blooms.

It is well-suited for garden trellises and is relatively easy to grow in a variety of soil types.
