Cattleya warscewiczii var. delicata-superba

Cattleya warscewiczii var. delicata-superba, commonly known as the Superb Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces large, showy blooms with a mix of white, pink, and lavender hues. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its hardiness and ability to rebloom regularly.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a parent in many hybrid orchid varieties.
