Cadia rubra

Cadia rubra, commonly known as the red cadia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. Cadia rubra is well adapted to hot, dry climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
