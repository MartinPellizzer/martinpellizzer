Cytisus Laburnum Subsp. Alpinus

Cytisus laburnum subsp. alpinus is a striking member of the legume family known for its vibrant yellow flowers. This subspecies is native to the alpine regions of Europe and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its slender stems and clustered blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It is also valued for its hardiness and ability to grow in harsh environments. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in non-native habitats.
