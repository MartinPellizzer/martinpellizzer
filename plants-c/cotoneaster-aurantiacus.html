Cotoneaster aurantiacus

Cotoneaster aurantiacus, commonly known as the orange cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, orange-red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers in the spring.

Cotoneaster aurantiacus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
