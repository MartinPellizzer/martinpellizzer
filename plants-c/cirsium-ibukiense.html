Cirsium ibukiense

Cirsium ibukiense, commonly known as the Ibuki thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
