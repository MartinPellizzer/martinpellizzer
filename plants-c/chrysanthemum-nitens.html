Chrysanthemum nitens

Chrysanthemum nitens, commonly known as the snow chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its white to pale yellow flowers and hairy leaves. This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional medicine.
