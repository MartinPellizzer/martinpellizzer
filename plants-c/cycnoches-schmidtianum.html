Cycnoches schmidtianum

Cycnoches schmidtianum, commonly known as the Schmidt's cycnoches, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features striking, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
