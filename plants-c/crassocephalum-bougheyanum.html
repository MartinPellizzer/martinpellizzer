Crassocephalum bougheyanum

Crassocephalum bougheyanum, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range from purple to pink. The leaves are usually velvety and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
