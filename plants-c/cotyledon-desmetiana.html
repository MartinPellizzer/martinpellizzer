Cotyledon desmetiana

Cotyledon desmetiana, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a ghostly appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
