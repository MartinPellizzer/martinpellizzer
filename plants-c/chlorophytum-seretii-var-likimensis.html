Chlorophytum seretii var. likimensis

Chlorophytum seretii var. likimensis, commonly known as the likimensis spider plant, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with shades of white, yellow, and green, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
