Cystorchis stenoglossa

Cystorchis stenoglossa, commonly known as the narrow-lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and the flowers are arranged in a loose raceme.

Cystorchis stenoglossa is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens.
