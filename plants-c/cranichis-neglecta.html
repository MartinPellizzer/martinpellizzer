Cranichis neglecta

Cranichis neglecta, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and along stream banks. The plant features a delicate, grass-like appearance with narrow leaves and a single, slender flower spike. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinct three-lobed lip.

Cranichis neglecta is known for its subtle beauty and is often overlooked due to its modest size and inconspicuous appearance.
