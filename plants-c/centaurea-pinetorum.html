Centaurea pinetorum

Centaurea pinetorum, commonly known as the pine mountain star-thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and grasslands. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, gray-green leaves that have a rough texture. Its flowers are blue to purple in color, arranged in dense, conical clusters, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Centaurea pinetorum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
