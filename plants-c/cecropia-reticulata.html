Cecropia reticulata

Cecropia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined cecropia, is a tropical tree belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. The plant typically grows in humid, lowland forests across Central and South America. Its bark is smooth and often has a reddish or grayish hue.
