Ceropegia delicata

Ceropegia delicata, commonly known as the silver leaf or moon vine, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and attractive, waxy leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a subtle fragrance. Ceropegia delicata thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
