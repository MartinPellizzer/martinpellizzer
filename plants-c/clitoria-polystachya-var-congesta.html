Clitoria polystachya var. congesta

Clitoria polystachya var. congesta, commonly known as the clustered winged bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are typical of the legume family. The plant produces elongated pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and nutritional benefits.

The variety 'congesta' is distinguished by its dense flowering clusters, making it a popular choice for gardens and agricultural settings.
