Coleus capitatus

Coleus capitatus, commonly known as the Cape Verde morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Coleus capitatus is typically grown as an annual in temperate climates.
