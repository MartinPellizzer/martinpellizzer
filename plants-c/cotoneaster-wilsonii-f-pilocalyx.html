Cotoneaster wilsonii f. pilocalyx

Cotoneaster wilsonii f. pilocalyx, commonly known as the Wilson's cotoneaster, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, dark green leaves and produces clusters of red berries in the autumn. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

The f. pilocalyx form is distinguished by its more compact and rounded shape compared to the typical form.
