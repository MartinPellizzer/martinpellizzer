Chresta martii

Chresta martii, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, soft pubescence.

Chresta martii thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
