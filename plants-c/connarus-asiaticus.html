Connarus asiaticus

Connarus asiaticus, commonly known as the Asian connarus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Connaraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its woody structure and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are usually white or pale yellow.

Connarus asiaticus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
