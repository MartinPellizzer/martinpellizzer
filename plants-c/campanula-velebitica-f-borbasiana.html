Campanula velebitica f. borbasiana

Campanula velebitica f. borbasiana, commonly known as the Velebit bellflower, is a rare and endemic plant species native to Croatia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its limited distribution and unique ecological niche, it is considered a conservation concern.
