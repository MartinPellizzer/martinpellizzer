Cleome aculeata var. cordobensis

Cleome aculeata var. cordobensis, commonly known as the Cordoba cleome, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for ornamental gardens.

Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance.
