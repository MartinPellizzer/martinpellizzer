Cattleya munchowiana

Cattleya munchowiana, commonly known as the Munchow's Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a wide range of colors, including white, pink, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya munchowiana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
