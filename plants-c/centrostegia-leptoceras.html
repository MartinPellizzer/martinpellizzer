Centrostegia leptoceras

Centrostegia leptoceras, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centrostegia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
