Chrysanthemum crassum

Chrysanthemum crassum, commonly known as the broadleaf chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant has sturdy, upright stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Chrysanthemum crassum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional use in horticulture.
