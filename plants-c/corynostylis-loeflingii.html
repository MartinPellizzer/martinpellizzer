Corynostylis loeflingii

Corynostylis loeflingii, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive spurred labellum. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or wetlands.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
