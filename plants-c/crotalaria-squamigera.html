Crotalaria squamigera

Crotalaria squamigera, commonly known as the velvetleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in various climates due to its adaptability.

Crotalaria squamigera is also noted for its potential use in agriculture as a green manure crop.
