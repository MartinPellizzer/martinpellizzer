Calogyne holtzeana

Calogyne holtzeana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Calogyne holtzeana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
