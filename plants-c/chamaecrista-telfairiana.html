Chamaecrista telfairiana

Chamaecrista telfairiana, commonly known as Telfair's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is typically shrub-like, with hairy stems and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and along stream banks.

The species is noted for its ecological importance, providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
