Clitoria annua var. emarginata

Clitoria annua var. emarginata, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It features distinctive butterfly-shaped leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and hairy stems, which help in identification. Its fruit is a pod containing several edible seeds, which are rich in protein.

This variety is often cultivated for its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.
