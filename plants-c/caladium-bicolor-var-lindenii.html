Caladium bicolor var. lindenii

Caladium bicolor var. lindenii, commonly known as the "Crimson Queen," is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant red, pink, and white patterns. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

It is typically grown as an annual in regions with cold winters but can be overwintered in mild climates.
