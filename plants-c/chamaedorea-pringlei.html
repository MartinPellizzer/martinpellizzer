Chamaedorea pringlei

Chamaedorea pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's bamboo palm, is a member of the Arecaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a small evergreen palm that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 3 meters. The plant features slender, clustered stems with long, narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Chamaedorea pringlei is popular as a houseplant due to its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.
