Cassia durangensis var. iselyi

Cassia durangensis var. iselyi, commonly known as the Durango cassia, is a shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
