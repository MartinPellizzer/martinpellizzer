Carissa edulis var. nummularis

Carissa edulis var. nummularis, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and culinary uses.
