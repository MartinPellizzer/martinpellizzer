Coelogyne Latifolia

Coelogyne latifolia is a striking orchid native to the forests of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, fragrant flowers and elegant appearance, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires careful attention to light and moisture. Its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of showy blooms make it a standout in any collection. With proper care, Coelogyne latifolia can bloom reliably year after year.
