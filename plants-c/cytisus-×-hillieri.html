Cytisus × hillieri

Cytisus × hillieri, commonly known as the hybrid broom, is a deciduous shrub native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces slender, woody stems.

Cytisus × hillieri is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.
