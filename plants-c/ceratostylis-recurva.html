Ceratostylis recurva

Ceratostylis recurva, commonly known as the recurved horned orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces striking, pale green to yellowish flowers with prominent, recurved sepals and petals. It typically blooms in the summer months and is found in moist, shaded environments.

Ceratostylis recurva is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
