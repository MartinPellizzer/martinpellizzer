Clitoria berteroana

Clitoria berteroana, commonly known as the velvet flower or the butterfly pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers, which are often adorned with a distinctive yellow or white stripe. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Botanically, it belongs to the Fabaceae family and is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
