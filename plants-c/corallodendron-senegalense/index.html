Corallodendron Senegalense

Corallodendron senegalense is a rare and unique plant native to the arid regions of West Africa. It is known for its distinctive coral-like appearance and ability to thrive in harsh desert conditions. This species has adapted to extreme temperatures and limited water availability through specialized root systems and water-storing tissues. Its striking appearance has made it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike. Despite its resilience, it remains a threatened species due to habitat loss and over-collection.
