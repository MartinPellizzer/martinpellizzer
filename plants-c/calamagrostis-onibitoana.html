Calamagrostis onibitoana

Calamagrostis onibitoana, commonly known as the Japanese silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-tinged foliage and graceful, arching form. The plant produces fluffy, seed-bearing heads that add a delicate texture to its appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Calamagrostis onibitoana is often used in ornamental landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
