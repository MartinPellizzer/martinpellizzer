Citrus × nobilis subf. jyuma-unshu

Citrus × nobilis subf.

jyuma-unshu, commonly known as the Yuzu or Japanese citrus, is a hybrid citrus plant native to East Asia. It is characterized by its small, yellow, aromatic fruits that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree with glossy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are fragrant and white, often blooming in spring.

The fruit develops rapidly, reaching maturity within a few months, and is prized for its unique flavor and essential oils.
