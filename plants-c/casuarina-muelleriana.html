Casuarina muelleriana

Casuarina muelleriana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved she-oak, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant has needle-like leaves arranged in spirals, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, woody cones that contain seeds, and it is often used in reforestation due to its ability to stabilize soil.

Casuarina muelleriana is also valued for its hardy nature and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
