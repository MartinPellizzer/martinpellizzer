Cucurbita pepo var. lutea

Cucurbita pepo var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow squash or summer squash, is a variant of the common gourd species.

It is characterized by its edible, yellow to orange fruits that are typically harvested when immature. The plant is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, pumpkins, and melons. It produces large, heart-shaped leaves and tendrils typical of the family.

The flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.
