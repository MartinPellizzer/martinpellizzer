Cocleorchis dressleri

Cocleorchis dressleri, commonly known as the Dressler's orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern, often in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its challenging habitat.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, Cocleorchis dressleri is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
