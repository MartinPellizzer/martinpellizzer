Coelogyne kamborangensis

Coelogyne kamborangensis, commonly known as the Kamborang orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Coelogyne kamborangensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
