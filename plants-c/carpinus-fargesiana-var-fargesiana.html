Carpinus fargesiana var. fargesiana

Carpinus fargesiana var. fargesiana, commonly known as the Chinese hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and distinctive, spiny fruit clusters. The leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and have a serrated margin. It typically grows in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas at higher elevations.
