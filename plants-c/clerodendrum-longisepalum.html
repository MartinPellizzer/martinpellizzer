Clerodendrum longisepalum

Clerodendrum longisepalum, commonly known as the long-sepal clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Clerodendrum longisepalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
