Caladium porphyroneuron

Caladium porphyroneuron, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with deep red veins contrasting against bright green or white leaves. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

Caladium porphyroneuron thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated for its decorative value in gardens and as a houseplant.
