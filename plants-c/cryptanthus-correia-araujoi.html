Cryptanthus correia-araujoi

Cryptanthus correia-araujoi, commonly known as the Brazilian star moss, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant features small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, adding to its visual appeal. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Cryptanthus correia-araujoi is often cultivated as a decorative ground cover or in containers due to its ability to tolerate partial shade and its low maintenance requirements.
