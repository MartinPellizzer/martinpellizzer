Cyperus boninensis

Cyperus boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Bonin Islands.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, commonly known as the sedge family. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, exhibiting a grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are characteristic of the Cyperaceae family.

Cyperus boninensis is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to saline or brackish environments.
