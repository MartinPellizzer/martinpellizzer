Crinum redouteanum

Crinum redouteanum, commonly known as the Redoute's crinum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which can last several months.
