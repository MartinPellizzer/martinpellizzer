Cleisostoma vacherotiana

Cleisostoma vacherotiana, commonly known as the Vacherot's cleistostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are usually elliptical and dark green, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cleisostoma vacherotiana is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
