Croton tyndaridum

Croton tyndaridum, commonly known as the yellow croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that can range from bright yellow to deep red, often with contrasting patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
