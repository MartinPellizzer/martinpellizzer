Cycnium tubulosum

Cycnium tubulosum, commonly known as the slender gourd, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, squash, and melons. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, tubular fruits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are slender and trailing.
