Clytostoma itatiaiense

Clytostoma itatiaiense, commonly known as the Itatiaia star orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are known for their intricate structure and subtle fragrance, which attracts specific pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native range.
