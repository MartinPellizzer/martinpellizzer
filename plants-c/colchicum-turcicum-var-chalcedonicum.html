Colchicum turcicum var. chalcedonicum

Colchicum turcicum var. chalcedonicum, commonly known as the Chalcedonic Colchic, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Turkey.

It features striking, showy flowers that range from white to deep purple, often with a distinctive chalcedony-like sheen. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that emerge in early spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
