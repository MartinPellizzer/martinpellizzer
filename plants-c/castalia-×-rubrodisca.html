Castalia × rubrodisca

Castalia × rubrodisca, commonly known as the red-flowered castalia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking red spathe and a white spadix, which together create a visually appealing display. The plant originates from tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid environments. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a glossy surface.

Castalia × rubrodisca is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and foliage.
