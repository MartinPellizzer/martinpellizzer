Clethra mexicana

Clethra mexicana, commonly known as Mexican sweet pepper, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form with glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
