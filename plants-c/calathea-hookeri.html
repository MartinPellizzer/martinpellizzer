Calathea hookeri

Calathea hookeri, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of green, silver, and burgundy. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture that enhances their visual appeal. This plant thrives in humid environments with indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
