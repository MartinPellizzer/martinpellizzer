Cantharospermum elongatum

Cantharospermum elongatum, commonly known as the slender senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow-orange flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a slender, elongated pod that contains several seeds.

Cantharospermum elongatum is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
