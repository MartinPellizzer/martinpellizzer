Chamaedorea variabilis

Chamaedorea variabilis, commonly known as the variable bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and feather-like, pinnate leaves that give it a graceful appearance. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ability to tolerate low light and moderate watering.

Its slow growth rate and air-purifying qualities make it a favored choice for both ornamental and environmental purposes.
