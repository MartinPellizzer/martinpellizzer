Crassula schoenlandii

Crassula schoenlandii, commonly known as the Little Leaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tinge. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
