Caladium enkeanum

Caladium enkeanum, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with deep green leaves featuring red or purple veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter.

Caladium enkeanum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and partial shade for optimal growth.
