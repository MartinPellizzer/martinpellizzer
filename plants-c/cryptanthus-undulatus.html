Cryptanthus undulatus

Cryptanthus undulatus, commonly known as the zigzag firecracker.

This plant is native to Brazil and is part of the amaryllis family, known for its striking, colorful flowers. It features fleshy, grass-like leaves that are often used in succulent arrangements. The flowers are typically red or orange, with a zigzag pattern on the petals, giving it its common name.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
