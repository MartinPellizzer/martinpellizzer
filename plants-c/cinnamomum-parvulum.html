Cinnamomum parvulum

Cinnamomum parvulum, commonly known as the small-leaf cinnamon or Chinese cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant bark and small, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elliptical, dark blue berries. It is often cultivated for its essential oils and medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both traditional medicine and the spice trade.
