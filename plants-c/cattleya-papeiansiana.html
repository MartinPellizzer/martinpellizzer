Cattleya papeiansiana

Cattleya papeiansiana, commonly known as the Papeian's orchid, is a species native to the rainforests of Colombia.

It is a tall orchid with long, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces fragrant, butterfly-shaped petals and a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cattleya papeiansiana is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
