Caladium nigrescens

Caladium nigrescens, commonly known as the black nightshade caladium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that range from deep green to almost black in color. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium nigrescens thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated for its dramatic foliage.

It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its lush leaves.
