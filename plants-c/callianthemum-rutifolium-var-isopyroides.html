Callianthemum rutifolium var. isopyroides

Callianthemum rutifolium var. isopyroides, commonly known as the Pyrenean bellflower, is a species of flowering plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It is a low-growing perennial with lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing showy inflorescences that attract pollinators. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for rock gardens and alpine collections.
