Citharexylum × jamaicense

Citharexylum × jamaicense, commonly known as Jamaican coral tree, is a hybrid shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and well-drained soils. It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, winged fruits.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and coastal landscapes.
