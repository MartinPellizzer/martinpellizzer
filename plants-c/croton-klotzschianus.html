Croton klotzschianus

Croton klotzschianus, commonly known as the Klotzsch's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often display a mix of green, yellow, and red hues. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
