Crinum brisbanicum

Crinum brisbanicum, commonly known as the Brisbane lily, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Australia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in umbels. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and slightly wavy at the edges. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near waterways.

Crinum brisbanicum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
