Cineraria crispa

Cineraria crispa, commonly known as the curly cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, crinkled, silver-gray leaves and bright blue or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
