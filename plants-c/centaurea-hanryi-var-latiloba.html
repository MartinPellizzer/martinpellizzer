Centaurea hanryi var. latiloba

Centaurea hanryi var. latiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaved centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often broader than those of the typical variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry gardens.

Its ornamental value and ability to naturalize make it a popular choice in horticulture.
