Calanthe Simulans

Calanthe simulans is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This plant thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity and well-drained soil. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a prized species among orchid enthusiasts. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this beautiful and endangered plant from habitat loss.
