Coelogyne eberhardtii

Coelogyne eberhardtii, commonly known as the Eberhardt's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of 3 to 5 on long, arching stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, often arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Coelogyne eberhardtii is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
