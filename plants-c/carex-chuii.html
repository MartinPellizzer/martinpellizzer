Carex chuii

Carex chuii, commonly known as the Chui's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and wet meadows.

Carex chuii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in water garden settings.
