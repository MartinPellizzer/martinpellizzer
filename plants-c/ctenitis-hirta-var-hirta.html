Ctenitis hirta var. hirta

Ctenitis hirta var. hirta, commonly known as the hairy sea holly, is a marine plant that thrives in intertidal zones.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy, green to red-colored leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is typically found in shallow waters along rocky shores and sandy beaches.

Its adaptations to saltwater environments make it a resilient and hardy species within its ecological niche.
