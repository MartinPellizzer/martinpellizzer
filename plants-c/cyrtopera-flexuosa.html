Cyrtopera flexuosa

Cyrtopera flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible clubmoss, is a species of lycophyte that belongs to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Cyrtopera flexuosa produces spore-bearing cones, which are clustered at the tips of its stems.

This plant plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
