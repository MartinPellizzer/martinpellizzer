Crassula forskoleana

Crassula forskoleana, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
