Cinnamomum magnificum

Cinnamomum magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent cinnamon tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown bark, which is the source of its name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by oval-shaped berries containing a single seed. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a strong, spicy scent.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
