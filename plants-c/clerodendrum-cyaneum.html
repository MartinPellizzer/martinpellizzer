Clerodendrum cyaneum

Clerodendrum cyaneum, commonly known as the blue mist shrub, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers that resemble mist, giving it its common name. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a fragrant aroma. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Clerodendrum cyaneum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
