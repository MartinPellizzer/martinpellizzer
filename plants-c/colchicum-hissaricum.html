Colchicum hissaricum

Colchicum hissaricum, commonly known as the Caucasian colchic, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from August to September. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are basal and persist through the winter. It produces capsules containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Colchicum hissaricum is adapted to grow in rocky or dry soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
