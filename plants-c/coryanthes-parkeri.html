Coryanthes parkeri

Coryanthes parkeri, commonly known as the giant flame orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length, with vibrant red and orange hues. The plant has a unique structure, featuring a spadix surrounded by a fleshy, colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of moisture and specific nutrient-rich soil.

Coryanthes parkeri is known for its complex pollination mechanism, relying on specific insects for reproduction.
