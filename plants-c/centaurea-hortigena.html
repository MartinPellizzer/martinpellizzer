Centaurea hortigena

Centaurea hortigena, commonly known as the garden centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright blue or purple flowers and bract-like leaves that form a rosette at the base. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. It produces small, narrow leaves and has a taproot system that allows it to thrive in well-drained conditions.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
