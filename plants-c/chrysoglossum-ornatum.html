Chrysoglossum ornatum

Chrysoglossum ornatum, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features silvery-white, velvety leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking foliage and compact growth habit.
