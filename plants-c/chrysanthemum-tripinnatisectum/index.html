Chrysanthemum Tripinnatisectum

Chrysanthemum tripinnatisectum is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its intricate leaf structure and vibrant blooms. This plant is native to regions of Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates and well-drained soils. Its finely divided leaves create a delicate, fern-like appearance that sets it apart from other chrysanthemum varieties. The flowers, which range from white to deep yellow, add a touch of elegance to gardens and floral arrangements. Due to its unique beauty and adaptability, it has become a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
