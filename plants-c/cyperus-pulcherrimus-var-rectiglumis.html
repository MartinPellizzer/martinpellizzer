Cyperus pulcherrimus var. rectiglumis

Cyperus pulcherrimus var. rectiglumis, commonly known as the spike rush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to wetland environments.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often with a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences, which are typically green or reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight sheen, and it thrives in moist, sunny conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland habitats.
