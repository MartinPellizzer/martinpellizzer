Callitriche nuttallii

Callitriche nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, streams, and wet meadows. The plant has delicate, floating or submerged leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. Its small, star-shaped flowers are usually white or pale pink and bloom in the spring.

Callitriche nuttallii plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and amphibians.
