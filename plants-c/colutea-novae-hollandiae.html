Colutea novae-hollandiae

Colutea novae-hollandiae, commonly known as the Australian cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Colutea novae-hollandiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
