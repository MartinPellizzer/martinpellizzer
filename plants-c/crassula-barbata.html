Crassula barbata

Crassula barbata, commonly known as the woolly crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are covered in a fine, white, woolly coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It is a hardy, low-maintenance species well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
