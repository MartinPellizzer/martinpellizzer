Croton urucurana var. draconoideus

Croton urucurana var. draconoideus, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Croton, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that often display vibrant shades of green, red, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its sap is famous for its deep red color and has been used traditionally in various medicinal applications.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
