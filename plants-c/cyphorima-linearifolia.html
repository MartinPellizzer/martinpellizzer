Cyphorima linearifolia

Cyphorima linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cyphorima, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
