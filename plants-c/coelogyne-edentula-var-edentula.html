Coelogyne edentula var. edentula

Coelogyne edentula var. edentula, commonly known as the toothless orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with erect, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive yellow or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
