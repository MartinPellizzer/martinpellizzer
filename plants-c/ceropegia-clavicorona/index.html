Ceropegia Clavicorona

String of hearts, scientifically known as Ceropegia clavicorona, is a popular houseplant cherished for its trailing vines and heart-shaped leaves. Native to South Africa, this succulent thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite for hanging baskets and shelves, adding a touch of elegance to any space. Easy to care for, it requires minimal watering and can adapt well to indoor environments. With its vibrant foliage and low maintenance needs, the String of hearts is a versatile and beautiful addition to any plant collection.
