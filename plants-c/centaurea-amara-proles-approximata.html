Centaurea amara proles approximata

Centaurea amara proles approximata, commonly known as the bitter centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, reddish stems and narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, rocky soils.
