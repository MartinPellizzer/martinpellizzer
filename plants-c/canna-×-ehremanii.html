Canna × ehremanii

Canna × ehremanii, commonly known as the red banana, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Canna indica and Canna flaccida.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. Its striking flowers are usually red or orange, with a distinctive purple or brown center.

Canna × ehremanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
