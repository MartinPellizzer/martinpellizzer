Chionolaena arbuscula var. hololeuca

Chionolaena arbuscula var. hololeuca, commonly known as the white-flowered chionolaena, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance and easy care requirements.
