Croton magneticus

Croton magneticus, commonly known as the magnetic croton, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from deep red to bright yellow and orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
