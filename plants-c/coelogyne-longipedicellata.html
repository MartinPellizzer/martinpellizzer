Coelogyne longipedicellata

Coelogyne longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-stemmed coelogyne, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender rhizomes and its ability to form dense colonies. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a reddish or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne longipedicellata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
