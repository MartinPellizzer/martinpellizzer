Clompanus rufa

Clompanus rufa, commonly known as the red-stemmed clompanus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems that display a reddish hue, especially under direct sunlight. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming during the summer months. Clompanus rufa thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a notable species in its native habitat.
