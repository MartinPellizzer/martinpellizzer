Calophyllum kiong

Calophyllum kiong, commonly known as the Kiong or Kiong tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Calophyllaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and glossy, dark green leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits containing a single seed. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is adapted to coastal and lowland environments.
