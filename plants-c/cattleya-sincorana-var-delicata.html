Cattleya sincorana var. delicata

Cattleya sincorana var. delicata, commonly known as the delicate cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive white lip. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that support its growth. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
