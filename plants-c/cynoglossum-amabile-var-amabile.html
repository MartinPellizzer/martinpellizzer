Cynoglossum amabile var. amabile

Cynoglossum amabile var. amabile, commonly known as heartleaf blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features basal rosettes of velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking clusters of violet-blue flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized areas.
