Crotalaria goreensis var. angustifolia

Crotalaria goreensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and distinctive yellow flowers with prominent black veins. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It has a erect growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

The seeds are enclosed in pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds through mechanical means.
