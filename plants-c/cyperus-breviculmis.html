Cyperus breviculmis

Cyperus breviculmis, commonly known as the short-branched flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly rough texture.

Cyperus breviculmis plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland areas and provides habitat for various small organisms.
