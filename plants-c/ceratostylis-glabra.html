Ceratostylis glabra

Ceratostylis glabra, commonly known as the smooth fringed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant has slender, erect stems that bear a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, white to pale green, and have a distinctive fringed lip.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
