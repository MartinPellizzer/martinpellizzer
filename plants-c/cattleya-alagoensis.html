Cattleya alagoensis

Cattleya alagoensis, commonly known as the Alagoas Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and often have a yellow throat. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Cattleya alagoensis is known for its fragrant blossoms and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
