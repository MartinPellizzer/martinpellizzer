Crotalaria vogelioides

Crotalaria vogelioides, commonly known as the velvetleaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a hairy, glandular surface that gives it a distinctive appearance. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
