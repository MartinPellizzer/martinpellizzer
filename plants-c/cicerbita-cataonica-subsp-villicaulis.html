Cicerbita cataonica subsp. villicaulis

Cicerbita cataonica subsp.

villicaulis, commonly known as the Caucasian bittercress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family. It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers.

The plant produces slender, elongated pods that contain several seeds. Native to the Caucasus region, it thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.
