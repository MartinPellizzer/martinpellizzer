Chlorophytum fasciculatum

Chlorophytum fasciculatum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with white stripes, and it produces small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and its ease of care, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening. It also has the ability to propagate through offsets, which are easily separated and grown into new plants.

This versatility and aesthetic appeal contribute to its widespread cultivation.
