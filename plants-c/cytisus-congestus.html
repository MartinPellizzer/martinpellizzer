Cytisus congestus

Cytisus congestus, commonly known as the clustered broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, often twisted stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various dry environments.

Cytisus congestus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
