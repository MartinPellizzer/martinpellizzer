Cirsium hasunumae

Cirsium hasunumae, commonly known as the Japanese thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
