Cryptotaenia japonica var. warabiana

Cryptotaenia japonica var. warabiana, commonly known as Japanese cow parsley, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and often covered in a fine white down. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
