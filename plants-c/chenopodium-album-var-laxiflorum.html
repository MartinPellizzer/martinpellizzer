Chenopodium album var. laxiflorum

Chenopodium album var. laxiflorum, commonly known as slender prickly goosefoot, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and Asia.

It features alternate, ovate leaves with a rough, sandpapery texture and a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by hard, triangular seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered a common weed in agricultural settings.
