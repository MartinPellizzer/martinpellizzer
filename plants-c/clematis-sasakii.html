Clematis sasakii

Clematis sasakii, commonly known as the Japanese vine, is a deciduous climbing plant native to Japan.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, often with a distinct violet or purple hue at the base. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires support for its vigorous growth.

Clematis sasakii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
