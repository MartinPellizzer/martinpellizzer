Cleistocactus bruneispinus

Cleistocactus bruneispinus, commonly known as the brown spined cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus with a ribbed stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, brown spines that provide protection from herbivores. It produces small, white flowers during the summer months, which are typically short-lived.

This species is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
