Cistus × stenophyllus

Cistus × stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes various species of evergreen shrubs characterized by their aromatic foliage and showy flowers. The plant is known for its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink and purple, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Cistus × stenophyllus is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
