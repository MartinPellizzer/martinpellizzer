Colchicum palmetorum

Colchicum palmetorum, commonly known as the palmate colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its palmately divided leaves and striking, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces distinctive, spathaceous bracts that enclose its showy inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Colchicum palmetorum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
