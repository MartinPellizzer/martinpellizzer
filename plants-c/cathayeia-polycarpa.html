Cathayeia polycarpa

Cathayeia polycarpa, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers bloom in summer and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
