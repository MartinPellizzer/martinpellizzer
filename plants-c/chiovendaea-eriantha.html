Chiovendaea eriantha

Chiovendaea eriantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, velvety-textured leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
