Chamaefilix Lobulata

Chamaefilix lobulata is a unique flowering plant native to the rocky slopes of the Mediterranean region. It is known for its compact, cushion-like growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens. Its resilience and ornamental value have made it a popular addition to botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to more commonly cultivated species.
