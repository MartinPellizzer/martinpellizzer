Calycorectes australis var. impressovenosus

Calycorectes australis var. impressovenosus, commonly known as the Southern Australian leafy spurge, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It features a robust, upright stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often have a distinctive venation pattern that gives the plant its specific epithet. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense, branched inflorescences.

This plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
