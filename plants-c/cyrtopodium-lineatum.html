Cyrtopodium lineatum

Cyrtopodium lineatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a greenish-yellow lip and a distinctive zigzag pattern on its petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cyrtopodium lineatum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
