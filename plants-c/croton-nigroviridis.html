Croton nigroviridis

Croton nigroviridis, commonly known as the black-veined croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves with dark green veins on a lighter green background. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
