Curio talinoides var. chordifolius

Curio talinoides var. chordifolius, commonly known as the String of Hearts, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It features long, trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
