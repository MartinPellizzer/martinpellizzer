Coleus Galeatus

Coleus galeatus is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, colorful foliage. It belongs to the mint family and is native to Southeast Asia. This plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular watering. Its unique leaf patterns make it a favorite for indoor and outdoor gardens.
