Centaurea Ruprechtii

Centaurea ruprechtii is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It is known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy choice for gardens. Its unique appearance and resilience have made it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. This species also plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
