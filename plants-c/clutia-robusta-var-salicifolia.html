Clutia robusta var. salicifolia

Clutia robusta var. salicifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf clutia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lanceolate, slightly serrated leaves that are often a deep green with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber in some regions.
