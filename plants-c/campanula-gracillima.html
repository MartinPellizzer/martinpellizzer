Campanula gracillima

Campanula gracillima, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

Campanula gracillima is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
