Cotyledon cymosa

Cotyledon cymosa, commonly known as the "Coral Cotyledon," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
