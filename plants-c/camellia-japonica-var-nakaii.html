Camellia japonica var. nakaii

Camellia japonica var. nakaii, commonly known as the Nakai camellia, is a species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
