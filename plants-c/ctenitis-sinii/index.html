Ctenitis Sinii

Sinii ctenitis, scientifically known as Ctenitis sinii, is a rare and intriguing fern species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This plant belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its unique fronds and delicate structure. Despite its modest appearance, Ctenitis sinii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat. Its conservation status is of growing concern due to habitat loss and over-collection.
