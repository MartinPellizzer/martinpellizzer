Clusia haughtii

Clusia haughtii, commonly known as the waxy leaf plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and have a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. Clusia haughtii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience to drought.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
