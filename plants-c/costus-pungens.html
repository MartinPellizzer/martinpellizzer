Costus pungens

Costus pungens, commonly known as the spiked costus, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features tall, erect stems with narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often reddish at the base. Its striking inflorescence consists of bright yellow bracts with red-tipped flowers, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Costus pungens thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
