Carex spatiosa var. bogorensis

Carex spatiosa var. bogorensis, commonly known as the Bogorensis sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and dark green, with a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of inconspicuous flowers, which are pollinated by wind. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in wetland habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing wetland environments.
