Cypripedium kentuckiense f. summersii

Cypripedium kentuckiense f. summersii, commonly known as the Summer's Evening Lady, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, pale pink to white flowers with deep red markings, which resemble the evening sky. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands, preferring rich, well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, while its rhizome is thick and fleshy.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
