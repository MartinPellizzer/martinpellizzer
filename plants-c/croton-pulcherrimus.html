Croton pulcherrimus

Croton pulcherrimus, commonly known as the red croton, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, with leaves that range from bright yellow to deep red and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
