Crassula sladenii

Crassula sladenii, commonly known as the Sladen's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
