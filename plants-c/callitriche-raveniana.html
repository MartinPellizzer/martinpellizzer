Callitriche raveniana

Callitriche raveniana, commonly known as the Raven's wing water-starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has delicate, floating leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern around its stems. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Callitriche raveniana plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small invertebrates and stabilizing water surfaces.
