Calathea divaricata

Calathea divaricata, commonly known as the variegated calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. The leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
