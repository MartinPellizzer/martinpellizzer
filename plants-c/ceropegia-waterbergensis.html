Ceropegia waterbergensis

Ceropegia waterbergensis, commonly known as the "string of hearts," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia waterbergensis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
