Cyclopia vogelii var. brachypoda

Cyclopia vogelii var. brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stemmed bushveld cyclopia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in shrubland or grassland habitats. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.
