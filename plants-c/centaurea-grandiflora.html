Centaurea grandiflora

Centaurea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and striking blue to purple flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant produces spiky, bristly bracts surrounding its composite flowers, which are composed of numerous small florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Centaurea grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
