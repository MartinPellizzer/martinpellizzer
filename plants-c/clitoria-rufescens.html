Clitoria rufescens

Clitoria rufescens, commonly known as the winged bean or butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, butterfly-like blue or purple flowers and compound leaves with wing-like structures. The plant produces edible pods that contain seeds and can be used in traditional cuisines. It is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.

Clitoria rufescens is a legume, capable of fixing nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
