Clematis hefengensis

Clematis hefengensis, commonly known as the Hefeng clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, fluffy fruits that mature in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for its attractive blooms and foliage.
