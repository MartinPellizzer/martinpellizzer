Cheirostylis oligantha

Cheirostylis oligantha, commonly known as the slender-styled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a slender, upright stem and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
