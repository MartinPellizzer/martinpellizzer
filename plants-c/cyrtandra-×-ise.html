Cyrtandra × ise

Cyrtandra × ise, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra and another orchid species.

It is characterized by its unique floral morphology, often exhibiting a blend of traits from both parent species. The plant typically features vibrant, showy flowers in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, Cyrtandra × ise is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections worldwide.
