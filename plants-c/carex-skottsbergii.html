Carex skottsbergii

Carex skottsbergii, commonly known as Skottsberg's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to the Andes mountains in South America, specifically in regions of Ecuador and Peru. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as cloud forests and rocky slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies in high-altitude ecosystems.
