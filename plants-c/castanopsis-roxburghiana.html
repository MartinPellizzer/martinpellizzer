Castanopsis roxburghiana

Castanopsis roxburghiana, commonly known as the Indian chinkapin, is a species of evergreen tree native to the Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and beeches, and is characterized by its hard, durable wood. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by spiny, cupule-enclosed nuts. Its leaves are typically elliptical, leathery, and have a rough texture.
