Cardaminopsis media

Cardaminopsis media, commonly known as the alpine buttercup, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant features basal leaves that are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are yellow, with five petals, and are borne on tall, slender stalks.

Cardaminopsis media is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
