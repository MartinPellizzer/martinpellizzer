Caladium picturatum var. albostriatulum

Caladium picturatum var. albostriatulum, commonly known as the white-striped caladium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with bold, contrasting patterns of white and green. The variegated foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often used as a decorative plant in shaded areas or as a houseplant.
