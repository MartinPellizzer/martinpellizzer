Crotonogyne ikelembensis

Crotonogyne ikelembensis, commonly known as the Nigerian croton, is a tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful foliage. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that can vary in shape and color depending on the variety. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsules containing numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
