Cryptanthus fernseeoides

Cryptanthus fernseeoides, commonly known as the Brazilian carpet plant, is a member of the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features striking, colorful foliage with red, orange, and yellow hues, often resembling a carpet of vibrant leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cryptanthus fernseeoides is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
