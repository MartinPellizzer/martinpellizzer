Coelogyne tenella

Coelogyne tenella, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and erect, branched inflorescences. The flowers are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive yellow lip, and they bloom during the dry season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil.

Coelogyne tenella is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
