Cryptocoryne walkeri

Cryptocoryne walkeri, commonly known as the Java cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. This plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments. It is popular in aquariums due to its hardy nature and ability to adapt to various water conditions.

Cryptocoryne walkeri is also known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of temperatures and water parameters.
