Conophytum meyeri f. semilunulum

Conophytum meyeri f. semilunulum, commonly known as the moonstone plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, forming a rosette shape. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
