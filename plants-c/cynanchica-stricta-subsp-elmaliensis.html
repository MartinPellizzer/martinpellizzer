Cynanchica stricta subsp. elmaliensis

Cynanchica stricta subsp.

elmaliensis, commonly known as the Elmaliensis milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various ailments.
