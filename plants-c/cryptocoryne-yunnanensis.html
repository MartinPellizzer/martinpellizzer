Cryptocoryne yunnanensis

Cryptocoryne yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. This plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water conditions and prefers moderate to low light levels. It is often found in slow-moving streams and ponds, where it forms dense underwater carpets.

Cryptocoryne yunnanensis is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
