Cryptanthus bromelioides var. tricolor

Cryptanthus bromelioides var. tricolor, commonly known as the Tricolor Zebra Plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Brazil.

It features striking, grass-like leaves with a distinctive tricolor pattern of red, orange, and green. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which appear in late summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular for its vibrant foliage and low maintenance requirements.
