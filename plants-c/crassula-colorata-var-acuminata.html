Crassula colorata var. acuminata

Crassula colorata var. acuminata, commonly known as the Campfire Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues along the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
