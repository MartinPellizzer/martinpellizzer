Cereus ballivianii

Cereus ballivianii, commonly known as the Christmas star cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, segmented stems and clusters of bright, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant typically grows in a shrub-like form, with stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Cereus ballivianii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and seasonal blooming.
