Crotalaria nitens

Crotalaria nitens, commonly known as the slender crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and pinnately compound leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its seeds are enclosed in a pod-like structure, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.

Crotalaria nitens is also notable for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
