Calla brevis

Calla brevis, commonly known as the short calla lily, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces elegant, spatulate flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, with a prominent, boat-shaped spathe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Calla brevis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
