Croton odontadenius

Croton odontadenius, commonly known as the African croton, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It features alternate, simple, and often lobed leaves that vary in shape depending on the species. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish-green flowers followed by distinctive three-lobed capsule fruits. It is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The leaves and roots have been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
