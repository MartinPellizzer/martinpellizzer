Chaenomeles Speciosa Var. Cathayensis

Chaenomeles speciosa var. cathayensis is a striking member of the Rosaceae family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to China, this ornamental shrub is prized for its early spring blooms that range from pink to red. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and hardiness. The plant's unique fruit, which resembles a small, spiky apple, adds visual interest throughout the growing season. With its compact size and long blooming period, it is a versatile addition to both landscape designs and container gardens.
