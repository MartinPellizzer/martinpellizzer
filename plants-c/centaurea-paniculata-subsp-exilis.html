Centaurea paniculata subsp. exilis

Centaurea paniculata subsp.

exilis, commonly known as the slender garden centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces bright yellow to orange flowers arranged in paniculate inflorescences, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
