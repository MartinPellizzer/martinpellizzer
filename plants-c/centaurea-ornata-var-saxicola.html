Centaurea ornata var. saxicola

Centaurea ornata var. saxicola, commonly known as the saxicola centaury, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in rocky or dry soils. It is well adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
