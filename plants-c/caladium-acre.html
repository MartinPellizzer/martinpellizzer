Caladium acre

Caladium acre, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored in shades of red, white, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Caladium acre is a fast-growing vine that can be cultivated in containers or as a ground cover in suitable environments.
