Calathea macrosepala

Calathea macrosepala, commonly known as the leopard plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled leaves with contrasting green and white patterns. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

Calathea macrosepala is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
