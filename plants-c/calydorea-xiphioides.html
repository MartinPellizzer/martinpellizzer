Calydorea xiphioides

Calydorea xiphioides, commonly known as the slender flame plant, is a rare and striking member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in specialized greenhouses and botanical gardens.
