Cymbopetalum hintonii subsp. hintonii

Cymbopetalum hintonii subsp.

hintonii, commonly known as the Hinton's corymbia, is a member of the Myrtaceae family. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub native to Australia.

The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
