Cymbidium

Cymbidium, commonly known as the orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family.

These plants are native to Asia and are known for their elegant, long-lasting blooms. Cymbidium orchids typically have strap-like leaves and produce flowers in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. They are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are popular in gardens and as potted plants.

Many species of Cymbidium are adapted to grow in cool climates and require specific care to thrive.
