Cleobulia leiantha

Cleobulia leiantha, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are widely used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
