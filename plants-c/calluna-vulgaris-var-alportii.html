Calluna vulgaris var. alportii

Calluna vulgaris var. alportii, commonly known as the Alport's heather, is a variant of the common heather.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Scottish Highlands. The plant features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and needle-like, arranged in whorls around the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in acidic, well-drained soils.
