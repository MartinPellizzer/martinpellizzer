Cassia suffruticosa

Cassia suffruticosa, commonly known as golden shower tree or yellow flame, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are compound, with multiple small leaflets arranged along a central axis.

Cassia suffruticosa is also valued for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional remedies.
