Cactus bonaerensis

Cactus bonaerensis, commonly known as the Buenos Aires cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has a short, cylindrical stem that is often covered in a dense layer of white, waxy hair. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
