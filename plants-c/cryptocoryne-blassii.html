Cryptocoryne blassii

Cryptocoryne blassii, commonly known as the Blass's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. Its rhizomes form dense mats, which help stabilize substrates in aquariums and natural water bodies.

Cryptocoryne blassii is popular in the aquarium trade for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
