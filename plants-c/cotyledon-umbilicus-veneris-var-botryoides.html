Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. botryoides

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. botryoides, commonly known as the "moonshine plant," is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, especially under direct sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive addition to rock gardens or container collections.

It requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
