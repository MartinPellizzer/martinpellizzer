Cyathea andohahelensis

Cyathea andohahelensis, commonly known as the Andohahela tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its upright structure.

The fronds are typically green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
