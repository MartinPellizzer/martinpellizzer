Cytisus megalanthus

Cytisus megalanthus, commonly known as the giant yellow broom, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and rocky soils, thriving in sunny and well-drained conditions. Cytisus megalanthus is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh coastal environments.
