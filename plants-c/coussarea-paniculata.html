Coussarea paniculata

Coussarea paniculata, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a large evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that appear in summer. Its inflorescences are paniculate, consisting of numerous small flowers arranged in a branched, loose cluster.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens for its striking floral display.
