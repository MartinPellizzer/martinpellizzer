Calhounia mocinniana

Calhounia mocinniana, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Calhounia mocinniana is also valued for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
