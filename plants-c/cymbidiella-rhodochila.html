Cymbidiella rhodochila

Cymbidiella rhodochila, commonly known as the red-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive red veins. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidiella rhodochila is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
