Campanula redowskiana

Campanula redowskiana, commonly known as the Redowskiana bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Campanula redowskiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
