Cotoneaster horizontalis var. wilsonii

Cotoneaster horizontalis var. wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries in the autumn. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and seasonal color changes.
