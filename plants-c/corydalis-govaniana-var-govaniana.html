Corydalis govaniana var. govaniana

Corydalis govaniana var. govaniana, commonly known as the Governor's corydalis, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
