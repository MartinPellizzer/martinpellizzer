Cattleya labiata f. purpureostriata

Cattleya labiata f. purpureostriata, commonly known as the purple-striated cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking floral display.

It features large, showy flowers with a distinctive purple stripe running along the lip, which sets it apart from other cattleya varieties. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native tropical habitats. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
