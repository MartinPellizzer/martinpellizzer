Cotoneaster rehderi

Cotoneaster rehderi, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters and features dense, upright growth with dark green leaves that turn reddish in autumn. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during late spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster rehderi is also used in landscaping for its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade conditions.
