Clerodendrum carnosulum

Clerodendrum carnosulum, commonly known as the sweet thorn or African hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features opposite, simple leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive sweet scent. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its fruits are three-lobed capsules that release seeds when mature.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
