Camaridium wercklei

Camaridium wercklei, commonly known as the Werckle's camaridium, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-like arrangement of leaves that form a dense, compact rosette. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the cooler months. Its foliage is covered in fine, silver-hyaline hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
