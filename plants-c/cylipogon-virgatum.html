Cylipogon virgatum

Cylipogon virgatum, commonly known as the slender bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescences that resemble a bottlebrush, composed of numerous small, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often elliptical in shape.

Cylipogon virgatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
