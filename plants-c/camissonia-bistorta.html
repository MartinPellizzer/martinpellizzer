Camissonia bistorta

Camissonia bistorta, commonly known as the broadleaf bistort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants such as buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its small, greenish-white flowers bloom in clusters, typically from June to August.
