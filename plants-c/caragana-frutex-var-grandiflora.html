Caragana frutex var. grandiflora

Caragana frutex var. grandiflora, commonly known as the yellow shrub lupin, is a deciduous shrub native to central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil structure.
