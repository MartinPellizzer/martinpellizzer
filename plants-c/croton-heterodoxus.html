Croton heterodoxus

Croton heterodoxus, commonly known as the flame tree or fire tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red, which bloom in clusters. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Croton heterodoxus is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
