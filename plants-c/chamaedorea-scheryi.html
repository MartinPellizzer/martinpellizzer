Chamaedorea scheryi

Chamaedorea scheryi, commonly known as the Schery's bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Mexico and Guatemala. This evergreen plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and feathery, compound leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions and requires well-draining, moist soil.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
