Cyrtopera trilamellata

Cyrtopera trilamellata, commonly known as the three-lobed fern, is a rare and delicate species belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, trifoliate fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a sensitive indicator of undisturbed ecosystems.
