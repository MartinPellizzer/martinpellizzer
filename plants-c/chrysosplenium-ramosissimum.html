Chrysosplenium ramosissimum

Chrysosplenium ramosissimum, commonly known as the hairy chrysosplenium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. The plant features small, opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom in early spring.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
