Crassula pellucida subsp. brachypetala

Crassula pellucida subsp.

brachypetala, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features small, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in response to sunlight.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
