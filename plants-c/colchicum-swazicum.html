Colchicum swazicum

Colchicum swazicum, commonly known as the Swazi colchicum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces large, showy blooms that appear in late spring or early summer.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
