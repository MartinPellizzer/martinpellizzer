Charpentiera ovata var. niuensis

Charpentiera ovata var. niuensis, commonly known as the Niue fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the island of Niue in the South Pacific and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, green fig-like fruits. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

The species is of cultural and ecological significance to the local communities of Niue.
