Cineraria × kewensis

Cineraria × kewensis, commonly known as the hybrid cineraria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and fuzzy, silver-green leaves. The plant is native to the Canary Islands and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Cineraria × kewensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
