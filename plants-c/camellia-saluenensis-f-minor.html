Camellia saluenensis f. minor

Camellia saluenensis f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf camellia, is a compact variant of the Camellia saluenensis species.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in winter. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
