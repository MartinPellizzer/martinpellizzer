Cyanus niger

Cyanus niger, commonly known as the black-eyed Susan, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow petals surrounding a dark center. The plant produces small, spiny seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in prairies and open fields.

Cyanus niger is known for its hardy nature and is a popular choice for garden landscapes.
