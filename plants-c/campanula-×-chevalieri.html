Campanula × chevalieri

Campanula × chevalieri, commonly known as the hybrid bellflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula × chevalieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
