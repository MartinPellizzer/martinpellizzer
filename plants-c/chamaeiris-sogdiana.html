Chamaeiris sogdiana

Chamaeiris sogdiana, commonly known as the Sogdian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet. Chamaeiris sogdiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
