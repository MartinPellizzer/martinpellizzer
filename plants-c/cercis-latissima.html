Cercis latissima

Cercis latissima, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are broad and often have a velvety texture. The tree produces showy, fragrant, white or pale purple flowers in late spring. Its fruit is a long, cylindrical pod that splits open to release winged seeds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
