Chrysophyllum litseiflorum

Chrysophyllum litseiflorum, commonly known as the African cherry or Litsea-flowered chrysophyllum, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible, sweet-tart berries.
