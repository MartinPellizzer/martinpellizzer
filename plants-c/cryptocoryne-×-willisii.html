Cryptocoryne × willisii

Cryptocoryne × willisii, commonly known as the hybrid cryptocoryne, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cryptocoryne balanseana and Cryptocoryne wendtii. This plant is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that can vary in shape depending on water conditions. It thrives in low to moderate light and requires stable water parameters to prevent stress and potential leaf decay.

Cryptocoryne × willisii is widely cultivated in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
