Campanula modesta

Campanula modesta, commonly known as the modest bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate, flowering display.
