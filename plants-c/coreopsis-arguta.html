Coreopsis arguta

Coreopsis arguta, commonly known as the rough coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. Its botanical characteristics include hairy stems and bracts that surround the flower head.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
