Clitoria philippensis

Clitoria philippensis, commonly known as the Philippine winged bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves and distinctive butterfly-like purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in tropical climates and is often used in traditional agriculture and as a ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it valuable for both ecological and nutritional purposes.
