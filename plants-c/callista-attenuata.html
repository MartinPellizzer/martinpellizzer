Callista attenuata

Callista attenuata, commonly known as the slender callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Callista attenuata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
