Centaurea kerneriana subsp. kerneriana

Centaurea kerneriana subsp.

kerneriana, commonly known as Kerner's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
