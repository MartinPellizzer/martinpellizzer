Cinnamomum poissonii

Cinnamomum poissonii, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or Cinnamomum poissonii, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the樟科 (Lauraceae) family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant typically grows to a height of 3–5 meters and features elliptical, dark green leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in panicles, while the fruit is a small, dark purple drupe.

The bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their aromatic properties.
