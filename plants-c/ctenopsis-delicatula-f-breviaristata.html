Ctenopsis delicatula f. breviaristata

Ctenopsis delicatula f. breviaristata, commonly known as the short-armed ctenopus, is a rare and delicate epiphytic orchid native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, softly textured leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
