Cheirostylis tortilacinia var. tortilacinia

Cheirostylis tortilacinia var. tortilacinia, commonly known as the spiral-stem orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, twisting stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spiral-arranged inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
