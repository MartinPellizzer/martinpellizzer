Cattleya trianae var. backhausiana

Cattleya trianae var. backhausiana, commonly known as the Backhausian Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink, often with a contrasting lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
