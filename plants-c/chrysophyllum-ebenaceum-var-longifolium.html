Chrysophyllum ebenaceum var. longifolium

Chrysophyllum ebenaceum var. longifolium, commonly known as the African star apple or long-leaved star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. The tree produces edible, star-shaped fruits that are rich in nutrients and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and is used in furniture and construction.

The plant is also cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
