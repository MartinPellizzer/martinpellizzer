Codiaeum variegatum f. longifolium

Codiaeum variegatum f. longifolium, commonly known as the flame maple or croton, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its large, elongated, palmately lobed leaves.

The foliage displays vibrant coloration, often featuring combinations of red, orange, yellow, and green, making it highly decorative. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vivid hues. The plant is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.

Its leaves are also known for their ability to change color with seasonal variations and environmental conditions.
