Cyrtandra oblanceolata

Cyrtandra oblanceolata, commonly known as the broad-leaved cyrtandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and mountainous areas.

Cyrtandra oblanceolata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
