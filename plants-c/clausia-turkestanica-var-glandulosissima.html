Clausia turkestanica var. glandulosissima

Clausia turkestanica var. glandulosissima, commonly known as the woolly clausia, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glandular pubescence, which gives it a fuzzy appearance. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. Its leaves are opposite and narrow, with a leathery texture.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.
