Cyanotis repens subsp. repens

Cyanotis repens subsp.

repens, commonly known as the creeping bluebell, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, rhizomatous growth habit.
