Carthamus gracillimus

Carthamus gracillimus, commonly known as the slender safflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, spiky seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and low-maintenance addition to gardens.
