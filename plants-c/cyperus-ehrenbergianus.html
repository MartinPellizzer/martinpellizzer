Cyperus ehrenbergianus

Cyperus ehrenbergianus, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. Its slender, triangular stems are often covered with small, scale-like leaves.

Cyperus ehrenbergianus is characterized by its small, spike-like inflorescences that produce tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
