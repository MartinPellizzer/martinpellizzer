Centaurea jacea var. pratensis

Centaurea jacea var. pratensis, commonly known as field centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in grassy areas and meadows. The plant features bright yellow flowers with red or purple markings, which are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often with a bluish-green hue.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
