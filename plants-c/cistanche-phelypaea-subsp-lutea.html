Cistanche phelypaea subsp. lutea

Cistanche phelypaea subsp.

lutea, commonly known as the yellow parasitic sandmat, is a flowering plant in the Orobanchaceae family. It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially penetrates the roots of host plants to obtain water and nutrients.
