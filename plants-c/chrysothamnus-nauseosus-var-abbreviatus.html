Chrysothamnus nauseosus var. abbreviatus

Chrysothamnus nauseosus var. abbreviatus, commonly known as the short-stemmed goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. Its leaves are narrow and grayish-green, with a distinctive resinous scent, and its yellow flowers bloom in late summer.
