Celmisia insignis

Celmisia insignis, commonly known as the silver tufted bellflower, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, often on rocky slopes or in open forests. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its ornamental value.
