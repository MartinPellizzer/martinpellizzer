Chenopodium × hybridum

Chenopodium × hybridum, commonly known as garden goosefoot, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Its stems are erect and can grow up to 1 meter in height, making it a common weed in cultivated areas.

Chenopodium × hybridum is adapted to a wide range of soil types and is often found in disturbed soils, gardens, and along roadsides.
