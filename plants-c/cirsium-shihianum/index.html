Cirsium Shihianum

Shihian thistle, scientifically known as Cirsium shihianum, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its spiny leaves and showy purple flowers. This species is particularly notable for its ecological significance, providing habitat and food for various pollinators and small wildlife. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important subject for botanical study and environmental protection.
