Calanthe granatensis

Calanthe granatensis, commonly known as the granate calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that often feature a combination of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, preferring well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and leathery, and it produces a single or few flower spikes during the spring or early summer.

Calanthe granatensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
