Clitoria pozuzoensis var. schunkei

Clitoria pozuzoensis var. schunkei, commonly known as the Pozuzo bean or Schunke's winged bean, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Peru.

It belongs to the legume family Fabaceae and is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for both ornamental and nutritional purposes.

Its leaves are trifoliate, and the plant has a climbing habit, making it suitable for trellises or supports in gardens.
