Cotoneaster oligocarpus

Cotoneaster oligocarpus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, red berries that are typically consumed by birds, aiding in seed dispersal. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Cotoneaster oligocarpus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
