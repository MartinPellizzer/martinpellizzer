Celosia falcata

Celosia falcata, commonly known as the flame grass or croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like bracts that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens. Celosia falcata typically grows as an annual or perennial, depending on the climate, and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for floral arrangements and landscape design.
