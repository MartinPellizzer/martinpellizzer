Centaurea ixodes

Centaurea ixodes, commonly known as the purple centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking purple to blue flowers and spiny, hairy stems. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant produces small, bract-like leaves and has a compact growth habit.

Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, making it a distinctive and ornamental species.
