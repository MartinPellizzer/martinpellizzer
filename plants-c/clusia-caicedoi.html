Clusia caicedoi

Clusia caicedoi, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Clusiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.

Clusia caicedoi is also valued for its ability to tolerate various soil types and its resilience to environmental stress.
