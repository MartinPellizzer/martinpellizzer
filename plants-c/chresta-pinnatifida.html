Chresta pinnatifida

Chresta pinnatifida, commonly known as the broad-leafed chresta, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged with elongated, slender leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
