Cereus biolleyi

Cereus biolleyi, commonly known as the desert rose, is a cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom at night. It has spines that are usually white or yellow, and it is adapted to arid environments.

Cereus biolleyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
