Crotalaria polyphylla

Crotalaria polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leafed cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and palmate leaves that are divided into several lobes. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has a hairy stem with opposite leaf arrangement. Crotalaria polyphylla is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a valuable plant in agricultural settings.
