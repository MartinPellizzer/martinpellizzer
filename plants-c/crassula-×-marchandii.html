Crassula × marchandii

Crassula × marchandii, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Money Plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Crassula × marchandii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
