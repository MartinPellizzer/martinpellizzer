Callistemon montis-zamiae

Callistemon montis-zamiae, commonly known as the Zambezi bottlebrush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
