Clematis meyeniana var. meyeniana

Clematis meyeniana var. meyeniana, commonly known as the yellow clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to South America.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive, intricate structure. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis meyeniana var. meyeniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or structures.
