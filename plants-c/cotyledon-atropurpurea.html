Cotyledon atropurpurea

Cotyledon atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple-leafed cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically purple or red in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
