Croton cliffordii

Croton cliffordii, commonly known as the red torch croton, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage, which often features shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with high light exposure.
