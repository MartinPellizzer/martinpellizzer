Cattleya praestans

Cattleya praestans, commonly known as the "Pink Orchid," is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers with a prominent red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, warm environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in conditions with seasonal dry periods.

Cattleya praestans is prized for its beauty and fragrance, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
