Cirsium funagataense

Cirsium funagataense, commonly known as the Funagata thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss and deter herbivores.

Cirsium funagataense is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
