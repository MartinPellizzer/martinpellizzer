Cyperus × insidiosus

Cyperus × insidiosus, commonly known as the swamp spikerush, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like stems and dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and usually have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Cyperus × insidiosus is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support aquatic ecosystems.
