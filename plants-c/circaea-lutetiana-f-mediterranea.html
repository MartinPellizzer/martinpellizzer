Circaea lutetiana f. mediterranea

Circaea lutetiana f. mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean white-flowered cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky soils and sunny habitats, such as cliffs and gravelly slopes. The plant features small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in coastal and inland areas with well-drained soils.
