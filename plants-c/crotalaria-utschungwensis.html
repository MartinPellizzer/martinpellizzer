Crotalaria utschungwensis

Crotalaria utschungwensis, commonly known as the Utsungwensi cowpea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant produces bright yellow flowers and has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in tropical and subtropical climates.

Crotalaria utschungwensis is also noted for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a forage crop.
