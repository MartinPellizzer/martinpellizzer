Cytisus bucovinensis

Cytisus bucovinensis, commonly known as the Bucovina broom, is a shrub native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typical of many legumes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native range.
