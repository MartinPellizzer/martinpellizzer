Conophytum misellum

Conophytum misellum, commonly known as the "Succulent Stone" or "Little Stone," is a member of the Aizoaceae family native to South Africa.

It is a small, compact succulent with fleshy, ribbed leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water to survive.

Conophytum misellum is well adapted to arid environments, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
