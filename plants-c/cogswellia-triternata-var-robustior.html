Cogswellia triternata var. robustior

Cogswellia triternata var. robustior, commonly known as the robust cogswellia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the growing season, adding a striking visual contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
