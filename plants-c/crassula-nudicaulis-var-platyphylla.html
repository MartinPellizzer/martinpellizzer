Crassula nudicaulis var. platyphylla

Crassula nudicaulis var. platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and attractive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
