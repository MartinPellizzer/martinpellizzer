Crassula capitella

Crassula capitella, commonly known as the "Snowball Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, rounded leaves that form a compact, ball-like structure. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white, star-shaped flowers during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
