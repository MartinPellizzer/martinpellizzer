Callista magnifica

Callista magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent callista, is a striking flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of vibrant colors. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Callista magnifica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
