Cineraria brasiliensis

Cineraria brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian cineraria, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green leaves and showy blue or violet flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cineraria brasiliensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
