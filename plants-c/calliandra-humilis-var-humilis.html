Calliandra humilis var. humilis

Calliandra humilis var. humilis, commonly known as the small flame tree, is a small, deciduous shrub or tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It features compound leaves with numerous small, delicate leaflets and produces showy, clustered inflorescences of bright red or orange flowers. The plant is characterized by its slender, often thorned stems and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant floral display.

The species is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
