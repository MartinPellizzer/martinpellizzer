Clusia bicolor

Clusia bicolor, commonly known as the wax plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or white, which bloom in clusters. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Clusia bicolor is also known for its tolerance to various environmental conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
