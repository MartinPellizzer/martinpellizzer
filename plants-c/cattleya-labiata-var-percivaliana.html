Cattleya labiata var. percivaliana

Cattleya labiata var. percivaliana, commonly known as the Percivaliana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display a combination of pink, white, and purple hues. The plant features long, arching inflorescences and broad, leathery leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty in both greenhouse and garden settings.
