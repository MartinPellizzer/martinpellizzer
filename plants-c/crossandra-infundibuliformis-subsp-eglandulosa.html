Crossandra infundibuliformis subsp. eglandulosa

Crossandra infundibuliformis subsp.

eglandulosa, commonly known as the glandular crossandra, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange.
