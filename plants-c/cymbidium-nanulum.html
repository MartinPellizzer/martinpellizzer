Cymbidium nanulum

Cymbidium nanulum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small plant, typically reaching a height of only a few centimeters, making it one of the smallest orchids in the world. The plant features a compact rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique size and elegant blooms make it a popular choice for miniature orchid collections.
