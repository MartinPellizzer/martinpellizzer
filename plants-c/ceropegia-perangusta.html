Ceropegia perangusta

Ceropegia perangusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species that features tubular, fleshy leaves and produces small, intricate flowers. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soil. It prefers bright, indirect light and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Ceropegia perangusta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable environment.
