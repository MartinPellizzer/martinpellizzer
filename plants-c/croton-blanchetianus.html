Croton blanchetianus

Croton blanchetianus, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that can be green, red, or variegated, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Croton blanchetianus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
