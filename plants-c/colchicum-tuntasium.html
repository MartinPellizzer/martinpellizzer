Colchicum tuntasium

Colchicum tuntasium, commonly known as the Tuntasium colchicum, is a member of the Colchicaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are usually purple or pink in color. Its bulbs are toxic and contain colchicine, a compound used in medicine.

It is native to specific areas in the Mediterranean region and is often found in high-altitude environments.
