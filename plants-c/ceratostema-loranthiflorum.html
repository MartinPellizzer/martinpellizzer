Ceratostema loranthiflorum

Ceratostema loranthiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on the branches of other trees.

It has small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually opposite in arrangement. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, red berry that contains several seeds.

This species is native to China and is often found in forested areas where it attaches to host trees for nutrients.
