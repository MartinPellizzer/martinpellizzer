Crataegus oxyacantha var. microxyacantha

Crataegus oxyacantha var. microxyacantha, commonly known as the miniature hawthorn, is a compact variant of the hawthorn species.

It is characterized by its small stature, typically reaching heights of only 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dense, thorny branches and produces small, white flowers in early spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that ripens in the fall.

This variety is often cultivated as a decorative shrub due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
