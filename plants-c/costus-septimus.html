Costus septimus

Costus septimus, commonly known as the red ginger or false arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
