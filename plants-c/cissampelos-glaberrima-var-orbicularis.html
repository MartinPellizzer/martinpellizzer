Cissampelos glaberrima var. orbicularis

Cissampelos glaberrima var. orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaf moon vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, orbicular leaves that are usually smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
