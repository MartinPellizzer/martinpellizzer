Capparis kbangensis

Capparis kbangensis, commonly known as the Kbang caper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Capparidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval and slightly hairy.

Capparis kbangensis thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in dry, rocky habitats.
