Codiaeum palawanense

Codiaeum palawanense, commonly known as the Palawan croton, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Codiaeum palawanense is also appreciated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
