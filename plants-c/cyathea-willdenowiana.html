Cyathea willdenowiana

Cyathea willdenowiana, commonly known as the Willdenow's tree fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Cyatheaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous, spongy layer called the rhyzome. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Cyathea willdenowiana is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
