Chaetospora oligostachya

Chaetospora oligostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, feathery inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Chaetospora oligostachya is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
