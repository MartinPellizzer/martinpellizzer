Cattleya warscewiczii var. burfordiensis

Cattleya warscewiczii var. burfordiensis, commonly known as the Burford's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white, often with a contrasting lip. The plant features long, arching pseudobulbs and broad, dark green foliage. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
