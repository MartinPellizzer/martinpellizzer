Ciconium

Ciconium, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a water plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Nymphaeaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that float on the surface of still water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide shade and support for aquatic life. Its flowers are typically pink or white, with a unique structure that allows for pollination by insects and birds.

The ciconium is also valued for its symbolic meaning in various cultures, representing purity and enlightenment.
