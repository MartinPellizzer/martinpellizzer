Conophytum ceresianum

Conophytum ceresianum, commonly known as the Cape cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
