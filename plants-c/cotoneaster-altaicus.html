Cotoneaster altaicus

Cotoneaster altaicus, commonly known as the Altai cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptability to harsh climates. The plant features small, glossy leaves and produces clusters of red berries in the autumn. Its flowers are usually white or pink, appearing in the spring.

Cotoneaster altaicus is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
