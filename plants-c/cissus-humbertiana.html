Cissus humbertiana

Cissus humbertiana, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in shaded areas.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
