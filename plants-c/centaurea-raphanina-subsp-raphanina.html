Centaurea raphanina subsp. raphanina

Centaurea raphanina subsp.

raphanina, commonly known as the garden centaury, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant produces small, spiny seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread. Native to southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and gardens.
