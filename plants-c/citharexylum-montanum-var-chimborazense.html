Citharexylum montanum var. chimborazense

Citharexylum montanum var. chimborazense, commonly known as the Chimborazo citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of Ecuador.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in humid and cool environments. The plant produces fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
