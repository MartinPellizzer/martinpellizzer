Cycnium elskensii

Cycnium elskensii, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. It produces round, green berries that turn red when mature, containing toxic seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
