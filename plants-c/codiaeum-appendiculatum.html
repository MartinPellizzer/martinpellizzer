Codiaeum appendiculatum

Codiaeum appendiculatum, commonly known as the flame leaf or red leaf plant, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically broad, heart-shaped, and display striking shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in indoor and outdoor gardens.
