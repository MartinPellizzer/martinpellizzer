Clutia robusta var. rhododendroides

Clutia robusta var. rhododendroides, commonly known as the red-flowered clutia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and emits a mild, sweet fragrance. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its vibrant floral display.
