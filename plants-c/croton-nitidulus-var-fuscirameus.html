Croton nitidulus var. fuscirameus

Croton nitidulus var. fuscirameus, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
