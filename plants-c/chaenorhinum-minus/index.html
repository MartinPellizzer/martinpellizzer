Chaenorhinum Minus

Chaenorhinum minus is a small, flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is commonly known as the lesser yellowtop and belongs to the plantain family. This species is characterized by its delicate yellow flowers and low-growing habit. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly areas. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in supporting pollinators and adding beauty to natural landscapes.
