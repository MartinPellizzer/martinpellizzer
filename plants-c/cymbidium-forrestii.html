Cymbidium forrestii

Cymbidium forrestii, commonly known as the Forster's orchid, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae. The plant features erect, linear leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers. Its blooms are typically white or pale yellow with greenish or reddish markings.

Cymbidium forrestii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
