Croton fucatus

Croton fucatus, commonly known as the black croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking dark green, almost black, foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.

Croton fucatus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
