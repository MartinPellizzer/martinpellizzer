Canthium congestiflorum

Canthium congestiflorum, commonly known as the clustered spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and showy inflorescences. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, sunny habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.
