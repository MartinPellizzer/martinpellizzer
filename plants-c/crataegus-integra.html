Crataegus integra

Crataegus integra, commonly known as the smooth hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces red, juicy berries that are an important food source for wildlife. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Crataegus integra is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
