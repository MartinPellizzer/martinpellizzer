Croton velleriflorus

Croton velleriflorus, commonly known as the velvetflower croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from dark green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Croton velleriflorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage.
