Conyza mildbraedii

Conyza mildbraedii, commonly known as the mildbraed's weed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. The plant has hairy stems and produces small, white to yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.

Botanically, it is characterized by its simple leaves and composite flower heads, typical of the Asteraceae family.
