Callianthe jaliscana

Callianthe jaliscana, commonly known as the Jalisco orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, while its flowers come in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Callianthe jaliscana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
