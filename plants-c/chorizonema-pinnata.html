Chorizonema pinnata

Chorizonema pinnata, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its silver-green foliage and small, clustered white flowers. The leaves are pinnately compound, giving it the species name *pinnata*, and the tree is often found in coastal and riverine habitats.

It plays an important ecological role in providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
