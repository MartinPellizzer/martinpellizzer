Cheiridopsis amabilis

Cheiridopsis amabilis, commonly known as the sweetface ice plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its characteristic flowers and fleshy, water-storing leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often blooming in spring. Its leaves are typically blue-green and form a dense, low-growing mat.
