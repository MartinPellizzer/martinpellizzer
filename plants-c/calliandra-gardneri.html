Calliandra gardneri

Calliandra gardneri, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its feathery, pink to red inflorescences that resemble showerheads, which bloom in summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Calliandra gardneri is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
