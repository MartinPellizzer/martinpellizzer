Cactus scheeri

Cactus scheeri, commonly known as the Scheeri cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white spines that provide protection from herbivores and excessive sunlight. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and enabling survival in extreme desert conditions.

Overall, Cactus scheeri exemplifies the adaptive strategies of desert flora.
