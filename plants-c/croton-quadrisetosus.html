Croton quadrisetosus

Croton quadrisetosus, commonly known as the four-set croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and as a houseplant.

Croton quadrisetosus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
