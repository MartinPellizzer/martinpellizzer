Chuquiraga argentea var. dusenii

Chuquiraga argentea var. dusenii, commonly known as the silverleaf chuquiraga, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often growing in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
