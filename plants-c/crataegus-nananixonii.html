Crataegus nananixonii

Crataegus nananixonii, commonly known as the Nanixon's hawthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, deciduous leaves and clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It is typically found in woodland areas and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
