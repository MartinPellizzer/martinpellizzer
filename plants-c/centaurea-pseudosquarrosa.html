Centaurea pseudosquarrosa

Centaurea pseudosquarrosa, commonly known as the pale centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Centaurea pseudosquarrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
