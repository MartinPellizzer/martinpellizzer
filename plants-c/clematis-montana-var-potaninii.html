Clematis montana var. potaninii

Clematis montana var. potaninii, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, showy form. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that are capsules, containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
