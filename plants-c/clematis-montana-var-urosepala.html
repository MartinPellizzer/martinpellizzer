Clematis montana var. urosepala

Clematis montana var. urosepala, commonly known as the smooth Clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to eastern North America.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets that are smooth and often slightly hairy. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability in garden settings.
