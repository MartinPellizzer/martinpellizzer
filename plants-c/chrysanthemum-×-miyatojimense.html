Chrysanthemum × miyatojimense

Chrysanthemum × miyatojimense, commonly known as the Japanese chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and a wide range of flower colors, including white, yellow, and red. The plant produces showy, daisy-like blooms that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in traditional Japanese floral arrangements.
