Crotalaria junodiana

Crotalaria junodiana, commonly known as the golden chain bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its legume pods and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces bright yellow flowers in elongated clusters. Its seeds are enclosed in hairy, elongated pods that split open when mature.

Crotalaria junodiana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
