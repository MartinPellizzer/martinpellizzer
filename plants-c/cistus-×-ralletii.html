Cistus × ralletii

Cistus × ralletii, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes several species of evergreen shrubs. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Cistus × ralletii is often used in xeriscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
