Chrozophora gracilis

Chrozophora gracilis, commonly known as slender broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in soft, grayish-green foliage. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer.

Chrozophora gracilis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
