Camellia thea var. lasiocalyx

Camellia thea var. lasiocalyx, commonly known as the Sichuan camellia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape.

The flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
