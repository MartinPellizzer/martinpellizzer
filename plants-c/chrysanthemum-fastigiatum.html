Chrysanthemum fastigiatum

Chrysanthemum fastigiatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and finely dissected, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in a range of colors including white, yellow, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This species is often appreciated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
