Camellia brevipetiolata

Camellia brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-petioled camellia, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its elegant, glossy dark green leaves with short petioles. The plant produces striking camellia flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental gardens and container planting.

The flowers are borne on short pedicels and are often used in floral arrangements due to their beauty and fragrance.
