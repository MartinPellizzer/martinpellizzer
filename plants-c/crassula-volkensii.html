Crassula volkensii

Crassula volkensii, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Firetail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula volkensii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
