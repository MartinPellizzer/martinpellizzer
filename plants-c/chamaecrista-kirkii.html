Chamaecrista kirkii

Chamaecrista kirkii, commonly known as Kirk's burr legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Chamaecrista kirkii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native habitats.
