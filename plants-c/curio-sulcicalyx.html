Curio sulcicalyx

Curio sulcicalyx, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a unique species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped stems that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Curio sulcicalyx is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unusual appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
