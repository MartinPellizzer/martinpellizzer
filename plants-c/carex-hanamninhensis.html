Carex hanamninhensis

Carex hanamninhensis, commonly known as the Hanamninheng sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are often hidden among its foliage.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
