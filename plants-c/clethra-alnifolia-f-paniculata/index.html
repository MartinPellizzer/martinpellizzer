Clethra Alnifolia F. Paniculata

Sweet pepperbush, scientifically known as Clethra alnifolia f. paniculata, is a striking ornamental shrub native to eastern North America. This unique cultivar showcases a dense, rounded growth habit with showy white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage provides a vibrant green backdrop, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and ecological value. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in wetland restoration projects. With its fragrant blooms and adaptability, Sweet pepperbush is a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
