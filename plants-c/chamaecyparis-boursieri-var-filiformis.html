Chamaecyparis boursieri var. filiformis

Chamaecyparis boursieri var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender Japanese cypress, is a rare and ornamental conifer native to Japan.

It is characterized by its narrow, feather-like foliage that gives it a delicate, almost ethereal appearance. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves that are densely packed along the branches, creating a compact and elegant form. It typically grows in a columnar shape, making it suitable for small garden spaces or as a specimen plant.

This variety is valued for its unique texture and slow growth rate, which makes it a popular choice in horticultural collections.
