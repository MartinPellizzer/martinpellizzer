Cinchona howardiana

Cinchona howardiana, commonly known as the pink-veined quinine tree, is a member of the Rubiaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

Cinchona howardiana is typically found in humid, shaded environments at elevations between 1,500 and 2,500 meters.

Its ornamental value has also made it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in tropical and subtropical regions.
