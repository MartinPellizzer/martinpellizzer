Ceanothus diversifolius var. foliosus

Ceanothus diversifolius var. foliosus, commonly known as the coast ceanothus, is a flowering shrub native to the coastal regions of California.

It features slender, often branched stems covered in dense, gray-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and alternate, with some variation in shape among individuals.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal climates with mild winters and warm, dry summers.
