Cyathea bellisquamata var. australis

Cyathea bellisquamata var. australis, commonly known as the Australian tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height, with a slow growth rate. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive dark green color and a leathery texture.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
