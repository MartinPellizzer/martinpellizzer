Carbeni benedicta

Carbeni benedicta, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, with deep green coloration and a waxy texture. Its flowers, which bloom in a range of colors from white to pink, are highly symbolic in various cultures and religions. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, requiring ample sunlight for optimal growth.

Carbeni benedicta is also valued for its ability to purify water and support aquatic ecosystems.
