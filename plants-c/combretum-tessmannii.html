Combretum tessmannii

Combretum tessmannii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are often glossy and elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and seasonal display of vibrant blooms make it a notable species in its native habitat.
