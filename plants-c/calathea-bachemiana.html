Calathea bachemiana

Calathea bachemiana, commonly known as the Bachemiana Calathea, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns. The leaves are typically broad and oval, displaying a range of hues including green, pink, and red, often with a metallic sheen. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
