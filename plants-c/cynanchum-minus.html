Cynanchum minus

Cynanchum minus, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It is characterized by its slender stems and trifoliate leaves, which are often hairy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds.

It is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, and it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
