Conophytum obcordellum var. germanum

Conophytum obcordellum var. germanum, commonly known as the German conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, triangular leaves that are often covered in a white, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
