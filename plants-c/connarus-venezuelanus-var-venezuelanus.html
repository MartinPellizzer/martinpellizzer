Connarus venezuelanus var. venezuelanus

Connarus venezuelanus var. venezuelanus, commonly known as the Venezuelan connarus, is a member of the Connaraceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Venezuela. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in greenish or yellowish clusters, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and has some traditional uses in local communities.
