Caesalpinia tara

Caesalpinia tara, commonly known as tara tree, is a leguminous shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its distinctive pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in dry forest ecosystems.

The tara tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local cultures.
