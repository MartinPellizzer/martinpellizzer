Coelogyne parvula var. parvula

Coelogyne parvula var. parvula, commonly known as the dwarf orchid or small coelogyne, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, and its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and clustered. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its delicate appearance and ease of care, it is often favored by orchid enthusiasts.
