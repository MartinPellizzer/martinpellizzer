Cleome gynandra var. nana

Cleome gynandra var. nana, commonly known as dwarf spider plant, is a compact, herbaceous annual plant native to tropical Africa.

It features small, pale green leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces clusters of yellow flowers that attract pollinators. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its delicate appearance and easy care requirements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Cleome gynandra var. nana is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
