Crudia ledermannii

Crudia ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's thornveld, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping for erosion control and as a decorative plant.
