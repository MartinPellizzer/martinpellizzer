Convolvulus trichosanthes var. tenellus

Convolvulus trichosanthes var. tenellus, commonly known as the lesser bottle gourd, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, bottle-shaped fruits. It is native to Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates.
