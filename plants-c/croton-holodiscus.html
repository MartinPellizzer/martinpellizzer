Croton holodiscus

Croton holodiscus, commonly known as the silver croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that often display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with unique sap and floral structures. Its leaves are alternate and typically have a waxy, glossy appearance.

Croton holodiscus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
