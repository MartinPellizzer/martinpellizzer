Calathea loeseneri

Calathea loeseneri, commonly known as the velvetleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, and patterned leaves that display a range of colors, including deep green, silver, and purple. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Its leaves are often adorned with intricate markings that change subtly in response to light and temperature.

Calathea loeseneri thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.
