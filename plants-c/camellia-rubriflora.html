Camellia rubriflora

Camellia rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered camellia, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces showy, pink to red flowers in the spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Camellia rubriflora is also valued for its historical and cultural significance in traditional Chinese medicine.
