Conospermum ellipticum

Conospermum ellipticum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical flower spikes that resemble bottle brushes.
