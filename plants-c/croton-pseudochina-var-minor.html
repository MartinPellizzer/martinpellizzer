Croton pseudochina var. minor

Croton pseudochina var. minor, commonly known as the Chinese croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful foliage, which can range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
