Circaea Lutetiana Proles Alpina

Circaea lutetiana proles alpina is a rare and delicate plant species found in high altitude environments. It belongs to the primrose family and is known for its small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. This subspecies is adapted to cold climates and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas. Its survival is threatened by climate change and habitat loss. Protecting this plant is crucial for maintaining biodiversity in mountain ecosystems.
