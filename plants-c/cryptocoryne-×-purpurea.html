Cryptocoryne × purpurea

Cryptocoryne × purpurea, commonly known as the purple cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple coloration on its leaves, which contrast with its dark green undersides. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires stable water parameters to maintain its vibrant color. It is often cultivated in aquariums for its ornamental value and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne × purpurea is a popular choice among aquarists due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
