Crassula jacobseniana

Crassula jacobseniana, commonly known as the Jacobson's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
