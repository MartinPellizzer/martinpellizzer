Cleome Inornata

Cleome, scientifically known as Cleome inornata, is a striking flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. This annual species is celebrated for its vibrant, daisy-like blooms that appear in late summer and early fall. With its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers, Cleome inornata adds a bold visual impact to gardens and natural landscapes. It is easy to grow and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. Due to its beauty and ecological benefits, Cleome inornata is increasingly appreciated as a valuable addition to native plant collections.
