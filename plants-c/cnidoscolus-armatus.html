Cnidoscolus armatus

Cnidoscolus armatus, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into spiny, edible fruits. Its roots contain compounds that have been used for their medicinal properties.

However, it is important to note that the plant is toxic if ingested in large quantities.
