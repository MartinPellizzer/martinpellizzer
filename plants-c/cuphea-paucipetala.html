Cuphea paucipetala

Cuphea paucipetala, commonly known as the few-flowered cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique flower structure and vibrant colors make it an attractive addition to both ornamental and pollinator-friendly landscapes.
