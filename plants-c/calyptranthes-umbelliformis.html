Calyptranthes umbelliformis

Calyptranthes umbelliformis, commonly known as the umbrella calyptranthes, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland forest areas. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
