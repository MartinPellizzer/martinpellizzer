Capparis oligostema

Capparis oligostema, commonly known as the narrow-leaved caper, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and features small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, hard seeds. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a slightly rough texture.

Capparis oligostema is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
