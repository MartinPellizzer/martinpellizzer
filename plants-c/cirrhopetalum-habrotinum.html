Cirrhopetalum habrotinum

Cirrhopetalum habrotinum, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and a complex root system.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
