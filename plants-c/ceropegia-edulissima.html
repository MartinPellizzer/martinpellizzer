Ceropegia edulissima

Ceropegia edulissima, commonly known as the string of hearts plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Ceropegia edulissima is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and moderate temperatures.
