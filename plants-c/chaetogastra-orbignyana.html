Chaetogastra orbignyana

Chaetogastra orbignyana, commonly known as the orbignyana grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to South America, particularly found in Brazil and Argentina. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soils and provide habitat for native wildlife.
