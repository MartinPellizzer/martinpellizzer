Coelogyne benguetensis

Coelogyne benguetensis, commonly known as the Benguet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are long and strap-like, arranged in a fan shape around the pseudobulbs.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil.
