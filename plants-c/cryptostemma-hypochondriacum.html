Cryptostemma hypochondriacum

Cryptostemma hypochondriacum, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a fibrous root system and produces delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid conditions and can tolerate periods of drought.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
