Calceolaria crenata subsp. australis

Calceolaria crenata subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically bright red or orange with a yellow throat.

The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun to partial shade. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy blooms from late winter to early spring.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
