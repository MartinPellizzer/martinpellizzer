Chlorophytum ustulatum

Chlorophytum ustulatum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a hardy, evergreen perennial that features long, upright leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal indoor plant. It produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the summer months.

Its air-purifying properties make it a popular choice for both residential and commercial environments.
