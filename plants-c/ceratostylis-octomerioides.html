Ceratostylis octomerioides

Ceratostylis octomerioides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest undergrowth, where it thrives in humid conditions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in a loose inflorescence, often with a distinctive hood-like structure.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
