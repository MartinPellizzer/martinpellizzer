Chusquea Angusta

Chusquea angusta is a unique grass-like plant native to the cloud forests of South America. It belongs to the bromeliad family and is known for its narrow, sword-shaped leaves. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
