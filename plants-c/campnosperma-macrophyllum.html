Campnosperma macrophyllum

Campnosperma macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf senna, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has bipinnate leaves, which are compound and consist of multiple pairs of leaflets. Its yellow flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the warmer months.

The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
