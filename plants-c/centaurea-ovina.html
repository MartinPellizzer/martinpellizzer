Centaurea ovina

Centaurea ovina, commonly known as silky centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Centaurea ovina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
