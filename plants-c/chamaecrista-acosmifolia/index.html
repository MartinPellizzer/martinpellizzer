Chamaecrista Acosmifolia

Coral bean, scientifically known as Chamaecrista acosmifolia, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. With its vibrant red and orange pods and feathery foliage, it stands out in gardens and natural landscapes. This species is part of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes and is known for its nitrogen-fixing abilities. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits make it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological gardening. Despite its beauty, it requires specific conditions to thrive and is often overlooked in favor of more common plants.
