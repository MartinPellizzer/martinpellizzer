Cymbidium ovatum

Cymbidium ovatum, commonly known as the common orchid or the Chinese evergreen orchid, is a species of orchid native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in winter or early spring. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cymbidium ovatum is often cultivated for its beauty and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
