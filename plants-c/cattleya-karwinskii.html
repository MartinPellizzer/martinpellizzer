Cattleya karwinskii

Cattleya karwinskii, commonly known as the Karwinski's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

Cattleya karwinskii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
