Cymbidium praecox

Cymbidium praecox, commonly known as the early orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the Orchidaceae family. The plant features erect, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cymbidium praecox is known for its ability to bloom early in the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
