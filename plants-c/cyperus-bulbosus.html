Cyperus bulbosus

Cyperus bulbosus, commonly known as the bulbous flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Its leaves are grass-like, typically narrow and linear, with a smooth surface.

Cyperus bulbosus is often found in wetland habitats, including marshes and along the edges of water bodies.
