Cousinia pulchra

Cousinia pulchra, commonly known as the Persian Daisy, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.
