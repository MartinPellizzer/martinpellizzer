Chloris amethystea

Chloris amethystea, commonly known as the purple grass, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, violet-colored flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that becomes more prominent during the growing season.
