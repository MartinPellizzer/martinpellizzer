Calanthe poiformis

Calanthe poiformis, commonly known as the broad-leafed calanthe, is a herbaceous perennial belonging to the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, India, and Myanmar. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy texture, and it thrives in shaded, humid environments.

Calanthe poiformis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
