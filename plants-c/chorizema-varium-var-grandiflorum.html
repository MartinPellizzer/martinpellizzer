Chorizema varium var. grandiflorum

Chorizema varium var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered chorizema, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
