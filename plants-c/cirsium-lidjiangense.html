Cirsium lidjiangense

Cirsium lidjiangense, commonly known as the Yunnan thistle, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist climates. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, globular flower heads.

Cirsium lidjiangense is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
