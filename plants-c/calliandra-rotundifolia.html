Calliandra rotundifolia

Calliandra rotundifolia, commonly known as the globe flower, is a species of legume native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive spherical flower heads, which are composed of numerous small, pink to red flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, rounded leaflets, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Calliandra rotundifolia is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
