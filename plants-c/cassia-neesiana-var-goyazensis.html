Cassia neesiana var. goyazensis

Cassia neesiana var. goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis Cassia, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ornamental value, with its bright, fern-like foliage and clusters of golden blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.
