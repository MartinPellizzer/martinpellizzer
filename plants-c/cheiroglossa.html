Cheiroglossa

Cheiroglossa, commonly known as the golden crown orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. Its flowers are showy, with a golden-yellow color and a distinctive crown-like structure.

Cheiroglossa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
