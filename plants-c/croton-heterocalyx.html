Croton heterocalyx

Croton heterocalyx, commonly known as the warty croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, warty, and often colorful bark, which can range from yellow to red. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

Croton heterocalyx is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
