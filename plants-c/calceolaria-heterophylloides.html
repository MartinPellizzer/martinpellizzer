Calceolaria heterophylloides

Calceolaria heterophylloides, commonly known as the heterophyllous calceolaria, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, hood-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant exhibits heterophylly, meaning it produces different types of leaves depending on environmental conditions. Its flowers are typically borne on tall, slender stems and are adapted for pollination by insects.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
