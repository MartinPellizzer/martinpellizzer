Chenopodium concatenatum f. integrifolium

Chenopodium concatenatum f. integrifolium, commonly known as the smooth goosefoot, is a variant of the common goosefoot species.

It is an annual herbaceous plant characterized by its smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by egg-shaped, edible seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

This subspecies is valued for its nutritional content and has been used in traditional cuisines.
