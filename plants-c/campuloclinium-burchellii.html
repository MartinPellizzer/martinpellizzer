Campuloclinium burchellii

Campuloclinium burchellii, commonly known as Burchell's bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branched stems that produce clusters of small, sky-blue flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
