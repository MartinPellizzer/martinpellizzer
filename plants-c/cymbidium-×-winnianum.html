Cymbidium × winnianum

Cymbidium × winnianum, commonly known as the Winnian orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of long, narrow, greenish-white to pinkish-yellow petals. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cymbidium × winnianum is valued in horticulture for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
