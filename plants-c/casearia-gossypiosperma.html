Casearia gossypiosperma

Casearia gossypiosperma, commonly known as the cottonseed casearia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family, which includes willows and poplars, and is characterized by its opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by distinctive, woody fruits that contain fibrous, cotton-like seeds.
