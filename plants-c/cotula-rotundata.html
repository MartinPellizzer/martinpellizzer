Cotula rotundata

Cotula rotundata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of silver-green, rounded leaves and produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is drought-tolerant and has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Cotula rotundata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
