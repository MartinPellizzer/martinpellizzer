Combretum lopolense

Combretum lopolense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its distinctive red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture. The plant is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

It plays an important ecological role by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
