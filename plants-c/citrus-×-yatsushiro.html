Citrus × yatsushiro

Citrus × yatsushiro, commonly known as the Yatsushiro mandarin.

It is a hybrid citrus fruit native to Japan, characterized by its small, round, and fragrant orange-colored fruit. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, with glossy, dark green leaves and a compact growth habit. It produces fruit in late winter to early spring, which is known for its sweet and tangy flavor.

The botanical features of Citrus × yatsushiro make it a popular choice for ornamental and culinary purposes.
