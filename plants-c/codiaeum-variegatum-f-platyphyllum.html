Codiaeum variegatum f. platyphyllum

Codiaeum variegatum f. platyphyllum, commonly known as the Shiny Cabbage or Variegated Cabbage, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant mix of green, red, and yellow. The leaves are broad and thick, with a glossy surface that enhances its visual appeal. This cultivar is prized for its ability to thrive in bright, indirect light and its relatively low maintenance requirements.

It is often used in landscaping and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and adaptability.
