Calanthe longipes

Calanthe longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and showy, lance-shaped flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem. Calanthe longipes thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Its striking floral display makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
