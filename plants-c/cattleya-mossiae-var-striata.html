Cattleya mossiae var. striata

Cattleya mossiae var. striata, commonly known as the Striated Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

This variety features elongated, narrow petals and a distinctive striated pattern on its petals and sepals. It originates from the tropical regions of South America, where it grows as an epiphyte on trees. The plant typically blooms in late spring or early summer, producing clusters of showy, orchid-like flowers.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
