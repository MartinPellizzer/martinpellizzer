Croton denisii

Croton denisii, commonly known as the Denis's croton, is a shrub native to parts of East Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful leaves that display a variety of shades, including green, red, and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a distinctive veining pattern.

Croton denisii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
