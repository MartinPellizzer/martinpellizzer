Clematis × splendida

Clematis × splendida, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to East Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis × splendida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
