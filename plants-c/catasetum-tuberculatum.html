Catasetum tuberculatum

Catasetum tuberculatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence and vibrant, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are notable for their intricate structure, with specialized pollination mechanisms that attract specific insect pollinators.

Catasetum tuberculatum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and distinctive appearance.
