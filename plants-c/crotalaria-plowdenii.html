Crotalaria plowdenii

Crotalaria plowdenii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein. Crotalaria plowdenii is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
