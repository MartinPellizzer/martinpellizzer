Callicarpa longifolia var. glabrescens

Callicarpa longifolia var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth flameberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red berries that persist through the winter, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, often found in moist, woodland habitats.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife due to its showy fruits.
