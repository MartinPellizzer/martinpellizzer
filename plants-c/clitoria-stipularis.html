Clitoria stipularis

Clitoria stipularis, commonly known as the spurred butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet-blue flowers with spurred petals that resemble butterfly wings. The plant is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and is often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are also popular in floral arrangements and as a source of natural dye.

Botanically, it belongs to the Fabaceae family, known for its leguminous properties.
