Calochortus luteus var. weedii

Calochortus luteus var. weedii, commonly known as the yellow mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the lily family (Liliaceae) and is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single stem that bears a cluster of blooms.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
