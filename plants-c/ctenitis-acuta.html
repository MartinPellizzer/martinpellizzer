Ctenitis acuta

Ctenitis acuta, commonly known as the acute ctenitis, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bioluminescent in certain conditions. Ctenitis acuta plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various microorganisms and small fauna.
