Chlonanthes latifolia

Chlonanthes latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Caricaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that develop into star-shaped fruits with a distinct, waxy texture. Its fruit is edible and has a mild, sweet flavor, often used in local cuisines.
