Consolida stenocarpa

Consolida stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-seeded sweet pea, is a flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of the Mediterranean region. The plant features delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The seed pods are slender and cylindrical, giving the plant its common name.
