Celosia huttonii

Celosia huttonii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spiky bracts that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are usually red, orange, or yellow, and they bloom in dense clusters.

Celosia huttonii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in warm, sunny conditions.
