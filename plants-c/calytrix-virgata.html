Calytrix virgata

Calytrix virgata, commonly known as the pink mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, woody fruits. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils.

Calytrix virgata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
