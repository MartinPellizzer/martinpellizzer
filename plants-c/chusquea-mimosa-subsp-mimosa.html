Chusquea mimosa subsp. mimosa

Chusquea mimosa subsp.

mimosa, commonly known as the silver bamboo, is a clump-forming evergreen bamboo native to South America. It features slender, hollow stems that are covered in a silvery-white sheath, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by fluffy, feathery seed heads. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often used in landscaping and horticultural gardens.
