Cracca Wallichii

Blue poppy, scientifically known as Cracca wallichii, is a strikingly beautiful flower native to the Himalayas. With its vibrant blue petals and delicate appearance, it captivates botanists and nature lovers alike. This species thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Despite its beauty, the blue poppy is rare and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Its unique coloration and ecological significance make it a subject of both admiration and conservation concern.
