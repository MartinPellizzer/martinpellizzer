Chenopodium Indicum

Chenopodium indicum is a versatile plant commonly known as Indian goosefoot. It is native to Asia but has been introduced to many parts of the world. This plant is valued for its edible leaves and seeds, which are used in various cuisines. It thrives in a variety of soil conditions and can grow in both temperate and tropical climates. Its nutritional value and adaptability make it an important species in agriculture and nutrition.
