Cyclamen creticum f. erubescens

Cyclamen creticum f. erubescens, commonly known as the Crimson Cyclamen, is a striking hybrid plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant, tubular flowers that range from pink to deep red. The plant produces showy blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often adorned with a reddish tinge, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Cyclamen creticum f. erubescens thrives in cool, well-drained soil and requires moderate light to flourish.
