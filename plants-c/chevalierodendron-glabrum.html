Chevalierodendron glabrum

Chevalierodendron glabrum, commonly known as the smooth chevalierodendron, is a rare and distinctive plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color, which bloom in clusters. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
