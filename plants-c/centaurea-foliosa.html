Centaurea foliosa

Centaurea foliosa, commonly known as the leafy centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright blue to purple flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Centaurea foliosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
