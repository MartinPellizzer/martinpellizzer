Croton magnifolius

Croton magnifolius, commonly known as the magnificent croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in panicles, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
