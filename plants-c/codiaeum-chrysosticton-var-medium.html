Codiaeum chrysosticton var. medium

Codiaeum chrysosticton var. medium, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage that displays shades of green, yellow, and orange. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture that enhances their ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its coloration.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
