Cordia senegalensis var. pelida

Cordia senegalensis var. pelida, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive white, fluffy flowers that appear in clusters, giving it a snowball-like appearance. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry or semi-arid climates.
