Chirita monantha

Chirita monantha, commonly known as the single-flower chirita, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its most distinctive characteristic is the solitary, showy flower that blooms in shades of yellow or orange.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
