Cynorkis × ranaivosonii

Cynorkis × ranaivosonii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a hybrid plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native habitat. Its flowers are often fragrant and come in a variety of hues, including yellow, orange, and red.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
