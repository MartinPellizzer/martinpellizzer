Cotyledon virgata

Cotyledon virgata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to full sun. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
