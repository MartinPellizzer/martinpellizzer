Cyclanthera elegans var. genuina

Cyclanthera elegans var. genuina, commonly known as the elegant snakefruit, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and heart-shaped, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into elongated, fleshy fruits resembling small melons. The fruit is typically green when unripe and turns yellow or orange when mature.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
