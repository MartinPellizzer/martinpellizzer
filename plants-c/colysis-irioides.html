Colysis irioides

Colysis irioides, commonly known as the iridescence orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, iridescent petals that display a spectrum of colors under different light conditions. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms that appear during the spring season. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and a complex root system adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
