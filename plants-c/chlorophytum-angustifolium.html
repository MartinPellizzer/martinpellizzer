Chlorophytum angustifolium

Chlorophytum angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features long, slender leaves that are typically green with white stripes running along their length. The plant produces small, grass-like inflorescences that bear white or pale green flowers. It is well adapted to grow in a variety of soil types and thrives in partial to full sunlight.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
