Cordia Grandiflora

Codia grandiflora is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant flowers and unique appearance. Native to regions in South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of sunlight. Its large, showy blooms come in shades of red, pink, and white, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. The plant's leaves are broad and glossy, adding to its visual appeal. Despite its beauty, Cordia grandiflora requires careful cultivation to flourish in non-native environments.
