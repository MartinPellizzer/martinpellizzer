Ceropegia simplex

Ceropegia simplex, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent with trailing stems that produce heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or white. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia simplex is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
