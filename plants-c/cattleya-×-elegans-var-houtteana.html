Cattleya × elegans var. houtteana

Cattleya × elegans var. houtteana, commonly known as the Houtteana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant typically features long, arching stems with clusters of showy, pendulous blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple. It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in sections with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
