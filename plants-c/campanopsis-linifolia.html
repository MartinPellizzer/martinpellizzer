Campanopsis linifolia

Campanopsis linifolia, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Campanopsis linifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
