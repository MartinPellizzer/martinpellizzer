Ceropegia hardyi

Ceropegia hardyi, commonly known as the hardy string of hearts, is a climbing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Ceropegia hardyi is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
