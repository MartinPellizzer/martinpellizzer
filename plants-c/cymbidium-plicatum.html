Cymbidium plicatum

Cymbidium plicatum, commonly known as the pleated orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its unique, pleated leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with numerous fragrant, lance-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium plicatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
