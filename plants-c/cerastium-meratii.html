Cerastium meratii

Cerastium meratii, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. Cerastium meratii thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.
