Campanula stellarioides

Campanula stellarioides, commonly known as star-like bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, erect stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers with five pointed lobes. The flowers are typically blue to violet in color and bloom from late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
