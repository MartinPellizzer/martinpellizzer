Cliffortia polygonifolia var. ternata

Cliffortia polygonifolia var. ternata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are typically tubular in shape and attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
