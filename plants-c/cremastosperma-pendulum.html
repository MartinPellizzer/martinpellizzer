Cremastosperma pendulum

Cremastosperma pendulum, commonly known as the hanging false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, hanging stems with small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow and are followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods that aid in wind dispersal.

Cremastosperma pendulum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
