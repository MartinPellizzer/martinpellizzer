Cypella geniculata

Cypella geniculata, commonly known as the slender cypella, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Cypella geniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
