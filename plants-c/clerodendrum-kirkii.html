Clerodendrum kirkii

Clerodendrum kirkii, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, green, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.
