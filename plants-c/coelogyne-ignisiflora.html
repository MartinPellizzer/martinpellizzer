Coelogyne ignisiflora

Coelogyne ignisiflora, commonly known as the fire-flower orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne ignisiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
