Chuanxinia microcarpa

Chuanxinia microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited chuanxinia, is a member of the Rosaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and small mammals. Native to the mountainous regions of China, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
