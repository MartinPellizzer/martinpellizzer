Croton meeboldianus

Croton meeboldianus, commonly known as the Meeboldian croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from red to orange and yellow hues. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
