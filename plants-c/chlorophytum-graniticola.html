Chlorophytum graniticola

Chlorophytum graniticola, commonly known as the granite plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
