Crassula micrantha

Crassula micrantha, commonly known as the dwarf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Crassula micrantha is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
