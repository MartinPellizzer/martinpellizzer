Cascarilla Macrocarpa

Cascarilla macrocarpa is a unique plant known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. It thrives in specific tropical environments, often found in coastal regions with sandy soils. The plant's most notable feature is its large, showy flowers that bloom in vibrant colors. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and importance of this fascinating species.
