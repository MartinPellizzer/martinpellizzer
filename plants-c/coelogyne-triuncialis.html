Coelogyne triuncialis

Coelogyne triuncialis, commonly known as the fragrant orchid or the white orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spicy scent. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne triuncialis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
