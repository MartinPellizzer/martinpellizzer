Cattleya peetersii

Cattleya peetersii, commonly known as the Peeters' orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with intricate patterns. The plant has broad, elliptical leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya peetersii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and fragrance.
