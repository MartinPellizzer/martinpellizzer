Capillipedium kuoi

Capillipedium kuoi, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, velvety green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid soil.

Capillipedium kuoi is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
