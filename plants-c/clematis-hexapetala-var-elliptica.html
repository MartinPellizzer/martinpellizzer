Clematis hexapetala var. elliptica

Clematis hexapetala var. elliptica, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue, with five to six petals. The leaves are elliptical, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.
