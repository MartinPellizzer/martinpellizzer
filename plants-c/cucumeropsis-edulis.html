Cucumeropsis edulis

Cucumeropsis edulis, commonly known as the edible cucumber plant, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to cucumbers, which are typically harvested when immature. Its fruits are used in various culinary applications, including salads and pickling.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
