Castilleja Fissifolia Subsp. Nubigena

Castilleja fissifolia subsp. nubigena is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high elevations of the western United States. It thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in montane meadows and forested areas. This subspecies is characterized by its vibrant red and orange blooms, which appear in late spring. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this unique and ecologically important plant.
