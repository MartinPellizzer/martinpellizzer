Chaenomeles maulei f. alba

Chaenomeles maulei f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered Chinese quince, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant produces small, yellow, edible fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
