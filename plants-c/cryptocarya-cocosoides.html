Cryptocarya cocosoides

Cryptocarya cocosoides, commonly known as the coconut cryptocarya, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and fragrant flowers. Its fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in some cultures.
