Conophytum indutum

Conophytum indutum, commonly known as the "Laceleaf Stonecrop," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. Conophytum indutum is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
