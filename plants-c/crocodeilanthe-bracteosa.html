Crocodeilanthe bracteosa

Crocodeilanthe bracteosa, commonly known as the bracteosa fern, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant produces small, inconspicuous spore-bearing structures called sporangia on the underside of its leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
