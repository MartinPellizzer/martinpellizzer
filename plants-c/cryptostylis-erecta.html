Cryptostylis erecta

Cryptostylis erecta, commonly known as the erect cryptostylis, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, silky hair.

Cryptostylis erecta is known for its ability to survive in harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient addition to native plant collections.
