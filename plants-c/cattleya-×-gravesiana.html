Cattleya × gravesiana

Cattleya × gravesiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a captivating species within the Cattleya genus.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The leaves are broad and strap-like, typically arranged in a fan shape.

It is prized in horticulture for its beauty and fragrance, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
