Calathea lindbergii

Calathea lindbergii, commonly known as the Lindberg's prayer plant, is a tropical foliage plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a combination of green, silver, and pink hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value and unique leaf patterns. Calathea lindbergii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its foliage changes color throughout the day, resembling a prayerful motion, hence its common name.
