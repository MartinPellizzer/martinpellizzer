Cupressus sempervirens var. stricta

Cupressus sempervirens var. stricta, commonly known as the Italian cypress, is a cultivar of the Mediterranean cypress.

It is characterized by its narrow, columnar form and dark green, scale-like foliage. This plant is evergreen and thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. It is often used in landscaping for its formal appearance and drought tolerance.

The variety is notable for its slow growth rate and longevity, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
