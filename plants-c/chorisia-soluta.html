Chorisia soluta

It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds with tufts of silky hair, aiding in wind dispersal.

Chorisia soluta is well adapted to drought conditions and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy and ornamental plant in xeriscaped gardens.
