Chamguava gentlei

Chamguava gentlei, commonly known as the silverleaf guava, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry with a sweet and aromatic flavor.

Chamguava gentlei is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit.
