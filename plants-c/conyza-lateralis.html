Conyza lateralis

Conyza lateralis, commonly known as the slenderleaf horseweed, is an annual flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in a variety of habitats such as roadsides and fields. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Conyza lateralis is known for its ability to thrive in a wide range of environmental conditions.
