Calathea mirabilis

Calathea mirabilis, commonly known as the rainbow calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns of green, pink, and white. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often featuring a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
