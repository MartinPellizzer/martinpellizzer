Cassia chamaecrista var. diffusa

Cassia chamaecrista var. diffusa, commonly known as yellow cassia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
