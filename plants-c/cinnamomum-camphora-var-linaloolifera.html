Cinnamomum camphora var. linaloolifera

Cinnamomum camphora var. linaloolifera, commonly known as the Chinese camphor tree, is a species native to East Asia.

It is a large evergreen tree that can grow up to 30 meters tall, featuring thick, rough bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant is known for producing a valuable essential oil rich in linalool, which is used in perfumery and medicine.

Its wood is also valued for its aromatic properties and durability, making it a significant plant in traditional practices.
