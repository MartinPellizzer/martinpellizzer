Crataegus monogyna var. azarella

Crataegus monogyna var. azarella, commonly known as the Azarolus hawthorn, is a variety of the common hawthorn.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features thorny branches and clusters of white or pink flowers in spring. Its berries are typically red or orange, providing food for birds and other wildlife.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
