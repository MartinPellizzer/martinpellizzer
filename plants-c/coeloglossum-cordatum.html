Coeloglossum cordatum

Coeloglossum cordatum, commonly known as the heartleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Coeloglossum cordatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
