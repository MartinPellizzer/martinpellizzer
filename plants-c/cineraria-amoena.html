Cineraria amoena

Cineraria amoena, commonly known as the velvetleaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-green, velvety leaves and produces daisy-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cineraria amoena is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.
