Coppensia × francacesari

Coppensia × francacesari, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between *Coppensia frutescens* and *Coppensia cerasifera*.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The leaves are usually ovate or elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface depending on the cultivar.
