Cissus sicyoides f. oblongolanceolata

Cissus sicyoides f. oblongolanceolata, commonly known as the oblong-lanceolate cissus, is a variant of the climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped to ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a deep green color. The leaves are typically oblong-lanceolate in shape, giving the plant its specific epithet. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by edible, citrus-scented berries.

This plant thrives in humid environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.
