Chrysosplenium aurantium

Chrysosplenium aurantium, commonly known as golden spleenwort, is a member of the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a small evergreen perennial plant characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the spring and early summer.

Native to moist, shaded habitats in temperate regions, it is often found in rocky or woodland soils.
