Cyathea yambrasensis

Cyathea yambrasensis, commonly known as the Yambrasensis tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cyathea yambrasensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
