Cyrtochilum orthotis

Cyrtochilum orthotis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, showy flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow or greenish-white blooms that are often fragrant. Cyrtochilum orthotis thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it a distinctive species within the orchid genus.
