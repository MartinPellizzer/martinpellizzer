Cytisus lasiosemius

Cytisus lasiosemius, commonly known as the woolly broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry stems covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, with a slightly hairy surface.

Cytisus lasiosemius is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
