Cyrtanthus inaequalis

Cyrtanthus inaequalis, commonly known as the unequal bell-shaped orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and red. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Cyrtanthus inaequalis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
