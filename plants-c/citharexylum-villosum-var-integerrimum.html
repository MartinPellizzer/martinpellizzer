Citharexylum villosum var. integerrimum

Citharexylum villosum var. integerrimum, commonly known as the silky cithara, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features soft, hairy leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

The variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
