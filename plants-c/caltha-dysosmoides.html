Caltha dysosmoides

Caltha dysosmoides, commonly known as the yellow marsh marigold, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered in a fine layer of silky hairs.
