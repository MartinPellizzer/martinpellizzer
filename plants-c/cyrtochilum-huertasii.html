Cyrtochilum huertasii

Cyrtochilum huertasii, commonly known as the Huerta's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive three-lobed structure.

Cyrtochilum huertasii is a rare and endemic species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
