Caladium griseoargenteum

Caladium griseoargenteum, commonly known as the silver-veined caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with striking silver and cream veins against a deep green background. The plant thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant, decorative appearance.

Caladium griseoargenteum is relatively low maintenance but requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
