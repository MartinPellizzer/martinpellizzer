Conyza dilatata

Conyza dilatata, commonly known as the common ragweed, is an annual flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like leaves and yellow flowers that appear in the summer months. The plant is native to North America and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It is known for producing a large amount of pollen, which can contribute to seasonal allergies.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to spread easily across various habitats.
