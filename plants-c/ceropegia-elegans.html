Ceropegia elegans

Ceropegia elegans, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a trailing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or pink markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which are pollinated by specific insects. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia elegans is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
