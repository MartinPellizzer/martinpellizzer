Cypripedium flavum

Cypripedium flavum, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the northeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow pouch-shaped flower, which is unique among orchids. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in nutrient-rich, well-drained soils. It has a long blooming period, usually from late spring to early summer.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often protected in its natural habitat.
