Cryptocarya glabriflora

Cryptocarya glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The fruit is a small, woody drupe that matures to a dark color, containing a single seed.
