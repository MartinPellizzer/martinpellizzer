Callista sinuata

Callista sinuata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Callista sinuata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
