Cattleya gaskelliana var. amabilis

Cattleya gaskelliana var. amabilis, commonly known as the Gaskell's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

This variety is prized for its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant mix of pink, white, and purple hues. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. The plant produces fragrant blooms that can last several weeks, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its unique coloration and elegant form contribute to its status as a sought-after ornamental plant.
