Clematis Dioica Var. Brasiliana

Clematis dioica var. brasiliana is a striking climbing vine native to the rainforests of South America. Known for its vibrant, showy flowers and lush foliage, it adds dramatic visual interest to gardens and natural landscapes. This variety is prized for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its unique floral structure features both male and female flowers on separate plants, showcasing the plant's dioecious nature. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure optimal growth and flowering.
