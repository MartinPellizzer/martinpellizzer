Cascabela ovata

Cascabela ovata, commonly known as the round-fruit cascabela, is a member of the Loganiaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval, yellow fruits that turn orange when ripe. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.
