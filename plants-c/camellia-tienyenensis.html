Camellia tienyenensis

Camellia tienyenensis, commonly known as the Tienyen camellia, is a species native to southern China.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. Camellia tienyenensis thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is valued in horticulture for its winter blooming habit and aesthetic appeal.
