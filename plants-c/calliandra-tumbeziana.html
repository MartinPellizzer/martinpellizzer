Calliandra tumbeziana

Calliandra tumbeziana, commonly known as the Tumbes calliandra, is a shrub native to coastal regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences composed of bright red or orange tubular flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
