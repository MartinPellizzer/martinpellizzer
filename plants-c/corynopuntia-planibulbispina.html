Corynopuntia planibulbispina

Corynopuntia planibulbispina, commonly known as the flat-bulb spine cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its flat, bulbous stems that are covered in spines, which help to reduce water loss and protect against herbivores. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in hot, dry climates. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, and they appear during the warmer months.

Corynopuntia planibulbispina is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for xeriscaping and desert gardens.
