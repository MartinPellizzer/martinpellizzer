Capsella ramselensis

Capsella ramselensis, commonly known as the slender weed, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. Native to certain regions of Africa, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces small, rounded seed pods that release minute seeds when mature.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
