Crocanthus mossambicensis

Crocanthus mossambicensis, commonly known as the Madagascar star moss, is a unique species of moss found primarily in the tropical regions of Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Pottiaceae and is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form. The plant exhibits a dark green to brownish coloration, with small, tightly packed leaves that form a mat on the forest floor. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
