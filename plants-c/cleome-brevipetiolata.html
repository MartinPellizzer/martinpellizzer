Cleome brevipetiolata

Cleome brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-petioled cleome, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The leaves are simple, ovate, and have a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a suitable plant for xeriscaping.

This species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
