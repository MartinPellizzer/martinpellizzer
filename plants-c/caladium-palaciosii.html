Caladium palaciosii

Caladium palaciosii, commonly known as the Palacios' arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium palaciosii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
