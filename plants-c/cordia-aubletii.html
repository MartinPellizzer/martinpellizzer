Cordia aubletii

Cordia aubletii, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
