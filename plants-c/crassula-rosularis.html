Crassula rosularis

Crassula rosularis, commonly known as the Rosular Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with fleshy, slightly ribbed leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
