Coelogyne mindanaensis

Coelogyne mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao coelogyne, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a pseudobulb with elongated leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, often in clusters of 5 to 10 flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
