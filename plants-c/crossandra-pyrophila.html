Crossandra pyrophila

Crossandra pyrophila, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Crossandra pyrophila is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
