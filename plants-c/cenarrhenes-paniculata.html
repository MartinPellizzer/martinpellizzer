Cenarrhenes paniculata

Cenarrhenes paniculata, commonly known as the golden paniculata, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, branched inflorescences that produce clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, sunny habitats.
