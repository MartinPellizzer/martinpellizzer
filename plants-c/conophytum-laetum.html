Conophytum laetum

Conophytum laetum, commonly known as the "sun orchid," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, leaf-like structures that store water. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.
