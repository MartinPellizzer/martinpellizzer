Cymbonotus lawsonianus

Cymbonotus lawsonianus, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that emit a faint golden hue when exposed to light. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cymbonotus lawsonianus is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
