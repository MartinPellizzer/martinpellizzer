Clarkia Rubicunda Subsp. Blasdalei

Blasdale's clarkia, scientifically known as Clarkia rubicunda subsp. blasdalei, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California. This subspecies is distinguished by its vibrant red petals and slender stems, which bloom during the spring and early summer. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in open habitats along the coastline. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and human disturbance. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant for future generations.
