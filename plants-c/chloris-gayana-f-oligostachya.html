Chloris gayana f. oligostachya

Chloris gayana f. oligostachya, commonly known as slender grass or slender Mitchell grass, is a variant of the Mitchell grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit and is often found in grassland and woodland habitats. The plant has a tufted appearance with dense foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry and semi-arid conditions, making it a valuable component of native vegetation in these regions.

Its botanical features make it an important forage plant for livestock in certain areas of Australia.
