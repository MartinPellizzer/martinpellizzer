Coleus huberi

Coleus huberi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of purple, white, and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Coleus huberi is appreciated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
