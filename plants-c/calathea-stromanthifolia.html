Calathea stromanthifolia

Calathea stromanthifolia, commonly known as the Stromantia calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns and variegated leaf surfaces. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture that enhances their visual appeal. This plant thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
