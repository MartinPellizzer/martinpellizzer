Cheilanthes × insularis

Cheilanthes × insularis, commonly known as the island shield fern, is a hybrid species within the polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that are typically gray-green in color. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
