Caladium utile

Caladium utile, commonly known as the arrowleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrow-like leaves that come in shades of green, red, and white. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Caladium utile is typically grown as an annual in temperate regions but can be perennial in its native habitat.
