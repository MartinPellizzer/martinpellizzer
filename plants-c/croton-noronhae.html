Croton noronhae

Croton noronhae, commonly known as the Brazilian croton, is a tropical shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red. The plant produces small, greenish flowers followed by distinctive, spiny fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
