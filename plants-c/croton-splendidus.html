Croton splendidus

Croton splendidus, commonly known as the splendid croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and sometimes toxic species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Croton splendidus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.
