Codiaeum variegatum f. lobatum

Codiaeum variegatum f. lobatum, commonly known as the flame maple or crocodile plant, is a striking ornamental foliage plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that display shades of red, orange, yellow, and green, often with a variegated pattern. The plant has heart-shaped, lobed leaves that are glossy and can grow quite large, making it a popular choice for indoor or outdoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among plant enthusiasts for its visual appeal and ease of care.
