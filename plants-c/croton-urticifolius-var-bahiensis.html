Croton urticifolius var. bahiensis

Croton urticifolius var. bahiensis, commonly known as the Brazilian croton, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its hairy, oval-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded areas.

The species is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
