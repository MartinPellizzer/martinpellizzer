Cousinia stroterolepis

Cousinia stroterolepis, commonly known as the Stroterolepis gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

Cousinia stroterolepis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in mountain ecosystems.
