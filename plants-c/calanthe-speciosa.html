Calanthe speciosa

Calanthe speciosa, commonly known as the showy calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant produces striking, often brightly colored blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are usually broad and arranged in a rosette pattern at the base of the plant.

Calanthe speciosa is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its beauty.
