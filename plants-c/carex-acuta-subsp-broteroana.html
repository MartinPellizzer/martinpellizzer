Carex acuta subsp. broteroana

Carex acuta subsp.

broteroana, commonly known as the acute sedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems.
