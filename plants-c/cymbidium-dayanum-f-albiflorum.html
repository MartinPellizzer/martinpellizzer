Cymbidium dayanum f. albiflorum

Cymbidium dayanum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a unique variant of the Cymbidium genus native to China.

It is characterized by its striking white blooms, which contrast sharply with its dark green, strap-like leaves. This orchid thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant appearance.

Its flowers typically bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a touch of beauty during the colder months.
