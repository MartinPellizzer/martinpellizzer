Citrus × bigaradia var. mammillata

Citrus × bigaradia var. mammillata, commonly known as the rough lemon.

This variety is characterized by its thick, bumpy rind and small, sour fruit. It is a hybrid citrus plant, originating from a cross between the citron and the mandarin. The leaves are dark green and slightly hairy, while the fruit has a high acidity and is often used in culinary applications.

It is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and hardiness.
