Cynanchum maccartii

Cynanchum maccartii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
