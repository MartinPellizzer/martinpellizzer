Cattleya velutina var. lietzei

Cattleya velutina var. lietzei, commonly known as the Lietzei Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, showy flowers.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and striking, fragrant blooms. The flowers typically feature a mix of pink, white, and purple hues, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. This variety is known for its vigorous growth and ability to produce multiple blooms per inflorescence.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
