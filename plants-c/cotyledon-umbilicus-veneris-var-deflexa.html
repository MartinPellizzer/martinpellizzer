Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. deflexa

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. deflexa, commonly known as the "moonstone plant," is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with a waxy texture. The leaves are often curled or crinkled, giving the plant a unique, almost sculptural appearance. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with minimal water.
