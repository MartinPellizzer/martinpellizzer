Clematis gagnepaniana

Clematis gagnepaniana, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves, and it thrives in partial shade to full sun.

Clematis gagnepaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
