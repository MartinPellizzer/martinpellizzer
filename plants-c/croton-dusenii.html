Croton dusenii

Croton dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to yellow and red. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, spiny capsules containing seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
