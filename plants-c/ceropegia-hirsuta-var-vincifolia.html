Ceropegia hirsuta var. vincifolia

Ceropegia hirsuta var. vincifolia, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features distinctive, waxy, silver-gray leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
