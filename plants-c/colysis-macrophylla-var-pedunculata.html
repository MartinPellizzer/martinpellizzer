Colysis macrophylla var. pedunculata

Colysis macrophylla var. pedunculata, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, showy inflorescences.

It features palmately compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that provide a lush green canopy. The plant produces clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers, which are borne on long, pendulous peduncles. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is notable for its rapid growth and ability to adapt to various soil types.
