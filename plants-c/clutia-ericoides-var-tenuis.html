Clutia ericoides var. tenuis

Clutia ericoides var. tenuis, commonly known as slender clutia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded habitats.
