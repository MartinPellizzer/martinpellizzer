Clethra brasiliensis

Clethra brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian clethra, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Clethraceae and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a glossy appearance.

Clethra brasiliensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
