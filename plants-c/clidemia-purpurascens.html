Clidemia purpurascens

Clidemia purpurascens, commonly known as the purpleleaf mallow, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that range in color from dark green to reddish-purple. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Clidemia purpurascens is also known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
