Cypripedium × fascinatum

Cypripedium × fascinatum, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow pouch-shaped lip, which is a key feature for attracting pollinators. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, loamy soils. Its flowers are usually borne on tall, slender stems and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Cypripedium × fascinatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
