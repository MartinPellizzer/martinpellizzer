Conophytum glabrum

Conophytum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth conophytum, is a member of the Aizoaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is a small, succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, paired leaves that form a rosette shape. Conophytum glabrum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
