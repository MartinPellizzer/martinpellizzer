Cotoneaster kongboensis

Cotoneaster kongboensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are clustered in groups of three, which are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Cotoneaster kongboensis typically thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its adaptability and winter interest make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, low-maintenance plants.
