Cyclosorus cyatheoides

Cyclosorus cyatheoides, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. Its fronds are large, pinnate, and often arching, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Cyclosorus cyatheoides is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in various cultures.
