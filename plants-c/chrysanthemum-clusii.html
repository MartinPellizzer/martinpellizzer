Chrysanthemum clusii

Chrysanthemum clusii, commonly known as the African chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to autumn.
