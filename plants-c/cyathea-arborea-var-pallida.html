Cyathea arborea var. pallida

Cyathea arborea var. pallida, commonly known as the pale tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and longevity. Its fronds are typically a pale greenish-white color, giving it its common name.

This species is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and mountainous areas.
