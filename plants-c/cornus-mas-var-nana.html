Cornus mas var. nana

Cornus mas var. nana, commonly known as dwarf cornel.

This variety is a compact shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its small, rounded form. It features small, white to pinkish flowers that appear in early spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness, thriving in a variety of soil conditions.

It is often used in gardens for its seasonal interest and adaptability to different climates.
