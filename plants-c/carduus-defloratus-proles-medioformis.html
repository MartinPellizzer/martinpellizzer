Carduus defloratus proles medioformis

Carduus defloratus proles medioformis, commonly known as the medium-formed common thistle.

This plant is a perennial species belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. It typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant has a robust root system and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

It is often found in grasslands, roadsides, and agricultural fields.
