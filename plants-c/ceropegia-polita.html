Ceropegia polita

Ceropegia polita, commonly known as the African violet or velvet leaf.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Ceropegia polita is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
