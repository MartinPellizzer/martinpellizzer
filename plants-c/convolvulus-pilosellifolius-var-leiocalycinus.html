Convolvulus pilosellifolius var. leiocalycinus

Convolvulus pilosellifolius var. leiocalycinus, commonly known as the silky leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The flowers are typically pale blue to purple in color, with five petals and a distinctive spurred structure. It is native to regions of southern Africa and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.

This plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
