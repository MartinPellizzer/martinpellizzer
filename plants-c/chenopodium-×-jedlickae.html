Chenopodium × jedlickae

Chenopodium × jedlickae, commonly known as the hybrid goosefoot, is a species that results from the cross between Chenopodium album and Chenopodium berlandieri.

It is an annual herbaceous plant characterized by its alternate, lobed leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in various habitats such as fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

It is known for its edible seeds, which have been used historically in some cultures for food and medicinal purposes.
