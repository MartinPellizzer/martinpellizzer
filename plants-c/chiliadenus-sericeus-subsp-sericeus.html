Chiliadenus sericeus subsp. sericeus

Chiliadenus sericeus subsp.

sericeus, commonly known as the silky chiliadenus, is a low-growing shrub native to South Africa. It features slender, hair-covered stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine silvery down.

The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
