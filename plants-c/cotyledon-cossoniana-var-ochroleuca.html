Cotyledon cossoniana var. ochroleuca

Cotyledon cossoniana var. ochroleuca, commonly known as the White-veined Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with white or pale yellow veins. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
