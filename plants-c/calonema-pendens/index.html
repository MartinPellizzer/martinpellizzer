Calonema Pendens

Calonema pendens is a unique and striking plant known for its cascading, fleshy leaves that dangle elegantly from its stems. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments where it can flourish without direct sunlight. This plant is popular among indoor gardeners due to its low maintenance and eye-catching appearance. Its ability to grow in hanging baskets or as a trailing plant makes it a versatile addition to any space. With proper care, Calonema pendens can bring a touch of natural beauty and tranquility to any home or office setting.
