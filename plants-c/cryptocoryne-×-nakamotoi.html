Cryptocoryne × nakamotoi

Cryptocoryne × nakamotoi, commonly known as the Japanese cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that often feature a striking red or purple midrib. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is popular among aquarium enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers soft, slightly acidic water and can grow in both planted and floating forms.

Cryptocoryne × nakamotoi is known for its resilience and ease of care, making it a favored choice for beginners in the hobby.
