Campanula schimperi

Campanula schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in rocky or open habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Campanula schimperi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
