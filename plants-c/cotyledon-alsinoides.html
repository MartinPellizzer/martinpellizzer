Cotyledon alsinoides

Cotyledon alsinoides, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically silver or blue-green in color, covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, which attract pollinators like bees. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to many succulent collections.
