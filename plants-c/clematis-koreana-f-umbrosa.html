Clematis koreana f. umbrosa

Clematis koreana f. umbrosa, commonly known as the Korean shade clematis, is a unique cultivar of the Clematis koreana species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to moist, well-drained soils.
