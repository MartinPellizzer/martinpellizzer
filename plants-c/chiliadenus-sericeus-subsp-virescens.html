Chiliadenus sericeus subsp. virescens

Chiliadenus sericeus subsp.

virescens, commonly known as the silverleaf chiliadenus, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa. It features silver-gray, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique texture and color contrast.
