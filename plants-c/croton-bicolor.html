Croton bicolor

Croton bicolor, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with shades of green, yellow, red, and orange. The leaves are typically large, ovate, and have a rough texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color.

This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
