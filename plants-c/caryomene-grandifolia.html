Caryomene grandifolia

Caryomene grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered caryomene, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, waxy texture.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
