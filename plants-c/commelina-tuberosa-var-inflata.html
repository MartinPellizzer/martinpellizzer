Commelina tuberosa var. inflata

Commelina tuberosa var. inflata, commonly known as the swollen arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and small, blue or violet flowers. The plant produces tubers underground, which serve as storage organs for nutrients. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
