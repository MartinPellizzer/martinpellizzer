Clerodendrum neriifolium

Clerodendrum neriifolium, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often brightly colored in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
