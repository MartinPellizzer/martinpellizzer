Cladomyza multinervis

Cladomyza multinervis, commonly known as the fine-veined fern, is a species of fern characterized by its intricate and delicate leaf structure.

Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with finely divided pinnae that give the plant a lacy appearance. The plant exhibits a complex vascular system that supports its growth in moist, shaded environments. Cladomyza multinervis is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological studies.
