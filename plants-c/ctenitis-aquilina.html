Ctenitis aquilina

Ctenitis aquilina, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

Ctenitis aquilina is an important component of its ecosystem, providing habitat and contributing to the forest's biodiversity.
