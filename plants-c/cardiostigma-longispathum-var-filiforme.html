Cardiostigma longispathum var. filiforme

Cardiostigma longispathum var. filiforme, commonly known as the slender-heart grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple and are visited by various pollinators.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
