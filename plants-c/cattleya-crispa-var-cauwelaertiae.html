Cattleya crispa var. cauwelaertiae

Cattleya crispa var. cauwelaertiae, commonly known as the Caewelaertia Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow with deep red or orange markings. The plant has pendulous, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
