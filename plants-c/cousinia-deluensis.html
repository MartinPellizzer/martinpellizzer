Cousinia deluensis

Cousinia deluensis, commonly known as Deluens carpet grass, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers. It is adapted to harsh environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and efficient water use.

Cousinia deluensis plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest in ecological and botanical studies.
