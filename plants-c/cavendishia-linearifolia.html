Cavendishia linearifolia

Cavendishia linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf cactus, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and adaptability to arid conditions.

Cavendishia linearifolia thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
