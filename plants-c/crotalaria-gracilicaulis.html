Crotalaria gracilicaulis

Crotalaria gracilicaulis, commonly known as slender stem crotalaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stem and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Crotalaria gracilicaulis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
