Croton flavens f. richardii

Croton flavens f. richardii, commonly known as the richardii croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display vibrant yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its foliage is a key feature, making it popular as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
