Cattleya elatior

Cattleya elatior, commonly known as the large flowered Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter. The plant typically has broad, leathery leaves and grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya elatior is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for both greenhouse cultivation and floral arrangements.
