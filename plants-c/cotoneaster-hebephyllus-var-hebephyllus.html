Cotoneaster hebephyllus var. hebephyllus

Cotoneaster hebephyllus var. hebephyllus, commonly known as the small-leaved cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are usually dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

The species is adapted to a range of soil types and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
