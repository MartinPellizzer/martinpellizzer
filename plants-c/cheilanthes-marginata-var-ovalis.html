Cheilanthes marginata var. ovalis

Cheilanthes marginata var. ovalis, commonly known as the oval lady's tresses, is a terrestrial fern that thrives in rocky and arid environments.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features dark green, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a distinctive margin that is often curled or rolled. It produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its fronds.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to harsh conditions and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
