Comparettia brevis

Comparettia brevis, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

The flowers, which bloom in shades of white and yellow, are relatively small but have a delicate, spicy aroma.
