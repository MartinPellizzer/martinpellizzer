Coryphantha muehlenpfordtii subsp. robustispina

Coryphantha muehlenpfordtii subsp.

robustispina, commonly known as the robust spiny cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico. It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and dense spines that provide protection from herbivores.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for desert environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
