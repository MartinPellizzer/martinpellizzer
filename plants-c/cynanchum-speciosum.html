Cynanchum speciosum

Cynanchum speciosum, commonly known as the showy milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes. Its leaves are opposite and pinnately divided, while its stems are often hairy.

Cynanchum speciosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
