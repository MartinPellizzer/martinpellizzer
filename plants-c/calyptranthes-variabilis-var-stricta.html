Calyptranthes variabilis var. stricta

Calyptranthes variabilis var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved calyptranthes, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

The leaves are often aromatic and have a slightly hairy texture, which is a common trait among plants in the Myrtaceae family.
