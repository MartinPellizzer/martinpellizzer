Chlorophytum fibrosum

Chlorophytum fibrosum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with green and white or yellow markings. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal houseplant. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Chlorophytum fibrosum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, as it can remove toxins from indoor environments.
