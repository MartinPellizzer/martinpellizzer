Clintonia nutans var. macrostema

Clintonia nutans var. macrostema, commonly known as the large-stemmed nodding white-flowered plant, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, grass-like leaves and showy, nodding white flowers. The varietal name "macrostema" refers to its relatively large floral stems compared to the typical variety.
