Centaurea psammogena

Centaurea psammogena, commonly known as the sand centaury, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. It is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.

Centaurea psammogena is often found in coastal dunes and open grasslands.
