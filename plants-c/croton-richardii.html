Croton richardii

Croton richardii, commonly known as the Richard's croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that often display shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Croton richardii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
