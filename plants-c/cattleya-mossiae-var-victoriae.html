Cattleya mossiae var. victoriae

Cattleya mossiae var. victoriae, commonly known as the Victoria Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, showy flowers.

It features vibrant purple and white petals with a distinctive yellow lip, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.

The Victoria Cattleya is also known for its strong fragrance, which adds to its ornamental appeal.
