Centaurea svanetica

Centaurea svanetica, commonly known as the Svaneti centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous areas. It produces vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

Its slender stems and narrow leaves are adapted to the harsh, high-altitude environments where it thrives.
