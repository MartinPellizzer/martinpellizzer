Clitoria hanceana var. thailandica

Clitoria hanceana var. thailandica, commonly known as the Thai butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that resemble butterflies. The plant is typically shrub-like, with trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes or traditional herbal uses.

The flowers are also used in food coloring and traditional medicine.
