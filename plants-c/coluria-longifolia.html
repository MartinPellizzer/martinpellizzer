Coluria longifolia

Coluria longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf coluria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

Coluria longifolia is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its distinctive foliage and resilience to harsh conditions.
