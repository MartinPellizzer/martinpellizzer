Chionanthus diversifolius

Chionanthus diversifolius, commonly known as the variableleaf fringeflower, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant white flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and often variable in shape leaves, which contribute to its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
