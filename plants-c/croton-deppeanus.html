Croton deppeanus

Croton deppeanus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, lobed leaves that range in color from green to red, depending on the season and light exposure. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.

Croton deppeanus has also been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
