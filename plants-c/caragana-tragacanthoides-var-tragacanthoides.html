Caragana tragacanthoides var. tragacanthoides

Caragana tragacanthoides var. tragacanthoides, commonly known as the Turk's Turban, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with small, oval leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers in early summer, followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

Its root system is deep, allowing it to thrive in poor soils and harsh climates.
