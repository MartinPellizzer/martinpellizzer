Chlorophytum pilosissimum

Chlorophytum pilosissimum, commonly known as the curly spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, curly, silver-green leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on long, slender stems during the spring and summer months. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum pilosissimum is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
