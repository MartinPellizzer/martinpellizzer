Chlorophytum stenopetalum var. stenopetalum

Chlorophytum stenopetalum var. stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features linear, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green with a silvery-blue hue on the underside. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers arranged in loose clusters during the summer months. It is a hardy species that thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
