Centaurea limbata proles venusta

Centaurea limbata proles venusta, commonly known as the showy centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, bright blue to purple flower heads that are often adorned with white or yellow rays. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
