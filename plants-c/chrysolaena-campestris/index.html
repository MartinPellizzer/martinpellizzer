Chrysolaena Campestris

Chrysolaena campestris is a hardy shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is known for its vibrant yellow leaves that add a striking visual contrast to any landscape. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for garden borders and container planting. With low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
