Crepis klikitatensis

Crepis klikitatensis, commonly known as the Klikitat thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to western North America, particularly found in the Pacific Northwest region. The plant is characterized by its prickly leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
