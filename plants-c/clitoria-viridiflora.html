Clitoria viridiflora

Clitoria viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered bean or green orchid, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, butterfly-like blue or violet flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Clitoria viridiflora is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
