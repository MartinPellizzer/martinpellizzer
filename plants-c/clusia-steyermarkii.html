Clusia steyermarkii

Clusia steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's glorybower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its evergreen nature and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are often surrounded by bright red bracts, making it visually striking. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant floral display.

Clusia steyermarkii prefers warm, humid climates and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
