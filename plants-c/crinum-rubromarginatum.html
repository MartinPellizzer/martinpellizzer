Crinum rubromarginatum

Crinum rubromarginatum, commonly known as the red-marginated crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, with distinctive red margins on the petals. The plant produces a tall, slender stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms, which appear in late summer to early autumn.
