Calophyllum elegans

Calophyllum elegans, commonly known as the elegant calophyllum, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and large, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody, orange-red fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
