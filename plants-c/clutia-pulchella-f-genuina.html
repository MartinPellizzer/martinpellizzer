Clutia pulchella f. genuina

Clutia pulchella f. genuina, commonly known as the white clutia, is a rare and striking variant of the clutia plant.

It is characterized by its pale white or cream-colored flowers, which contrast with its glossy, dark green leaves. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, tropical environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded gardens and as a houseplant.

Its unique coloration makes it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts.
