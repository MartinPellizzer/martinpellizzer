Cyathea bontocensis

Cyathea bontocensis, commonly known as the Bontoc tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in humid and tropical climates. Its rhizome system allows it to anchor itself firmly in the soil while absorbing nutrients from decomposing organic matter.

Cyathea bontocensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to forest structure and providing shelter for various small organisms.
