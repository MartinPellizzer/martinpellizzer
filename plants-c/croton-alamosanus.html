Croton alamosanus

Croton alamosanus, commonly known as the Alamos croton, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful leaves that display a range of hues including yellow, red, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry climates.

Croton alamosanus is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental leaf structure.
