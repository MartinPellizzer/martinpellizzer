Clematis patens f. grandiflora

Clematis patens f. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered clematis, is a cultivar of the clematis plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and its ability to climb using its twining stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue, appearing in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is heart-shaped and provides a lush green backdrop to the blooms.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its visual impact.
