Cyrtandra argentata subsp. latifolia

Cyrtandra argentata subsp.

latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf silver heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink.
