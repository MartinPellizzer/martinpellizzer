Croton nervosus var. villosus

Croton nervosus var. villosus, commonly known as the hairy croton, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green hues. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, spiny capsules containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is also valued as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
