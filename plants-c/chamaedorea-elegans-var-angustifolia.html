Chamaedorea elegans var. angustifolia

Chamaedorea elegans var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bamboo palm, is a compact, evergreen plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features slender, upright stems that produce arching, feather-like fronds with narrow, dark green leaflets. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying properties. It has a slow growth rate and requires well-draining soil with regular but moderate watering.

Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for adding a touch of greenery to homes and offices.
