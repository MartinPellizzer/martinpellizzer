Clitoria speciosa

Clitoria speciosa, commonly known as the winged bean or the butterfly pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, butterfly-like purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, trifoliate leaves and produces edible pods containing small, nutritious beans. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.

Its rapid growth and nitrogen-fixing ability make it valuable in sustainable farming systems.
