Cycnoseris australis

Cycnoseris australis, commonly known as the Southern Cygnus Rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, often growing in dense clusters. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in spike-like inflorescences, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous texture that contributes to its resilience in arid conditions.

Cycnoseris australis is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or saline environments.
