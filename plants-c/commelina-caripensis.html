Commelina caripensis

Commelina caripensis, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking red flowers with a distinctive white throat. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks, preferring moist and shady environments. Commelina caripensis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
