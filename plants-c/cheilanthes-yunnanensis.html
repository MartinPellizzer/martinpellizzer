Cheilanthes yunnanensis

Cheilanthes yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan rock fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates, thriving in arid or semi-arid environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and leathery, with a distinctive, serrated margin.
