Citharexylum mocinoi f. williamsii

Citharexylum mocinoi f. williamsii, commonly known as the silverleaf citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs that give them their distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
