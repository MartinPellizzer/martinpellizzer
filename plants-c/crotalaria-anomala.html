Crotalaria anomala

Crotalaria anomala, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are dispersed by wind, contributing to its wide distribution.

Crotalaria anomala is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
