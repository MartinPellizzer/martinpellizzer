Clerodendrum colebrookeanum var. denticulatum

Clerodendrum colebrookeanum var. denticulatum, commonly known as the toothed clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that are often followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
