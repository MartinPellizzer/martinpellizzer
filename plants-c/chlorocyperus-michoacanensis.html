Chlorocyperus michoacanensis

Chlorocyperus michoacanensis, commonly known as the Michoacan green sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, commonly known as the sedge family. This species is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in environments with high humidity and saturated soil.

Chlorocyperus michoacanensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
