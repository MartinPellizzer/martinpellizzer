Crotalaria nummularia

Crotalaria nummularia, commonly known as the coinleaf false nectarine, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its small, coin-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

Crotalaria nummularia is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its potential health benefits.
