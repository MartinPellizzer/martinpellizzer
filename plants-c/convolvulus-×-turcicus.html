Convolvulus × turcicus

Convolvulus × turcicus, commonly known as Turkish bindweed, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Bindweed family (Convolvulaceae).

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is native to southern Europe and western Asia but has been introduced to other regions. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in gardens.

Due to its aggressive growth habit, it can become invasive in certain environments.
