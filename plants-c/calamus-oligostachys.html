Calamus oligostachys

Calamus oligostachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed rattan, is a climbing plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its long, slender stems and pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. Its inflorescences are clustered and bear small, inconspicuous flowers.

Calamus oligostachys is valued for its strong, flexible stems, which are used in handicrafts and as a source of rattan.
