Crassula deminuta

Crassula deminuta, commonly known as the baby tears plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, round leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Crassula deminuta is often grown as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and easy care requirements.
