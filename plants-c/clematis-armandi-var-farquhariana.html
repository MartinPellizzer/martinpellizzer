Clematis armandi var. farquhariana

Clematis armandi var. farquhariana, commonly known as the Farquharian clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, which are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to violet, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
