Cypripedium × gloriosum

Cypripedium × gloriosum, commonly known as the glory orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive lip that often displays intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and leathery, and it produces a single, upright stem with a single flower.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
