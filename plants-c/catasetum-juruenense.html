Catasetum juruenense

Catasetum juruenense, commonly known as the Jurua Catasetum, is a rare orchid species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers have a unique structure with specialized pollination mechanisms, including the presence of a pollinia.

Catasetum juruenense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
