Crassula capensis

Crassula capensis, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an easy-to-care-for ornamental plant.
