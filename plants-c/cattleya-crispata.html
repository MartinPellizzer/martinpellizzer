Cattleya crispata

Cattleya crispata, commonly known as the curly cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native environment.

Cattleya crispata is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
