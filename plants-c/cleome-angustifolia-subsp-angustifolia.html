Cleome angustifolia subsp. angustifolia

Cleome angustifolia subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf spiderflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the southern United States. It features slender, branching stems and small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. It is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
