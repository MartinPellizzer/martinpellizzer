Crassula sericea var. hottentotta

Crassula sericea var. hottentotta, commonly known as the silver crassula, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
