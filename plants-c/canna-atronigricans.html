Canna atronigricans

Canna atronigricans, commonly known as the black-cinnamon cannas, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, broad leaves that are dark green with a reddish tinge, and its flowers are typically orange or red, with a distinctive red throat. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and showy blooms in gardens and landscapes.

Canna atronigricans is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
