Crassula capitata

Crassula capitata, commonly known as the "Snowball Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, rounded leaves that form a rosette shape, giving it a compact and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
