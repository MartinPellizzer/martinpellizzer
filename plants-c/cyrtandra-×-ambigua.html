Cyrtandra × ambigua

Cyrtandra × ambigua, commonly known as the hybrid blue ginger, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra tenuifolia and Cyrtandra hirsuta. This plant is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and succulent, fleshy leaves. It is native to Hawaii and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
