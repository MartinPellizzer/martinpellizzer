Carex arnellii var. hondoensis

Carex arnellii var. hondoensis, commonly known as the Hondoensis sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense clumps, making it a popular choice for water garden edges or bog gardens.

The inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often reddish in color, adding ornamental value to the plant.
