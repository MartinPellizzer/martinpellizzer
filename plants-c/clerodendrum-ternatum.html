Clerodendrum ternatum

Clerodendrum ternatum, commonly known as the red hibiscus or Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers, which are often borne in clusters and resemble those of true hibiscus species. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
