Caragana gyirongensis

Caragana gyirongensis, commonly known as the Gyirong thorny shrub, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Tibet and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and small, compound leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers in spring, which are followed by pod fruits containing several seeds. It is well adapted to harsh, arid environments and is often found in rocky soils at high altitudes.

Due to its hardy nature, it plays a role in stabilizing soils and preventing erosion in its native habitat.
