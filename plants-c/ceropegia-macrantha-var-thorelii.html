Ceropegia macrantha var. thorelii

Ceropegia macrantha var. thorelii, commonly known as the Thorelii African Violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tuberous roots and produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a fine, fuzzy coating. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
