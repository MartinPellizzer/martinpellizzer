Comparettia rubriflora

Comparettia rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered comparettia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
