Cogswellia cusickii

Cogswellia cusickii, commonly known as the desert rose.

This succulent plant is native to the deserts of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It features a thick, branching stem with fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that store water. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months.

Its ability to thrive in arid conditions makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
