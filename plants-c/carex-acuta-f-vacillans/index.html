Carex Acuta F. Vacillans

Carex acuta f. vacillans is a unique variant of the common spike rush known for its distinctive growth patterns and adaptability. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks. Its foliage exhibits a subtle variation in color and texture compared to the standard Carex acuta. The plant's ability to flourish in diverse conditions makes it a valuable addition to ecological restoration projects. Its presence highlights the importance of genetic diversity in plant species.
