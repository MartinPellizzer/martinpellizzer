Corydalis clematis

Corydalis clematis, commonly known as the slender corydalis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant features slender, creeping stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Asia.

Corydalis clematis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
