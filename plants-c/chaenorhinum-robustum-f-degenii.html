Chaenorhinum robustum f. degenii

Chaenorhinum robustum f. degenii, commonly known as the Degen's robust nightshade, is a rare and distinctive variant of the robust nightshade species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically violet or purple, with a distinctive tubular shape and a five-lobed corolla. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This form is notable for its unique morphology and is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.
