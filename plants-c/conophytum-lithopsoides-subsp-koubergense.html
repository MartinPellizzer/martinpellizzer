Conophytum lithopsoides subsp. koubergense

Conophytum lithopsoides subsp.

koubergense, commonly known as the Kouberg stone plant, is a succulent native to South Africa. It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often mistaken for stones due to their shape and coloration.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
