Cleistopholis staudtii

Cleistopholis staudtii, commonly known as the Staudt's thornveld, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features thorny stems and small, alternate leaves with a serrated margin. It produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its seeds are enclosed in hairy pods.
