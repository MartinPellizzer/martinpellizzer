Cryptocoryne vietnamensis

Cryptocoryne vietnamensis, commonly known as Vietnamese cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green in color. This plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. It is often cultivated in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne vietnamensis is also notable for its ability to tolerate a range of water parameters, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced aquarists.
