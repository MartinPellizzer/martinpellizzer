Chlonanthes glabra

Chlonanthes glabra, commonly known as the smooth horned cucumber, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces elongated, spiny fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its botanical features include tendrils for climbing and a robust root system.

The plant is valued for its edible fruits and medicinal properties in various cultures.
