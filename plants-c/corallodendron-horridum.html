Corallodendron horridum

Corallodendron horridum, commonly known as the spiny coral tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, coral-like inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
