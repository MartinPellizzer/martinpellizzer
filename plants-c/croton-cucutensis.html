Croton cucutensis

Croton cucutensis, commonly known as the Cucutensis Croton, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that can vary in shape and texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a woody stem and branching structure.

Croton cucutensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
