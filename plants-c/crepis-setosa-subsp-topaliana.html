Crepis setosa subsp. topaliana

Crepis setosa subsp.

topaliana, commonly known as the slender hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are linear and hairy, with a distinctive basal rosette formation.
