Calamagrostis villosa var. brachytricha

Calamagrostis villosa var. brachytricha, commonly known as silky blue grass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It features narrow, blue-gray blades with a silky texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and marshy areas.

Its dense growth habit makes it an effective erosion control plant in wetland restoration projects.
