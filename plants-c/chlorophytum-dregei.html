Chlorophytum dregei

Chlorophytum dregei, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features long, grass-like leaves that are typically green with yellow or white stripes, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
