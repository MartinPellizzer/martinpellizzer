Carpinus Betulus F. Columnaris

Columnar european hornbeam, scientifically known as Carpinus betulus f. columnaris, is a striking variation of the common hornbeam tree. This compact, upright shrub or small tree is prized for its unique, columnar growth habit that adds architectural interest to gardens. Native to Europe, it thrives in a variety of soil types and is highly adaptable to different climate conditions. Its dense foliage and year-round appeal make it a popular choice for urban landscaping and small spaces. With its elegant form and low maintenance requirements, it stands out as a versatile and aesthetically pleasing plant.
