Chrysanthemum sipikorense

Chrysanthemum sipikorense, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
