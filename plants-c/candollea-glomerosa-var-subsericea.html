Candollea glomerosa var. subsericea

Candollea glomerosa var. subsericea, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after specimen in horticulture and botanical studies.
