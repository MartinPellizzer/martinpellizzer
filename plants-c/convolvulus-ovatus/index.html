Convolvulus Ovatus

Blue morning glory, scientifically known as Convolvulus ovatus, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is celebrated for its vibrant blue petals that open in the early morning sun and close by midday. This annual herb thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes. Its delicate yet bold appearance has made it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. With its unique blooming pattern and easy care requirements, it remains a symbol of beauty and resilience in the plant world.
