Chrysocoma horizontalis

Chrysocoma horizontalis, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, fern-like leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually pale yellow to white in color.

It is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional medicine.
