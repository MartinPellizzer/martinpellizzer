Crotalaria vandenbrandii

Crotalaria vandenbrandii, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling a crown, hence its common name. The plant has palmate leaves with three to five leaflets, and its growth habit is typically shrub-like or herbaceous. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Crotalaria vandenbrandii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
