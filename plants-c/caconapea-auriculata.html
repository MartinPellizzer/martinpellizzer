Caconapea auriculata

Caconapea auriculata, commonly known as the ear-shaped leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Caconapea auriculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
