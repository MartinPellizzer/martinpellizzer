Columellia arborescens

Columellia arborescens, commonly known as the tree columella, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its woody stem and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and unique foliage structure.
