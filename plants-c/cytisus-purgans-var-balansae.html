Cytisus purgans var. balansae

Cytisus purgans var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its presence in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
