Catasetum Saccatum Var. Incurvum

Catasetum saccatum var. incurvum is a striking orchid known for its vibrant, often yellow or orange flowers. This variety is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light. The plant is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral structure and long-lasting blooms. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and botanical collections.
