Cotyledon serrata

Cotyledon serrata, commonly known as the serrated cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves with serrated edges that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
