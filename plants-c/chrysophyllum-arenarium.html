Chrysophyllum arenarium

Chrysophyllum arenarium, commonly known as the yellow star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers.
