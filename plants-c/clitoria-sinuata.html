Clitoria sinuata

Clitoria sinuata, commonly known as the winged bean or evergreen bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with distinctive wing-like structures on the petioles. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink pea-like flowers, which are often used in ornamental gardening. Its pods contain edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients.

Clitoria sinuata is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural purposes.
