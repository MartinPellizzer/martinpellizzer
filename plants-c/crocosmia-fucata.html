Crocosmia fucata

Crocosmia fucata, commonly known as the orange spider lily, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its vivid orange-red flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are narrow and pointed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Crocosmia fucata is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
