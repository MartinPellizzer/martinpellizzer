Campanula lactiflora var. glabriuscula

Campanula lactiflora var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with a fine, soft pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable addition to rock gardens or alpine beds.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in garden settings.
