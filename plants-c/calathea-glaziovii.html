Calathea glaziovii

Calathea glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's calathea, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, intricate leaf patterns. The leaves are typically oval to lance-shaped with a waxy texture and vibrant coloration, often featuring a mix of green, silver, and red hues. Calathea glaziovii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
