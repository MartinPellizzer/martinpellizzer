Calanthe bursicola

Calanthe bursicola, commonly known as the Bursicola Calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces elegant, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically blooms with small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Calanthe bursicola is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
