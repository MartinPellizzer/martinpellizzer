Chrysosplenium japonicum var. cuneifolium

Chrysosplenium japonicum var. cuneifolium, commonly known as Japanese cuneate chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the spring season. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive species within the Saxifragaceae family.
