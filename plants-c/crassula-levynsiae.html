Crassula levynsiae

Crassula levynsiae, commonly known as the Lemon Lime Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a yellowish or lime-colored hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
