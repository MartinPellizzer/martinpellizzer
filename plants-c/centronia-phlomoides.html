Centronia phlomoides

Centronia phlomoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centronia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in whorls, producing a fluffy appearance that resembles a cloud.

Centronia phlomoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
