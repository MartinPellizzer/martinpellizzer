Chelonistele pinniloba

Chelonistele pinniloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chelonistele, is a rare and delicate fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, pinnately lobed leaves that give it a lacy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the underside of the fronds, a common feature in ferns.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern in many areas.
