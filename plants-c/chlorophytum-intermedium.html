Chlorophytum intermedium

Chlorophytum intermedium, commonly known as the snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Chlorophytum intermedium is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, as it can help remove toxins from the environment.
