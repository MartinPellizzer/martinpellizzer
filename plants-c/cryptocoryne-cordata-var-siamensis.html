Cryptocoryne cordata var. siamensis

Cryptocoryne cordata var. siamensis, commonly known as Siam cryptocoryne, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery sheen. This variety is well-suited for low-light conditions and can thrive in a range of water temperatures. Its rhizome grows horizontally, producing dense clusters of foliage.

It is often used in planted tanks for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
