Croton skutchii

Croton skutchii, commonly known as the Skutch's croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display a mix of red, yellow, and green hues. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its growth habit is compact, with multiple stems emerging from the base.

Croton skutchii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in well-drained, sunny conditions.
