Cyphostemma rubromarginatum

Cyphostemma rubromarginatum, commonly known as the red-marginated moon vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its pale green, waxy leaves with distinctive red margins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in woodland or rocky areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions as a decorative plant.
