Cremastogyne lanata

Cremastogyne lanata, commonly known as the hairy cremastogyne, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
