Cryptocoryne × batangkayanensis

Cryptocoryne × batangkayanensis, commonly known as the Batangkayanensis Cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that often exhibit a striking red or purple coloration on the underside. This plant thrives in moderately lit freshwater environments with stable water parameters. It is popular among aquarium enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne × batangkayanensis is known for its ability to adapt well to a variety of water conditions, making it a versatile choice for planted tanks.
