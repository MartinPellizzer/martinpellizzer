Calathea dasycarpa

Calathea dasycarpa, commonly known as the velvetleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display intricate patterns and a deep green coloration. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.
