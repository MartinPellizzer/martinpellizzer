Campanopsis humilis

Campanopsis humilis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas and has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth surface. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Campanopsis humilis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
