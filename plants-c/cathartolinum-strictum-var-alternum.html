Cathartolinum strictum var. alternum

Cathartolinum strictum var. alternum, commonly known as the variable cathartolinum, is a unique variant of the cathartolinum species.

It is characterized by its distinct leaf morphology, with alternating leaflets that set it apart from other varieties. The plant exhibits a climbing habit, often forming dense foliage that can shade the surrounding area. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically appearing in clusters along the stems.

This variant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
