Clematoclethra floribunda

Clematoclethra floribunda, commonly known as the Japanese clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Clematoclethra floribunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its delicate floral display.
