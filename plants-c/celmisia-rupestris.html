Celmisia rupestris

Celmisia rupestris, commonly known as the rock celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing form. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
