Cosmianthemum dido

Cosmianthemum dido, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It has hairy, narrow leaves and thrives in well-drained soils.
