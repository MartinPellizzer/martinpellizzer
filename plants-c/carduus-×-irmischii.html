Carduus × irmischii

Carduus × irmischii, commonly known as the Irmisch thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. It is often found in grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides.

Due to its hardy nature, it can sometimes become invasive in certain areas.
