Cryptocoryne versteegii

Cryptocoryne versteegii, commonly known as the Versteegii cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that are often marked with intricate patterns. This plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. It is popular among aquarium enthusiasts due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne versteegii is also known for its ability to tolerate a range of water parameters, making it a versatile choice for planted tanks.
