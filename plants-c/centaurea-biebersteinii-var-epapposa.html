Centaurea biebersteinii var. epapposa

Centaurea biebersteinii var. epapposa, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada starflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and spiny, bristly foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in open habitats such as meadows and slopes. It has a compact growth habit with upright stems and is often found in high elevation environments.

The species is adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is considered a rare and endemic plant in its native range.
