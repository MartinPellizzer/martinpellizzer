Coelogyne swaniana

Coelogyne swaniana, commonly known as the Swan's Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters on a slender stem. The plant produces long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne swaniana is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
