Cleistes Catharinensis

Cleistes catharinensis is a rare and delicate orchid native to Brazil. It is known for its unique, lantern-like flowers that bloom in the early morning. This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments within the Atlantic Forest. Its conservation status is threatened due to habitat destruction and illegal collection. Studying Cleistes catharinensis offers insights into the biodiversity of South America's endangered ecosystems.
