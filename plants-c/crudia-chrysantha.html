Crudia chrysantha

Crudia chrysantha, commonly known as the yellow-flowered bushveld daisy, is a South African native plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shrubland and grassland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, cylindrical flower heads that are often clustered in loose arrays.

Crudia chrysantha is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystem.
