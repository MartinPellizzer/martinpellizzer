Cypripedium × io

Cypripedium × io, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cypripedium calceolus and Cypripedium malycense.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with a distinctive, showy flower that resembles a slipper, typically in shades of pink, white, or purple. The plant features a single, erect stem that can grow up to 60 cm tall, bearing a single, large, pouch-shaped lip. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette at the base, and it thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade.

Cypripedium × io is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
