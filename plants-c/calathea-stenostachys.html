Calathea stenostachys

Calathea stenostachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed prayer plant, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often feature intricate patterns in shades of green, white, and purple. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often adorned with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Calathea stenostachys is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
