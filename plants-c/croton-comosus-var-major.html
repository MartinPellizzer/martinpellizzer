Croton comosus var. major

Croton comosus var. major, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features large, palmate leaves with lobed margins, often displaying a striking green or variegated coloration. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.
