Crassula griquaensis

Crassula griquaensis, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
