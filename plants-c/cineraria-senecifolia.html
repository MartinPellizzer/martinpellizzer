Cineraria senecifolia

Cineraria senecifolia, commonly known as the woolly cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, silver-gray foliage and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique texture and color, making it a standout in coastal and Mediterranean landscapes.
