Cattleya × elegans var. turneri

Cattleya × elegans var. turneri, commonly known as the Turners' orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant typically produces large, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, with a distinctive ruffled lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid growers for its elegance and relatively easy care requirements.
