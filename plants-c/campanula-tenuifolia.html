Campanula tenuifolia

Campanula tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a hairy texture.

Campanula tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ease of care.
