Campanula velebitica

Campanula velebitica, commonly known as the Velebit bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Dinaric Alps, particularly in Croatia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Campanula velebitica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also of interest in botanical studies due to its unique habitat preferences.
