Cynodon Praecox

Cynodon grass, scientifically known as Cynodon praecox, is a hardy perennial grass native to southern Africa. It thrives in warm climates and is commonly found in grasslands and along roadsides. This plant is valued for its dense growth and ability to withstand drought and grazing. Its fine texture and vibrant green color make it a popular choice for ornamental and turfgrass applications. Cynodon praecox plays an important ecological role by supporting local wildlife and preventing soil erosion.
