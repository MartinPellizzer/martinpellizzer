Cynanchum juzepczukii

Cynanchum juzepczukii, commonly known as the Juzepczuk's milkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes many milkweed species. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbellate clusters. Its milky sap is a characteristic feature, containing latex compounds.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.
