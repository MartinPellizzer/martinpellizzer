Croton guatemalensis

Croton guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan croton, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant features opposite, simple, and usually ovate or elliptic leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically greenish-yellow in color.

The fruit is a three-lobed capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
