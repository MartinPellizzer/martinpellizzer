Chamaecrista plumosa var. erecta

Chamaecrista plumosa var. erecta, commonly known as the upright silky senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal or sandy environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its light, airy foliage and long-lasting blooms.
