Calea clausseniana var. regnelliana

Calea clausseniana var. regnelliana, commonly known as the velvetleaf calea, is a striking plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer, adding a dramatic visual element to its appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections.
