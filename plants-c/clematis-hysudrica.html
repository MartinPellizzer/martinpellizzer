Clematis hysudrica

Clematis hysudrica, commonly known as the giant clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that range from white to deep purple, often with a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, hooked hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring support for its rapid growth.

Clematis hysudrica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
