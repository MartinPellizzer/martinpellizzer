Citrus macrocalyx

Citrus macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered citrus.

It is a species of citrus native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its large, showy white flowers and thick, waxy leaves. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically not used for commercial citrus products but are valued for their ornamental appeal. Its botanical features include a rugged, shrub-like growth habit and a strong, aromatic scent.

The plant is often cultivated in warm climates for its visual beauty and potential medicinal properties.
