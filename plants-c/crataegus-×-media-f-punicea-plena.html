Crataegus × media f. punicea-plena

Crataegus × media f. punicea-plena, commonly known as the double-flowered red hawthorn, is a cultivar of the hawthorn plant characterized by its dense, showy flowers.

It features large, double blooms that are typically pink to red in color, providing ornamental value in gardens. The plant is a compact shrub with thorny branches and small, oval leaves that are dark green in color. It produces edible berries in the fall, which are attractive to birds.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
