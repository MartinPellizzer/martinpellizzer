Crambe reniformis

Crambe reniformis, commonly known as the kidney bean plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds, often referred to as kidney beans, are edible and used in various culinary applications.

However, the plant also has potential agricultural uses due to its nitrogen-fixing properties.
