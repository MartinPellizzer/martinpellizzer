Ceramia obliqua

Ceramia obliqua, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging inflorescence and striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Ceramia obliqua is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
