Crepis tybakiensis

Crepis tybakiensis, commonly known as the Tybakiens crepis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of linear leaves and slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, dandelion-like yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a single head. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in rocky or sandy soils.
