Cuphea acuta

Cuphea acuta, commonly known as the spiked cuphea, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Cuphea acuta is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact size.
