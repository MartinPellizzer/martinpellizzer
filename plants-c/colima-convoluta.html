Colima convoluta

Colima convoluta, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a unique species of fern native to the high-altitude regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of fronds, which gives it a striking and intricate appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or shaded environments, thriving in cool and moist climates. Its fronds are narrow and elongated, with a leathery texture that helps it retain moisture.

Colima convoluta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
