Cardanthera africana var. schweinfurthii

Cardanthera africana var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African red trugan, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking red-orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are arranged in dense, conical heads.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
