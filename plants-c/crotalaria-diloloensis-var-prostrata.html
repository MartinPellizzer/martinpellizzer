Crotalaria diloloensis var. prostrata

Crotalaria diloloensis var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate crotalaria, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling habit, with small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a rough texture and prominent veins. This species belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with rhizobia.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
