Citrus boholensis

Citrus boholensis, commonly known as the Bohol citrus or Bohol lemon, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It produces small, round, yellow fruits with a thick, aromatic rind and a juicy, acidic pulp. The plant thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its fruit.

The fruit is used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent in local cuisine.
