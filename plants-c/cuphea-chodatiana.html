Cuphea chodatiana

Cuphea chodatiana, commonly known as the red-hot cat's tail, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

The plant belongs to the Lythraceae family and is known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
