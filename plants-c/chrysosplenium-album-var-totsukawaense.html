Chrysosplenium album var. totsukawaense

Chrysosplenium album var. totsukawaense, commonly known as the white chrysosplenium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
