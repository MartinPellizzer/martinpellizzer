Centaurea lacera

Centaurea lacera, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its slender stems and small, blue to purple flowers are notable features.

Centaurea lacera is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
