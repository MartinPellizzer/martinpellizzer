Cymbopogon Nardus

Cymbopogon nardus is a fragrant grass known for its distinctive lemony scent. Native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa, it thrives in warm, humid climates. This plant is commonly used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine for its calming properties. Its essential oil is widely extracted and utilized in perfumes and natural remedies. Cymbopogon nardus remains a valuable resource in both ecological and commercial contexts.
