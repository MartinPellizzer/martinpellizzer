Canavalia stocksii

Canavalia stocksii, commonly known as the African burr bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a legume characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces large, spiny pods that contain edible seeds, which are used in local cuisines. Canavalia stocksii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.
