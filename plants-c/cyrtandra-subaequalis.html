Cyrtandra subaequalis

Cyrtandra subaequalis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cyrtandra, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth margin.
