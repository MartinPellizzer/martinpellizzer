Caladenia pholcoidea subsp. augustensis

Caladenia pholcoidea subsp.

augustensis, commonly known as the August Spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils within open forests and grasslands.

The plant features a single, slender stem with a single, lance-shaped or elliptic leaf. Its flowers are pale yellow to cream, with a distinctive spider-like appearance due to their elongated, narrow petals.

This subspecies is known for its striking morphology and is often found in regions with a temperate climate.
