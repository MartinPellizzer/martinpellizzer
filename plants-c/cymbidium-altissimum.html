Cymbidium altissimum

Cymbidium altissimum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and large, showy flowers. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, often in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in cool, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cymbidium altissimum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
