Cirsium arisanense

Cirsium arisanense, commonly known as the Arisan thistle, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
