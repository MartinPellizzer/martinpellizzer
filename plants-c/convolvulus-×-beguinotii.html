Convolvulus × beguinotii

Convolvulus × beguinotii, commonly known as the hybrid sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a vigorous climber with tendrils that allow it to ascend supports, often producing abundant flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, elongated seed pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.

Due to its hybrid nature, it may exhibit variable characteristics depending on the parent species.
