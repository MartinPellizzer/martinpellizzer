Cytisopsis leiocalycina

Cytisopsis leiocalycina, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
