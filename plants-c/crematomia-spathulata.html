Crematomia spathulata

Crematomia spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved arrowhead, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically broad and heart-shaped at the base. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Crematomia spathulata is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
