Croton cristalensis

Croton cristalensis, commonly known as the Crystal Croton, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red, often with a waxy texture. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with unique sap and floral structures. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters in its natural habitat.

Due to its aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in outdoor gardens in warm climates.
