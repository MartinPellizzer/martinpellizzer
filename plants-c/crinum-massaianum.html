Crinum massaianum

Crinum massaianum, commonly known as the Massai lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to East Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern around the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Crinum massaianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
