Crassula mucronata

Crassula mucronata, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Crassula mucronata is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
