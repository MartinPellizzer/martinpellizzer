Cattleya elongata var. rosea

Cattleya elongata var. rosea, commonly known as the Rose Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant pink to red flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features a tall, slender stem with multiple blooms, each having a distinctive lip that is often a different color from the rest of the flower. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
