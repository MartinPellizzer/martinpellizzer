Cryptantha denticulata

Cryptantha denticulata, commonly known as the toothed cryptantha, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Cryptantha denticulata is adapted to arid environments and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native range.
