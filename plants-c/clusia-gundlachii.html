Clusia gundlachii

Clusia gundlachii, commonly known as the brightleaf spurge, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with yellow or red on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Clusia gundlachii is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage and resilience.
