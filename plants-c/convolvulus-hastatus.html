Convolvulus hastatus

Convolvulus hastatus, commonly known as the spurred morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Convolvulus hastatus is a climbing or trailing plant that can be found in various habitats across southern Europe and North Africa.
