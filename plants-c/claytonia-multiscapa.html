Claytonia multiscapa

Claytonia multiscapa, commonly known as the "spring beauty," is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, early spring blooms that appear on low-growing stems. The plant typically has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it is often found in clusters along the ground.
