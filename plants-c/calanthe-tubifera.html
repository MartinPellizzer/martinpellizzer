Calanthe tubifera

Calanthe tubifera, commonly known as the tuberous calanthe, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive tubular shape. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Calanthe tubifera thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
