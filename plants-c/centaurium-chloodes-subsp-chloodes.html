Centaurium chloodes subsp. chloodes

Centaurium chloodes subsp.

chloodes, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

The plant is characterized by its narrow leaves and is often found in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands. It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
