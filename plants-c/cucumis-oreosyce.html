Cucumis oreosyce

Cucumis oreosyce, commonly known as the desert cucumber, is a species native to arid regions of North America.

It is a small, trailing vine that produces small, pale green fruits with a slightly bitter taste. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. It is adapted to survive in hot, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

Its botanical features make it a unique example of desert flora adapted to extreme conditions.
