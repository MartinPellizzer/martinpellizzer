Cirrhopetalum acuminatum

Cirrhopetalum acuminatum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
