Clerodendrum philippinum f. pleniflorum

Clerodendrum philippinum f. pleniflorum, commonly known as the Philippine flame bush, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, making it visually striking. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The inflorescence is a panicle, which contributes to its showy appearance.
