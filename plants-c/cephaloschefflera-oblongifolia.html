Cephaloschefflera oblongifolia

Cephaloschefflera oblongifolia, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like crown of leaves that give it its common name. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves with elongated, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
