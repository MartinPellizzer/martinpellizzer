Cnidoscolus albomaculatus

Cnidoscolus albomaculatus, commonly known as the white-spotted arrowhead, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with distinctive white or pale green spots, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its stems are often hairy and can be somewhat fleshy, adapting to its humid, tropical environment.

The plant is also known for its edible leaves, which are used in traditional cuisines in some regions.
