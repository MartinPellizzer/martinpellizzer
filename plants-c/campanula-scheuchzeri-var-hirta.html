Campanula scheuchzeri var. hirta

Campanula scheuchzeri var. hirta, commonly known as the Alpine bellflower, is a perennial herb native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in harsh mountain environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Campanula scheuchzeri var. hirta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
