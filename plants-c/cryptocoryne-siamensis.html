Cryptocoryne siamensis

Cryptocoryne siamensis, commonly known as Siam's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and dark green, waxy leaves that are typically lance-shaped. The plant is well adapted to low-light conditions and can thrive in a variety of water temperatures. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to help maintain water quality.

Cryptocoryne siamensis is also known for its resilience and ease of care, making it a popular choice among aquarists.
