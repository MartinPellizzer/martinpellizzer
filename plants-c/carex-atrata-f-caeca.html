Carex atrata f. caeca

Carex atrata f. caeca, commonly known as the black sedge, is a variant of the common sedge species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
