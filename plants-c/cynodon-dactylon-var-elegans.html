Cynodon dactylon var. elegans

Cynodon dactylon var. elegans, commonly known as the velvetgrass, is a variant of the common Bermuda grass.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and fine, silky blades. The plant is native to Africa and thrives in warm, tropical climates. It is often used for ornamental purposes due to its lush, green appearance.

Its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions makes it adaptable to various environments.
