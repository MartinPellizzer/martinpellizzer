Cyathea lindigii

Cyathea lindigii, commonly known as the Lindig's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height, with a slow growth rate. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting the upright structure of the plant.

Cyathea lindigii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
