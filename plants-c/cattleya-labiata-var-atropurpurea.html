Cattleya labiata var. atropurpurea

Cattleya labiata var. atropurpurea, commonly known as the Purple-lipped Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, velvety flowers.

It features large, showy blooms with a striking combination of deep purple and white petals, often with a contrasting lip. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this orchid thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in clusters with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
