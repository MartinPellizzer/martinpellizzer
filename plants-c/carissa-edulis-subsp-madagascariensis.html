Carissa edulis subsp. madagascariensis

Carissa edulis subsp.

madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar yellow star. It is a shrub native to Madagascar, characterized by its spiny stems and fragrant, star-shaped yellow flowers.

The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible and often used in traditional cuisine. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.
