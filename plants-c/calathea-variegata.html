Calathea variegata

Calathea variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular houseplant among collectors and enthusiasts.
