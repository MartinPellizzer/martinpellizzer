Corbularia graellsii

Corbularia graellsii, commonly known as the Cape Flats daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.

Corbularia graellsii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
