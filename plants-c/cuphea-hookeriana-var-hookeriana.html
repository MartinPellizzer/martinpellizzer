Cuphea hookeriana var. hookeriana

Cuphea hookeriana var. hookeriana, commonly known as the flame flower, is a perennial shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
