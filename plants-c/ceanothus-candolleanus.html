Ceanothus candolleanus

Ceanothus candolleanus, commonly known as the Santa Barbara ceanothus, is a shrub native to California and Baja California.

It is characterized by its aromatic, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically evergreen, providing year-round structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

Ceanothus candolleanus plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
