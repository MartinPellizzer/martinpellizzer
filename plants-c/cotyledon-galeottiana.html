Cotyledon galeottiana

Cotyledon galeottiana, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, orange berries that are edible and have a tangy flavor. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cotyledon galeottiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
