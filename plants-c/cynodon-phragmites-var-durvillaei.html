Cynodon phragmites var. durvillaei

Cynodon phragmites var. durvillaei, commonly known as Durvillaei grass, is a variant of the common grass species found in various habitats across Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green and have a rough texture. This plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal dunes or disturbed areas. It plays a role in stabilizing soils and preventing erosion due to its extensive root system.

Additionally, it is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental value and resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
