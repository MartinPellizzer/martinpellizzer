Calonema audasii

Calonema audasii, commonly known as the hardy begonia, is a perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright red or orange tubular flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Calonema audasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
