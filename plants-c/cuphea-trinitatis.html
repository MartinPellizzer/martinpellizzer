Cuphea trinitatis

Cuphea trinitatis, commonly known as the Trinidad cuphea, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with opposite, ovate leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
