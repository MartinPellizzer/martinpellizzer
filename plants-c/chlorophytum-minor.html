Chlorophytum minor

Chlorophytum minor, commonly known as the spider plant, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green streaks. The plant produces small, grass-like flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is highly adaptable and thrives in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it easy to grow and maintain.
