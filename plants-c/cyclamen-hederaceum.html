Cyclamen hederaceum

Cyclamen hederaceum, commonly known as the ivy-leaved cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive wavy edge that resembles ivy leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically blooming in winter and early spring. It thrives in cool, shady environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Cyclamen hederaceum is popular as a decorative plant due to its elegant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
