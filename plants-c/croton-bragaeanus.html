Croton bragaeanus

Croton bragaeanus, commonly known as the African croton, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features striking, palmate leaves with vibrant green or reddish hues, depending on the season. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
