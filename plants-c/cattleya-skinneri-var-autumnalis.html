Cattleya skinneri var. autumnalis

Cattleya skinneri var. autumnalis, commonly known as the Autumn Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, often orange or red blooms.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features broad, waxy leaves and produces pendulous flowers that bloom in the fall, hence its common name. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
