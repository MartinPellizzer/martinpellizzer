Cafe

Cafe, commonly known as *Coffea*, is a genus of flowering plants in the Rubiaceae family.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The most commercially important species are *Coffea arabica* and *Coffea canephora*, which are cultivated for their seeds, known as coffee beans. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that develop into red or yellow berries containing the seeds.

Botanically, *Coffea* species are shrubs or small trees with opposite, simple leaves and a unique fruit structure that encloses the aromatic seeds.
