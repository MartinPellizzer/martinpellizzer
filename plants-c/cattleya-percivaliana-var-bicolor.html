Cattleya percivaliana var. bicolor

Cattleya percivaliana var. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking floral display.

It features large, showy blooms with a unique bicolor pattern, typically combining white and pink or purple hues. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in warm, humid environments. It has thick, fleshy roots that help it store moisture, adapting to its natural habitat.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
