Cotoneaster permutatus

Cotoneaster permutatus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Cotoneaster permutatus is adaptable to various soil types and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
