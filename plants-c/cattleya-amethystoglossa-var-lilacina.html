Cattleya amethystoglossa var. lilacina

Cattleya amethystoglossa var. lilacina, commonly known as the Lilac Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking floral display.

It features large, showy flowers with a pale lilac coloration on the petals and a deep purple lip. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its botanical appeal lies in its elegant form and the rich, velvety texture of its blooms.
