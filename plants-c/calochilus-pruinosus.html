Calochilus pruinosus

Calochilus pruinosus, commonly known as the glossy-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a compact, rhizomatous growth habit with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy, glossy coating. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or rainforest areas.

Calochilus pruinosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice for cultivation in gardens.
