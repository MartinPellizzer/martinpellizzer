Citharexylum macrocarpum

Citharexylum macrocarpum, commonly known as the large-fruited cithara, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, simple, and often aromatic leaves. Its flowers are small and usually clustered in inflorescences, and the fruits are woody, spherical berries.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
