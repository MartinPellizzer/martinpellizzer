Croton draconoides

Croton draconoides, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and a distinctive, umbrella-shaped crown. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its bark and sap have historically been valued for their medicinal properties.

Croton draconoides thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
