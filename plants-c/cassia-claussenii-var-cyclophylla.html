Cassia claussenii var. cyclophylla

Cassia claussenii var. cyclophylla, commonly known as the cyclophylla cassia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow, fluffy flower clusters that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Cassia claussenii var. cyclophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
