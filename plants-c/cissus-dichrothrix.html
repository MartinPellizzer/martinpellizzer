Cissus dichrothrix

Cissus dichrothrix, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features distinctive heart-shaped leaves that are pale green with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by green, round fruits that turn red when mature. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cissus dichrothrix is often used in hanging baskets or as a trailing plant due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
