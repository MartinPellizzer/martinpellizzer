Cattleya guttata var. williamsiana

Cattleya guttata var. williamsiana, commonly known as the Williamsiana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety petals and a distinctive yellow throat, which sets it apart from other Cattleya species. The plant typically produces large, fragrant blooms in shades of white, pink, and yellow, often with a contrasting center. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
