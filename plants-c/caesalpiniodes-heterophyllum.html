Caesalpiniodes heterophyllum

Caesalpiniodes heterophyllum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange floral displays. The plant has pinnate leaves with alternate arrangement, and its flowers are typically clustered in racemes. It produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
