Crossyne Guttata

Guttate crossvine, scientifically known as Crossyne guttata, is a unique and visually striking plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. Its name comes from the small, drop-like markings on its leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. This climbing plant is often found in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in humid and shaded environments. With its vibrant green foliage and delicate flowers, it adds both beauty and ecological value to its surroundings. Despite its ornamental appeal, Crossyne guttata remains a relatively obscure species in the world of horticulture.
