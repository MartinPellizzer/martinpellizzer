Coleus pictus

Coleus pictus, commonly known as the Marvelous Marvel, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a wide range of hues including red, pink, purple, and green. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

This ornamental plant is often used in landscaping and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal.
