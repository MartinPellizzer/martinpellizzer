Cassia ixodes

Cassia ixodes, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping panicles of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with multiple pairs of oval, pointed leaflets. Its flowers are rich in nectar and attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and birds.

Cassia ixodes is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
