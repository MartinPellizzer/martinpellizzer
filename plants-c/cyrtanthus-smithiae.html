Cyrtanthus smithiae

Cyrtanthus smithiae, commonly known as the Smith's bell-shaped iris, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant blooms in late spring to early summer, often in clusters at the tips of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Cyrtanthus smithiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
