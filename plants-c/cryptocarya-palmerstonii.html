Cryptocarya palmerstonii

Cryptocarya palmerstonii, commonly known as the Palmerston's cryptocarya, is a small evergreen tree native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves with a smooth surface and a distinctive, aromatic scent. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits.

Cryptocarya palmerstonii is typically found in coastal and lowland forest habitats, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
