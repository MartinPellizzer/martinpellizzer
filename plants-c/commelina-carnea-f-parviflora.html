Commelina carnea f. parviflora

Commelina carnea f. parviflora, commonly known as the pink commelina or small-flowered commelina, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color to shaded garden spaces.
