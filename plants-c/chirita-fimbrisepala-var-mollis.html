Chirita fimbrisepala var. mollis

Chirita fimbrisepala var. mollis, commonly known as the velvet leaf chireya, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive texture. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are small and tubular, usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and are clustered in inflorescences.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
