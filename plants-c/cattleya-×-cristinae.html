Cattleya × cristinae

Cattleya × cristinae, commonly known as the Cristina Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with distinctive red or purple markings. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
