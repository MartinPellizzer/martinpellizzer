Cotoneaster robustior

Cotoneaster robustior, commonly known as the hardy cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy appearance.

Cotoneaster robustior is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness in various climates.
