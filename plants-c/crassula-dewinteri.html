Crassula dewinteri

Crassula dewinteri, commonly known as the Snow Rose or Frost Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
