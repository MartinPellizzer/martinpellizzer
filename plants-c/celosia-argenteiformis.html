Celosia argenteiformis

Celosia argenteiformis, commonly known as the silver feather or flame of the woods, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like bracts that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often surrounded by the showy bracts. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its unique appearance and ease of cultivation contribute to its popularity among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
