Caladium speciosum

Caladium speciosum, commonly known as the flameleaf caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Caladium speciosum is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant for its dramatic leaf patterns.
