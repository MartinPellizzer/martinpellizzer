Chrysopogon lancearius

Chrysopogon lancearius, commonly known as the narrowleaf bluestem, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to stabilize soil and tolerate various environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its range.
