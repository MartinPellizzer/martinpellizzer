Calamus poilanei

Calamus poilanei, commonly known as the Malagasy calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araucariaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnately compound leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its reproductive structures include small, clustered cones that produce seeds.
