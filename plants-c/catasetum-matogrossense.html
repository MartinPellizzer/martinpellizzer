Catasetum matogrossense

Catasetum matogrossense, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive inflorescence and vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and deriving moisture and nutrients from the air.
