Cotyledon flanaganii

Cotyledon flanaganii, commonly known as the Flanagan's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
