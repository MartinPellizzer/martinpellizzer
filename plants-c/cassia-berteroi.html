Cassia berteroi

Cassia berteroi, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant produces flat, elliptical pods that contain several seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

Cassia berteroi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
