Crassula setulosa f. latipetala

Crassula setulosa f. latipetala, commonly known as the broad-leaved crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green color with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
