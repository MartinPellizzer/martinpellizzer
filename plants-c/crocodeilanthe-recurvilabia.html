Crocodeilanthe recurvilabia

Crocodeilanthe recurvilabia, commonly known as the twisted-leaf fern, is a rare and unique epiphytic fern native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, allowing it to anchor itself to its host while absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.
