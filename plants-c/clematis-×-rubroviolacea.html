Clematis × rubroviolacea

Clematis × rubroviolacea, commonly known as the red viola clematis, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, with clusters of flowers appearing along the stems. It has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are often covered in fine, white hairs.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
