Celosia argentea var. margaritacea

Celosia argentea var. margaritacea, commonly known as the "plume grass" or "coyote tail," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery inflorescences that resemble plumes or tails, which can range in color from white to pink and red. The plant is a herbaceous annual with slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of cultivation contribute to its widespread use in both gardens and floral arrangements.
