Cybelion pallidiflorum

Cybelion pallidiflorum, commonly known as the paleflower cybelion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
