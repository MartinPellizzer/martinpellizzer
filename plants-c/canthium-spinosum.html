Canthium spinosum

Canthium spinosum, commonly known as the spiny canthium, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features palmate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and fruits, which are capsule-like structures containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
