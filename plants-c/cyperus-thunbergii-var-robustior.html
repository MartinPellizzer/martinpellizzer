Cyperus thunbergii var. robustior

Cyperus thunbergii var. robustior, commonly known as the broadleaf paper plant, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features tall, cylindrical stems that are often used in floral arrangements due to their aesthetic appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its leaves are broad and flat, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.
