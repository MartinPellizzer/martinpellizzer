Crotalaria stenocladon

Crotalaria stenocladon, commonly known as narrow-leaved cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. Its seeds are encased in hard, pod-like structures and are often used as a protein source in some parts of the world.

Crotalaria stenocladon is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a valuable plant in agricultural systems.
