Clinopodium chinense subsp. grandiflorum

Clinopodium chinense subsp.

grandiflorum, commonly known as Chinese betony, is a perennial herb native to East Asia. It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with serrated edges, often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces showy, clustered blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties have made it a valued plant in traditional Chinese medicine.
