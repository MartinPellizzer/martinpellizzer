Chrysocalyx schimperi

Chrysocalyx schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in montane forests at elevations above 2,000 meters. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rocky substrates where it grows.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
