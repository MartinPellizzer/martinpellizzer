Codomale purpurascens

Codomale purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered codomale, is a striking member of the Codonaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple petals and delicate, branching structure. Native to specific regions of South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, green berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.

Its unique floral morphology makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
