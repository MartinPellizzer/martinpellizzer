Cyclamen saldense

Cyclamen saldense, commonly known as the Salentine cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and prefers cool, moist conditions. Cyclamen saldense is a perennial species that thrives in shaded environments, often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and delicate addition to their plant collection.
