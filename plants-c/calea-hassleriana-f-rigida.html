Calea hassleriana f. rigida

Calea hassleriana f. rigida, commonly known as the rigid calea, is a striking plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale blue flowers that resemble a crown. The plant features a robust, upright stem with hairy leaves that are often tinged with purple. Its foliage is succulent, adapted to withstand dry conditions, and it thrives in well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its unique appearance and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
