Coelogyne pallide-flavens var. brevilabrata

Coelogyne pallide-flavens var. brevilabrata, commonly known as the pale yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale yellow with a white or cream-colored lip, and they bloom in clusters. This orchid prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable environment.
