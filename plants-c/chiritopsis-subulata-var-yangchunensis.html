Chiritopsis subulata var. yangchunensis

Chiritopsis subulata var. yangchunensis, commonly known as the Yangchun dandelion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, narrow leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It is often found in mountainous regions of Guangdong and Guangxi provinces.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
