Carpesium faberi

Carpesium faberi, commonly known as the Chinese flame vine, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental plants.

Due to its invasive potential in some regions, it requires careful management to prevent spread.
