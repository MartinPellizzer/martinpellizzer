Chamaecrista Orbiculata Var. Ustulata

Chamaecrista orbiculata var. ustulata is a unique and resilient plant native to the southern United States. It is known for its striking red and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This variety thrives in sandy soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for coastal and dry landscapes. Its common name, "Firecracker Bean," reflects the vibrant color of its blossoms. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in favor of more traditional garden plants.
