Centaurea redempta subsp. redempta

Centaurea redempta subsp.

redempta, commonly known as the red sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red to orange flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and the stems are usually sparsely branched.

This subspecies is native to regions of southern Europe and parts of the Middle East.
