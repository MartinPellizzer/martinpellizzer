Columnea cochinchinensis

Columnea cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese flame flower, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces showy, spurred flowers that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Columnea cochinchinensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
