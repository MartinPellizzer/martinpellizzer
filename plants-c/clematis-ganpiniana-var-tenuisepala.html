Clematis ganpiniana var. tenuisepala

Clematis ganpiniana var. tenuisepala, commonly known as the narrow-sepaled clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, typically white or pale blue flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, hairy fruit capsules.
