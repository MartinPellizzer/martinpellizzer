Cymbopogon iwarancusa subsp. iwarancusa

Cymbopogon iwarancusa subsp.

iwarancusa, commonly known as the Iwarancusa grass, is a perennial grass native to the tropical regions of South America. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is characterized by its aromatic foliage, which contains essential oils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
