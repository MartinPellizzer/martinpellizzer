Callista rigescens

Callista rigescens, commonly known as the rigid callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, upright leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
