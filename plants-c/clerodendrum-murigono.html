Clerodendrum murigono

Clerodendrum murigono, commonly known as the woolly clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes other aromatic herbs and shrubs. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.
