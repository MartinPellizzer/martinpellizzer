Carduus corbariensis

Carduus corbariensis, commonly known as the common cocklebur.

This plant is a species of thistle native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa. It is characterized by its spiny leaves and large, spherical seed heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.

Its botanical features include a robust taproot and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
