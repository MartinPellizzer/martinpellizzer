Clinopodium umbrosum var. japonicum

Clinopodium umbrosum var. japonicum, commonly known as Japanese wood betony, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a strong mint-like aroma. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its medicinal properties have been utilized in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
