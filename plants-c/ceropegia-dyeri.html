Ceropegia dyeri

Ceropegia dyeri, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a trailing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or pink markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or white, which are pollinated by specific insects. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
