Cardamine depressa subsp. stellata

Cardamine depressa subsp.

stellata, commonly known as starleaf watercress, is a small herbaceous plant native to northern temperate regions. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

The plant features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive star-shaped arrangement of leaflets. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and it produces elongated, cylindrical fruits.

The species is valued for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
