Cactus oaxacensis

Cactus oaxacensis, commonly known as the Oaxacan cactus, is a species native to the Mexican state of Oaxaca.

It is a spineless cactus characterized by its columnar shape and smooth, green skin. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
