Cynodon coursii

Cynodon coursii, commonly known as African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, red or brown flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Cynodon coursii is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
