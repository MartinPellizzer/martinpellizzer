Clematis yuanjiangensis

Clematis yuanjiangensis, commonly known as the Yujiang Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, usually blue or purple flowers. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, follicle-like fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments in its native habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it remains relatively rare in horticultural trade.
