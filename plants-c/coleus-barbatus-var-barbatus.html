Coleus barbatus var. barbatus

Coleus barbatus var. barbatus, commonly known as the flame wheel plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage with intricate patterns of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

This plant is popular in ornamental horticulture for its striking visual appeal.
