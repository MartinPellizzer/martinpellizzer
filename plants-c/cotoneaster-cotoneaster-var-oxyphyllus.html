Cotoneaster cotoneaster var. oxyphyllus

Cotoneaster cotoneaster var. oxyphyllus, commonly known as the glossy cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are glossy green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can be invasive in some regions due to its aggressive growth habit.
