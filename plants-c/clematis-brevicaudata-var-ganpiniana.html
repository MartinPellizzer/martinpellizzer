Clematis brevicaudata var. ganpiniana

Clematis brevicaudata var. ganpiniana, commonly known as the Ganpinian Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features slender, twining stems that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue and purple during the summer months. Its foliage consists of simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
