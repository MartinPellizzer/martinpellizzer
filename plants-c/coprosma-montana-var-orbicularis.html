Coprosma montana var. orbicularis

Coprosma montana var. orbicularis, commonly known as the oval-leaf coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruits are bright red berries that mature in the fall, providing food for native birds.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
