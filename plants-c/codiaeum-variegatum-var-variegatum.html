Codiaeum variegatum var. variegatum

Codiaeum variegatum var. variegatum, commonly known as the flame leaf or croton plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from red, yellow, and orange to green and variegated patterns. The leaves are typically palmately lobed and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its striking appearance.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
