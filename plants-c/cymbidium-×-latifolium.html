Cymbidium × latifolium

Cymbidium × latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in winter or early spring. The plant prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative ornamental. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its long-lasting blooms and elegant appearance.

Cymbidium × latifolium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
