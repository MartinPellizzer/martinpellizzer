Coluteastrum spinescens

Coluteastrum spinescens, commonly known as the spiny cowpea, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, white to yellowish hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated margin. Its small, pale yellow flowers are clustered in loose racemes, and the plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods containing several seeds.

Botanically, it is classified within the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its adaptability to various soil types.
