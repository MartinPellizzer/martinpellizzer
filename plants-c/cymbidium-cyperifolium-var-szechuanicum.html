Cymbidium cyperifolium var. szechuanicum

Cymbidium cyperifolium var. szechuanicum, commonly known as the Sichuan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
