Cotyledon patens

Cotyledon patens, commonly known as the "butterfly plant," is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and purple. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. Cotyledon patens thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
