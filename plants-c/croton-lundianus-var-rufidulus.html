Croton lundianus var. rufidulus

Croton lundianus var. rufidulus, commonly known as the red-leaf croton, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often reddish or orange-colored foliage, which makes it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine in some regions for their purported health benefits.
