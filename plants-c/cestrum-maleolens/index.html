Cestrum Maleolens

Cestrum maleolens is a striking plant known for its vibrant night-blooming flowers. It is native to the tropical regions of the Americas and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular blossoms that open in the evening. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, adding to its visual appeal. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
