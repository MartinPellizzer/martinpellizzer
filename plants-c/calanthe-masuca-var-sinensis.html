Calanthe masuca var. sinensis

Calanthe masuca var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese calanthe, is a rare and elegant orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. It has a pseudobulbous structure, which helps it store water and nutrients.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after in horticultural circles.
