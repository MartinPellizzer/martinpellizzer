Colocasia affinis

Colocasia affinis, commonly known as the African elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in diameter. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other familiar plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a velvety texture and can have a slight purple tint on the underside.

Colocasia affinis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and thrives in moist, shady environments.
