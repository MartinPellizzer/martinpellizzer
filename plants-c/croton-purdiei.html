Croton purdiei

Croton purdiei, commonly known as the Cape Croton, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from green to red, orange, and yellow, depending on the season and environmental conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant leaf colors.
