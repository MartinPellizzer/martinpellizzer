Chrysanthemum dolichophyllum

Chrysanthemum dolichophyllum, commonly known as the long-stemmed chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal use.
