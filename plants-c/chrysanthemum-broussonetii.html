Chrysanthemum broussonetii

Chrysanthemum broussonetii, commonly known as the Chinese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a range of colors. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in the fall.

Chrysanthemum broussonetii is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes and has cultural significance in traditional Chinese medicine.
