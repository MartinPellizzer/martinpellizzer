Cicerbita azurea var. glabra

Cicerbita azurea var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth blue cicerbit, is a member of the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves that are typically lobed and aromatic. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers in umbels, which are clustered in a flat-topped arrangement. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
