Cinnamomum chekiangense

Cinnamomum chekiangense, commonly known as Chinese cinnamon or Cinnamomum chekiangense, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark, which contain essential oils. The plant typically grows in subtropical regions and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are elliptical with a glossy surface and a strong cinnamon scent.

The bark is often harvested for its aromatic properties and traditional medicinal uses.
