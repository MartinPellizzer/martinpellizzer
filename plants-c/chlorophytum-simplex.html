Chlorophytum simplex

Chlorophytum simplex, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green stripes. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright green, balloon-like seed pods. It is a hardy plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Chlorophytum simplex is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
