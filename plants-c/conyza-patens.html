Conyza patens

Conyza patens, commonly known as the common ironweed, is an annual flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows up to 1.5 meters tall, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its small yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom from late summer into early autumn.

Conyza patens is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
