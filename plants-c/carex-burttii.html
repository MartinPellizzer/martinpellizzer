Carex burttii

Carex burttii, commonly known as Burtt's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, grasses, and related plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
