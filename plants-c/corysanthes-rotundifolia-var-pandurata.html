Corysanthes rotundifolia var. pandurata

Corysanthes rotundifolia var. pandurata, commonly known as the broad-leafed corysanthes, is a species native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
