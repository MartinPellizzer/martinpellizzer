Cardiospermum corindum f. canescens

Cardiospermum corindum f. canescens, commonly known as the hairy heartseed.

This variant of the heartseed plant is characterized by its hairy foliage and stems, which provide a distinguishing feature from the typical form. It is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found in open habitats. The plant produces distinctive heart-shaped fruits that enclose its seeds, a feature typical of the genus Cardiospermum.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological study.
