Calla radicans

Calla radicans, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant produces striking white or cream-colored spatulate flowers that are often surrounded by a showy purple bract. It thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Calla radicans is often used in water gardens or as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and adaptability.
