Cistus populifolius f. narbonensis

Cistus populifolius f. narbonensis, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, particularly found in southern France and Spain.

It features aromatic, grey-green leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped, adapted to withstand dry and rocky conditions. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its root system is extensive, helping to stabilize soil in arid environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
