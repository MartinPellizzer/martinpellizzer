Crotalaria fysonii var. glabra

Crotalaria fysonii var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth crotalaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
