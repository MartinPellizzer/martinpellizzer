Cardamine glara

Cardamine glara, commonly known as narrowleaf watercress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small white or pale yellow flowers. Native to moist, calcareous habitats, it thrives in wet meadows and stream sides. The plant is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native environment.
