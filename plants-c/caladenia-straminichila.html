Caladenia straminichila

Caladenia straminichila, commonly known as the greenhood orchid, is a species native to southeastern Australia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in heathland and woodland habitats. The plant features a single, erect stem with a single leaf and a distinctive green hood-shaped labellum. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a narrow, elongated shape.

Caladenia straminichila is known for its unique morphology and is often found in areas with well-drained soils.
