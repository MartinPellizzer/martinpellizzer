Cardamine fagetina

Cardamine fagetina, commonly known as the alpine buttercup, is a small herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers with five petals. It is often found in rocky soils and alpine meadows, growing at high altitudes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species in many areas.
