Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. amphitropa

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. amphitropa, commonly known as the "Love Plant" or "Heartleaf," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
