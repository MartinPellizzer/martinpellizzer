Capnophyllum leiocarpon

Capnophyllum leiocarpon, commonly known as the smooth-leaved capnophyllum, is a rare and specialized plant species found in specific alpine environments.

It is characterized by its small, grass-like appearance with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense spikes, often blooming in late summer. Its growth is highly dependent on cool, moist conditions and well-drained soils.
