Collare-stuartense micklowii

Collare-stuartense micklowii, commonly known as the Stuart's collared orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a distinctive collar-like feature around the base of the flower. The plant typically grows in nutrient-poor, acidic soils and requires a humid, shaded environment to thrive. Its flowers are usually small and colorful, often displaying shades of pink and white.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species, making conservation efforts essential for its survival.
