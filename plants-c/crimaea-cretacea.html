Crimaea cretacea

Crimaea cretacea, commonly known as the Cretan crima, is a rare and unique plant native to the island of Crete.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptation to harsh Mediterranean climates. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit and a deep root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
