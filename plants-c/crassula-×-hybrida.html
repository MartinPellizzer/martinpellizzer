Crassula × hybrida

Crassula × hybrida, commonly known as the Jade Plant, is a popular succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tint, arranged in a compact, bushy shape. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Crassula × hybrida is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
