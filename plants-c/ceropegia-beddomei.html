Ceropegia beddomei

Ceropegia beddomei, commonly known as the silver leaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tuberous roots and produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
