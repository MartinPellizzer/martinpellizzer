Cistus × pourretii

Cistus × pourretii, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or dry landscapes.

Cistus × pourretii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
