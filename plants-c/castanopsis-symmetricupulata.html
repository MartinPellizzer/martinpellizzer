Castanopsis symmetricupulata

Castanopsis symmetricupulata, commonly known as the symmetrical evergreen oak, is a species of flowering plant in the Fagaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and its ability to thrive in humid, temperate climates. Its fruit is a nut enclosed in a woody cupule, typical of the Fagaceae family.

Castanopsis symmetricupulata plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
