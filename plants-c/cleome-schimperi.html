Cleome schimperi

Cleome schimperi, commonly known as the "spider plant" or "flowering spurge," is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Cleome schimperi is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
