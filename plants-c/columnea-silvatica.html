Columnea silvatica

Columnea silvatica, commonly known as the silverleaf columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features elliptical, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Columnea silvatica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
