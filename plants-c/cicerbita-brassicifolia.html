Cicerbita brassicifolia

Cicerbita brassicifolia, commonly known as the field cress, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides.

Cicerbita brassicifolia is an annual herb that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.
