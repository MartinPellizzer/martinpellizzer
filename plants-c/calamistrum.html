Calamistrum

Calamistrum, commonly known as the "sickle plant," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or white. They are adapted to survive in dry conditions, with some species exhibiting succulent properties to store water.
