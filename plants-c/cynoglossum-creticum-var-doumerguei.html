Cynoglossum creticum var. doumerguei

Cynoglossum creticum var. doumerguei, commonly known as the Doumergue's forget-me-not, is a rare and striking plant native to southern France.

It is characterized by its deep blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and slightly lobed. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique coloration and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
