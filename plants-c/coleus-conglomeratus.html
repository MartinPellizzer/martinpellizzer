Coleus conglomeratus

Coleus conglomeratus, commonly known as the clustered coleus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which displays shades of red, orange, and purple in varying combinations. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting veins. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden displays and indoor cultivation.
