Capparis arrabidae

Capparis arrabidae, commonly known as the Arrabida caper, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a shrub characterized by its thorny stems and evergreen leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruits are green berries that mature into a dark red color.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
