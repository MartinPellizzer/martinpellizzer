Calceolaria hians

Calceolaria hians, commonly known as the "Chinese hat" or "hanging lantern" plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, spurred flowers that resemble small, hooded lanterns, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Calceolaria hians is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
