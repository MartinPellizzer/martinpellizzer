Centunculus pulcherrimus

Centunculus pulcherrimus, commonly known as the pretty centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Primulaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
