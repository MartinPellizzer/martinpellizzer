Crassula subulata

Crassula subulata, commonly known as the Needle Leaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of tiny, needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Crassula subulata is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
