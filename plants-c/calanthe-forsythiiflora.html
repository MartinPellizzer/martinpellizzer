Calanthe forsythiiflora

Calanthe forsythiiflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and striking yellow flowers with a distinctive greenish throat. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

Calanthe forsythiiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
