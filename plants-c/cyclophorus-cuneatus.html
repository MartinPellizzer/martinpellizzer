Cyclophorus cuneatus

Cyclophorus cuneatus, commonly known as the narrowleaf cyclophorus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to parts of southern Africa, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
