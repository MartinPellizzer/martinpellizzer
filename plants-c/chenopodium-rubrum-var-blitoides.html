Chenopodium rubrum var. blitoides

Chenopodium rubrum var. blitoides, commonly known as red goosefoot, is a herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features alternate, ovate to rhombic leaves that are typically red-veined and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, which are followed by triangular, beak-like fruits. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered a common weed in agricultural settings.
