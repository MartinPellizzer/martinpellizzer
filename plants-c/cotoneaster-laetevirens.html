Cotoneaster laetevirens

Cotoneaster laetevirens, commonly known as the evergreen cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, sparsely branched structure. The plant features small, glossy green leaves that turn red in the fall, adding ornamental value. Its showy clusters of white flowers appear in spring, followed by bright red berries in autumn.

Cotoneaster laetevirens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardiness and attractive seasonal display.
