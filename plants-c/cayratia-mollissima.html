Cayratia mollissima

Cayratia mollissima, commonly known as velvetleaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in some areas.
