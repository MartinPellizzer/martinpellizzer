Castanea vesca var. computensis

Castanea vesca var. computensis, commonly known as the European hornbeam.

This variant is a deciduous tree or shrub native to Europe, characterized by its smooth, gray bark and oval-shaped leaves. It produces small, spiky fruits called nuts, which are encased in a spiny husk. The plant is adapted to a variety of soil types and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and its ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
