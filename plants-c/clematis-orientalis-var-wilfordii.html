Clematis orientalis var. wilfordii

Clematis orientalis var. wilfordii, commonly known as the Wilford's oriental clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to deep blue, depending on the pH of the soil. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are typically armed with hooked thorns. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to climb trellises or walls.
