Corchorus elderi

Corchorus elderi, commonly known as the African mallow, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces showy, spiky flowers that range in color from red to purple, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its fibrous stems and leaves have been used traditionally for fiber and medicinal purposes.
