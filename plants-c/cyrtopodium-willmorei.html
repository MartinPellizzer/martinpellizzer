Cyrtopodium willmorei

Cyrtopodium willmorei, commonly known as the Willmore's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and long, linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cyrtopodium willmorei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
