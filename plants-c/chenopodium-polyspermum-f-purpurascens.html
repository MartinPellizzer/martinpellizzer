Chenopodium polyspermum f. purpurascens

Chenopodium polyspermum f. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple flowering goosefoot, is a cultivated form of the goosefoot plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple flowers and serrated leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and showy blooms, which appear in late summer.

It is often grown as a decorative plant in gardens and public spaces.
