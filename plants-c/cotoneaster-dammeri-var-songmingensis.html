Cotoneaster dammeri var. songmingensis

Cotoneaster dammeri var. songmingensis, commonly known as the Songming cotoneaster, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in horticulture for its seasonal interest and compact growth habit.
