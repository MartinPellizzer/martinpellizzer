Chlorospatha tokioensis

Chlorospatha tokioensis, commonly known as the Tokyo green fern, is a rare and exotic plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that exhibit a striking emerald-green coloration. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized collections.

Due to its limited natural habitat, it is considered endangered and is often cultivated in controlled settings.
