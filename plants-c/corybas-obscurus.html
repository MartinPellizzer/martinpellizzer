Corybas obscurus

Corybas obscurus, commonly known as the dark helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower that is typically greenish-brown in color. The plant grows in shaded, moist environments, often in rainforest or heathland areas. Its flowers are pollinated by flies, which are attracted to its strong, decaying odor.

Corybas obscurus is a small plant, usually reaching only a few centimeters in height.
