Clerodendrum chinense var. hamrense

Clerodendrum chinense var. hamrense, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside, and its stems are woody with a reddish-brown hue. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
