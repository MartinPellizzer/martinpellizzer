Citrus × limetta var. fructo-stellata

Citrus × limetta var. fructo-stellata, commonly known as the starfruit or carambola, is a unique citrus hybrid characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit.

The plant is a small evergreen shrub or tree with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit develops in a symmetrical, five-lobed shape, turning from green to yellow-orange when ripe. The fruit's structure is adapted for seed dispersal, with each segment containing a single seed.

This variety is valued for its sweet, tangy flavor and is cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
