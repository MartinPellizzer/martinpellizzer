Colona serratifolia var. floribunda

Colona serratifolia var. floribunda, commonly known as the fragrant serrated colona, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its aromatic foliage and showy blooms make it a popular ornamental plant.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
