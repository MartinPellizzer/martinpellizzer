Cyperus ehrenbergii f. fulvescens

Cyperus ehrenbergii f. fulvescens, commonly known as the red-flowered flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flower heads. The plant produces reddish-brown flowers that are often subtended by bracts, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies.

This species is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it plays a role in aquatic ecosystems.
