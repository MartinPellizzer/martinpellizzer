Chenopodium macrospermum f. subviride

Chenopodium macrospermum f. subviride, commonly known as the greenleaf goosefoot, is a variant of the common goosefoot plant.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in disturbed soils and arid environments. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.

This subspecies is notable for its adaptability and is often found in regions with temperate to semi-arid climates.
