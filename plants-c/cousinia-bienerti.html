Cousinia bienerti

Cousinia bienerti, commonly known as the Kharak Valley bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, which helps reduce water loss.

Cousinia bienerti is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in alpine meadows.
