Cynodon gracilis

Cynodon gracilis, commonly known as slender couch grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and tufted inflorescences that produce small, pale green to brown flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in lawns, pastures, and disturbed areas. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly and form dense mats.

Cynodon gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in erosion control due to its hardy nature.
