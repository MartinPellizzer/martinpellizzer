Cynoglossum montanum var. asiaticum

Cynoglossum montanum var. asiaticum, commonly known as the Asian bellflower, is a perennial herb native to regions of Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white down. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
