Cymbidium hoosai f. hakuran

Cymbidium hoosai f. hakuran, commonly known as the Hakuran Cymbidium, is a hybrid orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
