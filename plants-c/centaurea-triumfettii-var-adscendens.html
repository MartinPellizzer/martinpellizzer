Centaurea triumfettii var. adscendens

Centaurea triumfettii var. adscendens, commonly known as the ascending centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features bright yellow flowers with red-orange centers, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in poor soils.
