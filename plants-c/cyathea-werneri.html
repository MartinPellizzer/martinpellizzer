Cyathea werneri

Cyathea werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in favorable conditions. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, providing structural support and aiding in nutrient absorption.

Cyathea werneri is often found in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests.
