Chrysanthemum lushanense

Chrysanthemum lushanense, commonly known as the Lushan chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in temperate climates. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered blooms.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
