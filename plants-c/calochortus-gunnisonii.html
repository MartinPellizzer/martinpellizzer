Calochortus gunnisonii

Calochortus gunnisonii, commonly known as Gunnison's mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes various species of mariposa lilies known for their showy, showy blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with a distinctive, cup-like structure.

It is often found in open habitats such as meadows and slopes, where it thrives in full sun.
