Calytrix brachychaeta var. tenuifolia

Calytrix brachychaeta var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal or sandy habitats and has narrow, linear leaves. It is often found in shrubland or heath communities, and it produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
