Cynanchum asterion

Cynanchum asterion, commonly known as the star-leaf milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes many milkweed species known for their milky sap. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Cynanchum asterion is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
