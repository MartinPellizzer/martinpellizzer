Crinum purpurascens

Crinum purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from white to deep purple, often with a violet or red hue. The plant produces tall, sturdy stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with lance-shaped, strap-like leaves. Its bulbs are adapted for storing water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Crinum purpurascens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to bloom reliably in warm climates.
