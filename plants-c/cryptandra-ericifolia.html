Cryptandra ericifolia

Cryptandra ericifolia, commonly known as the heath cryptandra, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It typically grows in heathlands and sandy soils, preferring well-drained conditions.

Cryptandra ericifolia is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal and dry environments.
