Cistus × porphyreus

Cistus × porphyreus, commonly known as the purple rock rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple. The plant is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, thriving in full sun conditions. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cistus × porphyreus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
