Calamagrostis lanceolata f. aramosa

Calamagrostis lanceolata f. aramosa, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to eastern North America.

It features tall, slender culms that are covered in a silvery-white bloom, giving it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces fluffy, light-green plumes that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

This cultivar is valued for its elegant form and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental appeal.
