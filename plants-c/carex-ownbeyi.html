Carex ownbeyi

Carex ownbeyi, commonly known as Ownbey's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats. Its slender, triangular stems are often clustered, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early summer.
