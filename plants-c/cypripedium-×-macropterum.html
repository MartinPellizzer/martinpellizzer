Cypripedium × macropterum

Cypripedium × macropterum, commonly known as the large-flowered slipper orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble a slipper, with a distinctive pouch-like structure at the base. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on the forest floor or in rocky crevices. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces a single, large, fragrant flower per stem.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
