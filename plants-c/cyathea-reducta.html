Cyathea reducta

Cyathea reducta, commonly known as the dwarf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and a crown of feathery, compound leaves. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is typically found in humid, shaded environments. Cyathea reducta has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular specimen plant in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
