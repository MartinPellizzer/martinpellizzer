Chrysopsis hirsutissima

Chrysopsis hirsutissima, commonly known as the hairy goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
