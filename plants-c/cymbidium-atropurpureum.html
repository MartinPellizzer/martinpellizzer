Cymbidium atropurpureum

Cymbidium atropurpureum, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with long, sword-shaped leaves that form a dense rosette. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous showy flowers with a striking purple to reddish hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cymbidium atropurpureum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
