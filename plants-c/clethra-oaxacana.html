Clethra oaxacana

Clethra oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca clethra, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Mexico.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Clethra oaxacana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
