Cattleya speciosissima

Cattleya speciosissima, commonly known as the Imperial Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, featuring vibrant pink and white petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers emit a sweet, spicy fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cattleya speciosissima is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
