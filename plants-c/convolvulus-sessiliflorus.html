Convolvulus sessiliflorus

Convolvulus sessiliflorus, commonly known as the sessileflower morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale purple to white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it adaptable to various garden settings.
