Carduus personata var. albidus

Carduus personata var. albidus, commonly known as the white thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features striking silver-blue leaves with spiny margins and produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is characterized by its robust root system and is often found in dry, disturbed soils. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
