Croton glandulosus var. floridanus

Croton glandulosus var. floridanus, commonly known as the Florida croton, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features vibrant, colorful foliage with leaves that can range from green to red, orange, or yellow, depending on the season and light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its botanical interest lies in its unique leaf coloration and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
