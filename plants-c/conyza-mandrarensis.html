Conyza mandrarensis

Conyza mandrarensis, commonly known as the mountain aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble clusters of tiny flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh, dry conditions. Its leaves are often narrow and hairy, helping to reduce water loss.

Conyza mandrarensis produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
