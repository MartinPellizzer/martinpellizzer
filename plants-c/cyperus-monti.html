Cyperus monti

Cyperus monti, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in moist or wet environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. Its slender, grass-like stems are often found in dense clusters, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets.

Cyperus monti is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and filter water.
