Colquhounia coccinea var. vestita

Colquhounia coccinea var. vestita, commonly known as the velvet flame vine, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The leaves are typically ovate and covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

This variety is a cultivar of Colquhounia coccinea, known for its vibrant color and unique foliage.
