Calophyllum diepenhorstii

Calophyllum diepenhorstii, commonly known as the Diepenhorst's torch tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a glossy appearance.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic wood.
