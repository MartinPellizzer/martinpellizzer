Croton monticola

Croton monticola, commonly known as the mountain croton, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton monticola is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its potential medicinal properties.
