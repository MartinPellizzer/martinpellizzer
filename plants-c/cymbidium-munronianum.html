Cymbidium munronianum

Cymbidium munronianum, commonly known as the Munro's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in winter or early spring. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, velvety texture. It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Cymbidium munronianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
