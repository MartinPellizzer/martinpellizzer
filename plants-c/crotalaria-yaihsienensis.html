Crotalaria yaihsienensis

Crotalaria yaihsienensis, commonly known as the Yaihsien false cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed soils or open woodlands. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the pods contain small, hard seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and research settings.
