Callistemon × nyallingensis

Callistemon × nyallingensis, commonly known as the Sydney Red Bottlebrush, is a hybrid shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange bottlebrush-like inflorescences, which are composed of numerous dense, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 2–4 meters and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds and insects.
