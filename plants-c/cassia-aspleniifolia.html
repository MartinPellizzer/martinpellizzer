Cassia aspleniifolia

Cassia aspleniifolia, commonly known as the fernleaf cassia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are often light green to yellowish in color. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Cassia aspleniifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
