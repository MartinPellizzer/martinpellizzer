Carum amomum

Carum amomum, commonly known as star anise, is a perennial herb native to China and Vietnam.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like carrots and parsley. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped fruit, which is harvested for its essential oils and medicinal properties. The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory and digestive benefits.

Its distinctive aroma comes from compounds such as shikimic acid and anethole.
