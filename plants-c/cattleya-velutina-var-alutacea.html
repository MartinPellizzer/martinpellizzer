Cattleya velutina var. alutacea

Cattleya velutina var. alutacea, commonly known as the velvety cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, velvety-textured flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically produces large, showy blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
