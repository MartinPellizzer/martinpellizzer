Canarium kostermansii

Canarium kostermansii, commonly known as the Kostermans' candlenut, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. The tree also plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
