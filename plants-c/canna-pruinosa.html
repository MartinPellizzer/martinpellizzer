Canna pruinosa

Canna pruinosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to the warm climates of the Americas.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are softly hairy, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, spadix-like flowers surrounded by vibrant, colorful bracts that can range from yellow to red. Canna pruinosa thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical appeal makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and water features.
