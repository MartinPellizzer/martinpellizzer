Calliandra inermis

Calliandra inermis, commonly known as the silky calliandra, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that produce showy, pink to red flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, delicate leaflets and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Calliandra inermis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in sustainable gardening practices.
