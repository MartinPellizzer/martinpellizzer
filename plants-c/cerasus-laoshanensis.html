Cerasus laoshanensis

Cerasus laoshanensis, commonly known as the Laoshan cherry, is a species of Prunus native to China.

It is characterized by its small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, red fruits that are edible but not widely consumed. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
