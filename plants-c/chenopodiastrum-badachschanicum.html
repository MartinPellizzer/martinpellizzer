Chenopodiastrum badachschanicum

Chenopodiastrum badachschanicum, commonly known as the Badakhshan goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and erect, branched stems. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to dry conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in green or reddish hues.

It is sometimes cultivated for its edible leaves, which are rich in nutrients.
