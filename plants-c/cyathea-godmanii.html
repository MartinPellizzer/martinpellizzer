Cyathea godmanii

Cyathea godmanii, commonly known as the Godman's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its fronds are typically bright green and can develop a whitish bloom on the underside.

Cyathea godmanii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
