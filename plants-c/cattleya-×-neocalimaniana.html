Cattleya × neocalimaniana

Cattleya × neocalimaniana, commonly known as the Neocalimaniana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a contrasting lip.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive.
