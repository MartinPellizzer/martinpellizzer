Coelogyne rubens

Coelogyne rubens, commonly known as the red flame orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers, which are often adorned with yellow and green markings. The plant has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Coelogyne rubens is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
