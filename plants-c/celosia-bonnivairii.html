Celosia bonnivairii

Celosia bonnivairii, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright red bracts that resemble flames. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by colorful, papery bracts. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
