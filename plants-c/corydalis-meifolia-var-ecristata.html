Corydalis meifolia var. ecristata

Corydalis meifolia var. ecristata, commonly known as the white-flowered corydalis, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive, early spring blossoms.
