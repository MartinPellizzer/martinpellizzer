Carex rufulistolon

Carex rufulistolon, commonly known as the red-stem sedges, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, strap-shaped leaves and cylindrical, spike-like inflorescences. Carex rufulistolon plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
