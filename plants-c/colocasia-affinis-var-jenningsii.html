Colocasia affinis var. jenningsii

Colocasia affinis var. jenningsii, commonly known as the Blue Congo arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, with a deep blue-green coloration. The plant produces striking white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

This variety is popular in water gardens and as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.
