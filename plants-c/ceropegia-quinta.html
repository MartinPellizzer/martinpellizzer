Ceropegia quinta

Ceropegia quinta, commonly known as the String of Hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and pairs of heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Ceropegia quinta is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored plant among beginners and experienced growers alike.
