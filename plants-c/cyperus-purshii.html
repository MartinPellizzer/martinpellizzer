Cyperus purshii

Cyperus purshii, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green or purple in color. Cyperus purshii thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

It is an important species for wetland ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife.
