Callerya reticulata var. stenophylla

Callerya reticulata var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf callerya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
