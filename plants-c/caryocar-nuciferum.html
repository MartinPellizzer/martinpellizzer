Caryocar nuciferum

Caryocar nuciferum, commonly known as the Brazil nut tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Caryocaraceae and is characterized by its large, compound leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The tree produces large, woody fruits that contain multiple seeds, which are the edible Brazil nuts. The botanical features of this plant include its unique fruit morphology and the presence of a thick, leathery outer layer.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in the Amazon rainforest.
