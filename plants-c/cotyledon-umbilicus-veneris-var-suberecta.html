Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. suberecta

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. suberecta, commonly known as the "Cape Sunset" or "Cotyledon sunset," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically reddish-pink to purple, with a waxy texture that helps it store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it a visually appealing addition to any succulent collection.
