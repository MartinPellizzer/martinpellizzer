Ceropegia spiralis

Ceropegia spiralis, commonly known as the spiral string plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in umbels. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a slightly waxy texture.

Ceropegia spiralis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
