Coelostegia kostermansii

Coelostegia kostermansii, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its showy, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a strong fragrance. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
