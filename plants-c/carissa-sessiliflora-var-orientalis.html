Carissa sessiliflora var. orientalis

Carissa sessiliflora var. orientalis, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and produces fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its fruit is a small, yellow, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of aromatic foliage.
