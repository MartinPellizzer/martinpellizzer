Corybas recurvus

Corybas recurvus, commonly known as the recurved hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its unique, hood-like flower that is typically green or brown in color. The plant produces a single, showy flower that is often adorned with intricate patterns and a distinctive, recurved lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or rocky areas.

Corybas recurvus is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
