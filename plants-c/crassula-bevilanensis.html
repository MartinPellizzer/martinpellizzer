Crassula bevilanensis

Crassula bevilanensis, commonly known as the White Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for succulent gardens and indoor plant collections.
