Cienfuegosia affinis var. humilis

Cienfuegosia affinis var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf cienfuegosia, is a small shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of white or pale pink.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in tropical and subtropical climates.
