Ctenodon histrix var. histrix

Ctenodon histrix var. histrix, commonly known as the hairy ctenodon, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hairy stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its foliage is often covered in fine, stiff hairs, which provide some protection against herbivores.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
