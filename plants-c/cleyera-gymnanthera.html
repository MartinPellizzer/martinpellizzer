Cleyera gymnanthera

Cleyera gymnanthera, commonly known as the glossy leaf cleyera, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and often have a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by orange-red berries in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Cleyera gymnanthera is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to provide seasonal interest.
