Cuspidaria ovalis

Cuspidaria ovalis, commonly known as the oval spikerush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Cuspidaria ovalis plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
