Cinnamomum szechuanense

Cinnamomum szechuanense, commonly known as Sichuan cinnamon, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries. Its bark contains essential oils and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The species is valued for its unique fragrance and potential medicinal properties.
