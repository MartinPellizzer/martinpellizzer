Cynoglossum officinale var. virens

Cynoglossum officinale var. virens, commonly known as blueweed or narrow-leaved bugloss, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant is typically found in dry, disturbed soils and is often seen along roadsides and in grasslands.
