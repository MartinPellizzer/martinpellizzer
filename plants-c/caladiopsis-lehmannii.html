Caladiopsis lehmannii

Caladiopsis lehmannii, commonly known as the lehmann's caladium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and exhibit a wide range of variegated patterns in green, white, and red. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
