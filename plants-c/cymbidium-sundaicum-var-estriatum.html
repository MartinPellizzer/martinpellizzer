Cymbidium sundaicum var. estriatum

Cymbidium sundaicum var. estriatum, commonly known as the Sunda Orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The flowers typically feature a vibrant orange lip with contrasting purple and white stripes, making it highly ornamental. This variety is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a range of environmental conditions.

It is widely cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and floral arrangements.
