Campnosperma micranteium

Campnosperma micranteium, commonly known as the small-fruited boxwood, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, urn-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruits are woody capsules that contain several seeds.
