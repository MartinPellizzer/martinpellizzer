Croton leucadenius

Croton leucadenius, commonly known as the white croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored in shades of green, red, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Croton leucadenius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
