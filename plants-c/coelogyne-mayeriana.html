Coelogyne mayeriana

Coelogyne mayeriana, commonly known as the Mayer's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, often white or pale yellow blossoms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne mayeriana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
