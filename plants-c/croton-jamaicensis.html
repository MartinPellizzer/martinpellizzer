Croton jamaicensis

Croton jamaicensis, commonly known as Jamaican croton, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many plants with milky sap. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, often with a striking yellow or orange coloration. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive orange or red berries.

Croton jamaicensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for landscaping purposes.
