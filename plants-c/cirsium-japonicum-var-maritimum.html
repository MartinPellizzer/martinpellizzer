Cirsium japonicum var. maritimum

Cirsium japonicum var. maritimum, commonly known as sea holly, is a coastal plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, blue to purple flowers and silver-gray, fuzzy leaves. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found along shorelines and dunes. Its robust root system helps stabilize coastal soils and prevent erosion.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate salt spray.
