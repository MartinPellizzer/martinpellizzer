Crotalaria cupricola

Crotalaria cupricola, commonly known as copperleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive copper-colored leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It has a fibrous root system and is typically found in disturbed soils or open habitats.

Crotalaria cupricola is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a useful plant in certain ecological contexts.
