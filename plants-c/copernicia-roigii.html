Copernicia roigii

Copernicia roigii, commonly known as the Mexican palm or roig's palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its slender, erect trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by oval, fibrous fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts, and the palm is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Copernicia roigii thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
