Cistus populifolius var. celtibericus

Cistus populifolius var. celtibericus, commonly known as the Celtiberian rock rose, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and clusters of pale pink to white flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.

Its adaptability to harsh environments makes it a resilient species in its native habitat.
