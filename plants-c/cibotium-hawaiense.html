Cibotium hawaiense

Cibotium hawaiense, commonly known as the Hawaiian tree fern, is a distinctive member of the Dicksoniaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various native species.
