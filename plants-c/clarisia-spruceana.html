Clarisia spruceana

Clarisia spruceana, commonly known as the wax plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces clusters of vibrant orange or yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions.
