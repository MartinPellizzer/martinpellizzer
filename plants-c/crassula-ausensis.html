Crassula ausensis

Crassula ausensis, commonly known as the Cape Town crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Crassula ausensis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
