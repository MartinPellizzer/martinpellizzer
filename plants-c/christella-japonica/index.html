Christella Japonica

Christella japonica is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to Japan. It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is known for its unique, bell-shaped blooms. This plant thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation. Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and needs conservation efforts to survive.
