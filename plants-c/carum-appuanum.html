Carum appuanum

Carum appuanum, commonly known as the Appuan caraway, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like parsley and dill. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the family. Its leaves are finely divided and have a mild, aromatic scent.
