Cryptocoryne wendtii var. rubella

Cryptocoryne wendtii var. rubella, commonly known as the red velvet cryptocoryne, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking deep red to purple coloration, which is most vivid in low-light conditions. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that form dense, bushy growths, making it an excellent choice for foreground or midground in aquariums. It is relatively easy to care for, thriving in a variety of water conditions with moderate to high light.

Its vibrant color and hardy nature make it a favorite among both beginner and advanced aquarists.
