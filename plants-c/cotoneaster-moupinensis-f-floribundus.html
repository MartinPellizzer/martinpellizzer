Cotoneaster moupinensis f. floribundus

Cotoneaster moupinensis f. floribundus, commonly known as the flowering cotoneaster, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features attractive, glossy dark green leaves that turn reddish in autumn. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that persist into winter. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens for its seasonal interest.

This cultivar is relatively low maintenance and thrives in a variety of soil types.
