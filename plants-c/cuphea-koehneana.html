Cuphea koehneana

Cuphea koehneana, commonly known as the velvetleaf cuphea, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Cuphea koehneana is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
