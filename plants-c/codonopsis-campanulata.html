Codonopsis campanulata

Codonopsis campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped codonopsis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Codonopsis campanulata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
