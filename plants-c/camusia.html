Camusia

Camusia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. Camusia is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed soils.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
