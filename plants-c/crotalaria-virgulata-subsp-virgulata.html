Crotalaria virgulata subsp. virgulata

Crotalaria virgulata subsp.

virgulata, commonly known as the slender crotalaria, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It features erect, branched stems and palmate leaves with three to five leaflets.

The plant produces yellow, spurred flowers that are characteristic of the Crotalaria genus. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a weed in agricultural settings.
