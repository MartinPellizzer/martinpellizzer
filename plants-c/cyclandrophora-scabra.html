Cyclandrophora scabra

Cyclandrophora scabra, commonly known as the rough-stemmed sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant has small, scale-like leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland dunes.
