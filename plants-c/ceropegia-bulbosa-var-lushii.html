Ceropegia bulbosa var. lushii

Ceropegia bulbosa var. lushii, commonly known as the Lushii string of hearts, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features cascading stems with heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often pale pink or white, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its unique foliage and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
