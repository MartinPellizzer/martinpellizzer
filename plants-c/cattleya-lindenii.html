Cattleya lindenii

Cattleya lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and sometimes include a greenish tinge. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants rather than in soil.

Cattleya lindenii is prized for its beauty and fragrance, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
