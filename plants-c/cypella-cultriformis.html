Cypella cultriformis

Cypella cultriformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cypella, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact cluster. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland areas.
