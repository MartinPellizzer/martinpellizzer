Calceolaria stellariifolia

Calceolaria stellariifolia, commonly known as the star-leaved calceolaria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Calceolaria stellariifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and blooms.
