Crotalaria viminea

Crotalaria viminea, commonly known as the slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive, narrow leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elongated seed pods that contain several seeds, typical of the genus Crotalaria. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and savannas.

Due to its ornamental value and nitrogen-fixing ability, it is sometimes cultivated for both aesthetic and agricultural purposes.
