Coleus robustus

Coleus robustus, commonly known as the robust coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is known for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a textured surface, and it produces small, tubular flowers in clusters.

Coleus robustus thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
