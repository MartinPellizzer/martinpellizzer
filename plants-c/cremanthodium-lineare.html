Cremanthodium lineare

Cremanthodium lineare, commonly known as the linear leaf cremanthodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
