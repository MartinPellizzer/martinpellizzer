Capsella bursa-pastoris f. setchelliana

Capsella bursa-pastoris f. setchelliana, commonly known as the Setchellian prickly lettuce, is a variant of the common weed known as common groundsel.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae, characterized by its small, yellow flowers and hairy stems. The subspecies f. setchelliana is distinguished by its more robust growth and increased prickliness compared to the typical form. Its leaves are typically lobed and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of temperate climates.
