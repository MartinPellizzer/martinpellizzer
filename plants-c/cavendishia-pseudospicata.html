Cavendishia pseudospicata

Cavendishia pseudospicata, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant produces showy, spathaceous bracts that are often more prominent than the actual flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
