Caladium picturatum var. osyanum

Caladium picturatum var. osyanum, commonly known as the Osyanum caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have intricate patterns that resemble painted designs. This variety thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant and visually appealing leaves.
