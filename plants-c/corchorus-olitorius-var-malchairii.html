Corchorus olitorius var. malchairii

Corchorus olitorius var. malchairii, commonly known as Malchair's Ethiopian spinach, is a leafy vegetable native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its tender, edible leaves that are rich in nutrients. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are typically harvested before flowering to ensure maximum flavor and nutritional value.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and potential as a sustainable food source in arid regions.
