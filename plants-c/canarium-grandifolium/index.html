Canarium Grandifolium

Canarium with large leaves, scientifically known as Canarium grandifolium, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. This species is recognized for its broad, glossy foliage that can reach impressive sizes, making it a focal point in lush, humid environments. Its unique appearance has made it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. The plant thrives in warm, moist climates and is often found in lowland forests and coastal areas. Despite its beauty, Canarium grandifolium remains relatively underappreciated in the broader horticultural community.
