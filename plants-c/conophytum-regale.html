Conophytum regale

Conophytum regale, commonly known as the royal conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Conophytum regale is valued for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
