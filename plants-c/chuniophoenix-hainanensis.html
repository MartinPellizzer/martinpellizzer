Chuniophoenix hainanensis

Chuniophoenix hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan fan palm, is a rare and endemic palm species native to the tropical regions of Hainan Island in China.

It is characterized by its slender, robust trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is typically found in lowland rainforests. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces fruit that is an important food source for local wildlife.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
