Cryptandra parvifolia

Cryptandra parvifolia, commonly known as the small-flowered cryptandra, is a flowering plant native to western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
