Calanthe discolor var. amamiana

Calanthe discolor var. amamiana, commonly known as the Amami calanthe, is a rare orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking white and green striped petals and a unique, elongated lip. This plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate beauty to its natural habitat.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native range.
