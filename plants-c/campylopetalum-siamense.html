Campylopetalum siamense

Campylopetalum siamense, commonly known as the Siam flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its environment. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are usually dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Campylopetalum siamense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes due to its dramatic floral display.
