Cynanchum racemosum var. guatemalense

Cynanchum racemosum var. guatemalense, commonly known as Guatemalan milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes many milkweeds known for their milky sap. The plant features slender, ascending stems with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and are typically greenish-white with a distinctive corona.

It is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
