Cyrtochilum fallens

Cyrtochilum fallens, commonly known as the hanging orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous growth habit, with flowers that hang downward from the stems. The plant typically produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of white and pale pink. Cyrtochilum fallens thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
