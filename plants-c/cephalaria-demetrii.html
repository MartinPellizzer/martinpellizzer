Cephalaria demetrii

Cephalaria demetrii, commonly known as the Demetrius' fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small and yellow, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Cephalaria demetrii is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
