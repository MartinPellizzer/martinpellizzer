Calamagrostis suka

Calamagrostis suka, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, silver-blue foliage with a feathery texture, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Calamagrostis suka is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
