Chrysophyllum giganteum

Chrysophyllum giganteum, commonly known as the giant satinleaf, is a tropical tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by yellow or orange berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a slow growth rate.
