Caragana lidou

Caragana lidou, commonly known as the Chinese peashrub, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable for soil enrichment. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.

Caragana lidou is also utilized in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
