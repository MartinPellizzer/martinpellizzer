Clematis yunnanensis var. brevipedunculata

Clematis yunnanensis var. brevipedunculata, commonly known as the Yunnan clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, often with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs, and it thrives in partial shade to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
