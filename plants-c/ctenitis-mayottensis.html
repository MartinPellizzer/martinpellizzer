Ctenitis mayottensis

Ctenitis mayottensis, commonly known as the Mayotte fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Comoros Islands.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizomes are fibrous and contribute to its structural support.

The fronds are typically bright green and have a distinctive, feathery appearance.
