Ciconium multiflorum

Ciconium multiflorum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, brightly colored blooms in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Ciconium multiflorum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
