Clausena dentata var. nana

Clausena dentata var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf sour apple or small-leaf sour apple, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and yellowish-orange fruits that are typically sour in taste. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments. Its botanical features include smooth, often reddish-brown bark and small, clustered inflorescences.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
