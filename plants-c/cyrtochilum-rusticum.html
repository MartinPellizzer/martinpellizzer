Cyrtochilum rusticum

Cyrtochilum rusticum, commonly known as the rugged orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow or brownish flowers that bloom in late summer. Cyrtochilum rusticum is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, often growing in shaded areas with poor soil.

Its unique morphology and ecological niche make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
