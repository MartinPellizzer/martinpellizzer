Collomia Cavanillesii F. Unidentata

Collomia cavanillesii f. unidentata is a rare and fascinating plant native to the western United States. This variant of Collomia cavanillesii is known for its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found in mountainous regions. The plant's unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Its survival is threatened by habitat loss and climate change, highlighting the need for continued research and protection.
