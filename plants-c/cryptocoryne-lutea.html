Cryptocoryne lutea

Cryptocoryne lutea, commonly known as the yellow cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow leaves, which are often variegated with green and white markings. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams. Cryptocoryne lutea is a popular choice for aquariums due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense underwater carpets.
