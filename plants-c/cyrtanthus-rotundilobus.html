Cyrtanthus rotundilobus

Cyrtanthus rotundilobus, commonly known as the round-lobed cyclamen, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive, rounded shape. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers cool, moist conditions.

Cyrtanthus rotundilobus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
