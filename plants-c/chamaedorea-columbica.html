Chamaedorea columbica

Chamaedorea columbica, commonly known as the bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, arching fronds that give it a graceful, palm-like appearance. The plant has a compact, upright growth habit and is well-suited for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for both ornamental and environmental purposes.
