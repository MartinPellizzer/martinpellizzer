Caladium bicolor var. devosianum

Caladium bicolor var. devosianum, commonly known as the flameleaf caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which includes shades of red, orange, and white, often with intricate patterns. The leaves are heart-shaped and can grow up to 12 inches in diameter, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its vibrant, showy leaves rather than its small, inconspicuous flowers.
