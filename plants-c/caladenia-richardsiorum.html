Caladenia richardsiorum

Caladenia richardsiorum, commonly known as Richards' spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and a single, intricately shaped flower. The orchid typically blooms in spring, producing a striking pink or purple flower with a distinctive spider-like appearance. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathland and shrubland environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species.
