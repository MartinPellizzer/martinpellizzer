Ceterach phillipsianum

Ceterach phillipsianum, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and rocky habitats. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to harsh environments, including high winds and salt spray.
