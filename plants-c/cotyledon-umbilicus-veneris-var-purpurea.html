Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. purpurea

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Velvet Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, and somewhat triangular leaves that are typically purple or reddish in color. The leaves are covered in a fine, velvety texture, which gives the plant its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or summer months.

This plant is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
