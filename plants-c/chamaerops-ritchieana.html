Chamaerops ritchieana

Chamaerops ritchieana, commonly known as the Ritchie's date palm, is a species of palm native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has a slender trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark and produces feathery, compound leaves that are arranged in a fan shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces edible fruits that are similar to dates.

The plant is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
