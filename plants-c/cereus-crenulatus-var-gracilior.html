Cereus crenulatus var. gracilior

Cereus crenulatus var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender night bloomer, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces striking white flowers that open at night, emitting a sweet fragrance to attract pollinators. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water.

It is a popular choice for xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
