Cypripedium × alice

Cypripedium × alice, commonly known as the Alice's Lady's Slipper, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the genus Cypripedium.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a key feature of the Cypripedium genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single, fragrant flower per stem.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
