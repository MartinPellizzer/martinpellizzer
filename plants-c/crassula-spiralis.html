Crassula spiralis

Crassula spiralis, commonly known as the spiral crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that form dense, spiraling clusters along its stems. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique spiral growth pattern and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
