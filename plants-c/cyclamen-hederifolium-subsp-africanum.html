Cyclamen hederifolium subsp. africanum

Cyclamen hederifolium subsp.

africanum, commonly known as the African cyclamen, is a species native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers cool, moist environments. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, silky hair, which helps to retain moisture.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
