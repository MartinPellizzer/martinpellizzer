Canthium tenuiflorum

Canthium tenuiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered canthium, is a member of the Rubiaceae family, characterized by its small, simple leaves and showy, often yellow or orange flowers.

Its flowers are usually clustered in terminal or axillary cymes, and the fruit is a dry, dehiscent capsule that releases small, wind-dispersed seeds.
