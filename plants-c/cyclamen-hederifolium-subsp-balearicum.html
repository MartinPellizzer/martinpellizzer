Cyclamen hederifolium subsp. balearicum

Cyclamen hederifolium subsp.

balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink and white.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often grown as a decorative indoor plant. Its foliage and flowers are evergreen, providing ornamental value throughout the year.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and shaded borders in gardens.
