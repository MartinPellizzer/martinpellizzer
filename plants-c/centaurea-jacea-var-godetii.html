Centaurea jacea var. godetii

Centaurea jacea var. godetii, commonly known as the French poppy, is a striking annual herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features vibrant red or orange flowers with a distinctive black center, surrounded by narrow, pointed bracts. The plant has feathery, gray-green foliage that adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Its showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
