Centaurea prostrata f. tenuisecta

Centaurea prostrata f. tenuisecta, commonly known as the slenderleaf sand centaury, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in width, giving it a distinctive, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
