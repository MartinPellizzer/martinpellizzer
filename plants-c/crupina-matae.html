Crupina matae

Crupina matae, commonly known as the Matae grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Pacific, including parts of Polynesia and Micronesia. The plant features erect, cylindrical stems covered in short, stiff hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or purple, with two-lipped petals.
