Calophyllum montanun

Calophyllum montanun, commonly known as mountain calophyllum, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Calophyllaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces woody, aromatic fruits that contain oily seeds used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland and mountainous forest regions.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a significant species in its native habitat.
