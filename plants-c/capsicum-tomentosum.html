Capsicum tomentosum

Capsicum tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, hairy leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into bright red, round fruits resembling tiny tomatoes. Capsicum tomentosum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and edible fruits.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
