Camellia shensiensis

Camellia shensiensis, commonly known as the Sichuan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a yellow center. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. It thrives in partially shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Camellia shensiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
