Cleidion castaneifolium

Cleidion castaneifolium, commonly known as the Brazilian chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with long, slender leaflets. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by oval, smooth, and shiny fruits. Its bark is thick and fissured, often with a reddish or brownish hue.
