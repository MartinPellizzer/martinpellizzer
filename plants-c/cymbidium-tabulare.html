Cymbidium tabulare

Cymbidium tabulare, commonly known as the Tabular Cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces clusters of long, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically blooms with fragrant, showy flowers that are often pale yellow or greenish-white in color. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Cymbidium tabulare is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
