Cattleya labiata var. mendelii

Cattleya labiata var. mendelii, commonly known as the Mendel's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant and showy flowers.

It features large, often fragrant blooms with a distinctive coloration that typically includes pink, white, and yellow hues. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it grows in humid, shaded environments. It has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less fertile soils.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
