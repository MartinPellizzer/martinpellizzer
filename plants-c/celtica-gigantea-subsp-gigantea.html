Celtica gigantea subsp. gigantea

Celtica gigantea subsp.

gigantea, commonly known as the giant celtica, is a tall, herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Europe. It features large, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found near water bodies.

Due to its size and striking appearance, it is a notable species in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
