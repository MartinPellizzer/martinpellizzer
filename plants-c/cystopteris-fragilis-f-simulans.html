Cystopteris fragilis f. simulans

Cystopteris fragilis f. simulans, commonly known as the brittle fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to various regions in the United States, particularly in the southeastern states.
