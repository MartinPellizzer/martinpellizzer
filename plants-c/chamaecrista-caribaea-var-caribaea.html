Chamaecrista caribaea var. caribaea

Chamaecrista caribaea var. caribaea, commonly known as the Carib bean or Carib pea, is a perennial legume native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces clusters of yellow to orange flowers. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and is often found in moist, tropical environments. Its pods are elongated, containing several seeds, and it is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.

This species is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in soil enrichment.
