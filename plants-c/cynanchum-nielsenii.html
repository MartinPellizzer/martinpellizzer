Cynanchum nielsenii

Cynanchum nielsenii, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed or Nielsens milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and it is known for its milky sap, which is a distinguishing feature. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Cynanchum nielsenii is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
