Centaurea aristata subsp. langeana

Centaurea aristata subsp.

langeana, commonly known as the Lange's star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces dense, spiny heads of yellow flowers.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
