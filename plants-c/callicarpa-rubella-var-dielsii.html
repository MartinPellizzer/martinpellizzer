Callicarpa rubella var. dielsii

Callicarpa rubella var. dielsii, commonly known as the red beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of bright red berries that persist through the winter, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly glossy surface. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, often found in moist woodland areas.

The plant is also valued for its fragrant flowers and its role in supporting local wildlife.
