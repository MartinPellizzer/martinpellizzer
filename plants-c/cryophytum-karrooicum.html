Cryophytum karrooicum

Cryophytum karrooicum, commonly known as the Karoo iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it adaptable to harsh, dry conditions.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
