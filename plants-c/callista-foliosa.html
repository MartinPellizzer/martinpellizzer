Callista foliosa

Callista foliosa, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant has elongated, narrow leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Callista foliosa is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
