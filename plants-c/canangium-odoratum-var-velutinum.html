Canangium odoratum var. velutinum

Canangium odoratum var. velutinum, commonly known as the velvet flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a velvety texture, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant also has a mild fragrance, which contributes to its appeal in gardens and landscapes.
