Chelone campanuloides

Chelone campanuloides, commonly known as the Canadian bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to blue or purple, depending on soil pH. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Chelone campanuloides is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and woodland areas.
