Calonemorchis filamentosa

Calonemorchis filamentosa, commonly known as the filamentous calonemorchis, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, thread-like stems and small, delicate flowers that often bloom in clusters. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in moist, organic-rich soils.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicately balanced species in its ecosystem.
