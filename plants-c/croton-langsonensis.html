Croton langsonensis

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. Croton langsonensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
