Camellia thailandica

Camellia thailandica, commonly known as the Thai camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Thailand.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia thailandica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
