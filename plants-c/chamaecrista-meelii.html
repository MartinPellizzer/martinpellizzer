Chamaecrista meelii

Chamaecrista meelii, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in dense, branched clusters, which bloom during the dry season. Its small, bean-like pods contain seeds that are often dispersed by wind.

Chamaecrista meelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
