Caladium akkermansii

Caladium akkermansii, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a bright green background. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Caladium akkermansii prefers partial to full shade and is best suited for container gardening or as a houseplant.
