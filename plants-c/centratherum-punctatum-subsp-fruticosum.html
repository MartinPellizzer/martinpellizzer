Centratherum punctatum subsp. fruticosum

Centratherum punctatum subsp.

fruticosum, commonly known as the clustered centatherum, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact form.
