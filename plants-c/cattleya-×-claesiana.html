Cattleya × claesiana

Cattleya × claesiana, commonly known as the Claesiana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning it can grow on other plants without being parasitic.

The species is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
