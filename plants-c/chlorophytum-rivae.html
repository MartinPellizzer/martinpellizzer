Chlorophytum rivae

Chlorophytum rivae, commonly known as the river spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is a succulent that prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum rivae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
