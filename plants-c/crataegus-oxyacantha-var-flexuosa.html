Crataegus oxyacantha var. flexuosa

Crataegus oxyacantha var. flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible hawthorn, is a variant of the common hawthorn.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant is characterized by its arching, flexible branches and clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit, known as a pome, is typically red or orange and contains several seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in hedges or as a specimen plant in gardens.
