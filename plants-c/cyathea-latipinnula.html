Cyathea latipinnula

Cyathea latipinnula, commonly known as the broad-pinioned tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant's fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to several meters. Cyathea latipinnula thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
