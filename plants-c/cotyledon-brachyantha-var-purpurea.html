Cotyledon brachyantha var. purpurea

Cotyledon brachyantha var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple leaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a striking purple or reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
