Calluna vulgaris f. bicolor

Calluna vulgaris f. bicolor, commonly known as the heather with two colors, is a variant of the common heather.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to western Europe. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, creating a striking contrast. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, providing a dense, cushion-like appearance.

This cultivar is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in acidic, well-drained soils.
