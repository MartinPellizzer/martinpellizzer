Chirita humilis

Chirita humilis, commonly known as the slender leaf chireta, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Chirita humilis is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
