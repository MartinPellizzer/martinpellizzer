Cuspidaria pulchra

Cuspidaria pulchra, commonly known as the pretty spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it contains toxic sap, so care should be taken when handling it.
