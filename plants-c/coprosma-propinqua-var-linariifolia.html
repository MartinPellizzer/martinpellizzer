Coprosma propinqua var. linariifolia

Coprosma propinqua var. linariifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by dark blue berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in coastal and rocky habitats, often growing in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
