Cardamine argyi

Cardamine argyi, commonly known as the Chinese water shield, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It typically grows in moist or aquatic environments, such as marshes and stream banks. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy coating.

Cardamine argyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
