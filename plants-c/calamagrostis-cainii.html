Calamagrostis cainii

Calamagrostis cainii, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, silver-green plumes that give it a striking visual appearance. The plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a soft, silky texture that adds to its ornamental value.

Calamagrostis cainii is valued in landscaping for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
