Clerodendrum Bakeri

Clerodendrum bakeri is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. This species belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants. Its unique foliage and long blooming season make it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and water conditions to flourish.
