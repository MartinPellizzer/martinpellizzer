Cistus tuberaria

Cistus tuberaria, commonly known as the tuberous rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces showy, pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually small, leathery, and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Cistus tuberaria is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
