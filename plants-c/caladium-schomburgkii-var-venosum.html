Caladium schomburgkii var. venosum

Caladium schomburgkii var. venosum, commonly known as the veiny caladium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with intricate, vein-patterned foliage that displays vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its dramatic leaf patterns.

Caladium schomburgkii var. venosum is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas where it can flourish without direct sunlight.
