Cylicodaphne floribunda

Cylicodaphne floribunda, commonly known as the fragrant sago palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
