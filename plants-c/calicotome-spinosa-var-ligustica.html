Calicotome spinosa var. ligustica

Calicotome spinosa var. ligustica, commonly known as the Ligurian broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, silvery hairs. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight bluish tint.

It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland scrub habitats.
