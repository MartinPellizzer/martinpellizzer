Cyrtochilum angustatum

Cyrtochilum angustatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more textured. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
