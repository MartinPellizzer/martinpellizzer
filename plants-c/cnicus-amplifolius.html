Cnicus amplifolius

Cnicus amplifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds. Cnicus amplifolius is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in agricultural fields.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
