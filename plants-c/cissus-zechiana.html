Cissus zechiana

Cissus zechiana, commonly known as the African nettle or climbing fig, is a member of the Vitaceae family.

It is a vigorous climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with a distinctive network of veins, giving it its common name. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits.

Cissus zechiana thrives in humid environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage.
