Cyathea rengifoi

Cyathea rengifoi, commonly known as the Rengifoi tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant exhibits a unique growth pattern, with the trunk forming from the base of the fronds rather than a root system. Its fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to several meters, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cyathea rengifoi plays a role in tropical ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
