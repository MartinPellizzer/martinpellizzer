Cineraria doria

Cineraria doria, commonly known as the Doria's cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically has hairy, gray-green leaves and produces bright yellow or orange flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cineraria doria is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
