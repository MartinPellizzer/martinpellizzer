Conophytum lavisianum

Conophytum lavisianum, commonly known as the Lavisian's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth form. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months.
