Cotyledon salzmannii f. purpureoapiculata

Cotyledon salzmannii f. purpureoapiculata, commonly known as the purple-tipped cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with purple or reddish markings along the edges. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
