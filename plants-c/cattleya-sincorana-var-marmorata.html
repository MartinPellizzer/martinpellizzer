Cattleya sincorana var. marmorata

Cattleya sincorana var. marmorata, commonly known as the marbled cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
