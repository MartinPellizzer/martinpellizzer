Centrogenium kuhlmannianum

Centrogenium kuhlmannianum, commonly known as the Kuhlmann's centrose, is a rare and elusive plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring a short, stout rhizome and a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom in late spring. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
