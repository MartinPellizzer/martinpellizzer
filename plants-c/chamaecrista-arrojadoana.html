Chamaecrista arrojadoana

Chamaecrista arrojadoana, commonly known as the Brazilian burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant features compound leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its seeds are encased in hairy, pod-like fruits that can persist on the plant for extended periods.

Chamaecrista arrojadoana is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
