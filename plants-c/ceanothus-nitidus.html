Ceanothus nitidus

Ceanothus nitidus, commonly known as the glossy ceanothus, is a shrub native to California and southern Oregon.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to violet, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in chaparral and woodland habitats.

Ceanothus nitidus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to support local pollinators.
