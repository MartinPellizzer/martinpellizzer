Clusia araracuarae

Clusia araracuarae, commonly known as the Araracuara Clusia, is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in humid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its bark contains compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal properties.

Clusia araracuarae is often found growing in low-light conditions under the dense canopy of the rainforest.
