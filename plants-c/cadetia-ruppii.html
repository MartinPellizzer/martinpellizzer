Cadetia ruppii

Cadetia ruppii, commonly known as the rock gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention.

Cadetia ruppii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in alpine meadows and woodland edges.
