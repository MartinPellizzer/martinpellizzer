Cryptocoryne crispatula var. kubotae

Cryptocoryne crispatula var. kubotae, commonly known as the Kubotae cryptocoryne, is a compact aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin that gives it a unique appearance. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is well-suited for planted aquariums. It is known for its hardiness and ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters.

Its slow growth rate makes it an excellent choice for background planting in freshwater setups.
