Casearia laurifolia

Casearia laurifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf casearia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
