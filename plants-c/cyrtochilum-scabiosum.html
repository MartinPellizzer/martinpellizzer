Cyrtochilum scabiosum

Cyrtochilum scabiosum, commonly known as the scabious orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually linear and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Cyrtochilum scabiosum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
