Carum tenue

Carum tenue, commonly known as lesser cumin, is a small annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in umbels, which are characteristic of the Apiaceae family. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that are aromatic and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Carum tenue is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
