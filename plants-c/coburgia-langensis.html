Coburgia langensis

Coburgia langensis, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Coburgia langensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
