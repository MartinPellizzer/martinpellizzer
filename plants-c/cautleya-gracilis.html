Cautleya gracilis

Cautleya gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Himalayas and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often spotted or striped.

Cautleya gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
