Cyathea speciosa

Cyathea speciosa, commonly known as the elegant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive central vein.

Cyathea speciosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to shaded, humid environments.
