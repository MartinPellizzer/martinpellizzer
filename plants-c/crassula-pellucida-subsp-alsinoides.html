Crassula pellucida subsp. alsinoides

Crassula pellucida subsp.

alsinoides, commonly known as the silver leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, covered in a fine layer of white wax.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
