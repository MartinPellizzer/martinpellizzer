Chlorophytum pergracile

Chlorophytum pergracile, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spider plant, is a low-maintenance, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features long, grass-like leaves that are narrow and slightly curled at the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its ability to propagate easily through offsets makes it a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
