Cerastium willdenowii var. latifolium

Cerastium willdenowii var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. Its leaves are rounded and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
