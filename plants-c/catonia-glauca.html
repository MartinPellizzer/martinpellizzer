Catonia glauca

Catonia glauca, commonly known as the blue catonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, compact foliage. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, providing a textured appearance.

Catonia glauca is well adapted to dry conditions, making it a hardy plant suitable for xeriscaping.
