Coreopsis celendinensis

Coreopsis celendinensis, commonly known as the Celendine Coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Coreopsis celendinensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
