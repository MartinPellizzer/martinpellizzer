Clintonia podanisia var. glabrata

Clintonia podanisia var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-flowered Clintonia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, thriving in rich, well-drained soils. The species is notable for its ornamental value and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
