Crepidium leucodon

Crepidium leucodon, commonly known as the white orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a slender stem with a single, showy white or pale yellow flower. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette at the base.

The orchid is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
