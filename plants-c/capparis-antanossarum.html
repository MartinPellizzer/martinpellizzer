Capparis antanossarum

Capparis antanossarum, commonly known as the Antanossarum Caper, is a flowering plant species native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family, which includes various shrubs and herbs characterized by their distinctive floral structures. The plant typically features thorny stems and produces small, yellowish flowers that are often followed by fleshy, red berries. Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing resilience to dry conditions.
