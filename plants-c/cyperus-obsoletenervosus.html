Cyperus obsoletenervosus

Cyperus obsoletenervosus, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats. The plant has slender, grass-like stems and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a distinct vein pattern, which contributes to its scientific name.

Cyperus obsoletenervosus is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in wetlands and disturbed soils.
