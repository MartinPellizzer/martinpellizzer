Corymbia papuana

Corymbia papuana, commonly known as the papuan bloodwood, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to northern Australia and parts of New Guinea, where it grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also an important component of its native ecosystems.
