Crabbea longipes

Crabbea longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed crabbea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Crabbea longipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
