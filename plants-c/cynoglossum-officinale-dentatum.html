Cynoglossum officinale dentatum

Cynoglossum officinale dentatum, commonly known as the toothed hound's tongue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a distinctive, broad, tongue-shaped leaf with serrated edges, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum officinale dentatum is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along roadsides.
