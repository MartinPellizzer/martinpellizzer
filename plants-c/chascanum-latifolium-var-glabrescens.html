Chascanum latifolium var. glabrescens

Chascanum latifolium var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf chascanum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces showy yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant flowers and hardy nature.
