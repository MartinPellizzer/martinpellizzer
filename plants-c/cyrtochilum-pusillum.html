Cyrtochilum pusillum

Cyrtochilum pusillum, commonly known as the small-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces delicate, pale green to yellowish flowers with narrow, lance-shaped petals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Cyrtochilum pusillum is appreciated for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
