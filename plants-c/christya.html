Christya

Christya, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Nymphaeaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, buoyant stalks. Christya is also valued for its ability to purify water and provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.
