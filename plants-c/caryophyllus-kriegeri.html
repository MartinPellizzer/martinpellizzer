Caryophyllus kriegeri

Caryophyllus kriegeri, commonly known as the Krieger's carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has slender, branching stems. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, and it prefers well-drained soils in full sun.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
