Chimonobambusa utilis

Chimonobambusa utilis, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or golden bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and long, narrow leaves that are often a vibrant green. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its use in landscaping.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to form dense clumps, it can also be used in erosion control.
