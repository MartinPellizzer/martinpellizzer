Capsicum eshbaughii

Capsicum eshbaughii, commonly known as the Eshbaugh chili or the Mexican yellow chili, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its yellow or orange fruits, which are typically small and oblong. The plant produces white or pale green flowers and has hairy stems and leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as for its mild to medium heat level in the chili.

Capsicum eshbaughii thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
