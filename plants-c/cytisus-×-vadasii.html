Cytisus × vadasii

Cytisus × vadasii, commonly known as the hybrid broom, is a deciduous shrub native to the British Isles.

It is a natural hybrid between Cytisus scoparius (common broom) and Cytisus coccineus (red broom). The plant features bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of small, trifoliate leaves, and it has a sprawling growth habit.

Cytisus × vadasii is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
