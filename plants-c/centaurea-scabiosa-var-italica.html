Centaurea scabiosa var. italica

Centaurea scabiosa var. italica, commonly known as Italian bloodroot, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces striking red-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and has a taproot system. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and historical use in herbal medicine.
