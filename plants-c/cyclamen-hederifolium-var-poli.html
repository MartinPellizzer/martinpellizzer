Cyclamen hederifolium var. poli

Cyclamen hederifolium var. poli, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a deep green hue and intricate veining. The plant produces clusters of delicate, nodding flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, moist conditions and prefers well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for indoor or shaded garden settings.
