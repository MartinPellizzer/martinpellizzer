Calea leptophylla

Calea leptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf calea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Calea leptophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
