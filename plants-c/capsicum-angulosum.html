Capsicum angulosum

Capsicum angulosum, commonly known as the angular pepper or horn pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature. These fruits are often used in local cuisines for their mild to medium heat level and unique flavor.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities due to its attractive foliage and vibrant fruit.
