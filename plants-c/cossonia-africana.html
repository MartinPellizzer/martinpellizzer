Cossonia africana

Cossonia africana, commonly known as the African elephant tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and large, palm-like leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Cossonia africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
