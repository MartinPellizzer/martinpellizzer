Cestrum rojasianum

Cestrum rojasianum, commonly known as the red-flowered cestrum, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.
