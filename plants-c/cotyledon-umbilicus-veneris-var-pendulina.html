Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. pendulina

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. pendulina, commonly known as the hanging cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is a trailing or hanging plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for hanging baskets and rock gardens.
