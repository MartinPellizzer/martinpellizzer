Cicerbita Spicata Var. Integrifolia

Spiny caper, scientifically known as Cicerbita spicata var. integrifolia, is a unique and hardy plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient choice for xerophytic gardens. Its edible young shoots and leaves have been used for centuries in traditional cuisine and herbal remedies. Despite its tough exterior, the spiny caper offers both ornamental value and practical uses in sustainable landscaping.
