Caladium dussii

Caladium dussii, commonly known as the "Crimson Queen," is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium dussii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, especially in shaded or partially sunny areas.
