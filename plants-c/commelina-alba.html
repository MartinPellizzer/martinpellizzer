Commelina alba

Commelina alba, commonly known as the white commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features striking white flowers with three petals and three sepals, often accompanied by a blue or purple bract. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
