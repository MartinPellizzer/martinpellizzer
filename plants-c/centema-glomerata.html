Centema glomerata

Centema glomerata, commonly known as clustered centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.

Centema glomerata is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
