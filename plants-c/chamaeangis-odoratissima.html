Chamaeangis odoratissima

Chamaeangis odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant rock orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring, often in shades of white and pale pink. Its leaves are linear and densely packed, forming a rosette structure that helps it conserve moisture.
