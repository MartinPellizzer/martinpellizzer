Cirsium × subalpinum

Cirsium × subalpinum, commonly known as the alpine thistle, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flowers, which bloom in late summer. This plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its robust root system allows it to survive in harsh, cold climates.

Cirsium × subalpinum plays an important ecological role by providing food and habitat for various insect and bird species.
