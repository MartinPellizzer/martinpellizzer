Cassia fraxinifolia

Cassia fraxinifolia, commonly known as golden shower tree or *Cassia* species, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and long, drooping racemes of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces oblong, woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia fraxinifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
