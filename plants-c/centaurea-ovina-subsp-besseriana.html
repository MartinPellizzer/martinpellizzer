Centaurea ovina subsp. besseriana

Centaurea ovina subsp.

besseriana, commonly known as the lesser centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, bright blue or purple flowers.

The plant produces bracteate involucres with pale yellow to reddish flowers, which are typically arranged in dense clusters. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in rocky and dry habitats.

Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
