Carex nitens

Carex nitens, commonly known as the shining sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, rushes, and grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its upright, narrow leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are clustered in a cylindrical inflorescence.

Carex nitens thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and wooded areas.
