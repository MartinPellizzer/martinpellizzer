Crassula swellingrebliana

Crassula swellingrebliana, commonly known as the Swelling Rebliana.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the summer months. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Crassula swellingrebliana prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.
