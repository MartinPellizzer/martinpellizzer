Crepidium tubulosum

Crepidium tubulosum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tubular, pale green or yellowish stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, contributing to its understory habitat preference.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
