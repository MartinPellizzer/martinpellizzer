Croton calderi

Croton calderi, commonly known as the Caldeira croton, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that range from deep red to bright yellow and orange. The leaves are typically large, thick, and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Croton calderi is often cultivated as a houseplant or in outdoor gardens due to its unique foliage and ability to add visual interest to any space.
