Cerinthe major var. elegans

Cerinthe major var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant toadflax, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
