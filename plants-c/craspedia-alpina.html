Craspedia alpina

Craspedia alpina, commonly known as the alpine everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is known for its long-lasting blooms, which remain intact even when dried, making it popular in floral arrangements.
