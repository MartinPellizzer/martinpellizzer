Crotalaria dewildemaniana

Crotalaria dewildemaniana, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit includes both annual and perennial forms, depending on the region, and it thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.
