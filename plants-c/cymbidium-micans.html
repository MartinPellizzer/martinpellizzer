Cymbidium micans

Cymbidium micans, commonly known as the night-blooming orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that open at night. The plant produces fragrant, waxy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which often emit a sweet scent. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Cymbidium micans is a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and indoor cultivation due to its beauty and unique flowering pattern.
