Conyza asteroides

Conyza asteroides, commonly known as common ironweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a composite head. The plant is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils. It is often found in grasslands, roadsides, and agricultural fields.

Conyza asteroides is noted for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain regions.
