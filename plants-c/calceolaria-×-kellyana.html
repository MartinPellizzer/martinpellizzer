Calceolaria × kellyana

Calceolaria × kellyana, commonly known as the Kelly's slipper orchid, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its foliage consists of oval, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Native to South America, it is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
