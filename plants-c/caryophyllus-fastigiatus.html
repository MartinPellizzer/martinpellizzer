Caryophyllus fastigiatus

Caryophyllus fastigiatus, commonly known as the upright carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
