Cardamine alberti var. minor

Cardamine alberti var. minor, commonly known as the lesser watercress, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like buttercups and lilies.
