Croton sacaquinha

Croton sacaquinha, commonly known as the Brazilian croton, is a shrub native to the Brazilian Amazon.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and striking, colorful, often variegated foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by capsule-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
