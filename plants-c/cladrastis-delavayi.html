Cladrastis delavayi

Cladrastis delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's trumpet tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pendulous, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and unique branching structure.
