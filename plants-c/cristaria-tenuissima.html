Cristaria tenuissima

Cristaria tenuissima, commonly known as the slender glasswort, is a halophytic plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It thrives in saline environments such as salt marshes and tidal flats, where it plays a crucial role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion. The plant has a succulent, cylindrical stem with small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss in its arid habitat. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color.

Its ability to tolerate high salt concentrations makes it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
