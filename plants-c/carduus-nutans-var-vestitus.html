Carduus nutans var. vestitus

Carduus nutans var. vestitus, commonly known as the hairy sowthistle, is a variant of the common sowthistle.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy, often grayish-green leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to a variety of soil types and environments.
