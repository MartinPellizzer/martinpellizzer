Cymbidium chloranthum

Cymbidium chloranthum, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching inflorescence that bears multiple fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Cymbidium chloranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique coloration and fragrance.
