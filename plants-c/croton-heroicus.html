Croton heroicus

Croton heroicus, commonly known as the "hero's croton," is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from red to orange and yellow, often displaying striking patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often toxic species. Croton heroicus is typically found in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
