Cymbidium dayanum var. angustifolium

Cymbidium dayanum var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, slender stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
