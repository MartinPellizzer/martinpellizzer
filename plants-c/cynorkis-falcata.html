Cynorkis falcata

Cynorkis falcata, commonly known as the sickle leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, sickle-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
