Coelogyne crassiloba

Coelogyne crassiloba, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. Its flowering period usually occurs in the spring or early summer.

Coelogyne crassiloba is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
