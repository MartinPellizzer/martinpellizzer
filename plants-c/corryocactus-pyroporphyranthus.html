Corryocactus pyroporphyranthus

Corryocactus pyroporphyranthus, commonly known as the fiery red cactus, is a unique species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange spines, which give it its striking appearance. The plant has a cylindrical stem that stores water, enabling it to survive in dry conditions. Its flowers, which appear in spring, are typically yellow or orange and are clustered at the top of the plant.
