Cnicus darwasicus

Cnicus darwasicus, commonly known as the Darwazi sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinct, often bitter taste.
