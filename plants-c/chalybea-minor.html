Chalybea minor

Chalybea minor, commonly known as the small ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Chalybea minor is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and filter water.
