Cynanchum likiangense

Cynanchum likiangense, commonly known as the Yunnan milkvetch, is a perennial climbing plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruits are follicles that contain toxic latex. It is often found in rocky or forested areas and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

However, due to its toxic compounds, it must be handled with care.
