Cyclamen persicum var. purpureum

Cyclamen persicum var. purpureum, commonly known as the purple cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a deep green color and striking, showy flowers in shades of purple and pink. The flowers typically bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens during the colder months. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.

It prefers cool, moist conditions and well-draining soil to thrive.
