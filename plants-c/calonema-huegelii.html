Calonema huegelii

Calonema huegelii, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, coastal areas.
