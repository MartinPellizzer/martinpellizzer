Crossandra zuluensis

Crossandra zuluensis, commonly known as the Zulu flame or Zulu crossandra, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Crossandra zuluensis thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
