Cheirostylis gunnarii

Cheirostylis gunnarii, commonly known as the Gunns' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in rainforest or heathland areas. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent white lip, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Cheirostylis gunnarii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
