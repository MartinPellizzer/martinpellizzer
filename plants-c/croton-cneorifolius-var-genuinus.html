Croton cneorifolius var. genuinus

Croton cneorifolius var. genuinus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
