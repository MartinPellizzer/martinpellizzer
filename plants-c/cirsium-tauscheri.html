Cirsium tauscheri

Cirsium tauscheri, commonly known as the Tauscher's thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, woolly leaves. The plant produces purple to pinkish-blue flower heads that are composed of numerous small florets. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in scrublands and open woodlands.

Cirsium tauscheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
