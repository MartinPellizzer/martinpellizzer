Conyza sericea

Conyza sericea, commonly known as silverleaf ironweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to the presence of fine, silky hairs on its leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Conyza sericea is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds dispersed by wind.
