Chirita liujiangensis

Chirita liujiangensis, commonly known as Liujiang chireta, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical significance lies in its role in the study of plant diversity within the Gesneriaceae family.
