Coelogyne alpina

Coelogyne alpina, commonly known as the Alpine Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high altitudes of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, strap-like leaves and fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in cool, humid environments, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with red or purple markings, adding to its ornamental value.

Coelogyne alpina is valued in horticulture for its beauty and ability to thrive in controlled indoor conditions.
