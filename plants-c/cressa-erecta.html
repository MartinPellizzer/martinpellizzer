Cressa erecta

Cressa erecta, commonly known as the slender cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard family plants. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with slender, upright stems and small, bright green leaves. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in racemes, and it blooms from spring to early summer.

Cressa erecta is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
