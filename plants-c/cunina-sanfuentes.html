Cunina sanfuentes

Cunina sanfuentes, commonly known as the Sanfuentes' cunina, is a lesser-known plant species belonging to the family Cunineae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of tiny, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its botanical features include a woody stem and a unique leaf structure that aids in water retention.
