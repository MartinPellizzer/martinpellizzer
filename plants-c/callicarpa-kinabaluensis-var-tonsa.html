Callicarpa kinabaluensis var. tonsa

Callicarpa kinabaluensis var. tonsa, commonly known as the Borneo beautyberry, is a shrub native to the highlands of Borneo.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple berries that appear in clusters, which are a key feature of the species. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native region.

The berries are not only ornamental but also serve as a food source for local wildlife.
