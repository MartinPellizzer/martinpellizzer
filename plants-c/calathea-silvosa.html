Calathea silvosa

Calathea silvosa, commonly known as the silver stripe calathea, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green stripes that shimmer in the light. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The plant's unique leaf patterns and ability to thrive in low-light conditions contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
