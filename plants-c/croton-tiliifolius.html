Croton tiliifolius

Croton tiliifolius, commonly known as the Indian croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins and produces clusters of small, yellow-green flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
