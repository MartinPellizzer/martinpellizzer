Cryptostylis alismifolia

Cryptostylis alismifolia, commonly known as the water lily orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves that resemble those of the water lily, hence its common name. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive, recurved lip that is often brightly colored. It thrives in moist, shady environments, typically near streams or in wetlands.

Cryptostylis alismifolia is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
