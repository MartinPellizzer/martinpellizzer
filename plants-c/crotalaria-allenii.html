Crotalaria allenii

Crotalaria allenii, commonly known as the Allen's crown flower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with a dark purple or black center. The plant has palmate leaves and produces pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and potentially invasive in certain environments.
