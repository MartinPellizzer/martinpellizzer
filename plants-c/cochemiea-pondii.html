Cochemiea pondii

Cochemiea pondii, commonly known as the Desert Beauty, is a species of cactus native to the Sonoran Desert in North America.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. Its leaves are modified into spines, which help reduce water loss and deter herbivores.

Cochemiea pondii is well-suited for xeriscaping and requires minimal water once established.
