Chlorocyperus balansae

Chlorocyperus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae flat sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and swampy areas. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a distinct, fibrous texture.

The inflorescence consists of a single, spike-like structure that bears small, clustered flowers.
