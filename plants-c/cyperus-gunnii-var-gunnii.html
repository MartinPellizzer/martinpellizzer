Cyperus gunnii var. gunnii

Cyperus gunnii var. gunnii, commonly known as the Gunns flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale green or brown flowers. It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.

Cyperus gunnii var. gunnii is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage and adaptability to various aquatic conditions.
