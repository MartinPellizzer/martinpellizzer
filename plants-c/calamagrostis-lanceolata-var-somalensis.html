Calamagrostis lanceolata var. somalensis

Calamagrostis lanceolata var. somalensis, commonly known as the Somali silver grass, is a perennial grass native to the Horn of Africa.

It features narrow, silver-gray blades that give it a distinctive appearance, and it typically grows in open, sunny habitats. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms and inflorescences that are often panicle-like in structure. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in regions with suitable climates.
