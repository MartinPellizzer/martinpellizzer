Cyperus polystachyos var. glaucescens

Cyperus polystachyos var. glaucescens, commonly known as the blue flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically blue or purplish in color. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic conditions.

The blue flatsedge is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
