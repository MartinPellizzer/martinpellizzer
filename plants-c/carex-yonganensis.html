Carex yonganensis

Carex yonganensis, commonly known as the Yongan sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features triangular, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.

Carex yonganensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
