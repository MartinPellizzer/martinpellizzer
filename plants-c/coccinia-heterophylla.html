Coccinia heterophylla

Coccinia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf cucumber.

It is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves that vary in size and shape depending on environmental conditions. Its small, yellow-orange flowers are followed by edible, round fruits that resemble miniature cucumbers.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
