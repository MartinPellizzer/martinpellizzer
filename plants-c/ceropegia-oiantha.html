Ceropegia oiantha

Ceropegia oiantha, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that features fleshy, branching stems and small, waxy leaves. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and can thrive in full sun.

Its unique appearance and ability to store water make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
