Cypripedium × aeson

Cypripedium × aeson, commonly known as the yellow or white lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Cypripedium genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers with a pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single, large flower per stem.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and protected areas.
