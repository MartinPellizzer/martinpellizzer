Columnea microsepala

Columnea microsepala, commonly known as the small-sepaled flame flower, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub features showy, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom during the summer months. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value.
