Cyrtochilum panduratum

Cyrtochilum panduratum, commonly known as the panduratum orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant features a pseudobulb and linear leaves, typical of many orchid genera. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cyrtochilum panduratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
