Combesia muscosa var. acuminata

Combesia muscosa var. acuminata, commonly known as the slender combesia, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the family Araceae, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. This variant is distinguished by its acuminate (sharply pointed) leaf tips, contributing to its unique botanical appearance.
