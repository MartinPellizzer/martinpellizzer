Cinnamomum chingchuanium

Cinnamomum chingchuanium, commonly known as the Yunnan cinnamon, is a species of tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant typically grows in subtropical regions with high humidity and mild temperatures. Its bark is used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to cinnamon.

The leaves are also utilized in local culinary practices and herbal remedies.
