Combretum infundibuliforme

Combretum infundibuliforme, commonly known as the flame tree or fire tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
