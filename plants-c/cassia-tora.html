Cassia tora

Cassia tora, commonly known as flame tree or sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Cassia tora is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
