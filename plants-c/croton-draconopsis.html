Croton draconopsis

Croton draconopsis, commonly known as the dragon's breath croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from red to orange and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
