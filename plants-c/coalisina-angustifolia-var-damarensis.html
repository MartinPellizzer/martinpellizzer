Coalisina angustifolia var. damarensis

Coalisina angustifolia var. damarensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved coalisina, is a perennial herb native to specific regions in Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in greenish-white or pale yellow hues.

This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
