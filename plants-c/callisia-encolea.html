Callisia encolea

Callisia encolea, commonly known as the silver sword, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming structure and silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, Callisia encolea is often cultivated in both garden and container settings.
