Chimonanthus praecox f. versicolor

Chimonanthus praecox f. versicolor, commonly known as the Variable Witch Hazel, is a unique variation of the Witch Hazel plant.

It is characterized by its strikingly colorful flowers that range from yellow to red, orange, and even purple, depending on the stage of bloom. This plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. The foliage is typically dark green with a glossy appearance, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden settings.
