Cnicus argentatus

Cnicus argentatus, commonly known as the silver cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that are covered in a powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and rocky areas.

Cnicus argentatus is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
