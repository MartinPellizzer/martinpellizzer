Crassula tomentosa var. glabrifolia

Crassula tomentosa var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the Woolly Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor and outdoor conditions.
