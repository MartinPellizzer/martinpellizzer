Colchicum tenorei

Colchicum tenorei, commonly known as the Sicilian colchic, is a flowering plant native to the island of Sicily.

It belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant features a short, stout rhizome and produces large, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white down. Its flowers are usually purple or reddish in color, with prominent, spurred petals.

Colchicum tenorei is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.
