Centaurea setosa

Centaurea setosa, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with narrow, linear leaves.

Centaurea setosa is often found in disturbed areas and can be an indicator of poor soil conditions.
