Cnicus incomptus

Cnicus incomptus, commonly known as the sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial plant that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lobed and often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, round fruits that contain seeds.

Cnicus incomptus thrives in sandy or disturbed soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry grasslands.
