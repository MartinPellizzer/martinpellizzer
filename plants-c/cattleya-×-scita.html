Cattleya × scita

Cattleya × scita, commonly known as the scented cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from various Cattleya species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and often feature a distinctive fragrance. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, providing a lush, green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
