Cattleya × gerhard-santosii

Cattleya × gerhard-santosii, commonly known as the Gerhard Santos Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with red or purple markings. The plant features upright, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This orchid is prized for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
