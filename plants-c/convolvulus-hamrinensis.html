Convolvulus hamrinensis

Convolvulus hamrinensis, commonly known as the Hamrin bindweed, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their climbing or trailing growth habits.
