Convolvulus pinto

Convolvulus pinto, commonly known as the painted convolvulus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
