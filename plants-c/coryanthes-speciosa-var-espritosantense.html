Coryanthes speciosa var. espritosantense

Coryanthes speciosa var. espritosantense, commonly known as the Esprito Santo orchid, is a rare and striking tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter, with vibrant red and yellow hues. The plant features a robust rhizome and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation.

Its unique morphology and colorful blooms make it a prized specimen in botanical gardens and among orchid enthusiasts.
