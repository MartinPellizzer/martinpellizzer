Cordia multicapitata

Cordia multicapitata, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white, spherical flower clusters that appear at the ends of its branches, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Cordia multicapitata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
