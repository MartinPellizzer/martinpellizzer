Calochilus montanus

Calochilus montanus, commonly known as the mountain spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals and a distinctive, narrow spur. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in alpine or subalpine regions. Its flowers are usually a mix of white, green, and purple, and it blooms during the cooler months.

Calochilus montanus is a perennial herb that is often found in open habitats with good drainage.
