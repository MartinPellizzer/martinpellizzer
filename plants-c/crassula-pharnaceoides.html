Crassula pharnaceoides

Crassula pharnaceoides, commonly known as the silver crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula pharnaceoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
