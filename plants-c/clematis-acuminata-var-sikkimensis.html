Clematis acuminata var. sikkimensis

Clematis acuminata var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim Clematis, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, usually blue or violet flowers. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, green fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
