Chrozophora brocchiana

Chrozophora brocchiana, commonly known as the brocchi's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features slender, branching stems covered with fine hairs and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a slender, spiral-shaped capsule that contains several seeds.
