Clusia niambiensis

Clusia niambiensis, commonly known as the Niambien Clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often dark green and glossy, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Clusia niambiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its resilience and aesthetic qualities.
