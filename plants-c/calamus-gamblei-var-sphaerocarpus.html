Calamus gamblei var. sphaerocarpus

Calamus gamblei var. sphaerocarpus, commonly known as the globular calamus, is a variant of the genus Calamus, which belongs to the family Arales.

It is characterized by its unique, spherical-shaped spathes that distinguish it from other calamus species. This plant is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. It thrives in wet, tropical environments, often growing in swampy or marshy areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its distinctive foliage.
