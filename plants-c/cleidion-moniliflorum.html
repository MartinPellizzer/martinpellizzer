Cleidion moniliflorum

Cleidion moniliflorum, commonly known as the bead-leaved sterculia, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bead-like fruits that hang in clusters, giving it a unique appearance. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

This plant is often valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
