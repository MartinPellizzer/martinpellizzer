Croton decorsei

Croton decorsei, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that often feature shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its fast growth and adaptability to various light conditions. Its leaves are typically broad and lobed, with a waxy texture that enhances its ornamental appeal.

Croton decorsei is popular as a houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
