Cynoglossum tosaense

Cynoglossum tosaense, commonly known as Tosa bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine pubescence.
