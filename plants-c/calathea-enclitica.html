Calathea enclitica

Calathea enclitica, commonly known as the zigzag calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a unique zigzag pattern. The foliage often features shades of green, silver, and white, creating a visually appealing contrast. This plant thrives in humid environments with indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
