Cattleya tereticaulis

Cattleya tereticaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya tereticaulis is also valued for its relatively easy care requirements compared to other orchid species.
