Cattleya walkeriana var. bulbosa

Cattleya walkeriana var. bulbosa, commonly known as the Bulbosa Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a robust pseudobulb that supports its tall, arching stems and showy blooms. Its flowers are typically pink or white with a yellow lip, and they bloom during the summer months.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
