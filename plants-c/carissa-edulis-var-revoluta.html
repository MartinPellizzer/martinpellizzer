Carissa edulis var. revoluta

Carissa edulis var. revoluta, commonly known as the Natal plum or yellow carissa, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces small, yellow, fleshy fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit is rich in vitamin C and is valued for its sweet, tangy flavor.
