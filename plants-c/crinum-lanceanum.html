Crinum lanceanum

Crinum lanceanum, commonly known as the lanceleaf spider lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow or greenish throat. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Crinum lanceanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
