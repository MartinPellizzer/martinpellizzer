Croton albescens

Croton albescens, commonly known as the white croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Croton albescens is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
