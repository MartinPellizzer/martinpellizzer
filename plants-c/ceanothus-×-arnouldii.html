Ceanothus × arnouldii

Ceanothus × arnouldii, commonly known as the Santa Barbara ceanothus, is a hybrid shrub native to California.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of sky-blue to white flowers in late spring, which are highly attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is often cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
