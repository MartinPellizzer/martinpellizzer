Caapeba

Caapeba, commonly known as the Cape bushveld plant, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties. The plant features small, compound leaves and produces yellow or white flowers that attract pollinators. Caapeba is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought.

Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization in native plant landscaping.
