Cephalaria Bidens

Cephalaria bidens is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique plant structure. Native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. The species is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat various ailments. With its elegant form and ecological significance, Cephalaria bidens stands out as a remarkable plant worth exploring.
