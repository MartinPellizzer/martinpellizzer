Crassula manaia

Crassula manaia, commonly known as the Manaia Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often blue-green in color, with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Crassula manaia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and aesthetic appeal.
