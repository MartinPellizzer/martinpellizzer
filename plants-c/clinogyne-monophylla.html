Clinogyne monophylla

Clinogyne monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of south-eastern Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant is characterized by its single, broad, lance-shaped leaf and its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Clinogyne monophylla thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is adapted to the cooler, temperate climates of its native range.
