Ceratostylis malaccensis

Ceratostylis malaccensis, commonly known as the Malacca horned-stylis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, lanceolate leaf. Its flowers are pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive, elongated spur.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
