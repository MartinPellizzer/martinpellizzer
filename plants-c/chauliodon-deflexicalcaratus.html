Chauliodon deflexicalcaratus

Chauliodon deflexicalcaratus, commonly known as the Moonlit Fern, is a rare and delicate plant native to the misty highlands of the Andes.

It features slender, arching fronds that are covered in a silvery, waxy coating, which helps reflect sunlight and retain moisture. The plant's roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in cool, shaded environments with high humidity. It blooms with small, pale green flowers that emit a faint, sweet scent, attracting nocturnal pollinators.

Due to its endangered status, it is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists alike.
