Catasetum × faustoi

Catasetum × faustoi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant with unique botanical features.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical and subtropical regions. Its flowers are notable for their specialized structure, including a labellum that plays a role in pollination.

Catasetum × faustoi is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
