Ceanothus jepsonii var. jepsonii

Ceanothus jepsonii var. jepsonii, commonly known as the Jepson's ceanothus, is a shrub native to California.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of blue to violet flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat. It is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub ecosystems.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
