Cyrtochilum deltoideum

Cyrtochilum deltoideum, commonly known as the delta orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers that are often pale yellow or greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
