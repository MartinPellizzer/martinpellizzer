Carduus defloratus var. medius

Carduus defloratus var. medius, commonly known as the common thistle.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features spiny leaves and a flowering head that produces purple to pink flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological studies and traditional herbal medicine.
