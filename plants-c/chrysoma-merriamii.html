Chrysoma merriamii

Chrysoma merriamii, commonly known as the Merriam's chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
