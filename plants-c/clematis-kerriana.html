Clematis kerriana

Clematis kerriana, commonly known as the Kerriana Clematis, is a flowering vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, climbing stems that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and blue, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Clematis kerriana thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
