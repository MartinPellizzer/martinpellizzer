Calophyllum nodosum

Calophyllum nodosum, commonly known as the sea mahoe or tropical mahoe, is a flowering plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It is a member of the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces a heavy, aromatic resin that has been used traditionally for its medicinal and aromatic properties. Its hard, dense wood is highly valued for crafting and is often used in the production of fine furniture and musical instruments.

The plant thrives in coastal and tropical environments, often found in mangrove ecosystems.
