Cirsium kisoense

Cirsium kisoense, commonly known as the Kiso thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. It has a robust root system that helps it thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Cirsium kisoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
