Campanopsis oocarpa

Campanopsis oocarpa, commonly known as the tropical bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
