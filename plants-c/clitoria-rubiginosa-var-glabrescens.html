Clitoria rubiginosa var. glabrescens

Clitoria rubiginosa var. glabrescens, commonly known as the velvet bean or scarlet bower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red, violet, or purple pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is adaptable to various soil types.

The seeds of this plant are also used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein in some regions.
