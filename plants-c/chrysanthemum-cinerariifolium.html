Chrysanthemum cinerariifolium

Chrysanthemum cinerariifolium, commonly known as the cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy leaves and vibrant yellow or white flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky environments.

Its historical use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes adds to its botanical significance.
