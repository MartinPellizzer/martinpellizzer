Chloanthes parviflora

Chloanthes parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered chloanthes, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Chloanthes parviflora is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
