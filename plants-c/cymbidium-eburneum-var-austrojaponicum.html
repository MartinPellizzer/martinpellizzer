Cymbidium eburneum var. austrojaponicum

Cymbidium eburneum var. austrojaponicum, commonly known as the Japanese white cymbidium, is a rare and elegant orchid native to parts of East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of large, white to pale pink flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a prized addition to winter gardens. It prefers cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and delicate appearance.
