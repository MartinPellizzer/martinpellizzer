Corneria pierrei

Corneria pierrei, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine, while the flowers are prized for their ornamental value.

Corneria pierrei thrives in well-draining soil and requires consistent humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.
