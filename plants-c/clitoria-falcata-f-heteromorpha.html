Clitoria falcata f. heteromorpha

Clitoria falcata f. heteromorpha, commonly known as the variable sickle pea, is a variant of the sickle pea species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive sickle-shaped leaves and vibrant purple to pink flowers. The plant is a climbing legume with trifoliate leaves and pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
