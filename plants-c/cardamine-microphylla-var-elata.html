Cardamine microphylla var. elata

Cardamine microphylla var. elata, commonly known as the tall watercress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and tiny white flowers. This variant is distinguished by its taller growth habit compared to the typical form of Cardamine microphylla. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland areas.

The plant is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental and edible qualities.
