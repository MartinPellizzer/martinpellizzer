Ceropegia distinctantha

Ceropegia distinctantha, commonly known as the Cape nettle or spider plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Ceropegia distinctantha is adapted to arid environments, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
