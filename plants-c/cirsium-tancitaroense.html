Cirsium tancitaroense

Cirsium tancitaroense, commonly known as the Tancitaro thistle, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its morphology includes a tall, branched stem and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
