Crotalaria anthylloides

Crotalaria anthylloides, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry to moderately moist conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
