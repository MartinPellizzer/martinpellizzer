Crowea angustifolia

Crowea angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf crowea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a yellow center. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warmer climates.

Crowea angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
