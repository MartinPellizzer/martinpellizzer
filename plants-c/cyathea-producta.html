Cyathea producta

Cyathea producta, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Pacific Islands. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. Its fronds are large, feather-like, and can grow up to several meters in length.

Cyathea producta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
