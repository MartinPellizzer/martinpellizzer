Chaetaria pallens var. tenuifolia

Chaetaria pallens var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf chaetaria, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a composite head, and bloom during the warmer months.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky habitats.
