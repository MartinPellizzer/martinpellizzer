Columnea rubricalyx

Columnea rubricalyx, commonly known as the red-leafed flame bell, is a striking plant native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family, characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive red or orange underside, adding to its ornamental appeal. Columnea rubricalyx is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
