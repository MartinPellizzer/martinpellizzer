Chamaecytisus danubialis

Chamaecytisus danubialis, commonly known as the Danube broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
