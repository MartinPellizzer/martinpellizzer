Cattleya × itabapoanaensis

Cattleya × itabapoanaensis, commonly known as the Itabapoana orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features long, arching stems with broad, leathery leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
