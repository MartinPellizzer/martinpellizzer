Cleome grata

Cleome grata, commonly known as spider flower, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features erect, branching stems that are covered in fine, hairy texture. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with petals that range from white to pink, often with a yellow center. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Cleome grata thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower landscapes.
