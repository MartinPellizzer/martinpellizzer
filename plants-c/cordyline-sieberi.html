Cordyline sieberi

Cordyline sieberi, commonly known as the Sieber's cordyline, is a striking evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow streaks, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is well-suited for garden cultivation. Its foliage provides ornamental value, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

While it does not produce showy flowers, its foliage remains the primary ornamental feature throughout the year.
