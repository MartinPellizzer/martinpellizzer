Carex shanghaiensis

Carex shanghaiensis, commonly known as Shanghai sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green or brown in color. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Carex shanghaiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
