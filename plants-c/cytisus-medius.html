Cytisus medius

Cytisus medius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom, is a flowering shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, often spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow and leathery, with a distinctive dark green color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

The plant is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
