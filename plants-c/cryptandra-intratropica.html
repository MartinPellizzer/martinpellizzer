Cryptandra intratropica

Cryptandra intratropica, commonly known as the Northern River Red Gum, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the northern regions of Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, urn-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters.

It is adapted to seasonally dry environments and plays an important role in local ecosystems.
