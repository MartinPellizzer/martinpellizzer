Chamaephyton Supinum

Chamaephyton supinum is a low-growing plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. It thrives in harsh environments with minimal water and nutrient availability. Its prostrate growth habit allows it to spread across the ground, forming dense mats. The plant is well adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and frequent droughts. Despite its resilience, it remains a relatively obscure species in botanical studies.
