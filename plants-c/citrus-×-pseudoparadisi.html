Citrus × pseudoparadisi

Citrus × pseudoparadisi, commonly known as the Natal orange.

This hybrid citrus plant is native to South Africa and is characterized by its small, round fruits with a thick, yellow-orange rind. The leaves are broad and dark green, often with a slightly waxy texture. It produces a sweet, aromatic fruit that is typically used for juice and culinary purposes.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities as well as its fruit yield in suitable climates.
