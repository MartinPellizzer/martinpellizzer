Cliffortia reniformis

Cliffortia reniformis, commonly known as the kidneyleaf sandthorn, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, kidney-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, often blooming in spring. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in sandy soils.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
