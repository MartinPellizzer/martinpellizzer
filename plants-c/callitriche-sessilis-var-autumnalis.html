Callitriche sessilis var. autumnalis

Callitriche sessilis var. autumnalis, commonly known as autumn water starwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to temperate regions.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and wet meadows. The plant features tiny, star-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often occur in clusters near the water's surface.

This variety is notable for its seasonal occurrence, often blooming in late summer to early autumn.
