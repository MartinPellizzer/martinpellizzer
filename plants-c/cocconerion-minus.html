Cocconerion minus

Cocconerion minus, commonly known as the dwarf arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces clusters of small, white, tubular flowers. The plant's leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

The plant is valued for its edible tubers, which are similar in texture to yams.
