Cryptandra pomaderroides

Cryptandra pomaderroides, commonly known as the smooth cryptandra, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with smooth, glabrous bark and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
