Cynoglossum australe var. australe

Cynoglossum australe var. australe, commonly known as Australian cowpea or blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Cynoglossum australe var. australe is a hardy perennial that is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
