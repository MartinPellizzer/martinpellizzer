Cynanchica suffruticosa

Cynanchica suffruticosa, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed or Chinese dogbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features woody stems that become more pronounced with age, giving it a shrub-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Cynanchica suffruticosa is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
