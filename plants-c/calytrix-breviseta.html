Calytrix breviseta

Calytrix breviseta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved calytrix, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are usually pink or red, arranged in clusters, and attract various pollinators.

Calytrix breviseta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
