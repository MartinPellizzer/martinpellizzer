Chamaecyparis acuta

Chamaecyparis acuta, commonly known as the Japanese cypress or kurume cypress, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its narrow, conical shape and scale-like leaves that are dark green on top and whitish underneath. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its slow growth rate and elegant appearance make it a valued addition to both landscapes and collections.
