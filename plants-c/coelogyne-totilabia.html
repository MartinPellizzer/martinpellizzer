Coelogyne totilabia

Coelogyne totilabia, commonly known as the Toto lily, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect inflorescence bearing clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features a pseudobulb system with fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are typically borne on a scape that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height.

Coelogyne totilabia is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
