Cyathea bartlettii

Cyathea bartlettii, commonly known as the Bartlett's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous, spongy material. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Cyathea bartlettii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of tropical ecosystems.
