Cypripedium × ventricosum var. virescens

Cypripedium × ventricosum var. virescens, commonly known as the green-tongued lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that is typically greenish in color, giving it its common name. The plant features a single, upright stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to green, with a prominent, pouch-like lip that is often tinged with red or brown.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
