Chenopodium neumanii

Chenopodium neumanii, commonly known as desert goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent leaves and small, greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often on rocky soils or in desert scrub habitats. Chenopodium neumanii is often used in traditional medicine by indigenous peoples for its purported health benefits.
