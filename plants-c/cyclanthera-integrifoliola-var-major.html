Cyclanthera integrifoliola var. major

Cyclanthera integrifoliola var. major, commonly known as the giant leaf cucumber, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a long, slender, spiny gourd that can grow up to 1 meter in length.

This variant is notable for its vigorous growth and is often cultivated for its ornamental and edible qualities.
