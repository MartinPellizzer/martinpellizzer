Carex schweinitzii

Carex schweinitzii, commonly known as Schweinitz's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, grasses, and rushes. This plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with triangular stems that often form dense clumps.

Schweinitz's sedge produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets, which are typically wind-pollinated.
