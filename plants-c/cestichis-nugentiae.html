Cestichis nugentiae

Cestichis nugentiae, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a complex root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cestichis nugentiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
