Calanthe reflexa

Calanthe reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clustered, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces fragrant, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive reflexed lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
