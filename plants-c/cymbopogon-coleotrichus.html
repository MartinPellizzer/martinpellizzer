Cymbopogon coleotrichus

Cymbopogon coleotrichus, commonly known as the silver grass or silver reed, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and tall, slender inflorescences. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Cymbopogon coleotrichus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
