Corneria elata

Corneria elata, commonly known as the tall corner plant, is a flowering species native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or purple. Corneria elata is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
