Codonopsis tubulosa subsp. tubulosa

Codonopsis tubulosa subsp.

tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China. It is characterized by its erect stem and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple.

The plant produces small, oval fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
