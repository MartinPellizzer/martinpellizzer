Cattleya holfordii

Cattleya holfordii, commonly known as the Holford's cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting lip. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya holfordii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
