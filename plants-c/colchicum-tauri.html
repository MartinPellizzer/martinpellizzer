Colchicum tauri

Colchicum tauri, commonly known as the Taurian colchic, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Caucasus region.

It produces striking violet to purple flowers during late summer to early autumn. The plant has a short, stout stem that supports its showy blooms. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and often withering before flowering.

Colchicum tauri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
