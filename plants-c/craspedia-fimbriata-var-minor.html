Craspedia fimbriata var. minor

Craspedia fimbriata var. minor, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starburry, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and often have a serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
