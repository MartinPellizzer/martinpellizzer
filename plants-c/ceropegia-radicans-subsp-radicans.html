Ceropegia radicans subsp. radicans

Ceropegia radicans subsp.

radicans, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are typically pale green to reddish in color.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, often appearing in clusters. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
