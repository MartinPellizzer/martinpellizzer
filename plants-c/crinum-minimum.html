Crinum minimum

Crinum minimum, commonly known as the dwarf rain lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its compact size, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Crinum minimum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
