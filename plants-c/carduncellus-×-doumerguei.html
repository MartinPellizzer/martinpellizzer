Carduncellus × doumerguei

Carduncellus × doumerguei, commonly known as the hybrid carduncellus, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems and large, fern-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance and hardy nature.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
