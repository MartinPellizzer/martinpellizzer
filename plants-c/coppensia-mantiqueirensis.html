Coppensia mantiqueirensis

Coppensia mantiqueirensis, commonly known as the Mantiqueira cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and reduced leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to withstand drought. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, adding a striking visual element to its habitat.

Coppensia mantiqueirensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native region.
