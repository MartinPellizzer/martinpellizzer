Clematis urophylla

Clematis urophylla, commonly known as the silky clematis, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a deciduous vine that produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and has a climbing habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Clematis urophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
