Crocosmia aurea

Crocosmia aurea, commonly known as the orange flame lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features sword-shaped, dark green leaves that are lanceolate and often have a reddish sheen. The plant produces tall, erect stems that bear clusters of bright orange to red tubular flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Crocosmia aurea is valued for its vibrant blooms and is often used in gardens for its ornamental appeal.
