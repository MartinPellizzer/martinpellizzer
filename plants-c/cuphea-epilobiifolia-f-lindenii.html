Cuphea epilobiifolia f. lindenii

Cuphea epilobiifolia f. lindenii, commonly known as the Lindeni's cuphea, is a compact, herbaceous shrub native to Mexico.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are typically upright and branching. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
