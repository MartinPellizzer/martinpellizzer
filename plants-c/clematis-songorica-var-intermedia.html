Clematis songorica var. intermedia

Clematis songorica var. intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, typically arranged in a palmate pattern. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
