Caladium bicolor splendens

Caladium bicolor splendens, commonly known as the flameleaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is prized for its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, orange, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining, fertile soil. It is often grown as an ornamental plant in gardens and containers due to its dramatic foliage.

Caladium bicolor splendens is typically propagated through tuberous roots and prefers partial to full shade.
