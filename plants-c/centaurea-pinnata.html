Centaurea pinnata

Centaurea pinnata, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with reddish-brown centers.

Centaurea pinnata is often used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
