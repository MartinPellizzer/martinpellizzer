Corydalis Ramosa Var. Nana

Corydalis ramosa var. nana is a compact, flowering plant known for its delicate beauty and unique growth habits. Native to regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland settings. This variety is prized for its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and lavender. Its short stature makes it ideal for rock gardens and as a border plant in smaller spaces. Despite its modest size, it holds a special place in the hearts of horticultural enthusiasts.
