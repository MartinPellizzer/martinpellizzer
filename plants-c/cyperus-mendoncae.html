Cyperus mendoncae

Cyperus mendoncae, commonly known as the Mexican flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to southern Texas and northern Mexico, where it thrives in wetland and riparian habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green or brown in color. It is often found in marshy areas and can tolerate periodic flooding.

Cyperus mendoncae plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
