Canarium hirsutum var. tomentellum

Canarium hirsutum var. tomentellum, commonly known as the woolly canarium, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, grayish foliage and small, ovoid leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers followed by fleshy, orange-red fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local communities.
