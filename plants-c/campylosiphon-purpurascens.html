Campylosiphon purpurascens

Campylosiphon purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-scented orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with a distinctive appearance, featuring slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
