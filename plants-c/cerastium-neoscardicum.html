Cerastium neoscardicum

Cerastium neoscardicum, commonly known as the rock chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and hairy stems. Cerastium neoscardicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.
