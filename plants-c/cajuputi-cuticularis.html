Cajuputi cuticularis

Cajuputi cuticularis, commonly known as the glossy leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that provide a shiny appearance and help reduce water loss. The plant has a slender, upright stem with a smooth, grayish-brown bark. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often followed by spherical, woody fruits.

Cajuputi cuticularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a shade plant.
