Clethra purpusii

Clethra purpusii, commonly known as the Mexican sweet pepper tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, fragrant foliage and showy white to pinkish flowers. Clethra purpusii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
