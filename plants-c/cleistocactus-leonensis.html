Cleistocactus leonensis

Cleistocactus leonensis, commonly known as the Leon's close-cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a short stem and dense, woolly spines. The flowers are typically yellow or orange and bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

This plant is adapted to arid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
