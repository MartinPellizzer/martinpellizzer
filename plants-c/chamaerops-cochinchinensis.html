Chamaerops cochinchinensis

Chamaerops cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese fan palm, is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of stiff, fan-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often near water sources. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts and as thatching material.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for landscaping purposes.
