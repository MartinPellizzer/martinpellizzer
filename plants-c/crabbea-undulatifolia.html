Crabbea undulatifolia

Crabbea undulatifolia, commonly known as the undulating leaf crabbea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Crabbea undulatifolia is also known for its ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
