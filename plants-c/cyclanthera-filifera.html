Cyclanthera filifera

Cyclanthera filifera, commonly known as the thread-leaf cucumber, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and squash. The plant is characterized by its slender, tendriled stems and small, heart-shaped leaves with fine, thread-like veins. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant, and it produces small, green, cucumber-like fruits.
