Campanula bononiensis var. tenuiflora

Campanula bononiensis var. tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
