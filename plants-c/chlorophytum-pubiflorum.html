Chlorophytum pubiflorum

Chlorophytum pubiflorum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in a variety of light conditions.

Its ability to purify indoor air makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
