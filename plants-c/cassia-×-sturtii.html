Cassia × sturtii

Cassia × sturtii, commonly known as the Sturt's Cassia, is a hybrid shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, fluffy inflorescences that appear in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various soil types.

Cassia × sturtii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
