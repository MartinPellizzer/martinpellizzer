Columnea nervosa

Columnea nervosa, commonly known as the veined flame flower, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins, which give it its scientific name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Columnea nervosa is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
