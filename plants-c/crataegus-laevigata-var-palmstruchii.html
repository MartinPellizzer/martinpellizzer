Crataegus laevigata var. palmstruchii

Crataegus laevigata var. palmstruchii, commonly known as the smooth Hawthorn or the white thorn, is a shrub or small tree native to western Europe.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and small, white flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits in the fall, which are edible but often used in jams and jellies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in hedgerows and woodland edges.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
