Canna glauca var. siamensis

Canna glauca var. siamensis, commonly known as the Siam canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, broad leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a silvery-blue hue on the upper surface. The plant produces showy, tropical-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

Its foliage and floral display make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
