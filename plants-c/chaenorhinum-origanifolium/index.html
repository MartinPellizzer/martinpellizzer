Chaenorhinum Origanifolium

Chaenorhinum origanifolium is a striking flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. Known for its vibrant purple and white blooms, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. This species belongs to the nightshade family, which includes many familiar plants like tomatoes and peppers. Its unique leaves, resembling those of oregano, give it a distinctive appearance. It is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
