Calophyllum procerum

Calophyllum procerum, commonly known as the slender calophyllum, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Calophyllaceae and is characterized by its smooth, often reddish-brown bark. The tree produces large, showy yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a prominent midrib.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
