Callicarpa shikokiana

Callicarpa shikokiana, commonly known as the Japanese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

The berries are not only ornamental but also attract birds and other wildlife.
