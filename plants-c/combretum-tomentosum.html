Combretum tomentosum

Combretum tomentosum, commonly known as the wooly combretum, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
