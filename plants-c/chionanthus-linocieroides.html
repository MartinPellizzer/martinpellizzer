Chionanthus linocieroides

Chionanthus linocieroides, commonly known as the silky tree or silky chionanthus, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

The silky tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
