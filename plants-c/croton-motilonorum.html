Croton motilonorum

Croton motilonorum, commonly known as the Motilonorum croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often strikingly ornamental species. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed margins, and its flowers are small and clustered in complex inflorescences. It is often cultivated for its decorative foliage rather than its flowers.

The plant is relatively low-maintenance and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
