Chrysanthemum Bornmuelleri

Chrysanthemum bornmuelleri is a rare and beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. This species is known for its striking yellow and white blooms that appear in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Due to its limited natural habitat, it is considered endangered and is often cultivated in botanical gardens. Its unique appearance makes it a prized addition to any collection of ornamental plants.
