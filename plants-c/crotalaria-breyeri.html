Crotalaria breyeri

Crotalaria breyeri, commonly known as the golden chain bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in long, drooping clusters. The plant has palmately divided leaves and produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Crotalaria breyeri is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
