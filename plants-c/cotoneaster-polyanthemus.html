Cotoneaster polyanthemus

Cotoneaster polyanthemus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It has glossy, dark green leaves that provide ornamental value throughout the growing season.

Cotoneaster polyanthemus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.
