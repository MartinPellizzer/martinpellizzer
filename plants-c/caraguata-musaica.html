Caraguata musaica

Caraguata musaica, commonly known as the Brazilian banana.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features large, broad leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers followed by elongated, fleshy fruits.

The fruit is typically consumed raw or cooked and is valued for its nutritional content and unique flavor.
