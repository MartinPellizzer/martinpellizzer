Catalpa longissima

Catalpa longissima, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The tree produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Catalpa longissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can reach heights of up to 30 meters.

It is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to urban environments.
