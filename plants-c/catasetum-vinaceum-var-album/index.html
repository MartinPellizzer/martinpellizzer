Catasetum Vinaceum Var. Album

Catasetum vinaceum var. album is a rare and striking orchid known for its elegant white blooms. Native to the tropical rainforests of South America, this variant stands out for its unique coloration and delicate appearance. It belongs to the Catasetum genus, which is famous for its specialized pollination mechanisms. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Its beauty and botanical significance make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
