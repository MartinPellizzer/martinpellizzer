Calceolaria uspallatensis

Calceolaria uspallatensis, commonly known as the Uspallata slipperwort, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant produces showy, bilaterally symmetrical blooms that are adapted for specific pollinators, such as certain species of bees.
