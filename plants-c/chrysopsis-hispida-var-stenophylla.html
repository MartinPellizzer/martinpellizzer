Chrysopsis hispida var. stenophylla

Chrysopsis hispida var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as rocky soils and dry slopes. Its botanical features include upright stems and small, clustered inflorescences.
