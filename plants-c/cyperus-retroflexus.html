Cyperus retroflexus

Cyperus retroflexus, commonly known as spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant has erect, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous root system that helps it thrive in wetland environments.

Cyperus retroflexus is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds and lakes.
