Cynanchum diazmirandae

Cynanchum diazmirandae, commonly known as the Dizmiran milkweed, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes milkweeds and related species. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, bead-like seed pods.

This species is noted for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
