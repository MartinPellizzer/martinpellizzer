Citrus × anonyma

Citrus × anonyma, commonly known as the bitter orange.

It is a hybrid citrus species native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its thick, aromatic peel and bitter, seedy fruit. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is typically used in traditional medicine and for making marmalade.

The botanical features of Citrus × anonyma make it an important species in both horticulture and pharmacology.
