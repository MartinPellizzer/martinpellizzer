Costus muluensis

Costus muluensis, commonly known as the African costus, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features upright, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a pleasant, aromatic scent.

Costus muluensis thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.
