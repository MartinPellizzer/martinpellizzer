Cattleya havenithii

Cattleya havenithii, commonly known as the Havenith Cattleya, is a rare and striking orchid native to the rainforests of Costa Rica.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange, with a distinctive dark red or purple lip. The plant has thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing structural support and aiding in photosynthesis.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
