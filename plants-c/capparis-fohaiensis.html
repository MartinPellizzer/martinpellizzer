Capparis fohaiensis

Capparis fohaiensis, commonly known as the Fohai sea holly, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Capparidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline environments. Its leaves are usually pinnately compound, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.

Capparis fohaiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in coastal ecosystems.
