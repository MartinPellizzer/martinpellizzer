Chlorophytum pubescens

Chlorophytum pubescens, commonly known as spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green stripes. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stalks, which are followed by clusters of baby plants called "spiderettes.". It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Chlorophytum pubescens is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
