Crassula natans var. natans

Crassula natans var. natans, commonly known as water crassula, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It thrives in shallow water and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in aquatic gardens.
