Clematis ochotensis var. japonica

Clematis ochotensis var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, recurved shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow berries that mature to a dark blue or black color. It thrives in temperate climates and requires support for its vigorous growth.

Clematis ochotensis var. japonica is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden settings.
