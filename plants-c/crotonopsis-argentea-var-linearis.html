Crotonopsis argentea var. linearis

Crotonopsis argentea var. linearis, commonly known as silverleaf croton.

This variant is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa, characterized by its striking silver-green foliage. The leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique leaf coloration and adaptability to various growing conditions.
