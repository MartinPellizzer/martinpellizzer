Ceratostylis linearis

Ceratostylis linearis, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its linear, grass-like leaves and a single, elongated spike of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. It has a short rhizome and produces numerous tiny, fragrant flowers that bloom during the rainy season.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
