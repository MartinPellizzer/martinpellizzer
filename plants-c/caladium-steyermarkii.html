Caladium steyermarkii

Caladium steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The leaves are typically a vibrant mix of green, red, and orange, making it a striking ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

The plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
