Cirsium × jeanjeanii

Cirsium × jeanjeanii, commonly known as the Jeanjean's thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads that resemble those of other thistle species. This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
