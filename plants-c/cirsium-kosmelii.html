Cirsium kosmelii

Cirsium kosmelii, commonly known as the Kosmelii thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are covered in stiff, bristly hairs, which help to reduce water loss.

Cirsium kosmelii is often found in open woodlands and along coastal areas.
