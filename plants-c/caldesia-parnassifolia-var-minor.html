Caldesia parnassifolia var. minor

Caldesia parnassifolia var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf Chinese evergreen, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to China.

It features glossy, dark green lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, pale greenish-white flowers on slender stalks during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-draining, moist soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
