Cattleya hatae

Cattleya hatae, commonly known as the Hatae's Cattleya, is a rare and striking orchid native to the rainforests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant produces thick, fleshy roots that help it absorb moisture from the humid environment. It thrives in high-altitude regions with ample sunlight and well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
