Croton novae-astigis

Croton novae-astigis, commonly known as the New Astigis Croton, is a tropical plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow quite large, adding a dramatic visual impact to any landscape.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
