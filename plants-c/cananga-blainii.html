Cananga blainii

Cananga blainii, commonly known as the African cananga, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.
