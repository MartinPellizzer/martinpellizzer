Corydalis verticillaris subsp. parviflora

Corydalis verticillaris subsp.

parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered cirnus, is a member of the Fumariaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers and slender stems.

Its botanical features make it a desirable addition to rock gardens and shaded borders.
