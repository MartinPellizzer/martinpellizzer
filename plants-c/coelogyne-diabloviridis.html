Coelogyne diabloviridis

Coelogyne diabloviridis, commonly known as the Diablo Green orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking emerald-green flowers, which are often adorned with contrasting yellow markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that help it conserve moisture in its tropical environment.

The orchid blooms during the dry season, showcasing its vibrant coloration and unique morphology.
