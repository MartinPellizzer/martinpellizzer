Cattleya bicolor var. wrigleyana

Cattleya bicolor var. wrigleyana, commonly known as the Wrigley's cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking bicolor flowers, typically featuring a mix of white and pink hues with a contrasting lip. The plant produces large, showy blooms that are highly prized in horticulture for their beauty and fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ease of cultivation and long-lasting floral display.
