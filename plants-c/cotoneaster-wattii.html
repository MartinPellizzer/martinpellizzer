Cotoneaster wattii

Cotoneaster wattii, commonly known as the Himalayan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it blooms with clusters of white or pink flowers in spring.

Cotoneaster wattii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
