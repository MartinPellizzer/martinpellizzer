Cuphea linarioides var. minutifolia

Cuphea linarioides var. minutifolia, commonly known as the tiny leaf cuphea, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features small, ovate leaves that are densely covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and orange, which bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
