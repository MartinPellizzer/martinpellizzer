Calea martiana

Calea martiana, commonly known as the "flower of the night," is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale purple flowers that open at night and close in the morning. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique nocturnal blooming habit.

Calea martiana is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
