Columnea polyantha

Columnea polyantha, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement.

Columnea polyantha is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract hummingbirds.
