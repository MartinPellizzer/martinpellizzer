Cleome felina

Cleome felina, commonly known as cat's tail, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery seed pods that resemble a cat's tail, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. Cleome felina is an annual herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and easy care requirements.
