Chamaedorea oblongata var. conferta

Chamaedorea oblongata var. conferta, commonly known as the compact bamboo palm, is a compact, evergreen fern-like plant native to Mexico.

It features narrow, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that grow in a dense, clustered formation. The plant has a short, stout stem with multiple offsets, giving it a bushy appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
