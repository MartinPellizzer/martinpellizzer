Cytisus zanonii

Cytisus zanonii, commonly known as the Zanon's broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate.

Cytisus zanonii is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
