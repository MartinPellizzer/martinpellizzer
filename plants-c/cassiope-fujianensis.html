Cassiope fujianensis

Cassiope fujianensis, commonly known as the Fujian snowbell, is a rare evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and showy white flowers. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
