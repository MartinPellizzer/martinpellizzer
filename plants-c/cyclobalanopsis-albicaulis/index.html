Cyclobalanopsis Albicaulis

Cyclobalanopsis albicaulis is a rare and ancient tree species native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and chestnuts, and is known for its longevity and ecological significance.
