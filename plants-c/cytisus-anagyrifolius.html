Cytisus anagyrifolius

Cytisus anagyrifolius, commonly known as the yellow broom of the Canary Islands, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has trifoliate leaves, which are compound and divided into three leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the island's climate.

Cytisus anagyrifolius is often found in coastal and open habitats, contributing to the unique flora of the Canary Islands.
