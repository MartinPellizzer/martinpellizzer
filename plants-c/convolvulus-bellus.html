Convolvulus bellus

Convolvulus bellus, commonly known as the pretty morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often opening in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Convolvulus bellus is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail along structures.
