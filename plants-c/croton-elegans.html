Croton elegans

Croton elegans, commonly known as the elegant croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton elegans typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
