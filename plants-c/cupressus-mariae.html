Cupressus mariae

Cupressus mariae, commonly known as the Mexican cypress, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the cypress family (Cupressaceae) and is characterized by its slender, columnar shape and scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, typical of conifers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
