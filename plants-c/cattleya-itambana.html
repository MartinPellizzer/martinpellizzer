Cattleya itambana

Cattleya itambana, commonly known as the Itambana orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a striking addition to any collection. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

The leaves are broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant flowers.
