Cristaria borchersii

Cristaria borchersii, commonly known as the Borchers' crista, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a bluish-green hue. The plant produces striking, yellow-orange flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
