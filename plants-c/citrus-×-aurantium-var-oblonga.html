Citrus × aurantium var. oblonga

Citrus × aurantium var. oblonga, commonly known as the sour orange.

It is a citrus species native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its oval-shaped, greenish-yellow fruits. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers and has dark green, glossy leaves. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and as a rootstock for other citrus plants.

The fruit is typically used in cooking and traditional medicine due to its high acidity and aromatic properties.
