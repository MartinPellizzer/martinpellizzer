Cleisostoma evrardii

Cleisostoma evrardii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces strikingly beautiful flowers with a golden-yellow coloration and a distinct, spurred labellum. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Cleisostoma evrardii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
