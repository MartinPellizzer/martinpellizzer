Clerodendrum spinescens var. parviflorum

Clerodendrum spinescens var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered spiny clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive blooms and unique foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
