Carex hudsonii var. polystachya

Carex hudsonii var. polystachya, commonly known as Hudson's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and swamps, and is often found in temperate regions.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense colonies, contributing to soil stabilization in wetland habitats.
