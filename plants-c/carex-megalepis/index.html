Carex Megalepis

Carex megalepis is a unique sedge species known for its striking appearance and ecological significance. Native to wetland environments, it thrives in moist soils and plays a vital role in maintaining aquatic ecosystems. Its tall, grass-like stems and clustered flower heads make it easily recognizable among other sedges. This plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities in garden settings. Despite its beauty, Carex megalepis remains relatively underappreciated in horticultural circles.
