Cynorkis fastigiata var. decolorata

Cynorkis fastigiata var. decolorata, commonly known as the variegated candle plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, candle-like stems that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. Its leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its elegant appearance.

It is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light.
