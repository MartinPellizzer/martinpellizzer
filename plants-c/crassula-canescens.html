Crassula canescens

Crassula canescens, commonly known as the Blue Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of bluish-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
