Corispermum sibiricum subsp. jenisseense

Corispermum sibiricum subsp.

jenisseense, commonly known as the Jenisean sandmat, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the cold climates of Siberia. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with small, fleshy leaves.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, arctic or alpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
