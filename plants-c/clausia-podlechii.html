Clausia podlechii

Clausia podlechii, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a unique member of the Cactaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in yellow spines, which provide protection from herbivores and excessive sunlight. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual element to its habitat. Its succulent structure allows it to store water efficiently, adapting to arid environments.

Clausia podlechii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
