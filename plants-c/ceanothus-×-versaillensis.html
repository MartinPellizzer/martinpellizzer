Ceanothus × versaillensis

Ceanothus × versaillensis, commonly known as the California lilac.

It is a hybrid shrub native to California, characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for coastal and Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are typically dark green and leathery, contributing to its drought tolerance.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
