Cardamine pancicii

Cardamine pancicii, commonly known as Pancici's watercress, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, alternate leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors.

Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it is often found in temperate regions of Europe.
