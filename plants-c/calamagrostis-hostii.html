Calamagrostis hostii

Calamagrostis hostii, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands or along streams. It has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a bluish-green color.

Calamagrostis hostii is valued in landscaping for its elegant look and ability to attract wildlife.
