Cirsium × grandii

Cirsium × grandii, commonly known as the giant thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, spiny leaves and showy purple to pink flower heads. Native to Europe, this species thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows and disturbed soils. The plant's robust growth and distinctive appearance make it a notable feature in its native environment.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
