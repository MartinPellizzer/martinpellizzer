Chenopodium stellulatum

Chenopodium stellulatum, commonly known as sandmat, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically thrives in sandy or disturbed soils. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are usually oval or lance-shaped. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as prairies and roadsides.

Chenopodium stellulatum is known for its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
