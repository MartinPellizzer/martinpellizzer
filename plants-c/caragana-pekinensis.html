Caragana pekinensis

Caragana pekinensis, commonly known as the Chinese pea shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-branched stems and small, compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, followed by pea-like pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant for dry climates.

Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in poor soils and prevent soil erosion.
