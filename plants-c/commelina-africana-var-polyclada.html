Commelina africana var. polyclada

Commelina africana var. polyclada, commonly known as the African spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Commelinaceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and vibrant blue or purple flowers.
