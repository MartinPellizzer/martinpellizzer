Chorizema lineare

Chorizema lineare, commonly known as the slender chorizema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming in late spring or early summer. Its fibrous root system helps it thrive in arid and rocky environments.

Chorizema lineare is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
