Chrysophyllum caeruleum

Chrysophyllum caeruleum, commonly known as the custard apple, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the Sapotaceae family.

It is native to regions of Africa, the Caribbean, and parts of Asia, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.
