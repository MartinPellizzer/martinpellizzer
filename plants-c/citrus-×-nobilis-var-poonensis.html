Citrus × nobilis var. poonensis

Citrus × nobilis var. poonensis, commonly known as kaffir lime, is a citrus plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pebbled, greenish-yellow fruit, which is widely used in culinary and aromatic applications. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a distinctive serrated edge. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to the aromatic fruit.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its essential oils and zest.
