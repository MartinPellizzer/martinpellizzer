Capraea acuminata

Capraea acuminata, commonly known as the acuminated rock cress, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is typically found in rocky or gravelly habitats, often in alpine or arctic regions. It has a short life cycle and is adapted to cold climates.
