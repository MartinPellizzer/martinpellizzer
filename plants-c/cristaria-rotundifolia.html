Cristaria rotundifolia

Cristaria rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed crista, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its rounded, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to parts of Africa and thrives in moist, tropical environments.
