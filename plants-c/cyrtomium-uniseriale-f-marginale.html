Cyrtomium uniseriale f. marginale

Cyrtomium uniseriale f. marginale, commonly known as the marginated holly fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive fronds with a narrow, wavy margin.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are dark green with a contrasting lighter margin, giving it an elegant appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

The fronds are leathery and persist for several years, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
