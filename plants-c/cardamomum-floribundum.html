Cardamomum floribundum

Cardamomum floribundum, commonly known as the fragrant flowering cardamom, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is closely related to the spice cardamom and shares similar aromatic properties. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and foliage.
