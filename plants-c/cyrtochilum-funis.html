Cyrtochilum funis

Cyrtochilum funis, commonly known as the thread orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive, elongated shape.

Cyrtochilum funis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
