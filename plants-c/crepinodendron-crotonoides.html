Crepinodendron crotonoides

Crepinodendron crotonoides, commonly known as the African croton, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant is notable for its ornamental value and has been used traditionally for various purposes in some African cultures.
