Carex Pamiroalaica

Carex pamiroalaica is a unique sedge species native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia. It thrives in cold, arid environments where few plants can survive. This plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and robust root system. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it an important part of its ecosystem. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species for botanical study.
