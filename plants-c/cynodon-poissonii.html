Cynodon poissonii

Cynodon poissonii, commonly known as the Poisson's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are usually green or brown in hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or along riverbanks.

Cynodon poissonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
