Campanula lusitanica var. cymosospicata

Campanula lusitanica var. cymosospicata, commonly known as the Cymosospicata bellflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
