Calyptrion Aubletii

Calyptrion aubletii is a rare and striking plant known for its unique, lantern-like flowers. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments. Its vibrant blooms, often in shades of yellow and orange, make it a popular choice among horticulturists. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ease of care. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
