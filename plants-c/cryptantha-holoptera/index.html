Cryptantha Holoptera

Blue flax, scientifically known as Cryptantha holoptera, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its deep blue to violet blooms that appear in late spring and early summer. This species thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for native gardens and restoration projects. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively understudied plant in the botanical world.
