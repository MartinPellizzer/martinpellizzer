Commelina acutispatha

Commelina acutispatha, commonly known as the sharp-leaved spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a pointed tip, which gives it its common name. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Commelina acutispatha is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
