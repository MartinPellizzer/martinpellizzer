Codonoboea Ridleyana

Codonoboea ridleyana is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often displaying shades of red and orange. This species is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists due to its unique appearance and limited natural habitat. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensuring its survival in the wild.
