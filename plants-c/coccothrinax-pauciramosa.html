Coccothrinax pauciramosa

Coccothrinax pauciramosa, commonly known as the slender palm, is a species of palm native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and a slender, unbranched stem. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to salty, windy conditions. Its inflorescences are clustered at the base of the leaves, and it produces small, fragrant flowers.

The fruit is a dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.
