Cineraria anampoza

Cineraria anampoza, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
