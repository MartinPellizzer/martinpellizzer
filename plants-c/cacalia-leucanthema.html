Cacalia leucanthema

Cacalia leucanthema, commonly known as whitehead's cacalia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Cacalia leucanthema is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
