Carex fenghuangshanica

Carex fenghuangshanica, commonly known as the Fenghuangshan Carex, is a species of sedge native to China.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flower spikes. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense mats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and wetland conservation efforts.
