Centaurea aspera subsp. marotii

Centaurea aspera subsp.

marotii, commonly known as the rough centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features erect, branched stems covered in stiff, grayish hairs.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers with purple-pink centers, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
