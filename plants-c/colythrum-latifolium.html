Colythrum latifolium

Colythrum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf cow-wheat, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. The seeds are dispersed by animals that eat the pods, contributing to its spread in natural habitats.
