Codiaeum illustre

Codiaeum illustre, commonly known as the croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can display shades of red, yellow, orange, and green. The leaves are typically broad and have a glossy texture, often with intricate patterns of veins. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This plant is popular as a ornamental species due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
