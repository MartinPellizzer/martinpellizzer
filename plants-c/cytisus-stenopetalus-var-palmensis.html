Cytisus stenopetalus var. palmensis

Cytisus stenopetalus var. palmensis, commonly known as the palmensis broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are simple, alternate, and typically have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to coastal and rocky environments.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and resilience in Mediterranean climates.
