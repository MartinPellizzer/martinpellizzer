Coelogyne longilabra

Coelogyne longilabra, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to white flowers with red or orange markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne longilabra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
