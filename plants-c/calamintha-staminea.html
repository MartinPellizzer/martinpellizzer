Calamintha staminea

Calamintha staminea, commonly known as narrowleaf catnip, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically pale blue to purple and are arranged in whorls along the stem.

Calamintha staminea is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
