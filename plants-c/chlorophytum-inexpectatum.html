Chlorophytum inexpectatum

Chlorophytum inexpectatum, commonly known as the unexpected spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with a distinctive white or silver stripe running along the midrib. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
