Cimicifuga kashmiriana

Cimicifuga kashmiriana, commonly known as Kashmir bugbane, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Kashmir and northern India.

It features tall, slender stems that bear large, white to pale purple flowers arranged in an upright panicle. The plant has palmately compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Cimicifuga kashmiriana is valued in herbal medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
