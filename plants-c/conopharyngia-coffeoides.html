Conopharyngia coffeoides

Conopharyngia coffeoides, commonly known as the coffee-like fern, is a unique plant species characterized by its intricate frond structure.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a serrated margin, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural environment.
