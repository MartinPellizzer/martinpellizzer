Cucumis dudaim

Cucumis dudaim, commonly known as the Persian cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe, North Africa, and western Asia. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that are typically harvested when immature and tender. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.
