Codonopsis meleagris

Codonopsis meleagris, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet to blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Codonopsis meleagris is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its traditional use in herbal medicine.
