Cephalaria leucantha var. effusa

Cephalaria leucantha var. effusa, commonly known as the whitehead cephalaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, slender stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
