Crocodilodes stobaeodes

Crocodilodes stobaeodes, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. This plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often resemble the patterns of crocodile skin, hence its common name. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or along riverbanks.

The plant has a complex root system and produces a single, upright stem with clustered inflorescences.
