Crassula columnaris subsp. prolifera

Crassula columnaris subsp.

prolifera, commonly known as the "Columnar Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features erect, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in response to sunlight.

The leaves are small, fleshy, and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements.
