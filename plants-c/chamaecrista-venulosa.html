Chamaecrista venulosa

Chamaecrista venulosa, commonly known as the slender crownflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in the spring.
