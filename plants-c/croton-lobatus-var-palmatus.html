Croton lobatus var. palmatus

Croton lobatus var. palmatus, commonly known as the palmate croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
