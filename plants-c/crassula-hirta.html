Crassula hirta

Crassula hirta, commonly known as the woolly crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves covered in a fine, white pubescence that gives it a woolly appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula hirta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and resilience.
