Clematicissus angustissima

Clematicissus angustissima, commonly known as the narrowleaf clematis, is a vine native to South America.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, often with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of blue or violet, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for climbing structures.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to gardens and landscapes.
