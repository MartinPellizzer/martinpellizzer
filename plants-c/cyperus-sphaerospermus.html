Cyperus sphaerospermus

Cyperus sphaerospermus, commonly known as the globe sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its compact, spherical inflorescence, which gives it its common name. It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and swamps.

Cyperus sphaerospermus is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist environments.
