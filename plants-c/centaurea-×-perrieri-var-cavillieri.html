Centaurea × perrieri var. cavillieri

Centaurea × perrieri var. cavillieri, commonly known as the Corsican centaury, is a hybrid plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30–60 cm. The plant features striking blue to violet flower heads, which are surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
