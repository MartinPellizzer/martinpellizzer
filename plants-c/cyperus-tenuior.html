Cyperus tenuior

Cyperus tenuior, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or seasonally wet environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, three-lobed flowers arranged in spikes. It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Cyperus tenuior plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
