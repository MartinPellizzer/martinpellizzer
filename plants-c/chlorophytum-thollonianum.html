Chlorophytum thollonianum

Chlorophytum thollonianum, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail Plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves with white or yellow markings. The plant produces long, arching inflorescences with small, greenish-white flowers. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
