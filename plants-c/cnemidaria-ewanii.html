Cnemidaria ewanii

Cnemidaria ewanii, commonly known as the Ewan's grasshopper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
