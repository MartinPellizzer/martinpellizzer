Cytisus ardoinoanus var. sauzeanus

Cytisus ardoinoanus var. sauzeanus, commonly known as the Sauzean broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, silvery hairs. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of narrow, elliptical leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant in suitable climates.
