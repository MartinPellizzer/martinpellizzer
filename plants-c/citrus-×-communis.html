Citrus × communis

Citrus × communis, commonly known as the common lemon.

It is a hybrid citrus plant originating from the cross between the citron and the pomelo. The tree produces small, yellow, oval-shaped fruits with a thin, peels and a juicy, acidic pulp. The leaves are evergreen, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It is widely cultivated for its fruit, which is used in cooking, beverages, and traditional medicine.
