Cryptanthus lavrasensis

Cryptanthus lavrasensis, commonly known as the Lavrasense carpet plant, is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is native to Brazil.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with dense clusters of small, colorful flowers that range from red to purple. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
