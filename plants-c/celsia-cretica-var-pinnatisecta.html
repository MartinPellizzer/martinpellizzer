Celsia cretica var. pinnatisecta

Celsia cretica var. pinnatisecta, commonly known as the Cretan celsia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Crete.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, pinnatisected leaves that give it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to white, which bloom in late summer.
