Cotoneaster natmataungensis

Cotoneaster natmataungensis, commonly known as the Natmataung cotoneaster, is a species of shrub native to the mountainous regions of Myanmar.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forest edges and rocky habitats.
