Cyclogramma omeiensis

Cyclogramma omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei cyclogramma, is a species of legume native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its seeds are encased in pods, which contribute to its ecological role in soil stabilization.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
