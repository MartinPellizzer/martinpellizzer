Crotalaria quangensis var. mullendersii

Crotalaria quangensis var. mullendersii, commonly known as the Quang Province crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, well-drained soils. It is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility, and is sometimes used in agroforestry systems.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological and horticultural studies.
