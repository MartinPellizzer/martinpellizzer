Capsella bursa-pastoris var. rubella

Capsella bursa-pastoris var. rubella, commonly known as the red weed or red shepherd's purse, is a variant of the common shepherd's purse.

It is an annual herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive triangular seed pods that turn red as they mature. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

The plant's botanical features make it easily identifiable and ecologically significant in various habitats.
