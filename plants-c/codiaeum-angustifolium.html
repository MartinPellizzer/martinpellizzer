Codiaeum angustifolium

Codiaeum angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage changes with the seasons, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
