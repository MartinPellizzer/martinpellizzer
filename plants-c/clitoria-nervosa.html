Clitoria nervosa

Clitoria nervosa, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and produces clusters of butterfly-like blue or purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and pods that contain edible seeds and beans. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

Botanically, it belongs to the Fabaceae family and is valued for its ornamental and agricultural significance.
