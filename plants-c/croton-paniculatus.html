Croton paniculatus

Croton paniculatus, commonly known as the Christmas croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that develop into orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
