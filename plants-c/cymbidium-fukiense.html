Cymbidium fukiense

Cymbidium fukiense, commonly known as the Fukiense orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China and Japan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit. The plant produces striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a waxy cuticle. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance.

Cymbidium fukiense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in traditional horticulture.
