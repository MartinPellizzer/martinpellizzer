Cyperus litwinowii

Cyperus litwinowii, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, often found in temperate regions. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale to brownish flowers. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and its ability to thrive in saturated soil conditions.

Cyperus litwinowii plays a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
