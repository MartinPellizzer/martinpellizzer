Clerodendrum porphyrocalyx

Clerodendrum porphyrocalyx, commonly known as the purple calyx clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant purple bracts that encase its small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
