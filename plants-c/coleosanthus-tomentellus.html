Coleosanthus tomentellus

Coleosanthus tomentellus, commonly known as the velvet tangle.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas, particularly found in parts of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
