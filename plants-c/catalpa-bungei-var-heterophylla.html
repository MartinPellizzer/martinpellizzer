Catalpa bungei var. heterophylla

Catalpa bungei var. heterophylla, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and vary in size, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a range of climates.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical significance in traditional medicine.
