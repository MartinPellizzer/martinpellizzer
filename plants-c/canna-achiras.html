Canna achiras

Canna achiras, commonly known as the blood lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting black or purple center. The plant features broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Canna achiras is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
