Cambessedesia pityrophylla

Cambessedesia pityrophylla, commonly known as the yellow-flowered daisies, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa. The plant features alternate, narrow, and hairy leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. Its showy yellow flowers are arranged in composite heads, with ray florets surrounding disk florets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
