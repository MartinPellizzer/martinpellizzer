Croton pseudochina

Croton pseudochina, commonly known as the Chinese croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, often featuring shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are typically palmately lobed, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Croton pseudochina is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
