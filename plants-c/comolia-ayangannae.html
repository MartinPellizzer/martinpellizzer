Comolia ayangannae

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, translucent fronds that resemble ghostly apparitions. Due to habitat loss, Comolia ayangannae is considered endangered and is of great interest to botanists studying rare plant adaptations.
