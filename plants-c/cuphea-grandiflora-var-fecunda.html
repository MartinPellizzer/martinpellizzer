Cuphea grandiflora var. fecunda

Cuphea grandiflora var. fecunda, commonly known as the showy cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it an ornamental favorite in tropical and subtropical regions.
