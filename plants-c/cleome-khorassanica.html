Cleome khorassanica

Cleome khorassanica, commonly known as the Persian spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and its clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that resemble spider legs. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for dry gardens.

Cleome khorassanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
