Coelogyne pumila var. pumila

Coelogyne pumila var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. Its rhizome is short and thick, supporting its upright, pseudobulb-like structure.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate blooms.
