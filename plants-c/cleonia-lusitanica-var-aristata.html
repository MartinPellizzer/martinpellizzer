Cleonia lusitanica var. aristata

Cleonia lusitanica var. aristata, commonly known as the Portuguese cleonia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
