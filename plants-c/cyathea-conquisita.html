Cyathea conquisita

Cyathea conquisita, commonly known as the Quinquisita tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, making it a striking feature in its natural habitat.

Cyathea conquisita is often found in cloud forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
