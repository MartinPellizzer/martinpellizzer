Crotalaria sphaerocarpa subsp. polycarpa

Crotalaria sphaerocarpa subsp.

polycarpa, commonly known as the globe crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae. It is characterized by its spherical seed pods and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in disturbed soils. It has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces numerous small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators.
