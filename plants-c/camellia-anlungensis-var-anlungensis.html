Camellia anlungensis var. anlungensis

Camellia anlungensis var. anlungensis, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species native to southern China.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces striking, double flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a smooth texture and a waxy surface. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia anlungensis var. anlungensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter blooming period.
