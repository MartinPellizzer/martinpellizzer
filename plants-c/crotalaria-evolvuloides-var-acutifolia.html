Crotalaria evolvuloides var. acutifolia

Crotalaria evolvuloides var. acutifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and trifoliate leaves with acute leaflets. The plant produces yellow, pea-like flowers that are clustered in racemose inflorescences. Its seeds are enclosed in hairy pods that split open when mature.

This species is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
