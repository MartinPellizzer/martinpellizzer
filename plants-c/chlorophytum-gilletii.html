Chlorophytum gilletii

Chlorophytum gilletii, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers that bloom in late summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
