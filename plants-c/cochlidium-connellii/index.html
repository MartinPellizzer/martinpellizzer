Cochlidium Connellii

Cochlidium connellii, scientifically known as Cochlidium connellii, is a rare and elusive land snail found primarily in the Pacific Northwest of the United States. This species belongs to the family Cochlididae and is characterized by its small, delicate shell that is often difficult to distinguish from other similar species. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat requirements, Cochlidium connellii is considered a species of conservation concern. Its survival is closely tied to the health of its specific microhabitats, which are increasingly threatened by environmental changes. Understanding and protecting Cochlidium connellii is essential for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
