Clethra canescens var. luzonica

Clethra canescens var. luzonica, commonly known as Luzon clethra, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the native flora in its endemic region.
