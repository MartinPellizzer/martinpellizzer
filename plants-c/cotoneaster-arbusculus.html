Cotoneaster arbusculus

Cotoneaster arbusculus, commonly known as the dwarf cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its compact size and ornamental berries make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
