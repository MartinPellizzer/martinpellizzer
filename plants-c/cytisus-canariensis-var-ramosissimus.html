Cytisus canariensis var. ramosissimus

Cytisus canariensis var. ramosissimus, commonly known as the Canary Island broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a striking visual element to its habitat. The plant has slender, often branched stems that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its leaves are small, oval-shaped, and arranged alternately along the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in suitable climates.
