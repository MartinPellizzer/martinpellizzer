Clematis homblei

Clematis homblei, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, recurved shape. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for climbing structures in gardens.

Clematis homblei is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
