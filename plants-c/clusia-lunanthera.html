Clusia lunanthera

Clusia lunanthera, commonly known as the moonflower clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, blooming at night. Clusia lunanthera is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in landscaping.
