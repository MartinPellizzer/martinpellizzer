Carex borbonica var. pallidior

Carex borbonica var. pallidior, commonly known as the pale Carex, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. This variety is native to parts of Madagascar and is adapted to moist, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense mats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
