Cladium Mariscus Subsp. Californicum

Cladium mariscus subsp. californicum is a unique plant species native to coastal regions of California. It thrives in brackish and saltwater environments, often found in marshes and tidal flats. This subspecies is known for its dense, grass-like foliage that helps stabilize wetland soils. Its ability to tolerate high salinity makes it an important component of coastal ecosystems. Despite its resilience, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
