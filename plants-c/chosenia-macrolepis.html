Chosenia macrolepis

Chosenia macrolepis, commonly known as the giant bamboo or Chinese bamboo, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of small, spikelet-like flowers.

Chosenia macrolepis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
