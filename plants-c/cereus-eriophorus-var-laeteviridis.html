Cereus eriophorus var. laeteviridis

Cereus eriophorus var. laeteviridis, commonly known as the green-flowered night bloomer, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces striking green flowers that open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and nocturnal blooming habit, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
