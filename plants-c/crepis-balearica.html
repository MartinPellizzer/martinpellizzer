Crepis balearica

Crepis balearica, commonly known as the Balearic cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and hairy, narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a compact growth habit and produces small, achene fruits.
