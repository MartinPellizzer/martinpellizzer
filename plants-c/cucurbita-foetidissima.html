Cucurbita foetidissima

Cucurbita foetidissima, commonly known as the stink cucumber, is a species of flowering plant in the gourd family.

It is native to Central and South America and is characterized by its large, rough, green to yellowish fruits. The plant produces a strong, unpleasant odor, which is most noticeable when the fruit is mature. It has broad, lobed leaves and can grow up to several meters in height.

Despite its pungent smell, it is sometimes cultivated for its edible seeds and ornamental value.
