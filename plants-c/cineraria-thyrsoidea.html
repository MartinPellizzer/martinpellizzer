Cineraria thyrsoidea

Cineraria thyrsoidea, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features silver-gray foliage with a woolly texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its blooms, which range from white to pale blue, are often used in ornamental horticulture.
