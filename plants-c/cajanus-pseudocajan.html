Cajanus pseudocajan

Cajanus pseudocajan, commonly known as false pigeon pea, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow or greenish flowers. Its pods are typically slender and contain several seeds.

Cajanus pseudocajan is often grown as a forage crop and can thrive in poor soil conditions.
