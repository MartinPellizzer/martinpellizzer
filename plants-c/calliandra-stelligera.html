Calliandra stelligera

Calliandra stelligera, commonly known as the coastal calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. The plant has bipinnate leaves, with each leaflet being small and oval-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites.

Calliandra stelligera is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil restoration efforts.
