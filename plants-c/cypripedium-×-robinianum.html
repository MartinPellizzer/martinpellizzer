Cypripedium × robinianum

Cypripedium × robinianum, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is used to trap pollinators. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and has a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
