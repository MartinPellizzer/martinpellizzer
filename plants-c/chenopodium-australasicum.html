Chenopodium australasicum

Chenopodium australasicum, commonly known as the Australian goosefoot, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Australia and nearby islands.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often pubescent. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in coastal areas, sandy soils, and agricultural fields.

Due to its adaptability, it can become a weed in various environments.
