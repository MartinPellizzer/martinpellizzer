Conyza acuminata

Conyza acuminata, commonly known as goatweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers that develop into fluffy, white to grayish seeds. Its leaves are typically narrow, hairy, and have a serrated edge.

Conyza acuminata is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates.
