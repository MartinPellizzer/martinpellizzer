Carex sinoaristata

Carex sinoaristata, commonly known as the Chinese basket sedge, is a species of sedges native to eastern Asia.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats, which can help stabilize soil in wetland areas.

Carex sinoaristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
