Cattleya nobilior f. amaliae

Cattleya nobilior f. amaliae, commonly known as the Amaliae Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant pink or red color with a contrasting white or cream lip. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants rather than in soil. Its native range includes parts of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in tropical climates.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
