Cyclachaena pedicellata

Cyclachaena pedicellata, commonly known as the pedicellate cycad, is a member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen cycad characterized by its stout, unbranched stem and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines.

Cyclachaena pedicellata is adapted to grow in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
