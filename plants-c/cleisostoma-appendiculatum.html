Cleisostoma appendiculatum

Cleisostoma appendiculatum, commonly known as the appendiculate cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, creeping rhizome and lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to white in color, with a distinctive labellum. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
