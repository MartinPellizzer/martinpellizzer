Canna indica var. rubra

Canna indica var. rubra, commonly known as the red canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy leaves and vibrant red-orange floral bracts that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens. Its foliage is broad and lance-shaped, adding a dramatic visual impact to any landscape.

The red canna is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
