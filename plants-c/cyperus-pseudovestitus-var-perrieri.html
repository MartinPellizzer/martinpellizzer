Cyperus pseudovestitus var. perrieri

Cyperus pseudovestitus var. perrieri, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and seasonally flooded areas. Its foliage is often used in horticulture for ornamental purposes due to its delicate appearance.

The varietal form perrieri is distinguished by its slender stems and reduced leaf blades compared to the typical species.
