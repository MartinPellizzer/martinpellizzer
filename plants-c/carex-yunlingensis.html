Carex yunlingensis

Carex yunlingensis, commonly known as Yunling sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province, and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest floors. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, ribbon-shaped leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers, typical of the Cyperaceae family.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Carex yunlingensis is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
