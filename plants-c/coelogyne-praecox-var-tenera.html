Coelogyne praecox var. tenera

Coelogyne praecox var. tenera, commonly known as the early blooming coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces racemes of white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
