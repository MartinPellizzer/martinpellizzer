Chenopodium carinatum f. rubellum

Chenopodium carinatum f. rubellum, commonly known as red sandmat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and alternate, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its seeds are small, smooth, and typically dispersed by wind.

This species is native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas and is often found in disturbed soils.
