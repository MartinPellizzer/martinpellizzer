Calonema brevisurum

Calonema brevisurum, commonly known as the short-leaf calonema, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically short and rounded, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
