Cotoneaster drogochius

Cotoneaster drogochius, commonly known as the Drogochius cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, red berries that mature in the fall. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with dense, thorny branches. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green upper surface and a pale underside.

The flowers are white or pink, appearing in clusters, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
