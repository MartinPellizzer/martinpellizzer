Carum franchetii

Carum franchetii, commonly known as Chinese parsley or Chinese caraway, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like parsley and dill. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, white or greenish-yellow flowers in umbels. Its seeds are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a spice.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.
