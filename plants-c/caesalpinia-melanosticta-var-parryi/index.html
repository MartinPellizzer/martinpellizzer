Caesalpinia Melanosticta Var. Parryi

Caesalpinia melanosticta var. parryi is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant red and yellow flowers. It belongs to the legume family and is native to parts of Central and South America. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm climates. Its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish.
