Coffea ratsimamangae

Coffea ratsimamangae, commonly known as the Ratsimamanga coffee.

It is a species of coffee native to Madagascar, characterized by its small, oval-shaped berries. The plant produces a unique aroma and flavor profile, distinct from other coffee species. It thrives in the humid, shaded environments of the island's rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
