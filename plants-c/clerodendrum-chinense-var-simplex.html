Clerodendrum chinense var. simplex

Clerodendrum chinense var. simplex, commonly known as Chinese flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it can grow as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
