Clematis viticella var. lineariloba

Clematis viticella var. lineariloba, commonly known as the linear-leaved Clematis, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers in early to mid-summer. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, giving the plant a distinctive, refined appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
