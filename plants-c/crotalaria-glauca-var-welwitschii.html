Crotalaria glauca var. welwitschii

Crotalaria glauca var. welwitschii, commonly known as the welwitschii crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters, which bloom during the warm season. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.

The species is also known for its potential medicinal properties and has been studied for its alkaloid content.
