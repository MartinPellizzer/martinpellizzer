Crassula schmidtii

Crassula schmidtii, commonly known as the Schmidth's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its slow growth and unique appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
