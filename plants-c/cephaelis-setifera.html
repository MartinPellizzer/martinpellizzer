Cephaelis setifera

Cephaelis setifera, commonly known as the slender flame vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features delicate, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange, with a distinctive flame-like appearance. The plant has compound leaves with slender, thread-like leaflets, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cephaelis setifera is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
