Cryptocarya insularis

Cryptocarya insularis, commonly known as the island cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the island of New Caledonia, where it grows in humid, tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its bark and leaves contain essential oils, which have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
