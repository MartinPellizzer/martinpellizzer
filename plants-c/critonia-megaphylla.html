Critonia megaphylla

Critonia megaphylla, commonly known as the giant fern, is a striking member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant features a robust rhizome system that helps it anchor and absorb nutrients from the soil. Its leaves are divided into numerous segments, creating a feathery appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
