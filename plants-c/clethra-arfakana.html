Clethra arfakana

Clethra arfakana, commonly known as the Arfakan sweet pepper, is a species native to New Guinea.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
