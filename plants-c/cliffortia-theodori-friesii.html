Cliffortia theodori-friesii

Cliffortia theodori-friesii, commonly known as the Karoo daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and gray-green, helping to reduce water loss.

It is often found in the Karoo region, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
