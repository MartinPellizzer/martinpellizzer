Clidemia clementiana

Clidemia clementiana, commonly known as the Hawaiian mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

Clidemia clementiana plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting various pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of Hawaiian ecosystems.
