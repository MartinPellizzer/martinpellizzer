Cnidoscolus angustidens

Cnidoscolus angustidens, commonly known as the arrow leaf or arrowhead plant, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape, which gives it its common name. Its stems are often covered in small, stiff spines, providing a defensive mechanism against herbivores.

The plant is also known for its ability to thrive in moist, shady environments.
