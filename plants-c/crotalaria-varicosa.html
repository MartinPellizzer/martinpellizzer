Crotalaria varicosa

Crotalaria varicosa, commonly known as the variable crownflower, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and palmate leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides. Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Crotalaria varicosa is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
