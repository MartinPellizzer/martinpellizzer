Chrysanthemum alpinum f. hutchisiifolium

Chrysanthemum alpinum f. hutchisiifolium, commonly known as the alpine chrysanthemum, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow to orange flower heads. The foliage is distinctive, with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.

It is also cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and alpine landscapes.
