Cheirolophus junonianus var. isoplexiphyllus

Cheirolophus junonianus var. isoplexiphyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine white or grayish pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
