Calathea cannoides

Calathea cannoides, commonly known as the pink princess calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and intricate leaf patterns, which often feature shades of pink, red, and green. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture that enhances their visual appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
