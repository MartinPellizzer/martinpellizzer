Cnestis trichopoda

Cnestis trichopoda, commonly known as the hairy leaf euphorbia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, a characteristic feature of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant produces a milky latex sap, which is a common trait among members of its family.
