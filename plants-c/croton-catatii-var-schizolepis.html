Croton catatii var. schizolepis

Croton catatii var. schizolepis, commonly known as the Schizolepis croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features thick, waxy leaves with intricate patterns of red, yellow, and green, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
