Crepidiastrum × semiauriculatum

Crepidiastrum × semiauriculatum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a hybrid plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often feature striking variegation with yellow or white markings. The plant is a member of the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular houseplant.
