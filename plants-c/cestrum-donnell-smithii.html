Cestrum donnell-smithii

Cestrum donnell-smithii, commonly known as the Donnell-Smith's cestrum, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green appearance.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, yellowish seeds.
