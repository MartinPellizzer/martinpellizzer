Cyperus mutisii var. plenus

Cyperus mutisii var. plenus, commonly known as the plenum flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of small, three-sided spikelets. It thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in marshy areas or along waterways.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various wetland environments.
