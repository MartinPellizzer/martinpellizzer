Crabbea pinnatifida

Crabbea pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Crabbea pinnatifida is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
