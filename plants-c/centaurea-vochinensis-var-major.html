Centaurea vochinensis var. major

Centaurea vochinensis var. major, commonly known as the Greater Vochin's Centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, clustered flower heads.
