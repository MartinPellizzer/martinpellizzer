Carduus mollis

Carduus mollis, commonly known as the common sowthistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, spiny leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and fields. It is native to Europe but has been naturalized in other parts of the world.

The leaves are edible when young but become bitter and less palatable as the plant matures.
