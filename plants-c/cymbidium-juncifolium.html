Cymbidium juncifolium

Cymbidium juncifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and tall flowering stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

Cymbidium juncifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
