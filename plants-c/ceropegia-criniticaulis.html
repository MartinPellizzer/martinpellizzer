Ceropegia criniticaulis

Ceropegia criniticaulis, commonly known as the curly-stemmed ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy, succulent stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Ceropegia criniticaulis is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
