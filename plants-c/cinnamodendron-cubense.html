Cinnamodendron cubense

Cinnamodendron cubense, commonly known as the Cuban cinnamon tree, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Cuba and the Caribbean. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its bark and leaves contain essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal and aromatic properties.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in humid, lowland areas.
