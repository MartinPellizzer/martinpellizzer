Crassula vaginata subsp. vaginata

Crassula vaginata subsp.

vaginata, commonly known as the Cape Prostrate Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, arranged in a prostrate or semi-upright growth habit.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

This species is popular in rock gardens and as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
