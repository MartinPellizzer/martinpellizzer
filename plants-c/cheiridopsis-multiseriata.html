Cheiridopsis multiseriata

Cheiridopsis multiseriata, commonly known as the Many-Rowed Iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder. It produces small, pink to violet flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value to its appearance.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
