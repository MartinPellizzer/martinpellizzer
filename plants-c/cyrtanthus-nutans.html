Cyrtanthus nutans

Cyrtanthus nutans, commonly known as the nodding bellflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that droop downward, typically in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cyrtanthus nutans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
