Cyperus onerosus

Cyperus onerosus, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowering structures. Cyperus onerosus typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and pond margins, where it thrives in moist to aquatic conditions.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for water gardens and pond landscapes.
