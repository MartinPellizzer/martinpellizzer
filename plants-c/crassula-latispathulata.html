Crassula latispathulata

Crassula latispathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a compact rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.

Crassula latispathulata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
