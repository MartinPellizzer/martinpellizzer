Cotyledon sulphurea

Cotyledon sulphurea, commonly known as the yellow cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically yellow or orange in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Cotyledon sulphurea is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its vibrant color and ease of care.
