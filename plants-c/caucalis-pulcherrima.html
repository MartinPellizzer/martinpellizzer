Caucalis pulcherrima

Caucalis pulcherrima, commonly known as the pretty caucalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant features slender, hairy stems and linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Caucalis pulcherrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and easy maintenance in gardens.
