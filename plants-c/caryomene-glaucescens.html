Caryomene glaucescens

Caryomene glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green caryomene, is a flowering plant species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that exhibit a bluish-green hue, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Caryomene glaucescens typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in ecological and horticultural contexts.
