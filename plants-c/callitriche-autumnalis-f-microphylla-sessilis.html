Callitriche autumnalis f. microphylla-sessilis

Callitriche autumnalis f. microphylla-sessilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water starwort, is a aquatic plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically less than 1 cm in length, arranged in whorls around the stem.
