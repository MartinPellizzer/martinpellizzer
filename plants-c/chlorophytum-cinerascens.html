Chlorophytum cinerascens

Chlorophytum cinerascens, commonly known as the "Cineraria" or "Cinerascens Snake Plant," is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a silvery or grayish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, which bloom in late summer. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Chlorophytum cinerascens is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
