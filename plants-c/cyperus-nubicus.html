Cyperus nubicus

Cyperus nubicus, commonly known as the desert flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with deep roots that help it access water from deeper soil layers.

Cyperus nubicus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small desert organisms.
