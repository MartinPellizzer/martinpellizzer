Crassula schimperi f. filamentosa

Crassula schimperi f. filamentosa, commonly known as the filamentous crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
