Costus ruber

Costus ruber, commonly known as red costus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Costaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers arranged in a spike. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Costus ruber is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscapes for its vibrant color and architectural form.
