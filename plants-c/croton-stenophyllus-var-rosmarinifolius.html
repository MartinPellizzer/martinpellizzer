Croton stenophyllus var. rosmarinifolius

Croton stenophyllus var. rosmarinifolius, commonly known as rosemary leaf croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its distinctive foliage.

Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a silvery-green appearance with prominent veins, resembling the foliage of rosemary. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
