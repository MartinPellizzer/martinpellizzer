Croton palawanensis

Croton palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan croton, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a distinctive, often lobed shape.
