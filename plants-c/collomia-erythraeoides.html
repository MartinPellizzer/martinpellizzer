Collomia erythraeoides

Collomia erythraeoides, commonly known as the red globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features showy, red or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the stems are often hairy.

It is adapted to arid environments and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
