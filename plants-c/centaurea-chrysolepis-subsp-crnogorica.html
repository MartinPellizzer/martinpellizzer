Centaurea chrysolepis subsp. crnogorica

Centaurea chrysolepis subsp.

crnogorica, commonly known as the Balkan star-thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula. It features spiny, glabrous stems and lanceolate leaves with a bluish-gray hue.

The plant produces bright yellow to orange composite flowers arranged in dense, spherical heads. It thrives in dry, calcareous soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and invasive potential, it is sometimes cultivated or considered a weed in certain regions.
