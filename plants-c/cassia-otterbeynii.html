Cassia otterbeynii

Cassia otterbeynii, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features palmate leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its most striking characteristic is its bright yellow to orange inflorescences that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Cassia otterbeynii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
