Cotoneaster multiflorus proles granatensis

Cotoneaster multiflorus proles granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense Cotoneaster, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, rounded growth with small, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in autumn. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a hedging plant.
