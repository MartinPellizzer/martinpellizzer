Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. microphyllum

Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. microphyllum, commonly known as Ceylon cinnamon or true cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its slender, aromatic bark that is harvested for its essential oils and medicinal properties. The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a distinctive cinnamon scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aromatic wood and bark.

This variety is distinguished by its smaller leaves compared to other cinnamon species.
