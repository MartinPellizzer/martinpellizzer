Callitriche aestivalis

Callitriche aestivalis, commonly known as summer waterstar, is a small aquatic plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds, ditches, and slow-moving streams. The plant has small, star-shaped leaves that are often floating or partially submerged. Its flowers are tiny, white or pale blue, and appear in clusters near the water's surface.

Callitriche aestivalis is a perennial species that spreads through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
