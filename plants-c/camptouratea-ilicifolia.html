Camptouratea ilicifolia

Camptouratea ilicifolia, commonly known as the spiny holly.

It is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its small, holly-like leaves with spiny margins. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain seeds.

Camptouratea ilicifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
