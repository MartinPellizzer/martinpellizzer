Chamaecrista kirkii var. glabra

Chamaecrista kirkii var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth cassia, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, elliptical leaflets and small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ornamental use and ecological restoration.
