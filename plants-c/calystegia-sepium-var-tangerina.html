Calystegia sepium var. tangerina

Calystegia sepium var. tangerina, commonly known as the tangerine bindweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow or orange throat. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release small, dry seeds when mature.
