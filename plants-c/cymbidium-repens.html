Cymbidium repens

Cymbidium repens, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with long, slender pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cymbidium repens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
