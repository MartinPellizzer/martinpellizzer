Cirsium × patens

Cirsium × patens, commonly known as the common thistle, is a hybrid species within the thistle family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in a variety of habitats, including meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils. It is native to Europe but has been introduced to other parts of the world.

Cirsium × patens is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to spread rapidly.
