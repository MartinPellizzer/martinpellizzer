Chrysanthemum minimum

Chrysanthemum minimum, commonly known as the dwarf chrysanthemum, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features finely divided, gray-green leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.

Chrysanthemum minimum is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
