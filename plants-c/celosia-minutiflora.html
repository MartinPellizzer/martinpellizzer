Celosia minutiflora

Celosia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf crown flower, is a compact, annual plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features dense, branching stems covered with small, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces bracts that resemble a crown, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a bedding plant.
