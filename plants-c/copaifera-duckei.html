Copaifera duckei

Copaifera duckei, commonly known as Brazilian copaiba, is a species of flowering plant in the family Leguminosae.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest and is characterized by its aromatic, resinous sap. The plant typically grows as a medium to large tree, reaching heights of up to 30 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers are small and clustered, with both male and female parts present on the same plant.
