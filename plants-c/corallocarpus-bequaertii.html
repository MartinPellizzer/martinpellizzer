Corallocarpus bequaertii

Corallocarpus bequaertii, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae, native to South Africa. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
