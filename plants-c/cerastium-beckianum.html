Cerastium beckianum

Cerastium beckianum, commonly known as Beck's mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae, which includes many flowering plants with showy blooms. The plant features small, succulent leaves that are rounded and often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are white or pale pink, typically appearing in clusters at the ends of stems.
