Cnidoscolus graminifolius

Cnidoscolus graminifolius, commonly known as the arrowleaf stinging tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. Its stems and leaves contain toxic compounds, making it potentially hazardous to handle without proper protection.
