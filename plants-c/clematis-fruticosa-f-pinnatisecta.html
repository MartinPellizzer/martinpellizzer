Clematis fruticosa f. pinnatisecta

Clematis fruticosa f. pinnatisecta, commonly known as the pinnatisect clematis, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It features finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple during late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its elegant foliage and blooms.
