Chamaedorea boliviensis

Chamaedorea boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Aroid family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clustered, feather-like leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, making it well-suited for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is glossy and dark green, adding an elegant touch to any space.

It is relatively low-maintenance, requiring moderate watering and indirect sunlight.
