Cucumis insignis

Cucumis insignis, commonly known as the horned cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like shape and spiny texture. The plant produces yellow flowers and small, edible fruits that are typically harvested when immature. Its leaves are broad and lobed, and it thrives in warm, tropical climates.

It is often grown as an ornamental or for its unique fruit, which has a mild, cucumber-like flavor.
