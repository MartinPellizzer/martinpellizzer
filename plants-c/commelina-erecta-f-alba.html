Commelina erecta f. alba

Commelina erecta f. alba, commonly known as the white commelina, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its erect growth habit and striking white flowers. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The white petals and prominent blue-violet sepals make it a visually distinctive species in its habitat.
