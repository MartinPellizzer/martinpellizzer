Camptopus flamignii

Camptopus flamignii, commonly known as the flamecup, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and coastal areas. Camptopus flamignii is a perennial herb that grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.

It is valued in horticulture for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
