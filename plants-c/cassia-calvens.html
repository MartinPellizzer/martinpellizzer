Cassia calvens

Cassia calvens, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cassia calvens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
