Commelina diffusa var. gigas

Commelina diffusa var. gigas, commonly known as giant commelina, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking blue-violet flowers with three petals and three sepals, often accompanied by two white bracts. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
