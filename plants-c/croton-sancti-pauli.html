Croton sancti-pauli

Croton sancti-pauli, commonly known as the Paul's croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are typically thick and leathery, with a distinctive veining pattern.

Croton sancti-pauli thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
