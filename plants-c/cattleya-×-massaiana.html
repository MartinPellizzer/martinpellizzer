Cattleya × massaiana

Cattleya × massaiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, showy flowers with vibrant colors, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has broad, leathery leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Its flowers are highly prized in horticulture for their beauty and fragrance.
