Chrysosplenium maximowiczii

Chrysosplenium maximowiczii, commonly known as the silverleaf sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Saxifragaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage with a silvery appearance due to the presence of fine, soft hairs on its leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in late spring or early summer. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
