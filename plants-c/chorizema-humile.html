Chorizema humile

Chorizema humile, commonly known as the small chorizema, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant characteristics.

Its botanical structure reflects its evolutionary adaptation to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.
