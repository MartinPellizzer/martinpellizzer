Cordyline zeylanica

Cordyline zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon cordyline, is a tropical plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant typically grows as a clump-forming shrub, reaching heights of up to 6 feet. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental foliage in gardens.

Cordyline zeylanica thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
