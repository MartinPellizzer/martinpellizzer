Cotyledon laxa var. paniculata

Cotyledon laxa var. paniculata, commonly known as the panicle cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, and it produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in summer. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience to drought.
