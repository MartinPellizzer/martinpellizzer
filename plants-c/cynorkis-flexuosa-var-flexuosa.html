Cynorkis flexuosa var. flexuosa

Cynorkis flexuosa var. flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible water plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its creeping rhizomes and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers arranged in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in wetlands, marshes, and slow-moving freshwater environments.

Due to its adaptability, it is often found in regions with high humidity and abundant water sources.
