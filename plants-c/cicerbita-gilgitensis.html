Cicerbita gilgitensis

Cicerbita gilgitensis, commonly known as the Gilgit bittercress, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine layer of hair, while its flowers are small and white or pale yellow.

Cicerbita gilgitensis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
