Coelogyne selebica

Coelogyne selebica, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments and has fibrous roots that help it anchor in the soil. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, providing a lush appearance.

Coelogyne selebica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
