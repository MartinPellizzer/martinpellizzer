Coffea canephora var. gossweileri

Coffea canephora var. gossweileri, commonly known as the Gossweiler variety, is a cultivated form of the robusta coffee plant.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and relatively large, oval-shaped berries. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its robust flavor and high caffeine content, making it a significant contributor to the global coffee industry.
