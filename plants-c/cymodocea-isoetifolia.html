Cymodocea isoetifolia

Cymodocea isoetifolia, commonly known as the slender seagrass, is a marine flowering plant belonging to the family Cymodoceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that grow in dense underwater meadows. This species is typically found in shallow coastal waters, often in association with sandy or muddy substrates. Cymodocea isoetifolia plays an important role in coastal ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various marine organisms.
