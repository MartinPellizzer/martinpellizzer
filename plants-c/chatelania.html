Chatelania

Chatelania, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Bignoniaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling a shower of golden droplets. Chatelania is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
