Cacalia Fruticulosa

Cacalia fruticulosa is a striking member of the Apiaceae family known for its vibrant purple flowers and fern-like foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and cool climates. Its delicate appearance belies its hardy nature, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. The plant's unique structure and color make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. With its historical use in traditional medicine, Cacalia fruticulosa holds both ornamental and medicinal significance.
