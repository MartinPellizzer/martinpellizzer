Cyperus luteus var. luteus

Cyperus luteus var. luteus, commonly known as the yellow flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, such as swamps and roadside ditches. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in dense spikes. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
