Clitoria falcata var. latifolia

Clitoria falcata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped blue or purple flowers. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. This plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It is also cultivated for ornamental purposes and as a source of natural dyes.
