Columnea heterophylla

Columnea heterophylla, commonly known as the variable flame flower, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has alternate, simple, and often hairy leaves that vary in shape depending on light conditions. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is commonly cultivated as a decorative plant.

Columnea heterophylla is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
