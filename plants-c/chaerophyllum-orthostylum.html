Chaerophyllum orthostylum

Chaerophyllum orthostylum, commonly known as the upright hogweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors. The plant features a tall, unbranched stem with large, compound leaves that are deeply lobed. Its small, white flowers are arranged in an umbel, typical of the carrot family.
