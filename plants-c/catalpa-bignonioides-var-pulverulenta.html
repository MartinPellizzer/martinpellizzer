Catalpa bignonioides var. pulverulenta

Catalpa bignonioides var. pulverulenta, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The tree produces long, pendulous clusters of white to pale yellow flowers in the spring. Its bark is smooth and gray, with a distinctive flaky texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
