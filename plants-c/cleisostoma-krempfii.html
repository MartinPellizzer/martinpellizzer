Cleisostoma krempfii

Cleisostoma krempfii, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant has thick, fleshy roots adapted for absorbing moisture from the air. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Cleisostoma krempfii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
