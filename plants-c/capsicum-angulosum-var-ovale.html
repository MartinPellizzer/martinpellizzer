Capsicum angulosum var. ovale

Capsicum angulosum var. ovale, commonly known as the round horn pepper, is a variant of the horn pepper species native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded, horn-like fruit shape, which can grow up to 15 centimeters in length. The plant produces yellow or orange fruits that mature to a reddish color, often used in culinary applications for their mild to medium heat level. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions with well-drained soil, and requires a long growing season to reach maturity.

The plant's botanical features make it a unique addition to both ornamental and edible gardens.
