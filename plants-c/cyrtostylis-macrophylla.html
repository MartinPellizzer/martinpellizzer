Cyrtostylis macrophylla

Cyrtostylis macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like inflorescence and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually greenish-white with a yellowish lip, and they bloom during the autumn season.

Cyrtostylis macrophylla is a terrestrial orchid that relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.
