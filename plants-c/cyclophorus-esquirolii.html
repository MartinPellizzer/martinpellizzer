Cyclophorus esquirolii

Cyclophorus esquirolii, commonly known as the Esquiroli's cyclophorus, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to purple, which attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
