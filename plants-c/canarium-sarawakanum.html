Canarium sarawakanum

Canarium sarawakanum, commonly known as the Sarawak canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by hard, woody fruits containing oily seeds. It is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, thriving in humid, shaded environments.

The tree is valued for its durable wood and has traditional uses in local communities.
