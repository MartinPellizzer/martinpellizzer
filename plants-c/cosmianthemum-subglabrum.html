Cosmianthemum subglabrum

Cosmianthemum subglabrum, commonly known as the smooth cosmos, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure to thrive.

Its botanical name reflects its smooth, hairless appearance, which distinguishes it from related species.
