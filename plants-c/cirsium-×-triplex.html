Cirsium × triplex

Cirsium × triplex, commonly known as the triplex thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, serrated leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats. It is native to Europe but has been introduced to other regions, including parts of North America.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered a potential invasive species in some areas.
