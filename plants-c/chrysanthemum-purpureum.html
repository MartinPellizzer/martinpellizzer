Chrysanthemum purpureum

Chrysanthemum purpureum, commonly known as the purple chrysanthemum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, depending on the variety. The plant has finely divided, feather-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Chrysanthemum purpureum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
