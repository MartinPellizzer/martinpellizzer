Crinum carinifolium

Crinum carinifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed crinum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, sometimes unpleasant fragrance. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are arranged in a fan shape at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

Crinum carinifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
