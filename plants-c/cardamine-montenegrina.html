Cardamine montenegrina

Cardamine montenegrina, commonly known as the Montenegro watercress, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along streams. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately divided, and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and arranged in racemes, typical of the family.
