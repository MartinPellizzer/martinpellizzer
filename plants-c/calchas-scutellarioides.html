Calchas scutellarioides

Calchas scutellarioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved scutellaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Calchas scutellarioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
