Cynanchum stenomeres

Cynanchum stenomeres, commonly known as narrow-leaved milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is known for its milkweed species. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and produce a milky sap that can be toxic.

Cynanchum stenomeres is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands.
