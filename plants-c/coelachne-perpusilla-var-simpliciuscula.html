Coelachne perpusilla var. simpliciuscula

Coelachne perpusilla var. simpliciuscula, commonly known as the small-leafed grass.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa. The species is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, pale green to blue-gray flowers. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open, dry habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered plant species.
