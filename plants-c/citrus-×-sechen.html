Citrus × sechen

Citrus × sechen, commonly known as the hybrid citron.

It is a hybrid citrus plant resulting from the cross between the citron (Citrus medica) and the pomelo (Citrus maxima). This plant typically produces large, thick-skinned fruits with a high content of essential oils and a distinct aromatic profile. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a glossy green surface and prominent veins.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the extraction of citron oil used in perfumery and food industries.
