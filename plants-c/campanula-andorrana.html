Campanula andorrana

Campanula andorrana, commonly known as the Alpine bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and is often found at high altitudes.

Campanula andorrana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
