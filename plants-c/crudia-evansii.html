Crudia evansii

Crudia evansii, commonly known as the Cape barometer, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that resemble a barometer, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Crudia evansii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
