Calathea zebrina var. binotii

Calathea zebrina var. binotii, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a tropical foliage plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of green, silver, and white markings, creating a zebra-like pattern. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a glossy surface that enhances their ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
