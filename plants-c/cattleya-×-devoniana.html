Cattleya × devoniana

Cattleya × devoniana, commonly known as the Devonian orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya × devoniana is known for its strong fragrance and is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its role in orchid hybridization.
