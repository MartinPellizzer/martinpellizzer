Cryptocarya infectoria var. opaca

Cryptocarya infectoria var. opaca, commonly known as the opaca cryptocarya, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, clustered flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

The fruit is a small, woody drupe that matures to a dark color.
