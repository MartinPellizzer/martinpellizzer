Cacalia capitata

Cacalia capitata, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Cacalia capitata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate appearance and ease of care.
