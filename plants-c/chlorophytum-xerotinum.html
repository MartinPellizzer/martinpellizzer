Chlorophytum xerotinum

Chlorophytum xerotinum, commonly known as the curly snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, curly, dark green leaves that often have a slightly variegated appearance. The plant is a succulent, capable of storing water in its fleshy leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, white flowers on slender stalks during the summer months.

Chlorophytum xerotinum is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and air-purifying qualities.
