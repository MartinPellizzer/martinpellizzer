Crassula ramuliflora var. bewsii

Crassula ramuliflora var. bewsii, commonly known as the Bewsii Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge under stress. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
