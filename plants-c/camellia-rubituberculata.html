Camellia rubituberculata

Camellia rubituberculata, commonly known as the red tubercled camellia, is a species native to southern China and northern Vietnam.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and striking red flowers with prominent tubercles. The plant produces showy, camellia-like flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Camellia rubituberculata prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
