Combretum rhodanthum

Combretum rhodanthum, commonly known as the red-flowered combretum, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a notable species in the Combretaceae family.
