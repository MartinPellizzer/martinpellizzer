Calamagrostis eminens var. tunariensis

Calamagrostis eminens var. tunariensis, commonly known as the Tunariensis Panic Grass, is a tall, perennial grass native to South America.

It features narrow, silver-gray blades that are often adorned with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, spike-like flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental and ecological landscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to stabilize soil in wet or marginal areas.
