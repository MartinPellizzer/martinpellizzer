Callitriche × hybrida

Callitriche × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid water starwort, is a semi-aquatic plant belonging to the plantain family (Plantaginaceae).

It typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and slow-moving streams. The plant has small, star-shaped leaves that are often floating or partially submerged, with a distinctive waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, and they bloom in clusters near the water's surface.

Callitriche × hybrida is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
