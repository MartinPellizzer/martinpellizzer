Curculigo petiolata

Curculigo petiolata, commonly known as the slender arrowhead plant, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and small, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and wetland restoration projects.
