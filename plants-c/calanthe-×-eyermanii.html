Calanthe × eyermanii

Calanthe × eyermanii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Calanthe triplicata and Calanthe discolor, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. This plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to the vibrant blooms.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded rockeries.
