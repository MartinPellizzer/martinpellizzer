Calathea zebrina

Calathea zebrina, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a tropical foliage plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a zebra-like pattern of dark green and silver stripes. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its unique leaf patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Calathea zebrina is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
