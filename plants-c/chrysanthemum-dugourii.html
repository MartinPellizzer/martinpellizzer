Chrysanthemum dugourii

Chrysanthemum dugourii, commonly known as the African chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in horticulture for both decorative and medicinal purposes.
