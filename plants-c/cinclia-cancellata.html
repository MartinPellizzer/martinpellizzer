Cinclia cancellata

Cinclia cancellata, commonly known as the white-tailed spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are often adorned with distinctive markings. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests or heathlands.

Cinclia cancellata is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
