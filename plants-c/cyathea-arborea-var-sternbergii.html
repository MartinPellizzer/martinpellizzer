Cyathea arborea var. sternbergii

Cyathea arborea var. sternbergii, commonly known as the Sternberg's tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a large, evergreen tree fern characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feathery fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bioluminescent and can grow up to several meters in length.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
