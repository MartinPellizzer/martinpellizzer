Cyperus ohwii

Cyperus ohwii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. Its slender, grass-like stems are often clustered and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Cyperus ohwii produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are arranged in spikelets, typical of the sedge family.
