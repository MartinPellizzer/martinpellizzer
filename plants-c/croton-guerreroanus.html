Croton guerreroanus

Croton guerreroanus, commonly known as the Guerrero croton, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often strikingly ornamental species. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, typical of the genus Croton.

The plant is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage rather than its flowers.
