Calonemorchis rigens

Calonemorchis rigens, commonly known as the rigid calonemorchis, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often violet or purple, flowers. The plant typically produces a single, erect stem bearing a cluster of showy orchid-like blooms. Native to specific regions in South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among botanical enthusiasts.
