Camellia jinyunshanica

Camellia jinyunshanica, commonly known as the Jin Yun Shan camellia, is a species native to China, specifically the Jin Yun Shan region.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white, blooming in late winter to early spring. Camellia jinyunshanica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for temperate climates.
