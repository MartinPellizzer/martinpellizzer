Collinsia heterophylla var. austromontana

Collinsia heterophylla var. austromontana, commonly known as the Coast Mountains bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along streambanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and attractive addition to native plant gardens.
