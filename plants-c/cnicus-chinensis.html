Cnicus chinensis

Cnicus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides. Its leaves are edible and have been used in traditional Chinese cuisine and medicine.

The plant produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
