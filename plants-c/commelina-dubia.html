Commelina dubia

Commelina dubia, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Commelina family, which includes many species of dayflowers. The plant features striking violet to red flowers with three petals and a distinctive three-lobed spathe. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy texture.

Commelina dubia is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
