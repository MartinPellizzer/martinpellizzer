Clerodendrum bungei var. megacalyx

Clerodendrum bungei var. megacalyx, commonly known as the giant calyx clerodendrum, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy bracts that can reach up to 20 cm in length, often displaying vibrant shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, surrounded by the colorful bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often used in tropical gardens and as a potted plant.
