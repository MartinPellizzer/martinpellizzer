Cleisostoma latifolium var. fuscum

Cleisostoma latifolium var. fuscum, commonly known as the dark-leaved orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, pale yellow or white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
