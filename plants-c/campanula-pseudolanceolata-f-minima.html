Campanula pseudolanceolata f. minima

Campanula pseudolanceolata f. minima, commonly known as the miniature bellflower, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features small, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white down. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its delicate appearance and easy care make it a favored addition to many gardens.
