Caulanthus hastatus

Caulanthus hastatus, commonly known as the narrowleaf silversword, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces dense clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Caulanthus hastatus is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with similar floral structures.
