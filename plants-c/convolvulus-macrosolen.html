Convolvulus macrosolen

Convolvulus macrosolen, commonly known as the large-flowered bindweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant has hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often lobed. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Convolvulus macrosolen is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
