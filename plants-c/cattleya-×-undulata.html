Cattleya × undulata

Cattleya × undulata, commonly known as the wavy cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America. The plant typically produces pendulous blooms with ruffled, wavy petals and a prominent lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya × undulata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
