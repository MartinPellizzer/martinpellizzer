Commidendrum Spurium

Commidendrum spurium is a rare and unique plant native to the arid regions of East Africa. It belongs to the sunflower family and is known for its striking, spiky appearance. This plant thrives in harsh desert conditions, showcasing remarkable adaptability to extreme environments. Its survival relies on specialized structures that help it retain moisture and withstand high temperatures. Due to its rarity and ecological importance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
