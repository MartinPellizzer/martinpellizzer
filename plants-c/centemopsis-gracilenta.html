Centemopsis gracilenta

Centemopsis gracilenta, commonly known as the slender centemopsis, is a flowering plant native to the subtropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural habitat.
