Chamaecereus silvestrii

Chamaecereus silvestrii, commonly known as the Silvestri's cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing, spineless plant that typically forms a dense, rounded clump. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fleshy stems are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
