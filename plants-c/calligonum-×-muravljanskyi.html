Calligonum × muravljanskyi

Calligonum × muravljanskyi, commonly known as the hybrid saltbush, is a shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a natural hybrid between Calligonum comosum and Calligonum trichotomum, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant is adapted to saline and alkaline soils, making it a valuable species for soil stabilization in harsh environments. It has small, gray-green leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions makes it an important plant in ecological restoration projects.
