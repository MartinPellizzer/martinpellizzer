Chironia krebsii

Chironia krebsii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact size and long-lasting blooms.

Chironia krebsii is often cultivated as a houseplant because of its adaptability to indoor conditions.
