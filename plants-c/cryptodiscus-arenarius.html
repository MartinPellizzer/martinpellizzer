Cryptodiscus arenarius

Cryptodiscus arenarius, commonly known as the sand discus, is a unique aquatic plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its distinctive disc-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, sand-like coating. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with soft, acidic substrates. Its roots anchor it to the substrate, while its floating leaves provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.

Cryptodiscus arenarius is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appearance and ability to help maintain water clarity.
