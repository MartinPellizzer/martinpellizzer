Chenopodium chenopodioides var. degenianum

Chenopodium chenopodioides var. degenianum, commonly known as sandmat, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces minute, dry fruits called utricles, which contain single seeds. It thrives in sandy or disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

This species is sometimes considered a weed but can also serve as a food source for certain wildlife.
