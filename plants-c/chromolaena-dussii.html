Chromolaena dussii

Chromolaena dussii, commonly known as flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it visually striking. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in disturbed habitats.

Chromolaena dussii is also known for its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively, sometimes becoming invasive in certain areas.
