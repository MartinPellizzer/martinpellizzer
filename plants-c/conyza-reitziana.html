Conyza reitziana

Conyza reitziana, commonly known as the Reitz's weed, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its small, white to yellowish composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and grasslands. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges.

Conyza reitziana is an annual herb that produces numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
