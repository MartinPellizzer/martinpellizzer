Cucumis melo var. leucospermus

Cucumis melo var. leucospermus, commonly known as the Persian melon, is a variety of melon native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to green rind and sweet, aromatic flesh. The plant is a herbaceous vine that produces large, round fruits with a distinctive netted pattern. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its flavorful fruit and ornamental value.
