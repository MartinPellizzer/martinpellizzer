Corydalis ambigua f. lineariloba

Corydalis ambigua f. lineariloba, commonly known as the slenderleaf fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. Native to regions of Asia, including China and the Himalayas, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. The species is often found in woodland areas and along streambanks, where it forms dense mats.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders in horticultural settings.
