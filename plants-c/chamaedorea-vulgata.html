Chamaedorea vulgata

Chamaedorea vulgata, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a compact, upright palm native to Mexico and Central America.

It features a slender trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown surface and grows to a height of about 3 to 5 feet. The plant has feathery, arching fronds that are dark green and glossy, adding a tropical aesthetic to indoor spaces. It is well-suited for low-light conditions and requires moderate watering, making it an ideal houseplant.

Chamaedorea vulgata is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in humid environments.
