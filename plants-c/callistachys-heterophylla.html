Callistachys heterophylla

Callistachys heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf hollies, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes, which vary from ovate to lanceolate, depending on the growth conditions. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruits are small, red berries that mature in late summer, providing food for native wildlife.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a range of soil types.
