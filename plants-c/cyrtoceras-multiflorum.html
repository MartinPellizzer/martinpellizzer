Cyrtoceras multiflorum

Cyrtoceras multiflorum, commonly known as the many-flowered cyrtoceras, is a flowering plant native to the Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bulbous stem and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers.
