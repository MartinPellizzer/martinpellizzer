Callista disticha

Callista disticha, commonly known as the silverleaf daphne, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which features silvery-white undersides and dark green upper surfaces. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is widely cultivated in gardens across temperate regions.
