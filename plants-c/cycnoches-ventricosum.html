Cycnoches ventricosum

Cycnoches ventricosum, commonly known as the balloon vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has a distinctive, inflated, balloon-like structure at the base of its stems, which is a unique botanical feature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
