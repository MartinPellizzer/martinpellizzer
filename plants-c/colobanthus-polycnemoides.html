Colobanthus polycnemoides

Colobanthus polycnemoides, commonly known as the Antarctic hair grass, is a flowering plant native to the cold climates of Antarctica and sub-Antarctic regions.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming species with small, narrow leaves that are adapted to withstand extreme environmental conditions. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typical of the Caryophyllaceae family.
