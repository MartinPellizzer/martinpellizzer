Combretum leonense

Combretum leonense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a valuable addition to gardens and urban landscapes.
