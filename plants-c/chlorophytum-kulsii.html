Chlorophytum kulsii

Chlorophytum kulsii, commonly known as the "Spider Plant" or "Chlorophytum kulsii," is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often curl at the tips. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum kulsii is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and air-purifying qualities.
