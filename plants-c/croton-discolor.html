Croton discolor

Croton discolor, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its vibrant appearance.

Croton discolor thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
