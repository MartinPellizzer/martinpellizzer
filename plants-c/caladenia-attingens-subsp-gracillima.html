Caladenia attingens subsp. gracillima

Caladenia attingens subsp.

gracillima, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in open habitats. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

This subspecies is known for its rarity and is often found in specific regions with suitable environmental conditions.
