Caladenia minor

Caladenia minor, commonly known as the Western Greenhood, is a small orchid native to Western Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features a single, slender stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a deep green hood, and they bloom from late winter to early spring.

Caladenia minor is a protected species due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity.
