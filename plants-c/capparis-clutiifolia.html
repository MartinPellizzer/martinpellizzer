Capparis clutiifolia

Capparis clutiifolia, commonly known as the African mountain thorn, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Capparis clutiifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
