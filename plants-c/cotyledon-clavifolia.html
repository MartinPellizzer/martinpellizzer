Cotyledon clavifolia

Cotyledon clavifolia, commonly known as the knobbed cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often adorned with distinctive knobs or tubercles. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Cotyledon clavifolia is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
