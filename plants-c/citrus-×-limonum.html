Citrus × limonum

Citrus × limonum, commonly known as lemon.

It is a hybrid citrus fruit that originated from the cross between the citron and the pomelo. The plant is characterized by its evergreen, spiny leaves and fragrant, yellow-orange fruits. The fruit's peel, or zest, contains essential oils that contribute to its strong aroma and various medicinal properties.

Citrus × limonum is widely cultivated for its fruit, which is used in culinary, cosmetic, and pharmaceutical applications.
