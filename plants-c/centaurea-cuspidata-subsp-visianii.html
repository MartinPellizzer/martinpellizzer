Centaurea cuspidata subsp. visianii

Centaurea cuspidata subsp.

visianii, commonly known as the Visianii thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features erect, spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in dense, conical heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
