Chaetanthera argentea

Chaetanthera argentea, commonly known as silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature.
