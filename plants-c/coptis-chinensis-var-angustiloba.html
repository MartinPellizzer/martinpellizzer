Coptis chinensis var. angustiloba

Coptis chinensis var. angustiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goldthread, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender, dark green leaves and yellow flowers. The plant contains berberine, a yellow alkaloid used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its medicinal value, it is also cultivated for use in herbal remedies.
