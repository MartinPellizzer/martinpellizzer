Caladium neumannii

Caladium neumannii, commonly known as the Neumann's arrowleaf, is a tropical perennial plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. Its foliage displays a variety of colors, including shades of green, red, and white, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Caladium neumannii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
