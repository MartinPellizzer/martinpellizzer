Crassula heterotricha

Crassula heterotricha, commonly known as the "Lemon Tree" or "Lemon Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Crassula heterotricha is popular as a low-maintenance houseplant due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
