Crusea tricocca

Crusea tricocca, commonly known as the three-leaf crusea, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Europe.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.
