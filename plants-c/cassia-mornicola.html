Cassia mornicola

Cassia mornicola, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, fluffy flower clusters that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with multiple small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cassia mornicola is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
