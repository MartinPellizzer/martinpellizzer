Cucumis flexuosus var. terra

Cucumis flexuosus var. terra, commonly known as the creeping cucumber, is a vigorous vining plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and produces small, yellow flowers that are typically unisexual. The fruit is a long, green, edible cucumber that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its culinary use and ornamental value in gardens.
