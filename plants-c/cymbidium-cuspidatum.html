Cymbidium cuspidatum

Cymbidium cuspidatum, commonly known as the Chinese orchid or sword orchid, is a species native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in forested areas with high humidity. The plant features long, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and cultural significance.

Cymbidium cuspidatum is often cultivated for horticultural purposes and is adapted to thrive in cool, moist environments.
