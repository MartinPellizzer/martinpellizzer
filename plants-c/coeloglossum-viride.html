Coeloglossum viride

Coeloglossum viride, commonly known as the green hawksbeard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a single, flat-topped inflorescence. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Coeloglossum viride is often found in meadows and woodland edges, thriving in well-drained soils.
