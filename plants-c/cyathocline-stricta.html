Cyathocline stricta

Cyathocline stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf cyathocline, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
