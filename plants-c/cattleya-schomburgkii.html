Cattleya schomburgkii

Cattleya schomburgkii, commonly known as the Schomburgk's Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with intricate patterns. The plant features long, arching stems and broad, leathery leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It blooms once a year, usually in the summer months, and is prized for its beauty and fragrance.

This orchid is well-suited for cultivation in greenhouses or warm climates due to its specific temperature and light requirements.
