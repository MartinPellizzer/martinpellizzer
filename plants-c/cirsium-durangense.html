Cirsium durangense

Cirsium durangense, commonly known as the Durango thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Mexico, specifically the state of Durango, where it grows in rocky and arid environments. The plant is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads, which are typical of the Cirsium genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
