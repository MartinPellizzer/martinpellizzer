Chloris berroi

Chloris berroi, commonly known as the red grass or red couch, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted leaves and distinctive reddish hue, especially during the flowering season. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Chloris berroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in some ecological restoration projects.
