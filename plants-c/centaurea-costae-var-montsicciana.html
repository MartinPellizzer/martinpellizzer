Centaurea costae var. montsicciana

Centaurea costae var. montsicciana, commonly known as the Montsiccian thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems with rough, hairy surfaces and produces striking blue to purple composite flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Asteraceae family.
