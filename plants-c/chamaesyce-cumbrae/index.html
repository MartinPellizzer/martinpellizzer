Chamaesyce Cumbrae

Chamaesyce cumbrae is a small, resilient plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. It thrives in harsh, rocky environments where few other species can survive. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, helping to reflect sunlight and retain moisture. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing food and shelter for local wildlife. This unique plant is a testament to nature's ability to adapt and flourish in extreme conditions.
