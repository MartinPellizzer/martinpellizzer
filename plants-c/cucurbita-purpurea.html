Cucurbita purpurea

Cucurbita purpurea, commonly known as the ornamental gourd, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often multicolored fruits that range from orange to deep purple. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is primarily cultivated for its decorative value rather than for consumption.

Cucurbita purpurea thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental plants.
