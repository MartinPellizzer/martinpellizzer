Cyperus gracilinux

Cyperus gracilinux, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshes, pond edges, and along streams.

Cyperus gracilinux is an important component of wetland ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
