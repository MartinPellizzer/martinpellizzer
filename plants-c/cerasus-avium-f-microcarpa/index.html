Cerasus Avium F. Microcarpa

Cerasus avium f. microcarpa is a unique cultivar of the sweet cherry tree known for its compact size and ornamental value. This form exhibits smaller fruit compared to standard Cerasus avium, making it a popular choice for smaller gardens and containers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth. The plant is valued for its early spring blossoms and manageable growth habit. Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts.
