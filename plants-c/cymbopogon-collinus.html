Cymbopogon collinus

Cymbopogon collinus, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and tall, slender inflorescences. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments. Its fine texture and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Cymbopogon collinus is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
