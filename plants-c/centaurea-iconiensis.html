Centaurea iconiensis

Centaurea iconiensis, commonly known as the Iconia thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, glandular leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often in mountainous areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
