Calligonum × densum

Calligonum × densum, commonly known as the dense sandmat, is a hybrid shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its low, sprawling growth habit and dense clusters of small, silver-gray leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It is well adapted to harsh environments, tolerating drought, salt, and poor soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for stabilizing sandy soils and for use in xeriscaping.
