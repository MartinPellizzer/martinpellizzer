Callianthemum rutifolium

Callianthemum rutifolium, commonly known as the woolly yellow saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, white to grayish down, which helps to retain moisture and protect against harsh weather.

Callianthemum rutifolium is a perennial species that is often found in meadows and scree slopes at high elevations.
