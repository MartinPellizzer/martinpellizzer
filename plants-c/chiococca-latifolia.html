Chiococca latifolia

Chiococca latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf chiococca, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.
