Crotalaria cylindrica subsp. cylindrica

Crotalaria cylindrica subsp.

cylindrica, commonly known as the slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and parts of Asia. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds.

The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are trifoliate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in certain agricultural contexts.
