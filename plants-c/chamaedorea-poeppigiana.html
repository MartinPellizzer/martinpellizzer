Chamaedorea poeppigiana

Chamaedorea poeppigiana, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen or poeppigiana palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central America and southern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and pinnately compound leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Chamaedorea poeppigiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
