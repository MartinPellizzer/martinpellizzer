Croton antanosiensis var. ambohibyi

Croton antanosiensis var. ambohibyi, commonly known as the Madagascar croton, is a shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and sometimes toxic species. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
