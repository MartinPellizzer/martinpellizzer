Chamaefistula fruticosa

Chamaefistula fruticosa, commonly known as the Mediterranean buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark, shiny berries. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its hardy nature makes it a resilient plant in arid environments.
