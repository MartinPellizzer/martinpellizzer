Carum rigidum

Carum rigidum, commonly known as rigid caraway, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like parsley and dill. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, and its leaves are typically narrow and aromatic. Carum rigidum is often found in dry, rocky soils and can thrive in sunny to partially shaded environments.

Its seeds are used in some traditional herbal remedies and for their mild aromatic properties.
