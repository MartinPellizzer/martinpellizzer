Coelogyne foxworthyi

Coelogyne foxworthyi, commonly known as the Foxworth's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

Coelogyne foxworthyi is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
