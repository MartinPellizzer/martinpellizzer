Cleome graveolens

Cleome graveolens, commonly known as spider flower, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its branched stems and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Cleome graveolens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
