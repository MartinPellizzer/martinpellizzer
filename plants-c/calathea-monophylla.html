Calathea monophylla

Calathea monophylla, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, dark green leaves with intricate patterns that change color depending on light conditions. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea monophylla is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
