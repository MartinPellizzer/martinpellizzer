Caustis flexuosa var. rectiramulosa

Caustis flexuosa var. rectiramulosa, commonly known as the slender flame bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems that form a dense, tangled growth habit. The leaves are narrow, linear, and often slightly hairy, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
