Canna altensteinii

Canna altensteinii, commonly known as the dwarf canna, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, broad leaves that are often a deep green, with a waxy texture. Its showy flowers are usually orange-red or yellow, with three narrow petals and prominent stamens.

Canna altensteinii is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and striking floral display.
