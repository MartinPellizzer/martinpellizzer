Carex praecox f. gracillima

Carex praecox f. gracillima, commonly known as the slender early sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green to brown in color. This plant thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. It is often found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, including parts of Europe and North America.

The plant's rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
