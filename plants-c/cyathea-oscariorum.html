Cyathea oscariorum

Cyathea oscariorum, commonly known as the Oscar's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its ornamental value in botanical gardens.

Cyathea oscariorum plays a significant role in the ecosystems where it is found, providing habitat and contributing to forest biodiversity.
