Cynanchum crassiantherae

Cynanchum crassiantherae, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruits are follicles containing numerous seeds.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
