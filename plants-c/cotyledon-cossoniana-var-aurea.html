Cotyledon cossoniana var. aurea

Cotyledon cossoniana var. aurea, commonly known as the golden cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with prominent yellow or orange markings. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
