Croton confertus

Croton confertus, commonly known as the dense-leaved croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for container gardening or as a houseplant.

Croton confertus thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
