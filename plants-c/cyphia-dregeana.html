Cyphia dregeana

Cyphia dregeana, commonly known as the velvetleaf cyphia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences, typically in shades of yellow or orange.

Cyphia dregeana is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
