Clitoria froesii

Clitoria froesii, commonly known as the purple crown flower, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features showy, butterfly-like flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
