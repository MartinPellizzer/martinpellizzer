Chlorospatha maculata

Chlorospatha maculata, commonly known as the spotted green fern, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It features delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow spots. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

Chlorospatha maculata is also valued for its ability to purify the air and thrive in indoor settings.
