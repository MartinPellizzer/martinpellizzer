Celosia rogersii

Celosia rogersii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, sunny climates. Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Celosia rogersii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
