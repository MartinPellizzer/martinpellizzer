Cereus speciosissimus var. suwaroffi

Cereus speciosissimus var. suwaroffi, commonly known as the Suwaroff's Christmas Cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a flowering cactus characterized by its thick, segmented stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant has spiny areoles and produces yellow or orange flowers with red filaments. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
