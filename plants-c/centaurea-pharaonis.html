Centaurea pharaonis

Centaurea pharaonis, commonly known as the Pharaoh's flower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea pharaonis is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and abandoned fields.
