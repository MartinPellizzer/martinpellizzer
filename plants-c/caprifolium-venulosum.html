Caprifolium venulosum

Caprifolium venulosum, commonly known as the slender honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Asia and the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slight pubescence. The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
