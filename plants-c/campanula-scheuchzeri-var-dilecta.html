Campanula scheuchzeri var. dilecta

Campanula scheuchzeri var. dilecta, commonly known as the alpine bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often seen in mountainous habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
