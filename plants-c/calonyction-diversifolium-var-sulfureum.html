Calonyction diversifolium var. sulfureum

Calonyction diversifolium var. sulfureum, commonly known as the sulfurleaf plant, is a striking member of the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange leaves, which are often covered in a waxy coating that gives them a metallic sheen. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its foliage is not only visually appealing but also contains high levels of carotenoids, contributing to its unique coloration.
