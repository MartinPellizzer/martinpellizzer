Cycas dolichophylla

Cycas dolichophylla, commonly known as the long-leaf cycad, is a unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. This plant features a stout, unbranched trunk that can reach several meters in height, with a crown of large, feather-like leaves. Its foliage is characterized by long, pinnate leaves that are often blue-green in color.

Cycas dolichophylla is known for its slow growth and long lifespan, making it a striking and ancient-looking plant in both natural and cultivated settings.
