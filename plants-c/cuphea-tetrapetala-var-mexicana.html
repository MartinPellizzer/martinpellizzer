Cuphea tetrapetala var. mexicana

Cuphea tetrapetala var. mexicana, commonly known as Mexican flame flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround small, tubular flowers, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant belongs to the Lythraceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding contrast to its showy blooms.
