Cyrtandra × nuda

Cyrtandra × nuda, commonly known as the naked cyrtandra, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Pacific, including Hawaii and parts of Southeast Asia. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
