Crataegus Monogyna Var. Filicifolia

Crataegus monogyna var. filicifolia is a striking variant of the common hawthorn known for its unique foliage. This plant features finely cut, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, almost ethereal appearance. While it shares the hardy nature of its parent species, this variety stands out with its ornamental value in gardens and natural landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for horticultural use. Its beauty and resilience make it a notable addition to both wild and cultivated environments.
