Carex kanaii

Carex kanaii, commonly known as Kanai's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. This plant typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, stream banks, and forest understories. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers.

Carex kanaii is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
