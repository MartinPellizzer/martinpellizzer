Clematis mairei

Clematis mairei, commonly known as the Maire's Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, round berries that are edible but not particularly flavorful.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for gardeners looking to add ornamental value to their landscapes.
