Cinnamomum lucienii

Cinnamomum lucienii, commonly known as the Laos cinnamon or wild cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for flavoring.

Cinnamomum lucienii is valued for its essential oils and is often cultivated for its aromatic properties.
