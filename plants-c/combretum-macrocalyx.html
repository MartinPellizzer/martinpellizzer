Combretum macrocalyx

Combretum macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-fruited flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Combretum macrocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
