Chirita minutihamata

Chirita minutihamata, commonly known as the small-leaved chirita, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, heart-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a houseplant in some regions.
