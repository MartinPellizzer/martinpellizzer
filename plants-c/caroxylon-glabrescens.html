Caroxylon glabrescens

Caroxylon glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth carob tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caesalpiniaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces long, pod-like fruits that contain sweet, edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, temperate climates.

Caroxylon glabrescens is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural gum.
