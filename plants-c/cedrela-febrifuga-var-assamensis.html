Cedrela febrifuga var. assamensis

Cedrela febrifuga var. assamensis, commonly known as Assam cedar, is a deciduous tree native to the eastern Himalayas and northeastern India.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, characterized by its aromatic wood and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by winged fruits, which are dispersed by wind.
