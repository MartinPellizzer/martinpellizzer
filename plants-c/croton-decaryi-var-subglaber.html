Croton decaryi var. subglaber

Croton decaryi var. subglaber, commonly known as the smooth-leaved croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are smooth and often glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
