Crassula schimperi f. abbreviata

Crassula schimperi f. abbreviata, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a compact rosette, giving it a dense, bushy appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental appeal make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
