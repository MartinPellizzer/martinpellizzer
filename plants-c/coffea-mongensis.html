Coffea mongensis

Coffea mongensis, commonly known as the western bean or the African coffee.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family, native to western Africa. The plant produces small, red berries containing the seeds used to make coffee. It is often cultivated for its beans, which have a mild flavor compared to other coffee varieties.
