Comparettia Langlassei

Comparettia langlassei is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of Colombia. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is known for its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant is particularly sensitive to environmental changes, making it a valuable indicator of ecosystem health. Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it faces significant conservation challenges. Despite its beauty and ecological importance, little is known about its biological characteristics and reproductive habits.
