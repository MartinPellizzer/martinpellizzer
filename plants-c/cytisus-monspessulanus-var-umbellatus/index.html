Cytisus Monspessulanus Var. Umbellatus

Cytisus monspessulanus var. umbellatus is a strikingly beautiful flowering shrub known for its vibrant yellow blooms. Native to southern Europe, this plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its unique feature is the umbellate arrangement of its flowers, which gives it a distinctive and eye-catching appearance. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly used in gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its health and longevity.
