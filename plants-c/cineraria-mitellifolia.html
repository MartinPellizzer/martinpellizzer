Cineraria mitellifolia

Cineraria mitellifolia, commonly known as the Canary Island cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. Cineraria mitellifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
