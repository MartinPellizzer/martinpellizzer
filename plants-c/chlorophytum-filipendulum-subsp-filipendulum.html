Chlorophytum filipendulum subsp. filipendulum

Chlorophytum filipendulum subsp.

filipendulum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa. It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ability to purify indoor air and its low maintenance requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation.
