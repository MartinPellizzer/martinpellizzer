Cistus plantaginifolius

Cistus plantaginifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
