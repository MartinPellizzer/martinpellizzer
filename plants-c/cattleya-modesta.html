Cattleya modesta

Cattleya modesta, commonly known as the modest cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact size and produces fragrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually dark green and broad, arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Cattleya modesta is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
