Camellia phanii

Camellia phanii, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, single or semi-double flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Camellia phanii thrives in partially shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
