Crotalaria deflexa

Crotalaria deflexa, commonly known as the drooping cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It has a hairy stem and produces pods that contain several seeds.

Crotalaria deflexa is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
