Clematis × cartmanii

Clematis × cartmanii, commonly known as the Cartmanii Clematis, is a hybrid plant that showcases a blend of characteristics from its parent species.

It is a vigorous climbing vine with large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The foliage is often a deep green, and the plant can reach lengths of several meters, making it suitable for trellises or fences. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, thriving in temperate climates.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
