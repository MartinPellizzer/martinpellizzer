Chisocheton setosus

Chisocheton setosus, commonly known as the hairy chisocheton, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges and small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, tropical climates.
