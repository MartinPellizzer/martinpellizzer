Cuphea brownei

Cuphea brownei, commonly known as the brown-eyed cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a shrub-like species with showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Cuphea brownei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds.
