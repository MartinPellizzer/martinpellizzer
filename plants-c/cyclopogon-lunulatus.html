Cyclopogon lunulatus

Cyclopogon lunulatus, commonly known as the moon-leaf cyclopogon, is a unique member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that often display a waxy, silver-blue sheen. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of white and pink.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and specialized species in botanical studies.
