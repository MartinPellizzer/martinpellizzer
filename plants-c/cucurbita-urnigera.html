Cucurbita urnigera

Cucurbita urnigera, commonly known as the bottle gourd, is a vine species native to the Americas.

It produces large, cylindrical fruits that are typically green when mature, with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucumbers.

The fruit is used in various culinary and traditional applications across different cultures.
