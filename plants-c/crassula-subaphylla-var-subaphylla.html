Crassula subaphylla var. subaphylla

Crassula subaphylla var. subaphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, dense rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Due to its hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
