Crepis newii subsp. newii

Crepis newii subsp.

newii, commonly known as the Newii hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its basal rosette of linear to lanceolate leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or sandy soils. It produces small, achene fruits that are dispersed by wind.

This subspecies is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
