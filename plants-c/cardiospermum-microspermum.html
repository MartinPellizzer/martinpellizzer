Cardiospermum microspermum

Cardiospermum microspermum, commonly known as the small-seeded heartseed.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant produces distinctive heart-shaped fruits that enclose small, hard seeds. Its leaves are typically palmately lobed and can vary in size depending on environmental conditions.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
