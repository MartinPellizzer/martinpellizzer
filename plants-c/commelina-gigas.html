Commelina gigas

Commelina gigas, commonly known as the giant spiderwort, is a striking herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a white or yellow central stripe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Commelina gigas thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates.
