Coelogyne mossiae

Coelogyne mossiae, commonly known as the Moss's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive pink and white coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Coelogyne mossiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
