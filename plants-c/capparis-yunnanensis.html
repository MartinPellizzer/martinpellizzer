Capparis yunnanensis

Capparis yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan caper, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered together. Its leaves are typically ovate and have a rough texture.

Capparis yunnanensis is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
