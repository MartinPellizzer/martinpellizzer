Cynoctonum ovalifolium

Cynoctonum ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaf cynoctonum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within rainforests. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by nocturnal insects such as moths.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
