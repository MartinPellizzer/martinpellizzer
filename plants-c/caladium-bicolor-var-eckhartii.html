Caladium bicolor var. eckhartii

Caladium bicolor var. eckhartii, commonly known as the Eckhartii caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor displays.

It is typically grown as an annual in temperate regions due to its sensitivity to cold temperatures.
