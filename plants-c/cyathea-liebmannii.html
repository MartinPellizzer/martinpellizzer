Cyathea liebmannii

Cyathea liebmannii, commonly known as the Liebmann's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and mountainous areas. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth form.

Cyathea liebmannii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
