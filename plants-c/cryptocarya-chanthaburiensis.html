Cryptocarya chanthaburiensis

Cryptocarya chanthaburiensis, commonly known as the Chanthaburi cryptocarya, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern Thailand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a dark purple drupe, containing a single seed.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often found in humid, shaded forest environments.
