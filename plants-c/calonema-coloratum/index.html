Calonema Coloratum

Calonema coloratum is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its vibrant, colorful foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This evergreen shrub is prized for its ornamental value and easy care requirements. Its leaves display a range of hues from deep greens to rich reds and purples. Whether grown indoors or outdoors, Calonema coloratum adds a touch of elegance to any space.
