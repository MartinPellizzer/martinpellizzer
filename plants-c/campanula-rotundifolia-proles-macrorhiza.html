Campanula rotundifolia proles macrorhiza

Campanula rotundifolia proles macrorhiza, commonly known as the large-rooted bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has rounded, hairy leaves and a robust root system that contributes to its hardiness. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
