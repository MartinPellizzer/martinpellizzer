Coelogyne convallariiformis var. convallariiformis

Coelogyne convallariiformis var. convallariiformis, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and the leaves are elongated with a waxy surface.

The flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators, making it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
