Ceropegia tessellata

Ceropegia tessellata, commonly known as the laceleaf string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that have a distinctive waxy, textured surface. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
