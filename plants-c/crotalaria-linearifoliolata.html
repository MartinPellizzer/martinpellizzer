Crotalaria linearifoliolata

Crotalaria linearifoliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf velvet bean, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein.

Its growth habit is typically prostrate or ascending, making it adaptable to various soil types and climatic conditions.
