Corycium dracomontanum

Corycium dracomontanum, commonly known as the dragon's blood orchid, is a unique epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, blood-red flowers that resemble drops of dried blood, hence its common name. The plant has a slender, wiry stem and produces clusters of small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of light for optimal blooming.

Its distinctive appearance and rarity make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
