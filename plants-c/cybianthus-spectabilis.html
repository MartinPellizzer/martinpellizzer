Cybianthus spectabilis

Cybianthus spectabilis, commonly known as the showy milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Cybianthus spectabilis plays an important role in supporting local pollinators, particularly butterflies and bees.
