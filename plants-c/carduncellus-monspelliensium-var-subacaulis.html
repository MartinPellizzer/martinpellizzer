Carduncellus monspelliensium var. subacaulis

Carduncellus monspelliensium var. subacaulis, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems and pale green, lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, spherical clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
