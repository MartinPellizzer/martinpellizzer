Cassia fasciculata var. ferrisiae

Cassia fasciculata var. ferrisiae, commonly known as golden shower tree or *Cassia* species, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pendant flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant produces elongated, narrow leaves that are often trifoliate, with three leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The seeds are encased in long, slender pods that mature to a reddish-brown color.
