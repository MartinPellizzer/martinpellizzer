Coleus morletii

Coleus morletii, commonly known as the golden flame coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, featuring shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically ovate to heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its striking appearance.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
