Chamaedorea holmgrenii

Chamaedorea holmgrenii, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a compact, upright palm native to Mexico.

It features a slender trunk and is characterized by its glossy, dark green, pinnately compound leaves. This plant thrives in shady to partially sunny conditions and prefers well-draining, moist soil. It is often used as a decorative indoor plant due to its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Chamaedorea holmgrenii is also valued for its ability to improve indoor air quality.
