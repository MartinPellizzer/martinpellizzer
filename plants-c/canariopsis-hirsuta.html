Canariopsis hirsuta

Canariopsis hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy canariopsis, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in coastal areas.
