Cineraria vulneraria

Cineraria vulneraria, commonly known as the wounded cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves that are adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat. It produces small, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cineraria vulneraria is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
