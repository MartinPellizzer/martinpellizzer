Carex shuchengensis

Carex shuchengensis, commonly known as the Shucheng sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested or wetland habitats. Its foliage is grass-like, with narrow, linear leaves that are often dark green in color.

The flowering structure consists of spikelets, which produce small, wind-pollinated flowers.
