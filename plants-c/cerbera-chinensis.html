Cerbera chinensis

Cerbera chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese cerbera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to about 1-2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its fruit is a bright orange, fleshy capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The leaves are glossy and elliptical, with a strong, somewhat aromatic scent.
