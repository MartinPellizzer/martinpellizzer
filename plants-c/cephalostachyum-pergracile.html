Cephalostachyum pergracile

Cephalostachyum pergracile, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a low-growing, perennial plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescences. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant's flowering spikes are typically adorned with small, spikelet-like flowers, which are often pale green to brown in color.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
