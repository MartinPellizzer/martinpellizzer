Cuphea lutescens

Cuphea lutescens, commonly known as the yellow cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces vibrant yellow to orange bract-like flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique floral structure and bright color make it an attractive addition to ornamental plantings.
