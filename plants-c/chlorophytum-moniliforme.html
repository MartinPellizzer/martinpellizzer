Chlorophytum moniliforme

Chlorophytum moniliforme, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that form a rosette shape, and it produces small white or greenish flowers on slender stalks. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and its tolerance for different soil types. It is also noted for its ease of care and ability to propagate through offsets, making it a popular houseplant.

Its botanical features make it a versatile and aesthetically pleasing addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
