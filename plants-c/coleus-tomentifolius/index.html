Coleus Tomentifolius

Coleus tomentifolius is a striking plant known for its vibrant, colorful foliage. It belongs to the mint family and is native to tropical regions of Asia. This plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. Its fuzzy, velvety leaves come in shades of red, purple, and green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is often used in landscaping and indoor gardening.
