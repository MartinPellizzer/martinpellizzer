Caladium bovei

Caladium bovei, commonly known as the flameleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like calla lilies. Caladium bovei thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
