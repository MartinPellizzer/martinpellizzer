Clematis dasyandra

Clematis dasyandra, commonly known as the waxy leaf clematis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has compound leaves with waxy, elliptical leaflets that give it its common name. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often climbing or twining around supports.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.
