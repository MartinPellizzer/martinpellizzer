Crassula werneri

Crassula werneri, commonly known as the Werneri Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and attractive appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
