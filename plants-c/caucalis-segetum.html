Caucalis segetum

Caucalis segetum, commonly known as field scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Caprifoliaceae and is characterized by its clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. Caucalis segetum is an annual or biennial plant that produces seeds in its second year, contributing to its widespread distribution.
