Cyclamen × wellensiekii

Cyclamen × wellensiekii, commonly known as the wellensiekii cyclamen, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Cyclamen persicum and Cyclamen coum.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and showy, bowl-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East and is often cultivated as a decorative plant for indoor and outdoor use. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Cyclamen × wellensiekii is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper conditions.
