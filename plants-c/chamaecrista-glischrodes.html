Chamaecrista glischrodes

Chamaecrista glischrodes, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

Chamaecrista glischrodes is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
