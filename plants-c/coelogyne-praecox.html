Coelogyne praecox

Coelogyne praecox, commonly known as the early blooming orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its long, slender rhizomes and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive golden lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne praecox is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
