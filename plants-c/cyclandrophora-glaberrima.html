Cyclandrophora glaberrima

Cyclandrophora glaberrima, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Cyclandrophora glaberrima is known for its ability to bloom year-round under optimal conditions.
