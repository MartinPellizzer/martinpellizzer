Coelogyne irawatiana

Coelogyne irawatiana, commonly known as the Irawatiana orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting orchid flowers that are typically yellow with red and purple markings. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, contributing to its unique appearance.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and fragrance.
