Cynoglossum uncinatum

Cynoglossum uncinatum, commonly known as hooked tongue or purple heart, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue and prominent veins. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum uncinatum is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ease of cultivation.
