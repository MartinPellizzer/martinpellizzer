Coeloglossum peristyloides

Coeloglossum peristyloides, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, typically arranged in a rosette pattern around the base.

Coeloglossum peristyloides thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
