Croton willdenowii

Croton willdenowii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red or yellow. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
