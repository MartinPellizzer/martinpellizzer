Chrysanthemum macrotum

Chrysanthemum macrotum, commonly known as the giant chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with sturdy stems and broad, lobed leaves. Its flowers consist of both ray and disc florets, which contribute to its ornamental value.

Chrysanthemum macrotum is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
