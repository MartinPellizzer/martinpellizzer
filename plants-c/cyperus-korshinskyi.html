Cyperus korshinskyi

Cyperus korshinskyi, commonly known as the umbrella sedge, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and distinctive umbrella-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist or wet environments, such as marshes and along water edges. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread readily in suitable habitats.

Cyperus korshinskyi is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
