Cochlearia lichuanensis

Cochlearia lichuanensis, commonly known as the Lichuan horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with distinctive cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow with four petals, and it produces small, round pods that contain seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
