Campanula Velutina Var. Malacitana

Campanula velutina var. malacitana is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family known for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Spain, this plant thrives in rocky and limestone soils. Its velvety leaves and lavender-blue blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine collections. The variety malacitana is particularly noted for its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display. It requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish, making it a rewarding plant for gardeners with the right conditions.
