Croton mearnsii

Croton mearnsii, commonly known as the Mearns' croton, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from yellow to red and orange hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Croton mearnsii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
