Cochemiea angelensis

Cochemiea angelensis, commonly known as the Angel's Cochemiea, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water.

Its unique appearance and compact size make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
