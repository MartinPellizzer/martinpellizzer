Cotoneaster granatensis

Cotoneaster granatensis, commonly known as the Granada cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Its leaves are ovate and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cotoneaster granatensis is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and seasonal color display.
