Cleisostoma complicatum

Cleisostoma complicatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Cleisostoma complicatum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
