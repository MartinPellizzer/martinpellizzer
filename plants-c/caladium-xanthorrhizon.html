Caladium xanthorrhizon

Caladium xanthorrhizon, commonly known as the flameleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is typically grown as an ornamental foliage plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial to full shade.

Its vibrant leaves make it a popular choice for garden displays and container planting.
