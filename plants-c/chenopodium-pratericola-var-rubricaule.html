Chenopodium pratericola var. rubricaule

Chenopodium pratericola var. rubricaule, commonly known as the red-stemmed goosefoot, is a herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, hairy stems and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered greenish flowers that are typically wind-pollinated. Its reddish stems and bracts are notable features, giving it its common name.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
