Callitris sinensis

Callitris sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese white cypress, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Cupressaceae and is characterized by its slender, conical shape and scale-like leaves. The tree produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
