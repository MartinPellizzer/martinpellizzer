Carduus discolor

Carduus discolor, commonly known as the pale cardinal.

It is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its rough, hairy stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
