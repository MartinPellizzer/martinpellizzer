Calluna erica proles beleziae

Calluna erica proles beleziae, commonly known as the Belezia heather, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color, blooming in late summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and woodland environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
