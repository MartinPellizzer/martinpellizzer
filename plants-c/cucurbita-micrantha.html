Cucurbita micrantha

Cucurbita micrantha, commonly known as the little gourd or the Mexican gourd, is a species of flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and Central America, where it thrives in warm, tropical climates. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color, often with a rough, textured surface. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, and it bears yellow flowers that are both showy and fragrant.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruits.
