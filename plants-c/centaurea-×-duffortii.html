Centaurea × duffortii

Centaurea × duffortii, commonly known as the Duffort's knapweed, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flower heads and spiny bracts. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has lance-shaped leaves with rough textures and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
