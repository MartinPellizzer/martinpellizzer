Ceropegia Schoenlandiana

Ceropegia schoenlandiana is a fascinating flowering plant native to southern Africa. Known for its unique waxy leaves and delicate, tubular flowers, it captivates plant enthusiasts with its striking appearance. This species is often grown as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its ability to bloom indoors makes it a popular choice for those seeking a touch of nature in their living spaces.
