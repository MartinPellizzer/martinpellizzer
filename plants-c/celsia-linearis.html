Celsia linearis

Celsia linearis, commonly known as the slender celsia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The species is relatively hardy and can adapt to a variety of growing conditions, making it a versatile choice for horticultural use.
