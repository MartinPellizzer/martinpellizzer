Ceratostylis pleurothallis

Ceratostylis pleurothallis, commonly known as the pleurothallis orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its inflorescence is usually a raceme, and the flowers are characterized by their intricate, spurred petals.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance and fragrance.
