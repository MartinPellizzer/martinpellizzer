Callitris calcarata

Callitris calcarata, commonly known as the smooth bark cypress, is a shrub or small tree native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its fibrous, smooth bark that often peels in thin strips. The plant has scale-like leaves that are densely packed along the branches, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of coniferous plants.

Callitris calcarata is well adapted to dry conditions, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
