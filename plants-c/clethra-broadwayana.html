Clethra broadwayana

Clethra broadwayana, commonly known as the broadwayana clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding seasonal interest. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Clethra broadwayana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
