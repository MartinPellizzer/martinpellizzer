Clausena crenulata

Clausena crenulata, commonly known as the sour orange or wild lime, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits that are highly acidic and used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
