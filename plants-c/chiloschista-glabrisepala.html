Chiloschista glabrisepala

Chiloschista glabrisepala, commonly known as the smooth-sepal chiloschista, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and rhizomatous structure. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth surface and a firm texture.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
