Cyathea taiwaniana

Cyathea taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate fronds that can reach up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in subtropical and tropical forests.

Cyathea taiwaniana plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
