Cereus pentlandii var. miniatoroseus

Cereus pentlandii var. miniatoroseus, commonly known as the miniature rose cactus, is a compact, spiny cactus native to South America.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with a pink or rose-colored hue, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, night-blooming flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its unique coloration and compact size, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
