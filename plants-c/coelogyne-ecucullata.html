Coelogyne ecucullata

Coelogyne ecucullata, commonly known as the white-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne ecucullata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
