Coelogyne porrecta

Coelogyne porrecta, commonly known as the straight orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne porrecta is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
