Chamaecyparis lawsoniana filifera

Chamaecyparis lawsoniana filifera, commonly known as the Lawson's cypress, is a variant of the Lawson cypress, characterized by its unique foliage.

It features scale-like leaves that are densely packed, giving the plant a soft, feathery appearance. The plant is evergreen, with a conical shape that becomes more rounded with age. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

This cultivar is prized for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden environments.
