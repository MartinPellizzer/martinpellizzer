Calanthe burmanica

Calanthe burmanica, commonly known as Burmese calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense clumps over time.

Calanthe burmanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
