Calceolaria tracheliifolia

Calceolaria tracheliifolia, commonly known as the "butterfly flower," is a member of the Campanulaceae family and is native to South America.

Calceolaria tracheliifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
