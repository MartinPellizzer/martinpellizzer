Cactus miquelii

Cactus miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. Its spines are short and cylindrical, providing protection from herbivores.

Cactus miquelii is well adapted to desert environments, thriving in low rainfall and high temperatures.
