Ceratanthus ocimoides

Ceratanthus ocimoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ceratanthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It features slender, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring.
