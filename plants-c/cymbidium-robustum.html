Cymbidium robustum

Cymbidium robustum, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia and the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial orchid that typically grows in forested areas with high humidity. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces tall, branching inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Cymbidium robustum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
