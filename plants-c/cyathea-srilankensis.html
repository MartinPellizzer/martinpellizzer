Cyathea srilankensis

Cyathea srilankensis, commonly known as the Srilankan tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its fronds are typically dark green and can be quite broad, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cyathea srilankensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
