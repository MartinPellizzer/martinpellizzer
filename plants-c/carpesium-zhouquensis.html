Carpesium zhouquensis

Carpesium zhouquensis, commonly known as the Zhouqu goldenweed, is a perennial herb native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its flowering period occurs in the summer months.
