Cheiranthus × muralis

Cheiranthus × muralis, commonly known as wallflower, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils and is often found in urban environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Cheiranthus × muralis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
