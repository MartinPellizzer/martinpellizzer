Cyperus iria f. diaphaniria

Cyperus iria f. diaphaniria, commonly known as the translucent flatsedge, is a variant of the flatsedge plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a tendency to form dense mats in wet or moist soils.

This variety is notable for its translucent, waxy leaf surfaces, which give it its distinguishing appearance.
