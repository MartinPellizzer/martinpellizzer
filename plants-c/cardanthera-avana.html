Cardanthera avana

Cardanthera avana, commonly known as the desert star.

It is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are small, narrow, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs to reduce water loss.

The species is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
