Centaurea zangezuri

Centaurea zangezuri, commonly known as the Zangezur centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spiky bracts around its flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
