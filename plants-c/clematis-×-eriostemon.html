Clematis × eriostemon

Clematis × eriostemon, commonly known as the Japanese clematis, is a hybrid vine native to East Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis × eriostemon is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
