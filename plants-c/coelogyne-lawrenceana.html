Coelogyne lawrenceana

Coelogyne lawrenceana, commonly known as the Lawrence's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically dark green and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Coelogyne lawrenceana is often cultivated for its beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
