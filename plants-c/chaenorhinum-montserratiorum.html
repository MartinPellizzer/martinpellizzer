Chaenorhinum montserratiorum

Chaenorhinum montserratiorum, commonly known as the Montserrat nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually violet or purple in color. It has simple, alternate leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
