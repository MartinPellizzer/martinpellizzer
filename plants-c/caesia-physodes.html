Caesia physodes

Caesia physodes, commonly known as the pink sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the coastline. The plant produces small, pink to lavender-colored flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine, silky hair.

Caesia physodes is adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds, making it a hardy and resilient species.
