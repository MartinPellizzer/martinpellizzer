Cyrtochilum stenochilum

Cyrtochilum stenochilum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped cyclochilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow to brownish flowers with narrow, lip-like petals. Cyrtochilum stenochilum thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
