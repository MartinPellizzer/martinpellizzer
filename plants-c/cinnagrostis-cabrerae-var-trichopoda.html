Cinnagrostis cabrerae var. trichopoda

Cinnagrostis cabrerae var. trichopoda, commonly known as the hairy leaf cinnagrostis, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture and appearance.

It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
