Cyathea obscura

Cyathea obscura, commonly known as the dark fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and can grow up to several meters in height. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its striking appearance.

Cyathea obscura plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
