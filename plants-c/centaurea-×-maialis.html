Centaurea × maialis

Centaurea × maialis, commonly known as the hybrid gladiolus, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright-colored flowers that resemble those of gladiolus. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually borne in dense clusters at the top of the plant, with vibrant hues of red, orange, and yellow.

Centaurea × maialis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
