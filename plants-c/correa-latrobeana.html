Correa latrobeana

Correa latrobeana, commonly known as the pink correa, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in shrubland and woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a lush backdrop to the vibrant floral display.

Correa latrobeana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
