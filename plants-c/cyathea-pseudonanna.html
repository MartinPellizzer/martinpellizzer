Cyathea pseudonanna

Cyathea pseudonanna, commonly known as the dwarf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect trunk and a crown of pinnate fronds. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are often dark green and can reach lengths of up to 1.5 meters.

Cyathea pseudonanna is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
