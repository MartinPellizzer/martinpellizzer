Catutsjeron

Catutsjeron, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant orange and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dazzling display during the warmer months. The plant has slender, erect stems that support its showy inflorescences, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
