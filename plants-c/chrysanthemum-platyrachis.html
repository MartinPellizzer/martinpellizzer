Chrysanthemum platyrachis

Chrysanthemum platyrachis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and finely divided, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and seasonal flowering.
