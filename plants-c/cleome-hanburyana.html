Cleome hanburyana

Cleome hanburyana, commonly known as the Chinese spider flower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its tall, branching stems and showy, often pink or white flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Cleome hanburyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
