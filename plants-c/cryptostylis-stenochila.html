Cryptostylis stenochila

Cryptostylis stenochila, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often hairy foliage and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Cryptostylis stenochila is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
