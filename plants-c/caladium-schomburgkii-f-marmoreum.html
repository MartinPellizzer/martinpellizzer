Caladium schomburgkii f. marmoreum

Caladium schomburgkii f. marmoreum, commonly known as the Marble Queen caladium, is a striking foliage plant native to South America.

It is a hybrid of Caladium schomburgkii and Caladium marmoratum, prized for its vibrant, mottled leaves that display shades of white, pink, and green. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to maintain its lush, colorful appearance.
