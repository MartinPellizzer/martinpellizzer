Calathea oscariana

Calathea oscariana, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often display intricate patterns in shades of green, white, and pink. The plant's foliage is known to fold shut at night, a behavior that gives it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Calathea oscariana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
