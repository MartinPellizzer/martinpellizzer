Camellia caudata var. caudata

Camellia caudata var. caudata, commonly known as the long-tailed camellia, is a species native to China and is characterized by its distinctive, elongated petals.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments. Its foliage consists of dark green, leathery leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Camellia caudata var. caudata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
