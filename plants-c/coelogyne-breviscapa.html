Coelogyne breviscapa

Coelogyne breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, often sympodial growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant typically features a short, stocky rhizome with a few pseudobulbs, and its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests.

Coelogyne breviscapa is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its beautiful, long-lasting blooms.
