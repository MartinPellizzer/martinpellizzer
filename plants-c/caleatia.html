Caleatia

Caleatia, commonly known as the flaming sword plant, is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence.

It features a tall, leafy stem with a spathe that resembles a sword, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant's spadix, which is the actual flower cluster, is surrounded by the colorful spathe. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments.

Caleatia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant and dramatic appearance.
