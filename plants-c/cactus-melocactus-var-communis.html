Cactus melocactus var. communis

Cactus melocactus var. communis, commonly known as the Mexican hat cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive crown of spiny, globular stems that resemble a hat. The plant features dense, white to pale green spines and produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to survive in hot, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for xeriscaping.
