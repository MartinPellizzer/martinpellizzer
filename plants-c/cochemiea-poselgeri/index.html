Cochemiea Poselgeri

Cochemiea poselgeri is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. It is known for its distinctive blue-green color and unique, spiny appearance. This plant thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping. Its resilience to drought and heat makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its small size, Cochemiea poselgeri adds a striking visual element to any desert-inspired landscape.
