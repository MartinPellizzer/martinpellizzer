Cordiera concolor var. concolor

Cordiera concolor var. concolor, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cordiera, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters during the spring season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
