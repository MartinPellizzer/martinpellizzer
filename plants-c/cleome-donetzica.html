Cleome donetzica

Cleome donetzica, commonly known as the prairie cleome, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and showy, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with serrated edges, and it produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds.

Cleome donetzica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
