Centaurea paniculata subvar. catalaunica

Centaurea paniculata subvar. catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan spiked speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It blooms from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This subvariety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
