Cuphea glossostoma

Cuphea glossostoma, commonly known as the velvetleaf cuphea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces small, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cuphea glossostoma is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
