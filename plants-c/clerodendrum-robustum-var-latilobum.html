Clerodendrum robustum var. latilobum

Clerodendrum robustum var. latilobum, commonly known as the broad-lobed cleerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is part of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants.
