Centaurea calcitrapa var. gracilis

Centaurea calcitrapa var. gracilis, commonly known as slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in dry, rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in open habitats such as coastal dunes and limestone areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
