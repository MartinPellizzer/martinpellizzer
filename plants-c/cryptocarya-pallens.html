Cryptocarya pallens

Cryptocarya pallens, commonly known as the white-flowered cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and showy white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are often visited by native bees and other pollinators.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
