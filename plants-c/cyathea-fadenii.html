Cyathea fadenii

Cyathea fadenii, commonly known as the Faden's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant is known for its slow growth and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cyathea fadenii is often found in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in cool, humid climates.
