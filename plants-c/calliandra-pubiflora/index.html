Calliandra Pubiflora

Calliandra tree, scientifically known as Calliandra pubiflora, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America. It is renowned for its vibrant, fluffy flower clusters that bloom in shades of pink and red, creating a visually stunning display. This tree is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in warm climates. Its feathery foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and public landscapes. Despite its beauty, the Calliandra tree requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish optimally.
