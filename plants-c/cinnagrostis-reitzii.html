Cinnagrostis reitzii

Cinnagrostis reitzii, commonly known as the Reitz's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in alpine or subalpine environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are clustered in a single head.

Cinnagrostis reitzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
