Cyperus krameri

Cyperus krameri, commonly known as the flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically borne on branched inflorescences. Cyperus krameri is often found in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in humid environments.

It is widely distributed across tropical and subtropical regions around the world.
