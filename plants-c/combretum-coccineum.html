Combretum coccineum

Combretum coccineum, commonly known as red combretum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The bark is thick and fissured, and the plant is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
