Cinnamomum zwartzii

Cinnamomum zwartzii, commonly known as Swartz's cinnamon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their essential oils.
