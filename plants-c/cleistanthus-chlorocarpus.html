Cleistanthus chlorocarpus

Cleistanthus chlorocarpus, commonly known as the yellow-flowered cleistanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains small, hard seeds.

Cleistanthus chlorocarpus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
