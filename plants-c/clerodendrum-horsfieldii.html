Clerodendrum horsfieldii

Clerodendrum horsfieldii, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in tropical and subtropical gardens.
