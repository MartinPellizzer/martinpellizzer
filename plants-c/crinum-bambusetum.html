Crinum bambusetum

Crinum bambusetum, commonly known as the bamboo lily, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, strap-like leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its bulbs are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.

Crinum bambusetum is also noted for its air-purifying properties, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
