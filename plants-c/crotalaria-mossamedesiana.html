Crotalaria mossamedesiana

Crotalaria mossamedesiana, commonly known as the African horsebean, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces bean-like pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
