Cyphomandra tenuisetosa

Cyphomandra tenuisetosa, commonly known as the slenderleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and a slightly hairy texture.
