Croton seretii

Croton seretii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often adorned with striking patterns and colors. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its bark and leaves contain compounds that have been traditionally used in herbal medicine.

Croton seretii thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
