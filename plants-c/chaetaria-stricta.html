Chaetaria stricta

Chaetaria stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf chaetaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered umbels of white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy surface. It is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
