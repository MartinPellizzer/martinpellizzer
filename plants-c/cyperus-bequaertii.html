Cyperus bequaertii

Cyperus bequaertii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly found in wetlands and swampy areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist to waterlogged soils and is often found in marshy environments.

Cyperus bequaertii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
