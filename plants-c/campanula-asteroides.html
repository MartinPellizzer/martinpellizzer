Campanula asteroides

Campanula asteroides, commonly known as the star bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, bell-like flowers that range in color from blue to violet, often with a white or yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula asteroides is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its attractive blooms and ease of care.
