Canna houlletii

Canna houlletii, commonly known as the red canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features large, showy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple hue, and its flowers are bright red with a yellow center. The plant belongs to the Cannaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

Canna houlletii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
