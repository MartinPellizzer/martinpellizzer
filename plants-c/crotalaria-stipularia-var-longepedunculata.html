Crotalaria stipularia var. longepedunculata

Crotalaria stipularia var. longepedunculata, commonly known as the long pedunculate crotalaria, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has alternate, pinnately compound leaves with stipules that are often glandular and hairy. Its seeds are encased in a hard, pod-like structure that splits open when mature, releasing the seeds to the environment.

This species is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of moist to semi-arid conditions.
