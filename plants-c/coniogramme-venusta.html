Coniogramme venusta

Coniogramme venusta, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds that are adorned with bright yellow spore sacs, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.

Coniogramme venusta is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of shaded spaces.
