Chomelia ignambiensis

Chomelia ignambiensis, commonly known as the flameleaf chomelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that range from red to orange, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
