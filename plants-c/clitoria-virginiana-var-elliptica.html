Clitoria virginiana var. elliptica

Clitoria virginiana var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive violet-blue flower that blooms in clusters. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates make it a popular choice for ornamental and agricultural purposes.

The elliptical leaves and showy flowers contribute to its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes.
