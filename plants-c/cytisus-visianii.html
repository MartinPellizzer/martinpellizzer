Cytisus visianii

Cytisus visianii, commonly known as the Visianii broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its stems are typically woody and covered in small, glandular hairs.

Cytisus visianii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in Mediterranean climates.
