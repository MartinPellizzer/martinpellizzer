Ceanothus foliosus var. foliosus

Ceanothus foliosus var. foliosus, commonly known as the California scrub ceanothus, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is a valued species in native plant gardens.
