Caladenia vulgaris var. nunguensis

Caladenia vulgaris var. nunguensis, commonly known as the Nunguena Spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Western Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, elongated flower that resembles a spider in appearance. The flower has a narrow, hairy labellum and is typically pink or purple in color. It blooms during the cooler months, usually between June and August.

This variety is adapted to sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and woodlands.
