Cyperus paniceus

Cyperus paniceus, commonly known as flat sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Cyperus paniceus is used in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in aquatic gardens.
