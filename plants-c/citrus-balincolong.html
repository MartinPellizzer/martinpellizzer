Citrus balincolong

Citrus balincolong, commonly known as the Java lemon.

It is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant produces small, yellow, citrus fruits that are similar in appearance to lemons but have a distinct flavor. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and oval-shaped, while its flowers are fragrant and white.

The plant is often cultivated for its fruit and ornamental value.
