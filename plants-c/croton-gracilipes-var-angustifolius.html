Croton gracilipes var. angustifolius

Croton gracilipes var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish-green flowers that bloom during the wet season.
