Chromolaena Umbelliformis

Chromolaena umbelliformis is a fast-growing shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is known for its dense foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. This plant is often considered an invasive species in many parts of the world. Its bright green leaves and clusters of small white flowers make it visually striking. Despite its beauty, it poses a significant threat to native ecosystems.
