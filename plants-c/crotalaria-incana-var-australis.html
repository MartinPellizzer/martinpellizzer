Crotalaria incana var. australis

Crotalaria incana var. australis, commonly known as the slender cowpea or Australian crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual with upright, branching stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

The species is noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in certain agricultural contexts.
