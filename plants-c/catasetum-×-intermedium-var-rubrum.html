Catasetum × intermedium var. rubrum

Catasetum × intermedium var. rubrum, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and striking, showy flowers that often feature a mix of red, yellow, and orange hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are known for their unique structure, with specialized pollinia that are adapted for specific pollinators.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
