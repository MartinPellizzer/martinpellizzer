Clematis ajanensis

Clematis ajanensis, commonly known as the Ajanensis Clematis, is a flowering vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are typically woody and climbing. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis ajanensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to climb trellises or structures.
