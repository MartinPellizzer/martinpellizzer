Caesalpinia kauaiensis

Caesalpinia kauaiensis, commonly known as Kauai flame tree, is a species native to the island of Kauai in Hawaii.

It is a small shrub or tree that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its bright red to orange flowers, which are clustered in cylindrical spikes. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into smaller leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance.

Caesalpinia kauaiensis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry forests.
