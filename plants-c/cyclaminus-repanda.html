Cyclaminus repanda

Cyclaminus repanda, commonly known as the broadleaf cyclamen, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, spurred flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive waxy coating. It thrives in cool, moist conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Cyclaminus repanda is typically grown in containers or as a houseplant due to its sensitivity to direct sunlight and frost.
