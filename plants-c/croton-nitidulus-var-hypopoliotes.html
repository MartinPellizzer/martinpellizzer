Croton nitidulus var. hypopoliotes

Croton nitidulus var. hypopoliotes, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a distinctive velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded gardens.
