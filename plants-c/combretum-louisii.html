Combretum louisii

Combretum louisii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
