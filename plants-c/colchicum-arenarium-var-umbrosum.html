Colchicum arenarium var. umbrosum

Colchicum arenarium var. umbrosum, commonly known as the shadow lily, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help it retain moisture in arid environments. It is a member of the Colchicaceae family, known for its toxic alkaloids, including colchicine.
