Cyperus cartilagineus var. laevissimus

Cyperus cartilagineus var. laevissimus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale green to reddish flowers. The plant is characterized by its fibrous, spongy roots and is often found in wetland or marshy environments. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical structure makes it a common species in aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
