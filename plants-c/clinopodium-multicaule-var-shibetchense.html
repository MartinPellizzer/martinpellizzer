Clinopodium multicaule var. shibetchense

Clinopodium multicaule var. shibetchense, commonly known as Japanese wood betony, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a serrated margin, while the flowers are small and clustered in whorls.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses in Asian herbal medicine.
