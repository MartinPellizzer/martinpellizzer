Caesalpinia subtropica

Caesalpinia subtropica, commonly known as the tropical flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange floral spikes that appear at the base of its pinnate leaves. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Caesalpinia subtropica is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant floral display.
