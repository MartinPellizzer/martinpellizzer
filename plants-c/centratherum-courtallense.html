Centratherum courtallense

Centratherum courtallense, commonly known as the Courtallense grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale yellow to white flowers.

Centratherum courtallense is often used in landscaping and as a forage plant due to its hardiness and adaptability.
