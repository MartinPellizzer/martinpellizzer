Caladium picturatum

Caladium picturatum, commonly known as the painted caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is prized for its vibrant, colorful leaves that display shades of pink, red, white, and green. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have intricate patterns that resemble painted designs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This ornamental plant is widely cultivated for its decorative foliage in gardens and as houseplants.
