Chloris cynodontoides

Chloris cynodontoides, commonly known as the slender grass or slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats on the ground. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its resilience and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and used in erosion control.
