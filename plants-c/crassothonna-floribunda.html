Crassothonna floribunda

Crassothonna floribunda, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

The flowers are typically borne on long, slender stalks, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
