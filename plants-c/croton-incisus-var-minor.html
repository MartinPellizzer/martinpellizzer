Croton incisus var. minor

Croton incisus var. minor, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, often serrated leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
