Cordylanthus wrightii var. pauciflorus

Cordylanthus wrightii var. pauciflorus, commonly known as the Wright's cordylanthus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and often compound leaves.
