Clermontia × leptoclada var. holopsila

Clermontia × leptoclada var. holopsila, commonly known as the white-flowered clermontia, is a hybrid plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture. This variety is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate appearance.
