Cheilanthes chihuahuaensis

Cheilanthes chihuahuaensis, commonly known as the Chihuahua fern, is a species of lithophyte belonging to the Polypodiaceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Mexico, particularly in the state of Chihuahua. This plant typically grows on rocky outcrops and is adapted to low moisture environments. Its fronds are leathery and evergreen, with a compact growth habit.

The plant exhibits a unique ability to survive in harsh conditions, making it an important component of desert ecosystems.
