Combretum multidiscum

Combretum multidiscum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flower clusters that appear at the ends of its branches. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape and have a slightly rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
