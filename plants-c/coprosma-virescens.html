Coprosma virescens

Coprosma virescens, commonly known as the New Zealand glory bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a glossy green, with some varieties displaying red or purple hues. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by bright red berries. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers moist, well-drained conditions.

Coprosma virescens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for native wildlife.
