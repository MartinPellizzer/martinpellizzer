Carex motoskei

Carex motoskei, commonly known as the Tatra sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Carex motoskei is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
