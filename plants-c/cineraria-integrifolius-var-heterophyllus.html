Cineraria integrifolius var. heterophyllus

Cineraria integrifolius var. heterophyllus, commonly known as the variegated cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its strikingly variegated foliage, featuring a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant produces clusters of showy blue or violet flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a favored plant among gardeners and botanists alike.
