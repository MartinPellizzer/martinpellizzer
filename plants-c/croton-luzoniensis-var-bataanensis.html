Croton luzoniensis var. bataanensis

Croton luzoniensis var. bataanensis, commonly known as the Bataan croton, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in lowland forests.

The Bataan croton is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
