Calendula dentata

Calendula dentata, commonly known as the toothed marigold, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically daisy-like in appearance. The plant has serrated or toothed leaves that are alternate and hairy in texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Calendula dentata is also valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
