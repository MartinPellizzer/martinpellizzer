Coreopsis venusta

Coreopsis venusta, commonly known as the showy coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has feathery, slender leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in moist to wet soils along stream banks and wetlands.

Coreopsis venusta is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
