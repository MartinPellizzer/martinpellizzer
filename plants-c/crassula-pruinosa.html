Crassula pruinosa

Crassula pruinosa, commonly known as the silver crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves covered in a fine, powdery coating that gives it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
