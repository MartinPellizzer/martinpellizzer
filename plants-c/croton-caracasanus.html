Croton caracasanus

Croton caracasanus, commonly known as the Caracasanus croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that range from red to yellow and green. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
