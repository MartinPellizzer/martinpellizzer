Cryptocarya bowiei

Cryptocarya bowiei, commonly known as the New Zealand olive, is a small evergreen tree native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Cryptocarya bowiei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dense foliage and winter flowering.
