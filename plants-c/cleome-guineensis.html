Cleome guineensis

Cleome guineensis, commonly known as African Marvel, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spade-shaped leaves and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant grows as an annual herb, often reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its flowers are typically borne on long, slender pedicels, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

Cleome guineensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
