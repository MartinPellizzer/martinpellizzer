Cotoneaster oliganthus

Cotoneaster oliganthus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster oliganthus is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and adaptability.
