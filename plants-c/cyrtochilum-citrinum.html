Cyrtochilum citrinum

Cyrtochilum citrinum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically small and clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has a compact growth habit with pseudobulbs and is often found in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Cyrtochilum citrinum is valued in horticulture for its vibrant color and ornamental appeal.
