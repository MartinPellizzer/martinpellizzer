Cylicodaphne leefeana

Cylicodaphne leefeana, commonly known as the Lemon Scented Pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, strap-like leaves that emit a citrus-like fragrance when crushed. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern and are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural fragrance.

The Cylicodaphne leefeana is well adapted to humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
