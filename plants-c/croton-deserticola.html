Croton deserticola

Croton deserticola, commonly known as the desert croton, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in hot, dry environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Croton deserticola is well adapted to survive in harsh desert conditions with minimal water availability.
