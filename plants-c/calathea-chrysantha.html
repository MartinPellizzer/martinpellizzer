Calathea chrysantha

Calathea chrysantha, commonly known as the golden flame calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with bright yellow and green patterns. The leaves are broad and oval, often with a waxy texture that enhances their visual appeal. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant colors.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
