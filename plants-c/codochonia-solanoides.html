Codochonia solanoides

Codochonia solanoides, commonly known as the purpleleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features hairy, upright stems and produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. The plant's leaves are usually lobed and have a slightly succulent texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
