Cattleya × gottoana

Cattleya × gottoana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cattleya mossiae and Cattleya loddigesii.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, white, and purple. The flowers are borne on sturdy, branching stems and are known for their strong fragrance.

Cattleya × gottoana is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
