Cotoneaster taylorii

Cotoneaster taylorii, commonly known as Taylor's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, dark green leaves that turn red in the autumn, adding ornamental value to the landscape. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Cotoneaster taylorii is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
