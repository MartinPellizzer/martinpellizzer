Clematis chaohuensis

Clematis chaohuensis, commonly known as the Chaohu clematis, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are compound, with three to five leaflets, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Clematis chaohuensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
