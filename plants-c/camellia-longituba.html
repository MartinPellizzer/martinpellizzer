Camellia longituba

Camellia longituba, commonly known as the long-stemmed camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and provide a lush backdrop to its blooms. Camellia longituba thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
