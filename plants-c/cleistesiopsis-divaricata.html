Cleistesiopsis divaricata

Cleistesiopsis divaricata, commonly known as the variable cleistesiopsis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, often appearing in clusters.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
