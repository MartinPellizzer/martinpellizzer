Croton corymbulosoides

Croton corymbulosoides, commonly known as the red-leaf croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often multicolored foliage, which can range from deep red to green and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its aesthetic appeal, it is widely cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
