Chrysocoma schelechteri

Chrysocoma schelechteri, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Chrysocoma schelechteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
