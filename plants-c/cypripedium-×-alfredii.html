Cypripedium × alfredii

Cypripedium × alfredii, commonly known as the Chinese lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pouch-like flower that resembles a slipper, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant produces a single, showy flower per stem, which blooms in late spring or early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with rich, humus-filled soil.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is protected in many areas.
