Coelogyne karoensis

Coelogyne karoensis, commonly known as the Karo orchid, is a species of orchid native to the lowland rainforests of Sumatra, Indonesia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Coelogyne karoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
