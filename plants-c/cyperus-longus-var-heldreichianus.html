Cyperus longus var. heldreichianus

Cyperus longus var. heldreichianus, commonly known as the Heldreich's flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly known as the sedge family, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, ditches, and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important species for wetland ecosystems and ecological studies.
