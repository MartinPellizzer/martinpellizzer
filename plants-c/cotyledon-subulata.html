Cotyledon subulata

Cotyledon subulata, commonly known as the needle cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, needle-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
