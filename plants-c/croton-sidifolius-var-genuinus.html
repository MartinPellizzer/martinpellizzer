Croton sidifolius var. genuinus

Croton sidifolius var. genuinus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated foliage with deep green, yellow, and red hues. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
