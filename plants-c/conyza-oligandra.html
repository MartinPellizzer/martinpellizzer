Conyza oligandra

Conyza oligandra, commonly known as the whiteleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Conyza oligandra is known for its ability to spread rapidly through wind-dispersed seeds.
