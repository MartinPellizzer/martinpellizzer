Cedrela toona var. puberula

Cedrela toona var. puberula, commonly known as the silky toon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and elliptical leaves with a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing flat, winged seeds. It is valued for its durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction, and for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its resilience to environmental stress.
