Croton subasperrimum

Croton subasperrimum, commonly known as the rough-leaved croton, is a shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a rough texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Croton subasperrimum is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
