Camerium trispermum

Camerium trispermum, commonly known as the three-seed bladder, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, rounded, green pods that contain three seeds each, giving it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small white or yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
