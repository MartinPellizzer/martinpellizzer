Centunculus erectus

Centunculus erectus, commonly known as the upright mouse-ear.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, hairy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pink.

Centunculus erectus is native to parts of Europe and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
