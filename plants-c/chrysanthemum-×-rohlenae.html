Chrysanthemum × rohlenae

Chrysanthemum × rohlenae, commonly known as the Rohlhauer chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and a variety of flower colors, including white, yellow, and orange. Native to China, this plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy blooms and resilience.

The plant is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
