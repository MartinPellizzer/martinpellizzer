Chenopodium × zahnii

Chenopodium × zahnii, commonly known as hybrid goosefoot, is a species resulting from the cross between Chenopodium album and Chenopodium berlandieri.

It is an annual herbaceous plant characterized by its succulent leaves and erect growth habit. The plant typically produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters. Chenopodium × zahnii is often found in disturbed soils and is native to North America.

It has been used historically for its edible leaves and seeds, though it is also considered a weed in agricultural settings.
