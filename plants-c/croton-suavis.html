Croton suavis

Croton suavis, commonly known as the sweet croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that range from yellow to red and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton suavis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
