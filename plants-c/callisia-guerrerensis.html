Callisia guerrerensis

Callisia guerrerensis, commonly known as the Guerrerensis spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. Callisia guerrerensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
