Cyathogyne viridis var. subintegra

Cyathogyne viridis var. subintegra, commonly known as the green cyathogyne, is a unique member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant green leaves, which are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often green or white in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
