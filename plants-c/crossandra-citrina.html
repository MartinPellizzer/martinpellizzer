Crossandra citrina

Crossandra citrina, commonly known as the yellow flame plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.
