Calochortus krelagei

Calochortus krelagei, commonly known as the Krelage's mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes many species of mariposa lilies characterized by their showy, showy flowers. This particular species is known for its striking, often pink or purple, petals and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. It typically blooms in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
