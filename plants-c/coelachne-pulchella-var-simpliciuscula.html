Coelachne pulchella var. simpliciuscula

Coelachne pulchella var. simpliciuscula, commonly known as the slender false brome, is a grass species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is often hidden among the leaves.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
