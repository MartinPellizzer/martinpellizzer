Cyclamen × drydeniae

Cyclamen × drydeniae, commonly known as the hybrid cyclamen, is a perennial flowering plant characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for winter bedding plants and container gardening.
