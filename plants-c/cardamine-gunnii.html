Cardamine gunnii

Cardamine gunnii, commonly known as Gunn's watercress, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with showy blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. Its leaves are trifoliate, with finely divided lobes, and it produces small, white or greenish flowers.
