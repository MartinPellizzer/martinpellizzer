Caprifolium puniceum

Caprifolium puniceum, commonly known as the red honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that often grow along the ground or climb other structures. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Caprifolium puniceum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
