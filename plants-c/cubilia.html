Cubilia

Cubilia, commonly known as the Cuban nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Cubilia produces small, round berries that are toxic and should not be consumed.
