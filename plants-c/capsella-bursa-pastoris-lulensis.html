Capsella bursa-pastoris lulensis

Capsella bursa-pastoris lulensis, commonly known as the common chickweed, is a low-growing annual herb belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It features small, white or pink flowers and has alternate, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, fields, and along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in many regions.
