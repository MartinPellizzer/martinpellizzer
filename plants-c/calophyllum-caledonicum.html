Calophyllum caledonicum

Calophyllum caledonicum, commonly known as the New Caledonia flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown to black bark and its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Calophyllum caledonicum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine by local communities.
