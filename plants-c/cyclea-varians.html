Cyclea varians

Cyclea varians, commonly known as the "Rat's tail" or "Garden spider plant," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces fleshy, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Cyclea varians thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
