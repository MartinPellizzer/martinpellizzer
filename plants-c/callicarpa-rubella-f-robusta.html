Callicarpa rubella f. robusta

Callicarpa rubella f. robusta, commonly known as the red honeysuckle, is a variant of the Chinese beautyberry.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its small, clustered, violet-black berries that turn red when ripe. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is native to eastern Asia, including China and Japan.

This ornamental plant is valued for its vibrant berries and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
