Cheirostylis ovata

Cheirostylis ovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its ovate, broad leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cheirostylis ovata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
