Commiphora schimperi

Commiphora schimperi, commonly known as African myrrh, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family, which includes frankincense and myrrh-producing species. The resin is harvested from the bark and has a strong, balsamic scent similar to myrrh.
