Chenopodium zerovii

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Chenopodium zerovii is typically found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
