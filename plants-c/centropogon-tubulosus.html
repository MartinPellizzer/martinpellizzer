Centropogon tubulosus

Centropogon tubulosus, commonly known as the slender pipe orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, tubular flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for anchoring in soil, while its leaves are long and narrow.

Centropogon tubulosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
