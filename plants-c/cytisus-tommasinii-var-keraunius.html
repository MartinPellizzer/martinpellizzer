Cytisus tommasinii var. keraunius

Cytisus tommasinii var. keraunius, commonly known as the Keraunius broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically green. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand drought and salt spray.
