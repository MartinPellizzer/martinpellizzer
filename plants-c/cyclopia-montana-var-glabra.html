Cyclopia montana var. glabra

Cyclopia montana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth mountain cyclopia, is a South African plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its glabrous (smooth) stems and leaves, which are typically hairy in other varieties of the species. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flower heads that are often clustered in groups. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
