Chimonobambusa hejiangensis

Chimonobambusa hejiangensis, commonly known as the Hejiang bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Hejiang region, where it thrives in mountainous and forested areas.
