Cynanchum sodiroi

Cynanchum sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is known for its characteristic milky sap and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually pale green to yellowish in color, attracting various pollinators.
