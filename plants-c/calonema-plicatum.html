Calonema plicatum

Calonema plicatum, commonly known as the pleated calonema, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial that originates from the island of Madagascar. The plant is characterized by its dense, fleshy foliage that is often pleated or folded, giving it a unique appearance. It produces small, bell-shaped white or pink flowers during the summer months.

Calonema plicatum is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.
