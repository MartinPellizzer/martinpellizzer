Chrysosplenium pseudopilosum var. pseudopilosum

Chrysosplenium pseudopilosum var. pseudopilosum, commonly known as the hairy chrysosplenium, is a member of the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, arranged in clusters, and bloom in early spring.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or woodland habitats.
