Cynanchum verticillare

Cynanchum verticillare, commonly known as blue milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds known for their milky sap. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a follicle that splits open to release seeds with silky appendages.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along streambanks.
