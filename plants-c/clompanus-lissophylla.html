Clompanus lissophylla

Clompanus lissophylla, commonly known as the smooth-leafed clompanus, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom during the late spring and early summer. Clompanus lissophylla thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in dense forest undergrowth.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species and is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
