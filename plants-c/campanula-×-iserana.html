Campanula × iserana

Campanula × iserana, commonly known as the alpine bellflower, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the alpine regions of Central Europe and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in full sun. It has slender, upright stems and produces small, oval leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Campanula × iserana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural alpine habitat.
