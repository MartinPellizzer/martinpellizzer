Cytisus austriacus f. ochroleucus

Cytisus austriacus f. ochroleucus, commonly known as the Austrian broom or yellow broom, is a flowering shrub native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are arranged in racemes. Its foliage is typically dark green and its growth habit is upright with thorny stems.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
