Cleisostoma Filiforme

Cleisostoma filiforme is a rare and delicate orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is known for its long, thin petals that give it its name, meaning "thread-like." This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a prized addition to botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and endangered in the wild.
