Cymbidium variciferum

Cymbidium variciferum, commonly known as the variable cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in winter. The plant produces panicles of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers with yellow and green markings. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cymbidium variciferum is a hardy orchid that can thrive in a variety of soil types when provided with proper drainage and light conditions.
