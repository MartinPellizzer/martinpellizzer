Cyperus virens var. virens

Cyperus virens var. virens, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Its root system helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion in aquatic habitats.
