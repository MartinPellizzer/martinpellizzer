Consolea × Acaulis

Coral bells, scientifically known as Consolea × acaulis, are striking succulent plants native to southern Africa. They are prized for their vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and orange. These plants are easy to care for, making them popular among both novice and experienced gardeners. Their unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make them a favorite choice for rock gardens and container planting. With proper care, Coral bells can thrive and add a colorful touch to any landscape.
