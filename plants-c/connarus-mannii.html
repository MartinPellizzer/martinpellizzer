Connarus mannii

Connarus mannii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Connaraceae and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish flowers followed by spherical fruits containing several seeds.

Connarus mannii is often found in dry forest and woodland habitats, where it plays an important ecological role.
