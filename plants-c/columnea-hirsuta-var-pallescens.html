Columnea hirsuta var. pallescens

Columnea hirsuta var. pallescens, commonly known as the silver-leaved columnea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. The leaves are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
