Cineraria cyanomontana

Cineraria cyanomontana, commonly known as the blue mountain cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are often hairy and grayish-green, providing a contrast to the vibrant blue flowers.

Cineraria cyanomontana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
