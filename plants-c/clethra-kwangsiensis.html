Clethra kwangsiensis

Clethra kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of elliptical, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn.
