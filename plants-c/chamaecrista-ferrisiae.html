Chamaecrista ferrisiae

Chamaecrista ferrisiae, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of bright yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Chamaecrista ferrisiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
