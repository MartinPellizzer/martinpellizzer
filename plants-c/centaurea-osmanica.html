Centaurea osmanica

Centaurea osmanica, commonly known as Osman's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spiky seed heads.

Centaurea osmanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
