Cortaderia sodiroana

Cortaderia sodiroana, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, silver-tipped plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and ornamental gardens. Its foliage is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting, elegant form.

Cortaderia sodiroana is also valued for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
