Cineraria difformis

Cineraria difformis, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant features hairy, lobed leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Cineraria difformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
