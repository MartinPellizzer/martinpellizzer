Cnicus lucidus

Cnicus lucidus, commonly known as the shining sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas. Its leaves are smooth and slightly hairy, with a distinctive light green color.

Cnicus lucidus is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in many regions.
