Crassula vestita

Crassula vestita, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are pale green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or container planting.
