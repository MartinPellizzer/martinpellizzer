Chaenactis glabriuscula var. curta

Chaenactis glabriuscula var. curta, commonly known as the short-stemmed plains coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy foliage. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as grasslands and along roadsides. It features small, yellowish to white ray flowers surrounded by yellow disk florets.

Its short, compact stature and drought-tolerant nature make it well adapted to arid environments.
