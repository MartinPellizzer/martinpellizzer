Callistemon lanceolatus var. semperflorens

Callistemon lanceolatus var. semperflorens, commonly known as the Christmas bottlebrush, is a compact shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink bottlebrush flowers that bloom throughout the year, especially in warm climates. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance contribute to its ornamental value.
