Croton guerzesiensis

Croton guerzesiensis, commonly known as the Guerze Islands croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is characterized by its diverse array of species with often striking foliage. The plant is noted for its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from green to red and yellow, making it a visually appealing species. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
