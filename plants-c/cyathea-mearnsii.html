Cyathea mearnsii

Cyathea mearnsii, commonly known as the Mearns' tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and longevity, often living for many decades. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a distinctive, elegant appearance.

Cyathea mearnsii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
