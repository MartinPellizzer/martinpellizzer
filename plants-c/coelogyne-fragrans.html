Coelogyne fragrans

Coelogyne fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Coelogyne fragrans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its pleasant, sweet scent.
