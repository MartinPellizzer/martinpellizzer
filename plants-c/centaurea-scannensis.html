Centaurea scannensis

Centaurea scannensis, commonly known as the Scandia knapweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange-red flowers, which are typically arranged in dense, spherical clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Centaurea scannensis is often found in disturbed areas and is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
