Columnea orientandina

Columnea orientandina, commonly known as the oriental flame vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features trailing stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking floral display.

Columnea orientandina is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
