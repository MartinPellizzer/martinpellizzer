Crassula barklyana

Crassula barklyana, commonly known as the Barkly Crassula, is a succulent plant native to Australia.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
