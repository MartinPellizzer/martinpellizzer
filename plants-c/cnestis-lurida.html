Cnestis lurida

Cnestis lurida, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-white flowers followed by small, hard, woody fruits. It is known for its high density and durability, making it valuable for construction and as a source of timber.

Cnestis lurida is also used in traditional medicine for its various purported health benefits.
