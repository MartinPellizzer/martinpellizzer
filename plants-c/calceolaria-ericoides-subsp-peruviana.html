Calceolaria ericoides subsp. peruviana

Calceolaria ericoides subsp.

peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian slipper orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America. It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color.

The plant has hairy, glandular leaves and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often found in rocky soils at high elevations.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its distinctive floral morphology.
