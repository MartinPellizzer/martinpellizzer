Calycera herbacea var. viridiflora

Calycera herbacea var. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered calycera, is a herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin. Its most distinctive feature is the small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters at the tips of the stems.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
