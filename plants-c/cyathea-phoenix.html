Cyathea phoenix

Cyathea phoenix, commonly known as the Phoenix tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, trunk-like stem covered in a layer of dead fronds. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Cyathea phoenix thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
