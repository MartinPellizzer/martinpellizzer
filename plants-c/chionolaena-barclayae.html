Chionolaena barclayae

Chionolaena barclayae, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fluffy, white to pale yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and gray-green, with a woolly texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
