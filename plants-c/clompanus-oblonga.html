Clompanus oblonga

Clompanus oblonga, commonly known as the broad-leaved clompanus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often blooming during the warmer months. Clompanus oblonga thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
