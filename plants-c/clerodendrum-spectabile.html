Clerodendrum spectabile

Clerodendrum spectabile, commonly known as the flame bush or red torch, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy bract clusters that resemble torches, often displaying shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically hidden within the colorful bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum spectabile is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
