Cylindropuntia clavellina

Cylindropuntia clavellina, commonly known as the Mexican apple cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and has spines for protection.

Its structure and appearance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and cactus collections.
