Cryptocoryne dalzellii

Cryptocoryne dalzellii, commonly known as the Dalzell's cryptostegia, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, ribbon-like leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically thrives in low to moderate light conditions and prefers soft, slightly acidic water. It is often cultivated in aquariums due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne dalzellii is also known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced aquarists.
