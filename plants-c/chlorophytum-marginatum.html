Chlorophytum marginatum

Chlorophytum marginatum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with long, arching leaves that are typically green with a white or silver margin. The plant produces small, grass-like flowers that are usually white or pale green in color. It is well-known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and its ease of care.

Chlorophytum marginatum is also notable for its ability to produce offsets, making it easy to propagate.
