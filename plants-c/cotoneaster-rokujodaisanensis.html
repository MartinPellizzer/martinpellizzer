Cotoneaster rokujodaisanensis

Cotoneaster rokujodaisanensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Japan, specifically the Ryukyu Islands, where it grows in woodland and scrub habitats. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that matures in autumn, providing food for various wildlife.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native environment.
