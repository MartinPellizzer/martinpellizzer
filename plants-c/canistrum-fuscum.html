Canistrum fuscum

Canistrum fuscum, commonly known as the dark canistrum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features woody stems and opposite, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are clustered in cyathia, a characteristic feature of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Canistrum fuscum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
