Cotyledon lassithiensis

Cotyledon lassithiensis, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
