Chamaedorea undulatifolia

Chamaedorea undulatifolia, commonly known as the wavy-leaf bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features gracefully arching, wavy-edged leaves that give it a distinctive and elegant appearance. The plant has a slender, upright stem with a fibrous root system, making it well-suited for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its lush foliage.

Its compact size and air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for both residential and office environments.
