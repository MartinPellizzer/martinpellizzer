Cleomella Sparsifolia

Cleomella sparsifolia is a unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking, sparsely arranged flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. This species thrives in harsh environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to drought and poor soil conditions. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike. Despite its beauty, Cleomella sparsifolia remains a relatively obscure plant in the world of horticulture.
