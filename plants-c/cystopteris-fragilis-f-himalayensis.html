Cystopteris fragilis f. himalayensis

Cystopteris fragilis f. himalayensis, commonly known as the Himalayan brittle fern, is a fern species native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures that are typically bipinnate. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories at high altitudes. Its fronds are thin and fragile, giving it the common name "brittle fern.".

This fern is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
