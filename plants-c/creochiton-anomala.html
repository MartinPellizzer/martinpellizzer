Creochiton anomala

Creochiton anomala, commonly known as the giant warty sea slug, is a species of sea slug found in the intertidal zones of the Pacific Northwest.

It belongs to the family Eulimidae and is characterized by its large, soft body covered in distinct warts or papillae. The coloration of this species can vary, often displaying a range of hues from brown to orange, which helps it blend into its marine environment. Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to marine biologists studying coastal ecosystems.
