Cucubalus niveus

Cucubalus niveus, commonly known as the white cucumber tree, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cucurbitaceae, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and gourds. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a long, cylindrical berry that turns from green to yellow as it matures.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
