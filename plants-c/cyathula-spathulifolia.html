Cyathula spathulifolia

Cyathula spathulifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf cyathula, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly China, India, and Southeast Asia. The plant features heart-shaped, waxy leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in cyathia, which are characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
