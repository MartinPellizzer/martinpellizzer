Cecropia cobariana

Cecropia cobariana, commonly known as the silver ceiba, is a fast-growing tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and distinctive, clustered inflorescences. The plant has a robust stem with a hollow core, which is often used by animals for shelter. Its flowers are small and white, producing nectar that attracts various pollinators.
