Colpodium Elbursense

Colpodium elbursense is a rare and unique plant species native to the Elburz Mountains in Iran. It thrives in rocky, arid environments and is known for its distinctive leaf morphology. This plant has adapted to extreme conditions, showcasing remarkable resilience and survival strategies. Its discovery has sparked interest among botanists and conservationists alike. Studying Colpodium elbursense offers insights into ecological adaptation and biodiversity in challenging landscapes.
