Columnea incredibilis

Columnea incredibilis, commonly known as the "Incredible" or "Columnea of the Andes," is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or a trailing vine, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its foliage consists of glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green and often have a velvety texture.

Columnea incredibilis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
