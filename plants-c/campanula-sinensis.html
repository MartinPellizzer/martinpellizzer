Campanula sinensis

Campanula sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It produces striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch.

Campanula sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
