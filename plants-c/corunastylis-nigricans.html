Corunastylis nigricans

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the sedge family. Its dark green foliage and compact growth habit make it a suitable candidate for ornamental use in moist, shaded garden environments.

Corunastylis nigricans thrives in wetlands and marshy areas, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
