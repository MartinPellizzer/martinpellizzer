Cynodon brizoides

Cynodon brizoides, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and slightly rolled, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in summer.

Cynodon brizoides is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
