Cattleya labiata var. dowiana

Cattleya labiata var. dowiana, commonly known as the Dow's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, tropical environments.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and fragrance.
