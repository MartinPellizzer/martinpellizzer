Canastra

Canastra, commonly known as *Anadenathera macrocarpa*, is a species of leguminous tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The tree produces woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Canastra is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.

Its botanical features make it an important plant in both ecological and cultural contexts.
