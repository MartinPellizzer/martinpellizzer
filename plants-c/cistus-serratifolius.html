Cistus serratifolius

Cistus serratifolius, commonly known as the serrated leaf rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Cistus serratifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional herbal medicine.
