Chrysanthemum okiense

Chrysanthemum okiense, commonly known as the Oki Island chrysanthemum, is a species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically blooms in the fall and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Chrysanthemum okiense is also valued for its historical and cultural significance in Japanese horticulture.
