Coleus swynnertonii

Coleus swynnertonii, commonly known as Swynnerton's coleus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often marked with striking patterns. Its flowers are small and tubular, typically appearing in clusters, and are usually pale blue or purple in color.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
