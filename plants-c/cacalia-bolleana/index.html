Cacalia Bolleana

Cacalia bolleana is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Italy. It is known for its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Cacalia bolleana faces threats to its survival, highlighting the need for protective measures.
