Conophytum orbicum

Conophytum orbicum, commonly known as the "Cape tulip," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
