Chrysanthemum montis-stellarum

Chrysanthemum montis-stellarum, commonly known as the star mountain chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and branched.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in alpine gardens.
