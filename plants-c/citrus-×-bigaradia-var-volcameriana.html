Citrus × bigaradia var. volcameriana

Citrus × bigaradia var. volcameriana, commonly known as the volcambiara lemon, is a hybrid citrus plant originating from the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its small, yellow, and highly aromatic fruit with a thin, smooth skin. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is prized for its high vitamin C content and unique flavor, making it a popular choice for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
