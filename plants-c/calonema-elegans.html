Calonema elegans

Calonema elegans, commonly known as the elegant calonema, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor displays.
