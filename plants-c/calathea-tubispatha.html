Calathea tubispatha

Calathea tubispatha, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, patterned leaves that often change color in response to light conditions. Its leaves are typically a deep green with intricate markings that resemble prayer hands, hence its common name.

Calathea tubispatha is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
