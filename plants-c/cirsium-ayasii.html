Cirsium ayasii

Cirsium ayasii, commonly known as the Ayasian thistle, is a perennial plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, and it is often found in mountainous regions.

Cirsium ayasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
