Coelogyne hutchinsoniana

Coelogyne hutchinsoniana, commonly known as the Hutchinson's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features a short rhizome with fleshy, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are showy, with a pale yellow to greenish-white color and a distinctive red or orange lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
