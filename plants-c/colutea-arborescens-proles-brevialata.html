Colutea arborescens proles brevialata

Colutea arborescens proles brevialata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broomrape, is a parasitic plant that lacks chlorophyll and relies on host plants for nutrients.

Due to its parasitic nature, it can significantly affect the growth and health of its host plants.
