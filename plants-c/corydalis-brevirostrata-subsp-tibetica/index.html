Corydalis Brevirostrata Subsp. Tibetica

Corydalis brevirostrata subsp. tibetica is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of Tibet. It belongs to the genus Corydalis, which is known for its diverse and colorful flowers. This subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure. It thrives in cold, rocky environments and is often found in alpine meadows. Due to its limited distribution and ecological sensitivity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study.
