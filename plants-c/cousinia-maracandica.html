Cousinia maracandica

Cousinia maracandica, commonly known as the Kashmir stonecress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and often edible seeds. Cousinia maracandica is often used in traditional medicine and has ornamental value due to its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
