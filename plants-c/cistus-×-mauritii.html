Cistus × mauritii

Cistus × mauritii, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, thriving in poor soils and full sun. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

Cistus × mauritii is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.
