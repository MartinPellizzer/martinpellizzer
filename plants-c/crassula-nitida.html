Crassula nitida

Crassula nitida, commonly known as the Ice Plant or String of Buttons, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and fleshy, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula nitida is often used in rock gardens or as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
