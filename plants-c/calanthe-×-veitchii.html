Calanthe × veitchii

Calanthe × veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's calanthe, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. Calanthe × veitchii prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated in shaded garden beds or as a potted plant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and unique floral display.
