Carduus hohenackeri

Carduus hohenackeri, commonly known as Hohenacker's thistle, is a perennial plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its flowers are visited by various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
