Cymbidium celebicum

Cymbidium celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to orange-red blooms, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade for optimal growth.

Cymbidium celebicum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
