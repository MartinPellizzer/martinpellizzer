Cypripedium × excellens

Cypripedium × excellens, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch that resembles a lady's shoe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Its foliage consists of broad, grass-like leaves, and it produces a single flower per stem.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
