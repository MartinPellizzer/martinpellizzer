Chrysanthemum larvatum

Chrysanthemum larvatum, commonly known as the Japanese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in a range of colors including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sunlight to produce its showy blooms.

The flowers are often used in ornamental gardening and traditional herbal medicine.
