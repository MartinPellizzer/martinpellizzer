Clerodendrum ixoriflorum

Clerodendrum ixoriflorum, commonly known as the Madagascar glorybower, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, clustered fruits that are capsule-like and contain numerous seeds.

Clerodendrum ixoriflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates.
