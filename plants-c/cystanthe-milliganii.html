Cystanthe milliganii

Cystanthe milliganii, commonly known as the Milligan's flaxflower, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has hairy, linear leaves that are often blue-green in color. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are usually pink to purple in hue, attracting various pollinators.
