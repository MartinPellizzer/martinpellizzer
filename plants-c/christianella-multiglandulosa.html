Christianella multiglandulosa

Christianella multiglandulosa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
