Capsicum velutinum

Capsicum velutinum, commonly known as the velvet pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. Its leaves are broad and covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it its common name.

Capsicum velutinum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some culinary uses in local traditions.
