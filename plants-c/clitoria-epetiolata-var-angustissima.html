Clitoria epetiolata var. angustissima

Clitoria epetiolata var. angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaf butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically a striking purple or blue color. The plant has slender, almost leafless stems, which give it its species name "epetiolata.". Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a distinctive heart-shaped base.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive blooms and is also used in traditional medicine.
