Clematis Subumbellata

Clematis subumbellata is a striking vine native to the eastern United States. Known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers, it adds a splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. This plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardeners. Its unique umbellate flower clusters set it apart from other clematis species. While not as common as some varieties, it's a rewarding plant for those willing to provide the right conditions.
