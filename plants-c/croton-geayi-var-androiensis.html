Croton geayi var. androiensis

Croton geayi var. androiensis, commonly known as the Androensis Croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in its native habitats.
