Croton alainii

Croton alainii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with bright yellow and green patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and have a waxy texture.

Croton alainii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
