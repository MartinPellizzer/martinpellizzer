Convolvulus blandus

Convolvulus blandus, commonly known as the blushing convolvulus, is a flowering plant belonging to the bindweed family (Convolvulaceae).

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it both a popular ornamental plant and a potential weed.
