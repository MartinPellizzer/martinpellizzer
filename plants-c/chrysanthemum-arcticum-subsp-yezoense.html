Chrysanthemum arcticum subsp. yezoense

Chrysanthemum arcticum subsp.

yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo chrysanthemum, is a hardy perennial plant native to the cold climates of East Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its foliage is typically gray-green, with hairy leaves that help insulate it against harsh weather conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cooler environments.
