Clitoria obidensis

Clitoria obidensis, commonly known as the "Bee Flower" or "Flower of the Bee," is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features delicate, butterfly-like flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, with a distinctive shape that resembles a bee. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Clitoria obidensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
