Cinnamomum endlicheriicarpum

Cinnamomum endlicheriicarpum, commonly known as the Malabar cinnamon or Ceylon cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is used to produce the spice cinnamon. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small and yellow, and it produces berries that contain aromatic seeds.

This species is closely related to other cinnamon species and is valued for both its ornamental and medicinal properties.
