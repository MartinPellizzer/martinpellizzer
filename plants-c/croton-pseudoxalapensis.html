Croton pseudoxalapensis

Croton pseudoxalapensis, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from bright red to deep orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. It typically grows in humid, shady environments and is valued for its vibrant foliage.

Croton pseudoxalapensis is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
