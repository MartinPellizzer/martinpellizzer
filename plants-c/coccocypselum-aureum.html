Coccocypselum aureum

Coccocypselum aureum, commonly known as the golden star sphaerocarp, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sphaerocarpaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters.
