Calliandra stipulacea

Calliandra stipulacea, commonly known as the yellow calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its clustered, showy yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has feathery, bipinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Calliandra stipulacea is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, benefiting soil fertility in agricultural settings.
