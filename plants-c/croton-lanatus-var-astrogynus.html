Croton lanatus var. astrogynus

Croton lanatus var. astrogynus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, simple, and often lobed leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive three-lobed capsules. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
