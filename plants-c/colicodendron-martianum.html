Colicodendron martianum

Colicodendron martianum, commonly known as the Martian Flame Tree, is a unique and striking plant native to the arid regions of Mars.

It features vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display against the red soil. The plant has adapted to extreme conditions with thick, waxy leaves that reduce water loss and a deep root system for accessing underground moisture. Its sap contains compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal properties.

Despite its alien appearance, it plays a vital role in the local ecosystem by providing shelter and sustenance to various Martian organisms.
