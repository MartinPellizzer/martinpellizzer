Chamaedorea paradoxa

Chamaedorea paradoxa, commonly known as the Paradox Palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Mexico. This plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and pinnately compound leaves that are often bright green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Chamaedorea paradoxa is popular as a houseplant due to its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.
