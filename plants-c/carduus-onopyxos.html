Carduus onopyxos

Carduus onopyxos, commonly known as the purple thistle.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
