Corydalis heracleifolia

Corydalis heracleifolia, commonly known as the rock corydalis, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. Corydalis heracleifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
