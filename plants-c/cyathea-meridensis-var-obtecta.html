Cyathea meridensis var. obtecta

Cyathea meridensis var. obtecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its fronds are typically a dark green, with a smooth, waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
