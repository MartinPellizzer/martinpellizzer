Centaurea × pampaninii

Centaurea × pampaninii, commonly known as the hybrid centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers consist of ray florets and disk florets, forming a composite structure typical of the Asteraceae family.

Centaurea × pampaninii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant colors and long blooming period.
