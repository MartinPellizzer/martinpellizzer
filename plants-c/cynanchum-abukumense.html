Cynanchum abukumense

Cynanchum abukumense, commonly known as the Abukumense milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes many milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
