Cymbolaena longifolia

Cymbolaena longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf cymbopogon, is a tropical plant native to regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are often aromatic and used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily in moist, shaded environments.

Cymbolaena longifolia is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating respiratory and digestive ailments.
