Croton aromaticus

Croton aromaticus, commonly known as the fragrant croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its aromatic properties. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic benefits.

Croton aromaticus thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
