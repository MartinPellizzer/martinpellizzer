Cyclamen apiculatum

Cyclamen apiculatum, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran and Turkey.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive waxy coating, and its flowers are typically pink or white, with curled petals that give it a unique appearance. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant for indoor or outdoor gardens.

Cyclamen apiculatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and light conditions.
