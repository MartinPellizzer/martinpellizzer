Cypripedium × albanense

Cypripedium × albanense, commonly known as the Albanian lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a key feature of the Cypripedium genus. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats, often in mountainous areas. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single, fragrant flower per stem.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
