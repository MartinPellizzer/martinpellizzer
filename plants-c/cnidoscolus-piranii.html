Cnidoscolus piranii

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by edible, starchy tubers that are used in local cuisines. Due to its unique appearance and culinary uses, it is of interest to both botanists and food enthusiasts.
