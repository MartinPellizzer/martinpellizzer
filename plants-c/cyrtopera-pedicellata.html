Cyrtopera pedicellata

Cyrtopera pedicellata, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often near water sources. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white.

Cyrtopera pedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
