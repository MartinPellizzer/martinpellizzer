Castanopsis sieboldii var. lutchuensis

Castanopsis sieboldii var. lutchuensis, commonly known as the Lutchu chestnut, is a species of evergreen tree native to the island of Lutchu (Luzon) in the Philippines.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and chestnuts, and is characterized by its dense, glossy leaves and robust, cone-like fruits. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, typically in moist, well-drained soils within lowland to montane forests.
