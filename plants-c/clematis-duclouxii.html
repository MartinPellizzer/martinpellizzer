Clematis duclouxii

Clematis duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, red berries that mature in the fall, which are an important food source for birds.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.
