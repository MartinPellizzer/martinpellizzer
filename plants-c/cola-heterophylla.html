Cola heterophylla

Cola heterophylla, commonly known as African cola, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its caffeine content, similar to that found in coffee beans.
