Centaurea pineticola

Centaurea pineticola, commonly known as the pine forest centaury, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as pine forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are blue to purple in color.
