Cicerbita songarica

Cicerbita songarica, commonly known as the Chinese bitter melon, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to East Asia and is characterized by its hairy, elongated fruits that are typically green to yellow in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has a trailing growth habit with lobed leaves. Its fruit is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and is known for its bitter taste.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
