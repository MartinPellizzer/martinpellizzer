Cereus hendriksenianus

Cereus hendriksenianus, commonly known as the Christmas star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of bright, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant has thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. Its leaves are typically reduced to small, spine-like structures, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers that turn pink as they mature.

This plant is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and seasonal blooming habit.
