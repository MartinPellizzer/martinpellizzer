Cassebeeria erecta

Cassebeeria erecta, commonly known as the erect cassia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the warm seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The seeds are enclosed in pods and dispersed by wind, aiding in its natural propagation.
