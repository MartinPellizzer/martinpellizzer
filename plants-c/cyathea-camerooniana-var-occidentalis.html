Cyathea camerooniana var. occidentalis

Cyathea camerooniana var. occidentalis, commonly known as the West African tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the rainforests of West Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizome system supports the growth of new shoots, allowing it to form dense clusters.

This species plays a vital ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
