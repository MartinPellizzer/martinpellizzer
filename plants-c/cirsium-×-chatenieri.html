Cirsium × chatenieri

Cirsium × chatenieri, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to the British Isles.

It is a hybrid between Cirsium vulgare and Cirsium horridulum, resulting in a plant with distinctive, spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands, hedgerows, and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates. It has a robust root system and can spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and horticultural practices.
