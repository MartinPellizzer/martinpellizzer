Cotyledon sedoides

Cotyledon sedoides, commonly known as the "Coral Cotyledon," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces striking, coral-like flowers in shades of pink and orange during the summer months. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently.

Cotyledon sedoides is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal watering to thrive.
