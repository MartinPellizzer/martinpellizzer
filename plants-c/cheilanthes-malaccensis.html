Cheilanthes malaccensis

Cheilanthes malaccensis, commonly known as the Malacca rock fern, is a terrestrial fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops and in shaded, humid environments. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients efficiently.
