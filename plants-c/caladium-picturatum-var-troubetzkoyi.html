Caladium picturatum var. troubetzkoyi

Caladium picturatum var. troubetzkoyi, commonly known as the Troubetzkoyi caladium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant patterns of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its dramatic visual appeal.

It is typically grown as an annual in regions with cooler climates.
