Calicotome spinosa var. fontqueri

Calicotome spinosa var. fontqueri, commonly known as the Fontqueri's broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.
