Clematis × pellieri

Clematis × pellieri, commonly known as the purple clematis, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Clematis vitalba and Clematis terniflora.

It is a vigorous climbing vine characterized by its arching stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically purple or lilac in color, with five petals and a distinctive, recurved shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
