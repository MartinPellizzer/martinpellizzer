Caladenia beaugleholei

Caladenia beaugleholei, commonly known as the Beauglehole's spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Western Australia.

It typically grows in sandy soils within shrubland and woodland habitats. The plant produces a single, slender stem with a single, strikingly colored flower that resembles a spider. Its flowers are usually pale yellow with reddish or brown markings, and they bloom during the autumn season.

The species is part of the Caladenia genus, which is known for its intricate and often showy floral structures.
