Centaurea antiochia

Centaurea antiochia, commonly known as Antioch centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features wiry stems and striking blue to purple ray florets surrounded by yellow disk flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Centaurea antiochia is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in sunny, well-drained environments.
