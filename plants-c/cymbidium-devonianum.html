Cymbidium devonianum

Cymbidium devonianum, commonly known as the Devonian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant produces broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in two rows along the stem. It is a perennial herb that thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Cymbidium devonianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
