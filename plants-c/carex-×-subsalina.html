Carex × subsalina

Carex × subsalina, commonly known as the hybrid sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its grass-like foliage and rhizomatous growth habit. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and wetlands. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a distinct midrib.

Carex × subsalina is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
