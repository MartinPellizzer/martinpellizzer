Convolvulus elegantissimus

Convolvulus elegantissimus, commonly known as the elegant morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant produces spiral-shaped seed pods that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing as a climbing vine.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
