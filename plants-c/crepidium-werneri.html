Crepidium werneri

Crepidium werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white with subtle markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Crepidium werneri is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
