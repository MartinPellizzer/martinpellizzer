Ceratostylis brevipes

Ceratostylis brevipes, commonly known as the short-styled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its inflorescence consists of a single spike bearing several tiny, clustered flowers.

Botanically, it is notable for its unique floral structure and is often found in association with specific fungal mycorrhizae.
