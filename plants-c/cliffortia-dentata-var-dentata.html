Cliffortia dentata var. dentata

Cliffortia dentata var. dentata, commonly known as the serrated cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, evergreen leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found growing on rocky outcrops.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, particularly in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
