Callichilia

Callichilia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Callichilia species typically have thick, waxy leaves that help retain moisture in their native arid environments. They are popular as houseplants due to their attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

The flowers often bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
