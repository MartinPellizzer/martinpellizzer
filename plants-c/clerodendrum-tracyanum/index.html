Clerodendrum Tracyanum

Clerodendrum tracyanum is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is native to regions in Southeast Asia. The plant features broad, glossy leaves and clusters of red or orange blossoms that attract pollinators. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its visual appeal. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to thrive in different climates.
