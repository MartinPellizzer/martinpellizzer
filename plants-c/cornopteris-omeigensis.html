Cornopteris omeigensis

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rocky or wooded slopes. Its spore-bearing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, a common feature in many fern species.

Cornopteris omeigensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
