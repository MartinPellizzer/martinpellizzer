Combretum mellifluum var. mellifluum

Combretum mellifluum var. mellifluum, commonly known as the sweet-scented flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated edge, while the bark is smooth and greyish in color. The plant is adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun, making it a popular ornamental species in warm climates.

Its unique floral scent and vibrant display make it a notable botanical specimen in its native habitat.
