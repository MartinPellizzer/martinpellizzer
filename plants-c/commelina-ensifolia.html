Commelina ensifolia

Commelina ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf commelina, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant features long, narrow leaves that resemble a sword, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Commelina ensifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
