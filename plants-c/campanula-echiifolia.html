Campanula echiifolia

Campanula echiifolia, commonly known as the lanceleaf bellflower, is a perennial herb native to central and southern Europe.

It features lance-shaped leaves and produces bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.

Campanula echiifolia is also noted for its hardiness and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
