Chlorophytum palghatense

Chlorophytum palghatense, commonly known as the Palghat spider plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests and rocky areas. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
