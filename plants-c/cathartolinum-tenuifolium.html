Cathartolinum tenuifolium

Cathartolinum tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaf cathartol, is a herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the Americas.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often blooming in late spring. Its root system is fibrous and contains compounds with mild laxative properties.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and open fields.
