Cyphochilus bilobus

Cyphochilus bilobus, commonly known as the two-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Cyphochilus bilobus is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories.
