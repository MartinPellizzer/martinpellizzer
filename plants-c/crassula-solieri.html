Crassula solieri

Crassula solieri, commonly known as the Solier's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula solieri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
