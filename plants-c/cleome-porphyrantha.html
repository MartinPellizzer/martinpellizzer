Cleome porphyrantha

Cleome porphyrantha, commonly known as the purpleleaf cleome, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture, and it produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.

Cleome porphyrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
