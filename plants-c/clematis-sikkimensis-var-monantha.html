Clematis sikkimensis var. monantha

Clematis sikkimensis var. monantha, commonly known as the Sikkim monantha clematis, is a climbing vine native to the Himalayas.

It features striking, large, showy flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at higher elevations.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
