Chloris brachyathera

Chloris brachyathera, commonly known as narrow-leaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Chloris brachyathera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
