Chlorophytum cameronii var. cameronii

Chlorophytum cameronii var. cameronii, commonly known as the Cameron's spider plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with a white midrib, forming a rosette shape. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the summer months. It is well adapted to grow in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
