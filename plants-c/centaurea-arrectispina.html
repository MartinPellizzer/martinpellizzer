Centaurea arrectispina

Centaurea arrectispina, commonly known as the prickly centaurea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and bright blue to purple composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Centaurea arrectispina is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.
