Chenopodium berlandieri subsp. euberlandieri

Chenopodium berlandieri subsp.

euberlandieri, commonly known as buffalo bur, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family. It is native to North America and is characterized by its spiny, spherical fruits that resemble small burs.

The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed areas such as roadsides and prairies. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is known for its adaptability and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
