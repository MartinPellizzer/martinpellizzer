Crossotropis mollis

Crossotropis mollis, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a small, deciduous shrub that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple pairs of small, feathery leaflets.

The silky acacia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
