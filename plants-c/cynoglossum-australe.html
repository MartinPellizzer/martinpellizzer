Cynoglossum australe

Cynoglossum australe, commonly known as Australian bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple at the base. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Cynoglossum australe is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in gardens for its unique floral display.
