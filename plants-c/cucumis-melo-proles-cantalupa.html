Cucumis melo proles cantalupa

Cucumis melo proles cantalupa, commonly known as the cantaloupe melon, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive netted rind and sweet, aromatic flesh. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers and has a sprawling growth habit. Its fruit develops from a single pistillate flower and is typically harvested when fully mature.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
