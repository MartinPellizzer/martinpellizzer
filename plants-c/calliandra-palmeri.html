Calliandra palmeri

Calliandra palmeri, commonly known as the Palmer's calliandra, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feather-like inflorescences that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Calliandra palmeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to provide habitat for various pollinators.
