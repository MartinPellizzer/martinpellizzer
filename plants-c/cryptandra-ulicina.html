Cryptandra ulicina

Cryptandra ulicina, commonly known as the small-leaf cryptandra, is a shrub native to southwestern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that attract nectar-feeding insects. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to dry, arid environments.

Cryptandra ulicina is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in full sun.
