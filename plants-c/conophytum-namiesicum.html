Conophytum namiesicum

Conophytum namiesicum, commonly known as the Namibia rock fig, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is often found growing on rocky outcrops and in sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
