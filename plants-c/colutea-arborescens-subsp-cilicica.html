Colutea arborescens subsp. cilicica

Colutea arborescens subsp.

cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Turkey and Syria. It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces bright yellow flowers in early spring.

The plant has a woody stem and is adapted to rocky and dry environments. It is often found in open woodlands and along rocky slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient species suitable for ecological restoration projects.
