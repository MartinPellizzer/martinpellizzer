Cattleya walkeriana

Cattleya walkeriana, commonly known as the Walker's Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting lip. The plant has broad, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya walkeriana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
