Carduus spinigerus

Carduus spinigerus, commonly known as prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, spiny leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's structure and growth habits make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
