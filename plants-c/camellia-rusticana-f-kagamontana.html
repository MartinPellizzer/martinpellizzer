Camellia rusticana f. kagamontana

Camellia rusticana f. kagamontana, commonly known as the Kagamontana camellia, is a rare and ornamental evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a contrasting red or purple edge. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that provide a striking backdrop to its blooms. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.

This cultivar is prized for its winter blooming habit and unique floral appearance.
