Clerodendrum nyctaginifolium

Clerodendrum nyctaginifolium, commonly known as the nightshade-leaved clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture, while its stems are woody and somewhat branched.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
