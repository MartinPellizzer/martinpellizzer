Croton elegantissimum

Croton elegantissimum, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often a vibrant green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with multiple stems and dense foliage.

Croton elegantissimum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental leaves and ability to thrive in humid, warm climates.
