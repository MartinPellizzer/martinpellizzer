Chrysosplenium filipes

Chrysosplenium filipes, commonly known as slender leaf chrysosplenium, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Chrysosplenium filipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
