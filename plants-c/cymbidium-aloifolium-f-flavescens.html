Cymbidium aloifolium f. flavescens

Cymbidium aloifolium f. flavescens, commonly known as the Yellow Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, grass-like leaves and striking yellow flowers with a subtle greenish tinge. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.

This variety is particularly valued for its vibrant color and elegant appearance in floral arrangements.
