Cheilanthes henryi, commonly known as Henry's rockcap, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen rosette of fleshy, leathery leaves that are typically pale green to grayish in color. Cheilanthes henryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
