Carex ischnostachya

Carex ischnostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed sedge.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical structure is typical of the Cyperaceae family, with rhizomes and a robust root system.
