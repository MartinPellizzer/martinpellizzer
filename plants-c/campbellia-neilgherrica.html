Campbellia neilgherrica

Campbellia neilgherrica, commonly known as the Neilgherric camellia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Campbellia neilgherrica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
