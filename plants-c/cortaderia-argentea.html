Cortaderia argentea

Cortaderia argentea, commonly known as silver grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, slender culms covered in silvery-white to grayish-white leaves that shimmer in the sunlight. The plant produces feathery, plume-like inflorescences that add a dramatic visual element, especially in autumn and winter. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for perennial gardens and landscape designs.

Its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for both residential and commercial landscaping.
