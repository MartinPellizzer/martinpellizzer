Cyrtochilum maasii

Cyrtochilum maasii, commonly known as the Maas's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a dense inflorescence. Cyrtochilum maasii thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
