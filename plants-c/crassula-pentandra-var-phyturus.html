Crassula pentandra var. phyturus

Crassula pentandra var. phyturus, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Silver Dollar Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or silver hue, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
