Clerodendrum brachystemon

Clerodendrum brachystemon, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces striking clusters of bright red or orange tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and birds. Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and showy inflorescences.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
