Catasetum gladiatorium

Catasetum gladiatorium, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped petals and a distinctive, elongated lip that often resembles a blade. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the dry season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Catasetum gladiatorium is also notable for its unique pollination mechanism, involving specialized structures that facilitate the transfer of pollen to specific pollinators.
