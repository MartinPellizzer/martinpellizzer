Clerodendrum congestum

Clerodendrum congestum, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, showy clusters of red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Clerodendrum congestum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
