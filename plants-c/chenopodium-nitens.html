Chenopodium nitens

Chenopodium nitens, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its succulent, triangular leaves that are covered in fine, stinging hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by edible, tangy berries. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Chenopodium nitens is also valued for its nutritional content and has been used in traditional cuisines.
