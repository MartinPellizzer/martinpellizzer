Cardamine Palustris Var. Brevipes

Cardamine palustris var. brevipes is a delicate, herbaceous plant commonly found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is known for its small, clustered leaves and subtle flowers. This variant is distinguished by its shorter stems and compact growth habit compared to other Cardamine species. It thrives in wetlands, stream banks, and other damp habitats across northern regions. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems and biodiversity.
