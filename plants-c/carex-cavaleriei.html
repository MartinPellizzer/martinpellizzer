Carex cavaleriei

Carex cavaleriei, commonly known as the Cavalerie sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. This species typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wet meadows. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually green with a slightly bluish tint.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often hidden among its leaves, contributing to its subtle and delicate appearance.
