Croton recurvifolius

Croton recurvifolius, commonly known as the curly-leaved croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that often curl or twist, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in warm climates.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
