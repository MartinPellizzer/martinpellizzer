Cautleya

Cautleya, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to Asia, particularly China, and are characterized by their showy, fragrant flowers. Cautleya species typically have strap-like leaves and produce clusters of small, colorful blooms. They are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

The genus includes several species, such as Cautleya fragrans, which is prized for its sweet fragrance.
