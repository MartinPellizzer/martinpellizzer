Callista armata

Callista armata, commonly known as the mountain devil, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky, coastal areas. The plant features small, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is well adapted to coastal conditions, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.

Callista armata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
