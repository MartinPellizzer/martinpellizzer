Clausena anisum-olens

Clausena anisum-olens, commonly known as wild lime or Malabar tamarind, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and yellowish flowers. The fruit is a small, sour berry that contains a single seed, and it is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used for centuries in various cultures for its medicinal and flavoring benefits.
