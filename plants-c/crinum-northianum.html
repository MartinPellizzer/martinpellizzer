Crinum northianum

Crinum northianum, commonly known as the African lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces tall, leafless stems that bear the flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Crinum northianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
