Cyathea versteegei

Cyathea versteegei, commonly known as the Versteeg's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a smooth, leathery texture.

Cyathea versteegei is often found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in cool and humid conditions.
