Coelogyne perplexa var. perplexa

Coelogyne perplexa var. perplexa, commonly known as the perplexing coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters of 5 to 10. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
