Centaurea junoniana var. isoplexiphylla

Centaurea junoniana var. isoplexiphylla, commonly known as the Junon's Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, seed-like fruits called cypsela. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and resilience in arid conditions.
