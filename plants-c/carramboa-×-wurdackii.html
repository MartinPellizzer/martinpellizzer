Carramboa × wurdackii

Carramboa × wurdackii, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Carramboa and Wurdackii species.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and attractive foliage.

The hybrid is also valued for its adaptability to various garden conditions.
