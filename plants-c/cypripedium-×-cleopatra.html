Cypripedium × cleopatra

Cypripedium × cleopatra, commonly known as the Cleopatra lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, organic soil. It has a long blooming period, often from late spring to early summer.

Due to its ornamental value and limited natural range, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
