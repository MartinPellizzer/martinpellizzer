Clematis vitalba var. gouriana

Clematis vitalba var. gouriana, commonly known as the Gouriana honeysuckle, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, often with a slight hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
