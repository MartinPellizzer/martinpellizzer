Cunonia pterophylla var. glabrescens

Cunonia pterophylla var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved cunonia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and small, pale yellow to green flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its compact growth habit and unique leaf structure.
