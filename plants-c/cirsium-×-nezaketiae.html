Cirsium × nezaketiae

Cirsium × nezaketiae, commonly known as the Nezaketia thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers, which are typical of the Cirsium genus. The plant is native to the western United States, particularly in California and Oregon, where it thrives in dry, sunny environments. Its growth habit is usually upright, with a compact form that makes it suitable for rock gardens or as an ornamental plant.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a variety of morphological traits that can vary between individual plants.
