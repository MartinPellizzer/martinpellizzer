Caladium brongniartii

Caladium brongniartii, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that come in shades of green, red, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It prefers partial to full shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

Caladium brongniartii is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies.
