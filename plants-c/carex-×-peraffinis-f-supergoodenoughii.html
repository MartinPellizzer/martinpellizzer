Carex × peraffinis f. supergoodenoughii

Carex × peraffinis f. supergoodenoughii, commonly known as the Super Goodenough sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Carex genus, which includes over 2,000 species of sedges.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and cylindrical spikes of small, clustered flowers. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities, featuring attractive, dark green leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in moist to wet conditions, making it suitable for wetlands, bog gardens, or water gardens.

The plant is relatively low maintenance and can serve as an effective ground cover in suitable environments.
