Campanula afra var. kremeri

Campanula afra var. kremeri, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The leaves are lanceolate, with a hairy texture, and the plant thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

This variety is known for its resilience and adaptability to various growing conditions.
