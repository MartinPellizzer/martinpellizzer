Cyperus tenoreanus

Cyperus tenoreanus, commonly known as the swamp spikerush, is a flowering plant belonging to the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. It has grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often green or reddish in color.

Cyperus tenoreanus is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its ability to thrive in moist conditions and its attractive appearance.
