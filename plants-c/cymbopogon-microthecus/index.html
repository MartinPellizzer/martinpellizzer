Cymbopogon Microthecus

Cymbopogon microthecus is a lesser-known grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa. It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes many familiar plants like rice and wheat. This plant is often found in moist, tropical environments and thrives in shaded areas. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
