Codiaeum musaicum

Codiaeum musaicum, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a wide range of hues, including red, orange, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Codiaeum musaicum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
