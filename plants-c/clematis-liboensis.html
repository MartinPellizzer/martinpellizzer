Clematis liboensis

Clematis liboensis, commonly known as the Yunnan clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit. The plant produces showy, typically blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of simple, alternate leaves that are often finely divided.

Clematis liboensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
