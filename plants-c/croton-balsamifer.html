Croton balsamifer

Croton balsamifer, commonly known as the balsam croton, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that range from green to red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its sap is thick and sticky, often emitting a balsamic or aromatic scent.

Croton balsamifer is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf colors and adaptability to various growing conditions.
