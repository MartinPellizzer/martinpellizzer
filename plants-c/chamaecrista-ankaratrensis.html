Chamaecrista ankaratrensis

Chamaecrista ankaratrensis, commonly known as the Madagascar velvet bean, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and nutritional properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and for agricultural purposes.
