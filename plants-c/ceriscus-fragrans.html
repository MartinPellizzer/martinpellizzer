Ceriscus fragrans

Ceriscus fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant ceriscus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that emit a sweet, aromatic scent. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a lush green backdrop to the showy blooms.

Ceriscus fragrans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and pleasant fragrance.
