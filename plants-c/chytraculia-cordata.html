Chytraculia cordata

Chytraculia cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf chytraculia, is a unique plant species characterized by its heart-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the family Chytraculaceae and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with dark green, glossy foliage. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically appearing in clusters.

Chytraculia cordata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
