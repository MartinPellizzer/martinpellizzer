Cryptandra multispina

Cryptandra multispina, commonly known as the many-spined cryptandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically visited by various pollinators. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Cryptandra multispina is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
