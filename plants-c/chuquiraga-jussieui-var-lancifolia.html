Chuquiraga jussieui var. lancifolia

Chuquiraga jussieui var. lancifolia, commonly known as the lancifoliate chuquiraga, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in high-altitude environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it requires well-drained conditions to flourish.
