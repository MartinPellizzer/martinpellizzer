Cannabina laevis

Cannabina laevis, commonly known as the smooth cannabis plant, is a species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless leaves and small, greenish flowers. The plant contains cannabinoids such as THC and CBD, which contribute to its psychoactive and medicinal properties. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and moderate sunlight.

Due to its chemical composition, it has been cultivated for both recreational and therapeutic uses.
