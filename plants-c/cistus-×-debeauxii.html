Cistus × debeauxii

Cistus × debeauxii, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has aromatic leaves and produces resinous secretions, which are often used in perfumery. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal gardens.

Cistus × debeauxii is also valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
