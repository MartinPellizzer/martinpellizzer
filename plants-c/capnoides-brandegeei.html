Capnoides brandegeei

Capnoides brandegeei, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers and succulent leaves. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is often found in rocky or arid environments. Its unique morphology includes a short stem and clustered inflorescences.

Capnoides brandegeei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
