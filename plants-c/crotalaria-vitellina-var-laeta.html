Crotalaria vitellina var. laeta

Crotalaria vitellina var. laeta, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

The species is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
