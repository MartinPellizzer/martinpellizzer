Cinnamomum politum

Cinnamomum politum, commonly known as the smooth cinnamon or Chinese cinnamon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, aromatic bark and leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are dark green and have a smooth texture, contributing to its common name.

The essential oils extracted from its bark and leaves are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.
