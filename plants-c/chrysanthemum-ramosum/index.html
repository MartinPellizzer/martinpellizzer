Chrysanthemum Ramosum

Chrysanthemum ramosum is a hardy perennial plant known for its vibrant blooms and bushy growth habit. Native to regions of Asia, it thrives in temperate climates and well-drained soils. This species is prized for its ability to produce an abundance of flowers throughout the growing season. Its compact form makes it ideal for garden borders, containers, and mixed beds. With proper care, Chrysanthemum ramosum can add color and texture to any landscape.
