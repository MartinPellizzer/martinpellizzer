Cattleya mossiae var. blakei

Cattleya mossiae var. blakei, commonly known as Blake's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces showy, pendulous blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
