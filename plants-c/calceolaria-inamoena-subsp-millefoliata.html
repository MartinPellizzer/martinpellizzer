Calceolaria inamoena subsp. millefoliata

Calceolaria inamoena subsp.

millefoliata, commonly known as the Mexican hooded orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico. It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive hood-shaped flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or shady areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing showy, violet-colored flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
