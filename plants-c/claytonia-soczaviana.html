Claytonia soczaviana

Claytonia soczaviana, commonly known as the Soczava's springbeauty, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

The plant has small, rounded leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white coating, which helps to reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.
