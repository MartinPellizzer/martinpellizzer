Coelogyne aspera

Coelogyne aspera, commonly known as the rough coelogyne, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, while its stems are wiry and often branched.

Coelogyne aspera is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its long-lasting, showy blooms.
