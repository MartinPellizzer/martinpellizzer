Cyperus kituiensis

Cyperus kituiensis, commonly known as Kitui flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to East Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland areas, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. It produces small, clustered spikelets that are often wind-pollinated.

Cyperus kituiensis is often found in disturbed habitats and can be an indicator species for wetland ecosystems.
