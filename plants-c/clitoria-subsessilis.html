Clitoria subsessilis

Clitoria subsessilis, commonly known as the lesser butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are rich in anthocyanins, giving them their vibrant color and making them popular in herbal teas.

Clitoria subsessilis is also valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
