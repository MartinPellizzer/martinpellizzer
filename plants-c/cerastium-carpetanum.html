Cerastium carpetanum

Cerastium carpetanum, commonly known as the carpet cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Cerastium carpetanum thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.
