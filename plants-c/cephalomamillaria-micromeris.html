Cephalomamillaria micromeris

Cephalomamillaria micromeris, commonly known as the small-fruited starflower, is a member of the Montiaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its low-growing habit and succulent leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
