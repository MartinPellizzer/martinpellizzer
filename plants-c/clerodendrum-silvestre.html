Clerodendrum silvestre

Clerodendrum silvestre, commonly known as wild chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

Clerodendrum silvestre is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
