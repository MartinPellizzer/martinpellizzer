Calliandra mollissima

Calliandra mollissima, commonly known as silky calliandra, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery inflorescences that produce showy, pink or red flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, velvety leaflets that give it a soft, tactile appearance. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Silky calliandra is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
