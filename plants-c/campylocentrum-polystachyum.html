Campylocentrum polystachyum

Campylocentrum polystachyum, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth and ability to produce abundant blooms make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
