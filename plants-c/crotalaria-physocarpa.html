Crotalaria physocarpa

Crotalaria physocarpa, commonly known as the inflated false morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, inflated seed pods. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with a rough texture.
