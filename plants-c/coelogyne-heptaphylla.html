Coelogyne heptaphylla

Coelogyne heptaphylla, commonly known as the seven-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces a single, long spike bearing multiple flowers, which bloom in succession. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern and are broad, leathery, and dark green.

Coelogyne heptaphylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
