Callaeum reticulatum

Callaeum reticulatum, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, net-like vein patterns on its leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
