Calliandra lanata

Calliandra lanata, commonly known as the silky calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its profuse display of bright red or orange flowers, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences. The plant has compound leaves with soft, hairy surfaces, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Calliandra lanata is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
