Cirsium kagamontanum var. spinuliferum

Cirsium kagamontanum var. spinuliferum, commonly known as the Kagamontan thistle, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its robust, upright growth habit. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, and the stems are often armed with stiff, hooked prickles.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
