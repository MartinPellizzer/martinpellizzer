Citrus × aurantium subf. anonyma

Citrus × aurantium subf.

anonyma, commonly known as the sour orange. It is a citrus species native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, yellow, sour fruits.

The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are fragrant and white, typically appearing in clusters.

The fruit is commonly used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its high vitamin C content.
