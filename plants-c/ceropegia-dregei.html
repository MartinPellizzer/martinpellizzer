Ceropegia dregei

Ceropegia dregei, commonly known as the dwarf string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, attracting specific pollinators like moths. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
