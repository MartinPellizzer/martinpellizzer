Chlorophytum hypoxiforme

Chlorophytum hypoxiforme, commonly known as the snake plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow markings. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps over time.

Chlorophytum hypoxiforme is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
