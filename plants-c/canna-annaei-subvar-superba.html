Canna annaei subvar. superba

Canna annaei subvar. superba, commonly known as the giant canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow center. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, providing a dramatic visual impact. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Canna annaei subvar. superba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
