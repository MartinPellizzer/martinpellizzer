Columnea sulcata

Columnea sulcata, commonly known as the zigzag columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive wavy or zigzag margin, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Columnea sulcata is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its ornamental value and ease of care.
