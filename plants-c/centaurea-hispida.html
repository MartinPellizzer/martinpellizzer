Centaurea hispida

Centaurea hispida, commonly known as hispid centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves covered in fine, bristly hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are bright yellow with red or purple markings, forming dense, spike-like clusters.

Centaurea hispida is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
