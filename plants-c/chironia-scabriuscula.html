Chironia scabriuscula

Chironia scabriuscula, commonly known as the rough-leaved chironia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features dark green, rough-textured leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

Chironia scabriuscula is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
