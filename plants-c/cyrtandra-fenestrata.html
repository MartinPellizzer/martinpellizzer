Cyrtandra fenestrata

Cyrtandra fenestrata, commonly known as the windowleaf heliconia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features large, elongated leaves with distinctive fenestrations, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Cyrtandra fenestrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
