Centaurea caprina

Centaurea caprina, commonly known as goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery appearance with pale blue to purple flowers. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats.

Centaurea caprina is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
