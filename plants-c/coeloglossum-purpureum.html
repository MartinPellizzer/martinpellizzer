Coeloglossum purpureum

Coeloglossum purpureum, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a slender, upright stem with few leaves and produces numerous small, fragrant flowers in a dense spike. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Coeloglossum purpureum is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
