Coprosma pubens var. typica

Coprosma pubens var. typica, commonly known as the silver coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, urn-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Coprosma pubens var. typica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
