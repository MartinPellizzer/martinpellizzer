Cabralea

Cabralea, commonly known as the "butterfly tree," is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble butterflies, hence its common name. Cabralea species are typically small trees or shrubs with compound leaves and pod fruits.

These plants are often found in moist, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy.
