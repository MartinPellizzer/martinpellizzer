Calophyllum confusum

Calophyllum confusum, commonly known as the confusum flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its ornamental, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually striking plant. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Calophyllum confusum is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes.
