Cynorkis ringens

Cynorkis ringens, commonly known as the ringed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa.

It typically grows in grassland habitats and is characterized by its unique, ring-like structure in its flower. The plant has a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive coloration. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with red or purple markings, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Cynorkis ringens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
