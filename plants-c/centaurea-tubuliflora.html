Centaurea tubuliflora

Centaurea tubuliflora, commonly known as the tubular fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with tubular central florets surrounded by ray florets.

Centaurea tubuliflora is often found in disturbed areas and is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
