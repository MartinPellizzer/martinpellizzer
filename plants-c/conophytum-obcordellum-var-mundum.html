Conophytum obcordellum var. mundum

Conophytum obcordellum var. mundum, commonly known as the "Fairy Raincoat," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces yellow flowers during the winter months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
