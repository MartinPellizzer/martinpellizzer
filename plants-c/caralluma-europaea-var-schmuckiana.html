Caralluma europaea var. schmuckiana

Caralluma europaea var. schmuckiana, commonly known as the Schmuck's devil's backbone, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe.

It features spiny, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple, and it produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
