Campanula Napuligera F. Minima

Campanula napuligera f. minima is a rare and delicate flowering plant known for its small, bell-shaped blooms. Native to the alpine regions of southern Italy, this variety thrives in cool, moist environments. Its compact size makes it a favorite among gardeners seeking unique, low-maintenance plants. The plant's flowers come in shades of blue and violet, adding a touch of elegance to any garden. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of specialized horticultural circles.
