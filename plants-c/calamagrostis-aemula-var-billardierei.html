Calamagrostis aemula var. billardierei

Calamagrostis aemula var. billardierei, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that produce feathery, silver-blue plumes during the flowering season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its elegant appearance.

This variety is particularly valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract wildlife.
