Clethra alnifolia var. glabella

Clethra alnifolia var. glabella, commonly known as sweet pepperbush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
