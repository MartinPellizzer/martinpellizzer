Clematis fusca var. ajanensis

Clematis fusca var. ajanensis, commonly known as the Ajanensis Clematis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are often covered in fine, hairy hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
