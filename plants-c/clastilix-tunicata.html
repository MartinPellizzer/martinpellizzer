Clastilix tunicata

Clastilix tunicata, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Clastilix tunicata is an annual herb that plays a role in both ecological and agricultural systems.
