Crotalaria stipularia var. oblongata

Crotalaria stipularia var. oblongata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spurred cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features distinctive spurred petals and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, giving it a unique appearance.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
