Centaurea pectinata var. fuscata

Centaurea pectinata var. fuscata, commonly known as the dark centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, spiny bracts that enclose its yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped, gray-green leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
