Ctenitis pallens

Ctenitis pallens, commonly known as the pale ctenitis, is a species of fern belonging to the family Todeaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically light green to pale yellow in color. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to moist, shaded environments.

Ctenitis pallens thrives in humid, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories.
