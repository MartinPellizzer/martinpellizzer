Cryptocoryne × zukalii nothovar. zukalii

Cryptocoryne × zukalii nothovar. zukalii, commonly known as the Zukalii Cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a subtle metallic sheen. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires stable water parameters. It is often cultivated in freshwater aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Its growth is relatively slow, making it suitable for smaller to medium-sized tanks.
