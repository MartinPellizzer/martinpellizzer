Conyza Welwitschii

Conyza welwitschii is a flowering plant native to southern Africa known for its striking appearance and ecological significance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant features small white flowers and hairy leaves, making it easily identifiable in its natural habitat. It plays a vital role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining ecosystem balance. Due to its unique characteristics, it is often studied for its potential in environmental conservation and botanical research.
