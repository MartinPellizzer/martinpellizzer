Clematis jialasaensis

Clematis jialasaensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are typically compound, with serrated edges, and it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
