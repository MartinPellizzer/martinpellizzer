Colocasia mucronata

Colocasia mucronata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white, yellow, or cream patterns. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spadices, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

Colocasia mucronata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.
