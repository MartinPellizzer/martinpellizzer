Centaurea boissieri var. atlantica

Centaurea boissieri var. atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic centaury, is a flowering plant native to the western Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and blue to violet flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, bristly seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
