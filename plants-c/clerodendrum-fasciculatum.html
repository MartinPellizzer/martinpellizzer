Clerodendrum fasciculatum

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
