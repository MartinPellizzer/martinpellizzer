Crocodilodes corymbosa

Crocodilodes corymbosa, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has a straight, slender trunk with smooth, greyish bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate in shape, with a glossy green surface.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
