Cattleya × rossii

Cattleya × rossii, commonly known as the Rosii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with a distinctive purple or red lip. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for floral arrangements. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
