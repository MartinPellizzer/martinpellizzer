Clerodendrum dembianense

Clerodendrum dembianense, commonly known as the Dembian flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
