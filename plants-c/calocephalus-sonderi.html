Calocephalus sonderi

Calocephalus sonderi, commonly known as the red sandrat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Calocephalus sonderi is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
