Ceterach Aureum

Ceterach aureum is a rare and delicate seaweed species found in the coastal waters of southern Australia. It thrives in rocky intertidal zones where it clings to moist surfaces with its fibrous holdfast. This plant has a golden-yellow color that shimmers in the sunlight, making it visually striking. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes and pollution. Its conservation is crucial for maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat.
