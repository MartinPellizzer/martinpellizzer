Clematis gulinensis

Clematis gulinensis, commonly known as the Guangzhou clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and blue, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fibrous roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Clematis gulinensis thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
