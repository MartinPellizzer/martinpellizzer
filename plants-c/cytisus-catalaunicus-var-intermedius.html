Cytisus catalaunicus var. intermedius

Cytisus catalaunicus var. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are typical of the Genisteae tribe. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and scrubland habitats.

Its growth habit is compact, with woody stems and small, trifoliate leaves.
