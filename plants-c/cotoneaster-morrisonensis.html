Cotoneaster morrisonensis

Cotoneaster morrisonensis, commonly known as Morrison's cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, red berries that appear in the fall. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy upper surface.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
