Crepidium celebicum

Crepidium celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Indonesian island of Celebes.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for study in tropical botany.
