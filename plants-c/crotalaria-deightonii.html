Crotalaria deightonii

Crotalaria deightonii, commonly known as the African bladder nut, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is known for its erect, branching stems. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the flowers are often clustered in racemes.

Crotalaria deightonii is also noted for its seeds, which are encased in thick, leathery pods.
