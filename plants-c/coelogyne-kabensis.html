Coelogyne kabensis

Coelogyne kabensis, commonly known as the Kabense Cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

Coelogyne kabensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
