Ctenitis subdryopteris

Ctenitis subdryopteris, commonly known as the narrow-veined rock fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, finely divided structure. Ctenitis subdryopteris is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
