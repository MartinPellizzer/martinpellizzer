Combretum benthamianum

Combretum benthamianum, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its bark is smooth and reddish in color.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits, and it also serves as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
