Cumminsia

Cumminsia, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Cumminsia thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
