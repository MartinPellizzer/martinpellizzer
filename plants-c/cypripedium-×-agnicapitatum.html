Cypripedium × agnicapitatum

Cypripedium × agnicapitatum, commonly known as the fire-lily or flame orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a deep red or orange hue and a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments and has a sympodial growth habit. Its leaves are broad and leathery, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Cypripedium × agnicapitatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
