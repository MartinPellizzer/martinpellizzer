Cynorkis catatii

Cynorkis catatii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a compact rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinct red or orange lip. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high light exposure.

Cynorkis catatii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
