Cymbidium kanran f. viridescens

Cymbidium kanran f. viridescens, commonly known as the green-flowered kanran orchid, is a hybrid cultivar of the Cymbidium genus native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking greenish-white to yellowish-green flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows as an upright herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light exposure, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and is often used in traditional Chinese horticulture and floral arrangements.
