Crassula moschata

Crassula moschata, commonly known as the sweet scented crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its aromatic leaves and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
