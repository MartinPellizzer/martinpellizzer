Centaurea razgradensis

Centaurea razgradensis, commonly known as the Razgrad centaury, is a flowering plant native to Bulgaria.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its compact, upright growth habit. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, bracteate flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
