Croton loretensis

Croton loretensis, commonly known as Loretto's croton, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, often with a striking red or purple coloration. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Croton loretensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
