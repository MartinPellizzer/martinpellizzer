Cyclamen tunetanum

Cyclamen tunetanum, commonly known as the Tunisian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant prefers cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental species. Its tuberous roots allow it to survive in adverse conditions, making it resilient in certain climates.

Cyclamen tunetanum is well-suited for indoor cultivation and adds a delicate touch to gardens and pots.
