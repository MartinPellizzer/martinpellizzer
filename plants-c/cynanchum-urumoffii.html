Cynanchum urumoffii

Cynanchum urumoffii, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed vine, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, climbing stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Cynanchum urumoffii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
