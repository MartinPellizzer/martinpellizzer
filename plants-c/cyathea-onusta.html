Cyathea onusta

Cyathea onusta, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome that supports its tall stature, and its fronds are often adorned with a distinctive, waxy coating. Cyathea onusta thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
