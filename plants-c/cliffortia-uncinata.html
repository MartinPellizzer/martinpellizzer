Cliffortia uncinata

Cliffortia uncinata, commonly known as the hooked cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
