Croton scabiosus

Croton scabiosus, commonly known as the rough croton, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features distinctive, palmately lobed leaves with a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Croton scabiosus is often found in prairies, open woodlands, and along roadsides.
