Centropogon brachyandrus

Centropogon brachyandrus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centropogon, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Centropogon brachyandrus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
