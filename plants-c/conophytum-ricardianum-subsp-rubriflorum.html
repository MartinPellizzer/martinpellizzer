Conophytum ricardianum subsp. rubriflorum

Conophytum ricardianum subsp.

rubriflorum, commonly known as the red-flowered conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, rounded leaves that store water.
