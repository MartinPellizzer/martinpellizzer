Cardamine tenuifolia f. parviflora

Cardamine tenuifolia f. parviflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved watercress, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. This variety is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to thrive in humid, temperate climates.
