Calliandra Speciosa

Showy calliandra, scientifically known as Calliandra speciosa, is a striking tropical shrub native to South America. This plant is celebrated for its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its feathery foliage and long-lasting blooms add both visual appeal and ecological value to the landscape. The plant thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often used in landscaping for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. With its unique appearance and hardy nature, Showy calliandra is a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
