Cistus linearis

Cistus linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas. It has a woody stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Cistus linearis is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine and perfumery.
