Clematis stans

Clematis stans, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach lengths of up to 15 feet. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis stans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be used in gardens to cover trellises or walls.
