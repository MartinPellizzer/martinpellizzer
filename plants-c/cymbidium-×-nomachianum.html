Cymbidium × nomachianum

Cymbidium × nomachianum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a popular species within the orchid family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant is native to regions of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, where it thrives in temperate climates. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, red, and yellow, making it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts.

It requires well-draining soil, bright indirect light, and consistent moisture to flourish.
