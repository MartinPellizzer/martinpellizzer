Clematis wallichii

Clematis wallichii, commonly known as the Kashmir clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the Himalayas.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and blue, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, hairy pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Clematis wallichii is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to climb trellises or walls.
