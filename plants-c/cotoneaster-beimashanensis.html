Cotoneaster beimashanensis

Cotoneaster beimashanensis, commonly known as the Bei Mountains cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
