Cattleya × duveenii

Cattleya × duveenii, commonly known as the Duveen Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to white with intricate patterns. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is highly prized for its beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
