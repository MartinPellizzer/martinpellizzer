Conyza alata

Conyza alata, commonly known as the common weed or goatweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in various habitats across temperate regions. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, white to yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive, serrated edge.

Conyza alata is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through wind-dispersed seeds.
