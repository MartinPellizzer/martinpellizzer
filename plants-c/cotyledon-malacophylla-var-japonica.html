Cotyledon malacophylla var. japonica

Cotyledon malacophylla var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese copperleaf, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, which become more pronounced in cooler temperatures. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

Cotyledon malacophylla var. japonica prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.
