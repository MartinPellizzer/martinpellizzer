Cleistogenes songorica var. hognochanica

Cleistogenes songorica var. hognochanica, commonly known as the Hognochanic grass, is a perennial C4 grass species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its resilience and adaptability, it is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.
