Cytisus welwitschii var. gallecicus

Cytisus welwitschii var. gallecicus, commonly known as the Cape rose bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to its habitat. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically green. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
