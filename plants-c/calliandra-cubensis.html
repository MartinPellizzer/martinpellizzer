Calliandra cubensis

Calliandra cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban calliandra, is a leguminous shrub native to the Caribbean.

It features compound leaves with numerous small, delicate leaflets and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and yellow. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its striking appearance. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Calliandra cubensis also has the ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
