Croton glandulosus var. croftiae

Croton glandulosus var. croftiae, commonly known as the Croton croftiae, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its succulent-like leaves help it retain moisture in its native arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
