Circaea alpina var. himalaica

Circaea alpina var. himalaica, commonly known as the Himalayan mouse-ear, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers.
