Citrus × bigaradia var. cyathifera

Citrus × bigaradia var. cyathifera, commonly known as the sour orange.

This variety is characterized by its small, yellow-orange fruits that are typically bitter and not suitable for fresh consumption. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces fragrant white flowers. It is a hybrid citrus species that is often used in the cultivation of other citrus varieties due to its hardy nature.

The fruit is primarily used for its juice and as a rootstock in citrus grafting.
