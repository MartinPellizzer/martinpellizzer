Cattleya chocoensis

Cattleya chocoensis, commonly known as the Chocoan Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its humid, shaded environment. Its leaves are broad and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Cattleya chocoensis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
