Ctenanthe lubbersiana

Ctenanthe lubbersiana, commonly known as the Black Velvet Ctenanthe, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark, velvety foliage with striking green veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath its leafy bracts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
