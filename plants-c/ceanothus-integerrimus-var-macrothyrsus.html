Ceanothus integerrimus var. macrothyrsus

Ceanothus integerrimus var. macrothyrsus, commonly known as the coastal ceanothus, is a shrub native to coastal regions of California.

It features aromatic, evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy clusters of blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal climates with high salinity and strong winds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
