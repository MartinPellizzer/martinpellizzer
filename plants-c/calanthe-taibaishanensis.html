Calanthe taibaishanensis

Calanthe taibaishanensis, commonly known as the Taibaishan calanthe, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments at high altitudes. Its blooming period occurs in late spring to early summer, and it is prized for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native region.
