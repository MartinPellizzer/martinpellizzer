Calochortus Luteus Var. Citrinus

Calochortus luteus var. citrinus is a striking member of the amaryllis family known for its vibrant yellow blooms. Native to the western United States, this plant thrives in rocky soils and sunny environments. Its unique coloration and delicate petals make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. The variety citrinus stands out for its intense golden hue, setting it apart from other calochortus species. With proper care, this plant can flourish and add a splash of color to any landscape.
