Croton dogsoniae

Croton dogsoniae, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red or yellow. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
