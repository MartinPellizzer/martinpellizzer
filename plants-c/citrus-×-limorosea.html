Citrus × limorosea

Citrus × limorosea, commonly known as the sour orange.

It is a hybrid citrus plant resulting from the cross between the mandarin and the pummelo. The tree is small to medium in size, with thick, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is typically small, round, and has a thick, bitter rind.

The plant is widely used in the production of citric acid and as a rootstock for other citrus varieties.
