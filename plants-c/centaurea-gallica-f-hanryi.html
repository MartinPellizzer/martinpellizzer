Centaurea gallica f. hanryi

Centaurea gallica f. hanryi, commonly known as the French centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers with a distinctive central disk, and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in Mediterranean regions.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
