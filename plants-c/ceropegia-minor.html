Ceropegia minor

Ceropegia minor, commonly known as the dwarf string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and ornamental foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
