Cynoglossum laxiflorum

Cynoglossum laxiflorum, commonly known as the looseflower mouse-eared germander, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

Cynoglossum laxiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported healing properties.
