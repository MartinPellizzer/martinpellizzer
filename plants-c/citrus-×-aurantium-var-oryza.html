Citrus × aurantium var. oryza

Citrus × aurantium var. oryza, commonly known as pomelo, is a citrus fruit native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, thick-skinned fruit with a sweet, mild flavor. The plant is a small evergreen tree with fragrant white flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. Its fruit is often used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

The variety is valued for its high water content and nutritional benefits.
