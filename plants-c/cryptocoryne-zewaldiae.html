Cryptocoryne zewaldiae

Cryptocoryne zewaldiae, commonly known as the Zewald's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with subtle white or yellow veins. This plant thrives in low-light conditions and is well-suited for planted tanks with soft, acidic water. Its rhizomes spread along the substrate, allowing it to form dense underwater carpets.

Cryptocoryne zewaldiae is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in aquarium settings.
