Coleus xanthanthus

Coleus xanthanthus, commonly known as the golden coleus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with shades of yellow, green, and red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly textured surface. This plant thrives in partial to full sunlight and prefers well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking leaf coloration and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
