Craterostigma Nanum

Craterostigma nanum is a small, resilient plant native to southern Africa. It thrives in harsh environments, including rocky soils and arid regions. This species is known for its ability to survive extreme drought conditions. Its compact size and unique leaf structure make it a fascinating subject for botanical study. Despite its modest appearance, it holds significant ecological value in its native habitat.
