Croton ericius

Croton ericius, commonly known as the silverleaf croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a mix of green, white, and silver hues. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
