Cattleya labiata var. reineckeana

Cattleya labiata var. reineckeana, commonly known as the Reinecke's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and covered in a waxy coating.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty in both greenhouses and gardens.
