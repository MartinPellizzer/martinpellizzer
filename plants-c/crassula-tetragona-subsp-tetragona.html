Crassula tetragona subsp. tetragona

Crassula tetragona subsp.

tetragona, commonly known as the Four-angled Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a distinctive four-sided, columnar shape.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
