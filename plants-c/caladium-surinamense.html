Caladium surinamense

Caladium surinamense, commonly known as the Surinam arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can be green, red, or variegated in color. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.

Caladium surinamense prefers partial to full shade and is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.
