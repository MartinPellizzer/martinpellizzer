Conyza messerii

Conyza messerii, commonly known as Messer's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as deserts and rocky soils, and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Conyza messerii plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a food source for certain insects and animals.
