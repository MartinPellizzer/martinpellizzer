Cliffortia nitidula

Cliffortia nitidula, commonly known as the smooth cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cliffortia nitidula is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in full sun.
