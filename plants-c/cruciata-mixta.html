Cruciata mixta

Cruciata mixta, commonly known as the hybrid crosswort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight hairy texture.

Cruciata mixta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
