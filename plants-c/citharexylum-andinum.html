Citharexylum andinum

Citharexylum andinum, commonly known as the Andean citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, montane forests. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
