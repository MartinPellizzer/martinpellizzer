Campelia mexicana

Campelia mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican trumpet tree, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
