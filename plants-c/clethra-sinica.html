Clethra sinica

Clethra sinica, commonly known as Chinese clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Clethra sinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
