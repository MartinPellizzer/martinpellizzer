Cyperus conceptionis

Cyperus conceptionis, commonly known as the Conceptionis flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the sedge family, Cyperaceae, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown, often subtended by bracts. Cyperus conceptionis thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and marshes.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland restoration projects.
