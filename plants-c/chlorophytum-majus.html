Chlorophytum majus

Chlorophytum majus, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, grass-like flowers that are usually white or pale green in color. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in a variety of light conditions.

Chlorophytum majus is also known for its ability to produce offsets, making it easy to propagate.
