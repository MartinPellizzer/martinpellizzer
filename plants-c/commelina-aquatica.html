Commelina aquatica

Commelina aquatica, commonly known as watercommelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds and streams. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, blue or white flowers with three petals. It is often found in shallow water or moist soil, and it can form dense colonies.

Commelina aquatica is a member of the Commelinaceae family, which includes many aquatic and semi-aquatic plants.
