Cnestis calantha

Cnestis calantha, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a low shrub with upright stems and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Cnestis calantha is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
