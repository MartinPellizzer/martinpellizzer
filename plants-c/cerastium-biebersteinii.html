Cerastium biebersteinii

Cerastium biebersteinii, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, rounded leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Cerastium biebersteinii is often used in alpine gardens for its hardiness and ornamental value.
