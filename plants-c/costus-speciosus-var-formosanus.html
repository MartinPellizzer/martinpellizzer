Costus speciosus var. formosanus

Costus speciosus var. formosanus, commonly known as the Taiwan costus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking, feather-like leaves that are often green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
