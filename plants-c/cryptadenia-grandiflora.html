Cryptadenia grandiflora

Cryptadenia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered cryptadenia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils within subtropical and temperate rainforests. The plant features elliptical to ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. Its most distinctive feature is the showy, white or pale pink flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that is edible and often consumed by native animals.
