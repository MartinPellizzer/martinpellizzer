Cryptanthus × osyanus

Cryptanthus × osyanus, commonly known as the rainbow plant or zebra plant, is a hybrid species within the genus Cryptanthus.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant features dense, grass-like rosettes of leaves that display a wide range of hues, including red, orange, yellow, and green. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, which appear in late summer.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
