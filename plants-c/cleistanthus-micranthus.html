Cleistanthus micranthus

Cleistanthus micranthus, commonly known as the small-flowered cleistanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks.
