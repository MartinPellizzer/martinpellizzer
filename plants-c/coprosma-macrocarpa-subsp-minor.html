Coprosma macrocarpa subsp. minor

Coprosma macrocarpa subsp.

minor, commonly known as the small-fruited coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that ranges from dark green to reddish hues.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate urban environments.
