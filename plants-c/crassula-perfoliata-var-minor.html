Crassula perfoliata var. minor

Crassula perfoliata var. minor, commonly known as the "Baby Blue Plant," is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are blue-green in color, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
