Conophytum koupense

Conophytum koupense, commonly known as the Koupense rock fig, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded body and fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Conophytum koupense is often grown as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
