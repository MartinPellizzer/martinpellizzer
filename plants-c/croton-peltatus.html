Croton peltatus

Croton peltatus, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of soft, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
