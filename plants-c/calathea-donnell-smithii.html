Calathea donnell-smithii

Calathea donnell-smithii, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage that changes color in response to light conditions. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular houseplant among foliage enthusiasts.
