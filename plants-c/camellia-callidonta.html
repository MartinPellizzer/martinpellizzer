Camellia callidonta

Camellia callidonta, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Camellia callidonta is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
