Callista macrantha

Callista macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces numerous small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Callista macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
