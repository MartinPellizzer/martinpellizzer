Calophyllum tacamahaca var. parviflorum

Calophyllum tacamahaca var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered torch tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers followed by orange-red drupes. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
