Crotalaria spartea

Crotalaria spartea, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and hairy, ovate leaves. The plant is native to tropical Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates. It has a short growth cycle and is often cultivated for its seeds, which are used in traditional medicine.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
