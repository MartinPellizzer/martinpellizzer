Cineraria coronata

Cineraria coronata, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its evergreen, succulent-like leaves and vibrant blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy, dandelion-like blooms. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Cineraria coronata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
