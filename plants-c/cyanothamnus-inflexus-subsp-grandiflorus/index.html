Cyanothamnus Inflexus Subsp. Grandiflorus

Blue everlasting, scientifically known as Cyanothamnus inflexus subsp. grandiflorus, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. Its name derives from the vivid blue color of its flowers and its tendency to remain in bloom throughout the year. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its hardy nature and visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a versatile choice for coastal and semi-arid environments. With its unique appearance and resilience, Blue everlasting is a valuable addition to native plant collections.
