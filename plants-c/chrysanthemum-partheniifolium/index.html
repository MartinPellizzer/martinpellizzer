Chrysanthemum Partheniifolium

Parthenium chrysanthemum, scientifically known as Chrysanthemum partheniifolium, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to the Mediterranean region. This flowering plant is admired for its vibrant yellow to orange blooms that resemble tiny suns, often appearing in clusters along its slender stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. With its delicate yet resilient nature, it has been cultivated for both ornamental and medicinal purposes throughout history. Despite its beauty, it can sometimes become invasive in certain regions, requiring careful management to prevent overgrowth.
