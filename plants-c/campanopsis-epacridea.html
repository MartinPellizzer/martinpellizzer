Campanopsis epacridea

Campanopsis epacridea, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tubular, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in woodland and rainforest habitats. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Campanopsis epacridea is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
