Ceropegia lawii var. maccannii

Ceropegia lawii var. maccannii, commonly known as the Maccannii String of Hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces a unique type of flower known as a ceropegia, which is adapted for pollination by specific insects. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
