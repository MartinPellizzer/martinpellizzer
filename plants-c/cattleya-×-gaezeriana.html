Cattleya × gaezeriana

Cattleya × gaezeriana, commonly known as the Gaezerian Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species originating from crosses between Cattleya mossiae and Cattleya bicolor.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and often feature a distinctive purple or red lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, and it prefers bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Cattleya × gaezeriana is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
