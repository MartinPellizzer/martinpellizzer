Centrosema dubium

Centrosema dubium, commonly known as the small-flowered centrosema, is a tropical legume native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a climbing habit and produces small, elliptical pods containing several seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

Centrosema dubium is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and forage production.
