Clerodendrum varium

Clerodendrum varium, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow or green center. The plant produces small, green, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are typically opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.
