Camellia liberistyla

Camellia liberistyla, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Camellia liberistyla is also valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
