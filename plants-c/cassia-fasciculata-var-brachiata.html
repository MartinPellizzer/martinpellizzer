Cassia fasciculata var. brachiata

Cassia fasciculata var. brachiata, commonly known as golden shower tree or *Cassia* brachiata, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are alternate, compound, and often have a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, three-lobed pods that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
