Chrysanthemum lavandulifolium var. lavandulifolium

Chrysanthemum lavandulifolium var. lavandulifolium, commonly known as the lavenderleaf chrysanthemum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features finely divided, silver-gray foliage that resembles lavender leaves, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and long-lasting blooms.
