Clusia chiribiquetensis

Clusia chiribiquetensis, commonly known as the Chiribiquet glory, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Chiribiquet National Park in Colombia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the unique microclimates of its rainforest habitat.

Clusia chiribiquetensis is also noted for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and its potential use in horticulture.
