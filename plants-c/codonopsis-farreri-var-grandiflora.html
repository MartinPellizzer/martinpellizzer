Codonopsis farreri var. grandiflora

Codonopsis farreri var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered Chinese bellflower, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

The plant belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
