Chrysanthemum argenteum

Chrysanthemum argenteum, commonly known as the silverleaf chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety leaves and showy yellow to orange flower heads. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for garden landscapes and floral arrangements.
