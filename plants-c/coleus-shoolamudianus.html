Coleus shoolamudianus

Coleus shoolamudianus, commonly known as the Shoolamudian coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are often heart-shaped with a velvety texture and display a wide range of colors, including red, purple, and green.

It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
