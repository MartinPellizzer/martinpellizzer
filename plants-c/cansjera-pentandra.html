Cansjera pentandra

Cansjera pentandra, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Cansjera pentandra is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
