Coleus guerkei

Coleus guerkei, commonly known as the Guerke's coleus, is a species of flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant. It typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Coleus guerkei prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in warm, humid climates.
