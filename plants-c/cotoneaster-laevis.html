Cotoneaster laevis

Cotoneaster laevis, commonly known as the smooth cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are smooth and glossy, typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Cotoneaster laevis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also valued for its role in wildlife habitats, providing food for birds and other small animals.
