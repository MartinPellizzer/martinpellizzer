Centaurea polyphylla

Centaurea polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leafed centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright blue or purple flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Centaurea polyphylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
