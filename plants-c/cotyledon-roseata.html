Cotyledon roseata

Cotyledon roseata, commonly known as the roseleaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is known for its compact growth habit and ability to store water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

Cotyledon roseata thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
