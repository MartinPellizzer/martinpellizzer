Codonoboea serrata

Codonoboea serrata, commonly known as the serrated codonopsis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. Its flowers are typically clustered in umbels, featuring showy, tubular corollas in shades of yellow to orange.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
