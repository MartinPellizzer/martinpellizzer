Cucumis rapallito

Cucumis rapallito, commonly known as the small cucumber or wild cucumber, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically less than 5 centimeters in length. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture, and it has tendrils that help it climb.

The flowers are yellow and are followed by the development of its characteristic small, green fruits.
