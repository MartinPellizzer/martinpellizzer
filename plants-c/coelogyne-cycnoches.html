Coelogyne cycnoches

Coelogyne cycnoches, commonly known as the black orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals and sepals, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of purple and black. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Coelogyne cycnoches is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique coloration.
