Cybianthus densicoma var. grandifolius

Cybianthus densicoma var. grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf Cybianthus, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are typically broad and leathery, providing a striking visual contrast to the bright blooms. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various climate conditions.
