Calibrachoa heterophylla

Calibrachoa heterophylla, commonly known as the variable calibrachoa, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina, and thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Calibrachoa heterophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and containers due to its vibrant blooms and compact size.
