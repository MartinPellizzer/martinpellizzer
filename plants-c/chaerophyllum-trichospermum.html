Chaerophyllum trichospermum

Chaerophyllum trichospermum, commonly known as the hairy cow parsley, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small white or greenish flowers arranged in umbels. Its botanical features make it a common weed in disturbed soils and a valuable plant for ecological studies.
