Crotalaria colorata subsp. erecta

Crotalaria colorata subsp.

erecta, commonly known as the upright crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
