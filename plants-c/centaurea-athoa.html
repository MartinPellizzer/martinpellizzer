Centaurea athoa

Centaurea athoa, commonly known as the Athos thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, leafy stems and striking purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Centaurea athoa is known for its hardy nature and is often used in ornamental gardening.
