Ceanothus thyrsiflorus var. macrothyrsus

Ceanothus thyrsiflorus var. macrothyrsus, commonly known as the tall ceanothus, is a shrub native to California.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy clusters of blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
