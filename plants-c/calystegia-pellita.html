Calystegia pellita

Calystegia pellita, commonly known as the slender bindweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that grows along the ground, producing trailing stems that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Calystegia pellita is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain areas.
