Cymbidium graminoides

Cymbidium graminoides, commonly known as the grass orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its grass-like foliage and slender, often pendulous flower spikes. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium graminoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
