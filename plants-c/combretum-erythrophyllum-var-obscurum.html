Combretum erythrophyllum var. obscurum

Combretum erythrophyllum var. obscurum, commonly known as the dark-leaved flame bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its striking, showy red flowers that appear in clusters. The leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a somewhat waxy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adapted to well-drained soils.

This plant is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its vibrant floral display and hardy nature.
