Camellia furfuracea var. yaoshanica

Camellia furfuracea var. yaoshanica, commonly known as the Yaoshan camellia, is a rare and ornamental evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink, often with a distinctive yellow or white base. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are glossy and provide a striking contrast to its blooms. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized horticultural collections.
