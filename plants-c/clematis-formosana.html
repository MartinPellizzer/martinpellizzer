Clematis formosana

Clematis formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan Clematis, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a deciduous vine that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis formosana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant blooms.
