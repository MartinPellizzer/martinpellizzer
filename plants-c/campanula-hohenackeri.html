Campanula hohenackeri

Campanula hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Campanula hohenackeri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
