Casearia sanchezii

Casearia sanchezii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.

Casearia sanchezii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
