Cyclanthera hystrix

Cyclanthera hystrix, commonly known as the spiny cucumber, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbit plants like melons and gourds. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and heart-shaped, lobed leaves. Its fruits are elongated, angular, and covered in rough, spiny projections.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
