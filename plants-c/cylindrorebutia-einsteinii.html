Cylindrorebutia einsteinii

Cylindrorebutia einsteinii, commonly known as the Einstein's cylindrical rebutia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and can store water, a characteristic typical of many succulents. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
