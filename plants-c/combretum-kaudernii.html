Combretum kaudernii

Combretum kaudernii, commonly known as the red-flowered flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in arid and semi-arid regions.

Combretum kaudernii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a garden plant.
