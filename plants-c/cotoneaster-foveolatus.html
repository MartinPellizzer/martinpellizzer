Cotoneaster foveolatus

Cotoneaster foveolatus, commonly known as the smooth cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and have a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster foveolatus is also valued for its use in wildlife habitats and as a ground cover plant.
