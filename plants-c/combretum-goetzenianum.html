Combretum goetzenianum

Combretum goetzenianum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its bark is smooth and grayish in color. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes planted in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
