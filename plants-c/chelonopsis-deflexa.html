Chelonopsis deflexa

Chelonopsis deflexa, commonly known as the flexed chelonopsis, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat.

Chelonopsis deflexa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and resilience to drought.
