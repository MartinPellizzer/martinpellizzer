Chaenorhinum origanifolium f. genuinum

Chaenorhinum origanifolium f. genuinum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallflower, is a compact, annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom from late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive, rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
