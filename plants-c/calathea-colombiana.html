Calathea colombiana

Calathea colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns and contrasting colors. The leaves are typically broad and have a velvety texture, often with a waxy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
