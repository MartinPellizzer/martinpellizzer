Cliffortia polygonifolia var. polygonifolia

Cliffortia polygonifolia var. polygonifolia, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, blooming throughout the warmer months.
