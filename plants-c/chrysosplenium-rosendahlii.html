Chrysosplenium rosendahlii

Chrysosplenium rosendahlii, commonly known as the Rosendahl's golden spleenwort, is a species of flowering plant in the family Spleenworts.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features delicate, alternate leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually greenish-yellow in color, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.
