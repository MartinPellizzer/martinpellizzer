Commersonia hermanniifolia

Commersonia hermanniifolia, commonly known as the Hermann's rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters. Its leaves are narrow, fleshy, and often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
