Coelogyne mooreana

Coelogyne mooreana, commonly known as the Moore's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and red. The plant typically blooms in late winter or early spring, depending on its growing conditions. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

Coelogyne mooreana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
