Cremanthodium reniforme

Cremanthodium reniforme, commonly known as the kidneyleaf sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that are often kidney-shaped.
