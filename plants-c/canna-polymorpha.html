Canna polymorpha

Canna polymorpha, commonly known as the giant cannas, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, and its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, typically growing from rhizomes, and it thrives in moist, sunny environments. Its foliage and flowers make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.

Canna polymorpha is also known for its ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions, making it relatively easy to cultivate.
