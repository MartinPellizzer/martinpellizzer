Croton nipensis

Croton nipensis, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that often display vibrant red, orange, or yellow hues. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its colorful foliage.
