Cryptocoryne palawanensis

Cryptocoryne palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to the freshwater habitats of the Philippines.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is often found in slow-moving or still waters. It is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne palawanensis is also notable for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced aquarists.
