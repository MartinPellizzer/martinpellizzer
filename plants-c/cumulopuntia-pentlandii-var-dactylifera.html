Cumulopuntia pentlandii var. dactylifera

Cumulopuntia pentlandii var. dactylifera, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a succulent plant native to Brazil.

It features flat, segmented stems that are often adorned with vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has a distinctive branching pattern and produces offsets that can be propagated easily. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its blooming season typically occurs during the holiday season, making it a popular ornamental plant.
