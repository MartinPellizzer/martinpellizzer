Chamaecyparis Obtusa F. Crippsii

Chamaecyparis obtusa f. crippsii is a rare and ornamental variant of the Japanese cedar known for its unique foliage. This form features dense, dark green needles that are often curled and twisted, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is prized for its compact growth habit. The plant is a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded landscapes due to its hardiness and visual appeal. Its unusual form makes it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts and horticulturists.
