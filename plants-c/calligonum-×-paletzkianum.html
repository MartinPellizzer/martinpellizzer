Calligonum × paletzkianum

Calligonum × paletzkianum, commonly known as the hybrid sea buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a cross between Calligonum comosum and Calligonum roborovskii, resulting in a plant with adaptability to harsh environmental conditions. The species is characterized by its dense, thorny growth and yellow-orange berries that are rich in vitamin C. It thrives in saline and alkaline soils, making it a valuable plant for reclaiming degraded lands.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological restoration projects in arid environments.
