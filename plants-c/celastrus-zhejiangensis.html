Celastrus zhejiangensis

Celastrus zhejiangensis, commonly known as Zhejiang staffvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
