Cypripedium × amesianum grandiflorum

Cypripedium × amesianum grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, slipper-shaped pouch that is typically pink or white in color. The plant has a single, erect stem that bears a single, large flower with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and requires well-drained soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
