Corylus colurna var. bachycarpa

Corylus colurna var. bachycarpa, commonly known as the Turkish hazelnut, is a variant of the European hazelnut.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the eastern Mediterranean region. The plant produces small, edible nuts enclosed in a spiny husk. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated edges.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and unique fruiting characteristics.
