Crataegus × Canescens

Crataegus × canescens, commonly known as the sand cherry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the rose family. It is native to eastern North America and thrives in sandy or well-drained soils. This shrub is valued for its ornamental qualities, featuring showy white or pink flowers in spring. Its hardy nature makes it adaptable to various garden conditions. The plant also has historical uses in traditional medicine and landscaping.
