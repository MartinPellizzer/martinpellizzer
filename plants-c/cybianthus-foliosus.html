Cybianthus foliosus

Cybianthus foliosus, commonly known as the leafy cybianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Cybianthus foliosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
