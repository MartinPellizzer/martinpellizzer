Chusquea swallenii

Chusquea swallenii, commonly known as the Swallen's bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in moist, shaded environments at higher elevations.
