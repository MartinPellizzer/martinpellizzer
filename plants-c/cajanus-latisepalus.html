Cajanus latisepalus

Cajanus latisepalus, commonly known as the broad-sepal clusterbean, is a species of legume native to India.

It is characterized by its distinctive broad, showy sepals that give it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and it produces small, rounded pods that contain several seeds.

Cajanus latisepalus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
