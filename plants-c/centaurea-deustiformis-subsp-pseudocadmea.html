Centaurea deustiformis subsp. pseudocadmea

Centaurea deustiformis subsp.

pseudocadmea, commonly known as the false cadmean centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or limestone soils, often in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It produces slender, hairy leaves and has a compact growth habit, making it well adapted to its arid environment.
