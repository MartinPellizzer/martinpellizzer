Cybianthus pseudoicacoreus

Cybianthus pseudoicacoreus, commonly known as the false ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among gardeners in dry climates.
