Cosmidium simplicifolium

Cosmidium simplicifolium, commonly known as the slender water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to tropical regions.

It has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in shallow, still freshwater environments such as ponds and marshes. Its fronds are usually floating or submerged, depending on the water conditions.

Cosmidium simplicifolium plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
