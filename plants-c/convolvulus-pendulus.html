Convolvulus pendulus

Convolvulus pendulus, commonly known as the trailing morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to blue, often opening in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it both ornamental and ecologically beneficial.
