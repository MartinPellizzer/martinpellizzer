Centaurea pichleri var. ancyrensis

Centaurea pichleri var. ancyrensis, commonly known as the Ancyrene thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features narrow, grayish-green leaves with spiny margins, and its flowers are bright yellow with purple-red bracts. It produces achenes, which are small, dry fruits that disperse seeds via the wind.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
