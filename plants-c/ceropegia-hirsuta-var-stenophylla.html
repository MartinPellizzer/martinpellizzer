Ceropegia hirsuta var. stenophylla

Ceropegia hirsuta var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its trailing stems and opposite, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, cascading foliage.
