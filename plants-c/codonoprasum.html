Codonoprasum

Codonoprasum, commonly known as sweet cicely, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white, umbel-shaped flowers. The plant produces aromatic seeds that are often used in culinary applications. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both gardens and herbal medicine.
