Cybianthus barbosae

Cybianthus barbosae, commonly known as the Barbossa's cybianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the island's unique climate.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
