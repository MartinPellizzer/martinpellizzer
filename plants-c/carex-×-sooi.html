Carex × sooi

Carex × sooi, commonly known as the hybrid sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grass-like leaves and spike-like flower clusters. This plant thrives in moist to wet environments, often found in marshes, pond edges, and other waterlogged habitats. Carex × sooi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.

Its reproductive strategy involves wind pollination, typical of many sedges.
