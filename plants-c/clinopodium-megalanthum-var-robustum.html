Clinopodium megalanthum var. robustum

Clinopodium megalanthum var. robustum, commonly known as mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high elevations, preferring full sun. Its leaves are often used in herbal medicine for their carminative and antispasmodic properties.

The species is notable for its resilience in harsh alpine environments.
