Clematis fawcettii var. colorata

Clematis fawcettii var. colorata, commonly known as the Fawcettii colorata Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and showy, large, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
