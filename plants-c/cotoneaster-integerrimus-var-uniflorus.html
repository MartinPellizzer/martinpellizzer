Cotoneaster integerrimus var. uniflorus

Cotoneaster integerrimus var. uniflorus, commonly known as the single-flowered cotoneaster, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to red in color, blooming in early spring. The plant produces orange to red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
