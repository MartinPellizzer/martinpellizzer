Chionanthus bumelioides subsp. cubensis

Chionanthus bumelioides subsp.

cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban whiteflower plant, is a flowering shrub native to Cuba. It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white, pendulous flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.
