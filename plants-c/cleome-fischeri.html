Cleome fischeri

Cleome fischeri, commonly known as the Mexican Marvel or spider flower, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. Cleome fischeri is an annual herb that thrives in warm climates and well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
