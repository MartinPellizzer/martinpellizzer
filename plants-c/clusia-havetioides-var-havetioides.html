Clusia havetioides var. havetioides

Clusia havetioides var. havetioides, commonly known as the evergreen shrub or broadleaf tree, is a species native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas or near water.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
