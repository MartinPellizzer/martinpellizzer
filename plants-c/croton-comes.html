Croton comes

Croton comes, commonly known as the croton plant, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful leaves that come in a variety of shapes and patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by capsule fruits. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
