Cynanchum × vernyi

Cynanchum × vernyi, commonly known as the hybrid milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, and its fruits are follicles that contain toxic seeds. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
