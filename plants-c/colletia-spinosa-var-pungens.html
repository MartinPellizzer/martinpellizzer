Colletia spinosa var. pungens

Colletia spinosa var. pungens, commonly known as the spiny colletia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are covered in sharp spines, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding a delicate contrast to its rugged appearance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in desert flora.
