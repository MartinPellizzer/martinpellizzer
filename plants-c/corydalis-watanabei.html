Corydalis watanabei

Corydalis watanabei, commonly known as the Japanese Corydalis, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces small, oval-shaped capsules as its fruit, containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
