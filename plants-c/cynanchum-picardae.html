Cynanchum picardae

Cynanchum picardae, commonly known as the Picard's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweed species. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Cynanchum picardae is known for its milky sap, which is a common feature among members of its family.
