Crassula browniana

Crassula browniana, commonly known as the Brown's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
