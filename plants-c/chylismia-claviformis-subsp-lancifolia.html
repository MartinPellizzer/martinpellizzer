Chylismia claviformis subsp. lancifolia

Chylismia claviformis subsp.

lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chylismia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
