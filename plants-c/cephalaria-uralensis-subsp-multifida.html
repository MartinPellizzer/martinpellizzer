Cephalaria uralensis subsp. multifida

Cephalaria uralensis subsp.

multifida, commonly known as the Ural cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant is distinguished by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
