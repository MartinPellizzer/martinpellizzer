Cnesmone javanica var. javanica

Cnesmone javanica var. javanica, commonly known as the Java cnesmone, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by cylindrical, woody fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
