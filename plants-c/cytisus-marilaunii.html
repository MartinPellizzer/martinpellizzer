Cytisus marilaunii

Cytisus marilaunii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-branched stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several narrow, linear leaflets. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Cytisus marilaunii is adapted to a Mediterranean climate and is relatively drought-tolerant.
