Croton erythrostachys var. muriculatus

Croton erythrostachys var. muriculatus, commonly known as the Crimson Spine Croton, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that range from deep red to orange and yellow, often with a distinctive spiny texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
