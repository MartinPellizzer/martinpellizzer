Cubanicula

Cubanicula, commonly known as the Cuban starflower, is a flowering plant native to the island of Cuba.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its star-shaped, blue to purple petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Cubanicula is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
