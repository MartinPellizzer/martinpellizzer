Cattleya mcmorlandii

Cattleya mcmorlandii, commonly known as the McMorland's Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
