Cuphea Ciliata

Whorled cuphea, scientifically known as Cuphea ciliata, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. With its vibrant, tubular flowers and unique whorled leaf arrangement, it stands out in any garden or landscape. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, sunny conditions. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for borders, containers, and hanging baskets. Whether grown for its visual appeal or its resilience, Whorled cuphea is a versatile and eye-catching addition to any plant collection.
