Camptostylus mannii

Camptostylus mannii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Camptostylus mannii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
