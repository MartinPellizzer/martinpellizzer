Caladium bicolor var. transparens

Caladium bicolor var. transparens, commonly known as the translucent caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display a range of colors, including shades of green, white, and pink. The leaves are often translucent, with a papery texture that allows light to pass through, creating a unique visual effect. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

This ornamental plant is widely cultivated for its decorative foliage in gardens and as a houseplant.
