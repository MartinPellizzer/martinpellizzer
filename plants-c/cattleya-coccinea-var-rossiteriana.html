Cattleya coccinea var. rossiteriana

Cattleya coccinea var. rossiteriana, commonly known as the Rossiteriana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid celebrated for its vibrant red and orange flowers.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with showy, fragrant blooms that can reach up to 10 inches in length. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its striking coloration and ornamental appeal.
