Coryphantha muehlenpfordtii var. robustispina

Coryphantha muehlenpfordtii var. robustispina, commonly known as the robust spiny coryphantha, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines, which provide protection against herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual element to its desert habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
