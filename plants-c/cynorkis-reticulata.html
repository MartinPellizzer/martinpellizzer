Cynorkis reticulata

Cynorkis reticulata, commonly known as the netted orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, net-like patterns on its petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a subtle fragrance.

Cynorkis reticulata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
