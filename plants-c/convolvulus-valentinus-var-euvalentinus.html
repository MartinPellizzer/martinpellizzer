Convolvulus valentinus var. euvalentinus

Convolvulus valentinus var. euvalentinus, commonly known as the Valentine's convolvulus, is a flowering plant belonging to the bindweed family, Convolvulaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that are usually pink or violet, often blooming in clusters. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
