Calathea splendida

Calathea splendida, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns of deep green, silver, and pink. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea splendida is known for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic of any room.
