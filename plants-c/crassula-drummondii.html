Crassula drummondii

Crassula drummondii, commonly known as the Drummond's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
