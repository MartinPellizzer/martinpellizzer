Cinnamomum verum

Cinnamomum verum, commonly known as true cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is harvested and dried to produce the spice cinnamon. The plant belongs to the laurel family ( Lauraceae ) and can grow up to 15 meters in height. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy surface with a strong aromatic scent.

The tree thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
