Cinnagrostis mandoniana

Cinnagrostis mandoniana, commonly known as the Mandon's cinnamomoid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets and white to pale pink ray florets. Cinnagrostis mandoniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
