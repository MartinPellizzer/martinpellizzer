Cliffortia filicauloides

Cliffortia filicauloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
