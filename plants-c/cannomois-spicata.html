Cannomois spicata

Cannomois spicata, commonly known as the spike cannas, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of upright spikes of brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in full sun and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for water gardens and tropical landscapes.

Cannomois spicata is known for its vigorous growth and ability to produce multiple flowering stalks throughout the growing season.
