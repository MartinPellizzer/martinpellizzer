Camellia chinmeiae

Camellia chinmeiae, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, camellia-like flowers that bloom in winter or early spring. The plant produces elliptical, red or pink flowers with numerous stamens, which are highly ornamental. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Camellia chinmeiae is also valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand colder temperatures compared to other camellia species.
