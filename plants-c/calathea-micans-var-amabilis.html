Calathea micans var. amabilis

Calathea micans var. amabilis, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that change color in response to light conditions, a phenomenon known as nyctinasty. The plant features broad, heart-shaped foliage with intricate patterns of green, silver, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

Calathea micans var. amabilis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
