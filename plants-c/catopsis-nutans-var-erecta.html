It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in humid, tropical regions. This plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with dense rosettes of narrow, strap-like leaves. The leaves are typically dark green and may have a waxy coating, which helps in water retention.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden within the leaf rosette, contributing to its unique botanical appearance.
