Citrus × mitis f. shikikitsu

Citrus × mitis f. shikikitsu, commonly known as the Shikikitsu mandarin, is a hybrid citrus plant originating from Japan.

It is a small evergreen tree with dark green, leathery leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is typically small, round, and has a thin, smooth peel with a sweet, tangy flavor. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often grown in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

The plant is relatively cold-hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
