Clitoria vaupellii

Clitoria vaupellii, commonly known as the purple orchid bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, with trifoliate leaves and pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
