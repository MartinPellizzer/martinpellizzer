Cyperus kyllingiella

Cyperus kyllingiella, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often subtended by bracts. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in suitable habitats.

Cyperus kyllingiella is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils.
