Ceanothus microphyllus var. serpyllifolius

Ceanothus microphyllus var. serpyllifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ceanothus, is a shrub native to California.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale blue to lavender flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
