Caladenia drakeoides

Caladenia drakeoides, commonly known as the Drake's Spider-orchid, is an Australian native orchid belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals and a narrow, hooded labellum. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in spring, often with a subtle fragrance.

Caladenia drakeoides is a protected species due to its limited distribution and susceptibility to habitat loss.
