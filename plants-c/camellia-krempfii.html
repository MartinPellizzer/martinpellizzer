Camellia krempfii

Camellia krempfii, commonly known as the pink camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a contrasting red or purple edge. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are dark green and leathery in texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Camellia krempfii is also valued for its cold hardiness and ability to bloom reliably in winter.
