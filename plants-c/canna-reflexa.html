Canna reflexa

Canna reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed canna, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, often with a wavy or reflexed margin. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically in clusters at the base of the plant. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Canna reflexa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
