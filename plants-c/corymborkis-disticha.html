Corymborkis disticha

Corymborkis disticha, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence and small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
