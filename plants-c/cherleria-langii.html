Cherleria langii

Cherleria langii, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a distinctive purple or brown center.

Cherleria langii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
