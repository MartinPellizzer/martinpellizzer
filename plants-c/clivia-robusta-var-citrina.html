Clivia robusta var. citrina

Clivia robusta var. citrina, commonly known as the yellow clivia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of vibrant yellow flowers during the late winter to early spring season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Clivia robusta var. citrina is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
