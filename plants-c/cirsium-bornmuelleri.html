Cirsium bornmuelleri

Cirsium bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Bornmueller's thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. It produces a single, tall flowering stalk that can reach up to 1 meter in height.

Cirsium bornmuelleri is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
