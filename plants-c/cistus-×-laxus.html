Cistus × laxus

Cistus × laxus, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It is often found in coastal areas and mountainous regions where it forms dense, low-growing vegetation.

Cistus × laxus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
