Caladenia congesta

Caladenia congesta, commonly known as the dense orchid, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually arranged in dense clusters, giving it its common name.

Caladenia congesta is known for its ephemeral blooms, which appear during the cooler months and last only a few weeks.
