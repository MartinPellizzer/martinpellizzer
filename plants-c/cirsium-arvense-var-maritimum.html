Cirsium arvense var. maritimum

Cirsium arvense var. maritimum, commonly known as sea thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in maritime environments. Its robust root system allows it to tolerate salt spray and strong winds.

It is considered a resilient species that can colonize disturbed coastal habitats.
