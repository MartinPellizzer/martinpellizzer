Centrilla sagrana

Centrilla sagrana, commonly known as the sacred centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in dry, rocky soils. It is often found in open habitats such as scrublands and coastal areas.

Centrilla sagrana is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
