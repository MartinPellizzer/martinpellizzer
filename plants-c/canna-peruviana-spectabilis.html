Canna peruviana-spectabilis

Canna peruviana-spectabilis, commonly known as the Peruvian canna, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, spadix-like flowers surrounded by vibrant, colorful bracts that range from red to orange and yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its dramatic foliage and long-lasting blooms.
