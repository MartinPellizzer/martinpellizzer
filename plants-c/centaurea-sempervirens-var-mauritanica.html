Centaurea sempervirens var. mauritanica

Centaurea sempervirens var. mauritanica, commonly known as the Mauritania everlasting, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, bright blue to violet flowers. The plant produces bracts that persist after flowering, giving it a long-lasting appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
