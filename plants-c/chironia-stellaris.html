Chironia stellaris

Chironia stellaris, commonly known as the starry chironia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a fragrant aroma. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Chironia stellaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
