Cactus jucundus

Cactus jucundus, commonly known as the happy cactus, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, thick stems and spiny appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
