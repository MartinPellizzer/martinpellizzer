Commelina medica

Commelina medica, commonly known as medicinal commelina, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to violet flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Commelina medica is also valued for its traditional medicinal uses in various cultures.
