Carduncellus × mairei

Carduncellus × mairei, commonly known as the Maire cardoon, is a hybrid plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, spiny leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a long flowering period, usually from late spring to early autumn.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
