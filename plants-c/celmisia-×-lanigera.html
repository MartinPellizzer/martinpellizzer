Celmisia × lanigera

Celmisia × lanigera, commonly known as the woolly celmisia, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of yellow or green, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with specific environmental requirements.
