Cynoglossum × salesianorum

Cynoglossum × salesianorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf forget-me-not, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Cynoglossum × salesianorum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
