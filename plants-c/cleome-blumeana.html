Cleome blumeana

Cleome blumeana, commonly known as the Blume's spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cleome blumeana is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
