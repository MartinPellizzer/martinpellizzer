Cephalaria linearifolia

Cephalaria linearifolia, commonly known as the slender cephalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to regions of Europe and western Asia, often found in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in cylindrical inflorescences, typically displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained, sunny conditions.
