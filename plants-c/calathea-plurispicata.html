Calathea plurispicata

Calathea plurispicata, commonly known as the zigzag calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the leaves. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with contrasting colors, such as green, silver, and pink. Calathea plurispicata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
