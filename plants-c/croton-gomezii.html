Croton gomezii

Croton gomezii, commonly known as the Gomez's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and diverse leaf shapes. The plant features alternate, palmately lobed leaves that range in color from green to variegated patterns. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically clustered in dense inflorescences.
