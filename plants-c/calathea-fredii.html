Calathea fredii

Calathea fredii, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of deep green, silver, and pink. The leaves are oval-shaped and often have a waxy texture, which helps in retaining moisture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
