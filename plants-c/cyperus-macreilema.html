Cyperus macreilema

Cyperus macreilema, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, such as swamps and floodplains. Its slender, grass-like stems are often found in dense clusters, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets.

Cyperus macreilema is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for aquatic organisms.
