Carex goetghebeurii

Carex goetghebeurii, commonly known as the narrowleaf sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, rushes, and grass-like plants. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or marshy areas. It has slender, grass-like leaves that are usually dark green and have a smooth texture.
