Cyrtanthus tuckii var. tuckii

Cyrtanthus tuckii var. tuckii, commonly known as the Tuck's bell-shaped lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or lilac in color, blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

Cyrtanthus tuckii var. tuckii prefers full sun to partial shade and requires minimal water once established.
