Centaurea pallidior

Centaurea pallidior, commonly known as the pale centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are usually pale blue or pink in color.

Centaurea pallidior is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
