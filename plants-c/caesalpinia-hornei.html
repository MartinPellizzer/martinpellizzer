Caesalpinia hornei

Caesalpinia hornei, commonly known as the orange flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and often reddish in color, and it produces elongated pods containing several seeds.

Caesalpinia hornei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
