Centropogon foliosus

Centropogon foliosus, commonly known as the leafy centropogon, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. Centropogon foliosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
