Clerodendrum discolor var. macrocalyx

Clerodendrum discolor var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered glorybower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive tubular shape. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The varietal name 'macrocalyx' refers to its large, prominent flower buds.
