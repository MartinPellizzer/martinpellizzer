Clarkia whitneyi

Clarkia whitneyi, commonly known as Whitney's Clarkia, is a species of flowering plant in the evening primrose family (Onagraceae).

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, such as grasslands and woodland edges. It produces showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a yellow center.

The leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
