Cytisus leiotrichus

Cytisus leiotrichus, commonly known as the slender broom, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in racemes. Its small, leathery leaves are opposite and have a glandular surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
