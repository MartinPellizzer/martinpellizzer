Cordyline sturmii

Cordyline sturmii, commonly known as the Sturm's cordyline, is a striking evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height, supporting large, sword-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant blue-green color. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red berries. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Cordyline sturmii prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, thriving in temperate climates.
