Calesiam speciosum

Calesiam speciosum, commonly known as the ornate caryophyll, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
