Centaurea squarrosa var. pilosiuscula

Centaurea squarrosa var. pilosiuscula, commonly known as the silky centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright blue to purple flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a dense layer of soft, white to grayish hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, dainty look.
