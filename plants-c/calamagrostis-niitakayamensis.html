Calamagrostis niitakayamensis

Calamagrostis niitakayamensis, commonly known as the Niitakayama reed, is a grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and riverbanks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland conservation.
