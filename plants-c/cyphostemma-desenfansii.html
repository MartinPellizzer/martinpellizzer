Cyphostemma desenfansii

Cyphostemma desenfansii, commonly known as the Desert Trumpet, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has woody stems and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is well adapted to drought conditions.

Cyphostemma desenfansii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
