Crotalaria lachnocarpa var. strictissima

Crotalaria lachnocarpa var. strictissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaf crotalaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and is considered a potential weed in agricultural settings.
