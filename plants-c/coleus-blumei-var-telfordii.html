Coleus blumei var. telfordii

Coleus blumei var. telfordii, commonly known as the telfordii coleus, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep reds and oranges to purples and yellows. The plant features opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking appearance.

Its growth is typically compact, making it suitable for container gardening and landscape use.
