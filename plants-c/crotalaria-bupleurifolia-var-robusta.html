Crotalaria bupleurifolia var. robusta

Crotalaria bupleurifolia var. robusta, commonly known as the robust crotalaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. It has a bushy growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

The seeds are encased in a hard pod and are an important food source for various bird and insect species.
