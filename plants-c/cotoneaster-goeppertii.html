Cotoneaster goeppertii

Cotoneaster goeppertii, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster goeppertii is also valued for its role in wildlife habitat and erosion control.
