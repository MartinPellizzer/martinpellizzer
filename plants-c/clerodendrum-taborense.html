Clerodendrum taborense

Clerodendrum taborense, commonly known as the Tabore Clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow as a shrub or small tree.
