Cattleya labiata var. beyrodtiana

Cattleya labiata var. beyrodtiana, commonly known as the Beyrodtiana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features tall, upright stems with large, showy blooms that come in shades of pink, red, and white. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, providing a lush green backdrop to the striking flowers.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
