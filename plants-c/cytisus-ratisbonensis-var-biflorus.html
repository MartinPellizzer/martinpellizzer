Cytisus ratisbonensis var. biflorus

Cytisus ratisbonensis var. biflorus, commonly known as the two-flowered broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces slender, woody pods that contain seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant in gardens.
