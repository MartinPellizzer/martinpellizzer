Calamophyllum teretifolium

Calamophyllum teretifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved calamophyllum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, compact foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Calamophyllum teretifolium is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal and inland areas with well-drained soils.
