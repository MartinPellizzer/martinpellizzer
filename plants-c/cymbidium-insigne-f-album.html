Cymbidium insigne f. album

Cymbidium insigne f. album, commonly known as the white cymbidium orchid, is a hybrid cultivar of the cymbidium orchid family.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers, which often have a subtle pink or red hue at the base. This orchid is native to China and is prized for its elegant appearance and fragrance. It typically blooms in winter or early spring, adding a touch of beauty to gardens during the colder months.

The plant prefers cool, bright conditions and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
