Clitoria magentea

Clitoria magentea, commonly known as the scarlet bower vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its striking purple to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Clitoria magentea is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
