Coix aquatica

Coix aquatica, commonly known as water bamboo, is a aquatic plant native to Asia.

It has long, slender stems that grow either submerged or emergent in water. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often floating on the water's surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters.

Coix aquatica is often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures.
