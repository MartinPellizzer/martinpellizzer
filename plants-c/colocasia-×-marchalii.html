Colocasia × marchalii

Colocasia × marchalii, commonly known as the giant elephant ear, is a hybrid plant in the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant originates from tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with rich, well-draining soil. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring shades of green, white, and yellow.

It is popular as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to add dramatic visual impact to gardens.
