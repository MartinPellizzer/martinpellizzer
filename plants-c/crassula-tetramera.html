Crassula tetramera

Crassula tetramera, commonly known as the Four-Leaf Crassula, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula tetramera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
