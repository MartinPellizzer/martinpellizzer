Coleus briquetii

Coleus briquetii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white, yellow, or silver markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Coleus briquetii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
