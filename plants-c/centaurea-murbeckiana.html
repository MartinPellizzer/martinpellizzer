Centaurea murbeckiana

Centaurea murbeckiana, commonly known as the Murbeck's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Centaurea murbeckiana is often found in open grasslands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
