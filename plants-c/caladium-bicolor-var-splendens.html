Caladium bicolor var. splendens

Caladium bicolor var. splendens, commonly known as the flameleaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that come in vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade. It is often cultivated for its striking foliage rather than its small, inconspicuous flowers.

Caladium bicolor var. splendens is a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
