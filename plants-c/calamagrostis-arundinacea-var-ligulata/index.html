Calamagrostis Arundinacea Var. Ligulata

Calamagrostis arundinacea var. ligulata is a striking ornamental grass known for its elegant silver-green foliage and plume-like flower spikes. This variety is prized for its ability to thrive in a range of conditions, making it a versatile choice for gardeners. Its feathery appearance adds a soft, flowing texture to landscapes and containers alike. Often used in borders or as a background plant, it brings a touch of grace to any setting. With minimal maintenance, it offers year-round visual interest and a natural, wild aesthetic.
