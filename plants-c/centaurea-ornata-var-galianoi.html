Centaurea ornata var. galianoi

Centaurea ornata var. galianoi, commonly known as the Galiano's star-thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads, which are surrounded by spiky, bristly leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its stems are usually erect and branched, with a rough, hairy texture.

The flowers bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
