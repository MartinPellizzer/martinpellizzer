Casearia subopaca

Casearia subopaca, commonly known as the dark casearia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
