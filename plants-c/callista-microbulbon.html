Callista microbulbon

Callista microbulbon, commonly known as the small-bulbous callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

Callista microbulbon is known for its compact size and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
