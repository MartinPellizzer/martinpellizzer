Colquhounia coccinea var. mollis

Colquhounia coccinea var. mollis, commonly known as the soft red spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and covered in a fine, soft pubescence.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
