Calolisianthus pedunculatus var. damazianus

Calolisianthus pedunculatus var. damazianus, commonly known as the Damazianus hibiscus, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves and grows as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in tropical regions.
