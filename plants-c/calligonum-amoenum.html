Calligonum amoenum

Calligonum amoenum, commonly known as the velvetleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and can thrive in harsh desert environments.

Calligonum amoenum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
