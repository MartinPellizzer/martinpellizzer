Cristaria foliosa

Cristaria foliosa, commonly known as the featherleaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that give it its common name.

Native to specific regions in South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a compact growth habit with elongated, lobed leaves that can reach up to 15 centimeters in length. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-white in color, and appear in clusters.

Cristaria foliosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
