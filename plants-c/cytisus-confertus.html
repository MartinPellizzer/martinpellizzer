Cytisus confertus

Cytisus confertus, commonly known as the dense broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
