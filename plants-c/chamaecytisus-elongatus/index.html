Chamaecytisus Elongatus

Chamaecytisus elongatus is a unique evergreen shrub known for its vibrant red flowers and adaptability to coastal environments. Native to the British Isles, it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime climates. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and its ability to stabilize eroding soils along cliffs and dunes. Its dense growth habit makes it an excellent choice for landscaping in harsh coastal areas. Despite its hardy nature, it requires well-drained soil and protection from strong winds.
