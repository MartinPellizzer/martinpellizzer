Carex huitensis

Carex huitensis, commonly known as Huitzilac sedge, is a perennial plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. This species typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its leaves are grass-like, with a triangular cross-section typical of sedges.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often hidden among its foliage.
