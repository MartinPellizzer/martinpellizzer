Centaurea × xanthothrix

Centaurea × xanthothrix, commonly known as the yellow centaury, is a hybrid perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically arranged in dense, cone-shaped heads. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Centaurea × xanthothrix is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
