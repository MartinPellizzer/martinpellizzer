Cynoglossum rotatum

Cynoglossum rotatum, commonly known as the purple heart or heartleaf forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland edges. It blooms from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cynoglossum rotatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate flowers.
