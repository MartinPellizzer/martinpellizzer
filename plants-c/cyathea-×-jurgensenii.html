Cyathea × jurgensenii

Cyathea × jurgensenii, commonly known as the Jurgensen's tree fern, is a hybrid species within the Cyatheaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant is native to tropical regions of the Americas, particularly in areas with high humidity and moderate temperatures. Its rhizome system supports the growth of the trunk and anchors the plant in moist, well-drained soil.

Cyathea × jurgensenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
