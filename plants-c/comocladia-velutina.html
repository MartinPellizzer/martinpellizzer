Comocladia velutina

Comocladia velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Violaceae.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It is often found in wetland areas and can thrive in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
