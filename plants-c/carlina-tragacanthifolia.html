Carlina tragacanthifolia

Carlina tragacanthifolia, commonly known as the woolly cudweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties.
