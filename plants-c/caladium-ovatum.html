Caladium ovatum

Caladium ovatum, commonly known as the waxleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with shades of white, pink, and red. The leaves are thick and waxy, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full shade.

This ornamental plant is popular for its striking foliage and is often used in gardens and as a houseplant.
