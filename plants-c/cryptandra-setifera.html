Cryptandra setifera

Cryptandra setifera, commonly known as the slender cryptandra, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces tiny, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Cryptandra setifera is adapted to arid conditions and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for native pollinators.
