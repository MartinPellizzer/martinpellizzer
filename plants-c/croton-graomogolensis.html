Croton graomogolensis

Croton graomogolensis, commonly known as the Graomogolensis Croton, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored foliage, which can range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
