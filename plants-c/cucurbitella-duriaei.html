Cucurbitella duriaei

Cucurbitella duriaei, commonly known as the Duriaei cucumber, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It produces elongated, cylindrical fruits that are typically green when mature, with a slightly rough texture. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its botanical classification places it in the family Cucurbitaceae, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and squash.

The species is valued for its edible fruit, which has a mild, crisp flavor.
