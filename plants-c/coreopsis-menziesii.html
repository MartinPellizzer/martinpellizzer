Coreopsis menziesii

Coreopsis menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairies. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces seeds in small, fluffy capsules.

Coreopsis menziesii is a hardy perennial that is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
