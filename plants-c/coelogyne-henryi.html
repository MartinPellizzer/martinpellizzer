Coelogyne henryi

Coelogyne henryi, commonly known as Henry's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender pseudobulbs and long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Coelogyne henryi is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
