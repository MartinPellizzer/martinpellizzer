Ceanothus Verrucosus Var. Rigidus

Bluecurls, scientifically known as Ceanothus verrucosus var. rigidus, is a strikingly beautiful shrub native to the coastal regions of California. With its vibrant blue and purple flowers, it stands out among the local flora during the spring season. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its unique curling petals and dense growth habit add both visual interest and ecological value. Despite its hardiness, Bluecurls requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and potential pest issues.
