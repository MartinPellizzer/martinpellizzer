Cyperus deckenii

Cyperus deckenii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly known as the sedge family, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, such as swamps, marshes, and along riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Cyperus deckenii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.
