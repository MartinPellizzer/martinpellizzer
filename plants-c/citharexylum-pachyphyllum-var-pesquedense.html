Citharexylum pachyphyllum var. pesquedense

Citharexylum pachyphyllum var. pesquedense, commonly known as the Pesquedense Citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are thick and glossy, providing a striking visual contrast to its delicate blooms.
