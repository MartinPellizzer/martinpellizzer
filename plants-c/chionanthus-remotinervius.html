Chionanthus remotinervius

Chionanthus remotinervius, commonly known as the remote-nerved snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats.
