Chamaecrista tonduzii

Chamaecrista tonduzii, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
