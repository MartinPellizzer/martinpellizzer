Cleyera japonica var. japonica

Cleyera japonica var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese cleyera, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide seasonal interest.
