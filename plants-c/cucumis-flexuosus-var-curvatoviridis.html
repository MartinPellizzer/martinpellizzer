Cucumis flexuosus var. curvatoviridis

Cucumis flexuosus var. curvatoviridis, commonly known as the curly green cucumber, is a variant of the wild cucumber species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its long, slender fruits that often exhibit a distinctive green coloration. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with hairy stems and compound leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

This variety is valued for its unique appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
