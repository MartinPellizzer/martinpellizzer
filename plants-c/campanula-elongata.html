Campanula elongata

Campanula elongata, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, adorned with tubular, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
