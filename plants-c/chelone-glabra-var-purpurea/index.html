Chelone Glabra Var. Purpurea

Chelone glabra var. purpurea is a striking perennial known for its unique, nodding flowers. This plant belongs to the gentian family and is native to parts of eastern North America. Its purple blooms add a dramatic touch to gardens and natural landscapes. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found along stream banks. Its beauty and ecological value make it a popular choice for native plant enthusiasts.
