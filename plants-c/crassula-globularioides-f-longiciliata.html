Crassula globularioides f. longiciliata

Crassula globularioides f. longiciliata, commonly known as the Long-hair Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically a vibrant green, with a bluish tint, and they are arranged in a compact, globular shape. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low water requirements.
