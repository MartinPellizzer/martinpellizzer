Chlorophytum setosum

Chlorophytum setosum, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail or Tiger's Head plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, strap-like leaves that often have a distinctive yellow or white stripe. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by blue or purple berries. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
