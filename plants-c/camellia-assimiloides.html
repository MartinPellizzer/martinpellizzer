Camellia assimiloides

Camellia assimiloides, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a yellow or orange center. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Camellia assimiloides is also valued for its ability to bloom in winter, adding color to the landscape during colder months.
