Crinum bulbispermum

Crinum bulbispermum, commonly known as the Cape lily, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, straight stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a strong, fragrant scent. Its bulbous root system is essential for its survival, allowing it to store nutrients and water.

Crinum bulbispermum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
