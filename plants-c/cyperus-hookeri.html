Cyperus hookeri

Cyperus hookeri, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Cyperus hookeri is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive, upright growth habit and adaptability to various soil conditions.
