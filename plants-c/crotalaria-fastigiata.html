Crotalaria fastigiata

Crotalaria fastigiata, commonly known as the upright crownflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, yellow-orange flowers that resemble those of the crown flower. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces bean-like pods that contain several seeds. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
