Coleospadix porrectus

Coleospadix porrectus, commonly known as the broad-leaved spadix, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence, which is a dense cluster of small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Coleospadix porrectus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
