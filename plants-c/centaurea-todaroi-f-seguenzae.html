Centaurea todaroi f. seguenzae

Centaurea todaroi f. seguenzae, commonly known as the Seguenza's cornflower, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

The plant features bright blue to violet flowers with a distinctive central disc, surrounded by ray florets.
