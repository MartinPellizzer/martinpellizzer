Cynanchum asthmaticum

Cynanchum asthmaticum, commonly known as Chinese milkweed or asthma plant, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its erect stem and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its milky sap contains toxic compounds, which have been traditionally used in herbal medicine for respiratory ailments.

However, due to its poisonous nature, it requires careful handling and is not commonly cultivated.
