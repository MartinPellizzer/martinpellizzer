Callisia delicatula

Callisia delicatula, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming foliage with a striking silver-green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often followed by red berries. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.

Callisia delicatula thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it adaptable to a variety of growing conditions.
