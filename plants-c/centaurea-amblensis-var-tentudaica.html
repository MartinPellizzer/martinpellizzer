Centaurea amblensis var. tentudaica

Centaurea amblensis var. tentudaica, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and feathery leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its slender stems and small blue to purple flowers make it a distinctive and ornamental species.

It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salty and arid conditions.
