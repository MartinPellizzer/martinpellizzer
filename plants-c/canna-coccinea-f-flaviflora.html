Canna coccinea f. flaviflora

Canna coccinea f. flaviflora, commonly known as the red ginger or flame canna, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a lush backdrop to its colorful blooms. The flowers are usually red or orange, though the flaviflora form exhibits a distinctive yellow hue. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a tropical plant in containers.
