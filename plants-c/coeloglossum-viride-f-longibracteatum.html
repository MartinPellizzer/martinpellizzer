Coeloglossum viride f. longibracteatum

Coeloglossum viride f. longibracteatum, commonly known as the long-bract green helleborine, is a variant of the green helleborine orchid.

It is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae, characterized by its lanceolate leaves and showy greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of several flowers with long, strap-like bracts, which are a distinguishing feature of this form.

The leaves are usually broad and have a waxy texture, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.
