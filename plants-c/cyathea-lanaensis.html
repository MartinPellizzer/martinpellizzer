Cyathea lanaensis

Cyathea lanaensis, commonly known as the Lanao cycad tree, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments, often in lowland forests. Its reproductive structures include both strobili and spore-bearing cones, typical of ferns.

Cyathea lanaensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
