Crypteronia lutea

Crypteronia lutea, commonly known as the yellow crypteronia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, shrub-like species that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences, which are often visited by insects. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Crypteronia lutea is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
