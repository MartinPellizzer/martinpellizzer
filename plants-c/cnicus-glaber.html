Cnicus glaber

Cnicus glaber, commonly known as the goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with a mat-forming habit, featuring yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, and it often thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Cnicus glaber is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
