Cineraria fussii

Cineraria fussii, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking silver-blue foliage and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
