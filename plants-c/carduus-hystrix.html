Carduus hystrix

Carduus hystrix, commonly known as the thistle.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads. The plant is native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia, and it thrives in a variety of habitats including meadows and disturbed soils. Its leaves are typically broad, with a rough, hairy texture and sharp margins.

The flowers are typically composite, consisting of many small florets that bloom in late summer to early autumn.
