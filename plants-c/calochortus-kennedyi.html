Calochortus kennedyi

Calochortus kennedyi, commonly known as Kennedy's mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes various species of flowering plants commonly referred to as mariposa lilies. This particular species is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant has fibrous roots and produces a basal rosette of leaves, making it well adapted to its arid environment.
