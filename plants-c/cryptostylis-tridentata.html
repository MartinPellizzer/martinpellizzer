Cryptostylis tridentata

Cryptostylis tridentata, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fragrant flowers that often have a three-lobed lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Cryptostylis tridentata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
