Cylindropuntia fulgida var. mamillata

Cylindropuntia fulgida var. mamillata, commonly known as the Mexican fire cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered with small, white spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
