Cryptanthus ilhanus

Cryptanthus ilhanus, commonly known as the Brazilian carnation, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Amaryllidaceae and is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces vibrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often blooming in late winter to early spring. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a compact rosette.

Cryptanthus ilhanus is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
