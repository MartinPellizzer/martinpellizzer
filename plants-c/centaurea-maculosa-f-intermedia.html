Centaurea maculosa f. intermedia

Centaurea maculosa f. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate spotted knapweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its striking purple to pink flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. It has a taproot system and produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered an invasive species in some regions.
