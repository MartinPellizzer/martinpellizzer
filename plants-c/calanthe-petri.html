Calanthe petri

Calanthe petri, commonly known as the petri calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs. Its flowers are borne on upright spikes and typically bloom in the spring.

Calanthe petri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
