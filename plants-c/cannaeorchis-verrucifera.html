Cannaeorchis verrucifera

Cannaeorchis verrucifera, commonly known as the疣唇兰, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a yellow or orange lip with a distinctive warty or rough texture. The plant grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Cannaeorchis verrucifera is valued in horticulture for its unique floral structure and vibrant colors.
