Croton bocquillonii

Croton bocquillonii, commonly known as the Bocquillon's croton, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a wide range of hues, including green, red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil.
