Cimicifuga foetida var. bifida

Cimicifuga foetida var. bifida, commonly known as American bugbane, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features tall, unbranched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, crowned with large, white, lacecap flowers. The plant has compound leaves with pinnately arranged leaflets, and its root system is fibrous and deep. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and wetlands.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has a long history of use in traditional medicine.
