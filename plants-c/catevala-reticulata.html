Catevala reticulata

Catevala reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, reticulated vein patterns on its leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pale green or yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
