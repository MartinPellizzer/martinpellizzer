Cucubalus baccifer var. cavalerieri

Cucubalus baccifer var. cavalerieri, commonly known as the Cavalerieri's starfruit, is a variant of the starfruit plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, yellow, star-shaped fruits that are typically harvested when fully ripe. The plant thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The fruit is not only edible but also valued for its unique flavor and nutritional content.
