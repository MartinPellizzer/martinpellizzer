Calochilus richiae

Calochilus richiae, commonly known as the rich's spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals and a distinctive, narrow labellum. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a cool, humid environment. It blooms in spring, producing flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white.

Calochilus richiae is a terrestrial orchid that is often found in coastal regions of New South Wales and Victoria.
