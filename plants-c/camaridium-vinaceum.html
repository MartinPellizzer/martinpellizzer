Camaridium vinaceum

Camaridium vinaceum, commonly known as the red vine plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and trailing stems that often grow along the ground or over supports. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Camaridium vinaceum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
