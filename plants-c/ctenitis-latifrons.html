Ctenitis latifrons

Ctenitis latifrons, commonly known as the broad-fronded tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate fronds that give it a graceful, tree-like appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The fronds are used in traditional medicine in some cultures, highlighting its ecological and cultural significance.
