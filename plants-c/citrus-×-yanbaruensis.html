Citrus × yanbaruensis

Citrus × yanbaruensis, commonly known as the Okinawan lemon.

It is a hybrid citrus species native to Japan, particularly Okinawa, and is characterized by its small, yellow-orange fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is used for its aromatic zest and juice, though it is not as widely cultivated as other citrus varieties.

The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and ample sunlight.
