Campanula cephalaria var. ovaliphylla

Campanula cephalaria var. ovaliphylla, commonly known as the oval-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lanceolate to oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
