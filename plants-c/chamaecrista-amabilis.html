Chamaecrista amabilis

Chamaecrista amabilis, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
