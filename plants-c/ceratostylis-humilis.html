Ceratostylis humilis

Ceratostylis humilis, commonly known as the dwarf spider orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, creeping rhizome with erect, leafy stems and small, greenish-white flowers. Its flowers have a distinctive spathaceous structure and are often clustered in loose inflorescences.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
