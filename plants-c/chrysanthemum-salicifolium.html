Chrysanthemum salicifolium

Chrysanthemum salicifolium, commonly known as the willow-leaf chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its serrated, willow-like leaves. The plant produces daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the autumn season. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and distinctive foliage.
