Chirita cycnostyla

Chirita cycnostyla, commonly known as the velvet leaf begonia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Chirita cycnostyla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
