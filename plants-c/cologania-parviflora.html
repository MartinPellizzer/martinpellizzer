Cologania parviflora

Cologania parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered cologania, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, small white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Cologania parviflora is often found in humid tropical climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
