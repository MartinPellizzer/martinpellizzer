Cotyledon pendulina

Cotyledon pendulina, commonly known as the hanging cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Cotyledon pendulina is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
