Cyperus flavescens f. rubromarginatus

Cyperus flavescens f. rubromarginatus, commonly known as the red-margin flatsedge, is a variant of the flat sedge species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive red margin along the edges. It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and swamps.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological studies and wetland conservation efforts.
