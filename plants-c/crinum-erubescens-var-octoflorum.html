Crinum erubescens var. octoflorum

Crinum erubescens var. octoflorum, commonly known as the red crinum or the red-flowered crinum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers. The plant features strap-shaped, grass-like leaves and produces umbels of showy, fragrant blooms. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Crinum erubescens var. octoflorum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
