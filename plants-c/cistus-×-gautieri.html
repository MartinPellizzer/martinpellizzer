Cistus × gautieri

Cistus × gautieri, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments. Its essential oils, derived from the leaves and flowers, are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.

Cistus × gautieri is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
