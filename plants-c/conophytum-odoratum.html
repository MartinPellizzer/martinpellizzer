Conophytum odoratum

Conophytum odoratum, commonly known as the sweet-scented conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and fragrant blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
