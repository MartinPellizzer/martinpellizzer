Cassia Hispidula Var. Killipii

Cassia hispidula var. killipii is a unique variant of the cassia genus native to specific regions of eastern Africa. This plant is known for its striking yellow flowers and feathery foliage that add visual interest to any landscape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardeners in temperate climates. The variety killipii is particularly noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Due to its beauty and adaptability, it has become a popular plant in both cultivated gardens and natural habitats.
