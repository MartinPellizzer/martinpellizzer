Chirita geoffrayi

Chirita geoffrayi, commonly known as the Geoffray's gesneriad, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Chirita geoffrayi thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers humid environments with well-draining soil.
