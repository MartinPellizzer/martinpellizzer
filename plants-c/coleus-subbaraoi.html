Coleus subbaraoi

Coleus subbaraoi, commonly known as the red-leaf coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep reds to purples and blues. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is closely related to other members of the Coleus genus. Its leaves are typically ovate or lance-shaped with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

Coleus subbaraoi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking leaf colors and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
