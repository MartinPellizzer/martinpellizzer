Cirsium carniolicum subsp. carniolicum

Cirsium carniolicum subsp.

carniolicum, commonly known as the Carnian thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Alps and surrounding regions. It typically grows in moist meadows and forest edges, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters.

The plant features spiny leaves and purple to pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in summer. It has a robust root system and is adapted to cool, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
