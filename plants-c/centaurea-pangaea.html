Centaurea pangaea

Centaurea pangaea, commonly known as the giant centaurea, is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant features showy, bright red or orange flower heads that are surrounded by pale blue to purple bracts. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Centaurea pangaea thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate, making it a notable species in its native habitat.
