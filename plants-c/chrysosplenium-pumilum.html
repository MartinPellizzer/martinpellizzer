Chrysosplenium pumilum

Chrysosplenium pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Asia and North America.

It typically features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
