Cinnamomum loheri

Cinnamomum loheri, commonly known as the Malabar cinnamon or Ceylon cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-scented leaves and bark, which are rich in essential oils. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by elliptical, dark purple berries. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy, dark green upper surface.

This species is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
