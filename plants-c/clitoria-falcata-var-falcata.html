Clitoria falcata var. falcata

Clitoria falcata var. falcata, commonly known as the sickle bean or falcate pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with lanceolate leaflets and produces vibrant purple or violet pea-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its slender, twining stems and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
