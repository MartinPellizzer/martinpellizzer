Cinchona roraimae

Cinchona roraimae, commonly known as Roraima quinine tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the tepuis of South America, particularly in Venezuela and Guyana. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, as it contains quinine, a compound used in the treatment of malaria. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes.
