Coleus forskalaei var. adoensis

Coleus forskalaei var. adoensis, commonly known as the Adoensis coleus, is a variant of the Coleus genus native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, purple, and green. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy beneath. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens for its striking appearance and ability to add visual interest to shaded areas.
