Ceratostylis javanica

Ceratostylis javanica, commonly known as the Javanese horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces a single, showy white or pale green flower with a distinctive, elongated spur. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
