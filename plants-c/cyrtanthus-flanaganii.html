Cyrtanthus flanaganii

Cyrtanthus flanaganii, commonly known as the Flanagan's bell-shaped iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and produces narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.

Cyrtanthus flanaganii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
