Camphora thouarsii

Camphora thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar camphor tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark that contain camphor. The plant has simple, alternate, elliptical leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue, fleshy fruits.

The tree is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used in traditional practices.
