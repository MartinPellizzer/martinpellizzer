Cryptocarya cagayanensis

Cryptocarya cagayanensis, commonly known as the Cagayan cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the Cagayan Valley region. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are glossy, elliptical, and have a fragrant aroma, characteristic of many members of the Lauraceae family.

The flowers are small and clustered, and the fruit is a dark, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.
