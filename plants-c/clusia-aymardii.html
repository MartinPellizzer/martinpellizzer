Clusia aymardii

Clusia aymardii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a fleshy, orange-colored aril. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

Clusia aymardii thrives in humid, warm climates and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
