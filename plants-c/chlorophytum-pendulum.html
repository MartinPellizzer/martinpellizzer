Chlorophytum pendulum

Chlorophytum pendulum, commonly known as the hanging ivy plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems that can trail down from hanging baskets or shelves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, often with a slight sheen.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
