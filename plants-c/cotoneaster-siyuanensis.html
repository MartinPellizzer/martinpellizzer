Cotoneaster siyuanensis

Cotoneaster siyuanensis, commonly known as the Siyuan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly serrated edge.

Cotoneaster siyuanensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
