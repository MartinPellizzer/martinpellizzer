Ctenitis silaensis

Ctenitis silaensis, commonly known as the Silaensian tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and its fronds are sometimes harvested for ornamental purposes.
