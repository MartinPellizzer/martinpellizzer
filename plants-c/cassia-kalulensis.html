Cassia kalulensis

Cassia kalulensis, commonly known as the Kalulensis senna, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.

Cassia kalulensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
