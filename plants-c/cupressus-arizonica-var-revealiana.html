Cupressus arizonica var. revealiana

Cupressus arizonica var. revealiana, commonly known as the Arizona cypress, is a variant of the Arizona cypress native to the southwestern United States.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen conifer with scale-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for landscaping in desert regions.
