Celmisia × christensenii

Celmisia × christensenii, commonly known as the Christensen's celmisia, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, often glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or pale green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in native forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it requires careful management to prevent it from becoming invasive.
