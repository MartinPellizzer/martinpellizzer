Calamus quangngaiensis

Calamus quangngaiensis, commonly known as the Quang Ngai calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, where it grows in humid lowland forests. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish flowers, which are followed by dark, spherical fruits.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
