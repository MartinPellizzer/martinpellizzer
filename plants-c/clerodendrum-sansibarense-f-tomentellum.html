Clerodendrum sansibarense f. tomentellum

Clerodendrum sansibarense f. tomentellum, commonly known as the woolly clerodendrum, is a shrub native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
