Calosmon

Calosmon, commonly known as the golden star, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, yellow flowers that resemble small stars, hence its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in a compact, shrub-like form. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Calosmon is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
