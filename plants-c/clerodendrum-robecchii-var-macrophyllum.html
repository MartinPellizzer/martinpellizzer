Clerodendrum robecchii var. macrophyllum

Clerodendrum robecchii var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant-leaved clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
