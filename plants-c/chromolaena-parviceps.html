Chromolaena parviceps

Chromolaena parviceps, commonly known as the small-headed chromolaena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Chromolaena parviceps is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain areas.
