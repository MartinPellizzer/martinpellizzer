Crotalaria stenoptera var. latifolia

Crotalaria stenoptera var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically broad and deeply lobed.
