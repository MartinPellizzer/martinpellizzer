Centaurea adusta

Centaurea adusta, commonly known as the dark centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, glandular leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its height can range from 30 to 100 centimeters, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

Centaurea adusta is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional herbal medicine.
