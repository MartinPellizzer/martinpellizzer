Canna × aurantiaca

Canna × aurantiaca, commonly known as the red canna, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cannaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Canna × aurantiaca is often used as an ornamental plant due to its bold foliage and showy blooms.
