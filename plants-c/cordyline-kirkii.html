Cordyline kirkii

Cordyline kirkii, commonly known as the Kikuyu cordyline, is a striking evergreen shrub native to East Africa.

It features tall, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a waxy, blue-green sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters at the base of the leaves. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.

Cordyline kirkii thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
