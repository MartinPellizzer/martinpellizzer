Cephalotaxus harringtonia f. sphaeralis

Cephalotaxus harringtonia f. sphaeralis, commonly known as the spherical yew, is a rare and unique variant of the yew family.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and distinctive, scale-like leaves that are densely packed. The plant produces small, red berries that are a striking feature of its appearance. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its slow growth and evergreen foliage.

This variety is particularly valued in horticulture for its unique form and adaptability to various soil conditions.
