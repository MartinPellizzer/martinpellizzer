Cicerbita microcephala

Cicerbita microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed bittercress, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous species. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The small, yellow flowers appear in clusters and are followed by small, round pods that contain several seeds.
