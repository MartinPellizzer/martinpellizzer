Crassula subulata var. fastigiata

Crassula subulata var. fastigiata, commonly known as the Compact Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, upright growth with small, pointed leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it drought-tolerant and adaptable to a variety of growing conditions.
