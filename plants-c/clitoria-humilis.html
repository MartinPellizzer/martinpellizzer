Clitoria humilis

Clitoria humilis, commonly known as the small butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
