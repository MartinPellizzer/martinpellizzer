Cheirostylis chuxiongensis

Cheirostylis chuxiongensis, commonly known as the Chuxiong orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, where it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient absorption. Its botanical features include strap-like leaves and a compact inflorescence with small, clustered blooms.
