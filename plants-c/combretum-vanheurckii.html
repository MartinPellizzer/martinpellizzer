Combretum vanheurckii

Combretum vanheurckii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its bark is smooth and reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
