Cortaderia quila

Cortaderia quila, commonly known as the silver grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, arching inflorescences that are covered in silvery-white plumes, giving it a dramatic and elegant appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a deep green color, providing a nice contrast to its fluffy flower heads. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Cortaderia quila is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to add texture and movement to garden spaces.
