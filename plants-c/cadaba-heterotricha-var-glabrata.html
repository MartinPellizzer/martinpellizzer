Cadaba heterotricha var. glabrata

Cadaba heterotricha var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth cadaba, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are typically hairless, giving it the "glabrata" designation. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
