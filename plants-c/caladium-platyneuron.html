Caladium platyneuron

Caladium platyneuron, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored in shades of red, orange, and white. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for vertical gardening or as a hanging plant.

The foliage is popular in ornamental horticulture for its striking visual appeal.
