Ceropegia Carnosa Subsp. Glabra

Ceropegia carnosa subsp. glabra is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that attract specific pollinators. Native to southern Africa, this species thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, helping it store water for long periods. The plant's unusual appearance has made it a popular choice among collectors and gardeners. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
