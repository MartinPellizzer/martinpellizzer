Cyclaminos

Cyclaminos, commonly known as the cyclamen, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, spurred flowers that come in a variety of colors. Cyclaminos thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia.
