Callista parviflora

Callista parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has hairy, narrow leaves. It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.

Callista parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
