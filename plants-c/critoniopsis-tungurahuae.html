Critoniopsis tungurahuae

Critoniopsis tungurahuae, commonly known as the Tungurahua grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, characterized by its tall, cylindrical inflorescence and narrow leaves. Native to the Andean region of South America, it thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures. The plant is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in mountainous areas.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it an important species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
