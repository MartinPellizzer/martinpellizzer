Cnidoscolus urens

Cnidoscolus urens, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible, spiny fruits. Its leaves contain compounds that have been used in traditional medicine.

However, the plant is toxic if ingested in large quantities, so caution is advised when handling it.
