Clematis herrei

Clematis herrei, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale blue flowers. The plant is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to climb using its tendrils. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis herrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
