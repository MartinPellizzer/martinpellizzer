Centaurea aucheri subsp. elbursensis

Centaurea aucheri subsp.

elbursensis, commonly known as the Elburs centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Elburs Mountains in Iran. It is a herbaceous perennial with upright, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces showy, bright yellow to orange-red flowers in dense, spherical clusters, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
