Cryptanthus morrenianus

Cryptanthus morrenianus, commonly known as the Morren's winterbloom, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
