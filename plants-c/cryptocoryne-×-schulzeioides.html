Cryptocoryne × schulzeioides

Cryptocoryne × schulzeioides, commonly known as the Schultzeioides cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense rosette of sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery sheen. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is well-suited for planted aquariums due to its hardy nature. Its leaves are often used in aquascaping for their aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for small aquatic life.

Cryptocoryne × schulzeioides is also valued for its ability to help maintain water clarity by absorbing nutrients and reducing algae growth.
