Chlorophytum geayanum

Chlorophytum geayanum, commonly known as the African peace lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its strap-like, grassy leaves and striking white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.
