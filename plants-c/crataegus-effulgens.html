Crataegus effulgens

Crataegus effulgens, commonly known as the showy crataegus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to North America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red fruits, which are typically produced in late summer to early autumn. The plant features thorny branches and elliptical leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Crataegus effulgens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
