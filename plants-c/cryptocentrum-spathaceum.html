Cryptocentrum spathaceum

Cryptocentrum spathaceum, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom on long, elegant stems. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, providing a habitat for various aquatic life. Cryptocentrum spathaceum thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ponds and water gardens.
