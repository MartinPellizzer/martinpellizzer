Carex runssoroensis

Carex runssoroensis, commonly known as the Runssoro sedge, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Uganda and Tanzania. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its morphology includes slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered spikelets that produce wind-pollinated flowers.

Carex runssoroensis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
