Crotalaria pumila var. obcordata

Crotalaria pumila var. obcordata, commonly known as the smallflower cowpea, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is a low-growing annual herb with yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. It has small, obcordate (heart-shaped) leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
