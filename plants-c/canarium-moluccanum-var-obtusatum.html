Canarium moluccanum var. obtusatum

Canarium moluccanum var. obtusatum, commonly known as the Moluccan canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing aromatic seeds. It is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, thriving in humid climates with well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its timber and traditional uses in local communities.
