Commelina corbisieri

Commelina corbisieri, commonly known as the Madagascar arum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
