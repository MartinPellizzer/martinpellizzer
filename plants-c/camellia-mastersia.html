Camellia mastersia

Camellia mastersia, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces striking flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. Camellia mastersia thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its frost-sensitive nature requires protection in colder climates.
