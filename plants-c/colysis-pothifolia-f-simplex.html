Colysis pothifolia f. simplex

Colysis pothifolia f. simplex, commonly known as the simple leaf pottia, is a variant of the species Pottia pothifolia.

It is a small, mat-forming moss that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features simple, elongated leaves that are often curved at the tips. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This form is notable for its simplified leaf structure compared to other variants of the species.
