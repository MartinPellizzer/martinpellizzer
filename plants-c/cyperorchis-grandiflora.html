Cyperorchis grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered sedge orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of East Asia.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, orchid-like flowers with showy, often purple or pink petals.
