Chamaerepes

Chamaerepes, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its segmented stems that are often adorned with vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. Its unique blooming season, typically during the holiday season, has earned it its common name.

Chamaerepes thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
