Cleisostoma equestre

Cleisostoma equestre, commonly known as the horse cavalry orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, stocky rhizome and produces small, clustered flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a dark purple or brownish lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in terrariums or greenhouses.
