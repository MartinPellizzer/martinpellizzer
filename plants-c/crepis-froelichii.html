Crepis froelichii

Crepis froelichii, commonly known as the Froelich's hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and yellow composite flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like inflorescences.

It is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
