Cheiridopsis macrocalyx

Cheiridopsis macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant sand mat, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit and small, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
