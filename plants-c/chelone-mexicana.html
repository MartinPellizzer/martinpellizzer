Chelone mexicana

Chelone mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Chelone mexicana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and wetland habitats.
