Combretum aureum

Combretum aureum, commonly known as golden flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
