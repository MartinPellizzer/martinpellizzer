Chamaedorea alternans

Chamaedorea alternans, commonly known as the stripe-leaf bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the Aroid family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. This plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive striped pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Chamaedorea alternans is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.
