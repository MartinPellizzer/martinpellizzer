Clematis heracleifolia var. heracleifolia

Clematis heracleifolia var. heracleifolia, commonly known as the giant clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to climb trellises or walls.
