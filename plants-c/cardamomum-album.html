Cardamomum album

Cardamomum album, commonly known as sweet flag, is a perennial herb native to wet tropical regions.

It features a rhizomatous root system with aromatic, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its essential oils, extracted from the rhizomes, are valued for their aromatic and medicinal properties.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in traditional medicine and perfumery.
