Cryptocoryne × ardyi

Cryptocoryne × ardyi, commonly known as the Java cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in soft, moderately warm water with good lighting and nutrient-rich conditions. It is often used in planted tanks for its aesthetic appeal and ability to help maintain water clarity.

Cryptocoryne × ardyi is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among aquarium enthusiasts.
