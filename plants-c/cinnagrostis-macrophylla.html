Cinnagrostis macrophylla

Cinnagrostis macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved cinnagrostis, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to regions of Asia, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands and along the edges of water bodies.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
