Cassia crenulata

Cassia crenulata, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its dense, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are compound, with oval-shaped leaflets that have a slightly serrated edge.

Cassia crenulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
