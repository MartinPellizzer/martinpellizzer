Coelogyne plicatissima

Coelogyne plicatissima, commonly known as the pleated orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pleated petals and a long, slender flower spike that bears multiple fragrant blossoms. The plant typically grows on trees or rocky surfaces, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are often pink or white, with a distinctive waxy texture and a strong, sweet fragrance.

Coelogyne plicatissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
