Combretum pellucidum

Combretum pellucidum, commonly known as the white-leaf combretum, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and opposite, oval-shaped leaves that often have a waxy appearance. Combretum pellucidum is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
