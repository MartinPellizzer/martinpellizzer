Chimonobambusa armata f. tuberculata

Chimonobambusa armata f. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate bamboo, is a unique variant of the species Chimonobambusa armata.

This plant is characterized by its distinctive tuberculate nodes, which give it a rugged and textured appearance. It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in China and Vietnam. The bamboo thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its growth habit is clumping, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
