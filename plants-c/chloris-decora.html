Chloris decora

Chloris decora, commonly known as the ornamental grass, is a perennial plant native to Australia.

It features slender, upright stems that produce small, fluffy seed heads, which are often used in floral arrangements. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Chloris decora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture to garden landscapes.
