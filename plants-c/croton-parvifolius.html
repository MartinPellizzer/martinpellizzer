Croton parvifolius

Croton parvifolius, commonly known as the small-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, often displaying vibrant shades of green, red, and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton parvifolius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
