Carex Spicata F. Humilis

Carex spicata f. humilis is a low-growing sedge that thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is a compact variant of the common spikerush, known for its delicate, grass-like foliage. This plant is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and woodland areas where it contributes to ecological stability. Its small, spike-like inflorescence adds a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining soil health.
