Chenopodium berlandieri var. boscianum

Chenopodium berlandieri var. boscianum, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its rough, spiny leaves and small yellow flowers.
