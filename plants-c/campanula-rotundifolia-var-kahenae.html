Campanula rotundifolia var. kahenae

Campanula rotundifolia var. kahenae, commonly known as the Kahena bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It features rounded, palmate leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
