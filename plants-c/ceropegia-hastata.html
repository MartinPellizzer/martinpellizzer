Ceropegia hastata

Ceropegia hastata, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow, which are adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Ceropegia hastata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
