Croton yunnanensis var. megadontus

Croton yunnanensis var. megadontus, commonly known as the Yunnan croton, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southwestern China.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often a striking green or yellowish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive orange to red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
