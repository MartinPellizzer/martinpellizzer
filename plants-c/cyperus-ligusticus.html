Cyperus ligusticus

Cyperus ligusticus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. Cyperus ligusticus is native to parts of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds and lakes.
