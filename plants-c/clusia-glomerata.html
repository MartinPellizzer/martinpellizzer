Clusia glomerata

Clusia glomerata, commonly known as the wax plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are typically followed by orange or red berries. It is evergreen and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Clusia glomerata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
