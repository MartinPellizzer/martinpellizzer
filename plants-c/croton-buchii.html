Croton buchii

Croton buchii, commonly known as the silver croton, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins and its clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Croton buchii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
