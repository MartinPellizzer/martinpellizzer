Colocasia antiquorum f. eguimo

Colocasia antiquorum f. eguimo, commonly known as the Eguimo colocasia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter, often with a dark green to almost black coloration. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant. Its foliage is also used in traditional practices in some cultures.

The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique floral structures.
