Cleomella alata

Cleomella alata, commonly known as the winged cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, wing-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Cleomella alata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.
