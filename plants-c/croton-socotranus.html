Croton socotranus

Croton socotranus, commonly known as Socotra's croton, is a unique plant endemic to the island of Socotra.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and striking, colorful flowers. The plant has adapted to the arid and rugged environment of Socotra through its deep root system and succulent leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or red, and it produces small, round fruits that contain seeds.

Due to its restricted habitat and conservation status, it is considered a vulnerable species.
