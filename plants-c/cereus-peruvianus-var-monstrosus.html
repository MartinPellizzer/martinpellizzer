Cereus peruvianus var. monstrosus

Cereus peruvianus var. monstrosus, commonly known as the monstrous night bloomer, is a cacti species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance and striking floral display make it a popular choice for desert gardens and collections.
