Conophytum bolusiae subsp. primavernum

Conophytum bolusiae subsp.

primavernum, commonly known as the Spring Beauty, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
