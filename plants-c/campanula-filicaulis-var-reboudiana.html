Campanula filicaulis var. reboudiana

Campanula filicaulis var. reboudiana, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and natural landscapes.
