Coeloglossum lacertiferum

Coeloglossum lacertiferum, commonly known as the dragon's blood orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that resemble a dragon's blood, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its unique floral structure includes a spurred lip that serves as a nectar source for pollinators.

Coeloglossum lacertiferum is a rare and protected species, often found in specific microhabitats across its native range.
