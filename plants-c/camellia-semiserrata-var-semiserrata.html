Camellia semiserrata var. semiserrata

Camellia semiserrata var. semiserrata, commonly known as the Japanese camellia, is a species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are dark green and slightly serrated along the edges. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia semiserrata var. semiserrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
