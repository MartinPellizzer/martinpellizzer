Combretum clarense

Combretum clarense, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often displaying a fiery orange hue. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannah or woodland habitats.

Combretum clarense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
