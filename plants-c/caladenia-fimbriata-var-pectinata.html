Caladenia fimbriata var. pectinata

Caladenia fimbriata var. pectinata, commonly known as the fringed spider orchid, is a striking terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and woodland areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy orchid flower that has a distinctive, intricate pattern of purple and green markings. Its unique floral structure includes a narrow, elongated labellum that enhances its appeal to pollinators.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegance.
