Chamaecrista obolaria

Chamaecrista obolaria, commonly known as the Brazilian burr legume, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, yellow, pea-like flowers and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces elongated, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Chamaecrista obolaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
