Chysis archilae

Chysis archilae, commonly known as the slender chysis, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers.
