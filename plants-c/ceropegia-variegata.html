Ceropegia variegata

Ceropegia variegata, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its delicate appearance and ease of care, it is widely appreciated as a decorative and low-maintenance plant.
