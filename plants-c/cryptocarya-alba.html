Cryptocarya alba

Cryptocarya alba, commonly known as the white cryptocarya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its small, white flowers and elliptical leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments.
