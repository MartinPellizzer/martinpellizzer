Chionanthus macrothyrsus

Chionanthus macrothyrsus, commonly known as the large-thorned chionanthus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
