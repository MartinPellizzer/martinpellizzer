Catasetum tenebrosum f. smaragdinum

Catasetum tenebrosum f. smaragdinum, commonly known as the Emerald Catasetum, is a unique orchid species characterized by its striking emerald-green foliage and intricate floral structure.

It belongs to the Catasetum genus, which is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms involving orchid bees. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers with a distinctive labellum that serves as a landing platform for pollinators. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
