Cotoneaster × crispii

Cotoneaster × crispii, commonly known as the hybrid cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster × crispii is often used in gardens for its seasonal color changes and moderate growth habit.
