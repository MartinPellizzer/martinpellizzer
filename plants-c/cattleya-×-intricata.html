Cattleya × intricata

Cattleya × intricata, commonly known as the intricate cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from various Cattleya species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with intricate patterns and vibrant colors, often featuring a combination of pink, white, and purple hues. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures rather than in soil. It produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical environments.

Cattleya × intricata is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
