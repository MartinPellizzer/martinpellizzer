Convolvulus beluchistanensis

Convolvulus beluchistanensis, commonly known as the Beluchistan bindweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Convolvulaceae, which includes many climbing and trailing plants. The species is characterized by its delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The plant's hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves are notable botanical features.
