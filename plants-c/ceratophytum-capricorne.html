Ceratophytum capricorne

Ceratophytum capricorne, commonly known as the horned fig, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, greenish-gray leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is a drought-tolerant species, adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
