Codonopsis lanceolata f. emaculata

Codonopsis lanceolata f. emaculata, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, elongated stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer, and its fruits are legumes containing several seeds.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
