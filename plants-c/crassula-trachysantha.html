Crassula trachysantha

Crassula trachysantha, commonly known as the Wooly Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves covered in a fine, woolly coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
