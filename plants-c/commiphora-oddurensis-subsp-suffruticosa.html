Commiphora oddurensis subsp. suffruticosa

Commiphora oddurensis subsp.

suffruticosa, commonly known as the African myrrh, is a shrub-like plant native to parts of Africa. It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic resinous exudates.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has small, leathery leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.
