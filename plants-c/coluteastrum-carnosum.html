Coluteastrum carnosum

Coluteastrum carnosum, commonly known as the woolly bladder campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features hairy, fleshy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in dry conditions. Its flowers are pink to purple and are arranged in clusters, adding visual interest to its appearance.

The plant is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
