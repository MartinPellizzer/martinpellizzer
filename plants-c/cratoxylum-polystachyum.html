Cratoxylum polystachyum

Cratoxylum polystachyum, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability.
