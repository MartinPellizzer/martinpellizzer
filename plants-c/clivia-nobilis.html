Clivia nobilis

Clivia nobilis, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a striking evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a reddish or purple tint along the edges. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of striking orange or red flowers. Clivia nobilis thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
