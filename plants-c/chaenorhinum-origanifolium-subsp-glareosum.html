Chaenorhinum origanifolium subsp. glareosum

Chaenorhinum origanifolium subsp.

glareosum, commonly known as the golden chaenorhinum, is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family. It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils in sunny to partially shaded environments.
