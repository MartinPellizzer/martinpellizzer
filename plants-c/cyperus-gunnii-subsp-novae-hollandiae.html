Cyperus gunnii subsp. novae-hollandiae

Cyperus gunnii subsp.

novae-hollandiae, commonly known as the New Holland flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to Australia. It belongs to the sedge family (Cyperaceae) and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves.

The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally wet habitats, such as swampy areas and along waterways. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

This subspecies is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to tolerate saline conditions.
