Cremosperma serratum

Cremosperma serratum, commonly known as the serrated evergreen, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with dark green, leathery leaves that have serrated margins. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.

Cremosperma serratum is also valued for its ability to thrive in shaded environments.
