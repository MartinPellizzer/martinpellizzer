Cleistanthus myrianthus f. ovalis

Cleistanthus myrianthus f. ovalis, commonly known as the oval-leaved cleistanthus, is a variant of the cleistanthus genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, woody capsules containing seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
