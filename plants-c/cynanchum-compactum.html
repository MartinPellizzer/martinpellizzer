Cynanchum compactum

Cynanchum compactum, commonly known as the compact milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

Cynanchum compactum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.
