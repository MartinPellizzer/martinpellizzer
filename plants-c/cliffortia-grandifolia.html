Cliffortia grandifolia

Cliffortia grandifolia, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils along coastal cliffs and mountains. The plant produces delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, giving it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, adapted to the harsh coastal conditions.

Cliffortia grandifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
