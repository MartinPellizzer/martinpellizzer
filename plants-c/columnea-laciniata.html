Columnea laciniata

Columnea laciniata, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Columnea, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and deeply lobed, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Columnea laciniata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in hanging baskets or as a trailing plant.
