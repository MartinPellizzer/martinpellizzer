Cyperus venturii

Cyperus venturii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It is native to parts of Africa and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Cyperus venturii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
