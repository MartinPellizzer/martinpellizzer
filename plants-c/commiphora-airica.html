Commiphora airica

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic resinous sap, which has been used traditionally for medicinal and incense purposes. Commiphora airica is valued for its unique chemical composition, including compounds with potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.
