Canscora diffusa

Canscora diffusa, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its succulent stems and leaves, which store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Canscora diffusa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
