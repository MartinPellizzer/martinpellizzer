Cybianthus densiflorus

Cybianthus densiflorus, commonly known as the denseflower cybianthus, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its thick, fleshy leaves and branched stems. It thrives in arid environments and is adapted to the unique climate of Socotra.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species.
