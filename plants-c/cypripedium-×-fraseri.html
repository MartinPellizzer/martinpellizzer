Cypripedium × fraseri

Cypripedium × fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flower with a deep pink or purple pouch and intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, organic soil. It has a long blooming period, often from late spring to early summer.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation efforts.
