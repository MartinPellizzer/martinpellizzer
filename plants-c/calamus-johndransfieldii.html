Calamus johndransfieldii

Calamus johndransfieldii, commonly known as the Johndansfield's canes.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family, characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnately compound leaves. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often harvested for their aromatic properties and traditional medicinal uses.
