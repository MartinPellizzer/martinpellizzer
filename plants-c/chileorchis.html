Chileorchis

Chileorchis, commonly known as the "chile orchid," is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to South America, particularly in regions of Chile and Argentina. They are characterized by their showy, often brightly colored flowers that resemble chili peppers, hence their common name. Chileorchis species typically grow in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with their succulent-like leaves.

The genus includes several species, each with unique floral structures and ecological adaptations.
