Clethra fargesii

Clethra fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese sweet pepper, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Clethra fargesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
