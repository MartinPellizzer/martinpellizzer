Croton xanthochloros

Croton xanthochloros, commonly known as the golden croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that ranges from yellow to orange and red hues. The plant has large, palmate leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Croton xanthochloros is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant and unique appearance.
