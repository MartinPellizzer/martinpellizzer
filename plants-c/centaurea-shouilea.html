Centaurea shouilea

Centaurea shouilea, commonly known as the Shouilea knapweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features bright blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bristly seed heads. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often found in disturbed areas and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
