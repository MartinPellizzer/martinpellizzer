Cynanchum caudigerum

Cynanchum caudigerum, commonly known as the long-tailed milkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia, including China and Korea.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and features slender, branching stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, producing a distinctive appearance.

Cynanchum caudigerum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
