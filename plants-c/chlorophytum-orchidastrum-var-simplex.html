Chlorophytum orchidastrum var. simplex

Chlorophytum orchidastrum var. simplex, commonly known as the orchid-flowered spider plant, is a hybrid species that combines features of both spider plants and orchids.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and clusters of small, white, orchid-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its delicate, fragrant blooms.

It is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and moderate watering.
