Cattleya mendelii f. morganiae

Cattleya mendelii f. morganiae, commonly known as the Morgan's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant produces large, showy blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
