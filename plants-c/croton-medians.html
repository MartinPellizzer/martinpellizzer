Croton medians

Croton medians, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
