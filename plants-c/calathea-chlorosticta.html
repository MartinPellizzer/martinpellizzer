Calathea chlorosticta

Calathea chlorosticta, commonly known as the silver stripe calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of green, white, and silver hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in indoor gardening. Its leaves are often marked with intricate patterns and have a waxy texture.
