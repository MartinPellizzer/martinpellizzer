Cyathea acutula var. rufescens

Cyathea acutula var. rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk that develops a reddish hue as it matures. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular ornamental plant in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
