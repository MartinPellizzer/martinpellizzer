Centropogon Coccineus Var. Leucostoma

Centropogon coccineus var. leucostoma is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant red flowers that contrast beautifully with its white or pale pink petals. This variant of Centropogon coccineus is often found in humid, tropical environments with well-drained soil. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat requirements.
