Carduus × loretii var. catalaunicus

Carduus × loretii var. catalaunicus, commonly known as the Catalan thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads, which are typical of the Carduus genus. The plant is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in dry, rocky soils. It is often found in Mediterranean climates and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
