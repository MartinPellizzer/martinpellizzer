Ciconium bluecherianum

Ciconium bluecherianum, commonly known as the Bluecherian Bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces elongated, slender stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized horticultural collections.
