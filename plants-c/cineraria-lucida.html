Cineraria lucida

Cineraria lucida, commonly known as the shining cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a silvery sheen, contributing to its striking appearance.

Cineraria lucida is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
