Cistus × dansereaui

Cistus × dansereaui, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a natural hybrid between Cistus ladaniferus and Cistus creticus, characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its evergreen leaves and drought tolerance make it a hardy, low-maintenance plant for Mediterranean climates.
