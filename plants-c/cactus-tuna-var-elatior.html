Cactus tuna var. elatior

Cactus tuna var. elatior, commonly known as the giant cactus tuna, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, ribbed stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh desert environments.

The plant is also known for its ability to produce edible fruits, which are rich in nutrients.
