Coleus anamallayensis

Coleus anamallayensis, commonly known as the Anamallayensis coleus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. It typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
