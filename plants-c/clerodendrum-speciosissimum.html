Clerodendrum speciosissimum

Clerodendrum speciosissimum, commonly known as the flame of the woods, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered fruits that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates.
