Cynoglossum germanicum subsp. rotundum

Cynoglossum germanicum subsp.

rotundum, commonly known as round-leaved forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers with five petals.

The plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The subspecies is distinguished by its rounded leaves and compact growth habit compared to other members of the genus.
