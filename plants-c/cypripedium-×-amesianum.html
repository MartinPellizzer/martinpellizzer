Cypripedium × amesianum

Cypripedium × amesianum, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy, slipper-shaped petals and a distinctive, pouch-like structure at the base of the flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and is known for its vibrant pink to purple hues. It has a long blooming period in late spring to early summer and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened in some regions and is often protected in conservation efforts.
