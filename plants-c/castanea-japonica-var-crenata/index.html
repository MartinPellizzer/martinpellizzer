Castanea Japonica Var. Crenata

Castanea japonica var. crenata is a unique variant of the Japanese chestnut known for its distinctive wavy leaf margins. This plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in shaded forest environments. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners and landscapers. The species is also valued for its edible nuts, which are highly sought after. Despite its beauty and utility, it remains relatively obscure compared to other chestnut varieties.
