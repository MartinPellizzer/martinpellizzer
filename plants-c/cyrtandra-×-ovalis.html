Cyrtandra × ovalis

Cyrtandra × ovalis, commonly known as the hybrid Cyrtandra, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra corymbosa and Cyrtandra pedata. This plant is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
