Ceiba schottii

Ceiba schottii, commonly known as the red ceiba, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and a broad, umbrella-like crown. The tree produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are woody capsules that split open to release seeds.

The bark and leaves of Ceiba schottii have been used in traditional medicine for their potential therapeutic properties.
