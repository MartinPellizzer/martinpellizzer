Cymbidium canaliculatum f. fuscum

Cymbidium canaliculatum f. fuscum, commonly known as the dark-veined cymbidium, is a rare and ornamental orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and striking, fragrant flowers with dark purple markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive. It is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

This orchid is also noted for its cold hardiness, making it suitable for temperate climates.
