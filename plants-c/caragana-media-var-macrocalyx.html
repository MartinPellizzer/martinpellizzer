Caragana media var. macrocalyx

Caragana media var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the Siberian pea shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to Asia.

It features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces showy, pea-like flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant is characterized by its extensive root system, which helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for dry landscapes.

This shrub is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
