Cotyledon fimbriata

Cotyledon fimbriata, commonly known as the fimbriated cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often adorned with intricate, frilly edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
