Convolvulus valentinus var. oranensis

Convolvulus valentinus var. oranensis, commonly known as the Oranensis bindweed, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
