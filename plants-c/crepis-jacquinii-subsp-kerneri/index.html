Crepis Jacquinii Subsp. Kerneri

Crepis jacquinii subsp. kerneri is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of eastern Asia. It is known for its slender stems and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. This subspecies thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous areas. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, it faces threats that highlight the importance of protecting such specialized flora.
