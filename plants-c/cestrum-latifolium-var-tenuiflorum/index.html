Cestrum Latifolium Var. Tenuiflorum

Cestrum tenuiflorum, scientifically known as Cestrum latifolium var. tenuiflorum, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is prized for its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant is commonly cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and nocturnal fragrance. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that all parts of the plant are toxic to humans and animals.
