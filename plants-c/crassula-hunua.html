Crassula hunua

Crassula hunua, commonly known as the Huna Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula hunua is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
