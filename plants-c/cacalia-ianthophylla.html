Cacalia ianthophylla

Cacalia ianthophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and often have a silvery or grayish hue, giving it its common name.

Cacalia ianthophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
