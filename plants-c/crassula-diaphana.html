Crassula diaphana

Crassula diaphana, commonly known as the Glass Plant or Ghost Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, translucent leaves that give it its unique appearance, often appearing almost crystalline in texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its striking visual appeal and low maintenance make it a sought-after houseplant.
