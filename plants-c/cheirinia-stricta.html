Cheirinia stricta

Cheirinia stricta, commonly known as the rigid cheirinia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually gray-green in color. Its flowers are pale blue or violet, arranged in clusters, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Cheirinia stricta is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
