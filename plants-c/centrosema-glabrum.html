Centrosema glabrum

Centrosema glabrum, commonly known as smallflower velvetbean, is a leguminous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in a variety of soil types. It has a deep root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, making it valuable in agricultural systems.

Its foliage is used as forage for livestock, and it is also used in traditional medicine.
