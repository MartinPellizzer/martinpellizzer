Cathartolinum alternum

Cathartolinum alternum, commonly known as the alternating cathartic plant, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions.

It features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and white, which bloom during the warm seasons. Its root system is fibrous and contains bioactive compounds that have been studied for their medicinal properties.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments within dense forest understories.
