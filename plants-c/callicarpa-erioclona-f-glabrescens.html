Callicarpa erioclona f. glabrescens

Callicarpa erioclona f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaf beautyberry, is a variant of the beautyberry plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and clusters of vibrant purple berries that mature in the fall. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in dense spikes, and it is valued for its ornamental berries and ecological benefits.

The smooth-leaf form distinguishes it from other varieties within the Callicarpa genus.
