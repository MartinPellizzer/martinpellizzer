Centaurea hoffmanniana

Centaurea hoffmanniana, commonly known as Hoffmann's coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, bristly leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea hoffmanniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
