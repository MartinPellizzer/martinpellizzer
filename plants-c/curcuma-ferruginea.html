Curcuma ferruginea

Curcuma ferruginea, commonly known as the rust-colored turmeric, is a rhizomatous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces erect, leafy stems with lance-shaped leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. Its rhizomes are valued for their medicinal properties and are often used in traditional remedies.
