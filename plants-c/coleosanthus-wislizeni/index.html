Coleosanthus Wislizeni

Coleosanthus wislizeni is a rare and striking plant native to the southwestern United States. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring bright yellow flowers that stand out against its green foliage. This species thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its name honors the botanist John Andreas wislizenus, who made significant contributions to the study of desert flora. Despite its beauty, Coleosanthus wislizeni remains a lesser-known plant, often overlooked in mainstream botanical discussions.
