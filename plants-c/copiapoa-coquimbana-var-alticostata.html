Copiapoa coquimbana var. alticostata

Copiapoa coquimbana var. alticostata, commonly known as the Alticostate cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Atacama Desert in Chile.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a slow growth rate and is adapted to arid conditions with minimal water requirements.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and desert gardens.
