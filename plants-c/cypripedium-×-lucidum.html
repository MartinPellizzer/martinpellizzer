Cypripedium × lucidum

Cypripedium × lucidum, commonly known as the golden tassel orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers with prominent, spurred sepals and a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces a single, showy flower per stem.

Cypripedium × lucidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
