Cranichis turkeliae

Cranichis turkeliae, commonly known as the Turkel's orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptation to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
