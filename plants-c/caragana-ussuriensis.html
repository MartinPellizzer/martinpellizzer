Caragana ussuriensis

Caragana ussuriensis, commonly known as Siberian pea shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable for soil enrichment. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often used in reclamation projects due to its hardiness.

Its small, edible pods contain seeds that are used in traditional medicine and as a food source.
