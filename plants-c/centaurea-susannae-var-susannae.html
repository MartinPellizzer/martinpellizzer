Centaurea susannae var. susannae

Centaurea susannae var. susannae, commonly known as the Susan's Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, bristly foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in disturbed soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and has been cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
