Commelina pedunculosa

Commelina pedunculosa, commonly known as the blue false forget-me-not, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems and produces small, blue or violet flowers with three petals. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
