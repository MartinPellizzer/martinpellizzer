Cyrtonema hirtellum

Cyrtonema hirtellum, commonly known as the Chinese sandmat, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its mat-forming growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Cyrtonema hirtellum is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
