Coptosapelta

Coptosapelta, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that turn a striking yellow in the autumn. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by winged fruits that aid in seed dispersal. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Leguminosae, also known as the Fabaceae family.
