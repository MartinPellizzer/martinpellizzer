Cotoneaster lidjiangensis

Cotoneaster lidjiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny branches and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

Cotoneaster lidjiangensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness.
