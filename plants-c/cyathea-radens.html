Cyathea radens

Cyathea radens, commonly known as the red-stemmed tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a fibrous, reddish-brown sheath. The plant produces large, compound fronds that can reach several meters in length, with a distinctive, feather-like appearance. Cyathea radens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in tropical ecosystems.
