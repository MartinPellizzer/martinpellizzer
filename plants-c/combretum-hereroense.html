Combretum hereroense

Combretum hereroense, commonly known as the Herero flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its bright red to orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
