Croton rakotoniainii

Croton rakotoniainii, commonly known as the Madagascar croton, is a unique shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, often multicolored leaves that range from green to red, yellow, and orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton rakotoniainii typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it requires careful handling due to its toxic sap.
