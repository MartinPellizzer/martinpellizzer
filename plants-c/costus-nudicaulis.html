Costus nudicaulis

Costus nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender costus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric, and is characterized by its aromatic, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some tropical gardens.
