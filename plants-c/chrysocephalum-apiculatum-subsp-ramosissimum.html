Chrysocephalum apiculatum subsp. ramosissimum

Chrysocephalum apiculatum subsp.

ramosissimum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring.
