Clerodendrum poggei

Clerodendrum poggei, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a rough texture. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Clerodendrum poggei is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
