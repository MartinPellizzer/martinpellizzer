Cardiopteris moluccana

Cardiopteris moluccana, commonly known as the mountain fern or Chinese mountain fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical structure reflects its adaptation to humid, forested habitats.
