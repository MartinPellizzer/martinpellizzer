Coelogyne mooreana f. alba

Coelogyne mooreana f. alba, commonly known as the White Moore's orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers, which are often adorned with subtle pink or purple markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, storing moisture and nutrients.

This orchid is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and rarity, making it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts.
