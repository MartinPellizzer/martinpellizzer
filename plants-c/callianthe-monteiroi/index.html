Callianthe Monteiroi

Callianthe monteiroi is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Brazil. Known for its vibrant purple and pink flowers, it captivates botanists and nature lovers alike. This species was named in honor of the Brazilian botanist Monteiro, who contributed significantly to plant taxonomy. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of scientific interest. Despite its beauty, Callianthe monteiroi remains under threat due to habitat loss and environmental changes.
