Cleisostoma aciliatum

Cleisostoma aciliatum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its striking, fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cleisostoma aciliatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
