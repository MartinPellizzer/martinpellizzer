Chenopodium Ambrosioides Var. Anthelminticum

Chenopodium ambrosioides var. anthelminticum is a unique plant known for its medicinal properties. It is commonly referred to as wormseed or wild buckwheat. This variant of Chenopodium is native to various regions in the Americas. The plant has been traditionally used to treat parasitic infections due to its anthelmintic effects. Its historical and contemporary uses highlight its importance in herbal medicine.
