Codiaeum Montanum

Codiaeum, scientifically known as Codiaeum montanum, is a striking tropical plant renowned for its vibrant, colorful leaves. Native to the Philippines, it thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant. Its foliage displays a dazzling array of hues, including red, yellow, orange, and green, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens. Easy to care for, it requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight to flourish. This plant not only adds visual interest to any space but also brings a touch of the tropics to any setting.
