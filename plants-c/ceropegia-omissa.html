Ceropegia omissa

Ceropegia omissa, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a favored ornamental plant in many gardens.
