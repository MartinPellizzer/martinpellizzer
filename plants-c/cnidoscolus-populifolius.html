Cnidoscolus populifolius

Cnidoscolus populifolius, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by edible, spiny fruits. Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine, and the plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
