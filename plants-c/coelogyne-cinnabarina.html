Coelogyne cinnabarina

Coelogyne cinnabarina, commonly known as the Red Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

Coelogyne cinnabarina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
