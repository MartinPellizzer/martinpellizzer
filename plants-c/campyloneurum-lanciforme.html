Campyloneurum lanciforme

Campyloneurum lanciforme, commonly known as the lance-leaf fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its slender, lance-shaped fronds that are typically 30 to 60 centimeters long. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that produce new shoots. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
