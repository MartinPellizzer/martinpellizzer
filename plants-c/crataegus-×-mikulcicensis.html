Crataegus × mikulcicensis

Crataegus × mikulcicensis, commonly known as the Mikulčić's hawthorn, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the Carpathian Mountains and is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red or yellow berries that are typically edible but often have a tart flavor. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.

Crataegus × mikulcicensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
