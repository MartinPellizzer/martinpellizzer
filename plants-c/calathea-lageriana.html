Calathea lageriana

Calathea lageriana, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, patterned leaves that change color and orientation throughout the day, a phenomenon known as nyctinasty. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings. Its foliage features intricate veining and a variety of hues, including deep green, silver, and purple.

Calathea lageriana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
