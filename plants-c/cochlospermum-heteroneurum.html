Cochlospermum heteroneurum

Cochlospermum heteroneurum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
