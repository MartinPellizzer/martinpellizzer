Conyza canariensis

Conyza canariensis, commonly known as "Goatweed," is an annual flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. Its leaves are usually hairy and alternate along the stem.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
