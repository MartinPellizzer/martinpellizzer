Cotyledon pendens

Cotyledon pendens, commonly known as the hanging cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor plant collections.
