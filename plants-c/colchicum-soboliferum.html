Colchicum soboliferum

Colchicum soboliferum, commonly known as the Siberian colchicum, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking violet to blue-purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has a short, sturdy stem that supports its large, showy inflorescence. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and often persist through the winter.

Colchicum soboliferum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in well-drained soil.
