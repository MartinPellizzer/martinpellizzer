Chirita swinglei

Chirita swinglei, commonly known as Swingle's ivy, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a trailing perennial shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Chirita swinglei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
