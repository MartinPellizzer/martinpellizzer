Conamomum squarrosum

Conamomum squarrosum, commonly known as the squarrosum wild banana, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of elongated, edible berries. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. Its berries are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
