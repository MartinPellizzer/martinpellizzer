Ceanothus × burkwoodii

Ceanothus × burkwoodii, commonly known as the Burbank ceanothus, is a hybrid shrub native to California.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, blue to purple flowers in the spring. The plant is evergreen, with a compact growth habit that makes it suitable for small gardens or rockeries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
