Conyza neocandolleana

Conyza neocandolleana, commonly known as the Neocandollean Conyza, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, producing small yellow flowers that attract pollinators.

The species is often found in regions with temperate to subtropical climates.
