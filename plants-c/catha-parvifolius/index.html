Catha Parvifolius

Catha parvifolius is a unique plant native to the arid regions of East Africa. It belongs to the same family as the khat plant, known for its stimulant properties. This species is characterized by its small leaves and robust stems that thrive in harsh environments. Despite its limited distribution, it holds cultural and historical significance in local communities. Its potential medicinal and psychoactive uses continue to attract scientific interest.
