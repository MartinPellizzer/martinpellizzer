Croton oligostemon

Croton oligostemon, commonly known as the red-flowered croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant red bracts that surround small yellow flowers, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
