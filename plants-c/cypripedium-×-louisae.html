Cypripedium × louisae

Cypripedium × louisae, commonly known as the Showy Lady's Slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white with a yellow throat. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems emerging from a rhizome system. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette and are broad and lance-shaped.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
