Corniola melia

Corniola melia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, waxy leaves that are typically heart-shaped and come in various shades of green. Its showy, tubular flowers bloom in shades of violet, pink, and white, often with a contrasting center.

Corniola melia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
