Cissus leonardii

Cissus leonardii, commonly known as the African snow vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Vitaceae and is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
