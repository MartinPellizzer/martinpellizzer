Coelogyne celebensis

Coelogyne celebensis, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.

Coelogyne celebensis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
