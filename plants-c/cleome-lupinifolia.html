Cleome lupinifolia

Cleome lupinifolia, commonly known as the spider flower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like blooms that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, while its stems are often branched and upright.
