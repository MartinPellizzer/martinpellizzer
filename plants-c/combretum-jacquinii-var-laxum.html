Combretum jacquinii var. laxum

Combretum jacquinii var. laxum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in dense clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glabrous and have a smooth texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warmer climates.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to produce a profusion of blooms during the dry season.
