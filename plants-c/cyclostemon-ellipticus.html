Cyclostemon ellipticus

Cyclostemon ellipticus, commonly known as the elliptic cyclostemon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its elliptical, waxy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
