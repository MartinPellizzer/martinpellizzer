Coelogyne cristata var. hololeuca

Coelogyne cristata var. hololeuca, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers and a compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks in the understory of forests. Its leaves are ovate and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty in both home and garden settings.
