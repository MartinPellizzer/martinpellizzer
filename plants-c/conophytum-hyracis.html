Conophytum hyracis

Conophytum hyracis, commonly known as the hyrax conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
