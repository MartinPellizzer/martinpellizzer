Centaurea macedonica subsp. parnonia

Centaurea macedonica subsp.

parnonia, commonly known as the Parnon centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers.
