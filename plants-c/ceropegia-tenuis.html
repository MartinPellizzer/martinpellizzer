Ceropegia tenuis

Ceropegia tenuis, commonly known as the slender ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its trailing stems and fleshy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia tenuis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
