Cyclogramma leveillei

Cyclogramma leveillei, commonly known as the Cape Verde sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It has narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to arid environments.

Cyclogramma leveillei is often found in coastal and inland areas with low rainfall.
