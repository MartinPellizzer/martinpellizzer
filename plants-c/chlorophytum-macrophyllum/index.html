Chlorophytum Macrophyllum

Chlorophytum macrophyllum is a striking plant known for its long, arching leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardens and hanging baskets. The plant's name refers to its large leaves, which are often variegated with white or yellow streaks. Easy to care for, it's a favorite among both novice and experienced plant lovers.
