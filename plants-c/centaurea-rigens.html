Centaurea rigens

Centaurea rigens, commonly known as the rigid centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its slender, erect stems are often covered in fine hairs, and its blue to purple flowers bloom in summer.

Centaurea rigens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
