Croton lenheirensis

Croton lenheirensis, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that vary in color from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange or red fruits that contain three seeds each. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
