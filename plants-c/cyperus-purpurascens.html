Cyperus purpurascens

Cyperus purpurascens, commonly known as purple flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the sedge family (Cyperaceae) and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Cyperus purpurascens is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive purple-tinged foliage.
