Corydalis decipiens

Corydalis decipiens, commonly known as the "false cowslip," is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

Corydalis decipiens is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and rocky habitats, where it thrives in partial shade to full sun.
