Corypha licuala

Corypha licuala, commonly known as the giant bamboo palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery leaves that give it a palm-like appearance. The plant produces a unique, elongated inflorescence that resembles a sword, containing both male and female flowers. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive addition to tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
