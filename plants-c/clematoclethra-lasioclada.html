Clematoclethra lasioclada

Clematoclethra lasioclada, commonly known as the Japanese clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers in clusters during late spring to early summer. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that release seeds in the fall.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
