Crinum knyffii

Crinum knyffii, commonly known as the Knyff's crinum, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllis family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces strap-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Crinum knyffii is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be found in gardens and botanical collections.
