Cinnamomum delavayi var. aromaticum

Cinnamomum delavayi var. aromaticum, commonly known as the fragrant cinnamon tree, is a species native to China, particularly in Yunnan Province.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with lance-shaped, dark green leaves that have a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers followed by small, round berries that contain a single seed. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their aromatic properties.

The variety is valued for its unique fragrance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
