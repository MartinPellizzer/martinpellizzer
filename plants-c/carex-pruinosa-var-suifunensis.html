Carex pruinosa var. suifunensis

Carex pruinosa var. suifunensis, commonly known as the Suifun sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
