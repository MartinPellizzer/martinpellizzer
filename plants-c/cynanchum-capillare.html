Cynanchum capillare

Cynanchum capillare, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes other milkweeds known for their milky sap. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring slender stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellowish-green with a distinctive hooded structure, and it produces small, round fruits called follicles.

Cynanchum capillare is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
