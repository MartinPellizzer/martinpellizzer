Chenopodium linifolium

Chenopodium linifolium, commonly known as silverleaf goosefoot, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides or in fields, and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round seeds.

Chenopodium linifolium is sometimes used in traditional medicine and is also considered a weed in agricultural settings.
