Clitoria mearnsii

Clitoria mearnsii, commonly known as Australian wisteria, is a climbing legume native to Australia.

It features trifoliate leaves and produces vibrant purple or violet pea-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and as a nitrogen-fixing cover crop.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not managed properly.
