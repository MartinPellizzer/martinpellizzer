Cattleya dowiana var. aurea-alba

Cattleya dowiana var. aurea-alba, commonly known as the White Gold Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking white and gold-colored flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features long, slender leaves and produces large, fragrant blooms with a distinctive golden hue. It is typically grown as a hybrid for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.

This variety is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
