Capparis reflexa

Capparis reflexa, commonly known as the rock caper, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, thorny stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces edible, tangy fruits that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are alternate and have a slightly hairy texture.

Capparis reflexa is well adapted to arid environments and can thrive in rocky soils.
