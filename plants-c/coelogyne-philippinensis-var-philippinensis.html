Coelogyne philippinensis var. philippinensis

Coelogyne philippinensis var. philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a short, stocky rhizome and produces a single, large, lance-shaped pseudobulb. The plant bears a cluster of narrow, linear leaves and is renowned for its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.
