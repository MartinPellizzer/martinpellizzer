Cyperus rubroviridis

Cyperus rubroviridis, commonly known as the red and yellow flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the sedge family (Cyperaceae) and is characterized by its triangular stems and dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Cyperus rubroviridis thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking color contrast and ability to thrive in waterlogged soils.
