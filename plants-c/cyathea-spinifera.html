Cyathea spinifera

Cyathea spinifera, commonly known as the spinifera tree fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Cyatheaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that give it a lush, tropical appearance. Cyathea spinifera is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

Its growth is slow, making it a long-lived species with a unique ecological role in its native habitat.
