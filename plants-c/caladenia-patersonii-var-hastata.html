Caladenia patersonii var. hastata

Caladenia patersonii var. hastata, commonly known as the Paterson's Spider Orchid, is a striking orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its elongated, spider-like appearance, with a single, slender flower that is typically white or pale pink. The plant grows from a bulbous tuber and produces a single leaf that is often broader than it is long. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathland or open forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
