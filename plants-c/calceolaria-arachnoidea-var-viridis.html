Calceolaria arachnoidea var. viridis

Calceolaria arachnoidea var. viridis, commonly known as the green spider orchid, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru, where it thrives in high-altitude cloud forests. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.
