Crassula plegmatoides

Crassula plegmatoides, commonly known as the Fat Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Crassula plegmatoides is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
