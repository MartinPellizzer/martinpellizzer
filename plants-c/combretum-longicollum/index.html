Combretum Longicollum

Combretum longicollum is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, delicate flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of red and orange. This species thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its health and longevity.
