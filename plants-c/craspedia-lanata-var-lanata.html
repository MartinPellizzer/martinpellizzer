Craspedia lanata var. lanata

Craspedia lanata var. lanata, commonly known as the woolly craspedia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique texture and color contrast make it a visually striking addition to any garden.
