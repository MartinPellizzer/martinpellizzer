Carex leporina subsp. ovalis

Carex leporina subsp.

ovalis, commonly known as the oval sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of North America. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants commonly found in wet or moist environments.
