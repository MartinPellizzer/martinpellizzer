Cirsium pubigerum var. spinosum

Cirsium pubigerum var. spinosum, commonly known as the prickly thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs and have a distinctive serrated edge, while its stems are often hairy and spiny.

This species is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
