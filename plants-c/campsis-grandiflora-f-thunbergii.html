Campsis grandiflora f. thunbergii

Campsis grandiflora f. thunbergii, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree or Thunberg's flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red tubular flowers that appear in clusters, attracting birds and insects. The leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to produce vibrant autumn foliage.
