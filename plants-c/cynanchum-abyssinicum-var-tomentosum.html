Cynanchum abyssinicum var. tomentosum

Cynanchum abyssinicum var. tomentosum, commonly known as Abyssinian milkweed, is a perennial shrub native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally in local remedies.
