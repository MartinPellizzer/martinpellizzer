Cattleya × picta

Cattleya × picta, commonly known as the picta orchid, is a hybrid orchid species prized for its vibrant and intricate flower patterns.

It originates from crosses between Cattleya labiata and Cattleya mossiae, resulting in a unique blend of characteristics from both parent species. The plant is known for its large, showy blooms that often feature contrasting color zones and intricate designs. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Cattleya × picta is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
