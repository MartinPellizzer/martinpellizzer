Cousinia kelardashtiana

Cousinia kelardashtiana, commonly known as the Kelardasht cudweed, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It features slender stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Cousinia kelardashtiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
