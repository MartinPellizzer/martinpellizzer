Coelogyne speciosa subsp. fimbriata

Coelogyne speciosa subsp.

fimbriata, commonly known as the fimbriate orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia. It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive yellow lip. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
