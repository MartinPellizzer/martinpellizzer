Calceolaria rugosa var. incisa

Calceolaria rugosa var. incisa, commonly known as the serrated slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically hairy and have a serrated edge, giving the plant its specific variety name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
