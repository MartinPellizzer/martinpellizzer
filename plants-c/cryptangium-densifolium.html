Cryptangium densifolium

Cryptangium densifolium, commonly known as the dense-leaved cryptangium, is a shrub native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage that provides habitat for various small animals and insects. The plant typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its flowers are small and white, often clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to its ecological importance and unique morphology, it is a notable species in its native flora.
