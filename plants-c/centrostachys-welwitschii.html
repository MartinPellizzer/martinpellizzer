Centrostachys welwitschii

Centrostachys welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's centrostachys, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and grayish-green foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
