Croton macrostachyus

Croton macrostachyus, commonly known as the giant croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of yellow-green flowers followed by woody, spiny capsules containing small, oval seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its striking foliage, it is popular as an ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
