Cannabis sativa var. afghanica

Cannabis sativa var. afghanica, commonly known as Afghan cannabis, is a variety native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan.

It is characterized by its tall stature, often reaching heights of up to 5 meters, and its long, narrow leaves. The plant produces dense, resinous buds that are rich in cannabinoids such as THC and CBD. It thrives in temperate climates with ample sunlight and well-drained soil.

Afghan cannabis is valued for its strong psychoactive effects and historical use in traditional medicine and cultural practices.
