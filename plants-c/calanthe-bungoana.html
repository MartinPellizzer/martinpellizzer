Calanthe bungoana

Calanthe bungoana, commonly known as the Bungoan orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. Calanthe bungoana prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
