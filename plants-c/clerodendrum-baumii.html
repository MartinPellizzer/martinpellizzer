Clerodendrum baumii

Clerodendrum baumii, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often brightly colored in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge.
