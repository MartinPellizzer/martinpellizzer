Cereus knightii

Cereus knightii, commonly known as the Knight's candlestick cactus, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a tall, columnar cactus with a ribbed stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces white, nocturnal flowers that bloom during the summer months. Its stems are covered in sparse, white spines and are adapted to store water in arid environments.

Cereus knightii thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
