Cucumis glaber

Cucumis glaber, commonly known as the naked cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant produces small, smooth-skinned fruits that are typically green and slightly bitter. It thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and requires full sunlight.

The plant is often cultivated for its edible fruits, which are used in various culinary applications.
