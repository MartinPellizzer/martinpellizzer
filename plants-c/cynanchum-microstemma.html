Cynanchum microstemma

Cynanchum microstemma, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruit is a follicle containing several seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
