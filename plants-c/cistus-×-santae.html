Cistus × santae

Cistus × santae, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Cistus × santae plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
