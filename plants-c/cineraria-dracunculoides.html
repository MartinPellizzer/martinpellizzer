Cineraria dracunculoides

Cineraria dracunculoides, commonly known as the giant cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are large, palmately lobed, and often covered in a dense, woolly coating.

The flowers are usually blue or violet, with yellow centers, and bloom in spring and summer.
