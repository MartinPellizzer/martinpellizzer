Cytisogenista megalantha

Cytisogenista megalantha, commonly known as the giant goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and its profuse display of bright yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces legume pods that contain its seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cytisogenista megalantha is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
