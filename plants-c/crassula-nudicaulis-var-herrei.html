Crassula nudicaulis var. herrei

Crassula nudicaulis var. herrei, commonly known as the "Herr's naked stem crassula," is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a distinctive addition to rock gardens or indoor collections.
