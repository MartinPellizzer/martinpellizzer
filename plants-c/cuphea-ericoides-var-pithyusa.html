Cuphea ericoides var. pithyusa

Cuphea ericoides var. pithyusa, commonly known as the pink flame bush, is a compact shrub native to Mexico.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, which attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has dense, glandular hairs on its stems and leaves, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its foliage is typically narrow and upright, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
