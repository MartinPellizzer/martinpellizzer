Codonoboea puncticulata

Codonoboea puncticulata, commonly known as the punctuated codonopsis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.
