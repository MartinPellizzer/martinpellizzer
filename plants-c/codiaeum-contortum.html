Codiaeum contortum

Codiaeum contortum, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from red, orange, and yellow to green, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by winged fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Codiaeum contortum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.
