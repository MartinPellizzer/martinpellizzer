Cattleya × schroederiana

Cattleya × schroederiana, commonly known as the Schröder's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits a range of vibrant flower colors, including pink, red, and white.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant typically produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker than the rest of the bloom. It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and fragrance, which can be quite intoxicating.

Cattleya × schroederiana is a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and garden displays due to its adaptability and long-lasting blooms.
