Cotyledon whitei

Cotyledon whitei, commonly known as the white cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a white or pale green coloration. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, usually in summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cotyledon whitei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
