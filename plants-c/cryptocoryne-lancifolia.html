Cryptocoryne lancifolia

Cryptocoryne lancifolia, commonly known as the lancifolia waterweed, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moderately lit freshwater environments and is often used in planted aquariums. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread easily across the substrate.

Its ability to tolerate a wide range of water conditions makes it a versatile choice for both novice and experienced aquarists.
