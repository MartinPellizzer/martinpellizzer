Ctenopteris spongiosa

Ctenopteris spongiosa, commonly known as the sponge fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures that resemble a sponge, giving it its common name. The plant features a creeping rhizome with numerous small, clustered fronds that are typically light green in color. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a soft, feathery texture.

Ctenopteris spongiosa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
