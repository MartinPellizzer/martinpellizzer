Cattleya amethystina

Cattleya amethystina, commonly known as the purple lady, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and white flowers, which bloom in a spiral arrangement on a sturdy stem. The plant has broad, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cattleya amethystina is prized for its beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
