Cyathea hartii

Cyathea hartii, commonly known as the Hart's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of leaves that can reach several meters in height, giving it a majestic appearance. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting the plant's growth and contributing to its structural stability.

Cyathea hartii plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
