Cyrtonema sphenolobum

Cyrtonema sphenolobum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are typically orange to red in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Cyrtonema sphenolobum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
