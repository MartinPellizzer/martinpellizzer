Crinum speciosum

Crinum speciosum, commonly known as the showy crinum, is a striking herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and yellow. The plant features strap-like, linear leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Crinum speciosum typically grows in wetlands or along riverbanks, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.

It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
