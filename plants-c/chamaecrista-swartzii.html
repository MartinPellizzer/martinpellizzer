Chamaecrista swartzii

Chamaecrista swartzii, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
