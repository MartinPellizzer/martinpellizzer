Cattleya lundii

Cattleya lundii, commonly known as the pink lady, is a striking orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and white flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Cattleya lundii is prized for its beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
