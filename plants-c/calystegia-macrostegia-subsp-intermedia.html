Calystegia macrostegia subsp. intermedia

Calystegia macrostegia subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the slender bladderbladder, is a herbaceous vine native to North America. It is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture.

The flowers are yellow with five petals and are borne in clusters along the stems. The fruit is a bladder-like capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.
