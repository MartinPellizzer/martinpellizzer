Conchocarpus macrophyllus

Conchocarpus macrophyllus, commonly known as the giant swamp banyan, is a tropical tree belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in wetland and swampy environments, often forming dense thickets.
