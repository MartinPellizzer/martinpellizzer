Calycobolus amazonicus

Calycobolus amazonicus, commonly known as the Amazonian starflower, is a rare flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from deep violet to pale blue. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research.
