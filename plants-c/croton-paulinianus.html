Croton paulinianus

Croton paulinianus, commonly known as the yellow croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red and orange hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance.

Croton paulinianus thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
