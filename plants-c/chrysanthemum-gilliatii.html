Chrysanthemum gilliatii

Chrysanthemum gilliatii, commonly known as the Cape chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows as a shrub with narrow, linear leaves and produces showy yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Chrysanthemum gilliatii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
