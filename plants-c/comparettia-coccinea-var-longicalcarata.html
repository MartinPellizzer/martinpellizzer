Comparettia coccinea var. longicalcarata

Comparettia coccinea var. longicalcarata, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features striking, showy flowers in shades of red and orange, often with a yellow throat, and is prized for its ornamental value. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and produces aerial roots that help it cling to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, contributing to its overall aesthetic appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
