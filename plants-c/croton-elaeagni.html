Croton elaeagni

Croton elaeagni, commonly known as the silver croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often display shades of yellow, white, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton elaeagni is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.
