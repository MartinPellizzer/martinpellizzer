Ceropegia tsaiana

Ceropegia tsaiana, commonly known as the African starfish plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, branching stems that often bear clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its leaves and stems, making it drought-tolerant. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, with a unique, tubular structure that attracts specific pollinators.

Ceropegia tsaiana is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
