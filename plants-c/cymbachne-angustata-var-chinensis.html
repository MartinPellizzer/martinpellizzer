Cymbachne angustata var. chinensis

Cymbachne angustata var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese narrowleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and mountainous regions. The plant has slender, elongated leaves with a narrow, grass-like appearance, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its fine texture and ability to thrive in humid, temperate climates.

This variety is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
