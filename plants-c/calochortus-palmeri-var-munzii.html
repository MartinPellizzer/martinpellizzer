Calochortus palmeri var. munzii

Calochortus palmeri var. munzii, commonly known as the Munz's mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and gravelly soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bulb-like structures called corms.

This variety is named after the botanist John T. Munz and is often found in California and Nevada.
