Crateva radiatiflora

Crateva radiatiflora, commonly known as the radiate soursop, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a large, spiky berry that contains a single large seed.

The botanical characteristics of Crateva radiatiflora make it a distinctive member of the Malvaceae family.
