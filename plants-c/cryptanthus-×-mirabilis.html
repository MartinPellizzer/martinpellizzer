Cryptanthus × mirabilis

Cryptanthus × mirabilis, commonly known as the flame flower or rainbow plant, is a hybrid succulent native to Brazil.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, grass-like leaves that display a wide range of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and green. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among collectors and is often grown in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
