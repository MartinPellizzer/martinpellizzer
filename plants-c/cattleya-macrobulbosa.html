Cattleya macrobulbosa

Cattleya macrobulbosa, commonly known as the giant cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter. The plant produces thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in seasonal climates. Its flowers are typically fragrant and come in a range of colors including white, pink, and purple.

Cattleya macrobulbosa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
