Croton drouetii

Croton drouetii, commonly known as the Drouet's croton, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored leaves that can range from green to red, orange, or even purple. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton drouetii typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.
