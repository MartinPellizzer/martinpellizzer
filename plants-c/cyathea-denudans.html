Cyathea denudans

Cyathea denudans, commonly known as the naked tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to several meters.

Cyathea denudans plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
