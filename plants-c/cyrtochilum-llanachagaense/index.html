Cyrtochilum Llanachagaense

Llanachaga orchid, scientifically known as Cyrtochilum llanachagaense, is a rare and unique species found exclusively in the Llanachaga Forest Reserve in Uganda. This delicate orchid is characterized by its striking violet petals and intricate floral structure, which make it a standout among tropical orchids. Despite its beauty, the plant faces significant threats from habitat loss and climate change, putting its survival at risk. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endemic species and preserve its ecological role. Its discovery and study offer valuable insights into the biodiversity of the region and the importance of protecting fragile ecosystems.
