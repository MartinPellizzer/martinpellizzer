Calytrix ecalycata subsp. pubescens

Calytrix ecalycata subsp.

pubescens, commonly known as the silky calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, glandular-hairy foliage and showy, pink to red flowers.

The species belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. Its flowers are typically arranged in clusters and bloom during the spring and summer seasons.

The plant is adapted to a range of soil types and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.
