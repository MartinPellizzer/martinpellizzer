Crassula quadrifaria subsp. coegensis

Crassula quadrifaria subsp.

coegensis, commonly known as the Coega crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge under stress.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This subspecies is valued for its compact growth habit and adaptability to arid environments.
