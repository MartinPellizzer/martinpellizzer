Craspedia uniflora var. grandis

Craspedia uniflora var. grandis, commonly known as the large-flowered swamp everlasting, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a creeping habit with soft, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in wet or damp soils, often found in swampy areas or along waterways.

Its flowers are long-lasting and are used in floral arrangements, making it a popular ornamental plant.
