Crotalaria zuccariniana

Crotalaria zuccariniana, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has palmately compound leaves and produces elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in grasslands and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Crotalaria zuccariniana is also used in ecological restoration projects due to its role in supporting pollinators and improving soil fertility.
