Coxella dieffenbachii

Coxella dieffenbachii, commonly known as the Dieffenbachia, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Coxella dieffenbachii is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
