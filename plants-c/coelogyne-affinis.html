Coelogyne affinis

Coelogyne affinis, commonly known as the white-lipped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with narrow, dark green leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
