Coelogyne merrillii

Coelogyne merrillii, commonly known as the Borneo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, with flowers that can reach up to 15 cm in length. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, and it prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Coelogyne merrillii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in both greenhouse and outdoor settings.
