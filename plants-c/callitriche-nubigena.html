Callitriche nubigena

Callitriche nubigena, commonly known as the cloud willow or floating heart, is a small aquatic plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, streams, and wet meadows. The plant has slender, branching stems that float on the water's surface, with small, heart-shaped leaves that are often submerged.
