Centaurea desmicephala

Centaurea desmicephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny habitats. The plant features slender stems and small, spiny leaves, which help it conserve water in arid conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Centaurea desmicephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
