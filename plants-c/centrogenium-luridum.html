Centrogenium luridum

Centrogenium luridum, commonly known as the yellow centrose.

It is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in wetlands and along the edges of swamps.

Centrogenium luridum is an important species for pollinators and contributes to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
