Calathea Micans

The calathea micans is a captivating tropical plant known for its striking, patterned leaves. Often referred to as the prayer plant, it is prized for its unique foliage that changes color and orientation throughout the day. Native to the rainforests of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its ability to adapt to indoor conditions makes it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts. With proper care, calathea micans can bring a touch of exotic beauty to any home or office space.
