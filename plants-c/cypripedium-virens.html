Cypripedium virens

Cypripedium virens, commonly known as the green waxlip, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often reddish or brownish in color. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single flower per stem.

Cypripedium virens is a perennial herb that thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in association with other orchid species.
