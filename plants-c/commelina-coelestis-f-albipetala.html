Commelina coelestis f. albipetala

Commelina coelestis f. albipetala, commonly known as the white-petaled blue mistflower, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant blue-violet flowers with striking white petals, creating a striking contrast that makes it highly desirable in gardens. The plant has heart-shaped, broad leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Its unique appearance and easy care requirements make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
