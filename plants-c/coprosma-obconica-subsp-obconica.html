Coprosma obconica subsp. obconica

Coprosma obconica subsp.

obconica, commonly known as the broadleaf coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It features elliptical to ovate leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers clustered in umbels, followed by red or orange berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, compact growth.
