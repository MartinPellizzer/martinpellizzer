Cotula peduncularis

Cotula peduncularis, commonly known as the yellow daisy or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Cotula peduncularis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
