Centaurea kizildaghensis

Centaurea kizildaghensis, commonly known as the Kizildag centaury, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing a compact growth form with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disc of tubular flowers.

Centaurea kizildaghensis is noted for its ornamental value and is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique adaptations to its habitat.
