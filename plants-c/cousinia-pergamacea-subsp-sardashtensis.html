Cousinia pergamacea subsp. sardashtensis

Cousinia pergamacea subsp.

sardashtensis, commonly known as the Sardashtensis cudweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, compact growth habit.

The plant features basal leaves that are linear to oblanceolate in shape, often with a bluish-gray hue. Its flowers are small and clustered in composite heads, typically blooming in shades of white or pale yellow.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.
