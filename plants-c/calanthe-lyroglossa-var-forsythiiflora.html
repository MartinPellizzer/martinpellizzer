Calanthe lyroglossa var. forsythiiflora

Calanthe lyroglossa var. forsythiiflora, commonly known as the Yellow Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which are typically borne on upright inflorescences. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts worldwide.
