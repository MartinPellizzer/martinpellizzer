Cyphocarpus rigescens

Cyphocarpus rigescens, commonly known as the rigid cyphocarp, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly rough texture.

Cyphocarpus rigescens is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.
