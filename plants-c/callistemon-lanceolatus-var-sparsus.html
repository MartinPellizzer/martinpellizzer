Callistemon lanceolatus var. sparsus

Callistemon lanceolatus var. sparsus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and bright red, bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

It is also valued for its ability to attract native birds and pollinators.
