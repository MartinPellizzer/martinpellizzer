Croton smithianus

Croton smithianus, commonly known as the red croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from green to shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens where it can add vibrant visual interest.
