Crassula subulata var. subulata

Crassula subulata var. subulata, commonly known as the Blue Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with dense clusters of narrow, blue-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
