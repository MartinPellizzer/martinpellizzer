Colchicum vanjaarsveldii

Colchicum vanjaarsveldii, commonly known as the Van Jaarsveld colchicum, is a striking bulbous perennial plant native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It features showy, nodding flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces a single, tall stem that bears several flowers, which bloom in late winter or early spring. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are typically evergreen, providing a lush base for the floral display.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and elegant appearance.
