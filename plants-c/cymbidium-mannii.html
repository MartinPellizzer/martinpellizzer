Cymbidium mannii

Cymbidium mannii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that blooms during the summer months.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
