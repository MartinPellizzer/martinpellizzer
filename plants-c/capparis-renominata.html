Capparis renominata

Capparis renominata, commonly known as the desert fig or mountain fig, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellowish flowers, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a red or orange color.

Capparis renominata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
