Cattleya dowiana f. carmoniana

Cattleya dowiana f. carmoniana, commonly known as the Carmona Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, fragrant blooms in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. It has thick, fleshy roots that help it store water, adapting to its natural habitat.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
