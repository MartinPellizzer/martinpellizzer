Capparis nummularia var. minor

Capparis nummularia var. minor, commonly known as the lesser caper, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, glossy, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by small, round, edible fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
