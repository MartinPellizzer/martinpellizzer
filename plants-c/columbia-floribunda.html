Columbia floribunda

Columbia floribunda, commonly known as the everblooming rose, is a hybrid rose species native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to produce abundant flowers throughout the growing season. The plant typically features dark green, glossy leaves and sturdy, thornless stems. Its flowers are usually a deep red or pink color, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Columbia floribunda is well-suited for landscaping and garden use due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
