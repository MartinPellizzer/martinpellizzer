Catevala Glauca

Catevala glauca is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its striking blue-violet petals and unique leaf structure, it has captivated botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now classified as endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical gem for future generations.
