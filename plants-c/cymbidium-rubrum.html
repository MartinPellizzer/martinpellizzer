Cymbidium rubrum

Cymbidium rubrum, commonly known as the red orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of showy, red and orange flowers. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, adding vibrant color to gardens during the colder months. Its leaves are long, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture.

Cymbidium rubrum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in both indoor and outdoor settings.
