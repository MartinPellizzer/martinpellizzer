Coccocypselum canescens

Coccocypselum canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a daisy-like cluster, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
