Calceolaria andina var. verbascifolia

Calceolaria andina var. verbascifolia, commonly known as the Andean slipper orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to the Andes Mountains.

It features striking, bilaterally symmetrical flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped lower lip. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapting to its mountainous environment.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
