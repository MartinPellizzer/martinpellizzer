Cyclamen × atkinsii

Cyclamen × atkinsii, commonly known as the hybrid cyclamen, is a popular ornamental plant prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cyclamen persicum and Cyclamen coum, combining traits from both parent species. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.

Cyclamen × atkinsii requires well-draining soil and indirect light to flourish.
