Cleome brachycarpa var. longipetiolata

Cleome brachycarpa var. longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellow, daisy-like flowers. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a hairy texture. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive blooms and easy care requirements.
