Clematis denticulata var. ulbrichiana

Clematis denticulata var. ulbrichiana, commonly known as the Ulbrichiana Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound, pinnate leaves with finely toothed margins, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, appearing in early summer. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds with a tuft of silky hairs.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
