Callipteris permirabilis

Callipteris permirabilis, commonly known as the silky sand pine, is a member of the Arecaceae family, native to the coastal regions of southern Florida and the Bahamas.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing dune systems and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
