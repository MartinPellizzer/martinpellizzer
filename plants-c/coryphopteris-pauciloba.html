Coryphopteris pauciloba

Coryphopteris pauciloba, commonly known as the few-leaved hederaceae, is a fern native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

Coryphopteris pauciloba plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various microorganisms and small invertebrates.
