Clidemia astrotricha

Clidemia astrotricha, commonly known as the starry mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered white to pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins and a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas and forest understories.

Clidemia astrotricha is also known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative propagation.
