Cryptandra pogonoloba

Cryptandra pogonoloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cryptandra, is a small shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalypts and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, pinkish flowers that appear in clusters. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats.

Cryptandra pogonoloba is often found in heathlands and is adapted to the dry, Mediterranean climate of its native region.
