Crocodilodes cruciata

Crocodilodes cruciata, commonly known as the crossleaf crocodile plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem with small, lance-shaped leaves arranged in a cross-like pattern. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its distinctive appearance and ability to store water make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
