Cyperus Glandulosus

Cyperus glandulosus is a unique plant known for its distinctive appearance and adaptability. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along water bodies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like flowers. Its name refers to the glandular structures on its stems and leaves. This species is both ecologically important and aesthetically appealing in natural landscapes.
