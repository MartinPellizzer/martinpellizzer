Chusquea tovarii

Chusquea tovarii, commonly known as the Tovar's bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Bolivia and Peru. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 6 meters. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a dense, clumping growth habit.

It is often found in humid, temperate forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential use in traditional crafts.
