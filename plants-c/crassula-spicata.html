Crassula spicata

Crassula spicata, commonly known as the spike crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula spicata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
