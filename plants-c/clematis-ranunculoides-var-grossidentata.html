Clematis ranunculoides var. grossidentata

Clematis ranunculoides var. grossidentata, commonly known as the large-flowered clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow or green throat. The plant has compound, trifoliate leaves that are typically lobed and hairy. It thrives in partial to full sun and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Clematis ranunculoides var. grossidentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant spring blooms.
