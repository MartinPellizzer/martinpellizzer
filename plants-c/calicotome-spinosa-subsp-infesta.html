Calicotome spinosa subsp. infesta

Calicotome spinosa subsp.

infesta, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh climates and it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
