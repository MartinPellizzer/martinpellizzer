Cryptogramma brunoniana var. sinensis

Cryptogramma brunoniana var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese cryptogramma, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and dark green, closely appressed leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually elongated and have a distinct midrib, which is a key identifying feature.

This variety is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
