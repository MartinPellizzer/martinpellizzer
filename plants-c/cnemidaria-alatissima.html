Cnemidaria alatissima

Cnemidaria alatissima, commonly known as the slender grasshopper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It produces both ray and disk florets, which are typical of the Asteraceae family.

Cnemidaria alatissima is adapted to arid conditions and has a deep root system to access water.
