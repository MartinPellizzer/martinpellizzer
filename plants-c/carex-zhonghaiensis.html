Carex zhonghaiensis

Carex zhonghaiensis, commonly known as the Zhonghai sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
