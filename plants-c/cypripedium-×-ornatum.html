Cypripedium × ornatum

Cypripedium × ornatum, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a unique adaptation for pollination. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments and has a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
