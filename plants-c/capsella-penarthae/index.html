Capsella Penarthae

Capsella penarthae is a rare and intriguing plant species discovered in the Welsh mountain region. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is known for its delicate, white flowers. This plant has captured the attention of botanists due to its unique genetic makeup and limited distribution. Its discovery has sparked interest in conservation efforts to protect its fragile habitat. Despite its small size, Capsella penarthae holds significant scientific value.
