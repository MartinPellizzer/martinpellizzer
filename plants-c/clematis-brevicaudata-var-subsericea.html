Clematis brevicaudata var. subsericea

Clematis brevicaudata var. subsericea, commonly known as the silky clematis, is a flowering vine native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, often with a distinctive violet throat. The plant has slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture. Its blooms typically appear in late spring to early summer, and it thrives in partial shade to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
