Chionolaena stolonata

Chionolaena stolonata, commonly known as silverleaf groundcherry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Americas.

It features prostrate stolons that root at the nodes, forming dense mats of foliage. The leaves are hairy, ovate, and often covered in a silvery bloom, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.
