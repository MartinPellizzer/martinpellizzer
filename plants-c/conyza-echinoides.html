Conyza echinoides

Conyza echinoides, commonly known as the roughleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It has a taproot system and is often found in temperate regions.

Conyza echinoides is known for its ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.
