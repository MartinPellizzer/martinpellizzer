Cynorkis gymnadenoides

Cynorkis gymnadenoides, commonly known as the African orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant produces attractive, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive hooded lip. It is characterized by its upright, grass-like leaves and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
