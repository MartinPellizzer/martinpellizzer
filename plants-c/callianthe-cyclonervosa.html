Callianthe cyclonervosa

Callianthe cyclonervosa, commonly known as the spiral-veined fern, is a unique species of fern characterized by its intricate and spiraled venation pattern.

Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its fronds are typically lance-shaped and exhibit a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant's vascular system is highly specialized, allowing for efficient nutrient transport despite its complex leaf structure.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Callianthe cyclonervosa is considered a valuable subject for botanical study.
