Corryocactus otuyensis

Corryocactus otuyensis, commonly known as the Otuyense Coryocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in a layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized plant in its native habitat.
