Columnea verecunda

Columnea verecunda, commonly known as the red-flowered shrub, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy. Columnea verecunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
