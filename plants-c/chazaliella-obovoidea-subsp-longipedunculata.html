Chazaliella obovoidea subsp. longipedunculata

Chazaliella obovoidea subsp.

longipedunculata, commonly known as the long pedunculate chazaliella, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. It is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
