Croton aridicola

Croton aridicola, commonly known as the desert croton, is a succulent shrub native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and distinctive, often colorful, bracts that surround its small flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in dry environments, storing water in its stems and leaves. It produces yellow or orange flowers, which are typically followed by small, fleshy fruits.

Croton aridicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
