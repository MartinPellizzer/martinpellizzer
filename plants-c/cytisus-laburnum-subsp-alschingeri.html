Cytisus laburnum subsp. alschingeri

Cytisus laburnum subsp.

alschingeri, commonly known as the Alsinger's laburnum, is a shrub native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that appear in long, drooping racemes.

The plant has compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
