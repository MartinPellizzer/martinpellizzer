Crotalaria lebrunii

Crotalaria lebrunii, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and bean-like seed pods. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Its seeds are adapted for dispersal by animals and wind.
