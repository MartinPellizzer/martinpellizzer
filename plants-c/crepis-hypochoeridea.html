Crepis hypochoeridea

Crepis hypochoeridea, commonly known as the slender mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, grassy habitats and is often found in meadows and along roadsides. It has hairy, linear leaves and produces small achenes as its fruit.
