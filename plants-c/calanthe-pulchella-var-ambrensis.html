Calanthe pulchella var. ambrensis

Calanthe pulchella var. ambrensis, commonly known as the Ambrensis Calanthe, is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive purple or violet lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
