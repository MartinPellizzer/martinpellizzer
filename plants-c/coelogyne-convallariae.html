Coelogyne convallariae

Coelogyne convallariae, commonly known as the red-tipped coelogyne, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, arching stems with numerous lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive red tip on the petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Coelogyne convallariae is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
