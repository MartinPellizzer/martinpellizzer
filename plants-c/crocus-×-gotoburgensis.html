Crocus × gotoburgensis

Crocus × gotoburgensis, commonly known as the Gotland crocus, is a hybrid plant native to the Baltic region.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple or white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-20 cm, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its bulbs are adapted to cool climates and well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its early blooming habit.
