Cyperus laetus var. balansae

Cyperus laetus var. balansae, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. Its slender, grass-like stems are usually erect, and it produces small, clustered flowers in spikelets.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support wetland biodiversity.
