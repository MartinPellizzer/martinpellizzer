Calceolaria arachnoidea subsp. arachnoidea

Calceolaria arachnoidea subsp.

arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America. It is characterized by its unique, intricate flower shape that resembles a spider's web, with a bilaterally symmetrical structure.

The plant produces showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow under suitable conditions.
