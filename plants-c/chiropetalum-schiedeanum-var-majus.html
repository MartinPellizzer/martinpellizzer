Chiropetalum schiedeanum var. majus

Chiropetalum schiedeanum var. majus, commonly known as the large-flowered chiropetalum, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves, which are arranged in pairs along the stems. Its flowering period occurs during the warm season, and it thrives in humid, well-drained soils.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
