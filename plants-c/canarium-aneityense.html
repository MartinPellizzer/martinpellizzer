Canarium aneityense

Canarium aneityense, commonly known as the Philippine candlenut, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its latex is used in traditional medicine and has potential applications in modern pharmacology.
