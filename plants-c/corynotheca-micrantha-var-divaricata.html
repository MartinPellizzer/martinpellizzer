Corynotheca micrantha var. divaricata

Corynotheca micrantha var. divaricata, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with fleshy, succulent stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing white or pale pink flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
