Croton damayeshu

Croton damayeshu, commonly known as the African croton, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and diverse leaf structures. The plant features alternate, often palmate or lobed leaves that vary in shape and size. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
