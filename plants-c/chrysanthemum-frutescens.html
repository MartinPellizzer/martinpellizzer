Chrysanthemum frutescens

Chrysanthemum frutescens, commonly known as the African chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a hardy shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring upright stems and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are usually dark green and slightly hairy, providing a textured appearance.

Chrysanthemum frutescens is known for its ability to thrive in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in arid regions.
