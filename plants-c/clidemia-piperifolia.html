Clidemia piperifolia

Clidemia piperifolia, commonly known as the Hawaiian mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters.
