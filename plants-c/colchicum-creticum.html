Colchicum creticum

Colchicum creticum, commonly known as Cretan colchicum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Greece.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features striking purple to violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are broad and arrow-shaped, and it produces distinctive red or orange seeds in a capsule.

Colchicum creticum is part of the Colchicaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
