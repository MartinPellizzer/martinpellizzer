Cirsium insubricum

Cirsium insubricum, commonly known as the Italian thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its hardy nature makes it a resilient species in its native habitat.
