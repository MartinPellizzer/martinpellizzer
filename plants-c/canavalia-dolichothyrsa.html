Canavalia dolichothyrsa

Canavalia dolichothyrsa, commonly known as the long-pod cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval-shaped leaflets and produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. The plant is characterized by its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Canavalia dolichothyrsa is often cultivated for its edible seeds, which are used in various cuisines.
