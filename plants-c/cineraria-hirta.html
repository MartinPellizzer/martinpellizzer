Cineraria hirta

Cineraria hirta, commonly known as the woolly cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal environments. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a woolly appearance.

Cineraria hirta produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months.
