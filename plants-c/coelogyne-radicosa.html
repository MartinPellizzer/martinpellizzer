Coelogyne radicosa

Coelogyne radicosa, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers and pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are strap-shaped and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value.

Coelogyne radicosa thrives in warm, humid climates with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.
