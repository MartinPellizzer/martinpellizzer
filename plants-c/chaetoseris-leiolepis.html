Chaetoseris leiolepis

Chaetoseris leiolepis, commonly known as the slender starry dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, with a distinct basal rosette structure.

The flowers are yellow and appear in spring, followed by the development of wind-dispersed seeds.
