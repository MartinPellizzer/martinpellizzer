Chlorophytum elatulum

Chlorophytum elatulum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a prominent white midrib. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments. It produces small, greenish-white flowers on slender stalks during the spring or summer months.

Chlorophytum elatulum is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
