Chomelia cinerea

Chomelia cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf chomelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its grayish-white, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Chomelia cinerea is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.
