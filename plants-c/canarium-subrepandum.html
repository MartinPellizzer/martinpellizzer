Canarium subrepandum

Canarium subrepandum, commonly known as the rough-skin nut or Malabar chestnut, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and simple, alternate leaves. Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
