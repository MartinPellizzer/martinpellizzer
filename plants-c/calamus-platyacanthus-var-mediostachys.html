Calamus platyacanthus var. mediostachys

Calamus platyacanthus var. mediostachys, commonly known as the narrow-stalked calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping rhizomes and narrow, elongated leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
