Clintonia parviflora

Clintonia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered Clintonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. Clintonia parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in woodland gardens.
