Catasetum laminatum

Catasetum laminatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with a unique structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often yellow or orange with a distinctive, spurred lip.

Catasetum laminatum is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
