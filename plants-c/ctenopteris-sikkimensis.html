Ctenopteris sikkimensis

Ctenopteris sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim cliff fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are typically bright green and have a distinctive, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocky slopes or in dense forests.

This plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to biodiversity and soil stability.
