Caspareopsis

Caspareopsis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with prominent stamens that attract pollinators. Caspareopsis is often found in wetlands and disturbed soils, thriving in moist environments.
