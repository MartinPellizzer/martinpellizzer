Citharexylum dentatum var. dentatum

Citharexylum dentatum var. dentatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near streams.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
