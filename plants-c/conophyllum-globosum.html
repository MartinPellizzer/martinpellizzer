Conophyllum globosum

Conophyllum globosum, commonly known as the globular conophyllum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white, spherical flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Conophyllum globosum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
