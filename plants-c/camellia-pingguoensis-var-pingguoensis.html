Camellia pingguoensis var. pingguoensis

Camellia pingguoensis var. pingguoensis, commonly known as the Guangxi camellia, is a species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and large, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a distinctive double petal structure.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
