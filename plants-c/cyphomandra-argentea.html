Cyphomandra argentea

Cyphomandra argentea, commonly known as silver potato or silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes, potatoes, and peppers. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has hairy, silver-green leaves that give it its common name. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.
