Conophytum viridicatum

Conophytum viridicatum, commonly known as the green-flowered conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, barrel-shaped leaves that store water. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
