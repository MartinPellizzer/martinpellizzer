Calantica olivacea

Calantica olivacea, commonly known as the oliveleaf calantica, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly aromatic scent when crushed.

Calantica olivacea is often found in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
