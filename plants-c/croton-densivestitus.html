Croton densivestitus

Croton densivestitus, commonly known as the dense-leaved croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton densivestitus thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
