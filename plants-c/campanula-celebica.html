Campanula celebica

Campanula celebica, commonly known as the Celebes bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Campanula celebica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
