Catasetum stevensonii

Catasetum stevensonii, commonly known as Stevenson's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate and often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are notable for their unique structure, with specialized pollinia that are adapted for specific pollinators.

Catasetum stevensonii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
