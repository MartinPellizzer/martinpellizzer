Centropogon gesneriformis

Centropogon gesneriformis, commonly known as the Madagascar lily, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a waxy texture and a smooth margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Centropogon gesneriformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
