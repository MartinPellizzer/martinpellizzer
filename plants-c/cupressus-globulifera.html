Cupressus globulifera

Cupressus globulifera, commonly known as the globular cypress, is a species of conifer native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, globular shape and dark green foliage. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, typical of the Cupressaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact form and evergreen nature make it a valuable addition to gardens and parks.
