Ciminalis × digenea

Ciminalis × digenea, commonly known as the hybrid ciminalis, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between two distinct species.

It typically exhibits characteristics of both parent plants, often displaying a mix of foliage and flowering traits. The plant is known for its robust growth and adaptability to various soil conditions. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Its flowers are usually small and clustered, adding ornamental value to gardens.
