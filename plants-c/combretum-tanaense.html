Combretum tanaense

Combretum tanaense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often pubescent, and its bark is smooth and reddish in color. It is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
