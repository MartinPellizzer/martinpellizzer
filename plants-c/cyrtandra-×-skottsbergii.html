Cyrtandra × skottsbergii

Cyrtandra × skottsbergii, commonly known as the hybrid Cyrtandra, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Cyrtandra corymbosa and Cyrtandra pedata, characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range from pink to red. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, fleshy fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists worldwide.
