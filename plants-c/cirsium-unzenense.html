Cirsium unzenense

Cirsium unzenense, commonly known as the Unzen thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are covered in stiff, bristly hairs, and its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Cirsium unzenense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
