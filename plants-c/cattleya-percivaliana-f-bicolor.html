Cattleya percivaliana f. bicolor

Cattleya percivaliana f. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits a striking color contrast between its petals and sepals.

This orchid is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of white and purple hues. It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant blooms during the warmer months.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique coloration.
