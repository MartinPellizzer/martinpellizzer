Ctenitis hirtula

Ctenitis hirtula, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The fronds are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving the plant its common name. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas.
