Clarkia tenella subsp. tenuifolia

Clarkia tenella subsp.

tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf clarkia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, showy flowers.
