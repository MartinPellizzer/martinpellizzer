Chamaecrista nomame

Chamaecrista nomame, commonly known as yellow top, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Chamaecrista nomame is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
