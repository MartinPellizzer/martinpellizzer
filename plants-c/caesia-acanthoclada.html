Caesia acanthoclada

Caesia acanthoclada, commonly known as the spiny leaf caesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its narrow, spiny leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and thrives in partial shade to full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the warmer months of the year.

Caesia acanthoclada is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique foliage and attractive blooms.
