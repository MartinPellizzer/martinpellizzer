Cheirostylis barbata

Cheirostylis barbata, commonly known as the hairy-styled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features a short, horizontal rhizome with several erect, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive hairy structure on the column.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique appearance.
