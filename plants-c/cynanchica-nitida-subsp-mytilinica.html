Cynanchica nitida subsp. mytilinica

Cynanchica nitida subsp.

mytilinica, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, trifoliate leaves.

The plant produces yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The subspecies is distinguished by its more compact growth form and smaller leaves compared to other members of the species.
