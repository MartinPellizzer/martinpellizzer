Cyrtosperma afzelii

Cyrtosperma afzelii, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-white spathes that enclose small, greenish flowers. Its rhizomes are used as a starchy root vegetable, similar to taro.
