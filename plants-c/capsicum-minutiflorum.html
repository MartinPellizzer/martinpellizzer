Capsicum minutiflorum

Capsicum minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flower pepper.

It is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae, native to Central America. The plant produces small, yellow or orange fruits that are typically harvested when they are still immature. Its flowers are also small and yellow, contributing to its ornamental value.

It is often grown as a decorative plant due to its compact size and vibrant floral display.
