Coreopsis heterocarpa

Coreopsis heterocarpa, commonly known as the narrowleaf coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the eastern and central United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Coreopsis heterocarpa is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value.
