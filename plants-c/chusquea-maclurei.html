Chusquea maclurei

Chusquea maclurei, commonly known as the Maclure's bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Chusquea genus in the Poaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. This plant is characterized by its clump-forming growth habit and tall, slender culms that can reach several meters in height. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests and mountainous regions.
