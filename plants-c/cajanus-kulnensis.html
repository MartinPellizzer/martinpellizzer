Cajanus kulnensis

Cajanus kulnensis, commonly known as the kulnensis pigeon pea, is a leguminous plant native to India.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and erect growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pod fruits containing several seeds. It is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and is often grown for its nutritional value.

Cajanus kulnensis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it an important crop in sustainable agriculture.
