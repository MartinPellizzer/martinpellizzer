Campylocentrum itatiaiae

Campylocentrum itatiaiae, commonly known as the Itatiaia orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its flowers are small but striking, often featuring intricate patterns and a strong fragrance.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened and is of significant interest for botanical research and conservation efforts.
