Cyperus Alternifolius Var. Petersianus

Cyperus alternifolius var. petersianus is a striking aquatic plant known for its unique, grass-like foliage. It thrives in shallow water and is often found in wetlands and ponds around the world. This variant stands out with its narrow, dark green leaves that grow in an alternating pattern. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for ornamental water gardens. Despite its beauty, it requires careful maintenance to prevent it from becoming invasive.
