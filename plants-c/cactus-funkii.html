Cactus funkii

Cactus funkii, commonly known as the "Funkii cactus," is a unique species native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It features a compact, globular shape with thick, ribbed stems that store water efficiently. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual element to its desert habitat. Its spines are short and often covered in a fine, white coating that helps reduce water loss.

Cactus funkii is well adapted to harsh environments, making it a resilient and interesting subject for botanical study.
