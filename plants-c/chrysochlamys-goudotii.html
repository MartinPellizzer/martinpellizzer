Chrysochlamys goudotii

Chrysochlamys goudotii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the warmer months. The plant has a slender, branching stem with glossy, dark green leaves that are elongated and pointed. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces.
