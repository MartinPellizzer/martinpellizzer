Ceropegia maughanii

Ceropegia maughanii, commonly known as the Maughan's African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots and fleshy, succulent stems. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
