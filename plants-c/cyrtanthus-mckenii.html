Cyrtanthus mckenii

Cyrtanthus mckenii, commonly known as the McKenzi's bell-shaped iris, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive greenish tinge. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and is typically found in rocky or sandy soils. It blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a valuable addition to gardens with cool climates.

Cyrtanthus mckenii is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
