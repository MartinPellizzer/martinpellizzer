Calamagrostis speciosa

Calamagrostis speciosa, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a tall grass native to North America.

It features striking silver-gray foliage that contrasts beautifully with its deep green blades. The plant produces feathery plumes in late summer, adding ornamental value to gardens and natural landscapes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for moist to dry conditions.

Its resilience and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for native plant gardens and erosion control.
