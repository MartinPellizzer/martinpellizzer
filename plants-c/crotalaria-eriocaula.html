Crotalaria eriocaula

Crotalaria eriocaula, commonly known as the velvetleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred structure. The plant produces small, round pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can become a weed in agricultural fields.

Botanically, it belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
