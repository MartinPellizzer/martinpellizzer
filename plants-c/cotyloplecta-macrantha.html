Cotyloplecta macrantha

Cotyloplecta macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

The flowers, which can be pink or white, bloom in clusters and are pollinated by various insect species.
