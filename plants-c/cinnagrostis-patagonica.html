Cinnagrostis patagonica

Cinnagrostis patagonica, commonly known as Patagonian feathergrass, is a flowering plant native to southern South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. Cinnagrostis patagonica is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
