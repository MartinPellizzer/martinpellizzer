Caprifolium perfoliatum var. pallidum

Caprifolium perfoliatum var. pallidum, commonly known as the pale deadnettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

This variant is distinguished by its pale coloration compared to other varieties of deadnettle.
