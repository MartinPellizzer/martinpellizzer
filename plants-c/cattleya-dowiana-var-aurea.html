Cattleya dowiana var. aurea

Cattleya dowiana var. aurea, commonly known as the golden Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, golden-yellow flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its blooms are typically large, with a complex structure that includes a showy lip and a fragrant scent.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
