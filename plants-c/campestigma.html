Campestigma

Campestigma, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a basal rosette.

Campestigma is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
