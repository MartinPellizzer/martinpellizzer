Ceropegia picta

Ceropegia picta, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or pink markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, attracting specific pollinators like flies. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
