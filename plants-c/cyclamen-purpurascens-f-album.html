Cyclamen purpurascens f. album

Cyclamen purpurascens f. album, commonly known as the white cyclamen, is a hybrid form of the species Cyclamen purpurascens.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast with its deep green foliage. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and as a potted plant. It prefers cool, humid conditions and well-drained soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique coloration and delicate appearance make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
