Chamaecrista falcinella var. intermedia

Chamaecrista falcinella var. intermedia, commonly known as the coastal senna, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with small, opposite leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is adapted to saline and well-drained environments, making it a hardy plant in coastal ecosystems.
