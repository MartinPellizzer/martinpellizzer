Cyperus caesius

Cyperus caesius, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to various regions in Africa, Asia, and Australia, often found in wetland or marshy environments. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly in suitable habitats.

Cyperus caesius is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
