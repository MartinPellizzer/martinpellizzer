Cymbidium shidianense

Cymbidium shidianense, commonly known as the Shidian orchid, is a species native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Cymbidium shidianense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
