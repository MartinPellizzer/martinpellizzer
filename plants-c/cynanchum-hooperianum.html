Cynanchum hooperianum

Cynanchum hooperianum, commonly known as Hooper's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the United States, particularly found in the central and eastern regions.

It is a member of the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweed species known for their milk-like sap. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds with silky hairs.

Cynanchum hooperianum is also noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
