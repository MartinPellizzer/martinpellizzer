Cyathea klossii

Cyathea klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The plant has large, compound fronds that can grow up to several meters in length, with a distinctive feather-like appearance. Cyathea klossii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its slow growth rate and unique morphology make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
