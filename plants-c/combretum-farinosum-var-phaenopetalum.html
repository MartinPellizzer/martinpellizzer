Combretum farinosum var. phaenopetalum

Combretum farinosum var. phaenopetalum, commonly known as the silver flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from pink to red, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
