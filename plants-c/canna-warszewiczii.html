Canna warszewiczii

Canna warszewiczii, commonly known as the Warszewicz canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, tropical-looking leaves and showy, spadix-shaped inflorescences that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and dramatic floral display.

Canna warszewiczii is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both garden beds and container planting.
