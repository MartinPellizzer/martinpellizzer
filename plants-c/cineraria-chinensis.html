Cineraria chinensis

Cineraria chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese cineraria, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Cineraria chinensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
