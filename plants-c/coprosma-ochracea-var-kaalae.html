Coprosma ochracea var. kaalae

Coprosma ochracea var. kaalae, commonly known as the Kaalae coprosma, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its fruit is a bright red drupe that attracts native birds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

This species plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
