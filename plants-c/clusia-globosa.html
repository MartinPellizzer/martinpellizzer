Clusia globosa

Clusia globosa, commonly known as the globe clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. Clusia globosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

It is also valued for its hardiness and tolerance to various soil conditions.
