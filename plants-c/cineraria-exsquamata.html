Cineraria exsquamata

Cineraria exsquamata, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy blue or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Cineraria exsquamata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
