Cicer ervoides

Cicer ervoides, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a leguminous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and trifoliate leaves with small, hairy stipules. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, bean-like pods containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.

Cicer ervoides is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
