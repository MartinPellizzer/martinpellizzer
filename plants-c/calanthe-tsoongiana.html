Calanthe tsoongiana

Calanthe tsoongiana, commonly known as the Tsoong's calanthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces striking, lance-shaped leaves and bears fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. Calanthe tsoongiana thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
