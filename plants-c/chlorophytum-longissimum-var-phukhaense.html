Chlorophytum longissimum var. phukhaense

Chlorophytum longissimum var. phukhaense, commonly known as the Phuket spider plant, is a variant of the spider plant characterized by its long, arching leaves.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand, and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that prefers well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
