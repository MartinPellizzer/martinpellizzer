Caragana forrestii

Caragana forrestii, commonly known as Chinese peashrub, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable for soil enrichment. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Caragana forrestii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in landscaping and erosion control.
