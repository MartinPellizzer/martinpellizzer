Camellia xichangensis

Camellia xichangensis, commonly known as the Xichang camellia, is a rare and ornamental evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Camellia xichangensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
