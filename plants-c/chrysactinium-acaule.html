Chrysactinium acaule

Chrysactinium acaule, commonly known as the slender flame flower, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking, bright red flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. These flowers are often described as resembling small flames, giving the plant its common name.

Chrysactinium acaule thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially sunny habitats.
