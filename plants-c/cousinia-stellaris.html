Cousinia stellaris

Cousinia stellaris, commonly known as star daisy, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are typically linear or lance-shaped, and it often forms low, compact clumps. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Cousinia stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens.
