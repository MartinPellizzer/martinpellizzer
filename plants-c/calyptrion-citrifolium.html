Calyptrion citrifolium

Calyptrion citrifolium, commonly known as the lemonleaf plant, is a flowering shrub native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often bright green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
