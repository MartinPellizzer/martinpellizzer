Campanula × smithii

Campanula × smithii, commonly known as the hybrid bellflower, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula × smithii is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
