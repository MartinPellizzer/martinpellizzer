Correa Alba Var. Pannosa

Correa alba var. pannosa is a unique and captivating plant native to southern Australia. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, known for its fragrant and often ornamental species. This variety is distinguished by its soft, silvery foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires ample sunlight to flourish. Its delicate flowers add beauty to gardens and are a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts.
