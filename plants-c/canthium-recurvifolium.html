Canthium recurvifolium

Canthium recurvifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spurge.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, spiny capsules.

The species is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to grow in full sun.
