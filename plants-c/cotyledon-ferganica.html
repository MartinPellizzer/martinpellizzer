Cotyledon ferganica

Cotyledon ferganica, commonly known as the Fergana cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to direct sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
