Chlorocyperus glomeratus f. stenostachys

Chlorocyperus glomeratus f. stenostachys, commonly known as the narrow-stalked flatsedge, is a grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescences. It typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
