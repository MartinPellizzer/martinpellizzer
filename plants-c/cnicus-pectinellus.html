Cnicus pectinellus

Cnicus pectinellus, commonly known as the yellow cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are palmately divided, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
