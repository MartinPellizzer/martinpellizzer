Coelogyne tenuiflora

Coelogyne tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne tenuiflora is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
