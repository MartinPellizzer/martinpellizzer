Coleocephalocereus minensis

Coleocephalocereus minensis, commonly known as the Minas night-blooming cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its spiny stems and nocturnal blooming habit. The plant produces large, fragrant white flowers that open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its unique blooming pattern makes it a popular choice for desert gardens and botanical collections.
