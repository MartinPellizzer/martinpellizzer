Calceolaria × standishii

Calceolaria × standishii, commonly known as the hybrid slipper orchid, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Calceolaria cuneata and Calceolaria tenuis.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant is native to the Andes Mountains in South America and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Calceolaria × standishii is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
