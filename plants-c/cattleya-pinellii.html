Cattleya pinellii

Cattleya pinellii, commonly known as the Pinell's cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad, elongated, and often covered in a waxy coating.

Cattleya pinellii is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
