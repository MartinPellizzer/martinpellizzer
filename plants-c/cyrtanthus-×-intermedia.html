Cyrtanthus × intermedia

Cyrtanthus × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid cyclamen, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive red or orange stripe. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen and persist through the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers cool, shady conditions, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Cyrtanthus × intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
