Cassia fruticosa var. gatunensis

Cassia fruticosa var. gatunensis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and clusters of bright yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces long, slender pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden use.
