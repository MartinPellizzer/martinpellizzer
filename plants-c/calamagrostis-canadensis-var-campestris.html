Calamagrostis canadensis var. campestris

Calamagrostis canadensis var. campestris, commonly known as Canada bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, bluish-green foliage. The plant produces tall, cylindrical inflorescences that bloom in late summer, adding ornamental value to landscapes. It thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, ditches, and along riverbanks.

This variety is valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
