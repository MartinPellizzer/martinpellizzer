Cypripedium × minerva

Cypripedium × minerva, commonly known as the Minerva lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, large, pouch-like flower that is typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces a single, showy bloom with intricate patterns on its petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained, acidic soils.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
