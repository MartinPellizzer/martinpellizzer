Cleisostoma rostratum

Cleisostoma rostratum, commonly known as the rostrate hooded orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, wiry stem with several fleshy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose potting media.

Cleisostoma rostratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
