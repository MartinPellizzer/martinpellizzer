Cleistanthus milleri

Cleistanthus milleri, commonly known as the red-flowered cleistanthus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Cleistanthus milleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
