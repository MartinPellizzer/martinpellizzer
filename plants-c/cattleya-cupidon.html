Cattleya cupidon

Cattleya cupidon, commonly known as the Cupid's Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display vibrant shades of pink, red, and white. The plant produces pendulous blooms with a distinctive lip that is often marked with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.

Cattleya cupidon is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and fragrance.
