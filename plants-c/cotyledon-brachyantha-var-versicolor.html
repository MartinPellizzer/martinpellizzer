Cotyledon brachyantha var. versicolor

Cotyledon brachyantha var. versicolor, commonly known as the rainbow cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often colored in shades of green, red, and purple, with a striking variegation pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
