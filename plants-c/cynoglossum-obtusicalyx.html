Cynoglossum obtusicalyx

Cynoglossum obtusicalyx, commonly known as the broad-leaved bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, broad leaves that are often covered in a fine downy texture. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Cynoglossum obtusicalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural landscapes.
