Chilita saffordii

Chilita saffordii, commonly known as the Safford flax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in desert or semi-arid environments.
