Chenopodium × fallax f. procerum

Chenopodium × fallax f. procerum, commonly known as the tall lambsquarters, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and is often found in disturbed soils such as fields and roadsides.

This species is known for its rapid growth and is considered a common weed in agricultural settings.
