Cynanchum guianense

Cynanchum guianense, commonly known as Guianese dogbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which is characterized by its milky sap and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and wetlands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
