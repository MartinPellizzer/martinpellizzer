Centaurea nigrescens var. nana

Centaurea nigrescens var. nana, commonly known as dwarf night-centaury, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features dark purple to black flowers that bloom in summer, often surrounded by shorter, blue to purple bracts. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
