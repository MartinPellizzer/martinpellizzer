Cousinia urumiensis

Cousinia urumiensis, commonly known as the Urumie Desert cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family.
