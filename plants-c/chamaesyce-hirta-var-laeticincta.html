Chamaesyce hirta var. laeticincta

Chamaesyce hirta var. laeticincta, commonly known as the hairy false nettle, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces tiny greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
