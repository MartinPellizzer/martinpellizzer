Cleome polytricha

Cleome polytricha, commonly known as the "spider flower," is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features branching stems covered in fine, hairy structures, and its flowers are typically yellow or white, with five petals and numerous stamens. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Cleome polytricha thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed habitats.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ease of cultivation.
