Calathea squarrosa

Calathea squarrosa, commonly known as the pink flamingo plant, is a tropical foliage plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that often display intricate patterns in shades of pink, white, and green. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance. Its leaves are velvety to the touch, and the plant is known for its ability to produce small, inconspicuous white flowers under optimal conditions.

Calathea squarrosa is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
