Ceropegia sprengeri

Ceropegia sprengeri, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a trailing or climbing succulent with fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant.
