Crassula atropurpurea var. watermeyeri

Crassula atropurpurea var. watermeyeri, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Christmas Jade, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tint, especially in cooler conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the warmer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for indoor gardening and decorative displays.
