Croton dichrous

Croton dichrous, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
