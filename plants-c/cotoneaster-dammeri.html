Cotoneaster dammeri

Cotoneaster dammeri, commonly known as the dwarf cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a small, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels, followed by bright red berries that are ornamental and attract birds.

Cotoneaster dammeri is often cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal interest.
