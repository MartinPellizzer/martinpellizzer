Chusquea ciliatifolia

Chusquea ciliatifolia, commonly known as the silky bamboo or silky chusquea, is a species of grass belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Bolivia, Ecuador, and Peru. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a decorative plant in botanical gardens and private collections.
