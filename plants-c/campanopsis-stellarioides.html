Campanopsis stellarioides

Campanopsis stellarioides, commonly known as the star-like bellflower, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Campanopsis stellarioides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ability to attract pollinators.
