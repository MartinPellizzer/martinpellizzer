Chlorophytum breviflorum

Chlorophytum breviflorum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its air-purifying properties also contribute to its widespread cultivation and appreciation.
