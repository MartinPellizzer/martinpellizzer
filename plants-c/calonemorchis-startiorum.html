Calonemorchis startiorum

Calonemorchis startiorum, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers during the wet season.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
