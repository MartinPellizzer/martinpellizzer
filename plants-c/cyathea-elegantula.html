Cyathea elegantula

Cyathea elegantula, commonly known as the elegant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant produces spores on the undersides of its fronds, which are released into the air for reproduction. Cyathea elegantula thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
