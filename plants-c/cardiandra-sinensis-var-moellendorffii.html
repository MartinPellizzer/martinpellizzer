Cardiandra sinensis var. moellendorffii

Cardiandra sinensis var. moellendorffii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are not particularly showy but are important for its reproductive cycle. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and air-purifying properties.
