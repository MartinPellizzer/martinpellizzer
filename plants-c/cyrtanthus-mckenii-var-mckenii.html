Cyrtanthus mckenii var. mckenii

Cyrtanthus mckenii var. mckenii, commonly known as the McKenii rain lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is known for its ability to bloom reliably in cooler climates, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early spring flowering and compact growth habit.
