Cytisus fontanesii subsp. fontanesii

Cytisus fontanesii subsp.

fontanesii, commonly known as the Alpine broom, is a shrub native to the alpine regions of Europe. It features slender, wiry stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

This subspecies is notable for its hardiness and is often found in mountainous areas across the Alps.
