Chondrilla graminea

Chondrilla graminea, commonly known as slender goat's beard, is a perennial flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, also known as the mustard family, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers. Chondrilla graminea is also used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
