Cyrtorchis ringens

Cyrtorchis ringens, commonly known as the slender flame orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant has a unique root system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are often arranged in loose clusters and are pollinated by specific insects.

Cyrtorchis ringens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a subject of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological significance.
