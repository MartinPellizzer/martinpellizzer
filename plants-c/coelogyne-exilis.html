Coelogyne exilis

Coelogyne exilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a subtle pink or red hue. Its rhizome is fibrous and can form dense clumps over time.

Coelogyne exilis is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
