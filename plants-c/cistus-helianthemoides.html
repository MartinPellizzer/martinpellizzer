Cistus helianthemoides

Cistus helianthemoides, commonly known as the yellow rockrose, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand dry, rocky conditions. It is often found in coastal areas and is well-suited to poor, sandy soils.

Cistus helianthemoides is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
