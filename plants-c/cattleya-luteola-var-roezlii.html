Cattleya luteola var. roezlii

Cattleya luteola var. roezlii, commonly known as the Yellow Cattleya, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features vibrant yellow petals and a contrasting deep orange lip, making it highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in late summer, adding a splash of color to the dense tropical environment.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
