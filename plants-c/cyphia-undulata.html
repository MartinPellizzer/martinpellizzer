Cyphia undulata

Cyphia undulata, commonly known as the wavy cyphia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, wavy-edged leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cyphia undulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
