Cyathea marattioides

Cyathea marattioides, commonly known as the Marattioid fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in ideal conditions. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and storing nutrients.

Cyathea marattioides is often found in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
