Croton mansfeldii

Croton mansfeldii, commonly known as the Mansfeld's croton, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are often deeply lobed and have a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Croton mansfeldii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in warm climates.
