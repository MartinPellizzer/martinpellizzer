Connarus ruber var. acutissimus

Connarus ruber var. acutissimus, commonly known as the sharp-leaved red connarus, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae, which includes coffee and gardenias. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are sharply pointed, giving it its common name. Its small, white flowers are clustered in umbels and are followed by red, fleshy fruits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens for its vibrant foliage.
