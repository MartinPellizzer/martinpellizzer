Caladium splendidum

Caladium splendidum, commonly known as the flameleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it popular in gardens and as a houseplant.

Caladium splendidum is typically grown as an annual in temperate climates but can be perennial in tropical regions.
