Corvisartia

Corvisartia, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plants typically have slender stems and are adapted to grow in rocky or crevice habitats. They are native to regions in the Mediterranean and parts of Asia.

Corvisartia species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
