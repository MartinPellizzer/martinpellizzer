Cirsium nikkoense

Cirsium nikkoense, commonly known as the Nikko thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces purple to pinkish-purple flower heads that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Cirsium nikkoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
