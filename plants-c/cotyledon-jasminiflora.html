Cotyledon jasminiflora

Cotyledon jasminiflora, commonly known as the jasmine cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that resemble jasmine, typically in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cotyledon jasminiflora is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
