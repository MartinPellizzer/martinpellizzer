Calanthe pulchra var. formosana

Calanthe pulchra var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan orchid, is a species native to Taiwan.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically during the spring season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native habitat.
