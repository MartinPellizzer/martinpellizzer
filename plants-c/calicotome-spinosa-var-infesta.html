Calicotome spinosa var. infesta

Calicotome spinosa var. infesta, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has small, spiny leaves and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization due to its hardy nature.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in xeriscaping.
