Crassula portulacea

Crassula portulacea, commonly known as the Littleleaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Crassula portulacea is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
