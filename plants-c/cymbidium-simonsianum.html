Cymbidium simonsianum

Cymbidium simonsianum, commonly known as the Simonsian orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southern China and northern Vietnam.

It is a terrestrial orchid with erect, grass-like leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. This orchid thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
