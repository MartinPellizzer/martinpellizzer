Chlorophytum heyneanum

Chlorophytum heyneanum, commonly known as the tiger tooth plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are often marked with dark green stripes, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Chlorophytum heyneanum is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
