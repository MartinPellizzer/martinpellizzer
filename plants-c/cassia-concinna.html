Cassia concinna

Cassia concinna, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Cassia concinna is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
