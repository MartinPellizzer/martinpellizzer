Cyclopia buxifolia

Cyclopia buxifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf cyclopia, is a South African plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is native to the fynbos biome and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance.

Cyclopia buxifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
