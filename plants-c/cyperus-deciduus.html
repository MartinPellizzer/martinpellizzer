Cyperus deciduus

Cyperus deciduus, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, including marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are sword-shaped and often have a purple tinge, while its flowering heads are small and clustered.

Cyperus deciduus is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to moist conditions.
