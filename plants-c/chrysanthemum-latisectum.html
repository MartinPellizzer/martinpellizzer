Chrysanthemum latisectum

Chrysanthemum latisectum, commonly known as the Shasta daisy, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer to early fall. The plant has a bushy growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 feet. Its foliage consists of deeply lobed, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
