Cortaderia nitida

Cortaderia nitida, commonly known as silver reed, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features slender, silver-gray blades that are soft to the touch and create a dramatic visual effect in gardens. The plant produces tall, feathery plumes in late summer, adding movement and texture to the landscape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of climates.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for modern landscape designs.
