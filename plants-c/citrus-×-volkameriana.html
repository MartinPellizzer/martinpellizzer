Citrus × volkameriana

Citrus × volkameriana, commonly known as the Volkamer lemon.

It is a hybrid citrus plant originating from the cross between the lemon (Citrus × limon) and the mandarin (Citrus reticulata). The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub with dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a small, yellow citrus with a thin peel and a high concentration of essential oils.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its fragrance and hardiness.
