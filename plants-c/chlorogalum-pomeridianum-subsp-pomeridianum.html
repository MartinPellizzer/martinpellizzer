Chlorogalum pomeridianum subsp. pomeridianum

Chlorogalum pomeridianum subsp.

pomeridianum, commonly known as the evening glory, is a perennial herb native to California. It features a rosette of fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically blue-green in color.

The plant produces striking white to pink flowers that open in the late afternoon and close at night. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique flowering behavior.
