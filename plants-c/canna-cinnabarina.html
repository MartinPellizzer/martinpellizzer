Canna cinnabarina

Canna cinnabarina, commonly known as the red canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in summer, adding a bold visual impact to gardens. Canna cinnabarina thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
