Caldcluvia paniculosa

Caldcluvia paniculosa, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in large, showy panicles. The plant has pinnate leaves with multiple leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often grayish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers emit a mild fragrance, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
