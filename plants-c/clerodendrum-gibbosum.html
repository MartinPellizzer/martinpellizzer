Clerodendrum gibbosum

Clerodendrum gibbosum, commonly known as the globular clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom in summer. Its fruits are spherical, woody capsules that contain several seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
