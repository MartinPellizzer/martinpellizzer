Caladium humboldtii var. myriostigma

Caladium humboldtii var. myriostigma, commonly known as the painted leaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a wide range of hues including red, orange, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and often have intricate patterns that make them highly ornamental. This variety thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor settings due to its vibrant foliage.
