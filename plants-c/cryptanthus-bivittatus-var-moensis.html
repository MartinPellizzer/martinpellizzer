Cryptanthus bivittatus var. moensis

Cryptanthus bivittatus var. moensis, commonly known as the moensis pincushion plant, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South America.

It features dense, rosette-shaped foliage with striking, velvety leaves that range from deep red to dark purple, often with contrasting white or silver markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.

Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
