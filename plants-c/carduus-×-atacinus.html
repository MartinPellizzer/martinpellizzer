Carduus × atacinus

Carduus × atacinus, commonly known as the ornamental thistle, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from various species of thistles.

It is characterized by its spiny, purple-tinged foliage and showy, purple flower heads that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to Europe and has been cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its invasive potential in some regions, it should be grown with caution.
