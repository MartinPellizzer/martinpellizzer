Cryptomeria japonica f. compacta

Cryptomeria japonica f. compacta, commonly known as the Japanese cedar dwarf form, is a compact, slow-growing variant of the Japanese cedar.

It features a dense, rounded shape with short, stiff needles that give it a unique, ornamental appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate, making it suitable for small spaces or container gardening. It retains its foliage year-round, providing evergreen coverage in gardens.

The compact form is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in landscape design.
