Cavendishia alnifolia

Cavendishia alnifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of purple and pink, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cavendishia alnifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to indoor conditions.
