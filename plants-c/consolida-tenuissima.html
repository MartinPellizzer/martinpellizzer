Consolida tenuissima

Consolida tenuissima, commonly known as the slender rocket or narrowleaf sweet pea, is a flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, elongated pods containing several seeds, and it thrives in well-drained soil in full sun.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and is also used in some traditional herbal remedies.
