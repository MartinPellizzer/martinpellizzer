Chlorophytum aureum

Chlorophytum aureum, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail or Golden Edge Snake Plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive yellow or white margins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Chlorophytum aureum is also valued for its air-purifying qualities, as it can help remove toxins from the environment.
