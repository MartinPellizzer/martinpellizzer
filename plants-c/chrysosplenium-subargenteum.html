Chrysosplenium subargenteum

Chrysosplenium subargenteum, commonly known as silverleaf chrysosplenium, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, silver-tomentose leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in cymes and bloom in early spring.

Chrysosplenium subargenteum is often found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
