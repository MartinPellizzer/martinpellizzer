Casearia flexicaulis

Casearia flexicaulis, commonly known as the flexible casearia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Salicaceae, which includes willows and poplars, and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves. The species is noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
