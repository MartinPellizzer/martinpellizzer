Croton koehneanus

Croton koehneanus, commonly known as the Koehne's croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Tanzania and Kenya. The plant is characterized by its striking, often colorful foliage with varied leaf shapes and textures. Its leaves are typically alternate, simple, and can range from ovate to elliptic in shape.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits, which contain the seeds.
