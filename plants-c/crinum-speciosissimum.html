Crinum speciosissimum

Crinum speciosissimum, commonly known as the showy crinum, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant produces strap-shaped, grass-like leaves and has a bulbous underground stem known as a corm. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Crinum speciosissimum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
