Conyza vestita

Conyza vestita, commonly known as the paleleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as fields and disturbed soils. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance.
