Congea villosa

Congea villosa, commonly known as hairy congea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Congea villosa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
