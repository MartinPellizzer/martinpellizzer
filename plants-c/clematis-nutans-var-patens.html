Clematis nutans var. patens

Clematis nutans var. patens, commonly known as the spreading clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, appearing in clusters. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis nutans var. patens is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to cover trellises or fences.
