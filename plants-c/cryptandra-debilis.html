Cryptandra debilis

Cryptandra debilis, commonly known as the slender cryptandra, is a small shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in heathlands and coastal areas, where it plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for native wildlife.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
