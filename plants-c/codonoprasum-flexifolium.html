Codonoprasum flexifolium

Codonoprasum flexifolium, commonly known as the flexible leaf codonopsis, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive, often curling leaves.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in humid conditions. Its leaves are known for their flexibility and can be used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, producing a mild, aromatic scent that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.
