Croton eriocladoides

Croton eriocladoides, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in spikes, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
