Cattleya × imperator

Cattleya × imperator, commonly known as the Emperor Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species prized for its vibrant and showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features large, pendulous blooms with intricate patterns of purple, pink, and white. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in humid, well-drained environments.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
