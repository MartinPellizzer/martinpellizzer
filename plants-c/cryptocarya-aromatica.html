Cryptocarya aromatica

Cryptocarya aromatica, commonly known as the fragrant cryptocarya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which emit a pleasant scent when crushed. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces edible berries that are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
