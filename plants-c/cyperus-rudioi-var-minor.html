Cyperus rudioi var. minor

Cyperus rudioi var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf flatsedge, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, ditches, and the edges of ponds.

Due to its compact size and adaptability, it is often found in disturbed or aquatic habitats.
