Calathea vandenheckei

Calathea vandenheckei, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, patterned leaves that change color and orientation in response to light and temperature, a phenomenon known as nyctinasty. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with intricate veining that adds to its visual appeal.

Calathea vandenheckei thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
