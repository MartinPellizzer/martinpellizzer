Carduus nigrescens var. australis

Carduus nigrescens var. australis, commonly known as the Australian thistle, is a perennial plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its spiny, dark green leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in arid conditions.

Due to its invasive nature, it can outcompete native vegetation in certain regions.
