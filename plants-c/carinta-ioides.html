Carinta ioides

Carinta ioides, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to parts of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

The silverleaf tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
