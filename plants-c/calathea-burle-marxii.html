Calathea burle-marxii

Calathea burle-marxii, commonly known as the Black Magic plant, is a striking tropical species native to Brazil.

It features dark, velvety leaves with intricate patterns of silver and green, creating a dramatic visual effect. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its unique foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative plant arrangements.
