Convallaria majalis var. variegata

Convallaria majalis var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated lily of the valley, is a striking variant of the traditional lily of the valley.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring a mix of green and white or yellow markings. The plant produces bell-shaped, white flowers that bloom in early spring. It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded borders.
