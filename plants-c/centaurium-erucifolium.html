Centaurium erucifolium

Centaurium erucifolium, commonly known as lesser centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branching stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slight hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Centaurium erucifolium is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.
