Cleome sodiroi

Cleome sodiroi, commonly known as the desert spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and it produces capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
