Carex tsoi

Carex tsoi, commonly known as Tsoi's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Mongolia. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and wetlands.

Carex tsoi is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
