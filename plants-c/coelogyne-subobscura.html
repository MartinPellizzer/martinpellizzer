Coelogyne subobscura

Coelogyne subobscura, commonly known as the dark-veined orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, dark green leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, recurved lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne subobscura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
