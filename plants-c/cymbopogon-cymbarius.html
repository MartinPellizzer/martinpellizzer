Cymbopogon cymbarius

Cymbopogon cymbarius, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue blades and tufted inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. Its foliage is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting, elegant look.

Additionally, it has been studied for its potential aromatic properties and ecological benefits.
