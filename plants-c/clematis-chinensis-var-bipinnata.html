Clematis chinensis var. bipinnata

Clematis chinensis var. bipinnata, commonly known as the Chinese climbing vine, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with two pairs of leaflets, giving it a finely divided, feathery appearance. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
