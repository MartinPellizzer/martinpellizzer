Calamagrostis autumnalis var. microtis

Calamagrostis autumnalis var. microtis, commonly known as the silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular ornamental grass in gardens. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet and produces delicate, feathery seed heads in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture and color to landscapes throughout the growing season.
