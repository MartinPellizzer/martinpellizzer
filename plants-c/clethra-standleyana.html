Clethra standleyana

Clethra standleyana, commonly known as the Standley's sweet pepperbush, is a deciduous shrub native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the plant family Ericaceae, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves. Clethra standleyana is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for various wildlife species.
