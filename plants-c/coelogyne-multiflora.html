Coelogyne multiflora

Coelogyne multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered pseudobulbs and long, arching stems that bear numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale pink with yellow or orange markings, and they bloom in late winter to early spring. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne multiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
