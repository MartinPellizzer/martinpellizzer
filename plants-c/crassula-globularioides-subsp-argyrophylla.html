Crassula globularioides subsp. argyrophylla

Crassula globularioides subsp.

argyrophylla, commonly known as the silver leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, globular rosettes of thick, fleshy, silver-gray leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid conditions.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
