Clerodendrum buchananii var. glabrum

Clerodendrum buchananii var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth clerodendrum, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually pink or red, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
