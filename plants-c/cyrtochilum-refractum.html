Cyrtochilum refractum

Cyrtochilum refractum, commonly known as the refractum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow to white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Cyrtochilum refractum prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
