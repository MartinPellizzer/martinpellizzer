Castra

Castra, commonly known as the common tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant features yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, with finely divided, fern-like leaves. Castra is characterized by its strong, aromatic scent and has been historically used in herbal remedies.

Its botanical name, *Tanacetum vulgare*, reflects its association with the tansy plant family.
