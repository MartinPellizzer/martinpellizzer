Cyrtanthus debilis

Cyrtanthus debilis, commonly known as the slender bell-shaped flower.

It is a species of flowering plant in the genus Cyrtanthus, native to southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Cyrtanthus debilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
