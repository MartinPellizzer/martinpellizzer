Centaurea tuzgoluensis

Centaurea tuzgoluensis, commonly known as Tuzgol Mountain Centaury, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss.

Centaurea tuzgoluensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and potential medicinal properties.
