Campderia langsdorffii

Campderia langsdorffii, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Campderia langsdorffii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
