Coleus omahekensis

Coleus omahekensis, commonly known as the Omahekensis coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is known for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its foliage is often used in horticultural displays due to its striking appearance.
