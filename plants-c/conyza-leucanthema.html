Conyza leucanthema

Conyza leucanthema, commonly known as whitehead crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture, and it produces small, round seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some areas.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is known for its composite flower heads.
