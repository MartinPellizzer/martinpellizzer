Crassocephalum behmianum

Crassocephalum behmianum, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows up to 1 meter in height, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its scientific name reflects its robust growth and distinctive head-like inflorescence.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
