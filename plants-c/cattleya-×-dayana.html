Cattleya × dayana

Cattleya × dayana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cattleya labiata and Cattleya mossiae.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that support its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya × dayana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
