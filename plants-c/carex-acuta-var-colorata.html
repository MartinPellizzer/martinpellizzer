Carex acuta var. colorata

Carex acuta var. colorata, commonly known as the colorful rush, is a variant of the common rush species Carex acuta.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green, with some cultivars displaying striking variegation or coloration. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
