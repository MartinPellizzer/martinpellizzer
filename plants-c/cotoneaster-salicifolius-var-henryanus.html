Cotoneaster salicifolius var. henryanus

Cotoneaster salicifolius var. henryanus, commonly known as the Henryan's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the autumn. These berries are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
