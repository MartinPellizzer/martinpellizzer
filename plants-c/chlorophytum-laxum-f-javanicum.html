Chlorophytum laxum f. javanicum

Chlorophytum laxum f. javanicum, commonly known as the Java spider plant, is a hybrid form of the spider plant.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant features long, arching leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months.

This species is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and air-purifying properties.
