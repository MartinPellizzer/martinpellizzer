Cinnamomum lucens

Cinnamomum lucens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cinnamon or Ceylon cinnamon, is a species of tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is used to produce the spice cinnamon. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are berry-like and contain several seeds.

The botanical features of Cinnamomum lucens make it a valuable species in both traditional medicine and the spice trade.
