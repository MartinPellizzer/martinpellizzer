Clerodendrum alatum var. floribundum

Clerodendrum alatum var. floribundum, commonly known as the winged clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, wing-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
