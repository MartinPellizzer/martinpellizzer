Celsia faurei var. acanthifolia

Celsia faurei var. acanthifolia, commonly known as the spiny celsia, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. Its unique morphology makes it a striking addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is gaining popularity among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
