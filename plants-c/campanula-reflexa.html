Campanula reflexa

Campanula reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and slightly hairy, with a basal rosette formation. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, often found in rocky or woodland habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its delicate, bell-like blooms.
