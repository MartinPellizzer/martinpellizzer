Cyrtostylis oblonga

Cyrtostylis oblonga, commonly known as the oblong spider-orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, spider-like flowers with a distinctive greenish-yellow color. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as rainforests or under dense vegetation. Its flowers are usually borne on a single stem and bloom during the warmer months.

Cyrtostylis oblonga is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare and exotic plants.
