Crotalaria vogelii

Crotalaria vogelii, commonly known as the Vogel's cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant produces striking yellow or orange flowers, often with distinctive spurred petals. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Crotalaria vogelii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
