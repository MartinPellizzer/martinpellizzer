Cynodendron revolutum

Cynodendron revolutum, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are ovate with wavy margins and have a rough texture.

Cynodendron revolutum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
