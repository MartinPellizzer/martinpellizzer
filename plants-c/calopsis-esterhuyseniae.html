Calopsis esterhuyseniae

Calopsis esterhuyseniae, commonly known as the Esterhuysenia's calopsis, is a rare flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.
