Cerbera ahouai

Cerbera ahouai, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a waxy texture, while its fruits are follicles that contain toxic seeds.
