Chaeturus fasciculatus

Chaeturus fasciculatus, commonly known as the clustered cat's claw.

This plant is a climbing vine native to South America, particularly in regions of Brazil and Peru. It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries.

It is often found in humid, tropical forests and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
