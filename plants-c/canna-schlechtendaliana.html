Canna schlechtendaliana

Canna schlechtendaliana, commonly known as the purple canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Canna schlechtendaliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
