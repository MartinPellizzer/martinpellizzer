Cotoneaster franchetii var. cinerascens

Cotoneaster franchetii var. cinerascens, commonly known as the silver cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically a glossy green on top and silvery white underneath, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of pinkish-white flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens for its seasonal interest.

Cotoneaster franchetii var. cinerascens is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil.
