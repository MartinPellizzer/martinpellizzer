Crossandra diffusa

Crossandra diffusa, commonly known as the spreading crossandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Crossandra diffusa is known for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
