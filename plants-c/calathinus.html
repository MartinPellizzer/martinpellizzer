Calathinus

Calathinus, commonly known as the candleplant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its unique, candle-like inflorescences that consist of a central stem surrounded by a cluster of small, pale yellow to white flowers. These plants are often cultivated for their ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with moderate to high light exposure.
