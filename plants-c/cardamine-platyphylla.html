Cardamine platyphylla

Cardamine platyphylla, commonly known as broadleaf watercress, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often submerged or floating in aquatic environments. Its small, white flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in early spring.

Cardamine platyphylla is valued for its peppery flavor and is sometimes used in local cuisines.
