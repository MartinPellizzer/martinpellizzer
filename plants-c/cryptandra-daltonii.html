Cryptandra daltonii

Cryptandra daltonii, commonly known as Dalton's cryptandra, is a small shrub native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pinkish-white flowers in winter. It is adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils and can withstand periods of drought.

Cryptandra daltonii is often found in heathlands and is a component of the local flora in its native habitat.
