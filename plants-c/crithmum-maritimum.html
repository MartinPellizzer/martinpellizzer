Crithmum maritimum

Crithmum maritimum, commonly known as sea fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fern-like leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often growing in maritime climates. Its essential oils, derived from the leaves and seeds, are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.

Crithmum maritimum is also valued as a culinary herb, particularly in Mediterranean cuisine.
