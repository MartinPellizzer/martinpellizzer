Coelogyne reichenbachiana

Coelogyne reichenbachiana, commonly known as the "Burmese orchid," is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The blooms are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet scent. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
