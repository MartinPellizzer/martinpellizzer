Cakile edentula subvar. apetala

Cakile edentula subvar. apetala, commonly known as the barren leaf sandbur, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, yellow flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
