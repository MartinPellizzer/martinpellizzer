Cynanchum sarcostemmatoides

Cynanchum sarcostemmatoides, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed vine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and milky sap. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and often have a rough texture.

Cynanchum sarcostemmatoides is adapted to grow in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
