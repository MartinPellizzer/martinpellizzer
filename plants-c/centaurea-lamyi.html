Centaurea lamyi

Centaurea lamyi, commonly known as the French centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright, branched stems and bright blue to purple flowers. The plant produces small, spiky seed heads that are distinctive and often used in ornamental gardening. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Centaurea lamyi is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky soils.
