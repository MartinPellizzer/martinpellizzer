Cymbidium luteum

Cymbidium luteum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched inflorescence and showy, golden-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it is a perennial herbaceous plant.

Cymbidium luteum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for floral arrangements.
