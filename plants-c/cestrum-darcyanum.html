Cestrum darcyanum

Cestrum darcyanum, commonly known as the purple cestrum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

The flowers, which are usually purple or pink, bloom in clusters and are attractive to pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.
