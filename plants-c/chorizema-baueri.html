Chorizema baueri

Chorizema baueri, commonly known as the desert star, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a succulent, branching structure that allows it to store water, adapting to its harsh environment. Its leaves are typically small and fleshy, reducing water loss through transpiration.

Chorizema baueri is often found in rocky soils and is a hardy species that thrives in low-nutrient conditions.
