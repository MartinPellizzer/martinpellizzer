Cissus duparquettii

Cissus duparquettii, commonly known as the silver arrow cissus, is a climbing plant native to Madagascar.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Cissus duparquettii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
