Cyathea caudipinnula

Cyathea caudipinnula, commonly known as the long-pinnate tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and longevity. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a distinctive, elongated shape with long, narrow segments.

Cyathea caudipinnula is often found in humid, shaded environments, such as forests and along stream banks.
