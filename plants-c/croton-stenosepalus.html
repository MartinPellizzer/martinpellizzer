Croton stenosepalus

Croton stenosepalus, commonly known as the narrow-sepal croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with prominent venation. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Croton stenosepalus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaf colors.
