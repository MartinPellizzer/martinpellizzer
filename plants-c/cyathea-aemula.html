Cyathea aemula

Cyathea aemula, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of feathery fronds that can grow up to 3 meters in length. Cyathea aemula thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rhizomes produce both roots and new plantlets, aiding in its propagation and ecological role.
