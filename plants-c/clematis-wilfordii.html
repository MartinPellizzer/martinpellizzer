Clematis wilfordii

Clematis wilfordii, commonly known as the Wilford's Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five ovate leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pink. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, with its flowers appearing in clusters along the stems. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Clematis wilfordii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
