Cyperus inops

Cyperus inops, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and wetlands. The plant has slender, grass-like stems and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a bluish-green hue.

Cyperus inops plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
