Cinnamomum sleumeri

Cinnamomum sleumeri, commonly known as the Bornean cinnamon or Sleumer's cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to the island of Borneo.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval, dark blue berries. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a leathery texture and a strong, spicy aroma.

The bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their aromatic properties.
