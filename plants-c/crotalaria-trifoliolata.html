Crotalaria trifoliolata

Crotalaria trifoliolata, commonly known as the three-leaved cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant features trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are typically yellow with prominent violet or purple markings, and it produces elongated pods containing several seeds.

Crotalaria trifoliolata is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
