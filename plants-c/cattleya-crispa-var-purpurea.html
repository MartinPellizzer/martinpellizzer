Cattleya crispa var. purpurea

Cattleya crispa var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-streaked cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking floral display.

It features large, showy flowers with a velvety purple hue and intricate patterns of darker purple streaks. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native habitat of Central and South America. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it produces multiple inflorescences during the flowering season.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
