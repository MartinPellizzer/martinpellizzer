Cacalia bulbifera var. acerina

Cacalia bulbifera var. acerina, commonly known as the red-flowered cacalia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that produce clusters of small, showy pink to reddish-purple flowers. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are hairy and often tinged with red or purple hues. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and delicate appearance.
