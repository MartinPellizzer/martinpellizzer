Cyanea rivularis

Cyanea rivularis, commonly known as the stream orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, leafy stem that often grows near water sources. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from damp environments.

Cyanea rivularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded gardens.
