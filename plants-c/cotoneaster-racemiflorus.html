Cotoneaster racemiflorus

Cotoneaster racemiflorus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in racemes during the spring. The plant produces red berries that are attractive to birds and persist through the winter. Its leaves are typically oval with serrated edges, and it can grow up to 6 meters tall.

Cotoneaster racemiflorus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
