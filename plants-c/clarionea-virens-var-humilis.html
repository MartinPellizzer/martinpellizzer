Clarionea virens var. humilis

Clarionea virens var. humilis, commonly known as the low-growing green clarion, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, and its leaves are ovate with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and green, which bloom throughout the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique subject for ecological and horticultural study.
