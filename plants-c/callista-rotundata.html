Callista rotundata

Callista rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or stream banks. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and a short, upright stem.

Callista rotundata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
