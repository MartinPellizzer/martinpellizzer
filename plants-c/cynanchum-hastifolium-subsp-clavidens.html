Cynanchum hastifolium subsp. clavidens

Cynanchum hastifolium subsp.

clavidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dogbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia. It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stems.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its fruits are follicles that split open to release seeds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, known for its milky sap and milkweed-like characteristics.
