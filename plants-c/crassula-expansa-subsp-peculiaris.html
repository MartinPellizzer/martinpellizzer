Crassula expansa subsp. peculiaris

Crassula expansa subsp.

peculiaris, commonly known as the "Lemon Lime Crassula," is a compact, succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a slight bluish tint.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique leaf coloration and easy care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
