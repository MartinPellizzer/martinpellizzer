Cockburnia socotrana

Cockburnia socotrana, commonly known as Socotra cypress, is a unique evergreen conifer native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-shaped crown and scale-like leaves that are blue-green in color. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several centuries, making it a long-lived species. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky or limestone soils.

The Socotra cypress is also notable for its historical and cultural significance on the island.
