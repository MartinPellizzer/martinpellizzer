Cocculus villosus var. glabratus

Cocculus villosus var. glabratus, commonly known as the smooth cocculus, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces distinctive, warty, greenish-yellow fruits that contain a single large seed. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
