Charlwoodia rigidifolia

Charlwoodia rigidifolia, commonly known as the rigid-stemmed charlwoodia, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense, glandular leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is native to parts of Africa and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments.
