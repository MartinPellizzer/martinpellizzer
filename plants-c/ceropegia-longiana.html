Ceropegia longiana

Ceropegia longiana, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and small, waxy leaves. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are often pale green or white in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-draining soil.

Ceropegia longiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
