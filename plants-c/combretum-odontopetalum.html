Combretum odontopetalum

Combretum odontopetalum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

The bark is smooth and can become fissured with age, adding to its visual appeal.
