Cuphea ericoides var. hervita

Cuphea ericoides var. hervita, commonly known as the Hervita heather.

It is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Caribbean. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in dense clusters. It has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with narrow, evergreen leaves.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
