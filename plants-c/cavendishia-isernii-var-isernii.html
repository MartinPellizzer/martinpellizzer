Cavendishia isernii var. isernii

Cavendishia isernii var. isernii, commonly known as the Isern's Cavendishia, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that range from red to orange. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which can hold water. Its flowers are typically borne in terminal clusters and are short-lived, lasting only a few weeks.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
