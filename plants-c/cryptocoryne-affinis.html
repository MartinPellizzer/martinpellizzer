Cryptocoryne affinis

Cryptocoryne affinis, commonly known as the Java Cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a distinctive white or pale midrib. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in the understory of dense vegetation. Cryptocoryne affinis is popular in aquariums due to its hardy nature and ability to tolerate a wide range of water conditions.

Its rhizomes form dense mats, which help in stabilizing the substrate and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
