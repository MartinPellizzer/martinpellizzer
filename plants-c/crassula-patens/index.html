Crassula Patens

Crassula patens is a unique and striking succulent native to South Africa. Known for its compact, rosette-shaped growth and vibrant blue-green foliage, it stands out among other plants in the Crassula genus. This plant is particularly popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ease of care and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions. Its ability to bloom with clusters of small, star-shaped flowers adds to its ornamental appeal. Whether grown indoors or outdoors, Crassula patens is a versatile and eye-catching addition to any collection.
