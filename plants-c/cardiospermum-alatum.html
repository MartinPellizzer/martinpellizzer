Cardiospermum alatum

Cardiospermum alatum, commonly known as the Bat's Wing or Crown of Thorns, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and showy, pink or white flowers. The plant produces distinctive, heart-shaped fruits that split open to reveal seeds surrounded by a fleshy, red aril. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and striking blooms contribute to its aesthetic appeal in both garden and horticultural settings.
