Cyperus testui

Cyperus testui, commonly known as the spiny flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy areas, thriving in moist soils. Its slender, triangular stems are often covered with small, spiny bristles.

Cyperus testui produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated.
