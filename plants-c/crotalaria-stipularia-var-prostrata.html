Crotalaria stipularia var. prostrata

Crotalaria stipularia var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate stembur rattlepod, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, prostrate stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a hairy texture.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional medicinal practices.
