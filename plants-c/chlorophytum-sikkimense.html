Chlorophytum sikkimense

Chlorophytum sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim spider plant, is a perennial herb native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of sword-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
