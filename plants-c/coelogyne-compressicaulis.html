Coelogyne compressicaulis

Coelogyne compressicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often curved stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer, producing pale yellow to orange-red blooms with a distinctive, narrow shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne compressicaulis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
