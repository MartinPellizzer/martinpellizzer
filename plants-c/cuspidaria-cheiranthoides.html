Cuspidaria cheiranthoides

Cuspidaria cheiranthoides, commonly known as the slender-flowered mustard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, featuring slender stems and small, yellow flowers. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

The seeds are small and contain compounds that have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.
