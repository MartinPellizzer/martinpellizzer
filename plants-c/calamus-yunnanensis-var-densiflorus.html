Calamus yunnanensis var. densiflorus

Calamus yunnanensis var. densiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered rattan, is a species of climbing palm native to southern China.

It is characterized by its robust, woody stems and pinnately compound leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, yellowish-white flowers, which are typical of the Arecaceae family. It thrives in humid, tropical or subtropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and utility, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
