Crotalaria distans subsp. distans

Crotalaria distans subsp.

distans, commonly known as the narrowleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume morphology, including pod-bearing fruits.

The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in slender, elongated pods, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
