Chylismia Claviformis Var. Aurantiaca

Chylismia claviformis var. aurantiaca is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant orange flowers and unique leaf structure. Native to the arid regions of South Africa, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun exposure. This variant stands out for its bright coloration, which adds a bold visual element to any garden or collection. Its delicate yet resilient nature makes it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
