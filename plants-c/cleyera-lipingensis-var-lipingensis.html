Cleyera lipingensis var. lipingensis

Cleyera lipingensis var. lipingensis, commonly known as the Liping holly, is a shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by small, red berries in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping for its dense foliage and seasonal interest.
