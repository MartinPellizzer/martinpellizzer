Crotalaria leonardiana

Crotalaria leonardiana, commonly known as the Leonard's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features alternate, pinnately compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets. Its flowers are showy, with two larger petals and two smaller ones, often in shades of yellow and orange.

The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
