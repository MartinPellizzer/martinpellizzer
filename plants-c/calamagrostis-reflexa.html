Calamagrostis reflexa

Calamagrostis reflexa, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its silver-blue foliage and graceful, arching inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered with fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance.

Calamagrostis reflexa is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting wildlife and stabilizing soil.
