Cuphea kubeorum

Cuphea kubeorum, commonly known as the velvet tamarack, is a striking tropical shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its unique, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, which are highly attractive to hummingbirds. The plant features dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name, and it grows in a compact, rounded form. Cuphea kubeorum thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
