Cotyledon breviflora var. variegata

Cotyledon breviflora var. variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
