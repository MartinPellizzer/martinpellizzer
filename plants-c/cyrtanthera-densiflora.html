Cyrtanthera densiflora

Cyrtanthera densiflora, commonly known as the denseflowered sand orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit and produces numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually clustered in dense inflorescences, giving it its common name.

Cyrtanthera densiflora is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
