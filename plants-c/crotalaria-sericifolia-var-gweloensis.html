Crotalaria sericifolia var. gweloensis

Crotalaria sericifolia var. gweloensis, commonly known as the Gwelo velvetleaf, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typical of the Crotalaria genus. It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in grassland and woodland areas.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and potential use in traditional medicine.
