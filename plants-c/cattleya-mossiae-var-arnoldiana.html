Cattleya mossiae var. arnoldiana

Cattleya mossiae var. arnoldiana, commonly known as the Arnold's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It features large, showy blooms with a distinctive color pattern of deep pink and white, often with a contrasting lip. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.

Its popularity among orchid enthusiasts is due to its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
