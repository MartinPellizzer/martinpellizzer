Cryptomeria japonica var. pendula

Cryptomeria japonica var. pendula, commonly known as the weeping cedar, is a variant of the Japanese cedar characterized by its distinctive cascading foliage.

This evergreen conifer features long, pendulous branches that hang downward, creating a unique, flowing appearance. The leaves are needle-like, dark green, and arranged spirally along the branches. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

The plant is native to Japan and is valued for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and parks.
