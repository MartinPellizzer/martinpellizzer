Cyathiscus productus

Cyathiscus productus, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features small, yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and early summer. It has a low, sprawling growth habit with gray-green foliage adapted to conserve water.

Cyathiscus productus is well-suited to rocky soils and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy plant for xeriscaping.
