Ceropegia tenuifolia

Ceropegia tenuifolia, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Ceropegia tenuifolia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
