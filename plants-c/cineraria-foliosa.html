Cineraria foliosa

Cineraria foliosa, commonly known as the leafy cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy foliage and showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature contribute to its appeal in horticulture.
