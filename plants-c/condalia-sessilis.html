Condalia sessilis

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
