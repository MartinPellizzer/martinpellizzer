Cavendishia chocoensis var. chocoensis

Cavendishia chocoensis var. chocoensis, commonly known as the Chocoensis Cavendishia, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often yellow or orange, and they bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
