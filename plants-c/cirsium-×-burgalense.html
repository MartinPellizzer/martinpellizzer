Cirsium × burgalense

Cirsium × burgalense, commonly known as the Burgalense thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads that bloom in summer. Native to the Iberian Peninsula, it thrives in sunny, dry environments such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant's structure includes a robust stem with bracts that protect the flower heads.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and often invasive species in its native habitat.
