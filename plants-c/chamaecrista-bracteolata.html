Chamaecrista bracteolata

Chamaecrista bracteolata, commonly known as the bracteolate sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive, curved pods that contain several seeds. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple pairs of small, feather-like leaflets.

Chamaecrista bracteolata is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
