Cleome augustinensis var. augustinensis

Cleome augustinensis var. augustinensis, commonly known as the yellow cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and low maintenance requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation in warm climates.
