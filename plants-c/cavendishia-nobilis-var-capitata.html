Cavendishia nobilis var. capitata

Cavendishia nobilis var. capitata, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often spiny, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces bright red flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a favored plant in both gardens and indoor settings.
