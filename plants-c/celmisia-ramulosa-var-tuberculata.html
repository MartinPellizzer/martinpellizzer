Celmisia ramulosa var. tuberculata

Celmisia ramulosa var. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, hairy, tuberculate stems. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
