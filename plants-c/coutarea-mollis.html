Coutarea mollis

Coutarea mollis, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, velvety bark and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody, spiny fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
