Cleome welwitschii

Cleome welwitschii, commonly known as the African spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven leaflets, and it often grows in a bushy, shrub-like form. Cleome welwitschii is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in arid regions.
