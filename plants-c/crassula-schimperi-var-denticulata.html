Crassula schimperi var. denticulata

Crassula schimperi var. denticulata, commonly known as the Denticulate Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with fleshy, triangular leaves that have a distinctive toothed or serrated edge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
