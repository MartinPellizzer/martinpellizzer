Crepidium exilis

Crepidium exilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white with yellow markings. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
