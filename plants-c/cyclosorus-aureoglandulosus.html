Cyclosorus aureoglandulosus

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Cyclosorus aureoglandulosus is primarily distributed in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
