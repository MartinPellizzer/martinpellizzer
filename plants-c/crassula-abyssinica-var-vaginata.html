Crassula abyssinica var. vaginata

Crassula abyssinica var. vaginata, commonly known as the "Cape snowflake," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often adorned with a powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
