Crucifera laelia

Crucifera laelia, commonly known as the fairy crosswort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, cruciform (cross-shaped) flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Crucifera laelia is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
