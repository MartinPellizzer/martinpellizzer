Comparettia ignea

Comparettia ignea, commonly known as the fiery torch orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red and yellow flowers, which resemble a torch, and its ability to thrive in warm, humid environments. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically broad and elliptical, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Comparettia ignea is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
