Caladenia minor var. exigua

Caladenia minor var. exigua, commonly known as the small spider orchid, is a small terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces a single, slender stem that bears a single, delicate orchid flower with a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate markings that aid in pollination.

This variety is part of the Caladenia genus, known for its specialized relationships with specific pollinators.
