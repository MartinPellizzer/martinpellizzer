Campanula tubulosa

Campanula tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Campanula tubulosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and can be found in various regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
