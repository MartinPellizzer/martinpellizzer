Clitoria cajanifolia f. glabrior

Clitoria cajanifolia f. glabrior, commonly known as the velvet bean or butterfly pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers, which are often used in traditional medicine and as a natural dye. The plant is native to tropical regions of Asia and is cultivated for its edible seeds and ornamental value. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the flowers are borne in racemes, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.

The f. glabrior form is distinguished by its reduced pubescence, giving it a smoother appearance compared to the typical variety.
