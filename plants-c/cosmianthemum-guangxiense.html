Cosmianthemum guangxiense

Cosmianthemum guangxiense, commonly known as Guangxi cosmos, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
