Combretum taitense

Combretum taitense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that appear in clusters, giving it a vibrant and showy appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
