Citrus × bigaradia var. dulcis

Citrus × bigaradia var. dulcis, commonly known as the sweet orange.

It is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its round, thick-skinned fruit with a high sugar content. The leaves are broad and oval, with a slightly waxy texture, and the plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub. Its flowers are fragrant and white, producing a high-quality fruit prized for its juice and culinary uses.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
