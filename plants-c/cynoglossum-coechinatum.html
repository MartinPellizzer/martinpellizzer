Cynoglossum coechinatum

Cynoglossum coechinatum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that resemble bells, which bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ease of cultivation.
