Crinum tavelianum

Crinum tavelianum, commonly known as the Tavelian crinum, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow, often with a strong, spicy scent. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically broad and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Crinum tavelianum is also noted for its ability to naturalize and spread through bulb division.
