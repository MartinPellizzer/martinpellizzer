Cryptantha maritima var. vizcainensis

Cryptantha maritima var. vizcainensis, commonly known as the coastal cryptantha, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of Baja California, Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the shoreline. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.
