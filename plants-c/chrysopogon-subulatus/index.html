Chrysopogon Subulatus

Chrysopogon subulatus is a rare and delicate grass species native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas. It thrives in cold, arid environments where few plants can survive. Its slender, needle-like leaves and compact growth make it unique among grasses. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and largely unknown to the general public. This plant offers valuable insights into ecological adaptation and biodiversity in extreme climates.
