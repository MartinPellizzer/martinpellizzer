Cabralea rojasii

Cabralea rojasii, commonly known as the Chilean wild coffee.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of northern Chile. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture and a dark green color. It produces small white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, round fruits.

The species is noted for its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments.
