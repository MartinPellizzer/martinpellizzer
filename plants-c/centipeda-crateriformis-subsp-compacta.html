Centipeda crateriformis subsp. compacta

Centipeda crateriformis subsp.

compacta, commonly known as the compact centipeda, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions. It features compact, rosette-like growth with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cup-like inflorescence, which is characteristic of the genus Centipeda. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
