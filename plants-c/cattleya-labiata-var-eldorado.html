Cattleya labiata var. eldorado

Cattleya labiata var. eldorado, commonly known as the Eldorado Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, pendulous blooms with a combination of pink and white hues, often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant is native to Brazil and thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light. It has thick, fleshy roots that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
