Cunonia purpurea

Cunonia purpurea, commonly known as the purple cunonia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in spring. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Cunonia purpurea is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
