Cotyledon chiclensis

Cotyledon chiclensis, commonly known as the Mexican ice plant, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Cotyledon chiclensis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and drought tolerance.
