Copaifera trapezifolia var. crassiuscula

Copaifera trapezifolia var. crassiuscula, commonly known as Brazilian copaiba, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and produces a yellow to reddish resin known for its aromatic properties. The plant belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is valued for its medicinal and commercial uses. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and contributing to the biodiversity of the region.
