Cotyledon cossoniana

Cotyledon cossoniana, commonly known as the Cosson's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, which help it store water and withstand arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Cotyledon cossoniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
