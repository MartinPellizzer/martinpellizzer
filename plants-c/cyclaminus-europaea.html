Cyclaminus europaea

Cyclaminus europaea, commonly known as the European cyclamen, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, bell-like flowers that come in shades of pink, white, and violet. Native to southern Europe and western Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for indoor or outdoor gardens due to its ornamental value.

Cyclaminus europaea is a perennial herb that produces tuberous roots, which help it survive in adverse conditions.
