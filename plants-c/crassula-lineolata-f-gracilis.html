Crassula lineolata f. gracilis

Crassula lineolata f. gracilis, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Crassula, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens, containers, or as a houseplant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique foliage and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
