Chamaecrista glaziovii

Chamaecrista glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its pods are long and curved, containing seeds that are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant has a fibrous root system and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
