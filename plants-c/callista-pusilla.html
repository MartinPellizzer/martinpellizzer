Callista pusilla

Callista pusilla, commonly known as the small-flowered callista, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters and features delicate, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale blue petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Callista pusilla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its compact size and attractive blooms.
