Colchicum kunkelianum

Colchicum kunkelianum, commonly known as the Kunkel's colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features basal leaves that are broad and often covered in a dense layer of hairs. Its flowers are usually purple to pink in color and have a distinctive, spurred shape.

Colchicum kunkelianum is adapted to grow in rocky or well-drained soils, often in alpine or subalpine habitats.
