Corallodiscus flabellatus var. sericeus

Corallodiscus flabellatus var. sericeus, commonly known as the silky coral tree, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, coral-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of pink and white. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered with a fine, silvery pubescence, giving the plant its "sericeus" designation. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
