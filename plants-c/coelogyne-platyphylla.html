Coelogyne platyphylla

Coelogyne platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves that are broad and flat, adapted for storing water. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Coelogyne platyphylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
