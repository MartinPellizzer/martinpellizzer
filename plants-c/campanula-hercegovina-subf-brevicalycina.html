Campanula hercegovina subf. brevicalycina

Campanula hercegovina subf.

brevicalycina, commonly known as the Hercegovina bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family. It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. Its subsp. brevicalycina distinguishes itself with a shorter calyx compared to other subspecies.

This perennial herb is native to the Balkan region and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
