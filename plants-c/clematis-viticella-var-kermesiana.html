Clematis viticella var. kermesiana

Clematis viticella var. kermesiana, commonly known as the Kermesiana Clematis, is a compact, deciduous vine native to Europe.

It features arching stems that can reach up to 6 feet in length, adorned with small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale blue flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well-suited for growing in partial shade to full sun and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, ornamental vine.
