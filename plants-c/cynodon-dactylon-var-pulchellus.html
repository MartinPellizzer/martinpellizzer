Cynodon dactylon var. pulchellus

Cynodon dactylon var. pulchellus, commonly known as the velvet grass, is a variant of the common Bermuda grass.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and fine, silky blades that give it a velvety texture. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, sunny climates. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.

The variety is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and heavy foot traffic.
