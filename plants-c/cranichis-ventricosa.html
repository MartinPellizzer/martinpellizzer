Cranichis ventricosa

Cranichis ventricosa, commonly known as the balloon orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the southern United States, particularly Florida and Texas, where it thrives in wetlands and marshy areas. The plant is characterized by its unique, inflated, balloon-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. These flowers are typically borne on long, slender stems and bloom during the spring and summer months.

Cranichis ventricosa is a perennial herb that plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
