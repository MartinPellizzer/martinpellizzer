Cynoglossum maroccanum

Cynoglossum maroccanum, commonly known as the Moroccan tongue-shaped flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers.
