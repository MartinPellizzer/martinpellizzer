Calonemorchis elegans

Calonemorchis elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as tropical forests. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Calonemorchis elegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
