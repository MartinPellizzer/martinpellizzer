Cryptocoryne lutea var. minor

Cryptocoryne lutea var. minor, commonly known as the yellow cryptocoryne, is a compact aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features bright yellow to orange leaves that are typically lance-shaped and slightly serrated. This variety thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers soft, slightly acidic water and can tolerate a range of temperatures.

The plant is known for its ease of care and ability to grow in both planted and floating forms.
