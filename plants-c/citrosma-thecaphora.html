Citrosma thecaphora

Citrosma thecaphora, commonly known as the mountain citron.

It is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Thailand and Malaysia. The plant belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a distinctive aromatic scent.
