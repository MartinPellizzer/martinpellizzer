Crassula decumbens var. decumbens

Crassula decumbens var. decumbens, commonly known as the string of pearls plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact size and unique foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
