Catalpa × erubescens var. japonica

Catalpa × erubescens var. japonica, commonly known as the Japanese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The tree produces long, bean-like pods that contain seeds covered in silky hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its rapid growth and unique foliage make it a distinctive addition to landscapes.
