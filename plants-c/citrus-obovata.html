Citrus obovata

Citrus obovata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lemon or the Chinese lemon, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The fruit is typically round or elongated, with a thin, yellowish rind and a high concentration of essential oils. The plant is valued for its citrus scent and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes or for its aromatic properties.

It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
