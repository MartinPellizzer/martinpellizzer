Cynoglossum coelestinum

Cynoglossum coelestinum, commonly known as blueweed or forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.
