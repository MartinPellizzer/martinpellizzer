Carduus scordium

Carduus scordium, commonly known as goat's weed or tansy.

It is a flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae, native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features a tall, spiny stem with narrow, gray-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in dry, disturbed soils and can thrive in rocky or sandy habitats.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine, though its medicinal properties are not well-documented.
