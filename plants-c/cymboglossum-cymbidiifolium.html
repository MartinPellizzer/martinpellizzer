Cymboglossum cymbidiifolium

Cymboglossum cymbidiifolium, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking to add visual interest to their landscapes.
