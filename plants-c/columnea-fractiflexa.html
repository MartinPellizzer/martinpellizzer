Columnea fractiflexa

Columnea fractiflexa, commonly known as the flexible flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow throat. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is known for its attractive, showy bracts that resemble petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Columnea fractiflexa is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
