Cyclaminos mindleri

Cyclaminos mindleri, commonly known as the Mindleri cyclamen, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy leaves that are typically heart-shaped and often adorned with distinctive patterns. The plant produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Cyclaminos mindleri prefers cool, moist environments and is often grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for those seeking ornamental foliage and seasonal blooms.
