Chamaecrista eitenorum var. eitenorum

Chamaecrista eitenorum var. eitenorum, commonly known as the Eiten's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

It is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a forage crop.
