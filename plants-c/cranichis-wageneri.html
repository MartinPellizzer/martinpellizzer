Cranichis wageneri

Cranichis wageneri, commonly known as the Wagener's orchid, is a rare and delicate species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific conditions to bloom successfully.

Due to its habitat specificity and limited distribution, it is considered an endangered species.
