Clerodendrum melanocrater

Clerodendrum melanocrater, commonly known as the black-berried cleerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its fruit is a globose capsule containing small, black seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
