Centaurea antalyensis

Centaurea antalyensis, commonly known as the Antalya centaurea, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its bright yellow flowers are surrounded by spiny bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Turkey.
