Cyperus atronervatus

Cyperus atronervatus, commonly known as black-veined flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded environments, such as marshes and wetlands. Its dark green leaves with prominent, contrasting veins are notable features.

Cyperus atronervatus is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
