Calytrix variabilis

Calytrix variabilis, commonly known as the variable calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact shrub form and showy, pink or red flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Calytrix variabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
