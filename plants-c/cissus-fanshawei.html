Cissus fanshawei

Cissus fanshawei, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with silver and green patterns. The plant produces small, pale green flowers followed by round, waxy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Cissus fanshawei is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
