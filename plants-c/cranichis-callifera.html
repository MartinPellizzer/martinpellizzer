Cranichis callifera

Cranichis callifera, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species of orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the acidic soils in which it thrives.

The orchid has a delicate, grass-like appearance with narrow leaves and is often found in clusters.
