Campnosperma wallichii

Campnosperma wallichii, commonly known as the Indian ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. The tree is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in dry forests and rocky areas.
