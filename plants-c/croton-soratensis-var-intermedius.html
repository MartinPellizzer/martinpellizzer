Croton soratensis var. intermedius

Croton soratensis var. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red or purple. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.

This variant is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
