Coleus cunenensis

Coleus cunenensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage is often a deep green, with some varieties displaying striking variegation.
