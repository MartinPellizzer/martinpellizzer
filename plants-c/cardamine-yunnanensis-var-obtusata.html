Cardamine yunnanensis var. obtusata

Cardamine yunnanensis var. obtusata, commonly known as the Yunnan water shield, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of rounded, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
