Cattleya × Laurae

Cattleya × laurae is a hybrid orchid known for its vibrant and showy blooms. This plant is a cross between Cattleya loddigesii and Cattleya bowringiana. It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking color patterns and strong fragrance. The flowers typically feature a combination of red, yellow, and white petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.
