Cliffortia pulchella var. mucronulata

Cliffortia pulchella var. mucronulata, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that produces delicate, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs.

It is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.
