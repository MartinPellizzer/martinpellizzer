Cattleya forbesii f. rubra

Cattleya forbesii f. rubra, commonly known as the Red Forbes Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, deep red blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features long, arching stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. Its flowers are fragrant and can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, making it a striking ornamental plant.

This variety is often cultivated for its beauty and is relatively easy to care for in the right growing conditions.
