Camellia hunanica

Camellia hunanica, commonly known as the Hunan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia hunanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
