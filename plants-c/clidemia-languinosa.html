Clidemia languinosa

Clidemia languinosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf or tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests. It has a woody stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Clidemia languinosa is also known for its role in supporting certain butterfly species, particularly the monarch butterfly.
