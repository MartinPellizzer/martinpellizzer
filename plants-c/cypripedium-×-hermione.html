Cypripedium × hermione

Cypripedium × hermione, commonly known as the Hermione lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, typically pink or white in color. The plant is native to regions of Asia, including China and Japan, where it thrives in cool, moist forest environments. It has a pseudobulb structure and produces long, strap-like leaves.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts, though it is also considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
