Ceropteris Triangularis

Ceropteris triangularis is a unique fern species known for its distinctive triangular fronds. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections. The plant's ability to adapt to various soil types enhances its appeal to plant enthusiasts. With its historical significance in traditional medicine, it holds both aesthetic and cultural value.
