Cineraria reniformis

Cineraria reniformis, commonly known as the broadleaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves and produces showy blue or violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Cineraria reniformis is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
