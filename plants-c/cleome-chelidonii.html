Cleome chelidonii

Cleome chelidonii, commonly known as yellow weed or spider flower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring slender stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields.

Cleome chelidonii is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
