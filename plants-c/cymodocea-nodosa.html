Cymodocea nodosa

Cymodocea nodosa, commonly known as the ribbon grass, is a marine flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its long, ribbon-like leaves that are often found in shallow coastal waters. Its rhizomes form extensive networks, contributing to sediment stabilization and coastal protection.
