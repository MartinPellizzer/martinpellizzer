Crassula sarcocaulis var. scaberula

Crassula sarcocaulis var. scaberula, commonly known as the Woolly Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
