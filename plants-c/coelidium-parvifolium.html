Coelidium parvifolium

Coelidium parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaf coelidium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
