Corethrogyne incana var. rigida

Corethrogyne incana var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid silverleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
