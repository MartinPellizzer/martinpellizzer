Clematis japonica var. tosaensis

Clematis japonica var. tosaensis, commonly known as Tosa Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to Japan.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, woody stems that can climb by means of small tendrils. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, palmate leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy.

It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.
