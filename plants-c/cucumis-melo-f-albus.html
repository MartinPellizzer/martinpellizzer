Cucumis melo f. albus

Cucumis melo f. albus, commonly known as the white muskmelon, is a botanical variant of the muskmelon species.

It is characterized by its pale green, smooth rind and sweet, aromatic flesh. The plant typically grows as a vine with lobed leaves and produces yellow flowers. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its flavor and is often cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
