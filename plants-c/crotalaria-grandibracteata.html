Crotalaria grandibracteata

Crotalaria grandibracteata, commonly known as the large bract crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven leaflets, and its stems are often hairy. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
