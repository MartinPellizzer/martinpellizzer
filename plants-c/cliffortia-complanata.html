Cliffortia complanata

Cliffortia complanata, commonly known as the flatleaf flax or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found growing on slopes and cliffs. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators.
