Cyrtanthus breviflorus

Cyrtanthus breviflorus, commonly known as the short-flowered bell-shaped orchid, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Cyrtanthus breviflorus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
