Cryptocoryne albida

Cryptocoryne albida, commonly known as the white cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, ribbon-like leaves that are typically pale green to white in color. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light levels. Cryptocoryne albida is often cultivated in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Its rhizome system allows it to spread and form dense mats, making it a popular choice for background planting in planted tanks.
