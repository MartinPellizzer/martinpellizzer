Coryanthes lafontainei

Coryanthes lafontainei, commonly known as the LaFontaine's hooded orchid, is a rare and striking tropical orchid native to Madagascar.

This species is characterized by its large, intricate flowers that resemble elaborate hats or hoods, with a unique structure that includes a spur and a labellum. The plant produces vibrant, often colorful blooms that can range from red to orange, with a distinctive dark central stripe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specialized habitat, Coryanthes lafontainei is considered a conservation concern.
