Chamaecrista desvauxii var. graminea

Chamaecrista desvauxii var. graminea, commonly known as the grassy burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange pods that contain several seeds. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in savannas and along roadsides, contributing to soil stabilization through its extensive root system.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
