Cyathea arborea var. regnellii

Cyathea arborea var. regnellii, commonly known as the Regnell's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical environments. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its massive fronds, and it plays a role in forest ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to nutrient cycling.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
