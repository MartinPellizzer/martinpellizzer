Crawfurdia championii

Crawfurdia championii, commonly known as the red-flowered spider lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. Its bulbs are underground storage organs that help it survive dry periods.

Crawfurdia championii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
