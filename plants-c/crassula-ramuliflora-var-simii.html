Crassula ramuliflora var. simii

Crassula ramuliflora var. simii, commonly known as the rock rose or miniature crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its hardy nature allows it to withstand periods of drought and mild frost.
