Chenopodium × smardae

Chenopodium × smardae, commonly known as tumble mustard, is a hybrid species within the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and production of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas. Its leaves are succulent and covered in fine hairs, while its seeds are small and dispersed by wind.

It is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid growth and ability to colonize new areas.
