Clematis obvallata var. iyoensis

Clematis obvallata var. iyoensis, commonly known as the Iyoensis Clematis, is a climbing vine native to Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
