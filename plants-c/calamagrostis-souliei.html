Calamagrostis souliei

Calamagrostis souliei, commonly known as the Chinese silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It features slender, silver-haired leaves and produces fluffy, silver-pink seed heads that add ornamental value to gardens. The plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. It is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and its aesthetic appeal in landscaping.

Calamagrostis souliei is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
