Cestrum azuense

Cestrum azuense, commonly known as the Azuense Trumpetflower, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, with a strong, sweet fragrance. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a rough texture.

Cestrum azuense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
