Cyphokentia macrostachya

Cyphokentia macrostachya, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is native to the Pacific islands, including Hawaii and Tahiti. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish flowers that are followed by edible, fibrous fruits.

Its robust growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
