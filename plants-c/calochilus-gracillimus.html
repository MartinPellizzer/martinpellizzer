Calochilus gracillimus

Calochilus gracillimus, commonly known as the slender sun orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pendulous flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Calochilus gracillimus is often found in coastal regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
