Cosmos linearifolius var. linearifolius

Cosmos linearifolius var. linearifolius, commonly known as the linearleaf cosmos, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming throughout the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its growth habit is typically bushy, with multiple stems emerging from the base.
