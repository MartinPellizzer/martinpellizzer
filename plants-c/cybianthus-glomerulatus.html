Cybianthus glomerulatus

Cybianthus glomerulatus, commonly known as the clustered broom.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds.

Cybianthus glomerulatus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive blooms and drought tolerance.
