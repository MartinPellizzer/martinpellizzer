Croton tartonrairoides

Croton tartonrairoides, commonly known as the red-veined croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves.

The foliage features deep green veins contrasted with bright red or orange hues, creating a visually arresting display. Native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light. Its leaves are often adorned with intricate patterns and can vary in shape from ovate to lanceolate.

While it is primarily grown as an ornamental plant, it also has historical uses in traditional medicine.
