Cnidoscolus vitifolius var. cnicodendrum

Cnidoscolus vitifolius var. cnicodendrum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead or arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by edible, spiny fruits that contain kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitat.
