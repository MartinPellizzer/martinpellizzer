Camellia mairei var. mairei

Camellia mairei var. mairei, commonly known as the Maire's camellia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a distinctive yellow or orange center. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The camellia is also valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its winter blooming habit.
