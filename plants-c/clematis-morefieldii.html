Clematis morefieldii

Clematis morefieldii, commonly known as the Morefield's clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features climbing stems with thornless, slightly hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple during late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include compound leaves with three to five leaflets and a long, trailing growth habit.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
