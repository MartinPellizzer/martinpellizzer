Ceropegia splendidior

Ceropegia splendidior, commonly known as the magnificent arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tuberous roots. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia splendidior is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
