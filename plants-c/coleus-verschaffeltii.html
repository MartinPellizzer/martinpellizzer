Coleus verschaffeltii

Coleus verschaffeltii, commonly known as the velvetleaf coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs and shrubs. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance.

Coleus verschaffeltii prefers warm, humid conditions and is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.
