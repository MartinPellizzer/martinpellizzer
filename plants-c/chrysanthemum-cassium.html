Chrysanthemum cassium

Chrysanthemum cassium, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a central disk, and its leaves are typically gray-green and hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Chrysanthemum cassium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
