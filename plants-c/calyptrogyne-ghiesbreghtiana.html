Calyptrogyne ghiesbreghtiana

Calyptrogyne ghiesbreghtiana, commonly known as the mangrove apple, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
