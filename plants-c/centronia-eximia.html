Centronia eximia

Centronia eximia, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant native to regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

Centronia eximia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
