Chasmanthe floribunda

Chasmanthe floribunda, commonly known as the flame lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as birds and insects. Chasmanthe floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
