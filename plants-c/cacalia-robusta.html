Cacalia robusta

Cacalia robusta, commonly known as the robust cacalia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and Georgia. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Cacalia robusta thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.
