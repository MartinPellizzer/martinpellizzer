Cymopterus aboriginum var. oblongus

Cymopterus aboriginum var. oblongus, commonly known as the narrowleaf scrophularia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Australia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white.
