Croton olanchanus

Croton olanchanus, commonly known as the yellow croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often bright yellow or green, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.

Croton olanchanus is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
