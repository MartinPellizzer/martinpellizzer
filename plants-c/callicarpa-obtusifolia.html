Callicarpa obtusifolia

Callicarpa obtusifolia, commonly known as the blunt-leaf beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant purple berries that appear in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

The berries are not only ornamental but also serve as a food source for birds and other wildlife.
