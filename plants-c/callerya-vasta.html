Callerya vasta

Callerya vasta, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter, often in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has thick, fleshy stems and broad, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its native habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in forest understories or near water sources.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
