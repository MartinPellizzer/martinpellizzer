Calyptranthes musciflora var. obscura

Calyptranthes musciflora var. obscura, commonly known as the dark mossy calyptranthes, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and unique, moss-like appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservatories.
