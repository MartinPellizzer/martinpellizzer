Cyclopogon adhaesus

Cyclopogon adhaesus, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a species of grass native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the islands of Cuba and Hispaniola. This plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Cyclopogon adhaesus is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
