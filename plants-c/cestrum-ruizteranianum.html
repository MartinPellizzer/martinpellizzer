Cestrum ruizteranianum

Cestrum ruizteranianum, commonly known as the Ruizterranian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its attractive, fragrant flowers that bloom at night, typically in shades of white and purple. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but should be consumed with caution due to their mild toxicity. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
