Campanula × pechlaneri

Campanula × pechlaneri, commonly known as the hybrid bellflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula × pechlaneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
