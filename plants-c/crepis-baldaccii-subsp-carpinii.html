Crepis baldaccii subsp. carpinii

Crepis baldaccii subsp.

carpinii, commonly known as the Carpinii mouse-ear, is a flowering plant species native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and hairy leaves.
