Clitoria ternatea f. subpolyadelpha

Clitoria ternatea f. subpolyadelpha, commonly known as the multibract pea or the butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-like flowers. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces clusters of vibrant blue or purple flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated pods, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

This variant is distinguished by its subpolyadelphous stamens, a feature that sets it apart from other forms of Clitoria ternatea.
