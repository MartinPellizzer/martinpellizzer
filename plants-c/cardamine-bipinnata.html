Cardamine bipinnata

Cardamine bipinnata, commonly known as the fernleaf watercress, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions of Asia and North America. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves that are deeply divided into small, pinnate segments. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and borne in clusters at the base of the plant.

Cardamine bipinnata thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and forest floors.
