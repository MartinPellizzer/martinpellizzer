Cattleya dowiana var. dowiana

Cattleya dowiana var. dowiana, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a species of orchid native to the highlands of Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers with a distinctive pouch-like lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
