Crossandra grandidieri

Crossandra grandidieri, commonly known as the Madagascar flame flower, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface and is often grown for its vibrant floral display. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Crossandra grandidieri is popular in gardens and as a potted plant due to its long-lasting blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
