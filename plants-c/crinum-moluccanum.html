Crinum moluccanum

Crinum moluccanum, commonly known as the Moluccan crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to Indonesia.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant has strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Crinum moluccanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
