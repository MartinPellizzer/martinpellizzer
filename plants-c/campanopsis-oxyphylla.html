Campanopsis oxyphylla

Campanopsis oxyphylla, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, often with a pinkish hue. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Campanopsis oxyphylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
