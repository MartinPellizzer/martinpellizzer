Ceanothus hirsutus var. orcuttii

Ceanothus hirsutus var. orcuttii, commonly known as the Orcutt's ceanothus, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. It is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native gardens and ecological restoration projects.
