Clerodendrum navesianum

Clerodendrum navesianum, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow berries that contain seeds. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum navesianum thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
