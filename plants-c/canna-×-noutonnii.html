Canna × noutonnii

Canna × noutonnii, commonly known as the hybrid canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a hybrid between Canna indica and Canna flaccida, resulting in a plant with large, colorful leaves and showy flowers. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, often with a reddish or purplish tint. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with three prominent petals and a central cluster of stamens.

This plant thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often used in gardens for its vibrant appearance.
