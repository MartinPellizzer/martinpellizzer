Clausena brevistyla var. brevistyla

Clausena brevistyla var. brevistyla, commonly known as the shortstyle Clausena, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptic to ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by edible, orange-yellow berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its ornamental value and edible fruits make it a desirable plant for both landscaping and cultivation.
