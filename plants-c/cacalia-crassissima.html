Cacalia crassissima

Cacalia crassissima, commonly known as the broadleaf cacalia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a central disk, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a rough texture.
