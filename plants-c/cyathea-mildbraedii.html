Cyathea mildbraedii

Cyathea mildbraedii, commonly known as the mildbraed's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that can reach up to 3 meters in height. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Cyathea mildbraedii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
