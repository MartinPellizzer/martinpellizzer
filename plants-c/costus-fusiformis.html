Costus fusiformis

Costus fusiformis, commonly known as the spindle costus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features tall, cylindrical stems with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and are borne in clusters at the base of the plant.

Costus fusiformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
