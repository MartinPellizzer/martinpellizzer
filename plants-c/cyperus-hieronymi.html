Cyperus hieronymi

Cyperus hieronymi, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant features slender, grass-like stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense clumps. Its leaves are narrow and cylindrical, with a fibrous texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

Cyperus hieronymi is often found in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
