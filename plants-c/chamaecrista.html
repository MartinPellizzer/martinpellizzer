Chamaecrista

Chamaecrista, commonly known as the ticktrefoil, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

These plants are characterized by their compound leaves, often with small, bean-like pods that contain seeds. Many species are native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in the Americas. They are often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
