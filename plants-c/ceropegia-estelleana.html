Ceropegia estelleana

Ceropegia estelleana, commonly known as the String of Hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with pairs of heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow, adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
