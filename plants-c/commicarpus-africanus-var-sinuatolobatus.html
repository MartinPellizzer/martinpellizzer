Commicarpus africanus var. sinuatolobatus

Commicarpus africanus var. sinuatolobatus, commonly known as the African commicarpus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh conditions.
