Cytisus arboreus var. haplotrichus

Cytisus arboreus var. haplotrichus, commonly known as the red broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are small, oval, and slightly hairy, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
