Cleome conrathii

Cleome conrathii, commonly known as the pink cleome, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features branching stems and small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately lobed, with a slightly hairy texture.

Cleome conrathii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
