Cheilanthes distans

Cheilanthes distans, commonly known as the remote lip fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear fronds that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures. It is native to regions of North America, particularly in the southwestern United States and parts of Mexico.
