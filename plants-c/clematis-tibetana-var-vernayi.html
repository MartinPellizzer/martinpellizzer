Clematis tibetana var. vernayi

Clematis tibetana var. vernayi, commonly known as the Tibetan Clematis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Tibet.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows. Its leaves are typically compound, with finely divided leaflets, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers in late spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
