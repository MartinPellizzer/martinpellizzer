Chione italica

Chione italica, commonly known as the Italian snowbell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a suitable choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and graceful appearance.
