Carduus euosmus

Carduus euosmus, commonly known as the European thistle.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe, characterized by its spiny leaves and purple composite flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its robust growth habit. Its leaves are covered in dense, sharp prickles, which serve as a natural defense mechanism.

The flowers bloom in late summer and are important for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
