Cryptanthus schwackeanus

Cryptanthus schwackeanus, commonly known as the Schwacke's winterbloom, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom during the cooler months. Its foliage consists of fleshy, succulent leaves that help it survive in dry conditions.

Cryptanthus schwackeanus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
