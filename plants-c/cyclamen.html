Cyclamen

Cyclamen, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, bowl-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Cyclamen is often cultivated as a decorative plant, especially during the winter months.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
