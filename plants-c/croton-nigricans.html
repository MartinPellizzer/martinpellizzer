Croton nigricans

Croton nigricans, commonly known as black croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and striking, often brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
