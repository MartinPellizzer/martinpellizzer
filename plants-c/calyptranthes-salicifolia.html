Calyptranthes salicifolia

Calyptranthes salicifolia, commonly known as the willowleaf calyptranthes, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that resemble those of willow trees, hence its common name. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.
