Cosmiusa repens

Cosmiusa repens, commonly known as the creeping cosmos, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
