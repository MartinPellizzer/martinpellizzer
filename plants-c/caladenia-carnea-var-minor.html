Caladenia carnea var. minor

Caladenia carnea var. minor, commonly known as the Pink Sun-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in open habitats. It features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy flower that blooms during spring.

This variety is relatively small compared to other members of the Caladenia genus, making it a unique and delicate addition to the orchid flora.
