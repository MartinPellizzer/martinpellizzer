Consolida teheranica f. ecalcarata

Consolida teheranica f. ecalcarata, commonly known as the Persian rocket or Persian larkspur, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it produces elongated seed pods that contain small, rounded seeds.

This subspecies is native to regions of Iran and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
