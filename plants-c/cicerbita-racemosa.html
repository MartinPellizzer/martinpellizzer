Cicerbita racemosa

Cicerbita racemosa, commonly known as the clustered bittercress, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with small, four-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
