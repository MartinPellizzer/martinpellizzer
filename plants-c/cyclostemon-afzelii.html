Cyclostemon afzelii

Cyclostemon afzelii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has a distinctive, often multi-branched stem with rough, ridged bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a pointed tip.

Cyclostemon afzelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates due to its vibrant floral display.
