Cucumis aculeatus

Cucumis aculeatus, commonly known as the spiny cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its hairy, spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into elongated, spiny fruits. The fruit is typically harvested when it is still green, though it can ripen to a yellow or orange color.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and is also used in some traditional cuisines.
