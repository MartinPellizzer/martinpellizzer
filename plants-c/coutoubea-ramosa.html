Coutoubea ramosa

Coutoubea ramosa, commonly known as the ramose coutoubea, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers during the summer months.

The plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
