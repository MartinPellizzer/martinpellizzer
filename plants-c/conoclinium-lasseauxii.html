Conoclinium lasseauxii

Conoclinium lasseauxii, commonly known as Mexican blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Conoclinium lasseauxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and hummingbirds.
