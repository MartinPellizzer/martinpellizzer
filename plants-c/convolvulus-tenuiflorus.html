Convolvulus tenuiflorus

Convolvulus tenuiflorus, commonly known as slender-flowered morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive funnel-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or blue. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
