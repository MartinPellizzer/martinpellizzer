Ceropegia leachii var. oviflora

Ceropegia leachii var. oviflora, commonly known as the African violet or velvet plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to violet, often with a distinctive pattern of purple and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular as a houseplant and in horticultural collections.
