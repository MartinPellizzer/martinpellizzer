Cyperus consors

Cyperus consors, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, three-sided spikelets that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies.

Cyperus consors is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and improve water quality.
