Cirsium samniticum

Cirsium samniticum, commonly known as the Samnite thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts.

Cirsium samniticum is often found in regions with Mediterranean climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
