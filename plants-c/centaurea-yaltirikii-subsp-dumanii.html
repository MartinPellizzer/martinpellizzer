Centaurea yaltirikii subsp. dumanii

Centaurea yaltirikii subsp.

dumanii, commonly known as the Duman's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. It features upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, composite flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
