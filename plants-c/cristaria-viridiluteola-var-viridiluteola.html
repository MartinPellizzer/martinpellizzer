Cristaria viridiluteola var. viridiluteola

Cristaria viridiluteola var. viridiluteola, commonly known as the green-veined orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with a pale green or white hue. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a greenish tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
