Cymbidium atrolabium

Cymbidium atrolabium, commonly known as the black-labellum orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous flowers with dark, velvety petals and a distinctive blackish labellum. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green in color.

This orchid is valued for its striking appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and collections.
