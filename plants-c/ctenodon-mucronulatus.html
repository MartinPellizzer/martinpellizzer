Ctenodon mucronulatus

Ctenodon mucronulatus, commonly known as the slender spinytail skink, is a small, legless lizard native to parts of Southeast Asia.

The plant-like appearance of its body, with segmented scales, can sometimes lead to confusion with certain succulent plants. Its ecological role is primarily that of a predator, feeding on small invertebrates and contributing to the balance of its ecosystem.
