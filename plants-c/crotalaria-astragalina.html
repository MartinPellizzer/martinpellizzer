Crotalaria astragalina

Crotalaria astragalina, commonly known as the slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
