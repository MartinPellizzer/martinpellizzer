Codiphus

Codiphus, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Nelumbonaceae and is characterized by its large, showy flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is often associated with spiritual and cultural significance in various Asian cultures. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
