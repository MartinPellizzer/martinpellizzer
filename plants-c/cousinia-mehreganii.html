Cousinia mehreganii

Cousinia mehreganii, commonly known as Mehregan's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has small, serrated leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Cousinia mehreganii is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
