Campella media

Campella media, commonly known as the common campella, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring upright stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale blue or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and alternate along the stem, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Campella media is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
