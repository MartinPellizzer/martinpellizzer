Camellia tuberculata var. atuberculata

Camellia tuberculata var. atuberculata, commonly known as the smooth camellia, is a species native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are dark green and leathery in texture. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.

Camellia tuberculata var. atuberculata prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.
