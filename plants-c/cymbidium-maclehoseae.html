Cymbidium maclehoseae

Cymbidium maclehoseae, commonly known as the Maclehose orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern China and northern Vietnam.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores nutrients. The plant produces tall, slender stems with several broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically fragrant, with petals and sepals that are often white or pale pink, and a distinctive, often darker lip.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its elegant blooms.
