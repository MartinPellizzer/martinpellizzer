Chlorophytum grewenii

Chlorophytum grewenii, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail Plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Chlorophytum grewenii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
