Canavalia dura

Canavalia dura, commonly known as the hard bean or burr bean, is a species of legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing plant characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive, spiny pods. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom along its stems. Its seeds are encased in thick, woody pods, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Canavalia dura is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in agricultural settings.
