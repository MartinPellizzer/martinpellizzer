Clematis jingxiensis

Clematis jingxiensis, commonly known as the Jingxi Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are compound, with three to five leaflets, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Clematis jingxiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
