Carduus mouillefarinii

Carduus mouillefarinii, commonly known as the Mouillefarin thistle, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other thistle and daisy species.

The plant is not considered invasive and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
