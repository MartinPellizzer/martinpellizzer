Campanula trachelium var. oligosantha

Campanula trachelium var. oligosantha, commonly known as the lesser bellflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate regions.
