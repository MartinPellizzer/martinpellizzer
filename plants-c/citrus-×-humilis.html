Citrus × humilis

Citrus × humilis, commonly known as the dwarf lemon.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its fragrant, small, yellow citrus fruits. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit.

The species is also used in horticulture for its cold hardiness and compact growth habit.
