Clytostoma sciuripabulum

Clytostoma sciuripabulum, commonly known as the squirrel-eared orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pendulous inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring white or pale yellow petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
