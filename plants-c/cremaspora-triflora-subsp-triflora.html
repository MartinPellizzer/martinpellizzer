Cremaspora triflora subsp. triflora

Cremaspora triflora subsp.

triflora, commonly known as the three-flowered cremaspora, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or semi-arid regions.
