Conyza linearifolia

Conyza linearifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides, and it thrives in temperate climates. Its linear, narrow leaves are a distinguishing feature, and it produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer.

Conyza linearifolia is often found in dry, sandy soils and is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize new areas.
