Centaurea pseudophrygia subsp. alutacea

Centaurea pseudophrygia subsp.

alutacea, commonly known as the Alutaceae centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces vibrant blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight hairy texture.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
