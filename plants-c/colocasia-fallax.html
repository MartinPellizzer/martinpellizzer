Colocasia fallax

Colocasia fallax, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies. Its foliage is typically a deep green with a waxy texture.

Colocasia fallax is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
