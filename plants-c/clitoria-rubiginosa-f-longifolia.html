Clitoria rubiginosa f. longifolia

Clitoria rubiginosa f. longifolia, commonly known as the long-flowered butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.

Additionally, it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
