Chirita linglingensis

Chirita linglingensis, commonly known as the Lingling begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, ornamental foliage and vibrant flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and bright green coloration. Its flowers are usually red or orange, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

Chirita linglingensis is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
