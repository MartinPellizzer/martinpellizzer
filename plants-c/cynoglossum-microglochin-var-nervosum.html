Cynoglossum microglochin var. nervosum

Cynoglossum microglochin var. nervosum, commonly known as the narrow-veined forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive veining pattern, which gives the variety its name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or along stream banks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
