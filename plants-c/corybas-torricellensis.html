Corybas torricellensis

Corybas torricellensis, commonly known as the Torricella hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its unique, hood-like flower that is often brightly colored, typically in shades of red or orange. The plant produces a single, fleshy, club-shaped pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Corybas torricellensis is a rare and protected species, found primarily in the South Island of New Zealand.
