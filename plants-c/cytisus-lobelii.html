Cytisus lobelii

Cytisus lobelii, commonly known as the Lobel's broom, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It features slender, often branched stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Cytisus lobelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
