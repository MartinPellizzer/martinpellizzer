Cotyledon sinus-alexandri

Cotyledon sinus-alexandri, commonly known as the Alexandrian cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often ribbed and have a distinctive wavy or notched margin. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
