Cactus klugii

Cactus klugii, commonly known as the Klug's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green, spiky structure. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for desert gardens and rockeries.
