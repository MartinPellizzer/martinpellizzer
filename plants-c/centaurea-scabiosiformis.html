Centaurea scabiosiformis

Centaurea scabiosiformis, commonly known as the Scabious Centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are blue to purple in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
