Calesiam velutinum

Calesiam velutinum, commonly known as the velvet leaf caleia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Calesiam velutinum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
