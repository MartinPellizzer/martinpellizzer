Campanula rotundifolia var. giesekiana

Campanula rotundifolia var. giesekiana, commonly known as the round-leaf bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has rounded, hairy leaves that are typically clustered at the base, giving it a compact appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
