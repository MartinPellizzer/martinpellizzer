Cerastium decalvans f. robustum

Cerastium decalvans f. robustum, commonly known as the robust moss campion, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its succulent appearance.

It thrives in well-drained, rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
