Clintonia alpina var. udensis

Clintonia alpina var. udensis, commonly known as the Udens' alpine bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, white to pale green flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and ornamental addition to specialized gardens.
