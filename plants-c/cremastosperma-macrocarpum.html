Cremastosperma macrocarpum

Cremastosperma macrocarpum, commonly known as the large-seeded star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats. The plant features a robust, upright stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring.

The fruit is a distinctive, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.
