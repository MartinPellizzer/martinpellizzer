Cosmos landii var. achalconensis

Cosmos landii var. achalconensis, commonly known as the Mexican blue cosmos, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous annual, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and branched.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
