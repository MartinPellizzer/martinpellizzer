Corydalis solida f. digitata

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant produces slender, branching stems and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
