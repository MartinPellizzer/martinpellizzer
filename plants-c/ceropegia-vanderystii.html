Ceropegia vanderystii

Ceropegia vanderystii, commonly known as the snake plant or mother of thousands, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
