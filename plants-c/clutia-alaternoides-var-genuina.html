Clutia alaternoides var. genuina

Clutia alaternoides var. genuina, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, star-shaped white or purple flowers and smooth, dark green leaves. It produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe.

This species is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
