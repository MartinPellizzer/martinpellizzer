Chamaecytisus × versicolor

Chamaecytisus × versicolor, commonly known as the variable broom.

This hybrid shrub is native to the British Isles and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. It features small, bright yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has silvery-green foliage that contrasts beautifully with its vibrant floral display.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
