Ctenophyllum adanum

Ctenophyllum adanum, commonly known as the Adanum fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Ctenophyllum adanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial shade.
