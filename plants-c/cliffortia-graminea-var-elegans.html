Cliffortia graminea var. elegans

Cliffortia graminea var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant hairveld grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
