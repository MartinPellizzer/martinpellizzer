Cyrtandra × ferruginosa

Cyrtandra × ferruginosa, commonly known as the hybrid flame bush, is a tropical plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. Cyrtandra × ferruginosa is native to regions in Southeast Asia and is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
