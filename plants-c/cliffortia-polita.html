Cliffortia polita

Cliffortia polita, commonly known as the silky cotyledon, is a low-growing, succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a touch of color to its otherwise subdued foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
