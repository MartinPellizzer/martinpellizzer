Chuselaginella Lonko-Batu

Lonko-batu chuselaginella, scientifically known as Chuselaginella lonko-batu, is a rare and enigmatic plant species found in the remote highlands of Southeast Asia. This unique flora is characterized by its striking violet petals and delicate, spiral-shaped leaves that shimmer under the sun. Despite its beauty, very little is known about its ecological role or reproductive habits. Its habitat is increasingly threatened by deforestation and climate change, raising concerns for its survival. Conservation efforts are urgently needed to protect this extraordinary plant from extinction.
