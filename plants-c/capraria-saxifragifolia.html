Capraria saxifragifolia

Capraria saxifragifolia, commonly known as the rock saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Capraria saxifragifolia is well adapted to cold climates and is often found in areas with poor soil conditions.
