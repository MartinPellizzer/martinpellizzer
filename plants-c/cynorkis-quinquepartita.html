Cynorkis quinquepartita

Cynorkis quinquepartita, commonly known as the five-parted orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, fragrant flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
