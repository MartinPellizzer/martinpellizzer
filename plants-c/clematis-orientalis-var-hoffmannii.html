Clematis orientalis var. hoffmannii

Clematis orientalis var. hoffmannii, commonly known as the Hoffmann's oriental clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, round, green berries that mature to a dark purple or black color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or walls.
