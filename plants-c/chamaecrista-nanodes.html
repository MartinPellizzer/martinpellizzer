Chamaecrista nanodes

Chamaecrista nanodes, commonly known as smallleaf burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing annual shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, pinnately compound leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its seeds are encased in distinctive, spiny pods that remain on the plant after maturity.

Chamaecrista nanodes is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to full sun conditions.
