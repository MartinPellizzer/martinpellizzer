Calothamnus planifolius f. latifolius

Calothamnus planifolius f. latifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved bottlebrush, is a variant of the native Australian plant Calothamnus planifolius.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves and showy, bottlebrush-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils in arid and semi-arid regions of southern Australia. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

The foliage and floral structure make it an ornamental plant suitable for gardens in suitable climates.
