Conyza martii

Conyza martii, commonly known as the smallflower blueweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Conyza martii is often found in arid environments and is adapted to thrive in low-nutrient soils.
