Chrysanthemum × maximoviczianum

Chrysanthemum × maximoviczianum, commonly known as the Maximowicz chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and a wide range of flower colors, including white, yellow, and red. Native to eastern Asia, this plant thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy blooms and long flowering period.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
