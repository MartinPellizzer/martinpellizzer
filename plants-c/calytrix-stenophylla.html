Calytrix stenophylla

Calytrix stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Calytrix stenophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
