Crotalaria foliosa var. obovata

Crotalaria foliosa var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf crotalaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its obovate leaves, which are broad and rounded at the tips, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in racemes, which are often accompanied by stipules that are prominent and leaf-like. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

This variety is known for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
