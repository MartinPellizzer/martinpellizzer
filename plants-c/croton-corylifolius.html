Croton corylifolius

Croton corylifolius, commonly known as the Chinese croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that resemble those of the castor oil plant. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, spiny capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
