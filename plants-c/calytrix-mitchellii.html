Calytrix mitchellii

Calytrix mitchellii, commonly known as Mitchell's calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and sandy soils.

Calytrix mitchellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
