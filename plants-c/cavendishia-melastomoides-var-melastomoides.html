Cavendishia melastomoides var. melastomoides

Cavendishia melastomoides var. melastomoides, commonly known as the purple-leafed melastome, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple leaves and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It belongs to the Melastomataceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species.

Its foliage and floral structure make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
