Calliandra Tweediei Var. Sancti-Pauli

Sancti pauli calliandra, scientifically known as Calliandra tweediei var. sancti-pauli, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. This evergreen shrub is prized for its vibrant, feather-like clusters of flowers that come in a range of colors from pink to red. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and landscape design. With proper care, this plant can add a tropical flair to any outdoor space.
