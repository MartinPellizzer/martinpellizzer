Cyclamen insulare

Cyclamen insulare, commonly known as the island cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin and produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas. Cyclamen insulare has a short rhizome and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

It is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the winter months.
