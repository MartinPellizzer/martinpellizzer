Comparettia langkastii

Comparettia langkastii, commonly known as the Langkastii slipper orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its intricate, slipper-shaped pouch that traps insects for pollination. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually a vibrant mix of yellow and red, with a complex structure that aids in its unique pollination strategy.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
