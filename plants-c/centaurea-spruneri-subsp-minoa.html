Centaurea spruneri subsp. minoa

Centaurea spruneri subsp.

minoa, commonly known as the Minot centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking blue to purple flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its resilience.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads.
