Combretum adenophyllum

Combretum adenophyllum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Combretum adenophyllum is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
