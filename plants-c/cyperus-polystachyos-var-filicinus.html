Cyperus polystachyos var. filicinus

Cyperus polystachyos var. filicinus, commonly known as the fern-like flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that resemble fern fronds, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found in disturbed soils near water bodies.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various moist habitats, contributing to its widespread distribution.
