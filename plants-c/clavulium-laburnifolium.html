Clavulium laburnifolium

Clavulium laburnifolium, commonly known as the goldenchain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental in gardens.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
