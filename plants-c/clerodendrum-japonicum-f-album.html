Clerodendrum japonicum f. album

Clerodendrum japonicum f. album, commonly known as the white-flowered Chinese lantern plant, is a compact shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lantern-shaped, white bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that are followed by bright red, orb-shaped fruits. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in garden settings.
