Cotyledon trigyna

Cotyledon trigyna, commonly known as the three-lobed cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. Its leaves are often marked with distinctive patterns and can vary in shape depending on environmental conditions.

Cotyledon trigyna is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
