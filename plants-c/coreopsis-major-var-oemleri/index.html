Coreopsis Major Var. Oemleri

Coreopsis major var. oemleri is a striking flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its vibrant yellow blooms that appear in early summer. this variety is a popular choice for gardeners due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in various soil conditions. it attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to any landscape. its upright growth habit and long blooming period make it a standout in both wild and cultivated settings.
