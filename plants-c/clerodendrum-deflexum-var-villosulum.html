Clerodendrum deflexum var. villosulum

Clerodendrum deflexum var. villosulum, commonly known as the hairy clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and opposite leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
