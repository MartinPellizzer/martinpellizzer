Cynosurus cristatus f. tenuissimus

Cynosurus cristatus f. tenuissimus, commonly known as the slender bentgrass, is a variant of the common bentgrass species.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming grass that typically thrives in moist, acidic soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive, slightly curled appearance. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical structure makes it a valuable component of ecological studies and habitat restoration efforts.
