Calendula rigida

Calendula rigida, commonly known as rigid fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are rigid and often branched.

Calendula rigida is a hardy perennial that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
