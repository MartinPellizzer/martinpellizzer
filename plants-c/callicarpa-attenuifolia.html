Callicarpa attenuifolia

Callicarpa attenuifolia, commonly known as the slender beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, pale purple to pink berries that appear in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple on the underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Callicarpa attenuifolia is valued for its ornamental berries and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
