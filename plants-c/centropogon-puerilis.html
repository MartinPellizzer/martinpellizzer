Centropogon puerilis

Centropogon puerilis, commonly known as the small centropogon, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

Centropogon puerilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
