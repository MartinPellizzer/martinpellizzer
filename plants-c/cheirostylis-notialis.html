Cheirostylis notialis

Cheirostylis notialis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically borne on the upper portion of the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
