Cistus salviifolius f. longipedunculatus

Cistus salviifolius f. longipedunculatus, commonly known as the long peduncled salvifolius rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pink or white flowers in late summer.
