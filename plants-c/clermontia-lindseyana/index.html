Clermontia Lindseyana

Clermontia lindseyana is a rare and endangered plant native to the island of Hawaii. It belongs to the ginger family and is known for its striking white flowers with green bracts. This species is found only in a few isolated locations on the island of Maui. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this unique botanical treasure.
