Cucumis melo var. ecostatus

Cucumis melo var. ecostatus, commonly known as the Persian melon, is a variant of the cantaloupe species.

It is characterized by its rough, netted rind and sweet, aromatic flesh. The plant is a vine that produces large, spherical fruits with a distinctive warty appearance. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is typically harvested when it reaches full size and changes color, indicating maturity.
