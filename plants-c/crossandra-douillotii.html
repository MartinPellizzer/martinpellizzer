Crossandra douillotii

Crossandra douillotii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Crossandra douillotii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
