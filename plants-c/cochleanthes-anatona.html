Cochleanthes anatona

Cochleanthes anatona, commonly known as the Anatom's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of purple, white, and yellow hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Cochleanthes anatona is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
