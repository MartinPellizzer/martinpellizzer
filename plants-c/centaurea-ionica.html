Centaurea ionica

Centaurea ionica, commonly known as the Ionian centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Centaurea ionica is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
