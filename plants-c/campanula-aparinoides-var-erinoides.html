Campanula aparinoides var. erinoides

Campanula aparinoides var. erinoides, commonly known as the Erinoides bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
