Caladium lindenii var. sylvestre

Caladium lindenii var. sylvestre, commonly known as the wild caladium, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant mix of red, pink, and white, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Caladium lindenii var. sylvestre is valued in horticulture for its unique leaf patterns and adaptability to humid climates.
