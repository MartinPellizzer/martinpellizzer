Cryptandra lanosiflora

Cryptandra lanosiflora, commonly known as the woolly cryptandra, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its grey-green, woolly foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for native pollinators.

Cryptandra lanosiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
