Cirsium × brunneri

Cirsium × brunneri, commonly known as the Brunner's thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to the western United States, it thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to survive in poor soil conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
