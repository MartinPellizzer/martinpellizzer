Cyrtosperma johnstonii

Cyrtosperma johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Pacific Islands, particularly found in regions such as Fiji and Tonga. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color.

Cyrtosperma johnstonii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
