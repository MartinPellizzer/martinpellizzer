Ctenitis spekei

Ctenitis spekei, commonly known as the Speke's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of fiddlehead-like fronds that unfurl as they mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Ctenitis spekei plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
