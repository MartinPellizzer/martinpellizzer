Cyrtanthus × hybridus

Cyrtanthus × hybridus, commonly known as the hybrid cyclamen, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in spring, with petals that can range from white to pink, red, or even yellow. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically evergreen and form a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers cool, moist conditions, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in horticulture for its beauty and resilience.
