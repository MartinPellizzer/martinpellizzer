Clematis macropetala var. macropetala

Clematis macropetala var. macropetala, commonly known as the large-flowered clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern North America.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to violet in color, with a distinctive white throat. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are usually three to five leaflets. Its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs, and it produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that are follicles.

This species is valued for its ornamental value and is often grown in gardens for its early spring blooms.
