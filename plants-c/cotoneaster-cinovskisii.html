Cotoneaster cinovskisii

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in parts of Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The leaves are usually oval-shaped with a glossy upper surface and a lighter underside.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.
