Centaurea sylvatica nothof.

microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf wood centaury, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
