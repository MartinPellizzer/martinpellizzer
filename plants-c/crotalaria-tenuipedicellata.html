Crotalaria tenuipedicellata

Crotalaria tenuipedicellata, commonly known as the slender-seeded cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in racemes. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its seed pods are slender and contain numerous tiny, oblong seeds.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and agricultural fields.
