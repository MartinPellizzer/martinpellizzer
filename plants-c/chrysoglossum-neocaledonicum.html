Chrysoglossum neocaledonicum

Chrysoglossum neocaledonicum, commonly known as the New Caledonian chrysoglossum, is a rare and endemic plant species found exclusively in the rainforests of New Caledonia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often near stream banks or in rocky crevices. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters.
