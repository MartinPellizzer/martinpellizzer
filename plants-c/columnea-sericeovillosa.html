Columnea sericeovillosa

Columnea sericeovillosa, commonly known as the silky villosa columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant typically grows as a shrub or trailing vine, with opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Columnea sericeovillosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
