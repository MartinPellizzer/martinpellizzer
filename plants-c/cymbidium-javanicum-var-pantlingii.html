Cymbidium javanicum var. pantlingii

Cymbidium javanicum var. pantlingii, commonly known as the Pantlingii orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant blooms in shades of white, pink, and red, making it a prized ornamental species. It prefers cool, humid conditions and well-draining soil, often growing in mountainous regions.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral structure.
