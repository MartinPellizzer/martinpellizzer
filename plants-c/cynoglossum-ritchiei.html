Cynoglossum ritchiei

Cynoglossum ritchiei, commonly known as the purple heart or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep purple or violet color. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum ritchiei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms.
