Corynephorus gracilis

Corynephorus gracilis, commonly known as the slender grass or narrowleaf grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the grass family. Corynephorus gracilis is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.
