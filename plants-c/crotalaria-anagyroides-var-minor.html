Crotalaria anagyroides var. minor

Crotalaria anagyroides var. minor, commonly known as lesser crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its seeds are encased in a hard, spiny pod, and it is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its invasive potential in some areas, it is sometimes considered a weed.
