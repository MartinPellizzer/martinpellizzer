Chrysosplenium trachyspermum

Chrysosplenium trachyspermum, commonly known as broad-leaved chrysosplenium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of ovate to elliptic leaves with a hairy surface. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
