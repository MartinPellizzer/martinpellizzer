Chlorospatha croatiana var. enneaphylla

Chlorospatha croatiana var. enneaphylla, commonly known as the Croatian green fern, is a rare and striking variant of the original species.

It is characterized by its vibrant green fronds, which are typically longer and more delicate than those of the standard variety. The plant exhibits a unique leaf structure with nine leaflets, giving it the species name *enneaphylla*. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a botanical curiosity and a subject of interest for horticultural enthusiasts.
