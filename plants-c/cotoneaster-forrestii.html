Cotoneaster forrestii

Cotoneaster forrestii, commonly known as the Forster's cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Cotoneaster forrestii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
