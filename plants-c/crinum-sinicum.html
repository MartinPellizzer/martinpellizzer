Crinum sinicum

Crinum sinicum, commonly known as the Chinese spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to China.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, spidery appearance. The plant is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its bulbous structure allows it to store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in various environmental conditions.

Crinum sinicum is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in traditional Chinese medicine.
