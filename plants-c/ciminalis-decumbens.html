Ciminalis decumbens

Ciminalis decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate ciminalis, is a low-growing perennial plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features spreading stems that lie close to the ground, often forming a dense mat. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Ciminalis decumbens is often found in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or stream banks.
