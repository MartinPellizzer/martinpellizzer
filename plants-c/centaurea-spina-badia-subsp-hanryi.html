Centaurea spina-badia subsp. hanryi

Centaurea spina-badia subsp.

hanryi, commonly known as the Hanryi thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It has a compact growth habit with a short, upright stem and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

The subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and floral characteristics compared to other members of the Centaurea genus.
