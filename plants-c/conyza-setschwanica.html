Conyza setschwanica

Conyza setschwanica, commonly known as the Chinese ironweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender stems with opposite leaves that are lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
