Cranichis schlimii

Cranichis schlimii, commonly known as the Schlim's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually violet or purple in color and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Cranichis schlimii is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of conservation interest.
