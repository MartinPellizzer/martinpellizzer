Cirsium nishiokae

Cirsium nishiokae, commonly known as the Nishioka thistle, is a rare flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils and is found in specific regions of Honshu. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered.

Its botanical features make it an important subject for ecological and conservation studies.
