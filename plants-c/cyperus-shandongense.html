Cyperus shandongense

Cyperus shandongense, commonly known as the Shandong flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its morphology includes slender, triangular stems and small, grass-like leaves.

The inflorescence consists of compact clusters of flowers, which are often surrounded by bracts.
