Canna rubrolutea

Canna rubrolutea, commonly known as the red and yellow canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, which bloom from summer to early autumn. Canna rubrolutea thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

It is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and floral display in gardens and landscapes.
