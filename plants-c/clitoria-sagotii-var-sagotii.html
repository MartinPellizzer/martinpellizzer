Clitoria sagotii var. sagotii

Clitoria sagotii var. sagotii, commonly known as the winged bean, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant produces edible pods containing nutritious seeds, which are rich in protein and minerals. Its leaves are trifoliate and often used as a vegetable in various cuisines.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is commonly cultivated for both its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.
