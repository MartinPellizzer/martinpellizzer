Cybianthus densicoma

Cybianthus densicoma, commonly known as the densecomb lily, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing adaptations to its harsh habitat. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of botanical interest.

Cybianthus densicoma is also noted for its medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
