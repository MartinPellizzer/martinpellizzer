Combretum bongense

Combretum bongense, commonly known as African broom.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa. The plant features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

The plant is adapted to dry climates and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.
