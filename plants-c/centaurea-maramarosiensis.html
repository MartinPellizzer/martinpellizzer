Centaurea maramarosiensis

Centaurea maramarosiensis, commonly known as the Maramaros thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Maramaros region of Ukraine.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive purple-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features spiny, lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and rocky soils.

Centaurea maramarosiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
