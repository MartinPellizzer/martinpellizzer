Cardiospermum giganteum

Cardiospermum giganteum, commonly known as the giant spurred gentian, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue or purple flowers with distinctive spurred petals. The plant has palmate leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
