Carex uleana

Carex uleana, commonly known as the slender sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like leaves and spike-like flower clusters, typical of sedges. The plant typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and wet meadows. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands.

Carex uleana plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
