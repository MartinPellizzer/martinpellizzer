Crambe darwasica

Crambe darwasica, commonly known as the Darwasica crambe, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many economically important crops like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
