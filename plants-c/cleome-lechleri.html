Cleome lechleri

Cleome lechleri, commonly known as the "dragon's blood tree," is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, branching structure and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is notable for its latex-rich sap, which has a deep red color and has been used traditionally for medicinal purposes. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Cleome lechleri plays a significant ecological role in its native habitat, supporting various insect and bird species.
