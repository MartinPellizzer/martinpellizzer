Cynoctonum sessilifolium var. microphyllum

Cynoctonum sessilifolium var. microphyllum, commonly known as the dwarf pittosporum, is a compact shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy, with a dense, bushy growth habit. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in late spring, which are clustered in umbels. Its foliage remains evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
