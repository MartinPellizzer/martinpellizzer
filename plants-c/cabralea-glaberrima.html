Cabralea glaberrima

Cabralea glaberrima, commonly known as the silky cabralea, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and delicate, fragrant white flowers. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a velvety texture.

Cabralea glaberrima is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
