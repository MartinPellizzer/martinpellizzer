Calamagrostis kirishimensis

Calamagrostis kirishimensis, commonly known as the Japanese feather grass, is a perennial grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, arching blades that form a graceful, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage remains green throughout the growing season, with a soft, silky texture.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
