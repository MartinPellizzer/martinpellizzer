Cleome ornithopodioides f. daghestanica

Cleome ornithopodioides f. daghestanica, commonly known as the Daghestan cleome, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and showy, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soils. Its foliage consists of palmately lobed leaves, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.
