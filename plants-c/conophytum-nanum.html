Conophytum nanum

Conophytum nanum, commonly known as the dwarf conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its small, fleshy, paired leaves that form a compact rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Conophytum nanum is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
