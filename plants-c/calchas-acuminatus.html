Calchas acuminatus

Calchas acuminatus, commonly known as the sharp-tailed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spurred flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a deep red or purple lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Calchas acuminatus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
