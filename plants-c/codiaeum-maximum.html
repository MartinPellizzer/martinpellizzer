Codiaeum maximum

Codiaeum maximum, commonly known as the croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, yellow, orange, and green, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
