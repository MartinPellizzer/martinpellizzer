Citharexylum poeppigii var. calvescens

Citharexylum poeppigii var. calvescens, commonly known as the fragrant hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a fragrant scent. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
