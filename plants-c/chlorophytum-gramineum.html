Chlorophytum gramineum

Chlorophytum gramineum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with white stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stems, which can be grown indoors or outdoors in suitable climates. It is a hardy and low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and indirect sunlight.

Chlorophytum gramineum is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for houseplants.
