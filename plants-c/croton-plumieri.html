Croton plumieri

Croton plumieri, commonly known as the Jamaica croton, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically green with a distinctive reddish or purple underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton plumieri is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
