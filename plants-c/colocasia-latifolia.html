Colocasia latifolia

Colocasia latifolia, commonly known as the giant taro, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks. Its rhizomes are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.

The plant also has ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
