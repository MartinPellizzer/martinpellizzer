Clematis mauritiana var. sonneratii

Clematis mauritiana var. sonneratii, commonly known as the Mauritius clematis, is a climbing vine native to Madagascar.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, with five petals and a prominent central stamen. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
