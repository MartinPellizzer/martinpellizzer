Cotoneaster staintonii

Cotoneaster staintonii, commonly known as Stainton's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly serrated margin, and it flowers with clusters of white or pinkish flowers in the spring.

Cotoneaster staintonii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
