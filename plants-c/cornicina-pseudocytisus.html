Cornicina pseudocytisus

Cornicina pseudocytisus, commonly known as the false buckthorn, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dark green, oval-shaped leaves.
