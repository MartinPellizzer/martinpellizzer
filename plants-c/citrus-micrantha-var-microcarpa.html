Citrus micrantha var. microcarpa

Citrus micrantha var. microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf citron, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It produces small, round, green fruits that are typically less than 2 inches in diameter. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
