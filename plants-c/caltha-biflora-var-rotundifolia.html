Caltha biflora var. rotundifolia

Caltha biflora var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the roundedleaf celandine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow, cup-shaped flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, bright green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
