Cytisus supinus var. virescens

Cytisus supinus var. virescens, commonly known as the greenleaf broom, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright green leaves that are oval and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for garden use.
