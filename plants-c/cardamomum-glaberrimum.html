Cardamomum glaberrimum

Cardamomum glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth cardamom, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in spikes, which are followed by capsule fruits containing aromatic seeds. Cardamomum glaberrimum is valued for its fragrant seeds, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
