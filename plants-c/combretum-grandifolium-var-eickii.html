Combretum grandifolium var. eickii

Combretum grandifolium var. eickii, commonly known as the Eickii flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that appear in dense clusters during the dry season. The leaves are typically large, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This species thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant in warmer climates.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a notable addition to botanical gardens and landscapes.
