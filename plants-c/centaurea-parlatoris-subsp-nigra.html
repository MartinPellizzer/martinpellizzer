Centaurea parlatoris subsp. nigra

Centaurea parlatoris subsp.

nigra, commonly known as black centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright, branching stems that are typically covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces striking purple to violet flowers, which are surrounded by narrow, spiny bracts. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

This subspecies is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
