Crassula setigera

Crassula setigera, commonly known as the Ice Plant or Frost Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in arid conditions, showcasing its resilience as a member of the Crassulaceae family.
