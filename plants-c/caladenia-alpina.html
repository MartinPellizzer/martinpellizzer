Caladenia alpina

Caladenia alpina, commonly known as the Alpine Spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, intricate flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in alpine and subalpine regions. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive spider-like appearance.

Caladenia alpina is an ephemeral species, blooming only during the cooler months and often disappearing by early spring.
