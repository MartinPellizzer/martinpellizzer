Chamaecassia grandiflora

Chamaecassia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered chamaecassia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in soft, gray-green leaves. Its showy, yellow flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
