Cteisium

Cteisium, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, winged seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
