Combretum schweinfurthii

Combretum schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in dense clusters, making it a visually appealing plant. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental use in landscaping and traditional medicine.
