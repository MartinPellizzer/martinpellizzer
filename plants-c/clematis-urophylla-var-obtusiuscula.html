Clematis urophylla var. obtusiuscula

Clematis urophylla var. obtusiuscula, commonly known as the broadleaf clematis, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its woody vines and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a smooth texture. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to climb trellises or fences.
