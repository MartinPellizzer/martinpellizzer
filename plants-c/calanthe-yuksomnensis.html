Calanthe yuksomnensis

Calanthe yuksomnensis, commonly known as the Yuksom orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in the Himalayan region of India.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, lanceolate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest habitats at high altitudes. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with a distinctive yellow lip, and it blooms during the spring season.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
