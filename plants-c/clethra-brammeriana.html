Clethra brammeriana

Clethra brammeriana, commonly known as the Brammer clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Clethra brammeriana is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
