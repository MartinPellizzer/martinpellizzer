Cyclosorus pozoi, commonly known as the velvetleaf water fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

Cyclosorus pozoi is often found in slow-moving freshwater habitats, such as ponds and streams, where it plays a role in aquatic ecosystem dynamics.
