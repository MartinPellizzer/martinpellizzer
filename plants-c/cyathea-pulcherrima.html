Cyathea pulcherrima

Cyathea pulcherrima, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, feathery fronds that give it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant has a robust, unbranched trunk that is covered in a layer of fibrous roots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Cyathea pulcherrima is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
