Cistus × recognitus

Cistus × recognitus, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its leaves are leathery and aromatic, often releasing a strong scent when crushed.

Cistus × recognitus is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
