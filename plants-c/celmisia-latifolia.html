Celmisia latifolia

Celmisia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Celmisia latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
