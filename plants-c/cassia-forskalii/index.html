Cassia Forskalii

Cassia tree, scientifically known as Cassia forskalii, is a striking evergreen tree native to the arid regions of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula. It is prized for its vibrant golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a splash of color to its surroundings. The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood and its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine, highlighting its cultural and practical significance. With its adaptability and beauty, the Cassia forskalii remains an important species in both ecological and human contexts.
