Callisthene major var. major

Callisthene major var. major, commonly known as the broad-leafed bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized areas.
