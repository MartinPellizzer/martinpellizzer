Cheiranthus arenarius

Cheiranthus arenarius, commonly known as the sand rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that thrives in sandy and rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention.

It is often found in coastal and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
