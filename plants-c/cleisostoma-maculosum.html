Cleisostoma maculosum

Cleisostoma maculosum, commonly known as the spotted cleisostoma, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, creeping rhizome and produces small, clustered flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture.

Cleisostoma maculosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
