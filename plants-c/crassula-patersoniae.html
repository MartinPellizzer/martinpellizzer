Crassula patersoniae

Crassula patersoniae, commonly known as the Paterson's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant typically grows in a compact rosette form, with leaves that are often blue-green in color. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months.

Crassula patersoniae is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
