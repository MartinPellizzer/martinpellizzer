Cyphia stenopetala var. johannesburgensis

Cyphia stenopetala var. johannesburgensis, commonly known as the Johannesburg cyphia, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, elongated, and often recurved leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
