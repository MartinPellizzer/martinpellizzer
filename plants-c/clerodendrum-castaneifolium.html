Clerodendrum castaneifolium

Clerodendrum castaneifolium, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
