Cestrum ekmanii

Cestrum ekmanii, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly rough texture.
