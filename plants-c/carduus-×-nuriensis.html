Carduus × nuriensis

Carduus × nuriensis, commonly known as the Nuriensian thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its robust, spiny stems and large, lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
