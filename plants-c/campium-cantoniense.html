Campium cantoniense

Campium cantoniense, commonly known as the Guangdong senna, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shady areas and is used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and medicinal studies.
