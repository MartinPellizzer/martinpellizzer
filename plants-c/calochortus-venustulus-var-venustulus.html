Calochortus venustulus var. venustulus

Calochortus venustulus var. venustulus, commonly known as the showy glory-of-the-snow, is a perennial bulbous plant native to California.

It features striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and often have a yellow or orange center. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a basal rosette of foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
