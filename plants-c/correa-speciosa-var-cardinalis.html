Correa speciosa var. cardinalis

Correa speciosa var. cardinalis, commonly known as the Red Correa, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red to orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and aromatic. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract native pollinators.
