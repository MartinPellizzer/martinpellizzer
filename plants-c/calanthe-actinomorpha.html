Calanthe actinomorpha

Calanthe actinomorpha, commonly known as the showy calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce pseudobulbs and leaves. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

The botanical features of Calanthe actinomorpha make it a prized ornamental plant in horticulture.
