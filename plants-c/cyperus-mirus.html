Cyperus mirus

Cyperus mirus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often wind-pollinated. Cyperus mirus is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
