Croton sepalinus

Croton sepalinus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often brightly colored leaves that can vary in shape and texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Croton sepalinus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
