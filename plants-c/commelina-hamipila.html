Commelina hamipila

Commelina hamipila, commonly known as the slender commelina, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Commelinaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of China, India, and Southeast Asia. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces small, white or pale blue flowers with three petals. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Commelina hamipila thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
