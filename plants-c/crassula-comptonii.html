Crassula comptonii

Crassula comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Crassula comptonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
