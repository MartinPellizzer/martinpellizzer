Clerodendrum macrophyllum var. myrmecophilum

Clerodendrum macrophyllum var. myrmecophilum, commonly known as the ant-loving clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range from pink to purple. The plant is notable for its unique relationship with ants, which inhabit the specialized structures called domatia found on its stems. Its growth habit includes a climbing or trailing form, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises.

The botanical features of this plant make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
