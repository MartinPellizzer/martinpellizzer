Clerodendrum kalbreyeri

Clerodendrum kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's glorybower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive red throat. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom throughout the warm seasons. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Clerodendrum kalbreyeri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant floral display.
