Calanthe rubens

Calanthe rubens, commonly known as the red calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly colorful flowers, which typically feature a combination of red, pink, and white hues. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of showy, fragrant blooms. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Calanthe rubens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
