Convolvulus bourgaei

Convolvulus bourgaei, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant blue to purple flowers, which bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and twining stems that climb or trail along the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
