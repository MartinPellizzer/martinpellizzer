Cardamine greatrexii

Cardamine greatrexii, commonly known as the Greatrex watercress, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of western Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland floors. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often submerged or partially submerged in water. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in a raceme, and it produces elliptical, dark brown seeds.

Cardamine greatrexii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local biodiversity in wetland ecosystems.
