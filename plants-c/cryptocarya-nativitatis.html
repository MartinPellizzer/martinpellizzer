Cryptocarya nativitatis

Cryptocarya nativitatis, commonly known as the New Zealand native laurel.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae, native to New Zealand. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically glossy and oval in shape. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by dark blue berries.
