Chlorophytum nigricans

Chlorophytum nigricans, commonly known as the black sword plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. Chlorophytum nigricans is often used as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
