Caladium mooreanum

Caladium mooreanum, commonly known as the Moorean arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium mooreanum thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
