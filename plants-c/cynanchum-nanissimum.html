Cynanchum nanissimum

Cynanchum nanissimum, commonly known as Chinese milkweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly heart-shaped base.

The plant contains milky sap, which is characteristic of the Asclepiadaceae family, and is often used in traditional medicine.
