Combretum flammeum

Combretum flammeum, commonly known as the flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red, papery bracts that give it its striking appearance, especially during the flowering season. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and rocky areas.

Combretum flammeum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly used in landscaping due to its vibrant color and hardy nature.
