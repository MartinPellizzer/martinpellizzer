Calceolaria lorentziana

Calceolaria lorentziana, commonly known as the Lorentziana slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting veins. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Calceolaria lorentziana is valued for its unique floral structure and vibrant hues, making it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
