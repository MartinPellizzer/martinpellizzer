Chenopodium × perhybridum

Chenopodium × perhybridum, commonly known as hybrid goosefoot, is a cross between several species of the Chenopodium genus.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its broad, succulent leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are typically small and wind-dispersed. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in agricultural areas and along roadsides.

Due to its adaptability, it can become invasive in certain environments.
