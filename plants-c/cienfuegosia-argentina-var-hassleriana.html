Cienfuegosia argentina var. hassleriana

Cienfuegosia argentina var. hassleriana, commonly known as the Argentine cienfuegosia, is a species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking visual contrast to its delicate blossoms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cooler, humid climates.
