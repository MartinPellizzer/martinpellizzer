Cotoneaster acutifolius sinensis

Cotoneaster acutifolius sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the autumn. These berries are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
