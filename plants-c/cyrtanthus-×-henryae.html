Cyrtanthus × henryae

Cyrtanthus × henryae, commonly known as the Henry’s crown iris, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring, featuring vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant is native to South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall stem with a cluster of flowers at the top.

Cyrtanthus × henryae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for with proper sunlight and drainage.
