Cardaminopsis arenosa var. multijuga

Cardaminopsis arenosa var. multijuga, commonly known as the sandstone buttercup, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small yellow flowers. This species is adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often found in mountainous regions. It produces capsules containing numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
