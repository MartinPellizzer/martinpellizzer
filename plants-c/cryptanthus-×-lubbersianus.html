Cryptanthus × lubbersianus

Cryptanthus × lubbersianus, commonly known as the Lubb's carpet plant, is a hybrid species native to South America.

It belongs to the amaranth family (Amaranthaceae) and is characterized by its dense, low-growing rosettes of fleshy, colorful leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne in clusters at the base of the rosette. Its foliage exhibits a wide range of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and green, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Cryptanthus × lubbersianus thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.
