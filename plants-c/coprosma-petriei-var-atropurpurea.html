Coprosma petriei var. atropurpurea

Coprosma petriei var. atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple coprosma, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark purple to black stems. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a glossy, dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that persist into winter.

This plant is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
