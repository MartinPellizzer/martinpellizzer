Campanula aucheri var. compacta

Campanula aucheri var. compacta, commonly known as the compact bellflower, is a compact, flowering perennial native to Europe.

It features dense clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for small spaces and container gardening.
