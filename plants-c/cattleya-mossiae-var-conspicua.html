Cattleya mossiae var. conspicua

Cattleya mossiae var. conspicua, commonly known as the showy Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It features large, pendulous blooms with bright orange petals and a contrasting white lip, making it a striking ornamental plant. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. This variety is known for its ease of cultivation and ability to rebloom regularly under proper care.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid that showcases the diversity within the Cattleya genus.
