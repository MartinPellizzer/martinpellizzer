Ceratostylis eriaeoides

Ceratostylis eriaeoides, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Ceratostylis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Indonesia. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive, elongated spur.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
