Caprifolium javanicum

Caprifolium javanicum, commonly known as the Java honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces small, fragrant flowers with four petals. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
