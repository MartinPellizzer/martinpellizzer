Cytisus austriacus var. pindicola

Cytisus austriacus var. pindicola, commonly known as the Pindus broom, is a shrub native to the Balkan region.

It features slender, wiry stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are small, alternate, and trifoliate, with a pale green color.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to rocky or mountainous environments.
