Chionogentias demissa

Chionogentias demissa, commonly known as the silver-haired bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its leaves are narrow and silver-haired, giving it a distinctive appearance.
