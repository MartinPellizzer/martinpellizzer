Chrysanthemum spathulatum

Chrysanthemum spathulatum, commonly known as the spoonleaf chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, spoon-shaped leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
