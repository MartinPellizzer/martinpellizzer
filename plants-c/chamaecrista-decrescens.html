Chamaecrista decrescens

Chamaecrista decrescens, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces clusters of yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a recognizable species within the Fabaceae family.
