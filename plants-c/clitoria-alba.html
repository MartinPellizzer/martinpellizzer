Clitoria alba

Clitoria alba, commonly known as the white pea or sacred bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a climbing legume characterized by its delicate, white, butterfly-like flowers. The plant produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds. Clitoria alba is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive blooms.

It is also used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
