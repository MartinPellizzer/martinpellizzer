Ceropegia merrilliana

Ceropegia merrilliana, commonly known as the Christmas star or star vine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Ceropegia merrilliana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
