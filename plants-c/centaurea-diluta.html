Centaurea diluta

Centaurea diluta, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive bracts. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with prominent central disk flowers surrounded by ray florets.

Centaurea diluta is often used in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
