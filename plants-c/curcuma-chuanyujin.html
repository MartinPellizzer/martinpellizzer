Curcuma chuanyujin

Curcuma chuanyujin, commonly known as the Yunnan turmeric, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and other tropical plants. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and lanceolate, dark green leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

The rhizomes of Curcuma chuanyujin are used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
