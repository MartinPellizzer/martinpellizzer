Centaurea koeieana subsp. koeieana

Centaurea koeieana subsp.

koeieana, commonly known as the Koeie's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features striking blue to violet flowers arranged in a conical shape, with bracts that are often reddish or purplish. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and it produces small, elongated achenes as its seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
