Crepidomanes Walleri

Crepidomanes walleri is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is known for its unique, small, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to habitat loss, it faces threats to its survival and is considered vulnerable. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
