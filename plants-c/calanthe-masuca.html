Calanthe masuca

Calanthe masuca, commonly known as the Japanese calanthe, is a species of orchid native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in shaded, moist forest environments. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Calanthe masuca prefers cool temperatures and high humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
