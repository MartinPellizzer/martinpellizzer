Calogyne chinensis

Calogyne chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese star orchid, is a rare and ornamental plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted for absorbing moisture from the air.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
