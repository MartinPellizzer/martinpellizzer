Cirsium japonicum var. okiense

Cirsium japonicum var. okiense, commonly known as the Okinawan thistle, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features robust, spiny stems and dark green, lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces purple to pinkish flowers in a spherical shape, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassy areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
