Claoxylon marianum

Claoxylon marianum, commonly known as the Marianum Ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and evergreen foliage. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 30 meters. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing structural support and protection.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
