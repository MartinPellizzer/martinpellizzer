Caladenia flavovirens

Caladenia flavovirens, commonly known as the Yellow-green Spider-orchid, is an exquisite member of the orchid family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and strikingly colored flowers that range from yellow to greenish hues. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing delicate, spider-like flowers with a single, prominent lip. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and woodlands.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
