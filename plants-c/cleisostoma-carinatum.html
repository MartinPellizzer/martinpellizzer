Cleisostoma carinatum

Cleisostoma carinatum, commonly known as the ridge-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves that have a prominent midrib, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cleisostoma carinatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
