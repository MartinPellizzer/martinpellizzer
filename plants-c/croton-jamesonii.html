Croton jamesonii

Croton jamesonii, commonly known as the Jameson's croton, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically followed by woody capsules containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
