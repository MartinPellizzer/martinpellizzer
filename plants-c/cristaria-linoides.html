Cristaria linoides

Cristaria linoides, commonly known as the slender water plantain, is a aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and wetlands. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often submerged or floating. Its flowers are small and clustered, with three petals and three sepals.

Cristaria linoides plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
