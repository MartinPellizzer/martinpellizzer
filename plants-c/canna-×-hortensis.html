Canna × hortensis

Canna × hortensis, commonly known as the ornamental canna, is a hybrid plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy leaves and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with broad, palmately lobed leaves that are a striking feature. Its flowers are usually pink, red, or yellow, and they bloom in summer.

Canna × hortensis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes due to its vibrant appearance and ease of care.
