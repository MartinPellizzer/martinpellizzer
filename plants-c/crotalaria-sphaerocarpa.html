Crotalaria sphaerocarpa

Crotalaria sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spherical seed pods. The plant produces yellow flowers and has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a forage crop.

The seeds are edible and can be used in various culinary applications.
