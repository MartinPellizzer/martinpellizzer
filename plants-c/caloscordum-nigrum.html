Caloscordum nigrum

Caloscordum nigrum, commonly known as black nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are toxic to humans and animals. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Caloscordum nigrum is also known for its ornamental value due to its striking flowers and berries.
