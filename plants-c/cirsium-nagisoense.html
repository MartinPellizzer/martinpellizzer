Cirsium nagisoense

Cirsium nagisoense, commonly known as the Nagiso thistle, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland areas and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are covered in stiff hairs and have a distinctive, often serrated appearance.

Cirsium nagisoense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
