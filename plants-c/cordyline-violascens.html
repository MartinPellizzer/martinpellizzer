Cordyline violascens

Cordyline violascens, commonly known as the purple passion plant, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cordyline violascens is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
