Croton scaber

Croton scaber, commonly known as rough croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, textured leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody, spiny capsules containing numerous seeds. Croton scaber is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage.

It prefers warm, humid climates and well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.
