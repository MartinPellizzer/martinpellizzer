Carex praealta

Carex praealta, commonly known as tall sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. This plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and pond edges, where it thrives in moist to aquatic conditions. Carex praealta has tall, narrow leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often green or reddish in color.

It plays an important ecological role by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
