Chrysosplenium uniflorum

Chrysosplenium uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flowered chrysosplenium, is a member of the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and dense, mat-forming foliage. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with five petals. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

Native to temperate regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.
