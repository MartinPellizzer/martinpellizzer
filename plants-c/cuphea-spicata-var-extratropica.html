Cuphea spicata var. extratropica

Cuphea spicata var. extratropica, commonly known as the tropical cuphea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features striking, tubular red bracts that surround its small, yellowish flowers, giving it a vibrant appearance. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces showy inflorescences that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its eye-catching foliage and flowers.
