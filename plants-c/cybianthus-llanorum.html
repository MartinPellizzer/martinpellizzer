Cybianthus llanorum

Cybianthus llanorum, commonly known as the Llanos candleplant, is a flowering plant native to the grasslands of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, candle-like yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments. Its botanical features include narrow leaves and a lack of branches, giving it a distinctive, elongated appearance.

Cybianthus llanorum is often found in open habitats such as savannas and grasslands.
