Centaurea × ephelidea

Centaurea × ephelidea, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets, creating a composite appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
