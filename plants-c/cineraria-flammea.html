Cineraria flammea

Cineraria flammea, commonly known as the flame cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange-red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are usually hairy and grayish-green, providing a contrasting texture to its vibrant blooms.

Cineraria flammea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its eye-catching appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
