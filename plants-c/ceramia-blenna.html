Ceramia blenna

Ceramia blenna, commonly known as the blenna lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet petals and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Ceramia blenna is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
