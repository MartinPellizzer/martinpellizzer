Crassula ihlenfeldtii

Crassula ihlenfeldtii, commonly known as the Ilenfeldt's Crassula, is a small, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
