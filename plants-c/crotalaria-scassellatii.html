Crotalaria scassellatii

Crotalaria scassellatii, commonly known as the African tephrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant characterized by its erect stem and alternate leaves. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its seeds are enclosed in pod-like fruits, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Crotalaria scassellatii is also noted for its nitrogen-fixing properties, making it valuable in agricultural systems.
