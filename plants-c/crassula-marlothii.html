Crassula marlothii

Crassula marlothii, commonly known as the Blue Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula marlothii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and aesthetic appeal.
