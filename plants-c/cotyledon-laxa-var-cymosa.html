Cotyledon laxa var. cymosa

Cotyledon laxa var. cymosa, commonly known as the clustered cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
