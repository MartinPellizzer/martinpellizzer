Cassebeeria tomentella

Cassebeeria tomentella, commonly known as the woolly cassie, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in rainforest understory environments.
