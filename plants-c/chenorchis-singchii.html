Chenorchis singchii

Chenorchis singchii, commonly known as the Yunnan orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rocky soil, and it plays a significant role in the local ecosystem.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Chenorchis singchii is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation programs.
