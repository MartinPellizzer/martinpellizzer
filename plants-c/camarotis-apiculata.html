Camarotis apiculata

Camarotis apiculata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Camarotis apiculata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
