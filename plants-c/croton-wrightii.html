Croton wrightii

Croton wrightii, commonly known as Wright's croton, is a species of flowering plant in the spurge family, Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and parts of Georgia. Croton wrightii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
