Cymbopogon vanderystii

Cymbopogon vanderystii, commonly known as Vanderyst's grass, is a perennial grass species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
