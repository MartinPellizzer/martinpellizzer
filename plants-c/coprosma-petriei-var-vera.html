Coprosma petriei var. vera

Coprosma petriei var. vera, commonly known as the Kauri Coprosma, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland forest habitats. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. Its small, bright red berries are an important food source for native birds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in New Zealand's ecosystems.
