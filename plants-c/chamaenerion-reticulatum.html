Chamaenerion reticulatum

Chamaenerion reticulatum, commonly known as the redstem fireweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems that are often reddish in color, especially when mature. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Chamaenerion reticulatum is adapted to cold climates and can thrive in poor, well-drained soils.
