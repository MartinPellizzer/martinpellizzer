Cotyledon × fergusoniae

Cotyledon × fergusoniae, commonly known as the hybrid succulent, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Cotyledon tomentosa and Cotyledon orbiculata.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues, especially under stress or in direct sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts and indoor gardening.
