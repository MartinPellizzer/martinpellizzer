Cuprespinnata

Cuprespinnata, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive reddish hue. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Cuprespinnata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
