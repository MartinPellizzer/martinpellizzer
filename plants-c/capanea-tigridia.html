Capanea tigridia

Capanea tigridia, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that resemble tiger stripes, hence its common name. Capanea tigridia typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments.

Its unique floral structure and striking appearance make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and enthusiasts.
