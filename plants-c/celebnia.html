Celebnia

Celebnia, commonly known as the "scented geranium," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves that emit a strong, spicy scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub with lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pink or white flowers. Celebnia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in aromatherapy due to its essential oils.

Its adaptability to various climates makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
