Caryota onusta

Caryota onusta, commonly known as the giant palm or Chinese windmill palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant has a slender, unbranched trunk that is often covered in a fibrous sheath. Its inflorescences are large and produce both male and female flowers, which are typically clustered in pendulous clusters.

Caryota onusta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
