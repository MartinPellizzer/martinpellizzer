Cacalia lycopersicum

Cacalia lycopersicum, commonly known as the tomato plant, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which also includes potatoes and peppers. The plant produces clusters of flowers that develop into bright red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

The tomato plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
