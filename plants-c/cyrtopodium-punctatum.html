Cyrtopodium punctatum

Cyrtopodium punctatum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with distinctive red or purple spots, adding to its ornamental value.

Cyrtopodium punctatum is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
