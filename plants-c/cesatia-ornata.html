Cesatia ornata

Cesatia ornata, commonly known as the ornate cesatia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with woody stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are arranged in dense clusters, often blooming in late spring to early summer.

Cesatia ornata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
