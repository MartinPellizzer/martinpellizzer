Calochortus amabilis

Calochortus amabilis, commonly known as the showy onion, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Calochortus.

It is native to California and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and chaparral. It produces bulbous roots and has narrow, grass-like leaves.

Calochortus amabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms.
