Callicarpa rubella f. crenata

Callicarpa rubella f. crenata, commonly known as the red beauty honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are slightly wavy along the edges. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red berries that are highly ornamental and attract birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable choice for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant fruit make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
