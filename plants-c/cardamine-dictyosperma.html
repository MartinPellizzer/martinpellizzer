Cardamine dictyosperma

Cardamine dictyosperma, commonly known as the fernleaf watercress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically alternate and finely divided. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, followed by small, round pods containing several seeds.

Cardamine dictyosperma thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and woodland areas.
