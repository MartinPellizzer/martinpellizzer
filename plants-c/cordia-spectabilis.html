Cordia spectabilis

Cordia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy cordia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically orange or red when ripe, containing several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Cordia spectabilis is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
