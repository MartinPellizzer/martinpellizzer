Crassula micans

Crassula micans, commonly known as the glittering jade or the silver jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Crassula micans thrives in bright, indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.
