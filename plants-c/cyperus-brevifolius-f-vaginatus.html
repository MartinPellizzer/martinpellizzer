Cyperus brevifolius f. vaginatus

Cyperus brevifolius f. vaginatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flatsedge, is a variant of the Cyperus brevifolius species.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae, commonly found in wetland and marshy environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, three-ranked flowers.

This subspecies is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability to wet conditions.
