Citrus sabon f. mitsuyu

Citrus sabon f. mitsuyu, commonly known as the Mitsuyu citrus, is a rare and unique citrus variety native to Japan.

It is characterized by its small, round fruits with a thick, waxy rind that is often used in traditional Japanese crafts. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, orange-colored fruits. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental qualities and its use in local culinary and aromatic applications.
