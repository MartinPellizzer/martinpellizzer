Crucihimalaya ovczinnikovii

Crucihimalaya ovczinnikovii, commonly known as the Himalayan crucifix, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many familiar plants such as cabbages and mustards. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble a crucifix, hence its common name. It typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions.
