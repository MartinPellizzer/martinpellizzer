Cyperus schottii

Cyperus schottii, commonly known as the swamp cyperus, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to aquatic conditions. The plant has slender, grass-like stems with whorled leaves that are often submerged or floating. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, three-stamen flowers, which are typically greenish in color.

Cyperus schottii plays a role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to nutrient cycling.
