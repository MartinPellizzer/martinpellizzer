Cowania davidsonii

Cowania davidsonii, commonly known as the Davidson's cowania, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, yellow flowers on short stalks during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Cowania davidsonii is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in dry climates.
