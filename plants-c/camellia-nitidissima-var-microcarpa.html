Camellia nitidissima var. microcarpa

Camellia nitidissima var. microcarpa, commonly known as the miniature camellia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy, camellia-like flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of climates.

Its small size and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
