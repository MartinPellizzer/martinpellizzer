Citrus × aurantium var. limetiformis

Citrus × aurantium var. limetiformis, commonly known as the sour orange.

This variety is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its small, round, and typically green or yellowish fruit. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and oval in shape, with a slightly serrated edge. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The fruit is known for its high acidity and is often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
