Cynoglossum pauciflorum

Cynoglossum pauciflorum, commonly known as narrowleaf mouseear, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Cynoglossum pauciflorum is a perennial herb that is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
