Caltha Laeta Subsp. Alpestris

Caltha laeta subsp. alpestris is a striking alpine plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers. It thrives in high-altitude environments where few species can survive. This subspecies is often found in mountain meadows and rocky slopes across Europe. Its delicate petals and unique growth habits make it a subject of botanical interest. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare and vulnerable to environmental changes.
