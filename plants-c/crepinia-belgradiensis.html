Crepinia belgradiensis

Crepinia belgradiensis, commonly known as the Belgradiensis sedge, is a flowering plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flower heads.
