Coreopsis schaffneri

Coreopsis schaffneri, commonly known as Schaffner's coreopsis, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soils.

Coreopsis schaffneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
