Crotalaria medicaginea var. rigida

Crotalaria medicaginea var. rigida, commonly known as rigid spurred crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are arranged in racemes, and its seeds are enclosed in hairy pods.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields, where it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.
