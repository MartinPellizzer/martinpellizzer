Croton macrostachyus var. mollissimus

Croton macrostachyus var. mollissimus, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green with a soft, fuzzy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
