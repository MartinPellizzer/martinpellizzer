Chlorophytum velutinum

Chlorophytum velutinum, commonly known as the Velvet Leaf Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, grass-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silky coating, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
