Conophytum angelicae subsp. angelicae

Conophytum angelicae subsp.

angelicae, commonly known as the angel's tears or angel's tears cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
