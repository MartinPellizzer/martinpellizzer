Cyperus parvinux

Cyperus parvinux, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas. The plant has slender, grass-like stems and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Cyperus parvinux is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds and streams.
