Cerasus Glabra

Cerasus glabra is a lesser-known species of cherry tree native to eastern Asia. It is known for its delicate pink flowers and small, sweet fruits. This plant thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated compared to more common cherry varieties.
