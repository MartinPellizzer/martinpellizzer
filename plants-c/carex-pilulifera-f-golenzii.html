Carex pilulifera f. golenzii

Carex pilulifera f. golenzii, commonly known as the Golenzio sedge, is a variant of the Carex pilulifera species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance. The plant features dense, cylindrical clusters of spikelets, which are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments.
