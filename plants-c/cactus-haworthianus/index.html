Cactus Haworthianus

Cactus haworthianus is a unique and resilient plant native to the arid regions of Mexico. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is known for its compact, globular shape and spiny appearance. This species thrives in dry, well-drained soil and requires minimal water to survive. Its small, tubular flowers bloom in shades of yellow and orange, adding a touch of beauty to its rugged form. Despite its tough exterior, Cactus haworthianus is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners who appreciate low-maintenance, hardy plants.
