Cotoneaster multiflorus

Cotoneaster multiflorus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with 5 to 9 leaflets, and it produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in the spring. The plant bears red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Cotoneaster multiflorus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
