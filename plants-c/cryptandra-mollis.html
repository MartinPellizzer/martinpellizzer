Cryptandra mollis

Cryptandra mollis, commonly known as the slender cryptandra, is a shrub native to the southwestern region of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in winter. The plant is adapted to arid conditions, with a deep root system that helps it survive in dry environments. It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils along coastal areas.

Cryptandra mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
