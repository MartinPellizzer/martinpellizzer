Croton albellus

Croton albellus, commonly known as the silver croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
