Ceratostylis tricallifera

Ceratostylis tricallifera, commonly known as the narrow-leafed star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
