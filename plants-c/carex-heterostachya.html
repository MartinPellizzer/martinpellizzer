Carex heterostachya

Carex heterostachya, commonly known as the slender club-moss sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like leaves and spike-like inflorescences. Native to various regions of Asia, including China and Japan, it typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and wetlands. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wet soil conditions.
