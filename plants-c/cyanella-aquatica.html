Cyanella aquatica

Cyanella aquatica, commonly known as water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade and oxygen to the water. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, with a distinctive spiral arrangement of petals. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Cyanella aquatica plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by supporting aquatic life and improving water quality.
