Camellia yunnanensis var. trichocarpa

Camellia yunnanensis var. trichocarpa, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and striking, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant is typically found in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. Its flowers bloom in winter, making it a notable ornamental plant.

It is also valued for its potential in the tea industry due to its caffeine content.
