Carex zunyiensis

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, where it grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. It typically reaches a height of about 30 to 50 centimeters and has a rhizomatous growth habit.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Carex zunyiensis is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
