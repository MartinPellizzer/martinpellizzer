Callistemon rugulosus var. laevifolius

Callistemon rugulosus var. laevifolius, commonly known as the smooth-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or pink bottlebrush flowers that bloom in winter and spring. The leaves are narrow, smooth, and often have a bluish-green hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract birds and insects.
