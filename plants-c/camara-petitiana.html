Camara petitiana

Camara petitiana, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
