Croton peekelii

Croton peekelii, commonly known as the Peckel's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that display a variety of hues including red, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its milky sap and diverse species. Croton peekelii typically grows in humid, shaded environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
