Croton glandulosus var. divaricatus

Croton glandulosus var. divaricatus, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a tropical plant characterized by its distinctive, colorful foliage.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a striking combination of green, yellow, and red hues. The plant is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
