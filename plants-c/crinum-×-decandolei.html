Crinum × decandolei

Crinum × decandolei, commonly known as the hybrid Crinum luteum × Crinum bulbisacrum, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense, terminal clusters. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

Crinum × decandolei is also known for its strong fragrance, which can be both pleasant and overwhelming.
