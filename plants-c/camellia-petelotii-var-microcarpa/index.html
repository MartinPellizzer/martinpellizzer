Camellia Petelotii Var. Microcarpa

Camellia petelotii var. microcarpa is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its striking, deep red flowers that bloom in late winter and early spring. This variant is distinguished by its smaller, more compact leaves compared to other Camellia species. It thrives in cool, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, it is a prized specimen among plant enthusiasts and botanists alike.
