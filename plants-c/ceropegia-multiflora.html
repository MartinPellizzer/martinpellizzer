Ceropegia multiflora

Ceropegia multiflora, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, attracting specific pollinators like moths. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is popular as a houseplant and in hanging baskets.
