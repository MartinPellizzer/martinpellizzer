Cocculus orbiculatus var. mollis

Cocculus orbiculatus var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-leafed morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped, smooth, and often hairy leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, blooming in the morning. Its fruit is a spiny capsule that splits open to release its seeds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and floral display.
