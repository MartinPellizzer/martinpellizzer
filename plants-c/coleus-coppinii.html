Coleus coppinii

Coleus coppinii, commonly known as the Cape Verdean coleus, is a tropical shrub native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from deep red to bright orange and yellow. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Its foliage is often used in landscaping for its striking visual appeal.
