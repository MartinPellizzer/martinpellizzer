Cattleya mossiae var. marmorata

Cattleya mossiae var. marmorata, commonly known as the marbled cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, intricate patterns.

It features large, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a metallic sheen. The flowers are typically white with deep purple and maroon markings, creating a marbled effect. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
