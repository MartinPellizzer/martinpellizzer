Calendula nudicaulis

Calendula nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender calendula, is a small flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly rough texture.

Calendula nudicaulis is a hardy perennial that is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with well-drained soil.
