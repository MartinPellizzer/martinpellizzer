Chironia katangensis

Chironia katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga cypress, is a member of the Cupressaceae family.

It is a coniferous tree native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo, typically found in moist, well-drained soils. The plant has scale-like leaves arranged in opposite pairs along the branches. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in local construction and crafts.
