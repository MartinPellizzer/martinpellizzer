Croton chelsonii

Croton chelsonii, commonly known as the yellow croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy yellow and orange leaves, which are often variegated. It is a shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, with smooth, succulent stems.

Croton chelsonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
