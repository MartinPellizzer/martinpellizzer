Crassula setulosa var. setulosa

Crassula setulosa var. setulosa, commonly known as the woolly crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fuzzy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
