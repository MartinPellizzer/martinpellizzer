Campanula bulgarica

Campanula bulgarica, commonly known as the Bulgarian bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan region.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Campanula bulgarica is valued in gardens for its attractive blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
