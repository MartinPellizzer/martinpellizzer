Caiophora tomentosula

Caiophora tomentosula, commonly known as the woolly caiophora, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and plant enthusiasts.
