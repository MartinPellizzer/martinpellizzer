Craniospermum gubanovii

Craniospermum gubanovii, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, fern-like foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance and fragrant properties make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it is important to note that it can be invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
