Cyathula uncinulata var. pleiocephala

Cyathula uncinulata var. pleiocephala, commonly known as the broad-headed spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, clustered inflorescence known as a cyathium. The plant features yellowish-green flowers surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has a sprawling growth habit.
