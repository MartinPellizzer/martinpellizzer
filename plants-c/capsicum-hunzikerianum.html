Capsicum hunzikerianum

Capsicum hunzikerianum, commonly known as the Hunziker's chili, is a species of capsicum native to South America.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that range in color from green to red when mature. Its leaves are ovate with a serrated margin and are covered in fine pubescence.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and mild to spicy flavor, making it suitable for both culinary and horticultural use.
