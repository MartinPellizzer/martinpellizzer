Crucianella latifolia subvar. monspeliaca

Crucianella latifolia subvar. monspeliaca, commonly known as the broad-leaved crucianella, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which are often borne on short pedicels. It is native to regions of southern Europe and is commonly found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

The subspecies is distinguished by its broader leaves compared to other varieties within the species.
