Cernohorskya kotschyana

Cernohorskya kotschyana, commonly known as the Kashmir snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

Cernohorskya kotschyana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
