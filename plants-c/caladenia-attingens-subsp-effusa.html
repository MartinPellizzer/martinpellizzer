Caladenia attingens subsp. effusa

Caladenia attingens subsp.

effusa, commonly known as the Western River Spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Western Australia. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along riverbanks and open woodlands.

The plant features a single, slender stem with a single flower that is usually pale yellow to cream in color. The dorsal sepal and petals are narrowly lance-shaped, while the labellum is distinctive with a broad, hood-like structure.

It is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific environmental conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
