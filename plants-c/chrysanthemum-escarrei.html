Chrysanthemum escarrei

Chrysanthemum escarrei, commonly known as the Escarre's chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale purple petals. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in sunny environments. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Chrysanthemum escarrei is also noted for its adaptability to coastal conditions.
