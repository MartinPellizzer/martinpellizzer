Crossandra rupestris

Crossandra rupestris, commonly known as the rock crossandra, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing or ascending stems and vibrant, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange-red. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
