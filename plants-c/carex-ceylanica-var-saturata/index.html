Carex Ceylanica Var. Saturata

Carex ceylanica var. saturata is a striking sedge species native to southern Asia, known for its vibrant, bluish-green foliage. This variety stands out for its dense, grass-like appearance and its ability to thrive in moist, shady environments. It is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and forest floors where it contributes to ecological stability. The plant's unique coloration and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal have led to increased interest in its cultivation and conservation.
