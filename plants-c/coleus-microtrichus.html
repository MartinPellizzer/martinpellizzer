Coleus microtrichus

Coleus microtrichus, commonly known as the dwarf coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and display a wide range of colors, including red, purple, and green. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale pink.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
