Chaenorhinum rubrifolium subsp. rubrifolium

Chaenorhinum rubrifolium subsp.

rubrifolium, commonly known as the red-leaf chaenorhinum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive reddish tinge, especially on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
