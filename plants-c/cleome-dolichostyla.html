Cleome dolichostyla

Cleome dolichostyla, commonly known as the tall cleome, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its branching stems and large, showy flowers. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with five petals and a long, narrow corolla. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
