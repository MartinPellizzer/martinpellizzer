Croton isertii

Croton isertii, commonly known as the African croton, is a tropical shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from green to red, orange, and yellow, often with distinct veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton isertii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
