Cassia mimosoides var. telfairiana

Cassia mimosoides var. telfairiana, commonly known as Telfair's cassia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves and clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas or disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
