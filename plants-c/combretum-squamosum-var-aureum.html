Combretum squamosum var. aureum

Combretum squamosum var. aureum, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the rainy season. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
