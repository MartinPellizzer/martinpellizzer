Convolvulus chinensis

Convolvulus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese morning glory, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of purple and pink. The plant is a trailing vine that produces showy, five-petaled blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often found in gardens and wild areas.

Its seeds are encased in a distinctive, spiny capsule that aids in seed dispersal.
