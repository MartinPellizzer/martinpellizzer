Campanula Setacea

Campanula setacea, known as the silky bellflower, is a captivating member of the Campanulaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its delicate, bell-shaped flowers range in color from white to pale blue, adding a touch of elegance to alpine landscapes. The plant's hairy leaves and stems give it a soft, silky appearance that distinguishes it from other bellflowers. Often cultivated for its ornamental value, Campanula setacea is a favorite among gardeners seeking to add a touch of wild beauty to their gardens.
