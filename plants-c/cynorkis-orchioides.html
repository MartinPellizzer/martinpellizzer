Cynorkis orchioides

Cynorkis orchioides, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, intricate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant prefers moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.
