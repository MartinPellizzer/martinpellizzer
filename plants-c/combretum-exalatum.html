Combretum exalatum

Combretum exalatum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves that are often arranged in whorls, and its bark is smooth and reddish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
