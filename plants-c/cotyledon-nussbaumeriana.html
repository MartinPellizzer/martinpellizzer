Cotyledon nussbaumeriana

Cotyledon nussbaumeriana, commonly known as the Nussbaumer's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and purple. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Cotyledon nussbaumeriana is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
