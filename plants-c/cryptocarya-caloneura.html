Cryptocarya caloneura

Cryptocarya caloneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cryptocarya, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a dark blue drupe, often containing a single seed.

Cryptocarya caloneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
