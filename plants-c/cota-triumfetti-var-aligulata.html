Cota triumfetti var. aligulata

Cota triumfetti var. aligulata, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
