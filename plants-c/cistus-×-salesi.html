Cistus × salesi

Cistus × salesi, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments.

Cistus × salesi is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
