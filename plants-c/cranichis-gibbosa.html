Cranichis gibbosa

Cranichis gibbosa, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with distinctive spurred petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as pine flatwoods and wetlands.

Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
