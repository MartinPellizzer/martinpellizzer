Cnicus hawaiensis

Cnicus hawaiensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy yellow flowers. This species typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It plays an important role in the native plant communities of Hawaii.

However, it is considered endangered due to habitat loss and invasive species competition.
