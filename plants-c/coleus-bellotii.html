Coleus bellotii

Coleus bellotii, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is native to tropical Africa and is a member of the Gesneriaceae family. It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can vary in size depending on the cultivar. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
