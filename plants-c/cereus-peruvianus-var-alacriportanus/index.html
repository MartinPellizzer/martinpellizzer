Cereus Peruvianus Var. Alacriportanus

Cereus peruvianus var. alacriportanus is a striking cactus native to the arid regions of South America. It is known for its tall, spiny stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom at night. This variety is a popular choice for collectors due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements. Its ability to thrive in dry conditions makes it well-suited for desert gardens and indoor environments. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its sharp spines and toxic sap.
