Cicerbita canadensis var. integrifolia

Cicerbita canadensis var. integrifolia, commonly known as Canada prickly lettuce, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features a rosette of broadly lance-shaped, hairy leaves with wavy margins. The plant produces yellow flowers in a composite head, followed by spiny, ribbed fruits. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
