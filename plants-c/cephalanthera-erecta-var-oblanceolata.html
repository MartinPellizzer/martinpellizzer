Cephalanthera erecta var. oblanceolata

Cephalanthera erecta var. oblanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lady's slipper, is a striking orchid native to parts of Asia.

It features a single, showy pink or purple flower with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is noted for its elegant appearance and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities.
