Chenopodium villosum

Chenopodium villosum, commonly known as hairy goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features alternate, ovate leaves covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery-gray appearance. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, followed by triangular, beak-like fruits. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Chenopodium villosum is a member of the Amaranthaceae family, which includes many edible and ornamental plants.
