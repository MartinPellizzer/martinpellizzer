Cereus mendory

Cereus mendory, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a flowering cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and clusters of showy, night-blooming flowers. The plant thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers are typically white or pink and emit a sweet fragrance.

Cereus mendory is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to bloom under proper care.
