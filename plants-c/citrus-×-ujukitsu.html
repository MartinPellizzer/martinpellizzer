Citrus × ujukitsu

Citrus × ujukitsu, commonly known as the yuzu.

This hybrid citrus plant is native to Japan and is a cross between Citrus jambhiri and Citrus nobilis. It produces small, aromatic fruits with a thick, yellow rind and a high concentration of essential oils. The leaves are glossy and dark green, with a distinctive shape that sets them apart from other citrus species.

The plant is valued for its culinary uses and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
