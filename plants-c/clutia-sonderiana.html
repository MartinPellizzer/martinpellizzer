Clutia sonderiana

Clutia sonderiana, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Clutia sonderiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
