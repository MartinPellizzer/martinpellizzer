Cenchrus gracillimus

Cenchrus gracillimus, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
