Cyrtomium yamamotoi

Cyrtomium yamamotoi, commonly known as the Yamamoto's holly fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense foliage. The fronds are typically dark green, glossy, and have a leathery texture. It produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its fronds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shady gardens and as a houseplant.
