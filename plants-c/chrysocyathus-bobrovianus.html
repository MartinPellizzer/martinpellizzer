Chrysocyathus bobrovianus

Chrysocyathus bobrovianus, commonly known as the Bobrovian coral.

It is a rare and delicate species of stony coral found in shallow tropical waters. The coral exhibits a branching structure with bright yellow skeletal elements, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in clear, warm waters with good light penetration.

Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species.
