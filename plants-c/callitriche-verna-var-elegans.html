Callitriche verna var. elegans

Callitriche verna var. elegans, commonly known as the spring water starwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to temperate regions.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and slow-moving streams. The plant features tiny, star-shaped flowers that are often submerged or floating, with four to six petals. Its leaves are narrow and ribbon-like, arranged in whorls around the stem.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small invertebrates and contributing to water filtration.
