Cymbopogon sennarensis

Cymbopogon sennarensis, commonly known as the Sennarensis grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stature and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in open areas, including grasslands and disturbed soils, and is well adapted to warm climates. It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes many other grasses.

Cymbopogon sennarensis is often used in traditional medicine and has potential applications in horticulture and ecological restoration.
