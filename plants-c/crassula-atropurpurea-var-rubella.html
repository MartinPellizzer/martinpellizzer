Crassula atropurpurea var. rubella

Crassula atropurpurea var. rubella, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Christmas Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge, especially under bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
