Chenopodium latifolium

Chenopodium latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, succulent leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, known as bloom. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to North America and thrives in disturbed soils and sunny environments.

Chenopodium latifolium is often used in traditional medicine and is also a common weed in agricultural settings.
