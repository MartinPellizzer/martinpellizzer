Citharexylum svensonii

Citharexylum svensonii, commonly known as the Hawaiian cherry or Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are showy, with five petals, and range in color from white to pink.

It produces small, fleshy berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.
