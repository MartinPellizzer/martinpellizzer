Crinum ornatum var. longistylum

Crinum ornatum var. longistylum, commonly known as the long-stemmed crinum, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters, supporting large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The foliage consists of strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. This species is often cultivated for its dramatic floral display and air-purifying qualities.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardens and landscapes.
