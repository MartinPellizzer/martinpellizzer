Combretum alatum

Combretum alatum, commonly known as the winged flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, winged branches that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Combretum alatum is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
