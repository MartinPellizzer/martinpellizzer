Cousinia tenuifolia

Cousinia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf cushion flower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellow to orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Cousinia tenuifolia is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
