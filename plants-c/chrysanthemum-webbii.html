Chrysanthemum webbii

Chrysanthemum webbii, commonly known as the African chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Chrysanthemum webbii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
