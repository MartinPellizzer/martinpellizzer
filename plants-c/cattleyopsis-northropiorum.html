Cattleyopsis northropiorum

Cattleyopsis northropiorum, commonly known as the Northrop's cattleya orchid, is a rare and unique species native to the high altitudes of Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its high-altitude, cool environment. Its leaves are strap-like and evergreen, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
