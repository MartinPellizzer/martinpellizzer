Campylocentrum lankesteri

Campylocentrum lankesteri, commonly known as the Lankester's starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, yellow to orange fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy surface.
