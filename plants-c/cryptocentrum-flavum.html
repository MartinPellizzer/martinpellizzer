Cryptocentrum flavum

Cryptocentrum flavum, commonly known as the golden cryptocentrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant features bright yellow flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Cryptocentrum flavum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and indoor settings.
