Cnidium fontanesii

Cnidium fontanesii, commonly known as the Chinese snake herb, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and umbellate flowers. The plant is known for its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its rhizomes and roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Cnidium fontanesii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
