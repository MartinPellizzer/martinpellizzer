Cynanchum andringitrense

Cynanchum andringitrense, commonly known as the Madagascar milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive corona structure.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry, rocky areas.
