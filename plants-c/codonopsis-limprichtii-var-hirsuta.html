Codonopsis limprichtii var. hirsuta

Codonopsis limprichtii var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy codonopsis, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
