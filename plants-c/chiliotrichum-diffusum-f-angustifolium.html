Chiliotrichum diffusum f. angustifolium

Chiliotrichum diffusum f. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved chiliotrichum, is a variant of the species native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically arranged alternately along the stem and are often covered in fine, hairy structures. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in dense inflorescences, and can range in color from white to pale pink.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
