Crotalaria anthyllopsis var. albopilosa

Crotalaria anthyllopsis var. albopilosa, commonly known as the white-hair crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately compound, and often have a velvety texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
