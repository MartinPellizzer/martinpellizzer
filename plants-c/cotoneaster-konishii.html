Cotoneaster konishii

Cotoneaster konishii, commonly known as the Japanese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that turn red in the fall, adding ornamental value. It produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in autumn.

Cotoneaster konishii is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
