Cerastium semidecandrum f. dolsum

Cerastium semidecandrum f. dolsum, commonly known as the Dolsoy snowdrop, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs.

This form is a naturally occurring variant of the species, distinguished by its unique floral structure and compact growth habit.
