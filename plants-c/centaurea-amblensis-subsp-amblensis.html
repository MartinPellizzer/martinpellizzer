Centaurea amblensis subsp. amblensis

Centaurea amblensis subsp.

amblensis, commonly known as the Amblensian knapweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive blue to purple flower heads.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments. Centaurea amblensis subsp.

amblensis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
