Cucurbita maxima convar. ecoronata

Cucurbita maxima convar. ecoronata, commonly known as the acorn squash, is a cultivated variant of the species Cucurbita maxima.

It is characterized by its elongated, asymmetrical fruit shape and a rough, textured exterior. The plant produces large, typically green or yellowish fruits with a hard, fibrous rind. The leaves are broad, lobed, and often have a hairy surface.

This variety is prized for its culinary versatility and nutritional value.
