Chamaecytisus × cetius

Chamaecytisus × cetius, commonly known as the evergreen broom, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer. The plant has slender, arching stems covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are small, leathery, and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its hardy nature and attractive floral display.
