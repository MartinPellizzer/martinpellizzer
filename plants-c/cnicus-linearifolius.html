Cnicus linearifolius

Cnicus linearifolius, commonly known as the linear-leaved sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces yellow flowers with five petals. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
