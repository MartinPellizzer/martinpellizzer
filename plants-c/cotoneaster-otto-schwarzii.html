Cotoneaster otto-schwarzii

Cotoneaster otto-schwarzii, commonly known as Otto Schwarz's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster otto-schwarzii is often used in landscaping for its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
