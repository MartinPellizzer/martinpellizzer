Chrysojasminum humile

Chrysojasminum humile, commonly known as the dwarf golden jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and pleasant aroma.
