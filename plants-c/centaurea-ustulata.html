Centaurea ustulata

Centaurea ustulata, commonly known as the scorched centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Centaurea ustulata is known for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
