Cotoneaster rockii

Cotoneaster rockii, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Cotoneaster rockii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability.
