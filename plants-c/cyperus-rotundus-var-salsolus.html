Cyperus rotundus var. salsolus

Cyperus rotundus var. salsolus, commonly known as sandspur, is a flowering plant in the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often pale green or brown. It is characterized by its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it a common weed in many regions.

The plant is also known for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
