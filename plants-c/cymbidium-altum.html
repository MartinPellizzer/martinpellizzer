Cymbidium altum

Cymbidium altum, commonly known as the tall orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia and parts of China.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red.

Cymbidium altum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
