Camellia brevistyla var. brevistyla

Camellia brevistyla var. brevistyla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved camellia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that are shorter and narrower than those of other camellia varieties. The plant produces showy, camellia-like flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a source of tea leaves.
