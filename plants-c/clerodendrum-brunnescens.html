Clerodendrum brunnescens

Clerodendrum brunnescens, commonly known as the brownstem clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, featuring opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by distinctive, globose, yellowish fruits.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
