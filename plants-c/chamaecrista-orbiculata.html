Chamaecrista orbiculata

Chamaecrista orbiculata, commonly known as the globe ticktree, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a perennial herb that produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has palmately compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in sandy or rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
