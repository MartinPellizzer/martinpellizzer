Conophytum barbatum

Conophytum barbatum, commonly known as the hairy conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Conophytum barbatum is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
