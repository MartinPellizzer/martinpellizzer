Costus Maximus

Costus maximus is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers and unique foliage. Native to regions across Southeast Asia and the Pacific, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its tall, sturdy stems and clusters of bright orange or red blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional healing practices. With its bold appearance and cultural significance, Costus maximus stands out as both a decorative and functional plant.
