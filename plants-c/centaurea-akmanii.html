Centaurea akmanii

Centaurea akmanii, commonly known as Akman's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
