Chenopodium polyspermum subsp. cymosum

Chenopodium polyspermum subsp.

cymosum, commonly known as red root goosefoot, is a perennial herb native to North America. It typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, stream banks, and prairie wetlands.

The plant features alternate, lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, compact inflorescences, and it produces oblong, fleshy fruits.

It is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.
