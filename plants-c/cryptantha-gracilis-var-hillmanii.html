Cryptantha gracilis var. hillmanii

Cryptantha gracilis var. hillmanii, commonly known as the slender cryptantha, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This variety is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant is often found in California and Oregon, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
