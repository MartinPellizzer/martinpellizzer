Calliandra unijuga var. pueblensis

Calliandra unijuga var. pueblensis, commonly known as the Pueblan calliandra, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces bright orange or red flowers that attract pollinators. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.

The variety is noted for its resilience in arid and semi-arid environments.
