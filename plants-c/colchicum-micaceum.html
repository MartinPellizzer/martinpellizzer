Colchicum micaceum

Colchicum micaceum, commonly known as the silverweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Colchicum micaceum is also known for its medicinal properties, though it contains toxic compounds that require careful handling.
