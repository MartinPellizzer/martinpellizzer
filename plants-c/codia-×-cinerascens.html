Codia × cinerascens

Codia × cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf codia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding subtle ornamental value. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental appeal and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
