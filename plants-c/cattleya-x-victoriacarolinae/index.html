Cattleya × Victoriacarolinae

Cattleya × victoriacarolinae is a hybrid orchid known for its striking beauty and vibrant blooms. This plant is a cross between Cattleya × labiata and Cattleya bicolor, resulting in a unique combination of traits. It features large, showy flowers with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance. Native to the Caribbean, it thrives in warm, humid environments with ample sunlight. Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and collectors.
