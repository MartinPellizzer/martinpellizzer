Croton decaryi

Croton decaryi, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that can range from red to yellow, orange, and even purple, making it a visually appealing plant. The leaves are usually palmately lobed, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant foliage.
