Centropogon Glabratus

Centropogon glabratus is a unique flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom during the winter months. This plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dune environments. Its ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions makes it an important part of the local ecosystem. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the world of horticulture.
