Chrysanthemum burbankii

Chrysanthemum burbankii, commonly known as the Burbank chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and showy flower heads. The plant produces daisy-like flowers in a range of colors, including yellow, orange, and red, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is widely cultivated for both decorative and commercial purposes.
