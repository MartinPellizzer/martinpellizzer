Clerodendrum blumeanum

Clerodendrum blumeanum, commonly known as the blue mist shrub, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a capsule containing numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
