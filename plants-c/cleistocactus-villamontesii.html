Cleistocactus villamontesii

Cleistocactus villamontesii, commonly known as the Mexican night bloomer, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces fragrant, night-blooming flowers that are typically pink or purple. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique flowering behavior, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
