Cylindrorebutia auranitida

Cylindrorebutia auranitida, commonly known as the golden torch cactus, is a species native to South America, specifically Argentina.

It is a low-growing, branching cactus with cylindrical stems that are typically bright yellow to orange in color. The plant produces vibrant orange flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
