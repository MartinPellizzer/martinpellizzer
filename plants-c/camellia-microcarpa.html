Camellia microcarpa

Camellia microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf camellia, is a species native to China.

It is a evergreen shrub characterized by its small, glossy leaves and showy, camellia-like flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. Its flowers, which range in color from white to deep red, bloom in late winter to early spring.

Camellia microcarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
