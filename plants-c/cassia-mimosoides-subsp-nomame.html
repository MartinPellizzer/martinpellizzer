Cassia mimosoides subsp. nomame

Cassia mimosoides subsp.

nomame, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, bright yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, flat pods that contain several seeds.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
