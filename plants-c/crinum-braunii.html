Crinum braunii

Crinum braunii, commonly known as the Braun's glory lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Crinum braunii is also noted for its strong fragrance, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
