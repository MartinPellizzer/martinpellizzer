Capsicum annuum var. parvoacuminatum

Capsicum annuum var. parvoacuminatum, commonly known as the dwarf chili pepper, is a compact variety of the common chili plant.

It is characterized by its small, pointed fruits that typically range in color from red to yellow. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as for its mild to medium heat level. The plant has a bushy growth habit with numerous small leaves and flowers.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
