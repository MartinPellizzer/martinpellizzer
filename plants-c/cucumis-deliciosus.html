Cucumis deliciosus

Cucumis deliciosus, commonly known as the wild cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to various regions in Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically green and spiny when mature. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture.

The plant thrives in warm climates and is often found growing in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
