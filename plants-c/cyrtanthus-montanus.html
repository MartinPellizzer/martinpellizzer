Cyrtanthus montanus

Cyrtanthus montanus, commonly known as the mountain crocus, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Cyrtanthus montanus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
