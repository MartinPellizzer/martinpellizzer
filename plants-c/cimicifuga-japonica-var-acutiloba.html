Cimicifuga japonica var. acutiloba

Cimicifuga japonica var. acutiloba, commonly known as Japanese bugbane, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features tall, erect stems with large, white, lacecap flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has compound leaves composed of several narrow, serrated leaflets. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical name reflects its origin and distinctive leaf shape.
