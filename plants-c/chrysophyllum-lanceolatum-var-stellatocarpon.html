Chrysophyllum lanceolatum var. stellatocarpon

Chrysophyllum lanceolatum var. stellatocarpon, commonly known as the starfruit or carambola, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit, which is typically yellow when ripe. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are lanceolate in shape and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is not only ornamental but also edible, with a sweet and tangy flavor.
