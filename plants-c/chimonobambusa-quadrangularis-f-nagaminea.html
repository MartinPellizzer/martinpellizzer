Chimonobambusa quadrangularis f. nagaminea

Chimonobambusa quadrangularis f. nagaminea, commonly known as the Nagamine's square bamboo, is a unique variant of the square bamboo species.

It is characterized by its distinctive quadrangular stem shape, which sets it apart from other bamboo varieties. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

This bamboo is also appreciated for its ability to grow in a variety of climates, making it a versatile choice for gardeners and botanists alike.
