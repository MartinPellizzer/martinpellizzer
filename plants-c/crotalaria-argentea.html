Crotalaria argentea

Crotalaria argentea, commonly known as silverleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family, and is known for its nitrogen-fixing ability. Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines, while the plant also has medicinal properties.

Crotalaria argentea is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
