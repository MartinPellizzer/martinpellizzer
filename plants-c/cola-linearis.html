Cola linearis

Cola linearis, commonly known as the slender cola or African cola, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the rainforests of West and Central Africa. Cola linearis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
