Cymbidium umbellatum

Cymbidium umbellatum, commonly known as the umbrella orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched inflorescence that bears numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

Cymbidium umbellatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for both horticultural and floral arrangement purposes.
