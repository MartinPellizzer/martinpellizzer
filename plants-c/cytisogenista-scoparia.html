Cytisogenista scoparia

Cytisogenista scoparia, commonly known as the desert milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early spring.
