Croton allemii

Croton allemii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that can vary in shape and hue depending on the species. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Croton allemii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
