Comoneura klaineana

Comoneura klaineana, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, leathery leaves and produces clusters of white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
