Cattleya trianae var. corningii

Cattleya trianae var. corningii, commonly known as the Corning's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically produces large, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
