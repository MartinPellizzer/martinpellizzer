Cytisus capensis

Cytisus capensis, commonly known as the yellow sweetpea, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, compound leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Cytisus capensis is also known for its fragrant blooms and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
