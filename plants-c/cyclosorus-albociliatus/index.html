Cyclosorus Albociliatus

Cyclosorus albociliatus is a rare and delicate fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments. This plant is known for its unique, finely divided fronds that give it a feathery appearance. It is commonly found in temperate regions where the climate supports its growth habits. Its white cilia along the leaf edges make it easily identifiable. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of botany.
