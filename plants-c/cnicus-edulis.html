Cnicus edulis

Cnicus edulis, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, silver-green leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in coastal environments and well-drained soils, often found in sandy or rocky areas. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, requiring careful management to prevent overgrowth.
