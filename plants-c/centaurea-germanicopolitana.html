Centaurea germanicopolitana

Centaurea germanicopolitana, commonly known as the Germanicopolitan centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

It is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils.
