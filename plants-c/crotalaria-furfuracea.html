Crotalaria furfuracea

Crotalaria furfuracea, commonly known as the hairy crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
