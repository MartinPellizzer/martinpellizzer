Chrysanthemum × rubellum

Chrysanthemum × rubellum, commonly known as the red chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange flowers, which bloom in dense, showy clusters. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of 1 to 3 feet, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets, typical of the daisy family.

This species is native to China and is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking color and compact growth habit.
