Cytisus austriacus var. virescens

Cytisus austriacus var. virescens, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a flowering shrub native to Central Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that are often reddish-brown in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

It is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
