Crotalaria stuhlmannii var. crassicarpa

Crotalaria stuhlmannii var. crassicarpa, commonly known as the thickpod crotalaria, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces oblong, thickened pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it an important plant in ecological studies and agricultural research.
