Cardiopteris rumphii var. subhamata

Cardiopteris rumphii var. subhamata, commonly known as the silver-stemmed arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, white spadices that are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and indoor plant collections.
