Caladenia × cala

Caladenia × cala, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Australia.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Caladenia crosbyi and Caladenia flava, showcasing a blend of characteristics from both parent plants. The plant typically features a single, slender stem with a single flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its flowers are usually pink or purple with intricate patterns, and it blooms during the spring season.

Caladenia × cala is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to the temperate climate of southern Australia.
