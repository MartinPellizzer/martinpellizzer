Chamaefistula angusta

Chamaefistula angusta, commonly known as the narrowleaf boxwood, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval, evergreen leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is valued for its dense, compact growth habit, making it suitable for hedges and topiary.

Chamaefistula angusta prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
