Centaurea dufourei

Centaurea dufourei, commonly known as the Dufour's knapweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, rocky soils.
