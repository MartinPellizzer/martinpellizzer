Carex macounii

Carex macounii, commonly known as Macoun's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, grasses, and reeds. This plant typically grows in moist to wet environments such as bogs, marshes, and along stream banks. Its leaves are grass-like, narrow, and usually have a bluish-green hue.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
