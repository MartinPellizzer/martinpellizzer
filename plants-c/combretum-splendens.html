Combretum splendens

Combretum splendens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange inflorescences that appear in clusters during the dry season. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun.
