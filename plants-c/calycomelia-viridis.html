Calycomelia viridis

Calycomelia viridis, commonly known as the green calycomelia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and lobed, often with a velvety texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
