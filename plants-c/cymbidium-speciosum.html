Cymbidium speciosum

Cymbidium speciosum, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens and horticulture. Cymbidium speciosum prefers cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and cultural significance.

Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained soil conditions.
