Clerodendrum silvanum var. nuxioides

Clerodendrum silvanum var. nuxioides, commonly known as the Indian flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences of bright red or orange flowers, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

The bark is smooth and often has a reddish or brownish hue, adding to its ornamental value.
