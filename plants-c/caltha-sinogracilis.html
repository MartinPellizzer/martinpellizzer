Caltha sinogracilis

Caltha sinogracilis, commonly known as the Chinese slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for wetland gardens and conservation efforts.
