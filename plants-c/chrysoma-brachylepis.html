Chrysoma brachylepis

Chrysoma brachylepis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed chrysoma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers are arranged in compact clusters.
