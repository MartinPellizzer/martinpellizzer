Cleisostoma medogense

Cleisostoma medogense, commonly known as the Medog cleisostoma, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the Himalayan region of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color, with a distinctive lip structure. It thrives in shaded, humid environments at high altitudes, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species requiring conservation efforts.
