Clerodendrum wallii

Clerodendrum wallii, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from red to orange, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum wallii is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
