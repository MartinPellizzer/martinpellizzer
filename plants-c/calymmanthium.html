Calymmanthium

Calymmanthium, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its silver-green foliage and daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. Native to southern Africa, these plants thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. They are often used in ornamental gardening for their drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal.

The genus includes several species, such as Calymmanthium glabrum and Calymmanthium africanum.
