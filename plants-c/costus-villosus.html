Costus villosus

Costus villosus, commonly known as the hairy costus, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in spikes at the top of the plant.
