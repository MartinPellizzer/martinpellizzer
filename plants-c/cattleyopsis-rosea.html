Cattleyopsis rosea

Cattleyopsis rosea, commonly known as the rose orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit, featuring pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a distinctive rose-like appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Cattleyopsis rosea is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
