Calamagrostis Huebneriana Var. Flavescens

Calamagrostis huebneriana var. flavescens is a striking ornamental grass known for its vibrant golden hue. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands and marshy areas. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add visual interest to gardens. Its feathery plumes create a dramatic effect during the fall season. With its unique color and adaptability, it's a popular choice among landscape designers and garden enthusiasts.
