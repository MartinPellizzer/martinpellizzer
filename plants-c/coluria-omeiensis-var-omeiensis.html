Coluria omeiensis var. omeiensis

Coluria omeiensis var. omeiensis, commonly known as the Chinese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
