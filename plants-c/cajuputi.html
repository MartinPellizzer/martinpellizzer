Cajuputi

Cajuputi, commonly known as *Toona sinensis*, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, light green bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by oval, woody fruits containing several seeds. Cajuputi is valued for its timber, which is used in construction and furniture-making.

It also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
