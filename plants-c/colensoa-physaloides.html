Colensoa physaloides

Colensoa physaloides, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its flowers open in the morning and close by late afternoon, hence its common name.

Colensoa physaloides is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
