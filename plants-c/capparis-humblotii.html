Capparis humblotii

Capparis humblotii, commonly known as the Madagascar glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly succulent texture.

Capparis humblotii is adapted to grow in arid or semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
