Chamaedorea fractiflexa

Chamaedorea fractiflexa, commonly known as the flexible bamboo palm, is a compact, upright evergreen with slender, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance.

Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a slender trunk with a fibrous sheath and produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by dark blue berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and moderate watering.

Its slow growth and air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for indoor spaces.
