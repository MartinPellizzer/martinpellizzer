Cartiera amplexicaulis

Cartiera amplexicaulis, commonly known as the clasping leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its unique foliage, where the leaves encircle the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a spike.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
