Coelogyne stenopetala

Coelogyne stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Coelogyne stenopetala is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
