Camellia fleuryi

Camellia fleuryi, commonly known as the Fleurie's camellia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, leathery leaves that provide year-round visual interest. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Camellia fleuryi is also valued for its cold hardiness and ability to bloom in late winter or early spring.
