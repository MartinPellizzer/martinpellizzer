Coryphantha organensis

Coryphantha organensis, commonly known as the Organ Mountains cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the southwestern United States, particularly the Organ Mountains in New Mexico. This plant is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white to grayish spines. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Coryphantha organensis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and desert landscapes.
