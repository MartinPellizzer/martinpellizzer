Casearia hosei

Casearia hosei, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vivid display during the flowering season. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Casearia hosei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks due to its showy flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
