Cotoneaster schubertii

Cotoneaster schubertii, commonly known as Schubert's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Cotoneaster schubertii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
