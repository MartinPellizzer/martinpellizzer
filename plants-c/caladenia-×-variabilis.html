Caladenia × variabilis

Caladenia × variabilis, commonly known as the variable spider-orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant produces a single, erect stem with several flowers arranged along its length. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and heathlands.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists.
