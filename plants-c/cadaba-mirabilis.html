Cadaba mirabilis

Cadaba mirabilis, commonly known as the silverleaf plectranthus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Cadaba mirabilis is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
