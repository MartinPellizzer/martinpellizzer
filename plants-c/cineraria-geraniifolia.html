Cineraria geraniifolia

Cineraria geraniifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy leaves and showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a striking addition to any botanical collection.
