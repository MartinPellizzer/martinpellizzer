Calamagrostis stricta var. geniculata

Calamagrostis stricta var. geniculata, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, arching stems that produce feathery, silver-green plumes, giving it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along streambanks. Its narrow leaves are typically bluish-green and remain upright throughout the growing season.

This variety is valued in landscaping for its elegance and ability to tolerate wet conditions.
