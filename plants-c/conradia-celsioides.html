Conradia celsioides

Conradia celsioides, commonly known as the dwarf conradia, is a low-growing succulent native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are typically green or blue-green in color, often with a slight powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
