Cheirostylis macrantha

Cheirostylis macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered cheirostylis, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that bear large, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often more elaborate than the other petals.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
