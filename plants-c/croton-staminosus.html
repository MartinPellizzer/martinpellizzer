Croton staminosus

Croton staminosus, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.

Croton staminosus is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
