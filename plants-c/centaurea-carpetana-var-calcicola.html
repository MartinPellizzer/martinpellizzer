Centaurea carpetana var. calcicola

Centaurea carpetana var. calcicola, commonly known as the limestone centaury, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It thrives in dry, calcareous soils and is often found in rocky or limestone-rich habitats. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically bright blue or purple, forming dense, composite-like inflorescences.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
