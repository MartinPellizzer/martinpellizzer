Cynometra sessiliflora var. laurentii

Cynometra sessiliflora var. laurentii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and its dense, spreading canopy. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by hard, woody fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

This species is valued for its durable wood, which is used in construction and traditional crafts.
