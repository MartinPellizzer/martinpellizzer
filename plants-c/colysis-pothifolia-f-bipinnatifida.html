Colysis pothifolia f. bipinnatifida

Colysis pothifolia f. bipinnatifida, commonly known as the bipinnatifid pottia, is a rare and delicate moss species belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, bipinnatifid leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rock crevices. Its small, capsule-like sporophytes develop at the base of the leaves, contributing to its reproductive cycle.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in temperate regions with high humidity and low light conditions.
