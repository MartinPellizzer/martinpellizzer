Chlorophytum zavattarii

Chlorophytum zavattarii, commonly known as the Variegated Spider Plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that are clustered in umbels. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect light.

Chlorophytum zavattarii is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to purify indoor air and its attractive, trailing growth habit.
