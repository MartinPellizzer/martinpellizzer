Combretum transvaalense var. ochrolepidotum

Combretum transvaalense var. ochrolepidotum, commonly known as the yellow-leafed combretum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often yellow or reddish in color. The plant produces small, pale yellow or greenish flowers arranged in clusters. Its bark is smooth and may exude a milky sap when damaged.

It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in woodland and savanna habitats.
