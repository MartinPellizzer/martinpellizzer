Campanula filicaulis var. intermedia

Campanula filicaulis var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is valued for its attractive blooms and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
