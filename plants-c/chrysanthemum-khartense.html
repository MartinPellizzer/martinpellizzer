Chrysanthemum khartense

Chrysanthemum khartense, commonly known as the Sudan chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets and white to pale pink ray florets. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated for garden use.
