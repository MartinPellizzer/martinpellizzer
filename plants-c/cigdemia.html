Cigdemia

Cigdemia, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the eastern United States.

It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are often a deep green color. The plant produces striking red flowers with a distinctive spider-like appearance, which bloom in the summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Cigdemia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural landscapes.
