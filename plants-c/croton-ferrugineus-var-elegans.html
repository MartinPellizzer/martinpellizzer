Croton ferrugineus var. elegans

Croton ferrugineus var. elegans, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish-brown.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, warm climates. Its leaves are often adorned with intricate patterns and can vary in shape, adding to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers, which are typically followed by capsules containing seeds.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive indoors with proper care and light.
