Cryptocoryne wendtii var. krauteri

Cryptocoryne wendtii var. krauteri, commonly known as the Red Cryptocoryne, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking red or pinkish leaves that are often variegated with green, making it a visually appealing addition to aquariums. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires stable water parameters to maintain its vibrant coloration. It is a hardy species that can grow both as a stem plant and a bushy plant, depending on the aquarium setup.

Cryptocoryne wendtii var. krauteri is also known for its ability to help in biological filtration and provide shelter for small aquatic life.
