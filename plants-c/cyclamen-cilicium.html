Cyclamen cilicium

Cyclamen cilicium, commonly known as the Cilician cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves with a distinctive violet or purple hue, and it produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Cyclamen cilicium has a short growing season, blooming in late winter to early spring.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for gardens and botanical collections.
