Cereus pfersdorffii

Cereus pfersdorffii, commonly known as the Christmas star cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Brazil.

It is characterized by its thick, segmented stems that bear clusters of bright red or pink flowers during the holiday season. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its spiny stems and showy blooms, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique blooming period and striking appearance make it a favorite among cactus enthusiasts.
