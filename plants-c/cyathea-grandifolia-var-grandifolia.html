Cyathea grandifolia var. grandifolia

Cyathea grandifolia var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaf tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is a member of the order Cyatheales. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This fern is often found in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
