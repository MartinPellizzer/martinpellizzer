Carduus revolutus

Carduus revolutus, commonly known as the common thistle.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features spiny, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in meadows.

The plant has a robust root system and can spread through both seeds and vegetative means.
