Chthamalia major

Chthamalia major, commonly known as the giant sea holly, is a species of red algae found primarily in the intertidal zones of the Pacific Northwest.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy thalli that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant has a distinctive branching structure with a central axis and lateral branches that are often covered in small, rounded bumps.
