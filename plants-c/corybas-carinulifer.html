Corybas carinulifer

Corybas carinulifer, commonly known as the hooded helmet orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-like labellum that resembles a hood, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understoreys. Its flowers are usually small and pale, with a showy, spurred lip that attracts specific pollinators.

Corybas carinulifer is native to New Zealand and is known for its rare and delicate botanical features.
