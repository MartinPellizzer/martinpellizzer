Croton aequatoris

Croton aequatoris, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. Croton aequatoris typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

Its foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.
