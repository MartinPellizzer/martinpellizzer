Chrysocephalum semipapposum subsp. asperum

Chrysocephalum semipapposum subsp.

asperum, commonly known as the rough everlasting, is a perennial herb native to Australia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers.
