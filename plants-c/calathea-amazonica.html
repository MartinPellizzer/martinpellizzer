Calathea amazonica

Calathea amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, patterned leaves that display a variety of colors and intricate vein patterns. The plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often used in horticultural displays due to its aesthetic appeal.

Calathea amazonica is known for its ability to purify the air, adding to its value as a decorative and functional plant.
