Crotalaria lotononis

Crotalaria lotononis, commonly known as the lotonon tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The seeds of Crotalaria lotononis are encased in a hard pod and are often dispersed by wind or animals.
