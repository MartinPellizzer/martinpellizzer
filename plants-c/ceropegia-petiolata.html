Ceropegia petiolata

Ceropegia petiolata, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are typically borne on slender stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
