Codonoboea beccarii

Codonoboea beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari's codonopsis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to deep red. The plant typically grows as an erect herb with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
