Chamaedorea brevifrons

Chamaedorea brevifrons, commonly known as the short-fruited bamboo palm, is a compact, evergreen plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It features slender, upright stems that produce clustered, feather-like leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by dark blue berries, though it is more valued for its ornamental foliage than its fruit. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and ability to tolerate low light conditions make it a popular choice for indoor spaces.
