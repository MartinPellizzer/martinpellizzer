Cynoglossum tubiflorum

Cynoglossum tubiflorum, commonly known as broadleaf ironweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with a rough texture. The plant produces tall, branching stems that bear small, blue to purple flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its flowers are typically tubular and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Cynoglossum tubiflorum is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed soils.
