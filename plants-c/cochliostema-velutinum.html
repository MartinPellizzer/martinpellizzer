Cochliostema velutinum

Cochliostema velutinum, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

This plant is a member of the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, hairy leaves. It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.
