Cenchrus agrimonioides var. laysanensis

Cenchrus agrimonioides var. laysanensis, commonly known as Laysan panicgrass, is a grass species native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in coastal and dry inland habitats, often found on sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
