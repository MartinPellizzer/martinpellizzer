Campyloneurum macrosorum

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and dark green to brownish-green foliage. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a distinct midrib and a slightly curled appearance when dry. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rock surfaces or in forest floors.
