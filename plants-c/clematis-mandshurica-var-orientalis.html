Clematis mandshurica var. orientalis

Clematis mandshurica var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and can be trained to grow on trellises or walls.
