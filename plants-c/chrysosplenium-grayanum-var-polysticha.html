Chrysosplenium grayanum var. polysticha

Chrysosplenium grayanum var. polysticha, commonly known as the silver saxifrage, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the family Saxifragaceae and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring cool and moist environments. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in its ecological niche.
