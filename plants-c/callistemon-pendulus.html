Callistemon pendulus

Callistemon pendulus, commonly known as the weeping bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, bright red flower spikes that resemble bottlebrushes, which bloom during the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically grey-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a versatile addition to landscaping.
