Clintonia bergiana

Clintonia bergiana, commonly known as the white starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Clintonia bergiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to native pollinators.
