Cattleya walkeriana var. princeps

Cattleya walkeriana var. princeps, commonly known as the Prince's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and lavender. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and ease of cultivation.

This variety is often used in hybridization to enhance floral characteristics in other Cattleya species.
