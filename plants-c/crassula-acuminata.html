Crassula acuminata

Crassula acuminata, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Christmas Jade, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems that branch out into pairs of thick, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and bonsai cultivation.
