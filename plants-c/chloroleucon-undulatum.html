Chloroleucon undulatum

Chloroleucon undulatum, commonly known as the zigzag dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, undulating leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its spread.

The species is noted for its ecological importance, providing nectar for various pollinators.
