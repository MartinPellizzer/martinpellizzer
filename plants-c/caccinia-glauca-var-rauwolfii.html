Caccinia glauca var. rauwolfii

Caccinia glauca var. rauwolfii, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant blue to violet flowers, which bloom in the early morning. The plant has a trailing growth habit and is often found growing along sandy or rocky shores. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy, bluish-green appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
