Cestrum aurantiacum f. regelii

Cestrum aurantiacum f. regelii, commonly known as the Regel's yellow bell, is a compact shrub native to Mexico.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually hairy beneath. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and fragrant scent.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, berry-like seeds.
