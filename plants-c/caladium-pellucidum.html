Caladium pellucidum

Caladium pellucidum, commonly known as the glassy arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that often display vibrant shades of green, red, and white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. Caladium pellucidum thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique and colorful foliage.
