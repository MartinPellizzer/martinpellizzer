Cleome kermesina

Cleome kermesina, commonly known as the spider flower, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the southern United States.

It features erect, branching stems that produce clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant's foliage consists of serrated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Cleome kermesina thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance ornamental plants.

Its flowering period typically occurs from late spring through early autumn.
