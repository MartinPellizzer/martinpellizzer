Coelogyne × burfordiensis

Coelogyne × burfordiensis, commonly known as the Burford's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant, and often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial orchid, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, and it produces inflorescences with multiple flowers that bloom in spring or early summer.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
