Clematis junghuhniana

Clematis junghuhniana, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover.

Clematis junghuhniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
