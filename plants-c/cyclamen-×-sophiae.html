Cyclamen × sophiae

Cyclamen × sophiae, commonly known as the hybrid cyclamen, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cyclamen persicum and Cyclamen coum.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as pink, red, and white. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening during the colder months. Cyclamen × sophiae prefers cool, bright conditions and well-draining soil to thrive.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
