Crassula massonii

Crassula massonii, commonly known as the "Lucky Plant" or "Good Luck Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water and survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its compact size and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for both decorative and therapeutic purposes.
