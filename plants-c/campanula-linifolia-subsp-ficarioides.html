Campanula linifolia subsp. ficarioides

Campanula linifolia subsp.

ficarioides, commonly known as the Ficaria bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, upright stems and produces bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft, woolly appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
