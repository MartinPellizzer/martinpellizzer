Campanula yildirimlii

Campanula yildirimlii, commonly known as the Yildirimli bellflower, is a species native to Turkey.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its upright stems and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically blue or violet in color, and they bloom in the summer months. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
