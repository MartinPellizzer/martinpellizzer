Clitoria epetiolata

Clitoria epetiolata, commonly known as the winged bean or the blue pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or violet pea-shaped flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is an annual legume that grows upright with slender stems and often produces pods that contain edible seeds. Its botanical features include compound leaves with three leaflets and a unique winged structure on the pods.

Clitoria epetiolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in traditional agriculture and cuisine.
