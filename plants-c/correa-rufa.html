Correa rufa

Correa rufa, commonly known as the red correa, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its attractive, tubular red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Correa rufa thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
