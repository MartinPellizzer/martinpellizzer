Chrysalidocarpus brevinodis

Chrysalidocarpus brevinodis, commonly known as the slender palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue or black berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
