Cytisus transiens

Cytisus transiens, commonly known as the wandering broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, woody stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are small, trifoliate, and have a slightly hairy surface.

Cytisus transiens is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
