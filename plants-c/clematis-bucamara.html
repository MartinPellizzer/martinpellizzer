Clematis bucamara

Clematis bucamara, commonly known as the Brazilian vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant has slender, twining stems that can grow up to 15 meters in length. Its foliage consists of simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape.

Clematis bucamara thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
