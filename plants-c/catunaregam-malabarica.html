Catunaregam malabarica

Catunaregam malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters. Its rhizomes are the primary part used, valued for their starchy texture and culinary uses.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Araceae, which includes other plants like taro and caladium.
