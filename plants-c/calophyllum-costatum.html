Calophyllum costatum

Calophyllum costatum, commonly known as the tropical beauty or golden flame tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Calophyllaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing during the dry season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Calophyllum costatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display and ability to thrive in warm, sunny climates.
