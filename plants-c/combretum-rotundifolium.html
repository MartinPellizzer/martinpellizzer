Combretum rotundifolium

Combretum rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaf flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The leaves are broadly ovate with rounded tips and are often covered in fine hairs. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a notable species in its native habitat.
