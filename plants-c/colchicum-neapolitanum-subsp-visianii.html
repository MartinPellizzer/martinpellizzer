Colchicum neapolitanum subsp. visianii

Colchicum neapolitanum subsp.

visianii, commonly known as the Naples colchicum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe. It features striking, showy flowers that are typically purple or violet in color, with a distinctive spurred petal.

The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that die back after flowering. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
