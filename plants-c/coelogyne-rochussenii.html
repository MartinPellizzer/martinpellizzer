Coelogyne rochussenii

Coelogyne rochussenii, commonly known as the Red Orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with leathery leaves that store water, adapting to its natural habitat. Its flowers are highly fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Coelogyne rochussenii is prized in horticulture for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
