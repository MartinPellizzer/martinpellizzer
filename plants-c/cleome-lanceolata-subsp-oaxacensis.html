Cleome lanceolata subsp. oaxacensis

Cleome lanceolata subsp.

oaxacensis, commonly known as the Oaxacan cleome, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico. It belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for gardens in warmer climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and relatively low maintenance requirements.
