Centaurium breviflorum

Centaurium breviflorum, commonly known as the short-flowered centaury, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, with five petals and a prominent central tube. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged alternately along the stem.

Centaurium breviflorum is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
