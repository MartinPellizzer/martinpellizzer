Calathea polyphylla

Calathea polyphylla, commonly known as the laceleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate foliage with a mosaic of colors and patterns. The leaves are typically oval or heart-shaped, with a velvety texture and a waxy surface. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
