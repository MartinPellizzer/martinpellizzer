Coffea arabica var. murta

Coffea arabica var. murta, commonly known as the Murta coffee plant, is a variant of the Arabica coffee species native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its unique, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional brewing methods. The plant produces small, red berries containing the seeds used for coffee production. It thrives in high-altitude regions with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its distinct flavor profile, it is highly valued in specialty coffee markets.
