Citharexylum joergensenii

Citharexylum joergensenii, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat.
