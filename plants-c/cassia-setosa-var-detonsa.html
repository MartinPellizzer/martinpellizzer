Cassia setosa var. detonsa

Cassia setosa var. detonsa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
