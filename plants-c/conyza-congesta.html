Conyza congesta

Conyza congesta, commonly known as the clustered aster, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disc.

Conyza congesta is often found in coastal areas and can tolerate saline conditions.
