Conyza andringitrana

Conyza andringitrana, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open areas and has hairy, branching stems. Its small white to pale yellow flowers are arranged in clusters, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Conyza andringitrana is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
