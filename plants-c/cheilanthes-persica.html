Cheilanthes persica

Cheilanthes persica, commonly known as the Persian lip fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant's ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it an important component of its ecosystem.
