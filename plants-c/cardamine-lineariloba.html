Cardamine lineariloba

Cardamine lineariloba, commonly known as the slender water crowfoot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white flowers are arranged in a single umbel and bloom in early spring.

Cardamine lineariloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
