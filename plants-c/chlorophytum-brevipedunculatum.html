Chlorophytum brevipedunculatum

Chlorophytum brevipedunculatum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with prominent white or yellow stripes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers on slender stalks, though it is more valued for its ornamental foliage than its blooms.

Its air-purifying properties also make it a popular choice for improving indoor air quality.
