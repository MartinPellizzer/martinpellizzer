Corchorus coreta

Corchorus coreta, commonly known as Indian mallow, is a herbaceous plant native to South Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes other plants like cotton and hibiscus. The plant features hairy, alternate leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months.
