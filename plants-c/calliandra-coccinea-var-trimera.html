Calliandra coccinea var. trimera

Calliandra coccinea var. trimera, commonly known as the red coral tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, feathery inflorescences that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has compound leaves composed of multiple small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
