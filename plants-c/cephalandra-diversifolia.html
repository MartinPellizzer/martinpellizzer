Cephalandra diversifolia

Cephalandra diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated ivy or the golden ivy, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking yellow or white variegation. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage and ability to climb trellises or walls.
