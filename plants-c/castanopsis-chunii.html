Castanopsis chunii

Castanopsis chunii, commonly known as the Chinese tallow or Chinese chestnut.

It is a deciduous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The plant features glossy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish. Its fruits are woody capsules containing one or two seeds.

Castanopsis chunii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
