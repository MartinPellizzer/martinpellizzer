Calliandra sancti-pauli

Calliandra sancti-pauli, commonly known as the pink calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink to red flowers, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and its stems are often woody and thorny. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Calliandra sancti-pauli is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.
