Campanula ficarioides var. gautieri

Campanula ficarioides var. gautieri, commonly known as Gautier's bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically blue or violet in color, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in garden settings.
