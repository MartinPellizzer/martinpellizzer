Campanula ecklonii

Campanula ecklonii, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It produces showy blooms from late spring to early autumn, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Campanula ecklonii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
