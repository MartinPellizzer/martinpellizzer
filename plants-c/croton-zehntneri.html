Croton zehntneri

Croton zehntneri, commonly known as the Zehntner's croton, is a striking shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique foliage, it is popular in horticulture for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes.
