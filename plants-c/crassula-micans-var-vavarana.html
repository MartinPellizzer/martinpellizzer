Crassula micans var. vavarana

Crassula micans var. vavarana, commonly known as the Frost Plant or Blue Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant color.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
