Calandrinia compressa var. macilenta

Calandrinia compressa var. macilenta, commonly known as the slender-flowered portulaca, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing, succulent annual that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The species is valued in horticulture for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
