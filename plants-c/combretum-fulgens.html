Combretum fulgens

Combretum fulgens, commonly known as the flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in dense clusters during the dry season. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodlands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
