Callista speciosa

Callista speciosa, commonly known as the showy callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with compound leaves and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Callista speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
