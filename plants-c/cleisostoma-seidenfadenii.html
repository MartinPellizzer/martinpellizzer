Cleisostoma seidenfadenii

Cleisostoma seidenfadenii, commonly known as the Orchid of the Moon, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers typically bloom during the rainy season, adding a delicate beauty to the forest understory.

Cleisostoma seidenfadenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
