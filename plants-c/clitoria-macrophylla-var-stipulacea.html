Clitoria macrophylla var. stipulacea

Clitoria macrophylla var. stipulacea, commonly known as the winged bean or evergreen bean, is a tropical legume characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped purple flowers.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features compound leaves with small, leaf-like stipules, giving it its specific variety name. Its pods contain edible seeds and are often cultivated for both ornamental and nutritional purposes.

The species is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which enriches the soil.
