Cavendishia wercklei

Cavendishia wercklei, commonly known as the Wercklei cavendishia, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Brazil.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its cylindrical stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant features spiny areoles and produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are edible. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for succulent gardens.
