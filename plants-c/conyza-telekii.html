Conyza telekii

Conyza telekii, commonly known as the desert fleabane, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant is typically herbaceous, with small, yellowish to white flowers that bloom in clusters. It has hairy leaves and stems, adapted to withstand drought conditions.
