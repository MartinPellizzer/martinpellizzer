Cynanchum lanhsuense

Cynanchum lanhsuense, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed vine, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiny fruits. It contains milky sap, which is a characteristic feature of the genus Cynanchum.

Due to its chemical composition, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various therapeutic purposes.
