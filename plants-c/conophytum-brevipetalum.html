Conophytum brevipetalum

Conophytum brevipetalum, commonly known as the "Little Petal Coneplant," is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded body with fleshy, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise rugged appearance. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique morphology and resilience make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
