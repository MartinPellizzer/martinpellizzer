Croton Androiensis

Andro's croton, scientifically known as Croton androiensis, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Madagascar. This unique species belongs to the Croton family, which is known for its diverse and often colorful foliage. Its leaves are particularly notable for their vibrant hues and intricate patterns, making it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, the plant remains little-known outside of specialized botanical circles. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Andro's croton is considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
