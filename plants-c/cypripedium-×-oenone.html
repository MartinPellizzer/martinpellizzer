Cypripedium × oenone

Cypripedium × oenone, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive pink or reddish lip that resembles a slipper. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments and has a tall, slender stem with a single or few flowers at the top. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.

The species is also noted for its slow growth and specific cultivation requirements.
