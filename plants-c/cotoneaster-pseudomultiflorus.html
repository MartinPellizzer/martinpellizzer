Cotoneaster pseudomultiflorus

Cotoneaster pseudomultiflorus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and turn red in the fall. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping.

Cotoneaster pseudomultiflorus is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.
