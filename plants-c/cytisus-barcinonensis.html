Cytisus barcinonensis

Cytisus barcinonensis, commonly known as the Baiona broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume features. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are trifoliate, with small, oval leaflets.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
