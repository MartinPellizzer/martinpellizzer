Cryptocoryne petchii

Cryptocoryne petchii, commonly known as the Petchii crypts, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves that often have a subtle metallic sheen. The plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water with moderate to high light conditions. It is popular in planted aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne petchii is also noted for its ability to tolerate a range of water parameters, making it a versatile choice for hobbyists.
