Cyclosorus decadens, commonly known as the delicate water fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, delicate fronds that often form a rosette shape, allowing it to thrive in shallow, still waters. Cyclosorus decadens plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and contributing to water filtration.
