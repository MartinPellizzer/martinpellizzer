Cryptocoryne crispatula var. yunnanensis

Cryptocoryne crispatula var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often curled or twisted.
