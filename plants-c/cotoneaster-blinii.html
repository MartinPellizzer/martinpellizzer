Cotoneaster blinii

Cotoneaster blinii, commonly known as the Blinii cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and compact growth habit.

Cotoneaster blinii is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil.
