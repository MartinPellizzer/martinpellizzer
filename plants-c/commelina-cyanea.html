Commelina cyanea

Commelina cyanea, commonly known as the blue commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features striking blue-violet flowers with three petals and three sepals, which are often mistaken for petals due to their size and color. The plant grows from a rhizomatous root system and typically thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Commelina cyanea is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display and ease of care.
