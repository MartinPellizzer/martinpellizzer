Colocasia boryi

Colocasia boryi, commonly known as the Bory's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other well-known genera like Calla and Dieffenbachia. The plant is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. It produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Colocasia boryi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage and unique flowering structure.
