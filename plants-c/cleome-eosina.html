Cleome eosina

Cleome eosina, commonly known as the Chinese spider flower, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its flowers have five petals and are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cleome eosina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
