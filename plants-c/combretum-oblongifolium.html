Combretum oblongifolium

Combretum oblongifolium, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red flowers that appear in dense clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite, and often elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and striking floral display make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
