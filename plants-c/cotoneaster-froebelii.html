Cotoneaster froebelii

Cotoneaster froebelii, commonly known as Froebel's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster froebelii is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
