Castanea fargesii

Castanea fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves and spiny husks enclosing its edible nuts. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, while its fruits provide food for various wildlife species.

Castanea fargesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
