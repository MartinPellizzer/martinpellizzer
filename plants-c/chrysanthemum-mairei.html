Chrysanthemum mairei

Chrysanthemum mairei, commonly known as the Maire's chrysanthemum, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, white, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
