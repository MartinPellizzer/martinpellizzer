Cistus × gardianus

Cistus × gardianus, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes species known for their aromatic foliage and showy flowers. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, adapted to thrive in dry, rocky soils.

Cistus × gardianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
