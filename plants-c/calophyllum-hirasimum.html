Calophyllum hirasimum

Calophyllum hirasimum, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, glossy green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. The fruit contains a single seed and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

It is also valued for its ornamental appearance and potential economic use in the pharmaceutical industry.
