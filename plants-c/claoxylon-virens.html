Claoxylon virens

Claoxylon virens, commonly known as the green flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale green bark and its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Claoxylon virens is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
