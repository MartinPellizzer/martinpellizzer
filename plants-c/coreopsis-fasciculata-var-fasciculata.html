Coreopsis fasciculata var. fasciculata

Coreopsis fasciculata var. fasciculata, commonly known as the narrowleaf coreopsis, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant yellow to orange daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in marshy areas or along stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wetland environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a useful plant for ecological restoration projects.
