Cypella yatayphila

Cypella yatayphila, commonly known as the Yatay palm, is a small evergreen tree native to the subtropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that develop into clustered, fibrous fruits. Its growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental use in tropical and subtropical gardens.

Cypella yatayphila is adapted to well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate to thrive.
