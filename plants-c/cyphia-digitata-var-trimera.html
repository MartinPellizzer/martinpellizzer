Cyphia digitata var. trimera

Cyphia digitata var. trimera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Cyphia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers.
