Cumbalu

Cumbalu, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers. Cumbalu thrives in well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.
