Crotonopsis argentea var. elliptica

Crotonopsis argentea var. elliptica, commonly known as silverleaf croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves with a waxy texture, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
