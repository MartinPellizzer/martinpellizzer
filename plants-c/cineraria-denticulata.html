Cineraria denticulata

Cineraria denticulata, commonly known as the toothed cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, toothed leaves and showy blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.

Cineraria denticulata is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
