Clematis huangjingensis

Clematis huangjingensis, commonly known as the Huangjingensis Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, typically blue or purple flowers. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces long, slender stems that can reach several meters in length.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
