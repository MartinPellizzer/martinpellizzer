Caroxylon Acanthocladum

Caroxylon acanthocladum is a rare and ancient plant species that thrives in the harsh conditions of the Namib Desert. This unique plant is known for its distinctive spiny leaves and ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems. It plays a vital role in the desert ecosystem by providing shelter and resources to various small animals. Despite its resilience, Caroxylon acanthocladum faces threats from climate change and human activity. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into the adaptations of desert flora.
