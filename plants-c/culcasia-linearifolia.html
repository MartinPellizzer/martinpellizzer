Culcasia linearifolia

Culcasia linearifolia, commonly known as the arrow leaf elephant's ear, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture and prominent veins. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.
