Cymbidium strictum

Cymbidium strictum, commonly known as the stiff cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, narrow pseudobulbs. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cymbidium strictum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
