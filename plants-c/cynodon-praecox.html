Cynodon praecox

Cynodon praecox, commonly known as early bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the warm temperate regions of the Americas.

It typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in spikelets, and it produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Cynodon praecox is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in ecological restoration projects.
