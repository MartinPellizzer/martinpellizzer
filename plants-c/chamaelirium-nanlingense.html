Chamaelirium nanlingense

Chamaelirium nanlingense, commonly known as the Nanling mountain gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, preferring well-drained soils. It has slender, herbaceous stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
