Calathea kerchoveana

Calathea kerchoveana, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, patterned leaves that change color and orientation throughout the day, a phenomenon known as nyctinasty. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Its foliage features intricate veining and a wide range of hues, including green, silver, and pink.

Calathea kerchoveana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.
