Cattleya gibeziae

Cattleya gibeziae, commonly known as the Macaranga Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

Cattleya gibeziae thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires good air circulation for optimal growth.
