Cytisus absinthioides var. grandiflorus

Cytisus absinthioides var. grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, often thorned stems and is characterized by its bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
