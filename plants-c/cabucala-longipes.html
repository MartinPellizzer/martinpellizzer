Cabucala longipes

Cabucala longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed cabucala, is a unique plant species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a dark green coloration. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a pale yellow or white hue.

This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forest understories.
