Columnea billbergiana

Columnea billbergiana, commonly known as the flame bell or red bell plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
