Calathea achira

Calathea achira, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that exhibit a unique pattern of coloration, often featuring deep green with contrasting silver or white markings. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Calathea achira prefers humid environments and well-draining soil to ensure optimal growth.
