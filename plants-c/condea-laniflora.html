Condea laniflora

Condea laniflora, commonly known as the woolly condea, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Condea laniflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
