Carissa spinarum

Carissa spinarum, commonly known as the Natal plume or Natal holly, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, glossy green leaves. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in warm climates.
