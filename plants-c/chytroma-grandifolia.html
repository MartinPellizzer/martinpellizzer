Chytroma grandifolia

Chytroma grandifolia, commonly known as the giant leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, frond-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture in its soil. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The botanical features of Chytroma grandifolia make it a striking addition to botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
