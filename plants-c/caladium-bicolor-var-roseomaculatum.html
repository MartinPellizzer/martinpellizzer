Caladium bicolor var. roseomaculatum

Caladium bicolor var. roseomaculatum, commonly known as the variegated caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with shades of white, pink, and green. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have a mottled or marbled appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens and as a houseplant for its vibrant, decorative leaves.
