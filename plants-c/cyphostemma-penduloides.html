Cyphostemma penduloides

Cyphostemma penduloides, commonly known as the hanging cyphostemma, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features thin, vine-like stems that can trail or climb, often reaching lengths of several meters. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, red berries. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and typically have a slightly waxy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various soil types.
