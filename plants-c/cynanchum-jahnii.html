Cynanchum jahnii

Cynanchum jahnii, commonly known as the yellow milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes many species of milkweeds. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.
