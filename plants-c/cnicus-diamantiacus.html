Cnicus diamantiacus

Cnicus diamantiacus, commonly known as the Diamondweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
