Crassula marnieriana

Crassula marnieriana, commonly known as the Marnier's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or gray tint. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months.

Crassula marnieriana is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
