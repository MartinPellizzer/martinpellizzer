Callista wardiana

Callista wardiana, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Callista wardiana is often cultivated as an ornamental tree due to its vibrant floral display.
