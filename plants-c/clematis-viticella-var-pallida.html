Clematis viticella var. pallida

Clematis viticella var. pallida, commonly known as the pale clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped, deeply lobed leaves that are typically a dark green with a pale underside. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale blue, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is known for its hardiness and is often used in garden borders or as a climbing plant on trellises.
