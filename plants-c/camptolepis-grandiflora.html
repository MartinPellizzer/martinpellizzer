Camptolepis grandiflora

Camptolepis grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered camptolepis, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Camptolepis grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in regions with suitable climates.
