Crotalaria alata

Crotalaria alata, commonly known as the winged bean, is a tropical leguminous plant characterized by its distinctive winged pods.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is native to Southeast Asia. The plant features compound leaves with stipules and produces yellow flowers that attract pollinators. Its seeds are encased in elongated, winged pods, which aid in seed dispersal.

Crotalaria alata is cultivated for its edible seeds and is also used in traditional medicine.
