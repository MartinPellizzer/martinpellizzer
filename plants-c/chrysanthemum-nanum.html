Chrysanthemum nanum

Chrysanthemum nanum, commonly known as the dwarf chrysanthemum, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features small, daisy-like flowers in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and orange, which bloom in the fall. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly serrated. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a versatile addition to gardens and floral arrangements.
