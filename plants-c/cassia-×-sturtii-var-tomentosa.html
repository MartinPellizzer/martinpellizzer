Cassia × sturtii var. tomentosa

Cassia × sturtii var. tomentosa, commonly known as the silky cassia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the legume family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

The variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
