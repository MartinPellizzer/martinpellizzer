Celtis kunmingensis

Celtis kunmingensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hackberry, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Ulmariales order and the Elaeocarpaceae family. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by spherical, green berries that mature to a dark purple color.

Celtis kunmingensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
