Carduus × durieuanus

Carduus × durieuanus, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. It has a rosette of basal leaves and a tall, branching stem that supports its composite flower heads.

Due to its ornamental value and potential for invasiveness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
