Crataegus × hafniensis

Crataegus × hafniensis, commonly known as the Hafnian hawthorn, is a hybrid plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the eastern United States and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. Its leaves are typically oval with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Crataegus × hafniensis is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
