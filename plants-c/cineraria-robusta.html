Cineraria robusta

Cineraria robusta, commonly known as the robust cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Cineraria robusta is relatively drought-tolerant and prefers full sun to partial shade.
