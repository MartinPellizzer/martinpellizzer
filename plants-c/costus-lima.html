Costus lima

Costus lima, commonly known as the white costus, is a tropical plant belonging to the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features tall, slender stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green. Its most distinguishing feature is the showy, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the stems.

Costus lima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
