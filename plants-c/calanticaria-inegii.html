Calanticaria inegii

Calanticaria inegii, commonly known as the woolly sandwort, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Calanticaria inegii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
