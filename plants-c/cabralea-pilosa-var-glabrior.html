Cabralea pilosa var. glabrior

Cabralea pilosa var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth pilosa.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Brazil. This variant is characterized by its reduced pubescence compared to the typical form, giving it a smoother appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months.

It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in regions with seasonal rainfall.
