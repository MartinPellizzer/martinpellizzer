Chenopodium murale f. albescens

Chenopodium murale f. albescens, commonly known as white goosefoot, is a variant of the common goosefoot plant.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features alternate, ovate to rhombic leaves that are typically covered in a fine, whitish coating. Its flowers are small and greenish, arranged in dense, terminal clusters.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.
