Cotoneaster horizontalis var. variegatus

Cotoneaster horizontalis var. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated horizontal cotoneaster, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves with white and green variegation, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries in the fall, which are a food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
