Centaurea carduiformis subsp. orientalis

Centaurea carduiformis subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental sicklecentaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its distinctive sickle-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. It is a perennial species that can reach heights of up to 60 cm, with a compact, upright growth habit.

The subspecies orientalis is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology compared to other subspecies of Centaurea carduiformis.
