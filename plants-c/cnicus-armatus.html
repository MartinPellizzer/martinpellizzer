Cnicus armatus

Cnicus armatus, commonly known as the sandspur or goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its prickly stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that are covered in stiff, bristly hairs, which aid in seed dispersal. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Cnicus armatus is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
