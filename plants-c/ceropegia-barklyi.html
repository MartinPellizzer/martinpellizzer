Ceropegia barklyi

Ceropegia barklyi, commonly known as the Lion's Head or the String of Hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with opposite, fleshy, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
