Cyclopia sessiliflora

Cyclopia sessiliflora, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cyclopia sessiliflora is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
