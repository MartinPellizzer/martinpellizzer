Copernicia × dahlgreniana

Copernicia × dahlgreniana, commonly known as the hybrid copernicia, is a species of palm native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in tropical climates.

The hybrid nature of this plant results from crosses between different Copernicia species, contributing to its unique botanical features.
