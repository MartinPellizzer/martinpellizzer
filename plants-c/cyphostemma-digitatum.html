Cyphostemma digitatum

Cyphostemma digitatum, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some regions.
