Cirsium dealbatum

Cirsium dealbatum, commonly known as the woolly thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its fuzzy, grayish foliage gives it a distinctive appearance, and it is part of the Asteraceae family.

Cirsium dealbatum is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
