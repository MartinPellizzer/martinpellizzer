Croton serratifolius var. rupestris

Croton serratifolius var. rupestris, commonly known as the rock croton, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often reddish or purple in color, with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
