Centaurea melitensis

Centaurea melitensis, commonly known as the Maltese thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a compact growth habit and can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.

Centaurea melitensis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
