Cytisus lindemannii

Cytisus lindemannii, commonly known as Lindemann's broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, often spiny stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
