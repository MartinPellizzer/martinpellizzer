Combretum latialatum

Combretum latialatum, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Combretum latialatum is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
