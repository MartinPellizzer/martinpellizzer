Coalisina angustifolia var. pteropoda

Coalisina angustifolia var. pteropoda, commonly known as the narrowleaf coalisina, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features slender, linear leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a delicate, greenish-white color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
