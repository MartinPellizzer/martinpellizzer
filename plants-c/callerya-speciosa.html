Callerya speciosa

Callerya speciosa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Callerya speciosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
