Corymborkis

Corymborkis, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its unique, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange. Corymborkis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking and unusual floral display.
