Callicarpa feddei

Callicarpa feddei, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant purple berries that appear in the fall, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

The berries are not only visually striking but also attract birds and other wildlife.
