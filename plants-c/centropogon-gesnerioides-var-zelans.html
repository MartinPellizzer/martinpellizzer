Centropogon gesnerioides var. zelans

Centropogon gesnerioides var. zelans, commonly known as the Zelans flame violet, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
