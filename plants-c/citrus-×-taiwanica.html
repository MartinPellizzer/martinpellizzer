Citrus × taiwanica

Citrus × taiwanica, commonly known as the Taiwan lemon.

It is a hybrid citrus plant native to Taiwan, characterized by its small, round, yellow-orange fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is known for its high vitamin C content and mild, tangy flavor.

It is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and as a source of edible fruit.
