Chenopodium triandrum var. lanuginosum

Chenopodium triandrum var. lanuginosum, commonly known as silky goosefoot, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

The species is noted for its adaptability to harsh conditions and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
