Connaropsis diversifolia

Connaropsis diversifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf connaraceae, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in panicle-like inflorescences, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is notable for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
