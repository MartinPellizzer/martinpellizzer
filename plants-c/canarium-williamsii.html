Canarium williamsii

Canarium williamsii, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small white flowers that bloom in winter, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky outcrops.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
