Cineraria linifolia

Cineraria linifolia, commonly known as the silky cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded conditions.

Cineraria linifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
