Conyza kirschsteineana

Conyza kirschsteineana, commonly known as the Kirschstein's weed, is an annual flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and open fields.
