Cupressus nutkatensis

Cupressus nutkatensis, commonly known as the Nuttall's cypress, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its pyramidal shape and scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its dense foliage makes it a valuable ornamental plant in horticulture.
