Campanula cashmeriana

Campanula cashmeriana, commonly known as the Kashmir bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula cashmeriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, bell-like flowers.
