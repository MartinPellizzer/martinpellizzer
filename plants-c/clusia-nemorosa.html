Clusia nemorosa

Clusia nemorosa, commonly known as the evergreen shrub or tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually yellow or greenish in color. Clusia nemorosa is known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments and its resistance to pests.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
