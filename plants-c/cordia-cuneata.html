Cordia cuneata

Cordia cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cordia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its simple, ovate to lanceolate leaves. It is valued for its ornamental potential and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
