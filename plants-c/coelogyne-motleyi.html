Coelogyne motleyi

Coelogyne motleyi, commonly known as the Motley's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of 5 to 10. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a waxy texture.

Coelogyne motleyi prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
