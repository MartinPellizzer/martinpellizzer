Coccocypselum hirsutum var. uniflorum

Coccocypselum hirsutum var. uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flowered coccocypselum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. The plant has hairy leaves and stems, which give it its specific epithet "hirsutum.". It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and ecological adaptations.
