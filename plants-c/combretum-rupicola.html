Combretum rupicola

Combretum rupicola, commonly known as the rock combretum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it a resilient species in its native habitat.
