Cynanchum otophyllum

Cynanchum otophyllum, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its erect stem, trifoliate leaves, and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in temperate climates.

Cynanchum otophyllum has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
