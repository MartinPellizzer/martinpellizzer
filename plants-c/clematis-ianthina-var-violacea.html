Clematis ianthina var. violacea

Clematis ianthina var. violacea, commonly known as the Violet Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five oval leaflets and produces small, cylindrical fruit capsules. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and graceful growth habit.
