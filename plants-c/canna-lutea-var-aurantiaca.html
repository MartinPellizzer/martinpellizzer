Canna lutea var. aurantiaca

Canna lutea var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange canna, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It features large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish or purple underside. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both landscape design and container gardening.
