Coleus venteri

Coleus venteri, commonly known as the red-stemmed coleus, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and exhibit a range of colors, including shades of red, purple, and green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
