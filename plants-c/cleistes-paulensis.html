Cleistes paulensis

Cleistes paulensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has a delicate appearance with narrow, grass-like leaves and a short, erect stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cleistes paulensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare and exotic plants.
