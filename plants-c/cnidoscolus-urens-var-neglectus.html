Cnidoscolus urens var. neglectus

Cnidoscolus urens var. neglectus, commonly known as the arrow leaf cucumber or arrowhead plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible fruits that resemble cucumbers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and edible properties, it is often cultivated in gardens and for traditional use.
