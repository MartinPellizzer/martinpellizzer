Citharexylum teres

Citharexylum teres, commonly known as the velvetleaf citharexylum, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas or near riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
