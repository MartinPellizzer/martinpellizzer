Carduus esdraelonicus

Carduus esdraelonicus, commonly known as the Esdraelon thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a robust root system that allows it to survive in harsh environmental conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
