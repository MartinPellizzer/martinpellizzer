Collabium chinense

Collabium chinense, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.
