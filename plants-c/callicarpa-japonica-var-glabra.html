Callicarpa japonica var. glabra

Callicarpa japonica var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth Japanese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

The berries are not only ornamental but also attract birds and other wildlife.
