Carex uluguruensis

Carex uluguruensis, commonly known as the Uluguru sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the Uluguru Mountains in Tanzania, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Due to its restricted distribution, Carex uluguruensis is considered a rare and potentially vulnerable species.
