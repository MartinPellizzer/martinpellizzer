Clidemia barbata

Clidemia barbata, commonly known as the hairy miconia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures. Clidemia barbata is also known for its ability to form dense thickets, which can impact local ecosystems.
