Cattleya alexandrae var. elegans

Cattleya alexandrae var. elegans, commonly known as the Alexandra's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species originating from Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a vibrant mix of pink and white hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces long, pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its colorful flowers.
