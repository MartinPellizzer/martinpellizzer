Cassia ochnacea

Cassia ochnacea, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds.

Cassia ochnacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
