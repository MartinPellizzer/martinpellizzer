Chenopodium × borbasii var. sedlecensis

Chenopodium × borbasii var. sedlecensis, commonly known as the Sedlecensis goosefoot, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically reddish or purplish in color. The plant produces small, green flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is native to Central Europe and is known for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

This variety is also valued in horticulture for its ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
