Cucubalus clavicarpus

Cucubalus clavicarpus, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces fruit with a star-shaped pattern on its skin. The fruit contains a juicy, sweet pulp surrounded by a fibrous layer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruit.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental features and ecological benefits.
