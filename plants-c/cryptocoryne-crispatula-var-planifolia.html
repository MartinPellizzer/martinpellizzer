Cryptocoryne crispatula var. planifolia

Cryptocoryne crispatula var. planifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly wavy along the edges. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is well-suited for planted aquariums. It has a rhizome that anchors it to the substrate and produces new shoots from its base.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in freshwater environments.
