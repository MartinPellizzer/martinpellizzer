Calothamnus lateralis f. rigidus

Calothamnus lateralis f. rigidus, commonly known as the rigid swamp everlasting, is a variant of the swamp everlasting plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, stiff stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in wet, swampy areas and is adapted to waterlogged soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This form is distinguished by its more rigid stem structure compared to the typical form of the species.
