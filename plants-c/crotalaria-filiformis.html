Crotalaria filiformis

Crotalaria filiformis, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions.

Crotalaria filiformis is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a useful plant in agricultural settings.
