Coelogyne flaccida var. crenulata

Coelogyne flaccida var. crenulata, commonly known as the wavy-leaf coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping inflorescences and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte on trees or rocks, and its leaves are often wavy or undulating in shape. It prefers warm, humid environments with good air circulation and partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
