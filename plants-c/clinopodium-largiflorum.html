Clinopodium largiflorum

Clinopodium largiflorum, commonly known as large-flowered wood betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces spikes of blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens and a valuable plant for ecological studies.
