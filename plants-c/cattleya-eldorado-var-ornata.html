Cattleya eldorado var. ornata

Cattleya eldorado var. ornata, commonly known as the Eldorado Orchid, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Cattleya genus.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers with vibrant pink and white petals, often adorned with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in seasonal dry periods.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
