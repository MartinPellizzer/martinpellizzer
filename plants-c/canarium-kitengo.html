Canarium kitengo

Canarium kitengo, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, orange-red fruits that are rich in nutrients. Its fruits are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of food.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
