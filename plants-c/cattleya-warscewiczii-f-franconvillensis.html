Cattleya warscewiczii f. franconvillensis

Cattleya warscewiczii f. franconvillensis, commonly known as the Franconville Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces pendulous blooms on tall, branching inflorescences, which are a striking feature of its appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and adaptability to cultivation.
