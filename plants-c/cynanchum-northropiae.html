Cynanchum northropiae

Cynanchum northropiae, commonly known as the Northrop's dogbane, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its showy, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in umbels and attract various pollinators.

Cynanchum northropiae is often found in the understory of forests and is valued for its ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
