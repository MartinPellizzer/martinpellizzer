Coelogyne flaccida var. longiracemosa

Coelogyne flaccida var. longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical forests. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant floral display.
