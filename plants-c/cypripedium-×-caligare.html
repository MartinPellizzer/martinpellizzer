Cypripedium × caligare

Cypripedium × caligare, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy pink or purple pouch-like flower that resembles a slipper. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats and has a rhizomatous root system. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by bees, and it produces small, dark brown seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
