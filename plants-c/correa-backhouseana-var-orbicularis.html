Correa backhouseana var. orbicularis

Correa backhouseana var. orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular correa, is a compact shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, glossy, orbicular (rounded) leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its evergreen foliage and showy blooms.
