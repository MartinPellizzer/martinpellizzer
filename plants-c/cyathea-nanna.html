Cyathea nanna

Cyathea nanna, commonly known as the dwarf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its compact stature, typically growing to a height of less than two meters. The plant features a stout, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a soft, light green color and a distinct, elegant appearance.

Cyathea nanna thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
