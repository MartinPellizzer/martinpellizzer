Chamaecrista deltoidea

Chamaecrista deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fibrous membrane. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Chamaecrista deltoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
