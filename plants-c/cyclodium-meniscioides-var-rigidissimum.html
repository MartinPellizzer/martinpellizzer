Cyclodium meniscioides var. rigidissimum

Cyclodium meniscioides var. rigidissimum, commonly known as the rigid thornweed, is a perennial shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with rigid, often branching stems that are covered in fine, woolly hairs. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often recurved, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cyclodium meniscioides var. rigidissimum is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with low rainfall and high temperatures.
