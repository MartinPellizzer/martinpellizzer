Croton sellowianus f. brevifolius

Croton sellowianus f. brevifolius, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a compact shrub native to Brazil.

It features striking, variegated leaves with a mix of green, yellow, and white patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
