Coelogyne edelfeldtii

Coelogyne edelfeldtii, commonly known as the Edelfeldt's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne edelfeldtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
