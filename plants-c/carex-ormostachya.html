Carex ormostachya

Carex ormostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sedge.

It is a perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and cylindrical. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are often clustered in a dense inflorescence.

Carex ormostachya is often found in moist, shady habitats such as woodlands and wetlands.
