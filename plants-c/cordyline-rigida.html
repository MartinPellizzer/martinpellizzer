Cordyline rigida

Cordyline rigida, commonly known as the rigid cordyline, is a striking evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are often a deep green with red or purple markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a fibrous root system and can grow up to several meters in height, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Cordyline rigida is known for its resilience and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
