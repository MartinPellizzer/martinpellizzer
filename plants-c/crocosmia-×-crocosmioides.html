Crocosmia × crocosmioides

Crocosmia × crocosmioides, commonly known as the Montbretia, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Iridaceae and is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-shaped, sword-like leaves that are typically bright green and emit a mild scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Crocosmia × crocosmioides is known for its ability to naturalize and spread through rhizomes, creating dense foliage and vibrant floral displays.
