Coelogyne alata

Coelogyne alata, commonly known as the flying orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne alata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
