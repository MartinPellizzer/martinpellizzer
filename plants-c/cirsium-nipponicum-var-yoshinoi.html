Cirsium nipponicum var. yoshinoi

Cirsium nipponicum var. yoshinoi, commonly known as Yoshino's thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are covered in dense, bristly hairs, and its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Yoshino's thistle is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
