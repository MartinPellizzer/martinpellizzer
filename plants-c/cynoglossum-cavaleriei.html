Cynoglossum cavaleriei

Cynoglossum cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese ironweed, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
