Collinsia violacea f. pallida

Collinsia violacea f. pallida, commonly known as the pale violet.

This perennial herb is native to the western United States, particularly California. It features delicate, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pale lavender to white. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in open woodland or meadow habitats.

Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
