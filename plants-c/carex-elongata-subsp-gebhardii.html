Carex elongata subsp. gebhardii

Carex elongata subsp.

gebhardii, commonly known as the Gebhard's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, rushes, and grass-like plants.

This subspecies is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of brownish flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows.
