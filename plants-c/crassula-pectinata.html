Crassula pectinata

Crassula pectinata, commonly known as the Jelly Bean Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, round leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula pectinata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and easy care requirements.
