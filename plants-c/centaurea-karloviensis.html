Centaurea karloviensis

Centaurea karloviensis, commonly known as Karlovy Vary centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Czech Republic.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, composite flower heads.
