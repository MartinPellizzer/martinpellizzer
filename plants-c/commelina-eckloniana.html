Commelina eckloniana

Commelina eckloniana, commonly known as the blue spire or blue star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy habitats. The plant features striking blue-violet flowers that are clustered in umbels, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, which helps in water retention.

Commelina eckloniana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist environments.
