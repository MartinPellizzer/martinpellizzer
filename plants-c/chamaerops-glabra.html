Chamaerops glabra

Chamaerops glabra, commonly known as the slender date palm, is a species of palm native to southern Europe, North Africa, and the Middle East.

It is a small to medium-sized palm with a slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are often feather-like in appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible dates, though the fruit is not as prominent as in other date palm species. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to various climates.

The plant is also valued for its ability to thrive in coastal and arid environments.
