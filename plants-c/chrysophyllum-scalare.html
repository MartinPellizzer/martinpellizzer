Chrysophyllum scalare

Chrysophyllum scalare, commonly known as the Natal plum or star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa, the Caribbean, and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy green leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits with a yellow or orange skin and a sweet, juicy pulp containing edible seeds.
