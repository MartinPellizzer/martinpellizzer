Cotoneaster cooperi

Cotoneaster cooperi, commonly known as the Cooper's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by red berries in the fall. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Cotoneaster cooperi is also valued for its role in wildlife habitats, providing food for birds and small mammals.
