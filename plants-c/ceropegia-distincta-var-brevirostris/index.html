Ceropegia Distincta Var. Brevirostris

Ceropegia distincta var. brevirostris is a rare and delicate plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its unique, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. This species thrives in shady, humid environments and requires careful watering to avoid root rot. Its foliage is characterized by thin, trailing stems and small, waxy leaves. Due to its beauty and rarity, it is a popular choice among collectors and enthusiasts of rare plants.
