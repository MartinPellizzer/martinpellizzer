Cicerbita glabra

Cicerbita glabra, commonly known as the smooth bittercress, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are smooth and oval-shaped, while its small white or yellow flowers bloom in clusters.

The seeds are enclosed in a capsule and are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in natural habitats.
