Christensonella uncata

Christensonella uncata, commonly known as the slender bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that form bladder-like structures used to trap small aquatic organisms. Its unique morphology and carnivorous adaptation make it a fascinating subject in botanical studies.
