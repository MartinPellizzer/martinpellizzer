Clethra sprucei

Clethra sprucei, commonly known as the Spruce clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Clethra sprucei is often used in woodland gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
