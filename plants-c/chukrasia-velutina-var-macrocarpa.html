Chukrasia velutina var. macrocarpa

Chukrasia velutina var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The tree has a smooth, greyish-brown bark and produces long, slender pods containing flat, winged seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a velvety texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 30 meters tall.
