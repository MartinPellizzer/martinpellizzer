Cleome omanensis

Cleome omanensis, commonly known as the Oman cleome, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant features simple, alternate leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. It belongs to the family Cleomaceae, which includes other flowering plants with similar characteristics.

Cleome omanensis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal and inland regions of Oman.
