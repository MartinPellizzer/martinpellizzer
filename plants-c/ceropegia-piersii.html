Ceropegia piersii

Ceropegia piersii, commonly known as the Piersii String of Hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia piersii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
