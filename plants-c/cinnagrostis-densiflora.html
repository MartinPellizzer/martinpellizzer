Cinnagrostis densiflora

Cinnagrostis densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. Cinnagrostis densiflora is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local pollinator populations.
