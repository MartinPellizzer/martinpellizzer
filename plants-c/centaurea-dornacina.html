Centaurea dornacina

Centaurea dornacina, commonly known as the Dornacina thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Centaurea dornacina is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
