Chamaecytisus austriacus subsp. stefanoffii

Chamaecytisus austriacus subsp.

stefanoffii, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a shrub native to the Alps. It features slender, thorny stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or open woodland habitats.
