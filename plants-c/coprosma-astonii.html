Coprosma astonii

Coprosma astonii, commonly known as the Kauai coprosma, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage, which is typically a deep green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red or orange berries that are a key feature of its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Coprosma astonii is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and its use in traditional Hawaiian practices.
