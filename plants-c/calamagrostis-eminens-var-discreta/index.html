Calamagrostis Eminens Var. Discreta

Calamagrostis eminens var. discreta is a striking ornamental grass known for its tall, feathery plumes and elegant appearance. Native to the wetlands and marshes of eastern North America, this plant thrives in moist to wet soil conditions. Its unique varietal form, discreta, stands out with its more compact growth and softer, bluer foliage compared to the standard variety. Often used in garden design for its visual interest and ability to add movement with the wind, it is a favorite among landscapers and botanists alike. This plant not only enhances the aesthetic of natural and cultivated spaces but also supports local wildlife by providing habitat and food.
