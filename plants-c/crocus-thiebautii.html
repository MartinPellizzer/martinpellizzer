Crocus thiebautii

Crocus thiebautii, commonly known as the Thiebaut's crocus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Iridaceae.

It is native to southern Europe, particularly found in regions such as Italy, Spain, and France. This plant is characterized by its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The flowers are usually pale yellow to orange in color, with a distinctive, rounded shape.
