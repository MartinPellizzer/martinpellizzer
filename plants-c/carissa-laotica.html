Carissa laotica

Carissa laotica, commonly known as the Laos carissa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a yellow, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

The leaves are glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture.
