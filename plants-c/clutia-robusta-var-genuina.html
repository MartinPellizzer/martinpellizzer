Clutia robusta var. genuina

Clutia robusta var. genuina, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Clutia robusta var. genuina is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
