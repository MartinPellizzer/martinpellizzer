Cypripedium × huybrechtsianum

Cypripedium × huybrechtsianum, commonly known as the Huybrechts lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, typically pink or purple in color. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a fan-like pattern, and it thrives in shaded, moist forest environments.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
