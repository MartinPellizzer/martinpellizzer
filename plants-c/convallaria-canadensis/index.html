Convallaria Canadensis

Convallaria canadensis, also known as Canadian lily, is a striking plant native to eastern North America. It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, adding beauty to woodland gardens. This perennial plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forested areas. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts. However, it contains toxic compounds, requiring caution when handling or consuming its parts.
