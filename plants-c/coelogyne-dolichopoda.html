Coelogyne dolichopoda

Coelogyne dolichopoda, commonly known as the long-lobed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and striking, often fragrant flowers that can be pink, white, or yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Coelogyne dolichopoda is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
