Centaurea × maramuresensis

Centaurea × maramuresensis, commonly known as the Maramures star-thistle, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Carpathian Mountains in Romania.

It is characterized by its spiny, bract-like leaves and striking purple to pink flower heads that resemble stars. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to temperate climates. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its robust growth habit.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological resilience, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
