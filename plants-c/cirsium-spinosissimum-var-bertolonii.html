Cirsium spinosissimum var. bertolonii

Cirsium spinosissimum var. bertolonii, commonly known as the Bertoloni's thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, purple-blue flower heads that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, prickly hairs. This variety is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other thistle species.

It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.
