Cephalaria leucantha var. scabra

Cephalaria leucantha var. scabra, commonly known as the rough cephalaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in rough, hairy texture, giving it its varietal name "scabra.". The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly rough or scabrous surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
