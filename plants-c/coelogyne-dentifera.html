Coelogyne dentifera

Coelogyne dentifera, commonly known as the toothed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with red or purple markings. The plant has erect, hairy pseudobulbs and produces linear, leathery leaves. It blooms during the rainy season, often in clusters of 3 to 12 flowers.

Coelogyne dentifera is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
