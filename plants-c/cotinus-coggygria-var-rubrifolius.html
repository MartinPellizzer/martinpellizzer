Cotinus coggygria var. rubrifolius

Cotinus coggygria var. rubrifolius, commonly known as the red leaf smoke bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery panicles that produce pink to purple flowers in late summer. The foliage is a striking feature, turning vibrant red in the fall, which adds ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

This plant is also valued for its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
