Curcuma gracillima

Curcuma gracillima, commonly known as slender turmeric, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers. Its rhizomes, which are underground stems, are the primary part used for its medicinal and culinary properties.

Curcuma gracillima is valued for its potential health benefits, similar to other curcuma species.
