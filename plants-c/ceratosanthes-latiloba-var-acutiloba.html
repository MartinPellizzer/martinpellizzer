Ceratosanthes latiloba var. acutiloba

Ceratosanthes latiloba var. acutiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved candlewood, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy surface. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
