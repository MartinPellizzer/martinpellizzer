Codonopsis viridiflora var. chlorocodon

Codonopsis viridiflora var. chlorocodon, commonly known as the green-flowered codonopsis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically features lanceolate leaves with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained, loamy soils.
