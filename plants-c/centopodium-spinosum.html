Centopodium spinosum

Centopodium spinosum, commonly known as the spiny centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its spiny stems and small, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Centopodium spinosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
