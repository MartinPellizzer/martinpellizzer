Crassula robusta

Crassula robusta, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Christmas Jade, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Crassula robusta is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
