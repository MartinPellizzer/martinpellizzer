Corybas acutus

Corybas acutus, commonly known as the acute helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower that is typically green or yellowish in color. The plant produces a single, erect spike bearing several small, hooded flowers. Corybas acutus thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas.

It is a rare and protected species, reflecting its ecological significance in New Zealand's flora.
