Columnea calotricha var. breviflora

Columnea calotricha var. breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered flame vine, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing plant characterized by its vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract hummingbirds.
