Croton insignis

Croton insignis, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that give way to orange or red fruits containing numerous seeds. Croton insignis thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical gardens.
