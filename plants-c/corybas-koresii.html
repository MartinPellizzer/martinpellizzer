Corybas koresii

Corybas koresii, commonly known as the Koresii helmet orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the island of Keros in the Hellenic Seas.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower that is typically deep purple or reddish in color. The plant has a short, stocky rhizome and produces a single, showy flower per stem. Its botanical features include a fleshy, greenish-brown scape and specialized structures called resupinate petals and sepals.

Corybas koresii is classified within the tribe Epidendrieae and is closely related to other helmet orchids found in the Mediterranean region.
