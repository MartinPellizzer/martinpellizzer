Calamagrostis holmii

Calamagrostis holmii, commonly known as Holm's reed, is a perennial grass native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, riverbanks, and wet meadows. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

The inflorescence consists of a panicle of spikelets, which produce small, spikelet-based flowers.
