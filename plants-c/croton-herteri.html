Croton herteri

Croton herteri, commonly known as the Herter's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that can range from green to red, orange, and even purple. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
