Crotalaria renieriana

Crotalaria renieriana, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with distinctive spurred petals, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with trifoliate or palmate arrangements.

Crotalaria renieriana is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and grasslands.
