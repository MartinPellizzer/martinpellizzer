Croixia Semaram

The croixia semaram is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern europe. it is known for its striking blue flowers that bloom in early summer. this species is characterized by its narrow leaves and compact growth habit. due to habitat loss and climate change, it is now considered endangered. conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this unique botanical treasure.
