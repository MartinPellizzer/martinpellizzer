Croton procumbens

Croton procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate croton, is a low-growing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features vibrant, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its rapid growth and striking foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking bold visual impact.
