Cimicifuga lancifoliolata

Cimicifuga lancifoliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bugbane, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of white, lace-like flowers in late summer. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves with lanceolate lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
