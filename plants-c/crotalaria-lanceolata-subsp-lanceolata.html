Crotalaria lanceolata subsp. lanceolata

Crotalaria lanceolata subsp.

lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow, spurred flowers.

The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a woody structure with compound leaves. It is often found in open woodlands and disturbed soils, thriving in warm climates.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods and can be dispersed by wind or animals.
