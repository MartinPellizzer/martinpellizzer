Canavalia megalantha var. falanruwiae

Canavalia megalantha var. falanruwiae, commonly known as the Koa tree, is a flowering plant native to Hawaii.

It is a legume with compound leaves and produces distinctive yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its sturdy, woody stem and can grow into a small tree or shrub. It plays an important ecological role in Hawaiian forests, providing habitat and food for native wildlife.

The seeds of this species are also used in traditional Hawaiian practices.
