Cnidoscolus lobatus

Cnidoscolus lobatus, commonly known as the arrow leaf balsam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with lobed margins. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
