Colchicum × floribundum

Colchicum × floribundum, commonly known as the hybrid colchicum or garden colchicum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces striking, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant features large, waxy leaves that are often broader than those of its parent species. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of flower colors and forms, enhancing its ornamental value.
