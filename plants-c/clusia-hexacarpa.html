Clusia hexacarpa

Clusia hexacarpa, commonly known as the waxplant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by bright orange, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
