Callista rigida

Callista rigida, commonly known as the rigid callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercially available.
