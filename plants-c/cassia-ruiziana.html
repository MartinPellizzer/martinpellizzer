Cassia ruiziana

Cassia ruiziana, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia ruiziana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
