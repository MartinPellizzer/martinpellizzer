Colchicum Bertolonii Var. Angustifolium

Colchicum bertolonii var. angustifolium is a striking member of the Colchicaceae family known for its vibrant autumn blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and limestone soils. Its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy purple flowers make it a unique addition to alpine gardens. Despite its beauty, it is considered rare and requires specific growing conditions to flourish. This plant holds both ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
