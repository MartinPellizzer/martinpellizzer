Cardamine siifolia

Cardamine siifolia, commonly known as Siifoliate Watercress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small white or yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and forest floors.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional medicine.
