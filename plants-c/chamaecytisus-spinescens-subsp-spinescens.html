Chamaecytisus spinescens subsp. spinescens

Chamaecytisus spinescens subsp.

spinescens, commonly known as the spiny broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dense, spiny branches and small, leathery leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky habitats.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
