Croton pilargyros

Croton pilargyros, commonly known as the golden croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red and orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal.
