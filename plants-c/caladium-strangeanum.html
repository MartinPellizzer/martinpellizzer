Caladium strangeanum

Caladium strangeanum, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a pale green background. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Caladium strangeanum is typically grown as an annual in regions with cold winters.
