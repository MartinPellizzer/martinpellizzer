Cleretum criniflorum

Cleretum criniflorum, commonly known as the silkyflower cleretum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and showy, fluffy white flowers that resemble clouds. The plant produces small, green, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Cleretum criniflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown for its unique, cloud-like floral display.
