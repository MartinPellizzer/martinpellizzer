Cliffortia nivenioides

Cliffortia nivenioides, commonly known as the Niven's cliffortia, is a South African flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive dark purple or brownish base. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often on mountain slopes or in fynbos vegetation. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces legumes as its fruit, which contain the seeds.

Cliffortia nivenioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
