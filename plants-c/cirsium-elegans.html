Cirsium elegans

Cirsium elegans, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It features spiny, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, typically from July to September. The plant has a robust root system and hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, prickly hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Cirsium elegans is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.
