Calliprena Cernua

Cape ivy, scientifically known as Calliprena cernua, is a fast-growing evergreen vine native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, trailing foliage and ability to cover large areas quickly. Often used for erosion control and as a decorative plant, it thrives in moist, shady environments. Despite its beauty, it can become invasive if not managed properly. Its popularity stems from its adaptability and aesthetic appeal in landscaping.
