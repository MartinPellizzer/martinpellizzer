Cineraria fulva

Cineraria fulva, commonly known as the yellow cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has silvery-green, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Cineraria fulva is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
