Crossandra buntingii

Crossandra buntingii, commonly known as the Bunting's crossandra, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
