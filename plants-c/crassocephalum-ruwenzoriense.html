Crassocephalum ruwenzoriense

Crassocephalum ruwenzoriense, commonly known as the Ruwenzori sunflower, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its large, yellow-orange daisy-like flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is typically found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many familiar flowering plants.
