Cyrtochilum compactum

Cyrtochilum compactum, commonly known as the compact orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or white, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Cyrtochilum compactum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
