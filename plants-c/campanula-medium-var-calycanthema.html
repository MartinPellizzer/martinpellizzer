Campanula medium var. calycanthema

Campanula medium var. calycanthema, commonly known as the broad-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Campanula medium var. calycanthema is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and natural landscapes.
