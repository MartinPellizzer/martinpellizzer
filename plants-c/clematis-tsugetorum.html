Clematis tsugetorum

Clematis tsugetorum, commonly known as the Japanese Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are typically a dark green color. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its stems are woody and can grow quite long, allowing it to climb and cover structures effectively.

Clematis tsugetorum prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
