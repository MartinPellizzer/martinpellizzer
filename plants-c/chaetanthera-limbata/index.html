Chaetanthera Limbata

Chaetanthera limbata is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its unique, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This species is often found in shaded, moist forest understories where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
