Cytisus Stenopetalus

Cytisus stenopetalus is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This shrub thrives in rocky soils and harsh climates, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem. Its unique appearance and limited distribution have made it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this species is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its natural habitat.
