Cassia lundii

Cassia lundii, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping panicles of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces oblong, woody pods that contain flat, spiral-shaped seeds. Cassia lundii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm, humid climates.
