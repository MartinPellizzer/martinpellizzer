Combretum eugeneanum

Combretum eugeneanum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often resembling flames, hence its common name. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The bark is rough and fissured, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to withstand drought conditions.
