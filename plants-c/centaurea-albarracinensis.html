Centaurea albarracinensis

Centaurea albarracinensis, commonly known as the Albarracin thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has silver-green, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and open grasslands.

Centaurea albarracinensis is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
