Cyperus Squarrosus Var. Runyonii

Cyperus squarrosus var. runyonii is a striking wetland plant known for its unique, fluffy appearance and adaptability to various aquatic environments. This variant of Cyperus squarrosus features dense, white to pale pink bracts that create a soft, cloud-like effect, making it visually appealing. It is often found in marshes, ponds, and slow-moving water bodies across the southeastern United States. The plant plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and stabilizing soil. Its ornamental value has also made it a popular choice for water gardeners and landscape designers.
