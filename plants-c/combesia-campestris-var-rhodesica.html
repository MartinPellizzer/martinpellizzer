Combesia campestris var. rhodesica

Combesia campestris var. rhodesica, commonly known as the Rhodesian combesia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of lanceolate, glaucous leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tall, branching stems that bear clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the Asteraceae family.
