Costus productus

Costus productus, commonly known as the zigzag ginger, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems and narrow, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically in shades of yellow or orange.
