Ceratotheca reniformis

Ceratotheca reniformis, commonly known as the kidney bean plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, kidney-shaped pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in culinary applications. It is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.

The plant's adaptability to various soil types and climates contributes to its widespread cultivation.
