Caiophora fiebrigii

Caiophora fiebrigii, commonly known as the golden candelabra, is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, branching structure and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, known as bloom. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
