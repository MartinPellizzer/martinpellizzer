Cynoglossum alticola

Cynoglossum alticola, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It has a basal rosette of leaves and slender stems that support its delicate flowers.

Cynoglossum alticola is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to cold, high-altitude climates.
