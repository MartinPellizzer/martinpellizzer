Cordyline lineata var. doucetii

Cordyline lineata var. doucetii, commonly known as the Red-veined Dracaena, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves with bright red veins contrasting against its deep green foliage. The plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its upright growth habit makes it an excellent choice for borders or as a specimen plant in gardens.
