Cineraria lanceolata var. candida

Cineraria lanceolata var. candida, commonly known as the white cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
