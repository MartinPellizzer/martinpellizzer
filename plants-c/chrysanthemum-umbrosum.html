Chrysanthemum umbrosum

Chrysanthemum umbrosum, commonly known as the dark chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
