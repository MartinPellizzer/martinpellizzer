Chlorophytum asphodeloides

Chlorophytum asphodeloides, commonly known as the asphodel lily, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The plant produces tall spikes of small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and delicate blooms.
