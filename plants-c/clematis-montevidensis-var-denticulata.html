Clematis montevidensis var. denticulata

Clematis montevidensis var. denticulata, commonly known as the denticulate clematis, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It features compound leaves with finely toothed margins and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue and purple. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires support for its vigorous growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The variety is distinguished by its finely serrated leaves and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
