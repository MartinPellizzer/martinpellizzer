Cactus odierianus

Cactus odierianus, commonly known as the Odiern's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its columnar stem, which is often ribbed and covered in spines for protection. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. It has a shallow root system adapted to absorb water quickly from infrequent rains.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is well-suited for xeriscaping and desert gardens.
