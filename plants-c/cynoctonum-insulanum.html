Cynoctonum insulanum

Cynoctonum insulanum, commonly known as the island hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a prominent yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or near water bodies.

Cynoctonum insulanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
