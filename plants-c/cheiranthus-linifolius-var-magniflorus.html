Cheiranthus linifolius var. magniflorus

Cheiranthus linifolius var. magniflorus, commonly known as the showy wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and long flowering period.
