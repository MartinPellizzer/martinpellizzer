Crassocephalum diversifolium var. polycephalum

Crassocephalum diversifolium var. polycephalum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of velvety, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
