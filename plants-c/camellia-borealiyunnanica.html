Camellia borealiyunnanica

Camellia borealiyunnanica, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to southwestern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white or yellow base. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Camellia borealiyunnanica is also valued for its potential in horticulture and botanical research due to its unique floral characteristics.
