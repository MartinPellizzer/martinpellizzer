Combretum pierrei

Combretum pierrei, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often pubescent, and its bark is smooth and reddish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Combretum pierrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
