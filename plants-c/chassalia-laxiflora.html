Chassalia laxiflora

Chassalia laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered chassalia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Chassalia laxiflora is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
