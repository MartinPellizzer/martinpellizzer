Cassia sophera var. torulosa

Cassia sophera var. torulosa, commonly known as golden shower tree or *Cassia torulosa*, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping inflorescences that bear clusters of bright yellow, pea-like flowers. The leaves are bipinnate, with numerous small leaflets that give the plant a feathery appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal and disturbed areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
