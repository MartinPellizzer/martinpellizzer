Cliffortia berberidifolia

Cliffortia berberidifolia, commonly known as the yellow-flowered carpetbunch, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in spring, which are borne on slender, branching stems. Its leaves are small, elliptical, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

It is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
