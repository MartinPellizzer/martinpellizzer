Colax barringtoniae

Colax barringtoniae, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of multiple oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Colax barringtoniae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrant blossoms.
