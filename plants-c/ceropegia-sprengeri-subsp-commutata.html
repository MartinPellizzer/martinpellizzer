Ceropegia sprengeri subsp. commutata

Ceropegia sprengeri subsp.

commutata, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, heart-shaped leaves.

commutata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
