Chiloglottis cornuta

Chiloglottis cornuta, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like spur at the base of its flowers, which is a key botanical feature. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a single, prominent lip.

Chiloglottis cornuta is known for its specialized pollination mechanism, relying on specific insect species for reproduction.
