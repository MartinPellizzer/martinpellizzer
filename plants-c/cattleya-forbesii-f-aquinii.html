Cattleya forbesii f. aquinii

Cattleya forbesii f. aquinii, commonly known as the Aquinii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces large, showy blooms in shades of pink and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
