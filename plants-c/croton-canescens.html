Croton canescens

Croton canescens, commonly known as the white croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that often display a variety of colors, including green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Croton canescens is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
