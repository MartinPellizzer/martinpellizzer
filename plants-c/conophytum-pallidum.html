Conophytum pallidum

Conophytum pallidum, commonly known as the pale conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Conophytum pallidum is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
