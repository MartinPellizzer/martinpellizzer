Cyphomandra sciadostylis var. trichocarpa

Cyphomandra sciadostylis var. trichocarpa, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its hairy stems and dark purple, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, berry-like fruits. Its foliage and fruit are often used in traditional medicine and culinary practices.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
