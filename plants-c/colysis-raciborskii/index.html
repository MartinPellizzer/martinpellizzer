Colysis Raciborskii

Colysis raciborskii is a unique freshwater cyanobacterium known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich environments. This organism is often found in lakes and reservoirs where it can form dense surface blooms. It is capable of producing toxins that can pose risks to both aquatic life and human health. Despite its potential dangers, it plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat. Understanding Colysis raciborskii is essential for managing water quality and preventing harmful algal blooms.
