Crinum caffrum

Crinum caffrum, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, straight stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy white or pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are strap-shaped and often have a waxy texture.

Crinum caffrum is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils.
