Coelogyne lacinulosa

Coelogyne lacinulosa, commonly known as the lace-leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated pseudobulbs and long, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with intricate markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne lacinulosa is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
