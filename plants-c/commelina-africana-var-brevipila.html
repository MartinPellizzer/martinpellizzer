Commelina africana var. brevipila

Commelina africana var. brevipila, commonly known as the African spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stem and heart-shaped leaves.
