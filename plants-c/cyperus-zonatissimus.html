Cyperus zonatissimus

Cyperus zonatissimus, commonly known as the zonate flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant features slender, triangular stems and produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale blue or white flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

Cyperus zonatissimus is often found in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies.
