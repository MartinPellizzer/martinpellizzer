Carex borealihinganica

Carex borealihinganica, commonly known as the Arctic sedge, is a perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance and spike-like inflorescences. Carex borealihinganica plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small Arctic organisms.
