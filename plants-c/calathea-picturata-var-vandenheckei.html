Calathea picturata var. vandenheckei

Calathea picturata var. vandenheckei, commonly known as the Vandenheckei Calathea, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, ornate foliage with intricate patterns of deep green, silver, and cream. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often featuring a waxy texture and a velvety sheen. This variety thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
