Cryptocoryne axelrodii

Cryptocoryne axelrodii, commonly known as Axelrod's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green in color. This plant thrives in low-light conditions and is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments. It is popular in aquariums due to its hardy nature and ability to grow in a variety of water conditions.

The plant's rhizome system allows it to spread and form dense underwater mats.
