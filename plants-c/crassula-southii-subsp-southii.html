Crassula southii subsp. southii

Crassula southii subsp.

southii, commonly known as the Cape Town crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
