Coburgia Quitensis

Coburgia, scientifically known as Coburgia quitensis, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America. This unique species belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton, and is characterized by its vibrant, showy blooms. Its striking appearance has made it a subject of interest among botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, Coburgia quitensis is highly threatened due to habitat loss and over-collection. Understanding and protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystem.
