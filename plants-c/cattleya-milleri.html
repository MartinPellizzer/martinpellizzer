Cattleya milleri

Cattleya milleri, commonly known as the Purple Lady, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant features long, arching inflorescences and is prized for its vibrant, fragrant blossoms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya milleri is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
