Calamus leptostachys

Calamus leptostachys, commonly known as the slender-stalked calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, feather-like leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.
