Cheirostylis schliebenii

Cheirostylis schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown.

Cheirostylis schliebenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
