Cyrtanthus pondoensis

Cyrtanthus pondoensis, commonly known as the Pondo bulbous lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous underground stems. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cyrtanthus pondoensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
