Colchicum leptanthum

Colchicum leptanthum, commonly known as the slender autumn crocus, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces striking violet-blue flowers in the autumn, typically from September to November. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous corm. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
