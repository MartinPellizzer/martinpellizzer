Carex stans

Carex stans, commonly known as the upright sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. Carex stans typically grows in moist to wet environments such as marshes, bogs, and wet meadows. Its leaves are stiff and grass-like, with a triangular cross-section characteristic of sedges.
