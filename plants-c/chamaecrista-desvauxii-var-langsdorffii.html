Chamaecrista desvauxii var. langsdorffii

Chamaecrista desvauxii var. langsdorffii, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
