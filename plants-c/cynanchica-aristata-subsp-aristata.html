Cynanchica aristata subsp. aristata

Cynanchica aristata subsp.

aristata, commonly known as the arrowleaf milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the western United States. It is characterized by its slender stems and trifoliate leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and sagebrush steppe.

The species is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water absorption.
