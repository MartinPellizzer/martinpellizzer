Croton neuwiedii

Croton neuwiedii, commonly known as the Neuwied croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that often display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often spiny members. Croton neuwiedii produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsules containing seeds.
