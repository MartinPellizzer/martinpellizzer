Clematis peterae var. mollis

Clematis peterae var. mollis, commonly known as the soft Clematis, is a flowering vine native to China.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in partial shade to full sun. Its foliage is heart-shaped and often has a velvety texture.

Clematis peterae var. mollis is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
