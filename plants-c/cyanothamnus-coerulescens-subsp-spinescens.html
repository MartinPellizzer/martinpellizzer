Cyanothamnus coerulescens subsp. spinescens

Cyanothamnus coerulescens subsp.

spinescens, commonly known as the blue daisy bush, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and produces striking blue or purple flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its spiny stems and leaves, which provide a protective feature against herbivory. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
