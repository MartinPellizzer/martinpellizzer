Cusparia nodosa

Cusparia nodosa, commonly known as the clustered spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple, and it produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular ornamental plant in xeriscaping.
