Conophytum stephanii subsp. helmutii

Conophytum stephanii subsp.

helmutii, commonly known as the Helmut's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
