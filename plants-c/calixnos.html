Calixnos

Calixnos, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a unique species native to arid regions.

It features striking silver-green foliage that helps reduce water loss through reflective surfaces. The plant has small, waxy leaves that are adapted to thrive in hot, dry climates. It produces delicate white flowers during the summer months.

Calixnos is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
