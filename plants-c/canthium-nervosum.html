Canthium nervosum

Canthium nervosum, commonly known as the hairy spurge or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its alternate, simple leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs.
