Commelina nipponica

Commelina nipponica, commonly known as Japanese spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
