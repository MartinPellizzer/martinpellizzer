Cotyledon attenuata

Cotyledon attenuata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a powdery white coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Cotyledon attenuata is drought-tolerant and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
