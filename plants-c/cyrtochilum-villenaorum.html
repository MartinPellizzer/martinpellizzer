Cyrtochilum villenaorum

Cyrtochilum villenaorum, commonly known as the Villena's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered in racemes. Its roots are specialized for epiphytic growth, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Cyrtochilum villenaorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
