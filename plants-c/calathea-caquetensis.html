Calathea caquetensis

Calathea caquetensis, commonly known as the caquetensis calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America, specifically Colombia.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, often displaying shades of green, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and requires consistent moisture in the soil.

Calathea caquetensis is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
