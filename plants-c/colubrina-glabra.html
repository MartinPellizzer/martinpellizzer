Colubrina glabra

Colubrina glabra, commonly known as smooth colubrina, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 10 feet. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval to elliptic in shape. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically blue to violet in color and bloom in the spring.

Colubrina glabra is often found in moist, well-drained soils along streambanks and in woodland areas.
