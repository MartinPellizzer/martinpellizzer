Cirsium exigium

Cirsium exigium, commonly known as the dwarf thistle, is a perennial plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and spiny, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, pale purple to white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in sagebrush steppe ecosystems.

Cirsium exigium is adapted to arid conditions and has a deep root system for water storage.
