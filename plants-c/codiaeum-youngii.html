Codiaeum youngii

Codiaeum youngii, commonly known as the flame leaf or velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated foliage that can range from deep red to yellow and green. The leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and covered in a fine, velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by reddish-brown capsules containing numerous seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
