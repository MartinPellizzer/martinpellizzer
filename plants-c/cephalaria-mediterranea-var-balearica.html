Cephalaria mediterranea var. balearica

Cephalaria mediterranea var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly the Balearic Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features tall, slender stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences, usually in shades of pink or purple.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
