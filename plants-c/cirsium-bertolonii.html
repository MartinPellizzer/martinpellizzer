Cirsium bertolonii

Cirsium bertolonii, commonly known as the Bertoloni thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads. Cirsium bertolonii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
