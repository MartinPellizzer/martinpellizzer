Calathea soconuscum

Calathea soconuscum, commonly known as the zigzag calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a mix of green, white, and pink hues. The leaves have a unique zigzag pattern along the midrib, which gives the plant its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea soconuscum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.
