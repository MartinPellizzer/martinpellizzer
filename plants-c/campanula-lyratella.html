Campanula lyratella

Campanula lyratella, commonly known as the lyrated bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula lyratella is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens.
