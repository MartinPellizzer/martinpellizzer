Campanula Napuligera Var. Redux

Campanula napuligera var. redux is a unique and lesser-known member of the bellflower family. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of southern Italy, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its delicate blue-violet flowers bloom in early summer, adding a touch of elegance to alpine meadows. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles due to its limited distribution. This rare variety offers botanists and gardeners alike a fascinating subject for study and cultivation.
