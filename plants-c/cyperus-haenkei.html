Cyperus haenkei

Cyperus haenkei, commonly known as the umbrella sedge, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and distinctive, umbrella-shaped inflorescence. It typically grows in wetland or moist soil environments, making it a common sight in marshy areas.

Cyperus haenkei is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various conditions.
