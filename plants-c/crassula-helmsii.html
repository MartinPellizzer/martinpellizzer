Crassula helmsii

Crassula helmsii, commonly known as the stonecrop or sempervivum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the growing season. It is often grown as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements.

Crassula helmsii is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
