Croton lundianus var. hilarii

Croton lundianus var. hilarii, commonly known as the Hilary's croton, is a striking plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, yellow, and green. The leaves are typically large and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to add visual interest to gardens and indoor spaces.
