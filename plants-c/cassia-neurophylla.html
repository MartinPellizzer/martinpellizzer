Cassia neurophylla

Cassia neurophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cassia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical spikes. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight pubescence.

Cassia neurophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
