Carex × allensteiniensis

Carex × allensteiniensis, commonly known as the Allenstein sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and riparian zones. It has a clumping growth habit and forms compact tussocks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is often used in wetland restoration and garden landscapes.
