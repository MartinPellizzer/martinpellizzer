Chlorophytum subpetiolatum f. variegatum

Chlorophytum subpetiolatum f. variegatum, commonly known as the Tiger Tooth Plant, is a variegated form of the snake plant.

It features long, sword-shaped leaves with a striking variegation pattern of green and white. The plant is native to South Africa and is known for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
