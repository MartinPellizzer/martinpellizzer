Cyrtandra rubiginosa

Cyrtandra rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed ginger, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

Cyrtandra rubiginosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
