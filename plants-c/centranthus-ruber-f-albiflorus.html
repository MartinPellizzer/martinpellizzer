Centranthus ruber f. albiflorus

Centranthus ruber f. albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered red centrose.

This cultivar is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, contrasting with its dark green foliage. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in gardens for its visual appeal and drought tolerance.
