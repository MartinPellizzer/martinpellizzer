Cypripedium × superciliare

Cypripedium × superciliare, commonly known as the giant flame orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with bright orange petals and a distinctive red lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats, often in rich, organic soils. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it produces a single, elongated stem that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
