Colocasia lihengiae

Colocasia lihengiae, commonly known as the Liheng's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking purple to white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
