Cynanchum guatemalense

Cynanchum guatemalense, commonly known as Guatemalan milkweed, is a perennial herb native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes many milkweed species known for their milky sap. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually yellow or greenish in color.

The plant is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
