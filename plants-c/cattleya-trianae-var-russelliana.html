Cattleya trianae var. russelliana

Cattleya trianae var. russelliana, commonly known as the Russell's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant features upright, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant blooms that can last for several weeks. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
