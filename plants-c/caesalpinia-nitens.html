Caesalpinia nitens

Caesalpinia nitens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers that bloom in dense, feathery clusters. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its fruit is a long, pod-like structure that contains several seeds.

Caesalpinia nitens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils.
