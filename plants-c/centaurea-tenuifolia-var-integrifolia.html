Centaurea tenuifolia var. integrifolia

Centaurea tenuifolia var. integrifolia, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is distinguished by its entire leaf margins, which set it apart from other centaurea species.
