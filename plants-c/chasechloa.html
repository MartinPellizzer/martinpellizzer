Chasechloa

Chasechloa, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and compact growth habit. This plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. Chasechloa is drought-tolerant and has a low maintenance requirement, making it a popular choice for native landscaping.

Its seed heads are distinctive, adding ornamental value to the plant.
