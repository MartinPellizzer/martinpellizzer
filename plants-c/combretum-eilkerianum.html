Combretum eilkerianum

Combretum eilkerianum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting birds and insects. The leaves are simple, opposite, and have a rough, textured surface. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
