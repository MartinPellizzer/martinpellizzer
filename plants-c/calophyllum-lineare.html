Calophyllum lineare

Calophyllum lineare, commonly known as the narrow-leaf calophyllum, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Calophyllum lineare is often valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
