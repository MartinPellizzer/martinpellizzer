Chrysosplenium rimosum

Chrysosplenium rimosum, commonly known as the creeping chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of delicate, yellowish-white flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
