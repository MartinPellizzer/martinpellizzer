Clitoria javitensis var. javitensis

Clitoria javitensis var. javitensis, commonly known as the Java winged bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking violet to purple butterfly-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and produces edible pods containing seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nutritional benefits.

The species is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural systems.
