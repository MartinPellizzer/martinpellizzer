Colobachne gerardi var. heldreichii

Colobachne gerardi var. heldreichii, commonly known as the Heldreich's grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing minute, two-lobed flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
