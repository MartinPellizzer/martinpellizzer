Cattleya elongata

Cattleya elongata, commonly known as the elongated cattleya, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves. The plant produces pendulous flowers that are typically pink or white with yellow markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya elongata is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
