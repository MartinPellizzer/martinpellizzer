Cryptolepis dubia

Cryptolepis dubia, commonly known as the African bitters or bitterleaf, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional medicine. Its roots and leaves contain bioactive compounds such as alkaloids and flavonoids.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
