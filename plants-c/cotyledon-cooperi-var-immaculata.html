Cotyledon cooperi var. immaculata

Cotyledon cooperi var. immaculata, commonly known as the White Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically white or pale green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
