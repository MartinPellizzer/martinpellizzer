Chondrosum eriostachyum

Chondrosum eriostachyum, commonly known as the silverleaf chondrosum, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in dry, rocky habitats.

Chondrosum eriostachyum is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
