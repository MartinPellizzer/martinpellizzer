Crassula crenata

Crassula crenata, commonly known as the silver leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Crassula crenata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
