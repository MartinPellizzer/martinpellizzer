Cytisus proliferus var. palmensis

Cytisus proliferus var. palmensis, commonly known as the Canary Island broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat. The plant has slender, thornless stems and bipinnate leaves that are finely divided. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native region.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
