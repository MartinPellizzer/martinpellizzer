Calicorema capitata

Calicorema capitata, commonly known as the red-flowered waratah, is an evergreen shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, dense clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Calicorema capitata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant collections.
