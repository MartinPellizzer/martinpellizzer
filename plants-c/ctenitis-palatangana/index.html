Ctenitis Palatangana

Palatangana brake, scientifically known as Ctenitis palatangana, is a rare and distinctive fern species found in specific tropical regions. This plant is characterized by its unique fronds and specialized growth patterns that adapt to its humid, shaded habitats. Despite its limited distribution, it plays a vital role in maintaining the ecological balance of its native environment. Its delicate structure and intricate leaf arrangements make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. However, habitat loss and environmental changes pose significant threats to its survival.
