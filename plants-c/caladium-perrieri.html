Caladium perrieri

Caladium perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like calla lilies. Caladium perrieri thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it is less commonly cultivated than other caladium species.
