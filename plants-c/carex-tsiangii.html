Carex tsiangii

Carex tsiangii, commonly known as the Tsiang's sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streams.

Carex tsiangii is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wet environments.
