Commelina bainesii var. glabrata

Commelina bainesii var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth commelina, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves.
