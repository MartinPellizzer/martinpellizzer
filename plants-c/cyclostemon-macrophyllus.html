Cyclostemon macrophyllus

Cyclostemon macrophyllus, commonly known as the broadleaf cyclostemon, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and is often found in rainforests or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
