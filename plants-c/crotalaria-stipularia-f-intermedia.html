Crotalaria stipularia f. intermedia

Crotalaria stipularia f. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate stipular crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It is known for its erect growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and research settings.
