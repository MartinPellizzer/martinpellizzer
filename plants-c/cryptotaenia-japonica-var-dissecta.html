Cryptotaenia japonica var. dissecta

Cryptotaenia japonica var. dissecta, commonly known as Japanese cow parsley or Japanese parsley.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Japan, characterized by its finely dissected, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, lacey appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
