Cleisostoma bicuspidatum

Cleisostoma bicuspidatum, commonly known as the two-lobed cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its leaves are ovate and arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a waxy texture.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
