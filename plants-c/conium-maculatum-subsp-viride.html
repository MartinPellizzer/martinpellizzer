Conium maculatum subsp. viride

Conium maculatum subsp.

viride, commonly known as green hemlock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its finely divided leaves and umbellate flowers.
