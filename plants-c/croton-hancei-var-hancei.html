Croton hancei var. hancei

Croton hancei var. hancei, commonly known as the Hance's croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that display shades of green, red, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a waxy texture.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, clustered in panicles, and are followed by three-lobed, capsule-like fruits.
