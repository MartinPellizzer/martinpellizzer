Cirsium hanamakiense

Cirsium hanamakiense, commonly known as the Hanamaki thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. It produces small, composite flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
