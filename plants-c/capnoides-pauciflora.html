Capnoides pauciflora

Capnoides pauciflora, commonly known as the slender piper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of flowering plants known for their aromatic properties. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spikes.

Capnoides pauciflora is often found in tropical rainforests and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
