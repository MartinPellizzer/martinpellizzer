Cucurbita pepo var. melopepo

Cucurbita pepo var. melopepo, commonly known as the acorn squash, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, ribbed fruit that typically ranges in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant produces large, palmately lobed leaves and yellow flowers that are both male and female. The fruit is rich in nutrients, including vitamins A and C, and is often used in culinary applications.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.
