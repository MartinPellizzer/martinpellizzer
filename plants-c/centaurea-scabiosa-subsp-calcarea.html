Centaurea scabiosa subsp. calcarea

Centaurea scabiosa subsp.

calcarea, commonly known as limestone cudweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in calcareous soils, thriving in rocky or limestone-rich environments.

The plant features upright stems with narrow, gray-green leaves and produces bright yellow to orange flower heads. Its inflorescence consists of ray flowers surrounding a central disk, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is often found in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.
