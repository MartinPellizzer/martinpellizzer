Croton lapanus

Croton lapanus, commonly known as the yellow croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that range from yellow to red, often with contrasting patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Croton lapanus thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
