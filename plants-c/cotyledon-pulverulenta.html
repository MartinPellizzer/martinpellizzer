Cotyledon pulverulenta

Cotyledon pulverulenta, commonly known as the dust cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
