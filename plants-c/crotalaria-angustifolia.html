Crotalaria angustifolia

Crotalaria angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf cowpea, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, branching stems and alternate, narrow, ovate leaves with a pointed tip. The plant produces distinctive yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by pod-like fruits containing kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Crotalaria angustifolia is noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in agricultural systems.
