Cryptocoryne unilocularis

Cryptocoryne unilocularis, commonly known as the slender cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments. Its rhizome system allows it to anchor itself in substrates and spread vegetatively.

Cryptocoryne unilocularis is popular in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
