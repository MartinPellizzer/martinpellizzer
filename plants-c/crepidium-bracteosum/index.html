Crepidium Bracteosum

Crepidium bracteosum is a rare and delicate orchid native to the high altitudes of South America. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. This species is known for its small, intricate flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Due to its limited habitat and environmental threats, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical wonder.
