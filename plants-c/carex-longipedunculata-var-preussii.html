Carex longipedunculata var. preussii

Carex longipedunculata var. preussii, commonly known as the Preuss's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clustered spikes of small, spikelet-bearing flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or wetland habitats.
