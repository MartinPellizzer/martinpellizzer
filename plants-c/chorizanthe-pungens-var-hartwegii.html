Chorizanthe pungens var. hartwegii

Chorizanthe pungens var. hartwegii, commonly known as Hartweg's spiny bluestem, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, spiny stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as plains and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually linear and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.
