Crotalaria laburnifolia subsp. eldomae

Crotalaria laburnifolia subsp.

eldomae, commonly known as the golden chain crotalaria, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It features bright yellow, pea-like flowers arranged in long, drooping clusters, giving it a striking appearance.

The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces distinctive, spiny seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
