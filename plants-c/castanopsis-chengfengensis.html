Castanopsis chengfengensis

Castanopsis chengfengensis, commonly known as the Chengfeng tallow tree, is a species of evergreen broadleaf tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and beeches, and is characterized by its hard, durable wood. The plant produces small, cup-shaped fruits that contain one or two seeds, typical of the Fagaceae family. It thrives in humid, subtropical climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils.

Due to its ecological and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture and conservation projects.
