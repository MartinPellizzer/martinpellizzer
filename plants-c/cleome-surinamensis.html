Cleome surinamensis

Cleome surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam cleome, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, branching stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cleome surinamensis is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
