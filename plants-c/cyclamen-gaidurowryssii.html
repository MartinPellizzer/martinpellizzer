Cyclamen gaidurowryssii

Cyclamen gaidurowryssii, commonly known as the Gaidurow's cyclamen, is a rare and ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is typically found in rocky habitats at high altitudes, where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions.

Cyclamen gaidurowryssii is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic and ecological significance.
