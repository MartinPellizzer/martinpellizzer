Carum calcicola

Carum calcicola, commonly known as the limestone caraway, is a flowering plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the family. Its leaves are finely divided and often have a mild, aromatic scent.
