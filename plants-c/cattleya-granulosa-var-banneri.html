Cattleya granulosa var. banneri

Cattleya granulosa var. banneri, commonly known as the Banner's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, and showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
