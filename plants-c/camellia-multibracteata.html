Camellia multibracteata

Camellia multibracteata, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Camellia multibracteata is also valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its winter blooms.
