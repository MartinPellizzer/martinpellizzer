Cousinia tenuisecta

Cousinia tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cousinia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers in dense clusters. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in steppe and desert environments.

Cousinia tenuisecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
