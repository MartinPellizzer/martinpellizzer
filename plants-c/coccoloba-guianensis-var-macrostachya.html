Coccoloba guianensis var. macrostachya

Coccoloba guianensis var. macrostachya, commonly known as the giant coccoloba, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Guyana, and Suriname. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
