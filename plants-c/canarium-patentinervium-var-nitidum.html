Canarium patentinervium var. nitidum

Canarium patentinervium var. nitidum, commonly known as the glossy canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent midribs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible fruits that are encased in a spiny, woody shell. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
