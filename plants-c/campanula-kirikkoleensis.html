Campanula kirikkoleensis

Campanula kirikkoleensis, commonly known as the Kirikkale bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

Campanula kirikkoleensis is also noted for its resilience and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
