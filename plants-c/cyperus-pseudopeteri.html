Cyperus pseudopeteri

Cyperus pseudopeteri, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. Cyperus pseudopeteri is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both freshwater and brackish habitats.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and contributing to soil stabilization in wetland ecosystems.
