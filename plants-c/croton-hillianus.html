Croton hillianus

Croton hillianus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and vibrant, colorful flowers that range from yellow to red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in savannas and forest margins.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its striking foliage and floral display.
