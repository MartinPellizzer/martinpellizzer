Cassia coluteoides

Cassia coluteoides, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain numerous flat, dark brown seeds. Cassia coluteoides thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
