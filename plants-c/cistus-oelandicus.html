Cistus oelandicus

Cistus oelandicus, commonly known as the Oeland rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant. Its foliage contains essential oils, which contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.

Cistus oelandicus plays an important role in its native ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
