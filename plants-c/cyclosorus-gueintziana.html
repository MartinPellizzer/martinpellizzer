Cyclosorus gueintziana

Cyclosorus gueintziana, commonly known as the Gueintziana water fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Water ferns.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that are typically divided into small, rounded lobes. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams, where it can receive adequate sunlight. Its rhizomes are submerged, anchoring the plant and absorbing nutrients from the water.

Cyclosorus gueintziana plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and contributing to water filtration.
