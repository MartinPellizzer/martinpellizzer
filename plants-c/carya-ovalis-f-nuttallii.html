Carya ovalis f. nuttallii

Carya ovalis f. nuttallii, commonly known as the Nuttall's hickory, is a variant of the shellbark hickory.

It is a deciduous tree native to the central and eastern United States. The plant has a distinctive, rugged trunk with a thick, flaky bark. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a serrated margin.

The fruit is a large, edible nut enclosed in a spiny husk.
