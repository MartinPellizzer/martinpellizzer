Chamorchis Alpina

Chamorchis alpina is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the European Alps. It thrives in rocky and arid environments where few other plants can survive. This species is known for its small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Due to its limited habitat and environmental threats, Chamorchis alpina is considered vulnerable. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique alpine flora from extinction.
