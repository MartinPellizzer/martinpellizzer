Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. patula

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. patula, commonly known as the "Moonstone Plant," is a succulent with thick, fleshy leaves that often display striking color variations.

Its leaves are typically rounded and can range from pale green to deep red or purple, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
