Carex stricta var. microstachya

Carex stricta var. microstachya, commonly known as slender bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces slender, cylindrical spikelets that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in shallow water or moist soils, making it a common sight in marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its rhizomes help stabilize soil and prevent erosion, contributing to wetland health.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and environmental benefits.
