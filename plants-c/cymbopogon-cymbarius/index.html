Cymbopogon Cymbarius

Cymbopogon cymbarius is commonly known as 'bermuda grass' in some regions, scientifically known as Cymbopogon cymbarius, it is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical areas. This plant is widely used for erosion control due to its dense growth and rapid spread. It thrives in sunny conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for lawns and landscaping. Despite its benefits, it can be invasive and difficult to eradicate once established.
