Centaurea × moisili

Centaurea × moisili, commonly known as the Moisil's cornflower, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, with lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Centaurea × moisili is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its colorful blooms.
