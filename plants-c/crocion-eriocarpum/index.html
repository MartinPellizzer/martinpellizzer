Crocion Eriocarpum

Crocion eriocarpum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It is known for its unique, hairy seed pods that give it its scientific name, meaning "hairy fruit." This plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires specific environmental conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important species.
