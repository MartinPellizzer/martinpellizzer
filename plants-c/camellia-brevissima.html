Camellia brevissima

Camellia brevissima, commonly known as the dwarf camellia, is a species of flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and evergreen foliage, with broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces showy, camellia-like flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and as a container plant.

Camellia brevissima is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
