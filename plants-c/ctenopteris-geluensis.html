Ctenopteris geluensis

Ctenopteris geluensis, commonly known as the Java cliff fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, feathery appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on cliffs or in dense forests.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
