Cyrtandra aurantiicarpa

Cyrtandra aurantiicarpa, commonly known as the orange-fruited cyrtandra, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. Cyrtandra aurantiicarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique fruit structure.
