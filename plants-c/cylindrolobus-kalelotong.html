Cylindrolobus kalelotong

Cylindrolobus kalelotong, commonly known as the giant cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, stout trunk and broad, feather-like leaves that can reach impressive lengths. The plant produces cones rather than flowers, a feature typical of cycads, and it has a slow growth rate. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is often protected in its native habitats.
