Chamaedorea fenestrata

Chamaedorea fenestrata, commonly known as the bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It features slender, upright stems with a smooth, dark green, glossy foliage. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its narrow, elongated leaves that often have a subtle veining pattern. It is well-suited for indoor environments due to its low maintenance requirements and air-purifying qualities.

Chamaedorea fenestrata thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers consistently moist, well-draining soil.
