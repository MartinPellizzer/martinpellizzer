Cypripedium sallieri

Cypripedium sallieri, commonly known as the showy lady's tresses, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, flowering stem and dense, spiraling arrangement of flowers that resemble a tresses of hair. The plant produces showy, fragrant orchid-like blooms in shades of white, pink, or purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Cypripedium sallieri is a perennial herb with a rhizomatous root system and is often found in association with other orchid species.
