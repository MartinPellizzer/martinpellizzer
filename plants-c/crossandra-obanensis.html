Crossandra obanensis

Crossandra obanensis, commonly known as the Oban flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Crossandra obanensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
