Corysanthes arachnoidea

Corysanthes arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance. The plant features a tall, wiry stem with dense clusters of small, pale green flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a protected species in many regions.
