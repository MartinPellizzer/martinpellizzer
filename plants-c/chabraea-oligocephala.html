Chabraea oligocephala

Chabraea oligocephala, commonly known as the Christmas tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, frond-like leaves that resemble a tree's foliage. The plant is typically found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Chabraea oligocephala is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
