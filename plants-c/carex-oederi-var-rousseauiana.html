Carex oederi var. rousseauiana

Carex oederi var. rousseauiana, commonly known as the Rousseau's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native, low-maintenance plants.
