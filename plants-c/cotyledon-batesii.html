Cotyledon batesii

Cotyledon batesii, commonly known as the "Cape tulip," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Cotyledon batesii is drought-tolerant and is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
