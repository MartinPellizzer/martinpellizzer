Chromolaena laevigata

Chromolaena laevigata, commonly known as the Hawaiian tridax, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. Chromolaena laevigata is known for its rapid growth and has become an invasive species in several parts of the world.
