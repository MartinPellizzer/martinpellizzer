Crassula rhodesica

Crassula rhodesica, commonly known as the Rhodesian Crassula, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Crassula rhodesica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
