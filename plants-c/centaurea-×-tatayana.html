Centaurea × tatayana

Centaurea × tatayana, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its striking blue to purple composite flowers and spiny bracts. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color.

Centaurea × tatayana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
