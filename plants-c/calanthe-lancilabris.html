Calanthe lancilabris

Calanthe lancilabris, commonly known as the slender-labellum calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and striking, lance-shaped flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features a single, showy flower per stem, with a distinctive, elongated lip that is often brightly colored. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate, arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

Calanthe lancilabris thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticultural collections.
