Calothamnus torulosus var. leptophyllus

Calothamnus torulosus var. leptophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features distinctive red or orange bottlebrush flowers that bloom during the warmer months. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, upright habit. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens and landscaping.
