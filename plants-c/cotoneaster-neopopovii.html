Cotoneaster neopopovii

Cotoneaster neopopovii, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster neopopovii is also valued for its use in landscaping and as a source of food for wildlife.
