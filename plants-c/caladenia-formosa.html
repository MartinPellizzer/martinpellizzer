Caladenia formosa

Caladenia formosa, commonly known as the showy spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, strikingly colored flower that typically ranges from pink to purple. The flower has a slender, curling labellum and is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is protected in some regions.
