Colocasia obtusiloba

Colocasia obtusiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaved arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshes and wetlands. Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental water gardening.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.
