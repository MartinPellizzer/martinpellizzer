Cynanchum melanthum

Cynanchum melanthum, commonly known as green milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist woodlands and has opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually greenish-white in color.

It serves as a host plant for certain butterfly species and has been used in traditional medicine.
