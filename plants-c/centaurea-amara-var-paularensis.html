Centaurea amara var. paularensis

Centaurea amara var. paularensis, commonly known as the bitter centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and bright blue to purple flowers. The plant has serrated, grass-like leaves and produces small, bracteate flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
