Cyrtandra × sylvestris

Cyrtandra × sylvestris, commonly known as the hybrid Cyrtandra, is a flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Cyrtandra caribea and Cyrtandra sylvestris, characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky crevices. It exhibits a variety of morphological traits that reflect its hybrid origin, including variable leaf shapes and flower structures.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
