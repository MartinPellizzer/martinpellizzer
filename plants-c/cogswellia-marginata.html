Cogswellia marginata

Cogswellia marginata, commonly known as the Marginata Cogswellia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by capsule fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight, making it a popular choice for succulent gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
