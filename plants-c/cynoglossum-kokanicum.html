Cynoglossum kokanicum

Cynoglossum kokanicum, commonly known as the Kokanica bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
