Cymbidium nutans

Cymbidium nutans, commonly known as the nodding orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that droop downward, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cymbidium nutans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
