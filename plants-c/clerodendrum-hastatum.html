Clerodendrum hastatum

Clerodendrum hastatum, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow throat. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated margin. It produces small, clustered fruits that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature.

Clerodendrum hastatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
