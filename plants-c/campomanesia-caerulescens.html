Campomanesia caerulescens

Campomanesia caerulescens, commonly known as the blue campomanesia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, blue-black berries. Its fruits are often used in local cuisine and traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
