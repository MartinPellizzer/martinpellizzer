Costus globosus var. velutinus

Costus globosus var. velutinus, commonly known as velvet costus, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and striking, orange-red inflorescences. The plant produces small, tubular flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Velvet costus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
