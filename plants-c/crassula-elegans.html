Crassula elegans

Crassula elegans, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Cash Register, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and bonsai.
