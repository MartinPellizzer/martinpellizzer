Covellia

Covellia, commonly known as the silverleaf shrub, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by dark blue berries. Covellia thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value contribute to its cultivation in various horticultural settings.
