Chrysocoma pauciflora

Chrysocoma pauciflora, commonly known as the silverleaf chrysocoma, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Chrysocoma pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
