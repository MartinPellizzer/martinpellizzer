Canarium pachypodum

Canarium pachypodum, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, palmate leaves that are typically lobed. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in spherical clusters, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Canarium pachypodum is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
