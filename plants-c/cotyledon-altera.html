Cotyledon altera

Cotyledon altera, commonly known as the variegated cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often variegated with white, yellow, or pink markings. The plant belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is known for its ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

Cotyledon altera is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
