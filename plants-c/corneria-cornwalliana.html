Corneria cornwalliana

Corneria cornwalliana, commonly known as the Cornwall bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the British Isles.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Due to its rarity, it is considered a protected species in some regions.
