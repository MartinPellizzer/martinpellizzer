Celosia nervosa

Celosia nervosa, commonly known as the flame flower or cockscomb, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and pink. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Celosia nervosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for container gardening.

Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adding to its visual appeal.
