Cinnagrostis rigida

Cinnagrostis rigida, commonly known as stiff goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright, rigid stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils in prairies, wetlands, and along stream banks. Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Cinnagrostis rigida plays an important role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
