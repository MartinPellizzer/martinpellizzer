Caladium thripedestum

Caladium thripedestum, commonly known as the tri-color caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that come in shades of white, pink, and green. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow up to 12 inches in length.

Caladium thripedestum thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.
