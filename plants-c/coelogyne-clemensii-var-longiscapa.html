Coelogyne clemensii var. longiscapa

Coelogyne clemensii var. longiscapa, commonly known as the long-stemmed clemens coelogyne, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and Myanmar. The plant is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and pendant inflorescences that bear fragrant, showy flowers. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it typically blooms during the winter months.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
