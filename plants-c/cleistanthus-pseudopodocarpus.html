Cleistanthus pseudopodocarpus

Cleistanthus pseudopodocarpus, commonly known as the Chinese tallowtree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and alternate, simple, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish flowers followed by orange-red berries that contain toxic seeds.
