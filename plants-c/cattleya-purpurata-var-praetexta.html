Cattleya purpurata var. praetexta

Cattleya purpurata var. praetexta, commonly known as the Purple Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, fleshy leaves and produces pendulous blooms with a distinctive purple and white coloration. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and gardeners.
