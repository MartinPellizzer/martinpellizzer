Calyptridium arizonicum

Calyptridium arizonicum, commonly known as Arizona tansy, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that help reduce water loss.
