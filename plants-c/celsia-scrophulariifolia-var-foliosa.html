Celsia scrophulariifolia var. foliosa

Celsia scrophulariifolia var. foliosa, commonly known as the broad-leaved celsia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and broad, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.
