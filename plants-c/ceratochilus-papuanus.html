Ceratochilus papuanus

Ceratochilus papuanus, commonly known as the Papuan helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Guinea.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique helmet-shaped flower. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are striking with a deep purple to violet hue.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
