Chamaemelum nobile var. duplicatum

Chamaemelum nobile var. duplicatum, commonly known as the double chamomile, is a cultivar of the chamomile plant characterized by its double flower heads.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to Europe. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces white to pale yellow flowers with a fluffy, double bloom. It is often used in herbal teas and aromatherapy due to its calming properties.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal uses.
