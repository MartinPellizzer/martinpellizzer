Crotalaria herbacea

Crotalaria herbacea, commonly known as the slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and small, yellow flowers. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately divided, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Crotalaria herbacea is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
