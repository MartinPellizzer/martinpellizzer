Cyperus litos

Cyperus litos, commonly known as the rigid flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and wetlands. Its cylindrical, grass-like leaves are usually dark green and have a slightly rough texture.

Cyperus litos produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically wind-pollinated.
