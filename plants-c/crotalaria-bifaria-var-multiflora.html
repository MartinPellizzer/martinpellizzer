Crotalaria bifaria var. multiflora

Crotalaria bifaria var. multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with spurred petals. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately compound, and often have a hairy texture.

The seeds are enclosed in pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds through mechanical means.
