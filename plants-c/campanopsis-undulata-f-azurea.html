Campanopsis undulata f. azurea

Campanopsis undulata f. azurea, commonly known as the Azure Bellflower, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, azure-blue to violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and elegant form.
