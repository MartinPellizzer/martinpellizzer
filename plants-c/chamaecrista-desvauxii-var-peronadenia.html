Chamaecrista desvauxii var. peronadenia

Chamaecrista desvauxii var. peronadenia, commonly known as the Peronadenia burr legume, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
