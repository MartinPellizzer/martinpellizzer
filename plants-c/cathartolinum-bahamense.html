Cathartolinum bahamense

Cathartolinum bahamense, commonly known as the Bahamian arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Bahamas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to saline conditions.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for wetland ecosystems.
