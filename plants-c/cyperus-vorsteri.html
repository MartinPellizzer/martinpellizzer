Cyperus vorsteri

Cyperus vorsteri, commonly known as the Vorster's flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower spikes that bloom in the summer months. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense vegetation in suitable habitats.

Cyperus vorsteri plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
