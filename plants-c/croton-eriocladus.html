Croton eriocladus

Croton eriocladus, commonly known as the silky croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural gardens.
