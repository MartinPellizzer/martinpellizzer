Catasetum osakadianum

Catasetum osakadianum, commonly known as the Japanese orchid, is a species native to Japan and parts of Korea.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces showy, colorful flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Catasetum osakadianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
