Cucurbita andreana

Cucurbita andreana, commonly known as the Andean pumpkin, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. Its fruit, which can grow up to 30 centimeters in length, is typically orange to red in color.

This species is valued for its edible fruit and is also used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
