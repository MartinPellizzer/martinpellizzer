Cenchrus Thulinii

Cenchrus thulinii is a resilient grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. It thrives in harsh environments with low rainfall and poor soil conditions. This plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand extreme temperatures. Its dense growth habit makes it useful for erosion control and livestock forage. Despite its tough appearance, Cenchrus thulinii plays an important ecological role in stabilizing degraded landscapes.
