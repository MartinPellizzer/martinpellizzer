Cola scheffleri

Cola scheffleri, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical shrub native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces elliptical, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and bloom in spring.

Cola scheffleri is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
