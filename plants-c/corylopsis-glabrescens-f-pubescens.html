Corylopsis glabrescens f. pubescens

Corylopsis glabrescens f. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent flame twisted vine, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with many branches, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This cultivar is noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
