Craspedaria vacciniifolia

Craspedaria vacciniifolia, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are small but numerous, forming dense clusters that bloom in late spring.

Craspedaria vacciniifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialty gardens.
