Cyperus platystachys

Cyperus platystachys, commonly known as broad-stemmed flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown, and it thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes and pond edges.

Cyperus platystachys is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
