Calligonum alatosetosum

Calligonum alatosetosum, commonly known as the hairy goat's beard, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, hairy foliage that helps reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater in dry conditions.

Calligonum alatosetosum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
