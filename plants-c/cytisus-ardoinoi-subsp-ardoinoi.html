Cytisus ardoinoi subsp. ardoinoi

Cytisus ardoinoi subsp.

ardoinoi, commonly known as the Ardoin's broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure and nitrogen-fixing ability.

The plant features narrow, leathery leaves and produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
