Culcasia orientalis

Culcasia orientalis, commonly known as the giant taro, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant thrives in moist, warm climates and is often cultivated for its edible corms. Its foliage is used in traditional medicine and as a decorative element in gardens.

The plant plays an important role in both ecological and cultural contexts in its native regions.
