Callicarpa moldenkeana

Callicarpa moldenkeana, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant purple to violet berries that appear in late summer. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by the showy fruits. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
