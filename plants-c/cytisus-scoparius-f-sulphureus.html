Cytisus scoparius f. sulphureus

Cytisus scoparius f. sulphureus, commonly known as the sulphur yellow broom, is a flowering shrub native to the western United States.

It is a naturally occurring variant of the common broom, characterized by its striking yellow flowers. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
