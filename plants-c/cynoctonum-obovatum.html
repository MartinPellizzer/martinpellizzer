Cynoctonum obovatum

Cynoctonum obovatum, commonly known as the Brazilian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cynoctonum obovatum is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
