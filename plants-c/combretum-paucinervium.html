Combretum paucinervium

Combretum paucinervium, commonly known as the narrow-veined flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The bark is smooth and can become fissured with age, and the plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
