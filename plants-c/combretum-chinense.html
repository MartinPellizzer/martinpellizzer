Combretum chinense

Combretum chinense, commonly known as Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
