Cystorchis versteegii

Cystorchis versteegii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant produces a single spike of flowers, with each flower having three petals and three sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cystorchis versteegii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
