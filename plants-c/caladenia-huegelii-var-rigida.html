Caladenia huegelii var. rigida

Caladenia huegelii var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive hooded labellum and slender, erect stems. The plant typically produces a single, narrow leaf and blooms with a single flower that has a striking, intricate structure. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats, often in heathlands or coastal areas.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
