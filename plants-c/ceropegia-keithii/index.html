Ceropegia Keithii

String of hearts, scientifically known as Ceropegia keithii, is a strikingly beautiful trailing plant that captivates with its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant pink flowers. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in warm, sunny environments and is a popular choice for hanging baskets and indoor spaces. Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and beginners alike. The plant's ability to produce long, cascading stems adds a touch of elegance to any setting. With proper care, String of hearts can flourish and bring a touch of natural beauty to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
