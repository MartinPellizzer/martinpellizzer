Callistemma brachiatum

Callistemma brachiatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight reddish tinge on the underside.

It is valued in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
