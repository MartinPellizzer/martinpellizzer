Croton cavaleriei

Croton cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that vary in color from green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
