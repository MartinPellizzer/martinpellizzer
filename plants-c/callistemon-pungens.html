Callistemon pungens

Callistemon pungens, commonly known as the rough bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, cylindrical, red or pink flower spikes that resemble bottlebrushes. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The flowers attract nectar-feeding birds and insects, contributing to its ecological value.
