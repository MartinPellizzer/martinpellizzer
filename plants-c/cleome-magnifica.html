Cleome magnifica

Cleome magnifica, commonly known as the splendid cleome, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and showy, yellow-centered flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Cleome magnifica thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its foliage is often covered in fine, silky hairs, adding to its distinctive appearance.
