Cordiera myrciifolia var. barbata

Cordiera myrciifolia var. barbata, commonly known as the barbate cordiera, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy dark green surface and prominent veins. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by ornamental red berries. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
