Chuquiraga Spinosa Subsp. Rotundifolia

Chuquiraga spinosa subsp. rotundifolia is a unique plant native to the arid regions of South America. It is known for its distinctive rounded leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. This subspecies thrives in harsh environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to drought and poor soil conditions. Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects. Despite its resilience, it remains a relatively obscure species within the broader Chuquiraga genus.
