Centaurea vinyalsii

Centaurea vinyalsii, commonly known as the Balearic centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, specifically the Balearic Islands.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright stems and vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in dense, composite inflorescences.

Centaurea vinyalsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in wildflower gardens and natural habitats.
