Ceratia

Ceratia, commonly known as the horned fern, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Araliaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, compound leaves that often have a spiny or horn-like appearance. They produce small, inconspicuous flowers followed by berries that are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
