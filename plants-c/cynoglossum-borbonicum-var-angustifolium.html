Cynoglossum borbonicum var. angustifolium

Cynoglossum borbonicum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet to blue in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. The plant has lanceolate, narrow leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
