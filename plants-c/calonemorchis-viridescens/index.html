Calonemorchis Viridescens

Green orchid, scientifically known as Calonemorchis viridescens, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. Its unique appearance features vibrant green petals and a slender, elegant stem that sets it apart from other orchid species. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Despite its beauty, Calonemorchis viridescens remains a relatively obscure species among orchid enthusiasts. Its cultivation requires careful attention to moisture and light conditions to ensure its survival and blooming.
