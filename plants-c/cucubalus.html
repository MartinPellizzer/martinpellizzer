Cucubalus

Cucubalus, commonly known as the African eggplant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which also includes tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces large, egg-shaped fruits that can range in color from green to purple. Its leaves are broad and lobed, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

The fruit is often used in traditional cuisines and has various medicinal uses in some cultures.
