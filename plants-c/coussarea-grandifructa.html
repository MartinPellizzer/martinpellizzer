Coussarea grandifructa

Coussarea grandifructa, commonly known as the giant fruit-bearing coussarea, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of red and orange. The plant produces substantial, fleshy fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

Coussarea grandifructa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
