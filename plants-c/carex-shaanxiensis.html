Carex shaanxiensis

Carex shaanxiensis, commonly known as the Shaanxi sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. This species typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wet meadows. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, clustered flower heads.

Carex shaanxiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
