Cattleya forbesii f. beatriciana

Cattleya forbesii f. beatriciana, commonly known as the Beatrice Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces pendulous blooms with a mix of pink, white, and yellow hues. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
