Ceratostema Flexuosum

Ceratostema flexuosum is a unique and delicate flowering plant known for its graceful, arching stems and clusters of small, white blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this species thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded landscapes. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare and requires careful cultivation to flourish. This plant offers both ornamental value and a glimpse into the diverse flora of its native habitat.
