Cypripedium × platycolor

Cypripedium × platycolor, commonly known as the broad-leaf lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, pinkish-white flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, and it produces a single flower per stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral structure.
