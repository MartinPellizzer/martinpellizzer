Cranichis subumbellata

Cranichis subumbellata, commonly known as the common grass-of-Parnassus, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces a dense inflorescence of small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a subumbellate pattern. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in open woodlands and meadows.

Cranichis subumbellata is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and North America.
