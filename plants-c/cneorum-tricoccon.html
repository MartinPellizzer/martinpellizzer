Cneorum tricoccon

Cneorum tricoccon, commonly known as the sweet box, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are aromatic when crushed. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a foundation plant.

Its ornamental value and pleasant scent make it a popular choice in horticulture.
