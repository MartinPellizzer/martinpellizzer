Clematis viticella var. purpurea-plena

Clematis viticella var. purpurea-plena, commonly known as the purple giant, is a vigorous, deciduous climbing vine native to Europe.

It is characterized by its large, double flowers that range from deep purple to violet, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has strong, flexible stems that can climb trellises or walls with ease. Its foliage is heart-shaped and provides a lush green backdrop to the showy blooms.

It is relatively hardy and suitable for growing in USDA hardiness zones 5 through 8.
