Carthamus

Carthamus, commonly known as safflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow or red flowers. The plant produces seeds that are used in various culinary and industrial applications. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Safflower is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in dye production.
