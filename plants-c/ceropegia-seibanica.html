Ceropegia seibanica

Ceropegia seibanica, commonly known as the Seiban's ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tuberous roots. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
