Cyperus dunensis

Cyperus dunensis, commonly known as the desert flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically wind-pollinated. Cyperus dunensis is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with deep root systems that help it access water from deeper soil layers.

It often grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is sometimes found in disturbed areas.
