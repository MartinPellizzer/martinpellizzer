Cirsium × heerianum

Cirsium × heerianum, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. Its botanical features include a robust root system and a tall, upright stem that supports its showy inflorescence.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
