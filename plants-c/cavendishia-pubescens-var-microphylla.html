Cavendishia pubescens var. microphylla

Cavendishia pubescens var. microphylla, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
