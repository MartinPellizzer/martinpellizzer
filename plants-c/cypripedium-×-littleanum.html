Cypripedium × littleanum

Cypripedium × littleanum, commonly known as the Little Anemone Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers with a distinctive green lip, which is a notable botanical feature. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodland habitats, preferring rich, organic soil. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces a single, showy bloom each year.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern in some regions.
