Carex constanceana

Carex constanceana, commonly known as the Constant's sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and wetlands. Carex constanceana is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
