Chamaedorea moliniana

Chamaedorea moliniana, commonly known as the Molina's bamboo palm, is a compact, evergreen plant native to Mexico.

It features slender, upright stems that produce feathery, arching leaves, giving it a lush, tropical appearance. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering requirements. It has a slow growth rate, making it ideal for small spaces or as a decorative houseplant.

Its foliage remains evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.
