Cymbidium Cordatum

Cymbidium cordatum is a striking member of the orchid family known for its elegant, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant flower spikes. Native to eastern Asia, this plant thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its flowers, which bloom in late winter or early spring, come in a range of colors including white, pink, and red. Easy to care for, it's a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. With its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms, Cymbidium cordatum adds a touch of exotic beauty to any indoor or outdoor space.
