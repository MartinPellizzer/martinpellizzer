Croton majesticus

Croton majesticus, commonly known as the royal croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. The plant features large, palmately lobed leaves that come in shades of green, yellow, and red, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Croton majesticus is known for its ability to produce small, inconspicuous flowers followed by unique, spiky seed capsules.
