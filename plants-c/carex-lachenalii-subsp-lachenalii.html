Carex lachenalii subsp. lachenalii

Carex lachenalii subsp.

lachenalii, commonly known as the Lachenal's sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family. It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Native to southern Africa, this species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. The plant is often found in woodland margins and stream banks, where it contributes to soil stabilization.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
