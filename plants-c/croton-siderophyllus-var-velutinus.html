Croton siderophyllus var. velutinus

Croton siderophyllus var. velutinus, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, velvety leaves that range in color from dark green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in warm climates.
