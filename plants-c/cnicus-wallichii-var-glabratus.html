Cnicus wallichii var. glabratus

Cnicus wallichii var. glabratus, commonly known as the smooth tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant features yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is adapted to high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils.
