Centaurium erythraea subsp. bernardii

Centaurium erythraea subsp.

bernardii, commonly known as the Bernard's redhead, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, bright red flowers.
