Carissa spectabilis

Carissa spectabilis, commonly known as the yellow bell tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant is part of the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance.
