Camellia brevistyla var. microphylla

Camellia brevistyla var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf camellia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its small, glossy dark green leaves and vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained, acidic soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably in winter.

This variety is also noted for its cold hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
