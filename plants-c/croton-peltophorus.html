Croton peltophorus

Croton peltophorus, commonly known as the red-leaf croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often features shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are typically palmately lobed, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Croton peltophorus is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
