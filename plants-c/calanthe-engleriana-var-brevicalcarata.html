Calanthe engleriana var. brevicalcarata

Calanthe engleriana var. brevicalcarata, commonly known as the short-awned calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers with prominent, short spurs. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
