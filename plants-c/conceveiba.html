Conceveiba

Conceveiba, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that float on the water's surface, with vibrant colors ranging from white to pink and purple. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide shade and oxygen to the water. Its roots are submerged, absorbing nutrients from the aquatic environment.

Conceveiba is revered in many cultures for its spiritual and medicinal properties.
