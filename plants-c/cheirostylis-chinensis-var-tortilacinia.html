Cheirostylis chinensis var. tortilacinia

Cheirostylis chinensis var. tortilacinia, commonly known as the twisted-leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often twisted or curled. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its unique foliage and ornamental value in horticulture.
