Calathea jocosa

Calathea jocosa, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with intricate patterns and colors that change throughout the day. The leaves are typically oval or lance-shaped, and the plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea jocosa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
