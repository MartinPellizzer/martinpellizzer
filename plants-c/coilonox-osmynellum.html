Coilonox osmynellum

Coilonox osmynellum, commonly known as the fragrant fern, is a unique plant species characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that emit a subtle, sweet aroma.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant's leaves are composed of multiple narrow, curling segments that unfurl as they mature. Its reproductive structures are concealed within specialized leaf axils, contributing to its distinctive botanical appearance.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
