Chusquea pulchella

Chusquea pulchella, commonly known as the elegant bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often a striking green color. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
