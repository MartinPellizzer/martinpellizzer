Cirrhopetalum stramineum var. purpureum

Cirrhopetalum stramineum var. purpureum, commonly known as the purple-flowered cirrhopetalum, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, showy inflorescences. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are often clustered in racemes, with a striking purple hue. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.
