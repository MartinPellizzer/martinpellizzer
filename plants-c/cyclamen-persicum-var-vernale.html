Cyclamen persicum var. vernale

Cyclamen persicum var. vernale, commonly known as the spring cyclamen, is a hybrid cultivar of the cyclamen plant.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bowl-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive, swollen base. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

This variety is popular for its ornamental value and is typically grown in pots or as a bedding plant.
