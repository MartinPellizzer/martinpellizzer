Centaurea × serresii

Centaurea × serresii, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flower heads that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with slender, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are composed of ray florets surrounding a central disk, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Centaurea × serresii prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens and borders.
