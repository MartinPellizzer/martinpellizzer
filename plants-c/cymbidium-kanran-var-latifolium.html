Cymbidium kanran var. latifolium

Cymbidium kanran var. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates, often in shaded forest environments. Its broad, leathery leaves are evergreen and contribute to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is prized for its elegance and is frequently cultivated for both horticultural and ornamental purposes.
