Cryptandra ericoides

Cryptandra ericoides, commonly known as the heath cryptandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is a notable species in the region's flora.
