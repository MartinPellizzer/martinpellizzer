Cyathea isaloensis

Cyathea isaloensis, commonly known as the Isalo tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its tall, fibrous trunk, while its fronds are responsible for photosynthesis.

Cyathea isaloensis plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to forest biodiversity.
