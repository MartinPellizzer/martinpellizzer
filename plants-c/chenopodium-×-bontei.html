Chenopodium × bontei

Chenopodium × bontei, commonly known as hybrid goosefoot, is a perennial plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, and its seeds are encased in a hard, triangular capsule. Chenopodium × bontei is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is sometimes used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and its tolerance to harsh climates.
