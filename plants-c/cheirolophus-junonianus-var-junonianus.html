Cheirolophus junonianus var. junonianus

Cheirolophus junonianus var. junonianus, commonly known as the Junonian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early summer.
