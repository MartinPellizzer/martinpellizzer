Cryptocoryne longicauda

Cryptocoryne longicauda, commonly known as the long-tailed cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, ribbon-like leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water with moderate to high light conditions. It is often used in planted tanks for its aesthetic appeal and ability to oxygenate the water.

Cryptocoryne longicauda is also known for its resilience and adaptability to various water parameters.
