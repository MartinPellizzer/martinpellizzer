Chaetogastra lindeniana

Chaetogastra lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenberg's thorn.

It is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a rough texture, while its stems are woody and covered in thorns.

This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
