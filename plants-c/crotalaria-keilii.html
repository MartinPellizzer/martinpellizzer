Crotalaria keilii

Crotalaria keilii, commonly known as the keil's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces spherical seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid conditions.

Crotalaria keilii is also known for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
