Cattleya lobata

Cattleya lobata, commonly known as the Lumpy Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

Cattleya lobata is prized for its vibrant colors and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
