Cycnoches egertonianum var. egertonianum

Cycnoches egertonianum var. egertonianum, commonly known as the Egertonian water lily, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that form a dense canopy on the water surface. The plant produces showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the early morning and close by midday. Its rhizomes are submerged, anchoring the plant in shallow water while absorbing nutrients.

This species plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
