Chrysobalanus icaco subsp. orbicularis

Chrysobalanus icaco subsp.

orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular chrysobalanus, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height.

The plant features elliptic to orbicular leaves that are glossy and dark green on top, with a lighter underside. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in panicles, and it produces woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture and landscaping.
