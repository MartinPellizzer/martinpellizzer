Cytisus austriacus f. umbrosus

Cytisus austriacus f. umbrosus, commonly known as the Austrian broom dwarf form, is a compact shrub native to the Alps.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This form is distinguished by its more compact growth and darker foliage compared to the typical species.
