Calanthe veratrifolia var. densissima

Calanthe veratrifolia var. densissima, commonly known as the dense-leaved calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense arrangement of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellowish throat. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
