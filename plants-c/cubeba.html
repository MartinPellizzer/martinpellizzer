Cubeba

Cubeba, commonly known as *Eupatorium cubebam*, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its essential oils, which are extracted from its leaves and stems. Cubeba is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.

Its growth habit includes upright stems and a preference for well-drained soils in tropical climates.
