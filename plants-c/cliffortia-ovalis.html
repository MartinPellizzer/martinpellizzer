Cliffortia ovalis

Cliffortia ovalis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisy or silver daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features delicate, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and silvery-green, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Cliffortia ovalis is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
