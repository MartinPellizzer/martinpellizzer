Cotoneaster wanbooyensis

Cotoneaster wanbooyensis, commonly known as the Wanbooye cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

Cotoneaster wanbooyensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.
