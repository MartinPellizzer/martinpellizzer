Cnicus weyrichii

Cnicus weyrichii, commonly known as the Weyrich's thistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are typically lobed and covered in coarse hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Cnicus weyrichii plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food and habitat for various insects and small animals.
