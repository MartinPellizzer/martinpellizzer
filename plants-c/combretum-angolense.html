Combretum angolense

Combretum angolense, commonly known as the Angolan flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
