Cynanchum sublanceolatum var. obtusulum

Cynanchum sublanceolatum var. obtusulum, commonly known as the blunt-leaved milkweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds known for their unique floral structures. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with a bluish-green hue and a distinctive, milky sap that is toxic to many animals. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are typically pink to reddish in color, attracting various pollinators.

This variant is distinguished by its obtuse leaf tips, setting it apart from other members of the species.
