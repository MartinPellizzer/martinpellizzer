Cotyledon greenei

Cotyledon greenei, commonly known as the African Sunset, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, providing a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cotyledon greenei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
