Citrus hystrix var. annamensis

Citrus hystrix var. annamensis, commonly known as the Annam citron, is a rare citrus variant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, spiny rind and small, sour fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, with a strong citrus aroma.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and unique flavor profile.
