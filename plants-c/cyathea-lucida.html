Cyathea lucida

Cyathea lucida, commonly known as the shining tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its fronds are typically bright green and can persist for several years before dying and being replaced.

Cyathea lucida is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance in horticulture.
