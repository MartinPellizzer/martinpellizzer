Callerya Cochinchinensis

Chinese callerya, scientifically known as Callerya cochinchinensis, is a striking ornamental plant native to eastern Asia. It belongs to the legume family and is prized for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and can thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Often used in gardens and landscapes, it adds both beauty and ecological value. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and nature enthusiasts.
