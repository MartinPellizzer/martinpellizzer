Campanula rhomboidalis var. goudetiana

Campanula rhomboidalis var. goudetiana, commonly known as the Goudet's bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in summer. The plant has lance-shaped to rhomboid leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
