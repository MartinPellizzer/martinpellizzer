Coix Lacryma-Jobi Var. Ma-Yuen

Coix lacryma-jobi var. ma-yuen is a unique and ancient grain known for its distinct appearance and nutritional value. Often referred to as Job's tears, this plant has been cultivated for thousands of years in Asia. Its seeds are small, elongated, and covered in a thin, translucent membrane that resembles tears. The plant is valued not only for its health benefits but also for its cultural and medicinal significance. It is increasingly gaining attention in modern health and wellness circles.
