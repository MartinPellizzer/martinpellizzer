Cotyledon jurgensenii

Cotyledon jurgensenii, commonly known as the Jurgensen's windowleaf, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often lobed or notched, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Cotyledon jurgensenii is prized in horticulture for its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
