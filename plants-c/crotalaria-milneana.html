Crotalaria milneana

Crotalaria milneana, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in racemes, and its compound leaves with stipules. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and cleared areas.

Crotalaria milneana is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in soil enrichment.
