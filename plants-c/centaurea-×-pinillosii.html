Centaurea × pinillosii

Centaurea × pinillosii, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Centaurea cyanus (blue cornflower) and Centaurea jacea (red cornflower). The plant is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers and serrated leaf margins. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Centaurea × pinillosii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
