Cyrtostylis oblonga var. rotundifolia

Cyrtostylis oblonga var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like inflorescence and its broad, ovate leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within rainforest habitats. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
