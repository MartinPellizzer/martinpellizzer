Calathea longipetiolata

Calathea longipetiolata, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns and a deep green color. The plant features long, slender petioles that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Calathea longipetiolata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
