Combretum laurentii

Combretum laurentii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental species in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth rate and ability to tolerate drought conditions further enhance its appeal in landscaping.
