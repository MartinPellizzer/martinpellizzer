Cereus speciosissimus var. hansii

Cereus speciosissimus var. hansii, commonly known as the Hansii Christmas Cactus, is a hybrid of the Schlumbergera truncata and Schlumbergera russelliana species.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its thick, segmented stems that bear clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular houseplant due to its long blooming period, often extending from late autumn through winter.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a favored choice for holiday displays.
