Cryptocarya lyoniifolia

Cryptocarya lyoniifolia, commonly known as the Lyoniifolia cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

Cryptocarya lyoniifolia is often found in rainforest and littoral rainforest habitats, growing as an understory plant.
