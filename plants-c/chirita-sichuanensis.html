Chirita sichuanensis

Chirita sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan chireta, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant orange or red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
