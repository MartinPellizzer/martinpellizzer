Cousinia × heterogenetos

Cousinia × heterogenetos, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Cousinia × heterogenetos is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
