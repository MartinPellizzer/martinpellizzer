Coronilla repanda var. dura

Coronilla repanda var. dura, commonly known as the hardy crown vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is known for its deep root system, which helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is often used in landscaping and restoration projects due to its hardiness and adaptability.
