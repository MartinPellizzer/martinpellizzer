Cattleya neozaslawskii

Cattleya neozaslawskii, commonly known as the Neozaslawskii Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in hue. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat with minimal rainfall. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it prefers bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms.
