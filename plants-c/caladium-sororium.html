Caladium sororium

Caladium sororium, commonly known as the pink caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is the main ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for garden displays.

Caladium sororium is typically grown as an annual in cooler regions but can be perennial in tropical environments.
