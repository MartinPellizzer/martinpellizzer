Ctenium digitatum

Ctenium digitatum, commonly known as the slender ctenium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in dense, branched clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It is native to regions of southern Africa and thrives in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in natural habitats.
