Cleisostoma schneideri

Cleisostoma schneideri, commonly known as the Schneiders' cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cleisostoma schneideri is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
