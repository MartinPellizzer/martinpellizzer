Chamaedorea seifrizii

Chamaedorea seifrizii, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen or the bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, pinnate leaves. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light conditions. Its compact size and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by dark, spherical berries.
