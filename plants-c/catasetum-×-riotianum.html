Catasetum × riotianum

Catasetum × riotianum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, colorful flowers that often feature a distinctive lip or labellum. The plant produces a unique structure called a "column," which is a fused stamen and pistil, typical of orchids. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Catasetum × riotianum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
