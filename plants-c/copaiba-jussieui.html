Copaiba jussieui

Copaiba jussieui, commonly known as copaiba, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic resinous sap, which has been used for centuries in traditional medicine. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall, with smooth, grayish-brown bark that exudes a fragrant oil when cut. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, while its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically yellow or green.

The plant is valued not only for its medicinal properties but also for its role in the ecosystem and its contribution to biodiversity.
