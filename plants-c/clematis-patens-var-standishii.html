Clematis patens var. standishii

Clematis patens var. standishii, commonly known as the Standish clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, blue-black berries in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to climb trellises or fences.
