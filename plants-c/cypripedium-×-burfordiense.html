Cypripedium × burfordiense

Cypripedium × burfordiense, commonly known as the Burford yellow root, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and has a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
