Cryptochilus reticulatus

Cryptochilus reticulatus, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, net-like vein patterns on its leaves, which give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique foliage structure.
