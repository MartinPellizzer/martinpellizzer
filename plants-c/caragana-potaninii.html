Caragana potaninii

Caragana potaninii, commonly known as the Siberian pea shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, legume-like pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to harsh environments and is often used for soil stabilization and erosion control.

Caragana potaninii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soils.
