Carex monile var. colorata

Carex monile var. colorata, commonly known as the colorata sedge, is a striking ornamental plant native to parts of Asia.

It features dense, upright clusters of bright green to orange-red foliage that adds vibrant color to gardens. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early summer, which are typically followed by three-sided, cylindrical seed heads. It thrives in moist to wet soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for water gardens and bog gardens.

Its compact size and colorful foliage make it an excellent addition to modern landscape designs.
