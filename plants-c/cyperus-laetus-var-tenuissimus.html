Cyperus laetus var. tenuissimus

Cyperus laetus var. tenuissimus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant native to wetland environments.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in shallow water or damp soil, making it a common sight in marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

It is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for aquatic life.
