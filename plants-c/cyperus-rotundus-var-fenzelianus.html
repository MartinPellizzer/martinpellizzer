Cyperus rotundus var. fenzelianus

Cyperus rotundus var. fenzelianus, commonly known as purple flatsedge, is a perennial grassy plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in wet or disturbed soils. It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its rapid growth rate.

Botanically, it is classified within the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants.
