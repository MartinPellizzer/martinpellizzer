Callipeltis Factorovskyi

Callipeltis factorovskyi is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This unique species is known for its striking appearance and delicate floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in dense forests with high biodiversity. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Studying this orchid offers valuable insights into ecological adaptation and the importance of preserving fragile ecosystems.
