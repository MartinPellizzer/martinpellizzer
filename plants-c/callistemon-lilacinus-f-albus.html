Callistemon lilacinus f. albus

Callistemon lilacinus f. albus, commonly known as the white bottlebrush, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to Australia.

It features striking white flowers that resemble bottlebrushes, blooming in winter and early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically evergreen, providing year-round foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract birds and insects.
