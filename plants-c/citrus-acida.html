Citrus acida

Citrus acida, commonly known as sour orange.

It is a species of citrus native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, yellow-orange fruits with a high acidity content. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, while its flowers are white and fragrant.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils due to its aromatic properties.
