Cymbidium kanran var. purpureohiemale

Cymbidium kanran var. purpureohiemale, commonly known as the purple-leafed kanran orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pendulous flowers. The leaves are typically broad and dark green, while the flowers display a striking combination of purple and white hues. This variety thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in horticultural gardens.
