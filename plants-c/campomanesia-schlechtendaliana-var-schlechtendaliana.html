Campomanesia schlechtendaliana var. schlechtendaliana

Campomanesia schlechtendaliana var. schlechtendaliana, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or araçá-do-brejo, is a small evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red, edible berries that are rich in vitamin C and are often used in traditional medicine.
