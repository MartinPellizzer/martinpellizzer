Crossandra brachystachys

Crossandra brachystachys, commonly known as the short-stemmed flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Crossandra brachystachys is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking floral display.
