Cheiridopsis hutchinsonii

Cheiridopsis hutchinsonii, commonly known as the silver leaf ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil.
