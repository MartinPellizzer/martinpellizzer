Cleome fritzscheae

Cleome fritzscheae, commonly known as the Mexican spider flower, is a herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features alternate, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Cleome fritzscheae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
