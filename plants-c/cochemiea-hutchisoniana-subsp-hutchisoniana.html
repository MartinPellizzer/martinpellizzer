Cochemiea hutchisoniana subsp. hutchisoniana

Cochemiea hutchisoniana subsp.

hutchisoniana, commonly known as the Mexican snowball cactus, is a succulent plant native to Mexico. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clustered growth habit.

The plant typically produces white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
