Calanthe puberula var. reflexa

Calanthe puberula var. reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed calanthe, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, lanceolate leaves that are often covered with a dense layer of fine, soft hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive reflexed lip that sets it apart from other calanthe species.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
