Cyclosorus papyraceus

Cyclosorus papyraceus, commonly known as the paper moss, is a small, terrestrial liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a distinctive leaf structure with a broad, undulating margin and a central midrib. Due to its delicate appearance and ecological importance, it is a valuable indicator species for assessing habitat quality.
