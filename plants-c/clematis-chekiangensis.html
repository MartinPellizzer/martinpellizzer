Clematis chekiangensis

Clematis chekiangensis, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis chekiangensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal blooms.
