Cucurbita polymorpha

Cucurbita polymorpha, commonly known as the naked-seed gourd, is a versatile vine native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like pumpkins and squash. The plant produces a variety of fruits with different shapes, sizes, and colors, ranging from small round gourds to elongated varieties. Its flowers are typically yellow, and the fruits can be harvested at various stages of maturity.

The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and has historical uses in traditional medicine and food preparation.
