Chorizanthe breweri

Chorizanthe breweri, commonly known as the Brewster's choris, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellow to orange blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
