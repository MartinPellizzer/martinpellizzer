Cynoglossum holosericeum

Cynoglossum holosericeum, commonly known as the silky leaf bellflower, is a herbaceous plant native to central and southern Europe.

It is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils. It blooms from late spring to early summer, and its flowers are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Cynoglossum holosericeum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
