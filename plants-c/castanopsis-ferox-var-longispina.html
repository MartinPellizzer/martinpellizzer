Castanopsis ferox var. longispina

Castanopsis ferox var. longispina, commonly known as the long-spined tanoak, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and chestnuts, and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates. Its fruit, which contains one or two seeds, is an important food source for various wildlife species.
