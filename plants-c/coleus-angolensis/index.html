Coleus Angolensis

Coleus angolensis is a strikingly colorful plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the mint family, known for its vibrant foliage and ornamental value. This species is prized for its unique leaf patterns and shades of red, purple, and green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens. Its beauty makes it a popular choice for those looking to add visual interest to their plant collections.
