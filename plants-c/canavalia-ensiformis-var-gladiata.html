Canavalia ensiformis var. gladiata

Canavalia ensiformis var. gladiata, commonly known as the sword bean or gladiola bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features elongated, narrow leaves and produces distinctive, showy flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and nutritional content, it is cultivated both for gardens and as a food crop.
