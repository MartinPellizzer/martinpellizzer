Colchicum stevenii var. vernale

Colchicum stevenii var. vernale, commonly known as the early autumn colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces a scape with several purple or reddish-purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or alpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
