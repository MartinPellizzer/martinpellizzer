Cotyledon flanaganii subsp. flanaganii

Cotyledon flanaganii subsp.

flanaganii, commonly known as the Flanagan's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, especially under stress or in bright light.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is a low-growing species, often forming dense clusters, and is well adapted to arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and collections.
