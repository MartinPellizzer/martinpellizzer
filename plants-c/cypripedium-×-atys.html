Cypripedium × atys

Cypripedium × atys, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow pouch-shaped lip, which is a key feature in its pollination strategy. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and requires well-drained, acidic soil. It produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Cypripedium × atys is a rare and protected species, valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
