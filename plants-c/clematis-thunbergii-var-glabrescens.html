Clematis thunbergii var. glabrescens

Clematis thunbergii var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to East Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
