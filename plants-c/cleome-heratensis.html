Cleome heratensis

Cleome heratensis, commonly known as the spider flower, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia.

It features showy, often pink or red, flower clusters that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant has alternate, ovate to lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Cleome heratensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with full sun.
