Capparis micracantha var. henryi

Capparis micracantha var. henryi, commonly known as the Chinese basket flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant has woody stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its ecological role in seed dispersal.
