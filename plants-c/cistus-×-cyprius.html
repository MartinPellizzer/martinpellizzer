Cistus × cyprius

Cistus × cyprius, commonly known as the Cyprus rock rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties, with essential oils extracted from its flowers and leaves used in traditional remedies.
