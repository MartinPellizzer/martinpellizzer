Ceropegia officinalis

Ceropegia officinalis, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tubular, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pink, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
