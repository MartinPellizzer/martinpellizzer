Campanula hercegovina f. umbrosa

Campanula hercegovina f. umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow campanula, is a hybrid cultivar of the campanula genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and deep blue to violet bell-shaped flowers. This variety thrives in partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens or shaded rock gardens. Its foliage is typically dark green and slightly hairy, adding texture to the landscape.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably in cooler climates.
