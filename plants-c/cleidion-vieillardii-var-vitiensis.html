Cleidion vieillardii var. vitiensis

Cleidion vieillardii var. vitiensis, commonly known as Vitiensis Clethra, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Clethraceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered white flowers and elliptical leaves with a slightly waxy texture. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in coastal or mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value is appreciated for its fragrant blooms and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for gardens in tropical climates.
