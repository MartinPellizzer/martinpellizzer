Coelogyne kaliana

Coelogyne kaliana, commonly known as the Kaliana orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Malaysia and Indonesia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its inflorescence is racemose, with numerous small to medium-sized blooms.

Coelogyne kaliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
