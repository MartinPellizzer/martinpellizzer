Cotyledon pistorinia

Cotyledon pistorinia, commonly known as the rock cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
