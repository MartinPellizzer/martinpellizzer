Cinnamomum tsangii

Cinnamomum tsangii, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or Cinnamomum tsangii, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The species is valued for its unique fragrance and medicinal properties.
