Callicoma serratifolium var. ferruginea

Callicoma serratifolium var. ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty-leafed callicoma, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its serrated, dark green leaves that have a distinctive rusty or reddish-brown underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
