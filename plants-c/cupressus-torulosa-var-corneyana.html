Cupressus torulosa var. corneyana

Cupressus torulosa var. corneyana, commonly known as the Corneyana curlleaf cypress, is a rare and unique variety of the curlleaf cypress.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and distinctive, scale-like leaves that give it a textured appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by woody cones, which contain the seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its hardiness and unique form.
