Crudia harmsiana var. velutina

Crudia harmsiana var. velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf Crudia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant has small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.
