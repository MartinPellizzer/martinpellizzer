Cotyledon Edulis

Cotyledon edulis is a unique plant known for its edible leaves and striking appearance. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its thick, fleshy leaves store water, making it well adapted to harsh climates. The plant has gained attention for its potential as a sustainable food source. Its resilience and nutritional value make it a promising candidate for future agriculture.
