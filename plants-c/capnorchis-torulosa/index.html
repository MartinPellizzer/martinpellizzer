Capnorchis Torulosa

Capnorchis torulosa is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the mountainous regions of Mexico. This unique plant is known for its striking, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires specific humidity levels to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Understanding and protecting this species is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
