Calanticaria brevifolia

Calanticaria brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved calanticaria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial herb that produces yellow flowers, which attract various pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
