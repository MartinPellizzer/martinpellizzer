Catospermum goodeniacea

Catospermum goodeniacea, commonly known as the Goodeniaceae species, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often showy flowers. The plant typically has slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
