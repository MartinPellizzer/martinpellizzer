Ceropegia longii

Ceropegia longii, commonly known as the dwarf string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and tubular, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
