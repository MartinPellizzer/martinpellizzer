Cordia patens f. polycephala

Cordia patens f. polycephala, commonly known as the many-headed cordia, is a variant of the Cordia patens species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and multiple heads, which give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This form is particularly valued in horticulture for its ability to form a dense, rounded shrub.
