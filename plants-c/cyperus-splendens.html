Cyperus splendens

Cyperus splendens, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its upright, grass-like stems and small, clustered inflorescences that often display a striking purple or pink color. Its leaves are narrow and slightly pointed, with a fibrous texture.

Cyperus splendens is often found in wetland habitats, including marshes and along the edges of ponds and lakes.
