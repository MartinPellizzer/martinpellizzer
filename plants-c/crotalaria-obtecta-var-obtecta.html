Crotalaria obtecta var. obtecta

Crotalaria obtecta var. obtecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, characterized by its legume pods and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with erect stems and trifoliate leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow with prominent violet veins, and it produces small, elongated pods containing several seeds.
