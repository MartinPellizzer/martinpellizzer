Chamaerops humilis var. elegans

Chamaerops humilis var. elegans, commonly known as the slender palm or the Mediterranean fan palm, is a compact, ornamental palm native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It features a slender, upright trunk with a crown of feathery, arching leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, black fruits that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping in Mediterranean climates.
