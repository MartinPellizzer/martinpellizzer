Carduus defloratus subsp. medioformis

Carduus defloratus subsp.

medioformis, commonly known as the common thistle. This subspecies is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a robust stem covered in spiny leaves and produces purple or pink flowers. The plant is adapted to grow in a variety of habitats, including meadows and disturbed soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
