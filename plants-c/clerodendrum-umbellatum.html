Clerodendrum umbellatum

Clerodendrum umbellatum, commonly known as the hanging basket plant, is a flowering shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
