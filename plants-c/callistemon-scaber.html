Callistemon scaber

Callistemon scaber, commonly known as the rough bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, cylindrical, bright red or pink flower spikes that resemble bottle brushes. The plant has smooth, grey-green leaves that are often covered in fine, rough hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The rough bottlebrush is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract birds and insects.
