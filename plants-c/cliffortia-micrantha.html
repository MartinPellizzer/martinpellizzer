Cliffortia micrantha

Cliffortia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in its arid environment.

Cliffortia micrantha is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
