Cerastium brachypetalum subsp. corcyrense

Cerastium brachypetalum subsp.

corcyrense, commonly known as the Corcyrene chickweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May.

The plant has hairy, succulent stems and leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Caryophyllaceae family.
