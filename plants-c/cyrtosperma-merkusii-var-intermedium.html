Cyrtosperma merkusii var. intermedium

Cyrtosperma merkusii var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate merkusia, is a climbing plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white spadices. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It has a fibrous root system and can grow up to several meters in length.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in botanical gardens and ecological studies.
