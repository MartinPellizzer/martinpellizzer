Calathea argyraea

Calathea argyraea, commonly known as the silver stripe calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled leaves that feature silvery-white veins against a deep green background. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea argyraea is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
