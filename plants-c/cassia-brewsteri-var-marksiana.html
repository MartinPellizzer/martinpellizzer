Cassia brewsteri var. marksiana

Cassia brewsteri var. marksiana, commonly known as the desert cassia, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions. Its seeds are encased in elongated pods and are dispersed by wind.

Cassia brewsteri var. marksiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
