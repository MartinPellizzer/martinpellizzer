Canavalia megalantha

Canavalia megalantha, commonly known as the giant jack bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and is characterized by its large, compound leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Canavalia megalantha is also notable for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.

However, its seeds contain toxic compounds and should be handled with caution.
