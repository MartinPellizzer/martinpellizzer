Catevala coarctata

Catevala coarctata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Catevala coarctata is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in forest understories or along stream banks.
