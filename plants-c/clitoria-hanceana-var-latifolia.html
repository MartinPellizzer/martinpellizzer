Clitoria hanceana var. latifolia

Clitoria hanceana var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that resemble butterflies, which are typically clustered in racemes. The plant has heart-shaped, broad leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The flowers are also used in herbal teas and as a natural dye due to their vibrant color.
