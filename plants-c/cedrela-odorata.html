Cedrela odorata

Cedrela odorata, commonly known as Spanish cedar, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic wood, which is highly valued for its durability and resistance to pests. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release winged seeds.

Cedrela odorata plays an important role in tropical ecosystems and is also used in traditional medicine.
