Coelogyne aculeata

Coelogyne aculeata, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of 10 to 20, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Coelogyne aculeata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
