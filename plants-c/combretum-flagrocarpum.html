Combretum flagrocarpum

Combretum flagrocarpum, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its bark is smooth and often reddish in color, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Combretum flagrocarpum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
