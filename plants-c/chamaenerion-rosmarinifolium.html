Chamaenerion rosmarinifolium

Chamaenerion rosmarinifolium, commonly known as rosemary fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are aromatic and often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, pink to purple flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
