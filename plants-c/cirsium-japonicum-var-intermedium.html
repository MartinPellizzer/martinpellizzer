Cirsium japonicum var. intermedium

Cirsium japonicum var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate thistle, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped with rough, prickly edges, and its stems are often hairy.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various garden conditions.
