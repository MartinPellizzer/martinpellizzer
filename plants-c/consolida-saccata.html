Consolida saccata

Consolida saccata, commonly known as the Saccate Consolida, is a flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and features bright yellow or orange flowers with a distinctive spurred shape. The plant has compound leaves and produces pods that contain small, rounded seeds. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and easy care requirements.
