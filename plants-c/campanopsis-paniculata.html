Campanopsis paniculata

Campanopsis paniculata, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and blue, often arranged in panicles. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, and features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Campanopsis paniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
