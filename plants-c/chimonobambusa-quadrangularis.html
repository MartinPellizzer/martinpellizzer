Chimonobambusa quadrangularis

Chimonobambusa quadrangularis, commonly known as the square bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctively square-shaped stems, which are a defining botanical feature. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and arranged in a dense, compact form. It grows in clumps and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
