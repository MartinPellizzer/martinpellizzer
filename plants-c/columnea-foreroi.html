Columnea foreroi

Columnea foreroi, commonly known as the Forero's flame.

It is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America. This evergreen shrub produces striking red and orange tubular flowers that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
