Chrysanthemum pyrethroides

Chrysanthemum pyrethroides, commonly known as the yellow chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The species is also valued for its potential in traditional medicine and as a source of natural insecticides.
