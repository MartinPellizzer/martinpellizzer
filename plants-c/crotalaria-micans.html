Crotalaria micans

Crotalaria micans, commonly known as the silverleaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically borne on slender peduncles. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately lobed, often with a velvety texture.

Crotalaria micans is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
