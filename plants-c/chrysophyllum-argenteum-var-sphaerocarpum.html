Chrysophyllum argenteum var. sphaerocarpum

Chrysophyllum argenteum var. sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the silverleaf starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, star-shaped yellow fruits. The plant produces clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit, while edible, is often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
