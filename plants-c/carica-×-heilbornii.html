Carica × heilbornii

Carica × heilbornii, commonly known as the hybrid papaya.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Carica papaya and Carica officinalis. This plant typically exhibits characteristics of both parent species, including a combination of fruit size, color, and flavor. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential fruit production.

The hybrid is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of tropical and subtropical climates.
