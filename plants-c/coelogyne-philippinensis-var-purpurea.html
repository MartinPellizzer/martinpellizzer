Coelogyne philippinensis var. purpurea

Coelogyne philippinensis var. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple-Flowered Orchid, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, hairy stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to purple flowers with dark purple markings, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
