Calanthe bingtaoi

Calanthe bingtaoi, commonly known as the Bingtao calanthe, is a rare and ornamental orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale pink to white blooms with a distinct, intricate pattern on the petals and sepals. It prefers cool, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
