Conyza bifrons var. radiata

Conyza bifrons var. radiata, commonly known as the radiate conyza, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its composite flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk flowers, giving it a daisy-like appearance. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas and is native to parts of Africa and the Americas. It has hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

The flowers bloom in the summer and produce small, wind-dispersed seeds.
