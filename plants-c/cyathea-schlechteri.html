Cyathea schlechteri

Cyathea schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's tree fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Cyatheaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a layer of persistent, dark brown, fibrous roots. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Cyathea schlechteri thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in rainforest ecosystems.
