Cattleya velutina

Cattleya velutina, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, dark green leaves and its striking, fragrant flowers that range from white to pale pink. The plant produces pendulous blooms with prominent, showy sepals and petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya velutina is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty in both gardens and as a cut flower.
