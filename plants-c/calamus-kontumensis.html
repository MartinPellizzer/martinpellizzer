Calamus kontumensis

Calamus kontumensis, commonly known as the Kontum reed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Vietnam, particularly in the Kontum province. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calamus kontumensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited use in traditional medicine.
