Citrosma dimidiata

Citrosma dimidiata, commonly known as the two-seed citrosma, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruits are small, round, and have a thick, leathery rind.
