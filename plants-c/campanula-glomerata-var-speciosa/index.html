Campanula Glomerata Var. Speciosa

Campanula glomerata var. speciosa is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its clusters of bell-shaped blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. This variety is prized for its vibrant blue to violet flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. It is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental value and ecological benefits. Its delicate appearance makes it a standout addition to rock gardens and woodland borders.
