Corymborkis sakisimensis

Corymborkis sakisimensis, commonly known as the Sakishima orchid, is a rare and unique species native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

This terrestrial orchid features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth. The plant is noted for its compact size and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited natural range and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native environment.
