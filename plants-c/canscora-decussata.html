Canscora decussata

Canscora decussata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a distinctive zigzag stem that aids in climbing and supports its growth habit. The plant has small, opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer.

Canscora decussata is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
