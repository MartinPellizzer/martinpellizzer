Cranichis pilosa

Cranichis pilosa, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
