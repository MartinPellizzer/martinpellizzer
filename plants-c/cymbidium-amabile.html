Cymbidium amabile

Cymbidium amabile, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that produces tall, erect stems with clustered flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a sweet, fragrant aroma. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
