Cyperus festivus

Cyperus festivus, commonly known as the festive flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, making it a common sight in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes allow it to spread vegetatively, contributing to its ability to form dense stands.

Cyperus festivus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various water conditions.
