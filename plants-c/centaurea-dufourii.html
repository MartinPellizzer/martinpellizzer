Centaurea dufourii

Centaurea dufourii, commonly known as the Dufour's thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems and bright blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny environments such as rocky soils and coastal areas.

Centaurea dufourii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
