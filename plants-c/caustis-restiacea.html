Caustis restiacea

Caustis restiacea, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.
