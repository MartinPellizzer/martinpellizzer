Cordia platythyrsa

Cordia platythyrsa, commonly known as the broad-leafed cordia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.
