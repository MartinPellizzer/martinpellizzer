Cayaponia racemosa var. palmatipartita

Cayaponia racemosa var. palmatipartita, commonly known as the broad-leafed cayaponia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves that can reach several feet in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its roots are known for their medicinal properties, used traditionally in local herbal remedies.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests and along riverbanks.
