Cyrtandra × prasina

Cyrtandra × prasina, commonly known as the hybrid Cyrtandra, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra pedata and Cyrtandra elliptica. This species is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to tropical regions of the Pacific, including Hawaii and the Marquesas Islands.

Cyrtandra × prasina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is found in shaded, moist environments.
