Camellia yunnanensis

Camellia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a yellow center. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are dark green and leathery in texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Camellia yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
