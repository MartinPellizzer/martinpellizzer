Citharexylum integerrimum

Citharexylum integerrimum, commonly known as the broadleaf cithara or the honeycomb tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in terminal clusters, which bloom during the warmer months.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
