Coccoloba verapazensis

Coccoloba verapazensis, commonly known as the Verapaz beautyberry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is native to Central America, particularly found in countries like Honduras and Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
