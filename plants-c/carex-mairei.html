Carex mairei

Carex mairei, commonly known as the Maire sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spike-like flower clusters. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest understories.

Carex mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
