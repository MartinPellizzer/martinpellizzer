Croton floribundus var. piauhyensis

Croton floribundus var. piauhyensis, commonly known as the Piauhy croton, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful foliage, which can range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
