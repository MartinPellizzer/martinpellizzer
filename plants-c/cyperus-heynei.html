Cyperus heynei

Cyperus heynei, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and wetlands. Its slender, grass-like stems are often clustered at the base, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Cyperus heynei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist environments.
