Combretum stenophyllum

Combretum stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its lanceolate, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, followed by distinctive, woody capsules as fruits. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in savannas and woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its vibrant floral display.
