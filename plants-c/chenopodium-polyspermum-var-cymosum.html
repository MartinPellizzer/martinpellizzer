Chenopodium polyspermum var. cymosum

Chenopodium polyspermum var. cymosum, commonly known as goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is characterized by its broad, succulent leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, green flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by edible, round, red berries. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in disturbed soils.

This plant is also valued for its nutritional content and has been used in traditional cuisines.
