Cyclopogon glabrescens

Cyclopogon glabrescens, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cyclopogon, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its slender, unbranched stem.
