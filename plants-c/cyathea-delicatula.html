Cyathea delicatula

Cyathea delicatula, commonly known as the delicate tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and longevity. Its fronds are typically light green and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters.

Cyathea delicatula is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
