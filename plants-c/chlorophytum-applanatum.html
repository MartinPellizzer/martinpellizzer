Chlorophytum applanatum

Chlorophytum applanatum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and long, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with green and white patterns. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers during the growing season. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to purify the air and its low maintenance requirements further enhance its appeal.
