Chamaecrista Monticola

Chamaecrista monticola is a hardy legume native to the southeastern united states. it thrives in sandy soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal landscapes. this plant is known for its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. it is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting surrounding plants. its resilience and beauty make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
