Calathea marcelli

Calathea marcelli, commonly known as the Marcelli Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea marcelli is relatively low maintenance but sensitive to temperature changes and drafts.
