Calamus javensis var. purpurascens

Calamus javensis var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia, where it thrives in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems that often exhibit a distinctive purple hue, especially in younger growth. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a waxy texture, while the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
