Cyperus aster var. aster

Cyperus aster var. aster, commonly known as the star sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, clustered flower spikes that bloom in summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils.
