Cotyledon eliseae

Cotyledon eliseae, commonly known as the Elisea's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Cotyledon eliseae is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
