Cymbidium limbatum

Cymbidium limbatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces tall, upright stems with clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow to orange in color. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, often with a waxy coating.

Cymbidium limbatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
