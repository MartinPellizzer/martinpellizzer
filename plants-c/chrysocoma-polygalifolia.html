Chrysocoma polygalifolia

Chrysocoma polygalifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf chrysocoma, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters, typically in the spring. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Chrysocoma polygalifolia thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
