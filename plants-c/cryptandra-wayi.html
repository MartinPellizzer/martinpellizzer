Cryptandra wayi

Cryptandra wayi, commonly known as the West Australian cryptandra, is a shrub native to the southwestern region of Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are woody capsules that release seeds when mature.

Cryptandra wayi is adapted to sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland heathlands.
