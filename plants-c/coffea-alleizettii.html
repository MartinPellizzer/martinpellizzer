Coffea alleizettii

Coffea alleizettii, commonly known as the Brazilian coffee tree, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a small evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that contain the seeds used for coffee production. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
