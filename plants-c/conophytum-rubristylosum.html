Conophytum rubristylosum

Conophytum rubristylosum, commonly known as the red-stemmed conophytum, is a member of the Aizoaceae family, native to South Africa.

It is a small, succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
