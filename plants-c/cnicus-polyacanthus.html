Cnicus polyacanthus

Cnicus polyacanthus, commonly known as the prickly sow-thistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is native to Europe, western Asia, and parts of North Africa. The plant features lobed, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Cnicus polyacanthus is an annual plant that can be invasive in certain regions.
