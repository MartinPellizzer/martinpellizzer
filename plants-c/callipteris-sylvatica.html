Callipteris sylvatica

Callipteris sylvatica, commonly known as the scrub palmetto, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the coastal areas of the Gulf Coast. This plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and a short, sturdy trunk. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and scrub habitats.

The scrub palmetto plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
