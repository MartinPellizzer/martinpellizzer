Clinopodium chinense var. urticifolium

Clinopodium chinense var. urticifolium, commonly known as Chinese hedge nettle, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spike-like clusters, typically blooming in summer. Its stems are erect and can grow up to 1 meter in height, often branching near the base.

This species is adapted to moist, shady environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
