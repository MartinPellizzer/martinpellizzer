Centaurea scabiosa f. angustifrons

Centaurea scabiosa f. angustifrons, commonly known as narrow-leaved cudweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
