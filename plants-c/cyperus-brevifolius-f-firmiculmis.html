Cyperus brevifolius f. firmiculmis

Cyperus brevifolius f. firmiculmis, commonly known as the firm-leaved flatsedge, is a variant of the flatsedge plant characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically thrives in wetland or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to moist conditions. The leaves are narrow and often have a firm, thick texture, distinguishing it from other Cyperus species. This plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the Cyperaceae family.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, contributing to the ecological diversity of wetland habitats.
