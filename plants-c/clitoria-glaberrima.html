Clitoria glaberrima

Clitoria glaberrima, commonly known as the African blue pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing legume characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Clitoria glaberrima thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
