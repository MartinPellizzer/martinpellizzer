Croton hasslerianus

Croton hasslerianus, commonly known as the Hassler's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 3 meters.
