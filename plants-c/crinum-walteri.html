Crinum walteri

Crinum walteri, commonly known as Walter's crinum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white or pale purple flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with moderate sunlight.

Crinum walteri is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
