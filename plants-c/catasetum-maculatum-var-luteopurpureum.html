Catasetum maculatum var. luteopurpureum

Catasetum maculatum var. luteopurpureum, commonly known as the Purple-spotted Catasetum, is a tropical orchid characterized by its striking, colorful flowers.

It features showy, spurred blooms that range in color from purple to pink and yellow, with a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its vibrant, exotic appearance.
