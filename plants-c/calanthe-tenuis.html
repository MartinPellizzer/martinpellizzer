Calanthe tenuis

Calanthe tenuis, commonly known as the slender calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, pale greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Calanthe tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
