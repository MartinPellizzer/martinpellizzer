Corallodendron umbrosum

Corallodendron umbrosum, commonly known as the shadow tree, is a unique member of the Bignoniaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, coral-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy appearance.

Corallodendron umbrosum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
