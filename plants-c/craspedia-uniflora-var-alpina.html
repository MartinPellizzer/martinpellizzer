Craspedia uniflora var. alpina

Craspedia uniflora var. alpina, commonly known as the alpine starburry, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of South America.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. It thrives in cool, dry climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations.
