Calycorectes rodriguesii

Calycorectes rodriguesii, commonly known as Rodrigues's starling, is a unique plant species native to the island of Rodrigues in the Indian Ocean.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant has a compact growth habit with dense foliage, and its leaves are usually small and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
