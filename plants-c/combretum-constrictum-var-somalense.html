Combretum constrictum var. somalense

Combretum constrictum var. somalense, commonly known as the Somali flame bush, is a shrub native to the Horn of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in local traditional medicine.
