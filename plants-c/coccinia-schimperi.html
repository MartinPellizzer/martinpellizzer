Coccinia schimperi

Coccinia schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian cucumber or African leaf vine, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, gourds, and melons. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow, cucurbit-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in traditional medicine and local agriculture.
