Calystegia Silvatica

Calystegia silvatica is a flowering plant known for its striking white to pale purple flowers. It is commonly found in woodland and shrubland habitats across Europe and parts of Asia. This perennial vine is often mistaken for other similar species due to its delicate appearance. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adding to its distinctive look. The plant plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance.
