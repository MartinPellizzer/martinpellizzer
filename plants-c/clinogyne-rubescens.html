Clinogyne rubescens

Clinogyne rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered rock heath.

It is a flowering plant native to the eastern regions of Australia, typically found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, showy red flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It has a low-growing habit with narrow, leathery leaves that help it conserve water.

Clinogyne rubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
