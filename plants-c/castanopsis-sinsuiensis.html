Castanopsis sinsuiensis

Castanopsis sinsuiensis, commonly known as the Sinsui chestnut.

It is a species of evergreen tree belonging to the Fagaceae family, native to the mountainous regions of southern China. The plant is characterized by its dense, glossy leaves and produces small, spiny fruits that contain edible nuts. It thrives in humid, subtropical climates and is often found in mixed forests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
