Carenidium venustum

Carenidium venustum, commonly known as the golden spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically bright green with a yellow or orange tint. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
