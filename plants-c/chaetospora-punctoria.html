Chaetospora punctoria

Chaetospora punctoria, commonly known as the punctured sponge.

It is a marine red alga that thrives in shallow, warm waters, often found in coral reef environments. The plant has a distinctive branching structure with a soft, spongy texture. Its reproductive structures are embedded within the thallus, and it produces both male and female gametes.

Chaetospora punctoria plays a role in providing habitat and food for various marine organisms.
