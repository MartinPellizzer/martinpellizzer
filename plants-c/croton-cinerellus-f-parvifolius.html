Croton cinerellus f. parvifolius

Croton cinerellus f. parvifolius, commonly known as the small-leaf croton, is a tropical plant characterized by its distinctive foliage and branching structure.

It features palmate leaves with a velvety texture and a range of colors from green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
