Crotalaria bernieri

Crotalaria bernieri, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in racemes. The plant has palmate leaves with three to five lobes, and it produces pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun.
