Cryptanthus ruthae

Cryptanthus ruthae, commonly known as the "Firecracker" or "Red Curls" plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and vibrant, colorful inflorescences. The plant produces small, spiky flowers that range in color from red to orange, which are borne on short, erect stems. Cryptanthus ruthae thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and striking appearance.
