Cymbopetalum magnifructum

Cymbopetalum magnifructum, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting birds and insects. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Cymbopetalum magnifructum is valued for its vibrant floral display and can be grown as a vine or trained on trellises.
