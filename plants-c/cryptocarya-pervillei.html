Cryptocarya pervillei

Cryptocarya pervillei, commonly known as the Perville's cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

Cryptocarya pervillei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
