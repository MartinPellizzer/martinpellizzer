Centaurea rhapontica var. angustifolia

Centaurea rhapontica var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf rhapontic centaury, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia and parts of Europe.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces striking blue to purple flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological studies and horticultural use.
