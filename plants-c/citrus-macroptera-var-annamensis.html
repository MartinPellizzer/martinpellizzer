Citrus macroptera var. annamensis

Citrus macroptera var. annamensis, commonly known as the Annam citron, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellow-orange fruit, which is smaller and more acidic than typical citrus fruits. The plant has thick, dark green leaves and produces small, white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

The fruit is used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent in local cuisines.
