Cinnamomum cebuense

Cinnamomum cebuense, commonly known as the Cebu laurel, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, aromatic bark. The plant belongs to the Lauraceae family and is valued for its medicinal and aromatic properties. Its essential oils are often used in perfumery and traditional medicine.

The tree is also an important component of the native flora in its natural habitat.
