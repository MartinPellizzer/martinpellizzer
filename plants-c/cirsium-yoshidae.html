Cirsium yoshidae

Cirsium yoshidae, commonly known as Yoshida's thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a robust root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
