Cotula pyrethrifolia

Cotula pyrethrifolia, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with numerous small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Cotula pyrethrifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
