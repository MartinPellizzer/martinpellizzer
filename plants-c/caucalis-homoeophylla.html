Caucalis homoeophylla

Caucalis homoeophylla, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Western Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight hairy texture.

Caucalis homoeophylla is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with proper soil and moisture conditions.
