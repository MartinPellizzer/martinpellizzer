Chimonobambusa marmorea var. variegata

Chimonobambusa marmorea var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated marble bamboo, is a striking ornamental bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its unique variegated foliage, featuring a mix of white, green, and pink hues on its leaves. The plant grows as a clump-forming bamboo, with slender, gracefully arching stems that add a refined appearance to gardens. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Due to its cold hardiness, it is well-suited for temperate climates and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
