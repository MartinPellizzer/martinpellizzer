Cotoneaster silvestrii

Cotoneaster silvestrii, commonly known as the Italian cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedging plant.

Cotoneaster silvestrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract wildlife.
