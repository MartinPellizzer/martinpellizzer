Callicarpa glabrifolia

Callicarpa glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright purple berries that ripen in the fall. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are oval to elliptic in shape. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The berries are not only visually striking but also attract birds and other wildlife.
