Chenopodium carinatum var. melanocarpum

Chenopodium carinatum var. melanocarpum, commonly known as black goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that contain several seeds.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes used as a forage crop or in traditional medicine.
