Calochortus benthamii var. wallacei

Calochortus benthamii var. wallacei, commonly known as the Wallace's mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes a variety of flowering plants commonly referred to as mariposa lilies. This particular variety is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
