Carruthia capensis

Carruthia capensis, commonly known as the Cape carruthia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Carruthia capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
