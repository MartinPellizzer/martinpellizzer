Cotinus coggygria var. chengkouensis

Cotinus coggygria var. chengkouensis, commonly known as the Chengkou Chinese smoke tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fuzzy, pinkish-red inflorescences that appear in late spring to early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth margin and a glossy green upper surface. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden settings.
