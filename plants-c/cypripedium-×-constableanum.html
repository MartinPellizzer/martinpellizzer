Cypripedium × constableanum

Cypripedium × constableanum, commonly known as the Constable's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is typically pink or white in color. The plant is a terrestrial orchid, growing from a rhizome and producing a single stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and protected areas.
