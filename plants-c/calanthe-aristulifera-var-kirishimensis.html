Calanthe aristulifera var. kirishimensis

Calanthe aristulifera var. kirishimensis, commonly known as the Kirishima calanthe, is a rare and ornamental orchid native to Japan.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate beauty to its natural habitat.

Botanically, it is characterized by its unique inflorescence and specialized root system adapted to its specific ecological niche.
