Carex erawinensis

Carex erawinensis, commonly known as the Erawan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Thailand and Malaysia. This perennial herb typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and swamps. The plant has grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
