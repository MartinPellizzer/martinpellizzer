Calamagrostis Robusta

Calamagrostis robusta, commonly known as the robust reed grass, is a tall and hardy perennial grass native to parts of Europe and Asia. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks. With its upright growth habit and attractive silver-green foliage, this plant adds visual interest to landscapes. Its ability to tolerate waterlogged conditions makes it a valuable plant for erosion control and wetland restoration. Calamagrostis robusta is also a popular choice for ornamental gardens due to its graceful appearance and resilience.
