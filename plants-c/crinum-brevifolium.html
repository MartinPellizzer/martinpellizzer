Crinum brevifolium

Crinum brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved crinum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its strap-shaped, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Crinum brevifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in warm climates.
