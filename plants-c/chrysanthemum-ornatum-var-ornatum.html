Chrysanthemum ornatum var. ornatum

Chrysanthemum ornatum var. ornatum, commonly known as the garden chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 150 centimeters and has finely divided, feathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical features include a composite flower head composed of ray and disk florets, which contribute to its ornamental value.
