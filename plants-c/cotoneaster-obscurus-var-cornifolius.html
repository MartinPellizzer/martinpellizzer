Cotoneaster obscurus var. cornifolius

Cotoneaster obscurus var. cornifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a versatile addition to landscapes.
