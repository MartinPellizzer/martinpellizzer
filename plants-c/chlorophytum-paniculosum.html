Chlorophytum paniculosum

Chlorophytum paniculosum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often have a white or silver stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters at the ends of its stems. It is a hardy species that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum paniculosum is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to propagate through offsets.
