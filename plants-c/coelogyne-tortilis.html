Coelogyne tortilis

Coelogyne tortilis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spiraling inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne tortilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
