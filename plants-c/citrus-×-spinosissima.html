Citrus × spinosissima

Citrus × spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny lemon.

It is a hybrid citrus plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its thorny stems and small, sour fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with dark green, ovate leaves that have a rough texture. Its flowers are fragrant and white, often appearing in clusters, while the fruit is similar to a lemon but smaller and more acidic.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance and citrus scent.
