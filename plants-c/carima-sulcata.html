Carima sulcata

Carima sulcata, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Carima sulcata is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its flowers typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white, adding a striking visual appeal to indoor spaces.
