Chaetospora hexandra

Chaetospora hexandra, commonly known as the African orchid or the yellow orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive purple lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The botanical features of Chaetospora hexandra make it a unique and valuable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
