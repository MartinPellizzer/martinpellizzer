Convolvulus Erubescens Var. Angustissimus

Convolvulus erubescens var. angustissimus is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its vibrant red and orange flowers. It belongs to the bindweed family and is native to southern Africa. This variety is distinguished by its narrow leaves and compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its unique coloration and delicate appearance make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
