Cyrtanthus pumilio

Cyrtanthus pumilio, commonly known as the dwarf rain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, bell-shaped flowers. Cyrtanthus pumilio thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
