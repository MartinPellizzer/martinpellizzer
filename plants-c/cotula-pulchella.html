Cotula pulchella

Cotula pulchella, commonly known as the pretty daisy or yellow daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers surrounded by white or pale pink ray florets, creating a daisy-like appearance. Cotula pulchella is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate, cheerful blooms.
