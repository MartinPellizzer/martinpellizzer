Croton mac-arthurii

Croton mac-arthurii, commonly known as the MacArthur's croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that display shades of red, yellow, and green. It typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
