Cleisostoma inflexilobum

Cleisostoma inflexilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed hooded orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive hooded flower structure. The plant produces small, fragrant blossoms that are typically greenish-yellow with a white throat. Its leaves are usually clustered and strap-shaped, adapted for epiphytic growth on trees.

Cleisostoma inflexilobum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
