Capethia

Capethia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a waxy coating. Capethia species are popular as houseplants due to their ability to thrive in indoor conditions with moderate light and humidity.

They produce small, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and yellow.
