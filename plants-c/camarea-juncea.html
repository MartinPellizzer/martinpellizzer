Camarea juncea

Camarea juncea, commonly known as the slender panther's tail, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like philodendrons and peace lilies. The plant is characterized by its striking, elongated inflorescence that resembles a tail, hence its common name. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped and have a glossy texture.

Camarea juncea is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in humid environments.
