Cassia kleinii

Cassia kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's cassia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cassia kleinii is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
