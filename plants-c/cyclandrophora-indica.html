Cyclandrophora indica

Cyclandrophora indica, commonly known as the Indian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

Cyclandrophora indica is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
