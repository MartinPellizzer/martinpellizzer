Capnoides nobilis

Capnoides nobilis, commonly known as the noble capnoid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Capnoides nobilis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
