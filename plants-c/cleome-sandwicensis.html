Cleome sandwicensis

Cleome sandwicensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian cleome, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and have a lanceolate shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, with five petals and numerous stamens, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Cleome sandwicensis is adapted to the tropical climate of Hawaii and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and dry forests.
