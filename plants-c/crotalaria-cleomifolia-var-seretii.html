Crotalaria cleomifolia var. seretii

Crotalaria cleomifolia var. seretii, commonly known as the seretii crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The plant has been studied for its potential medicinal properties due to the presence of alkaloids in its foliage.
