Casearia hexagona var. gelonioides

Casearia hexagona var. gelonioides, commonly known as the gelonioides casearia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, elliptic leaves with smooth margins and a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by oval, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological studies of tropical flora.
