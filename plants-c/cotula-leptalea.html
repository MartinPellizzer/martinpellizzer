Cotula leptalea

Cotula leptalea, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, often arranged in clusters. Cotula leptalea is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
