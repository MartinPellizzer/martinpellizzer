Clerodendrum magnificum

Clerodendrum magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent glorybower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Clerodendrum magnificum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in warm climates.
