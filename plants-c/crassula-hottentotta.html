Crassula hottentotta

Crassula hottentotta, commonly known as the "Lucky Button" or "Hottentot's Button," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, round leaves that are typically green with a slight red or purple hue on the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula hottentotta is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
