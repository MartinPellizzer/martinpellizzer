Crotalaria mentiens

Crotalaria mentiens, commonly known as the "Cape York morning glory," is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and it produces elongated seed pods that split open when mature. Crotalaria mentiens thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grassy areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
