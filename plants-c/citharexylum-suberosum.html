Citharexylum suberosum

Citharexylum suberosum, commonly known as the silky tree or silky ash, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often covered in fine hairs.

Citharexylum suberosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
