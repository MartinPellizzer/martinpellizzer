Cotoneaster microphyllus rotundifolius

Cotoneaster microphyllus rotundifolius, commonly known as the small-leaved cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The species is also valued for its use in landscaping and as a hedging plant.
