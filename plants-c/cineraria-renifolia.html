Cineraria renifolia

Cineraria renifolia, commonly known as the woolly cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or coastal environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
