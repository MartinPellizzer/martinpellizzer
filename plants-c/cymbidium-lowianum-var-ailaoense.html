Cymbidium lowianum var. ailaoense

Cymbidium lowianum var. ailaoense, commonly known as the Ailaos orchid, is a rare and striking species native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and requires well-draining soil and moderate light.

This variety is highly valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
