Cassia × nealiae

Cassia × nealiae, commonly known as the hybrid cassia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds covered in a hairy, fibrous coating. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.

Cassia × nealiae is also used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
