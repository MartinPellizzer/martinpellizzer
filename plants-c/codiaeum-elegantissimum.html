Codiaeum elegantissimum

Codiaeum elegantissimum, commonly known as the flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated leaves that can display shades of red, orange, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
