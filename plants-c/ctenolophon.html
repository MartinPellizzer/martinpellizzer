Ctenolophon

Ctenolophon, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky blue or purple flowers that resemble a sea creature, hence its common name. Ctenolophon is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
