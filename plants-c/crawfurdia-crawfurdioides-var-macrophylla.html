Crawfurdia crawfurdioides var. macrophylla

Crawfurdia crawfurdioides var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved crawfurdia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 15 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
