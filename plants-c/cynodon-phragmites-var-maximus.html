Cynodon phragmites var. maximus

Cynodon phragmites var. maximus, commonly known as the tall couchgrass, is a variant of the common couchgrass species.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in moist or wet environments, such as marshes and along waterways. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces dense clusters of small, spike-like flowers. It is characterized by its robust growth habit and ability to thrive in nutrient-rich soils.

This variant is often found in wetland ecosystems and can play a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
