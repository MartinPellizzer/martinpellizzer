Clerodendrum inerme var. oceanicum

Clerodendrum inerme var. oceanicum, commonly known as the coastal clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in saline and humid environments, making it well-suited for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the year under favorable conditions.
