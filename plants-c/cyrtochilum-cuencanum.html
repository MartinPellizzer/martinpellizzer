Cyrtochilum cuencanum

Cyrtochilum cuencanum, commonly known as the Cuencan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Cyrtochilum cuencanum is noted for its ornamental value and is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral structure.
