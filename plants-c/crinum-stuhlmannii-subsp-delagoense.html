Crinum stuhlmannii subsp. delagoense

Crinum stuhlmannii subsp.

delagoense, commonly known as the Delagoa crinum, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa. It features large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has strap-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
