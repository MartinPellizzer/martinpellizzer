Cymbidium longibracteatum

Cymbidium longibracteatum, commonly known as the longibracteatum orchid, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers with red or purple markings. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Cymbidium longibracteatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown for its long-lasting blooms.
