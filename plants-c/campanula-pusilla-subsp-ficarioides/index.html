Campanula Pusilla Subsp. Ficarioides

Campanula pusilla subsp. ficarioides is a small, delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is known for its tiny, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring. This subspecies is often found in rocky or alpine environments where it thrives in well-drained soil. Its compact size and unique appearance make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. Despite its small stature, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and ecosystems.
