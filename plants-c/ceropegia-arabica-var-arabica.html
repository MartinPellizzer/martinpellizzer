Ceropegia arabica var. arabica

Ceropegia arabica var. arabica, commonly known as the silver leaf creeper, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive, waxy, silver-green leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various indoor environments.
