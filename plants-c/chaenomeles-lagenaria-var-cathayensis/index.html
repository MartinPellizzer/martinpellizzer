Chaenomeles Lagenaria Var. Cathayensis

Chaenomeles lagenaria var. cathayensis is a unique and lesser-known cultivar of the Chinese quince plant. It is prized for its ornamental value and distinct fruit characteristics. This variety is often found in subtropical and temperate climates where it thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom in early spring, adding beauty to gardens. Despite its charm, it remains relatively obscure compared to other quince varieties.
