Cissampelos ovalifolia f. ovatomucronata

Cissampelos ovalifolia f. ovatomucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed moon vine, is a variant of the moon vine species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing plant characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem with thorn-like projections, which help it to climb and support its growth. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The botanical features of this plant make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological study.
