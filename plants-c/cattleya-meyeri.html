Cattleya meyeri

Cattleya meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer's Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, and showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cattleya meyeri is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
