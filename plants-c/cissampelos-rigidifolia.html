Cissampelos rigidifolia

Cissampelos rigidifolia, commonly known as the rigid leaf moonvine, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

Cissampelos rigidifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along riverbanks.
