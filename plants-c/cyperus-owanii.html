Cyperus owanii

Cyperus owanii, commonly known as the African flat sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often inconspicuous, typical of many sedges. Cyperus owanii is often found in wetland habitats, including marshes and along the edges of water bodies.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and as a water purifier.
