Cortaderia planifolia

Cortaderia planifolia, commonly known as the flat leaf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It features slender, arching blades that are narrow and flat, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray plumes that add visual interest, especially in the fall and winter. It is often used in ornamental landscaping for its graceful form and seasonal color changes.

Cortaderia planifolia thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.
