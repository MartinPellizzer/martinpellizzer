Cineraria coincyi

Cineraria coincyi, commonly known as the Canary Islands cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has silvery-gray leaves that help reduce water loss in its arid environment.

Cineraria coincyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to full sun exposure.
