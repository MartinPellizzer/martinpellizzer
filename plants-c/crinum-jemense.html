Crinum jemense

Crinum jemense, commonly known as the Jemense glory lily, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Crinum jemense typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
