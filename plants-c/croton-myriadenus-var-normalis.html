Croton myriadenus var. normalis

Croton myriadenus var. normalis, commonly known as the giant croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, colorful leaves.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. The plant features broad, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length, often with a vibrant green or reddish hue. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by orange or yellow capsules containing seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in warm climates.
