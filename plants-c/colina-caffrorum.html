Colina caffrorum

Colina caffrorum, commonly known as the Cape coltsfoot, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped basal leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Colina caffrorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
