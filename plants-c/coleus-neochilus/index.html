Coleus Neochilus

Coleus neochilus is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor spaces. Its foliage is not only visually appealing but also adds a unique touch to any setting. Due to its adaptability and ease of care, it has become a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
