Cliffortia juniperina var. muricata

Cliffortia juniperina var. muricata, commonly known as the rough-leaved cliffortia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its unique morphology allows it to retain water efficiently, making it resilient to drought conditions.

This variety is often found growing on coastal cliffs and mountainous regions where it is exposed to strong winds and salt spray.
