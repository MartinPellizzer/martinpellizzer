Cuphea nitens

Cuphea nitens, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often grown as an ornamental in gardens. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Cuphea nitens is also valued for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
