Centrogenium setaceum

Centrogenium setaceum, commonly known as the featherleaf centipede plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
