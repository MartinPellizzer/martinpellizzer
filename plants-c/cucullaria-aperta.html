Cucullaria aperta

Cucullaria aperta, commonly known as the open hooded bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbellate inflorescence and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, often in the Mediterranean region. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and the fruit is a dry, two-valved schizocarp.

Cucullaria aperta is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
