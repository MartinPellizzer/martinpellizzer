Campyloneurum atlanticum

Campyloneurum atlanticum, commonly known as the Atlantic campyloneurum, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, acrocarpous growth form and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a distinct costa that extends to the apex. It exhibits a dark green color when dry and a vibrant green when hydrated.

This plant is often used in ecological studies due to its sensitivity to environmental changes.
