Centaurea goeksunensis

Centaurea goeksunensis, commonly known as the Goeksu Mountain star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Centaurea goeksunensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
