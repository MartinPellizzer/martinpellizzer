Columnea laevis

Columnea laevis, commonly known as the smooth columnea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, with a smooth texture that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and greenhouses.

The plant produces showy blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators.
