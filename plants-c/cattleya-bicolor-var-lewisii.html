Cattleya bicolor var. lewisii

Cattleya bicolor var. lewisii, commonly known as the Lewis's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking bicolor flowers, typically featuring a combination of white and pink or purple hues. The plant produces large, showy blooms that are often fragrant, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is known for its ease of cultivation and resilience, making it a favored option for both novice and experienced growers.
