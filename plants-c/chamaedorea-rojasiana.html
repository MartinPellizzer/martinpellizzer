Chamaedorea rojasiana

Chamaedorea rojasiana, commonly known as the Rojasiana bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and feather-like leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, making it well-suited for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is dense and glossy, adding an attractive visual element to any space.

It is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and indirect sunlight to thrive.
