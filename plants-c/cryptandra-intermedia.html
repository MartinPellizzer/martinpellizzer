Cryptandra intermedia

Cryptandra intermedia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cryptandra, is a flowering plant native to the south-western region of Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in shrublands and heathlands. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters, with dense, compact growth habit.
