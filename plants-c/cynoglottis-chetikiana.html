Cynoglottis chetikiana

Cynoglottis chetikiana, commonly known as the African buttonweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and oval, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Cynoglottis chetikiana is known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it both a weed and a potential ornamental plant.
