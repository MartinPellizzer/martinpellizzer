Cyrtosperma ferox

Cyrtosperma ferox, commonly known as the Ferox palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, spiny fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
