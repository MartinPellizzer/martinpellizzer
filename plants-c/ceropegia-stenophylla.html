Ceropegia stenophylla

Ceropegia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its succulent, trailing stems and small, opposite leaves.
