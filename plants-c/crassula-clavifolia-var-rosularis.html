Crassula clavifolia var. rosularis

Crassula clavifolia var. rosularis, commonly known as the Rosular Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit, with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
