Cotoneaster rubens var. minimus

Cotoneaster rubens var. minimus, commonly known as the dwarf cotoneaster, is a compact shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features small, glossy dark green leaves and produces clusters of bright red berries in the fall. The plant is characterized by its dense, matted growth habit and is often used as a ground cover or in rock gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is also appreciated for its ability to attract birds and other wildlife.
