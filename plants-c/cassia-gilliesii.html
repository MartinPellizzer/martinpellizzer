Cassia gilliesii

Cassia gilliesii, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Cassia gilliesii is an important species in Australian flora and is the national floral emblem of Australia.
