Combretum platypetalum subsp. virgatum

Combretum platypetalum subsp.

virgatum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences that appear in clusters of red or orange flowers.

The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a valuable addition to landscapes in warm climates.
