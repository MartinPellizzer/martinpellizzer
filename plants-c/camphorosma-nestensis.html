Camphorosma nestensis

Camphorosma nestensis, commonly known as the Nestean camphorweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.
