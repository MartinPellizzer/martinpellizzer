Ceropegia naorojii

Ceropegia naorojii, commonly known as the African violet or the silver leaf plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a silvery or white pubescence. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pale green or white with a distinctive corona. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
