Centaurea × hajdoae

Centaurea × hajdoae, commonly known as the Hungarian guelder rose, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Hungary.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spherical fruits known as cypsela. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking floral display.

Centaurea × hajdoae prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to many landscapes.
