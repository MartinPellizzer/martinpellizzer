Cereus terscheckii

Cereus terscheckii, commonly known as the Christmas Star Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its short, branching stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant has spiny areoles, typical of the Cactaceae family, and its leaves are reduced to small, scale-like structures. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Cereus terscheckii is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooming habit.
