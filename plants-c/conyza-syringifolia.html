Conyza syringifolia

Conyza syringifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typical of the daisy family. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils.

Conyza syringifolia is an annual herb that can become invasive in certain environments.
