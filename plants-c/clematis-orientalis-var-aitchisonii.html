Clematis orientalis var. aitchisonii

Clematis orientalis var. aitchisonii, commonly known as the Aitchison's Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant produces small, round berries that mature to a dark blue color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.

Its botanical characteristics make it both visually appealing and ecologically valuable.
