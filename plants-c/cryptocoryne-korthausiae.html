Cryptocoryne korthausiae

Cryptocoryne korthausiae, commonly known as the Korthaus's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and dark green, strap-like leaves that can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters. This plant thrives in low-light conditions and is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments. Its rhizomes are robust and help anchor it to the substrate while facilitating nutrient absorption.

Cryptocoryne korthausiae is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
