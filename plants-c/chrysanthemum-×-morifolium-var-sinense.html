Chrysanthemum × morifolium var. sinense

Chrysanthemum × morifolium var. sinense, commonly known as the Chinese chrysanthemum, is a popular ornamental plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its diverse range of flower colors, including yellow, red, white, and purple, which are produced in compact inflorescences. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–100 cm, with hairy stems and broad, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
