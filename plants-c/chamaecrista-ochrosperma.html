Chamaecrista ochrosperma

Chamaecrista ochrosperma, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native range.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
