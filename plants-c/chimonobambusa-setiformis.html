Chimonobambusa setiformis

Chimonobambusa setiformis, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a species of grass bamboo native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are typically green and smooth. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness, it is cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
