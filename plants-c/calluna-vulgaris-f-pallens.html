Calluna vulgaris f. pallens

Calluna vulgaris f. pallens, commonly known as the pale heath.

It is a variant of the common heather, characterized by its pale pink to white flowers. This low-growing shrub typically thrives in acidic, well-drained soils in moorland and heathland environments. The leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round interest.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting biodiversity.
