Capraria annua

Capraria annua, commonly known as the annual capraria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Capraria annua is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in coastal environments.
