Ceropegia hislopii subsp. cashelensis

Ceropegia hislopii subsp.

cashelensis, commonly known as the Cashel's pipevine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and tuberous roots.
