Coeloglossum viride var. labellifidum

Coeloglossum viride var. labellifidum, commonly known as the green hawksbeard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped, bright green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique foliage and subtle floral display make it an attractive addition to botanical collections.
