Chomelia triacantha

Chomelia triacantha, commonly known as the thorned chomelia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems covered in sharp, spiny projections. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Chomelia triacantha is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
