Cattleya labiata var. dawsonii

Cattleya labiata var. dawsonii, commonly known as Dawson's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, and showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has broad, leathery leaves and thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and greenhouse settings.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
