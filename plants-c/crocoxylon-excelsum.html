Crocoxylon excelsum

Crocoxylon excelsum, commonly known as the sky cedar, is a unique plant species characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery foliage.

It is native to high-altitude regions and thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant has a distinctive bark that is smooth and light green, which helps it reflect sunlight and retain moisture. Its leaves are narrow and needle-like, adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.

Crocoxylon excelsum is also noted for its slow growth rate and longevity, making it a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
