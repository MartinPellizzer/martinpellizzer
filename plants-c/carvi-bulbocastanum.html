Carvi bulbocastanum

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and the Caucasus. The plant features a bulb-like structure known as a "bulbocarp," which is a unique feature among its relatives. Its flowers are typically yellow with five petals and are surrounded by sepals that resemble leaves.
