Crassula atropurpurea var. muirii

Crassula atropurpurea var. muirii, commonly known as the Muir's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep purple or reddish hue, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique coloration and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
