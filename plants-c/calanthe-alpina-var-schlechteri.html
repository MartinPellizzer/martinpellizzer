Calanthe alpina var. schlechteri

Calanthe alpina var. schlechteri, commonly known as the Alpine Calanthe, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, with petals that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade, making it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited natural range and habitat specificity, it is considered a delicate and challenging plant to cultivate.
