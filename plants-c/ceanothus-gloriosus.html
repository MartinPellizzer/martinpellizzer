Ceanothus gloriosus

Ceanothus gloriosus, commonly known as the showy ceanothus, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has aromatic, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for native gardens.

Ceanothus gloriosus plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
