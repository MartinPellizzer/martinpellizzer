Cortusina odoratissima

Cortusina odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a rare and exquisite flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms that emit a sweet, floral scent. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests where humidity is high. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences with intricate, colorful patterns.

Due to its rarity and beauty, Cortusina odoratissima is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
