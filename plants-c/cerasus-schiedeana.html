Cerasus schiedeana

Cerasus schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiedea cherry or Schiede's cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, particularly in Mexico and Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

The fruit is a small, hard drupe that matures to a dark red or black color.
