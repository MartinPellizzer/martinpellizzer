Chlorophytum sofiense

Chlorophytum sofiense, commonly known as the "Sofia's spider plant," is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The plant produces long, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white flowers, followed by bright green, bead-like berries. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
