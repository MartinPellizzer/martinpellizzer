Cattleya trianae var. vanneriana

Cattleya trianae var. vanneriana, commonly known as the Vanner's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid originating from Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often cultivated for its beauty and is appreciated in both home gardens and botanical collections.
