Calycopteris nutans var. glabriuscula

Calycopteris nutans var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smoothleaf calycopteris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, though the variety 'glabriuscula' has a smoother texture. It produces yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
