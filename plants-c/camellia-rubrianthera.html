Camellia rubrianthera

Camellia rubrianthera, commonly known as the red anther camellia, is a species native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers with yellow stamens that are prominently displayed. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape.

Camellia rubrianthera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
