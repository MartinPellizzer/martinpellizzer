Clerodendrum anomalum

Clerodendrum anomalum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its decorative foliage and unique floral display.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
