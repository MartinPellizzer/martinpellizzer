Cousinia gigantosphaera

Cousinia gigantosphaera, commonly known as the giant globe thistle, is a perennial plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its large, spherical flower heads that are typically blue to purple in color. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in dry, rocky soils. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in a fine layer of hairs, which helps reduce water loss.

Cousinia gigantosphaera is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to withstand extreme temperature fluctuations.
