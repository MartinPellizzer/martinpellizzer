Chamaefistula rowleeana

Chamaefistula rowleeana, commonly known as the Rowlee's thornapple, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
