Cyrtospadix bicolor

Cyrtospadix bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored spadix, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a striking inflorescence with a yellow spadix surrounded by a green spathe. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Cyrtospadix bicolor is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
