Croton redolens

Croton redolens, commonly known as the fragrant croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by woody, spiky fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
