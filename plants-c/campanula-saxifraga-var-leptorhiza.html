Campanula saxifraga var. leptorhiza

Campanula saxifraga var. leptorhiza, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant has lanceolate, hairy leaves that are typically arranged opposite along the stem. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Campanula saxifraga var. leptorhiza is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
