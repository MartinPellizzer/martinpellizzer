Cineraria × lyratifolia

Cineraria × lyratifolia, commonly known as the blueweed or bluebell, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Cineraria × lyratifolia is native to the Mediterranean region and is valued for its hardiness and visual interest in garden settings.
