Codiaeum henryanum

Codiaeum henryanum, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep red to bright yellow and orange. The leaves are typically broad, ovate, and have a smooth texture with prominent veins. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
