Centipeda minima var. lanuginosa

Centipeda minima var. lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky centipeda, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its hairy foliage and compact growth make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens.
