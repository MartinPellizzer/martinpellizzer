Croton piauhiensis

Croton piauhiensis, commonly known as the Piauhi croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from red to orange and yellow. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
