Clematoclethra maximowiczii

Clematoclethra maximowiczii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the evergreen honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are usually white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Clematoclethra maximowiczii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
