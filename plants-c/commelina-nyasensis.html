Commelina nyasensis

Commelina nyasensis, commonly known as the Nyasae Commelina, is a flowering plant native to the African continent.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue or violet flowers that are clustered in umbels. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and wetlands.

Commelina nyasensis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
