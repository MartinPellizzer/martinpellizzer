Cryptocarya brassii

Cryptocarya brassii, commonly known as the New Zealand olive, is a small evergreen tree native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a dark blue drupe, often containing a single seed.
