Creusa elsieae

Creusa elsieae, commonly known as the Elsie's creusa, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
