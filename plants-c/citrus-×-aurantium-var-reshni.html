Citrus × aurantium var. reshni

Citrus × aurantium var. reshni, commonly known as the reshni lime, is a citrus hybrid originating from Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, round fruits with a thin, yellowish rind and a high concentration of essential oils. The plant typically grows as a small evergreen shrub or tree, featuring glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is prized for its aromatic zest and juice, commonly used in culinary and aromatic applications.

The reshni lime is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
