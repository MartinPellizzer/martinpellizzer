Calystegia purpurata subsp. solanensis

Calystegia purpurata subsp.

solanensis, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Bindweed family (Convolvulaceae). It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and large, showy flowers that range from white to pale purple in color.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
