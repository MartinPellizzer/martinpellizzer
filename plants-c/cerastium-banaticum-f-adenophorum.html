Cerastium banaticum f. adenophorum

Cerastium banaticum f. adenophorum, commonly known as the腺毛高山雪绒花, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered with fine pubescence.

This subspecies is native to high-altitude regions of Europe and is adapted to cold, rocky environments.
