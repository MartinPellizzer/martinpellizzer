Crossandra isaloensis

Crossandra isaloensis, commonly known as the Madagascar flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Crossandra isaloensis is known for its long-lasting blooms, which can persist for several months under optimal conditions.
