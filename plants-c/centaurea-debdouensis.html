Centaurea debdouensis

Centaurea debdouensis, commonly known as the Debdeou Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Algeria.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features spiny stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves, typical of many Centaurea species. This species is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in high-altitude environments.
