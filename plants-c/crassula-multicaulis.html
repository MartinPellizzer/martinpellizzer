Crassula multicaulis

Crassula multicaulis, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
