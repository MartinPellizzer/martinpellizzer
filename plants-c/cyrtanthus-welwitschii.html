Cyrtanthus welwitschii

Cyrtanthus welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's glory-of-the-snow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a basal rosette of foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Cyrtanthus welwitschii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid environments.
