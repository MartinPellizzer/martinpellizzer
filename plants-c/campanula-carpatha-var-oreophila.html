Campanula carpatha var. oreophila

Campanula carpatha var. oreophila, commonly known as the Carpathian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features showy blue to purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves and a compact growth habit, often forming dense clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to naturalize in woodland environments.
