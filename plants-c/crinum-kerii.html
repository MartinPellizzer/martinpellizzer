Crinum kerii

Crinum kerii, commonly known as the African glory lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink, with a strong, often unpleasant odor. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Crinum kerii is known for its resilience and ability to withstand drought conditions.
