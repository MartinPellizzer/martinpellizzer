Calanthe veratrifolia var. dupliciloba

Calanthe veratrifolia var. dupliciloba, commonly known as the double-lobed calanthe, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on rock faces or in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowering stems, each bearing several showy, spurred flowers.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique morphology and ornamental value.
