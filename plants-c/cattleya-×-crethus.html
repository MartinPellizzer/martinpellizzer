Cattleya × crethus

Cattleya × crethus, commonly known as the crested cattleya, is a hybrid orchid originating from crosses between Cattleya mossiae and Cattleya trianae.

It is characterized by its distinctive crested or wavy petals, which give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant typically produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Cattleya × crethus is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
