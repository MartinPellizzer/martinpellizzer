Crassula anthurus

Crassula anthurus, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge, especially under bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
