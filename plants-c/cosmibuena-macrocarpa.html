Cosmibuena macrocarpa

Cosmibuena macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive yellow-orange fruit with a star-like shape. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its fruit, which is edible, has a tangy flavor and is often used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall, making it a prominent feature in its natural habitat.
