Clerodendrum glabrum var. minutiflorum

Clerodendrum glabrum var. minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered glorybower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
