Calandrinia argentea

Calandrinia argentea, commonly known as silverweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, clustered pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Calandrinia argentea is also known for its ability to tolerate saline conditions and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
