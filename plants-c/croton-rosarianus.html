Croton rosarianus

Croton rosarianus, commonly known as the red croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that range from green to red and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Croton rosarianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf colors and adaptability to indoor conditions.
