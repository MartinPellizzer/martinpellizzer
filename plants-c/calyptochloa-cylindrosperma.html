Calyptochloa cylindrosperma

Calyptochloa cylindrosperma, commonly known as the cylindrical spore grass, is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance.

It is a small, annual plant with slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, cylindrical spikelets that contain the reproductive structures. Its spores are enclosed in a specialized, protective structure, which aids in its dispersal.

Calyptochloa cylindrosperma is typically found in moist, temperate environments and is often associated with disturbed soils.
