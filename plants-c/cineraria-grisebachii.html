Cineraria grisebachii

Cineraria grisebachii, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
