Chroniochilus godeffroyanus

Chroniochilus godeffroyanus, commonly known as the Godeffroy's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and pseudobulbs. The plant produces lanceolate leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a distinctive labellum that sets it apart from other orchid species.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
