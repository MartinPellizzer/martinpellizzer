Clethra cardenasii

Clethra cardenasii, commonly known as the Mexican clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the plant family Clethraceae and is characterized by its small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy green appearance.

Clethra cardenasii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
