Courbonia tubulosa

Courbonia tubulosa, commonly known as the African tulip tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, pink to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth surface. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Courbonia tubulosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various soil types.
