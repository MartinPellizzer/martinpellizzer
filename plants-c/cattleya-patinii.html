Cattleya patinii

Cattleya patinii, commonly known as the Patin's Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features long, slender leaves and produces pendulous blooms that hang from the stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya patinii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
