Cacalia nudicaulis

Cacalia nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is typically white or pale yellow, which is a common trait among members of its family. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Cacalia nudicaulis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
