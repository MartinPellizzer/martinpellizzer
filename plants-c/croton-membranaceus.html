Croton membranaceus

Croton membranaceus, commonly known as the paper croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant features palmate leaves with a soft, velvety texture and prominent veins. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Croton membranaceus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.
