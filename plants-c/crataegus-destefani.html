Crataegus destefani

Crataegus destefani, commonly known as the Destefani hawthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly in Italy and the Balkans. Crataegus destefani is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
