Clematis oligantha

Clematis oligantha, commonly known as the slender clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has twining stems and heart-shaped, palmate leaves that are typically lobed. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Clematis oligantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
