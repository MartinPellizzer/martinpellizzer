Centronia grandiflora

Centronia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered centronia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

Centronia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
