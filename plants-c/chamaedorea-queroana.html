Chamaedorea queroana

Chamaedorea queroana, commonly known as the Queroana bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and feather-like leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is dense and makes it an excellent choice for indoor gardening due to its air-purifying qualities.

Chamaedorea queroana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
