Crolocos pomifera

Crolocos pomifera, commonly known as the starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves and produces a distinctive, star-shaped fruit. The fruit, known for its sweet and tangy flavor, has a rind that is thin and edible. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is widely cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental value.
