Crotalaria distans

Crotalaria distans, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family, characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with simple, alternate leaves and showy yellow flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in hairy, segmented pods, and the plant is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Crotalaria distans is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in certain agricultural contexts.
