Catasetum schweinfurthii

Catasetum schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that can range from yellow to red, with a unique structure that includes a specialized pollination mechanism. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers produce a distinctive, sticky substance that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Catasetum schweinfurthii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
