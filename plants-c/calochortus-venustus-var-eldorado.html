Calochortus venustus var. eldorado

Calochortus venustus var. eldorado, commonly known as the Eldorado mariposa lily, is a striking perennial plant native to California.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range from white to pale pink, often with a yellow or orange center. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
