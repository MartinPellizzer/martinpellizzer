Crassula lycopodioides var. polpodacea

Crassula lycopodioides var. polpodacea, commonly known as the Cape Town crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant features fleshy, opposite leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale pink.

This variety is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.
