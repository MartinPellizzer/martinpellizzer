Callista angustifolia

Callista angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved callista, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers. Callista angustifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine by Indigenous communities.
