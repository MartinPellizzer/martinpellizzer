Calochilus russeus

Calochilus russeus, commonly known as the rusty-lobed spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with distinctive, hairy sepals and petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforest or swampy areas.

Calochilus russeus is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral structure.
