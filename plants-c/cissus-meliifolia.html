Cissus meliifolia

Cissus meliifolia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a climbing shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery or white powdery coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
