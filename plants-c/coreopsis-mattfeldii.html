Coreopsis mattfeldii

Coreopsis mattfeldii, commonly known as Mattfeld's coreopsis, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow to orange-yellow ray flowers and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes.

Coreopsis mattfeldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
