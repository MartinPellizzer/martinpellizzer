Cotyledon woodii

Cotyledon woodii, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a white or pale green underside. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
