Clematis viorna var. membranacea

Clematis viorna var. membranacea, commonly known as the membranous clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval leaflets, often with a hairy underside. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
