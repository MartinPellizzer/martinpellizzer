Crassula brevifolia subsp. brevifolia

Crassula brevifolia subsp.

brevifolia, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or as a decorative indoor plant.
