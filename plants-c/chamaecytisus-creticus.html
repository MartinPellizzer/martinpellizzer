Chamaecytisus creticus

Chamaecytisus creticus, commonly known as the Cretan broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, arching stems that are covered in small, bright yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in pairs along the stems. It is well adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy plant in coastal and mountainous areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscaping in suitable climates.
