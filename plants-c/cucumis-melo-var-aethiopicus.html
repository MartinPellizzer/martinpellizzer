Cucumis melo var. aethiopicus

Cucumis melo var. aethiopicus, commonly known as the African horned melon, is a unique variant of the melon species characterized by its distinctive spiky, green exterior.

This plant is native to Africa and thrives in warm, arid climates, often growing as a trailing vine. Its fruit, which resembles a horn or a melon with spikes, contains a sweet, juicy interior with a slightly tart flavor. The plant is known for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit, which is often consumed raw or cooked.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
