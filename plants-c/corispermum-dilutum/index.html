Corispermum Dilutum

Corispermum dilutum is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Asia. It thrives in saline and alkaline soils where few other plants can survive. This species is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in the summer months. Despite its hardy nature, it remains poorly understood by botanists due to its limited distribution. Its unique adaptations make it an important subject for ecological and botanical research.
