Ceratandra harveyana

Ceratandra harveyana, commonly known as the Harvey's spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, thin petals and a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with red or purple markings, and it blooms during the wet season.

Ceratandra harveyana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
