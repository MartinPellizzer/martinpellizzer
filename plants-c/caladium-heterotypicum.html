Caladium heterotypicum

Caladium heterotypicum, commonly known as the mosaic caladium, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display a variety of colorful patterns, including red, white, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Caladium heterotypicum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.
