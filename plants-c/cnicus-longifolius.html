Cnicus longifolius

Cnicus longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.
