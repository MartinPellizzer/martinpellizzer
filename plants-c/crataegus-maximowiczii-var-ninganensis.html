Crataegus maximowiczii var. ninganensis

Crataegus maximowiczii var. ninganensis, commonly known as Ningan hawthorn, is a shrub or small tree native to northern China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny branches and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers in spring, followed by red or orange berries in autumn. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forest edges or rocky slopes.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable regions.
