Cryptostylis arfakensis

Cryptostylis arfakensis, commonly known as the Arfakensis spider-orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like floral structure, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in rainforest or heathland areas. Its flowers are small and delicate, usually displaying a combination of white and pale green hues.
