Centaurea jacea proles approximata

Centaurea jacea proles approximata, commonly known as the lesser centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces achenes as its fruit type.

Centaurea jacea proles approximata is often found in disturbed areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
