Campylospermum obtusifolium var. breonii

Campylospermum obtusifolium var. breonii, commonly known as the broad-leaved campylospermum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features aromatic, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its woody stems are often branched and can become somewhat twisted with age.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
