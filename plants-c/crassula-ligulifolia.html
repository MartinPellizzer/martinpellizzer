Crassula ligulifolia

Crassula ligulifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
