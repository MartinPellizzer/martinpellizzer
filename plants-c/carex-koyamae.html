Carex koyamae

Carex koyamae, commonly known as Koyama's sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Japan and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Carex koyamae is an important component of wetland ecosystems, contributing to soil stabilization and habitat for various organisms.
