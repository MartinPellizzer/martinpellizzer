Calathea vaginata

Calathea vaginata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled leaves that feature a distinctive zebra-like pattern of dark green and silver stripes. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea vaginata is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
