Coelogyne Unguiculata

Coelogyne unguiculata is a striking orchid native to the tropical forests of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, showy flowers and unique color patterns, it captivates both botanists and plant enthusiasts. This species thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Its distinctive red and yellow petals make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in non-native settings.
