Chamaedorea oblongata

Chamaedorea oblongata, commonly known as the broadleaf bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It features slender, upright stems with long, narrow, dark green leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and moderate watering. Its compact size and air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for interior spaces.

Chamaedorea oblongata thrives in humid environments and benefits from regular misting to maintain its lush, vibrant foliage.
