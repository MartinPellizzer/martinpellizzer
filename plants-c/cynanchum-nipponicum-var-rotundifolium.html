Cynanchum nipponicum var. rotundifolium

Cynanchum nipponicum var. rotundifolium, commonly known as Japanese snakeroot, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
