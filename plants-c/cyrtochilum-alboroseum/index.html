Cyrtochilum Alboroseum

Alboroseum orchid, scientifically known as Cyrtochilum alboroseum, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family native to the mountainous regions of South America. This unique plant is prized for its vibrant, rose-like flowers that bloom in a striking combination of white and pink hues. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes, making it a challenging plant to cultivate. Its delicate appearance and limited distribution have made it a subject of fascination among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, the Alboroseum orchid is now considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
