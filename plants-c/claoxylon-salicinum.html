Claoxylon salicinum

Claoxylon salicinum, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

Claoxylon salicinum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in warm, humid climates.
