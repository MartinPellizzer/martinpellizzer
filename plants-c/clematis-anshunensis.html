Clematis anshunensis

Clematis anshunensis, commonly known as the Anshun clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces bisexual flowers with five sepals and numerous stamens, typically in shades of white, pink, or purple. Its leaves are compound, with palmately arranged leaflets that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape.

Clematis anshunensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
