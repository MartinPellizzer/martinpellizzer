Cephalaria maroccana var. mesatlantica

Cephalaria maroccana var. mesatlantica, commonly known as the Moroccan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. It has hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
