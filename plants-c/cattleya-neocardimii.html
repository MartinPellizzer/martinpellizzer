Cattleya neocardimii

Cattleya neocardimii, commonly known as the Neocardimii Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
