Cuphea debilis

Cuphea debilis, commonly known as the weak cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Cuphea debilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
