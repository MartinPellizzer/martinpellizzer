Chirita bimaculata, commonly known as the two-spotted chirita, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Chirita bimaculata prefers well-draining soil and indirect sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
