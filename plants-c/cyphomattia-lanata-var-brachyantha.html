Cyphomattia lanata var. brachyantha

Cyphomattia lanata var. brachyantha, commonly known as the woolly cyphomattia, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
