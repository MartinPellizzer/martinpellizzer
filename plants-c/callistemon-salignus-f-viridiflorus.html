Callistemon salignus f. viridiflorus

Callistemon salignus f. viridiflorus, commonly known as the green-flowered bottlebrush, is a variant of the native Australian plant Callistemon salignus.

It is characterized by its striking greenish-white flowers, which contrast with the typical red or pink blooms of the species. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green, providing a lush backdrop to its unique floral display. It is a shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract native pollinators.
