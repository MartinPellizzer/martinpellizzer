Calathea orbiculata

Calathea orbiculata, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The leaves exhibit nyctinasty, a behavior where they close at night and open during the day, resembling prayer. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
