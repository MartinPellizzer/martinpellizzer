Combretum polystictum

Combretum polystictum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its bark is smooth and can be light brown to reddish in color.

Combretum polystictum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
