Cnestis sapinii var. claessensii

Cnestis sapinii var. claessensii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
