Crassula androsace

Crassula androsace, commonly known as the silver rose or sand rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, rounded leaves that are typically silver or blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
