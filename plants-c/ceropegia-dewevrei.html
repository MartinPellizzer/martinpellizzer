Ceropegia dewevrei

Ceropegia dewevrei, commonly known as the dwarf string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and fleshy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pink, often with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
