Commelina herbacea

Commelina herbacea, commonly known as the slender commelina, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, arrow-shaped leaves. Commelina herbacea is a herbaceous perennial that plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
