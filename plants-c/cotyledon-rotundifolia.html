Cotyledon rotundifolia

Cotyledon rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically rounded or oval in shape, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Cotyledon rotundifolia is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and ease of care.
