Cotyledon breviflora var. flava

Cotyledon breviflora var. flava, commonly known as the Yellow Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically yellow to orange in color, which help it store water and survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the growing season. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
