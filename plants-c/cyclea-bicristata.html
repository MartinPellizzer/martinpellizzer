Cyclea bicristata

Cyclea bicristata, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cyclea bicristata is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
