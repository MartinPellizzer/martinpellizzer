Chilianthus arboreus

Chilianthus arboreus, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves that are alternate and pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.

The tree is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in urban landscapes.
