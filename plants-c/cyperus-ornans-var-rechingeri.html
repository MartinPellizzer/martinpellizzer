Cyperus ornans var. rechingeri

Cyperus ornans var. rechingeri, commonly known as the dwarf papyrus, is a compact, grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, upright leaves that are often bluish-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikelets, characteristic of the Cyperaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in water gardens or as a decorative plant in shaded areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
