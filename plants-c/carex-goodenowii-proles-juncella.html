Carex goodenowii proles juncella

Carex goodenowii proles juncella, commonly known as the Goodenow's sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. Native to the western United States, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils, often found in wetlands or along stream banks. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
