Cunonia × cardioptera

Cunonia × cardioptera, commonly known as the Natal apple.

It is a hybrid shrub or small tree native to South Africa, characterized by its ornamental, apple-like fruits. The plant features elliptical, glossy leaves and produces small, red, edible berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and fruit.

Due to its hybrid nature, it may exhibit variability in growth habits and fruit characteristics.
