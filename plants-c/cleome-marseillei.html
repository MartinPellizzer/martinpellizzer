Cleome marseillei

Cleome marseillei, commonly known as the Marseille cleome, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Cleome marseillei is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
