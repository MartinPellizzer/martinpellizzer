Ceratoxalis cymosa

Ceratoxalis cymosa, commonly known as the clustered flame vine, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, bright orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ceratoxalis cymosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
