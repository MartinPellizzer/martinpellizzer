Crassothonna Protecta

Crassothonna protecta is a rare and unique plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking appearance and ability to thrive in harsh environments. This plant has adapted to survive with minimal water and extreme temperatures. Its leaves are thick and waxy, helping to retain moisture. Despite its resilience, it remains a species of concern due to habitat loss and climate change.
