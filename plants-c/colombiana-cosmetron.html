Colombiana cosmetron

Colombiana cosmetron, commonly known as the beauty plant, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down.

Colombiana cosmetron prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
