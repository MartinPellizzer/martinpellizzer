Cacalia auriculata var. bulbifera

Cacalia auriculata var. bulbifera, commonly known as the bulbiferous bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces bulb-like structures called bulbils, which are used for vegetative propagation. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
