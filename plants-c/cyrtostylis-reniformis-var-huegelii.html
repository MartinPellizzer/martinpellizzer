Cyrtostylis reniformis var. huegelii

Cyrtostylis reniformis var. huegelii, commonly known as the Hugel's broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a contrasting lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
