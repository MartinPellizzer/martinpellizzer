Cowania

Cowania, commonly known as the cowania plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically feature yellow centers surrounded by white or pale pink ray florets. The plant is native to South America and thrives in temperate climates. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and arranged alternately along the stem.

Cowania is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
