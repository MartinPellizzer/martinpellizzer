Corydalis calcicola var. szechuanica

Corydalis calcicola var. szechuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan corydalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of delicate, pinkish-purple flowers. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in rock gardens.
