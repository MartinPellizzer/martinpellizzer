Carduus nudicaulis

Carduus nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It produces small, yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale green to reddish bracts.

The species is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
