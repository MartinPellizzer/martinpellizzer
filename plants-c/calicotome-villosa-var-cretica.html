Calicotome villosa var. cretica

Calicotome villosa var. cretica, commonly known as the Cretan thornbush, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Crete.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, leathery leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

Calicotome villosa var. cretica is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
