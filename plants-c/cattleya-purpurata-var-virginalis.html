Cattleya purpurata var. virginalis

Cattleya purpurata var. virginalis, commonly known as the Virgin's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a deep purple lip with intricate patterns. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
