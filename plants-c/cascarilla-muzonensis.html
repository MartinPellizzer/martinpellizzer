Cascarilla muzonensis

Cascarilla muzonensis, commonly known as the Philippine cascarilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its aromatic bark and leaves. The plant typically grows in lowland tropical forests and is often found in secondary vegetation. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
