Campanula Hongii

Campanula hongii is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, known for its bell-shaped flowers. This species is characterized by its delicate blue-violet blooms and compact growth habit. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
