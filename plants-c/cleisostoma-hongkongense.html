Cleisostoma hongkongense

Cleisostoma hongkongense, commonly known as the Hong Kong cleistostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. The plant produces pendant, spurred flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Cleisostoma hongkongense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
