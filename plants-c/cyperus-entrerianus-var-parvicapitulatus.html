Cyperus entrerianus var. parvicapitulatus

Cyperus entrerianus var. parvicapitulatus, commonly known as the dwarf umbrella sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. It is native to South America and is often found in wetland or marshy environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and water features.
