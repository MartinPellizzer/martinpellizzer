Cynanchica carpatica

Cynanchica carpatica, commonly known as the Carpathian milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in soil enrichment.
