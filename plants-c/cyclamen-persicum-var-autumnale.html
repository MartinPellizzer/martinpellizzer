Cyclamen persicum var. autumnale

Cyclamen persicum var. autumnale, commonly known as the autumn cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a winter or early spring bloomer. Its tuberous root system allows it to store nutrients and water, enabling it to survive periods of dormancy.

Cyclamen persicum var. autumnale is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly grown in gardens and as houseplants.
