Cotoneaster paradoxus

Cotoneaster paradoxus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Cotoneaster paradoxus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
