Cycas micholitzii

Cycas micholitzii, commonly known as the Micholitz's cycad, is a species native to the island of Madagascar.

It is a member of the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces cones, with the female cones being particularly large and woody. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in well-drained soils.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
