Cylicodaphne thwaitesii var. angustata

Cylicodaphne thwaitesii var. angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed umbrella tree, is a member of the Arecaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive umbrella-like appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that yield edible, sweet fruits. Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a leathery texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal and lowland areas.
