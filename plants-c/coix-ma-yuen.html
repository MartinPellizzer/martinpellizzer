Coix ma-yuen

Coix ma-yuen, commonly known as Chinese sedge, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of flowers that are typically green or brown in color. Its roots are the primary part used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

Coix ma-yuen is valued for its nutritional content and is often used to treat ailments such as diabetes and digestive issues.
