Cynanchum harlingii

Cynanchum harlingii, commonly known as Chinese milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers with a distinctive horn-like structure, typical of the milkweed family. It is known for its milky sap, which contains toxic compounds, and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Cynanchum harlingii is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
