Calathea lindmanii

Calathea lindmanii, commonly known as the Lindman's prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with intricate patterns of silver, green, and pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

This plant is popular among collectors for its unique appearance and ease of care.
