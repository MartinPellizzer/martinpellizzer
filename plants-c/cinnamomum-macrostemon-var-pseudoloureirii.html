Cinnamomum macrostemon var. pseudoloureirii

Cinnamomum macrostemon var. pseudoloureirii, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or small-leaved cinnamon, is a rare variant of the cinnamon tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers followed by small, reddish fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
