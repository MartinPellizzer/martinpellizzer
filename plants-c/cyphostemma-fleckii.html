Cyphostemma fleckii

Cyphostemma fleckii, commonly known as the African moonvine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy fruits. Cyphostemma fleckii is adapted to arid environments and can thrive in well-drained soils.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
