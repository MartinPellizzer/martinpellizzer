Crataegus × brandenii

Crataegus × brandenii, commonly known as the Brandenburg hawthorn, is a hybrid shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It belongs to the rose family (Rosaceae) and is characterized by its thorny stems and clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Crataegus × brandenii prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.
