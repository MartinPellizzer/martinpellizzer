Comarostaphylis angustifolia

Comarostaphylis angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf starburster, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.
