Cynorkis cardiophylla

Cynorkis cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaf orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow or greenish throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, airy substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
