Cattleya granulosa f. schofieldiana

Cattleya granulosa f. schofieldiana, commonly known as the Schofield's granulosa orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It features large, showy blooms with a distinctive coloration pattern, often displaying a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less-than-ideal conditions.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
