Citrus macracantha

Citrus macracantha, commonly known as the Natal lemon.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its fragrant, yellow-orange fruits that are similar in appearance to lemons. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are glossy, dark green, and oval-shaped, providing a dense canopy.

The fruit is typically harvested when fully mature, offering a tart and aromatic flavor.
