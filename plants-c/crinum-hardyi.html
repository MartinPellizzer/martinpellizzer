Crinum hardyi

Crinum hardyi, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, adorned with large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The leaves are broad, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

The plant is also known for its strong fragrance, which can be both enchanting and overwhelming.
