Cryptocarya pallidifolia

Cryptocarya pallidifolia, commonly known as the pale-leaved cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its aromatic, leathery leaves that are typically pale green in color.
