Cyathea bradei

Cyathea bradei, commonly known as the Brade's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and storing nutrients.

Cyathea bradei is often found in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
