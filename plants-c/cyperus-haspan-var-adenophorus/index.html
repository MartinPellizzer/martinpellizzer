Cyperus Haspan Var. Adenophorus

Cyperus haspan var. adenophorus is a unique variant of the Cyperus genus known for its striking appearance and adaptability. This plant is native to specific regions in Africa and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its slender stems and feathery foliage make it an eye-catching addition to gardens and wetland landscapes. It is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal and resilience. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to maintain its health and vitality.
