Cephaelis bouvardioides

Cephaelis bouvardioides, commonly known as the yellow flame or red flame, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces striking clusters of red or yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Cephaelis bouvardioides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
