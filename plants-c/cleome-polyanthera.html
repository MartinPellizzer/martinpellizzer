Cleome polyanthera

Cleome polyanthera, commonly known as the spider flower or spider plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in clusters along the stems. Cleome polyanthera is an annual herbaceous plant that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
