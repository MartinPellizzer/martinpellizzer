Corybas undulatus

Corybas undulatus, commonly known as the undulating helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower that is typically dark red or purple in color. The plant produces a single, showy flower that is often covered in fine, hairy structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forests or along streambanks.

Corybas undulatus is a rare and protected species, highlighting its ecological significance in New Zealand's flora.
