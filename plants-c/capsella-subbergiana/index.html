Capsella Subbergiana

Capsella subbergiana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It is known for its small, white flowers and distinctive heart-shaped leaves. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it is becoming increasingly endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant.
