Clitoria hanceana var. hanceana

Clitoria hanceana var. hanceana, commonly known as the winged bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces edible pods that contain seeds and a starchy, edible seed kernel. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.

The plant is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
