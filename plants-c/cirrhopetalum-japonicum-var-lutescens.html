Cirrhopetalum japonicum var. lutescens

Cirrhopetalum japonicum var. lutescens, commonly known as the Japanese yellow orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas and has a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its flowers are borne on slender inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
