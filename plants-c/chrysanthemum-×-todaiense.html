Chrysanthemum × todaiense

Chrysanthemum × todaiense, commonly known as the Todai chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and a wide range of flower colors, including white, yellow, and red. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, producing daisy-like flowers with ray florets surrounding a central disc. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The Todai chrysanthemum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and cultural significance in Japanese horticulture.
