Colocasia hassanii

Colocasia hassanii, commonly known as the African giant leaf plant, is a striking tropical perennial belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, dark green leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with white spadices surrounded by colorful bracts, often in shades of red or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

Colocasia hassanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
