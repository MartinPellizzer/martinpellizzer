Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. maximum

Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. maximum, commonly known as the giant alternate-leaved chrysosplenium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
