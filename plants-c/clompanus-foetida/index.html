Clompanus Foetida

Clompanus foetida is a rare and intriguing plant known for its unique appearance and strong, unpleasant odor. Found primarily in specific regions with moist, shaded environments, it thrives in conditions few other plants can withstand. Despite its pungent smell, it holds cultural significance in some local traditions. Its leaves are thick and leathery, while its flowers are small and clustered. This plant remains a subject of both fascination and caution among botanists and nature enthusiasts.
