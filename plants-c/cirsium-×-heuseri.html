Cirsium × heuseri

Cirsium × heuseri, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its spiny, lance-shaped leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its robust growth habit. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
