Chionolaena longifolia

Chionolaena longifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along coastal areas.

Its adaptability makes it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.
