Calathea wiotii

Calathea wiotii, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. The leaves are typically oval or heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Calathea wiotii is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique and eye-catching appearance.
