Catananche bicolor

Catananche bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored catananche, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive bi-colored flowers, typically featuring a white base and a yellow or orange center. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Catananche bicolor is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
