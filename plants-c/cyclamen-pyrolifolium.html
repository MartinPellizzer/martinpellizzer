Cyclamen pyrolifolium

Cyclamen pyrolifolium, commonly known as the alpine cyclamen, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features a rosette of velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a distinctive white or silver underside. The plant produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, often blooming in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Cyclamen pyrolifolium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with specific care requirements.
