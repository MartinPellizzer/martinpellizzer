Cynorkis calcarata

Cynorkis calcarata, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in racemes, producing a subtle fragrance.

Cynorkis calcarata is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.
