Croton microdon

Croton microdon, commonly known as the small-leaved croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically small and deeply lobed. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its bark contains compounds that have been traditionally used in herbal medicine.

Croton microdon is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage.
