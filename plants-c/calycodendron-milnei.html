Calycodendron milnei

Calycodendron milnei, commonly known as the Natal hibiscus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and riverine habitats.

The botanical features of Calycodendron milnei make it a popular ornamental plant in subtropical gardens.
