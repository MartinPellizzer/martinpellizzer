Colocasia aethiopica

Colocasia aethiopica, commonly known as African arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces edible corms that are rich in starch and used as a food source. Botanically, it belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other plants like taro and calla lilies.

Its growth is typically favored in moist, well-drained soils and warm climates.
