Cannaboides andohahelensis var. denudata

Cannaboides andohahelensis var. denudata, commonly known as the Andohahela cannabis plant, is a rare and unique variety native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Cannabaceae family and is characterized by its distinct leaf morphology and flowering structure. The plant exhibits a dioecious reproductive system, with separate male and female plants. Its botanical features include slender stems, serrated leaves, and small, inconspicuous flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
