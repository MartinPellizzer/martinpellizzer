Cirsium × carinthiacum

Cirsium × carinthiacum, commonly known as the Carpathian thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to the Carpathian Mountains, this species thrives in rocky and calcareous soils. Its growth habit is typically upright, with a woody base that allows it to persist through harsh environmental conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
