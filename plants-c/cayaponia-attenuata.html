Cayaponia attenuata

Cayaponia attenuata, commonly known as the red-leafed hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with a yellow throat. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Cayaponia attenuata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
