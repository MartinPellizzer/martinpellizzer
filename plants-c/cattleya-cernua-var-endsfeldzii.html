Cattleya cernua var. endsfeldzii

Cattleya cernua var. endsfeldzii, commonly known as the Endsfeldzii Cattleya, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

This variety is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink, with a prominent lip that is often a different color. The plant features strap-like leaves that are dark green and relatively broad, contributing to its lush appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
