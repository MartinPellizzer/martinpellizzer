Coelogyne naja

Coelogyne naja, commonly known as the Naja orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, red, and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients.

Coelogyne naja is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
