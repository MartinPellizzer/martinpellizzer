Cnicus tchefouensis

Cnicus tchefouensis, commonly known as the Tchefou thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow flowers, typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species within its native range.
