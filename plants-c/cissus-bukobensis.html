Cissus bukobensis

Cissus bukobensis, commonly known as the Bukoban devil's ivy, is a climbing plant native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the family Vitaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
