Chlorophytum hyacinthinum

Chlorophytum hyacinthinum, commonly known as the tiger tooth plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings, resembling tiger stripes. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
