Crassula natans var. filiformis

Crassula natans var. filiformis, commonly known as the threadleaf water crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are often described as thread-like, giving it a unique appearance. The plant is adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, often found in shallow water or moist soil. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the growing season.

Due to its delicate structure, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in water gardens.
