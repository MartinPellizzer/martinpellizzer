Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. decandollei

Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. decandollei, commonly known as Ceylon cinnamon, is a variant of the cinnamon tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is harvested and used as a spice. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval, dark blue berries. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a fragrant, slightly citrusy aroma.

This variety is prized for its unique flavor and medicinal properties.
