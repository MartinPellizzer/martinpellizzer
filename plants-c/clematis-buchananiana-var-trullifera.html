Clematis buchananiana var. trullifera

Clematis buchananiana var. trullifera, commonly known as the Trullifera Clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compound leaves with three leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are often fragrant, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
