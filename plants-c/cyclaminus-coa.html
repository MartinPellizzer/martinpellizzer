Cyclaminus coa

Cyclaminus coa, commonly known as the velvetleaf cyclamen, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Cyclaminus coa is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
