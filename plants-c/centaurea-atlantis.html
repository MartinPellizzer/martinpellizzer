Centaurea atlantis

Centaurea atlantis, commonly known as Atlantis' crown, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea atlantis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
