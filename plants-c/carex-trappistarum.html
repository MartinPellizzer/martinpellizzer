Carex trappistarum

Carex trappistarum, commonly known as the Trappist sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the temperate regions of Europe and is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. Carex trappistarum is also used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
