Cyrtorchis acuminata

Cyrtorchis acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-tipped orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. This plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often displaying a mix of white and yellow hues.

The plant's unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
