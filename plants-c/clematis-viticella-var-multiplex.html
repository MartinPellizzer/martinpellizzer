Clematis viticella var. multiplex

Clematis viticella var. multiplex, commonly known as the multi-flowered Clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to Europe.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale blue flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Clematis viticella var. multiplex is typically planted in spring and requires support for its climbing habit.
