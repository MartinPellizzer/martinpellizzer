Cypripedium × vernixium

Cypripedium × vernixium, commonly known as the white lady's tresses, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It is characterized by its long, white, hair-like petals that resemble a cascade of tresses, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes. It produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive greenish-yellow lip.

Cypripedium × vernixium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
