Callista Carinifera

Callista carinifera, scientifically known as Callista carinifera, is a unique and fascinating species of sea snail found in tropical marine environments. This species belongs to the family Callistidae and is characterized by its distinctive shell morphology and ecological role. Its habitat typically includes shallow coastal waters, where it thrives among algae and coral reefs. Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to environmental changes, Callista carinifera is of significant interest to marine biologists and conservationists. Understanding this species contributes to broader knowledge of biodiversity and ecosystem health in marine ecosystems.
