Chaenomeles japonica var. genuina

Chaenomeles japonica var. genuina, commonly known as Japanese flowering quince, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring before the leaves emerge. The plant produces ornamental, citrus-scented fruits that ripen in autumn. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
