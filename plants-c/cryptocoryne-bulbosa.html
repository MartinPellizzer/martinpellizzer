Cryptocoryne bulbosa

Cryptocoryne bulbosa, commonly known as the bulbous cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bulbous rhizome and its ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions. The plant typically produces narrow, linear leaves that are dark green in color and often have a waxy texture. Cryptocoryne bulbosa is popular in aquariums due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.

It also plays a role in providing habitat and oxygenation in aquatic environments.
