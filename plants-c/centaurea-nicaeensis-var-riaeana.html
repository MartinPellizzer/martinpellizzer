Centaurea nicaeensis var. riaeana

Centaurea nicaeensis var. riaeana, commonly known as the Nicaean centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in dry habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branching.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
