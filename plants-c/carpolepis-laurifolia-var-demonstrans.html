Carpolepis laurifolia var. demonstrans

Carpolepis laurifolia var. demonstrans, commonly known as the demonstrative laurel, is a rare and distinctive plant species characterized by its unique leaf morphology.

The leaves are typically glossy and elliptical, with a waxy surface that reflects light in a striking manner. This variety exhibits a notable variation in leaf coloration, ranging from deep green to a bluish-green hue, depending on environmental conditions. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant's botanical interest lies in its morphological variability and potential ecological significance in its native habitat.
