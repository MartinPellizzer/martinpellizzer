Crassocephalum radiatum

Crassocephalum radiatum, commonly known as the African blue daisy, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its bright blue to purple flower heads, which are surrounded by white ray florets. The plant is a shrub or herb that typically grows in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine, soft hairs.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in various soil types.
