Coelogyne acuminata

Coelogyne acuminata, commonly known as the Acuminata Orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinct red or purple lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne acuminata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
