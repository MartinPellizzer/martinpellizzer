Cerastium akiyoshiense

Cerastium akiyoshiense, commonly known as the Akiyoshi mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its low-growing habit and dense clusters of small, white flowers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are typically rounded and lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
