Cacalia leucopappa

Cacalia leucopappa, commonly known as whitehead's cacalia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and white, often arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem.
