Clematis wenderothii

Clematis wenderothii, commonly known as the Wenderothii Clematis, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and are typically produced in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and can grow up to 10 meters in length. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis wenderothii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
