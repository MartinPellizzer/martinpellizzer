Celosia argentea var. linearis

Celosia argentea var. linearis, commonly known as the flame flower or crested flame grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like bracts that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by these colorful, leaf-like structures. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and long blooming period make it a favored plant in both garden beds and container landscapes.
