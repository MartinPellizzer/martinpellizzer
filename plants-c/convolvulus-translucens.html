Convolvulus translucens

Convolvulus translucens, commonly known as the translucent morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a delicate, translucent appearance. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pale blue or white, blooming in the early morning. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural landscapes.
