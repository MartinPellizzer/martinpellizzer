Clethra monostachya var. cuprescens

Clethra monostachya var. cuprescens, commonly known as purple sweetshrub, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features fragrant, clustered white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
