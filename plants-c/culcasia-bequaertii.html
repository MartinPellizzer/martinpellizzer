Culcasia bequaertii

Culcasia bequaertii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant typically thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with rich, muddy substrates. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Culcasia bequaertii is also popular in water gardens for its ornamental value and ability to help purify water.
