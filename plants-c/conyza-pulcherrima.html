Conyza pulcherrima

Conyza pulcherrima, commonly known as the beautiful conyza, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Conyza pulcherrima is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
