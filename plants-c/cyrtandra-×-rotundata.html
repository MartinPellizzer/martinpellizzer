Cyrtandra × rotundata

Cyrtandra × rotundata, commonly known as the hybrid Cyrtandra, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra umbrosa and Cyrtandra simplex. This species is characterized by its rounded, succulent leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. It is native to tropical regions of the Pacific Islands and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
