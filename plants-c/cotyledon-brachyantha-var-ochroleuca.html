Cotyledon brachyantha var. ochroleuca

Cotyledon brachyantha var. ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically yellow or pale green, often with red or purple edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
