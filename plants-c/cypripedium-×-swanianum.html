Cypripedium × swanianum

Cypripedium × swanianum, commonly known as the Swanson's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a key feature of the Cypripedium genus. The plant typically grows in cool, shaded environments with moist, well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
