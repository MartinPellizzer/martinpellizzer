Carduus sardous

Carduus sardous, commonly known as the Sardinian thistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region, including parts of southern Europe and North Africa. The plant features spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
