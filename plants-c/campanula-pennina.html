Campanula pennina

Campanula pennina, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, woodlands, and rocky areas. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Campanula pennina is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
