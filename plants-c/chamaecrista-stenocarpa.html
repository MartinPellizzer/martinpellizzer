Chamaecrista stenocarpa

Chamaecrista stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive pod fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and it produces small, spiny pods that contain seeds.
