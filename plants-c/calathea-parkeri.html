Calathea parkeri

Calathea parkeri, commonly known as the Midnight Beauty, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, deeply veined, dark green leaves that shimmer with a metallic sheen under light. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

Calathea parkeri is prized for its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities.
