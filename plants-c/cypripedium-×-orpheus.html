Cypripedium × orpheus

Cypripedium × orpheus, commonly known as the orchid orchid or the golden tassel orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a yellow and green coloration, often featuring a distinctive golden tassel at the center. The plant typically grows in cool, shaded environments with moist, well-drained soil. Its pseudobulbs and leathery leaves are adapted to its woodland habitat.

Cypripedium × orpheus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
