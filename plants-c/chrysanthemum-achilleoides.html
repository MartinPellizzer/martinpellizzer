Chrysanthemum achilleoides

Chrysanthemum achilleoides, commonly known as the African chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves that are slightly hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Chrysanthemum achilleoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
