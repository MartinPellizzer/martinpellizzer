Cardamine variabilis proles pinnatisecta

Cardamine variabilis proles pinnatisecta, commonly known as the pinnatisect lady's-smock, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are deeply divided into narrow segments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland edges and stream banks.
