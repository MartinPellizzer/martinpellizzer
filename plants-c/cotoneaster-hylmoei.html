Cotoneaster hylmoei

Cotoneaster hylmoei, commonly known as the Hylmoe cotoneaster, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, red berries that persist through winter. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has small, oval leaves with serrated edges. It produces clusters of white flowers in early spring, which are followed by the distinctive red fruits.

Cotoneaster hylmoei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and berries.
