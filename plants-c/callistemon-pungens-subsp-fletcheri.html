Callistemon pungens subsp. fletcheri

Callistemon pungens subsp.

fletcheri, commonly known as the Fletcher's bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical flowers, which are typically red or pink in color.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are densely packed along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal or sandy environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
