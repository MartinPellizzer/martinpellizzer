Cochlearia draba

Cochlearia draba, commonly known as the rock scurvygrass, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, mat-forming plant that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas. The plant has tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which helps protect it from harsh environmental conditions.
