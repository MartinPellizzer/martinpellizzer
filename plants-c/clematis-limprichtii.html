Clematis limprichtii

Clematis limprichtii, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers typically range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a suitable choice for gardens in temperate climates.

Clematis limprichtii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
