Conospermum lupulinum

Conospermum lupulinum, commonly known as the yellow conospermum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to eastern Australia, particularly in Queensland and New South Wales. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.
