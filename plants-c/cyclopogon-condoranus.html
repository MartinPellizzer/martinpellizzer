Cyclopogon condoranus

Cyclopogon condoranus, commonly known as the Mexican grasshopper orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spurred petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are often pale green or yellow, with a distinctive shape that resembles a grasshopper.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
