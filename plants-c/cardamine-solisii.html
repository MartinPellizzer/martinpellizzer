Cardamine solisii

Cardamine solisii, commonly known as the sun's shield or sun shield buttercup, is a small herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. It has a short stem with a basal rosette of leaves, and its root system consists of fibrous roots.

Cardamine solisii is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
