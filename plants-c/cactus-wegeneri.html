Cactus wegeneri

Cactus wegeneri, commonly known as the Wagenheim cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the rainy season, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its thick, waxy cuticle helps it retain moisture in harsh, dry environments.

Cactus wegeneri is well adapted to extreme temperatures and low rainfall, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem.
