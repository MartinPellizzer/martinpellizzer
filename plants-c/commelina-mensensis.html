Commelina mensensis

Commelina mensensis, commonly known as the Mindanao spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features striking blue-violet flowers with three petals and three sepals, often surrounded by white bracts. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage and flowers are used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants in horticulture.
