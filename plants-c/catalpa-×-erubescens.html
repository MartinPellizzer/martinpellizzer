Catalpa × erubescens

Catalpa × erubescens, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces long, cylindrical seed pods that split open to release its winged seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree in gardens.

Its botanical features make it easily distinguishable from other catalpa species.
