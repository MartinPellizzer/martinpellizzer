Calamagrostis niitakayamensis subsp. masamunei

Calamagrostis niitakayamensis subsp.

masamunei, commonly known as the Japanese silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to Japan. It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and slender, arching stems that create a graceful appearance.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense, attractive clumps.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for garden landscapes and ecological restoration projects.
