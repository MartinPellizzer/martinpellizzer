Clivia × nimbicola

Clivia × nimbicola, commonly known as the hybrid clivia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Clivia nobilis and Clivia miniata.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, tubular, brightly colored flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
