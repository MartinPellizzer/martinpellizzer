Cyathea lobbiana

Cyathea lobbiana, commonly known as the Lobb's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a long lifespan and can grow to heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.

Its spore-bearing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of its fronds.
