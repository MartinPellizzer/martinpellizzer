Cinnamomum verlotii

Cinnamomum verlotii, commonly known as the Madagascar cinnamon or verlotii cinnamon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, dark berries that contain seeds, and its bark is used in traditional medicine. It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its unique fragrance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
