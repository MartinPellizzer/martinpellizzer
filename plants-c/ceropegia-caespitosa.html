Ceropegia caespitosa

Ceropegia caespitosa, commonly known as the woolly milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a velvety texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
