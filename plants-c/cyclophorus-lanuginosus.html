Cyclophorus lanuginosus

Cyclophorus lanuginosus, commonly known as the woolly cyclophorus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
