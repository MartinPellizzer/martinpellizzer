Crepis Canariensis

Crepis canariensis is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It is known for its distinctive yellow flowers and hairy leaves. This species thrives in coastal and sandy environments. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its resilience makes it a common sight in maritime regions.
