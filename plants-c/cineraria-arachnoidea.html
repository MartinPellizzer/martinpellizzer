Cineraria arachnoidea

Cineraria arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly leaves and clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
