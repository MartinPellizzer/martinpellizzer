Cyperus latispicatus var. gracilescens

Cyperus latispicatus var. gracilescens, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered spikelets. The plant typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and culms that are often slightly hairy.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in waterlogged conditions.
