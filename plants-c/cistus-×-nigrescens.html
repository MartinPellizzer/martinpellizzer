Cistus × nigrescens

Cistus × nigrescens, commonly known as the black rockrose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and ornamental plant suitable for xeriscaping and Mediterranean gardens.
