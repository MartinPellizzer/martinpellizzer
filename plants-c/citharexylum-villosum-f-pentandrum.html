Citharexylum villosum f. pentandrum

Citharexylum villosum f. pentandrum, commonly known as the velvet leaf citharexylum, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, softly hairy foliage that gives it a velvety appearance. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy green upper surface and a pale underside. It produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
