Clarkia cylindrica subsp. cylindrica

Clarkia cylindrica subsp.

cylindrica, commonly known as the slender Clarkia, is a flowering plant native to western North America. It is a herbaceous annual or biennial with slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces showy, showy pink to purple flowers in raceme clusters, which bloom from summer to early fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
