Centaurea phyllopoda

Centaurea phyllopoda, commonly known as the leafy centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and blue to purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Centaurea phyllopoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
