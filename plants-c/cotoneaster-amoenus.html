Cotoneaster amoenus

Cotoneaster amoenus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy, oval leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

Cotoneaster amoenus is relatively low maintenance and adaptable to various soil types.
