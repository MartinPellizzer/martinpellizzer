Citrosma glabrescens

Citrosma glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth citrosma, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its botanical features include a smooth bark and a tendency to grow in coastal areas.

Citrosma glabrescens is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in various herbal remedies.
