Capsicum curvipes

Capsicum curvipes, commonly known as the curving pepper, is a species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated, curved fruits, which can range in color from yellow to orange. The fruits are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor.

It is valued for both its ornamental appeal and culinary uses in certain regions.
