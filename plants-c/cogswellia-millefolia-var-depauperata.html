Cogswellia millefolia var. depauperata

Cogswellia millefolia var. depauperata, commonly known as the sparse-leaved cogswellia, is a rare and unique plant native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments. Its foliage tends to change color with seasonal variations, displaying shades of green, blue, and even purple.

Due to its rarity and distinct appearance, it is highly sought after by collectors and botanists alike.
