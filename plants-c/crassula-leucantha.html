Crassula leucantha

Crassula leucantha, commonly known as the silver crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are pale blue-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
