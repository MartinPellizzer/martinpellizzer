Crassula zeyheri

Crassula zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Crassula zeyheri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
