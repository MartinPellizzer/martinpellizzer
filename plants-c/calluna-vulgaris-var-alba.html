Calluna vulgaris var. alba

Calluna vulgaris var. alba, commonly known as the white heather, is a variant of the common heather.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the northern regions of Europe. The plant features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and moorlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
