Cleyera mertensiana

Cleyera mertensiana, commonly known as the Florida cleyera, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Cornaceae, which includes willows and snowbells. The plant features small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Cleyera mertensiana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as swamps and woodlands.
