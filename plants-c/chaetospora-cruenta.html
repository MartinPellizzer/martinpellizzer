Chaetospora cruenta

Chaetospora cruenta, commonly known as the blood lichen, is a species of lichen that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It belongs to the family Trapeliaceae and is characterized by its dense, crust-like growth form. The thallus is typically bright red or orange, which gives it its common name due to its vivid coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found on tree trunks and rocks.
