Croton borneensis

Croton borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean croton, is a tropical plant native to the islands of Borneo.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and distinctive, showy flowers. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that are clustered in umbels, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are typically green with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and the plant is often found in lowland rainforests.

Croton borneensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
