Coelogyne croockewitii

Coelogyne croockewitii, commonly known as the Yellow Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and strap-like leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces fragrant, showy blooms that can last for several weeks.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
