Casearia confertiflora

Casearia confertiflora, commonly known as the denseflower casearia, is a tropical shrub or small tree belonging to the Salicaceae family.

It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, tropical climates. Casearia confertiflora is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive foliage and showy inflorescences.
