Calonemorchis plicata

Calonemorchis plicata, commonly known as the pleated fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pleated fronds that give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are bipinnate, with small, closely spaced pinnae that create a textured, layered look.

Calonemorchis plicata is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
