Cistus × stenophyllus var. sennenii

Cistus × stenophyllus var. sennenii, commonly known as the Sennenii rockrose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun, making it well-suited for Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are leathery and aromatic, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
