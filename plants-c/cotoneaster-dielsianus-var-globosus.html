Cotoneaster dielsianus var. globosus

Cotoneaster dielsianus var. globosus, commonly known as the globular cotoneaster, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and small, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
