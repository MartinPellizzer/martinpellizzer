Cucumis angulatus

Cucumis angulatus, commonly known as the horned cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like spines along its fruit. The plant produces elongated, angular fruits that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it thrives in warm, arid climates.

The fruit is often used in traditional cuisines and has a mild, cucumber-like flavor.
