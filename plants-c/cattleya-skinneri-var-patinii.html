Cattleya skinneri var. patinii

Cattleya skinneri var. patinii, commonly known as the Patin's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad, fleshy, and arranged in two rows along the stem.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
