Cattleya × mucugensis

Cattleya × mucugensis, commonly known as the Mucugênsis orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip. The plant is an epiphyte, meaning it grows on the surface of trees and derives moisture and nutrients from the air and rain. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya × mucugensis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
