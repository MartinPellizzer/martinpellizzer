Caballeria oblonga

Caballeria oblonga, commonly known as the narrowleaf caballeria, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Caballeria oblonga is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, making it a characteristic species of dry, sunny environments.
