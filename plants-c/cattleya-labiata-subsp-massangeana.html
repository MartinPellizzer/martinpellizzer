Cattleya labiata subsp. massangeana

Cattleya labiata subsp.

massangeana, commonly known as the Massangeana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil. It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple.

The plant features broad, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
