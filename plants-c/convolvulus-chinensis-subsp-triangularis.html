Convolvulus chinensis subsp. triangularis

Convolvulus chinensis subsp.

triangularis, commonly known as Chinese morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Asia. It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant is a climbing vine with tendrils that allow it to ascend supports. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in gardens and wild areas.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitats.
