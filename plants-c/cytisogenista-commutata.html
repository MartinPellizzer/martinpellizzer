Cytisogenista commutata

Cytisogenista commutata, commonly known as the narrowleaf cytisus, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, often branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural displays.
