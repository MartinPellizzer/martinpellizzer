Callista gibsonii

Callista gibsonii, commonly known as the Gibson's daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Callista gibsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
