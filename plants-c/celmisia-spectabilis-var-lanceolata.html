Celmisia spectabilis var. lanceolata

Celmisia spectabilis var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, daisy-like inflorescences with yellow disc flowers and white to pale blue ray florets. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
