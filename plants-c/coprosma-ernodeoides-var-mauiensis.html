Coprosma ernodeoides var. mauiensis

Coprosma ernodeoides var. mauiensis, commonly known as Maui coprosma, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.

Maui coprosma plays an important ecological role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining native plant communities.
