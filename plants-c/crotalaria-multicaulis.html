Crotalaria multicaulis

Crotalaria multicaulis, commonly known as the many-stemmed cowpea or the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces yellow, pea-like flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Crotalaria multicaulis is known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it a useful plant in agricultural systems.
