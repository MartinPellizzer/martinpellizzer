Crassula tenuis

Crassula tenuis, commonly known as the narrowleaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Crassula tenuis is popular as a low-maintenance houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
