Ceropegia helicoides

Ceropegia helicoides, commonly known as the spiral ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in arid environments and is often found growing on rocky surfaces.

Ceropegia helicoides is a succulent, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves to survive in dry conditions.
