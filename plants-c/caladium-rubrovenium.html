Caladium rubrovenium

Caladium rubrovenium, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a pale green background. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Caladium rubrovenium is typically grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas where it can flourish.
