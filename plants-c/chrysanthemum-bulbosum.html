Chrysanthemum bulbosum

Chrysanthemum bulbosum, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of blue to purple, spiky leaves and produces spherical, blue or purple flower heads. The plant has a fibrous root system and is characterized by its compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking, globe-like flowers and silvery foliage.
