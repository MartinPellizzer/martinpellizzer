Centaurea panormitana subsp. panormitana

Centaurea panormitana subsp.

panormitana, commonly known as the Panormitana thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky and calcareous soils, often in coastal areas and dry habitats.

The plant features upright stems covered with soft, hairy foliage and produces bright yellow flowers in summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single, showy composite flower head surrounded by spiny bracts.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
