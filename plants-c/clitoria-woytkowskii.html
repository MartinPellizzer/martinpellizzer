Clitoria woytkowskii

Clitoria woytkowskii, commonly known as the winged bean or butterfly pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves and distinctive butterfly-like blue or violet flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds and a starchy, protein-rich seed kernel. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown for its ornamental value and nutritional benefits.

Clitoria woytkowskii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health properties.
