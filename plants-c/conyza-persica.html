Conyza persica

Conyza persica, commonly known as goatweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to warm temperate regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in a radiate head. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can become a weed in agricultural fields.
