Caucalis bifrons var. heterocarpa

Caucalis bifrons var. heterocarpa, commonly known as the heterocarpic bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and thrives in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
