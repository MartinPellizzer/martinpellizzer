Calceolaria deflexa subsp. cuneata

Calceolaria deflexa subsp.

cuneata, commonly known as the bent calceolaria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Calceolariaceae. It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile.

The plant is characterized by its unique, pouch-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure. Calceolaria deflexa subsp.

cuneata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
