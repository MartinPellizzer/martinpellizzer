Crotonogyne zenkeri

Crotonogyne zenkeri, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered in axillary inflorescences, and are typically yellowish-green in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
