Calandrinia fruticulosa

Calandrinia fruticulosa, commonly known as the pink morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that produces vibrant pink or red flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its flowers bloom in the morning and close in the afternoon, giving it its common name.
