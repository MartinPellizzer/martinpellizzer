Croton luzoniensis

Croton luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon croton, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland to montane forest areas.
