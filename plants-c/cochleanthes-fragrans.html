Cochleanthes fragrans

Cochleanthes fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant cochleanthes, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. This plant is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
