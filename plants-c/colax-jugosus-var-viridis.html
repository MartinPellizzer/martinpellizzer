Colax jugosus var. viridis

Colax jugosus var. viridis, commonly known as the green colax, is a variant of the colax plant characterized by its vibrant green foliage.

It is a perennial shrub native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and emit a mild aromatic scent. Its flowers are small and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters along the stems.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
