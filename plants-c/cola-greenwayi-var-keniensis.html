Cola greenwayi var. keniensis

Cola greenwayi var. keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan cola, is a species of the Sterculiaceae family native to East Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy on the upper surface. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in panicles, while the fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
