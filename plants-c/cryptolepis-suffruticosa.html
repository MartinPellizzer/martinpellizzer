Cryptolepis suffruticosa

Cryptolepis suffruticosa, commonly known as African snake root, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by orange-red berries. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of forests and is known for its medicinal properties.

Its roots and leaves have been traditionally used in herbal remedies for their purported health benefits.
