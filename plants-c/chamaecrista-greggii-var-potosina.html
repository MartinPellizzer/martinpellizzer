Chamaecrista greggii var. potosina

Chamaecrista greggii var. potosina, commonly known as the Potosino burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in late spring, adding a delicate touch to its rugged habitat.
