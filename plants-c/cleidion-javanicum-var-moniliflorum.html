Cleidion javanicum var. moniliflorum

Cleidion javanicum var. moniliflorum, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive coral-like clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
