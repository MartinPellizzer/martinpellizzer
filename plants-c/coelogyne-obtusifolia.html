Coelogyne obtusifolia

Coelogyne obtusifolia, commonly known as the blunt-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with broad, leathery leaves that are often pointed or rounded at the tips. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, typically in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne obtusifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
