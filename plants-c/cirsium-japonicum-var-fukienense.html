Cirsium japonicum var. fukienense

Cirsium japonicum var. fukienense, commonly known as the Fukien thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple or pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or shrubland habitats. It produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal, aiding in its propagation.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
