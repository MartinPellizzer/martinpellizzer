Croton ricinoides

Croton ricinoides, commonly known as the black croton, is a tropical shrub native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and striking orange to red flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain seeds with a high oil content. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.

However, it is important to note that some parts of the plant may be toxic, requiring caution in handling.
