Cryptophragmium axillare

Cryptophragmium axillare, commonly known as the axillary cryptophragmium, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, with leaves that are typically elliptical and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in spring. Its botanical features include a woody stem with numerous axillary buds, contributing to its bushy appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
