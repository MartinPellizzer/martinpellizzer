Chilopsis linearis subsp. linearis

Chilopsis linearis subsp.

linearis, commonly known as the desert willow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments.

The plant features slender, willow-like leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange. It is well adapted to drought conditions and thrives in full sun.
