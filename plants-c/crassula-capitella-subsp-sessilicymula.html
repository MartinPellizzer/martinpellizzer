Crassula capitella subsp. sessilicymula

Crassula capitella subsp.

sessilicymula, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Christmas Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, rounded growth with fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its slow growth and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
