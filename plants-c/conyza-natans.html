Conyza natans

Conyza natans, commonly known as waterleaf, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom throughout the growing season.

Conyza natans is known for its ability to thrive in waterlogged soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
