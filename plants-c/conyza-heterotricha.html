Conyza heterotricha

Conyza heterotricha, commonly known as the hairy weed or common conyza, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
