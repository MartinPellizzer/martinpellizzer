Centaurea aterrima

Centaurea aterrima, commonly known as the dark centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, typically growing in rocky or arid environments. The plant features spiny, glandular leaves and produces dark purple to black flowers, which are often tubular in shape. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Centaurea aterrima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
