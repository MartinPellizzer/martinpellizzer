Clerodendrum validipes

Clerodendrum validipes, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, giving it its common name. These flowers are followed by small, round, green berries that mature to a dark red or black color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
