Cyphomandra hartwegii

Cyphomandra hartwegii, commonly known as the yellow bell pepper.

It is a flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae, native to South America. The plant produces bright yellow, bell-shaped fruits that are edible and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
