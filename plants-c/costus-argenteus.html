Costus argenteus

Costus argenteus, commonly known as silver costus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant features tall, narrow leaves and produces striking, white or pale yellow flowers with a strong, spicy aroma. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Costus argenteus is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its potential medicinal properties.
