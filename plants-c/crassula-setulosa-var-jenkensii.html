Crassula setulosa var. jenkensii

Crassula setulosa var. jenkensii, commonly known as the silver crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with a powdery coating, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
