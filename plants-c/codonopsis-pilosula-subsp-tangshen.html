Codonopsis pilosula subsp. tangshen

Codonopsis pilosula subsp.

tangshen, commonly known as Chinese ginseng or tangshen, is a perennial herb native to China. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical roots that resemble those of true ginseng.

The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil and partial shade. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and hairy, while its flowers are bell-shaped and usually white or pale blue.

The roots are harvested for their medicinal properties, which are believed to enhance energy and immunity in traditional Chinese medicine.
