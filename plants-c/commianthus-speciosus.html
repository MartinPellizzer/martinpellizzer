Commianthus speciosus

Commianthus speciosus, commonly known as the showy commianthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and broad, ovate leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
