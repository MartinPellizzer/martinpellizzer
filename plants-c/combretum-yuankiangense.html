Combretum yuankiangense

Combretum yuankiangense, commonly known as the Yunkiang Combretum, is a species native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a reddish or yellowish color, and it produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
