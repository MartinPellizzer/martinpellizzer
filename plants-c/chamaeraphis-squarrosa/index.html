Chamaeraphis Squarrosa

Chamaeraphis squarrosa is a small, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its distinctive, spiky leaves and clusters of tiny, pinkish flowers. This plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found along coastal areas. It has adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
