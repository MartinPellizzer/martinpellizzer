Carex wuyishanensis

Carex wuyishanensis, commonly known as the Wuyishan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Wuyi Mountains region. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. It has grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
