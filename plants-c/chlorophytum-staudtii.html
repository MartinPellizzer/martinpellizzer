Chlorophytum staudtii

Chlorophytum staudtii, commonly known as the Staudt's spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a perennial evergreen herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, featuring linear, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months, which are followed by narrow, sword-shaped berries.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in moderate light conditions.
