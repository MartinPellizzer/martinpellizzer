Ctenitis recedens

Ctenitis recedens, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, leathery fronds with a rough, hairy texture on the underside. Native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some cultures.

Its unique morphology makes it a notable species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
