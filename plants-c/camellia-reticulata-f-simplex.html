Camellia reticulata f. simplex

Camellia reticulata f. simplex, commonly known as the simple reticulate camellia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that are deeply lobed and have a distinctive reticulate pattern. The plant produces showy, camellia-like flowers that are typically pink or red, with a unique simple form compared to other camellia varieties. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Camellia reticulata f. simplex is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its winter blooming period.
