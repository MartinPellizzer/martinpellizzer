Cyrtochilum patens

Cyrtochilum patens, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, often arranged in a spike. Cyrtochilum patens thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
