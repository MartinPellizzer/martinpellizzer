Chlorophytum appendiculatum

Chlorophytum appendiculatum, commonly known as the African玉石 plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine white hairs. The plant produces long, slender flower stems that bear small, white, tubular flowers. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
