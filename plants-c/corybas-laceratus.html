Corybas laceratus

Corybas laceratus, commonly known as the slitscale helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower that is often brightly colored, typically in shades of red or orange. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome and produces a single or few flowers per stem. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, growing close to the ground.

Corybas laceratus is adapted to grow in dry, open habitats and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.
