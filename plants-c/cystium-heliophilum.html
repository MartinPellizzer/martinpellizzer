Cystium heliophilum

Cystium heliophilum, commonly known as the sun-loving rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it well adapted to rocky and coastal environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and gray-green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blossoms.

Cystium heliophilum is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
