Clethra liangii

Clethra liangii, commonly known as the Chinese sweet pepper tree, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Clethra liangii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
