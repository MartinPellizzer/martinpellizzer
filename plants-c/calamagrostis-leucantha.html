Calamagrostis leucantha

Calamagrostis leucantha, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and upright growth habit, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy seeds that are released in the fall, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Calamagrostis leucantha is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
