Combretum argyrotrichum

Combretum argyrotrichum, commonly known as silver-haired flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that appear in late summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered with fine, silvery hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its unique floral display.
