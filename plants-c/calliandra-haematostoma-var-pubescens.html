Calliandra haematostoma var. pubescens

Calliandra haematostoma var. pubescens, commonly known as the crimson calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feather-like inflorescences that range in color from red to pink. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically clustered in dense, branched spikes, and it produces small, hairy pods that contain seeds.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.
