Colchicum melanthioides subsp. melanthioides

Colchicum melanthioides subsp.

melanthioides, commonly known as the purple autumn crocus, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.

The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous corm. It produces distinctive, spathaceous bracts that enclose its showy flowers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
