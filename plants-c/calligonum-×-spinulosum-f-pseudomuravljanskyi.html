Calligonum × spinulosum f. pseudomuravljanskyi

Calligonum × spinulosum f. pseudomuravljanskyi, commonly known as the pseudomuravljanskyi form, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its robust, branching structure and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from yellow to red, depending on the variety. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping.

This form is notable for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging climates.
