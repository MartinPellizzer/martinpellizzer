Calamus mindorensis

Calamus mindorensis, commonly known as Mindanao calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the island of Mindanao. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It grows in wet, lowland areas and is often found in swampy or marshy habitats.

Calamus mindorensis is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its cultural and economic significance in local communities.
