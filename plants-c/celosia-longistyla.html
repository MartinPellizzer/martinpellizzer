Celosia longistyla

Celosia longistyla, commonly known as the flame flower or crested grass.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant features striking, feather-like bracts that resemble flames, which are actually modified leaves rather than flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Celosia longistyla is known for its long blooming period, adding vibrant color to gardens throughout the growing season.
