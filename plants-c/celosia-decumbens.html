Celosia decumbens

Celosia decumbens, commonly known as the flame flower or red dwarf celosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spiky inflorescences that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a low, spreading shrub with fleshy, hairy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Celosia decumbens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
