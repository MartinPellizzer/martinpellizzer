Cyphia heterophylla

Cyphia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf cyphia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to red. The plant exhibits heterophylly, meaning it has different leaf shapes depending on its growth conditions, such as shade or full sun. Its leaves are often elliptical or ovate, with some varieties displaying a velvety texture.

Cyphia heterophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for with proper drainage and sunlight.
