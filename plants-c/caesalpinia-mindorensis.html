Caesalpinia mindorensis

Caesalpinia mindorensis, commonly known as Mindanao ticktree, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in open forests and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
