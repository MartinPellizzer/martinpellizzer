Crocosmia cinnabarina

Crocosmia cinnabarina, commonly known as the red flame torch lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear clusters of bright orange-red flowers resembling torches. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom from late summer through autumn.
