Conophytum stevens-jonesianum

Conophytum stevens-jonesianum, commonly known as the "Stevens-Jones rock conophytum," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, basal rosette of fleshy, ribbed leaves. The leaves are typically green with a reddish or purplish hue, especially when exposed to sunlight. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
