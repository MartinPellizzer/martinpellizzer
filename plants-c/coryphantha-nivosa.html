Coryphantha nivosa

Coryphantha nivosa, commonly known as the Snow Ghost Cactus, is a species of cactus native to the United States, particularly in Arizona and New Mexico.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with dark green, ribbed stems that are covered in white, woolly hair. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, and they bloom in late spring to early summer. This plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

It is a perennial species that is often found in desert scrub and limestone habitats.
