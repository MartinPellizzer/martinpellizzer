Calamus regularis

Calamus regularis, commonly known as the common arrowroot, is a member of the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features a rhizomatous stem that produces numerous narrow, sword-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

The plant is notable for its ability to thrive in wetland environments and its historical use in traditional medicine.
