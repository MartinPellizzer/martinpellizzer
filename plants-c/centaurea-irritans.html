Centaurea irritans

Centaurea irritans, commonly known as the prickly centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and bright yellow flowers with purple centers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Centaurea irritans is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
