Commelina decumbens

Commelina decumbens, commonly known as the spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or moist environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, blue or violet flowers with three petals. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in partial shade.

Commelina decumbens is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
