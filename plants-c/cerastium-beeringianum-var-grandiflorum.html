Cerastium beeringianum var. grandiflorum

Cerastium beeringianum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the Arctic mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers.
