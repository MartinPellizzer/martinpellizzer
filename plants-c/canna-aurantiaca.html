Canna aurantiaca

Canna aurantiaca, commonly known as the red canna, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It features large, broad leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, often with a reddish or bronze hue. The plant produces showy, colorful inflorescences that range from orange to red, adding vibrant visual interest to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Canna aurantiaca is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable conditions.
