Ceropegia longifolia var. sinensis

Ceropegia longifolia var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or Sinensis ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and unique floral morphology.
