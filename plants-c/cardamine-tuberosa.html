Cardamine tuberosa

Cardamine tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia and Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with showy blooms. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its tuberous roots are an important part of its structure and are often used in traditional medicine.

Cardamine tuberosa thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
