Colocasia antiquorum f. acuatica

Colocasia antiquorum f. acuatica, commonly known as the African giant leaf or giant water shield, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1.5 meters in diameter. The plant thrives in shallow, still water and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy surface, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged.

This plant plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and shade for various aquatic organisms.
