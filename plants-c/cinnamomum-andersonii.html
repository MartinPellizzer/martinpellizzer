Cinnamomum andersonii

Cinnamomum andersonii, commonly known as the Anderson's cinnamon tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Cambodia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It produces dark, glossy berries that contain the seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and for research purposes.
