Croton heterotrichus

Croton heterotrichus, commonly known as the red-corked croton, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that range from green to red and orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton heterotrichus typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.
