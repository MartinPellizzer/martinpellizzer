Catananche

Catananche, commonly known as the common catananche, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as fields and meadows. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

Catananche is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in disturbed soils.
