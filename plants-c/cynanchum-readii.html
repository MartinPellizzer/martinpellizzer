Cynanchum readii

Cynanchum readii, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed or Chinese dogbane, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late summer. Its latex contains toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.

It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
