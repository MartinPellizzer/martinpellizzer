Campium longiflagellatum

Campium longiflagellatum, commonly known as the longflagellate campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, branching stems that can extend up to several meters in length. Its flowers are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Campium longiflagellatum is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
