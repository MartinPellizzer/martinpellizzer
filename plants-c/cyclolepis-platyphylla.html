Cyclolepis platyphylla

Cyclolepis platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaved cyclolepis, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has rough, hairy stems and leathery, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.

Cyclolepis platyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
