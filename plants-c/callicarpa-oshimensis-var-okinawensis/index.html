Callicarpa Oshimensis Var. Okinawensis

Callicarpa oshimensis var. okinawensis is a unique variant of the beautyberry plant known for its striking red berries. Native to the island of Okinawa, this plant thrives in humid, subtropical climates. Its vibrant fruit clusters make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and natural habitats. The plant's leaves are heart-shaped and often have a glossy texture. It plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and adding visual interest to landscapes.
