Calluna vulgaris var. ciliaris

Calluna vulgaris var. ciliaris, commonly known as the heather with ciliate leaves, is a variant of the common heather.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to western Europe. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that are softly hairy along the edges, giving it its distinctive appearance. It produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in late summer.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and acidic soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
