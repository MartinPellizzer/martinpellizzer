Coelorachis ramosa

Coelorachis ramosa, commonly known as the branched staghorn fern, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its branched, tree-like fronds that resemble antlers. Coelorachis ramosa is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in hanging baskets or mounted on driftwood.
