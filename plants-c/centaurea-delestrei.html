Centaurea delestrei

Centaurea delestrei, commonly known as the Delestre's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its flowers are blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Centaurea delestrei is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
