Cyrtochilum orientale

Cyrtochilum orientale, commonly known as the oriental cyclochilum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Cyrtochilum orientale thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
