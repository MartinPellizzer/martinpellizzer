Crotalaria polychroma

Crotalaria polychroma, commonly known as the multicolored cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often with contrasting patterns. Its leaves are palmately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Crotalaria polychroma is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
