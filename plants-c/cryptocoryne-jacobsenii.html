Cryptocoryne jacobsenii

Cryptocoryne jacobsenii, commonly known as the red cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red-tinged leaves, which can vary in color depending on water conditions. This plant is often cultivated in freshwater aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. It prefers moderate lighting and can thrive in a range of water temperatures.

Cryptocoryne jacobsenii is also known for its ability to tolerate low light and nutrient-poor conditions.
