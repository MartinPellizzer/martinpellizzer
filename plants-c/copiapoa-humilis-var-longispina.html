Copiapoa humilis var. longispina

Copiapoa humilis var. longispina, commonly known as the long-spined cactus, is a species native to the Atacama Desert in Chile.

It is a low-growing, globular cactus with a short stem and dense, spiny foliage. The plant features elongated, pale yellow spines that are often curved, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in arid environments with minimal rainfall and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its unique morphology and hardiness in extreme desert conditions.
