Calluna vulgaris f. plena

Calluna vulgaris f. plena, commonly known as the full-flowered heather, is a variant of the common heather native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its profuse display of small, bell-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically a vibrant pink to red color, blooming in late summer to early autumn. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in acidic, well-drained soils.

It is often used in landscaping for its ability to form attractive ground cover.
