Cynanchum cubense

Cynanchum cubense, commonly known as Cuban milkweed, is a perennial herb native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes many species of milkweeds. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive corona structure.

Cynanchum cubense produces a milky sap, which is a common feature among milkweed species.
