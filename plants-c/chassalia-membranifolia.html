Chassalia membranifolia

Chassalia membranifolia, commonly known as the membranous chassalia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish to orange-colored blooms. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

Chassalia membranifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
