Coelogyne rigidula

Coelogyne rigidula, commonly known as the rigid orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and serve as storage organs for nutrients.

Coelogyne rigidula is valued for its ornamental flowers and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
