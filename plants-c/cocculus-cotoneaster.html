Cocculus cotoneaster

Cocculus cotoneaster, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for landscaping and hedges.
