Crassula aurusbergensis

Crassula aurusbergensis, commonly known as the Sunset Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and elegant appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
