Cecropia reticulata f. alboreticulata

Cecropia reticulata f. alboreticulata, commonly known as the white-veined cecropia, is a variant of the cecropia tree characterized by its distinctive white veins on the leaves.

This plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. Its leaves are typically large, alternate, and have a prominent reticulate venation pattern. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
