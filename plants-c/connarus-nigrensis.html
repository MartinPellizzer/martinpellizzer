Connarus nigrensis

Connarus nigrensis, commonly known as the black connarus, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Connaraceae and is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant produces yellowish-orange fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense tropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
