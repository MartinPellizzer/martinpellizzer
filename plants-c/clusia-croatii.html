Clusia croatii

Clusia croatii, commonly known as the Croatian glorybower, is a tropical shrub native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae, characterized by its showy, brightly colored flowers. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is known for its ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions.

Clusia croatii is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
