Centaurea pungens

Centaurea pungens, commonly known as the prickly centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Centaurea pungens is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
