Chrysanthemum rockii

Chrysanthemum rockii, commonly known as the rock chrysanthemum, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its foliage is gray-green with a fuzzy texture, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

Chrysanthemum rockii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
