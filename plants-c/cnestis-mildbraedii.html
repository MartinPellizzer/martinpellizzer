Cnestis mildbraedii

Cnestis mildbraedii, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slight pubescence.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
