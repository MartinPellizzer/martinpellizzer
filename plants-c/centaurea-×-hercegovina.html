Centaurea × hercegovina

Centaurea × hercegovina, commonly known as the Hercegovina starflower, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in summer and are arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Native to the Balkan region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
