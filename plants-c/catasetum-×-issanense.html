Catasetum × issanense

Catasetum × issanense, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial orchid, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are notable for their unique structure, with specialized pollinia that are adapted for specific pollinators.

Catasetum × issanense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
