Casearia coriifolia

Casearia coriifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf casearia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
