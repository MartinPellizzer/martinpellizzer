Calanthe lilacina

Calanthe lilacina, commonly known as the lilac orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep lilac. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Calanthe lilacina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its unique floral display.
