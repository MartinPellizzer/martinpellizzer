Cerastium gracile subsp. velenovskyi

Cerastium gracile subsp.

The plant typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or arctic environments. Its compact growth habit and frost-resistant nature make it well adapted to harsh climatic conditions.
