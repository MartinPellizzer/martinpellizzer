Cattleya × tenuata

Cattleya × tenuata, commonly known as the slender cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow pseudobulbs and strikingly large, showy flowers with a velvety texture. The plant typically produces fragrant, bicolor blooms in shades of pink, white, and yellow, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cattleya × tenuata is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
