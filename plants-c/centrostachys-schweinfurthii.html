Centrostachys schweinfurthii

Centrostachys schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its compact, mound-like growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
