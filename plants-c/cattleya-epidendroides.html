Cattleya epidendroides

Cattleya epidendroides, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its long, pendant-like flower spikes that bear numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya epidendroides is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
