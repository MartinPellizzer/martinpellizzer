Cotula abyssinica

Cotula abyssinica, commonly known as African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Cotula abyssinica has small, hairy leaves and produces numerous tiny seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its bright, cheerful appearance.
