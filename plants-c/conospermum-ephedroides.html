Conospermum ephedroides

Conospermum ephedroides, commonly known as the yellow conospermum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its dense, cylindrical stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces follicles that contain seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by wind. It is often found in heathlands and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and nectar for native wildlife.
