Comolia berberifolia

Comolia berberifolia, commonly known as the yellow-leafed broom.

It is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in the Andes region. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and narrow, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
