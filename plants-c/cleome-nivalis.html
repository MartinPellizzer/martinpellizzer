Cleome nivalis

Cleome nivalis, commonly known as snow cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its stems are often hairy and branched, and the leaves are alternate with a serrated margin.

Cleome nivalis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
