Callicarpa qianyiyongii

Callicarpa qianyiyongii, commonly known as the Qianyi yellow berry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow berries that mature in the autumn. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a fragrant, reddish-brown bark. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fruit color, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
