Capsicum hebephyllum

Capsicum hebephyllum, commonly known as the yellow bell pepper.

This plant belongs to the Solanaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. It is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped fruits that mature to a red-orange color. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and lobed leaves.

It is cultivated for its edible fruits, which are used in various culinary applications.
