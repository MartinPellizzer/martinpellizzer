Calla sagittifolia

Calla sagittifolia, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial native to Central and South America.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers with a spadix, surrounded by a large, showy spathe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Calla sagittifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
