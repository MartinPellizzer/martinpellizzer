Carex ninae

Carex ninae, commonly known as the Nina's sedge, is a perennial plant native to specific regions of North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes various sedges and grass-like plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and wetlands.
