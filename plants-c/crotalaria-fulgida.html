Crotalaria fulgida

Crotalaria fulgida, commonly known as the fiery crown or golden crown, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in racemes at the top of the plant. The plant has palmate leaves with three to five lobes, and its stems are often hairy and branching. Crotalaria fulgida is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.

It is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
