Cliffortia × semiteres

Cliffortia × semiteres, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, needle-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. It is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

Due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for coastal landscaping and rock gardens.
