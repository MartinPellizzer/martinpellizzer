Ceratostema miniatum

Ceratostema miniatum, commonly known as the miniature horned bellflower, is a small, flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Ceratostema miniatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
