Ceratophyllum tanaieticum

Ceratophyllum tanaieticum, commonly known as the slender hornwort, is a freshwater plant belonging to the family Ceratophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, branching structure and small, scale-like leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. This plant thrives in slow-moving or still water bodies, often found in ponds and lakes. It plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological importance, it is a valuable species for water quality assessment and ecological studies.
