Carduncellus mitissimus

Carduncellus mitissimus, commonly known as the soft cardoon.

It is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. The plant features thick, bluish-gray leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowering stalks produce yellow flowers in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
