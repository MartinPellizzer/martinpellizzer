Chrysocoryne myosuroides

Chrysocoryne myosuroides, commonly known as the golden crown or yellow crown, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble crowns. The plant has smooth, green, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
