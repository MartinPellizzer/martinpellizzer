Cypripedium × perseus

Cypripedium × perseus, commonly known as the imperial yellow lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow pouch and intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often in rich, organic soil. It has a pseudobulb structure and produces a single, upright flower stem with several showy blooms.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is considered a valuable and often protected species in its native habitat.
