Centaurea marmorea var. marmorea

Centaurea marmorea var. marmorea, commonly known as the marble centaury, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking, spiky blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, surrounded by pale green bracts. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
