Calathea kappleriana

Calathea kappleriana, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, patterned leaves that display a mix of deep green, silver, and pink hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often described as having a velvety texture, which adds to its ornamental appeal.
