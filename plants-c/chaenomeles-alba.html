Chaenomeles alba

Chaenomeles alba, commonly known as white Chinese quince, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces rough, woody fruits that mature to a yellowish-orange color in late summer. Its leaves are ovate and have a serrated edge, and the plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.
