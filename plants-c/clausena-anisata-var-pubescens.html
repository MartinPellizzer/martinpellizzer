Clausena anisata var. pubescens

Clausena anisata var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy tamarind tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by elongated, woody fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
