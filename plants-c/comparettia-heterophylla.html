Comparettia heterophylla

Comparettia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Comparettia heterophylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
