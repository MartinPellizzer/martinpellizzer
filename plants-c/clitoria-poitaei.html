Clitoria poitaei

Clitoria poitaei, commonly known as the purple orchid bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are often clustered in racemes. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.

Clitoria poitaei is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
