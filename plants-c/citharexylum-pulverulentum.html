Citharexylum pulverulentum

Citharexylum pulverulentum, commonly known as the dusty cithara, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its pleasant scent.
