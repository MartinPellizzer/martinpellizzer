Calochortus nudus

Calochortus nudus, commonly known as the naked belladonna lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes a variety of spring-blooming plants often referred to as glory-of-the-snow or mariposa lilies.
