Cryptocoryne hansenii

Cryptocoryne hansenii, commonly known as Hansen's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 15 cm in length. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. It is often cultivated in aquariums due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne hansenii is also notable for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced aquarists.
