Cypripedium × canhamiae

Cypripedium × canhamiae, commonly known as the canham's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy pink or purple flowers with a pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper. The plant typically grows in cool, moist habitats such as forests and meadows, often in shaded environments. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to anchor firmly in the soil while absorbing nutrients from decomposing organic matter.

Cypripedium × canhamiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
