Crossopetalum myrsinoides

Crossopetalum myrsinoides, commonly known as the silverleaf hibiscus, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery underside. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both visually appealing and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
