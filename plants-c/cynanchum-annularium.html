Cynanchum annularium

Cynanchum annularium, commonly known as Chinese snakeroot, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in forested areas and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

The roots are used to treat various ailments, including inflammation and respiratory conditions.
