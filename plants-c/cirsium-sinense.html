Cirsium sinense

Cirsium sinense, commonly known as Chinese thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its foliage is covered in stiff, bristly hairs, which contribute to its defensive adaptations.

Cirsium sinense is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
