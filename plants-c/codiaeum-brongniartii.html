Codiaeum brongniartii

Codiaeum brongniartii, commonly known as the flameleaf or painted leaf plant, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
