Camelina Hispida Var. Lasiocarpa

Camelina hispida var. lasiocarpa is a rare and fascinating plant native to the western United States. It is known for its distinctive appearance and unique ecological role in its habitat. This variety of Camelina features dense, hairy foliage that helps it thrive in arid environments. Its seeds are not only a food source for local wildlife but also hold potential for agricultural use. Despite its hardiness, it remains a relatively obscure species in botanical studies.
