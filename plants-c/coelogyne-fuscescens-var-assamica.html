Coelogyne fuscescens var. assamica

Coelogyne fuscescens var. assamica, commonly known as the Assam orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to orange-red flowers. The plant is epiphytic, often growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. It thrives in high altitudes and requires specific climatic conditions for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists.
