Cirsium × muellneri

Cirsium × muellneri, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, leafy stems and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually lobed and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in a composite head.

Cirsium × muellneri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance and hardy nature.
