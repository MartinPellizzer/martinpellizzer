Cynanchum kiusianum

Cynanchum kiusianum, commonly known as Kiusianum milkweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its milky sap, which is a common feature among milkweeds. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in umbels, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
