Combretum baumii

Combretum baumii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna or woodland habitats.

The bark is thick and fissured, providing a rugged appearance to the plant.
