Centaurea orumiehensis

Centaurea orumiehensis, commonly known as the Orumieh centaury, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

Centaurea orumiehensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
