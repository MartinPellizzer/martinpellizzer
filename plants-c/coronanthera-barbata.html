Coronanthera barbata

Coronanthera barbata, commonly known as the hairy helmet orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while the inflorescence consists of a single, showy flower.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
