Cattleya × harrisii

Cattleya × harrisii, commonly known as the Harris Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This orchid is popular among growers for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
