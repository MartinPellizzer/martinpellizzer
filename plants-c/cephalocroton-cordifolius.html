Cephalocroton cordifolius

Cephalocroton cordifolius, commonly known as the heartleaf cephalocroton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Cephalocroton cordifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses in its native region.
