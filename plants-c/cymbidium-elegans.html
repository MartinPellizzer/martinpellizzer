Cymbidium elegans

Cymbidium elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in winter. The plant produces lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It is a perennial herb that thrives in cool, humid climates with well-draining soil.

Cymbidium elegans is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
