Cuphea anisophylla

Cuphea anisophylla, commonly known as the flame wheel plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Cuphea anisophylla is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
