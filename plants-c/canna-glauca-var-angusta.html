Canna glauca var. angusta

Canna glauca var. angusta, commonly known as narrowleaf cannonball plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are a deep green with a bluish tinge, and it produces large, showy orange-red bracts that resemble flowers. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and dramatic appearance in garden settings.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
