Cyrtomium umbonatum

Cyrtomium umbonatum, commonly known as the mountain holly fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery fronds that are often dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to form dense colonies.
