Calamagrostis lanceolata f. macrathera

Calamagrostis lanceolata f. macrathera, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a compact, ornamental grass with distinctive silvery-blue foliage.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are slightly curled and have a soft, feathery appearance. The plant produces delicate, pale green to silver plumes during the summer months, adding visual interest to gardens. It is typically grown as a clump-forming perennial, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is prized for its low maintenance and ability to add texture and color to landscapes.
