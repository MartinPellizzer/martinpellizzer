Codiaeum medium

Codiaeum medium, commonly known as the flame plant or croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid environments. Its leaves are typically broad and have a textured surface, adding visual interest to gardens and indoor spaces.
