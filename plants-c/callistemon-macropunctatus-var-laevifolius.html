Callistemon macropunctatus var. laevifolius

Callistemon macropunctatus var. laevifolius, commonly known as the smooth-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange bottlebrush-like flowers that appear in summer. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy texture, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract native birds and pollinators.
