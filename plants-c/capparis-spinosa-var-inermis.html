Capparis spinosa var. inermis

Capparis spinosa var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth caper, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thornless stems and small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant produces edible, round, greenish-yellow buds that are harvested before they open. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly succulent texture.
