Chirita fusca

Chirita fusca, commonly known as the dark leaf hoya, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and opposite, dark green leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
