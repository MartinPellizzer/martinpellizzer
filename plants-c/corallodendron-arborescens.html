Corallodendron arborescens

Corallodendron arborescens, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, coral-like clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is well adapted to coastal environments and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
