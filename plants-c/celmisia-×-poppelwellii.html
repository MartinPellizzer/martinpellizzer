Celmisia × poppelwellii

Celmisia × poppelwellii, commonly known as the Poppelwellii Celmisia, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its herbaceous growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of yellow to orange flowers in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it requires specific care to maintain its health and bloom.
