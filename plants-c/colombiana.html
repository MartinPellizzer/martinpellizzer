Colombiana

Colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian rose, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red petals and its fragrant aroma. The plant typically grows as a shrub with woody stems and dark green foliage. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

Colombiana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.
