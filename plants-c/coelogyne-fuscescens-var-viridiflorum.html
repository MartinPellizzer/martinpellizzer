Coelogyne fuscescens var. viridiflorum

Coelogyne fuscescens var. viridiflorum, commonly known as the Emerald-flowered Coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically dark green and strap-shaped, providing a lush backdrop for the delicate flowers.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique coloration and ornamental value.
