Chamaedorea montana

Chamaedorea montana, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a compact, elegant plant native to Central America.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown surface and is crowned with a dense cluster of long, slender, dark green leaves. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture without becoming waterlogged. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and air-purifying qualities.

Its foliage makes it an attractive addition to shaded areas in gardens or as a decorative houseplant.
