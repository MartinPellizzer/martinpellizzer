Chrysanthemum × intermedium

Chrysanthemum × intermedium, commonly known as the Shishigashira chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and broad, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Chrysanthemum × intermedium is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.
