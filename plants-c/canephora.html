Canephora

Canephora, commonly known as robusta, is a species of coffee plant native to Central and Western Africa.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and produces red, fleshy berries containing two seeds. The plant is known for its high caffeine content and robust flavor profile compared to other coffee species. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Canephora is widely cultivated for its use in both pure and blended coffee products.
