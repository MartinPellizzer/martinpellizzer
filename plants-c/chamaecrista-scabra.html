Chamaecrista scabra

Chamaecrista scabra, commonly known as the hairy burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, yellow,蝶形花 (dibyamal) flowers and compound leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in sandy or disturbed soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Chamaecrista scabra is used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
