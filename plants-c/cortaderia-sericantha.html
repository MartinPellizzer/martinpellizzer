Cortaderia sericantha

Cortaderia sericantha, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, slender culms that are covered in silvery-white, feathery inflorescences, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. It is often used in landscaping for its elegant, flowing form and ability to add texture to garden designs.

Its foliage remains decorative throughout the year, making it a versatile choice for both residential and commercial landscapes.
