Capparis flanaganii

Capparis flanaganii, commonly known as the Flanagan's caper, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often accompanied by distinctive, fleshy, red or orange fruits. Its leaves are typically small, oval, and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Capparis flanaganii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
