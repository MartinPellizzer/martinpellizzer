Chrysanthemum visianii

Chrysanthemum visianii, commonly known as the Visianii chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to white. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Chrysanthemum visianii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
