Cerastium kitaibelii

Cerastium kitaibelii, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

Cerastium kitaibelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
