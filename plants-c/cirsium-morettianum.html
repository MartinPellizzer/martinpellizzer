Cirsium morettianum

Cirsium morettianum, commonly known as the Morettian thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs and have a serrated edge, contributing to its defensive features.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
