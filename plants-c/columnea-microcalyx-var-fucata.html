Columnea microcalyx var. fucata

Columnea microcalyx var. fucata, commonly known as the silverleaf columnea, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, red-orange flowers that are often adorned with a white or silver stripe. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in warm, humid environments.
