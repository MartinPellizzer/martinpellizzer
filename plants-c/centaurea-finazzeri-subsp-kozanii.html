Centaurea finazzeri subsp. kozanii

Centaurea finazzeri subsp.

kozanii, commonly known as the Kozan centaury, is a flowering plant native to Turkey. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and grayish-green, while the flowers are surrounded by bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique ecological niche.
