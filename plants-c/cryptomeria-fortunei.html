Cryptomeria fortunei

Cryptomeria fortunei, commonly known as the Japanese cedar, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and its dense, pyramidal crown. The leaves are scale-like and dark green, arranged in crossed pairs along the branches. The tree produces small, spherical cones that contain winged seeds.

It is valued for its timber and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental appearance.
