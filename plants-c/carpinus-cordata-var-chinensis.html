Carpinus cordata var. chinensis

Carpinus cordata var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its oval, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, followed by edible, dark blue berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological landscaping.
