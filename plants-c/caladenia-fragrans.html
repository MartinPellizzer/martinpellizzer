Caladenia fragrans

Caladenia fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils within heathlands and open forests. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, elongated flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish with a deep red or maroon lip.

The species is noted for its fragrant scent, which is most noticeable in the early morning or during cooler weather.
