Catasetum caatingense

Catasetum caatingense, commonly known as the caatinga orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate floral structure. The plant produces unique, often colorful flowers with specialized pollination mechanisms. Its roots are adapted to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.

Catasetum caatingense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
