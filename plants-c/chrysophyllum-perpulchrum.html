Chrysophyllum perpulchrum

Chrysophyllum perpulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful chrysophyllum, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often consumed fresh or used in culinary applications. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
