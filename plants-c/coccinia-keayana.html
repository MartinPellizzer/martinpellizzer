Coccinia keayana

Coccinia keayana, commonly known as the Keay's bubble gourd, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, pumpkins, and gourds. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its leaves are heart-shaped and can grow up to 20 centimeters in diameter.
