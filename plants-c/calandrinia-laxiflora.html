Calandrinia laxiflora

Calandrinia laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered portulaca, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Portulacaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Calandrinia laxiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
