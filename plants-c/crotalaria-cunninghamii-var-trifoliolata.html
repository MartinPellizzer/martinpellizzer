Crotalaria cunninghamii var. trifoliolata

Crotalaria cunninghamii var. trifoliolata, commonly known as the trifoliate crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces yellow, spurred flowers that are typical of the Crotalaria genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
