Cardiospermum elegans

Cardiospermum elegans, commonly known as the sweetleaf or heartleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its fruits are distinctive, consisting of inflated, bladder-like pods that contain seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
