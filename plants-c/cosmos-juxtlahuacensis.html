Cosmos juxtlahuacensis

Cosmos juxtlahuacensis, commonly known as the Juxtlahuaca cosmos, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flower heads. Cosmos juxtlahuacensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
