Chamaedorea angustisecta

Chamaedorea angustisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bamboo palm, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear feathery, dark green leaves with a narrow, elongated shape. The plant has a dense, bushy appearance with multiple stems growing from the base, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Chamaedorea angustisecta prefers indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens.
