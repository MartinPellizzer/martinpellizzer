Cattleya × kienastiana

Cattleya × kienastiana, commonly known as the Kienastiana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in tropical and subtropical environments. Its flowers are borne on erect, branching inflorescences, and it prefers bright, indirect light with high humidity.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
