Crataegus × macrocarpa nothovar. macrocarpa

Crataegus × macrocarpa nothovar. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited hawthorn, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring thorny branches, white to pink flowers, and red to orange berries.
