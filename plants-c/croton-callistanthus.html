Croton callistanthus

Croton callistanthus, commonly known as the flame tree or red croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has alternate, simple, ovate leaves that are usually green with a smooth texture. Its botanical features include a woody stem with a fibrous bark and small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
