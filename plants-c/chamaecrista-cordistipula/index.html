Chamaecrista Cordistipula

Chamaecrista cordistipula is a rare and fascinating legume native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. this plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry woodlands. it plays an important role in local ecosystems by fixing nitrogen and supporting pollinators. due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
