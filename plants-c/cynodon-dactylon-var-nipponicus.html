Cynodon dactylon var. nipponicus

Cynodon dactylon var. nipponicus, commonly known as Japanese lawngrass, is a variant of the common Bermuda grass.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its fine, dense foliage and rhizomatous growth habit. This plant is native to Japan and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, requiring regular maintenance for optimal growth.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for various climates, though it can be invasive if not properly managed.
