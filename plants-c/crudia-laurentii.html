Crudia laurentii

Crudia laurentii, commonly known as the African firethorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, spiny branches. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries in the summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

The plant is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
