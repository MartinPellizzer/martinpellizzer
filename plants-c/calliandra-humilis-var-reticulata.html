Calliandra humilis var. reticulata

Calliandra humilis var. reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences composed of numerous small, colorful flowers that range from pink to red. The plant has compound leaves with a finely toothed margin and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climate gardens.

The species is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
