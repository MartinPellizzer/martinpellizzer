Cardosoa athanasioides

Cardosoa athanasioides, commonly known as the Ethiopian sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing adaptations to harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
