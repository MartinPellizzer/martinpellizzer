Cnestis volubilis

Cnestis volubilis, commonly known as the climbing cnestis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a vine that produces small, yellowish flowers and is often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a climbing habit, making it suitable for trellises or support structures. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
