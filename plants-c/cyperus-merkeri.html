Cyperus merkeri

Cyperus merkeri, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically brown or reddish in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in wet or moist environments.

Cyperus merkeri is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies.
