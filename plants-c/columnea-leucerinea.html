Columnea leucerinea

Columnea leucerinea, commonly known as the scarlet ice plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from bright red to orange. It produces small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

Columnea leucerinea is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
