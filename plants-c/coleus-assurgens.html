Coleus assurgens

Coleus assurgens, commonly known as the silver leaf coleus, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage that features a mix of silver, white, and green hues. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a slightly hairy surface. This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant coloration.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor spaces.
