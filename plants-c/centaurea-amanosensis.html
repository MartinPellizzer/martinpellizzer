Centaurea amanosensis

Centaurea amanosensis, commonly known as the Amanos Mountain centaurea, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spiky bracts around its flower heads.

Centaurea amanosensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
