Caladenia arrecta

Caladenia arrecta, commonly known as the erect spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils within open forests or heathlands. The plant features a single, erect stem with a single spike of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers have a distinctive spider-like appearance.

Caladenia arrecta is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its delicate and ephemeral blooms.
