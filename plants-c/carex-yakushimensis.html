Carex yakushimensis

Carex yakushimensis, commonly known as the Yakushiman carex, is a species of sedge native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically produces small, clustered flowers that are often green or brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Carex yakushimensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
