Clematis meyeniana var. pavoliniana

Clematis meyeniana var. pavoliniana, commonly known as the purple clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from deep purple to violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, round berries that mature to a dark blue or black color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a climbing plant.
