Critesion jubatum

Critesion jubatum, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, creating a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
