Cydista picta

Cydista picta, commonly known as the painted orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that display a mix of purple, pink, and white hues. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Cydista picta thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found growing in shaded forest understories.
