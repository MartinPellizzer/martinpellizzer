Cynanchum viridiflorum

Cynanchum viridiflorum, commonly known as greenflower milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by follicles containing seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Cynanchum viridiflorum is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
