Cattleya forbesii f. delicata

Cattleya forbesii f. delicata, commonly known as the Delicate Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, fragrant flowers.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of pendulous, showy blooms with a rich, velvety texture. The petals are typically a deep red or pink, often with a contrasting white or yellow lip. This variety is native to the tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
