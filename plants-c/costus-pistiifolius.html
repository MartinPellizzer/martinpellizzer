Costus pistiifolius

Costus pistiifolius, commonly known as the spiny costus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Costaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, orange-red inflorescence. The plant features narrow, sword-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers arranged in a spike. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its striking appearance.
