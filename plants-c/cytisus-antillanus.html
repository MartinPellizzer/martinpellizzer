Cytisus antillanus

Cytisus antillanus, commonly known as the Antillean broom, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer. Its leaves are small, narrow, and often have a slight pubescence.

Cytisus antillanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
