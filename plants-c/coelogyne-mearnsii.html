Coelogyne mearnsii

Coelogyne mearnsii, commonly known as the Mearns' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red or purple throat. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne mearnsii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
