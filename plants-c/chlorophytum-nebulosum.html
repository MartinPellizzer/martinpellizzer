Chlorophytum nebulosum

Chlorophytum nebulosum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically striped with green and white patterns. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stems, which can be followed by bright orange berries. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in a variety of light conditions.

Chlorophytum nebulosum is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to purify indoor air and its ease of care.
