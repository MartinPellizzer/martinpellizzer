Croton stylosus

Croton stylosus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often features vibrant red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant has palmate leaves with lobed margins, and its flowers are small and clustered in panicles. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Croton stylosus is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
