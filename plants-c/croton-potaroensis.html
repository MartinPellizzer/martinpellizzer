Croton potaroensis

Croton potaroensis, commonly known as the Potaro croton, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of northern South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically alternate and can vary in shape from ovate to lanceolate.

Croton potaroensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded garden settings.
