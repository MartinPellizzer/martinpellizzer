Casearia randioides

Casearia randioides, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly aromatic scent. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule that splits open to release seeds.
