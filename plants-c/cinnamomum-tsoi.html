Cinnamomum tsoi

Cinnamomum tsoi, commonly known as Tsoi's cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark purple berries. Its bark is used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to cinnamon.

However, it is less commonly cultivated compared to other cinnamon species.
