Clitoria mariana var. pubescentia

Clitoria mariana var. pubescentia, commonly known as the pubescent winged bean, is a tropical legume native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers, which are typically blue or purple in color. The plant produces edible pods that contain seeds and a starchy, protein-rich pulp. Its leaves are trifoliate, with hairy surfaces that give it the "pubescent" designation.

This variety is often cultivated for its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.
