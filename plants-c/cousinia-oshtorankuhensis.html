Cousinia oshtorankuhensis

Cousinia oshtorankuhensis, commonly known as the Oshtorankuh cudweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Iran.

Cousinia oshtorankuhensis is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, contributing to the ecological diversity of its native environment.
