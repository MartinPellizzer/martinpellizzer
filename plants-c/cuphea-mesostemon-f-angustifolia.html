Cuphea mesostemon f. angustifolia

Cuphea mesostemon f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is often grown as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and compact size.
