Chrysanthemum delavayanum

Chrysanthemum delavayanum, commonly known as the Delavay's chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Chrysanthemum delavayanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
