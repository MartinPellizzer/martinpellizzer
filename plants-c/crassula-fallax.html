Crassula fallax

Crassula fallax, commonly known as the "Lace Leaf Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-forming foliage with fleshy, silver-green leaves that have a textured, lace-like appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
