Coelogyne alboviridis

Coelogyne alboviridis, commonly known as the white-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white and green floral patterns, which are unique among the Coelogyne genus. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette, often with a waxy texture.

This species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
