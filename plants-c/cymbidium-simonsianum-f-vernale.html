Cymbidium simonsianum f. vernale

Cymbidium simonsianum f. vernale, commonly known as the early blooming cymbidium, is a hybrid orchid species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green color. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often grown as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
