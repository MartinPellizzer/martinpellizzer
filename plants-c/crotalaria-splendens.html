Crotalaria splendens

Crotalaria splendens, commonly known as the golden chain plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in long, cascading clusters, resembling chains. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Crotalaria splendens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates.
