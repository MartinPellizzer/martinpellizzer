Calamagrostis × calammophila

Calamagrostis × calammophila, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a hybrid grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking silver-gray foliage that contrasts beautifully with its deep green blades. The plant produces tall, slender inflorescences that are often adorned with panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Calamagrostis × calammophila is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
