Cattleya skinneri

Cattleya skinneri, commonly known as the Skinner's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a hybrid orchid, resulting from crosses between Cattleya trianae and Cattleya mossae. This plant is characterized by its large, fragrant, and showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The leaves are broad and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blossoms.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
