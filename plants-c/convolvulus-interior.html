Convolvulus interior

Convolvulus interior, commonly known as the common morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue, purple, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Convolvulus interior is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail along structures.
