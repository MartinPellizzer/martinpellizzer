Centaurea paniculata subsp. geresensis

Centaurea paniculata subsp.

geresensis, commonly known as the Geresen Centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, branching stems that often grow up to 60 cm in height.

The plant produces showy, pink to reddish-purple flower heads composed of both ray and disk florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and rocky habitats.
