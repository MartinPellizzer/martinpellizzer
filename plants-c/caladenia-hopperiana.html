Caladenia hopperiana

Caladenia hopperiana, commonly known as the Hopper's Spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Western Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is found in regions with a Mediterranean climate. The plant features a single, slender stem that bears a single, intricately patterned orchid flower. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color with dark markings, resembling a spider in appearance.

Caladenia hopperiana is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its unique and delicate floral structure.
