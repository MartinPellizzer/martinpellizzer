Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris subsp. gaditana

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris subsp.

gaditana, commonly known as the "Love Plant," is a succulent species native to South Africa. It is characterized by its fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent gardens.
