Clusia maguireana

Clusia maguireana, commonly known as the waxleaf clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, providing a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by orange or red fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Clusia maguireana is also valued for its ability to tolerate a variety of soil conditions and its resilience to drought.
