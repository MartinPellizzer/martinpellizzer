Campanula scheuchzeri var. grandiflora

Campanula scheuchzeri var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Alps.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are typically hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula scheuchzeri var. grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
