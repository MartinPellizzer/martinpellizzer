Cnicus pauciflorus

Cnicus pauciflorus, commonly known as the few-flowered sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Cnicus pauciflorus is adapted to sandy and rocky soils, often found in coastal and inland dunes.
