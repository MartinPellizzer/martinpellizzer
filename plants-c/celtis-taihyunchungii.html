Celtis taihyunchungii

Celtis taihyunchungii, commonly known as the Chinese hackberry, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, followed by small, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species.

It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in forested areas and along riverbanks.
