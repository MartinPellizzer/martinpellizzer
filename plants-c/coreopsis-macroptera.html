Coreopsis macroptera

Coreopsis macroptera, commonly known as the broadleaf coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 2 feet.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
