Cyathea surinamensis

Cyathea surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is a large, evergreen fern that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered with persistent, dark brown to blackish-brown leaves. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive, glossy green color and a leathery texture.

Cyathea surinamensis is native to tropical regions of South America and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
