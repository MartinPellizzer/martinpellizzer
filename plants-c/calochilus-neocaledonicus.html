Calochilus neocaledonicus

Calochilus neocaledonicus, commonly known as the New Caledonian spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like flower structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, round pseudobulbs.

Calochilus neocaledonicus is a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native habitat.
