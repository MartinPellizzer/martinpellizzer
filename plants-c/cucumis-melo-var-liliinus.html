Cucumis melo var. liliinus

Cucumis melo var. liliinus, commonly known as the Persian melon, is a variant of the melon plant characterized by its unique botanical features.

It typically produces oval-shaped fruits with a thick, smooth rind that ranges in color from pale green to yellowish-orange. The fruit is known for its sweet, aromatic flesh and is often used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
