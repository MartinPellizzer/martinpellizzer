Cymbalaria Longipes

Cymbalaria longipes is a unique flowering plant known for its delicate, trailing growth habit. Native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, it thrives in rocky and dry environments. This species is often mistaken for weeds due to its low, sprawling form. Its small, bell-shaped flowers bloom in shades of pink and white, adding subtle beauty to its habitat. Despite its humble appearance, Cymbalaria longipes holds ecological significance as a food source for various pollinators.
