Colquhounia coccinea var. parviflora

Colquhounia coccinea var. parviflora, commonly known as the scarlet flame vine, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
