Combretum youngii

Combretum youngii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
