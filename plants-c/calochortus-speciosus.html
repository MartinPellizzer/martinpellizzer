Calochortus speciosus

Calochortus speciosus, commonly known as the showy globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its bulbs are an important food source for various insects and small animals.

Calochortus speciosus is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
