Carex kumaonensis

Carex kumaonensis, commonly known as the Kumaon sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas, particularly in India and Nepal. The plant features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and grass-green in color. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Carex kumaonensis is often found in moist, rocky habitats and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
