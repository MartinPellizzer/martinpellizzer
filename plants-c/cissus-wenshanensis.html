Cissus wenshanensis

Cissus wenshanensis, commonly known as the Wenshan devil's ivy, is a climbing plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically inconspicuous. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.
