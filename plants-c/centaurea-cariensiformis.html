Centaurea cariensiformis

Centaurea cariensiformis, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in compact heads.

Centaurea cariensiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
