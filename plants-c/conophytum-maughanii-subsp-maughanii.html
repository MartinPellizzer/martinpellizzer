Conophytum maughanii subsp. maughanii

Conophytum maughanii subsp.

maughanii, commonly known as the Maughan's conophytum, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa. It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
