Corydalis × hybrida

Corydalis × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid corydalis, is a flowering plant in the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, often pink or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and features slender, branched stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.
