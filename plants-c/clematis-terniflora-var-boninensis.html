Clematis terniflora var. boninensis

Clematis terniflora var. boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin clematis, is a climbing vine native to the Bonin Islands.

It features compound leaves with three leaflets, which are usually opposite and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be used in gardens or as a climbing plant.
