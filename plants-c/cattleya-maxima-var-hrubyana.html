Cattleya maxima var. hrubyana

Cattleya maxima var. hrubyana, commonly known as the Hruby's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, showy flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple. It is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often used in orchid shows and exhibitions for its striking appearance and fragrance.
