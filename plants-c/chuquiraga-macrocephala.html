Chuquiraga macrocephala

Chuquiraga macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed chuquiraga, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
