Chlorophytum intermedium var. subundulatum

Chlorophytum intermedium var. subundulatum, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail Plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, long, arching leaves that often have a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
