Clerodendrum darrisii

Clerodendrum darrisii, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow center. The plant produces small, clustered fruits that are typically green when mature and turn red upon ripening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Clerodendrum darrisii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and bees.
