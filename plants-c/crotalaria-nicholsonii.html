Crotalaria nicholsonii

Crotalaria nicholsonii, commonly known as the Nicholson's crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with a crown-like structure. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils.

Crotalaria nicholsonii is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
