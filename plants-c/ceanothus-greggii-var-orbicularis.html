Ceanothus greggii var. orbicularis

Ceanothus greggii var. orbicularis, commonly known as the Arizona bluebush, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It features dense, rounded clusters of small, sky-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has gray-green, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, bushy form. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for xeriscaping in desert landscapes.
