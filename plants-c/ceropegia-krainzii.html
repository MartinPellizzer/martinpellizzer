Ceropegia krainzii

Ceropegia krainzii, commonly known as the rainbow vine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant features fleshy, opposite leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia krainzii is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its ornamental flowers and ease of care.
