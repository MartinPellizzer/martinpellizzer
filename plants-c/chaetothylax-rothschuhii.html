Chaetothylax rothschuhii

Chaetothylax rothschuhii, commonly known as the Rothschild's spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are alternate and lance-shaped, with a distinctive waxy coating.

It is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
