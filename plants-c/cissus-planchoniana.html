Cissus planchoniana

Cissus planchoniana, commonly known as the African snow vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its stems are succulent and can be used for propagation.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
