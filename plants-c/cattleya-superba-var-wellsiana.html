Cattleya superba var. wellsiana

Cattleya superba var. wellsiana, commonly known as the Wellsiana Cattleya, is a striking orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant pink or red hues with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant produces long, arching inflorescences that bear multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for horticultural displays. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beautiful, fragrant flowers.
