Cymbidium × charlesworthii

Cymbidium × charlesworthii, commonly known as the Charlesworth orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear numerous racemes of fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers typically have a vibrant color palette, ranging from white to pink and yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. This orchid thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
