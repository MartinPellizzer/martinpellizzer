Caladenia varians

Caladenia varians, commonly known as the Variable Spider-orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and its distinctive, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or pale yellow. The plant produces a single, erect spike bearing several small, elongated flowers. Caladenia varians thrives in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats, often in regions with a Mediterranean climate.

It is a protected species due to habitat loss and is often found in conservation reserves.
