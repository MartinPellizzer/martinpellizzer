Cherleria stellata

Cherleria stellata, commonly known as the star-flowered cherleria, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Cherleria stellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate and intricate floral display.
