Cecropia telealba

Cecropia telealba, commonly known as the white ceiba, is a fast-growing tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and striking white flowers. The plant has palmate leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in tropical rainforests and plays a role in supporting local biodiversity.
