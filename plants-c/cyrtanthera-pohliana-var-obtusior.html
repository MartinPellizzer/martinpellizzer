Cyrtanthera pohliana var. obtusior

Cyrtanthera pohliana var. obtusior, commonly known as the broad-leafed sand orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
