Crotalaria nuda

Crotalaria nuda, commonly known as the naked crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and distinctive pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with simple, alternate leaves. Crotalaria nuda is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
