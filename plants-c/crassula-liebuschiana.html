Crassula liebuschiana

Crassula liebuschiana, commonly known as the Liebuse's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its small, star-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Crassula liebuschiana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
