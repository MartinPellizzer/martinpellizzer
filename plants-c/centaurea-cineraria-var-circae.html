Centaurea cineraria var. circae

Centaurea cineraria var. circae, commonly known as the Spanish blueweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue to violet flower heads surrounded by pale blue bracts, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, grayish-white hairs. Its leaves are narrow, serrated, and also covered in a fine, powdery coating.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
