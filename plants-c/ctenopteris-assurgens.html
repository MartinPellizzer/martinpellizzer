Ctenopteris assurgens

Ctenopteris assurgens, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a member of the fern family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, compound fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are divided into segments with a distinctive, overlapping structure.

Ctenopteris assurgens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
