Cinnabarinea

Cinnabarinea, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a striking plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and green, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its unique and showy floral display. Cinnabarinea thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and enthusiasts of exotic flora.
