Carduus tianschanicus

Carduus tianschanicus, commonly known as the Tian Shan thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh climates. Its leaves are generally spiny and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The flowers, which appear in late summer, are yellow and arranged in a spherical shape, attracting various pollinators.
