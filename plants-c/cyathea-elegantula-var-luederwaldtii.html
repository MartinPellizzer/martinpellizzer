Cyathea elegantula var. luederwaldtii

Cyathea elegantula var. luederwaldtii, commonly known as the Luederwaldt's tree fern, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is a member of the order Cyatheales. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology makes it a striking addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
