Cnidoscolus ulei

Cnidoscolus ulei, commonly known as the arrow leaf or arrowhead plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, spiny fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is adaptable to various light conditions.
