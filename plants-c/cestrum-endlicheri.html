Cestrum endlicheri

Cestrum endlicheri, commonly known as the night blooming jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that open at night. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a slightly hairy texture.

Cestrum endlicheri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its nocturnal fragrance.
