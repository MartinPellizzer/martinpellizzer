Cribbia

Cribbia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Cribbia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
