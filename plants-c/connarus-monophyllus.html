Connarus monophyllus

Connarus monophyllus, commonly known as the single-leaf connarus, is a unique flowering plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its singular, large, leathery leaf that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understorey of tropical forests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Connarus monophyllus is considered an endangered species.
