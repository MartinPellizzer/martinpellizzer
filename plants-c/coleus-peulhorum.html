Coleus peulhorum

Coleus peulhorum, commonly known as the African coleus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Coleus peulhorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
