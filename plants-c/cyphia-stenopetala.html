Cyphia stenopetala

Cyphia stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled Cyphia, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub native to South Africa, particularly found in the Western Cape region. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists interested in native flora.
