Croton rehderianus

Croton rehderianus, commonly known as the red croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from bright yellow to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight.

Croton rehderianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.
