Coeloglossum viride subsp. eurasiaticum

Coeloglossum viride subsp.

eurasiaticum, commonly known as the Eurasian green hawksbeard, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves and bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. It has a fibrous root system and can spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in certain environments.

Its botanical features make it easily distinguishable from other similar species in the genus.
