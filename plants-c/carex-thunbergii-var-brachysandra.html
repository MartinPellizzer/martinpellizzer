Carex thunbergii var. brachysandra

Carex thunbergii var. brachysandra, commonly known as narrow-leaved sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly curled at the edges. The plant produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
