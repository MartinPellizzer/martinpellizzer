Columnea forseithii

Columnea forseithii, commonly known as the Forseith's flame.

It is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America. This species is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically elliptical and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
