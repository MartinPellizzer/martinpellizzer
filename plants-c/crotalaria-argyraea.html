Crotalaria argyraea

Crotalaria argyraea, commonly known as silverleaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions.

The seeds are encased in a hard pod and are dispersed by wind or animals.
