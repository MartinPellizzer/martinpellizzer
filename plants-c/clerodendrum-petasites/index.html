Clerodendrum Petasites

Clerodendrum petasites is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers and unique foliage. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful pruning to maintain its shape and prevent overgrowth.
