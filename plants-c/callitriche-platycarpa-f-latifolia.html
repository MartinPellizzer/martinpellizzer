Callitriche platycarpa f. latifolia

Callitriche platycarpa f. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf water starwort, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers and narrow, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface. The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving waters, such as ponds and marshes. Its leaves are often broad and flat, aiding in buoyancy and photosynthesis.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small invertebrates and contributing to water clarity.
