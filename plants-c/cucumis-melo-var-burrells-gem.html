Cucumis melo var. burrells-gem

Cucumis melo var. burrells-gem, commonly known as the Burrell's Gem melon, is a variety of muskmelon characterized by its compact growth habit and high yield.

It produces small, round fruits with a dark green rind and a sweet, aromatic flesh. The plant typically thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is a trailing vine that benefits from trellising to improve air circulation and reduce disease risk.

This variety is prized for its early maturity and excellent flavor, making it a popular choice for home gardeners and commercial growers alike.
