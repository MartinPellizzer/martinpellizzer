Chytranthus pilgerianus

Chytranthus pilgerianus, commonly known as the Desert Sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Chytranthus pilgerianus is adapted to harsh desert conditions, with deep roots that help it survive in low-water environments.
