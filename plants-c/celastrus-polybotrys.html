Celastrus polybotrys

Celastrus polybotrys, commonly known as the Chinese glorybower, is a deciduous climbing shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Celastraceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The plant is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
