Cynanchum kwangsiense

Cynanchum kwangsiense, commonly known as the Guangxi milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in umbels. It is often found in moist, forested areas and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its latex contains compounds that have been studied for potential pharmaceutical applications.
