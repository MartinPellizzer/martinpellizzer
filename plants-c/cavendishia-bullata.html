Cavendishia bullata

Cavendishia bullata, commonly known as the Bullata Cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Cavendishia bullata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
