Cyperus rivularis var. lagunetto

Cyperus rivularis var. lagunetto, commonly known as the Lagunetto flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in South America.

It typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of lakes and rivers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Its root system helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion in aquatic habitats.

It is an important component of the ecological balance in its native wetland ecosystems.
