Cleistanthus inundatus var. velutinus

Cleistanthus inundatus var. velutinus, commonly known as the velvet cleistanthus, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
