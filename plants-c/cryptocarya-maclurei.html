Cryptocarya maclurei

Cryptocarya maclurei, commonly known as the Maclure's cryptomeria, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, preferring well-drained soils and mild climates.

Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with a distinct, spicy scent when crushed, and it produces small, yellowish-green fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.
