Crotalaria phyllostachys

Crotalaria phyllostachys, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and pinnately compound leaves with narrow, elongated leaflets. The plant produces clusters of yellow to orange flowers, which are followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often grown as a forage crop or for its ornamental value.

Crotalaria phyllostachys is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which enhances soil fertility.
