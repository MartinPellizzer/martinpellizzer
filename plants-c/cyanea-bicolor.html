Cyanea bicolor

Cyanea bicolor, commonly known as the Hawaiian violet, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet and white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs.

Cyanea bicolor is also noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
