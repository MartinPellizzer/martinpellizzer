Carduus acicularis

Carduus acicularis, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe, North Africa, and western Asia, and is often found in dry, disturbed soils. The plant has spiny, needle-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is typically perennial, growing up to 1 meter tall, and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
