Cotyledon glabra

Cotyledon glabra, commonly known as the smooth cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, bright yellow or orange flowers during the growing season. Cotyledon glabra is known for its ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
