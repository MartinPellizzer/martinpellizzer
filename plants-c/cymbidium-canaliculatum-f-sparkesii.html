Cymbidium canaliculatum f. sparkesii

Cymbidium canaliculatum f. sparkesii, commonly known as the Sparkes' Cymbidium, is a rare and elegant orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive red or pink lip. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a prized addition to orchid collections. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in two rows along the stem.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
