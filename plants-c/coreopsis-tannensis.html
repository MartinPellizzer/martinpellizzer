Coreopsis tannensis

Coreopsis tannensis, commonly known as the tannensis coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces bright yellow to orange flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically beneficial, supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
