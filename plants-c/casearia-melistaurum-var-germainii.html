Casearia melistaurum var. germainii

Casearia melistaurum var. germainii, commonly known as the Germain's casearia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a small, capsule-like structure containing several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soil and full sun.
