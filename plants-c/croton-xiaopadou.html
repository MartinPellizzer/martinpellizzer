Croton xiaopadou

Croton xiaopadou, commonly known as the small padou croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, with leaves that display a range of hues from yellow to red and orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
