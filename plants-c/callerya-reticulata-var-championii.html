Callerya reticulata var. championii

Callerya reticulata var. championii, commonly known as the Champion's callerya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and a woody stem that can grow into a small tree or shrub. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
