Centaurium calycosum var. breviflorum

Centaurium calycosum var. breviflorum, commonly known as the short-flowered centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its small, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant is herbaceous, with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It blooms from late spring to early summer, producing clusters of small, attractive flowers that are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
