Carex straminea var. schkuhrii

Carex straminea var. schkuhrii, commonly known as Schkuhrii sedge, is a perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces spikes of small, pale brown flowers in the summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full to partial sun. It is often found in wet meadows, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and naturalized landscapes.
