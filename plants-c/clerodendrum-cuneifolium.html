Clerodendrum cuneifolium

Clerodendrum cuneifolium, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often mistaken for flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and serrated edges. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, clustered in umbels.

Clerodendrum cuneifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
