Cleidion lasiophyllum

Cleidion lasiophyllum, commonly known as the glossy leaf cleidia, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage with a striking glossy appearance, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth margin and a dark green upper surface. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by round, woody fruits containing several seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
