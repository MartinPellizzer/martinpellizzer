Crotalaria miranda

Crotalaria miranda, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and hairy, ridged stems. The plant produces small, spiny pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Crotalaria miranda is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
