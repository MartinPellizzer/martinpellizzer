Cypripedium × behrensianum

Cypripedium × behrensianum, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a hybrid between Cypripedium yunnanense and Cypripedium calceolus, exhibiting a blend of characteristics from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its large, showy, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white with a yellow throat. It thrives in cool, moist, shaded habitats and requires specific soil conditions for successful growth.

Due to its rarity and horticultural appeal, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
