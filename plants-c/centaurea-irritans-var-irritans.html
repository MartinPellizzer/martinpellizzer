Centaurea irritans var. irritans

Centaurea irritans var. irritans, commonly known as the prickly centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers with purple centers. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
