Cyrtochilum schildhaueri

Cyrtochilum schildhaueri, commonly known as the Schildhauer's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth during periods of drought. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive greenish-yellow color and a dark red lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cyrtochilum schildhaueri is appreciated for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
