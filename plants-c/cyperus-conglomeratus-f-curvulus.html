Cyperus conglomeratus f. curvulus

Cyperus conglomeratus f. curvulus, commonly known as the curly flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and narrow, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and shallow water areas. Its leaves are usually curved, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
