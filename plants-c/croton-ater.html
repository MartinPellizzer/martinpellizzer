Croton ater

Croton ater, commonly known as the black croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and striking red or orange bracts that surround its small yellow flowers. Croton ater is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and unique appearance.

It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.
