Cyperus iria f. multiflora

Cyperus iria f. multiflora, commonly known as the silver sword sedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically white or pale green, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water edges.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for water gardens and landscape design.
