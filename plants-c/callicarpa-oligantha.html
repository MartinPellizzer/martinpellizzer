Callicarpa oligantha

Callicarpa oligantha, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, vibrant purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are oval-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

The berries are not only ornamental but also serve as a food source for various birds and small mammals.
