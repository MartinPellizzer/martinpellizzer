Centrogenium calcaratum

Centrogenium calcaratum, commonly known as the rock lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

Centrogenium calcaratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
