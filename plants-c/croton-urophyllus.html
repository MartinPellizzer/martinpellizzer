Croton urophyllus

Croton urophyllus, commonly known as the red-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which includes shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically thick and leathery, with a waxy surface.

Croton urophyllus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
