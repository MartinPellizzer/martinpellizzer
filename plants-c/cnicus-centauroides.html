Cnicus centauroides

Cnicus centauroides, commonly known as the centaur's thorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its spiny stems and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
