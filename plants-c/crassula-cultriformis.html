Crassula cultriformis

Crassula cultriformis, commonly known as the knife-shaped crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
