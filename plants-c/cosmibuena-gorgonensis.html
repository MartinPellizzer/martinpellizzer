Cosmibuena gorgonensis

Cosmibuena gorgonensis, commonly known as the Gorgon's Beauty, is a rare flowering plant native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bioluminescent petals that emit a soft blue glow at night. The plant's leaves are broad and glossy, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture in its tropical habitat. Its unique reproductive system involves a symbiotic relationship with specific pollinating insects.

Due to its endangered status, conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this extraordinary species.
