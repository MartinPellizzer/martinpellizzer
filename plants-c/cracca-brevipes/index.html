Cracca Brevipes

Cracca brevipes is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique, compact growth habit. This species is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists due to its ornamental value and ecological significance. Unfortunately, it faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this fascinating flora for future generations.
