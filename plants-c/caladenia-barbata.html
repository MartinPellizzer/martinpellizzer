Caladenia barbata

Caladenia barbata, commonly known as the feathered spider-orchid, is an elegant orchid native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and delicate, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with intricate patterns, and they bloom during the spring and summer months.

Caladenia barbata is a protected species, highlighting its ecological significance and the need for conservation efforts.
