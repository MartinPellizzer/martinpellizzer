Clematis davidiana

Clematis davidiana, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pink. The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil. Its vigorous growth habit makes it an excellent choice for trellises or arbors.

Clematis davidiana is also valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various garden conditions.
