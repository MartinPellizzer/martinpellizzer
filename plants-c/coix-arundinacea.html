Coix arundinacea

Coix arundinacea, commonly known as bamboo grass or sweet grass, is a perennial grass native to Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Coix arundinacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in various cultures.

Its rhizomes are sometimes used in herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
