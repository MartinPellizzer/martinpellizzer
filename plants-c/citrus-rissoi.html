Citrus rissoi

Citrus rissoi, commonly known as the desert citron, is a small, deciduous shrub native to the deserts of North Africa and the Middle East.

It produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or green in color, with a thick, aromatic rind. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is well adapted to arid environments. Its fruit is used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent.

Citrus rissoi is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh climates.
