Cubeba nigrescens

Cubeba nigrescens, commonly known as cubeb or java pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae and is characterized by its small, dark purple berries. The plant produces a distinctive aroma due to the presence of essential oils, particularly cubebene. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Cubeb is often cultivated for its medicinal and aromatic properties.
