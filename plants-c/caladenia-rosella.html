Caladenia rosella

Caladenia rosella, commonly known as the pink-stemmed spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy pink or mauve flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowering period occurs during the autumn season, usually between September and November.

The species is part of the Caladenia genus, which is known for its intricate and often colorful floral structures.
