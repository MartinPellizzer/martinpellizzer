Clusia dardanoi

Clusia dardanoi, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its evergreen nature and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

Clusia dardanoi is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.
