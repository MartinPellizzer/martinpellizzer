Cryptocoryne zonata

Cryptocoryne zonata, commonly known as the Zonate Amazon Sword, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive green to bluish-green coloration and a lighter midrib. The plant thrives in tropical freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life. It prefers moderate to high lighting and can grow up to 30 cm in height.

Cryptocoryne zonata is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for both novice and experienced aquarists.
