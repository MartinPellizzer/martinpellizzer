Coelogyne lucbanensis

Coelogyne lucbanensis, commonly known as the Lucban orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs and linear leaves, typically found in moist, shaded forest areas. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers with a distinctive yellow and red coloration, often with a fragrant scent. Its blooming period occurs during the rainy season, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Coelogyne lucbanensis is also noted for its ecological importance in its native habitat.
