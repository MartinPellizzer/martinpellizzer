Campanula chinalug

Campanula chinalug, commonly known as Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to regions of East Asia.

It features striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula chinalug is known for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
