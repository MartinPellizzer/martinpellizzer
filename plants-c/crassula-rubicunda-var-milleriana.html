Crassula rubicunda var. milleriana

Crassula rubicunda var. milleriana, commonly known as the Miller's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically red or pinkish in color, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
