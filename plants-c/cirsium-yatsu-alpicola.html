Cirsium yatsu-alpicola

Cirsium yatsu-alpicola, commonly known as the Japanese thistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and cool climates. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or gravelly areas.
