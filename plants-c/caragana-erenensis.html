Caragana erenensis

Caragana erenensis, commonly known as the Tien Shan caragana, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to harsh environments, including arid and semi-arid regions, and is often found in mountainous areas. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds, and it plays a role in soil stabilization and erosion control.

Caragana erenensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits.
