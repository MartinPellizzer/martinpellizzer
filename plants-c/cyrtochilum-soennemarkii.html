Cyrtochilum soennemarkii

Cyrtochilum soennemarkii, commonly known as the Soennemark's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped pouch that encloses the reproductive structures. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and has small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Cyrtochilum soennemarkii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
