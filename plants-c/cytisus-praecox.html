Cytisus praecox

Cytisus praecox, commonly known as early flowering broom, is a flowering shrub native to Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and heaths. The plant features slender, thorned stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom early in the spring. Its leaves are small, compound, and often covered in fine hairs.

Cytisus praecox is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
