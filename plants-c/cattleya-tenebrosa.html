Cattleya tenebrosa

Cattleya tenebrosa, commonly known as the dark cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and striking dark purple to black flowers with yellow lips. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a dramatic visual contrast to its foliage.

Cattleya tenebrosa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique coloration and ornamental value.
