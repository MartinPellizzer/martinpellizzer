Chamaecrista polyadena

Chamaecrista polyadena, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for gardens and landscapes.

Chamaecrista polyadena is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant floral display.
