Coreopsis setigera

Coreopsis setigera, commonly known as the slender coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and wetlands. The plant features slender, branching stems and produces daisy-like yellow flowers with white ray florets. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often have a rough texture.

Coreopsis setigera is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
