Cypripedium parviflorum

Cypripedium parviflorum, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a herbaceous perennial orchid native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, large, pouch-shaped flower that is typically pink or white with a yellow lip. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, aiding in water retention.

Cypripedium parviflorum is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value and ecological importance.
