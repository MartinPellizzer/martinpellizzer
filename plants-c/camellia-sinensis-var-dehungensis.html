Camellia sinensis var. dehungensis

Camellia sinensis var. dehungensis, commonly known as the Dehungensis tea plant, is a variant of the tea plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and robust growth habit. The plant thrives in subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil. It is primarily cultivated for its leaves, which are used to produce black and oolong teas.

Due to its unique flavor profile and aroma, it is highly valued in specialty tea markets.
