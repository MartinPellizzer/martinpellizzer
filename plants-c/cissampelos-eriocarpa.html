Cissampelos eriocarpa

Cissampelos eriocarpa, commonly known as the velvet leaf or African moon vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by elongated, fleshy fruits. Its stems are often armed with short, hooked spines, which aid in climbing and defense.

Cissampelos eriocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
