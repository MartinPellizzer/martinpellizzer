Colutea × media

Colutea × media, commonly known as the hybrid false peacock.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. The plant features compound leaves with stipules and small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is native to temperate regions of Europe and is cultivated for its ornamental value.
