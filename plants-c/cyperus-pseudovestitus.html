Cyperus pseudovestitus

Cyperus pseudovestitus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. It has slender, triangular stems and small, scale-like leaves.

Its flowering spikes are usually borne at the base of the plant and produce tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
