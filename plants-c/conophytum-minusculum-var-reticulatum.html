Conophytum minusculum var. reticulatum

Conophytum minusculum var. reticulatum, commonly known as the miniature conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a distinctive reticulated pattern. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
