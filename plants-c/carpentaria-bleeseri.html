Carpentaria bleeseri

Carpentaria bleeseri, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant belongs to the family Cactaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in indoor environments. Its flowers typically come in shades of red, pink, and white, and it prefers bright, indirect light.

Carpentaria bleeseri is a popular ornamental plant due to its long blooming period and ease of care.
