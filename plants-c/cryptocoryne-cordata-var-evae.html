Cryptocoryne cordata var. evae

Cryptocoryne cordata var. evae, commonly known as the Eve's cryptocoryne, is a rare and ornamental aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, narrow leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly translucent appearance. The plant is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. It thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires stable water parameters to maintain its health.

This variant is highly sought after by aquarists for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
