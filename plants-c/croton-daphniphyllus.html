Croton daphniphyllus

Croton daphniphyllus, commonly known as the silver leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green hues. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its leaves are alternate and often have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
