Crassula ausensis subsp. giessii

Crassula ausensis subsp.

giessii, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa. It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically pale green to bluish-gray, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
