Cycnopodium latifolium

Cycnopodium latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf burrweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by spiny, burr-like fruits. It typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.

Cycnopodium latifolium is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through its persistent, seed-bearing fruits.
