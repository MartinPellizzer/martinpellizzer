Conophytum fibuliforme

Conophytum fibuliforme, commonly known as the "lizard's tail" or "spoon-leafed stone plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, fleshy body with thick, spoon-shaped leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Conophytum fibuliforme is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
