Cynanchica kemulariae

Cynanchica kemulariae, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to regions of Asia, including China, Korea, and Japan, and thrives in temperate climates. It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its growth habit includes both herbaceous and shrub-like forms depending on environmental conditions.
