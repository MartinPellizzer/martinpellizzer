Clematis × boweri

Clematis × boweri, commonly known as the Boweri Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to climb using its tendrils. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is typically heart-shaped and provides a lush green backdrop to the flowers.

Clematis × boweri is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
