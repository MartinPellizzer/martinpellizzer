Cedrela toona var. pilipetala

Cedrela toona var. pilipetala, commonly known as the Philippine sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly the Philippines.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, characterized by its aromatic wood and unique botanical features. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. The plant is valued for its durable, lightweight timber and its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
