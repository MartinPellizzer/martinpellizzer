Caryophyllus balansae

Caryophyllus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes various species of carnations and related plants. The plant produces showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, typical of many Caryophyllaceae species.

Caryophyllus balansae is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.
