Chrysanthemum rigidum

Chrysanthemum rigidum, commonly known as the rigid chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are rigid and upright.

Chrysanthemum rigidum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
