Centaurea pouzinii var. macrocephala

Centaurea pouzinii var. macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, cone-shaped flower heads that range in color from pink to purple. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
