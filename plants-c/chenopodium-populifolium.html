Chenopodium populifolium

Chenopodium populifolium, commonly known as silverleaf goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, ovate to triangular leaves that are typically covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. It has a fibrous root system and can grow up to 1 meter in height.

Silverleaf goosefoot is sometimes considered a weed but can be used in traditional herbal medicine.
