Cotula featherstonei

Cotula featherstonei, commonly known as the featherleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Cotula featherstonei is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
