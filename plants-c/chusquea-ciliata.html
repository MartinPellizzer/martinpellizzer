Chusquea ciliata

Chusquea ciliata, commonly known as the silky bamboo or cloud bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in humid, temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Chusquea ciliata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
