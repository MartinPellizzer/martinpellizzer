Consolida scleroclada var. rigida

Consolida scleroclada var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid scarlet rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces striking clusters of bright red, spurred flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and adaptable to various garden conditions.
