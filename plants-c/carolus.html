Carolus

Carolus, commonly known as the Carolus plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to violet. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a slightly rough texture.

Carolus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
