Clematis oliveri

Clematis oliveri, commonly known as Oliver's clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis oliveri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and can be used to cover trellises or walls.
