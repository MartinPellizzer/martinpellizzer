Cissus Capreolata

Cissus capreolata is a unique and hardy vine native to southern Africa. Known for its trailing growth habit and distinctive heart-shaped leaves, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal maintenance, making it ideal for beginners. Its ability to climb or trail makes it versatile for decorating shelves, hanging baskets, or trellises. With its lush green foliage and easy care requirements, Cissus capreolata is a great addition to any plant collection.
