Cyathula schimperiana

Cyathula schimperiana, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Cyathula schimperiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
