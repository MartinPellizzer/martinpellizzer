Ciminalis dshimilensis

Ciminalis dshimilensis, commonly known as the Shimmering Crimson, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Eastern Rift Valley.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red petals that emit a faint bioluminescent glow at night. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its arid habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a conservation priority.
