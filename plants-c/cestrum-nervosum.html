Cestrum nervosum

Cestrum nervosum, commonly known as night blooming cereus, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. The plant is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that open at night and close by dawn. Its stems are woody and often branched, with opposite, simple leaves.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
