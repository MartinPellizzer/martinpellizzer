Centaurea ognjanoffii

Centaurea ognjanoffii, commonly known as the Ognjanoff's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.
