Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. gredense

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. gredense, commonly known as the "Crown of Thorns," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
