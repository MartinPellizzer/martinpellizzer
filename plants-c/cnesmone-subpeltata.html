Cnesmone subpeltata

Cnesmone subpeltata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cnesmone, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a desirable plant for ornamental horticulture.
