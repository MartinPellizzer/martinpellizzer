Codiaeum balansae

Codiaeum balansae, commonly known as the flame leaf or fire leaf plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that ranges from red to orange and yellow, often with contrasting green veins. The leaves are typically broad, heart-shaped, and have a velvety texture. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This plant is known for its ability to thrive in humid environments and is often used as a decorative foliage plant.
