Chlorophytum piquetbergense

Chlorophytum piquetbergense, commonly known as the Piquetberg snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
