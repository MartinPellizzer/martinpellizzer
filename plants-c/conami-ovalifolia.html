Conami ovalifolia

Conami ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf conami, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the rainy season. Its root system is fibrous and well-developed, allowing it to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Conami ovalifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
