Centaurea seusana var. lingulata

Centaurea seusana var. lingulata, commonly known as the Alboran Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been historically used in traditional herbal medicine.
