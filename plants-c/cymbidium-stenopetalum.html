Cymbidium stenopetalum

Cymbidium stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its flowers are borne on tall, branching inflorescences and are often used in floral arrangements.

Cymbidium stenopetalum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate in temperate climates.
