Caladium bicolor f. punctatissumum

Caladium bicolor f. punctatissumum, commonly known as the spotted caladium, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves.

The foliage displays a unique combination of deep green, white, and red hues, with prominent white veins and red borders that create a dramatic visual effect. It is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light. This variety is prized for its foliage rather than its flowers, which are small and inconspicuous.

It is often used in gardens and as a houseplant to add color and texture to landscapes.
