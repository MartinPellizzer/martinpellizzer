Coryanthes bueraremensis

Coryanthes bueraremensis, commonly known as the Buerareme Orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks and branches in its natural habitat. Its unique floral structure includes a specialized lip that aids in pollination by insects and birds.

Coryanthes bueraremensis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting diverse pollinator species.
