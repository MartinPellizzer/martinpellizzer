Carex yajiangensis

Carex yajiangensis, commonly known as the Yajiang sedge, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants commonly found in moist environments. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
