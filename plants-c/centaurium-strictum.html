Centaurium strictum

Centaurium strictum, commonly known as lesser centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often hairy. Its small, bright yellow flowers are clustered in dense spikes, and they bloom from June to August.

The plant is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
