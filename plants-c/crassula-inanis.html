Crassula inanis

Crassula inanis, commonly known as the silver bush or woolly crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant typically grows in a compact, shrub-like form with thick, fleshy leaves that store water. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months.

Crassula inanis is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
