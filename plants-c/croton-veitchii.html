Croton veitchii

Croton veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's croton, is a species of flowering plant in the spurge family, Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. Croton veitchii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf colors and adaptability to various growing conditions.
