Corynophallus afzelii var. spectabilis

Corynophallus afzelii var. spectabilis, commonly known as the giant pteridium, is a striking fern-like plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, frond-like leaves that can reach several meters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its unique morphology includes a distinct, club-shaped base with a central stem.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
