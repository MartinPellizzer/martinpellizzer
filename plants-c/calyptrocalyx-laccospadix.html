Calyptrocalyx laccospadix

Calyptrocalyx laccospadix, commonly known as the hooded spadix, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure. The plant features a spadix covered by a modified leaf called a calyx, which gives it its common name.
