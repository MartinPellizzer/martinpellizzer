Cattleya × elegans var. wolstenholmiae

Cattleya × elegans var. wolstenholmiae, commonly known as the Wolstenholme's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically display a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that support its growth. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
