Cnicus helenioides

Cnicus helenioides, commonly known as the common sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive lobed shape.

It thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.
