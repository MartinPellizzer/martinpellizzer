Chlorophytum comosum var. bipindense

Chlorophytum comosum var. bipindense, commonly known as the Variegated Spider Plant, is a hybrid variety of the spider plant known for its striking, variegated foliage.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings, which are a result of genetic variation. The plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa, where it thrives in warm, sunny conditions. It is a low-maintenance plant that prefers well-draining soil and moderate watering.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
