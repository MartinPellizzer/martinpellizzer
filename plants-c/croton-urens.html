Croton urens

Croton urens, commonly known as the velvetleaf or small-leaved croton, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Croton urens is also noted for its medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
