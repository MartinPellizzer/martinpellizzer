Chirita lilacina

Chirita lilacina, commonly known as the purple leaf begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves that display a striking purple or reddish hue. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Chirita lilacina is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
