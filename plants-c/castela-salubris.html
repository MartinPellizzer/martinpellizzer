Castela salubris

Castela salubris, commonly known as the sacred castela, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to red. Castela salubris is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in some indigenous communities.
