Calamintha heterotricha

Calamintha heterotricha, commonly known as woolly betony, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features aromatic, hairy leaves that are often blue-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet, arranged in loose clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Calamintha heterotricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
