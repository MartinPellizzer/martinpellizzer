Centaurea vicentina

Centaurea vicentina, commonly known as the Spanish thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and vibrant purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean regions.

Its distinctive appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in botanical studies.
