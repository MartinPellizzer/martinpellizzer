Chirita cordifolia

Chirita cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds.

Its foliage and flowers contribute to its popularity among horticultural enthusiasts.
