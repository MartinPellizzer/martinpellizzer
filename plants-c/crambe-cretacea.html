Crambe cretacea

Crambe cretacea, commonly known as the Crete crambe, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring upright stems and lobed leaves. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Crambe cretacea is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.
