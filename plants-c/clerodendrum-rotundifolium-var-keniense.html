Clerodendrum rotundifolium var. keniense

Clerodendrum rotundifolium var. keniense, commonly known as Kenyan roundleaf clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils near streams or in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some local cultures.
