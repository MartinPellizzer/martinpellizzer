Ceropegia racemosa var. voiensis

Ceropegia racemosa var. voiensis, commonly known as the spiral milkweed, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage structure.
