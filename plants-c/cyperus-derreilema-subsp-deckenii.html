Cyperus derreilema subsp. deckenii

Cyperus derreilema subsp.

deckenii, commonly known as the narrowleaf sedgeland sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves.

The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and wetlands. It produces small, clustered spikes of flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color.

This subspecies is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
