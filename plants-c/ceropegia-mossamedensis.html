Ceropegia mossamedensis

Ceropegia mossamedensis, commonly known as the Cape Kruger ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its trailing stems and tubular flowers. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers that are often clustered in groups. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
