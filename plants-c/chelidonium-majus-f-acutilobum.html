Chelidonium majus f. acutilobum

Chelidonium majus f. acutilobum, commonly known as the acute-lobed greater celandine, is a variant of the greater celandine plant.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features bright yellow flowers and has distinctive, lobed leaves with a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle it with care.
