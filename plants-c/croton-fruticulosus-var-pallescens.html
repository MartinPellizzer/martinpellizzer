Croton fruticulosus var. pallescens

Croton fruticulosus var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale green to grey-green stems and broad, ovate leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow-green flowers followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
