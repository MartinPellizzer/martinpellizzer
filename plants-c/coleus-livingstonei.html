Coleus livingstonei

Coleus livingstonei, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is native to tropical Africa and belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae. It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety foliage with colorful patterns and striking flowers that range from white to pink and purple. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy texture, while the flowers are tubular with two lips.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
