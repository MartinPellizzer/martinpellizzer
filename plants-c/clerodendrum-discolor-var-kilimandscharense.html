Clerodendrum discolor var. kilimandscharense

Clerodendrum discolor var. kilimandscharense, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro flame bush, is a shrub native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It features striking red and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in tropical and subtropical climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
