Calathea zingiberina

Calathea zingiberina, commonly known as the Ginger Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a mix of green, silver, and pink hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea zingiberina is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
