Croton humblotii

Croton humblotii, commonly known as the Blunt-leaf Croton, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful foliage, which can range from deep green to vibrant red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are usually thick and leathery, with a rough texture.

Croton humblotii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental leaves and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
