Cassia ketschta

Cassia ketschta, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves composed of several narrow, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Cassia ketschta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
