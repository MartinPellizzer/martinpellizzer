Coelogyne Pangasinanensis

Coelogyne pangasinanensis is a rare and striking orchid native to the Philippines. It is known for its vibrant, colorful blooms that appear during the dry season. This species is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and limited availability. Unfortunately, it faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
