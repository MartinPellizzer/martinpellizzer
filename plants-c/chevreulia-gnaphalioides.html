Chevreulia gnaphalioides

Chevreulia gnaphalioides, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for its durability and strength. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
