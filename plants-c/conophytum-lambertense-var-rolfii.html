Conophytum lambertense var. rolfii

Conophytum lambertense var. rolfii, commonly known as the Lambert's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit and fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a desirable addition to succulent collections.
