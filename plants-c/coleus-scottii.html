Coleus scottii

Coleus scottii, commonly known as the Scott's coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Coleus scottii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
