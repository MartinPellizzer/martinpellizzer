Chylismia Claviformis Subsp. Aurantiaca

Chylismia claviformis subsp. aurantiaca is a rare and striking plant known for its vivid orange flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique coloration makes it a sought-after species among plant enthusiasts and botanists. The subspecies is distinguished by its elongated, club-shaped leaves and vibrant floral display. Due to habitat loss, it is now classified as endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
