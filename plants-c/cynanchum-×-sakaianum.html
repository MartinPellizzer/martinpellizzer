Cynanchum × sakaianum

Cynanchum × sakaianum, commonly known as the Japanese milkvetch, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by follicles that release seeds with silky hairs. It is native to East Asia and thrives in moist, shady environments.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
