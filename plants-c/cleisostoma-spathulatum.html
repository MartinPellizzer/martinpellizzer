Cleisostoma spathulatum

Cleisostoma spathulatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed cleisostoma, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are broad and ovate, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Cleisostoma spathulatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
