Ceropegia schultzei

Ceropegia schultzei, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and thick, waxy leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or white, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia schultzei is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
