Chaenactis brachiata var. stansburyana

Chaenactis brachiata var. stansburyana, commonly known as the Stansbury's rayhead, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are yellow to orange in color, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind.
