Calochilus metallicus

Calochilus metallicus, commonly known as the metallic orchid, is a species native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking metallic blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or inland regions. Its flowers are short-lived but highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
