Clintonia odorata

Clintonia odorata, commonly known as the sweet-scented bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features attractive, bell-shaped white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and is often found in rich, well-drained soils.

Clintonia odorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
