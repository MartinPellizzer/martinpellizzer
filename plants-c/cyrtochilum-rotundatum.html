Cyrtochilum rotundatum

Cyrtochilum rotundatum, commonly known as the round-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its tropical habitat. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often found growing in shaded, well-drained soils.

Cyrtochilum rotundatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
