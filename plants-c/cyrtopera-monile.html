Cyrtopera monile

Cyrtopera monile, commonly known as the necklace spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with dark markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cyrtopera monile is often cultivated for its unique and ornamental floral structure.
