Cyrtochilum betancurii

Cyrtochilum betancurii, commonly known as the Betancurii slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and small, intricate flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
