Cullumia rigida

Cullumia rigida, commonly known as rigid cullumia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Cullumia rigida is known for its resilience and is often found in desert and semi-desert regions.
