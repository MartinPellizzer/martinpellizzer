Crinum insigne

Crinum insigne, commonly known as the noble lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, fragrant scent. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Crinum insigne is known for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
