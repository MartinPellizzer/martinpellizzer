Croton sarcopetaloides

Croton sarcopetaloides, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its bark is thick and fibrous, which contributes to its hardy nature.

Croton sarcopetaloides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
