Coccoloba rufescens f. longifolia

Coccoloba rufescens f. longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf coccoloba, is a variant of the red coccoloba, characterized by its elongated leaves.

This plant is a member of the Polygonaceae family, known for its distinctive leaf morphology and growth habits. It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. The leaves are usually dark green with a waxy surface, and the plant produces small, clustered flowers.
