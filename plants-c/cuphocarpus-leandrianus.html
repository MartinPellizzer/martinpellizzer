Cuphocarpus leandrianus

Cuphocarpus leandrianus, commonly known as the sand mat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming shrub that thrives in sandy and rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

Cuphocarpus leandrianus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
