Carex jiuhuaensis

Carex jiuhuaensis, commonly known as the Jiuhua sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Jiuhua Mountain region of Anhui Province. This plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grass-like leaves and spike-like inflorescences. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Carex jiuhuaensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in maintaining soil stability in its native habitat.
