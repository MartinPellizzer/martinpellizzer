Clelia ornata

Clelia ornata, commonly known as the orange clelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or a trailing plant, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Clelia ornata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
