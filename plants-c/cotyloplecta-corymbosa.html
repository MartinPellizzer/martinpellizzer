Cotyloplecta corymbosa

Cotyloplecta corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Cotyloplecta corymbosa is often found in shaded, moist habitats such as forest understories.
