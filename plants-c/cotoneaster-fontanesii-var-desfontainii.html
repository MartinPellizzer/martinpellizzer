Cotoneaster fontanesii var. desfontainii

Cotoneaster fontanesii var. desfontainii, commonly known as the Desfontaines cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter in color on the underside. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

This variety is well-suited to a variety of soil types and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
