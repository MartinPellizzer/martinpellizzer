Coelogyne cuprea var. planiscapa

Coelogyne cuprea var. planiscapa, commonly known as the red flame orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, fleshy leaf and produces showy, red-orange flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. It is prized for its vibrant color and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and sought-after species in horticulture.
