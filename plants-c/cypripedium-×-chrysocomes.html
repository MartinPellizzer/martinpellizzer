Cypripedium × chrysocomes

Cypripedium × chrysocomes, commonly known as the golden-lip cypripedium, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the genus Cypripedium.

It is characterized by its showy, yellowish-orange flowers with a distinctive lip that resembles a lip or petal. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of broad, grass-like leaves, and it produces a single flower per stem.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
