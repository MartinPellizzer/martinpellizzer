Crotalaria cleomifolia var. iodesia

Crotalaria cleomifolia var. iodesia, commonly known as the purple crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a contrasting yellow center. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces elongated seed pods that split open when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both aesthetic and practical purposes.
