Chione cubensis

Chione cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Chione cubensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
