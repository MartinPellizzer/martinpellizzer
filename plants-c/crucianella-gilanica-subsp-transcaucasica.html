Crucianella gilanica subsp. transcaucasica

Crucianella gilanica subsp.

transcaucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian sea bean, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Caucasus region. It belongs to the family Leguminosae and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pod-like fruits.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
