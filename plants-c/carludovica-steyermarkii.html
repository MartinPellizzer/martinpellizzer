Carludovica steyermarkii

Carludovica steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to poor soil conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
