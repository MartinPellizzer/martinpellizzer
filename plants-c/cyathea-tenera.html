Cyathea tenera

Cyathea tenera, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its roots form a dense network that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Cyathea tenera is often found in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests.
