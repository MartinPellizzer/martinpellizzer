Ceropegia cycniflora

Ceropegia cycniflora, commonly known as the silver leaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tuberous roots and has small, opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery waxy coating. The plant produces delicate, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in umbels, which are typically pollinated by specific insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
