Calea clausseniana var. balansana

Calea clausseniana var. balansana, commonly known as the Balansana's candleplant, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, candle-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, white to pale yellow ray florets. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in native gardens.
