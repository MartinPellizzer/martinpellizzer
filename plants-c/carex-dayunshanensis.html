Carex dayunshanensis

Carex dayunshanensis, commonly known as the Dayunshan sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its leaves are grass-like, with a prominent midrib, and it produces small, clustered spikelets during the flowering season.

Carex dayunshanensis is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
