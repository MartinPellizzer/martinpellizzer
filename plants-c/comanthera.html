Comanthera

Comanthera, commonly known as the milkweed vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The flowers are typically small, clustered in umbels, and feature showy, pink to purple petals. Comanthera produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is a common weed in agricultural areas.
