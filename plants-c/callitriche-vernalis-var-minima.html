Callitriche vernalis var. minima

Callitriche vernalis var. minima, commonly known as the spring starwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It typically grows in shallow waters, such as ponds, ditches, and slow-moving streams. The plant has small, star-shaped leaves that are usually less than 1 centimeter in diameter. Its flowers are tiny, greenish-white, and appear in clusters at the water's surface.

It is an annual species that blooms in early spring, contributing to the biodiversity of wetland ecosystems.
