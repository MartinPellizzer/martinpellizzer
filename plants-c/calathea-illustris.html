Calathea illustris

Calathea illustris, commonly known as the pink diamond plant, is a tropical perennial belonging to the Marantaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive pink and white veining pattern. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Calathea illustris is also known for its ability to purify the air, adding to its appeal as a decorative and beneficial houseplant.
