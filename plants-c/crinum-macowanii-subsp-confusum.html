Crinum macowanii subsp. confusum

Crinum macowanii subsp.

confusum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved crinum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers in umbels.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation. It is known for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrower leaves and slightly different floral structure compared to other Crinum species.
