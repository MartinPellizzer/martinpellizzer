Chloranthus nervosus var. nervosus

Chloranthus nervosus var. nervosus, commonly known as the narrow-veined chloranthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with prominent venation. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are woody drupes that mature to a dark color, often containing a single seed.
