Canangium brandisanum

Canangium brandisanum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its bark contains compounds used in traditional medicine, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various animals.

The tree is also valued for its fast growth and ability to withstand urban conditions.
