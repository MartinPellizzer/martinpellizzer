Catasetum brenesii

Catasetum brenesii, commonly known as the Yellow Catasetum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip that is often used in pollination by specific wasps. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. It has erect, often branching stems with lance-shaped leaves.

Catasetum brenesii is valued in horticulture for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
