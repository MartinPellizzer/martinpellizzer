Chlorophytum vestitum subsp. vestitum

Chlorophytum vestitum subsp.

vestitum, commonly known as the hooded spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa. It is characterized by its rosette of broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, white or silver bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces long, arching inflorescences bearing small, greenish-white flowers. It is a hardy and drought-tolerant species, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its foliage is often used in horticultural displays for its ornamental value.
