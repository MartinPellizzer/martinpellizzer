Cotula nudicaulis

Cotula nudicaulis, commonly known as the narrowleaf daisy, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming throughout the warmer months. The plant has thin, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Cotula nudicaulis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
