Callitris cupressiformis

Callitris cupressiformis, commonly known as the coastal she-oak, is a hardy evergreen shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves and small, cone-like fruits. The plant thrives in coastal and sandy soils, often found in dune environments. It has a strong, aromatic scent and is well adapted to withstand salt spray and wind.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and stabilizing coastal ecosystems.
