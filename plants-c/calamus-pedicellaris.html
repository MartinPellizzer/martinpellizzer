Calamus pedicellaris

Calamus pedicellaris, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. Calamus pedicellaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
