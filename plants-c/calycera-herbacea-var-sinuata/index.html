Calycera Herbacea Var. Sinuata

Calycera herbacea var. sinuata is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of eastern Asia. It is characterized by its slender stems and small, pale green leaves that often display subtle sinuous patterns. The plant produces tiny, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now considered endangered in many areas. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
