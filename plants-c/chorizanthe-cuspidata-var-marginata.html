Chorizanthe cuspidata var. marginata

Chorizanthe cuspidata var. marginata, commonly known as the Coast Horned Evening Primrose, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of California.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy stems and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the evening. The leaves are alternate, linear, and often have a distinctive white or pale margin. This species thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to coastal climates with moderate rainfall.

It plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ecological and ornamental qualities.
