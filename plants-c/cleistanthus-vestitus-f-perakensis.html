Cleistanthus vestitus f. perakensis

Cleistanthus vestitus f. perakensis, commonly known as the Perak velvet tree, is a variant of the Cleistanthus vestitus species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks. Its leaves are thick and leathery, providing a unique texture and contributing to its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its potential in ecological restoration due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
