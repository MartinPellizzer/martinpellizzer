Celastrus obovatifolius

Celastrus obovatifolius, commonly known as the broad-leafed staff-vine, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. The leaves are typically broad and oval-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant's fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.
