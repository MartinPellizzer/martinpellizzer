Campanula ovacikensis subsp. capitellata

Campanula ovacikensis subsp.

capitellata, commonly known as the Ovacik bellflower, is a rare and endemic species native to Turkey. It is characterized by its bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils in alpine or subalpine regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces compact, spherical flower clusters.
