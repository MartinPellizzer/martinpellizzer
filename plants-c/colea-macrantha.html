Colea macrantha

Colea macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered colea, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Colea macrantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
