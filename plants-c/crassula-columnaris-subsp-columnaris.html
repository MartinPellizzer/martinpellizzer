Crassula columnaris subsp. columnaris

Crassula columnaris subsp.

columnaris, commonly known as the columnar crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its upright, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several feet in height.

The plant produces small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color, depending on light exposure. It blooms with clusters of star-shaped, pink or white flowers during the cooler months.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens or as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
