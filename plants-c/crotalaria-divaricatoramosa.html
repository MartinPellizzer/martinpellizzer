Crotalaria divaricatoramosa

Crotalaria divaricatoramosa, commonly known as the spreading crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in disturbed soils. It has a bushy growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, which splits open when mature to release the seeds.
