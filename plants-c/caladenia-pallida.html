Caladenia pallida

Caladenia pallida, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats. It blooms during the autumn season, producing delicate flowers with a single lip that is often pale in color.

Caladenia pallida is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its ornamental value and ecological significance.
