Carex capillacea var. capillacea

Carex capillacea var. capillacea, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetlands and marshy areas in North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in moist to wet soil conditions and is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds and streams.

This species is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for aquatic and terrestrial wildlife.
