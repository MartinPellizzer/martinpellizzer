Christella cavaleriei

Christella cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lanceolate leaves with a smooth texture. It produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Christella cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
