Croton pygmaeus

Croton pygmaeus, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lobed leaves that range in color from deep green to variegated yellow and white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its foliage.

Due to its compact size, it is suitable for container gardening and small garden spaces.
