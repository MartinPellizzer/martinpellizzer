Cochemiea Guelzowiana

Cochemiea guelzowiana is a striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring cylindrical stems covered in dense, white spines. This plant thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for desert gardens. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of yellow and orange, add a vibrant touch to its otherwise stark form. Despite its beauty, it requires minimal care and is well-suited for xeriscaping.
