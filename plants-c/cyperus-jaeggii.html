Cyperus jaeggii

Cyperus jaeggii, commonly known as the dwarf flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Cyperus jaeggii is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various water conditions.
