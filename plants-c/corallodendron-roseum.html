Corallodendron roseum

Corallodendron roseum, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive coral-like inflorescences, which consist of clusters of small, bright red or pink flowers. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Corallodendron roseum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
