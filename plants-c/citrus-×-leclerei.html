Citrus × leclerei

Citrus × leclerei, commonly known as the lemon lime.

It is a hybrid citrus fruit that results from the cross between a lemon and a lime, combining the characteristics of both parent species. The plant is a small evergreen tree with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is typically small, round, and has a thin, smooth rind.

The fruit is prized for its acidic juice, which is used in culinary and beverage applications.
