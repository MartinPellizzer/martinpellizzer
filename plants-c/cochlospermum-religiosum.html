Cochlospermum religiosum

Cochlospermum religiosum, commonly known as the sacred tree or red torch tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers and palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces fleshy, red berries that are edible and often consumed during religious festivals. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and showy blooms.

Cochlospermum religiosum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
