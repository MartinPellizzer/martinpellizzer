Cineraria macrophylla

Cineraria macrophylla, commonly known as the giant cineraria, is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
