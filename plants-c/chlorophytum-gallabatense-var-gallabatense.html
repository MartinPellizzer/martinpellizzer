Chlorophytum gallabatense var. gallabatense

Chlorophytum gallabatense var. gallabatense, commonly known as the Gallabatense spider plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
