Cytisus lanigerus var. rigidus

Cytisus lanigerus var. rigidus, commonly known as the rigid broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being narrow and leathery. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This plant is known for its hardiness and ability to colonize disturbed sites.
