Cassia chartacea

Cassia chartacea, commonly known as the paper cassia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has feathery, bipinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shady environments.

Cassia chartacea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
