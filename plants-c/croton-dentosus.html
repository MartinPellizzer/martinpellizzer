Croton dentosus

Croton dentosus, commonly known as the toothed croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often bright green with a metallic sheen. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically yellow or green, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Croton dentosus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
