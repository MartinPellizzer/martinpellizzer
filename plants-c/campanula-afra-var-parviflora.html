Campanula afra var. parviflora

Campanula afra var. parviflora, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
