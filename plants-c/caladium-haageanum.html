Caladium haageanum

Caladium haageanum, commonly known as the Angel Wings caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and green. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Caladium haageanum is typically grown as an annual in cooler climates due to its sensitivity to frost.
