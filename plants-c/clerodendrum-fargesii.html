Clerodendrum fargesii

Clerodendrum fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, clustered fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with a strong aromatic scent.

Clerodendrum fargesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
