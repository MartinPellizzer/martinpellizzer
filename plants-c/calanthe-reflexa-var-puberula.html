Calanthe reflexa var. puberula

Calanthe reflexa var. puberula, commonly known as the hairy reflexed calanthe, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports a pair of opposite leaves. The plant produces strikingly elegant flowers with pale green to white petals and a distinctive, reflexed lip. Its foliage is covered in fine, soft pubescence, giving it its common name.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
