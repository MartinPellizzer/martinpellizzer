Cassia floribunda

Cassia floribunda, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces long, narrow pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
