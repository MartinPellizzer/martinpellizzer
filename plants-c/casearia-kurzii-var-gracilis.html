Casearia kurzii var. gracilis

Casearia kurzii var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender casearia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, elliptical leaves with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, orange-red fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a compact, drought-tolerant shrub.
