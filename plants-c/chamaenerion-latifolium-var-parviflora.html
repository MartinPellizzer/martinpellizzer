Chamaenerion latifolium var. parviflora

Chamaenerion latifolium var. parviflora, commonly known as narrowleaf fireweed, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as tundra and wet meadows. The plant features slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink to reddish in color.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing nectar for pollinators in its native environment.
