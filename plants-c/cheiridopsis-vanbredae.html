Cheiridopsis vanbredae

Cheiridopsis vanbredae, commonly known as the Vanbrederode's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated in regions with Mediterranean climates.
