Chamaeleon macrophyllus var. incanus

Chamaeleon macrophyllus var. incanus, commonly known as the silver-leafed chamaeleon, is a rare and striking plant characterized by its large, waxy leaves that reflect light, giving it a silvery appearance.

The plant's foliage is often covered in fine, white hairs, which contribute to its unique visual texture.
