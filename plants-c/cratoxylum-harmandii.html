Cratoxylum harmandii

Cratoxylum harmandii, commonly known as the Harmand's cratoxylum, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
