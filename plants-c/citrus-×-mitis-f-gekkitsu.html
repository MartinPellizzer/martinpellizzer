Citrus × mitis f. gekkitsu

Citrus × mitis f. gekkitsu, commonly known as the Japanese lime or Yuzu, is a hybrid citrus plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped fruits with a thick, aromatic rind and a juicy, tangy pulp. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is widely used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its unique flavor and essential oils.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various climates.
