Cyrtanthus salmonoides

Cyrtanthus salmonoides, commonly known as the salmon bell orchid, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, salmon-pink to orange-red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cyrtanthus salmonoides is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
