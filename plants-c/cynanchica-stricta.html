Cynanchica stricta

Cynanchica stricta, commonly known as the rigid milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Cynanchica stricta is known for its milky sap, which can be toxic to some animals and humans.
