Cirsium × smirnovii

Cirsium × smirnovii, commonly known as the Smirnov's thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lance-shaped leaves and purple to pink flower heads. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it adapts to cold and harsh environmental conditions.

Its morphology reflects evolutionary adaptations to its native environment, making it a notable species in botanical studies.
