Crassula grisea var. bakeri

Crassula grisea var. bakeri, commonly known as the Baker's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
