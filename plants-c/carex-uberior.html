Carex uberior

Carex uberior, commonly known as glossy sedge, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. This plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a glossy green appearance, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early spring.

Carex uberior is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
