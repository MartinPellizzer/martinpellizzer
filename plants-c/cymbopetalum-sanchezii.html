Cymbopetalum sanchezii

Cymbopetalum sanchezii, commonly known as the pink-flowered candelabra plant, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, cascading clusters of pink, bell-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, leathery leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbopetalum sanchezii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
