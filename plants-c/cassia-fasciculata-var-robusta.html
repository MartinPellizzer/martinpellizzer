Cassia fasciculata var. robusta

Cassia fasciculata var. robusta, commonly known as the robust cassia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets that are often arranged in pairs. The plant produces bright yellow, clustered flowers that bloom in summer, followed by cylindrical pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for hedging and landscape design.
