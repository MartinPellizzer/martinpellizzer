Cacalia Cincta

Cacalia cincta is a delicate flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It is known for its small, daisy-like blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Despite its modest size, Cacalia cincta holds cultural significance in traditional herbal medicine.
