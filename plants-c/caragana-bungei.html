Caragana bungei

Caragana bungei, commonly known as the Chinese pea shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces yellow flowers in early summer. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

Its root system helps prevent soil erosion, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
