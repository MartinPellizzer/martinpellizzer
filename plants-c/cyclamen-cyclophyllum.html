Cyclamen cyclophyllum

Cyclamen cyclophyllum, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features distinctive heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its blooming period typically occurs in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Cyclamen cyclophyllum is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
