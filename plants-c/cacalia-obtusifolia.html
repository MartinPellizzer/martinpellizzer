Cacalia obtusifolia

Cacalia obtusifolia, commonly known as the bluntleaf cacalia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features basal leaves that are broad and rounded, with a rough texture. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, producing small, white or pale yellow flowers.
