Cyrtopera plantaginea

Cyrtopera plantaginea, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and daisy-like white flowers with a yellow center. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
