Crinum himalense

Crinum himalense, commonly known as the Himalayan crinum, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas.

It is a bulbous plant that produces large, showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The leaves are broad, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
