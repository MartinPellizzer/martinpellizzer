Cypholophus brunneolus

Cypholophus brunneolus, commonly known as the brown-spored burrweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

Cypholophus brunneolus is often found in arid and semi-arid environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
