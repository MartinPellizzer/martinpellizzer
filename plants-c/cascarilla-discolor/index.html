Cascarilla Discolor

Cascarilla discolor is a lesser-known but fascinating plant native to parts of Central and South America. It is known for its unique appearance and medicinal properties that have been used by indigenous communities for centuries. The plant's name translates to "discolored" due to its distinctive bark and leaves that change color with age. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in humid, shaded environments. Despite its potential, it remains under-researched and overlooked in modern botanical studies.
