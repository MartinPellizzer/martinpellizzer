Croton erythrostachys var. erythrostachys

Croton erythrostachys var. erythrostachys, commonly known as the red-stemmed croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and large, palmate leaves with a deep green color. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers arranged in panicle inflorescences, which are followed by woody, spiny capsules containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
