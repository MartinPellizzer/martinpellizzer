Celosia populifolia var. artemisioides

Celosia populifolia var. artemisioides, commonly known as the fernleaf celosia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features feathery, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive, delicate appearance. The plant produces vibrant, plume-like inflorescences in shades of red, pink, and orange, making it popular in gardens and floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a versatile choice for both landscape design and cut flower production.
