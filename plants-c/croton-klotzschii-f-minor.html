Croton klotzschii f. minor

Croton klotzschii f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, palmate leaves with vibrant colors ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
