Cyrtochilum portillae

The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and produces intricate, fragrant blooms that are often clustered near the base of the stem. Cyrtochilum portillae is adapted to humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to thrive, making it a challenging species to cultivate.
