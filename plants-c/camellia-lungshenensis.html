Camellia lungshenensis

Camellia lungshenensis, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white, often with a distinctive yellow or orange center. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture and arranged alternately along the stems. Camellia lungshenensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter blooming period.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for temperate climates.
