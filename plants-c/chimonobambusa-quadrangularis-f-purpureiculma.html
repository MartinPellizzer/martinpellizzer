Chimonobambusa quadrangularis f. purpureiculma

Chimonobambusa quadrangularis f. purpureiculma, commonly known as the purple-leafed bamboo, is a unique variant of the quadrangular bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its green stems. This plant is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and the plant exhibits a clumping growth habit.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic qualities.
