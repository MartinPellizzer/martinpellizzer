Camphusia glabra var. kauaiensis

Camphusia glabra var. kauaiensis, commonly known as Kauai camphorweed, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features smooth, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
