Ctenodon rosei

Ctenodon rosei, commonly known as the rose spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its succulent, often hairy stems and leaves that are typically green to reddish in color. It is native to parts of Africa and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments.
