Cypripedium insigne var. rubrum-maculatum

Cypripedium insigne var. rubrum-maculatum, commonly known as the red-spotted lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive red and purple coloration, often featuring prominent spots or maculations. The plant has a pseudobulb structure and produces a single, intricate flower with a lip that is typically a different color from the rest of the flower. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade and well-drained soil.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
