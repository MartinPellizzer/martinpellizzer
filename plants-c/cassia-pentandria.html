Cassia pentandria

Cassia pentandria, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated pods that split open to release the seeds.

Cassia pentandria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
