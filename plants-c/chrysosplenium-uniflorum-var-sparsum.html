Chrysosplenium uniflorum var. sparsum

Chrysosplenium uniflorum var. sparsum, commonly known as the slender chrysosplenium, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, typically in clusters along the stems. The leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and are arranged alternately along the stem. This species prefers moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
