Chlora dodecandra

Chlora dodecandra, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds similar to those of the common dandelion.

Chlora dodecandra is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
