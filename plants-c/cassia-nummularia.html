Cassia nummularia

Cassia nummularia, commonly known as golden mohair, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically round or oval and are covered in a fine, silky hair.

Cassia nummularia is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique texture and drought tolerance.
