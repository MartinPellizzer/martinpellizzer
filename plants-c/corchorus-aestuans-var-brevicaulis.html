Corchorus aestuans var. brevicaulis

Corchorus aestuans var. brevicaulis, commonly known as the short-stemmed dock, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, slender stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
