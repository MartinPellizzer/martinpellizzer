Carduus × loretii

Carduus × loretii, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a composite plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Carduus benedictus (blessed thistle) and Carduus nutans (Goat's beard). The plant typically features spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads that resemble those of its parent species. It is often found in disturbed soils and open areas, thriving in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
