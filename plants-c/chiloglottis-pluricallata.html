Chiloglottis pluricallata

Chiloglottis pluricallata, commonly known as the variable lip orchid, is an epiphytic or lithophytic orchid native to eastern Australia.

It features a compact, sympodial growth habit with fleshy, terete pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves. The plant produces pendulous flowers in shades of yellow to orange, with a distinctive lip that is often notched or lobed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Chiloglottis pluricallata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
