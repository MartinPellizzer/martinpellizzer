Croton tenuicuspis

Croton tenuicuspis, commonly known as the narrow-cusped croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. Croton tenuicuspis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
