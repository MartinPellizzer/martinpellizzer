Carduus nutans subsp. micropterus

Carduus nutans subsp.

micropterus, commonly known as the lesser hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, often with a bluish-gray hue. This subspecies is typically found in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides and in meadows.

It is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
