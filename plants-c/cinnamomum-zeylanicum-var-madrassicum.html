Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. madrassicum

Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. madrassicum, commonly known as Ceylon cinnamon, is a variety of the cinnamon tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is harvested and dried to produce the spice cinnamon. The plant belongs to the Lauraceae family and is known for its rich essential oils and phytochemical compounds. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, with a strong, spicy scent.

This variety is prized for both its culinary and medicinal uses.
