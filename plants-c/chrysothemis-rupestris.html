Chrysothemis rupestris

Chrysothemis rupestris, commonly known as the rock chrysothemis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to its arid habitat. Its leaves are fleshy and often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Chrysothemis rupestris is well adapted to dry conditions, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
