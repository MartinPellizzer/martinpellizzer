Centropogon guatemalensis

Centropogon guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, colorful blooms in shades of yellow and orange, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
