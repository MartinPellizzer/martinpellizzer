Calamus kunstleri subsp. tiomanensis

Calamus kunstleri subsp.

tiomanensis, commonly known as the Tioman calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae. It is characterized by its tall, slender rhizomes and long, narrow leaves that are often arched or curved.

The plant produces compact, spadix-like inflorescences with small, greenish flowers. It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
