Cattleya Dormaniana

Cattleya dormaniana is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors including pink, red, and white. This species is highly valued in the horticultural trade for its beauty and fragrance. It requires specific growing conditions to thrive, including bright light and well-draining soil. Despite its popularity, it remains a relatively rare and delicate plant in cultivation.
