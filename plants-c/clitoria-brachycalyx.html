Clitoria brachycalyx

Clitoria brachycalyx, commonly known as the short-calyx butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant produces vibrant purple or blue flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and slightly hairy.

The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
