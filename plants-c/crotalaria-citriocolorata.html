Crotalaria citriocolorata

Crotalaria citriocolorata, commonly known as the Citriocolora Crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers. The plant has palmately compound leaves with three to five leaflets. It produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, typical of the genus Crotalaria.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in certain cultures.
