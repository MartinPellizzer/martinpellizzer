Canna lambertii

Canna lambertii, commonly known as the red bamboo canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features tall, grass-like leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in height, with a distinctive red stalk that becomes more vibrant as it matures. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer, adding dramatic visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Canna lambertii is often used in landscaping for its bold foliage and long-lasting floral display.
