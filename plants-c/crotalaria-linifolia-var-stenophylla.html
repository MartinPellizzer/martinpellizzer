Crotalaria linifolia var. stenophylla

Crotalaria linifolia var. stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and yellow, spurred flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It has small, elliptical pods that contain several seeds, which are sometimes used as a food source.

The species is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in agricultural settings.
