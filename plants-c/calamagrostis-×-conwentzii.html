Calamagrostis × conwentzii

Calamagrostis × conwentzii, commonly known as the Chinese silvergrass, is a hybrid grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, silver-tipped plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes, especially in water features.

It is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
