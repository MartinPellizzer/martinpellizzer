Crotalaria shevaroyensis

Crotalaria shevaroyensis, commonly known as the Shevaroy hill crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its erect, branching stems and alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces clusters of yellow, pea-like flowers, which are typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open forests.
