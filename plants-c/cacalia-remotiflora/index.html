Cacalia Remotiflora

Cacalia remotiflora is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its delicate, daisy-like blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its slender stems and pale yellow flowers make it a unique addition to any garden or natural setting. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and potential uses of Cacalia remotiflora.
