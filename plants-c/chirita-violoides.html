Chirita violoides

Chirita violoides, commonly known as the violet leaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display variegation. Its foliage and blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental plants with unique leaf patterns.
