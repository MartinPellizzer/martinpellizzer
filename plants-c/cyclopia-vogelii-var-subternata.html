Cyclopia vogelii var. subternata

Cyclopia vogelii var. subternata, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow petals and a central disk. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Cyclopia vogelii var. subternata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
