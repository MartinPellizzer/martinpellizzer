Conophytum angustum

Conophytum angustum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy stems and small, triangular leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
