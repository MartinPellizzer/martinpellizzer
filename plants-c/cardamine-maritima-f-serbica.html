Cardamine maritima f. serbica

Cardamine maritima f. serbica, commonly known as the Serbica watercress, is a rare and specialized plant species adapted to coastal and saline environments.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and flies. It thrives in moist, sandy soils along shorelines and is often found in areas with high salinity.
