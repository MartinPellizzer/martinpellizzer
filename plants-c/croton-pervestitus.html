Croton pervestitus

Croton pervestitus, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that range in color from green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Croton pervestitus is valued for its ornamental leaves and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
