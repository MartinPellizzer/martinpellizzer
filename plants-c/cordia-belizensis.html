Cordia belizensis

Cordia belizensis, commonly known as the Belize cordia, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
