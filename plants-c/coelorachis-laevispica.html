Coelorachis laevispica

Coelorachis laevispica, commonly known as the smooth-spurred orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
