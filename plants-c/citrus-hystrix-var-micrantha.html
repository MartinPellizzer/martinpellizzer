Citrus hystrix var. micrantha

Citrus hystrix var. micrantha, commonly known as the dwarf citron or Mexican lime, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features small, fragrant, yellow-orange fruits that are typically harvested when they are still green, though they can ripen to a golden hue. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the use of its fruit in culinary and medicinal applications.

The plant is well-suited to tropical and subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.
