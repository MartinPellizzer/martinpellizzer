Caprifolium loureiroi

Caprifolium loureiroi, commonly known as the Brazilian honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
