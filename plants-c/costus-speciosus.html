Costus speciosus

Costus speciosus, commonly known as the sacred lotus or red ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces vibrant red or orange flowers with a distinctive, fragrant aroma. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

Costus speciosus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
