Campylotropis Macrocarpa Var. Hupehensis

Campylotropis macrocarpa var. hupehensis is a lesser-known but strikingly beautiful plant native to parts of eastern Asia. This variant of the genus Campylotropis is characterized by its unique foliage and delicate flower structure. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. The plant's leaves are broad and heart-shaped, adding a lush texture to its natural habitat. Despite its ornamental value, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles.
