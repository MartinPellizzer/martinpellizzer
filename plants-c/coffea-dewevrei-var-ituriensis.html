Coffea dewevrei var. ituriensis

Coffea dewevrei var. ituriensis, commonly known as the Ituri coffee.

This variant is native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped red berries. The plant produces a unique aroma and flavor profile, often described as earthy and slightly tart. It thrives in the humid, shaded understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
