Conyza capillipes

Conyza capillipes, commonly known as the slender weed or goatweed, is an annual flowering plant native to Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, hairy leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, producing small seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Conyza capillipes is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
