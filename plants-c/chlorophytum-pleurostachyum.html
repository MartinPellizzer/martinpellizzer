Chlorophytum pleurostachyum

Chlorophytum pleurostachyum, commonly known as the pleurostachyum spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by narrow, sword-shaped berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it easy to grow and maintain.
