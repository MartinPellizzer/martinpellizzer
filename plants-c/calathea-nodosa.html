Calathea nodosa

Calathea nodosa, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often display patterns of silver, green, and pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, which enhances their visual appeal. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
