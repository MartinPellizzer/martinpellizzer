Coelogyne elegantula

Coelogyne elegantula, commonly known as the Elegant Lady's Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with broad, leathery leaves that store water, adapting it to its seasonal habitat. Its inflorescence typically produces multiple flowers arranged in a raceme, enhancing its ornamental value.

Coelogyne elegantula thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
