Colchicum mirzoevae

Colchicum mirzoevae, commonly known as the Mirzoeva's colchicum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Colchicaceae family.

It is native to specific regions in the Caucasus, particularly in Georgia, where it thrives in mountainous environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically purple or violet in color, with a distinctive spurred shape. It is a perennial herb that prefers well-drained soils and partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
