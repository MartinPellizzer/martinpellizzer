Covellia webbiana

Covellia webbiana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. Covellia webbiana is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine and horticultural practices.
