Coelogyne Jiewhoei

Coelogyne jiewhoei is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This species is known for its vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in a unique spiral pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered a conservation concern. This article explores the characteristics, cultivation, and significance of Coelogyne jiewhoei.
