Cyathea lindsayana

Cyathea lindsayana, commonly known as Lindsay's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, pinnate fronds. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky outcrops. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth.

Cyathea lindsayana plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
