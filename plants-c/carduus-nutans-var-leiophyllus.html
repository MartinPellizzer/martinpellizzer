Carduus nutans var. leiophyllus

Carduus nutans var. leiophyllus, commonly known as the smooth milk thistle, is a variant of the common milk thistle.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features a tall, spiny stem with large, lobed leaves that are less hairy compared to the typical variety. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months.

The plant is characterized by its milky sap, which is a distinguishing feature of the genus Carduus.
