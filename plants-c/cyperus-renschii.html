Cyperus renschii

Cyperus renschii, commonly known as the narrowleaf paperplant, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa, including Kenya and Tanzania, and thrives in wetland environments. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green or reddish in color. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its elegant appearance and ability to tolerate moist soil conditions.

Cyperus renschii is also valued for its role in ecological systems, providing habitat for various small aquatic organisms.
