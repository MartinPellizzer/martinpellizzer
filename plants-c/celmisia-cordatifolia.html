Celmisia cordatifolia

Celmisia cordatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a valued species in New Zealand's flora.
