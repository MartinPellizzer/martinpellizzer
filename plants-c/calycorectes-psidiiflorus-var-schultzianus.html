Calycorectes psidiiflorus var. schultzianus

Calycorectes psidiiflorus var. schultzianus, commonly known as the Schultze's holly, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree with dense, leathery foliage. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a glossy dark green surface and a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red or orange berries.
