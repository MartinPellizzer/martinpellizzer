Crucifera tristis

Crucifera tristis, commonly known as the sad crucifer, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pinnately compound leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is found in various parts of Europe and Asia. It has small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and often overlooked species within its native range.
