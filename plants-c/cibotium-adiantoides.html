Cibotium adiantoides

Cibotium adiantoides, commonly known as the golden tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a dense layer of fibrous, dark brown roots. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that are typically bright green with a glossy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary rainforests.

Cibotium adiantoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
