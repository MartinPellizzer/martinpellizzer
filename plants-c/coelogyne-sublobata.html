Coelogyne sublobata

Coelogyne sublobata, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces fragrant, butterfly-shaped blooms that are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne sublobata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
