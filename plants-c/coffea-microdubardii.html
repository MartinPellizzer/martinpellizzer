Coffea microdubardii

Coffea microdubardii, commonly known as the Dubard's coffee.

It is a rare and lesser-known species of coffee native to Madagascar. This plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped berries and dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and low yield, it is not commonly cultivated or commercially available.
