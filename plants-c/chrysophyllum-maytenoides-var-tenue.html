Chrysophyllum maytenoides var. tenue

Chrysophyllum maytenoides var. tenue, commonly known as the slender star apple, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in tropical gardens and used in traditional medicine.
