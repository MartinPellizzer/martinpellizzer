Chisocheton celebicus

Chisocheton celebicus, commonly known as the Celebes chisocheton, is a tropical tree native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers followed by orange-red berries containing several seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
