Cleome viminea

Cleome viminea, commonly known as the slender cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Cleome viminea thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance.
