Caiophora andina

Caiophora andina, commonly known as the Andean caiophora, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. Due to its unique habitat and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
