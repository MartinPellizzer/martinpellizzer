Croton plicatus

Croton plicatus, commonly known as the zigzag croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, deeply lobed leaves that often display a vibrant mix of green, red, and yellow hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton plicatus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
