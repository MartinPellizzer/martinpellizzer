Crotalaria adamsonii

Crotalaria adamsonii, commonly known as the Adamson's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Crotalaria adamsonii is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
