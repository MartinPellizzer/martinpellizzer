Calytrix tenuifolia

Calytrix tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

Calytrix tenuifolia is also valued for its ability to stabilize dunes and prevent erosion.
