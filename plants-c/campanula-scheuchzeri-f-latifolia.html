Campanula scheuchzeri f. latifolia

Campanula scheuchzeri f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved bellflower, is a perennial plant native to the high altitudes of the Alps.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often broader than those of the typical species, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and alpine settings.
