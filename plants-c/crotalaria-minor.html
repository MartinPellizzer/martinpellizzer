Crotalaria minor

Crotalaria minor, commonly known as the lesser crotalaria, is a small annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with a spurred petal. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.

Crotalaria minor is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
