Columnea hirsuta

Columnea hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy flame plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Columnea hirsuta prefers well-drained soil and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
