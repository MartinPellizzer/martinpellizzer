Cheiranthus Capitatus

Cheiranthus capitatus is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant purple blooms and compact growth habit. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This species is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with similar characteristics. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
