Convolvulus Valentinus Var. Suffruticosus

Convolvulus valentinus var. suffruticosus is a unique and charming flowering plant that captivates with its delicate, trumpet-shaped blooms. Native to southern Africa, this variety thrives in warm, sunny climates and is known for its vibrant pink to purple hues. It is a versatile plant that can be grown in gardens or as a potted specimen, making it a popular choice among horticultural enthusiasts. Its trailing habit and long flowering season make it an excellent addition to borders, hanging baskets, and rock gardens. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and regular watering to flourish optimally.
