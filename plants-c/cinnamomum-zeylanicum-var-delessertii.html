Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. delessertii

Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. delessertii, commonly known as the Ceylon cinnamon or Sri Lankan cinnamon, is a variant of the cinnamon tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-like bark, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by clustered berries. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique chemical composition, including essential oils and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
