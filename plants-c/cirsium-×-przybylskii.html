Cirsium × przybylskii

Cirsium × przybylskii, commonly known as the Przybylski thistle, is a hybrid plant species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and large, showy purple to violet flower heads. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant exhibits robust growth and is often found in alpine meadows and dry slopes.

Its morphology includes basal leaves that are typically lobed or toothed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
