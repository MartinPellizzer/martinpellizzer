Cyclamen orbiculatum

Cyclamen orbiculatum, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Persia and the Caucasus.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces vibrant, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Cyclamen orbiculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
