Combretum erythrophloeum

Combretum erythrophloeum, commonly known as redwood combretum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-barked trunk and opposite, ovate leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
