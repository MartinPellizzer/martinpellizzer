Centaurea tweediei

Centaurea tweediei, commonly known as Tweedie's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its bright blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
