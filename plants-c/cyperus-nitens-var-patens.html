Cyperus nitens var. patens

Cyperus nitens var. patens, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies, preferring moist to wet soil conditions. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often green or reddish in color. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

This species is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in aquatic environments.
