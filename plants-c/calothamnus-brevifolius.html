Calothamnus brevifolius

Calothamnus brevifolius, commonly known as the short-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bottlebrush-shaped flowers, which are typically red or orange in color. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in sandy or gravelly habitats.

Calothamnus brevifolius is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display and drought tolerance.
