Connarus ruber var. sprucei

Connarus ruber var. sprucei, commonly known as the red connarus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are thick and leathery, often with a reddish tinge when young.
