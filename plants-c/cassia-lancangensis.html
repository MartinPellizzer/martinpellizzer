Cassia lancangensis

Cassia lancangensis, commonly known as the Lancang cassia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like China, Thailand, and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Cassia lancangensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its seeds are sometimes used as a spice, similar to other cassia species.
