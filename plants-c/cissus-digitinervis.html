Cissus digitinervis

Cissus digitinervis, commonly known as the boneleaf or climbing fig.

It is a perennial climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive network of veins, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Cissus digitinervis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
