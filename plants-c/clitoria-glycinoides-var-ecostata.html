Clitoria glycinoides var. ecostata

Clitoria glycinoides var. ecostata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like, with compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
