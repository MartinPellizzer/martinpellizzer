Coleus floribundus var. longipes

Coleus floribundus var. longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed coleus, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often features shades of red, purple, and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in gardens and as a potted plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
