Citharexylum flexuosum

Citharexylum flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters, with a dense, often thorny structure. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and clustered in terminal or axillary inflorescences, often in shades of white or pink.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
