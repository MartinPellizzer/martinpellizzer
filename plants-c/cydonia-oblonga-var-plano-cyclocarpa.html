Cydonia oblonga var. plano-cyclocarpa

Cydonia oblonga var. plano-cyclocarpa, commonly known as the flat-ribbed quince, is a variant of the quince species native to southern Europe and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its flattened, ribbed fruit that matures to a yellowish-orange color. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by the distinctive, aromatic fruit. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a velvety texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique fruit, which is used in cooking and traditional medicine.
