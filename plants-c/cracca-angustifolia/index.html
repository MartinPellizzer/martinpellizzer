Cracca Angustifolia

Cracca angustifolia is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky and sunny environments. This species is often found in alpine meadows and open woodlands where it competes with other hardy flora. Its slender leaves and showy blooms make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Due to habitat loss, Cracca angustifolia is now considered endangered in many parts of its range.
