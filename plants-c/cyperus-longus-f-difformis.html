Cyperus longus f. difformis

Cyperus longus f. difformis, commonly known as the variable flatsedge, is a variant of the flatsedge genus Cyperus.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its grass-like appearance and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, cylindrical cluster of spikelets, which are often subtended by bracts.

This plant is notable for its adaptability to various aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
