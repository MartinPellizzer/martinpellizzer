Codiaeum variegatum f. medium

Codiaeum variegatum f. medium, commonly known as the "Marble Queen" or "Variegated Croton," is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that display a striking combination of creamy white, yellow, and green hues. The plant is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is typically grown as a houseplant due to its sensitivity to cold temperatures. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its vibrant foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
