Combretum stenopetalum

Combretum stenopetalum, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often pubescent, and its bark is typically smooth with a reddish or brownish hue. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
