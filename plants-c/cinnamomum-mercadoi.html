Cinnamomum mercadoi

Cinnamomum mercadoi, commonly known as the Philippine cinnamon tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by oval, dark blue berries. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their aromatic properties.

The species is valued for its unique fragrance and is often cultivated for commercial purposes.
