Cyclopia grandiflora

Cyclopia grandiflora, commonly known as the greater cyclopia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Cyclopia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional medicinal uses.
