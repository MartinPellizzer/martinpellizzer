Caladium bicolor var. vellozianum

Caladium bicolor var. vellozianum, commonly known as the Brazilian shield fern, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with red, white, and green patterns. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is the main ornamental feature, making it popular as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

It has a rhizomatous growth habit and can spread through runners, forming dense clumps over time.
