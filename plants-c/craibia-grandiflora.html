Craibia grandiflora

Craibia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered craibia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Craibia grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.
