Corchorus linearis

Corchorus linearis, commonly known as common jute, is a flowering plant native to Asia and parts of Africa.

It is a member of the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its linear, grass-like leaves and tall, branched stems. The plant thrives in moist, tropical climates and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
