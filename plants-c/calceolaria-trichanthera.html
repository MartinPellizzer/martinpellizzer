Calceolaria trichanthera

Calceolaria trichanthera, commonly known as the velvetleaf calceolaria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, pouch-like flowers that are often brightly colored. Calceolaria trichanthera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
