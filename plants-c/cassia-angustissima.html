Cassia angustissima

Cassia angustissima, commonly known as narrowleaf senna, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are enclosed in long, slender pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

Cassia angustissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
