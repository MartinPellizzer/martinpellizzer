Catasetum floribundum

Catasetum floribundum, commonly known as the orchid with abundant flowers, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or purple markings. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of numerous small, clustered blooms.

The leaves are usually broad and elliptical, often with a waxy coating that helps retain moisture in its natural habitat.
