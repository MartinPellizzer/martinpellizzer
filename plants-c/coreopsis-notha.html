Coreopsis notha

Coreopsis notha, commonly known as the narrowleaf coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow to orange flowers in clusters. It blooms from late spring to early summer and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Coreopsis notha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
