Chrysanthemum jugorum var. tanacetopsis

Chrysanthemum jugorum var. tanacetopsis, commonly known as the slender chrysanthemum, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features finely divided, feathery leaves that are gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pale green, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its aromatic foliage and delicate blooms make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and medicinal gardens.
