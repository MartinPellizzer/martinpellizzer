Camellia × williamsii

Camellia × williamsii, commonly known as the Williams camellia, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, camellia-like flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a contrasting eye. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden ornamental planting.

Camellia × williamsii is also valued for its ability to bloom reliably in winter and early spring.
