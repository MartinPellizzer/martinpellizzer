Calochortus nitidus

Calochortus nitidus, commonly known as the shining mariposa lily, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Calochortus.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada. This plant typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, such as grasslands and slopes. It produces showy, cup-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, with a yellow center.

The plant is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
