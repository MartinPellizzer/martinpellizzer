Cephalomanes densinervium

Cephalomanes densinervium, commonly known as the dense-veined headman, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, moist soil of its habitat.
