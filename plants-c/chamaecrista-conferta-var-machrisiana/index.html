Chamaecrista Conferta Var. Machrisiana

Chamaecrista conferta var. machrisiana is a unique legume native to the southeastern United States. This plant is known for its striking, fern-like foliage and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks. Its adaptability makes it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and restoration projects. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively overlooked in mainstream horticulture.
