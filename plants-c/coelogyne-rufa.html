Coelogyne rufa

Coelogyne rufa, commonly known as the red lady orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and yellow flowers, which bloom during the dry season. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Coelogyne rufa is often cultivated for its beauty and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
