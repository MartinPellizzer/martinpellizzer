Convolvulus lacunosus

Convolvulus lacunosus, commonly known as the zigzag morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in the morning, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Convolvulus lacunosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
