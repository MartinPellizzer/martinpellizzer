Clematis ixyushuensis

Clematis ixyushuensis, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its roots are fibrous and can become invasive if not properly managed.

Clematis ixyushuensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
