Camphorata ovata

Camphorata ovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed camphor tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its wood is highly valued for its aromatic properties and durability.

Camphorata ovata is also known for its medicinal uses, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
