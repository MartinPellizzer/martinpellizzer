Cranichis luteola

Cranichis luteola, commonly known as the yellow lady's-turban, is a striking orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns resembling turbans, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
