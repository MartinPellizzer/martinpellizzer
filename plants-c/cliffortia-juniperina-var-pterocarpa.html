Cliffortia juniperina var. pterocarpa

Cliffortia juniperina var. pterocarpa, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense, bushy appearance.

The variety pterocarpa is distinguished by its unique seed pod structure, which is elongated and winged, aiding in wind dispersal.
