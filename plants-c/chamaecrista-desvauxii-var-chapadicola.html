Chamaecrista desvauxii var. chapadicola

Chamaecrista desvauxii var. chapadicola, commonly known as the Brazilian burr legume, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
