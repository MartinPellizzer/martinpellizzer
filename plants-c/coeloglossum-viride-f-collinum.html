Coeloglossum viride f. collinum

Coeloglossum viride f. collinum, commonly known as the green hawksbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flower heads that are composed of small, tubular florets. It typically thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Coeloglossum viride f. collinum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
