Coleus scutellarioides

Coleus scutellarioides, commonly known as the scarlet sage, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are often mistaken for flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers well-draining soil.

Coleus scutellarioides is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking foliage and ability to add color to shady areas.
