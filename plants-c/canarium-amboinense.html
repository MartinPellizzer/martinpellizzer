Canarium amboinense

Canarium amboinense, commonly known as the Borneo nut or Ambon nut, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, opposite leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by round, woody fruits containing a single seed. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitats.
