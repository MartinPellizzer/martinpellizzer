Calanthe Longicornu

Calanthe longicornu is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Its unique appearance features long, slender petals that resemble horns, giving it its intriguing name. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and is often difficult to cultivate outside its natural habitat. Due to its rarity and ecological significance, conservation efforts are essential to protect this delicate species.
