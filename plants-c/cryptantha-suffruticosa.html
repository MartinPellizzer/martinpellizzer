Cryptantha suffruticosa

Cryptantha suffruticosa, commonly known as the desert flax, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the spring, and its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Cryptantha suffruticosa is often found in sandy soils and is well-suited to withstand drought and extreme temperatures.
