Cyperus semitribrachiatus

Cyperus semitribrachiatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, three-lobed flowers, which are typically pale green to brown in color.
