Cola bipindensis

It is native to tropical West Africa, where it grows in humid lowland forests. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The botanical features of Cola bipindensis make it an important species in both ecological and cultural contexts.
