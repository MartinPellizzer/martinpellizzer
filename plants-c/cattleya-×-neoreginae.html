Cattleya × neoreginae

Cattleya × neoreginae, commonly known as the Neoregina Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elliptical and slightly fleshy, adapting to its humid, shaded habitat.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, fragrant blooms.
