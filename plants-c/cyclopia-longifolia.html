Cyclopia longifolia

Cyclopia longifolia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Cyclopia longifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
