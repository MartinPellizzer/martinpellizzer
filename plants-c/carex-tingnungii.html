Carex tingnungii

Carex tingnungii, commonly known as the Hainan sedge, is a perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant features slender, triangular stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a waxy surface.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
