Cortaderia pungens

Cortaderia pungens, commonly known as the sword fern or pampas grass, is a tall, grass-like plant native to South America.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces plume-like inflorescences that are fluffy and white, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its robust growth and striking foliage make it a notable species in botanical gardens and landscapes.
