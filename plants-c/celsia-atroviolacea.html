Celsia atroviolacea

Celsia atroviolacea, commonly known as the dark violet celsia, is a member of the Rubiaceae family, known for its ornamental value.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features opposite, elliptic leaves with a glossy dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its flowers are small, clustered in cymes, and typically have a dark violet to almost black hue.

Celsia atroviolacea prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden settings.
