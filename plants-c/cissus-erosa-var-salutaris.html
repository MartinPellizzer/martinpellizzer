Cissus erosa var. salutaris

Cissus erosa var. salutaris, commonly known as the sacred grape or prayer plant, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Vitaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb using tendrils, making it suitable for trellises or vertical gardens.
