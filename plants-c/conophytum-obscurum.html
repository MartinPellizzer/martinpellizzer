Conophytum obscurum

Conophytum obscurum, commonly known as the dark conophytum, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, rounded leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
