Cyperus diandrus var. elongatus

Cyperus diandrus var. elongatus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in moist soil conditions. Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system that aids in spreading and stabilizing the soil.

It is often found in wetlands, rice fields, and other aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats.
