Cynanchum ephedroides

Cynanchum ephedroides, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by follicles containing seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Cynanchum ephedroides is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
