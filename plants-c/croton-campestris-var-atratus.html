Croton campestris var. atratus

Croton campestris var. atratus, commonly known as the Black Croton, is a striking plant characterized by its dark, almost black foliage.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically deep green to black in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by unique, spiny seed capsules.
