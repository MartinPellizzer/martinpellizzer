Centaurea ammocyanus

Centaurea ammocyanus, commonly known as the sand centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright blue or purple flowers and spiny, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
