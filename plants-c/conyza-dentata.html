Conyza dentata

Conyza dentata, commonly known as the toothed cudweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas. The plant has small, daisy-like yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Conyza dentata is often found in prairies, roadsides, and fields across its range.
