Coelogyne suaveolens

Coelogyne suaveolens, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant blooms in clusters, often with a single flower per stem, and has a pleasant, spicy scent. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Coelogyne suaveolens is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and aromatic qualities.
