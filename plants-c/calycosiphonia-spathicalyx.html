Calycosiphonia spathicalyx

Calycosiphonia spathicalyx, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that bear showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
