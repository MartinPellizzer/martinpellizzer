Colchicum palaestinum

Colchicum palaestinum, commonly known as the Palestine colchicum, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid soils. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and often withering before flowering.

Colchicum palaestinum is part of the Colchicaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
