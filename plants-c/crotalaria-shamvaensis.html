Crotalaria shamvaensis

Crotalaria shamvaensis, commonly known as the Shamva crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, which enriches the soil.

Crotalaria shamvaensis is also noted for its medicinal properties, used traditionally in some African communities for various health purposes.
