Camellia litchi

Camellia litchi, commonly known as the Chinese lantern or litchi, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are glossy and elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, camellia-like flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its fruit, the litchi, is a tropical fruit with a rough, red outer shell and a sweet, white, juicy interior.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its edible fruit.
