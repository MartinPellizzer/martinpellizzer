Clematis loureiroana var. subpeltata

Clematis loureiroana var. subpeltata, commonly known as the silverleaf clematis, is a deciduous vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, silver-mottled leaves that provide a striking visual contrast. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and biodiversity.
