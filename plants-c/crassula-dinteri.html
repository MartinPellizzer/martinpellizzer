Crassula dinteri

Crassula dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
