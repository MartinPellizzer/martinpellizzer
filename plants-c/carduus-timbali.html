Carduus timbali

Carduus timbali, commonly known as the Timbali thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, and the flowers are arranged in a composite head.

Carduus timbali is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
