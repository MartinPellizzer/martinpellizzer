Camax fraxinea

Camax fraxinea, commonly known as the silky fraxin, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves that give it a soft, silky appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, which bloom in the spring. Camax fraxinea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
