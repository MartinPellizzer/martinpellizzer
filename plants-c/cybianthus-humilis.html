Cybianthus humilis

Cybianthus humilis, commonly known as the lowland sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing pink to purple flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Cybianthus humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
