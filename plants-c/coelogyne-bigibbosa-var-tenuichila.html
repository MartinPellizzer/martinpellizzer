Coelogyne bigibbosa var. tenuichila

Coelogyne bigibbosa var. tenuichila, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red-brown markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in fluctuating conditions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
