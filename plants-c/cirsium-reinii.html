Cirsium reinii

Cirsium reinii, commonly known as the Rein's thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland edges. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cirsium reinii is a hardy species that can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
