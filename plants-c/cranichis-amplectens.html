Cranichis amplectens

Cranichis amplectens, commonly known as the embracing orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a complex root system and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically pink or purple and have a distinctive, intricate structure.

It is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific ecological conditions for successful growth.
