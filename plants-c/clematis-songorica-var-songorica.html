Clematis songorica var. songorica

Clematis songorica var. songorica, commonly known as the Singapore clematis, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, twining stems that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
