Colchicum Montanum Var. Cupanii

Colchicum montanum var. cupanii is a striking member of the Colchicaceae family known for its elegant, bell-shaped flowers. This variant is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It blooms in late spring, producing vibrant purple or pink petals that stand out against its foliage. The plant is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal. Due to its rarity, it is also of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
