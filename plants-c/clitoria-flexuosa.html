Clitoria flexuosa

Clitoria flexuosa, commonly known as the butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet-blue flowers that resemble butterflies, which are clustered in racemes. The plant is a climbing legume with trifoliate leaves and is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dye. Its flowers are rich in flavonoids and have a unique ability to change color when exposed to acids or bases.

Clitoria flexuosa is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens and landscapes worldwide.
