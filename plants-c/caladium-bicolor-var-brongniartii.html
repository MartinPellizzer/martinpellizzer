Caladium bicolor var. brongniartii

Caladium bicolor var. brongniartii, commonly known as the "Pinstripe" caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with contrasting white veins against a deep green background. This variety thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial to full shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

The leaves are typically used in garden displays during the growing season.
