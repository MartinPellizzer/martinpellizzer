Crateranthus talbotii var. multibracteatus

Crateranthus talbotii var. multibracteatus, commonly known as the multibracteatus variant of the talbotii crateranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
