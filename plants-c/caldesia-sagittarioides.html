Caldesia sagittarioides

Caldesia sagittarioides, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Caldesia sagittarioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in water garden settings.
