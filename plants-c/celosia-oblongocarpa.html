Celosia oblongocarpa

Celosia oblongocarpa, commonly known as the flame flower or red dwarf celosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spiky inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Celosia oblongocarpa is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and striking appearance.
