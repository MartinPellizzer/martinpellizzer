Crinum surinamense

Crinum surinamense, commonly known as the Surinam crinum, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that grow from a bulbous base. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.
