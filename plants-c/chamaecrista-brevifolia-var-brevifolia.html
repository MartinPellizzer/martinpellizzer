Chamaecrista brevifolia var. brevifolia

Chamaecrista brevifolia var. brevifolia, commonly known as the dwarf velvetbean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and clusters of yellow to orange pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, bean-shaped pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses, it is cultivated in some regions for both aesthetic and practical purposes.
