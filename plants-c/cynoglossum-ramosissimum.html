Cynoglossum ramosissimum

Cynoglossum ramosissimum, commonly known as the hairy bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum ramosissimum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
