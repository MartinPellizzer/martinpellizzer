Cyperus olivetorum

Cyperus olivetorum, commonly known as the narrowleaf paperplant, is a member of the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually green or brown in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and colonize new areas effectively.

Cyperus olivetorum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
