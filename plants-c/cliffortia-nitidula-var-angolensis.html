Cliffortia nitidula var. angolensis

Cliffortia nitidula var. angolensis, commonly known as the Angolan silverweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found on coastal cliffs and sandy soils. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging conditions.
