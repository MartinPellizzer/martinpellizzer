Croton asteroides

Croton asteroides, commonly known as the star croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that often display a mix of green, yellow, and red hues. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
