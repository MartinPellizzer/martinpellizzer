Calytrix empetroides

Calytrix empetroides, commonly known as the native bluebell, is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple, blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. It is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Calytrix empetroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in garden landscaping.
