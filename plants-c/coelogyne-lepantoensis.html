Coelogyne lepantoensis

Coelogyne lepantoensis, commonly known as the Lepanto orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators.

Coelogyne lepantoensis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its significance in local horticulture.
