Crassula swaziensis f. argyrophylla

Crassula swaziensis f. argyrophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery coating, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
