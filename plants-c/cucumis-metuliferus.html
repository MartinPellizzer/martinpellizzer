Cucumis metuliferus

Cucumis metuliferus, commonly known as the horned melon, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its distinctive spiky, green, warty fruit. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers and has palmately lobed leaves. The fruit is typically 5 to 10 centimeters in diameter and contains a gelatinous, edible pulp with black seeds.

It is cultivated for its unique appearance and edible fruit, which has a flavor similar to a cucumber.
