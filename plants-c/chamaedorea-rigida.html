Chamaedorea rigida

Chamaedorea rigida, commonly known as the rigid bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, upright stems covered in smooth, dark green leaves that are elongated and lance-shaped. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it ideal for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits.

Chamaedorea rigida is valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to add a touch of greenery to any space.
