Convolvulus obscurus

Convolvulus obscurus, commonly known as the dark convolvulus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Convolvulaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant characterized by its slender, winding stems and small, funnel-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are usually pale to dark purple or brown, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments, making it an adaptable and resilient plant.
