Citrus erythrocarpa

Citrus erythrocarpa, commonly known as the scarlet lime, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its small, round, red-orange fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a fragrant, aromatic scent typical of the citrus family.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the unique flavor of its fruit.
