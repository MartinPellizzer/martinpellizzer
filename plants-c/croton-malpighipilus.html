Croton malpighipilus

Croton malpighipilus, commonly known as the Malpighiopsis, is a species of flowering plant in the Croton family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful leaves that can vary in shape and texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
