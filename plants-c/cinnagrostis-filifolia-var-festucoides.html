Cinnagrostis filifolia var. festucoides

Cinnagrostis filifolia var. festucoides, commonly known as slender false oatgrass, is a perennial grass that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It features fine, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. This variety is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. Its botanical characteristics include a rhizomatous growth habit and narrow, linear leaves.

It is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
