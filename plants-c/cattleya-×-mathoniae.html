Cattleya × mathoniae

Cattleya × mathoniae, commonly known as the Mathonia orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant is an epiphyte, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Cattleya × mathoniae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
