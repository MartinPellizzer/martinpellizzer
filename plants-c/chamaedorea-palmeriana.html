Chamaedorea palmeriana

Chamaedorea palmeriana, commonly known as the Palmer's bamboo palm, is a member of the Arecaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a slow-growing, evergreen plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and feather-like leaves. The plant typically reaches a height of 3 to 5 feet and prefers bright, indirect light. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.

Its foliage provides a lush, tropical appearance, making it a popular choice for interior landscaping.
