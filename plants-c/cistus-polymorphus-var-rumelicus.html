Cistus polymorphus var. rumelicus

Cistus polymorphus var. rumelicus, commonly known as the Rumeli Cistus, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its evergreen nature and drought tolerance make it a hardy addition to Mediterranean gardens.
