Cissus milnei

Cissus milnei, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery-white border. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its stems are succulent and can be used for propagation.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
