Cirsium mohavense

Cirsium mohavense, commonly known as the Mohave thistle, is a perennial plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as sandy soils in desert regions. It has a robust root system that helps it survive in harsh, dry conditions.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to the extreme climate of its native habitat.
