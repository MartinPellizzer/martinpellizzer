Citrus × asahikan

Citrus × asahikan, commonly known as the Asahikan mandarin, is a hybrid citrus plant originating from Japan.

It is characterized by its small, sweet, and seedless fruits that are typically harvested in late winter. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

The Asahikan mandarin is prized for its ornamental value and unique flavor profile.
