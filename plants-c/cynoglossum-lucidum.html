Cynoglossum lucidum

Cynoglossum lucidum, commonly known as the shining heartwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often bright green. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in late summer, which are arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Cynoglossum lucidum is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and blooms.
