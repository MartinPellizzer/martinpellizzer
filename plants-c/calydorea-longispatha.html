Calydorea longispatha

Calydorea longispatha, commonly known as the long-spurred calydorea, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive elongated, spurred petals that give it its scientific name. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically small and come in shades of white and yellow, with a fragrant scent.

Calydorea longispatha is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
