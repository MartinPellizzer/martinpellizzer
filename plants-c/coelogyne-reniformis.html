Coelogyne reniformis

Coelogyne reniformis, commonly known as the kidney orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces long, pendulous inflorescences.

Coelogyne reniformis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
