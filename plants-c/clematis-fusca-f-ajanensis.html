Clematis fusca f. ajanensis

Clematis fusca f. ajanensis, commonly known as the Ajanensis Clematis, is a rare and unique variant of the species Clematis fusca.

It is native to specific regions in China, where it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soils. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and blue. It is typically found in shaded, moist environments such as forests or along stream banks.
