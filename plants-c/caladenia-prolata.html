Caladenia prolata

Caladenia prolata, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale green to brownish flowers that resemble spiders. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. Caladenia prolata is a perennial herb with a short-lived rhizome and produces its flowers during the spring season.

It is listed as a vulnerable species due to habitat loss and environmental changes.
