Chenopodium nuttalliae

Chenopodium nuttalliae, commonly known as Nuttall's goosefoot, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to the western United States and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. Its seeds are edible and have been used by indigenous peoples for food and medicinal purposes.

The plant is adapted to thrive in poor soils and can tolerate drought conditions.
