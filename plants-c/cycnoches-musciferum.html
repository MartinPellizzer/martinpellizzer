Cycnoches musciferum

Cycnoches musciferum, commonly known as the musk cycnoches, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter, featuring a unique combination of colors including violet, yellow, and green. The plant produces a strong, musky fragrance, which is particularly noticeable during the evening hours. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cycnoches musciferum is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and distinctive aroma.
