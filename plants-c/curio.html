Curio

Curio, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and lush, velvety foliage. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Curio plants are often propagated through leaf cuttings or division, ensuring the continuation of their ornamental beauty.
