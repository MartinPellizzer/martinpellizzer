Cirsium numidicum

Cirsium numidicum, commonly known as the Numidian thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, purple to pinkish-blue flower heads. The plant features deeply lobed, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Cirsium numidicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its striking appearance.
