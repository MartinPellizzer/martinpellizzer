Ctenopteris decipiens

Ctenopteris decipiens, commonly known as the false button fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble those of the true button fern, though it belongs to a different genus. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense mat of tiny, scale-like structures.

Ctenopteris decipiens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in humid, indoor conditions.
