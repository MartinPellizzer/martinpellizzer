Coelogyne elmeri

Coelogyne elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant orchid flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, providing structural support.

Coelogyne elmeri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its showy, long-lasting blooms.
