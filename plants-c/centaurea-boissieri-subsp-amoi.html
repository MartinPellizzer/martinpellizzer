Centaurea boissieri subsp. amoi

Centaurea boissieri subsp.

amoi, commonly known as the Aomori centaury, is a flowering plant native to Japan. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to purple composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, elongated seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
