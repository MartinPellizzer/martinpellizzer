Chrysojasminum odoratissimum

Chrysojasminum odoratissimum, commonly known as the golden jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant belongs to the Oleaceae family and is known for its ornamental value. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
