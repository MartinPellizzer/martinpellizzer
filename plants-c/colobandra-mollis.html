Colobandra mollis

Colobandra mollis, commonly known as the smooth colobandra, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the spring.

Colobandra mollis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
