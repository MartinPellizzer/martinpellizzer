Cyphostemma orondo

Cyphostemma orondo, commonly known as the Orondo moonvine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, woody stems and heart-shaped leaves. Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
