Cylindropuntia schickendantzii

Cylindropuntia schickendantzii, commonly known as the Schickendantz's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a spineless cactus with cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

This cactus is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
