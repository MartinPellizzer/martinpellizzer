Callicarpa heterotricha

Callicarpa heterotricha, commonly known as the variable beautyberry, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible for humans and birds. Callicarpa heterotricha thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant berries and attractive foliage.
