Clinostigma robustum

Clinostigma robustum, commonly known as the robust climber, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color.
