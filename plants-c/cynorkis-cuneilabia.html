Cynorkis cuneilabia

Cynorkis cuneilabia, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
