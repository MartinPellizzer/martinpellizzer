Clematis yunnanensis var. chingtungensis

Clematis yunnanensis var. chingtungensis, commonly known as the Chingtung Clematis, is a climbing vine native to southern China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, often with a distinctive yellow or green throat. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil with partial to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
