Celosia pandurata var. elobata

Celosia pandurata var. elobata, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forests, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Its botanical appeal lies in its unique flowering structure and adaptability to warm climates.
