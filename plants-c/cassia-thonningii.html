Cassia thonningii

Cassia thonningii, commonly known as the African cassia, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in dry savannas and is adapted to arid conditions.

Cassia thonningii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses.
