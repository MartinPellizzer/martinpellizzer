Clavija sparsifolia

Clavija sparsifolia, commonly known as the sparse-leaved clavija, is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile.

Clavija sparsifolia is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens and for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
