Croton abnormis

Croton abnormis, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display a mix of red, yellow, and green hues. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
