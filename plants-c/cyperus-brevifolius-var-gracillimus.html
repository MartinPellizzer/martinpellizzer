Cyperus brevifolius var. gracillimus

Cyperus brevifolius var. gracillimus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flower spikes. This variety is native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found in wet or moist environments. The plant typically grows in shallow water or damp soil, making it a common sight in marshy areas and along waterways.

Its botanical features make it an important species for wetland ecosystems.
