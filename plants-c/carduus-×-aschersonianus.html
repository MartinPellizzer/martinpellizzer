Carduus × aschersonianus

Carduus × aschersonianus, commonly known as the common thistle.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to various parts of the world, often growing in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its leaves are typically lobed and covered in stiff, bristly hairs, while the flowers are composite, consisting of both ray and disk florets.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in herbal remedies.
