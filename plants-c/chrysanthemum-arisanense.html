Chrysanthemum arisanense

Chrysanthemum arisanense, commonly known as the Aisan chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky areas. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Chrysanthemum arisanense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
