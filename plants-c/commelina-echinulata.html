Commelina echinulata

Commelina echinulata, commonly known as the spiny leaf commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with a rough texture and may bear small spines along the edges.

Commelina echinulata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
