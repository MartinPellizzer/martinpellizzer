Callista kunstleri

Callista kunstleri, commonly known as the Kunstler's callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow disk flowers surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Callista kunstleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
