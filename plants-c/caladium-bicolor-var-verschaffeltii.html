Caladium bicolor var. verschaffeltii

Caladium bicolor var. verschaffeltii, commonly known as the 'Verschaffeltii' caladium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant, multicolored patterns in shades of red, white, and green. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its showy foliage rather than its small, inconspicuous flowers.

This variety is particularly valued for its ability to add dramatic visual interest to gardens and indoor spaces.
