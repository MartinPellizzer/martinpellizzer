Combretum pilosum

Combretum pilosum, commonly known as the silky combretum, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pink. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
