Callista stuposa

Callista stuposa, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk, creating a striking visual display. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its botanical name reflects its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
