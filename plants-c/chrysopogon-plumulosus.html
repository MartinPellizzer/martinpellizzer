Chrysopogon plumulosus

Chrysopogon plumulosus, commonly known as the silky grass or silky bamboo grass, is a perennial grass native to Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted shoots and dense, fluffy plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate to subtropical climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a soft, silky texture that contributes to its ornamental value.

It is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to form dense ground cover.
