Cleophora loddigesii

Cleophora loddigesii, commonly known as the Loddiges' cleome, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cleomaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with branching stems and alternate leaves. Its flowers are often clustered in racemes and are surrounded by leaf-like bracts.

Cleophora loddigesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
