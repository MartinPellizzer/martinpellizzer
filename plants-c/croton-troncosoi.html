Croton troncosoi

Croton troncosoi, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, often woody stem and large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its striking foliage.
