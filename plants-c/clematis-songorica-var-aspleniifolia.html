Clematis songorica var. aspleniifolia

Clematis songorica var. aspleniifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf clematis, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, lacey appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale blue, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its unique foliage and modest flowering make it a valuable addition to shaded landscapes.
