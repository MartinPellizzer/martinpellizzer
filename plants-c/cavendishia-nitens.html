Cavendishia nitens

Cavendishia nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that have a silvery or pale green hue. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cavendishia nitens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
