Chrysanthemum hakusanense

Chrysanthemum hakusanense, commonly known as the Hakusan chrysanthemum, is a perennial flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its blooming season occurs in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens during this time.
