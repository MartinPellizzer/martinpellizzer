Chrysosplenium ramosum f. microphyllum

Chrysosplenium ramosum f. microphyllum, commonly known as the dwarf chrysosplenium, is a low-growing herbaceous plant characterized by its small, ovate leaves and clusters of delicate, white or pale yellow flowers.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is native to moist, shaded environments in temperate regions. The plant typically forms dense mats, with stems that are often prostrate or ascending. Its foliage is usually covered in a fine, soft pubescence, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
