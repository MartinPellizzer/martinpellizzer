Cymbidium goeringii var. mackinnonii

Cymbidium goeringii var. mackinnonii, commonly known as the Mackinnon's orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a terrestrial orchid with erect, linear leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers are typically white to pale pink with a yellow lip, and they bloom in late winter to early spring. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for temperate climates.
