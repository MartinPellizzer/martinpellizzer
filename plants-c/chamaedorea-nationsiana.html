Chamaedorea nationsiana

Chamaedorea nationsiana, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is a small, upright palm with slender, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 feet and prefers bright, indirect light. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in moderate humidity.

The plant's foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
