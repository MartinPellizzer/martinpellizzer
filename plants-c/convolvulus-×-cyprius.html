Convolvulus × cyprius

Convolvulus × cyprius, commonly known as the Cyprus bindweed, is a hybrid species belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain environments.
