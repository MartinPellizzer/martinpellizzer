Croton eluteria

Croton eluteria, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red or orange bracts that surround small yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough, textured surface.

Croton eluteria is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a street tree due to its vibrant foliage and rapid growth.
