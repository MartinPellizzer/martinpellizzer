Croton brevipes

Croton brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed croton, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Croton brevipes is valued for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
