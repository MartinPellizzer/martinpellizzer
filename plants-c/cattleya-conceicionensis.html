Cattleya conceicionensis

Cattleya conceicionensis, commonly known as the Conceição Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in the wild. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its blooming period usually occurs during the summer months, making it a prized ornamental plant among orchid enthusiasts.
