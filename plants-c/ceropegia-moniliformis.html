Ceropegia moniliformis

Ceropegia moniliformis, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems that produce pairs of heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
