Cotyledon cossoniana var. versicolor

Cotyledon cossoniana var. versicolor, commonly known as the Variegated Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
