Calathea crotalifera

Calathea crotalifera, commonly known as the arrowleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a velvety texture. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Its leaves are typically a deep green with contrasting veins and a waxy surface.

Calathea crotalifera thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
