Chrysanthemum audibertii

Chrysanthemum audibertii, commonly known as the Audibert's chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, white, and orange, which bloom in the autumn. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
