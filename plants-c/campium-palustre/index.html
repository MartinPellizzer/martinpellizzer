Campium Palustre

Campium palustre is a rare and elusive plant that thrives in wetland environments. It is known for its delicate, grass-like appearance and its ability to flourish in moist, nutrient-poor soils. This species is often overlooked due to its subtle features and limited distribution. Despite its small size, it plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat. Its survival is threatened by habitat loss and environmental changes, making it a subject of conservation interest.
