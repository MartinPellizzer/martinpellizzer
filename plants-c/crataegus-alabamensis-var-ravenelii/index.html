Crataegus Alabamensis Var. Ravenelii

Crataegus alabamensis var. ravenelii is a rare and unique plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the rose family and is known for its distinctive red fruit and ornamental value. this variety is named after the botanist francis ravenel who studied it extensively. it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. due to its limited range and habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
