Cucumis laevigatus

Cucumis laevigatus, commonly known as the smooth cucumber, is a species of flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its smooth, green, elongated fruit that resembles a typical cucumber. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has a trailing growth habit with hairy stems. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

This species is cultivated for its edible fruit, which is used in various culinary applications.
