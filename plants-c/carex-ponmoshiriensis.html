Carex ponmoshiriensis

It is native to Japan and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers. Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
