Capsella

Capsella, commonly known as mouse-ear or common mouse-ear, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, herbaceous growth habit and distinctive, oval-shaped leaves that resemble mouse ears. The flowers are typically small, white or pale purple, and arranged in racemes.
