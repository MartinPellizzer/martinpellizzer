Coelogyne dalatensis

Coelogyne dalatensis, commonly known as the Dalat orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces a raceme of fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers are typically white to pale pink with a yellow lip, and they bloom in the winter or early spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
