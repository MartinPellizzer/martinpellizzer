Coreopsis buchneri

Coreopsis buchneri, commonly known as the Buchner's coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and stream banks. The plant features showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Coreopsis buchneri is valued in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
