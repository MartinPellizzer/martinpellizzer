Cyrtomium tsinglingense

Cyrtomium tsinglingense, commonly known as the Tsingling holly fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features leathery, dark green fronds with a glossy surface, and its spore-producing structures are located on the underside of the leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
