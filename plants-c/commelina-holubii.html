Commelina holubii

Commelina holubii, commonly known as the Holub's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Commelina genus within the family Commelinaceae. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, blue-violet flowers with three petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Commelina holubii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
