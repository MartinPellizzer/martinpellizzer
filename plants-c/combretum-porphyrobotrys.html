Combretum porphyrobotrys

Combretum porphyrobotrys, commonly known as the red-flowered combretum, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, which are followed by distinctive, woody capsules. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local communities.
