Cirsium glabrum

Cirsium glabrum, commonly known as the smooth thistle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny, gray-green leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, and the stems are usually unbranched.

Cirsium glabrum is known for its hardy nature and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
