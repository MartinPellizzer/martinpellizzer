Cyperus lechleri

Cyperus lechleri, commonly known as sangre de drago, is a perennial plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like stems and distinctive reddish-brown exudate, which is often used in traditional medicine. The plant belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly known as the sedge family. Its rhizomes contain compounds with antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

Due to its medicinal value, it is also cultivated for its latex, which is used in natural remedies and cosmetics.
