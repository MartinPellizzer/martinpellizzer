Cyperus panicoides

Cyperus panicoides, commonly known as flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered spikes of flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. The plant produces small, pale brown flowers that are wind-pollinated, and it has rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively.

Cyperus panicoides is often found in disturbed habitats and can be an invasive species in some areas.
