Cattleya × colnagiana

Cattleya × colnagiana, commonly known as the orchid with the most intense red flowers, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy blooms that can range from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant typically has upright, strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with multiple flowers that bloom sequentially. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its vibrant colors and long-lasting blooms.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
