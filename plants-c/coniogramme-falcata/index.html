Coniogramme Falcata

Coniogramme falcata is a unique fern species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its distinctive, sickle-shaped leaves that give it its common name. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forests. Its intricate fronds make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively uncommon in the global horticultural trade.
