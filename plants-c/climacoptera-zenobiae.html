Climacoptera zenobiae

Climacoptera zenobiae, commonly known as the Zenobia's climbing cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a branched stem system that allows it to climb and spread across rocky surfaces, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming during the cooler months. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, enabling it to survive prolonged dry periods.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species within its native range.
