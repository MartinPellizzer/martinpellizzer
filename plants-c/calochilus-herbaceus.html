Calochilus herbaceus

Calochilus herbaceus, commonly known as the slender spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with a compact growth habit, typically reaching heights of up to 20 cm. The plant features a single, hairy stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. Its most distinctive feature is its delicate, spider-like flowers, which are usually pale green or yellow with a contrasting lip.

Calochilus herbaceus thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.
