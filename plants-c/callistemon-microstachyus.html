Callistemon microstachyus

Callistemon microstachyus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that form dense, brush-like clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its unique floral structure makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and wildlife habitats.
