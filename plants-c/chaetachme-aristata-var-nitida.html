Chaetachme aristata var. nitida

Chaetachme aristata var. nitida, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often used in xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
