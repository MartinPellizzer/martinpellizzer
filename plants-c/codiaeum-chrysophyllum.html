Codiaeum chrysophyllum

Codiaeum chrysophyllum, commonly known as the flame leaf or croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in landscaping and indoor gardening. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for those seeking a visually appealing and low-maintenance plant.
