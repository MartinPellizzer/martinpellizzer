Capparis detonsa

Capparis detonsa, commonly known as the rock caper, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, which bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds.

Capparis detonsa is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
