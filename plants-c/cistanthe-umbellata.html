Cistanthe umbellata

Cistanthe umbellata, commonly known as the desert star, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has a low-growing, mat-forming habit with succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in harsh environments. Cistanthe umbellata is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.
