Cotoneaster intermedius

Cotoneaster intermedius, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Cotoneaster intermedius is relatively low maintenance and thrives in a variety of soil types.
