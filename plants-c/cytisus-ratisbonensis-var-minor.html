Cytisus ratisbonensis var. minor

Cytisus ratisbonensis var. minor, commonly known as the small broom, is a compact shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and long blooming period.
