Calathea trianae

Calathea trianae, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns and variegation. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and exhibit a unique ability to fold upwards at night, a behavior known as nyctinasty. This plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
