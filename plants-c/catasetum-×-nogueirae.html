Catasetum × nogueirae

Catasetum × nogueirae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers with a distinctive lip.

Catasetum × nogueirae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
