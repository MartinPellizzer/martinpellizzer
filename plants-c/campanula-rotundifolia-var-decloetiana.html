Campanula rotundifolia var. decloetiana

Campanula rotundifolia var. decloetiana, commonly known as the rounded-leaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are rounded and often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft, fuzzy appearance. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
