Ceropegia mwinilungensis

Ceropegia mwinilungensis, commonly known as the Mwinilungensis African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
