Callicarpa subglabra

Callicarpa subglabra, commonly known as smooth beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Callicarpa subglabra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in gardens for its vibrant fruit and attractive foliage.
