Cheilanthes marantae

Cheilanthes marantae, commonly known as the Marantae rock fern, is a terrestrial fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped, leathery leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rocky substrates.
