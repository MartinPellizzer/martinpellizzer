Crassula gillii

Crassula gillii, commonly known as the Gillii's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, rounded leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Crassula gillii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
