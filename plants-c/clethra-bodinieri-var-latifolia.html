Clethra bodinieri var. latifolia

Clethra bodinieri var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf sweet pepper tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese cuisine. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic with a smooth margin.

It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a versatile ornamental plant.
