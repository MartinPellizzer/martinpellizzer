Cynometra simplicifolia var. oblongata

Cynometra simplicifolia var. oblongata, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The tree produces a heavy fruit with a thick, fibrous outer layer and a smooth, oily seed inside. Its bark and seeds are used in traditional medicine and for producing shea butter.

The plant is also valued for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity in its native habitat.
