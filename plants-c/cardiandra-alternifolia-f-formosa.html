Cardiandra alternifolia f. formosa

Cardiandra alternifolia f. formosa, commonly known as the variegated heartleaf.

This plant is a variant of the heartleaf species, characterized by its striking variegation in its foliage. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The leaves are typically heart-shaped, with a smooth margin and a waxy texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
