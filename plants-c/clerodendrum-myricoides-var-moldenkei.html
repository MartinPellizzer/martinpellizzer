Clerodendrum myricoides var. moldenkei

Clerodendrum myricoides var. moldenkei, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange bracts that resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
