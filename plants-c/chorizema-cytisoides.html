Chorizema cytisoides

Chorizema cytisoides, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, flame-like flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
