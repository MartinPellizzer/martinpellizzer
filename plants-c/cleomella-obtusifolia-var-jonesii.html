Cleomella obtusifolia var. jonesii

Cleomella obtusifolia var. jonesii, commonly known as Jones' bluntleaf cleomella, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Cleomaceae family, which includes various species of flowering plants often found in warm climates. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
