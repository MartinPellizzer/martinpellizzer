Cordia Alba

Cordia alba is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its white flowers and vibrant red fruits. Native to tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in warm, humid climates. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are edible and can be used in various culinary applications. Overall, Cordia alba is a versatile and visually appealing plant with both ecological and practical significance.
