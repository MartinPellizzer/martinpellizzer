Calliandra urbanii

Calliandra urbanii, commonly known as the red calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that produce vibrant pink to red bracts. The plant is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Calliandra urbanii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its showy flowers and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
