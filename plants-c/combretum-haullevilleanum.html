Combretum haullevilleanum

Combretum haullevilleanum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Combretum haullevilleanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
