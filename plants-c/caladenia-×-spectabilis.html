Caladenia × spectabilis

Caladenia × spectabilis, commonly known as the showy spider-orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the south-western region of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple, with intricate patterns on the labellum. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests. It produces a single, erect stem with several flowers, each featuring a slender, elongated petal.

Due to its beauty and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species.
