Ceropegia chrysantha

Ceropegia chrysantha, commonly known as the yellow ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, trailing stems and fleshy leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
