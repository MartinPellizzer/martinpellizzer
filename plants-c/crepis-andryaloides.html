Crepis andryaloides

Crepis andryaloides, commonly known as the narrowleaf mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

Crepis andryaloides is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
