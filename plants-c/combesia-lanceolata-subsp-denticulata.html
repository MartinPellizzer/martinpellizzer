Combesia lanceolata subsp. denticulata

Combesia lanceolata subsp.

denticulata, commonly known as the denticulate combesia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in racemes. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats.
