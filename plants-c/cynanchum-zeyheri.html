Cynanchum zeyheri

Cynanchum zeyheri, commonly known as Zeyher's milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is known for its distinctive milky sap and showy flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, attracting various pollinators.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy and resilient species.
