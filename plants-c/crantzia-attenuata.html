Crantzia attenuata

Crantzia attenuata, commonly known as the slender crantzia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands. Its flowers are small and yellow, with a distinct ray floret appearance.

Crantzia attenuata is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
