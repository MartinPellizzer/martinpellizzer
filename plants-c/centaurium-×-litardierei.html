Centaurium × litardierei

Centaurium × litardierei, commonly known as the hybrid centaury, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in dense spikes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in moist, calcareous soils. It is closely related to other species in the genus Centaurium, such as Centaurium erythraea.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
