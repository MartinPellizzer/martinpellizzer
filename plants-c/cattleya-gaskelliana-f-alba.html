Cattleya gaskelliana f. alba

Cattleya gaskelliana f. alba, commonly known as the White Gaskell Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species noted for its striking white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, pendulous blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its elegance and unique coloration.
