Cyclolobium vecchii

Cyclolobium vecchii, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and used in various cuisines. Cyclolobium vecchii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in agricultural systems.
