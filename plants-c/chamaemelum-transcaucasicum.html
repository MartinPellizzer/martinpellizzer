Chamaemelum transcaucasicum

Chamaemelum transcaucasicum, commonly known as Caucasian chamomile, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and linear, aromatic leaves. Native to the Caucasus region, it thrives in dry, sunny environments. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional remedies.

Its essential oils contain compounds like chamazulene, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and calming effects.
