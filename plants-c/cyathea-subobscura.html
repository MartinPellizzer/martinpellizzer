Cyathea subobscura

Cyathea subobscura, commonly known as the dark shield fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a distinctive dark, leathery appearance, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cyathea subobscura plays an important ecological role in New Zealand's native ecosystems.
