Coelogyne chinensis

Coelogyne chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are usually white or yellow with red or orange markings. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

Coelogyne chinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
