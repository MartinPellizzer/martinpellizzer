Cedrela nebulosa

Cedrela nebulosa, commonly known as the misty cedrela, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its deciduous nature, shedding its leaves during the dry season. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall, featuring a straight trunk and a broad, umbrella-like canopy. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth upper surface and hairy lower surface.

The flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in panicles, while the fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.
