Cactus schlechtendalii

Cactus schlechtendalii, commonly known as the Schlechtendal's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green and covered with spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are often clustered at the ends of the stems. It is adapted to survive in harsh desert conditions with minimal water requirements.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for succulent collections and xeriscaping.
