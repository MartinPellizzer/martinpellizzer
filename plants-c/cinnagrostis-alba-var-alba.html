Cinnagrostis alba var. alba

Cinnagrostis alba var. alba, commonly known as white goosgrass, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found along riverbanks and roadsides.
