Chlorophytum sphacelatum

Chlorophytum sphacelatum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are succulent, storing water to help it survive in dry environments.

Chlorophytum sphacelatum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
