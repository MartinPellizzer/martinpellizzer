Cyperus fischerianus var. ugandensis

Cyperus fischerianus var. ugandensis, commonly known as the Uganda flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes, ponds, and along the edges of water bodies.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing aquatic habitats.
