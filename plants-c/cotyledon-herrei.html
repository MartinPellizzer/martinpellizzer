Cotyledon herrei

Cotyledon herrei, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Cotyledon herrei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
