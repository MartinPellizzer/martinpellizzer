Cynanchum heterophyllum

Cynanchum heterophyllum, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed or the blue milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, and its latex contains milky sap. It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
