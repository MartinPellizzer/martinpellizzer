Cytisus × praecox f. albus

Cytisus × praecox f. albus, commonly known as the white broom, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers, which contrast with its green, trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as heaths and grasslands. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
