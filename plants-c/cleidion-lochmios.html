Cleidion lochmios

Cleidion lochmios, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and dense clusters of white, fluffy flowers that appear in spring. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded areas of forests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental flowers and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
