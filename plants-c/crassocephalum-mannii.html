Crassocephalum mannii

Crassocephalum mannii, commonly known as the African blue daisy, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Crassocephalum mannii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
