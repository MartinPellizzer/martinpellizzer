Clusiella

Clusiella, commonly known as the "spiny beauty," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Clusiaceae.

These plants are characterized by their woody stems, often adorned with spines or thorns, and their striking, brightly colored flowers. Clusiella species typically thrive in humid environments and are often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
