Cnestis polyphylla

Cnestis polyphylla, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain several seeds. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Due to its hardy nature and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
