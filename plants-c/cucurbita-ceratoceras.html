Cucurbita ceratoceras

Cucurbita ceratoceras, commonly known as the horned squash, is a species of winter squash native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive horn-shaped fruit, which can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other squashes, gourds, and cucumbers. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The fruit is typically orange to yellow in color and is used in traditional Mexican cuisine.
