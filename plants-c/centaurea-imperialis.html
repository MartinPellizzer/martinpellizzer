Centaurea imperialis

Centaurea imperialis, commonly known as the imperial centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, tall spikes of purple to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea imperialis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
