Clematis finetiana var. lutchuensis

Clematis finetiana var. lutchuensis, commonly known as the Lutchuensis Clematis, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features attractive, showy flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, with five petals and a distinctive spurred structure. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and it produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
