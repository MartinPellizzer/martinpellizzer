Carduus × conrathii

Carduus × conrathii, commonly known as the Conrath's thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads, which consist of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. It has a rosette of basal leaves and a tall, erect stem that supports its composite flowers.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
