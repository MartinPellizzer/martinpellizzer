Chlorophytum inarticulatum

Chlorophytum inarticulatum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are usually green with white or yellow variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal indoor plant. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Chlorophytum inarticulatum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, as it can help remove toxins from the air.
