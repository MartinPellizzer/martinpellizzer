Cordia nashii

Cordia nashii, commonly known as the red-flowered cordia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly found in Jamaica and Cuba.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its showy, red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
