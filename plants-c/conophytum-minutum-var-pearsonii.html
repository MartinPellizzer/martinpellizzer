Conophytum minutum var. pearsonii

Conophytum minutum var. pearsonii, commonly known as the pearsonii conophytum, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
