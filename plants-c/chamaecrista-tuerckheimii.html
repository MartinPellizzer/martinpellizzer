Chamaecrista tuerckheimii

Chamaecrista tuerckheimii, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
