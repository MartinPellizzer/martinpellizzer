Coeloglossum islandicum

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant grows in rocky or coastal habitats, often in shaded environments. It has linear leaves and a slender stem, adapted to its arid and coastal climate.
