Catasetum pulchrum

Catasetum pulchrum, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are notable for their intricate structure and vivid hues, often featuring a unique lip that aids in pollination.

Catasetum pulchrum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
