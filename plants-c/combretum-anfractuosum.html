Combretum anfractuosum

Combretum anfractuosum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in tropical and subtropical regions.
