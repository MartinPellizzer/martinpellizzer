Callitropsis nootkatensis

Callitropsis nootkatensis, commonly known as Alaska cedar, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the western coast of North America.

It belongs to the cypress family (Cupressaceae) and is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and scale-like leaves. The tree produces small, rounded cones that contain winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal rainforests.
