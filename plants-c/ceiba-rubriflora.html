Ceiba rubriflora

Ceiba rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered ceiba, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, palmate leaves. The tree produces showy, red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing numerous seeds.

Ceiba rubriflora plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for wildlife.
