Clematis urophylla var. heterophylla

Clematis urophylla var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variableleaf clematis, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf morphology, with leaves varying in shape and size along the stem. The plant typically produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

This varietal is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to different garden conditions.
