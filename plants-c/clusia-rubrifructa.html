Clusia rubrifructa

Clusia rubrifructa, commonly known as the red-fruited clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers, followed by bright red, fleshy fruits that are edible. Clusia rubrifructa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland tropical forests.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
