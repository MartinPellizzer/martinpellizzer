Catalpa bignonioides f. rehderi

Catalpa bignonioides f. rehderi, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a cultivar of the catalpa tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic and ecological benefits. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, and it is relatively hardy in temperate climates.

This cultivar is also noted for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in gardens and parks.
