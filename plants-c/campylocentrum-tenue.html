Campylocentrum tenue

Campylocentrum tenue, commonly known as the slender starfruit, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

The botanical features of Campylocentrum tenue make it a unique addition to both gardens and botanical collections.
