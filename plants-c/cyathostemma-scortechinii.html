Cyathostemma scortechinii

Cyathostemma scortechinii, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense, clustered inflorescences. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Cyathostemma scortechinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
