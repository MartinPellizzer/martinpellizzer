Catasetum claveringii

Catasetum claveringii, commonly known as the Clavering's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Catasetum claveringii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
