Campanula mollis var. maroccana

Campanula mollis var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
