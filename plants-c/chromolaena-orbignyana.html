Chromolaena orbignyana

Chromolaena orbignyana, commonly known as flame weed, is a fast-growing shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinctive reddish or orange hue on the underside. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by fluffy, pale brown seed heads that are dispersed by the wind. It is known for its aggressive growth and is considered an invasive species in many parts of the world.

Its adaptability to various soil types and ability to thrive in disturbed areas contribute to its widespread distribution.
