Carum angelicifolium

Carum angelicifolium, commonly known as angelica leaf caraway, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in umbels. The plant produces small, aromatic seeds that are used in culinary and medicinal applications. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both horticultural and ecological purposes.
