Crocus leichtlinii

Crocus leichtlinii, commonly known as the Alpine crocus, is a species of flowering plant in the iris family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe. This plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soil. The flowers are usually pale yellow or white, with a distinctive, showy appearance.

It blooms in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge.
