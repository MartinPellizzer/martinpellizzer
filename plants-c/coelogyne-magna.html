Coelogyne magna

Coelogyne magna, commonly known as the giant coelogyne, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, making it one of the most impressive orchids in terms of size. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its inflorescence consists of multiple blooms, often in shades of white, yellow, and orange, which are highly fragrant.

Coelogyne magna thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
