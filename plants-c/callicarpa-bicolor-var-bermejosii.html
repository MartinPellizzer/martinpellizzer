Callicarpa bicolor var. bermejosii

Callicarpa bicolor var. bermejosii, commonly known as the red-leaf beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant red leaves that contrast beautifully with its clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces dark purple berries that are both ornamental and edible, though they are often consumed by birds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping for its seasonal color changes.
