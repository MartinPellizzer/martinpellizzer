Calathea portobelensis

Calathea portobelensis, commonly known as the pink star calathea, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display intricate patterns in shades of pink, green, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a deep green base and a pink to red margin. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
