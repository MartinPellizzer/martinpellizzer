Coelogyne longipes

Coelogyne longipes, commonly known as the long-iped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne longipes is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
