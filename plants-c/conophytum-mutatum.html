Conophytum mutatum

Conophytum mutatum, commonly known as the "Lucky Star" or "Cushion Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded body with fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
