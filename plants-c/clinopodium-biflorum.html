Clinopodium biflorum

Clinopodium biflorum, commonly known as slender wood betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its small, tubular flowers are usually violet or blue, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Clinopodium biflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
