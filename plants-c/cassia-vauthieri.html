Cassia vauthieri

Cassia vauthieri, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Cassia vauthieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
