Carduncellus Carthamoides

Carduncellus carthamoides is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region known for its distinctive spiny leaves and yellow flowers. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is commonly found in rocky and dry habitats. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening despite its prickly foliage. The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies. However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
