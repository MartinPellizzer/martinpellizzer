Cistus × akamantis

Cistus × akamantis, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to local biodiversity.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
