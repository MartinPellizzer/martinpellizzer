Curio crassulifolius

Curio crassulifolius, commonly known as the African violet or the common curio, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, often blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
