Conophytum globuliforme

Conophytum globuliforme, commonly known as the globular conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is often grown as a decorative indoor plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Conophytum globuliforme is well-suited for rock gardens or containers, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
