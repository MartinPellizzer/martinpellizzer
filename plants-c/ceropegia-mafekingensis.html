Ceropegia mafekingensis

Ceropegia mafekingensis, commonly known as the Mafeking ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tubular flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
