Crassula natans var. minus

Crassula natans var. minus, commonly known as the lesser floating jade, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant grows in water, often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats. Its compact growth habit and ability to float make it an attractive addition to aquatic gardens.

It is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal maintenance and thriving in moderate light conditions.
