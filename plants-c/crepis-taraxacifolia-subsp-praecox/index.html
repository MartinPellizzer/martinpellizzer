Crepis Taraxacifolia Subsp. Praecox

Crepis taraxacifolia subsp. praecox is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and diverse species. This subspecies is characterized by its small, dandelion-like leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this unique plant from habitat loss and environmental changes.
