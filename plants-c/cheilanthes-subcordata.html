Cheilanthes subcordata

Cheilanthes subcordata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock fern, is a species of fern native to arid and rocky environments in North America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
