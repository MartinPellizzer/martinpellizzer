Caladium houbyanum

Caladium houbyanum, commonly known as the "Rainbow Caladium," is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that comes in shades of pink, red, white, and green. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have intricate patterns, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is typically grown as an annual in temperate regions due to its sensitivity to frost.
