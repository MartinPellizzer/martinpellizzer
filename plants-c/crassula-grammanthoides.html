Crassula grammanthoides

Crassula grammanthoides, commonly known as the stonecrop or ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.

Crassula grammanthoides prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
