Chlorophytum poggei

Chlorophytum poggei, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail Plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are typically light green with distinct, darker green stripes. The plant produces small, white flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant in arid regions.
