Commelina orchidophylla

Commelina orchidophylla, commonly known as the orchid-leaf commelina, is a striking flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a white or pale pink center. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of veins. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking unique and exotic plants.
