Combretum undulatomarginatum

Combretum undulatomarginatum, commonly known as the undulating-margin combretum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with undulating margins. This species is often found in open woodland and along riverbanks, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
