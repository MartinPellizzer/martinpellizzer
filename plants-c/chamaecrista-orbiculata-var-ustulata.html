Chamaecrista orbiculata var. ustulata

Chamaecrista orbiculata var. ustulata, commonly known as the flame bean, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features palmately compound leaves with ovate to orbicular leaflets, and it produces bright orange to red, pea-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its cylindrical, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and ecological landscaping.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
