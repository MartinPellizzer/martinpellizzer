Carthamus Dianius

Cape marigold, scientifically known as Carthamus dianius, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is renowned for its vibrant yellow and orange blooms that add a splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes. Its historical use in traditional medicine highlights its significance beyond aesthetics. With its unique appearance and versatile applications, Cape marigold remains a valuable and admired plant in horticulture and herbal practices.
