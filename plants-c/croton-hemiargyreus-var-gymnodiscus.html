Croton hemiargyreus var. gymnodiscus

Croton hemiargyreus var. gymnodiscus, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a dense, velvety layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in horticulture.
