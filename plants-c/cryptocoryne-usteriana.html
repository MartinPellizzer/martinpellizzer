Cryptocoryne usteriana

Cryptocoryne usteriana, commonly known as the Java cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, broad leaves that are often adorned with a distinctive white or yellow midrib. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams, and prefers moderate to high light conditions. Its rhizome system allows it to anchor itself in substrates and spread vegetatively.

Cryptocoryne usteriana is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
