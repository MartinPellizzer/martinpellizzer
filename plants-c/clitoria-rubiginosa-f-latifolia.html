Clitoria rubiginosa f. latifolia

Clitoria rubiginosa f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved winged bean, is a tropical legume characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and showy, butterfly-like purple flowers.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is valued for its edible pods, which contain seeds and a starchy pulp used in various culinary applications.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing abilities, making it a useful plant in agroforestry systems.
