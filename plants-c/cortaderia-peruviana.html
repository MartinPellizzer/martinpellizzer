Cortaderia peruviana

Cortaderia peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian feather reed, is a tall, grass-like plant native to South America.

It features long, slender, silver-green leaves that form a dense, feathery plume at the top. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Cortaderia peruviana is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
