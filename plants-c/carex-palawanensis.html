Carex palawanensis

Carex palawanensis, commonly known as Palawan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the island of Palawan. This plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grass-like leaves and cylindrical flower spikes. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.

Carex palawanensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms in its native ecosystem.
