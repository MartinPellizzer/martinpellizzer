Cleome ovalifolia

Cleome ovalifolia, commonly known as the ovalleaf cleome, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Cleome ovalifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
