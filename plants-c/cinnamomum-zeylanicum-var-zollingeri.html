Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. zollingeri

Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. zollingeri, commonly known as Zollinger's cinnamon, is a variant of the cinnamon tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is used in traditional medicine and as a spice. The plant typically grows in tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are ovate and have a strong, spicy scent.

This variety is valued for its essential oils and has historical significance in herbal remedies.
