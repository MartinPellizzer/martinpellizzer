Crambe filiformis granatensis

Crambe filiformis granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense slender crambe, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
