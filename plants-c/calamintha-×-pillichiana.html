Calamintha × pillichiana

Calamintha × pillichiana, commonly known as sweet germander, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, pink or white flowers and fuzzy, gray-green leaves. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. The plant is often used in herbal medicine for its calming properties.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
