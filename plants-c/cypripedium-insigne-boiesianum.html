Cypripedium insigne boiesianum

Cypripedium insigne boiesianum, commonly known as the Boiesian lady's tresses, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of Asia.

It features a tall, slender stem with a single, showy inflorescence composed of numerous small, fragrant flowers arranged in a loose spiral. The plant has a distinctive, narrow, grass-like foliage and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. Its flowers are typically pink or white, with a lip that is often marked with intricate patterns.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in specialized gardens due to its rarity and specific growing requirements.
