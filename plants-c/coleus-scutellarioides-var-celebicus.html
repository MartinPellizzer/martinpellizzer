Coleus scutellarioides var. celebicus

Coleus scutellarioides var. celebicus, commonly known as the Celebes coleus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that range in color from red to purple, which are often mistaken for flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green, with prominent veins. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscape design and as a potted plant.
