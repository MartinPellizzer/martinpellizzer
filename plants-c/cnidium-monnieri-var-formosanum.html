Cnidium monnieri var. formosanum

Cnidium monnieri var. formosanum, commonly known as the Formosan cowplant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with serrated margins, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers.
