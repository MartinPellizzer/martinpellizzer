Cogswellia alata

Cogswellia alata, commonly known as the spiral jade, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spiraling growth pattern, which gives it a distinctive, almost otherworldly appearance. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base, adding to its visual appeal. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often sought after by collectors and enthusiasts of unusual plants.
