Cyathea brooksii

Cyathea brooksii, commonly known as the Brooks's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of compound leaves that give it a lush, tropical appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Cyathea brooksii plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
