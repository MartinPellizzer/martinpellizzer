Coelorachis impressa

Coelorachis impressa, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence and strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, with a smooth texture.

Coelorachis impressa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or botanical gardens.
