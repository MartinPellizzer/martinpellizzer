Ceratostylis acutilabris

Ceratostylis acutilabris, commonly known as the sharp-labied sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf that is often marked with distinctive veins. The plant produces a single, elongated spike bearing several small, pale green to white flowers with a unique, spurred lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
