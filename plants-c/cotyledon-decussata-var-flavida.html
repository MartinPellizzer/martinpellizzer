Cotyledon decussata var. flavida

Cotyledon decussata var. flavida, commonly known as the yellow-flowered cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a yellow or orange hue, especially under direct sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to store water in its leaves.
