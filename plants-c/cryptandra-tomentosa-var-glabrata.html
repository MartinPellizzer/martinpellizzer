Cryptandra tomentosa var. glabrata

Cryptandra tomentosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth cryptandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and woody stems. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, though the variety glabrata has reduced hair, giving it a smoother appearance. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland shrublands.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
