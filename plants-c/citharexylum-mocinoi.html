Citharexylum mocinoi

Citharexylum mocinoi, commonly known as the Mocino's citharexylum, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in small umbels, often accompanied by opposite, simple leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
