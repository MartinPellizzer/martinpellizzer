Cistus × clausonii nothosubsp. clausonii

Cistus × clausonii nothosubsp.

clausonii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and gray-green foliage, with small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and leathery, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting various pollinators in its native habitat.
