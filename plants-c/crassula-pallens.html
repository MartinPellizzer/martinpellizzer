Crassula pallens

Crassula pallens, commonly known as the pale crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
