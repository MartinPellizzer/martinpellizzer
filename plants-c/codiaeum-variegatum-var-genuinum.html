Codiaeum variegatum var. genuinum

Codiaeum variegatum var. genuinum, commonly known as the flame leaf or croton plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage with leaves displaying shades of red, orange, yellow, and green. The plant has palmate leaves with a waxy texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its striking appearance, it is popular in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
