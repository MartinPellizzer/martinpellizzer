Chamaecrista fagonioides var. fagonioides

Chamaecrista fagonioides var. fagonioides, commonly known as the yellow cassia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
