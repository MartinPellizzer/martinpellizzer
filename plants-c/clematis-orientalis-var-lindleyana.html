Clematis orientalis var. lindleyana

Clematis orientalis var. lindleyana, commonly known as the Chinese vine or Lindley's clematis, is a deciduous climbing plant native to China.

It features attractive, fragrant, single or semi-double flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its beautiful blooms.
