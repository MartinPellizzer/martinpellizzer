Cyathea conspicua

Cyathea conspicua, commonly known as the showy tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its bright green, glossy fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Cyathea conspicua is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
