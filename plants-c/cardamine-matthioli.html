Cardamine matthioli

Cardamine matthioli, commonly known as the yellow corydalis, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, typically found in moist, shaded woodland areas. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. It is often noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
