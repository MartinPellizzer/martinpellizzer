Coffea homollei

Coffea homollei, commonly known as the Ethiopian coffee tree, is a species native to Ethiopia.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by red berries containing the coffee seeds. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Coffea homollei is closely related to other coffee species and is valued for its unique flavor profile in specialty coffee blends.
