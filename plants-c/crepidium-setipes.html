Crepidium setipes

Crepidium setipes, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often curled or twisted. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
