Coussarea janeirensis

Coussarea janeirensis, commonly known as the Brazilian coussarea, is a tropical plant native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.
