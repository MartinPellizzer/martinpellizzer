Clinostigma pancheri

Clinostigma pancheri, commonly known as the Kashmiri bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a hairy texture.
