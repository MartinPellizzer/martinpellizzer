Cistus parviflorus var. brevifolius

Cistus parviflorus var. brevifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

This variety is often found in coastal and mountainous areas where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
