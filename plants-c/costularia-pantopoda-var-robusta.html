Costularia pantopoda var. robusta

Costularia pantopoda var. robusta, commonly known as the robust costularia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are leathery and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the delicate flowers.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and air-purifying properties.
