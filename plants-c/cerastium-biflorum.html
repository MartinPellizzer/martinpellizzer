Cerastium biflorum

Cerastium biflorum, commonly known as the two-flowered mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive, velvety texture.
