Coreopsis mitis

Coreopsis mitis, commonly known as the slender coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies adds ecological value to its habitat.
