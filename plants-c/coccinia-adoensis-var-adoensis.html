Coccinia adoensis var. adoensis

Coccinia adoensis var. adoensis, commonly known as the African butterfruit, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and pumpkins, and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The fruit is typically round, with a smooth, waxy skin that turns from green to orange as it matures.
