Cistanche Lutea

Cistanche lutea is a parasitic plant known for its unique relationship with its host. It lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on other plants for nutrients. Found primarily in arid regions of Asia, it thrives in harsh desert environments. Despite its lack of traditional leaves, it produces vibrant yellow flowers. This plant has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.
