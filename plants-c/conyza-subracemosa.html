Conyza subracemosa

Conyza subracemosa, commonly known as the slenderleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a racemose inflorescence. It is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

Conyza subracemosa is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
