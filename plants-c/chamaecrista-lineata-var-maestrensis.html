Chamaecrista lineata var. maestrensis

Chamaecrista lineata var. maestrensis, commonly known as the Maestrense Crownflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
