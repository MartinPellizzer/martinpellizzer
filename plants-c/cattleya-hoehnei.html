Cattleya hoehnei

Cattleya hoehnei, commonly known as the Hoehne's Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with showy, strap-like petals and a distinctive, wide lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Cattleya hoehnei is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
