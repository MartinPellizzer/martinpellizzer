Caroxylon kleinfonteini

Caroxylon kleinfonteini, commonly known as the Kleinfontein cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Cycadaceae and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, compound leaves. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and human encroachment.

Conservation efforts are essential to protect this unique botanical species from extinction.
