Caryota urens

Caryota urens, commonly known as the horse's tail palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, feathery leaves that resemble a horse's tail, giving it its common name. The plant has a short trunk with a crown of pinnate leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
