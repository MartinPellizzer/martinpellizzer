Caroxylon genistoides

Caroxylon genistoides, commonly known as the African broom.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with pairs of opposite leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Caroxylon genistoides is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
