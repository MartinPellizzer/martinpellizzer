Cucurbita pepo var. rhenana

Cucurbita pepo var. rhenana, commonly known as the common gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a herbaceous vine that produces both male and female flowers on the same plant. The fruit is typically elongated and varies in color from green to yellow or orange depending on maturity. It has a thick, ridged rind and a sweet, nutty flavor when ripe.

This variety is often cultivated for its edible fruits and ornamental value.
