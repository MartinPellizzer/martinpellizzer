Cymbidium linearisepalum f. atropurpureum

Cymbidium linearisepalum f. atropurpureum, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and striking purple and white flower spikes. The flowers typically bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens during the colder months. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
