Cycnium tenuisectum

Cycnium tenuisectum, commonly known as the slenderleaf marshmallow, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its stems are slender and branching. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Cycnium tenuisectum is valued for its ability to thrive in moist soils and its role in supporting local wildlife.
