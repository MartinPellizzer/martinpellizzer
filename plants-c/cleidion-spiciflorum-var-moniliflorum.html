Cleidion spiciflorum var. moniliflorum

Cleidion spiciflorum var. moniliflorum, commonly known as the chain-flower tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive inflorescences, which consist of long, drooping clusters of small, white flowers resembling chains. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
