Centaurea strepens

Centaurea strepens, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its slender stems and blue to violet flowers bloom from late spring to early summer.

Centaurea strepens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
