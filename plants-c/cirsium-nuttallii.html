Cirsium nuttallii

Cirsium nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's thistle, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges and covered in fine hairs.

The species is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.
