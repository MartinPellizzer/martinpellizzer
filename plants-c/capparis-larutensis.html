Capparis larutensis

Capparis larutensis, commonly known as the Larut Caper, is a flowering plant native to the Malay Peninsula.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, yellow flowers that are typically borne in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Capparis larutensis is often found in forested areas and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native region.
