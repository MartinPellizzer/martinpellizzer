Calanthe reflexilabris

Calanthe reflexilabris, commonly known as the reflexed calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its flowers are borne on long, slender stems and are often adorned with subtle markings.

Calanthe reflexilabris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
