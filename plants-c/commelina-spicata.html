Commelina spicata

Commelina spicata, commonly known as the spike commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands.
