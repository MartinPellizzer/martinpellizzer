Chiliadenus sericeus

Chiliadenus sericeus, commonly known as the silverleaf chiliadenus, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
