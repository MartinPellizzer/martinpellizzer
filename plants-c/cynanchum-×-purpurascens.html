Cynanchum × purpurascens

Cynanchum × purpurascens, commonly known as the purple milkvetch, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets that give it a delicate appearance. Its flowers are typically pink to purple in color, arranged in small clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

This plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.
