Citrosma petiolaris

Citrosma petiolaris, commonly known as the yellow citrus tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes other citrus and aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round citrus-like berry with a thick, leathery skin.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine and perfumery.
