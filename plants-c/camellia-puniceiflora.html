Camellia puniceiflora

Camellia puniceiflora, commonly known as the pink-flowered camellia, is a species native to southern China and northern Vietnam.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from deep pink to almost red in color. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia puniceiflora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter-blooming flowers.
