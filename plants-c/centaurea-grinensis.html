Centaurea grinensis

Centaurea grinensis, commonly known as the Gornja Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its striking blue or purple flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Centaurea grinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
