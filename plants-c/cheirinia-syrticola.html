Cheirinia syrticola

Cheirinia syrticola, commonly known as the desert flax, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale blue or purple flowers. Cheirinia syrticola plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
