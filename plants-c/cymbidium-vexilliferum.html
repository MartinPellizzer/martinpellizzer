Cymbidium vexilliferum

Cymbidium vexilliferum, commonly known as the Chinese everlasting orchid, is a species native to China and is part of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, humid climates. The plant features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Its flowers are characterized by their long, narrow petals and a distinctive lip that is often more vibrant in color.

Cymbidium vexilliferum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
