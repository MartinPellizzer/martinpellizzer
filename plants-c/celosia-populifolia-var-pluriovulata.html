Celosia populifolia var. pluriovulata

Celosia populifolia var. pluriovulata, commonly known as the broadleaf celosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that resemble those of a poplar tree, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant, plume-like inflorescences in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both visually striking and relatively easy to cultivate in warm climates.
