Croton penninervis

Croton penninervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically narrow and have a distinctive, intricate venation pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
