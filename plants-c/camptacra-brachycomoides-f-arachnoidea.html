Camptacra brachycomoides f. arachnoidea

Camptacra brachycomoides f. arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider-like dandelion, is a rare and unique botanical variant characterized by its distinctive, spider-like bracts.

This fern-like plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to specific regions in southern Africa. Its foliage consists of finely divided, almost hair-like leaves that give it a delicate, ethereal appearance. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a subtle floral display.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
