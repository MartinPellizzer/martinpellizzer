Celastrus arbutifolius

Celastrus arbutifolius, commonly known as the strawberry tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that turn red in the autumn. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and a woody stem with thick, corky bark.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in rocky or coastal environments.
