Canarium vrieseanum

Canarium vrieseanum, commonly known as the Vrieseanum candlenut, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and large, leathery leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. The plant is also noted for its ornamental value and use in traditional medicine.
