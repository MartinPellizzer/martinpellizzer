Convolvulus brownii

Convolvulus brownii, commonly known as the brown's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Convolvulus brownii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
