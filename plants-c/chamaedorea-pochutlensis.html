Chamaedorea pochutlensis

Chamaedorea pochutlensis, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a compact, upright palm with slender, dark green leaves that form a dense canopy. The plant thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments and prefers well-draining, moist soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.

Chamaedorea pochutlensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
