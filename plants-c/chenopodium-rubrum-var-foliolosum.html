Chenopodium rubrum var. foliolosum

Chenopodium rubrum var. foliolosum, commonly known as red goosefoot, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often reddish or bronze in color, especially on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, followed by triangular, beak-like seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in agricultural settings.
