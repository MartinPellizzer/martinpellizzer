Cotyledon crassifolia

Cotyledon crassifolia, commonly known as the thickleaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
