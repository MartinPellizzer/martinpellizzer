Conophytum × marnierianum

Conophytum × marnierianum, commonly known as the hybrid stone plant, is a succulent belonging to the Aizoaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the warmer months. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
