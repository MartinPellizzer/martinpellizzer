Cylicodaphne pallida

Cylicodaphne pallida, commonly known as the silver bamboo or white bamboo, is a member of the palms family (Arecaceae).

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and feathery, light green foliage that gives it a soft, elegant appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cylicodaphne pallida is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
