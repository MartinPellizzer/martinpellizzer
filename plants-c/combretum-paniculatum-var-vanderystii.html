Combretum paniculatum var. vanderystii

Combretum paniculatum var. vanderystii, commonly known as the Vanderyst's flame bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that appear in dense, paniculate inflorescences. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
