Camellia bidoupensis

Camellia bidoupensis, commonly known as the Bidoup Mountain Camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and well-drained soil. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Camellia bidoupensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
