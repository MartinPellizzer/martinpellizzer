Caragana frutescens

Caragana frutescens, commonly known as the Siberian pea shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for reclamation projects.

Its root system helps prevent soil erosion, and it is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration.
