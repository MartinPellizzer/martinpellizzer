Crepidium crenatilobum

Crepidium crenatilobum, commonly known as the wrinkled-lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and along stream banks. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and have a distinctive, wrinkled lip that gives it its common name.

Crepidium crenatilobum is notable for its delicate appearance and its role in supporting local pollinators.
