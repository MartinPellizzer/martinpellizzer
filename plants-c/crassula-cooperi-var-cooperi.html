Crassula cooperi var. cooperi

Crassula cooperi var. cooperi, commonly known as the Cooper's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
