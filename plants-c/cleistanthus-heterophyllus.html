Cleistanthus heterophyllus

Cleistanthus heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable cleistanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, which can range from ovate to elliptic. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, a feature typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
