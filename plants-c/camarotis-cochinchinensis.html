Camarotis cochinchinensis

Camarotis cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its colorful blooms.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
