Chlorophytum fernandesii

Chlorophytum fernandesii, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, striped leaves that resemble a zebra's pattern. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Chlorophytum fernandesii is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
