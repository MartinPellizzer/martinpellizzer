Carex straminea f. echinodes

Carex straminea f. echinodes, commonly known as the spiny sedge, is a variant of the common sedge species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically light green in color. Its inflorescence consists of a cylindrical spike that bears small, three-lobed flowers.

This plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.
