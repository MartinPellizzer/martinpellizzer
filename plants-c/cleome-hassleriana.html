Cleome hassleriana

Cleome hassleriana, commonly known as spider flower, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces distinctive, elongated seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Cleome hassleriana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
