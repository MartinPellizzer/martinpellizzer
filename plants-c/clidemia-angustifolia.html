Clidemia angustifolia

Clidemia angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf false hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

Clidemia angustifolia is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
