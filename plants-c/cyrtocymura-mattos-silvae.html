Cyrtocymura mattos-silvae

Cyrtocymura mattos-silvae, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in humid and shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and green, and it produces clusters of small, colorful blooms.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
