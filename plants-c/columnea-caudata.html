Columnea caudata

Columnea caudata, commonly known as the trailing columnea, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its pendulous, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
