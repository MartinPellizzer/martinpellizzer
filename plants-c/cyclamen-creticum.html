Cyclamen creticum

Cyclamen creticum, commonly known as the Cretan cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the island of Crete.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, with spurred petals that are characteristic of the cyclamen genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers cool, shaded environments.

Cyclamen creticum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
