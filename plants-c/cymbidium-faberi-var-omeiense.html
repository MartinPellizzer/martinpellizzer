Cymbidium faberi var. omeiense

Cymbidium faberi var. omeiense, commonly known as the Omeiense Orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for winter gardening. It prefers cool, humid conditions and is often cultivated in shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
