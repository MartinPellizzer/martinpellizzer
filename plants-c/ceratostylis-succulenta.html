Ceratostylis succulenta

Ceratostylis succulenta, commonly known as the succulent orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a slight greenish tint. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
