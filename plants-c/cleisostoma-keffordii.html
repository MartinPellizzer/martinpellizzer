Cleisostoma keffordii

Cleisostoma keffordii, commonly known as the Kefford's cleisostoma, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Cleisostoma keffordii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
