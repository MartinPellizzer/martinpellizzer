Cyathea nitens

Cyathea nitens, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately divided fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Cyathea nitens is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological importance.
