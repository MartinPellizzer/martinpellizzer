Carya dabieshanensis

Carya dabieshanensis, commonly known as the Dabieshan hickory, is a deciduous tree native to China, specifically found in the Dabieshan mountain range.

It belongs to the walnut family (Juglandaceae) and is characterized by its compound leaves and edible nuts. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a broad, spreading crown and rough, fissured bark.

Its fruits are encased in a spiny husk and contain a single large, hard-shelled nut.
