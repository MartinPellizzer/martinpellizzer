Clematis florida f. plena

Clematis florida f. plena, commonly known as the double-flowered clematis, is a cultivar of the clematis vine that features showy, double blooms.

It produces large, fluffy flowers with numerous petals, giving it a lush, ornamental appearance. The plant is native to the eastern United States and thrives in partial to full sun. It has a climbing habit and requires a support structure to grow properly.

Its foliage is typically green and provides a nice backdrop to the vibrant flowers.
