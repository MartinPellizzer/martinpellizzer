Calanthe henryi

Calanthe henryi, commonly known as Henry's calanthe, is a species of orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its flowers are borne on tall, slender stems and are often clustered in racemes.

Calanthe henryi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
