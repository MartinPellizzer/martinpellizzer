Chenopodium lanceolatum f. opizii

Chenopodium lanceolatum f. opizii, commonly known as the Opiz's goosefoot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its lanceolate leaves and small, greenish flowers. The plant typically thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is notable for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
