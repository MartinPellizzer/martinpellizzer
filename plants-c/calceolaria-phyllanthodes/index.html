Calceolaria Phyllanthodes

Mexican hat, scientifically known as Calceolaria phyllanthodes, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family native to Mexico. This vibrant flowering plant is celebrated for its unique, hat-like blooms that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. With its delicate petals and contrasting dark centers, it captivates gardeners and botanists alike. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile choice for both containers and garden beds. Its ornamental value and ease of care have made it a popular choice in modern horticulture.
