Crotalaria burkeana

Crotalaria burkeana, commonly known as the golden chain bean, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in long, drooping racemes. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces distinctive pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Crotalaria burkeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
