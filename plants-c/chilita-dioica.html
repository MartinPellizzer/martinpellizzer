Chilita dioica

Chilita dioica, commonly known as the male and female flowering plant, is a dioecious species, meaning individual plants are either male or female.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is native to certain regions of South America. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, with the male and female flowers occurring on separate plants.

It is often found in moist, shady environments and has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties.
