Calophyllum floribundum

Calophyllum floribundum, commonly known as the tropical beauty tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Calophyllaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a whitish, fleshy aril. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The tree thrives in humid, warm climates and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
