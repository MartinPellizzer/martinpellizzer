Cyathea exilis

Cyathea exilis, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that supports its growth and helps it thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to several meters.

Cyathea exilis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add a tropical aesthetic to gardens.
