Cypripedium barbatum var. superbum

Cypripedium barbatum var. superbum, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a striking orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch and vivid pink or red hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist woodlands and thrives in acidic soils. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single, fragrant flower per stem.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often protected in its native habitats.
