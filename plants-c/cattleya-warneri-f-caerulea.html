Cattleya warneri f. caerulea

Cattleya warneri f. caerulea, commonly known as the Blue Warner Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking blue-violet flower petals.

Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It features a sturdy, erect pseudobulb and produces large, showy blooms that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter. The flowers have a velvety texture and emit a subtle fragrance, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Its unique coloration and elegant appearance contribute to its status as a sought-after ornamental plant.
