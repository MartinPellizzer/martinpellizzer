Calandrinia oblonga

Calandrinia oblonga, commonly known as the narrowleaf portulaca, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water, and its stems are often succulent.

Calandrinia oblonga is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
