Colchicum fharii subsp. gibraltaricum

Colchicum fharii subsp.

gibraltaricum, commonly known as the Gibraltar autumn crocus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Colchicaceae. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and parts of North Africa, including Gibraltar.

The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers with prominent yellow stamens during late summer to autumn. It has lance-shaped leaves that are typically basal and covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
