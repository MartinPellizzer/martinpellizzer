Cogswellia jaredii

Cogswellia jaredii, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Crassula ovata, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring or summer months. It is drought-tolerant and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cogswellia jaredii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
