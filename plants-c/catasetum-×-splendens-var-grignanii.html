Catasetum × splendens var. grignanii

Catasetum × splendens var. grignanii, commonly known as the Grignan Catasetum, is a hybrid orchid native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often bicolor flowers that come in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of showy, asymmetrical flowers with a distinctive lip.

The species is valued in horticulture for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
