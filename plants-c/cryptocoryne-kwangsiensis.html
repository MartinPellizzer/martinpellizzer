Cryptocoryne kwangsiensis

Cryptocoryne kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, elongated leaves that are often ribbed and have a waxy texture. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and marshes. Its rhizome system allows it to anchor itself in the substrate while absorbing nutrients from the water.

Cryptocoryne kwangsiensis is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to improve water quality.
