Crassula socialis

Crassula socialis, commonly known as the "Social Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water.

Crassula socialis is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.
