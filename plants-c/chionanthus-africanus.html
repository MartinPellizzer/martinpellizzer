Chionanthus africanus

Chionanthus africanus, commonly known as the African snowbell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
