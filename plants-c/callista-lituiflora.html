Callista lituiflora

Callista lituiflora, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing form and features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has hairy, grey-green leaves that are adapted to withstand arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Callista lituiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
