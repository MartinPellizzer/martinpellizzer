Cosmianthemum obtusifolium

Cosmianthemum obtusifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf cosmos, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers bloom throughout the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
