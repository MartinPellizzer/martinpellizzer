Combretum tetrandrum subsp. tetrandrum

Combretum tetrandrum subsp.

tetrandrum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season.

The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The bark is smooth and can be reddish or greyish, and the plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
