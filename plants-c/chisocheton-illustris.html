Chisocheton illustris

Chisocheton illustris, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vivid display during the flowering season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Chisocheton illustris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
