Cleistocalyx nicobaricus

Cleistocalyx nicobaricus, commonly known as the Nicobar nut or white nut, is a flowering plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically borne in clusters, and it is notable for its edible, hard-shelled nuts.
