Clerodendrum bequaertii var. debeerstii

Clerodendrum bequaertii var. debeerstii, commonly known as the Bequerst's Clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting yellow throats. The plant produces small, green, oval-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a glossy texture.
