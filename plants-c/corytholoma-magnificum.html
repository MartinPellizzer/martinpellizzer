Corytholoma magnificum

Corytholoma magnificum, commonly known as the royal crown fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, fan-shaped fronds.

Corytholoma magnificum is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of indoor spaces.
