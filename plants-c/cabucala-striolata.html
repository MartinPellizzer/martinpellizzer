Cabucala striolata

Cabucala striolata, commonly known as the striped orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that often display distinct stripes or patterns. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
