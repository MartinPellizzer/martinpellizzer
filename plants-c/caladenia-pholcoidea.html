Caladenia pholcoidea

Caladenia pholcoidea, commonly known as the Spiny Spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils within dry, open habitats. The plant features a single, erect stem with a single, showy pink or purple flower that resembles a spider. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the autumn season.

The species is characterized by its unique floral structure and is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.
