Cereus gaumeri

Cereus gaumeri, commonly known as the Christmas star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stem and clusters of vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant has sharp, cylindrical spines and is adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a striking addition to any collection.
