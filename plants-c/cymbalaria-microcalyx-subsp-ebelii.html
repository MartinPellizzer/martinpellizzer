Cymbalaria microcalyx subsp. ebelii

Cymbalaria microcalyx subsp.

ebelii, commonly known as the Ebel's figwart, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in hue.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
