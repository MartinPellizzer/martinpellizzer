Crassula rotundifolia

Crassula rotundifolia, commonly known as the Round-leaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are circular in shape, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula rotundifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
