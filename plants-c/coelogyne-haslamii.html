Coelogyne haslamii

Coelogyne haslamii, commonly known as the Haslam's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with several leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, adding ornamental value to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland or shaded garden settings.

Coelogyne haslamii is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
