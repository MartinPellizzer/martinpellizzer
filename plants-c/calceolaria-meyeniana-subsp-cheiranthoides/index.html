Calceolaria Meyeniana Subsp. Cheiranthoides

Calceolaria meyeniana subsp. cheiranthoides is a striking member of the Calceolaria genus known for its unique, pouch-like flowers. Native to the high Andes of South America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments with high altitudes. Its vibrant colors and intricate flower structure make it a favorite among horticulturists and botanists alike. The subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in controlled settings. Understanding its ecological role and cultivation needs is key to preserving its beauty in both natural and garden habitats.
