Coelogyne modesta

Coelogyne modesta, commonly known as the modest orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining potting media and regular watering during its growing season.

Coelogyne modesta is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant floral display.
