Codiaeum eyrei

Codiaeum eyrei, commonly known as the flame maple or red leaf croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright red to orange and yellow, depending on the variety. The plant has heart-shaped to lanceolate leaves that are often variegated with patterns of white, yellow, and red. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

Codiaeum eyrei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant and long-lasting leaf colors.
