Camellia mileensis

Camellia mileensis, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a distinctive yellow or orange center. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia mileensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
