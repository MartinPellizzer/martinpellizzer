Cyphochilus collinus

Cyphochilus collinus, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks in montane forests. Cyphochilus collinus is a terrestrial orchid with a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.
