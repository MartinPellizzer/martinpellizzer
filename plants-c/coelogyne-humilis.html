Coelogyne humilis

Coelogyne humilis, commonly known as the dwarf orchid or the lowland orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and produces pseudobulbs that bear leaves and flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Coelogyne humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
