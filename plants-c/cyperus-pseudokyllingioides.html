Cyperus pseudokyllingioides

Cyperus pseudokyllingioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, triangular stems and small, clustered flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes and wetlands. Its leaves are typically narrow and grassy, and it produces spikelets that are often reddish in color.

Cyperus pseudokyllingioides plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms in its native ecosystems.
