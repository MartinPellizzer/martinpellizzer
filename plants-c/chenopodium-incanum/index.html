Chenopodium Incanum

Chenopodium incanum is a hardy plant native to arid and semi-arid regions. It is commonly known as the white goosefoot due to its distinctive leaf shape and color. This species is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in harsh environmental conditions. Its ability to survive in dry climates makes it an important part of local ecosystems. The plant has been used traditionally for various purposes, including food and medicinal applications.
