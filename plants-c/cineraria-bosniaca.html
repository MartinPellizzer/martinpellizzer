Cineraria bosniaca

Cineraria bosniaca, commonly known as the Bosnian cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually hairy and grayish-green, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cineraria bosniaca is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
