Cymbalaria pallida f. pallida

Cymbalaria pallida f. pallida, commonly known as the pale leaf figwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
