Chenopodium × zobelii f. hircinifolium

Chenopodium × zobelii f. hircinifolium, commonly known as the woolly goosefoot, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, woolly hairs. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowering stems produce small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The species is notable for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
