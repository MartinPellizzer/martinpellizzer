Cliffortia ferruginea var. latifolia

Cliffortia ferruginea var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf sandflora, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along coastal areas and cliffs. It is adapted to coastal conditions, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.

This variety is distinguished by its broader leaves compared to the typical form of Cliffortia ferruginea.
