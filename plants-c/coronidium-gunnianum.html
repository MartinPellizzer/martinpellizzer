Coronidium gunnianum

Coronidium gunnianum, commonly known as the Mount Lofty mountain lily, is an endemic flowering plant native to South Australia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single, showy flower per stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species.
