Crassula quadrifaria subsp. quadrifaria

Crassula quadrifaria subsp.

quadrifaria, commonly known as the Four-leaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features dense clusters of fleshy, thick, and somewhat crinkled leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring and summer seasons. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soil make it a versatile and low-maintenance ornamental plant.
