Carex ouensanensis

Carex ouensanensis, commonly known as the Ouenza sedge, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, particularly parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grass-like leaves and spike-like flower clusters. It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils.

Carex ouensanensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
