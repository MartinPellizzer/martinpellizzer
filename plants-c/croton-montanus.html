Croton montanus

Croton montanus, commonly known as the mountain croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from yellow to red and orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough, textured surface.

Croton montanus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
