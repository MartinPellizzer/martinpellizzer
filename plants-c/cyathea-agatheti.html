Cyathea agatheti

Cyathea agatheti, commonly known as the Agatheti tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, making it a long-lived species. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its fronds, which are used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
