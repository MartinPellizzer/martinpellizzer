Cereus × jusbertii

Cereus × jusbertii, commonly known as the hybrid night blooming cereus, is a cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are typically covered in spiny areoles. The plant produces large, showy flowers that open at night, often in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
