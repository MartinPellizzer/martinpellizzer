Colchicum knersvlaktense

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking, violet-blue flowers during the winter months. The plant belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is characterized by its erect, leafless stems and showy inflorescences. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat.
