Cirsium compactum

Cirsium compactum, commonly known as the dwarf thistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial species that typically grows in grasslands, woodlands, and disturbed soils. The plant features spiny, gray-green leaves and produces purple to pinkish flowers in late summer. Its compact growth habit and hardy nature make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other thistle species.
