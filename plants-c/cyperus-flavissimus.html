Cyperus flavissimus

Cyperus flavissimus, commonly known as the golden flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in dense spikes. It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in marshes, ponds, and along waterways.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
