Cordia mhaya

Cordia mhaya, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its white, woolly flowers that bloom in spherical clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval or elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and savanna regions.

Cordia mhaya is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
