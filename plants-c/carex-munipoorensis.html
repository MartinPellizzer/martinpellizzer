Carex munipoorensis

Carex munipoorensis, commonly known as the Munipoor Island sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the island of Munipoor in the Andaman Islands, India, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Carex munipoorensis is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
