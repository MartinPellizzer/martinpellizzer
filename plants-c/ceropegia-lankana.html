Ceropegia lankana

Ceropegia lankana, commonly known as the Lankana milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tuberous roots. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, attracting specific pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
