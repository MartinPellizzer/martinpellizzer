Cineraria purpurascens

Cineraria purpurascens, commonly known as the purple cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has velvety, dark green leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Cineraria purpurascens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
