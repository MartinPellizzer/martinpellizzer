Cyperus purpureoglandulosus

Cyperus purpureoglandulosus, commonly known as purple glandular flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive purple or reddish coloration on the underside.

The inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are usually green or reddish in color.
