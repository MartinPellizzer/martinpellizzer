Ceratostylis rubra

Ceratostylis rubra, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it is cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers usually bloom in shades of pink and red, making it a striking addition to botanical gardens.
