Cyperus sonderi

Cyperus sonderi, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along water bodies.

Cyperus sonderi is adaptable and can grow in a variety of aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
