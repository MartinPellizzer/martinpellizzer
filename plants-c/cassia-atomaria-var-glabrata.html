Cassia atomaria var. glabrata

Cassia atomaria var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth cassia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems and bipinnate leaves that are glabrous, or smooth to the touch. The plant produces bright yellow inflorescences composed of numerous small, fragrant flowers. Its seeds are encased in slender, elongated pods that split open when mature.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
