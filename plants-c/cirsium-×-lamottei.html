Cirsium × lamottei

Cirsium × lamottei, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Cirsium alpinum and Cirsium vulgare, both of which are native to Europe. The plant typically grows to a height of 60–120 cm, featuring robust, spiny stems and large, velvety leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are purple to pink, forming dense clusters at the top of the plant, and it blooms from late summer to early autumn.

Cirsium × lamottei is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, and it is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
