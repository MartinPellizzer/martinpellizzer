Cynoglossum coeruleum var. winkleri

Cynoglossum coeruleum var. winkleri, commonly known as blue mouse ear, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants.
