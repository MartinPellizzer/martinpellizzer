Croton sebiferum

Croton sebiferum, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored foliage that includes shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a waxy texture.

Croton sebiferum is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
