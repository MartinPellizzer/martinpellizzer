Croton spiraeifolius

Croton spiraeifolius, commonly known as the spiral-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally arranged leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
