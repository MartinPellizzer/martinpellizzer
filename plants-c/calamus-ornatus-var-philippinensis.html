Calamus ornatus var. philippinensis

Calamus ornatus var. philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Aroidaceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its tall, slender rhizomes and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white spadices that are typical of the genus Calamus. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in lowland and montane forests.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
