Canna jacquinii

Canna jacquinii, commonly known as the red banana, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, banana-like leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in summer, adding bold color to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Canna jacquinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm climates.
