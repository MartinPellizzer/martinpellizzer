Crucifera maritima

Crucifera maritima, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and economically important crops. The plant is characterized by its bright green, fern-like leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in salty, sandy soils and is often found along shorelines and tidal zones.

Sea rocket is known for its ability to tolerate harsh maritime conditions and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
