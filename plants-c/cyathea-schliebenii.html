Cyathea schliebenii

Cyathea schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its massive, compound leaves, which can reach several meters in length.

Cyathea schliebenii plays a significant role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to forest structure.
