Calathea longibracteata

Calathea longibracteata, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often display shades of green, silver, and pink. The leaves have a unique pattern of parallel veins, which is a distinguishing feature of the genus Calathea. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

The plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
