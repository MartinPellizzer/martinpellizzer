Cyperus acaulescens

Cyperus acaulescens, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded environments. The plant has a slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers. It lacks a distinct stem, with the leaves arising directly from the base.

Cyperus acaulescens is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.
