Cytisogenista patens

Cytisogenista patens, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive silvery foliage.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, with a broad, rounded crown. The leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple small leaflets arranged in a feathery pattern. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

The plant is native to parts of Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
