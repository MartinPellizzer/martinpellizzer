Callicarpa peii

Callicarpa peii, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright purple berries that appear in late summer to early autumn. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The berries are not only visually striking but also attract birds and other wildlife.
