Carex uzoni

Carex uzoni, commonly known as Uzon sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to the Russian Far East and is typically found in wetland or marshy environments. The plant features grass-like leaves that are often dark green and have a smooth texture. Its flowering stems produce small, clustered spikes of pale green to brownish flowers.

Carex uzoni is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
