Callista comata

Callista comata, commonly known as the silky callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its flowers bloom in spring and attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Callista comata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
