Croton vaccinioides

Croton vaccinioides, commonly known as the waxleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. Its growth habit is bushy, with multiple stems emerging from the base.

Croton vaccinioides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
