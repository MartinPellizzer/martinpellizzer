Cactus erectacanthus

Cactus erectacanthus, commonly known as the erect spiny cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems covered in sharp, spiny areoles. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh desert conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing at the tips of the stems during the warmer months.

The species is native to parts of North America, particularly the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.
