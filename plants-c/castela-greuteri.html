Castela greuteri

Castela greuteri, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, seed-like fruits known as cypsela.

Castela greuteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
