Crassula nealeana

Crassula nealeana, commonly known as the Neale's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, rounded leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
