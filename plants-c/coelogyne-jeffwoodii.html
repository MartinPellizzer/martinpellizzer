Coelogyne jeffwoodii

Coelogyne jeffwoodii, commonly known as the Jeffwood's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant, and often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces inflorescences with multiple blooms.

Coelogyne jeffwoodii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
