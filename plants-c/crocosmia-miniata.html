Crocosmia miniata

Crocosmia miniata, commonly known as the dwarf flame violet, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant orange-red flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped blooms in late summer. Crocosmia miniata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its compact size and colorful flowers make it an excellent addition to borders and rock gardens.
