Cnicus norrisii

Cnicus norrisii, commonly known as Norris' thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a rough, hairy texture, and its flowers are yellow, forming a dense cluster at the top of the stem.

Norris' thistle is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
