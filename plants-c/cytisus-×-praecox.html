Cytisus × praecox

Cytisus × praecox, commonly known as the early flowering broom, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-stemmed growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom early in the spring. The plant produces trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant in temperate climates.

Cytisus × praecox is often used in landscaping for its attractive foliage and long-lasting floral display.
