Calamagrostis epigejos var. reichenbachiana

Calamagrostis epigejos var. reichenbachiana, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-green foliage that turns coppery in the fall. The plant produces tall, slender culms with feathery panicles that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
