Citrus hongheensis

Citrus hongheensis, commonly known as the Honghe citrus or Yunnan citrus, is a species native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It is a small evergreen tree characterized by its fragrant, sour citrus fruits. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruits are typically round or oval, with a thick, leathery rind and a high concentration of essential oils.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential use in the citrus industry.
