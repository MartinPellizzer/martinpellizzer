Cleisostoma sagittatum

Cleisostoma sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features pendant, fleshy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive arrowhead-shaped lip. The plant has thick, fleshy roots adapted for water storage, typical of epiphytic species. Its leaves are strap-like and often clustered at the base of the plant.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.
