Carex baviensis

Carex baviensis, commonly known as the Baviensian sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and is typically found in wetland or moist environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and filter water.
