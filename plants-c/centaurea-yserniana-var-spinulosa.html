Centaurea yserniana var. spinulosa

Centaurea yserniana var. spinulosa, commonly known as the Ysern's thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny, bristly foliage and striking purple to red flower heads that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped with rough textures, and the flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local pollinators.
