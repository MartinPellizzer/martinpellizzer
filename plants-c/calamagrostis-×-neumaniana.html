Calamagrostis × neumaniana

Calamagrostis × neumaniana, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a hybrid grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and striking silver-gray foliage that contrasts with its deep green leaves. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, attractive clumps.

The plant produces feathery plumes in the fall, adding visual interest to gardens and natural areas.
