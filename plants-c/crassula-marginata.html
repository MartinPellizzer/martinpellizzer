Crassula marginata

Crassula marginata, commonly known as the Christmas Chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula marginata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
