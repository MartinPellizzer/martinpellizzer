Castanopsis ninbienensis

Castanopsis ninbienensis, commonly known as the Ninh Binh chestnut.

It is a deciduous tree native to Vietnam, belonging to the Fagaceae family. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that contain one or two seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated margin.

Castanopsis ninbienensis is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in lowland and montane forest areas.
