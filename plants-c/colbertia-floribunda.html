Colbertia floribunda

Colbertia floribunda, commonly known as the golden bell orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces a dense cluster of blooms during the warm seasons, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. Colbertia floribunda thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialty plant collections.
