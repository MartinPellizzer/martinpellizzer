Clematis jingdungensis

Clematis jingdungensis, commonly known as the Jingdung Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy flowers. The plant produces fragrant, usually white or pale blue flowers with five to eight petals. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small leaflets, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
