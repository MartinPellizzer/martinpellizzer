Centaurea sonchifolia var. oscilans

Centaurea sonchifolia var. oscilans, commonly known as the wavy bluet, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its blue to purple flowers and slender, branching stems. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in open habitats across Europe and parts of Asia.
