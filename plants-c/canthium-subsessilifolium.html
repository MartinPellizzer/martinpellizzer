Canthium subsessilifolium

Canthium subsessilifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with simple, alternate leaves that are usually narrow and sessile. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, a unique structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
