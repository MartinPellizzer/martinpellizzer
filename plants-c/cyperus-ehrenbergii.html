Cyperus ehrenbergii

Cyperus ehrenbergii, commonly known as the spiny flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower spikes that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Cyperus ehrenbergii is characterized by its spiny bracts that protect its flowering structures.
