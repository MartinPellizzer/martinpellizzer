Cylichnanthus ciliatus

Cylichnanthus ciliatus, commonly known as the silky tree or silky hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, silky-hairy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Cylichnanthus ciliatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
