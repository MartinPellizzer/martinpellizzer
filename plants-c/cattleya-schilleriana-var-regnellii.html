Cattleya schilleriana var. regnellii

Cattleya schilleriana var. regnellii, commonly known as the Schiller's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant produces pendulous blooms on sturdy stems, often with a fragrant scent. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, arranged in a fan shape, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral characteristics.
