Camellia andrefrancisii

Camellia andrefrancisii, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy, camellia-like flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is typically evergreen and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

Camellia andrefrancisii is also valued for its historical and cultural significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
