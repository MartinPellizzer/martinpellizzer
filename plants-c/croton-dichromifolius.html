Croton dichromifolius

Croton dichromifolius, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored foliage with shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Croton dichromifolius is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf patterns and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
