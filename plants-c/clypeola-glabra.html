Clypeola glabra

Clypeola glabra, commonly known as the smooth clypeola, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Clypeola glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
