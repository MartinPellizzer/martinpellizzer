Clidemia sessiliflora var. angustifolia

Clidemia sessiliflora var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
