Cryptocoryne moehlmannii

Cryptocoryne moehlmannii, commonly known as the Moehlmann's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that often have a waxy texture. This plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments. Its rhizome system helps it anchor to substrates and absorb nutrients efficiently.

Cryptocoryne moehlmannii is popular in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
