Cirsium yesoense

Cirsium yesoense, commonly known as the Yeso thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a robust root system and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
