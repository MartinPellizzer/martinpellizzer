Campium virens

Campium virens, commonly known as green campion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant that typically grows up to 60 cm in height. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Campium virens is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
