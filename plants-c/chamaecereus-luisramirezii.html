Chamaecereus luisramirezii

Chamaecereus luisramirezii, commonly known as the Luis Ramirez cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its rugged appearance. It is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
