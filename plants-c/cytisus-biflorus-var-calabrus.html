Cytisus biflorus var. calabrus

Cytisus biflorus var. calabrus, commonly known as the Calabrian broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, often spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and hardy nature.
