Clerodendrum hysteranthum

Clerodendrum hysteranthum, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to regions of India and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has opposite, simple, and often lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and can be reddish or brown in color.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
