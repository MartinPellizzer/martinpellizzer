Chrysanthemum yunnanense

Chrysanthemum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often found in mountainous regions with a temperate climate.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also have traditional uses in herbal medicine.
