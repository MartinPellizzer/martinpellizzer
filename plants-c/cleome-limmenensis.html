Cleome limmenensis

Cleome limmenensis, commonly known as the limmenensis spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spindly stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Cleome limmenensis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.

It prefers full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
