Cystopteris gigantea

Cystopteris gigantea, commonly known as the giant water fern, is a member of the family Pellaeaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Cystopteris gigantea plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and oxygen to aquatic ecosystems.
