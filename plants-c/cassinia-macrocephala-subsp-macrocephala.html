Cassinia macrocephala subsp. macrocephala

Cassinia macrocephala subsp.

macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed cassinia, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It features small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters, which are characteristic of the genus Cassinia.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This subspecies is often found in coastal and inland regions, contributing to local biodiversity.
