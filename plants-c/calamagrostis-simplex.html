Calamagrostis simplex

Calamagrostis simplex, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-blue foliage and erect, slender inflorescences that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, attractive clumps.

Calamagrostis simplex is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
