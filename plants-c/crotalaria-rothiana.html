Crotalaria rothiana

Crotalaria rothiana, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with palmate leaves and erect stems.
