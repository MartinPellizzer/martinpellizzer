Cattleya × neoschilleriana

Cattleya × neoschilleriana, commonly known as the Schiller's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species prized for its large, showy flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features long, arching stems with multiple blooms, each consisting of a vibrant, velvety pouch-like lip. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
