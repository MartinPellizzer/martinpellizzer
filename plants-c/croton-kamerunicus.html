Croton kamerunicus

Croton kamerunicus, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a variety of colors, ranging from green to reddish-brown. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically yellow or green, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Croton kamerunicus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
