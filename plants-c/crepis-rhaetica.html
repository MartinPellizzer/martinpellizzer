Crepis rhaetica

Crepis rhaetica, commonly known as the Rhaetian hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to the Alps and other mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. Crepis rhaetica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
