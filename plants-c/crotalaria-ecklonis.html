Crotalaria ecklonis

Crotalaria ecklonis, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant characterized by its erect, branching stems and palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom throughout the growing season. It is part of the legume family, Fabaceae, and has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen through its root nodules.

Crotalaria ecklonis is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
