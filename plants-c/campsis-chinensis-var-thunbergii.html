Campsis chinensis var. thunbergii

Campsis chinensis var. thunbergii, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree or Thunberg's trumpetflower, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in summer, attracting birds and insects. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces woody, capsule-like fruits that split open to release seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
