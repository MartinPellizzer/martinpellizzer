Ceropegia longirostris

Ceropegia longirostris, commonly known as the long-beaked ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species with fleshy, tuberous roots and thin, trailing stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white with purple or red markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia longirostris is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
