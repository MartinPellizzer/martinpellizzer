Cattleya elegantissima

Cattleya elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has thick, fleshy roots and grows as an epiphyte, often attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, and it produces fragrant blooms that can last for several weeks.

Cattleya elegantissima is prized for its beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
