Cryptocarya tawaensis

Cryptocarya tawaensis, commonly known as Tawa, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing seeds.

Cryptocarya tawaensis is often found in forest margins and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
