Chrysophyllum zimmermannii

Chrysophyllum zimmermannii, commonly known as the African star apple, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The tree produces edible, sweet, and juicy fruits with a star-shaped appearance, which are often consumed fresh or used in culinary applications.
