Cratoxylum cochinchinense var. calcareum

Cratoxylum cochinchinense var. calcareum, commonly known as the limestone cratoxylum, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in calcareous soils, which is reflected in its scientific name.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
