Canarium commune var. minor

Canarium commune var. minor, commonly known as the lesser candlenut, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and elliptical, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
