Cotyledon oreades

Cotyledon oreades, commonly known as the rock cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
