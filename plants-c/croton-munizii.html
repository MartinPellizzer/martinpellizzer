Croton munizii

Croton munizii, commonly known as the Muniz's croton, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton munizii typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.
