Ceropegia punctata

Ceropegia punctata, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
