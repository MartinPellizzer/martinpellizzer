Cucumis melo var. alboviridis

Cucumis melo var. alboviridis, commonly known as the white-fruited melon, is a variety of the muskmelon species native to warm climates.

It is characterized by its pale green to white rind and sweet, aromatic flesh. The plant typically grows as a trailing vine with palmately lobed leaves and yellow flowers. Its fruit develops from a single pistillate flower and is often harvested when fully mature for culinary use.

This variety is valued for its mild flavor and is cultivated in various regions for both fresh consumption and processing.
