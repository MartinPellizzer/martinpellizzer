Croton megistocarpus

Croton megistocarpus, commonly known as the giant croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and showy, orange-red flowers. The plant produces thick, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its striking appearance, it is cultivated as an ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
