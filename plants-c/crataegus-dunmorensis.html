Crataegus dunmorensis

Crataegus dunmorensis, commonly known as Dunmore hawthorn, is a species of shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny branches and clusters of white flowers that appear in the spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species.

Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soils in partial to full sunlight.
