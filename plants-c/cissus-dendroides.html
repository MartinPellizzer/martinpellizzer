Cissus dendroides

Cissus dendroides, commonly known as the bone plant or rock fig, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cissus dendroides is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
