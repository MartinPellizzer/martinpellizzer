Calamagrostis canescens f. geniculata

Calamagrostis canescens f. geniculata, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a ornamental grass characterized by its silvery-blue foliage and graceful, arching inflorescences.

It is a cool-season grass that thrives in moist to wet soils, making it suitable for wetland gardens or bog environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces plume-like flowers in late summer, adding visual interest to the landscape.

This cultivar is often used in water feature gardens due to its ability to tolerate standing water and its aesthetic appeal.
