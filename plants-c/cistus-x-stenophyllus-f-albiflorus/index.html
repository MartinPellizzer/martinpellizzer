Cistus × Stenophyllus F. Albiflorus

Cistus × stenophyllus f. albiflorus is a unique hybrid plant known for its striking white flowers. It belongs to the rockrose family and is native to the Mediterranean region. This plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, making it well-suited for coastal and arid environments. Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-resistant plants. The white blooms add a delicate and elegant touch to any landscape.
