Coccothrinax australis

Coccothrinax australis, commonly known as the Australian palm, is a species of palm native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters, with a slender trunk and a crown of pinnate leaves. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and scrublands. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces edible fruits that are consumed by various wildlife.
