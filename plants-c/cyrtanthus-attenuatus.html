Cyrtanthus attenuatus

Cyrtanthus attenuatus, commonly known as the slender bell-shaped flower.

It is a perennial plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.

Cyrtanthus attenuatus is known for its long blooming period, typically from late winter to early spring.
