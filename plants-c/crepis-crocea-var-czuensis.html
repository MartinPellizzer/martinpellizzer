Crepis crocea var. czuensis

Crepis crocea var. czuensis, commonly known as the Chinese yellow hawk's-beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate, rough, and often covered with fine hairs.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
