Craterostigma niamniamense

Craterostigma niamniamense, commonly known as the Niamniam rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its botanical features include linear leaves and a short, hairy stem, which help it conserve water in its arid native conditions.

Despite its beauty, it is a rare and less-studied species, making it of interest to botanists and conservationists.
