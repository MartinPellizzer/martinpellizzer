Cyclocodon truncatus

Cyclocodon truncatus, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils. Its fruit is a dry capsule that contains several seeds.

Cyclocodon truncatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
