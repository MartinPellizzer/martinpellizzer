Cucumis muriculatus

Cucumis muriculatus, commonly known as the spiny gourd, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a hairy texture.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
