Calanthe discolor var. divaricatipetala

Calanthe discolor var. divaricatipetala, commonly known as the variegated white calanthe, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
