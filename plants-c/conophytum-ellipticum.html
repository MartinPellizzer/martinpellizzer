Conophytum ellipticum

Conophytum ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptical conophytum, is a small, succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
