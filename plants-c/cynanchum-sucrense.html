Cynanchum sucrense

Cynanchum sucrense, commonly known as the Sucrenses milkweed, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is known for its milkweed species. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy surface and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers. Its milky sap contains latex, a characteristic common among milkweeds.

Cynanchum sucrense is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides in its native habitat.
