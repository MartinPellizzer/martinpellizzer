Crotalaria lebeckioides

Crotalaria lebeckioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved crotalaria, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that attract pollinators. Its seeds are enclosed in a pod and are often used in traditional medicine.

Crotalaria lebeckioides is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soils, making it a hardy species in various environments.
