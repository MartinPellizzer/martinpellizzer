Croton vogelianus

Croton vogelianus, commonly known as the Vogel's croton, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often features a mix of green, red, and yellow hues. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Croton vogelianus thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its leaves are typically thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.
