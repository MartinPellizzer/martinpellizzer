Cyrtochilum ringens

Cyrtochilum ringens, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth pattern, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette.

Cyrtochilum ringens thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
