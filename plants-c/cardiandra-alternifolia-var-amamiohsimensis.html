Cardiandra alternifolia var. amamiohsimensis

Cardiandra alternifolia var. amamiohsimensis, commonly known as the Amami Island flamebush, is a flowering plant native to the Amami Islands in Japan.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
