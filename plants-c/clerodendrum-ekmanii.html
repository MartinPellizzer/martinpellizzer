Clerodendrum ekmanii

Clerodendrum ekmanii, commonly known as the purple passionvine, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple to violet flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are often covered in fine, white pubescence.
