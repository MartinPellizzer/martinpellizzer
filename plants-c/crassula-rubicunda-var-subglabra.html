Crassula rubicunda var. subglabra

Crassula rubicunda var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are smooth and slightly rounded, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
