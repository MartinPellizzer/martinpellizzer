Coelogyne garrettii

Coelogyne garrettii, commonly known as the Garret's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters on the stem. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Coelogyne garrettii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
