Caryota speciosa

Caryota speciosa, commonly known as the umbrella palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that form a dense, umbrella-like canopy. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that eventually develop into clustered, edible fruits. Its rapid growth rate and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The trunk is slender and often has a fibrous, rough texture, adding to its distinctive visual appeal.
