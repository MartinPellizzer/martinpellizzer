Ceropegia rollae

Ceropegia rollae, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in response to sunlight. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, with a unique structure that attracts specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and decorative displays.
