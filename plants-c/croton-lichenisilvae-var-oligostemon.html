Croton lichenisilvae var. oligostemon

Croton lichenisilvae var. oligostemon, commonly known as the forest croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated leaves that display a range of colors including green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule containing numerous tiny seeds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
