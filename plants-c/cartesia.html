Cartesia

Cartesia, commonly known as the false heather, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its foliage consists of evergreen, needle-like leaves that provide a dense, ornamental appearance.
