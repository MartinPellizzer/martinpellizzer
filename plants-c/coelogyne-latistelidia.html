Coelogyne latistelidia

Coelogyne latistelidia, commonly known as the broad-leafed coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are broad at the base and taper towards the tip. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with red or brown markings. It typically grows as an epiphyte on trees, relying on air roots for moisture and nutrients.

Coelogyne latistelidia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
