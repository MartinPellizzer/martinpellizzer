Crotalaria kipilaensis

Crotalaria kipilaensis, commonly known as the Kipila velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces distinctive yellow flowers with prominent violet markings, and its leaves are typically trifoliate. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, open habitats.

Crotalaria kipilaensis is notable for its ornamental value and potential use in traditional medicine.
