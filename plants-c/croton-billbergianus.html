Croton billbergianus

Croton billbergianus, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that ranges from deep red to vibrant green and yellow. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with a velvety texture, and its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

The plant prefers warm, humid conditions and well-draining soil.
