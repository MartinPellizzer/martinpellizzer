Calathea ucayalina

Calathea ucayalina, commonly known as the Ucayali calathea, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, iridescent foliage. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with intricate patterns that change color in response to light and temperature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
