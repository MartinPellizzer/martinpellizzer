Cyperus Nanus Var. Subtenuis

Cyperus nanus var. subtenuis is a unique grass-like plant native to wetland environments. It is known for its slender, grass-like stems and small, clustered flowers. This variant of Cyperus nanus thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes and swamps. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it plays an important ecological role in maintaining wetland biodiversity.
