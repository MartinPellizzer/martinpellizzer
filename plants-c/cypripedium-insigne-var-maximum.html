Cypripedium insigne var. maximum

Cypripedium insigne var. maximum, commonly known as the large yellow lady's slipper, is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow pouch-shaped flower, which is highly specialized for attracting pollinators. The plant typically grows in cool, moist forest habitats and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are broad and leathery, and it produces a single, erect stem each year.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
