Centaurea fajvuschii

Centaurea fajvuschii, commonly known as the Fajvuschen's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains in Central Europe.

The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Centaurea fajvuschii is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
