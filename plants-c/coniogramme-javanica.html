Coniogramme javanica

Coniogramme javanica, commonly known as the Javanese fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clustered, pinnately compound leaves. Its foliage is characterized by a dark green color and a leathery texture.

Coniogramme javanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
