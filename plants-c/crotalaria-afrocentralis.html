Crotalaria afrocentralis

Crotalaria afrocentralis, commonly known as African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 1 to 2 meters in height, featuring compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. Its seeds are enclosed in hairy, spiny pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it adaptable to various soil types and climates.
