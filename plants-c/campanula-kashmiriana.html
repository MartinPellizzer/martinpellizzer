Campanula kashmiriana

Campanula kashmiriana, commonly known as Kashmir bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula kashmiriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate, bell-like blooms.
