Crinum verecundum

Crinum verecundum, commonly known as the shy lily, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces large, showy blooms that typically appear in summer, often in clusters at the top of tall, strap-like leaves. Crinum verecundum is known for its strong fragrance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
