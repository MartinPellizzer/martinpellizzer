Calonema pachychilum

Calonema pachychilum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rocky substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
