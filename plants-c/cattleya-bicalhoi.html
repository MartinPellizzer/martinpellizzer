Cattleya bicalhoi

Cattleya bicalhoi, commonly known as the Bicalhoi Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features long, arching inflorescences and is known for its ease of cultivation in warm, humid environments. It thrives in well-draining potting mix and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relatively straightforward care requirements.
