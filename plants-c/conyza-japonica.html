Conyza japonica

Conyza japonica, commonly known as Japanese fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a slender, branching stem covered with fine hairs and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers. The plant has opposite, serrated leaves that are covered in fine, sticky hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Conyza japonica is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through wind-dispersed seeds.
