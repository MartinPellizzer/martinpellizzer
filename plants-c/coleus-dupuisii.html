Coleus dupuisii

Coleus dupuisii, commonly known as the Madagascar coleus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and purple. Its foliage is often ornamental, featuring striking patterns and colors that make it a popular choice for gardens.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.
