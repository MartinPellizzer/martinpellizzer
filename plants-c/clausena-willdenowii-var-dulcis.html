Clausena willdenowii var. dulcis

Clausena willdenowii var. dulcis, commonly known as the sweet Clausena, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters, while the fruit is a smooth, spherical drupe with a sweet, edible pulp.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
