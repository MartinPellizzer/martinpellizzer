Copaiba kuntzei

Copaiba kuntzei, commonly known as yellow copaiba, is a species of tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elongated, elliptical leaves. The plant produces aromatic, resinous sap that has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically yellow or green, and it produces woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

Copaiba kuntzei plays an important ecological role in its habitat, contributing to biodiversity and providing resources for various wildlife species.
