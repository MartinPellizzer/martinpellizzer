Chomelia Grandifolia

Chomelia grandifolia is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers and unique foliage. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features broad, waxy leaves and clusters of bright yellow or orange blooms that appear throughout the growing season. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens. With proper care, Chomelia grandifolia can add a burst of color and life to any space.
