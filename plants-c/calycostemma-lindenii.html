Calycostemma lindenii

Calycostemma lindenii, commonly known as the Lindberg's calycostemma, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
