Cleistanthus myrianthus subsp. attenuatus

Cleistanthus myrianthus subsp.

attenuatus, commonly known as the slender cleistanthus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens in warmer climates.
