Cyperus strigosus var. robustior

Cyperus strigosus var. robustior, commonly known as the robust flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers that are typically brown or pale purple. The plant thrives in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas, swamps, or along waterways. It has a fibrous root system and can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in aquatic environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various wetland habitats.
