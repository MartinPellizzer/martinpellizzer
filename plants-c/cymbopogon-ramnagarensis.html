Cymbopogon ramnagarensis

Cymbopogon ramnagarensis, commonly known as the Ramnagare grass, is a species of perennial grass native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, linear leaves. This plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its growth habit makes it suitable for use in ornamental landscaping and erosion control.

Additionally, it may have potential applications in traditional medicine or as a forage crop.
