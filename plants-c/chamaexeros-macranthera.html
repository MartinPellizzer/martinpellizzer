Chamaexeros macranthera

Chamaexeros macranthera, commonly known as the large-flowered chamaexeros, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to pink. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a sprawling, bushy habit. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
