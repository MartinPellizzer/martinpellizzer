Coelogyne tuberculata

Coelogyne tuberculata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant has long, arching leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically yellow with red and brown markings, resembling a zigzag pattern.

Coelogyne tuberculata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
