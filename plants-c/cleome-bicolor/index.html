Cleome Bicolor

Cleome bicolor is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant, bi-color blooms. It belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is commonly found in warm climates around the world. This plant is popular among gardeners for its ease of care and eye-catching appearance. Its flowers typically feature a mix of red and yellow, creating a visually appealing contrast. Cleome bicolor is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
