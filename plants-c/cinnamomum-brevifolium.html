Cinnamomum brevifolium

Cinnamomum brevifolium, commonly known as the small-leaved cinnamon or Bornean cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark and small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers and oblong, dark purple berries. It is closely related to other cinnamon species and is valued for its essential oils and medicinal properties.

Its habitat typically includes lowland forests and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
