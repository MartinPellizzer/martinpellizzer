Callista stricklandiana

Callista stricklandiana, commonly known as the Strickland's callista, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shrubland and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Callista stricklandiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
