Cyclopia vogelii var. intermedia

Cyclopia vogelii var. intermedia, commonly known as the Cape goldbos, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and succulent, fleshy leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in coastal regions and is known for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
