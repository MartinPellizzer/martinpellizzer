Chionanthus pauciflorus var. evolutior

Chionanthus pauciflorus var. evolutior, commonly known as the Chinese fringeflower, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its showy, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are arranged in racemes and have a unique, cascading appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.
