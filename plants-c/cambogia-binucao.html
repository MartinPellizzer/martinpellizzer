Cambogia binucao

Cambogia binucao, commonly known as the Malabar gooseberry, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow, acidic fruits. The plant has simple, alternate leaves and produces small, white flowers. Its fruit is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
