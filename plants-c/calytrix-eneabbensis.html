Calytrix eneabbensis

Calytrix eneabbensis, commonly known as the Eneabba calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink to purple flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It is adapted to sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Calytrix eneabbensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
