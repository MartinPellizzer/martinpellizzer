Cotula perpusilla

Cotula perpusilla, commonly known as the common daisy or little daisy, is a small flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, yellow flowers with white ray florets, which bloom throughout the growing season.

Cotula perpusilla is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and ease of cultivation.
