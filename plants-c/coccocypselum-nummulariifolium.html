Coccocypselum nummulariifolium

Coccocypselum nummulariifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, globular shape with a dense covering of silver-gray hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water, and it is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.

Coccocypselum nummulariifolium is well-suited to rock gardens and xeriscaping, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil.
