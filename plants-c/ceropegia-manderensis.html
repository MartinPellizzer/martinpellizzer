Ceropegia manderensis

Ceropegia manderensis, commonly known as the Mander's ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tubular flowers. The plant produces small, pale yellow to greenish flowers that are often clustered in groups. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Ceropegia manderensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
