Citrus medica var. limon

Citrus medica var. limon, commonly known as the lemon.

This variety is a citrus fruit native to Asia and is characterized by its yellow, acidic fruit. The plant is a small evergreen tree with thick, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is rich in vitamin C and contains essential oils that contribute to its distinct aroma.

The botanical features of Citrus medica var. limon make it a valuable species in both culinary and medicinal applications.
