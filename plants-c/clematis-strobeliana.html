Clematis strobeliana

Clematis strobeliana, commonly known as the Strobel's Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to Central Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, often with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vigorous growth and ability to climb trellises or other supports.
