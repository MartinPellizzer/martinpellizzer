Culcasia lanceolata

Culcasia lanceolata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant thrives in shallow, still water and is often used in water gardens and ponds. Its roots help in filtering water, making it beneficial for aquatic ecosystems.

Culcasia lanceolata is also popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying properties.
