Cotyledon gibbiflora

Cotyledon gibbiflora, commonly known as the swollen cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the growing season, typically in spring or early summer. Its leaves are ribbed and can vary in color from green to reddish hues depending on light exposure.

Cotyledon gibbiflora is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering to thrive.
