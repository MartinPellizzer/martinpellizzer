Clerodendrum fugitans

Clerodendrum fugitans, commonly known as the wandering glorybower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous clusters of pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and a strong, spicy scent. Its stems are often woody and can become somewhat vine-like in growth habit.

Clerodendrum fugitans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often used in garden landscapes.
