Catalpa fargesii f. alba

Catalpa fargesii f. alba, commonly known as the white catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters during the summer months. The tree produces long, cylindrical seed pods that split open to release its winged seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
