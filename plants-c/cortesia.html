Cortesia

Cortesia, commonly known as the "flower of the sea," is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue or violet petals, which resemble the color of the ocean, and its delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found growing near the shoreline.
