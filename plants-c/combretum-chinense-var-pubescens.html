Combretum chinense var. pubescens

Combretum chinense var. pubescens, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered with fine pubescence, giving the plant a soft, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental planting in warm climates.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
