Citharexylum ulei

Citharexylum ulei, commonly known as the silky leaf citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are soft, velvety, and often have a silvery-green appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
