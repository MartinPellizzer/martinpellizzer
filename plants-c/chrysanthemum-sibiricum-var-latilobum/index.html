Chrysanthemum Sibiricum Var. Latilobum

Autumn chrysanthemum, scientifically known as Chrysanthemum sibiricum var. latilobum, is a hardy flowering plant native to eastern Asia. It is prized for its vibrant blooms that appear in late summer and autumn, adding color to gardens during the cooler months. This variety is particularly popular for its resilience and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions. Its delicate petals and compact growth make it a favorite among gardeners seeking seasonal beauty. With its long blooming period and low maintenance requirements, it remains a beloved choice for both novice and experienced gardeners alike.
