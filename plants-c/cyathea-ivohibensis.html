Cyathea ivohibensis

Cyathea ivohibensis, commonly known as the Ivohibena tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cyathea ivohibensis plays an important ecological role in its native forests, providing habitat and contributing to forest structure.
