Cypripedium japonicum var. glabrum

Cypripedium japonicum var. glabrum, commonly known as the Japanese saxifrage orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive pouch-like lip, typically in shades of white or pale pink. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
