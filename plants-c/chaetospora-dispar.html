Chaetospora dispar

Chaetospora dispar, commonly known as the hairy sponge spore.

It is a type of marine red alga belonging to the family Rhodophyceae. The plant is characterized by its branched, filamentous thallus with a gelatinous texture. It produces distinctive spores enclosed in a complex, sponge-like structure called a sorus.

This species is typically found in shallow coastal waters, where it plays a role in marine ecosystems.
