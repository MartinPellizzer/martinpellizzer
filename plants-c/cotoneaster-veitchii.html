Cotoneaster veitchii

Cotoneaster veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has a compact growth habit with dense, dark green foliage. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster veitchii is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun conditions.
