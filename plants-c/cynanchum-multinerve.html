Cynanchum multinerve

Cynanchum multinerve, commonly known as the many-veined milkweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds known for their medicinal and ecological significance. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins, and its flowers are clustered in umbels, producing a distinctive milky sap. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant's chemical composition includes various alkaloids, contributing to its traditional use in herbal medicine.
