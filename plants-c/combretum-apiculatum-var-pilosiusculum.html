Combretum apiculatum var. pilosiusculum

Combretum apiculatum var. pilosiusculum, commonly known as the silky combretum, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its small, oval leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or riverine habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
