Chlorophytum tripedale

Chlorophytum tripedale, commonly known as the snake plant, is a popular indoor plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with yellow or white markings. This plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering. Its botanical features include fibrous roots and a rosette growth pattern.

Chlorophytum tripedale is also noted for its air-purifying properties, making it a valuable addition to any indoor space.
