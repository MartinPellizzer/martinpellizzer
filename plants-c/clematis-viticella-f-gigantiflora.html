Clematis viticella f. gigantiflora

Clematis viticella f. gigantiflora, commonly known as the giant-flowered Clematis viticella, is a hybrid shrub native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and its ability to climb or trail along supports. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale blue, with a distinctive, slightly fragrant scent. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to the blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
