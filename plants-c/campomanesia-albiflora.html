Campomanesia albiflora

Campomanesia albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered campomanesia, is a small evergreen tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of white, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are edible and have a sweet, acidic flavor. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
