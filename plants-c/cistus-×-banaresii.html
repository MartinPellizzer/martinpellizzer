Cistus × banaresii

Cistus × banaresii, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments. Its flowers are typically visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological importance.

The species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its hardiness and attractive appearance.
