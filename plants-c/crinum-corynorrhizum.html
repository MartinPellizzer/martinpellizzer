Crinum corynorrhizum

Crinum corynorrhizum, commonly known as the African lily, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink, with a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are often blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its bulbs are toxic, so care should be taken when handling the plant.
