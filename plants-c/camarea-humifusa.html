Camarea humifusa

Camarea humifusa, commonly known as the ground orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. Camarea humifusa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
