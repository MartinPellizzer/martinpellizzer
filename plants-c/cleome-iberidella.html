Cleome iberidella

Cleome iberidella, commonly known as the Mexican spider flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, branching stems and hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble spider-like structures, giving it its common name. Cleome iberidella thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental plants.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
