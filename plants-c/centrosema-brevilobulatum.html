Centrosema brevilobulatum

Centrosema brevilobulatum, commonly known as small-lobed centrosema, is a leguminous plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with small, rounded lobes and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Centrosema brevilobulatum is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural systems.
