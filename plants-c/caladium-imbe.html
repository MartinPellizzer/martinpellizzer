Caladium imbe

Caladium imbe, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Caladium imbe is typically propagated through tuberous roots, which can be planted in spring for optimal growth.
