Crotalaria rotundicarinata

Crotalaria rotundicarinata, commonly known as the round-leaved cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, characterized by its pod-bearing fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators. Its seeds are encased in hard pods, and the plant is often used in traditional medicine.

Crotalaria rotundicarinata is also valued as a forage crop for livestock in some regions.
