Comaclinium

Comaclinium, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Comaclinium is native to various regions in the Americas and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
