Cyclopia cordifolia

Cyclopia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf cyclopia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cyclopia cordifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal medicine.
