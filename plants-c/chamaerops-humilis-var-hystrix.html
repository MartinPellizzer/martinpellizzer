Chamaerops humilis var. hystrix

Chamaerops humilis var. hystrix, commonly known as the European fan palm, is a hardy, cold-resistant palm native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its fan-shaped leaves, which are typically blue-green in color and have a distinctive hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into black, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact size and adaptability make it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
