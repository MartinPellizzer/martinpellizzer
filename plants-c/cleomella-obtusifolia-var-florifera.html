Cleomella obtusifolia var. florifera

Cleomella obtusifolia var. florifera, commonly known as the showy cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value comes from its clusters of delicate, papery petals and its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
