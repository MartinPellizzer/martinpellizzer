Clutia abyssinica var. ovalifolia

Clutia abyssinica var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the African clutia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
