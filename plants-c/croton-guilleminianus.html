Croton guilleminianus

Croton guilleminianus, commonly known as the Guilleminian croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central America and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often strikingly colored plants. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed segments, and its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the genus Croton. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.
