Cytisus Cinereus

Cytisus cinereus, also known as the silver broom, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of western europe. its slender stems and bright yellow flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. the plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various climates. its unique silvery foliage adds visual interest throughout the year. with its hardy nature and attractive appearance, cytisus cinereus is a versatile plant for both gardeners and landscapers.
