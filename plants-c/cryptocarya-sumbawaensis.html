Cryptocarya sumbawaensis

Cryptocarya sumbawaensis, commonly known as Sumbawa nut.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Indonesia, specifically found on the island of Sumbawa. The plant belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant flowers. It produces edible, woody fruits that contain a single seed, which is often used in traditional medicine.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
