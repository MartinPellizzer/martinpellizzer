Calathea wallisii

Calathea wallisii, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, patterned foliage that often changes color in response to light conditions. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with intricate veining that enhances its ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and foliage displays.
