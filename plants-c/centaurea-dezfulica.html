Centaurea dezfulica

Centaurea dezfulica, commonly known as the Dezful thistle, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing adaptations to dry environments. Its leaves are spiny and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a purple center.

Centaurea dezfulica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
