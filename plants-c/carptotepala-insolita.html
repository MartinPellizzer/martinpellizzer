Carptotepala insolita

Carptotepala insolita, commonly known as the rare desert orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

The plant has adapted to survive in extreme conditions with a deep root system and thick, waxy leaves that reduce water loss. Its blooming period occurs during brief periods of rainfall, making it a rare and ephemeral sight in its natural habitat.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Carptotepala insolita is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation areas.
