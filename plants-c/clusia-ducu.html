Clusia ducu

Clusia ducu, commonly known as the evergreen shrub or broadleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. Clusia ducu is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
