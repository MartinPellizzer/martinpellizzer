Cubeba cubeba

Cubeba cubeba, commonly known as cubeb or Java pepper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae and is characterized by its small, dark purple berries. The plant produces a pungent essential oil from its dried fruits, which is used in traditional medicine and perfumery. Its leaves are often used in cooking, particularly in Indonesian and Malaysian cuisines.
