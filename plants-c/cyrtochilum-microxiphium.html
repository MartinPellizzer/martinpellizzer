Cyrtochilum microxiphium

Cyrtochilum microxiphium, commonly known as the narrow-sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale green or white flowers with a distinctive, narrow lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
