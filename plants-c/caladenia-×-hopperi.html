Caladenia × hopperi

Caladenia × hopperi, commonly known as the Hopper's spider orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces a single, erect stem that bears several showy, lance-shaped flowers. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in open habitats with adequate sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
