Caladenia carnea var. subulata

Caladenia carnea var. subulata, commonly known as the slender spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It features a single, slender stem that bears a single, elongated flower with a distinctive pinkish-red coloration. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a unique, spider-like flower structure with a narrow, hooded labellum. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is typically found in open habitats such as heathlands and grasslands.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
