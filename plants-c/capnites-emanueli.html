Capnites emanueli

Capnites emanueli, commonly known as the Emanuel's caper.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Capparaceae family, characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and pungent, edible leaves. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in coastal areas.
