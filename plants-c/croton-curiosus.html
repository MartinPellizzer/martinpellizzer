Croton curiosus

Croton curiosus, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems that help it climb or trail along the ground. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. Its leaves are typically ovate with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Croton curiosus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
