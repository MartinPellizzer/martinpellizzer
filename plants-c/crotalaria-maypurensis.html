Crotalaria maypurensis

Crotalaria maypurensis, commonly known as the Mayapur crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in both open fields and forest edges. It has a short lifecycle and is often found in humid, warm climates.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods and can remain viable in the soil for several years.
