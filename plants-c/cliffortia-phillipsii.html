Cliffortia phillipsii

Cliffortia phillipsii, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with dense, evergreen foliage that thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the winter months. It is adapted to coastal and mountainous regions, often growing on cliffs or slopes.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
