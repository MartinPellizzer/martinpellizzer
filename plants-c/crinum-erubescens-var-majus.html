Crinum erubescens var. majus

Crinum erubescens var. majus, commonly known as the showy crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant is known for its strong, spicy fragrance, which is particularly noticeable in the evening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
