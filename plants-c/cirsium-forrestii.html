Cirsium forrestii

Cirsium forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its foliage is covered in stiff, bristly hairs, which contribute to its defensive mechanisms against herbivores.

Cirsium forrestii is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
