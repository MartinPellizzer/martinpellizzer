Colubrina ruffa

Colubrina ruffa, commonly known as the Florida colubrina, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in sandy soils along coastal areas. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Colubrina ruffa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
