Cirsium echinopifolium

Cirsium echinopifolium, commonly known as the prickly centaury, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny, lobed leaves and produces purple to pinkish flowers. It thrives in rocky and dry habitats, often found in mountainous regions.
