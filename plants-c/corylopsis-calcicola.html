Corylopsis calcicola

Corylopsis calcicola, commonly known as the limestone cotoneaster, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant thrives in calcareous soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions. Its leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, providing structure throughout the year.

Corylopsis calcicola is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
