Collinsia sparsiflora

Collinsia sparsiflora, commonly known as the slender fairybell, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a bluish tinge. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Collinsia sparsiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
