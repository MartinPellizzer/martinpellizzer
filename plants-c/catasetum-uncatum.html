Catasetum uncatum

Catasetum uncatum, commonly known as the orchid with a unique floral structure, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that feature a specialized column, a feature common in orchids but particularly pronounced in this species. The plant produces long, slender stems that bear clusters of flowers, often in shades of yellow and green.
