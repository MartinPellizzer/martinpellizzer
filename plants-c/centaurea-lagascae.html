Centaurea lagascae

Centaurea lagascae, commonly known as the Lagasce thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea lagascae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
