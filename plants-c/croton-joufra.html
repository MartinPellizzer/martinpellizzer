Croton joufra

Croton joufra, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the plant a dramatic appearance. The leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a glossy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
