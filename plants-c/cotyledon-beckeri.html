Cotyledon beckeri

Cotyledon beckeri, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, waxy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a unique appearance. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Cotyledon beckeri is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
