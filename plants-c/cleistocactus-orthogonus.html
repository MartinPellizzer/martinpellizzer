Cleistocactus orthogonus

Cleistocactus orthogonus, commonly known as the Mexican sunburst cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, thick stem and a distinctive yellowish-green color. The plant produces yellow flowers that open only at night, a characteristic known as nocturnal flowering. It has spines that are typically reddish-brown and can be quite numerous.

Cleistocactus orthogonus is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
