Commelina rhodesica

Commelina rhodesica, commonly known as Rhodesian spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Commelina genus in the family Commelinaceae. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers with three prominent petals and a distinctive three-lobed bract. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants.
