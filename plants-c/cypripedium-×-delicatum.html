Cypripedium × delicatum

Cypripedium × delicatum, commonly known as the fragrant lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper, which is a key feature in its pollination strategy. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest habitats and has a long, slender rhizome. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a strong, sweet fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
