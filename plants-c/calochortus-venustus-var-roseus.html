Calochortus venustus var. roseus

Calochortus venustus var. roseus, commonly known as the roseate mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to California and Baja California.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to rose-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats such as chaparral and coastal sage scrub. Its bulbs are adapted to survive in arid conditions, and it blooms from spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, delicate flowers.
