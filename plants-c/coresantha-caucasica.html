Coresantha caucasica

Coresantha caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in loose, radiate inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and rocky areas.
