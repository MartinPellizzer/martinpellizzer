Crassula pedicellosa

Crassula pedicellosa, commonly known as the "Lemon Lime Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bright green leaves with a slight yellowish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Crassula pedicellosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
