Cephalanthera × taubenheimii

Cephalanthera × taubenheimii, commonly known as the hybrid green orchid, is a fascinating member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Cephalanthera damasonium and Cephalanthera falcata, exhibiting a mix of characteristics from both parent species. The plant is notable for its striking greenish-yellow flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in woodland areas across Europe.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in many regions.
