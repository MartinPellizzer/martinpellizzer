Colea rubra

Colea rubra, commonly known as the red coleus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red, purple, and variegated leaves, which are often used in ornamental gardening. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in whorled clusters.

Colea rubra thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
