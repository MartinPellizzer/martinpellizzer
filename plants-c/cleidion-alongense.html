Cleidion alongense

Cleidion alongense, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
