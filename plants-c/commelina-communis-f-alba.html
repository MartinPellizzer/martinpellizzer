Commelina communis f. alba

Commelina communis f. alba, commonly known as the white commelina, is a flowering plant belonging to the Commelinaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers that contrast with its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

The white petals and overall compact growth make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
