Chlorophytum fuchsianum

Chlorophytum fuchsianum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features long, upright leaves that are typically variegated with green and white or yellow markings. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant.

Chlorophytum fuchsianum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
