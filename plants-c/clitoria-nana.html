Clitoria nana

Clitoria nana, commonly known as the dwarf butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, featuring heart-shaped leaves and clusters of blue or purple pea-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in tropical climates with well-drained soil. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant colors and compact size.

Clitoria nana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
