Cyrtandra × acuminifolia

Cyrtandra × acuminifolia, commonly known as the hybrid Cyrtandra, is a flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra pedata and Cyrtandra acuminata, both native to tropical regions of the Pacific. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
