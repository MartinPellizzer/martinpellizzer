Cliffortia tenuis

Cliffortia tenuis, commonly known as the narrowleaf cliffortia, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to its habitat. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in its arid environment.

Cliffortia tenuis is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in full sun.
