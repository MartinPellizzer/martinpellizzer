Crotalaria distans subsp. macaulayae

Crotalaria distans subsp.

macaulayae, commonly known as the Macaulay's spurred cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive spurred flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has palmately compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands due to its adaptability to various soil types.
