Ceradenia congesta

Ceradenia congesta, commonly known as the clustered ceradenia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

Ceradenia congesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
