Conophytum koubergense

Conophytum koubergense, commonly known as the Kouberg sunstone, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short, erect stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ability to store water in its leaves make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
