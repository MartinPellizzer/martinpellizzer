Carex preslii

Carex preslii, commonly known as Preslii sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, rushes, and similar plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, ditches, and along stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in early summer.

Carex preslii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
