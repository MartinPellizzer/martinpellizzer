Cyrtomium fortunei var. atropunctatum

Cyrtomium fortunei var. atropunctatum, commonly known as the black dot holly fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to China.

It features dark green, leathery, bipinnate fronds with distinctive black dots along the veins, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage. It is often used in horticulture for its compact size and elegant appearance.

The varietal form atropunctatum is distinguished by its more intense coloration and unique spotting pattern compared to the typical variety.
