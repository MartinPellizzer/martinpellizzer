Campanula rapunculoides var. trachelioides

Campanula rapunculoides var. trachelioides, commonly known as the blue bell bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features striking blue to purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable habitats.
