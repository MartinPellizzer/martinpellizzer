Cryptantha Affinis

Cryptantha affinis is a small flowering plant native to the western united states and northern mexico. it belongs to the borage family and is known for its delicate blue or purple flowers. this plant often grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. it is commonly found in desert regions and along coastal areas. despite its modest size, cryptantha affinis plays an important role in its ecosystem.
