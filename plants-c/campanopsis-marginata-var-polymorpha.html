Campanopsis marginata var. polymorpha

Campanopsis marginata var. polymorpha, commonly known as the variable bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.
