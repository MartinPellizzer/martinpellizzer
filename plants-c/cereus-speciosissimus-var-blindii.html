Cereus speciosissimus var. blindii

Cereus speciosissimus var. blindii, commonly known as the blindii cactus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a spineless, columnar cactus that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features large, showy white flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its stems are ribbed and can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

It is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.
