Cattleya oweniana

Cattleya oweniana, commonly known as the Owen's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya oweniana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
