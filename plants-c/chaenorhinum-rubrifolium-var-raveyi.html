Chaenorhinum rubrifolium var. raveyi

Chaenorhinum rubrifolium var. raveyi, commonly known as the red-leaf chaenorhinum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
