Cattleya × binotii

Cattleya × binotii, commonly known as the Binotii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features upright, leathery leaves and produces large, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

Cattleya × binotii requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light to flourish.
