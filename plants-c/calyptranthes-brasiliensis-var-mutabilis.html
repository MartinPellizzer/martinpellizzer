Calyptranthes brasiliensis var. mutabilis

Calyptranthes brasiliensis var. mutabilis, commonly known as the variable calyptranthes, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
