Chlorophytum longum

Chlorophytum longum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green stripes. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright green, rope-like berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Chlorophytum longum is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and air-purifying qualities.
