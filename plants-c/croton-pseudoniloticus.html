Croton pseudoniloticus

Croton pseudoniloticus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that vary in shape and hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
