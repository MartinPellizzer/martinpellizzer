Calyptrostylis rudgei

Calyptrostylis rudgei, commonly known as the Rudge's hooded orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, hooded flower structure, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within rainforests. Its flowers are usually green or yellowish with intricate patterns, and it produces small, round pseudobulbs.

Calyptrostylis rudgei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
