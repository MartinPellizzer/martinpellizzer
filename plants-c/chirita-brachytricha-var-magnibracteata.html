Chirita brachytricha var. magnibracteata

Chirita brachytricha var. magnibracteata, commonly known as the broad-bract chirita, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
