Clitoria sericea

Clitoria sericea, commonly known as the silky blue pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy, butterfly-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Clitoria sericea is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
