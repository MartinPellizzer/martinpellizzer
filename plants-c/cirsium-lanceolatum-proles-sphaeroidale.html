Cirsium lanceolatum proles sphaeroidale

Cirsium lanceolatum proles sphaeroidale, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spherical blue or purple flower heads, which are composed of tightly packed bracts that resemble small, rounded flowers. The plant has spiny, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
