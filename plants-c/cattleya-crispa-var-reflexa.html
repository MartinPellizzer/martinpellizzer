Cattleya crispa var. reflexa

Cattleya crispa var. reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed cattleya, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, and showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and lavender. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with reflexed petals that give it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
