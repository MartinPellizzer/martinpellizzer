Cynoglossum macropterum

Cynoglossum macropterum, commonly known as the large-winged bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are often hairy.

Cynoglossum macropterum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate.
