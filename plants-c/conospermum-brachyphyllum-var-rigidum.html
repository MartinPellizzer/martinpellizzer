Conospermum brachyphyllum var. rigidum

Conospermum brachyphyllum var. rigidum, commonly known as the rigid leaf banksia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, rigid leaves that are typically grey-green in color. The plant produces cylindrical flower spikes that bloom in spring, featuring yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
