Convolvulus hastatus var. major

Convolvulus hastatus var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered convolvulus, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
