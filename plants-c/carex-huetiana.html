Carex huetiana

Carex huetiana, commonly known as the Huet's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive bluish-green color.

Carex huetiana produces small, clustered spikes of brownish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
