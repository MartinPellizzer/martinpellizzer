Cupressus macrocarpa subsp. lobosensis

Cupressus macrocarpa subsp.

lobosensis, commonly known as the coastal cupressus, is a subspecies native to the Mediterranean region. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen conifer with a narrow, pyramidal shape.

The foliage consists of scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain aromatic seeds.

This plant is well adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils.
