Chesneya volkii

Chesneya volkii, commonly known as the Volk's chesneya, is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of Namibia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
