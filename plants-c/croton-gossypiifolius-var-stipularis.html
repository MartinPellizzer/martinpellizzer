Croton gossypiifolius var. stipularis

Croton gossypiifolius var. stipularis, commonly known as the stipular croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lobed leaves that range from green to red or purple, depending on the season and light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
