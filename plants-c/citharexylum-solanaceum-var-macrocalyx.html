Citharexylum solanaceum var. macrocalyx

Citharexylum solanaceum var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow throat. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
