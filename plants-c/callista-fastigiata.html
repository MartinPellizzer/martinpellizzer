Callista fastigiata

Callista fastigiata, commonly known as the narrowleaf evergreen hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Callista fastigiata is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking floral display and adaptability to various climates.
