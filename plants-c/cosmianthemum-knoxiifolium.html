Cosmianthemum knoxiifolium

Cosmianthemum knoxiifolium, commonly known as the Knox's cosmos, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in local ecosystems.
