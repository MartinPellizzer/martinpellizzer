Cissus microdonta f. deltoidea

Cissus microdonta f. deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid leaf cissus, is a tropical vine characterized by its distinctive, deltoid-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the family Vitaceae and is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and Malaysia. The plant features tendrils that aid in climbing and can grow up to several meters in length. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a slightly waxy texture, while the stems are succulent and can store water.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
