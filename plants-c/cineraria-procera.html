Cineraria procera

Cineraria procera, commonly known as the tall cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, velvety blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy blooms. Cineraria procera thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
