Cyathea amabilis

Cyathea amabilis, commonly known as the beautiful tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. Cyathea amabilis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
