Carex davalliana var. androgyna

Carex davalliana var. androgyna, commonly known as the Androgyna sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces spike-like inflorescences that bear small, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or wetlands.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.
