Carex goodenowii f. stenostachya

Carex goodenowii f. stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-staminal sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are generally narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

This subspecies is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other moist habitats across its native range.
