Centaurea integrisquama

Centaurea integrisquama, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features striking blue to purple flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, which are characteristic of the genus Centaurea. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a hairy texture.

The species is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
