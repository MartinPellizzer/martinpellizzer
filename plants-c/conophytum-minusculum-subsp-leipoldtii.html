Conophytum minusculum subsp. leipoldtii

Conophytum minusculum subsp.

leipoldtii, commonly known as the dwarf conophytum, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa. It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge under stress.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
