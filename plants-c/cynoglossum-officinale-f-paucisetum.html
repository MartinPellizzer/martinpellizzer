Cynoglossum officinale f. paucisetum

Cynoglossum officinale f. paucisetum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved forget-me-not, is a variant of the common forget-me-not.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its slender stems and small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
