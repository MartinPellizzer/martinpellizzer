Cyperus laetus var. maximus

Cyperus laetus var. maximus, commonly known as the broadleaf paperplant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often green or bluish-green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late summer or early fall.

This species is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to thrive in moist soils.
