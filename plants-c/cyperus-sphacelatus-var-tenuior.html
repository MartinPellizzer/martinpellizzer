Cyperus sphacelatus var. tenuior

Cyperus sphacelatus var. tenuior, commonly known as narrowleaf flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to wetland and marshy environments.

It has slender, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 1 mm wide, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are usually inconspicuous and greenish in color. It thrives in shallow water or moist soil and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
