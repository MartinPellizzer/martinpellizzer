Cryptocarya shoreifolia

Cryptocarya shoreifolia, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern or the fern tree, is a unique plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant features a tall, slender trunk with a crown of fronds resembling a tree fern. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, and it produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible berries.

Cryptocarya shoreifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
