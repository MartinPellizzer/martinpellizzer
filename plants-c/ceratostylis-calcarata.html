Ceratostylis calcarata

Ceratostylis calcarata, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and features a single, narrow leaf with a short, erect stem bearing a single flower. The flower has a distinctive, spurred lip and is typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in forest undergrowth.

The plant is valued for its unique appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
