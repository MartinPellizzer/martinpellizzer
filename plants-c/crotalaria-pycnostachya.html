Crotalaria pycnostachya

Crotalaria pycnostachya, commonly known as the dense-stalked crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with hairy stems and small, compound leaves that help it thrive in arid environments.
