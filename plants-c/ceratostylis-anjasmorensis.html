Ceratostylis anjasmorensis

Ceratostylis anjasmorensis, commonly known as the Anjasmorensis Sword Fern, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect rhizome and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a spike, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth.
