Cattleya amethystoglossa var. prinzii

Cattleya amethystoglossa var. prinzii, commonly known as the Prinzii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It features large, showy blooms with a deep purple lip and a white to pale pink dorsal sepal. This variety is native to Brazil and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.

The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in its natural habitat.
