Chassalia afzelii

Chassalia afzelii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branching.

Chassalia afzelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
