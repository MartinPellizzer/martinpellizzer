Castanella riparia

Castanella riparia, commonly known as the riparian castanet, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions with moist, temperate climates. Castanella riparia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
