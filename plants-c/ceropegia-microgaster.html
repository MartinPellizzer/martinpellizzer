Ceropegia microgaster

Ceropegia microgaster, commonly known as the dwarf string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
