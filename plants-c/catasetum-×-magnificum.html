Catasetum × magnificum

Catasetum × magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without being parasitic. Its flowers are notable for their intricate structure, including specialized pollination mechanisms.

Catasetum × magnificum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral features.
