Camphora jussieui

Camphora jussieui, commonly known as the Chinese camphor tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the樟科 (Lauraceae) family and is characterized by its aromatic, oily wood and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by round, dark blue berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

The tree is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine and as a source of camphor.
