Chenopodium carinatum

Chenopodium carinatum, commonly known as goosefoot, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its alternate, broadly ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are encased in a dry, three-lobed capsule. Chenopodium carinatum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is also known for its nutritional value and has been used in traditional cuisines.
