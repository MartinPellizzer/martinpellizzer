Cleistocactus hoffmannii

Cleistocactus hoffmannii, commonly known as the Hoffmann's cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short stem and dense, woolly covering. The plant produces yellow flowers that emerge from the top of the stem, often at night. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and desert gardens.
