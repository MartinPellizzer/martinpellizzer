Citrus × aurantium var. fructo-costata

Citrus × aurantium var. fructo-costata, commonly known as the citron.

This variety is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its large, thick-skinned fruits that are typically yellow to green in color. The fruit has a high concentration of essential oils and is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. It is native to Southeast Asia and has been cultivated for centuries for its aromatic properties and medicinal value.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
