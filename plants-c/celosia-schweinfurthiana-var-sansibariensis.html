Celosia schweinfurthiana var. sansibariensis

Celosia schweinfurthiana var. sansibariensis, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a strikingly colorful plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its vivid red, orange, and yellow bracts that resemble flames, which are actually modified leaves rather than flowers. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow as an upright shrub or a small tree.

The species is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and bees.
