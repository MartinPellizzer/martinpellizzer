Ceropegia Splendida

Ceropegia splendida is a striking plant known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in warm, arid environments. This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and ease of care. Its foliage often features intricate patterns and a trailing growth habit. Whether grown indoors or outdoors, it adds a touch of exotic beauty to any space.
