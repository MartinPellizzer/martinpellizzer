Canarium chinare

Canarium chinare, commonly known as the Chinese canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and broad, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed. Canarium chinare is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important species in tropical forest ecosystems.
