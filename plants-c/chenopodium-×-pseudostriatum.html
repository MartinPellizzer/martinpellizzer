Chenopodium × pseudostriatum

Chenopodium × pseudostriatum, commonly known as silverleaf goosefoot, is a hybrid species within the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are broadly ovate to rhombic in shape, with a rough texture and a prominent midrib.

The flowers are small and clustered in spikes, producing seeds that are dispersed by wind.
