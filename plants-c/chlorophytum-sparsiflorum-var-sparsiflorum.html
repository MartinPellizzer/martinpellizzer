Chlorophytum sparsiflorum var. sparsiflorum

Chlorophytum sparsiflorum var. sparsiflorum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation, forming a rosette pattern. The plant produces long, slender flower stalks that bear small, white flowers in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal houseplant.

Its air-purifying qualities and ease of care have made it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
