Cassia Barclayana Var. Pubescens

Cassia barclayana var. pubescens is a strikingly ornamental plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and feathery foliage. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm climates with ample sunlight and well-draining soil. This variety is prized for its ability to add a bold splash of color to gardens and landscapes. Its unique pubescence, or fine hair-like covering, gives it a distinctive appearance. Whether grown as a specimen plant or in mass plantings, it offers both aesthetic value and ecological benefits.
