Conospermum polycephalum var. leianthum

Conospermum polycephalum var. leianthum, commonly known as the Woolly Conospermum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
