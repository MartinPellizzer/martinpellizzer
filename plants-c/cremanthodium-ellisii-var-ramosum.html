Cremanthodium ellisii var. ramosum

Cremanthodium ellisii var. ramosum, commonly known as the Sierra gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and slopes.
