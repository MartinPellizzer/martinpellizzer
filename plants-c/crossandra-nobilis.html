Crossandra nobilis

Crossandra nobilis, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Crossandra nobilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
