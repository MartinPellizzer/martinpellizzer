Cymbidiopsis lancifolia

Cymbidiopsis lancifolia, commonly known as the lancifoliate orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural collections.
