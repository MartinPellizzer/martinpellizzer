Chionanthus nitens

Chionanthus nitens, commonly known as the silverflower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and have a silvery underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

The silverflower tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
