Chamaeangis hildebrandtii

Chamaeangis hildebrandtii, commonly known as the Hildebrandt's lily, is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
