Chrysosplenium discolor var. viridescens

Chrysosplenium discolor var. viridescens, commonly known as the greenleaf chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
