Cyathea wolfii

Cyathea wolfii, commonly known as the Wolf's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of feathery leaves that can reach several meters in length. Cyathea wolfii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
