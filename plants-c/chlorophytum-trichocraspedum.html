Chlorophytum trichocraspedum

Chlorophytum trichocraspedum, commonly known as the curly spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, curly, grass-like leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers on slender stems during the spring and summer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
