Croton orangeae

Croton orangeae, commonly known as the Orangeae Croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Croton orangeae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
