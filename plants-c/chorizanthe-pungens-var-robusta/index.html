Chorizanthe Pungens Var. Robusta

Chorizanthe pungens var. robusta is a striking member of the Amaranthaceae family known for its vibrant, spiky flowers and resilient nature. Native to the arid regions of California, this plant thrives in harsh, dry conditions with minimal water requirements. Its unique appearance, featuring bright red bracts and feathery foliage, makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens. Despite its tough exterior, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish. This plant is not only aesthetically pleasing but also plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
