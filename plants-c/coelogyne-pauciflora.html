Coelogyne pauciflora

Coelogyne pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to white blossoms that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Coelogyne pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
