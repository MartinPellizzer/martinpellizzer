Croton musaicus

Croton musaicus, commonly known as the flame tree or red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Croton musaicus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and showy blooms.
