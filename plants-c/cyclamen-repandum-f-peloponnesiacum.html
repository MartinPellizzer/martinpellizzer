Cyclamen repandum f. peloponnesiacum

Cyclamen repandum f. peloponnesiacum, commonly known as the Peloponnesian cyclamen, is a rare and charming plant native to the Peloponnese region of Greece.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver-white underside and produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant. Its foliage and flowers add a touch of elegance to gardens and interiors during the winter months.

Due to its limited natural habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
