Caladium bicolor var. pictum

Caladium bicolor var. pictum, commonly known as the Painted Leaf or Rainbow Caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, heart-shaped leaves that display a wide range of variegated patterns in shades of white, red, pink, and green. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Caladium bicolor var. pictum is often used in landscapes for its vibrant, eye-catching appearance during the growing season.
