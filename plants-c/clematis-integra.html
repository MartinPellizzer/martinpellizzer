Clematis integra

Clematis integra, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its woody stems can be trained to climb trellises or other supports.

Clematis integra is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
