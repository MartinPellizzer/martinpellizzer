Calliandra rekoi

Calliandra rekoi, commonly known as the pink calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences. The plant produces vibrant pink to red bracts that surround small, pea-like flowers, which are often mistaken for petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Calliandra rekoi is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
