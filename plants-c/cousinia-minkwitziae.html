Cousinia minkwitziae

Cousinia minkwitziae, commonly known as the Minkwitz's cudweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in compact clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It has silver-gray foliage with fuzzy leaves, adapted to withstand harsh, dry conditions.

Cousinia minkwitziae is often found in rocky soils and is well-suited to xerophytic environments.
