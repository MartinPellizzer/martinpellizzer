Ceropegia Molaventi

The ceropegia molaventi is a rare and delicate plant native to southern africa. it belongs to the apocynaceae family and is known for its unique, tubular flowers. this plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. its striking appearance makes it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts. due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and needs conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
