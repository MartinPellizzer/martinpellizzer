Coelogyne truncicola

Coelogyne truncicola, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink and yellow, bloom in late winter to early spring.

Coelogyne truncicola is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
