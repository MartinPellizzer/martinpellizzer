Crotalaria afzelii

Crotalaria afzelii, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces bean-like pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Crotalaria afzelii is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural purposes.
