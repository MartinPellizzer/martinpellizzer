Callista subacaulis

Callista subacaulis, commonly known as the slender callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Callista subacaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
