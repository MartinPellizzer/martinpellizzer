Cyclamen persicum var. marginatum-purpureum

Cyclamen persicum var. marginatum-purpureum, commonly known as the purple-edged cyclamen, is a hybrid variety of the cyclamen plant characterized by its striking foliage and flowers.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple margin and vibrant pink or violet flowers with a white throat. This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for indoor or outdoor gardens due to its ornamental value.

The species is known for its winter blooming habit, making it a popular choice for seasonal displays.
