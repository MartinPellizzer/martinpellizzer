Cryptostylis arachnites

Cryptostylis arachnites, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with elongated, thread-like leaves and a slender, curling inflorescence. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of yellow and green, often resembling the shape of a spider. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in rainforest or heathland areas.

Cryptostylis arachnites is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
