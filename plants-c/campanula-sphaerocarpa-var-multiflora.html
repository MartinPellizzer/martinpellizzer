Campanula sphaerocarpa var. multiflora

Campanula sphaerocarpa var. multiflora, commonly known as the globe bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features spherical, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, blooming in late summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique flower shape and ornamental value contribute to its appeal in horticultural settings.
