Crocodilodes platypterum

Crocodilodes platypterum, commonly known as the flat-winged crocodile plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, broad, flat leaves that resemble the scales of a crocodile, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of green and yellow, and it produces fruit that is edible for certain animals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
