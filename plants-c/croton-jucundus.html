Croton jucundus

Croton jucundus, commonly known as the yellow croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display shades of yellow, red, and orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.

Croton jucundus thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
