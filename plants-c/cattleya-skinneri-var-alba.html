Cattleya skinneri var. alba

Cattleya skinneri var. alba, commonly known as the White Skinner Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a subtle pinkish tinge and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a thick, leathery sheath.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance in botanical gardens and private collections.
