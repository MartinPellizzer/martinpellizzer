Cyrtochilum flexuosum

Cyrtochilum flexuosum, commonly known as the flexuous orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy coating to retain moisture.

Cyrtochilum flexuosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its delicate, intricate blooms.
