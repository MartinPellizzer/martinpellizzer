Carex tenuistachya f. pallida

Carex tenuistachya f. pallida, commonly known as the pale form of narrow-leaved sedge.

This plant is a perennial herbaceous species belonging to the Cyperaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets. The pale form is distinguished by its lighter green foliage compared to the typical green variant.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
