Carex neesiana

Carex neesiana, commonly known as the Nees' sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant features triangular stems and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Its foliage is typically green and grass-like, with a dense growth habit.

Carex neesiana is often found in wet or moist habitats such as marshes and along stream banks.
