Columnea splendens

Columnea splendens, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with a fine, silky pubescence.

Columnea splendens is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded gardens.
