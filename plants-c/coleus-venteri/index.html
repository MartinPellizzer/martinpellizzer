Coleus Venteri

Coleus venteri is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, colorful foliage. Native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves come in shades of red, purple, and green, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. This plant requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish. Despite its beauty, it is toxic to pets and should be handled with care.
