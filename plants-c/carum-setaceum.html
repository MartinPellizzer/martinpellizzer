Carum setaceum

Carum setaceum, commonly known as the slender caraway, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, often found in rocky or dry habitats. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, fern-like leaves that are hairy to the touch. Its flowers are small and white or greenish, arranged in umbels, typical of the carrot family.
