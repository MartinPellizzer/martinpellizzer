Cynorkis rolfei

Cynorkis rolfei, commonly known as the Rolfe's orchid, is a species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water.

The flowers, which are usually white or pale pink, bloom in late summer and attract various pollinators.
