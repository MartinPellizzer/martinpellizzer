Chamaesyce deltoidea

Chamaesyce deltoidea, commonly known as deltoid leaf sandwort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous species that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. The plant has alternate, deltate (triangular) leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Chamaesyce deltoidea is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in open habitats such as prairies and disturbed areas.
