Crocus pelistericus

Crocus pelistericus, commonly known as the Pelister crocus, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its early blooming habit, often appearing in late winter or early spring. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
