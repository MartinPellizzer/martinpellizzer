Celosia

Celosia, commonly known as flame flower or cockscomb, is a genus of flowering plants in the amaranth family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its colorful, plume-like inflorescences that come in shades of red, orange, pink, and purple. Celosia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its long blooming period and low maintenance requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation.
