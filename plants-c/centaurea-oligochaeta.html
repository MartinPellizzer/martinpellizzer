Centaurea oligochaeta

Centaurea oligochaeta, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and northern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright blue to purple flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.
