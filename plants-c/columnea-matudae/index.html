Columnea Matudae

Columnea matudae is a rare and striking epiphytic plant native to the cloud forests of Colombia. It is known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss, Columnea matudae is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
