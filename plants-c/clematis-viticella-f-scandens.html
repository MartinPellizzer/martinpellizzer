Clematis viticella f. scandens

Clematis viticella f. scandens, commonly known as the compact clematis, is a vigorous climbing plant native to Europe.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant is characterized by its strong, flexible stems that can climb using twining growth. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in gardens.
