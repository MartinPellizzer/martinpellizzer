Ctenitis molinae

Ctenitis molinae, commonly known as the Molina's rock fern, is a species of fern native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its spore-producing structures are located on the underside of its leaves, a common feature in many fern species.
