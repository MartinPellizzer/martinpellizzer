Citrus maideniana

Citrus maideniana, commonly known as the Philippine lemon.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines, characterized by its smooth, pale-green bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are similar in appearance to lemons but have a distinct, aromatic flavor. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Due to its unique taste and limited cultivation, it is considered a rare and valuable citrus species.
