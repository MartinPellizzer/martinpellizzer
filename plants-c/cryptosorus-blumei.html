Cryptosorus blumei

Cryptosorus blumei, commonly known as the Blume's cryptosorus, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spore-producing structures, called sorus, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Cryptosorus blumei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
