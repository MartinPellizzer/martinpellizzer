Clutia abyssinica var. deserticola

Clutia abyssinica var. deserticola, commonly known as the desert clutia, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Ethiopia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it well adapted to desert environments.

Its resilience and unique appearance make it a notable species in xerophytic vegetation.
