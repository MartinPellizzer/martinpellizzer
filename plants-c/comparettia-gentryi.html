Comparettia gentryi

Comparettia gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's comparettia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, showy floral structure. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
