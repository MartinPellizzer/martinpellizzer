Caladium heterophyllum

Caladium heterophyllum, commonly known as the Indian shot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in a variety of vibrant colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

Caladium heterophyllum prefers partial shade and can be propagated through seeds or division.
