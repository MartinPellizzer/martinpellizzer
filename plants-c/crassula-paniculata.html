Crassula paniculata

Crassula paniculata, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula paniculata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
