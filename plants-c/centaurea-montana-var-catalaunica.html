Centaurea montana var. catalaunica

Centaurea montana var. catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan mountain centaurea, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and rocky slopes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
