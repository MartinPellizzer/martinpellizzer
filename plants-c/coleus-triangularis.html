Coleus triangularis

Coleus triangularis, commonly known as the triangular coleus, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, purple, and green. The leaves are typically triangular in shape, giving the plant its common name, and are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.

The plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
