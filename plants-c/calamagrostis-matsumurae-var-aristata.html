Calamagrostis matsumurae var. aristata

Calamagrostis matsumurae var. aristata, commonly known as the Japanese feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and fine, silver-blue foliage that adds ornamental value to gardens. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, making it suitable for wetland or pond margins. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer, adding a delicate floral display.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of environmental conditions.
