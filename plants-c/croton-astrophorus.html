Croton astrophorus

Croton astrophorus, commonly known as the star croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are typically lobed or deeply serrated, adding to its visual appeal.

Croton astrophorus is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
