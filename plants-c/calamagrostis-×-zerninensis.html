Calamagrostis × zerninensis

Calamagrostis × zerninensis, commonly known as the silvergrass or Chinese silvergrass, is a hybrid grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its elegant foliage and showy inflorescences. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Calamagrostis × zerninensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
