Chaenocephalus macrophyllus

Chaenocephalus macrophyllus, commonly known as the broad-leafed chaenocephalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces yellow-orange composite flowers, which are composed of many small florets. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
