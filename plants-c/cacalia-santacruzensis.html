Cacalia santacruzensis

Cacalia santacruzensis, commonly known as the Santa Cruz cacalia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
