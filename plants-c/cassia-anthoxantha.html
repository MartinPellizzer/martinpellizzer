Cassia anthoxantha

Cassia anthoxantha, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and greyish, while the wood is hard and durable.

Cassia anthoxantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
