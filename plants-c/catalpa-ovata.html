Catalpa ovata

Catalpa ovata, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, bell-like white flowers that bloom in late spring. The tree produces long, cylindrical seed pods that split open to release its winged seeds. Catalpa ovata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also known for its fast growth rate and tolerance to urban environments.
