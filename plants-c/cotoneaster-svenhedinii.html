Cotoneaster svenhedinii

Cotoneaster svenhedinii, commonly known as the Svenhedin cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers in the spring, which are followed by red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Cotoneaster svenhedinii is also valued for its role in ecological habitats, providing food for birds and other wildlife.
