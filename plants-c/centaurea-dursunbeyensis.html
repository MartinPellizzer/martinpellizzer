Centaurea dursunbeyensis

Centaurea dursunbeyensis, commonly known as the Dursunbey thistle, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually purple or pink, with bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

It is a relatively rare species, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
