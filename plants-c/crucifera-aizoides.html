Crucifera aizoides

Crucifera aizoides, commonly known as the Aizoides mustard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds. Native to certain regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
