Calceolaria graminifolia

Calceolaria graminifolia, commonly known as the grass-leaved slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. Calceolaria graminifolia is a perennial herb that blooms in the spring and early summer.
