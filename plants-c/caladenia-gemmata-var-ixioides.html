Caladenia gemmata var. ixioides

Caladenia gemmata var. ixioides, commonly known as the Ixioides Spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It features a slender, wiry stem with a single, narrow, greenish-white to pale pink flower. The flower has a distinctive, elongated lip that resembles a spider, giving it its common name. It typically blooms during late spring to early summer.

This variety is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats.
