Cleisostoma ionosmum f. luchuense

Cleisostoma ionosmum f. luchuense, commonly known as the Luchuan orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, white to pale yellow blooms with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
