Cassia bulbotricha

Cassia bulbotricha, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, oval-shaped pods that contain flat, winged seeds. Cassia bulbotricha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
