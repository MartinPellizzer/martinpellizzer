Cyclamen purpurascens f. carmineolineatum

Cyclamen purpurascens f. carmineolineatum, commonly known as the Carmine-flowered Cyclamen, is a hybrid form of the species Cyclamen purpurascens.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with a deep red or carmine hue, which contrast beautifully with its delicate, spurred flowers. The plant typically produces flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a distinctive purple or crimson tinge. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and blooms.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable growing environment.
