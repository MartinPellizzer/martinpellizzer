Crepis pallens

Crepis pallens, commonly known as the white hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Crepis pallens is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
