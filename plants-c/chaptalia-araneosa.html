Chaptalia araneosa

Chaptalia araneosa, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance due to its finely divided leaves. Chaptalia araneosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
