Cyphostemma pannosum

Cyphostemma pannosum, commonly known as the African moon vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features woody stems with a distinctive, rough, and often peeling bark. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Cyphostemma pannosum is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
