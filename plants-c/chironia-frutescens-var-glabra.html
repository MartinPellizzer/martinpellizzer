Chironia frutescens var. glabra

Chironia frutescens var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth chironia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically smooth and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a specimen plant in horticultural collections.
