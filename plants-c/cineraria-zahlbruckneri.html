Cineraria zahlbruckneri

Cineraria zahlbruckneri, commonly known as the blue cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant features silvery-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
