Cleyera yangchunensis

Cleyera yangchunensis, commonly known as the Yangchun cleyera, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Guangdong province, where it thrives in subtropical climates. Cleyera yangchunensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its ability to thrive in shaded environments.
