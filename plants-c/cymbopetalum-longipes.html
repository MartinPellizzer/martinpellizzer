Cymbopetalum longipes

Cymbopetalum longipes, commonly known as the pink torch orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its unique floral structure includes a spurred labellum and showy, spurred sepals.

Cymbopetalum longipes is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
