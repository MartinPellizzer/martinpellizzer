Crassula susannae

Crassula susannae, commonly known as the "Susanna's Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that can range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
