Columnea poeppigiana

Columnea poeppigiana, commonly known as the red-tailed columnea, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that are often tinged with orange or yellow, arranged in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in hanging baskets.

Columnea poeppigiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
