Cephalocereus cuyabensis

Cephalocereus cuyabensis, commonly known as the Cuyabeno cactus, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest of Ecuador.

It is a columnar cactus with a tall, spiny stem that can grow up to several meters in height. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems. The plant is adapted to the humid, tropical climate and thrives in well-drained soil.

It plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
