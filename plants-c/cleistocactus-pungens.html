Cleistocactus pungens

Cleistocactus pungens, commonly known as the ghost cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in sharp, spiny tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the evening, making it a nocturnal flowerer. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to survive.

Its unique appearance and nocturnal blooming make it a distinctive and interesting plant for botanical study.
