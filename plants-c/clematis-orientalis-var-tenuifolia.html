Clematis orientalis var. tenuifolia

Clematis orientalis var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf oriental clematis, is a deciduous vine native to East Asia.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are narrow and delicate, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and is often used in gardens for its climbing habit and floral display.
