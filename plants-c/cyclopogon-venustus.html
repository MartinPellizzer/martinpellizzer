Cyclopogon venustus

Cyclopogon venustus, commonly known as the beautiful cyclopogon, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-gray hue.

Cyclopogon venustus is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
