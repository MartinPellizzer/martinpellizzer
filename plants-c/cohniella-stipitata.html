Cohniella stipitata

Cohniella stipitata, commonly known as the velvet leaf fungus, is a unique plant with a distinctive appearance.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety texture and dark green, broad leaves. The plant has a compact growth habit, often forming dense clusters. It is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
