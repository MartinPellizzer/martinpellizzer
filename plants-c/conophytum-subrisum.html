Conophytum subrisum

Conophytum subrisum, commonly known as the "Cape tulip," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid environments. Conophytum subrisum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
