Cardamomum speciosum

Cardamomum speciosum, commonly known as the showy cardamom, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features erect, branched stems and lanceolate leaves with a fragrant, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers arranged in spikes, which are followed by capsule fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its essential oils, derived from the seeds, are valued for their medicinal and culinary uses.

Cardamomum speciosum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.
