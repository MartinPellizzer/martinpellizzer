Coleus scutellarioides var. grandifolius

Coleus scutellarioides var. grandifolius, commonly known as the giant leaf coleus, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, colorful, and velvety leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is closely related to other coleus species. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its foliage is often used in landscape design for its vibrant visual impact.
