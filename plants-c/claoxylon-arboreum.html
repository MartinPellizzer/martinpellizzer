Claoxylon arboreum

Claoxylon arboreum, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood and dense foliage. The plant has smooth, greyish bark that becomes fissured with age. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a leathery texture.

Claoxylon arboreum is also valued for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional medicine.
