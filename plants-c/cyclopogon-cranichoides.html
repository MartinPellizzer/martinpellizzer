Cyclopogon cranichoides

Cyclopogon cranichoides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy environments and is native to parts of South America. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Due to its habitat preferences, it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
