Cyperus filifolius

Cyperus filifolius, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower spikes that are typically brown or reddish in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Cyperus filifolius is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
