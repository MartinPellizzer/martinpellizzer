Croton corymbulosus var. thermophilus

Croton corymbulosus var. thermophilus, commonly known as the flame tree or fire tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in large, showy clusters. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets that are often arranged in a feather-like pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.

The species is also valued for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types, making it adaptable to different environments.
