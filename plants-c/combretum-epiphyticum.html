Combretum epiphyticum

Combretum epiphyticum, commonly known as the epiphytic combretum, is a unique species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of other plants, typically trees, without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in inflorescences, and can be either white or pale pink in color.

It plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various small organisms.
