Corydalis makinoana var. japonica

Corydalis makinoana var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese Corydalis, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant has slender stems and heart-shaped leaves, and it thrives in moist, shady environments. It is often found in forest understories and along streambanks.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
