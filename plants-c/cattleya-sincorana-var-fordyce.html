Cattleya sincorana var. fordyce

Cattleya sincorana var. fordyce, commonly known as the Fordyce Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pale yellow to deep orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
