Campanula caperonoides

Campanula caperonoides, commonly known as the Cape Bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Campanula caperonoides is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
