Cleisostoma armigera

Cleisostoma armigera, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pendulous growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a velvety texture. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Cleisostoma armigera thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
