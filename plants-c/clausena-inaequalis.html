Clausena inaequalis

Clausena inaequalis, commonly known as the sour orange or kaffir lime, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The fruit is a small, oval-shaped citrus fruit with a thick, rough rind and a strong, aromatic scent. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
