Crassula sessilicymula

Crassula sessilicymula, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
