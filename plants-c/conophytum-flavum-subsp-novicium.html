Conophytum flavum subsp. novicium

Conophytum flavum subsp.

novicium, commonly known as the Orange Ghost, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are typically yellow to orange in color, giving it its common name.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, usually in spring or summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
