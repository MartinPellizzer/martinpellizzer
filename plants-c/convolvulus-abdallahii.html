Convolvulus abdallahii

Convolvulus abdallahii, commonly known as the Arabian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate and have a heart-shaped appearance.

Convolvulus abdallahii is adapted to survive in harsh conditions with minimal water requirements.
