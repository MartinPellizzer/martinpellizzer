Croton nuntians

Croton nuntians, commonly known as the "golden flame" or "firecracker plant," is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often vibrant leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton nuntians typically grows in humid, shady environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant cultivation.
