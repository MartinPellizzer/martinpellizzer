Ceropegia geminata subsp. decora

Ceropegia geminata subsp.

decora, commonly known as the Decorated Ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, succulent stems.
