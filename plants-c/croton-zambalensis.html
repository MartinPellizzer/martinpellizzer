Croton zambalensis

Croton zambalensis, commonly known as the Zambal croton, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant's unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
