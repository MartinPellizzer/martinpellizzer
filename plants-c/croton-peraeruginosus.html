Croton peraeruginosus

Croton peraeruginosus, commonly known as the Blue Croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and blue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Croton peraeruginosus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
