Coelogyne reflexa

Coelogyne reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its long, narrow pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with red or purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne reflexa is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
