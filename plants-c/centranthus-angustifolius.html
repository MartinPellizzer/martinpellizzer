Centranthus angustifolius

Centranthus angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved redhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bright red, tubular flowers. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in dry garden landscapes.
