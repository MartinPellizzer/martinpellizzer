Caladium hendersonii

Caladium hendersonii, commonly known as the "Rainbow Caladium," is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is prized for its striking, colorful foliage, which features broad, heart-shaped leaves with intricate patterns of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often grown as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor settings.

Caladium hendersonii is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other plants like peace lilies and calla lilies.
