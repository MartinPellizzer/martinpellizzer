Cicendiola vaillantii

Cicendiola vaillantii, commonly known as the Vaillant's cycad, is a unique cycad species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, typical of cycads. It thrives in tropical climates with well-drained soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native environment.
