Ctenodon falcatus var. hassleri

Ctenodon falcatus var. hassleri, commonly known as the Hassler's golden thread, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, cascading inflorescences that produce vibrant yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its ethereal appearance.
