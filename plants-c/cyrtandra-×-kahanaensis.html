Cyrtandra × kahanaensis

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Cyrtandra × kahanaensis is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of Hawaii and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.
