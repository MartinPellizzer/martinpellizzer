Calea hassleriana f. mollis

Calea hassleriana f. mollis, commonly known as the velvet calea, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its velvety, hairy leaves.

Native to Central America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. Its foliage is dense and textured, making it an attractive addition to shaded gardens or indoor spaces.

It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
