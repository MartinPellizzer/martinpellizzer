Centropogon latifolius

Centropogon latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf centropogon, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Centropogon latifolius is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
