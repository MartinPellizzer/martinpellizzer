Conyza spinidens

Conyza spinidens, commonly known as roughleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that attract pollinators. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.

Conyza spinidens is an annual herb that plays a role in ecological systems by supporting insect life and contributing to soil stability.
