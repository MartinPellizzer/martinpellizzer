Cotoneaster peduncularis

Cotoneaster peduncularis, commonly known as the clustered cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, white to pink flowers that appear in clusters during late spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

The berries, which are red and edible, ripen in the fall and are a food source for birds and small mammals.
