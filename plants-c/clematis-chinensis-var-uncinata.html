Clematis chinensis var. uncinata

Clematis chinensis var. uncinata, commonly known as the Chinese climbing clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, red berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its vigorous growth habit requires support structures for optimal display.
