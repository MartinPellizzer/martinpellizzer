Coronilla emerus subsp. emeroides

Coronilla emerus subsp.

emeroides, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces.

The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable in ecological restoration and soil enrichment.
