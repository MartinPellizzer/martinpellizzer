Cliffortia oligodonta

Cliffortia oligodonta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sand-thrift, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Cliffortia oligodonta is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it forms dense colonies.
