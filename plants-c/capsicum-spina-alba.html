Capsicum spina-alba

Capsicum spina-alba, commonly known as the white-spined pepper.

It is a species of chili pepper native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. Its fruits are typically small, round, and turn red when mature.

Botanically, it belongs to the Solanaceae family and is closely related to other Capsicum species.
