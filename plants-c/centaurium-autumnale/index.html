Centaurium Autumnale

Centaurium autumnale is a delicate flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. It is known for its small, bright red blooms that appear in late summer and early autumn. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its scientific name reflects its mythological associations with centaurs and its medicinal uses in traditional herbal medicine. Despite its modest appearance, Centaurium autumnale holds ecological and cultural significance in its native habitats.
