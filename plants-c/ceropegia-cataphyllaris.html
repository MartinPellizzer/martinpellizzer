Ceropegia cataphyllaris

Ceropegia cataphyllaris, commonly known as the silver leaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tuberous roots and produces clusters of small, pale green or white flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
