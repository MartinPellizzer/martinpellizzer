Cathariostachys capitata

Cathariostachys capitata, commonly known as the red-stemmed goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
