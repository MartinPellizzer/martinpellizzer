Cymbidium elegans var. obcordatum

Cymbidium elegans var. obcordatum, commonly known as the obcordate cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically blooms in winter or early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are long, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
