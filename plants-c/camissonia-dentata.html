Camissonia dentata

Camissonia dentata, commonly known as the toothed camissonia, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the family Camissoniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a distinctive toothed or serrated edge.

Camissonia dentata is known for its short stature and is often found in coastal regions of California.
