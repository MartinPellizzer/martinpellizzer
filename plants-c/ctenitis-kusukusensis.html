Ctenitis kusukusensis

Ctenitis kusukusensis, commonly known as the Kusukusensis tree fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in rainforests. Its rhizome system is extensive, allowing it to anchor firmly in the soil and absorb nutrients efficiently.
