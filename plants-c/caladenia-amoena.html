Caladenia amoena

Caladenia amoena, commonly known as the Elegant Spider-Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and dry sclerophyll forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with a single or few flowers that are predominantly pink or pale purple in color. Its flowers have a distinctive spider-like appearance, with a narrow, erect labellum and recurved petals.

Caladenia amoena is a perennial herb that produces showy blooms during spring, attracting various pollinators.
