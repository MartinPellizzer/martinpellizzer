Centaurea splendens var. tenuifolia

Centaurea splendens var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces vibrant blue to purple flower heads that are often surrounded by pale yellow rays. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its colorful blooms.
