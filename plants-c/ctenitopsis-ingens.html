Ctenitopsis ingens

Ctenitopsis ingens, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a striking plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, branched leaves that give it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. Its leaves are covered in fine, hair-like structures that help reduce water loss and provide some protection from strong winds.

Ctenitopsis ingens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and specialty nurseries.
