Clitoria galactia

Clitoria galactia, commonly known as the milkweed bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, green pods that contain seeds, and it is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil through its root nodules.

Clitoria galactia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
