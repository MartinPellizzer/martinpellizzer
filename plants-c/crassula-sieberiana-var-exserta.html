Crassula sieberiana var. exserta

Crassula sieberiana var. exserta, commonly known as the Blue Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique coloration and ease of care.
