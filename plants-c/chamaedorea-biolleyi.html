Chamaedorea biolleyi

Chamaedorea biolleyi, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a compact, upright palm native to southern Mexico.

It features a slender trunk with smooth, grayish-brown bark and grows to a height of about 3 to 5 feet. The plant produces feathery, bright green fronds that are arranged in a rosette pattern around the trunk. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate humidity.

Chamaedorea biolleyi is prized for its attractive appearance and air-purifying qualities.
