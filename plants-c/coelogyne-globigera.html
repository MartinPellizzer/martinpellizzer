Coelogyne globigera

Coelogyne globigera, commonly known as the globigera orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant produces clustered inflorescences with fragrant, white to pale yellow petals and sepals. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Coelogyne globigera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
