Cotoneaster tengyuehensis

Cotoneaster tengyuehensis, commonly known as the Tengyuehensis cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
