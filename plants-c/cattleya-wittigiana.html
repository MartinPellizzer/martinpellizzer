Cattleya wittigiana

Cattleya wittigiana, commonly known as the Wittigiana Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
