Callista brymerana

Callista brymerana, commonly known as the red-stemmed daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flower heads surrounded by pale green ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its stems are often reddish in color, which distinguishes it from similar species.

Callista brymerana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
