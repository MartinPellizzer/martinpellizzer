Caladium marmoratum

Caladium marmoratum, commonly known as the marble queen caladium, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with marbled patterns of white, pink, and green. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Caladium marmoratum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaf patterns.
