Cycnium serratum f. elatum

Cycnium serratum f. elatum, commonly known as the tall hedge nettle, is a variant of the hedge nettle species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Apiaceae family.
