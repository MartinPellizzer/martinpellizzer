Chamartemisia compacta

Chamartemisia compacta, commonly known as the compact tansy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, low-growing form with small, narrow leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clustered heads, typical of the daisy family, which bloom in late summer. Chamartemisia compacta thrives in harsh, cold environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its aromatic foliage and resilience to extreme conditions make it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
