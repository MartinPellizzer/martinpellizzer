Caladium rugosum

Caladium rugosum, commonly known as the roughleaf arrowhead, is a tropical perennial plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often brightly colored in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically borne on slender stems. Caladium rugosum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.
