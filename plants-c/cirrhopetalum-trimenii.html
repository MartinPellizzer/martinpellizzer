Cirrhopetalum trimenii

Cirrhopetalum trimenii, commonly known as the Malay orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its flowers are often pink or white with intricate patterns.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural range.
