Cineraria palmensis

Cineraria palmensis, commonly known as the Canary Island cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces showy blooms during the spring and summer months.

Cineraria palmensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
