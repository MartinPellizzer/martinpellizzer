Combretum comosum

Combretum comosum, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces woody, cylindrical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal and inland regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and urban landscapes.
