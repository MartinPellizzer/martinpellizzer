Centaurea nitiida

Centaurea nitiida, commonly known as the Italian tarweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Centaurea nitiida is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
