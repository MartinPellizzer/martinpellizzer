Cristaria patens

Cristaria patens, commonly known as the starry water-lily, is a species of aquatic plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its floating, heart-shaped leaves and showy, white flowers with a distinctive star-like pattern. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and marshes. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs that help trap air and support buoyancy.

Cristaria patens plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
