Cryptocoryne yujii var. hendrikii

Cryptocoryne yujii var. hendrikii, commonly known as the Hendrik's Cryptocoryne, is a rare and striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, often with a dark green to bluish-green hue. The plant is noted for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it popular among aquarium enthusiasts. It has a slow growth rate and requires stable water parameters to maintain its health.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a sought-after addition to planted tanks.
