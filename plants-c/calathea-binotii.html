Calathea binotii

Calathea binotii, commonly known as the spotted calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves with dark green veins and pale green to white spots. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea binotii is known for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of any space.
