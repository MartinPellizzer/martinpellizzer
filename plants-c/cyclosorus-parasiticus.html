Cyclosorus parasiticus

Cyclosorus parasiticus, commonly known as the parasitic liverwort, is a non-vascular plant belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it derives some of its nutrients from host plants while also performing photosynthesis. The plant lacks true roots, stems, and leaves, and instead has a complex system of rhizoids for anchoring and nutrient absorption.
