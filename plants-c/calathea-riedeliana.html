Calathea riedeliana

Calathea riedeliana, commonly known as the Riedel's calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its unique leaf venation and colorful undersides. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
