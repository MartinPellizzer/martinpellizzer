Cyanorkis

Cyanorkis, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a rare and striking plant belonging to the orchid family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly the Andes. The plant is characterized by its vibrant blue flowers, which range in hue from pale sky blue to deep cobalt. Cyanorkis is noted for its delicate, intricate petals and a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
