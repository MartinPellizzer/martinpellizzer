Cotoneaster nummularius

Cotoneaster nummularius, commonly known as the penny cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and western Asia.

It features small, rounded, silver-white leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Cotoneaster nummularius is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
