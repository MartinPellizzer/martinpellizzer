Columnea eriantha

Columnea eriantha, commonly known as the silverleaf columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, which gives it its common name. It produces vibrant, often red or orange, spurred flowers that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.

Columnea eriantha is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
