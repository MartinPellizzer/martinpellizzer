Chlorophytum triflorum

Chlorophytum triflorum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers on slender stalks, which are usually white or green in color. It is a hardy plant that thrives in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its air-purifying qualities also make it a popular choice for improving indoor air quality.
