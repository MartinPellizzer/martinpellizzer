Cotoneaster sikkimensis

Cotoneaster sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It typically grows in mountainous regions at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,500 meters. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are pink to red, appearing in clusters, and it produces bright red berries that are a food source for birds.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture for its hardy nature and seasonal color display.
