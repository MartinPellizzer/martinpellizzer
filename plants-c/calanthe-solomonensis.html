Calanthe solomonensis

Calanthe solomonensis, commonly known as Solomon's calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the Solomon Islands.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and linear leaves that are often covered in a dense, woolly pubescence. The plant produces a single spike of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with showy, spurred petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Calanthe solomonensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
