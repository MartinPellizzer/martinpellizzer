Clusia stenocarpa

Clusia stenocarpa, commonly known as the broadleaf clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland tropical forests.

Clusia stenocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in landscaping.
