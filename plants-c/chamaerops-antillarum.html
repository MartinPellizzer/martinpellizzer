Chamaerops antillarum

Chamaerops antillarum, commonly known as the windmill palm, is a species of palm native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to saline and windy environments. Its trunk is slender and often covered with a fibrous, leafy sheath.

Chamaerops antillarum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.
