Cytisogenista haplophylla

Cytisogenista haplophylla, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments, where it adapts to harsh conditions through its deep root system.
