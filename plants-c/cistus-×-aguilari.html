Cistus × aguilari

Cistus × aguilari, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping. Its fragrant foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.

Cistus × aguilari is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small wildlife.
