Catostemma micranthum

Catostemma micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered catostemma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassland and woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, white to pale pink flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom during the cooler months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
