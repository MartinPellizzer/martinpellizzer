Crupina acuta

Crupina acuta, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features rigid, spiny leaves and produces bright blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It has a robust root system that helps it thrive in saline and well-drained environments.

Crupina acuta is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
