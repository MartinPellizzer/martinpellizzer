Caamembeca spectabilis

Caamembeca spectabilis, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a favored choice for landscaping in warm climates.
