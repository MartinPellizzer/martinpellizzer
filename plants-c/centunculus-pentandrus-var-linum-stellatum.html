Centunculus pentandrus var. linum-stellatum

Centunculus pentandrus var. linum-stellatum, commonly known as the starry sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters along the stems. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with hairy, linear leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and rocky habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in its native range.
