Cuphea penicillaria

Cuphea penicillaria, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features upright, branched stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Its unique flower shape and long blooming period make it a popular choice for adding color to landscapes.
