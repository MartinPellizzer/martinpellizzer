Cattleya warscewiczii var. lageriana

Cattleya warscewiczii var. lageriana, commonly known as the Lageriana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, fragrant flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant typically features long, arching inflorescences with large, velvety petals and a prominent, spurred lip. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
