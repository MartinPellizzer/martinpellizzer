Cyperus tenuis

Cyperus tenuis, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

Cyperus tenuis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.
