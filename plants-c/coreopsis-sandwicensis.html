Coreopsis sandwicensis

Coreopsis sandwicensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal areas and dry forests. The plant features bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Coreopsis sandwicensis is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of Hawaii and is considered a rare and endangered species.
