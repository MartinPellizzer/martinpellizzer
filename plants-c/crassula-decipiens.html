Crassula decipiens

Crassula decipiens, commonly known as the "False Crassula" or "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive white or silver underside. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
