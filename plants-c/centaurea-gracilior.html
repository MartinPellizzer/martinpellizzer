Centaurea gracilior

Centaurea gracilior, commonly known as slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers and slender, branched stems. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Centaurea gracilior is often found in open habitats such as scrublands and coastal areas.
