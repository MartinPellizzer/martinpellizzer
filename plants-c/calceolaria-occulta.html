Calceolaria occulta

Calceolaria occulta, commonly known as the hidden slipper orchid, is a member of the Calceolarieae tribe within the orchid family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
