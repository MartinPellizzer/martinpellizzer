Cucurbita maxima convar. semiturbana

Cucurbita maxima convar. semiturbana, commonly known as the Turk's turban squash, is a cultivar of the species Cucurbita maxima.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, turban-shaped fruits that can vary in color from green to orange or yellow. The plant is a vine that produces large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its edible fruits, which are used in various culinary applications.
