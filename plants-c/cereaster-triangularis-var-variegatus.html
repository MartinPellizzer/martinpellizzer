Cereaster triangularis var. variegatus

Cereaster triangularis var. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated triangular orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped leaves and vibrant, variegated foliage that features a mix of green, white, and sometimes yellow hues. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often arranged in a spiky inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
