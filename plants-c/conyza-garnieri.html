Conyza garnieri

Conyza garnieri, commonly known as whiteleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a serrated edge, while the stems are often branched and covered in fine hairs.
