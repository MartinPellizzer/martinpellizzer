Crotalaria willdenowiana subsp. varmae

Crotalaria willdenowiana subsp.

varmae, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and distinctive pod-like seed capsules.

The plant is a legume, meaning it belongs to the Fabaceae family, and it has the ability to fix nitrogen in the soil through its root nodules. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

The subspecies varmae is distinguished by its smaller, more compact growth form compared to other Crotalaria species.
