Cynanchum doianum

Cynanchum doianum, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Cynanchum doianum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
