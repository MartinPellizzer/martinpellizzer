Crowea angustifolia var. angustifolia

Crowea angustifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved crowea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright orange to red tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and fragrant nectar, which attract pollinators like birds and insects.
