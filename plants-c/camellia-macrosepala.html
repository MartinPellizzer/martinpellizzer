Camellia macrosepala

Camellia macrosepala, commonly known as the large-sepal camellia, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. Camellia macrosepala is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile addition to temperate climates.
