Combretum monetaria

Combretum monetaria, commonly known as the silver dollar tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, coin-shaped leaves that are glossy and often silver-white in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by round, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Its unique foliage and adaptability contribute to its appeal in both horticulture and ecological settings.
