Carex khoii

Carex khoii, commonly known as the Khoi sedge, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of brownish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along waterways.

Carex khoii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
