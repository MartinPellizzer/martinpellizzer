Chione elliptica

Chione elliptica, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a silvery, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
