Cunonia × bernieri

Cunonia × bernieri, commonly known as the yellow cunonia, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are yellow, with a distinctive papilionaceous structure, and it produces legume pods that contain seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in various regions with suitable climates.
