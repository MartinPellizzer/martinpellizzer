Cirsium inamoenum var. inamoenum

Cirsium inamoenum var. inamoenum, commonly known as the purple thistle, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.

Its distinctive appearance and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
