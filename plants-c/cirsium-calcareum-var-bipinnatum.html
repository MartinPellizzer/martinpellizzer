Cirsium calcareum var. bipinnatum

Cirsium calcareum var. bipinnatum, commonly known as the limestone thistle, is a perennial plant native to calcareous soils in Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many flowering plants with composite flower heads.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
