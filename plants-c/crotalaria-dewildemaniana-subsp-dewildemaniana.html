Crotalaria dewildemaniana subsp. dewildemaniana

Crotalaria dewildemaniana subsp.

dewildemaniana, commonly known as the dwarf rattlepod, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes, which are followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. Its leaves are opposite and pinnately lobed, with a rough texture.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
