Culcasia seretii

Culcasia seretii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a glossy surface, and it produces small, white flowers that bloom in the evening.

Culcasia seretii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is popular in aquatic gardens and as a houseplant.
