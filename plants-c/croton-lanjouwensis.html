Croton lanjouwensis

Croton lanjouwensis, commonly known as the Lanjouwensis Croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to the lowland rainforests of Indonesia, specifically the islands of Sulawesi and neighboring regions. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful foliage, which can vary from green to shades of red and yellow. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
