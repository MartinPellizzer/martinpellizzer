Cymbidium eburneum var. williamsianum

Cymbidium eburneum var. williamsianum, commonly known as the Williams' orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial orchid characterized by its strap-like, dark green leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
