Cleyera Longicarpa

Cleyera longicarpa is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the family Actinidiaceae. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this evergreen shrub thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its distinctive foliage and unique floral structure make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. While not widely cultivated, it holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitat. Further study could reveal more about its potential uses and conservation status.
