Chrysopogon subtilis

Chrysopogon subtilis, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes numerous grass species.

Chrysopogon subtilis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
