Cambessedesia hilariana var. longifolia

Cambessedesia hilariana var. longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming during the rainy season. The leaves are elongated and have a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a source of nectar for pollinators.
