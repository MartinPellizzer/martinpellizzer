Capsella bursa-pastoris angustiloba

Capsella bursa-pastoris angustiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf velvetleaf, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
