Cissus schlechteri

Cissus schlechteri, commonly known as the African stonecrop, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that features fleshy, thick leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it well-suited for rocky or sandy soils. Its stems can climb or trail, and it often forms dense mats.

The botanical features of Cissus schlechteri make it an attractive and resilient plant for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
