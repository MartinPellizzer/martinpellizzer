Cytisus linkii

Cytisus linkii, commonly known as the Link's broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its slender stems and fragrant blooms make it a distinctive addition to coastal and dry landscapes.

Cytisus linkii is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to its ecological significance.
