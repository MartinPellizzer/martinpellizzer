Choristosoria pteroides

Choristosoria pteroides, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that resemble feathers. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
