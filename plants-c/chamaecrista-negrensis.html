Chamaecrista negrensis

Chamaecrista negrensis, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open woods and along roadsides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
