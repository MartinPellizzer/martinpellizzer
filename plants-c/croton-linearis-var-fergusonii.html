Croton linearis var. fergusonii

Croton linearis var. fergusonii, commonly known as the Ferguson's croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are typically a vibrant green with reddish or orange veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value comes primarily from its foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
