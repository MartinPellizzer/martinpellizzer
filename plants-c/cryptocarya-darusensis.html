Cryptocarya darusensis

Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in axillary or terminal inflorescences, and it produces small, spherical berries that are edible for some bird species.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Cryptocarya darusensis is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
