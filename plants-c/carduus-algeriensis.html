Carduus algeriensis

Carduus algeriensis, commonly known as Algerian thistle, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces a tall flowering stem.

The seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.
