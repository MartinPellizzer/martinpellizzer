Cyclobalanopsis delavayi

Cyclobalanopsis delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's tara, is a species of evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and chestnuts, and is characterized by its hard, durable wood. Due to its ecological importance and limited distribution, it is considered a valuable but vulnerable species.
