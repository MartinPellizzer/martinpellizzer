Cyperus confertus var. debilior

Cyperus confertus var. debilior, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. This variant is typically found in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist to waterlogged soils. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
