Clematopsis bojeri var. macrophylla

Clematopsis bojeri var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf clematopsis, is a unique hybrid plant originating from South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often a striking dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is a shrub-like species that thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
