Cheiridopsis nelii

Cheiridopsis nelii, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
