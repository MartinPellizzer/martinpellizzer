Crotalaria fwamboensis

Crotalaria fwamboensis, commonly known as the Fwambo bean plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly Tanzania, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves, which are typical of the genus Crotalaria. It produces pods that contain small, edible seeds, which are used in local cuisines.

The plant also has medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
