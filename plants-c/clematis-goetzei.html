Clematis goetzei

Clematis goetzei, commonly known as the Goetze's Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets that are elliptical to ovate in shape. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its stems are covered in fine, hairy pubescence, and it prefers well-drained soil and full sun.

Clematis goetzei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and can be trained on trellises or walls.
