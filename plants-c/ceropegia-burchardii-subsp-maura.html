Ceropegia burchardii subsp. maura

Ceropegia burchardii subsp.

maura, commonly known as the Maura milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its trailing stems and fleshy, opposite leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
