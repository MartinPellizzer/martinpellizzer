Callisia warmingiana

Callisia warmingiana, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a tropical perennial belonging to the family Commelinaceae.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its striking silver-variegated leaves and delicate, spider-like inflorescences. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Callisia warmingiana prefers well-draining soil and indirect sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
