Calpurnia subdecandra

Calpurnia subdecandra, commonly known as the Indian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its bark and leaves have been traditionally used in herbal medicine for their potential health benefits.
