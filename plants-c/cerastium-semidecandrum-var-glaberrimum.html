Cerastium semidecandrum var. glaberrimum

Cerastium semidecandrum var. glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant has a mat-forming habit, with hairy stems and alternate, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its flowers are often visited by pollinators like bees and flies, contributing to its ecological significance.
