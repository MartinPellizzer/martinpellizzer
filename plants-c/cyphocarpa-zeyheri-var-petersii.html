Cyphocarpa zeyheri var. petersii

Cyphocarpa zeyheri var. petersii, commonly known as the Zeyher's cyphocarpa, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal.
