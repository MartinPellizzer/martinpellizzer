Collabium nebulosum

Collabium nebulosum, commonly known as the cloud mist plant, is a unique species characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage that resembles clouds.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are covered in a fine, waxy coating that helps retain moisture in its native habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, Collabium nebulosum is considered a conservation concern in many areas.
