Chlorophytum pilosum

Chlorophytum pilosum, commonly known as the curly spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically bluish-green with a waxy coating, giving them a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in various light conditions.

Its foliage is often used in decorative arrangements, and it is also known for its air-purifying properties.
