Crassula thunbergiana

Crassula thunbergiana, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge under stress. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
