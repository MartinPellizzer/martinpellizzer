Centaurea crucheti

Centaurea crucheti, commonly known as the purplehead centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its striking purple-blue flowers are surrounded by spiny bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance.
