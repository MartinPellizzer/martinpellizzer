Cymbidium appendiculatum

Cymbidium appendiculatum, commonly known as the appendiculate orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features tall, upright stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinct, elongated lip.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
