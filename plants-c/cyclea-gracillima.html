Cyclea gracillima

Cyclea gracillima, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched stem and a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs, typical of cycads. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its slow growth and limited distribution, Cyclea gracillima is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
