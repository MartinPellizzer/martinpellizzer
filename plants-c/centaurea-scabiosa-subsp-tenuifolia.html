Centaurea scabiosa subsp. tenuifolia

Centaurea scabiosa subsp.

tenuifolia, commonly known as slender centaury, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia. It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs.

Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters at the ends of the stems. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy surface.

This species is valued in gardens for its ornamental flowers and is often used in wildflower meadows.
