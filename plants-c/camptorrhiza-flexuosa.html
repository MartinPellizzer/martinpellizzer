Camptorrhiza flexuosa

Camptorrhiza flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag root or zigzagged root, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its unique, serpentine root system that twists and turns, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in desert or semi-arid environments.

Camptorrhiza flexuosa is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used by indigenous peoples for various health purposes.
