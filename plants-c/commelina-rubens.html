Commelina rubens

Commelina rubens, commonly known as the red spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking red flowers with a distinctive blue throat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, while its roots are fibrous and rhizomatous.

Commelina rubens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
