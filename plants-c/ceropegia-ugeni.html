Ceropegia ugeni

Ceropegia ugeni, commonly known as the Ugeni milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, yellowish-green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia ugeni is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
