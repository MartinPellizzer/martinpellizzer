Callianthe nivea

Callianthe nivea, commonly known as the white-flowered rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a compact, low-growing habit with succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are white or pale pink, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Callianthe nivea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
