Chaetospora

It is characterized by its intricate thallus structure, which consists of a symbiotic partnership between a fungus and a green alga. Chaetospora species are usually found on tree bark, rocks, or soil in temperate and subtropical regions.

These lichens play an important role in ecological systems by contributing to soil formation and serving as indicators of environmental conditions.
