Convolvulus nyctagineus

Convolvulus nyctagineus, commonly known as nightshade convolvulus, is a flowering plant belonging to the bindweed family, Convolvulaceae.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
