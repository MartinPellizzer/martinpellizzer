Chaenactis Alpina Var. Rubella

Chaenactis alpina var. rubella is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. Known for its vibrant red petals and compact growth habit, this variety stands out among its relatives in the aster family. It thrives in rocky, alpine environments where few plants can survive due to harsh conditions. The plant's ability to bloom in such extreme settings makes it a symbol of resilience and adaptability. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
