Croton sexmetralis

Croton sexmetralis, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from red to orange. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive, spiky, red or orange fruits. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

The botanical features of Croton sexmetralis make it both visually appealing and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
