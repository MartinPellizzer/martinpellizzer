Cyperus compressus var. capillaceus

Cyperus compressus var. capillaceus, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale green to brown flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or muddy habitats, such as marshes and along waterways. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical structure is adapted to thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments.
