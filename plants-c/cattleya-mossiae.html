Cattleya mossiae

Cattleya mossiae, commonly known as the Mossia's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has long, arching stems and produces multiple blooms during the growing season. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Cattleya mossiae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and fragrance.
