Cheilanthes lozanoi var. seemannii

Cheilanthes lozanoi var. seemannii, commonly known as the Sikkim rock fern, is a perennial fern belonging to the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and is typically found growing on rocky substrates in high-altitude regions. The plant has a rhizomatous structure that allows it to thrive in harsh, arid environments. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a dark green color with a waxy surface.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to extreme conditions and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
