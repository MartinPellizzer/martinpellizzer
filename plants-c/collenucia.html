Collenucia

Collenucia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants typically have showy, daisy-like flowers with white or yellow petals and a central disk. They are often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. The leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and the plants are drought-tolerant.

Collenucia species are popular in gardens for their ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
