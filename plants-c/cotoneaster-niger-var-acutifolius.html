Cotoneaster niger var. acutifolius

Cotoneaster niger var. acutifolius, commonly known as the black cotoneaster, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features dark, glossy leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a pointed tip. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its dense foliage and winter interest.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
