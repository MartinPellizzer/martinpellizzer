Cymbopogon laniger

Cymbopogon laniger, commonly known as silver grass or silver reed, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and tall, slender flowering stalks. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are aromatic and have been used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Cymbopogon laniger is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
