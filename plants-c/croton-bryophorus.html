Croton bryophorus

Croton bryophorus, commonly known as the moss croton, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, moss-like leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Croton bryophorus thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique foliage and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
