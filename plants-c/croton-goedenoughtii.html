Croton goedenoughtii

Croton goedenoughtii, commonly known as the Goedenougtii croton, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking, palmate leaves with vibrant green and yellow hues, often adorned with red veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
