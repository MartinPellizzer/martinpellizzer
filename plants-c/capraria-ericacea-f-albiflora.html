Capraria ericacea f. albiflora

Capraria ericacea f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland or moorland environments. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
