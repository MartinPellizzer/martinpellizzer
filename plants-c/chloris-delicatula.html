Chloris delicatula

Chloris delicatula, commonly known as the slender grass or slender blue grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale blue or white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its delicate appearance and drought tolerance, it is often used in native plant gardens.
