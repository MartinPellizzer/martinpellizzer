Clidemia Cymosa

Clidemia cymosa is a fast-growing shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. It is known for its dense foliage and ability to colonize disturbed areas quickly. This plant has become an invasive species in many regions, outcompeting native vegetation. Its rapid spread poses a significant threat to local ecosystems. Understanding its biology and impact is crucial for effective management and conservation efforts.
