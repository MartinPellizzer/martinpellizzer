Croton ekmanii

Croton ekmanii, commonly known as the Kikuyu croton, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often features vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton ekmanii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
