Cerris oliviformis

Cerris oliviformis, commonly known as the olive ash or olive tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oleaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Cerris oliviformis is well adapted to coastal and dry environments, making it a resilient plant in diverse ecological settings.
