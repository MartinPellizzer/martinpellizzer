Coleus Leiperi

Coleus leiperi is a unique and lesser-known member of the mint family native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This plant is prized for its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a wide range of hues including red, purple, and green. While it shares some similarities with other coleus species, Coleus leiperi has distinct characteristics that set it apart in both appearance and growth habits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the horticultural world, making it a fascinating subject for plant enthusiasts and botanists alike.
