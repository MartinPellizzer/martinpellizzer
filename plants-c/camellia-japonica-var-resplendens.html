Camellia japonica var. resplendens

Camellia japonica var. resplendens, commonly known as the Japanese camellia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink, often with a contrasting central blotch. The plant produces elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained, acidic soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is noted for its winter blooming habit, adding ornamental value to gardens during the colder months.
