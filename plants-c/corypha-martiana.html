Corypha martiana

Corypha martiana, commonly known as the giant swamp taro, is a large herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces a tall, spadix inflorescence surrounded by a large, showy purple bract. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks.

Corypha martiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
