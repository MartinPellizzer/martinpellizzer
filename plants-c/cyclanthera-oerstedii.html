Cyclanthera oerstedii

Cyclanthera oerstedii, commonly known as the Oersted's bitter gourd, is a climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces elongated, spiny fruits that are typically green when mature, though they may change color as they ripen. Its leaves are palmately lobed and have a distinctive appearance, while its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The plant is valued for its edible fruits, which are often used in traditional cuisines despite their bitter taste.
