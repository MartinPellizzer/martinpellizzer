Cymbidium × purpuratum

Cymbidium × purpuratum, commonly known as the orchid with a purple-tinged bloom, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces large, lance-shaped leaves and blooms in late winter to early spring. Its flowers are often bicolor, featuring a combination of white, yellow, and purple hues.

Cymbidium × purpuratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
