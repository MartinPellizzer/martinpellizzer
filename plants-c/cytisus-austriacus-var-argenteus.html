Cytisus austriacus var. argenteus

Cytisus austriacus var. argenteus, commonly known as the silver flowering broom, is a shrub native to central Europe.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
