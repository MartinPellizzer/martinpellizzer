Coleus atropurpureus var. ramosus

Coleus atropurpureus var. ramosus, commonly known as Purple Leaf Nerve Plant, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple foliage with contrasting green veins, which give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant color make it a favored addition to hanging baskets and container gardens.
