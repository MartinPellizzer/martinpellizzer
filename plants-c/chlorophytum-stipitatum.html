Chlorophytum stipitatum

Chlorophytum stipitatum, commonly known as the shawl lily, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, or orange, which appear on slender stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
