Cyathea indefinita

Cyathea indefinita, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Cyathea indefinita thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
