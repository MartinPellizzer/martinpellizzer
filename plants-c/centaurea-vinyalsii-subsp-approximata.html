Centaurea vinyalsii subsp. approximata

Centaurea vinyalsii subsp.

approximata, commonly known as the Valencia centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its upright, branched stems and striking violet-blue flowers.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and wildflower displays.
