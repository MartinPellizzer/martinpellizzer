Carex divulsa subsp. orsiniana

Carex divulsa subsp.

orsiniana, commonly known as the Orsinian sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family. It is characterized by its grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers.

This subspecies is typically found in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense stands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
