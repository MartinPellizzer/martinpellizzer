Crockeria chrysantha

Crockeria chrysantha, commonly known as the golden crockeria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically bright green and grow in a dense rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant and long-lasting blooms.
