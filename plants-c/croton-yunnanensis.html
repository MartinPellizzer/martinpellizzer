Croton yunnanensis

Croton yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan croton, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from green to red, yellow, and orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.

Croton yunnanensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in subtropical and temperate regions.
