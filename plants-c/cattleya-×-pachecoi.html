Cattleya × pachecoi

Cattleya × pachecoi, commonly known as the Pacheco's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and violet, often with intricate patterns. The plant is native to Brazil and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive periods of drought.

This orchid is popular among growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
