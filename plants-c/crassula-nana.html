Crassula nana

Crassula nana, commonly known as the baby crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
