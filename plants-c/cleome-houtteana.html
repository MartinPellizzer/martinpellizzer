Cleome houtteana

Cleome houtteana, commonly known as spider flower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery flowers that resemble spider legs, typically blooming in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant is an annual or biennial herb with slender, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Cleome houtteana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
