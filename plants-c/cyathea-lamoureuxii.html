Cyathea lamoureuxii

Cyathea lamoureuxii, commonly known as the Lamoureux's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a unique appearance with a smooth, fibrous trunk that can grow up to several meters in height. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a dark green color and a waxy texture.

Cyathea lamoureuxii is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
