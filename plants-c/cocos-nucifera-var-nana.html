Cocos nucifera var. nana

Cocos nucifera var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf coconut palm, is a compact variety of the coconut tree.

It features a short, stocky trunk and a dense crown of feathery leaves. The plant produces small, rounded coconut fruits that mature in about 8 to 12 months. It thrives in warm, tropical climates with well-drained soil.

Its unique size makes it suitable for ornamental landscaping and small-scale cultivation.
