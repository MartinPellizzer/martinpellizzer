Canangium odoratum

Canangium odoratum, commonly known as the sacred lotus or holy water plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found near water bodies. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often used in traditional rituals.

The plant's botanical significance is enhanced by its role in cultural and religious practices across various regions.
