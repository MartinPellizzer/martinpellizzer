Convolvulus demissus var. ovatus

Convolvulus demissus var. ovatus, commonly known as the slender morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of purple and blue. The plant has opposite, hairy leaves and twining stems that climb or trail along the ground. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a versatile plant for ornamental landscaping.
