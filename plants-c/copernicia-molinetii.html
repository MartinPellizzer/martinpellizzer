Copernicia molinetii

Copernicia molinetii, commonly known as the Molinet's copernicia, is a species of palm native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its slender, slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and windy conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
