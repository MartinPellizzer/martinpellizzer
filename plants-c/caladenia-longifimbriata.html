Caladenia longifimbriata

Caladenia longifimbriata, commonly known as the long-fringed spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and a single, showy flower with a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, producing a pale yellow or greenish-yellow flower with intricate patterns. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
