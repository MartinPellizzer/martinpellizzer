Cyathea novae-zelandiae

Cyathea novae-zelandiae, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to 10 meters in height, with a trunk that is covered in a fibrous, spongy layer. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters.

Cyathea novae-zelandiae plays an important ecological role in New Zealand's forests, providing habitat and contributing to the local biodiversity.
