Cotula dielsii

Cotula dielsii, commonly known as the dwarf daisy or small daisy, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with slender stems and small, fuzzy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Cotula dielsii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
