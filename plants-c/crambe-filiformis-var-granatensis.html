Crambe filiformis var. granatensis

Crambe filiformis var. granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of salt-resistant hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
