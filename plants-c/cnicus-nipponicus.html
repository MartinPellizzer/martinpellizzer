Cnicus nipponicus

Cnicus nipponicus, commonly known as Japanese thistle, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Cnicus nipponicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
