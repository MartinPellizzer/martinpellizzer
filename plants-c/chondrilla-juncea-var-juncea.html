Chondrilla juncea var. juncea

Chondrilla juncea var. juncea, commonly known as slender goatweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, sandy or gravelly soils and is characterized by its low-growing, spreading habit. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, blooming from late spring to early summer.

It is often found in disturbed areas and is considered a weed in some agricultural contexts.
