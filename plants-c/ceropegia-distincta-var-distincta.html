Ceropegia distincta var. distincta

Ceropegia distincta var. distincta, commonly known as the string of turtles, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and pairs of opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia distincta var. distincta is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
