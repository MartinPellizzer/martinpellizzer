Cybianthus guyanensis subsp. guyanensis

Cybianthus guyanensis subsp.

guyanensis, commonly known as the Guyana candlewood, is a tropical tree native to South America. It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark green foliage.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are often fragrant. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic qualities.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.
