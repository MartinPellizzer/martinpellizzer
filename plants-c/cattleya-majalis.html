Cattleya majalis

Cattleya majalis, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers. The plant features a single, showy bloom with a pink or white lip, and its leaves are broad and strap-like. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cattleya majalis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
