Chrysostachys ovatifolia

Chrysostachys ovatifolia, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

The bark and leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
