Cleomella longipes var. grandiflora

Cleomella longipes var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated plant communities.
