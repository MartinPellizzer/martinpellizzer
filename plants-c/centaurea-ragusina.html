Centaurea ragusina

Centaurea ragusina, commonly known as the Ragusan centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea ragusina is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
