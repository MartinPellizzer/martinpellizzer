Colchicum melanthioides subsp. transvaalense

Colchicum melanthioides subsp.

transvaalense, commonly known as the Transvaal colchicum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa. It produces striking purple to violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.

The plant has a distinctive, elongated corm that stores nutrients for the next growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
