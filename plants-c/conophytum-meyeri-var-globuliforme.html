Conophytum meyeri var. globuliforme

Conophytum meyeri var. globuliforme, commonly known as the globe-shaped conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded leaves that form a dense, globular shape, resembling a small, fleshy ball. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
