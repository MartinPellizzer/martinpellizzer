Clematis caripensis

Clematis caripensis, commonly known as the Kauai clematis, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in tropical gardens.

Clematis caripensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing nectar for pollinators.
