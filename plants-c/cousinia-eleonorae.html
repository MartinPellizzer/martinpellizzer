Cousinia eleonorae

Cousinia eleonorae, commonly known as the Eleanor's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

Cousinia eleonorae is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
