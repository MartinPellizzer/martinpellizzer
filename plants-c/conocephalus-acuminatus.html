Conocephalus acuminatus

Conocephalus acuminatus, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical inflorescence composed of spikelets, which are typically green to brown in color. Conocephalus acuminatus plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic invertebrates.
