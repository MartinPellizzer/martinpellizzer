Coelogyne fimbriata var. leungiana

Coelogyne fimbriata var. leungiana, commonly known as the Leung's fringe orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces long, narrow petals and sepals with a distinctive fringed edge, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
