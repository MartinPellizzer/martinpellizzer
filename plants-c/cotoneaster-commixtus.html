Cotoneaster commixtus

Cotoneaster commixtus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Cotoneaster commixtus is known for its compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
