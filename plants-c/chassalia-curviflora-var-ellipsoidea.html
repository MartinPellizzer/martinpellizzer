Chassalia curviflora var. ellipsoidea

Chassalia curviflora var. ellipsoidea, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chassalia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, elliptical leaves and produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
