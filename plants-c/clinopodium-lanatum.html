Clinopodium lanatum

Clinopodium lanatum, commonly known as woolly mountain mint, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Clinopodium lanatum plays a role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its habitat.
