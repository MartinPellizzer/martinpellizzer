Cimicifuga dahurica var. fertilis

Cimicifuga dahurica var. fertilis, commonly known as Chinese bugbane, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features tall, sturdy stems with large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has compound leaves with feather-like divisions, and its roots are used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties.
