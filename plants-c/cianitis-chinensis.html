Cianitis chinensis

Cianitis chinensis, commonly known as Chinese bluebell, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Cianitis chinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
