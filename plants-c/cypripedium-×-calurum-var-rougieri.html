Cypripedium × calurum var. rougieri

Cypripedium × calurum var. rougieri, commonly known as the Rougieri's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces a single, large, fragrant bloom each year. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
