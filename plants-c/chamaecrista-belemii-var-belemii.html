Chamaecrista belemii var. belemii

Chamaecrista belemii var. belemii, commonly known as the Brazilian burr legume, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and traditional uses.
