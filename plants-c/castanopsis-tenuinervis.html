Castanopsis tenuinervis

Castanopsis tenuinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined talaud, is a species of evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and chestnuts, and is characterized by its hard, durable wood. The species is valued for its ecological role in forest ecosystems and its potential for sustainable timber use.
