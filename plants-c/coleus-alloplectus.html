Coleus alloplectus

Coleus alloplectus, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is known for its ornamental value due to its striking leaves. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can display a variety of colors including red, purple, and green.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
