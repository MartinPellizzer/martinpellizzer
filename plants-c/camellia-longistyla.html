Camellia longistyla

Camellia longistyla, commonly known as the long-styled camellia, is a species native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces striking, large, showy flowers with deep red petals and prominent yellow stamens. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter blooms. Its leaves are elliptical, dark green, and have a smooth texture.

Camellia longistyla is also appreciated for its historical and cultural significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
