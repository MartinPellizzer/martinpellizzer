Cnicus lanceolatus

Cnicus lanceolatus, commonly known as the common centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with yellow flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and has a fibrous root system. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Cnicus lanceolatus is also known for its historical use in traditional medicine.
