Clivia miniata var. flava

Clivia miniata var. flava, commonly known as the yellow clivia, is a striking evergreen perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant yellow flowers. The flowers are tubular and clustered in loose inflorescences, blooming in spring and early summer. This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering.

Its botanical beauty makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and as a houseplant.
