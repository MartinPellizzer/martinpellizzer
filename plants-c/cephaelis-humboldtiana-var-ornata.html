Cephaelis humboldtiana var. ornata

Cephaelis humboldtiana var. ornata, commonly known as the golden flame bush, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant floral display and compact growth habit.

It is often used in garden landscapes for its visual appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
