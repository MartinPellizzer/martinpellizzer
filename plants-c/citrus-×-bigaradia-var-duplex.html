Citrus × bigaradia var. duplex

Citrus × bigaradia var. duplex, commonly known as the sour orange.

This hybrid citrus plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically yellow when ripe. The leaves are glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated edge. It is a vigorous grower with a bushy habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

The plant is also valued for its aromatic peel, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
