Cyrtophlebium phyllitidis

Cyrtophlebium phyllitidis, commonly known as the velvet leaf cycad, is a unique cycad species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery, dark green foliage that gives it a velvety appearance. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and conservation status, it is a subject of interest in botanical research and horticulture.
