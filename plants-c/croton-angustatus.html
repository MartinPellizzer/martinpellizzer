Croton angustatus

Croton angustatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Croton angustatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and resilience in various soil conditions.
