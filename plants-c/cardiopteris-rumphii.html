Cardiopteris rumphii

Cardiopteris rumphii, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or shaded environments, often on cliffs or in mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help it anchor to steep surfaces.
