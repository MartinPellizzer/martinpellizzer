Cyperus cubensis

Cyperus cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Cyperus cubensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
