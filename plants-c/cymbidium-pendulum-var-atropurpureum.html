Cymbidium pendulum var. atropurpureum

Cymbidium pendulum var. atropurpureum, commonly known as the purple-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces long, pendant inflorescences. The leaves are broad, elliptical, and often have a distinctive purple or reddish hue, especially under sunlight. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink with a deep purple lip, adding to its ornamental value.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its striking foliage and elegant blooms.
