Cistus sericeus

Cistus sericeus, commonly known as silver cistus, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited to arid environments. Its aromatic foliage contains essential oils, which contribute to its use in traditional herbal remedies.

Cistus sericeus also plays a role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife in its native habitat.
