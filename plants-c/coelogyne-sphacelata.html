Coelogyne sphacelata

Coelogyne sphacelata, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow pseudobulbs and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base of the pseudobulbs. It blooms during the dry season and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Coelogyne sphacelata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
