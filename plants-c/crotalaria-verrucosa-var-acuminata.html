Crotalaria verrucosa var. acuminata

Crotalaria verrucosa var. acuminata, commonly known as the acuminated crotalaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, South America, and parts of Asia. The plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands, woodland edges, and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
