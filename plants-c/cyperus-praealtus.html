Cyperus praealtus

Cyperus praealtus, commonly known as the tall flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green or brown in color. It is often found in wetland habitats, including marshes and along the edges of water bodies.

Cyperus praealtus is a perennial plant that can spread through rhizomes, making it a common component of aquatic and semi-aquatic ecosystems.
