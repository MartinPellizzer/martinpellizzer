Cattleya intermedia f. parthenia

Cattleya intermedia f. parthenia, commonly known as the Parthenia Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid with striking floral characteristics.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant is known for its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. It produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts.
