Cryptocoryne tortilis

Cryptocoryne tortilis, commonly known as the twisted cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally twisted leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams. Its leaves are dark green and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Cryptocoryne tortilis is popular in aquariums due to its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.
