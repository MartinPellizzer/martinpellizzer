Cyclosorus pilosiusculus

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like fronds that are covered in fine, hairy structures, giving it a distinctive appearance. Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is often found in humid, shaded areas where it can thrive without direct sunlight.
