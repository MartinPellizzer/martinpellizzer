Convolvulus dufourea

Convolvulus dufourea, commonly known as the golden morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and twining stems that climb or trail along the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to ornamental landscaping.
