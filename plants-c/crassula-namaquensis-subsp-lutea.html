Crassula namaquensis subsp. lutea

Crassula namaquensis subsp.

lutea, commonly known as the Orange Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or orange tinge, especially under sunny conditions.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
