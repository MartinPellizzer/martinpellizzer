Cercidiphyllum japonicum var. sinense

Cercidiphyllum japonicum var. sinense, commonly known as the Chinese katsura tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that turn a striking yellow in the fall. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers in early spring, followed by smooth, light brown fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and parks.
