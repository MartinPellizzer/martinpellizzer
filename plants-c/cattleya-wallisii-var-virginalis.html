Cattleya wallisii var. virginalis

Cattleya wallisii var. virginalis, commonly known as the White Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, fragrant white flowers.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. This variety is known for its elegant, waxy petals and a strong, sweet scent that attracts pollinators. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.

Its popularity in horticulture is due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
