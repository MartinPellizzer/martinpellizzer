Ciliochloa elegans

Ciliochloa elegans, commonly known as the elegant hairgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and distinctive, hairy inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. Its slender, grass-like leaves are often used in horticultural displays for their ornamental value.

Ciliochloa elegans is well adapted to coastal conditions, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.
