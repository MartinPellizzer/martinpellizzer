Coreopsis stillmanii

Coreopsis stillmanii, commonly known as the stillmanii coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, ornamental plants.
