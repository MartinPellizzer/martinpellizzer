Croton suassunae

Croton suassunae, commonly known as the Brazilian croton, is a shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. Its leaves are known for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional medicine.

Croton suassunae thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
