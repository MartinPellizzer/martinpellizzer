Cotula anthemoides

Cotula anthemoides, commonly known as the common groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves and produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Cotula anthemoides is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal remedies.
