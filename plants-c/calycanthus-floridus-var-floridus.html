Calycanthus floridus var. floridus

Calycanthus floridus var. floridus, commonly known as the sweetshrub, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features fragrant, reddish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a glossy texture and dark green color. Its bark is reddish-brown and becomes fissured with age.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.
