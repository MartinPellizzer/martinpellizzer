Chirita lunglinensis var. amblyosepala

Chirita lunglinensis var. amblyosepala, commonly known as the narrow-sepaled chirita, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers.
