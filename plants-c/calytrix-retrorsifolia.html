Calytrix retrorsifolia

Calytrix retrorsifolia, commonly known as the red-flowered rock heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or inland regions. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, with a leathery texture, and it produces small, woody fruits.

Calytrix retrorsifolia is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in coastal heathlands.
