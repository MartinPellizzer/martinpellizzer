Croton sutup

Croton sutup, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that range from green to red and orange. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its striking foliage, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
