Connarus salomoniensis

Connarus salomoniensis, commonly known as the Solomon Islands ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the Solomon Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by woody fruits containing several seeds.

Connarus salomoniensis is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
