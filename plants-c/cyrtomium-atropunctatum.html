Cyrtomium atropunctatum

Cyrtomium atropunctatum, commonly known as the black-punctured holly fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery fronds with a dark green coloration. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
