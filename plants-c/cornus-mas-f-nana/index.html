Cornus Mas F. Nana

Cornus mas f. nana is a unique variant of the cornelian cherry known for its compact growth habit. This shrub is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions. It produces small yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that attract wildlife. Its dwarf size makes it ideal for small gardens or container planting. Despite its modest stature, it holds significant horticultural appeal.
