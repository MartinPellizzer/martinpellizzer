Cleidion javanicum

Cleidion javanicum, commonly known as the Javan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

Cleidion javanicum is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitats.
