Chomelia buchananii

Chomelia buchananii, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters and are often used in ornamental horticulture. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Chomelia buchananii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
