Citrus × nobilis var. ponki

Citrus × nobilis var. ponki, commonly known as the Ponkan mandarin.

This variety is a hybrid citrus fruit originating from Southeast Asia, characterized by its small, round, and smooth skin. The fruit typically has a sweet and tangy flavor with a high juice content. It is often used in both culinary and ornamental contexts due to its attractive appearance and pleasant taste.

The plant is evergreen, with glossy leaves and is well-suited for subtropical climates.
