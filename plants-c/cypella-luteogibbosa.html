Cypella luteogibbosa

Cypella luteogibbosa, commonly known as the yellow-tubercled cypella, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow, tuberculate (wart-like) stems. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

Cypella luteogibbosa is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
