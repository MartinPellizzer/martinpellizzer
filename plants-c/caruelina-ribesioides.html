Caruelina ribesioides

Caruelina ribesioides, commonly known as the red-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Caruelina ribesioides is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
