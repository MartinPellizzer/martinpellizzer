Cibotium princeps

Cibotium princeps, commonly known as the Prince's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of overlapping, glossy green fronds that give it a lush, tropical appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The fronds are used in traditional Hawaiian culture for ceremonial and practical purposes.
