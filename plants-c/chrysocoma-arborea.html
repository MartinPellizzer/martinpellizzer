Chrysocoma arborea

Chrysocoma arborea, commonly known as the tree heather, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in coastal and rocky habitats. The plant produces small, showy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a dense canopy.

Chrysocoma arborea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soils.
