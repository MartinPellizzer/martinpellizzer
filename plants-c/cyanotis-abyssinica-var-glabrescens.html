Cyanotis abyssinica var. glabrescens

Cyanotis abyssinica var. glabrescens, commonly known as the Abyssinian gentian, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, smooth texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
