Capparis scandens

Capparis scandens, commonly known as the climbing cucumber or sour cabbage, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or climbing plant with woody stems and thorny branches. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by fleshy, edible fruits.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
