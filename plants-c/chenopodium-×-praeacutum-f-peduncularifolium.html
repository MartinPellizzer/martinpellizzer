Chenopodium × praeacutum f. peduncularifolium

Chenopodium × praeacutum f. peduncularifolium, commonly known as the curled leaf goosefoot, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curled leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The inflorescence consists of small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads.
