Cleanthe melaleuca

Cleanthe melaleuca, commonly known as the broad-leafed tea tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small white or pink flowers that are clustered in spikes, which bloom in spring. Its bark is smooth and often peels in thin layers, revealing a reddish or greyish inner surface.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and has a long history of cultural significance among Indigenous Australian communities.
