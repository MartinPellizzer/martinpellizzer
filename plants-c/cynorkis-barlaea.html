Cynorkis barlaea

Cynorkis barlaea, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

Cynorkis barlaea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
