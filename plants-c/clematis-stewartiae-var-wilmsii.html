Clematis stewartiae var. wilmsii

Clematis stewartiae var. wilmsii, commonly known as the Wilmsii Clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or fences.
