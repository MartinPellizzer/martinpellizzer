Croton platanifolius

Croton platanifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its striking foliage.
