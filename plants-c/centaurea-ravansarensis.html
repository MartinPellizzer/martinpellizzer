Centaurea ravansarensis

Centaurea ravansarensis, commonly known as the Ravansar centaury, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features bright blue to purple ray florets surrounding a central disc of yellow tubular flowers. It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture and botanical gardens.
