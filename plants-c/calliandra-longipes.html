Calliandra longipes

Calliandra longipes, commonly known as the longpod calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.

Calliandra longipes is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, benefiting soil fertility in agricultural settings.
