Chenopodium badachschanicum

Chenopodium badachschanicum, commonly known as the Badakshan goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial species that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and are pollinated by wind.

The seeds are small and dry, often dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
