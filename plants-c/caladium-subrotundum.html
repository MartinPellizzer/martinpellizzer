Caladium subrotundum

Caladium subrotundum, commonly known as the rounded caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, brightly colored leaves that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.

Caladium subrotundum is typically grown as an annual in temperate climates due to its sensitivity to frost.
