Crataegus delosii

Crataegus delosii, commonly known as the Delosii hawthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly in the Balkans. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and the plant often grows as a shrub or small tree.

Crataegus delosii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
