Cuphea ciliata f. vestita

Cuphea ciliata f. vestita, commonly known as the velvet cuphea, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its dense, velvety foliage.

The unique texture of its leaves adds visual interest, making it a popular choice for modern landscaping designs.
