Clermontia carinifera

Clermontia carinifera, commonly known as the Kauai slender lady's tresses, is a rare orchid native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and a single, slender flower spike that bears several small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in montane forests. It is listed as endangered due to habitat loss and invasive species.

Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect and propagate this unique botanical species.
