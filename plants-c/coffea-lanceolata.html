Coffea lanceolata

Coffea lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate coffee.

The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves and bears small white flowers that develop into red berries containing the seeds used for coffee. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
