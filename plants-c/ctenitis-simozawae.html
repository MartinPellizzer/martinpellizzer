Ctenitis simozawae

Ctenitis simozawae, commonly known as the Japanese tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its fronds are typically light green and can develop a reddish tint at the base.

Ctenitis simozawae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to shaded, moist environments.
