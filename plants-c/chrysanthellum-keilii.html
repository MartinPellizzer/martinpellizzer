Chrysanthellum keilii

Chrysanthellum keilii, commonly known as the Keil's chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

Chrysanthellum keilii is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
