Cymbidium × nujiangense

Cymbidium × nujiangense, commonly known as the Nujiang orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces large, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale pink blooms. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
