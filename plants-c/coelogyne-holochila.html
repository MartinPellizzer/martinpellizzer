Coelogyne holochila

Coelogyne holochila, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green coloration, with long, slender petals and a prominent lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often appearing in late winter or early spring.

Coelogyne holochila is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
