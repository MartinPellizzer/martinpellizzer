Ceropegia villetiae

Ceropegia villetiae, commonly known as the string of hearts or the heartleaf fig, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia villetiae is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
