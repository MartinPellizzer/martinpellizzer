Centaurea biokovensis

Centaurea biokovensis, commonly known as the Biokov centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Biokovo Mountains in Croatia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and slender, branching stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a grayish-green color.

Centaurea biokovensis is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
