Cephalomanes Javanicum Var. Sumatranum

Cephalomanes javanicum var. sumatranum is a unique and lesser-known species of sea anemone found primarily in the coastal waters of Sumatra. This variant stands out for its distinctive morphological features and ecological role within its marine habitat. Despite its beauty and importance, it remains under-researched and often overlooked in marine biodiversity studies. Its presence highlights the rich and diverse ecosystems of the Indo-Pacific region. Understanding this species is crucial for conservation efforts and marine ecological balance.
