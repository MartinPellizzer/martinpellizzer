Cleyera japonica var. montana

Cleyera japonica var. montana, commonly known as mountain cleyera, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in shady gardens.
