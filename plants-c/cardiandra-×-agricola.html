Cardiandra × agricola

Cardiandra × agricola, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a cross between *Cardiandra* and *Hibiscus agricola*.

It is a tropical shrub characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Cardiandra × agricola is relatively easy to care for, requiring regular watering and partial to full sunlight.
