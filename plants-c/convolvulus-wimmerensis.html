Convolvulus wimmerensis

Convolvulus wimmerensis, commonly known as Wimmer's bindweed, is a flowering plant native to South Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, twining stems that often climb or trail along the ground. Its small, funnel-shaped flowers are usually pale purple or white, with five petals.

The leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
