Coelogyne ventricosa

Coelogyne ventricosa, commonly known as the bubble orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, inflated pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Coelogyne ventricosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its striking floral displays.
