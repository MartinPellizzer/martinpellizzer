Clematis peterae

Clematis peterae, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, sweet fragrance. The plant produces small, round berries that turn red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Clematis peterae is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
