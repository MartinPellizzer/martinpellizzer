Calanthe moluccensis

Calanthe moluccensis, commonly known as the Moluccan calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the Moluccas and surrounding islands in Indonesia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces strikingly colorful, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in hue. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Calanthe moluccensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
