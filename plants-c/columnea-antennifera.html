Columnea antennifera

Columnea antennifera, commonly known as the feather-leaf columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive feathery appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Columnea antennifera is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
