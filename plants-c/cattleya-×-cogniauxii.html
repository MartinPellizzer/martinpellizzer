Cattleya × cogniauxii

Cattleya × cogniauxii, commonly known as the Cogniaux's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and yellow. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
