Cypripedium × vernixium var. atratum

Cypripedium × vernixium var. atratum, commonly known as the black-lip lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its unique and striking floral morphology.

It features a large, velvety purple or black lip that is distinctly shaped and often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in cool, moist forest environments, where it thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting specific pollinators such as bees and flies.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
