Cryptocoryne tirtadinatae

Cryptocoryne tirtadinatae, commonly known as the Tirtadinatae cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features dark green, waxy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden beneath its leaves.

Cryptocoryne tirtadinatae is popular in aquariums due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.
