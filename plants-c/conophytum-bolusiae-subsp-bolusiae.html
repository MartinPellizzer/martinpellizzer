Conophytum bolusiae subsp. bolusiae

Conophytum bolusiae subsp.

bolusiae, commonly known as the Bolus's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, rounded leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
