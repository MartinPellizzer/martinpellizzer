Carex orbicularis subsp. altaica

Carex orbicularis subsp.

altaica, commonly known as the Altaian sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its rosette-like growth habit and produces dense, cylindrical spikelets that bear small, triangular flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often in alpine or subalpine habitats. Its leaves are broad and flat, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green coloration.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude environments.
