Chesneya yunnanensis

Chesneya yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan chesneya, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, tubular florets arranged in a composite head.

Chesneya yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
