Campanula chinensis

Campanula chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Campanula chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
