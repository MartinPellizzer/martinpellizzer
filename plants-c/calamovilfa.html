Calamovilfa

Calamovilfa, commonly known as the giant reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to wetland areas.

It has long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It is often used in landscaping for erosion control due to its strong root system.

Calamovilfa thrives in moist environments and can grow up to several meters in height.
