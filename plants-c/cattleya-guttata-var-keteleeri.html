Cattleya guttata var. keteleeri

Cattleya guttata var. keteleeri, commonly known as the Keteleeri Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking dark green to blackish-purple petals and sepals, often with a contrasting white or light green band. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in a horizontal growth pattern with pseudobulbs.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
