Centaurea Phrygia Subsp. Abnormis

Centaurea phrygia subsp. abnormis is a rare and intriguing plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique floral morphology and coloration. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study.
