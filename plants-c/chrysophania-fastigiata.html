Chrysophania fastigiata

Chrysophania fastigiata, commonly known as the stiffstem goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in panicles. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and ecologically valuable plant for pollinators.
