Cheirostylis gymnochiloides

Cheirostylis gymnochiloides, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features slender, upright pseudobulbs that support its linear, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive narrow lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
