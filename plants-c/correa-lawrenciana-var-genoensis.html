Correa lawrenciana var. genoensis

Correa lawrenciana var. genoensis, commonly known as the Genoese Correa, is a compact shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces striking clusters of tubular, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and drought tolerance in garden settings.
