Cyclamen pentelici

Cyclamen pentelici, commonly known as the Greek cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its blooming period typically occurs in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Cyclamen pentelici is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
