Cyclosorus didymosorus

Cyclosorus didymosorus, commonly known as the two-lobed liverwort, is a small, thalloid liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a dorsiventral thallus with distinct lobes and a central axis. It reproduces via spores, which are released from cup-like structures called sporophytes.

This species is often used in ecological studies due to its sensitivity to environmental changes.
