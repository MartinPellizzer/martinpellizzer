Ceradenia spixiana

Ceradenia spixiana, commonly known as the Spix's ceradenia, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation programs.
