Cirsium × haussmannii var. ausserdorferi

Cirsium × haussmannii var. ausserdorferi, commonly known as the Austrian thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit and is typically found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant features large, spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads that bloom in late summer. It is native to Central Europe and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its striking appearance.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
