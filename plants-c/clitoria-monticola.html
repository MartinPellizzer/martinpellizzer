Clitoria monticola

Clitoria monticola, commonly known as the mountain blue pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod-bearing fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces vibrant purple or blue pea-like flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Clitoria monticola is also valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
