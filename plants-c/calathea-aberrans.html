Calathea aberrans

Calathea aberrans, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage with intricate patterns of silver and white veins. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a waxy texture that gives them a unique, almost metallic appearance. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
