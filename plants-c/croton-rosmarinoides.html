Croton rosmarinoides

Croton rosmarinoides, commonly known as the fragrant croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that emit a pleasant scent when crushed. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton rosmarinoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and distinctive fragrance.
