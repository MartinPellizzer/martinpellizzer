Cerastium latifolium var. compactum

Cerastium latifolium var. compactum, commonly known as the compact snowdrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, hairy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or rocky crevices.

Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive and hardy ground cover for garden settings.
