Campanula pinnatifida var. germanicopolitana

Campanula pinnatifida var. germanicopolitana, commonly known as the Germanicopolitan bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically blue to purple in color. The leaves are pinnately divided, giving the plant a delicate, lace-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
