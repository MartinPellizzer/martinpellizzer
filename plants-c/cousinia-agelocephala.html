Cousinia agelocephala

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant is known for its dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in harsh environments. It produces showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the spring and summer.
