Cynanchum hoedimeerium subsp. hoedimeerium

Cynanchum hoedimeerium subsp.

hoedimeerium, commonly known as the Hoedimeer's milkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky outcrops, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters.

The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds with silky appendages, aiding in wind dispersal.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional herbal remedies.
