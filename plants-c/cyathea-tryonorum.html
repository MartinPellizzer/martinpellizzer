Cyathea tryonorum

Cyathea tryonorum, commonly known as the Tryon's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its striking appearance.

Cyathea tryonorum plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
