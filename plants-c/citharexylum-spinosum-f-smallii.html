Citharexylum spinosum f. smallii

Citharexylum spinosum f. smallii, commonly known as the smallii form of the spinous citharexylum, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, usually white or pale pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to a variety of soil conditions.

This form is noted for its compact growth habit and resistance to common pests and diseases.
