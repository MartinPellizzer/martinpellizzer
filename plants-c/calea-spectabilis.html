Calea spectabilis

Calea spectabilis, commonly known as the showy calea, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Calea spectabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
