Crotalaria vialis

Crotalaria vialis, commonly known as the common spurred crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing annual herb with erect or spreading stems and opposite, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Crotalaria vialis is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a useful plant in soil enrichment.
