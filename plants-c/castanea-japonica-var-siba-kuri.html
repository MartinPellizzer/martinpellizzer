Castanea japonica var. siba-kuri

Castanea japonica var. siba-kuri, commonly known as the Siba-kuri chestnut, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves and edible, sweet chestnuts. The plant produces husks that encase the nuts, which are typically harvested in the fall. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its nutritious fruit.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
