Cleomella arborea var. arborea

Cleomella arborea var. arborea, commonly known as the tree cleome, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, rounded seeds.

Cleomella arborea var. arborea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
