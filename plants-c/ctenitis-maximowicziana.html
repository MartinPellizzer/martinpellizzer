Ctenitis maximowicziana

Ctenitis maximowicziana, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and longevity. Its fronds are composed of numerous pinnae, which are arranged in a distinctive, overlapping pattern.
