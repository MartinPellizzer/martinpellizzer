Ceratostylis calceiformis

Ceratostylis calceiformis, commonly known as the shoe-shaped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, shoe-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ceratostylis calceiformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its elegant appearance.
