Conospermum elegantulum

Conospermum elegantulum, commonly known as the elegant honeysuckle, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in heathlands and sclerophyllous forests, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, and it produces small, edible berries that are a food source for native wildlife.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
