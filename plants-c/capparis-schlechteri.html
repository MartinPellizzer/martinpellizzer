Capparis schlechteri

Capparis schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's caper, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often followed by red or orange berries. Its leaves are typically lobed and have a slightly succulent texture.

Capparis schlechteri is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
