Centrochilus gracilis

Centrochilus gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, wiry stem and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, narrow spur. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are long and strap-like, adapted for efficient water absorption.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
