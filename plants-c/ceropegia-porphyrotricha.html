Ceropegia porphyrotricha

Ceropegia porphyrotricha, commonly known as the red-stemmed African violet, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, tuberous roots and has small, hairy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of delicate, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
