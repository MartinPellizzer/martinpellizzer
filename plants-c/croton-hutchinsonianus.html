Croton hutchinsonianus

Croton hutchinsonianus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
