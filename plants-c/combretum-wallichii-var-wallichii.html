Combretum wallichii var. wallichii

Combretum wallichii var. wallichii, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and typically have five petals.

The plant produces woody capsules containing small, dry seeds.
