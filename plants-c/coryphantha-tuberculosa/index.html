Coryphantha Tuberculosa

Coryphantha with tubercles, scientifically known as Coryphantha tuberculosa, is a striking member of the Cactaceae family native to the arid regions of North America. This cactus is characterized by its unique, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive, almost sculpted appearance. Despite its delicate look, it is remarkably resilient, capable of surviving in harsh desert conditions with minimal water. Its vibrant flowers, often yellow or orange, bloom in late spring, adding a burst of color to the landscape. The plant's compact size and striking features make it a popular choice for both collectors and gardeners seeking to cultivate desert flora.
