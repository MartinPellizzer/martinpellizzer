Chiloschista fasciata

Chiloschista fasciata, commonly known as the banded rock cress, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its small, cruciform flowers. Chiloschista fasciata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
