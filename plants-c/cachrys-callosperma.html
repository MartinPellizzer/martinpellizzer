Cachrys callosperma

Cachrys callosperma, commonly known as the roughleaf cypress, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and leathery, dark green leaves that are often covered in small, raised bumps. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cachrys callosperma is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid environments.
