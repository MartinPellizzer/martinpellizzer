Cleistocactus samaipatanus

Cleistocactus samaipatanus, commonly known as the Samaipata cactus, is a species native to Bolivia.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant produces flowers that are typically yellow or orange, which bloom in the evening. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
