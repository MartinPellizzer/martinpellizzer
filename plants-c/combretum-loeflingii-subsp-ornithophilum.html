Combretum loeflingii subsp. ornithophilum

Combretum loeflingii subsp.

ornithophilum, commonly known as the bird-loving combretum, is a shrub native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in certain regions.
