Cytisus linifolius

Cytisus linifolius, commonly known as the slender broom, is a flowering shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
