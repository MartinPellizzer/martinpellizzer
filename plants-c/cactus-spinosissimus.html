Cactus spinosissimus

Cactus spinosissimus, commonly known as the spiny cactus, is a species native to arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in harsh desert environments. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its spiny exterior provides protection against herbivores and excessive water loss.

Cactus spinosissimus is often used in traditional medicine and is a popular choice for xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
