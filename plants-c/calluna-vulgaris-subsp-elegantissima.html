Calluna vulgaris subsp. elegantissima

Calluna vulgaris subsp.

elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant heather, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the British Isles. It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety.

The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that remain green throughout the year.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to form dense, attractive ground cover in garden settings.
