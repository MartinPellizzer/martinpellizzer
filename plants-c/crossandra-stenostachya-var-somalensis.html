Crossandra stenostachya var. somalensis

Crossandra stenostachya var. somalensis, commonly known as the Somali flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Somalia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is adapted to drought conditions.
