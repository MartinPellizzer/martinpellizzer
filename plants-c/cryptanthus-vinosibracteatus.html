Cryptanthus vinosibracteatus

Cryptanthus vinosibracteatus, commonly known as the purple bract cryptanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is known for its compact, low-growing form. The plant features vibrant, velvety leaves that range in color from deep purple to reddish-brown, often with intricate patterns. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically green or yellow and appear in clusters.

Cryptanthus vinosibracteatus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
