Cynara makrisii

Cynara makrisii, commonly known as the Makrisii thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in sharp, prickly hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a dense cluster.

Cynara makrisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
