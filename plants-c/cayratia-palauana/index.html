Cayratia Palauana

Cayratia palauana is a rare flowering plant native to the Pacific island of Palau. It belongs to the vine family and is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in dense forest undergrowth. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, Cayratia palauana is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
