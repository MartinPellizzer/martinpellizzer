Camellia longgangensis var. patens

Camellia longgangensis var. patens, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a rare and ornamental evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a distinctive yellow or green base. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in partially shaded environments with well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialty nurseries.
