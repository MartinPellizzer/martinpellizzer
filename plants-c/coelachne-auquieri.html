Coelachne auquieri

Coelachne auquieri, commonly known as the African grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
