Cineraria elodes

Cineraria elodes, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Cineraria elodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
