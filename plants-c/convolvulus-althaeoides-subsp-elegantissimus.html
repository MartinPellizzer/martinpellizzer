Convolvulus althaeoides subsp. elegantissimus

Convolvulus althaeoides subsp.

elegantissimus, commonly known as the elegant convolvulus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces attractive, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple.

The plant features trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
