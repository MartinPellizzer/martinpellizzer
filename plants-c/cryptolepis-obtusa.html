Cryptolepis obtusa

Cryptolepis obtusa, commonly known as the broadleaf cryptolepis, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with smooth or slightly hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in cymes, and its fruit is a capsule containing numerous tiny seeds.

Cryptolepis obtusa is often found in moist, wooded areas and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
