Crepidium stolleanum

Crepidium stolleanum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking yellow flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The orchid's flowering period occurs during the spring and early summer, making it a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
