Cleisostoma oblongisepalum

Cleisostoma oblongisepalum, commonly known as the broad-sepal orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. Cleisostoma oblongisepalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
