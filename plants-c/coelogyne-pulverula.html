Coelogyne pulverula

Coelogyne pulverula, commonly known as the dust orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to brownish-red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it its common name.

Coelogyne pulverula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
