Convolvulus natalensis var. transvaalensis

Convolvulus natalensis var. transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant blue or purple flowers with five petals. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and twining stems that climb or trail along the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
