Cytisus valdesii

Cytisus valdesii, commonly known as the Valdes' broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in Mediterranean climates.
