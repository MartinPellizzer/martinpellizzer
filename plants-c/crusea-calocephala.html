Crusea calocephala

Crusea calocephala, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink.

Crusea calocephala is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
