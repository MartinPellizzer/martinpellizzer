Cytisus boavista

Cytisus boavista, commonly known as the Cape Verde broom, is a shrub native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to arid environments.

Cytisus boavista is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
