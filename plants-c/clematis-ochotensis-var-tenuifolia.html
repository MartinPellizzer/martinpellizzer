Clematis ochotensis var. tenuifolia

Clematis ochotensis var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the Far East.

It features compound leaves with slender, elongated leaflets that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue and purple during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
