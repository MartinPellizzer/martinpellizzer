Chionodoxa sardensis

Chionodoxa sardensis, commonly known as the Sardonic Snowflake, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the island of Sardinia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, spherical bulbs and clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate, early-blooming flowers that add a touch of elegance to gardens in late winter and early spring.
