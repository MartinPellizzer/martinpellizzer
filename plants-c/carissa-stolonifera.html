Carissa stolonifera

Carissa stolonifera, commonly known as the silver leaf carissa, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
