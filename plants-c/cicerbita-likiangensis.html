Cicerbita likiangensis

Cicerbita likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bittercress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, alternate leaves, and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are dry pods that release seeds when mature, a typical feature of the family.

Cicerbita likiangensis is often found in moist, rocky habitats and is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations.
