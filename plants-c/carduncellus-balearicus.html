Carduncellus balearicus

Carduncellus balearicus, commonly known as the Balearic thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas. The plant features spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, sunny environments.
