Callicarpa dunniana

Callicarpa dunniana, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright purple berries that appear in the fall, making it a visually striking plant. The leaves are simple, opposite, and oval-shaped, with a smooth texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
