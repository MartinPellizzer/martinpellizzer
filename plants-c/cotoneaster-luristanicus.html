Cotoneaster luristanicus

Cotoneaster luristanicus, commonly known as the Luristan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and mammals. The plant typically grows in rocky and dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth texture, while its flowers are white or pink, appearing in clusters.

Cotoneaster luristanicus is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in its native ecosystem.
