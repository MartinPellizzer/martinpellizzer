Crotonopsis elliptica

Crotonopsis elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are often surrounded by colorful bracts. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and unique floral display.

Crotonopsis elliptica prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
