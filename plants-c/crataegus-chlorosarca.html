Crataegus chlorosarca

Crataegus chlorosarca, commonly known as the yellow hawthorn, is a species of shrub in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its yellowish-green leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The fruit is a red, fleshy pome that ripens in the fall. This plant is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is often found in woodlands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
