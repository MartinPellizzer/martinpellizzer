Croton phuquocensis

Croton phuquocensis, commonly known as Phu Quoc croton, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Croton.

It is native to the island of Phu Quoc in Vietnam, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, often multicolored leaves, which can range from green to red and yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

Croton phuquocensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
