Cavendishia fortunensis

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, anchoring itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are often broad and arranged in a rosette pattern, facilitating water collection.
