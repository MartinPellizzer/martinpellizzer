Cynanchum ruizteranii

Cynanchum ruizteranii, commonly known as Ruizteran's milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

The flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually greenish-white in color, with a distinctive corona structure.
