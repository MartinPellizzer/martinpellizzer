Carduus carniolicus

Carduus carniolicus, commonly known as the Carpathian thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features a robust stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, covered in coarse, hairy texture. The plant produces spiny, lobed leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. Its flowers are yellow and occur in a single head, surrounded by spiny bracts.

It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
