Cyperus rigens subsp. rigens

Cyperus rigens subsp.

rigens, commonly known as the rigid flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants.

The plant typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshes, wetlands, and along waterways. Its slender, triangular stems are usually erect, and it produces small, clustered flowers in spikelets.

The species is characterized by its narrow leaves and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
