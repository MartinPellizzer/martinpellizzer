Chileorebutia glabrescens

Chileorebutia glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-tubered crassula, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically smooth and rounded, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Chileorebutia glabrescens is well-suited for rock gardens and containers, requiring minimal water and full sun.
