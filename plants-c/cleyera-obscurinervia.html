Cleyera obscurinervia

Cleyera obscurinervia, commonly known as the dark-veined cleyera, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves with dark, prominent veins. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, spherical berries.

Cleyera obscurinervia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
