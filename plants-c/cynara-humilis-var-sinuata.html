Cynara humilis var. sinuata

Cynara humilis var. sinuata, commonly known as the Italian thistle or narrow-leaf thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and spiny, lobed leaves that are often sinuate, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into spiny, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens for both aesthetic and ecological purposes.
