Chrysocoma liburnica

Chrysocoma liburnica, commonly known as the Liburnian goldenbush, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Chrysocoma liburnica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
