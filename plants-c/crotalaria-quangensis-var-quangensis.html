Crotalaria quangensis var. quangensis

Crotalaria quangensis var. quangensis, commonly known as the Quang Province crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and research settings.
