Cacalia calvoana

Cacalia calvoana, commonly known as the narrowleaf cacalia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture.

Cacalia calvoana produces small blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.
