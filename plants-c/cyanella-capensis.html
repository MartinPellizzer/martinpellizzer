Cyanella capensis

Cyanella capensis, commonly known as the Cape cyanella, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring. It is often used in rock gardens due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
