Centaurea diffusa var. pallida

Centaurea diffusa var. pallida, commonly known as the pale centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender stems and small, pale blue or white flowers arranged in spiked inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
