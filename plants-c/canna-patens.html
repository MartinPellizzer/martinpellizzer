Canna patens

Canna patens, commonly known as the broadleaf canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful bracts, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Canna patens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
