Celmisia spectabilis subsp. magnifica

Celmisia spectabilis subsp.

magnifica, commonly known as the showy celmisia, is a striking herbaceous plant native to New Zealand. It features tall, slender stems that bear dense clusters of bright yellow to orange inflorescences, which are highly ornamental.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its unique appearance.

This subspecies is valued for its vibrant flowers and is a popular choice in horticultural settings.
