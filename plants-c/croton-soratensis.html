Croton soratensis

Croton soratensis, commonly known as the Sooty Croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often variegated foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually thick and waxy, with a broad, lance-shaped form.
