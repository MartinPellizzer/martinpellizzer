Conophytum × cupreiflorum

Conophytum × cupreiflorum, commonly known as the copper-flowered conophytum, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of orange and red, which bloom during the warmer months. Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Conophytum × cupreiflorum requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
