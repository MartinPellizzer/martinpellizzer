Convolvulus juramenti

Convolvulus juramenti, commonly known as the sacred convolvulus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species in horticulture.
