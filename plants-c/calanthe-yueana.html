Calanthe yueana

Calanthe yueana, commonly known as the Yuean Orchid, is a species native to China, specifically found in the provinces of Yunnan and Sichuan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, preferring well-drained soil. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pale pink, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Calanthe yueana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
