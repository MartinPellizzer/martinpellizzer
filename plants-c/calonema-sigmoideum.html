Calonema sigmoideum

Calonema sigmoideum, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
