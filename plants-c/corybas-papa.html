Corybas papa

Corybas papa, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a species of orchid native to the island of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its striking, hooded flower that resembles a Christmas hat, hence its common name. The plant produces a single, showy bloom that is typically greenish-yellow with reddish markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Corybas papa is a terrestrial orchid with a short, underground rhizome and is known for its unique floral structure.
