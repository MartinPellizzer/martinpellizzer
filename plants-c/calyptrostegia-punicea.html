Calyptrostegia punicea

Calyptrostegia punicea, commonly known as the red-stemmed mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on the branches of various host trees.

Calyptrostegia punicea is native to parts of South America and is often found in humid, forested areas.
