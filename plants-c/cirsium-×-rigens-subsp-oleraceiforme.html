Cirsium × rigens subsp. oleraceiforme

Cirsium × rigens subsp.

oleraceiforme, commonly known as the garden thistle, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, and the flower heads are surrounded by a series of bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and floral display.
