Chlorophytum togoense

Chlorophytum togoense, commonly known as Tiger's Tail, is a perennial herb native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its long, strap-like leaves that are often striped with green and white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Due to its ornamental appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
