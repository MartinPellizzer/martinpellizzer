Ceterach haughtonii

Ceterach haughtonii, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal dunes and cliffs. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.
