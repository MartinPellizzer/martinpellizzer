Cypripedium insigne var. madouxianum

Cypripedium insigne var. madouxianum, commonly known as the Madoux's lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, slipper-shaped flower that is typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a single, erect stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists.
