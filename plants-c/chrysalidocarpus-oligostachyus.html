Chrysalidocarpus oligostachyus

Chrysalidocarpus oligostachyus, commonly known as the slender palm or sea date palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the coastal regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, coconut-like fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in saline environments.
