Cochemiea boolii

Cochemiea boolii, commonly known as the Mexican snowball cactus, is a species of succulent native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its dense, globular shape and gray-green foliage. The plant produces white, spherical flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its stems are ribbed and covered in small, white bristles, which help reduce water loss.

Cochemiea boolii is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.
