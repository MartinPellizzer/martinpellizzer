Cotyledon dinteri

Cotyledon dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Cotyledon dinteri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.
