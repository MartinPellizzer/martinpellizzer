Crocus zagrosensis

Crocus zagrosensis, commonly known as the Zagros crocus, is a species of flowering plant native to the Zagros Mountains in Iran.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, early spring-blooming flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and well-drained soils, often in alpine or subalpine regions. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with six petals and a distinctive, rounded shape.
