Citrus × aurantium var. balearica

Citrus × aurantium var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic lemon, is a citrus hybrid native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a small evergreen tree with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is typically small, round, and has a thick, yellow rind with a high oil content. It is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in perfumery and traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
