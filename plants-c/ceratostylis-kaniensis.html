Ceratostylis kaniensis

Ceratostylis kaniensis, commonly known as the Kaniense snake orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and produces a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. Its unique floral structure and specific habitat requirements make it a rare and specialized species.

It is often found in shaded, moist environments within forest understories.
