Cyrtosperma angustilobum

Cyrtosperma angustilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a starchy tuberous root that is used in traditional cooking and medicine. Its growth habit includes a rhizomatous stem system, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Cyrtosperma angustilobum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
