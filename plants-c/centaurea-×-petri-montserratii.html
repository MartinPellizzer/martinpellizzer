Centaurea × petri-montserratii

Centaurea × petri-montserratii, commonly known as the Pyrenean centaury, is a hybrid plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and bright blue or purple flowers. The plant typically reaches a height of 10–30 cm and blooms from late spring to early summer. It prefers sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable component of mountain flora.
