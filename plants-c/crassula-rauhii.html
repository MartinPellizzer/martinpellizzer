Crassula rauhii

Crassula rauhii, commonly known as the "Little Leaf Crassula," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-forming foliage with small, fleshy, dark green leaves that have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
