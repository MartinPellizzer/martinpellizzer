Conyza newii

Conyza newii, commonly known as the Newii weed, is an annual flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
