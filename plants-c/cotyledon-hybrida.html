Cotyledon hybrida

Cotyledon hybrida, commonly known as the African beauty, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, pink, and white. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
