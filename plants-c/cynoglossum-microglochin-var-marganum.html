Cynoglossum microglochin var. marganum

Cynoglossum microglochin var. marganum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or wet meadows.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
