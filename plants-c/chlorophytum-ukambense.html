Chlorophytum ukambense

Chlorophytum ukambense, commonly known as the East African spider plant, is a perennial herb native to East Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by narrow, grass-like seed spikes. It is well adapted to semi-arid conditions and thrives in well-drained soil.

Chlorophytum ukambense is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
