Conyza subscaposa

Conyza subscaposa, commonly known as the slenderleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems that are often hairy, with opposite leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Conyza subscaposa is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate, where it blooms during the warmer months.
