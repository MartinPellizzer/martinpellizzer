Cheiranthus × hybridus

Cheiranthus × hybridus, commonly known as the wallflower hybrid, is a perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, colorful flowers that typically bloom in spring and early summer. The plant produces small, rounded seed pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found growing along walls or in rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
