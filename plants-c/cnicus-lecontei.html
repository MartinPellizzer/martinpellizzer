Cnicus lecontei

Cnicus lecontei, commonly known as LeConte's thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and cliffs. It has a taproot system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Cnicus lecontei is also known for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in herbal remedies.
