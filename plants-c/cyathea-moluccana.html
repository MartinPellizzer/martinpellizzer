Cyathea moluccana

Cyathea moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in rainforests. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth.

Cyathea moluccana plays a significant role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
