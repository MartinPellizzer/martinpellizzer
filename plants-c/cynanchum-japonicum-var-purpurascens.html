Cynanchum japonicum var. purpurascens

Cynanchum japonicum var. purpurascens, commonly known as Japanese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain small, oval seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its leaves are typically pinnately compound, with numerous small leaflets.
