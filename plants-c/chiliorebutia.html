Chiliorebutia

Chiliorebutia, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a member of the Cactaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features spiny areoles and produces small, rounded stems that can branch out. Chiliorebutia is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant among cactus enthusiasts.
