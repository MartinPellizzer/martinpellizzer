Cordiglottis westenenkii

Cordiglottis westenenkii, commonly known as the Westenend's heartleaf, is a rare and striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spathe and spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
