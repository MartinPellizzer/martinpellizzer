Chytraculia tussaceana

Chytraculia tussaceana, commonly known as the tussac grass, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that can reach heights of up to three meters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its root system is extensive, helping to stabilize the soil in its mountainous habitat.
