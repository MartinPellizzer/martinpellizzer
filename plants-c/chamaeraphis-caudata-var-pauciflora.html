Chamaeraphis caudata var. pauciflora

Chamaeraphis caudata var. pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered glassworts, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other saline habitats. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in dense inflorescences.

This variety is distinguished by its reduced flowering compared to other forms of Chamaeraphis caudata.
