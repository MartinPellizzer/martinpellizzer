Cirsium makinoi var. abukumense

Cirsium makinoi var. abukumense, commonly known as the Abukuma thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, and the flowers are arranged in a dense, spherical cluster.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
