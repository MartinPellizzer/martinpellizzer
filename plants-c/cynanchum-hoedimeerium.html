Cynanchum hoedimeerium

Cynanchum hoedimeerium, commonly known as Hoedmeier's milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various ailments.
