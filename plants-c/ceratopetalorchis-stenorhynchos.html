Ceratopetalorchis stenorhynchos

Ceratopetalorchis stenorhynchos, commonly known as the narrow-mouthed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often recurved. It is typically found in shaded, moist habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
