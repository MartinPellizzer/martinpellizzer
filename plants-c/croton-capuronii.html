Croton capuronii

Croton capuronii, commonly known as the Capuron's croton, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
