Clausena dentata

Clausena dentata, commonly known as the sour orange or wild lime, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or yellowish in color. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to yellow or orange when ripe.
