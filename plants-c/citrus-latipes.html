Citrus latipes

Citrus latipes, commonly known as the Japanese sour orange.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to East Asia, particularly Japan and China. The plant produces small, round, yellow-orange fruits with a thick, aromatic rind. Its leaves are dark green, elliptical, and have a slightly rough texture.

Citrus latipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
