Croton cneorifolius var. lanceolatus

Croton cneorifolius var. lanceolatus, commonly known as the lanceleaf croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
