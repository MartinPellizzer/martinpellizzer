Cleome ruta

Cleome ruta, commonly known as spider flower, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy, spire-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features alternate, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers range in color from white to pink and are often accompanied by bracts that resemble small petals.

Cleome ruta is an annual plant that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.
