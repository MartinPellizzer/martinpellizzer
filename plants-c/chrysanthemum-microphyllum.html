Chrysanthemum microphyllum

Chrysanthemum microphyllum, commonly known as the dwarf chrysanthemum, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, finely divided leaves and produces daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, white, and orange. The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Chrysanthemum microphyllum is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
