Cyclamen stenopetalum

Cyclamen stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant, spurred flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its ornate appearance. Cyclamen stenopetalum has a tuberous root system that allows it to survive periods of dormancy.

Its unique floral structure and foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
