Caryopteris ningpoensis

Caryopteris ningpoensis, commonly known as the Ningpo blue mistflower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of blue-lilac flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight pubescence, contributing to its ornamental value.

Caryopteris ningpoensis is valued in horticulture for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
