Cattleya tigrina var. caerulea

Cattleya tigrina var. caerulea, commonly known as the Blue Tiger Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking blue-violet petals and white lip.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, where it grows as an epiphyte on trees. The plant features long, arching stems with multiple flower buds, and its blooms are typically fragrant. It requires bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
