Calochilus imperiosus

Calochilus imperiosus, commonly known as the Imperial Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically features dark purple or violet petals with a contrasting yellow or white lip. It thrives in coastal regions and is often found growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Calochilus imperiosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
