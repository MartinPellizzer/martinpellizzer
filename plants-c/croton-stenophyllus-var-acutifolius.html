Croton stenophyllus var. acutifolius

Croton stenophyllus var. acutifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive green and yellow coloration, often adorned with prominent veins. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
