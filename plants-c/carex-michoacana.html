Carex michoacana

Carex michoacana, commonly known as Michoacan sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in shaded to partially shaded environments. It has grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often inconspicuous.

Carex michoacana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland habitats.
