Catanthera peltata

Catanthera peltata, commonly known as the velvetleaf catanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Catanthera peltata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
