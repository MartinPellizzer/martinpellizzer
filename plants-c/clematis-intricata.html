Clematis intricata

Clematis intricata, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil. It is known for its rapid growth and can reach lengths of up to 20 feet.

Clematis intricata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
