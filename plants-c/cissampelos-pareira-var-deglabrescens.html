Cissampelos pareira var. deglabrescens

Cissampelos pareira var. deglabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed moon vine, is a climbing perennial vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are elongated, fleshy berries that turn red when mature, containing several seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
