Crassula tenuicaulis

Crassula tenuicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, dark green leaves that are somewhat cylindrical in shape. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the spring season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Crassula tenuicaulis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
