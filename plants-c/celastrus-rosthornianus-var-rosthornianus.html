Celastrus rosthornianus var. rosthornianus

Celastrus rosthornianus var. rosthornianus, commonly known as the Rosthorn Celastrus, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant bears red, fleshy berries that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Celastraceae family.
