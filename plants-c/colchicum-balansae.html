Colchicum balansae

Colchicum balansae, commonly known as the Balansae colchicum, is a perennial plant belonging to the Colchicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the eastern Mediterranean, particularly in Turkey and Cyprus. The plant features striking, waxy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are basal, forming a rosette, and are typically gray-green in color.

Colchicum balansae is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
