Citrus × limetta var. fructo-pumila

Citrus × limetta var. fructo-pumila, commonly known as the pomelo citrus or sweet orange.

It is a hybrid citrus fruit characterized by its large, thick-skinned fruit with a sweet, aromatic flavor. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree with evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is typically harvested when fully mature, and it is valued for both its culinary and ornamental uses.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
