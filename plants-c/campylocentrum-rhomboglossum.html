Campylocentrum rhomboglossum

Campylocentrum rhomboglossum, commonly known as the rhomboid-leaved piper.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae, native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
