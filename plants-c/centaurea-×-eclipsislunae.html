Centaurea × eclipsislunae

Centaurea × eclipsislunae, commonly known as the Moonshadow Blazingstar, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, deep purple to black flowers that resemble the night sky, with bright yellow centers that stand out vividly. The plant produces bracts that are often mistaken for petals, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
