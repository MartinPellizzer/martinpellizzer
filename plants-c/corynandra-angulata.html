Corynandra angulata

Corynandra angulata, commonly known as the angular corynandra, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, angular leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It is often found in coastal and inland regions with well-drained soils.

Corynandra angulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
