Crepidomanes boninense

Crepidomanes boninense, commonly known as the Bonin sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Bonin Islands.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs.

It plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing food for insects and contributing to soil stability.
