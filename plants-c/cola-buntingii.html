Cola buntingii

Cola buntingii, commonly known as the African cola tree, is a member of the Sterculiaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a large evergreen tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, followed by elliptical, woody pods containing seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The tree is valued for its seeds, which are used to produce caffeine-containing products.
