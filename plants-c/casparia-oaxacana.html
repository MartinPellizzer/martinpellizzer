Casparia oaxacana

Casparia oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca thistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Mexico.

The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Casparia oaxacana is adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
