Cyrtochilum brachypterum

Cyrtochilum brachypterum, commonly known as the short-winged cyclochilum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and small, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually linear to ovate, and the flowers are often clustered in loose inflorescences.

Cyrtochilum brachypterum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
