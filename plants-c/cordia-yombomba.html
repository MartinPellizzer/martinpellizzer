Cordia yombomba

Cordia yombomba, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a snow-like appearance. The plant has a sturdy, often multi-stemmed trunk and produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive botanical appearance.
