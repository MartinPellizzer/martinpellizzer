Cynorkis madagascarica

Cynorkis madagascarica, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Cynorkis madagascarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
