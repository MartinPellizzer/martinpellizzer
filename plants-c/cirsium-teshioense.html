Cirsium teshioense

Cirsium teshioense, commonly known as the Teshio thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, temperate climates. Its foliage is covered in dense, stiff hairs, which help protect it from herbivores.

Cirsium teshioense is a relatively rare species, making it of interest to botanists and conservationists.
