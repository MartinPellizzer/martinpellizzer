Centaurea × zubiae var. somedana

Centaurea × zubiae var. somedana, commonly known as the Sueda centaurea, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, glandular leaves. Native to the Iberian Peninsula, it thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with upright stems and is often found in disturbed or marginal areas.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
