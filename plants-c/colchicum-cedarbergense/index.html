Colchicum Cedarbergense

Colchicum cedarbergense is a rare and striking plant native to the Cedarberg region of South Africa. It is known for its vibrant purple flowers that bloom in late summer. This species belongs to the Colchicaceae family, which includes several toxic plants. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
