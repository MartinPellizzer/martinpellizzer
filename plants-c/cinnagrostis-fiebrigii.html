Cinnagrostis fiebrigii

Cinnagrostis fiebrigii, commonly known as the yellow flag grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to regions of Asia, including China, Korea, and Japan, and thrives in wet or marshy environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps near water bodies.

Cinnagrostis fiebrigii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
