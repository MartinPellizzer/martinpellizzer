Cyperus plurinodosa

Cyperus plurinodosa, commonly known as the swamp cyperus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, making it a common sight in wetlands and along water bodies.

Cyperus plurinodosa is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
