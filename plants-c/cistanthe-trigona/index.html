Cistanthe Trigona

Cistanthe trigona is a unique flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia. It is known for its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes. Despite its beauty, Cistanthe trigona remains a relatively obscure plant in the world of botany.
