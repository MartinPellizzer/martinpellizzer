Celosia globosa var. globosa

Celosia globosa var. globosa, commonly known as the globe amaranth, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescences that resemble small globes, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered together. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

Celosia globosa var. globosa is drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soil.
