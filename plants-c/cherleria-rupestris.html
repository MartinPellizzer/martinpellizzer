Cherleria rupestris

Cherleria rupestris, commonly known as the rock spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Cherleria rupestris is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
