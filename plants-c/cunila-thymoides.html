Cunila thymoides

Cunila thymoides, commonly known as wild thyme, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features small, aromatic leaves that resemble those of thyme, with a strong, pungent scent. The plant produces tiny white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open woodlands and rocky areas.

Cunila thymoides is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
