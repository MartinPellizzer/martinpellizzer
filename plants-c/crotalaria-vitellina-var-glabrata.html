Crotalaria vitellina var. glabrata

Crotalaria vitellina var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth yellow cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are typical of the Crotalaria genus. The plant produces elongated pods containing kidney-shaped seeds, which are often used in traditional agriculture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to warm climates.

Due to its ornamental value and potential as a forage crop, it is cultivated in various parts of the world.
