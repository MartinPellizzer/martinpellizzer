Chondrosum karwinskyi

Chondrosum karwinskyi, commonly known as the Karwinsky's rockrose, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and poor soil conditions. Its leaves are typically cylindrical and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Chondrosum karwinskyi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
