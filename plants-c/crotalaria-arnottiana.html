Crotalaria arnottiana

Crotalaria arnottiana, commonly known as the flame flower or yellow cowpea, is a tropical shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that resemble those of the cowpea plant. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Crotalaria arnottiana is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
