Croton pontis

Croton pontis, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, spiny capsules containing seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes in regions with suitable climates.
