Calamagrostis olympica

Calamagrostis olympica, commonly known as the Olympic grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, silver-green foliage that turns coppery in the fall, creating striking visual interest throughout the growing season. The plant produces fluffy, golden plumes in late summer, adding a dramatic effect to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Calamagrostis olympica is drought-tolerant and suitable for perennial borders or as a backdrop plant in landscape designs.
