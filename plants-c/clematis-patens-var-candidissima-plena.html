Clematis patens var. candidissima-plena

Clematis patens var. candidissima-plena, commonly known as the white-flowered Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, double white blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
