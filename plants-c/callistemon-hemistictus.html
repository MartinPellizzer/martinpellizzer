Callistemon hemistictus

Callistemon hemistictus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
