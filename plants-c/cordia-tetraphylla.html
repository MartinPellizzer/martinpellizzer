Cordia tetraphylla

Cordia tetraphylla, commonly known as the four-leafed cordia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and usually oval in shape.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hard seeds.
