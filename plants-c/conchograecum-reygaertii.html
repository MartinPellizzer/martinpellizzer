Conchograecum reygaertii

Conchograecum reygaertii, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
