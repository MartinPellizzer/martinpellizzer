Cirsium mite

Cirsium mite, commonly known as the thistle mite, is a tiny arachnid belonging to the family Tetranychidae.

It is often found on the leaves of thistle plants, which are part of the Asteraceae family. The mite feeds on the plant's sap, causing damage that can lead to leaf deformation and reduced growth. Its presence is often indicated by the stippling or yellowing of the foliage.

Despite its small size, the thistle mite plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its host plant.
