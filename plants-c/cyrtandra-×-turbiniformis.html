Cyrtandra × turbiniformis

Cyrtandra × turbiniformis, commonly known as the Turbiniform Cyrtandra, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive tubular, often curved flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Cyrtandra × turbiniformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
