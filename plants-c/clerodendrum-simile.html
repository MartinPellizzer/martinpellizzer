Clerodendrum simile

Clerodendrum simile, commonly known as the "Chinese lantern plant," is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are typically red or orange in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by distinctive, elongated fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.
