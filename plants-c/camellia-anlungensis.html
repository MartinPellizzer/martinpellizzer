Camellia anlungensis

Camellia anlungensis, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves and showy, camellia-like flowers that bloom in winter or early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Camellia anlungensis is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
