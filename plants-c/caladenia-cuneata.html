Caladenia cuneata

Caladenia cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance. The plant produces a single, slender stem that bears a single, brightly colored flower with a distinctive hooded labellum. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in open forests and woodlands.

Caladenia cuneata is known for its ephemeral blooms, which appear during the cooler months and are short-lived.
