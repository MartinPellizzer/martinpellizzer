Crataegus noelensis

Crataegus noelensis, commonly known as the Noël hawthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. This deciduous shrub or small tree typically grows to a height of 3–6 meters and features thorny branches. Its flowers are white or pink, appearing in clusters, and it produces small, red berries that are edible but often tart.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
