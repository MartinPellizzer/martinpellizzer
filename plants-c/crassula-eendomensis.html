Crassula eendomensis

Crassula eendomensis, commonly known as the Eendomans Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and fleshy, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Crassula eendomensis is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in horticultural displays for its ornamental value.
