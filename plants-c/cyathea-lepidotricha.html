Cyathea lepidotricha

Cyathea lepidotricha, commonly known as the Lepidotricha tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive appearance with a smooth, grayish trunk and fronds that can grow up to several meters in length.

Cyathea lepidotricha is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
