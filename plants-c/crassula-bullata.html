Crassula bullata

Crassula bullata, commonly known as the Wooly Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, woolly coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
