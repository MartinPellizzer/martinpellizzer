Combretum butayei

Combretum butayei, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The bark is thick and fissured, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses.
