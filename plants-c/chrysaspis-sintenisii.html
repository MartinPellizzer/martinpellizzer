Chrysaspis sintenisii

Chrysaspis sintenisii, commonly known as the golden leaf orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in high humidity and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf burn. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited natural habitat, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
