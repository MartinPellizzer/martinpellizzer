Chrysophyllum monopyrenum var. microphyllum

Chrysophyllum monopyrenum var. microphyllum, commonly known as the dwarf star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Africa.

It features small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by edible, star-shaped fruits that are sweet and aromatic. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The dwarf star apple is valued for its ornamental appeal and fruit production in suitable climates.
