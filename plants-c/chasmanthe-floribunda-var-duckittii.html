Chasmanthe floribunda var. duckittii

Chasmanthe floribunda var. duckittii, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of blooms during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a tropical look in temperate climates.
