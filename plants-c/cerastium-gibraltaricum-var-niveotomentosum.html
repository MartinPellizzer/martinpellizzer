Cerastium gibraltaricum var. niveotomentosum

Cerastium gibraltaricum var. niveotomentosum, commonly known as the Gibraltar snowy haircentle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to April. The plant has hairy, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance, especially when covered in frost. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
