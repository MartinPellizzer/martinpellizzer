Callistachys retusa

Callistachys retusa, commonly known as the Chinese lantern vine, is a deciduous climbing shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces showy, inflated seed pods that resemble small lanterns, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
