Cyperus pallens

Cyperus pallens, commonly known as the silver grass or slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. Its slender, grass-like stems are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Cyperus pallens produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically wind-pollinated.
