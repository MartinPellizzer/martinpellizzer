Calyptronoma swartzii

Calyptronoma swartzii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Calyptronoma swartzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
