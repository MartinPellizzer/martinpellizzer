Codonopsis levicalyx

Codonopsis levicalyx, commonly known as the small-flowered monkshood, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant has palmate leaves and produces follicular fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
