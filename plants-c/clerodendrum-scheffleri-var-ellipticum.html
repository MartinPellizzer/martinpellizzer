Clerodendrum scheffleri var. ellipticum

Clerodendrum scheffleri var. ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic cleerodendrum, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop for its showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking exotic and colorful foliage.
