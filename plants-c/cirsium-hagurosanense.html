Cirsium hagurosanense

Cirsium hagurosanense, commonly known as the Hagurosan thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to temperate climates. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
