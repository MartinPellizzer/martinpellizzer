Columnea allenii

Columnea allenii, commonly known as the Allen's flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and drawing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Columnea allenii thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
