Cymbidium dayanum var. dayanum

Cymbidium dayanum var. dayanum, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species native to China and is prized for its ornamental value.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with long, strap-like leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

This variety is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
