Convolvulus capitatus

Convolvulus capitatus, commonly known as the clustered bindweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. Its leaves are heart-shaped and arranged alternately along the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
