Chenopodium ambrosioides var. viridis

Chenopodium ambrosioides var. viridis, commonly known as green wormwood, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, lanceolate leaves that are usually green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and its seeds are small and wind-dispersed. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and roadsides.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in various herbal remedies.
