Cissampelos nepalensis

Cissampelos nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal moonseed, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
