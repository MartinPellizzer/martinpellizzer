Ceterach reichardtii

Ceterach reichardtii, commonly known as the Reichardt's ceterach, is a species of seagrass found in the coastal waters of southern Africa.

It thrives in shallow, sandy habitats and is often found in association with other marine vegetation. The plant has long, ribbon-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. It plays an important role in stabilizing sediments and providing habitat for various marine organisms.

Ceterach reichardtii is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.
