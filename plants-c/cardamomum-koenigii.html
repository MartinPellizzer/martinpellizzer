Cardamomum koenigii

Cardamomum koenigii, commonly known as black pepper.

This plant is a member of the Piperaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Asia. It produces small, dark red berries that contain the aromatic seeds used as a spice. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
