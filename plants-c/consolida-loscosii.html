Consolida loscosii

Consolida loscosii, commonly known as the Loscosii sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Tajikistan. The plant features slender, twining stems and produces clusters of fragrant, spurred flowers in shades of pink and purple. Its leaves are pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
