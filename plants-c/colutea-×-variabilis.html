Colutea × variabilis

Colutea × variabilis, commonly known as the variable cowpea, is a hybrid plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces small, round pods containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it both an ornamental and a potential medicinal plant.
