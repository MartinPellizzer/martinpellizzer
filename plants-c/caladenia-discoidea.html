Caladenia discoidea

Caladenia discoidea, commonly known as the Spotted Spider-Orchid, is an exquisite orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals and a distinctive, often spotted labellum. The plant typically grows in heathland and woodland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle yet elegant presence to its environment.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often protected in its natural habitat.
