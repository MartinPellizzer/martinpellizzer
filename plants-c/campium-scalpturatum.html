Campium scalpturatum

Campium scalpturatum, commonly known as the scarlet spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along its square, hairy stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
