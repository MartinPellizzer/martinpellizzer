Cirsium × bipontinum

Cirsium × bipontinum, commonly known as the bipontine thistle, is a hybrid plant species within the genus Cirsium, which belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe, this plant often grows in disturbed soils and along roadsides. It is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive growth.

The plant's robust structure and adaptability make it a notable presence in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
