Coussarea platyphylla var. longiflora

Coussarea platyphylla var. longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered coussarea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that are highly fragrant and attract various pollinators. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a yellowish color.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
