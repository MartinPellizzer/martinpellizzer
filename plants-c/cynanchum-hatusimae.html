Cynanchum hatusimae

Cynanchum hatusimae, commonly known as the Japanese milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces a milky sap, which is a distinguishing feature of the genus Cynanchum. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
