Ctenocladium mildbraedii

Ctenocladium mildbraedii, commonly known as the Cape barbed wire plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in small, barbed spines that provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptations, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid and semi-arid regions.
