Chrysosplenium barbatum

Chrysosplenium barbatum, commonly known as the hairy chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Asia and North America.

It features small, ovate leaves with a hairy texture, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.

Chrysosplenium barbatum thrives in moist, shaded environments, making it a common sight in woodland areas.
