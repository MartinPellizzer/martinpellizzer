Cystopteris fragilis var. dickieana

Cystopteris fragilis var. dickieana, commonly known as the Dickie's brittle fern, is a variant of the fragile spleenwort.

It is a small, delicate fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rocky crevices and forest floors. The plant features a rhizome that produces a rosette of pinnate, often fragile fronds. Its leaves are usually light green and have a distinctive, finely divided structure.

This variety is characterized by its slender, wiry fronds and is often found in temperate regions with high humidity.
