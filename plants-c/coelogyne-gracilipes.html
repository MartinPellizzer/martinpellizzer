Coelogyne gracilipes

Coelogyne gracilipes, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and pendant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for epiphytic growth, allowing it to thrive in diverse habitats.

Coelogyne gracilipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beautiful, long-lasting blooms.
