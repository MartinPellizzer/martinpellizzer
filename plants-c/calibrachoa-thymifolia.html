Calibrachoa thymifolia

Calibrachoa thymifolia, commonly known as the thyme-leaved calibrachoa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its compact growth habit. Its leaves are aromatic, with a scent reminiscent of thyme, contributing to its common name.

Calibrachoa thymifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and fragrant foliage.
