Calamagrostis Arundinacea Var. Pyramidalis

Calamagrostis arundinacea var. pyramidalis is a striking ornamental grass known for its tall, feathery plumes. This variant stands out with its narrow, pyramid-shaped form and vibrant autumn coloration. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its graceful appearance adds movement and texture to any setting. This plant is both drought-tolerant and easy to maintain, offering beauty with minimal care.
