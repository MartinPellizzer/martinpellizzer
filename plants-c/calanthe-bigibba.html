Calanthe bigibba

Calanthe bigibba, commonly known as the giant calanthe, is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters, with vibrant colors ranging from white to pink and purple. The plant has erect, hairy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Calanthe bigibba thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
