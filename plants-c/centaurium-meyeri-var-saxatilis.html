Centaurium meyeri var. saxatilis

Centaurium meyeri var. saxatilis, commonly known as the rock centaury, is a flowering plant native to rocky and alpine regions of Europe.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. This variety is often found in mountainous areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
