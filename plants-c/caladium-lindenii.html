Caladium lindenii

Caladium lindenii, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with vibrant red veins against a deep green background. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is the main ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.

Caladium lindenii is typically propagated through rhizomes and can grow up to 2 feet tall under optimal conditions.
