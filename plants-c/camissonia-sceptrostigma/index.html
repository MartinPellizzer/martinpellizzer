Camissonia Sceptrostigma

Camissonia sceptrostigma is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is known for its small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it is considered vulnerable and faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and endangered plant.
