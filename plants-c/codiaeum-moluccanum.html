Codiaeum moluccanum

Codiaeum moluccanum, commonly known as the flame leaf or mosaic plant, is a tropical shrub native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated foliage, which can display shades of red, yellow, and green. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its vibrant appearance and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
