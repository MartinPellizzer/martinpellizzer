Clematis thunbergii var. microcuspis

Clematis thunbergii var. microcuspis, commonly known as the miniature clematis, is a compact, flowering vine native to China.

It features small, delicate white to light pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, twining stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are finely toothed. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its graceful appearance.
