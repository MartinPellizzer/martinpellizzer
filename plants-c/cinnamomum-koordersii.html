Cinnamomum koordersii

Cinnamomum koordersii, commonly known as the Koorders' cinnamon tree, is a species of evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical climates and can reach heights of up to 20 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinct cinnamon-like scent when crushed.

The tree is valued for its essential oils and has been used in traditional medicine and as a source of aromatic compounds.
