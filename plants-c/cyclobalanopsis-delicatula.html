Cyclobalanopsis delicatula

Cyclobalanopsis delicatula, commonly known as the slender talaud, is a species of evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Fagaceae, which includes oaks and chestnuts, and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, cup-shaped fruits that contain one or two seeds, typical of the Fagaceae family. It typically grows in lowland to montane forests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in some regions.
