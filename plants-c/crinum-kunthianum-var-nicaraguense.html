Crinum kunthianum var. nicaraguense

Crinum kunthianum var. nicaraguense, commonly known as the Nicaraguan crinum, is a bulbous herbaceous plant native to Central America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in umbels. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
