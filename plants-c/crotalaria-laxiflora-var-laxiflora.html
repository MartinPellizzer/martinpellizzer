Crotalaria laxiflora var. laxiflora

Crotalaria laxiflora var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers with two lips, typical of the Crotalaria genus. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several kidney-shaped seeds.

The botanical features of this species make it both ecologically significant and potentially useful in agricultural systems.
