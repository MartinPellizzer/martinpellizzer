Claytonia grayi

Claytonia grayi, commonly known as the gray's springbeauty, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Portulacaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, cushion-like form. Claytonia grayi is often found in rocky soils and open woodlands, thriving in partial shade to full sun.
