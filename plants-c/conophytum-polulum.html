Conophytum polulum

Conophytum polulum, commonly known as the "Cape Hottentot" or "Milkstone," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy, and often ribbed leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Conophytum polulum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
