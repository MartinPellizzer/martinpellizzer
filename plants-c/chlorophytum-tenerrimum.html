Chlorophytum tenerrimum

Chlorophytum tenerrimum, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail or Tiger's Head, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and striking, long-lasting inflorescences that resemble a tiger's tail. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
