Cydista amoena

Cydista amoena, commonly known as the white-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, with five petals arranged in a tubular shape. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Cydista amoena is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
