Calochilus holtzei

Calochilus holtzei, commonly known as the Holtzea orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Australia, particularly found in the southern regions of Western Australia. This terrestrial orchid typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant produces showy, often violet or purple-colored blooms that are highly sought after by horticulturists.

It is a perennial herb with a rhizomatous root system, and it thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions.
