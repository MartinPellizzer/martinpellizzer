Combretum dumetorum

Combretum dumetorum, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded foliage and clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is adapted to arid environments and can thrive in well-drained soils.

The bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
