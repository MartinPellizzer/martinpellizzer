Crassula pentandra subsp. catalaunica

Crassula pentandra subsp.

catalaunica, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, rosette-forming leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, with a waxy texture to reduce water loss.

The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
