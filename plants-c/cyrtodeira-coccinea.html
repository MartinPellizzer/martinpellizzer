Cyrtodeira coccinea

Cyrtodeira coccinea, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red stems and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, often as an epiphyte on trees or rocks. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from pink to deep red.

Cyrtodeira coccinea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
