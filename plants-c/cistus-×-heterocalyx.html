Cistus × heterocalyx

Cistus × heterocalyx, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and horticultural use.
