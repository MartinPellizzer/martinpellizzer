Cotyledon cacalioides

Cotyledon cacalioides, commonly known as the "Coral Cotyledon," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often red or pink, with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in spring, typically in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
