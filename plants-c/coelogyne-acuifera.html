Coelogyne acuifera

Coelogyne acuifera, commonly known as the sharp-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte on trees or rocks, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers that bloom sequentially, often lasting several weeks.

Coelogyne acuifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
