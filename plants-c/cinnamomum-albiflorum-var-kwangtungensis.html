Cinnamomum albiflorum var. kwangtungensis

Cinnamomum albiflorum var. kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong cinnamon or white-flowered cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated margin. Its bark is thin and reddish-brown, often exuding a pleasant cinnamon scent.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
