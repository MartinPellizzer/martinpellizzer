Cynorkis morlandii

Cynorkis morlandii, commonly known as Morland's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, erect stem and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, woodland environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
