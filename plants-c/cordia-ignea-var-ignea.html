Cordia ignea var. ignea

Cordia ignea var. ignea, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the warmer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and urban landscapes.
