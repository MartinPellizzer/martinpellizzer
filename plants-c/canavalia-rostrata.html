Canavalia rostrata

Canavalia rostrata, commonly known as the sword bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a legume characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces distinctive elongated pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in culinary applications. Canavalia rostrata is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.

Its flowers are typically bright yellow, adding ornamental value to gardens.
