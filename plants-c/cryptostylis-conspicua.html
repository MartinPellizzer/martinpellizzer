Cryptostylis conspicua

Cryptostylis conspicua, commonly known as the showy cryptostylis, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests and wetlands. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is a subject of interest in botanical studies.
