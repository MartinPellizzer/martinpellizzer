Crassula rustii

Crassula rustii, commonly known as the Rustii Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Crassulaceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant typically grows in rocky areas and has a compact, bushy appearance. Its leaves are usually a vibrant green, sometimes with a reddish tinge, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink.

Crassula rustii is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
