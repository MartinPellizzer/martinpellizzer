Carex zhenkangensis

Carex zhenkangensis, commonly known as the Zhenkang sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Zhenkang region in Sichuan Province. This perennial plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, greenish flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands.

Carex zhenkangensis is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
