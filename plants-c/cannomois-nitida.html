Cannomois nitida

Cannomois nitida, commonly known as the silver cane plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, silver-gray stems that are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly waxy texture.

Cannomois nitida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
