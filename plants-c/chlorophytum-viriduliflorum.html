Chlorophytum viriduliflorum

Chlorophytum viriduliflorum, commonly known as the Green-flowered Spider Plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with a white stripe, and it produces small, bell-shaped green flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. It is also noted for its air-purifying properties and ease of care.

Chlorophytum viriduliflorum is often used in landscaping and as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
