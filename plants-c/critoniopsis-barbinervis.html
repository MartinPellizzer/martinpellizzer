Critoniopsis barbinervis

Critoniopsis barbinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive pinkish-white color and a subtle scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
