Cutandia rigescens

Cutandia rigescens, commonly known as the rigid cutandia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile, where it thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features upright, stiff stems covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense heads, typically displaying yellow to orange hues.

Cutandia rigescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
