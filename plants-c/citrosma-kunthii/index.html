Citrosma Kunthii

Citrosma kunthii is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to the tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. This plant is known for its unique fragrance and medicinal properties that have been used in traditional remedies for centuries. Its leaves and fruits contain essential oils that are valued in both perfumery and herbal medicine. Despite its potential, Citrosma kunthii remains under-researched and is often overlooked in modern botanical studies.
