Carex takeshimensis

Carex takeshimensis, commonly known as the Takeshim Island sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. It has grass-like leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy.

Its flowering spikes are small and clustered, producing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
