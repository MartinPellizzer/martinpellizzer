Colocasia antiquorum var. patens

Colocasia antiquorum var. patens, commonly known as the giant taro, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length, often with a deep green to almost black coloration. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its dramatic foliage rather than its small, pale yellow flowers. It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure.

This variety is particularly valued in tropical gardens for its bold, tropical appearance.
