Cotyledon libanotica f. major

Cotyledon libanotica f. major, commonly known as the large-leaved African snowball, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with a waxy coating, adapted for water storage. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring or early summer. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

It requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
