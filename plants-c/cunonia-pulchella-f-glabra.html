Cunonia pulchella f. glabra

Cunonia pulchella f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth cunonia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture.
