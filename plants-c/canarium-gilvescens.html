Canarium gilvescens

Canarium gilvescens, commonly known as the yellow canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale yellow bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The tree thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.
