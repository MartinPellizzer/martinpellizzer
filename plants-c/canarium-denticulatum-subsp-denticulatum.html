Canarium denticulatum subsp. denticulatum

Canarium denticulatum subsp.

denticulatum, commonly known as the serrated canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and lanceolate leaves with serrated margins.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spiny fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its timber and has some traditional medicinal uses.
