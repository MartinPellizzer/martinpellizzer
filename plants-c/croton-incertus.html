Croton incertus

Croton incertus, commonly known as the uncertain croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often multicolored leaves that can range from green to red, yellow, and orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton incertus typically grows as an understory plant in humid forests, preferring partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it may require specific care to thrive.
