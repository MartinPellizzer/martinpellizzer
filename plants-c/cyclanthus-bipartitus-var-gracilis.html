Cyclanthus bipartitus var. gracilis

Cyclanthus bipartitus var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender mistletoe, is a unique plant belonging to the Cyclanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy, and somewhat succulent leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is often found growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit.
