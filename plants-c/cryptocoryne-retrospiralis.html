Cryptocoryne retrospiralis

Cryptocoryne retrospiralis, commonly known as the spiral crypts, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water conditions and is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and can grow up to 15 cm in length.

Cryptocoryne retrospiralis is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
