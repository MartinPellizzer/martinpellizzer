Cyclamen africanum subsp. saldense

Cyclamen africanum subsp.

saldense, commonly known as the Cape cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive purple underside and produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink and white.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or shaded borders. Cyclamen africanum subsp.

saldense is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
