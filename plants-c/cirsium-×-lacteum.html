Cirsium × lacteum

Cirsium × lacteum, commonly known as the milk thistle, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant is native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of the Middle East. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Its latex-like sap contains compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal uses.
