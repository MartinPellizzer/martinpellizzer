Conophytum minusculum subsp. aestiflorens

Conophytum minusculum subsp.

aestiflorens, commonly known as the miniature conophytum, is a small, succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or arid environments.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
