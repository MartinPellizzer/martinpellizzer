Cubilia rumphii

Cubilia rumphii, commonly known as the Rumphius' cubicle, is a rare tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that are often ribbed and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
