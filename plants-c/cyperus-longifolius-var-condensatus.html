Cyperus longifolius var. condensatus

Cyperus longifolius var. condensatus, commonly known as the dense flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in dense clusters at the base. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are often clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in wet or moist soil conditions, commonly found in marshy areas or along water bodies.

Its dense growth habit makes it a common weed in aquatic and semi-aquatic environments.
