Cotoneaster qungbixiensis

Cotoneaster qungbixiensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cotoneaster, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
