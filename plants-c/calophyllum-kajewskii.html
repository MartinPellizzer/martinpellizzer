Calophyllum kajewskii

Calophyllum kajewskii, commonly known as the Kajewskii's torch tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale grey bark and large, glossy leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and is used in furniture and construction.

The plant also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.
