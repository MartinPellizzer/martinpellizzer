Chelystachya bancoensis

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It is often found in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
