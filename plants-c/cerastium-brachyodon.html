Cerastium brachyodon

Cerastium brachyodon, commonly known as the short-stemmed mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Its leaves are typically rounded and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
