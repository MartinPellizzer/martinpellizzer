Cactus seemannii

Cactus seemannii, commonly known as the Shiny Cactus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are often followed by small, red fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sunlight.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
