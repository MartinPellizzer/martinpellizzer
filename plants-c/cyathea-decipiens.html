Cyathea decipiens

Cyathea decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in rainforests. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cyathea decipiens plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
