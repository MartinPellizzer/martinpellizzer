Crassula fragarioides

Crassula fragarioides, commonly known as the ice plant or snow plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the cooler months. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with well-draining soil.

Crassula fragarioides is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
