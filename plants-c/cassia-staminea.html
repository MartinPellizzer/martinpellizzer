Cassia staminea

Cassia staminea, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia staminea is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
