Consolida stapfiana

Consolida stapfiana, commonly known as the Stapf's sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that range in color from pink to white, often with a contrasting base. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous annual, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Native to regions of Ethiopia and Eritrea, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.
