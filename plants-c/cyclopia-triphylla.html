Cyclopia triphylla

Cyclopia triphylla, commonly known as the honeybush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is part of the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants. Its flowers are rich in antioxidants and are used to make herbal teas.

Cyclopia triphylla thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
