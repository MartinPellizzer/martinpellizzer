Cistus obscurus

Cistus obscurus, commonly known as the dark rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in coastal and mountainous areas, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.

Cistus obscurus is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
