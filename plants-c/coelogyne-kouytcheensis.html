Coelogyne kouytcheensis

Coelogyne kouytcheensis, commonly known as the Kourou orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange, often with dark red or purple markings. The plant produces a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, a feature common among many orchid species. Its leaves are strap-like and evergreen, contributing to its overall aesthetic appeal.

Coelogyne kouytcheensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
