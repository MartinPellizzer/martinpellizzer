Corydalis Solida Subsp. Remota

Corydalis solida subsp. remota is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Fumariaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of East Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its delicate flowers, often in shades of pink and purple, add a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. Despite its modest appearance, it holds ecological significance as a food source for certain pollinators. This subspecies remains underappreciated, yet it offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity.
