Caladenia mesocera

Caladenia mesocera, commonly known as the pink-streaked spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in heathland and woodland habitats, often in well-drained soils. The plant features a single, erect stem that bears a single, strikingly colored orchid flower with a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its flowers are usually pink or pale purple with dark streaks, and they bloom during the spring.

Caladenia mesocera is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
