Centaurea microptilon subsp. emporitana

Centaurea microptilon subsp.

emporitana, commonly known as the Emosia thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, which are surrounded by pale blue to purple bracts. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to the arid and semi-arid environments of its native range.
