Crepis nigra

Crepis nigra, commonly known as black cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, and it is often found in disturbed soils.

Crepis nigra is a perennial species that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.
