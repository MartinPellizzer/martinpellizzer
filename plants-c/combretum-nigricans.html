Combretum nigricans

Combretum nigricans, commonly known as black flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, reddish-pink flowers that bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a rough texture.

The bark is thick, dark-colored, and has a distinctive flaky appearance.
