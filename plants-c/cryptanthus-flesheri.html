Cryptanthus flesheri

Cryptanthus flesheri, commonly known as the Flesher's winterbloom, is a striking plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to produce vibrant, tubular flowers during the winter months. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves that store water, adapting it to its arid environment. Its flowers, which range in color from red to orange, are often clustered in tight, showy inflorescences.

Cryptanthus flesheri is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
