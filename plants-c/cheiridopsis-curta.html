Cheiridopsis curta

Cheiridopsis curta, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a small succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it an ideal plant for xeriscaping and low-maintenance gardening.
