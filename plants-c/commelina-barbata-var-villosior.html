Commelina barbata var. villosior

Commelina barbata var. villosior, commonly known as the hairy commelina, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and hairy foliage.
