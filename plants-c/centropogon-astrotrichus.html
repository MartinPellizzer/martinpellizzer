Centropogon astrotrichus

Centropogon astrotrichus, commonly known as the star-tipped grass.

It is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly found in Jamaica and Cuba. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. Its inflorescence features bracts that resemble stars, giving it its common name.

Centropogon astrotrichus is typically found in moist, open habitats such as coastal areas and disturbed soils.
