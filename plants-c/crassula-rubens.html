Crassula rubens

Crassula rubens, commonly known as the red crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula rubens is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental value.
