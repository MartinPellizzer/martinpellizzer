Cyclamen coum f. albissimum

Cyclamen coum f. albissimum, commonly known as the white cyclamen, is a rare and striking variant of the cyclamen species.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast sharply with its velvety, deep green foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its tuberous roots allow it to survive harsh winters, making it a hardy perennial.

The plant's unique appearance makes it a prized addition to rock gardens and shaded borders.
