Croton rottleri

Croton rottleri, commonly known as the Rottler's croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored foliage, which can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough, textured surface. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its coloration.

This plant is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
