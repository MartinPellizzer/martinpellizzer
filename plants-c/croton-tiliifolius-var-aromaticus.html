Croton tiliifolius var. aromaticus

Croton tiliifolius var. aromaticus, commonly known as the aromatic croton, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often aromatic when crushed. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and unique fruiting structures.
