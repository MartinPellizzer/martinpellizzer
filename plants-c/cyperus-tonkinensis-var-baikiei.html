Cyperus tonkinensis var. baikiei

Cyperus tonkinensis var. baikiei, commonly known as the Baikie's flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower spikes.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to moist soils.
