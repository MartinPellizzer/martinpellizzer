Ctenitis boholensis

Ctenitis boholensis, commonly known as the Bohol fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of compound leaves that can reach several meters in height. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.
