Cymbocarpum erythraeum

Cymbocarpum erythraeum, commonly known as the red sand mat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and clusters of small, red flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by wind. It is well-suited to hot, dry environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of desert ecosystems.
