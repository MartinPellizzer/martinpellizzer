Ctenitis mollicoma

Ctenitis mollicoma, commonly known as the soft frond fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, soft fronds that are typically divided into narrow, feather-like segments. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients efficiently.

Ctenitis mollicoma is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid, indoor conditions.
