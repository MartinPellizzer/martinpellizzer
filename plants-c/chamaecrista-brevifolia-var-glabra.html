Chamaecrista brevifolia var. glabra

Chamaecrista brevifolia var. glabra, commonly known as smooth ticktree, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, glabrous bark, which is a key feature of the varietal name "glabra.". It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

The plant is noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
