Colchicum montanum var. cupanii

Colchicum montanum var. cupanii, commonly known as the alpine colchicum, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has a short, stout stem with a single, showy flower at the top. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and often withering before flowering.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.
