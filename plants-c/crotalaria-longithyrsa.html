Crotalaria longithyrsa

Crotalaria longithyrsa, commonly known as the long-thyrsa crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual with hairy stems and alternate, palmate leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are often accompanied by prominent, spurred petals. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several kidney-shaped seeds.

Crotalaria longithyrsa is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
