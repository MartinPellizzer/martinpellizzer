Campanula Glomerata Var. Aggregata

Campanula glomerata var. aggregata is a charming flowering plant known for its dense clusters of blue or violet bell-shaped blooms. Native to mountainous regions of Europe, this variety thrives in cool, moist environments and well-drained soil. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes where it adds a splash of color to the landscape. The plant's compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens and borders. Its delicate appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
