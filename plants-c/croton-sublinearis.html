Croton sublinearis

Croton sublinearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, linear leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Croton sublinearis is valued for its ornamental leaves, which display a range of colors from green to reddish-brown.
