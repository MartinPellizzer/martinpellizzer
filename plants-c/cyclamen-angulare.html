Cyclamen angulare

Cyclamen angulare, commonly known as the angular cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive angular outline and produces delicate, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding color to gardens during the colder months.

Cyclamen angulare is also valued for its ability to naturalize and form dense foliage over time.
