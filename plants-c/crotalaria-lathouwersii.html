Crotalaria lathouwersii

Crotalaria lathouwersii, commonly known as the African crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and savannas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some parts of the world.
