Cnicus pungens

Cnicus pungens, commonly known as the Prickly Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features hairy, rough stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, bristly hairs. Its yellow flowers bloom in the spring and are followed by small, oval-shaped fruits.

Cnicus pungens is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.
