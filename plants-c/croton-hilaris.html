Croton hilaris

Croton hilaris, commonly known as the flame tree or fire tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that range from red to orange and yellow, often resembling flames. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing seeds. Croton hilaris thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its purported therapeutic properties.
