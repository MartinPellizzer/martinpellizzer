Croton timandroides

Croton timandroides, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a rough texture.

Croton timandroides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
