Cotyledon sikokiana

Cotyledon sikokiana, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the cooler months, which resemble snowballs, hence its common name. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cotyledon sikokiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
