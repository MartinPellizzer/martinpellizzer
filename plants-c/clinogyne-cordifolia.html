Clinogyne cordifolia

Clinogyne cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It features lanceolate to ovate leaves that are often heart-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces attractive, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers with six petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Clinogyne cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
