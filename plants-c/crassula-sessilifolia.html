Crassula sessilifolia

Crassula sessilifolia, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped growth with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
