Camellia costei

Camellia costei, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and arranged alternately along the stems. Camellia costei thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
