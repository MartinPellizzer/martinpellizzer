Croton cerinus

Croton cerinus, commonly known as the flame tree or red croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from red to orange and yellow, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by spiny, three-lobed fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton cerinus is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
