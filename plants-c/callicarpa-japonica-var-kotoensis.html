Callicarpa japonica var. kotoensis

Callicarpa japonica var. kotoensis, commonly known as the Kotohime beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant purple berries that appear in the autumn, which are not only ornamental but also edible. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green during the growing season. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy flowers and colorful fruit, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
