Chiloglottis dockrillii

Chiloglottis dockrillii, commonly known as the golden sun orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow and orange flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It has linear leaves and produces a single, showy inflorescence with multiple flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
