Chenopodium setigerum

Chenopodium setigerum, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges and glandular hairs that give it a rough texture. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, followed by triangular, beak-like seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in various regions.
