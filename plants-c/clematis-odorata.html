Clematis odorata

Clematis odorata, commonly known as the sweet autumn clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
