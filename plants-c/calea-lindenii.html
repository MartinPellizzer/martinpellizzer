Calea lindenii

Calea lindenii, commonly known as the "flower of the night," is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers that open at night and close in the morning. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic leaves. Calea lindenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nocturnal blooming habit.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.
