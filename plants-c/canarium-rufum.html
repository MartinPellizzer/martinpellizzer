Canarium rufum

Canarium rufum, commonly known as the red canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. Canarium rufum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
