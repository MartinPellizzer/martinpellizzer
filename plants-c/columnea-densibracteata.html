Columnea densibracteata

Columnea densibracteata, commonly known as the dense bract columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces dense clusters of bracts, which are modified leaves that often resemble petals, giving it a vibrant and ornamental appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.

Columnea densibracteata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for with proper light and moisture conditions.
