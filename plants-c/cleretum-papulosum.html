Cleretum papulosum

Cleretum papulosum, commonly known as the woolly cleretum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has hairy, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Cleretum papulosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
