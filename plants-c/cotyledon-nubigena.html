Cotyledon nubigena

Cotyledon nubigena, commonly known as the cloud cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. The leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a distinctive powdery coating called farina. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
