Chamaesyce Nuttallii

Chamaesyce nuttallii is a hardy, flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its unique and often striking blooms. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its modest size and subtle appearance.
