Crataegus × monostevenii

Crataegus × monostevenii, commonly known as the hybrid hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems, oval to ovate leaves, and showy white or pink flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces red or orange berries in the fall, which are an important food source for birds and small mammals.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
