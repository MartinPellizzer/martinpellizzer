Cynanchum itremense

Cynanchum itremense, commonly known as the creeping antirrhinum, is a perennial herb native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
