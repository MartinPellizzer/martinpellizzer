Conophytum friedrichiae

Conophytum friedrichiae, commonly known as the Friedrich's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, leaf-like structures that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid environments.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
