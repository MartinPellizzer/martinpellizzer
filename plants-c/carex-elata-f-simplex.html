Carex elata f. simplex

Carex elata f. simplex, commonly known as the smooth tall sedge, is a perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces cylindrical inflorescences that bear small, greenish flowers. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Its simple, elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in water feature settings.
