Ceroxylon

Ceroxylon, commonly known as the wax palm, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Arecaceae.

These plants are native to South America, particularly in regions of Colombia, Ecuador, and Peru. Ceroxylon species typically grow in humid, tropical environments and can reach heights of up to 30 meters.
