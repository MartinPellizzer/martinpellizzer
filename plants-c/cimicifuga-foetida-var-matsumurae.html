Cimicifuga foetida var. matsumurae

Cimicifuga foetida var. matsumurae, commonly known as Japanese bugbane, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that bear large, white, lacecap flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are compound, with pinnate arrangement and serrated edges.

The roots contain bioactive compounds, making it a subject of interest in traditional and modern herbal medicine.
