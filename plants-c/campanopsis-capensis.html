Campanopsis capensis

Campanopsis capensis, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanopsis capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
