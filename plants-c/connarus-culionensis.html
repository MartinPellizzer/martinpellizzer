Connarus culionensis

Connarus culionensis, commonly known as the Culion tree, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The plant has simple, alternate, elliptical leaves with a smooth margin. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in clusters, and it produces woody fruits containing several seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in secondary forests and disturbed areas.
