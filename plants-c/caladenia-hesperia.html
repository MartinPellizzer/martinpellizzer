Caladenia hesperia

Caladenia hesperia, commonly known as the Esperance spider-orchid, is a striking orchid native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance with a single, erect flower that is typically pale green to yellow in color. The plant has a fibrous root system and features a single leaf that is narrow and grass-like. It blooms during the autumn season, producing a unique and delicate flower that is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts.

This species is listed as vulnerable due to habitat loss and is often found in sandy soils in heathlands and coastal areas.
