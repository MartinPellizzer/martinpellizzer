Cypripedium parviflorum var. parviflorum

Cypripedium parviflorum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy, slipper-shaped flower that is typically pink or white with a yellow throat. The plant has a short rhizome and produces a single, large, fleshy leaf. It thrives in moist, shaded forest habitats and is often found in rich, well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also considered a symbol of resilience and grace in botanical gardens.
