Crotalaria sennii

Crotalaria sennii, commonly known as the Senna tree or African Senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in spikes, which are characteristic of the Crotalaria genus. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
