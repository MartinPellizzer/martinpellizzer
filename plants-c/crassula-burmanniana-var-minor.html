Crassula burmanniana var. minor

Crassula burmanniana var. minor, commonly known as the Miniature Crassula or Burmann's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge under stress or in bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
