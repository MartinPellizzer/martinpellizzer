Chacaya trinervis

Chacaya trinervis, commonly known as the Andean chachalaca, is a member of the Polygonaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the Andean regions of South America. The plant features palmate leaves with three to five lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Chacaya trinervis is adapted to high-altitude environments and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
