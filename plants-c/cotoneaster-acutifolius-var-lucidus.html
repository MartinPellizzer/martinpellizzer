Cotoneaster acutifolius var. lucidus

Cotoneaster acutifolius var. lucidus, commonly known as the shining cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking visual contrast in the landscape. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
