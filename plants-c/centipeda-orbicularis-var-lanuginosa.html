Centipeda orbicularis var. lanuginosa

Centipeda orbicularis var. lanuginosa, commonly known as the fuzzy centipeda, is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its woolly, lanuginous foliage.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are broadly rounded and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
