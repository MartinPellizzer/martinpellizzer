Croton trichotomus var. pulchellus

Croton trichotomus var. pulchellus, commonly known as the pretty croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
