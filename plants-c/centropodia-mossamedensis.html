Centropodia mossamedensis

Centropodia mossamedensis, commonly known as the Mossamedes centropodia, is a rare and specialized moss species native to the high-altitude regions of the Ethiopian Highlands.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, cushion-like formations that help it retain moisture in its arid environment. The plant's leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. It thrives in rocky substrates and is adapted to the cool, humid microclimates found in its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
