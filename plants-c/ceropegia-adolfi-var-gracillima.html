Ceropegia adolfi var. gracillima

Ceropegia adolfi var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which are adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for hanging baskets and indoor gardens.
