Croton rubescens

Croton rubescens, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental members. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and typically have a rough texture.

Croton rubescens is primarily cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf colors and adaptability to various growing conditions.
