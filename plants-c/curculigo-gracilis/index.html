Curculigo Gracilis

Curculigo gracilis is a unique tropical plant known for its striking, spiral-shaped leaves. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. This species is prized for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish.
