Cyrtanthus stenanthus var. stenanthus

Cyrtanthus stenanthus var. stenanthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved glory-of-the-snow, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

The flowers bloom in spring, adding a striking display to the landscape.
