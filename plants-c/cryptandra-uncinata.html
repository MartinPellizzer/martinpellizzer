Cryptandra uncinata

Cryptandra uncinata, commonly known as the hooked cryptandra, is a small shrub native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Cryptandra uncinata is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal blooms.
