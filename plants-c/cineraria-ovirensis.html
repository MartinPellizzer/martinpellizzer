Cineraria ovirensis

Cineraria ovirensis, commonly known as the Canary Islands cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, white coating, which helps reduce water loss in its arid environment.

Cineraria ovirensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
