Centaurea limbata var. microblepharis

Centaurea limbata var. microblepharis, commonly known as the small-eyed centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright blue to purple composite flowers. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs. This variety is distinguished by its smaller, more rounded flower heads compared to other Centaurea species.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
