Clitoria javitensis f. truncata

Clitoria javitensis f. truncata, commonly known as the dwarf butterfly pea, is a variant of the butterfly pea plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing legume characterized by its heart-shaped, violet-blue flowers that resemble butterflies. The plant has trifoliate leaves with stipules and produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
