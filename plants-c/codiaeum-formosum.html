Codiaeum formosum

Codiaeum formosum, commonly known as the flame leaf or croton plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated foliage, which can display shades of red, orange, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its unique, palmate leaves that can vary in shape and color depending on the cultivar. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in gardens.
