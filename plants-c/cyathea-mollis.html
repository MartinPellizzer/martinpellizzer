Cyathea mollis

Cyathea mollis, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome that supports its tall growth, and its fronds are typically green with a waxy texture. Cyathea mollis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
