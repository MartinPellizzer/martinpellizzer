Calathea anulque

Calathea anulque, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled leaves with a distinctive zebra-like pattern of light and dark green. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate veins that add to its ornamental appeal.

Calathea anulque requires consistently moist soil and high humidity to flourish.
