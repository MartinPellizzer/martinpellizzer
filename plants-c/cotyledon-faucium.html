Cotyledon faucium

Cotyledon faucium, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red, orange, and purple. The plant has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers during the growing season.

Cotyledon faucium is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
