Cleome lutea var. polyphylla

Cleome lutea var. polyphylla, commonly known as yellow cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its branching stems and showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a rough texture that distinguishes it from other Cleome species.
