Cnidoscolus jurgensenii

Cnidoscolus jurgensenii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, stinging hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a spiny capsule that contains several seeds.

The species is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some indigenous communities.
