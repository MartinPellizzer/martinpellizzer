Chodanthus splendens var. grandiflora

Chodanthus splendens var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora chodanthus, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that display vibrant colors, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking to add visual interest to their landscapes.
