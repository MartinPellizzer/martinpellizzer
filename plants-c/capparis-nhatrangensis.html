Capparis nhatrangensis

Capparis nhatrangensis, commonly known as the Nhatrang caper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often followed by round, greenish fruits. Its leaves are typically lobed and have a somewhat succulent texture.

The plant is adapted to coastal and sandy environments, showing some degree of salt tolerance.
