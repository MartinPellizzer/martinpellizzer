Cirsium nipponicum var. nipponicum

Cirsium nipponicum var. nipponicum, commonly known as Japanese thistle, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its spiny, leafy stems and large, purple to pink flower heads that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
