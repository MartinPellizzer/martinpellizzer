Celmisia × flaccida

Celmisia × flaccida, commonly known as the flaccid celmisia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its hairy, herbaceous stems and clustered, small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed outside its native range.
