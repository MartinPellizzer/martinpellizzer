Cacalia koualapensis var. bulbifera

Cacalia koualapensis var. bulbifera, commonly known as the bulbiferous bellflower, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces bulb-like structures, which are an adaptation for vegetative propagation and storage of nutrients. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

This variant is notable for its unique reproductive strategy and ornamental value in horticulture.
