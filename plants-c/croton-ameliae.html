Croton ameliae

Croton ameliae, commonly known as the Amelia's croton, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage, which often displays shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
