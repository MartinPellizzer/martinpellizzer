Croton pottsii var. thermophilus

Croton pottsii var. thermophilus, commonly known as the flame tree or fire tree, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, flame-like foliage.

Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The leaves are typically large, palmately lobed, and display a range of colors from deep red to orange and yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

This plant is well-suited for tropical and subtropical gardens, where it can add dramatic visual interest.
