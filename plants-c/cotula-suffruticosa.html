Cotula suffruticosa

Cotula suffruticosa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, mat-like growth habit and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are typically narrow and slightly serrated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cotula suffruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
