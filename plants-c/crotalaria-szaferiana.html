Crotalaria szaferiana

Crotalaria szaferiana, commonly known as the Szafer's cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is often found in disturbed areas. Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Crotalaria szaferiana is also noted for its potential use in agricultural systems due to its growth habit and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
