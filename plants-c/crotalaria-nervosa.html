Crotalaria nervosa

Crotalaria nervosa, commonly known as the narrow-veined crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant features palmately divided leaves and produces bright yellow or orange flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its seeds are encased in distinctive, inflated pods that split open when mature.

Crotalaria nervosa is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types but thrives in well-drained, sunny environments.
