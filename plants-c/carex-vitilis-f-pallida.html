Carex vitilis f. pallida

Carex vitilis f. pallida, commonly known as the pale form of the white sedge.

This plant is a perennial herbaceous species native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Its pale coloration is a distinguishing characteristic of this particular form.
