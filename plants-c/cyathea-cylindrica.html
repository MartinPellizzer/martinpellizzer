Cyathea cylindrica

Cyathea cylindrica, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a unique structure with a central trunk covered in a layer of old fronds, which gives it a distinctive appearance. Cyathea cylindrica thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests.

Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a valuable component of New Zealand's native flora.
