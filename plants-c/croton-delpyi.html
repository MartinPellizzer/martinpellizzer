Croton delpyi

Croton delpyi, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from green to reddish-purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically thick and leathery, with a velvety texture that gives it its common name.

Croton delpyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.
