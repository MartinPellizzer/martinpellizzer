Campanopsis Dichotoma

Campanopsis dichotoma is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family known for its bell-shaped flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique appearance and delicate blooms make it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the global horticultural community. This article explores its characteristics, habitat, and potential for cultivation.
