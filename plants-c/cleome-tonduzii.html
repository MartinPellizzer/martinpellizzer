Cleome tonduzii

Cleome tonduzii, commonly known as the red cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cleome tonduzii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
