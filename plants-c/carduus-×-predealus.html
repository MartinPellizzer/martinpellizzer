Carduus × predealus

Carduus × predealus, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant that belongs to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads, which consist of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in various habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in a range of environmental conditions.

Due to its invasive nature in some regions, it is often considered a weed that can outcompete native vegetation.
