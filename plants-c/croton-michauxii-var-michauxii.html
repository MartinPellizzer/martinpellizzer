Croton michauxii var. michauxii

Croton michauxii var. michauxii, commonly known as the Michaux's croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves that display a variety of shades, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and indoor collections.
