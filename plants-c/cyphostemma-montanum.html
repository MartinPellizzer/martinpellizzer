Cyphostemma montanum

Cyphostemma montanum, commonly known as the Mountain Cyphostemma, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its thin, woody stems and opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by follicles containing seeds. Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
