Calanthe buccinifera

Calanthe buccinifera, commonly known as the horned calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often feature a distinctive horn-like spur. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth, waxy surface.

Calanthe buccinifera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
