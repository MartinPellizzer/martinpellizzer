Campanula lanceolata var. hornungiana

Campanula lanceolata var. hornungiana, commonly known as the Hornung's bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lanceolate, with a pointed tip and a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
