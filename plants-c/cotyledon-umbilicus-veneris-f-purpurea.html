Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris f. purpurea

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris f. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Heart Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically purple or reddish in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor plant collections.
