Cryptocarya palmerensis

Cryptocarya palmerensis, commonly known as Palmer's cryptocarya, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southern United States, particularly in Texas and Arizona.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.
