Crateva benthamii var. leptopetala

Crateva benthamii var. leptopetala, commonly known as the slender petal crateva, is a climbing shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its distinctive fruit, which is a spiny, spherical capsule containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
