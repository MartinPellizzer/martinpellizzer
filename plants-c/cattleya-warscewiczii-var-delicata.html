Cattleya warscewiczii var. delicata

Cattleya warscewiczii var. delicata, commonly known as the Delicate Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces vibrant, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, with showy, pendulous flowers that are highly prized in horticulture. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, arranged in a fan shape, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
