Carduus × variabilis subsp. carbonelii

Carduus × variabilis subsp.

carbonelii, commonly known as the carbonelii thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features a robust stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, covered with coarse, spiny hairs.

The plant produces large, showy purple to violet composite flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are deeply lobed, with a rough texture and often have a bluish-green hue.

The species is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
