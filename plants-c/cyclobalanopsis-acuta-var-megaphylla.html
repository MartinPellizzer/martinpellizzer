Cyclobalanopsis acuta var. megaphylla

Cyclobalanopsis acuta var. megaphylla, commonly known as the large-leafed ironwood, is a variant of the cyclobalanopsis genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often prominently veined. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by hard, woody fruits containing one or two seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland and hill forests.

Due to its durable timber, it has been historically valued for construction and furniture-making.
