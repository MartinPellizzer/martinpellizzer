Carex arisanensis subsp. arisanensis

Carex arisanensis subsp.

arisanensis, commonly known as the Arisan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae. It is native to Japan, specifically the island of Kyushu, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The plant is characterized by its grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is often found in mountainous regions and along stream banks.
