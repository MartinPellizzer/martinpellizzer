Cryptocoryne kerrii

Cryptocoryne kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant typically forms dense clusters and is often found in slow-moving freshwater habitats. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and can thrive in a variety of water conditions.

Cryptocoryne kerrii is popular among aquarium hobbyists due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
