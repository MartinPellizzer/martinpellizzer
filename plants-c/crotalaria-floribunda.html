Crotalaria floribunda

Crotalaria floribunda, commonly known as the clustered crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and dense clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets, and its flowers are typically borne in terminal inflorescences. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
