Calathea micans var. robustior

Calathea micans var. robustior, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that fold upwards at night, a behavior known as nyctinasty. The foliage displays intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink, often with a metallic sheen. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
