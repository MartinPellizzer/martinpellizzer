Canavalia stenophylla

Canavalia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and cylindrical, hairy pods that contain edible seeds. The plant thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its protein-rich seeds. It has a deep root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, improving soil fertility.

Canavalia stenophylla is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
