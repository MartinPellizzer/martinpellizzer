Correa revoluta

Correa revoluta, commonly known as the mountain correa, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features attractive, fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Correa revoluta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
