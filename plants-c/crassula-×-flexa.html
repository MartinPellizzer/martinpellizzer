Crassula × flexa

Crassula × flexa, commonly known as the Christmas Jade, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
