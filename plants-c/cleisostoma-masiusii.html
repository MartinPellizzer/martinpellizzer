Cleisostoma masiusii

Cleisostoma masiusii, commonly known as the golden cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms during the warmer months, and its inflorescence consists of a single raceme with numerous small, clustered flowers. Cleisostoma masiusii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
