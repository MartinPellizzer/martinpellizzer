Carex sciocapensis

Carex sciocapensis, commonly known as the dark sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a dark green coloration that gives it its common name.

Carex sciocapensis produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the sedge family.
