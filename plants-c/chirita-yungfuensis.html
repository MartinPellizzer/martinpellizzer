Chirita yungfuensis

Chirita yungfuensis, commonly known as the Yungfu vining plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to southern China, particularly Yunnan Province, where it grows in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit, heart-shaped leaves, and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of humidity.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
