Chamaecrista villosissima

Chamaecrista villosissima, commonly known as the silky crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, yellow-orange pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with numerous small leaflets, and it produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
