Clematis montana rubra

Clematis montana rubra, commonly known as the red clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep red, depending on the variety. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Clematis montana rubra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
