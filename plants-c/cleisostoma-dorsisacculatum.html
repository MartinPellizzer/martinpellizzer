Cleisostoma dorsisacculatum

Cleisostoma dorsisacculatum, commonly known as the saddle-shaped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique saddle-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Cleisostoma dorsisacculatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
