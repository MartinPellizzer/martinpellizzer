Colchicum neapolitanum var. tenorei

Colchicum neapolitanum var. tenorei, commonly known as the Naples colchicum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone-rich habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens but is also considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
