Chrysosplenium yunnanense

Chrysosplenium yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan's golden spleenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit and dense foliage. The plant typically features small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale yellow, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate to elliptical in shape, and covered in fine hairs.

Chrysosplenium yunnanense is adapted to cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or shaded forest habitats.
