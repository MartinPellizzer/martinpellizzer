Cardamine purpurea f. albiflora

Cardamine purpurea f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered purple cress, is a rare variant of the purple cress species.

It is characterized by its pale or white flowers, which contrast with the typical purple blooms of the parent species. This plant is a member of the Brassicaceae family and typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors.
