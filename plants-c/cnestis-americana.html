Cnestis americana

Cnestis americana, commonly known as American morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces trumpet-shaped, violet to blue flowers that bloom in the morning. Its seeds are enclosed in a spiral-shaped capsule that twists as it matures.

Cnestis americana is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.
