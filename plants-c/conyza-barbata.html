Conyza barbata

Conyza barbata, commonly known as the hairy fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as fields and roadsides. The plant has small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are covered in fine hairs. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Conyza barbata is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in some regions.
