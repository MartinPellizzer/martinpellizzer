Cotyledon grandiflora

Cotyledon grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often adorned with striking patterns and colors. The plant produces showy, violet or pink flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Cotyledon grandiflora is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
