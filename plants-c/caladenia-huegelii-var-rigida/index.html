Caladenia Huegelii Var. Rigida

Caladenia huegelii var. rigida is a striking orchid native to the arid regions of Australia. This rare and delicate plant is known for its unique, spurred petals and vibrant coloration. It thrives in rocky soils and sparse vegetation, showcasing remarkable adaptations to harsh environments. Its blooming period typically occurs during late winter to early spring. Due to habitat loss and climate change, this species faces increasing threats to its survival.
