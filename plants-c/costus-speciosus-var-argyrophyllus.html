Costus speciosus var. argyrophyllus

Costus speciosus var. argyrophyllus, commonly known as the silver-leafed costus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features striking, sword-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-gray underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to tropical and subtropical gardens.
