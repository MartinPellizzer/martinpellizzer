Callisia ionantha

Callisia ionantha, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Mexico.

It features trailing stems that produce clusters of small, white, spider-like flowers, which give it its common name. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for hanging baskets or as a trailing plant.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
