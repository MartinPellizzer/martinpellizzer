Cirsium uetsuense

Cirsium uetsuense, commonly known as the Uetsu thistle, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Cirsium uetsuense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
