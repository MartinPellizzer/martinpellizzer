Clausena dunniana

Clausena dunniana, commonly known as the Malayan tamarind tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature, containing a single seed. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
