Corchorus parviflorus var. gracilescens

Corchorus parviflorus var. gracilescens, commonly known as slenderleaf cottonthorn, is a herbaceous annual plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with hairy foliage and small, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant produces capsules that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
