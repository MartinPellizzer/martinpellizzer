Catasetum stenochilum

Catasetum stenochilum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces striking, often brightly colored flowers with a specialized lip that aids in pollination by specific insects. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Catasetum stenochilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
