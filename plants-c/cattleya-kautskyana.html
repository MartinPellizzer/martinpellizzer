Cattleya kautskyana

Cattleya kautskyana, commonly known as the Kautsky cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and sometimes include green or yellow markings. The plant produces pendulous blooms on tall, erect stems, and its leaves are usually broad and strap-like. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and fragrance.
