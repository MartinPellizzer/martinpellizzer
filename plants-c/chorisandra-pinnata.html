Chorisandra pinnata

Chorisandra pinnata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chorisandra, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a pinnate pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Chorisandra pinnata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
