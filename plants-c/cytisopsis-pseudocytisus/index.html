Cytisopsis Pseudocytisus

Cytisopsis pseudocytisus is a lesser-known but fascinating member of the Fabaceae family. Native to the Canary Islands, this plant thrives in rocky and coastal environments. It is characterized by its delicate yellow flowers and feathery foliage. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique plant from habitat loss and environmental changes.
