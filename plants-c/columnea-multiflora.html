Columnea multiflora

Columnea multiflora, commonly known as the flame flower or red-tipped columnea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Columnea multiflora is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
