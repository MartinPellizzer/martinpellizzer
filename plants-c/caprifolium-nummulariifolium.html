Caprifolium nummulariifolium

Caprifolium nummulariifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white, waxy hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and delicate blossoms make it a valuable addition to biodiversity-rich landscapes.
