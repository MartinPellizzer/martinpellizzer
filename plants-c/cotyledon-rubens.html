Cotyledon rubens

Cotyledon rubens, commonly known as the red-leaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically red or purple in color, which help it store water and perform photosynthesis efficiently. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container gardening.

Cotyledon rubens thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.
