Cryptocoryne mekongensis

Cryptocoryne mekongensis, commonly known as the Mekong cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, broad leaves that often exhibit a variegated pattern with light green or yellowish markings. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as rivers and lakes, and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often found in shaded areas with soft, nutrient-rich substrates.

Cryptocoryne mekongensis is popular among aquarium enthusiasts due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
