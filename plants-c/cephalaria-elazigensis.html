Cephalaria elazigensis

Cephalaria elazigensis, commonly known as the Elazig cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
