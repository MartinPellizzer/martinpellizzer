Crassula volkensii subsp. volkensii

Crassula volkensii subsp.

volkensii, commonly known as the "Snowflake Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa. It features dense clusters of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
