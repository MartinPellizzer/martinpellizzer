Clitoria racemosa

Clitoria racemosa, commonly known as the winged bean or butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain edible seeds and beans. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often cultivated for both ornamental and nutritional purposes.

The flowers are also used in traditional medicine and as a natural dye.
