Crassula sphaeritis

Crassula sphaeritis, commonly known as the globe-shaped crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Crassula sphaeritis is popular as a low-maintenance indoor plant due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
