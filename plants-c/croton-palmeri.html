Croton palmeri

Croton palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's croton, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Croton palmeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and unique appearance.
