Campanula bononiensis var. latifolia

Campanula bononiensis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
