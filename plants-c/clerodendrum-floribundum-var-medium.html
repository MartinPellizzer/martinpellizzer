Clerodendrum floribundum var. medium

Clerodendrum floribundum var. medium, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often mistaken for flowers. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered within the showy bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and container planting.
