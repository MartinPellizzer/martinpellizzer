Callitriche drummondii

Callitriche drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, streams, and wetlands. The plant has tiny, star-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. It produces small, white flowers that bloom from June to September.

Callitriche drummondii is often found in still or slow-moving water and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-rich substrates.
