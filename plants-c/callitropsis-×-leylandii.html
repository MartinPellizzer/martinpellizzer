Callitropsis × leylandii

Callitropsis × leylandii, commonly known as Leyland cypress, is a hybrid coniferous evergreen shrub or tree native to the western coast of Britain.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and needle-like foliage that is dark green on top and light green underneath. The plant is highly valued for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types, making it a popular choice for hedges and ornamental landscaping. However, it is susceptible to diseases such as root rot and can be prone to wind damage due to its weak wood.

Its botanical features make it a useful but sometimes problematic plant in both natural and cultivated environments.
