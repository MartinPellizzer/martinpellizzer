Centaurea geluensis

Centaurea geluensis, commonly known as the Geluensian knapweed, is a flowering plant native to Romania.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It features upright stems with narrow leaves and produces vibrant blue or purple flowers.

Centaurea geluensis is noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
