Cirsium biebersteinii

Cirsium biebersteinii, commonly known as the Sierra cliff thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, violet-blue or purple flower heads. The plant has compound leaves that are typically grayish-green with a rough texture. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
