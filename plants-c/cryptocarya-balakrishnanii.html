Cryptocarya balakrishnanii

Cryptocarya balakrishnanii, commonly known as the Nilgiri cryptocarya, is a small evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats at moderate altitudes. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a distinct aromatic scent when crushed.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and potential medicinal properties.
