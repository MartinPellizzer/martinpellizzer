Cynanchica garganica

Cynanchica garganica, commonly known as the Garganica milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its erect stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Cynanchica garganica is adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a resilient plant in arid environments.
