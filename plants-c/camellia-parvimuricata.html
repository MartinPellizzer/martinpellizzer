Camellia parvimuricata

Camellia parvimuricata, commonly known as the dwarf camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Camellia parvimuricata is also valued for its cold hardiness and ability to bloom in late winter or early spring.
