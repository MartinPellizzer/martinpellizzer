Campanula sparsa subsp. sparsa

Campanula sparsa subsp.

sparsa, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to full sun conditions. It is often found in open woodlands, grasslands, and along coastal cliffs. Campanula sparsa subsp.

sparsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
