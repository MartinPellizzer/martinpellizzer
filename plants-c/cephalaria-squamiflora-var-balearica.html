Cephalaria squamiflora var. balearica

Cephalaria squamiflora var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
