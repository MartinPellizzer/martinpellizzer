Cleome sulfurea

Cleome sulfurea, commonly known as yellow cleome, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, round seeds. Its foliage is typically green with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Cleome sulfurea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and ease of care.
