Conyza lucida

Conyza lucida, commonly known as the white dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces lightweight seeds with parachute-like structures, aiding in wind dispersal. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open fields and along roadsides.

Conyza lucida is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
