Cocos cogniauxiana

Cocos cogniauxiana, commonly known as the African coconut palm, is a tall, slender tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its pinnate leaves and large, fibrous fruits. The plant produces a coconut-like fruit that contains a white, fleshy aril and a hard, inner shell. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in sandy or coastal soils.

The plant is often cultivated for its fruit and ornamental value in warm climates.
