Corybas vinosus

Corybas vinosus, commonly known as the wine cup orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, hooded flower that resembles a wine cup, typically in shades of purple or red. The plant produces a single, showy flower per stem, which blooms in late winter to early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests or along stream banks.

The orchid's unique morphology and vibrant coloration make it a notable species in its native habitat.
