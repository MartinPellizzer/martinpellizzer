Cipura northiana var. coelestis

Cipura northiana var. coelestis, commonly known as the sky blue cipura, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue-pink petals that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil to flourish. Its leaves are broad and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
