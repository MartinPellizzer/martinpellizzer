Chlorophytum anthericoideum

Chlorophytum anthericoideum, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks that bear small, white, bell-shaped flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Chlorophytum anthericoideum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and resilience.
