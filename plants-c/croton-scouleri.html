Croton scouleri

Croton scouleri, commonly known as the mountain croton, is a shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright yellow to deep red and orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically ovate or elliptical with serrated edges.

Croton scouleri thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
