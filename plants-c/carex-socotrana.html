Carex socotrana

Carex socotrana, commonly known as Socotra sedge, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, adapting well to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.

Carex socotrana is notable for its unique morphology and is an important part of the island's endemic flora.
