Cattleya warocqueana

Cattleya warocqueana, commonly known as the Warocqueana orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya warocqueana is prized for its beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
