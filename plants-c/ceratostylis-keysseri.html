Ceratostylis keysseri

Ceratostylis keysseri, commonly known as the Keyser's spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single spike bearing several small, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow calli. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ceratostylis keysseri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
