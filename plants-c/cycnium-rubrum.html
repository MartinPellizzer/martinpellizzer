Cycnium rubrum

Cycnium rubrum, commonly known as the red cocklebur, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and spiny, spherical fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, brown coating. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spherical heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. Cycnium rubrum thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and along riverbanks.

It is an annual plant that completes its life cycle within one growing season.
