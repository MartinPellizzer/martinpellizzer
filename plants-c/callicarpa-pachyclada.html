Callicarpa pachyclada

Callicarpa pachyclada, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive clusters of vibrant purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

The berries are not only ornamental but also serve as a food source for birds and other wildlife.
