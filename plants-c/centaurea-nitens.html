Centaurea nitens

Centaurea nitens, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky, or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, sparsely arranged blue to purple flowers. It blooms from late spring to early summer, and its seeds are dispersed by wind.

Centaurea nitens is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
