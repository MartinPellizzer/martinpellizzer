Cyrenea

Cyrenea, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with distinctive silvery-gray foliage. The leaves are oval-shaped and have a leathery texture, giving the plant its characteristic appearance. Cyrenea produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, followed by edible, round fruits.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to dry, rocky soils.
