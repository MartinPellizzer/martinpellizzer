Caladium schomburgkii f. pictum

Caladium schomburgkii f. pictum, commonly known as the Painted Caladium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a variety of hues including red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its foliage is the main attraction, making it a popular choice for adding visual interest to landscapes and interiors.
