Cheirostylis celebensis

Cheirostylis celebensis, commonly known as the Celebes crested orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species found in the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, crested floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in montane forests. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it has a short, rhizomatous stem.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists.
