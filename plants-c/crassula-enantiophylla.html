Crassula enantiophylla

Crassula enantiophylla, commonly known as the "Cape snowflake," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
