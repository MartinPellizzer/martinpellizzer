Cynoxylon Kousa

Kousa dogwood, scientifically known as Cynoxylon kousa, is a striking ornamental tree native to eastern Asia. With its showy bracts and vibrant seasonal color changes, it is a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The tree is prized for its unique flowering pattern and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its elegant form and seasonal beauty make it a standout in both residential and public spaces. Whether in bloom or in fall foliage, the Kousa dogwood offers year-round visual interest.
