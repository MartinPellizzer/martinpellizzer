Cotyledon breviflora var. rhodantha

Cotyledon breviflora var. rhodantha, commonly known as the red-veined cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple veins. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
