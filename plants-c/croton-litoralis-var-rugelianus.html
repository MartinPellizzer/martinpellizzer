Croton litoralis var. rugelianus

Croton litoralis var. rugelianus, commonly known as the rugged croton, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a compact form and hairy stems.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.
