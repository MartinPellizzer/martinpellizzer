Croton squamigerus var. acutifolius

Croton squamigerus var. acutifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in panicle inflorescences. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
