Caladium haematostigma

Caladium haematostigma, commonly known as the bloodleaf, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with deep red, pink, and orange veins against a green or white background. The leaves are usually heart-shaped or arrowhead-shaped, and they are often used as ornamental plants in gardens and indoor settings. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.
