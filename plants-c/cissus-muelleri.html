Cissus muelleri

Cissus muelleri, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a species of climbing plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are usually a striking silver-green color. The plant produces small, pale green flowers followed by edible, round, yellow fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cissus muelleri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
