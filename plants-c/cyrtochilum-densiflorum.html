Cyrtochilum densiflorum

Cyrtochilum densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered cyclochilum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of dense, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of white and yellow, which are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. Cyrtochilum densiflorum thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
