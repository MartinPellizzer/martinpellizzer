Chimonobambusa sangzhiensis

Chimonobambusa sangzhiensis, commonly known as the Sangzhi bamboo, is a species of grass family plant native to China.

It is a clump-forming bamboo that typically grows in mountainous regions with high humidity and cool temperatures. The plant features slender, hollow stems that are often used in traditional crafts and construction. Its leaves are long and narrow, providing a dense canopy that helps retain moisture in its natural habitat.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental qualities and sustainable use in eco-friendly products.
