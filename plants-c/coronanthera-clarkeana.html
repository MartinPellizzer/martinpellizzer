Coronanthera clarkeana

Coronanthera clarkeana, commonly known as the Clarke's coronanthera, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China and northern Vietnam.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that allow it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, while the flowers are borne on erect, hairy stems.
