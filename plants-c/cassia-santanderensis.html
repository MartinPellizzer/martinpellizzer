Cassia santanderensis

Cassia santanderensis, commonly known as the Santander Cassia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, specifically Colombia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
