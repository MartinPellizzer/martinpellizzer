Canangium latifolium

Canangium latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf cananga, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and fragrant, white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.

Canangium latifolium thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
