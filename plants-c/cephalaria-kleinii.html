Cephalaria kleinii

Cephalaria kleinii, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, clustered blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Cephalaria kleinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
