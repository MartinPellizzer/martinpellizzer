Cleistocactus ritteri

Cleistocactus ritteri, commonly known as the Ritter's cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in the evening, a unique trait among cacti. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and striking flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
