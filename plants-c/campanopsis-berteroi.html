Campanopsis berteroi

Campanopsis berteroi, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, blooming in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Campanopsis berteroi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
