Chamaecrista nictitans var. aeschinomene

Chamaecrista nictitans var. aeschinomene, commonly known as the velvet bean or cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with a velvety texture and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and used in various cuisines. It also has a deep root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

Additionally, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
