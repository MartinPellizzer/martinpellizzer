Clematis uhehensis

Clematis uhehensis, commonly known as the African Clematis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a deciduous vine that produces showy, blue to violet flowers in the spring. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
