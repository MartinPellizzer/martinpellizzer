Cattleya skinneri var. parviflora

Cattleya skinneri var. parviflora, commonly known as the miniature cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically produces 3 to 5 blooms per stem, each with a velvety texture and intricate petal structure. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
