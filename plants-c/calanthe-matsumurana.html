Calanthe matsumurana

Calanthe matsumurana, commonly known as the Japanese calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its upright, rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, with petals that are often adorned with intricate markings. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe matsumurana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its elegance.
