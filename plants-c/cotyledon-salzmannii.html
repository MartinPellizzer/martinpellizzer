Cotyledon salzmannii

Cotyledon salzmannii, commonly known as the Salzmann's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
