Chrysophyllum caracasanum

Chrysophyllum caracasanum, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant white flowers. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
