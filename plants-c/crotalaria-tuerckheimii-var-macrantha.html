Crotalaria tuerckheimii var. macrantha

Crotalaria tuerckheimii var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered crotalaria, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its erect, branching stem and distinctive yellow flowers with a spurred structure. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are typically dispersed by wind or animals.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
