Callitriche sonderi

Callitriche sonderi, commonly known as Sonder's water-star, is a small aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and marshes. The plant has slender, ribbon-like leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces small, white flowers that are often submerged or floating.

Callitriche sonderi is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich waters and is often used in wetland restoration projects.
