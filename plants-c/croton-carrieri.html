Croton carrieri

Croton carrieri, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
