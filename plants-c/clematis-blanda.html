Clematis blanda

Clematis blanda, commonly known as the sweet autumn clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern North America.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that range from white to pale blue, blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has heart-shaped, lobed leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in partial to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Clematis blanda is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or other structures.
