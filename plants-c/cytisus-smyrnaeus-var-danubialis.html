Cytisus smyrnaeus var. danubialis

Cytisus smyrnaeus var. danubialis, commonly known as the Danube broom, is a shrub native to southeastern Europe.

It features slender, arching stems covered in bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as hillsides and grasslands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden planting in temperate climates.
