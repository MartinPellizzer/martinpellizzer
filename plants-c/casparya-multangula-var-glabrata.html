Casparya multangula var. glabrata

Casparya multangula var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth caspary, is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
