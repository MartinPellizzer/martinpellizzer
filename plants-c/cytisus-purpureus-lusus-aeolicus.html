Cytisus purpureus lusus aeolicus

Cytisus purpureus lusus aeolicus, commonly known as the Aeolic purple broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, bright purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
