Cyclamen × marxii

Cyclamen × marxii, commonly known as the hybrid cyclamen, is a perennial flowering plant characterized by its striking, often bi-colored flowers.

It is a hybrid between Cyclamen persicum and Cyclamen coum, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy blooms in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and containers.

Cyclamen × marxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the winter months.
