Coleus toroensis

Coleus toroensis, commonly known as the Toroensis coleus, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and exhibit a wide range of colors, including red, purple, and green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
