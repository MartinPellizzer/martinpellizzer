Curio acaulis

Curio acaulis, commonly known as the silver tree, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, tree-like appearance with a thick, woody stem and dense clusters of small, fleshy leaves. The leaves are typically a striking silver-gray color, which helps the plant reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise spiky form.

Due to its slow growth and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
