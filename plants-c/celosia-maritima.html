Celosia maritima

Celosia maritima, commonly known as saltbush, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant is adapted to saline and arid environments, making it a hardy species for coastal landscapes. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly succulent, helping to reduce water loss.

Celosia maritima is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and resilience to harsh conditions.
