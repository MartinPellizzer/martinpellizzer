Crepinia dumalis

Crepinia dumalis, commonly known as the slender sedum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crepinia dumalis is often grown as an ornamental plant in rock gardens or as a houseplant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
