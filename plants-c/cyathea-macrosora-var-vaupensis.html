Cyathea macrosora var. vaupensis

Cyathea macrosora var. vaupensis, commonly known as the Vaupensis tree fern, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is a large, evergreen tree fern characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and longevity. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, and the plant is often found in moist, shaded environments.
