Centranthus maroccanus var. macrocentron

Centranthus maroccanus var. macrocentron, commonly known as the Moroccan centranthus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces distinctive, elongated seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
