Conomorpha oblongifolia

Conomorpha oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed conomorpha, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. Conomorpha oblongifolia is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and can be found in various horticultural collections.
