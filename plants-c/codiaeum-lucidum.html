Codiaeum lucidum

Codiaeum lucidum, commonly known as the shining croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated foliage, which can range from bright yellow to deep red and green. The leaves are typically broad, heart-shaped, and have a glossy surface that enhances their visual appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its vibrant and long-lasting foliage.
