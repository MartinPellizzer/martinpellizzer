Chesneya euphratensis

Chesneya euphratensis, commonly known as the Euphrates chesneya, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East.

It is a perennial herb with slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
