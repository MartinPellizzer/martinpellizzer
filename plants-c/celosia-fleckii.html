Celosia fleckii

Celosia fleckii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spiky inflorescences that resemble flames. The plant produces showy, colorful bracts that can range from red to orange and yellow, often mistaken for flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Celosia fleckii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its resilience to drought conditions.
