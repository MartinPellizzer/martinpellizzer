Caladenia × erminea

Caladenia × erminea, commonly known as the ermine spider-orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in regions with a Mediterranean climate. It is a perennial herb with a short, erect stem and narrow leaves.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a protected species in some areas.
