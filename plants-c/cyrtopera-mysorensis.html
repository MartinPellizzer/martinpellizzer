Cyrtopera mysorensis

Cyrtopera mysorensis, commonly known as the Mysore bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the Western Ghats of India, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine pubescence.

Cyrtopera mysorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
