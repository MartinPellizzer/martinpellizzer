Crassula setulosa nothovar. schlechteri

Crassula setulosa nothovar. schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's Crassula, is a compact, succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a subtle reddish tinge under stress. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
