Croton cavalliensis

Croton cavalliensis, commonly known as the Madagascar croton, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton cavalliensis typically grows in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.
