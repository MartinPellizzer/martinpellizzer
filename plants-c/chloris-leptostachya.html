Chloris leptostachya

Chloris leptostachya, commonly known as slender spikegrass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Chloris leptostachya is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife and its use in horticulture as an ornamental plant.
