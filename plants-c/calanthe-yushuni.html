Calanthe yushuni

Calanthe yushuni, commonly known as the Yushun Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces striking, showy inflorescences with pale pink to white flowers. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and its roots are fibrous and adapted to its rocky habitat. Calanthe yushuni thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

It is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and challenging cultivation requirements.
