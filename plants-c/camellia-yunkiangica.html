Camellia yunkiangica

Camellia yunkiangica, commonly known as the Yunkiang camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its striking, large, double flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Camellia yunkiangica is also valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
