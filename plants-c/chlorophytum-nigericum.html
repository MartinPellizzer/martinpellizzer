Chlorophytum nigericum

Chlorophytum nigericum, commonly known as the Black Chlorophytum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a dark green or almost black hue. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Chlorophytum nigericum thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.
