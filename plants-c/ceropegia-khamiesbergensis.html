Ceropegia khamiesbergensis

Ceropegia khamiesbergensis, commonly known as the Khamiesberg milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. The plant produces unique, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. Its leaves are fleshy and often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss.

It is adapted to survive in low-water conditions, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
