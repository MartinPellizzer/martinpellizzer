Calamagrostis viridis

Calamagrostis viridis, commonly known as green panic grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green, with a fine texture. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with feathery bracts. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshy areas or along waterways.

Calamagrostis viridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
