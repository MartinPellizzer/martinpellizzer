Cattleya × dolteana

Cattleya × dolteana, commonly known as the Dolteana orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
