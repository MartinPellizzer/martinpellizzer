Clusia brachystyla var. guaiquinimensis

Clusia brachystyla var. guaiquinimensis, commonly known as the Guaiquinimense Clusia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae, also known as the guttiferous family. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color.

This variant is noted for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
