Cirrhopetalum mannii

Cirrhopetalum mannii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are notable for their intricate, often colorful structures and specialized pollination mechanisms.
