Clematis kassneri

Clematis kassneri, commonly known as the yellow clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis kassneri is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or walls.
