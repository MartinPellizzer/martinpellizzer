Calamus giganteus var. robustus

Calamus giganteus var. robustus, commonly known as the giant arrowroot or robust calamus, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a vigorous, rhizomatous plant that can grow up to several meters in height, characterized by its large, strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with spadices and umbels, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rhizomes are sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.
