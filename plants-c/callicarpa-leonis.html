Callicarpa leonis

Callicarpa leonis, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, violet-blue berries that mature in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

The berries are not only ornamental but also serve as a food source for birds and other wildlife.
