Campanula trachelium var. virgata

Campanula trachelium var. virgata, commonly known as the bellflower or fairy bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
