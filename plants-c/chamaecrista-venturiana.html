Chamaecrista venturiana

Chamaecrista venturiana, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, featuring opposite, trifoliate leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flowers arranged in dense, branching clusters, which bloom during the dry season. Its fruits are small, pod-like structures that contain several seeds, and the plant is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Chamaecrista venturiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
