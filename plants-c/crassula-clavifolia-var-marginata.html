Crassula clavifolia var. marginata

Crassula clavifolia var. marginata, commonly known as the Rainbow Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with striking red or pink margins. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
