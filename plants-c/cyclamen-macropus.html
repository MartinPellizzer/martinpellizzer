Cyclamen macropus

Cyclamen macropus, commonly known as the large-flowered cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The flowers are typically borne on long, slender stems and have a unique, spurred shape. Cyclamen macropus thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blossoms.
