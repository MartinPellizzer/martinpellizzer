Cytisus hillebrandtii

Cytisus hillebrandtii, commonly known as the Hillebrandt's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer months. Its leaves are small, leathery, and trifoliate, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Cytisus hillebrandtii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
