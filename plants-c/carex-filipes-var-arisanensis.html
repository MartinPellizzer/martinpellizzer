Carex filipes var. arisanensis

Carex filipes var. arisanensis, commonly known as the Arisan sedge, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like leaves and cylindrical inflorescence. Native to East Asia, particularly Japan and Taiwan, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically wind-pollinated.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in wetland gardens.
