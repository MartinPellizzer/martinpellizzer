Conyza tamborensis

Conyza tamborensis, commonly known as the tropical ragwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with slender, branched stems and hairy leaves. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Conyza tamborensis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and agricultural fields.
