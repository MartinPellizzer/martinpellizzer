Caladium splendens

Caladium splendens, commonly known as the flameleaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is prized for its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, pink, white, and green. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium splendens thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its foliage is often used in gardens and as cut flowers due to its vibrant color and unique texture.
