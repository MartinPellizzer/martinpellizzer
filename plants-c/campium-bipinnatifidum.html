Campium bipinnatifidum

Campium bipinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrowleaf campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are finely divided, giving it a delicate appearance. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Campium bipinnatifidum is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
