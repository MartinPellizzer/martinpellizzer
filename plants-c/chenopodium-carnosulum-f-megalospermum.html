Chenopodium carnosulum f. megalospermum

Chenopodium carnosulum f. megalospermum, commonly known as the large-seeded goosefoot, is a variant of the goosefoot plant characterized by its enlarged, fleshy seeds.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is native to regions of southern Africa. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to dry conditions. Its leaves are usually succulent, with a slightly prickly texture, and it produces small, greenish flowers.

This form is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential use in traditional medicine.
