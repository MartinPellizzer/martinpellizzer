Combretum geitonophyllum var. crotonoides

Combretum geitonophyllum var. crotonoides, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by distinctive orange-red fruits. It is adapted to dry climates and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
