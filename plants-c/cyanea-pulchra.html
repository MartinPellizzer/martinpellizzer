Cyanea pulchra

Cyanea pulchra, commonly known as the Hawaiian bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and somewhat woody.
