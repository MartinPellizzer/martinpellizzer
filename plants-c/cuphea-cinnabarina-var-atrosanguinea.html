Cuphea cinnabarina var. atrosanguinea

Cuphea cinnabarina var. atrosanguinea, commonly known as the dark red torch flower, is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red flowers that resemble torches, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its fiery blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a popular choice for adding color and interest to landscapes.
