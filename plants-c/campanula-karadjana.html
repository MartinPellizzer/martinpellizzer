Campanula karadjana

Campanula karadjana, commonly known as the Karadjana bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or alpine gardens.

Campanula karadjana is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
