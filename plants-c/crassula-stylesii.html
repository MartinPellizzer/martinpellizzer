Crassula stylesii

Crassula stylesii, commonly known as the Forest Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula stylesii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
