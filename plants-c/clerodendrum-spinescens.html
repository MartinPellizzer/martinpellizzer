Clerodendrum spinescens

Clerodendrum spinescens, commonly known as the spiny clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are clustered in panicles and are typically purple or pink in color.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, flat seeds.
