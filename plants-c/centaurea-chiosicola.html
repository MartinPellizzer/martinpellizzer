Centaurea chiosicola

Centaurea chiosicola, commonly known as the Chios star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and bright blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
