Crinum weinmannii

Crinum weinmannii, commonly known as the Cape Canary.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-shaped, grass-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Crinum weinmannii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
