Cryptocarya infectoria var. caudata

Cryptocarya infectoria var. caudata, commonly known as the long-tailed cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Australia, particularly found in the eastern regions of Queensland and New South Wales. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and distinctive, elongated fruiting bodies. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils within subtropical rainforests or coastal areas.

The varietal name "caudata" refers to the elongated or tail-like appearance of its fruits.
