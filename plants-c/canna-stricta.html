Canna stricta

Canna stricta, commonly known as the giant cannas, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy leaves and vibrant, spadix-like flowers that come in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows to heights of 3 to 6 feet, making it a prominent feature in ornamental gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Canna stricta is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes, often forming dense clumps over time.
