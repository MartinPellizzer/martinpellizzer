Cerastium bambuseti

Cerastium bambuseti, commonly known as the bamboo chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is native to parts of Asia and thrives in moist, shady environments.

Due to its unique morphology, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
