Cotyledon filicaulis

Cotyledon filicaulis, commonly known as the laceleaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, waxy leaves that are often lobed or scalloped, giving it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant belongs to the family Aizoaceae, also known as the ice plant family. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
