Cattleya × devoniensis var. alba

Cattleya × devoniensis var. alba, commonly known as the White Devonian Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are broad, leathery, and arranged in a fan shape, providing structural support.

This variety is highly prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and floral display.
