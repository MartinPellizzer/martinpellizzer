Carduus × schulzeanus subsp. laxus

Carduus × schulzeanus subsp.

laxus, commonly known as the slender sowthistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its erect, sparsely hairy stems and bright yellow composite flowers.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and may have a slightly rough texture. It is native to Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

This subspecies is noted for its adaptability and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
