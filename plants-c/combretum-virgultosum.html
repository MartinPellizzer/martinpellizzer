Combretum virgultosum

Combretum virgultosum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.
