Chamaecrista caribaea

Chamaecrista caribaea, commonly known as Carib bean or Caribbean ticktreed.

It is a shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of South America, characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
