Cynara cardunculus var. eucardunculus

Cynara cardunculus var. eucardunculus, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky blue or purple flower heads surrounded by dense, silver-gray bracts. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The globe thistle is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
