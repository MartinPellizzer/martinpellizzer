Centaurea × didieri

Centaurea × didieri, commonly known as the hybrid centaury, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers, which are typically tubular and arranged in dense, spherical clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
