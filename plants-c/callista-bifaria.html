Callista bifaria

Callista bifaria, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming from spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense clusters.

Callista bifaria is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.
