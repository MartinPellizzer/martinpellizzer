Conophytum obcordellum subsp. rolfii

Conophytum obcordellum subsp.

rolfii, commonly known as the Rolf's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
