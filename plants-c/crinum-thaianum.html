Crinum thaianum

Crinum thaianum, commonly known as the Thai lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in umbels at the top of sturdy, leafy stems. The plant has strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Crinum thaianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its fragrant blooms.
