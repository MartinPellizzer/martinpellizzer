Cattleya mossiae var. complanata

Cattleya mossiae var. complanata, commonly known as the flat-lipped Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its broad, flat petals and a prominent, recurved lip that sets it apart from other Cattleya varieties. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and lavender. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and ease of care.
