Carex qimenensis

Carex qimenensis, commonly known as the Qimen sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Qimen County region of Anhui Province. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale yellow to greenish flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
