Clematis nobilis

Clematis nobilis, commonly known as the Virgin's Bower, is a flowering vine native to Asia.

It produces fragrant, white to light blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and is known for its vigorous climbing habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and the presence of silky, white filaments that surround the flowers.
