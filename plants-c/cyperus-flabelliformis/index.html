Cyperus Flabelliformis

Cyperus flabelliformis is a unique wetland plant known for its striking, fan-shaped leaves. It thrives in marshy areas and shallow water, making it a common sight in tropical and subtropical regions. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to filter water. Its distinctive appearance has made it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it can also be invasive in certain environments, requiring careful management.
