Croton macbridei

Croton macbridei, commonly known as the MacBride's croton, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Croton.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

Croton macbridei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
