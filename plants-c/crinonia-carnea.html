Crinonia carnea

Crinonia carnea, commonly known as the red crinum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a strong, spicy fragrance.

Crinonia carnea is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
