Crepidium Malabarica

Crepidium malabarica is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the tropical regions of India. This delicate plant is known for its unique floral structure and striking color patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and is often found in protected natural habitats. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
