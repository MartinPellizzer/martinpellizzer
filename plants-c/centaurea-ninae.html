Centaurea ninae

Centaurea ninae, commonly known as the Nina's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea ninae is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
