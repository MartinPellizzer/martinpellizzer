Cymbidium lineare

Cymbidium lineare, commonly known as the linear orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that produces tall, straight stems with clustered flowers. The flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
