Cousinia finitima

Cousinia finitima, commonly known as the Kashmir copper grass, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold and arid environments.

Cousinia finitima is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardy nature and unique appearance.
