Calamagrostis canadensis var. lactea

Calamagrostis canadensis var. lactea, commonly known as the Canadian milletgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-white to light green foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. It produces narrow, spike-like inflorescences that are often pink to red in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils.
