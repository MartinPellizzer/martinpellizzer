Chrysanthemum arcticum var. yezoense

Chrysanthemum arcticum var. yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo chrysanthemum, is a perennial herb native to the Arctic regions of Asia.

It thrives in cold, dry environments and is typically found in tundra and alpine habitats. The plant produces small, yellow to white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in harsh climates.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental qualities.
