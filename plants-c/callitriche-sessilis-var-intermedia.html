Callitriche sessilis var. intermedia

Callitriche sessilis var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to temperate regions.

It typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has slender, branching stems with small, opposite leaves that are often submerged. Its flowers are tiny, white or pale blue, and appear in clusters near the water surface.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and fish.
