Cryptostylis acutata var. borneensis

Cryptostylis acutata var. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean cryptostylis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that are typically arranged in racemes. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, a feature common among many orchid species. Its leaves are usually narrow and strap-like, adapted to thrive in the humid, shaded environments of its native habitat.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique floral display.
