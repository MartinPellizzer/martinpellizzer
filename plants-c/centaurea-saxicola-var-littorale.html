Centaurea saxicola var. littorale

Centaurea saxicola var. littorale, commonly known as the coastal centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

This variety is adapted to withstand saline conditions and is often found in coastal dunes and cliffs.
