Crotalaria kambanguensis

Crotalaria kambanguensis, commonly known as the Kambangu tephrosia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces bean-like pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
