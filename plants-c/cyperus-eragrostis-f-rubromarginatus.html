Cyperus eragrostis f. rubromarginatus

Cyperus eragrostis f. rubromarginatus, commonly known as the red-edged flatsedge, is a variant of the common flatsedge.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance. The plant features slender, erect stems and narrow leaves with a distinctive reddish margin. It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it distinguishable from other Cyperus species, particularly in its leaf coloration and habitat preferences.
