Campanula Glomerata Var. Grandiflora

Campanula glomerata var. grandiflora is a striking member of the bellflower family known for its showy clusters of blue or violet flowers. This perennial plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow environments. Its delicate, bell-shaped blooms attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to gardens. The variety grandiflora stands out for its larger, more prominent flowers compared to the typical form. It is a hardy plant that adds both beauty and ecological value to the landscape.
