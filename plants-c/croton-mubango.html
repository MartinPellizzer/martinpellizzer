Croton mubango

Croton mubango, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually alternate and can vary in shape, sometimes being lobed or deeply divided.
