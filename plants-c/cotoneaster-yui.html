Cotoneaster yui

Cotoneaster yui, commonly known as the Japanese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red berries that are attractive to birds, aiding in seed dispersal. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Cotoneaster yui is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
