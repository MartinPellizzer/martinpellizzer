Cosmos hybridus

Cosmos hybridus, commonly known as the Chinese cosmos, is an annual flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features feathery, bipinnate leaves. Its flowers bloom from summer to fall, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cosmos hybridus thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
