Chlorophytum ramosissimum

Chlorophytum ramosissimum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically dark green with a lighter green or yellowish stripe running down the center. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is not only aesthetically pleasing but also contributes to air purification by absorbing toxins.

Chlorophytum ramosissimum is a popular ornamental plant due to its resilience and ease of care.
