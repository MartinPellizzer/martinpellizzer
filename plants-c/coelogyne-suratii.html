Coelogyne suratii

Coelogyne suratii, commonly known as the Surat orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the production of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, leathery leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a contrasting purple or red lip.

Coelogyne suratii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
