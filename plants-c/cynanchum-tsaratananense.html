Cynanchum tsaratananense

Cynanchum tsaratananense, commonly known as the Madagascar milkweed, is a herbaceous plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces distinctive flowers with a spurred structure, typical of the milkweed family. It is adapted to the humid and often shaded environments of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
