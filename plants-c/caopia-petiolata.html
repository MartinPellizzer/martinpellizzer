Caopia petiolata

Caopia petiolata, commonly known as the slender leaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species with fleshy, often hairy stems and leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually greenish or yellowish in color. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is adapted to store water in its stems.

Caopia petiolata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
