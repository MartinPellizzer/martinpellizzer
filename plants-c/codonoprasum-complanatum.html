Codonoprasum complanatum

Codonoprasum complanatum, commonly known as the flatleaf codonopsis, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of elliptical, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
