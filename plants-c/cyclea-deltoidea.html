Cyclea deltoidea

Cyclea deltoidea, commonly known as the delta cycad, is a species of cycad native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched stem and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soils.

Due to its slow growth and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
