Convolvulus cancerianus

Convolvulus cancerianus, commonly known as the Cancerian bindweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in clusters. It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its rapid growth and invasive potential make it a notable species in ecological studies.
