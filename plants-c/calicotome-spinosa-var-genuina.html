Calicotome spinosa var. genuina

Calicotome spinosa var. genuina, commonly known as the African broom.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its spiny stems and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in arid regions.
