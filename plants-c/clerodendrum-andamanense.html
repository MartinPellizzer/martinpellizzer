Clerodendrum andamanense

Clerodendrum andamanense, commonly known as the Andaman flame bush, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas of its native habitat.
