Cleisostoma linearilobatum

Cleisostoma linearilobatum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed cleisostoma, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, stocky rhizome and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with white petals and a yellow lip. The leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
