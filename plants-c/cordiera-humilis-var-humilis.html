Cordiera humilis var. humilis

Cordiera humilis var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf cordiera, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dense, leathery foliage with elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
