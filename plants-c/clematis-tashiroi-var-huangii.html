Clematis tashiroi var. huangii

Clematis tashiroi var. huangii, commonly known as the Huangii Clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle yellow center. The plant produces compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its beauty and hardiness.
