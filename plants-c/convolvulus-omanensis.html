Convolvulus omanensis

Convolvulus omanensis, commonly known as the Oman morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in the early morning. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Convolvulus omanensis is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low water availability.
