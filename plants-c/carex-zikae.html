Carex zikae

Carex zikae, commonly known as Zikae sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and rushes. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical structure makes it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
