Carex roraimensis

Carex roraimensis, commonly known as Roraima sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly found in the rainforests of Venezuela, Brazil, and Guyana. This plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.

Carex roraimensis plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its native ecosystems.
