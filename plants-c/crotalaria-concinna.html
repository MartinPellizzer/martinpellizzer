Crotalaria concinna

Crotalaria concinna, commonly known as the slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive seed pods that are typically cylindrical and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Crotalaria concinna is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in certain agricultural contexts.
