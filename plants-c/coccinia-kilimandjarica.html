Coccinia kilimandjarica

Coccinia kilimandjarica, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro cucumber, is a tropical vine native to East Africa.

It produces small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and bears yellow flowers that attract pollinators. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for climbing structures in gardens.

The fruit is rich in nutrients and has a mild, sweet flavor.
