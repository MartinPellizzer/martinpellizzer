Calamus tonkinensis var. brevispicatus

Calamus tonkinensis var. brevispicatus, commonly known as the short-spurred calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Vietnam and Cambodia. This plant is characterized by its slender, creeping rhizomes and narrow, elongated leaves. The inflorescence consists of a single, elongated spike bearing small, clustered flowers.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.
