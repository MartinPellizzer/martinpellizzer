Cyclophorus Beddomeanus

Cyclophorus beddomeanus is a rare and unique plant species native to the western ghats of india. it belongs to the family apocynaceae and is known for its distinctive leaf morphology and medicinal properties. this plant is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests and is considered an important part of local ecosystems. due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it is currently listed as endangered. its study is crucial for both conservation efforts and the development of new pharmaceuticals.
