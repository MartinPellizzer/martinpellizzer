Combretum grandifolium

Combretum grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and the plant is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
