Calamagrostis × haussknechtiana

Calamagrostis × haussknechtiana, commonly known as the purple moor grass, is a hybrid grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and striking purple to pink plumes that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a silver-gray hue that adds ornamental value to gardens.

It is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to tolerate a variety of environmental conditions.
