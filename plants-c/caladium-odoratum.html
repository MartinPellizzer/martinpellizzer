Caladium odoratum

Caladium odoratum, commonly known as the sweet-scented arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that come in a variety of colors, including shades of green, red, and white. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. Caladium odoratum thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and pleasant scent.
