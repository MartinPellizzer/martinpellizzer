Commelina africana var. villosior

Commelina africana var. villosior, commonly known as the African spiderwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, often hairy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers with three petals, which are borne on slender stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
