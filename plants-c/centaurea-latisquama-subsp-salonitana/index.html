Centaurea Latisquama Subsp. Salonitana

Centaurea salonitana, scientifically known as Centaurea latisquama subsp. salonitana, is a rare and endemic plant species native to southern Europe. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found in limestone areas and scrublands. This subspecies is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant conservation challenges. Protecting Centaurea salonitana is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
