Cardamine × tatrensis

Cardamine × tatrensis, commonly known as the alpine buttercup.

It is a hybrid plant species that occurs in the alpine regions of Central Europe. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, yellow flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
