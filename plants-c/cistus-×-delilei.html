Cistus × delilei

Cistus × delilei, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes various species of evergreen shrubs characterized by their aromatic foliage and showy flowers. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal gardens.

Its resilience to drought and salt spray makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal landscapes.
