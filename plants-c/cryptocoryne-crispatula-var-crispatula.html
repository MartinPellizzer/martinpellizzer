Cryptocoryne crispatula var. crispatula

Cryptocoryne crispatula var. crispatula, commonly known as the curly cryptocoryne, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and its distinctive, curling leaves that give it its common name. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is well-suited for planted tanks due to its hardy nature. Its rhizome system allows it to spread and form thick carpets on the substrate.

It is also known for its ability to improve water quality by absorbing nitrates and other nutrients.
