Cucubalus chloranthus

Cucubalus chloranthus, commonly known as the green-flowered cucumber, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional cuisines. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine pubescence.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
