Ceropegia barica

Ceropegia barica, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, often with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia barica is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
