Calathea dubia

Calathea dubia, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with patterns that change color depending on light conditions. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to maintain its vibrant colors.

Due to its unique appearance and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
