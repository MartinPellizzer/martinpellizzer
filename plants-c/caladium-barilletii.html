Caladium barilletii

Caladium barilletii, commonly known as the pink caladium, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of pink, red, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium barilletii thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, especially in shaded garden beds or as a potted plant.
