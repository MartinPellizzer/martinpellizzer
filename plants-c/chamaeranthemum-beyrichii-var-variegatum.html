Chamaeranthemum beyrichii var. variegatum

Chamaeranthemum beyrichii var. variegatum, commonly known as the Variegated Cape Violet, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, rounded foliage with striking variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and cream. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and pink during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and long-lasting blooms, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
