Coeloglosshabenaria jacksonii

Coeloglosshabenaria jacksonii, commonly known as the Jackson's hooded orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid forest environments, often on tree trunks or branches. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with reddish markings, adding to its ornamental value.
