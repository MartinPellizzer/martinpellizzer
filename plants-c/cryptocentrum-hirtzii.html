Cryptocentrum hirtzii

Cryptocentrum hirtzii, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant has a creeping growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight.

Cryptocentrum hirtzii is often used in ornamental gardening for its textural appeal.
