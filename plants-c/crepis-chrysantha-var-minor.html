Crepis chrysantha var. minor

Crepis chrysantha var. minor, commonly known as the lesser goldenweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has slender, hairy stems and small, alternate leaves.
