Callisia nizandensis

Callisia nizandensis, commonly known as the Nizan's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
