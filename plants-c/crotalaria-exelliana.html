Crotalaria exelliana

Crotalaria exelliana, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit is erect, with hairy stems and can thrive in a variety of soil types, making it adaptable to different environments.
