Cheiridopsis robusta

Cheiridopsis robusta, commonly known as the robust ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments.
