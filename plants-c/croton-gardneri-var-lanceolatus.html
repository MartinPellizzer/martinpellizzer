Croton gardneri var. lanceolatus

Croton gardneri var. lanceolatus, commonly known as the lanceleaf croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are often surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
