Cryptanthus diamantinensis

Cryptanthus diamantinensis, commonly known as the diamond mountain carnation, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in its native arid environment.

Cryptanthus diamantinensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
