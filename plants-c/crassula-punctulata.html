Crassula punctulata

Crassula punctulata, commonly known as the Snowball Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
