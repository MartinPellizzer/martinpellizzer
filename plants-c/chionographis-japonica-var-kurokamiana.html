Chionographis japonica var. kurokamiana

Chionographis japonica var. kurokamiana, commonly known as the Japanese snowflake plant, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and elegant plant.
