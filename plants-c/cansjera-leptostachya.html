Cansjera leptostachya

Cansjera leptostachya, commonly known as the slender-stemmed grass, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with delicate, pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
