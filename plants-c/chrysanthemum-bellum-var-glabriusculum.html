Chrysanthemum bellum var. glabriusculum

Chrysanthemum bellum var. glabriusculum, commonly known as the woolly chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, yellow to orange flowers and hairy, grayish-green foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is well adapted to arid environments. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and is often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
