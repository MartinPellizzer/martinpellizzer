Combretum wittei

Combretum wittei, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that turn red or orange before falling in autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or brownish hue, adding to its visual appeal.
