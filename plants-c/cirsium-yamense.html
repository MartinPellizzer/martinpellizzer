Cirsium yamense

Cirsium yamense, commonly known as the Yamense Thistle, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia, particularly China and Korea.

It is characterized by its spiny, serrated leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine. Cirsium yamense thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in forest edges and rocky habitats.

Its hardy nature makes it a resilient species in its native environment.
