Callista tetraedris

Callista tetraedris, commonly known as the four-sided callista, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Thailand and Malaysia. Callista tetraedris is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
