Cattleya × Irrorata

Cattleya × irrorata is a hybrid orchid known for its striking beauty and vibrant colors. This plant belongs to the Cattleya genus and is prized for its large, showy flowers that often feature intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and collectors. With proper care, Cattleya × irrorata can bloom repeatedly, adding a touch of elegance to any indoor or outdoor garden.
