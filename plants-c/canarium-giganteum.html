Canarium giganteum

Canarium giganteum, commonly known as the giant candlenut tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody capsules containing oily seeds. Its bark is thick and fissured, often exuding a resinous sap.

The plant is valued for its timber, oil, and traditional medicinal uses.
