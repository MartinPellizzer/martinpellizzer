Cliffortia linearifolia var. nitidula

Cliffortia linearifolia var. nitidula, commonly known as the smoothleaf cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, smooth waxy coating. The plant produces small, delicate yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to coastal or mountainous environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often found in specific regions of the Western Cape province.
