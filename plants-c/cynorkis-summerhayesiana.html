Cynorkis summerhayesiana

Cynorkis summerhayesiana, commonly known as the Summerhayes' orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Cynorkis summerhayesiana is often found in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats.
