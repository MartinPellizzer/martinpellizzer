Cyrtorchis arcuata subsp. leonensis

Cyrtorchis arcuata subsp.

leonensis, commonly known as the Leonensis Orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions of South America. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure, featuring intricate, spurred petals and a distinct lip.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests or montane regions. Its flowers are typically pale green or yellowish, with a subtle fragrance that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
