Codiaeum warrenii

Codiaeum warrenii, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that can display shades of red, orange, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by orange or red capsule fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Codiaeum warrenii is often cultivated for its striking foliage and is used in ornamental gardening.
