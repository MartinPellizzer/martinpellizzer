Cuspidaria biebersteinii var. stenocarpa

Cuspidaria biebersteinii var. stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited cuspidaria, is a rare and endemic plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves and distinctive, narrow, berry-like fruits. The plant typically thrives in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a pale greenish-white color.
