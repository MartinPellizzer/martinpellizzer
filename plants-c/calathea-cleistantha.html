Calathea cleistantha

Calathea cleistantha, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns and shades of green, often featuring contrasting veins. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.
