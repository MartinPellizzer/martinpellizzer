Crypteronia wallichii

Crypteronia wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a shrub that typically grows in temperate and subtropical regions, characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped flowers. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant white or pale yellow blooms during the spring and summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to well-drained soils.

Crypteronia wallichii is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
