Cleistocalyx nigrans

Cleistocalyx nigrans, commonly known as the black-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which are typically tubular and lack showy petals. The plant produces small, oval fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in subtropical gardens.
