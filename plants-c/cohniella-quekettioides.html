Cohniella quekettioides

Cohniella quekettioides, commonly known as the common sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is a small, herbaceous annual with a compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in dry environments.

Cohniella quekettioides is often found in sandy soils and is characteristic of coastal and inland desert ecosystems.
