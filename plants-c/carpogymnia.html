Carpogymnia

Carpogymnia, commonly known as the "false lichen," is a genus of lichens belonging to the family Pilocarpaceae.

It is characterized by its crustose or foliose thallus, which is often bright yellow or orange in color. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship between a fungus and a green alga or cyanobacterium. Carpogymnia species are typically found on rock surfaces in arid or semi-arid environments.
