Conophytum lucipunctum

Conophytum lucipunctum, commonly known as the star-shaped rock rose, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, basal rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Conophytum lucipunctum is prized for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in rocky, dry habitats.
