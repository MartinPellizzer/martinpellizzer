Cavendishia sandemanii

Cavendishia sandemanii, commonly known as the Sandeman's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and can display a range of hues, including pink, red, and white.

Cavendishia sandemanii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
