Celosia fadenorum

Celosia fadenorum, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spiky bracts that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its foliage is narrow and lance-shaped, with a bluish-green hue.

Celosia fadenorum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
