Cyclophyllum francii

Cyclophyllum francii, commonly known as the French cyclophyllum, is a unique species within the Cyclophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiral-shaped leaves that are often described as resembling a helix. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a dry, dehiscent capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.

Cyclophyllum francii is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
