Cenostigma diversifolium

Cenostigma diversifolium, commonly known as the variable-leaf cenostigma, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes, which vary in size and texture depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are often arranged alternately along the stems, and the plant has a woody structure.

Cenostigma diversifolium is adapted to grow in dry, rocky habitats and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
