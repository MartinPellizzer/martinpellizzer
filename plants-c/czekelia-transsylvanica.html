Czekelia transsylvanica

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and thrives in woodland or meadow habitats. It has lanceolate leaves with a rough texture and produces small, capsule-like fruits.
