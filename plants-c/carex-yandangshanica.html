Carex yandangshanica

Carex yandangshanica, commonly known as Yandangshan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yandangshan region of Zhejiang Province. This perennial herbaceous plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are characteristic of the sedge family.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in maintaining soil stability in its native habitat.
