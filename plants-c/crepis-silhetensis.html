Crepis silhetensis

Crepis silhetensis, commonly known as the Silhet hairgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the wetlands and marshy areas of Bangladesh, particularly in the Silhet region. The plant is characterized by its slender, hair-like leaves and small, yellowish-green flower heads. It typically grows in shallow water or damp soil, thriving in humid climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
