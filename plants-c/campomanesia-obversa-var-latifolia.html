Campomanesia obversa var. latifolia

Campomanesia obversa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved satinash, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern Brazil.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are broad and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by edible, orange-red berries. These berries are rich in vitamin C and are often used in local cuisine and traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.
