Cola stelechantha

Cola stelechantha, commonly known as the African cola tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain elliptical, glossy seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Cola stelechantha is notable for its role in traditional medicine and as a source of natural caffeine.
