Cryptandra grandiflora

Cryptandra grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered cryptandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
