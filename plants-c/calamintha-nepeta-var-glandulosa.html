Calamintha nepeta var. glandulosa

Calamintha nepeta var. glandulosa, commonly known as caraway lemon or lemon balm, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, slightly hairy leaves with a distinctive lemony scent, and its small white or pale blue flowers bloom in summer. The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, including calming effects and digestive benefits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Calamintha nepeta var. glandulosa is often used in herbal teas and traditional remedies.
