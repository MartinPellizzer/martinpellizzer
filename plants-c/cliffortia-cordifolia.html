Cliffortia cordifolia

Cliffortia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
