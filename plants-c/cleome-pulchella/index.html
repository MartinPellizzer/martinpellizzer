Cleome Pulchella

Cleome pulchella is a flowering plant known for its striking appearance and easy care requirements. Native to the southeastern United States, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. This annual plant produces vibrant yellow blooms that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its unique, branching structure makes it a popular choice for gardeners looking to add visual interest. With its low maintenance needs, Cleome pulchella is a great addition to both novice and experienced gardens.
