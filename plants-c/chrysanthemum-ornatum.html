Chrysanthemum ornatum

Chrysanthemum ornatum, commonly known as the garden chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 100 centimeters and has finely divided, feathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and valuable in traditional medicine.
