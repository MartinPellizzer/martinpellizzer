Cryptocoryne cruddasiana

Cryptocoryne cruddasiana, commonly known as the Orange Cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange to yellowish-green leaves, which are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in shaded areas with soft substrates. Cryptocoryne cruddasiana is popular in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It plays a role in maintaining water quality by absorbing nutrients and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
