Cordia cinerascens

Cordia cinerascens, commonly known as the smoke tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the plant a smoky appearance. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and tropical gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
