Clerodendrum microphyllum

Clerodendrum microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. The leaves are small, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
