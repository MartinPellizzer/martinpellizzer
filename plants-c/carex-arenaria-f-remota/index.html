Carex Arenaria F. Remota

Carex arenaria f. remota is a rare and distinctive variant of the common sedge plant. It thrives in sandy, coastal environments where few other plants can survive. This fern-like grass has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in the summer. Its unique habitat and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Studying this plant offers insights into adaptation and ecological resilience in harsh environments.
