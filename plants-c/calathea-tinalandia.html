Calathea tinalandia

Calathea tinalandia, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, patterned leaves that change color in response to light conditions. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture in its soil. Its foliage often displays vibrant shades of green, pink, and white, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Calathea tinalandia is known for its ability to purify the air and is often used in indoor gardening.
