Camellia fraterna f. amoena

Camellia fraterna f. amoena, commonly known as the pink camellia, is a variant of the camellia plant characterized by its striking pink flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub native to southern China and is valued for its ornamental qualities. The plant features broad, glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy, camellia-like blooms in the spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Camellia fraterna f. amoena is also appreciated for its ability to withstand mild frosts, enhancing its adaptability in various climates.
