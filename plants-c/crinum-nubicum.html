Crinum nubicum

Crinum nubicum, commonly known as the African white lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces showy, white or pale pink flowers with a strong scent. The plant is characterized by its large, clustered inflorescence and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas. Crinum nubicum is adapted to dry conditions and is drought-tolerant once established.

It is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
