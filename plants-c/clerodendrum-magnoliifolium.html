Clerodendrum magnoliifolium

Clerodendrum magnoliifolium, commonly known as the magnolia-leaf clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that resemble those of magnolia species, hence its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and showy inflorescences.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
