Combretum mucronatum

Combretum mucronatum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red flowers that appear in dense clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various tropical and subtropical environments.

The species is also noted for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in ornamental horticulture.
