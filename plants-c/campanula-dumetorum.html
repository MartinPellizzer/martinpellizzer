Campanula dumetorum

Campanula dumetorum, commonly known as mouse ear hawkweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine white coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as meadows and woodlands.

Campanula dumetorum is also known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some areas.
