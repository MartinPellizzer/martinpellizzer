Canaca vieillardii

Canaca vieillardii, commonly known as the Vieillard's canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its habitat. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
