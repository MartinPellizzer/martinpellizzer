Cotoneaster conspicuus var. decorus

Cotoneaster conspicuus var. decorus, commonly known as the showy cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of pinkish-white flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

Cotoneaster conspicuus var. decorus prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
