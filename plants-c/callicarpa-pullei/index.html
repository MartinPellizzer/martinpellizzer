Callicarpa Pullei

Callicarpa pullei is a strikingly beautiful shrub known for its vibrant red berries that attract a variety of wildlife. Native to eastern Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage offers a pleasant shade of green throughout the growing season, making it a valuable addition to gardens and natural landscapes. The plant's ornamental value has led to its cultivation in many regions beyond its native range. With its unique appearance and ecological benefits, Callicarpa pullei is a noteworthy species in the world of horticulture and botany.
