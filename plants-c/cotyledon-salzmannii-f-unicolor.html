Cotyledon salzmannii f. unicolor

Cotyledon salzmannii f. unicolor, commonly known as the White Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a white or pale variegation pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
