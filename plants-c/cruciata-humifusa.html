Cruciata humifusa

Cruciata humifusa, commonly known as the groundsel or common groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

Cruciata humifusa is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in many regions.
