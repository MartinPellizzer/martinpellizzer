Cyphostemma bambuseti var. glandulosissimum

Cyphostemma bambuseti var. glandulosissimum, commonly known as the glandular cyphostemma, is a climbing shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often glandular-dotted. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are capsules that split open to release seeds.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
