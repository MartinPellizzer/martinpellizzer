Campynemanthe parva

Campynemanthe parva, commonly known as the miniature arrowroot, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are followed by bright orange berries. Campynemanthe parva is typically grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

It prefers warm, humid environments and is relatively easy to care for.
