Chrysocoma ovata

Chrysocoma ovata, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has small, ovate, dark green leaves that are opposite on the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky areas.

Chrysocoma ovata is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
