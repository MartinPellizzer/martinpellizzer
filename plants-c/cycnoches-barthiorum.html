Cycnoches barthiorum

Cycnoches barthiorum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
