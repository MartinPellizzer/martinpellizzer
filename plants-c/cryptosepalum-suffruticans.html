Cryptosepalum suffruticans

Cryptosepalum suffruticans, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant has a compact growth habit with a short stem and a single, erect flower spike. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with three sepals and three petals, and are often clustered in a spike.

The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
