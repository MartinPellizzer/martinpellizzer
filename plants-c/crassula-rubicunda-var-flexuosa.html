Crassula rubicunda var. flexuosa

Crassula rubicunda var. flexuosa, commonly known as the Crimson Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that turn a striking red or pink when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
