Camphora chinensis

Camphora chinensis, commonly known as the camphor laurel, is a large evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, aromatic wood that contains camphor. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has a broad, spreading crown. Its flowers are small and white, appearing in clusters, and it produces woody capsules that contain pale yellow seeds.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used in traditional medicine and as a source of camphor.
