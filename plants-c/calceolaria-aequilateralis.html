Calceolaria aequilateralis

Calceolaria aequilateralis, commonly known as the "Slipper Orchid," is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers that are often brightly colored.
