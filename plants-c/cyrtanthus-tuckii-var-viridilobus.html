Cyrtanthus tuckii var. viridilobus

Cyrtanthus tuckii var. viridilobus, commonly known as the green-lobed glory lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a greenish tinge. The plant has strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically green and slightly textured. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
