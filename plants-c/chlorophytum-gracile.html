Chlorophytum gracile

Chlorophytum gracile, commonly known as the slender spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that grow in rosettes, often with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the spring and summer months. It is a hardy species that thrives in well-drained soil and moderate light conditions.

Chlorophytum gracile is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and air-purifying qualities.
