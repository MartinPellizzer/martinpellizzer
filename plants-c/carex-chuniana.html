Carex chuniana

Carex chuniana, commonly known as the Chinese sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, grasses, and rushes. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Carex chuniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
