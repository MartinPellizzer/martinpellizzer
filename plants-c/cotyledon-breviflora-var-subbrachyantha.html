Cotyledon breviflora var. subbrachyantha

Cotyledon breviflora var. subbrachyantha, commonly known as the broad-leafed cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and rounded, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
