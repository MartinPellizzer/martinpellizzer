Callianthemum tibeticum

Callianthemum tibeticum, commonly known as the Tibetan crownflower, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. Callianthemum tibeticum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in alpine gardens.
