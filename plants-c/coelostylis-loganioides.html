Coelostylis loganioides

Coelostylis loganioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf coelostylis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Coelostylis loganioides is often found in association with other epiphytic and terrestrial orchids in its native habitat.
