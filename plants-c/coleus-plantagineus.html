Coleus plantagineus

Coleus plantagineus, commonly known as the scarlet sage, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is known for its aromatic foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor settings.
