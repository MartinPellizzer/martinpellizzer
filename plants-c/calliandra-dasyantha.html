Calliandra dasyantha

Calliandra dasyantha, commonly known as the showy calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their vibrant appearance and long blooming period.

Calliandra dasyantha is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
