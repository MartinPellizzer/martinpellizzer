Cressa cretica var. australis

Cressa cretica var. australis, commonly known as the Australian cress, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers and pinnately compound leaves. It is often found in sandy soils and can thrive in saline environments.

This variety is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions.
