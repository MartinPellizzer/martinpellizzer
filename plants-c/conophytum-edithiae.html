Conophytum edithiae

Conophytum edithiae, commonly known as the Edith's conophytum, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, rounded leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
