Cypripedium × cythera

Cypripedium × cythera, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid species resulting from the cross between Cypripedium calceolus and Cypripedium micranthum.

It is characterized by its large, showy, slipper-shaped pouch that typically ranges in color from pink to white, with a contrasting veined pattern. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is known for its unique and elegant floral structure. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas with acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
