Coelogyne trinervis

Coelogyne trinervis, commonly known as the three-veined orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, and long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and have a prominent midrib. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne trinervis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
