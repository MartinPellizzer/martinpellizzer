Cotoneaster × watereri

Cotoneaster × watereri, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright red berries in the fall. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that provide attractive foliage throughout the growing season. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster × watereri is also valued for its resistance to pests and diseases, making it a low-maintenance choice for gardens.
