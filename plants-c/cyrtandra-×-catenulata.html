Cyrtandra × catenulata

Cyrtandra × catenulata, commonly known as the hybrid blue ginger, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra sericea and Cyrtandra scandens. This species is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers and velvety foliage. It is native to Hawaii and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
