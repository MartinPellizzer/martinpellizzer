Chrysitrix capensis var. capensis

Chrysitrix capensis var. capensis, commonly known as the Cape chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
