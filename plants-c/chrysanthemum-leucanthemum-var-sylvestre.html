Chrysanthemum leucanthemum var. sylvestre

Chrysanthemum leucanthemum var. sylvestre, commonly known as the woodland chrysanthemum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in woodland habitats, preferring moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers with yellow centers. It blooms from late summer into early autumn, adding delicate floral interest to shaded gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in naturalized settings.
