Cotoneaster teijiashanensis

Cotoneaster teijiashanensis, commonly known as the Teijiashan cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, ornamental berries and dense, glossy foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces white flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall.

This species is valued for its hardiness and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
