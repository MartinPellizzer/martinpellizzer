Commelina bangii

Commelina bangii, commonly known as the blue spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features striking blue-violet flowers that are borne on slender, erect stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and is often found in moist, shady environments. It is a member of the Commelinaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants.

Commelina bangii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
