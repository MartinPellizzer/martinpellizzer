Columnea matudae

Columnea matudae, commonly known as the Matudae flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Columnea matudae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
