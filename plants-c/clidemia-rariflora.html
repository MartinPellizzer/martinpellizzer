Clidemia rariflora

Clidemia rariflora, commonly known as the rare-flowered mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Due to habitat loss, Clidemia rariflora is considered a vulnerable species in many areas.
