Christolea scaposa

Christolea scaposa, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are bell-shaped, typically blue or violet, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Christolea scaposa is often found in rocky soils and open habitats, where it thrives in full sun.
