Ceropegia parvipuncta subsp. truncata

Ceropegia parvipuncta subsp.

truncata, commonly known as the dwarf dot ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features small, fleshy leaves with a distinctive pattern of tiny, white dots.

The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
