Caucaea phalaenopsis

Caucaea phalaenopsis, commonly known as the Caucaea orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, long-lasting flowers that resemble those of the Phalaenopsis orchid. The plant typically produces clusters of white or pale pink blooms, which are often adorned with a subtle pattern resembling wings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
