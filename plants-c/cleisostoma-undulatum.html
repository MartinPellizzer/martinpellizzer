Cleisostoma undulatum

Cleisostoma undulatum, commonly known as the undulating waxy orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and rain.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to watering and light conditions.
