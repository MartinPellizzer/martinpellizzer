Croton decarianus

Croton decarianus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that range from bright red to deep orange and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf colors.

Croton decarianus is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
