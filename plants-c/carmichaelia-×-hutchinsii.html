Carmichaelia × hutchinsii

Carmichaelia × hutchinsii, commonly known as the coastal kowhai, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salt-laden winds. It has compound leaves and produces pods that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Carmichaelia × hutchinsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in coastal ecosystems.
