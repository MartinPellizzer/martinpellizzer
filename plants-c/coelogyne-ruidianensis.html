Coelogyne ruidianensis

Coelogyne ruidianensis, commonly known as the Ruidianensis orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces a single, elongated spike bearing several blooms, which are often fragrant. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
