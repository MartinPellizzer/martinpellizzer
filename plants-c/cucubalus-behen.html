Cucubalus behen

Cucubalus behen, commonly known as behenial or Indian butternut, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cucurbitaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a rough, spiny capsule containing oily seeds.
