Cyathea kinabaluensis

Cyathea kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Kinabalu tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, supporting its vertical growth.

Cyathea kinabaluensis plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
