Caucalis capensis

Caucalis capensis, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
