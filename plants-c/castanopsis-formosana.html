Castanopsis formosana

Castanopsis formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan chestnut.

It is a deciduous evergreen tree native to Taiwan, belonging to the Fagaceae family. The plant features dense, glossy green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. Its fruit is a spiny cupule that encloses one or two seeds, which are typically dispersed by animals.
