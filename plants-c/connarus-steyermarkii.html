Connarus steyermarkii

Connarus steyermarkii, commonly known as the Brazilian connarus, is a member of the Connaraceae family.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody capsules as fruits.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage.
