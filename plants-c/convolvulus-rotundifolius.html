Convolvulus rotundifolius

Convolvulus rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or pink. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. It is often found growing along roadsides and in disturbed areas due to its adaptability.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its spread.
