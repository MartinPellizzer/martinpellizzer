Ceropegia geminata

Ceropegia geminata, commonly known as the twinleaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
