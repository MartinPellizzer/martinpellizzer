Codiaeum chrysosticton var. latifolium

Codiaeum chrysosticton var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy texture and prominent venation. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.
