Chlorophytum tuberosum

Chlorophytum tuberosum, commonly known as African potato, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary part used for culinary and medicinal purposes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a silver underside. It produces small, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months.

This plant is often grown for its ornamental value as well as its edible tubers.
