Cymbidium nagifolium

Cymbidium nagifolium, commonly known as the Japanese sword fern orchid, is a species of orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that resemble the shape of a sword, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with a red or purple lip. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cymbidium nagifolium is a popular choice for gardeners due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
