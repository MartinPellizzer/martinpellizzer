Chloerum

Chloerum, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, panicle-like inflorescences that produce clusters of golden-yellow flowers in the spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species in tropical and subtropical regions.

Chloerum is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in urban landscapes.
