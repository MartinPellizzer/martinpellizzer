Cymbidium eburneum var. longzhouense

Cymbidium eburneum var. longzhouense, commonly known as the Longzhou orchid, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
