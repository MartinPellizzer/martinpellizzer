Convolvulus pulcherrimus

Convolvulus pulcherrimus, commonly known as the beautiful morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves and has a climbing habit, often using its tendrils to ascend supports. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
