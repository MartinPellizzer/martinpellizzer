Calea solidaginea subsp. solidaginea

Calea solidaginea subsp.

solidaginea, commonly known as the sacred candle plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico. It is characterized by its unique, candle-like inflorescence composed of numerous white, tubular flowers.

The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its striking appearance and medicinal properties. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal remedies due to its purported health benefits.
