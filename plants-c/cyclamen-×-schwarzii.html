Cyclamen × schwarzii

Cyclamen × schwarzii, commonly known as the hybrid cyclamen, is a decorative plant characterized by its striking, heart-shaped foliage and showy, tubular flowers.

It is a hybrid between Cyclamen persicum and Cyclamen coum, resulting in a plant that combines the best traits of both parents. The flowers typically bloom in shades of pink, red, or white, often with a distinctive dark blotch near the base. The plant prefers cool, bright environments and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for containers or as a bedding plant in shaded areas.
