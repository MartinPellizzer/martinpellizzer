Citrus × limonum var. striata

Citrus × limonum var. striata, commonly known as the variegated lemon.

This variant is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its distinctive variegated leaves with white and green patterns. It is a cultivar of the lemon species, known for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruits. The leaves are typically broader and more textured compared to standard lemons.

It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
