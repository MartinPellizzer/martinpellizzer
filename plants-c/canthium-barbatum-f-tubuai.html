Canthium barbatum f. tubuai

Canthium barbatum f. tubuai, commonly known as the tubuai variant of the African hogweed, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins and produces small, clustered white flowers. The plant is characterized by its robust growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphological traits and potential ecological roles in its native habitats.
