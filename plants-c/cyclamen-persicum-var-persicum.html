Cyclamen persicum var. persicum

Cyclamen persicum var. persicum, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. The plant is typically grown as a winter annual, blooming in late autumn to early spring. It prefers cool, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking foliage and delicate blooms, which add a touch of elegance to any setting.
