Cassine burkeana

Cassine burkeana, commonly known as the Burke's cassia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a smooth texture.

Cassine burkeana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
