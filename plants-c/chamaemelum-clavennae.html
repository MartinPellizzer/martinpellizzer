Chamaemelum clavennae

Chamaemelum clavennae, commonly known as the narrowleaf tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense, spherical heads. The plant has linear, aromatic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in dry, sunny environments.

It is often found in rocky soils and is used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
