Calyptracordia Alba

Calyptracordia alba is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the high altitudes of southern South America. Known for its striking white flowers and unique leaf structure, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species is often found in cloud forests where it clings to rocky outcrops. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of both scientific interest and botanical fascination.
