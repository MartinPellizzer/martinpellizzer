Cnestis zenkeri

Cnestis zenkeri, commonly known as the African starwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small shrub or tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Cnestis zenkeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
