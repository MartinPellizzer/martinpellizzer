Centaurea deustiformis

Centaurea deustiformis, commonly known as the narrowleaf star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets that give it a star-like appearance.

Centaurea deustiformis is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
