Cryptostylis sigmoidea

Cryptostylis sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in open forests and heathlands.

Cryptostylis sigmoidea is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
