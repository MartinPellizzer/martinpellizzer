Calliandra trinervia var. pilosifolia

Calliandra trinervia var. pilosifolia, commonly known as silky calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and clusters of small, fluffy, pink or red flowers. The leaves are bipinnate, with numerous fine, hairy leaflets that give the plant a soft, velvety appearance. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms.

The plant is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
