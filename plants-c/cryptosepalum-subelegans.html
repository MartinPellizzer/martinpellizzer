Cryptosepalum subelegans

Cryptosepalum subelegans, commonly known as the elegant cryptosepalum, is a rare and intriguing member of the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the montane forests of Southeast Asia, particularly in regions of India and Nepal. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a threatened species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
