Caucalis hispida

Caucalis hispida, commonly known as the rough hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Caucalis hispida is often considered a weed but can serve as a food source for certain insects.
