Cattleya jenmanii

Cattleya jenmanii, commonly known as the Jenman's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and dark green, often covered in a waxy coating.

Cattleya jenmanii is prized in horticulture for its beauty and fragrance, making it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
