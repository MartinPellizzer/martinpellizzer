Calathea veitchiana

Calathea veitchiana, commonly known as the veitchian calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with intricate patterns of green, white, and pink. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, often featuring a glossy texture that enhances their visual appeal. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect light.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
