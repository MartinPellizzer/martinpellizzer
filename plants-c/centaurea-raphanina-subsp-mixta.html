Centaurea raphanina subsp. mixta

Centaurea raphanina subsp.

mixta, commonly known as the mixed radish centaurea, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies.
