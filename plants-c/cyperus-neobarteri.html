Cyperus neobarteri

Cyperus neobarteri, commonly known as the narrowleaf sedges, is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa, including countries like Kenya and Tanzania, where it typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually green or brown in color. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
