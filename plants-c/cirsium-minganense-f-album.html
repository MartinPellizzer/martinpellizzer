Cirsium minganense f. album

Cirsium minganense f. album, commonly known as the white-flowered swamp thistle, is a rare botanical variant native to specific regions of Canada.

This plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive white or pale purple flower heads. Its leaves are typically spiny and lobed, adapted to thrive in wetland environments. The plant's floral structure includes both ray and disk flowers, typical of the genus Cirsium.
