Cyrtochilum robustum

Cyrtochilum robustum, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short, horizontal rhizome and a cluster of fleshy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and borne in racemes, often in shades of white, pink, or purple.

Cyrtochilum robustum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
