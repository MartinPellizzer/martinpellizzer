Cycnium filicalyx

Cycnium filicalyx, commonly known as the broadleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It is native to parts of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
