Ceropteris stubelii

Ceropteris stubelii, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to moist soil or rock surfaces.

Ceropteris stubelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
