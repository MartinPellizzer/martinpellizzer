Caladenia christineae

Caladenia christineae, commonly known as Christine's Spider-orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance. The plant produces a single, slender flower with a distinctive, intricate labellum that resembles a spider's leg. It thrives in sandy soils and is typically found in open habitats with adequate sunlight.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and habitat loss.
