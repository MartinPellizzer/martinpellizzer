Cypripedium × porphyrospilum

Cypripedium × porphyrospilum, commonly known as the red-spotted lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a deep pink or red pouch and intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and requires well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and oval, and it produces a single, fragrant flower per stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
