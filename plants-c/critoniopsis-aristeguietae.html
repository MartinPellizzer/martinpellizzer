Critoniopsis aristeguietae

Critoniopsis aristeguietae, commonly known as the Aristeguieta's crinoid, is a rare and unique plant species characterized by its intricate, branching structure that resembles a sea lily.

It belongs to the order Crinoidea, which includes marine animals, though this particular species is a fossilized plant. The plant exhibits a complex arrangement of calyptera, which are specialized structures that protect the reproductive organs.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator of ancient marine environments and its contribution to understanding early plant evolution.
