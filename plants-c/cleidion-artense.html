Cleidion artense

Cleidion artense, commonly known as the Artense ceiba, is a tropical tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and towering height, often reaching over 30 meters. The tree produces showy, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing seeds with a hard outer coat.

Cleidion artense plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for wildlife.
