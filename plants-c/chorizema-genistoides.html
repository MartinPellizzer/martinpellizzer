Chorizema genistoides

Chorizema genistoides, commonly known as the African broom, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its root system is deep, allowing it to access water from deeper soil layers.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in local remedies.
