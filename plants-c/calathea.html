Calathea

Calathea, commonly known as prayer plants, are tropical flowering plants native to South America.

They are characterized by their striking, often colorful foliage with intricate patterns and contrasting veins. The leaves are typically heart-shaped or arrowhead-shaped and often change color depending on the light conditions. Calathea species are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-light environments.

Their unique leaf patterns and vibrant hues make them a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
