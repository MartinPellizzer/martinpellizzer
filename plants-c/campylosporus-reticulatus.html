Campylosporus reticulatus

Campylosporus reticulatus, commonly known as the reticulate spore moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. Its intricate spore morphology and unique growth habits make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
