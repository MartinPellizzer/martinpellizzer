Cleisostoma hainanense

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments, thriving in high-altitude forests. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale yellow, are enclosed within a hood-like structure called a cleistogamous capsule, a unique reproductive feature.
