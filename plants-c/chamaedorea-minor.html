Chamaedorea minor

Chamaedorea minor, commonly known as the Mexican feather plant, is a compact, evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It features delicate, arching fronds that give it a graceful, airy appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil to flourish. Its foliage remains lush and green throughout the year, adding a touch of natural elegance to any space.

Chamaedorea minor is relatively low maintenance, making it ideal for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
