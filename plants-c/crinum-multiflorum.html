Crinum multiflorum

Crinum multiflorum, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem with numerous strap-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Crinum multiflorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
