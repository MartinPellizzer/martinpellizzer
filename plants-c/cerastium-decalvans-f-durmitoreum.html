Cerastium decalvans f. durmitoreum

Cerastium decalvans f. durmitoreum, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear cress, is a low-growing, perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

The plant typically forms dense mats on rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in harsh, high-altitude environments.
