Cotyledon spatulata

Cotyledon spatulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted for water storage. The plant produces striking orange or red flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are often shaped like spatulas, giving it its scientific name.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
