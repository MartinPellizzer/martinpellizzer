Cypripedium spectabile

Cypripedium spectabile, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or white flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces a single, prominent flower per stem.

Cypripedium spectabile is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
