Cliffortia verrucosa

Cliffortia verrucosa, commonly known as the woolly flaxflower, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found growing on slopes and cliffs.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
