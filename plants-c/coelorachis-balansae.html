Coelorachis balansae

Coelorachis balansae, commonly known as the Balansae orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, intricate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant prefers cool, humid environments and thrives in shaded, moist forest understories. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered endangered in its natural range.
