Camissonia sceptrostigma

Camissonia sceptrostigma, commonly known as the slenderstem camissonia, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Camissoniaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to the dry, sunny conditions of its habitat.

Camissonia sceptrostigma is often found in rocky or sandy soils along the coast and in inland valleys.
