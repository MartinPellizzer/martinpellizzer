Cooperia traubii

Cooperia traubii, commonly known as the Traubii grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Cooperia traubii is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
