Cotoneaster harrysmithii

Cotoneaster harrysmithii, commonly known as Harry Smith's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, glossy green leaves that turn yellow in autumn, and it produces clusters of bright red berries in the winter. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Cotoneaster harrysmithii is also valued for its ability to attract birds with its berries.
