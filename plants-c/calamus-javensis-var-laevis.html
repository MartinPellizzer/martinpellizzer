Calamus javensis var. laevis

Calamus javensis var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is a tall, slender palm-like plant characterized by its long, smooth, and often waxy leaves. The flowers are small and clustered, producing a distinctive, fragrant aroma that attracts various pollinators.
