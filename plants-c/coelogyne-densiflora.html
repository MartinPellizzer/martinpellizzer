Coelogyne densiflora

Coelogyne densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces numerous small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, hairy layer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne densiflora is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beautiful, long-lasting blooms.
