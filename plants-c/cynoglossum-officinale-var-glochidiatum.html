Cynoglossum officinale var. glochidiatum

Cynoglossum officinale var. glochidiatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive heart-shaped leaf structure. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves that are often covered in glandular dots, giving it a velvety appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or streambanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural habitats.
