Clematis × verschaffeltii

Clematis × verschaffeltii, commonly known as the Dutch hybrid clematis, is a deciduous climbing shrub native to Europe.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has strong, twining stems that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters, making it suitable for trellises or arches. Its foliage consists of palmately compound leaves with three to five leaflets, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Clematis × verschaffeltii prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, requiring regular pruning to encourage vigorous growth and flowering.
