Cystorchis fusca

Cystorchis fusca, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and is often found in tropical rainforests. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Cystorchis fusca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
