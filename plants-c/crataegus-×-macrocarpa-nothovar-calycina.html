Crataegus × macrocarpa nothovar. calycina

Crataegus × macrocarpa nothovar. calycina, commonly known as the California hawthorn, is a hybrid plant native to California.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and showy white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The fruit is a red, fleshy pome that ripens in late summer. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

It is also valued for its role in supporting local wildlife.
