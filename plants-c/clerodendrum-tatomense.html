Clerodendrum tatomense

Clerodendrum tatomense, commonly known as the Tatomense Flame Vine, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clerodendrum tatomense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
