Cinnamomum walteri

Cinnamomum walteri, commonly known as Walter's cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like cinnamon and laurel. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its bark and leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its aromatic properties.

Walter's cinnamon is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the extraction of aromatic compounds.
