Cyperus aterrimus var. atroviridis

Cyperus aterrimus var. atroviridis, commonly known as black rush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features dark green, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in dense clusters around a central stem. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Its botanical structure is characterized by rhizomes that help in its spread and establishment in suitable habitats.
