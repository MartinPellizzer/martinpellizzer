Chuquiraga chrysantha var. longiflora

Chuquiraga chrysantha var. longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered chuquiraga, is a South American plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers, which are typically arranged in dense, spherical clusters. The plant features hairy, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
