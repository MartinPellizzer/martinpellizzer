Cattleya intermedia var. angustifolia

Cattleya intermedia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often a different hue. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are relatively narrow, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and adaptability to cultivation.
