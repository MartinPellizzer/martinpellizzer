Cymbidium intermedium

Cymbidium intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that support its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces upright, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are showy, with long, slender petals and a distinctive, often fragrant lip.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.
