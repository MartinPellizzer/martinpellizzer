Cerradicola eriosematoides

Cerradicola eriosematoides, commonly known as the velvetleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
