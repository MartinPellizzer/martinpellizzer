Chlorophytum campanulatum

Chlorophytum campanulatum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or blue flowers that bloom from the center. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among both novice and experienced gardeners.
