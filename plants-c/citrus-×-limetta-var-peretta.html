Citrus × limetta var. peretta

Citrus × limetta var. peretta, commonly known as the Peretta lemon.

It is a hybrid citrus fruit characterized by its small, round shape and thin, yellow-orange peel. The plant is a vigorous grower with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is prized for its high acidity and is often used in culinary applications and for making essential oils.
