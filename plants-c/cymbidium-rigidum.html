Cymbidium rigidum

Cymbidium rigidum, commonly known as the hardy orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect pseudobulbs that support strap-like leaves. The plant produces inflorescences of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light.

Cymbidium rigidum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
