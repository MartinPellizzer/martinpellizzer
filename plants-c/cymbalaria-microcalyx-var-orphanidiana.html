Cymbalaria microcalyx var. orphanidiana

Cymbalaria microcalyx var. orphanidiana, commonly known as the dwarf heartleaf springbeauty, is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves.

It produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early spring. This variety is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained, sunny to partially shaded environments. Its botanical features include hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss in its native climate.

It is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
