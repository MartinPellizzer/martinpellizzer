Cattleya × leeana

Cattleya × leeana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a popular epiphytic orchid native to South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, tropical climates.

Cattleya × leeana is prized for its ornamental value and is widely cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
