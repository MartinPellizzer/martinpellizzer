Cynophalla flexuosa

Cynophalla flexuosa, commonly known as the wandering candle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy coating.

Cynophalla flexuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
