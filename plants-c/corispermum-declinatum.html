Corispermum declinatum

Corispermum declinatum, commonly known as the leaning bladderwrath, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is adapted to cold climates. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.
