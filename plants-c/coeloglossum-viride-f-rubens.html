Coeloglossum viride f. rubens

Coeloglossum viride f. rubens, commonly known as the red-stemmed green orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species characterized by its vibrant green foliage and distinctive red stems.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to specific regions in South America, particularly in Brazil. The plant produces delicate, pendulous flowers that are typically green with a subtle red or purple tinge, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
