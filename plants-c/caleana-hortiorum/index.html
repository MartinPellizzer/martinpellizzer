Caleana Hortiorum

Caleana hortiorum is a striking orchid native to australia known for its unique floral structure. this plant features a distinctive red and yellow flower that resembles a hummingbird's beak. it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in southeastern australia. the species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity. its conservation status highlights the importance of protecting native flora.
