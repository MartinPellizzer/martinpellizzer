Cyclamen europaeum var. colchicum

Cyclamen europaeum var. colchicum, commonly known as the Colchicum cyclamen, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically green with a silvery underside. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
