Chamaedorea corallina

Chamaedorea corallina, commonly known as the coral bamboo palm, is a compact, slow-growing evergreen palm native to Mexico and Central America.

It features slender, arching leaves that form a dense, fountain-like shape, giving it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture without becoming waterlogged. Its ability to tolerate low light makes it a popular choice for indoor spaces.

Chamaedorea corallina is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
