Cyrtandra × forbesii

Cyrtandra × forbesii, commonly known as the hybrid blue ginger, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub that features showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive tubular shape. The plant is native to Hawaii and is characterized by its lance-shaped, evergreen leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and forest habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
