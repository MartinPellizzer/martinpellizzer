Clematis viticella var. venosa

Clematis viticella var. venosa, commonly known as the variegated Clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to Europe.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves with white and green patterns, which add visual interest to gardens. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rapid growth and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for trellises and garden structures.
