Carex supina f. pallida

Carex supina f. pallida, commonly known as the pale form of the prostrate sedge.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically pale green in color. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spike-like flowers.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
