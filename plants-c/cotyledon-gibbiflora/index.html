Cotyledon Gibbiflora

Cotyledon gibbiflora, scientifically known as Cotyledon gibbiflora, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. This unique plant features fleshy, waxy leaves that are often adorned with a distinctive red or purple hue. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens and indoor spaces. The plant is prized for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and minimal water. With its vibrant appearance and low maintenance requirements, Cotyledon gibbiflora has become a popular addition to many plant collections.
