Carex × ohmuelleriana

Carex × ohmuelleriana, commonly known as the hybrid sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Carex echinata and Carex brevicuspis, exhibiting a mix of characteristics from both parent species. The plant typically forms dense clumps of grass-like foliage with narrow, grass-green leaves. It produces spikes of small, inconspicuous flowers that are often green or brown in color.

Carex × ohmuelleriana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
