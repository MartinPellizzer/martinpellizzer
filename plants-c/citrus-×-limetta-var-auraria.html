Citrus × limetta var. auraria

Citrus × limetta var. auraria, commonly known as the golden lemon.

This variant is a hybrid citrus plant that exhibits a unique golden-yellow coloration compared to the typical green of standard lemons. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits with a thin, smooth rind and a high concentration of aromatic oils. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and vigorous growth habit.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its flavorful, juicy fruit used in culinary and medicinal applications.
