Ceratandra venosa

Ceratandra venosa, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive greenish tinge. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Ceratandra venosa thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in botanical gardens.
