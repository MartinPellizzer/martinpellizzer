Codonoboea platypus

Codonoboea platypus, commonly known as the flat-leaf codonopsis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often flat or slightly folded. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
