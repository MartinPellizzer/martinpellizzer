Cyanotis speciosa subsp. speciosa

Cyanotis speciosa subsp.

speciosa, commonly known as the blue-eyed grass, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for garden cultivation in tropical and subtropical regions.
