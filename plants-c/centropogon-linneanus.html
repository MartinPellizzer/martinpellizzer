Centropogon linneanus

Centropogon linneanus, commonly known as the yellow centropogon, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or limestone soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stem is erect and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.
