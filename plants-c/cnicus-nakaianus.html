Cnicus nakaianus

Cnicus nakaianus, commonly known as the New Zealand thistle, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pink flowers that bloom in spring. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its invasive nature, it is considered a noxious weed in some areas outside its native range.
