Cotyledon lineata

Cotyledon lineata, commonly known as the African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often colored in shades of green, purple, and red. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Cotyledon lineata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
