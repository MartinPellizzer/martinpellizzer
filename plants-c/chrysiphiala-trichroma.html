Chrysiphiala trichroma

Chrysiphiala trichroma, commonly known as the tri-colored chrysiphiala, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored foliage, which displays shades of green, red, and yellow, making it highly ornamental. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with contrasting patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
