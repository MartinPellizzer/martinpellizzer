Clerodendrum versteegii

Clerodendrum versteegii, commonly known as the African bignonia, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture and a dark green upper surface. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
