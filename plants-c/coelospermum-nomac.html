Coelospermum nomac

Coelospermum nomac, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in green or reddish bracts.

Coelospermum nomac is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
