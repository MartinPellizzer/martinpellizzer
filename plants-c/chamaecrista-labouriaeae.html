Chamaecrista labouriaeae

Chamaecrista labouriaeae, commonly known as the African burr legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, spiny seed pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in erosion control and as a forage plant.
