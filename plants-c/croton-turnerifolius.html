Croton turnerifolius

Croton turnerifolius, commonly known as the Turner's croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that vary in color from deep green to reddish hues. Its flowers are small and clustered in yellowish-green cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Croton turnerifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
