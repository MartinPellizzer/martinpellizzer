Coleus rotundifolius var. nigra

Coleus rotundifolius var. nigra, commonly known as Black Knight Coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black foliage with a velvety texture, which gives it a striking appearance. The leaves are round and broad, with a slight waxy coating that enhances its visual appeal. This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaf color and ability to thrive in partial shade.

It prefers well-drained soil and regular watering, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking bold foliage.
