Calystegia reniformis

Calystegia reniformis, commonly known as the heartleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to purple.
