Coleus igniarioides

Coleus igniarioides, commonly known as the flameleaf coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires partial to full sunlight.

This plant is popular in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance and ability to add visual interest to landscapes.
