Coelogyne longifolia

Coelogyne longifolia, commonly known as the long-flowered orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces multiple inflorescences with numerous showy, lance-shaped petals and sepals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne longifolia is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
