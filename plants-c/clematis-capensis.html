Clematis capensis

Clematis capensis, commonly known as the Cape clematis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is known for its vigorous growth habit. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis capensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
