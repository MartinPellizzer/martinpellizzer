Ceropegia halipedicola

Ceropegia halipedicola, commonly known as the silver leaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a trailing succulent with fleshy, silver-green leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow, adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
