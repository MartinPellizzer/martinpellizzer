Callicarpa chejuensis

Callicarpa chejuensis, commonly known as the Jeju beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Korean island of Jeju.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that turn red in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
