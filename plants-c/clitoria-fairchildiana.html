Clitoria fairchildiana

Clitoria fairchildiana, commonly known as the purple orchid bean, is a striking flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces vibrant purple to violet flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clitoria fairchildiana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
