Chenopodina setigera

Chenopodina setigera, commonly known as the slender goosefoot, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

Chenopodina setigera is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in dry conditions.
