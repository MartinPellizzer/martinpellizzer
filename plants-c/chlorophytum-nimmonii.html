Chlorophytum nimmonii

Chlorophytum nimmonii, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Spider Plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically bright green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by small, round berries. It is well adapted to arid conditions and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.
