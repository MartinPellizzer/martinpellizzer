Calea platylepis var. mollis

Calea platylepis var. mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf calea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
