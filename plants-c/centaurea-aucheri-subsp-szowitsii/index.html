Centaurea Aucheri Subsp. Szowitsii

Centaurea aucheri subsp. szowitsii is a rare and beautiful plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its striking blue to purple flowers. This subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
