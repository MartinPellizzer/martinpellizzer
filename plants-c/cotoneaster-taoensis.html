Cotoneaster taoensis

Cotoneaster taoensis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Cotoneaster taoensis is relatively low maintenance and adaptable to various environmental conditions.
