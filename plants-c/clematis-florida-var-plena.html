Clematis florida var. plena

Clematis florida var. plena, commonly known as the Japanese vine or full-flowered clematis, is a deciduous climbing plant native to Japan.

It features showy, double flowers that are typically white or pale blue, with numerous petals arranged in a rounded cluster. The plant has slender, flexible stems that can reach lengths of up to 15 feet, making it suitable for trellises or arbors. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves with three heart-shaped leaflets.

Clematis florida var. plena thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
