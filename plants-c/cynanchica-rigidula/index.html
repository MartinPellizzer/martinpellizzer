Cynanchica Rigidula

Cynanchica rigidula is a hardy plant known for its resilience in harsh environments. It thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, often found in rocky soils and open landscapes. The plant features slender stems and small, often spiny leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically pale or greenish, blending with the surroundings for camouflage. Despite its tough appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and stabilizing soil in its habitat.
