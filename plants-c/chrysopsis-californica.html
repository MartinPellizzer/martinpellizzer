Chrysopsis californica

Chrysopsis californica, commonly known as the California goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to California and Baja California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its yellow flowers. Chrysopsis californica is a hardy, drought-tolerant species well adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native range.
