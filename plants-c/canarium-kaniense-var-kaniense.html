Canarium kaniense var. kaniense

Canarium kaniense var. kaniense, commonly known as the Kaniense candlenut, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing a single seed. The tree is valued for its hard, heavy wood and its potential use in traditional medicine.
