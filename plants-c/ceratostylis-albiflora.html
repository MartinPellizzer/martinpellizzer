Ceratostylis albiflora

Ceratostylis albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered horned orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, horn-shaped spathes that enclose the inflorescence. The flowers are predominantly white with a subtle pinkish tint, and they emit a faint, sweet fragrance. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
