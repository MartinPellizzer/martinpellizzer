Camellia setiperulata

Camellia setiperulata, commonly known as the long-hair camellia, is a species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces striking, large flowers with deep red petals and a yellow center. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Camellia setiperulata is also valued for its cold hardiness and ability to bloom in late winter or early spring.
