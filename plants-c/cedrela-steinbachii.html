Cedrela steinbachii

Cedrela steinbachii, commonly known as yellow cedar or red cedar, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, durable wood. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.
