Chenopodium rubrum var. zachae

Chenopodium rubrum var. zachae, commonly known as red goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its reddish stems and leaves that are typically ovate to rhombic in shape. The plant produces small, green flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it has been introduced to other regions as a weed.

The plant is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in dry conditions.
