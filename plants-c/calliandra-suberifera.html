Calliandra suberifera

Calliandra suberifera, commonly known as the silky calliandra, is a shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences that produce showy, pink to red flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its visual appeal.

Calliandra suberifera is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in ecological restoration projects.
