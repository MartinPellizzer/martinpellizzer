Cinnamomum liangii

Cinnamomum liangii, commonly known as the Liangshan cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue berries. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive cinnamon scent when crushed.

The bark is used in traditional medicine and for its aromatic properties.
