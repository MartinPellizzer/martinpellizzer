Camellia lutescens

Camellia lutescens, commonly known as the yellow camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has leathery, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Camellia lutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
