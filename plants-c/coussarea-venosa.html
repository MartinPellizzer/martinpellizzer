Coussarea venosa

Coussarea venosa, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces woody pods containing flat, edible seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
