Capsella rubiginosa

Capsella rubiginosa, commonly known as the red weed or common mouse-ear.

It is an annual flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant has small, pink to reddish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

Capsella rubiginosa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
