Crassula erubescens

Crassula erubescens, commonly known as the "Lamb's Ear" or "Red Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with a red or pinkish tint, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
