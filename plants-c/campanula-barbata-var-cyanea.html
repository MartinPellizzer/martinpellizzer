Campanula barbata var. cyanea

Campanula barbata var. cyanea, commonly known as the blue bell bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to violet in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
