Cissampelos macrosepala

Cissampelos macrosepala, commonly known as the large-sepal cissampelos, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers.
