Crypsinus morianus

Crypsinus morianus, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a rare and endemic species found exclusively in Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforests of Madagascar. Its flowers are small but showy, often displaying a combination of purple and white hues.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Crypsinus morianus is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
