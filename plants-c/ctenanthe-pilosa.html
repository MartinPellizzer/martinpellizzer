Ctenanthe pilosa

Ctenanthe pilosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf caladium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of green, red, and orange. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Ctenanthe pilosa thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant in shaded outdoor spaces.
