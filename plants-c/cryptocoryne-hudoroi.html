Cryptocoryne hudoroi

Cryptocoryne hudoroi, commonly known as the Hodoroi cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. It is often found in the undergrowth of tropical rainforests near streams and rivers.

Cryptocoryne hudoroi is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to help maintain water quality.
