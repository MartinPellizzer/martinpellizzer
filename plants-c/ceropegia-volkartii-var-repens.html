Ceropegia volkartii var. repens

Ceropegia volkartii var. repens, commonly known as the string of hearts plant, is a climbing or trailing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, with a distinctive, intricate structure. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
