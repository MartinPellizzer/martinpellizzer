Cryptocoryne bullosa var. scurrilis

Cryptocoryne bullosa var. scurrilis, commonly known as the Scurrilis Java Cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments. Its rhizome system allows it to spread easily and form dense underwater carpets.

It is popular in planted aquariums due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
