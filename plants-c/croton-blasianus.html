Croton blasianus

Croton blasianus, commonly known as the Blasianus croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Uganda and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that can range from green to red, orange, or even purple. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Croton blasianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
