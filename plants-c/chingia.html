Chingia

Chingia, commonly known as the "Chinese lantern plant," is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the genus Physalis and is characterized by its vibrant orange, lantern-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Chingia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
