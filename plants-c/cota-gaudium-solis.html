Cota gaudium-solis

Cota gaudium-solis, commonly known as the Sunflower of Joy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweed. The plant is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that follow the sun's movement throughout the day, a phenomenon known as heliotropism. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

The plant thrives in full sunlight and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, sun-loving ornamental plants.
