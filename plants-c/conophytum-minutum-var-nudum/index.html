Conophytum Minutum Var. Nudum

Conophytum minutum var. nudum is a striking succulent native to southern Africa, known for its compact, rock-like appearance and vibrant floral display. This variety stands out due to its unique lack of leaves, relying solely on its fleshy stems for survival. It thrives in arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to extreme conditions. Its blooming period typically occurs in late winter or early spring, adding a burst of color to its natural habitat. Despite its small size, this plant is a favorite among succulent enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
