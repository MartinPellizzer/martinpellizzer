Cypripedium × daviesianum

Cypripedium × daviesianum, commonly known as the Showy Lady's Slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, slipper-shaped petals that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments and has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, and it produces small, dark brown seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
