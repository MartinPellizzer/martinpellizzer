Cleome potosina

Cleome potosina, commonly known as the Mexican spider flower, is a herbaceous plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dark seeds. Cleome potosina thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
