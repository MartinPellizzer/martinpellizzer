Cyperus ischnos

Cyperus ischnos, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Africa and Southeast Asia.

Cyperus ischnos plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
