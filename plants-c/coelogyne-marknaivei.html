Coelogyne marknaivei

Coelogyne marknaivei, commonly known as the Marknaive's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forest understories. The plant features a pseudobulb-like structure and produces elegant, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Coelogyne marknaivei is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
