Callicarpa vansteenisii

Callicarpa vansteenisii, commonly known as the red-berried beauty, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the honeysuckle family (Caprifoliaceae) and is characterized by its clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, adding ornamental value to gardens.
