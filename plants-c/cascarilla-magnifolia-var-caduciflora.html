Cascarilla magnifolia var. caduciflora

Cascarilla magnifolia var. caduciflora, commonly known as the fragrant magnolia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that emit a subtle, sweet fragrance. The plant belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its striking blooms and evergreen foliage. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and glossy, providing a lush backdrop for the flowers.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
