Colysis lioui

Colysis lioui, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow floral clusters that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the family Fabaceae, also known as the legume or pea family. Its seeds are encased in a pod-like fruit and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
