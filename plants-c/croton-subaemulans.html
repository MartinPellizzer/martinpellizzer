Croton subaemulans

Croton subaemulans, commonly known as the narrow-leaved croton, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
