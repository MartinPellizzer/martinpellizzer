Cymbidium sinense var. taiwanianum

Cymbidium sinense var. taiwanianum, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a species native to Taiwan and belongs to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically blooms in winter or early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens and landscapes. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

Its unique floral structure and cultural significance make it a prized ornamental plant in horticulture.
