Castanopsis sieboldii subsp. lutchuensis

Castanopsis sieboldii subsp.

lutchuensis, commonly known as the Lushan tanoak, is a member of the Fagaceae family and is native to southern China. It is a broadleaf evergreen tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in subtropical forest ecosystems.
