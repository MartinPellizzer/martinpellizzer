Chuquiraga acicularis

Chuquiraga acicularis, commonly known as the needle bush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, needle-like leaves and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. Its structure is adapted to conserve water, with reduced leaves and a deep root system.

Chuquiraga acicularis is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
