Costus violaceus

Costus violaceus, commonly known as violet-costly or purple costus, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant features tall, slender stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, typically displaying shades of purple and white.
