Cylichnanthus unibiflorus

Cylichnanthus unibiflorus, commonly known as the single-flower cypress.

It is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and conical shape, resembling a small tree or shrub. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
