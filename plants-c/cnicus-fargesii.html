Cnicus fargesii

Cnicus fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a low-growing habit and produces small, inconspicuous fruits.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
