Calamagrostis sorengii

Calamagrostis sorengii, commonly known as the Soreng's reed, is a perennial grass native to the Himalayas.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. The plant features tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Calamagrostis sorengii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
