Chamaecrista Mississippiensis

Chamaecrista mississippiensis is a native plant of the southeastern united states known for its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. it thrives in wetland environments and is often found along the banks of rivers and streams. this species plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. its common name, "mississippi bean," reflects its historical and ecological significance. despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in favor of more showy native plants.
