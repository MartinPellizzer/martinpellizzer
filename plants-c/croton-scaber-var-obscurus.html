Croton scaber var. obscurus

Croton scaber var. obscurus, commonly known as the rough croton, is a species of flowering plant in the spurge family, Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its thick, rough-textured leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy surface. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually clustered in dense, greenish-yellow inflorescences.

The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its unique foliage and ability to tolerate various soil conditions.
