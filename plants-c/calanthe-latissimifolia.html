Calanthe latissimifolia

Calanthe latissimifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and streamside areas. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense colonies in suitable habitats.

Calanthe latissimifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
