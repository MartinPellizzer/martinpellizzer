Conophytum fraternum

Conophytum fraternum, commonly known as the "Succulent Rock" or "Cape Flats Rock," is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Conophytum fraternum is prized for its unique appearance and resilience in harsh climates.
