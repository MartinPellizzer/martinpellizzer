Cotoneaster simonsii

Cotoneaster simonsii, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, glossy green leaves that turn red in the autumn, adding ornamental value. Its flowers are typically white or pink, appearing in clusters, followed by bright red berries that attract birds.

Cotoneaster simonsii is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
