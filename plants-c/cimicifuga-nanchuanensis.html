Cimicifuga nanchuanensis

Cimicifuga nanchuanensis, commonly known as Nanchuan bugbane, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely leafy stems. The plant produces large, white to cream-colored flowers in late summer, which are clustered in panicles. Its roots are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported medicinal properties.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.
