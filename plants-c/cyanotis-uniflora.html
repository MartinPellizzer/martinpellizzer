Cyanotis uniflora

Cyanotis uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower bluebell, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, including India and Sri Lanka. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with blue or purple hues. Its most striking characteristic is its single, bell-shaped blue flower that blooms in the spring.

Cyanotis uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
