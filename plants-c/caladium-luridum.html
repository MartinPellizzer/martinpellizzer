Caladium luridum

Caladium luridum, commonly known as the lurid arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting green veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium luridum thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its foliage is the primary attraction, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen.
