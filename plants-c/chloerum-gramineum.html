Chloerum gramineum

Chloerum gramineum, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Native to temperate regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. The plant is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of ecological restoration projects.
