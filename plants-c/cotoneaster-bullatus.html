Cotoneaster bullatus

Cotoneaster bullatus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. The plant has elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster bullatus is also valued for its role in wildlife habitats, providing food for birds and small mammals.
