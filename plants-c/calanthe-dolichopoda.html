Calanthe dolichopoda

Calanthe dolichopoda, commonly known as the long-stemmed calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Calanthe dolichopoda thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
