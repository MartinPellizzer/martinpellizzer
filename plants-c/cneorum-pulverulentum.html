Cneorum pulverulentum

Cneorum pulverulentum, commonly known as the dusty cneorum, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, five-petaled flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and fragrant foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
