Cattleya verboonenii

Cattleya verboonenii, commonly known as the Verboonenii Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, warm climates.

This orchid is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
