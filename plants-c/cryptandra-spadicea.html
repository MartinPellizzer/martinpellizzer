Cryptandra spadicea

Cryptandra spadicea, commonly known as the yellow cryptandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom during the warmer months. Its growth habit is typically low and spreading, with woody stems that can become twisted over time.

Cryptandra spadicea is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland heathlands.
