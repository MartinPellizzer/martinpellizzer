Clerodendrum schweinfurthii var. bakeri

Clerodendrum schweinfurthii var. bakeri, commonly known as the Baker's Clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple, often with a yellow or orange throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in warm, humid climates.
