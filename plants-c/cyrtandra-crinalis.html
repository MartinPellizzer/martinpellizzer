Cyrtandra crinalis

Cyrtandra crinalis, commonly known as the fragrant flame or red lily, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that are often red or orange in color. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a rough, textured surface.
