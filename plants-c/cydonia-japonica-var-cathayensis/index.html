Cydonia Japonica Var. Cathayensis

Cydonia japonica var. cathayensis is a rare and fascinating plant native to parts of eastern Asia. It belongs to the Rosaceae family, which includes many familiar fruits like apples and cherries. This variant is known for its unique, fragrant flowers and its potential medicinal properties. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in modern horticulture. Its study offers valuable insights into ancient plant genetics and ecological adaptation.
