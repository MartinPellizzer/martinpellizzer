Cyrtomium salicipinnum

Cyrtomium salicipinnum, commonly known as the willow fern, is a species of evergreen fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and help it spread vegetatively.
