Coelogyne kelabitensis

Coelogyne kelabitensis, commonly known as the Kelabit orchid, is a species of orchid native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters of 5 to 10. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne kelabitensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
