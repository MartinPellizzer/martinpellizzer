Callitriche reflexa

Callitriche reflexa, commonly known as water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Callitrichaceae.

It is typically found in freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and slow-moving streams. The plant has delicate, floating or submerged leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. Its small, white flowers are usually borne in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Callitriche reflexa plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
