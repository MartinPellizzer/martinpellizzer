Coelogyne tumida

Coelogyne tumida, commonly known as the fragrant orchid or the pink lady orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of India, China, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and orange. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered with a thick layer of brownish bark.

Coelogyne tumida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a warm, humid environment.
