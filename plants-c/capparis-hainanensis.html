Capparis hainanensis

Capparis hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan caper, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features pinnately compound leaves and produces yellow or greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruits are capsules that contain several seeds, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
