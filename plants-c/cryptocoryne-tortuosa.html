Cryptocoryne tortuosa

Cryptocoryne tortuosa, commonly known as the twisted cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, ribbon-like leaves that curl and twist, creating a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves can reach lengths of up to 60 centimeters, making it a striking addition to planted aquariums.

Cryptocoryne tortuosa is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive under a variety of lighting conditions.
