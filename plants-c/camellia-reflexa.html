Camellia reflexa

Camellia reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed camellia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia reflexa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
