Crataegus oxyacantha var. monostyla

Crataegus oxyacantha var. monostyla, commonly known as the single-stemmed hawthorn, is a variant of the common hawthorn.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, thornless stems and clusters of white flowers in spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy pome that ripens in late summer.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
