Cotoneaster integerrimus var. vulgaris

Cotoneaster integerrimus var. vulgaris, commonly known as the common cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that persist into winter. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster integerrimus var. vulgaris is also valued for its use in hedging and as a landscape plant.
