Chlorophytum distichum

Chlorophytum distichum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are usually bright green with white stripes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires minimal watering. It produces small, white flowers on long, slender stems during the growing season.

Chlorophytum distichum is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
