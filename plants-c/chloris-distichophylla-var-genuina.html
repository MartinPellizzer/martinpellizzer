Chloris distichophylla var. genuina

Chloris distichophylla var. genuina, commonly known as the slender feather grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Australia.

It features fine, narrow leaves that are typically two-ranked, giving it a distinctive, delicate appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native gardens and restoration projects.
