Cynodon neesii

Cynodon neesii, commonly known as the African dogstail grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense mats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. Its leaves are narrow, hairy, and often have a bluish-gray appearance.

Cynodon neesii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh conditions.
