Cassia benitoensis

Cassia benitoensis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are compound, with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Cassia benitoensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
