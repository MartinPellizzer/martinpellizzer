Cavendishia stenophylla

Cavendishia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions.
