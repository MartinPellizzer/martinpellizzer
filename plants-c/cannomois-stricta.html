Cannomois stricta

Cannomois stricta, commonly known as the rigid canna, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features tall, stiff, sword-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, with three prominent petals and a central cluster of stamens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Cannomois stricta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
