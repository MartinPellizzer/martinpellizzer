Cyperus nitens var. membranaceus

Cyperus nitens var. membranaceus, commonly known as the narrowleaf paperplant, is a species of sedge native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often near water bodies. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
