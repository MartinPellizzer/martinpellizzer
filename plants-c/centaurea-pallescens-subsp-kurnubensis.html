Centaurea pallescens subsp. kurnubensis

Centaurea pallescens subsp.

kurnubensis, commonly known as the Kurnub centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of North Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The plant features slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves, with bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions and has a low-growing habit.
