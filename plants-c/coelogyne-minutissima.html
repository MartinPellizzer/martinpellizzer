Coelogyne minutissima

Coelogyne minutissima, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the tiny coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of white and green. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Due to its rarity and small size, it is highly valued in the horticultural trade.
