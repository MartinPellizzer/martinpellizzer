Crotalaria rotundifolia var. linaria

Crotalaria rotundifolia var. linaria, commonly known as the roundleaf cowpea or linaria crotalaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. It has a short growth cycle and is sometimes cultivated as a forage crop or for ornamental purposes.

The seeds are encased in a hard pod and can remain viable in the soil for several years.
