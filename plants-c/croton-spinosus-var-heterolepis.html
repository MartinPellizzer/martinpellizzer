Croton spinosus var. heterolepis

Croton spinosus var. heterolepis, commonly known as the spiny croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features distinctive, spiny stems and leaves that are typically opposite and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a soft texture.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
