Cleome Rutidosperma Var. Burmanni

Cleome rutidosperma var. burmanni is a unique and lesser-known flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the Cleomaceae family, known for its showy blooms and distinctive foliage. This variant stands out due to its compact growth habit and striking, colorful flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. Its resilience and adaptability make it a promising candidate for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
