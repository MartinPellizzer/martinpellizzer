Convolvulus laevigatus

Convolvulus laevigatus, commonly known as the smooth morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant has hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to spread easily in various habitats.
