Clerodendrum pubescens

Clerodendrum pubescens, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, velvety-textured leaves and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is adaptable to various soil types.

Clerodendrum pubescens is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
