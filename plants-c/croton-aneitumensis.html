Croton aneitumensis

Croton aneitumensis, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cup-like structures called cyathia. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist forest understories.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
