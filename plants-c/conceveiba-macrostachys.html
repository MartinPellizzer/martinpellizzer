Conceveiba macrostachys

Conceveiba macrostachys, commonly known as the large-stalked ceiba, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and smooth, gray bark. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green appearance.
