Coryanthes cavalcantei

Coryanthes cavalcantei, commonly known as the red coryanthe, is a striking tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically bright red or orange with intricate patterns. The plant produces a unique, columnar structure that supports its fleshy, hooded petals and a long, narrow spur. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
