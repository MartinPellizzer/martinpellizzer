Centaurea tauromenitana

Centaurea tauromenitana, commonly known as the Tauromenine thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking purple to pink flowers and spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often in coastal areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea tauromenitana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
