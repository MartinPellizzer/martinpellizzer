Cynoglossum officinale var. umbrosum

Cynoglossum officinale var. umbrosum, commonly known as the shadow plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features striking, dark purple to almost black flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple, contributing to its dramatic appearance. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soils, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its unique coloration and ornamental value make it a popular choice for shady garden spaces.
