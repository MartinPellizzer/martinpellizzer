Cestichis insectifera

Cestichis insectifera, commonly known as the insect-bearing cestichis, is a rare and unique plant species characterized by its specialized adaptations for nutrient acquisition.

Its reproductive system involves the production of spores, which are dispersed by wind to ensure propagation in suitable environments.
