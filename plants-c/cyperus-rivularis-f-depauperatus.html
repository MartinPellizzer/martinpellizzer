Cyperus rivularis f. depauperatus

Cyperus rivularis f. depauperatus, commonly known as the sparse cyperus, is a rare and less common variant of the common flatsedge.

It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance and unique morphological features. This plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to aquatic conditions. Its reduced foliage and compact growth habit distinguish it from other members of the species.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare botanical specimen.
