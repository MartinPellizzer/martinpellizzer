Carduus × caflischii

Carduus × caflischii, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
