Crepidium circeum

Crepidium circeum, commonly known as the circeum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a faintly fragrant scent.

Crepidium circeum is characterized by its unique rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
