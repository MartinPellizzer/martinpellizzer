Coprosma atropurpurea

Coprosma atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple coprosma, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, dark green leaves with a distinctive purple underside. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that ripen to a bright red color, attracting birds and other wildlife.

Coprosma atropurpurea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
