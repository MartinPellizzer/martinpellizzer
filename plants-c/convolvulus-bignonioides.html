Convolvulus bignonioides

Convolvulus bignonioides, commonly known as the yellow morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is native to regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. Its leaves are heart-shaped and slightly hairy, while its stems are hairy and tend to twine around supports.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
