Coudenbergia warmingii

Coudenbergia warmingii, commonly known as the African tamarind tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. Its flowers are typically yellow or green, and it produces edible seeds that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.
