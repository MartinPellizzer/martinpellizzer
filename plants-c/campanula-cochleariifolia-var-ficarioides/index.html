Campanula Cochleariifolia Var. Ficarioides

Campanula cochleariifolia var. ficarioides is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family known for its unique appearance and ornamental value. This variant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, adding a vibrant touch to gardens and landscapes. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in rocky and limestone soils, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens. Its delicate foliage and long blooming period make it a favorite among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. With proper care, this plant can flourish in both natural and cultivated environments.
