Ctenodon molliculus

Ctenodon molliculus, commonly known as the silky ctenodon, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical climates.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat preferences, it is a subject of interest in botanical studies.
