Crotalaria tristis

Crotalaria tristis, commonly known as the weeping cowpea, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, characterized by its typical pod-bearing structure and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated pods, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types.

Crotalaria tristis is sometimes considered a weed due to its invasive potential in agricultural settings.
