Cymbidium elegans var. lutescens

Cymbidium elegans var. lutescens, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers with a contrasting deep red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. It prefers cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Cymbidium elegans var. lutescens is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
