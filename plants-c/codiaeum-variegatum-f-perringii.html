Codiaeum variegatum f. perringii

Codiaeum variegatum f. perringii, commonly known as the Perring's croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored foliage featuring shades of red, yellow, and green. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a textured surface that adds to its visual appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical and subtropical gardens.
