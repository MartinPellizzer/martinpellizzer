Cipadessa fruticosa var. cinerascens

Cipadessa fruticosa var. cinerascens, commonly known as the cinerascens cipadessa, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

This species is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
