Cryptanthus micrus

Cryptanthus micrus, commonly known as the miniature ground orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features compact, rosette-like foliage with fleshy, grass-like leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink, red, or white. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
