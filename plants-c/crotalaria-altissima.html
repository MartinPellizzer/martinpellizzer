Crotalaria altissima

Crotalaria altissima, commonly known as the tall crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with pinnate arrangement and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and potentially invasive in certain areas.
