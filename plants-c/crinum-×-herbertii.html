Crinum × herbertii

Crinum × herbertii, commonly known as the Herbert's crinum, is a hybrid plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and often have a waxy texture. It is native to South Africa and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun.

Crinum × herbertii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
