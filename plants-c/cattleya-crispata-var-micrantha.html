Cattleya crispata var. micrantha

Cattleya crispata var. micrantha, commonly known as the Micrantha Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with flowers that have a delicate, crinkled appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively ease of cultivation.
