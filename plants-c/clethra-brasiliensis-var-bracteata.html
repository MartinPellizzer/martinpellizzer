Clethra brasiliensis var. bracteata

Clethra brasiliensis var. bracteata, commonly known as the Brazilian clethra, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, providing a striking visual contrast to the pale flowers.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.
