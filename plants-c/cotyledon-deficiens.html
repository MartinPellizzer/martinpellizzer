Cotyledon deficiens

Cotyledon deficiens, commonly known as the narrow-leafed African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Cotyledon deficiens requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light to thrive.
