Cimicifuga japonica

Cimicifuga japonica, commonly known as Japanese bugbane, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features tall, unbranched stems that bear clusters of white, lacecap flowers. The plant has large, palmately compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Cimicifuga japonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
