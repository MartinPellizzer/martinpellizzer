Cyathea presliana

Cyathea presliana, commonly known as the Preslia's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cyathea presliana plays a role in forest ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
