Cordia rusbyi

Cordia rusbyi, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of white, fleshy fruits that resemble snowballs, which are an important food source for birds and small mammals. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

Cordia rusbyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy flowers and unique fruiting structures.
