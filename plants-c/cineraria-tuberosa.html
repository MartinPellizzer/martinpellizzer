Cineraria tuberosa

Cineraria tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in its arid native habitat. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Cineraria tuberosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
