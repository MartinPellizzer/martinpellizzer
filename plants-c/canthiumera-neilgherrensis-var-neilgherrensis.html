Canthiumera neilgherrensis var. neilgherrensis

Canthiumera neilgherrensis var. neilgherrensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri canthium, is a species of flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive, fragrant flower cluster. Its flowers are usually small and yellowish-green, arranged in dense, terminal spikes.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its propagation in the wild.
