Ceropegia zebrina

Ceropegia zebrina, commonly known as the zigzag plant or string of pearls, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, succulent stems. The plant features small, waxy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and often have a zigzag pattern along their edges.

Ceropegia zebrina thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
