Cyperus thyrsiflorus var. vegetior

Cyperus thyrsiflorus var. vegetior, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of small, spike-like flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, such as swamps and seasonally flooded areas. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense stands.

The botanical characteristics of this variety make it adaptable to a range of wetland environments.
