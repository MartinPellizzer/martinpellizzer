Crotalaria randii var. congoensis

Crotalaria randii var. congoensis, commonly known as the Congo velvet bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, oblong pods that contain several seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
