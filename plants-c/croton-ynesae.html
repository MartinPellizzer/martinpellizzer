Croton ynesae

Croton ynesae, commonly known as the Ynesae croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, varied leaf colors, which range from deep red to bright yellow and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton ynesae is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.
