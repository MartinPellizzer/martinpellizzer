Cerionanthus

Cerionanthus, commonly known as the snowdrop, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Amaryllidaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, nodding flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. Their delicate appearance and early blooming habit make them a symbol of hope and renewal in the early spring season.
