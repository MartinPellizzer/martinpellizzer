Centella cuneifolia

Centella cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf centella, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Centella cuneifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
