Conomorpha curvivenia

Conomorpha curvivenia, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its root system is specialized for nutrient absorption in poor soil conditions.

Conomorpha curvivenia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
