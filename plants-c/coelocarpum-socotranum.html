Coelocarpum socotranum

Coelocarpum socotranum, commonly known as Socotra cactus, is a unique plant endemic to the island of Socotra.

It is a spiny, shrub-like plant that belongs to the family Cactaceae. The plant features a short, thick stem with numerous spines and produces small, white flowers. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss in its arid environment.

Coelocarpum socotranum is adapted to the extreme climate of Socotra, showcasing remarkable resilience and evolutionary adaptation.
