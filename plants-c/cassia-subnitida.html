Cassia subnitida

Cassia subnitida, commonly known as golden shower tree or yellow cassia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces long, slender pods containing flat, winged seeds. Cassia subnitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
