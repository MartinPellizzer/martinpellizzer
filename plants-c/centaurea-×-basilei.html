Centaurea × basilei

Centaurea × basilei, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue to purple flower heads that are typically surrounded by white to pale blue bracts. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Centaurea × basilei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
