Cleome foliosa var. namibensis

Cleome foliosa var. namibensis, commonly known as the Namibian spider flower, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Namibia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils. It is often found growing along coastal dunes and in rocky habitats.

Cleome foliosa var. namibensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
