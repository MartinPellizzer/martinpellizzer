Conyza lurida

Conyza lurida, commonly known as roughleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, rough-textured leaves and produces small, pale yellow to white flowers. It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks.

Conyza lurida is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in moist to wet conditions.
