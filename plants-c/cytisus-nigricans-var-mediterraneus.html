Cytisus nigricans var. mediterraneus

Cytisus nigricans var. mediterraneus, commonly known as the Mediterranean broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, wiry stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.
