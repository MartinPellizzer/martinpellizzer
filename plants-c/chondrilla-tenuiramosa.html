Chondrilla tenuiramosa

Chondrilla tenuiramosa, commonly known as narrow-branched goatweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, arid environments and is adapted to withstand harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs to reduce water loss.

Chondrilla tenuiramosa is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
