Cordyline hectorii

Cordyline hectorii, commonly known as Hector's candle, is a striking evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features tall, slender, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with a waxy coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its unique look and hardiness, it is often cultivated in coastal and temperate regions.
