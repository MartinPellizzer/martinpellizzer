Crotalaria pervillei

Crotalaria pervillei, commonly known as the Perville's cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces cylindrical, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions.

Crotalaria pervillei is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
