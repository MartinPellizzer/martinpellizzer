Cynorkis henrici

Cynorkis henrici, commonly known as the Henry's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, intricate flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicately protected species in many regions.
