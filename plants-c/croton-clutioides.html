Croton clutioides

Croton clutioides, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red, yellow, or even purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a garden plant in warm climates.
