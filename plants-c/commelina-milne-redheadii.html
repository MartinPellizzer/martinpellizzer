Commelina milne-redheadii

Commelina milne-redheadii, commonly known as the redhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often surrounded by bright blue or white bracts. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy surface, aiding in water retention.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
