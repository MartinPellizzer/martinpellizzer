Coincya nivalis

Coincya nivalis, commonly known as the snowflake plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its frost-resistant nature.
