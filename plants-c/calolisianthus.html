Calolisianthus

Calolisianthus, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, flame-like flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to 10 meters in height. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a favorite in gardens and public spaces.
