Corrigiola litoralis var. perennans

Corrigiola litoralis var. perennans, commonly known as sea sandwort, is a perennial flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found in dunes and rocky shores. The plant features small, fleshy leaves and produces clusters of white or pink flowers. It is adapted to withstand harsh maritime conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.

Its ecological importance includes stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
