Cleisostoma aspersum

Cleisostoma aspersum, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, anchoring itself with its rhizomes and absorbing moisture from the air. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their intricate, spurred petals.

Cleisostoma aspersum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
