Conopharyngia pachysiphon

Conopharyngia pachysiphon, commonly known as the thick-stemmed conopharyngia, is a unique plant species characterized by its robust, woody stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the family Conopharyngiaceae and is native to specific regions in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant exhibits a distinctive growth pattern with a single trunk that can reach significant height, often branching near the base.
