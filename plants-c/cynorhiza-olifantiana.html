Cynorhiza olifantiana

Cynorhiza olifantiana, commonly known as the elephant's foot, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its massive, fleshy, and often bulbous root system that can grow to enormous sizes. The plant has a short, thick stem with narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss. Its flowers are small and typically green or yellow, growing in clusters at the base of the plant.

The plant is known for its ability to store water, making it highly drought-resistant.
