Ceropegia officinalis subsp. delaetiana

Ceropegia officinalis subsp.

delaetiana, commonly known as the velvet plant or silver string plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa. It features trailing stems with tubular, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are typically borne in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
