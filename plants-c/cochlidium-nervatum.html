Cochlidium nervatum

Cochlidium nervatum, commonly known as the slender zigzag snail, is a species of air-breathing land snail.

It belongs to the family Cochlididae and is characterized by its elongated, spiral shell with distinct ridges and a pointed apex. The shell is typically pale in color, often with a faintly granular surface. This species is found in humid environments, such as forests and grasslands, where it thrives on moist substrates.

Its diet primarily consists of algae, fungi, and decaying plant matter, contributing to nutrient cycling in its ecosystem.
