Cotyledon gendjnamensis

Cotyledon gendjnamensis, commonly known as the Gendjnamensis Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted for storing water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
