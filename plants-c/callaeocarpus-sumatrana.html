Callaeocarpus sumatrana

Callaeocarpus sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
