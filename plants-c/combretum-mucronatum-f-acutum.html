Combretum mucronatum f. acutum

Combretum mucronatum f. acutum, commonly known as the sharp-pointed combretum, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves with acuminate tips. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It is often found in moist woodland and riverine habitats.

The species is notable for its ecological importance in supporting local wildlife and its potential use in traditional medicine.
