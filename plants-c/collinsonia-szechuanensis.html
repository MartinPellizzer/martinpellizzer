Collinsonia szechuanensis

Collinsonia szechuanensis, commonly known as Sichuan collinsonia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and showy blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers bloom in clusters at the top of the plant.

Collinsonia szechuanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
