Cattleya × espirito-santensis

Cattleya × espirito-santensis, commonly known as the Espirito Santo cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
