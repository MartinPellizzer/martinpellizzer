Coronilla balansae

Coronilla balansae, commonly known as the Balansae clover, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant features small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small oval-shaped leaflets.

Coronilla balansae is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
