Cactus woburnensis

Cactus woburnensis, commonly known as the Woburn cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and reduced leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with a thick, fleshy stem that stores water. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique adaptations, it is well-suited to harsh desert environments.
