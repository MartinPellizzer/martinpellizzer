Cotula fallax

Cotula fallax, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with a mat-forming habit, featuring silver-gray foliage that reflects its common name. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers, typically blooming in spring and summer. Cotula fallax thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
