Cheirinia arkansana

Cheirinia arkansana, commonly known as the Arkansas milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and prairies. It produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds.
