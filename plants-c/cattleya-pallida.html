Cattleya pallida

Cattleya pallida, commonly known as the pale cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pale pink to white, with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green and often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya pallida is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
