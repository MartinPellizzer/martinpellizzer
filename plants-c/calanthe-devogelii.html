Calanthe devogelii

Calanthe devogelii, commonly known as the Devogel's calanthe, is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
