Carex brachysandra

Carex brachysandra, commonly known as narrowleaf sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, grasses, and related plants. This species typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. Carex brachysandra plays an important ecological role by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
