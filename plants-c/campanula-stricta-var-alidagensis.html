Campanula stricta var. alidagensis

Campanula stricta var. alidagensis, commonly known as the Alidagen bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically blue to violet in color, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
