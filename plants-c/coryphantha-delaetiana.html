Coryphantha delaetiana

Coryphantha delaetiana, commonly known as the Delaet's coryphantha, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and spiny areoles. The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized plant.
