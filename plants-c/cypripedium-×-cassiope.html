Cypripedium × cassiope

Cypripedium × cassiope, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a deciduous perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white with a yellow throat. The plant has a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces a single, large, showy flower per stem. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
