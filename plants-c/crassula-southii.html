Crassula southii

Crassula southii, commonly known as the Cape Town crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
