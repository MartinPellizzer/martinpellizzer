Cleome monophylla var. madagascariensis

Cleome monophylla var. madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar cleome, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cleome monophylla var. madagascariensis is a member of the Cleomaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with similar morphological features.
