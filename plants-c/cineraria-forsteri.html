Cineraria forsteri

Cineraria forsteri, commonly known as the desert cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a fuzzy texture, adapted to the arid environment.

Cineraria forsteri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience to dry conditions.
