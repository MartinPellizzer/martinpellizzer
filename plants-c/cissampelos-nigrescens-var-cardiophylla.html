Cissampelos nigrescens var. cardiophylla

Cissampelos nigrescens var. cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaf moon vine, is a climbing perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
