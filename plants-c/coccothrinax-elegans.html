Coccothrinax elegans

Coccothrinax elegans, commonly known as the elegant palm, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and a crown of pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by yellowish fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
