Cyrtanthus smithianus

Cyrtanthus smithianus, commonly known as the Smith's bell-shaped iris, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It produces narrow, grass-like leaves and is known for its ability to bloom in late spring or early summer.

Cyrtanthus smithianus is a popular choice for rock gardens and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
