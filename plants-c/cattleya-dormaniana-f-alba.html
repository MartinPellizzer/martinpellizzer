Cattleya dormaniana f. alba

Cattleya dormaniana f. alba, commonly known as the white form of the Dorman's cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking white petals and vibrant yellow lip.

Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It produces large, showy flowers that bloom once or twice a year, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. The plant prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture during its growing season.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and valuable addition to both greenhouse and garden collections.
