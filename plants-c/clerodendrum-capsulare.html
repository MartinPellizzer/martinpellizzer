Clerodendrum capsulare

Clerodendrum capsulare, commonly known as the red-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive aromatic scent. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Clerodendrum capsulare is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
