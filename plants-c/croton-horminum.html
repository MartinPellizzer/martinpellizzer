Croton horminum

Croton horminum, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
