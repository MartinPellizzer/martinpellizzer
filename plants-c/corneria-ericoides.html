Corneria ericoides

Corneria ericoides, commonly known as the dwarf heath.

It is a low-growing shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Its flowers are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to coastal climates with high salinity and strong winds.
