Coccinia polyantha

Coccinia polyantha, commonly known as the purple leaf morning glory, is a tropical climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that range from green to purple in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the morning. Its fruit is a round, fleshy berry that turns bright orange when mature.

Coccinia polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
