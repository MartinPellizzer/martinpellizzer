Capparis hastigera var. obcordata

Capparis hastigera var. obcordata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved caper, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its thorny stems and aromatic, often yellowish flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and obcordate, giving the plant its specific epithet. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
