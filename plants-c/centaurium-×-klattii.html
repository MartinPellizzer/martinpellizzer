Centaurium × klattii

Centaurium × klattii, commonly known as the hybrid centaury, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its whorled arrangement of small, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically reaches heights of 10–30 cm and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Centaurium × klattii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
