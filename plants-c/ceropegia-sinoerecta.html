Ceropegia sinoerecta

Ceropegia sinoerecta, commonly known as the Chinese hooded ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often branched and covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces unique, hooded flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
