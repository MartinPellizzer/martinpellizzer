Cyphia zeyheriana var. eckloniana

Cyphia zeyheriana var. eckloniana, commonly known as the Ecklon's cyphia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to succulent collections.
