Cattleya × behrensiana

Cattleya × behrensiana, commonly known as the Behrensiana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants rather than in soil.

This orchid is highly valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
