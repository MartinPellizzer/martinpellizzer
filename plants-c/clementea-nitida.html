Clementea nitida

Clementea nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf clementine, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit, a type of citrus, is small and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Clementea nitida is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
