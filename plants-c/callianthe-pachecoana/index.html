Callianthe Pachecoana

Callianthe pachecoana is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of Ecuador. It belongs to the orchid family and is known for its unique and striking floral structure. This species was first discovered in the early 20th century and has since been the subject of much botanical interest. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this beautiful and delicate species.
