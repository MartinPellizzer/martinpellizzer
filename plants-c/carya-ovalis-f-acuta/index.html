Carya Ovalis F. Acuta

Carya ovalis f. acuta is a unique variant of the hickory tree known for its distinctive, sharp-pointed leaves. This plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mixed forests across the eastern United States. Its acutely pointed foliage sets it apart from other hickory species, making it easily recognizable. While it shares many traits with its parent species, its morphology showcases a fascinating adaptation to its environment. This plant is not only ecologically significant but also holds value in horticultural and ornamental gardening.
