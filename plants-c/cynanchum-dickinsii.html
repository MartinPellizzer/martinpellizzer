Cynanchum dickinsii

Cynanchum dickinsii, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed vine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its milky sap contains toxic compounds, which can cause skin irritation.
