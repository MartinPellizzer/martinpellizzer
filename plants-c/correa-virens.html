Correa virens

Correa virens, commonly known as the green correa, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The leaves are ovate and glossy, providing a lush backdrop for the vibrant yellow-orange blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract native pollinators and its adaptability to various climates.
