Cyperus atroferrugineus

Cyperus atroferrugineus, commonly known as black rust sedgeland, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Africa and is characterized by its dark brown to blackish stems and leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along waterways. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Cyperus atroferrugineus is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and improve water quality.
