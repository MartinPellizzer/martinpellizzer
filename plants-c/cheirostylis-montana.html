Cheirostylis montana

Cheirostylis montana, commonly known as the mountain cheirostylis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in montane forests. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cheirostylis montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
