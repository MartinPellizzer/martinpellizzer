Chlorophytum pachyrrhizum

Chlorophytum pachyrrhizum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a yellowish margin. The plant is well-known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its minimal water requirements. It produces small, white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season.

Chlorophytum pachyrrhizum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
