Calliandra serjanioides

Calliandra serjanioides, commonly known as the pink calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, deciduous tree or shrub that produces clusters of bright pink, fluffy flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

The flowers are not only visually striking but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
