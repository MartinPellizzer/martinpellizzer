Cyathea miquelii

Cyathea miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, contributing to its dramatic appearance.

Cyathea miquelii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
