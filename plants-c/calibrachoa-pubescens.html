Calibrachoa pubescens

Calibrachoa pubescens, commonly known as the hairy calibrachoa, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its small, vibrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple.
