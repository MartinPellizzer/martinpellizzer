Cerastium bialynickii

Cerastium bialynickii, commonly known as the Bialynicki's mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
