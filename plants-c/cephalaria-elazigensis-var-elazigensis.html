Cephalaria elazigensis var. elazigensis

Cephalaria elazigensis var. elazigensis, commonly known as the Elazig's cephalaria, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy composite flowers. The plant features slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowering period typically occurs in late summer, producing yellow to orange disc flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique morphological traits.
