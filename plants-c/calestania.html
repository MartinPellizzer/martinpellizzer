Calestania

Calestania, commonly known as the silverleaf calestania, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Calestania is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought-resistant properties.
