Centaurea arabica

Centaurea arabica, commonly known as the Arabian star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking purple to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has spiny leaves and produces small, hard seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and rocky soils.

Centaurea arabica is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
