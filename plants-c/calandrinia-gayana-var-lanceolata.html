Calandrinia gayana var. lanceolata

Calandrinia gayana var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf purslane, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent leaves and stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped, hence the variety name, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often found in disturbed areas and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
