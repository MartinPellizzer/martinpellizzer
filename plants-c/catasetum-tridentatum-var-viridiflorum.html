Catasetum tridentatum var. viridiflorum

Catasetum tridentatum var. viridiflorum, commonly known as the green-flowered catasetum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant green flowers, which contrast strikingly with its dark green foliage. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, often fragrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and fragrance.
