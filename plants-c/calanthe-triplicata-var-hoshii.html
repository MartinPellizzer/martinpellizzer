Calanthe triplicata var. hoshii

Calanthe triplicata var. hoshii, commonly known as the Hoshii calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
