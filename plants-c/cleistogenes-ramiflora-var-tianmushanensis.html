Cleistogenes ramiflora var. tianmushanensis

Cleistogenes ramiflora var. tianmushanensis, commonly known as the Tianmushan false brome, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain florets with lemma-like structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is notable for its ecological significance in maintaining soil stability in its native habitat.
