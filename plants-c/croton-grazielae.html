Croton grazielae

Croton grazielae, commonly known as the Grace's croton, is a striking tropical plant native to the lowland rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often striking members. Croton grazielae typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters in its natural habitat.
