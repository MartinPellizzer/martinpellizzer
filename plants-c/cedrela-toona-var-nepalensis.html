Cedrela toona var. nepalensis

Cedrela toona var. nepalensis, commonly known as Nepal toona, is a deciduous tree native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other tropical hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The tree is valued for its durable wood and is used in traditional medicine and construction.
