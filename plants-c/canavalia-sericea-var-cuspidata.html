Canavalia sericea var. cuspidata

Canavalia sericea var. cuspidata, commonly known as the silky cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with silky, hair-covered surfaces, and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated pods. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in agricultural settings. Its seeds are edible and often used in various cuisines.

The variety *cuspidata* is distinguished by its pointed leaflets and robust growth habit.
