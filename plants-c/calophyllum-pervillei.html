Calophyllum pervillei

Calophyllum pervillei, commonly known as the Perville's calophyllum, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Calophyllaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, and its bark is often used in traditional medicine.

Calophyllum pervillei thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
