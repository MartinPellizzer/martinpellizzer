Cucubalus stellatus

Cucubalus stellatus, commonly known as the star cucumber, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It produces small, star-shaped fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn yellow or orange when mature. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and is often found growing along fences or in shaded areas. Its fruit is edible when fully ripe and has a mild, tangy flavor.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
