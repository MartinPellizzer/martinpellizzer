Ceratostema ellipticum

Ceratostema ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic horned bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features distinctive, helmet-shaped flowers that are typically blue to violet in color, with a prominent central horn-like projection. The plant has elliptical, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Ceratostema ellipticum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
