Chaptalia confinis

Chaptalia confinis, commonly known as the narrowleaf chaptalia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in the spring, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits known as cypselae.
