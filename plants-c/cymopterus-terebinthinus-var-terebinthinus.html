Cymopterus terebinthinus var. terebinthinus

Cymopterus terebinthinus var. terebinthinus, commonly known as the California buckwheat, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the western United States.

It features slender, branched stems that often grow in dense clusters, and it produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with numerous narrow leaflets, and its flowers are typically arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
