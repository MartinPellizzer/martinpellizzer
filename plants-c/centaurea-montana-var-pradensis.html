Centaurea montana var. pradensis

Centaurea montana var. pradensis, commonly known as the alpine blueweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and slender, upright stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
