Chlorophytum longifolium var. aureum

Chlorophytum longifolium var. aureum, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail or Golden Edge Spider Plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often feature a striking yellow or gold border, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by delicate, grass-like seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

This hardy plant is easy to care for and is often used in hanging baskets or as a trailing plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
