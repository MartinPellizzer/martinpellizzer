Cyperus pluristamineus

Cyperus pluristamineus, commonly known as the broadleaf flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Cyperus pluristamineus is a perennial herb that can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in wetland areas.
