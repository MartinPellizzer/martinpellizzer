Campomanesia dardano-limai

Campomanesia dardano-limai, commonly known as the Brazilian wax apple or dardano-limai.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae, native to Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and small, round, yellow or orange fruits that resemble apples. The leaves are glossy and elliptical, with a smooth surface.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its edible fruits, which have a sweet, tangy flavor.
