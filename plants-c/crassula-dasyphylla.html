Crassula dasyphylla

Crassula dasyphylla, commonly known as the Woolly Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The leaves are typically green with a bluish tint and are arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.
