Cliffortia odorata var. hypoleuca

Cliffortia odorata var. hypoleuca, commonly known as the white-flowered cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it withstands strong winds and variable temperatures.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance and pleasant scent.
