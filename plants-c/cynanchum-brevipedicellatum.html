Cynanchum brevipedicellatum

Cynanchum brevipedicellatum, commonly known as the short-stemmed dogbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Asia.

Its fruit is a follicle that splits open to release seeds with silky appendages, aiding in wind dispersal.

Cynanchum brevipedicellatum is often found in moist meadows and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial to full sunlight.
