Cattleya grosvenorii

Cattleya grosvenorii, commonly known as the Grosvenor Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with multiple blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in horticulture.
