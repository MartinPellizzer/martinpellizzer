Cattleya trianae var. osmannii

Cattleya trianae var. osmannii, commonly known as the Osmannii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant mix of pink, white, and purple hues. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a parent in many modern orchid hybrids.
