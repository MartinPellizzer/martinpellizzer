Cnidoscolus lombardii

Cnidoscolus lombardii, commonly known as the "Lombardii arrowhead," is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, spiny fruits. Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

However, the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
