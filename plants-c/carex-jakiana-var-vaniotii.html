Carex jakiana var. vaniotii

Carex jakiana var. vaniotii, commonly known as the Vaniot's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants commonly found in wet or moist environments. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for wetland gardens and bog landscapes.
