Ctenium cirrosum

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is found in coastal areas and dunes. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
