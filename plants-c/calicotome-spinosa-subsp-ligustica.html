Calicotome spinosa subsp. ligustica

Calicotome spinosa subsp.

ligustica, commonly known as the Ligustic Bush or Ligustic Caltrop, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant produces small, round, spiny fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
