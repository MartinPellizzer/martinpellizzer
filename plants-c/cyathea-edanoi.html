Cyathea edanoi

Cyathea edanoi, commonly known as the Edanoi tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant exhibits a unique growth habit, with a well-developed root system that supports its upright structure. Its fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to several meters.

Cyathea edanoi is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests.
