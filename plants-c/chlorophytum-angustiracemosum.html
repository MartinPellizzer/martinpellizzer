Chlorophytum angustiracemosum

Chlorophytum angustiracemosum, commonly known as the narrow-branch spider plant, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with white stripes, and it produces small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its ability to form offsets, which can be easily propagated to create new plants. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value and ease of care contribute to its widespread cultivation.
