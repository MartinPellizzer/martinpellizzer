Camellia viridicalyx var. viridicalyx

Camellia viridicalyx var. viridicalyx, commonly known as the Emerald Beauty camellia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is evergreen and thrives in partial shade with well-drained, acidic soil. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.

Camellias like this variety are also appreciated for their ability to bloom in late winter to early spring.
