Chloris leptostachya var. intermedia

Chloris leptostachya var. intermedia, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces inflorescences of spikelets that are typically arranged in a loose, open panicle. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This variety is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions and is used in both ecological and ornamental horticulture.
