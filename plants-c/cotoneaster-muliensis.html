Cotoneaster muliensis

Cotoneaster muliensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits in the fall. Cotoneaster muliensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
