Cirsium spitzneri

Cirsium spitzneri, commonly known as Spitzner's thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
