Crassula fragilis var. suborbicularis

Crassula fragilis var. suborbicularis, commonly known as the Orange Jewel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with a reddish tinge under direct sunlight. The leaves are typically orbicular in shape, giving the plant its varietal name. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

This species is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.
