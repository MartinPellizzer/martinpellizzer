Chlorophytum melanocarpum

Chlorophytum melanocarpum, commonly known as the Moon Cuss or Black Nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are edible but have a mild, sweet taste. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its attractive foliage and unique fruit, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
