Cnicus araneosus

Cnicus araneosus, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features wiry, branching stems covered in small, green, ovate leaves. Its flowers are small, yellow, and borne in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
