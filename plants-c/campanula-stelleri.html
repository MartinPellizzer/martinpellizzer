Campanula stelleri

Campanula stelleri, commonly known as Steller's bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

Campanula stelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
