Cyperus svensonii

Cyperus svensonii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Cyperus svensonii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
