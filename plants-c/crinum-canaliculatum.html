Crinum canaliculatum

Crinum canaliculatum, commonly known as the narrow-channelled crinum, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Crinum canaliculatum is also noted for its strong fragrance, which attracts pollinators such as bees and moths.
