Conradia pedunculosa

Conradia pedunculosa, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

Conradia pedunculosa is often found in open, sunny habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
