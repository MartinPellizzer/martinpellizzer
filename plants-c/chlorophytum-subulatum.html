Chlorophytum subulatum

Chlorophytum subulatum, commonly known as the needleleaf spider plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, which help it store water efficiently. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on thin stems, which are followed by delicate, grass-like seed heads. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant.
