Calystegia grandiflora

Calystegia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered bindweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a vigorous climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink.
