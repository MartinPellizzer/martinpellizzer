Comparettia falcata f. alba

Comparettia falcata f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered comparettia, is a hybrid form of the species Comparettia falcata.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its elegant, white petals and a compact, pseudobulbous structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often grown as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
