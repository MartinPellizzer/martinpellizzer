Cymodocea angustata

Cymodocea angustata, commonly known as slender turtle grass, is a seagrass species native to tropical and subtropical coastal regions.

It typically grows in shallow, sandy or muddy substrates in marine environments. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often curved and arranged in dense underwater mats. It plays a crucial role in stabilizing sediments and providing habitat for marine life.

Cymodocea angustata is also important in carbon sequestration and coastal protection.
