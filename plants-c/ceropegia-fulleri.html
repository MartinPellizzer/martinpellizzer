Ceropegia fulleri

Ceropegia fulleri, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia fulleri is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
