Cotyledon coutinhoi

Cotyledon coutinhoi, commonly known as the orange cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bright yellow or orange in color, with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, pale green flowers during the summer months. It is a low-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
