Citrus pachiderma

Citrus pachiderma, commonly known as the giant citron.

This plant is a large, evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It produces large, yellow-orange fruits that are typically used for their aromatic rinds rather than their juice. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape.

The plant is known for its strong citrus scent and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
