Calliandra compacta

Calliandra compacta, commonly known as the compact calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, branched structure and clusters of bright red or pink flowers. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Calliandra compacta is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
