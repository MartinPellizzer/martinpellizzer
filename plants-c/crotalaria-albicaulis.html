Crotalaria albicaulis

Crotalaria albicaulis, commonly known as the white-stemmed crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Crotalaria albicaulis is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
