Cyathea divergens var. divergens

Cyathea divergens var. divergens, commonly known as the broad-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately divided fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizome system supports the growth of multiple fronds, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The plant plays an important ecological role in tropical rainforests, providing habitat and contributing to forest structure.
