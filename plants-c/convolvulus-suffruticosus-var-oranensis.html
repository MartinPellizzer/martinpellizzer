Convolvulus suffruticosus var. oranensis

Convolvulus suffruticosus var. oranensis, commonly known as the Algerian bindweed, is a perennial shrub native to North Africa.

It features trailing stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to harsh conditions, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
