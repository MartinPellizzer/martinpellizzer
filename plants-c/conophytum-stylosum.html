Conophytum stylosum

Conophytum stylosum, commonly known as the "Gardeners' Delight," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. Conophytum stylosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
