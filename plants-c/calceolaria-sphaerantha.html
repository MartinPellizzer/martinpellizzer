Calceolaria sphaerantha

Calceolaria sphaerantha, commonly known as the globe calceolaria, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, urn-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces small, rounded leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its compact appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking blooms.

Calceolaria sphaerantha is a member of the family Calceolariaceae and is known for its delicate and ephemeral flowering season.
