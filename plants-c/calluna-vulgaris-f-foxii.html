Calluna vulgaris f. foxii

Calluna vulgaris f. foxii, commonly known as the Foxii heath, is a variant of the common heather.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub characterized by its dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing vivid pink to red hues. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and moorlands.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for landscaping purposes.
