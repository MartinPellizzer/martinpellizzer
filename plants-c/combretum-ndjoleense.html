Combretum ndjoleense

Combretum ndjoleense, commonly known as the Cameroon flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the tropical rainforests of Cameroon.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its bark is smooth and pale in color.
