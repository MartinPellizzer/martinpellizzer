Calceolaria gnidiifolia

Calceolaria gnidiifolia, commonly known as the Cape Verde slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically bright orange or red in color. The plant produces small, hairy leaves that are adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Calceolaria gnidiifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a member of the Calceolaria genus, which includes several other species with similar floral structures.
