Cereus dayamii

Cereus dayamii, commonly known as the Dayami Cereus, is a species of cactus native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, showy flowers that bloom at night. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and have a subtle fragrance.

Cereus dayamii is a popular choice for collectors due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
