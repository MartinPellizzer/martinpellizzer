Connarus culionensis var. culionensis

Connarus culionensis var. culionensis, commonly known as the Culion tree, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in lowland and montane rainforests. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape with a smooth margin. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
