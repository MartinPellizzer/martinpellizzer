Cytisus scoparius f. andreanus

Cytisus scoparius f. andreanus, commonly known as the dwarf yellow bush, is a compact, shrub-like form of the common broom.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant splash of color to gardens. The plant has slender, thornless stems and small, leathery leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
