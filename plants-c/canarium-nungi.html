Canarium nungi

Canarium nungi, commonly known as the Nungi candlenut, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
