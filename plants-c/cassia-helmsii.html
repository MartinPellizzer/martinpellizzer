Cassia helmsii

Cassia helmsii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cassia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in cylindrical spikes. Cassia helmsii is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
