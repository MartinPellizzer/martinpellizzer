Ceratoxalis stricta

Ceratoxalis stricta, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features showy, red-orange inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental in gardens.

Its rapid growth and striking floral display make it a notable addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
