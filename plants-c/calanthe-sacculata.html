Calanthe sacculata

Calanthe sacculata, commonly known as the sacculate calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and possesses lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive sac-like structure at the base of the petals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe sacculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique floral morphology.
