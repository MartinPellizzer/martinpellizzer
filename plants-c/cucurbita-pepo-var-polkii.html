Cucurbita pepo var. polkii

Cucurbita pepo var. polkii, commonly known as the Turk's Turban, is a cultivated variety of the common gourd.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, turban-shaped fruits that range in color from pale green to deep yellow. The plant is a vine that produces large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun and well-drained soil.

This variety is often grown for its ornamental value and can also be used in culinary applications.
