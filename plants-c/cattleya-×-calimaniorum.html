Cattleya × calimaniorum

Cattleya × calimaniorum, commonly known as the Calimaniorum Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant mix of pink, white, and purple hues. The plant produces thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants rather than in soil, and it requires high humidity and good air circulation.

This orchid is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
