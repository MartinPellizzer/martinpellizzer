Cynodon barberi f. longifolia

Cynodon barberi f. longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved carpetgrass, is a variant of the species Cynodon barberi, which belongs to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length, giving it a distinctive appearance.
