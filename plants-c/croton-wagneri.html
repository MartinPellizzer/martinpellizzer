Croton wagneri

Croton wagneri, commonly known as the Wagner's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Croton wagneri is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant foliage and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
