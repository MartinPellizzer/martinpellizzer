Cistus hybridus

Cistus hybridus, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Cistus hybridus is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
