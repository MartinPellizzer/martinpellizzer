Croton haumanianus

Croton haumanianus, commonly known as the Hauman's croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, multicolored foliage. The plant typically features large, palmately lobed leaves that display a range of hues, including green, red, and yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Croton haumanianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant and unique leaf patterns.
