Cryptomeria japonica var. compacta

Cryptomeria japonica var. compacta, commonly known as the Japanese cedar dwarf form, is a compact, slow-growing evergreen conifer.

It features dense, pyramidal foliage with needle-like leaves that are short and scale-like. The plant produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a specimen plant.

Its ornamental value lies in its compact form and year-round visual interest.
