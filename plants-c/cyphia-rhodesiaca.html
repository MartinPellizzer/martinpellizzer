Cyphia rhodesiaca

Cyphia rhodesiaca, commonly known as the Rhodesian cyphia, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. Its flowers bloom in clusters, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cyphia rhodesiaca thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.
