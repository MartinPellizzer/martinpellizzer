Commelina equinoctialis

Commelina equinoctialis, commonly known as the equinox flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and produces small, blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
