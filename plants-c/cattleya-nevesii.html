Cattleya nevesii

Cattleya nevesii, commonly known as the Neves' orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features long, arching inflorescences and is known for its fragrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Cattleya nevesii is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
