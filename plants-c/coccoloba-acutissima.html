Coccoloba acutissima

Coccoloba acutissima, commonly known as the sharpleaf glorybower, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in panicles, blooming during the dry season.
