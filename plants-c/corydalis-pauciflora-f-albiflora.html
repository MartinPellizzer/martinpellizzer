Corydalis pauciflora f. albiflora

Corydalis pauciflora f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered corydalis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its flowers have spurred petals, a distinctive feature of the genus Corydalis.
