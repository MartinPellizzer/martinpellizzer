Cyphostemma cyphopetalum subsp. craterum

Cyphostemma cyphopetalum subsp.

craterum, commonly known as the mountain cyphostemma, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, adapted to arid environments.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

It is often found growing in rocky outcrops and mountainous regions.
