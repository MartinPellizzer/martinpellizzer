Croton nitrariifolius var. divaricatus

Croton nitrariifolius var. divaricatus, commonly known as the Variegated Croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, yellow, and white. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
