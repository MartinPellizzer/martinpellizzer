Costus sextus

Costus sextus, commonly known as the red ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features tall, erect stems with narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color. Its most striking feature is its clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which resemble those of ginger.
