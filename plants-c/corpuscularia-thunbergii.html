Corpuscularia thunbergii

Corpuscularia thunbergii, commonly known as the Japanese spider plant, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. Its ability to propagate through both seeds and offsets makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
