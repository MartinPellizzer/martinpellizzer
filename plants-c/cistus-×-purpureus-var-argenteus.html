Cistus × purpureus var. argenteus

Cistus × purpureus var. argenteus, commonly known as silver cistus, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silvery-white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, pinkish-purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal uses.
