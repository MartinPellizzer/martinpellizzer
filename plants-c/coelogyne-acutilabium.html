Coelogyne acutilabium

Coelogyne acutilabium, commonly known as the sharp-labied orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, pendant-like pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne acutilabium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
