Commelina multicaulis

Commelina multicaulis, commonly known as the spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a waxy texture.
