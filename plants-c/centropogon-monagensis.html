Centropogon monagensis

Centropogon monagensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers.
