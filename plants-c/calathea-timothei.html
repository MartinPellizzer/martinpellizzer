Calathea timothei

Calathea timothei, commonly known as the Midnight Beauty, is a striking tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its deep green, velvety leaves with intricate, contrasting patterns that resemble a mosaic. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its unique foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
