Cotoneaster sherriffii

Cotoneaster sherriffii, commonly known as Sherriff's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spreading growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the autumn. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

Cotoneaster sherriffii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
