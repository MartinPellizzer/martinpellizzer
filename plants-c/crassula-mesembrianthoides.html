Crassula mesembrianthoides

Crassula mesembrianthoides, commonly known as the Blue Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, which help it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
