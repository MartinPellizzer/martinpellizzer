Chloris anisopoda

Chloris anisopoda, commonly known as slender grass or narrowleaf grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Chloris anisopoda is a hardy plant that is adaptable to various environmental conditions.
