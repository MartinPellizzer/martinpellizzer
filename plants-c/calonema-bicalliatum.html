Calonema bicalliatum

Calonema bicalliatum, commonly known as the two-lobed calonema, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by its fleshy leaves and adaptations to arid environments. Calonema bicalliatum is prized for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
