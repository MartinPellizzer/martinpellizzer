Clerodendrum fallax

Clerodendrum fallax, commonly known as the red-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and emits a mild, pleasant fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum fallax is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
