Clianthus dampieri var. tricolor

Clianthus dampieri var. tricolor, commonly known as the tricolor kowhai, is a striking flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tri-colored flowers that range from yellow to red and orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves, and it thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

The tricolor kowhai is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
