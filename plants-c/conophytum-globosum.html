Conophytum globosum

Conophytum globosum, commonly known as the globe-shaped stone plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, rounded leaves that form a dense, compact rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
