Ciconium acetosellum

Ciconium acetosellum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas where it can become a weed.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction make it a notable species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
