Cynanchum obscurum

Cynanchum obscurum, commonly known as dark milkweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes milkweeds known for their milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist woodland areas and has opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually greenish-white in color.

The plant is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally by Native American tribes.
