Crassula pyramidalis

Crassula pyramidalis, commonly known as the Pyramidal Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, shrub-like growth habit with dense, fleshy leaves arranged in a pyramid shape. The leaves are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
