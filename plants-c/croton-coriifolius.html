Croton coriifolius

Croton coriifolius, commonly known as the fragrant croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by orange or red fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton coriifolius is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
