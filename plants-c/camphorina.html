Camphorina

Camphorina, commonly known as the camphor tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of India, China, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and fragrant flowers, which emit a strong camphor scent. Its bark and leaves contain essential oils rich in camphor, making it valuable in traditional medicine and perfumery.

Camphorina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
