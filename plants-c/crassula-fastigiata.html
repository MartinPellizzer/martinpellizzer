Crassula fastigiata

Crassula fastigiata, commonly known as the "Cushion Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded growth habit with dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Crassula fastigiata thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance.
