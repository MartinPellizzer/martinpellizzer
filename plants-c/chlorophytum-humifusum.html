Chlorophytum humifusum

Chlorophytum humifusum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems that produce clusters of small, white flowers followed by bright green, sword-shaped leaves. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its rhizomes allow it to spread easily, forming dense mats of foliage.

Chlorophytum humifusum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
