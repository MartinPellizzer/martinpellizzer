Cymbidium hengbungense

Cymbidium hengbungense, commonly known as the Hengbung orchid, is a species native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan province.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. The plant produces tall, upright flower spikes that bear multiple fragrant, lance-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its leaves are broad, fleshy, and arranged in two rows, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cymbidium hengbungense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
