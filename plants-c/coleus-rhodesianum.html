Coleus rhodesianum

Coleus rhodesianum, commonly known as the African shield leaf.

It is a member of the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is native to southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of green, silver, and purple. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires well-draining soil.

Coleus rhodesianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its foliage rather than its flowers.
