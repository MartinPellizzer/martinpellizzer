Centaurea pseudomicroptilon

Centaurea pseudomicroptilon, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in composite heads.
