Cotyledon purpurea

Cotyledon purpurea, commonly known as the purple leaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically purple or reddish in color, with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Cotyledon purpurea is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
