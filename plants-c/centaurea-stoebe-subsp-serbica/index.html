Centaurea Stoebe Subsp. Serbica

Centaurea stoebe subsp. serbica is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads. This subspecies is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is characterized by its purple-blue flowers and narrow, hairy leaves. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems.
