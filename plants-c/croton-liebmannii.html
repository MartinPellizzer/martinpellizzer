Croton liebmannii

Croton liebmannii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, colorful leaves that often display vibrant shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown as a decorative plant in gardens and landscapes.
