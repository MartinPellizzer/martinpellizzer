Cyclophyllum cardiocarpum

Cyclophyllum cardiocarpum, commonly known as the heart-fruit cyclophyllum, is a unique plant species belonging to the family Cyclophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped fruits, which are typically green when mature and contain a single, large seed. The plant is native to certain tropical regions and thrives in humid, warm climates. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface.

The botanical features of Cyclophyllum cardiocarpum make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany and plant morphology.
