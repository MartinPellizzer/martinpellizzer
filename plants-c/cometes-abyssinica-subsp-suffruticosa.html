Cometes abyssinica subsp. suffruticosa

Cometes abyssinica subsp.

suffruticosa, commonly known as the Abyssinian comet, is a shrub-like plant native to Ethiopia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with small, narrow leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers.

The plant thrives in high-altitude regions with cool, moist conditions. It is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain, where it adapts to poor soil conditions.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for ecological and horticultural study.
