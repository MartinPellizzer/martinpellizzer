Chrysanthemum Semenovii

Chrysanthemum semenovii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. Known for its vibrant yellow blooms and compact growth habit, it stands out among other chrysanthemum species. This plant thrives in cool, well-drained soils and requires careful attention to light and moisture levels. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a prized addition to botanical collections. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Chrysanthemum semenovii is now considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
