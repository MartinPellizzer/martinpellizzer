Croton crozophoroides

Croton crozophoroides, commonly known as the red croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from green to red and orange hues. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton crozophoroides is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
