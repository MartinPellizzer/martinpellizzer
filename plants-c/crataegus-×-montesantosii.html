Crataegus × montesantosii

Crataegus × montesantosii, commonly known as the Montesanto hawthorn, is a hybrid shrub or small tree native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
