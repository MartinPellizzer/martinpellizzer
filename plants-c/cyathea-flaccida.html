Cyathea flaccida

Cyathea flaccida, commonly known as the flaccid tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. Cyathea flaccida thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its slow growth and unique appearance make it a popular ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
