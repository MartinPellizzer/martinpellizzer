Cynodon dactylon f. villosus

Cynodon dactylon f. villosus, commonly known as hairy Bermuda grass, is a variant of the common Bermuda grass species.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its coarse, hairy texture and rhizomatous growth habit. The plant is native to Africa and has been naturalized in many warm climates around the world. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for lawns and erosion control.

Its adaptability and rapid growth rate contribute to its widespread use in landscaping and agricultural settings.
