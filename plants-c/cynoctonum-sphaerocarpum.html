Cynoctonum sphaerocarpum

Cynoctonum sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the globe-fruit, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small shrub that produces distinctive spherical fruits, which are yellow or orange in color when mature. The plant's leaves are elliptical and arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are small and white, often clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is notable for its hard, leathery exterior and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
