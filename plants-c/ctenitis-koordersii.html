Ctenitis koordersii

Ctenitis koordersii, commonly known as the Koorders' tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. Ctenitis koordersii is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
