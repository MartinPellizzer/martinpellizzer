Centella Uniflora

Centella uniflora is a lesser-known member of the Apiaceae family known for its delicate appearance and medicinal properties. This small herbaceous plant is native to parts of Asia and is often found growing in moist, shaded environments. Its unique structure features a single flower per umbel, giving it a distinctive look that sets it apart from other similar species. Despite its modest size, Centella uniflora has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its potential health benefits continue to attract interest from botanists and herbalists alike.
