Clusianthemum densiflorum

Clusianthemum densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower clusia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, white or yellow flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It belongs to the family Clusiaceae, which includes other plants with similar adaptive features.

The denseflower clusia is also valued for its air-purifying properties and resilience in indoor environments.
