Cyperus Macrocarpus

Cyperus macrocarpus is a unique aquatic plant known for its striking, spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in shallow waters or moist soils. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits. Its cylindrical stems and clustered flowers make it a popular choice for pond and garden landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful management to prevent overgrowth in natural habitats.
