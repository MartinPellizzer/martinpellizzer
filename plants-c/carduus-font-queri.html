Carduus font-queri

Carduus font-queri, commonly known as the French thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in dry, rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads that resemble those of thistles. It produces seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in suitable habitats.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant in gardens.
