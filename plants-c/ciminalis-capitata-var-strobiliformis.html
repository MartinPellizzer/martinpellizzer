Ciminalis capitata var. strobiliformis

Ciminalis capitata var. strobiliformis, commonly known as the spiked ciminalis, is a unique variant of the ciminalis species.

It is characterized by its dense, cone-like inflorescence, which gives it the species name *strobiliformis*. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
