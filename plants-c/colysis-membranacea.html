Colysis membranacea

Colysis membranacea, commonly known as the membrane leaf plant, is a flowering plant species native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that give it a unique visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
