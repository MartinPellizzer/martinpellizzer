Cestrum eriochiton

Cestrum eriochiton, commonly known as the silver bell or white cestrum, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to Central America and features showy, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.
