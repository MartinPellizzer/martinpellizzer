Cystopteris fragilis proles dentata

Cystopteris fragilis proles dentata, commonly known as the fragrant water fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to various regions in Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features intricate, finely divided leaves that are often described as lace-like due to their delicate appearance. The fronds are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a fragile texture that gives the plant its common name.
