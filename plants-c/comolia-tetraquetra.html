Comolia tetraquetra

Comolia tetraquetra, commonly known as the four-ribbed comolia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its distinctive four-ribbed leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
