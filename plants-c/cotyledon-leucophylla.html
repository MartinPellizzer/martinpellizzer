Cotyledon leucophylla

Cotyledon leucophylla, commonly known as the white-leafed cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green to white in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
