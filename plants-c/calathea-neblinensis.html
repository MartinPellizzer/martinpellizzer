Calathea neblinensis

Calathea neblinensis, commonly known as the Neblin's calathea, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate leaf patterns that often feature a combination of green, silver, and pink hues. The plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture that adds to its aesthetic appeal.

Calathea neblinensis is also known for its ability to purify the air, contributing to its status as a beneficial houseplant.
