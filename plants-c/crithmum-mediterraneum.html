Crithmum mediterraneum

Crithmum mediterraneum, commonly known as sea fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and rocky soils. The plant features aromatic, fern-like leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its root system is fibrous, and it produces small, rounded seeds.

Sea fennel is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, as well as its role in coastal ecosystems.
