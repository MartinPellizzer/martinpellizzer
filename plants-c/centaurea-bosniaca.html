Centaurea bosniaca

Centaurea bosniaca, commonly known as Bosnian starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine areas. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive central yellow disk. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Centaurea bosniaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
