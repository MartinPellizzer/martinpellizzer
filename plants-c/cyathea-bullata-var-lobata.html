Cyathea bullata var. lobata

Cyathea bullata var. lobata, commonly known as the lobate tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and longevity, often living for many decades. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive lobed shape, contributing to its ornamental value.

This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
