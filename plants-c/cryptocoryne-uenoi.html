Cryptocoryne uenoi

Cryptocoryne uenoi, commonly known as the Uenoi crypts, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating. This plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is well-suited for planted aquariums. Its rhizome system allows it to anchor itself in substrates, and it produces small, inconspicuous white flowers.

Cryptocoryne uenoi is valued for its hardiness and ability to improve water quality in aquatic environments.
