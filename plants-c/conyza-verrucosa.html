Conyza verrucosa

Conyza verrucosa, commonly known as roughleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as fields and roadsides. The plant features narrow, rough-textured leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often found in the eastern and central United States.

Conyza verrucosa is part of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
