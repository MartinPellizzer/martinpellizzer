Coleus eminii

Coleus eminii, commonly known as the eminii coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Coleus eminii prefers well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight.
