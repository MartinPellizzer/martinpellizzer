Cryptocarya stocksii

Cryptocarya stocksii, commonly known as the New Zealand native tree, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and conservation efforts.
