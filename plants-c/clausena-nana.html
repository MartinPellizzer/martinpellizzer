Clausena nana

Clausena nana, commonly known as dwarf mangosteen, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces edible, citrus-scented fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

Due to its compact size, it is well-suited for container gardening and small landscapes.
