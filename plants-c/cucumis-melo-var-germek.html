Cucumis melo var. germek

Cucumis melo var. germek, commonly known as the Persian melon, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family and is native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed rind and sweet, aromatic flesh, which is typically yellow or orange in color.
