Cissus Tweedieana

Cissus tweedieana is a unique trailing plant known for its heart-shaped leaves and ability to climb. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in warm, humid environments. This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental appeal and ease of care. Its stems can root at nodes, making it simple to propagate. Despite its beauty, it requires indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.
