Cirsium sintenisii subsp. galaticum

Cirsium sintenisii subsp.

galaticum, commonly known as the Galatic thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, such as grasslands and slopes. It has a rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem that can reach up to 1 meter in height.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
