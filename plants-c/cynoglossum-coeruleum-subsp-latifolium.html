Cynoglossum coeruleum subsp. latifolium

Cynoglossum coeruleum subsp.

latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features basal leaves that are broad and heart-shaped, with a bluish-green coloration.

The plant produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to gardens and ecological restoration projects.
