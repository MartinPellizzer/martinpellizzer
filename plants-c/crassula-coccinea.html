Crassula coccinea

Crassula coccinea, commonly known as the Ruby Crassula, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically red or pink in color, which help it store water and withstand arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners alike.
