Convolvulus nitens

Convolvulus nitens, commonly known as the slender morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 1 meter in height, featuring hairy stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple, blooming in the morning. Its seeds are encased in a fibrous, spiny capsule that disperses easily by wind.

Convolvulus nitens is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, showing adaptability to various environmental conditions.
