Caleana sullivanii

Caleana sullivanii, commonly known as the Sullivan's candleplant, is a striking orchid native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, candle-like flower spike that can reach up to 1 meter in height. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant red and orange flowers that resemble candles. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is adapted to hot, dry conditions.

Caleana sullivanii is a protected species due to its limited distribution and ecological significance.
