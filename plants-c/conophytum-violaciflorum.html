Conophytum violaciflorum

Conophytum violaciflorum, commonly known as the violet-flowered conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Conophytum violaciflorum prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.
