Cryptocoryne meeboldii

Cryptocoryne meeboldii, commonly known as the Meebold's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with subtle veins. This plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. It is a popular choice for aquariums due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.

Cryptocoryne meeboldii is also known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters.
