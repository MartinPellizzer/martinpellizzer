Cypripedium × simonii

Cypripedium × simonii, commonly known as the Chinese lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers with a pouch-like lip and a single upright spur. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces a single flower per stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
