Colchicum hantamense

Colchicum hantamense, commonly known as the Hantam colchicum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces a bulbous corm, which serves as its primary storage organ. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
