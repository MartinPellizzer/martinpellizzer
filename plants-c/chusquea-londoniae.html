Chusquea londoniae

Chusquea londoniae, commonly known as the London bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found growing in mountainous areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
