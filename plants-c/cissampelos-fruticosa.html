Cissampelos fruticosa

Cissampelos fruticosa, commonly known as the African moonvine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange to red berries that contain seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along riverbanks.
