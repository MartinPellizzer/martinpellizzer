Crotalaria lundensis

Crotalaria lundensis, commonly known as the Lund's crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Crotalaria lundensis is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, contributing to soil fertility.
