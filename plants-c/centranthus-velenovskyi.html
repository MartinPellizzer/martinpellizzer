Centranthus velenovskyi

Centranthus velenovskyi, commonly known as the Velenovsky's redflower.

It is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region, characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular red flowers. The plant typically reaches heights of 60 to 100 cm and produces clusters of bright red blooms in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and borders.
