Cynanchum minahassae

Cynanchum minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture, while its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually yellow or greenish.

Cynanchum minahassae is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
