Cristaria integerrima var. lobulata

Cristaria integerrima var. lobulata, commonly known as the lobed water shield, is a aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds and slow-moving streams. The plant features broad, floating leaves with a waxy surface and distinctive lobed margins. Its flowers are small, yellow, and borne on slender stalks above the water.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
