Cyathea lastreoides

Cyathea lastreoides, commonly known as the lastreoides tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cyathea lastreoides plays an important ecological role in tropical forests, providing habitat and contributing to biodiversity.
