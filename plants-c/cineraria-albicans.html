Cineraria albicans

Cineraria albicans, commonly known as the white cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly leaves and showy blue or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
