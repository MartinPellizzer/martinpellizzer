Convallaria majalis var. keiskei

Convallaria majalis var. keiskei, commonly known as the Keiske lily, is a rare and ornamental variant of the lily of the valley.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in early spring. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and lance-shaped, evergreen leaves that form a dense carpet. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
