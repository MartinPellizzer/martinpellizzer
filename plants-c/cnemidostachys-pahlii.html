Cnemidostachys pahlii

Cnemidostachys pahlii, commonly known as the Pahli's spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, thin petals that resemble the legs of a spider. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered together.

Due to its striking appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
