Croton moraharivensis

Croton moraharivensis, commonly known as the Madagascar croton, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored leaves. The plant features alternate, palmately lobed leaves that can vary in shape and color depending on the variety. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive seed capsules.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a decorative plant in warm climates.
