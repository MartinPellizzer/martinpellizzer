Chenopodium hybridum var. simplex

Chenopodium hybridum var. simplex, commonly known as garden spinach, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, and its seeds are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile crop for cultivation.

Its nutritional value and adaptability have made it a valuable plant in both culinary and agricultural contexts.
