Cryptocarya glaucescens var. nitida

Cryptocarya glaucescens var. nitida, commonly known as the glossy cryptocarya, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
