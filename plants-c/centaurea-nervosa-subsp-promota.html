Centaurea nervosa subsp. promota

Centaurea nervosa subsp.

promota, commonly known as the Promontory centaury, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a perennial herb with slender, branching stems and small, blue to purple flowers.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins, which are characteristic of the Centaurea genus. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical structure supports its adaptation to arid environments, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem.
