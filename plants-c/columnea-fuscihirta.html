Columnea fuscihirta

Columnea fuscihirta, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

This evergreen shrub is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It features showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
