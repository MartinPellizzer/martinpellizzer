Chenopodium × borbasii var. bernburgense

Chenopodium × borbasii var. bernburgense, commonly known as the hybrid goosefoot, is a botanical hybrid that combines traits from both Chenopodium borbasii and Chenopodium × borbasii.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its succulent, lobed leaves that often exhibit a distinctive bluish or purplish hue. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is found in temperate regions. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and its seeds are dispersed by wind.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
