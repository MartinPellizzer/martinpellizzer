Cleisostoma nugentii

Cleisostoma nugentii, commonly known as the golden cleisostoma, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, thick stems and broad, leathery leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Cleisostoma nugentii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
