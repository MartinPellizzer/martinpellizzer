Cyathea yunnanensis

Cyathea yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in montane forests. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.

The plant's unique morphology and ecological significance make it an important subject in botanical research.
