Chlorophytum molle

Chlorophytum molle, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically striped with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by fluffy, white seed heads. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and indirect sunlight.

Chlorophytum molle is also known for its ability to purify indoor air and is often used as a houseplant.
