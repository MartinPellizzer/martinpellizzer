Centaurea macrorrhiza var. boetica

Centaurea macrorrhiza var. boetica, commonly known as the Boetica thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems with spiny leaves and produces bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant has a robust root system and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
