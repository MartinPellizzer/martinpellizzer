Carthamus tenuis subsp. foliosus

Carthamus tenuis subsp.

foliosus, commonly known as the narrowleaf safflower, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with prominent, showy ray florets and tubular disk florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
