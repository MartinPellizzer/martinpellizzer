Cattleya × saint-gilles

Cattleya × saint-gilles, commonly known as the Saint-Gilles Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features tall, upright pseudobulbs and produces large, colorful blooms in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
