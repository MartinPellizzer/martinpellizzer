Cattleya × rigbyana

Cattleya × rigbyana, commonly known as the Rigbyana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink, often with a contrasting lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

The orchid is also valued for its fragrance and is often used in floral arrangements.
