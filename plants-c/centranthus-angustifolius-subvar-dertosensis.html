Centranthus angustifolius subvar. dertosensis

Centranthus angustifolius subvar. dertosensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved red campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and striking clusters of pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Gentianaceae family, valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
