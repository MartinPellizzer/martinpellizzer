Cliffortia recurvata

Cliffortia recurvata, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often growing on cliffs and slopes.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
