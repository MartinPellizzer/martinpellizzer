Cattleya rex

Cattleya rex, commonly known as the King of the Orchids, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, featuring vibrant colors such as pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and often have a waxy texture, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Cattleya rex is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
