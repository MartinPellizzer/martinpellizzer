Casuarina lucida

Casuarina lucida, commonly known as the shining she-oak, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant has needle-like leaves that are arranged in fascicles, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, yellowish flowers are borne in spikes and are followed by woody, cone-like fruits.

Casuarina lucida is known for its ability to tolerate salt and wind, making it well-suited for coastal environments.
