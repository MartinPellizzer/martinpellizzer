Croton brieyi

Croton brieyi, commonly known as the Hawaiian croton, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant features palmate leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant hues of green, red, and yellow. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Croton brieyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of Hawaii.
