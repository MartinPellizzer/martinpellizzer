Ceropegia floribundior

Ceropegia floribundior, commonly known as the flowering chain plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with opposite, ovate leaves that have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, often blooming in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia floribundior is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
