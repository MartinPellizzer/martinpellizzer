Cotyledon mucizonia f. glabra

Cotyledon mucizonia f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the growing season. Its leaves are usually smooth and have a distinctive, sometimes variegated appearance.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
