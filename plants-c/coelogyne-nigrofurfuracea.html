Coelogyne nigrofurfuracea

Coelogyne nigrofurfuracea, commonly known as the black velvet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals and sepals, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of deep red and black. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne nigrofurfuracea is a popular choice for collectors due to its ornamental value and unique coloration.
