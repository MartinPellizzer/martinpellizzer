Calycogonium bissei

Calycogonium bissei, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a climbing plant belonging to the family Menispermaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds. Botanically, it is noted for its unique morphology and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
