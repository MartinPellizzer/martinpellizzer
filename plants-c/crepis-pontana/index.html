Crepis Pontana

Crepis pontana is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads. This species is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils due to its adaptability. Its small yellow flowers bloom in the summer months. The plant is also known for its hairy stems and leaves, which help it survive in various environments.
