Crassula Ovata

Jade plant, scientifically known as Crassula ovata, is a popular and resilient succulent native to South Africa. It is cherished for its glossy, rounded leaves and ability to thrive with minimal care. Often referred to as the "money plant," it is believed to bring prosperity and good fortune to its owners. Its ease of care and adaptability make it a favorite among both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts. With its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements, the Jade plant is a timeless addition to any indoor or outdoor space.
