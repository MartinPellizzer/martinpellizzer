Cottendorfia Tillandsioides

Cottendorfia tillandsioides is a rare and unique epiphytic plant native to the cloud forests of Central America. It is known for its striking rosette-shaped leaves and delicate, star-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to moisture and light conditions. Its unusual appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Due to habitat loss, Cottendorfia tillandsioides faces threats to its survival, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
