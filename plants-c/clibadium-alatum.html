Clibadium alatum

Clibadium alatum, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged leaves and pods that contain edible seeds and beans. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its botanical features include trifoliate leaves, yellow flowers, and a fibrous root system.

The plant is also valued for its nutritional content and use in both traditional and modern agriculture.
