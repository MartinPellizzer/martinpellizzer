Clematis × jouiniana

Clematis × jouiniana, commonly known as the evergreen clematis, is a hybrid plant originating from crosses between Clematis vitalba and Clematis tetragona.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to climb using its twining stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is evergreen, with dark green, leathery leaves that provide year-round visual interest.

It thrives in partial to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
