Carduus × grenieri nothosubsp.

grenieri, commonly known as the hybrid garden thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and large, purple to pink flower heads that bloom in the summer.

The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be found in gardens and wild areas.
