Carmichaelia australis var. egmontiana

Carmichaelia australis var. egmontiana, commonly known as the Egmontia Carmichaelia, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils.
