Croton verbascoides

Croton verbascoides, commonly known as the yellow croton, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped with a rough texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Croton verbascoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
