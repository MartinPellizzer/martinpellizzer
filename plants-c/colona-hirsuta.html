Colona hirsuta

Colona hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy colona, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Colona hirsuta is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and marshy areas.
