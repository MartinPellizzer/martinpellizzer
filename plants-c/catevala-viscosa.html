Catevala viscosa

Catevala viscosa, commonly known as the sticky cat's tail, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems that produce a sticky, resinous substance. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs.

Catevala viscosa is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
