Croton thoii

Croton thoii, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
