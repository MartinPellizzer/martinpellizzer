Coleus hockii

Coleus hockii, commonly known as the Hockii coleus, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture and display shades of red, purple, and green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
