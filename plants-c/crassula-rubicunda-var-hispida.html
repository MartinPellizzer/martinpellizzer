Crassula rubicunda var. hispida

Crassula rubicunda var. hispida, commonly known as the woolly crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for succulent gardens and indoor collections.
