Crassula muscosa var. obtusifolia

Crassula muscosa var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the Woolly Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The leaves are typically blue-green in color and are arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
