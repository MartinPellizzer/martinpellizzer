Callista lucens

Callista lucens, commonly known as the shining callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are erect and often branched.

Callista lucens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
