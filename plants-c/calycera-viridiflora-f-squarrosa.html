Calycera viridiflora f. squarrosa

Calycera viridiflora f. squarrosa, commonly known as the green-flowered calycera with squarrose inflorescence, is a variant of the species Calycera viridiflora.

It is characterized by its distinctive squarrose arrangement of inflorescences, where the flower clusters are tightly curled or rolled. The plant typically features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is a herbaceous plant native to specific regions in South America.

Its botanical features make it of interest in studies of plant morphology and floral development.
