Chaetoseris cyanea var. paniculata

Chaetoseris cyanea var. paniculata, commonly known as the blueflowered chaetoseris, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small blue or purple flowers in dense, paniculate inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
