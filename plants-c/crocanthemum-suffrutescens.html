Crocanthemum suffrutescens

Crocanthemum suffrutescens, commonly known as the red sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces vibrant red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a grayish-green color.

It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
