Catasetum × lindenii

Catasetum × lindenii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers that display a wide range of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant produces specialized pollination structures called "nectaries" that secrete nectar to attract specific pollinators, primarily bees. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Catasetum × lindenii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique reproductive biology.
