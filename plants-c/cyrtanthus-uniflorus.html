Cyrtanthus uniflorus

Cyrtanthus uniflorus, commonly known as the single-flowered bell orchid, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a bell-shaped appearance. The plant produces a single flower per stem, which blooms in late summer or early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Cyrtanthus uniflorus is well-suited for rock gardens and prefers well-drained soil and full sun.
