Coffea montis-sacri

Coffea montis-sacri, commonly known as the sacred mountain coffee.

It is a species of coffee plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia. This plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and produces red berries containing the seeds used for coffee. It thrives in cool, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
