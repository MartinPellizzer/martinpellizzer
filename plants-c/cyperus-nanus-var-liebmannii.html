Cyperus nanus var. liebmannii

Cyperus nanus var. liebmannii, commonly known as dwarf flatsedge, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowering spikes that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies.

Its compact size and adaptability make it a common species in both natural and cultivated wetland habitats.
