Clerodendrum robustum

Clerodendrum robustum, commonly known as the robust clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in clusters, typically in shades of red, pink, or white. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
