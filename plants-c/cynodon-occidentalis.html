Cynodon occidentalis

Cynodon occidentalis, commonly known as West African couchgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features tufted, rhizomatous growth with dense, low-growing foliage. The plant has narrow, bluish-green leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to a wide range of soil types and is drought-tolerant.

Cynodon occidentalis is often used in landscaping and as a forage crop due to its hardiness and rapid growth.
