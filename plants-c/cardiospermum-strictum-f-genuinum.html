Cardiospermum strictum f. genuinum

Cardiospermum strictum f. genuinum, commonly known as the Indian spinach or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers.
