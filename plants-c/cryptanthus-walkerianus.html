Cryptanthus walkerianus

Cryptanthus walkerianus, commonly known as the rainbow plant or zebra plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble petals. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overshadowed by its vibrant foliage. It is a compact, evergreen perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade.

Cryptanthus walkerianus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
