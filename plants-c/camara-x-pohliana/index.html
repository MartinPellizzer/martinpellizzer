Camara × Pohliana

Camara × pohliiana is a striking hybrid plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers that add a bold touch to any garden. This unique species is the result of crossbreeding between Camara glabra and Camara crocata, combining the best traits of both parents. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. Its long blooming period and low maintenance requirements further enhance its appeal. Whether grown in containers or in the ground, Camara × pohliana is a standout addition to any floral display.
