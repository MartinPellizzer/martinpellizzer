Cypripedium × baconis

Cypripedium × baconis, commonly known as the bacon's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the genus Cypripedium.

It is characterized by its large, showy, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pink or reddish in color. The plant is native to the eastern United States, where it thrives in moist, shaded forest environments. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, fragrant blooms in late spring.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
