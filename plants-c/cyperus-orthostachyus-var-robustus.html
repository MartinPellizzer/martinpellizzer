Cyperus orthostachyus var. robustus

Cyperus orthostachyus var. robustus, commonly known as the broadleaf paperplant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly known as the sedge family, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces dense clusters of small, pale green to brownish spikelets that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along water bodies.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and used in landscape design.
