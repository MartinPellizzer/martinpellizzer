Coelogyne nervosa

Coelogyne nervosa, commonly known as the veined orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant features broad, dark green leaves with prominent veins, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne nervosa is often cultivated for its beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
