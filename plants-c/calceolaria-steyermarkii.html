Calceolaria steyermarkii

Calceolaria steyermarkii, commonly known as the Andean slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. The plant produces small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Calceolaria steyermarkii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
