Celmisia lindsayi

Celmisia lindsayi, commonly known as the Lindsay's celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring cool and moist conditions. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers.

Celmisia lindsayi is often found in the South Island and is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations.
