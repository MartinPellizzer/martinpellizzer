Combretum lepidopetalum

Combretum lepidopetalum, commonly known as the yellow flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
