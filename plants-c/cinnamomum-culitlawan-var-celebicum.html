Cinnamomum culitlawan var. celebicum

Cinnamomum culitlawan var. celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes cinnamon, is a species of tree native to the Moluccas in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elliptical, dark blue berries. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to cinnamon.

The species is valued for its unique fragrance and potential medicinal properties.
