Cyperus phleoides var. kauaiensis

Cyperus phleoides var. kauaiensis, commonly known as the Kauai flatsedge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It typically grows in wetland and marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often green or reddish in color. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively.

This species plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for native wildlife.
