Convolvulus althaeoides subsp. tenuissimus

Convolvulus althaeoides subsp.

tenuissimus, commonly known as the slenderleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a herbaceous annual with slender, trailing stems and small, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of blue or purple.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners, though it can be invasive in some regions.
