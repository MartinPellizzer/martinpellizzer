Cola pulcherrima

Cola pulcherrima, commonly known as the beautiful cola, is a species of flowering plant in the family Sterculiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Congo and Gabon. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit of Cola pulcherrima contains seeds surrounded by a fleshy, aromatic pulp.
