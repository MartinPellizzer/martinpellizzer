Cytisus glabratus

Cytisus glabratus, commonly known as the smooth broom, is a flowering shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and compound leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in racemes during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Cytisus glabratus is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, which can improve soil fertility in certain habitats.
