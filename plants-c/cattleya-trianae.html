Cattleya trianae

Cattleya trianae, commonly known as the Triana's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya trianae is also valued for its fragrant blooms and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and greenhouses.
