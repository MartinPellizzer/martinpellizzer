Choretrum preissianum

Choretrum preissianum, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces bright yellow flowers resembling those of the dandelion. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to reduce water loss.

It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing food for local wildlife.
