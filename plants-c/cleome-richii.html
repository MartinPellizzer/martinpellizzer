Cleome richii

Cleome richii, commonly known as the Mexican spider flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, green, spiny capsules that contain numerous seeds. It typically grows in dry, open habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.

Cleome richii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
