Chenopodium ambrosioides var. typicum

Chenopodium ambrosioides var. typicum, commonly known as wormseed goosefoot, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to the Americas and is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

The seeds of this species are small and contain compounds that have been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
