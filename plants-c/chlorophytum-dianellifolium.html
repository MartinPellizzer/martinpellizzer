Chlorophytum dianellifolium

Chlorophytum dianellifolium, commonly known as the silver sword, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched stems with small, white flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
