Crotalaria hoffmannii var. glabra

Crotalaria hoffmannii var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with erect, branching stems and pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow with a distinctive bilabiate structure, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

This species is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional medicinal practices.
