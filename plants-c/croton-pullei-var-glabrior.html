Croton pullei var. glabrior

Croton pullei var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-leaved croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. Its growth habit is upright, with multiple branching stems.

It thrives in tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.
