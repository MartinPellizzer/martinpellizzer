Chaetoseris yunnanensis

Chaetoseris yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan's hairy aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high elevations. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces yellow-orange flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
