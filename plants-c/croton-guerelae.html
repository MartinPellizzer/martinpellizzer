Croton guerelae

Croton guerelae, commonly known as the Guerelae croton, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Croton.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, varied foliage, with leaves often displaying shades of red, yellow, and green. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

Croton guerelae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking leaf patterns and adaptability to different growing conditions.
