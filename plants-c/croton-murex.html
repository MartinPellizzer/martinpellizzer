Croton murex

Croton murex, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that range from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton murex is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.
