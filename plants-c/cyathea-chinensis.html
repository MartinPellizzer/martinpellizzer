Cyathea chinensis

Cyathea chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feathery fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its tall stem and contributes to its structural stability.

Cyathea chinensis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can be found in tropical and subtropical gardens.
