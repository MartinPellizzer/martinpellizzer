Cyanothamnus inflexus subsp. grandiflorus

Cyanothamnus inflexus subsp.

grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered bluebush, is a shrub native to Australia. It features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

The plant has grey-green, aromatic leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in well-drained soils in coastal and tableland regions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
