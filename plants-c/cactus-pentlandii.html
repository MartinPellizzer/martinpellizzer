Cactus pentlandii

Cactus pentlandii, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and a dense covering of pale yellow spines. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for desert gardens and indoor cultivation.
