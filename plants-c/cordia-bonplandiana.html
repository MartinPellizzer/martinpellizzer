Cordia bonplandiana

Cordia bonplandiana, commonly known as the Amazonian gloryflower, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
