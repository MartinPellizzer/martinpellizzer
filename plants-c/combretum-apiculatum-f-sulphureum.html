Combretum apiculatum f. sulphureum

Combretum apiculatum f. sulphureum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered flame tree, is a striking tropical shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which appear in dense clusters and bloom during the warmer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive reddish or purple underside. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique coloration and seasonal display make it a notable species in botanical gardens and landscapes.
