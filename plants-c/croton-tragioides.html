Croton tragioides

Croton tragioides, commonly known as the black croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
