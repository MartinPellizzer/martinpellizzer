Cerastium nutans var. vestitum

Cerastium nutans var. vestitum, commonly known as the creeping snowdrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural habitats.
