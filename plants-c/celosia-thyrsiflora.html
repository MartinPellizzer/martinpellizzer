Celosia thyrsiflora

Celosia thyrsiflora, commonly known as the flame flower or crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, spiky foliage and vibrant, showy bracts that resemble flames. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by the colorful, leaf-like structures. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Celosia thyrsiflora is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
