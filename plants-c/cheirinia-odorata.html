Cheirinia odorata

Cheirinia odorata, commonly known as the sweet-scented geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves that emit a pleasant, spicy fragrance. The plant produces small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and delicate blossoms.
