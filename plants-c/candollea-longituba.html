Candollea longituba

Candollea longituba, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in the understory of rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Candollea longituba is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
