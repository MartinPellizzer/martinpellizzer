Copernicia yarey var. robusta

Copernicia yarey var. robusta, commonly known as the robusta copernicia, is a species of palm native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, coconut-like fruits. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
