Callistanthos roseoalbus

Callistanthos roseoalbus, commonly known as the white-flowered starling bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has slender, woody stems and small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
