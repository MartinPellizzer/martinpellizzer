Cotyledon albiflora

Cotyledon albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, pale yellow or white flowers during the warmer months, which are borne on short stalks. Its leaves are often adorned with intricate patterns and can change color in response to light conditions.

Cotyledon albiflora is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
