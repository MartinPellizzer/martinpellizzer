Convolvulus stocksii

Convolvulus stocksii, commonly known as the mountain bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color. Its leaves are opposite and have a slightly hairy texture.
