Calathea carlae

Calathea carlae, commonly known as the Carla's Calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, intricately patterned leaves that display a range of colors including green, silver, and pink. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Calathea carlae thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its foliage changes color in response to light exposure, making it a fascinating subject for plant enthusiasts.
