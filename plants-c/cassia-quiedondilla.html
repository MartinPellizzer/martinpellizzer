Cassia quiedondilla

Cassia quiedondilla, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, fluffy flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has palmate leaves composed of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia quiedondilla is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
