Cattleya dijanceana

Cattleya dijanceana, commonly known as the Dijanceana orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a herbaceous plant with long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The flowers typically bloom in late winter or early spring, making it a sought-after orchid for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

This orchid is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its elegance and fragrance.
