Colax viridis var. puydtii

Colax viridis var. puydtii, commonly known as the green colax, is a variant of the colax plant characterized by its vibrant green foliage.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions in Europe, particularly in the Pyrenees. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
