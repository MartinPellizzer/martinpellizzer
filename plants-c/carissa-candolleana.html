Carissa candolleana

Carissa candolleana, commonly known as the yellow bell tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow bell-shaped flowers. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains seeds, and the plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
