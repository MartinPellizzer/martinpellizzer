Cassia occidentalis var. sophera

Cassia occidentalis var. sophera, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of yellow flowers that resemble a shower of golden beads. The plant produces small, flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
