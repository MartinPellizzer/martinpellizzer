Cardamine rocheliana var. longirostris

Cardamine rocheliana var. longirostris, commonly known as the long-beaked watercress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early spring.

The species is characterized by its elongated, narrow siliques that contain numerous small, oval seeds.
