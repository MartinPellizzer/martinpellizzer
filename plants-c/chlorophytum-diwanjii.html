Chlorophytum diwanjii

Chlorophytum diwanjii, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail Plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that resemble a tiger's tail, hence its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in summer. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum diwanjii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
