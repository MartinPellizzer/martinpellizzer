Carex dispalata var. niigatensis

Carex dispalata var. niigatensis, commonly known as the Niigata sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. This variety is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.

The plant has a fibrous root system and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland habitats.
