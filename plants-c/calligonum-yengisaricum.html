Calligonum yengisaricum

Calligonum yengisaricum, commonly known as the Yengisar thorny shrub, is a perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its robust, thorny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, and it is well adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Calligonum yengisaricum plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and preventing desertification in its native habitat.
