Crepidiastrum sonchifolium var. elegans

Crepidiastrum sonchifolium var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant leaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped, silver-green leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the top of a slender stem. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and delicate blooms, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
