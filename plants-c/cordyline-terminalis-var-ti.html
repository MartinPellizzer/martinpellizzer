Cordyline terminalis var. ti

Cordyline terminalis var. ti, commonly known as the Ti plant or good luck plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It features striking, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a red or purple tint along the midrib. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its resilience and attractive foliage make it a favorite in both indoor and outdoor settings.
