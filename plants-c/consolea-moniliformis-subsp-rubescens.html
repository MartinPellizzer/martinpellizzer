Consolea moniliformis subsp. rubescens

Consolea moniliformis subsp.

rubescens, commonly known as the red-tipped consolea, is a climbing epiphytic cactus native to Mexico. It features a slender, cylindrical stem that is often reddish at the tips, giving it its distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habit, it is a popular choice for indoor and terrarium cultivation.
