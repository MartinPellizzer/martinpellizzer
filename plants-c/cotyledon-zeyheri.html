Cotyledon zeyheri

Cotyledon zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's windowleaf, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The leaves are usually bright green with a variety of colorful markings, ranging from red to purple. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
