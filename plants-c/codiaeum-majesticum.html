Codiaeum majesticum

Codiaeum majesticum, commonly known as the flame maple or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that can display shades of red, orange, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
