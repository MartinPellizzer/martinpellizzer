Crawfurdia nienkui

Crawfurdia nienkui, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces striking, star-like blue to purple flowers during the summer months. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous underground stem, which stores nutrients for growth.

Crawfurdia nienkui is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils in full sun.
