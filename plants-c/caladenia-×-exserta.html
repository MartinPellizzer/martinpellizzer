Caladenia × exserta

Caladenia × exserta, commonly known as the Spurred Helmet-orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to south-eastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped flowers with a prominent spur. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats, such as heathlands and woodlands. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange in color, with a contrasting dark red or brown lip.

Caladenia × exserta is a short-lived perennial, often flowering in spring and relying on specific pollinators for reproduction.
