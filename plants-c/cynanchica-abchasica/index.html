Cynanchica Abchasica

Cynanchica abchasica is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. Known for its delicate, star-shaped flowers and deep green foliage, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species is often found in shaded forest floors and rocky crevices, where it adapts well to limited sunlight. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this plant is essential for preserving the biodiversity of its native habitat.
