Cotyledon mucizonia var. parviflora

Cotyledon mucizonia var. parviflora, commonly known as the Parviflora Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, which help it store water and protect against intense sunlight. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or red, typically blooming in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
