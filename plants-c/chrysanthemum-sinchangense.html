Chrysanthemum sinchangense

Chrysanthemum sinchangense, commonly known as the Sinchang chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade to full sun. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its foliage and blooms are often cultivated for both aesthetic and therapeutic purposes.
