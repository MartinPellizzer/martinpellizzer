Crepidiastrum ameristophyllum

Crepidiastrum ameristophyllum, commonly known as the American starleaf, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often adorned with distinctive star-shaped markings.
