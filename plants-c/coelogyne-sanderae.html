Coelogyne sanderae

Coelogyne sanderae, commonly known as the Sanderae orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has erect, fleshy pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves that help it conserve water in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often borne on terminal racemes and can last for several weeks.

Coelogyne sanderae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
