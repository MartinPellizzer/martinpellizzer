Clerodendrum lindenianum

Clerodendrum lindenianum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often brightly colored in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in umbels, and it has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
