Corallodendron Ovalifolium

Corallodendron ovalifolium is a rare and striking plant native to the rocky coastal regions of southern Africa. It is known for its unique, coral-like appearance and its ability to thrive in harsh, saline environments. This plant features oval-shaped leaves and produces delicate, white flowers that bloom in spring. Its slow growth and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it remains under threat from habitat loss and climate change.
