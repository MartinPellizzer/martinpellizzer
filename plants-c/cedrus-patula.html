Cedrus patula

Cedrus patula, commonly known as the Kashmir cedar, is a coniferous tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and its ability to thrive in high-altitude environments. The tree has scale-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in flat sprays. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The wood of Cedrus patula is highly valued for its durability and aromatic properties.
