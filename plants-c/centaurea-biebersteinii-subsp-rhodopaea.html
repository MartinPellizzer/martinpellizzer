Centaurea biebersteinii subsp. rhodopaea

Centaurea biebersteinii subsp.

rhodopaea, commonly known as the Rhodope centaury, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers.
