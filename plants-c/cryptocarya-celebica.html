Cryptocarya celebica

Cryptocarya celebica, commonly known as the Celebes cryptocarya, is a species of evergreen tree native to the Indonesian islands of Java and Sumatra.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in lowland to montane forests, thriving in humid, shaded environments.

Its fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
