Cattleya hennisiana

Cattleya hennisiana, commonly known as the Hennisiana orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with red and purple markings. The plant produces large, showy blooms that can reach up to 5 inches in diameter, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
