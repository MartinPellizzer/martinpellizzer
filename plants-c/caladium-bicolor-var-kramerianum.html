Caladium bicolor var. kramerianum

Caladium bicolor var. kramerianum, commonly known as the flameleaf caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can display shades of red, pink, white, and green. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have a waxy texture, making them visually appealing. This variety thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaf patterns and is commonly used in gardens and as houseplants.
