Cryptocoryne nevillii

Cryptocoryne nevillii, commonly known as the Nevill's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, elongated leaves that are often ribbed and can grow up to 30 cm in length. This plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water and is often found in slow-moving or still freshwater environments. It is a popular choice for aquariums due to its hardiness and ability to adapt to various water conditions.

Cryptocoryne nevillii is also known for its ability to tolerate low light levels, making it a versatile addition to planted tanks.
