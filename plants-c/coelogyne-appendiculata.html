Coelogyne appendiculata

Coelogyne appendiculata, commonly known as the appendiculate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. Its flowers typically bloom in spring and are characterized by their intricate, spurred petals.

Coelogyne appendiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
