Crassula portulacaria

Crassula portulacaria, commonly known as the African snowball or elephant bush, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge, arranged in a compact, bushy shape. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
