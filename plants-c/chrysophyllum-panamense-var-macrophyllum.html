Chrysophyllum panamense var. macrophyllum

Chrysophyllum panamense var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf chrysophyllum, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 20 centimeters in length. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, orange-colored fruits resembling small apples.
