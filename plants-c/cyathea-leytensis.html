Cyathea leytensis

Cyathea leytensis, commonly known as the Leyte tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, contributing to its structural support.

Cyathea leytensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
