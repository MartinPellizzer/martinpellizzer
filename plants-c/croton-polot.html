Croton polot

Croton polot, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red, orange, or even purple. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton polot thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas or near water sources.
