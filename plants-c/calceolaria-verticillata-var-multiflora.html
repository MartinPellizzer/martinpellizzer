Calceolaria verticillata var. multiflora

Calceolaria verticillata var. multiflora, commonly known as the clustered slipper orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features striking, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically orange or red in color, with a distinctive hood-like structure. The plant grows in dense clusters, with multiple flower stems arising from a central base. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude regions.

Its unique floral morphology and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
