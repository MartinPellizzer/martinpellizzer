Connaropsis rubescens

Connaropsis rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed connaraceae, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy upper surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in inflorescences, and are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
