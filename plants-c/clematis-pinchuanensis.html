Clematis pinchuanensis

Clematis pinchuanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant features compound leaves with trifoliate or pinnate structures, and its flowers typically range in color from white to pink. Clematis pinchuanensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
