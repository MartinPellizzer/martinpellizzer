Calamus schlechteri

Calamus schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's canes.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family, native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and horticultural collections.
