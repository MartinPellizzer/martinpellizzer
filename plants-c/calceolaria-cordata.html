Calceolaria cordata

Calceolaria cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf calceolaria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking, hooded flowers. Calceolaria cordata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
