Clematis gebleriana

Clematis gebleriana, commonly known as the silky clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to Central America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive blue or purple hue at the base. The plant has palmately divided leaves with a soft, silvery underside, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis gebleriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
