Clerodendrum multibracteatum

Clerodendrum multibracteatum, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often arranged in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum multibracteatum is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
