Cucumis anguria var. longipes

Cucumis anguria var. longipes, commonly known as the wild cucumber or horned cucumber, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features elongated, spiny fruits that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length, with a distinct horn-like shape at the tip. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has heart-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to both ornamental and edible plant collections.
