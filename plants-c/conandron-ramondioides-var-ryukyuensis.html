Conandron ramondioides var. ryukyuensis

Conandron ramondioides var. ryukyuensis, commonly known as Ryukyu holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan, where it grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a leathery texture. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in catkins, followed by small, round berries.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
