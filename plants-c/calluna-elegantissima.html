Calluna elegantissima

Calluna elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant heather, is a species of flowering plant in the heath family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe, particularly in the Pyrenees and the Alps. This plant is characterized by its low-growing habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense ground cover.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as moors and rocky slopes.
