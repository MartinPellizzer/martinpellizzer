Croton nutans

Croton nutans, commonly known as the drooping croton, is a tropical plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Croton nutans is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and unique appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
