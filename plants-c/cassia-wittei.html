Cassia wittei

Cassia wittei, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow inflorescences that appear in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Cassia wittei thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
