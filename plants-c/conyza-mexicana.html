Conyza mexicana

Conyza mexicana, commonly known as Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Conyza mexicana is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in arid environments.
