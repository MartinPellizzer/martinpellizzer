Cytisus nubigenus

Cytisus nubigenus, commonly known as the cloud mist broom, is a flowering shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Cytisus nubigenus is noted for its ornamental value and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
