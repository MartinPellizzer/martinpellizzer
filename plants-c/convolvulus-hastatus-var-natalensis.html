Convolvulus hastatus var. natalensis

Convolvulus hastatus var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and twining stems that climb or trail along the ground. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

The flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
