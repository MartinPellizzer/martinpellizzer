Coelogyne Eduardii

Coelogyne eduardii is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, fragrant flowers and unique morphology, it captivates both botanists and horticultural enthusiasts. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Its name honors Eduard, likely a botanist or patron who contributed to its discovery. Coelogyne eduardii remains a sought-after plant for its beauty and rarity in the orchid world.
