Centaurea Paniculata Var. Biformis

Centaurea paniculata var. biformis is a striking plant known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. This variant is distinguished by its unique two-lobed bracts that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower displays.
