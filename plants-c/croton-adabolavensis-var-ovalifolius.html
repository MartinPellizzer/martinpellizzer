Croton adabolavensis var. ovalifolius

Croton adabolavensis var. ovalifolius, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in open woodlands and along stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
