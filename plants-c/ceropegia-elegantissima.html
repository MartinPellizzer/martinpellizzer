Ceropegia elegantissima

Ceropegia elegantissima, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, trailing stems with tubular, pinkish-white flowers that resemble a lion's tail. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, making it drought-tolerant. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for hanging baskets and rock gardens.
