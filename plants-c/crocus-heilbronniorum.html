Crocus heilbronniorum

Crocus heilbronniorum, commonly known as the Heilbronn crocus, is a rare and endemic species native to a small region in southern Germany.

It is a bulbous plant that produces delicate, early spring blooms with vivid purple or violet petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks. Its flowers are relatively small, usually less than two inches in diameter, and feature a distinctive, spurred petal at the base.
