Chorizema costatum var. lineare

Chorizema costatum var. lineare, commonly known as the linearleaf chorisma, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers clustered in umbels, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant plant collections.
