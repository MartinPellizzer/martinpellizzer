Crassula setulosa var. deminuta

Crassula setulosa var. deminuta, commonly known as the dwarf crassula or miniature snowball plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slight blue or silver hue. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped white flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
