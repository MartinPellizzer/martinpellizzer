Callitriche stagnalis var. kuetzingii

Callitriche stagnalis var. kuetzingii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to freshwater environments.

It typically grows in shallow, still waters such as ponds and marshes. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually less than 1 cm in length. Its flowers are tiny, greenish-white, and borne in clusters at the nodes.

It plays a role in stabilizing sediments and providing habitat for aquatic invertebrates.
