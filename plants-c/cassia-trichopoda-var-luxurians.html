Cassia trichopoda var. luxurians

Cassia trichopoda var. luxurians, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces elongated, flat pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
