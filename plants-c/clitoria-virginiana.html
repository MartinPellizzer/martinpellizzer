Clitoria virginiana

Clitoria virginiana, commonly known as the winged bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds and a starchy, tuberous root. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

Clitoria virginiana is also valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
