Clypeola jonthlaspi var. glabra

Clypeola jonthlaspi var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth clypeola, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, annual herb characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to southern Africa. Its smooth, glabrous leaves distinguish it from other varieties in the species.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and gardens.
