Cordia macrostachya

Cordia macrostachya, commonly known as the large-stemmed cordia, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces woody capsules containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.

Cordia macrostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some indigenous cultures.
