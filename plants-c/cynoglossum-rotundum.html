Cynoglossum rotundum

Cynoglossum rotundum, commonly known as roundleaf forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its rounded, velvety leaves and clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and along stream banks.

Cynoglossum rotundum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
