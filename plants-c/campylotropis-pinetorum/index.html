Campylotropis Pinetorum

Campylotropis pinetorum is a rare and intriguing plant species native to specific regions of eastern Asia. It belongs to the legume family, known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. This shrub is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and endangered due to habitat loss. Its unique ecological role highlights the importance of preserving such rare botanical treasures.
