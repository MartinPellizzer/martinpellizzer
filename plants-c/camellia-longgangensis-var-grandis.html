Camellia longgangensis var. grandis

Camellia longgangensis var. grandis, commonly known as the large-flowered camellia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape.

The flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring, are prized for their ornamental value in gardens and horticulture.
