Chamaecrista tragacanthoides var. rasa

Chamaecrista tragacanthoides var. rasa, commonly known as the slender burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential agricultural use.
