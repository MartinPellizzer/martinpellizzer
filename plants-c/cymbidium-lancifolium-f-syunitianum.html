Cymbidium lancifolium f. syunitianum

Cymbidium lancifolium f. syunitianum, commonly known as the Syunitian orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a yellowish throat. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This cultivar is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and rarity.
