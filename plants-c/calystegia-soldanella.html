Calystegia soldanella

Calystegia soldanella, commonly known as the small-flowered morning glory, is a climbing vine belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, funnel-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale blue. The fruit is a capsule that releases small, dry seeds when mature.
