Carex ornithopodioides f. elongata

Carex ornithopodioides f. elongata, commonly known as the elongated sedge, is a variant of the ornithopodioides sedge species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of North America. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for wetland restoration and ecological landscaping.
