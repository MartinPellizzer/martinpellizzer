Centaurea susannae var. paivae

Centaurea susannae var. paivae, commonly known as the Paivai blueweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bracteate flower clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is often cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
