Cynanchum intermedium

Cynanchum intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as marshes and riverbanks. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Cynanchum intermedium produces small, greenish-white flowers clustered in umbels, which attract various pollinators.
