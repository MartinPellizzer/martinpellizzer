Cnicus orgyalis

Cnicus orgyalis, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, often forming dense mats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
