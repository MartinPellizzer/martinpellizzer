Costus lima var. lima

Costus lima var. lima, commonly known as the yellow costus, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
