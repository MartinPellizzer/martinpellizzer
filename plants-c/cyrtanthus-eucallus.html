Cyrtanthus eucallus

Cyrtanthus eucallus, commonly known as the White Star Lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive yellow or orange central throat. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cyrtanthus eucallus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant blooms.
