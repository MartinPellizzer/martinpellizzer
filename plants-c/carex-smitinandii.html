Carex smitinandii

Carex smitinandii, commonly known as the Smitinandii sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known for their unique floral structures. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy habitats.
