Calamagrostis × hartmaniana var. laxior

Calamagrostis × hartmaniana var. laxior, commonly known as silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feathery inflorescences that add ornamental value to gardens. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and is often used in wetland restoration or as a landscape plant in water features.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological landscaping.
