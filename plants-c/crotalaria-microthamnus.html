Crotalaria microthamnus

Crotalaria microthamnus, commonly known as the small-leaf crownflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous properties. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Crotalaria microthamnus is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
