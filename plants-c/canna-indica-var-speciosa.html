Canna indica var. speciosa

Canna indica var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, colorful, and showy bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by these vibrant bracts. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Canna indica var. speciosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
