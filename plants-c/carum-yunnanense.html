Carum yunnanense

Carum yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan caraway, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes parsley and dill, and is characterized by its umbellate inflorescences. Carum yunnanense is also used as a spice in some culinary applications.
