Clerodendrum splendens var. puberulentum

Clerodendrum splendens var. puberulentum, commonly known as the velvet clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow throats. The plant has hairy, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name and contributes to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative shrub or small tree.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, tropical foliage and flowers.
