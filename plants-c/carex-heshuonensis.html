Carex heshuonensis

Carex heshuonensis, commonly known as the Heshuonensis sedge, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to specific regions in China, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green or brown in color. It is often found in wetlands, stream sides, and other damp habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
