Capparis billardieri

Capparis billardieri, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, segmented stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in the winter season. The plant belongs to the Capparaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in bright, indirect light. Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular ornamental plant.

Capparis billardieri requires well-draining soil and moderate watering to flourish.
