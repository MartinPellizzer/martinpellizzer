Crinum abyssinicum

Crinum abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Ethiopia.

It features large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 cm in diameter, typically in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant has strap-shaped leaves that are broad and often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Crinum abyssinicum is also known for its strong fragrance, which can be both pleasant and overwhelming.
