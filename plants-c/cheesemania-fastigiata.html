Cheesemania fastigiata

Cheesemania fastigiata, commonly known as the upright cheesemania, is a unique plant with a distinctive upright growth habit.

It features narrow, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, columnar form. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale in color, adding a subtle ornamental appeal. Its foliage is dense and dark green, providing a striking visual contrast against its pale blooms.

While not widely cultivated, it is valued for its unusual structure and potential medicinal properties in some traditional practices.
