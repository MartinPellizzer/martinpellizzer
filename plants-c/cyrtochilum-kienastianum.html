Cyrtochilum kienastianum

Cyrtochilum kienastianum, commonly known as the Kienast's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, typically featuring a combination of white, green, and purple hues. Cyrtochilum kienastianum thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in the ecological diversity of its native habitats.
