Cyperus tenuifolius var. tenuifolius

Cyperus tenuifolius var. tenuifolius, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and cylindrical flower clusters. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively and colonize new areas.

Its small, inconspicuous flowers are wind-pollinated, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems.
