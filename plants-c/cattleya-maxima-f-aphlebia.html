Cattleya maxima f. aphlebia

Cattleya maxima f. aphlebia, commonly known as the Aphlebia Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species renowned for its large, showy flowers.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and produces fragrant, often bicolor blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.

This hybrid is frequently used in breeding programs to develop new orchid varieties with enhanced floral characteristics.
