Ceropegia semota subsp. orientalis

Ceropegia semota subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its fleshy, tuberous roots and thick, succulent stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.
