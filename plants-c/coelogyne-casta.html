Coelogyne casta

Coelogyne casta, commonly known as the Casta Cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to orange-red flowers with a distinctive white throat. It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Coelogyne casta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
