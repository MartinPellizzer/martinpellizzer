Chenopodium giganteum

Chenopodium giganteum, commonly known as giant goosefoot, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Chenopodium giganteum is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
