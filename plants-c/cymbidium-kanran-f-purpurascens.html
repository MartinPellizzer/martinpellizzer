Cymbidium kanran f. purpurascens

Cymbidium kanran f. purpurascens, commonly known as the Purple Orchid, is a rare and highly prized orchid species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of long, strap-like leaves that are often a deep green color.

This orchid is celebrated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant and vibrant blooms.
