Centaurea iranshahrii

Centaurea iranshahrii, commonly known as the Iranian centaury, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, with ray florets surrounding a central disc.

Centaurea iranshahrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
