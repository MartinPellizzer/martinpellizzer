Cuphea vesiculigera

Cuphea vesiculigera, commonly known as the bubblecup, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy red or pink tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, round seed pods that resemble bubbles, giving it its common name.
