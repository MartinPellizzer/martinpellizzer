Combretum fulvum

Combretum fulvum, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its bark is smooth and reddish-brown in color. It is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
