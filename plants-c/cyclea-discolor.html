Cyclea discolor

Cyclea discolor, commonly known as the yellow bell vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Cyclea discolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
