Calathea hagbergii

Calathea hagbergii, commonly known as the Hagberg's Calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, colorful foliage with patterns resembling delicate veins, often in shades of green, silver, and pink. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture that enhances their visual appeal. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect light to flourish.

Its unique aesthetic makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
