Cymbidium faberi f. viridiflorum

Cymbidium faberi f. viridiflorum, commonly known as the Green-flowered Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its long, arching inflorescences and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The flowers are notable for their vibrant green petals and sepals, which contrast with the yellow lip. This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its unique coloration and elegance.

It requires specific growing conditions, including cool temperatures and high humidity, to thrive.
