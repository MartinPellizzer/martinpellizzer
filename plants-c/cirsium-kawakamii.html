Cirsium kawakamii

Cirsium kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakami's thistle, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered with dense, sharp prickles.

The flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
