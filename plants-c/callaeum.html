Callaeum

Callaeum, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow flowers that resemble shower-like droplets, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the family Bignoniaceae and is known for its fast growth and ornamental value. Its leaves are typically opposite and ovate, with a smooth surface.

Callaeum is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
