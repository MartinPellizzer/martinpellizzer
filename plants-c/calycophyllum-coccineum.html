Calycophyllum coccineum

Calycophyllum coccineum, commonly known as the scarlet calycophyllum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Calycaraceae.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.
