Chrysanthemum tianschanicum

Chrysanthemum tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tian Shan chrysanthemum, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia, particularly the Tian Shan mountain range.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically reaches a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has finely divided, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
