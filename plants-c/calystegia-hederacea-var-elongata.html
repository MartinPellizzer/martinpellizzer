Calystegia hederacea var. elongata

Calystegia hederacea var. elongata, commonly known as the long-stemmed bindweed, is a perennial vine native to North America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink to purple in color.
