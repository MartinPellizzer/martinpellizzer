Cotoneaster ganghobaensis

Cotoneaster ganghobaensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by red berries that are edible for birds.

It thrives in well-drained soil and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness.
