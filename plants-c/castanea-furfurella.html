Castanea furfurella

Castanea furfurella, commonly known as the Chinese chestnut.

It is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces edible nuts enclosed in spiny husks, similar to other chestnut species. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated edges, while its bark is smooth and grayish in color.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to provide both food and timber.
