Cymbidium autumnale

Cymbidium autumnale, commonly known as the autumn orchid, is a species of orchid native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the fall season. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cymbidium autumnale is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to cultivation.
