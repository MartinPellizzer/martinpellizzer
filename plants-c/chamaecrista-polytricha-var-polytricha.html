Chamaecrista polytricha var. polytricha

Chamaecrista polytricha var. polytricha, commonly known as the slender crownbeard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southern United States.

It typically grows in sandy or loamy soils in open areas such as roadsides and prairies. The plant features slender, branched stems with opposite, pinnately compound leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy yellow flowers are arranged in dense, terminal clusters, and it produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
