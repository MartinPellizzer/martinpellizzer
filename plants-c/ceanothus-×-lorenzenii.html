Ceanothus × lorenzenii

Ceanothus × lorenzenii, commonly known as the coastal ceanothus, is a hybrid shrub native to coastal regions of California.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for coastal and Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are typically narrow and leathery, providing a drought-tolerant feature.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators and stabilizing coastal soils.
