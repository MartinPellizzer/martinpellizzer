Calea insignis

Calea insignis, commonly known as the sacred flame plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that resemble flames. The plant has velvety, dark green leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance and cultural significance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
