Cleistocactus chotaensis

Cleistocactus chotaensis, commonly known as the Chotaensis Cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Andes Mountains of Peru.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of small, spiny stems. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the evening, a unique trait among cacti. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
