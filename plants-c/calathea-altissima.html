Calathea altissima

Calathea altissima, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often feature intricate patterns and deep green hues. The plant is known for its unique leaf movement, where the leaves fold upward at night, resembling a praying posture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea altissima is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
