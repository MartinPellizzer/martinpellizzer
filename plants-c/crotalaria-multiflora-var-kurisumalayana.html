Crotalaria multiflora var. kurisumalayana

Crotalaria multiflora var. kurisumalayana, commonly known as the Kurisumalayana Crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with compound leaves and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers. Its botanical features include hairy stems, stipules, and a unique seed pod that splits open to release its seeds.

This species is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
