Convolvulus venustus

Convolvulus venustus, commonly known as the beautiful convolvulus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind, facilitating its spread in suitable habitats. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are hairy and often twining.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.
