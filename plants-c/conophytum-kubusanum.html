Conophytum kubusanum

Conophytum kubusanum, commonly known as the Kubusanum stone plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance with fleshy, triangular leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid environments.

Conophytum kubusanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to store water in its thick leaves.
