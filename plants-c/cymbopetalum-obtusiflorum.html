Cymbopetalum obtusiflorum

Cymbopetalum obtusiflorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters, displaying a range of colors from purple to red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in moist forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and ecological role in supporting local pollinator species.
