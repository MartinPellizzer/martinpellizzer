Cyperus mindorensis

Cyperus mindorensis, commonly known as Mindanao flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and along water bodies.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
