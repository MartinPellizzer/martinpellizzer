Cattleya skinneri var. bowringiana

Cattleya skinneri var. bowringiana, commonly known as the Bowring's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with red or brown markings. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated for floral arrangements and ornamental purposes.
