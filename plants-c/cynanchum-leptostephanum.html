Cynanchum leptostephanum

Cynanchum leptostephanum, commonly known as the slender-stephanum milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes many species of milkweeds. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its milky sap is characteristic of the genus and can be toxic.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
