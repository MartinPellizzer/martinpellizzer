Chiliadenus candicans

Chiliadenus candicans, commonly known as the candleberry plant, is a flowering shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white, candle-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
