Cracca bracteolata var. microfoliolata

Cracca bracteolata var. microfoliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cracca, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive narrow, finely toothed margin. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in the summer months.

This plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in sunny, well-drained habitats.
