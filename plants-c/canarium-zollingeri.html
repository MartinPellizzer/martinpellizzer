Canarium zollingeri

Canarium zollingeri, commonly known as the Zollinger's canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The species also has traditional uses in local medicine and as a source of aromatic resins.
