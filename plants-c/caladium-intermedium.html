Caladium intermedium

Caladium intermedium, commonly known as the Giant Elephant Ear, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The leaves are typically a deep green with striking red or pink veins, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its dramatic foliage and can be grown both outdoors and in containers.
