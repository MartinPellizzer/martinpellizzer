Convolvulus zernyi

Convolvulus zernyi, commonly known as the Zernyi morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Convolvulus zernyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habits.
