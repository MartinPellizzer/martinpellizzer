Cyperus rubidomontanus

Cyperus rubidomontanus, commonly known as the red-veined flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, such as swamps and floodplains. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive reddish hue along the midrib.

Cyperus rubidomontanus is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to moist conditions.
