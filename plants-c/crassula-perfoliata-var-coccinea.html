Crassula perfoliata var. coccinea

Crassula perfoliata var. coccinea, commonly known as the Red Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique coloration and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
