Columnea sericea

Columnea sericea, commonly known as the silverleaf columnea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has velvety, silver-green leaves that give it its common name and contribute to its ornamental appeal. It typically grows as a shrub or vine, often climbing or trailing in its natural habitat.

Columnea sericea thrives in bright, indirect light and is popular among gardeners for its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
