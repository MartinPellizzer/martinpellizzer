Cistus albidus var. attenuatifolius

Cistus albidus var. attenuatifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silver-gray, woolly foliage that is narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and poor soil conditions.

Its aromatic leaves and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
