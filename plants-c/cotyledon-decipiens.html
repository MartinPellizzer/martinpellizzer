Cotyledon decipiens

Cotyledon decipiens, commonly known as the false cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are capable of storing water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions.

Cotyledon decipiens is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
