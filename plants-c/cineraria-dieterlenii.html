Cineraria dieterlenii

Cineraria dieterlenii, commonly known as the Dieterlen's cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features silvery-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, which helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
