Cissampelos capensis

Cissampelos capensis, commonly known as the African moon vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
