Cassia forskalii

Cassia forskalii, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves with narrow, elongated leaflets and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. Cassia forskalii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
