Cattleya crispa var. delicatissima

Cattleya crispa var. delicatissima, commonly known as the delicate cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It features elongated, pendant-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellow throat. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, producing new shoots from the base of the old ones. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

This variety is prized for its elegance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
