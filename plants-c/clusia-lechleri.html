Clusia lechleri

Clusia lechleri, commonly known as the rubber tree or copal tree, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is known for producing a milky sap that has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties. Its ability to thrive in humid tropical environments makes it an important species in ecological and economic contexts.
