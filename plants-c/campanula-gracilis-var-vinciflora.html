Campanula gracilis var. vinciflora

Campanula gracilis var. vinciflora, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically blue to purple in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and extended blooming period.
