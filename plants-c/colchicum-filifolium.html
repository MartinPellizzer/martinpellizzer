Colchicum filifolium

Colchicum filifolium, commonly known as the filifolium colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and studied for its potential pharmaceutical applications.
