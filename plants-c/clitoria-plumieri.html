Clitoria plumieri

Clitoria plumieri, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers, which are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has compound leaves with three leaflets and produces edible pods that contain beans rich in protein. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both its nutritional value and ornamental beauty.

Clitoria plumieri is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
