Croton gracilis

Croton gracilis, commonly known as the slender croton, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often bright green with a reddish or purple underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Croton gracilis is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
