Corbularia obesa var. minor

Corbularia obesa var. minor, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a unique and compact succulent plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a swollen, often colorful stem that resembles a small, rounded fruit, while its root system is adapted to store water efficiently. The plant's leaves are typically small, fleshy, and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
