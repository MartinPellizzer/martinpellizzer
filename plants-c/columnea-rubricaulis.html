Columnea rubricaulis

Columnea rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
