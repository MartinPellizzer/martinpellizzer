Caladium splendidens

Caladium splendidens, commonly known as the splendid caladium, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium splendidens thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage is the main ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for garden displays and container planting.
