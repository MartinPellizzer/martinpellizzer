Centaurea cytherea

Centaurea cytherea, commonly known as the Cyprus star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea cytherea is often considered an invasive species in areas outside its native range.
