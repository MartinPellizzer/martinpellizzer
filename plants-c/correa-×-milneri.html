Correa × milneri

Correa × milneri, commonly known as the Milner's correa, is a hybrid shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters and features narrow, leathery leaves with a glossy surface. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, are borne in clusters and attract nectar-feeding birds and insects.

Correa × milneri is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
