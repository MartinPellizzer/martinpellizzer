Cytinus canariensis

Cytinus canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Island broom.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae, characterized by its distinctive tubular flowers and hairy stems. Native to the Canary Islands, it thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.
