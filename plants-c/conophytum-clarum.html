Conophytum clarum

Conophytum clarum, commonly known as the clear conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves that form a compact rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. Conophytum clarum is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
