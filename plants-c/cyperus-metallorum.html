Cyperus metallorum

Cyperus metallorum, commonly known as the metallum sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas or along water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered flower heads.

Cyperus metallorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
