Cananga odorata var. fruticosa

Cananga odorata var. fruticosa, commonly known as the fruticose cananga, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that emit a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits, which contain hard, spiny seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and use in perfumery.

Its botanical features make it a significant species in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
