Cotoneaster gesneri

Cotoneaster gesneri, commonly known as the Gesner's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white, five-petaled flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster gesneri is also valued for its role in wildlife habitats, providing food for birds and small mammals.
