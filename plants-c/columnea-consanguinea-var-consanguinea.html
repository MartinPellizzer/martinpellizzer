Columnea consanguinea var. consanguinea

Columnea consanguinea var. consanguinea, commonly known as the scarlet cardinal.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. Its foliage consists of dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
