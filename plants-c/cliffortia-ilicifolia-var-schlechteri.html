Cliffortia ilicifolia var. schlechteri

Cliffortia ilicifolia var. schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's silverleaf, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage and small, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
