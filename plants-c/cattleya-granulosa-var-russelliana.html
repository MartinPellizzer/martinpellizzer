Cattleya granulosa var. russelliana

Cattleya granulosa var. russelliana, commonly known as Russell's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant features long, arching pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its showy blooms and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
