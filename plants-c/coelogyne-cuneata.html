Coelogyne cuneata

Coelogyne cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves and blooms during the summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical or subtropical climate.

Coelogyne cuneata is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
