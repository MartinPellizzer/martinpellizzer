Castelaria Galapagei

Castelaria galapagei is a rare and unique plant species found exclusively on the Galapagos Islands. It thrives in the humid, volcanic soil of its native habitat, adapting to the island's distinct climate. This plant is notable for its striking appearance, featuring vibrant green leaves and delicate, flowering structures. Due to its limited range and environmental threats, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this fascinating species for future generations.
