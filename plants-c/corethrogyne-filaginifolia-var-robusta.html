Corethrogyne filaginifolia var. robusta

Corethrogyne filaginifolia var. robusta, commonly known as the robust corethrogyne, is a rare and distinctive plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its robust, woody stems and dense, leathery foliage that exhibits a striking blue-green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which are followed by unique, spiny seed capsules. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and is of interest to horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
