Centaurea sphaerocephala var. oligocentra

Centaurea sphaerocephala var. oligocentra, commonly known as the narrowhead centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, sparsely haired growth habit and produces small, blue to violet flowers arranged in dense, spherical heads. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy surface. This variety is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its ability to thrive in rocky or dry soils.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
