Calanthe catilligera

Calanthe catilligera, commonly known as the Chinese calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the presence of dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil.

Calanthe catilligera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
