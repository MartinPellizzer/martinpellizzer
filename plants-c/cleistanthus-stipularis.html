Cleistanthus stipularis

Cleistanthus stipularis, commonly known as the red-flowered cleistanthus, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous small, winged seeds.

Cleistanthus stipularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy flowers.
