Cotoneaster chuanus

Cotoneaster chuanus, commonly known as the Yunnan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Cotoneaster chuanus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
