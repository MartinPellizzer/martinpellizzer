Chloris paniculata

Chloris paniculata, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and delicate panicles of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its ornamental value comes from its graceful appearance and ability to provide texture in landscape designs.

Chloris paniculata is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and drought tolerance.
