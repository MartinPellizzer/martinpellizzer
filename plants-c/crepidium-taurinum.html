Crepidium taurinum

Crepidium taurinum, commonly known as the Taurine Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a single, erect stem that bears a few lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive hooded labellum, typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Crepidium taurinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
