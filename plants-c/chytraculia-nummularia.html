Chytraculia nummularia

Chytraculia nummularia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, silvery-gray foliage that is covered in fine, waxy hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
