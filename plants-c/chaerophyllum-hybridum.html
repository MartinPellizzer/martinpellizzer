Chaerophyllum hybridum

Chaerophyllum hybridum, commonly known as the cow parsley or small cow parsley, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its aromatic foliage and showy flowers make it a valuable plant for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
