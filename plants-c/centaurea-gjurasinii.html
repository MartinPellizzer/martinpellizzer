Centaurea gjurasinii

Centaurea gjurasinii, commonly known as the Gjurasi thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Basin.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves with spiny margins and produces purple to pinkish composite flower heads. Its inflorescence consists of both ray and disk flowers, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Centaurea gjurasinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
