Chenopodium purpurascens

Chenopodium purpurascens, commonly known as red goosefoot, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to the western United States and is characterized by its distinctive reddish stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Chenopodium purpurascens is also known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a hardy species in dry environments.
