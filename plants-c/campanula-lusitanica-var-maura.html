Campanula lusitanica var. maura

Campanula lusitanica var. maura, commonly known as the purple bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are generally hairy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula lusitanica var. maura is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
