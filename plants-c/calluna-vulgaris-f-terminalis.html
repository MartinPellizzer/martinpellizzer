Calluna vulgaris f. terminalis

Calluna vulgaris f. terminalis, commonly known as the trailing heather, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to western Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, mat-like form. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in heathland and coastal areas.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to create a dense ground cover in gardens.
