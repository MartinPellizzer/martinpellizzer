Cyperus lorentzianus var. elatus

Cyperus lorentzianus var. elatus, commonly known as the tall flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale green to brown flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense stands.

It is often found in floodplains, riverbanks, and other waterlogged habitats.
