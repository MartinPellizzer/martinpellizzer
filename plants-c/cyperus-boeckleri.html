Cyperus boeckleri

Cyperus boeckleri, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its triangular stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wet or marshy environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown, and it is often found in disturbed soils or along waterways.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats, making it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
