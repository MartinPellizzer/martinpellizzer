Cattleya × britoi

Cattleya × britoi, commonly known as the Royal Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding substrate.

Cattleya × britoi is prized for its ornamental value and is widely cultivated in horticulture for its beauty and fragrance.
