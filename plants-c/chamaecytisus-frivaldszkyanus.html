Chamaecytisus frivaldszkyanus

Chamaecytisus frivaldszkyanus, commonly known as the Frivaldszky's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in racemes and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is well adapted to the harsh, arid conditions of its native habitat, showcasing resilience to drought and salt spray.
