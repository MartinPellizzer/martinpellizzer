Centronia tomentosa

Centronia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly centronia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, hairy foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a waxy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Centronia tomentosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
