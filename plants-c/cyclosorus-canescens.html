Cyclosorus canescens

Cyclosorus canescens, commonly known as the silver moss, is a species of moss that belongs to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, carpet-like growth and silvery-gray appearance due to its fine, papillate leaves. Cyclosorus canescens plays an important role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for microorganisms and small invertebrates.
