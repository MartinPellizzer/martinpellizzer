Calycera herbacea var. sinuata

Calycera herbacea var. sinuata, commonly known as the sinuous calycera, is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of East Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The leaves are lance-shaped with a distinctive sinuous or wavy margin, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences, blooming in early summer.

This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
