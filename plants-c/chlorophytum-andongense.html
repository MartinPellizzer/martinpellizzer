Chlorophytum andongense

Chlorophytum andongense, commonly known as the Andong spider plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Korea.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically light green with white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in summer. It is a hardy and low-maintenance plant, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its unique foliage rather than its flowers.
