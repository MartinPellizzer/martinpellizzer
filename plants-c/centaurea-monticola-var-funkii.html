Centaurea monticola var. funkii

Centaurea monticola var. funkii, commonly known as the Sierra centaury, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of California.

It features slender, hairy stems and bright blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bristly seeds in its flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky slopes.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
