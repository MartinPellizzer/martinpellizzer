Cynophalla heterophylla

Cynophalla heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, spiny stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are often reduced to spines, while the stems are thick and fleshy.

Cynophalla heterophylla is well adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem.
