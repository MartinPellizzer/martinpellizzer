Commelina triangulispatha

Commelina triangulispatha, commonly known as the triangular leaf commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinct triangular base, and small, delicate blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. It has a fibrous root system and produces seeds in capsule-like structures.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Commelina family, known for its ornamental and ecological significance.
