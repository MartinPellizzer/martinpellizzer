Chenopodium philippianum

Chenopodium philippianum, commonly known as the Philippine goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and typically grows in moist, disturbed soils. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with a rough texture and produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. Its seeds are often dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in various habitats.

Chenopodium philippianum is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
