Calamagrostis × hylanderi

Calamagrostis × hylanderi, commonly known as the hybrid reed grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to the United States.

It features narrow, silver-green leaves that turn coppery in the fall, creating a striking visual display. The plant produces feathery, golden-brown plumes in late summer, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often used in wetland restoration projects.

Its growth habit is clump-forming, making it suitable for both natural and cultivated landscapes.
