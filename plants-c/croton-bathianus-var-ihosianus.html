Croton bathianus var. ihosianus

Croton bathianus var. ihosianus, commonly known as the Ihosia croton, is a unique and striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep reds to yellows and greens, depending on the season and light conditions. The plant typically features thick, waxy leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting veins. It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height, making it a dramatic addition to gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in warm, sunny climates where it thrives in well-drained soils.
