Calliandra amblyphylla

Calliandra amblyphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences composed of numerous small, pink to red flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Calliandra amblyphylla is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive floral display and drought tolerance.
