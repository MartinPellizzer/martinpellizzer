Clematis esquirolii

Clematis esquirolii, commonly known as the Esquiroli clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, with a distinctive, recurved form. The plant has compound leaves with three to five oval leaflets and produces small, red berries that mature in autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis esquirolii is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and relatively hardy nature.
