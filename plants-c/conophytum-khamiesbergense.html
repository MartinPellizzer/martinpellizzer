Conophytum khamiesbergense

Conophytum khamiesbergense, commonly known as the Khamiesberg conophytum, is a succulent plant native to Namibia.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
