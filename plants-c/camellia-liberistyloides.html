Camellia liberistyloides

Camellia liberistyloides, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with showy, double petals and a prominent central cone.

Camellia liberistyloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter blooming habit.
