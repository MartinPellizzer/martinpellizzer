Cyperus eragrostis var. micronux

Cyperus eragrostis var. micronux, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Its botanical structure is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and the presence of triangular stems, typical of many sedges.
