Cynosurus cristatus f. tenuis

Cynosurus cristatus f. tenuis, commonly known as the slender cock's-comb grass, is a variant of the cock's-comb grass species.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and meadows. The plant features a tufted growth habit with narrow, blade-like leaves and distinctive, plume-like inflorescences. Its foliage is generally more delicate and less densely packed compared to the typical form of Cynosurus cristatus.
