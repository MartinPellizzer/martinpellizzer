Cotoneaster cotoneaster var. pluriflorus

Cotoneaster cotoneaster var. pluriflorus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It has a compact growth habit with dense, dark green foliage.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
