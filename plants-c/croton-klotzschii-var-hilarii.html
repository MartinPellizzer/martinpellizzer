Croton klotzschii var. hilarii

Croton klotzschii var. hilarii, commonly known as the Hilarii Croton, is a species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful foliage, which can range from deep red to vibrant green and yellow. The plant typically features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually green or yellow in color. It is a shrub or small tree that thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with suitable conditions.
