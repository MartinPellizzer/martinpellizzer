Curcuma burttii

It is a rhizomatous perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces large, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive, fragrant aroma.

The rhizomes of Curcuma burttii are used in traditional medicine and have similar properties to other turmeric species.
