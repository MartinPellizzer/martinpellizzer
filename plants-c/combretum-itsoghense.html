Combretum itsoghense

Combretum itsoghense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warmer climates.
