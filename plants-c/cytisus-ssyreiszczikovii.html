Cytisus ssyreiszczikovii

Cytisus ssyreiszczikovii, commonly known as the Mongolian broom, is a shrub native to Mongolia and northern China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky areas. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are often visited by pollinators like bees.

Its hardy nature makes it adaptable to harsh environmental conditions.
