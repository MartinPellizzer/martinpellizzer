Cansjera polystachya

Cansjera polystachya, commonly known as the giant bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces long, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering occurs in clusters, producing small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale yellow.

Cansjera polystachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
