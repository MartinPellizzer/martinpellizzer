Cattleya × amesiana

Cattleya × amesiana, commonly known as the Amesiana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from Cattleya and Laelia orchids.

It is native to the highlands of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This plant is characterized by its large, fragrant, and showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and yellow. It has sympodial growth, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and its leaves are typically broad and strap-like.

Cattleya × amesiana is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
