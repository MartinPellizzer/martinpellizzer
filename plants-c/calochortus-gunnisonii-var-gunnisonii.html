Calochortus gunnisonii var. gunnisonii

Calochortus gunnisonii var. gunnisonii, commonly known as Gunnison's mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes a variety of flowering plants commonly referred to as mariposa lilies.
