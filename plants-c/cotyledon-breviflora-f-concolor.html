Cotyledon breviflora f. concolor

Cotyledon breviflora f. concolor, commonly known as the variegated leaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container gardening.
