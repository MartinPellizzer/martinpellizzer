Cattleya lemoniana

Cattleya lemoniana, commonly known as the Lemon Blossom Orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive lemon-yellow hue. The plant produces pendulous blooms that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a subtle sweet scent. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cattleya lemoniana is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
