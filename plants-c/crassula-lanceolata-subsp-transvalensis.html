Crassula lanceolata subsp. transvalensis

Crassula lanceolata subsp.

transvalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge, depending on light conditions.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
