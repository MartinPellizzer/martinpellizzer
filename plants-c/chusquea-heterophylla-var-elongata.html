Chusquea heterophylla var. elongata

Chusquea heterophylla var. elongata, commonly known as the long-leaf weeping bamboo, is a variant of the Chusquea genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, arching leaves that give it a cascading appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests. Its culms are slender and exhibit a fibrous texture, while the leaves are narrow and have a waxy surface.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique aesthetic.
