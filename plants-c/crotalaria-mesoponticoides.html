Crotalaria mesoponticoides

Crotalaria mesoponticoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has small, bean-like pods that contain seeds, and it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The species is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
