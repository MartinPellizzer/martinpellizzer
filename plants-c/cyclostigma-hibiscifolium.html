Cyclostigma hibiscifolium

Cyclostigma hibiscifolium, commonly known as the Hibiscifolium cyclostigma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, hibiscus-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and emits a mild fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Cyclostigma hibiscifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
