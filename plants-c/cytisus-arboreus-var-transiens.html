Cytisus arboreus var. transiens

Cytisus arboreus var. transiens, commonly known as the Transylvanian broom, is a shrub native to the Carpathian region.

It features slender, woody stems with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
