Cirsium × bourgaeanum

Cirsium × bourgaeanum, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, purple-blue flower heads and robust, upright stems. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. Its leaves are generally lance-shaped with serrated edges and a rough texture.

Cirsium × bourgaeanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
