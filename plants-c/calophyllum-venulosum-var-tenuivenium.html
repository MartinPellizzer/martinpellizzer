Calophyllum venulosum var. tenuivenium

Calophyllum venulosum var. tenuivenium, commonly known as the slender-veined calophyllum, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Calophyllaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, with five petals, and the fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds. This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.
