Cymbidium xichouense

Cymbidium xichouense, commonly known as the Xichou orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, branched inflorescence and large, showy flowers with a distinctive color pattern. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, humid climates. Its leaves are sword-shaped and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value.

This orchid is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
