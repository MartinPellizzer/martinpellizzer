Citrus × aurantium var. fetifera

Citrus × aurantium var. fetifera, commonly known as the pomelo or sweet orange.

It is a citrus fruit characterized by its large, thick-skinned fruit with a sweet and slightly acidic flavor. The plant is a small evergreen tree with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. It thrives in subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

The fruit is widely cultivated for both fresh consumption and processing into juices and other products.
