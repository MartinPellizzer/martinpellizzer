Cischweinfia glicensteinii

Cischweinfia glicensteinii, commonly known as the red-flowered cischweinfia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
