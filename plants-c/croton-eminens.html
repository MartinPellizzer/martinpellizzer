Croton eminens

Croton eminens, commonly known as the giant croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, colorful, and often variegated leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its unique, branched inflorescence that produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton eminens thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
