Cookia chlorosperma

Cookia chlorosperma, commonly known as the yellow cookia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a slightly hairy texture.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
