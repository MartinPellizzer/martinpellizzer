Cineraria hypoleuca

Cineraria hypoleuca, commonly known as the white cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its blue to violet flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Cineraria hypoleuca is a perennial species that prefers full sun to partial shade.
