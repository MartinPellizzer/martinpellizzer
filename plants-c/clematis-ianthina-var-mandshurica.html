Clematis ianthina var. mandshurica

Clematis ianthina var. mandshurica, commonly known as the Siberian Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, with a distinctive spurred structure. The plant produces small, round berries that are edible but not particularly flavorful.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.
