Chenopodium biebersteinianum

Chenopodium biebersteinianum, commonly known as desert spinach, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Chenopodium biebersteinianum is also valued for its nutritional content, containing high levels of vitamins and minerals.
