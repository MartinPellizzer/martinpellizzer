Ceropegia tribounii

Ceropegia tribounii, commonly known as the silver leaf string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, succulent stems that often have a silvery or white coating. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while the flowers are small and tubular, often with a distinctive waxy appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This plant is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
