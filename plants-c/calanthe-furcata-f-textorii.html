Calanthe furcata f. textorii

Calanthe furcata f. textorii, commonly known as the Textor's calanthe, is a rare and elegant orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with intricate, spurred petals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
