Calanthe mindorensis

Calanthe mindorensis, commonly known as the Mindanao calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within mountainous regions. Its rhizomatous root system helps it thrive in rocky or forested soils.

Calanthe mindorensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
