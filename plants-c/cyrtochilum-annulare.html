Cyrtochilum annulare

Cyrtochilum annulare, commonly known as the annular orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a compact, hairy rhizome and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in the summer. The flower has a distinctive, tubular shape with a yellow lip and purple markings, making it visually striking. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cyrtochilum annulare is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
