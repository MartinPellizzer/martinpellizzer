Clematis × insignis

Clematis × insignis, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and blue, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, round berries that turn red when mature. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis × insignis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
