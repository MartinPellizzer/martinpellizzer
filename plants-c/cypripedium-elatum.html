Cypripedium elatum

Cypripedium elatum, commonly known as the showy lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and dense, spiraled inflorescence of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a single row along the stem.

Cypripedium elatum is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate and elegant appearance.
