Calamagrostis yanyuanensis

Calamagrostis yanyuanensis, commonly known as the Yanyuan reed, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and riverbanks.

Calamagrostis yanyuanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.
