Chiliotrichum diffusum f. typicum

Chiliotrichum diffusum f. typicum, commonly known as the common chiliotrichum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and leaves that are covered in fine, thread-like trichomes. Native to South America, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
