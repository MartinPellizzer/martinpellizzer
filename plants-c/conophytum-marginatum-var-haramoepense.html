Conophytum marginatum var. haramoepense

Conophytum marginatum var. haramoepense, commonly known as the "Cape karoo stone plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern with thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
