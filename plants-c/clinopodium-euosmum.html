Clinopodium euosmum

Clinopodium euosmum, commonly known as blue germander, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and along stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct blue-green hue. Its small, tubular flowers are usually white or pale blue and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Clinopodium euosmum is valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
