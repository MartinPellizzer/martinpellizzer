Crotalaria pilulicarpa var. schinzii

Crotalaria pilulicarpa var. schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine.
