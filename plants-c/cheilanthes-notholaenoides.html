Cheilanthes notholaenoides

Cheilanthes notholaenoides, commonly known as the rock fern or false shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, clustered fronds with a leathery texture and dark green coloration. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in arid gardens.
