Cattleya trichopiliochila var. virginalis

Cattleya trichopiliochila var. virginalis, commonly known as the Virgin's Cattleya, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, and showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
