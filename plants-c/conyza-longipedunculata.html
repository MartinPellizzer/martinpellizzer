Conyza longipedunculata

Conyza longipedunculata, commonly known as the long peduncle weed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are usually narrow and opposite, and it often thrives in disturbed soils.

Conyza longipedunculata is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through wind-dispersed seeds.
