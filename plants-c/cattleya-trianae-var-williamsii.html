Cattleya trianae var. williamsii

Cattleya trianae var. williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from pink to white with a yellow lip. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
