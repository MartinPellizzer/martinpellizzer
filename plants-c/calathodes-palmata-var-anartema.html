Calathodes palmata var. anartema

Calathodes palmata var. anartema, commonly known as the Anartema palmate.

This variant is a member of the Arecaceae family, characterized by its palm-like structure. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and potential ecological roles in its native habitats.
