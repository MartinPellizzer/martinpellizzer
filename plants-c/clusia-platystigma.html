Clusia platystigma

Clusia platystigma, commonly known as the broad-stemmed clusia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Clusia platystigma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
