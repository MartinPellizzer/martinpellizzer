Cattleyopsis lindenii

Cattleyopsis lindenii, commonly known as the Lindley's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cattleyopsis lindenii is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
