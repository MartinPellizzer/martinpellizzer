Chrysanthemum turkestanicum

Chrysanthemum turkestanicum, commonly known as the Turkistan chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It has lance-shaped leaves and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
