Cryptophoranthus acaulis

Cryptophoranthus acaulis, commonly known as the naked-stemmed cryptophoranthus, is a unique flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its lack of a true stem, with leaves directly emerging from the base of the plant. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically bright red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its distinctive appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
