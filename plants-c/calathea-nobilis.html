Calathea nobilis

Calathea nobilis, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that changes with the time of day, a phenomenon known as nyctinasty. The leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often feature intricate patterns in shades of green, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular houseplant among enthusiasts.
