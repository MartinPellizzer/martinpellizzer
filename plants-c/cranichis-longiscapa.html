Cranichis longiscapa

Cranichis longiscapa, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence with long, slender flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a distinct purple lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Cranichis longiscapa is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
