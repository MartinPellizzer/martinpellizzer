Colona floribunda

Colona floribunda, commonly known as the tropical hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a prominent yellow or orange center. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Colona floribunda is also known for its rapid growth and ability to produce abundant blooms throughout the growing season.
