Cylicodaphne

Cylicodaphne, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath the leaves. Cylicodaphne is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
