Centropogon holtonis

Centropogon holtonis, commonly known as the Holton's spikegrass, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It typically grows in wetland or marshy areas, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
