Cinchona humboldtiana

Cinchona humboldtiana, commonly known as the Humboldt cinchona, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that contain seeds, and it thrives in humid, high-altitude environments.
