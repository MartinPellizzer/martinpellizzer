Cynorkis guttata

Cynorkis guttata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow spots. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, pale yellow or greenish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Cynorkis guttata is often found in rocky or grassland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
