Conospermum densiflorum subsp. densiflorum

Conospermum densiflorum subsp.

densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered conospermum, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and features small, elliptical leaves that are typically grey-green in color.

The plant produces clusters of small, showy white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
