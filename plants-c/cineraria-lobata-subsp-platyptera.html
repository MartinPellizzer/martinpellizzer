Cineraria lobata subsp. platyptera

Cineraria lobata subsp.

platyptera, commonly known as the flat-leaf cineraria, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dark green, broad leaves and showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its broader, flatter leaves compared to other cineraria species.
