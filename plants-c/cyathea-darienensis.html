Cyathea darienensis

Cyathea darienensis, commonly known as the Darien tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, and the plant is known for its slow growth rate.

Cyathea darienensis plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
