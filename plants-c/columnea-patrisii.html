Columnea patrisii

Columnea patrisii, commonly known as the Patrisii flame flower, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Columnea patrisii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant floral display and ornamental value.
