Chrysocoma florida

Chrysocoma florida, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, yellow-green foliage and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Chrysocoma florida is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
