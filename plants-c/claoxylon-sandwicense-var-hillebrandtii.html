Claoxylon sandwicense var. hillebrandtii

Claoxylon sandwicense var. hillebrandtii, commonly known as the Hillebrandt's sandwicense.

It is a rare, small tree endemic to the Hawaiian Islands, specifically found on the island of Maui. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody fruits containing several seeds.
