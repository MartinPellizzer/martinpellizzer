Cassia leiandra

Cassia leiandra, commonly known as golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow inflorescences that appear in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia leiandra is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
