Coniogramme rubicaulis

Coniogramme rubicaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
