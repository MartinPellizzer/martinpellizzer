Cumbulu

Cumbulu, commonly known as the "Cape gooseberry," is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which also includes tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, orange-yellow berries that are rich in nutrients and have a tangy flavor. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Cumbulu is often cultivated for its edible fruits and is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
