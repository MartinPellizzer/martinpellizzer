Clusiella impressinervis

Clusiella impressinervis, commonly known as the impress-nerved clusiella, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Clusiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves with distinctive impressed veins. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
