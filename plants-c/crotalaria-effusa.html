Crotalaria effusa

Crotalaria effusa, commonly known as the slender crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers with a crown-like structure. The plant has palmate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological studies and horticultural interest.
