Cyperus conglomeratus var. ensifolius

Cyperus conglomeratus var. ensifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often surrounded by bracts, typical of the Cyperaceae family. It thrives in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas or along water bodies.

Its botanical structure makes it adaptable to various aquatic and semi-aquatic environments.
