Coleus tenuiflorus

Coleus tenuiflorus, commonly known as narrow-flowered coleus, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a distinct, slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coleus tenuiflorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
