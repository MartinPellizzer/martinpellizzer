Campanula reiseri

Campanula reiseri, commonly known as the Reiser's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine down.

Campanula reiseri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
