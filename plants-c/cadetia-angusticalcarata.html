Cadetia angusticalcarata

Cadetia angusticalcarata, commonly known as narrow-lobed rockcress, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or mountainous regions. It has basal leaves that are narrow and lobed, with a hairy texture.

Cadetia angusticalcarata is generally found in open, sunny habitats and is adapted to cold climates.
