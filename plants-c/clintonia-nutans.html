Clintonia nutans

Clintonia nutans, commonly known as the nodding whitebell, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of nodding, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and has lance-shaped, alternate leaves. Its flowers bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge.
