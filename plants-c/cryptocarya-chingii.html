Cryptocarya chingii

Cryptocarya chingii, commonly known as the Chinese cryptocarya, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often aromatic and have a dense, compact growth habit. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits.

Cryptocarya chingii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens.
