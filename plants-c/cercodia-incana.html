Cercodia incana

Cercodia incana, commonly known as the silverleaf cercodia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cercodia incana is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
