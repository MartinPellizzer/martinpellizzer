Croton tiglioides

Croton tiglioides, commonly known as the Indian croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves and vibrant, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain toxic seeds, which are used in traditional medicine. Its latex is also known for its potent irritant properties.

Due to its toxicity, it is important to handle the plant with care.
