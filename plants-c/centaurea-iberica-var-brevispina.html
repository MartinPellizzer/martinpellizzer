Centaurea iberica var. brevispina

Centaurea iberica var. brevispina, commonly known as the Iberian centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a perennial herb with upright, branched stems that typically grow up to 30 cm in height. The plant produces dense clusters of bright yellow flowers with reddish-brown bracts, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, arranged alternately along the stem.

This species is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
