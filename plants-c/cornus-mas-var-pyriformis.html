Cornus mas var. pyriformis

Cornus mas var. pyriformis, commonly known as the oval cornel.

This variant is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Europe and western Asia. It features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. The leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth margin and a slightly hairy underside.

It is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
