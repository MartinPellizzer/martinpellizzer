Campanula rigescens

Campanula rigescens, commonly known as the rigid bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to violet. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate, bell-like blooms.
