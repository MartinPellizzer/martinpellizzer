Cleisostoma ramosum

Cleisostoma ramosum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip petal. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Cleisostoma ramosum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
