Ceratostylis cebolleta

Ceratostylis cebolleta, commonly known as the Cebolleta orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically features a short rhizome with a single, fleshy leaf and produces small, fragrant orchid flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Ceratostylis cebolleta thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
