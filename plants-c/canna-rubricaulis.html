Canna rubricaulis

Canna rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed cannas, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central and South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, with a distinctive red or purple stem that adds visual interest. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, often with a contrasting red or orange center. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires moist, well-draining soil.

Canna rubricaulis is often used in gardens for its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
