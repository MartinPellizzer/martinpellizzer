Calamagrostis × laxior

Calamagrostis × laxior, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a hybrid grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and silvery-blue foliage that adds a striking visual appeal to gardens. The plant produces feathery plumes in late summer, which are both ornamental and beneficial for pollinators. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often used in wetland restoration projects.

Calamagrostis × laxior is a hardy perennial that requires minimal maintenance once established.
