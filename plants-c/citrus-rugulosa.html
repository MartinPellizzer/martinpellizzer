Citrus rugulosa

Citrus rugulosa, commonly known as the rough lemon.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its rough, pebbled bark and oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are typically used for their high acidity and essential oils. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.

Citrus rugulosa is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various climates.
