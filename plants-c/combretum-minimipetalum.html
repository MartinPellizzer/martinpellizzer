Combretum minimipetalum

Combretum minimipetalum, commonly known as the small-petaled flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences that appear in clusters. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange with showy bracts. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant in subtropical regions.

The seeds are dispersed by wind, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
