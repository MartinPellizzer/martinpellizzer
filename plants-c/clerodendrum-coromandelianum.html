Clerodendrum coromandelianum

Clerodendrum coromandelianum, commonly known as the Indian flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a rough texture.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental gardening.
