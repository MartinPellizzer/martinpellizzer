Coelidium roseum

Coelidium roseum, commonly known as the roseum orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety pink petals and a slender, erect stem. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often displaying a unique blend of colors ranging from pale pink to deep rose.

Coelidium roseum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in greenhouse environments due to its specific climate requirements.
