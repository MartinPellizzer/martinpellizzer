Crucifera wahlenbergii

Crucifera wahlenbergii, commonly known as the Wahlenberg's crucifer, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant produces slender, elongated pods that contain several seeds. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both ecological and horticultural studies.
