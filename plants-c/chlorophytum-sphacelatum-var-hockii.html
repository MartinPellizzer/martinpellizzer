Chlorophytum sphacelatum var. hockii

Chlorophytum sphacelatum var. hockii, commonly known as Hock's spider plant, is a varietal form of the spider plant characterized by its unique foliage.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically bright green with a silvery-blue stripe running down the center. This plant is native to South Africa and thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to produce offsets.

The plant is also known for its air-purifying qualities and adaptability to various indoor environments.
