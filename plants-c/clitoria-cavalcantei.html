Clitoria cavalcantei

Clitoria cavalcantei, commonly known as the purple orchid bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are often clustered in racemes. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in secondary forests or disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
