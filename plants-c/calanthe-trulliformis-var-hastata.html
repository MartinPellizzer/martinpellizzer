Calanthe trulliformis var. hastata

Calanthe trulliformis var. hastata, commonly known as the broad-leafed calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and strikingly colored flowers that typically range from white to pink. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are arranged in a spike and bloom during the spring and early summer.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its beauty and unique foliage.
