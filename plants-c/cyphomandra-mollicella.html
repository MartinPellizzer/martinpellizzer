Cyphomandra mollicella

Cyphomandra mollicella, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or yellow bell pepper, is a species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant that produces yellow, bell-shaped flowers. The plant's fruits are small, yellow, and have a tart, citrus-like flavor. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
