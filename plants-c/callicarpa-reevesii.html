Callicarpa reevesii

Callicarpa reevesii, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant purple to red berries that appear in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The berries are not only ornamental but also attract birds and other wildlife.
