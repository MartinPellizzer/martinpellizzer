Cineraria spathulifolia

Cineraria spathulifolia, commonly known as the spoonleaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
