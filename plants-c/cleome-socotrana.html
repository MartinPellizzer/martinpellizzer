Cleome socotrana

Cleome socotrana, commonly known as Socotra cleome, is a flowering plant native to the Socotra archipelago.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, yellow or white flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptation to the harsh climate of its native habitat. Its structure includes branching stems and small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.
