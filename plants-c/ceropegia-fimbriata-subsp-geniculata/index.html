Ceropegia Fimbriata Subsp. Geniculata

Ceropegia fimbriata subsp. geniculata is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. Known for its delicate, trailing stems and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. This subspecies is particularly valued for its ability to thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage often features intricate patterns and a soft, velvety texture. Despite its beauty, it requires careful watering to avoid root rot and ensure optimal growth.
