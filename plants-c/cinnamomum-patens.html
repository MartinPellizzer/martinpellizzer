Cinnamomum patens

Cinnamomum patens, commonly known as the cinnamon tree or wild cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic bark and leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark purple berries. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinct cinnamon-like aroma.

The tree is valued for its essential oils, which are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.
