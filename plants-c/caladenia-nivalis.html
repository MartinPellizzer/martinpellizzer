Caladenia nivalis

Caladenia nivalis, commonly known as the Snowy Spider Orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to south-eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance with a single, erect stem bearing a single, showy pink or white flower. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. It blooms during the cooler months, usually from August to October, and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is also a protected species in some regions.
