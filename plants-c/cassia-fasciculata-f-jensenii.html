Cassia fasciculata f. jensenii

Cassia fasciculata f. jensenii, commonly known as the Jensen's clustered cassia, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its dense foliage and long-lasting blooms.
