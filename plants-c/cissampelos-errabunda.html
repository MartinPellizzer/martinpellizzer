Cissampelos errabunda

Cissampelos errabunda, commonly known as the African moon vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
