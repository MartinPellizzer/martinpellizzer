Cleome diffusa

Cleome diffusa, commonly known as spider flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and it produces seed pods that split open to release seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Cleome diffusa is often used as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and easy maintenance.
