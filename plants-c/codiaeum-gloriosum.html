Codiaeum gloriosum

Codiaeum gloriosum, commonly known as the flame plant or croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its striking foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Codiaeum gloriosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its eye-catching appearance.
