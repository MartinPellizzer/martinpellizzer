Catasetum pileatum

Catasetum pileatum, commonly known as the velvet tassel orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its striking, hairy, and often brightly colored flowers that resemble tassels. Its inflorescence consists of a spike bearing multiple showy, fragrant blooms.
