Canna saturate-rubra

Canna saturate-rubra, commonly known as the red flame cannas, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow center. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Canna saturate-rubra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a focal point in tropical landscapes.
