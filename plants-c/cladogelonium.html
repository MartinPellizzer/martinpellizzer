Cladogelonium

Cladogelonium, commonly known as the golden star, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow star-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. Cladogelonium thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and low maintenance requirements.
