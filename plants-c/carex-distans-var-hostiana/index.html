Carex Distans Var. Hostiana

Carex distans var. hostiana is a unique variant of the common sedges known for its striking appearance and adaptability. This plant thrives in moist environments, often found in wetlands and along stream banks. Its foliage features a mix of green and silver hues, creating an eye-catching visual effect. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits, supporting local wildlife and improving soil stability. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively underappreciated in the world of horticulture.
