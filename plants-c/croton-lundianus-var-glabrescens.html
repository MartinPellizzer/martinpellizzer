Croton lundianus var. glabrescens

Croton lundianus var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved croton, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are smooth and often slightly waxy in texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
