Calamus wedaensis

Calamus wedaensis, commonly known as the Weda calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender rhizomes and elongated, strap-like leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spadices.
