Cyperus tenuiculmis var. tenuiculmis

Cyperus tenuiculmis var. tenuiculmis, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread and establish in moist environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
