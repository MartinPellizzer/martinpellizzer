Cryptocarya brevipes

Cryptocarya brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed candlewood, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. It produces small white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, reddish-brown fruits.

Cryptocarya brevipes is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in local cultures.
