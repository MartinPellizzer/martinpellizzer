Clausia turkestanica var. subintegrifolia

Clausia turkestanica var. subintegrifolia, commonly known as the Turkistan clausia, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, elliptical leaves with a distinctive subintegrifoliate margin. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
