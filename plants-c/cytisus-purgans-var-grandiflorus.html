Cytisus purgans var. grandiflorus

Cytisus purgans var. grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, woody stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in racemes, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal or dry garden environments.
