Combretum hensii var. pyriforme

Combretum hensii var. pyriforme, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in summer, consisting of small, bright orange to red flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
