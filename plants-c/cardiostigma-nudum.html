Cardiostigma nudum

Cardiostigma nudum, commonly known as the naked heartleaf, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or forest margins.
