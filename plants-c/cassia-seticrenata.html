Cassia seticrenata

Cassia seticrenata, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright orange to red inflorescences that appear in late summer. The plant has compound leaves with multiple oval-shaped leaflets, and its flowers are typically clustered in terminal panicles. Cassia seticrenata thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in warm climates.
