Crepis schachtii

Crepis schachtii, commonly known as the Schacht's hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Crepis schachtii is often found in temperate climates and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
