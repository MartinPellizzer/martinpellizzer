Chrysolaena desertorum

Chrysolaena desertorum, commonly known as the desert chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to high temperatures and low rainfall.

Chrysolaena desertorum is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
