Ceradenia knightii

Ceradenia knightii, commonly known as the Knight's ceradenia, is a rare orchid species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant prefers high-altitude environments with cool, humid conditions and is often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it has a short, underground rhizome.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Ceradenia knightii is considered a threatened species.
