Clusia utilis

Clusia utilis, commonly known as the wax plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often green on the top and white or silver on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Clusia utilis is also known for its resilience and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
