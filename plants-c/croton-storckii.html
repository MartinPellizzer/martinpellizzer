Croton storckii

Croton storckii, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features alternate, simple, and often hairy leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, with yellowish or greenish petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release the seeds.
