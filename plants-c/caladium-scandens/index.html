Caladium Scandens

Caladium scandens is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of pink, red, and white. Native to the rainforests of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with plenty of indirect light. This climbing plant can grow up to 10 feet tall, making it a dramatic addition to any indoor or outdoor garden. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for decorating patios, terraces, and hanging baskets. With proper care, Caladium scandens can become a stunning centerpiece for plant lovers seeking bold, colorful beauty.
