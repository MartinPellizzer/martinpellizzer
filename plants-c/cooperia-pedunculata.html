Cooperia pedunculata

Cooperia pedunculata, commonly known as the clustered sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Cooperia pedunculata plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
