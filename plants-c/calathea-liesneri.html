Calathea liesneri

Calathea liesneri, commonly known as the velvetleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display intricate patterns of green, silver, and purple. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior decoration due to its aesthetic appeal.
