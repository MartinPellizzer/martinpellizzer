Clitoria irwinii

Clitoria irwinii, commonly known as the "Blue Bean" or "Velvet Bean," is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to blue flowers, which are often bi-lobed and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. Clitoria irwinii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowers.

It is also used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
