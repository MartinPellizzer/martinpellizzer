Cypripedium macranthos var. flavum

Cypripedium macranthos var. flavum, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper, is a striking orchid native to the eastern United States.

It features a large, showy yellow pouch-like flower that attracts pollinators such as bees and wasps. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and has a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, which helps retain moisture.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
