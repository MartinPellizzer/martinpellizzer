Caladium picturatum var. porphyroneuron

Caladium picturatum var. porphyroneuron, commonly known as the Crimson Queen caladium, is a tropical perennial known for its striking, colorful foliage.

Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with vibrant red, orange, and yellow hues, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor spaces due to its aesthetic appeal.

The foliage is the primary feature, as the plant produces minimal flowers.
