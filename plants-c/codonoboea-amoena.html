Codonoboea amoena

Codonoboea amoena, commonly known as the pink orchid.

It is a flowering plant native to Madagascar, belonging to the orchid family (Orchidaceae). The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to white. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Codonoboea amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and attractive blooms.
