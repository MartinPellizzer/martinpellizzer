Claytonia tenuis

Claytonia tenuis, commonly known as the slender spring beauty, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, rocky soils and is often found in forested areas. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are usually white or pale pink, with five petals arranged in a bell shape. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms in early spring.

Claytonia tenuis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its early blooming habit.
