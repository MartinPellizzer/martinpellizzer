Clueria Laciniata

Clueria laciniata is a striking plant known for its unique, fern-like leaves and vibrant blossoms. Native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, tropical climates. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. Its foliage can be used in traditional medicine, adding to its cultural significance. Despite its beauty, it requires careful care to flourish in non-native environments.
