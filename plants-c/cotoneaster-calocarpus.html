Cotoneaster calocarpus

Cotoneaster calocarpus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy leaves that turn red in the fall, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Cotoneaster calocarpus is often used in landscaping for its compact growth and seasonal color changes.
