Crantzia chinensis

Crantzia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese crantzia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has small, green, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Crantzia chinensis is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
