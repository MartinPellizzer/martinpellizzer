Capparis hasseltiana

Capparis hasseltiana, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has woody stems and produces small, round, greenish-yellow fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
