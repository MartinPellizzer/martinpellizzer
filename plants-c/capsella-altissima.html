Capsella altissima

Capsella altissima, commonly known as the tall chickweed, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has alternate, oval-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions, often growing in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Capsella altissima is typically found in moist environments and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
