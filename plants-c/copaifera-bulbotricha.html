Copaifera bulbotricha

Copaifera bulbotricha, commonly known as the Brazilian copaiba, is a species of tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall stature and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a valuable resin, which has been traditionally used for medicinal purposes. Its bark and wood are also used in various local crafts and industries.

Copaifera bulbotricha plays an important role in the ecological balance of its habitat.
