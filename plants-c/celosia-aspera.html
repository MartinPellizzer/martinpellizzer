Celosia aspera

Celosia aspera, commonly known as firecracker grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spiky bracts that resemble small fireworks. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking red, orange, and yellow hues. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Celosia aspera is also known for its resilience and ability to grow in a variety of climates.
