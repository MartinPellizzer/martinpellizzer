Ceropegia lenewtonii

Ceropegia lenewtonii, commonly known as the silver leaf string of hearts, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-white coating, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
