Cymbidium × wiganianum

Cymbidium × wiganianum, commonly known as the Wiganian orchid, is a hybrid orchid species originating from crosses between Cymbidium goeringii and Cymbidium lowianum.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, often dark-colored flowers. The plant is native to China and is well adapted to temperate climates, thriving in cool, humid conditions. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in various soil types.

It is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in horticulture for its longevity and aesthetic appeal.
