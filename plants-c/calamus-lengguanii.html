Calamus lengguanii

Calamus lengguanii, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or Chinese calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender rhizomes and large, arrowhead-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are typically enclosed in a spadix and a spathe.

Calamus lengguanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
