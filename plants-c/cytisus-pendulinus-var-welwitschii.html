Cytisus pendulinus var. welwitschii

Cytisus pendulinus var. welwitschii, commonly known as the Cape Barbed Wire Bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in rocky areas and along coastal regions.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a popular choice for landscaping in dry climates.
