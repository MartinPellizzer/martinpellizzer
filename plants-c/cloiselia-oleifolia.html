Cloiselia oleifolia

Cloiselia oleifolia, commonly known as the oliveleaf vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, which are often fragrant. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, hard seeds.

Cloiselia oleifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
