Cotoneaster horizontalis var. typicus

Cotoneaster horizontalis var. typicus, commonly known as the horizontal cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are an important food source for birds. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and persistent fruit, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
