Cattleya alvarenguensis

Cattleya alvarenguensis, commonly known as the Alvarenga Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink, often with a contrasting lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs and possesses long, narrow leaves that are usually dark green. It blooms once a year, typically in the summer months, and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
