Cyathea merapiensis

Cyathea merapiensis, commonly known as the Merapi tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, contributing to its striking appearance.

Cyathea merapiensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
