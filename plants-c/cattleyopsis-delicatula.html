Cattleyopsis delicatula

Cattleyopsis delicatula, commonly known as the delicate cattleya orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow or green throat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
