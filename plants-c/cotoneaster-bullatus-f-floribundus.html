Cotoneaster bullatus f. floribundus

Cotoneaster bullatus f. floribundus, commonly known as the profuse-flowered cotoneaster, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of white to pale pink, typically in late spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
