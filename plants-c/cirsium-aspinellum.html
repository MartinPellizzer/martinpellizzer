Cirsium aspinellum

Cirsium aspinellum, commonly known as the prickly centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, calcareous soils and thrives in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its flowering stems are often branched and bear several flower heads.

Cirsium aspinellum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.
