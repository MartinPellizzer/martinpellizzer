Clusia modesta

Clusia modesta, commonly known as the waxleaf clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color. Clusia modesta is well adapted to humid, shady environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to tolerate a variety of growing conditions.
