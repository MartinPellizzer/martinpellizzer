Chlorophytum wilmsii

Chlorophytum wilmsii, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, grass-like flowers that are usually green or white in color. It is a hardy species that thrives in well-drained soil and moderate light conditions.

Chlorophytum wilmsii is also known for its ability to produce offsets, making it easy to propagate.
