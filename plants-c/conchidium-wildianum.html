Conchidium wildianum

Conchidium wildianum, commonly known as the wildian wild cucumber, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like pumpkins and melons. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a elongated, spiky capsule that contains numerous seeds.
