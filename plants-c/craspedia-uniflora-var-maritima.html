Craspedia uniflora var. maritima

Craspedia uniflora var. maritima, commonly known as the sea dandelion, is a coastal plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual with yellow flowers and a distinctive woolly foliage. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils along the shoreline, tolerating salt spray and wind. Its flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing dunes and preventing erosion in coastal environments.
