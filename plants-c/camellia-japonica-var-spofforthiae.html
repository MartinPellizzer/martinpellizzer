Camellia japonica var. spofforthiae

Camellia japonica var. spofforthiae, commonly known as the Spofforth's camellia, is a rare and ornamental evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink, often with a distinctive white or pale pink base. The plant has glossy, elliptical leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique ornamental plant.

This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in botanical collections.
