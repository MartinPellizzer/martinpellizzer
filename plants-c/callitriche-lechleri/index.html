Callitriche Lechleri

Callitriche lechleri, also known as the "dragon's blood tree," is a rare and ancient plant native to the rainforests of South America. Its sap is renowned for its deep red color and potent medicinal properties. Historically, it has been used by indigenous cultures for healing wounds and treating various ailments. Despite its significance, the plant faces threats from habitat loss and overharvesting. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
