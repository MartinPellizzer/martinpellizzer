Crocodilodes atractylodes

Crocodilodes atractylodes, commonly known as Chinese yarrow, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.
