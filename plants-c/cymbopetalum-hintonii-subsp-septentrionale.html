Cymbopetalum hintonii subsp. septentrionale

Cymbopetalum hintonii subsp.

The plant has slender, climbing stems that often require support as they grow, and its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
