Cattleya labiata var. caerulea

Cattleya labiata var. caerulea, commonly known as the Blue Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking blue-violet flowers.

It belongs to the Cymbidieae subfamily and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features tall, upright pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves that support its showy inflorescences. Its flowers typically bloom in late summer or early autumn, producing a fragrant, spurred shape.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
