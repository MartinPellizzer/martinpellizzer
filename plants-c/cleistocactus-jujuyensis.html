Cleistocactus jujuyensis

Cleistocactus jujuyensis, commonly known as the Jujuy candle cactus, is a species native to the Andes mountains of Argentina.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive candle-like appearance, characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise spiny form. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and botanical gardens.
