Chamaedorea simplicifrons

Chamaedorea simplicifrons, commonly known as the simple-fruited bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, upright stems with feather-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
