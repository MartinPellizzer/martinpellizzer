Calophyllum pentapetalum var. pentapetalum

Calophyllum pentapetalum var. pentapetalum, commonly known as the five-petaled calophyllum, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and has economic importance in local communities.
