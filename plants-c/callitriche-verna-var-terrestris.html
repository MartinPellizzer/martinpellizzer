Callitriche verna var. terrestris

Callitriche verna var. terrestris, commonly known as the spring water-star, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to temperate regions.

It typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often near the edges of ponds, streams, and ditches. The plant has tiny, star-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces small white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring.

This variety is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in wetland environments.
