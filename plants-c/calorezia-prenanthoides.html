Calorezia prenanthoides

Calorezia prenanthoides, commonly known as the Southern Cross-flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while its flowers are small and pale, often appearing in a distinctive cross shape.
