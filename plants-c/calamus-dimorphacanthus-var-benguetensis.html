Calamus dimorphacanthus var. benguetensis

Calamus dimorphacanthus var. benguetensis, commonly known as the Benguet calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Benguet. The plant is characterized by its long, slender rhizomes and narrow, linear leaves. It produces inflorescences with spadices, typical of the Araceae family, and has small, inconspicuous flowers.

This variety is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental features.
