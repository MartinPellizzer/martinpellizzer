Chamaecyparis boursieri var. compacta

Chamaecyparis boursieri var. compacta, commonly known as the Japanese false cypress or compact Japanese cypress, is a compact evergreen conifer native to Japan.

It features dense, scale-like foliage that forms a rounded, bushy shape, making it ideal for small garden spaces. The plant has a slow growth rate and is often used as a topiary or ornamental shrub. Its foliage is dark green and retains its color year-round, providing visual interest in winter.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, thriving in temperate climates.
