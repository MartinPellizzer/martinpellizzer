Caryocar glabrum subsp. glabrum

Caryocar glabrum subsp.

glabrum, commonly known as the smooth nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America. It belongs to the family Caryocaraceae and is characterized by its smooth, bark-like trunk and dense, leathery leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by distinctive, fleshy, red berries that contain a single large, hard seed. Its fruit is valued for its aromatic seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The species is adapted to humid, tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
