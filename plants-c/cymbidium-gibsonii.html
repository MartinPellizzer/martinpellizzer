Cymbidium gibsonii

Cymbidium gibsonii, commonly known as the Gibson's orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern China and northern Vietnam.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. The plant produces tall, upright stems with clustered, fragrant flowers that are usually pink to purple in color. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

Cymbidium gibsonii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
