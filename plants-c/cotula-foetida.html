Cotula foetida

Cotula foetida, commonly known as stinkcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small annual herb that grows in open, sandy or gravelly areas. The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive, strong, unpleasant odor.

Cotula foetida is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
