Clematis meyeniana var. granulata

Clematis meyeniana var. granulata, commonly known as the granulate clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to South America.

It features showy, single or double flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves with a distinctive granular texture on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis meyeniana var. granulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and can be trained on trellises or fences.
