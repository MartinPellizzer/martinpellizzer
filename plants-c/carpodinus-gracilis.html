Carpodinus gracilis

Carpodinus gracilis, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or near streams. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers in clusters. Its flowering period usually occurs during the wet season, and it is often found in association with other epiphytic plants.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
