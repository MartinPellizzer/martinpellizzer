Ceropegia verdickii

Ceropegia verdickii, commonly known as the velvet tangle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Ceropegia verdickii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
