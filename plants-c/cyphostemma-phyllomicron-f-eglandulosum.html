Cyphostemma phyllomicron f. eglandulosum

Cyphostemma phyllomicron f. eglandulosum, commonly known as the glandular leaf cyphostemma, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are often covered with fine, glandular hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Its stems are cylindrical and can branch out, forming dense, compact growth.

It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
