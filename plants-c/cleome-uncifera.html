Cleome uncifera

Cleome uncifera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a pointed tip and a smooth surface. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance ornamental plants.

It is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
