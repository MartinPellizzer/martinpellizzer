Cola gigantea var. glabrescens

Cola gigantea var. glabrescens, commonly known as the giant cola or smooth cola, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces elliptical, woody pods containing several seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of caffeine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
