Cotyledon mairei

Cotyledon mairei, commonly known as the Maire's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
