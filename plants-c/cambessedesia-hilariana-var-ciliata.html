Cambessedesia hilariana var. ciliata

Cambessedesia hilariana var. ciliata, commonly known as the silky leaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a sprawling growth habit and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are typically ovate with ciliate margins, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
