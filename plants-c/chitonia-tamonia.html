Chitonia tamonia

Chitonia tamonia, commonly known as the red chitons, is a species of sea snail found in tropical and subtropical marine environments.

It belongs to the class Polyplacophora, characterized by its calcareous plates that cover its body. The shell of Chitonia tamonia is typically elongated and has a distinctive red or orange coloration. It is often found attached to rocks or other substrates in shallow coastal waters.
