Calathea luciani

Calathea luciani, commonly known as the "Carnival Plant," is a striking tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which features intricate patterns of pink, red, and green on its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its unique leaf coloration changes throughout the day, creating a dynamic visual effect.

Calathea luciani requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to maintain its health and aesthetic appeal.
