Carex haematostachys

Carex haematostachys, commonly known as the blood-stem sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly in regions such as South Africa and Namibia. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, cylindrical leaves and clustered flowering spikes. Its stems are often reddish or purplish, giving it its common name.
