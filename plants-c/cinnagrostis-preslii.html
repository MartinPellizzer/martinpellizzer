Cinnagrostis preslii

Cinnagrostis preslii, commonly known as the Preslii goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its compact, upright growth habit. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cinnagrostis preslii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environments.
