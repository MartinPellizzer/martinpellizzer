Cyperus asconeglectus

Cyperus asconeglectus, commonly known as the swamp spikerush, is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are usually green or brown in color.

Cyperus asconeglectus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
