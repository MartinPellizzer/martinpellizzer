Centrosema grazielae

Centrosema grazielae, commonly known as the Grace's sensitiveplant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators. It is known for its rapid growth and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
