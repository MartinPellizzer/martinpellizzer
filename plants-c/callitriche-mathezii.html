Callitriche mathezii

Callitriche mathezii, commonly known as Mathez's water star, is a small aquatic plant native to the wetlands of southern China.

It typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments. The plant has tiny, star-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the water's surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
