Cyrtandra × kukuiensis

Cyrtandra × kukuiensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian tree-ferns, is a hybrid plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its tall, tree-like structure with a trunk and fronds. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
