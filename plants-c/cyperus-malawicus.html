Cyperus malawicus

Cyperus malawicus, commonly known as the Malawi flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to Africa, particularly found in wetland and marshy areas. The plant has slender, grass-like stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are usually narrow and grassy, often growing in dense clusters.

Cyperus malawicus is adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, making it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
