Cattleya walkeriana var. dolosa

Cattleya walkeriana var. dolosa, commonly known as the Dolosa Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences, and its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to thrive in humid environments. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.

This variety is highly prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
