Cryptocoryne tonkinensis

Cryptocoryne tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. Its rhizomes form dense mats, which help stabilize sediment and provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.

Cryptocoryne tonkinensis is popular in aquariums due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
