Coleus Confertiflorus

Coleus confertiflorus is a vibrant and striking plant known for its colorful leaves and unique floral display. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments with plenty of sunlight. Its foliage comes in shades of red, purple, pink, and green, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant's flowers, which appear in clusters, add an additional layer of visual interest to its already eye-catching appearance. While relatively easy to care for, it requires proper attention to soil, water, and light to flourish.
