Croton appertii

Croton appertii, commonly known as the red croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that range from deep red to yellow and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its showy, spiky inflorescences. Croton appertii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
