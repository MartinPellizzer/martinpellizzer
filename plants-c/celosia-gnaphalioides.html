Celosia gnaphalioides

Celosia gnaphalioides, commonly known as the woolly celosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces vibrant, plume-like flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its foliage is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

Celosia gnaphalioides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking floral display and drought tolerance.
