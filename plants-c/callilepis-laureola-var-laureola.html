Callilepis laureola var. laureola

Callilepis laureola var. laureola, commonly known as the Cape rose or African rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
