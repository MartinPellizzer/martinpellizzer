Citrus bigena

Citrus bigena, commonly known as the Chinese lemon.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its fragrant, glossy leaves and small, yellow-orange fruits. The fruit has a high concentration of citric acid and is often used in culinary applications. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
