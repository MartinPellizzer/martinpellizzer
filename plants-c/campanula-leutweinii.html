Campanula leutweinii

Campanula leutweinii, commonly known as the Leutwein bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems with lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula leutweinii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
