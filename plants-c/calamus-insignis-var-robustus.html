Calamus insignis var. robustus

Calamus insignis var. robustus, commonly known as the robust arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Araucariaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Its leaves are long and strap-shaped, often with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

This species is native to Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
