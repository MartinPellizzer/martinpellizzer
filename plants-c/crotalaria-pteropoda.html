Crotalaria pteropoda

Crotalaria pteropoda, commonly known as the winged cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive winged pods. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are usually yellow with reddish veins, and it produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Crotalaria pteropoda is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a useful plant in agricultural settings.
