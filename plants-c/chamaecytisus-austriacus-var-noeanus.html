Chamaecytisus austriacus var. noeanus

Chamaecytisus austriacus var. noeanus, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the Alps.

It features slender, woody stems covered in dense, soft, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in racemes. It is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it suitable for alpine gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for erosion control and ornamental landscaping.
