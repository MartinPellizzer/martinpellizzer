Centaurea hemiptera

Centaurea hemiptera, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and western Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers, which are typically small and clustered in dense heads. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with toothed margins. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and gardens.

Centaurea hemiptera is known for its rapid blooming cycle, often opening its flowers in response to sunlight.
