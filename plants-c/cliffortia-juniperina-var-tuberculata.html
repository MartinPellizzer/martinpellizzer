Cliffortia juniperina var. tuberculata

Cliffortia juniperina var. tuberculata, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and mountainous regions. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also valued for its ability to thrive in harsh, sunny conditions with minimal water requirements.
