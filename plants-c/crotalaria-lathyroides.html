Crotalaria lathyroides

Crotalaria lathyroides, commonly known as the horsegram or cluster bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with stipules and produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators. Its seeds are used in various cuisines and are also valued for their protein content.

Additionally, the plant has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
