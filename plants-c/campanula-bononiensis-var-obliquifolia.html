Campanula bononiensis var. obliquifolia

Campanula bononiensis var. obliquifolia, commonly known as the obliquifoliate bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to violet. The leaves are lanceolate and often have a slight twist, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
