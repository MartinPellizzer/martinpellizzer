Chenopodium mixtifolium

Chenopodium mixtifolium, commonly known as red goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its succulent, lobed leaves that are often reddish in color. Chenopodium mixtifolium is an annual herb that plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects.
