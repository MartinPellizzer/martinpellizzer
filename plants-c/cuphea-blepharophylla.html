Cuphea blepharophylla

Cuphea blepharophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf cuphea, is a striking herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

The plant belongs to the Lythraceae family, which includes other flowering plants such as poinsettias and Lagerstroemia. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant ornamental plants.
