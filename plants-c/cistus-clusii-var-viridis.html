Cistus clusii var. viridis

Cistus clusii var. viridis, commonly known as green rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, leathery leaves that are typically green, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
