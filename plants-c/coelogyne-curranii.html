Coelogyne curranii

Coelogyne curranii, commonly known as the Curran's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched inflorescence that bears numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne curranii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
