Colymbada Salonitana

Salonitana water-flea, scientifically known as Colymbada salonitana, is a rare and fascinating aquatic crustacean found in specific freshwater habitats. This tiny creature belongs to the order Cladocera, which plays a vital role in maintaining the ecological balance of its environment. Despite its small size, the Salonitana water-flea is an essential indicator species for water quality and ecosystem health. Its unique morphology and behavior make it a subject of interest for researchers studying freshwater biodiversity. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, this species faces increasing threats to its survival.
