Chresta alpestris

Chresta alpestris, commonly known as the alpine chresta, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps.

Chresta alpestris is adapted to cold climates and is often found in alpine meadows and tundra environments.
