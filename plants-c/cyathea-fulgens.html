Cyathea fulgens

Cyathea fulgens, commonly known as the shining tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan. Its fronds are typically bright green, with a glossy appearance that enhances its ornamental value.

Cyathea fulgens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
