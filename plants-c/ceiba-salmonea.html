Ceiba salmonea

Ceiba salmonea, commonly known as the salmon ceiba, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and expansive, umbrella-like crown. The tree has large, palmate leaves and produces showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing fibrous seeds, and it plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.

The wood of Ceiba salmonea is lightweight and has been used historically for various purposes.
