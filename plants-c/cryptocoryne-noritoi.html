Cryptocoryne noritoi

Cryptocoryne noritoi, commonly known as the Noritoi Cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and prefers soft, slightly acidic water. It is often found in slow-moving or still freshwater environments such as ponds and marshes.

Cryptocoryne noritoi is popular among aquarists for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
