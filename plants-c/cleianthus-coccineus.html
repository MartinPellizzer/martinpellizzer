Cleianthus coccineus

Cleianthus coccineus, commonly known as the scarlet cleianthus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.
