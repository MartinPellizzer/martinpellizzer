Convolvulus splendens

Convolvulus splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Morning Glory, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.

Its rapid growth and vibrant blooms make it a striking addition to gardens and landscapes.
