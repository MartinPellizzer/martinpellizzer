Camaridium densum

Camaridium densum, commonly known as the dense broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Camaridium densum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
