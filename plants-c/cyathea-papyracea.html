Cyathea papyracea

Cyathea papyracea, commonly known as the paper fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a fibrous, papery sheath that peels away as the plant matures. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Cyathea papyracea thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
