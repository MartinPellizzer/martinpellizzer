Caladenia × enigma

Caladenia × enigma, commonly known as the Enigma Spider-orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender sepals and petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats, often in full sun. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, which attract specific pollinators.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
