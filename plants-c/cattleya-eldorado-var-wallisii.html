Cattleya eldorado var. wallisii

Cattleya eldorado var. wallisii, commonly known as the Eldorado Wallisii orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces fragrant blooms that can last several weeks, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

The variety is noted for its vigor and ease of cultivation, though it requires specific care to thrive.
