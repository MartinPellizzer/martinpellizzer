Codonopsis hongii

Codonopsis hongii, commonly known as the Hongshan Codonopsis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant produces a cylindrical root that is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

The species is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its medicinal properties.
