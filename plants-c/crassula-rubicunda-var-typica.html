Crassula rubicunda var. typica

Crassula rubicunda var. typica, commonly known as the Red Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
