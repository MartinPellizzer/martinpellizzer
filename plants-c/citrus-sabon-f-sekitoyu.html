Citrus sabon f. sekitoyu

Citrus sabon f. sekitoyu, commonly known as the Sekitoyu citrus, is a hybrid citrus plant originating from Japan.

It is characterized by its small, round fruits with a thick, aromatic rind and a sweet, tangy pulp. The plant typically grows as a small shrub or tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The Sekitoyu citrus is valued for its unique flavor and is often used in both culinary and ornamental applications.
