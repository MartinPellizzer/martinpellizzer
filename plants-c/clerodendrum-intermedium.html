Clerodendrum intermedium

Clerodendrum intermedium, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped red bracts that enclose small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often overlooked due to the showy bracts. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Clerodendrum intermedium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
