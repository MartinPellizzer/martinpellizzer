Cyrtopodium fowliei

Cyrtopodium fowliei, commonly known as the Fowliei's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cyrtopodium fowliei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
