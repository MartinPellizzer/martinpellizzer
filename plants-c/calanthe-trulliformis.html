Calanthe trulliformis

Calanthe trulliformis, commonly known as the spurred calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with distinctive spurred petals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe trulliformis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
