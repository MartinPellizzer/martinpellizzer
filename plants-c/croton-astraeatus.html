Croton astraeatus

Croton astraeatus, commonly known as the celestial croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiny capsules containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
