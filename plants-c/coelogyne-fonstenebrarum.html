Coelogyne fonstenebrarum

Coelogyne fonstenebrarum, commonly known as the dark-veined coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with prominent, contrasting veins. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne fonstenebrarum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
