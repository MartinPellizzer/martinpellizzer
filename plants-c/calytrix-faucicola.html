Calytrix faucicola

Calytrix faucicola, commonly known as the coastal calytrix, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal regions, often in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in saline environments.

Calytrix faucicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
