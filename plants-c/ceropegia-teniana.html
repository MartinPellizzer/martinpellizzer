Ceropegia teniana

Ceropegia teniana, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is popular as a houseplant and for creating hanging displays.
