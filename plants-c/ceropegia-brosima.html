Ceropegia brosima

Ceropegia brosima, commonly known as the silver leaf or brosima ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia brosima is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
