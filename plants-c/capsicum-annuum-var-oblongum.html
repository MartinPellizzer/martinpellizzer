Capsicum annuum var. oblongum

Capsicum annuum var. oblongum, commonly known as the elongated pepper or oblong chili, is a variety of the common chili pepper.

It is characterized by its long, slender fruits that typically range from 5 to 15 centimeters in length. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth. Its flowers are yellow and followed by the characteristic red, orange, or yellow fruits depending on the variety and ripeness.

This variety is valued for its use in culinary applications and for its ornamental value in gardens.
