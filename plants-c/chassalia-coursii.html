Chassalia coursii

Chassalia coursii, commonly known as the Cape mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, herbaceous stems.
