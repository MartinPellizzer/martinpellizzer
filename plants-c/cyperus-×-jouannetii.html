Cyperus × jouannetii

Cyperus × jouannetii, commonly known as the variegated flatsedge, is a hybrid plant in the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, pond edges, and disturbed soils. Its foliage often exhibits variegation, with white or yellow markings along the leaf margins.

Cyperus × jouannetii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in waterlogged conditions.
