Centaurea schischkinii

Centaurea schischkinii, commonly known as Schischkin's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its bright blue to purple flowers and spiny bracts, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea schischkinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
