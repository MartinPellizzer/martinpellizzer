Centratherum englerianum

Centratherum englerianum, commonly known as the Engler's centatherum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the branches.

It is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.
