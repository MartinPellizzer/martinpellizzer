Calceolaria Glabrata Var. Meyeniana

Calceolaria glabrata var. meyeniana is a striking member of the Calceolaria genus known for its unique, pouch-like flowers. Native to the Andes mountains of South America, this plant thrives in cool, high-altitude environments. Its vibrant colors and intricate floral structure make it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. The variety meyeniana is particularly noted for its adaptability and ornamental value. This plant not only adds beauty to gardens but also plays an important role in local ecosystems.
