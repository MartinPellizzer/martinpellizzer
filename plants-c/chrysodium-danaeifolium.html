Chrysodium danaeifolium

Chrysodium danaeifolium, commonly known as the golden danae, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Chrysodium danaeifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
