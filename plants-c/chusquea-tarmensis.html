Chusquea tarmensis

Chusquea tarmensis, commonly known as the Tarmes bamboo, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Peru and Bolivia. This plant is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and slender, hollow stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.
