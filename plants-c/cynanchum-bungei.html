Cynanchum bungei

Cynanchum bungei, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed or Bunge's milkvetch, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and it is known for its milky sap, which contains toxic compounds.

Cynanchum bungei is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
