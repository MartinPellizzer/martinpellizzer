Chromolaena sagittifera

Chromolaena sagittifera, commonly known as the arrowleaf bush.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas, characterized by its narrow, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings.

Chromolaena sagittifera is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soils.
