Corysanthes puberula

Corysanthes puberula, commonly known as the hairy corysanthes, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural displays.
