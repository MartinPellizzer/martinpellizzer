Calathea amplissima

Calathea amplissima, commonly known as the giant calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in diameter. The leaves are adorned with intricate patterns of deep green, silver, and purple, creating a visually stunning effect. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
