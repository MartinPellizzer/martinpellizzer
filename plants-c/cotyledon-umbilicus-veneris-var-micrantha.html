Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. micrantha

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. micrantha, commonly known as the "Crown of Thorns," is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces striking red, pink, or orange flowers during the warmer months, which are often followed by small, round fruits. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, providing structural support and aiding in water storage.

This variety is well-suited to arid environments and requires minimal watering once established.
