Caladium amoenum

Caladium amoenum, commonly known as the Amoenum arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that exhibit a range of vibrant colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium amoenum thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen.
