Centaurea linaresii

Centaurea linaresii, commonly known as the Linares' cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright blue to purple ray florets and yellow disc florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, open habitats. It is a perennial species with upright stems and narrow, linear leaves.
