Chamaecrista Huntii Var. Correntina

Chamaecrista huntii var. correntina is a rare and striking plant native to the southeastern United States. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers and feathery foliage that make it a standout in any garden. This variety belongs to the legume family and has adapted to thrive in sandy, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits have made it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure and underappreciated in the broader horticultural community.
