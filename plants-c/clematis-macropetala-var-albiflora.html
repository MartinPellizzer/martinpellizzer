Clematis macropetala var. albiflora

Clematis macropetala var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring, often preceding the foliage. The plant has slender, twining stems and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
