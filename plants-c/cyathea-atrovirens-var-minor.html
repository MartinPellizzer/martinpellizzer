Cyathea atrovirens var. minor

Cyathea atrovirens var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and a crown of feathery, dark green fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its tall, slender trunk and contributes to its structural stability.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant, graceful form.
