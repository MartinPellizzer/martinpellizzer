Cotoneaster bacillaris var. parvifolius

Cotoneaster bacillaris var. parvifolius, commonly known as the small-leaved cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of white to pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is relatively disease-resistant and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
