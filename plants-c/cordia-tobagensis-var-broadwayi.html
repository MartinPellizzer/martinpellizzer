Cordia tobagensis var. broadwayi

Cordia tobagensis var. broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway cordia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
