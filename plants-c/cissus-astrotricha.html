Cissus astrotricha

Cissus astrotricha, commonly known as the star-leaf cissus, is a climbing plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves, which are usually dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its stems are succulent and can trail or climb, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises.

Cissus astrotricha prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
