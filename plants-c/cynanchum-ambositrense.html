Cynanchum ambositrense

Cynanchum ambositrense, commonly known as the Madagascar moonseed, is a climbing plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, ribbed, spherical fruits. It is adapted to grow in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in secondary forests.

Due to its unique morphology and restricted distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
