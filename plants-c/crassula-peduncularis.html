Crassula peduncularis

Crassula peduncularis, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a rosette shape, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Crassula peduncularis is valued for its low maintenance requirements and aesthetic appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
