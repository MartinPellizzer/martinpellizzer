Croton perdicipes

Croton perdicipes, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often features vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically large, ovate, and have a velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.
