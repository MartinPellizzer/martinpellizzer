Coreopsis schimperi

Coreopsis schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features bright yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Coreopsis schimperi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
