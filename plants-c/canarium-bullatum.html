Canarium bullatum

Canarium bullatum, commonly known as the white nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
