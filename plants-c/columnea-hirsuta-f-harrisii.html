Columnea hirsuta f. harrisii

Columnea hirsuta f. harrisii, commonly known as the hairy columnea, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features velvety, hairy leaves that give it its common name and help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of forests.

This cultivar is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
