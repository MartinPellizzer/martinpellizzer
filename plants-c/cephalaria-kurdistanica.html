Cephalaria kurdistanica

Cephalaria kurdistanica, commonly known as the Kurdish cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Kurdistan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a clustered inflorescence.

It is valued for its potential in traditional medicine and as a source of ornamental beauty.
