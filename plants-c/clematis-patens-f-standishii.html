Clematis patens f. standishii

Clematis patens f. standishii, commonly known as the Standishii Clematis, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically in shades of white and pink. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that provide a delicate texture to the foliage. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its elegant appearance.
