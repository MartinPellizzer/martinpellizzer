Croton hondensis

Croton hondensis, commonly known as Honduran croton, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, spiky fruit capsules. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its unique foliage.
