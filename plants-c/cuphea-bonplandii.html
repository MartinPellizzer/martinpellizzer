Cuphea bonplandii

Cuphea bonplandii, commonly known as the velvet tamales plant, is a flowering shrub native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Cuphea bonplandii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
