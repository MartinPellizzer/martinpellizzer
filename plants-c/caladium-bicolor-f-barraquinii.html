Caladium bicolor f. barraquinii

Caladium bicolor f. barraquinii, commonly known as the Barraquinii caladium, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

This variety is often cultivated for its decorative value in gardens and as a houseplant.
