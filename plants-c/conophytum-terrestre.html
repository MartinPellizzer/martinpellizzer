Conophytum terrestre

Conophytum terrestre, commonly known as the "Little Desert Rose," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
