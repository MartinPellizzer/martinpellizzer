Centaurea ucriae

Centaurea ucriae, commonly known as the Ucria's centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. It features slender, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.
