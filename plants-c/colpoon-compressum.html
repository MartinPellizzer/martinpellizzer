Colpoon compressum

Colpoon compressum, commonly known as the flatleaf colpoon, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically compressed and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Colpoon compressum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
