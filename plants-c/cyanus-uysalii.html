Cyanus uysalii

Cyanus uysalii, commonly known as the Turkish blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Cyanus uysalii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
