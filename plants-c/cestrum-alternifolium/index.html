Cestrum Alternifolium

Cestrum alternifolium is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped blooms. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrant flowers that bloom at night. Its leaves are alternate and lance-shaped, adding to its visual appeal. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic alkaloids, requiring caution when handling.
