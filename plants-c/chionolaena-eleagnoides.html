Chionolaena eleagnoides

Chionolaena eleagnoides, commonly known as silverleaf groundcherry, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence, which helps reduce water loss. Its small, yellow flowers are typically clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its propagation in suitable habitats.
