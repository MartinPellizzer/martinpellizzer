Cyclophyllum multiflorum

Cyclophyllum multiflorum, commonly known as the many-flowered cyclophyll, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cucurbitaceae.

It is characterized by its distinct, spiral-shaped fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, warm climates. The fruit, which matures to a bright orange color, contains numerous seeds enclosed in a fibrous pulp.
