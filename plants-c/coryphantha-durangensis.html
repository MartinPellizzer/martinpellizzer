Coryphantha durangensis

Coryphantha durangensis, commonly known as the Durango coryphantha, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically green and covered with spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
