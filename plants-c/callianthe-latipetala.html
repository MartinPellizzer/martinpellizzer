Callianthe latipetala

Callianthe latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petaled orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, broad, and colorful petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and can bloom in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.
