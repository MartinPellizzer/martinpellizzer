Ceropegia woodii

Ceropegia woodii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which are pollinated by specific insects. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ease of care have made it a favored ornamental plant among horticulturists.
