Cleistanthus tonkinensis

Cleistanthus tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin cleistanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and yellowish-green, often clustered in axillary or terminal inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release the seeds, which are small and often covered in a sticky substance.
