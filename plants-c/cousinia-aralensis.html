Cousinia aralensis

Cousinia aralensis, commonly known as the Aral Sea cudweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils.

Cousinia aralensis is adapted to withstand drought and extreme temperature fluctuations.
