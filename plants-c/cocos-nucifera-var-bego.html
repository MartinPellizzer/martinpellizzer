Cocos nucifera var. bego

Cocos nucifera var. bego, commonly known as the Bego coconut, is a variety of the coconut palm native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, elongated leaves. The fruit produces a sweet, aromatic coconut water and a white, fleshy mesocarp. This variety is valued for its high yield and quality of coconut products.

It thrives in tropical climates with ample sunlight and well-drained soil.
