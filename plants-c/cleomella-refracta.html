Cleomella refracta

Cleomella refracta, commonly known as the slender cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters at the leaf axils, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Cleomella refracta is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
