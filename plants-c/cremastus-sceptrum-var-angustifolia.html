Cremastus sceptrum var. angustifolia

Cremastus sceptrum var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goldenthread, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a perennial herb with delicate, fern-like leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, giving it its specific variety name, angustifolia.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
