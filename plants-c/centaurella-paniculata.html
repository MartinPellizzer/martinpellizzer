Centaurella paniculata

Centaurella paniculata, commonly known as the panicled centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers. It blooms from late summer to early autumn, and its seed heads are distinctive, resembling a panicle structure.

Centaurella paniculata is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
