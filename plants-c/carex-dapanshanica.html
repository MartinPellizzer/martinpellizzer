Carex dapanshanica

Carex dapanshanica, commonly known as the Dapan Mountain sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Dapan Mountains region. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream sides. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers.

Its foliage is grass-like, with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
