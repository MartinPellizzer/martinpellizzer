Cirsium lucens var. lucens

Cirsium lucens var. lucens, commonly known as the shining thistle, is a flowering plant native to the northern regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads made up of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are usually purple or pink in color.

It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
