Camellia synaptica var. parviovata

Camellia synaptica var. parviovata, commonly known as the small-flowered synaptica camellia, is a rare and ornamental species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.

Due to its limited distribution and ornamental value, it is often sought after by plant enthusiasts and botanists.
