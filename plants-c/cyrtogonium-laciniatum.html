Cyrtogonium laciniatum

Cyrtogonium laciniatum, commonly known as the slitleaf water fern, is a delicate aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in freshwater environments, often found in slow-moving streams and ponds. The plant has finely divided, lacy fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. Its rhizomes are submerged, anchoring it to the substrate while absorbing nutrients.

Cyrtogonium laciniatum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms and contributing to water filtration.
