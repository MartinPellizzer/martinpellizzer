Colocasia argyroneura

Colocasia argyroneura, commonly known as the silver-veined elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with silvery veins. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance.

Colocasia argyroneura is also known for its ability to grow rapidly, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
