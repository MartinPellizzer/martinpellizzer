Chaetaria Mauritiana

Chaetaria mauritiana is a rare and unique plant species found primarily in the remote regions of Mauritius. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, known for its diverse and often ornamental flowering plants. This plant is characterized by its striking, hairy leaves and delicate yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Due to its limited habitat and threats from environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical treasure.
