Clethra Alnifolia Var. Scabra

Scabrous clethra, scientifically known as Clethra alnifolia var. scabra, is a unique and lesser-known variant of the clethra genus. Native to the eastern United States, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its distinctive rough, scaly bark and fragrant white flowers make it a striking addition to native plant gardens. Despite its ornamental value, scabrous clethra remains underappreciated and often overlooked in horticultural literature. This article explores its ecological significance, cultivation tips, and the reasons why it deserves more attention in the botanical world.
