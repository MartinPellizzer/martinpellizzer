Colocasia antiquorum

Colocasia antiquorum, commonly known as the giant elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to three feet in diameter. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental and edible plants. Its foliage is often used in landscaping for its dramatic visual impact.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.
