Cremosperma pusillum

Cremosperma pusillum, commonly known as the small-leafed cryptantha, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in dry, disturbed soils in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Cremosperma pusillum is adapted to survive in harsh environments, showcasing typical features of desert flora.
