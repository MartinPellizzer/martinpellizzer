Coleus aliciae

Coleus aliciae, commonly known as the Alice's coleus, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and display a wide range of hues, including red, purple, and green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor settings.
