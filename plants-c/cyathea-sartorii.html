Cyathea sartorii

Cyathea sartorii, commonly known as the Sartorii tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, and it produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves.

Cyathea sartorii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
