Crotalaria passerinoides

Crotalaria passerinoides, commonly known as the slender crownflower, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its delicate, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Crotalaria passerinoides is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
