Celmisia × mollis

Celmisia × mollis, commonly known as the silver tuft, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant features dense, silver-gray foliage with a woolly texture, and it produces clusters of small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Celmisia × mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
