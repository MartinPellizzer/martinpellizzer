Celosia altissima

Celosia altissima, commonly known as the firecracker plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, plume-like inflorescences that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Celosia altissima is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
