Cinnagrostis polygama var. polygama

Cinnagrostis polygama var. polygama, commonly known as yellow wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features trifoliate leaves with three heart-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to the landscape.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
