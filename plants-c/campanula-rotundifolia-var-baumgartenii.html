Campanula rotundifolia var. baumgartenii

Campanula rotundifolia var. baumgartenii, commonly known as the Baumann's bellflower, is a variant of the round-leaf bellflower.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. The plant features showy, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are rounded and often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.
