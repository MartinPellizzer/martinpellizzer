Cyrtandra splendens

Cyrtandra splendens, commonly known as the splendour heliotrope, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Its leaves are typically opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

Cyrtandra splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
