Cyclamen repandum subsp. balearicum

Cyclamen repandum subsp.

balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balearic Islands. It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive waxy coating and produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or woodland habitats. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens. Cyclamen repandum subsp.

balearicum is also known for its ability to go dormant during the summer months.
