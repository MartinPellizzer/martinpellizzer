Colchicum Bertolonii Var. Cupanii

Colchicum bertolonii var. cupanii is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its elegant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. This variant of Colchicum bertolonii is distinguished by its unique coloration and leaf structure. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine habitats. Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
