Caladenia longicauda subsp. eminens

Caladenia longicauda subsp.

eminens, commonly known as the tall spider-orchid, is a striking orchid species native to southeastern Australia. It features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance.

The flower is typically pale green with a deep red lip, and it blooms during the autumn season. This subshrub is characterized by its fibrous root system and narrow, linear leaves.

It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open woodland habitats.
