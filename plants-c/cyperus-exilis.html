Cyperus exilis

Cyperus exilis, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant has slender, erect stems with small, scale-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often submerged or floating in water.

Cyperus exilis plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
