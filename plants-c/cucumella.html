Cucumella

Cucumella, commonly known as the cucumber leaf or Cucumella indica, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves that are typically light green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by cucumbers, which are similar in appearance to common cucumbers but often have a slightly different flavor. Cucumella is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is valued for its edible fruits and is often cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
