Cymbidium × gongshanense

Cymbidium × gongshanense, commonly known as the Gongshan orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in winter or early spring, producing blooms in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It prefers cool, bright environments with good air circulation and moderate watering.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
