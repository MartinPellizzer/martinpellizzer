Clitoria kaessneri

Clitoria kaessneri, commonly known as the blue pea or blue orchid vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, butterfly-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and is often grown for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Clitoria kaessneri is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
