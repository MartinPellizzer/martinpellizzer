Coussapoa floccosa

Coussapoa floccosa, commonly known as the silky tree or white silky tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, often covered in a fine, silky down.

Coussapoa floccosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique visual characteristics.
