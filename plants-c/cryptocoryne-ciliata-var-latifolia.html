Cryptocoryne ciliata var. latifolia

Cryptocoryne ciliata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf cryptocoryne, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and a distinctive serrated edge. This variety is well-suited for planted tanks due to its ability to thrive in a range of water conditions. It grows in dense clusters and is often used as a background plant in aquariums.

The plant is also valued for its moderate to high light requirements and its ability to help maintain water clarity.
