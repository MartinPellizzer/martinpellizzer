Clematis fortunei

Clematis fortunei, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to climb using its tendrils. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis fortunei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
