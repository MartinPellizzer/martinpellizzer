Cattleya × elegans var. dayi

Cattleya × elegans var. dayi, commonly known as the Day's orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features tall, erect stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually a mix of white, yellow, and green hues, often with a distinctive green throat.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
