Cattleya bullieri

Cattleya bullieri, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple with white markings. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences that bear multiple blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Cattleya bullieri is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
