Campanula medium f. calycantha

Campanula medium f. calycantha, commonly known as the calycanthus campanula, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
