Campderia nematostachya

Campderia nematostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed campderia, is a herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Campderia nematostachya is typically found in marshy or swampy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
