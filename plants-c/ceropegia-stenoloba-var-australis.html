Ceropegia stenoloba var. australis

Ceropegia stenoloba var. australis, commonly known as the Australian milkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, tuberous roots that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy coating.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
