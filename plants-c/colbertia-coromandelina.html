Colbertia coromandelina

Colbertia coromandelina, commonly known as the Coromandel lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a prominent green throat. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and lanceolate, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Colbertia coromandelina is often cultivated for its elegant blooms and is a popular choice in garden landscapes.
