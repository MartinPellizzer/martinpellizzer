Clitoria mariana var. orientalis

Clitoria mariana var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental winged bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, butterfly-like blue or violet flowers. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain seeds used in traditional cuisines. It is often grown for its ornamental value as well as for its nutritional and agricultural benefits.

The leaves are trifoliate, and the plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
