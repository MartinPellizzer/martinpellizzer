Crepidiastrum

Crepidiastrum, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, green berries that mature to a dark purple color. Silver vine is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
