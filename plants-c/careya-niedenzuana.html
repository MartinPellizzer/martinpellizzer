Careya niedenzuana

Careya niedenzuana, commonly known as the white sand tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Careya niedenzuana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
