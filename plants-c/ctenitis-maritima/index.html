Ctenitis Maritima

Maritime ctenitis, scientifically known as Ctenitis maritima, is a unique and resilient sea anemone found in coastal waters around the world. This fascinating creature thrives in shallow, turbulent environments where it attaches itself to rocks or other substrates. Its translucent body is adorned with long, flowing tentacles that it uses to capture prey in the nutrient-rich marine environment. Despite its delicate appearance, the maritime ctenitis is remarkably adaptable, surviving in harsh conditions with minimal resources. It plays an important ecological role in marine ecosystems, contributing to the balance of coastal food webs.
