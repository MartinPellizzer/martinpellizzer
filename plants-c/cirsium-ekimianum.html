Cirsium ekimianum

Cirsium ekimianum, commonly known as the Ekinian thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
