Cotoneaster matrensis f. pluriflorus

Cotoneaster matrensis f. pluriflorus, commonly known as the multi-flowered matrensis cotoneaster, is a compact shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It features dense, broad ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers in late spring, which are followed by bright red, persistent berries. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a range of soil types.

This cultivar is often used in gardens for its winter interest and structural form.
