Cyperus incomtus var. venturii

Cyperus incomtus var. venturii, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and along riverbanks.
