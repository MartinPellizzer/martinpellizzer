Codiaeum aucubifolium

Codiaeum aucubifolium, commonly known as the velvetleaf or croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that vary in color from deep green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant displays.
