Coelogyne bella

Coelogyne bella, commonly known as the beauty orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of pink and white petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

Coelogyne bella is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its beauty and fragrance in horticultural gardens.
