Centaurea aplolepa subsp. lunensis

Centaurea aplolepa subsp.

lunensis, commonly known as the Lunar Centaury, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, bright yellow to orange flowers.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and limestone soils.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different floral morphology compared to other Centaurea species.
