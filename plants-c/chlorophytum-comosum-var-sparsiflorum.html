Chlorophytum comosum var. sparsiflorum

Chlorophytum comosum var. sparsiflorum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by delicate, grass-like seed heads. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and indirect sunlight.

This variety is particularly noted for its ability to purify indoor air and its ease of propagation through its offsets.
