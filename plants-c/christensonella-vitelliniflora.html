Christensonella vitelliniflora

Christensonella vitelliniflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered rock cress, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or arctic regions. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Christensonella vitelliniflora is adapted to cold climates and is often found in high-altitude environments.
