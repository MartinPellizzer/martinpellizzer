Crassula sericea var. sericea

Crassula sericea var. sericea, commonly known as the silver crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container gardening.
