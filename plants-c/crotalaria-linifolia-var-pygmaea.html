Crotalaria linifolia var. pygmaea

Crotalaria linifolia var. pygmaea, commonly known as the dwarf silverleaf cowpea, is a compact, annual legume native to tropical regions.

It features small, ovate leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by pod-like fruits containing elliptical seeds. It is often grown as a cover crop or for its ornamental value due to its delicate foliage.

Crotalaria linifolia var. pygmaea is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting subsequent crops.
