Croton adenophorus var. genuinus

Croton adenophorus var. genuinus, commonly known as the red-stemmed croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and leaves that vary in color from green to red or purple. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
