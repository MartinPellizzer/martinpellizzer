Calophyllum lanigerum var. lanigerum

Calophyllum lanigerum var. lanigerum, commonly known as the white mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit, which resembles a small mango, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

The plant is valued not only for its ornamental qualities but also for its medicinal and culinary uses.
