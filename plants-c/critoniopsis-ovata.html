Critoniopsis ovata

Critoniopsis ovata, commonly known as the ovate leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The plant's unique morphology makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
