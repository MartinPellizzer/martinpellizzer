Critoniopsis pycnantha

Critoniopsis pycnantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
