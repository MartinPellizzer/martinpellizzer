Coelogyne rotundilabia

Coelogyne rotundilabia, commonly known as the round-labellum orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces strikingly colorful, fragrant flowers. The plant features a unique labellum that is rounded and often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne rotundilabia is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in both home gardens and botanical collections.
