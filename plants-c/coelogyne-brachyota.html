Coelogyne brachyota

Coelogyne brachyota, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to orange-red blooms, which appear during the dry season. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, and its leaves are broad and leathery.

Coelogyne brachyota is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
