Cyathea akawaiorum

Cyathea akawaiorum, commonly known as the Hawaiian tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in coastal and lowland forests. Its rhizome system allows it to anchor firmly in soil while absorbing nutrients from the surrounding environment.

Cyathea akawaiorum plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various native species.
