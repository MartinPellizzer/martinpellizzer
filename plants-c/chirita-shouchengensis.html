Chirita shouchengensis

Chirita shouchengensis, commonly known as the Sichuan chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, while the flowers range in color from white to pale yellow.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
