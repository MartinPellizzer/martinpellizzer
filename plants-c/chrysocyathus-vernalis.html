Chrysocyathus vernalis

Chrysocyathus vernalis, commonly known as the spring flame cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a compact, globular stem that is covered in short, spiny bristles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring season, which are clustered at the top of the stem. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss in its harsh environment.

Chrysocyathus vernalis is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient and interesting addition to xeriscapes.
