Chamaedorea aguilariana

Chamaedorea aguilariana, commonly known as the Aguilari's bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and feather-like leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plants.
