Clusia pusilla subsp. jauaensis

Clusia pusilla subsp.

jauaensis, commonly known as the Jauaensis Clusia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean region. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often waxy and have a distinctive texture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests. Clusia pusilla subsp.

jauaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate various soil conditions.
