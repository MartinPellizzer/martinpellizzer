Ceropegia ciliatula

Ceropegia ciliatula, commonly known as the Cape ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its trailing stems and small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or white, often with a faintly colored throat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
