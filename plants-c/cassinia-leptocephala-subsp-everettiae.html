Cassinia leptocephala subsp. everettiae

Cassinia leptocephala subsp.

everettiae, commonly known as the slender-headed cassinia, is a shrub native to Australia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the flowers are often covered in fine, hairy structures.

It plays a role in supporting local wildlife, particularly insects and small birds.
