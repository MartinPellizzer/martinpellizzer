Citharexylum grenzebachianum

Citharexylum grenzebachianum, commonly known as the white-flowered citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance. Its most notable characteristic is its clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the year.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and horticulture.
