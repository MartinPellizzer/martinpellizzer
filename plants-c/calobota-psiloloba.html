Calobota psiloloba

Calobota psiloloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf calobota, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
