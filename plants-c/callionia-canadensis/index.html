Callionia Canadensis

Callionia canadensis is a rare and fascinating plant native to the northeastern United States. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often edible members. This delicate flowering plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its unique appearance includes small, white to pale yellow flowers and finely divided leaves. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
