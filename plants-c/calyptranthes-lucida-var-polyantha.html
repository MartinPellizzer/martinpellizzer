Calyptranthes lucida var. polyantha

Calyptranthes lucida var. polyantha, commonly known as the silverleaf calyptranthes, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Calyptranthes lucida var. polyantha is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
