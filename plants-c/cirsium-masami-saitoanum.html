Cirsium masami-saitoanum

Cirsium masami-saitoanum, commonly known as Saito's thistle, is a flowering plant species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a short stature, usually reaching heights of less than 1 meter.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
