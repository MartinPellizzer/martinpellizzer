Cordia crenata subsp. shinyangensis

Cordia crenata subsp.

shinyangensis, commonly known as the Guangxi cordia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern China. It is characterized by its simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive orange to reddish-orange fruits that split open to release seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
