Cytisus schimperianus

Cytisus schimperianus, commonly known as the Schimper's broom, is a flowering shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are small, oval, and often hairy, while its bark is smooth and grayish in color.

Cytisus schimperianus is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
