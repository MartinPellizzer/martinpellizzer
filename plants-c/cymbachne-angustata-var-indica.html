Cymbachne angustata var. indica

Cymbachne angustata var. indica, commonly known as the narrowleaf cymbachne, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grassy areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of certain ecosystems and a subject of study in plant taxonomy.
