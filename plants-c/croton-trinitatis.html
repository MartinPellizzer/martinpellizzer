Croton trinitatis

Croton trinitatis, commonly known as the Trinidad croton, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is characterized by its milky sap and often striking foliage. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed segments, giving it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens.
