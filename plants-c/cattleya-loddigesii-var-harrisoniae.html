Cattleya loddigesii var. harrisoniae

Cattleya loddigesii var. harrisoniae, commonly known as the Harrison's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant and showy flowers.

It features large, pendulous blooms with a rich color palette that often includes shades of pink, white, and orange. The plant is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. It has thick, fleshy roots that help it store water in its natural habitat.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
