Catasetum maculatum var. flavescens

Catasetum maculatum var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-streaked orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green foliage, with flowers that often display a combination of yellow, green, and red hues. The plant produces distinctive, pendulous flowers with a specialized structure that facilitates pollination by specific insects. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
