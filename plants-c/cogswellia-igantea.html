Cogswellia igantea

Cogswellia igantea, commonly known as the giant cactus, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features spines and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss in its harsh environment.

Cogswellia igantea is adapted to survive in extreme desert conditions with minimal water availability.
