Calliandra schultzei

Calliandra schultzei, commonly known as the red calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that range from pink to deep red. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological diversity.

Additionally, the plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
