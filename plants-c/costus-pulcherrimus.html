Costus pulcherrimus

Costus pulcherrimus, commonly known as the beautiful costus, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features tall, narrow leaves and produces striking, showy inflorescences with bright red or orange flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
