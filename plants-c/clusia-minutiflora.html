Clusia minutiflora

Clusia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It is well adapted to humid environments and can thrive in partial to full shade.

Clusia minutiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ability to tolerate a variety of soil conditions.
