Ceropegia lutea subsp. vaga

Ceropegia lutea subsp.

vaga, commonly known as the wandering ghost or ghost plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Apocynaceae family. It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive, fleshy, tuberous stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.
