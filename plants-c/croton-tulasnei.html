Croton tulasnei

Croton tulasnei, commonly known as the Tulasne's croton, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. Its showy, yellow-orange inflorescences consist of small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Croton tulasnei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.
