Chabraea suaveolens

Chabraea suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented chabraea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and emit a mild fragrance.

Chabraea suaveolens is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its traditional use in herbal medicine.
