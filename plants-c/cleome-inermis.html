Cleome inermis

Cleome inermis, commonly known as spider flower, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the southern United States.

It features branching stems that are often hairy, with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually pubescent. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming from summer to fall. Cleome inermis is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.

It is a popular choice for gardeners due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
