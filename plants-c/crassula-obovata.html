Crassula obovata

Crassula obovata, commonly known as the Fat Plant or Jade Plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Crassula obovata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
