Cavendishia rosea

Cavendishia rosea, commonly known as the pink torch cactus, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that display a pinkish hue. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
