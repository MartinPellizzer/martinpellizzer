Croton venturii var. cheirotenos

Croton venturii var. cheirotenos, commonly known as the pink croton, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that display shades of red, orange, and pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The flowers are small and clustered, often greenish-yellow, and are followed by woody capsules containing seeds.
