Carex stokesii var. aristata

Carex stokesii var. aristata, commonly known as Stokes' sedge with a spike.

This variant of Stokes' sedge is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States. It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces cylindrical spike-like inflorescences.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in wetland ecosystems and a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
