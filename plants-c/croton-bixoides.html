Croton bixoides

Croton bixoides, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves that vary in shape and hue. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
