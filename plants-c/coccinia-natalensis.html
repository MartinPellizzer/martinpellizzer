Coccinia natalensis

Coccinia natalensis, commonly known as Natal cucumber, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, pumpkins, and gourds. The plant produces small, green, edible fruits that are similar in appearance to cucumbers. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and trailing.

Coccinia natalensis is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental and culinary uses.
