Cavendishia tolimensis

Cavendishia tolimensis, commonly known as the Tolima Cavendish, is a species of flowering plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
