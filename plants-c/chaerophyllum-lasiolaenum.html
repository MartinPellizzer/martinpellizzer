Chaerophyllum lasiolaenum

Chaerophyllum lasiolaenum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved parsley.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, clustered white flowers.
