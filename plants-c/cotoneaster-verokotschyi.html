Cotoneaster verokotschyi

Cotoneaster verokotschyi, commonly known as the Verokotschyi cotoneaster, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are usually white or pink, appearing in clusters.

Cotoneaster verokotschyi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal interest.
