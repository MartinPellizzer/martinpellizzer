Cephalaria rigida var. balearica

Cephalaria rigida var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic rigid cephalaria, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and clustered, yellowish-green flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while the flowers develop into small, dry fruits.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
