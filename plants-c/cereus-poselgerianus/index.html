Cereus Poselgerianus

Cereus poselgerianus is a striking member of the cactus family native to southern Africa. It is known for its tall, spiny stems and impressive night-blooming flowers. This plant thrives in arid conditions and requires minimal water to survive. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its hardy nature, it demands careful handling due to its sharp spines.
