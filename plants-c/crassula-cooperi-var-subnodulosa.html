Crassula cooperi var. subnodulosa

Crassula cooperi var. subnodulosa, commonly known as the Jade Necklace or Shiny Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and ability to form dense clusters, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright light, though it can tolerate partial shade.

Its slow growth rate and ease of propagation make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
