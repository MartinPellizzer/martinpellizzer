Calophyllum celebicum

Calophyllum celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes calophyllum, is a tropical tree native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the family Calophyllaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The tree produces yellowish-white flowers that are often clustered in panicles, followed by woody, oval fruits containing a single seed.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and potential ecological role in its native habitat.
