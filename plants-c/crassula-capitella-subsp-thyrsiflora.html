Crassula capitella subsp. thyrsiflora

Crassula capitella subsp.

thyrsiflora, commonly known as the "Christmas Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions.

The plant produces dense clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the winter months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowering display and drought tolerance.
