Chamaecrista katangensis var. katangensis

Chamaecrista katangensis var. katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga burr legume, is a shrub native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
