Chrysanthemum vestitum

Chrysanthemum vestitum, commonly known as the velvet chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. Its leaves are broad and deeply lobed, providing a striking contrast to the bright blooms.

Chrysanthemum vestitum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
