Cleistocactus fossulatus

Cleistocactus fossulatus, commonly known as the fissured cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, columnar cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure and a gray-green color. The plant features prominent, shallow grooves along its stem, which give it its common name. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Cleistocactus fossulatus is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
