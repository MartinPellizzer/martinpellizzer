Calpicarpum ornatum

Calpicarpum ornatum, commonly known as the ornate calpicon, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant in some regions.
