Cryptocoryne dewitii

Cryptocoryne dewitii, commonly known as the dwarf cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. This plant thrives in low-light conditions and is often found in slow-moving or still freshwater habitats. Cryptocoryne dewitii is popular among aquarists for its ease of care and ability to help maintain water clarity.

Its rhizome system allows it to spread slowly and form dense underwater carpets.
