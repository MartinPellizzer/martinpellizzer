Cleome friesii

Cleome friesii, commonly known as the yellow cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Cleome friesii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ease of cultivation contribute to its widespread use in both wild and cultivated settings.
