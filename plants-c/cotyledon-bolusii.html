Cotyledon bolusii

Cotyledon bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, crinkled leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues along the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Cotyledon bolusii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique leaf patterns.
