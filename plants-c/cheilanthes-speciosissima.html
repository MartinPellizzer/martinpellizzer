Cheilanthes speciosissima

Cheilanthes speciosissima, commonly known as the showy rock fern, is a species of fern native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, bright green fronds that often display a reddish hue on the underside. The plant typically grows on rocky slopes and cliffs, where it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the undersides of the fronds, a typical feature of ferns.

Cheilanthes speciosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and dry landscapes.
