Ceropegia butaguensis

Ceropegia butaguensis, commonly known as the Cape Town moonvine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and succulent stems. Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
