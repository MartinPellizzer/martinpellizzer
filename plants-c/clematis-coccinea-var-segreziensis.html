Clematis coccinea var. segreziensis

Clematis coccinea var. segreziensis, commonly known as the red clematis, is a striking flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

However, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
