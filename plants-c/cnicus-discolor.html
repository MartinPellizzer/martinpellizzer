Cnicus discolor

Cnicus discolor, commonly known as the white bur marigold, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers with white markings and rough, hairy stems. The plant produces small, spiky fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
