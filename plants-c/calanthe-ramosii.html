Calanthe ramosii

Calanthe ramosii, commonly known as the Yunnan orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Calanthe ramosii produces inflorescences with multiple flowers, each featuring three sepals and three petals, with the latter often being more prominent.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
