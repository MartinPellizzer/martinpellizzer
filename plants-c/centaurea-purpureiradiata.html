Centaurea purpureiradiata

Centaurea purpureiradiata, commonly known as the purple radiata centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright purple to pink flowers, which are arranged in dense, spherical clusters. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has slender, branched stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
