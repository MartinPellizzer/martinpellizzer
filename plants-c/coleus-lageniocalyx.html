Coleus lageniocalyx

Coleus lageniocalyx, commonly known as the large calyx coleus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from red to purple and green, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking visual appeal.

The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
