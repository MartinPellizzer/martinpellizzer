Canna paniculata var. glabra

Canna paniculata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms in garden settings.
