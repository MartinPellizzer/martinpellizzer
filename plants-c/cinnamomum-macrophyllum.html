Cinnamomum macrophyllum

Cinnamomum macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf cinnamon or *Cinnamomum macrophyllum*, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that give way to dark purple berries containing winged seeds. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and as a source of aromatic compounds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in local ecosystems.
