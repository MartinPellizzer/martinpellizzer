Cleome yunnanensis

Cleome yunnanensis, commonly known as the Chinese spider flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spindly stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Cleome yunnanensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
