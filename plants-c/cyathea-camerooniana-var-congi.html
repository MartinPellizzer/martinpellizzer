Cyathea camerooniana var. congi

Cyathea camerooniana var. congi, commonly known as the Cameroon tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to central Africa.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth form.

The fronds are evergreen, contributing to the plant's ornamental value and ecological role in its native habitat.
