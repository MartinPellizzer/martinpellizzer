Coelogyne carrii

Coelogyne carrii, commonly known as the Carri's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms once a year, with flowers appearing in late winter to early spring. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and rain, making it well-suited for humid environments.

Coelogyne carrii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
