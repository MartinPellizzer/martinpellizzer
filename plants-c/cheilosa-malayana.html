Cheilosa malayana

Cheilosa malayana, commonly known as the Malay orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. Cheilosa malayana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
