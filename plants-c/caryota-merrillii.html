Caryota merrillii

Caryota merrillii, commonly known as the Chinese fan palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clusters of black, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Caryota merrillii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
