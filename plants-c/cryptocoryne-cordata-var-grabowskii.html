Cryptocoryne cordata var. grabowskii

Cryptocoryne cordata var. grabowskii, commonly known as the Grabowski's cryptocoryne, is a rare aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle yellowish tint. The plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water conditions and prefers moderate to low light. It is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams.

Its unique variability makes it a sought-after species among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
