Colchicum drenowskii

Colchicum drenowskii, commonly known as the Drenowsky colchic, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and often have a bluish-green hue.

The flowers are usually pale purple to white, with reflexed petals and a distinctive, spurred structure at the base.
