Chionanthus urbanii

Chionanthus urbanii, commonly known as the European snowdrop, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features delicate, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a short, upright stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Chionanthus urbanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and early blooming habit.
