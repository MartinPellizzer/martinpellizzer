Cestichis serrulata

Cestichis serrulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cestichis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves with serrated edges, giving it its scientific name. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters.

Cestichis serrulata is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
