Cytisus striatus var. welwitschii

Cytisus striatus var. welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitschii broom, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has slender, often thorned stems and small, leathery leaves that are arranged in pairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and resilient species within the Fabaceae family.
