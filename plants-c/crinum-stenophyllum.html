Crinum stenophyllum

Crinum stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved crinum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its strap-shaped, narrow leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its bulbs are perennial and can produce multiple stems and flowers during the growing season.

Crinum stenophyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and distinctive floral display.
