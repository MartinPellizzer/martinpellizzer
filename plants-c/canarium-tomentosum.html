Canarium tomentosum

Canarium tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly canarium, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, or tomentose, leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces drupes that are typically harvested for their oil-rich seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The tree has economic value due to its seed oil, which is used in various industrial applications.
