Chlorophytum longifolium

Chlorophytum longifolium, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with long, narrow leaves that are typically variegated with white and green streaks. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stalks, which are followed by clusters of bright green, grass-like seeds. It is well adapted to a wide range of growing conditions and is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

Its ability to thrive in low light and its ease of care make it a favored choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
