Cestrum atroxanthum

Cestrum atroxanthum, commonly known as the dark orange bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to deep orange. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Cestrum atroxanthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
