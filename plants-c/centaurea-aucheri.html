Centaurea aucheri

Centaurea aucheri, commonly known as the velvet flower or autumn centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking, spiky purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

Centaurea aucheri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
