Cirsium nagatoense

Cirsium nagatoense, commonly known as the Nagato thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, showy purple flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist climates. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cirsium nagatoense is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
