Convallaria keiskei

Convallaria keiskei, commonly known as the Japanese lily of the valley, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Convallariaceae and is characterized by its clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces red berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
