Cnidoscolus Multilobus

Cnidoscolus multilobus is a striking tropical plant known for its large, lobed leaves and vibrant green color. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage structure. Its leaves can grow up to several feet in length, making it a focal point in gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds, requiring caution when handling or consuming any part of the plant.
