Cattleya × isaacii

Cattleya × isaacii, commonly known as the Isaac's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features tall, erect stems with showy, bilaterally symmetrical blooms in shades of pink, orange, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among horticultural enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
