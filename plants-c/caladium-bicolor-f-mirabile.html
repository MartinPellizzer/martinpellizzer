Caladium bicolor f. mirabile

Caladium bicolor f. mirabile, commonly known as the "Painted Leaf" or "Variegated Caladium," is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with intricate patterns of white, pink, and green, creating a visually stunning display. This cultivar thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

It is often used in gardens and as a houseplant for its decorative value.
