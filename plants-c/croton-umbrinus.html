Croton umbrinus

Croton umbrinus, commonly known as the shadow croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, yellow, and red. The leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
