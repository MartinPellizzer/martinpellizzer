Cytisus kreczetoviczii

Cytisus kreczetoviczii, commonly known as the Kreczetowicz's broom, is a shrub native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
