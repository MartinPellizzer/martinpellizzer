Cattleya forbesii f. maculata

Cattleya forbesii f. maculata, commonly known as the speckled cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, colorful blooms.

It features large, velvety petals and a prominent lip, often with distinctive speckles or patterns. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.

The plant is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively manageable care requirements.
