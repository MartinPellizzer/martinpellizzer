Citrus × aurantium var. fructo-minutissima

Citrus × aurantium var. fructo-minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf lemon.

This variety is a small citrus tree characterized by its compact growth habit and highly fragrant, dark green leaves. It produces small, round, yellow fruits that are typically less than 2 inches in diameter. The fruit has a thin, smooth peel and a high concentration of essential oils, making it valuable in aromatherapy and culinary applications.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
