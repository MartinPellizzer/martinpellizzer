Chrysojasminum parkeri

Chrysojasminum parkeri, commonly known as the golden jasmine.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a radiant appearance. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
