Cyperus hensii

Cyperus hensii, commonly known as the narrowleaf sedges, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Cyperus hensii is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive, upright growth habit.
