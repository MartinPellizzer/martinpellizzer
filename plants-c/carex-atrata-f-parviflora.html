Carex atrata f. parviflora

Carex atrata f. parviflora, commonly known as the slender black sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, dark green leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands.

This variety is distinguished by its smaller, more delicate flowers compared to the typical form of Carex atrata.
