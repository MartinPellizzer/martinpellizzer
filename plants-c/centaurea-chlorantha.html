Centaurea chlorantha

Centaurea chlorantha, commonly known as the green centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Centaurea chlorantha is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
