Commelina zebrina

Commelina zebrina, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking blue-violet flowers with white markings, and its leaves are characterized by their distinctive zebra-like patterns of dark and light green. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream sides. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Commelina zebrina is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
