Cynanchum lightii

Cynanchum lightii, commonly known as the light's milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes milkweeds, and is characterized by its opposite, lanceolate leaves and showy, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that contains toxic compounds, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores.

Its flowering period typically occurs in late summer, and it thrives in well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats.
