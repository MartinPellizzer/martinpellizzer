Camellia hainingii

Camellia hainingii, commonly known as the Haining camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Camellia hainingii is also valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its winter blooming period.
