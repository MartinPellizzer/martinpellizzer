Cucurbita maxima var. ovalis

Cucurbita maxima var. ovalis, commonly known as the acorn squash, is a cultivar of the species Cucurbita maxima.

It is characterized by its elongated, oval-shaped fruits with a dark green, slightly ribbed rind. The flesh is typically orange to yellow in color and is rich in nutrients such as beta-carotene and vitamin C. This variety is native to the Americas and is widely cultivated for its culinary and nutritional value.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
