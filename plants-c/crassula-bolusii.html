Crassula bolusii

Crassula bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the summer months.

Crassula bolusii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
