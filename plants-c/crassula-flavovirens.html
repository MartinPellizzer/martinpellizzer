Crassula flavovirens

Crassula flavovirens, commonly known as the Yellow-green Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a yellowish tinge, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and green, often during the summer months. It is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
