Ceropegia volubicaulis

Ceropegia volubicaulis, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia volubicaulis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
