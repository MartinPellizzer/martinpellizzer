Croton milanjensis

Croton milanjensis, commonly known as the Milanjensis croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often strikingly ornamental species. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red or purple, depending on the variety. It typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
