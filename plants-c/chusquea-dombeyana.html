Chusquea dombeyana

Chusquea dombeyana, commonly known as the Dombey's bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in humid, temperate climates.

Chusquea dombeyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
