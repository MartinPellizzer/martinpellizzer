Chingia fijiensis

Chingia fijiensis, commonly known as the Fiji orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the island of Fiji.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant blossoms. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within the tropical rainforests of Fiji. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil of its habitat.

Due to habitat loss, Chingia fijiensis is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
