Croton decalobus

Croton decalobus, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical plant native to Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, spiny capsules containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.

Croton decalobus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
