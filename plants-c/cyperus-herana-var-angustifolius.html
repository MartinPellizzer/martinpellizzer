Cyperus herana var. angustifolius

Cyperus herana var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrowleaf sedges, is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers. This plant is typically found in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It has a robust root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Due to its adaptability, it is often used in ecological restoration projects and ornamental landscaping.
