Cirsium chienii

Cirsium chienii, commonly known as the Chinese thistle, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its dense, bristly foliage. It produces small, composite flower heads that attract various pollinators.

Cirsium chienii is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
