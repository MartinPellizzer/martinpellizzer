Cryptophoranthus cymbula

Cryptophoranthus cymbula, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in a variety of colors. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
