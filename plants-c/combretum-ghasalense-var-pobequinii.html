Combretum ghasalense var. pobequinii

Combretum ghasalense var. pobequinii, commonly known as the Pobequinii flame bush, is a shrub native to parts of East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange inflorescences that appear during the dry season. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in local horticulture.
