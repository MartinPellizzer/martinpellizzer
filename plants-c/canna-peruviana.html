Canna peruviana

Canna peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian canna, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, ornamental leaves that can reach up to two meters in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Canna peruviana is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
