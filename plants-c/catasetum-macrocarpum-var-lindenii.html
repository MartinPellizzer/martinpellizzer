Catasetum macrocarpum var. lindenii

Catasetum macrocarpum var. lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's large-fruited catasetum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. Its inflorescence consists of a single, elongated spike bearing numerous flowers, with the lower ones often being more developed.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
