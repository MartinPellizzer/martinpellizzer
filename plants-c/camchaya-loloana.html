Camchaya loloana

Camchaya loloana, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the cloud forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped blossom that sets it apart from other orchid varieties. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its flowering period typically occurs during the wet season, adding to its ecological significance in its native habitat.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in both horticultural and conservation contexts.
