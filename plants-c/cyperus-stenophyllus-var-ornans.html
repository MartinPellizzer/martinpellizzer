Cyperus stenophyllus var. ornans

Cyperus stenophyllus var. ornans, commonly known as the ornamental flatsedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and distinctive spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. Its foliage is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its elegant appearance.

Botanically, it is distinguished by its rhizomatous growth and the presence of three-ranked leaves.
