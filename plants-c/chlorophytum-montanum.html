Chlorophytum montanum

Chlorophytum montanum, commonly known as the mountain spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces long, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum montanum is relatively low maintenance and is often used as a decorative houseplant.
