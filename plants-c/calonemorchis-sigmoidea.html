Calonemorchis sigmoidea

Calonemorchis sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a unique epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and they bloom in clusters during the rainy season.

Calonemorchis sigmoidea requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
