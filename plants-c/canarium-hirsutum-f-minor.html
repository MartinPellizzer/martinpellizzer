Canarium hirsutum f. minor

Canarium hirsutum f. minor, commonly known as the small-hair candlenut.

It is a variant of the candlenut tree, characterized by its smaller size and reduced hair coverage compared to the typical Canarium hirsutum. This plant is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers and develops woody fruits containing oily seeds.
