Cormonema spinosum

Cormonema spinosum, commonly known as the spiny corm plant, is a unique member of the Cyclanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its swollen, underground corms, which serve as storage organs for nutrients. The plant produces striking, spiky inflorescences that are often adorned with colorful bracts. It is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after plant among horticulturists.
