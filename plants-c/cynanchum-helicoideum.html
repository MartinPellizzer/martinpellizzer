Cynanchum helicoideum

Cynanchum helicoideum, commonly known as spiral milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its whorled leaves and distinctive spiral arrangement of its stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded areas along stream banks.

Cynanchum helicoideum is a member of the Asclepiadaceae family, known for its milkweed relatives.
