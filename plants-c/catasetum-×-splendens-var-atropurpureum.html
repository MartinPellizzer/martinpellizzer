Catasetum × splendens var. atropurpureum

Catasetum × splendens var. atropurpureum, commonly known as the Purple Passion Catasetum, is a hybrid orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers with a deep, almost black center. The plant produces showy, often fragrant blooms that are typically 3 to 5 inches in diameter. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining, loose soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
