Crotalaria pallida var. pallida

Crotalaria pallida var. pallida, commonly known as the pale crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

The seeds are enclosed in pods and can remain viable in the soil for several years.
