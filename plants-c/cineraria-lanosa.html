Cineraria lanosa

Cineraria lanosa, commonly known as the woolly cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in various parts of the world.
