Cyathea zenkeri

Cyathea zenkeri, commonly known as the Zenker's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizome system is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in moist, shaded environments.

Cyathea zenkeri plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
