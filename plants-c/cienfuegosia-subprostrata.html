Cienfuegosia subprostrata

Cienfuegosia subprostrata, commonly known as the low-growing cienfuegosia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with sprawling stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Cienfuegosia subprostrata is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and humid environments.
