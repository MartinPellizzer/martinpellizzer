Costus igneus

Costus igneus, commonly known as the fireplant, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric, and is characterized by its vibrant, often red or orange-colored inflorescences. Costus igneus thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
