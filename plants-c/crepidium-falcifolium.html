Crepidium falcifolium

Crepidium falcifolium, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that resemble a sword, hence its common name. The plant produces striking, orchid-like flowers with a distinctive greenish-yellow color and a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
