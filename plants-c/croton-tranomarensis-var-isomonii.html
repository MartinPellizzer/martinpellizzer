Croton tranomarensis var. isomonii

Croton tranomarensis var. isomonii, commonly known as the Madagascar croton, is a shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often multicolored leaves that display a range of hues from yellow to red and purple. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
