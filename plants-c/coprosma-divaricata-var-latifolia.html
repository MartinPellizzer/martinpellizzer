Coprosma divaricata var. latifolia

Coprosma divaricata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved coprosma, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, rounded foliage. The leaves are broad, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
