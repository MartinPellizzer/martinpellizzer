Calonemorchis thinicola

Calonemorchis thinicola, commonly known as the slender calonemorchis, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in dense forest undergrowth. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
