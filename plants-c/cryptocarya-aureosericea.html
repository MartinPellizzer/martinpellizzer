Cryptocarya aureosericea

Cryptocarya aureosericea, commonly known as the golden-haired cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its distinctive golden-brown hairs on its young leaves and stems. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The flowers are small and clustered, with white petals, and it produces small, dark berries as fruit.
