Cattleya luetzelburgii

Cattleya luetzelburgii, commonly known as the Luetzelburg's cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This species is prized by orchid growers for its beauty and fragrance.
