Calyptrostylis capitata

Calyptrostylis capitata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a prominent, helmet-like labellum that distinguishes it from other orchid species. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinct central ridge.

Calyptrostylis capitata is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
