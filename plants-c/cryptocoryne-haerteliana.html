Cryptocoryne haerteliana

Cryptocoryne haerteliana, commonly known as the Java crypt, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, ribbon-like leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, making it a striking addition to aquariums. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and prefers soft, slightly acidic water. It is known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters, making it an excellent choice for beginner aquarists.

Cryptocoryne haerteliana is also popular for its ease of propagation through runners and its ability to help maintain a natural, lush aquatic environment.
