Cunila Capitata

Cunila capitata is a small flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its delicate appearance and unique fragrance. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. It is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits. Its compact size makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
