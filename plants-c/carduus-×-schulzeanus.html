Carduus × schulzeanus

Carduus × schulzeanus, commonly known as the Schultze's thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is found in regions of Europe and western Asia. It is a perennial species that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain areas.
