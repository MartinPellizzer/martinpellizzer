Canarium dichotomum var. lucidulum

Canarium dichotomum var. lucidulum, commonly known as the glossy canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, orange-colored fruits that are rich in oils. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and its potential economic importance due to the oil content of its fruits.
