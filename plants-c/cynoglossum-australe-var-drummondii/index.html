Cynoglossum Australe Var. Drummondii

Cynoglossum australe var. drummondii is a striking member of the Boraginaceae family known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers. This perennial plant is native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping. Its unique, tongue-shaped petals and compact growth habit add both beauty and interest to gardens. This variety is prized for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
