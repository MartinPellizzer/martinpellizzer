Coelogyne sarasinorum

Coelogyne sarasinorum, commonly known as the Sarasin's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne sarasinorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
