Ceratostylis brevicostata

Ceratostylis brevicostata, commonly known as the short-costate helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces a single, short-spurred flower with a distinctive helmet-like lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth.

Its small size and unique floral structure make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
