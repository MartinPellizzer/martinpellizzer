Crotalaria yunnanensis var. heqingensis

Crotalaria yunnanensis var. heqingensis, commonly known as the Heqing false flax, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. It has small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This variety is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and potential ecological roles.
