Cryptantha fragilis

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, branching stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.
