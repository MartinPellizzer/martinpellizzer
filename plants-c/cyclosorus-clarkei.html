Cyclosorus clarkei

Cyclosorus clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's cyclosorus, is a member of the family Pteridaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features delicate, pinnately divided fronds with a distinctive, curling appearance. Its spores are produced in sori that are located on the underside of the fronds.
