Cotyledon spuria

Cotyledon spuria, commonly known as the pale cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are usually pale green or blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Cotyledon spuria is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
