Centaurea reuteriana var. pinnatisecta

Centaurea reuteriana var. pinnatisecta, commonly known as the pinnatisected coneflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, arranged in dense, conical inflorescences. The leaves are deeply lobed and pinnatisected, giving the plant a distinctive, feathery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
