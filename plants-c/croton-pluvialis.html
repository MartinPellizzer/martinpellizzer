Croton pluvialis

Croton pluvialis, commonly known as the rain croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
