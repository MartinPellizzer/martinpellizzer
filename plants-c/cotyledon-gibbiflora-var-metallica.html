Cotyledon gibbiflora var. metallica

Cotyledon gibbiflora var. metallica, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically covered in a silvery or metallic sheen, giving it its common name. The leaves are often arranged in a rosette pattern and can vary in color from green to blue-gray depending on light exposure. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

This plant is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
