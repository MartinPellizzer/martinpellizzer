Castanopsis psilophylla

Castanopsis psilophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tanoak, is a species of evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of southern China and northern Vietnam.

Castanopsis psilophylla thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mixed forests at elevations ranging from 1,000 to 2,500 meters.
