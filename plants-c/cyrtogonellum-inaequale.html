Cyrtogonellum inaequale

Cyrtogonellum inaequale, commonly known as the unequal horned grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically green or yellowish in color.

Cyrtogonellum inaequale is often found in open habitats such as fynbos vegetation and is adapted to dry conditions.
