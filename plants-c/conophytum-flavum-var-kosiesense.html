Conophytum flavum var. kosiesense

Conophytum flavum var. kosiesense, commonly known as the yellow conophytum, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically yellow to orange in color, with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
