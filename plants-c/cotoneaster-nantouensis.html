Cotoneaster nantouensis

Cotoneaster nantouensis, commonly known as Nantou cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its deciduous nature, producing small, ornamental berries in the fall. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. It features compound leaves and produces clusters of white or pink flowers in the spring.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
