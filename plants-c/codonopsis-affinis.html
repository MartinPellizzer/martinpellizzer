Codonopsis affinis

Codonopsis affinis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to blue. Codonopsis affinis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
