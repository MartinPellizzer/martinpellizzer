Centaurea dracunculifolia var. linearifolia

Centaurea dracunculifolia var. linearifolia, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, spindly stems that bear small, purple to pink flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
