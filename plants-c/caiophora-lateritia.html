Caiophora lateritia

Caiophora lateritia, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features basal leaves that are alternate and have a pinnate structure. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and arranged in clusters, blooming in early spring.

Caiophora lateritia is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
