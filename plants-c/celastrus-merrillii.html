Celastrus merrillii

Celastrus merrillii, commonly known as the Chinese staff-vine, is a climbing shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
