Cyperus ustulatus

Cyperus ustulatus, commonly known as the flame grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and dense clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found in disturbed soils near water bodies.

Cyperus ustulatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to various parts of the world for horticultural purposes.
