Castanopsis echinocarpa

Castanopsis echinocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese chinkapin, is a species of evergreen tree native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, beeches, and chestnuts. The tree is characterized by its dense, glossy evergreen foliage and produces small, spiny fruits that contain edible seeds. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions.
