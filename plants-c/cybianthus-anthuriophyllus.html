Cybianthus anthuriophyllus

Cybianthus anthuriophyllus, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features showy, orange-red flowers with intricate patterns that resemble flames. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Cybianthus anthuriophyllus thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
