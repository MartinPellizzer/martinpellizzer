Chrysanthemum pulvinatum

Chrysanthemum pulvinatum, commonly known as the woolly chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal regions and is well adapted to arid environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
