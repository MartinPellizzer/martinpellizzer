Croton tonduzii

Croton tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii croton, is a striking shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often multicolored leaves that display shades of red, yellow, and green, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability, it is widely cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
