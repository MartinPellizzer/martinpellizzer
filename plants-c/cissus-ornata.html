Cissus ornata

Cissus ornata, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, silvery-green leaves that have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cissus ornata is often used as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
