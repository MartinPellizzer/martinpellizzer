Collinsia sparsiflora var. collina

Collinsia sparsiflora var. collina, commonly known as the Sierra larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers.
