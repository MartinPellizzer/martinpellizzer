Clusia nemorosa var. lhotzkyana

Clusia nemorosa var. lhotzkyana, commonly known as the Lhotzkyana glorybower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, showy yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Clusia nemorosa var. lhotzkyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
