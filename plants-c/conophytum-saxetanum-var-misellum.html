Conophytum saxetanum var. misellum

Conophytum saxetanum var. misellum, commonly known as the rock rose or misellum conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
