Crassula simiana

Crassula simiana, commonly known as the "Cape snowflake," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, branching stems that are covered in small, fleshy leaves resembling snowflakes. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Crassula simiana is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
