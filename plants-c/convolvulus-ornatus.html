Convolvulus ornatus

Convolvulus ornatus, commonly known as the showy bindweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Convolvulus ornatus is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
