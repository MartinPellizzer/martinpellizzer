Cattleya gigas var. imperialis

Cattleya gigas var. imperialis, commonly known as the Imperial Cattleya, is a large, showy orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences and large, vibrant flowers that often display a mix of pink, white, and purple hues. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and often covered in a waxy coating.

This variety is highly prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively robust growth habits.
