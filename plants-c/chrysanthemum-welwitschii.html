Chrysanthemum welwitschii

Chrysanthemum welwitschii, commonly known as the Cape chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with woody stems and narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
