Chascanum africanum

Chascanum africanum, commonly known as the African torch lily, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are often evergreen.
