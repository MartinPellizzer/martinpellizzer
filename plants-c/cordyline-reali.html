Cordyline reali

Cordyline reali, commonly known as the red cordyline, is a striking evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant red or purple color, especially when young. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its unique foliage makes it a standout choice for both indoor and outdoor landscaping.
