Cassia olesiphylla

Cassia olesiphylla, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping inflorescences that produce numerous yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with narrow, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Cassia olesiphylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
