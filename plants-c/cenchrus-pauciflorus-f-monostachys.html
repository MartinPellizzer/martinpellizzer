Cenchrus pauciflorus f. monostachys

Cenchrus pauciflorus f. monostachys, commonly known as the single-stemmed slender grass, is a variant of the species Cenchrus pauciflorus.

It is a grassy plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with minimal water requirements.

Its botanical characteristics make it suitable for use in erosion control and as a forage plant in certain environments.
