Chrysanthemum Heterotomum

Chrysanthemum heterotomum is a rare and unique species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. This plant is known for its strikingly beautiful blooms that range from white to deep yellow in color. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other popular garden plants. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
