Clausena anisum-olens subsp. calciphila

Clausena anisum-olens subsp.

calciphila, commonly known as the limestone citrus, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves.

The plant thrives in calcareous soils and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates. Its fruits are small, yellow-orange, and have a unique, aromatic flavor.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
