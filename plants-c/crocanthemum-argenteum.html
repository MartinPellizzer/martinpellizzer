Crocanthemum argenteum

Crocanthemum argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and sandy soils, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs that give it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
