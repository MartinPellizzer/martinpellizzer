Cotyledon caespitosa var. paniculata

Cotyledon caespitosa var. paniculata, commonly known as the paniculate cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and fleshy, often variegated leaves. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences that bear small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among succulent collectors and gardeners.
