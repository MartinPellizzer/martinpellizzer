Castalia Blanda

Castalia blanda is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Central America. Known for its striking blue-violet petals and soft green foliage, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This species is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists due to its unique beauty and ecological significance. Unfortunately, its habitat is increasingly threatened by deforestation and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensuring the survival of this enchanting plant.
