Clinopodium dumetorum

Clinopodium dumetorum, commonly known as wild thyme, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a creeping growth habit with square stems and is often found in temperate regions.

Clinopodium dumetorum is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
