Ceropegia leachiana

Ceropegia leachiana, commonly known as the silver leaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and distinctive, waxy, silver-green leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia leachiana is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
