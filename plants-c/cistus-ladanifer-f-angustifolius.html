Cistus ladanifer f. angustifolius

Cistus ladanifer f. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic evergreen foliage and showy pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, and it produces a fragrant resin known as ladanum.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
