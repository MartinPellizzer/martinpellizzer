Cleome chilensis var. glabra

Cleome chilensis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth cleome, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, spiny stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
