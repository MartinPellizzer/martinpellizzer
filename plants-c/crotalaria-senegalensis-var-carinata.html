Crotalaria senegalensis var. carinata

Crotalaria senegalensis var. carinata, commonly known as the African crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant characterized by its erect growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically visited by bees and other pollinators. Its seeds are enclosed in pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds by wind.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
