Croton kinondoensis

Croton kinondoensis, commonly known as the Kinondo croton, is a species of flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and showy, yellow or orange flowers. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

Croton kinondoensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local communities.
