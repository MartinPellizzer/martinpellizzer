Cyrtopera angolensis

Cyrtopera angolensis, commonly known as the Angola grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa, particularly Angola and Namibia. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as savannas and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid environments with low rainfall.
