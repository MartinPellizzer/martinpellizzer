Cypripedium insigne var. elegantissimum

Cypripedium insigne var. elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features a large, showy flower with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a tall, slender stem with a single, waxy flower that blooms in late spring. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, arranged in a rosette at the base of the stem.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in specialized gardens due to its specific growing requirements.
