Combretum collinum subsp. binderianum

Combretum collinum subsp.

binderianum, commonly known as the red-flowered combretum, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is commonly found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
