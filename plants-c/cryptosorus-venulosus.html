Cryptosorus venulosus

Cryptosorus venulosus, commonly known as the veined cryptosorus, is a fern that belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green to brownish in color. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Cryptosorus venulosus is known for its intricate venation patterns, which contribute to its unique appearance and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
