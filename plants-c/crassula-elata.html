Crassula elata

Crassula elata, commonly known as the Christmas Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Crassula elata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
