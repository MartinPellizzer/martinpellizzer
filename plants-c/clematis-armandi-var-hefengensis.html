Clematis armandi var. hefengensis

Clematis armandi var. hefengensis, commonly known as the Hefeng Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and twining habit, making it suitable for trellises or fences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. Its foliage is typically heart-shaped and provides attractive foliage throughout the growing season.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
