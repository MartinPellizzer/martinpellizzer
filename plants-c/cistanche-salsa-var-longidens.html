Cistanche salsa var. longidens

Cistanche salsa var. longidens, commonly known as the long-stemmed Chinese parasitic plant, is a member of the Orobanchaceae family.

It is a hemiparasitic plant that attaches to the roots of host plants, primarily those in the Fabaceae family, to obtain water and nutrients. This species is native to arid and semi-arid regions of China and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
