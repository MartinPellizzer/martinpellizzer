Campomanesia Xanthocarpa

Campomanesia xanthocarpa is a lesser-known but fascinating plant native to the rainforests of Brazil. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is known for its unique yellow-orange fruits that ripen in the summer months. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a glossy surface and emit a mild fragrance when crushed. Despite its beauty and potential for ornamental use, it remains relatively obscure in the global horticultural community.
