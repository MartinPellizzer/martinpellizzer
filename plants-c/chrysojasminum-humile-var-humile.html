Chrysojasminum humile var. humile

Chrysojasminum humile var. humile, commonly known as the dwarf golden jasmine, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions.

It features small, dark green leaves that are glossy and oval in shape. The plant produces fragrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
