Coelogyne parviflora

Coelogyne parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces compact, pseudobulbous stems and features delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its blooms are usually small and clustered, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Coelogyne parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
