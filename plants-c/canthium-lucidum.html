Canthium lucidum

Canthium lucidum, commonly known as the shining senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds coated in a sticky, resinous substance. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.
