Cheirolophus Sempervirens

Cheirolophus sempervirens is a striking alpine plant known for its unique flower structure and resilience in harsh environments. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, it thrives in rocky and arid conditions where few other plants can survive. Its most distinctive feature is the elongated, tube-like petals that curve inward, creating a striking visual effect. This plant plays an important role in its ecosystem, supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity. Despite its beauty, it remains a rare and delicate species, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
