Crassula wyliei

Crassula wyliei, commonly known as the Wylie's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
