Chlorophytum beniense

Chlorophytum beniense, commonly known as the African lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its strap-like, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and ease of care. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight.

Chlorophytum beniense is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air.
