Cynara alba

Cynara alba, commonly known as white thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, silver-green leaves and pale yellow to white flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Cynara alba is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and hardy species in its native habitat.
