Cyathea nayarii

Cyathea nayarii, commonly known as the Nayar fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. Cyathea nayarii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
