Chenopodium neomexicanum var. arizonicum

Chenopodium neomexicanum var. arizonicum, commonly known as desert goosefoot, is a shrub-like plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as deserts and rocky soils. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and are wind-pollinated.

The species is valued for its adaptability to harsh conditions and has been used traditionally for various purposes.
