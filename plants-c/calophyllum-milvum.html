Calophyllum milvum

Calophyllum milvum, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and large, glossy green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellow flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. The fruit contains a single seed and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
