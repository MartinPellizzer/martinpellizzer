Crotalaria warfae

Crotalaria warfae, commonly known as the Warfae cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are often used as a food source in some local communities.

Crotalaria warfae is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural practices.
