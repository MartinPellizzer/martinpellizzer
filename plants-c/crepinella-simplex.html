Crepinella simplex

Crepinella simplex, commonly known as the slender crepe jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Crepinella simplex is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, visually appealing plant.
