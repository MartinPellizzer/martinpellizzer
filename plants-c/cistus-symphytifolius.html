Cistus symphytifolius

Cistus symphytifolius, commonly known as the rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, evergreen leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils and is drought-tolerant.

Cistus symphytifolius is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
