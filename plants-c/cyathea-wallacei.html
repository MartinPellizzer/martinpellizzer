Cyathea wallacei

Cyathea wallacei, commonly known as the Wallace's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in favorable conditions. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and storing nutrients.

Cyathea wallacei is often found in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests.
