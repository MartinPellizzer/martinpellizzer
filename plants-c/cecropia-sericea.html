Cecropia sericea

Cecropia sericea, commonly known as silky ceiba, is a fast-growing tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and striking, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a fibrous, buttressed trunk and produces woody fruits that contain seeds with a fleshy aril. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Cecropia sericea plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
