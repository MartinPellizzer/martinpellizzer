Cymbidium lowianum var. lowianum

Cymbidium lowianum var. lowianum, commonly known as the Low's cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of fragrant, pendulous flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in cool to temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
