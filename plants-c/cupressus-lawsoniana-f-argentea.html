Cupressus lawsoniana f. argentea

Cupressus lawsoniana f. argentea, commonly known as the Argentea Leyland cypress, is a cultivar of the Leyland cypress characterized by its distinctive silvery-blue foliage.

This ornamental tree is native to the United Kingdom and is widely cultivated for its fast growth and dense foliage. The leaves are narrow, scale-like, and arranged in crossed pairs, giving the plant a unique appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for hedges and privacy screens.

However, it is susceptible to certain diseases and pests, requiring regular maintenance for optimal health.
