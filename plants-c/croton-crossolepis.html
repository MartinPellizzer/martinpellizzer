Croton crossolepis

Croton crossolepis, commonly known as the red-flowered croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, often featuring shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. Its leaves are typically broad and have a rough, textured surface.

Croton crossolepis thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
