Casearia latifolia

Casearia latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf casearia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers.
