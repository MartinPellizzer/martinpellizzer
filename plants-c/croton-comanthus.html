Croton comanthus

Croton comanthus, commonly known as the red-stemmed croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
