Carex nilagirica

Carex nilagirica, commonly known as Nilagiri sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the Himalayas, particularly found in regions of India and Nepal. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets.

Carex nilagirica is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland habitats.
