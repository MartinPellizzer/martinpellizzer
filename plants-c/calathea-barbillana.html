Calathea barbillana

Calathea barbillana, commonly known as the "Cape Cod calathea," is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled leaves with intricate patterns in shades of green, white, and silver. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea barbillana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
