Cattleya × exoniensis

Cattleya × exoniensis, commonly known as the Exoniensis Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the Amazon basin.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.

This hybrid is often used in breeding programs to develop new orchid varieties with enhanced flower colors and fragrance.
