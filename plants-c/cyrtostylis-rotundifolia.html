Cyrtostylis rotundifolia

Cyrtostylis rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed torch orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, hairy stem that supports a single, showy flower with a distinctive red or pink coloration. The plant has oval-shaped, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

The orchid's unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a notable species in its native habitat.
