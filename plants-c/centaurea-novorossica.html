Centaurea novorossica

Centaurea novorossica, commonly known as the Novorossiysk centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region and parts of southern Russia.

It is a perennial herb with upright, branched stems that bear blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in temperate climates.

Centaurea novorossica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
