Clerodendrum tracyanum

Clerodendrum tracyanum, commonly known as the red flame or red trumpet tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its striking red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
