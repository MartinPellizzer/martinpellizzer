Critesion procerum

Critesion procerum, commonly known as the slender cress, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of North America.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils along stream banks and wet meadows. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped. Its flowers are white or pink, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

It is often found in wetland habitats and is an important species for pollinators.
