Cipuropsis capituligera

Cipuropsis capituligera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a small perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant has basal leaves that are narrow and linear, with a hairy texture. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in a dense cluster at the top of the stem.
