Calycanthemum

Calycanthemum, commonly known as sweetshrub, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Calycanthaceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States and features aromatic, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically dark green, glossy, and arranged oppositely on the stems. The plant produces small, red or orange fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Calycanthemum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in partial shade.
