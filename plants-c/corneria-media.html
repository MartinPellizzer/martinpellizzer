Corneria media

Corneria media, commonly known as the common corneria, is a herbaceous plant native to temperate regions.

It typically grows to a height of 30-60 cm and features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a versatile garden plant.

Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal remedies for its mild medicinal properties.
