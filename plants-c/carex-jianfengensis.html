Carex jianfengensis

Carex jianfengensis, commonly known as the Jianfeng sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the island of Hainan, where it grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in clusters. It is characterized by its dense, rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
