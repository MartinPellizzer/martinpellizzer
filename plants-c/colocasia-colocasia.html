Colocasia colocasia

Colocasia colocasia, commonly known as the elephant ear, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tubers of the plant are also edible when properly prepared.
