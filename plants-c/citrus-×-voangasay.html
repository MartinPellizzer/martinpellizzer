Citrus × voangasay

Citrus × voangasay, commonly known as the Cambodian lime, is a hybrid citrus plant originating from Cambodia.

It is characterized by its small, round, and highly acidic fruits, which are typically harvested when they are still green. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and ample sunlight. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, while the flowers are fragrant and white.

This citrus variety is valued for its unique flavor and is often used in local cuisine and traditional medicine.
