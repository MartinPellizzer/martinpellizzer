Cyathea schanschin

Cyathea schanschin, commonly known as the Schanschiner tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting the growth of the upright stem.

Cyathea schanschin plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
