Caladenia gladiolata

Caladenia gladiolata, commonly known as the sword-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. The plant features a single, erect stem with a single, showy orchid flower that is usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and sword-shaped, giving it its common name.

Caladenia gladiolata is part of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its delicate and striking floral display.
