Cassia kituiensis

Cassia kituiensis, commonly known as Kitui cassia, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in racemes, and it produces elongated, woody pods containing seeds.

Cassia kituiensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
