Crassula rubicunda var. lydenburgensis

Crassula rubicunda var. lydenburgensis, commonly known as the Lydenburg crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
