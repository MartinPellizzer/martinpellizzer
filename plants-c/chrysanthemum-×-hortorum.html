Chrysanthemum × hortorum

Chrysanthemum × hortorum, commonly known as the garden chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including yellow, red, white, and purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of 30 to 150 centimeters. Its leaves are usually alternate, with a rough, hairy texture, and its stems are often branched.

The flowers bloom in the fall, making it a popular choice for autumn gardens and floral arrangements.
