Chomelia multiflora

Chomelia multiflora, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has large, palmate leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental tree.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
