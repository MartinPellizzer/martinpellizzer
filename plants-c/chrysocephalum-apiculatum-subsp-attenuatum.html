Chrysocephalum apiculatum subsp. attenuatum

Chrysocephalum apiculatum subsp.

attenuatum, commonly known as the slender white everlasting, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, silver-green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant belongs to the family Asteraceae, commonly known as the daisy or sunflower family. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils.
