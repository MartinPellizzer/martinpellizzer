Cattleya alexandrae var. tenebrosa

Cattleya alexandrae var. tenebrosa, commonly known as the Black Orchid, is a striking orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black petals and sepals, which contrast beautifully with its white or pale yellow lip. This variety belongs to the Cattleya genus, known for its showy, fragrant flowers and epiphytic growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

The plant's unique coloration makes it a highly sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
