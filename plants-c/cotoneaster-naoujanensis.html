Cotoneaster naoujanensis

Cotoneaster naoujanensis, commonly known as the Naoujan cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Turkey and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit.
