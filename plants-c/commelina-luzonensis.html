Commelina luzonensis

Commelina luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes grass-like plants with distinctive floral structures. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue or violet flowers with three petals. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
