Callistemon lilacinus f. carminus

Callistemon lilacinus f. carminus, commonly known as the red bottlebrush, is a compact shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, crimson-red cylindrical flower clusters that resemble bottle brushes, which bloom from late winter to early spring. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract birds and pollinators.
