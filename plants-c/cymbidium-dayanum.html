Cymbidium dayanum

Cymbidium dayanum, commonly known as the Yunnan orchid, is a species native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae. The plant features erect, sword-shaped leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers. Its blooms, which are typically white or pale yellow, appear in late winter to early spring.

Cymbidium dayanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
