Chusquea arachniformis

Chusquea arachniformis, commonly known as the spider fern, is a species of bamboo that belongs to the grass family, Poaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Bolivia and Peru. The plant is characterized by its dense, thornless culms that form a clumping growth habit. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky texture.

This unique appearance gives it the common name "spider fern" due to its delicate and web-like appearance.
