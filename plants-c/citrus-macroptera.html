Citrus macroptera

Citrus macroptera, commonly known as the Natal orange.

It is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa, characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is typically small, round, and has a thin, leathery rind with a sweet, juicy pulp. It is often used in traditional medicine and for its aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
