Cleistes rodeiensis

Cleistes rodeiensis, commonly known as the Rodeiense Bellflower, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, bell-shaped flowers that are often pale green or white.
