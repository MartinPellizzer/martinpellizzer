Centaurea gigantea subsp. gigantea

Centaurea gigantea subsp.

gigantea, commonly known as the giant centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and striking blue to violet flowers.

The plant features spiny, grass-like leaves and produces seed heads that are often used in herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
