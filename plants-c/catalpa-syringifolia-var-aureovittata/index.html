Catalpa Syringifolia Var. Aureovittata

Catalpa syringifolia var. aureovittata is a striking plant known for its vibrant yellow and green variegation. This variant of the catalpa tree is prized for its ornamental value and unique leaf patterns. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens. The plant is native to eastern Asia and has gained attention for its beauty and adaptability. Its distinct appearance makes it a standout in any landscape.
