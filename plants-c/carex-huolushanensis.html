Carex huolushanensis

Carex huolushanensis, commonly known as the Huolushan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Hubei and Hainan provinces. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are characteristic of the sedge family.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and small fauna.
