Clusiella macropetala

Clusiella macropetala, commonly known as the large-petaled clusiella, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are predominantly yellow or orange, with prominent, fleshy petals that attract various pollinators.
