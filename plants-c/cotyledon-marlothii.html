Cotyledon marlothii

Cotyledon marlothii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Cotyledon marlothii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
