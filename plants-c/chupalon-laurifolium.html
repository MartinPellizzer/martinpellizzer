Chupalon laurifolium

Chupalon laurifolium, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer months. These flowers are followed by small, round, woody fruits containing a single seed.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
