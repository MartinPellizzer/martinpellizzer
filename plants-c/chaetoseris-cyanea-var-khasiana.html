Chaetoseris cyanea var. khasiana

Chaetoseris cyanea var. khasiana, commonly known as the Himalayan bluehead, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to violet flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in alpine ecosystems.
