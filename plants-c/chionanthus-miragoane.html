Chionanthus miragoane

Chionanthus miragoane, commonly known as the Miragoane chionanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, specifically Haiti and the Dominican Republic.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring or early summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly waxy texture.
