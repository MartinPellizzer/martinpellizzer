Coelogyne fleuryi

Coelogyne fleuryi, commonly known as the Fleury's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting the plant's growth and storage of water and nutrients.

Coelogyne fleuryi is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
