Coelogyne longeciliata

Coelogyne longeciliata, commonly known as the long-armed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, rhizomatous stem and broad, fleshy leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. Its blooming period typically occurs during the summer months, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Coelogyne longeciliata requires well-drained soil and moderate light conditions to thrive.
