Croton belanseanus

Croton belanseanus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red and orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
