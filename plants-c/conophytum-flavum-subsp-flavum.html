Conophytum flavum subsp. flavum

Conophytum flavum subsp.

flavum, commonly known as the yellow conophytum, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, paired leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.
