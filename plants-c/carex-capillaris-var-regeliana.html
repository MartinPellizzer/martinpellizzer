Carex capillaris var. regeliana

Carex capillaris var. regeliana, commonly known as the Regel's sedge, is a variant of the common sedge species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are followed by triangular, three-sided seed capsules.

This variety thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
