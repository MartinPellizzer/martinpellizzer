Cirsium virgenum

Cirsium virgenum, commonly known as Virgin's thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and can be found in prairies and open woodlands. It has a robust root system that helps it survive in harsh environmental conditions.

The flowers attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
