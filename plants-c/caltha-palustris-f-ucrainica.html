Caltha palustris f. ucrainica

Caltha palustris f. ucrainica, commonly known as the Ukrainian marsh marigold, is a rare botanical variant of the marsh marigold.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. This plant thrives in wetland environments, such as marshes and along stream banks. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green.

The species is notable for its ecological significance in supporting pollinators and maintaining wetland biodiversity.
