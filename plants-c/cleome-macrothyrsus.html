Cleome macrothyrsus

Cleome macrothyrsus, commonly known as the tall cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, branched inflorescences that bear small, yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Cleome macrothyrsus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its showy blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
