Chaenactis Alpina

Chaenactis alpina, also known as the alpine hawkweed, is a hardy flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Northern Hemisphere. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh climatic conditions. With its delicate white and yellow flowers, it adds a touch of beauty to alpine meadows. This plant plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems. Its unique adaptations make it a fascinating subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
