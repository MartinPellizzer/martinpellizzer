Calanthe tsoongiana var. guizhouensis

Calanthe tsoongiana var. guizhouensis, commonly known as the Guizhou calanthe, is a rare and ornamental orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within mountainous regions. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and horticultural interest.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
