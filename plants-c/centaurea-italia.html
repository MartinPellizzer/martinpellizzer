Centaurea italia

Centaurea italia, commonly known as Italian centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bracteate flower heads.

Centaurea italia is valued both for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
