Chassalia pauciflora

Chassalia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered chassalia, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of small, yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

Chassalia pauciflora is often found in grasslands and open woodland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
