Chamaecrista linearifolia

Chamaecrista linearifolia, commonly known as the slender crownflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptations to drought conditions. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in spring, and it is often found in rocky soils or along dry streambeds.
