Crassocephalum notonioides

Crassocephalum notonioides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant is often cultivated as a decorative ornamental due to its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
