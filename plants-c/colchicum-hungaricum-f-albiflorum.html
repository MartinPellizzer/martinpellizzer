Colchicum hungaricum f. albiflorum

Colchicum hungaricum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the Hungarian colchicum with white flowers, is a botanical variant of the colchicum genus.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe, particularly Hungary. The plant features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of basal, sword-shaped leaves that emerge in early spring.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
