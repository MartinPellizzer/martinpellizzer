Chaenactis cusickii

Chaenactis cusickii, commonly known as Cusick's whitetop, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer.

Its hardy nature allows it to thrive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
