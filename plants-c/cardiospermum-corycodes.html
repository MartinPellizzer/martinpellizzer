Cardiospermum corycodes

Cardiospermum corycodes, commonly known as the pink heartleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, showy pink or purple flowers. Its fruits are distinctive, resembling small, inflated capsules that split open to release seeds.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and floral display.
