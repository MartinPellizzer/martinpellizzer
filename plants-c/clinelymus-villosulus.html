Clinelymus villosulus

Clinelymus villosulus, commonly known as the silky burrweed, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, wiry stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, spiny fruits. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Clinelymus villosulus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
