Cassia eitenorum var. regana

Cassia eitenorum var. regana, commonly known as the Eiten's cassia, is a shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain seeds covered in a fluffy, white coating. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in dry, open woodlands.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
