Cirsium pungens

Cirsium pungens, commonly known as the Prickly Lettuce, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated, spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. Its leaves are covered in stiff, bristly hairs, which give it its common name.

The flowers are usually yellow or purple and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
