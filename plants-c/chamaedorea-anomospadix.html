Chamaedorea anomospadix

Chamaedorea anomospadix, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bamboo palm, is a member of the Arecaceae family native to Mexico.

It is a compact, upright plant with slender, dark green leaves that are narrow and elongated. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, red fruits that contain edible seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

Due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
