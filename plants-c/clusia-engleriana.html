Clusia engleriana

Clusia engleriana, commonly known as the waxleaf clusia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Clusiaceae family, characterized by its evergreen leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant is known for its glossy, thick foliage that remains green throughout the year. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.

Clusia engleriana can grow as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat.
