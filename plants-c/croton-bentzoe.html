Croton bentzoe

Croton bentzoe, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically broad and have a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Croton bentzoe is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
