Columnea fawcettii

Columnea fawcettii, commonly known as the Fawcett's flame.

This evergreen shrub is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a contrasting yellow or orange throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
