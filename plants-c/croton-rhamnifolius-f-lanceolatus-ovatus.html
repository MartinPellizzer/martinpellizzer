Croton rhamnifolius f. lanceolatus-ovatus

Croton rhamnifolius f. lanceolatus-ovatus, commonly known as the lanceolate-ovate croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
