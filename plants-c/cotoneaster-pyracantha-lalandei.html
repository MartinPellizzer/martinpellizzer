Cotoneaster pyracantha lalandei

Cotoneaster pyracantha lalandei, commonly known as the Laland's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy dark green leaves that turn red in the autumn. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters and has a compact, rounded form.

Cotoneaster pyracantha lalandei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness.
