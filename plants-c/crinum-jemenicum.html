Crinum jemenicum

Crinum jemenicum, commonly known as the Yemen rain lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the arid regions of Yemen.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a red or purple stripe down the center. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for dry climates.

Crinum jemenicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral displays.
