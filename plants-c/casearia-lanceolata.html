Casearia lanceolata

Casearia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf casearia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family, which includes willows and poplars, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves. Casearia lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
