Cynorkis elegans

Cynorkis elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with intricate patterns, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Cynorkis elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
