Capsella hyrcana

Capsella hyrcana, commonly known as the Persian cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Central Asia and thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by rounded, green pods containing several seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Capsella hyrcana is often found in disturbed soils and can be a weed in agricultural settings.
