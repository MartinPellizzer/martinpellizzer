Cestichis persimilis

Cestichis persimilis, commonly known as the slender cestichis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
