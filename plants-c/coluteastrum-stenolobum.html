Coluteastrum stenolobum

Coluteastrum stenolobum, commonly known as narrowleaf cowpea, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a slender, elongated pod that contains several seeds.

This species is often grown as a forage crop due to its nutritional value and adaptability to various soil types.
