Campomanesia Guazumifolia

Campomanesia guazumifolia is a tropical evergreen shrub known for its ornamental value and ecological importance. Native to regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments under the canopy of forests. The plant features glossy, elliptical leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit, a yellowish berry, is edible and often consumed by local wildlife. Due to its unique characteristics, it is increasingly being cultivated for horticultural purposes.
