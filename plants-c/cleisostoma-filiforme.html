Cleisostoma filiforme

Cleisostoma filiforme, commonly known as the slender cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, filamentous leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant prefers humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a unique structure that facilitates pollination by specific insect species.

Cleisostoma filiforme is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
