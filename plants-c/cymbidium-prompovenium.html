Cymbidium prompovenium

Cymbidium prompovenium, commonly known as the Prompovenium orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect stems that bear clusters of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, producing pink to purple blooms with a yellow lip. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
