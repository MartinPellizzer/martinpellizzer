Chassalia pteropetala

Chassalia pteropetala, commonly known as the winged chassalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow petals and a central disc of smaller florets. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Chassalia pteropetala thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
