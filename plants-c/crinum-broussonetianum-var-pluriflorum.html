Crinum broussonetianum var. pluriflorum

Crinum broussonetianum var. pluriflorum, commonly known as the African lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in umbels. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and slightly wavy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
