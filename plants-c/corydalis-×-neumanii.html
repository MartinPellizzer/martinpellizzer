Corydalis × neumanii

Corydalis × neumanii, commonly known as the hybrid corydalis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

Corydalis × neumanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
