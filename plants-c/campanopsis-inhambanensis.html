Campanopsis inhambanensis

Campanopsis inhambanensis, commonly known as the Inhambane bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pale blue. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils in dry, open habitats. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
