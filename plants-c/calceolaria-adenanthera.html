Calceolaria adenanthera

Calceolaria adenanthera, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, saucer-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

Its unique floral structure and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice for horticultural displays.
