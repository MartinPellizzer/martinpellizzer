Callitriche deflexa

Callitriche deflexa, commonly known as the flexible water starwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

The plant has slender, branching stems that often lie flat on the water surface, with small, opposite leaves that are usually submerged.
