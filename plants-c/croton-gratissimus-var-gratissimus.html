Croton gratissimus var. gratissimus

Croton gratissimus var. gratissimus, commonly known as the golden croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that display shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are typically ovate or elliptical with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its vibrant foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
