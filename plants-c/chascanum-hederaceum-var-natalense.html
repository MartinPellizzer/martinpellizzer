Chascanum hederaceum var. natalense

Chascanum hederaceum var. natalense, commonly known as Natal ivy, is a climbing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves with a distinctive reddish or purplish hue on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. It thrives in moderate to high light conditions and prefers well-drained soil.

Natal ivy is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
