Chionanthus sessiliflorus

Chionanthus sessiliflorus, commonly known as the Chinese fringeflower, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate to elliptic leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

The genus Chionanthus is characterized by its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
