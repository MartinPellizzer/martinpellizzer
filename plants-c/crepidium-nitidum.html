Crepidium nitidum

Crepidium nitidum, commonly known as the smooth leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
