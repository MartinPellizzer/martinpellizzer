Cookia glabrescens

Cookia glabrescens, commonly known as the silky glabrous cocklebur, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Cookia glabrescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
