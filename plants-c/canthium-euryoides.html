Canthium euryoides

Canthium euryoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually narrow and lance-shaped. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are clustered in cyathia, a characteristic feature of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Canthium euryoides is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
