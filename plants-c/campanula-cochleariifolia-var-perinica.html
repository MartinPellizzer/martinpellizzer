Campanula cochleariifolia var. perinica

Campanula cochleariifolia var. perinica, commonly known as the Perinica bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lanceolate, with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
