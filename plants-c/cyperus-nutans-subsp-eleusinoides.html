Cyperus nutans subsp. eleusinoides

Cyperus nutans subsp.

eleusinoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf sedges, is a member of the Cyperaceae family. It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces small, three-ranked spikelets that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
