Chlorophytum belgaumense

Chlorophytum belgaumense, commonly known as the Belgaum spider plant, is a flowering plant native to India.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, pendant inflorescences with small white or pale green flowers. It is typically found in rocky or coastal areas and is adapted to well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is sometimes grown as a houseplant.
