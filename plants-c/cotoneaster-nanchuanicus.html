Cotoneaster nanchuanicus

Cotoneaster nanchuanicus, commonly known as the Nanchuan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its attractive clusters of red berries that persist through the winter. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It features small, ovate leaves and produces white or pink flowers in the spring.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
