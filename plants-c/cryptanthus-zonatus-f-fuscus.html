Cryptanthus zonatus f. fuscus

Cryptanthus zonatus f. fuscus, commonly known as the dark zonatus cryptanthus, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to Brazil.

It features dense clusters of small, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green to almost black in color, giving it a striking, almost mysterious appearance. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it retain moisture in its native arid environment.

This cultivar is valued for its unique coloration and is often grown as an ornamental plant in succulent collections.
