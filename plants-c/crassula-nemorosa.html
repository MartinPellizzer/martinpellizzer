Crassula nemorosa

Crassula nemorosa, commonly known as the forest crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the spring. It is often grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Crassula nemorosa prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight to thrive.
