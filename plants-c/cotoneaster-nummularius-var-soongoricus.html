Cotoneaster nummularius var. soongoricus

Cotoneaster nummularius var. soongoricus, commonly known as the Korean cotoneaster, is a shrub native to Korea and parts of eastern Asia.

It features small, round, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and suitable for use in gardens or as part of landscaping.
