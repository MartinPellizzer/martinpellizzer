Crinum australasicum var. angustifolium

Crinum australasicum var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved crinum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in umbels. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often emerging from a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
