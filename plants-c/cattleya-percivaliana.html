Cattleya percivaliana

Cattleya percivaliana, commonly known as the Percival's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant pink or purple hues with intricate patterns. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a striking ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Cattleya percivaliana is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
