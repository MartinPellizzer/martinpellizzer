Cleistocactus jajoanus

Cleistocactus jajoanus, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is typically found in rocky, arid environments, where it thrives in full sun and well-draining soil. Its flowers are often nocturnal, opening at night and closing during the day, a unique adaptation to its environment.

The plant is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
