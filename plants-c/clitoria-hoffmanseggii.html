Clitoria hoffmanseggii

Clitoria hoffmanseggii, commonly known as the Hoffman's pea or the blue pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing legume with delicate, butterfly-like blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clitoria hoffmanseggii is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
