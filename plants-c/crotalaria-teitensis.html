Crotalaria teitensis

Crotalaria teitensis, commonly known as the Taiti crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is typically shrub-like, with hairy stems and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
