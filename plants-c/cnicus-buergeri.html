Cnicus buergeri

Cnicus buergeri, commonly known as the Buerger's thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan, this plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats. It is often found in open areas such as fields and along roadsides.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
