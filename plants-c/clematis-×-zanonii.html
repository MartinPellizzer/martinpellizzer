Clematis × zanonii

Clematis × zanonii, commonly known as the Zanonii Clematis, is a hybrid vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to climb using its tendrils. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

Clematis × zanonii prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardens and landscapes.
