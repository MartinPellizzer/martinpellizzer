Cordia macrophylla

Cordia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved cordia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.
