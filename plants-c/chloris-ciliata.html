Chloris ciliata

Chloris ciliata, commonly known as the silver grass or slender feather grass, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery plumes. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive silvery sheen along the edges.

Chloris ciliata is often used in ornamental gardening for its ornamental plumes and low maintenance requirements.
