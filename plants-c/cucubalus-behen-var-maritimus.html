Cucubalus behen var. maritimus

Cucubalus behen var. maritimus, commonly known as sea buckthorn, is a shrub native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange berries, which are rich in vitamin C and other nutrients. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, making it well adapted to maritime environments. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ecological benefits, including soil stabilization and habitat provision for wildlife.
