Cestrum limitatis

Cestrum limitatis, commonly known as the limited cestrum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes tomatoes and peppers, and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers. Cestrum limitatis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrance.
