Costus sericeus

Costus sericeus, commonly known as silver costus, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, dark green leaves with a silvery underside. The plant produces striking, orange-red spadices that are highly ornamental. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Costus sericeus is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor settings.
