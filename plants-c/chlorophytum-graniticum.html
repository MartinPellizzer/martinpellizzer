Chlorophytum graniticum

Chlorophytum graniticum, commonly known as the Granite Spider Plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of small, grass-like leaves that often exhibit a bluish-green hue. The plant produces delicate, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Chlorophytum graniticum is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and air-purifying properties.
