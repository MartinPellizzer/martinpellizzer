Crassula margaritifera

Crassula margaritifera, commonly known as the "Pearl Necklace Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
