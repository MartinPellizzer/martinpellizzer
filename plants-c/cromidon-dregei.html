Cromidon dregei

Cromidon dregei, commonly known as the Cape York daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it requires well-drained conditions to flourish.
