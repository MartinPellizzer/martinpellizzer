Calytrix oncophylla

Calytrix oncophylla, commonly known as the pink mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in coastal regions and sandy soils. It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in well-drained conditions.

Calytrix oncophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
