Collinsonia Szechuanensis

Collinsonia szechuanensis is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China. It belongs to the family Polemoniacaceae and is known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a unique addition to woodland gardens. Its ornamental value has led to increased interest among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Collinsonia szechuanensis remains underappreciated and is often overlooked in favor of more common garden plants.
