Calyptronoma occidentalis

Calyptronoma occidentalis, commonly known as the western calyptron, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has a compact growth habit and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its botanical features include woody stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that help reduce water loss.
