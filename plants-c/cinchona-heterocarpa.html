Cinchona heterocarpa

Cinchona heterocarpa, commonly known as the red cinchona, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rubiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Peru and Ecuador, where it grows in humid, mountainous forests. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
