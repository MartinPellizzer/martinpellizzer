Cakile edentula var. americana

Cakile edentula var. americana, commonly known as American rockcress, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. It has basal leaves that are pinnately lobed and often covered in fine hairs.
