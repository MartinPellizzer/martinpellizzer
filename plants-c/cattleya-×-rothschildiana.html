Cattleya × rothschildiana

Cattleya × rothschildiana, commonly known as the Rothschild's cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically produces large, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a distinctive yellow or orange lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
