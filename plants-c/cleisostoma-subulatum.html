Cleisostoma subulatum

Cleisostoma subulatum, commonly known as the slender lip orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, delicate flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often pale yellow or white. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, adapted for efficient water retention in its natural habitat.

Cleisostoma subulatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ease of cultivation under suitable conditions.
