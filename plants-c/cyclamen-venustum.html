Cyclamen venustum

Cyclamen venustum, commonly known as the noble cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its tubers store nutrients, allowing it to survive harsh winters and regrow in spring.

Cyclamen venustum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
