Cosmos pulcherrimus

Cosmos pulcherrimus, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has feathery, finely divided leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Cosmos pulcherrimus is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
