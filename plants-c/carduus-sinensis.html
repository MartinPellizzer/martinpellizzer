Carduus sinensis

Carduus sinensis, commonly known as Chinese thistle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive leaf structures. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pink flowers that attract pollinators. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and can be invasive in certain regions.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and a potential weed in agricultural settings.
