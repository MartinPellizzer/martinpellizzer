Congea unguiculata

Congea unguiculata, commonly known as the Indian mallow or kudzu.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is also known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
