Cirsium × fontqueri

Cirsium × fontqueri, commonly known as the Fountain Creek thistle, is a hybrid plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial herb with spiny, gray-green leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a robust root system that helps it survive in harsh conditions.

Its flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, contributing to local biodiversity.
