Crotalaria staneriana

Crotalaria staneriana, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Crotalaria staneriana is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
