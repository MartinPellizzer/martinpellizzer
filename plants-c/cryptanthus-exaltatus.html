Cryptanthus exaltatus

Cryptanthus exaltatus, commonly known as the flame grass or red cryptanthus, is a striking member of the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant features compact, mat-forming growth with bright red or orange bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its colorful display in both garden and indoor settings.
