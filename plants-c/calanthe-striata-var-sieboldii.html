Calanthe striata var. sieboldii

Calanthe striata var. sieboldii, commonly known as the Siebold's calanthe, is a species of orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, lance-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that are often arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its delicate and elegant appearance.
