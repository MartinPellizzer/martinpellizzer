Clinopodium × hostii

Clinopodium × hostii, commonly known as the Hosta mint or silver mint, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features soft, velvety, silver-green foliage that is often variegated with white or cream markings. It produces small, pale blue or violet flowers in whorls along the stems, typically blooming in late summer.

Clinopodium × hostii is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and mild mint-like fragrance.
