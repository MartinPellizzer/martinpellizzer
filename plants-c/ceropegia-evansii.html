Ceropegia evansii

Ceropegia evansii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia evansii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
