Cephalophyllum bredasdorpense

Cephalophyllum bredasdorpense, commonly known as the Bredasdorp headplant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its adaptations to arid environments. Its leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

It produces small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange, adding visual interest to its habitat.
