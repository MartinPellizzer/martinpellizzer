Croton touranensis

Croton touranensis, commonly known as the Touranensis croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Tanzania and Kenya. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display a mix of green, red, and yellow hues. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The leaves are alternate and have a rough texture, while the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits.
