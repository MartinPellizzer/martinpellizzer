Crotalaria hilariana

Crotalaria hilariana, commonly known as the golden crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces spherical seed pods that split open to release its seeds when mature. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its vibrant blooms.
