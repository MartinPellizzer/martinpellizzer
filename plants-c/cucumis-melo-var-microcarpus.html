Cucumis melo var. microcarpus

Cucumis melo var. microcarpus, commonly known as the Persian melon, is a compact, bushy plant that produces small, round fruits with a sweet, aromatic flavor.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its edible fruit, which is rich in vitamins and minerals.
