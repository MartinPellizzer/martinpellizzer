Cyclamen repandum var. repandum

Cyclamen repandum var. repandum, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive wavy margin and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers with showy, spurred petals. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor or outdoor plant. Its bulbs are adapted to survive in harsh conditions, allowing it to bloom in early spring.

Cyclamen repandum var. repandum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
