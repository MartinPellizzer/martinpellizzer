Centaurea jacea var. cadevallii

Centaurea jacea var. cadevallii, commonly known as the Cadevallii Blazing Star, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in a compact, bushy form. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and ecological landscapes.
