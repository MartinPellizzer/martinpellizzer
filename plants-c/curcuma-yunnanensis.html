Curcuma yunnanensis

Curcuma yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan turmeric, is a perennial herb native to the Yunnan province in China.

It belongs to the ginger family, Zingiberaceae, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces large, lance-shaped leaves and has showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to other Curcuma species.

The botanical features of C. yunnanensis make it a valuable plant for both horticultural and medicinal purposes.
