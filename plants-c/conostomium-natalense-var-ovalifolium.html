Conostomium natalense var. ovalifolium

Conostomium natalense var. ovalifolium, commonly known as Natal's conostomium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, oval-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
