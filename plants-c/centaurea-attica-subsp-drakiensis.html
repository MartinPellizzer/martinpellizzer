Centaurea attica subsp. drakiensis

Centaurea attica subsp.

drakiensis, commonly known as the Drakonisos thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems and striking purple to red flowers.

The plant has lance-shaped, gray-green leaves with sparse, stiff hairs. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as slopes and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native range.
