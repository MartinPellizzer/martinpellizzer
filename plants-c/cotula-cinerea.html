Cotula cinerea

Cotula cinerea, commonly known as silver cotula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with hairy, gray-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cotula cinerea is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
