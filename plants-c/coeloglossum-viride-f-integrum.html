Coeloglossum viride f. integrum

Coeloglossum viride f. integrum, commonly known as the green hawksbeard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright, slender stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces yellow composite flowers arranged in a single terminal head, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
