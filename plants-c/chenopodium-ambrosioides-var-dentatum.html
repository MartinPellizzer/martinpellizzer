Chenopodium ambrosioides var. dentatum

Chenopodium ambrosioides var. dentatum, commonly known as rough goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its serrated, lance-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas. It has a fibrous root system and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
