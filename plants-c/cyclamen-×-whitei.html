Cyclamen × whitei

Cyclamen × whitei, commonly known as the hybrid cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive waxy coating, and its flowers are typically pink or white, with curled petals and a spurred shape. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Cyclamen × whitei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its delicate beauty and seasonal blooming.
