Crotalaria foliosa var. oblonga

Crotalaria foliosa var. oblonga, commonly known as the oblong leaf cowpea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its oblong, compound leaves and produces yellow flowers that are followed by pod-like fruits containing kidney-shaped seeds. It is often grown as a forage crop and has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties.

The plant is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural systems.
