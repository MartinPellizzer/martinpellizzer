Chloranthus macranthera

Chloranthus macranthera, commonly known as the large-flowered goosefoot, is a member of the Chloranthaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like China, Japan, and the Philippines. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in appearance. Its most distinctive feature is its showy, clustered inflorescence composed of small, greenish-yellow flowers.

Chloranthus macranthera typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
