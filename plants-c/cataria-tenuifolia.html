Cataria tenuifolia

Cataria tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender catnip, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and basil. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, oval-shaped leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink to purple in color, blooming in summer.

Cataria tenuifolia is known for its mild mint-like scent and is often used in herbal teas and as a decorative plant.
