Cyperus surinamensis var. lutescens

Cyperus surinamensis var. lutescens, commonly known as the golden flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

This variety is distinguished by its vibrant yellow flowers, which contrast with its dark foliage.
