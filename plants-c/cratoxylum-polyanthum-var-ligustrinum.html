Cratoxylum polyanthum var. ligustrinum

Cratoxylum polyanthum var. ligustrinum, commonly known as the purple-flowered cratoxylum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically orange or red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
