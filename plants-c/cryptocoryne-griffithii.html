Cryptocoryne griffithii

Cryptocoryne griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with subtle silver veins. This plant thrives in soft, moderately warm water with low to moderate light conditions. It is popular among aquarium enthusiasts for its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.

Cryptocoryne griffithii is also known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters, making it a versatile addition to planted tanks.
