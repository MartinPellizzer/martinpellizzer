Callitriche bolusiana

Callitriche bolusiana, commonly known as the Bolus's water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds, lakes, and slow-moving streams. The plant has slender, branching stems that are often floating or partially submerged, with small, delicate leaves arranged in whorls. Callitriche bolusiana plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
