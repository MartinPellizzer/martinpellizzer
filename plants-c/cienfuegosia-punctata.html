Cienfuegosia punctata

Cienfuegosia punctata, commonly known as the Cuban beauty, is a small evergreen shrub native to Cuba.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species.
