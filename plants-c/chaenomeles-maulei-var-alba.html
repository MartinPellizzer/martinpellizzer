Chaenomeles maulei var. alba

Chaenomeles maulei var. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered Chinese quince, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant produces rough, spiny fruits that turn from yellow to red as they mature. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and a dark green upper surface.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
