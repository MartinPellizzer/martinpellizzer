Cylindrolobus uniflorus

Cylindrolobus uniflorus, commonly known as the single-flowered cylinder-lobed plant, is a rare and unique species native to specific regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical stems and leaves that are often lobed, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces a single, showy flower per inflorescence, typically in shades of yellow or green. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
