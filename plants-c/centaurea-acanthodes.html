Centaurea acanthodes

Centaurea acanthodes, commonly known as the prickly centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking blue to purple flowers. The plant features spiny leaves and stems, which contribute to its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Centaurea acanthodes is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
