Centaurea squarrosa var. brevispina

Centaurea squarrosa var. brevispina, commonly known as the narrow-spined centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems covered in fine, white to grayish hairs. The plant produces small, bright blue or purple flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly woolly texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
