Conomorpha ptariensis

Conomorpha ptariensis, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Conomorpha ptariensis is classified as an endangered species.
