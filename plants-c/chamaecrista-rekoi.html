Chamaecrista rekoi

Chamaecrista rekoi, commonly known as the African ticktree, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its seeds are enclosed in long, slender pods that turn brown as they mature.

Chamaecrista rekoi is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and floral display.
