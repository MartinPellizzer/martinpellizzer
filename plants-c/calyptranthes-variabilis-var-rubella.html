Calyptranthes variabilis var. rubella

Calyptranthes variabilis var. rubella, commonly known as the red-leaf calyptranthes, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, reddish-brown leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
