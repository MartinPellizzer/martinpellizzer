Calophyllum motleyi

Calophyllum motleyi, commonly known as Motley's calophyllum, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Calophyllaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces woody, orange-colored fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species.
