Croton erythrochyloides

Croton erythrochyloides, commonly known as the red-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored leaves that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a three-lobed capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Croton erythrochyloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
