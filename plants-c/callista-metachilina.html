Callista metachilina

Callista metachilina, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the rainy season. The tree has a broad, umbrella-like canopy and exudes a sweet fragrance. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Callista metachilina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and public spaces.
