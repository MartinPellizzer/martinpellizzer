Carissa yunnanensis

Carissa yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan carissa, is a flowering shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

Carissa yunnanensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
