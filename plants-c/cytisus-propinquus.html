Cytisus propinquus

Cytisus propinquus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems covered in small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has pinnately compound leaves composed of several small, narrow leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Cytisus propinquus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
