Crotalaria filipes var. trichophora

Crotalaria filipes var. trichophora, commonly known as the hairy spurred crotalaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

Its leaves are trifoliate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces yellow or orange flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators.
