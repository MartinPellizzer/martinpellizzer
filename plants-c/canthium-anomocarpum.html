Canthium anomocarpum

Canthium anomocarpum, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the spurge family.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.
