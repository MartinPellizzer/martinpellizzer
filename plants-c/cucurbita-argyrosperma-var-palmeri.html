Cucurbita argyrosperma var. palmeri

Cucurbita argyrosperma var. palmeri, commonly known as the Palmer's silverburr gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant produces large, pale green, lobed leaves and yellow flowers. Its fruits are typically elongated, with a rough, silver-gray surface and a hard, inedible rind.

It is primarily grown for its ornamental value rather than for consumption.
