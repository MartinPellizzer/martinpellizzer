Chrysopsis linearifolia subsp. dressii

Chrysopsis linearifolia subsp.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as rocky soils and dry slopes. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy, composite flower heads.
