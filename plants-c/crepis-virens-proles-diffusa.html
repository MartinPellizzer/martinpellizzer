Crepis virens proles diffusa

Crepis virens proles diffusa, commonly known as the slender mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, while the flower heads are small and yellowish-green in color.

It is often found in disturbed habitats and can spread through wind-dispersed seeds.
