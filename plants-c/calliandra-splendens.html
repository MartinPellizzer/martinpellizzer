Calliandra splendens

Calliandra splendens, commonly known as the splendour calliandra, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage. The plant produces striking, bright red or pink flower clusters that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Calliandra splendens is adapted to warm, sunny climates and prefers well-drained soil.
