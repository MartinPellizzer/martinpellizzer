Croton mavoravina var. gymnolepis

Croton mavoravina var. gymnolepis, commonly known as the Variegated Croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves.

The foliage features a combination of green, yellow, and red hues, creating a visually appealing appearance. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to several feet.

Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to thrive in warm, humid environments.
