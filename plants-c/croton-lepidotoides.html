Croton lepidotoides

Croton lepidotoides, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features showy, colorful bracts that range from yellow to red, which are often mistaken for flowers. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, greenish flowers followed by three-lobed seed capsules.

Croton lepidotoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
