Codiaeum brageanum

Codiaeum brageanum, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that can range from red, orange, and yellow to green, often changing with age. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Codiaeum brageanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
