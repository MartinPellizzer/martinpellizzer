Caladium bicolor f. robustum

Caladium bicolor f. robustum, commonly known as the giant caladium, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, displaying vibrant colors such as red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it ideal for shaded garden areas. It is a tropical perennial that is often grown as an annual in cooler climates.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for adding dramatic visual interest to gardens and landscapes.
