Cinnamomum × durifruticeticola

Cinnamomum × durifruticeticola, commonly known as the durifruit cinnamon, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lauraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of small, dark red berries. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its berries are used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to cinnamon.

However, it is less commonly cultivated compared to other cinnamon species due to its limited commercial use.
