Chamaecrista persoonii

Chamaecrista persoonii, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
