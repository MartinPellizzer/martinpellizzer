Cytisus purpureus

Cytisus purpureus, commonly known as the purple broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces bright yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, often thorned stems and small, leathery leaves. It is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Cytisus purpureus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
