Chamaecrista trichothyrsus

Chamaecrista trichothyrsus, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in racemes. The plant produces pods containing seeds that are rich in protein and used in various traditional and industrial applications. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and economically valuable.
