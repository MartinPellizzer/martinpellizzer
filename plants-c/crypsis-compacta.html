Crypsis compacta

Crypsis compacta, commonly known as the dense crypt plant, is a compact, low-growing species native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked due to their muted colors. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access moisture deep within the soil.

Overall, Crypsis compacta exhibits adaptations typical of xerophytes, making it well-suited for survival in dry climates.
