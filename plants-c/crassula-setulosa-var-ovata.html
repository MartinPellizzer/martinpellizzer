Crassula setulosa var. ovata

Crassula setulosa var. ovata, commonly known as the Fat Plant or Jade Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically rounded and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months.

Its compact growth habit and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
