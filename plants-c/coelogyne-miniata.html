Coelogyne miniata

Coelogyne miniata, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red lips on the petals and a compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a contrasting red lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Coelogyne miniata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
