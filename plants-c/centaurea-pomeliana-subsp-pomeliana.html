Centaurea pomeliana subsp. pomeliana

Centaurea pomeliana subsp.

pomeliana, commonly known as the Pomeliana thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its spiny, rough leaves and bright yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
