Cymbopogon pruinosus

Cymbopogon pruinosus, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, silver-gray blades that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of small, pale flowers.

Cymbopogon pruinosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
