Corydalis Solida Var. Lineariloba

Corydalis lineariloba, scientifically known as Corydalis solida var. lineariloba, is a striking member of the Fumariaceae family native to the mountainous regions of China. This delicate flowering plant is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of delicate, lilac-pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas at high elevations. Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly valued by botanists and horticulturists alike. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered species from habitat loss and over-collection.
