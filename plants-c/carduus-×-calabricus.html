Carduus × calabricus

Carduus × calabricus, commonly known as the common thistle.

It is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. It has a rosette growth habit and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Its botanical features make it both a weed and a plant of interest in ecological studies.
