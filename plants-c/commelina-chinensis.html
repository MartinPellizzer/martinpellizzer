Commelina chinensis

Commelina chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Commelinales order and the Commelinaceae family. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, blue-violet flowers that attract pollinators. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Commelina chinensis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
