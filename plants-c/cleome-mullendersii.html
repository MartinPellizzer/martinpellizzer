Cleome mullendersii

Cleome mullendersii, commonly known as the Mexican spider flower, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a vibrant, low-maintenance flowering plant.
