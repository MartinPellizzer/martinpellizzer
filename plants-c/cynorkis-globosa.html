Cynorkis globosa

Cynorkis globosa, commonly known as the globular orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and dense clusters of small, pale green to white flowers. The plant features fibrous roots and fleshy, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Cynorkis globosa is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its unique appearance and ecological significance.
