Coleosanthus melissifolius

Coleosanthus melissifolius, commonly known as the honeyleaf plant, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of small, white flowers. The leaves are typically ovate with a smooth texture and emit a mild, sweet scent. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
