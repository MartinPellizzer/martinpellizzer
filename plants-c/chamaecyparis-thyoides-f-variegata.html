Chamaecyparis thyoides f. variegata

Chamaecyparis thyoides f. variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Hinoki cypress, is a cultivar of the Hinoki cypress, native to Japan.

It is characterized by its narrow, feather-like foliage that displays a striking combination of bright yellow and dark green. The plant has a compact, conical shape and is often used as an ornamental evergreen in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, requiring minimal maintenance.

Its slow growth rate makes it suitable for small spaces and topiary designs.
