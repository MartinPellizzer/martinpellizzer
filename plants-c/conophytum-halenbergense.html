Conophytum halenbergense

Conophytum halenbergense, commonly known as the Halenberg carpet plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer.

Due to its slow growth and specific habitat requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
