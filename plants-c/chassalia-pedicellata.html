Chassalia pedicellata

Chassalia pedicellata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. Chassalia pedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
