Cleistes acuminata

Cleistes acuminata, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Oenotheraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida. The plant is notable for its unique, lantern-like flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. It produces showy, fragrant blooms that open in the evening and close by midday.

Cleistes acuminata thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
