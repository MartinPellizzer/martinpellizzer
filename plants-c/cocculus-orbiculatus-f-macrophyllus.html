Cocculus orbiculatus f. macrophyllus

Cocculus orbiculatus f. macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf morning glory, is a variant of the morning glory family (Convolvulaceae).

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in humid, warm climates. It climbs using its tendrils and can reach significant heights, making it a vigorous vine.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.
