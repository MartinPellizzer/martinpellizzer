Croton distans

Croton distans, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Croton distans is typically grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
