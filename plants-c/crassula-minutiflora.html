Crassula minutiflora

Crassula minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf crassula or miniature flower crassula, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its slow growth and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
