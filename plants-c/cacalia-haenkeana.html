Cacalia haenkeana

Cacalia haenkeana, commonly known as the Chilean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Cacalia haenkeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, bell-shaped blooms.
