Croton glandulosus var. glabratus

Croton glandulosus var. glabratus, commonly known as the smooth croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its vibrant foliage.
