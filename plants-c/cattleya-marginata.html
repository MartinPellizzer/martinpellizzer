Cattleya marginata

Cattleya marginata, commonly known as the marginated orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Cattleya marginata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
