Cyclaminus ibericus

Cyclaminus ibericus, commonly known as the Iberian cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that come in shades of pink, white, and purple. Cyclaminus ibericus is known for its ability to bloom throughout the winter and early spring.
