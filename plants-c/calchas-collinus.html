Calchas collinus

Calchas collinus, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features striking silver-white leaves that reflect sunlight, reducing water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well adapted to semi-desert conditions.

Calchas collinus is often used in xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
