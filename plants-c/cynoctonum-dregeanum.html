Cynoctonum dregeanum

Cynoctonum dregeanum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is typically shrub-like, with a woody stem and can grow up to several feet in height. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cynoctonum dregeanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
