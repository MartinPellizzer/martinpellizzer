Coreopsis oemleri

Coreopsis oemleri, commonly known as the narrowleaf coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. Coreopsis oemleri is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
