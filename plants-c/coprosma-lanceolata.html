Coprosma lanceolata

Coprosma lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved coprosma, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland forest areas. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its hardiness and ability to thrive in various conditions.
