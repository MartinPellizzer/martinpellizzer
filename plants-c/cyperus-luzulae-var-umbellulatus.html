Cyperus luzulae var. umbellulatus

Cyperus luzulae var. umbellulatus, commonly known as the umbrella sedge, is a perennial plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, upright growth and distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescence that gives it its common name. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.
