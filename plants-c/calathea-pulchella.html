Calathea pulchella

Calathea pulchella, commonly known as the pink velvet plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns. The foliage often displays a combination of pink, white, and green hues, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Calathea pulchella is known for its ability to purify the air and add a touch of elegance to any indoor space.
