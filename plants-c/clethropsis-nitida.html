Clethropsis nitida

Clethropsis nitida, commonly known as the silky clethra, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Clethraceae and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Clethropsis nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
