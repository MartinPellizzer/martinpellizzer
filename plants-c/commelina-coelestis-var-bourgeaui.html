Commelina coelestis var. bourgeaui

Commelina coelestis var. bourgeaui, commonly known as the sky blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its flowers are bilaterally symmetrical, with a distinctive spurred petal that aids in pollination by attracting specific insect pollinators.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in humid climates.
