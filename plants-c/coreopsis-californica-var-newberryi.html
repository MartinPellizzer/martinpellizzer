Coreopsis californica var. newberryi

Coreopsis californica var. newberryi, commonly known as Newberry's coreopsis, is a perennial herb native to California.

It features bright yellow, daisy-like flowers with a central disk and ray florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped, gray-green leaves that are aromatic when crushed.

It is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.
