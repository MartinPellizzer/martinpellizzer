Camellia minahassae

Camellia minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa camellia, is a species native to Indonesia.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its floral display. Camellia minahassae thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
