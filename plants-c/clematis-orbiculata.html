Clematis orbiculata

Clematis orbiculata, commonly known as the globe clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis orbiculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
