Cirsium isophyllum

Cirsium isophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust root system and erect stems that are typically covered in dense, spiny hairs. The plant produces purple to pink flowers in composite flower heads, which are composed of numerous small florets. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in a thick layer of fine hairs, providing a distinctive appearance.

Cirsium isophyllum is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
