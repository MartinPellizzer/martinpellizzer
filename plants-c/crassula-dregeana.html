Crassula dregeana

Crassula dregeana, commonly known as the "Snowball Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, rounded clusters of fleshy, silver-green leaves that give it a frost-like appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Crassula dregeana is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and unique aesthetic.
