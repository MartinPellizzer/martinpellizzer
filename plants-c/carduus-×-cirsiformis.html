Carduus × cirsiformis

Carduus × cirsiformis, commonly known as the common sowthistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its yellow flowers that bloom in a cluster. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in various regions across Europe and parts of Asia. It is an annual or biennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.

Its botanical features make it identifiable in both cultivated and wild settings.
