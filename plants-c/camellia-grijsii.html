Camellia grijsii

Camellia grijsii, commonly known as the Gray camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy, camellia-like flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is evergreen and thrives in acidic, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.

Camellia grijsii is valued for its winter blooming habit and ability to add color to the garden during colder months.
