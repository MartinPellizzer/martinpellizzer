Cacalia mespilifolia

Cacalia mespilifolia, commonly known as the mespilifolia bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.
