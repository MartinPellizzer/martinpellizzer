Croton crocodilorum var. meridionalis

Croton crocodilorum var. meridionalis, commonly known as the Southern Croton, is a shrub native to parts of southern Africa.

It features striking, palmate leaves with a vibrant green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in catkins. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage, which adds visual interest to gardens and landscapes.
