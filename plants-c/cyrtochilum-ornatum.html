Cyrtochilum ornatum

Cyrtochilum ornatum, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in photosynthesis.

Cyrtochilum ornatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
