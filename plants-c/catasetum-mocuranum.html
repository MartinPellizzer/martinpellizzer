Catasetum mocuranum

Catasetum mocuranum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and erect, branched inflorescences. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and wasps.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
