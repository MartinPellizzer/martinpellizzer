Calanthe Decora

Calanthe decora is a striking member of the orchid family known for its elegant, showy blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in cool, humid climates with partial shade. Its flowers, often in shades of white, pink, and purple, bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features rhizomatous growth and lance-shaped leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal. Due to its beauty and rarity, Calanthe decora is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
