Crotalaria cylindrostachys

Crotalaria cylindrostachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces distinctive yellow flowers clustered in racemes. Its seed pods are cylindrical and twisted, giving it the specific epithet "cylindrostachys.".

The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage structure.
