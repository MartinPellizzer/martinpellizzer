Campanula rotundifolia var. calisii

Campanula rotundifolia var. calisii, commonly known as the round-leaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet or blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has rounded, hairy leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and meadows.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
