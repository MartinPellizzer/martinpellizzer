Cyrtanthera pohliana var. velutina

Cyrtanthera pohliana var. velutina, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
