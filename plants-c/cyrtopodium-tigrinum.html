Cyrtopodium tigrinum

Cyrtopodium tigrinum, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous flowers that have intricate, tiger-like patterns on their petals and sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Cyrtopodium tigrinum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in both greenhouse and garden settings.
