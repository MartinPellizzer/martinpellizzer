Callitriche verna var. fontana

Callitriche verna var. fontana, commonly known as spring water-star, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic flowering plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in shallow water or damp soil, often found in ponds, ditches, and wet meadows. The plant has tiny, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring, usually from April to June. Its leaves are opposite, floating or submerged, and have a delicate, waxy texture.

It is an important species for aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and amphibians.
