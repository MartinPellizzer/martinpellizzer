Cleome austroarabica subsp. muscatensis

Cleome austroarabica subsp.

muscatensis, commonly known as the muscatense cleome, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in warm climates.

The leaves are simple and alternate, often with a slightly hairy texture.
