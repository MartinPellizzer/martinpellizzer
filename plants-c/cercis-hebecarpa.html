Cercis hebecarpa

Cercis hebecarpa, commonly known as the Chinese redbud, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, elongated legumes that mature into a distinctive, woody fruit. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Cercis hebecarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in gardens.
