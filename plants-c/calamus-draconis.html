Calamus draconis

Calamus draconis, commonly known as dragon's reed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in wet, swampy environments. Its flowering spikes produce small, fragrant flowers that attract various pollinators.
