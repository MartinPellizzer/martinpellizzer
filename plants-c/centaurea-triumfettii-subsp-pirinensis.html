Centaurea triumfettii subsp. pirinensis

Centaurea triumfettii subsp.

pirinensis, commonly known as the Pirin centaury, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It features slender, hairy stems that grow up to 30 cm in height, often with a reddish tinge.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers with purple bracts, which bloom from June to August. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapted to the harsh alpine environment.

It is typically found in rocky soils and is a characteristic species of the subalpine vegetation in the Pirin Mountains.
