Chlorophytum blepharophyllum subsp. blepharophyllum

Chlorophytum blepharophyllum subsp.

blepharophyllum, commonly known as the silver edge snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family. It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often marked with a distinctive silver or white stripe along the edges.

The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in well-draining soil with moderate light exposure. It is a low-maintenance plant that is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties and ability to tolerate dry conditions.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and resilient choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
