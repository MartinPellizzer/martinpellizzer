Cotyledon macrantha var. virescens

Cotyledon macrantha var. virescens, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge, adapted for storing water. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
