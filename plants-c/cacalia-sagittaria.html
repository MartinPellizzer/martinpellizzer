Cacalia sagittaria

Cacalia sagittaria, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils along stream banks and wetlands. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for wetland restoration and ornamental gardening.
