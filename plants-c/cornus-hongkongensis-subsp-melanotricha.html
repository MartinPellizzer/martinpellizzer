Cornus hongkongensis subsp. melanotricha

Cornus hongkongensis subsp.

melanotricha, commonly known as Hong Kong cornel. It is a shrub native to southern China and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy white flowers.

The species belongs to the Cornaceae family and is notable for its small, persistent fruits that turn red in autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
