Coelogyne brunnea

Coelogyne brunnea, commonly known as the black orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, petals and sepals, which give it its striking appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. It produces fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring.

Coelogyne brunnea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
