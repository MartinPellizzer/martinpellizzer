Conophytum peersii var. multipunctatum

Conophytum peersii var. multipunctatum, commonly known as the spotted conophytum, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with distinct white or yellow spots. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its striking appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
