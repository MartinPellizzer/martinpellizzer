Cotoneaster rotundifolius var. tongolensis

Cotoneaster rotundifolius var. tongolensis, commonly known as the Tongolensis cotoneaster, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It features small, elliptical to ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
