Ceratandropsis grandiflora

Ceratandropsis grandiflora, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in tropical regions.
