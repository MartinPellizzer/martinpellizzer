Campanula vandesii

Campanula vandesii, commonly known as the Van der Vegt bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula vandesii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
