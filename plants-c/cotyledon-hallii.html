Cotyledon hallii

Cotyledon hallii, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white or silver hair, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is known for its ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant.

Cotyledon hallii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
