Croton alnifolius

Croton alnifolius, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage.
