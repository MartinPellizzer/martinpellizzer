Cadetia lucida

Cadetia lucida, commonly known as the shining bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Cadetia lucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
