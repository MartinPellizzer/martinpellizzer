Cotyledon subrigida

Cotyledon subrigida, commonly known as the wrinkled cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The leaves are typically green with red or purple markings, especially when exposed to bright light. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.
