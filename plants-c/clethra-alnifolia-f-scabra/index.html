Clethra Alnifolia F. Scabra

Clethra alnifolia f. scabra is a unique variant of the sweet pepper tree known for its distinctive, rough-textured leaves. This plant belongs to the Ericaceae family and is native to eastern North America. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland areas. The shrub produces fragrant white flowers in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
