Cyathea usambarensis

Cyathea usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambarensis tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome that supports its vertical growth, and its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

The plant plays a significant role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
