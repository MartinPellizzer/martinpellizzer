Carduus × javorkae

Carduus × javorkae, commonly known as the hybrid cardoon, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Carduus nutans and Carduus acanthoides, characterized by its robust growth and large, spiny leaves. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
