Carludovica mattogrossensis

Carludovica mattogrossensis, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and it is adapted to thrive in the humid, tropical conditions of its native habitat.
