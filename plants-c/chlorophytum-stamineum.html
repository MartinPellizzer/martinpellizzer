Chlorophytum stamineum

Chlorophytum stamineum, commonly known as the tiger tooth plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, grass-like leaves with distinctive white or yellow stripes that run vertically along the blade. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in a spike-like inflorescence during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
