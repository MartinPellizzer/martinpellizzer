Campanula hornungiana

Campanula hornungiana, commonly known as the Hornung's bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula hornungiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
