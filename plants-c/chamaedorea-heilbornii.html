Chamaedorea heilbornii

Chamaedorea heilbornii, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a compact, upright plant native to the tropical regions of Mexico.

It features glossy, dark green, feather-like leaves that give it a lush, tropical appearance. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and air-purifying qualities.

The plant's slow growth rate makes it ideal for small spaces and container gardening.
