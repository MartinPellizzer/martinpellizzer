Cola rostrata

Cola rostrata, commonly known as the rostrated cola, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of bright red, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
