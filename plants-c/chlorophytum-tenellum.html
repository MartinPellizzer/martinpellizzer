Chlorophytum tenellum

Chlorophytum tenellum, commonly known as spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often have a white stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, followed by delicate white or greenish-white berries. It is a hardy, easy-to-care-for plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Chlorophytum tenellum is also known for its ability to purify indoor air and propagate readily through offsets.
