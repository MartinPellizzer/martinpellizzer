Cucubalus secundus

Cucubalus secundus, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Cucurbitaceae, which includes cucumbers and gourds. The plant produces small, pale green fruits that mature into orange-red berries, often containing a single seed. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Cucubalus secundus is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
