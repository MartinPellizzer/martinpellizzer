Croton ustulatus

Croton ustulatus, commonly known as the flame tree or fire tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange-red, often resembling flames. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are typically alternate and pinnately compound, with serrated edges.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a street tree in tropical regions.
