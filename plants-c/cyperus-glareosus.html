Cyperus glareosus

Cyperus glareosus, commonly known as the golden flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and swampy soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often yellow or golden in color.

Cyperus glareosus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various water conditions.
