Cimicifuga japonica f. peltata

Cimicifuga japonica f. peltata, commonly known as Japanese bugbane, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features striking, large, white, lacecap flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to gardens and ecological landscapes.
