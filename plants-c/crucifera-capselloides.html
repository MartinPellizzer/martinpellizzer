Crucifera capselloides

Crucifera capselloides, commonly known as the narrowleaf mustard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has linear, narrow leaves and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It produces small, round pods that contain several seeds.

This species is known for its ecological importance as a food source for various pollinators and insects.
