Cyrtomium mediocre

Cyrtomium mediocre, commonly known as the poor fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen fern characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features dark green, leathery fronds that are typically divided into narrow, serrated segments. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cyrtomium mediocre is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in cultivated settings.
