Cyrtanthera liboniana

Cyrtanthera liboniana, commonly known as the Philippine violet.

It is a flowering plant native to the Philippines, particularly found in the lowland forests of Luzon and Mindanao. The plant features showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are typically ovate and have a smooth texture, while its stems are slender and often covered in fine hairs.

Cyrtanthera liboniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
