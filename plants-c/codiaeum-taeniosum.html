Codiaeum taeniosum

Codiaeum taeniosum, commonly known as the croton plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that display a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its striking foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Codiaeum taeniosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.
