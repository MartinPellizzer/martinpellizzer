Cleretum flos-solis

Cleretum flos-solis, commonly known as the sunflower of the morning, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and herbaceous growth habit. Cleretum flos-solis thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.
