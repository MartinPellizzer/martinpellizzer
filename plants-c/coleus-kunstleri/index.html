Coleus Kunstleri

Coleus kunstleri is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, colorful leaves. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. This ornamental plant is popular among gardeners for its ability to add a splash of color to any indoor or outdoor space. Its foliage comes in shades of red, purple, pink, and green, making it a visually appealing choice. With proper care, Coleus kunstleri can flourish and provide long-lasting beauty throughout the growing season.
