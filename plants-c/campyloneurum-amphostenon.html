Campyloneurum amphostenon

Campyloneurum amphostenon, commonly known as the broad-leafed fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Asia.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
