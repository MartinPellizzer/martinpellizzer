Cyste

Cyste, commonly known as the common cyst, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cysteaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of tiny, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments, such as along stream banks or in forest understories. Its reproductive structures are adapted for wind pollination, which is a common trait among plants in its family.

Cyste is often found in temperate regions and is relatively rare in cultivation.
