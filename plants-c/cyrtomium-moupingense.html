Cyrtomium moupingense

Cyrtomium moupingense, commonly known as the Mouping shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense fronds that form a rosette-like structure. The plant features bipinnate leaves with a distinctive shield-shaped appearance, giving it its common name. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cyrtomium moupingense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
