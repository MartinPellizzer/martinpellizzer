Centaurea praealta

Centaurea praealta, commonly known as the tall centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands. Centaurea praealta is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy, composite flower heads.
