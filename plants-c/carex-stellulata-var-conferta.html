Carex stellulata var. conferta

Carex stellulata var. conferta, commonly known as the dense star sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. This variety is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of pale green to brownish flowers, typically appearing in late summer.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream edges.
