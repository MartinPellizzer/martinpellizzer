Celastrus paniculatus var. balansae

Celastrus paniculatus var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae bindweed, is a climbing vine native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, simple, and usually ovate or elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Celastraceae family.
