Crotalaria kurisumalayana

Crotalaria kurisumalayana, commonly known as the Kurisumalayana Crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Western Ghats of India and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It has been observed to have medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

However, it is also considered a potential weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
