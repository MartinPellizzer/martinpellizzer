Casuarina microstachya

Casuarina microstachya, commonly known as the small-stamined casuarina, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, needle-like foliage that resembles the structure of a pine tree. The plant produces small, cylindrical flower spikes that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in coastal and sandy soils, often found in dune environments.

Casuarina microstachya is known for its adaptability to saline and arid conditions, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
