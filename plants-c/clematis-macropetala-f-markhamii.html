Clematis macropetala f. markhamii

Clematis macropetala f. markhamii, commonly known as the Markham's large-flowered clematis, is a hybrid variety native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features compound, trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture, and it thrives in partial shade to full sun. It is a vigorous climber that requires a sturdy support structure to grow.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and green spaces.
