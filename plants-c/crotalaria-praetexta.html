Crotalaria praetexta

Crotalaria praetexta, commonly known as the "Cape flower" or "Cape jasmine," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Crotalaria praetexta is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
