Conyza floribunda var. subleiotheca

Conyza floribunda var. subleiotheca, commonly known as the narrow-leaved fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, while its yellow flowers bloom in clusters.

It is an annual herb that produces small seeds dispersed by the wind.
