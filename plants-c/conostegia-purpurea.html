Conostegia purpurea

Conostegia purpurea, commonly known as the purple conostegia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, pinkish-purple flowers bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Conostegia purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
