Chirita lienxienensis

Chirita lienxienensis, commonly known as the Lienxiensis begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that often resemble petals. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, featuring heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.
