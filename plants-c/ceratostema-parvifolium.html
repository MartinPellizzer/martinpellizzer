Ceratostema parvifolium

Ceratostema parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaved horned bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to violet in color, with a distinctive horn-like structure at the base of the calyx. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for woodland gardens.

Ceratostema parvifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
