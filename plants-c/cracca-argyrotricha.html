Cracca argyrotricha

Cracca argyrotricha, commonly known as silverleaf cracca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, which give it its common name. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Cracca argyrotricha is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
