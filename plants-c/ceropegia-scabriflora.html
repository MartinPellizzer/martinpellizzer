Ceropegia scabriflora

Ceropegia scabriflora, commonly known as the rough-leaved ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a fine, rough texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Ceropegia scabriflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
