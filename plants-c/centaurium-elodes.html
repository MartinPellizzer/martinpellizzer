Centaurium elodes

Centaurium elodes, commonly known as lesser centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a small herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant features slender stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, tubular, and clustered in dense spikes, usually tinged with red or purple hues.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
