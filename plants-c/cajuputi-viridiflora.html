Cajuputi viridiflora

Cajuputi viridiflora, commonly known as green-flowered cajuputi, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Cajuputi viridiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
