Cadetia wariana

Cadetia wariana, commonly known as the Warian rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically basal, forming a rosette, and are covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Cadetia wariana is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy species found in alpine and subalpine zones.
