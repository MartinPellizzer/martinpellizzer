Caiophora × herbertii

Caiophora × herbertii, commonly known as the Herbert's caiophora, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are typically green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are often surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
