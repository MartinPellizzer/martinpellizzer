Crupina morisii

Crupina morisii, commonly known as Moris's crupina, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and sandy environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Crupina morisii is adapted to saline and well-drained soils, making it a hardy species in coastal areas.

It is often found in dunes and other disturbed habitats where it plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils.
