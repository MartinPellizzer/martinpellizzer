Celosia loandensis

Celosia loandensis, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spiky inflorescences that resemble flames. The plant features upright, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually green or reddish in color. Celosia loandensis thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden displays.

Its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts.
