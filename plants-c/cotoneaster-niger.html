Cotoneaster niger

Cotoneaster niger, commonly known as black cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, dark purple to black berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are typically oval to elliptical in shape. It produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by its distinctive berries.

Cotoneaster niger is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
