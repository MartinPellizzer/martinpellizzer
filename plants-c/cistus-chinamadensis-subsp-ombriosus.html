Cistus chinamadensis subsp. ombriosus

Cistus chinamadensis subsp.

ombriosus, commonly known as the misty rock rose, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white.

The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. It is often found in alpine meadows and forest margins, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
