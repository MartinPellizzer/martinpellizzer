Crotalaria cylindrica

Crotalaria cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical seed pods. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with compound leaves and yellow flowers. Its seeds are encased in long, slender pods that mature to a reddish-brown color.

Crotalaria cylindrica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
