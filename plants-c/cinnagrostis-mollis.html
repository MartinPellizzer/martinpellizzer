Cinnagrostis mollis

Cinnagrostis mollis, commonly known as the slender false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It features upright, branching stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.
