Cymbidium albomarginatum

Cymbidium albomarginatum, commonly known as the white-margin orchid, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs, each bearing a single leaf. The flowers are typically white with yellow or greenish margins, and they bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant prefers cool, humid conditions and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
