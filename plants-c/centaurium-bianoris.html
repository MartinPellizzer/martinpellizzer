Centaurium bianoris

Centaurium bianoris, commonly known as the white centaury, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Gentianaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in moist meadows and woodland edges. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale blue, with five petals and a prominent yellow throat. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Centaurium bianoris is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
