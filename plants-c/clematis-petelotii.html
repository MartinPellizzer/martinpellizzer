Clematis petelotii

Clematis petelotii, commonly known as the white clematis, is a flowering vine native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are typically hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Clematis petelotii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
