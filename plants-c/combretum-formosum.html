Combretum formosum

Combretum formosum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape and have a rough texture. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Combretum formosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
