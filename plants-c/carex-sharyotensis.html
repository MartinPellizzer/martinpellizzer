Carex sharyotensis

Carex sharyotensis, commonly known as Sharyot's sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the temperate regions of Asia, particularly found in parts of China and the Russian Far East. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often in woodland or marshy areas. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are characteristic of the sedge family.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and small fauna.
