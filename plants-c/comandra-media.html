Comandra media

Comandra media, commonly known as the silverleaf comandra, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features linear, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to drought conditions and harsh climates.
