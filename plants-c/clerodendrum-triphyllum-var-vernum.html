Clerodendrum triphyllum var. vernum

Clerodendrum triphyllum var. vernum, commonly known as the spring-leaf clethra, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are opposite and have three leaflets. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate regions.
