Calceolaria plantaginea var. magellanica

Calceolaria plantaginea var. magellanica, commonly known as the Magellan's slipper orchid, is a species native to the southern regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically borne on erect, hairy stems. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
