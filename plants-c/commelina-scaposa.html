Commelina scaposa

Commelina scaposa, commonly known as the scarlet false arum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. Commelina scaposa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
