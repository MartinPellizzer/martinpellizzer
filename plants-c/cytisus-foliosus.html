Cytisus foliosus

Cytisus foliosus, commonly known as the leafy broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring slender, often thorny stems. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its flowers are yellow, borne in racemes, and appear in early summer.

The plant is adapted to poor, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
