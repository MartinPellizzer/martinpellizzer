Coelogyne rigidifolia

Coelogyne rigidifolia, commonly known as the rigid-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves that are often rigid and dark green. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the dry season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne rigidifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
