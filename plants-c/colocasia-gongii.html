Colocasia gongii

Colocasia gongii, commonly known as the Gong's arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, with a deep green color and a smooth, waxy texture. The plant produces striking purple to red spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value and unique foliage make it a sought-after addition to botanical collections and tropical gardens.
