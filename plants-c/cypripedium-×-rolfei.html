Cypripedium × rolfei

Cypripedium × rolfei, commonly known as the Chinese lily or the yellow lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a yellow pouch-like lip and purple or brown petals. The plant is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous root system and grows in shaded, moist forest environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it produces a single, large flower per stem.

Cypripedium × rolfei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
