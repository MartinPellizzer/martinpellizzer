Cotyledon schuldtiana

Cotyledon schuldtiana, commonly known as the Schuldt's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Cotyledon schuldtiana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
