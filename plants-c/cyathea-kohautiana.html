Cyathea kohautiana

Cyathea kohautiana, commonly known as the Kohaut's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous, spongy layer. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. Cyathea kohautiana is a slow-growing species that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
