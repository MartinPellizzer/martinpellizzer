Cuculina

Cuculina, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a sacred plant in various cultural and religious traditions.

It belongs to the Nymphaeaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on water surfaces, often in serene ponds or lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, while its roots are submerged, absorbing nutrients from the water.

The botanical significance of Cuculina lies in its symbolic and ecological roles across different regions.
