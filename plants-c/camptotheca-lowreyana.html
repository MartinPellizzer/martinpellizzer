Camptotheca lowreyana

Camptotheca lowreyana, commonly known as Lowrey's smoke tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, smoky-pink flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically red or black when mature. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

Camptotheca lowreyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun conditions.
