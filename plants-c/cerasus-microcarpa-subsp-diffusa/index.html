Cerasus Microcarpa Subsp. Diffusa

Cerasus microcarpa subsp. diffusa is a lesser-known member of the Prunus family known for its unique characteristics. This subspecies is often found in specific geographic regions with particular climate conditions. It features small, delicate flowers and is valued for its ornamental qualities. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated compared to more common cherry varieties. Understanding its biology and habitat can enhance its cultivation and conservation efforts.
