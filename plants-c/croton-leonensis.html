Croton leonensis

Croton leonensis, commonly known as the African croton, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red, yellow, or orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its adaptability, it can be found in various habitats, including forests and disturbed soils.
