Calliandra purpurea var. dussiana

Calliandra purpurea var. dussiana, commonly known as the pink calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red bracts that surround small, yellow flowers, creating a visually appealing display. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening in warm climates.

Due to its showy appearance, it is often cultivated for landscaping and as a cut flower.
