Cytisus wulfii

Cytisus wulfii, commonly known as the Wulf's broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its slender stems and evergreen foliage make it a distinctive feature in its natural habitat.

Cytisus wulfii is adapted to grow in rocky and coastal environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
