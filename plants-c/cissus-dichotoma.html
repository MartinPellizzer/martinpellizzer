Cissus dichotoma

Cissus dichotoma, commonly known as the African snowball vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in winter. Its stems are succulent and can be used for propagation.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate watering, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
