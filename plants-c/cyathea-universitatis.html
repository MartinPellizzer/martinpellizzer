Cyathea universitatis

Cyathea universitatis, commonly known as the University fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant.

The fronds are evergreen and can live for several years, contributing to the plant's aesthetic appeal.
