Callicarpa japonica var. dichotoma

Callicarpa japonica var. dichotoma, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright purple berries that appear in late summer to early autumn. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The berries are not only visually striking but also attract birds and other wildlife.
