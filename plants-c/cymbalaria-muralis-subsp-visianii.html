Cymbalaria muralis subsp. visianii

Cymbalaria muralis subsp.

visianii, commonly known as the wall geometer, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is a low-growing herb with a sprawling habit, often found growing on walls, rocks, and disturbed soils.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, which helps it retain moisture in dry environments.

It is a perennial species that thrives in sunny to partially shaded conditions.
