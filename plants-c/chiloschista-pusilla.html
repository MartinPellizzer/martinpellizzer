Chiloschista pusilla

Chiloschista pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf chiloschista, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salt spray and harsh weather conditions.
