Cousinia robusta

Cousinia robusta, commonly known as the robust cinquefoil, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, which bloom in summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Cousinia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
