Camellia brevistaminata

Camellia brevistaminata, commonly known as the short-stamen camellia, is a species native to southern China.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces striking, cup-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. Camellia brevistaminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter blooming habit.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and attractive addition to horticultural collections.
