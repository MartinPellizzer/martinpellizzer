Cordiglottis longipedicellata

Cordiglottis longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-armed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and large, showy flowers with elongated petals. Its roots are fibrous and often grow in dense clusters.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
