Clitoria phyrne

Clitoria phyrne, commonly known as the purple winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple-pink flowers, which are bilaterally symmetrical and have a distinctive wing-like structure. The plant produces small, elliptical pods that contain several edible seeds, often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and slightly hairy.

Clitoria phyrne thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
