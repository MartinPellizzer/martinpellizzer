Cynanchum latifolium

Cynanchum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia and North America.

It features a robust root system and produces opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture. The plant blooms with small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in racemes, and its fruits are follicles that release seeds when mature. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
