Crambe schugnana

Crambe schugnana, commonly known as the yellow coltsfoot, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a biennial plant that produces yellow flowers and has hairy, lobed leaves. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist, disturbed soils. Its seeds are small and can be dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread.

The plant has been historically used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
