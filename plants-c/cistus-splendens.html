Cistus splendens

Cistus splendens, commonly known as the shining rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited to arid environments. Its flowers bloom in spring and are visited by various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Cistus splendens is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
