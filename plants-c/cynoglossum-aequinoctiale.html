Cynoglossum aequinoctiale

Cynoglossum aequinoctiale, commonly known as the evening snow or nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and a strong, somewhat unpleasant odor. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum aequinoctiale is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
