Cyclopia vogelii var. falcata

Cyclopia vogelii var. falcata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cyclopia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, falcate (sickle-shaped) leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It is often found growing in rocky, well-drained soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

Cyclopia vogelii var. falcata is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
