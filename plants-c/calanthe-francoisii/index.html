Calanthe Francoisii

Calanthe francoisii is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its elegant, spurred flowers and lush green foliage, this plant is a botanical gem. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered endangered. This article explores its unique characteristics, ecological significance, and conservation efforts.
