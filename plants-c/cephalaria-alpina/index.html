Cephalaria Alpina

Cephalaria alpina is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, delicate white flowers that bloom in early summer. This alpine species thrives in high-altitude environments where few plants can survive. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Cephalaria alpina faces increasing threats to its survival.
