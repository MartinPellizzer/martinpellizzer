Cynanchum viride

Cynanchum viride, commonly known as green milkweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds known for their milky sap. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its stems are often hairy and can be somewhat woody at maturity.

Cynanchum viride is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
