Citrus × poonensis

Citrus × poonensis, commonly known as the kaffir lime.

This hybrid citrus plant is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its aromatic, lime-green leaves and small, yellow-orange fruits. The leaves are wrinkled and have a distinctive citrus scent, making them popular in culinary applications. The fruit is typically used for its zest and juice, adding a strong, sharp flavor to dishes.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
