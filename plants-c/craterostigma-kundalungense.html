Craterostigma kundalungense

It belongs to the Poaceae family, commonly known as grasses, and is characterized by its compact rosette structure and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is notable for its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions, including cold temperatures and poor soil quality. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil in its alpine habitat.
