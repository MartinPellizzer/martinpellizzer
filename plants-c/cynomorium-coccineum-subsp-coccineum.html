Cynomorium coccineum subsp. coccineum

Cynomorium coccineum subsp.

coccineum, commonly known as the red hemiparasite, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially relies on other plants for water and nutrients through its root system.

The plant produces showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sun-exposed areas.

Its unique ecological role and striking appearance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
