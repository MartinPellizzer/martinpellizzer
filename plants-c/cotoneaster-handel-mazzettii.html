Cotoneaster handel-mazzettii

Cotoneaster handel-mazzettii, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries in autumn. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster handel-mazzettii is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
