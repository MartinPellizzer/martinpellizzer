Cyclamen tauricum

Cyclamen tauricum, commonly known as the Caucasian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers with distinctive spurred petals. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Cyclamen tauricum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.
