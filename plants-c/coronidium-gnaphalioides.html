Coronidium gnaphalioides

Coronidium gnaphalioides, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage and produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Coronidium gnaphalioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its drought tolerance and long-lasting flowers.
