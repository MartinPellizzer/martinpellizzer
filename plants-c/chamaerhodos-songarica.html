Chamaerhodos songarica

Chamaerhodos songarica, commonly known as the Songarica flameflower, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and drought tolerance.
