Centranthus macrosiphon

Centranthus macrosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved redflower.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular red or pink flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in native habitats.
