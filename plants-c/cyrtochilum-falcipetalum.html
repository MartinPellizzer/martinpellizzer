Cyrtochilum falcipetalum

Cyrtochilum falcipetalum, commonly known as the sickle-petaled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, sickle-shaped petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and has a short, creeping rhizome. Its flowers are small and often have a distinct, intricate structure.

Cyrtochilum falcipetalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
