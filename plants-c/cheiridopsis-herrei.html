Cheiridopsis herrei

Cheiridopsis herrei, commonly known as the silverleaf ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid environments. The plant produces small, white flowers during the winter months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
