Ceropegia nilotica var. simplex

Ceropegia nilotica var. simplex, commonly known as the dwarf string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which are pollinated by specific moths. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and container planting.
