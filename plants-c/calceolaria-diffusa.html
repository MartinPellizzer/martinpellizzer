Calceolaria diffusa

Calceolaria diffusa, commonly known as the common slipper orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features striking, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Calceolaria diffusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
