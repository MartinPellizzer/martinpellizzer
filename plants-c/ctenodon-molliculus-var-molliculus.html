Ctenodon molliculus var. molliculus

Ctenodon molliculus var. molliculus, commonly known as the velvet leaf ctenodon, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and compact growth habit.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.
