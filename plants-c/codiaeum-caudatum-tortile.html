Codiaeum caudatum-tortile

Codiaeum caudatum-tortile, commonly known as the curly leaf croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with deep green, yellow, and red hues. The leaves are typically elongated and have a distinctive curling or twisting pattern, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its coloration.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental species due to its eye-catching appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
