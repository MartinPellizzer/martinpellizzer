Chaetostoma petronianum

Chaetostoma petronianum, commonly known as the Petronian waterwheel, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of leaves, which give it a wheel-like appearance. The plant typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. Its leaves are narrow and spiral around the stem, often with a bluish-green coloration.

It is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
