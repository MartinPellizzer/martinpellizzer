Chlorophytum breviscapum

Chlorophytum breviscapum, commonly known as the tiger tooth plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are often striped with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
