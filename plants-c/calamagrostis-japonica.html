Calamagrostis japonica

Calamagrostis japonica, commonly known as Japanese feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It features slender, silver-gray leaves that are often described as having a feathery appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, pale green to golden flowers in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Calamagrostis japonica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
