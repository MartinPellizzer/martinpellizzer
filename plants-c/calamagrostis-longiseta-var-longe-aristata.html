Calamagrostis longiseta var. longe-aristata

Calamagrostis longiseta var. longe-aristata, commonly known as the tall feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 6 feet, with long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces feathery, plume-like inflorescences in late summer to early fall, which add ornamental value to gardens and natural landscapes. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
