Cotyledon mucizonia subsp. ortegae

Cotyledon mucizonia subsp.

ortegae, commonly known as the Ortega's thickleaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
