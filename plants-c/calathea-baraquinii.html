Calathea baraquinii

Calathea baraquinii, commonly known as the Shiny Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of green, silver, and purple. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often featuring a waxy texture that enhances their visual appeal. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This plant requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.
