Calandrinia spectabilis

Calandrinia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy purslane, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is an annual herb that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has fleshy, succulent-like leaves and stems, which help it retain moisture in arid environments. Its flowers typically bloom in the summer months and are pollinated by insects.

Calandrinia spectabilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of growth.
