Centaurea × semiaustriaca

Centaurea × semiaustriaca, commonly known as the Austrian Centaury, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flower heads, which are composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to Central Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
