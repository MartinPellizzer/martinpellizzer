Cyclamen vernum var. caucasicum

Cyclamen vernum var. caucasicum, commonly known as the Caucasian spring cyclamen, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features distinctive heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces delicate, upward-facing flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms in early spring, adding a touch of elegance to woodland gardens. It prefers cool, moist environments and can be grown in shaded areas with well-draining soil.

Cyclamen vernum var. caucasicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in woodland settings.
