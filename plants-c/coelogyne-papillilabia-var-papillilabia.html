Coelogyne papillilabia var. papillilabia

Coelogyne papillilabia var. papillilabia, commonly known as the Papillilabia Cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or purple markings. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad, strap-shaped, and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
