Calophyllum vergens

Calophyllum vergens, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish bark and dark, aromatic wood. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.

Calophyllum vergens is valued for its durable wood, which has been traditionally used in furniture and decorative items.
