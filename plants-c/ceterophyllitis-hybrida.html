Ceterophyllitis hybrida

Ceterophyllitis hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

Ceterophyllitis hybrida is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
