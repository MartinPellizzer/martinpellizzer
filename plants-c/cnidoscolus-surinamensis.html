Cnidoscolus surinamensis

Cnidoscolus surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam chaya, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by spiny, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their nutritional and medicinal properties.

The plant is also valued for its high content of nutrients such as vitamins and minerals.
