Cystopteris fragilis var. protrusa

Cystopteris fragilis var. protrusa, commonly known as the fragrant spleenwort, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Pteridaceae and is native to regions of eastern North America. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a distinctive, feathery appearance.
