Cubilia cubili

Cubilia cubili, commonly known as the spiky leaf plant, is a unique species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage that helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and green. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store moisture efficiently.

Cubilia cubili is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
