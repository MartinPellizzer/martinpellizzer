Clerodendrum alatum

Clerodendrum alatum, commonly known as the winged clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive wing-like structures on its stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Clerodendrum alatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
