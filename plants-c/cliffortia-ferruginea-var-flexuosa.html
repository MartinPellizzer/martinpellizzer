Cliffortia ferruginea var. flexuosa

Cliffortia ferruginea var. flexuosa, commonly known as the red-hot poker or fireplant, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, spiky flower clusters that bloom in shades of orange-red, giving it its common name. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, rusty-colored bloom, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable addition to botanical collections and ornamental gardens.
