Cynorkis rotundifolia

Cynorkis rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its distinctive, rounded leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or white in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in rocky or wooded areas.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
