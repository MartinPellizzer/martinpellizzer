Calanthe discolor var. rufoaurantiaca

Calanthe discolor var. rufoaurantiaca, commonly known as the red-lipped calanthe, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and showy, pale pink to white flowers with distinctive red or orange lips. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it thrives on the forest floor. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
