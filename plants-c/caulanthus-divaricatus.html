Caulanthus divaricatus

Caulanthus divaricatus, commonly known as the Sierra bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Caulanthus divaricatus is an herbaceous perennial that blooms in spring and is adapted to dry, well-drained soils.
