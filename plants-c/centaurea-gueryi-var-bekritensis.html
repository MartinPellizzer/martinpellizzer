Centaurea gueryi var. bekritensis

Centaurea gueryi var. bekritensis, commonly known as the Bekrit centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright blue to purple ray florets and spiny bracts. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Its small, lance-shaped leaves are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
