Cyathea Traillii

Traill's tree fern, scientifically known as Cyathea traillii, is a striking and ancient plant that belongs to the tree fern family. Native to the tropical rainforests of New Zealand, it has adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments. With its tall, slender trunk and fronds that can reach several meters in length, it resembles a primitive tree.
