Cnicus acantholepis

Cnicus acantholepis, commonly known as the thistle-berry or red thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in dry, rocky soils. The plant features spiny leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Cnicus acantholepis is adapted to arid environments and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
