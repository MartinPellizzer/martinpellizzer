Coelogyne cuprea var. cuprea

Coelogyne cuprea var. cuprea, commonly known as the copper orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth. The plant produces striking red and orange flowers during the dry season, which are highly prized in horticulture. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
