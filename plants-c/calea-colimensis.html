Calea colimensis

Calea colimensis, commonly known as the "mirror plant," is a striking herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its unique, waxy, mirror-like leaves that reflect light and create a shimmering effect. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Calea colimensis is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and is also used in traditional medicine.
