Cattleya × mendonciana

Cattleya × mendonciana, commonly known as the Mendocino Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant and showy flowers.

It features large, pendulous blooms with intricate patterns of pink, white, and purple, often resembling the flowers of its parent species. The plant is native to the humid highlands of Costa Rica and is adapted to grow in shaded, well-drained environments. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to thrive in tropical climates with seasonal dry periods.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
