Crassula propinqua

Crassula propinqua, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
