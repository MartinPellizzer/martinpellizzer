Chrysosplenium pseudofauriei var. nipponense

Chrysosplenium pseudofauriei var. nipponense, commonly known as Japanese chrysosplenium, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue and a dense covering of fine hairs. It produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas.
