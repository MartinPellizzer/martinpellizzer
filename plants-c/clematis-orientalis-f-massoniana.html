Clematis orientalis f. massoniana

Clematis orientalis f. massoniana, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a hybrid plant that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a deciduous vine with compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to China and is known for its vigorous growth and ability to climb various structures. Its flowers typically come in shades of white, pink, and red, with a distinctive spurred shape.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
