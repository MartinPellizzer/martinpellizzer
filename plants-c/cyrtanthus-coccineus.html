Cyrtanthus coccineus

Cyrtanthus coccineus, commonly known as the red gladiolus, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves and has a corm as its storage organ. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Cyrtanthus coccineus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
