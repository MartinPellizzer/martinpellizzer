Croton scouleri f. glabriusculus

Croton scouleri f. glabriusculus, commonly known as the smoothleaf croton, is a rare and unique variant of the Croton genus.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves, which distinguish it from other forms of Croton scouleri. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its foliage is usually a vibrant green, with some cultivars displaying variegation.

While not widely cultivated, it is appreciated for its ornamental value and botanical interest.
