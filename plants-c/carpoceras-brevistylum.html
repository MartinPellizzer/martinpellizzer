Carpoceras brevistylum

Carpoceras brevistylum, commonly known as the broad-leaved cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its basal rosette of broad, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typical of the mustard family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
