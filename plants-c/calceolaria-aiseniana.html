Calceolaria aiseniana

Calceolaria aiseniana, commonly known as the Aisenian slipper orchid, is a striking flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that come in a variety of vibrant colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions with well-drained soil. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Calceolaria aiseniana is valued in horticulture for its ornate blooms and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
