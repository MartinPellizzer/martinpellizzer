Calendula suffruticosa var. tunetana

Calendula suffruticosa var. tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian calendula, is a shrub-like plant native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically gray-green with a rough texture, and the plant thrives in dry, sunny conditions. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, demonstrating its adaptability to harsh climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
