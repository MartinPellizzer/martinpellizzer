Cotyledon calyculata

Cotyledon calyculata, commonly known as the "Cape tulip," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
