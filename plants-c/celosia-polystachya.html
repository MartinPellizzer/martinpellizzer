Celosia polystachya

Celosia polystachya, commonly known as the flame flower or red dwarf celosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.

Celosia polystachya is relatively drought-tolerant and can be grown in containers or as part of a mixed flower bed.
