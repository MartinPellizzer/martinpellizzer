Calamus wanggaii

Calamus wanggaii, commonly known as the Wanggai bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. The plant's rhizomes are sometimes used in local medicine and as a source of fiber.
