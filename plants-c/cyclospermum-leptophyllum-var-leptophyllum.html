Cyclospermum leptophyllum var. leptophyllum

Cyclospermum leptophyllum var. leptophyllum, commonly known as the slender cyclospermum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in gardens and greenhouses.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
