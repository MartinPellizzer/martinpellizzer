Conophytum minutum var. lisabeliae

Conophytum minutum var. lisabeliae, commonly known as the Lisabel's rock rose, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
