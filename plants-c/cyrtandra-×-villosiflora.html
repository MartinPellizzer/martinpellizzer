Cyrtandra × villosiflora

Cyrtandra × villosiflora, commonly known as the woolly-flowered cyrtandra, is a hybrid plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, or villosous, floral structures. The plant typically features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Cyrtandra × villosiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
