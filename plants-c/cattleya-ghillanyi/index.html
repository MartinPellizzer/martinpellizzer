Cattleya Ghillanyi

Cattleya ghillanyi is a rare and striking orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil. Known for its vibrant flowers and unique shape, it is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil. Its blooming period typically occurs during the summer months. Despite its beauty, it is often challenging to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
