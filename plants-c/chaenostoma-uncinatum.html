Chaenostoma uncinatum

Chaenostoma uncinatum, commonly known as the hooked chaenostoma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces delicate violet flowers. It is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
