Caudanthera

Caudanthera, commonly known as the golden beard orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. Its most distinctive feature is the clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Caudanthera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
