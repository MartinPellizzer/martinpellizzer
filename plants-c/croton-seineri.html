Croton seineri

Croton seineri, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that often display vibrant colors ranging from green to red and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Croton seineri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
