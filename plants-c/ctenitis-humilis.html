It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that typically grow in a rosette pattern. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are short and produce a network of roots that help anchor it in damp soil.

Ctenitis humilis plays a role in forest ecosystems by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
