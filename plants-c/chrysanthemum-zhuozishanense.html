Chrysanthemum zhuozishanense

Chrysanthemum zhuozishanense, commonly known as the Zhuozishan chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its finely divided, feather-like leaves and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in the fall, producing a range of yellow to orange flower colors. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in Chinese horticulture.
