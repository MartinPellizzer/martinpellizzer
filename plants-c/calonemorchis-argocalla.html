Calonemorchis argocalla

Calonemorchis argocalla, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

The orchid's blooming period occurs during the warmer months, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
