Citharexylum weberbaueri

Citharexylum weberbaueri, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly Hawaii.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from white to pink or red, and they are often fragrant.
