Combretum littoreum

Combretum littoreum, commonly known as sea hibiscus, is a shrub native to coastal regions of Africa and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Combretum littoreum is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
