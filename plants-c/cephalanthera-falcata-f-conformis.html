Cephalanthera falcata f. conformis

Cephalanthera falcata f. conformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and a distinctive, pouch-like flower that is typically pink or white in color. The plant produces a single, showy flower per stem, which is adapted to attract specific pollinators such as bees and flies.
