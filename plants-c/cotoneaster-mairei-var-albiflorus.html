Cotoneaster mairei var. albiflorus

Cotoneaster mairei var. albiflorus, commonly known as the Whiteflower cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of white, five-petaled flowers in early spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its compact growth and seasonal interest.
