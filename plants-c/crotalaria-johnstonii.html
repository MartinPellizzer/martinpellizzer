Crotalaria johnstonii

Crotalaria johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.

The plant is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
