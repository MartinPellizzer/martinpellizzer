Cissus Botria

Cissus botria is a unique climbing plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its ability to grow on rocky surfaces and walls. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine white powder. It is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and easy care requirements. Cissus botria also has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
