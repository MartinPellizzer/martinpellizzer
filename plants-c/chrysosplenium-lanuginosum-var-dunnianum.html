Chrysosplenium lanuginosum var. dunnianum

Chrysosplenium lanuginosum var. dunnianum, commonly known as the downy chrysosplenium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, soft stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
