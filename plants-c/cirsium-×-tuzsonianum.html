Cirsium × tuzsonianum

Cirsium × tuzsonianum, commonly known as the Tuzson's thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, showy purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to the Mediterranean region. It produces both bisexual and unisexual flowers, which contribute to its reproductive strategy.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
