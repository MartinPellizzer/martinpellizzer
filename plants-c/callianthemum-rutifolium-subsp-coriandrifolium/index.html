Callianthemum Rutifolium Subsp. Coriandrifolium

Callianthemum rutifolium subsp. coriandrifolium is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is known for its small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. This subspecies is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a unique and elegant appearance. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this beautiful and ecologically important plant from further decline.
