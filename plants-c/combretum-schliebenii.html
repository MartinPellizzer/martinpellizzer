Combretum schliebenii

Combretum schliebenii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a velvety texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and furniture-making.
