Calanthe truncata

Calanthe truncata, commonly known as the truncated calanthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features lanceolate, dark green leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe truncata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
