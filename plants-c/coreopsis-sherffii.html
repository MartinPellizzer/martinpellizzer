Coreopsis sherffii

Coreopsis sherffii, commonly known as the Texas coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
