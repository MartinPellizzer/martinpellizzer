Cedrelopsis microfoliolata

Cedrelopsis microfoliolata, commonly known as the small-leaved cedrelopsis, is a member of the Cupressaceae family, native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub, characterized by its narrow, conical shape and dense foliage. The leaves are scale-like, arranged in crossed pairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It produces small, reddish-brown cones that mature to a dark brown, and it is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.
