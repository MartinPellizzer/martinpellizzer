Commelina firma

Commelina firma, commonly known as the hardy spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, three-lobed seed capsules. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Commelina firma is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
