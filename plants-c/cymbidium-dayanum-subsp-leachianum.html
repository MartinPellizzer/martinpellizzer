Cymbidium dayanum subsp. leachianum

Cymbidium dayanum subsp.

leachianum, commonly known as the Leach's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow.

The plant typically blooms during the winter and early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often grown for its elegant floral display.
