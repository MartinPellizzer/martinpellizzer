Cleidion lutescens

Cleidion lutescens, commonly known as the yellow sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions.
