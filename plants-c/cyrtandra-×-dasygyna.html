Cyrtandra × dasygyna

Cyrtandra × dasygyna, commonly known as the hybrid beautyberry, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra ochracea and Cyrtandra dasygyna, both native to tropical regions of the Pacific. The plant typically features showy, clustered inflorescences with violet to purple flowers, which are attractive to pollinators. Its foliage is often dark green and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
