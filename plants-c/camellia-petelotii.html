Camellia petelotii

Camellia petelotii, commonly known as the Petelot's camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 meters and features glossy, dark green leaves. Camellia petelotii thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade.

It is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
