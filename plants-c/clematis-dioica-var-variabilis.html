Clematis dioica var. variabilis

Clematis dioica var. variabilis, commonly known as the variable clematis, is a climbing vine native to parts of Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that can range in color from white to pink, violet, and even blue. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
