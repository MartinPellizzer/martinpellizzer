Centaurea latisquama

Centaurea latisquama, commonly known as the broad-leaved centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant blue to purple flowers. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Centaurea latisquama is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
