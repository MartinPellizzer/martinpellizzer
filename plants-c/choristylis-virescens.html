Choristylis virescens

Choristylis virescens, commonly known as the green leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a loose spike, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often found in coastal or sandy environments.
