Clematis viticella var. leiostylis

Clematis viticella var. leiostylis, commonly known as the purple clematis, is a compact, deciduous vine native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and blue. The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to climb or trail along supports. It blooms in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscaping.
