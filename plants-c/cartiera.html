Cartiera

Cartiera, commonly known as the spider plant, is a hardy evergreen perennial native to southern Africa.

It features arching stems with long, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with white or silver streaks. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.

Cartiera is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for households and offices.
