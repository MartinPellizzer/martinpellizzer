Cardiopteris rumphii var. blumeana

Cardiopteris rumphii var. blumeana, commonly known as the Blume's shield fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features bipinnate, deltoid-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and have a leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shady garden settings.
