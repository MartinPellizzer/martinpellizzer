Cyrbasium erosum

Cyrbasium erosum, commonly known as the roughleaf spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features succulent, somewhat hairy leaves that are usually gray-green in color. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Cyrbasium erosum is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.
