Canavalia picta var. elliptica

Canavalia picta var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions.

It features compound leaves with elliptical-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with tendrils that help it ascend supports. Its pods are typically elongated and contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein.

The elliptic cowpea is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to various soil conditions.
