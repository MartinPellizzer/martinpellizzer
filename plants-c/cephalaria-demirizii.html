Cephalaria demirizii

Cephalaria demirizii, commonly known as the Turkish cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Cephalaria demirizii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
