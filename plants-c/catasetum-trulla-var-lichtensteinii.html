Catasetum trulla var. lichtensteinii

Catasetum trulla var. lichtensteinii, commonly known as the Lichtenstein's spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence and vibrant, often purple or red flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are pollinated by specific species of bees, which are attracted by the orchid's unique structure and scent.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its exotic appearance.
