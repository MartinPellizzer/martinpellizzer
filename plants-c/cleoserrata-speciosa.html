Cleoserrata speciosa

Cleoserrata speciosa, commonly known as the showy cleome, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from pink to red and yellow, often with a distinctive central boss. The plant has palmate leaves with three to five lobes, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often grown as an ornamental plant in gardens.
