Camara aculeata f. mista

Camara aculeata f. mista, commonly known as the hybrid nightshade, is a variant of the common nightshade plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage and flowering habits, which differ from the typical species. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple in color. It is native to regions with temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
