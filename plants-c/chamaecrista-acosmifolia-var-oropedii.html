Chamaecrista acosmifolia var. oropedii

Chamaecrista acosmifolia var. oropedii, commonly known as the Oropendola Bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
