Columnea stellata

Columnea stellata, commonly known as the star columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
