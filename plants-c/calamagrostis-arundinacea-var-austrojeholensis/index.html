Calamagrostis Arundinacea Var. Austrojeholensis

Calamagrostis arundinacea var. austrojeholensis is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in wetland environments. It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is often found in marshy areas and along riverbanks. This variant is distinguished by its tall, slender culms and its ability to form dense, attractive clumps. Its foliage is often described as having a soft, feathery texture that adds visual interest to landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated compared to more commonly cultivated grasses.
