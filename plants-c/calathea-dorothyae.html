Calathea dorothyae

Calathea dorothyae, commonly known as Dorothy's Calathea, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, intricately patterned leaves that display a mix of green, silver, and pink hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea dorothyae is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and aesthetic beauty.
