Cassinia vauvilliersii var. pallida

Cassinia vauvilliersii var. pallida, commonly known as the pale cassinia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow to white flower heads. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native pollinators.
