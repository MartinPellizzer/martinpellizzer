Cytisus arboreus var. ballianus

Cytisus arboreus var. ballianus, commonly known as the Ballianus broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, woody stems with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and hardy nature.
