Caladenia atradenia

Caladenia atradenia, commonly known as the Black-veined Spider-orchid, is a striking orchid native to southwestern Australia.

It features a slender, hairy stem with a single, showy orchid flower that has a distinctive black-veined lip. The plant typically grows in heathland and dry forest habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowering period occurs during late spring to early summer, adding a unique ornamental value to the landscape.

The species is appreciated for its intricate floral structure and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
