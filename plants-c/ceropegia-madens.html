Ceropegia madens

Ceropegia madens, commonly known as the Cape flower or the star vine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and attractive, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, often with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Ceropegia madens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a houseplant.
