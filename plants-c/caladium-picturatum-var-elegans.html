Caladium picturatum var. elegans

Caladium picturatum var. elegans, commonly known as the painted leaf caladium, is a tropical perennial prized for its striking, colorful foliage.

It features heart-shaped leaves with intricate patterns of red, orange, and white, creating a vivid display. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often grown as an ornamental plant in gardens or as a houseplant.

Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for adding visual interest to landscapes.
