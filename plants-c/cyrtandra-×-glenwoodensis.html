Cyrtandra × glenwoodensis

Cyrtandra × glenwoodensis, commonly known as the Glenwood hybrid, is a hybrid plant species within the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to Hawaii and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and unique genetic makeup, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
