Croton mekongensis

Croton mekongensis, commonly known as the Mekong croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from green to red and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton mekongensis typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural collections.
