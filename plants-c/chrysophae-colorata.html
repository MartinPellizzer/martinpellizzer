Chrysophae colorata

Chrysophae colorata, commonly known as the colorful chrysophae, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in shrubland habitats and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Chrysophae colorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its bright, long-lasting blooms.
