Cephaelis jervisei

Cephaelis jervisei, commonly known as the red-eyed gourd, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red flowers with yellow centers and hairy stems. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
