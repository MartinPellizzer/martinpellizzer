Chrysophyllum amazonicum

Chrysophyllum amazonicum, commonly known as the Amazonian star apple, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and nutritional properties.
