Carex langyaensis

Carex langyaensis, commonly known as the Langya sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants commonly found in wet or moist environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It typically grows in shaded, damp areas such as forest floors or wetlands.
