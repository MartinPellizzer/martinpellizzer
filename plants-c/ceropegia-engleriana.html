Ceropegia engleriana

Ceropegia engleriana, commonly known as the string of turtles, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and tubular, waxy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy coating. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia engleriana is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
