Coelogyne speciosa var. rubiginosa

Coelogyne speciosa var. rubiginosa, commonly known as the Crimson Orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers with a deep red coloration and intricate patterns, often with a yellow or orange lip. The plant has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids, supporting its epiphytic or terrestrial growth habit. Its leaves are elongated and leathery, adapted to store water in its native humid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
