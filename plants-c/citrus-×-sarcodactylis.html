Citrus × sarcodactylis

Citrus × sarcodactylis, commonly known as the rough lemon.

This hybrid citrus plant is characterized by its rough, pebbled rind and small, tart fruits. It is native to Southeast Asia and is often used as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance. The leaves are glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is also valued in horticulture for its cold hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
