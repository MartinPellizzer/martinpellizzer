Cyparissia

Cyparissia, commonly known as the cypress tree, is a genus of coniferous evergreen trees and shrubs belonging to the cypress family (Cupressaceae).

These plants are characterized by their scale-like leaves and often have a pyramidal or conical shape. Cyparissia species are known for their aromatic wood and are often used in landscaping for their ornamental value.
