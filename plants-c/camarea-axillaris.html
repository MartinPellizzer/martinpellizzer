Camarea axillaris

Camarea axillaris, commonly known as the slender panicle palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a slender, unbranched stem with a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves. Its inflorescence is a compound panicle, with small, white flowers arranged in clusters.

Camarea axillaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained, sandy soils.
