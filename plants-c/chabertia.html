Chabertia

Chabertia, commonly known as the common chabertia, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Chabertia species are often found in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions.
