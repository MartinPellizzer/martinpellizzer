Corynandra chelidonii

Corynandra chelidonii, commonly known as the yellow corynandra, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Corynandra chelidonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
