Centaurea Paui

Centaurea paui is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its vibrant blue to purple blooms that appear in late summer. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Centaurea paui faces threats to its survival, highlighting the need for protection.
