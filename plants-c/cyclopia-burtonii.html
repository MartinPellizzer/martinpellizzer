Cyclopia burtonii

Cyclopia burtonii, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow, dandelion-like flowers. The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

Cyclopia burtonii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
