Cucumis rostratus

Cucumis rostratus, commonly known as the horned cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like spines on the fruit. The plant produces small, green, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are hairy and trailing.

The flowers are yellow and typically appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
