Costus luteus

Costus luteus, commonly known as the yellow costus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Costaceae and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
