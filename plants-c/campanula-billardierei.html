Campanula billardierei

Campanula billardierei, commonly known as the Western bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It features showy blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has upright stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant gardens.

Campanula billardierei is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for native pollinators.
