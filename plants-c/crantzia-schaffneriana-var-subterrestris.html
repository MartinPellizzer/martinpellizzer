Crantzia schaffneriana var. subterrestris

Crantzia schaffneriana var. subterrestris, commonly known as the subterranean crantzia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
