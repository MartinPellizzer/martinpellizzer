Canavalia favieri

Canavalia favieri, commonly known as the white urd or white cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces showy, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant bears elongated pods that contain several angular, edible seeds. It is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it a useful crop in sustainable agriculture.

Canavalia favieri is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
