Crotalaria rhynchotropioides

Crotalaria rhynchotropioides, commonly known as the scarlet crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are usually hairy and branching.
