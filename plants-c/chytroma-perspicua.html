Chytroma perspicua

Chytroma perspicua, commonly known as the transparent chytroma, is a rare and unique plant species characterized by its translucent, gelatinous leaves.

It belongs to the family Chytromataceae and is native to the humid, shaded understories of tropical rainforests. The plant's most striking feature is its ability to absorb and refract light, creating a shimmering effect under sunlight.
