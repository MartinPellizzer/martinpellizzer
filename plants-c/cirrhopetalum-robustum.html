Cirrhopetalum robustum

Cirrhopetalum robustum, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often have a unique, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are pollinated primarily by insects and birds.
