Canna tineoi

Canna tineoi, commonly known as the Tineo canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, often displaying vibrant shades of green, red, and purple. The plant produces showy, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful bracts, typically in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Canna tineoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
