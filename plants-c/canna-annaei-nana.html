Canna annaei nana

Canna annaei nana, commonly known as the dwarf canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, often with a subtle reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Canna annaei nana is a low-maintenance plant that adds vibrant color to gardens and is often used in container gardening.
