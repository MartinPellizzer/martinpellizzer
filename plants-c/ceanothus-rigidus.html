Ceanothus rigidus

Ceanothus rigidus, commonly known as rigid ceanothus, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Ceanothus rigidus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
