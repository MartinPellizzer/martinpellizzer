Critonia hemipteropoda

Critonia hemipteropoda, commonly known as the golden leaf orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to golden yellow, depending on light exposure. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited natural habitat and slow growth rate, it is considered a conservation concern.
