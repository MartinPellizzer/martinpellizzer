Cirsium Shansiense

Cirsium shansiense is a rare and striking member of the thistle family native to central China. It is characterized by its spiny leaves and vibrant purple flowers that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in rocky and dry environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
