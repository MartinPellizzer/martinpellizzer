Centrosema salzmannii var. villosum

Centrosema salzmannii var. villosum, commonly known as silky lespedeza, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with pubescent surfaces, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, spikes, which are attractive to pollinators. Its root system has the ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in erosion control and forage production.
