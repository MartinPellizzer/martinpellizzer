Cyathea polyneuron

Cyathea polyneuron, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a species of tree fern belonging to the family Cyatheaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous, spongy layer called the rachis. Its fronds are large, pinnate, and can grow up to several meters in length.

Cyathea polyneuron plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
