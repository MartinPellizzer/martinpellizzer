Cirsium kubikialpicola

Cirsium kubikialpicola, commonly known as the alpine thistle, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Alps.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, hairy stems and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine, bristly hairs.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
