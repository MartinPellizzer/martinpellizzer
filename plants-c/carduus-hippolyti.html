Carduus hippolyti

Carduus hippolyti, commonly known as the tansy wormwood or tansy.

It is a flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae, native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features a bushy growth habit with gray-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

The plant has a long history of use in traditional medicine and as a source of aromatic compounds.
