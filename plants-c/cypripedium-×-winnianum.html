Cypripedium × winnianum

Cypripedium × winnianum, commonly known as the Chinese lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pouch-like flower with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch that resembles a lady's shoe. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments and has a rhizomatous root system. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a yellow or green lip, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

Cypripedium × winnianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation programs.
