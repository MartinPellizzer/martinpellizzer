Cylindrolobus cristatus

Cylindrolobus cristatus, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, helmet-like flower shape. The plant typically grows in heathland and open forest environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive crested or ridged appearance on the dorsal sepal.

Cylindrolobus cristatus is a perennial herb that produces small, linear leaves and is often found in coastal regions of southern Australia.
