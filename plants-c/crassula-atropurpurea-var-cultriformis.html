Crassula atropurpurea var. cultriformis

Crassula atropurpurea var. cultriformis, commonly known as the Purple Pearlplant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically purple or red in color, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
