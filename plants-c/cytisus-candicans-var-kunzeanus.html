Cytisus candicans var. kunzeanus

Cytisus candicans var. kunzeanus, commonly known as the golden broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, woody stems and trifoliate leaves with a leathery texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators.
