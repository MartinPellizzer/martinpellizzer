Camellia wardii var. wardii

Camellia wardii var. wardii, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters and thrives in partial shade with well-drained, acidic soil. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its showy blooms and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.

The flowers are also used in traditional Chinese medicine and tea production.
