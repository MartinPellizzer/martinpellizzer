Coelogyne bontocensis

Coelogyne bontocensis, commonly known as the Bontoc orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces striking, fragrant, and colorful flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, and its inflorescence can bear up to 20 flowers. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining substrates to thrive.

Coelogyne bontocensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
