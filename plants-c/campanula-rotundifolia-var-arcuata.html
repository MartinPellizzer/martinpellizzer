Campanula rotundifolia var. arcuata

Campanula rotundifolia var. arcuata, commonly known as the rounded-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its oval, rounded leaves and bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in garden settings.
