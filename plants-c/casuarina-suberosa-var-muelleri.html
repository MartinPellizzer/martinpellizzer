Casuarina suberosa var. muelleri

Casuarina suberosa var. muelleri, commonly known as the mueller's she-oak, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, consisting of needle-like leaves arranged in spirals around the stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in sandy or saline soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Casuarina suberosa var. muelleri is known for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
