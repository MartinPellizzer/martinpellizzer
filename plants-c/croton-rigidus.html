Croton rigidus

Croton rigidus, commonly known as the rigid croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are often green with distinctive yellow or red veins. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its bark and leaves contain compounds that have been used in traditional medicine.

Croton rigidus thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
