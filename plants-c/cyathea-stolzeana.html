Cyathea stolzeana

Cyathea stolzeana, commonly known as the Stolze's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length, giving it a distinctive and elegant appearance. Cyathea stolzeana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its slow growth rate and unique morphology make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical climates.
