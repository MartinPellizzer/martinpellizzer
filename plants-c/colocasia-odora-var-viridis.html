Colocasia odora var. viridis

Colocasia odora var. viridis, commonly known as the fragrant elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage is popular in horticulture for its striking appearance and ability to add texture to gardens.
