Cucumis melo f. kikumelon

Cucumis melo f. kikumelon, commonly known as the kikumelon, is a unique horticultural form of the cantaloupe.

It is characterized by its elongated, melon-like shape and thick, ribbed rind. The fruit typically has a pale green exterior with a textured surface, and its flesh is sweet and aromatic. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its distinct appearance and flavor, making it a popular choice in specialty markets.
