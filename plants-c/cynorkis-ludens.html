Cynorkis ludens

Cynorkis ludens, commonly known as the dancing orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that resemble dancing figures, hence its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Cynorkis ludens is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
