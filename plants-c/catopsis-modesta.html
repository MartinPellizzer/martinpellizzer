Catopsis modesta

Catopsis modesta, commonly known as the miniature fern, is a small, delicate plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth pattern. The plant features fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length, with a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rocks or in shaded forest floors.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
