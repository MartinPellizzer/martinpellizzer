Conyza limonifolia

Conyza limonifolia, commonly known as lemonleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as fields and roadsides. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Conyza limonifolia is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
