Croton pedersenii

Croton pedersenii, commonly known as the "Coral Beauty" or "Fire Tree," is a striking shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep reds and oranges to bright yellows and purples, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing seeds. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a distinctive, palmate shape with lobed margins.

Croton pedersenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes due to its dramatic and colorful appearance.
