Callistemon linearis

Callistemon linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and striking red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and garden environments. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract birds.

The species is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
