Calligonum nudatum

Calligonum nudatum, commonly known as the smooth sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia and the Middle East.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in desert or steppe environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
