Clematis aristata var. minor

Clematis aristata var. minor, commonly known as the lesser clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the mountainous regions of the western United States.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or violet, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or trellises.

Its botanical characteristics include slender stems with thorns and showy, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators.
