Calceolaria guentheri

Calceolaria guentheri, commonly known as the Guenther's slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers come in shades of purple and white.

Calceolaria guentheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
