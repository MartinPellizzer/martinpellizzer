Calanthe shweliensis

Calanthe shweliensis, commonly known as the Shweli orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Myanmar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color. Calanthe shweliensis thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
