Corynitis polyantha

Corynitis polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits. Corynitis polyantha thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
