Ceratostylis longipedunculata

Ceratostylis longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-stemmed horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soil conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
