Cavendishia callista

Cavendishia callista, commonly known as the velvet leaf, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a rich, almost metallic sheen. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
