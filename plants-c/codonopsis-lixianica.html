Codonopsis lixianica

Codonopsis lixianica, commonly known as the Lixian bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lanceolate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, elongated fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Codonopsis lixianica is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
