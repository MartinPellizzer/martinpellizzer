Cryptocoryne gasseri

Cryptocoryne gasseri, commonly known as the Gasseri Amazon sword, is a species of aquatic plant native to the Amazon River basin.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length. The plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water with moderate to high light conditions. It is often used in freshwater aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.

Cryptocoryne gasseri is also known for its resilience and ease of care, making it a popular choice among aquarium enthusiasts.
