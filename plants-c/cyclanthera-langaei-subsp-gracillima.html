Cyclanthera langaei subsp. gracillima

Cyclanthera langaei subsp.

gracillima, commonly known as the slender cucumber vine, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant. Its fruit is a slender, elongated berry that turns from green to yellow as it matures.

This species is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
