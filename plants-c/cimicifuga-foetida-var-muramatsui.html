Cimicifuga foetida var. muramatsui

Cimicifuga foetida var. muramatsui, commonly known as Japanese bugbane, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, white, lacecap flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are compound, with pinnate arrangement, and the plant produces small, yellowish flowers in the center of the inflorescence.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
