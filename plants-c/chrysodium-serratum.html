Chrysodium serratum

Chrysodium serratum, commonly known as the serrated chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely serrated leaves and clusters of small, showy yellow flowers. Native to regions of southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its growth habit is typically upright, with multiple stems that bear the flowering heads.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
