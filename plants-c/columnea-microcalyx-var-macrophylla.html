Columnea microcalyx var. macrophylla

Columnea microcalyx var. macrophylla, commonly known as the giant leaf columnea, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 10 inches in length. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warm season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for hanging baskets or as a houseplant.
