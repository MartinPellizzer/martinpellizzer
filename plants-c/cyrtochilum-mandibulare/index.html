Cyrtochilum Mandibulare

Cyrtochilum mandibulare is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. Known for its unique, mandible-shaped petals, this plant captivates botanists and plant lovers alike with its intricate floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species within the orchid family. Its conservation status highlights the need for further study and protection in its natural habitat.
