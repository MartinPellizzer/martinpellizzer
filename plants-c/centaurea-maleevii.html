Centaurea maleevii

Centaurea maleevii, commonly known as the Caucasian centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to purple flowers. Its flowers are surrounded by bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

Centaurea maleevii is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it thrives in cooler, temperate climates.
