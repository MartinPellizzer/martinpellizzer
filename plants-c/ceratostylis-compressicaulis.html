Ceratostylis compressicaulis

Ceratostylis compressicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure, which includes a distinctive horn-shaped spur.
