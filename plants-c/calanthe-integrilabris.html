Calanthe integrilabris

Calanthe integrilabris, commonly known as the smooth-labied calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink or white with a distinctive, smooth labellum.

Calanthe integrilabris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
