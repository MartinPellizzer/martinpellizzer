Corysanthes orbiculata

Corysanthes orbiculata, commonly known as the globe hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its round, orbicular-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects.

Corysanthes orbiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
