Cuphea lozanii

Cuphea lozanii, commonly known as the pink flame plant, is a flowering shrub native to Mexico.

It features showy, tubular red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Cuphea lozanii is known for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
