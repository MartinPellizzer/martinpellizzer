Ctenopteris rawlinsonensis

Ctenopteris rawlinsonensis, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and enabling it to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
