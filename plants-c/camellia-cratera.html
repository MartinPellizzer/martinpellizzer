Camellia cratera

Camellia cratera, commonly known as the mountain camellia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a member of the Theaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, camellia-like flowers. Camellia cratera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
