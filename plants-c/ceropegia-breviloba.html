Ceropegia breviloba

Ceropegia breviloba, commonly known as the dwarf string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white, powdery coating. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for hanging baskets and indoor gardens.
