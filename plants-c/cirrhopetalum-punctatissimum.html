Cirrhopetalum punctatissimum

Cirrhopetalum punctatissimum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spotted petals that range in color from white to pale pink. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
