Camaridium Hematoglossum

Red-veined orchid, scientifically known as Camaridium hematoglossum, is a rare and striking species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. Its vibrant red veins contrasting against its green foliage make it a standout among orchid varieties. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocky outcrops. Due to its unique appearance and limited habitat, it is highly sought after by botanists and collectors alike. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss and overharvesting.
