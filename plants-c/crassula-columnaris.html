Crassula columnaris

Crassula columnaris, commonly known as the columnar crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive upright, columnar growth habit with dense clusters of fleshy, triangular leaves. The leaves are typically a deep green color and have a slightly waxy texture, which helps the plant retain moisture. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
