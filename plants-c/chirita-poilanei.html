Chirita poilanei

Chirita poilanei, commonly known as the Polianei's violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in humid, shaded forest environments. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
