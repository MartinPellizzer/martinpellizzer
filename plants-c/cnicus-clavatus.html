Cnicus clavatus

Cnicus clavatus, commonly known as the yellow cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that grows up to 30 cm tall, featuring bright yellow flowers with five petals. The plant has alternate, pinnately divided leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and rocky areas.

Cnicus clavatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
