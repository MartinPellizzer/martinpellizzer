Coelogyne renae

Coelogyne renae, commonly known as the Renae's orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense, white to pale green pubescence. The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence bearing several fragrant, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Coelogyne renae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
