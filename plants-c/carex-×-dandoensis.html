Carex × dandoensis

Carex × dandoensis, commonly known as the Dando's sedge, is a hybrid plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Carex × dandoensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
