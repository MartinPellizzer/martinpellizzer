Combretum schinzii

Combretum schinzii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The bark is smooth and may exude a milky sap when damaged.
