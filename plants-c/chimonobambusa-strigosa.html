Chimonobambusa strigosa

Chimonobambusa strigosa, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or bamboo grass, is a species of grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant is often found in moist, shady environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its growth habit is clump-forming, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Chimonobambusa strigosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture.
