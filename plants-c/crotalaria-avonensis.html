Crotalaria avonensis

Crotalaria avonensis, commonly known as the Avon false flax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Crotalaria avonensis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
