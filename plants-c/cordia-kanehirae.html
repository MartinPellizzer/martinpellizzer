Cordia kanehirae

Cordia kanehirae, commonly known as the Hawaiian cordia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and dry forests. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a leathery texture with a smooth margin.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
