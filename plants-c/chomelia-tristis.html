Chomelia tristis

Chomelia tristis, commonly known as the weeping chomelia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its drooping inflorescences that give it its common name. Chomelia tristis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
