Ceropegia gemmiflora

Ceropegia gemmiflora, commonly known as the gemmiferous ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often branched and covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green flowers with a distinctive, tubular shape. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
