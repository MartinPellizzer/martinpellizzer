Cephaelis hirta

Cephaelis hirta, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. It is often found in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Cephaelis hirta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
