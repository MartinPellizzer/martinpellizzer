Chirita halongensis

It is native to Vietnam, specifically the coastal areas around Halong Bay, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is often found growing in rocky or shaded environments near the coast.
