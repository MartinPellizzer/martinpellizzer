Crotalaria ukingensis

Crotalaria ukingensis, commonly known as the African cowpea or red bush bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its erect growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive pods containing angular, hard seeds. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Crotalaria ukingensis is also noted for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a forage crop.
