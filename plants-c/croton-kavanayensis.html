Croton kavanayensis

Croton kavanayensis, commonly known as the Kavanay's croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored leaves, which can vary in shape and texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
