Calceolaria engleriana subsp. acuminata

Calceolaria engleriana subsp.

acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata slipper orchid, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family. It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru.

This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
