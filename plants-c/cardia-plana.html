Cardia plana

Cardia plana, commonly known as the flat heart plant, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Cardia plana thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its potential medicinal properties.
