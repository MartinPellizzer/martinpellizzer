Cineraria aurantiaca

Cineraria aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial herb, often with fuzzy, silver-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
