Cynorkis lilacina var. comorensis

Cynorkis lilacina var. comorensis, commonly known as the Comorian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Comoros Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or lilac-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy texture to retain moisture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique and vibrant floral display.
