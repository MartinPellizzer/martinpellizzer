Cyperus fortunei

Cyperus fortunei, commonly known as the zigzag sedge, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas or along water edges.

Cyperus fortunei produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green or brown, and it is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive, upright growth habit.
