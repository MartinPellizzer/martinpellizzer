Centaurium capense

Centaurium capense, commonly known as the Cape centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Centaurium capense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
