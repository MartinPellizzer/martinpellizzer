Callilepis laureola var. glabra

Callilepis laureola var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth Cape rose, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and elliptical, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
