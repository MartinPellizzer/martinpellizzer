Crocus kartaldagensis

Crocus kartaldagensis, commonly known as the Karroo crocus, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, early spring blooms. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive purple or brown center. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
