Cotoneaster × suecicus

Cotoneaster × suecicus, commonly known as the Swedish cotoneaster, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Cotoneaster horizontalis and Cotoneaster franchetii.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of red or orange berries that are typically ornamental and attract birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden use.

Cotoneaster × suecicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
