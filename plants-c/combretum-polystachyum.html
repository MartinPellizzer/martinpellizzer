Combretum polystachyum

Combretum polystachyum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in dense, showy clusters during the dry season. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture. The plant belongs to the family Combretaceae and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
