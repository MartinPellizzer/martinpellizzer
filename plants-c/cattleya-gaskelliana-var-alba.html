Cattleya gaskelliana var. alba

Cattleya gaskelliana var. alba, commonly known as the White Gaskell Cattleya, is a rare and striking orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white flowers with a subtle pinkish tinge at the base. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its pale blooms.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
