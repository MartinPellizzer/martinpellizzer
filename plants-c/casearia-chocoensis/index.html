Casearia Chocoensis

Casearia chocoensis is a rare and unique plant native to the cloud forests of western Colombia. It belongs to the Salicaceae family, which includes willows and poplars, though it exhibits distinct characteristics. This species is known for its striking foliage and delicate flowers, making it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Its beauty and ecological significance highlight the importance of preserving biodiversity in tropical regions.
