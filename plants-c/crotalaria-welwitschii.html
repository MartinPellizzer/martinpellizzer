Crotalaria welwitschii

Crotalaria welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven leaflets and produces yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are encased in hard, spiny pods, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid conditions.

Crotalaria welwitschii is also noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
