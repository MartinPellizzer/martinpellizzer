Cupania tenax

Cupania tenax, commonly known as the rough cupania, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy or rough to the touch. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually white or pale green, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.

Cupania tenax is valued for its ornamental potential and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
