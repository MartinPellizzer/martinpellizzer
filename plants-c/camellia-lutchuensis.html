Camellia lutchuensis

Camellia lutchuensis, commonly known as the Lushan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia lutchuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
