Combretum mooreanum

Combretum mooreanum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. The bark is smooth and may develop a reddish hue, adding to its visual appeal.
