Croton maidenii

Croton maidenii, commonly known as the Maori croton, is a tropical shrub native to New Zealand.

It features showy, palmate leaves with vibrant green and sometimes reddish hues. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
