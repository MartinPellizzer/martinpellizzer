Castanopsis pseudindica

Castanopsis pseudindica, commonly known as the Indian chestnut.

It is a deciduous evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India. The plant belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and beeches. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a leathery texture.

The tree produces small, chestnut-like fruits that are encased in a spiny husk.
