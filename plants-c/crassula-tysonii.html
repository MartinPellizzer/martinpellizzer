Crassula tysonii

Crassula tysonii, commonly known as the "Tyson's Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
