Campanula fragilis var. diffusa

Campanula fragilis var. diffusa, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
