Centaurea prolongi var. granatensis

Centaurea prolongi var. granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense Blazing Star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, sparsely branched stems that are covered in fine, grayish hairs. The plant produces striking, bright red to orange flowers that bloom in summer, arranged in dense, spiky inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with serrated edges and a rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
