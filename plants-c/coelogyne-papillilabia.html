Coelogyne papillilabia

Coelogyne papillilabia, commonly known as the papillilabium orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and bloom during the rainy season.

Coelogyne papillilabia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
