Chitonochilus papuanus

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and specialized growth habits. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments, often on rocky substrates or in shaded forest areas. Its flowers are small and delicate, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.
