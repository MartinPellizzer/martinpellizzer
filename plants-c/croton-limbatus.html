Croton limbatus

Croton limbatus, commonly known as the scarlet croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton limbatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.
