Cissampelos triloba

Cissampelos triloba, commonly known as the African moon vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces a milky latex sap, which is a distinguishing feature. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Cissampelos triloba is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
