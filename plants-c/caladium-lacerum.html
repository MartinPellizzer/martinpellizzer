Caladium lacerum

Caladium lacerum, commonly known as the scarlet lacerum, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that display a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its dramatic visual appeal.

Caladium lacerum is typically propagated through rhizomes or seeds, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
