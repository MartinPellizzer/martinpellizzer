Cryptanthus × makoyanus

Cryptanthus × makoyanus, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a hybrid plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Bryophyllum and is characterized by its segmented, fleshy stems that often bear bright, showy flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant blooms in shades of red, pink, orange, and yellow during the holiday season. Its leaves are usually thick and waxy, adapted to store water, which is typical of many succulent species.

Cryptanthus × makoyanus is popular as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
