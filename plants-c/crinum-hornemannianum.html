Crinum hornemannianum

Crinum hornemannianum, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often blue-green in color and can grow up to 1 meter in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Crinum hornemannianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
