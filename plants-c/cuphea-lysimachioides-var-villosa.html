Cuphea lysimachioides var. villosa

Cuphea lysimachioides var. villosa, commonly known as the velvet cuphea, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of tubular, red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
