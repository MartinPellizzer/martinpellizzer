Cyclogyne procumbens

Cyclogyne procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate cyclogyne, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its sprawling, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of delicate, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
