Calanthe micrantha

Calanthe micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically arranged in a loose inflorescence. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Calanthe micrantha is valued in horticulture for its delicate beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
