Cymbidium hoosai

Cymbidium hoosai, commonly known as the Hoosai orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of dark red or purple flowers with yellow lips, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and requires well-drained soil and moderate light.

Cymbidium hoosai is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
