Centaurea × chetiana

Centaurea × chetiana, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Centaurea cyanus (blue cornflower) and Centaurea stoebe (devil's bit scabious). The plant features vibrant blue to purple flowers with a distinctive ray floret structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and disturbed areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental value and ecological benefits.
