Chorizandra sphaerocephala

Chorizandra sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globular choral, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, spherical growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters at the top of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
