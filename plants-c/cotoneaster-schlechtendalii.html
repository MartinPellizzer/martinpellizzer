Cotoneaster schlechtendalii

Cotoneaster schlechtendalii, commonly known as the Schlechtendal cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster schlechtendalii is often used in landscaping for its compact growth habit and seasonal interest.
