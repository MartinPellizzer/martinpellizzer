Cerastium pallidum

Cerastium pallidum, commonly known as the pale stellaria, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae, which includes many flowering plants with showy blooms. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

Cerastium pallidum thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along streambanks.
