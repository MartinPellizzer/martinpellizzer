Calamagrostis Sylvatica Var. Pyramidalis

Calamagrostis sylvatica var. pyramidalis is a striking ornamental grass known for its tall, narrow leaves that form a dense, pyramidal shape. Native to Europe, this plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its foliage remains lush throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking texture and visual interest. The plant is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for wildlife and its low maintenance requirements. With its elegant appearance and ecological benefits, it is a versatile addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
