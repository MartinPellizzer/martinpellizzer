Caulophyllum robustum

Caulophyllum robustum, commonly known as the American ginseng, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in rich, moist woodland soils and is often found in shaded environments. Its roots are valued in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Caulophyllum robustum plays an important ecological role in forest ecosystems.
