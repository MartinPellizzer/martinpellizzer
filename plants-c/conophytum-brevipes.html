Conophytum brevipes

Conophytum brevipes, commonly known as the "Little Cone Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded leaves that form a dense rosette, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Conophytum brevipes is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
