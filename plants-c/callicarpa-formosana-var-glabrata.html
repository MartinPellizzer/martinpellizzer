Callicarpa formosana var. glabrata

Callicarpa formosana var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright purple to red berries that appear in the fall. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

The berries are not only visually striking but also attract birds and other wildlife.
