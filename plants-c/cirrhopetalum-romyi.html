Cirrhopetalum romyi

Cirrhopetalum romyi, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, velvety-textured leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts worldwide.
