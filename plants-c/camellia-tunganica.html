Camellia tunganica

Camellia tunganica, commonly known as the Tunganica camellia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces striking, double flowers with vibrant red petals. The plant thrives in humid, subtropical climates and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Camellia tunganica is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its potential in horticultural cultivation.
