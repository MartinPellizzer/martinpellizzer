Ceropegia filicalyx

Ceropegia filicalyx, commonly known as the "Lunar Vine," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia filicalyx is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
