Cerastium arvense var. tenuipes

Cerastium arvense var. tenuipes, commonly known as slender weed chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features prostrate or ascending stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, arranged in a rosette at the base.

It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in cultivated fields.
