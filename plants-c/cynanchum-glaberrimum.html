Cynanchum glaberrimum

Cynanchum glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces a milky sap, which is typical of many species in its family. Its leaves are alternate and often have a hairy texture, while its roots are deep and fibrous.
