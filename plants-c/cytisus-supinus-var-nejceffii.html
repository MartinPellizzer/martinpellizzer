Cytisus supinus var. nejceffii

Cytisus supinus var. nejceffii, commonly known as the dwarf yellow broom, is a compact shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators like bees. The plant has trifoliate leaves and is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
