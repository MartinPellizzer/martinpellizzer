Capparis luzonensis

Capparis luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon coralroot, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a member of the Capparidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, coral-like inflorescence. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded areas of mountainous regions. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellowish-white flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
