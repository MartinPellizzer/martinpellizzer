Convolvulus sloanei

Convolvulus sloanei, commonly known as the Sloane's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant features trailing stems with opposite leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a vibrant, low-maintenance plant.
