Caladium bicolor var. houlletii

Caladium bicolor var. houlletii, commonly known as the "Carmen" caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial to full shade. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for garden displays.

It is often used in containers or as part of a tropical garden arrangement.
