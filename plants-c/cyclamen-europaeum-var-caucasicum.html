Cyclamen europaeum var. caucasicum

Cyclamen europaeum var. caucasicum, commonly known as the Caucasian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink to lilac flowers with distinctive spurred petals. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along streambanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.
