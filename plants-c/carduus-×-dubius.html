Carduus × dubius

Carduus × dubius, commonly known as the common cocklebur.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and distinctive spherical fruits. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in agricultural fields. The plant produces a large number of seeds, which are dispersed by wind and can persist in the soil for several years.

Due to its invasive potential, it is considered a weed in many regions.
