Chenopodium × podperae

Chenopodium × podperae, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is an annual herbaceous plant characterized by its succulent stems and leaves.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is native to Europe but has been introduced to various parts of the world. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, and its seeds are dispersed by wind. It is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

The species is also noted for its high nutritional value, making it a potential forage crop in some regions.
