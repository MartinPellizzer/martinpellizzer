Cyperus giolii

Cyperus giolii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its slender, grass-like stems are often covered in fine, hair-like structures, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

Cyperus giolii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support aquatic ecosystems.
