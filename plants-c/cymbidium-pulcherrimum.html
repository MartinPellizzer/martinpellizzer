Cymbidium pulcherrimum

Cymbidium pulcherrimum, commonly known as the orchid of the sun, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and long, arching inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant flowers. The plant produces showy, often brightly colored blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which appear during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cymbidium pulcherrimum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and relatively easy care requirements.
