Calathea gandersii

Calathea gandersii, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a tropical foliage plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled leaves with contrasting green and silver patterns that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea gandersii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
