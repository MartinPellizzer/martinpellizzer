Commelina reflexa

Commelina reflexa, commonly known as the slender commelina, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy coating and produces small, blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, and it is often found in the understory of dense vegetation.

Commelina reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
