Chenopodium berlandieri var. macrocalycium

Chenopodium berlandieri var. macrocalycium, commonly known as buffalo bur, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical fruit heads that contain numerous small seeds. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs.

It is an important plant in the ecosystem, providing food for various wildlife species.
