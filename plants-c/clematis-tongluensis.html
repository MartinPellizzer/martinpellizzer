Clematis tongluensis

Clematis tongluensis, commonly known as the Tonglu clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, usually white or pale pink flowers. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are often armed with hooked prickles.

It thrives in temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
