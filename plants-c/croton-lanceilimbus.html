Croton lanceilimbus

Croton lanceilimbus, commonly known as the African croton, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or yellowish in color. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its unique foliage.
