Chlorophytum baudi-candeanum

Chlorophytum baudi-candeanum, commonly known as the Variegated Spider Plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial evergreen plant native to South Africa, characterized by its strap-like, variegated leaves that feature white and green patterns. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers on slender stalks, which are followed by bright blue berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both decorative and air-purifying purposes.
