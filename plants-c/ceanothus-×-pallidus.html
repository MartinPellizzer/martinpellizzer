Ceanothus × pallidus

Ceanothus × pallidus, commonly known as the silver bush ceanothus, is a hybrid shrub native to California.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance and helps reflect sunlight. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant blue or purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry gardens.

Ceanothus × pallidus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
