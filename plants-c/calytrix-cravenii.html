Calytrix cravenii

Calytrix cravenii, commonly known as the Crimson Beauty, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Calytrix cravenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
