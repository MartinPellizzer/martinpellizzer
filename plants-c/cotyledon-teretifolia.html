Cotyledon teretifolia

Cotyledon teretifolia, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the growing season. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Cotyledon teretifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
