Campylostemon pynaertii

Campylostemon pynaertii, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
