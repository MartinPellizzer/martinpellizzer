Coelogyne brachyptera

Coelogyne brachyptera, commonly known as the short-clubbed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its delicate, long-lasting blooms.

Coelogyne brachyptera is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and seasonal blooming cycle.
