Clitoria australis

Clitoria australis, commonly known as the Australian winged bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Australia and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet-blue, butterfly-shaped flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces edible pods containing seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both ornamental and nutritional purposes.

Clitoria australis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a useful plant in agricultural systems.
