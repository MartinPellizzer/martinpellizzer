Cochliospermum clementei

Cochliospermum clementei, commonly known as the African senna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain flat, winged seeds, which are adapted for wind dispersal.

It is often found in dry, open habitats and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
