Cleome cypria

Cleome cypria, commonly known as the "spider flower," is an annual flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It produces vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often with a distinctive spider-like appearance. The plant has palmate leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade, preferring well-drained soil.
