Cirsium × oenanum

Cirsium × oenanum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to coastal areas of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant is often found in maritime environments, where it tolerates salt spray and strong winds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
