Cestichis elliptica

Cestichis elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cestichis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine, white hairs. The leaves are elliptical in shape, with a pointed tip and a smooth, waxy surface. Small, yellowish flowers are borne in clusters at the ends of the stems, typically blooming in spring.

This plant is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
