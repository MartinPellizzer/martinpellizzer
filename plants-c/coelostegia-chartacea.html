Coelostegia chartacea

Coelostegia chartacea, commonly known as the paperleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are thin and papery in texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Coelostegia chartacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its beauty.
