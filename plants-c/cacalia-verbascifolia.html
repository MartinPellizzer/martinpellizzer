Cacalia verbascifolia

Cacalia verbascifolia, commonly known as the woolly thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its soft, woolly leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is covered in dense, fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Cacalia verbascifolia is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
