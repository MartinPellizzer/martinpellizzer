Chaetospora pauciflora

Chaetospora pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered chaetospora, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, clustered flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually alternate and simple, with a rough texture.
