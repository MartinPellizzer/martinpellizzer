Cucumis striatus

Cucumis striatus, commonly known as the horned cucumber, is a vine-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces elongated, spiny fruits that resemble horns, hence its common name. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is often used in culinary applications, though it has a distinct, tangy flavor.
