Chaptalia exscapa var. chilensis

Chaptalia exscapa var. chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean chaptalia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

Its leaves are often hairy and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its flowers are small and yellow, forming dense clusters.
