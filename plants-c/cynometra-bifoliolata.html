Cynometra bifoliolata

Cynometra bifoliolata, commonly known as the two-leaf sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-leaf structure at the base of its branches, giving it a unique appearance. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets arranged in pairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spiny fruits containing seeds.

This species is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
