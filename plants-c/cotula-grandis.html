Cotula grandis

Cotula grandis, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a daisy-like pattern. The plant has silver-gray, fuzzy leaves that give it its common name and help reflect sunlight. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Cotula grandis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
