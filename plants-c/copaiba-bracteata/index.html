Copaiba Bracteata

Copaiba bracteata is a unique plant native to the rainforests of South America. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used by indigenous communities for centuries. The plant produces a valuable resin that is often harvested for its therapeutic benefits. Its leaves and bark contain compounds with anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects. This species plays an important role in both traditional medicine and ecological systems.
