Crotalaria ononoides var. pubescens

Crotalaria ononoides var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and hairy foliage. The plant is often cultivated for its edible seeds, which are used in various cuisines. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable crop for soil enrichment.
