Coleus globosus

Coleus globosus, commonly known as the globe coleus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of small, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and can vary in color from green to purple. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening and landscape use.
