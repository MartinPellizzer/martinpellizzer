Cosmos scabiosoides var. typicus

Cosmos scabiosoides var. typicus, commonly known as the common cosmos, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, which bloom in summer. The plant has feathery, gray-green leaves and grows in a bushy form. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Cosmos scabiosoides var. typicus is a hardy perennial that prefers full sun and well-drained soil.
