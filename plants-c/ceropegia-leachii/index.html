Ceropegia Leachii

Ceropegia leachii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to southern Africa. Known for its unique tubular flowers and trailing stems, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. This species thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Its care requires patience and attention to avoid overwatering. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the horticultural world.
