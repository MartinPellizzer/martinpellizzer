Caladenia eminens

Caladenia eminens, commonly known as the tall spider-orchid, is a striking orchid species native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and striking, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces a single, elongated flower with a distinctive, narrow labellum that resembles a spider's leg. Caladenia eminens thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

Due to its ornamental value and conservation status, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and protected areas.
