Cucumis officinarum-melo

Cucumis officinarum-melo, commonly known as the Persian cucumber or melon, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated fruits with a thick, smooth rind and a sweet, juicy interior. The plant typically grows as a trailing vine with palmately lobed leaves and yellow flowers. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is cultivated for both its edible fruits and ornamental value.
