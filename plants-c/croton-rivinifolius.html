Croton rivinifolius

Croton rivinifolius, commonly known as the red-leaved croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red leaves that contrast with its green stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red fruits containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
