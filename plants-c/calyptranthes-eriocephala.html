Calyptranthes eriocephala

Calyptranthes eriocephala, commonly known as the woolly calyptranthes, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
