Cystorchis gracilis

Cystorchis gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are delicate, usually white or pale pink, and are arranged in a loose spike.

Cystorchis gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
