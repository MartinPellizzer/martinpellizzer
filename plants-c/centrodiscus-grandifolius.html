Centrodiscus grandifolius

Centrodiscus grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf centrodiscus, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
