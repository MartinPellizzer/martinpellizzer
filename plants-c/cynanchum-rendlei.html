Cynanchum rendlei

Cynanchum rendlei, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by follicle fruits containing seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its chemical composition includes various alkaloids, which contribute to its therapeutic uses.
