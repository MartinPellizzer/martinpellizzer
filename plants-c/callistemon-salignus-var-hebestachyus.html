Callistemon salignus var. hebestachyus

Callistemon salignus var. hebestachyus, commonly known as the silky bottlebrush, is a variant of the bottlebrush plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its long, cylindrical inflorescences that are covered in soft, silky pink to red hairs. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract nectar-feeding birds and insects.
