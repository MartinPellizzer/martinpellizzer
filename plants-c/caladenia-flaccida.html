Caladenia flaccida

Caladenia flaccida, commonly known as the slender spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a single, slender stem that bears a single, delicate orchid flower with a distinctive pinkish-brown coloration. Its floral structure is highly specialized, with a labellum that resembles a spider, contributing to its common name.

Caladenia flaccida is a rare and protected species, often found in specific microhabitats.
