Cleyera lipingensis

Cleyera lipingensis, commonly known as the Liping holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan and Guizhou provinces, where it thrives in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are elliptical and have a glossy surface. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
