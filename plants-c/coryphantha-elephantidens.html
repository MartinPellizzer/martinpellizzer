Coryphantha elephantidens

Coryphantha elephantidens, commonly known as the elephant's tooth.

It is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. This plant features cylindrical stems covered in spines and produces yellow flowers during the spring. The leaves are reduced to spines, and the plant is adapted to arid environments.

It is typically found in rocky soils and is well-suited for xerophytic conditions.
