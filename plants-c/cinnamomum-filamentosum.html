Cinnamomum filamentosum

Cinnamomum filamentosum, commonly known as the thread-leaved cinnamon, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue berries.

This species is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
