Croton adumbratus

Croton adumbratus, commonly known as the dark croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often multicolored foliage, which can range from deep green to vibrant red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough, textured surface.

Croton adumbratus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental leaves and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
