Chloris barbata var. decora

Chloris barbata var. decora, commonly known as the decorative grass or ornamental chloris, is a variety of the common chloris grass.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and delicate, feather-like inflorescence. This plant is native to Australia and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate drought conditions.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
