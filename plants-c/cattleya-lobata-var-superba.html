Cattleya lobata var. superba

Cattleya lobata var. superba, commonly known as the superb cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It features large, showy blooms with a mix of pink and white petals, often accented with deep red or purple markings. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It requires well-draining potting media and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated by orchid enthusiasts around the world.
