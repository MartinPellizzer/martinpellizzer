Capnoides govaniana

Capnoides govaniana, commonly known as the African mountain cedar, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptic to ovate in shape. The tree is valued for its durable wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
