Caladenia × tutelata

Caladenia × tutelata, commonly known as the tutelary spider-orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like appearance with elongated, narrow petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats, often in coastal areas. It produces vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species in some regions.
