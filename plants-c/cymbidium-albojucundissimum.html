Cymbidium albojucundissimum

Cymbidium albojucundissimum, commonly known as the white joy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, white flowers with yellow-green markings. The plant is evergreen and typically grows in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

Cymbidium albojucundissimum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
