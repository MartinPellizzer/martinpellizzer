Crotalaria pohliana var. balansae

Crotalaria pohliana var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae false cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.
