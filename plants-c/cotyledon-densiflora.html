Cotyledon densiflora

Cotyledon densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cotyledon densiflora is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact growth and ornamental appeal.
