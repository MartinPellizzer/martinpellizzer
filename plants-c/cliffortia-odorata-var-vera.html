Cliffortia odorata var. vera

Cliffortia odorata var. vera, commonly known as the sweet-scented snowbells, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that emit a sweet, honey-like fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense, ornamental appearance.
