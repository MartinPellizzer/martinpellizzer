Columnea tuerckheimii

Columnea tuerckheimii, commonly known as the red-tipped columnea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting white or yellow tip. The plant produces opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Columnea tuerckheimii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
