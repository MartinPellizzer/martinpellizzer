Clematis orientalis var. flava

Clematis orientalis var. flava, commonly known as the yellow clematis, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of compound leaves with three to five leaflets, which are usually dark green and glossy.

Clematis orientalis var. flava thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
