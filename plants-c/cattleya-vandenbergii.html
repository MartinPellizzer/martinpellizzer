Cattleya vandenbergii

Cattleya vandenbergii, commonly known as the Vandenberg's cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with intricate patterns. The plant features thick, fleshy roots adapted for absorbing moisture from the humid rainforest environment. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to thrive in seasonal conditions.

Cattleya vandenbergii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
