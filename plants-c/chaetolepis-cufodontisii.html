Chaetolepis cufodontisii

Chaetolepis cufodontisii, commonly known as the African waterwheel plant, is a unique aquatic plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves that resemble the spokes of a wheel, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams. Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing shelter for small fish and invertebrates.
