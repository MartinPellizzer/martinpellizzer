Crassula umbellata

Crassula umbellata, commonly known as the Umbrella Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, dark green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula umbellata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
