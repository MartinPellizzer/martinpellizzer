Chamaecytisus Glaber Var. Elongatus

Chamaecytisus glaber var. elongatus is a unique plant known for its long, slender stems and delicate, fragrant flowers. It belongs to the legume family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. This variety is a variation of the common broom, characterized by its extended growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for landscaping. Its hardy nature and ability to attract pollinators make it a favorite among horticulturists.
