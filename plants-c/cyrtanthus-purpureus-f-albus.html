Cyrtanthus purpureus f. albus

Cyrtanthus purpureus f. albus, commonly known as the White Rain Lily, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces showy, cup-shaped flowers that are predominantly white, with a subtle pinkish hue at the base. The plant blooms in early summer, typically from June to July, and is adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils. It is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

This cultivar is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
