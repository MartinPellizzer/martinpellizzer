Chamaedorea flavovirens

Chamaedorea flavovirens, commonly known as the Golden Bamboo Palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear lance-shaped, bright green leaves with a slight yellowish tinge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by dark blue-black berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

Chamaedorea flavovirens is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and elegant appearance.
