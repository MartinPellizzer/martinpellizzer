Centaurea tenuifolia var. willkommii

Centaurea tenuifolia var. willkommii, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in compact clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand arid conditions. It is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use in drought-resistant gardens.
