Ceropegia meyeriana

Ceropegia meyeriana, commonly known as the dwarf string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thin, fleshy stems that trail or climb, often adorned with small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, attracting specific pollinators like moths. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
