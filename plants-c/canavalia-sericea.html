Canavalia sericea

Canavalia sericea, commonly known as silky bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pods. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, hairy pods containing several seeds. Canavalia sericea is often grown as a forage crop due to its high nutritional value for livestock.
