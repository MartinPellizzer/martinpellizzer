Capsella bursa-pastoris f. macrocarpa

Capsella bursa-pastoris f. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-seed velvetleaf, is a variant of the common chickweed.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in loose inflorescences. Its most distinctive feature is the large, inflated, seed pod that resembles a pouch, giving it its common name.

This plant is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
