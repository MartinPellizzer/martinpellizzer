Chiropetalum Tricoccum

Chiropetalum tricoccum is a rare and elusive plant known for its striking yellow flowers and unique ecological niche. Found primarily in the dense, shaded forests of the eastern United States, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its scientific name reflects both its delicate appearance and its specialized habitat requirements. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, the species faces significant conservation challenges. This article explores the biology, habitat, and conservation status of this fascinating plant.
