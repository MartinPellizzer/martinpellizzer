Coronilla coronata f. macrantha

Coronilla coronata f. macrantha, commonly known as the purple crown vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces spikes of purple to pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its erect growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and restoration projects.
