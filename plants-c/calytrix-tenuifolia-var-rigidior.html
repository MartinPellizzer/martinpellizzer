Calytrix tenuifolia var. rigidior

Calytrix tenuifolia var. rigidior, commonly known as the rigid fairy fan, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers arranged in dense, fan-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight waxy coating.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.
