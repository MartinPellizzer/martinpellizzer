Cattleya mossiae var. flammea

Cattleya mossiae var. flammea, commonly known as the Flame Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fiery orange to red flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant blooms. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often cultivated for its striking color and long-lasting floral display.
