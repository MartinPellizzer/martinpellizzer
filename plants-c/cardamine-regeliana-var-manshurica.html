Cardamine regeliana var. manshurica

Cardamine regeliana var. manshurica, commonly known as the Manshurian watercress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological roles in its native habitats.
