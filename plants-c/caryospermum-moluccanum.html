Caryospermum moluccanum

Caryospermum moluccanum, commonly known as the Moluccan morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, hard seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and rapid growth habit.

Caryospermum moluccanum is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its purported health benefits.
