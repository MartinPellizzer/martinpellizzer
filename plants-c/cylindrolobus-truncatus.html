Cylindrolobus truncatus

Cylindrolobus truncatus, commonly known as the round-leafed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, clustered leaves that are often rounded at the tips. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
