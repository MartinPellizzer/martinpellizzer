Centaurea Belangeriana

Common centaury, scientifically known as Centaurea belangeriana, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its bright blue or purple flowers and spiny leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in rocky and dry environments, making it a hardy species well adapted to harsh conditions. Often found in meadows and along coastal areas, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its unique beauty and resilience make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
