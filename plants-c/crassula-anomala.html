Crassula anomala

Crassula anomala, commonly known as the Jade Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically oval or egg-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring or early summer. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Crassula anomala is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
