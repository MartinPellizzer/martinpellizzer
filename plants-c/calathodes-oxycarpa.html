It is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in inflorescences, and are usually pink or white in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds, which are small and often covered in a sticky substance.
