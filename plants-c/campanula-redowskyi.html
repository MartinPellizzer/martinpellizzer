Campanula redowskyi

Campanula redowskyi, commonly known as the Redowsky bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Campanula redowskyi is valued in gardens for its attractive blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
