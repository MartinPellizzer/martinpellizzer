Chrysanthemum anomalum

Chrysanthemum anomalum, commonly known as the mountain chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its foliage is dark green and finely serrated, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
