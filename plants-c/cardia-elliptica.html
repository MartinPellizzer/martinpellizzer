Cardia elliptica

Cardia elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical cardia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.
