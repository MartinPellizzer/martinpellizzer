Cryptocarya laevis

Cryptocarya laevis, commonly known as the smooth cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Sri Lanka, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Cryptocarya laevis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
