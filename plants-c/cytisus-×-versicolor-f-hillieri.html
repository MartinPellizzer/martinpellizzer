Cytisus × versicolor f. hillieri

Cytisus × versicolor f. hillieri, commonly known as the Hillieri's broom, is a hybrid shrub characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange floral display.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is native to the British Isles. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer, are clustered in racemes and are notable for their vibrant color and fragrance.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its showy blooms and hardy nature.
