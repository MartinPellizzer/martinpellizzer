Cattleya aclandiae var. salmonea

Cattleya aclandiae var. salmonea, commonly known as the Salmon Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, salmon-pink flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features broad, dark green leaves and produces large, showy blooms with a distinctive color gradient. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.

This variety is often used in horticultural displays and requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture for optimal growth.
