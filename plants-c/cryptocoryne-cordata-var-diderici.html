Cryptocoryne cordata var. diderici

Cryptocoryne cordata var. diderici, commonly known as the Diderici cryptocoryne, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color, often with a slight reddish or brownish tint along the edges. The plant is well adapted to low-light conditions and can thrive in a variety of water conditions, making it a popular choice for aquariums. It is known for its ability to oxygenate water and provide shelter for small aquatic life.

This variety is also valued for its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance in freshwater environments.
