Chrysopogon festucoides

Chrysopogon festucoides, commonly known as slender woodoat, is a perennial grass native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland margins. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and habitat restoration.
