Calamus microsphaerion

Calamus microsphaerion, commonly known as the dwarf calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and has slender, creeping rhizomes that produce small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of streams.
