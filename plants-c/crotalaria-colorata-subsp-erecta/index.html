Crotalaria Colorata Subsp. Erecta

Crotalaria colorata subsp. erecta is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant yellow blooms and unique leaf structure. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm climates with ample sunlight. This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for garden beds and containers. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds, requiring caution during handling and consumption.
