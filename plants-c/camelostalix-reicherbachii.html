Camelostalix reicherbachii

Camelostalix reicherbachii, commonly known as the Reichert's broom.

It is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and bloom in the spring.

Camelostalix reicherbachii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
