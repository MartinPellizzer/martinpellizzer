Cyclopogon furculus

Cyclopogon furculus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cyclopogon, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green flowers. It typically grows in moist, sandy soils in coastal areas.
