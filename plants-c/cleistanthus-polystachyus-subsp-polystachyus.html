Cleistanthus polystachyus subsp. polystachyus

Cleistanthus polystachyus subsp.

polystachyus, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its showy, red-orange flower clusters that appear in late summer.

The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it is often used in landscaping and reforestation projects.
