Crassuvia

Crassuvia, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that resemble bells, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its bright, cheerful blooms.
