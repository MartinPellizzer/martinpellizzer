Clusia guatemalensis

Clusia guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan glorybower, is a shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, glossy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and floral display.

Clusia guatemalensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
