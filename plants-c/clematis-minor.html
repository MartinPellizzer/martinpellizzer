Clematis minor

Clematis minor, commonly known as the small clematis, is a deciduous vine native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, twining stems that can climb to heights of several meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple during late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Clematis minor thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
