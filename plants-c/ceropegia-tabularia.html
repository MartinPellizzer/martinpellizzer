Ceropegia tabularia

Ceropegia tabularia, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Ceropegia tabularia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
