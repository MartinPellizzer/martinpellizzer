Centaurea semiusta

Centaurea semiusta, commonly known as the slender star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
