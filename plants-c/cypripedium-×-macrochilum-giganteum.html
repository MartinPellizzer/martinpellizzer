Cypripedium × macrochilum giganteum

Cypripedium × macrochilum giganteum, commonly known as the giant yellow lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow pouch-shaped flower with a distinctive green lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich soil. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and rarity in cultivation.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered in the wild.
