Chrysocephalum apiculatum subsp. odorum

Chrysocephalum apiculatum subsp.

odorum, commonly known as the sweet everlasting or golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers.

The plant is evergreen, with hairy, aromatic leaves that emit a pleasant scent. It typically grows in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
