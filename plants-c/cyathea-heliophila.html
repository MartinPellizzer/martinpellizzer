Cyathea heliophila

Cyathea heliophila, commonly known as the sun-loving tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with rich, well-draining soil. Its fronds are typically bright green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Cyathea heliophila is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its unique, elegant appearance.
