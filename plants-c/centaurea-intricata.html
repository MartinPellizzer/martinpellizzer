Centaurea intricata

Centaurea intricata, commonly known as the intricate centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
