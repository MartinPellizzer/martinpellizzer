Centaurea uniflora var. nervosa

Centaurea uniflora var. nervosa, commonly known as the nervy slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are narrow and often have prominent veins, giving the plant its varietal name "nervosa.". It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
