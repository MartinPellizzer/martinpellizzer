Callianthemum sachalinense subsp. kirigishiense

Callianthemum sachalinense subsp.

kirigishiense, commonly known as the Kirigishi sand anemone, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the coastal regions of eastern Russia. It typically forms dense mats of foliage and produces small, white to pale pink flowers in early spring.

The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh coastal environments. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes.
