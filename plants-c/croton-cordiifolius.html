Croton cordiifolius

Croton cordiifolius, commonly known as the heartleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
