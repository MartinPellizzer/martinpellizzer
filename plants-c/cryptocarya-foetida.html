Cryptocarya foetida

Cryptocarya foetida, commonly known as the stinking laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to eastern Australia and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, dark berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The leaves contain essential oils and have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties.
