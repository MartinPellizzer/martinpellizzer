Coelogyne primulina

Coelogyne primulina, commonly known as the Primrose Orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne primulina is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
