Cotula maniototo

Cotula maniototo, commonly known as the Maniototo daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in open, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to pale orange flowers.

Cotula maniototo is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate.
