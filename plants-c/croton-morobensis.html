Croton morobensis

Croton morobensis, commonly known as the Morobe croton, is a tropical shrub native to the island of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright yellow to deep red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
