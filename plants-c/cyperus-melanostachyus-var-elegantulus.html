Cyperus melanostachyus var. elegantulus

Cyperus melanostachyus var. elegantulus, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in dense, grassy clumps. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets, which are often clustered at the tips of its stems. It thrives in wetland environments, including marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common species in aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
