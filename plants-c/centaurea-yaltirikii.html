Centaurea yaltirikii

Centaurea yaltirikii, commonly known as the Yaltirikii knapweed, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually purple or pink, with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Centaurea yaltirikii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
