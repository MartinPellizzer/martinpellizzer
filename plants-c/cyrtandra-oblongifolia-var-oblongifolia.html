Cyrtandra oblongifolia var. oblongifolia

Cyrtandra oblongifolia var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the oblong-leaved cyrtandra, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
