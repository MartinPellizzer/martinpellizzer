Cattleya labiata var. ernestii

Cattleya labiata var. ernestii, commonly known as the Ernestii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It features elongated, pendulous blooms with a distinctive coloration of white and pink, often adorned with a deep red lip. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining potting media and consistent moisture during its growing season.

The variety is celebrated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
