Commicarpus adenensis

Commicarpus adenensis, commonly known as the Cape York burr.

It is a flowering plant native to northern Australia, particularly found in the Cape York Peninsula. The plant produces distinctive woody fruits that are covered in hooked bracts, which aid in seed dispersal by attaching to animals. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

The species is characterized by its low-growing habit and small, narrow leaves.
