Cordia bahiensis

Cordia bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia cordia, is a small evergreen tree native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the Bahamas and Cuba.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by fleshy, orange-red fruits that contain several seeds.
