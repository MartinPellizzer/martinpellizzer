Chirita lachenensis

Chirita lachenensis, commonly known as the Lachen's gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or red. The plant has ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Chirita lachenensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under suitable conditions.
