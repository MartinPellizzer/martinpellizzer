Croton sublucidus

Croton sublucidus, commonly known as the glossy croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that range from deep green to bright yellow and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Croton sublucidus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.
