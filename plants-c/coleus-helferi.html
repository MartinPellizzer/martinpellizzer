Coleus helferi

Coleus helferi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Coleus helferi is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
