Clintonia angustifolia

Clintonia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf Clintonia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers. Clintonia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in forest ecosystems.
