Cattleya purpurata var. russelliana

Cattleya purpurata var. russelliana, commonly known as the Russell's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, velvety petals and a prominent lip, often in shades of pink, purple, and white. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It requires well-draining potting mix and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.

The plant is known for its strong fragrance and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
