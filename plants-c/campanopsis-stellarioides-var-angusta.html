Campanopsis stellarioides var. angusta

Campanopsis stellarioides var. angusta, commonly known as narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and showy, bell-like blooms.
