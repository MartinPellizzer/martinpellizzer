Crassula obvallata

Crassula obvallata, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that form a dense rosette, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Crassula obvallata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
