Chlorophytum latifolium

Chlorophytum latifolium, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with white markings. The plant produces small white flowers in clusters, which are followed by green, berry-like fruits. It is a hardy species that thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Chlorophytum latifolium is also known for its ability to purify indoor air and is often used as a houseplant.
