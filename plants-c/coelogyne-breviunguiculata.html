Coelogyne breviunguiculata

Coelogyne breviunguiculata, commonly known as the short-tongued coelogyne, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to white, with a short, narrow spur.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
