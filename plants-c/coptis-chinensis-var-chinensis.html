Coptis chinensis var. chinensis

Coptis chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese goldthread, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and has dark green, trifoliate leaves. Its medicinal value is primarily derived from the alkaloids present in its rhizomes.

It is widely used in traditional Chinese medicine for its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
