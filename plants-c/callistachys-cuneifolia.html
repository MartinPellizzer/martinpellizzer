Callistachys cuneifolia

Callistachys cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly serrated. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or scrub habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden planting in suitable climates.
