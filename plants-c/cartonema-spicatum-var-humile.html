Cartonema spicatum var. humile

Cartonema spicatum var. humile, commonly known as the small-flowered cartonema, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It typically features slender, branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often arranged oppositely along the stems.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
