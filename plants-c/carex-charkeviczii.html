Carex charkeviczii

Carex charkeviczii, commonly known as the Charkevicz sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies. Carex charkeviczii is a hardy perennial that thrives in temperate climates.
