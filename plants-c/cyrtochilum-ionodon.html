Cyrtochilum ionodon

Cyrtochilum ionodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit, featuring a short rhizome and a few leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a spike, often with a slight fragrance. Cyrtochilum ionodon thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to orchid collections, particularly for its subtle appearance and specific cultivation needs.
