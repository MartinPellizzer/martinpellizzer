Cyathea callejasii

Cyathea callejasii, commonly known as the Calleja's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height, with a slow growth rate. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support, while its fronds are typically bipinnate, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Cyathea callejasii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
