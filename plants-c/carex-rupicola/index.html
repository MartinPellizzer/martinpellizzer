Carex Rupicola

Carex rupicola is a unique sedge species known for its ability to thrive in rocky and dry environments. It is commonly found in alpine and arctic regions where few plants can survive. This plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like flower clusters. Its resilience makes it an important part of its ecosystem. Despite its hardiness, it remains a rare and intriguing subject for botanical study.
