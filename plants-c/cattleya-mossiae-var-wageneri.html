Cattleya mossiae var. wageneri

Cattleya mossiae var. wageneri, commonly known as the Wagener's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that originates from Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a vibrant pink to red coloration and a white or pale pink lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad, dark green, and often have a waxy texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
