Cytisanthus hassertianus subsp. glabratus

Cytisanthus hassertianus subsp.

glabratus, commonly known as the smooth-leaved cytisanthus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact shrub form and glossy, dark green leaves that are smooth to the touch.

The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
