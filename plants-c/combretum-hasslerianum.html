Combretum hasslerianum

Combretum hasslerianum, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in dense clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
