Cremobotrys zebrina

Cremobotrys zebrina, commonly known as the zebra vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a zebra-like pattern of light and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often clustered in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires support for its vining growth habit.

Cremobotrys zebrina is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
