Cymbopogon gidarba

Cymbopogon gidarba, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray blades and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It is used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in full sun.

Cymbopogon gidarba is also known for its aromatic foliage, which can be used in essential oil production.
