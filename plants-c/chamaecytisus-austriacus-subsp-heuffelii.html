Chamaecytisus austriacus subsp. heuffelii

Chamaecytisus austriacus subsp.

heuffelii, commonly known as the Austrian Broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the Alps of Central Europe. It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant has leathery, evergreen leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

This subspecies is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh alpine conditions.
