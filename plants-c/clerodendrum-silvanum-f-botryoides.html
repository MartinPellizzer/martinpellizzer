Clerodendrum silvanum f. botryoides

Clerodendrum silvanum f. botryoides, commonly known as the clustered Clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, branched inflorescences that bear small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin and emits a mild, sweet fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
