Conyza viguieri

Conyza viguieri, commonly known as the "Cape ironweed," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads.

Conyza viguieri is known for its adaptability to arid conditions and is often found in disturbed soils.
