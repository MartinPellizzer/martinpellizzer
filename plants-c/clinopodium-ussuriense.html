Clinopodium ussuriense

Clinopodium ussuriense, commonly known as Ussuri Mint, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by aromatic foliage and square stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a minty aroma when crushed.

Clinopodium ussuriense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
