Caroxylon brevifolium

Caroxylon brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved caroxylon, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and woody stems, which are adapted to its arid and semi-arid habitats.
