Carex liangshanensis

Carex liangshanensis, commonly known as the Liangshan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Liangshan region, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown, arranged in spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streams.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
