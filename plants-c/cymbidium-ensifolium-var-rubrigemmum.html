Cymbidium ensifolium var. rubrigemmum

Cymbidium ensifolium var. rubrigemmum, commonly known as the red-flowered sword orchid, is a striking orchid native to China and Korea.

It is characterized by its long, sword-like leaves and vibrant red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forested areas. Its flowers are usually borne on tall, upright stems and can last for several weeks.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
