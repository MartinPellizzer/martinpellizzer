Cryptantha intermedia var. dumetorum

Cryptantha intermedia var. dumetorum, commonly known as the desert flax or sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing, perennial herb with hairy stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.

Cryptantha intermedia var. dumetorum plays a role in stabilizing soil in desert environments and provides nectar for pollinators.
