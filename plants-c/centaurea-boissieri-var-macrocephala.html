Centaurea boissieri var. macrocephala

Centaurea boissieri var. macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, sparsely branched stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces showy, bright yellow to orange-red flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters at the ends of the stems. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear to lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color.

This species is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
