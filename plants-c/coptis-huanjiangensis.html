Coptis huanjiangensis

Coptis huanjiangensis, commonly known as the Huanjiang goldthread, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and has medicinal properties due to the presence of alkaloids. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both ecological and pharmacological research.
