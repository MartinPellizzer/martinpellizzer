Crinum × paxtonii

Crinum × paxtonii, commonly known as the hybrid crinum, is a flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Crinum bulbisignum and Crinum candidum, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. The plant produces large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its bulbs are bulbous and can be used for propagation.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
