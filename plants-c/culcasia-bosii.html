Culcasia bosii

Culcasia bosii, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a member of the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to three meters in length. The plant produces striking yellowish-white spadices surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of its family. Its rhizomes are edible and are often used in traditional cuisines for their starchy texture.

Culcasia bosii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
