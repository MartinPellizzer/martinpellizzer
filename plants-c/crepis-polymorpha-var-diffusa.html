Crepis polymorpha var. diffusa

Crepis polymorpha var. diffusa, commonly known as the hairy goat's beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

This variant is distinguished by its more diffuse growth habit compared to other forms of Crepis polymorpha.
