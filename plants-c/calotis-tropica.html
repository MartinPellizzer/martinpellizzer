Calotis tropica

Calotis tropica, commonly known as the tropical starbursts, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Calotis tropica is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
