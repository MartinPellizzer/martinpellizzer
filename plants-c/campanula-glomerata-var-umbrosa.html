Campanula glomerata var. umbrosa

Campanula glomerata var. umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers, typically in shades of blue or purple. The plant thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments, making it well-suited for woodland gardens. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence.

Campanula glomerata var. umbrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shady garden spaces.
