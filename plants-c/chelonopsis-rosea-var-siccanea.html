Chelonopsis rosea var. siccanea

Chelonopsis rosea var. siccanea, commonly known as the Siccanea rosea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
