Cypripedium × nitens

Cypripedium × nitens, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family with distinctive floral morphology.

It features a large, showy pouch-shaped lip that is typically pink or purple, with a contrasting throat, and is adorned with intricate patterns. The plant is native to eastern Asia, particularly China and Japan, where it thrives in moist, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating to retain moisture.

Cypripedium × nitens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
