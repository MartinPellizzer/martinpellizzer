Carludovica lancifolia

Carludovica lancifolia, commonly known as the sword leaf cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that are often ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are long and narrow, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
