Crotalaria medicaginea

Crotalaria medicaginea, commonly known as the velvetleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a valuable cover crop in agricultural systems. It thrives in warm, moist environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

However, it can become invasive in some areas, posing challenges to native plant communities.
