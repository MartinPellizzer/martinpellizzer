Calamagrostis hakonensis var. koidzumiana

Calamagrostis hakonensis var. koidzumiana, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-tipped foliage that creates a striking visual effect in gardens. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage remains ornamental throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

It prefers well-drained soil and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
