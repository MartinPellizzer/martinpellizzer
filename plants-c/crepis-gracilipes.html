Crepis gracilipes

Crepis gracilipes, commonly known as slender mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. Crepis gracilipes is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered a common weed in some regions.
