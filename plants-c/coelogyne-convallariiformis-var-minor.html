Coelogyne convallariiformis var. minor

Coelogyne convallariiformis var. minor, commonly known as the lesser white orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect pseudobulbs and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with white or pale pink flowers that have a subtle fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
