Catasetum macrocarpum

Catasetum macrocarpum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, with vibrant colors ranging from yellow to orange. The plant produces unique, elongated pollinia that are transferred to the pollinators during its specialized pollination process. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Catasetum macrocarpum is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
