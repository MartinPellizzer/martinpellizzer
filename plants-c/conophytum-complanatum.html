Conophytum complanatum

Conophytum complanatum, commonly known as the flat-topped stone plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped body with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically flat and bluish-green, with a waxy surface to reduce water loss.

Conophytum complanatum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
