Cyperus oerstedii

Cyperus oerstedii, commonly known as the narrowleaf paperplant, is a flowering plant in the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and cylindrical flower clusters. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread vegetatively, making it a common species in wetland ecosystems.

Cyperus oerstedii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various water conditions.
