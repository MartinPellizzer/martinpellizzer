Calycothrix plumulosa

Calycothrix plumulosa, commonly known as the featherleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests or undergrowth. Its unique morphology includes hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that help it retain moisture.

Calycothrix plumulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
