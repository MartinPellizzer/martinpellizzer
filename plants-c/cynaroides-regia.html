Cynaroides regia

Cynaroides regia, commonly known as the royal thistle, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid conditions. Its flowers are composite, consisting of both ray and disk florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cynaroides regia is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
