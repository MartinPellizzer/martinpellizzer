Cynanchum pusilum

Cynanchum pusilum, commonly known as small milkweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a member of the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and features showy, yellowish-orange flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy coating.

Cynanchum pusilum produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
