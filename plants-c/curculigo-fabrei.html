Curculigo fabrei

Curculigo fabrei, commonly known as the Chinese star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a short rhizome with a single, fleshy, erect stem that bears a few lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in a spiral pattern and are characterized by their star-like appearance, with a distinctive central column.

Curculigo fabrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
