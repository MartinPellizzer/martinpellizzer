Corytholoma × donkelaeriana

Corytholoma × donkelaeriana, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is well adapted to full sun. Its botanical features include small, leathery leaves and a compact growth habit.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
