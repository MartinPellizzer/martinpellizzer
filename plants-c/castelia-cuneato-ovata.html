Castelia cuneato-ovata

Castelia cuneato-ovata, commonly known as the narrow-ovate castelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and spikes of small, tubular flowers.
