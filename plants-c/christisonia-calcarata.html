Christisonia calcarata

Christisonia calcarata, commonly known as the rock spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and thick, waxy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a cup-like structure. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.
