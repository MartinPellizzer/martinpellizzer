Cestrum amictum var. organense

Cestrum amictum var. organense, commonly known as the Orange Beauty Cestrum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly glossy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
