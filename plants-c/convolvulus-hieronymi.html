Convolvulus hieronymi

Convolvulus hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymus bindweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing herb that produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain areas.
