Coelogyne Glumacea

Coelogyne glumacea is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms, it is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists. This species is characterized by its unique, pendant-like flowers that come in shades of yellow and orange. Due to its beauty and scarcity, it is often sought after in the plant trade. Proper care and cultivation are essential to ensure its survival in both natural and cultivated environments.
