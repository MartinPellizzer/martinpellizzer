Cistus × florentinus

Cistus × florentinus, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes various species of evergreen shrubs characterized by their aromatic foliage and showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of pink or white tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are leathery and covered in fine hairs, and it is well adapted to dry, rocky soils.

Cistus × florentinus is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
