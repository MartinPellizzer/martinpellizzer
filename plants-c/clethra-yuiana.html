Clethra yuiana

Clethra yuiana, commonly known as the Chinese sweet pepper, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese cuisine. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Clethra yuiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and culinary uses in its native habitat.
