Celastrus yunnanensis

Celastrus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan staff-vine, is a climbing shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Celastraceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
