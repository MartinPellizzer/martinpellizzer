Chamaecrista paraunana

Chamaecrista paraunana, commonly known as the Brazilian burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed or open habitats. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and it produces small, spiny pods that contain seeds.
