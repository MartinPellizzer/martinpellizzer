Cotoneaster congestus

Cotoneaster congestus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Cotoneaster congestus is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

It is often used in landscaping for its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
