Coelogyne huettneriana

Coelogyne huettneriana, commonly known as the Huettner's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often in clusters. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Coelogyne huettneriana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
