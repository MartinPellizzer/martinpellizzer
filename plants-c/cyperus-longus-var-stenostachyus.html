Cyperus longus var. stenostachyus

Cyperus longus var. stenostachyus, commonly known as narrow-stalked flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, such as along waterways or in flooded soils.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
