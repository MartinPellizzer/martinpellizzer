Crassula Atropurpurea Var. Anomala

Crassula atropurpurea var. anomala is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant purple foliage and compact growth habit, it is a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. This variety is often mistaken for its parent species due to its similar appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for indoor or outdoor gardens. Its unique coloration and ease of care have made it a favorite in modern plant collections.
