Cacalia scabrifoliata

Cacalia scabrifoliata, commonly known as the rough-leaved cacalia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in dry, rocky environments. It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.
