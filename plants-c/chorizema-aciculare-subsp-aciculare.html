Chorizema aciculare subsp. aciculare

Chorizema aciculare subsp.

aciculare, commonly known as the acicular chorizema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that are arranged in dense clusters.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or white in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
