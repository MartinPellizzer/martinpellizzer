Centaurea takredensis

Centaurea takredensis, commonly known as the Takred Mountain Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Centaurea takredensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
