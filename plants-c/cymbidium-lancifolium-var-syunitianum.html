Cymbidium lancifolium var. syunitianum

Cymbidium lancifolium var. syunitianum, commonly known as the Syunitian orchid, is a species native to southern China and northern Vietnam.

It is a sympodial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring long, narrow leaves and pendulous inflorescences. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a red or orange lip, and they bloom during the winter months. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

It requires well-drained soil and bright, indirect light to thrive.
