Crepinella Pallens

Crepinella pallens is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It is known for its small, pale yellow blooms that appear in early spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive. Its unique beauty makes it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
