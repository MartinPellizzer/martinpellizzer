Cymbidium evrardii

Cymbidium evrardii, commonly known as the Evrard's orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China and Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces tall, upright stems with clusters of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers. The blossoms typically range in color from white to pale yellow, with some varieties displaying red or purple markings. This orchid thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
