Chylismia arenaria

Chylismia arenaria, commonly known as the sand dune lily, is a flowering plant native to arid and coastal regions.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and delicate, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in late summer. Chylismia arenaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in coastal ecosystems.
