Carex diaoluoshanica

Carex diaoluoshanica, commonly known as the Diaoluo Shan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan province. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
