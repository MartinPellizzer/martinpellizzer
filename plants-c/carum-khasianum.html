Carum khasianum

Carum khasianum, commonly known as Kashmiri caraway, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley. The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves and small white or greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels. Its seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a spice in local cuisines.
