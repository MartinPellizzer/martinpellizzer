Celmisia pellita

Celmisia pellita, commonly known as the slender celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Celmisia pellita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
