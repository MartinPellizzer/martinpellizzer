Cycnoches stenodactylon

Cycnoches stenodactylon, commonly known as the narrow-lobed cycnoches, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. The plant features long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to regions of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
