Chrysanthemum santolina

Chrysanthemum santolina, commonly known as santolina, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Santolina is known for its hardy nature, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
