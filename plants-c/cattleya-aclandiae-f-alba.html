Cattleya aclandiae f. alba

Cattleya aclandiae f. alba, commonly known as the White Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous fragrant, white blooms that often have a slight pinkish hue. It requires bright, indirect light and a humid environment to thrive.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
