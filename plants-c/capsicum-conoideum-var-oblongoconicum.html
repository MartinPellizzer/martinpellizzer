Capsicum conoideum var. oblongoconicum

Capsicum conoideum var. oblongoconicum, commonly known as the oblong-fruited chili pepper, is a variant of the broad-leafed pepper native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, oblong-shaped fruits that typically range from 5 to 10 centimeters in length. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange fruits that mature to a deep red, often used in both culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in warm, sunny climates with well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and moderate fertilization.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor profile and ornamental appeal in gardens.
