Corydalis adoxifolia

Corydalis adoxifolia, commonly known as the Chinese larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from purple to pink, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in woodland habitats and prefers partial shade with moist, well-drained soil.

Corydalis adoxifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ecological benefits to pollinators.
