Cliffortia × integerrima

Cliffortia × integerrima, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a hybrid shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and mountainous environments. It is often found in fynbos vegetation and is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.

Cliffortia × integerrima is also known for its ability to thrive in full sun and its resistance to coastal conditions.
