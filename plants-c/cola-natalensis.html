Cola natalensis

Cola natalensis, commonly known as Natal cola, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to South Africa, particularly found in the eastern regions of the country. The plant is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and produces clusters of red or yellow fruits. The seeds within the fruits are encased in a hard, woody shell.
