Crotalaria jijigensis

Crotalaria jijigensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

However, some parts of the plant contain toxic alkaloids, requiring caution in handling and use.
