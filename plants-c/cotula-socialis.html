Cotula socialis

Cotula socialis, commonly known as the social cotula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Cotula socialis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
