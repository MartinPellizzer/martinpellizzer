Clitoria steyermarkii

Clitoria steyermarkii, commonly known as the purple orchid bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.

The leaves are trifoliate, and the plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
