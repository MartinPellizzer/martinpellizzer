Carex ledebouriana subsp. ledebouriana

Carex ledebouriana subsp.

ledebouriana, commonly known as the Ledebour's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arctic and alpine regions of the Northern Hemisphere. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known for their rhizomatous growth and spike-like inflorescences.
