Cotyledon mollis

Cotyledon mollis, commonly known as the smooth cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Cotyledon mollis prefers well-draining soil and full sunlight to thrive.
