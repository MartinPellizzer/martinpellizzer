Citrullus lanatus var. vulgaris

Citrullus lanatus var. vulgaris, commonly known as watermelon, is a vine plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit, hairy stems, and large, lobed leaves. The plant produces green, ridged fruits that mature to a vibrant red or yellow color, depending on the variety. The fruit contains a high water content, with a sweet, juicy flesh that is often seeded.

It is widely cultivated for its refreshing flavor and nutritional value.
