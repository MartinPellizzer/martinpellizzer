Crotalaria callensii

Crotalaria callensii, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its seeds are often used in ecological restoration due to their ability to suppress weed growth.
