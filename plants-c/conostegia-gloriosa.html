Conostegia gloriosa

Conostegia gloriosa, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Conostegia gloriosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
