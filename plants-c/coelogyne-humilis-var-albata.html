Coelogyne humilis var. albata

Coelogyne humilis var. albata, commonly known as the white-coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
