Cota sanctijohannis

Cota sanctijohannis, commonly known as the sacred cowplant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in regions with mild temperatures and well-drained soil.
