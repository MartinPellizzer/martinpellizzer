Crotalaria polytricha

Crotalaria polytricha, commonly known as the hairy crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny pods and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in racemose inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Crotalaria polytricha is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
