Clematis yui

Clematis yui, commonly known as the Japanese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to Japan.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis yui is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
