Cephaelis ruelliifolia

Cephaelis ruelliifolia, commonly known as the pink flame or flame violet, is a herbaceous plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in shaded, forested areas and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Cephaelis ruelliifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
