Cyathea patellaris

Cyathea patellaris, commonly known as the dwarf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compact stature, typically reaching heights of less than two meters. The plant features a short, stout trunk covered in remnants of old fronds, with new fronds emerging from the crown. Its fronds are pinnate, with a dark green, leathery texture and a distinctive, glossy appearance.

Cyathea patellaris thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests and coastal areas.
