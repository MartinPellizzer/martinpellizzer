Conyza abyssinica

Conyza abyssinica, commonly known as African ironweed, is an annual herb native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
