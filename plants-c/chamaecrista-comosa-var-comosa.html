Chamaecrista comosa var. comosa

Chamaecrista comosa var. comosa, commonly known as the silky senna, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in open woodlands or along roadsides.
