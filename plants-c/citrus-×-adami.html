Citrus × adami

Citrus × adami, commonly known as the kaffir lime.

This hybrid citrus plant is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its fragrant, wrinkled green fruit with a distinct aromatic zest. The leaves are dark green and slightly serrated, while the flowers are white and fragrant, typical of many citrus species. The fruit is often used in cooking for its intense flavor and essential oils.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and culinary applications.
