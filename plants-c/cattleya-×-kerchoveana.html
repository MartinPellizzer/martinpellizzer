Cattleya × kerchoveana

Cattleya × kerchoveana, commonly known as the Kerchove's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant typically features upright, cane-like pseudobulbs and long, strap-shaped leaves. Its flowers are large, with a prominent lip and a range of colors including pink, red, and white.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
