Chamaecyparis Lawsoniana F. Nana

Chamaecyparis lawsoniana f. nana is a compact variant of the Lawson cypress known for its slow growth and dense foliage. This dwarf form is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden settings. It features a rounded shape with small, scale-like leaves that give it a unique, textured appearance. The plant is often used in rock gardens or as a container specimen due to its manageable size. Its resilience to cold and moderate drought makes it a versatile choice for landscaping.
