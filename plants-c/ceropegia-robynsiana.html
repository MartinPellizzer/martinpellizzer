Ceropegia robynsiana

Ceropegia robynsiana, commonly known as the Robyn's string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent species that features fleshy, trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
