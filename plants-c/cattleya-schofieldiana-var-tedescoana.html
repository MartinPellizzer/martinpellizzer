Cattleya schofieldiana var. tedescoana

Cattleya schofieldiana var. tedescoana, commonly known as the Tedescoana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant and showy flowers.

It features large, pendulous blooms with a mix of colors, often including shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant is native to Brazil and thrives in warm, humid climates with bright indirect light. It has long, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant flowers that bloom during the summer months.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
