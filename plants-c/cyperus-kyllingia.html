Cyperus kyllingia

Cyperus kyllingia, commonly known as the pink flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Cyperaceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically borne on slender, upright stems. It thrives in wet or marshy habitats and is often found in coastal areas.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in suitable environments.
