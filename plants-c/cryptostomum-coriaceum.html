Cryptostomum coriaceum

Cryptostomum coriaceum, commonly known as the coriaceous cryptostomum, is a small, perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a compact rosette of broad, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color, and appear in clusters during the spring.

Cryptostomum coriaceum is valued for its ornamental foliage and its ability to thrive in shaded garden settings.
