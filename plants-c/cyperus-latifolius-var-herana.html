Cyperus latifolius var. herana

Cyperus latifolius var. herana, commonly known as the broadleaf flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically broad and flat, with a dark green coloration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are usually clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes, ponds, and along the edges of water bodies.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it an important species in aquatic ecosystems.
