Coelogyne asperata

Coelogyne asperata, commonly known as the rough-coated orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with red or brown markings, and they bloom during the late winter to early spring.

Coelogyne asperata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
