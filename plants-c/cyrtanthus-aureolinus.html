Cyrtanthus aureolinus

Cyrtanthus aureolinus, commonly known as the golden rain lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive golden hue. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It blooms in spring and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Cyrtanthus aureolinus is also valued for its resilience and adaptability to various growing conditions.
