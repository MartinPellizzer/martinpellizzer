Callistemon Megalongensis

Callistemon megalongensis is a rare and unique plant native to the coastal regions of northern Australia. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and other aromatic shrubs. This species is known for its striking red flowers that appear in winter, making it a standout in its ecosystem. Its habitat is limited to specific sand dune environments, which makes it highly vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the face of climate change and land development.
