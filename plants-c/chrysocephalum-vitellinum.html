Chrysocephalum vitellinum

Chrysocephalum vitellinum, commonly known as the yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble daisies. The plant produces small, cylindrical flower heads that are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature. Its botanical features include narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit.
