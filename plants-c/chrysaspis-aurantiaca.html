Chrysaspis aurantiaca

Chrysaspis aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Chrysaspis aurantiaca is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
