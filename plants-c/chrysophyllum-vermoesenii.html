Chrysophyllum vermoesenii

Chrysophyllum vermoesenii, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers.
