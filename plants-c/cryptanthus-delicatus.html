Cryptanthus delicatus

Cryptanthus delicatus, commonly known as the "Rainbow Cranesbill," is a member of the Geraniaceae family and is native to Brazil.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features dark green, velvety leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. Its flowers are arranged in dense, upright clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Cryptanthus delicatus thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
