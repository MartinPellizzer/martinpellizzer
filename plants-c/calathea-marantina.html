Calathea marantina

Calathea marantina, commonly known as the black magic plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its striking, dark green, velvety foliage with intricate patterns. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with contrasting white or silver veins. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Calathea marantina is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
