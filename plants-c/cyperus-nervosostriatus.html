Cyperus nervosostriatus

Cyperus nervosostriatus, commonly known as the narrow-striated flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats. The plant has slender, grass-like stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, three-lobed flowers, which are usually pale green to brown in color.

Cyperus nervosostriatus is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils.
