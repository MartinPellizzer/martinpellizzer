Cymbidium canaliculatum var. barrettii

Cymbidium canaliculatum var. barrettii, commonly known as the Barrett's cymbidium, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of fragrant, dark violet to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. It thrives in cool, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
