Chamaecrista lineata var. clarensis

Chamaecrista lineata var. clarensis, commonly known as the narrowleaf burr legume, is a shrub native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It features small, yellow,蝶形花 (butterfly-shaped) flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant has compound leaves with pinnate structure, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species adapted to dry conditions.

The seeds are encased in a hard, spiny pod, which aids in seed dispersal by attaching to animal fur or clothing.
