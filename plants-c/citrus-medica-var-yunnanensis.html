Citrus medica var. yunnanensis

Citrus medica var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan citrus or Yunnan lemon, is a variety of the citron plant native to China.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange fruits with a thick, aromatic rind. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is used in traditional Chinese medicine and for its essential oils, which are valued for their fragrance and medicinal properties.
