Chrysojasminum fruticans

Chrysojasminum fruticans, commonly known as the golden jasmine shrub, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, providing a lush backdrop for its showy blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
