Cistus clusii var. fastigiatus

Cistus clusii var. fastigiatus, commonly known as the upright rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
