Cynorkis tryphioides var. leandriana

Cynorkis tryphioides var. leandriana, commonly known as the Cape fuchsia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to red, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
