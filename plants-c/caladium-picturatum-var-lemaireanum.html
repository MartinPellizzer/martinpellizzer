Caladium picturatum var. lemaireanum

Caladium picturatum var. lemaireanum, commonly known as the Lemaire's painted leaf caladium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which features intricate patterns of red, purple, and white on heart-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its foliage remains the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.
