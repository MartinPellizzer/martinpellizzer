Cardionema rosetta

Cardionema rosetta, commonly known as the rock rose.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, compact rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
