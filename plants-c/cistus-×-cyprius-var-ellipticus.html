Cistus × cyprius var. ellipticus

Cistus × cyprius var. ellipticus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rockrose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has leathery, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its hardy nature and ability to attract pollinators.
