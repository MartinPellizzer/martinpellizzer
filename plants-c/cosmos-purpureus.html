Cosmos purpureus

Cosmos purpureus, commonly known as the purple cosmos, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of pink to deep purple, which bloom throughout the summer months. The plant has feathery, bipinnate leaves that are finely divided, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Cosmos purpureus is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
