Chamaecytisus austriacus var. stefanoffii

Chamaecytisus austriacus var. stefanoffii, commonly known as the Stefanoff's rock rose, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has wiry, thornless stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and evergreen, providing year-round interest.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and dry, well-drained soils.
