Ceratostylis dischorensis

Ceratostylis dischorensis, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a slender, upright stem and a single, narrow spike of flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale green, with a distinctive, elongated lip that is often curved. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant has a short flowering period, usually in the summer months.
