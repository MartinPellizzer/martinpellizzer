It is characterized by its round, velvety leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a short, creeping stem and produces capsule fruits containing numerous small seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
