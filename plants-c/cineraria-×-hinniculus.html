Cineraria × hinniculus

Cineraria × hinniculus, commonly known as the hybrid cineraria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to violet. Native to the Canary Islands, this plant thrives in coastal areas with well-drained soil and full sun exposure. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and resilience to coastal conditions.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
