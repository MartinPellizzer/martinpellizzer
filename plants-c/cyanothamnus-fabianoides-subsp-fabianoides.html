Cyanothamnus fabianoides subsp. fabianoides

Cyanothamnus fabianoides subsp.

fabianoides, commonly known as the Fabian's bluebush, is a small shrub native to Western Australia. It features dense, compact growth with blue to purple flower clusters that bloom in spring.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs. The plant is adapted to arid conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
