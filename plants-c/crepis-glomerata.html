Crepis glomerata

Crepis glomerata, commonly known as the clustered mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Crepis glomerata is often found in disturbed habitats and can be an indicator of soil disturbance or agricultural activity.
