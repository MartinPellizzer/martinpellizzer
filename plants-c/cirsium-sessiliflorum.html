Cirsium sessiliflorum

Cirsium sessiliflorum, commonly known as the sessileflower thistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas. The plant features a tall, spiny stem with gray-green, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are purple to pink, forming a composite head of many small florets.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
