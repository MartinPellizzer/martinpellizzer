Clerodendrum discolor var. rubricalyx

Clerodendrum discolor var. rubricalyx, commonly known as the red-leaf clerodendrum, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and green variegated leaves, which add visual interest to gardens. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine, though its ornamental value is its most notable feature.
