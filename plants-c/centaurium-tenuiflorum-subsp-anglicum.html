Centaurium tenuiflorum subsp. anglicum

Centaurium tenuiflorum subsp.

anglicum, commonly known as English narrowflower centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to western Europe. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in grasslands, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, linear to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense umbels, and are typically pinkish to reddish in color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
