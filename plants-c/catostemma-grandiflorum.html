Catostemma grandiflorum

Catostemma grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant flame vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its long, woody stems and large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and emits a strong, pleasant fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Catostemma grandiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
