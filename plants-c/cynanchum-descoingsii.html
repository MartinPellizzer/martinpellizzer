Cynanchum descoingsii

Cynanchum descoingsii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers clustered in umbels, which bloom in the summer months. Its roots are milky and contain a latex-like substance, which is a distinguishing feature.

Cynanchum descoingsii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
