Cyperus sphaerospermoides

Cyperus sphaerospermoides, commonly known as the globe sedge, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its compact, spherical inflorescence, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a dense, mat-forming growth habit.

Cyperus sphaerospermoides is often found in moist, sandy soils near water bodies such as ponds and marshes.
