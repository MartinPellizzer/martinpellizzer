Chenopodium × dadakovae

Chenopodium × dadakovae, commonly known as the hybrid goosefoot, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Chenopodium album and Chenopodium berlandieri, characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields or along roadsides. It produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Chenopodium × dadakovae is an annual herb that can exhibit variability in morphology depending on environmental conditions.
