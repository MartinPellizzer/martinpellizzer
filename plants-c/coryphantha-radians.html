Coryphantha radians

Coryphantha radians, commonly known as the radiant coryphantha, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with rounded, ribbed stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and arid landscapes.
