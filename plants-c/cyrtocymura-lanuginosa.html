Cyrtocymura lanuginosa

Cyrtocymura lanuginosa, commonly known as the woolly cyrtocymura, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky areas. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and a rhizomatous root system.
