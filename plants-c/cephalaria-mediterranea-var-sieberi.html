Cephalaria mediterranea var. sieberi

Cephalaria mediterranea var. sieberi, commonly known as the Mediterranean cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
