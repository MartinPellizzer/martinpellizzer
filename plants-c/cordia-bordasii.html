Cordia bordasii

Cordia bordasii, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that turn red when mature, containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Cordia bordasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
