Clerodendrum villosulum var. debeerstii

Clerodendrum villosulum var. debeerstii, commonly known as the velvet clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
