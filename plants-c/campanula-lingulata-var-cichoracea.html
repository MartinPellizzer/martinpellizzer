Campanula lingulata var. cichoracea

Campanula lingulata var. cichoracea, commonly known as the heartleaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically blue to purple in color. The leaves are heart-shaped and have a distinctive lobed appearance, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
