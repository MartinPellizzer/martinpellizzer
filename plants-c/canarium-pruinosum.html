Canarium pruinosum

Canarium pruinosum, commonly known as the glossy tallow tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that give it a glossy appearance, and its small, fragrant yellow flowers. The tree produces woody capsules that contain oily seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of oil. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
