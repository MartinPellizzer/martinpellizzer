Cattleya nobilior var. hugueneyi

Cattleya nobilior var. hugueneyi, commonly known as the Hugueney's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant features a robust pseudobulb with long, strap-like leaves that are broad and dark green. It blooms during the summer months, producing fragrant, semi-upright flowers that are popular in horticulture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant and lasting blooms.
