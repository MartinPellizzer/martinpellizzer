Centaurea lyrata

Centaurea lyrata, commonly known as the lyrata thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Centaurea lyrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
