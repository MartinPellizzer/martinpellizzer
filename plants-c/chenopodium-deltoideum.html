Chenopodium deltoideum

Chenopodium deltoideum, commonly known as smooth goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features alternate, deltoid-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Chenopodium deltoideum is known for its adaptability and is sometimes considered a weed in cultivated areas.
