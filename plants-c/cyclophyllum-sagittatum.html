Cyclophyllum sagittatum

Cyclophyllum sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead fig, is a tropical plant belonging to the Moraceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round fig-like structure that ripens to a yellowish color.

Cyclophyllum sagittatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is found in warm climates around the world.
