Cymbidium × pauwelsii

Cymbidium × pauwelsii, commonly known as the Pauwelsii orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from various Cymbidium species.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure, which helps it store water and nutrients. The plant produces long, arching inflorescences bearing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant floral display.
