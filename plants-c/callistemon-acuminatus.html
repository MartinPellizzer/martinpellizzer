Callistemon acuminatus

Callistemon acuminatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Callistemon acuminatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscaping.
