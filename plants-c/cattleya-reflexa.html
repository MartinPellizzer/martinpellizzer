Cattleya reflexa

Cattleya reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad, fleshy, and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Cattleya reflexa is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
