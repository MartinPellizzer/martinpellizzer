Callista agrostophylla

Callista agrostophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy.

It is a flowering plant native to Australia, characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or white, with a central disc surrounded by ray florets.

Callista agrostophylla is a perennial species that is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
