Columnea zebranella

Columnea zebranella, commonly known as the zebra columnea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features striking, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange with yellow markings, resembling a zebra's stripes. The plant produces opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Columnea zebranella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for with proper moisture and light conditions.
