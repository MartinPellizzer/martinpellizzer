Crotalaria styracifolia

Crotalaria styracifolia, commonly known as the styracifolia false nectarine, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds.

It is often grown for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
