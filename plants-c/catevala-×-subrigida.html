Catevala × subrigida

Catevala × subrigida, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Catevala and Subrigida species.

It is characterized by its intricate floral structure, featuring showy, colorful petals and a distinct lip that aids in pollination. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticultural enthusiasts.
