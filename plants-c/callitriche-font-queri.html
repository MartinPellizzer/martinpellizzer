Callitriche font-queri

Callitriche font-queri, commonly known as water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Callitrichaceae.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, streams, and ditches. The plant has whorled leaves that are usually less than 1 inch in length, with a slender, branching stem. Its small, white flowers are often clustered at the ends of the branches, and it produces tiny, capsule-like fruits.

Callitriche font-queri is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
