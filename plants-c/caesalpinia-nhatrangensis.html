Caesalpinia nhatrangensis

Caesalpinia nhatrangensis, commonly known as Nhatrang flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange floral bracts that resemble flames, which are highly ornamental. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its striking appearance.

The leaves are pinnate, with multiple leaflets, and the plant has a woody structure.
