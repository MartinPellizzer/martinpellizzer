Ceropegia stenifolia

Ceropegia stenifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and succulent stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Ceropegia stenifolia is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
