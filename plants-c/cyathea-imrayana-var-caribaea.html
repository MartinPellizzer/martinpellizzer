Cyathea imrayana var. caribaea

Cyathea imrayana var. caribaea, commonly known as the Caribaea tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, pinnately compound fronds. The fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to several meters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

It plays a significant ecological role by providing habitat and contributing to forest biodiversity.
