Ceanothus sonomensis

Ceanothus sonomensis, commonly known as the Sonoma ceanothus, is a flowering shrub native to California.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as chaparral and coastal scrub. The plant produces showy clusters of blue to purple flowers in early spring. Its leaves are aromatic and often have a silvery or grayish appearance.

Ceanothus sonomensis plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its native environment.
