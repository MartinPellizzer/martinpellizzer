Calanthe × sandhurstiana

Calanthe × sandhurstiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Calanthe triplicata and Calanthe inopinata.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to China, characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pink or white flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Calanthe × sandhurstiana is valued in horticulture for its beauty and adaptability to garden settings.
