Coppensia rupestris

Coppensia rupestris, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in mountainous regions.

Coppensia rupestris is a hardy plant that thrives in full sun and requires minimal water once established.
