Calathea ornata var. albolineata

Calathea ornata var. albolineata, commonly known as the White Line Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive white veins and a deep green background. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often described as having a subtle, almost metallic sheen.

Calathea ornata var. albolineata requires consistent moisture and high humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance.
