Crotalaria nitens var. gracilis

Crotalaria nitens var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender crownflower, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its delicate, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a short, upright stem with hairy foliage and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ability to fix nitrogen, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
