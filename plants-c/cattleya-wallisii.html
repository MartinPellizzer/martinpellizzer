Cattleya wallisii

Cattleya wallisii, commonly known as the Wallisii orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Cattleya wallisii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
