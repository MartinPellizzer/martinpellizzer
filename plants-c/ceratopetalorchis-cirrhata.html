Ceratopetalorchis cirrhata

Ceratopetalorchis cirrhata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the soil.

Ceratopetalorchis cirrhata is often found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
