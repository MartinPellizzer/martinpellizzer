Cylindrolobus elatus

Cylindrolobus elatus, commonly known as the slender lobe orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in well-drained environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a unique, tubular shape that attracts specific pollinators.
