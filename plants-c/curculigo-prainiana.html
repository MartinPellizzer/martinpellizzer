Curculigo prainiana

Curculigo prainiana, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers with a golden-yellow coloration, often with a contrasting purple or violet throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
