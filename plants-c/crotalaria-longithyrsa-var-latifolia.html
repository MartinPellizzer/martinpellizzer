Crotalaria longithyrsa var. latifolia

Crotalaria longithyrsa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its seeds are enclosed in pods, and the plant is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
