Cavendishia campii

Cavendishia campii, commonly known as the Campi's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored blooms with a distinctive lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cavendishia campii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
