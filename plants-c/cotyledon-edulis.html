Cotyledon edulis

Cotyledon edulis, commonly known as the edible cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often used in traditional cuisine. The plant belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are rich in nutrients and can be eaten raw or cooked.

Cotyledon edulis is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in arid environments.
