Clematis otophora

Clematis otophora, commonly known as the yellow clematis, is a climbing vine native to parts of Asia.

It features showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun for optimal growth.

Clematis otophora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
