Campanula swellendamensis

Campanula swellendamensis, commonly known as the Swellendam bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Campanula swellendamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
