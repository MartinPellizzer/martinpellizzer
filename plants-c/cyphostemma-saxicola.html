Cyphostemma saxicola

Cyphostemma saxicola, commonly known as the rock cyphostemma, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are often followed by small, fleshy fruits. It is adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a resilient species.

Cyphostemma saxicola is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
