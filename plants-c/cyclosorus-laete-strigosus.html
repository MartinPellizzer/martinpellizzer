Cyclosorus laete-strigosus

Cyclosorus laete-strigosus, commonly known as the woolly cyclosorus, is a perennial fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a creeping rhizome system, allowing it to spread along the forest floor. Its fronds are typically dark green and covered in fine, soft hairs.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate rainforests.
