Carduncellus mitissimus var. caulescens

Carduncellus mitissimus var. caulescens, commonly known as the slender cardoon, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, spiny leaves that are often covered in a white, waxy coating. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and hardy member of the Asteraceae family.
