Calathea ornata var. majestica

Calathea ornata var. majestica, commonly known as the Black Velvet Plant, is a tropical foliage plant prized for its striking, velvety dark green leaves with intricate patterns.

Native to South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with consistent moisture. The plant's leaves are adorned with contrasting white and green markings, creating a visually stunning effect. It requires well-draining soil and indirect light to prevent leaf burn.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
