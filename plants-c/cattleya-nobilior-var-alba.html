Cattleya nobilior var. alba

Cattleya nobilior var. alba, commonly known as the white noble cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, fragrant white blooms.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, arching stems with large, showy flowers that have three petals and a prominent, spurred lip. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a fan shape.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
