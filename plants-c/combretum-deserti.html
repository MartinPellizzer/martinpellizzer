Combretum deserti

Combretum deserti, commonly known as the desert combretum, is a shrub native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, oval leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with deep roots that help it access underground water. Its bark is often rough and fissured, providing a distinctive appearance.

Combretum deserti plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and supporting local wildlife in its native habitat.
