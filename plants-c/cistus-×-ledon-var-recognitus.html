Cistus × ledon var. recognitus

Cistus × ledon var. recognitus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, glandular leaves that emit a distinctive fragrance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ecological and ornamental purposes in Mediterranean climates.
