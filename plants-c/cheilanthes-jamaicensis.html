Cheilanthes jamaicensis

Cheilanthes jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican rock fern, is a perennial fern native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

Cheilanthes jamaicensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
