Cineraria burkei

Cineraria burkei, commonly known as the blue cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small, daisy-like blooms.

Cineraria burkei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
