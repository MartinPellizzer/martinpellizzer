Ceropegia dichotoma subsp. fusca

Ceropegia dichotoma subsp.

fusca, commonly known as the dark leafed string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially under bright light.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or violet in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
