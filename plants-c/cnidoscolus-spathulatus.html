Cnidoscolus spathulatus

Cnidoscolus spathulatus, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically a deep green and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is also used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
