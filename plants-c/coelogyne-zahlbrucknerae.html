Coelogyne zahlbrucknerae

Coelogyne zahlbrucknerae, commonly known as the Zahnbuckner's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering during the growing season.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
