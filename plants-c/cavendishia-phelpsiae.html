Cavendishia phelpsiae

Cavendishia phelpsiae, commonly known as the Phelps' Cavendish, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences. The plant features strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a water-holding cup at the center. It produces showy, long-lasting blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow.
