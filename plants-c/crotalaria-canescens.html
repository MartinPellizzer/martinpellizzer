Crotalaria canescens

Crotalaria canescens, commonly known as the white crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Crotalaria canescens is also noted for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional remedies.
