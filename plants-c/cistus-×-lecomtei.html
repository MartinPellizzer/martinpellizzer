Cistus × lecomtei

Cistus × lecomtei, commonly known as the Lecomte's rockrose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun, making it well-suited for coastal and dryland environments. Its evergreen foliage and compact growth habit make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cistus × lecomtei is also valued for its role in traditional herbal medicine and its ability to support local pollinators.
