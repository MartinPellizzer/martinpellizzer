Crucifera erysimum

Crucifera erysimum, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits.

It is an annual or biennial herb that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a strong, peppery scent. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
