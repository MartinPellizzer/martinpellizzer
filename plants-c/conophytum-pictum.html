Conophytum pictum

Conophytum pictum, commonly known as the "Painted Conephytum," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, fleshy body with a distinctive, flattened, conical shape. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the summer months. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Conophytum pictum is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
