Callipteris Aberrans

Callipteris aberrans is a unique and lesser-known plant species that thrives in specific coastal environments. It is native to the southeastern United States, where it adapts to salty air and sandy soils. This plant is often mistaken for other grass-like species due to its appearance. Despite its resilience, it faces threats from habitat loss and environmental changes. Understanding Callipteris aberrans is key to preserving coastal biodiversity.
