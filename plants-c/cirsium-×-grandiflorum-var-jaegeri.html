Cirsium × grandiflorum var. jaegeri

Cirsium × grandiflorum var. jaegeri, commonly known as the Jaeger's thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, showy purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to the western United States. Its leaves are lance-shaped with rough, serrated edges, and the flowers bloom in late summer.

It is often found in grasslands, open woodlands, and along roadsides.
