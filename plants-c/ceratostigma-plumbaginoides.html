Ceratostigma plumbaginoides

Ceratostigma plumbaginoides, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that produces vibrant blue or purple flowers in the fall. The plant has dark green, waxy leaves and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its unique blooming period makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking seasonal color.
