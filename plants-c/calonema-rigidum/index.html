Calonema Rigidum

Calonema rigidum is a unique and resilient plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its distinctive, upright growth habit and its ability to thrive in harsh, rocky environments. This species has a compact, shrub-like form with thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its hardiness, Calonema rigidum remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
