Croton tridesma

Croton tridesma, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that range from bright red to deep orange and green. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton tridesma is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance.
