Carduus × dobrogensis

Carduus × dobrogensis, commonly known as the Dobrogian thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads, which consist of numerous small florets. Native to the Carpathian region, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. It has been introduced to other parts of Europe and is sometimes considered an invasive species.

Its botanical features make it an important subject in studies of plant hybridization and ecological adaptation.
