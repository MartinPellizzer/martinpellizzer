Circaea Lutetiana Var. Alpina

Circaea lutetiana var. alpina is a rare and delicate flowering plant found in high-altitude regions of Europe. It belongs to the primrose family and is known for its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the early spring. This alpine variant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in mountainous environments. Its unique characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies in alpine ecosystems. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
