Cassia multiflora

Cassia multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features large, palmately compound leaves that are bright green and often have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces showy, bright yellow inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are followed by long, slender pods that contain several seeds.

Cassia multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
