Coprosma repens var. pumila

Coprosma repens var. pumila, commonly known as dwarf coprosma, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, compact foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its compact form and evergreen nature make it suitable for rock gardens and ground cover.
