Coprosma myrtillifolia var. linearis

Coprosma myrtillifolia var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forest environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation projects.
