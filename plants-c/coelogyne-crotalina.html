Coelogyne crotalina

Coelogyne crotalina, commonly known as the snake orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, elongated pseudobulb with a crown of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers with a deep red coloration and a unique, snake-like appearance. It blooms during the dry season, typically from December to March.

Coelogyne crotalina requires bright, indirect light and well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
