Chlorophytum engleri

Chlorophytum engleri, commonly known as the Emerald Gem, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features strap-shaped, bright green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the base of the leaves. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its ability to thrive in low light conditions makes it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
