Cleome karjaginii

Cleome karjaginii, commonly known as the white cleome, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cleome karjaginii is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
