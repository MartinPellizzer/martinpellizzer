Crassula nyikensis

Crassula nyikensis, commonly known as the Nyika crassula, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in full sun. Its small, star-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in late spring or early summer.

Crassula nyikensis is a low-maintenance plant that is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
