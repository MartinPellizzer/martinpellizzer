Clerodendrum yunnanense var. yunnanense

Clerodendrum yunnanense var. yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan Clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are clustered in terminal panicles and are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive tubular corolla.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its showy blooms.
