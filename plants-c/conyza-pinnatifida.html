Conyza pinnatifida

Conyza pinnatifida, commonly known as the slenderleaf weed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small yellow flowers that appear in clustered inflorescences. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has a hairy stem and produces achenes as its seeds.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural and natural settings.
