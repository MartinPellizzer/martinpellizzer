Clematopsis scabiosifolia subsp. uhehensis

Clematopsis scabiosifolia subsp.

uhehensis, commonly known as the Uhehensis Clematopsis, is a member of the Rosaceae family. It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its slender, woody stems and compound leaves with serrated edges.

The flowers are small, white to pink, and arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It is native to specific regions in Africa, particularly in the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
