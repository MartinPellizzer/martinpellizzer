Cereus speciosissimus var. peintneri

Cereus speciosissimus var. peintneri, commonly known as the Peintner's Christmas Cactus, is a succulent plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that are segmented and often adorned with spines. The plant produces vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
