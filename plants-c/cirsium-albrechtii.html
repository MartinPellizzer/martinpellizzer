Cirsium albrechtii

Cirsium albrechtii, commonly known as the albrecht's thistle, is a perennial herb native to central Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its flowers are visited by various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Cirsium albrechtii is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.
