Clinopodium dalmaticum subsp. dalmaticum

Clinopodium dalmaticum subsp.

dalmaticum, commonly known as Dalmatian oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant has aromatic, fuzzy leaves and produces small, pale blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are often used in culinary applications, adding a sharp, minty flavor to dishes.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
