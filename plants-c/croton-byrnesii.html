Croton byrnesii

Croton byrnesii, commonly known as the Byrnes' croton, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture.

Croton byrnesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in warm climates.
