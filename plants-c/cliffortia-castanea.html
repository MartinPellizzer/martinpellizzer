Cliffortia castanea

Cliffortia castanea, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Cliffortia castanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
