Crataegus cullasagensis

Crataegus cullasagensis, commonly known as the Cullasagen hawthorn, is a species of shrub or small tree native to specific regions of North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny branches and clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained, acidic conditions.
