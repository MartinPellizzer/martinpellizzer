Cotoneaster rubens

Cotoneaster rubens, commonly known as the red cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that persist through winter. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Cotoneaster rubens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
