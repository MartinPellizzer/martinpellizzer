Crassula stachyera

Crassula stachyera, commonly known as the "Lace Leaf Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with leaves that are thick, fleshy, and often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The leaves are typically a vibrant green, sometimes with a bluish or reddish tinge depending on light exposure. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or summer.

This plant is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens or container cultivation.
