Clematis flammula sempervirens

Clematis flammula sempervirens, commonly known as evergreen clematis, is a deciduous to evergreen vine native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that remain green throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its vigorous growth habit makes it suitable for climbing structures or as a ground cover in shaded gardens.
