Campanula lanceolata var. tenuifolia

Campanula lanceolata var. tenuifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces upright stems that bear bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and borders.
