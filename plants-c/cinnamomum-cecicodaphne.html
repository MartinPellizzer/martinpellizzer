Cinnamomum cecicodaphne

Cinnamomum cecicodaphne, commonly known as the Philippine white cinnamom, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by oval, woody fruits containing dark brown seeds.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its essential oils and medicinal properties.
