Cyclophorus stellatus

Cyclophorus stellatus, commonly known as the star-shaped cyclophorus, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive star-like appearance.

It belongs to the family Cyclophoraceae and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. The plant features small, clustered leaves that give it a compact and ornamental look. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
