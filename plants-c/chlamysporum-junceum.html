Chlamysporum junceum

Chlamysporum junceum, commonly known as the slender water plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in regions with temperate climates. Its botanical structure is adapted for aquatic or semi-aquatic conditions, with rhizomes that help anchor it in wet substrates.

Chlamysporum junceum plays a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
