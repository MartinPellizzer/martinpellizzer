Cryptocarya borneensis

Cryptocarya borneensis, commonly known as Bornean cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically greenish-yellow in color, and it produces dark, glossy berries as fruits.

Cryptocarya borneensis plays a role in its ecosystem by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
