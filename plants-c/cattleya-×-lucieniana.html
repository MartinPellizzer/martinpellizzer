Cattleya × lucieniana

Cattleya × lucieniana, commonly known as the Lucieniana orchid, is a hybrid orchid that originated from crosses between Cattleya labiata and Cattleya mossiae.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure, producing multiple leaves and flower spikes from the base. The plant is native to Brazil and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its flowers are large, fragrant, and typically come in shades of white, pink, and purple, with a distinctive lip.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
