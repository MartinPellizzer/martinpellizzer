Corymborkis subdensa

Corymborkis subdensa, commonly known as the dense-flowered orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette, while the stems are short and often obscured by the foliage. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
