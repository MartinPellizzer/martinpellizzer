Cyperus Betchei

Cyperus betchei is a unique wetland plant native to parts of Africa and Asia. It is known for its tall, grass-like appearance and its ability to thrive in moist environments. This species is often found in marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. Its striking silver-green foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and supporting local wildlife.
