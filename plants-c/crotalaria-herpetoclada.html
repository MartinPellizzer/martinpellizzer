Crotalaria herpetoclada

Crotalaria herpetoclada, commonly known as the snakevine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers. The plant has compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces elongated pods containing seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fallow fields.
