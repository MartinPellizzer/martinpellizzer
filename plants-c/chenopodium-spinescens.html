Chenopodium spinescens

Chenopodium spinescens, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. Its stems are often spiny, which helps in deterring herbivores.

Chenopodium spinescens is known for its adaptability and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
