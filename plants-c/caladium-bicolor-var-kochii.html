Caladium bicolor var. kochii

Caladium bicolor var. kochii, commonly known as the "Koch's flame" or "flame leaf," is a tropical perennial known for its striking, colorful foliage.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with intricate patterns of red, orange, and yellow, creating a vibrant display. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Caladium bicolor var. kochii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor spaces.
