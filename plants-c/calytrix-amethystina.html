Calytrix amethystina

Calytrix amethystina, commonly known as the purple-flowered honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with narrow, elliptical leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Calytrix amethystina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
