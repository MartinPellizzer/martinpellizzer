Cryptocarya obovata var. tropica

Cryptocarya obovata var. tropica, commonly known as the tropical cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by dark, spherical berries.

This plant is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
