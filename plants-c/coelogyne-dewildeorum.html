Coelogyne dewildeorum

Coelogyne dewildeorum, commonly known as the Dewilde's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments.

The flowers, which are usually pink or white, bloom in clusters and are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
