Crocosmia masoniorum

Crocosmia masoniorum, commonly known as the orange flame lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features tall, narrow leaves and produces showy, torch-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Crocosmia masoniorum is also valued for its ability to naturalize and spread through rhizomes, creating dense clumps over time.
