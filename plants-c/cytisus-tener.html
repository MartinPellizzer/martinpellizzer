Cytisus tener

Cytisus tener, commonly known as the soft broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Cytisus tener is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
