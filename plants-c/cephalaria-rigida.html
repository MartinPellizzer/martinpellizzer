Cephalaria rigida

Cephalaria rigida, commonly known as rigid cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, branched stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters, and the leaves are usually narrow and hairy.

Cephalaria rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
