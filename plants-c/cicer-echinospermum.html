Cicer echinospermum

Cicer echinospermum, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive spiny pods. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with trifoliate leaves and small, pale blue or violet flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in a hairy, spiny pod, which is a key botanical feature.

The plant is often found in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
