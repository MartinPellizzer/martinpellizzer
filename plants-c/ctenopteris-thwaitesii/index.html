Ctenopteris Thwaitesii

Ctenopteris thwaitesii, scientifically known as Ctenopteris thwaitesii, is a unique species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This plant is characterized by its distinctive fronds that exhibit a feathery, lace-like appearance, making it visually striking. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories where it contributes to the biodiversity of the ecosystem. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it has gained attention among botanists and horticultural enthusiasts. However, it faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
