Calostemma purpureum var. carneum

Calostemma purpureum var. carneum, commonly known as the pink-flowered spider orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a unique, elongated shape. The plant features a short, stocky stem and produces a cluster of showy, bilaterally symmetrical blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens and conservation efforts.
