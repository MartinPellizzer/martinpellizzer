Calophyllum obscurum

Calophyllum obscurum, commonly known as the dark calophyllum, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic benefits.
