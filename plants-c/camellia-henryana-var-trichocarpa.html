Camellia henryana var. trichocarpa

Camellia henryana var. trichocarpa, commonly known as the trichocarpic camellia, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, double pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
