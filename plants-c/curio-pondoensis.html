Curio pondoensis

Curio pondoensis, commonly known as the Pondoensis Curio, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
