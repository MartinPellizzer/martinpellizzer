Calyptraria africana

Calyptraria africana, commonly known as the African calyptraria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky crevices. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
