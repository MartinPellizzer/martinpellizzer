Crassula moniliformis

Crassula moniliformis, commonly known as the string of pearls plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, bead-like leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or summer. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for container gardening and rock gardens.
