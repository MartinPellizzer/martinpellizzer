Cogniauxiocharis luteola

Cogniauxiocharis luteola, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers with a star-like shape. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm and blooms in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is often grown in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
