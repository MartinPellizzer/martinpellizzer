Chaboissaea

Chaboissaea, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features striking, golden-yellow flowers with a star-shaped pattern, which are highly ornamental. Chaboissaea grows as an epiphytic plant, often found on trees in humid forests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and role in pollination by specific insect species.
