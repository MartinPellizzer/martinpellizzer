Cymbidium × cravenianim

Cymbidium × cravenianim, commonly known as the Craven's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Cymbidium goeringii and Cymbidium lowianum.

It is a tall, upright plant with linear, dark green leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The flowers are typically borne in racemes and are characterized by their showy, fragrant blooms in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It prefers cool, bright conditions with good air circulation and moderate watering.

This plant is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
