Cylista argentea

Cylista argentea, commonly known as silverleaf cylista, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in a dense layer of silvery-white hairs, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and also covered in a fine silvery coating.

Cylista argentea is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
