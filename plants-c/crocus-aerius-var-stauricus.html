Crocus aerius var. stauricus

Crocus aerius var. stauricus, commonly known as the star crocus, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a violet or purple tinge. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves and has a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its early blooming season, typically in late winter or early spring.
