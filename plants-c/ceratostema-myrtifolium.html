Ceratostema myrtifolium

Ceratostema myrtifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf ceratostema, is a perennial shrub native to southern China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow flowers in late spring, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
