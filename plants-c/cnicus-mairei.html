Cnicus mairei

Cnicus mairei, commonly known as Maire's prickly centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with spiny stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits called cypsela, which are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Cnicus mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
