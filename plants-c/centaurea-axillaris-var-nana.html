Centaurea axillaris var. nana

Centaurea axillaris var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf centaury, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Centaurea axillaris var. nana is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
