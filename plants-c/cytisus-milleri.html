Cytisus milleri

Cytisus milleri, commonly known as the Mill's broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
