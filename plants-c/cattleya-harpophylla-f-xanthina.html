Cattleya harpophylla f. xanthina

Cattleya harpophylla f. xanthina, commonly known as the golden-flowered cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that showcases striking yellow blooms.

This cultivar is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a vibrant yellow hue and intricate patterns. It originates from the cross between Cattleya harpophylla and another species, resulting in its unique coloration. The plant typically features broad, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, long-lasting flowers.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
