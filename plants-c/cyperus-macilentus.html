Cyperus macilentus

Cyperus macilentus, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spike-like flowers.

Cyperus macilentus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
