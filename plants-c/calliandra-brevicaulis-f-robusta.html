Calliandra brevicaulis f. robusta

Calliandra brevicaulis f. robusta, commonly known as the robust calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, branching structure and its showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically beneficial.
