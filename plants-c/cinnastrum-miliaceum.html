Cinnastrum miliaceum

Cinnastrum miliaceum, commonly known as the Chinese milk vetch, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, edible seeds that are used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Cinnastrum miliaceum is valued for its nutritional properties and ecological benefits.
