Clematis gouriana var. mollifolia

Clematis gouriana var. mollifolia, commonly known as the silky clematis, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its attractive foliage and flowers, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
