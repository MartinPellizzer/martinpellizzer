Crotalaria collettii

Crotalaria collettii, commonly known as the golden chain bean, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of yellow flowers that resemble small chains. The plant produces small, kidney-shaped pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

Crotalaria collettii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
