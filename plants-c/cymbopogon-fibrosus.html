Cymbopogon fibrosus

Cymbopogon fibrosus, commonly known as the narrowleaf grass or fibrous grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small fauna.

Cymbopogon fibrosus is also used in traditional medicine and has potential applications in herbal remedies.
