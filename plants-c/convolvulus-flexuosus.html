Convolvulus flexuosus

Convolvulus flexuosus, commonly known as the flexible morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Convolvulus flexuosus is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail along surfaces.
