Codiaeum triumphans var. harwoodianum

Codiaeum triumphans var. harwoodianum, commonly known as the Harwoodianum croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental species due to its eye-catching appearance.
