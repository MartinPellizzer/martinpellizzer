Cistus × braunii

Cistus × braunii, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes various species of flowering plants known for their aromatic foliage and showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and are pollinated by insects. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Cistus × braunii is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
