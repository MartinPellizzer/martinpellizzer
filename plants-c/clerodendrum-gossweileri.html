Clerodendrum gossweileri

Clerodendrum gossweileri, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a serrated edge. It produces small, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Clerodendrum gossweileri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
