Caustis flexuosa var. tenuirama

Caustis flexuosa var. tenuirama, commonly known as the narrow-leaf caustis, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in spring. Its foliage is aromatic and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
