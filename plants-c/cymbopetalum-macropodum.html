Cymbopetalum macropodum

Cymbopetalum macropodum, commonly known as the pink torch or fire torch, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red, resembling small torches. The plant produces clusters of tubular flowers that bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its colorful blooms.

Cymbopetalum macropodum thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
