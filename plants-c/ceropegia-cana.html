Ceropegia cana

Ceropegia cana, commonly known as the pale ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia cana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
