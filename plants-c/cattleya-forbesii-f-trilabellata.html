Cattleya forbesii f. trilabellata

Cattleya forbesii f. trilabellata, commonly known as the Trilabellate Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It features large, showy blooms with three distinct labellum lobes, giving it its name. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
