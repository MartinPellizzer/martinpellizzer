Cypripedium insigne var. cypheri

Cypripedium insigne var. cypheri, commonly known as the Cypher's White Lady, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip and greenish sepals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich soil. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to establish a robust root system, which helps it survive in its challenging habitat.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
