Calathea robin-fosteri

Calathea robin-fosteri, commonly known as the Robin Foster Calathea, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns and variegation. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, often featuring a waxy texture. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
