Centaurea bruguieriana var. spinosissima

Centaurea bruguieriana var. spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny centaury, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flowers, typically in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
