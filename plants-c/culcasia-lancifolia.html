Culcasia lancifolia

Culcasia lancifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The leaves are typically a deep green with a smooth texture and a pointed tip, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color.

This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
