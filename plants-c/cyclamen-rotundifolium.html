Cyclamen rotundifolium

Cyclamen rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaved cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and prefers cool, moist environments. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Cyclamen rotundifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
