Crocodilodes annectens

Crocodilodes annectens, commonly known as the swamp hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, swampy environments. It is often found growing near water bodies such as rivers and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of wetland ecosystems.
