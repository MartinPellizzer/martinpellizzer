Camellia pingguoensis

Camellia pingguoensis, commonly known as the Guangxi camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained acidic soils. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape, while its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring.

Camellia pingguoensis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its historical significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
