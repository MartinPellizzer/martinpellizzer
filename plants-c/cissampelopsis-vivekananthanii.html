Cissampelopsis vivekananthanii

Cissampelopsis vivekananthanii, commonly known as the Vivekananthan's evergreen vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
