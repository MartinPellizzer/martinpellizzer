Cyrtochilum verstraeteanum

Cyrtochilum verstraeteanum, commonly known as the Verstraeteanum orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure with dense, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Cyrtochilum verstraeteanum is also noted for its unique floral morphology and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
