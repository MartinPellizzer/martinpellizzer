Clutia alaternoides f. oblongata

Clutia alaternoides f. oblongata, commonly known as the broadleaf clutia, is a variant of the Clutia alaternoides species.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant features lanceolate to oblong leaves that are typically arranged in whorls along the stems. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
