Cirsium × purpureum

Cirsium × purpureum, commonly known as the purple thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, purple-tinged leaves and showy purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. Native to Europe, it has been introduced to various regions around the world, often as an ornamental plant. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a range of environmental conditions.

Its morphology includes a tall, woody stem with bracts that form a crown-like structure around the flower head.
