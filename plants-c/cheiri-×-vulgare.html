Cheiri × vulgare

Cheiri × vulgare, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a cross between *Dianthus cheiri* and *Dianthus vulgare*.

It is a flowering plant in the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, double blooms in a range of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its flowers are often used in ornamental horticulture due to their vibrant appearance and long-lasting blooms.
