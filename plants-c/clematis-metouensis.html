Clematis metouensis

Clematis metouensis, commonly known as the Metoue Clematis, is a climbing vine native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive blue or purple hue in the center. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.

Clematis metouensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
