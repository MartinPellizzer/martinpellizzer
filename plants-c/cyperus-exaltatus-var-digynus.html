Cyperus exaltatus var. digynus

Cyperus exaltatus var. digynus, commonly known as the zigzag sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in suitable habitats.
