Commersonia platyphylla var. lechenaultii

Commersonia platyphylla var. lechenaultii, commonly known as the broad-leaved commersonia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
