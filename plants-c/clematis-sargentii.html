Clematis sargentii

Clematis sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white to pink. The plant thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes. It is known for its vigorous growth and ability to climb trellises or walls.

Clematis sargentii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
