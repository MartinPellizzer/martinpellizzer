Crossandra leucodonta

Crossandra leucodonta, commonly known as the white-flowered crossandra, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular white flowers that bloom in clusters. Crossandra leucodonta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
