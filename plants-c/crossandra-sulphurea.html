Crossandra sulphurea

Crossandra sulphurea, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth surface with a slightly waxy texture.
