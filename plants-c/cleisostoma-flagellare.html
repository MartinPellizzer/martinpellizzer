Cleisostoma flagellare

Cleisostoma flagellare, commonly known as the flying flag orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated petals that resemble a flag. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Cleisostoma flagellare is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
