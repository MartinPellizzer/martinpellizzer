Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. veitchii

Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces clusters of white to pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. veitchii is also valued for its use in wildlife habitats and as a hedge plant.
