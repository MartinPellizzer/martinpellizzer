Clematis stolzii

Clematis stolzii, commonly known as the Stolzii Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to South America.

It features compound, fern-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, single or double flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis stolzii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
