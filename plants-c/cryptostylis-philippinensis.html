Cryptostylis philippinensis

Cryptostylis philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, humid environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
