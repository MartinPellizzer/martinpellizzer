Cistus × neyrauti

Cistus × neyrauti, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and vibrant, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments.

Cistus × neyrauti is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
