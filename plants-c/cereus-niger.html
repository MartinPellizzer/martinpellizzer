Cereus niger

Cereus niger, commonly known as the black cereus, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a succulent cactus that features a tall, spiny stem with sharp, needle-like spines. The plant produces large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a strong scent. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for dry climates.

Its striking appearance and unique blooming pattern make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
