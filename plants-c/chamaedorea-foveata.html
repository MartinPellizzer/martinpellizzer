Chamaedorea foveata

Chamaedorea foveata, commonly known as the zigzag palm, is a compact, evergreen plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and feather-like, pinnate leaves that exhibit a distinctive zigzag pattern along their length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

Due to its elegant appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
