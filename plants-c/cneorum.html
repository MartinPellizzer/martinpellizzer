Cneorum

Cneorum, commonly known as false spurge, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Cneoraceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are shrubs or small trees native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plants typically have alternate, simple leaves that are often glandular and sometimes aromatic. They produce small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color.

Cneorum species are often found in rocky or dry soils and are adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
