Cassia × herbertiana

Cassia × herbertiana, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a hybrid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its long, drooping panicles of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are bipinnate, with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Cassia × herbertiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display.
