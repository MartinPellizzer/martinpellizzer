Calanthe lutea var. lutea

Calanthe lutea var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow calanthe, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces showy, yellowish-orange flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
