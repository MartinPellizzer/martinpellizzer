Ceanothus orcuttii

Ceanothus orcuttii, commonly known as the Orcutt's ceanothus, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to dry climates and has a deep root system to access water.

Ceanothus orcuttii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
