Cysticapnos alexandrina

Cysticapnos alexandrina, commonly known as the Alexandrian sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.
