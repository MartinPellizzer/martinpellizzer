Cymbidium finlaysonianum var. atropurpureum

Cymbidium finlaysonianum var. atropurpureum, commonly known as the purple finlayson's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of large, showy flowers with deep purple to reddish-purple petals. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, producing fragrant, lance-shaped flowers that are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts. It prefers cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its striking coloration and ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
