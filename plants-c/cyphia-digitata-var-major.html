Cyphia digitata var. major

Cyphia digitata var. major, commonly known as the large-leaf cyphia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features prominently veined, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
