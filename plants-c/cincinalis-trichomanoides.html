Cincinalis trichomanoides

Cincinalis trichomanoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf holly fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, upright clump. Its rhizomes are wiry and help anchor it to moist, shaded environments.
