Cliffortia obovata

Cliffortia obovata, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-like growth habit and produces clusters of white or pink flowers during the winter months. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found growing on cliffs or slopes. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a soft, velvety texture.

Cliffortia obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
