Campanula glomerata var. stenosiphon

Campanula glomerata var. stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-siphon bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lanceolate and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
