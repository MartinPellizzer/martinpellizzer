Caladenia attenuata

Caladenia attenuata, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance with a single, elongated flower spike. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctively shaped lip.

Caladenia attenuata is a terrestrial orchid that blooms during the spring and early summer months.
