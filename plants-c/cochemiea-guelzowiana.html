Cochemiea guelzowiana

Cochemiea guelzowiana, commonly known as the Guelzow's cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact, rounded shape and a gray-green color. The plant features white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the spring. It has spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments.

Cochemiea guelzowiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
