Coprosma margarita

Coprosma margarita, commonly known as the silver coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery or grayish pubescence. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains several seeds.

Coprosma margarita is often found in coastal and lowland forest environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
