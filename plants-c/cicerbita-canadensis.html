Cicerbita canadensis

Cicerbita canadensis, commonly known as Canadian bittercress, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in fields, along roadsides, and in woodland edges. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers and has alternate, lance-shaped leaves.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, elongated seeds.
