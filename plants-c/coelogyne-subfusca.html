Coelogyne subfusca

Coelogyne subfusca, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a greenish tint. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Coelogyne subfusca is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
