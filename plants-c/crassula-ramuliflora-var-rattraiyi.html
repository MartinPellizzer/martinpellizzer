Crassula ramuliflora var. rattraiyi

Crassula ramuliflora var. rattraiyi, commonly known as the Rattray's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
