Chlorophytum mekongense

Chlorophytum mekongense, commonly known as the Mekong spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Cambodia, Laos, and Vietnam. This plant is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, often with a slight curl.

It is a hardy plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.
