Cyrtanthus stenanthus var. major

Cyrtanthus stenanthus var. major, commonly known as the giant spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, narrow shape that resembles spider legs, hence its common name. The plant typically grows to a height of up to 1.5 meters, with broad, strap-like leaves that are often blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a dramatic visual impact to the landscape.
