Camellia crassicolumna var. shangbaensis

Camellia crassicolumna var. shangbaensis, commonly known as the Shangba camellia, is a rare evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and showy, camellia-like flowers that range from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in shaded forest environments. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.

Due to its limited natural habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species.
