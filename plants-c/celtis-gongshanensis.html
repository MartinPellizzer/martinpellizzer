Celtis gongshanensis

Celtis gongshanensis, commonly known as the Gongshan hackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Cannabaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin and a glossy green upper surface.

The fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.
