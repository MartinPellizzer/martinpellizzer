Chrysanthemum Grubovii

Chrysanthemum grubovii is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its showy flower heads and diverse species. This particular variety is distinguished by its vibrant yellow blooms and unique leaf patterns. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important species.
