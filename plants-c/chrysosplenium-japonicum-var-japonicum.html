Chrysosplenium japonicum var. japonicum

Chrysosplenium japonicum var. japonicum, commonly known as Japanese chrysosplenium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky slopes. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver or white underside, which gives it a striking appearance. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom in early spring.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
