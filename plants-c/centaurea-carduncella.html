Centaurea carduncella

Centaurea carduncella, commonly known as lesser cardoon, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features spiny, lobed leaves and produces purple or pink flowers in composite heads. It is a perennial herb that can grow up to 1 meter tall.
