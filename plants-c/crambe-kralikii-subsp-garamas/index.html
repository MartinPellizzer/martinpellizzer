Crambe Kralikii Subsp. Garamas

Crambe kralikii subsp. garamas is a rare and lesser-known member of the Brassicaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and nutrient-poor soils. Its unique morphology includes small, yellow flowers and a distinct, branched stem structure. Despite its ornamental potential, it remains underappreciated in horticulture. Studying this species offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in alpine environments.
