Chlorophytum galpinii var. galpinii

Chlorophytum galpinii var. galpinii, commonly known as the Galpin's spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

This variety is popular for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
