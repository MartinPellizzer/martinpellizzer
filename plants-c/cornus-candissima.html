Cornus candissima

Cornus candissima, commonly known as the dwarf cornel.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and turn yellow in the autumn.

Cornus candissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
