Ceropegia fantastica

Ceropegia fantastica, commonly known as the Fantastica String of Hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or white, with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia fantastica is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
