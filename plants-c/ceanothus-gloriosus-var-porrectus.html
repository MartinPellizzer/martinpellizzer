Ceanothus gloriosus var. porrectus

Ceanothus gloriosus var. porrectus, commonly known as the Porrectus Ceanothus, is a flowering shrub native to California.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy clusters of blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in Mediterranean climates.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
