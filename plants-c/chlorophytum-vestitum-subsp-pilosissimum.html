Chlorophytum vestitum subsp. pilosissimum

Chlorophytum vestitum subsp.

pilosissimum, commonly known as the woolly spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
