Clutia alaternoides f. brachyphylla

Clutia alaternoides f. brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-leaved clutia, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically less than 2 cm in length, giving it its distinguishing "short-leaved" characteristic. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are often borne on short pedicels. Its foliage is dense and evergreen, making it suitable for use in ornamental gardening.

The species is noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in partial shade.
