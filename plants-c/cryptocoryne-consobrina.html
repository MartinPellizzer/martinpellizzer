Cryptocoryne consobrina

Cryptocoryne consobrina, commonly known as the broad-leaf cryptocoryne, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 15 cm in length, providing a lush, dense appearance in aquariums. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires stable water parameters for optimal growth. It is a hardy species that can be propagated through rhizome division or by planting its spores.

Cryptocoryne consobrina is favored for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in both freshwater and brackish environments.
