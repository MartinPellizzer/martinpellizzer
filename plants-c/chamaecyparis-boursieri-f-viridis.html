Chamaecyparis boursieri f. viridis

Chamaecyparis boursieri f. viridis, commonly known as the Emerald Green Japanese cypress, is a compact, evergreen conifer with a dense, columnar growth habit.

It features needle-like leaves that are a striking emerald green, providing a vibrant contrast to its dark bark. This cultivar is native to Japan and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal maintenance once established.

The plant is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban conditions, making it a popular choice for small spaces and rock gardens.
