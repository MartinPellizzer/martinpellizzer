Calystegia soldanella f. albiflora

Calystegia soldanella f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered field bindweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with five petals and a distinctive spurred structure. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along fence rows.
