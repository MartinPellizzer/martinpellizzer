Centaurea scopulorum

Centaurea scopulorum, commonly known as the rock centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow to orange-red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Centaurea scopulorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
