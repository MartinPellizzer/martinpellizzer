Carex Schiedeana Var. Perstricta

Carex schiedeana var. perstricta is a unique species of sedge known for its distinctive appearance and ecological importance. Native to specific regions in the southeastern United States, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its narrow, grass-like leaves and clustered flower heads make it easily identifiable in wetland environments. The plant plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Due to habitat loss, it is also a species of conservation concern.
