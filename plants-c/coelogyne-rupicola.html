Coelogyne rupicola

Coelogyne rupicola, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red speckles. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates, thriving in humid, shaded environments. It is a perennial epiphyte, often found in mountainous regions of countries like Thailand and Vietnam.

Coelogyne rupicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
