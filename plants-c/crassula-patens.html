Crassula patens

Crassula patens, commonly known as the Ice Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a dense rosette, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Crassula patens is also known for its ability to produce offsets, allowing it to propagate easily through vegetative means.
