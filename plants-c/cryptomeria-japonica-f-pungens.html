Cryptomeria japonica f. pungens

Cryptomeria japonica f. pungens, commonly known as the Japanese fir with prickly cones, is a variant of the Japanese cedar.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sharply pointed cones and needle-like leaves that are slightly twisted. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and evergreen nature.

The f. pungens form is particularly noted for its hardy and resilient characteristics.
