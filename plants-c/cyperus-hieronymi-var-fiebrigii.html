Cyperus hieronymi var. fiebrigii

Cyperus hieronymi var. fiebrigii, commonly known as the Fiebrig's flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower spikes.

Its botanical features make it an important species for wetland ecosystems and ecological studies.
