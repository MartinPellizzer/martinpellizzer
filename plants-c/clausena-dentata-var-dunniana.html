Clausena dentata var. dunniana

Clausena dentata var. dunniana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved curry leaf tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often aromatic and used in culinary applications. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by egg-shaped, fleshy fruits that are edible. Its botanical features include pinnate leaves and a distinctive, fragrant aroma.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the use of its leaves in traditional cooking.
