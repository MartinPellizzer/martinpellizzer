Castela polyandra

Castela polyandra, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several narrow, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Castela polyandra is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and bees.
