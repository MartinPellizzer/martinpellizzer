Caladium giganteum

Caladium giganteum, commonly known as the giant elephant ear, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to three feet in length. The foliage is typically a vibrant mix of green, red, and white, creating a dramatic visual display. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
