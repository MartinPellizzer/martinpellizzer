Camara lilacina var. parvifolia

Camara lilacina var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically smaller than those of the standard Camara lilacina. The plant produces clusters of small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its botanical classification places it within the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
