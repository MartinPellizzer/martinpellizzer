Centaurella autumnalis var. aestivalis

Centaurella autumnalis var. aestivalis, commonly known as the summer centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and produces vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It blooms from late spring to early summer, adding color to gardens and natural landscapes.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is well-suited for xeriscaping and low-maintenance gardening.
