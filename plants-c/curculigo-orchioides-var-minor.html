Curculigo orchioides var. minor

Curculigo orchioides var. minor, commonly known as the lesser orchid root or the small orchid root, is a rare and endangered plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies for its purported health benefits. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it is critically endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
