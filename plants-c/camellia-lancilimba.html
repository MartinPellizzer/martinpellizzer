Camellia lancilimba

Camellia lancilimba, commonly known as the long-leaved camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, camellia-type flowers that are typically pink or red, with a waxy texture. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to full sun.
