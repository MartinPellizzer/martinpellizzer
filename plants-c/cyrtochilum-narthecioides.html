Cyrtochilum narthecioides

Cyrtochilum narthecioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, hairy pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cyrtochilum narthecioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
