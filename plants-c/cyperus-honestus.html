Cyperus honestus

Cyperus honestus, commonly known as the common flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and small, triangular leaves. The plant produces spikes of tiny, clustered flowers, typically in late spring to early summer. Cyperus honestus is often found in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
