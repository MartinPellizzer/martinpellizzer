Cylista ferruginea

Cylista ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty cylista, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in a rusty or reddish-brown hue, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough, hairy texture.

Cylista ferruginea is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
