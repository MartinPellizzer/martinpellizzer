Callerya championii

Callerya championii, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical features of Callerya championii make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
