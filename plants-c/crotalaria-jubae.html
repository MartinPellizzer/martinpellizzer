Crotalaria jubae

Crotalaria jubae, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several elliptical seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Crotalaria jubae is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
