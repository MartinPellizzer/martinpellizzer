Croton maasii

Croton maasii, commonly known as the Maasai croton, is a tropical shrub native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its milky sap, which can be toxic if ingested. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a waxy texture.

Croton maasii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and eye-catching appearance.
