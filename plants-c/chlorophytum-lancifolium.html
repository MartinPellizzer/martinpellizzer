Chlorophytum lancifolium

Chlorophytum lancifolium, commonly known as the sword leaf snake plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are usually bright green with a slight blue tint. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the summer months. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Its ability to purify indoor air makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
