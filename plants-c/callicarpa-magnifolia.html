Callicarpa magnifolia

Callicarpa magnifolia, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant purple to pink berries that appear in late summer. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

The berries are not only ornamental but also attract birds and other wildlife.
