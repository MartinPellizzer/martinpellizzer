Corydalis pallida f. ramosissima

Corydalis pallida f. ramosissima, commonly known as the pale corydalis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
