Chelonopsis odontochila subsp. rosea

Chelonopsis odontochila subsp.

rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered chelonopsis, is a member of the Apiaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink.
