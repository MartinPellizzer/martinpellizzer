Correa pulchella var. triumphus-milneri

Correa pulchella var. triumphus-milneri, commonly known as the Crimson Correa, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features vibrant red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
