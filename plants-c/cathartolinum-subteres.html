Cathartolinum subteres

Cathartolinum subteres, commonly known as the slender cathartolinum, is a rare flowering plant native to the subtropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a humid, warm environment to flourish.
