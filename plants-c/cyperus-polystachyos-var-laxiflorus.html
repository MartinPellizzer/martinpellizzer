Cyperus polystachyos var. laxiflorus

Cyperus polystachyos var. laxiflorus, commonly known as the narrow-flowered flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale to brownish flowers, typical of many Cyperus species. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, often found in wetlands, ditches, and along waterways.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
