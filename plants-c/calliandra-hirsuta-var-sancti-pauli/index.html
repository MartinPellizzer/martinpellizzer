Calliandra Hirsuta Var. Sancti-Pauli

Sancti pauli calliandra, scientifically known as Calliandra hirsuta var. sancti-pauli, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. This unique variant of the calliandra species is distinguished by its vibrant pink to red flowers and its ability to thrive in warm, sunny environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing nectar for bees and other pollinators. The plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and landscapers. Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a standout addition to any tropical or subtropical garden.
