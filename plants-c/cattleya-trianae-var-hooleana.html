Cattleya trianae var. hooleana

Cattleya trianae var. hooleana, commonly known as the Hooleana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with intricate patterns. The plant features long, arching stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and vibrant blooms.
