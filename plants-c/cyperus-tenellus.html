Cyperus tenellus

Cyperus tenellus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered spikelets. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wetland and marshy environments. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and produces pale green to brown flowers.

Cyperus tenellus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
