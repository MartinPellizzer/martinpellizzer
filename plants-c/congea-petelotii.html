Congea petelotii

Congea petelotii, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It blooms throughout the warm seasons and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The seeds are enclosed in a fleshy, capsule-like fruit that splits open when mature.
