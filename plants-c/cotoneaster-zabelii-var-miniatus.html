Cotoneaster zabelii var. miniatus

Cotoneaster zabelii var. miniatus, commonly known as the dwarf cotoneaster, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant due to its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a versatile and attractive shrub.
