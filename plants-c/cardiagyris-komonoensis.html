Cardiagyris komonoensis

Cardiagyris komonoensis, commonly known as the Komono bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.
