Cuphea callosa

Cuphea callosa, commonly known as the velvet tamales plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, velvety seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
