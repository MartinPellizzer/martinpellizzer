Chiliotrichum virgatum

Chiliotrichum virgatum, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and hardiness.

Chiliotrichum virgatum is also valued for its ability to thrive in arid conditions.
