Chamaecrista pinoi

Chamaecrista pinoi, commonly known as the Pino's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba and the Bahamas.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often found in dry, sunny environments.
