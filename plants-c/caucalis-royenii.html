Caucalis royenii

Caucalis royenii, commonly known as the rock bellflower, is a flowering plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy blue or purple flowers. The plant typically has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that help reduce water loss in dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
