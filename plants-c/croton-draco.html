Croton draco

Croton draco, commonly known as Draco's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and striking, colorful flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, round seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton draco thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
