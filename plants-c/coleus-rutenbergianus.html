Coleus rutenbergianus

Coleus rutenbergianus, commonly known as the Rutenberg's coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a glossy surface and come in shades of red, purple, and green. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale purple or white, arranged in whorled clusters.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
