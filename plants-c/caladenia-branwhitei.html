Caladenia branwhitei

Caladenia branwhitei, commonly known as the Branwhite's Spider-orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and its distinctive, spider-like flowers that are typically pale pink to cream in color. The plant produces a single, elongated flower with a narrow, hooded labellum, which is a unique feature among orchids. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in open habitats with good drainage.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
