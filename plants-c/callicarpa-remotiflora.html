Callicarpa remotiflora

Callicarpa remotiflora, commonly known as the red-berried beauty or Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that appear in the spring. The plant produces striking clusters of bright red berries in the fall, which are a notable feature in its ornamental value. Callicarpa remotiflora thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
