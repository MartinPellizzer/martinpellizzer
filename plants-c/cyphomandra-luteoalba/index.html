Cyphomandra Luteoalba

Cyphomandra luteoalba is a lesser-known member of the Solanaceae family, commonly known as the nightshade family. This plant is native to tropical regions of South America and is often found in humid, shaded environments. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and edible fruits that resemble small tomatoes. Despite its potential for cultivation, it remains relatively obscure compared to more popular nightshade species. Its unique appearance and mild flavor make it an intriguing subject for botanical and culinary exploration.
