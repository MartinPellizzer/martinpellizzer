Coelogyne pterogyne

Coelogyne pterogyne, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves and produces long, pendant inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne pterogyne is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
