Cupressus torulosa var. majestica

Cupressus torulosa var. majestica, commonly known as the Queen's Cupress, is a variant of the Torulosa Cupress that exhibits distinct botanical features.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown, which gives it an elegant appearance. The leaves are scale-like and closely appressed to the branches, contributing to its compact and formal look. It produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green cones, typical of the Cupressaceae family.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
