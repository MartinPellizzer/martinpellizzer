Calanthe masuca var. fulgens

Calanthe masuca var. fulgens, commonly known as the flame calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a potted plant.
