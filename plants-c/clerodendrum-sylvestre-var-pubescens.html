Clerodendrum sylvestre var. pubescens

Clerodendrum sylvestre var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves that are often arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
