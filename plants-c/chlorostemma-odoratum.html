Chlorostemma odoratum

Chlorostemma odoratum, commonly known as the fragrant greenstem, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.

Chlorostemma odoratum thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun exposure.
