Crotalaria lynesii

Crotalaria lynesii, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and hairy, glandular stems. The plant typically grows in sandy or disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and fields. It is an annual herb that produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

The plant plays a role in supporting pollinators and can be used in ecological restoration projects.
