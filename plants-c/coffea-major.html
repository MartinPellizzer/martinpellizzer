Coffea major

Coffea major, commonly known as the large coffee plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Ethiopia, where it grows in high altitudes. Coffea major is valued for its robust growth and adaptability to various soil types in its native habitat.
