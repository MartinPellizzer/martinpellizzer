Cicerbita candolleana

Cicerbita candolleana, commonly known as the Candolleana bittercress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
