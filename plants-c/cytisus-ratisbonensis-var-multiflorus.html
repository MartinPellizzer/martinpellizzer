Cytisus ratisbonensis var. multiflorus

Cytisus ratisbonensis var. multiflorus, commonly known as the Ratisbon bush or yellow broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. The plant has slender, zigzagging stems and small, bipinnate leaves that are compound and trifoliate. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedging plant.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in regions with mild winters.
