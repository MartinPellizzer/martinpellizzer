Conyza groegeri

Conyza groegeri, commonly known as the hairy weed or groeger's conyza, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas. The plant features small, white to yellowish flowers and hairy stems and leaves. It is native to southern Africa but has been introduced to other regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
