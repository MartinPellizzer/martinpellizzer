Clidemia biolleyana

Clidemia biolleyana, commonly known as the silverleaf false mallow, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming during the warmer months.
