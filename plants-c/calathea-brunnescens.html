Calathea brunnescens

Calathea brunnescens, commonly known as the bronze-leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display a unique pattern of dark green and bronze hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea brunnescens is relatively low-maintenance but sensitive to temperature fluctuations and direct sunlight.
