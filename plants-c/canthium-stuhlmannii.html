Canthium stuhlmannii

Canthium stuhlmannii, commonly known as the African wolfberry, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa, characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
