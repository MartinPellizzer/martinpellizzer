Cryptotaenia calycina var. calycina

Cryptotaenia calycina var. calycina, commonly known as Japanese cow parsley, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It produces small white flowers in umbrella-like clusters, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
