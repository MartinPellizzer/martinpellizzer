Crassula falx

Crassula falx, commonly known as the sickle leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula falx is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
