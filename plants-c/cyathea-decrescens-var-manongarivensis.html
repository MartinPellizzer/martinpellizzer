Cyathea decrescens var. manongarivensis

Cyathea decrescens var. manongarivensis, commonly known as the Manongarivensis tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its massive fronds, which are essential for photosynthesis.

This variant is notable for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
