Canna stenantha

Canna stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, providing a dramatic foliage effect. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers with long, narrow petals, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Canna stenantha is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and wetland habitats.
