Cyperus obstinatus

Cyperus obstinatus, commonly known as the stubborn flatsedge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. Its grass-like appearance features narrow, linear leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences.

Cyperus obstinatus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
