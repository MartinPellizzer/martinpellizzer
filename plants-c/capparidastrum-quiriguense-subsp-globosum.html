Capparidastrum quiriguense subsp. globosum

Capparidastrum quiriguense subsp.

globosum, commonly known as the globular capparid, is a flowering plant native to Central America. It belongs to the Capparaceae family, which includes various species with distinctive floral structures.

The plant is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and fleshy, green leaves. It produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered together, followed by capsule fruits.

This subspecies is adapted to tropical and subtropical environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
