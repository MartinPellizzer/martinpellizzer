Crassula pubescens subsp. pubescens

Crassula pubescens subsp.

pubescens, commonly known as the woolly crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features dense, fuzzy foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance.

The leaves are typically blue-green in color and are arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
