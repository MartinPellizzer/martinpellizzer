Clitoria hanceana

Clitoria hanceana, commonly known as the winged bean or cluster bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking violet-blue flowers with distinctive wing-like petals that aid in pollination by attracting bees and butterflies. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines. Its leaves are also consumed as a vegetable in some regions.

Clitoria hanceana is valued not only for its nutritional content but also for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
