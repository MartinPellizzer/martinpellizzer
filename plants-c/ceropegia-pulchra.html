Ceropegia pulchra

Ceropegia pulchra, commonly known as the "Bead Leaf" or "String of Hearts," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia pulchra is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
