Cavendishia guatemalensis

Cavendishia guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan Cavendish, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its succulent leaves. It produces fruit that is edible and has a mild, sweet flavor.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
