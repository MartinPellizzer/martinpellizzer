Clitoria densiflora

Clitoria densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It features heart-shaped, bright green leaves and produces clusters of vibrant purple or blue flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense inflorescences and is often found in moist, sunny habitats. It has a fibrous root system and produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Clitoria densiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in some cultures.
