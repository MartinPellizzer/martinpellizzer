Chrysocoma graveolens

Chrysocoma graveolens, commonly known as the African bitter melon, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in groups of three. Its fruit is a small, bitter, orange-yellow berry that ripens in late summer.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
