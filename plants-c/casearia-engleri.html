Casearia engleri

Casearia engleri, commonly known as the White Flame Tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.
