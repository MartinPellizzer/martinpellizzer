Calathea peregrina

Calathea peregrina, commonly known as the wandering spider plant, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, arrow-shaped leaves that exhibit a mesmerizing pattern of contrasting green and silver hues. The plant's foliage is often adorned with intricate veins that create a visually appealing texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea peregrina is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
