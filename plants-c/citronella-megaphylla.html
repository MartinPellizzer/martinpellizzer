Citronella megaphylla

Citronella megaphylla, commonly known as the giant citronella or broadleaf citronella, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, aromatic leaves that emit a strong citronella scent, which is often used as a natural mosquito repellent. The plant belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is known for its tall, slender growth habit. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

Citronella megaphylla is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and for its practical use in pest control.
