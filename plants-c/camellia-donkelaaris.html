Camellia donkelaaris

Camellia donkelaaris, commonly known as the Donkelaaris camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, camellia-like flowers that bloom in winter or early spring. The flowers are typically pink or red, with a distinctive waxy appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

It is also valued for its fragrance and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
