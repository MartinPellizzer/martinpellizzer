Castalia odorata f. rosea

Castalia odorata f. rosea, commonly known as the pink castalia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking pinkish-white spathe and a narrow, elongated spadix, which are typical features of the genus Castalia.
