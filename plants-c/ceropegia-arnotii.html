Ceropegia arnotii

Ceropegia arnotii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia arnotii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
