Cyrtochilum fractiflexum

Cyrtochilum fractiflexum, commonly known as the bent-stem orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often curved stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or yellow with a distinctive, intricate pattern. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Cyrtochilum fractiflexum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
