Crassula karasana

Crassula karasana, commonly known as the Karasana Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
