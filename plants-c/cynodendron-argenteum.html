Cynodendron argenteum

Cynodendron argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among gardeners.
