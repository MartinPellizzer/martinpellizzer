Cyperus lanceus var. ramosus

Cyperus lanceus var. ramosus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, three-lobed flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, such as swamps and seasonally flooded areas. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in suitable habitats.
