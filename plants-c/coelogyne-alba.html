Coelogyne alba

Coelogyne alba, commonly known as the white coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous white, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms once a year, often during the winter months. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows along the pseudobulbs.

Coelogyne alba is prized in horticulture for its elegant appearance and ease of cultivation.
