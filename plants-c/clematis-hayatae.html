Clematis hayatae

Clematis hayatae, commonly known as the Japanese vine, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple in color. Clematis hayatae thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
