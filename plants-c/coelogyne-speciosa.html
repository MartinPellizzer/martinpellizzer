Coelogyne speciosa

Coelogyne speciosa, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. Its flowers are showy, with a complex structure that includes multiple petals and a prominent lip.

Coelogyne speciosa is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its fragrance and visual appeal.
