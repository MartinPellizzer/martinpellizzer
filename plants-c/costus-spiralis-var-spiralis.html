Costus spiralis var. spiralis

Costus spiralis var. spiralis, commonly known as the spiral costus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Due to its elegant foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
