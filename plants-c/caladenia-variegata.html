Caladenia variegata

Caladenia variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Spider-orchid, is a striking orchid native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a single, slender stem with a single flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its sepals and petals are often adorned with intricate patterns of red, yellow, and green.

Caladenia variegata is a terrestrial orchid that blooms during late spring to early summer.
