Cirsium Callilepis Var. Oregonense

Cirsium callilepis var. oregonense is a rare and striking plant native to the western United States. Known for its spiny leaves and vibrant purple flowers, it thrives in dry, rocky habitats. This variant of the thistle is often found in coastal areas and high elevation meadows. Its unique appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts. Protecting this plant is essential for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
