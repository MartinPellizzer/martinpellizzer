Callista bandonia

Callista bandonia, commonly known as the velvet tassel plant, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa, particularly found in the Eastern Cape and Western Cape provinces. The plant is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that are surrounded by pale green bracts, giving it a velvety appearance. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive texture.

Callista bandonia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
