Chiazospermum

Chiazospermum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elongated, woody pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. Chiazospermum is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
