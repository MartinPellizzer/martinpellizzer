Cistus × ovalifolius

Cistus × ovalifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves that are typically narrow and elliptical in shape. The plant produces vibrant pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Cistus × ovalifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand drought conditions.
