Canthium tetraphyllum

The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family, and are often surrounded by specialized bracts.
