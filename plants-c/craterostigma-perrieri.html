Craterostigma perrieri

Craterostigma perrieri, commonly known as the mountain rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a small shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and grayish-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing drought tolerance and efficient water use.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
