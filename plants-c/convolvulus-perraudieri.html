Convolvulus perraudieri

Convolvulus perraudieri, commonly known as the Perraudier's bindweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features tendrils that allow it to climb and twine around other vegetation. Its flowers are usually small, tubular, and come in shades of pink or purple.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along rocky outcrops.
