Cyperus alternifolius

Cyperus alternifolius, commonly known as umbrella plant or zigzag sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features distinctive, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a zigzag pattern along its stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist to wet environments, making it a popular choice for water gardens and bog habitats.

Its adaptability and ornamental appearance make it a favored plant in horticulture.
