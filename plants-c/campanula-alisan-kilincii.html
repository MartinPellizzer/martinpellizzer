Campanula alisan-kilincii

Campanula alisan-kilincii, commonly known as the Alishan-Kilincii bellflower, is a rare and endemic species native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, preferring moist, well-drained soils in mountainous environments. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
