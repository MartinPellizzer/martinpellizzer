Calanthe × mylesii

Calanthe × mylesii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Calanthe tripeta and Calanthe discolor.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are typically lanceolate and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in cool, shaded environments.

Calanthe × mylesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
