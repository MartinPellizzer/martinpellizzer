Carex rara f. yunnanensis

Carex rara f. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan sedge, is a rare and ornamental plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact, clustered inflorescences. Native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its foliage is often a deep green, adding a striking visual contrast in garden settings.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and botanists alike.
