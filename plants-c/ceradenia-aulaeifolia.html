Ceradenia aulaeifolia

Ceradenia aulaeifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf ceradenia, is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Ceradenia aulaeifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in botanical gardens for its distinctive foliage.
