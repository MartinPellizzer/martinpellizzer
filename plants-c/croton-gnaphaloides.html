Croton gnaphaloides

Croton gnaphaloides, commonly known as the silver croton, is a shrub native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton gnaphaloides thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.
