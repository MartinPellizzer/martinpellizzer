Croton tiglium var. xiaopadou

Croton tiglium var. xiaopadou, commonly known as the Chinese croton or small padou.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to China. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that often display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing toxic seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though caution is advised due to the presence of toxic compounds in its parts.
