Chaenomeles × superba f. alba

Chaenomeles × superba f. alba, commonly known as the white flowering quince, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and oval, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant white flowers in early spring before the leaves emerge. The fruit is a yellow, fleshy pome that ripens in late summer.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
