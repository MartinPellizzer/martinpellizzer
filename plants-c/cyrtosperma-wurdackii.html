Cyrtosperma wurdackii

Cyrtosperma wurdackii, commonly known as the Borneo arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in an inflorescence. Its rhizomes are used as a starchy food source, similar to traditional arrowroot.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and culinary applications in its native region.
