Cliffortia ferricola

Cliffortia ferricola, commonly known as the ironwood daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, needle-like leaves that are adapted to arid environments. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its resilience to drought and heat makes it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping.
