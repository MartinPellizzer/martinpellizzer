Cassia stellata

Cassia stellata, commonly known as star cassia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, slender pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Cassia stellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
