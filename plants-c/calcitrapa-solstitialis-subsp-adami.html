Calcitrapa solstitialis subsp. adami

Calcitrapa solstitialis subsp.

adami, commonly known as the summer evening orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant has a rosette-like growth habit and produces showy, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone-rich habitats.
