Cremastosperma dolichocarpum

Cremastosperma dolichocarpum, commonly known as the long-seeded senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated, woody pods, which contribute to its common name.
