Clerodendrum heterophyllum f. angustifolium

Clerodendrum heterophyllum f. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences and are often purple or pink in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
