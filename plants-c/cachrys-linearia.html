Cachrys linearia

Cachrys linearia, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It typically features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. It grows in rocky soils and is often found in desert or coastal areas.

Cachrys linearia is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
