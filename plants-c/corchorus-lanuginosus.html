Corchorus lanuginosus

Corchorus lanuginosus, commonly known as silky touch-hair, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features hairy, soft leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small, pale yellow flowers in spikes. The plant is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its fibrous stems and leaves have been historically used in traditional medicine and textile production.

Corchorus lanuginosus is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
