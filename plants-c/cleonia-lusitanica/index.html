Cleonia Lusitanica

Cleonia lusitanica is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to specific regions of southern Europe. It thrives in rocky and dry environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions. This small shrub is characterized by its narrow leaves and clusters of small, pale flowers. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in its local ecosystem. Further study is needed to fully understand its ecological significance and conservation status.
