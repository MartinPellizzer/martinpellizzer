Croton cardenasii

Croton cardenasii, commonly known as the Cienaga croton, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Croton cardenasii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
