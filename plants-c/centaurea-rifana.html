Centaurea rifana

Centaurea rifana, commonly known as the Rif centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea rifana is often found in mountainous areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
