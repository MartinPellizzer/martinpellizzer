Calonema Evanescens

Calonema evanescens is a rare and intriguing plant known for its fleeting beauty. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This species is characterized by its delicate, ephemeral flowers that bloom briefly before fading away. Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from habitat loss and over-collection.
