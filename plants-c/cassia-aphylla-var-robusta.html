Cassia aphylla var. robusta

Cassia aphylla var. robusta, commonly known as the robust cassia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and compound leaves. The plant features feathery, bipinnate leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in elongated pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and floral display.
