Crotalaria orbicularis

Crotalaria orbicularis, commonly known as the globe cowpea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive round, orbicular leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes, and it is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Crotalaria orbicularis is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop due to its nutritional value.
