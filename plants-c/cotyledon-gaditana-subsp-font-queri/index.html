Cotyledon Gaditana Subsp. Font-Queri

Cotyledon gaditana subsp. font-queri is a rare and striking succulent native to southern Spain. It is known for its thick, fleshy leaves that often display vibrant patterns of red, purple, and green. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it is relatively uncommon in the wild and is often cultivated by plant lovers.
