Callitriche pedunculata f. terrestris

Callitriche pedunculata f. terrestris, commonly known as the terrestrial callitriche, is a low-growing aquatic or semi-aquatic plant characterized by its small, floating or submersed leaves.

It typically features whorled arrangements of narrow, linear leaves that are often less than 1 cm in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are usually borne on short pedicels, and it is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.
