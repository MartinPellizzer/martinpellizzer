Centaurea occasus

Centaurea occasus, commonly known as the evening centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking purple-blue flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while its stems are erect and branched.

Centaurea occasus is often found in open habitats such as coastal areas and dry meadows.
