Conyza miniata

Conyza miniata, commonly known as the smallflower ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Conyza miniata is often found in disturbed soils and can be an indicator of wetland ecosystems.
