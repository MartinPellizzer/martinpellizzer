Coryanthes elegantium

Coryanthes elegantium, commonly known as the elegant coryanthes, is a striking tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter, with intricate, spurred petals and a distinctive lip. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its flowers are often pollinated by specific insect species, showcasing a unique ecological relationship.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
