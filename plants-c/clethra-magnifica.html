Clethra magnifica

Clethra magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Clethra magnifica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
