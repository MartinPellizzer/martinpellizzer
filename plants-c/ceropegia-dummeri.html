Ceropegia dummeri

Ceropegia dummeri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia dummeri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
