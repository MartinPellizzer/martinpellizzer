Chrysanthemum Gayanum

Chrysanthemum gayanum is a rare and striking flowering plant native to Sri Lanka. Known for its vibrant yellow blooms and unique foliage, it holds cultural and medicinal significance in local traditions. This species thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its delicate petals and compact growth make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental beauty. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered plant from habitat loss and overharvesting.
