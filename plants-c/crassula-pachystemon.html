Crassula pachystemon

Crassula pachystemon, commonly known as the rock rose or stone plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula pachystemon is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
