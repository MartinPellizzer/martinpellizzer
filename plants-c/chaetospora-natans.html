Chaetospora natans

Chaetospora natans, commonly known as the floating hair moss, is a unique aquatic plant belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, filamentous structure that allows it to drift on the surface of water. The plant forms dense mats that provide habitat for microorganisms and small aquatic life. Chaetospora natans thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and wetlands.
