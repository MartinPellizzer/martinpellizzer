Carpinus hupeana var. simplicidentata

Carpinus hupeana var. simplicidentata, commonly known as the Chinese hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It features small, ovate leaves with a smooth, dark green upper surface and a lighter, hairy underside. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by oval, woody fruits. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
