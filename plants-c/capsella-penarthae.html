Capsella penarthae

Capsella penarthae, commonly known as the Penarth weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a small annual herb characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Native to parts of Europe, it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and urban environments.
