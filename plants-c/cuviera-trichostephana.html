Cuviera trichostephana

Cuviera trichostephana, commonly known as the Mexican featherflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters that resemble feathery structures.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
