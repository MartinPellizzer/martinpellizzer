Cotyledon Font-Queri

Cotyledon fontqueri is a unique succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its striking, colorful leaves that often display a gradient of hues. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water. Its compact growth makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its beauty, it can be sensitive to overwatering and frost.
