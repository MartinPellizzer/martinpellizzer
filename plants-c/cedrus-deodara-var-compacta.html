Cedrus deodara var. compacta

Cedrus deodara var. compacta, commonly known as the Himalayan Blue Atlas Cedar, is a compact variety of the Deodar cedar native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, conical shape and dark blue-green foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, woody cones that contain aromatic seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a decorative specimen plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to tolerate cold climates.
