Cubeba siriboa

Cubeba siriboa, commonly known as cubeb, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that grows up to 1.5 meters tall, characterized by its aromatic, dark brown berries. The plant has pinnate leaves and produces small yellow flowers. Its fruit is used in traditional medicine and as a spice due to its essential oils.

The botanical features of cubeba siriboa make it valuable in both horticulture and pharmaceutical applications.
