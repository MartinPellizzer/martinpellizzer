Chlorophytum longiphyllum

Chlorophytum longiphyllum, commonly known as the sword lily or spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in racemes, often followed by bright blue or purple berries. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
