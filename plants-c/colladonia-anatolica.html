Colladonia anatolica

Colladonia anatolica, commonly known as the Anatolian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey.

It features showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or open habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
