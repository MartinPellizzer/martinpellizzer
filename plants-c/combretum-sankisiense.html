Combretum sankisiense

Combretum sankisiense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. It produces small, three-lobed fruits that contain one or two seeds.

Combretum sankisiense is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
