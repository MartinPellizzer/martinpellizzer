Codiaeum reginae

Codiaeum reginae, commonly known as the flame leaf or queen's leaf, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its foliage changes with the seasons, making it a popular choice for adding visual interest to landscapes.
