Cotoneaster divaricatus

Cotoneaster divaricatus, commonly known as the variable cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically reddish-brown beneath, and it produces clusters of white flowers in spring. The plant bears red or orange berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Cotoneaster divaricatus is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
