Chrysophyllum calomeris

Chrysophyllum calomeris, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white flowers. Chrysophyllum calomeris is also appreciated for its ability to tolerate shade and poor soil conditions.
