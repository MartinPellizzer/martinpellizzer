Centaurea stenocarpa

Centaurea stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruit centaury, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in dry, rocky, or sandy habitats. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers with yellow centers, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the fruits are small, cylindrical achenes.

Centaurea stenocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
