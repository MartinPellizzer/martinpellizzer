Cryptandra wilsonii

Cryptandra wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's cryptandra, is a small shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in full sun.

Cryptandra wilsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
