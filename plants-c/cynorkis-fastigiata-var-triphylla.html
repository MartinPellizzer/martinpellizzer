Cynorkis fastigiata var. triphylla

Cynorkis fastigiata var. triphylla, commonly known as the triphylla fern, is a unique variety of the fern species Cynorkis fastigiata.

It is characterized by its distinctive triphyllous structure, where each frond is divided into three separate leaflets. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland areas or along stream banks. Its fronds are narrow and elongated, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
