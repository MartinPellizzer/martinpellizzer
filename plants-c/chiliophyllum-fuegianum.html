Chiliophyllum fuegianum

Chiliophyllum fuegianum, commonly known as the Fuegian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in alpine or coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
