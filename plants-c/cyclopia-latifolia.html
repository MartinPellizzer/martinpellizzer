Cyclopia latifolia

Cyclopia latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved cyclopia, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South Africa, particularly the Western and Eastern Cape provinces. The plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Cyclopia latifolia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
