Cerasus × Moniwana

Cerasus × moniwana is a unique hybrid plant resulting from the cross between cherry and peach species. It combines the ornamental beauty of cherries with the fruiting potential of peaches. This plant is prized for its vibrant flowers and edible fruits that ripen in late summer. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Due to its versatility, it is a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and small-scale farming.
