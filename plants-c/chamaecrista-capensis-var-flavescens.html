Chamaecrista capensis var. flavescens

Chamaecrista capensis var. flavescens, commonly known as yellow burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, spiny seed pods that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
