Camellia japonica var. donckelarii

Camellia japonica var. donckelarii, commonly known as the Donckelarii camellia, is a rare and ornamental variant of the Japanese camellia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant is native to China and is valued for its winter blooming habit, which adds visual interest to gardens during the colder months. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This variety is also noted for its evergreen foliage, which provides year-round structure and beauty.
