Codiaeum chrysosticton var. angustifolium

Codiaeum chrysosticton var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage that displays shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant has palmate leaves with elongated, narrow lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
