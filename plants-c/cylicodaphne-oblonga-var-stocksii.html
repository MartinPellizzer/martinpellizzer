Cylicodaphne oblonga var. stocksii

Cylicodaphne oblonga var. stocksii, commonly known as the variegated sago palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk that produces a crown of feathery, pinnately compound leaves. The leaves are typically bright green with white or yellow variegation, adding visual interest to the plant. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
