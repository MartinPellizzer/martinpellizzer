Cyathea runensis

Cyathea runensis, commonly known as the Runa tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Cyathea runensis has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, making it a long-lived species.

Its fronds are used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants in horticulture.
