Columnea tulae var. tomentulosa

Columnea tulae var. tomentulosa, commonly known as the velvety columnea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety foliage and clusters of tubular, red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
