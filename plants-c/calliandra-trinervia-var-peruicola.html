Calliandra trinervia var. peruicola

Calliandra trinervia var. peruicola, commonly known as the Peruvian calliandra, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features showy, clustered pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves, with three leaflets arranged along a central stem.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil conditions.
