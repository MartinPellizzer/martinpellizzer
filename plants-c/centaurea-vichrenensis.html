Centaurea vichrenensis

Centaurea vichrenensis, commonly known as the Vichrenes Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Centaurea vichrenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
