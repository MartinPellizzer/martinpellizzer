Cytisus heuffelii var. romanicus

Cytisus heuffelii var. romanicus, commonly known as the Romanican broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has slender, woody stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal and dry environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
