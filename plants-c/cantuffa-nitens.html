Cantuffa nitens

Cantuffa nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils. The plant features a basal rosette of silver-gray, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are yellow and appear in late spring, followed by fluffy white seed heads.

Cantuffa nitens is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered a hardy, resilient species.
