Cyathea tenuis

Cyathea tenuis, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Cyathea tenuis has a slow growth rate and can live for many decades, making it a long-lived component of its ecosystem.

Its distinctive appearance and ecological role make it an important species in New Zealand's native flora.
