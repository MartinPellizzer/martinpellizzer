Crassula brachystachya

Crassula brachystachya, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula brachystachya is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
