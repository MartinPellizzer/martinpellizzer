Cylindrolobus tenuicaulis

Cylindrolobus tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed globular plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, globular shape with short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
