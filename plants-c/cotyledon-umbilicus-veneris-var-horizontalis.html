Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. horizontalis

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. horizontalis, commonly known as the horizontal cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, and they often display striking red or purple margins. The plant produces small, pale green flowers on slender stalks during the spring or summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
