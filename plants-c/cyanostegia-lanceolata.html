Cyanostegia lanceolata

Cyanostegia lanceolata, commonly known as the bluestem panicgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that often display a bluish tint, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and landscape design.
