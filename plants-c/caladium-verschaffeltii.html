Caladium verschaffeltii

Caladium verschaffeltii, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a lush green background. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant color contrast.

Caladium verschaffeltii is relatively low maintenance, requiring well-draining soil and regular watering.
