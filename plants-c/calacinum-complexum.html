Calacinum complexum

Calacinum complexum, commonly known as the moonflower vine, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, night-blooming flowers that emit a strong, sweet fragrance.

The botanical structure of Calacinum complexum includes a fibrous root system and a woody stem with prominent veins.
