Croton barorum

Croton barorum, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from bright red to orange and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
