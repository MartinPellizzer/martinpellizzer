Canarium odontophyllum f. multifidum

Canarium odontophyllum f. multifidum, commonly known as the multi-lobed candlenut.

It is a variant of the candlenut tree, characterized by its distinctive, deeply lobed leaves. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist lowland forests. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a serrated margin, contributing to its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued for its potential in traditional medicine and as a source of oil.
