Cassia × floribunda

Cassia × floribunda, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense, cylindrical clusters, typically from spring to early summer. The plant produces long, narrow, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. Its flowers are rich in nectar and attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Cassia × floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a street tree due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
