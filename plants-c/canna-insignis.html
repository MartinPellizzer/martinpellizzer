Canna insignis

Canna insignis, commonly known as the red canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves and showy, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Canna insignis is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
