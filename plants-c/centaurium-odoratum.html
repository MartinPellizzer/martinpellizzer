Centaurium odoratum

Centaurium odoratum, commonly known as sweet centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is known for its pleasant, sweet scent, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in bloom.
