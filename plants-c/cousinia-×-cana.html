Cousinia × cana

Cousinia × cana, commonly known as the hybrid snowball plant, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white or pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

Cousinia × cana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soils in harsh environments.
