Callisthene mollissima

Callisthene mollissima, commonly known as the silky callisthene, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a distinct waxy texture.

Callisthene mollissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
