Chrysothemis friedrichsthaliana

Chrysothemis friedrichsthaliana, commonly known as the golden tawny daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers with a central disk. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal or inland regions.
