Cliffortia propinqua var. chamissonis

Cliffortia propinqua var. chamissonis, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate ornamental value. Its leaves are usually narrow and succulent, adapted to withstand arid conditions.

It is valued in horticulture for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained environments.
