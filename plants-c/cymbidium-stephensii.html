Cymbidium stephensii

Cymbidium stephensii, commonly known as the Stephens' orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant prefers cool, humid environments and is often found growing in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, contributing to its striking visual appearance.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Cymbidium stephensii is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
