Couma multinervis

Couma multinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined couma, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with a distinct network of veins, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a legume, containing several seeds enclosed in a fibrous pod.

Couma multinervis is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
