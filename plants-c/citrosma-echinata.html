Citrosma echinata

Citrosma echinata, commonly known as the spiny citrosma, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its rough, spiny bark and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by oval, woody fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves and fruits are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
