Croton microtiglium

Croton microtiglium, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a small shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
