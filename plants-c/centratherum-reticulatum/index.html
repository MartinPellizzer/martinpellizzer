Centratherum Reticulatum

Centratherum reticulatum is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its delicate, yellow blooms that appear in clusters during the summer months. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its intricate leaf patterns and vibrant flowers make it a standout in botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
