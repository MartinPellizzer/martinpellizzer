Crassula lucens

Crassula lucens, commonly known as the "Sunlight Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring and summer months. It prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its low maintenance and ornamental appeal make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
