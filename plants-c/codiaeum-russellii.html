Codiaeum russellii

Codiaeum russellii, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers followed by winged fruits that disperse its seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Codiaeum russellii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
