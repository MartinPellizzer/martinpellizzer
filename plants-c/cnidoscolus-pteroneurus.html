Cnidoscolus pteroneurus, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, warm climates. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Its stems are often armed with spines, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores.
