Capparis oleoides

Capparis oleoides, commonly known as the Mediterranean caper, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features prickly, woody stems and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a dark purple color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Capparis oleoides is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor, rocky soils.
