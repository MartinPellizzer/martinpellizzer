Crotalaria bupleurifolia

Crotalaria bupleurifolia, commonly known as the Indian crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual with hairy stems and alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom during the warm season. Its seeds are encased in a distinctive, inflated pod that splits open when mature.

Crotalaria bupleurifolia is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
