Cissus argentea

Cissus argentea, commonly known as silver arrow or silver cissus, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cissus argentea is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
