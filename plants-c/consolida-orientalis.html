Consolida orientalis

Consolida orientalis, commonly known as the oriental sweet pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and features striking, colorful, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and has compound leaves with a feathery appearance. Its flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white, and it produces elongated seed pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds.

The plant is known for its vigorous growth and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
