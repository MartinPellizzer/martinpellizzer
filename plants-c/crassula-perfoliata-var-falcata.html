Crassula perfoliata var. falcata

Crassula perfoliata var. falcata, commonly known as the falcate crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, waxy leaves that are often curved or twisted, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight.

Due to its unique foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
