Centaurea resupinata subsp. rifana

Centaurea resupinata subsp.

rifana, commonly known as the Rif sand centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and linear. Its flowers are bright yellow with a distinctive red or purple center, forming dense clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in dry gardens.
