Corchorus argutus var. typica

Corchorus argutus var. typica, commonly known as the Chinese brookweed, is a herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often near water sources. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and arranged in spikes.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
