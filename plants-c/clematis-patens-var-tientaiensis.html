Clematis patens var. tientaiensis

Clematis patens var. tientaiensis, commonly known as the Tientai Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
