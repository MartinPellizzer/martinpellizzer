Chamaecrista nictitans var. leptadenia

Chamaecrista nictitans var. leptadenia, commonly known as the slender leaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and agricultural purposes.
