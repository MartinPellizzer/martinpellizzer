Calamus ciliaris

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, often forming dense stands. The plant features long, slender stems that are often used in basket weaving and other traditional crafts. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a subtle fragrance.
