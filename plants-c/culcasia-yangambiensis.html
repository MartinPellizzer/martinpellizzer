Culcasia yangambiensis

Culcasia yangambiensis, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf, is a large herbaceous plant native to the lowland rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its enormous, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and has a robust root system that helps it anchor in moist soil. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Culcasia yangambiensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its striking foliage.
