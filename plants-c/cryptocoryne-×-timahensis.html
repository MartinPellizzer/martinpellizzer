Cryptocoryne × timahensis

Cryptocoryne × timahensis, commonly known as the Timahensis cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that often exhibit a velvety texture. This plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is well-suited for planted aquariums. It is relatively hardy and can tolerate a range of water parameters, making it a popular choice for hobbyists.

The plant's ability to grow in both freshwater and brackish environments adds to its versatility in aquatic setups.
