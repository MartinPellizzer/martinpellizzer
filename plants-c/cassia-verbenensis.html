Cassia verbenensis

Cassia verbenensis, commonly known as yellow cassia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces elongated pods containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Cassia verbenensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
