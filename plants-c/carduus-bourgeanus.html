Carduus bourgeanus

Carduus bourgeanus, commonly known as the common thistle.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features spiny leaves and a purple to pink flower head that resembles a thistle. It typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

The species is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.
