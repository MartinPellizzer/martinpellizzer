Celosia bakeri

Celosia bakeri, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its upright growth habit and compact size. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Celosia bakeri thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
