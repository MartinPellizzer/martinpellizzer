Centaurea varnensis

Centaurea varnensis, commonly known as the Varnensian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with purple markings.

Centaurea varnensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
