Chamaecrista bissei

Chamaecrista bissei, commonly known as the Brazilian burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, yellow,蝶形花 (butterfly-shaped) flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. It produces small, spiny pods that contain several seeds.

Chamaecrista bissei is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
