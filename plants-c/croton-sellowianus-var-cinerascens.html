Croton sellowianus var. cinerascens

Croton sellowianus var. cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a distinctive silver or grayish-white underside. The leaves are typically alternate, simple, and have a rough, textured surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
