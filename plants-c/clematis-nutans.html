Clematis nutans

Clematis nutans, commonly known as the drooping clematis, is a flowering vine native to Asia.

It is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale blue flowers. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are typically green and hairy on the underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Clematis nutans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
