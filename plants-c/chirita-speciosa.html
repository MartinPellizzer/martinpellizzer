Chirita speciosa

Chirita speciosa, commonly known as the showy chirita, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. It typically grows as an herbaceous perennial with fleshy, oval-shaped leaves.

Chirita speciosa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
