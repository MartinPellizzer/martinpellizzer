Cymbidium yakibaran

Cymbidium yakibaran, commonly known as the Yakibaran orchid, is a species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a yellow or green throat. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium yakibaran is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
