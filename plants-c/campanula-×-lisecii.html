Campanula × lisecii

Campanula × lisecii, commonly known as the hybrid bellflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula × lisecii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
