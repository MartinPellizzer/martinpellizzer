Ceropegia wilsonii

Ceropegia wilsonii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing vines and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia wilsonii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
