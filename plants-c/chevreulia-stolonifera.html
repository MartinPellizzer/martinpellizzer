Chevreulia stolonifera

Chevreulia stolonifera, commonly known as the stoloniferous chevreulia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its woody stems and sprawling growth habit, often forming dense mats through stolons. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by round, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

It is valued in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
