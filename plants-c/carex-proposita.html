Carex proposita

Carex proposita, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. This species typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive blue-green coloration.

The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are often hidden among the foliage, contributing to its subtle appearance.
