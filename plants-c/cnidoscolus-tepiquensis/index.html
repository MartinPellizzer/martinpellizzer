Cnidoscolus Tepiquensis

Cnidoscolus tepiquensis is a unique plant native to parts of Central America. It is known for its striking appearance and unusual features. The plant has large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach impressive sizes. It is also notable for its ability to produce edible tubers, though caution is needed due to its toxic properties. This species holds cultural and ecological significance in its native regions.
