Calathea indecora

Calathea indecora, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns and a velvety texture. The leaves are typically oval or elliptical, and they often change color in response to light conditions. This plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ability to adapt to various environments contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
