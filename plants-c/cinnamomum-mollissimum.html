Cinnamomum mollissimum

Cinnamomum mollissimum, commonly known as the silky cinnamon tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are soft and velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue-black berries. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for aromatic purposes.

The plant is valued for its unique fragrance and is often cultivated for its ornamental and commercial uses.
