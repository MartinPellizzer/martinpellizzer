Ctenitis distans

Ctenitis distans, commonly known as the broad-leafed tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a unique structure with a fibrous root system and a crown of feathery leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Ctenitis distans thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
