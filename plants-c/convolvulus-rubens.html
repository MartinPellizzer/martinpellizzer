Convolvulus rubens

Convolvulus rubens, commonly known as red morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink to red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.

Convolvulus rubens is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail along surfaces.
