Cineraria hirsuta

Cineraria hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its woolly, hairy leaves and showy blue or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favored ornamental plant in Mediterranean climates.
