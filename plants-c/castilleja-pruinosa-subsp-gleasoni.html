Castilleja pruinosa subsp. gleasoni

Castilleja pruinosa subsp.

gleasoni, commonly known as the Gleason's sticky castilleja, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves.
