Coelogyne latens

Coelogyne latens, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers and long, pendant-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy texture.

Coelogyne latens is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
