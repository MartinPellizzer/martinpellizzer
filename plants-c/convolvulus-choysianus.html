Convolvulus choysianus

Convolvulus choysianus, commonly known as the Chinese morning glory, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to cover trellises or walls.
