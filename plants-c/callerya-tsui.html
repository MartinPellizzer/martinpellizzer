Callerya tsui

Callerya tsui, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

The flowers, which bloom in spring, are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive lip that attracts pollinators.
