Calanthe oblanceolata

Calanthe oblanceolata, commonly known as the broad-leafed calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe oblanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its elegant floral display.
