Crotalaria malangensis

Crotalaria malangensis, commonly known as Malang velvetleaf, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive crown-like structure. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as agricultural fields and forest edges.

Due to its invasive potential, it is considered a weed in many regions.
