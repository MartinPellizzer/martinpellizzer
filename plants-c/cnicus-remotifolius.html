Cnicus remotifolius

Cnicus remotifolius, commonly known as the common centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial plant with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, branching stems and leaves that are typically lobed or divided. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.

The flowers are pollinated by insects, and the plant produces small, dry fruits that disperse its seeds.
