Conophytum obcordellum subsp. stenandrum

Conophytum obcordellum subsp.

stenandrum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, fleshy, and often ribbed leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
