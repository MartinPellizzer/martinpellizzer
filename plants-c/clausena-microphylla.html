Clausena microphylla

Clausena microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved klipspringer, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 5 cm in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, orange-colored fruits that are similar in appearance to citrus. Its bark is smooth and greyish, with a fragrant aroma.

Clausena microphylla is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
