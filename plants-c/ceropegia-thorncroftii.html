Ceropegia thorncroftii

Ceropegia thorncroftii, commonly known as the Thorncroft's arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers that are often clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.
