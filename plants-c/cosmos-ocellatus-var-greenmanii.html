Cosmos ocellatus var. greenmanii

Cosmos ocellatus var. greenmanii, commonly known as the greenmanii cosmos, is a striking variant of the cosmos plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

This variety is native to Mexico and thrives in full sun, producing clusters of pink to purple blooms that are both ornamental and attract pollinators. The plant features slender, upright stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves that add a lush contrast to its colorful flowers. It is a popular choice for gardens due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical beauty makes it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
