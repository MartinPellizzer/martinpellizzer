Crassula clavifolia var. muirii

Crassula clavifolia var. muirii, commonly known as the Muir's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a slight blue or grayish hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
