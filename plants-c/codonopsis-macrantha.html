Codonopsis macrantha

Codonopsis macrantha, commonly known as the Chinese ginseng or the "golden bell" plant, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue. Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and medicinal purposes.
