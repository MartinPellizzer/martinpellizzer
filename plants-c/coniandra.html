Coniandra

Coniandra, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. Its flowers, which come in shades of purple, pink, and white, bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
