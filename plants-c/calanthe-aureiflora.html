Calanthe aureiflora

Calanthe aureiflora, commonly known as the golden-flowered calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and striking yellow flowers with dark purple markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.

Calanthe aureiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
