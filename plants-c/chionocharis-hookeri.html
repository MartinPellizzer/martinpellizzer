Chionocharis hookeri

Chionocharis hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's snowbell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring.
