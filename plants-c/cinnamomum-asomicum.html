Cinnamomum asomicum

Cinnamomum asomicum, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or Cinnamomum asomaticum, is a species of flowering plant in the laurel family.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces dark brown, aromatic bark that is used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable resource in both culinary and medicinal applications.
