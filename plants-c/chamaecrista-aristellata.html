Chamaecrista aristellata

Chamaecrista aristellata, commonly known as the yellow brier or false poinsettia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant has a climbing or shrub-like growth habit and produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
