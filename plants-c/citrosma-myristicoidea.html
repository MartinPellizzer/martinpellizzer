Citrosma myristicoidea

Citrosma myristicoidea, commonly known as the fragrant myristic tree, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, which is often used in traditional medicine. Its essential oils are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.

Citrosma myristicoidea thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
