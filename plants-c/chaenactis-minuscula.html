Chaenactis minuscula

Chaenactis minuscula, commonly known as the dwarf rayhead, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in open, rocky habitats such as cliffs and talus slopes. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the end of each stem.

This species is adapted to arid environments and is often found in regions with low precipitation.
