Ceropegia erectiflora

Ceropegia erectiflora, commonly known as the upright-flowered ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing or ascending stems and small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white with a prominent red or purple corona. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia erectiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
