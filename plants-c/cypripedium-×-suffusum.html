Cypripedium × suffusum

Cypripedium × suffusum, commonly known as the hybrid yellow lady's slipper, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its showy, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has a single, erect stem that bears a single, large, pouch-like flower with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Cypripedium × suffusum thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
