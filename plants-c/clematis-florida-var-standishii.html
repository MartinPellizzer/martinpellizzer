Clematis florida var. standishii

Clematis florida var. standishii, commonly known as the Standishii Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring, typically in shades of white to pale blue. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
