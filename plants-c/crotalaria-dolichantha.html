Crotalaria dolichantha

Crotalaria dolichantha, commonly known as the long-seed cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant produces yellow flowers and bean-like pods that contain small, elongated seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and useful in agricultural settings.
