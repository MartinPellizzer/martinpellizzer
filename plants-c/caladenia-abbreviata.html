Caladenia abbreviata

Caladenia abbreviata, commonly known as the Western River Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Western Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along riverbanks and rocky outcrops. The plant produces a single erect stem with a few narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small, usually pink or purple, and are clustered in a loose spike.

Caladenia abbreviata is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in wetter habitats during the warmer months.
