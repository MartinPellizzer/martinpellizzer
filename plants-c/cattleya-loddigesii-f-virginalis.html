Cattleya loddigesii f. virginalis

Cattleya loddigesii f. virginalis, commonly known as the Virgin's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically produces showy, white or pale pink blooms with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and relatively easy care requirements.
