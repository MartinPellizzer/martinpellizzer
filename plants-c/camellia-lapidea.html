Camellia lapidea

Camellia lapidea, commonly known as the mountain camellia, is a species native to China.

It is a evergreen shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. The plant produces showy, camellia-like flowers that range in color from white to deep red. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blossoms.

Camellia lapidea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
