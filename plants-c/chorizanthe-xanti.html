Chorizanthe xanti

Chorizanthe xanti, commonly known as the desert holly, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils.
