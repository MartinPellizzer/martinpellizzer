Cyperus debilissimus

Cyperus debilissimus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flower heads. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wetland environments and moist soils. The plant typically grows close to the ground, forming dense mats that can colonize wetlands and disturbed areas.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it both a useful indicator species and a potential invasive in certain ecosystems.
