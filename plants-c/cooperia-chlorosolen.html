Cooperia chlorosolen

Cooperia chlorosolen, commonly known as the golden cooperia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescence. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Cooperia chlorosolen is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
