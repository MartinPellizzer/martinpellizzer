Cnicus souliei

Cnicus souliei, commonly known as the Chinese bur marigold, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, prickly fruits that contain seeds, aiding in its dispersal.

Cnicus souliei is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
