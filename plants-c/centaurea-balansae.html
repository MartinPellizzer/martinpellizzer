Centaurea balansae

Centaurea balansae, commonly known as the Balansae knapweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its bright blue or purple flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Centaurea balansae is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
