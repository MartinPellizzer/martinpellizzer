Cytisus supinus var. georgievii

Cytisus supinus var. georgievii, commonly known as the dwarf broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
