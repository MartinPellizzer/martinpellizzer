Corylopsis himalayana var. griffithii

Corylopsis himalayana var. griffithii, commonly known as the Himalayan flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in early spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that turn yellow in autumn, contributing to its seasonal aesthetic. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornate floral display.
