Chamaedorea flexuosa

Chamaedorea flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, arching fronds that create a cascading effect, making it an attractive choice for indoor gardening. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, thriving in humid environments. It is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and pruning.

Its ability to tolerate low light conditions makes it a popular ornamental plant in both residential and commercial settings.
