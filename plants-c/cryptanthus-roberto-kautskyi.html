Cryptanthus roberto-kautskyi

Cryptanthus roberto-kautskyi, commonly known as the Brazilian firecracker.

It is a member of the amaryllis family, native to Brazil, and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge.

It is well-suited for container gardening and thrives in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light.
