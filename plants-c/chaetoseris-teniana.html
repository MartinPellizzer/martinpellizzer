Chaetoseris teniana

Chaetoseris teniana, commonly known as the narrowleaf syntrichia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, arid environments. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in dry grasslands and along coastal cliffs.
