Chenolea sedoides

Chenolea sedoides, commonly known as the desert daisy, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. It has succulent leaves that help it retain water in dry environments.
