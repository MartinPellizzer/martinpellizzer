Cotula cuneifolia

Cotula cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Cotula cuneifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden settings for its delicate appearance.
