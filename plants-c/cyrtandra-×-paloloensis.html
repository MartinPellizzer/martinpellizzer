Cyrtandra × paloloensis

Cyrtandra × paloloensis, commonly known as the Palolo hybrid, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant is a hybrid between Cyrtandra pedata and Cyrtandra hawaiiensis, showcasing genetic diversity within the genus. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in coastal and lowland forests.

Due to its limited distribution and unique floral morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
