Cassia loefgreniana

Cassia loefgreniana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and dark brown, while its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

Cassia loefgreniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
