Calamagrostis cinnoides

Calamagrostis cinnoides, commonly known as Chinese silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in a silvery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces feathery plumes that add ornamental value, particularly in late summer and autumn. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds.

Calamagrostis cinnoides is valued in landscaping for its elegant form and ability to attract wildlife.
