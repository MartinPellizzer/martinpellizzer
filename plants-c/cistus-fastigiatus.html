Cistus fastigiatus

Cistus fastigiatus, commonly known as the upright rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, upright growth with small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments.

Its aromatic foliage and showy flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
