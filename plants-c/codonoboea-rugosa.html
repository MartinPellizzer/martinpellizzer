Codonoboea rugosa

Codonoboea rugosa, commonly known as the rough codonopsis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
