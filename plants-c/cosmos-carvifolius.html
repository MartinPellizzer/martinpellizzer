Cosmos carvifolius

Cosmos carvifolius, commonly known as the serrated cosmos, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has feathery, serrated leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Cosmos carvifolius is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
