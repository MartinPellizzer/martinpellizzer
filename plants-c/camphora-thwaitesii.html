Camphora thwaitesii

Camphora thwaitesii, commonly known as the Chinese camphor tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the樟科 (Lauraceae) family and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and fragrant bark. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark, spherical berries. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and distinctive camphor-like scent.
