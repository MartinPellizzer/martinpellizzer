Carex Humilis F. Callitrichos

Carex humilis f. callitrichos is a unique variant of the common sedge known for its distinct morphological characteristics. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, making it a common sight in woodland areas and wetlands. Its compact growth habit and dense foliage make it an attractive addition to ecological restoration projects. The subspecies is particularly valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity. Its presence often indicates a healthy, undisturbed ecosystem.
