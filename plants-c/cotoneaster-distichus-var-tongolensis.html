Cotoneaster distichus var. tongolensis

Cotoneaster distichus var. tongolensis, commonly known as the Tongolensis cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
