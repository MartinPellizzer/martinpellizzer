Crassula tenuipedicellata

Crassula tenuipedicellata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative indoor or outdoor plant.
