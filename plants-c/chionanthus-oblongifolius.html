Chionanthus oblongifolius

Chionanthus oblongifolius, commonly known as the Chinese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in pendulous racemes. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it suitable for gardens and landscapes where a delicate, spring-blooming shrub is desired.
