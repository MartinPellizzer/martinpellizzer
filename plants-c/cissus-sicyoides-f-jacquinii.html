Cissus sicyoides f. jacquinii

Cissus sicyoides f. jacquinii, commonly known as the Jacquin's rock fig, is a compact, trailing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and ability to climb or trail make it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.
