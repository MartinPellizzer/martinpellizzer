Clerodendrum chlorisepalum

Clerodendrum chlorisepalum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with pale green sepals. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs.
