Ceanothus zeylanicus

Ceanothus zeylanicus, commonly known as the Ceylon ceanothus, is a shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to violet, blooming in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to a subtropical climate.

Ceanothus zeylanicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
