Calliandra santanderensis

Calliandra santanderensis, commonly known as the Santander calliandra, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Colombia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, feather-like leaves and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as savannas and rocky areas. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid conditions, making it a hardy species for certain ecological niches.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in some regions for landscaping purposes.
