Chlorospatha boosii

Chlorospatha boosii, commonly known as the Boosii ghost plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, translucent, waxy leaves that resemble ghostly shapes, giving it its common name. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
