Citrus × ponki

Citrus × ponki, commonly known as the Kaffir lime.

This hybrid citrus plant is a cross between the citron and the mandarin orange. It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its rough, bumpy green peel and aromatic, segmented white flesh. The leaves are dark green and slightly serrated, while the flowers are small and fragrant.

The fruit is often used in culinary applications for its intense, citrusy flavor and is also valued for its essential oils.
