Calopyxis sphaeroides

Calopyxis sphaeroides, commonly known as the globe sphaeroides, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, fleshy fruits that resemble small, smooth balls. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
