Calamus politus

Calamus politus, commonly known as the smooth calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in spadices. Calamus politus is often found in wetlands and marshy areas, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
