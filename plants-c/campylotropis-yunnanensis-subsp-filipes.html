Campylotropis yunnanensis subsp. filipes

Campylotropis yunnanensis subsp.

filipes, commonly known as the Yunnan filiped crotalaria, is a shrub native to southwestern China. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, forested areas and is adapted to subtropical climates. It has a taproot system and produces seed pods that release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
