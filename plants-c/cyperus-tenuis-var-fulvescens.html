Cyperus tenuis var. fulvescens

Cyperus tenuis var. fulvescens, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower spikes. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes or along water bodies. The plant has a rhizomatous root system, which helps it spread vegetatively.

Its flowers are typically pale brown to reddish in color, giving it a distinctive appearance.
