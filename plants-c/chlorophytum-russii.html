Chlorophytum russii

Chlorophytum russii, commonly known as the Russian spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces long, arching stems that bear small, white, star-shaped flowers. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum russii is often used as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
