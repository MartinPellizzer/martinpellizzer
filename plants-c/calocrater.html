Calocrater

Calocrater, commonly known as the "spiny calycanthus," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Calycaraceae.

It consists of a single species, Calocrater calycinus, which is native to southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that are typically orange or red in color. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with a rough texture.

Calocrater is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
