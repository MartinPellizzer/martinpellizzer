Cleistanthus membranaceus

Cleistanthus membranaceus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cleistanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually narrow and membranous in texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a distinct coloration that attracts pollinators.

Cleistanthus membranaceus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
