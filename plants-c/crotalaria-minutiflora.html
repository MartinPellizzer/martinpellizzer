Crotalaria minutiflora

Crotalaria minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers. The plant has palmately compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

Additionally, it has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
