Carmichaelia enysii var. orbiculata

Carmichaelia enysii var. orbiculata, commonly known as the Enys' rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and features showy, yellow flowers with prominent central disks. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, arranged in a basal rosette. This variety is often found in rocky or coastal habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
