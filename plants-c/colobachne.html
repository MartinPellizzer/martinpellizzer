Colobachne

Colobachne, commonly known as the needlegrass or false rice, is a genus of grasses belonging to the Poaceae family.

These plants are typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in wetland or marshy environments. Colobachne species are characterized by their slender, grass-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences. They play a role in soil stabilization and provide habitat for various small organisms.

Some species are used in traditional medicine or as forage for livestock.
