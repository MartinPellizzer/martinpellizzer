Carex × ilseana

Carex × ilseana, commonly known as the hybrid sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Carex plantaginea and Carex breviculmis. This plant typically forms dense clumps of grass-like foliage with narrow, grass-green leaves. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Carex × ilseana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
