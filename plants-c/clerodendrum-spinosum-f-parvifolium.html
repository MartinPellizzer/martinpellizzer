Clerodendrum spinosum f. parvifolium

Clerodendrum spinosum f. parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaved spiny clerodendrum, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in summer. Its spiny stems and compact growth habit make it a unique addition to gardens.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
