Cheirolophus lagunae

Cheirolophus lagunae, commonly known as the Laguna's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pale blue or white in color. It is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
