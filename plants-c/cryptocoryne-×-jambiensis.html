Cryptocoryne × jambiensis

Cryptocoryne × jambiensis, commonly known as the Jambien cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, broad, and lance-shaped leaves that often develop a striking red or purple tint under optimal conditions. The plant thrives in low to moderate light environments and requires stable water parameters to maintain its vibrant coloration. It is a popular choice for aquariums due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.

Cryptocoryne × jambiensis is also known for its ability to tolerate a range of water temperatures and pH levels, making it a versatile addition to planted tanks.
