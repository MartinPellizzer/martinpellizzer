Crassula subacaulis subsp. subacaulis

Crassula subacaulis subsp.

subacaulis, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit and fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
