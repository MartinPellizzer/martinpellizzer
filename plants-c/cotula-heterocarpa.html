Cotula heterocarpa

Cotula heterocarpa, commonly known as the yellow daisy or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Cotula heterocarpa is often used in ornamental gardening for its cheerful appearance and is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.

Its leaves are hairy and aromatic, contributing to its distinct botanical profile.
