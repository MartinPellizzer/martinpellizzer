Centaurea × genuensis

Centaurea × genuensis, commonly known as the hybrid Guinea cornflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple composite flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are composed of ray florets and tubular disc florets, typical of the daisy family.

Centaurea × genuensis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
