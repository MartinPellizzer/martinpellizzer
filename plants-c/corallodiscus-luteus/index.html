Corallodiscus Luteus

Corallodiscus luteus is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique coral-like appearance. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its intricate floral structure and vivid coloration make it a subject of fascination for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and conservation status of this remarkable species.
