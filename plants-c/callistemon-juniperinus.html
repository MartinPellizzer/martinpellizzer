Callistemon juniperinus

Callistemon juniperinus, commonly known as the rough bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, cylindrical red or pink flower spikes that resemble bottlebrushes. The plant produces small, woody fruits called capsules, which contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
