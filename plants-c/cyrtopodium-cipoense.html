Cyrtopodium cipoense

Cyrtopodium cipoense, commonly known as the Cipoense orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cyrtopodium cipoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
