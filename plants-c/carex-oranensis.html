Carex oranensis

Carex oranensis, commonly known as the Oran sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant features slender, triangular stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Carex oranensis is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats.
