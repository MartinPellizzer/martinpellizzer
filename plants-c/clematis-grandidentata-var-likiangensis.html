Clematis grandidentata var. likiangensis

Clematis grandidentata var. likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a faint purple hue. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping due to its vigorous growth and attractive blooms.
