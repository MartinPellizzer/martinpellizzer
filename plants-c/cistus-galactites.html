Cistus galactites

Cistus galactites, commonly known as the honeycomb rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal uses.
