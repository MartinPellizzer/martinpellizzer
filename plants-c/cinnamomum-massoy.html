Cinnamomum massoy

Cinnamomum massoy, commonly known as mountain cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like cinnamon and laurel. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their aromatic and medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
