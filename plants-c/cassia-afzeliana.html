Cassia afzeliana

Cassia afzeliana, commonly known as yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often pinnately arranged and have a bright yellow or golden hue. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by elongated, flat pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and open woodlands.

Cassia afzeliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
