Cattleya × devoniensis

Cattleya × devoniensis, commonly known as the Devonensis Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant blooms that are highly sought after in horticulture for their beauty and longevity. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native tropical environment.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
