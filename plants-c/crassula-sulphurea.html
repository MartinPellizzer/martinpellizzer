Crassula sulphurea

Crassula sulphurea, commonly known as the yellow crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It belongs to the Crassulaceae family, which includes many other succulent species.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
