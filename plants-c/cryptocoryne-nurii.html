Cryptocoryne nurii

Cryptocoryne nurii, commonly known as the Nurei cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly serrated edge. The plant thrives in soft, acidic water conditions and is often found in slow-moving or still freshwater habitats. Its rhizome system allows it to spread horizontally, forming dense mats that provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.

Cryptocoryne nurii is popular in aquariums due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
