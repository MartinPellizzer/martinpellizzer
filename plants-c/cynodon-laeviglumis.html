Cynodon laeviglumis

Cynodon laeviglumis, commonly known as the velvetgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and fine, silky-textured foliage. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for lawns and erosion control. Its leaves are narrow and have a distinctive smooth, leathery texture.

Cynodon laeviglumis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
