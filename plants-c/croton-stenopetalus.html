Croton stenopetalus

Croton stenopetalus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that often display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Croton stenopetalus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its eye-catching appearance.
