Codiaeum eminens

Codiaeum eminens, commonly known as the Emerald Gem, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, waxy leaves that display a range of colors, including green, red, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its coloration. It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Codiaeum eminens is relatively low-maintenance but benefits from regular watering and well-draining soil.
