Cabucala polysperma

Cabucala polysperma, commonly known as the Malayan star anise.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by star-shaped fruits that resemble anise seeds. Its botanical features include opposite leaves with a slightly hairy surface and a woody stem.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice due to its aromatic compounds.
