Chirita fangii

Chirita fangii, commonly known as the Fang's gesneriad, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, often velvety leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically brightly colored, ranging from white to pink or purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
