Cistus origanifolius

Cistus origanifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Cistus origanifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
