Coleosanthus betonicifolius

Coleosanthus betonicifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved spurge.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, typically in shades of green and white.

It is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
