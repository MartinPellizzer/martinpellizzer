Campanula rotundifolia var. linifolia

Campanula rotundifolia var. linifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are rounded and often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft, textured appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or rocky areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
