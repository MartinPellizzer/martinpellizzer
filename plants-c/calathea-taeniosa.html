Calathea taeniosa

Calathea taeniosa, commonly known as the Streaked Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green veins and pale yellow to white streaks. The leaves are typically broad, lance-shaped, and have a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Calathea taeniosa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
