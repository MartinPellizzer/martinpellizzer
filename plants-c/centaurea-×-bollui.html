Centaurea × bollui

Centaurea × bollui, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid between Centaurea cyanus (blue cornflower) and Centaurea stoebe (scented everlasting). The plant is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple flowers, which bloom in summer. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils.

Centaurea × bollui is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to various regions outside its native range.
