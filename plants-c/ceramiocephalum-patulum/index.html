Ceramiocephalum Patulum

Ceramiocephalum patulum is a rare and unique species of red algae found in specific coastal environments. It is characterized by its distinctive branching structure and vibrant red coloration. This organism thrives in shallow, warm waters where sunlight penetration is optimal. Its ecological role is crucial for supporting marine biodiversity. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and endangered due to environmental changes.
