Cousinia perovskiensis

Cousinia perovskiensis, commonly known as the Perovskian cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate, often with a bluish-gray hue due to dense pubescence. Its showy yellow flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters, blooming in late summer.

Cousinia perovskiensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
