Coleus rehneltianus

Coleus rehneltianus, commonly known as the silver stripe coleus, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green stripes, making it a popular ornamental plant. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and white, typically blooming in the summer months.

Coleus rehneltianus thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring moderate watering to prevent root rot.
