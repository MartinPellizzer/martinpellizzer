Cryptocoryne gracilis

Cryptocoryne gracilis, commonly known as the slender cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams, and prefers moderate to high light conditions. Its rhizome system helps anchor it to substrates while facilitating nutrient uptake.

Cryptocoryne gracilis is popular in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
