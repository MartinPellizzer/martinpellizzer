Coutarea Hexandra Var. Speciosa

Coutarea hexandra var. speciosa is a rare and striking plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. Known for its unique and intricate floral structure, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This variant of Coutarea hexandra stands out due to its larger, more showy blooms compared to its relatives. Its delicate petals and vibrant colors make it a subject of both scientific study and artistic admiration. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and underappreciated in the broader botanical world.
