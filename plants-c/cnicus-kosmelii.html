Cnicus kosmelii

Cnicus kosmelii, commonly known as the Kashmir thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Pakistan.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, showing adaptability to harsh climatic conditions. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

The plant plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
