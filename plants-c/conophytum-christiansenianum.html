Conophytum christiansenianum

Conophytum christiansenianum, commonly known as the "Christiansen's conophytum," is a species of succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape, typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
