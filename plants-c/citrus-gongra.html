Citrus gongra

Citrus gongra, commonly known as the Chinese lemon, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It produces small, yellow, citrus-like fruits that are typically harvested when they are fully mature. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a branched structure that allows for good air circulation. Its flowers are fragrant and white, which attract pollinators like bees.

The fruit is used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent in various cuisines.
