Clerodendrum africanum

Clerodendrum africanum, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange tubular flowers, which are attractive to birds and insects. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
