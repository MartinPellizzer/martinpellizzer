Campomanesia aurea var. hatschbachii

Campomanesia aurea var. hatschbachii, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Brazil.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval to elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, five-petaled yellow flowers with a distinctive red center, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to tropical and subtropical climates.
