Caladeniastrum Latifolium

Caladeniastrum latifolium is a rare and striking orchid native to the wetlands of southeastern Australia. This unique species is known for its vibrant pink and white petals and its distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near freshwater sources. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
