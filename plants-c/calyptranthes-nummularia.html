Calyptranthes nummularia

Calyptranthes nummularia, commonly known as the silverleaf calyptranthes, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of silvery hair, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Calyptranthes nummularia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
