Cnestis monadelpha

Cnestis monadelpha, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
