Codiaeum aurantiacum

Codiaeum aurantiacum, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves.

Native to Southeast Asia, it belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value. The leaves are typically broad, palmately lobed, and display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.
