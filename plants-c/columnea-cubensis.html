Columnea cubensis

Columnea cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Cuba.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Columnea cubensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in greenhouses and gardens for its striking floral display.
