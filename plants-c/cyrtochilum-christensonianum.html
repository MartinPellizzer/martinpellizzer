Cyrtochilum christensonianum

Cyrtochilum christensonianum, commonly known as the Christenson's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. Cyrtochilum christensonianum thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
