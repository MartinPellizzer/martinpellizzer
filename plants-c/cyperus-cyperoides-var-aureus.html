Cyperus cyperoides var. aureus

Cyperus cyperoides var. aureus, commonly known as golden flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with yellow or golden hues, particularly along the midrib. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, three-lobed flowers, typically green or brown in color. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, making it a common sight in wetlands and along water bodies.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage, which can add visual interest to aquatic gardens or natural landscapes.
