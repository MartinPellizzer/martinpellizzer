Croton floccosus

Croton floccosus, commonly known as the woolly croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hairy, or woolly leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Croton floccosus is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
