Capparis palawanensis

Capparis palawanensis, commonly known as Palawan caper, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its woody shrub structure. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a rough texture and a slightly aromatic scent.

Capparis palawanensis is typically found in forest understories and is adapted to humid, tropical conditions.
