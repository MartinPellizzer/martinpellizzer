Cuscuaria

Cuscuaria, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, floating stalks. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide shade over its water habitat. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Cuscuaria plays an important role in both ecological and cultural contexts in its native regions.
