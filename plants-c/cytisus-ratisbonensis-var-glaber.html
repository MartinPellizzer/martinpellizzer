Cytisus ratisbonensis var. glaber

Cytisus ratisbonensis var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, often branching stems covered in smooth, glabrous bark. The plant produces bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
