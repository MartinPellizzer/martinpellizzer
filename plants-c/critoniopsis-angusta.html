Critoniopsis angusta

Critoniopsis angusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf critoniopsis, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in high-altitude cloud forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
