Crepis schultzii

Crepis schultzii, commonly known as the Schultze's hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, typically growing in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces yellow ray flowers surrounded by a series of short, spiny bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is an annual or biennial herb that thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize disturbed areas.
