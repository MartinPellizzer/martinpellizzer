Callicarpa integerrima var. chinensis

Callicarpa integerrima var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant purple berries that appear in late summer to early autumn. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

The berries are not only ornamental but also attract birds and other wildlife.
