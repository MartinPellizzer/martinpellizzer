Calliandra formosa var. cubensis

Calliandra formosa var. cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features compound leaves with numerous small, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
