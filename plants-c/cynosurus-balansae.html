Cynosurus balansae

Cynosurus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Cynosurus balansae is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for invertebrates.
