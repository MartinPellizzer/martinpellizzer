Cassia abbreviata subsp. abbreviata

Cassia abbreviata subsp.

abbreviata, commonly known as the dwarf cassia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant produces elongated, flat pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
