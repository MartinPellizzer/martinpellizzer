Clerodendrum laevifolium var. pubiflorum

Clerodendrum laevifolium var. pubiflorum, commonly known as the hairy-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically smooth but can have fine pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are usually pink to purple in color. Its flowering period typically occurs during the warm seasons, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The species is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
