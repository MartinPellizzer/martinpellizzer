Coreopsis goffardii

Coreopsis goffardii, commonly known as the Mexican coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seed capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
