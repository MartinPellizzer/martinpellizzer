Cryptolepis oblongifolia

Cryptolepis oblongifolia, commonly known as African snake root, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant has simple, opposite, and elliptic-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and dark green. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in spikes, which appear in the axils of the leaves.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
