Cleome daryoushiana

Cleome daryoushiana, commonly known as the Mexican spider flower, is a herbaceous plant native to Central America.

It features showy, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Cleome daryoushiana is known for its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
