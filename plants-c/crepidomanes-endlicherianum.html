Crepidomanes endlicherianum

Crepidomanes endlicherianum, commonly known as the African rock cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that dehisces to release tiny seeds.

It is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
