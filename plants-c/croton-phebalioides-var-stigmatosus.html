Croton phebalioides var. stigmatosus

Croton phebalioides var. stigmatosus, commonly known as the scarlet croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage.

It features large, palmate leaves that can range in color from deep red to orange and green, depending on the season and light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by distinctive, spiky fruit capsules. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.
