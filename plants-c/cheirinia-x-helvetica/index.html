Cheirinia × Helvetica

Cheirinia × helvetica is a rare and intriguing hybrid plant that combines traits from its parent species. It is known for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Due to its limited distribution, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
