Clusia couleti

Clusia couleti, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, broad leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and attractive floral display. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Clusia couleti is also known for its ability to tolerate partial shade, making it a versatile choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
