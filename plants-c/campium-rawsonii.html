Campium rawsonii

Campium rawsonii, commonly known as the rawson's campion, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable addition to native plant collections and ecological restoration projects.
