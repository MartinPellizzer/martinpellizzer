Cymbidium estriatum

Cymbidium estriatum, commonly known as the stripe-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with distinct white stripes. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cymbidium estriatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
