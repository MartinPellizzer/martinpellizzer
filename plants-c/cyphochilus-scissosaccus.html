Cyphochilus scissosaccus

Cyphochilus scissosaccus, commonly known as the slit-mouthed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, terrestrial growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually greenish-white with a subtle scent, attracting specific pollinators.

The genus Cyphochilus is known for its unique floral structure, which includes a specialized labellum that aids in pollination.
