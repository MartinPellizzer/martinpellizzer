Crypsinus undulatosinuatus

Crypsinus undulatosinuatus, commonly known as the wavy-sinuous fern, is a unique plant characterized by its intricate, undulating fronds.

Its leaves are deeply divided, with a sinuous pattern that gives the plant its scientific name. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. It has a delicate, feathery appearance that makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The fronds are often covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its soft, textured look.
