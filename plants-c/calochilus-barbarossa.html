Calochilus barbarossa

Calochilus barbarossa, commonly known as the Red-streaked Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. Its flowers are usually pink or red with distinctive yellow markings, and it produces a single spike with several blooms.
