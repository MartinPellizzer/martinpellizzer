Centaurea jacea proles ruscinonensis

Centaurea jacea proles ruscinonensis, commonly known as the Ruscinonese sicklecentaurea, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and striking pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has lance-shaped leaves with rough surfaces and produces small, clustered flower heads.

This subspecies is distinguished by its unique floral morphology and geographic distribution.
