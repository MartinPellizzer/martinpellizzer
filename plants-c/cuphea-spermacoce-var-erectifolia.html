Cuphea spermacoce var. erectifolia

Cuphea spermacoce var. erectifolia, commonly known as the upright cuphea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
