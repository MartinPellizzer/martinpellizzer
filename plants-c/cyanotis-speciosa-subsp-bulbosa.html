Cyanotis speciosa subsp. bulbosa

bulbosa, commonly known as the bulbous bluebell, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in spring.
