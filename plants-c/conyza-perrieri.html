Conyza perrieri

Conyza perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's weed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and roadsides. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

Conyza perrieri is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
