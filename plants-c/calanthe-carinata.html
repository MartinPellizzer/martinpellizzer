Calanthe carinata

Calanthe carinata, commonly known as the ridge orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Calanthe carinata thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
