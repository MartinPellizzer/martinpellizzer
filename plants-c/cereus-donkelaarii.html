Cereus donkelaarii

Cereus donkelaarii, commonly known as the Ghost Cactus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, white, night-blooming flowers that are often fragrant. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
