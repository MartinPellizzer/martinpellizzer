Calothamnus planifolius f. angustifolius

Calothamnus planifolius f. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical flowers that are typically red or pink in color. The leaves are narrow and linear, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

This plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
