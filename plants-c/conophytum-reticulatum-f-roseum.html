Conophytum reticulatum f. roseum

Conophytum reticulatum f. roseum, commonly known as the rose-flowered conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
