Cammarum × paniculatum

Cammarum × paniculatum, commonly known as the panicled cammarum, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, often branching stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It is native to parts of southern Australia and thrives in well-drained soils.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive floral display.
