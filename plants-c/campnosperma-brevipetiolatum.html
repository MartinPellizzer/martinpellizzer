Campnosperma brevipetiolatum

Campnosperma brevipetiolatum, commonly known as the small-leaf boxwood, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits. It is often cultivated for its dense, compact growth and ornamental appeal.

The species is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun.
