Caprifolium stabianum

Caprifolium stabianum, commonly known as the Stabian honeysuckle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange, blooming in spring. Its fruits are small, red berries that ripen in late summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
