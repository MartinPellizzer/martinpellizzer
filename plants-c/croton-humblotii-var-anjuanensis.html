Croton humblotii var. anjuanensis

Croton humblotii var. anjuanensis, commonly known as the Anjuan croton, is a shrub native to the island of Anjuan in China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaf colors.
