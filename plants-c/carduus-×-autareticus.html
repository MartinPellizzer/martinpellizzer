Carduus × autareticus

Carduus × autareticus, commonly known as the giant thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, spiny leaves and showy purple or pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. It is native to regions of Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered an invasive species in some areas.
