Chesneya macrantha

Chesneya macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered chesneya, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to drought conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable climates.
