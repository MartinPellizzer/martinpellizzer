Campanula mentiens

Campanula mentiens, commonly known as the "speaking bellflower," is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically blue or violet in color, which bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Campanula mentiens is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
