Cephalocroton mollis

Cephalocroton mollis, commonly known as the silky cephalocroton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural gardens.
