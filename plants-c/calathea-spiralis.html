Calathea spiralis

Calathea spiralis, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a unique spiral pattern on the leaves. The plant is known for its ability to close its leaves at night, a behavior that gives it its common name. Its leaves are typically a deep green with silver or white markings, adding to its ornamental value.

Calathea spiralis prefers bright, indirect light and high humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
