Cymbidium micranthum

Cymbidium micranthum, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shady, humid environments. The plant features tall, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
