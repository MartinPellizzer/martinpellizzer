Crassula cymosa

Crassula cymosa, commonly known as the Compact Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Crassula cymosa is often grown as a decorative houseplant or in rock gardens due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
