Coelogyne graminea

Coelogyne graminea, commonly known as the grass orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its grass-like foliage and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-drained soil. It is a pseudobulbous plant with a rhizomatous root system that allows it to store water and nutrients.

Coelogyne graminea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
