Campanula darialica

Campanula darialica, commonly known as the Dariatic bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Campanula darialica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
