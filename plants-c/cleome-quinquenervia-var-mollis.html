Cleome quinquenervia var. mollis

Cleome quinquenervia var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-stemmed cleome, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features upright, branched stems covered in soft, fuzzy hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a pubescent surface. Its flowers are typically yellow or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

This plant is known for its rapid growth and is often cultivated as an ornamental or for its ability to attract pollinators.
