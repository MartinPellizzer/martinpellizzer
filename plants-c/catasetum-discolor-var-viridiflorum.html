Catasetum discolor var. viridiflorum

Catasetum discolor var. viridiflorum, commonly known as the green-flowered catasetum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive greenish-white flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It produces long, slender spikes bearing numerous small, fragrant blossoms.

This variety is valued for its unique coloration and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental appeal.
