Cavendishia engleriana var. ecuadorensis

Cavendishia engleriana var. ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorean Cavendishia, is a species native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is a member of the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems and spiny areoles. The plant typically produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid environments.
