Catasetum lucis f. tigrinum

Catasetum lucis f. tigrinum, commonly known as the Tiger Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, vividly colored flowers that often display a tiger-like pattern, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its botanical features include long, slender pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves.

The flowers are highly fragrant and bloom in clusters, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
