Croton argentealbidus

Croton argentealbidus, commonly known as silver-white croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its unique foliage.

Its leaves are large, elliptical, and covered in a dense layer of white, silky hairs, giving them a distinctive silver appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
