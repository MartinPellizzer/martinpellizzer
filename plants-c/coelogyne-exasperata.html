Coelogyne exasperata

Coelogyne exasperata, commonly known as the fiery orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often red or orange-colored flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant produces long, drooping inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a thick layer of brownish bark-like tissue.

Coelogyne exasperata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
