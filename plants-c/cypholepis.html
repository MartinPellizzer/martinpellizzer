Cypholepis

Cypholepis, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It comprises about 25 species, predominantly found in tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. Cypholepis species are often used in traditional medicine and for ornamental purposes due to their attractive flowers and hardy nature.
