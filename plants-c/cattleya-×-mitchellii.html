Cattleya × mitchellii

Cattleya × mitchellii, commonly known as the Mitchell's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has erect, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
