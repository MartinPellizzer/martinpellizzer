Cleome ofidocarpa

Cleome ofidocarpa, commonly known as the snake root cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and showy, yellow or orange flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds. Cleome ofidocarpa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive addition to gardens and natural habitats.
