Celmisia major var. major

Celmisia major var. major, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking alpine plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in subalpine and alpine regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a valuable component of New Zealand's native flora.
