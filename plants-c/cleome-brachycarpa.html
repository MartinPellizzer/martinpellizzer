Cleome brachycarpa

Cleome brachycarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited cleome, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and branching stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by distinctive, narrow, capsule-like fruits. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Cleome brachycarpa is typically found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.
