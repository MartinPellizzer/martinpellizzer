Croton cerinus f. angustifolius

Croton cerinus f. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features prominently veined, narrow leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into spiny, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
