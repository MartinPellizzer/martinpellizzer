Cotyledon humilis

Cotyledon humilis, commonly known as the dwarf African beauty, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight.

Cotyledon humilis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
