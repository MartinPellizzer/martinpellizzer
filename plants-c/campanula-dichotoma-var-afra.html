Campanula dichotoma var. afra

Campanula dichotoma var. afra, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems that often grow in a prostrate or ascending manner. The plant produces small, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
