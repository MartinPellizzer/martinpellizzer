Calathea retroflexa

Calathea retroflexa, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is adorned with intricate patterns that change subtly in response to light exposure.

Calathea retroflexa is a popular houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
