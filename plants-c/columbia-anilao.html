Columbia anilao

Columbia anilao, commonly known as the Philippine starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit, which turns yellow when ripe. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and glossy, while its flowers are small and white.

The fruit is often used in both culinary and medicinal applications due to its unique flavor and nutritional value.
