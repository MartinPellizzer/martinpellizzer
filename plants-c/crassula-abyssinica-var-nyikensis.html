Crassula abyssinica var. nyikensis

Crassula abyssinica var. nyikensis, commonly known as the Nyikensis Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its compact growth habit and unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.

It is also noted for its ability to produce small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months.
