Cratoxylum chinense

Cratoxylum chinense, commonly known as the Chinese boxwood, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant features dense, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

Cratoxylum chinense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
