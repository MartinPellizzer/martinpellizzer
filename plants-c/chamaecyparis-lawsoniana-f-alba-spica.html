Chamaecyparis lawsoniana f. alba-spica

Chamaecyparis lawsoniana f. alba-spica, commonly known as the White-Spined Lawson cypress, is a unique cultivar of the Lawson cypress.

It is characterized by its distinctive white or pale green foliage, which contrasts with its bluish-green stems. The plant exhibits a narrow, pyramidal shape, making it suitable for small garden spaces. It is a slow-growing evergreen that thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade.

This ornamental tree is prized for its striking appearance and adaptability to various climates.
