Conyza chingoyo

Conyza chingoyo, commonly known as Chingoyo, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of the Middle East.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and has hairy stems and leaves. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers bloom in clusters, and it produces fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Conyza chingoyo is often found in disturbed soils and can be a common weed in agricultural areas.
