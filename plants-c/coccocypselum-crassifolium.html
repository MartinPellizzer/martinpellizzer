Coccocypselum crassifolium

Coccocypselum crassifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved coccocypselum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically leathery in texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists studying tropical flora.
