Cousinia × hybrida

Cousinia × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid cinquefoil, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette growth habit and produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is native to regions of Central Asia and has been cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Cousinia × hybrida is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
