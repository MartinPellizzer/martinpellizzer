Ceradenia glabra

Ceradenia glabra, commonly known as the smooth ceradenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries such as Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests or high-altitude grasslands.

Ceradenia glabra is noted for its unique morphology and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
