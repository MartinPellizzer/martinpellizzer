Cytisus intermedius

Cytisus intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features slender, often spiny stems. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are small, pinnate, and covered in fine hairs.

Cytisus intermedius is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
