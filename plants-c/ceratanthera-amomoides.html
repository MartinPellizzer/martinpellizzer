Ceratanthera amomoides

Ceratanthera amomoides, commonly known as the pink orchid or pink fairy orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its unique morphology includes strap-like leaves and a slender stem that supports its showy inflorescence.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it requires careful attention to its specific growing conditions.
