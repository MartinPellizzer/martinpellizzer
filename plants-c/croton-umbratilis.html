Croton umbratilis

Croton umbratilis, commonly known as the shadow croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red, often with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic foliage.

Croton umbratilis requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
