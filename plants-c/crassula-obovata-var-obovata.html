Crassula obovata var. obovata

Crassula obovata var. obovata, commonly known as the Fat Plant or Jade Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
