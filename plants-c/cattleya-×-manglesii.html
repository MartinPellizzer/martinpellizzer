Cattleya × manglesii

Cattleya × manglesii, commonly known as the Mangles' Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in environments with seasonal dry periods. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it prefers bright, indirect light with high humidity.

This orchid is popular among growers for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
