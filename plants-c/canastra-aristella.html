Canastra aristella

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain seeds surrounded by a fibrous pulp. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry forest and savanna ecosystems.
