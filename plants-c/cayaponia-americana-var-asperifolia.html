Cayaponia americana var. asperifolia

Cayaponia americana var. asperifolia, commonly known as the roughleaf cayaponia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its rugged, serrated leaves that give it its varietal name, asperifolia. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
