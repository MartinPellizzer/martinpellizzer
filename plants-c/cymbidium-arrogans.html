Cymbidium arrogans

Cymbidium arrogans, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure that supports its growth. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with numerous showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in two rows, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Cymbidium arrogans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
