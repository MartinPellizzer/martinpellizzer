Citrosma suaveolens

Citrosma suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-smelling citrosma, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its fragrant leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its essential oils, extracted from the leaves and fruits, are valued for their aromatic and medicinal properties.

Citrosma suaveolens is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its pleasant scent and attractive foliage.
