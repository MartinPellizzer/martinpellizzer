Camellia zengii

Camellia zengii, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Camellia zengii thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also valued for its potential in horticulture and traditional medicine.
