Caragana litwinowii

Caragana litwinowii, commonly known as the Litwinowii shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions.

Its small, woody fruits contain seeds that are dispersed by wind and animals.
