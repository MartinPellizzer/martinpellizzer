Carthamodes

Carthamodes is a lesser-known but fascinating plant that belongs to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers and robust, upright growth habit. Native to various regions across the globe, it thrives in a range of climates and soils. This plant is not only ornamental but also holds ecological and medicinal significance. Its unique features make it a valuable addition to gardens and natural habitats alike.
