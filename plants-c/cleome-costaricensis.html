Cleome costaricensis

Cleome costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican cleome, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, colorful flowers. The plant typically features simple, alternate leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

Cleome costaricensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
