Crinum cumingii

Crinum cumingii, commonly known as the Cuming's lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink, with a strong, often fragrant scent. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Crinum cumingii is known for its hardiness and ability to bloom reliably in suitable climates.
