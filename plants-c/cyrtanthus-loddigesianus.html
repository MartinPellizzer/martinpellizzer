Cyrtanthus loddigesianus

Cyrtanthus loddigesianus, commonly known as the Loddigesian cyclamen, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cyrtanthus loddigesianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
