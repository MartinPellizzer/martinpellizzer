Cyanus Pinardi

Cyanus pinardi is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. Native to mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and alpine environments. Its unique coloration and compact growth habit make it a popular choice among horticulturists and botanists. The species is also valued for its medicinal properties and ecological importance in its native habitat. Despite its beauty, Cyanus pinardi remains relatively understudied, leaving much to discover about its biology and conservation status.
