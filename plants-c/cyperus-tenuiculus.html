Cyperus tenuiculus

Cyperus tenuiculus, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in wetland environments such as marshes and swamps. Its root system helps stabilize soil, making it useful in erosion control.

The plant is often found in shallow water or moist soil, contributing to the ecological balance of its habitat.
