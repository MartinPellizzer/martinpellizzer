Crepinia dalmatica

Crepinia dalmatica, commonly known as Dalmatian crepe jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Crepinia dalmatica is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
