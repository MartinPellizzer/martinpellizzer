Chamaedorea lanceolata

Chamaedorea lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow leaf bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It features slender, arching leaves that are elongated and lance-shaped, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant has a slender trunk that often branches into multiple stems, creating a bushy, palm-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.

Its foliage provides a lush, green aesthetic, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
