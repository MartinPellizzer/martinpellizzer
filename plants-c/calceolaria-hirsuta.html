Calceolaria hirsuta

Calceolaria hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Calceolaria hirsuta is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
