Cyathogyne dewevrei

Cyathogyne dewevrei, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a unique member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its striking, large, white, night-blooming flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
