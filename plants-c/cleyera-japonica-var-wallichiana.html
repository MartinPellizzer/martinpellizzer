Cleyera japonica var. wallichiana

Cleyera japonica var. wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan holly, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions.
