Chenopodium hybridum var. minus

Chenopodium hybridum var. minus, commonly known as lambsquarters, is an annual herbaceous plant native to temperate regions.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are dispersed by wind. Lambsquarters is often found in disturbed soils, such as agricultural fields and along roadsides.

It is a common weed that can thrive in a variety of soil types and environmental conditions.
