Crinum spofforthianum

Crinum spofforthianum, commonly known as the Spofforth's crinum, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer, featuring a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant has strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Crinum spofforthianum is also valued for its fragrance, which adds an aromatic element to the landscape.
