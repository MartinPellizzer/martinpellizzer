Coelogyne brevilabris

Coelogyne brevilabris, commonly known as the short-labied orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, narrow leaf and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red veins. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.

Coelogyne brevilabris is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
