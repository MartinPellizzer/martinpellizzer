Cypripedium callosum

Cypripedium callosum, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy pink or purple pouch-shaped flower, which is adapted to attract specific pollinators. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often form a rosette at the base.

The showy lady's slipper is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its ornamental value and ecological significance.
