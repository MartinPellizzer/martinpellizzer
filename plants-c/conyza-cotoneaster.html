Conyza cotoneaster

Conyza cotoneaster, commonly known as the pale coneflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small cones. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to white, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Conyza cotoneaster is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.
