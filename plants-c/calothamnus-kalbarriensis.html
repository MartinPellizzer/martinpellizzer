Calothamnus kalbarriensis

Calothamnus kalbarriensis, commonly known as the Kalbarri devil's claw, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, claw-like seed pods and bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in shrublands and open woodlands. It has adapted to the arid and semi-arid environments of its native region.
