Calendula suffruticosa subsp. balansae

Calendula suffruticosa subsp.

balansae, commonly known as the Balansae dwarf marigold, is a shrub-like plant native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a desirable plant for Mediterranean climates.
