Cirsium lanatum

Cirsium lanatum, commonly known as the downy thistle, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny, hairy stems and leaves, which are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces purple to pink flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in dry, disturbed soils and open habitats.

It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along the edges of forests.
