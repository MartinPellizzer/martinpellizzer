Ceropegia hookeri var. mollis

Ceropegia hookeri var. mollis, commonly known as the velvet leaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, which help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish-white, often attracting specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
