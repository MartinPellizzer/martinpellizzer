Camellia pubifurfuracea

Camellia pubifurfuracea, commonly known as the silky camellia, is a species native to China and is characterized by its distinctive, dense, and silky-haired foliage.

The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a unique, layered petal structure. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens. The leaves are broad, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its lush appearance.

Camellia pubifurfuracea is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to withstand mild frosts.
