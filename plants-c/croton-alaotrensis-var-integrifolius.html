Croton alaotrensis var. integrifolius

Croton alaotrensis var. integrifolius, commonly known as the Madagascar croton, is a unique plant species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that often display a mix of green, red, and yellow hues. The leaves are typically large, broad, and have a smooth, waxy texture. This plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species.
