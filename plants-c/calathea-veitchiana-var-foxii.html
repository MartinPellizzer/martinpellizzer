Calathea veitchiana var. foxii

Calathea veitchiana var. foxii, commonly known as the Foxii Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green, silver, and pink hues. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture that enhances its ornamental appeal. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
