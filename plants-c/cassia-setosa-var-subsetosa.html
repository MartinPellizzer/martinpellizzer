Cassia setosa var. subsetosa

Cassia setosa var. subsetosa, commonly known as the slender cassia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, branching stems with opposite, pinnately compound leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in terminal clusters, which are typical of the legume family. Its seeds are enclosed in narrow, elongated pods, and the plant is often used in traditional medicine.

Cassia setosa var. subsetosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in tropical and subtropical regions.
