Conyza solidaginoides

Conyza solidaginoides, commonly known as yellow ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late summer to early fall.

Conyza solidaginoides is also known for its ability to spread through wind-dispersed seeds, contributing to its widespread distribution.
