Clerodendrum preussii var. silvanum

Clerodendrum preussii var. silvanum, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
