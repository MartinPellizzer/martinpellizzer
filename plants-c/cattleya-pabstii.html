Cattleya pabstii

Cattleya pabstii, commonly known as the Pabst cattleya, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting lip. The plant features long, arching stems that bear several blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Cattleya pabstii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
