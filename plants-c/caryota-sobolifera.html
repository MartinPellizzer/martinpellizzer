Caryota sobolifera

Caryota sobolifera, commonly known as the giant fishtail palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, yellowish-white inflorescences that are often used in traditional medicine. Its roots and leaves are sometimes harvested for their medicinal properties.

Caryota sobolifera thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
