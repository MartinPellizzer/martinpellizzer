Cyathea solomonensis

Cyathea solomonensis, commonly known as the Solomon Islands tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. It has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades.

Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
