Calicotome spinosa subsp. rigida

Calicotome spinosa subsp.

rigida, commonly known as rigid calicotome, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its rigid, thorny stems and small, leathery leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.
