Cucumis prolatior

Cucumis prolatior, commonly known as the slender cucumber, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a climbing vine that produces elongated, thin fruits with a mild, crisp flavor. The plant has palmate leaves and yellow flowers typical of the Cucurbitaceae family. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both ornamental and culinary gardens.
