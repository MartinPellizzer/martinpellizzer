Coelogyne laetitia-reginae

Coelogyne laetitia-reginae, commonly known as the Queen of the Night orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom in a striking combination of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant produces long, pendulous inflorescences with numerous showy blooms, often appearing at night. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a dense layer of white, waxy hair.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
