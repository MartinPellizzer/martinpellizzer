Circaea lutetiana var. atrosanguinea

Circaea lutetiana var. atrosanguinea, commonly known as the dark blood cinquefoil, is a variant of the European cinquefoil species.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its small, dark red to purple flowers. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This variety is noted for its striking floral coloration and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
