Crossandra multidentata

Crossandra multidentata, commonly known as the multidentate crossandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, featuring opposite leaves with serrated margins. Its striking blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
