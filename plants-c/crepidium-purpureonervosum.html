Crepidium purpureonervosum

Crepidium purpureonervosum, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a short, slender rhizome with a dense cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spike, with showy, violet or purple petals that have distinct veining.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
