Ctenanthe dasycarpa

Ctenanthe dasycarpa, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in clusters at the base of the plant.

Ctenanthe dasycarpa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
