Commelina clandestina

Commelina clandestina, commonly known as the secret commelina, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant features striking blue-violet flowers with white markings, often surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for gardeners seeking exotic plants.
