Commercona

Commercona, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-colored berry that contains several seeds.

Commercona is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
