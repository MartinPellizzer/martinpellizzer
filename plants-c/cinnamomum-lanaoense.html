Cinnamomum lanaoense

Cinnamomum lanaoense, commonly known as the Philippine cinnamon or Lanao cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by egg-shaped, dark purple berries. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their aromatic properties.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
