Coelogyne ridleyi

Coelogyne ridleyi, commonly known as the Ridley's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender rhizomes and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms during the dry season and has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, woolly coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne ridleyi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
