Chorizema sericeum

Chorizema sericeum, commonly known as the silky chorizema, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub characterized by its grayish, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercialized.
