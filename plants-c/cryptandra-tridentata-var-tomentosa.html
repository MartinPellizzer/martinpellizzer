Cryptandra tridentata var. tomentosa

Cryptandra tridentata var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly cryptandra, is a shrub native to the south-western region of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, urn-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
