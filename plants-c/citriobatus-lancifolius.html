Citriobatus lancifolius

Citriobatus lancifolius, commonly known as the sharp-leafed citriobatus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
