Crotalaria dura subsp. dura

Crotalaria dura subsp.

dura, commonly known as the hard crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae. It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, yellowish flowers.

The plant produces distinctive, globose seed pods that contain several hard, angular seeds. It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects in dry regions.
