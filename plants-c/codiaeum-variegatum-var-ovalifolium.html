Codiaeum variegatum var. ovalifolium

Codiaeum variegatum var. ovalifolium, commonly known as the flame maple, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and variegated foliage, which typically features shades of red, orange, yellow, and green. The leaves are ovate in shape, with a glossy surface and prominent veins. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This plant is popular as a ornamental species due to its vibrant appearance and ability to add visual interest to gardens and indoor spaces.
