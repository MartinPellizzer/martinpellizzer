Croton microstachys

Croton microstachys, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright green to deep red and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with suitable conditions.
