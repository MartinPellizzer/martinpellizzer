Ceropegia cactiformis var. felina

Ceropegia cactiformis var. felina, commonly known as the Leopard Ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, spiny stem that resembles a cactus, with fleshy, thick leaves that store water. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
