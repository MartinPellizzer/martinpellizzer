Chenolea echinopsila

Chenolea echinopsila, commonly known as the spiny globe thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a rosette of spiny, blue to purple leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces spherical, spiny flower heads that are typically violet or blue in color. Chenolea echinopsila is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
