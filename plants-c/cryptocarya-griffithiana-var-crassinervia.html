Cryptocarya griffithiana var. crassinervia

Cryptocarya griffithiana var. crassinervia, commonly known as the broad-veined cryptocarya, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its elliptical to ovate leaves with prominent, broad veins. Its flowers are small and typically arranged in clusters, and it produces dark, glossy berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens.
