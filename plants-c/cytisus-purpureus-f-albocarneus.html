Cytisus purpureus f. albocarneus

Cytisus purpureus f. albocarneus, commonly known as the white-flowered broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bright yellow flowers that are slightly modified in this variety to have white petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique coloration and compact growth habit.
