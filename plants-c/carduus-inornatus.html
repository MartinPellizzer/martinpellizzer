Carduus inornatus

Carduus inornatus, commonly known as the slender cocklebur.

It is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow composite flowers. Native to North America, it thrives in disturbed soils and open areas. The plant produces small, rough fruits that are dispersed by wind.

Its structure and growth habits make it an important species in ecological studies and weed management.
