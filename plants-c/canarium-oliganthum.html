Canarium oliganthum

Canarium oliganthum, commonly known as the Malayan nut or red nut tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. Canarium oliganthum thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.
