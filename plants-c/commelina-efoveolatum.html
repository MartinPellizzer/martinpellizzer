Commelina efoveolatum

Commelina efoveolatum, commonly known as the white-veined spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with prominent white veins.
