Centaurea xanthocephala subsp. xanthocephaloides

Centaurea xanthocephala subsp.

xanthocephaloides, commonly known as the yellow-headed centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is a herbaceous perennial with upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers with purple veins, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
