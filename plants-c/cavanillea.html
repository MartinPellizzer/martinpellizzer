Cavanillea

Cavanillea, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

Cavanillea species are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are appreciated for their ability to thrive in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.
