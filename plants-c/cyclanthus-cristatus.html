Cyclanthus cristatus

Cyclanthus cristatus, commonly known as the sacred lotus of the Andes, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cyclanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features large, waxy leaves and produces flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in shaded, humid areas.

Cyclanthus cristatus is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in indigenous communities.
