Centaurea × beltekiana

Centaurea × beltekiana, commonly known as the hybrid garden centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with purple-blue petals and a yellow center, blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Centaurea × beltekiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
