Cortia candollei

Cortia candollei, commonly known as the African orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as yellow and orange. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
