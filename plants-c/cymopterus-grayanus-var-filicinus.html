Cymopterus grayanus var. filicinus

Cymopterus grayanus var. filicinus, commonly known as the filicinus lomatia, is a variant of the lomatia species native to western North America.

It is a flowering plant in the sunflower family (Asteraceae), characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.
