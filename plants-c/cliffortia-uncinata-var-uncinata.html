Cliffortia uncinata var. uncinata

Cliffortia uncinata var. uncinata, commonly known as the Hooked Cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils, often found on coastal cliffs and slopes. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with good drainage.
