Cumulopuntia hystrix

Cumulopuntia hystrix, commonly known as the thorned columnar cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features sharp, spine-like structures that provide protection from herbivores. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and bloom during the warmer months.

Cumulopuntia hystrix is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to survive.
