Cynorkis commersonii

Cynorkis commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cactaceae.

It is native to Argentina and is characterized by its cylindrical stems and spiny appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Cynorkis commersonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
