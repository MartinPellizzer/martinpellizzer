Campanula recta

Campanula recta, commonly known as the upright bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula recta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
