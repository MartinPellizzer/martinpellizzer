Crotalaria lanceolata var. malangensis

Crotalaria lanceolata var. malangensis, commonly known as the Malang cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.

Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.
