Chlorophytum stolzii

Chlorophytum stolzii, commonly known as the Stolzii spider plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features upright, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with white markings along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that give way to white, woolly seed heads. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions.

Its adaptability and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
