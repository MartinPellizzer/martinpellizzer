Cardiochlamys Discifera

Cardiochlamys discifera is a rare and fascinating plant known for its unique floral structure and ecological significance. Found primarily in specific tropical habitats, it thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its name derives from the heart-shaped petals and disk-like structure that distinguish it from other species. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and endangered due to habitat loss and climate change. This article explores its biology, habitat, and conservation challenges.
