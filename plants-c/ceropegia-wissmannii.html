Ceropegia wissmannii

Ceropegia wissmannii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
