Cardiagyris × sekimotoi

Cardiagyris × sekimotoi, commonly known as the hybrid Chinese evergreen, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Cardiagyris caudata and Cardiagyris macrophylla.

It is a member of the Araceae family and is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves with a prominent midrib. The plant produces striking inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
