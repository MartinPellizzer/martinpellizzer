Cineraria polycephala

Cineraria polycephala, commonly known as the rock cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features woolly, gray-green leaves. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Cineraria polycephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid conditions.
