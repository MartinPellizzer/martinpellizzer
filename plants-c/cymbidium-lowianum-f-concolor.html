Cymbidium lowianum f. concolor

Cymbidium lowianum f. concolor, commonly known as the Concolor Cymbidium, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences bearing clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive red lip. It is a hardy orchid that thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
