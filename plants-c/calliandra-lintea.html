Calliandra lintea

Calliandra lintea, commonly known as the silky calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, branching structure and clusters of small, fluffy pink or white flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with numerous small leaflets, and its flowers are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

The silky calliandra is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
