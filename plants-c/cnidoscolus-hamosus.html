Cnidoscolus hamosus

Cnidoscolus hamosus, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by edible, spiny fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates.
