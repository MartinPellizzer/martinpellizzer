Cereus speciosissimus var. albiflorus

Cereus speciosissimus var. albiflorus, commonly known as the White Flowered Christmas Cactus, is a hybrid cactus species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, spiny stems that form a bushy or tree-like structure. The plant produces showy, white, funnel-shaped flowers during the winter months, typically around Christmas. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
