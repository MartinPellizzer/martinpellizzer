Chrysocoma alata

Chrysocoma alata, commonly known as the winged chrysocoma, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically arranged in inflorescences. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
