Calonema vulgatum

Calonema vulgatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, characterized by its broad, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calonema vulgatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
