Campanula istriaca

Campanula istriaca, commonly known as the Istrian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkans, particularly Croatia and Slovenia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, bell-like blooms.
