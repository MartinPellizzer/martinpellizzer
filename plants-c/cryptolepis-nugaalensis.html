Cryptolepis nugaalensis

Cryptolepis nugaalensis, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a shrub native to eastern and southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated fruits that contain seeds covered in a fleshy, red aril.

It is often found in forested areas and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
