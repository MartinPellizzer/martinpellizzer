Carex vesicaria var. tenuistachya

Carex vesicaria var. tenuistachya, commonly known as narrow-stamen bladder sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, strap-shaped leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of pale green to brownish flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes, bogs, and along the edges of ponds.

This variety is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
