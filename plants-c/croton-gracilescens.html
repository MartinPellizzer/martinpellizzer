Croton gracilescens

Croton gracilescens, commonly known as the slender croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
