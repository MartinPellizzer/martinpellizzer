Coelogyne gracilis

Coelogyne gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowering period usually occurs in the spring, and it is prized for its elegant appearance and fragrance.

Coelogyne gracilis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and greenhouses.
