Cyanotis nilagirica

Cyanotis nilagirica, commonly known as the Nilagiri bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the Nilgiri Mountains in southern India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture.

Cyanotis nilagirica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
