Campanula hostii var. witasekiana

Campanula hostii var. witasekiana, commonly known as the Witasekiana bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained, moist soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
