Caladenia incensum

Caladenia incensum, commonly known as the smoky spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is found in regions such as South Australia and Victoria. The plant produces a single, slender stem with a single spike of flowers that are usually pale pink to reddish in color. Its flowers have a distinctive, intricate structure with a narrow, elongated labellum that is often darker in hue.

Caladenia incensum is known for its fragrant, smoky scent, which attracts its primary pollinators, such as the thynnid wasp.
