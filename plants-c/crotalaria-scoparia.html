Crotalaria scoparia

Crotalaria scoparia, commonly known as the narrowleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant features small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes, and its leaves are typically narrow and pinnately divided. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions.

The plant has been used traditionally in some regions for its medicinal properties.
