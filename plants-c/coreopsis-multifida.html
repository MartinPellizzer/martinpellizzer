Coreopsis multifida

Coreopsis multifida, commonly known as the divided coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Coreopsis multifida is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
