Commelina sphaerorrhizoma

Commelina sphaerorrhizoma, commonly known as the spherical-rooted spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes many species with showy flowers and diverse growth habits. The plant features a distinctive root system with swollen, spherical roots that store nutrients. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy coating.

The flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and typically have three petals, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
