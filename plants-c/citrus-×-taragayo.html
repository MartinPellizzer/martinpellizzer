Citrus × taragayo

Citrus × taragayo, commonly known as the hybrid mandarin-orange.

It is a natural hybrid between the mandarin (Citrus reticulata) and the sweet orange (Citrus sinensis). This plant typically produces small, oval-shaped fruits with a thin, smooth peel and a sweet, juicy pulp. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape.

It thrives in warm, subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
