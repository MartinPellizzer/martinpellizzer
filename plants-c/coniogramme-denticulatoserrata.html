Coniogramme denticulatoserrata

Coniogramme denticulatoserrata, commonly known as the finely toothed coniogramme, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araliaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features compound leaves with finely serrated margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in umbels, typical of the Araliaceae family.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to adapt to indoor growing conditions.
