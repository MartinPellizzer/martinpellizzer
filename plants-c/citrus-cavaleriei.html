Citrus cavaleriei

Citrus cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese lemon or Citronella tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree that typically grows to a height of about 5 meters. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are similar in appearance to lemons but have a distinct aroma. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and oval-shaped, providing a lush appearance.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
