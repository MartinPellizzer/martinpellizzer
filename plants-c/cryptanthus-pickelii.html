Cryptanthus pickelii

Cryptanthus pickelii, commonly known as the Pink Frost.

This evergreen perennial plant is native to Brazil and is characterized by its dense, low-growing foliage. It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
