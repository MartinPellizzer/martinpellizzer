Cynoglossum micranthum var. decurrens

Cynoglossum micranthum var. decurrens, commonly known as narrowleaf heartwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features basal rosettes of lanceolate, hairy leaves that are often tinged with purple. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers in loose clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically valuable species in its native habitats.
