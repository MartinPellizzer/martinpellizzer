Crambe orientalis var. juncea

Crambe orientalis var. juncea, commonly known as oriental crambe, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces yellow flowers and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it an interesting addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
