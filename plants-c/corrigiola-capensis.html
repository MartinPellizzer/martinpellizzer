Corrigiola capensis

Corrigiola capensis, commonly known as the Cape sandwort, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland dune environments. The plant features small, fleshy, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.

Corrigiola capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environments.
