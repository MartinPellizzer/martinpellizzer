Centaurea hyrcana

Centaurea hyrcana, commonly known as the Hyrcanian centaury, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia and parts of Europe.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in dry, open habitats. The plant features bright blue to purple flowers, which are arranged in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture.

Centaurea hyrcana is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
