Croton ferrugineus var. holtonii

Croton ferrugineus var. holtonii, commonly known as the Holton's croton, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features palmate leaves with lobes that are often reddish or purplish, adding visual interest. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
