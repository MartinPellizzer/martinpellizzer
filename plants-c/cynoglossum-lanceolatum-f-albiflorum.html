Cynoglossum lanceolatum f. albiflorum

Cynoglossum lanceolatum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered heartwound, is a variant of the heartwound plant characterized by its pale white or almost colorless flowers.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, bell-like flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and can thrive in well-drained soil.

This form is distinguished by its reduced pigmentation in the flowers compared to the typical blue or purple varieties.
