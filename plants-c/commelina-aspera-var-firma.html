Commelina aspera var. firma

Commelina aspera var. firma, commonly known as the firm-stemmed spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers that are clustered in umbels, often accompanied by white bracts. Its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes make it both a useful ornamental plant and a potential weed.
