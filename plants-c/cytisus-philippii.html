Cytisus philippii

Cytisus philippii, commonly known as the Philippi's broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, thorny stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in temperate climates.

Cytisus philippii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
