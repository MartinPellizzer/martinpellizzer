Cymbidium praemorsum

Cymbidium praemorsum, commonly known as the death orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern China and northern Vietnam.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, almost translucent appearance.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, Cymbidium praemorsum is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
