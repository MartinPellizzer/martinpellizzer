Cymbispatha commelinoides var. rotundifolia

Cymbispatha commelinoides var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed spathaceous plant, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with white to pale pink flowers, which are surrounded by modified leaves called spathes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Due to its unique foliage and floral structure, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
