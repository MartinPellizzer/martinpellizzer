Carex × neobigelowii

Carex × neobigelowii, commonly known as the hybrid sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. This plant is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along streambanks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of wetland environments.

Carex × neobigelowii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and ability to stabilize soil.
