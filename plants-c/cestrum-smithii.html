Cestrum smithii

Cestrum smithii, commonly known as the white cestrum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with opposite, ovate leaves. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and it blooms during the summer months.

Cestrum smithii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
