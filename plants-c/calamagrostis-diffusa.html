Calamagrostis diffusa

Calamagrostis diffusa, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It features slender, arching stems that produce fluffy, silver-white plumes, giving it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, preferring moist to wet soil conditions. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest throughout the growing season.

Calamagrostis diffusa is valued in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in waterlogged environments.
