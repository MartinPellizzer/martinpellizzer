Cattleya alaorii f. dietliana

Cattleya alaorii f. dietliana, commonly known as the Dietliana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features pendulous inflorescences with large, brightly colored blooms in shades of pink, red, and orange. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
