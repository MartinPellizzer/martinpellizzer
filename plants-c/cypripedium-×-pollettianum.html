Cypripedium × pollettianum

Cypripedium × pollettianum, commonly known as the Pollett's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy, slipper-shaped pouch, which is typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a single, erect stem that bears a single flower, and its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
