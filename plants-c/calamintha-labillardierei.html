Calamintha labillardierei

Calamintha labillardierei, commonly known as Australian bluebell, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Calamintha labillardierei is often cultivated for its attractive flowers and fragrant foliage.
