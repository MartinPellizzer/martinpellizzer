Cistus × dansereaui f. retroflexus

Cistus × dansereaui f. retroflexus, commonly known as the retroflex form of the hybrid cistus, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the rockrose genus, characterized by its aromatic evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers, which range from pink to purple, bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid environments.
