Chamaecytisus × pseudorochelii

Chamaecytisus × pseudorochelii, commonly known as the evergreen shrub or broom, is a hybrid plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in well-drained, sunny soils. Its leaves are small, leathery, and evergreen, contributing to its hardy nature.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
