Calliandra trinervia var. stenocylix

Calliandra trinervia var. stenocylix, commonly known as the narrow-leaf calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being narrow and linear. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract bees and other pollinators, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
