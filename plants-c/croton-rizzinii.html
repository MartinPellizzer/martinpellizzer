Croton rizzinii

Croton rizzinii, commonly known as the flame croton, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, often resembling flames. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically thick and leathery, with a rough texture and prominent veins.

Croton rizzinii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
