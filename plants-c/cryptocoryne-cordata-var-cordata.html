Cryptocoryne cordata var. cordata

Cryptocoryne cordata var. cordata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight yellowish tint. The plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions and requires stable water parameters. It is often cultivated in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne cordata var. cordata is known for its ability to tolerate a range of water temperatures and its resilience in planted tank setups.
