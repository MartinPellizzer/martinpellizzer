Clitoria pubescens

Clitoria pubescens, commonly known as the velvet bean or sacred bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet-blue flowers, which are often clustered in racemes. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.

Botanically, it is valued for its nitrogen-fixing properties and use in agricultural systems.
