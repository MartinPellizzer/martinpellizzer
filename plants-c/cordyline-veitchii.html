Cordyline veitchii

Cordyline veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's cordyline, is a striking tropical plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant shade of green or red. The plant produces striking, umbel-like clusters of white or pink flowers, which appear at the top of the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Cordyline veitchii is a popular ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
