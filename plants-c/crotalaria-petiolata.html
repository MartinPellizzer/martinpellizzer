Crotalaria petiolata

Crotalaria petiolata, commonly known as the slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and bean-like pods. Crotalaria petiolata is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a valuable plant in certain agricultural contexts.
