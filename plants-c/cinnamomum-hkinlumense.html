Cinnamomum hkinlumense

Cinnamomum hkinlumense, commonly known as the Hainan cinnamon, is a species native to the island of Hainan in China.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant produces aromatic leaves and bark, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth margin and a glossy green surface.

The tree is valued for its essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal and aromatic properties.
