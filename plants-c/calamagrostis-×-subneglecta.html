Calamagrostis × subneglecta

Calamagrostis × subneglecta, commonly known as fountain grass, is a hybrid grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its tufted, fountain-like growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant produces plume-like inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Calamagrostis × subneglecta is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
