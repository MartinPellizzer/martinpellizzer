Campanula glabrata

Campanula glabrata, commonly known as the smooth bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in rocky or dry soils. It blooms from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Campanula glabrata is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
