Cassia kurtzii

Cassia kurtzii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are compound, consisting of several narrow, lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain flat, winged seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
