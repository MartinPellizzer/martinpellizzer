Cucumis muelleri

Cucumis muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's cucumber, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous vine that produces small, green, edible fruits similar to cucumbers. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and hairy stems. It thrives in warm, temperate climates and is often found in coastal areas.

The fruit is typically harvested when immature, before it fully matures into a yellowish color.
