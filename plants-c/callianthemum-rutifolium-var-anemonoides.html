Callianthemum rutifolium var. anemonoides

Callianthemum rutifolium var. anemonoides, commonly known as the alpine anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often pink or white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are palmately divided, and it produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in spring.
