Cirsium × wimmeri

Cirsium × wimmeri, commonly known as the Wimmer's thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and showy purple to pink flower heads that bloom in late summer. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its growth habit is typically upright, with multiple stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
