Cynanchum buntingii

Cynanchum buntingii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Cynanchum buntingii thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and mountainous regions.
