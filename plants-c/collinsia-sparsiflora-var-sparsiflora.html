Collinsia sparsiflora var. sparsiflora

Collinsia sparsiflora var. sparsiflora, commonly known as the slender fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and lavender. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has slender, hairy stems and linear to lance-shaped leaves.

Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
