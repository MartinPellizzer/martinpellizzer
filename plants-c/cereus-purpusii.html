Cereus purpusii

Cereus purpusii, commonly known as the Christmas star cactus, is a popular ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It is a type of cactus belonging to the Cactaceae family, characterized by its segmented stems and spines. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. Its stems are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
