Ceropegia tundavalensis

Ceropegia tundavalensis, commonly known as the Tundavale ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and distinctive tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, and they bloom during the warmer months.
