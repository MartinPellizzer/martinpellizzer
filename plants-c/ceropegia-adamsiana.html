Ceropegia adamsiana

Ceropegia adamsiana, commonly known as the String of Hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which are pollinated by specific moths. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for hanging baskets and indoor gardening.
