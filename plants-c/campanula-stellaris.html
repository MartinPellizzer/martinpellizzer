Campanula stellaris

Campanula stellaris, commonly known as the star bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, star-like flowers that range in color from white to blue or purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy to the touch.

Campanula stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
