Chrysanthemum dabieshanense

Chrysanthemum dabieshanense, commonly known as the Dabieshan chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Dabieshan mountain range.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, white, and orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
