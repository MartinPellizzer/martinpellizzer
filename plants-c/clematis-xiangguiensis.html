Clematis xiangguiensis

Clematis xiangguiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi clematis, is a deciduous vine native to southern China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer.

Clematis xiangguiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
