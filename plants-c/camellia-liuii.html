Camellia liuii

Camellia liuii, commonly known as the Liuii camellia, is a species native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan Province.

It is a evergreen shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring dense, glossy dark green foliage. The plant produces striking, double pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Camellia liuii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.
