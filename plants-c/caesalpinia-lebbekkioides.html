Caesalpinia lebbekkioides

Caesalpinia lebbekkioides, commonly known as the Indian flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of India and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive, showy yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces elongated, flat pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.
