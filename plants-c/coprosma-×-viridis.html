Coprosma × viridis

Coprosma × viridis, commonly known as the New Zealand green coprosmas, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers that appear in spring, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in landscaping throughout its native range.
