Cimicifuga mairei var. foliolosa

Cimicifuga mairei var. foliolosa, commonly known as the Maire's bugbane, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features large, palmately compound leaves with serrated margins, and it produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers in panicles during the summer months.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a valuable addition to woodland gardens.
