Crocus clusii f. mauritii

Crocus clusii f. mauritii, commonly known as the Mauritius crocus, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Crocus.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant is a bulbous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is native to the island of Mauritius and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its rarity and specific growing requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
