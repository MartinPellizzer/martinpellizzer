Cryptocoryne crispatula var. tonkinensis

Cryptocoryne crispatula var. tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle bluish tint. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in shaded, soft water conditions. Its rhizome system allows it to spread and form dense underwater mats.

It is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
