Cuspidia Cernua Subsp. Annua

Cuspidia cernua subsp. annua is a unique and resilient plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its striking, downward-facing flowers that add a dramatic touch to any garden. This subspecies thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well-suited for xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and minimal water requirements make it an ideal choice for drought-tolerant landscaping. Despite its tough exterior, it offers a beautiful and hardy addition to succulent collections.
