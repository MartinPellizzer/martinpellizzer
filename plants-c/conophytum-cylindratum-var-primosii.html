Conophytum cylindratum var. primosii

Conophytum cylindratum var. primosii, commonly known as the cylindrical conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish tinge under stress. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique morphology and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
