Capsicum solanaceum var. glabrescens

Capsicum solanaceum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth pepper.

This variety belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. It is characterized by its smooth, hairless surface, distinguishing it from other pepper varieties. The plant typically produces small, round fruits that can range in color from green to red depending on maturity.

It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its culinary and ornamental value.
