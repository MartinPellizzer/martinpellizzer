Cyclanthus bifolius

Cyclanthus bifolius, commonly known as the two-leaved cyclanthus, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyclanthaceae and is characterized by its unique, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often found in moist, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually broad and elliptical, with a smooth texture.

Cyclanthus bifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
