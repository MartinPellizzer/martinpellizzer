Crotalaria virgulata subsp. grantiana

Crotalaria virgulata subsp.

grantiana, commonly known as the narrowleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes, and its seed pods are slender and cylindrical.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some regions for forage or ornamental purposes.
