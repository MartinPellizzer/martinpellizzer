Cucurbita pepo convar. melopepo

Cucurbita pepo convar. melopepo, commonly known as the common pumpkin, is a widely cultivated species within the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, spherical or oblong fruits that vary in color from orange to white or green. The plant typically grows as an annual vine with palmately lobed leaves and yellow flowers. Its fruit is rich in nutrients and used extensively in culinary and medicinal applications.

The species includes numerous cultivars that differ in size, shape, and color, making it a versatile crop in agriculture.
