Claytonia alba

Claytonia alba, commonly known as white mountain sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are spoon-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Its botanical structure is adapted to withstand cold temperatures and limited nutrient availability.
