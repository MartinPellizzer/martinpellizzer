Calandrinia acuminata

Calandrinia acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-tip purslane, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant produces small, fleshy leaves and bright yellow flowers with five petals. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Calandrinia acuminata is adapted to dry conditions, with a succulent stem that helps store water.
