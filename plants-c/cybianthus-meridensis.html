Cybianthus meridensis

Cybianthus meridensis, commonly known as the desert star.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It has a compact growth habit with silver-green foliage that helps it conserve water.

Cybianthus meridensis is adapted to hot, dry environments and is often found in rocky soils.
