Cynanchum racemosum var. rensonii

Cynanchum racemosum var. rensonii, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes many milkweed species known for their milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, producing a distinctive, fragrant odor.

The plant is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various ailments.
