Canna pedicellata

Canna pedicellata, commonly known as the red banana, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, providing a lush, tropical appearance. The plant produces showy, inflorescences with vibrant orange-red bracts that resemble banana flowers, though they are not edible. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Canna pedicellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and water features.
