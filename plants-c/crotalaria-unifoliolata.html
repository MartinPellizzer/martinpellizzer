Crotalaria unifoliolata

Crotalaria unifoliolata, commonly known as the single leaf cowpea, is a tropical and subtropical flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring a single leaflet per leaf, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its seeds are encased in hard pods and are used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein.

Crotalaria unifoliolata is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
