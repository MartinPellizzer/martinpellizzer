Chrysanthemum macrophyllum

Chrysanthemum macrophyllum, commonly known as the Japanese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant has dark green, lobed leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

Chrysanthemum macrophyllum is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a symbol in cultural practices.
