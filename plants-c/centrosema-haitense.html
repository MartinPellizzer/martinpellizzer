Centrosema haitense

Centrosema haitense, commonly known as Haitian sensitive plant, is a leguminous shrub native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and delicate, fern-like foliage. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by small, round pods.

Centrosema haitense is often found in moist, shaded areas and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
