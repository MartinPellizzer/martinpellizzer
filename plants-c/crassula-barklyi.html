Crassula barklyi

Crassula barklyi, commonly known as the Rainbow Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that often display vibrant red or pink hues at the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Crassula barklyi is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
