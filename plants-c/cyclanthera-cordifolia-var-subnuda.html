Cyclanthera cordifolia var. subnuda

Cyclanthera cordifolia var. subnuda, commonly known as the slender bitter apple, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, gourds, and melons. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow, fragrant flowers. Its fruit is a green, elongated berry that turns red when mature.
