Cytisus × kewensis

Cytisus × kewensis, commonly known as the Kew broom, is a hybrid shrub native to the United Kingdom.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its fast growth and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Cytisus × kewensis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
