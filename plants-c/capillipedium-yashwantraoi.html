Capillipedium yashwantraoi

Capillipedium yashwantraoi, commonly known as the Yashwantrao's capillary moss, is a rare and specialized member of the Capillipediaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like structures that form dense, carpet-like growths on moist, shaded surfaces. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with finely divided, translucent leaves that contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, nutrient-rich environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
