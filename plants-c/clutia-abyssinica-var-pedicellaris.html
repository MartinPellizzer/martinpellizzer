Clutia abyssinica var. pedicellaris

Clutia abyssinica var. pedicellaris, commonly known as African velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in some tropical gardens.
