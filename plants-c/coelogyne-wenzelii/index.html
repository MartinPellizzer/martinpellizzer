Coelogyne Wenzelii

Coelogyne wenzelii is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, colorful flowers and unique growth habits, this plant has captured the interest of botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. The species is named after the botanist Wenzel, who made significant contributions to orchid taxonomy. Its beauty and rarity make it a prized addition to specialized collections worldwide.
