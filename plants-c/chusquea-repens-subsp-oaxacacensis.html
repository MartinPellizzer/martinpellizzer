Chusquea repens subsp. oaxacacensis

Chusquea repens subsp.

oaxacacensis, commonly known as the Oaxaca bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is native to Mexico. It is a perennial plant characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are narrow, elongated, and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is often found in humid, shaded environments such as cloud forests and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
