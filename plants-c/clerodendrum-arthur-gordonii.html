Clerodendrum arthur-gordonii

Clerodendrum arthur-gordonii, commonly known as Arthur Gordon's Clerodendrum, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow centers. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, clustered fruits that are typically green when mature.

Clerodendrum arthur-gordonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
