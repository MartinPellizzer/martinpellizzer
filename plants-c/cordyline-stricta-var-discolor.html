Cordyline stricta var. discolor

Cordyline stricta var. discolor, commonly known as the Variegated Cabbage Tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, upright stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that display a mix of green, white, and pink hues. The plant is evergreen, with a slow growth rate, making it suitable for gardens or as a potted specimen. Its foliage adds a dramatic visual element, especially when planted in groups.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, thriving in coastal or temperate climates.
