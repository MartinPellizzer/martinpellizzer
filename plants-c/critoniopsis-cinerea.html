Critoniopsis cinerea

Critoniopsis cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, producing small yellow centers surrounded by white petals.
