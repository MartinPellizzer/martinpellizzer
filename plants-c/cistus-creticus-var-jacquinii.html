Cistus creticus var. jacquinii

Cistus creticus var. jacquinii, commonly known as the Cretan rock rose, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, silver-gray leaves and produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

The species is also valued for its historical use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
