Combretum jacquinii

Combretum jacquinii, commonly known as the red combretum, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its showy, red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.
