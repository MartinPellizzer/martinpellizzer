Cheiridopsis pulverulenta

Cheiridopsis pulverulenta, commonly known as the sand mat, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a dense mat of small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces yellow flowers during the winter months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
