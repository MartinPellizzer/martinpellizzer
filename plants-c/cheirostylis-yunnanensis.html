Cheirostylis yunnanensis

Cheirostylis yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cheirostylis, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically blooms during the spring and summer months, showcasing its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with rich, well-draining soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
