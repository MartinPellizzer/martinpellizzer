Centaurea cineraria var. pandataria

Centaurea cineraria var. pandataria, commonly known as the Canary Islands thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its pale blue to violet flowers and silver-gray, fuzzy foliage. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a compact, bushy growth habit and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
