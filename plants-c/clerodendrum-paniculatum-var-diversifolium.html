Clerodendrum paniculatum var. diversifolium

Clerodendrum paniculatum var. diversifolium, commonly known as the variable-leaved cleerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that produce small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a variable shape and coloration, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
