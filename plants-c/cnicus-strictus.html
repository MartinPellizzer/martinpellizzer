Cnicus strictus

Cnicus strictus, commonly known as the upright thistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial species that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features a tall, erect stem with spiny leaves that are covered in a white, powdery coating. Its flowers are yellow and appear in dense clusters at the top of the plant.

Cnicus strictus is known for its hardy nature and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its distinctive appearance.
