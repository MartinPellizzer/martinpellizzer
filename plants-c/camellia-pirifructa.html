Camellia pirifructa

Camellia pirifructa, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white base. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a notable ornamental plant in gardens.

Camellia pirifructa is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
