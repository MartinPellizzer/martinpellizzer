Clinostigma exorrhizum

Clinostigma exorrhizum, commonly known as the hairy root plant, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its root system is unique, featuring long, slender roots that can extend deep into the soil.

Clinostigma exorrhizum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
