Crinum esquirolii

Crinum esquirolii, commonly known as the Esquiroli's glory lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that can reach up to 20 cm in diameter, typically in shades of white, pink, and yellow. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Crinum esquirolii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
