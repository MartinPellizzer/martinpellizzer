Carex thinii

Carex thinii, commonly known as the Hubei sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Carex thinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland environments.
