Combretum nelsonii

Combretum nelsonii, commonly known as the Natal flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or greyish color, adding to its visual appeal.
