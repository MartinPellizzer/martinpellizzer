Colysis zosteriformis

Colysis zosteriformis, commonly known as the ribbon moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Hypnaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, carpet-like growth habit and its distinctive zosteriform (ribbon-like) arrangement of leaves. The leaves are narrow, elongated, and often have a slightly recurved tip, contributing to its unique appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rock crevices.

This plant plays an important role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its native habitats.
