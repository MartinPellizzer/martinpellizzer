Chamaecrista nana

Chamaecrista nana, commonly known as dwarf coral bean, is a small, herbaceous annual plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, feathery leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant grows low to the ground, typically reaching a height of only a few inches, and has a compact, bushy appearance. Its seeds are encased in small, pod-like fruits that split open to release the seeds.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental for its vibrant flowers and easy care requirements.
