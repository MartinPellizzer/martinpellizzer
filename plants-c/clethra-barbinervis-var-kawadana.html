Clethra barbinervis var. kawadana

Clethra barbinervis var. kawadana, commonly known as the Kawadana clethra, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a finely toothed margin.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
