Ctenanthe oppenheimiana

Ctenanthe oppenheimiana, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a tropical plant native to Central and South America, characterized by its striking, velvety, dark green leaves with intricate patterns. The leaves are typically broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture that gives them a luxurious appearance. It thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light to prevent leaf burn.

This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care.
