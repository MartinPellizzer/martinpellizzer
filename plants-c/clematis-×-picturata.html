Clematis × picturata

Clematis × picturata, commonly known as the painted clematis, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a wide range of colorful floral patterns.

It is a deciduous vine characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to climb various supports. The flowers are typically showy, with intricate colorations that often resemble brushstrokes or patterns, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This plant is prized in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to different climates.
