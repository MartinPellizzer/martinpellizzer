Cliffortia × mirabilis

Cliffortia × mirabilis, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Cliffortia × mirabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is well-suited to Mediterranean climates.
