Cynoctonum alatum

Cynoctonum alatum, commonly known as the arrow leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a shade tree in tropical regions.
