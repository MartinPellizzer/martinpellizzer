Citrus × limonia otaitensis

Citrus × limonia otaitensis, commonly known as the Kaffir lime.

This plant is a hybrid citrus species native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its aromatic, oval-shaped leaves and small, yellow fruits. The leaves are fragrant and often used in cooking, while the fruit is prized for its distinctive flavor and essential oils. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for both culinary and aromatic purposes.
