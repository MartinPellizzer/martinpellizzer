Crusea psyllioides

Crusea psyllioides, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Despite its ornamental appearance, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
