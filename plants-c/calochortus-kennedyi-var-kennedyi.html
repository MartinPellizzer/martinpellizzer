Calochortus kennedyi var. kennedyi

Calochortus kennedyi var. kennedyi, commonly known as the Kennedy's mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.
