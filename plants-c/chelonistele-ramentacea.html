Chelonistele ramentacea

Chelonistele ramentacea, commonly known as the silky fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the undersides of the fronds.

Chelonistele ramentacea plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
