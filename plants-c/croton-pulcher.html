Croton pulcher

Croton pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a variety of shades, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by elliptical, woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its striking foliage, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor settings.
