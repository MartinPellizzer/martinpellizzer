Cotyledon persica

Cotyledon persica, commonly known as Persian velvetleaf, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Cotyledon persica is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
