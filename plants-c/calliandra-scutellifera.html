Calliandra scutellifera

Calliandra scutellifera, commonly known as the scarlet calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange inflorescences that resemble plumes, which are composed of numerous small, delicate flowers. The plant has compound leaves with pinnate arrangement and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Additionally, it is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
