Cleisostoma lecongkietii

Cleisostoma lecongkietii, commonly known as the Lecungkiet's cleistostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and floral structures. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate, with a glossy surface and a distinct midrib.

Cleisostoma lecongkietii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
