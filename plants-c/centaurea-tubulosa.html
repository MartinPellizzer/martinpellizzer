Centaurea tubulosa

Centaurea tubulosa, commonly known as the slender knapweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it produces numerous tiny seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
