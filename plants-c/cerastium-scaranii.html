Cerastium scaranii

Cerastium scaranii, commonly known as the rock chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and alpine regions of Europe.

Cerastium scaranii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates in its native habitats.
