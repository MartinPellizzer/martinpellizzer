Cautleya gracilis var. robusta

Cautleya gracilis var. robusta, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Himalayas.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant orchid flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in montane forests. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Due to its delicate structure, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival.
