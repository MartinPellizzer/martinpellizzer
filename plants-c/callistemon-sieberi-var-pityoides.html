Callistemon sieberi var. pityoides

Callistemon sieberi var. pityoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and distinctive red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is evergreen, with a compact growth habit that makes it suitable for small gardens or containers. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its unique floral structure attracts nectar-feeding birds and insects, contributing to local biodiversity.
