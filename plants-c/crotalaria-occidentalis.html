Crotalaria occidentalis

Crotalaria occidentalis, commonly known as the western crown flower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the southern United States.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with a crown-like structure. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, arid conditions.
