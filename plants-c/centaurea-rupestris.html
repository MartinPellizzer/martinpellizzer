Centaurea rupestris

Centaurea rupestris, commonly known as the rock centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in rocky or gravelly soils. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Centaurea rupestris is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
