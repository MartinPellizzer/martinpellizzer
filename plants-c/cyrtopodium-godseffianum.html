Cyrtopodium godseffianum

Cyrtopodium godseffianum, commonly known as the Godseff's pterostigma, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces a raceme of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with dark markings. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil. It is relatively easy to cultivate for experienced orchid growers.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a prized addition to botanical collections.
