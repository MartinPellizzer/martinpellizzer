Ceropegia assimilis

Ceropegia assimilis, commonly known as the string of hearts plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
