Carduus × montis-majoris

Carduus × montis-majoris, commonly known as the mountain thistle.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or blue composite flowers. It is native to mountainous regions of Europe and is often found in rocky or dry soils. The plant produces a large, showy flower head that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
