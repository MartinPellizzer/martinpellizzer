Convolvulus nervosus

Convolvulus nervosus, commonly known as the slender bindweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Convolvulus nervosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
