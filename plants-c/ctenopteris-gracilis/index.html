Ctenopteris Gracilis

Ctenopteris gracilis is a small fern known for its delicate, finely divided fronds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions. This plant is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and easy care requirements. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens. Ctenopteris gracilis is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of any space.
