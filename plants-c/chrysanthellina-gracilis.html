Chrysanthellina gracilis

Chrysanthellina gracilis, commonly known as the slender chrysanthemum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in cool, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance and long blooming period.

The plant's botanical features make it a popular choice for both gardeners and botanists alike.
