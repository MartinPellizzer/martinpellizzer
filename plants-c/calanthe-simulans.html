Calanthe simulans

Calanthe simulans, commonly known as the Chinese calanthe, is a species of orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Calanthe simulans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
