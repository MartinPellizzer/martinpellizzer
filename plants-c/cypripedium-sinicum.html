Cypripedium sinicum

Cypripedium sinicum, commonly known as the Chinese lady's slipper, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive slipper-shaped flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually dark green and lance-shaped, while its flowers range in color from white to pink and yellow.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
