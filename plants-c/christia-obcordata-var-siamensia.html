Christia obcordata var. siamensia

Christia obcordata var. siamensia, commonly known as the Siam heartleaf, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
