Campanula afra subsp. afra

Campanula afra subsp.

afra, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa. It features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy.

The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Campanula afra subsp.

afra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and borders.
