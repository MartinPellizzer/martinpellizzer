Coeloglossum densiflorum

Coeloglossum densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence bearing numerous small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as pine flatwoods and wetlands. Its leaves are grass-like, linear, and often clustered at the base.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions, making it a common sight in swampy areas.
