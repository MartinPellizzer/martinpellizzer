Centaurium spicatum var. brachyanthum

Centaurium spicatum var. brachyanthum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered centaury, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to red in color. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface. This variety is characterized by its shorter flower spikes compared to the typical form of C. spicatum.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
