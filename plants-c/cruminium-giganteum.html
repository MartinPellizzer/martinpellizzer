Cruminium giganteum

Cruminium giganteum, commonly known as the giant crinium, is a striking herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, strap-like leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers come in shades of white, pink, and lilac, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Crinium giganteum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
