Carex distans var. oranensis

Carex distans var. oranensis, commonly known as the Oranese sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, rushes, and other grass-like plants. This variant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale brown to greenish flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or shrubland habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
