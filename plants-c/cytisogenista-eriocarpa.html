Cytisogenista eriocarpa

Cytisogenista eriocarpa, commonly known as the woolly cytisus, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of yellow, pea-like flowers. Native to certain regions of Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant has small, stipitate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
