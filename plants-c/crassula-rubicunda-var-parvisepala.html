Crassula rubicunda var. parvisepala

Crassula rubicunda var. parvisepala, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners alike.
