Conophytum herreanthus

Conophytum herreanthus, commonly known as the Herre's conophytum, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge and a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Conophytum herreanthus is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
