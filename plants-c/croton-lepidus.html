Croton lepidus

Croton lepidus, commonly known as the silver croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton lepidus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
