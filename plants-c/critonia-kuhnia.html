Critonia kuhnia

Critonia kuhnia, commonly known as the Kuhnia grass, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. Critonia kuhnia is adapted to dry conditions and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
