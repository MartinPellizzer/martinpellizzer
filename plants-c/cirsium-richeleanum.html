Cirsium richeleanum

Cirsium richeleanum, commonly known as the Richelean thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is found in Mediterranean climates. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in arid conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
