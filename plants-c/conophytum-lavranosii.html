Conophytum lavranosii

Conophytum lavranosii, commonly known as the "Lavranos's conophytum," is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, paired leaves that form a compact rosette. Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
