Chloris gabrielae

Chloris gabrielae, commonly known as Gabriel's grass, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and sandy areas.
