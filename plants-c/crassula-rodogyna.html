Crassula rodogyna

Crassula rodogyna, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Crassula rodogyna is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
