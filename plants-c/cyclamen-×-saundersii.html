Cyclamen × saundersii

Cyclamen × saundersii, commonly known as the hybrid cyclamen, is a popular ornamental plant valued for its attractive foliage and flowers.

It is a hybrid between Cyclamen persicum and Cyclamen coum, resulting in a plant that combines the best traits of both parents. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture, and the flowers come in a range of colors including pink, white, and red. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.

Cyclamen × saundersii is known for its long blooming period and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and light conditions.
