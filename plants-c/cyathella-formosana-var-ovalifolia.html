Cyathella formosana var. ovalifolia

Cyathella formosana var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the Taiwan cyathella, is a species of flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Cyathaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, cup-shaped inflorescence known as a cyathium. The leaves are typically ovate or elliptic in shape, giving the plant its varietal name ovalifolia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral structure.
