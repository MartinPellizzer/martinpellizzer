Campanula Scheuchzeri Subsp. Ficarioides

Campanula scheuchzeri subsp. ficarioides is a rare and ancient plant species with a unique botanical profile. Native to the alpine regions of Central Europe, it thrives in rocky and moist environments. This subspecies is distinguished by its bell-shaped flowers and compact growth habit. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a conservation concern. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
