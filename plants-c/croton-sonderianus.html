Croton sonderianus

Croton sonderianus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Croton sonderianus is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
