Chenopodium urbicum var. deltoideum

Chenopodium urbicum var. deltoideum, commonly known as broadleaf goosefoot, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, deltoid-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and it is typically found in disturbed soils such as roadsides and urban areas. It is an annual herb that can grow up to 30 cm tall, with a short, stout stem.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environments, contributing to its widespread distribution.
