Clitoria guianensis

Clitoria guianensis, commonly known as the winged bean or evergreen bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and showy, butterfly-shaped purple-blue flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. Clitoria guianensis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its nutritional benefits.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health properties.
