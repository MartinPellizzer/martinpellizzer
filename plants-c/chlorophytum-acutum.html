Chlorophytum acutum

Chlorophytum acutum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to southern Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with a white stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on long, slender stems, which can be followed by clusters of red berries. It is well adapted to a variety of growing conditions and is often used as a decorative indoor plant.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
