Cylindropuntia

Cylindropuntia, commonly known as the cholla cactus, is a genus of flowering plants in the cactus family, Cactaceae.

They are characterized by their cylindrical or clavate stems, which are often covered in spines and sometimes flowers. Cylindropuntia species are adapted to arid environments, storing water in their thick, fleshy stems.
