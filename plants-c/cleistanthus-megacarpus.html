Cleistanthus megacarpus

Cleistanthus megacarpus, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The tree produces woody, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by animals. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough, leathery texture.

Cleistanthus megacarpus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 30 meters tall in its natural habitat.
