Crotalaria sidamaensis

Crotalaria sidamaensis, commonly known as the Sidama crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It has small, hairy pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Crotalaria sidamaensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological role in its native environment.
