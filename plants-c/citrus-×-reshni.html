Citrus × reshni

Citrus × reshni, commonly known as the kumquat.

It is a hybrid citrus plant that originated from a cross between the mandarin and the pomelo. The plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped fruits that turn from green to orange as they mature. The fruit's peel is edible, making it unique among citrus varieties.

It thrives in warm, subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its fruit.
