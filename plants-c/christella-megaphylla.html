Christella megaphylla

Christella megaphylla, commonly known as the large-leaf christella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage. Christella megaphylla thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
