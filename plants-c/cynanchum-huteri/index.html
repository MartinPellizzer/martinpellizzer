Cynanchum Huteri

Cynanchum huteri is a rare and intriguing member of the Asclepiadaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This plant is known for its striking blue-violet flowers and its unique chemical properties. Despite its beauty, it is toxic and requires careful handling. Its study offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and medicinal potential.
