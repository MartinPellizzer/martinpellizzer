Cybianthus multicostatus

Cybianthus multicostatus, commonly known as the multibracteate Cybianthus, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
