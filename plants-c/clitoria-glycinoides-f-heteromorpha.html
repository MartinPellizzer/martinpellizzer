Clitoria glycinoides f. heteromorpha

Clitoria glycinoides f. heteromorpha, commonly known as the variable butterfly pea, is a variant of the butterfly pea plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking violet to blue flowers, which are typically clustered in racemes. The plant is a climbing legume with trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
