Caladenia × resupina

Caladenia × resupina, commonly known as the Resupinate Spider-orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats, such as woodlands and heaths. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by insects.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique and elegant appearance.
