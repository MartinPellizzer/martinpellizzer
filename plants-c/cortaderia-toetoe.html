Cortaderia toetoe

Cortaderia toetoe, commonly known as New Zealand feather grass, is a perennial grass native to New Zealand.

It features slender, arching leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, feathery appearance. The plant produces tall, slender flower spikes that bloom in summer, releasing light, airy plumes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Cortaderia toetoe is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for native wildlife.
