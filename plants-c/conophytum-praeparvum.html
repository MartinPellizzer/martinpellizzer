Conophytum praeparvum

Conophytum praeparvum, commonly known as the "Little Shield Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
