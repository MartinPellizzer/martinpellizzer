Crassula closiana

Crassula closiana, commonly known as the Christmas Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Crassula closiana is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
