Clematis × morelii

Clematis × morelii, commonly known as the Morello clematis, is a hybrid vine native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and its ability to climb using its twining stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is typically heart-shaped and provides a lush backdrop to the blooms.

Clematis × morelii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
