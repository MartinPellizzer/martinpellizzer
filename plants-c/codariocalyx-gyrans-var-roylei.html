Codariocalyx gyrans var. roylei

Codariocalyx gyrans var. roylei, commonly known as the spiral calyx plant, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in warm, humid climates.
