Celastrus hindsii var. henryi

Celastrus hindsii var. henryi, commonly known as the Chinese staff-vine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright orange berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical and subtropical regions.
