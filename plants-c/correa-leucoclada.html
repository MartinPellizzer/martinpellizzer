Correa leucoclada

Correa leucoclada, commonly known as the white correa, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant features lance-shaped, glossy leaves and produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sclerophyllous forests.
