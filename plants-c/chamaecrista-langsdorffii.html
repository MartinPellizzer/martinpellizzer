Chamaecrista langsdorffii

Chamaecrista langsdorffii, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed habitats.

Its ornamental value and ability to fix nitrogen make it useful in ecological restoration projects.
