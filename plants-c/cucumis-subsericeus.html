Cucumis subsericeus

Cucumis subsericeus, commonly known as the African horned melon, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its distinctive green, spiky fruit that resembles a horn. The fruit is typically harvested when it turns orange or red, and it is known for its tangy, slightly sweet flavor.

It is often cultivated for both ornamental and edible purposes.
