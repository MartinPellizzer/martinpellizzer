Carapa moluccensis var. gangetica

Carapa moluccensis var. gangetica, commonly known as Indian black pepperwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its dark, aromatic wood. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are drupes that contain a single seed, and the tree is valued for its timber and medicinal properties.
