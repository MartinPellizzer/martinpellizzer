Cryptocoryne bogneri

Cryptocoryne bogneri, commonly known as the Bornean cryptostegia, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, white flowers that are often submerged. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate light levels.

Cryptocoryne bogneri is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
