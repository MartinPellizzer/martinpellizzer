Chlorophytum affine var. curviscapum

Chlorophytum affine var. curviscapum, commonly known as the curly spider plant, is a variant of the spider plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that curl at the tips, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stalks, which are followed by bright green, egg-shaped berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This variety is known for its adaptability and ease of care, making it suitable for both novice and experienced plant growers.
