Congea chinensis

Congea chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese congea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous small seeds. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
