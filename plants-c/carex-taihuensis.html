Carex taihuensis

Carex taihuensis, commonly known as the Taihu sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern China.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

Carex taihuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
