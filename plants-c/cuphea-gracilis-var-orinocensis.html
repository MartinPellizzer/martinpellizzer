Cuphea gracilis var. orinocensis

Cuphea gracilis var. orinocensis, commonly known as the slender cuphea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often grown as an ornamental in gardens due to its attractive foliage and long blooming period.

Cuphea gracilis var. orinocensis is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
