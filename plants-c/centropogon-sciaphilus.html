Centropogon sciaphilus

Centropogon sciaphilus, commonly known as the shade-loving grass.

This plant is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions. It thrives in low-light conditions, making it suitable for shaded garden environments. Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, with a dense growth habit.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in shaded areas.
