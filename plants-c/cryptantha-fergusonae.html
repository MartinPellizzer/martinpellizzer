Cryptantha fergusonae

Cryptantha fergusonae, commonly known as Ferguson's bluecurls, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, blue or purple pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate and trifoliate, with hairy surfaces that help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Cryptantha fergusonae is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in desert and foothill regions.
