Columnea trollii

Columnea trollii, commonly known as the Trinidadian flame flower, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Caribbean.

It features showy, tubular red or orange flowers that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Columnea trollii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
