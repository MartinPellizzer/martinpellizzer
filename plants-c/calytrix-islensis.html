Calytrix islensis

Calytrix islensis, commonly known as the Christmas Island calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Christmas Island in the Indian Ocean.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine pubescence.

Calytrix islensis is adapted to the tropical climate of its habitat, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
