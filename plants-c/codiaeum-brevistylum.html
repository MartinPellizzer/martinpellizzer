Codiaeum brevistylum

Codiaeum brevistylum, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and velvety leaves that range from deep red to orange and green. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its aesthetic appeal.
