Clematis yingtzulinia

Clematis yingtzulinia, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval-shaped leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, showy structure. The plant produces small, red berries that contain the seeds, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun to partial shade.

Clematis yingtzulinia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant blooms.
