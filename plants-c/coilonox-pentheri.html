Coilonox pentheri

Coilonox pentheri, commonly known as the desert star cactus, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the rainy season, adding a striking visual element to its habitat. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and deterring herbivores.

Coilonox pentheri is well adapted to harsh environments, making it a resilient and unique botanical specimen.
