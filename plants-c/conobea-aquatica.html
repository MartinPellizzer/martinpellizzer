Conobea aquatica

Conobea aquatica, commonly known as water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade and oxygen to the water. Its flowers are typically white or pink, with a distinctive fragrance that attracts pollinators. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that anchors it in shallow water.

Conobea aquatica plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by supporting biodiversity and improving water quality.
