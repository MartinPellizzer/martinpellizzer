Cymbidium insigne var. sanderi

Cymbidium insigne var. sanderi, commonly known as the Sander's orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to China and Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, providing a striking contrast to its elegant blossoms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
