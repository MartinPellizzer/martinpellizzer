Carduus litigiosus var. spinosior

Carduus litigiosus var. spinosior, commonly known as the prickly thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and wastelands. Its leaves are covered in stiff, bristly hairs, and the flowers are arranged in a spherical shape.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
