Codiaeum volutum

Codiaeum volutum, commonly known as the croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that come in shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its striking foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Codiaeum volutum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.
