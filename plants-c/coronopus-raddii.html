Coronopus raddii

Coronopus raddii, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy and grow in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Coronopus raddii is a biennial plant that produces small, elongated seed pods containing numerous seeds.
