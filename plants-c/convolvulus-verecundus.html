Convolvulus verecundus

Convolvulus verecundus, commonly known as the shy convolvulus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a trailing or climbing herb that produces small, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its delicate appearance and shy growth habit, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
