Celmisia verbascifolia subsp. membranacea

Celmisia verbascifolia subsp.

membranacea, commonly known as the membranaceous celmisia, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage.

The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins and rocky areas.
