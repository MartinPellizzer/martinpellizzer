Cassia seleriana

Cassia seleriana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are rich in nectar and attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and birds.

Cassia seleriana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
