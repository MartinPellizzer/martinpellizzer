Citrus × bigaradia var. violacea

Citrus × bigaradia var. violacea, commonly known as the purple-leafed citron.

This variety is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its striking violet to purple foliage, which contrasts with its green leaves. It produces small, fragrant flowers and yellow-orange fruits similar to other citrus species. The plant is typically grown as an ornamental due to its unique leaf coloration.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.
