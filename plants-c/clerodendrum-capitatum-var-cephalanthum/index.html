Clerodendrum Capitatum Var. Cephalanthum

Clerodendrum capitatum var. cephalanthum is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, clustered flowers. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is native to regions in Southeast Asia. The plant features long, arching stems that make it a popular choice for hanging baskets or vertical gardens. Its unique appearance and easy care requirements have made it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. This variety is particularly noted for its ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
