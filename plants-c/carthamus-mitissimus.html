Carthamus mitissimus

Carthamus mitissimus, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray florets surrounded by a dense cluster of tubular disk florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Carthamus mitissimus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
