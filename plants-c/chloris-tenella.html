Chloris tenella

Chloris tenella, commonly known as silky grass or silky bluegrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Australia.

It features fine, silver-blue foliage that is densely packed, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue to greenish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.

Chloris tenella is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun to partial shade.
