Convallaria majalis subsp. manschurica

Convallaria majalis subsp.

manschurica, commonly known as the Korean Lily of the Valley, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia. It features clusters of bell-shaped, white flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
