Calliandra cualensis

Calliandra cualensis, commonly known as the yellow calliandra, is a flowering plant native to Central America and northern South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, feathery, yellow inflorescences. The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant and showy blooms. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.
