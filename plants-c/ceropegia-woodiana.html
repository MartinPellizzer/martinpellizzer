Ceropegia woodiana

Ceropegia woodiana, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is popular as a houseplant and in hanging baskets.
