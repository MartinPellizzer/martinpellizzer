Critoniopsis elbertiana

Critoniopsis elbertiana, commonly known as the Elbert's ghost orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, clustered leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
