Chrysanthemum cossonianum

Chrysanthemum cossonianum, commonly known as the Cossonian chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with serrated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow to orange in color, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
