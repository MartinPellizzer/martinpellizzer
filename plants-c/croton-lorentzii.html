Croton lorentzii

Croton lorentzii, commonly known as the Lorentz's croton, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that can vary in color from green to reddish-brown, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens in tropical climates.
