Clerodendrum robustum var. mafiense

Clerodendrum robustum var. mafiense, commonly known as the Mafiense Clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy bracts that range in color from red to purple, often mistaken for flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture and emits a strong, spicy fragrance. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variant is notable for its robust growth and adaptability to various soil types.
