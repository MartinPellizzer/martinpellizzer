Cassine holstii

Cassine holstii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dense clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in winter, giving it a striking appearance. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated edge.

Cassine holstii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal and sandy soils.
