Cotyledon breviflora f. maculigera

Cotyledon breviflora f. maculigera, commonly known as the spotted cotyledon, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive red or purple markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by pale green, egg-shaped fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
