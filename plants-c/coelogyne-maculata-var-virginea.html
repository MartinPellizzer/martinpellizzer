Coelogyne maculata var. virginea

Coelogyne maculata var. virginea, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a captivating epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with dark green spots, giving it its common name. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that have a distinctive spotted pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining substrates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of tropical flora.
