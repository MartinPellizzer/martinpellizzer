Cyperus correctus

Cyperus correctus, commonly known as the spiny flatsedge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, triangular leaves. The plant produces spikelets that are often covered in short, bristly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Cyperus correctus is typically found in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
