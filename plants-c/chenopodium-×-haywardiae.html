Chenopodium × haywardiae

Chenopodium × haywardiae, commonly known as the hybrid goosefoot, is a plant species belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, succulent leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, typically in the axils of the leaves. It is native to regions with dry climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Due to its hardy nature and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
