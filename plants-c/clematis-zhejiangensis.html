Clematis zhejiangensis

Clematis zhejiangensis, commonly known as the Zhejiang clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, spherical berries that are dark blue when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Clematis zhejiangensis is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscape design.
