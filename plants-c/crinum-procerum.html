Crinum procerum

Crinum procerum, commonly known as the slender rain lily, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a strong, fragrant scent. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water sources.

Crinum procerum is known for its ability to produce numerous bulb-like structures, which aid in its propagation.
