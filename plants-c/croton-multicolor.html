Croton multicolor

Croton multicolor, commonly known as the multicolored croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage that displays shades of green, yellow, red, and orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its striking, palmate leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its colorful appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in gardens.
