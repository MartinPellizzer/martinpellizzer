Cyathea aethiopica

Cyathea aethiopica, commonly known as the African tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous, dark brown rind. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. Cyathea aethiopica typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories.

Its spore-producing cones are clustered at the top of the trunk, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
