Crotalaria uncinella subsp. uncinella

Crotalaria uncinella subsp.

uncinella, commonly known as the hooked crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is a herbaceous annual or perennial species characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its seeds are small and often dispersed by wind, contributing to its wide distribution.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, making it a resilient weed in agricultural settings.
