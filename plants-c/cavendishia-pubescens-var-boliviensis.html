Cavendishia pubescens var. boliviensis

Cavendishia pubescens var. boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian velvet plant, is a striking member of the Cactaceae family native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety pubescence that covers its stems, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
