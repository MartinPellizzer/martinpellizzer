Catasetum kleberianum

Catasetum kleberianum, commonly known as the Kleber's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are notable for their unique structure, featuring specialized pollination mechanisms.

Catasetum kleberianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
