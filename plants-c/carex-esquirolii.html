Carex esquirolii

Carex esquirolii, commonly known as the Esquiroli's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. This species typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually bright green.

The plant produces small, clustered spikes of pale to brownish flowers, which are typical of the sedge family.
