Cytisus nigricans

Cytisus nigricans, commonly known as black thorn, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, thorny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Cytisus nigricans is known for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
