Crassula jasminiflora

Crassula jasminiflora, commonly known as the jasmine crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that resemble jasmine, blooming in shades of white and pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Crassula jasminiflora is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
