Cotoneaster armenus

Cotoneaster armenus, commonly known as Armenian cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster armenus is also valued for its role in ecological habitats and as a source of food for wildlife.
