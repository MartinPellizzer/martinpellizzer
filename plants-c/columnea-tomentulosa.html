Columnea tomentulosa

Columnea tomentulosa, commonly known as the woolly mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in hanging baskets or as a ground cover.
