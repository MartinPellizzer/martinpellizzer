Chrysanthemum barretii

Chrysanthemum barretii, commonly known as the Barret's chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Chrysanthemum barretii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
