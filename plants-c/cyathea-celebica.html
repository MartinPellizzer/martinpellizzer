Cyathea celebica

Cyathea celebica, commonly known as the Celebes tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its tall stem, while its fronds are used in traditional practices by local communities.

Cyathea celebica plays an important ecological role in maintaining forest biodiversity and soil stability.
