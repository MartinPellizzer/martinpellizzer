Cyperus eleusinoides

Cyperus eleusinoides, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia, and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered purple or brown flowers. It is an annual or perennial herb that can grow up to 1 meter in height.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly, making it a common weed in agricultural and aquatic environments.
