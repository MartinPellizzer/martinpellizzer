Cattleya granulosa var. buyssoniana

Cattleya granulosa var. buyssoniana, commonly known as the Buyssoniana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink, with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and unique floral structure.
