Cimicifuga japonica var. obtusiloba

Cimicifuga japonica var. obtusiloba, commonly known as Japanese bugbane, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of white, lacecap flowers at the top. The plant has large, pinnately compound leaves that are dark green and serrated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its historical use in traditional medicine.
