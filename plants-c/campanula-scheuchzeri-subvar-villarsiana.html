Campanula scheuchzeri subvar. villarsiana

Campanula scheuchzeri subvar. villarsiana, commonly known as the Alpine bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species in alpine flora.
