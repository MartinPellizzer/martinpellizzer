Cirsium × ganderi

Cirsium × ganderi, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to the northern regions of the United States and Canada.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and riverbanks. Its morphology includes a robust stem with rough, bristly texture, and it produces achenes with pappus for wind dispersal.

Cirsium × ganderi is often found in disturbed or wetland environments, showcasing its adaptability to various soil conditions.
