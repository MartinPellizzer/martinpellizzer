Canarium cinereum

Canarium cinereum, commonly known as the pale canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by round, woody fruits containing a single seed. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its timber and resin.
