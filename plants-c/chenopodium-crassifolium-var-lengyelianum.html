Chenopodium crassifolium var. lengyelianum

Chenopodium crassifolium var. lengyelianum, commonly known as the Lengyel's goosefoot, is a species native to Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and ovate. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in compact clusters, which are borne on slender pedicels. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The species is noted for its adaptability to harsh conditions and is sometimes cultivated for its edible leaves.
