Clara gracilis

Clara gracilis, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to parts of Asia and the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and its intricate, spiral arrangement of flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, with a unique, fragrant scent.

Clara gracilis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
