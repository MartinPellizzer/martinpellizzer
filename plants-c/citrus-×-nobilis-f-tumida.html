Citrus × nobilis f. tumida

Citrus × nobilis f. tumida, commonly known as the pummelo or Chinese grapefruit.

This variety is a cultivar of the citrus genus, characterized by its large, thick-skinned fruits. The fruit typically has a mild, sweet flavor with a lower acidity compared to other citrus fruits. It is native to Southeast Asia and is widely cultivated for both fresh consumption and juice production.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
