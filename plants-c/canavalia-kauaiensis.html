Canavalia kauaiensis

Canavalia kauaiensis, commonly known as Kauai morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a vine, with trifoliate leaves and a climbing habit. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to the tropical climate of Hawaii.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
