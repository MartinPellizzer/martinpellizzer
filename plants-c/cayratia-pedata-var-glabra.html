Cayratia pedata var. glabra

Cayratia pedata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth cayratia, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, smooth, and often hairy leaves that are alternate along the stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that mature to a bright red color.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and vigorous growth habit.
