Cyanothamnus anemonifolius subsp. variabilis

Cyanothamnus anemonifolius subsp.

variabilis, commonly known as the variable bluebottle, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is a herbaceous perennial with distinctive blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
