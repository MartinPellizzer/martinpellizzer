Calathea smaragdina

Calathea smaragdina, commonly known as the Emerald Beauty, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is prized for its striking, vividly colored foliage, which features intricate patterns in shades of green, silver, and gold. The leaves are oval-shaped and often have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This plant is also known for its ability to purify the air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
