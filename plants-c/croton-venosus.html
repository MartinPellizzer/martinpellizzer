Croton venosus

Croton venosus, commonly known as the veiny croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated, foliage with veins that are prominently visible. The leaves are typically palmately lobed, with a waxy texture and a range of colors from green to red and yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is popular as a ornamental due to its vibrant leaves and ability to thrive in warm, humid conditions.
