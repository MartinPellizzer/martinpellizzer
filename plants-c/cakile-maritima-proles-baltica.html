Cakile maritima proles baltica

Cakile maritima proles baltica, commonly known as the Baltic sea rocket, is a halophyte plant adapted to coastal environments.

It thrives in saline soils and is often found along the shores of the Baltic Sea. The plant has small, lobed leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, and it produces capsules that contain numerous seeds.

It plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and preventing erosion.
