Cyclopia grandifolia

Cyclopia grandifolia, commonly known as the greater cyclopia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spherical fruits. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Cyclopia grandifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional African medicine.
