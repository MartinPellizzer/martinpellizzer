Catasetum rooseveltianum

Catasetum rooseveltianum, commonly known as Roosevelt's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that can range from yellow to red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers have a unique structure with a specialized labellum that aids in pollination by specific wasps.

The genus Catasetum is known for its intricate floral morphology and specialized pollination mechanisms.
