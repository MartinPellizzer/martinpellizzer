Callistemon × hortensis

Callistemon × hortensis, commonly known as the bottlebrush plant, is a hybrid shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, brush-like inflorescences that range in color from red to pink, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is known for its aromatic leaves and showy flowers that attract birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its hardy nature and ability to bloom throughout the year add to its appeal in both landscapes and containers.
