Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. fuscopilosum

Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. fuscopilosum, commonly known as the brown-haired chrysosplenium, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine, brownish hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky stream banks.

Its foliage and floral structure make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
