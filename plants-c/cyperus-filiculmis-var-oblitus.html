Cyperus filiculmis var. oblitus

Cyperus filiculmis var. oblitus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of pale brown to reddish flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies.

This variant is distinguished by its reduced leaf structures and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
