Crotalaria sagittalis

Crotalaria sagittalis, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. Crotalaria sagittalis is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in some areas.
