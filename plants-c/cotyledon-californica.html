Cotyledon californica

Cotyledon californica, commonly known as the California copperleaf, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
