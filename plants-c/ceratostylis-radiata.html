Ceratostylis radiata

Ceratostylis radiata, commonly known as the Radiate Sword Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a spike, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
