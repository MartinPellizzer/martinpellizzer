Cerastites dubius

Cerastites dubius, commonly known as the yellow sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-yellow fruits. The plant has small, elliptical leaves and thrives in arid or semi-arid conditions. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to drought.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
