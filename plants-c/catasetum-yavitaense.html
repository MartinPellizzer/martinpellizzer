Catasetum yavitaense

Catasetum yavitaense, commonly known as the Yavita orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure, including specialized pollination mechanisms. The plant typically produces vibrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Catasetum yavitaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
