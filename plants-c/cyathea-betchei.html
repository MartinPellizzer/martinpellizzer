Cyathea betchei

Cyathea betchei, commonly known as the Betche's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Cyathea betchei is typically found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
