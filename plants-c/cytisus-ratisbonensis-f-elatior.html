Cytisus ratisbonensis f. elatior

Cytisus ratisbonensis f. elatior, commonly known as the greater yellow broom, is a flowering shrub characterized by its bright yellow pea-like flowers.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to southern Europe. The plant features slender, multi-branched stems covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are trifoliate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats.
