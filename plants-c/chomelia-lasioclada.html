Chomelia lasioclada

Chomelia lasioclada, commonly known as the velvetleaf chomelia, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Chomelia lasioclada is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
