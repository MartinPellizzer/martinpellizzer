Cadaba nakakope

Cadaba nakakope, commonly known as the Hawaiian milkweed, is a shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its latex sap is milky and can be used for various traditional purposes.

It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to salt spray and sandy soils.
