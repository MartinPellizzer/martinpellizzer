Chastenaea

Chastenaea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. Its rapid growth and invasive tendencies make it a common weed in agricultural and natural areas.
