Cnicus igniarius

Cnicus igniarius, commonly known as the flame thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts that resemble flames, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are spiny and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head.

Cnicus igniarius is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
