Cineraria Campestris Var. Umbellata

Cineraria campestris var. umbellata is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique umbellate inflorescence gives it a fluffy, cloud-like appearance, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures. Despite its beauty, it requires careful care to flourish in non-native settings.
