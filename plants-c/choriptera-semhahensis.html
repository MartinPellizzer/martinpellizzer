Choriptera semhahensis

Choriptera semhahensis, commonly known as the Semhah Valley moss, is a rare and specialized plant found in the high-altitude regions of the Semhah Valley.

It belongs to the family Choripteraceae and is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form, which helps it retain moisture in its harsh environment. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that are densely packed, providing insulation against cold temperatures. It thrives in shaded, moist microhabitats and is often found on rocky substrates.
