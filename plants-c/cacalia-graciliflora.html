Cacalia graciliflora

Cacalia graciliflora, commonly known as slenderflower cacalia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals.

Its slender stems and delicate foliage make it a distinctive member of its genus.
