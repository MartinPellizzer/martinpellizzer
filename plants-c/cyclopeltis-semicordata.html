Cyclopeltis semicordata

Cyclopeltis semicordata, commonly known as the heartleaf cyclopeltis, is a tropical climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are usually a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature.

Cyclopeltis semicordata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
