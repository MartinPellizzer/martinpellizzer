Calathea rusbyi

Calathea rusbyi, commonly known as the Rusby's Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, patterned foliage. The leaves are typically broad and have intricate, variegated patterns in shades of green, silver, and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.
