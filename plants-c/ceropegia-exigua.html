Ceropegia exigua

Ceropegia exigua, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
