Cereus speciosissimus var. selloii

Cereus speciosissimus var. selloii, commonly known as the Sello's Christmas cactus, is a hybrid cactus species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has thick, segmented stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its flowers are typically red, orange, or yellow, with a velvety texture and a strong scent.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.
