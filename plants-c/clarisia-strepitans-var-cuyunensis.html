Clarisia strepitans var. cuyunensis

Clarisia strepitans var. cuyunensis, commonly known as the Cuyunensis Trumpet Vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
