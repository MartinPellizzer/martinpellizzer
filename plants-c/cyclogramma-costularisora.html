Cyclogramma costularisora

Cyclogramma costularisora, commonly known as the Brazilian burr legume, is a climbing leguminous plant native to South America.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, spiny fruit pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Cyclogramma costularisora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
