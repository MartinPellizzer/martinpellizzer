Cuphea lanceolata var. silenoides

Cuphea lanceolata var. silenoides, commonly known as the silverleaf cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It features striking silver-gray foliage that contrasts beautifully with its vibrant red to pink tubular flowers. The plant produces showy, clustered blooms that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
