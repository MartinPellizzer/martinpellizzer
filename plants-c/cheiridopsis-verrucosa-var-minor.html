Cheiridopsis verrucosa var. minor

Cheiridopsis verrucosa var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
