Canarium beccarii

Canarium beccarii, commonly known as the beccari's candlenut, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its latex is used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural rubber.

The tree is also valued for its hard, heavy wood used in construction and furniture.
