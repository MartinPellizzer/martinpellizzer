Caladium macrophyllum

Caladium macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The leaves are typically a vibrant shade of red, white, or variegated with green, making it a striking ornamental plant. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in garden landscapes for its dramatic foliage.

The plant is a herbaceous perennial that prefers well-draining soil and partial to full shade.
