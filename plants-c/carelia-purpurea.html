Carelia purpurea

Carelia purpurea, commonly known as the purple carelia, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
