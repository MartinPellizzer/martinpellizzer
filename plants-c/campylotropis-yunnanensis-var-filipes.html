Campylotropis yunnanensis var. filipes

Campylotropis yunnanensis var. filipes, commonly known as the Yunnan filiped clematis, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant is notable for its unique inflorescence structure, where the flowers are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
