Cucurbita pepo var. scutiformis

Cucurbita pepo var. scutiformis, commonly known as the prickly pear cactus, is a species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that are often covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant flowers, typically yellow or orange, which develop into edible fruits known as tunas. The leaves are usually lobed and can vary in size depending on the environment.

This plant is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in traditional medicine and as a food source.
