Clitoria rubiginosa var. ecostata

Clitoria rubiginosa var. ecostata, commonly known as the white-winged blue pea, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white margin. The flowers are typically purple to violet, with a unique white stripe running down the center of each petal. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
