Celmisia densiflora

Celmisia densiflora, commonly known as the denseflowered celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, short hairs.

Celmisia densiflora is often found in coastal and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
