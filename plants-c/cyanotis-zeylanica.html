Cyanotis zeylanica

Cyanotis zeylanica, commonly known as the blue-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

Cyanotis zeylanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
