Cattleya kettieana

Cattleya kettieana, commonly known as the Kettie's Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces fragrant blooms that can last several weeks. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Cattleya kettieana is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
