Chamaecrista leptadenia var. jaliscensis

Chamaecrista leptadenia var. jaliscensis, commonly known as the Mexican burr legume, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, woody pods that contain small, round seeds. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
