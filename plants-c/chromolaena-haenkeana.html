Chromolaena haenkeana

Chromolaena haenkeana, commonly known as the tropical heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming throughout the year in warm climates. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it an important species in ecological studies and horticulture.
