Cineraria vallispacis

Cineraria vallispacis, commonly known as the mountain cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its distinctive blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous terrain, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
