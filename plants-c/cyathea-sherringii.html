Cyathea sherringii

Cyathea sherringii, commonly known as the Sherring's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest understories. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth.

Cyathea sherringii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
