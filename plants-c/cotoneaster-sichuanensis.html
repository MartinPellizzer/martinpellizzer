Cotoneaster sichuanensis

Cotoneaster sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spreading growth habit. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. Its leaves are ovate with a serrated margin and turn yellow in autumn.

Cotoneaster sichuanensis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
