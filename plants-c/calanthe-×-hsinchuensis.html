Calanthe × hsinchuensis

Calanthe × hsinchuensis, commonly known as the Hsinchu orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a distinctive lip that is often a different color.

Calanthe × hsinchuensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
