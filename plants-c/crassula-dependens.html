Crassula dependens

Crassula dependens, commonly known as the string of pearls plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that hang in clusters, giving it a cascading appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
