Camara lilacina var. media

Camara lilacina var. media, commonly known as the compact camara or dwarf camara, is a variant of the Camara lilacina species, which belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

This plant is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant, lilac-colored flowers. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant is native to parts of South America.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
