Cnidoscolus aequatoriensis

Cnidoscolus aequatoriensis, commonly known as the "arrowhead plant," is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by spiny, edible fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
