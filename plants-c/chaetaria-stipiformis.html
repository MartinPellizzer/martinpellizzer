Chaetaria stipiformis

Chaetaria stipiformis, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences. Chaetaria stipiformis plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
