Colea telfairii

Colea telfairii, commonly known as Telfair's coleus, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage and showy bract flowers. Colea telfairii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
