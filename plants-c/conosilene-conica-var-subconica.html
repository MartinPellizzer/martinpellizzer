Conosilene conica var. subconica

Conosilene conica var. subconica, commonly known as the slender conosilene, is a rare and distinctive member of the Conosilene genus.

It is characterized by its elongated, conical shape and smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
