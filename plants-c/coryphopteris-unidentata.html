Coryphopteris unidentata

Coryphopteris unidentata, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands and parts of Southeast Asia.

Its rhizome system allows it to form dense clumps, and it reproduces through spores rather than seeds.
