Cyrtorchis praetermissa

Cyrtorchis praetermissa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as pine woods and forest floors. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, narrow leaf. Its flowers are small, pale green to yellowish, and are arranged in a spike, often with a faintly fragrant scent.

Cyrtorchis praetermissa is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific environmental conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
