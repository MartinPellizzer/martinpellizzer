Cistus × porphyreus f. pallens

Cistus × porphyreus f. pallens, commonly known as the pale cistus, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its gray-green, fuzzy leaves and clusters of pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
