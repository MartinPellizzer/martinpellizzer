Crotalaria stipularia f. elliptica

Crotalaria stipularia f. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic spurred crotalaria, is a variant of the species Crotalaria stipularia, characterized by its elliptical leaf shape.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant produces distinctive spurred flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with small leaflets arranged along a central axis.

This variety is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
