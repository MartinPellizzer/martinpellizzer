Chlorophytum rhodesianum

Chlorophytum rhodesianum, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features long, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent white or yellow stripes, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, flowering stalks that bear small, white, star-shaped flowers. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.

Chlorophytum rhodesianum thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.
