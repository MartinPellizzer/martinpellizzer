Callipteris smithiana

Callipteris smithiana, commonly known as the coastal maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils along the edges of swamps and coastal areas. The plant features delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically green with a slight reddish tint. It is often found in shaded, humid environments, making it a characteristic plant of wetland ecosystems.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important component of its native habitat.
