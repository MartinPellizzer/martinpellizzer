Carex Pendula Var. Angustifolia

Carex pendula var. angustifolia is a striking sedge known for its delicate, drooping foliage that adds a graceful touch to wetland environments. This variant of Carex pendula features narrow, grass-like leaves that sway elegantly in the breeze, creating a soft, flowing appearance. Native to moist habitats, it thrives in areas with consistent water levels and partial to full shade. Its unique form makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects. With its refined beauty and ecological benefits, this plant is both functional and visually appealing in natural and cultivated landscapes.
