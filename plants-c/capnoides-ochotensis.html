Capnoides ochotensis

Capnoides ochotensis, commonly known as the Far Eastern capnoid, is a flowering plant native to the Russian Far East and northeastern China.

It belongs to the family Capnoidaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and leathery leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or forested soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
