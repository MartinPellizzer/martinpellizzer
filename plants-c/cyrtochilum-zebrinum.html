Cyrtochilum zebrinum

Cyrtochilum zebrinum, commonly known as the Zebra Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces small, showy blooms with intricate patterns resembling a zebra's stripes, which give it its common name. Cyrtochilum zebrinum thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
