Crocosmia fenestrata

Crocosmia fenestrata, commonly known as the golden flame lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces vivid orange-red flowers in late summer. The plant is part of the iris family, Iridaceae, and is characterized by its showy, funnel-shaped blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Crocosmia fenestrata is also noted for its ability to naturalize and spread readily in suitable conditions.
