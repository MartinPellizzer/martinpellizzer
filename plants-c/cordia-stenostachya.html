Cordia stenostachya

Cordia stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stamen cordia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are often followed by distinctive, fleshy, orange-red fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough or hairy texture.

Cordia stenostachya is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and fruiting display.
