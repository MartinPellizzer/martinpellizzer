Caladenia preissii

Caladenia preissii, commonly known as the Purple Sun-orchid, is a species of orchid native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands. Its flowers are usually arranged in a single stem and bloom during the cooler months of the year.

Caladenia preissii is noted for its elegant appearance and is a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.
