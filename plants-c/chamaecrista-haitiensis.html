Chamaecrista haitiensis

Chamaecrista haitiensis, commonly known as Haitian burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Haiti.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are often dispersed by wind or animals.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
