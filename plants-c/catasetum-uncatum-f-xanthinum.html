Catasetum uncatum f. xanthinum

Catasetum uncatum f. xanthinum, commonly known as the yellow form of the catasetum uncatum, is a unique orchid with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which contrast sharply with the typical green or brown hues of the species. The plant produces elongated, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant blooms. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
