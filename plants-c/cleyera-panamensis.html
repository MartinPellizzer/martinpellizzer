Cleyera panamensis

Cleyera panamensis, commonly known as Panamanian holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Aquifoliaceae.

It is native to Central America, particularly Panama, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. Cleyera panamensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
