Cattleya superba var. splendens

Cattleya superba var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendens Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features tall, upright stems with large, velvety petals that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. Its flowers are typically fragrant and bloom during the summer months.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
