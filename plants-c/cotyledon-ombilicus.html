Cotyledon ombilicus

Cotyledon ombilicus, commonly known as the baby's tears plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Cotyledon ombilicus thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
