Cissus lindenii

Cissus lindenii, commonly known as the rock fig or climbing fig, is a member of the Vitaceae family.

It is a perennial, woody vine that is native to southern Africa. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a waxy coating. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits.

Cissus lindenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in rocky or arid environments.
