Chlorophytum crispum

Chlorophytum crispum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white stripes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal indoor plant. Its leaves are succulent, storing water to help it survive in arid environments.

Chlorophytum crispum is also noted for its air-purifying properties, improving indoor air quality.
