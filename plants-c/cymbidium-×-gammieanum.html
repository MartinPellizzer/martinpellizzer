Cymbidium × gammieanum

Cymbidium × gammieanum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Cymbidium lowianum and Cymbidium goeringii.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to China, characterized by its tall, branched inflorescence and large, showy flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in shades of white, pink, and yellow, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
