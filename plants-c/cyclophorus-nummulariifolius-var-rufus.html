Cyclophorus nummulariifolius var. rufus

Cyclophorus nummulariifolius var. rufus, commonly known as the red-leaved cyclophorus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, round, waxy leaves that are typically a deep red or maroon color. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
