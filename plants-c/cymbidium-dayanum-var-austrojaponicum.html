Cymbidium dayanum var. austrojaponicum

Cymbidium dayanum var. austrojaponicum, commonly known as the Japanese cold orchid, is a species native to East Asia, including regions of China, Japan, and Korea.

It is a terrestrial orchid with upright, linear leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

The species is also noted for its resilience to cold temperatures, making it suitable for temperate climates.
