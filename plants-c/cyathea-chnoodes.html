Cyathea chnoodes

Cyathea chnoodes, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height, with a slow growth rate. Its roots form a dense network that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Cyathea chnoodes is often found in moist, shaded environments such as tropical forests and swampy areas.
