Calathea Myrosma

The calathea myrosma is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, intricately patterned leaves. Native to the rainforests of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage displays a unique mix of green, silver, and pink hues that shift with the light. This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities. Proper care, including consistent moisture and indirect light, is essential to maintain its health and beauty.
