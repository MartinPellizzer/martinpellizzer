Crotalaria axillifloroides

Crotalaria axillifloroides, commonly known as the axillary-flowered cowpea, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its seed pods are slender and contain several elliptical seeds.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
