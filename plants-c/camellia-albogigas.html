Camellia albogigas

Camellia albogigas, commonly known as the white giant camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a compact growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, requiring moderate watering.

Camellia albogigas is also valued for its cold hardiness and ability to produce fragrant blooms.
