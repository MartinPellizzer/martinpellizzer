Clematis intermedia

Clematis intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to East Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink and violet, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis intermedia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
