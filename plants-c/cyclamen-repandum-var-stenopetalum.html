Cyclamen repandum var. stenopetalum

Cyclamen repandum var. stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a wavy margin and produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The flowers typically bloom in late winter to early spring, adding early color to gardens. The plant thrives in cool, moist conditions and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a shaded garden setting.
