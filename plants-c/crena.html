Crena

Crena, commonly known as the water cress, is a aquatic or semi-aquatic plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It typically grows in shallow, flowing water or damp soil, often near streams and ponds. The plant has small, edible leaves that are rich in nutrients and have a peppery flavor. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and arranged in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Crena is known for its ability to thrive in moist environments and is often used in culinary applications.
