Capparis rosea

Capparis rosea, commonly known as the rose apple or pink caper, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It produces striking pink or red flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant has a woody structure with thorny branches and elliptical leaves. Its fruit is a small, round, yellowish berry that ripens in late summer.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional medicinal uses.
