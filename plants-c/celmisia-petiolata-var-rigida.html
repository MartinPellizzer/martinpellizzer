Celmisia petiolata var. rigida

Celmisia petiolata var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid petioled celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or on rocky slopes.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
