Croton scordioides

Croton scordioides, commonly known as the sour leaf or wild croton, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its alternate, simple, and often heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Croton scordioides is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
