Crotalaria filicaulis var. filicaulis

Crotalaria filicaulis var. filicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cowpea, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and pinnately compound leaves. The flowers are typically yellow with a distinctive black or purple spot at the base, resembling a cowpea. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

It is often grown as a forage crop or for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
