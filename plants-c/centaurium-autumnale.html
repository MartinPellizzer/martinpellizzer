Centaurium autumnale

Centaurium autumnale, commonly known as autumn centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the gentian family (Gentianaceae) and typically grows in moist, grassy habitats. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.
