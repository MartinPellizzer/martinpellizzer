Cleome rutidosperma var. hainanensis

Cleome rutidosperma var. hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan cleome, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and showy, yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive blooms.
