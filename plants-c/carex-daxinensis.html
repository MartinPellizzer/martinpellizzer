Carex daxinensis

Carex daxinensis, commonly known as the Daxin sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Daxin region in Guangxi Province. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers.

Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a rhizomatous root system, which help it thrive in its natural habitat.
