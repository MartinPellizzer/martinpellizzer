Croton lucidus

Croton lucidus, commonly known as the shining croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
