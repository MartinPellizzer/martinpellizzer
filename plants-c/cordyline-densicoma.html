Cordyline densicoma

Cordyline densicoma, commonly known as the dense-leaved cabbage tree, is a striking tropical plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its robust, upright stems and large, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that appear at the base of the leaves, followed by ornamental red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
