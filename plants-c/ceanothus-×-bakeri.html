Ceanothus × bakeri

Ceanothus × bakeri, commonly known as the Baker's ceanothus, is a hybrid shrub native to California.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic leaves and long-lasting blooms.

Ceanothus × bakeri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
