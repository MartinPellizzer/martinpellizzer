Commelina africana subsp. africana

Commelina africana subsp.

africana, commonly known as African spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to sub-Saharan Africa. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and riverbanks.

The plant features striking blue to violet flowers with three petals and prominent white spots. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy surface.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
