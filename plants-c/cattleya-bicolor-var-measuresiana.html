Cattleya bicolor var. measuresiana

Cattleya bicolor var. measuresiana, commonly known as the Measuresiana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking bicolor flowers, typically featuring a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant produces large, showy blooms that are often fragrant, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
