Cistus × mariae

Cistus × mariae, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with silver-white undersides, and it produces vibrant pink or purple flowers in the spring. The plant is well adapted to poor, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

Cistus × mariae is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.
