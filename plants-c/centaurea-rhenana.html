Centaurea rhenana

Centaurea rhenana, commonly known as the Rhine centaury, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe.

It typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are bright yellow with purple or brown markings, arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
