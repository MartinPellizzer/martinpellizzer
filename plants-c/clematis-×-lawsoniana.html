Clematis × lawsoniana

Clematis × lawsoniana, commonly known as the Lawson's clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces smooth, green stems with thornless tendrils that help it climb. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and attractive foliage.
