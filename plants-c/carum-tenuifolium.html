Carum tenuifolium

Carum tenuifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved caraway, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, aromatic leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its essential oils contain compounds like limonene and carvone, which contribute to its distinctive aroma and various therapeutic properties.
