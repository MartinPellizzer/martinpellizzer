Cyperus plukenetii

Cyperus plukenetii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. It has grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
