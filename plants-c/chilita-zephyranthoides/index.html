Chilita Zephyranthoides

Chilita zephyranthoides is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its striking purple and white blooms that appear in early spring. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires careful cultivation. Its unique appearance makes it a prized addition to botanical gardens. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and needs conservation efforts to survive.
