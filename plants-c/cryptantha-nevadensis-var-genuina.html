Cryptantha nevadensis var. genuina

Cryptantha nevadensis var. genuina, commonly known as the Sierra cryptantha, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, such as mountain slopes and open grasslands. It produces small, oval pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
