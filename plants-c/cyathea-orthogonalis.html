Cyathea orthogonalis

Cyathea orthogonalis, commonly known as the upright tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Cyathea orthogonalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
