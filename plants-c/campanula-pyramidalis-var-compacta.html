Campanula pyramidalis var. compacta

Campanula pyramidalis var. compacta, commonly known as the compact bellflower, is a compact, mat-forming perennial that produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine绒毛, contributing to its rugged appearance. It blooms from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variety is valued for its drought tolerance and suitability for rock gardens or as a border plant.
