Cyperus hexastachyos

Cyperus hexastachyos, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies.

Cyperus hexastachyos is a perennial herb that plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
