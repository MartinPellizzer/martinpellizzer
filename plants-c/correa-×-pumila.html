Correa × pumila

Correa × pumila, commonly known as the hybrid Correa, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight red or purple tinge along the veins. The plant produces clusters of tubular, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Correa × pumila is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
