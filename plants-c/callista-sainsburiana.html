Callista sainsburiana

Callista sainsburiana, commonly known as the Sainsbury's callista, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are arranged in dense, spherical clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
