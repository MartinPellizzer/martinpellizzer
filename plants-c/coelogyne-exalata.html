Coelogyne exalata

Coelogyne exalata, commonly known as the white-lipped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers with pinkish lips and a slender, erect stem. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Coelogyne exalata is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
