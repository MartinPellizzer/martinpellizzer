Capnoides bulbosa

Capnoides bulbosa, commonly known as the bulbous capnoid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features a bulb-like structure at its base, which serves as a storage organ for nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of white or pink.

Capnoides bulbosa is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a hardy species in dry environments.
