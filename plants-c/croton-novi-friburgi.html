Croton novi-friburgi

Croton novi-friburgi, commonly known as the New Friburg Croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright yellow to deep red. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.
