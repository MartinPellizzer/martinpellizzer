Combretum unyorense

Combretum unyorense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and turn yellow before falling off during the dry season. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
