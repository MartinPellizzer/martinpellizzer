Cyamopsis senegalensis var. stenophylla

Cyamopsis senegalensis var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Cyamopsis, is a flowering plant native to the Sahel region of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates. It produces pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients.

This variety is often used in traditional agriculture and has potential for sustainable farming practices.
