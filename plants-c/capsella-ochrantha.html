Capsella ochrantha

Capsella ochrantha, commonly known as the scarlet mallow, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Capsella ochrantha is an annual herb that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.
