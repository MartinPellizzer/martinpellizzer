Cirsium muraii

Cirsium muraii, commonly known as the Japanese thistle, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, and its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Cirsium muraii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other parts of the world for horticultural purposes.
