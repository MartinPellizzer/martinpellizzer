Centaurea chaixiana var. confusa

Centaurea chaixiana var. confusa, commonly known as the Confused Centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with some having a slight hairy texture.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
