Croton massangeanum

Croton massangeanum, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is popular in horticulture for its vibrant colors and unique leaf patterns.
