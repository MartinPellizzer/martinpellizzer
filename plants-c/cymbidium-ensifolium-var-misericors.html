Cymbidium ensifolium var. misericors

Cymbidium ensifolium var. misericors, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It prefers cool, humid environments and well-draining soil, making it suitable for cultivation in shaded garden areas or as a potted plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in traditional Chinese horticulture.
