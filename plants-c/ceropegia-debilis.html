Ceropegia debilis

Ceropegia debilis, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent that features long, thin stems with heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia debilis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
