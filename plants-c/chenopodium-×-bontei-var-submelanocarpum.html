Chenopodium × bontei var. submelanocarpum

Chenopodium × bontei var. submelanocarpum, commonly known as the blackstem goosefoot, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to rhombic in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in compact inflorescences, and its fruits are small, dry capsules containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

This variety is notable for its robust growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
