Croton zambesicus

Croton zambesicus, commonly known as the Zambesic Croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The plant is part of the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often striking foliage. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Croton zambesicus is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and can be grown as a houseplant or in outdoor gardens.
