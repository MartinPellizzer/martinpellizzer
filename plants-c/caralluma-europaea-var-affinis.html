Caralluma europaea var. affinis

Caralluma europaea var. affinis, commonly known as the European horned cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
