Carex harlandii var. liuguensis

Carex harlandii var. liuguensis, commonly known as the Liuguensis sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in early summer.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
