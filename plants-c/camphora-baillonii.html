Camphora baillonii

Camphora baillonii, commonly known as the African camphor tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark that contain camphor. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with a glossy surface and can grow up to 20 meters tall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The tree is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
