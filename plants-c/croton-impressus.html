Croton impressus

Croton impressus, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and purple. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.

Croton impressus prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
