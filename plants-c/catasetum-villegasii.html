Catasetum villegasii

Catasetum villegasii, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of showy, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and wasps.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
