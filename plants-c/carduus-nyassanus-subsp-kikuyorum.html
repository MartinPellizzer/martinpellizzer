Carduus nyassanus subsp. kikuyorum

Carduus nyassanus subsp.

kikuyorum, commonly known as Kikuyu thistle, is a perennial herb native to East Africa. It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, and the stems are often woody at the base.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking appearance.
