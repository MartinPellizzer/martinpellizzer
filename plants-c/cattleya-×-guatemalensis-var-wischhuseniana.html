Cattleya × guatemalensis var. wischhuseniana

Cattleya × guatemalensis var. wischhuseniana, commonly known as the Wischhuseniana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid originating from Central America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant produces showy, pendulous blooms with prominent lip structures, typical of the Cattleya genus. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
