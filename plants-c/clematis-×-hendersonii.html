Clematis × hendersonii

Clematis × hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson's Clematis, is a hybrid species native to China.

It is a vigorous climbing vine that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis × hendersonii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
