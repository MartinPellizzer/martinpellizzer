Chenopodium hederiforme var. dentatum

Chenopodium hederiforme var. dentatum, commonly known as curlyleaf goosefoot, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and are pollinated by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
