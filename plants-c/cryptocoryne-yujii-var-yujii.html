Cryptocoryne yujii var. yujii

Cryptocoryne yujii var. yujii, commonly known as the Yujii cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate venation patterns. The plant typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams. It is a popular choice among aquarium enthusiasts due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.

Cryptocoryne yujii var. yujii is also noted for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its ease of care.
