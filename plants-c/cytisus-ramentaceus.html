Cytisus ramentaceus

Cytisus ramentaceus, commonly known as the woolly broom, is a flowering shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, grayish foliage covered in fine, woolly hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Cytisus ramentaceus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
