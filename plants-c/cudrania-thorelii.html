Cudrania thorelii

Cudrania thorelii, commonly known as the African sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with a deep root system.
