Campanula stricta var. stricta

Campanula stricta var. stricta, commonly known as the upright bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, often with a bluish-green hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is known for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
