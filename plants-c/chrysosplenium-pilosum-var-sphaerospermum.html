Chrysosplenium pilosum var. sphaerospermum

Chrysosplenium pilosum var. sphaerospermum, commonly known as the woolly chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Asia.

It features small, hairy leaves that are typically rounded or ovate in shape. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands or stream banks.
