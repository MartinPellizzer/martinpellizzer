Catasetum linguiferum

Catasetum linguiferum, commonly known as the velvet tassel orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated flowers that resemble tassels, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces a distinctive scent that attracts specific pollinators, such as bees and wasps. Its inflorescence is typically composed of multiple flowers arranged in a raceme, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Catasetum linguiferum is known for its striking appearance and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
