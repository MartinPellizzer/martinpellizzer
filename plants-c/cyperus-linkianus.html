Cyperus linkianus

Cyperus linkianus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the sedge family, Cyperaceae, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often near water bodies. Its inflorescence consists of a cylindrical spikelet that produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Cyperus linkianus is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
