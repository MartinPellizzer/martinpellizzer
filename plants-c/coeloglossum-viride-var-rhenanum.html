Coeloglossum viride var. rhenanum

Coeloglossum viride var. rhenanum, commonly known as the Rhenish green helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces pale green to white flowers with three petals and three sepals, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and is often found in rich, loamy soils.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in many regions.
