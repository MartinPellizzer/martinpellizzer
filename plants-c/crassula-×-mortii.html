Crassula × mortii

Crassula × mortii, commonly known as the "Little Leaf Crassula," is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight red or purple hue on the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
