Camellia lienshanensis

Camellia lienshanensis, commonly known as the Lienshan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic, well-drained soil. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and elliptical in shape.

Camellia lienshanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical gardens.
