Cleidion ramosii var. lanceolatum

Cleidion ramosii var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceolate sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn.
