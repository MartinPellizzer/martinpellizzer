Cleistes divaricata

Cleistes divaricata, commonly known as the sweetshrub, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant produces small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Cleistes divaricata thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.
