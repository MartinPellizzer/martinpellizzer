Chilocarpus kuchingensis

Chilocarpus kuchingensis, commonly known as the Kuching ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and evergreen leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruits are woody capsules that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its durability and is often found in lowland rainforest areas.
