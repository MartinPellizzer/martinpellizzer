Croton quadripartitus

Croton quadripartitus, commonly known as the four-parted croton, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is characterized by its milky sap and often unusual floral structures. Croton quadripartitus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and unique appearance.
