Cascarilla Magnifolia Var. Rostrata

Cascarilla magnifolia var. rostrata is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique, elongated fruit, it stands out among its relatives in the Rubiaceae family. This variant is prized by botanists and horticulturists for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties. Its cultivation is challenging due to specific environmental requirements. Despite its rarity, it continues to captivate researchers and plant lovers alike.
