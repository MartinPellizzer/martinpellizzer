Cirsium × kelkitense

Cirsium × kelkitense, commonly known as the Turkish thistle, is a hybrid plant species native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, purple flowers and robust, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, and it produces achenes that are dispersed by wind.

Cirsium × kelkitense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its unique appearance.
