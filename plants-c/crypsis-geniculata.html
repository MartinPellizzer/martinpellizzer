Crypsis geniculata

Crypsis geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread along the forest floor. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

Crypsis geniculata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
