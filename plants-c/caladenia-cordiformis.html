Caladenia cordiformis

Caladenia cordiformis, commonly known as the heart-shaped spider orchid, is a member of the orchid family Orchidaceae.

It is native to southeastern Australia, particularly in New South Wales and Victoria. The plant is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped labellum, which gives it its common name. It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, often in sandy or gravelly soils.

Caladenia cordiformis is a terrestrial herb with a single stem that bears a single, showy flower.
