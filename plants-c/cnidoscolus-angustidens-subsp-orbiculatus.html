Cnidoscolus angustidens subsp. orbiculatus

Cnidoscolus angustidens subsp.

orbiculatus, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowleaf, is a shrub native to parts of South America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in small, stinging hairs.

The plant produces yellow flowers followed by edible, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
