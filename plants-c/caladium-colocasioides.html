Caladium colocasioides

Caladium colocasioides, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial that features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a vibrant array of colors.

Native to the wetlands of South America, it thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade. The plant's foliage is its main ornamental feature, often displaying shades of green, red, and white. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Caladium colocasioides is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
