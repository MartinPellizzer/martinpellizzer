Convolvulus cherleri

Convolvulus cherleri, commonly known as the French morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often blooming in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it suitable for various horticultural applications.
