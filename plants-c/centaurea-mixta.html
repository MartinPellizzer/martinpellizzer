Centaurea mixta

Centaurea mixta, commonly known as the Mixed Centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Centaurea mixta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
