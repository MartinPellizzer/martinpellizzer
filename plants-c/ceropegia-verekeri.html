Ceropegia verekeri

Ceropegia verekeri, commonly known as the African violet or velvet leaf.

This plant is native to South Africa and belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family. It is characterized by its trailing stems and fleshy, often hairy leaves. The flowers are small, tubular, and typically pink or purple in color.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
