Cirsium × spinosissimoides

Cirsium × spinosissimoides, commonly known as the giant thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, spiny leaves and showy purple to pink flower heads. The plant is native to the western United States and thrives in dry, rocky soils. It has a robust root system and can grow up to several feet tall.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens despite its potential invasiveness.
