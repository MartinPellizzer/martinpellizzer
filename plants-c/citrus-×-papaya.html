Citrus × papaya

Citrus × papaya, commonly known as the hybrid citrus-papaya plant, is a unique cross between citrus and papaya species.

It exhibits a combination of traits from both parent plants, including the citrus fruit's aromatic oils and the papaya's tropical appearance. The plant typically has broad, lobed leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible fruits. Its fruit is usually a hybrid of citrus and papaya, with a sweet, tangy flavor and a soft texture.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
