Crinum longifolium var. farinianum

Crinum longifolium var. farinianum, commonly known as the farinianum crinum, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer, often with a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant has strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its robust growth and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
