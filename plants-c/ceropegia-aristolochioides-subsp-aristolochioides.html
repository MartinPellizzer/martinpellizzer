Ceropegia aristolochioides subsp. aristolochioides

Ceropegia aristolochioides subsp.

aristolochioides, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that resemble small horns.
