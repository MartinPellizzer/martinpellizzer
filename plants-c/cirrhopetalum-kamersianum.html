Cirrhopetalum kamersianum

Cirrhopetalum kamersianum, commonly known as the Kamersian bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant features showy, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
