Chorizema magnifolium

Chorizema magnifolium, commonly known as the magnificent leaf chorizema, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant has a woody stem and is adapted to thrive in dry, rocky environments. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Chorizema magnifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
