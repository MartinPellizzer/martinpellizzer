Cyrtopera richardsiae

Cyrtopera richardsiae, commonly known as the Richardsia orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

The flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a range of colors from white to pale yellow.
