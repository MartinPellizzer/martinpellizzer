Cotyledon flanaganii subsp. mzimvubuensis

Cotyledon flanaganii subsp.

mzimvubuensis, commonly known as the Mzimvubu cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
