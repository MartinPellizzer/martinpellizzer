Coelogyne pholidotoides

Coelogyne pholidotoides, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or pink throat. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne pholidotoides is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
