Ceodes umbellifera f. acuminata

Ceodes umbellifera f. acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ceodes, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stem.
