Cynanchum muliense

Cynanchum muliense, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its roots are used to treat various ailments, including inflammation and respiratory conditions.
