Calathea pruinata

Calathea pruinata, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns of silver and green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish. Its foliage is often adorned with a waxy coating that gives it a distinctive, almost metallic appearance.

Calathea pruinata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
