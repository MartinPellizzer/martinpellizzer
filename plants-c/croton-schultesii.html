Croton schultesii

Croton schultesii, commonly known as the Schultesi croton, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that vary in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.

Croton schultesii is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
