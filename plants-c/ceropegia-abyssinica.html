Ceropegia abyssinica

Ceropegia abyssinica, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
