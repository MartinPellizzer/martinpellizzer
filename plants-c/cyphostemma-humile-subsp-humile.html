Cyphostemma humile subsp. humile

Cyphostemma humile subsp.

humile, commonly known as the dwarf moon vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa. It features small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by yellow berries.

The plant has thin, woody stems and is adapted to arid environments. It is often found growing in rocky areas and along riverbanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to xerophytic gardens.
