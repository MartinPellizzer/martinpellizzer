Carmichaelia australis var. strictissima

Carmichaelia australis var. strictissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Carmichaelia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
