Conophyllum abbreviatum

Conophyllum abbreviatum, commonly known as the dwarf conophyllum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of only a few centimeters. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow to white flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.

Conophyllum abbreviatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
