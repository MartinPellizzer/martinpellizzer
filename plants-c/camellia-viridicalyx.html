Camellia viridicalyx

Camellia viridicalyx, commonly known as the emerald leaf camellia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is an evergreen shrub that thrives in acidic, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Camellia viridicalyx is also valued for its cold hardiness and ability to bloom in late winter or early spring.
