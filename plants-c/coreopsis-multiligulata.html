Coreopsis multiligulata

Coreopsis multiligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved coreopsis, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics include a robust root system and a compact growth habit.
