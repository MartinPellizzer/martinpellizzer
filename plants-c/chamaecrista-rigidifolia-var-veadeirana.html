Chamaecrista rigidifolia var. veadeirana

Chamaecrista rigidifolia var. veadeirana, commonly known as the Veadeirana burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves with small, elliptical leaflets. The plant produces raceme-like inflorescences of bright yellow flowers, which are typical of the Fabaceae family. Its seeds are enclosed in small, leathery pods that mature to a dark brown color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
