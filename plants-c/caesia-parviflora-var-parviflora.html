Caesia parviflora var. parviflora

Caesia parviflora var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered caesia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems covered in narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This species is often found in wetlands, swamps, and along stream banks.
