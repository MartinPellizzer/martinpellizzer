Cheirostylis takeoi

Cheirostylis takeoi, commonly known as the Takeo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns on the petals.

Cheirostylis takeoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
