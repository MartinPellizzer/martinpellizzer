Calophyllum hasskarlii

Calophyllum hasskarlii, commonly known as the African sandpaper tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Clusiaceae family.

It is native to regions of Africa, particularly the tropical rainforests of Central and Eastern Africa. The plant is characterized by its rough, sandpaper-like bark and lance-shaped leaves. It produces yellow or orange flowers followed by woody, berry-like fruits.

Calophyllum hasskarlii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
