Cestichis latifolia

Cestichis latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf cestichis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Cestichis latifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
