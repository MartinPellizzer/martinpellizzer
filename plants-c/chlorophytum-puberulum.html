Chlorophytum puberulum

Chlorophytum puberulum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is a monocot with long, grass-like leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, grass-like flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright green, dangling plantlets that can be easily propagated. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate watering, making it an easy plant to care for.

Its ability to purify indoor air and tolerate low light conditions further adds to its appeal as a houseplant.
