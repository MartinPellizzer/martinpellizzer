Cryptandra nervata

Cryptandra nervata, commonly known as the narrow-veined cryptandra, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves with prominent venation. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often appearing in spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
