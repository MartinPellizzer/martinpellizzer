Cotula angustata

Cotula angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf mouseear, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Cotula angustata is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
