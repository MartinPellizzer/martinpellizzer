Centaurea arifolia

Centaurea arifolia, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with purple markings. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Centaurea arifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
