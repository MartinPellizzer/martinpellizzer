Centaurea × pagesii

Centaurea × pagesii, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flower heads, which consist of both ray and disc florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often gray-green in color. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Centaurea × pagesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
