Clematis peii

Clematis peii, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, with five to seven petals. The plant produces small, reddish-brown berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis peii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
