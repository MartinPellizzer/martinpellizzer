Calliandra laxa var. stipulacea

Calliandra laxa var. stipulacea, commonly known as the stipulate calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences composed of numerous small, showy flowers. The plant has compound leaves with stipules, which are small, leaf-like structures at the base of the leaf stalks. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant color and delicate appearance.
