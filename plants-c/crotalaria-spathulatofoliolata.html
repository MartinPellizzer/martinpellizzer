Crotalaria spathulatofoliolata

Crotalaria spathulatofoliolata, commonly known as the broad-leafed crotalaria, is a species belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, featuring broad, heart-shaped leaves with prominent leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences, which are often accompanied by spathelike bracts. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and nitrogen-fixing ability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
