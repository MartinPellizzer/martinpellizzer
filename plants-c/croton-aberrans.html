Croton aberrans

Croton aberrans, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright red to deep green and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
