Clitoria poiteaui

Clitoria poiteaui, commonly known as the pink orchid vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, butterfly-shaped pink or purple flowers. The plant is a climbing vine that produces small, bean-like pods containing seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Clitoria poiteaui is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
