Cryptophragmium pierrei

Cryptophragmium pierrei, commonly known as the French horn orchid, is a unique and rare orchid species native to the southeastern United States.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer, typically in shaded, moist environments. Cryptophragmium pierrei has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in limestone-rich soils.
