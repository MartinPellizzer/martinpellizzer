Crassula multicava

Crassula multicava, commonly known as the "Panda Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

Crassula multicava is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and unique aesthetic.
