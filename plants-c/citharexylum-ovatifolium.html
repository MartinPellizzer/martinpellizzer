Citharexylum ovatifolium

Citharexylum ovatifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters. The plant features ovate, glossy, dark green leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale pink, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months.
