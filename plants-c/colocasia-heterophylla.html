Colocasia heterophylla

Colocasia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that vary in size and shape depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces striking purple or red spadices that are often surrounded by a white or green spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along streams.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
