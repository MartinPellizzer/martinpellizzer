Crassula flava

Crassula flava, commonly known as the Orange Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish or orange tinge on the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula flava is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
