Clematis patens var. sophiae

Clematis patens var. sophiae, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to East Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of white to pink. The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can climb using its tendrils.

The flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer.
