Citharexylum Microphyllum

Citharexylum microphyllum is a small evergreen shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. It is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers and compact growth habit. This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and easy care requirements. Its leaves are small, glossy, and provide a lush green backdrop to its blossoms. Despite its modest size, it holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitats.
