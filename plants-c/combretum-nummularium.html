Combretum nummularium

Combretum nummularium, commonly known as the silverleaf combretum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, round, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
