Cassia planitiicola

Cassia planitiicola, commonly known as the African cassia, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa.

It features compound leaves with alternating pairs of leaflets, and it produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Cassia planitiicola is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
