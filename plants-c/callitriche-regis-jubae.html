Callitriche regis-jubae

Callitriche regis-jubae, commonly known as the royal jubaea water-starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Callitrichaceae.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams. The plant features tiny, star-shaped leaves that are often submerged, with some floating on the water surface. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Callitriche regis-jubae is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich waters and is often used in wetland restoration projects.
