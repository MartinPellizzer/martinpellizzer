Chromolaena trujillensis

Chromolaena trujillensis, commonly known as the Trujillense chromolaena, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by fluffy, white seed heads that aid in wind dispersal.
