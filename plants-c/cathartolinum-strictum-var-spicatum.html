Cathartolinum strictum var. spicatum

Cathartolinum strictum var. spicatum, commonly known as the spiculate cathartolinum, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
