Coelogyne Serratoi

Coelogyne serratoi is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms, this plant is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to its growing conditions. The flowers often display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues, making them visually stunning. Despite its beauty, Coelogyne serratoi is relatively rare in cultivation, making it a prized specimen for collectors.
