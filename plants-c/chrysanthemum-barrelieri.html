Chrysanthemum barrelieri

Chrysanthemum barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy yellow to orange flowers with ray florets surrounding a central disk. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Chrysanthemum barrelieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
