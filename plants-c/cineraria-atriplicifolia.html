Cineraria atriplicifolia

Cineraria atriplicifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant features dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cineraria atriplicifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
