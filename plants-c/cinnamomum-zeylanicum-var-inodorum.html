Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. inodorum

Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. inodorum, commonly known as the cinnamon tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain aromatic seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The variety is distinguished by its lack of strong cinnamon scent compared to other Cinnamomum species.
