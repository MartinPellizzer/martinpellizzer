Cyrtopodium punctatum var. bracteatum

Cyrtopodium punctatum var. bracteatum, commonly known as the bracteate spider orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spider-like flowers with intricate, often fragrant structures. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
