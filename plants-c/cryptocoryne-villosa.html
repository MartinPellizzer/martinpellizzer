Cryptocoryne villosa

Cryptocoryne villosa, commonly known as the velvet cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety texture and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and rivers. It is often used in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne villosa is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its tolerance to a range of water parameters.
