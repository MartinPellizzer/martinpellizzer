Caladenia magnifica

Caladenia magnifica, commonly known as the Splendid Spider-orchid, is a striking orchid species native to the southern regions of Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance with elongated petals and a distinctive labellum. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun, often in coastal or heathland environments. Its flowers are usually a vibrant mix of pink, red, and white, and they bloom during the spring and early summer months.

Caladenia magnifica is a protected species due to habitat loss and is often found in conservation areas.
