Cleome frutescens

Cleome frutescens, commonly known as spider flower, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

The plant is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
