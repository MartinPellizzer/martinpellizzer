Calonemorchis lodgeana

The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained, rocky soils, often growing on cliffs or among moss-covered stones. Its flowering period occurs during the late spring to early summer, and it is adapted to the extreme conditions of its mountainous habitat.
