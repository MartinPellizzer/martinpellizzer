Cymbidium chawalongense

Cymbidium chawalongense, commonly known as the Chawalong orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with long, slender flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
