Cinnamomum delavayi var. mekongense

Cinnamomum delavayi var. mekongense, commonly known as the Yunnan cinnamon or Mekong cinnamon, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces small, dark reddish-brown fruits, which contain one or two seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The leaves and bark of this species are used in traditional medicine and for their aromatic properties.
