Conophytum piluliforme subsp. edwardii

Conophytum piluliforme subsp.

edwardii, commonly known as the Edward's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its small, rounded, fleshy leaves that store water.
