Combretum sokodense

Combretum sokodense, commonly known as the Sokodense flame bush, is a shrub or small tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in dry or seasonally wet habitats. Its leaves are usually simple and opposite, with a rough or hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
