Columnea chocoensis var. chocoensis

Columnea chocoensis var. chocoensis, commonly known as the Chocoan flame bell, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow throats. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are highly attractive to hummingbirds and other pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add dramatic color to gardens and landscapes.
