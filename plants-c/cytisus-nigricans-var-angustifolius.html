Cytisus nigricans var. angustifolius

Cytisus nigricans var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, woody stems and small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive silvery underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal and rocky environments.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.
