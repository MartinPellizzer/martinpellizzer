Cattleya labiata var. warneri

Cattleya labiata var. warneri, commonly known as the Warner's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

This variety is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically display a combination of white, pink, and violet hues. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral characteristics.
