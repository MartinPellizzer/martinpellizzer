Crinum rigidum var. sumatranum

Crinum rigidum var. sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran Crinum, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soil.

The Sumatran Crinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
