Croton ramiflorus

Croton ramiflorus, commonly known as the branching-flower croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display a wide range of hues, including green, red, and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Croton ramiflorus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and unique appearance.
