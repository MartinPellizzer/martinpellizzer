Cyperus sesquiflorus f. latifolius

Cyperus sesquiflorus f. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf sedgeland sedge, is a variant of the sedgeland sedge species.

It is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically broader than those of the typical variety. It produces small, clustered flower spikes that are usually greenish or reddish in color.

This plant thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.
