Crepis neglecta subsp. cretica

Crepis neglecta subsp.

cretica, commonly known as the Cretan mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a small annual or biennial herb with hairy, linear leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.
