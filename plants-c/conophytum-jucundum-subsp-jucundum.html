Conophytum jucundum subsp. jucundum

Conophytum jucundum subsp.

jucundum, commonly known as the "Happy Conophytum," is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.
