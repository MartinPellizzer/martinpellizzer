Claoxylon tumidum

Claoxylon tumidum, commonly known as the round-leafed hibiscus, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves that are often rounded or oval in shape. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Claoxylon tumidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
