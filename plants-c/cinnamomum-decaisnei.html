Cinnamomum decaisnei

Cinnamomum decaisnei, commonly known as the Decaisne cinnamon or Chinese cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-scented leaves and bark. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a dark green upper surface.

The bark, which is the source of its aromatic compounds, is reddish-brown and peels off in thin layers.
