Cotyledon decussata var. rubra

Cotyledon decussata var. rubra, commonly known as the red-leaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The leaves are arranged in a cross shape, giving the plant its scientific name. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its vibrant color and unique appearance.
