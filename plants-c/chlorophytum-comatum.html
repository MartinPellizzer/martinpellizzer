Chlorophytum comatum

Chlorophytum comatum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often have a white or yellow stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, grass-like flowers that are typically green or white in color. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions.

Chlorophytum comatum is also known for its ability to purify indoor air and propagate readily through its offsets.
