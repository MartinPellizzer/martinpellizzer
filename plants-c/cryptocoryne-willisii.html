Cryptocoryne willisii

Cryptocoryne willisii, commonly known as the Willis's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a faintly visible midrib. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams, and prefers moderate to high light conditions. Its rhizome system allows it to anchor itself in the substrate while spreading vegetatively.

Cryptocoryne willisii is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
