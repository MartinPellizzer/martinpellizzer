Clematis kogenensis

Clematis kogenensis, commonly known as the Japanese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features climbing stems with large, showy flowers that range from white to deep purple. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its blooms typically appear in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Clematis kogenensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
