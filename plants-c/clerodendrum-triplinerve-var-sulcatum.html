Clerodendrum triplinerve var. sulcatum

Clerodendrum triplinerve var. sulcatum, commonly known as the Sulcatum Clerodendrum, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive triplinerve venation pattern. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The varietal name 'sulcatum' refers to the grooved or furrowed texture of its stems.
