Calamus schweinfurthii

Calamus schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's canes, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Indonesia, and the Philippines. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The leaves are long and narrow, often with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible, black, fleshy fruits.
