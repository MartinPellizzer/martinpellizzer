Commelina lukei

Commelina lukei, commonly known as the Hawaiian Commelina, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes a variety of ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features striking blue-violet flowers with white markings, typically blooming in the early morning. Its leaves are broad and often have a waxy texture, adapted to the humid tropical climate.

Commelina lukei is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest floors and stream banks.
