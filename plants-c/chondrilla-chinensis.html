Chondrilla chinensis

Chondrilla chinensis, commonly known as Chinese sandmat, is a perennial flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its low, mat-like growth habit with silver-green, fleshy leaves. Chondrilla chinensis is also noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in traditional Chinese medicine.
