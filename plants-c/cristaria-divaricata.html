Cristaria divaricata

Cristaria divaricata, commonly known as the variegated spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, trailing growth habit and striking variegated leaves that feature shades of green, white, and yellow. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in well-draining soil and moderate light conditions. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by red berries.

Cristaria divaricata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ability to purify the air.
