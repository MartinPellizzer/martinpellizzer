Chlorophora tenuifolia

Chlorophora tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leafed evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing smooth, oval seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
