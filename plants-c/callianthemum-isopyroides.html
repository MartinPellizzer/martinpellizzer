Callianthemum isopyroides

Callianthemum isopyroides, commonly known as the Pyrenean glory flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Callianthemum isopyroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, early spring blooms.
