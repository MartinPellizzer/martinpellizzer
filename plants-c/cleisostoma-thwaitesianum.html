Cleisostoma thwaitesianum

Cleisostoma thwaitesianum, commonly known as the Wight's cleisostoma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical orchids.
