Combretum paniculatum

Combretum paniculatum, commonly known as the red-veined flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in panicles, giving it a dramatic visual display. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often pubescent and have a distinctive reddish or bronze hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various tropical environments.

The species is also noted for its ability to withstand drought conditions once established.
