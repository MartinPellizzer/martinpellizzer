Cardiochlaena macrophylla var. discreta

Cardiochlaena macrophylla var. discreta, commonly known as the slender leaf mallow, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically 5-10 centimeters long and are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pink to purple, with five petals and appear in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
