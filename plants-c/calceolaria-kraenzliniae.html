Calceolaria kraenzliniae

Calceolaria kraenzliniae, commonly known as the Peruvian slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Peru.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands.

Calceolaria kraenzliniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its distinctive floral display.
