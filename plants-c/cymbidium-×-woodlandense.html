Cymbidium × woodlandense

Cymbidium × woodlandense, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high light exposure.

Cymbidium × woodlandense is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
