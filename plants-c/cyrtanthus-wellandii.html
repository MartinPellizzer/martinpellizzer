Cyrtanthus wellandii

Cyrtanthus wellandii, commonly known as the Wellandii bellflower, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant features strap-like, linear leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Cyrtanthus wellandii is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
