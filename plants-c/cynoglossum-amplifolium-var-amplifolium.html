Cynoglossum amplifolium var. amplifolium

Cynoglossum amplifolium var. amplifolium, commonly known as broadleaf forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical name reflects its enlarged leaves, with "amplifolium" meaning "enlarged leaf.".
