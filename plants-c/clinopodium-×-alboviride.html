Clinopodium × alboviride

Clinopodium × alboviride, commonly known as the silver-green betony, is a hybrid plant species within the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-green foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically features square stems and opposite leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, arranged in whorled clusters.

Clinopodium × alboviride is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
