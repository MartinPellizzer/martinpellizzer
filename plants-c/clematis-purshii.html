Clematis purshii

Clematis purshii, commonly known as Pursh's clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the western United States.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its climbing habit requires support, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty and fragrance.

Clematis purshii is also known for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.
