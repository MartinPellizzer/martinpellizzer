Coccoloba samanensis

Coccoloba samanensis, commonly known as the Saman tree or glory tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into round, red or orange fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in urban and coastal areas.
