Cerastium juniperorum

Cerastium juniperorum, commonly known as the juniper cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. Cerastium juniperorum is often found in alpine or subalpine environments, where it contributes to soil stabilization and biodiversity.
