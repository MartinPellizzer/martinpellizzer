Combretum annulatum

Combretum annulatum, commonly known as the ringed combretum, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark that often has a distinct ringed pattern. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are arranged in clusters, typically blooming during the dry season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Combretum annulatum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
