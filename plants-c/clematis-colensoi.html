Clematis colensoi

Clematis colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's clematis, is a climbing vine native to New Zealand.

It features slender, wiry stems that can grow up to 10 meters in length, supported by hooked tendrils. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and lilac, typically blooming in spring. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves with three heart-shaped leaflets.

Clematis colensoi thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
