Cotoneaster rugosus var. henryanus

Cotoneaster rugosus var. henryanus, commonly known as the Henryan's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy, ovate leaves that turn reddish in autumn. The plant produces clusters of white, five-petaled flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster rugosus var. henryanus is also valued for its role in ecological landscapes and as a hedge plant.
