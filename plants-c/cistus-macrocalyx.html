Cistus macrocalyx

Cistus macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers have been used in traditional herbal remedies.
