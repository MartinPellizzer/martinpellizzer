Clematis viticella var. amethystina

Clematis viticella var. amethystina, commonly known as the Amethystina Clematis, is a compact, deciduous vine that produces clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers.

It is native to Europe and is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is typically grown as a climbing or trailing plant. It blooms in early summer, adding a striking display of color to gardens and landscapes.

This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental value and ease of care.
