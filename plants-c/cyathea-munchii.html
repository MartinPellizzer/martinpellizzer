Cyathea munchii

Cyathea munchii, commonly known as the Munch's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. Cyathea munchii typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests and mountainous areas.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
