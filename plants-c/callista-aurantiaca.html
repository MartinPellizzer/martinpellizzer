Callista aurantiaca

Callista aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a slightly waxy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Callista aurantiaca is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
