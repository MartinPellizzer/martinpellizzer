Carduus kahenae

Carduus kahenae, commonly known as the Kahena thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which helps reduce water loss.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in herbal remedies.
