Cyrtochilum flavostellulare

Cyrtochilum flavostellulare, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically features a short, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, star-shaped blooms. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
