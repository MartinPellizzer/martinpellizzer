Clematis acuminata var. multiflora

Clematis acuminata var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale blue, often in clusters along the stems. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its vigorous growth habit requires support structures, and it is often used in ornamental landscaping.
