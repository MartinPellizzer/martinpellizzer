Caltha palustris var. orbicularis

Caltha palustris var. orbicularis, commonly known as the broadleaf marsh marigold, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. The plant has broad, rounded leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in bogs, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical features make it an important indicator species for wetland ecosystems.
