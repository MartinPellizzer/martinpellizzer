Cochlearia sinuata

Cochlearia sinuata, commonly known as the sinuous horned poppy, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, typically growing in moist, calcareous soils. Cochlearia sinuata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
