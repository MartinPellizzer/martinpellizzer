Crinum bequaertii

Crinum bequaertii, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink, often with a yellow or green throat. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that form a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Crinum bequaertii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
