Coelogyne steffiensii

Coelogyne steffiensii, commonly known as the Steffiens' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers are typically yellow to orange with red or brown markings, and they bloom during the rainy season. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
