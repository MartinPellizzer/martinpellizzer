Callisia diuretica

Callisia diuretica, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It is well adapted to humid environments and thrives in partial shade with well-draining soil.

Due to its ability to tolerate drought and its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
