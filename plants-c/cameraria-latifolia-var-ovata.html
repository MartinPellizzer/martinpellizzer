Cameraria latifolia var. ovata

Cameraria latifolia var. ovata, commonly known as the broadleaf cammeraria, is a variant of the cammeraria species characterized by its broad, ovate leaves.

It is a small shrub native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. The plant's leaves are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture that helps it retain moisture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by clusters of small, spherical fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
