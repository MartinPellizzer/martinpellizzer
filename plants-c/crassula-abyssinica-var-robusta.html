Crassula abyssinica var. robusta

Crassula abyssinica var. robusta, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy foliage that forms a compact, rounded shape, often with a bluish-gray hue. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
