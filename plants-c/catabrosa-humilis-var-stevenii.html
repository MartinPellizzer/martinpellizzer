Catabrosa humilis var. stevenii

Catabrosa humilis var. stevenii, commonly known as the Steven's catabrosa, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
