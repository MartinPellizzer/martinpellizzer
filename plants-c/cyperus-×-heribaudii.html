Cyperus × heribaudii

Cyperus × heribaudii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a hybrid grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Cyperus × heribaudii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
