Croton geayi

Croton geayi, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually thick and waxy, with a variety of shapes and colors, ranging from green to red and yellow.

Croton geayi produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits that contain seeds.
