Cattleya Virens

Cattleya virens is a striking orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America. Known for its vibrant green and white flowers, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike with its beauty. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to light and moisture. Its unique coloration and strong fragrance make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Despite its popularity, Cattleya virens remains a sought-after plant due to its rarity and elegance.
