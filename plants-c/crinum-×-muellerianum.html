Crinum × muellerianum

Crinum × muellerianum, commonly known as the hybrid crinum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in various colors such as white, yellow, and pink. The plant produces a tall, leafless stem that bears clusters of fragrant blooms, often blooming in late summer or early autumn.

Its bulbs are underground and can be propagated through division or by seed, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
