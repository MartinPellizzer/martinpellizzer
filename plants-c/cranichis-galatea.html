Cranichis galatea

Cranichis galatea, commonly known as the Galatea orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a slight fragrance.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
