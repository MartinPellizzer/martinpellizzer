Cynanchum natalitium

Cynanchum natalitium, commonly known as Natal's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
