Crossandra cloiselii var. brevis

Crossandra cloiselii var. brevis, commonly known as the dwarf crossandra, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, often with contrasting yellow or green markings. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
