Coespeletia Marcanoana

Coespeletia marcanoana is a rare and unique plant native to the high-altitude regions of Colombia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its striking floral structures and ecological significance. This species thrives in harsh environmental conditions, showcasing remarkable adaptations to its mountainous habitat. Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss, it is considered a conservation concern. Studying Coespeletia marcanoana offers valuable insights into biodiversity and the challenges of preserving endemic flora.
