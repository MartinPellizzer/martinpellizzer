Chlorophytum latifilamentatum

Chlorophytum latifilamentatum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically striped with green and white, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by clusters of bright green berries. It is well-known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and its ease of care, making it a popular houseplant.

Its leaves can also be used to propagate new plants, further contributing to its popularity among gardeners.
