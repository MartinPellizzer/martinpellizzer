Caladium hastifolium

Caladium hastifolium, commonly known as the arrowleaf caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that come in a variety of colors, including shades of green, red, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-draining soil. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Caladium hastifolium is typically propagated through tuberous roots and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.
