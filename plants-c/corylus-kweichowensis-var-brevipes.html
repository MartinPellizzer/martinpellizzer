Corylus kweichowensis var. brevipes

Corylus kweichowensis var. brevipes, commonly known as the Yunnan hazelnut, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Corylaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and unique nut-like fruits. The plant produces edible nuts enclosed in a persistent, woody husk. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and potential economic importance in local agriculture.
