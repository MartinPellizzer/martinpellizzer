Crupina crupinastrum proles visianii

Crupina crupinastrum proles visianii, commonly known as the Visianii crupina, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
