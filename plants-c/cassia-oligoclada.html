Cassia oligoclada

Cassia oligoclada, commonly known as yellow flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets, and it produces elongated pods containing seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia oligoclada is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
