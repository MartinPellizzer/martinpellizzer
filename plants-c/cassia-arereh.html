Cassia arereh

Cassia arereh, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, flame-like flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and its bark is smooth with a reddish-brown color. Cassia arereh is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm, humid climates.
