Cremanthodium virgaurea

Cremanthodium virgaurea, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Cremanthodium virgaurea is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its bright, daisy-like appearance.
