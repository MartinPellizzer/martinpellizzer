Carex siderosticta var. ciliatomarginata

Carex siderosticta var. ciliatomarginata, commonly known as the silver-haired sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants commonly found in moist or wetland environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive silvery sheen along the margins, giving it its common name. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, cylindrical spike that bears small, inconspicuous flowers.
