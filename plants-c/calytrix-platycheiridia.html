Calytrix platycheiridia

Calytrix platycheiridia, commonly known as the broad-leaved calytrix, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.
