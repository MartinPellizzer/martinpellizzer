Catasetum complanatum

Catasetum complanatum, commonly known as the flat-leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its flat, strap-like leaves and produces striking, pendulous flowers with a unique labellum. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are pollinated by specific bees, which have a specialized relationship with the orchid.

Catasetum complanatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
