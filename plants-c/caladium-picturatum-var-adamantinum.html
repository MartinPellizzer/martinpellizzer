Caladium picturatum var. adamantinum

Caladium picturatum var. adamantinum, commonly known as the "Adamantinum Caladium," is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow up to 12 inches in diameter. This variety thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant and unique leaf patterns.
