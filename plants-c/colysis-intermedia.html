Colysis intermedia

Colysis intermedia, commonly known as the common colysis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
