Chascolytrum Rotundatum

Chascolytrum rotundatum is a rare and intriguing plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is known for its unique, spherical bulbs that store water and nutrients. Despite its small size, this plant plays an important role in its ecosystem by supporting local insect and fungal life. Its striking purple and green foliage makes it a standout among alpine flora. Due to habitat loss, Chascolytrum rotundatum is now considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
