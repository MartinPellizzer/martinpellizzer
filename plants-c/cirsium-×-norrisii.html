Cirsium × norrisii

Cirsium × norrisii, commonly known as the Norris thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads that bloom in late summer. Native to the western United States, this perennial plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and full sun. Its ornamental value is often appreciated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.

The plant's structure and appearance make it a notable species within the Cirsium genus.
