Camellia brevicolumna

Camellia brevicolumna, commonly known as the dwarf camellia, is a species native to China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and produces showy, camellia-like flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Camellia brevicolumna is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for temperate climates.
