Cattleya × felix

Cattleya × felix, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a pink or purple lip with a contrasting white or yellow center. The plant produces a single, upright stem that bears several blooms, each with a velvety texture and a sweet, spicy fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Cattleya × felix is a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and botanical gardens due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
