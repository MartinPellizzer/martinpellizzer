Centaurea rhutenica

Centaurea rhutenica, commonly known as the Romanian knapweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a taproot system and can reach heights of up to 60 cm.

Centaurea rhutenica is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
