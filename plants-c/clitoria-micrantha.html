Clitoria micrantha

Clitoria micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered butterfly pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, bright green leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. The plant is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of clusters of tiny, showy flowers arranged in racemes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Clitoria micrantha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
