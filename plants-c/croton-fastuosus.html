Croton fastuosus

Croton fastuosus, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vivid display. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental tree.

Croton fastuosus is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its role in supporting local wildlife.
