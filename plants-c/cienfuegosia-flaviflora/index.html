Cienfuegosia Flaviflora

Cienfuegosia flaviflora is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Central America. Known for its vibrant yellow petals and unique foliage, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane regions with rich soil. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
