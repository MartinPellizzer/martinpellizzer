Clematis philippiana

Clematis philippiana, commonly known as the Philippine clematis, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a deciduous vine that can grow up to 10 meters in length, featuring slender, woody stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate, with a slightly hairy surface.

Clematis philippiana thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
