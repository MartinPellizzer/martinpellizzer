Crassula lycopodioides var. variegata

Crassula lycopodioides var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
