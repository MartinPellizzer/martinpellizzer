Coleus Triangularis

Coleus triangularis is a unique and visually striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, colorful leaves, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance. The leaves of this species are typically triangular in shape, which gives it its scientific name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. This plant is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening enthusiasts.
