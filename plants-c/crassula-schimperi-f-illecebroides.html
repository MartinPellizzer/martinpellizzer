Crassula schimperi f. illecebroides

Crassula schimperi f. illecebroides, commonly known as the silver crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
