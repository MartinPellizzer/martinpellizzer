Chrysanthemum foliosum

Chrysanthemum foliosum, commonly known as the leaf chrysanthemum, is a perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Chrysanthemum foliosum is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
