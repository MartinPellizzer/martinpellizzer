Convallaria globosa

Convallaria globosa, commonly known as the globular lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded, globe-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features grass-like leaves arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. Its flowers are typically pink to white in color and are borne on slender stems.

Convallaria globosa is often found in moist, shady woodlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
