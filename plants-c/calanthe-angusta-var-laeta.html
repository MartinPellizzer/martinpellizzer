Calanthe angusta var. laeta

Calanthe angusta var. laeta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
