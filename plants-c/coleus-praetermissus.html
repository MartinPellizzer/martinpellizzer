Coleus praetermissus

Coleus praetermissus, commonly known as the silver leaf coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver and green variegated foliage, which adds visual interest to gardens and landscapes. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
