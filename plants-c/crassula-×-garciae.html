Crassula × garciae

Crassula × garciae, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Christmas Jade, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and bonsai cultivation.
