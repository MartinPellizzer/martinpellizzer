Cattleya mossiae var. reineckeana

Cattleya mossiae var. reineckeana, commonly known as the Reinecke's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It originates from Brazil and is characterized by its large, showy blooms that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
