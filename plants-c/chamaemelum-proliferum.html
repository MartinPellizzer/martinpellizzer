Chamaemelum proliferum

Chamaemelum proliferum, commonly known as the yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar species. Its small, tough leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in dry, sunny environments.
