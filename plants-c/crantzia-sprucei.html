Crantzia sprucei

Crantzia sprucei, commonly known as Spruce's crantzia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in areas of Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Crantzia sprucei is often found in rocky soils and is a hardy species that thrives in cold, high-altitude climates.
