Chamaecrista duartei

Chamaecrista duartei, commonly known as Duarte's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring compound leaves and yellow or orange flowers. Its fruit is a pod that contains several seeds, and it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
