Cymbidium wenshanense

Cymbidium wenshanense, commonly known as the Wenshan orchid, is a species native to China, particularly the Yunnan province.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, slender pseudobulbs and long, arching leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cymbidium wenshanense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor settings.
