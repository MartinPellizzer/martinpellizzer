Calamintha × jahniana

Calamintha × jahniana, commonly known as the hybrid germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a strong mint-like fragrance.

Calamintha × jahniana is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming and digestive properties.
