Costularia melleri

Costularia melleri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Costularia melleri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
