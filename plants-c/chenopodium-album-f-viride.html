Chenopodium album f. viride

Chenopodium album f. viride, commonly known as green lambsquarters, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of greenish-white pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish flowers arranged in dense, branched clusters. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

This species is known for its rapid growth and ability to tolerate a wide range of environmental conditions.
