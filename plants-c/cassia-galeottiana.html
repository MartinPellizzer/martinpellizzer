Cassia galeottiana

Cassia galeottiana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves composed of multiple oval-shaped leaflets. It produces small, elongated pods that contain flat, winged seeds.

Cassia galeottiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
