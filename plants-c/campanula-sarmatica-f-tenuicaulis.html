Campanula sarmatica f. tenuicaulis

Campanula sarmatica f. tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed bellflower, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Carpathian region.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lanceolate, with a rough texture and a bluish-green hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
