Croton nummularius

Croton nummularius, commonly known as the silver croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a striking silver or pale green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Croton nummularius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
