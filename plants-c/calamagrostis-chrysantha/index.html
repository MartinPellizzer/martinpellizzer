Calamagrostis Chrysantha

Chrysanthus grass, scientifically known as Calamagrostis chrysantha, is a striking ornamental grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. Its feathery plumes and vibrant golden hue make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of climates. With its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements, it has gained widespread appreciation in horticulture. Whether used as a border plant or in large landscape beds, Chrysanthus grass adds a touch of sophistication to any garden.
