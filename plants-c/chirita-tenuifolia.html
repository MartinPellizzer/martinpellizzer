Chirita tenuifolia

Chirita tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically light green with a subtle metallic sheen. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, often arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is widely cultivated in horticulture.
