Columnea tulae var. flava

Columnea tulae var. flava, commonly known as the yellow-flowered columnea, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems, often resembling small, delicate bells. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ability to add color to shaded garden spaces.
