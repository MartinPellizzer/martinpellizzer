Cyclobalanopsis pinbianensis

Cyclobalanopsis pinbianensis, commonly known as the Pinbian ironwood, is a species of evergreen tree belonging to the Fagaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Guangxi provinces. The plant is characterized by its dense, hard wood and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. It produces small, cupule-enclosed nuts that are typically dispersed by animals.
