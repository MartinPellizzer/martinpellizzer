Combretum chrysophyllum

Combretum chrysophyllum, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The bark is smooth and greyish, often exuding a milky sap when damaged.
