Cucubalus kashmiriensis

Cucubalus kashmiriensis, commonly known as Kashmir date palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent, particularly found in the Kashmir region. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. It produces edible dates that are highly valued for their sweetness and nutritional content.

Due to its ecological and economic importance, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
