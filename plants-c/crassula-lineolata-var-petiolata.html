Crassula lineolata var. petiolata

Crassula lineolata var. petiolata, commonly known as the Jade Necklace, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically light green with a powdery white coating. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique leaf arrangement and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
