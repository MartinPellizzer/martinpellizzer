Cineraria densiflora

Cineraria densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
