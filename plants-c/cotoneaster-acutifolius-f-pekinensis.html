Cotoneaster acutifolius f. pekinensis

Cotoneaster acutifolius f. pekinensis, commonly known as the Peking cotoneaster, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in spring, followed by red berries that persist into winter. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and seasonal interest.
