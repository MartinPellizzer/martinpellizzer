Cymbidium omeiense

Cymbidium omeiense, commonly known as the Sichuan orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking orchid-like flowers with a distinctive coloration, typically in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in cool, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
