Convolvulus commersonii

Convolvulus commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Convolvulus commersonii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, which can improve soil fertility.
