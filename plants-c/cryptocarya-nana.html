Cryptocarya nana

Cryptocarya nana, commonly known as the dwarf cryptocarya, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant features simple, elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight sheen. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by spherical, black berries.

Cryptocarya nana thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in native forests.
