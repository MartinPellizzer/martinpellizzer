Calamagrostis hakonensis var. exaristata

Calamagrostis hakonensis var. exaristata, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Japan.

It features slender, silver-blue foliage that turns coppery in the autumn, creating a striking visual effect. The plant produces fluffy, feathery panicles that add a soft, airy texture to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
