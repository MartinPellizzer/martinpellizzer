Crassula aliciae

Crassula aliciae, commonly known as the Alice Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a subtle bluish or reddish tinge. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
