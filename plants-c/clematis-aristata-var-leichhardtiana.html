Clematis aristata var. leichhardtiana

Clematis aristata var. leichhardtiana, commonly known as the Leichhardt's Clematis, is a climbing vine native to Australia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant has slender, wiry stems and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its attractive blooms and vigorous growth.
