Cistus × lucasii

Cistus × lucasii, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes various species of evergreen shrubs native to the Mediterranean region. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and hairy, gray-green foliage. Its flowers are typically pink to purple in color, appearing in late spring to early summer.

It is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant plant.
