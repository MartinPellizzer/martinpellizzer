Ceodes brownii

Ceodes brownii, commonly known as the red-flowered ceodes, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright red flowers. It produces small, round fruits that are edible and contain a single seed.

Ceodes brownii is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salt spray.
