Citta nigricans

Citta nigricans, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are typically oval in shape and contain toxic seeds. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
