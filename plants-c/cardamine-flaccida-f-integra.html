Cardamine flaccida f. integra

Cardamine flaccida f. integra, commonly known as the smooth water-cress, is a variant of the water-cress species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with trifoliate leaves, each leaflet being ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils near streams and springs. Its flowers are small, white, and arranged in racemes, and it produces elongated, slender fruits.

This form is distinguished by its smooth, less hairy leaves compared to the typical form of Cardamine flaccida.
