Claoxylon patulum

Claoxylon patulum, commonly known as the Philippine ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Due to its slow growth and limited distribution, Claoxylon patulum is considered a rare and endangered species.
