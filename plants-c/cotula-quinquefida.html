Cotula quinquefida

Cotula quinquefida, commonly known as the five-leafed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, blooming from spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually lobed and arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Cotula quinquefida is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
