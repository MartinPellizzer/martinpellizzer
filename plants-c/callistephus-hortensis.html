Callistephus hortensis

Callistephus hortensis, commonly known as the garden cosmos, is an annual flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Asia and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with slender stems and feathery leaves. Its flowers bloom from late summer into autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow from seed.
