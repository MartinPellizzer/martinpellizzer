Coelogyne palawensis

Coelogyne palawensis, commonly known as the Palawan orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, producing clusters of long, narrow petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne palawensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
