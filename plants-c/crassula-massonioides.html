Crassula massonioides

Crassula massonioides, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are pale green to bluish-gray, giving it a ghostly appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Crassula massonioides is prized for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
