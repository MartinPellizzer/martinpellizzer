Colchicum jesdianum

Colchicum jesdianum, commonly known as the Jesdian colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Colchicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant features basal leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, with a short, erect stem that bears a single, showy violet or purple flower. It produces a capsule fruit that contains numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is adapted to cold, dry environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
