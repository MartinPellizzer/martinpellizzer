Cattleya acuensis

Cattleya acuensis, commonly known as the Acuense Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that can range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in the humid environment.

Cattleya acuensis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique bloom structure.
