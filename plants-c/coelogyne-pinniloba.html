Coelogyne pinniloba

Coelogyne pinniloba, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks in mountainous regions. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Coelogyne pinniloba is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
