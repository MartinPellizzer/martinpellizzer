Cattleya dowiana var. chrysotoxa

Cattleya dowiana var. chrysotoxa, commonly known as the golden-tipped cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, pendulous blooms with a distinctive golden-yellow lip that stands out against its white or pale pink petals. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its health and promote blooming.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
