Cotyledon similis

Cotyledon similis, commonly known as the African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinctive markings. The plant produces showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often in clusters. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Cotyledon similis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
