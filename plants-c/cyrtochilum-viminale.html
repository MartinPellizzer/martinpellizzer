Cyrtochilum viminale

Cyrtochilum viminale, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Cyrtochilum viminale is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
