Ceropegia lushii

Ceropegia lushii, commonly known as the Lushii tube-root, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, tuberous roots. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
