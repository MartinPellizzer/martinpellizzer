Cotula dichotoma

Cotula dichotoma, commonly known as the silver cotula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with a sprawling habit and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features alternate, narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Cotula dichotoma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
