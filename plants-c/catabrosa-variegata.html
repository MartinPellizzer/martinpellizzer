Catabrosa variegata

Catabrosa variegata, commonly known as the variegated catabrosa, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Catabrosa variegata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
