Crassula divaricata

Crassula divaricata, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
