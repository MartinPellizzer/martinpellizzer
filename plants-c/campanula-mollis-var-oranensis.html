Campanula mollis var. oranensis

Campanula mollis var. oranensis, commonly known as the Oranensis bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, often with a slight violet hue. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are softly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period add ornamental value to gardens throughout the summer.
