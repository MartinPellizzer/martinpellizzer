Cynanchum hubeiense

Cynanchum hubeiense, commonly known as the Hubei milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
