Carex × elytroides

Carex × elytroides, commonly known as the slender flat sedge, is a hybrid plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. This species is typically found in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and stream banks. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

The plant is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and filter water.
