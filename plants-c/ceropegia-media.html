Ceropegia media

Ceropegia media, commonly known as the string of hearts plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow, which are adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ability to propagate through stem cuttings makes it a popular choice among gardeners.
