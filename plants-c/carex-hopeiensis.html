Carex hopeiensis

Carex hopeiensis, commonly known as the Hope's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green to brown in color, and it blooms during the summer months. Carex hopeiensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms in its native environment.
