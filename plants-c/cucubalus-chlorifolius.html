Cucubalus chlorifolius

Cucubalus chlorifolius, commonly known as the green-leaved cucumber, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and melons. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are typically elongated and have a mild, cucumber-like flavor.

It is often cultivated for its edible fruit and ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.
