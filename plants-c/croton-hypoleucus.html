Croton hypoleucus

Croton hypoleucus, commonly known as the white croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red and orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Croton hypoleucus thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
