Corybas orbiculatus

Corybas orbiculatus, commonly known as the orbicular helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped floral structure, which is typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces a single, showy flower that blooms in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

The orchid's unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
