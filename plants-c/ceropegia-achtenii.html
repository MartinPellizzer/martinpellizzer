Ceropegia achtenii

Ceropegia achtenii, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features cascading stems with tubular, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white, waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for hanging baskets and indoor gardens.
