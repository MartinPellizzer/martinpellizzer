Callisia orientalis

Callisia orientalis, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Callisia orientalis is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
