Cubelium concolor

Cubelium concolor, commonly known as the smooth cube orchid, is a rare and unusual orchid species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive cube-shaped pseudobulbs, which are covered in a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, often arranged in compact clusters. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade and well-drained soil.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
