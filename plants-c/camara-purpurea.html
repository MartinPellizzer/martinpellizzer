Camara purpurea

Camara purpurea, commonly known as the purple nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns from green to red as it matures.
