Cyclostemon dinklagei

Cyclostemon dinklagei, commonly known as the Dinklage's cycad, is a unique species of cycad native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palm-like leaves that are spirally arranged and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain the seeds, typical of cycads. Cyclostemon dinklagei thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptation to harsh desert conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
