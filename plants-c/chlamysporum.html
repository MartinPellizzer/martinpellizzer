Chlamysporum

Chlamysporum, commonly known as the "false broomrape," is a parasitic plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It lacks chlorophyll and cannot perform photosynthesis, relying entirely on its host for nutrients. Due to its lack of visible above-ground structures, it is often overlooked in ecological studies.
