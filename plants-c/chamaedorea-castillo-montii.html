Chamaedorea castillo-montii

Chamaedorea castillo-montii, commonly known as the Castillo-Montii bamboo palm, is a member of the Arecaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a small, evergreen palm that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 3 meters. The plant features slender, clustered stems with long, narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It has a dense, bushy appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its foliage is glossy and contributes to its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
