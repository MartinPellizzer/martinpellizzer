Chlorophytum sparsiflorum var. bipindense

Chlorophytum sparsiflorum var. bipindense, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves with white and green stripes, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
