Cryptocoryne spiralis var. cognatoides

Cryptocoryne spiralis var. cognatoides, commonly known as the spiral crypts, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires stable water parameters to maintain its vibrant coloration. Its leaves are typically dark green with a bluish tint, and they can grow up to 15 centimeters in length.

This variety is popular among aquarium enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
