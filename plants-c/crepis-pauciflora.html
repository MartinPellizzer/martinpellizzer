Crepis pauciflora

Crepis pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered cinquefoil, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.
