Coussarea platyphylla var. vestita

Coussarea platyphylla var. vestita, commonly known as the velvet coussarea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers, which are often followed by red berries. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture, though it is relatively rare in cultivation.
