Coelogyne tenuitepala

Coelogyne tenuitepala, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect pseudobulbs and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers that are typically yellow or green with red or brown markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne tenuitepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
