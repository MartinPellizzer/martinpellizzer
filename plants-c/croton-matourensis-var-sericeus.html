Croton matourensis var. sericeus

Croton matourensis var. sericeus, commonly known as the silky croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
