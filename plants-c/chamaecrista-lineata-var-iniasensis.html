Chamaecrista lineata var. iniasensis

Chamaecrista lineata var. iniasensis, commonly known as the Iniasena shrub, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in open areas with well-drained soil.
