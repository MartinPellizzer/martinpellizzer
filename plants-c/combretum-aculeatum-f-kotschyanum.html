Combretum aculeatum f. kotschyanum

Combretum aculeatum f. kotschyanum, commonly known as the Kotschyan's flame tree, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red floral bracts that appear in clusters, giving it a dramatic visual display. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
