Centaurea pseudosolstitialis

Centaurea pseudosolstitialis, commonly known as the false mountain bluemink, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and is typically found in rocky or open habitats. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and typically blue or purple, arranged in dense clusters.

It is adapted to grow in dry, sunny environments and is often seen in alpine or subalpine regions.
