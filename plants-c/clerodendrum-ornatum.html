Clerodendrum ornatum

Clerodendrum ornatum, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to India and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its bark is smooth and grayish, while the stems are often woody and branched.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
