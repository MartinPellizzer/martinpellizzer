Cyperus glaber

Cyperus glaber, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Cyperus glaber is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and filter water.
