Cattleya labiata var. purpureolineata

Cattleya labiata var. purpureolineata, commonly known as the Purple-veined Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking floral display.

This variety features large, showy flowers with vibrant pink petals and deep purple veins that create a dramatic contrast. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. The plant has thick, fleshy roots that store moisture, making it relatively drought-tolerant.

Its unique coloration and fragrance make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
