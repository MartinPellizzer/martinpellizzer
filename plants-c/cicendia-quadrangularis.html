Cicendia quadrangularis

Cicendia quadrangularis, commonly known as the four-angle morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive quadrangular stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant purple or blue flowers that bloom in the early morning. It is often found in moist, shady areas and is part of the Convolvulaceae family.

Its unique morphology and flowering habits make it a notable species in botanical studies.
