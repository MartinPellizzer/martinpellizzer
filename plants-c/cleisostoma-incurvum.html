Cleisostoma incurvum

Cleisostoma incurvum, commonly known as the curved lip orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, adapting to its humid, shady habitat.

Cleisostoma incurvum thrives in warm, moist environments and is often found in lowland forests.
