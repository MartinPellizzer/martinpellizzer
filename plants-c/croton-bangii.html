Croton bangii

Croton bangii, commonly known as the Bangi croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its striking foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Croton bangii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.
