Codiaeum variegatum f. ambiguum

Codiaeum variegatum f. ambiguum, commonly known as the Variegated Croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in bright, indirect light. It has a compact, bushy growth habit and can be cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with proper care.
