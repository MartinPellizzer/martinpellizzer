Croton obtusus

Croton obtusus, commonly known as the blunt-leaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Croton obtusus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
