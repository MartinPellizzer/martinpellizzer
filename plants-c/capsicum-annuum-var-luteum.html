Capsicum annuum var. luteum

Capsicum annuum var. luteum, commonly known as the yellow bell pepper, is a variety of the common chili pepper species.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow fruits, which are typically rounded and slightly bell-shaped. The plant produces small, white flowers that develop into the edible fruits after pollination. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun exposure for optimal growth.

This variety is often used in cooking for its mild flavor and nutritional benefits.
