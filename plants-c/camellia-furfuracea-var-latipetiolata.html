Camellia furfuracea var. latipetiolata

Camellia furfuracea var. latipetiolata, commonly known as the broad petiole camellia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves with broad petioles, which give it its botanical name. The plant produces showy, camellia-like flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
