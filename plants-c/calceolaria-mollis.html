Calceolaria mollis

Calceolaria mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf slipper orchid, is a member of the Calceolaria genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Calceolaria mollis is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
