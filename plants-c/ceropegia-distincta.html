Ceropegia distincta

Ceropegia distincta, commonly known as the dwarf chainplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky surfaces or in sandy soils. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water.

Ceropegia distincta is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
