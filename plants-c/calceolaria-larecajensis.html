Calceolaria larecajensis

Calceolaria larecajensis, commonly known as the Peruvian slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Peru.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs.

Calceolaria larecajensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
