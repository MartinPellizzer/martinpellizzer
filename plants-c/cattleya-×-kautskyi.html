Cattleya × kautskyi

Cattleya × kautskyi, commonly known as the Kautsky Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, showy flowers.

It originates from a cross between Cattleya mossiae and Cattleya bicolor, resulting in a plant with vibrant, often bicolor blooms. The orchid features thick, fleshy roots adapted for absorbing moisture from its native tropical environments. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
