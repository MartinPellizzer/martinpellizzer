Chaetogastra rosmarinifolia

Chaetogastra rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features finely divided, silver-gray leaves that are aromatic and often used in culinary applications. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in umbels and bloom in the summer months.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in herbal medicine.
