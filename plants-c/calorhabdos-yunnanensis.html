Calorhabdos yunnanensis

Calorhabdos yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan calico orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that resemble a calico cat, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates, often found in mountainous areas of Yunnan Province. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are adapted to thrive in shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is considered a valuable and endangered species in its natural habitat.
