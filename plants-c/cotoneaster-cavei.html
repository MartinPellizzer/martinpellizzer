Cotoneaster cavei

Cotoneaster cavei, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of white to pink flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. These berries are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

Cotoneaster cavei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
