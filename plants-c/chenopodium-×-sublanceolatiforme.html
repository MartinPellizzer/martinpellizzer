Chenopodium × sublanceolatiforme

Chenopodium × sublanceolatiforme, commonly known as the hybrid goosefoot, is a member of the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its succulent, lobed leaves that range in color from green to reddish. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically in the summer months. It is native to regions of the United States, particularly in the western states, and thrives in disturbed soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often found in fields, roadsides, and other open habitats.
