Coccinia obbiadensis

Coccinia obbiadensis, commonly known as the African eggplant or red eggplant, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and melons, and is characterized by its vining growth habit. Coccinia obbiadensis is often cultivated for its edible fruits, which have a slightly tart flavor and are used in local cuisines.
