Calonema nobile

Calonema nobile, commonly known as the noble calonema, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. This evergreen shrub features attractive, ovate leaves with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months.

Calonema nobile is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
