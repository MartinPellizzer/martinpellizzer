Cassia rugosula

Cassia rugosula, commonly known as rough cassia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces elongated, woody pods containing seeds covered in a sticky resin. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Cassia rugosula is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in some Indigenous communities.
