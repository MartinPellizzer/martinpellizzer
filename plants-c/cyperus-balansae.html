Cyperus balansae

Cyperus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies.

Cyperus balansae is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
