Cryptosepalum arboreum

Cryptosepalum arboreum, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Cryptosepalum arboreum thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
