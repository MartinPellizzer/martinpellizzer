Capnorchis ochroleuca

Capnorchis ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Central and South America. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a yellowish hue, often adorned with intricate patterns. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Capnorchis ochroleuca thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
