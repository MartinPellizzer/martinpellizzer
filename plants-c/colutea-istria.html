Colutea istria

Colutea istria, commonly known as the Istrian cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Balkan Peninsula, particularly found in Croatia, Slovenia, and Italy. The plant features palmate leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a pod that splits open to release seeds, and it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.

Colutea istria is often found in rocky or limestone-rich habitats, contributing to the biodiversity of its native regions.
