Critoniopsis gynoxiifolia

Critoniopsis gynoxiifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
