Chamaecytisus × millenii

Chamaecytisus × millenii, commonly known as the Millennial broom, is a hybrid shrub native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal or rocky garden environments.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
