Crudia katikii

Crudia katikii, commonly known as the Natal barbwire bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Crudia katikii is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
