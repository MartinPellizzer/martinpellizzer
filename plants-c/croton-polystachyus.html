Croton polystachyus

Croton polystachyus, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which are clustered inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton polystachyus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.
