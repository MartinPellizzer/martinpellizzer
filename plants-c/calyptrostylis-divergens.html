Calyptrostylis divergens

Calyptrostylis divergens, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the subfamily Orchidoideae and is characterized by its distinctive, broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique structure that includes a spurred labellum. Calyptrostylis divergens typically grows in wet tropical environments, often near water sources.

Its botanical features make it a notable subject in both horticultural and ecological studies.
