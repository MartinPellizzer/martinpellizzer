Comparettia falcata var. falcata

Comparettia falcata var. falcata, commonly known as the falcate comparettia, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and striking, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Its botanical features make it a visually appealing addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
