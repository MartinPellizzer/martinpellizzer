Crotalaria podocarpa subsp. flexuosa

Crotalaria podocarpa subsp.

flexuosa, commonly known as the slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.

The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters. Its pods are slender and curved, containing several seeds, and the species is often found in dry, open habitats.

It is also noted for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a forage plant.
