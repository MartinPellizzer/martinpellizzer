Campanula linifolia var. ovalifolia

Campanula linifolia var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are oval-shaped and slightly hairy, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
