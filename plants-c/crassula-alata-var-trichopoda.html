Crassula alata var. trichopoda

Crassula alata var. trichopoda, commonly known as the Snowflake Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a powdery white coating, giving it a frost-like appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
