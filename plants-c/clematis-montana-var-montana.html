Clematis montana var. montana

Clematis montana var. montana, commonly known as the mountain clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to Europe.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and blue, depending on the pH of the soil. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. It thrives in partial to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or walls.
