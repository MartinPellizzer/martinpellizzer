Cirsium yuzawae

Cirsium yuzawae, commonly known as Yuzawa thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as mountain forests and stream banks. It has a compact growth habit with a rosette of basal leaves and a single stem bearing the flower heads.

Cirsium yuzawae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
