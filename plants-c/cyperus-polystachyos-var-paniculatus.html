Cyperus polystachyos var. paniculatus

Cyperus polystachyos var. paniculatus, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are often purple or reddish in color. The plant is typically found in wetland habitats, including marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. It has a fibrous root system and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
