Capnoides chaerophylla

Capnoides chaerophylla, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
