Campanula napuligera f. simplex

Campanula napuligera f. simplex, commonly known as the simple bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in gardens.
