Cola dorrii

Cola dorrii, commonly known as the African cola tree, is a member of the Sterculiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that develop into oval-shaped fruits. The fruit contains hard, brown seeds that are rich in caffeine and other alkaloids.

It is often cultivated for its seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a stimulant.
