Cistus Helianthemum Var. Nummularius

Cistus helianthemum var. nummularius is a striking evergreen shrub native to the rocky landscapes of southern Europe. Its silver-green leaves and clusters of fragrant yellow flowers make it a standout addition to gardens and natural habitats. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens and coastal areas. Known for its hardiness and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among horticulturists and landscape designers. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits contribute to its growing popularity in both ornamental and environmental contexts.
