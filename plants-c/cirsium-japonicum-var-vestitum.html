Cirsium japonicum var. vestitum

Cirsium japonicum var. vestitum, commonly known as the Japanese thistle or velvet thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features tall, spiny stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces purple to pinkish flowers in late summer, which are clustered in spherical heads. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a serrated edge and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
