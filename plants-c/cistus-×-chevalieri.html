Cistus × chevalieri

Cistus × chevalieri, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes various species of evergreen shrubs characterized by their aromatic leaves and showy flowers. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
