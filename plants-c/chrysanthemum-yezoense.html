Chrysanthemum yezoense

Chrysanthemum yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Chrysanthemum yezoense is also used in traditional Japanese horticulture and has cultural significance in some regions.
