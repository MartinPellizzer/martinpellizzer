Crinum menyharthii

Crinum menyharthii, commonly known as the Natal crinum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, often unpleasant fragrance. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, growing from a central bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Crinum menyharthii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
