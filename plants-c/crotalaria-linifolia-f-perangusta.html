Crotalaria linifolia f. perangusta

Crotalaria linifolia f. perangusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features trifoliate leaves with narrow, lance-shaped leaflets and produces vibrant yellow flowers. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can be toxic to livestock, necessitating caution in agricultural settings.
