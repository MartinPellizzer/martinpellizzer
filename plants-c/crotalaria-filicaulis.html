Crotalaria filicaulis

Crotalaria filicaulis, commonly known as the slender crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes, often accompanied by trifoliate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators make it a notable species in botanical gardens.
