Crassula globularioides

Crassula globularioides, commonly known as the Jelly Bean Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded leaves that are fleshy and often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
