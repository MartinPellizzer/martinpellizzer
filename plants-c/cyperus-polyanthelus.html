Cyperus polyanthelus

Cyperus polyanthelus, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant features slender, triangular stems and produces small, clustered inflorescences with purple or pink flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense clusters.

Cyperus polyanthelus is typically found in wetlands, marshes, and along water edges, where it thrives in moist soil conditions.
