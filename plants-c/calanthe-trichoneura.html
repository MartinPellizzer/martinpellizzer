Calanthe trichoneura

Calanthe trichoneura, commonly known as the hairy calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Calanthe trichoneura is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in its native habitats.
