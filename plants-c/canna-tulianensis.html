Canna tulianensis

Canna tulianensis, commonly known as the red banana, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, banana-like leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces showy, fiery red flowers that bloom in summer, adding dramatic visual interest to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Canna tulianensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown as a perennial in suitable climates.
