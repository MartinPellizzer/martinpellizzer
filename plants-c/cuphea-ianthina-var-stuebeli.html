Cuphea ianthina var. stuebeli

Cuphea ianthina var. stuebeli, commonly known as the silver-leaf cuphea, is a compact shrub native to Central America.

It features striking silver-white foliage with a velvety texture, which contrasts beautifully with its showy, tubular red flowers. The plant produces vibrant, two-lipped flowers that attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and easy care requirements in garden settings.
