Croton nitens

Croton nitens, commonly known as the silky croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red capsules containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
