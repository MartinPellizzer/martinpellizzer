Cyclopia subternata

Cyclopia subternata, commonly known as the small-leaved cyclopia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional African medicine.

Cyclopia subternata is also valued for its role in local ecosystems and as a source of nectar for pollinators.
