Cloezia × glaberrima

Cloezia × glaberrima, commonly known as the African daisy, is a hybrid perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has linear to lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Cloezia × glaberrima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
