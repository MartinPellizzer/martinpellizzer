Chrysopsis echioides

Chrysopsis echioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
