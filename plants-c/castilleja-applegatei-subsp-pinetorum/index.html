Castilleja Applegatei Subsp. Pinetorum

Castilleja applegatei subsp. pinetorum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western united states. it belongs to the orobanchaceae family and is known for its striking red and yellow blooms. this subspecies is often found in coniferous forests and is closely associated with pine trees. due to its limited habitat and threats from habitat loss, it is considered endangered. conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
