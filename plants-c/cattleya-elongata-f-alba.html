Cattleya elongata f. alba

Cattleya elongata f. alba, commonly known as the White Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its striking white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features long, slender stems and large, showy blooms that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter. Its flowers are typically fragrant and bloom during the late spring to early summer season.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and ornamental value.
