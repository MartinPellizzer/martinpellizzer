Coelogyne tenuibulba

Coelogyne tenuibulba, commonly known as the slender-bulb orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, yellowish-green to white flowers. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often growing in a rosette pattern.

Coelogyne tenuibulba is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
