Cissus reedii

Cissus reedii, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-blue hue and a textured surface. The plant produces small, pale green flowers followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cissus reedii is often used in hanging baskets or as a decorative climbing plant due to its ornamental foliage.
