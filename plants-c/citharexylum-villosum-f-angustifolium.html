Citharexylum villosum f. angustifolium

Citharexylum villosum f. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved citharexylum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. The leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its visual interest and fragrance.
