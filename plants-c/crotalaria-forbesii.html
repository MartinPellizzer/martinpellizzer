Crotalaria forbesii

Crotalaria forbesii, commonly known as Forbes' crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. Its seeds are enclosed in pods, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in certain agricultural contexts.

However, some species within the genus are toxic, so caution is advised when handling or consuming any part of the plant.
