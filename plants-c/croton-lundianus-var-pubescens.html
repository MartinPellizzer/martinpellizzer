Croton lundianus var. pubescens

Croton lundianus var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a shrub native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
