Calathea verecunda

Calathea verecunda, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns of green, silver, and red. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture that enhances its visual appeal.

Calathea verecunda thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light to maintain its vibrant foliage.
