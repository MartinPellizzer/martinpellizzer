Cryptolepis hypoglauca

Cryptolepis hypoglauca, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces elongated, greenish fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of forests and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
