Calophyllum gracillimum

Calophyllum gracillimum, commonly known as the slender flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and showy, bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and contain a single seed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.
