Cucumis ritchiei

Cucumis ritchiei, commonly known as the Mexican bitter gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its long, slender, spiny fruits that are typically yellow or green in color. The plant is a vigorous climber with palmately lobed leaves and yellow flowers. Its fruits are used in traditional Mexican cuisine, often cooked and seasoned.
