Conophytum semivestitum

Conophytum semivestitum, commonly known as the "Cape Flax" or "Cape Fossils," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to rocky soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
