Cannabis Kafiristanica

Cannabis kafiristanica is a unique species of the cannabis plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. It is known for its distinct physical characteristics and traditional uses among local communities. This plant has been cultivated for centuries and holds cultural significance in certain regions. Unlike its more well-known relatives, it is less widely studied and often overlooked in modern discussions. Its potential medicinal and industrial applications remain largely unexplored.
