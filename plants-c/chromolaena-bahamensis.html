Chromolaena bahamensis

Chromolaena bahamensis, commonly known as Bahamian chromolaena, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

Chromolaena bahamensis is often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
