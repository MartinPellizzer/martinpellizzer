Citharexylum poeppigii var. anomalum

Citharexylum poeppigii var. anomalum, commonly known as the variable citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.
