Centaurea × siegescui

Centaurea × siegescui, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Centaurea cyanus (blue cornflower) and Centaurea jacea (dog’s mercury). The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring bright blue to purple flowers with ray florets and a central disk. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
