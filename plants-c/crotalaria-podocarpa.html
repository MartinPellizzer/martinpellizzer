Crotalaria podocarpa

Crotalaria podocarpa, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein.

Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
