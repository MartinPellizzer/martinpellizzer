Cynorkis tenuicalcar subsp. andasilbeensis

Cynorkis tenuicalcar subsp.

andasilbeensis, commonly known as the Andasilbeensis Cynorkis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to specific regions in Africa, particularly in countries like Tanzania and Malawi.

The plant is characterized by its slender, terete pseudobulbs and elongated, narrow leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from greenish-white to pale yellow.

This subspecies is noted for its unique morphological features and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
