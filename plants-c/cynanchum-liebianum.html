Cynanchum liebianum

Cynanchum liebianum, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a follicle that contains numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Cynanchum liebianum is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
