Cranichis pseudomuscosa

Cranichis pseudomuscosa, commonly known as the false muscosa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and a single, slender stem that produces a single flower. The flower is typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

This species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often appreciated in horticultural collections.
