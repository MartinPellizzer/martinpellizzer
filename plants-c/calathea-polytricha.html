Calathea polytricha

Calathea polytricha, commonly known as the zigzag calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vivid, variegated leaves that display a unique zigzag pattern, often in shades of green, silver, and pink. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its rhizomatous growth habit. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Calathea polytricha requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive.
