Columellia oblonga

Columellia oblonga, commonly known as the oblong columnar cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The stems are covered in small, white spines and are capable of storing water, allowing the plant to survive in arid environments. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual element to its otherwise spiky appearance.

This plant is often cultivated for its unique shape and low maintenance requirements.
