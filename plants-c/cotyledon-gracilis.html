Cotyledon gracilis

Cotyledon gracilis, commonly known as the slender cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
