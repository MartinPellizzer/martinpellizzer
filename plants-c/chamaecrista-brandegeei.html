Chamaecrista brandegeei

Chamaecrista brandegeei, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain several small, round seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
