Cousinia deickii

Cousinia deickii, commonly known as the Armenian hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Cousinia deickii produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
