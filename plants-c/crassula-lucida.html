Crassula lucida

Crassula lucida, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Lion's Ear," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, shrub-like growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and purple during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Crassula lucida is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
