Chamaecrista desvauxii var. brevipes

Chamaecrista desvauxii var. brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed coral bean, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, compound leaves with a pinnate structure. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom during the warmer months. Its seeds are encased in small, pod-like fruits that split open to release the seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
