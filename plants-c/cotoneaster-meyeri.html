Cotoneaster meyeri

Cotoneaster meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that turn red in the fall, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Cotoneaster meyeri is often used in landscaping for its compact growth habit and seasonal interest.
