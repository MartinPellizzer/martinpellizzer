Calligonum × barsukiense

Calligonum × barsukiense, commonly known as the hybrid saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in the spring or early summer. It is highly salt-tolerant and thrives in saline soils, making it an important species for stabilizing coastal and desert environments.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it valuable for ecological restoration projects.
