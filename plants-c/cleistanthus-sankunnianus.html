Cleistanthus sankunnianus

Cleistanthus sankunnianus, commonly known as the Malabar cleistanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

Cleistanthus sankunnianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
