Croton baronii

Croton baronii, commonly known as the Baron's croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that range from green to red, yellow, and orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. Its leaves are often deeply lobed and have a distinctive veining pattern.

Croton baronii is typically grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.
