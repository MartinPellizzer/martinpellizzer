Cyphostemma Flaviflorum

Cyphostemma flaviflorum is a rare and striking plant native to parts of Africa. It is known for its unique, clustered leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. This species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh climates. Its distinctive appearance has made it a popular choice among collectors and horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader botanical world.
