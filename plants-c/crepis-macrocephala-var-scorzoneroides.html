Crepis macrocephala var. scorzoneroides

Crepis macrocephala var. scorzoneroides, commonly known as the scorzoneroid dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces a single, tall, hollow stem that bears a large, yellow composite flower head. Its seeds are enclosed in a pappus, which aids in wind dispersal.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
