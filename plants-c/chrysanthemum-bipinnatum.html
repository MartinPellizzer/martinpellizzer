Chrysanthemum bipinnatum

Chrysanthemum bipinnatum, commonly known as the doubleleaf chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its finely divided, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the autumn season. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its intricate foliage and showy blooms.

Chrysanthemum bipinnatum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
