Cymbidium lancifolium var. papuanum

Cymbidium lancifolium var. papuanum, commonly known as the Papuan sword orchid, is a species native to the highlands of New Guinea.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure and linear, sword-shaped leaves that are often a striking green or bluish-green. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It prefers cool, bright conditions with good air circulation and moderate watering.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.
