Cynoglossum plantaginifolium

Cynoglossum plantaginifolium, commonly known as heartworts, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue, with five lobes, and are arranged in a loose spike. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Cynoglossum plantaginifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
