Cirsium × andreanszkyanum

Cirsium × andreanszkyanum, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Central and Eastern Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in open fields.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
