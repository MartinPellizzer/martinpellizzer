Clematis liaotungensis

Clematis liaotungensis, commonly known as the Liaotung clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, typically arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Clematis liaotungensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
