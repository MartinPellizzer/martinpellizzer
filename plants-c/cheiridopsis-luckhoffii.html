Cheiridopsis luckhoffii

Cheiridopsis luckhoffii, commonly known as the Knysna sandveld daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, mat-forming succulent with fleshy, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cheiridopsis luckhoffii is often cultivated in rock gardens and is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
