Crassula rubicunda var. similis

Crassula rubicunda var. similis, commonly known as the "Rubicunda Variegated" or "Rubicunda Similaris," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or pink variegation, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique coloration and compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
