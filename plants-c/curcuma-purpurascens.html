Curcuma purpurascens

Curcuma purpurascens, commonly known as the purple ginger, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its ornamental rhizomes and vibrant, purple-tinged leaves that add visual interest to gardens.
