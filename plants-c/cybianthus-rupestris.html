Cybianthus rupestris

Cybianthus rupestris, commonly known as the rock lily, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen throughout the year.

Cybianthus rupestris is well adapted to dry conditions and is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
