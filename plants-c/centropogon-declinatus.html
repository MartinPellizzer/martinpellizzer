Centropogon declinatus

Centropogon declinatus, commonly known as the slender pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy yellow flowers. Its flowers are clustered in loose inflorescences and bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.

Centropogon declinatus is often found in coastal and lowland forest habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
