Conophytum perpusillum

Conophytum perpusillum, commonly known as the dwarf conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its small, rounded leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often growing in crevices or on rock faces.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
