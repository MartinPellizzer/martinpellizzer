Cotyledon undulata

Cotyledon undulata, commonly known as the wrinkled cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, crinkled leaves that are typically green or purple in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Cotyledon undulata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
