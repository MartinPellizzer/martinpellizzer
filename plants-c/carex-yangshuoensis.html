Carex yangshuoensis

Carex yangshuoensis, commonly known as the Yangshuo sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yangshuo region of Guangxi Province. This plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in swampy or marshy areas.

The botanical features of Carex yangshuoensis make it an important component of local wetland ecosystems.
