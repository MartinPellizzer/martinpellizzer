Cyperus familiaris

Cyperus familiaris, commonly known as umbrella plant or cyperus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, spike-like flowers at the top. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense clumps.

Cyperus familiaris is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in moist environments.
