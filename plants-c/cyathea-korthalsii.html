Cyathea korthalsii

Cyathea korthalsii, commonly known as the Korthals' tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its dramatic appearance.

Cyathea korthalsii plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
