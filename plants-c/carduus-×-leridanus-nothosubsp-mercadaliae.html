Carduus × leridanus nothosubsp. mercadaliae

Carduus × leridanus nothosubsp.

mercadaliae, commonly known as the mercadalia thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads that bloom in late summer.
