Calibrachoa Humilis

Calibrachoa humilis is a small, flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America. It is known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing in rocky soils. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental plants. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known species within the broader Calibrachoa genus.
