Clematis moisseenkoi

Clematis moisseenkoi, commonly known as the Moisseenko clematis, is a deciduous vine native to Central Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, typically arranged in a feather-like pattern. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
