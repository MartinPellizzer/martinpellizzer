Crotalaria phyllostachya

Crotalaria phyllostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume pods and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, narrow leaflets and produces yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its seeds are encased in elongated pods and are used as a food source in some regions.

Crotalaria phyllostachya is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
