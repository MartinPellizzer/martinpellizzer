Croton goldiei

Croton goldiei, commonly known as the golden croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of gold, yellow, and red. The leaves are typically large, broad, and have a textured surface, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its eye-catching appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
