Caralluma

Caralluma, commonly known as the desert fig or nightshade, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and southern Asia. Caralluma species are valued for their ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
