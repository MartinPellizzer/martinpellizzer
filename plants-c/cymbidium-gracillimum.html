Cymbidium gracillimum

Cymbidium gracillimum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and pendant, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Cymbidium gracillimum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to container gardening.
