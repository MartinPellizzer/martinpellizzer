Chrysophyllum hostmannianum

Chrysophyllum hostmannianum, commonly known as the Hostmann's cherimoya, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and its potential as a fruit crop in suitable climates.
