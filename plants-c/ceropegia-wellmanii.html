Ceropegia wellmanii

Ceropegia wellmanii, commonly known as the string of pearls, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features long, thin, waxy stems that hang downward, resembling beads on a string. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
