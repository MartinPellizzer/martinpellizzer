Callitriche deflexa var. glaziovii

Callitriche deflexa var. glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's water star, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic species that typically grows in shallow water environments. The plant has slender, branching stems that are often floating or partially submerged. Its small, star-shaped flowers are white or pale pink and bloom in early spring.
