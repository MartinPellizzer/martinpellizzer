Calorezia

Calorezia, commonly known as the flameblossom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, resembling flames. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its blooming period occurs in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Calorezia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
