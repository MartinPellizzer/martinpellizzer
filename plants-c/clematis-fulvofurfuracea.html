Clematis fulvofurfuracea

Clematis fulvofurfuracea, commonly known as the orange-flowered clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America.

It features large, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange, with a distinctive fuzzy texture on the petals. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, hairy strands. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis fulvofurfuracea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
