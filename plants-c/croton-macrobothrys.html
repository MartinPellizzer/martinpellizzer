Croton macrobothrys

Croton macrobothrys, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are typically bright green with a distinctive red or orange midrib. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into ornamental, spiky seed pods. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its striking foliage.

The botanical features of Croton macrobothrys make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
