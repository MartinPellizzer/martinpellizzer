Celmisia × pseudolyallii

Celmisia × pseudolyallii, commonly known as the Lyall’s celmisia, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect growth habit and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
