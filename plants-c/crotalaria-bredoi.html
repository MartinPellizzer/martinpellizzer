Crotalaria bredoi

Crotalaria bredoi, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its seeds are enclosed in a pod-like structure, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.

However, it can also be invasive in certain environments.
