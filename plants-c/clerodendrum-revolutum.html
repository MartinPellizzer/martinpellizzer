Clerodendrum revolutum

Clerodendrum revolutum, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow or white center. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture, while its stems are often covered in fine hairs.

Clerodendrum revolutum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping in warm climates.
