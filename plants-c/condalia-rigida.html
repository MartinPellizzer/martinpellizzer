Condalia rigida

Condalia rigida, commonly known as the rigid condalia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in complex inflorescences. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

Condalia rigida is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
