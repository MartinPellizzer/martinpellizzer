Chomelia

Chomelia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Lythraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the warm seasons. The plant typically grows as a tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters in its native habitat. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Chomelia thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sunlight to flourish.
