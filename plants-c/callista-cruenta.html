Callista cruenta

Callista cruenta, commonly known as the red-flowered devil's bit, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange-yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spiky fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
