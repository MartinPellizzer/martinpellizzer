Cotoneaster spongbergii

Cotoneaster spongbergii, commonly known as the Spongberg cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in the spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with finely toothed margins.

Cotoneaster spongbergii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal color changes.
