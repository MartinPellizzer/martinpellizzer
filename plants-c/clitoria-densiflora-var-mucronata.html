Clitoria densiflora var. mucronata

Clitoria densiflora var. mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf butterfly pea, is a tropical climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, violet-blue to purple flowers that are adorned with intricate patterns resembling butterfly wings. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces small, yellowish fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The species is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
