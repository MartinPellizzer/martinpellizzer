Crotalaria chiapensis

Crotalaria chiapensis, commonly known as Chiapan false poinsettia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
