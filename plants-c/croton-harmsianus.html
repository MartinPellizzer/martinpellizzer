Croton harmsianus

Croton harmsianus, commonly known as the Harms' croton, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored leaves that can range from green to red or purple. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it may be invasive in some regions.
