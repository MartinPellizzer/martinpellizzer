Chylismia lancifolia

Chylismia lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chylismia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
