Campanula ficarioides var. major

Campanula ficarioides var. major, commonly known as the large bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
