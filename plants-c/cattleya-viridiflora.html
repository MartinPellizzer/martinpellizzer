Cattleya viridiflora

Cattleya viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves and its unique, greenish-yellow flowers with a contrasting red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces fragrant blooms that are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.

Cattleya viridiflora is known for its relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
