Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. repens

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. repens, commonly known as the trailing cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact growth and ornamental appeal.
