Cattleya mossiae var. magnifica

Cattleya mossiae var. magnifica, commonly known as the Magnificent Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, fragrant flowers.

It features large, showy blooms with a vibrant mix of pink, white, and lavender hues, often with a contrasting lip. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.

Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and gardeners alike.
