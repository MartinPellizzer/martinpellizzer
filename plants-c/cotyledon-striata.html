Cotyledon striata

Cotyledon striata, commonly known as the "striped African violet," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The leaves are typically green with distinctive white or yellow stripes, giving the plant its common name. It produces small, pale pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.
