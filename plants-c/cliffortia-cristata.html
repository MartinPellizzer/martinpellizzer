Cliffortia cristata

Cliffortia cristata, commonly known as the helmet flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water. The plant produces striking, helmet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
