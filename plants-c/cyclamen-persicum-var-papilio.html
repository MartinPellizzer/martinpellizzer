Cyclamen persicum var. papilio

Cyclamen persicum var. papilio, commonly known as the Papilio cyclamen, is a striking ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus.

It is characterized by its vibrant, butterfly-shaped flowers that range from pink to deep red, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety foliage that adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens or as a houseplant.

Cyclamen persicum var. papilio is valued for its long blooming period and ability to add color to shaded areas.
