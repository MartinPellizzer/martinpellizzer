Cybianthus grandezii

Cybianthus grandezii, commonly known as the grandiflorous cybianthus, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with five petals and a prominent central stamen.

Cybianthus grandezii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
