Crotalaria sagittalis var. oblonga

Crotalaria sagittalis var. oblonga, commonly known as the arrowhead crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in open fields, roadsides, and waste areas.
