Cyperus hexastachyus

Cyperus hexastachyus, commonly known as the spike rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and a dense cluster of small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds, and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Cyperus hexastachyus is also used in traditional medicine and has ornamental value in landscaping.
