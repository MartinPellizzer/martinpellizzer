Calophyllum smilesianum var. luteum

Calophyllum smilesianum var. luteum, commonly known as the yellow calophyllum, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
