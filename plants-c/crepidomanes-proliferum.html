Crepidomanes proliferum

Crepidomanes proliferum, commonly known as the clustered sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or disturbed habitats.
