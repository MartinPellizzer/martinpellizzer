Cnidoscolus polyanthus

Cnidoscolus polyanthus, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves, which are often brightly colored and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically followed by edible, spiny fruits. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.
