Cyclamen lobospilum

Cyclamen lobospilum, commonly known as the mountain cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spurred flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Cyclamen lobospilum has a tuberous root system that helps it survive in its native habitat.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking unique and hardy plants.
