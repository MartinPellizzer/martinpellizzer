Camellia flavida var. patens

Camellia flavida var. patens, commonly known as the yellow camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Camellia flavida var. patens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
