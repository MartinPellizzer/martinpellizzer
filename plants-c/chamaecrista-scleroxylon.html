Chamaecrista scleroxylon

Chamaecrista scleroxylon, commonly known as the African burr legume, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, woody pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional practices. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used for soil stabilization in degraded landscapes.
