Carex × Alsatica Subsp. Subviridula

Carex × alsatica subsp. subviridula is a lesser-known member of the Carex genus, commonly found in moist, temperate regions. This plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Despite its ecological value, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Understanding this subspecies can enhance our appreciation for the diversity within the Carex family.
