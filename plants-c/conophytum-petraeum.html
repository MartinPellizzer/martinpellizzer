Conophytum petraeum

Conophytum petraeum, commonly known as the rock rose or petraeum ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a striking addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
