Crinum natalense

Crinum natalense, commonly known as Natal lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in summer, often with a strong, pleasant fragrance. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Crinum natalense is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
