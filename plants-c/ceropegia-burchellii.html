Ceropegia burchellii

Ceropegia burchellii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, attracting specific pollinators like moths. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
