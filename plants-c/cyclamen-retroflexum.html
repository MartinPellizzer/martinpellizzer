Cyclamen retroflexum

Cyclamen retroflexum, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces striking, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its tuberous roots allow it to survive harsh winter conditions.

Cyclamen retroflexum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
