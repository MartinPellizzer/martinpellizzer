Cedrela toona var. warburgii

Cedrela toona var. warburgii, commonly known as Warburg's cedrela, is a tropical tree species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

The wood of this species is highly prized for its durability and fine grain, making it a sought-after material in the timber industry.
