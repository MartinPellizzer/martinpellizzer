Clinopodium laxiflorum var. taiwanianum

Clinopodium laxiflorum var. taiwanianum, commonly known as Taiwan betony, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a mint-like fragrance.

It produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.
