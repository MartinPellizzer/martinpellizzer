Cactus zehntneri

Cactus zehntneri, commonly known as the Zehntner's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its stems are cylindrical and covered in a waxy cuticle that helps reduce water loss.

It is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments with minimal rainfall.
