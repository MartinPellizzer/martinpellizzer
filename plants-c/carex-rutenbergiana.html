Carex rutenbergiana

Carex rutenbergiana, commonly known as the Rutenberg sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often green or brown in color.

Carex rutenbergiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wet habitats.
