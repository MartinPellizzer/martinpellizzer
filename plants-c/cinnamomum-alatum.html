Cinnamomum alatum

Cinnamomum alatum, commonly known as the winged cinnamon or Chinese cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that have distinctive wing-like structures along the edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue berries that contain the seeds. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
