Cleisostoma platystele

Cleisostoma platystele, commonly known as the broad-stemmed hooded orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, hood-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.
