Cyperus rionensis

Cyperus rionensis, commonly known as the Rion grass, is a species of sedge native to South America.

It is characterized by its erect, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks.

Cyperus rionensis is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.
