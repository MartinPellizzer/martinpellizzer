Ceraia Kurashigei

Ceraia kurashigei is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan. It is known for its small, pale yellow blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in cool, humid climates and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
