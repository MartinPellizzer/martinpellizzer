Columnea villosissima

Columnea villosissima, commonly known as the hairy columnea, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has velvety, hairy leaves that give it its common name and contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.

Columnea villosissima is known for its ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
