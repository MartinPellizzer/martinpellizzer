Camellia pentastyla

Camellia pentastyla, commonly known as the five-styled camellia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its showy, double flowers that range in color from white to deep red. Camellia pentastyla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter blooming period.
