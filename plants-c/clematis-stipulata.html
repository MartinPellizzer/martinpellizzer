Clematis stipulata

Clematis stipulata, commonly known as the Japanese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to East Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or violet, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis stipulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beautiful blooms.
