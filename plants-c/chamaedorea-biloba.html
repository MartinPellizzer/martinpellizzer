Chamaedorea biloba

Chamaedorea biloba, commonly known as the bilobed bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clustered, feather-like leaves. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is typically a deep green, with a slightly waxy texture.
