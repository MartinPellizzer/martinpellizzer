Carduncellus mitissimus var. subacaulis

Carduncellus mitissimus var. subacaulis, commonly known as the slender globe-thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking spherical blue or purple flower heads that are surrounded by spiny bracts. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with upright, hairy stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
