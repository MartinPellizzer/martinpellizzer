Centaurella aestivalis

Centaurella aestivalis, commonly known as the summer centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with purple markings. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Centaurella aestivalis is known for its ability to thrive in dry, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.
