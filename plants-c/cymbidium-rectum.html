Cymbidium rectum

Cymbidium rectum, commonly known as the straight orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with long, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces tall spikes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cymbidium rectum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
