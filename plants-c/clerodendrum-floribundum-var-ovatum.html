Clerodendrum floribundum var. ovatum

Clerodendrum floribundum var. ovatum, commonly known as the fragrant flame bush, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.
