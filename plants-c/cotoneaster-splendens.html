Cotoneaster splendens

Cotoneaster splendens, commonly known as the golden cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy green leaves that turn vibrant red in the autumn. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers in the spring, followed by orange-red berries that persist through winter. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster splendens is relatively low maintenance and suitable for hedges or foundation plantings.
