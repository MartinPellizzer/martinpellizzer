Coelogyne pogonioides

Coelogyne pogonioides, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its natural habitat. It blooms during the dry season, often producing multiple flower spikes from the base of the plant.

Coelogyne pogonioides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
