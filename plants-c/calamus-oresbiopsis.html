Calamus oresbiopsis

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, creeping rhizomes and narrow, linear leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spadices.
