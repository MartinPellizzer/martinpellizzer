Clematis sericeopannosa

Clematis sericeopannosa, commonly known as the silky pannose clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its finely hairy stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to violet, typically in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis sericeopannosa is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
