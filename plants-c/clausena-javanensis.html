Clausena javanensis

Clausena javanensis, commonly known as the Java Clausena, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, orange-colored berries. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
