Curculigo annamitica

Curculigo annamitica, commonly known as the Annamite arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in spikes.
