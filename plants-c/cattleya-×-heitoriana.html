Cattleya × heitoriana

Cattleya × heitoriana, commonly known as the Heitoriana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant produces pendulous blooms with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya × heitoriana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
