Cynorkis speciosa

Cynorkis speciosa, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive color pattern. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky habitats and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a unique three-lobed lip that attracts pollinators.

Cynorkis speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
