Chloris breviaristata

Chloris breviaristata, commonly known as the narrow-awned grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, which contribute to its ability to colonize disturbed soils. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native vegetation in its native range.
