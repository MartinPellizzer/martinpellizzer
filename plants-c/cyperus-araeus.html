Cyperus araeus

Cyperus araeus, commonly known as the umbrella sedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its compact, rounded foliage that resembles an umbrella, giving it its common name. It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, making it a common sight in swampy areas.

Cyperus araeus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and adaptability to various water conditions.
