Carya kweichowensis

Carya kweichowensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hickory, is a deciduous tree native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the walnut family, Juglandaceae, and is characterized by its alternate, pinnately compound leaves. The tree produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in catkins, followed by edible nuts enclosed in a thick, husky shell.

Carya kweichowensis is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also cultivated for its nut production.
