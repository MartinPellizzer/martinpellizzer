Campanula linifolia var. tenuifolia

Campanula linifolia var. tenuifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
