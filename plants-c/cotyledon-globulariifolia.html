Cotyledon globulariifolia

Cotyledon globulariifolia, commonly known as the globe-shaped cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often rounded or orbicular in shape, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the warmer months. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

The leaves are capable of storing water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid environments.
