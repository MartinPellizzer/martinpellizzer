Cotyledon mucizonia f. glaberrima

Cotyledon mucizonia f. glaberrima, commonly known as the glaberrima cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
