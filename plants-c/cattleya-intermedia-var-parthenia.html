Cattleya intermedia var. parthenia

Cattleya intermedia var. parthenia, commonly known as the Parthenia Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its fragrance and long-lasting blooms, which can last several weeks.
