Canna ellipticifolia

Canna ellipticifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf cannas, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 inches in length, providing a dramatic visual impact in gardens. The plant produces showy, orange or red flowers that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Canna ellipticifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its foliage and floral display.
