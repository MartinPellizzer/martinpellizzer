Cirsium × rigens

Cirsium × rigens, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, purple flower heads and robust, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in grasslands, disturbed soils, and along roadsides, thriving in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are clustered in dense, spherical heads.

Cirsium × rigens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
