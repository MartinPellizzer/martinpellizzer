Chlorospatha silverstonei

Chlorospatha silverstonei, commonly known as the silverstone lily, is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well-suited to semi-desert environments. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh native habitat.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and limited distribution.
