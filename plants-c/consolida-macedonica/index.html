Consolida Macedonica

Macedonian consolida, scientifically known as Consolida macedonica, is a rare and endemic flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North Macedonia. This delicate species belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes well-known genera like Ranunculus and Anemone. With its vibrant, spurred flowers and slender stems, it is a striking addition to alpine meadows and rocky habitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, the plant faces threats from environmental changes and human activity. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure for future generations.
