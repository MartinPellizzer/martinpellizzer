Columbaria gramuntia

Columbaria gramuntia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-green leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
