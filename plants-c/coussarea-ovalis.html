Coussarea ovalis

Coussarea ovalis, commonly known as the oval flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Coussarea ovalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks due to its showy floral display.
