Cleome schlechteri

Cleome schlechteri, commonly known as the yellow cleome, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a member of the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and slender, branching stems. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Cleome schlechteri is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
