Catasetum ariquemense f. viride

Catasetum ariquemense f. viride, commonly known as the green-flowered catasetum, is a rare orchid species native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant green flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include long, slender stems and specialized pollination structures that attract specific wasp species.

This orchid is valued for its unique appearance and ecological role in its native habitat.
