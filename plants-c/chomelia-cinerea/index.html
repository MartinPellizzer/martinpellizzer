Chomelia Cinerea

Cineraria, scientifically known as Chomelia cinerea, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. With its vibrant blue and purple petals, it captivates gardeners and botanists alike. This species belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes many other ornamental flowers. Cineraria thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements have made it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts.
