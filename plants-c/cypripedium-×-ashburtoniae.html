Cypripedium × ashburtoniae

Cypripedium × ashburtoniae, commonly known as the Ashburton lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch and a deep pink to purple coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich soils. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single flower per stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
