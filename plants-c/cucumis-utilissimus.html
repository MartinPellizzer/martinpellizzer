Cucumis utilissimus

Cucumis utilissimus, commonly known as the wild cucumber, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and melons. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically green and spiny when mature. Its leaves are broad and lobed, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

The wild cucumber is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
