Centaurea × valentina

Centaurea × valentina, commonly known as the Valentine's Day mix, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features upright, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is widely appreciated in horticulture.
