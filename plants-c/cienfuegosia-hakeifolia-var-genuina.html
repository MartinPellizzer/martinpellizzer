Cienfuegosia hakeifolia var. genuina

Cienfuegosia hakeifolia var. genuina, commonly known as the Cuban flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It features striking, palmate leaves that are typically bright green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces showy, panicle-like inflorescences with clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer. Its ornamental value is enhanced by the vibrant red or orange fruits that follow the flowers.

This species is well-suited to warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes.
