Cystopteris fragilis var. acutidentata

Cystopteris fragilis var. acutidentata, commonly known as the acute-inedge brittle fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to regions in Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features bipinnate leaves with a distinctive, fragile texture that easily breaks apart. Its fronds are typically light green and have a finely serrated margin.
