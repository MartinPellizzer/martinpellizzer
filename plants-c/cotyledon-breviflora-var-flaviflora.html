Cotyledon breviflora var. flaviflora

Cotyledon breviflora var. flaviflora, commonly known as the Orange-Flowered Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to flourish.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
