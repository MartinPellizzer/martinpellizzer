Croton bastardii

Croton bastardii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its branched stem and large, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes. Its bark and leaves have been traditionally used in herbal medicine for their purported medicinal properties.

Croton bastardii thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.
