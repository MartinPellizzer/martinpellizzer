Caragana williamsii

Caragana williamsii, commonly known as Siberian pea shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features compound leaves with 10-20 pairs of small, oval leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers in early summer, followed by legume pods containing several seeds. It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and is often used for erosion control.

Its rapid growth and hardiness make it a popular choice for reforestation and ornamental purposes.
