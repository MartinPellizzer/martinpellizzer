Costus cupreifolius

Costus cupreifolius, commonly known as the copper-leaf costus, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Costaceae.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking copper-colored leaves and tall, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
