Chloris perrieri var. aristata

Chloris perrieri var. aristata, commonly known as the slender grass or slender chloris, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, narrow leaves and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, pale green to blue-green flowers that are typically clustered in spikelets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native gardens and restoration projects.
