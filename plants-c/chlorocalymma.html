Chlorocalymma

Chlorocalymma, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers surrounded by silver or white ray florets. The plants typically have a compact growth habit with hairy, narrow leaves. Native to southern Africa, Chlorocalymma species are often found in rocky or sandy soils.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
