Cytisus rochelii

Cytisus rochelii, commonly known as the Cape Verde broom, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and rocky soils, adapting well to arid and saline conditions. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are small, trifoliate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Cytisus rochelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environments.
