Cryptantha

Cryptantha, commonly known as the "blue flax" or "bitterroot," is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

These plants are native to North America and are characterized by their small, often blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. Some species are used in traditional medicine, while others are valued for their ornamental appeal.
