Campylospermum Subcordatum

Campylospermum subcordatum is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking, elongated leaves and delicate, fragrant blooms that appear during the rainy season. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense rainforests. Its scientific name reflects its distinctive leaf shape and heart-like base. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant in the botanical world.
