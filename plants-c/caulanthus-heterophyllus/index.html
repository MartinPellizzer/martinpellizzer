Caulanthus Heterophyllus

Caulanthus heterophyllus is a striking flowering plant native to the western united states. it belongs to the mustard family and is known for its vibrant purple blooms. this species thrives in rocky and coastal habitats, often found in california and oregon. its unique leaf shapes and adaptability to harsh environments make it a subject of botanical interest. the plant plays an important role in local ecosystems and is valued for its ecological and aesthetic contributions.
