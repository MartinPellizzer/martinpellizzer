Coelogyne distelidia

Coelogyne distelidia, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy blooms that range from red to orange in color. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

The flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.
