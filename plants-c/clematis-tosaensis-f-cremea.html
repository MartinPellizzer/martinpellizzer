Clematis tosaensis f. cremea

Clematis tosaensis f. cremea, commonly known as the Tosa Clematis, is a hybrid form of the Tosa Clematis, native to Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a vigorous climbing vine with compound leaves and is well-suited for trellises or arbors. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant blooms and ability to add vertical interest to gardens.
