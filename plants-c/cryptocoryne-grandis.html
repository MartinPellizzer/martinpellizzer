Cryptocoryne grandis

Cryptocoryne grandis, commonly known as the giant cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant is often found in slow-moving freshwater habitats such as ponds and lakes. It is a popular choice for aquariums due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne grandis is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its tolerance to a wide range of water parameters.
