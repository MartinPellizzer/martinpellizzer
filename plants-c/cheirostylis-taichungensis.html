Cheirostylis taichungensis

Cheirostylis taichungensis, commonly known as the Taichung orchid, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically grows in forested areas, where it thrives in the humid and shaded environment. Its rhizome is slender, and it produces pseudobulbs that support its growth.

The flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate markings that aid in pollination.
