Cavendishia praestans

Cavendishia praestans, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, large, and brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in a spiral pattern along the stem, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Cavendishia praestans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in greenhouses and gardens for its vibrant display.
