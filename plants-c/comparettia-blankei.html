Comparettia blankei

Comparettia blankei, commonly known as the Blanke's slipper orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
