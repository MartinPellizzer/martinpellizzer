Chrysopogon tenuiculmis

Chrysopogon tenuiculmis, commonly known as narrow-leaf bluestem, is a perennial grass native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and riparian areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue to purple flower heads. It is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Its botanical features make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
