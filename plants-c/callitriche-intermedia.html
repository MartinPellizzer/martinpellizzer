Callitriche intermedia

Callitriche intermedia, commonly known as narrowleaf water starwort, is a small aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It has slender, branching stems that often lie close to the water surface. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, typically arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are usually submerged or floating.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
