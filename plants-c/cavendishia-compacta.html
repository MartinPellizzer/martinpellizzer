Cavendishia compacta

Cavendishia compacta, commonly known as the compact orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, colorful blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple, often arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Cavendishia compacta is appreciated for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
