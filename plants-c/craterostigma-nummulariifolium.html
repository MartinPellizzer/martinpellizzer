Craterostigma nummulariifolium

Craterostigma nummulariifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf flax, is a small, perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in arid conditions.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Linaceae and is noted for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soils.
