Cleisostoma racemiferum

Cleisostoma racemiferum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features long, slender roots that help it anchor to trees and absorb moisture from the air. Its flowers typically bloom in summer and are known for their sweet, spicy aroma.

Cleisostoma racemiferum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
