Cucumis melo aurantiacus

Cucumis melo aurantiacus, commonly known as the orange melon, is a species of flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, round, orange-colored fruit with a thick, aromatic rind and sweet, juicy flesh. The plant is a vine that produces both male and female flowers, typical of many cucurbit species. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is cultivated for its edible fruit, which is valued for its flavor and nutritional content.
