Capitanopsis Brevilabra

Capitanopsis brevilabra is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its unique, spiky appearance. The plant features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments makes it a fascinating subject for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Capitanopsis brevilabra is now considered endangered and requires urgent protection.
