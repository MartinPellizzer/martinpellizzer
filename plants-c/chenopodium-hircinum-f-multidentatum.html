Chenopodium hircinum f. multidentatum

Chenopodium hircinum f. multidentatum, commonly known as the curly goosefoot, is a variant of the species Chenopodium hircinum.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curly leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and other open areas.

Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
