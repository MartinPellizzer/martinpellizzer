Cirsium nakae

Cirsium nakae, commonly known as the Nakae thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny leaves and a tall, erect stem that supports its showy purple to pink flower heads. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and is known for its ornamental value.

Cirsium nakae is also valued in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
