Cyclamen hederifolium subsp. creticum

Cyclamen hederifolium subsp.

creticum, commonly known as the Cretan cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features heart-shaped, velvety foliage with a deep green hue and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its flowers typically bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a valuable early-season ornamental. Cyclamen hederifolium subsp.

creticum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
