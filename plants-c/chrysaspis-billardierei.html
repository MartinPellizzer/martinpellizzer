Chrysaspis billardierei

Chrysaspis billardierei, commonly known as the pink-flowered rock heath, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder.

Chrysaspis billardierei is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
