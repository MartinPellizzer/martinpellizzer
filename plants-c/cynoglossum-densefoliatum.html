Cynoglossum densefoliatum

Cynoglossum densefoliatum, commonly known as the dense-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal leaves that are densely packed and oval in shape, with a hairy texture. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers in late summer, resembling bell-shaped blossoms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
