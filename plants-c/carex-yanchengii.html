Carex yanchengii

Carex yanchengii, commonly known as Yancheng sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland conservation.
