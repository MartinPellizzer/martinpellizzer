Cirsium greatrexii

Cirsium greatrexii, commonly known as Greatrex's thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with a robust stem covered in stiff bracts. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs and have a waxy texture, which helps reduce water loss in its arid habitat.

Cirsium greatrexii is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
