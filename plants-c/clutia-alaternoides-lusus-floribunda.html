Clutia alaternoides lusus floribunda

Clutia alaternoides lusus floribunda, commonly known as the showy clutia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
