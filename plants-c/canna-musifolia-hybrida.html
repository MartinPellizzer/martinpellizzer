Canna musifolia-hybrida

Canna musifolia-hybrida, commonly known as the giant cannas, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy leaves that can reach up to 2 feet in length and its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is a member of the Cannaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated in gardens for its bold foliage and long-lasting blooms.

Canna musifolia-hybrida is also valued for its role in ecological landscapes, providing habitat for pollinators and adding visual interest to water features.
