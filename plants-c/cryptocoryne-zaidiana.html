Cryptocoryne zaidiana

Cryptocoryne zaidiana, commonly known as the Zaidian Cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that are often speckled with small, white spots. The plant thrives in soft, acidic water conditions and prefers moderate to high light levels. It is a popular choice for aquariums due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.

Cryptocoryne zaidiana is also known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters, making it a versatile addition to planted tanks.
