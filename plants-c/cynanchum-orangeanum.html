Cynanchum orangeanum

Cynanchum orangeanum, commonly known as orange milkweed, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its milky sap, which contains latex. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and has opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive corona.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
