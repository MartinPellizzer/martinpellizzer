Corynopuntia kunzei

Corynopuntia kunzei, commonly known as the Mexican apple cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit, which resembles an apple, is edible and has a mild, sweet flavor.

Corynopuntia kunzei is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
