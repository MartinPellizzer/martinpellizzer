Catabrosa ochroleuca

Catabrosa ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow-flowered catabrosa, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Catabrosa ochroleuca is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
