Convolvulus vollesenii

Convolvulus vollesenii, commonly known as the Vollesen's bindweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with trailing stems that often form dense mats. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Convolvulus vollesenii is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
