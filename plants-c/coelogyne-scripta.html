Coelogyne scripta

Coelogyne scripta, commonly known as the script orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white and pink flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces showy, often fragrant blooms that can last for several weeks. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne scripta is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
