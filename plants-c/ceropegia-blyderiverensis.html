Ceropegia blyderiverensis

Ceropegia blyderiverensis, commonly known as the Blyderiver milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tubular flowers. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that are often clustered together. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia blyderiverensis is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
