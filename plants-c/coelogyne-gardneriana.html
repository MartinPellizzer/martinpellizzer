Coelogyne gardneriana

Coelogyne gardneriana, commonly known as the Gardner's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, narrow leaf and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms once a year, with flowers that can last several weeks. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne gardneriana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its long-lasting, showy blooms.
