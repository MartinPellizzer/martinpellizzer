Cyperus shepherdii

Cyperus shepherdii, commonly known as the desert flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial grass-like plant that grows from a rhizomatous root system, forming dense clusters of grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically wind-pollinated.
