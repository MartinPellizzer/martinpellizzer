Coronilla emeroides

Coronilla emeroides, commonly known as the hairy vetch, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It is often used as a cover crop due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Coronilla emeroides thrives in temperate climates and is adaptable to various soil types.
