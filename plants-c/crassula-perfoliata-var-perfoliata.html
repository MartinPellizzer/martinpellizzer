Crassula perfoliata var. perfoliata

Crassula perfoliata var. perfoliata, commonly known as the String of Buttons, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for container gardening and rockeries.
