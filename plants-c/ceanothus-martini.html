Ceanothus martini

Ceanothus martini, commonly known as the Santa Cruz ceanothus, is a shrub native to California, particularly the Santa Cruz Mountains.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. It is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.

Ceanothus martini plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for various wildlife species.
