Crassula acutifolia var. harveyi

Crassula acutifolia var. harveyi, commonly known as the Harvey's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
