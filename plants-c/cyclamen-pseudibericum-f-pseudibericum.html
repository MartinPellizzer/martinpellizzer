Cyclamen pseudibericum f. pseudibericum

Cyclamen pseudibericum f. pseudibericum, commonly known as the Iberian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant produces striking bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained, slightly acidic soil.

Cyclamen pseudibericum f. pseudibericum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
