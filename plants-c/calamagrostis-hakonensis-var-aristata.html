Calamagrostis hakonensis var. aristata

Calamagrostis hakonensis var. aristata, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a clump-forming grass native to Japan.

It features slender, arching blades with a silvery-blue hue along the midrib, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces feathery, panicle-like inflorescences that add ornamental value in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in water gardens or as a border plant.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
