Calycanthus chinensis

Calycanthus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to red in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but often have a mild, sweet flavor. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Calycanthus chinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
