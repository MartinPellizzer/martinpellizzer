Cinnamomum javanicum

Cinnamomum javanicum, commonly known as Java cinnamon or cinnamon bark, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is valued for its aromatic bark, which is used to produce cinnamon spice. The plant produces oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, and it bears dark blue to black berries.

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
