Coelogyne quinquecallosa

Coelogyne quinquecallosa, commonly known as the five-spurred orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a mix of white, yellow, and green hues. The plant produces long, slender pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to grow in shaded, moist environments.

Coelogyne quinquecallosa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
