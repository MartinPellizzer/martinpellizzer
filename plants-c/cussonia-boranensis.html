Cussonia boranensis

Cussonia boranensis, commonly known as the Boran arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces striking yellowish-green spathes with red-brown spadices, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cussonia boranensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
