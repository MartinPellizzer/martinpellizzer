Curtogyne undulata

Curtogyne undulata, commonly known as the wavy leaf thornapple, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. Curtogyne undulata is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.
