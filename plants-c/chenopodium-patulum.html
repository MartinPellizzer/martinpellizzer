Chenopodium patulum

Chenopodium patulum, commonly known as slender goosefoot, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically borne on slender peduncles. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Chenopodium patulum is an annual species that plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
