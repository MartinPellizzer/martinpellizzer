Cryptocarya lucidula

Cryptocarya lucidula, commonly known as the shining cryptocarya, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark purple berries.

Cryptocarya lucidula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
