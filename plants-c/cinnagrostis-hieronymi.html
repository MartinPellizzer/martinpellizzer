Cinnagrostis hieronymi

Cinnagrostis hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymi's cinquefoil, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and vibrant floral display.
