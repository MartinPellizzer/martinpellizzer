Centaurea barrasii

Centaurea barrasii, commonly known as Barras' cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its bright blue to purple flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
