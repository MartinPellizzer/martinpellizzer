Citrus × aurantium var. minutissima

Citrus × aurantium var. minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf citron, is a small, compact citrus plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The fruit is typically a small, yellow-orange citrus with a thin peel and a sweet, aromatic pulp. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and ornate appearance.

This variety is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
