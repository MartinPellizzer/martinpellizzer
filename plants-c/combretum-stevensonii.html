Combretum stevensonii

Combretum stevensonii, commonly known as Stevenson's flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
