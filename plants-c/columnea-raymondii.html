Columnea raymondii

Columnea raymondii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vivid, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow or green markings. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Columnea raymondii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
