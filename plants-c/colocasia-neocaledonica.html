Colocasia neocaledonica

Colocasia neocaledonica, commonly known as the giant taro, is a striking tropical plant native to New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter, often with a deep green color and a waxy texture. The plant produces striking purple or white spadices that emerge from the leaf axils, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic foliage.

Due to its rapid growth and potential invasiveness, it requires careful management in non-native regions.
