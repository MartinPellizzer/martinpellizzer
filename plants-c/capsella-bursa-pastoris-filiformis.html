Capsella bursa-pastoris filiformis

Capsella bursa-pastoris filiformis, commonly known as the slender weed or filiform common chickweed, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. Its fruit is a distinctive, three-lobed capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, fields, and along roadsides.
