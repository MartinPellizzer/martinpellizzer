Croton ysabelae

Croton ysabelae, commonly known as the Ysabelae Croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored foliage, which can range from deep reds to bright yellows and greens. The leaves are typically large and have a rough, textured surface, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
