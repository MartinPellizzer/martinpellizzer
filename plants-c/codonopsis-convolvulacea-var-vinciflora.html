Codonopsis convolvulacea var. vinciflora

Codonopsis convolvulacea var. vinciflora, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant has hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
