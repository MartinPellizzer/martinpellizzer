Calpicarpum albiflorum

Calpicarpum albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered calpicarpum, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate white blooms that appear in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
