Crinum broussonetianum

Crinum broussonetianum, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that can be white, pink, or purple, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, growing from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to naturalize and spread in suitable climates.
