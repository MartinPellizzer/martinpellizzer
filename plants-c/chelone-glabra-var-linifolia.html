Chelone glabra var. linifolia

Chelone glabra var. linifolia, commonly known as white helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. The plant produces showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, hood-like structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
