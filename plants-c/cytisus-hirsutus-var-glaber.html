Cytisus hirsutus var. glaber

Cytisus hirsutus var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth broom, is a shrub native to parts of Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often thornless stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for various garden settings.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to coastal and dry garden landscapes.
