Coelogyne fimbriata var. acuminata

Coelogyne fimbriata var. acuminata, commonly known as the Acuminata Cymbidium, is a flowering orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fimbriated (fringed) petals and a tall, upright growth habit. The plant typically produces multiple racemes of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the rainy season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
