Castanopsis tsaii

Castanopsis tsaii, commonly known as the Taiwan tanoak, is a deciduous tree native to southern China and Taiwan.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, chestnuts, and beeches. The plant produces small, elongated acorns that are encased in a thick, scaly cupule. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a leathery texture.
