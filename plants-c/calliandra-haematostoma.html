Calliandra haematostoma

Calliandra haematostoma, commonly known as the bloodflower, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces long, cylindrical flower clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
