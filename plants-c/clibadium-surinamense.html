Clibadium surinamense

Clibadium surinamense, commonly known as the Surinam cluster vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, slender stems and opposite leaves. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.
