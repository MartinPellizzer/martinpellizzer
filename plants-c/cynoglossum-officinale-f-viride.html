Cynoglossum officinale f. viride

Cynoglossum officinale f. viride, commonly known as green hound's tongue, is a variant of the hound's tongue plant characterized by its foliage that exhibits a more pronounced green coloration compared to the typical form.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, often found in rocky or dry habitats. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower displays.
