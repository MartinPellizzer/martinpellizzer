Crinum asiaticum var. pedunculatum

Crinum asiaticum var. pedunculatum, commonly known as the Asian Crinum or the Purple Crinum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
