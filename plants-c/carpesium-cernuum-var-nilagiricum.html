Carpesium cernuum var. nilagiricum

Carpesium cernuum var. nilagiricum, commonly known as the Nilagiri milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a follicle that releases seeds with silky hairs, aiding in wind dispersal.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
