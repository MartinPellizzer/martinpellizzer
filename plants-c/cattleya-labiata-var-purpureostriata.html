Cattleya labiata var. purpureostriata

Cattleya labiata var. purpureostriata, commonly known as the Purple-striped Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, velvety petals and a prominent lip that is often marked with striking purple stripes. This variety is native to the tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark and drawing moisture from the air.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
