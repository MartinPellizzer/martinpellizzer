Cylactis

Cylactis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spirally arranged leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Cylactis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
