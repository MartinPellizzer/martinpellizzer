Cattleya guttata var. purpurea

Cattleya guttata var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-streaked orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet and white flowers with distinctive purple streaks and spots. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
