Chrysanthemum lidbeckioides

Chrysanthemum lidbeckioides, commonly known as the yellow chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
