Cancrinia krasnoborovii

Cancrinia krasnoborovii, commonly known as the red-flowered sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It features small, fleshy leaves and produces clusters of bright red, four-petaled flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor itself in sandy or rocky soils.

Cancrinia krasnoborovii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates in its native habitat.
