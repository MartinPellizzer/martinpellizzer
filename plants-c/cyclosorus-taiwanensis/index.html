Cyclosorus Taiwanensis

Cyclosorus taiwanensis is a unique aquatic plant native to the freshwater ecosystems of Taiwan. It belongs to the family Marsileaceae, which includes several species of water clovers. This plant is characterized by its small, four-leaf clover-like structure that floats on the water surface. Its delicate leaves are often mistaken for algae due to their appearance. Cyclosorus taiwanensis plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat.
