Cosmos landii var. landii

Cosmos landii var. landii, commonly known as Mexican aster, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features slender, hairy stems. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Cosmos landii var. landii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
