Convolvulus macrorhizos

Convolvulus macrorhizos, commonly known as the large-rooted bindweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped, pink to purple flowers. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in a variety of soil types and environmental conditions. It is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in open grasslands.

Due to its aggressive growth habit, it can become invasive in certain regions.
