Cotyledon integra

Cotyledon integra, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Cotyledon integra thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
