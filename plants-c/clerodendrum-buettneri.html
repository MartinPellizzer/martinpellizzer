Clerodendrum buettneri

Clerodendrum buettneri, commonly known as the tropical hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red and yellow. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, green, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum buettneri is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
