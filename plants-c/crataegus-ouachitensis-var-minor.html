Crataegus ouachitensis var. minor

Crataegus ouachitensis var. minor, commonly known as the Ouachita hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features thorny branches and small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and turn yellow in the fall.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
