Callista denticulata

Callista denticulata, commonly known as the toothed bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and toothed, giving it its scientific name.

Callista denticulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
