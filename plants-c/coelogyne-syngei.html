Coelogyne syngei

Coelogyne syngei, commonly known as the Synge's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, lance-shaped leaf and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and are known for their intricate, intricate patterns.

Coelogyne syngei is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
