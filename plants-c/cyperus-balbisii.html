Cyperus balbisii

Cyperus balbisii, commonly known as the silver grass or Balbis' flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are often inconspicuous. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Cyperus balbisii is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive, silver-gray foliage.
