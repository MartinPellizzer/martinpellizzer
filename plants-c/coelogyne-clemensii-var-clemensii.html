Coelogyne clemensii var. clemensii

Coelogyne clemensii var. clemensii, commonly known as the Clements' orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-like leaves and blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
