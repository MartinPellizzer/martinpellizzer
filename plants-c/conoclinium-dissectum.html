Conoclinium dissectum

Conoclinium dissectum, commonly known as the Mexican honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

Conoclinium dissectum is well-suited for gardens and wildlife habitats due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
