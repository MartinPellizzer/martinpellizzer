Crotalaria trifoliastrum

Crotalaria trifoliastrum, commonly known as the three-leaved crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being oval to lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are typically yellow with prominent purple veins, and it produces distinctive pods that contain several seeds.

Crotalaria trifoliastrum is often found in disturbed soils and can be a weed in agricultural settings.
