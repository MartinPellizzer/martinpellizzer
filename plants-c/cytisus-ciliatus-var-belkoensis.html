Cytisus ciliatus var. belkoensis

Cytisus ciliatus var. belkoensis, commonly known as the Belko broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden use in suitable climates.
