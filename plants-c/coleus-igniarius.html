Coleus igniarius

Coleus igniarius, commonly known as fireplant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like foliage that displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are ovate to lance-shaped, with a velvety texture and prominent veins. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its coloration.

This ornamental plant is often grown for its decorative value in gardens and indoor spaces.
