Cyclamen africanum

Cyclamen africanum, commonly known as the African cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces showy, upward-facing blooms during the spring and summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Cyclamen africanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
