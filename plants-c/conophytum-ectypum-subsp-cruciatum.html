Conophytum ectypum subsp. cruciatum

Conophytum ectypum subsp.

cruciatum, commonly known as the cross-leaf conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape.

The leaves are typically green with a reddish tinge and have a textured surface. It produces small, yellow flowers during the winter months, which are followed by distinctive, triangular seed pods.
