Cryophytum neglectum

Cryophytum neglectum, commonly known as the snowdrop, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, white or greenish flowers that are highly ornamental.

Cryophytum neglectum is valued for its early blooming habit and its ability to add color to the landscape during the late winter and early spring.
