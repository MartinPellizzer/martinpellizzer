Crambe armena

Crambe armena, commonly known as Armenian crambe, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features alternate, pinnately compound leaves with a hairy texture. Its small, yellow flowers are typically arranged in racemes and bloom in the summer months.

Crambe armena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage and flowers.
