Coreopsis kilimandscharica

Coreopsis kilimandscharica, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, arid conditions.
