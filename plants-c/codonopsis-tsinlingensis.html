Codonopsis tsinlingensis

Codonopsis tsinlingensis, commonly known as the Tsingling Codonopsis, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its erect stem and bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil, preferring temperate climates. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, while its flowers are usually blue or violet in color.

It is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
