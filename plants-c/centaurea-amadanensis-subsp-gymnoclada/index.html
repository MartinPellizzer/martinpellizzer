Centaurea Amadanensis Subsp. Gymnoclada

Centaurea amadanensis subsp. gymnoclada is a rare and lesser-known member of the Asteraceae family. Native to the arid regions of western Asia, this subspecies thrives in harsh, dry environments. Its unique morphology includes narrow, hairless leaves and striking yellow-orange flowers. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly documented in botanical studies. This plant offers valuable insights into adaptive strategies in extreme climates.
