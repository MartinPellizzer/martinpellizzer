Chorizanthe rosea

Chorizanthe rosea, commonly known as the pink choris, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, showy pink to red flowers.
