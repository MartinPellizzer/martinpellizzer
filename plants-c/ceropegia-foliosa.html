Ceropegia foliosa

Ceropegia foliosa, commonly known as the "String of Hearts," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or pink, which are pollinated by specific moths. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
