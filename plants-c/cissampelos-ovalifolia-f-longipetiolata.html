Cissampelos ovalifolia f. longipetiolata

Cissampelos ovalifolia f. longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled arrowhead vine, is a variant of the arrowhead plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with long petioles, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
