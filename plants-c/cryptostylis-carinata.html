Cryptostylis carinata

Cryptostylis carinata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging flower spike and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. It has a fibrous root system and produces oval, dark green leaves.

Cryptostylis carinata is a rare and protected species, often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
