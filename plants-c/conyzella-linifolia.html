Conyzella linifolia

Conyzella linifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf conyzella, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typically herbaceous growth habit. It produces small, daisy-like flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
