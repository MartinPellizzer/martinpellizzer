Crotalaria khasiana var. macrophylla

Crotalaria khasiana var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf crotalaria, is a herbaceous plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and palmately lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in open woodlands or along riverbanks. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, and the flowers are clustered in dense racemes.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.
