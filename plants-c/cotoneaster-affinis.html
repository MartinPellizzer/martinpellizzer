Cotoneaster affinis

Cotoneaster affinis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Korea.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Cotoneaster affinis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its winter interest and hardy nature.
