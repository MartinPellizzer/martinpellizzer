Croton hentyi

Croton hentyi, commonly known as the Henty's croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Croton hentyi thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.
