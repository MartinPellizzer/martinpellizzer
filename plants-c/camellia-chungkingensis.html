Camellia chungkingensis

Camellia chungkingensis, commonly known as the Chungking camellia, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and robust, woody stems. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Camellia chungkingensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
