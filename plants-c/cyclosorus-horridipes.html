Cyclosorus horridipes

Cyclosorus horridipes, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, overlapping fronds that form a protective shield-like structure, which helps it thrive in wetland environments. Cyclosorus horridipes is often found in shallow, still waters and is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing aquatic habitats.
