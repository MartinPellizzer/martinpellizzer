Cardiospermum pterocarpum var. balegnoi

Cardiospermum pterocarpum var. balegnoi, commonly known as the Borneo heartleaf, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces showy, pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are distinctive, inflated pods that split open to release seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
