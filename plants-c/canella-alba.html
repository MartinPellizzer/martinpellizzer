Canella alba

Canella alba, commonly known as white sand ginger, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features aromatic, pale yellow flowers and has a distinctive, fragrant rhizome. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.
