Centaurea javornikiensis

Centaurea javornikiensis, commonly known as the Javornik's centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant blue to purple flowers in summer. Its flowering period attracts various pollinators, contributing to local biodiversity.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
