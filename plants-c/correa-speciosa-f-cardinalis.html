Correa speciosa f. cardinalis

Correa speciosa f. cardinalis, commonly known as the Cardinal Correa, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a subtle, spicy fragrance. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom from spring to autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for coastal and garden landscapes.
