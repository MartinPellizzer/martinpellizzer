Clematis jialasaensis var. macrantha

Clematis jialasaensis var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, often blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with slender, woody stems and compound leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are notable for their size and vibrant color.
