Conioselinum neglectum

Conioselinum neglectum, commonly known as the slender wormwood, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in umbels. Due to its hardy nature, it is considered a weed in some regions but is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
