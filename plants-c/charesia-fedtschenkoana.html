Charesia fedtschenkoana

Charesia fedtschenkoana, commonly known as the Fedtschenkoan lily, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cold, mountainous environments and is adapted to the harsh climatic conditions of its habitat. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to its endangered status, efforts are underway to protect and study this rare botanical species.
