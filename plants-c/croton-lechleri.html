Croton lechleri

Croton lechleri, commonly known as Sangre de Grado, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and striking red sap, which is rich in latex. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties. Its latex has been traditionally used by indigenous communities for its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.
