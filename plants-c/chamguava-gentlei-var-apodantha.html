Chamguava gentlei var. apodantha

Chamguava gentlei var. apodantha, commonly known as the slender chamguava, is a rare and unique variant of the chamguava species.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender stems and reduced foliage, which distinguish it from other varieties. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
