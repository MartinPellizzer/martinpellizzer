Caryopteris mairei

Caryopteris mairei, commonly known as Chinese blue mistflower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, blue-lilac flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Caryopteris mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
