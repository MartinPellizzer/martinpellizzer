Caltha minor

Caltha minor, commonly known as lesser caltha, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features delicate, nodding yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, white down.

Caltha minor is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in wet conditions.
