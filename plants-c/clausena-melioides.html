Clausena melioides

Clausena melioides, commonly known as the Indian sour plum or wild mango, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and fragrant flowers. The fruit is small, yellowish, and has a sour taste, often used in traditional medicine and culinary practices. The plant is also valued for its ornamental features and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.

Its medicinal properties include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, making it a subject of scientific interest.
