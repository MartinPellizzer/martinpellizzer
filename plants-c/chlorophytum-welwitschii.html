Chlorophytum welwitschii

Chlorophytum welwitschii, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive yellow or white stripes. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an excellent choice for indoor cultivation. Its air-purifying qualities have also made it popular in urban environments.
