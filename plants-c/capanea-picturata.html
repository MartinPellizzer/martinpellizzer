Capanea picturata

Capanea picturata, commonly known as the painted capanea, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs.

Capanea picturata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.
