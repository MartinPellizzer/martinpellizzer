Cotyledon macrantha

Cotyledon macrantha, commonly known as the giant cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to grow optimally.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
