Cistus philothei

Cistus philothei, commonly known as the Greek rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, pink or white flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Cistus philothei is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
