Codonopsis levicalyx var. hirsuticalyx

Codonopsis levicalyx var. hirsuticalyx, commonly known as the hairy codonopsis, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and traditional uses.
