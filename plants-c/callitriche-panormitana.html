Callitriche panormitana

Callitriche panormitana, commonly known as the Mediterranean water starwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to southern Europe and the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds, ditches, and streams. The plant has small, floating or submerged leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are tiny, greenish-white, and appear in clusters, usually near the water's surface.

Callitriche panormitana plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and small fish.
