Cynanchum maccartii var. latifolium

Cynanchum maccartii var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes milkweeds, and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.
