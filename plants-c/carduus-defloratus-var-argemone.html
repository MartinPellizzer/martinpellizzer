Carduus defloratus var. argemone

Carduus defloratus var. argemone, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces a tall flowering stem with numerous florets.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
