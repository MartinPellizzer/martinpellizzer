Chilocarpus nigrescens

Chilocarpus nigrescens, commonly known as black-leafed hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers with five petals that range in color from white to deep red. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
