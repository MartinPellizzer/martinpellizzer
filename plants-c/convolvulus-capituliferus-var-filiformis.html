Convolvulus capituliferus var. filiformis

Convolvulus capituliferus var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are alternate, narrow, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
