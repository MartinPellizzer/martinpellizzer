Crotalaria kilimandscharica

Crotalaria kilimandscharica, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a herbaceous shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the plant has a woody stem that becomes more pronounced with age.

It is adapted to survive in harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
