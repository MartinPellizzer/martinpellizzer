Caragana stenophylla var. parviflora

Caragana stenophylla var. parviflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved shrub vetch, is a deciduous shrub native to central Asia.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow flowers in late spring. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in dry climates.
