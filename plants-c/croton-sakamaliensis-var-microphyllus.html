Croton sakamaliensis var. microphyllus

Croton sakamaliensis var. microphyllus, commonly known as the miniature croton, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its limited distribution and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
