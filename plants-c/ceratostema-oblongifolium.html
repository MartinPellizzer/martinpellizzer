Ceratostema oblongifolium

Ceratostema oblongifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed bellflower, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Ceratostema oblongifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in horticulture.
