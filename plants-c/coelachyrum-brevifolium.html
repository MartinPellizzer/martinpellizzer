Coelachyrum brevifolium

Coelachyrum brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved coelachyrum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences and are usually pink or purple in color.

Coelachyrum brevifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
