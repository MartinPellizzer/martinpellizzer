Crinum rattrayii

Crinum rattrayii, commonly known as the Rattray lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and the Indian Ocean.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters at the top of tall, strap-like leaves. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to a variety of garden conditions. Its bulbs are toxic, so caution is advised when handling the plant.

Crinum rattrayii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
