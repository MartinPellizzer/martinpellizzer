Clerodendrum diversifolium

Clerodendrum diversifolium, commonly known as the Variegated Flame Bush, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
