Chrysanthemum tomentellum

Chrysanthemum tomentellum, commonly known as the woolly chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the autumn season. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
