Casearia glabra

Casearia glabra, commonly known as white casearia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Casearia glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
