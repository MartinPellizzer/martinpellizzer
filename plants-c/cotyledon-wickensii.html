Cotyledon wickensii

Cotyledon wickensii, commonly known as the Wicken's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges, providing a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cotyledon wickensii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
