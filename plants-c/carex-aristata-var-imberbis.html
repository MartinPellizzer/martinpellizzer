Carex aristata var. imberbis

Carex aristata var. imberbis, commonly known as the smooth sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and North America.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets of flowers. It typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks.
