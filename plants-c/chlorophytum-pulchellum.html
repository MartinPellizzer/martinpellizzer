Chlorophytum pulchellum

Chlorophytum pulchellum, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail or Tiger's Head plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, striped leaves that resemble a tiger's tail. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves with white or yellow markings. It produces tall, flowering stems with small white or greenish flowers.

This hardy plant is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
