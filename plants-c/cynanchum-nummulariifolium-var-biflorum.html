Cynanchum nummulariifolium var. biflorum

Cynanchum nummulariifolium var. biflorum, commonly known as the two-flowered milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
