Columnea katzensteiniae

Columnea katzensteiniae, commonly known as the Katzenstein's flame vine, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant produces opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
