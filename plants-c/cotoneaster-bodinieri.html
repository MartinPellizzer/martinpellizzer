Cotoneaster bodinieri

Cotoneaster bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, dark green leaves that turn reddish-brown in autumn. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster bodinieri is also valued for its role in wildlife habitats and erosion control.
