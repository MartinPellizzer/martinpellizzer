Chlorophytum sphacelatum var. sphacelatum

Chlorophytum sphacelatum var. sphacelatum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a perennial evergreen herb native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green stripes, which are a result of its natural genetic variation. The plant is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped foliage and produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks.
