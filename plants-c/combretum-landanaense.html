Combretum landanaense

Combretum landanaense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

The tree is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
