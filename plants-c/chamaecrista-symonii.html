Chamaecrista symonii

Chamaecrista symonii, commonly known as the yellow crowfoot, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-desert environments.
