Collomia linearis var. humilis

Collomia linearis var. humilis, commonly known as the slender phlox, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features delicate, pink to lavender flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and native plant landscapes.
