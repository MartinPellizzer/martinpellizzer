Corynephorus

Corynephorus, commonly known as the needlegrass, is a genus of flowering plants in the grass family Poaceae.

These plants are typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, where they thrive in sandy or gravelly soils. Corynephorus species are characterized by their slender, needle-like leaves and compact, often tufted growth habit.
