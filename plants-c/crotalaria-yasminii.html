Crotalaria yasminii

Crotalaria yasminii, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive spurred shape. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces seed pods that split open when mature. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower in some regions.
