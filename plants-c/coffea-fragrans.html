Coffea fragrans

Coffea fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant coffee plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small white flowers. The plant produces red berries containing seeds used for coffee production. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
