Centaurea paniculata f. litigiosa

Centaurea paniculata f. litigiosa, commonly known as the litigious centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads, which are composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
