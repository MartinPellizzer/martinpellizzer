Callisia disgrega

Callisia disgrega, commonly known as the zigzag spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive, zigzag-shaped stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, silky hairs.

Callisia disgrega thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.
