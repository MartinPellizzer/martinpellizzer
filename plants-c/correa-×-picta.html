Correa × picta

Correa × picta, commonly known as the hybrid correa, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Correa × picta is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in garden settings.
