Cyperus melanostachyus var. gracilis

Cyperus melanostachyus var. gracilis, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the Cyperaceae family. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found in disturbed soils near water bodies.

Its botanical structure makes it adaptable to various aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
