Catoblastus anomalus

Catoblastus anomalus, commonly known as the red-tailed spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are notable for their vibrant colors and intricate structures, which aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
