Cleome cubensis

Cleome cubensis, commonly known as Cuban spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and showy, spiky flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Cleome cubensis thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it both ecologically and aesthetically valuable.
