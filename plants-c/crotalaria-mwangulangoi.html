Crotalaria mwangulangoi

Crotalaria mwangulangoi, commonly known as the Mwangulango shrub, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant is often found in grassland and savanna habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It has a taproot system and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant.
