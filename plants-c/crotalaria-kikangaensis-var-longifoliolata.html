Crotalaria kikangaensis var. longifoliolata

Crotalaria kikangaensis var. longifoliolata, commonly known as the long-leaved crotalaria, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with elongated leaflets, giving it its varietal name. It produces vibrant yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemate inflorescences.

This species is often found in dry savanna habitats and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
