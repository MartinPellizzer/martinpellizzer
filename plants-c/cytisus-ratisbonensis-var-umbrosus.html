Cytisus ratisbonensis var. umbrosus

Cytisus ratisbonensis var. umbrosus, commonly known as the dark foliage broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded garden environments.
