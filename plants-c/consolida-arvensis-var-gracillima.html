Consolida arvensis var. gracillima

Consolida arvensis var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender sweet pea, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, purple, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with slender stems and feathery leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a range of environmental conditions.
