Ceratostylis subapoda

Ceratostylis subapoda, commonly known as the narrow-leafed spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, narrow ovate leaf that is often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces a single spike bearing several small, white or pale green flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

This orchid thrives in shaded, moist environments and is typically found in rainforests or along stream banks.
