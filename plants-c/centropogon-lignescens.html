Centropogon lignescens

Centropogon lignescens, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features upright stems that are woody at the base and covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped, hairy, and have a distinctive blue-green color.

The flowers are yellow and borne in clusters, producing small, wind-dispersed seeds.
