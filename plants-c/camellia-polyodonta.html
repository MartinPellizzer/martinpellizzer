Camellia polyodonta

Camellia polyodonta, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from red to pink and white. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Camellia polyodonta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
