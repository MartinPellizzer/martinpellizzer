Claoxylon linostachys subsp. linostachys

Claoxylon linostachys subsp.

linostachys, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and its striking golden-yellow inflorescences that appear in clusters.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that give way to woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
