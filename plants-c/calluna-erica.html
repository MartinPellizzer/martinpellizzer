Calluna erica

Calluna erica, commonly known as heather, is a flowering plant native to the northern hemisphere.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub that thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink, red, or white, blooming in late summer. It is often found in heathlands and moorlands, where it forms dense mats.

Calluna erica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in challenging environments.
