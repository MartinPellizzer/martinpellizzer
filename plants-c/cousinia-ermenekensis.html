Cousinia ermenekensis

Cousinia ermenekensis, commonly known as the Ermenek cudweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climates. Its foliage is usually basal, with narrow, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

The flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence.
