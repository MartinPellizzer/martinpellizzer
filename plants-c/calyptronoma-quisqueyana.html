Calyptronoma quisqueyana

Calyptronoma quisqueyana, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that display a vibrant mix of purple and white hues. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils in high-altitude regions. Its flowers bloom in late spring and early summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
