Calamus pallens

Calamus pallens, commonly known as the pale reed or slender calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and elongated, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It thrives in wet, tropical environments and is often found growing along riverbanks and in swampy areas.
