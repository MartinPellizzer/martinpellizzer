Chomelia ulei

Chomelia ulei, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the flowering season. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark and leaves of Chomelia ulei have been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
