Combretum racemosum var. flammeum

Combretum racemosum var. flammeum, commonly known as flame combretum, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or orange-pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has small, oval leaves that are usually covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
