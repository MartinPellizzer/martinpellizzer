Corymbis thouarsii

Corymbis thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments. Corymbis thouarsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
