Coptis quinquefolia var. quinquefolia

Coptis quinquefolia var. quinquefolia, commonly known as the American goldthread, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow rhizomes and trifoliate or five-leaflets leaves. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and traditional herbal contexts.
