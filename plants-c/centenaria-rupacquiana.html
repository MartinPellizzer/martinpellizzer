Centenaria rupacquiana

Centenaria rupacquiana, commonly known as the desert milkweed, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.
