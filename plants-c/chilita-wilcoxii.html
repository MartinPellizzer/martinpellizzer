Chilita wilcoxii

Chilita wilcoxii, commonly known as the Wilcox's chilita, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the unique climate of Hawaii. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange blooms.
