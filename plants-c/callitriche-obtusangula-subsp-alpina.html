Callitriche obtusangula subsp. alpina

Callitriche obtusangula subsp.

alpina, commonly known as alpine callitriche, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to alpine regions. It typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds, streams, and wet meadows.

The plant has narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale pink, with four petals.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and small fish.
