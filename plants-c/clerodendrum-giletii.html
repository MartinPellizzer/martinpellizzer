Clerodendrum giletii

Clerodendrum giletii, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, which attract various pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
