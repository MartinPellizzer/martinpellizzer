Cineraria othonnoides

Cineraria othonnoides, commonly known as the Canary Island cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has silvery-gray foliage that helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Cineraria othonnoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
