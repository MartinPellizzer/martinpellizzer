Clerodendrum preussii

Clerodendrum preussii, commonly known as the African glorybower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture with a strong scent.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.
