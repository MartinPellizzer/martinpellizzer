Cyclophorus obovatus

Cyclophorus obovatus, commonly known as the broad-leafed cyclophorus, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It features lanceolate to obovate leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.
