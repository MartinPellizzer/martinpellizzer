Chilita kunzeana

Chilita kunzeana, commonly known as the African snowbell, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, hairy stems and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Chilita kunzeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
