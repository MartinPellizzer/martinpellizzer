Caprifolium gibbiflorum

Caprifolium gibbiflorum, commonly known as the mountain nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades and tomatoes. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellow or orange berry that ripens in summer.

The species is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
