Clutia sonderiana var. ovalifolia

Clutia sonderiana var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved clutia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often glossy and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
