Carpinus fargesii

Carpinus fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by edible, round berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
