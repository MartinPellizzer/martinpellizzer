Cirsium eriophorum subsp. oviforme

Cirsium eriophorum subsp.

oviforme, commonly known as the ovate thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and spiny, ovate-shaped leaves.

The plant produces purple to pink flowers in dense, spherical heads, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it an important component of diverse ecological systems.
