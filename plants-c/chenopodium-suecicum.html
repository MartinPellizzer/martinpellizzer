Chenopodium suecicum

Chenopodium suecicum, commonly known as Swedish goosefoot, is a low-growing annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It has alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually hairless and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are enclosed in small, dry fruits. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Chenopodium suecicum is adapted to a wide range of environmental conditions, making it a resilient weed in agricultural settings.
