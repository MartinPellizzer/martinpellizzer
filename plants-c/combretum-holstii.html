Combretum holstii

Combretum holstii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

The tree is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility in its native habitat.
