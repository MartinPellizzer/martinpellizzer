Clomenocoma laciniata

Clomenocoma laciniata, commonly known as the laceleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its finely dissected, lobed leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its unique foliage, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
