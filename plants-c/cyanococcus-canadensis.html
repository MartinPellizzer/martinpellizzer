Cyanococcus canadensis, commonly known as the Canadian blue moss, is a type of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and its ability to thrive in dry, shaded environments. Cyanococcus canadensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing microhabitats for other organisms in its ecosystem.
