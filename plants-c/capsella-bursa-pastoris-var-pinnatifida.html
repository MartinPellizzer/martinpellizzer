Capsella bursa-pastoris var. pinnatifida

Capsella bursa-pastoris var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the heartleaf common groundsel, is a variant of the common weed Capsella bursa-pastoris.

It is an annual herbaceous plant characterized by its pinnately divided leaves, which give it a fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into distinctive bur-like seed pods. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it easily identifiable and adaptable to various environmental conditions.
