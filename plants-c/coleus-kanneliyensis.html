Coleus kanneliyensis

Coleus kanneliyensis, commonly known as the Kanneliyensis coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and purple. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in whorled clusters.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil and partial shade.
