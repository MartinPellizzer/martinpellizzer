Chenopodium rubrum var. blitum

Chenopodium rubrum var. blitum, commonly known as red goosefoot, is a herbaceous annual plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, broadly ovate leaves with a rough, sandpapery texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, typically flowering and seeding in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered a common weed in many regions.
