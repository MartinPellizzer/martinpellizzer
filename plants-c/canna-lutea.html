Canna lutea

Canna lutea, commonly known as the yellow canna, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, and its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces both male and female flowers, with the female flowers being more prominent and often used in ornamental gardens. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and floral display.

Canna lutea is also valued for its ornamental use in landscaping and as a source of ornamental roots.
