Cotyledon erecta

Cotyledon erecta, commonly known as the upright cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces striking yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Cotyledon erecta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
