Cryptostemon ericaeus

Cryptostemon ericaeus, commonly known as the African heath.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
