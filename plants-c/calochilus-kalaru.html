Calochilus kalaru

Calochilus kalaru, commonly known as the Kalaru spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or yellow in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Calochilus kalaru is a perennial herb that is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
