Cynorkis mellitula

Cynorkis mellitula, commonly known as the honeycomb orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, honeycomb-like structure on its petals, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with good drainage. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer.

Cynorkis mellitula is a perennial herb that is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.
