Centaurea nigra var. endressii

Centaurea nigra var. endressii, commonly known as the Blackweed Centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems and produces striking dark purple to black flowers with bright yellow centers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
