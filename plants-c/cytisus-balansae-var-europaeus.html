Cytisus balansae var. europaeus

Cytisus balansae var. europaeus, commonly known as the European broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are attractive to pollinators.

Its woody stems and adaptability to various soil types make it a resilient and ecologically significant species.
