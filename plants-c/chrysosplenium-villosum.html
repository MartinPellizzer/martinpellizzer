Chrysosplenium villosum

Chrysosplenium villosum, commonly known as hairy chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of Asia and North America.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with small, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of delicate, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and native garden landscaping.
