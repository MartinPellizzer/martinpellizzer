Cymbidium goeringii var. gracillimum

Cymbidium goeringii var. gracillimum, commonly known as the graceful cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, sweet scent. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
