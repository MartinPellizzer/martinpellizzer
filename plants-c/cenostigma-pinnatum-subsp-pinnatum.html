Cenostigma pinnatum subsp. pinnatum

Cenostigma pinnatum subsp.

pinnatum, commonly known as the pinnate cenostigma, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil. It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged with numerous small leaflets.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
