Crypteronia paniculata var. glabra

Crypteronia paniculata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth crypteronia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in arid or semi-arid regions.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and drought resistance.
