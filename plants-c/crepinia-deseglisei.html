Crepinia deseglisei

Crepinia deseglisei, commonly known as the Cape Town daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a low, spreading habit with hairy leaves. It produces small, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Crepinia deseglisei is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardiness in mild climates.
