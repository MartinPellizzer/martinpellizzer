Callista cucumerina

Callista cucumerina, commonly known as the cucumber tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces edible, cucumber-like fruits that are used in traditional cuisine.
