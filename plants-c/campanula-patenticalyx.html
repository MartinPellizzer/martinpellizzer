Campanula patenticalyx

Campanula patenticalyx, commonly known as the broadleaf bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Campanula patenticalyx is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
