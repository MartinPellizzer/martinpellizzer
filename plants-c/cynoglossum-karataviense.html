Cynoglossum karataviense

Cynoglossum karataviense, commonly known as the Karatav Valley bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It features striking violet-blue flowers that resemble small bells, typically blooming in summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Cynoglossum karataviense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
