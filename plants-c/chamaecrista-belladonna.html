Chamaecrista belladonna

Chamaecrista belladonna, commonly known as the pretty panicled trefoil, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring.
