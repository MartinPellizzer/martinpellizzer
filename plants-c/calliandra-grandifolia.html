Calliandra grandifolia

Calliandra grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered calliandra, is a tropical leguminous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery, pink or red inflorescences that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Calliandra grandifolia is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
