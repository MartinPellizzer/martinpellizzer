Citharexylum fruticosum var. villosum

Citharexylum fruticosum var. villosum, commonly known as the hairy citharexylum, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in coastal or disturbed areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The flowers bloom in axillary clusters, adding ornamental value to the plant.
