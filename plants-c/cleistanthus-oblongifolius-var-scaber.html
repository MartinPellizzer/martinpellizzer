Cleistanthus oblongifolius var. scaber

Cleistanthus oblongifolius var. scaber, commonly known as the rough-leaved cleistanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, rough-textured leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Cleistanthaceae family.
