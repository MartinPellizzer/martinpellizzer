Cynoctonum virens

Cynoctonum virens, commonly known as the green spider plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
