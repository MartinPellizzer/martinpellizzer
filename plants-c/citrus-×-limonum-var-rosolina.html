Citrus × limonum var. rosolina

Citrus × limonum var. rosolina, commonly known as the sweet orange.

This variety is characterized by its thick, segmented fruit with a thin, smooth peel and a juicy, sweet pulp. The leaves are glossy and dark green, with a strong aromatic scent. It is widely cultivated for its fruit, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
