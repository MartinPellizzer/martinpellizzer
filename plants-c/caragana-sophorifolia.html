Caragana sophorifolia

Caragana sophorifolia, commonly known as the Chinese pea tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable for soil enrichment. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for use in reforestation and erosion control projects.
