Cyperus stolonifer

Cyperus stolonifer, commonly known as spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of spike-like inflorescences, which are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, wind-pollinated flowers arranged in a cylindrical shape along the stem. Cyperus stolonifer thrives in wetland environments, such as marshes, ponds, and along the edges of water bodies.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and filter water.
