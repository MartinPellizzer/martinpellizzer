Calycothrix aurea

Calycothrix aurea, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that resemble stars, hence its common name. Calycothrix aurea typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the understory of tropical forests.

It has a compact growth habit with narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant blooms during the warmer months.
