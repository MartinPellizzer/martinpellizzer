Ctenopteris mollicoma

Ctenopteris mollicoma, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive appearance that sets it apart from other fern species.

Ctenopteris mollicoma is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
