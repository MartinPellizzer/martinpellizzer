Coelogyne uniflora

Coelogyne uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, showy flower that blooms in shades of white, pink, or red. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces aerial roots for attachment to trees. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne uniflora is appreciated for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
