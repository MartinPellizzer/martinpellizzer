Citrus × sinograndis

Citrus × sinograndis, commonly known as the Guangxi citrus or Guangxi mandarin.

It is a hybrid citrus species native to southern China, characterized by its small, round fruits with a thick, aromatic rind. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, featuring glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is prized for its sweet, juicy pulp and is often used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

The botanical features of Citrus × sinograndis make it a valuable species in horticulture and agriculture.
