Cucurbita pepo var. storkiana

Cucurbita pepo var. storkiana, commonly known as the stork's bill, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, vine-like growth habit and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, spiny fruits. Its fruit is typically green and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

It is native to the Americas and is often found in dry, open habitats.
