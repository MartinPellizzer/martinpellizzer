Carex shinnersii

Carex shinnersii, commonly known as Shinners' sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, grasses, and rushes. This plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often wind-pollinated.
