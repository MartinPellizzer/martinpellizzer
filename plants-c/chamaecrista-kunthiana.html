Chamaecrista kunthiana

Chamaecrista kunthiana, commonly known as the yellow cassia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in open woodlands and disturbed sites.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
