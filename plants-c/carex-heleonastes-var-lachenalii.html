Carex heleonastes var. lachenalii

Carex heleonastes var. lachenalii, commonly known as the Lachenal's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance and specialized inflorescence structures. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
