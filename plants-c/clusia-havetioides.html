Clusia havetioides

Clusia havetioides, commonly known as the broadleaf clusia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Clusiaceae family, characterized by its evergreen nature and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are often overlooked due to their subtle appearance. Clusia havetioides is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in both full sun and partial shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and resilience in tropical and subtropical climates.
