Caudoleucaena

Caudoleucaena, commonly known as the silky serissa, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pods that contain small, round seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Caudoleucaena is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
