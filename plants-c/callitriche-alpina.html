Callitriche alpina

Callitriche alpina, commonly known as alpine waterstar, is a small aquatic plant native to mountainous regions.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as streams, lakes, and wetlands. The plant has small, star-shaped leaves that are often floating or submerged. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, with four petals.

Callitriche alpina plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and small fish.
