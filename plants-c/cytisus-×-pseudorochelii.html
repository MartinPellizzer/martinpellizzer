Cytisus × pseudorochelii

Cytisus × pseudorochelii, commonly known as the hybrid broom, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and hairy, and it produces slender, woody pods that contain seeds.

Cytisus × pseudorochelii is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in coastal and dry environments.
