Ceropegia festucifolia

Ceropegia festucifolia, commonly known as the grass-leaved ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thin, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white, waxy hair. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
