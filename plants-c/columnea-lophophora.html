Columnea lophophora

Columnea lophophora, commonly known as the silver mist plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its striking, tubular, red flowers that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
