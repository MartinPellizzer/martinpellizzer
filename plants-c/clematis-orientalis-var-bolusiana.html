Clematis orientalis var. bolusiana

Clematis orientalis var. bolusiana, commonly known as the Bolusiana Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to South Africa.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
