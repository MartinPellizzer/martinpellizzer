Calandrinia acaulis var. acaulis

Calandrinia acaulis var. acaulis, commonly known as the slender-flowered portulaca, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual herb with fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its botanical features include small, alternate leaves and a compact growth habit that aids in water retention.
