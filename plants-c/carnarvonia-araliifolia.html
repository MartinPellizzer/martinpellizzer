Carnarvonia araliifolia

Carnarvonia araliifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf hakea, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Carnarvonia araliifolia is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in dry climates.
