Crotalaria medicaginea var. herniarioides

Crotalaria medicaginea var. herniarioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round pods containing several seeds, which are often used as a food source in some regions. It is native to tropical and subtropical areas and thrives in well-drained soils.

The species is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
