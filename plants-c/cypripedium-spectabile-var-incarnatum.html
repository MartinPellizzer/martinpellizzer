Cypripedium spectabile var. incarnatum

Cypripedium spectabile var. incarnatum, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features a large, showy pink or white pouch-shaped lip that serves as a pollination mechanism. The plant has a thick, fleshy rhizome and grows in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique beauty.
