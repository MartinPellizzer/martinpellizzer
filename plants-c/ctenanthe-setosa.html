Ctenanthe setosa

Ctenanthe setosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The leaves often display vibrant patterns of red, pink, and white, making it a popular ornamental plant. It prefers warm, humid environments and thrives in partial shade.

Ctenanthe setosa is typically grown as a houseplant or in greenhouse conditions due to its sensitivity to cold temperatures.
