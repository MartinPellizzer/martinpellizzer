Curio acaulis var. ecklonis

Curio acaulis var. ecklonis, commonly known as the Ecklon's tree fern, is a distinctive member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk that is covered with a fibrous, brownish layer. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Native to South Africa, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor cultivation.
