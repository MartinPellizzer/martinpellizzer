Callista antennata

Callista antennata, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, red-orange flowers with a distinctive spider-like appearance, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are often mistaken for grass or sedges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
