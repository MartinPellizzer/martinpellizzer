Cassine humbertii

Cassine humbertii, commonly known as the Humbert's cassine, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish bark and clusters of small, white flowers. Its botanical features make it an important component of riparian ecosystems.
