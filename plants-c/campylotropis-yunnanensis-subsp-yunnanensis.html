Campylotropis yunnanensis subsp. yunnanensis

Campylotropis yunnanensis subsp.

yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan campylotropis, is a shrub native to China. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soils. It has a taproot system and produces pods that contain several seeds.

This subspecies is of interest for its potential ecological and medicinal value.
