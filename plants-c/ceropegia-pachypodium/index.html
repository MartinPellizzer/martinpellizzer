Ceropegia Pachypodium

Ceropegia pachypodium is a unique succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its swollen, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that attract specific pollinators. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its beauty, it requires careful watering and well-draining soil to flourish.
