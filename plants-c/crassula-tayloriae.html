Crassula tayloriae

Crassula tayloriae, commonly known as the Taylor's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth pattern. The plant features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight red or purple tinge under stress. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months.

Crassula tayloriae is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
