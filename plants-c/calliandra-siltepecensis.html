Calliandra siltepecensis

Calliandra siltepecensis, commonly known as the Siltepec firetree, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences. The plant produces showy, bright red or orange flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are bipinnately compound, and it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Calliandra siltepecensis is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant blooms and ecological value.
