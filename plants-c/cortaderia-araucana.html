Cortaderia araucana

Cortaderia araucana, commonly known as the purple grass or pampas grass, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is a perennial grass that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring tall, feathery plumes that turn a striking purple color in the fall. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscapes and gardens.

Its architectural form and seasonal color changes make it a visually appealing addition to any garden setting.
