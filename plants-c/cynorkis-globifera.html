Cynorkis globifera

Cynorkis globifera, commonly known as the globular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Cynorkis globifera is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and ornamental value.
