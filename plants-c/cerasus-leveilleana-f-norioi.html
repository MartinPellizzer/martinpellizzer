Cerasus leveilleana f. norioi

Cerasus leveilleana f. norioi, commonly known as the Norioi cherry, is a rare and ornamental plant belonging to the Prunus genus.

It is characterized by its delicate pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a striking visual appeal to gardens. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for temperate climates.

Due to its limited distribution and unique floral characteristics, it is highly valued in botanical collections and conservation efforts.
