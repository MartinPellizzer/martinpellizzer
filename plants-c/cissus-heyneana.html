Cissus heyneana

Cissus heyneana, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-green leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb make it a popular choice for vertical gardening and indoor spaces.
