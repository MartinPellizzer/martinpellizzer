Cactus broadwayi

Cactus broadwayi, commonly known as the broadway cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its desert habitat. Its structure is adapted for minimal water loss, with spines that provide shade and deter herbivores.

Overall, Cactus broadwayi exemplifies the unique botanical adaptations of desert flora.
