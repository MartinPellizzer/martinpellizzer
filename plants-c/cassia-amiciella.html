Cassia amiciella

Cassia amiciella, commonly known as the friendship tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The tree is known for its ornamental value, featuring fragrant, golden-yellow flowers and palmately compound leaves. Cassia amiciella thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and reforestation projects.
