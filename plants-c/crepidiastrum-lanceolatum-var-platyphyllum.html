Crepidiastrum lanceolatum var. platyphyllum

Crepidiastrum lanceolatum var. platyphyllum, commonly known as the broadleaf pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.
