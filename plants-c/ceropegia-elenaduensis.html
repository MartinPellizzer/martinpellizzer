Ceropegia elenaduensis

Ceropegia elenaduensis, commonly known as the Elenaduensis Ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
