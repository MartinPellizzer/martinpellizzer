Croton ivohibensis var. alaotrensis

Croton ivohibensis var. alaotrensis, commonly known as the Madagascar croton, is a shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
