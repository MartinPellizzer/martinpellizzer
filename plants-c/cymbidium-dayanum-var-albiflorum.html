Cymbidium dayanum var. albiflorum

Cymbidium dayanum var. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae. The plant features long, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers with a fragrant scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
