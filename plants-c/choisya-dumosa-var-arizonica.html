Choisya dumosa var. arizonica

Choisya dumosa var. arizonica, commonly known as Arizona beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces dark purple berries that are a striking feature, particularly in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in arid climates.
