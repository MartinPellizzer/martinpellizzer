Centaurium quadrifolium subsp. linariifolium

Centaurium quadrifolium subsp.

linariifolium, commonly known as the four-leaved centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink.

The leaves are typically narrow and linear, often arranged in a whorled pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
