Crinitaria Cretica

Crinitaria cretica is a rare and unique plant native to specific coastal areas of the Mediterranean. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring long, delicate white filaments that resemble hair. This plant thrives in sandy and saline environments, often found in dunes and rocky shores. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it an important species for coastal ecosystems. Despite its resilience, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
