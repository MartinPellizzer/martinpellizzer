Ceratochloa festucoides

Ceratochloa festucoides, commonly known as the slender browntop, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, spike-like cluster of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

Ceratochloa festucoides is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem.
