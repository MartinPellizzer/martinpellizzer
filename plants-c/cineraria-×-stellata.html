Cineraria × stellata

Cineraria × stellata, commonly known as the star cineraria, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple flowers and fuzzy, silver-green foliage. Native to the Canary Islands, it thrives in coastal environments with well-drained soil. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
