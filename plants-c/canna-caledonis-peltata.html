Canna caledonis-peltata

Canna caledonis-peltata, commonly known as the Caledonian shield cannas, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the island of Ceylon (Sri Lanka).

It features large, broad leaves that are typically shield-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Canna caledonis-peltata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a tropical plant in warmer climates.
