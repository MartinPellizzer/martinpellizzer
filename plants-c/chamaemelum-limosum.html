Chamaemelum limosum

Chamaemelum limosum, commonly known as yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along riverbanks.

Chamaemelum limosum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
