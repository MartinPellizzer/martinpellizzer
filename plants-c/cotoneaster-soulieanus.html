Cotoneaster soulieanus

Cotoneaster soulieanus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny branches and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is often found in rocky or well-drained soils.

Cotoneaster soulieanus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
