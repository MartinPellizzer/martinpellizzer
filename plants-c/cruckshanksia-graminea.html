Cruckshanksia graminea

Cruckshanksia graminea, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a dense rosette. Its flowering stems produce small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Cruckshanksia graminea is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats.
