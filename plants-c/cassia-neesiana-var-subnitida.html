Cassia neesiana var. subnitida

Cassia neesiana var. subnitida, commonly known as golden shower tree or sapphire tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, golden-yellow inflorescences that resemble cascading showers of light. The leaves are bipinnate, with numerous small, feather-like leaflets that give the plant a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
