Cucumis africanus var. acutilobus

Cucumis africanus var. acutilobus, commonly known as the African horned cucumber, is a unique member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its spiny, elongated fruit that resembles a horn. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. Its fruit is edible when young and has a mild, cucumber-like flavor.

This variety is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its potential culinary uses.
