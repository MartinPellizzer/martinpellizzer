Centaurea cineraria var. busambarensis

Centaurea cineraria var. busambarensis, commonly known as the Busambarensis cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its visual appeal.

This variety is closely related to other Centaurea species, sharing similar botanical features and ecological preferences.
