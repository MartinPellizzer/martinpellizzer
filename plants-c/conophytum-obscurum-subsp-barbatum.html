Conophytum obscurum subsp. barbatum

Conophytum obscurum subsp.

barbatum, commonly known as the rock rose or desert rose, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features compact, rounded stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

The plant produces small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
