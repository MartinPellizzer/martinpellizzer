Chlorophytum vaginatum

Chlorophytum vaginatum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often curl into tight spirals, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the spring and summer months. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and indirect sunlight.

Chlorophytum vaginatum is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for households and offices.
