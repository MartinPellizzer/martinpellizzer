Conostegia minutiflora

Conostegia minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered cypress.

It is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact appearance. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Conostegia minutiflora is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
