Calycomelia pistaciifolia

Calycomelia pistaciifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf calycomelia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by spherical, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is popular among gardeners seeking a compact, drought-resistant plant.
