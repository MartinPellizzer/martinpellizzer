Chevaliera germinyana

Chevaliera germinyana, commonly known as the Germinyana bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
