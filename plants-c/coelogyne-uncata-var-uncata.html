Coelogyne uncata var. uncata

Coelogyne uncata var. uncata, commonly known as the white coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender pseudobulbs and long, arching inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks in mountainous regions. Its flowers are short-lived but highly ornamental, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

The plant is known for its ability to bloom multiple times a year under proper care.
