Cyperus pseudodistans

Cyperus pseudodistans, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly rough texture.

Cyperus pseudodistans is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
