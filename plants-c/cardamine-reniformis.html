Cardamine reniformis

Cardamine reniformis, commonly known as the kidneyleaf bittercress, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its kidney-shaped leaves and small white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Its leaves and flowers are often used in traditional herbal remedies due to their mild medicinal properties.
