Carduus olivescens

Carduus olivescens, commonly known as the olive thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features spiny, lobed leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a spherical cluster, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it both a distinctive ornamental plant and a potential weed in agricultural settings.
