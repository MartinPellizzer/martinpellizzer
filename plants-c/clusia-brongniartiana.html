Clusia brongniartiana

Clusia brongniartiana, commonly known as the wax plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It is highly adaptable and thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Clusia brongniartiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements.
