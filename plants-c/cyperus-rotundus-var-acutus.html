Cyperus rotundus var. acutus

Cyperus rotundus var. acutus, commonly known as spike rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical clusters of spikelets that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers. It is commonly found in wetland areas, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
