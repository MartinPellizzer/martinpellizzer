Columnea nummularia

Columnea nummularia, commonly known as the silverleaf columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or silver hair, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Columnea nummularia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and light conditions.
