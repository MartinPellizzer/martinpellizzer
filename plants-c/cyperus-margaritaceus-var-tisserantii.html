Cyperus margaritaceus var. tisserantii

Cyperus margaritaceus var. tisserantii, commonly known as the Tisserant's flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often pale green or white in color. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils, often found in wetlands or along water bodies. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in water gardens and bog landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ability to help stabilize soil and filter water in aquatic environments.
