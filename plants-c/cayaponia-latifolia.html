Cayaponia latifolia

Cayaponia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf cayaponia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a humid environment.

Cayaponia latifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some indigenous communities.
