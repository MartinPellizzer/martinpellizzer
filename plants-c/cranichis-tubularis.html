Cranichis tubularis

Cranichis tubularis, commonly known as the tubular orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces intricate, spurred flowers that are adapted for specific pollinators, such as bees and wasps. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Cranichis tubularis is often found in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and wetlands.
