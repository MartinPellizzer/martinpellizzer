Clematis sanderi

Clematis sanderi, commonly known as the Sander's Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern North America.

It features slender, twining stems that can grow up to 15 feet in length, supporting its showy flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant, blue-violet flowers in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is heart-shaped and provides attractive foliage color throughout the growing season.

Clematis sanderi thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
