Croton pallidulus var. glabrus

Croton pallidulus var. glabrus, commonly known as the smooth croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are often surrounded by bracts. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark and leaves contain latex, which can be toxic if ingested.
