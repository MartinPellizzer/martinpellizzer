Chloridion

Chloridion, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous seeds.

Chloridion is native to southern Africa and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
