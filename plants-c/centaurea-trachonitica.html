Centaurea trachonitica

Centaurea trachonitica, commonly known as the Cyprus star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny leaves, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a short stature, usually reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Centaurea trachonitica is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.
