Clematis heynei

Clematis heynei, commonly known as the yellow clematis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a woody vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically borne in clusters along the stems. Clematis heynei thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
