Centranthus longiflorus subsp. junceus

Centranthus longiflorus subsp.

junceus, commonly known as narrowleaf redflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
