Cleome hirsuta

Cleome hirsuta, commonly known as spider flower, is an annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, rugged appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Cleome hirsuta is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
