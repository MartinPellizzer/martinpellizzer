Cypripedium macranthos f. vulgare

Cypripedium macranthos f. vulgare, commonly known as the common yellow lady's slipper, is a striking orchid native to the eastern United States.

It features a single, showy yellow or orange pouch-shaped spur that serves as a nectar trap to attract pollinators. The plant has a robust rhizome and typically grows in moist, wooded habitats. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single, large, showy flower per stem.

It is a rare and protected species, valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
