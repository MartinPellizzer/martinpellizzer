Chrysanthemum × morifolium var. gracile

Chrysanthemum × morifolium var. gracile, commonly known as the graceful chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically features finely cut, serrated leaves that are a vibrant green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climate conditions.
