Clethra wuyishanica var. erosa

Clethra wuyishanica var. erosa, commonly known as the Wuyi mountain clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and turn reddish in autumn, adding to its ornamental value.

The species is noted for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for various wildlife.
