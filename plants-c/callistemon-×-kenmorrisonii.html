Callistemon × kenmorrisonii

Callistemon × kenmorrisonii, commonly known as the hybrid bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is a hybrid between Callistemon citrinus and Callistemon viminalis, resulting in a plant with distinctive crimson or red bottlebrush flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The flowers attract nectar-feeding birds and insects, contributing to its ecological value.
