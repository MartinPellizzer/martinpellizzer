Combretum barbatum

Combretum barbatum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its bark is smooth and greyish, often marked with fissures.

Combretum barbatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
