Chimonocalamus delicatus

Chimonocalamus delicatus, commonly known as the delicate bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China and is characterized by its slender, graceful stalks and fine, feathery leaves. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories or on rocky slopes. Chimonocalamus delicatus is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small wildlife.
