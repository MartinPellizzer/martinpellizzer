Cerastium Nutans Var. Obtectum

Cerastium nutans var. obtectum is a charming member of the Caryophyllaceae family known for its delicate, hanging flowers. This plant is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its small, white blooms appear in clusters, creating a soft, cascading effect that makes it a favorite among gardeners. The variety obtectum is distinguished by its more compact growth and slightly different leaf structure compared to the typical form. It is a hardy perennial that adds a touch of elegance to rock gardens and borders.
