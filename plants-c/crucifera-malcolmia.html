Crucifera malcolmia

Crucifera malcolmia, commonly known as the Malawi mustard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to eastern Africa, particularly Tanzania and Malawi, where it grows in moist, grassy areas. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, typical of the cruciferous family. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
