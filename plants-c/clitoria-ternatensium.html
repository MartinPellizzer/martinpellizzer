Clitoria ternatensium

Clitoria ternatensium, commonly known as the winged bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features striking butterfly-shaped purple flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant produces edible pods containing seeds and a starchy, protein-rich bean that is cultivated for food. Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Clitoria ternatensium thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown as an ornamental and agricultural crop.
