Cyphostemma hardyi

Cyphostemma hardyi, commonly known as the hardy cyphostemma, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

Cyphostemma hardyi is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
