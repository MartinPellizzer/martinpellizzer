Cuspidaria argentea

Cuspidaria argentea, commonly known as silverleaf cuspidaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
