Callistemon arborescens

Callistemon arborescens, commonly known as the pink bottlebrush, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright pink or red cylindrical flower spikes that resemble bottle brushes. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically grey-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract birds and insects.
