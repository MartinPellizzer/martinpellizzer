Combretum basslerianum

Combretum basslerianum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
