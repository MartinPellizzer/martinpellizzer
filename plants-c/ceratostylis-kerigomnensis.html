Ceratostylis kerigomnensis

Ceratostylis kerigomnensis, commonly known as the Kerigomnense orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
