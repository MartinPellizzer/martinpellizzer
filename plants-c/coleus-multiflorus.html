Coleus multiflorus

Coleus multiflorus, commonly known as the multibract coleus, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, purple, pink, and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coleus multiflorus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking leaf patterns and ability to add visual interest to gardens and indoor spaces.
