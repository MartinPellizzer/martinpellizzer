Cousinia × mesomorpha

Cousinia × mesomorpha, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellowish to reddish composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to harsh, dry environments. It has hairy leaves and stems, which help reduce water loss.

Cousinia × mesomorpha is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
