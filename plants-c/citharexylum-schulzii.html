Citharexylum schulzii

Citharexylum schulzii, commonly known as the velvetleaf or coral tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture, and its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, often displaying white or pink hues. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
