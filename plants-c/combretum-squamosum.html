Combretum squamosum

Combretum squamosum, commonly known as the silver flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its grayish-silver bark and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
