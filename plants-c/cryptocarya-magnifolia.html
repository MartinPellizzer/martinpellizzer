Cryptocarya magnifolia

Cryptocarya magnifolia, commonly known as the magnificent cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the wet tropical forests of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that are often aromatic. It produces small, clustered white flowers followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits.
