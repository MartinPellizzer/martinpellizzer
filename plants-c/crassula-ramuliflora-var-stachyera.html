Crassula ramuliflora var. stachyera

Crassula ramuliflora var. stachyera, commonly known as the Stachyera Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
