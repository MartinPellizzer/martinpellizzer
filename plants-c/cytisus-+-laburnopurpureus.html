Cytisus + laburnopurpureus

Cytisus + laburnopurpureus, commonly known as the purple laburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its striking, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features slender, thorny stems and pinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and hedges.

The purple laburnum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
