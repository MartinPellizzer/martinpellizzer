Crassula smithii

Crassula smithii, commonly known as the Smith's crassula, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
