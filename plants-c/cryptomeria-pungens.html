Cryptomeria pungens

Cryptomeria pungens, commonly known as the Japanese cryptomeria, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and conical shape, with needle-like leaves that are dark green and slightly twisted. The tree produces small, reddish-brown cones that mature in the second year. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Cryptomeria pungens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other parts of the world for landscaping purposes.
