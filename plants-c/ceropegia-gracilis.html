Ceropegia gracilis

Ceropegia gracilis, commonly known as the slender ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a trailing or climbing plant with thin, succulent stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia gracilis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
