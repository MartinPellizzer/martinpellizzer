Coleataenia longifolia subsp. elongata

Coleataenia longifolia subsp.

elongata, commonly known as the elongated leaf coleataenia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Africa. It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
