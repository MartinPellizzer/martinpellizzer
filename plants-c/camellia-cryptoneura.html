Camellia cryptoneura

Camellia cryptoneura, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia cryptoneura is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
