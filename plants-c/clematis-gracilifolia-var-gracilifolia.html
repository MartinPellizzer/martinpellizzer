Clematis gracilifolia var. gracilifolia

Clematis gracilifolia var. gracilifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf clematis, is a flowering vine native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, twining stems that climb by means of small tendrils. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale blue flowers in early summer. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a finely toothed margin.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
