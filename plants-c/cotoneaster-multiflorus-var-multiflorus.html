Cotoneaster multiflorus var. multiflorus

Cotoneaster multiflorus var. multiflorus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically green on top and slightly hairy underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This species is also valued for its use in landscaping and as a food source for birds.
