Coryanthes albertinae var. rodriguezii

This species belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pouch-like flowers that resemble a slipper. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, where it thrives on the forest floor or on rocky substrates.
