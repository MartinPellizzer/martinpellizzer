Ceanothus impressus var. nipomensis

Ceanothus impressus var. nipomensis, commonly known as the Nipponese ceanothus, is a shrub native to Japan.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often appearing in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or pale underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
