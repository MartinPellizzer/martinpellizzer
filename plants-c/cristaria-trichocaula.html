Cristaria trichocaula

Cristaria trichocaula, commonly known as the hairstem crista, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a slender, hairy stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem.

It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.
