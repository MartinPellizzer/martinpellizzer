Chamaecrista claussenii var. megacycla

Chamaecrista claussenii var. megacycla, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by distinctive, spiny seed pods. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
