Corylopasania

Corylopasania, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Corylopasania is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and air-purifying properties.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
