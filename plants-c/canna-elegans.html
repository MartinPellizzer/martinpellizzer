Canna elegans

Canna elegans, commonly known as the elegant cannas, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Americas.

It features large, colorful leaves that can reach up to 2 feet in length, often with a glossy texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom from summer to fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for water gardens and tropical landscapes.

Canna elegans is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
