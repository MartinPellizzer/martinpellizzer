Croton origanifolius var. gracilis

Croton origanifolius var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender-leaved croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, spiky seed pods. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
