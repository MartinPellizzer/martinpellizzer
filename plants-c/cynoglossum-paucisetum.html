Cynoglossum paucisetum

Cynoglossum paucisetum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Boraginaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.
