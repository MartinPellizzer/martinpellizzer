Curculigo gracilis

Curculigo gracilis, commonly known as the slender arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. The plant is characterized by its slender, tuberous roots and arrowhead-shaped leaves. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in a spike.

Curculigo gracilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
