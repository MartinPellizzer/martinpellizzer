Cyphostemma fragariifolium

Cyphostemma fragariifolium, commonly known as the red milk vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or orange flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are often consumed by local wildlife. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing along fences or trees.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
