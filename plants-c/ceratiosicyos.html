Ceratiosicyos

Ceratiosicyos, commonly known as the horned bottle gourd, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its thick, spiny stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces distinctive, bottle-shaped fruits that are covered in sharp, ridged spines. Its flowers are typically yellow and are borne in clusters.

Ceratiosicyos is valued for its edible fruits and is often cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
