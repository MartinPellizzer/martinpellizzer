Cotyledon racemosa

Cotyledon racemosa, commonly known as the coral cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. Its succulent leaves store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

It is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
