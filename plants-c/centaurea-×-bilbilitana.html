Centaurea × bilbilitana

Centaurea × bilbilitana, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are arranged in a composite head, with both ray and disc florets.

Centaurea × bilbilitana is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
