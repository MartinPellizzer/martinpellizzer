Conyza holosericea

Conyza holosericea, commonly known as the silverleaf weed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its finely hairy stems and leaves, which give it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Conyza holosericea is considered a weed in many regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
