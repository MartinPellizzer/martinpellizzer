Croton claussenianus

Croton claussenianus, commonly known as the Clausen's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often strikingly ornamental plants. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiny capsules containing seeds.

Croton claussenianus is valued in horticulture for its vibrant foliage and unique floral structures.
