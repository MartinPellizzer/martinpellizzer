Conospermum rigidum

Conospermum rigidum, commonly known as the rigid honeysuckle, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woody growth and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces distinctive, woody fruits that contain the showy, red or orange seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry sclerophyll forests.

Conospermum rigidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
