Cyrtonema digitatum

Cyrtonema digitatum, commonly known as the silverleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Cyrtonema digitatum is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
