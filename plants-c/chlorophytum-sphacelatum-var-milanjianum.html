Chlorophytum sphacelatum var. milanjianum

Chlorophytum sphacelatum var. milanjianum, commonly known as the Milanjianum spider plant, is a compact, low-maintenance plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with a subtle blue or silver sheen, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks, which can be grown indoors or outdoors in suitable climates. It is well-suited for hanging baskets or as a shelf plant due to its trailing growth habit.

This variety is particularly valued for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
