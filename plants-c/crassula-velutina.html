Crassula velutina

Crassula velutina, commonly known as the velvet crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The leaves are typically green to bluish-green and can develop a reddish tinge when exposed to bright light. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.
