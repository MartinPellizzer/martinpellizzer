Cynanchum yunnanense

Cynanchum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is of interest in traditional Chinese medicine.
