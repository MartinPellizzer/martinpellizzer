Croton petraeus var. ovatus

Croton petraeus var. ovatus, commonly known as the ovate croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
