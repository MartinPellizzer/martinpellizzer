Calliandra rutenbergiana

Calliandra rutenbergiana, commonly known as the Rutenberg's calliandra, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, feathery, pink to red inflorescences. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.

The leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple small, oval-shaped leaflets.
