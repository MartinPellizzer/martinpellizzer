Callista findlayana

Callista findlayana, commonly known as the pink fairy tern, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along coastal areas and in inland regions with a Mediterranean climate. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight waxy coating.

Callista findlayana is a hardy species that is well adapted to withstand drought and salty coastal conditions.
