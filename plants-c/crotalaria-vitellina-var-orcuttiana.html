Crotalaria vitellina var. orcuttiana

Crotalaria vitellina var. orcuttiana, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has palmate leaves with three to five leaflets and produces capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

The species is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in certain ecological contexts.
