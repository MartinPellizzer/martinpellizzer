Cyathea margarethae

Cyathea margarethae, commonly known as the Margaretha tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with spores produced on the undersides of the fertile fronds.

Cyathea margarethae is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests and along stream banks.
