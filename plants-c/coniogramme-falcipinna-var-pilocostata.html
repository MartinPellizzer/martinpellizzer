Coniogramme falcipinna var. pilocostata

Coniogramme falcipinna var. pilocostata, commonly known as the hairy fan-leaf fern, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, deeply divided, fan-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil to flourish. Its foliage displays a vibrant green hue, with some cultivars exhibiting variegated or golden margins.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
