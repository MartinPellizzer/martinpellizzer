Cyathea borbonica var. simulans

Cyathea borbonica var. simulans, commonly known as the false tree fern, is a variant of the Cyathea borbonica species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a fern belonging to the family Cyatheaceae, characterized by its tall, tree-like fronds that can reach up to several meters in height. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and produces large, compound leaves with a feathery appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and indoor cultivation.
