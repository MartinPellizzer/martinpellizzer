Ceropegia gymnopoda

Ceropegia gymnopoda, commonly known as the naked-stem ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a trailing or climbing succulent with thin, jointed stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces unique, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia gymnopoda is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
