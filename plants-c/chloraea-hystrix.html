Chloraea hystrix

Chloraea hystrix, commonly known as the zigzag greenhood, is an Australian plant belonging to the orchid family.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped labellum and bright green petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. It produces small, white or green flowers that bloom during the spring and summer seasons.

Chloraea hystrix is a perennial herb that is often found in the eastern regions of Australia.
