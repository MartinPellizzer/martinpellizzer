Calanthe brassii

Calanthe brassii, commonly known as the golden calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting purple or red lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as mountain forests, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are broad and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Calanthe brassii is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
