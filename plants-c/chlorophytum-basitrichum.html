Chlorophytum basitrichum

Chlorophytum basitrichum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with green and white stripes. This plant is known for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The leaves are succulent, allowing the plant to store water efficiently.

It also produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually followed by red berries.
