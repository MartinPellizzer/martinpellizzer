Clematis peterae var. peterae

Clematis peterae var. peterae, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Clematis peterae var. peterae is relatively hardy and can tolerate light frosts, making it suitable for temperate climates.
