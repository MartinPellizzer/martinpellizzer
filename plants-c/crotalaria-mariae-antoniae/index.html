Crotalaria Mariae-Antoniae

Crotalaria mariae-antoniae is a rare and striking flowering plant native to specific regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant yellow blooms and distinctive leaf structure, making it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists. This species belongs to the legume family and has unique adaptations that allow it to thrive in its natural habitat.
