Cotoneaster mingkwongensis

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and red berries. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it produces attractive foliage that changes color with the seasons.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it remains relatively rare in horticultural trade.
