Cephaloschefflera ovoidea

Cephaloschefflera ovoidea, commonly known as the eggplant tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, egg-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that resemble miniature pineapples. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
