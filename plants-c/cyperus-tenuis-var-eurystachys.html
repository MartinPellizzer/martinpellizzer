Cyperus tenuis var. eurystachys

Cyperus tenuis var. eurystachys, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are clustered at the ends of the stems. It thrives in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in aquatic and semi-aquatic environments.
