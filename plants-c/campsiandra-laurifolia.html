Campsiandra laurifolia

Campsiandra laurifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf campsiandra, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery, dark green leaves that are glossy on both sides. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
