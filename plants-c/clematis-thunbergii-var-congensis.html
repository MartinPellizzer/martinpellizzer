Clematis thunbergii var. congensis

Clematis thunbergii var. congensis, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval-shaped leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, showy structure. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that mature to a dark blue or black color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental climbing plants.

Its botanical characteristics make it both visually appealing and ecologically beneficial in appropriate habitats.
