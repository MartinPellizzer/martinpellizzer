Cynanchum dombeyanum

Cynanchum dombeyanum, commonly known as the Dombey's milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is known for its milkweed species. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive horn-like structure. Its milky sap contains latex, which can be toxic to some animals.

Cynanchum dombeyanum is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.
