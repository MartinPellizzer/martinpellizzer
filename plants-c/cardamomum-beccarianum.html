Cardamomum beccarianum

Cardamomum beccarianum, commonly known as the narrowleaf cardamom.

It is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. Its leaves are elongated and have a serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The plant is valued for its aromatic seeds, which are used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
