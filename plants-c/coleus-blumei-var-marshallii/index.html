Coleus Blumei Var. Marshallii

Coleus blumei var. marshallii is a striking ornamental plant known for its vibrant, colorful foliage. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments. This variety is prized for its unique leaf patterns and intense hues of red, purple, and green. It is commonly grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings. Its beauty and ease of care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
