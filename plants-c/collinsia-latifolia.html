Collinsia latifolia

Collinsia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf springbeauty, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Collinsia latifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
