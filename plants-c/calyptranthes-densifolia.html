Calyptranthes densifolia

Calyptranthes densifolia, commonly known as the denseleaf calyptranthes, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
