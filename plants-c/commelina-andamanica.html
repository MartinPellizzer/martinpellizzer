Commelina andamanica

Commelina andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands.

Commelina andamanica plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and supporting biodiversity in its native habitat.
