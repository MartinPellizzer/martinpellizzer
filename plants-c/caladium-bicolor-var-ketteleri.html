Caladium bicolor var. ketteleri

Caladium bicolor var. ketteleri, commonly known as the Ketteleri flameleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that display striking patterns of red, white, and green. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is the main ornamental feature, making it popular in gardens and as a houseplant.

It is typically propagated through tuberous roots and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
