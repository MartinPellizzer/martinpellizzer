Conospermum dorrienii

Conospermum dorrienii, commonly known as the Dorrien's conospermum, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, leathery foliage. The plant produces small, spherical, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
