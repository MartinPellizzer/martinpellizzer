Cyrtandra oxybapha

Cyrtandra oxybapha, commonly known as the white-flowered cyrtandra, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Papua New Guinea. The plant's leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
