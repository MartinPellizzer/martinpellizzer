Cyathea amboinensis

Cyathea amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is typically found in humid, tropical environments. Its rhizome is thick and woody, supporting the growth of its massive fronds.

Cyathea amboinensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
