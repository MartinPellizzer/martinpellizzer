Ctenitis palmensis

Ctenitis palmensis, commonly known as the palmate ctenitis, is a species of fern belonging to the family Ctenitaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that are palmately divided into segments, giving it a unique and intricate appearance. Native to tropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The fronds are typically leathery and have a deep green color, with a slightly serrated margin.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
