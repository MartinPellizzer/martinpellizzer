Cyperus szechuanensis

Cyperus szechuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and cylindrical inflorescence. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to moist habitats. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters.

Cyperus szechuanensis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil in wetland areas.
