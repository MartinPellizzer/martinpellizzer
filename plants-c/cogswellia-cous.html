Cogswellia cous

Cogswellia cous, commonly known as the desert rose.

It is a succulent plant native to arid regions, characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. It produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the warmer months.

Cogswellia cous is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-water conditions.
