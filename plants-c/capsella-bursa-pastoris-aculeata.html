Capsella bursa-pastoris aculeata

Capsella bursa-pastoris aculeata, commonly known as the common weed or shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular seed pods and small, alternate leaves with a hairy texture. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and thrives in temperate climates. It produces small white or yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
