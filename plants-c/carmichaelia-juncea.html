Carmichaelia juncea

Carmichaelia juncea, commonly known as the narrow-leaved kowhai, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Carmichaelia juncea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
