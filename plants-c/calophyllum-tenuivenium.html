Calophyllum tenuivenium

Calophyllum tenuivenium, commonly known as the narrow-veined calophyllum, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, alternate leaves.
