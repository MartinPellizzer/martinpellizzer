Cucubalus japonicus

Cucubalus japonicus, commonly known as Japanese horned melon, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and gourds. The plant produces striking, spiky green fruits that resemble horns, hence its common name. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with five petals.

The fruit is edible, though its texture is fibrous and it has a mild, slightly sweet flavor.
