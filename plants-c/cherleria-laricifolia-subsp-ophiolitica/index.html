Cherleria Laricifolia Subsp. Ophiolitica

Cherleria laricifolia subsp. ophiolitica is a rare and intriguing plant species found in specific alpine environments. It is characterized by its unique morphological features and ecological adaptations to harsh mountainous conditions. This subspecies thrives in rocky and nutrient-poor soils, showcasing remarkable resilience. Its distribution is limited to a few isolated regions, making it a subject of scientific interest. Understanding this plant contributes to broader knowledge of plant biodiversity in extreme habitats.
