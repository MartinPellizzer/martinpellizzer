Calonema argocallum

Calonema argocallum, commonly known as the silver-leafed calonema, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, which give it its common name. It typically grows as a low shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

Calonema argocallum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
