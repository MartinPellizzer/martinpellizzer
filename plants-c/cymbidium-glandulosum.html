Cymbidium glandulosum

Cymbidium glandulosum, commonly known as the glandular cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, glandular pubescence. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers with bright colors, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cymbidium glandulosum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
