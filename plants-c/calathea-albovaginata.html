Calathea albovaginata

Calathea albovaginata, commonly known as the white sheath calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white veins and green leaves. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.

Calathea albovaginata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
