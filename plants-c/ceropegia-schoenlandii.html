Ceropegia schoenlandii

Ceropegia schoenlandii, commonly known as the string of hearts plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which are adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
