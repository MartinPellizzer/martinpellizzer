Cytisus Multiflorus

Multiflorous broom, scientifically known as Cytisus multiflorus, is a striking member of the legume family native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. This shrub is recognized for its profuse display of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, creating a vivid contrast against its dark green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and ecological value. Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil also makes it an important plant for soil enrichment and biodiversity. Despite its beauty, it requires careful management to prevent it from becoming invasive in certain environments.
