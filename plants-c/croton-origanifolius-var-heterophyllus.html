Croton origanifolius var. heterophyllus

Croton origanifolius var. heterophyllus, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored foliage, which often features shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that vary in shape depending on the growth conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with moderate light.
