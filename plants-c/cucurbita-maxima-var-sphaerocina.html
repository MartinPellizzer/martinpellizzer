Cucurbita maxima var. sphaerocina

Cucurbita maxima var. sphaerocina, commonly known as the globe pumpkin, is a cultivar of the species Cucurbita maxima.

It is characterized by its round, spherical shape and vibrant orange to yellow skin. The plant produces large, edible fruits that are often used in both culinary and decorative applications. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and versatility in various dishes.
