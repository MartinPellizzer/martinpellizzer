Cheilanthes brandegeei

Cheilanthes brandegeei, commonly known as the Brandegee's rock fern, is a species of fern that thrives in arid and rocky environments.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. This plant is well adapted to drought conditions, with a thick rhizome that stores water and nutrients. It is often found growing on exposed rock faces or in crevices, where it can anchor itself securely.

The plant's ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it an important component of desert ecosystems.
