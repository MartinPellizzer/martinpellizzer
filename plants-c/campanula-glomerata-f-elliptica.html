Campanula glomerata f. elliptica

Campanula glomerata f. elliptica, commonly known as the clustered bellflower or elliptic bellflower, is a compact, mat-forming perennial with dense clusters of sky-blue to violet flowers.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. It is native to Europe and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.

The compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, decorative plants.
