Cotula dioica var. rotundata

Cotula dioica var. rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in yellow and white. The leaves are typically rounded and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
