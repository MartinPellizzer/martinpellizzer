Carex aethiopica

Carex aethiopica, commonly known as Ethiopian sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of small, greenish flowers. It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and along waterways.

Carex aethiopica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic conditions.
